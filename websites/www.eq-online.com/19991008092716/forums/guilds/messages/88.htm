Spirit of the Moon
Innoruuk Server
Based in West and Middle Antonica, with possible future expansion


EXCERPTED CHARTER

Purpose:
The Spirit of the Moon has been established to build a network of like-minded adventurers with a desire to help those in need, and to establish a feeling of family throughout Norrath.

Focus and Philosophy:
We are first and formost a family, and are sworn to serve our brothers and sisters. Furthermore, we are each tasked to foster a spirit of helpfulness and good roleplaying by our own example.

Membership:
Membership is open to all races and classes, so long as the prospective member abides by the letter of this charter. Traditionally evil races and classes must demonstrate that they have the morale character to break free of their respective stereotypes.
