The Emerald Covenant Reborne!

Greetings Fellow Adventurers,
I bring you news of an event which has transpired in the last several weeks. The Emerald
Covenant, a guild which was originally intended to be based on the Rallos Zek( PVP )
server, has been reborne. A growing group of fellow adventurers and I have decided that
PVP in EverQuest was never meant to be, and is not what we expected or want any
longer. Consequently, when the next new server opens its doors, we are all going to
pick up and make our home there, getting a fresh start with fresh players. We are looking
for prospective members, those who wish also to leave behind the burden of the
slowly decaying and already decadent PVP server, those who are on other servers and
would like to switch for the opportunity to take part in the creation of a great group of
players and an incredible guild, or even those just starting out in search of mentors to
help you on your way in the world. Our main goals and objectives remain what they
were before the release of the commercial version of EverQuest; namely the promotion
of an interwoven roleplaying environment, intense cooperation and organization among
members, extensive exploration of new dungeons, areas, and quests, and most importantly
the desire to use our numbers to encourage positive changes to the world of EverQuest.
If you think you might be interested in joining, please feel free to send me email at
the address listed below. If you have any questions or comments, or just want more
information, you can also contact me. So, in short, if you think you might want to get
a fresh start with a fresh group of players and get off on the right foot in a tightly
bound group of dedicated adventurers, The Emerald Covenant might be what you
are looking for!

Hail and Goodhunt,
Mustapha Kentaro
