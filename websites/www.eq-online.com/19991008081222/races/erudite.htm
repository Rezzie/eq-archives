Erudite

The final variant of the Human race, being the exact opposite of Barbarians. The high man prides himself on his intellectual prowess to such a great extent that the physical disciplines are nearly excluded completely. Erudites are self involved and take a narrow view of other races, to such a great extent that Erudites might have problems obtaining things from merchants outside there home city of Erudin. Some guards in human cities have even been known to take up arms at the mere sight of an Erudite.

The High man shuns common tasks and menial labor, finding overdeveloped muscles to be grotesque. And they have a tendencie to suffer because of their views towards physical effort.

Racial Modifiers

Erudites are highly intelligent and physically weaker.

Starting Area's

If you play an Erudite then Erudin will be your starting point.
