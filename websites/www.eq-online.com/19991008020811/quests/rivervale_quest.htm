Wall Squad Quest
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
4-6 (to actively participate) Marshal Ghobber, Sheriff Rogilo, Deputy Budo New deputy
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No Items Gained This quest is unique it involves gaining 1000 goblin points. To gain points you do the following: Give 4 Runnyeye Beads to Sheriff Rogilo (4points) Give Deathfist Slash Belt to Deput Budo (5 points) Give Grappling Device to Marshal Ghobber (not sure no one has seen it ever) Kill Mooto (20 points) -- beware he'll charm you and make you his pet for a while. Rewards: Along the way you will get styphons...experience and some silver/copper at every turn-in and after a whole bunch of either turnning in beads or belts you will receive a piece of leather armor and a bag. To get the main reward (must have 1000 points) You ask Marshal Ghobber "Am I one with the Wall?" You will receive a Squad Ring (200 hp charged healing device refillable at a Rivervale cleric)
Reward for completing this quest?
After said amount of points receive Squad Ring.

Bixie Sting
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
Level Any Joogl Honeybugger what hurts?
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No items This is a great way to improve factions in Rivervale, there is a downside though and that is that it lowers faction with the Freeport rogues guild, this quest also requires a little bit of cash :) ok, here is what you do, buy 100 bandages, this will cost you 7 plat or there abouts. Then take all 100 to Joogl Honneybugger who is in a hut in the SE corner of the misty thicket. Give him the bandages either 1 at a time or 4 at a time, but dont give him the whole stack of twenty, divide them up into 1's. You will receive once all bandages are given to him one notch of faction adjustment.
Reward for completing this quest?
Raises rogue, mayor, guard, and merchant factions in Rivervale, lowers Freeport rogues guild faction
