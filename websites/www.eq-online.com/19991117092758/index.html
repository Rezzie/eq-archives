Player run event on Erollisi Marr

The Cimmerii congregated inside the Temple that honored Innoruuk. Gathering quietly, each member looked at the others, they were all steadfast in their desire. They were about to commit an unholy ceremony, an ancient rite long since dead, revived by a vision and spread amongst Innoruuk’s most faithful followers. With a shared look the ritual began.

The members of The Semai approached the Altar of Desecration, a large jet-black tome in hand. Ceremoniously, they set the massive book on the Altar and whispered a prayer. This was The Tome of Hate, the Bible of Innoruuk. The midnight runes warded the Temple from those that dared to bring harm. The Ritual would not be disturbed, powerful magiks of a God shielded their work from the eyes of mortals.

Next, the soldiers of The Bloody Hand walked to the Altar. With them they carried the shroud of a freshly slain specter, many of them still wore the wounds of the battle. They placed the robe on the Altar, each saying a prayer and took their positions next to The Semai.

Finally, The Shield made their way to the Altar. They carried the final ingredient of their ritual. The Hate Stone. The Hate Stone was a key, a key to the Plane of Hate, a link between the mortal world and the splendor of Eternal Hate, the Home of Innoruuk. Setting it on the Altar, they said their prayers in turn and took their positions as well. With all the items in place, the Archmage Tzimisce and his Semai brother, Shaile, began the incantations. The members of the Hand and the Shield prayed to Innoruuk, strengthening the bond between them and Innoruuk. A blue flame appeared above the Altar, slowly growing larger and deeper in hue. They knew if they were weak in their Hatred, they would not survive the Ritual. Such is the whim of Innoruuk. Those not pure in their Hatred must, and will, perish. Upon reaching a deep indigo color, the flame burst forth with a black light, a moan drifted from the flame, a sound more horrible than any death imaginable.

It was the tormented scream of a thousand lives, it was the sound of pure Hatred. The Ritual was a success and Innoruuk had blessed The Cimmerii with His Hate. He had sent them The Blackness to help them with their vile and corrupt deeds. The Blackness was a void in the darkness, a pit of incomprehensible horror, a festering pool of evil and hatred. The dark form shifted slightly in the gloom, barely detectable. It opened its gaping maw in a silent scream as its form twisted in the candlelight. A mass of disfigured body parts and captured souls, The Blackness eyed its masters, hungry for their lives. The Hate of the members of The Cimmerii held The Blackness away, for they were Pure in their Hate, and no harm would come to them.

The thoughts of his masters reached the mind of The Blackness; evil deeds, cruel and wicked. Others, yes, he would feed on others. "You know your duty, Go!" the Archmage said.

The apparition shifted slightly and disappeared into the dead of the night seeking its prey and hungry for their souls.

-Laocoon Za'afiel
Knight Templar, Faithbringers

Bugglegupp sighting!!!

Blakwidow, a friend of ours from the Cazic thule server sent me some mail this morning regarding Bugglegupp. You may have heard of this troll if your a gnome, I have, but have never seen it. Here is screenshot proof that the troll exists. Here is the storie that she sent me:

Hi Wraith!
Just a quick mail to let you know what happened to me this morning, I killed Bugglegupp! Here's the storie post it if you like on your website. I was getting ready to leave Akanon this morning to go do some quests for my necro words when I got a tell from a 5th level enchanter named Surreniel. She asked for help. I asked her what was wrong and she replied that she was killed by a troll. A troll in Steamfont? I immediately asked her if it was Bugglegupp but she did not know, and only told me that whatever it was poisoned her and the guards would not kill it.
