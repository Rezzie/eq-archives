High Elves

High Elves are much more intellectual than their Woodland kin, and are the "royalty" of the Elven races. High elves tend to be recluse and do not offer information openly on their society or culture. Seeking to shelter themselves from the human influx of values and customs. High elves are notorious for speaking to one another in their native tongue, excluding commoners from their conversations.

High Elves as all elven races have infravision. High elves are slightly more agile than humans but less strong.

Racial Modifiers

Paladins tend to be common amongst the High elven races but grossly inferior classes such as the warrior or rogue are unheard of in Felwithe. High elves also seem to excel at magic, and often many are quite eager to learn or study it.

Starting Area's

If you play a High Elf your home city will be Felwithe "City of Marble".
