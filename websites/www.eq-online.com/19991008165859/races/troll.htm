Troll

Trolls...8 feet tall, big, ugly, and green. Trolls are an interesting race, able to eat almost anything (and sometimes do). They like to kill things in very unpleasant ways. There socials graces are simple, they have none. There home has stayed unchanged over the many years they have been here. Trolls intellect is low, and some say getting lower everyday. They are a physically powerful race that lacks the intelligence to use it wisely.

Trolls have an innate ability to regenerate lost hit poimts. Trolls also get the Slam ability as part of the race. They also have infravision, like many other races of Norrath.

Racial Modifiers

Trolls are strong, enough said.

Starting Area's

If you pla a Troll your home city will be Grobb.
