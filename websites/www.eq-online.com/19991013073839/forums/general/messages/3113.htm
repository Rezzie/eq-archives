In my experience (pun noted), quests - especiallly the "minor" quests - give you some tiny bit of experience but not much.

I once wondered if they really give you any. I turned in 18 gnoll fangs once and got 18 experience hits. Then I turned in the 18 moonstones; 18 more experience hits. And I saw no movement of my experience bar for those 32 "You gain experience!!" hits.

But with a newer character, I actually leveled up with the experience from a small quest. So ... it is probably tiny, but actually there. Are these quests worth it? Not to me, unless there are also so good cash and item awards, too.
