Spell Abbreviation Descriptions:

AOE Area of effect spell, centering from caster or target.
DD Direct damage refers to the amount of time the spells takes before effect, with Direct damage spells the effect and damage are instantaneous upon the completion of the spell.
DoT Refers to a Spell in which the damage accumulates over time. Some DoT spells effect the target faster than others. DoT type spells are sometimes also refered to as Persistent damage spells.
OD Refers to a spell that can only be cast Out Doors.
EVAC Refers to a spell that can be cast in dangerous situations to EVACuate the group to a safe location.

Misc Descriptions relating to spells:

Lull Refers to "Lulling" a target that might have been previously aggresive towards you. An example would be if you were to die and the monster is standing next to your corpse you could try and Lull it into a non-aggressive state to retreive your corpse.
AC and HP AC refers to Armor Class, and HP refers to Hit Points.
ARGO/Frenzy/Reaction Refers to a monsters "ARGO" range. If a monster is ARGO to you they will attack you once you enter their ARGO range. This range is different slightly for each monster in the game, some have wide ARGO range others not so wide. NPC's also generate an "ARGO list". Which simply means while in a fight they will remember who pissed them off the most. Clearing this list makes the monster forget who pissed it off, as some spell effects do. Reaction is the amount of time you have once entering the monsters ARGO range before they attack.
Root Root type spells render the target unable to move, although they can still cast spells and attack if something is close enough to them.
Buff/Debuff A "Buff" is a spell that increases stats, a Debuff is the opposite.
Upgrade Upgrades are better versions of the previous spell.
