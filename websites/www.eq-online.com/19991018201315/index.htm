Elmo's Image Viewer 5.0

ATTENTION FELLOW INHABITANTS OF NORRATH!!!

I, Drelmo of the Xegony server am on the verge of releasing the latest EQ utility for players everywhere. This program allows you to look at all your EQ screenshots at the click of a button. As if that weren't enough it can look at ANY image on your entire harddrive. And it all requires no effort. Just boot up the program, choose the directory in which your pictures are (you can change this directory at any time just by clicking, no typing involved) and then browse through the list of pictures that appears. It's as easy as pie!

Some of you may have EIB 4.x and use the program quite frequently. I agree whole heartedly that the 4.x series has many many holes in it. That is why I have looked at every detail, every one of those hundreds of e-mails and in-game tells and made 5.0 everything you could possibly want out of an image browser. Some new features in the program include:

- Color Schemes
- Extra applications when in the EQ mode (including Mana Calculator, EQ Spells, and possibly the Weapon Dmg Calculator)
- Scroll bars to look at all those 1024X768 pictures that you get off EQ Vault.
- Image Information displaying the image size in pixels.
- Many many more mini features included...

As Elmo's Image Browser 5.0 nears the ending of BETA, the final code is being put in. Now, the official release date is the 20th of October. That's the coming Wednesday. On that date, you will be able to download the latest program to help enhance your EQ experience. All you'll have to do is go to www.drelmo.net, click on the Elmo's Multimedia picture, and there will be a link to the download. All this available starting the 20th of October.

Drelmo

Harvest Festival of Prexus

Hail and well met, good travellers of Norrath! I am pleased to announce to you Prexus's first Shard-wide event, the HARVEST FESTIVAL! My own guild, the Heralds of Twilight and our allies The Wrath of the Dragonclaw, have banded together to present to you, our friends and future friends, a medium for fun and merriment! Hear and tell tales of times gone by! Gamble at the lotteries! Enact revenge on the griffins! Let the wine flow freely as you participate in our drunken races and duels! Enjoy, laugh, and live to your heart's content!

Do not forget to attend the marriage of friends, our own Meysa and Soeb, at the Druid Circle in West Commons at 1:30pm CST, where all in attendance will ask Erollisi Marr, the Goddess of Love, to bless their marriage eternally. There will, of course, be even more wine and plenty of food for everyone! The Harvest Festival will take place October 17, 1999 from about noon to 8 CST on the Prexus shard. Bring money and items you may want to sell, as well as many of your friends to enjoy the festivities with! The events by zone are as follows:

In East Commons

1. Player-Run Quests for prizes (money & items) Bring your brains, for the key to willing is solving the riddles before you.
2. Language Gatherings and Teachings. Form groups and share your knowledge with one another.
3. An all-out Griffin Revenge Hunt. Remember that flying freak that slew you as a newbie? Show him you haven't forgotten!

In West Commons

1. The binding of Meysa and Soeb in holy matrimony. (AKA Wedding party!) 1:30pm CST at the Druid Circle in WC

In North Ro

1. Bazaar: Buy, sell and trade wares. Bring your orc-ie banks!
2. Drunken Race. Run for your lives while sloshed all to Innoruk!

In West Freeport

1. Duel Tournament, drunken and otherwise. Can you slay the champion?
2. Festival Lottery. Leave your fixed magic dice at home please. =P

In East Freeport

1. Newbie Area ( for gifts and help )
2. Bindings / various buffs
3. Team Relay (spanning many zones **note** this might not be fair because some zoning times are reliant on computer times and connection speed)

If you are interested in helping host or participate in this festival, as either a single person or a guild, please email me at skyfire@tca.net so we can make this festival even better. Thanks!
