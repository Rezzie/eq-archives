Half Elf

Half elves are just that half human and half woodland elf. Looked down upon by the High elves, and somewhat distrusted by the humans, half elves have a hard time fitting into Norrathian Society.

Half elves like their elven kin have infravision, and can see in the dark.

Racial Modifiers

Not as strong as a human, but slightly smarter half elves mixed blood produces some interesting abilities.

Starting Area's

If you play an Half elf,either Freeport, Qeynos, Felwithe, or Kelethin will be your starting point.
