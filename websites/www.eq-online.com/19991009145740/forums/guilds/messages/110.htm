Statement of Purpose:
We of bind ourselves together in friendship and camaraderie for the purpose of
adventure and fun. We stake our claim to the principles of honor and valor. We abide by the law set forth in
the Writ of and honor our leaders with our duty. We purpose ourselves as
helpers and keepers of what is good in Norrath. We shun spoilers of fun. We do not propose war with any
other. We will defend our members and the honor of at all costs. We will uphold
the virtues of the Guild's way in all things.
This is a small section from the 6 page Writ of our guild. We still need 5 people to make it happen so if
interested give me an e mail and we can talk further. The first 10 people who sign up will be reserving for
themselves an officers position and a seat on the Council of 10.
This is a dwarven guild by nature, HOWEVER we are accepting ALL races except Dark elves, Ogres and
Trolls. Even Dwarves allowed Humans and elves to join thier ranks. We hope to hear from you soon.
