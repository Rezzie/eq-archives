The KoT has shown its presence in Major MUD's, Diablo, Ultima Online and numberous other online games. We are now looking to spread our presence to the world of EverQuest.

Looking for players who want to take what is theirs by force and swiftly cut down those rival guilds that try to stand in our way.

This is not a PVP guild out to kill everyone in the world, but one that looks to build a good group of players that want the taste of blood on their lips from a well organized war with other guilds.

None have ever stood in the way of the KoT and we plan on keeping it that way!

Remember it is your world, we just control it!
