Humans

Humans are the youngest race of Norrath. Humans have become the single most dominating faction in the world. Much to the annoyance of the other older races of Norrath like the High elves. The last race to be created by divine action, humans have been working hard to catch up with the rest of Norrathian society. Since the founding of Qeynos by Antonius Bayle, humans have been the driving force behind Norrath.

Humans are not detered by any odds, telling a human he can't do something only makes them try harder, and eventually succeed. A brash and passionate race humans will frequently rush into any situation without puasing to consider the consequences, this may be explained by their unusually short lifespan (compared to elves). Humans have no remarkable advantages or innate abilities, but they do seem to level faster. And one class only available to humans is the Monk class.

Racial Modifiers

Humans are considered normal or average when compared to other races and their stats are pretty well balanced.

Starting Area's

If you play a Dwarf your home city will be either Qeynos, or Freeport.
