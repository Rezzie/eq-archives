Scout Blade
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
5-10 (estimated) Laren Ask about the Scout blade
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No This quest is recieved by the NPC Laren who is in a hut opposite the scout guild hall. Tell him you seek the scout blade and he will give you some background information, finnaly he will give you a useless token, and he will direct you to high pass keep. Make the trip to high keep, and seek out a High elf NPC in the basement, and ask him where Fenn is, (Fenn is the NPC who you give the token to). This will cause Fenn to spawn. You then find out that Fenn is on the roof of the keep, you can reach the roof by going through the green rooms on the top floor and jumping up the balcony. Give fenn the token, and he will instruct you to assasinate Xentil Herkanon, ask about xentil and fenn will disappear, and xentil and his 2 bodyguards will spawn. The bodyguards are much higher level then xentil himself, and you are better off fighting Xentil alone, He can be found in a small room from a large room that has 4 doors in it. Take his head, and there is usually a random 2-5 plat item on him also. bring the head to kelethin, and give it to Laren, Laren will give you the scout blade, and about 25 gold.
Reward for completing this quest?
Scout Blade.
