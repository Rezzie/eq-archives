A wizard had the most powerful offensive spells of the three, but when his/her mana is gone, a wizard is helpless.
A necromancer has problems as an evil being, including being KOS by some guards and being unable to buy in stores.
I chose a magician for these reasons, and the fact that a magician and his pet make their own group. A magician is easy to solo for that reason.
(A magician also uses much of his offensive mana BEFORE battle, creating a pet. Then he can meditate and get it back, going into battle with a powerful tank/pet AND full mana.)
