The most important thing that I like a lot concerning this hedge trimmer is that it really is extremely lightweight and straightforward to make use of. Due to the fact it only weighs about five pounds overall it really is extremely straightforward to maneuver and control around the hedges.

Due to the fact it really is so lightweight my back no longer hurts me to clip the hedges or to trim up around my garden. One more thing I like a lot about the Garden Groom hedge trimmer is that sucks up all the trimmings as I cut.
