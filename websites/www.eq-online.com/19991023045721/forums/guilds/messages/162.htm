New guild forming on Erollisi Marr server. Chief objectives are to establish and control regular trade routes between the cities of Norrath, explore the expert workings of Trade Skills, document Quests that yield rare items, and accept orders for those unusual or hard to acquire items. We will have a web-site. If interested, e-mail me at azrihalzar@aol.com - be sure to include your log-in name, character name/race/class, and a return e-mail address. You can use an existing character, or create a new one on Erollisi Marr. All races and classes welcome. Role-players encouraged to apply.
