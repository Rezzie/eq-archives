We bring the Light where duty lead
By spears and spells, by words and deeds
Where malice dwells and hatred breeds

We gird for war and make our stand
Where tyrants rule with iorn hand
And strife and grief doth choke our land

To fight with power we pledge to thee
Until the hour where are all free
So peace may flower and evil flee

Bring in the host and sound the horn
Call all the pure and noble born
To march upon the morrow's morn

We need thine sword, we need thine tome
To join our cause and guard the loam
That is thine country and thine home

The Legions of Liberty is a new guild in
the middle stages of becoming a "real" guild.
We all are characters of noble blood, and noble race
trying to save our world from evil. We allow any level
character. We are currently located on the island of
Faydwer, as the guild grows we will search out
possibilities of moving to the mainland. If you feel
that thou art up to raise arms against evil, please feel
free to visit our site and join our cause.

-Methusalah-
