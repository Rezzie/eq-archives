Neriak City of the Dark Elves

Neriak is the city of the Dark elves, the undisputed home of evil, Norrath's biggest temple to Innoruuk lies within the walls of Neriak. The dark magics that envelope Neriak seek to choke out all of Norrath, for this is Neriak, Home of the Dark elves, Enter if you dare.

Neriak's 3rd Gate
Neriaks 3rd gate boasts some fabulous shops, and dangerous guilds.

Neriak's Commons
Neriaks commons are also home to quite a few shops and guilds.

Neriak's Foreign Quarter
Neriaks Foreign Quarter has quite a host of other races in it surprisingly enough. You can find Trolls, Halflings, Ogres, Humans, and other various races among the Foreign quarter.
