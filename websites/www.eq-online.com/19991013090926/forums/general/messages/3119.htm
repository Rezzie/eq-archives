: How do u sharpen a weapon with the sharpening stone?

Put both the weapon and the stone in a Forge and hit combine, if your skill is high enough, sometimes the weapon will become tarnished ( it makes your weapon faster by minus 2 attack delay. EXAMPLE: a rusty bastard sword has 42 atk delay, if sharpened ( tarnished ) it becomes 40 atk delay.
