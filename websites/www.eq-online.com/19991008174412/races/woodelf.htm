Wood Elves

Wood Elves, described by other races as "pesky". The wood elves look much like short humans. But their pointed ears set them apart. Wood elves derive their name because they live among the tree's of the forest of the Greater Feydark.

Wood elves as all other elves get infravision, they also have the innate abilitie to hide and forage. And feel a strong kinship to rangers.

Racial Modifiers

Wood Elves are not quite as strong as humans, but are amazingly agile and quick. Their intellect and wisdom is comparable to high man, but they are more charismatic and fair.

Starting Area's

If you play a Wood elf your home city will be Kelethin, Jewel of the Feydark.
