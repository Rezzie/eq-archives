The Tournament of Warriors

The Tournament of Warriors is a tournament being held on the Rallos Zek Server in the South Qeynos Arena on Monday the 6th of September, pacific standard time. All Warrior types (Warrior, Paladin, Shadowknight, and Ranger) at levels 10 and 11 may participate. The first man or woman to defeat the level 11 character, Slaeyn Ansalon, in a non-magical duel-to-the death will recieve a reward of : a suit of Banded-Mail, 2 Fine Steel weapons, various other items ( magical and mundane ), and approxiamately 20 platinum pieces.

The Rules are:

  • If anyone interferes in a match, the tournament will be called off.
  • Lay on Hands and Harm Touch are not allowed during combat.
  • Magical buffs by the character only are allowed before combat.
  • No magic WHATSOEVER is allowed during combat BY ANYONE, if used it will be seen as interference.
  • No looting of Slaeyn's corpse or of him looting anyone else's. If a corpse is looted, it will be seen as interference.
  • Levels 10 and 11 warriors, paladins, shadowknights, and rangers may attend.
  • Only one fight per person, that means if you die and come back, you may not fight again.

There is no entrance fee. The time again is this coming Monday, the 6th of September at 2:00 PM Pacific Time, the tournament will take about an hour to complete. Good luck to all...

Slaeyn Ansalon

Latest patch message

This server side patch contains the following:

1. Rangers now get Critical Hits with Bows.
2. Rogues now get Critical Hits with Thrown Weapons.
3. Parry and Dodge Skill Caps for Rangers have been raised.
4. The DoT bug realted to not getting experience or a corpse has been fixed.
5. New PvP rules: You can no longer loot any weapons that the victim had equipped in his primary, secondary, or ranged weapon slots.

Please note: This patch DOES NOT contain any changes to Necromancer's Pets, OR the Kiting/DoT changes.
