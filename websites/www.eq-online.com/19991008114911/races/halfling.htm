Halfling

Halflings are stocky little people, nearly as strong as a human but incredibly more agile and dexterous. Their obvious physical features include hair about the feet and hands, and an unstopable curiosity. Full of joy and quick to laughter halflings are probably one of the most fun loving races in all of Norrath.

Since halflings need to be good at not being around when one of their pranks goes off, or someone is missing a backpack, they have developed an innate ability to hide.

Racial Modifiers

Halflings, due to there dexterity and agility make excellent rogues, and decent warriors. They also have infravision.

Starting Area's

If you play an Halfling then Rivervale will be your starting point.
