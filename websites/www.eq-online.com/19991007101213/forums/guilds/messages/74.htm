Greetings adventurers well met. As you all know the road
can be a long and hard place in Norrath. Well I am
setting up a guild to make life a little bit easier for
those of us who live on the road. The Companions is a
guild that is dedicatied to the wandering soul and
adventurous spirit. We are neither a good nor evil
guild only one that wishes to expand our power and
experiences through travel and grand battles. This is
open to all of the good races of Norrath.

The Champions are currently accepting membership through
a few avenues. You can email me @ zdogg@psn.net or
stop me in my travels. I am currently in the Kerra
Plains heading to Freeport, give a hearty hail if you
see me.

Zakrul the Traveler
Companion
Erudite Wizard

OOC: I also have a wood elf ranger named Golaes in Kelethin,
who can help any in need, although he is a newbie.
