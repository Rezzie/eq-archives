Barbarians

Born and raised in the rugged Northlands, Barbarians are the a more harsh, warlike version of the human. Taller, stronger, and generally lacking more social graces than the Humans. Barbarians dont care much for Elves, Ogres, or Trolls, but get along well with the other races.

Barbarians have an ability to deliver a powerful Slam to their opponents that can render them stunned. This is an ability of all barbarians, and has nothing to do with the Class.

Racial Modifiers

Strength and stamina are the main attributes of the Barbarian.

Starting Area's

If you play a Barbarian, Halas, The Great Citadel of the North will be your starting point.
