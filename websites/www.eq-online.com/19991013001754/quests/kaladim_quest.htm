Scarab Armor
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
Any level Vacto Molunel Hail (npc name)
Item gained? Details of the quest:
Yes, Scarab armor This is not really a quest, technically, this NPC makes armor (which looks like green plate armor) out of Scarab carapaces. You have to get 2 scarab carapaces and 5 gold for a helm and one pristine carapace and 23 or 33 gold for a Scarab Breastplate. The scarab carapaces can be found on the small worker scarabs in butcherblock and the pristine scarab carapace can be found on Death Beetles in Unrest or possibly on giant scarabs. Or you can try to see if any merchants around butcherblock have these in their inventory. Once you get your faction high enough, he will also create Scarab Boots for you. These take a Cracked Giant Scarab Shell, 2 Scarab Legs, and 17 gold
Reward for completing this quest?
Scarab Armor.

Trumpy Irontoe
Level Needed? Obtained by: Keywords:
Level 7-10 Byzar Bloodforge You get this quest after completing ogre head above
Item gained? Details of the quest:
No Byzar wants you to go to find Trumpy Irontoe he says he doesnt know where he is but wants his head. Trumpy is in the Fish'n Ale in Qeynos, quite a trip, when you kill him and get his head take it back to Byzar and receive the Bloodforge Hammer. The secret to killing Trumpy is you have to get him away from Barstomper. In order to do this you ask him in the bar about Sragg. He then gets all puffy and walks out. If you follow him he will get quite a ways from the bar. You can then safely attack and kill him. Be warned he will run away so a good root spell or snare is a good idea. Also, he is in good with the corrupt guards and they might help him if they see him getting attacked. Do not chase him either he will come back if you let him and enventually he will run back by barstomper and then you have a much bigger fight on your hands. You must have a good faction in order to be able to get the hammer. Trumpy's head is now a no trade/no drop item, so you can't give it to a dwarf to get the hammer, but must raise your own faction to get it.
Reward for completing this quest?
Raises stormguard, kazon stormhammer, merchant, and miners guild 628 factions and lowers Crakneck Warrior faction.
