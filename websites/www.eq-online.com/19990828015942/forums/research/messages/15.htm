A language can be taught only up to your level of proficiency. If you are average at a language, you can bring someone else up to average. Remember that there is a range of skill within average. . .

This does not mean, however, that two people at average in a language can't get to excellent without outside intervention. Here's the trick:

When two people are at the same skill in a language (they have both spammed each other with no improvement), they can both change zones and teach each other a little bit more (1 or 2 points). Then, they can zone again and teach yet a little more. It's time consuming and tedious, but it costs no cash and doesn't require an excellent to get to excellent. In fact, if you can get two folks to Excellent in a needed language (Old Erudin, Dragon, etc.) I am sure you can charge several pp per minute of spam to others. . .)

With that said, I am always looking for people to do this with. If you are interested on Fennin Ro, give me a tell.
