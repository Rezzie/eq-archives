Posted by Zakrul the Travler on April 02, 1999 at 12:42:12:

I am starting up a guild based on the tenants of power and influence. This guild will not be political or religious in nature. This is a guild of
would be travlers and adventurers. If you want to dedicate your life to the road and what keeps you ticking is the sight you'll see on the next
horizon then drop me a line. I travel currently in the Eurid and Qeynos region but will be making rounds to all cities. I am a level 5 wizard. The
laws of the guild would be as such

1. All memebers of the Guild must lend aid to a fellow guild member. Helping or hurting those outside the guild is wholly up to you.

2. Any race/class/religion may join the guild but, we are not aligned to evil or good. We are just travlers trying to see the world and live to tell
of it.

3. The guild will be based in Qeynos with chapter houses in all cities to shelter the travling members.

4. The ultimate goal of all members is to gain personal power and glory in the name of your guild. By doing so you empower the guild.

5. Any attack on a member will be met with swift retaliation so to show that we to not take fools lightly.

6. The name of the Guild shall be Band of the Sore Feet.

As you can tell #6 is a joke. If anybody is interested in starting up a guild with such principles please email me or just reply to this post. The
name and all principles are up for discussion but I would like to stay close to what I sketched here, except for the name that is.
