Dwarves

Dwarves are short, but very strong and dexterous, much more than humans. And you would be hard pressed to find a dwarf without a beard or mustache. Even some of the ladies have beards. Dwarves are a hardy lot, who devote themselves to whatever task is at hand, whether it be battle, drinking, or mining for riches.

As with other underground races Dwarves have developed Infravision which allows them to move more freely in places a human would need light. Pholtric said it best when he said, "Follow the dwarf, he never gets lost!". Dwarves have an almost sixth sense and rarely get lost in underground caverns or caves. Which results in a heightened sense of direction.

Racial Modifiers

Dwarves are strong, but not particularily wise. Their agility is limited by their stature, yet their dexterity is unaffected.

Starting Area's

If you play a Dwarf your home city will be Kaladim, the Great city of the Dwarves.
