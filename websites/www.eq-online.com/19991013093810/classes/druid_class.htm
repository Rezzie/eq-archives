Druid Class:

A Druid is completely at home in the outdoors, and is a friend to all animals. Because of this, no animal will ever attack him unless first attacked by the Druid. They are spellcasters whose focus is all things natural, allowing them to call upon nature for aid. They share some woodland skills with their Ranger brothers.
