Necro Quests from the Temple of Solusek Ro


I was cruising the Internet yesterday trying to find out about the necro quests, and decided to write a guide on how to complete the quests. I have done all of them now, and there is some bad info out there on the Necro only Shadowbound armor quests. Before I go any further let me stress to please READ THIS ENTIRE GUIDE. Every part of it will be important and if you skip parts you may miss something. First of all let me say that the hardest parts of these quests is finding a tailor that can make the shadow silk for you. If you want to attempt it yourself here is the secret to it:
  • Shadow Silk (Used for boots):1 shadow wolf pelt, 1 gather shadows scroll, 1 silk swatch
  • Inky Shadow Silk (Used for gloves):1 shadow wolf pelt, 2 gather shadows scrolls, 1 silk swatch
  • Large Shadow Silk (Used for robe):2 shadow wolf pelts, 1 gather shadows scroll, 1 silk swatch

Harvester

The rest is quite easy. Here is how I did it, I took a look at the components you need, and then bound myself in Freeport, this is a good central point to all the places you need to go. Lets start off with the Harvester. This is pretty easy to get, although I should mention I am level 45, so everything I had to fight was green. You can do this quest two ways. I will explain further but lets see what we need for this quest, here is a list of the things you need:

  • Shadowed Scythe
  • Note (which you get after turning in the shadowed scythe since they are no rent)
  • Fungus Eye from Unrest
  • A shadowed knife from OOT
  • A fire opal

The easiest way to do this would be to get the shadowed scythe first and then take it to Vurgo to get the note. I got the shadowed scythe last, either way is fine I actually went Linkdead on my way to the temple and upon coming back I still had the scythe. But you take the scythe in and get a note, which you use later to get the Harvester. Once you have the note, you will need to get a few more items. My first stop was the Ocean of Tears (OOT). The goblin headmaster spawns on the same island that the oracle is on, best way to get there is to go to the sisterhood island in oot, first stop from freeport and get off the boat. Then point yourself straight out the dock and swim. You will arrive on the Oracle isle. The headmaster is near the back of the island, look for a large stone with 2 shamans, there is a tent near it where the headmaster spawns. He does not always have the dagger.

Next I went to Unrest. The mortuary fungus spawn in the basement of Unrest, a dangerous place to be even for someone my level, luckily a group was down there camping the ghost so it was easy to get the Eye.

I already had a fire opal on me from fighting Seafury cyclops earlier, so I didnt have to worry about that, but you can get these from Genni. Thats all there is to that quest, at the end you want to hand in the Note, Eye, Knife, and Fire opal to get the Harvester.

Shadowbound Gloves

Next I went after the Shadowbound Gloves. Here is what you need for that quest:

  • A skeletal finger
  • A hand of shadow
  • Inky Shadow Silk
  • Sceptre of Sorrow from Lower guk
Now there is something interesting here, I was told that you cannot have the sceptre from Mistmoore (Used in the boots quest) and the Sceptre from Lower guk at the same time. I verified this, it tells you that you cannot have duplicate lore items, BUT, DO NOT substitute one for the other, they ARE NOT the same item, if you use the one from mistmoore in the gloves quest it will fail! And visa-versa. I think the reason you cant have both at the same time is because the description is exactly the same not that they are the same item. And if the description were the same then if you had both how would you know which one to give to Syllina? There are some websites out there that say you can use either, this is NOT true I have tested it.

The skeletal finger comes from the Giant skeletons in the Rathe mountains, they spawn near the gypsie camp there, and also sometimes have bear or lizardman place holders. So you may need to kill some of them to get the skeletons to spawn.

The hand of shadow seems to drop infrequently on the shadow men, I found one after about an hour, this item is expendable, so get it to Syllina right away once you get it, I would suggest getting it last.

The Sceptre of Sorrow is easy to get to, first off you need to know how to get to Dead side of lower guk. If you are doing these quests I assume you do. If not get a map. Once on the dead side go down to the first T and hang a left, go a little ways and you will see a room with 3 froggies in it. And what looks kinda like a dead end, run up to it and you will see the circle staircase and waterfall that Syllina spoke of, run around the stairs and up to the top turn right into an alcove and look under the chair there. You will see the sceptre.

Shadowbound Boots

Then it was time to get the Shadowbound Boots. Here is what you need for this quest:

  • A skeletal toe
  • An Eye of Shadow
  • Sceptre of Tears from Mistmoore
  • Shadow Silk
Got gather shadows? Good use it and head to Mistmoore, go down to the graveyard to the tomb. There you will find a flower that you have to give to the man behind the tomb. It looks like a bag. Give it to him and he gives you the sceptre. As I said I tested this, my first set of boot components were wasted by using the Guk sceptre instead of the mistmoore one.

I got the skeletal toe the same time that I got the Skeletal finger, the same thing drops them.

Eye of Shadow is easy, just kill shadowmen, again :)

At this time I do not have the Robe, when I get it I will update this with my findings.
