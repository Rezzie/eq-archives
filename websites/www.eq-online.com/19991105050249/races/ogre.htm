Ogre

Ogres are without a doubt one of the tallest strongest humaniod races in all of Norrath. Standind nearly ten feet tall and weighing well over 400 pounds. Ogre's are a brutal mishapen race. Ogre's are starting to mimic human society to a point, a definite heirarchy can be found among their home town of Oggok. Most races are cautious around Ogres, especially the smaller races who tend to be used as footstools and appetizers. Ogre's are sensitive to their intellectual short comings and if made fun of prefer beating and pommeling the person making the insult.

Ogre's start with the Bash skill, and they posess infravision.

Racial Modifiers

Due to their size Ogre's are the strongest race on Norrath and have stamina only matched by the troll, their drawback is intelligence.

Starting Area's

If you play an Ogre then Oggok will be your starting point.
