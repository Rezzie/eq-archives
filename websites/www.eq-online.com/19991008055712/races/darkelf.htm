Dark Elves

The Dark Elves are the evil cousins to the Wood and High elves. They are bluish-black in appearance, and have white hair and the same pointed ears as their brethren. Due to the fact they live underground, dark elves posses Infravision and also have the innate ability to hide.

As the nature of the Dark Elf is evil, all the evil classes flourish in the dark elf city. Running a Necromancer or Shadow Knight is much easier in this town, as opposed to others.

Racial Modifiers

Not overly strong, the dark elves posses more intelligence and agility than strength. Which can be apparent if you are unfortunate enough to meet one in battle.

Starting Area's

If you play a dark elf Neriak, City of Hate will be your starting point.
