We of Clan Battlehammer bind ourselves together in friendship and camaraderie for the purpose of adventure and fun. We stake our claim to the principles of honor and valor. We abide by the law set forth in the Writ of Clan Battlehammer and honor our leaders with our duty. We purpose ourselves as helpers and keepers of what is good in Norrath. We shun spoilers of fun. We do not propose war with any other. We will defend our members and the honor of Clan Battlehammer at all costs. We will uphold the virtues of the Guild's way in all things.
