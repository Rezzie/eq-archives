Monk Sites:

  • EverQuest Monks - The original everquest monk site, run by Korzon, who is now semi-retired. Still contains lots of great information, as well as being the home of the most popular monk forum (and the one this site is linked to).
  • Everquest@STOMPED Monks - A great site listing all known equipment for monks in a well done database format. Hope of the "Ultimate Monk" list.
  • EQ Monk Realm - Another monk news/information site.
  • Monk Parser - A log parser specifically engineered for monk testing and evaluation.

General Sites:

  • Allakhazam's Magical Realm - The best hub of Everquest information on the net. Has tons of information, and if he doesn't have it, he's got a link to it. Plus now it finally looks half-decent too.
  • EQVault - One of the oldest everquest news pages. I still read it, although there's alot of useless information wrapping around the useful information. Beware their class guides - very out of date.
  • EQ Stratics - Everquest Strategy and Statistics, another old site. Very similar to EQVault, and once again, beware their out of date guides.
  • EQ'Lizer - Still one of the best spoiler sites on the net. They've got quite a database of items. All my zone guides have links to statistics on EQ'Lizer.
  • Everlore - While the site is mediocre, each zone and quest has a discussion forum. This can really help out with learning a new zone or quests. However, 80% of the information people post is inaccurate, so don't take anything you hear here at face value.
  • Cazic Thule Corner - I only list this site cause Cazic Thule is my server :)
  • EQ Links - A site full of everquest links. They added me, so I felt obligated to add them :)

Class Specific Sites:

  • Shaman's Crucible - A great Shaman site, with some of the most active forums. I visit it every day, and I don't even play a Shaman.
  • EQ Druids - Like the Shaman's Crucible, I visit this site every day, mostly to check the message boards. No, I don't play a druid either.
  • EQ Clerics - Another great class specific site with very active message boards.
  • Casters Realm - The king of class specific sites, especially for enchanters. The news section is also useful because Verant regularly talks to the operators of this site.
