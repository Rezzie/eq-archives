Note: These guides were made by me, mostly for use by our guild. However, when making them, I realized I needed maps to properly pinpoint locations and describe hunting grounds. Unfortunately, I am horrible at map making, so I've taken other people's maps, and marked them myself to display specific locations. These maps are used without permission, but are credited when I could find someone to credit them to. I apologize again for using other people's maps. If asked, I will take down the maps immediately.
