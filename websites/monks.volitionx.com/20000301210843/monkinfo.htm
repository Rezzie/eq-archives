Note: This section is currently being worked on. I plan to include things like quest information, debates (such as the infamous AC vs AGI debate), as well as detailed guides on skills such as Feign Death. If you have any articles or other information you would like to contribute, please send it in, I'll be happy to put it up. There are two types of articles- Guides and Essays. Guides are meant to be factual. Essay's are opinionated, either of the author, or of this site. Some guides may contain some opinions, and some essays may contain some facts. Standard disclaimer. I will not be posting articles on things such as monk petitions and "what new stuff the monk class needs". These things tend to be very fleeting and temporal, so it's not worth putting up here in permanent form.
