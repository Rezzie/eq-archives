Dave & Mike's

Adventures in Cyberspace

Welcome to the page from Dave and Mike, two boys from Chi-caw-go. We're here, we're queer and we've got computer software. Hence, this webpage.

About Mike (since it's his computer): He's 32, a native of the Chicago suburbs and acts in local theatre in such shows as SOUTH PACIFIC, AS YOU LIKE IT, THE MARQUISE and very soon, SCHOOL FOR SCANDAL. He's terrific. He also is the HTML expert.

About Dave (since he wrote Mike's): He's 35, a native of Rush Limbaugh's hometown of Cape Girardeau, Mo., and a 15-year veteran of daily newspapers and trade magazines. He is also the writer and editor of the web page.

Dave loves movies and television as much as Mike loves theatre. As opposite as we are, we seem to make things work, which we have for nearly three years. There's a really romantic "love-at-first-sight" story there somewhere, but you need to get your insulin ready first.

We live in a two-flat in the Ravenswood area of Chicago (northwest of Wrigley Field) with three cats, one beagle, and a straight roommate, Paula, a fellow journalist.
