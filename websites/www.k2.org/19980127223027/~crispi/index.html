    • My name is Crispina Chong, Crispi for short, and I am from Kuala Lumpur, the capital city of Malaysia.

    At this point I am graduating from Iowa State University with a B.Sc. in Journalism and Mass Communications (emphasis in Public Relations). I think my guardian angel who has been watching over me all my life has done her job once again, because yet another wonderful thing has happened to me: I have a job right out of graduation at a prestigious PR agency in Chicago and I will be moving there in a week.

    Some would say I deserved it as I *did* put in a bit of work in getting a job, but as always, things like this strike me as providential. Anyhow, believing that someone's watching over me is a comforting thought, and I hope and plan to make the most out of the opportunity that's been given me.

    Philosophizing aside, I'm excited about the life I'm going to and sad about the life I'll be leaving behind. Well, maybe not *that* sad.. I can't say I'll miss the homework, but I'll definitely miss my friends and the learning environment here.

    Some of the things I can look back on:

  • Arriving in Ames in the middle of the night and taking a cold shower because I wasn't smart enough to turn the knob all the way 'round *shiver*
  • Losing a childhood friend to a silly fight
  • Finding new lifelong friends
  • Waking up to country music every morning *shudder*
  • Getting hooked on the internet and IRC *grin* (yeah yeah I know)
  • Roadtripping to meet some of these IRC people
  • The 40-hour Greyhound bus trip to and from Santa Ana for Christmas '94 and the visit to Chinatown with an IRC friend for a guide
  • Living in a house with my closest friends one summer and breaking my front teeth in a biking accident
  • Learning to ballroom dance (and some salsa, merengue and tango too!)
  • My escapades with men (oy!)
  • Squeezing out a column for the college paper every week of a semester
  • Conquering my stuffy nose under the tutelage of voice teacher Mr. Tener (well, almost)

    And countless other things that one can do in three years at college.. (I'm terrible at conclusions so I'll leave this here for now :oP).
