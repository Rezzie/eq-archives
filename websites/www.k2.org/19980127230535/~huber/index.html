U of I

I studied computer science at the Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. While there, I worked in The University of Illinois Pablo Research Group, doing research on The Parallel Input/Output Problem. We designed an experimental system, PPFS, published a paper, and the project was also my thesis topic.

NIU

I attended Northern Illinois University in DeKalb, studying theoretical computer science, mathematics, and philosophy. I did a little research with Prof. Richard Blecksmith, on a project funded by the National Science Foundation.
