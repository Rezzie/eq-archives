Aliens Abduct Developer

"...and I liked it!"

By Ken U. Digget / Weekly World Woppers

NAPERVILLE, IL -- "I was walking home from the office party, when above me there was a strange whirring sound and flashing lights. Without warning there was a bright flash of light and I lay strapped to a table in a dark sterile room. Little blue people with buck teeth stood over the table, poking and prodding me …and I liked it." So claims Rufus Leaking of Naperville, IL after his reported abduction by visitors from beyond. His story continues that the creatures began conducting experiments on him. Afterwards he was back at home, with what felt like a "cosmic hangover."
