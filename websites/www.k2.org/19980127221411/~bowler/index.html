This Site is dedicated all of the autograph collectors who enjoy receiving celebrity autographs via mail. The thrill of opening an envelope addressed to you knowing that a signature of a celebrity is waiting to be opened. This is the easiest and the least expensive way to enjoy the hobby of autograph collecting.

 

I will be collecting data from my personal collection as well as from all of you...the collectors and organize a list of addresses, those who sign, those who don't, forgeries, autopens etc. I will be updating this page constantly. Any, one who has had recent successes with celebrity signings, please e-mail me bowler@enteract.com with the address. Throughout my years of collecting, I have received hundreds of signed items. These range from 8x10 pictures, post cards and 3x5 index cards. My objective is to provide as much information to anyone about celebrities, his or her signing habits, addresses and length of return.
