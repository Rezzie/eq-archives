How do you put a Counter on your page?
  1. NOTE: since you're on a text-only site, we're sort of assuming that you want a text-type counter, and counters are gifs. So, you put an invisible counter on your page as follows...
  2. Copy this text:

    <img src="/cgi-bin/Count.cgi?sh=F|dd=C|df=example.dat" align=absmiddle>


    and paste it directly into place on your web page.

    Don't know how to do a web page? See how to do a web page, then come back here.

  3. Change the word "example" to your site name (or any word that you want.)

  4. E-mail us here at support@enteract.com with a request to create 'yourcount.dat' file.

  5. Obviously, you now need to upload your new page to the server.

    (Once again, don't know how to do a web page? See how to do a web page )
