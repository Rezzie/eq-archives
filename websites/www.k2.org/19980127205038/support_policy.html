Support Policy

We at EnterAct realize that connecting and staying connected to the Internet can be a daunting task. It is our goal to provide you with a level of service and help that will set our company apart as one of the best. There is no charge for support.

There are many ways to get support at EnterAct:

  1. "The EnterFAQ"
    Go here to find solutions to common connection problems and instructions on setting up various internet applications.

  2. Email
    If you are able to send email, you may send a message to support@enteract.com. Describe your problem as best as you can. This is our preferred method for support. This account is always monitored and many times we check our mail at night after hours. Many simple issues get nearly instant responses.

  3. Newsgroups
    You may also find help and answers in the newsgroups enteract.help and enteract.discussion. Post your question here if you are experiencing a problem you feel may be a common one others will benefit from or know answers to. Both we and more knowledgeable EnterAct users read these newsgroups.

  4. Telephone: (773) 248-8511
    9-10PM Monday-Friday
    9-5PM Saturday
    12-5PM Sunday
    If you reach our voice mailbox, (it is our goal for this not to happen) leave a detailed message. We will get back to you as soon as possible. You can also fax us at (773) 325-9664.

  5. Pager: Phone: 800-SKY-8888 (PIN 1987354), or Web interface *Emergencies only*
    If you notice that the modem pools, DNS, authentication or other servers are down, feel free to page us anytime at the above number. Please keep in mind that the above number is for emergency use only and that normal support issues should be dealt with via one of the other three options listed above. Thanks.
