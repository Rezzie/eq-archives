Integrated Project Management Company, Inc.

Integrated Project Management Company is a full service, professional project management and engineering firm, born out of the corporate manufacturing environment’s need for resources who understand the complexities and cultural elements of American industry. These complexities and cultural elements extend well beyond engineering practices and principles, and include financial control systems, labor relations, safety protocol, production priorities, quality standards, communications, business philosophies and strategies, and other factors which compose the architecture of a modern industrial environment.
