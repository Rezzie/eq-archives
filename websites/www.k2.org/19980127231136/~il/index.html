Our mission is to protect the rights and promote and provide for the welfare of mentally ill persons and their families through research, education advocacy and support. We maintain the following facilities and activities to assist members of our community in coping with the effects of mental illness by means of:

Vocational Training Research
Drop-In Centers Education
Rehabilitation Opportunities Housing
Advocacy Family Support

This web site is intended as a resource to our members with facilities to access it for keeping abreast of our current activities. It is also hopefully a resource to the world community for information, references, and assistance in the resolution to problems with mental illness

 

WE FIRMLY RECOMMEND THAT IN ANY INSTANCE WHERE MENTAL ILLNESS IS EVEN SUSPECTED THAT AN EVALUATION BY A PSYCHIATRIST BE OBTAINED IMMEDIATELY. THE SOONER TREATMENT IS OBTAINED, THE LESS THE CHANCE THAT MENTAL ILLNESS WILL BECOME A LIFETIME PROBLEM.
