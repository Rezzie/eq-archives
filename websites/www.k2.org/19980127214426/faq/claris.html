Setting up Claris Emailer

  1. If you don't have it already, you can download Claris Emailer from Claris.
  2. After you have downloaded and unstuffed the program, you can run it by double clicking the program icon that was created.
  3. Go to the Setup pull-down menu and choose Internet Setup.

  4. You will see the following screen:

  5. The Account Name should be your username, entered in all lower case.
  6. The User Name can remain blank.
  7. Your Email Address takes the form of username@enteract.com.
  8. Your Email Account should be pop.enteract.com.
  9. Enter your password in the Email Password field.
  10. The SMTP host should be smtp.enteract.com.
  11. Click Save.

Checking Mail

  1. To get mail, you will need to go to your Mail/Connect Now menu.

  2. You will see the following screen:

  3. Click Connect to have Claris Emailer retrieve your mail.
  4. When it does, you will see the following screen:

  5. To read a message, simply double click the one you want to read.

Sending Mail

  1. To send mail, go to the Mail/New pull-down menu.
  2. You will see the following screen:

  3. Type the subject of your message in the Subject field located at the top of the screen.
  4. Fill in the Recipient field with the name of the person you wish to email.
  5. Enter the e-mail address of that person in the Address field.
  6. Write your message in the large space provided. When done, click the Send Now button at the top of the screen.
