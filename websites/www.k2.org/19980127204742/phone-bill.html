Ameritech Billing Problem
Incident Report Form
 
Some of you are having problems with Ameritech billing you incorrectly for calls made to our numbers.  If you are having problems then most often you are charged for a Band B or Band C call when it should be a Band A call. Band B and Band C calls are considered toll calls and you are charged per minute, while Band A calls are considered local calls and you are charged $0.05 per call.
 
EnterAct can not refund you for any incorrect charges on your Ameritech phone bill, but we are working with Teleport Communications Group and Metropolitan Fiber Systems, the providers of our telephone lines, to make sure all numbers are being billed in the correct manner.
If you feel you are being billed incorrectly from Ameritech for calls made to one of EnterAct's numbers then we would like to include your information in a formal complaint to Ameritech. A complaint is being filed by TCG and we are providing them with information from our customers in an effort to correct this problem. EnterAct is also working on a seperate letter of complaint to be sent to Ameritech and the appropriate government regulatory agencies on behalf of its customers.

EnterAct can not make any guarantees as far as what results these letters of complaints will have. Please contact Ameritech directly at (800) 244-4444 concerning any charges you feel are incorrect.
