Support Policy


We at EnterAct realize that connecting and staying connected to the Internet can be a daunting task. It is our goal to provide you with a level of service and help that will set our company apart as one of the best. There is no charge for support.

There are three ways to get support at EnterAct:

  • Email
    If you are able to send email, you may send a message to support@enteract.com. Describe your problem as best as you can. This is our prefered method for support. This account is always monitored and many times we check our mail at night afterhours. Many simple issues get instant responses.

  • News groups
    You may also find help and answers in the newsgroups enteract.help and enteract.discussion. Post your question here if you are experiencing a problem you feel may be a common one others will benefit from or know answers to. We read these newsgroups as well as knowlegable EnterAct users.

  • Phone
