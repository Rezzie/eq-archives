EnterAct Newsgroups

We'd like to foster a community spirit here at EnterAct. To help with that goal, we have created some local newsgroups where people can enter discussions, ask for help, give advice, or post announcements.

Our support staff reads these newsgroups too, so if you have a question for us that you feel might pertain to other members of EnterAct, ask here, and the responses will be seen by all.

You can read these newsgroups either through your web browser by following the links below, or you can use your favorite newsreader to access them.

Please note that these newsgroups are local to the news.enteract.com news server and so (unlike most other newsgroups, which have global scope) will not appear on any other news server besides news.enteract.com. (In other words, if the newsgroups aren't appearing, you probably have your web browser's news server configured to be something other than news.enteract.com).

Our newsgroups:

  • enteract.completeact Discussions specific to CompleteAct accounts.
  • enteract.discussion General chat among EnterAct customers and staff.
  • enteract.help The place to come for help and support with your account.
  • enteract.linux EnterAct LINUX users unite!
  • enteract.mac Help with your Mac.
  • enteract.news EnterAct system status and announcements.
  • enteract.test Did you get that news reader configured right?
  • enteract.windows Where you want to go today when Windows won't.
  • enteract.www The HTML/WWW/CGI/Netscape/homepage discussion center.

You can find more information on USENET, including a USENET newsgroup browser at the USENET Info Center Launch Pad (we carry many more newsgroups than are listed in their browser, but it is still a useful navigation tool).
