Uni-Label & Tag, Inc. is a full service, flexographic manufacturer of pressure-sensitive labels for a wide spectrum of end uses with customers in the U.S. From design through manufacturing, we offer total label and packaging-insert production, including complete electronic prepress services from concept initiation and implementation through platemaking.

Company Profile

Uni-Label & Tag, Inc. has been providing pressure-sensitive labels to a wide range of industries for more than 21 years. We serve a wide range of industries with an emphasis on the pharmaceutical and product identification industries. In the product identification area, current end uses range from smoke-alarms to sports helmets to golf-clubs. Almost any product that needs a retail or industrial identifying label is a candidate for Uni-Label’s service.

Turnaround time is an important consideration today, and we’ve stressed this concern throughout our operation. For example, our prepress system currently uses a state-of-the-art, around-the-clock Bulletin Board System and high-speed modem to move clients files easily and quickly. In the manufacturing area, our personnel have been trained to do everything possible to accommodate customer’s special needs whether it be a unique label construction or a super-rush job. Complete GMP’s and SOP’s are on file for each of our departments.
