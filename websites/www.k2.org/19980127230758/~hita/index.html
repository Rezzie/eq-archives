HERITAGE INTERNATIONAL TRADE ASSOCIATION


HITA is a not-for-profit, professional membership organization designed to offer to it's members, their business associates, students, entrepreneurs, small businesses, and others interested or involved in international business, the following services:

- Experience-Based Resources essential to successfully planning and conducting business in overseas markets;

- Networking Access to leading decision-makers and business executives operating successfully in the international business community;

- Management Assistance toward the successful completion of foreign market research, strategic planning, trade negotiations and international finance and investments.
