Checking Mail

To check your mail, simply double click the Inbox icon. You will be prompted for your password before any mail is downloaded to your machine.

As soon as you enter the correct password, your mail should automatically start to appear in Microsoft Exchange. To read a message, simply double click it.

Sending Mail

To send mail in Microsoft Exchange, click the Compose pull-down menu and select New Message.

You can then fill in the recipient's e-mail address as well as the subject of your message. Type the body of your message in the space provided.

When done, click on the File pull-down menu and select Send.

If you wrote your message off-line and have it queued to be sent at a later time, you will need to manually tell Exchange to send the message. Do this by going to the Tools pull-down menu and selecting Deliver Now.
