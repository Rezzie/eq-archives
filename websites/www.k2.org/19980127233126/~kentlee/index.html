My Favorite Links

  • NBA.com - The official site of the National Basketball Association.
  • The Chicago Bulls Home Page - The official site of the world champions. Get up to date stats of the team and individual players, current standings, feature stories and much more.
  • The Chicago Tribune - The online version of the Chicago newspaper. Get current international, national and local news.
  • Immanuel Baptist Church's Home Page - My church's presence on the web. Check the site out for information about Sunday services, directions to the church, and information about weekly fellowships and events.
  • Wheaton College - Wheaton is a Christian liberal arts college located in the western suburbs of Chicago. It is my alma mater.
  • Mapquest.com - This is an amazing site that gives you detailed directions and maps from and to anywhere.
  • The Daily.Wav - This a great source for all sorts of sounds in wav and midi formats.
  • ESPN Sportszone - Fantastic site to get the latest news and stats for sports.
  • Yahoo! - One of the earliest and still one of the best search engines around. Now, they also offer free web based e-mail. Check it out at http://mail.yahoo.com.
