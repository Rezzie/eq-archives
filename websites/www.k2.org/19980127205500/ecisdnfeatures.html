There is no need to order a new circuit - it's that simple!

STAC compression support
EnterAct's network allows for STAC compression at no extra charge. With the proper hardware, this means data transfers of compressed data - most commonly files - can reach transfer speeds of up to 2 ½ times that of the allocated bandwidth for your line ( 64Kbps or 128Kbps).
Home office option
This slightly less expensive option allows you to have a dedicated ISDN connection over a standard dial-up ISDN line. EnterAct will "peg-up" our end of your dial-up line, effectively giving you a 24 x 7 dedicated connection. An 8 IP subnet is standard w ith larger subnets available at additional costs. This option is ideal for residential line customers wishing to have a dedicated ISDN connection.
