Comet
  1. Download Comet.
  2. Your Mac should automatically unstuff Comet if you have an unstuffing program installed on your hard disk.
  3. Run Comet by double clicking the application.
  4. You will see the following screen:

  5. Type EnterAct in the Configure Session field.
  6. The Connection Type should be MacTCP Telnet.
  7. Click DEC VT100 for the Terminal Type.
  8. The Host Address should be shell.enteract.com.
  9. Click OK.
  10. At this point, the connection will be made to EnterAct and you will see the following screen:

  11. Type your username when you see the Login: prompt.
  12. Type your password when you see the Password: prompt.
  13. At this point you'll see the UNIX prompt. Feel free to explore your account using telnet!
