Free Agent

  1. If you don't have it already, download Free Agent from www.forteinc.com/getfa/download.htm.
  2. Put the executable file into its own directory on your hard drive and run it.
  3. You will need to configure the program. If during setup Free Agent doesn't automatically bring up your General Preferences, go to the Options/General Preferences pull down menu from the main screen.

  4. You will see the following screen:

  5. In the User tab, enter your e-mail address (username@enteract.com) and your full name. You can leave Organization blank, but you will want to fill in the Reply To field with your e-mail address (username@enteract.com).
  6. Click the System tab. You will see the following screen:

  7. Enter news.enteract.com as the News Server and smtp.enteract.com as the Email Server.
  8. The rest of the settings are optional. Feel free to change any or all of them. When finished, click OK. NOTE: You can go back to these settings at any time by going to the Options/General Preferences pull-down menu.
  9. Now you are ready to begin downloading the newsgroups. Click on the Online/Online pull down menu (or just hit control-o) to connect to the news server.
  10. After the 17,000+ news groups are finished downloading, you will be able to start subscribing to the groups that interest you.
  11. To quickly search through the groups for a certain topic, let's say music for example, you would go to Edit/Find and enter "music" (no quotes) in the field provided.

  12. Click Find First or Find Next until you have found the group you are looking for.

  13. To subscribe to a given group, simply double click the group and choose the Subscribe to Group button when prompted. Keep doing this until you've subscribed to all of the groups you are interested in.
  14. To view only the subscribed groups (instead of all 17,000+ groups), go to the Group/Show pull down menu and select Subscribed Groups.

  15. To read the articles in a group, simply double click the group you want to go into and then double click the article you want to read.

  16. That should get you started. For more information about Free Agent, check out their on-line Help menu from the main screen.
