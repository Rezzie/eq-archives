Internet Mail

Setting up Internet Mail
  1. Click on the Internet Mail icon in your Microsoft Internet Explorer program group. You will see the following screen:

  2. Go to the Mail menu and click Options. When the options window appears, click on the Server tab. You will see the following screen:

  3. Look to the Servers area. The Outgoing Mail (SMTP) server should be set to smtp.enteract.com, and the Incoming Mail(POP3) server should be set to pop.enteract.com. The POP3 Account is your username in lower case, and the Password is the same as your login password.
  4. In the fields at the top of the window, you may enter whatever you wish for the Name field--be it John Doe or Xena, Warrior Princess--depending on what you want to appear on the email you send. The same can be said for the < font color=#bb0000>Organization field; if you have a company name you wish to appear on your outgoing mail, enter it here. For Email Address enter your full e-mail address in the form username@enteract .com.
  5. Click OK and you're all set!

Sending Mail
  1. To send e-mail, click on the New Message icon in the upper-left corner of the Internet Mail window. You will see the following screen:

  2. Enter the e-mail address of the intended recipient in the To: field and give your message a title in the Subject: field. Then, type the body of your message in the space provided.
  3. When you are ready to send your message, click the Send button.

Checking Mail

When you first start Internet Mail, it will automatically connect and check for new mail. Otherwise, click the Send and Recieve button.
