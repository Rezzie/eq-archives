    EnterAct offers a full range of connectivity options. Here are our standard account types. If your needs are different, call us and we will give you a quote.

      * Direct full time Internet connectivity

      * Domain Name Registration

      * NetNews NNTP Access (NNTP News feed T1 only)

      * Services such as Web servers and FTP may be run on your site

Requirements

    Dedicated 28.8

      * Modem

      * Router capable of routing packets via either SLIP or PPP. There are lots of choices here, contact us for suggestions

      * Telephone line


    ISDN services

      * ISDN router or ISDN "modem"

      * The "modem" option requires a Router as above

    56kbps service

      * CSU/DSU capable of 56kbps

      * Router capable of synchronous PPP or HDLC

      * DSO circuit operating at 56kbps

    T1 services

      * CSU/DSU capable of T1 speeds

      * T1 capable router

      * T1 circuit
