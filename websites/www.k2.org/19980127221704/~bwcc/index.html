"A Church Without Walls"

Our church offices are at 373 S. Schmale Road, Suite 201, Carol Stream IL 60188. 630-668-9900
Bridgeway Community Church meets at the Outreach Community Center at 345 South President in Carol Stream on Sunday mornings at 10:00am. Just one block south of St.Charles Road between Schmale and Bloomingdale Roads. See map for directions.

"Love must be sincere. Hate what is evil; cling to what is good. Be devoted to one another in brotherly love. Honor one another above yourself" Rom 12:9-10

Bridgeway Community Church is an independent congregation of Bible-believing Christians from many different backgrounds. We have no membership in a formal denomination, but welcome all who seek a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ. Please take a few moments and let us give you an overview of Bridgeway.

We are enjoying a series on "From the Cradle to the Cross!". Please join us for this series which began on Dec 7th.
