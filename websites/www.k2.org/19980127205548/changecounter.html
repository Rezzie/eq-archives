Change the Count on a Web Page Counter



EnterAct subscribers can change the count on their already-created web counters from this web page. You must know your username, current password, and counter name to use this service. Your username is the name you input when logging onto the system. It's equivalent to the first part of your email address... i.e., yourname in "yourname@enteract.com".
