Edith Frost, a 32-year-old* refugee from an underground country-music scene in New York** who is now based in Chicago, has made a debut album that's built to last. More to the point, it's almost designed for a long wait before its discovery, with its close-to-the-vest inward guilelessness, its austerity and desolate emotionalism."
- Ben Ratliff, New York Times, June 15, 1997

** I'm really from Texas






Welcome...

Hey everybody! Thanks for visiting my site. Whether you're here for the first time or the fourteenth, I hope you find something that's useful to you, or at least slightly entertaining. I do this whole site all by myself (well, except for some of the photos and scripting and whatnot), so if you have any complaints or comments you'll have to direct them to me personally.

I like to play around with this place, it's sorta like my virtual house... there's a lot of stuff piled all over and some of the content is getting really old, so watch out for dust-bunnies!

(By the way, if you're stuck in somebody else's frame and can't view my site properly, just click here to break out.)
