Tapestries:

Welcome to Khadir Imports. This is a family run business which concentrates on selling imported tapestries to the public at wholesale prices. All tapestries are hand made in India and constructed with the finest quality of silk and cotton.

These tapestries can be used as:

  • Bed Covers
  • Table Covers
  • Sofa Throws
  • Wall Hangings
  • Rugs

Each tapestry is made of 70% silk and 30% cotton. Limited Supply.

Questions & Ordering:

If you have any questions or to order our products, please e-mail us at khadir@enteract.com

Our ordering process is simple. You first email us with the size, color number and name, quantity, and your e-mail address. We reply to your email with our mailing address and telephone number and the total price of your order. Then you mail us a check and an address where you want the products delivered. After receiving your check we ship you the products through UPS. Allow 3 to 4 weeks for delivery. We do not accept cash or credit cards.
