This is my home page. Everybody on the MORTAL KOMBAT team kept buggin' me to put one up... so here it is.


    I thought I'd go with a limited "TERMINATOR 2" theme for now, but later I want to put up images that would give an idea of how the T2 video game was created.

    Yes... I worked on the "TERMINATOR 2" arcade game. I've been an artist/designer at Williams/Bally/Midway for over seven years now. I've worked on "STRIKEFORCE", "TERMINATOR 2", "REVOLUTION-X", and all three "MORTAL KOMBAT" games.

    The images on this page are actual images from the game although they may not look as nice because the colors were reduced. The 'bullets' at the beginning of each line are the icons you can pick up during the game. The line drawings are the 'heads-up' display that appear when you pick up the power-up icons. The 'heads-up' display would show the player what it was he/she picked up, where it went, and what it did. The red and blue border is from the rack-up screen.
