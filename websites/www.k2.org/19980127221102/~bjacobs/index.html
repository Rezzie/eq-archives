Welcome to the Great River Rumble

Summary: Welcome! The Great River Rumble is an annual canoe trip down the Mississippi River, approximately the last week of July. Participants travel by canoe or kayak. The trip is about 130 miles long over seven days of paddling. We paddle by day and camp in tents by night. Gear is transported by truck from campsite to campsite. Meals are in local restaurants or catered. Those paddlers who cannot paddle the entire trip are welcome to join in for even just one of these days.
