Welcome to the Therapist List Directory

Our directory is designed to assist consumers of mental health services in finding a psychotherapist to meet their needs. This directory contains information reported by therapists throughout the United States and Canada. All information in this directory is self-reported by the therapist.

Because we are not a referral service, we recommend that you verify all information about the therapist you choose before beginning treatment.

In addition, we have provided you with a number of questions you may wish to ask the therapist in order to make an informed choice.

You may choose to begin a search immediately or learn more about the Therapist-list Directory, including how to target your geographical location most effectively and how to narrow your search. You will also see a sample file.

If you would like to be listed in this directory, please email TLIST@enteract.com for more information.
