Company Profile

HazChem Environmental Corporation is a full service environmental consultant with offices in the western suburbs of Chicago, Illinois. HazChem specializes in providing cost effective, pro-industry, solutions for common environmental concerns ranging from waste disposal to site remediation.

We have achieved our reputation as a valued environmental partner because of our unique approach to solving environmental problems. Unlike many consultants HazChem seeks the most appropriate business solution to a problem - we don't turn your problem into a science project or baffle you with regulations. Our success is founded on providing straightforward, no nonsense, solutions which exceed our clients expectations.

HazChem is staffed by hands on professional environmental personnel. All customer representatives are qualified project managers and perform all aspects of their own projects. You can rest assured that your representative has full knowledge of your project, and will be the only contact you will need through completion.
