The Leader in porosity sealing services since 1950, Casting Impregnators continues to meet the challenges into the next century.  Through total commitment to quality and service, CI brings together responsiveness and experience in solving the most demanding porosity problems.
 
Direct involvement with our customers helps CI understand their specific need and requirements.  Whether we are working with design engineers on a difficult problem, or assisting a quality control department in maintaining demanding sealing requirements, Casting Impregnators makes it work.
 
When orders are received, immediate attention to detail begins.  A computerized order tracking and control system, unique to the impregnation industry, provides the most consistent, reliable results.  Precise process controls combined with the use of advanced quality sealants result in achieving our goal of customer satisfaction. 
 
Casting Impregnators is a Loctite Corporation  authorized application process center.  CI uses the highest quality products from the Loctite Corporation, the world leader in impregnation sealants.  Loctite Corporation's Process Engineering Group teams with the Sales and Engineering Staff at CI to support our customers with their most demanding applications.
