NCSA Telnet
  1. Download NCSA Telnet.
  2. Open NCSA Telnet by double clicking on the program icon associated with it.
  3. Go to File/Open Connection.

  4. You will see the following screen:

  5. Enter the host to which you want to connect. If you wish to connect to EnterAct, simply type shell.enteract.com in the Name field.
  6. Type anything you like for the Window Name.
  7. Click Connect and you will see the following screen:

  8. Enter your login and password when prompted and you will be left with your UNIX prompt. Feel free to explore your account using telnet!
