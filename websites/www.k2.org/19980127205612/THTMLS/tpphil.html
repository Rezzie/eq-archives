Our Pricing Philosophy




Pricing

    Why do we charge the way we do?

    It's what we've wanted when we were Internet customers.

    If you're surfing heavily its nice to know there won't be a whopper of a bill at the end of the month (between $21 and $30 maximum in our case, depending on how you pay). Other months, when you barely check your E-Mail, its painful to pay a high 'flat rate' at the end of the month. Not at EnterAct!

    We feel as though a lot ISPs depend on users signing up for more connectivity than they need, then not using it.

    We don't think that's right.

    Whether you're moving, changing jobs, or just paying too much attention to your "real life," our $5 option gives you maximum continuity at the lowest possible price.

    EnterAct pledges to NEVER charge a customer bandwidth or storage charges without first contacting the customer and giving them the option of redesigning/ reducing a web page. This means you can rest assured that you will be charged the lowest possible rates for the resources used, and that you will never be surprised by a larger than expected bill.

Advance Payment

    We have one of the most generous payment plans possible when it comes to advance payment. Pay 3 months in advance and we'll give you 20% off, pay a year in advance and we'll give you 30% off. Cancel at any time and we'll give you a prompt, cheerful refund for the remaining portion of your subscription (pro-rated to the day you leave!). This means that for as little as $21 a month, you can get one of Chicagoland's most full-featured internet accounts with unlimited access!

    So you've pre-paid for 30 hours a month -- what if you don't use them all? You receive a credit in your next billing period for all those unused hours! We think that's simple and fair.

    Why would we do this? Are we crazy? Because most of our costs in servicing you are incurred up front. We have our excellent tech support, high-speed rack modems, and other significant initial investments. Our architecture scales up easily and relatively cheaply to higher and higher levels of connectivity.

    By paying in advance, you match our costs far better, and enable us to give you significant discounts.
