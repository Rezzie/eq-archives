Animation Station, Ltd. is a video graphics design studio located in Greektown, Chicago.


We specialize in affordable 3D and 2D animation. Full-color paint, text, color cycling, texture mapping, flying cutouts, rotoscoping, wipe transitions, and other special effects rendered/recorded to 3/4" or Betacam formats.
