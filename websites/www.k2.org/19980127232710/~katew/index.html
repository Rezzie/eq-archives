Welcome to the Homepage of the Saint.

CHICAGO -- January 1, 1998

Every once in a while, it is time for something completely different. This is the result -- a new look for the next dozen months or so.

This site is a Third Generation website -- the kind of page where information is grouped into different areas, with a unifying theme. The benefit of such pages is that the streamlined organization style allows the visitor to just explore those areas which interest her. Entrance and exit tunnels create anticipation and closure as the visitor enters, experiences, and exits the web site.

From this "front-door" you can choose to get information on the internet and web design, or you can choose to visit my personal or professional areas of interest.
