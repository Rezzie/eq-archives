The Christmas after my wife and I announced our engagement, her brother presented me with a gift which--more than any other gift could--initiated me into the family. He bought me homebrew supplies. Since then, I've been experimenting regularly and will post my most successful recipes here.
 
Gran Crue  |  Dark Wheat  |  Christmas Ale
 

    My first finished beer is a Gran Crue.  It turned out far better than I'd dared to hope.

Jrisele Jrang Crue
18.08.1997
jebottelt 02.09.97
 
            1/2 lb Belgian Grain Wheat

            1.) 60 Minuten Wheat mit !,5 gal. Wasser kochen
            2.) 1/2 lb extra light malt extract
                 4 lb gold syrup malt extract
                 2 lb honig
                1 oz Hallertauer Hopfen (boiling)
                1 oz frische Orangen Schale
                1,5 oz zerkleinerte Koreander Samen
                nochmal 25 Minuten kochen lassen
            3.) 1/3 oz Hallertauer Hopfen (Vorgeschmack)
                 weitere 33 Minuten kochen
            4.) 1/2 oz Hallertauer Hopfen (Nachgeschmack)
                 weitere 2 Minuten (insgesamt also 60 Minuten) kochen lassen
            5.) abkuehlen lassen auf 75 Grad F und dann Hefe dazugeben (Belgian White Beer yeast)
             

            1051, 7.5% vol. alc., 13.51”
            1006, 2% vol. alc.,
