Send an Emergency Page


We strive to keep our systems running seamlessly throughout the day, and we have systems in place to report problems automatically via page to our system administration staff, but should you discover a downed service (modem pools, DNS, authentication, mail, news, or web server) you can use this web page to report the problem to our emergency pager.

A different staff member at EnterAct is assigned to pager duty each night. Each staff member assigned to pager duty has a computer at home which they can use to look into the problem you are reporting. They can also coordinate contacting our system administration staff.

You can use this form to send a text message to our emergency pager. For other means of contacting us, please see Contacting EnterAct. For our office hours and all of our avenues of assistance, please see our Support Policy page.

To send an emergency page, you will need to authenticate yourself with your username and password. Your username is the name you input when logging onto the system. It's equivalent to the first part of your email address... i.e., username in "username@enteract.com".

Please also provide a brief message indicating the problem and, if you would like a call back, a phone number at which you can be reached.
