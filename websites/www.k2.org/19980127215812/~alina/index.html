For those of you who want to russify Solaris: I have koi8 encoded cyrillic fonts and Win cp-1252 encoded cyrillic fonts, both in BDF format. It is probably easy to find koi8 fonts for UNIX on the Internet but I haven't seen any win cp-1252 fonts. I also have instructions on how to install them on your system. They can also be used for Sun OS earlier versions but they need to be converted to appropriate format which is easy to do with BDF format.
