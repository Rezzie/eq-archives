John and Patty Coghlan
at Duneside on Kiawah Island

3 Bedroom / 3 Bath / Sleeps 6-8


Right on the BEACH at Kiawah Island, South Carolina.

This is a beautifully furnished beachfront property with a spectacular view! Located at Duneside villas, the westernmost villas on the beach. It is air-conditioned, with ceiling fans in the bedrooms; has a full kitchen with dishwasher, microwave, ice maker, and coffeemaker. We also have cable TV, VCR, and a built in stereo/cassette deck in the living room. The master bedroom also has a TV. Just off the kitchen is a laundry area with washer/dryer. The second floor deck and the first floor enclosed porch have a wonderful view of the beach!

You've probably already been to Kiawah if you're reading this. Hiking, biking, swimming and being outdoors are some of the things you can do if you don't golf or play tennis. We are walking distance from the Kiawah Island Inn, the West Beach Tennis Center, and the newly reopened Cougar Point Golf Course. All of the golf courses, tennis courts, Night Heron Park, etc., are reachable from the shuttle stop at the Inn. It's a short walk to the Kiawah River, a good place to see dolphins swimming!




Rental Information: Rates are competitively priced, but the real attractions are the picturesque setting and the tastefully decorated, comfortable townhome.

During the Summer, we rent Saturday to Saturday ONLY! During the Off Season - Daily rates are available.
