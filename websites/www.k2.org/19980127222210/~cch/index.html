Chicago Coalition for the Homeless (CCH)

This page is under heavy construction. Take a look, but revisit soon and see the new site.

CCH has over 7000 members, including activists, shelter providers, community organizations, housing advocates, homeless persons, religious persons, academicians, public servants, and interested individuals. We engage in campaigns to prevent homelessness and to meet the needs of our city's homeless population. We are dedicated to addressing both the emergency needs of homeless persons and the primary causes of the problem, such as lack of affordable housing and jobs that pay a living wage.

Della Mitchell, Lead Organizer for CCH, is attending Habitat II : United Nations Conference on Human Settlements -- Istanbul, June 3-14, 1996. CCH welcomes attendees to the Conference.
