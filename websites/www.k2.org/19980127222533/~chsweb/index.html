Code, Hennessy & Simmons, LLC. ("CHS") is a Chicago-based private investment firm. Founded in 1988, CHS manages a portfolio of equity investments in middle market companies which design, manufacture and distribute a broad array of consumer and industrial products. CHS manages $588 million of committed capital on behalf of a group of limited partners which includes private and public employee pension funds, corporations, commercial banks, money management firms, insurance companies and high net worth individuals.

As a strategic equity partner, CHS works closely with business owners and management teams to develop business plans, arrange financing and structure investments to close transactions quickly. After making an investment, CHS provides its portfolio companies with strategic resources, management support and additional capital to build long term shareholder value. Investments made by CHS are managed by the firm’s Partners, Andrew W. Code, Thomas J. Formolo, Peter M. Gotsch, Daniel J. Hennessy, Brian P. Simmons and Jon S. Vesely.

Today, CHS manages controlling equity investments in a diverse portfolio of 20 middle market companies with combined revenues of $1.8 billion.
