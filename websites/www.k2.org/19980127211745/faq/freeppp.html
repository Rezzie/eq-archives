FreePPP 2.5 Setup
  1. Open the FreePPP Folder on your hard disk.
  2. Double click the FreePPP Setup icon.
  3. You will see the following screen:

  4. Click the Modem Setup button found at the bottom of the window.
  5. You will see the following screen:

  6. Make sure you are connected to the Modem Port.
  7. Click OK.
  8. Click the Accounts tab at the top of the window.
  9. You will see the following screen:

  10. If you don't already have an EnterAct account, please click New.
  11. If you do already have an EnterAct account, please click Edit
  12. You will see the following screen:

  13. Enter the phone number in the appropriate field.
  14. You should choose to connect Directly.
  15. Enter your User name and Password where asked. Remember that usernames are always lowercase. Passwords are case sensitive.
  16. Click the Connection tab.
  17. You will see the following screen:

  18. Choose your Port Speed. This should be 57600 for 28.8 modems and 38400 for 14.4 modems.
  19. The Flow Control should be set to CTS & RTS (DTR).
  20. Check your modem manual for the correct modem initialization string. ATZ, AT&F, or AT&F1 are good default settings if you are unsure of the correct string for your modem.
  21. Click the Options tab.
  22. You will see the following screen:

  23. You need not change anything in this window.
  24. Click OK.
  25. You will be brought back to the main FreePPP Setup window. To connect from here, just click the Connect button. FreePPP will dial your modem and connect you to EnterAct. Good luck!
