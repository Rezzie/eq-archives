Welcome to Berwyn, Illinois, an 89-year-old city located just eight miles west of Chicago's Loop. This presentation has been prepared by the Berwyn Homeowners Association to help you learn more about our distinctive and diverse community. We've included information about Berwyn's history, government, schools, business and commerce, and churches. We hope you enjoy your visit to our "City of Homes."
