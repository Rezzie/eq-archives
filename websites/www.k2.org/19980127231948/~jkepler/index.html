Kepler Associates was founded in 1976 to provide writing and editorial assistance to Chicago publishers and other organizations. Now a full-service communications consulting firm, Kepler Associates has developed a list of clients that reads like a who's who of names in health care, consumer product manufacturing and distribution, customer service, marketing/sales, publishing, and education.

Books, manuals, periodicals, collaterals
In-touch correspondence, newsletters, promotions
Group and one-on-one presentations, training, coaching

Kepler Associates' principals are James Kepler, Ann Kepler, and Thomas Kepler. Their work includes planning and consulting, project management, writing, graphic design, production, and packaging for a wide variety of print-related and electronically produced products.

A personal invitation from Kepler Associates: Please take a few moments to browse through the list of clients we've served. Check our credentials. View samples of our work, including selected books and articles. See some of our awards and recognitions. Read testimonials from a few of our clients.

Let us hear from you. We'll respond right away by phone or e-mail to arrange a time to discuss how Kepler Associates can provide solutions for your communications needs.
