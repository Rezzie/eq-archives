Netscape News

  1. To configure Netscape News, begin by going to the Options/Mail and News Preferences pull-down menu.

  2. You will see the following screen:

  3. Make sure that the News [NNTP] Server is set to news.enteract.com.
  4. You will also want to fill in the Outgoing Mail [SMTP] Server (smtp.enteract.com) so that you can send replies to articles posted in the news groups.
  5. Click OK when done.
  6. To get to Netscape's news reader, go to the Window pull-down menu and select Netscape News. This will bring up the following window:

  7. If you don't see all the newsgroups show up in the upper right hand window, go to Options/Show All Newsgroups and they will appear.

  8. To subscribe to a group, simply put a checkmark in the box that appears to the right of the newsgroup you are interested in. To do this, just click in the box. You will see a yellow checkmark appear.

  9. To read an article, just click the article you wish to read and it will appear in the bottom half of your window.
  10. That should get you started. For more information about Netscape News, check out their on-line Help menu from the main screen.
