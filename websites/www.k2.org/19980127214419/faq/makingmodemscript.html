Making your own Script

This is recommended for Advanced uses only.

  1. Before you start make sure that your modem manufacture doesn't already have a modem script posted already.
  2. Download Apple Modem Script Generator.
  3. Get your Modem's Manual.
  4. Read the Acrobat File that comes with the Script Generator. It will help you with some of the fields and what they mean/do.
  5. After you get the Modem Scrip Generator and you start it you will see the following.



  6. Now just fill in the blanks using your Modem manual for answers.
  7. All the fields should be filled in for best results.
  8. After you have all your fields in just click on "Create"
  9. You may not create a perfect one the first time. We recommend either recreating a new script by starting over or modifying it using BBedit To Download.

Good Luck and Happy Surfing
