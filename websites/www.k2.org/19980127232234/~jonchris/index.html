The Celtic Knot Forge
    Run by friends of ours, Franklyn and Karen Garland out of their Brookfield, IL home as well as their shop at the Bristol Renaissance Faire during the summer, the Celtic Knot produces some of the finest metal crafts anywhere.  From hand hewn nails and sweet sounding triangles to coffee tables and curtain rods, the "Knot" is where Old World craftsmanship still lives!  Frank is also my major consultant on this web page as well as the one for the First United Methodist Church - Lansing, IL.  

The Dilbert Zone
    The web home of our favorite cartoon strip character.  Here you can find current as well as archived cartoons from Dilbert.  Along with Dilbert, you can jump around the rest of United Media's other pages devoted to the rest of the cartoons that they distribute.

Screensaver.com
We think the name says it all.  Look them up for some of the coolest screen savers around, many of them for free!!!  You can find everything from homegrown examples to some very slick ones produced by some major manufactures such as Guinness Brewing and Nissan.  Definitely a must see.

Kenland Cattery
    We had to include this link.  Kenland is run by a good friend of ours, Mary McCauley, DVM.  She is the lady that bred our pride and joy, Jonah.  She is a good source for pictures and information about Maine Coon cats as well as one of the best veterinarians we have ever had the pleasure to meet.  (P.S. It was her page that I got the dancing cat at the top of this page from.)  Mary actually has two pages, one doing with cats, that other deals with he second favorite pastime, genealogy.
