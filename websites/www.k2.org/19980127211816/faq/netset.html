Netscape Personal Edition Dialup Installation

Installation
  1. Download Netscape with dialer from Netscape's Web Site.
  2. Run the self-extracting executable to begin installation.
  3. There will be a few initial screens giving you an overview of what the Wizard will do. Just click on Next to continue past them.
  4. You will eventually see the following screen:

  5. Choose that you already have a service provider.
  6. Click Next.
  7. You will then be asked to name the icon that will be used to connect with us. You can name it EnterAct if you like.

  8. Click Next when done.
  9. The next few screens don't ask anything of you except to read what Netscape has to say. Just click the Next buttons to get through them.
  10. Eventually, you will get to a screen that asks you for your full name and company if applicable. Fill those values in and click Next.

  11. The next screen asks for your login and password. It looks like this:

  12. Make sure to enter your login name in all lower case. Your password is case sensitive too, so only enter capital letters if your password has capitals in it.
  13. You can leave the I will need a TTY window checkbox unchecked. Click Next.
  14. The next screen will ask for your e-mail login and password. These values are the same as you entered in the previous screen. Click Next when done.
  15. In the next screen, enter the area code and phone number you plan to dial to connect to us. Click Next.

  16. The next screen will ask for domain name server information. For Primary DNS Server, enter 207.229.143.1.
  17. For Secondary DNS Server, enter 207.229.143.2.
  18. For Domain Name, enter enteract.com.
  19. Click Next when done.

  20. Netscape is now going to ask for the mail servers. Please fill them in according to the following picture; The POP Server should be pop.enteract.com. The SMTP Server should be smtp.enteract.com. The NNTP Server should be news.enteract.com.
  21. Click the Next button when done.

  22. You will now be asked for information about your modem.

  23. If your modem is not listed, you will have to run the modem wizard.
  24. Click Next after you've made your selection.
  25. Fill in your phone number in the next screen. and click Next.

  26. If you want to disable call waiting, or need a prefix in front of the phone number in order to dial out of your office or home, fill in the appropriate fields on the screen.
  27. You will also be asked for Tone or Pulse. More than likely you are going to use Tone. Pulse is for rotary phones.

  28. Click Next.
  29. The last couple of screens mainly tell you that you have finished setting up your connection. You will be left with several icons in your Netscape program group; one of these will be your EnterAct dialer icon.
  30. Double click this icon.

  31. Fill out your username and password.
  32. To connect, click Dial
  33. That will dial your modem and connect you to EnterAct. From here you can search web sites in Netscape or read your mail.
  34. It is suggested that you check out the properties of your connection to set any options that you might want to change.
  35. From the dialer screen, click Properties.

  36. This screen allows you to change your dialing properties. You can change the phone number here.

  37. This screen allows you to set such things as making Netscape come up automatically after you are connected. The only setting you need to be aware of is the idle timeout value. We will disconnect you after 20 minutes of idle time so it is unnecessary to have Netscape disconnect you any sooner than that.

  38. The modem settings allow you to change the Com Port you are connected to as well as the baud rate setting. 57,600 is good for 28,800 modems. 38,400 works well for 14,400 modems.
  39. Click Advanced.

  40. Here you can change the modem initialization string should you need to.
  41. The last tab allows you to set the DNS addresses and the domain name. Verify that the primary DNS address is 207.229.143.1 and that the secondary is 207.229.143.2.

  42. When you are finished verifying all of your settings, you can click OK.
