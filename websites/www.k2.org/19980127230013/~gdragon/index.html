Green Dragon Productions/Cynhyrchiadau'r Ddraig Werdd is an enterprise which evolved from the desire of two musicians, Mari Morgan and Eluned Jones, to promote and present the music of Wales to the peoples of the world. To share the beauty and the essence of the Welsh music already written and to promote the exciting new works of Welsh-American composers. To seek out others who are influenced in their musicianship by their Welsh heritage.

Behind Green Dragon Productions lies a tale in a 'tail'. One shared by many who make their life and living away from their 'homeland'.
