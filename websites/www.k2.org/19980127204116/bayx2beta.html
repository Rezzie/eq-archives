Due to popular demand we are currently testing equipment from Bay Networks which supports the USR-developed X2 standard -- a protocol that allows users to connect at higher speeds than 33.6K over normal telephone lines. To insure that this upgrade to service will be feasable,this equipment is being tested on a separate set of phone numbers to discover and attempt to resolve any issues that may comealong before fully implementing this on our system. Since they are still in testing, these numbers are subject to change and we cannot guarantee their reliability.

We ask that any users that try out the service, and let us know how it appears to be working -- or not working. We are especially interested in any trouble reports. For these purposes we've set up a special mailbox x2@enteract.com, any general questions about the technology or future plans should still be directed to the usual support@enteract.com.
