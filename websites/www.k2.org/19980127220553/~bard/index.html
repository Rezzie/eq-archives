As you can see, I'm starting to aim more toward the rambling essay approach to a web page. Those familiar with previous incarnations know that while I had a few pretty slick imagemaps, my web pages themselves have been pretty damned mundane "this is my life story" sorta stuff. I figure that's dull. Everyone does that. And nobody actually wants to read it.

So what can I do to make people read my web page? I don't know. Put a bunch of porn on it. Well, they won't read it, but it'll get a lot of hits. Put links to all the other groovy sites on the web. Maybe I'll get hits, but only by folks passing through to find all my Babylon 5 links...and once they bookmark those for themselves, all my traffic will dissappear.

So my guess is for a rambling kinda essay thing. I like to ramble. I like to write essays. I like to show off my thoughts in a public sort of way without the pressure of feeling like I'll be judged. So I figure that's the way to go with my web page. Will it get me more hits? Probably not. I certainly won't drag in all the adolescents looking for porn. They'll see text and the word "politics" and flee like little bunnies. But if I do get a coupla readers, cool.

And if not...hell, I'll just write anyway. It'll keep me occupied, if no one else. So get yourself a beer, turn on some music, and browse away.
