Microsoft Outlook
  1. Start up Outlook by clicking its icon on your desktop or by clicking on the Start menu, then Programs, and then Microsoft Outlook.
  2. From the main window, go to Tools/Services...

  3. You will then see the following screen:

  4. If Internet Mail is not on this list, click on Add... and at the Add Service to Profile window, select Internet Mail and click OK. Then, select Internet Mail and click the Properties button. You will see the following screen:

  5. Fill out the following information:
    • Full name: your name as you would like it to appear on outgoing mail.
    • E-mail address: your email address, i.e., username@enteract.com.
    • Internet Mail server: enter pop.enteract.com. This is our incoming mail server.
    • Account name: your username, all in lower case.
    • Password: your password. You may leave this blank if you would prefer to be prompted for it when Outlook checks for new mail. Otherwise, Outlook will connect to our mail server automatically.
  6. Click on Advanced Options... You will see the following screen:

  7. In the Forward all outbound mail... field, enter smtp.enteract.com, and then click OK. This will take you back to the Internet Mail window. Click OK. This will take you back to the Services window. Click OK.
  8. Go to the Tools menu and click Options... You will see the following screen:

  9. Click on the E-mail tab. In the Check for new mail on field, make sure Internet Mail is checked. Click OK. You are now ready to send an receive email!

Sending Email

  1. From the main window, go to File/New/Mail Message.

  2. You will see the following screen:

  3. This is the Outlook composition screen. Enter the email address of the person you are sending the message to in the To: field. Enter a subject heading for your message in the Subject: field, and type the body of your message in the field below it.
  4. When you are ready to send your message, just click the Send button in the upper left hand corner of the window.

Checking Email

  1. To check your mail, go to the Tools menu and click Check for New Mail.

  2. If you did not enter your password in the Internet Mail Properties, you will see the following screen:

  3. Enter your password and click OK. Outlook will then begin downloading your mail.
