Windows

There's a great site for Windows Internet software at TUCOWS: (Windows 95 or Windows 3.x)
You might also try Stroud's Consummate Winsock List.

Here are some of the more popular Internet applications for Windows:

  • Winsock will get your Windows machine dialed-in and connected to us.

  • Pegasus is our favorite Windows email program.

  • Eudora is another popular email program.

  • Telnet will enable you to connect to other computers, such as our UNIX host.

Macintosh

There's a great site for Macintosh Internet software at The Mac Orchard. Be sure to scan the entire page (the link above jumps to the middle of the page, where the software links are located) for lots of great Macintosh links.

Here are some of the more popular Internet applications for the Macintosh:

  • FreePPP is the standard dial-up software for the Macintosh.

  • Eudora is a popular Mac email program.

  • NCSA Telnet will enable you to connect to other computers, such as our UNIX host.
