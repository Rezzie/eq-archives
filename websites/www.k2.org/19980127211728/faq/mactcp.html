MacTCP Setup
  1. Go to your System Folder.
  2. Open your Control Panels.
  3. Open MacTCP. You will see the following screen:

  4. Highlight PPP by clicking on it.
  5. Click the More... button.
  6. You will see the following screen:

  7. The first thing you want to do is click the Manually option under Obtain Address.
  8. Next, enter 0.0.0.0 as the Gateway Address in the Routing Information box.
  9. Click Server in the Obtain Address box.
  10. In the Domain Name Server Information box, erase any information that is presently there and enter enteract.com. as the domain on the first line and 207.229.143.1 as the IP Address next to it.
  11. In the second line, put a period as the domain. Enter 207.229.143.1 as the IP.
  12. In the third line, put a period as the domain. Enter 207.229.143.2 as the IP.
  13. Make sure the top line has the Default radio button filled in for it.
  14. Click OK.
  15. Restart your Mac.
