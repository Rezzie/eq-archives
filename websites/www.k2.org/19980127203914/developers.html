We Want Web Developers!

Here at EnterAct, we're very excited about The Developers' Program

The Pitch

We have a little dream. We know EnterAct is a terrific ISP. All right, we're a little biased. But our dream is to be such a great ISP that you'll line up to sign up.

In talking to some of our current clients, we've found most of you would like some combination of the following:

  1. An ISP that's responsive, quality-oriented, reliable, and knowledgeable; in short, an ISP you'd feel comfortable recommending to your clients.

  2. A chance to be a one-stop-shop for your customers.

  3. An ISP that refers business to you.

  4. An ISP that does not compete with your company.

  5. A way to make it worthwhile (i.e. profitable) to host your clients' sites.

The Deal

If you're a web developer (and who isn't these days), EnterAct offers the following benefits:

  1. Discount Accounts

    After the purchase of your personal CompleteAct, we'll sell you additional CompleteAct Accounts and all the additional services for your clients at wholesale prices - 25% off. In exchange for managing these accounts (being the first line of contact, handling billing, etc.), you can resell these accounts to your clients at whatever cost (and profit!) makes sense to you.

    Pre-pay discounts still apply! Some developers pre-pay for their clients on an annual basis (a 30% discount), then bill their clients monthly, giving them an even greater profit margin.

    The one-stop-shop concept is obviously a great marketing tool for you. Your customers have the convenience of a single monthly bill. For you, it's easier to sell a monthly retainer fee in addition to hosting fees.

    The reality is, your clients are your clients. If you have clients at another ISP right now, you know that your ISP is getting their money, and has the on-going business relationship with them (i.e., the opportunity to sell them additional services). EnterAct reverses that relationship.

    If and when you eventually grow to the point that you've got your own T1 and servers, we can help you make the transition. We can still work with you to provide dedicated lines and/or dial-up services to you or your clients.

  2. Co-located Servers

    For $495 per month you can co-locate a server on our network to host your customers' CompleteAct accounts on. This gives you the ability to have unlimited disk space, the operating system of your choice, and control over processor performance and load. (You can make those cgi applications hum!)

    In addition, once you've registered 15 CompleteAct accounts with us, we'll waive your co-location fees!

  3. Referrals

    Because EnterAct doesn't compete with you for web development business, we can send all those calls we get your way!

    If we've piqued your interest, contact us to learn more.
