USAMEX is an import/export consultant agency with offices in the United States and Mexico. Our principal objective is assisting Mexican, other Latinamerican, and United States based enterprises establish new commercial relationships with partners across their borders. We maintain an extensive data base of manufacturers, wholesalers, and agricultural and raw material producers located in Mexico, other countries in Latinamerica, and the United States. We can assist your business in finding a new buyer, or a new supplier of components or raw materials. We can also assist your business in finding a new location where you can manufacture your product at the minimum costs for the greatest profits.

We are a full service agency. Services offered include research, trade brokerage, and negotiation and drafting of contracts. Out staff consists of attorneys licensed to practice in the United States and Mexico, and import/export specialists. All possess extensive experience in dealing with NAFTA and other trade issues which are unique to international commerce. You choose the extent to which you utilize our services, from providing you with a list of prospects for your sales or purchasing specialists, to finding your buyer or supplier and getting his or her signature on the dotted line of a contract drafted in accordance with Mexican and American laws and regulations.
