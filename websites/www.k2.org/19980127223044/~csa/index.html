Welcome to the CSA Group

Within the past several years, hospitals, universities, and other service-based businesses have spent millions of dollars strengthening customer relations in an effort to distinguish themselves. Most realize that offering first-rate service is one of the best ways to attract and keep customers in an increasingly competitive arena; many, however, have no plan in place to evaluate their service programs from the only vantage that really matters: the customer's perspective.

How We Can Help You

The CSA Group conducts communication audits to evaluate the level of telephone service your organization provides. We initiate anonymous calls to your service centers that simulate realistic customer situations, thoroughly documenting each encounter. In the end, we provide you with both quantitative and qualitative evaluations that will assess how well your customers are being served.

Our team of associates conducts each call using survey materials tailored specifically to your organization’s service needs. We offer audit packages that evaluate the basic components of your service structure, including how representatives handle each call and whether they are knowledgeable enough to help customers consistently find what they need. We also offer more focused audits that can help you track certain areas or evaluate the success of service initiatives.

The CSA group also offers follow-up audits for those interested in monitoring progress throughout the course of several months or even years. And, for those who wish to complement an assessment with related training, our staff can prepare and conduct developmental seminars to strengthen communication skills or highlight techniques for better integrating technology and service.

Why You Should Choose CSA

The CSA Group is comprised of communication management professionals with over 15 years of experience in service-based industries. All our associates are trained in telemarketing with a special focus on both phone etiquette and call center technology. And, perhaps most important, we offer real-life experience as service consumers.
