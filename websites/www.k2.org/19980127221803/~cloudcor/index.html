Cloud Corporation





Cloud Corporation is both a contract packager and a packaging machinery manufacturer. We specialize in flexible pouch and blister packages containing powders and pieces. Our food packaging specialties include coffee, candy, nuts, sugar, dry soup mix, salt, seasonings, dry yeast, oatmeal, and drink mixes such as cocoa, cider, cappuccino, powdered soft drinks and other instant beverages. In a separate facility, we copack soap, detergent, fertilizer and other products in water-soluble PVA packaging. Our headquarters and main manufacturing facilities are located in suburban Chicago, just minutes from O'Hare airport.

Our contract packaging facilities manufacture close to 1 billion pouches and thermoformed blisters each year through copacking arrangements with the leading U.S. food companies.

Our packaging machinery operation manufactures high-speed, continuous-motion, horizontal, form-fill-seal pouch machines which are operating every day in food plants throughout the world, including our own contract packaging plants. Hope you saw us at Pack Expo 96.
