NEWS FLASH -- 1/26/98

DATELINE: CHICAGO, IL Two members of Chicago's The Good have exciting new success stories to tell!

Drummer John Goodman has been selected as an "Artist Endorser" for Pro-Mark Sticks! This honor puts "Shiney" in great company. The other Pro-Mark endorsers read like a who's who of the drum world, and John is proud to be among them. We're proud of him, too, but we're doing our best to keep his head from exploding. Check them out at http://www.promark-stix.com.

Bassist Dave Russell has also hit the big time by having an article published in "Bass Player" magazine! Dave pitched the idea of an article about his past experiences playing for a "corporate" band, and they ran with it. If you hurry, you can still catch the February issue on newsstands. Their website, http://www.bassplayer.com doesn't have the text of the article there, but you can check the site out anyway...haven't you always wondered what kind of strings Vic Wooten uses? We'll post the text of it here soon if we can.
