Command Performance Sports, is Chicago's premier athletic and social club. We have been dedicated to offering the very best in organized athletic programs since 1987.

At Command Performance Sports , we offer our members the advantage of year-round sports programs. Whether you're the competitive or non-competitive individual, you're able to take advantage of the CPS programs for all levels.

New in town, or looking to get on a team? Command Performance Sports organizes individuals into teams based on the level of competitiveness desired.

Membership, is $30.00 per year. You receive a membership kit which includes your CPS t-shirt and sponsor products. Your membership also entitles you to various activity discounts.
