Netscape Mail

  1. Setting up mail for Netscape is very simple. To begin, open Netscape and go to the Options pull-down menu.

  2. Select Mail and News Preferences and select the Servers tab. You'll see a screen that looks like this:

  3. The SMTP server should be smtp.enteract.com.
  4. The POP server should be pop.enteract.com.
  5. The POP3 User Name should be your username (all lower case.)
  6. Leave the Mail Directory as default.
  7. Also, make sure you have messages removed from the server instead of left on the server.

  8. Click the Identity tab. You'll see the following screen:

  9. Fill in your name, e-mail address (username@enteract.com), and Reply-to Address (same as e-mail address.)
  10. You can leave Your Organization and Signature File blank.
  11. Click OK and that's all there is to configuring Netscape Mail.

Checking Mail

If you go to the Window pull-down menu and select Netscape Mail, you will go into Netscape's mail reader.

The first thing you'll be prompted for is your password. Enter it and you will see your mail appear.

To read a message, simply double click it. It will show up on the bottom half of your screen, so if you can't see it, maximize your window.

Sending Mail

To send mail, simply click the To:Mail icon. You will be prompted for the recipient's email address and the subject of your message.

You can then begin your message. When done, click the Send button. Your message will be sent automatically.
