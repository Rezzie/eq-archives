Every social function reflects the tastes of the client and the *guests. Our task is to distill your ideas into an evening that is both entertaining and sensitive to the complications of bringing personalities together.

Music is the universal language. Between our library, your suggestions and guest requests we will create a mix to please most of the people. If necessary we will purchase music not in our collection. Feel free to bring your own selections.

Everyone has been annoyed by an over zealous dj, so before you select one of our djÕs we require you meet with him or her. If you like you may visit one of their shows to help you make the decision. You must have good feelings about the artist to eliminate unpleasant surprises. All successful shows are created on paper first. Face it, a party is not unlike any theatrical production. It is a good idea to have a script to follow. A script can always be thrown away or improvised upon. Think about the entrances, the funny bits, the conflicts, the movement, the blocking, the people, the characters, the audience and the exits. Let your mind go and make every guest feel welcome.

No one wants a corny party full of cliches. We must communicate. Tell us what frightens you most about having a dj and we will find the way to avoid scaring you. We will be hokey, only at your request.

*In order to successfully entertain every musical taste of our culturally diverse urban environment the DJ welcomes any Guest to bring their own Music (CD) and will do his very best to mix it into the program. Your guests must be notified prior to the event for this to be effective, (ie. flyers in the cafeteria and stairwells and a delegate to the compile suggestions). This effort almost guarantees satisfaction in all camps. We are more than happy to assist in any way.

Give us a call, we are thrilled to share ideas with you.
