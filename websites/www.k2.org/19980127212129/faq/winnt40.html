WindowsNT 4.0

Digital vs. Analog

If you cannot connect successfully with NT 4.0, try an analog number. There have been reports of people not being able to connect automatically (although they are fine if they dial manually) with the digital modems.

Service Pack 2

If you do experience problems dialing into our digital numbers, try applying the Service Pack 2 from Microsoft. Once that is installed, you will also have to apply the following fix to it.

  1. Obtain the Post Service Pack Hotfix, ras-fix, from Microsoft by going here.
  2. Click Readme.txt to find out which version of the fix you need to download.
  3. Apply the fix according the the readme file in step 2.

The Domain Field

When you dial NT, you are prompted for your username, password, and domain. Leave the domain field blank or you will have trouble logging in successfully.
