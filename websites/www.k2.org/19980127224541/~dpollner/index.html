Quant Trading Inc.

Quant Trading Inc. is a Connecticut corporation founded in 1989 with the objective of becoming the leading provider of front- and middle-office systems in the fixed-income marketplace. By 1993, Quant’s client base covered 25% of the Primary Dealer community and now includes nearly 25 broker-dealers, commercial banks, and investment banks. In 1996, Quant was recognized as a leading player in the aggressively growing market for fixed-income trading systems (see The Tower Group report on "US Government Securities Analytical Trading Systems" dated March 31, 1996). Quant is an IBM National Solutions Provider Business Partner, a Sun Microsystems Value-Added Reseller and a member of the Reuters Third Party Developers Program.

Company Profile

Quant is led by its founder and CEO, Daniel M. Pollner, a trader by background. Co-founder and Business Director, Emery J. Stephans, joined Quant following 25 years of senior management experience with IBM. Quant’s sales efforts are led by Joel D. Kazis, formerly Managing Director and head of fixed-income government sales and trading at UBS Securities (1991-93) and Smith Barney (1989-91), and a Director at Salomon Brothers (1979-89). John J. Mangini, a 45-year IBM veteran and retired Corporate Director of Worldwide Product Quality Measurement came to Quant in 1994 to assist in marketing and corporate development.
