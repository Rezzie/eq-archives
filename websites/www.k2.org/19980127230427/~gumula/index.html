           Located in unincorporated Calumet Township on the outskirts of Gary, IN, the Instrumental Music Program of the Lake Ridge Schools serves approximately 110 students in grades 6-12. Beginning at Lake Ridge Middle School students start their studies in the beginning band utilizing the Standards of Excellence method. These budding young musicians then progress into the Lake Ridge Middle School Band, a combined seventh and eighth grade ensemble. Each grade rehearses separately and later comes together in performance. The final step is to the Calumet High School Band, which includes students in grades 9-12 and encompasses marching as well as concert band work.
