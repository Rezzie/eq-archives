The HomePage of Ron Almiron


Welcome...

...to my humble (web) abode. Born, raised, and schooled in the Midwest, I currently reside in Chicago. Or is it St. Louis? Depends on the DMV. Anyway, I'm still a Cardinals fan. Get a Real Time View of the Chicago downtown area, my neck of the woods.

Neuter Dream

I'm a graduate of the University of Notre Dame, where Domers had the Times of their Lives, and only 28% of my freshman class was female. Catch a glimpse of a beloved campus landmark.



The Day Begins

Get up already. Time to shower, shave, etc. Eat breakfast. Read the paper. Grab some coffee. Leave for work. How will you go? Drive the car, ride the bike, or take public transpo? I keep my Ronmobile in the Dungeon at Cityfront Place, my residence. Those of you in between jobs can go shopping. Browsing the Internet Shopping Network means not having to leave your house. When in St. Louis, stop by the Galleria.

Lunch Break

Hungry? Well, do lunch with someone. Don't be out too long. Or perhaps take in a soap, like Bold. It's only a half-hour. Just long enough to get your fill. Warning: You will be sucked in. Speaking of getting sucked in, those domestically inclined can turn to Martha Stewart for guidance.

The Afternoon

Not too late for tea time. But maybe so for tee time. Regarding the latter, I've ventured into new, uncharted territory, this thing called golf. If Bold failed to suit your fancy , try a cybersoap, like The Spot, or Chicago's very own Lake Shore Drive. Oh, might as well plan dinner. (I know what you're thinking, and I do realize I've got matters more pressing than what I'll have for my next meal.) The Food Network has some good ideas.

Evening

So, what's for dinner? You mean it's not ready? No time (or ability) to cook. Redbook has some ideas for Fifteen-Minute Meals. If all else fails, then eat out. Zagat's sorts out the good from the bad. When in Boston, try Aujourd'hui. Let me know what you think, because I haven't dined there. But Ms. Julia Child has. The acclaimed New French restaurant provided the inspiration for the name and theme of the HomePage. Burp. That was delicioso. Oh boy, it's PrimeTime, PrimeTime. For those who don't watch TV, or at least not as much as I do, go work out. Ever thought about how long you have to live? Take a longevity test. Do you like music? Check out the SoundRoom for an interesting melange of musical tastes. Expand your horizons by taking up a hobby. I've recently taken up fantasy football. Learn more about one of it by visiting the SkyBox.

The Night

Ready to turn in? Catch Dave. Check out the Cool Site of the Day, the Mediocre Site of the Day, or Mirsky's Worst of the Web if any of my suggested links failed to entertain you. "You go to sleep!" Mom says. Dream on. And in the event you're still bored, then visit the GeekCorner.
