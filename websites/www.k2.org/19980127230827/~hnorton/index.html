My Dance Activities

Click on the image to see a larger version.

My wife, Susan, and I do several kinds of dancing, square dancing, round dancing, ballroom, and country & western. When we are not dancing ourselves, we are teaching. Our favorite form of dance is round dancing. Round dancing uses ballroom steps, but each dance is prechoreographed and the leader, called the cuer, calls out the steps so that all of the couples are going around the circle doing the same figures at the same time. It is very pretty and a lot of fun. I cue for a couple of dance clubs and have choreographed some of my own dances. The Diamond Dancers Home Page lists our class and Sunday dance schedules.
