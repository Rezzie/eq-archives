Welcome to the Chicago Area Chapter's Homepage


The Ninety-Nines, Inc. is a unique and special international organization of licensed women pilots. It was founded in 1929 primarily to provide a forum for coordinating the interests and efforts of women in the aviation field.

The Chicago Area Chapter meets once a month, each time at a different airport in the Chicago area. We organize various aviation related activities throughout the year including; safety seminars, airmarking, air races, flying competitions, and fly-ins.

Women interested in aviation are invited to attend a monthly meeting, and student pilots are welcome to participate in all the chapter's activities, but they may not vote or hold office. See the student page for information about our programs for women student pilots.
