Microsoft's Outlook Express

Outlook Express is an email application bundled with Microsoft's Internet Explorer 4.0. You can download a copy of Internet Explorer 4.0 here.

Configuring Outlook Express

  1. After launching Outlook Express, go to the Tools menu and select Accounts...

  2. Go to the Mail tab and click Add.

    This will start the Internet Connection Wizard, which will prompt you for the information necessary to set up Outlook Express.

  3. First, you will be asked for your name. Enter it as you would like it to appear on outgoing mail, and click Next.
  4. Enter your email address; for example, johndoe@enteract.com. Click Next.
  5. You will see the following screen:

    At the top, make sure that it says My incoming mail server is a POP3 server.
    For Incoming mail (POP3 or IMAP) server, enter pop.enteract.com.
    For Outgoing mail (SMTP) server, enter smtp.enteract.com.
    Click Next.

  6. You will see the following screen:

    Make sure that Log on using: is selected and enter your username in the POP account name: field. Enter your password in the Password: field. Click Next.

  7. You will then be asked for a Friendly Name, which is simply a name for the entry you have just created in Outlook Express' list of accounts. You can choose EnterAct, or whatever else you would prefer. Click Next.
  8. Finally, you will be asked how you connect to the Internet. Unless you are on a LAN, select Connect using my phone line. Click Next.
  9. Click Finish. You should now see your entry in the list of accounts.

Checking Email

Once you have configured Outlook Express, you can check your mail by clicking the Send and Receive icon in the toolbar, or by going to the Tools menu and selecting Send and Receive.

Sending Email

To compose and send a new message, click on the Compose Message icon in the toolbar, or by going to the Compose menu and selecting New Message. You will then see a composition window in which you can enter the recipient's address and the body of your message.

When you are done composing your message, click the Send icon in the toolbar of the message composition window, or by going to the File menu and selecting Send Message.
