EnterAct's EnterConnect ISDN services are ideal if you are a small to medium sized organization interested in connecting your Local Area Network (LAN) to the Internet. The speed and digital reliability of ISDN - Integra ted Services Digital Network - when combined with EnterAct's unique Centrex service, allows you to bring the power and versatility of a dedicated Internet connection to multiple desktops at a surprisingly affordable price.
Typically when an organization connects to the Internet via an ISDN connection, they are charged a per-minute rate by the phone company in addition to the charges from their dedicated line provider. EnterAct's Centrex ISDN service, the resu lt of a special agreement between EnterAct and Ameritech, allows you to avoid these per-minute charges, and pay only a flat monthly rate for the Ameritech portion of your charges. On average, Centrex ISDN will save you hundreds of dollars per month! If you are a residential line customer, and therefore not subject to Ameritech per-minute charges, our EnterConnect ISDN - Home Office is your best bet.
Whichever option is right for you, EnterConnect ISDN offers several additional advantages that can be a consideration for small and medium sized organizations looking for a full-time Internet connection - flexibility, speed, and a managed se rvice option.
ISDN allows your organization to connect to the Internet at speeds of 64Kbps or 128Kbps. You can specify one connection rate initially, and if you find you have too little or too much bandwidth change your service with just a simple call to EnterAct.
EnterConnect ISDN supports STAC compression, allowing you to enjoy data transfers nearly 2 ½ times faster than stated ISDN rates. This technology will give you web page loading, file transfers and more at speeds approaching fractional T1 l evels!
If your organization does not have the time or resources to manage a dedicated connection, EnterAct offers an optional managed service. With this service, EnterAct is responsible for the installation and management of all Internet related e quipment.
EnterAct's unique pricing options, coupled with the EnterConnect ISDN features, makes our dedicated ISDN service the best "bang-for-the-buck" in the dedicated line arena!
