Who are We?

Saint John Brebeuf is a Catholic community located in Niles, Illinois, just North of the city of Chicago, within the Archdiocese of Chicago. Our parish belongs to the Niles-Park Ridge Cluster which is composed of our parish and 4 other parishes in our area (St. Isaac Jogues and Our Lady of Ransom in Niles, Mary Seat of Wisdom and St. Paul of the Cross in Park Ridge). Our parish is currently composed of about 3,400 member families and has 54 official ministries in the parish and are continuing to look for more ways to reach out.

Our Mission Statement

"We are a people of faith who offer each other and those touched by us the means to experience God through prayer, sacrament, education and mutual support in light of the Gospel and Catholic Tradition."

Who was St. John Brebeuf?

Our parish is named for a French missionary and priest who was martyred for the faith, in what is in present day, Canada. He and Isaac Jogues became the first North American Martyrs.
