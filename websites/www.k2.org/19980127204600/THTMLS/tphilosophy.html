PHILOSOPHY

Our Philosophy



This might seem a little corny, but we feel it's extremely important for us to state, unequivocably, our beliefs, our plans and our intentions to our customers.

So here they are:

  1. Individual - We believe in the individual's ability to excel beyond their own expectations. If properly motivated and inspired, we can all do extraordinary things. We want to do them daily.

  2. Honesty - We will be honest with our customers and to each other.

  3. Commitment - We are committed to providing the best possible service to our customers.

  4. Passion - We are passionate about what we do.

  5. Humor - We maintain a sense of humor. Work should be fun!

  6. Expensive - It only costs a little bit more to go first class. When faced with a decision directly affecting our customers, we choose to spend a little bit more to improve service. We'll make it up in volume, right?

  7. Inexpensive - Where customer service is not affected, we strive to minimize expenses. This will ensure that we're around for our customers in the future.

  8. Rewards - We believe in rewarding hard work and results. All employees have a significant stake in the overall success of EnterAct, L.L.C.

  9. Privacy - We have absolute respect for the privacy of our customers and each other.

  10. Customers - Most of all, we believe that our customers should be so fanatical about our service and our respect for them that they become our most powerful marketing force.
