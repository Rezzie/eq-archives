    What We Do


    Erickson Planning & Consulting Services is a complete management & consulting service for both corporate and social endeavors.  With over 10 years in convention planning, catering, & meeting administration, we specialize in working out the details for you.  EPC will first meet with you work out the primary goals of your function, then we go to work for you.  

The most important event is yours; and it requires the consideration of planning & management.

No matter what size your function is, attention to detail is important. Having someone who listens what you are looking for, and helps to make it happen will help you be free to for what is important to you. Serving the Chicagoland area, Erickson Planning & Consulting can meet with you on your schedule, and discuss what services are needed.  We understand that a budget is as individual as each person is. We will work with you, so you can get the most for your money.

EPC will aid in the selection of your reception site, space and other vendors, then begin the process of planning your wedding or function.  This process involves constant contact with your vendors to confirm meeting room set-up, catering, guest & VIP arrangements, and the on-site coordination of the event.  For all of your personal, corporate event planning, or hospitality services.  Contact us.
