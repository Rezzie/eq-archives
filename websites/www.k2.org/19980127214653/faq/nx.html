News Express

  1. If you haven't already, download News Express from www.tucows.com.
  2. Unzip the program into its own directory on your hard drive.
  3. Run the nx.exe file that is created after you have unzipped the downloaded file.
  4. Once you're in the main menu, go to Config/Setup from the pull-down menus.

  5. You will see the following screen:

  6. The NNTP Server should be news.enteract.com.
  7. The SMTP Server should be smtp.enteract.com.
  8. Enter your name, or whatever name you'd like, in the Fullname field.
  9. In the E-Mail field, enter your e-mail address, username@enteract.com.
  10. The rest of the fields can be left alone. Click OK when done.
  11. Now choose Preferences from the Config pull-down menu.
  12. You will see the following screen:

  13. You want to select Retrieve new groups only [if any]. This will make News Express bring down only new news groups as opposed to every single news group (there are over 18,000 of them) every time you connect to the news server.
  14. The rest of the options are up to you. Feel free to look through all the tabs and change whatever settings you desire. When done, click OK.
  15. To connect to the news server for the first time, go to File/Connect. When you have finished downloading all of the newsgroups, you will see the following screen:

  16. Now, because you don't want to be subscribed to all 18,000+ groups (who has time to read them all anyway??), you want to subscribe to only those that interest you. News Express allows you to search for the groups you want. Let's say you want to see all of the music groups. You would then just type music in the Filter field located at the bottom of the screen. You would see the following screen:

  17. As you can see, only the groups with the string music are listed. To subscribe to one of them, just click the checkbox located to the left of the group name.
  18. Each time you connect to the news server, you will see only the groups you are subscribed to unless you go to View/All Groups. In addition when you connect, if there are new groups added to EnterAct's news server, you will be prompted if you want to subscribe to these or not.
  19. To read the articles in a group, simply double click the group you want to go into and then double click the article you want to read.
  20. That should get you started. For more information about News Express, check out their on-line Help menu from the main screen.
