Paul Friedman

The Voice of Wrigley Field

 

 Since 1995, I have been the Public Address Announcer at Wrigley Field in Chicago, Illinois. But it was at a game in 1972 with my father that I became a Cubs fan. I remember Ron Santo hitting a home run at my first game and from then on, I've been hooked. I think most anyone who's been to a game would know me if I told them what I do, but hopefully this site will give people a chance to find out a bit more about who I am.

Thank you very much!

Thank you to everyone, from literally all over the world, who has visited this site. It has been a wonderful experience for me to communicate with you and I am very grateful for your visits.
