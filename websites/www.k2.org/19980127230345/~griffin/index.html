Mike Griffin

Photographer


Exhibitions in New York, Paris, Chicago and other cities. Color work noted for its abstract layers in combination with a single negative or positive black and white image. "La Creation du Monde" is an example. Current work is black and white 30 x 40 inch prints with some computer manipulation to retouch, position, and scale the subjects.

Curated shows for the on line AB Chicago Gallery at Chicago Psyberview, American Society of Media Photographers, and the Ukrainian Institute of Modern Art.

Awarded the "Pris de Paris Raymond Duncan" and Best in Show: Cork Gallery, Avery Fisher Hall, Lincoln Center, New York.
