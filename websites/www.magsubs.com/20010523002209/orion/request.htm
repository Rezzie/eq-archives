 ORION is comprised of people who enjoy on-line roll playing games.  Several of our members have been playing together for over 5 years on various other games such as UO, Yirsurbus and Kingdom of Drakkar.  We hunt together, help each other out and in general enhance all our on-line gaming enjoyment by playing together.  

If you are just looking for a temporary stop to pick up some quick gear, this guild is not for you.  If you get easily upset when you die, this guild is not for you.  If you are a good sport, like to help others, and basically are an addicted game player, then this guild is for you!  

Guild Rules

1.  No Ks'ing Or Anything Else that Gives Guild a Bad name.  If you are a
 Player that Does this,   No need to apply.


 2.  Must be in Guild 30 days,  or 5 Major Guild Hunts to be on List to get
    items from hunts.  We are Not here to supply someone with
 gear for a day or two and then they Take Gear and leave guild.


3.  We like to get to Know a Person before We add them to Guild,   If you
   have the Desire to Join Orion,  You have to hunt with us first,  We will
    invited you to hunts if we know you are interested. This way you can get to know us as well as Us getting to know you.
