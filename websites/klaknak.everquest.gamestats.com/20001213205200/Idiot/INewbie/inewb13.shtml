Z - by Kressilac

Ok my character Kressilac started out life in Kelethin. After reaching level 4 I decided to go into the Orc camp like everyone else.

Turns out I got beat up real bad and decided I would give it another try later that evening. I ran back to the Orc lift, got on the lift and camped. Funny thing happened when I came back. The log went something like this:

You have entered Greater Faydark.
... hit for ungodly damage non-melee
You were injured from falling.
LOADING PLEASE WAIT.

Turns out that the lift wasn't under me anymore and when I came into game I was placed into the same x,y AND Z coordinates as when I left and promptly feel to my demise. OOPS, never do that again cause the death is not a bug in the game according to guides (I think it should be).
