Top Ten Ironic Deaths - by Cj

10 Climbing a ladder to get to a mob, kill it, then fall off the ladder

9 Using SOW to flee a monster that nearly kills you, then dying from falling off a steep hill because you are running so fast

8 Saving a lower level person from a female tormented dead, only to discover it's the Festering Hag

7 Saving your party from a Deepwater Crocodile, only to get stepped on by the Sand Giant on the other side of the dune

6 Dying and re-appearing at your bind point where another usually easy monster beats you to a pulp because your gear is still on the FIRST corpse

5 Converting one too many hitpoints into mana because you are trying to get off one last spell to kill the monster

4 Dueling with someone over kill stealing, and getting attacked by a REAL monster

3 Killing an easy mob with a spell, which attracts other close by powerful mobs you didn't see

2 Testing out the levitate spell just after you level, and not getting to the ground before it wears off.

And the number one Ironic way to die is:

1 Fleeing a zone in order to escape a train, and zoning into, another, larger train.
