Top 10 ways to know you're in a zone WAY over your level - by Cj

10 People with gear alot more impressive than yours are all running the OTHER way

9 Everything has the word Lord, King, Queen, Lady, or Avatar in it's name

8 You hit a (fill in the blank) for 1 damage. (every time)

7 Someone else looks at your weapon, and says, "You mean those WORK in here?"

6 The monsters don't attack you because they are helpless with laughter on the ground

5 You lose more than one bubble of health each time a mob hits you.

4 You hit a mob with your most powerful spell, it isn't resisted, and the damage bar doesn't visibly move

3 The monster's weapon is WAY better than your own.

2 People around you refer to the spectres as 'trash monsters' in this zone

And the number one way to know you're in a zone WAY over your level:

1 You overhear another party and hear, "Man, Can't we go back to killing Lady Vox? That was EASY!"
