Newbie Cleric - by Steve S.

All my life I played a Cleric in computer games and paper and pencil. So I purchase this game Everquest. And think, Lets make my Cleric! Human!! So I make "Bob" the Cleric. pad his STR and a little bit in WIsdom. (Notice no discussion about tutorials or book/manual reading)

Start in freeport...walking around. (walking) Fall in a little pond and almost crap my pants! O NO! I move around some more and my swimming skill goes up! YEAH! Figured out how to equip my Club...and see a guard. Cool, he is alone! Lets see how well I do, I mean, MAN, I put so much into my STr, I should be able to take him!

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

Wow, that sucked, ok, now where am I?.....move around city, find docks, move around some more... Find this cool looking Priest dude. WOW! I bet he is part of my guild. (Priest of Discord) So I talk to him. He asks for a book, WHICH I HAVE! cool. Give him book. Nothing in return. What the text say? O who cares. End up killing lots of rats and bats and make level 2! Wow, this is easy. But wait? How do I make money? I'm running out of food and water? and what level do I get spells? I hope Clerics can actually heal and such. Still no money, running out of food and water, I zone to east commons.

Run up a bit and see this little ball of light. A wisp? Cool, lemme try that. INVULNERABLE? O NO Someone runs by stops and says, "don't run, just die." Yeah fun game.

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

So I finally get into a group. and someone teaches me how to use spells. We get into a fight, and I start healing them...NO EFFECT! They die. Talk about some nasty messages. I disband saying "I tried, I swear!" Made it to level 4 before I realized 1. Loot what you kill to make money 2. I am PvP+ and can't cast spells on everyone in game except other (very rare) PvP+ PC's

Arg.

Delete this guy, and start over, 3 weeks later. I am now a level 29 Cleric, Human. And Officer of a top guild on the Innoruuk server. heh, the learning curve of this game is amazing!
