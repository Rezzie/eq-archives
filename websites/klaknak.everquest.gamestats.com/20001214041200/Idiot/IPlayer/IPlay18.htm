Befallen (nuff said) - by Brellium

Brellium, Gnome enchanter and master illusionist, walks smartly into Befallen. "I have slain hundreds of goblins, tens of Minotuars, and even more of those evil willow wisps" he thinks as he walks in the entrance of of the crumbling palace "This place can not be any harder than any of those events, I even spent time in the Gnoll stronghold, downing those silly gnolls left and right. How silly it was for them to come running out of the pit, into our well set trap time and time again." He continues thinking to himself on how he should hunt in this place.

"I've got it, first I shall find myself a good place to bind myself, for one never knows when tragedy will strike." The small gnome walks to a small passage jutting out of the wall and decides this shall be the spot. He then starts chanting and feels the familiar tingle of a successful bind. Jacobar shouts "Giving away all the keys to the befallen!" Brellium turns around and sees Jacobar red in the face after shouting. Brellium pokes Jacobar. Jacobar waves a Brellium. "Hail! I have just arrived at this place, are those keys needed?" the white haired gnome says. Jacobar says "Yes they are"

Jacobar gives Brellium the keys.

"Thank you" Brellium says as he bows. Jacobar bows and heads out. Brellium thinks to himself "Well now that I have these maybe I will have a look around." as he unlocks the the door in the back of the hall. He walks in then summons a pet to guard him. The yellow robed figure walks around and finds a suitable target, his vile nemesis, a willow wisp. The willow wisp attacks the enchanter, the enchanter steps back, and his pet engages the willow wisp. The pet hits the willow wisp, the willow wisps takes off down the hall. time passes time passes The gnome then realizes the pet has vanished, and begins to summon another as five figures come running down the hall. After seeing this the enchanter runs off. makes it to the door, but alas he can not find his key....... time passes time passes even more time passes The gnome is now at the small passage wearing just his plain clothes, with his equipment on the other side of the door.

Brellium walks to the door and starts chanting, after a short moment his belongings are resting below him. He picks up his equipment and after a short chuckle walks over to the alcove, and memorizes his spells. Schimley shouts "Ahhhhhhhh" The gnome looks up only to see everyone leaving, and he is alone, with a number of undead. time passes time passes even more time passes

The unlucky gnome then picks up his items again. "I know what I need, a group, so someone else can get hammered by these things." Brellium shouts "Gnome enchanter looking for a group!" Schimley says "We need one more for our group" Brellium turns around and notices Schimley. Schimley waves. "But one thing first," Schimley says "I need to retrieve our corpses." The gnome nods knowingly. time passes time passes even more time passes Schimley says "Thank you for helping us get our corpses, sorry about the ghoul"

The gnome says "Well it happens" The party heads out to the lower levels. A willow wisp pokes at Brellium, he reacts by swinging his staff and hits it squarly. The willow wisp flees. time passes Three skeletons attack the party from the direction of the wisp time passes Three skeletons are now in one pile The wisp attacks again, and flees time passes More skeletons time passes More piles of bones time passes More skeletons, this time the party is running.

The gnome pulls all of them on him, alone. time passes time passes even more time passes The gnome enchanter scratches his head near the entrance of the zone. Party runs out of befallen. Every thing chasing the party attacks the enchanter, again. time passes time passes........
