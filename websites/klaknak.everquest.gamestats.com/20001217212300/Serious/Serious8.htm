For all of you lusting after Lara Croft or the Everquest Wood Elves, here is the good news: Women are real! And here is even better news: They are not polygonal! That's right, a woman is NOT a creature for you to pet and love and treat like a toy. A woman, much like you (but omigod, so much unlike you (Klaknak drools)), has a brain. What does this mean? A woman, my friends, is a person ... a human being. If you ever forget this, not only will you probably get the shit kicked out of you, but you will probably never be able to spend more than five minutes with a girl except when stuck in a Hurricane Mania line at the local supermarket.

Many newbie men become so fixed with Items 5 and 7, that they never fully experience what it means to be caught in the Vulcan Mind Grip that is a woman's gaze. Beware. It is deadly and is as addictive, if not more so, than Everquest.

Anyway, I hope this brief anatomy primer has convinced you that women are not mythical creatures but are real, living beautiful people who will forever change your life. And I will ask you not to point out that my model is a painting. Women are real, I swear!
