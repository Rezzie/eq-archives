Why I Don't Do Windows

by Calisca, 18th Level Paladin of Tunare, Veeshan Server

Have you ever noticed that Hybrid classes get REEEEEEEEEEALLY cocky once they hit their first rank of spells? Like we're god or something...or even Aradune. Fortunately for the rest of Norrath, this phase doesn't last long as we squirm in ignominious death at the feet of a particularly nasty MOB. Still, however briefly, it does happen, and this phenomenon seems to affect Paladins like me the worst, since we start out with that god-like Lay Hands thingie.

My name is Calisca. I am an 18th Level Paladin, and recovering idiot. Remember this is an ongoing process, and I may never be fully cured. But here I share my stories with you, Gentle Reader, that you may be one of the few who bypasses Hybrid Spell GOD Syndrome....and may I advise you off the bat that just because you can cast spells doesn't mean your hit points increase. We tend to forget little things like that.

I had gotten my spells AND meditate, so I was pretty doggone full of myself, flouncing around Crushbone, PROUD of my spells and my "Infamous Villain" status with these oversized evil Smurfs with big nasty pointy teeth. I'd been doing pretty well, so when the opportunity came to join a group and finally venture into the Castle, I bit. I mean, how could I deprive a group of my Righteous Paladin-ness (that nickname continues to stick, by the way, much to my newfound chagrin)? In we marched, banners waving.

It didn't take long to clear a pretty big swath in the throne room, and my experience was zooming. So was my ego. We'd even (don't ASK me how, it was a miracle I haven't had the chance to repeat) managed to take down Crush so now I had a spiffy Dwarven Ringmail Tunic. WooooooOOOOOooooo!

So how hard could it be to take D'vinn, right? Oh, THAT hard, you say....OK...but I want a peek. Just a little peek, to see what he looked like. I guess I peeked a little too far in (doggone, what do you MEAN Paladins don't get Eye of Zomm?) because the first thing I see is a Royal Guard. And I am Infamous. REALLY Infamous, apparently, because he attacked me. And boiling right out behind him is D'Vinn and Crush. They don't seem to want to socialize with me, unless that extremely painful backstab was D'vinn's way of inviting me to tea and crumpets.

Stupidly, I ran into the Throne Room instead of OUT. Surrounded by big nasties, I started to panic. And Lay Hands was gone by now to save a fellow group member, so no use to me (though I kept pushing the button just in case!!). I was going down fast when the magic words came from my inattentive group leader...

"CALI! Use the WINDOWS!"

She didn't realize I was low on HP, being kind of busy herself. For that matter, neither did I. Or more accurately, I didn't realize I couldn't just FLY out, you know. Even in the face of death, I'm still god. And it's not that big a fall, right? So....I....jump, ready to bounce to my feet and run like hell to the zone.

Oh...not only do I not get Eye of Zomm, I don't get Safe Fall EITHER. "Ambassador Dvinn backstabs YOU for 87 points of damage!" "orc oracle begins to cast a spell" (where the hell did HE come from??) "you feel your skin numb as the Frost Rift strikes you!" "Emperor Crush bashes YOU for 30 points of damage!" "Pain and suffering try to strike YOU but miss!" "You died." "LOADING, PLEASE WAIT....."

Ooh. Ow. See what I mean? GOD what an ego. But I learned my lessons...
1) Crush and D'vinn don't take kindly to unannounced guests.
2) Paladins are NOT GOD just because they can cast spells.
3) It's never a good idea to jump out of a tower window when you have two bubbles of life left.
4) Egotistical Paladins falling out of windows to their death makes the assembled masses waiting for the Orc Warlord laugh...until that heavily aggro'd train derails onto them.

I laugh now, but it was a bruiser at the time. Needless to say...this is why I DON'T DO WINDOWS!
