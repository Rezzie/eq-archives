by Gokuo


This was, well, quite a while ago, and it was my first level 6. He was a human monk, and I had some REALLY cool leather armor (CK laughs). I decided as me being at qeynos, and wanting to explore, I venture to surefall glade. There I am having a great time hailing all the NPCs in sight. And as I explore, I come apon a pool of water. "hey, there's a cave behind that water fall!" So I go to explore. "Hmmm, interesting, a big bear, no prob, just got sneak, ok" Sneak sneak sneak "WHOO HOO, it worked. OH SHIT, another one!!!" Grizzly bear mauls you."RUN! Bears shouldn't be able to swim, so, hey!" Loading please wait...

So I recover my corpse and learn from my mistake. Try again. I get past both bears, but it's too dark. Go back, forget, run into bear, loading please wait...

Ok, I'll get someone to help me, and so I do. Walking off together. We both die. Try again, die. This time I have a plan. "You cast and run, I go in and get the corpse!" "ok" I go in, he's looting his corpse, DOAH! Runaway, die. Come back to surefall, he just died. I try again, alone, and get it, but as I am going out, I get attacked, and manage to high tail my sorry butt out of there and die on land instead, phew! That's my story, oh just about 9 deaths in an hour, how fun. Well, I learned my lesson.

Gokuo, now level 8 troll warrior
