AAA - by Tulluian

I had just hit level 5 with my Halfling Rogue when it occured to me that I'm rather bored of the Misty Thicket. Having never been to Qeynos, I decided that it would be fun to go there. It just so happened that I ran into a friendly wood elf ranger who wanted to get there as well. Unfortunately, neither of us knew the way, so we hired a Halfling guild for 1 pp. The elf payed for the both of us. Our guide told us to put all our stuff in the bank, which we did.

So we set off to Qeynos, zoned to Kithicor, and were slain by some orcs. "No Problem", our guide told us. We set off to Kithicor again, only to be slain by the same orcs. It didn't occur to us that we should wait until the orcs leave before heading out, so we got killed by the orcs a third time. Finally, some nice person killed them and we set out to Qeynos. We followed our guide to Highpass, but unfortunately we were traveling at midnight. Anyone who knows Kithicor will know that it is always dark, especially at night, and we were traveling without a light source. We got seperated from our guide and ran straight into an orc camp. Much to our dismay, our guide didn't realize this until he had reached Highpass. We lost another 20 minutes trying to find each other. Finally, we made it to Highpass.

"This should be an easy walk", our guide told us. Apparently, he wasn't aware of the orcs that lived there. BOOM! BANG! SMASH! CRUSH! RRRIP!!! LOADING, PLEASE WAIT... We retraced our steps to Highpass, this time sneaked passed the orcs, and finally made it to some sort of building. Our guide went in, and was slain by the prisoners in the basement of the prison in Highpass. It was then that I started doubting his ablities as a guide. To make matters worse, the elf went linkdead. Another 20 minutes went by, and finally he made it to the pass and the elf logged back on. We proceeded to get killed by the gnolls on the other end of Highpass. The elf went linkdead again, this time for good. We waited for him for half an hour, then kept going. We were killed by the orcs again, came back, killed by gnolls, came back, both went linkdead at the same time, logged on, found we were dead, wished we had never been born, came back, got lost and killed, came back...

To make a long story short (Too Late!), we finally made it through the @$^#*&! hole after an hour or so, and came upon a group of travelers who escourted us to Qeynos. We made it to Qeynos hills, and I could already feel Qeynos nearby. Ah, Qeynos, at last I made it... Hey, who's Varsoon? RRRIIIPPP, ROOAR, CRACK, SCREAM... LOADING, PLEASE WAIT... AAAAAAARG!!!!!!!!!!!

I appeared in Rivervale.

I left the game for a week, then came back after experiencing some withdrawl simptoms (like saying "forward-slash smile" instead of actually smiling in RL), and surprisingly enough found a party to lead me to Qeynos. It was simply fantastic. I was stunned and awed by the beauty and colors of the human city, how they were so different from Rivervale... I hailed a guard. He answered me! All the deputies in Rivervale were deaf-mutes (except when saying time to die a [insert monster name here])! Every NPC I hailed answered me! I hailed Nerissa Clothespinner. She told me about [rumors] of corruption and how she wished her [sister] was here. Intrigued, I began typing: W... h... a... Huh? Why are you atta... KICK! BASH! SMASH! SHRED! LOADING, PLEASE WAIT... I appeared in Rivervale.
