Don't drink and swim

by Ganex Connery

Well, it finally happened to me. I read your site every once in a while and promise that I won't do the idiotic things others had happen to them, so i ended up doing something different!

I'm a barbarian warrior with a good amount of crafted and a cool looking executioner axe. This story takes place when I finally get to level 30 by soloing centaurs in south karana. I'm so happy because my axe will finally start working to its full potential. So i kill two more centaurs to see the nice haste proc go off then i run to lake rathe to get some food, water and bandaids (soloing is tough). I purchase my stuff and i still have a load of silver left (i try to use up my silver instead of destroying it) and purchase a bunch of short mead to work on my alcohol tolerance and celebrate getting level 30.

I get myself nice and toasted and decide that it wouldn't hurt to swim drunk because i barely ever see anything in the lake, let alone something that could kill me. So i jump in (i'm still at 3 bubbles of health because i was soloing earlier) and start swimming. I didn't get 10 feet when this light blue blur starts attacking me. I tried swimming back to the gypsys but having tunnel vision kinda messes ya up. So I ended up dying and losing my level to a blue goblin that i could have easily soloed if i knew what was going on.

I finally got level 30 back and I'm now almost level 31 due to soloing in the lake of ill omen (must have an affinity for those lakes). So no drinking and swimming!!!

Warrior of the 30th Circle
Cazic Thule Server
