Dumb, Dumber, Dumbest - by Bradzinoa

Well, after hearing many tales of the things in befallen that I could get, I decided to go there at 1:00 am. By the way my name is Bradzinoa, and at the time I was a level 16 Dark Elf Magician. Having just reached level seventeen and having somewhere around three hundred something hit points (with shielding and items). So, while in Nektulos, I shout for people to go to befallen with me. What I get is 2 casters, level 12 and 9. So we find our way to befallen. I bind my self just outside of the zone line. We go in. We head straight for skeleton Llrodd - because people told me that's who has the keys. we kick the sh*t out of Llrodd and some others who are there and I proceed to meditate. While I'm waiting, my groupmates start to talking. Eventually, they come to the question "What's down this hole?". "Lets jump down and find out" the level 12 says. I'll go says the level 9. "Wait," says the level 12, "who has the most hit points?" That was me, and so I told them. "Well, get going" they tell me. So I jump up, and try to creep over on the edge. As I'm getting closer to the edge and looking down I realize this is the hole of death my friend told me about while reading Idiot Files Gold. Just as I realized that, I fell in, and got hurt for much damage. I turned around and saw a ghoul and an "Icebone" skeleton. Luckily they hadn't seen me. "Good," I thought, "I can gate then." I wasn't that lucky. My pet got down the hole in time for the monsters to get really angry. Suffice it to say - I died (and lost my newly found level). I asked them to try to summon my corpse up, after I gave them consent. I see the words "Uh oh" come up on my group talk. And the level 12 bites the dust. The level 9 tells us she thinks she can summon the level 12's corpse as it's right at the bottom. She falls in, screams, and well, has a long run from Nektulos. So, being the smart guy I am, I go to EC and try to find help. Well, this level 12 guy who's ass I saved earlier gives me a tell and says that there's a lvl 19 troll who'll help - they're in the same guild. He comes and to help us. we go in, destroy all the stuff around the hole, then prepare to go down. I use an eye of zomm to see if the monsters are there. The eye is swiftly killed. I send another one down. Yay, no more monsters, the others hastily jump in. As the lvl 12 loots his body the icebone skell returns with backup. Well, we try again. The troll gets his corpse and so does the lvl 12. they gate just as the monsters come down the hallway, 3rd attempt. I go down with troll, he holds off Icebone as long as possible. he ends up gating, and I die. The troll decides to log. It's past 3 am. I'm in trouble. Before he goes he tells me it's clear. I have no idea how he knew, but I went down, very scared. Picked up most of the stuff on my corpse. with 2 things left to loot (stuff that wasn't too important) I could feel the monsters closing in, so I just gated with too much fear to stay. Well, that was the last time I grouped with anyone more than a level below me. Finally, after 3 hours of hard work, I ended up a level lower, with less stuff, and plenty of fear of that place. At least the next wasn't as bad (until we drew an entire hallway of stuff from the 2nd locked door) - but that's a story for another time. Bradzinoa Grimm 22nd Level Master of Conjuration (Magician) Rallos Zek server
