Do NOT drop items near Kunark NPCs!

by Kroax Mastakilla


Hello. It happens that I got kunark today, and saw a couple turns of event which were quite peculiar. All in FP I saw things like a sarnak talbred (BP with 14 ac 10 sv magic and 10 hp and looks like orange mail) selling for upwards of 900p. So, I remembered to save that juicy tidbit of info for later, and travel to kunark. Now, in full darkforge with a FBSS and a deathwatch sword, money wasn't really an issue. Anyways, after an exhausting boat ride on a boat that resembles beaver excrement, I land in the Overthere. Hmm, Kunark's a nice lookin place. Check this out, an evil city. Neat. So, I explore the place and eventually come across a merchant selling good stuff some people were OBVIOUSLY too lazy to sell to a merchant. "That'll be 27 platinum, 7 gold and 4 silver for the Sarnak Emblazened Talbard."
WHOOOOO! I thought! I'm gonna be rich! I find the bank. Take out 28pp. Proceed to the merchant. Yes! Bought the bp! Now to deposit it in the bank...go to bank..whoops dropped it. Oh well I'll just pick it u.... " Banker Trasall sucks in the item. Litterbug must be reported to Sathir for prosecution"
WTF!! I screamed. I looked, but in vain. The item was destroyed. Not selling anywhere, and I just lost a good profit. so, I cried to my guild Clan Death Cloud on mith marr (If you're evil, join the guild! It rocks =)) and they said that Kunark NPCs pick up items you drop in order to stop twinking. AAAAAAAAAAAAAAARRRRGGGGHHHH!!!! This goes with the time I accidentily sold my Dark Reaver to a merchant for 12 pp! Doh =)
