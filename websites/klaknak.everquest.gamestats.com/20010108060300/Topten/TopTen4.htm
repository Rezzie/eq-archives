Top Ten Reasons Signs You Have Been Renewbified - by No Joke

10. Typing a reply to someone, and Hailing-jumping-ducking--- whoops.

9. Starting a new character of a race without infravision after playing any race that has it. (Human warrior fleeing from a young kodiak in the Eastern commonlands: "Where's the zone? Where's the zone? WHERE'S THE xfarghk ZONE!?!" I returned in daylight, to discover I had been running straight into the northern zone wall the whole time.)

8. Losing a level, item, or corpse to a bug, and /petitioning for help.

7. Forgetting to /con. ("An armed aqua goblin? I could use a few more arrows." *attack!... miss, bleed, crumple* "Oh... it's Glubbsink..." LOADING.)

6. Falling from Kelethin. (Bonus points if you were still bound in Freeport)

5. Attempting to Root-and-nuke something, but forgetting to target it first. ("Hey... Why's it still coming? Why can't I move? Oh, well, at least its hits are dropping fas--- Oh zxrf!")

4. Burying the needle on the bad-faction meter, because you weren't reading the faction adjustment messages while fighting or marathon questing. (Gnoll fangs or Deathfist belts, anyone?)

3. Forgetting to get a bind. Also, forgetting to bind -yourself-, or remembering to bind but forgetting to check nearby NPCs for KoS tendencies.

2. Specifically for bards, but variations can happen to anyone: Switching from weapon to drum while in full flight, and missing your inventory... /auction "Is anyone selling a (insert weapon name here)? I dropped mine somewhere in West Karana." (Guaranteed to get truly encouraging, sympathetic, and helpful responses, such as "U dum newbie!" and "Haha, u suk newbie!")

1. After finally getting to somewhere you can train... losing your level to your guildmaster. (The standard type "Hail" works... For variation, you can try practicing your new spell in your guildhall, without retargeting yourself. There's nothing like "Maesyn Trueshot resisted the Flame Lick spell!" to wake you up after a long night of EQ...)
