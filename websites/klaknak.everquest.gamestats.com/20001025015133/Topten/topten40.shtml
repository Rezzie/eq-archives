Top Ten Proposed Governments in Norrath - by Phaethon

10. Democratic Republic in Oggok. ("Tharg promise he not eat running mate.")

9. Theocracy in Kelethin. ("Tunare demands that you pay her tribute by giving all your money to me then running across the town while drunk!")

8. Anarchy in Qeynos. ("Halt! Halt in the name of... of...")

7. Lethargy in Halas. ("Ahhh... it's too cold. Let them do what they want." I know, I know, it's not a form of government.)

6. Aristocracy in Erudin. ("Ok, only a small proportion of the residents of Odus can be aristocrats, so I'll be Duke and you two are everyone else.")

5. Plutocracy in Grobb. ("Me have more froggies th'n you. Me king.")

4. Military Dictatorship in Freeport. ("Now I'll keep the people under my control by only giving out fertile farm soil to my friends... Desert of Ro?")

3. Parlimentary Democracy in Neriak. ("And the House of Putridity moves that we adjourn. What say you, House of Corpses?" Voice from back of meetinghouse: "We want more pleasant names!")

2. Monarchy in Felwithe. ("... and furthermore, I want everyone in Greater Faydark to pay a tax of... there are HOW many of them?!?!??!")

1. Bradocracy in Norrath. (In the name of game balance, all classes will have only two skills, Poke and Die. But all GM characters, regardless of class, get the Fiery Avenger.)
