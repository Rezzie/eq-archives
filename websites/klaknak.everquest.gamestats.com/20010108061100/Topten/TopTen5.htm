Top Ten Reasons That You MIGHT Be A Twinker

10. Your biggest decision every morning is whether to wear your Green Splinted Platemail, your Bronze Platemail, or your Crafted Platemail.

9. At level 5, when you accidentally attacked the shopkeeper, you didn't die.

8. You still can't figure out how you're going to sell all those extra Ghoulbanes…

7. Seeing all those newbies running around in Cloth and Patchwork sickens you.

6. You start making a habit of equipping Gold Pieces as a Throwing weapon.

5. You begin to wish Brad would stop asking you for extra items.

4. When your party attacked Lady Vox, she started to run away.

3. You're beginning to wonder if you can melt down all that Platinum to create a new type of armor.

2. You have a good win/loss record at duels with opponents 30 levels higher than you.

1. On the PvP server, you killed some guy named Aradune, and now wonder why the GMs won't leave you alone.
