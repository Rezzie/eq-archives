One Bad Skelly

by Greg H.


Okay, bear with me. I wish I could have lived for the screenshot to this one!

Like so many others I decided to make an Iksar as soon as I got my disk (a week late, but who's counting? After all, Ultima 9 was 4 months late!). I'm a casual player who's gotten a ways up the tree, so it's been a while since things as small as scorpions were a problem, but I got back into the swing of newbiedome with relative ease.

So here I am, hunting scaly wolf pups (who always seem to attack in packs) and collecting scorpion claws when I see an Iksar skelly in battle with some lizzy monk. All the newbies are doing their "wow!" thing as the monk repeatedly smashes the undead in the low teens when they can hit for 6 with a splintered club. It looks like he's got it under control when:

(name withheld) yells for help from to the left and in front of you!

The monk starts shouting curses as everyone just stands there and watches him die. I had never seen a Scalebone Skeleton before, but apparently it was a monk, too! Now there are two dead monks, but one is standing over the other. A message pops up to explain the situation to him:

(name withheld) says, out of character, "that's what you get for powerleveling in a newbie zone!"

But that was only the beginning of this idiotic screw-up.

The Scalebone nails the nearest newbie, who starts running without sending out a shout or ooc (because he really WAS new)... the skelly kills him in a few blows. But there are over 90 new Iksar in the Field of Bone, and the Scalebone starts slaughtering them all as it follows its prey toward the zone!

This part was unreal... I laughed so hard I almost bust a gut, even though I had to die to experience it.

The Scalebone was now standing in the exact spot that newbies appear when they die! The guards just stood there as literally dozens of newbies died seconds after they appeared! I watched my screen in awe:

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

You have been slain by a Scalebone Skeleton!

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

You have been slain by a Scalebone Skeleton!

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

You have been slain by a Scalebone Skeleton!

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

You have been slain by a Scalebone Skeleton!

And so on...

I died at least 6 times, and the pile of bodies got so dense it must have caused the crash that took me out of the game! At least the Scalebone was gone when I rebooted!
