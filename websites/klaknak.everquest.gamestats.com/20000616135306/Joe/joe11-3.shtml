Asheron's Call: The Eternal Void

by the Cackling Klaknak

Overview: Turbine takes over the United States and immediately increases the nation's size to better approximate the size of a large galaxy. They then force all animals, pets, people and plants to clump together in threes. Tax incentives are given to those who clump around fires or corpses. Citizens are then given free reign to do whatever they want, but for those who seek power, they must get a PhD in Statistics and have a lust for ennui.

The new American philosophy: 2nd taper = [taperShift * (Talisman + (Herb + 3) + ((powder + potion) * 2) + (scarab - 2))] mod 12

The new American reality: People standing around in groups wondering how to most efficiently take a leak for maximum experiential gain.

The New American FAQ:

1. Do you have to pay taxes?

*** If they fit the maximum Eo experiential gain/loss matrix, sure.

2. What is it like to, say, go to the grocery store.

*** There is no need. Citizens sustain themselves by eating left over Excel spreadsheet printouts.

3. What is the nation's mascot?

*** Excel

4. What are some of the popular magazines and newspapers?

*** Wharton's Statistical Digest

5. What does a typical citizen do everyday?

*** Complete Chi Square problems in the morning paper

6. What do people do for fun?

*** Computing 3x3x3 ANOVA tables in their head

7. What is the economy like?

*** The world is so large, that most people never actually see a town or people outside of their clump. When not completing actuarial tables, the clump people trade clothing and small rocks.

8. What is the greatest threat to the Commonweal?

*** No one knowing where the fuck they are.

9. What does this society view as fundamental human rights?

*** The fundamental right to statistics.

10. Which real life society does the AC America most resemble?

*** Wall Street, a hardcore Magic the Gathering Geek Fest
