Shadowed Man

by Dathon, a.k.a. Leata Amberthorn, 21st Warrior of the Vale, Brell Serilis Server

After tanking a shadowed man at the Nektulos spawn, my poor Warrior had to sit and wait until nearly all her hit points slowly regen'ed. (A Necro was the sole partner in the group.) As such, we were very bored, and as a result of boredom-induced delirium, I came up with this parody of my favorite musician/singer, Billy Joel. Hope you like it...

(sung to the tune of Billy Joel's "Piano Man")

/time...

It's nine o'clock on a Saturday
The Nektulos camp is set.

Busy summoning his skeleton pet.

'And what kind of loot it will drop?'
'Is it no-rent or lore?'
'Cause that's really a chore'

'Well, I'm certain that they will con red to me,'
