Top Ten Ways to Tell if You Are a Master Brewer - by Steve S.

10. People are always asking you, "Are you okay?"

9. Every Monk in Norrath is now your best friend

8. You have fallen to your death in Kelethin more than twice.

7. You have increased your skill in Alcohol Tolerance (155)

6. You have ever been thrilled that you were able to get that Bind Affinity off.

5. You have ever paid for one of those blackburrow casks

4. You have ever awoken in Crushbone with your arm draped over a legionnaire and your greaves missing.

3. You've ever actually been inside an Inn or Tavern

2. You know what Koala Nuts are used for.

And the # 1 way to tell you are a master brewer is: 1. You have increased your skill in Brewery (100)
