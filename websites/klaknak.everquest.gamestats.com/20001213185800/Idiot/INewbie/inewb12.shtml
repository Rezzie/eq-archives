Ah, the noble magician - by Saelaan, 5th circle Magician, Prexus Server

I had just started a new high elf magician, after my old one's corpse (and all of his money, weapons, regs and armor) was lost due to having to log out. But that's another story.

I had just brought my magician up to level 4, and decided to hunt around Crushbone. Being in Gfay, I had no bags or boxes. Those stupid orcs are just too stingy with their bags. Uhh disregard the previous statement. Anyway, the pickings were good, and in no time, I had more loot than I could carry in my inventory. But since I was near the orc lift, I figured I could just run over, activate the lift, go up, drop the extra rusty spear, sell the rest of my loot, and then pick back up the spear and sell it. If only I had known that items dropped on lifts don't stay on.

I started up, thinking that the spear was right beside me. When I reached the platform, I noticed that the spear was gone! I reactivated the lift, and when I reached the forest floor, the spear was back. Thinking that I just missed the spear, I rereactivated the lift. Once again, the spear was gone. I got off the lift, and someone activated it and it went down. Then I noticed the spear on the forest floor. Thinking someone would take it, I leapt boldly down, and was hit for 65 damage, just short of my hitpoints.

I was unconcious on the lift, but I figured I would just wait until I regained conciousness and could pick up the spear. Then, I heard the grinding of the lifts gears as it began to rise. Throughout this, I was pressing the direction arrows franticly to get off the lift when I regined conciousness. By the time I regained conciousness, the lift was more than halfway up. once again, I boldy, and accidentally, leapt off the lift. This time, however, it was no mere unconciousness. I received the too familliar LOADING, PLEASE WAIT..

To make matters worse, when I got back to the lift, the spear was gone.
