The Quest

a poem

by Doug Erickson - Aaeleaa Sou'leiss - Lanys T'vyl

A tale is told of Moggok's Stein
A journey long and full of time.

To seek an Ogre by the name of Clurg
Beyond the swamps you'll hear his dirge.

Of a book he lost in a distant land
Near the ocean, in a city of man.

A book that's full of Exotic drinks
The Bartender's Compendium or so he thinks.

To Freeport you go to start this quest
Look in east and not in west.

To seek the man, Gregor by name
He'll tell you his tale, now you must play his game.

Four things he needs before he gives you the book
Across all Norrath is where you will look.

In the Ocean of Tears is the Kiola Nut
Sold by Sisters in a little grass hut.

Now on to Rivervale, to buy Honey Jum
Watch out for those Kneebiters, they're greedy bums.

Now look for Kizzie in her halfling home
With 30 gold pieces and three honey combs.

Give her the stuff, you'll get a Honey Jum jar
But you're not done yet, you still must go far.

Now on to Erudin you must travel with haste
A long journey ahead, there is no time to waste.

Across hills and cities, across a sea of despair
Watch out for the Kerrans, that like to dwell there.

When you reach Hawk's Inn, you will be out of breath
Just throw down some silver for Innoruuk's Kiss of Death.

To the Erudin Library you must take the drink
To Sinnkin Highbrow, on the top floor you think.

Give her the drink and Erud's Tonic you'll get
Now just one more item and then you'll be set.

Now back across Erud's Crossing you go
To the Temple of Life in the city Qeynos.

There you must kill a Koalindl Fish
And finding it's corpse is your fondest wish.

With caution and care, beware now your life
For you have just angered the God Rodcet Nife.

Now if you're smart, don't run back just gate
Unless you are anxious to meet your fate.

Go back to Freeport to the bar by the sea
And look for Gregor, to finish this deed.

Give all the items including the Jum
And Gregor will give you the Compendium.

With the quest almost over, you've but one place to go
Back down to Ogguk, Way south of Ro.

In search of the city, through the Feerott you run
A gloomy place with no light or sun.

You enter Ogguk and head to the bar
You can taste it, the end is not far.

You walk up to Clurg to show him your find
You give him the book, he says "Here is da Stein".
