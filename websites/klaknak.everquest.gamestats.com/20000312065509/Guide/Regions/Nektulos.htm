Nektulos Forest/Neriak

Summary: Nektulos Forest is the home of the Dark Elves. Woe to all who had the misfortune of starting their first EQ character here. Not only did I (CK) start my first character here, but he was also a cleric, so things were pretty rough.

Notable Creatures:

1. The Wandering Guards and Guardians - In real life, you can be bleeding from the groin in front of the police station, and the police are nowhere to be found. But if you forget to affix your annual "City Annum Ad Hoc Ad Valorum Tax" sticker to your car, you get a ticket every single day until you pay the city $2.50. This is pretty much how it works in Nektulos. If you are being pounded into meal by a young kodiak, the wandering guards are nowhere to be found. But if you are powergaming your way through a few skeletons, the guards swoop down on you and steal your kills. As I will note later, the paucity of critters in this area is bad enough without these nitwits stealing your supper.

2. Young Kodiaks - Nektulos is the least subtle newbie area in the game. If you chase after a snake, the odds are pretty good that you will end up being chased by a young kodiak. All these critters seem to intermingle with each other in the newbie areas and beyond.

3. The Undead - The undead are a prominent fixture in this area, especially around the heavily camped (duh) ruins.

4. Halflings - There are a couple halflings camps here. WARNING: The halflings will stomp you into powder if you are not strong enough. They are stronger than they look (or con).

 

The Nitty Gritty:

This zone sucks. Even the most hardcore roleplaying DE player must cringe at the paucity of creatures and the inexplicably three-zoned city.

 

The pros and the cons of Nektulos/Neriak:

Pros:

1. Compared to Kelethin, Freeport, Qeynos and other areas, Nektulos and Neriak are fairly empty. This means that the idiot spam level is mercifully low.

2. Neriak has a great deal to offer, for an evil city.

3. Lavastorm, a truly unusual place, is nearby.

 

Cons:

1. Why oh why does Neriak need three zones?

2. The critter per character ratio is horrible.

3. The guards steal your kills.

4. It is just too damn dark. It most resembles Kithicor forest.

5. The last time I was there, the number of available quests was terrible.
