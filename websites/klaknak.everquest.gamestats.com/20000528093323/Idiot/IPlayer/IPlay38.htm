The Drum - by Menwein

My Human Bard had just made level 8. The new song for this level was nowhere to be found in the Qeynos area, so I decided to move on and see more of the world. I crossed Anatonica with a couple different travel partners. We moved fairly quickly using the Bard song along with a drum. The first partner I had stopped I asked to fight a mist wolf in North Karana. As soon as it became clear that this was a real bad idea, he told me to run to the guards at the tower. Of course the guards where not there, but I ran to the tower. The Wolf ate well on level 7 Monk that day.

I made it to the Stone bridge and hooked up with a new travel partner (The Monk needed to log off and was just happy that one of us made it). My new partner knew the way, so off we went. The plan was "don't stop running for anything". As we ran up along the river, he stopped to ask some higher level people if you could swim accross and take a short cut around HighPass Hold. As soon as he stopped, all of the raiders by the river attacked him. He was dead before I could say RUN! I made it to the wooden bridge and zoned through. I sat there and waited for him. He ran from Qeynos and looted his corpse. We met up again on the bridge on the Eastern Karana side. We decided to wait until daylight to make a run for High Pass Hold. I went AFK for a minute, and when I came back, I was in North Karana with only a few hit points of health. Someone had trained a Griffawn to the bridge. Apparently I was so close to the zone that the Giffawn knocked be into it. We healed in North Karana then continued on.

The rest of the trip was uneventfull (barring getting lost in Highpass). When we reached Freeport, we went out seperate ways. I restocked food and drink and caught the boat to Faydwer. After getting some general directions, I made my way to the Bard guild in Kelethin. I purchased my new song and scribed it. Then I thought "as long as I am here, I may as well see what kind of quests there are". The only quest that sounded like it had promise was to get some spider silks for one of the local Bards. I decided to go for it. It didn't take long before I was hopelessly lost in the forest of greater Faydark. As luck would have it, I stumbled on a zone.

I entered Lesser Faydark and started moveing along the zone wall (to my right). After a bit, I came to a building of sorts. I highlight the guy standing there and it is a Brownie Merchant. Cool! I need to sell and buy food and drink. I approach the merchant:

A Brownie guard begins to cast

A Brownie Merchant begins to cast

YOU are hit for over half of you total HP

YOU are hit for 54 points of damage

A Brownie Guard hits you for 38 points of damage

LOADING...PLEASE WAIT

I am back in Qeynos. Naked, copperless, and no food or drink. I bash enough low level critters to get 4 each food and drink and a new drum. I set off again. This time alone. I am tired of people dieing around me. I make it all the way to High Pass Hold. I get lost. I take a wrong turn. I die (quick). Back in Qeynos, repeat the bare handed vermin slaughter to get enough change for food and drink (skip the drum). I set off again. I make it to High hold and ask for an escort through. Some kind sole shows me the way. I run to freeport and get a bind at the dock. Beg for food and drink ( I ran out), and tell my sad story. A level 7 Wizard offers to help. I decline, no sense in getting us both killed. I get to Lesser Faydark and attempt to recover my corpse. It took me 6 or more attempts, but I did finally get it. Including the one I abandoned in High Pass, I had 5 corpse at one time. I did learn to /CON everything.

Menwein Lagras Human Bard of 27 seasons.
