How many times have YOU, the newbie, said this?

Well, Verant understands. We're very good at doing things for newbies to screw high level players, so let's begin with what this patch will add. Eating is now a much more viable skill, much like Sense Heading. You can now improve your "Eat" level, at which time you will be better at eating in an incomprehensible way, much like when you improve your Tracking skill. To Eat, which everyone must do or else you will not do any damage, your mana bar will never return, we'll charge you more every month, and you will get no loot from monsters, you must get several items you must keep on you at all times:

-Picnic Kit

-Plastic Utensils

-Napkins

-Plastic Cup (Dwarves may use Shotglasses)

-Disinfectant Wipies (Even Bertoxxulous followers)

-Little Hard To Open Sauce Packet

Once you have all of these, which must be purchased from potters, smiths (with a new quest involving Jum Jum Stalks), jewelers, tailors, and alchemists and will all be items that even at skill of 200 in the trade skill will only succeed once in fifty tries, or you cannot eat.

To make a Picnic Kit, you must buy a Paisley Cloth from a tailor first. Paisley Cloths are made from Blackburrow Gnoll Skins and Evil Eye Optic Nerves. Then, you must buy a Picnic Basket and a Picnic Handle from a smith. The Basket is made from a Jum Jum Stalk and the Handle is made from a Jum Jum Stalk. Combine these together to get a Picnic Kit.

That is one example of how to get the items. Now, you must get a baker to sell you three square meals. Here is an example of a mix of vegetarian and omnivorous breakfast foods, which provide you with a brief period of flatulence every time you consume them by right-clicking the picnic kit when your character gets whiny and hungry.

Breakfast (Consumed between 4 AM and 9 AM game time)

Example

-20 Tasty Snake Eggs (Found only on the sea serpent in Lake Rathe)

-3 Kobold Hinds (found only on kobold shaman in Nagafen's Lair)

-Two ripe foraged oranges (Found only in the Feerrott when foraging)

-Treant Bark (Found only on Xanusus or whoever, Hook named him, don't bug me)

-Miniature Oven (Found only on saccharine commercials for little girls)

Combine these to get a Kobold Bacon, Poached Egg, Orange Juice, Bark Toast Breakfast. Once you've eaten your three square meals, your character will sometimes grab his stomach and hunch over, usually after going down the ramp in East Commonlands with Spirit of Cheetah on. If this happens, you must wait and watch while he vomits up the meal. Please do not turn Particles "Off" under options so we can make you lose your desire to eat a meatball stromboli.

We have added a new trade skill to go along with this, called "Toiletries" for after your character consumes too much food and MUST use the "Squat" skill, learnable only by doing the Aradune quest for the Holy Mace in the Fear Plane. To use the new "Squat" skill, your character must find a quiet place, like an NPC household, and hit "Squat" when you get the message:

You are sickly.

ou are bloated.

You really need to go.

And your character starts dancing around on screen uncomfortably. After you finish, you consume one to four Toilet Papers and one to four Disinfectant Wipies [Druids and Rangers can use Foraged leaves], depending on how long you held it. If you don't have Toilet Paper (which looks like Bandages and can be used as them to lesser effect, but not vice versa), you will lose 50 CHA and 130 AGI until you buy some. If you have no Disinfectant Wipies, you will contract the Necromancer's highest level disease spell and it cannot be cured until you buy Disinfectant Wipies, so the Clerics, Druids, and Shamans don't mess up the economy by curing you. You will usually get the message during dangerous fights with enemies, and when you can't use the command because you're out in the open. Also, if you wait more than four minutes after getting the message, you are informed:

You are obviously mentally incompetent!

You need a change of clothes!

At which point your Waist and Leg armor, and any Robe you have equipped, will become "Soiled Mess" armor, and has AC 0, Weight 56.8, and CHA -450.

That's all for this patch message. Next patch we'll be adding the new lanuage, "Ye Olde Sign Language", which, unlike many players believe, will NOT include the /gesture command.
