"Below is a version I wrote thinking about what it would be like for trolls
at christmas time. Consider this submitted by Gildhar (from Solusek Ro
server)."

- by Gildhar (Solusek Ro server)

 

On da furst day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
a reelee reelee gud bashin stick

On da sekund day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
too gobbies ded
and a reelee reelee gud bashin stick

On da thurd day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
three trips to guk
too gobbies ded
and a reelee reelee gud bashin stick

On da forth day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
for froggies crushed
three trips to guk
too gobbies ded
and a reelee reelee gud bashin stick

On da fif day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
fyv shinee coins
for froggies crushed
three trips to guk
too gobbies ded
and a reelee reelee gud bashin stick

On da sixf day ov Kristmus, mah trollie gayv ta me
six bodees layin

for froggies crushed

too gobbies ded
and a reelee reelee gud bashin stick
