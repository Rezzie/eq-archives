The worst high level idiot story - by Ailisha, high elven magician

81 people in Sol B, 92 in Lguk including those waiting. I decide to head over the the OOT to see if the seafuries are camped, three groups of casters and a waiting list. Hmmmmm well guess the last thing to try is the butcherblock dwarves.

I arrive at butcherblock, two druids kiting the four "Kaladim Citizens" dwarves at the docks (I found this out when I took one only for a druid to unhide and tell me he's pacing the spawns). I sigh and head over to the fishing village. I counted TWELVE people waiting on three dwarf spawns. Mostly druids, a few tanks and one magician. With twelve people and three spawns (not including the guy who gives bad guard faction) the dwarves are no longer great experience. There were also two guys sitting beside waiting on a list.

I head over to the stones, usually the least favourite place to kill dwarves since they have a long spawn time. I see a human necro there. GREAT! we can share. She seems to be LD so I take one with no problem, killed it in about two shots. Pet barely scratched. The necro comes back just as the other one pops, she takes it on but half way through she goes linkdead again. I finish it off. All this time the dwarves have been fairly easy to beat (blue to 45).

We chat for a bit when she comes back, then another one pops up. I take this one, then I lag. Now it gets worse. When my packet loss drops from 70% to 20 ish, I see "You do not have a pet to command", half my hps gone and a very angry dwarf pummelling me. Necro is linkdead *sigh*. I try casting on it but my spells are interrupted, as I start making a run the necro comes back and starts blasting it, it kills me with a sliver of health then kills the necro (as she'd lost her pet).

I am bound in FREEPORT! I am also KOS in freeport. I bound right next to the fisherman in Freeport so I quickly invis myself and walk back to the docks, sit down and begin waiting for the boat. From this whole experience I've realised that guards and NPC's will see your invis if you're sitting down. Anyhow all of a sudden guards and olunea militin start attacking me, I jump into the water and hit /q (since the guards take the long way around) and avert death.

I relog in, invis myself, swim back to shore and repeat the process. Sure enough the guards get me again as I sit down. Repeat, I avert death again. I am now down to a quarter health. I limp back to the fisherman, invis again, then sit down under the docks. I hear guards shouting. Then all four are suddenly in front of me. I hit /q. When I come back, I am level 44. *sigh*. After logging in as a newbie and asking a huge favour from a friend to port me out, I finally collect my corpse. The necro is still there and we decided we want revenge.

Revenge! Necro positions herself behind the dwarf on the right and I in front of the one one the left. We start casting, I fizzle and she pulls both out of range from my spells. My pet is still on "/pet sit" and has not followed me. After retrieving my pet and sending it to attack the dwarves, I see the necro feign but it fails on one of the dwarves which attacks her. She dies. I gate. After corpse recovery, we try again. This time she positions herself to the side. She kites one away and I focus on one. My pet dies again (this has never happened before tonight). We die shortly after.

We both log out from frustration (I am now two thirds of a bub from 45) and I come back several hours later and kill dwarves for the whole night. With the help of an enchanter friend and another to refresh our sows, I regain all of my lost experience. I gate back to Felwithe and sell all the loot, a newbie I'd given bronze armor to wants me to duel him so he can twink his enchanter (with cloth). Anyhow we go up to a room in the armor shop beside the bank. I forget that there are thick patrols of guards outside and in their eyes, there are no such things as walls. Somehow we managed to duel long enough for me to kill him with my pitiful 1h blunt skill. The minute he dies, guards burst into the room and start hacking away at me, I try to escape but I'm met with an entire regiment at the door. I die.

I am cut down in my own city, and have lost half the experience for the whole night. I am now barely hanging onto 45, feeling sick and shouting feebly for an exp res in Felwithe. After a few minutes of this I am tired and log out. I con some merchants and the banker as I logout, they con apprehensive. WHEN did this happen?? I have a sneaking suspicion I'll be KOS in my own city soon.

I think you can all gather the reason why if I'll be away for quite a long while :(
