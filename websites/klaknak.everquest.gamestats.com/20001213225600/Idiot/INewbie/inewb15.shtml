Stoopid Troll Trick

by Grist the troll

Ok, here's one for 'stupid newbie tricks'.

When my troll was a lowly level 14 my group suggested we go to Runnyeye and kill goblins. Now I wanted to stay with the group, but they were going through Rivervale. "It's ok" they assured me, "we'll invis you". This sounded like a grand idea and I had never been invisible before, so off we went. Right before zoning into Rivervale the Mage cast invis on me and sure enough the halflings all left me alone. Before zoning into Misty Thicket I conned a halfling guard and immediately told my group "Hey da gards iz indiffrent, yu kan go ahed and bind Gristie here". Well needless to say, the first time I died in Runnyeye I appeared in the Rivervale tunnel (no longer invisible) and all the guards came running. By the time some from my guild arrived to rescue me I was a lowly level 12 troll.

Some in my guild still remind me occasionally of my 'stoopid troll trick' hehehe.
