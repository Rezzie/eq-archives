Verant’s New Warning Labels on Alcoholic Beverages

by Cyanna Thunderhammer

Some may call it a nerf, but Verant is now putting warning labels on alcoholic beverages. So now when you are slamming down short beers, working on the otherwise useless skill of Alcohol Tolerance, you can’t say you’ve not been warned!

1. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you think you are speaking in tells when you are not.

2. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may cause you to thay shings likethish.

3. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may leave you wondering what the hell happened to your greaves.

4. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may create the illusion that you can solo a hill giant.

5. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may lead you to believe you are invisible.

6. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may lead you to think people are laughing WITH you.

7. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may cause an influx in the time-space continuum, whereby small (and sometimes large) gaps of time may seem to disappear.

8. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you wonder how you got back to your bind point and where the hell are your clothes.

9. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you think you can fly.

10. WARNING: not to be imbibed within the Kelethin city limits.

11. WARNING: not to be imbibed while on the boat unless you want a long swim ahead of you.

12. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you believe you are no longer KOS in Neriak.

13. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you wonder why you keep casting spells on a monster, and your hit points are dropping like a rock and the monster is in perfect health.

14. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may make you attack monsters with a musical instrument or a fishing pole.

15. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may cause you to tell the same boring story over and over again until your group mates want to SMASH YOUR HEAD IN.

16. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may lead you to believe that guildmates are really dying for you to tell them about the problems with your RL girlfriend.

17. WARNING: consumption of alcohol may cause you tell total strangers (usually of the same sex) how much you love them.
