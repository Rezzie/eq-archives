Smiting Smokey - by Beejay Soulmender

I was soloing my 20th level Cleric on VZ in the Qeynos Hills area. I had been fighting a Rabid Grizzly and my health was running low. Having FM before the fight, I decided it was time for a quick breather. Cast Root on the Griz. Took a few steps back and popped off a couple heals. Decide that the Griz needs a little softening up, so I cast Smite. Suddenly, I was bathed in red light and my life bar dropped drastically. Oh shit, I'm being PKed. Time to drop this bear. Smite! Damn, they are lighting me up! Smite! I don't think I'm going to make it! Root breaks and the Griz comes over and chews my face off in one hit. As my body falls, I look down at the text part of the screen and see, in red "It would be more effective if you attacked someone other than yourself."
