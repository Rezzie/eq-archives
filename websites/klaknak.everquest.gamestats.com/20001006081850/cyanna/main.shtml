I have a friend who is recently married who we will call ‘Andrew’. Before he was married he was a very wild part guy. He went out with a lot of women and during his dating ‘slumps’ he would fill the time with professional help, if you know what I mean. After he got married, he and his wife moved close to where we live and we all started going out a lot. He said he told his wife about all the women he dated but that he did not tell her about the prostitutes and made me swear never to tell too. It has been very hard to keep this secret since I have gotten very close to his wife and consider her a good friend.

Last month we all went to the zoo together. They even had a giant stuffed elephant butt. His wife looked at the price tag and she could not believe that it cost 200 dollars. Before I could stop myself I said ‘That’s nothing. Andrew has paid more than that for a piece of ass.’

For those of you who visit the Official Everquest messageboards, you will notice that they are filled with horror stories of abusive GM’s. This is not one of those stories.

The other day my group was hunting in SolA killing goblins and gnome clockworks (sorry fellow nuggets). Some of the clockworks do a force shock that can throw you around. Since we were fighting in an area surrounded by lava, this is not a good thing. After the first time I got throw into molten lava, we decided it best if the tanks had levitate on them. The very next battle, I was hit by a force shock. My screen went all screwy and I started getting messages that I was being killed by molten lava. I tried to move, but I could not. My friends cast 5 greater heals but none of them affected me. In short order I died. I talked to my friends as I started my run back and they all said, I never left the platform and that they could all see me just standing there but no spells worked on me. Being that I am in a hell level and the exp I lost was many hours worth of hunting, I petitioned a GM. I had heard many horror stories so I was all set to be angry. In less than 5 min, I got a response from a GM, Sparrowhawk to be exact. He talked to my party members that were on the scene and saw that I died from lava but my body was lying on solid ground. In less than 5 more minutes, I got a res. He was friendly, fast, and nice. In all, I died from a bug and was back fighting with my group in about 15 min. I want to say thanks, Sparrowhawk, you are a good guy!
