Top 10 things that will be different in EQ2

by Cyanna Thunderhammer


10. Tanks can only equip a weapon if they are in a group

9. Casters (except druids and necros) can only cast a spell if they are in a group

8. Prices remain the same but all monsters only drop copper, rusty weapons, or cloth armor.

7. When you die, you go back to being first level

6. Non-casters cannot bind anywhere. When they die they respawn in the Plane of Fear

5. All mobs con green but can still kick your ass

4. All mobs have 8 hour spawns

3. Every magic item in the game is dropped off a named 100th level dragon.

2.All spell scrolls are dropped and are considered artifacts (only 1 per server)

1. The Vision ™ will be different. Oh, wait. No it won’t.
