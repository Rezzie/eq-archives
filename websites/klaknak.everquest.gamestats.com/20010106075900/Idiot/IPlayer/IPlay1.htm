G-String - by James S.

Happily hunting in Blackburrow with 2 other male companions, we are just about to make the climb to the snake pit when a streaker comes zipping past us. 'A streaker?!?!', I thought - not in EQ! So I turn around in time to see a half-naked elf running towards the bridge. Just as I am thinking 'Damn, that elf looks like she has a g-string on', the leader of our group says, 'Damn. Did you see that?'. Not liking to slip out of character too often, I merely reply with 'I can't believe they would program a chararcter to look like that!'

Then our mighty leader decides that he is going to ask the elf if she needs help. So running up to her, we start to describe her to each other in that oh so delicate way that we do when it's just 'the guys'. Being completely sucked into this little gawking episode, I run around behind her to see if my first impression was correct. Yep, looks like she has a g-string on.

Being that I have such a firm grasp of the english language and wishing to share my thoughts with my companions, I begin to type 'I dig her ass'. I finish typing the second 's' and just as my pinky finger lashes out to strike the 'enter' key - thus making my eloquent thoughts known only to those privileged enough to be in my group - I see the message 'Soandso joins the group' (Soandso being this remarkably garb female elf). I then notice - much to my dismay - that somehow, the message telling me that 'Soandso had joined the group', appears right above my descriptive message.

Immediately after noticing this detail, I get a /tell from the leader of the group saying 'Dude, she's in our group!' The next thing I see is a group message from Soandso - 'I'm not a sex object'.

Oh man!!!, says I. But always quick on my feet, I react with blinding speed.

I turn tail and run.

As I am running, I hit the disband key - convinced that I will soon get a message from one of the local Gm's that I am going to be kicked off the server. Well, the message doesn't appear, I'm still here, and my face is still red. I send about 30 /tell's to Soandso, apologizing profusely for my inappropriate behaviour.

Eventually I crawl back the group and throw myself into the task of finding her corpse - desperate to redeem myself. We never did find her corpse because about that time a train of about 15 gnolls comes blazing through the area we are standing in and unceremoniously slaughters everyone but me - I jumped in the water.
