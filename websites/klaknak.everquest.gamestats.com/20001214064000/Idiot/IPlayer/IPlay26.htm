Death by Drowning - by Debranna

I went to Kaladim to meet some friends. Being most enthusiastic at being in this odd city for the first time, I began running around and just killing time while they did whatever quest it was they were doing. I tried to walk the edge of the little water hole in the main part of the town. Low and behold I fell in. Well, guess what? Dummy me never learned to swim, so I promptly panic as my char begins to drown as my friends look on. I had to consent one of my friends to go down and get my things I was so upset by it all. I am now 33 and leader of a large guild, but they never let me forget that I too had humble beginnings and can drown in a couple feet of water in a fountain in the middle of Kaladim.
