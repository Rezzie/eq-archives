Ten More Ways Things Would Be Different If the World Was Everquest - by Phaethon

10. If you ever dropped anything but culinary implements, it would turn into a bag.

9. Esperanto would be replaced by "Common"

8. No one would ever fidget, only do brief actions then resume ever-so-slightly swinging their arms.

7. A brief look over their head would tell you who any stranger was.

6. Vishnu and Jesus would be ousted in favor of Karana and Tunare.

5. No more phones, you just /tell the person. (you have to say 10-10-321 first, though)

4. The only occupation that makes money will be mugging innocent people (ok, not that different)

3. Before you could hit anyone, they would have to agree to it.

2. No new technology until the next patch.

1. No sex, only talking about it.
