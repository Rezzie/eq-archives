Swimming Newbie, Idiot Newbie - by Gabriel

Well I must say that I have had my share of mistakes just like any other but have yet to hear of my very first newbie mistake from any other. I LOVE magic,....and I LOVE being smart. So of course I enter my new world as an Erudite Wizard. Oh yeah, gotta love it. Wander, battle, die, die, die, did I mention die. FINALLY level 4 (gasp) is reached and suddenly I am a mighty caster of magic. Obviously since fire beetles have lost there challenge and no one could possibly kill a Kerran I must need to relocate.

Fazzarya says out of character: How do I get off this island and get to the mainland??

(Anonymous) tells you: You need to go across the water dude.

You tell (Anonymous): Ok thanks man, which water?

(Anonymous) tells you: The docks in Erudin. Good Luck.

You tell (Anonymous): Alright, thanks alot

Hell, I know where those docks are...off I go. On arrival I realize that I cannot see the mainland. Wow... this game really is huge. HAha... I have swam to Kerra island many a time (mostly to retrieve dead bodies) so this should be cake. Off I go like the next olympic freestyle gold medal winner. Swim, swim, swim, swim, swim, damn this place is a long way off. I probably should stop and take some bearings. Do a quick 180....wait, or was that a 270.... had to been a full 360 degree turn. Swim, swim, swim, swim, swim. Crap, where the hell am I. Needless to say, I did not want to lose all my earthly possesions so I spent approximately 1 and a 1/2 hours swimming around looking for Erudin. Stupid boat. Stupid Wizard...137 intelligence my ass.
