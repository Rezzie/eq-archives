A Date

For many of us, the dating world is divided into two categories: the real world and the fantasy world. Sure, a lucky few have been known to meet that certain special someone with whom they can babble incoherently and not drive them off the cliffs of sanity, but for most of us, the world is not so rosy.

But even for these lucky few, for every one or two good dates, there are a bazillion bad ones, and for this reason, a date has little chance of tearing anyone from Everquest.

First, let's look at the fantasy date.

Fantasy Date:

Odds of actual occurrence: 1:1009849034913242.

Odds date will lead to sex: 1:1

Maude: Thank you for inviting me out to eat at The White Spot 24 hour diner. I too will have the Fried Egg Burger.

Todd: Excellent choice. The cook tonight is excellent. He cooks the egg just right, so that when you take a bite, the yolk perfectly mixes with the mayonaisse.

Maude: Yummm... (licks her fingers suggestively).

Todd: So, what do you like to do?

Maude: I like to drink beer, watch pornography, and play computer games.

Todd: I love you.

 

Now let's compare the fantasy date to a real date...
