The Nerd - by Cyanna Thunderhammer

I heard that they are creating a new class for EQ, The Nerd.

First, only humans can be nerds. Their primary stat is intelligence which is higher even than the erudites and their charisma scores make ogres and trolls seem like GQ cover models. They are limited to only hand-to-hand combat with a skill cap of 10, and can only wear cloth armor. They also get the tinkering skill. The nerd is a spell caster class similar to the enchanter. They have a similar skill set except they get dodge at first level (from all those years of dodging rocks, snowballs, and PE dodgeballs). Due to their low charisma scores they get the full line of fear-type spells. When a nerd approaches and starts to talk about their social life, stamp collecting, and who was the best star trek captain, most mobs and players run for the zone. They also have the skill of technobabble, which is similar to the enchanter line of lull and mesmerize spells. Just let a nerd start talking about networks, operating systems, and programming and not even the most well rested mob can stay awake.

In addition the nerd will have their own set of quest armor. They are:

  1. Taped black glasses - Face slot. +2 int, -2 dex, -2 agi (from constantly having to push them back up) Effect of Squint (similar to the mage series of glimpse spells). Wt. 0.1. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only.
  2. Ugly tie - Neck slot. AC 2, -2 cha, Wt. 0.1. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only. Plaid shirt - Chest slot. AC 4, -2 cha, Effect of Pocket Protector (acts as a small 4 slot container), Wt. 0.5. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only.
  3. 'Kick me' sign - Back slot. Effect of Aggro Mob, Wt. 0.1. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only. Calculator digital watch - Wrist slot, AC 1, +5 int, magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only.
  4. Highwater Pants - Leg slot. AC 4, +2 agility, +2 dex (no cuffs to trip over), -4 cha, Wt. 0.5. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only.
  5. White socks - Foot slot. AC 1, Effect of lightstone (they all but glow when worn with black pants and shoes), Wt. 0.1. magic, lore, no drop. Nerd only.

Verant was also going to put in a special nerd only quest called "Get Date" but the quest is currently broken.
