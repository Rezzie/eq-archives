I'd hate to be that gu...

by Silvertalon Drakesmere

Ok this happened quite a while ago, but after reading you other stories, i feel it has to be told. I am Silvertalon a ranger on the Karana server. This happened to me whe i was lvl 16.

Our group of adventure seeking souls set out from Freeport, we had Koshirae, lvl 16 paladin, Tooley lvl 15 Enchanter, Gryvon lvl 17 Ranger, and your truly. it was a good croc hunting group we had. we always had someone watching for the SGs so we could dive out of the aggro range or simply run away. Now both Gryvon and I were pulling for the group, and dooing pretty well in spacing out the pulls. (heck if we got 2 at once tooley would have a pet for a while)

We had just finished off a Deepwater Caiman when Tooley says "SG MOVE" so of course i'm looking around like a nitwit, and see 2 SG's chasing some poor fellow fairly close. I think "ok, that was the SG, it's aggroed on someone else we are fine." I begin to message the group "LOL i'd hate to be that gu...."

A Sand Giant hits YOU for 96 points of damage
A Sand Giant hits YOU for 83 points of damage

So like an idiot, i panic, where do i run? DIVE DIVE..into the ocean (aka SG wading pool)..

A Sand Giant hits YOU for 96 points of damage.

repeat as necessary

LOADING PLEASE WAIT..

needless to say my group got a laugh out of my "LOL I'd hate to be that guy". I believe Tooley's comment was.."Damn Silver, he got you mid laugh"

my humble submission for the idiot files :) Silvertalon Drakesmere (now lvl 30 Ranger of Karana)
