Oasis

Summary: If you are a character roughly 13-100 (or higher), Oasis is the place to go. It is a powergamer's delight, but the sand giants make it extremely dangerous. I love this place even though I am not really a powergamer.

Notable Creatures:

1. Sand Giants: There are two breeds of Sand Giants. They are both identical in power, but they differ in travelling patterns: (a) The northern sand giants - Many players like to sit on the zone edge between Northern Ro and Oasis picking off the various orcs that filter up from the orc camp to the south. Some days, everything is fine. Other days, the Sand Giants simply will not leave this area. My guess is that a giant just randomly wanders into this area, finds tons of PCs to kill, so never leaves. If this is the case, you might want to hunt in Northern Ro for a while; (b) the southern sand giants - they basically patrol the entire southern region with a cool, methodical vengeance. I honestly feel that hunting here is without SOW is suicide.

2. Madmen - Again, there are two types: (a) northern madmen - you seem to get a fair mix of spellcasters and nonspellcasters in the northern area. (b) southern madmen - maybe it was just bad luck, but it seems to me that all of the southern madmen are spell casters here and they are very plentiful. In case you haven't run into a spellcasting madman before, he can con green and still give you a really hard time unless you have unusually high spell resistance or you are a powerful warrior.

3. Spectres = death.

 

The Nitty Gritty:

This is the penultimate powergaming adrenaline zone. The critters are plentiful (near camps and near the central oasis) and danger is high.
