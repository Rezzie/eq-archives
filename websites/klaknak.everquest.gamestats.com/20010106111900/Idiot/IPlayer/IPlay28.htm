Loot Corpse - by Pallean

I am a level 12 Erudite Enchanter named Pallean.

I had been wait four levels to get Illusion Dark Elf so I could buy the Enchant Silver spell from the depths of Neriak. I made the trek from Erud's Crossing to East Commons in three hours, fighting through terrible refresh-rate lag and without SoW. Anyway, I had planned to bind outside Nektulos in East commons near the border. In my haste, I zoned without binding. Since the refresh lag was so bad, I did'nt re-zone, which would've taken forever, so I bound close to the border in Nek and went on my merry way. Anyway, I passed the guards outside with no problem and zoned into the Foreign Quarter and headed to the guard complex just inside when I hear:

L'Crit says 'Take your blasphemy to the next realm!'

L'Crit says 'Die like the fool you are!'

L'Crit slashes YOU for 120 damage!

L'Crit kicks YOU for 39 damage!

You writhe in the grip of agony.

LOADING PLEASE WAIT...

I came to my bind point in Nek, and cursed myself. I had forgotten to recast the illusion. But my tale of woe doesn't end there, that was only the beginning. I was not willing to risk another attempt with Illusion, so I /consented my 6th level DE rouge, Spyrokeet, to loot the corpse and buy the spell. I looted it, but I had to leave my 2 no-drops to loot with Pallean. I used my rouge to drag the corpse near the zone, and then bought the spell. I then took Spyrokeet out to E. Commons with the spell and most of the equipment and suicided by jumping off a cliff. I consented,camped and logged to Pallean. Unfortunately, the corpse was stuck in the cliff and I couldn't target it to summon. I logged back to Spyrokeet and /corpsed it into the open and relogged.

After I finished looting, I needed to return to Neriak to recover the no-drops. I brought up my illusions and entered Neriak, then I looted my corpse and gated to my bind point in Nektulos. I gated into a guard train, whup-dee-friggin doo. To make a long and painful story short, I died no less than 7 times, and because the guard hung around killing me as I spawned and not returning to their posts, I got 5 or 6 others killed and at least one other person twice.
