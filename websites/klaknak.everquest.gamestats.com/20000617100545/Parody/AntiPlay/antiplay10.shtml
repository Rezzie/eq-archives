You might be an EQ redneck if...

by Cyanna Thunderhammer

Your class armor is camouflage colored

You have a stuffed gnoll pup somewhere in your house

You petition to get ‘Bob’ as a second first name at level 10 (Joe Bob, Jim Bob, Billy Bob)

Your pets eat at the dinner table with you

Your AE DOTs involve baked beans and pulling your finger

You’ve ever eaten klaknak and think it tastes like chicken Ogres are offended by your hygiene

When you have an on-line wedding, your new spouse does not need to petition for a last name change

You think ‘Huntin’ and ‘campin’ are perfect times to work on your alcohol tolerance

Your bard can only play the banjo or harmonica

You have ever used your dragoon dirk to clean your ears

Your favorite Friday night activity is minotaur-tipping

You have ever cruised the Karanas looking for farm animals
