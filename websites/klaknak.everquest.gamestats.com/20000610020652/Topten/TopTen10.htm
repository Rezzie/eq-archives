Top 10 RL Exercises for Everquest - by Kellan

10) Add a 5 pound weight to your favorite drink-while-playing-EQ glass. If your beverage of preference is beer, make it a 10 pound weight.

9) Any time you need to go to the bathroom or fridge, run as fast as you can both ways.

8) See how many pushups you can do while meditating.

7) Attach the keyboard to the handles of an exercise bike. Pedal while you play.

6) Once you've mastered the previous, switch to a bike where the handles move back and forth.

5) Carry around as much weight as your character is at all times. Works particularly well if you make sure you get items of similar dimensions, like 8 bronze spears in a backpack. WARNING: not as effective for those who play monks.

4) Any time a bard sings a song, mosh around the room.

3) Set the screen resolution as high as possible, and the mouse sensitivity as low a possible. Conceivably would be even more effective with a heavier mouse, but I have yet to see the MS FS Mouse at CompUSA.

2) Mock practice your melee skills using garden implements. Your skills won't go up, but that's only because the air /cons green.

1) Use emotes constantly, and exuberantly act them out at home for added emphasis. Anyway, using your own review system, the Gym has about a half a Clak-nak's chance to get you away from EverQuest.

Don't waste your money. Get the computer, follow my suggestions, and you will be in the best shape of your life in no time flat. Good Travels, -Kellan, Solusek Ro.
