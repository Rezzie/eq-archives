Fallen in Befallen

by Michael


Once a few nights past I was sitting in my nice new land of WC (being a dark elf mage of lvl 13 I rather enjoyed it here). While sitting at the toll booth I saw a rather strange critter sitting near me. *click on creature* A werewolf. How nice.. I'll just go have a loo....
A werewolf attacks you more than you care to know about.
LOADING... PLEASE WAIT.
lucky i was bound near the toll booth. I wander up close and see my advisary..
/ooc Werewolf at t....
A werewolf attacks you again for more than he did last time.
LOADING... PLEASE WAIT
Needless to say, I hate werewolfs.

A night or two past I was a super mage at the age of 15 seasons and decided it was time to raid befallen. After several minutes of asking for partners I got a lvl 15 enchanter and 14 paladin to join the fun. We run part of the first lvl killing everything in sight without much of a problem. Ahhh, someone needs help.. NUKE NUKE.. dead mobs and we acquired a thankfull monk to our group. Feeling ever powerfull we press on into the dungeon. WHOA! where did the monk go..
Monk friend says: WTF!
Palidin: you dissappeared on us
Enchanter : what's going on?
my brilliant ass: I've heard tales of hole in this zone, maybe you fell into one..

Yeah that it. he fell into a hole. So, what to do, ahh I have a brilliant idea. Lets fall in the hole with him, quick trip down to next level.
Enchanter: you sure that's a good idea?
Palidin: You first!
Monk: come on down, it's nice =)
my brilliant ass: omw

2 secs later we are all in a room with no mobs.. but oooo. I'm a mage, and I had a pet upstairs. Wonder how he is doing
Taunting attacker master.. NOOOOOOOOO!!!
Every undead in befallen quickly joined us in this little room with only 1 exit. we managed to blast the first few critters as they came in but were soon overpowered by several ghouls and yel con'd skeles. After 2 more deaths each trying to get our corpses we finally found a nice cleric to help. Needless to say, I haven't been back.
