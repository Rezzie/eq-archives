Top 10 Best things about being a Warrior

by Cyanna Thunderhammer
(Note: I really do love being a warrior)


10. You never get lonely because soloing is not an option

9. No pesky feelings of guilt for being the only one to survive a bad pull

8. No need to camp warrior items since there are always plenty of friendly casters willing to sell them to you for thousands of plat

7. Never have the problem of choosing only 8 spells from your extensive list of useful and powerful spells

6. You can get a lot of reading and work around the house done while waiting hours for your hp to regen

5. The joy of getting beaten to a pulp while protecting some guy in a dress

4. The excitement of running through Nektulos without invis or sow

3. You get to meet a lot of people and form lasting friendships while constantly begging for binds

2. You really get to enjoy the spectacular graphics and design as you run through 10 zones to get your corpse

1. You get lots of exercise chasing monsters that are chasing your casters (Plus it is funny watching them run in circles screaming ‘Help! Get it off me!)
