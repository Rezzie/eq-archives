EQ Toys

I got a scoop on the new EQ toys that are being produced. So without further ado, here it is a list of the action figures under development.

There will be one line of toys featuring each of the classes of EQ. Consumers will pay a one time fee to purchase the figure but must pay Verant a monthly fee if they want to play with them. The initial figures come with lots of cool accessories like magic items and spells but each week, a Verant employee will come to your house and take back one of the accessories, until each figure is left with nothing. These limited edition collectables called “The Balanced” can be resold to the toy manufacturer for a fraction of the purchased price. This is good for the economy.

They are also creating a line of toy accessories but the manufacturer is only creating one of each item per month, and each item will go to only one designated store. The packages give the messages, “Can’t get enough of camping in Everquest? Experience the thrill of camping those special items in Real Life!” and “Watch out for Kill-Stealers!”

Special limited edition figures include:

  • The Cackling Klaknak – Pull the string and hear the famous phrase, “BWAHAHAHA”. Comes with a jumbo bottle of ether.
  • Aradune - Comes with all of the accessories of the class figures and includes a special feature, where if you squeeze him, he expels a blast of hot air.
  • Abashi – Includes ‘All customers are whiners’ t-shirt and special ‘exploding head’ effect.

They are also creating a line of EQ player toys featuring some of the popular players of EQ. They are:

  • The Hacker – Sold separately is an accessory pack including duplicates of all of the Hackers items plus lots of plat.
  • The Powergamer – Comes with a free calculator, stat sheet and a years supply of No-Doze.
  • The Roleplayer – Comes with a special voice chip that decodes speech to include lots of ‘thee’s and ‘thou’s.
  • The Kewl D00D – Pull the string and hear such famous phrases as ‘U SuX N3wB1e’ and ‘Give me ph@t l3wt B4 I PK U’.
  • The Newbie – Some assembly required, and instructions in Swahili.
  • The Nerd – Comes with a free personality and social life.

They are also coming out with some plush stuffed animals for the younger kids. Featured are gnoll pups, orc pawns, kobold runts, and goblin whelps.

“Everquest Action Figures. Get them before they show up on E-Bay!”
