EQ Lightbulb Jokes - by Dachannien

Q. How many Ogres does it take to change a lightbulb?

A. Two - one to eat the old lightbulb and one to barsh the new one into the socket.

***

Q. How many Gnomes does it take to change a lightbulb?

A. Just one, and you don't need a lightbulb - the glare off his head is bright enough as it is.

***

Q. How many Trolls does it take to change a lightbulb?

A. Three. One to get stuck in the doorway, one to get KOS'ed by the lightbulb merchant, and one to stand around and scratch his ass. Sure, the lightbulb never gets changed, but no troll is gonna figure out that you have to flip the switch to get light out of the thing.

***

Q. How many powergamers does it take to change a lightbulb?

A. Seventy-four. Four to change the lightbulb with a well-executed strategy, and seventy to /shout obscenities across the zone because the lightbulb doesn't burn out often enough.
