Top Ten Reasons to Drop Everything and Play Everquest - by Phaethon

10. Your wife has left you, you've been fired, the radiation from your monitor has given you cancer. What else do you have left? (This may just be me)

9. You just discovered you can't afford food, because the supermarket won't accept platinum pieces.

8. Your frail body and puny fists pale in comparison to a Monk's.

7. When Barbarians compliment your female Wood Elf's looks, it makes you feel... pretty. (Again, maybe just me)

6. You have forgotten the benefits of electricity, except in running Everquest.

5. You think that, since you're human, you only need to eat once a day, and then only muffins and milk; you can no longer summon the strength to walk away.

4. You know your bosses' character's name, and have repeatedly done things like telling him that Gnoll Commanders are weak, which is why they hide down there.

3. Your real body looks like a Troll's, but you play a buff Half Elf. (I actually look amazingly like one of the Half Elven faces)

2. You are hideously ugly and have no other social outlet (Okay, THIS one ain't me)

1. When you're not playing, you hear voices... strange voices... compelling you...
