And he didn't even get a toaster

by Sallender

Here is a little story I have to tell about my first and greatest and stupidest character ever.

(Based very closely on real events)

The character: Sallander, Cleric of Fennin Ro, Lvl 11

The Place: The dwarven city bank.

Enter Sallander.

Sallander: Hail banker, I wish to make a witdrawal.

Banker: Aye, do ye now? Allright, what will it be fur ya kind lord... A platinum piece mayhaps? Possibly two?

Sallander: Naye good Banker, I wish to withdraw ALL my holdings from thy bank.... My entire 12 platinum pieces....

Banker: Not a problem good lord, let me wrap up those platinum pieces fur thee.

Sallander: Oh hold on... did you say twelve? that doesnt sound very impressive.... Give me.... 120 Gold pieces.

Banker: Aye Sir, so be it.

Sallander: Actually, make that 1200 Silver pieces... or better yet... 12,000 of your finest shiny copper pieces!

Banker: Not a problem Sir. Here you go.

[you are encumbered]

Enter a strange person who I had not noticed standing next to me.

Stranger: Sallander... I just wanted to touch you.

Sallander: What? what do you mean?

Stranger: *silence*

Sallander: Hey! what did you do? why am I encumbered? What did you do to me?

Stranger: *silence*

Sallander: I cant move! Help! hey! come back! help! Sallander (having never been encumbered before) decides that strange person must have cast some sort of weird spell on him and decides to gate back to his bind to protect himself.

[Gate]

[Loading]

Sallander arrives on his bind, tries to move [you are encumbered]

Narrator: Thus ends the scene of Sallander the cleric, Encumbered to the point where he could no longer walk even one step he was forced to drop 8 pp in copper on the ground and hurry back to the bank to drop it (2 zones away) then rush back and..... uh oh... it was gone.... *sigh*
