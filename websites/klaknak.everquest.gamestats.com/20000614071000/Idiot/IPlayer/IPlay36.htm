The Drunk - by the Cackling Klaknak

A couple days ago, I had been through a living hell at work so I decided to go home and drink some wine to blow off some steam. Well, whether it was my alcohol gene in overdrive or just a total lack of will power, I ended up drinking a little more than I should have.

So I was sitting at home saying to myself:

"Shit. I am drunk. At home. By myself. How pathetic."

Desperate to make a little human contact, I decided to play a little Everquest. When I logged on, I decided to play my Halfling Thief whom I hadn't played in ages.

So I pull Fimble up and I notice he is in North Karanas. Fine, I thought, and I just started running in one direction.

Now, in real life, I zone out a lot. I am very much like an absent minded professor who kinda zones out and decides its a good idea to go to his car forgetting he is not wearing any pants.

But when I have been drinking, its far worse, and sure as hell, I zoned out. The next thing I knew, I was hearing these death noises and I snapped out of my trance to discover that Fimble was dead as a doorknob, killed by several lions. I had no idea where he was.

And I still don't. I woke up the next morning and tried to find the corpse but I soon gave up. If anyone on Rodcet Nife sees a little dead guy named Fimble, bury him for me. And call me the idiot of the day.
