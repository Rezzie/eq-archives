The Day I Unintentionally Killed All The Newbies - by Palatine

I guess we've all trained people before, but this is probably the worst thing that happened to me. I was in the open area of Everfrost Peaks and was attacked by an ice-boned skeleton. I don't know if this is still the case but an ice-boned skelly is very aggressive, attacking just about anyone within a certain radius of it. I didn't own any magic weapons, so I ran like hell to the outer guard area, just inside the labyrinth that divides Halas from the open fields of Everfrost (where the dungeon permafrost is). I don't know if this was a bug or anything, but the guard just stood there while the ice-boned skelly WHALED on me. I panicked and thought that maybe the guards at the entrance to the ramp to Halas would kill it, So I yelled "Ice boned skeleton to ramp!" and ran for it. I got about 50 game feet from the guards before the skelly killed me.

I spawned back in halas, swam the lake to Everfrost Peaks and was almost immediately surrounded by dead newbies. There were corpses everywhere! It looked like a UFO cult after judgement day! I guess this was a rare occasion when there were no high levels or twinks with magic weapons around. People were shouting and telling me that I was a jerk (which I was, I guess)! I finally had to log because it was getting so hot and I was sooo embarrassed!
