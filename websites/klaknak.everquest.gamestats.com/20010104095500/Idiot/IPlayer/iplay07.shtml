The Love Boat

by Silveranthelas

One day I decided to take the boat from Freeport so I waited for it to arrive for what seemed like a long time so I went to get a fishing pole and some bait to pass the time I go out the wrong door on accident. I see the boat pull up so I make a jump from one dock to another land right in the water started swimming over to the dock as the boat leaves. there went (30 minutes) wasted.

I finally get up on the dock and wait and start fishing (30 minutes) later the boat comes I walk up to it try to get on I figured someone must have been blocking the way so I walked a little to the left and what I fell into the water I didnt get on the boat I see the boat leave as Im getting up onto the dock (Sigh).

I fish again a few skill points and some fish later (30 minutes) I see the boat I RUN up to it and not knowing at the time that you have to jump to get on I go running and it pushes me off to the side. I get on the dock the boat already gone start fishing.

(30 minutes later) the boat comes I figured I would be smart and get a really big running start at it I start running (with SOW on) and go flying off the side into the water again (SIGH) I was not happy at this moment. I get back onto the dock the boat still there this time I had found the perfect way onto the dock by now I run for it and right before I reached it it started going I run right off the end of the dock into the water. I then shouted "Why cant I get onto the boat in FP?" Jhondoe tells you: you gotta jump.

I fished (30 muinutes later I see the boat I run jump up onto now what I noticed was a ledge. I kept running and went flying off I figured must have to jump twice. I didnt make it in time for a second attempt I started fishing.

(30 minutes) later I see the boat I jump onto the ledge then jump onto the boat.

(FEW) I figured my troubles where over. After about a (30 minute) boat ride I make it there but then me sitting and meditating and sense heading didnt realize this was my stop until a long time after then we zoned. (Sigh) ok I figured I would stay on the boat and ride it around (60 minutes) later me STANDING right by the edge waiting to get off well on my slow computer we went right past the dock while I was in the zone and were already almost into the next zone (Sigh).

I waited another (60 minutes) RIGHT BY THE EDGE WATCHING FOR WHEN IT SAID You have entered the Butcher Block Mountains. Right as I saw that I jumped off the boat and swam for the dock. I FINALLY shouted "I made it I made it home again home again after a 6 hour boat ride hour long boat ride I made it I made it". I got MANY questions that day about how did you ride the boat for 6 hours? This was all TOTALLY true even if it is stupid.
