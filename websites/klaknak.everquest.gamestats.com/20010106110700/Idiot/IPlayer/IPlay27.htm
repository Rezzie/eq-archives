Death by Fish and/or Mangler - by Kuri

Dear Cackling Klacknak, I'm another lady fan who truely adore's your wit and humor. So, I felt it necessary to express my love not in flowers, but in a sad story to share with you.

Now Im normally a native of Cazic Thule, but some friends from work asked me to join them on another server. Being the adventurous newbie sort (Read: innocently stupid) I thought Id try something new. Ohh, a hafling would be fun, and druids look neat! I'll try one of thoes.. Okay! Here I am in Rivervale, with my note and spells.. Oh I'll just put my spells in the book when I get to my guild.. I bet its just around this corner! (time passes) Hmm.. I know I've been around here before, and I havn't zoned yet, so the guild must be just around this -next- corner, right?

Oh look! There's a building I havn't seen before! Wow.. a wolf in the back.. but he's got a name, and isnt doing anything.. I know! Maybe its a high level druid with a nifty spell on! I'll go ask him where my guild is! Wolves like druids, right? Whats the worst that could happen! Eeep! Bad puppy! No biskit for you! Why are you attacking me? Lots? Hmm.. cons yellow.. better not try it.. Well Im in the city, one of these merchants or guards should leap to my aid any minuet.. yup.. any time now they will dash to my rescue.

Oh golly, better run for it.

Help! Brave higher level than me adventurers please save me from this mean puppydog impersonating a high level druid! Okay, no help there.. GUARDS! Look, lots of guards! They'll save me! Ack! Why wont anyone help me? Im nearly dead! AH! Salvation! I'll just jump in the water! Bet that nasty old wolf cant swim...

... wow.. a really big fish..

I didnt know they had these here.. I want a closer look!

LOADING.. PLEASE WAIT..

Aaa! Okay, no problem, just need to go back to the city, and fish my corps up out of the water. Im okay. Just find my guild and ZONING Ahh, back in Rive...

Uh oh.. puppy came to greet me.. I wonder if he's still..

LOADING.. PLEASE WAIT.. *sniffle*

Stupid wolfie. Who ever heard of wolves killing newbies like this? Okay, I'll just drop in the water and get the stuff from my body.. wonder what killed me down there anyway? Hey! That neat looking fish again! I'll just...

LOADING.. PLEASE WAIT..

*Sob* Stupid wolf! Stupid pirana.. wont let me get my corps, wont let me zone, *sniffle*. Im gonna lodge a complaint whenever I find that stupid guild.. lesse... /corps..

WHEW! Finally got it! yeay! My spells! Yeay! My.. where is it?! Where is my Guild note?! I turned off the computer and cried.

-Kuri
