Top Ten Everquest Spin-Offs - by Phaethon

10. The Late Show with Trumpy Irontoe: Trumpy making wisecracks with celebrity guests and a band. ("So I was walking on the docks last night, and some guy cuts my head off! Go figure. And here's our guest...")

9. The /con: An early-morning talk show starring an all-female cast, with Chanda Miller, Nerissa Clothespinner, Madame Serena, and Linaya Solwin.

8. Sabrinalla, the Teenage Wizard: A sitcom starring a newbie and two Level 50 wizards and an animatronic gnoll pup.

7. Buffe the Skeleton Slayer: A mid-level female cleric runs around buffeting things, and occasionally having affairs with undead creatures.

6. NWF: The Norrathian Wrestling Federation, consisting of ogres who /duel constantly. Occasionally will have guild wars when ratings are low.

5. Jeopard-e: U hav to answr questions abot stats. Only kevvl do0ds allowd.

4. Xenna, Warrior Guildmistress: A Level 50 female warrior is plagued by GMs as she roams the Karanas saving newbies.

3. Mighty Castin' Power Enchanters: Childrens' show in which enchanters battle giants, and, at the last possible second, cast illusions of incredibly powerful monsters.

2. Thargey: A lovable purple ogre. ("You love me, I love you... wait, which one go first?")

1. Boat Trek, Voyager: An elite group tries to go to Odus, but their ship gets lost. They spend their time trying to swim back, when they all have Sense Headings of 12.
