What if TV shows were based on EQ...? - by Terry F.

Farmed - A show about three KSing female wizards who sell their loot on EBay.

Seventh Heaven - Several groups of hardy adventurers try to tackle the planes. This series is a drama about their exploits (and multiple, horrible deaths).

Friends - A sitcom doomed to failure. Viewers constanly complain that 20 minutes is simply not enough.

Buffy the Vampire Slayer - Another drama, this time about a female paladin out to destroy the undead. Her name may be in violation of the naming policies though . . .

The X-Files - Eerie tales of strange occurrences in EQ and the pair of programmers who know that 'the bugs are out there'.

Diagnosis PK - A stirring series about players on the Zek servers. Who done it? Stay tuned to find out.

Walker, Norrath Ranger - Follow Walker, the human ranger, as he tries to right the wrongs of a corrupt society.

Greed - What happens when good items get randomed in a party and even those who can't use the item want in? Watch this (violent) game show to find out.

Norrath's Most Wanted - True stories of heinous KSers and ninja looters, and how YOU can bring them to justice.

FPPG Blue - Controversial drama about the Freeport paladin's guild and their war with the corrupt militia. Supposedly any almost naked wood elf females that show up will NOT be censored . . .
