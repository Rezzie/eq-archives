Kiter

(based on The Wizard, by Black Sabbath)

Cackling Klaknak

Misty morning, clouds in the sky

Without warning, the kiter runs by

Casting his shadow, weaving his spell

Funny clothes, tinkling bell

 

Never talking

Just keeps running

Spreading his magic

 

Sand Giant disappears

Griffawns worry when the kiter is near

He pisses everyone off

No one's happy when the kiter runs by

 

Never talking

Just keeps running

Spreading his magic

 

Sun is shining, clouds have gone by

All other players already have died

He has passed by, Sand Giant has fled

Left all the players feeling so dead

 

Never talking

Just keeps running

Spreading his magic
