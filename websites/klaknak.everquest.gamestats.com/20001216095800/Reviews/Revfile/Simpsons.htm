The Simpsons Reruns

Like Everquest, the Simpsons are not an entertainment product, they are an addictive substance that will destroy your life. Why is it so addictive? Let me tell you.

Homer Simpson: I want to be Homer. It is as simple as that. Addictive element: 9 (of 10).

Mr. Burns - Mr. Burns is very much modelled on every 1920's style tycoon stereotype you can think of (or, if you cant think of ... John Rockefeller.) His method of answering the phone ("Hoy? Hoy?") is something to be cherished. Addictive element: 8 (of 10).

Lenny - One of Homer's friends. I am not sure why I like him so much. Addictive element: 7 (of 10).

Groundskeeper Willy - The absolute best character in the whole show. Addictive element: 10 (of 10).

The best episodes - the season with Conan O'Brian (Brien? Brion? Freon?) as head writer/producer ... whatever he was.

The only thing that keeps the Simpsons from totally wrecking my EQ experience is the fact that I have seen every episode 100 times and they come on two times a night (in my local viewing area). Thankfully, I use this time to eat, else I would have died from malnutrition a long time ago (but with a level 20 character!)
