Everquest: The Great Theocracy

by the Cackling Klaknak

Overview: Brad takes over and immediately declares all reality, be it political, economic or anything else, a Great Mystery Only to Be Known by the Church. He then sets up a hierarchy of bishops to dispense wisdom and salvation in bits that the masses can understand. He also laces all religious tracts with crack cocaine, making the Church hated, but desired.

The new American philosophy: The Church Knows All.

The new American reality: The people grumble, but are too stoned to do anything about it.
