Lying is Wrong! Wrong I Tells Ya! - by Lleana

I play a Wood elf Bard on Solusek Ro named Lleana. Most of the time, I go out of my way to help people when I can, but sometimes (okay, lots of times), I have to deal with some real morons. Guys that ask me to find their bodies and after a hour long search, they ask if I can spare 10 pp? I'm sure you've met a few. So, I've found a way to avoid them when people for whatever reason have put me on their /friend list, and send me random tells asking me to help find their friend's body, or they need my songs for this that or the other. I was actually tickled to find that it showed up on your top ten list. Whenever my group is sitting and healing or whatever, and I get these annoying /tells, I usually tell them "Sorry, in a big fight", and maybe add in some typos to make it look real. "sorrry, figghting..." Well, one day, this guy that's been annoying me with alarming regularity sends me a tell while my group is waiting on some monsters to spawn. Not wanting to deal with him, I send a quick tell. "Busy, big fight.." And while panning around to see how my teammates are doing, who do I see? You got it. He was standing right behind me, watching me twiddle my thumbs. He sends a curious tell, "Oh really?" One of my most embarrasing moments ever, I had no idea what to say, but I haven't heard from him since. Thank goodness for small miracles and gigantic embarrasments. Jamye (Lleana)
