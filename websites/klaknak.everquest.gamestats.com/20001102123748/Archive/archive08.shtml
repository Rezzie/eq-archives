Hello peoples, I am back!

These last several weeks have been somewhat exciting, so I thought I would pass the goodness around.

As some of you know, I now work for a gaming company and guess what! All the whiners are right! Gaming companies are out to screw the customers every chance they get. My job is to hit on gamers' wives, girlfriends, and legal age daughters. My supervisor is Satan himself, which is cool, because he doesnt mind if I smoke cigars in the men's room. Anyway, we just had a meeting yesterday and we were yelled at for not screwing the gaming public hard enough, so I promise I will try harder today.

Last week was stressful, though. I now spend quite a bit of time in Asheron's Call and pretty much do nothing but die nonstop, although my online honey (bwahahah) Pali manages to keep me company and laugh at my mistakes. She has a level 20+ Ranger in Prexus and pretty soon I will start working on my barbarian so I can continue bringing the kind of choice everquest nuggets of goodness you have all grown to love and thirst for like the infotainment hungry dogs you are.

Oh, last week. Yeah, so I started my new job last week. So, I now have this on my plate:
1. Gaming job.
2. Social work job.
3. Filming Pornography.
4. The Cackling Klaknak
5. Asheron's Call
Ouch. But I make up for it by coming in drunk to work. I dont think anyone has noticed yet. I think they think I am merely eccentric.

But on top of the madness last week, I also had to ... sigh ... visit my brother and parents in Atlanta. This is just a quadruple double whammy to the nuts for several reasons:
1. I fucking hate flying.
2. I just started a new job and I was already stressed.
3. My Grandmother "You Are Too Fat" Maris was going to be there.
4. My Parents "You Need to Stop Fucking Up Your Life" Mariseses were there.
5. My brother informed me that he would not be able to find a loose woman for me this time. *sigh*
Dad then informs me that he wants me to stay for FOUR days and I just nearly just about died.

But my brother and I stayed drunk for most of those days (Grand Marnier, Newcastle Ale, and Maker's Mark, baby) so it was bearable. My grandmother is pretty cool when I am drunk, it turns out.

Anyway, so on the way back, my plane experienced turbulence the entire freaking trip back. I felt like I was going up and down in an elevator (at the speed of light) for about 2 hours and it took a couple days before I didn't feel like a World War I mustard gas veteran. That was, in all seriousness, one of the most hellish experiences of my life.

Until I returned to work ... my supervisor started yelling at me right when I walked in the door.

"Did you break up any gamer marriages this weekend?" he asked.

"No, Mr. Satan, my grandmother was ..."

"You are demoted to Obscene Phone Caller (aka Customer Service)."

*sigh*
