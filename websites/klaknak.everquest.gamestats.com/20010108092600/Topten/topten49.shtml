Top Ten Evil Dueling Techniques

by Phaethon

10. (NOTE: This one requires you be in front of Blackburrow, and that you're a spellcaster and they're not) Immediately after they accept, dash up the wall to the side of the entrance. Hop down from there to the snout of the gnoll face. Then start casting, unmolested, as he tries to figure out what happened.

9. If you can Feign Death, do so after attacking a powerful monster. It'll attack your opponent.

8. (NOTE: You must have levitate and your opponent must not) Duel by the water, and stand out there blasting with spells while your opponent tries to reach you. Pray they have no bow.

7. Kite them.

6. Perform the demonic "duck and back" technique of avoiding your opponent's blade.

5. If you have a spell that causes a big graphical effect, like Invoke Lightning, cast it then run and hide somewhere. Later, if he doesn't zone, you can ambush him.

4. Make a social button that's a /tell to your opponent. Spam them hideously until they ignore you.

3. Act insane, and ready to die if you can take him with you. Constantly blither on about rabbits.

2. Plan ahead, and bring five or six friends to heal you if you give a preordained signal. Act like a jerk beforehand, and have them send your opponent /tells about how they're "on his side".

1. Take the duel into the water, without Levitate. The murkier, the better. Then dive, and come up behind him. Stay in control of who dives when.
