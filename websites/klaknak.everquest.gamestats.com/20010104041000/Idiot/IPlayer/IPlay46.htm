Level 50 Idiot - by Janalee

This goes in the "level 50's can be idiots too" file

Normally as an enchanter I'd never have a pet in a dungeon. Our pets
are uncontrollable, and can be great train-starters when they get off on
a rampage and can't be called back. But in Permafrost doing the Ice
Giant Diplomat with just one caster buddy, a pet is a must since neither
of us can tank and the IG is notorious for resisting unless you're
within melee range. Plus, my level 49 pet can clear the entire dungeon
of gobbies itself, so I don't feel too nervous having a pet in Perma.
Anyway, there we were, hanging out near the Goblin King spawn site
waiting for the dip to pop again. We were bored, and as friends often
do when bored we began goofing off, jumping off ledges, running in
circles, climbing everything in sight, etc - just killing time.

Now I should mention a couple of things here. First, behind the king
spawn in Perma is what looks like giant ice cubes poking through the
wall. It's fairly common knowledge that right behind that wall is Lady
Vox and her retinue of Ice Giant guardians (one can even target Vox
through that wall and can hear her casting when she's there). Second,
my friend is a halfling and as such, is pathetically short (and funny
looking if you ask me). Me being a lithe, willowly high elf
enchantress, I began to see how high up on the "cubes" I could climb,
and to my delight I discovered I could get all the way to a little ledge
up there. My half-sized companion with his stubby legs had no hope of
following me up. I noticed that the ledge seemed to lead to a small
opening into the next room - too small to climb through, but definitely
connected. I was about to stand on the lip of the ledge and proclaim
myself Queen of All Permafrost (and make my diminutive friend jealous)
when I was greeted by the message every enchanter dreads seeing in a
dungeon: TAUNTING ATTACKER MASTER.

ACK! I'd forgotten about my pet and it had obviously followed me up to
the ledge and, being smaller, had obviously somehow scooted through the
opening and into the next room where it set off..............OH MY GOD
GATE GATE GATE FOR THE LOVE OF MARR GATE, I shouted to my companion.
Too late for me, within seconds I was teleported somewhere deeper in
(you have been SUMMONED, the terse message read) and found myself
surrounded by no less than five Ice Giants all pushing and shoving for
an opportunity to play Eat the Enchanter. My poor pet was piercing and
kicking for his life but there was little he could do against all five
(he did continue to gamely report to me that he was TAUNTING ATTACKER
MASTER). I have no idea how, but I managed to survive long enough to
run back to the King Room before I was turned into so much red and
oracle-robe blue mush.

The kicker is, Vox had been taken down on my server the night before.
If she hadn't......... <shudder>

Janalee,
Server withheld due to embarassment

P.S. My halfling buddy lived and had an enormous laugh over my
misadventure
