The next series of enhancements - by Tom G.

I'd don't so much mind that Verant does things that 'balance' the classes. The funniest thing is the backdoor slight-of-hand way the put them in. It's like their giving you such a good reacharound, that by the time you realize... well, you see where that's going.

A great example of this is from last weeks patch: Druid's Skin Like Nature has had a damage shield component added to it! Sounds good, except they fail to mention that this was done at the cost of the 2 point regeneration component it used to have.

Luckily, my sources at VI have smuggled to me what the next series of enhancements will be, and what the behind the scenes effects will be. I will share them with you now, in hopes that you will allow others to be enlightened by this knowledge. If I get any further information, I will share it at that time.

Rouges Poison skill is now resisted half as often! (It also taunts 8 times as much).

More research formulas added for pure casters! (Spells over level 12 are no longer available at guilds).

Monks may now carry 10% more weight! (However, if they go over it, items randomly disappear).

Druids/Rangers Ensnare now has a predicatable duration, like Snare, and its resistability has been greatly reduced! (The duration is 9 seconds. Snare no longer works on anything but bixies, giant bats, and goblin whelps).

Dyllin Starshine (for the Testament of Vaneer quest) is no longer a timed spawn, he is triggered! (He has a placeholder thats a level 49 Shadow Knight).

More mobs now drop the fishbone earring! (Innoruk and Cazic Thule).

Warriors have been given a slight ATK bonus! (Mobs have been given a huge ATK bonus). Warriors have been given 10% more hitpoints! (They no longer heal naturally).

Bandages heal 20% more hitpoints! (They now cost 40pp each. The mage summon bandage spell now requires a black pearl as a component).

Casting time on Evac spells has been greatly reduced! (There's now a 50-50 shot for each person in the group to be left behind).

Mobs pathing has been fixed so that they no longer teleport away when fleeing! (They can now teleport on to you if you aren't in melee range and you begin casting a spell).

Fennin Ro server has been split into two! (Players were distributed randomly. Most weren't lost).

Spirit of Wolf is now 10% faster, and last 20% longer! (It is negated by any healing spell, and does not stack with regeneration or chloroplast).

Manastones are now available via questing! (The quest requires rare drops off of Nagafen, King Xorbb, and LORE pelt from a rare invisible mist wolf in West Karana that shows up on track as 'a mist wolf'. The pelt looks invisible in the loot screen unless you right click on it. You need 200 skill in jewelcraft, baking, pottery, and brewing. You also need 3 Black Sapphires and 4,200,000 copper, which must be given to a rare spawn in Kedge that swims at cheetah speed, never stops, doesn't respond to hails. The guest giver is on the 7th island in the plane of Air. Manastones now only work in BlackBurrow and CrushBone).

Kunark has been opened, you can get there via boat or a new druid and wizard gate spell! (All other races are KoS to Iskar, and the boat docks in downtown Iskarville, where the Necromancers guild has a parade every game hour. The druid gate is simlar to the one in Butcherblock in that it has nothing but undead around it. Instead of dwarven skeletons, however, they are Revenging Knights. The wizard gate is naturally in the wizard's chamber in a 45+ dungeon).
