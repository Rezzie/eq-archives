When you wish upon a star - by Meddik Sawbones, Dwarven Cleric, Jaegergruppe Guild, Bristlebane Server

When you're camping a rare spawn
you will always wait too long.

The object that you most desire
Kill-Stealed from you.

Maybe be Bilge or even Gull,

When you're camping a rare spawn...
As they do,
Someone gates in.

He just drops a nuke or two,
Gets the XP and the loot.

So what, you waited,
what did you think he came here for?
Wonder what he'd sell it for?

When you're camping a rare spawn,
You'll get Kill Stealed!
