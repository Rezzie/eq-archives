How to Win Friends and Influence People by Yakyak Mukmuk

It was another nice day in the PoF. I was running around with my level 50 Warrior with his new full Lustrious Russet armor. I didn't have a group, and doing this in the PoF is a death wish. I was running around asking if anyone needed a level 50 warrior on their group. A dark elf cleric tells me to meet him by the hourglass and I can join his group. So, I go there (with my journeyman boots on and SOW cast on me by a level 50 shaman). I'm just running around looking for it (PoF is kinda hard to navagate). I finally find it and start running towards it. I see the cleric with his group. There are usually a large amount of people at the hourglass at any given time, but as I was getting closer, I noticed people started turning and running. I didn't think much of it, but as I got even closer, more people started to scatter. Everyone was scattering until even the dark elf who had invited me ran. I told him "Where are you going?" and he replys with a simple "dont stop running". Perplexed by this, I keep running. I run right past the hourglass. "Should I stop now?" I asked him, and he replyed "No dont think about it." Again wondering what he was talking about, I keep running. I wondered if I ran way past the hourglass, so i do a chase cam and see the hugest train following me of at least 10 level 45+ monsters. "AHHHHHHHHHHH!!!" I tell the dark elf.

Well, as I later discovered, the train had killed most of the people near the hourglass and almost killed me, but a wizard gated me out. After I came back to the PoF, I saw at least 20 corpses scattering the grounds next to the hourglass and at least 10 more in the immediate area. To make a long story from getting longer, I cost at least 10 people their level 50 status and made 20 other level 46 through 49's drop a lot of experience. "Well I guess I'll be staying out of the PoF for a while." I told my guild, half of whom was looting their corpses. Well after I begged for apology to the people I had killed, they decided to let me into the PoF again and not to hate this poor level 50 ogre too much.

*Yakyak Mukmuk crys for the dead*
