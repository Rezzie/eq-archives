4.Our cops would stand in one place all day and then attack something for no reason like for being a rat.

6.Everything except you would be able to walk through the walls at the store.

7.You could kill anybody you want when you want and nobody would care.

8.If you die you can just go back and get your corpse before it suddenly disappears

9.Everybody would say how Verant sux and then get struck down by other people who are also saying how much Verant sux.

10.We would not have to eat or sleep or drink.
