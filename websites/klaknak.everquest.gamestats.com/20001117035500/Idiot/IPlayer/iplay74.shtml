Run!

by Rolelin


I'm not sure how funny this is, but it was dumb. To protect the semi-innocent (and because I can't remember their names really well) I will not use names. Okay, here's the story.

I was grouped in Blackburrow with an enchanter, a shaman, and a rogue. We were in the pit by the snake ledge. I don't know if it's the snake pit or the spike pit. Anyway, the enchanter was going to pull some gnolls. There were two or three gnolls and a guardsman. He was supposed to pull ONE. He pulled them all. Then a patrolling gnoll was fighting too. I hurt it and it ran. I couldn't catch it, since some gnolls heard the fight and came over. Then the patroller brought a guard or two. "RUN" I shouted, and we did. The shaman and the rogue died somewhere near the ledge. the enchanter was ahead of me with SOW and lived. I only lived because a fellow named Chingi helped me. I'm telling his name because I want to honor him for helping me. As I ran across the top floor, I was really hurt. He healed me. Then I ran to the entrance. One foot away from the zone I was stunned and beaten. Stun wore of and I ran through and lived. In the end my group and I brought at least 15 gnolls to the zone. I think it was fifteen, but nobody was counting. Well, that's my story. It's boring, but my group and I were idiots, so it kinda qualifies as an idiot file. When I'm level 50 I'm gonna come back to BB and destroy every spawn in the place at least once. Then I'll laugh, surrounded by bodies.
