Dos Chicas Idiotas

by Person X

One day in the woods, I /tell my friend who I know is an idiot but think who can screw up orc hill? So anyways she tells me she is grouped with a wizard of the same level and they are going to lfay. Well, I go to the zone and zone in before them, even though they were already on their way. When I zone, they zone in behind me. We group up and go into the faydark.

Now comes the first idiot remark. Let's stick with the path. First of all, we have a ranger who can track, and second, only big mean orcs stay along the path. So we go along the path so that Ms. Wizard can find her way back. Well we go along and kill about four wasps before we get to an orc camp. I go and pull two wasps beyond the camp, expecting them to stay there, since their connection appear to be slower than 8k judging by their reaction time. I even had to loot the wasps for the wizard because she took so long. So I'm going back to them with the two wasps when I notice that their health bars are going down. Well I make it to them just in time to get slaughtered by 4 orc cents.

That wasn't even the beginning though. Well, ok, so it WAS the beginning, but the two-hour corpse retrieval is what makes them idiots. We zone back into lfay, and the ranger goes linkdead while the wizard disconnected while zoning, so I group the wizard when she comes back on and then we go get her corpse. Then she gets killed by an orc and I can strafe-run back to the zone. Then the ranger comes back on and runs to the lfay zone. We group up and all three of us head to the camp. We make it, and the corpses are between the camp and another orc. So I stop and we wait for the orc to leave, and when it starts moving it comes toward us and I am the only one to move. My two group members managed to stay totally still for long enough so that it had a nice easy kill of them before coming after me. The next few tries either the wizard or the ranger got a little too close to the orcs. All the while I was telling them to /consent me, since I had no doubt they would botch up /corpse by getting close to the orcs. I even managed to get a high level to clear out the camp, but alas, the two had wandered off and a renegade orc came by and slaughtered me. When they finally /consented me I ran with their corpses to the zone, this being the ONLY way that I could have gotten them their corpses.
