Top 10 Reasons I'm not going to Kunark

by Cyanna Thunderhammer

10. If you thought ogre and troll speak was bad, wait until you hear people roleplay iksar speak. They should put a limit on the number of s’s in one sentence.

9. The zones are even bigger than WK. This is a bad thing if you are a warrior without SOW or teleports. “Train to zone! You have 30 minutes to move before it gets there!”

8. I can finally hunt monsters without waiting in line or putting my name on a list.

7. The Titanic has a better safety record than the boat to Kunark.

6. I would need to sell my house to get a computer powerful enough to run it well.

5. I still have not received the CD that I ordered ‘next day mail’ a couple of weeks ago.

4. According to the new ROK strategy guide, I’m still looking for a necromancer to teleport me there.

3. According to Abashi, the monsters there are the same levels but are much harder. So I can solo blues on Antonica or die to greens on Kunark. Hmmm.

2. I’m KOS everywhere on the new continent.

1. Powergamers galore.
