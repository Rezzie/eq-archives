Norrathian Holidays - by Cyanna "Wonderfarm" Thunderhammer

Here are my Norrathian equivalent holidays, sure to amuse and offend.

Aradune Day - This is a weekly holiday in which Aradune comes out of his
house on the Plane of Sky and the first race or class he sees is instantly
nerfed. If he does not see anyone, we will have a week free of nerfings and
actual bugs and broken quests might actually get fixed.

Valentines Day - This is a holiday of romance and love. It usually involves
hordes of hormonally challenged teenagers asking every female character they
see for cybersex, heedless of the fact that the females in question might
actually be 300 pounds guys that rarely bathe.

King Luther Martin Day - This commemorates the founding of Erudin by the
dark skinned humans. They achieved independence from the stupid,
uncivilized whites, to found a culture based on equality and learning.

Fat Tuesday - This is the day before Ash Wednesday when people party like
they have never done before. People camp, killsteal and powergame for 24
straight hours, gorging themselves on food and booze before they have to
give it up for the next day.

Ash Wednesday - This is the day people give up the riches of the flesh.
They eat and drink only summoned food and water, and they give up their
powergaming ways to **gasp** roleplay!

Bristlebane Day - This is a day for wearing green and drinking, where people
crowd the taverns of Kelethin, for the wood elves' famous green beer. Then
everyone gets stinking drunk and falls off the platforms.

Passover - This is to commemorate a great even in history. A long time ago,
Aradune set forth to "balance" the classes of Norrath. People huddled
together in their homes waiting for his Angel of Nerfing to appear.
Luckily, a select few players who watched for signs on the test server,
posted 20 dollar bills on the frame of their door, and the Angel of Nerfing
passed them by (but it did take the money!)

Easter - This holiday is in remembrance of when a GM came down from the
Plane of Sky to visit with the PVP inhabitants of Norrath. Put the poor
bastard went link dead and the evil, misguided players killed him. He arose
3 minutes later and banned them all.

Independence Day - This is a dark elven holiday celebrating their
independence from the opprevssive goodness of the elven race. They
celebrate it by strapping high elves to giant kegs of dynamite and then
lighting it. It is also the only day of the year when the dark elves turn
off the neon signs of their homeland so as to better watch the "fireworks".

Veterans Day - This day, everyone celebrates the deeds of powergamers
everywhere. These veterans of many a camping session may not have social
lives, but at least they have a holiday named after them.

Labor Day - This is a day to celebrate the poor misguided fools that
actually spend time developing trade skills. People all over Norrath forgo
the usual ridicule to congratulate people who have spent hundreds of hours
and thousands of plats to do something else in the game besides kill things.

Halloween - This is a holiday for the darker races of Norrath. Trolls,
ogres and dark elves go forth with their backpacks asking folks for a "trick
or treat". If money and items are not soon forthcoming, they train you to
death with giants, griffons, and spectres.

Submuloc Day - This is to commemorate the discovery of the continent of
Erudin. Hundreds of years ago a human explorer named Submuloc sailed west
from Qeynos and discovered a new continent inhabited by friendly felines
called Kerrans. The humans then set about systematically exterminating
every kerran they found, and chasing them onto a small island off the coast
were they now reside, surrounded by humans.

Thanksgiving - This holiday is the time of year when everyone gathers
together with friends and guildmates and gives thanks for the few spells and
abilities they have left that have not been nerfed to hell.

Christmas - This is a time when everyone is overcome with the spirit of
giving and the plight of the unfortunates. It is a time of great twinking
for newbies, for many people go to the bank of Freeport handing out plat and
other goodies to the hordes of newbie beggars that congregate there.

Cyanna Thunderhammer
