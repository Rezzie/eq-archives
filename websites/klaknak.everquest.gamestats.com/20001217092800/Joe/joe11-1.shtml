Ultima Online: The Destruction of an Entire People

by the Cackling Klaknak

Overview: Lord British assumes power over the United States and immediately abolishes all laws. In their place, he institutes his Treatise on Noble Nobility. He then leaves the United States to work on other projects. A few overlords, with very limited power, oversee the commonweal.

The new American philosophy: The people can be relied upon to act peacefully and with integrity.

The new American reality: Country is plunged into chaos. Everything dies: people, pets, animals, house plants, etc. However, if someone uses a bad word, they are deported.

4. What are some of the popular magazines and newspapers?

*** None; a PKer may scribble "l00zer" on your forehead for your family to read

5. What does a typical citizen do everyday?

*** Lie around as a corpse

6. What do people do for fun?

*** Lie around as a corpse

7. What is the economy like?

*** Unemployment is not a problem.

8. What is the greatest threat to the Commonweal?

*** Everyone is dead.

9. What does this society view as fundamental human rights?

*** <Klaknak spews milk out his nose>

10. Which real life society does the UO America most resemble?

*** Attica, European football, Suburban housewives drunk on Peppermint Schnapps
