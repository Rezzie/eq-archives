Let the games begin!

by Borras Nimbletoes

Ok, I learned something today..... using my phone at work here, I pick up the receiver, press "drop", press "function" and press "3"... the 3 is for redial. If you do this repeatedly, it can be performed using either the right or the left hand in rapid succession, you are likely to find that .03 second gap between other callers and thusly avoid the dreaded busy signal.

Using this very method (similar to the already patented 3-finger salute so aptly named for re-booting a computer), I managed to connect to Zomax. I followed the prompts and then one of the prompts was "To find the status of your order, press 1". Ok, like the fool that I am, I press 1 and I received the message "Zomax is pleased to announce that the majority of the Runes Of Kunark were shipped April 22". It then hung up. Ok.. I was thinking... humm... let's play this fun lil game again!

After many many many many many many many many many redials, I manage connect again. Going AGAINST my instincts (my instincts were the same ones that felt I wouldn't receive the game on Monday), I follow the same prompts and I get the same message, I also get the same Click sound at the end of the message. I was feeling frustrated, but also very pleased and happy that Zomax was pleased and happy that the majority of the expansions were shipped out on the 22nd.

Ok, feeling lucky now, I decide to try this yet one more time, and the Race Is On!(Tm)

Again I place my weary and cramped fingers into the proper position for maximum speed and effect.

After many many many many many many many many many many many many many *inhale* many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many *inhale* many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many *inhale* many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many many redials, I manage to connect yet again. The going sense of triumph begins in my toes and slowly spreads upwards and brings my poor cramped calloused fingers back to life.

This time, I did follow my instincts and I carefully, ever so carefully, listened to the prompts until I got one that mentioned "All other requests, please press 2". Ok, I think I am on something here, so I press two, and Lo And Behold(c) I manage to Almost connect to an operator. Seems that for some very odd reason, all operators are currently busy... I think she was just in the bathroom. Whilst I was pondering my current dilemma and that poor Cuban boy and all of the other news-worthy events that have happened, and were currently happening, the same operator that was on a pee-break decided that I have waited long enough. I think the re-dials and the holding was simply a test... a test to weed out those weaklings who were only curious as to the status of the order the place, paid for and were waiting for to only have those die-hard losers with no Life (Tm by Milton-Bradley) and who would not simply hang up in frustration. I passed the test, barely. While I was performing this test of stamina and endurance holding the phone with my withered fingers against my now burning ear, the operator said, in a very pleasant voice "Zomax, how may I help you?". That question could have been answered in a multitude of different answers, each one getting successively worse. I answered her "You can help my by shipping out my fucking game you puss filled whore"... not in those exact words.. well.. my exact words were "I was wondering if you could help me, I want to know if you received my order for the expansion yet". Ok, so I sugar-coated it a bit, but I didn't want to play the redial game again. I am now burning with curiosity... whilst playing this Fun Game, I received perhaps, oh, 3 calls. I didn't answer them, they were most likely work-related and would have interrupted my camping of the Zomax Operator.

I gave her all of my most personal and intimate details about me and my life and those close to me... I wooed her and called her pretty names. She then caved into my most insidious method... the "Whore Falls For It" whereby a lady will listened in rapt attention to my every word, breathlessly waiting for the next pearl of wisdom to sprout forth from my beared face. In anticipation of the secret, I performed a ritual not many know of, I increased the volume on the phone lest I miss The Secret. She divulged a guarded secret, guarded in such a way others can find the secret nuclear weapons the USA has hidden in the farms in Nebraska more easily than the secret she revealed to me. In hushed tones, she spewed forth my Tracking Number. A hushed silence fell across the room... through the phone I heard a lady scream and grown men cry. I have met the challenge I have prevailed! Against all odds, I have received the Information! Only now did she realize her mistake... I laughed at here.... I taunted her and called her names! She has fallen for the oldest trick in the game.... the "Redial many times, stay on hold a long long time and be really really nice until they give you an answer" trick.

Triumphant and singing out of tune, I promptly hung up the phone (after thanking her). Ok... I now have the Tracking Number, I only need to find the key to unlock its lethal force. I searched and scoured the net, looking for something, anything that I can use to call to see where "The Expansion" is. I sifted through web site after web site, message board after message board. Then, in all I can say was Divine Intervention, the three letters that will forever be emblazoned in my mellon........ UPS. Wham! Bang! Crash! I plowed to the UPS website. There, in small letters, almost taunting me, was the caption "Tracking". With shaking fingers and weary eyes, I clicked on the icon. What did I see? I'll tell you... with tears in my eyes and a heart full of trepidation, I saw a box... an glorious empty box... a box designed for me.. a box whose sole purpose was to enter a tracking number. With a growing sense of dread in my heart, I entered the Tracking Number, the Zomax Secret, pilfered in a mid-morning assault they will not soon forget.

I clicked Submit. What will I see next? Has Zomax duped me and given me a false number? Is my Expansion now sitting in Moscow? The globe, it swirls and twirls, not releasing the prize most sought after. It spins and whirls.. and I wait.. my heart is beating rapidly.... will it release what I have longed for.. sought after and fought for? Or will it only say "404 Error, page not found"?

I waited in breathless anticipation. The screen... it changed. I sobbed aloud. Scroll damn you weak fingered right hand.. scroll like you've never scroll before! I scrolled down... and there, in print no different than any other print on the screen... was the key.. the key to ever lasting life, the key to all I hold most dear... the expansion is sitting in my home town.

Now begins the guessing game.. when will the UPS guy deliver the game? That is an answer not even I can tell you.
