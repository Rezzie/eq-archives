General Malignant - by Kneewhacker

One fateful day, some Minotaurs decided to form up in a troupe behind their leader General Malignant, and do an eight mile hike around Steamfont. Minotaurs, being rather rigidly militant (and none too bright, something like a democrat), are wont to do this sort of thing from time to time, just for physical conditioning purposes, you understand.

All was going well, young gnomes and gnomettes were dying by the dozen, as most fingerwaggling gnomes and gnomettes are wont to do when confronted with several tons of angry homo bovinus, when General Malignant heard a tiny voice from atop the highest peak in Steamfont.

"I'm wee red Kneewhacker of the Glen! Send up your best slaver and I'll send down 'is head!"

The General turned to his Hero and said, "Send up my best slaver, I want that gnomes head!"

Well up the hill that slaver went, and in two shakes of kobolds tail, down came his head.

"I'm wee red Kneewhacker of the Glen! Send up your best two Guards, and I'll send down their 'eads!"

General Malignant turned to the Hero and said," Send up my best three Guards, I want that gnomes head!"

Up the hill the Generals best three guards went and bumpity-bump-bump come down their heads.

"I'm wee red Kneewhacker of the Glen! Send up your whole damn army, for I'm properly warmed up and ready for some sport! And if thats not enough incentive, I have some Minotaur Steaks up here I'll share with you to build your strength, you hosers!"

General Malignant turned to the Hero and said, "Take the army up there and BRING ME THAT GNOMES HEAD!!!"

So the Hero and the rest of the troupe went running up the hill and there was the worst skreeking and howling and carrying on you could imagine for a couple of minutes, and then the Hero, bloody, beaten, and in that low health slow motion stagger you all know so well, came down the peak with Kneewhacker hot on his heels.

Just before he died he spoke. "Run General! It's a trap! There's two of them!"
