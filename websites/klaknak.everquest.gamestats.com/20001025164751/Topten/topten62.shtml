Top 16 signs that you have been playing EQ too much

by Bumzuk, Ythmelgrot, Kithinorr / Prexus Server


1. You wonder why the bank won't hold your spare set of clothes.

2. You keep spamming everyone in your carpool in Froglok.

3. You ask the gas station attendant for a SOW because you are late for work.

4. You yell, "Twinks suck!" at the 18 year old in the Mercedes next to you.

5. You go to the grocery store and shout, "Want to buy, One can tomato soup!!!, Send tells."

6. You've sewn every piece of clothing in your house into one big ball.

7. You go to church on Sundays hoping to get a few buffs.

8. Camping with your family, you seriously start eyeing up a bear

9. Instead of giving people the finger, you start doing some unrecognisable other gesture

10. You see a model in her underware, and wonder if you should see if she needs a locate corpse

11. You jump in the pool whenever you are bored "just to improve your swimming skill"

12. You find you actually snicker softly at things...

13. You have to buy an extra pillow for your computer chair because of the bruises from sitting in one place for too long

14. You read most of the EQ sites on the web every morning when you come into work.

15. You start saying things like "ding" and "lol" to people.

16. You think of your friends/family/co-workers in terms of what level they are and if you can take them or not.
