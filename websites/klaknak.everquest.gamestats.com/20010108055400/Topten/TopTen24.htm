Top Ten Signs EQ Runs Your Life - by Lawni

10: Your three best friends have names like Manheim, Urweil, or Krevlech.

9: Your favorite song is the Everquest melee combat music. (Bonus points if you make up lyrics)

8: Whenever your computer crashes you wonder if Verant nerfed your harddrive.

7: You make up long elaborate fantasies about the girl on the loading screen.

6: You refer to sleeping as "Going Linkdead."

5: You can give flawless directions in Freeport, but can't tell the pizza guy how to get to your house.

4: Whenever you go into the chatrooms you know everyone on a first name basis.

3: You plan real life events around server downtimes. (If we hurry I can be done with my wedding just as the new patch is done.)

2: In an effort to be more like your character you eat nothing but black bread and water, never sleep, and greet everyone with "Hail!"

1: You have a toilet for a computer chair.
