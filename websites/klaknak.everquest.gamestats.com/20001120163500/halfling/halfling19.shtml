Misty Thicket Halflings

by Chronocide

A young halfling warrior was walking through Misty Thicket, brandishing his new rusty sword. He walked up to one of the deputies at the Wall. "Check out my new rusty sword!" said the young halfling. "What's that for?" the deputy inquired. "Why, it's for protection against those Kodiaks!" replied the newbie. "Well you know, if you're going to use that as protection from Kodiaks, they say it helps if you take of the crossguard," stated the sage deputy. "Well, why is that?" "That way it doesn't hurt as much when the bear shoves it up your dqq."

* * *

Two halflings were walking through Misty Thicket. They were about to go past the wall for the first time, to start fighting young Kodiaks. One was a druid, and he cast SoW upon himself. "Why are you casting SoW on yourself?" asked his friend, a rouge. "Well, it's so I can outrun the bears." "What??" exclaimed the skeptic halfling. "You ain't ever gunna outrun a Kodiak, no matter how good your spell is!" "Well, that's true," stated the druid. "However, I don't have to outrun the bear, I only have to do is outrun you."
