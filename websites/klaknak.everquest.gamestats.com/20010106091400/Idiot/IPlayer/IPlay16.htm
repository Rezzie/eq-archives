Revenge of the Cat People - by Serinar

Serinar, Level 10 necro, has just been talked into going to Erud's Crossing. "It will be great!" said my party member. "Greater lightstones and rawhide armor = $$!" OK, so I take the short jaunt from Qeynos over to Erud's Crossing. Not knowing how the cat people would react to me, I decided to go invisible before I got on the boat.

When I arrived - I was pleased to see that invis was still intact! "Great!" I think to myself. I see another necro there and ask how the cat people judge him. "Amiable" comes the response. Since invis was still up, I selected and conned a cat person, but of course the the result came back "indifferent". OK then, time to drop invis! Realize that I was still rather new at the game. Realize also that I never bothered to click a "spell effect" icon on my main screen.

This being the case, I did not realize that, by merely clicking on the "Gather Shadows" icon I could dispel my invisibility. My preferred method of dropping invisibility was to cast a spell without a target. It drops invis and just says "You must select a target...".

I think you can see where this is going... I click the Poison Bolt spell. Strange...why is the casting time bar appearing...oh no! Walk away - interrupt it! You regain your concentration and continue your casting. (First time I remember this happening)

Suchandsuch Catperson resists the Poison Bolt spell!

Suchandsuch Catperson says "I really hate necromancers like you!"

Othersuchandsuch Catperson says "Time to die Serinar."

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

Oh well, time to find the boat again. Several days later, while discussing the incident, a friend says "Why didn't you just click on the spell icon?" to which I reply "That works?"

Your skill in Ignorance has increased! (24)

Moral of the story: Always make sure you check to make sure you don't unintentionally attack cat people by leaving them targeted while you cast Poison Bolt to dispel Gather Shadows.
