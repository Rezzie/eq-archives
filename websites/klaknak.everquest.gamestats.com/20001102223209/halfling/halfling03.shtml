Horrible Halfling Jokes
by DP Twisted

A barbarian journeys to the Feerott for the first time. In the mood for a fight, he walks up to an ogre couple and tells the male ogre, "Your wife is the ugliest woman on Norrath!"

The ogre turns to his woman and says, "See? I told you."

****

A ranger and a warrior are in the East Commonlands when they see a griffin flying straight towards them. The ranger immediately whips off his banded boots and starts putting on his tennis shoes. The warrior looks at him and says, "What are you doing? You'll never outrun that thing!" The ranger replies, "I don't have to; I just need to outrun YOU!"

***

A halfling, a troll, and a fire elemental walk into a bar. The barkeep looks up and says, "What is this, some kind of joke?"
