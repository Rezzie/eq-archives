ALWAYS re-bind yourself - by Bolenie, Eci server

I have enjoyed this site for many months now. I have made all the mistakes others make and died because of them but this one REALLY tops them all.

Last night I went on my 2nd FG raid into SolB. For those of you who havent done an FG raid they kill players even when 3 full groups of 45+'s attack. When it came time to kill Magi Roykl all us casters were told to bind on the ramp. This is a common practice so that if you die you will be closeby to roll for and loot the items. I did so and we finished the raid. Everyone got ported out and I logged.

Here is where I got stupid. :) The following morning I was camping specs with my guild in OOT. we were taking them down with little effort but a few members had RL issues and had to leave for a bit. It got down to 3 of us and we decided on one last pull before abandoning our camp. we pulled 2 specs, not too hard, killed one and another spawned. Okay now we are low on mana and I am down to 1 bub. We yell gate and I train the specs off the island. My 2 friends gate back to the Safety of Freeport while I confirm thier whereabouts. When I decide they are safe I tell them I am gating. No biggie afterall the 2 specs are WAY behind me :) oops...I forgot to Bind after the raid!

Well guess what, I'm back in the FG lair right on the ramp. Noone is around and no FG's :) I tell my group and they freak. I tell my guild and they freak as well. Okay calm down, do an /ooc and ask for help...noone is camping SS room. The FG's should be spawning soon. I was told to run for my life to the SS room to die and get resd later. I did but the 1st FG WIZ had respawned.

I died and luckly 2 very nice people came to my rescue, dragged my corpse to the zone and resd me...

Note to self. ALWAYS re-bind yourself after an FG raid! :)
