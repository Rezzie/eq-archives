Top Ten Everquest Blunders (all perpetrated by me) - by Phaethon

10. Diving for pennies in the pit in Blackburrow at Level 3.

9. Thinking I could beat Tovax Vmar when he conned black.

8. Jokingly casting Flame Lick on a friend near a guard. (And neither of us PVP)

7. Attacking those annoying blue balls of light without a magical weapon. (Damn wisps)

6. Insulting a high-level character and having every kill stolen for three hours.

5. Attacking a guard at Qeynos Gate and trying to make it to zone before I die, on a bet.

4. Ignoring the names "Froon" and "Choon" on my Tracking box.

3. Attempting to help kill a werewolf at Level 10.

2. Seeing how deep I could swim, and forgetting a light source.

1. Becoming commanding officer of a guild, just to find out there were only about twelve other members.
