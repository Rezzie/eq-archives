Keldeth and the 5 dwarves or How I got my corpse back - by Keldeth

Well there I was, having finally reached level 7 on a glorious Freeport morning. Feeling kind of down because things were getting either too easy or still too hard, I enlisted the help of 5 dwarves. To protect the innocent, (and to give my short bearded companions a laugh) I will call them Dopey, Grumpy, Sleepy, Bashful and Doc. Four Dwarven Clerics and a dwarven paladin.

We decided to do some hunting at the ruins in the East Commonlands. Laying waste to skeletons, zombies and mummys (oh my!) we decided that we needed something bigger, more exciting... a change of scenery.

Arriving at Befallen in the early evening, we went in feeling confident. Killing skeletons and even taking on Skeleton Lrodd gave us an even bigger boost. Level 8 was not far away for me. It was going very well.

And then I did the incredibly stupid. I decided to take a look down the well. When I realized what had happened, laying at the bottom of a well, I realized that these nasty ice-boned skeletons were beating me to a bloody pulp. So I did what any stalwart adventurer would do. I ran like hell.

The next day, standing around naked in East Freeport, I shouted out and found two of my dwarven friends and a couple other friends from previous adventures. We set out for Befallen in an attempt to retrieve my body. The plan was as follows: Go to the well, jump in, loot the corpse, and run like hell to back to the top. We felt pretty good about our chances and jumped in.

Not a one of us survived in what had to be the biggest corpse retrieval blunder of Norrath's history. Now there were 7 corpses at the bottom of a well.

Needless to say, my now less than thrilled saviors had friends in high places, so to speak, who helped us get all of our stuff back, and to them we are incredibly grateful.

The moral of the story: Don't go too near the well... that last drop is a doozy. OR Ice-boned skeletons really don't enjoy company just dropping in.
