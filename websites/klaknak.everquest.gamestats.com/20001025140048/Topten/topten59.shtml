Top 10 Sarcastic answers to an EQ refugee's AC questions

by Name

[editor's note: All of these were questions I actually heard, except for one. That one was asked by me. Guess which one?]

10. Q: Where is the bank? A: Go to the second story of the tavern. The banker’s name is Cyanna’s Mule. Just give her your stuff.

9. Q: How do I con someone? A: Write ‘Deed to Lytethorpe Castle’ on a piece of paper and sell it to newbies for 100 pyreals. By the way, interested in buying Fort Teth?

8. Q: How do I shout? A: Type everything in all caps and it displays the message to everyone in the zone.

7. Q: Where is the zone? A: Keep running north. You’ll get to it eventually.

6. Q: What the heck is a shreth? A: They are monsters that vary in difficulty. The gnawers are the toughest ones and the vorous are the easiest ones.

5. Q: Can I get a bind? A: Sure. Give me 10 pyreals and I’ll let you touch my big blue gem outside town.

4. Q: Can I get a sow? A: No, but there are some nice heifers at the farm near town.

3. Q: Where did you get that cool looking robe? A: Off a rare spawn named drudge at the campfire by the road. His placeholder is a regular drudge.

2. Q: Where can I buy spells? A: You have to go to (insert name of different race’s town). But be careful. (insert race of asker) are KOS there. You have to improve faction by turning in wasp wings to the town crier.

1. Q: Why does my magic item say it is out of mana? A: It needs to med. Put it on the ground for a few minutes and it will recover mana.
