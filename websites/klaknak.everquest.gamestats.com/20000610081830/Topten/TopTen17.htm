Top 10 Signs That Your Group is Too High for the Zone - by Jonathon Pratbons

10. You use an entirely different scale of difficulty when /conning the monsters. Light green, very green, really damn green, and supergreen.

9. The party's wizard just can't run out of mana, no matter how hard he tries.

8. Goblins start complaining over /shout about the trains of players that keep killing all of them. They are sick and tired of it!

7. When you drop excess cash or equipment on the ground, the other adventurers around dogpile on it.

6. The cleric casts her best ward undead spell, and every skeleton in the zone crumbles to dust.

5. You go afk for ten minutes and when you come back you realize you were under attack the whole time and haven't lost a bubble of hit points.

4. The other player characters in the zone mistake you for a GM because of the armor you're wearing.

3. You get Dvinn's Dragoon Dirk just so you can use it to practice your thrown weapon skill.

2. The orc battle cry is "Half elves like you have ruined your own lands! You can ruin ours too, if you want. Just please don't hurt me.. *sob*"

1. After a week of camping the Lord of the area, your experience bar still hasn't moved.
