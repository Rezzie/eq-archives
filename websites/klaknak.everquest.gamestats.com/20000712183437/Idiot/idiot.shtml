Idiot Files Gold

Every game has a certain kind of player whom I will call Joe. Joe purchases a copy of a game, plays it for a few hours, and then immediately jumps on the bulletin boards, boasting wildly about his unmatched skill and cunning. "What," he says. "You can't solo a Cleric? I can. I had my Cleric up to level 30 in ten minutes ..." Etc.

But secretly, Joe leads an impoverished life. The life ... of an idiot. For no matter how loudly he boasts (or protests to the contrary), even the most skilled player in the land occasionally does a very stupid thing. These files, the idiot files gold, are dedicated to such moments of idiocy and to the truly skillful players who are confident enough in their abilities to admit them.
