Top 10 Inevitabilities in EQ - by Cyanna Thunderhammer

10.You WILL die. A lot.

9. You WILL get killed right after gaining a level, dropping you back down to your previous level.

8. If you set a certain goal (one more spawn, 10 more minutes) you WILL get killed, effectively spending hours getting your corpse back.

7. When you sit down to med because of OOM or rest because of low health, a monster WILL spawn right on top of you.

6. The second the healer/evac’er logs of, goes AFK or goes LD, every monster spawns at once.

5. As soon as you find a good hunting strategy it WILL get nerfed in some way.

4. You WILL get killed 3 steps from the zone.

3. Your SOW, invis, or illusion WILL go off at the worst possible time.

2. You WILL get killed by typing H…A… while talking to an NPC at some time in your career.

1. When you are typing a /reply, you WILL get a tell from another person a split second before you hit enter.
