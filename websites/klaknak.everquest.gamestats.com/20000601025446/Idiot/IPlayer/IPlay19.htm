Drinking and Befallen is Contraindicated - by Drapper Everglade

I was a 15th level Druid, partied with one of my best buds, another 15th level Druid. We seem to both be very cautious, and we finally made the trip from Kelethin to Freeport. We decided that it would be great fun to check out Befallen. Well, it was late at night, we were both having a few beers in real life, and it was starting to get nerve racking.

First level, no problem. Second level, a little tougher. We would heal ourselves, med up, peek into the next room, then withdraw to discuss strategy. We would storm a room of 5 or 6 undead, kill them all, and med again. Thankfully the spawn rate is pretty slow there, and that allowed us to med in the rooms we just cleared. Now the good part. After a particularly hairy battle, which we barely won, we headed for the next room all fired up. I opened the door and side stepped to the left, back against the wall to survey the room. Arzak, my partner, came barreling into the room, and right into a hole in the floor *** CRUNCH ***. He tells me he's in a room with one door on the third level, and hes gating back to the entrance. So, like a good friend, and trembling at the thought of dying alone, I gate back also.

We head back in and fight our way back to the same room. I turn and tell him, "Now remember that hole, dont fall in again !" He assures me it wont happen again. Guess what? It was like instant replay, down the hole he goes again. Trying to contain my laughter, I can hear him cussing, but he comes up with a brilliant idea. Theres a fallen rock down there that leads back up to the room im in. So he scales it, and it pushes him to the crouch position. Due to some graphical bugs, which im sure youve all experienced, I can see his head and shoulders popping up through the floor, with that swishing sound of someone in crouch mode.

Unfortunately the skeleton in the room sees him too and starts attacking. Im in tears as I see him stop, spinning in circles, trying to see whats attacking him. After I regained composure, I kill the skeleton, and all that remains is an infected rat in my room. The ever determined Arzak swears he can get through the ceiling/floor back up to my room.

Then the tears started flying, his head ended up right under the rat. Arzak pauses, starts spinning in circles to get his bearings, stops and utters, "Tell me, is my head up a rats ass?"

That was it, I lost it, i couldnt play for nearly 15 minutes as I howled at his statement. I even called him in Canada from the States, just to laugh at him. Maybe this wouldnt have been so funny if we werent drinking, but it was a great nerve breaker. Drapper Everglade
