Petition for New Class: Pets - by Jamilyn Skyheart

To Verant, A Petition for a New Class Brought to you by S.T.O.O.P.I.D. (So That Our Odd Pets' Intelligence Drops).

We here at S.T.O.O.P.I.D. wish to bring forth a petition for a new class, in accordance with Feedback Code 187.69. As you also know, we worship Brad McQuaid, so we are assured our prayers . . . ermm . . . petition will be answered in a positive note.

We ask that a new class, the pet class, become available to your customers. We constantly seek enjoyment in EverQuest, but we feel that to truly enjoy this game, this new class is needed. Please find our detailing of this class, along with explanations where needed, below.

Class Type: Pet

Races Available: All. -- There are stupid people who show characteristics like that of the pet already in every race. It couldn't affect the delicate balance of EverQuest.

Genders Available: Both. -- A long, lengthy debate went on at the S.T.O.O.P.I.D. headquarters last week, discussing this very topic. Many felt that males should be the only gender, others women. Some said they should be unisex. To appease most, we decided to let things sit as is, at least for now.

Names: MUST be randomly generated, with occassional corny names appearing in the name generator (such as Konann and Loser). -- This feature is already implemented with the pets in the game.

Deities Available: Aradune Mithra, god of All. -- How could they worship anyone else? Heathens if they did!

Base STR: 90 -- Pets need to be strong, as they are tanks, but they also need to be fairly weak, so that no one could solo with them. After all, this is a massive multi-player online game. We can't have people trying to be non-socialable.

Base STA: 1000. -- If a current pet can float half-way across the zone, snag 20 orcs, bring them back without getting stunned, and still fight, so can the new pet class.

Base AGI: 60.

Base DEX: 60. -- They can't hit too often, or be missed too often. Again, see the note under strength about soloing.

Base WIS: 5. -- This gives a pet enough mana to cast one spell until it reaches full mana again, without meditate (because they cannot meditate), just as the pets in the game do. Base

INT: 1. -- There are two reasons for this incredibly low stat. 1) Pet's spells must be near completely useless. This gimps the power of their spells. 2) Pets are dumb. Plain dumb. It doesn't matter how many times you tell it to kill this, not that, or how many times you tell it to follow you, it will still not comply, because it isn't smart. It doesn't understand. This is one of the main features of this class as well. No one can blame you for pulling the whole dungeon to the zone, or for being so weak. You're programmed that way.

CHA: 10. -- Some people like pets. Most people don't. In addition to this stat, we ask that factions with all NPCs be lowered to dubious, and that factions with NPCs such as orcs, gnolls, and frogloks be lowered to the lowest available.

Skills Available: None. Pets cannot go up in any skill.

Weapons Available: For elven pets, none. For human pets, only the appearance of one. For evil races, all weapons are available, but disappear forever upon death.

Armor Available: None.

Spells Available: A random, level 1 spell.

Home City: Anywhere, but the first choice available will be the closest dungeon to the chosen race of the pet. We believe that you will find the new pet class extremely satisfying for game play. Please take this into review.

Your friends at S.T.O.O.P.I.D.

Attachments: $10,000,000 and it's equivalent in platinum.
