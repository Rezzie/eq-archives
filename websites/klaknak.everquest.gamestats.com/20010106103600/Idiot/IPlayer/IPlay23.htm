Befallen Claims Another Soul - by Sam

Ok, I guess it's time for me to humble myself in the idiot files. I don't usually play enough to be THIS dumb. And actually when I do play, I only die VERY infrequently. I'm generally a careful and intelligent player.

Last night was different.

You've heard of famous last words? How about "I think I'll check out that Befallen place that people keep telling me about." So, I made the trek from East Freeport, all the way out to Befallen. I carefully entered the zone and very carefully looked around. There were about 20 players in the zone and there were a few medding up at the top of the stairs in the entry room. So, I decide to wander around a bit very cautiously. "Hey", I think to myself, "there doesn't seem to be much here. I guess the spawns are under control right now. Cool." So, I wander a bit more until I see a putrid skeleton. "Hmm....green to me. Ok, I'll try elsewhere". I managed after a while to wander after another player through an open door and down into the second level where there still were almost no mobs.

"Hey. Another skeleton. Oh, it's Yellow. Ok, I'll leave this area".

I had been /oocing to get into a group, but there wasn't anyone interested. So, I decided to go back up and hope for some blues. Except my way is blocked by a necro initiate and his pet.

"Uh oh, they're red. But look! Someone is taking them away and killing them! good."

So, I go back upstairs and I'm about to go through the door back into the main area when I realize it's locked. I had followed someone else through so I didn't know that this was a locked door. No problem, there still aren't any mobs about. I'll just go back and look in these rooms for some blues to kill. After killing a blue skeleton and another green that jumped me in the process I go to see if someone is leaving and I see a guy running from a ghoul and a skeleton.

'Help, oh god help me!" he says as he runs by.

I wisely stand back and con the mobs. Red and Yellow.

I'd better...

Ok, no big deal. I'll go back and get my corpse. Back to Befallen we go. Wander around some more and there are still no mobs to be seen. The spawns are still under control. Oops, a green skeleton is hitting me. I have no equipment, but I (wisely) memorized my spells and had my shield up. Nuke it to kingdom come, no problem. I wander back to where I died. No corpse. Uhm.

/shout "Has anyone seen my corpse?"

No answer.

Well, maybe I forgot where I died. I know it was in this area. Wander around for 20 minutes. Open door. Walk through. "Hey, there are lots more mobs here than there were before."

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

Head back to Befallen. Wander around carefully. Walk into the area that I'm SURE I died. Still no body.

ARGH!
