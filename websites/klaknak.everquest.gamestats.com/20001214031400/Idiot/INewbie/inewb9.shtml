Fishing a gnoll - by Teresa L.

After fishing with my druid until I could fish no longer, I decided it was time to go beat the snot out of some gnolls.

Feeling sure of myself, I swaggered out into the newbie area and started swinging. I couldn't figure out for the life of me why I wasn't taking his health down.

In disbelief I kept swinging and didn't stop, in spite of the fact that my health was beginning to suffer. Just before I died, but too late to do anything about it, I realized... I had attacked the beast with a fishing pole.
