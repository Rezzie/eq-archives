Horrible Halfling Jokes
by Cyanna Thunderhammer

How do crazy people go through Kithicor Forest?
They take the psycho path.

How do clerics get holy water?
They boil the hell out of it.

What do barbarians get from sitting on the ice too long?
Polaroids

What lies at the bottom of the Ocean of Tears and twitches?
A nervous wreck

Where do you find a gorge hound with no legs?
Right where you left him.

Why do ogres have big nostrils?
Because they have big fingers
