Gaming Girls: The World's Sexiest Woman

As part of my continuing attempt to educate as well as entertain, I will talk about a few more absolute truths and then bring these truths together to argue that gaming girls are the world's sexiest women.

In an earlier essay, I mentioned that generalizations are stupid. For example, I go out into the world and see 3 or 4 hypocritical Christians. I therefore conclude that all Christians are hypocritical.

Clearly, this is wrong. Such overgeneralizations lead to the false belief that there are absolutes in the universe. Universal truths do not exist (except, maybe, for the fact that Computer Fishing Games suck).

What does exist in the universe is probability. Everything in the universe is based on probability. So, you can never really say all Christians are hypocrites even if you think they are because there always may be a nonhypocrite just around the corner. Instead, you may say (with increasing certainty but never absolute certainty) that a certain percentage of Christians are hypocrites.

So, what the fuck does this have to do with gaming girls? Plenty, but let me digress a little more.

Every person is also made up of probabilities and you can pick up on these probabilities by putting together clues about a person. To use an extreme example, if you see someone with a laptop, the probability is extremely low that he is a ditchdigger.

Or, to use an example closer to the topic at hand, if you are a gamer guy and you see a woman with a neat leather handbag, you will never score. Don't even try.

Why is that? Because that Eddie Bauer handbag has attached to it a string of probabilities that correlate with it. For example, she is probably some accountant wonk at a big corporation and thinks blow jobs are kinky. She is probably looking for a man with a neat haircut. Granted, none of this is a certainty ... nothing ever is (which was the point of my preceding ramble). But, if you start putting together that handbag with her expensive make up and her beginner model BMW, you can become more and more certain that she will not have a thing to do with you.

And I don't care how hot she is ... I don't care how desperate you are ... ultimately, that just flat out isn't sexy. She can be sexy in the sense that she is physically attractive, but for me, nothing shuts down the main pipeline faster than seeing a physically attractive woman with the charisma and attitude of someone who is contrary to everything I believe in including, but not limited to, the dating of me. Call her hot, call her fine, but she is not sexy.
