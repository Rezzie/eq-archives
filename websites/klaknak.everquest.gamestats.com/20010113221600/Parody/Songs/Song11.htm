Sonnets:

While I was playing EverQuest one day

I found myself at some small Dervish camp

Where Dervs would come and fight and die and play

And giants came to crush and stomp and stamp

While I had stood there waiting for my turn

Upon me came a higher level mage

When Dervs did spawn the mage began to burn

And stole my Derv so I began to rage

I yelled and screamed and called her kill stealer

But 'lo it did no good to cry in pain

Until a Thug spawned and went after her

And I stood by and laughed 'till she was slain

And so I write this to all kill stealers

You may be taking mine but you'll get yours

 

Upon a bridge in Kelethin I stood

To watch, below, my lovely wild rose,

The thong-clad one I loved, Tunare knows,

The beautious elf who lives in the wood...

I wonder as I lie at night in bed:

Why do the gods so love to fool my heart

With all of their skillful, sadistic art?

Think I as I come to lay down my head:

They have, it seems, a wicked, evil plan

To make each girl turn out to be a man.

Ballads:

Talym Shoontar (to the tune of "Pretty Boy Floyd" as sung by Woody Guthrie)

Come all you young half-elves...

A story I will tell

Of Talym Shoontar the outlaw;

The Karanas knew him well.

It was in the town of Qeynos...

It was Marrsday afternoon

A gnoll beside him in the wagon

And into town they rode.

Now, the guardsmen approached him

With a murderous desire

To slay his gnoll companion

And throw him on the fire.

Shoontar grabbed a combine,

The guardsman grabbed his blunt

And, in the fight that followed,

He slayed that guardsman runt.

Some will tell you of a hunter

Who came to sell some claws

And for the merchant's kindness

Saved him from lion's jaws.

Others tell you of a ranger

Who came to beg a flat

And, underneath his bedclothes,

Left a pile of shiny plat.

Now, as through Norrath you ramble,

And, as through Norrath you roam,

You will never see an outlaw

Drive a farmer from his home.

(Spoken: Not Tiny Miller, at least.)

It was in the town of Highpass

It was Wednesday afternoon

Shoontar died defending

The townsfolk from Choon.
