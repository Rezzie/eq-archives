Death Stories

by Chronocide

Death story number 1:

I was walkin through the west commonlands one day with my enchanter, bored off my arse. I passed the well known derv camp there, and decided to make some trouble.

I sat down with all the hordes of people waiting for the spawn, and then as soon as the camp respawned I stood up and charmed on of them. Now, at my level, most people don't know about charm, so I ran around in cirlces shouting "Help! Help!" People blasted the thing through and through, but still it wasn't dying. People tried to attack it, but they couldn't. So I ran around, the thing "chasing" me, chaos reigning, when someone nearby said "Run!"

Now, of course, I thought this was pretty silly, becuase I was running. I was about to make some snide comment, when the next thing I new a hill giant stepped on me. I think I deserved it.

 

Death story number 2:

I was in Unrest with my halfling warrior, fighting away, having a grand ole time, when I accidently pulled a train. It's Unrest, trains happen, no big deal. I ran and ran, but I was hopelessly far away from the zone, so I used my hat.

Now, I think I should tell you about my hat. To get this hat, you have to get four skins from all around the world, and a dragoon dirk. I had to walk around for a very long time, spent forever campin the damn alligator skin, paid upwards of 100 plat in expenses (75 pp dirk, 10 pp teleport, 1 pp bind, etc etc). After finally spending hours doing this, I finally got it. The hat has 7 ac (or was it nine?) +5 str, +5 agi, +5 sv magic, and +10 cha. It also has one charge of "Ring of Commons" (which I think should be renamed to "Save yer Ass").

Anyhoo, as I was saying, I used my hat, which mircaulessly wisked me away from the large amounts of undead trying to kill me. I pop not at the druid circle, but at the conveneatly places "Safe Area" that is in every zone. These areas never have bad guys around them, and usually teleport you there when you zone with very little life. Unfourtunely, this Safe Area was also located about 50 feet above the ground. I fell, took 99 damage, and died. This what it looked like
