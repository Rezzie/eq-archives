Golden Efreet Boot Camp

by Balgair
(to the tune of 'Hotel California')
(LBJ adds: by The Eagles!!!)


Here's a song written at a real GEB raid when I was bored to death. Its almost accurate.
- Balgair, Mage of Xegony

 

On a dark night in SOL B,
Sulphur in my hair
As we strode forth so boldly
Through Nagafen's Lair

Up ahead in the distance
Saw a terrible sight
Three big imps at the lava pool
Guess there'll be a big fight

There they stood in the doorway
As I cast my spell
I was thinking to myself
This could be easy or this could be Hell

Then we jazzed up the battle
And we blew them away
There were voices in the corridor
And I heard them /say

Welcome to the Golden Efreet Boots Camp
Such a deadly place (Such a deadly place)
It's a spawn time race
Plenty of imps at the Golden Efreet Boots Camp
Anytime of year
You can find imps here.

New guy is definitely twink-ed
Ykeshas in his hands
He got a lotta sixty level guys
He calls friends

And he claims he's not looted
His seventh boots yet
He camps all for Ebay
He camps to pay debt

I sent a tell to the Raid Boss
As he began to whine
Boss said, this guys been on this raid
Since last month, on the 9th

And still his whining keeps coming
From far away
Irritates all the members of my group,
I can hear them say

Welcome to the Golden Efreet Boots Camp
Such a twinked-out place (Such a twinked-out place)
It's an Ebay base
Plenty of twinks at the Golden Efreet Boots Camp
Anytime of year
You can find twinks here.

Fought past the lava pool
'Crept by on the right
Raid Boss said
All casters can meditate here
This is our last fight

Into Lord Djarn's chambers
We charged in for the beast
Stabbed him with our steely knives
Until Lord Djarn was deceased

Last thing I remembered
The Twink stole a pair of boots
Tried to run through passage back
And ninja off with the loot

He's Dead said the Boss man
That's the last time he'll deceive
We can port out anytime we like
But he can never leave.

(Guitar Solo with sounds of Imps tearing the ninja ranger apart as he tries to run to zone)
