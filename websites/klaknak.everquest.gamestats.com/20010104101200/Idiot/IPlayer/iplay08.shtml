Lore Items

by Flintis

My friend Krindol (lvl 15 dwarf paladin) and I (Flintis lvl 15 dwarf cleric), had decided to go on the screaming mace quest. So we fought emporer crush alot (with the help of another party member) and ended up with 2 count 'em 2 dwarven ringmail tunics! Well after waiting a very long time to get a shot at the warlord to get his bracer of battle, we heeded the advice of a friend and decided to abandon the quest after all. Ok, so no big deal, we'll just sell the tunics right? Hey for 2 that's a pretty penny! Well Krindol is the wheeler dealer of the two of us, so he is the one that usually finds buyers for our items.

And true to his nature he had a buyer for one of the tunics very quickly. His tunic was in the bank, and I had mine on me, so I went to meet him to finish the deal. Everything was ready, so I traded him the tunic.......um where did the tunic go? Did you get it Krin? No? Well I dont have it either! Where the hell could it have.....Oh dammit! He had one in the bank, so when I gave him mine *POOF* it just disapeared! 30 plat gone just like that!! Damn lore items....
