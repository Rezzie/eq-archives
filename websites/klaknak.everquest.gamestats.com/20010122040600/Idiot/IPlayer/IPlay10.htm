ESG - by the Cackling Klaknak

I had just taken my Cleric from level 14 to 15 so I decided it was time to assert myself in a more solo way in Oasis.

As soon as I pop in, a magi of some sort asked if I wanted to group, and I decided ... sure, why not ... maybe it will keep me from suffering from the inevitable "just levelled death."

So we group up and start fighting this Orc priest.

The fight was very frustrating because both of us were relying a little too much on melee/pets and the priest kept on healing himself. Finally, pissed a little, I said:

I said: Burn this guy hard.

My partner said:

He said: ESG!

It's a shame we can't have our actual real life thoughts somehow transferred to the screen for all the world to see, because this was my exact real life thought:

Me: ESG? What is an ESG? Is it some sort of staff?

At which point, a Sand Giant sent me packing to level 14.

The end.
