Burn Baby Burn - by Menix

About a month or 2 ago I was playing my wood elf druid who was level 16 or so, since the server I was playing on was fairly new I made some money by travelling from Freeport to Qeynos to sell derv rings. On one of those trips I was bored to hell I had bound myself in Highpass and when I reached Qeynos I decided that it would be faster to sell the rings and gate back without binding. So as any idiot does when I was selling one pair of rings I decided to buff my customer but ended up hitting my Ignite spell which of course didn't do any damage since we weren't PvP but it sure upset the guards, like a fool I ran the wrong way (inside Qeynos instead of outside) and fell in to the water and was promptly killed, I got so pissed when I respawned in Highpass that I stopped playing for half a week but finally made the trip to get my corpse.
