Lay Hands on Idiot - by Rob

Ah my first Paladin, Died a bit but figured out how to kill stuff After a while I figured I could start soloing white and yellow cons After many an attempt where my lay hands failed and I died I noticed that the monster I was just beating on was full on hit points I figured I had done some damage but I guess it just had a lot of hitpoints. After this occurring many times all coinciding with my failure with lay hands and my untimely demise shortly thereafter I happened upon a web page (perhaps Allakazam's) and read that lay hands never failed and couldn't be interrupted that's when I realized you had to click on yourself before laying hands I had been healing the monsters the whole time. Doh
