Why Bagels?

by Zolor, Dark Elf Shadow Knight The Nameless Server

About 3 weeks ago I got my wife hooked on EverQuest (I've been playing for months and months) so we both created new first-level characters, both Dark Elves, with me a Shadow Knight and her an Enchantress. We've had a lot of fun playing together in the same room, helping each other and teaming together to tackle monsters. As we gained levels, I started learing the Tailoring skill, and by level 8 or so I was doing pretty well financially.

When my wife reached level 8 (and the Enchant Silver spell became available) she decided to become a jeweler. Well, I was the one with all the cash (from making and selling patchwork and raw silk armor) so I went to the shop and bought her some silver bars and some Cat's Eye Agates. She went to her guild and trained in Jewelery Making, and then went to the Library in Neriak Third Gate to buy the Enchant Silver spell. Then we both met up for her first attempt at making jewelry. I initiated the trade, to give her the silver bars and gems. When she saw the Cat's Eye Agates in the trade window, she remarked out loud, "I thought you were going to buy me gems! Why in the world did you buy me a bunch of bagels?"

Evidently she'd seen Cat's Eye Agates for sale in shops before, but never clicked on them, thinking they were bagels (like the loaves of bread and muffins you can buy for food). I was laughing so hard it took me about 5 minutes before I could speak! :-)
