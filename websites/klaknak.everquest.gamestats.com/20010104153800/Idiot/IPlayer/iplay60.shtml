Level 30

by Curghann Bearfist, Rathe server

Well, here is my first official submission to your site for humor. I loved reading all the idiot files, thinking I was soo much better than all those fools till it happened.

I hit lvl 30 finally. I was so excited, it had finally happened! Yay. Ok, excitement time over, back to what I was doing, transferring some items to another char of mine. So a friend logs in a newbie mule at Halas for me and I give him lots of goodies. He takes them all and puts them on and says, "Hey man, I had to destroy my food and water to hold all that stuff, now that I'm wearing it I have room, kill me so it'll come back." and he promptly /duel's me.

I laugh at his puny lvl 1 ass and fire off my potent Frost Strike spell...WHAMMO! One dead lvl 1 barbarian warrior. Well the next 5 seconds were a blur of guards and stuns and lots and lots of damage, ending in the Loading, Please Wait.....You have lost a level, Welcome to Level 29! Guess the Halas guards didnt take to kindly to my murder.

So I'm mad, my friend comes back to his bind spot and sees my corpse and says he's real sorry. We do the item swaps and he logs in his druid, saying he's gonna help me get my lvl back. We whisk away to faydwer and enter the undersea world of Kedge Keep where he says he'll pull and heal me and I'll get it back fast. This works great except for a couple crashes of my machine and soon Im back in my lvl 30 glory. In that doing I got a fin needed for my totemic quest, yay! I'll go make my tunic. I rush to South Karana on my way to Lake Rathe when a guild friend of mine asks for a little help on the PH spawn. Me being the nice guy I am rush to his aid. I buff them, buff myself (put on a levitate, dont wanna fall off and die and lose my new lvl do I?) and heal them a bit.

I med as Im now low on mana and they ask me to kill the Avocet that has spawned, Im big and bad, Im lvl 30, "Sure!" I say. I med up a little more, slap on some ofmy fancy shmancy new lvl 29 spells and start whackin on that bird. It's going pretty good when another bird spawns on my head....Uh oh, Im getting pummeled. I send a quick "Im gonna zone" to my guildy and promptly run off the edge of the PH in the direction of Lake Rathe.....

I really should have checked to make sure that levitiate was still up...I zone all right....Loading, Please Wait.....Welcome to Level 29. /cry /cry /cry.

Well there it is, for your reading pleasure, the idiocy of a lvl 30...oops 29 shaman.

- Curghann Bearfist, Order of Valhalla Shaman of Rathe
