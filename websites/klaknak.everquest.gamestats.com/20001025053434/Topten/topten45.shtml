Top 10 things you would hear if they were old people in EQ - by Puck

10. I remember when I walked 15 zones to get to the boat and wait 4 hours to try to get on the boat just to get dumped in the ocean.

9. There was a day when you could sell a rune totem staff for 30pp! That was a lot in my day, which was good enough for a full set of leather!

8. All you little whippersnappers with your "plane raids" and "dragon raids". In my day we were happy if we didn't die in Blackburrow 4 times in one day! And let me tell you something, Blackburrow was the only place to hunt!

7. We were scared to leave our protected newbie zones! Rumors of Treants and Hill giants kept us away from the evil Karanas.

6. I remeber a day when there were only 30 people in Oasis at any one time, and that was during peak hours!

5. We didn't know about muffins and milk, we ate iron rations and water just because we didn't know what iron rations were!

4. We didn't have to wait to get into a group with all these "lists" and whatnot. We walked into the zone, shouted our class and level, and if we got a group we stayed. If we didn't we moved on to somewhere else!

3. The PGT was the best weapon!

2. And blacksmithing was almost a possible trade skill! None of this bronze or quest armor. It's all poppycock!

1. We said poppycock and we liked it!
