Color Wha? - by the Cackling Klaknak

Ok, I am far from being a newbie, but since I have basically played nothing but clerics and the occasional rogue, there is still much I do not understand about other characters and their powers.

My guildmates and I were fighting in Oasis and I was bored with my spells. Usually, I whack on the critter a bit, then maybe cast a heal spell or two, and wait for the next critter.

So I flipped through my spell book until I saw that I had memorized Blind Critter (or whatever the hell it is called), so I decided to use it. I had heard the spell was basically useless except for PvP, but with such a low mana cost, I figured "What the hell."

So when our group started fighting orcs and whatnot, I was surprised to find that the blind spell was amazingly effective. Whenever I cast the spell, the orc (or Whatnot) simply stopped fighting. After about an hour of this, I told my guildmates:

"Wow, I dont know why people dont use Blind more often. It rocks!"

At which time most of my guildmates responded (in near unison):

"That is color flux."

Oh.
