3. And if the group plans on tackling an undead dungeon ... forgetaboutit ... clerics rock! They have great damage and utility spells to aid the group in an adventure through Befallen or wherever.

The best way to make lots of plat and receive nice items is to go into a very dangerous place filled with upper level characters and perform free services for them.
