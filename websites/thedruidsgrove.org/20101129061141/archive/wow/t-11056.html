Moonfire can rack up the HK's in any BG, however WSG is not about kills anyway. It's about snare and go cap, rinse repeat. The team should be assisting to kill or snare a supporting healer perhaps. Other than that your just keeping the other team away from your carrier. Kill em and they just respawn with full mana and return to the fight while your mana and health are still suffering from the long fight. If you want to rack up a lot tof HK's or KB's spend time in AB or AV. Druids rarely get high numbers of KB's because they lack a high damage instant cast.

Warriors execute
Shaman shock
Mages fire blast or cone of cold
Warlocks shadowburn
Hunters multishot

But the moonfire is not enough dmg usually to get the killing blow if your fighting in groups. So I would definately just work on maximizing your HK's.
