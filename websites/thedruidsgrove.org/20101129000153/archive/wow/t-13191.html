As for spirit...Spirit is a caster stat, not healing or DPS.

+Hit = DPS
+MP5 = Healer
+Anything else = all casters, potentially class specific.

Spirit is HUGE for a boomkin. Spirit = More spellpower and more mana regen, + a more effective innervate!

Agree with Solar. For a min-maxing guild that is probably overrun with melee dps (DK's ad nauseum) a Boomkin is a huge get.

Besides, your 1500 dps output will double with barely a token effort on your part. Put your back into it and you'll be at or near the top of the WWS at the end of the night.

Spirit gear will tend to go to healing druids and priests first as they get the most benefit from it. However, any caster class can use it.

As we talked about above though, stack hit. As Boomkin you have so many mana regen talents available to you that you should never have a problem. Start by taking Moonglow, Intensity, and Dreamstate. If you are finishing raid boss fights with 1/3 or more mana, drop Dreamstate. Rinse and repeat for Intensity and Moonglow, remembering that you will always be forced to have one point in Moonglow to move on to the next talent tier.
