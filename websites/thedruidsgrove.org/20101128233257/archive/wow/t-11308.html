Mangle is way better than OoC when we need to choose now before the expansion. :)

I've been full pve tank spec since the patch -- 0/41/10. We've stopped raiding for the holidays now, so I'll be going 1/41/9 pvp spec until we start again. ^^

I must say, ILotP is very, very good in a tank spec. I figured it'd be handy, but at 6% now it's almost overpowered. When I *tank* (i.e. when I take a lot of damage) on boss fights and trash we've got on farm in BWL, AQ40 and Nax, I heal myself for about 10% of the damage I take, after substracting overhealing. That's pretty imba tbh, and I can see why they reduce it to 4% in the next patch.

It does work on npcs in your group as well. E.g. on Razuvious fight in Nax, stick a feral with ILotP in the priest mind control group, and grin as you see the understudies heal themselves (they healed over 120k from my aura in one fight -- not close to a full healer doing just healing, but very nice for a bear that was tanking at the same time.) For the same reason, don't use it in fights where you get mind controlled. Watching the boss regen from 5% to 11% while you're mind controlled, can't be very fun. :-< (Not happened to me, but have spoken to people who had it happen.)
