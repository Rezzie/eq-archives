Really depends. Some pieces of Elysian armor blow away Woven grass, other pieces do not.

Woven Grass bracers are a good example of WG being better then Elsyian for one. 9 wis 30 hp/mana on WG versus 3 wis 15 mana on Eylsian.

Woven Grass BP has more hp/mana then the Elysian tunic, though the elysian tunic has better resists. However, if your primarily raiding Luclin, resists are meaningless. 74 HP 80 mana on WG versus 30 hp 75 mana on Elysian.

Woven Grass Legs vs Elysian leggings are the real harder decision. 35 hp and 90 mana on WG leggings vs 95 HP on Eylsian leggings

Headpiece not worth it.

Boots: 50 mana WG boots vs 30 hp 15 mana Elysian boots

Arms: 30 hp/mana WG arms vs 43 hp and 25 mana. Elysian more hp here.
