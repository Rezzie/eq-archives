I agree with Eiram about getting items that increase your wis/HP/Mana. Here are a couple of suggestions that either do not have a level requirement or will last for a bit, items with an * are first choice:

Ear: Protection of Cabbage Quest for earrings: Earring of the Solstice and Xanthe's Earring of Nature - the items for this quest are usually sold in the bazaar and sometimes as the full set of items needed

Ring: Coral Ring*
Diamond Wedding Band*

Hands: Grey Flesh Gloves*
Cunning Gauntlets of the Kin

Feet: Golden Efreeti Boots*

Neck: Black Alloy Medallion *

Head: Othmir Fur Cap*
Nightshade Wreath
Savant's Cap

Legs: Stormreaper's Boned Leggins * (this is class/diety specific to Halfling druids - there is a whole line of this armor)
Brown Chitin Leggings
Bloodweave Pantaloons

Waist: Bone-clasped Girdle*
Runic Belt of Foresight
Girdle of Rapidity
Girdle of Mithaniel

Again, there is a whole line of halfling karana druid armor - not all of it is worth getting - but the legs and tunic might be something to look at.

Good luck!

you won't need to upgrade the chest slot for awhile. the +WIS and +STA gives you good hp/mana at the moment.

wait until at least 35 before getting a new tunic. at 35, you can start to wear the expert's level armor (from DoN cultural). then if you wish, you can start getting PoP armor, these have required level of 46 (something like dream haunt tunic).

if you want to upgrade, try boiled leather cuirass. if you really want to spend some cash, go with feran tunic 10-14K.
