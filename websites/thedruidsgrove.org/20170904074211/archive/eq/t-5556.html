Originally posted by Kineada
You don't get med mana while casting, moving or attacking. If you're not doing anything, then you get the meditate benefits.

You DO get med mana while casting. Not while moving or attacking, true, but when casting, yes you do gain the med benefit.

Test it. Go to toilers in ME, pull a single, and root/rot it without the horse. Now do another, and root/rot with the horse. If all your DoTs are applied and you have nothing else to do, keep casting a clickie DoT. Basically, just always be casting something.

You will see you have more mana doing it with the horse than compared to without.
