Note on this walkthrough: It's been over a month since I've done this quest and my memory is a bit rusty on it. I tried to ensure everything in it was correct, but if you see something wrong, please mention it and I'll correct it.

So, you've hit level 10, and you're wondering how to get bear form? Wonder no longer!

Depending on where you are at level 10, the druid trainers you talk to may direct you to Darnassus. If you're already in Darnassus, the trainer on the first floor will have you go talk to the one on the second floor, Mathrengyl Bearwalker. Mathrengyl will give you a new spell, Teleport: Moonglade, and direct you to Dendrite Starblaze in Nighthaven. Use the new teleport to get there; Dendrite is on the second floor of the building near where you port in.

Next up, you need to talk to the Great Bear Spirit; he's just a bit northwest of Nighthaven. You can see his location on the map attached at the bottom of this walkthrough. Head back to Dendrite, and then take the hippogryph back to Rut'theran then Darkshore -- the hippogryph master is in the southwest portion of Nighthaven (or just hearthstone back).

Lunaclaw is just east of Auberdine in Darkshore, inside a cave; see the map attached below for the location of the moonkin stone. Lunaclaw is level 13, and there are level 10-12 moonkin outside the cave, so you might wish to bring a friend or two to make it easier. However, it should be doable solo if you're careful. Be sure to be buffed up and ready to go before you summon him.

After you kill Lunaclaw and talk to his spirit, return to Moonglade and talk to Dendrite Starblaze again. Dendrite will send you back to Darnassus and Mathrengyl, who will allow you to learn bear form!
