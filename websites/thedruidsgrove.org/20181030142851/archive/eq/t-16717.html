Icefall glacier is decent xp. Either the spiders, mammoth's, or bears. Charm kiting or root rotting both work well. If you have a decent mana pool then the goblin fort in Loping Plains is great xp especially if you do the faction tasks. Fight or Flight, Creepfeet Thievery, and Reducing the Threat - Goblins are the only three I'd bother with as the others require too much running around and that means no xp while travelling. Be advised these goblins have a good bit of hit points, so if you are light on mana and/or aa's then it isn't a good idea.

Also you could kill gnolls in The Steppes, if you can slaughter them quickly. Either charming a nearby lizard or cat or root rotting work. They have less hp, but don't yield quite as much xp as the Icefall mobs do. Still should be a little safer and not so mana intensive as Icefall Glacier or Loping Plains.

I am sure there are other camps that work equally well.
