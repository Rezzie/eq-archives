Welcome!

Just wanted to say that in WoW we don't say "cross-class". There is no cross-classing.
A talent spec that mixes elements from two different roles (tanking, dps, healing) is usually called a hybrid-build.

With regards to gear: I suspect you are leveling at the moment, so just take what you can get. The stat priority given by Annikk is perfect for leveling such a build.
Don't be shy about equipping cloth items. Yes, we druids wear leather, but for a caster it makes no difference, really, and up until level 60 most good caster items are cloth.
