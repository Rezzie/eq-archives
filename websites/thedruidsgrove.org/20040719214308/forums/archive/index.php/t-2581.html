After completing 2 adventures in any given zone you can "Hail" one of the people there to give you some information. After you receive your 5th piece of info (10 completed adventures) you charm will get +2mana/hp. You can continue to do this at every camp up until 100 total, completed adventures in each theme (500 total) and your charm will be maxed at either double stats or +100/100 over what it started as (I'm not really sure).
