Nefarion does an ability that forces warriors into berserker stance, causing them to take a lot more damage.
That same ability has an effect on druids, too. It forces druids into catform.

A bear tanking the dragon that is suddenly forced into cat form is going to be very squishy indeed...
This means a bear tank would be no good on Nefarion, right?

Wrong.

A druid who has never learned cat form from the trainer is immune to Nefarion's class call ability. They don't turn into cats, and so never take extra damage and suffer no ill-effects whatsoever.

sneaky druid tricks.. =)
