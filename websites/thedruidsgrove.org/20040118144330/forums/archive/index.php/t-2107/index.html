1) If you have a manastone on a server its never dropped on. There's a good chance the GM will delete it when he finds it. So keep it quiet. There have been two that I know of Deleted on Morell Thule as soon as they hit the bazaar.

2) POP Zones won't work well for this. They have insane regen on the mobs so while your out clicking your mana up the mob will be regenerating like crazy. Your better off with a lumi-staff and resnaring while your mana regens normally for a big nuke. The only way this will work is with a partner who keeps aggro while you use the stone. Two people know then, its not a secret anymore. See item 1.

3) Level 60. Ding 61 and your exp changes signifigantly. I solo'd through 60 in the Grey and would do it again for cash/exp. Can't bind there though =( You can bind at the vampire castle in tenebrous though which would make an OK cash camp (tough at level 60). Or in Maidens eye or umbral Plains and quadkite.

I hit 60 with my druid right after Velious came out, thus I was soloing for it. What I would do is:
bind in Skyfire Mtns.
have a cleric with CH on the second box sitting at my favorite druid ring (lavastorm mtns)
blow mana as fast as I could killing 2-3 mobs in the NE corner of the zone
port to LS Mtns
hit the manastone hard
CH CH CH
full mana
gate back

rinse, repeat, recycle

If you don't have someone waiting to heal you when you land in the old world, I wouldn't even bother doing this.
