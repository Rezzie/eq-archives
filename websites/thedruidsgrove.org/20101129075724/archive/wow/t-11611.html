The number of rogues varies wildly, we had runs with none and runs with 7.

To prevent a wipe: If you are in the group as DD, wait stealthed in cat-form near the healer and pounce the moment the rogue pops out. Have patience that could take 10-20 secs of waiting and letting the other DPS. If you are the tank, stay close to your healer and bash the moment the rogue pops out. Use feral charge if you don't want to stay that close.

With that tactic you should no longer wipe because of rogues.

If the rogue still kills your healer: Shift out -> barkskin -> tranquility -> b-rezz -> innervate the healer -> throw some heals and hots around -> bear -> get aggro and keep the mobs of your healer.
The standard move in "oh crap"-situations.
