Okay, your best bets for upgrades are in the jewelry slots I'd suggest some Dwerium jewelry (namely earring and ring for you) put some Pear cut Prestidigtase, Harmonagate, Taaffeite (in order from cheapest to most expensive). Also get some round cut Emeralds(150mana aug for these pieces) and Opals(150hp augs for these pieces). Here is a link to a website that calculates the stats of the gear you could make (Dwerium Trio) http://www.kyros.info/Tradeskills/JC-Calc/
Other than that as for your secondary if you don't need the focus (didn't check your gear to see if you had other foci of this line) you could do 69.1 for a Blood defender -> http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=44733
If you happen to get TBS or SoF your options of soloable or duoable gear will expand. For example you can easily duo upgrades in SoF for example Chiliform Necklace, Moonlight Cape, Flail of Redemption, Drape of the Waterborn etc. there are countless upgrades you could get there with the mage pet tanking and you healing him. As for leveling up and AAs Icefall is your best bet, the swarm kiting exp there is the best you will get solo, if duo swarm kite but have your mage friend burn down the mob that gets on low health and sic his pet (preferably Earth one so he can root it and get it away from the pack so it doesn't die or mabye Air for stun). As for some AA device, I'd suggest getting the two mana regen lines maxed for your level, Expansive Mind and Mental Clarity.
