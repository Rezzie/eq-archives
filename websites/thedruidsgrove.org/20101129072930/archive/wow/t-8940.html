If you haven't been to Shimmering Flats in Tousand Needles or to Desolace those are 2 good places to start grabbing some quests. I particularily like 1K to get there you will need to head down to Booty Bay in STV and take the boat to Rachet. Once in Rachet you will need to head south to the great lift. Avoid Crossroads at the great lift you can cat sneak onto the lifts to avoid guard spam or you can jump off the cliff and suicide ress at the graveyard below. 1K might not be the best place for you if you are on a PvP server since getting there is traveling through horde dominant areas however Shimmering flats is goblin neutral.

Desolace is a bit higher but you can start quests there. You'll need to go from Astranaar through the passage to Stonetalon Mountains and south to Desolace.

I did a search on Allas for quests between levels 28 and 33, you'll have to hunt around in there for Alliance ones you haven't done but it's a start.
