As mentioned, the days of snare and spam flamelick and done and gone. You need to be doing damage if you intend to play chase-the-druid. The NBW line does wonders to maintain agro, but it is mana intensive. The Winter's line of nukes also is supposed to generate extra hate, although it doesn't seem like much to me.

Make sure your mage buddy has taunt off, and preferably using his water pet for the backstabs. The massive stuns from the air pet are going to gank agro from you regularly. Mage can also /pet nocast if he has the AAs for it, but then you are giving up a little damage from the pet.

Best way I have found to manage agro is by using HOTT. The instant you see the mob change target -- sit down and you'll get agro back right now. Nothing pisses a mob off more than a seated caster! It buys you a little time to get the mob coming back towards you so you can cast for more agro. Obviously, don't do this if the mob is a foot away from you hehe.
