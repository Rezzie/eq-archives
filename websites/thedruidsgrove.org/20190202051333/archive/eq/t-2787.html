Most PoP zones have DC-bait mobs in them (even the Elemental Planes). There is usually an animal specific one (druids), an undead specific one (necros) and a generic one for the chanters. You can't use them to tank like you can with a CoK or CoT pet, but they do add a pretty reasonable amount to your DPS in a group, especially once hasted.

The ones I remember (Tier 2+ - most Tier 1 zones have quite a few DC'able mobs, assuming the zone has animals of some sort):

PoValor - a small tree frog
PoStorms - a grezlan cub
PoTactics - one of the boars, can't remember its name
CoDecay - a scrounging rat
PoTorment - dunno, always forgot to check on raids.
BoThunder - None that I know of
Lair of Terris Thule - None.
HoHonor - a wrulon cub (or something like that)
HoHonorB - None.
Tower of Sol Ro - None that I know of
PoFire - a young obisidian spiderling
PoWater - can't remember name, but I'm sure I've seen green con mobs in track in here.
PoEarth - can't remember its name, but it spawns when one of the armadillo things dies
PoAir - dunno

Cheers,

Sorran.
