The only weak spot that doesn't really match up imo, is your legs. They're great for the clicky, but if you're looking for stats I'd recommend the DoN or Omens tier 1 armor quest (which you already have the boots for). Otherwise, Icedrop Pearl (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=70327) is a random zone drop in Riftseekers. From the look of it, your gear is pretty good and you have most of the foci that will be useful to ya. For a non-raider and except for the items above, you'd prolly need DoD mission loot to improve your gear significantly, or find a decent off-hours raiding guild (there's a few here and there).
