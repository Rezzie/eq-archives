[QUOTE=Talamas]I'm not sure if this is a problem with the PVP in Zek but you can get a couple peeps to RCoD and farm thenamed there for your RUnes as well as descent stuff to sell.

At 66 you would need a full group for RCoD. Things are normally ok there, but on occassion you do get a roaming raid mob take you out. The PvP aspect is also a threat, but RCoD even when OOW was new was eeriely quiet. There are a few epic mobs here, and I dont know how that works on a PVP server but imagine a raid force turning up while your party hunted? Would they not come and fleece you?

For gear, runes, augs and rare stuff to enhance the augs (slugworms etc, probably sell well), I would recommend Dranik Scar Hollows (Instanced zone).

You need 3 to get an expedition, the person getting the expedition sets the level. There are 2 names to be found in each but when you zone in the PH's are up. Take a couple of friends, a slower and a tank, as the named will summon.
There are 3 different instances, Murkglider Hive, Firepit and Watering Hole.
Each have some good drops, mainly the rare, and the chance of 66-68 spells.
Theres a regen neck (or earring) which may suit your Pally in Murkgliders, a FT3 shield in the Firepit, and a 2 regen ft2 ring in the watering hole.

But thats if the person is 65-67.

At 68 the locations of the named and some of the ph's change and so do the drops. Still only 66-68 runes drop but theres a quite nice Dru/Monk/Bsty wrist item in Murks and a very nice 1 h b for a Pally/Shd from the Firepit.

There are various things you can do here to maximise what ever you need;

3 People need to be in a group to get an expedition but they dont have to all
stay in it, you can drop the number to just 1 or change the people in the expedition (type /dzhelp for all the commands)

You can log your Pally on to get the expedition, then make someone else leader, remove your Pally add your Druid and farm for items for your Pally. No runes drop from instances under 65 though, but still some nice items for the under 60's.

I would say instances are the way to go. If you are after some nice gear and no drop stuff and want a challenge try the sewer instances in GoD.
