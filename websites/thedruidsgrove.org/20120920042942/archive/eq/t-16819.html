As a general rule, the first 1-3 levels of an AA give you the biggest bang for your buck. After that, you are going to start seeing diminishing returns whether it be healing AAs, damage AA's or survivability AA's. The more you accumulate, the harder you have to work for ever decreasing benefits.

Perhaps someone can provide details regarding the specific lines in your inquiry.

Let's look at Healing Gift:

The first three ranks of this ability grant you a chance to score an exceptional heal at 3, 6, and 10 percent.
Additional ranks further increase the chance by 2 percent per rank. An exceptional heal doubles the healing value of the spell.

The corresponding AA costs are:

2 4 6 2 3 4 3 6 9 6 6 6 7 9 12

So you get in steps:

19% HG for 12 AAs
6% HG for 9 AAs
6% HG for 18 AAs
6% HG for 18 AAs
6% HG for 28 AAs

I worked up to the 4th group and I can say it is worth it!

On any AA where it gives an increasing percentage for the first three, you don't add them together for the final amount unfortunately - the final figure is the total effect. The first three ranks of HG add up to 10% not 19%.

See here http://samanna.net/eq.shaman/demystified.shtml for a very detailed breakdown.
