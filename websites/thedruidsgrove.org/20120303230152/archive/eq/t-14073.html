Bear-Hide Boots (Do-able at Level 1)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1753
Very easy, easy quest for your first set of Focus Armor. (Extended Enhancement I)
Helps increase the duration of all 1-20 spells.

Wee Harvester (Do-able at Level 1)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=494
Also another very easy quest to do. This is a great starting weapon with a decent amount of +wis. It always takes you by the Bear-Hide Boots NPC so you can complete both quests at once.

Paw of Opolla (Do-able around level 25)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=2320
When your ready to upgrade your Wee Harvester it's time to get two nice hand-held items. This is one of them. Paw of Opolla is sometimes a time consuming quest but very easy for it's reward. It took me around 6-8 hours total to finish this quest. Give it a shoot, it's a very rewarding feeling to finally get it.

Testament of Vanear (Do-able around level 15)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=2336
To compliment your new Paw of Opolla, you have the Testament of Vanear. Quite possibly the easiest quest to do for a nice +10 wisdom Book. Only issue is the availablity of the quest NPC. He is camped round the clock, especially on the Combine Server.

Polished Bone Bracelet (Camp-able around level 20)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=785
Best camp EVER. Me and my Bard friend camped the Evil-eyes in the Gorge and in a matter of 4 hours, we generated around 300pp in Gems + Loot. Go there, now.
P.S. Polished Bone Hoop drops there also. They are starter earrings that just add Mana if I'm correct.

Chipped Bone Bracelet (Camp-able [alone] around level 30-35)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=809
Amazing Bracers for three reasons.
1) Non-Lore. You can have TWO!
2) +5 Wisdom
3) Afflication Efficiency II Focus. Decreases the mana cost of all DoTs. (Two Similar Focuses Do Not Stack)
Very easy camp once the spawn is broken. The Chief is a bit difficult, but Root + DoT = dead Chief. MAKE SURE HE STAYS ROOTED. If root breaks, and you are rooted. Your dead.

Bat-Fang Headband (Camp-able [alone] around level 30-35)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1400
Same camp as Chipped Bone Bracelet. Great cap with Extened Enhancement II Focus, upgrading your Beat-Hide Boots.

Aviak Charm (Camp-able around level 26)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1145
Besides the +1 wisdom for the neck piece which is a plus. The main reason for this item is the Burning Afflication I focus. This easily increased the damage of each one of my dots by 5-10 damage a tick. Very worth wild neck piece.

Kejekan Tribal Headband (Do-able around level 25)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6611
No real experience doing this quest, but the reward is well worth it.

Pearl Kedge Totem (Camp-able around level 30)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=2318
Something I am still camping at the moment. In Dagnor's Cauldron there is a named goblin that dorps this +18 Wisdom hand-held item. My best advice to you is the following. When you sign on, go to Dagnor's, run around the rim of the lake and check track. No goblin, no problem. Leave and enjoy your day. When your done EXPing/Questing for the day, head to Dagnor's and check on the goblin. I guarentee if you follow this process at one point you will catch the Goblin when he's up.

Reed Belt (Camp-able around level 30)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=959
Drops off of Blige the goblin in Dagnor's also. In addition to having great wisdom on the belt. (+5 Wisdom) It possesses Burning Afflication II! (I want this! :))

Braided Ivory Cords (Camp-able around level 25)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1021
Great +Wisdom shoulder piece. Everytime you go to Mistmoore to EXP, check track through Lesser Fay. You might catch this fella on track. I happen to finish exping in MM and was sellin at the mercant in LFay, when I heard him fighting the guards outside. Unfortunately the mob was near death so I couldn't get the Cords.



This is some low-mid range items easily aquireable for us druids out there. Hopefully this helps.
Take care.
Message me on Combine if you need any more help/advice.
