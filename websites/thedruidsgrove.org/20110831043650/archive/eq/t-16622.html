Allright ... where to start ...

Well one of my favourite stories always was working together with the SoE translators. I used to GM on the Ubisoft servers and we had quite the interesting translations there on the german and french server.

At one point rather early after the start of the EQ Europe project we realized that all - and I mean ALL - goblins in the game were called kobolds on the german and french server. When we asked the translators, why they did that they said, they didn't know how to translate the word goblin ... well ... there IS no translation to that word ... a goblin is a goblin, that goes for english, french or german.

So we off course inquired: "Well ... if you translated the goblins with kobolds ... what did you call the kobolds in the game?" And I couldn't believe it when they told us they translated them as "goblins". :eek:

Actually, the Sony translators provided us with quite some fun that we in the end had to fix ... By contract we were not allowed to translate stuff ourself, but we always ended up fixing the borked translation files ... in the end they sent them to us first, we proof-read them ... and they ended up putting in the wrong ones anyway :P
