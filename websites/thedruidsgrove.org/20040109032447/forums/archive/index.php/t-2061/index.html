kineada
Tier 1 until you get enough levels for PoV/PoS. Once you are tier 2 enabled, then old skool snare/DoT kite. You prolly won't have the manapool to straight up nuke kite just yet.

I would suggest switching spec to conjuration (if evocation spec, leave alteration alone and just kill a little bit slower - alteration druids get more efficient exp grouping than soloing anyways).

Don't worry about having to switch back to evoc for a while. You will need a ton of exp before you an start nuke kiting again. You'll need level 65, SCM3 (12aa), planar power (10aa), innate enlightenment (15aa) and, depending of wisdom from equipment, innate + advanced innate wisdom (15aa). You will prolly want dire charm (9aa) as well for that extra dps pet.

Once you get enough mana to nuke kite a mob down to 75% (with conj spec), then work on dire charm. Switch back to evoc then DC/nuke kite the rest of your AA's.
