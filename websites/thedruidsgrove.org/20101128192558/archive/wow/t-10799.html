You might be better off making a macro for the self buff/healer. Benecast was great for that (just click the little buttons that appear by your character portrait)....but it doesn't get updated as quick as needed now.

Here are some examples of macros that I have found.

Self Cast Nature's Swiftness/Healing Touch
/cast Nature's Swiftness
/target YourCharacterName
/cast Healing Touch(Rank 10)
/script TargetLastEnemy();

Nature's Swiftness Healing Touch
/cast Nature's Swiftness
/cast Healing Touch(Rank 10)

Self cast Innervate
/target YourCharacterName
/cast Innervate
/target YourCharacterName
/script TargetLastEnemy();

Hope that helps some!

Look for a addon on curse gaming called buffbot. I think that is what your looking for. Its bit out of date but it still works. Make sure its the updated one. There is an older version that is out of date and does not work right.

Basicly it works as follows. You set what buffs you want casted one each class and then it gives a warning a few seconds/mins before said buff wears out on your party. You click the button you hot keyed to it and it refreshes the buff. Once in while Omen messes up on it. And you need refresh the UI but reloading after aquiring new spells or you can camp and come back. It will also cast cures on those in your party/raid that need them but just hitting the related hot key. Over all its very nice addon all rolled into one.
