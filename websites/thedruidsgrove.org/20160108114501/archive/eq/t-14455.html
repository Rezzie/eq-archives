Hello. A really good spot that my brother and I found (he's a beastlord) are the bear caves in Icefall. There are quite a few bears that spawn fairly quickly and just to give you an idea of how much experience they give, I was getting 19% AA per -solo- kill in level 74. I haven't been back to check how much they give since I got 75. It is a really good spot, though, for a group, for kiting, or for pet kiting (which is what you'd be doing most likely). They are not immune to snare and they do not summon. One round of DoTs plus a couple nukes would probably bring a bear to about 80% health. It's definitely doable. I could kill one with 50% of my mana, with barely over a 10k mana pool.

Icefall is definitely going to be my new favorite zone in TSS at 70+... none of the animals are kos to a druid, wide open outdoor zone (think everfrost upgraded) and lots of spawns to accomodate even the 75 folks in zone in primetime with some left over. Choose your poison--- root/rotting, pet kiting, quad kiting if you have the mana-pool that allows it, charm kiting with a friendly wolf or bear... nothing seems to not work .

Caution though: Bears and wolves will cross-assist... mammoths seems to be neutral and do not cross-agro any of the other animals. Spiders are supposed to be excellent experience in zone as well, although I have not tried them (just too many bears/wolves/mammoths to keep my exp bar moving to try the unknown and risks associated with the unknown)
