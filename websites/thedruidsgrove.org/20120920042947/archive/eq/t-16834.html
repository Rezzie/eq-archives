Well, I can tell you how I would've done it. I run Hillary with a wide variety of other classes (SK, Monk, Ranger, Shaman, Cleric, Mage, Enc, Wiz, Bard, NEC) and if I had it to do over again, the path I would've taken would've been:

Run3
Metabolism 3
Spell Casting Mastery 3 (she's an Alteration spec)
Mnemonic Retention 1
Innate Camo
Entrap
Spirit of the Wood 1
Enhanced Root (for obvious reasons)
Healing Adept 3
Healing Gift 3
Call of the Wild (cheaper than EEs)
Shared Camouflage

...then I'd move into situational skills, depending on what's needed at the time. Usually, Hill's not counted on for DPS so her support and utility are of greater importance in my groups. I would suggest you go with the skills that best complement your beastlord.
