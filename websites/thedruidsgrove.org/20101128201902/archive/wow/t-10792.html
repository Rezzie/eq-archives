Definitely don't ignore caster stats. For feral you want to have the strength, stamina, agility....but don't forget to add some intellect in there too. You have to make sure you have enough mana to pop out every now and then so you can heal and then go right back into feral. Granted when you are in feral form you don't have to worry about mana.....but when you get caught in any sticky situations that extra mana will help.

I would suggest carrying 2 weapons with you at all times. Staves tend to have better caster/healer stats and Maces usually have a good combination of Feral and also some Caster/Healer stats.
