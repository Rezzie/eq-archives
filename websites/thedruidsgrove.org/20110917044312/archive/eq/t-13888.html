Attack Debuffs work well with mobs which have Low DB High DI, this applies to most trash mobs and single groupable named.

It doesn't slow the rate of damage, but lowers the amount of damage. This may make soloing a summoner easier unless it has a excessvily quick hit rate.

This is of course, assuming your trying to live through the damage spikes. If your dying due to being hit / interupted attack debuffs will not help you afaik.
