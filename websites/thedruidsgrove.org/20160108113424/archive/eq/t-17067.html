the 2nd fight took a little more time (not much) same thing had group 1 go and clear all the trash mobs ... then my group cleard to the bridge to make sure no mobs respawned then i walked up and once in the courtyard the mobs spawned.. group 1 engaged group 2 assisted... and they went down quick (mind & body killing body first) then they took on Gorlakt and we kept the MT healed as he took out Gorlakt.. we had a few Mag casting their DD's and i even got off a DoT or 2 before he went down.. all and all i think we spent more time buffing and getting the groups there then the total time of both fights combined...

i was a little nervous going into these fights... but they we're as bad as i thougt...
Just remember to clear the trash mobs first...

all i need now is Globe from Anguish and i'm done...

also as as side note... the next 2 mobs the 1 in Velk and the wurm in BW ... both soloable i found the mob in Velk in the room past Velketor room in the room where 6 mobs spawn... i just kept kill them till it spawned nothing big about this mob.. hits for about the same as the other mobs that spawn there..

the wurm in BW.. after getting him to spawn (spawned when i got close to loc pos 3000, pos 0. i just Dotted and ran.. got off 4 DoTs on him and his hp went down kinda quick... i got hit i think 2 times both nothing to worry about... but i did get kicked once.. it kicks for 5k but it died before it could kick me again..
