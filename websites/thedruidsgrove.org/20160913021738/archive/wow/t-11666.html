I was discussing taking with the warrior who heads the Kara runs I go on, and as part of the discussion, I came up with a spreadsheet to compare Druid and Warrior tanking stats with some arbitrary values set in.

Without going into specifics, the thing that was interesting is that while we have what you could consider basically equivalent gear, he's got a reasonable lead on me in physical damage taken from a level 73 mob, but if you drop the level to 72, which eliminates Crushing Blows, I take a significant lead on not only him, but I pass every single Warrior in our guild, including those on the "A-team" who have me seriously outgeared.

Now, there's more to tanking than just physical damage mitigation and there's a lot of assumptions built into the model, but it was a solid mathematical display for me of what people have been intuititively saying all along, that Druids are your best offtanks.
