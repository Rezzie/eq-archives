The trick to the war stomp is to understand it's limitations. Ironically, the "War" stomp seems best suited for running away.

When you stomp, it stuns the surrounding enemies for 2s. If you are fighting with 2h Staves though, chances are you have a 3-4s strike time. So you stomp, they are stunned, then a second after they are stunned, you finally hit them. It really does make it feel like it is useless. However, if you drop to a single hand mace, with a .5-1s strike time, then you get 2 or 3 hits in. Or cast Moonfire, or, suppose you were surprised, it can give you bonus time to get your entangle on them. But overall, it seems best for when you are surrounded by mobs and you just need the extra time to get the heck out of there.

I haven't tried the war stomp with any other class besides druid. I could see a dual-weilding warrior or rogue really being able to deal out some damage with it.
