I've been charming Worgs in LP with good success. They are not camped at all on Povar server like the gators always are. If you follow the road to HOS and kite them on the right side of the road you avoid all of the orc roamers. Not only are there a huge supply of mobs but they rarely resist anything though charm does fail fairly often.
I usually avoid leveling up until I have all of the AA's done for that level, so for instance at level 81 I will put AA's on about 90% and grind them out with LOTD each morning, by the time I ding 82 I've usually gotten all of the AA's done (120?) and need to level up to buy additional things. This expansion has a lot more new AA's so I'm doing 95% right now. As I gain levels the AA exp for each Worg is less, thats why I don't ding ding ding as fast as I can.
One other thing I've written about before is creating a "parking lot" with your almost dead mobs that are below flee health. Once they turn around to flee I let them live and just re-snare them. Then I keep pulling mobs and charm kill them down to <20% health (<10% is prefered), let it flee, and resnare. Once I get 12-15 mobs all standing around almost dead, I pop LOTD and kill them all at once, which give about 3 AA's initally and I can pretty easily get 2-3 more AA's for the duration of the LOTD. Getting 6 AA's for 45 minutes of soloing is pretty impressive IMO.

Actually I've gotten into the habit over the years to level up slowly after an expansion. I know certain raiding guilds often stress leveling up, but I can take several months to make the 5 levels.
My reasoning is that since its easier to get aa's at lower levels I spend 90% of my xp doing aa's until I have them all maxed out for that level, then I ding and start working on the next ones. Plus I make sure I have 30 aa's before I actually ding the next level.
