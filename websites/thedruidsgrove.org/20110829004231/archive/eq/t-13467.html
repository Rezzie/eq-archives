You will possiably upgrade 4 slots (Shoulders, Mask, Cloak and Neck) as well as possiable chest drops and after completing all 5, gain a spell which helps with healing by a large margain in certain cases.

After doing these I would try to go back and get the other 2 DoD Spells (with 9 possiable upgrades + random chests) - all grouped missions as well as finish out your 70 Runes (possiably soloable depending on gear, best off in group).

Reptile is not a raid-critical spell in the same way as having group steeloak or your best possible heal spell, while useful there isn't an encounter I can name that would be made significantly easier or requires the use of that spell.

If I were in your position, I would probably work on getting some groups in Riftseekers to round out whatever AAs you need (most likely all of your healing and curing ones if you're raiding and haven't gotten them or the latest versions yet) and collect your glowing runes. Then move on to the newer spells/access quests like Dreadspire, Anguish, etc.. and on the side work on other DoD missions for the gear if you have some weak slots you'd like to improve. As mentioned above it does matter where you want to jump back into the raiding scene.

Time - you're probably set to join one.
Tacvi - there's some flagging but you can most likely get piggied in or do the access quests
Anguish - Signets quest and you might be fine, if the guild looks a lot at gear, consider doing DoD missions to round out weak slots. Riftseekers is usually a good bet for glowing runes as well.
DoN - Not much actually ~needed~ but fire/cold resist are nice for dealing with dragons.
DoD - Dreadspire access quest, fill out all weak slots with DoD mission gear.
PoR - Theater of Blood access quest (which will give you your 70 aura and moonshadow in the process). ToB is kinda nasty with AEs so good gear is highly recommended.

In general, if you need gear - DoD missions, if you need spells - do appropriate missions/exp zones, and do the access quest(s) for the content you're going to be raiding with the new guild.
