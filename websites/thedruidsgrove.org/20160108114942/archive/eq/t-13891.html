I started quadding tables with just under 6k mana and each quad would take around 25-30 mins (resnare, med kite and long med before next).

The biggest difference was when I looted an Orb of Clinging Death from Time. On each circuit of tables I would use Orb on each member of the quad and cast 2 rain spells. Time for each quad was halved and it was much easier on mana as half of the damage was from the clicky dot.

Once I hit 66+ I switched to using Tempest Wind, slightly less efficient but I never saw a wave resisted.
