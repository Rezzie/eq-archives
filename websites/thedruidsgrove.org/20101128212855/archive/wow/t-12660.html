Yep. +35agi is THE enchant.

It is a bit expensive though... 8 Large Prismatic, 8 Greater Planars and 20 Arcane dust. If you are flush with mats go, otherwise you may want to save the mats for your earthwarden, or the druid S1/S2 pvp mace and put the 35agi on that.

You could also go with Savagrey +70ap, as it only costs 40 Arcane dust and 4 Large Prismatic Shards, though this is an inferior option.
