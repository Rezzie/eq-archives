Since you have already started arenas, I would strongly recommend that you go straight for 4 piece season 3.

That will account for the following slots:
Hat
Gloves
Legs
Chest

You can also get a large amount of good pvp healing gear from normal pvp. This trinket (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=28235) is particularly important to get - bind it to a key that will be easy to press quickly, to get you out of a stun or fear. This can be an absolute lifesaver, and is easily more important for pvp than any other trinket could be. The 20 resilience will help as well :>

Fill up your other slots with pvp gear from normal battlegrounds while you wait for the arena points to start flooding in. The first piece you will be able to afford is the gloves I believe.
