05-19-2006, 05:47 PM
0/30/21 is an awesome build for 20man and under content, and gets the job done for you in 40mans. Missing natural weapons and OOC is a blow to pvp, but honestly, imo, NS is one of the best talents in the game. healing yourself to full from 10HP with a lucky crit while some sucker of a hunter is drawing back for the last shot is a wonderful feeling =). It also does great things for your ability to keep 5 people up in raid content if you use it to anticipate.

Keep in mind, the first 20 points in resto isn't going to do much for your healing efficiency or staying power. At best you can forgo imp rejuv/subtlety for 10% mana savings on only HT. 0/30/21 is still a gear dependant build for healing. If you want to improve staying power as a healer without increasing gear you need to go 24/0/27 or 0/11/40.
