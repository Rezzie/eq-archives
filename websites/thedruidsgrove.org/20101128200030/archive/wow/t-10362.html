Sigh, why does a thread about druid builds turn into a WSG flag running thread?

As for your build? I suggest going mostly feral. You can respec later to resto if you choose. But for leveling purposes, feral is great. You will still be an effective healer in instances. Also, you mentioned that you were turned off by Nature's Grasp because it can only be used outdoors and therefore its lack of usefulness in dungeons. If you're getting beat on to the point where you need to even think about casting it, then your tank isn't doing a good job or you're overhealing, or just doing way too much damage. Either way, someone screwed up and Nature's Grasp most likely won't help.

In world PvP/dueling/BGs, Nature's Grasp is very useful. Especially against rogues who tend to get the jump on you. They will hit you once, and instantly get rooted (w/ all 5pts). At that point that rogue will be dead.
