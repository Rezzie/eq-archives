For the life of me, I cannot figure out how to view the AA tab on magelo profiles. So I'm not quite sure what you have / don't have atm. But here's my 2 cents.

I solo quite a bit so I invested in the DOT critical affliction / Blood Tithe line heavily. Although I do have a wide range of AA's with a total of 760 or so atm. As a grouping druid, I can see you putting this off as there are more benefits you can get from other lines first.

I saw from your magelo profile you are hovering around 370 AA points. I would suggest to continue on your path mixing up the DPS and Heal AA lines. As for the healer side, maxing out the Adept and Gift lines are vital. For an extra DPS boost - I found the Storm Strike AA to be incredibly helpful and fun! Mana free insta nukes always being a smile to my face when they crit.

If you're hurting in the mana recovery efforts, you can invest in the Mental Clarity and um.. that other one that increases mana regen cap from gear.

I agree that denfensive AA's are also important. Although I must admit I have yet to work on them much at 760 AA's. When you do decide to build this up, go for avoidance first, then dmg mitigation.

And of course - if you haven't gotten these yet:

Exodus
Shared Camoflauge

I would make them a priority!

Anyway - Saumey - I was able to find one little pixel to click on and get the AA tab to pop up. I see now my comments about exodus and shared camo were unneeded. Sorry. :banghead:

Of course there is a fine balance between Druid AA's for dmg vs. healing vs. class abilities. Below are my "enhanced" suggestions after looking at your specific AA breakdown.

Healing AA -
# 1 - Convergence of Spirits AA - provides a clicky base heal and extended HoT to a single target along with added AC / DS. While there are nine ranks to this line, I find it very useful for a quick patch heal to group member or self heal when I'm on the run (don't have to stop to cast this one).

# 2 - Fortified Survival - decreasing cast time and reuse time of this group saving heal is something I'm looking forward to working on next! I just got the spell myself (yay Warmly Fangbreaker faction).

# 3 - Max Healing Gift line.

DPS AA -
# 1 - The Storm Strike AA will definitely add to your DPS. While it's only 1 nuke that has 2 possible hits and also crits available only every 30 seconds, it is mana free clicky .5 cast time! I've gotten the 3rd rank and seen a double hit for for 1100 and one crit for 2200 or so. That was 3.3k dmg for no mana.

# 2 - I would go Fury of Magic over Destructive fury to increase chance for crit hits first.

Mnemonic Retenion - a 10th spell slot is not necessary but can be very useful!

Mana savings - Gift of Mana line
You're at GoM 1 atm which affects the lvl 65-70 spells. Gift of Radiant Mana will allow for similar procs to affect lvl 71-75 spells. Gift of Exquisite Radiant Mana will affect our 76-80 spells. These gifts of mana proc on both damage and heal spells. I personally like using the gift for that Nature's Blazing Wrath nuke / dot since it costs like 1200!

Agro management - Silent Casting has imo saved me from healing agro. When multiple mobs are pulled but not picked up by tanks, it's easy for me to get wacked by the 2nd or 3rd mob. I click the silent casting AA for added chance the mobs won't notice my heals and therefore give time for tanks to establish agro on all mobs (or get them mezed / rooted).

Well, that's all from me! It helped to look at this and review my own options for future AA purchase. :icon_lol:
