There are 2 additional questions that seem to crop up in every thread on this subject, so I am going to answer them preemptively:

1)You can turn in other classes armors too. They go to the relevant trainers for that class. All are named Level-60-Class-Title something (Heirophant Bob, Arch Mage Bob, Sorcerer Bob, Assassin Bob....)

2)Only the old Hate/Fear armors work. PoG armor does not. The newer armor from the 2nd floor of Hate does not. The Iksar specific armors do not. Cryosilk does not.
