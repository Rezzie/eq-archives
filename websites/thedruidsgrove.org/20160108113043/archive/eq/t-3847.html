I bought this tonight on a whim, and so far I like it.

As you can see on lucy (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3297&source=Live), level 1 cures 9 counters of curse/poison/diesease. Haven't had a chance to check out having one of each type on it, but I'd guess it would kill them all at once. It affects your whole group, it will also cure one no counter dot/debuff.

I did a little testing with it...

Put ONLY ensnare as a debuff on me.
It will cure it

Put ONLY winged death.
It will cure it

Put drones, winged death, epic on ( in that order )
It cures drones

ONLY epic
Cured

Ensnare, Fixation, Disjunction, Winged
NOTHING cured.

I believe in the final case, it was taking some invisible counters off them all, spreading out the "dispel", so that it was too weak to dispel any of them. I'm not quite sure =-(

Anyways, just thought i might share a little info about this ability. It costs 2 points for level 1, you can get level 2 a 63 for 4 points. So guessing 65 for level 3, and cost of 6 points.

Not sure I'll ever get level 3, but ifI do, it will likely be awhile =-)
