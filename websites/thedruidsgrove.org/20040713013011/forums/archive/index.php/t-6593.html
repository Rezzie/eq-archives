Coray has posted his last Mobhunter article (http://www.mobhunter.com), a Q&A interview with SOE where he both poses the questions and answers them! And in the same article he announced his retirement from Mobhunter.

Unlike his predecessor he isn't going to go to work for SOE, but he has decided to move on from Everquest.

Coray's articles were generally refreshingly direct in their criticism, and sometimes praise, of SOE's game decisions. Those of us who enjoyed his witty articles will miss them.
