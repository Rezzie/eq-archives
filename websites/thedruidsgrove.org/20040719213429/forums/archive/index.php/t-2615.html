DC pets are in several zones. I'd say they are more than toys or limited use.

Pre-elemental
CoD is really good xp rate and you'd never wait more than a few minutes to get a rat. Get a pet and some decent melee - xp is suprisingly good. With a fast respawn rate, multiples on pull, this can be a fun time.
I never really spent any time in PoN/PoD so can't speak for there.
HoH wrulons are fun to play with, but can have a bit of a wait to get one. My group fought to a camp after getting the DC pet so I can't speak about aggro/invising to kite spot.
PoTactics pit the DC pet is uncommon/semi-rare but fun once you get him. Quarters are a little tight, have to watch placement and pushing the mob.
PoS DC pet I've nicknamed Bigfoot - folks swear they've seen him, but ...

Elemental planes
PoEarth and PoFire both have easy to get DC pets. PoFire you cannot get DC pet past the castle, but you can farm lower named, tradeskill stuff while getting decent xp. PoEarth is good xp and farming tradeskill components as well. I haven't hunted in PoWater much for xp, few people like underwater zones so I can't really speak about that zone. As for PoAir, I've never seen a light blue con there but I haven't looked for one either.

Grouped with a slower, and anything pre-ele planes your pet can act as MT if you desire. 1 iCH per fight plus maybe a top off heal depending on how fast slow lands.

I got DC semi early on thinking that getting this would help me earn more xp faster. It helped me quite a lot in speeding up xp rate in groups, helped me xp a shaman (RL brother-in-law) from 58 to 65 and 10+ AAs in a couple of weeks - duoing in PoV.

Using a DC pet, I bring a good amount of DPS to the group even if all I do is heal or just pull with light nuking thrown in (no medding). I use DC pet as primary source of my contribution to the group damage and concentrate on main/backup healing, crowd control, pulling. Other druids in my guild have seen what it brings to a group and have moved up the priority for getting Dire Charm, as much as saying it's the next AA skill they are buying (and had still many good AA skills to choose from).

For perspective, I am in an elemental guild, I never (never ever) solo, I play healer to about 1/3 of the groups I join, and I didn't buy DC until after PoP.

In PoEarth, the Armadillos spawn three level 46 Miniature Dustrunners, also light blue that do approx 120-200+ dmg.

In PoFire, the Young Obsidian Tree Spiderling is also level 46 and does approximately the same damage as the Phoenix Scorchlets.

In PoWater, there is a small light blue sea turtle. I have not encountered this little guy yet and so cannot post any info regarding level or dmg output, but I would imagine that it also does approximately the same damage as the above listed and can be charmed.

In conclusion, each Planar zone has one animal-type mob that can be Druid charmed, with with CoK, CoT, or DC.
