Monster Mission = Group fights as a set of monsters of predetermined level classes. 1 time events.

Shroud = turns you into a certain Class/level and some abilities associated with that class. I can shroud as a level 30 rogue and join an LDON of that level and be able to exp with them like normal. As you progress with each particular shroud, better shrouds become available of that model.

Some monster missions I believe also have some form of progression but I am not sure.
