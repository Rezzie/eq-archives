I'd go for the Voodoo Shaker.

Just a little math here again ^^.

For a 6 minute duration (all of which is within the FSR):
Aran's Soothing Sapphire: 8 mp5
8 mp5 regenerate 576 mana.
No extra mana gained from Innervate.

Voodoo Shaker: 25 sprit
25 spirit (with Intensity and Living Spirit) give 5 mp5 which amounts to 360 mana.
25 spirit with Innervate give 300 mana (15 mp5 * 5 for 20 seconds)

So for a 6 minute cycle 8 mp5 give 576 mana and 25 spirit give 660 mana.
And this assumes that you are inside the FSR all the time. Spirit gets better with every second you are outside it.
