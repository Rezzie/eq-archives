Hrm, if you're doing this fight like that there are a couple of things:

2 Holy priests + 2 resto druids are not the most awesome combo for tank healing. It can work but it's tricky depending on your druid's spec.

Are you glyphed for nourish? If you spec empowered touch/nourish glyph your nourish spamming will be a lot more powerful for this fight. It works a lot better than a priest going disc (disc priest tank healing, while said to be awesome, is actually still a bit tricky).

Your tank *does* need to be hitting cooldowns but not all at once. What we generally do is have them chain cooldowns. Like if your tank is a warrior, they can pop Shield Wall when he first hits 3 inhales, then when shield wall wears off, Last Stand and Enraged Regen or something.

Have your holy priest pop Guardian Spirit on the tank when Shield Wall pops off for the increased healing.

As a druid don't worry about your cooldowns. NS+HT is good, swiftmend off CD is great but the #1 tool of tank healing as a druid is Rejuve/Regrowth hots on a tank and then spam the crap outta nourish.

Festergut does not parry-haste, even on 25 man. If he did that then I think every tank in the world would die instantly. >.<

at 3 inhales though he has a .9 second swing timer and is doing about 25-30k per swing.
