You can train enchanting at any of the major cities, just speak with the guards. They will put a note on your map, as they will for many NPC's.

As far as making money, I'm far from someone to ask about that, I never seem to have much coin at all but I suppose it would be much like any profession.. put some time and effort into it and yes you could make some coin. I suspect some of the uber enchants could net you some cash too should you find yourself in possession of one of those recipies. Take a look at some of the prices on AH for dust, essence etc. There seems to be a pretty penny selling those.

I was told when I first took Enchanting as a profession I would go broke working up the skills, but it is possible to farm for the items to DE. Also, find someone who is skilling up in Tailoring, you can pass off cloth (linen, wool, silk, etc) and a bit of spare coin for mats and have them pass you back items that will DE. I've not found a place yet where there was not a recipie using only dust that would skill me up. Granted, I'm only to 218 yet, but I'm really enjoying the profession.

Good Luck with it!
