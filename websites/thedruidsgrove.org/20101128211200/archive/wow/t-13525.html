Hey Mirko,

First please remove claw from your bar once you get mangle. I'm glad you read up and hopefully that'll help a bit but make sure you only mangle enough to keep the mangle debuff on the target.

It's all about your bleed timers and debuffs. Get some combo points and pop Savage Roar.

I'm at work so I can't really take a huge look at your talents and gear but the basic concept is stack agi until you can get a decent ArP rating, something like 500 without gems then start stacking ArP gems if you can also get some sort of ArP trinket.

You want to aim for at least over 50% crit unbuffed to smooth out your rotation.

Priority is:

SR
Mangle Debuff on Target (or an Arms Warrior)
Rake
Rip

Ferocious Bite can be used if you don't think the boss is going to live beyond 12 seconds or your Rip has 50% duration and your SR is up.

Glyphs: Savage Roar (a must), Rip, Shred

You could alternatively use beserk glyph but I prefer the other two.
