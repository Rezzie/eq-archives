Keep In mind those are off the Druid Base sets so yours may very in numbers but should come close to same % accross the board. Also you Must take into Account the Difference in Augs for example With SoF group and TS Armor you can only use type 7 augs, were as with the SoF and Solt Raid sets you can use all type 8 augs or even a mixture of both depending on how well your aug standing is. Take For instance a Breast Plate Group Gear = Hardened Coalstone of Purity +100 hp/mana Breast PLate Raid = Prism of Captured Energy +190hp +210mana. I would have to say as far as sets it is not good to just have a full set of any Armor but a mixure. Melee can get away with a full set of TS Elegant/Sublime or Elaborate/Sublime we are a caster must also take into account the focus effects needed for Raiding for better Heals/Dmg Criticals needed to preform at our level of game play or Higher. The loss of percentage with Last Blood Focus Effects drastically reduces your effectiveness in comparisent to the HP/Mana/AC gain you get.
