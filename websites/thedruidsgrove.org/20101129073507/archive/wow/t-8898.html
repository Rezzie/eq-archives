You can find the blood in the dark coffer in the vault, it's been there pretty much every time i've been through the instance and bothered to open it.

In the BRD vault, after you've killed everything there's a vault that opens with another vault inside it (the Dark Coffer vault). You need to read the text on the portrait just outside the doorway to that room and find out where the Dark Coffer key carrier is. Go hunt him down and get the key and then get your blood.
