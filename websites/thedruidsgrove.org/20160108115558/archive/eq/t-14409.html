10-02-2006, 05:26 PM
There are two ways to get to the spider area. The south canyon is easiest and has no mobs but you need lev. You can enter the south canyon at the south bears camp (by the ruins). The map shows an impass in the south canyon but that's only an issue if you don't have lev. The north canyon contains mobs, some of which see invis. You enter this canyon at the north bears camp.

If you come through the south valley you'll first encounter the goblin pit. Climb above the goblin pit and you'll see the spiders area. If you come from the north you'll see the spiders area first.
