There really aren't many quests that aren't simply grinding crap. Just thinking, if you do the wood elf illusion hatt for your cleric it means... endless boring grinding of faction. If you want to do the chardok cords and rings it's... endless boring grinding of faction followed by endless boring camping of a mob and endless boring killing of juggernaughts to get the tendon.

There is the VP and VT key "quests" although the final parts can't be duoed. Not yet anyway.

The LoY quest spells for both your cleric and druid would keep you busy killing really annoyingly tough green to low blue mobs. Charm of the Emerald Brotherhood is another one for your charm slot. The there are the various ring quests that you have to get the lugald flesh for.

The alternate entrance quests for PoP are somewhat interesting, Torment in particular.

If you have the flags for it the Binden Concerrentia is nice but better for people that can't gate/faithstone: everquest.allakhazam.com/...item=17786 (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17786)
