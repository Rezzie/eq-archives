Welcome! This is a great place to get help. Like the others have said, it would be helpful if you had given your Druids faction and type of server.

By your post mentioning Deadmines...I would make a guess that you are Alliance. Your first instance would be The Deadmines. As far as the first instance any faction can run....this is my favorite. If you are on a PvP server then it will be easiest for you to do Deadmines and Stockades. BFD isn't that hard to get to....just don't wander very far to the South of the entrance or you will find yourself running from Horde NPC's.

If you are Horde then you would do Ragefire Chasm first, then Wailing Caverns and then you can choose between C=bFD and SFK. Again, if you are on a PvP server then you might want to stay away from BFD....but the others are in friendly territory.

Now if you are on a PvE server then you can possibly get to some of the other factions instances.....but you have to be very careful. Wailing Caverns has some nice Druid gear.

Out of the lower instances...these, in my opinion, are the most enjoyable...

Deadmines
Stockades
Wailing Caverns
Shadowfang Keep

And if you are doing Skinning and LW then you can skin some of the things you kill in WC and SFK.

Hope this helps. Good luck....and don't hesitate to ask any questions about these instances....we all had to do them at some point.
