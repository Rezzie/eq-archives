The adds in WoS indeed pop outside the room. I believe that they all popped at the same time, although I may be wrong here, as I wasn't able to pay 100% attention to this part. They didn't spawn immediately though, it took some time for them to arrive.

Clearing trash beforehand in Qinimi is a good idea, otherwise you will have to deal with the dozens of grey adds during the named fight. Make sure that not only you don't zone in before the trash is dead, but also other druids who are at the same stage of the progression, because another druid - even if the druid has the shard container in bank - can spawn the mobs. Because of that, I can't really say if / when he returns to his room. But we fought him in his room and it worked okay.
