Hello Organtis.

I've been a MH Tree of Life forever. And recently respecced to Dreamstate to see what all the fuss was about.

My primary reason for doing this was because my +healing as a Tree - with decent gear - was only at +1538 unbuffed (and that drove me crazy). Immediately upon respeccing, my +healing went to +1711 unbuffed. Needless to say, I did a happy dance.

Soloing changed drastically. Before, I would stand far back and pull one at a time. With the Dreamstate build, I charged in like a maniac and pwnd. :thumbsup:

Changing my healing habits took a lot to get used to. 10+ Raids not a problem, but 5-man Heroics? Considerably harder. Not impossible, of course, but definately more stressful.

Dreamstate has it's benefits. No doubt about it. In fact, I love dreamstate. Love it.

But...(you knew that was coming, didn't you?)

I will be switching back to a Tree. I can go on forever about how I miss my Tree Form for healing. But my biggest issue is that having to cast larger heals just sucks my mana dry. Perhaps it's a gear problem on my part (I'm still using my original healing gear), perhaps I have the wrong enchants. Dunno. All the +healing in the world isn't gonna mean squat if I'm outta mana.

So, long story short....(oops! too late!).....

Dreamstate healing? Adequate. It can be done. And with the right group, it can be done well.

Tree healing? The best healing a Druid has to offer. Especially a Druid who has their sights on Main-Healing.
