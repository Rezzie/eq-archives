If you really wanted to, you could start assigning a single keystroke to two different hotkeys. This would activate two hotkeys at once with a single keystroke, letting you put a clicky item in one hotkey slot and a message in the other. It's a very common thing for monks to do, to tie their epic clicky to flying kick. Keep in mind it's a global change (applies to all characters played on that computer)...

You can get MacroQuest. Of course, if you're caught using it, SOE could technically ban you although I don't know of anyone that has been. As long as you don't set up obvious long abusive macros I doubt they'd care. Aside from letting you do simple things like what you want which the game should allow, it also lets you have exact mana amounts instead of just a %, when you /tar it will always work, not like without it where even someone standing right beside you often can't be targetted, it will show you the class/level of anon people in the same zone, it can give you the direction and distance to people and tradeskill items including groundspawn. You can also set up macros to do even the most insanely complex things like running around buying/foraging/picking up items, tradeskilling, selling etc.

Anyway, worth it just for the accurate mana numbers and proper targetting that won't give "out of range" or whatever on the tank right beside you you're trying to target for a heal. The exact direction and distance to players is pretty nice too, better than tracking and for classes without tracking it's really handy.
