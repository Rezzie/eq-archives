Okay, so most of us have done it at some time or another no matter how minutely or extravogently. Twinks. Everybody loves them, everybody hates them. They're fun! I got bored over the weekend and decided to brush off the bank and try something new. I've never played a Shadow Knight before, so I want to make a new one.

Enter Vampi, my new Inky Shadow Knight on Veeshan.

So... Here's what I did to twink her out the wazoo. I had about 200k to work with, but with some effort and farming I can raise more. If you can think of ways to improve this, cool! I'm concentrating, for the most part, on bazaar sold goods and equipment. Avoiding recommended levels, of course, to create the most impressive level one Shadow Knight Veeshan has ever seen =P Okay okay, here's what I did, please post any suggestions you might have and I hope other people find this useful in making their own twink tanks!

Vampi, Dark Elf, Shadow Knight.
Base stat adjustment: +10 STR, +10 STA.

Okay... So I load up my merchant and decide to do a little shopping. First things first, every tank needs a chestplate. It's all nice and shiney, so I look for something nice and shiney too for Vampi. I have an <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20483">Acrylic Chestplate</a> banked, but I pass on that. Lo-and-behold, a <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6754">Matchless Dragonhide Breastplate</a> is for sale! Cha-ching. There's yer shiney. Next, weapons! every tank's gotta have weapons. I have my old <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6093">The Horn of Hsagra</a> banked from my ill-fated attempt at a rogue, so that's definitely an option. However, I grab the <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=5745">Swiftblade of Zek</a> thats up because it's cheap and offers more HP (for now) to the fledgling SK. Heck, if I change my mind, the Horn's in the bank anyway.

Wrists: I grabbed the leftovers from my bard - <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=8962">Bracelet of Might</a> and <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=8960">Bracelet of Quickness</a> because I already have them lying around.

Some more shopping... <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20460">Grimling Earbone</a> is pretty nifty. <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7132">Hardened Gray Flesh Sleeves</a> too. <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7424">Clattering Bone Necklace</a> is a nice twink toy, although there's probably better if I dig. <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4243">Velium Fire Wedding Ring</a> and <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1643">Djarn's Amethyst Ring</a> Make a nice couple.

I grab <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7112">Shissar Scale Headband</a> for the headpiece... Too bad I can't afford the <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6766">White Dragon Helm</a> that's up right now. That would be great.

Footwear! <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=3977">Lodizal Shell Boots</a>. Great for the starting level because you can click it at one, in the tutorial, savign you the log buff log hastle of trying to smuggle temperance into your first few forays. <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7131">Shissar Scaled Gauntlets</a> too, because the stats and HP aren't too shabby at all.

Every twink should have a <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=18979">Cloak of Greater Pernicity</a>, I say... OR a cloak of flames / flowing water, whatever, if you don't have a haste belt.
<a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20510">Acrylia Inlaid Shield</a> makes a nice shield for the twink, I farmed it in Inner Acrylia myself. Still have another one in my trader bags.

Haste? <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=953">Runed Bolster Belt</a> of course. It's nice with a whopping 31% haste. It cost's a pretty penny, of course, but it's worth it!

Now I would have picked up a <a href="http://allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6447">Blade of Carnage</a> if I were able to afford it at the time, the damage and delay are nice enough and the stats only make it shinier. Sure, I'll dump it for something else way later on, but for the beginning it's pretty darn useful even without the proc. Just think when (if) Vampi gets Dual wield... With the Swiftblade, thats 200hp and a boatload of stats in just those two slots. If no Dual Wield, oh well, it's still very nice. BoC > Swiftblade.

I left a few equipment slots uncalled, because they are either filled with the best I could find or they're flat empty. If you have ideas for upgrades I'm all ears, Vampi's already around 850 HP unbuffed at level one. That's pretty darn impressive for a twink, I'd say, but I know it can get better. Ideas?
