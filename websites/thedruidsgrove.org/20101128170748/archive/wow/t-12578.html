IMO most heroics are pretty easy for any decently geared and coordinated group. But there sure are some hard ones:

Arcatraz - Mobs here seem to hit harder than in other heroics (or is it just my imagination?), and that first boss is a pain without shadow resistance. Luckily, he's skippable if you're just looking to finish your Trial of Tenacity without spending a full repair bill on one boss.

Mana Tombs - This instance is a PAIN towards the end without humanoid CC (think Shaffar's chamber and the stacking mage debuff) . Again, the first boss hurts without shadow resistance, but is doable without any.

Crypts - If you don't have Shackles, don't even think about it unless you've got one hell of a solid tank.

Shattered Halls - Also a PITA without humanoid CC. Not to mention that the schizophrenic second boss is just a nightmare.

Double hard hitters - The Defenders in SP and the Ravenguards on Sethekk Halls come to mind. These mobs come in pairs, can not be CCed, and hit like TRUCKS. If you don't have a hunter to dance a mob or a mage to slow kite one, your tank and healer had better to be up to the task of taking both of these at once or you are SOL.
