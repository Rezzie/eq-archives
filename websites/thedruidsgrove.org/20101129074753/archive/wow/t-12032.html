You can start the Kara key requirement prior to 70 so you're good to go.

- Go to Karazhan
- Just outside you'll find a guy with 2 quests, do them.
- That leads to a chain where you need to run Shadow Lab
- Then run Steamvaults & Arcatraz ( you don't have to run the all, just to the key container. In SV it is just before the first boss, and in Arc it is just after in the room with all the void walker guys and it can be stealthed to by a group of druids & rogues )
- Run Black Morass
- You are now keyed for Kara

As for other rep:
- Cenarion Expedition ( Exalted ) will get you access to the Earthwarden, which is the best tanking weapon unless you are in a guild running Serpentshrine Caverns.
- The Sha'tar ( Exalted ) for a really nice healing mace ( if you go resto )

You need Revered to run heroic difficulty instances ( Lower City, Cenarion Expedition, The Sha'tar, Keepers of Time, Honor Hold....I might be missing one >.< )

Cenarion Expedition has turn-ins to help with rep progress, the rest of them you have to run a specific instance over and over to hit your rep requirement: Black Morass, Steamvaults, Arcatraz, Shadow Lab, and Shattered Halls.

That's about all I can think of at the moment. Hope this gets you off on the right foot for your rep runs.

Here's also a wonderful online reputation calculator that shows you how many runs you have to do of what instance(s) to get to your next rep level: http://www.wowreputationcalculator.com/
