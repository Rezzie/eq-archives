Here is a post I put up on the SOE boards, it might be of some help. I've been doing walrus and ulthorks there since 34.

I use the F9 key while quadding Ulthorks with my 37 druid in EW. I put it to the far away view and slightly in front of me.

Basically my technique is run around ensnaring 4 hunters or warriors.
Run around in a circle grouping them up.
Run East towards coldain camp while pressing F9 and adjusting camera.
Once the camera is set. Stop and cast lightning strike.
Run till lightning strike becomes avail again, sit, count to four, stand and cast lightning strike again.
Repeat until all dead or 1 straggler left.
If there is a straggler cast stinging swarm on it and med till it dies.
Put camera back to first person and loot bodies.
Mem till full mana and repeat.

I've never been killed doing this and have also managed to actually kill 5 ulthorks a few times when I have picked one up by mistake. It gets untidy that way though. Also you make pretty descent plat as well and over 1 blue bub per kite. Oh one other thing, I'm not super twinked here and can still manage this with mana to spare
