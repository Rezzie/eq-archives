I think this has to be one of greatest abilities so far since Innervate. Frenzied regen saves a smart druid the mana required to heal, and shift back into Bear to attack. I don't know if it can be dispelled but when I fought a Shaman, surely enough, Regrowth, Rejuv, MotW was dispelled, but when I used Frenzied Regen either he didn't try to, or just couldn't.

It's not as effective as Regrowth + Rejuv, but like mentioned before it definately gives that buffer of health to mitigate damage being dealth and give us more time to regen the mana lost casting initially and shifting. Without this skill I probably couldn't handle some situations the way I can now. I was able to beat a 60 Warrior at level 54 without rooting + moonfire spam by literally out tanking him.
