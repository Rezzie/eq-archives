I have been trying to find fun doable quests since my return but I am currently looking for more. Anyone that has some suggestions please pass them on.

Main prereq is that they be soloable at 70ish (for the newly returned, I started most of this stuff at 65 when I came back at the beginning of April) or a 3 man group doable scenario, preferably solo, but in some cases you may only need a group for one or two encounters. I am normally alone or duo with another druid my level when I am questing.

Here are what I have done so far, or have in progress:

Epic 1.5 (need some help in a few spots but a lot of this is very soloable, information is all over this board so not going to repeat whats done)

Many of the quests below overlap with what and where you are hunting so you might as well get the benefit from more than one at a time if you can


Icefall/Valdeholm/Steppes
Ancient Tomes (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3715)
Nice Mana Pres VI aug from Valdeholm
Gnolls in NE corner by the Giant wall were the highest success rate

Gorbak's Bag (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3725)
Camo to quest giver, have /say ready, get quest and evac
Follow same procedure on turn in
** see Gorbaks other quests since you will finish them by default anyway

Kregnad's Knapsack (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52765)
Same procedure as Gorbak but wait until Kregnad paths to the highest point above or lowest point below dogs for best success rate

Cull the Gnolls (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3879)
Kind of worthless except you will auto finish it so you might as well, quest comes from Crescent

Toegnasher's Token (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52149)
Not superb augment but easy to get overall

Runic Ivory (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3891)
Faction only, but again, you will finish by default of the other others

Armor Supplies (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3665)
Nice bracer prescratched etc

Shattered Gift (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3989) this must start with My Pretty (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3609) in Steppes
I am halfway through Shattered Gift drops, but faction isnt what is needed yet in Valdeholm. The sword drop in My Pretty is a bit of a bear to get but I have seen them rot when other groups are there killing for another reason and have asked nicely and been allowed to loot the one I needed.

I am sure I am forgetting a few but if you kill mammoths, bears, gnolls, kobolds and have all of these quests + the 2 extra from Gorbak and Kregnad you will complete all of them together. Some will require faction, but the faction you need can be also received (mainly Valdeholm) by repeating the Runic Ivory and Armor Supply quests.

Big bummer... if you are trying to get Orc Faction the Orc quests do NOT give faction. Sucks, but thats the way it is, but you still get the bags out of it if you want.

Haesgrin #1 Incursion (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3939)
nice shield but a little tricky to do

Orb of Duskmold (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3670)
this is a heal/buff clicky but you need access to the back of the mines through this quest Access to Ancient Ruins (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3707)

Thinning the Pack (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3610)
Kobold quest, shaman type pet with mana regen buff, can be cast while you have a pet and you get buff but no pet
-- I cant get this quest for some reason but still trying, it didnt work for me the way the orcs did at KOS state, he ignored me and everyone else charged me :)

Stonefed Chip (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=64379)
Orc faction again, must be quick or get faction, nice aug

Orb of Mystic Fury (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=53286)
easy quest, starts in Steppes, nice aug

Others
Charm of Lore (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52354) that plus this at the same time Wanderlust series (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3680)
Makes a very nice charm and augment combo that is relatively easy to get to high stats alone

Jonas Dagmire's Skeletal Hand (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=67072)
Long TBS quest and there are a ton of mini quests but its an excellent hand augment and druid heaven to hunt in.

Crimson Cloak of Moonwaters (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3527)
does involve PoG so faction may be an issue for those that helped on guild raids in the old days

Tinmyn's Oil Can (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=69861)
nice level 80 charm, but kind of a pain imo based on how stats are calculated on coin you carry, nice at max stats though

Shop Keeper's Delight (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3396)
Decent ring for casters, earring for melee - looks fun, havent tried it but its likely not an upgrade for most people doing these things ie. Dweriun Trio is better

Crossfire (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3394)
Looks soloable for at least half of it, but a group would knock this out in no time for a nice augment

"Duskmold" is a quest that I would highly recommend. I _think_ it can be soloed; you'll need access to inner Vergalid first, which is a separate mini-quest that can also be soloed, though it's much quicker with help from a group.

I also recommend "Thinning the Pack" if you do not yet have a mana regen clicky.
