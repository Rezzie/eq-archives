It's still not configurable under options but it still works fine.

What it is: Using it on a hotkey lets you target the last people to send you tells cycling the names with TAB / Shift-TAB just like the hotkey for reply. They still have to in range but for buff requests in tells it makes it sooo easy. Can also type /rtarget to target last person to send you a tell.

How to configure: in eqclient.ini under the heading [KeyMaps] add the following:
KEYMAPPING_RTARGET_1=21
(this is for using the Y key, can replace with a key value for whichever key you wish to use).

How to use: There is a small bug in it still. It places a space after the name which causes the targeting to fail. So you hit [Y], press [TAB] and [SHift]+[Tab] to cycle thru names of people who sent you tells. Then press BACKSPACE and hit ENTER and if the person is in range they are targeted.

As an Enchanter I find this makes buffing soo much easier outside of using MGB on a raid. Usually it's Y, backspace, enter, ALT-X to cast the buff after somebody sends a request. Nice faste and few keys. One day they might even fix the space problem and allow it to be configured under options.
