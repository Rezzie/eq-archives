An excellent source for information on this quest maybe be on Allakhazam's Magical Realm at <everquest.allakhazam.com/...quest=971> (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=971>)

The locations for the giants are:
Priest Grenk - roams near Kael to the northwest
Priest Bjek - roams near the PoG tower to the south
Priest Delar - roams near the Dragon Circle supposedly
Derakor the Vindicator - Deep in Kael

There aren't any placeholders, and most the time they're stomping about and not too terribly difficult to find. I'm not sure what the exact respawn time is once they're killed, but I'm pretty certain it's 24 hours or less.

The priests can be killed with one group heavy on melee and healing as they're resistant to spells. It takes a very strong raiding party to kill Derakor.
