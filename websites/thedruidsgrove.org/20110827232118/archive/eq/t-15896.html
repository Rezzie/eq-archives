I'm pretty sure the answer is "no" in Ashengate. Sneaking while shrouded to a rogue effectively gives you regular invis and invis to undead at the same time. You'll be able to move around in areas that have mixed live and undead mobs, but you can't in places where mobs actually see invis. Shroud of Stealth is required to not be seen by those mobs, and the rogue shroud does NOT give you Shroud of Stealth.

Ashengate mobs see invis.

Most of Frostcrypt is merely undead and will not see through IvU. As a result, they will not see a shrouded rogue sneaking. The maze area in the north part of the zone is a different story, though.
