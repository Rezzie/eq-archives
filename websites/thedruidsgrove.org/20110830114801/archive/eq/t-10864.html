I never saw leather ornate drop in tactics in many many hours of xp there. Tactics for soloing is to charm a diaku or decorous boar from inside the downstairs tunnel, haste it etc and then send it in. The murks in NC also drop 4 pieces for a quest cloak that would be a huge upgrade for you. Its several pieces of a broken sword....you will have to get good facton with the dragorn loyalists but you need that for tier one armor as well.

There is a rare zone wide belt that can drop from any mob in NC thats well worthwhile, and also a zone wide secondary item. Both are great druid items. There is a third zone wide item, a melee cloak. But two out of three isnt bad ; ).


The named in NC often drop runes for 66-68 spells as well, they all summon but some arent too hard even so. The easiest of the lot is the ukun packleader who spawns at the xroads camp near the center of the zone. Running straight from DS to WOS you will pass a large gorge leading to your right, there is one roamer who spawns and paths through this girge...an ukun. He will either be the name or a pher. The name is the only thing here that will see invis. If you can solo him he will drop all your 66-68 runes, a soso mask and an ok bracer. He only melees and has no procs.
