Focus effects on items were never meant to stack with each other if they're the same type of effect. The "higher" one will always take effect.

So if I have 2 items with mana preservation, one with 10% and one with 20%, the 20% one will ALWAYS take precedence. The 10% one will never be used (unless I take the 20% one off of course).
