+6 stats scales with buffs like kings, I prefer the +6 than 150hp.

I also generally prefer +stam over agility. With Nax and beyond, what kills tanks is not sustained damage over time, but burst damage, and the more stam you have to buffer that burst and your 0 hp mark, the more time your healers have to react and get you back up to or near full hp.
