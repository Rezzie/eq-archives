I'm trying to recall what I did prior to getting Undead Slayers. Obviously I did How the Mighty Have Fallen and I think I did Recycled Arms (the one where you open the barrels in the undead elf camp). BTW, I think Grom has 5 of the 9 tasks you need to get the aug.

I've done the aug and I believe these are the 9 tasks you need (info from memory):

Daria:
How the Mighty Have Fallen
Where Has Sister Donna Gone

Brother Veras:
Recycled Arms

Grom:
Undead Slayers (kill a ton of undead)
A Mysterious Bond (kill a bunch of mushrooms, loot 8 bones)
Extract Science (loot 4 glowing ichor from boneguards, loot 4 cursed bark from treants)
Spark of Life (loot 5 spark of life from viridian ghosts)
Carrion my Wayard Something (collect 10 undead essence; click Undead Essence Extractor when undead mobs are at 5% or less)

Shearon:
Recoup from the Coop

All of these are single tasks. Spark of Life is fun since the viridian ghosts AE mez with mem blur.
