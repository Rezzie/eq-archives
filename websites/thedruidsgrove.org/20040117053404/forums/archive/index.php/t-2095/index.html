Right past the CoH spot in Plane of Fire where the two Birds spawn is a great xp spot as well. It's a SOLO Spot, not a bring a couple bots because there are Enchanter mobs there that will cast Dicate on you and charm you. If you're by yourself it's no biggie, just wait til charm is off then resume killing. If others are there you get sent on them. You can root/rot or if Birds are not up you can just kite and nuke.

Also, if birds are not up and you're up for a good challenge you can solo the Magma Guardians (Big Lava guys). They dont' summon but take awhile to kill. I was getting 22-24% per kill for them. No cool drops off them that I've seen but great xp.
