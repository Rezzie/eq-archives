This is a fairly simple quest out of relic in the Marketplace.

Kill 15 fallen spirits
Loot 4 Arcstone Spirit Dusts
Kill 15 Spirit Stags
Loot 4 spirit staf hoofs
Kill 1 master of the herd
deliver hoofs
deliver dusts


All these mobs are in arcstone, we did it with 3 warriors, a dru, cleric and mage. All random people, some of which were under lvl 70. So the mobs definatly wern't hard, probably even soloable, if you can handle summoning mobs :sumo:

Decent fairly easy quest, for anyone whos lacking in the aug department.

The trash mobs are definitely soloable. The spirit stags hit 300s max and are charmable with Nature's Beckon (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5359&source=Test) (possibly with lower level charms as well, but I haven't tested.) I have been having fun soloing this using one as a pet since charming is something I rarely do these days. This might not be the best idea if you are lacking in the hp department though, because when charm breaks mid-fight you are going to have two summoning mobs that are very unhappy with you.

Has anyone tried soloing MotH? Looks like he could be rough with the built-in adds following him around.

A couple other notes on the spirit stag pets: most seem to have the ability to backstab and will heal themselves with Kirin Touch (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=6635&source=Test) when low on hp.
