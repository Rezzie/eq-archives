I figured I would pass this on in case others did not know about this nice *cough* feature related to the death hovering.

So I was raiding last night with the guild I am apped to. And we were backflagging for Demiplane. Starting with the Bloodeye event.

So we clear the zone as normal and engaged Bloodeye. Due to a limited amount of curer's in the raid we had some deaths before Bloodeye himself was killed. So those of us that were dead and hovering at the time Bloodeye died and the task updated did not get the "Task completed" green text and we did not get the Blood Curse AA update. Petitions and bug reports were submitted and as far as I know only one was answered at the time of me logging out last night.

The answer was - if you are "hovering" in a mission or task at the completion, you will not get credit for the task completion.

This is a feature I, and others I am sure, would have liked to have known upon the implementation of this new "feature".

Good luck all.
