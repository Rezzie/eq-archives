Depending on your play style, dire charm can be great!!!

I am one of those that is doing aa's before leveling because if gives me more places to hunt and i enjoy the older zones -- level 59 druid with almost 60 aas.

I mistakenly took the advice of what seemed the consensus of threads I read that said dire charm was a 'parlor trick' and only marginally useful -- I guess in the higher level zones thats probably true. However, I just bought dire charm and regret not getting it much earlier. For example, in the grey which is currently a hot zone, I am getting 4 percent aaxp a kill at the sun revenants. Prior to getting dire charm, I could only root/rot 4 of the six before the first respawned. But with one of the worms charmed and tanking, I can do all six -- that's 24 percent aaxp in 17 minutes in a very safe low risk camp (albeit a bit boring after a while).

Conclusion? Think out of the box when it comes to dire charm and you may find it is more useful than you think.
