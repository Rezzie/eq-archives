Year 1
I make 10.
You make 20.
The gap is 10.

Year 2
I make 11.
You make 22.
The gap is 11.

Year 3
I make 12.1
You make 24.2
The gap is 12.1

Year 4
I make 13.31
You make 26.62
The gap is 13.31
