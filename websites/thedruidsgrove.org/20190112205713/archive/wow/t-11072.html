The last time I checked, about 3 months ago, health regen from items or buffs (i.e. troll blood potions) worked in combat. You can turn on periodic effects in your combat window to check this. However, the amount of health regen you can stack this way is still small compared to the DPS of any mob you would be fighting. As a class that can heal themselves, you usually will gain more from an item that increases your health (directly or via stamina) than from an item with health regen. A +7 health regen item will heal you for a maximum of 84 points in a 1 minute combat. How many fights last 1 min?

For a druid +Def reduces the number of critical hits. It also possibly decreases crushing blows and increases your dodge rate if your defense skill is not maxed (level X 5). You need +25 defense to reduce the chance of a critical hit by 1 percent. A mob your level has a base 5 percent chance of critically hitting you. While +Def is not a useless stat, as a feral druid that usually fights in cat form, I would trade +8 Defense for +8 any other stat that is useful to a druid. As a note, if you plan on tanking in the Molten Core (level 63 mobs) you will need a minimum of +300 defense to prevent critical hits. See the Warrior forums or WoWWiki for the math.
