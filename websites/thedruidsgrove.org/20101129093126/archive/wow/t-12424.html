The most valuable of those transmutes to have is probably Earth to Life. Primal Lifes sell for a lot of money as they are used in a lot of healing enchants, spell threads, etc. Primal Earth spawns abundantly from ore nodes, so miners tend to have loads of them. Consequently, the price of Primal Earth tends to be quite low.

On balance, though, if you are transmute master the best possible thing to transmute is Primal Might. These are worth loads of money and if you get a double, you've earned yourself a heap of money for nothing. :>
