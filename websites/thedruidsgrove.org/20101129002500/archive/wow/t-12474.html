Taken from the Balance One-Stop Shop (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2215646573&sid=1) Thread:

The most common casting rotation is this: IS/MF/SFx3
The highest DPS casting rotation is this: IS/MF/Wx6
The highest DPM casting rotation is this: IS/SFx4

I rotate between all 3 depending on the fight and how close the boss/add is to dying (ie al'ar I wrath spam embers).
