This is what I have come up with so far. I am NE, and not really familiar with horde starting areas, but I assume that they are filled with low lvl herbs just like Darnassus. Off the top of my head, this is what I remember:

Darnassus / Goldshire / Sentinel Hill area are great for Peacebloom, Earthroot and Silverleaf.

Ashenvale is great for Briarthorn and Mageroyal.

Wetlands and SS have quite a few herbs, but is excelent for Briarthorn (and swifthistle), Mageroyal and Liferoot

Arathi has Kingsblood and Goldthorn,

STV Goldthorn is everywhere

The area around ST (Marshlands?) is a gold mine for blindweed, liferoot and a bunch of other really useful herbs which I cant remember right now

Feralas is packed with sungrass

Tanaris has firebloom, that’s about it.

Blasted Lands has lots of Gromsblood.

Mara is the only place I’ve found for ghost mushrooms

Un’Goro is great for Mountain Silversage and Golden Sansam
