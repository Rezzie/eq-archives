Depends on the class. Enchanters already get them, and maybe clerics may get something specific to undead. However, for most other classes, in regards to DOTs or DDs, won't be getting many if any for that matter, chromatic based spells. Chromatic spells for the most part are given to classes like enchanters or clerics, where if an NPC is magic resistant, they are basically redered useless for the mass majority of their spell lines. However, with a druid, wizard, magician, or necro, they can still function if one specific resist type is extremely high.
