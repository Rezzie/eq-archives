WoWInsider (http://www.wowinsider.com/2007/10/27/rawr-a-gear-application-for-tanking-druids/), by the way of Adventures in Azeroth (http://nedruid.wordpress.com/2007/10/26/new-equipment-comparisonevalution-tool/), points out a sweet little application for bears called Rawr (http://druid.wikispaces.com/Rawr). Rawr is a armor analyzing tool. Not only will it analyze your current status, but you can see how things would appear with select buffs and debuffs. It'll also display upgrades, both in gear and gems.

It's a pretty sweet application. For anybody that's all into numbers, this is nothing but numbers. Unfortunately, it is Windows only as it requires the .NET 2.0 framework. Sorry Mac druids.
