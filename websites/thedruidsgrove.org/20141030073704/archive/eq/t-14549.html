The short answer is "no" but thats kinda deceptive as there are "some" advantages to Wood Elves and Halflings that I believe Human and Half Elf don't get. (And if I'm wrong please correct me with out flaming me so others will have the correct information)

Woof Elf
-Innate Hide (Useful at higher levels to break charm freeing a spell gem or finding a clicky)
-Fletching/Tailoring (Although Fletching isn't useful to us as a class if your an aspiring artisan it makes life alot easier. Aslo we have one of the better DoN Tailoring skill up routes)

Halfling(Karana Deity)
-Inate Hide(read above)
-Harvest Sacks (100% W/R bags that Halflings can only make!!! Good source of money when your skill is sufficient.)
-Fletching(Read above)
-10% xp bonus(Not sure if this was removed with the TSS expansion but if not another reason to be a "furry feet")

Drakkin
*** Don't own expansion so not sure of all there innate skills ***
-Breath Weapon(For those druids who want a free DPS in groups, but not even sure what kinda damage it does LOL.)

Drakkin attacks are based on color Green is Poison, Blue is Magic. The debuff is associated with the attack, thus green lowers poison resist and blue lowers magic. I have a Drakkin Rogue that is from the Black Dragon. It's attack is disease as is the associated debuff. I do find them fun to play.

A word of caution on the breath attack. It is an area attack. So if you only want it to affect a single critter, make sure there are no others around (I've called unwanted attention to myself on several occasions). It's useful though when you're being mobbed by a small group of critters close to your level. As you pick them off one by one they all have some amount of damage already.
