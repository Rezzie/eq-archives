Tier 1 Armor

Quest NPC: Lady Usher, -200, 1700 in Arcstone (she is just West of the port out platform)

She needs a mold and a Spirit Mark to make the armor. The molds can be bought from her (2100p for chest & leg mold, 1050p for all others.)

The Spirit Marks drop uncommonly off named mobs in Arcstone and Plane of Rage. Lady Usher gives out a task to retrive them.

HELM: Spirit Mark of Vision
Drops from: Thorn, named tangleroot in tangleroot section of Arcstone

ARMS: Spirit Mark of Air
Drops from: Tempest, named mob in Arcstone
