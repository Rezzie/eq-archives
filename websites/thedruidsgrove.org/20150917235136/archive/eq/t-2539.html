There is only 1 baracuda that can pop the right tainted one.
Killing other baracudas than the one at the loc u mentioned, can pop a tainted, but those tainted will not spawn the corupted.

The one u need to kill is as u already mentioned at loc N 900 N 900. Its on a 20 minute spawn as far as i remember so be prepared to some waiting for respawn. If u dont see a baracuda at this loc, it has already been killed, or maybe u are sitting at its spawn place, this might prevent it from spawning. So try move a bit.

The tainted, has a larger agro range that the normal baracudas, so just be ready if u sit to close. Anyway he is a fairly easy solo kill, snare him and get him up on land. He is a fast swimmer, so get him up where u have the advantage hehe :)

Placeholder is sort of near neg 900, neg 900, but not exactly. There's a single underwater pillar in that area, and the spawn is southeast of that, just out of view (or possibly south, and in view? I've seen it both ways, I think). Spawn time seems to be 6 minutes.

If, for some reason, you're one of those people who put off the epic for a long time and happened to get dire charm first (like me)...you can dire charm a bloodgill barracuda (higher level than the deepwater barracudas), give it a pair of one-handed weapons and plant it at the spawn point. It will happily munch through placeholders unassisted, and with savage spirit and maybe a few heals it has no trouble killing the tainted/corrupted.

Dire charm also makes the corrupted seahorse in kedge soloable (along with every other mob in the zone besides Phinigel), by the way. :)
