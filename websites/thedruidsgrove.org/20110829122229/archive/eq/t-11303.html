Looking at your gear, the thing that screams out to me is that you do not have augs. When I look at someone who apps to my guild, I don't care what gear they have, but what aug set they have. That shows me how much they care about their character.

Augs is the gear that you do for yourself for the most part. Guilds will give you gear, but augs are pretty much up to you until you get in a big guild. If I were you I would concentrate on getting some augs. Then as your gear improves, you can move the aug with you.

Since you have no augs, there is a few that you can get pretty easy. I would recommend doing DoNs and saving up for the 50 hp or mana augs, whichever you prefer. I believe in mana over hps after you hit 7k buffed, hps until you hit that personally. There is 10+ augs you can purchase from DoN.

I would also get a few people and do some Tipt aug runs. There is a 3 augs that drop there that would help you a lot. The first one is a 30hp 30mana aug called a Gem of Bolstered Power, the next is a 30hp 30 endurance aug and the third is a +2mana regen/regen aug. What you do is get an expedition, clear/invis up to the first boss, kill it, then drop expedition and rinse and repeat and you can get the augs pretty quick.

I would also try to get a group to do the Corruption trial in MPG. This is by far the easiest of the trials and one of its rewards is a 60hp 60mana aug. There is also several MPG trial loots that would be beneficial to you, but they may be more difficult unless you tag along with some well-geared friends. The good side to that is druids are very versatile in the trials.

LDON normals at your level are cake if you bring along some lower level 60s to bring the mobs down if you want to grab the 5 40hp/40 mana or 20hp/20 mana augs from there.

Kodtaz also has some 35hp and 35 mana rings along with a 50hp 50 mana aug you can camp.

DON augs would be the better way to go, but if you find Ldon is still actively being run on your server those arent too bad either.
