To get faction, either get in a group that is doing missions (you might not be able to ask for one yet, but you can certainly join a group with someone who can), or you can do the solo missions from the camp outside. Just make sure you speak to the right NPCs for the faction that you are interested.

And at the end of a group mission, make sure you hand in the faction gem you get to help boost your faction more.
