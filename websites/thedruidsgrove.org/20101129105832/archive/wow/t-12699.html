07-05-2008, 07:58 PM
I don't like clique either, it gave me tendonitis in my mouse arm.

What I do instead is a mouseover macro like the previous poster. Basically I keep my right hand on the mouse, left hand on the keyboard, move my mouse over the person to heal (I don't need to click at all) and then press the heal key with my left hand.

Here's a sample macro that I use

#show Lifebloom
/script UIErrorsFrame:Hide()
/use Ribbon of Sacrifice
/use lower city prayerbook
/use Pendant of the Violet Eye
/script UIErrorsFrame:Clear(); UIErrorsFrame:Show()
/cast [target=mouseover,help] [] lifebloom

How this works is if there is a valid mouseover target it'll hit that target, otherwise the [] says to use the default rules for targetting (current target, or yourself).
