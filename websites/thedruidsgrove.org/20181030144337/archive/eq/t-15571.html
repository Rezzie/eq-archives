Braaiins is a short timed event really...you have one hour (iirc) to get the drops that you need, then the town i raised against you. And once the town is raised, you die....if you even manage to kill one more mob after that point you are doing amazingly well.

You need to have 5-6 people to do it smoothly and you need to be almost constantly killing. Have a puller, have 2 healers (right click the rooted brains to heal target), and try to kill the healers first.

You will have to wander over most of the town to get enough kills, so for those not familiar with halflingvile its worth taking a character for a walkthrough before the mission. Also, VERY important, do not try and pull from the fields area. The pathing is very bad there and you will tend to get a huge overpull/ wipe.
