Open up your inventory and put your crucible in an open inventory slot (not inside a bag). Left-click and hold your left mouse key down until the invientory slot jumps onto the cursor. You'll know what I mean when you do this.

Now move your cursor (with inventory slot attached) over to one of the 10 positions in your hotkey bank and left click. The inventory slot should drop into that place.

To use the cruible, either left click on it or press the key on your keyboard assigned to that hotkey position.

Hope you can understand my instructions.

If you want my advice.....Winged Death is a must-mem, it contributes a lot to the dps of your group. I suggest de-mem all your other offensive nukes and dots and mem winged death instead. The rest can be buffs, heals, utility spells.

Like:
Sup heal
Chloroblast
Winged Death
Ensnare
Root
Thorns/Strength buff swapping. The strength buff stacks with shaman focus series.
Fury of Air, to stun gaters, this is an important spell for druids in dungeons. While enc's and clerics often do the stunning, sometimes they are busy or late.
Group chloro/regrowth

Use your highest level root for root parking, they last longer as they get higher.

One last tip: when you see the enchanter get hit, start healing right away, before their health bar drops, there's usually a few seconds as the mob is cutting through the enchanter's rune and by the time your heal hits they will need it. Sometimes it's a waste but often it's a life/group saver.

Go get yourself an Elder Spiritist's Gaunts for a few plat in the Bazaar and un-mem ensnare in these group situations. You can snare with the gaunts in a group situation after you have your first wave of dots landed...
