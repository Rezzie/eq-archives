As for servers, I reccomend just finding a guild that seems like a good feel for you and just going with them on their server.

I have tried several different leveling guides, and found that nothing seems quite as good as simply downloading "QuestHelper" & "TomTom" mods. It kinda sucks the RPG portion of WoW out, but its definatley the most efficient way to get to 80. You'll find your self just stock piling quests and then making a sweep through each zone. ( I was turning in between 5-10 quests at a time.)

The big hang up for me with the guides is you have to follow them without faulter or you end up waisting a lot of time. I found my self spending more time scowering over the guide than playing. So I prefer the mods. They tell you where to go, and easily dot up your mini map so you can plan your own travel plans (if you don't like the route TomTom picks for you).

I strongly reccomend you spend / farm some badges from your hunter and purrchase some heirlooms (Shoulders and Chest first). If you can, grab the weapon too.
