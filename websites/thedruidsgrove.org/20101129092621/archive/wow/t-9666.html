I'm not sure of all the potions that are used in recipes I don't get asked for very many of them since most of the ones needed are fairly low level both in the potions and the items made.
Thott Alchemy List (http://www.thottbot.com/?t=Alchemy)
If you follow the links on Thott for each potion it will tell you what quests and items need them. The only herb turn in that I know currently is for Thorium Brotherhood and it needs kingsblood.

There's several reasons why herbs sell better than potions; there are some alchemists that aren't herbalists, herbing is the time consuming part of alchemy, bank and bag space, and that for flasks require very large amounts of herbs, flasks are rarely sold but are the potions most desired for raiding. The primary one is the time consuming part our guild has about 10-15 fairly active herby/alchemists that spend a large amount of free time gathering just for raids but our banks will still buy stacks of herbs whenever they can from the AH to suppliment those we collect.

Dreamfoil, icecap, gromsblood, Plaguebloom, Mountain Silversage, Golden Sansam and Black Lotus are the big ones but we do use Wild Steelbloom, Goldthorn, Khadgar's Whisker and Purple Lotus's as well.

Black Lotus is used in every flask but you only need one per flask so don't try to gather stacks to sell just sell as you can in singles or small stacks (5ish).

Not sure which mana potion you mean:

Greater Mana Potion is 700-900 and is trainer purchased.

Superior Mana Potion is 900-1500 is a limited supply recipe from alchemist supply in Darnassas or Undercity.

Major Mana Potion is 1300-2250 and is purchsed from the ghost vendor outside of Scholomance. You can only see the vendor if you have the trinket from the quests. Walkthrough for quest series is here (http://wow.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=8695)

Superior is probably the one you mean you'll probably have to check the vendor quite often before you find it. I think that I ended up sticking an alt in front of the vendor so I wouldn't have to keep hiking to UC ever few hours.

Major Mana Potion is 1300-2250 and is purchsed from the ghost vendor outside of Scholomance. You can only see the vendor if you have the trinket from the quests.

A method has been discovered to circumvent this requirement for Horde - it's posted at thottbot under the recipe.

However, if you are level 60, the hardest thing about doing the quests are getting a group to 5-man Schololol. While the zone is a lot easier (by leaps and bounds) that when the game was new, it's still the most challenging 5-man content in the game. Perhaps for that reason, getting a (good) group to do it on an older server can be a challenge.

While it's possible to complete the entire quest line in 1 run, expect it to take 2-3.
