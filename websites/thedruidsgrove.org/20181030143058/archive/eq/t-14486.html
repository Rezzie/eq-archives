Cibby showed me a couple of tricks last night when I ran into her hunting bears on my server (go Nameless!)

1. Bears are very hard to snare if you don't have your L69 DoN chromatic snare, Serpent Vines. If you don't have it, you need to use your animal MR debuff or have a player in your group MR debuff the bear first.

2. Pull the bears from the lake edge up to the plateau where there is plenty of room to kite. Think drakes from Cobalt Scar.

3. Your cold rain spells will, of course, be ineffectual. Load up your spell bar with dots and Solstice Strike.
