Most of the PoP zones I've been in so far -- Nightmare, Valor, Storms, HoH, Torment and Tactics -- have DC-able mobs (the DC-ables seem to share a spawn table, so if an animal isn't up, kill till you get one).

That being said, I would advise against getting DC as your FIRST Class ability. Depending on the way you play, others are more useful -- MGB if you're a raiding druid, Exodus and/or Innate Camo for general utility. PoP abilities such as Spirit of the Wood and Radiant Cure also come in very handy for soloing, grouping AND raiding.

DC is great, but like someone said earlier, I'd advice against it until after you have a few necessities like Exodus and Innate Camo.

The dc animals in pop are very nice additions to groups. However, they are limited per zone. In PoV for instance there are 3 spawns - 1 for a chanter, 1 for a druid, 1 for a necro. Once those 3 are taken you are sol until that dc person leaves or releases their pet. So, there can only be 3 dc pets in the entire zone and considering all the enchanters with DC you're lucky if you can get one for yourself.

The other problem with DC pets in PoP is that they're basically located near the zone ins. DC pathing is notoriously buggy, making it almost impossible to take a pet to any real good camp in a zone. You're stuck with killing lower lvl mobs near the zone in unless you want to attempt to walk your pet step by step with risk of trains or having him run back to his spawn point.
