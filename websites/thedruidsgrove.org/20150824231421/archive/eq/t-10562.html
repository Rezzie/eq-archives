Your doability number corresponds to which position your forage (or fishing) button is on your Actions button pad. Mine is bottom right, so it's doability #6 (top left being #1).

My forage is #6 on the button pad (random choice). My macro looks like this:
/doability 6
/pause 5
/autoinventory
/pause 5
/autoinventory

-I have autoinv twice because of forage aa, often get 2 thingies.
-I make this a hotkey and then link it to a movement key, like the left arrow. Any time forage is up when I turn left, it forages and sticks it in my inventory.
-Make sure you have inventory room or you drop stuff that's droppable.

Hope this answers your question, at least in part.
