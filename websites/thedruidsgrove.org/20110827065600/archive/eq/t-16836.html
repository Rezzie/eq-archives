You probably want to get some AAs first. They changed things so that the less you have of them the easier it is to get them, and having the right AAs helps a lot with however you prefer to play EQ.

At level 55 you can still root/rot and kite, but soon that will become increasingly difficult and by the time you reach level 70 (inverse) charming is the only viable way to solo (i.e. you charm an animal, send it against another animal. Due to the changes to charm the uncharmed animal is vastly more powerful and will tear your charmed one apart. Just before it is killed you break charm and root it. You then charm the other animal and kill the previous charmed animal yourself. It is an evil way to play for a protector of nature but we have been so reduced in solo ability that it is about the only way left to us).

As for the location, you probably want to look at the appropriate zone in the Serpent's Spire exapansion.
