Druids had no equivlant armor to Crafted, Darkforge, Etc.

We gained class Armor in Fear (Verm), and Hate (Harmony) as well as Velious.

Lower Armor was then added in Quests from the Twilight Sea.

Droppable sets appeared from Elysians in Umbral, for at the time, higher end druids who could group/solo them.

The Armor of the Pine, honestly, sucks for the level range which was designed to complete the quests, namly because the armor is much harder to aquire then Crafted, Darkforge, or Ivy armor.

If your looking for a lower end Armor Set, the Twilight Sea is probly your best shot.
