AAs will continue to get faster at higher levels, so don't worry too much about stopping for AAs at a certain point. For example, when I'm soloing in PoStorms, I get between 10-12% AA per kill. PoN is actually still good for XP up through level 62. I found that the lower XP per kill was more than made up for by faster kills. At 63, a lot of PoN becomes light blue, and you get a new charm spell that works in PoStorms.

For which AAs to take, I agree with Tat. After getting those, you'll probably want to level up to 59 to be eligible for the class AAs. If you enjoy charming, Innate Camo is awesome. And exodus has saved my life countless times when I try something "daring."

It depends on what you plan on doing, solo'n or grouping. Raiding or exp'n more.

If your quad kiting work on your nuke crit aa's, if your more charm kiting (which is GREAT exp in PoStorms at 65) then work on your defensive aa's, if your grouping you may be more heal oriented, then work on those aa's.
