At 58, I highly recommend the VRs in TD if you can handle them. Quadkite the two VRs with two raptors.

If you are still looking for mid-range spells (up to 58), then I strongly suggest the named cycle in Skyfire. Three weeks of soloing there (on and off), and I filled in 7 large holes in my spell book. It is even MORE convenient now since we can pop up to the prime soloing area via winds of the north.

At the risk of crowding up a zone that I love so well I am gonna post on what I think is currently the best indoor zone for druid soloing, and that is chardok. Since the changes to chardok the bulk of the spawns are now static and most of the summoning mobs have been moved quite abit inside the dungeon so the risk of pulling a summoning mob in the zone in tunnel, zone out tunnel, zone in castle area or the tunnel above the castle are fairly slight. The thing that sets chardok apart from most other indoor zones for soloability is the ability to charm animals to do alot of your fighting for you. Especially for the zone in an dzone out tunnels one in 2-3 mobs will be chodikais who can be charmed with call of karana and then used to fight another mob which you root. After epic, dot bracer plus your friendly pet even a light blue pet will dependably finish off a solid blue without dying. Then you invis and kill your now VERY hurt pet off with free dots and get xp for it too. I find that I can usually keep 2 mobs free dotted/rooted while one of those mobs gets beat on by my pet. If I used 1-2 winged deaths I can usually get one chokidai whelp to safely finish off 2 mobs, then invis, kill whelp/growler rinse repeat. With potg/mask of the hunter/+ free dots and free regrowth you can do half of the castle easily or roughly 1 mob every 2 minutes maybe alil more or less. The pathing in front of the castle is less than stellar so sometimes your pets will run up the zone out tunnel if you work there but with some clever moving around you can avoid this. Also if you know where to pull at 60 you can single pretty much every mob on the castle battlements with ease and can even pull the nameds that spawn there which dont summon and kill them too. With a high lvl necro partner its even better xp, they can charm undead with reanimated partisans making the best pets and the 2 of you can just tear it up. With a necro partner we cleared all 10 of the castle plus maybe 6-10 extra mobs every spawn cycle very consistently and made excellent xp. If you dont have epic/free regrowth etc and arent lvl 60 using either the exit or zone in tunnel still works VERY well. One of the keys to this approach is using our glamour of tunare animal tash spell to help you successfully charm pets and to always pull your pets to you with ensnare if your pet breaks root pet or back away and recharm. Other key is to always keep rooted all mobs that arent pets as much as possible. I even root my potential pets and just pull to them oftentimes for added safety. I don't have extensive experience using this technique in sirens grotto but I imagine its very possible there as well. I have even used druid charm in solb on the bats very successfully while soloing LDCs. With their ability to stun mobs the bats tank pretty well and as long as your magic resist is reasonable you can easily resist their stun and recharm fairly easily. One other fun place to indoor kite is the Venril Sathir Remains balcony in VS. If you can break up the spawns near there it is pretty easy to keep 2-3 skeletons/dogs rooted and free dotted moving about on that very large balcony and is quite abit of fun. Again add enchanter or necro partner for increased pet power. The ramblings of a semibored indoor kiter ;)
