Palarran
Nope, don't know of a single list. I rely on lucy.fnord.net (http://lucy.fnord.net) to find out what druid usable focus items there. When I want a particular effect, like say improved damage, I search for a focus effect that I know has improved damage, such as Improved Damage I:
lucy.fnord.net/spell.html...ource=Live (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2336&source=Live)
I click on "Stacking" to see what other focus effects increase damage:
lucy.fnord.net/stacking.h...ource=Live (http://lucy.fnord.net/stacking.html?id=2336&source=Live)
Then I click on effects from the list that look promising. For example, looking down I see Anger of Ro:
lucy.fnord.net/spell.html...ource=Live (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3516&source=Live)
From the spell effects I see this increases damage done by fire based dd spells by 23%, and works on spells up to level 65.
At the bottom of the page is "Items With Spell". I see 3 items: Carved Jetsam Short Staff, Flickering Powersource, and Putrid Eye of the Possessed. Clicking on each one I see that the Carved Jetsam Short Staff is druid usable while the other two aren't.

Then usually I try to look up the item on allakhazam to see what's involved in getting the item...unless it has such a large hp bonus that I can just assume it came from elemental planes.

(Sorry if you already knew how to use lucy...it's a great resource for anyone doing research on items or spells.)
