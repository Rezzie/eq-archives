Previously, tree druids were immune to the holy attack, and she would automatically target the person closest to her who was not at the top of the aggro list. With the last big patch they fixed the treelol immunity and now her holy attack is random. Just need to dispel the effect as soon as possible. We use a priest and a pally and space them at 12 and 6 o'clock in the room so they will both be in range of anyone who needs cleansing. Also make sure everyone else is evenly spaced around the room to avoid the chain lightning attack.
