Kuolon No Surname
If BoT is anything on your server what it is like on Fennin Ro, good luck trying to find a camp. Smid and Spad are virtually perma-camped (best places to get specs, runes, ornate, items) and most of the time there's about 100 people in there. When there's a raid attempting to take down the Tower rares expect that number to rise from 100 to 150.

HoH would probably be your best bet. Far Left Basement seems to be where I've had the best luck with spectrals. If you can get another DoTing class if you, you can easily clear the basement and then pull the unsnarables (the guys in the black armor). One person can be aggro-kiting around the room applying DoTs while the other just meds and lays on the DoTs, adding in a nuke or two. The ones in the black armor seem to have the best rate at dropping the spectrals. Very very rarely will you see a spectral drop off of the guys in the white armor.

Hope this helps you out a bit and good luck :)
