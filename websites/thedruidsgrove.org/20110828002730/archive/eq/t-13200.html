Kaylani's and Kamion's posts pretty much explain how foci work. AFAIK (and IANAE), foci stack with each other as long as they are different kinds. That is to say, your increased healing and decreased mana cost foci will stack, but my 45% increased healing and my 40% increased healing do not make me an 85% better healer. The game picks the better of the two; whichever one will benefit you more (taking into account the max level and penalty for the focus). If they are different foci, they will stack...least I'm pretty sure.
