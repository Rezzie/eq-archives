SoF Druid BP Design

After doing polling both on EQlive and The Druid's Grove feedback seems fairly inline with what I expected.

Worn Focus:

One interesting suggestion was to have the worn focus be a 25% mana pres on our 3.75 second cast time heal spells. Failing this, the most common request was to have the Tunic have 25% mana pres to our SoF 3.75 Heal spell.

It should also be noted that we would like the TBS Tunic to be retro-fitted with a 25% mana pres focus for Pure Life Rank III.

Click Effect:

Due to the two sided nature of the class, The click either needs to help both styles of Druid, or be more offensive based in nature. The two most popular requests I saw were:

1: Clicky spell haste as per the Tunic of Everliving Brambles - this would allow for both Healing and DPS druids to gain from the Click.

2: A combined attack debuff effect.

It is important to note we do 'not' want another Damage Shield Click, nor do we want tunics with 15 minute recycle timers. The recast timer - if it has one, should be between 3 and 5 minutes.

-Fenier
