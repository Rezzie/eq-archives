expertise is very good, if you have access to it. I roll with 35 expertise rating right now (belt off vashj and shoulders from hydross).

At the risk of being dependent on online calculators, http://druid.wikispaces.com/ToskksDPSGearMethod, lets look at AP/AGI comparisons. Raid buffed in Kara/SSC gear, I float around 4k AP and 45% crit, ~100ish hit and 25 expertise, everbloom, malorne, relentless earthstorm.

As you can see, it values +hit and +expertise at the same level, because you're having the same effect, you're decreasing the amount the enemy is not hit. Hit reduces miss chance and expertise reduces dodge chance (we're assuming you're behind and not going to be parried). Thus the vashj belt is technically better for a dpsing druid than anything out of BT, unless you have 80 expertise already (which is no easy feat).

Note, the shapeshifter's signet is not very good by itself. In order for it to really shine, you need upwards of 8k AP and <50% crit (I've never seen anyone anywhere near that high. And if they did get near that high, they'd assuradly have close to 75% crit, which is the cap, which would make the shapeshifter's signet very poor).


It is not rated as high as strength or agility until you're floating around insanely high AP/Crit.

BTW, i said 60 expertise earlier was the cap. I was mistaken, it's 80 expertise.
