I've switched and changed my druid continually from about level 30 upwards, it's only play money and I love the various aspects of being a druid.

In truth I'm hard pushed to recommend Balance as a pure levelling spec. It is good fun to play certainly and you'll have no problems taking on multiple mobs a couple of levels higher than you. But in comparison to Feral the downtime between batches of mobs is that much higher due to your mana usage.

With feral you can just lay waste to a bunch of mobs, pop out, stick a heal or two down, pop back to cat and lay waste to another bunch of mobs while your mana regens. It's rare that you'll ever need to pop a mana pot or even a health pot for that matter and once you get the underspore pod from zangarmarsh, that'll amply cover your health mana needs pretty much from that point on for levelling.

If you're having a play and don't really care too much about "optimal" levelling speed then give balance a go, it's a hoot (sorry) if nothing else. But if you're planning on taking balance for speed of levelling then personally I'd suggest sticking Feral, you're a better out and out killing machine and the improved stealth pays massive dividends on many quests where you can't be arsed to slay everything in sight before you can pick up the foozle.
