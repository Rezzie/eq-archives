I'll list some preKara/heroics decent bear stats:

Armor :: Somewhere between 17-19k armor isn't overly too difficult. You don't need to have this much to start tanking kara / heroics (some of them) imo.

HP :: Tanking 5mans after I hit 70 I was sitting at about 11k unbuffed in bear and I've tanked Kara once with only this much too XD

Crit % :: I honestly do not really factor crit% into how I choose my tanking gear but for building up dodge and armor you should be loading up on agility which will increase your crit. It'll definitely be better than a prot warrior of about equal gear.
