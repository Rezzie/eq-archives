(2) The 15% bonus IS guaranteed. The bonus is not variable anymore as it screwed up buff timings.

(3) Yes, the enhanced effect only take effect when you see the "Your Amulet glows...." As far as I can tell, the screen messages are very reliable, always appearing if the circumstances are correct (correct spell type and level). The one exception is range focus items have a hiccup when combined with z axis range problems. In that case, the mobs move past the focus range and into normal range (in which the focus item won't kick in) while you're still getting the old
Your target is out of range!
Your target is out of range!
Your target is out of range!
Your target is out of range!
Your target is out of range!
Your target is out of range! runaround.
As a matter of fact, I use the focus messages as checks to make sure I'm not still using my CHA (vendor) equipment. :)
