The two strategies that have been listed here that have really served me well are angling down using the space bar to adjust altitude, and turning often. Before I went epic, I usually aimed to the side of the target on approach, then turned just in time to drop the bomb. If you start out aiming directly for your target you will either have to alter course or be shot down. This way you can alter course directly for your target.

There is one down side to having epic flight form during bombing runs- during Fires Over Skettis, I used to keep Autorun pressed and drop the bombs as I flew over the eggs. In epic form, if I do that, I'm out of range before the bomb lands. Small price to pay for my favorite druid ability of all time.
