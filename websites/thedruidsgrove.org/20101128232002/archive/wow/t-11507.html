I got mine Wednesday evening, I know Relevart ( also posts here ) has his and is 70 already ( good pvp'ing against you again dude =D )

It is the Ring of Blood quest line in Nagrand where you have to battle 6 different bosses.

1st is a Clefthoof - nothing really rough about this guy, was able to duo him with a priest healing me.

2nd - 2 mobs 65 elites ( I think ), again a very straight forward tank and spank fight. Again, able to duo these guys, but from here out we asked for help.

3rd - Giant - Does a knock back and a "pierce" which stacks up to 5 doing damage over time. It's kinda nasty, but had the priest heal me as I got 4 on me and it was a very straight forward fight.

4 - Void walker - a aggro reducing knockback, and shadow damage. Still a pretty straight forward fight.

5 - Warrior - I've seen the model vary a few times but he always has the same moves, random charge and mortal strike. Oh, and he hits kind of hard too. This was the hardest IMO.

6 - Shaman - The Ogre guy standing next to the quest giver. He drops totems, heals and frost shocks. After you down him he'll self rez and you get to do it again.
