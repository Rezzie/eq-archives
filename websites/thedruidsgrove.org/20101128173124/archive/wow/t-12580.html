I use itemrack, perl classic unit frames, druid bar, energy watch, ct raid assist, omen (grudingly), and power auras.

Power auras is an awesome addon. It adds a really nice visual element to any UI, it looks really sweet and can be handy for delivering certain types of information to you in a stylised manner.

Basically it lets you make glowy things appear on the screen depending on buffs and debuffs on you or your target, it can also be used to track combo points.

It's really awesome having flashy things pinging off when I get an omen of clarity proc. And the whole thing lights up an angry shade of red when i'm at 5 combo points. It makes dps rotation a lot more fun :> I also have some small transparent green leafs that appear when one of my buffs (motw, ooc, etc) has faded. Nice reminder without being intrusive and looks really sweet :> The leafs pulse gently..

I used to have a TON of addons, more than i can remember to list... Auctioneer and Beancounter (which i didn't understand) to name a few...

Since the latest patch, when all the addons be came out-of-date, I updated just about all of them, and made the switch from Itemrack to Outfitter (still getting used to Outfitter, but love that items that are used with different sets get displayed when I'm at the vendor and trying to empty my bags).

I started getting lag issues at some point, which caused a small problem during Kara runs, but became a MAJOR problem last week with Arena... I would engage, and then immediately lag for about 10 seconds, and then come back to being dead... it was causing my arena partner major grief (uderstandably) so i ended up trimming down my addons by a large margin...

i now run:
-Omen
-Deadly Boss Mods
-AtlasLoot
-Outfitter (a druid must-have)
-OmniCC (awesome for displaying cooldowns)
-Proximo (an Arena must-have)

i THINK the addon that was causing the lag was called Minna. it was supposed to be a damage meter, but i never used it anyways, and now i'll definitely be more scrutinizing when it comes to addons...

*edit* just made the switch from Proximo to Arena Unit Frames at the suggestion of my new 5v5 team last night... Unit Frames is SO much better! if anyone on your team targets someone, Unit Frames picks it up, unlike Proximo, where i *believe* (could be mistaken here) you have to actually ouse over the opponent...
