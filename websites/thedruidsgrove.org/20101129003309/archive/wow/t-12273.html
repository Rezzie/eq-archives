You look to be on the right path, but I wouldn't worry too much about gear as you level up, especially pre-outlands. As Ms Trxitaa said, the balance gear pre-outlands seemed to be geared to clothies and that was not the best for Boomkins.

You won't find a lot of +dmg gear, but pick up what you can. Don't skimp on +stam or int. Get Omen of Clarity when you can so you can be the melee boomkin for mana regen and free spells once you progress that far in your talent trees.

Good luck!
