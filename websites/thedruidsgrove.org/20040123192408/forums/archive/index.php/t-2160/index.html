Yeah, you can solo them. I'm unsure on whether they summon, I killed a few of them pre-charm nerf using a frog pet. My strategy was to snare/tash a frog in the forest, then drag it over through the tunnel to the desert. I'd then charm it when on the desert side of the tunnel.

Then, depending on where the diaku is, there might be a good place to set up camp, might not. Usually if you use Harmony of Nature on the mobs surrounding the diaku (the diaku is immune), and time your pulling right, you can pull out the diaku solo with no problem.

If you do get adds, use Savage Root and park them somewhere while you kill the diaku.

EDIT: So sorry, forgot some key info to answer an above post. No these do not summon, but the problem is that their PHs either a) summon and flurry or b) don't summon but nuke for over 2.5k, possibly a PvP style manaburn. Pull the diakus to the caves on either side of the desert, but watch for pathing as they depop is stuck in a wall. Be careful of the shammy diaku (wears chain and casts). He is the only one that could possibly present a problem as Malo, Pox, Bane and his Ice Based nuke could very quickly ruin your day.
