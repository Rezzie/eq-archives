You can pop healthstones and potions in bear form, there is a macro somewhere here that allows you to shape out potion/healthstone and back into bear mode. All within .2 seconds. I use it all the time tanking. Easy to avoid getting hit with .2 second useage. I have used it tanking several mobs 5-6 and clicked that macro and never once got hit out of bear form. Oh it works if your in cat form also if your dps'ing or soloing.
Edit: I found the macro its

#showtooltip
/cancelform [stance:1]
/use [nostance] Super Healing Potion
/use [nostance] Master Healthstone
/cast [nostance] Dire Bear Form
/stopmacro [nostance]
/cancelform [stance:3]
/use Super Healing Potion
/use Master Healthstone
/cast Cat Form

Just make SURE your global cooldown is UP when you click it otherwise you will be stuck in caster mode untill you manually shape back into bear.

For Neck I'm going-

Cat: Shattered Sun Pendant of Might,
Bear: Necklace of the Deep w/ 2x 15 Stamina Gems.

The necklace has about 6.5 more DPS than the Resolve. BUT there are no Gem slots like in the Necklace of the Deep, so IF you need specific gems like for the Relentless Earthstorm Diamond (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=32409), then you may need to make up some gem slots somewhere.

Bear staff I would take neither. Earthwarden is probably the easiest Bear tanking staff to get for now. Only the Wildfury Great Staff (world drop) or Pillar of Ferocity (Mount Hyjal) are better. And you only need CE rep for the Earthwarden. If you have a good price on, or already own, the Braxis then equip it until you have one of those 3. It is very good.

And in Kitty form I would choose the Bladefist Breadth's. In the short term, you want to replace that with either the Crystalforged Trinket (Ogri'la Revered) or Battlemaster's Determination (PvP). Otherwise that BB trinket is tight. Outside of drops in raid instances, the Bloodlust Brooch (41 badges) and Darkmoon Card: Crusade are top choices.

Also for bear form don't neglect your trinkets. You can have quite a bit of fun going after Mark of Tyranny (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=13966) and Badge of Tenacity (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=32658). The first one can be soloed up until UBRS in which you'll need 2 others to activate the first boss. After that, it can easily be done with one other assuming you know UBRS...but a 70 bear tank is a formidable beast.

And the Badge is a great event in Ogri'la (solo only) that you will do every 5 days of dailies which you want rep for the Kitty trinket.

And as an example, the Mark of Tyranny gives me over 1000 armor in bear form on top of the dodge. And the Aracane Resist will GUARANTEE you'll never be polymorphed. :grin:

P.S. I have no idea why my armor changes by over 1k simply by equipping that trinket. 410% (Dire Bear + Thick Hide) of 180 is not close to 1k. But that's what my character sheet shows when I equip/dequip it.
