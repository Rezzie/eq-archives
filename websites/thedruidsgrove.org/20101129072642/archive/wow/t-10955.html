I love group PvP in moonkin, especially in AV.

However, most people don't want to hear about AV, they just accept range is a big edge there. In WSG I love to stand stealthed midfield and root flag runners as they come straight for me, its also handy to be able to fall in behind a flag runner and just keep dropping MFs on him as you both run, no one survives long. In AB I am a great node defender whipping out the roots and long bombs from the bushes.

As balance, I feel it is my duty to help with the cc, and FF all rogues. I also try to focus on the targets I am strongest against (warriors), while others take care of the ones I am weakest against (casters). Moonkin form itself isn't terribly helpful, as I mostly PuG it and my group doesn't stick together on the field, but it is the only known AoE PvP taunt, which can be handy in itself (exaggerating slightly on the taunt, but you will have people coming for you breaking their own lines which can be used to strategic advantage).
