SuburbanLife
Hey folks,

I just wanted to let you know that I was duo-ing the Stormriders upstairs with a good druid friend of mine and the aaxp/xp is pretty decent. They drop fast because they have low HP, the only issue is the clerical ones that CH themselves and cast that Reckoning spell on you.

Just have your MR up maybe some gear to give you a little boost, and both of you should keep Storm's Fury memmed and when the critter starts to CH just alternate casts like every 5 seconds or so. In between you need to cast your dot's to make sure the fooker goes down. Some of them do gate as well, so you gotta watch out for that.

Hope this helps some of ya who have a good druid pal in game, it is really fast fun xp, and if you have a horse you can pretty much med the whole time, we never dipped below 60% mana unless a CH went off. And I don't have insane gear (yet) so I am pretty sure the majority of druids could camp here.
