I'm not quite sure what you are asking.

First, there are the basic spell skills: i.e., Alteration, Evocation, etc. These go up naturally as you cast spells of that type. They are capped by level. Don't worry about spending any points on these.

Second, there are the specializations for each basic spell skill. When you can, buy each one by putting one point in each. These also go up naturally as you cast spells. The first specialization to go beyond 50 is your "superspecialty," and will go to 200 (again, just by casting). Note that you cannot go beyond 50 in any specialization UNLESS you have put at least a point into ALL specializations (i.e., you have purchased all specializations).

To ensure that the specialization you want goes beyond 50, just cast spells of that type repeatedly until it hits 51.

An alternative strategy is to buy the one you want to go beyond 50 only, play normally, then when that one hits 49 or 50, go put a point in the other ones. Then just cast spells of the desired type until the first hits 51.

There is a quest to change your superspecialty. You can find information about that here on the board. It resets everything to 50, I believe.

The Secondary Forte AA permits one other specialization to go beyond 50. The first one to go beyond 50 (from normal casting) will be your secondary specialization.
