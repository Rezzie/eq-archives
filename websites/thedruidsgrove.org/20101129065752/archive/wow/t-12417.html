Def isn't worthless for pvp, its just that other stats are far more important.

Res is huge, as it not only reduces your chance to be crit, but also reduces DoT damage on you, and with the incoming mana drain nerf, reduces the amount of mana drained from you.

It also depends on your playstyle. If you want to primarily play kitty in bg's, your priority should be on being stealthy and killing things. If you want to be a huge ass meat shield and just run in and try to get FF'd in bear, sta and defense wouldn't be a bad idea. Just that your damage will drop significantly if you stack def over ap and crit.
