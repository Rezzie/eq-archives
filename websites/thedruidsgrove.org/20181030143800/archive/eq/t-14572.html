burial chambers
Take a left when you get off the elevator. head north. There will be a room with coffins in it and a statue guarding the door north. To the west is a statue on the wall which is a hidden doorway. I don't remember if the update is in the main room or through there.. but it is on one of the two.

entry hall Throne Room
I thought that part was removed?

Shrine of Zek
In the room I mentioned above, head north past the statue. Take a right inside and follow around the room to the north. The task should update as you travel east, counterclockwise. If you reach the 12 o' clock position, turn around and go back.
