Meg26
"HP isn't always greater than mana, it depends on if you tend to get hit alot."

I used to be a mana person, always looked for mana first. But now, hps have become more important. Of course I only go for items that are about equal in hp/mana and opt for the effect and resists over all. But I have to say that I think hps are overall more important if you're in a guild that kills a lot of mobs that have aoe's. If you're in the elementals, you'll encounter lots of aoe mobs and the more hp's you have, the more it helps you and the raid as a whole (so no one's burning mana and time trying to heal you constantly). Of course higher resists could help this as well but some aoe's are impossible or close to impossible to resist, so in that case higher hps would help. If you're in the elementals I don't see how this should be an issue anyway. You'll eventually have 100+hp/mana in ever slot. I quickly ditched my ss legs when pop came out for ornate ones. Why not if they're free from my guild? I sacraficed 10mana for 35hps and stats and a neat effect. I probably wouldn't bother at this point though if you can simply get elemental legs or seaweed legs though. For me I know it will be awhile before I get any since we have yet to see leather legs drop and lots of leather wearers to equip.
