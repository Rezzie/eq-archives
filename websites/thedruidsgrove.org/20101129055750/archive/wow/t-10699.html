I have begun my grind for the rank 10 gear, and got in a few ABs last night (which is a rarity on Alliance side).

I am really enjoying the increase in action over my normal grinding/ instance healing. Especially since I really get to let loose with all my moonkin fury.

I have noticed it before, but it was remarkably clear last night, that whenever I shifted to moonkin, I immediately became the target of every hordie in 100yds. I know some people look at this as a real downside, with all the extra deaths that inevitably result, but I was finding ways to make it useful.

For instance, during one assault on the BS I got moonki aggro on all the defenders, and backed away, dropping MFs and FFs, pulling like 5 hordies away from the flag with me clear to the bridge, I even got some heals from passing alliance priests. My teammates picked off 2 of them before they got me down, and they were so far from the flag now, one of our rogues stealth assaulted the node successfully.

I also find that there is no warrior on my server I can not beat with my moonkin. I do not think this is shameless boasting, I think it is the one class that really has a strong disadvantage in a 1 on 1 fight with me. Their physical attacks can't get through my AC and barkskin, and their AC doesn't protect them from my MF.

So I have taken to picking on a warrior or two on the other side and killing them early, and then emoting taunts the rest of the fight to bring them to me out of pride, and then I kill them again. Its a lot of fun.
