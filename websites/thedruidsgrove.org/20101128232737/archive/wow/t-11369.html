Yes, they upped the AC values on many items. Malfurion's Blessed Bulwark got a significant boost as well. I believe it was a straight percentage on armor only. This mean that your lovely Staff of Jordan has the same boring AC as before. . .

But I will say one thing about the new buffs to AC. I ride at about 12k with 23-25% dodge depending on the boss fight. I could easily go higher with AC, but I find that, in terms of overall damage mitigation, dodge becomes more effective once AC > 10k. Also don't forget that you need hefty stamina to pull off some of the higher end bosses (particularly those bosses with hard-hitting magic). So the one thing I'll say (took me long enough, right?) is that I am now switching away from AC heavy gear to slightly lower AC and major stat improvements in either the area(s) of agility and/or stamina.
