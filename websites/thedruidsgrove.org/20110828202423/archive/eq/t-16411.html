2nd what eagle said. And like he implied, it's situational as well. Soloing, if you're someone that can micromanage dots well, pulling multiple mobs and dotting them will by far and away blow nuking out of the water in dps and efficiency. For raids, I can hit 1200 dps (and easily maintain over 1k consistantly) for a parse fight alternating rains and nukes in with my highest curse dot keeping NBW on hand for GoRM procs.

Groups I can't help you with. I don't group often because I'm a blatant over-aggroer and early-nuker.

I group almost exclusively with another druid who has a Bard bot. We use the root/rot system and because we are both druids, some of our spells don't work together.
