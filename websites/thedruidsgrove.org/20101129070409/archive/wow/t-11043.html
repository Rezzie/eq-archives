Here goes,

Chances are you were dealing with some serious twinks in the lvl 19 bracket. At lvl 19 all you have is Bear >.< and unless you have some nice twink gear you're going to get stomped on.....a lot.

I pvp'd heavily for a while and hit rank 12. I was NEVER that high for HKs b/c I was busy running the flag, or stealth capping a node in AV or AB.

Since that time I have re-geared and re-spec'd to resto. Now I play full time heal support ( and healing a fury warrior that just mows through people lends to a ton of HKs ). In PUGs, if you heal you'll be loved and protected. In performed groups you'll have decided roles ahead of time.

PvP for druids becomes much more viable in the 30 - 39 backet since we get our travel form and can outrun everyon =D. Before that it is really rough.

In the end though, HKs mean nothing. It's the wins and how you play as a team that will get people respecting your abilities. A good team player is 100x better than an amazing go it aloner.
