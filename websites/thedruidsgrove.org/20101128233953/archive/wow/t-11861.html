Check out WoW Loot (http://www.wow-loot.com/druid.htm).

And for those that just hit lvl 70 and/or are about to hit lvl 70, I highly recommend their "Class Gear Planner (Beta)". It allows you to weigh gear specs (+ap, +int, etc.) and then provides the "top-5" pieces for each slot. Allows you to include/exclude rep gear, profession gear, Tier 1 RAID (kara, gruul, etc.), Tier 2 Raid (SSC, etc.). It makes a great "to do" list for those leveling. And given that many (most? all?) folks ding lvl 70 well before they complete BEM, Netherstorm and/or SMV it really helps to focus your time on which incomplete quests are worth doing.

"My shoulders suck, what is the best Feral shoulder I can get as a quest reward while I wait in queue and/or for an instance group?"
