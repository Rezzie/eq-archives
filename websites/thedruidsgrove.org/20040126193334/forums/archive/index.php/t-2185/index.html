It's not near the top of the stairs, you need to kill the gorillas in the NW corner. (after you reach the top of the stairs) There are only three tottering gorillas trackable in the NW corner, keep killing them as they respawn and the tainted will appear sooner or later. Just keep in mind that undead tainted gorillas will not spawn a corrupted, just kill it like a normal tottering so that hopefully the next spawn will be a live tainted.
