If the rest of your guild is well geared and goes through Kara regularly you will be carried through without causing any issues most likely. After a run or two you'll start to carry your own weight, providing you get some gearage. Make sure you get it properly enchanted and gemmed!

As far as a spec is concerned... if you want to go through Kara and truly not pull your weight, go for a hybrid pvp spec. If you want to contribute, then go for Tree of Life. I wish I could tell you there was a middle ground that is perfectly fine, but there isn't. I put 42 points into Resto and the rest in Balance. But there's a pvp talent in Resto and Nature's Grasp in Balance is nice for pvp.

I still play arenas but we're not serious pvp'ers and our score isn't great. This game requires most classes to pick what they're going to excel at, pvp or pve. Kind of blows you can't really do both, without dropping a ton of money into respecing.

8/11/42 is a really strong pvp / arena build that allows you ToL and other goodies for PvE healing

get 5/5 NG and 3/3 the talent that reduces root and cyclone time in balance, pick up feral talents up to Feral charge, then fill in Resto with all talents that increase HoT healing.

You'll be fine in Kara if they already have it on farm. Don't expect to top the healing meters or anythig, but you'll be ok to just sit in Tree and spam LB on random raid members, while the main healer focuses on the tank.
