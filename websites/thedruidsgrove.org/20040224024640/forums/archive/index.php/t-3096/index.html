Role Meggido says: It could just be that you are seeing market stabalization and not nerfed drop rates. I have no idea personally, but I offer this out there.

I have yet to a single case where an item's price gradually went UP over time, unless the item or access to the item was nerfed. It just doesn't happen, because the more people farm, the more supply there is, and the less demand there is (because people are constantly acquiring the item and it never goes away). If you are seeing less of the item and with higher prices, it signals a nerf.
