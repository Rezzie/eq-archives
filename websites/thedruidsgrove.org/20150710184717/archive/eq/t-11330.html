Wolf form reduces your faction by 2 steps in Velious cities, so don't use it there unless the guards con warmly or above. Iirc, wolf form is KoS in all Luclin cities. Wolf form does not change faction in LoY zones (you are always KoS there). I don't remember using wolf form in GoD, OOW, or DoN zones.

Special note: Never use wolf form in Dragon Necropolis.
