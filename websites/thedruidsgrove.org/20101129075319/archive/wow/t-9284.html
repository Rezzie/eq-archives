I haven't found Sea Lion form to be very useful. It's really more of a fun diversion.

But I just did a quest that was easy in Sea Lion form. It's called "The Vile Reef" and you get it from one of the Trolls in Grom'gol. Of course it's for Horde only and I was level 33 when I got it. I don't know what level you need to be to get the quest but Allakhazam lists it as 37 Elite. You have to get a piece of some artifact from the vile reef, which is right by the coast of Grom'gol.

What makes it so easy is that, although there are mobs all over down there, there are none right by the artifact. Just swim down to it, Shapeshift to normal, get the piece, shapeshift back to Sea Lion and go get your reward.
