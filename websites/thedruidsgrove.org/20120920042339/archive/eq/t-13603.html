I second Dayuna's suggestion, plus something else for you to think about:
Secondary Forty -- reduces mana use for certain spells of your choice. This one is important.
Innate Metabolism -- save food when you are on horse
Run 5 -- run faster (especially indoor zones)
Innate Agility line -- your AGI is not maximized, why not increase it, may help with avoiding melee damage. (I think your STA is almost maximized, if not may consider that line as well).
Silent casting line -- reduce hate from healing
secondary recall -- secondary bind point is nice
