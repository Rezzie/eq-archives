Wrath of the Wild on the MA, using it on the puller can create a summoning situation if the DS drops the mob to 97 or below in health.

I tend to use CoS as either a quick direct heal on a secondary group member, me after I cast Oaken Guard, or on the MA after a Direct Heal.

If you're getting agro from Convergence it might just be from the instant heal. Using it very early in a fight could pull agro, though I've never noticed it.

Wrath definately shouldn't make any changes to a group's agro. If anything you might generate a tiny bit of agro yourself if you cast it midfight due to the 1 pt rune.

I think our only direct agro mod-spell is Oaken Guard which can lower your hate on mobs swinging at you.
