For Comparison, the proc from Direwood Guard RK. 1 (Direwood Guard Parry http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=9908) decreases hate by 552.

It's like 10 hate reducing procs from direwood guard, and if you are summoned by a raid boss in top end content, chances are you won't survive long enough for direwood to proc 10 times.

It *Would* stack with Silent Casting, but you would use this after you cast your spells. 5000 hate on a raid is most definately at least 2 or 3 spots on a hate list, so it could be a real life-saver id say. There isn't a real reason to use this combined with Silent Casting tho.
