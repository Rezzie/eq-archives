If you only have Maul, use it. Just not on every swing, if you do not have enough rage.

When doing solo quests with 2 or more mobs you really should have no rage issues and can use Maul all the time. You should focus on one target and try to CC the other(s) - root them, stun them, whatever.

I often started in Cat Form and, out of stealth, built up 5 CP on one mob, Maim, shift out, throw some heals, go to Bear, finish them. But that only works when they do not hit hard.
