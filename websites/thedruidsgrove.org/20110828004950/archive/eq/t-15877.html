No, various shrouds are monsters but they are also a class. Most are berserkers, warriors, roques, wizards or enchanters.

When I unretired my SK, who was level 53 and naked, I played him shrouded a lot to catch him up. The fastest I found (altho shroud exp has been nerfed) was the wizard shrouds....kei (or potion) and sow (or potion) and I could kite fast'n'easy. The berserker basilisk is also good, does well tanking and has a nice regen buff.

And if you can manage to open the kobold rogue, that's worth it to watch him "play dead".
