The lure of Secondary Forte is its wider scope than Casting Fury. Specifically, it works with spells like Hand of Ro and Immolation of Ro (or, for you Evocation druids, it works with Tunare's and Karana's Renewal where HG doesn't.)

Comparing it to Casting Fury, I'd put it between FoMM1 and FoMM2. I believe the FoMM line costs 4, 8 and 12 points. If each level provides a 2% increased chance of a crit, 4 AA for a ~2% DPM* boost is the best purchase, but 2nd Spec's 3% mana savings for 10 AA is a better purchase than FoMM2.

* Comparing an increase in crit % to an increase in mana preservation cannot be done properly without considering a number of variables. The general form for damage per mana includes:

Base Damage*(1 + Expected Crit % + Focus %: (Max % + .01)/2 + Song %: .08)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Base Manacost*(1 - Specialization % - SCM % - Mana Pres Focus %: (Max % + .01)/2)

I'm defining marginal dpm as (DPM with New AA) / (DPM without). If you don't like math, "marginal dpm" = "bang".

With a few assumptions about the variables above, we can calculate marginal dpm for the AAs in question. For these examples, I've used the following: Alteration Primary specialization, SCM3 trained, FoM3 trained, no bard song. It turns out that the damage focus and mana preservation focus both affect the marginal dpm.

Marginal dpm for a 2% crit increase, as a function of damage focus
----------------------------------------------------------------
0 focus: 1.74%
ID4(10.5% focus): 1.59%
Wrath (18% focus): 1.5%

Marginal dpm for a 3% preservation benefit, as a function of mana preservation
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
0 mana pres:3.57%
MP4 (5.5% mana pres): 3.82%
Summer's Vengeance (13% mana pres on SF): 4.23%

Now to compare the AAs, we want "bang for your buck":
I've assumed Improved Damage IV, and Mana Pres 4
Points = 100 * Marginal DPM / AA cost

FoMM1 2% crits for 4 aa: 39.75 points
FoMM2 2% crits for 8 aa: 19.875 points
FoMM3 2% crits for 12 aa: 13.25 points
2nd Forte 3% manapres for 15 AA: 25.5 points

As it turns out, 2nd Forte is very close to FoMM1. All things considered, I'll probably get 2nd Forte before FoMM1 because of the benefits on debuffs and dots.

Well, the updated version of 2nd Forte still sits between FoMM1 and FoMM2 when you're comparing adjustments to dpm. At 15 AA points it becomes more of a luxury purchase, in the same league as FoMM2 and FoM3. I'll definitely get FoMM1 first now.
