My L65 druid's gear is a mixture of PoP Ornate and LDoN, with a few LDon purchased augments. I have upgraded for the OOW-GOD-DODH-DON expansions and am looking for a suitable upgrade path in general. Casual grouping is the extent of my ability to hunt for dropped or quested gear.

I'll try to keep my questions brief. Googling has helped me a lot, but these are the things I'm not sure where to go to find out:

What's better for me to save up for, GM Cultural, or the armour you buy with Radiant Crystals? (Piece for piece I can compare but it's a question of how I can upgrade the two different types with augs).
GM Cultural, fully aug'd, has superior stats than DoN Crystal purchased armor fully aug'd. However, both those routes take a great deal of time. You may want to consider getting your armor from PoR Tier 1 (see note #2 below) and using your DoN crystals to buy augments instead.

Can I only use augs bought with crystals, in armour bought with crystals, or can I also combine them with Cultural?
Augments can be placed in any armor with the corresponding augment slot. They can also be removed from armor and put into new armor. You just need to buy a solvent from an NPC vendor (ranging in price from a few pp to 1kpp+).

Will upgrading from LDoN gear mean my old augs are just plain obsolete or is there any non-LDoN armour with the same slots?
I don't know the LDoN aug slots off hand, but the new armor uses aug slots 7,9 & 12 most often.

I believe there is a way to sell your LDoN pieces back for LDoN points. You might want to look into doing that and repurchasing different augs that would fit the newer armor. Anyone have a link on how to do that?

Losing clicky-root or mana pres from my current gear would pain me. Are there similar effects on Radiant Crystal armour or Cultural? If not, would all the extra mana and wisdom make up for it?
You can't buy clicky root, but almost all focus effects can be bought with Radiant Crystals. Check this site (http://plurpage.com/everquest/don.php) for a searchable list of augments.

Two Notes Of Interest:

1 - While you are earning Radiant Crystals, you can upgrade a few visible armor pieces and many non-visible pieces quickly an easily with Monster Missions. Check out this list (http://eq.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=12277) which includes what missions have leather drops and non-visible slot rewards. The "Revenge of Farsooth" South Karana mission has a rare reward of an FT3 Ranged Slot item, as well.

2 - Consider buying the PoR expansion. You can get into a pick up group in Arcstone to camp the diff named mobs that drop the quest item to turn in to get this level of armor:
