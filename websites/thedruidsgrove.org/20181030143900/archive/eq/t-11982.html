Heya Milenax!

Welcome back to Norrath/AB! Greetings to the ogre aswell ;-)

No new spells that i know of (except you want to buy the DoN snare - bought with crystals (the DoN points), it's level 61 and has prismatic resist check) before lvl 66.
After you dinged, you need to trade in different kind of muramite runes for the 66+ spells (described here: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2927). The 66-68 ones are droppable and can be bought in baz, the 69/70 ones are no drop and need you to hunt in higher OoW zones. The runes can drop from nameds in nearly any OoW zone, besides Harbringer's, Dranik's Scar and Bloodfields.

Look at this thread for where to hunt: http://eq.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=11794 (first post mentions Noble's Causeway and the trashdrops you can get there), and this one: http://www.therunes.net/forums/viewtopic.php?t=8276 is a nice DoN primer. DoN is nice to group in, to get crystals to buy some augments and gear.

Hope this helped a bit,

Cya online hopefully!
