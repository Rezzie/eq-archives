Another good trick that used to make more money on my server than it currently does (damn farmers) is to Transmute water -> air

You can get this transmute from the Scholo quest line that gives you the Spectral Essence, allowing you talk to the ghost vendors. The vendor is the same one that you buy the major mana pot recipie from.

I used to buy Essence of water for like 6-8g, transmute it to air, then sell the essence of air for like 14-17g. Cooldown on this transmute is only 1 day, not 2. So that was like 8-10g per day, depending. Though recently there have been tons of people farming essence of air in Silithus, which have pushed the air prices down to like 10g. Now that the air market is flooded, I just transmute Undeath (1g) to Water, which I sell for about 6 to 8g.
