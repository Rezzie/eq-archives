If you comb through some of the threads here you'll find various info. Off the top of my head....

Elysians in UP -- They drop a couple of nice items that you can sell in the bazaar (Death Mask, Death Watcher's Shroud), although drops can be sparse at times. They usually drop 8-10pp per kill also.

They have a long summon timer ... Like 20 sec. Snare, root, free DoTs, nuke, nuke, get summoned, back up, heal, nuke, nuke, get summoned, back up, heal, nuke ... ad nausium. Viscid Roots (or even the SoL root ability whatever it's called) is a must for this type of solo.

You will want your fast cast nukes up for this encounter (Breath of Karana, Scoriae, etc).

Nice thing about corrupteds is that they like to summon you then cast a spell so their melee output is pretty low. Bad thing about this encounter is that the corrupteds have their fast cast nukes up (Breath of Karana, Scoriae, etc).

Corrupteds are level 58-59 druids. So if you take one on, you're pretty much in for a shootout. Risk of death is high, but a very fun fight.

There's a great money spot for druids in PoN - the ravenous nightstalker camp (follow zone wall to the right from the zone in). They are charmable with Call of Karana (the lvl 52 charm), and can be single pulled with Harmony of Nature. The drop raw diamonds and diamonds reasonably frequently (150pp or so each to a vendor) and can even drop blue diamonds less commonly. They go light blue at level 63, but before that, the xp is good, and in 3-4 hours you can get 1k+ of gems easily.

Disclaimer: its been a while since I have personally done this camp, so I don't know if the gem drop rate has been nerfed or anything.
