Not gone discuss all the spells and stuff, I will post here what I have found, however many (not all) spells and discs have several levels:

Lesser Ice Blast
Ice Blast
Greater Ice Blast

These are all spells for the same level. The Lesser Ice Blast will be easy to get, bought or groupable, the Ice Blast will need a good group event/named or a quest and the Greater Ice Blast will drop on Raid events.
