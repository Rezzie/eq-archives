In addition to the forums here, www.allakhazam.com is loaded with useful info. To find the port spell vendors click on Spells--->Druids and scroll down the list. The links for each spell usually supply vendor info.

I left EQ for WoW for 7 months and came back essentially "lost" like you. Took about a week to get a basic "grounding" enough to be able to solo and group. Within a month it was like I had never left. And at the time I had to relearn two classes, druid and enchanter.

Like others will say, it's to be expected your gear sucks now, even if you were in the best raiding guild on the server back then. But gear upgrades are readily available. There are posts in these forums which will help on that score or use the equipment search features on allakhazam. l
