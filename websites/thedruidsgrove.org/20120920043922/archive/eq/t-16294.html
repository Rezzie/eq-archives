I found an interesting thing that I think might help a lot of druids. When you cast a group port spell open your blocked buff list and add "Circle of whatever" or "Wind of whatever", like Circle of Grimling. When you cast the port everyone in the group near you will get sent there, but the druid stays. Just click off the blocked circle spell and you are gtg.

I really doubt that this would be something the devs would ban. I thought about it for a bit before posting. We are already given teleports for the new expansions, showing that they don't think druids should never get the ability.
