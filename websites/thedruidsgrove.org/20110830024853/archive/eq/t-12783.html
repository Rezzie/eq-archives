You need an "Invocation" item from Demi Plane tier 2 in order to get these upgrades, or rather "2.5". So far...

Tris Wallows III drops the caster one "Invocation of Night (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=83626)" (nec, enc, mag, wiz)

Devlin drops the hybrid one, Invocation of the Last Dawn (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=83624) (bst, ranger, paladin, sk, bard)

Roley has the pure melee one, while Performer obviously drops the Priest one.

After you get any of these pages, you need to find Eric Von Schnelling in Dreadspire and just give him your page. You will have an improved epic 2.0!

Look below for your class 2.0 and add the stats below the name to their base stats to get the result of this quest. Aditionally to that, the caster ones have what appears to be the same Focus, but it's not. Click on it to see what kind of foci you get. I haven't decyphered them yet.
