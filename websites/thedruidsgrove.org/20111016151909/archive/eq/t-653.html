Unfortunately, if s/he doesn't know the "real" name for KEI, he can't look it up.

KEI is the acronym for Koadic's Endless Intellect.

It's a group spell, costing 900 mana to cost; level 60 enchanter.

It has the following effects:
Increase Mana Pool by 250
Increase Mana by 14 per tick
Increase INT by 25
Increase WIS by 25

Unless the enchanter has AAs to make it last longer, it lasts 2 and 1/2 hours.

It doesn't stack with other Enchanter mana regen spells. It does NOT stack with Necro Lich spells, so far as I know, but other than that, stacks with other mana regen spells different classes have.

It's really noticable mana regen, and a pleasure to hunt with. Hope the info helps.
