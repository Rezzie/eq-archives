The two primary specilizations for druids are Alteration and Evocation. In most polls of druids we are fairly split down the center between those two specs. Alteration is used for most healing spells and Evocation is used for most Nukes. Your decision on what to specialize in should be based on what you like to do most as a druid. If you prefer being healer in a group then alteration might be the way to go. If you prefer to be a damage druid and nuking in groups then evocation is better. Your specilzation is not set in stone you can always do the quest at the Temple of Ro to reset it at a later date.

Very few druid specialize in the other areas however there are usually a few that take them up. Conjuration is best for druid swarm line of dots, Abjuration for most buffs. Druids have very few divination spells at higher levels.

Not true, I spec in evocation and also am my guild's best druidic healer. Specialization simply equates to a small amount of mana savings for casting spells in your speciality.
