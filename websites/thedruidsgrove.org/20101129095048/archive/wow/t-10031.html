The Elemental Leatherwork is a useful specialization for druids. The items produced tend to be more focused for specific events or playstyles than Tribal (i.e. the Living leg/shoulders/breastplate set are +healing, the volcanic set is +fire resists). Elemental LW is considered to be more expensive than Tribal since you will either need to pay AH prices for rare drop materials or spend a lot of time farming the materials (i.e. essence of air/water/earth drop from 50+ elementals in silithus/EPL/Azsharra, lava cores drop in MC).
