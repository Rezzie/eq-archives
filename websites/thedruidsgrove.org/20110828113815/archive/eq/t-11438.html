As suggested, go to Noble Causeway and kill murkglieders at trench close to Dranik Scar. You get good xp, they are easy and a lot of space to snare/root. If you are there long enough you will eventually get the zone random drops Foresight Sash (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30506) and Drakescale Shield (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30592). Also might have chance to get some good selling drops to buy yourself some upgrades. Collect the 4 pieces for this quest Zakariv's Shattered Sword (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2966) to get Woven Cape of the Militia (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32093).

Get to know the zone well - there is a great write-up on this board Noble's Causeway Guide (http://eq.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=10283). You can ooc lfg or if any drops rotting, so can get some other upgrades from named. For quest you need Dranik Loyalist faction, so after collecting the 4 pieces from the murks, you can go to the other side of zone close to WoS and kill dragorns for faction and they drop the zone random stuff also. For faction also camp in Dranik Scar Ukun Gutfeaster for Dull Stone of Sorrow (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30528) and Ukun Slavehunter for Eye of the Dragorn (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=31700). Faction is very difficult to raise though.

When camps are taken, you can try Barindu for Fractured Obsidian Band (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=26061) (i didnt manage to get that one myself yet).

And of course, check bazaar for cheap upgrades. Dont know how it is on other servers but on AB some common drops from oow named have become pretty cheap and might be of interest for you.

At lvl 67-68 I soloed Borso's ring quest in Relic (POR zone) although regular mobs summon, the supply runners do not.
With Mire Thorns and Glamour of Tunare you might be able to solo the bears in Icefall. If not check out who is killing them and try to get in their group or loot 8 quest pelts from the bears, to get 2 x 220mana bracers.

Work the Wraithguard faction (the quest above) a bit more with killing mammoths for Ivory and you can solo the Shield quest at the zone in, in Frostcrypt. Check alla for details, but basically set a hot key to 'stop' and spam the npc with it. Once the bad guy pops, dot it snare it nuke it and stay out the way of it and pray you get it dead before it kills the npc. Sadly the shield isnt useable til 75 though.
