I learned something from someone I saw in the new x-realm BGs.

Since priests can mind vision you and see where you are, there's no real need to go and hide (unless you're a punk and you're terrain hacking up on some ledge [then again, I can't blame you, what bliz doesn't fix gets exploited. no news...])...

Since there's no need to run and hide, I find the best spot to hang out while you wait for the horde flag to return is outside on the second level above the tunnel exit on the horde side... There is a hump there right next to the fence. Remember this is on the OTHER side of the fence. So the side facing the mid field. Just take a look there and you'll see the hum. You can easily run along the outer side of the fence to get there. The cool thing? If you're in cheetah, you can easily clear the fence just by jumping... But everyone else can't make that jump... They have to mount up in order to follow you. So if you hang out there, you can have a view of the mid field and see when people are coming to get you. If you get caught by a rouge, shift out of cheetah, warstomp, shift back into cheetah and jump the fence. They'll have to go all the way around to catch you. If it's 40+, the'll have to mount up. Either way, you're out of there long before they can do anything...

This doesn't apply if a mage/hunter/warlock/priest comes gunning for you, of course... But most of the time, I find it's a rogue/warrior/pally who comes to reclaim the flag. They'll never catch you in the 30-39 bracket. And even in the 60 bracket, this makes it very hard to be caught...

Sure beats sitting on the roof with my butt half into the wall and not able to see anything...
