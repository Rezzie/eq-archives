I hope you have the Feral versions of the S3 gear and not the Resto or balance versions. The feral versions are the ones with "Dragonhide" in their names (not Wrymhide or Kodohide). Assuming you have the feral version, keep the shoulders (if you have them) and the gloves. Those are 2 slots that are hard to fill (shoulders especially) for feral tanks. Heavy Clefthoof (craftable pieces) will give you 3 more slots (chest, legs, feet), stylin' purple hat, braxxis and/or earthwarden for your weapon.

Here's a good link (http://www.wowinsider.com/2008/04/15/shifting-perspectives-gearing-your-bear-tank-from-70-to-karazha/) that covers basic tanking gear (noob lvl 70, pre-kara, heorics, badge items).
