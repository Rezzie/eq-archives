Several of the folks in my guild got to 80 by Sunday, myself included. It doesn't require cheats and it doesn't require chaining yourself to the computer. It does require pinpointing places with good experience and being efficient.

Thallasius missions in Katta yield excellent experience. Go in, kill to the statue location, pop lesson, kill everything you can as fast as you can. If you're exping the mission itself is irrelevant.

Druids can swarm charm kite - I got a third of a level in 1 1/2 hours at my favorite spot. There are also numerous open zone camp sites in SoF. Added up all total, I'd guess about 20 hours of effort to got from 75.99 to 80.
