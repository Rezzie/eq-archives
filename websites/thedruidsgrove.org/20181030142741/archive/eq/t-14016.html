Both grove and life have disease counters, always have. Grove was 10 regen and 4 disease counters, Life was 30 regen and 2 disease counters; per tick. Following a farming of AMV in CoA, and most of the group died because we relied on Aura of the Grove, I looked into this further because prior to last night we were getting the Mark of Death cures accomplished with the level 55 Grove.

Well, there was a ninja swap unannounced in the 6/13 patch. Now, grove has 2 disease counters and life has 4 disease counters - per tick - and the regens have not changed. It takes 14 disease counters to cure the AMV's Mark of Death. Therefore, use Aura of Life on the AMV encounter. Oh, and the group members that didn't die to the Mark last night were cautious enough to use counteract disease potions.
