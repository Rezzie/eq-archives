Well after extensive testing on my part during Mount Hyjal and Black Temple raids I have confirmed the following.

Check spell rotation with Eclipse and noted the following.
- IS,MF,SF,SF repeat has 300 more dps than
- IS,MF,SF/Wrath(change every time eclipse hits) repeat had 130 more dps than
- IS,MF,Wrath repeat

Top rotation also did not go below 70% mana even when Bloodlust was used while the bottom two rotation did eventually make me go oom.

This was tested base on only boss fights. (I toppped dps on hyjal trash by spamming hurricane ftw!)


Both of those spell rotations only needed one MF per fight since I have glyph of SF (Kazrogal started with 13 sec MF dot and fight ended with same dot at 25 sec :P )

Thus I have concluded:
-eclipse sucks way 2 much to be considdered as an effective talent.
-spamming starfire is the best rotation for boss fights (but can be so boring).
-FF has not been tested but can up your dps quite a bit as well.
-Furor is awsome and the only resto talent I took in my boomkin spec.
