Discordant Energy and Discordant Fire work on Magic and Fire dots in that order, they increase damage output by 45% on them. Keep in mind that Discordant Flame does not.

Discordant Thoughts only work for "mez" type spells, so they mostly should go to Enchanters. I'm not sure on Discordant Malaise but i think it only works on Malo type spells... however i'm not completely sure if it works on Glamour of Tunare. If not, then these 2 are pretty much useless for druids.
