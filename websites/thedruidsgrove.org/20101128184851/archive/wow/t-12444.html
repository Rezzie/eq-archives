For mace, staff, etc skill, you will need to be hitting an enemy with your weapon whilst in caster form. Your "feral combat skill" automatically advances the full 5 levels each time you level up, ie your feral combat skill is always maxed.

Therefore it's not crucial to level staff or mace skill, although it can be nice when you run out of other easy ways to ugprade your character.

I would recommend going full feral to start with, and get Furor after you have mangle. Then fill out the rest of your feral points, saving enough points for 5 in naturalist from 61->66, then Omen of Clarity at 67, and intensity at 68, 69, 70.
