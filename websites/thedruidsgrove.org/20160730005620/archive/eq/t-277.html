If you dig around at allakhazam (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/) you'll get everything. Look at the quests in particular out of Skyshrine, Dragon Necropolis, Temple of Veeshan, Western Waste for dragon faction quests and Kael Drakkal for anti-dragon quests. The main dragon factions are Yelinak and Claws of Veeshan.

Kromzek faction, the giants without beards, are opposed to the dragons. Kromrif faction, the bearded giants are against the Coldain. Tormax the giant king has his own faction and his court are against both Yelinak the leader of the dragons and Dain Frostreaver, leader of the Coldain.
