What the stats do:

- stamina: increases your total health by 10 per point of stamina
- intellect: increases your total mana by 15 per point of intellect
- spirit: increases your mana regeneration while not casting by 0.56 every 5 seconds (0 while casting, unless you have the talent Intensity, then it is 0.08 every 5 seconds while casting)

Any stats on an item that begin with "use: " you have to click on the item in order to activate them.

So the first one will regenerate 0.56 mana every 5 seconds (b/c of the 1 spirit), and on activation of it will regenerate 250 mana in total over 15 seconds.
The second one will regenerate 1.68 mana every 5 seconds.

I'd go with the second one because I usually forget about my "use:"-items and therefor something that needs no using works better for me. But by the numbers, if you really use the first offhand every time the cooldown is up, it is better.
