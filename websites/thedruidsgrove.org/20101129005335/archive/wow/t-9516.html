I'm in an odd mood today. I felt like writing some poetry. Anyone else have something to share?

Senseless

He looks in your eyes,
frozen in each other's gaze,
He sees you, I can't.

The smell of your hair,
Your perfume for him alone,
He smells you, I can't.

Last he hears at night,
"sweet dreams darling, I love you"
He hear you, I can't.

The tase of your lips,
different flavored chapsticks,
He tastes you, I can't.

Caressing your face,
Buried in your silken hair,
He feels you, I can't.

Finishes your words,
knowing everything you are,
He knows you, I can't.

All the six of these,
You and he share together,
I am left senseless.
