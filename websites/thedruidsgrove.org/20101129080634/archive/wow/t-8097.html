After some research, I completed the quest last night. It's NOT the same as the Alliance side, although maybe you can do either/or?

Both halves of the medallion are inside black chests you have to open.

1. First half is located in the Barrens, at the bottom of the Sludge Fen - the pond northeast of the Crossroads. Really easy to get once you clear your way to the water, killing the mobs in your way.

2. The second half is on the coast off Silverpine Forest, at North Tide's, directly west of the beached shipwreck. You have to swim FAR and DEEP to get it, so stop at the bubble crevice to refresh your breath, then keep going west along the bottom of the ocean. Fatigue sets in before you reach the chest, so prepare to die if you're not fast enough. I died trying to swim back to shore once I got the medallion, but the important thing is I got it! :)

Seal form looks cool, and quite scary-looking!

I must say, this was the spookiest quest I have completed in my WoW career thus far. Mostly due to the swimming in deep, dark, open water. If you head west off the shipwreck, look for the jagged drop-off. It gets really deep after that. You're basically on the border of the Great Sea. If you head a little north from the jagged drop-off, you'll see some bubbles (AIR!) and the lockbox you're looking for. You should be able to do the whole thing without getting fatigued if you stay out of the Great Sea.

I was more frightened than my Tauren I think because of all the haunting years of Shark Week and watching them bait great whites with slabs of beef. I only saw a few sharks and waited bravely (thankfully I was underwater so wetting myself didn't do much) for them to pass.

If you're having trouble finding the shipwreck, head northerly from The Sepulcher and then west soon after you cross the little bridge, and over to the coast. Can't miss it. Thanks for the previous posts here, they were invaluable.

I never did find the people in Moonglade I was supposed to talk to for more information...
