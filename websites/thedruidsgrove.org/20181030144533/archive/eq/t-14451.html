What we gained from this spell.

1.95 second cast time reduction.

With a cast time of only 1.8 seconds, thats pretty much godly fast for druids. Its not meant to be used a lot or in place of Pure Life. Its an emergency fast heal when your tank, or caster, is going down fast and you need to drop *something* on them. Obviously, this probably isnt going to help much if you are the only healer in the group or something. But its meant to be an emergency patch heal while something larger gets put on.

In other words, its to be used in conjunction with our other heals and other healers. Its value is in its speed, not its efficency or hps healed. Its very situational and not meant to be used a lot.

That said, I like it. I'd like it even more, however, if it came with a seperate spell slot or was an AA or something...
