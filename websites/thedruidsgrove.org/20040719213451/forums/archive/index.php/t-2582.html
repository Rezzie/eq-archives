Level cap is 60. You'll be able to charm boars in the pens (diaku boar, war boar, and decorus boar) but you won't be able to charm anything in the pit.

You can check what a charm spell's max level is on lucy. Find the spell then click on "Raw Data". Look for the field "max1". I'm not sure why this info isn't put into the text--Charm(1/60) instead of Charm(1)...
