I have pride in my healing ability, but would never suggest I can heal as well as a cleric. I also can deal out damage in a nuker role as well, but I would never suggest I can out-damage a "real" nuker.

Where I think I have significant value, it is my ability to adjust to different circumstances. I can heal and DoT and Nuke all at the same time. My DoTs are varied, as are my Nukes, so in areas where one doesn't work well I can vary them "on the fly" and adjust to changes. In this respect, I think Druids excel over all classes, but that's just my opinion and I don't consider it worthwhile to engage in "mine's bigger than yours" debate.

In addition, my port abilities when done with a mission or raid or group are almost always useful. My buffs are always in much demand. And my evac and succor capability, together with my Spirit of Wood aa's have saved my group many, many times.

All told, I think Druids are the most fun to play since you can always vary things if you start to get jaded or bored. All that said, nothing I say is meant to detract or put down any other class. My most enjoyable times are when in a group and we enter a new zone and find that our standard modes of grouping are not working, and we adjust, change spells or our tactics to adjust to the new situation and overcome the difficulty. This requires creativity, concentration, and courage. Nothing is more enjoyable than overcoming a situation where you thought all was lost! And Druids are better at this than almost any class.
