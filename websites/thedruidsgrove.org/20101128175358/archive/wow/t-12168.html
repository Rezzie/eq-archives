For a druid, in the BGs, you'll become a squishy if you go cloth. I'd remain your normal spec and assist with crowd control, throughing a few heals here and there.

Selling your healing gear was a bad move, imo. But not to freight as you can do a different instant runs to regain some key peices. Mech, Bot and Arc all have some druid healing pieces you can get; boots/main hand in Mech, belt/offhand in Arc and shoulders/helm in Bot. I'm sure there's more, I just can't think of them off hand.

Look for Moonglade gear, that's our blue set in BC for healing.
