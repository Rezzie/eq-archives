Solo very doubtful.... Here are the things that will give you some trouble solo. Faydedar (soloable at 65 with PoTime/Qvic/Tacvi gear or 70 with grouped gear), Ulump (froggy in SonH, extremely resistant still was annoying to solo at 75 with CoA gear), Venril Sathir(soloed the spawned version several times, and you can probably find a partner to help seeing as he drops 2 pieces of the epic) and mabye the Jade Reaver(this part you can buy in baz though). If you can find a nice person on your server to help you out, you could knock out these four things, or three if you buy the reaver, in under an hour. But if you plan to level up to try to solo these things, the epic's power will be greatly diminished and only for getting epic 1.5/2.0 and a trophy. Around that level it is still extremely helpful as a free 1,650 damage per click.
