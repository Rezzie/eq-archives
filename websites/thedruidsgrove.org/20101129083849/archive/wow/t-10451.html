Easy way to do this quest is when you come to the first group of Firbogs take it slow and stay to one side of the path. By doing this you can draw them out one at a time or kill all of them on one side of the road and don't walk into the aggro range of the others. To get past the Warlocks do the same thing but stay off the path on the right side. When they spawn they're well out of aggro range and poof.. you're done the quest with time to spare.
