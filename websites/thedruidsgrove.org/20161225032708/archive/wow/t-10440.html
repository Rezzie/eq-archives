that's what I've been doing. Why use Moonfire at all?

Moonfire is a great and very usefull spell because it is a Damage over Time spell (DoT). Which means that once you cast it on someone, it will continue to hurt them for an additional 12 sec or something. This serves primarily to up your dps while you are throwing around wraths. It also is very handy incase, as niner said, you are fighting a humanoid, and they try to run. Your moonfire DoT will kill them before they get far enough to get some help. And all humanoids run.

Good combat strat for your level, if you like to cast:
Root, Faerie Fire, Moonfire, Wrath till dead, refreshing Moonfire when it no longer is 'ticking', or doing damage over time.

Good combat strat for your level, if you like to tank/bear:
Cast Regrowth and Rejuv on yourself before going to bear form, then just go pound away on them, mauling when you can. Casting both Heal over Time's on yourself makes you virtually invincible for about 12 seconds.
