I find the best instance for druids to farm Ancient Lichen is Steam Vaults. If you take the route from the entrance, across the large first room, then into the water (nearby the first boss). You can then swim right down the channel and come up again much later in the instance. From there go and sneak around the goblin boss picking the herbs there, then jump back down into the water and stealth back out again the way you came. Averages 4-6 lichens per reset plus some other herbs. Can get a stack in less than an hour :>
