[QUOTE]Originally posted by Stormlin
I was under the impression that haste stacked, up to a point. Including 1 spell haste, 1 song haste, and 1 item haste. Has this been changed or am I missing something here?

reply:

that was a mistaken assumption on many many peoples part for the longest time.

it was based on the fact, that you would get the Bard Icon/Spell to stack with the Chanter/Shaman haste spell.

However, only the HIGHEST haste percentage is used. Some bard hastes have other effects which still kick i
n, but only the highest Haste is counted.

Monk epic also gave this false impression by appearing to stack.
However, it didn't add any haste, just attack when used by a monk with Celerity (50)+ haste

So it really works like this.
1 Item Haste
1 Spell Haste (Bard/Chanter/Shaman/Bst/Clicky Item)
1 OVER Haste ((bards have a couple 50+ song lines that do stack
with all other hastes including thier own))
1 Quiver Haste (for bows only)

BTW more on Haste Stacking just as an example.

Vilia's Chorus of Celerity
Increase Attack Speed by 45%
http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1757&source=Live
is 45% standard haste in Slot 1

Celerity is
http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=171&source=Live
is up to 50% standard haste also in Slot 1

As such only Celerity's 50% kicks in because it is the same slot
But because bards are so broke, the icons will still stack.

Now something like Warsong of Vah Shir
http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2610&source=Live
Increase Haste v3 by 25%

its that V3 that makes it the 3rd type of stackable haste.
