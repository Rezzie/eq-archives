Aracnoid
Really now, if you're four below the FT cap and you still don't have IH4, I think you're doing your druid a disservice.

IH4 not only saves mana for you (in terms of healed HP-to-mana ratio) but just as importantly (or more important depending on who you talk to) it gives additional HP to the person you are healing. This additional HP goes a long way in helping clerics in raids where mobs have generally high dps. And even in xp groups, the added HP gives the tanks (and you) additional breathing room for extra damage.

Finally, even if you were a nuking druid, the fact that you're at FT11 means that its about time you give your healing self a boost.

Other than the above, you seem to be playing your druid very very well.

Incidentally, I'm a healing druid and i've got the gleamscale cape a long time ago.
