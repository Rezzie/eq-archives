1-7 your hometown/city or others, Field of bone is pretty good.
8-14 Crushbone
15-25 Paludal caverns ( To solo, snare yelloweyes and use him as a pet.)
26-30 HHK or dawnshroud peaks (HHK is deserted, but DSP is easy to get a group in usually.)
31-39 Dawnshroudpeaks or Echo caverns ( Both good for practicing root rot while exp'ing)
40-46 Echo caverns big side. (Prolly need a group, unless your hardcore root rotter.)
46-54 Plane of innovation (Will need a group, as some summon and not much room, if you want to solo, kite draniks scar)
55-62 Plane of valor, it will be slow :-( (Not much for a soloer at these levels, but you could try kiting the phlarg models in PoV while lfg.)
62-65 Bastion of Thunder or WoS. ( Soloing is kind of hard, but PoV should be okay.)
65-70 - ANYWHERE with decent exp. Muramite Proving Ground, Nobles Causeway, or soloing while LFG, any exp to help you get levels. Monster missions were nice for these levels.

Also, is the necro on a different account, at 46 take him kiting in nobles causeway and kill alot, levels will be fast.

PS: I just made this list off the list of where i leveled my last toon up, i'm sure there are better places to level, but those are okay.

Unrest is a great place to PL as a druid (since wolfy is non-KOS) but I'm not sure how well BEING PLed there would work. Iceclad ocean would be good. Throw a dot on a mob, hit it with the druid and FD...rinse and repeat. You get a nice XP bonus there, but I wouldn't head that way until you have your level 14 stinging swarm...the -100 magic resist should make it stick on most mobs.

Just make sure you get the FD off before the mob turns, or the necro will stay on the agro table (a well-timed root can solve this, but those mobs will be resisting that for quite a while)

Also, avoid the Eastern Waste side of the zone until you are a bit higher. The cougars near the tower of frozen shadow are easily pulled without all the wanderers that plague the EW side of the zone
