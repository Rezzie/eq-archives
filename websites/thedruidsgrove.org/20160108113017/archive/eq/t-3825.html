Made 350 AA this morning, and had points saved up so I stuck 15 in stamina. Below are the increase in HP per stamina point. I already had the HP booster AA (hell, I have pretty much everything at this point, really), so keep that in mind with regard to the numbers below. I had my cha outfit on, which allowed me to bracket 3000 hp nicely for the sake of comparison.

STA - HP

170 - 2951
172 - 2959
174 - 2966
176 - 2973
178 - 2980
180 - 2988
182 - 2994
184 - 3002
186 - 3008
188 - 3016
190 - 3023
192 - 3030
194 - 3037
196 - 3045
198 - 3051
200 - 3059

So, 15 AA in sta gets you 108 hp. How much would you pay for an item with 108 hp that didn't take up an equipment slot? Whether it's worth it to you depends on how many AA you have, of course.
