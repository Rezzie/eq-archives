It's about time we need a good guide from 1-65 from those who have no time to waste. I'll start off with some casual and most efficient loot/xp-wise places in Norrath/Luclin. Comments and additions are definitely needed.

1-10 - Yard trash in Misty Thicket, believe me...this is the best place for EXP especially with constant nuking at the Bat Hill in the second part of Misty.

10-14 - North Ro/South Ro kiting scarabs and what not can work, as well as Oasis since its mainly completely empty. Lavastorm workin with the elementals and what not works best because of the powerful nukes on Summoned.

14-24 - Quickest way to 24 is definitely in Paludal Caverns. You can kite/solo up to 17 if you want (killing the critters up the cavern past the bandit camps), or if you can get a group, get right in right away. Exp starts to slow at 20-24, but its better than most places if you have a constant exp group. Should take no more than 2 days of off and on EXPing (not full 24 days mind you). If you have KEI, this makes it 10 times faster.

24-34 - SK is an OK choice if you like to kite Gnolls especially with KEI, and LOIO is also good for soloing since it is completely empty nowadays. If you can, get a group in HighKeep...exp flies there just like Paludal. Once you hit 30, you can either goto Overthere, Dawnshroud, Marus Seru until 35 and group/solo (takes some time, but easy). Also, you can solo in Eastern Wastes from 30-34 if LOI/HighKeep doesn't work for you. If you're soloing in Eastern Wastes, go right from the Dragon Bridge and follow the right coast. Keep going until you hit the Coldain Camp, and if you want you can keep going until you see the lone tree. Don't use wolf form here whatever you do, oh and KEI is very useful here. Anyways, using tracking, try to pull singles of an Even or Blue cons for most efficiency. Completely avoid the Ulthork Warriors and try to pull regular Ulthorks and Walruses. The coldain camp is a great place to pull to, but they don't help...Try not to use AE nukes or anything. I found the quickest way was snare/nuke. You can also DOT since they rarely resist. KEI is extremely useful in both of these situations.

34-38 - I suggest quad-kiting the ulthorks/walruses in Eastern Wastes (2 bubs per quad normally), and cougars in Iceclad at the pirate camp if you like to solo. You can get tusks from the ulthorks and walruses which you can trade in at Cobalt Scar for a random jewel (you can even get BDs). The quickest way to get EXP grouping is probably in Dreadlands or even in Overthere still..not many choices for 34-38 really.

Note - KEI is a must if you really want to level as fast as possible and efficiently. Don't forget to practice spell casting.

Well Here is what I did. When I Started Kanus, I soloed, with no PL'ing at all, him from 1 - 55 in 1 month & that was with me getting my Epic during this time also. Then from 55 to 60 in 2 & a half weeks, could have been faster had I found the spot I hit 60 at faster. :)

1-12 (I think it was 12) Misty Thicket

12 - 15 Oasis

15 - 19 EK Killing Gnoll Reavers on the Reaver Hill. If you have done your Char right you should still be able to toe-to-toe these with Damage shield on.

19 - 22 Paludal Caverns

22 - 42 Dawnshroud Peaks. Start out pulling the 3 Mushrooms to the Right of the entrance of Sanctus Seru, & Pulling a Small or Regular Zelnik. Quad Kite those till they light blue, then Move up the Trail back tward Neth Lair. About half way between Sanctus & Neth you will run into alot of Regular & large Zelniaks. Quad those till the Large Zelniaks turn Light blue.

42 - 52 or 53 Quad Spirocs in Timorous Deep.

53 - 58 Quad Raptors in Timorous Deep.

58 - 60 Quad Coldian Expidition Guards in Great Divide. They are in the Mining Caves past the Giant Fort there Along the wall. If you have the mana you can try these guys at 55. There is a Trick to them though. Camo up & run down into the mines. There were will be 2 Guards at the bottom of the entrance just run past those for now. To the left there will be a room with Coldain Miners. Do not mess with those. Just follow the path to the back. You will pass a path to the right, Then another small room with 1 Guard & some miners in it on the left, Skip those. Ok Now you should come to a BIG Room with like 5 Miners, 1 Named Miner Dude, a Named Quard, & 3 Guards. Ok the entrance to the room where you come in at should have 2 Normal Coldain Expidition Guards. Ok Attack one of the guards by the tunnel that you came in & it should pull the other. Now as you get back to the front of the Tunnel where you past the First 2 Guards on your way in, Stop & look behind you to make sure the 2 you pulled are still there. Pathing is Screwy here & You have to keep them on if not you will lose them & have to Evac or zone to straiten it out. Now once you see the first 2 you pulled come, run up to the other 2 & just keep running back to the entrance. At the entrance stop & make sure they are coming. Once you see them RUN! Start Snaring them & you will have Plenty of kiting room out side. These guys hit hard & can take you down FAST if you are not carefull. Just learn the path & learn how to pull them & you will love it. I made 4% Per Quad at 58 & 2% per quad at 59 off them. I did this before PoP Came out. It took me longer to get from 53 - 58 than from 58 - 60. Took me a week & 1 day to get to 58 from 53 & only 2 days to hit 60. Then in 3 days I had Innate Runspeed 3, & Innate Metabolism 3 off the guards.

60 - 65 Hit the Planes.

I hope this helps you guys. Your Lucky though, I have NEVER told anyone outside my guild about DSP & the Guards in GD. Have fun & Happy Quading :)
