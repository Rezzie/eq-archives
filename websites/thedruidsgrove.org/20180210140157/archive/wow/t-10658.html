30/21 is an effective spec, its very popular. But in all honesty at level 22 its not something I would worry about yet. I think a lot of people get sucked into the specs early on and in the end it limits their game. Thing is, these specs are designed for a level 60. With that, its things that a level 60 would need. While leveling you may find yourself with other needs to fill, that are disregarded once you hit certain points in the game. This is due to many factors but largely gear, support talents with more points available and just the changing gaming environment. As you level you will be in many areas. Once you hit that 55ish mark you are going to start spending more time indoors, raiding or 5 manning the early late game instances so your needs will change. This particular spec, is a reflection of that need.

I guess that was a really long and drawn out way of saying don't worry. And there is a difference between an end game spec and a leveling spec.

Will this spec work as leveling? Yes and it would do pretty well. But its not going to see fruition til you hit 60. There are more effective methods of leveling.

And with that don't get sucked into this templat. What skills and spells are you using? Really what helps you? Not the things you use because you are speccing into that, what do you really find the most helpful? So..when you are leveling why not spec them to enhance the things you find useful. I only say this because you are asking about things before your level. Which is leading me to think you have not really taken into consideration the things you are really using NOW.

Just my opinion. Take if for what you will...

And if you want to know, I'd take
1) 5/5 Ferocity
2) 3/5 Feral Instict
3) 2/2 Brutal Impact
4) 2/2 Feline Swiftness
-You can have this all by level 21, basically giving you that little bit faster travel speed in cat form until you get travel form at level 30. Then I'd hit up:
5) 1/1 Nature's Grasp
6) 4/4 Improved Nature's Grasp
7) 5/5 Furor
8) 1/1 Feral Charge
-Now you are at level 32 and with this you will have a lot of utility for your level, from here there are many options available. If you like feral and want a good quick dps boost you could take Natural Weapons in balance, or maybe Natural Shifter to cut downtime while grinding your quests. You could go heavy into feral to get Heart of the Wild, but the talents along the way aren't as useful while leveling in my opinion while compared to other utility based talents. Another very good option would be to take up some more resto at this point like Nature's Focus and Imp. Healing Touch working your way up to pick up Nature's Swiftness by level 47.

Anyways, my point. Just do what you like while you are leveling. Explore your class. Don't worry about wasting points. If you are improving areas which you really use, its not a waste. When you hit that point around level 50-60 and you decide you have outgrown the need for some talents or you play style changes, then you can worry about respeccing. Its only 1 gold the first time, I guarantee you will have 1g. And at that point you will know your class, and will be able to make an educated plan to your spec, one that fits you among all the templats. You will understand why these templats exist and are so popular.

So just enjoy your class, worry about points later. Learn all you can while leveling and enjoy the now instead of focusing on what's going to happen at 60.

Just another thing to add...

If you are really set on going feral, I would highly encourage you to research warriors and rogue classes, since this is the area you will be emulating.
