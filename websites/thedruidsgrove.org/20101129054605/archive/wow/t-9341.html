Bear Form owns Mages. Trust me, it does. Catform does too, but against human mages hardly, but then again if you are horde, your chances of ambushing a human mage are much higher. Dueling a human mage as a Druid/Rogue is pointless, they know you are coming and use their racial to find you, if they are Gnome then they just AoE.

But, yeah, Bear Form definately has what it takes to topple mages. The trick is to get their timing down, don't use Bash to heal UNTIL they have used their Blink (otherwise they blink out of it). If/When they do Blink, use Feral Charge to get right back into their face. Not only do you need not worry about counter spell, but at the same time you aren't given them chance to use it when you do heal.

Like I said, catform still works best against Mages, but Bear Form is just as good, it just doesn't kill them as fast as Catform does, but it does give you an alternate play style against Mages.
