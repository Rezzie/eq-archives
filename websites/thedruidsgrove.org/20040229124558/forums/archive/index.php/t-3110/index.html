Palarran
The effect level for a worn effect determines what level to pretend the wearer is when applying the effect.

The effect Haste, for example, is:
1: &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp Increase Attack Speed by 2% (L1) to 50% (L49)

So if you wear an item with a worn effect of Haste and an effect level of 20, when calculating worn effects you are treated as if you're level 20 and so your haste is raised by 21% regardless of your actual level. The above line is where the "haste is effect level +1" comes from.
