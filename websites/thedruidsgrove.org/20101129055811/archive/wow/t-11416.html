What are your proffesions? I personally, picked up herbalism to go with enchanting after getting bored with leatherworking, just so I could farm for my epic mount easier.

In any case, what I do with herbalism is go to Un'Goro crater. There, I run around randomly and without ever having a certain direction. Here are what I look for in that place:


1: First and foremost, dreamfoil. Sells for alot. 265 herbalism required I think, somewhere around there.
2: Killing Stone Guardians. (The 60-61 elites roaming Un'Goro). They are very easy to find, as noone bothers to kill them, and most of them don't seem to be able to kill them solo, possibly simply because they believe they can't. But as a druid, especially with warlord gear, you should be able to kill them easy enough. The Stone Guardians drop nice loot, sometimes elemental earth, and with a 25% chance of Guardian Stones. Each stone sells for about 4g on my server.
3: If I am having problems finding any of the above two, I go to Fire Plume Ridge and kill the fire eles there for awhile for Essence/Heart of Fire.
4: And last, I pick up any other herbs I pass by.

Also, I'm not sure on this, but based on the fact that your server is a low-population server I'm guessing that things like Dark Iron Scraps still sell for alot on that server? On mine the price has lowered some albeit they're still worth a fair bit of cash. If the price for them is still high, go solo as much of BRD as you can. They often drop Dark Iron Scraps, and you make alot quickly by soloing.

Im a 300 Herbalist and all i did to finish my CC exaulted push was farm herbs to sell, then buy pages for turn in.

I focused on Winterspring and Felwood for the most part. Those two zones are very easy to travel back and forth to and in those two zones you can farn all the good stuff. Icecap, Mountain Silversage, Dreamfoil, Gromsblood, and even Black Lotus.

A couple other decent places for lesser herbs are Blasted Lands and Swamp of Sorrows. Between the two zones you can farm up alot of Blindweed, Sungrass, Gromsblood, and some Fadeleaf.

There is ALOT of gold to be made farming herbs.
