Only melee need FR at the baron, since all casters should be out of range from his fire waves. Also, we mostly heal the MT on this fight -- any melee dps that are stupid enough to not get out of his waves when they start (the first ones are a lot weaker than the final ones), can blame themselves. ^^

Basically, ignore everybody but the MT when healing on this fight, if you're struggling like you are. The rest can bandage. Also, if you've got problems keeping the MT up when the baron does his waves, yell at him to get out of range faster -- it sounds more like he needs to improve, not you. :)

I definitely wouldn't nerf my healing with FR gear in this fight -- it'd only make it harder, since the only fire damage you should be taking is when you're the bomb.

There are two places in MC where FR gear is good for a healing druid: the lava packs (between Garr and Sulfuron/Golemagg), and Ragnaros.
