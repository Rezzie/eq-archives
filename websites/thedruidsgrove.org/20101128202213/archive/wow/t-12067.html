In Cat Form around 100 hit rating are enough. I am usually between 90 and 100 and miss maybe two or three times per raid instance.

In Bear Form the same applies, but there is almost no good tanking gear with +hit rating on it. I do not have any hit rating in my tanking gear and still have no problems. For tanking gear, don't go out of your way to get it. Some items will help, however (Earthwarden, T5 Leggings, SSC Trash Pattern Boots, etc.).

For DPS, versus a boss, you'd need either 8.6% chance (135ish hit rating) or 6.00%-6.60% (I've seen it debated) chance (100ish hit rating) and 20 Feral Combat Skill. I have the latter approach in my DPS gear.
