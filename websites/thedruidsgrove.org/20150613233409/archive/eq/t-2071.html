Okay, I used to do this camp for people in my old, old, OLD guild. Gynok or his placeholders always spawn on teh bone at the zone line to West Commonlands. If you see any mob that is walking and has not been trained then that is a placeholder. Gynok ALWAYS spawns at least once ever 17 hours, I know this from over 27 camps of this mob, and at least 6 of those camps I was there for the full 17 hours. Each place holder spawns every 16 minutes (after the previous one is killed), will walk down to the third floor and back to zone line, stop for a minute then walk back to third floor, back up to zone line and de-spawn. the double walk takes about 26 minutes, so if you want a faster turn over in placeholders kill them.

Gynok can drop either the copper, gold or platinum ring. He can drop more than one ring at a time as well, but this is rare. The only good thing is that the plat is his common drop.

Good luck :)
