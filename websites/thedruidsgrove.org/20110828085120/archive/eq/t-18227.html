It's required to group, required to spam, and required that one of the people has a skill of at least 1 in goblin. You can teach to 1 point higher than the character spamming, so even if you box (which I've used to language up) you can just bounce +1 skill back and forth between two characters until both get 100. Zoning isn't required.
