From Lucy:

Nature's Beckon: mana 800, cast time 5, recast time 12, resist: magic, charm up to level 65, charm duration: 7.5 minutes

Command of Tunare: mana 420, cast time 8, recast time 24, resist: magic, charm up to 60, fizzle adjustment 5, charm duration: 20.5 minutes

***

There is no change in the resists, but level 65 animals probably have higher MR than level 60's. The good thing is the recast time is half what Command is, and has a lower fizzle rate, so you can recast it faster (17 vs 30) on resists/fizzles/wear-offs. Of course, to make up for that, the charm duration has been lowered.
