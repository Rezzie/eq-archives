TeriMoon
When I look at upgrades, I look at the combination of wis/mana/hps/resists in that order to try to decide what next. Mana and hps usually win out for me over most everything else. I don't know where you fight or what types of activities your guild does. But I suggest looking at your gear and trying first to upgrade slots that give you no additional mana, and when you are looking at upgrades, try to get ones that have added hps and/or resists or wis, whatever seems most important to you.
