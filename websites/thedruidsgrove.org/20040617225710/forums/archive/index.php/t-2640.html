<strong>The Basics</strong>

<em>Run Speed</em>

Pros: Great for indoor zones. Allows you to outrun most mobs even buffs drop. Absolutely necessary.

Cons: Nobody donates for SoW anymore ;)

<em>Regeneration</em>

Pros: Gives a small boost to HP recovery. Nice for kiting or taking AEs.

Cons: 3 HP/tick is not very noticable, especially for a class that can already regen/heal itself.

<em>Innate Wisdom</em>

Pros: A great way to boost Wis if you haven't hit the stat cap yet. Each point of Wis adds ~6 mana to your overall mana pool. Can allow some Wis gear to be traded out for Mana/HP/resist gear.

Cons: It's pretty easy to hit the initial Wis cap (255) with gear and can take awhile to farm enough AAs to open up a higher cap. C3/C4/C5 and stat food can also boost Wis a lot.

<em>Innate Metabolism</em>

Pros: Can save a lot of money over time if you carry around stat food. Especially nice if you spend a lot of time on horseback. Eliminates hassle.

Cons: Most stat foods are very easy to make or buy from the bazaar for reasonable prices.

<em>Tanaan Crafting Mastery</em>

Pros: An oft-overlooked basic, but the increasing benefits of tradeskills and availablilty of skillup paths make this a viable choice for the tradeskill-inclined.

Cons: If you don't like to tradeskill, this will be a complete waste of AAs.
