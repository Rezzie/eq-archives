The route I would take would depend greatly on the loot system and where I stood in it. If you're not near the top priority, best bet is to find the best upgrades you can in tacvi since tacvi gear will carry you a decent amount of time and generally isn't the most expensive stuff (assuming a dkp system) you can get. If you are near the top of the loot list then I would say make an alternate magelo with the few really nice pieces of gear that you want (i.e. Mask of the Ancients, Accursed Earhoop of Pain, etc...).

Clickies like Xxeric's Matted-Fur Mask and Earring of Pain Deliverance, are good ones to have.
