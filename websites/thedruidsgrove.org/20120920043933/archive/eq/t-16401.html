Nature's Guardian has a short range cast of 150 feet.... your must have a mob in target and you will summon a bear similar to pet spell but it will do about 300 max damage??? I cannot remember what every level of AA increases the damage by but the first level allows the bear to stay for 30 secs, 2nd 45 secs, 3rd 1 min, then every level after that raises attack power ... I think last level gives close to 800 max per hit for 1 min

Edit
The recast time of this is about 21-22 min and you can have it attack one mob unless u get another mob to hit u b4 the bear goes away, another note is that one hit to the bear makes it go away .... so if u hoping for it tank for u no luck there and be careful using in raids where the mobs do Wild Rampages
