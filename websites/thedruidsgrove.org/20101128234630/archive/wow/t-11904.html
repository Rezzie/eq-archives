In your place, I would go with Primal Fury before Shredding Attacks, because Shred is primarily a group ability--you're only going to use it as an opener after Pounce while soloing (though, granted, with Shredding Attacks you -might- be able to get two Shreds in before the stun wears off if you time it right), so you'll get more bang for your buck with extra combo points from Primal Fury.

My recommendation would be:

2/2 Primal Fury
3/3 Predatory Strikes
1/1 FFF
2/2 Savage Fury
2/2 Shredding Attacks

I think you could skip Shredding Attacks entirely if you have somewhere else you want to put the points. Again, this would ONLY apply to a build built around soloing. Shredding Attacks really shines when you're in a group.

Interested to hear what others might suggest.
