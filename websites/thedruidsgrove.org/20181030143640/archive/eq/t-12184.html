Those solo tasks don't give tokens, the faction hit occurs when you finish it. Destroy the silks (you can destroy as you loot them). There's 3 or 4 (I don't remember) different tasks available in that camp, 1 version of each for evil or good faction rewards, but iirc you can't do the one you just finished. The bit about tokens is that when you do a group/raid mission, you get a token at the end depending on whether it was a good or evil mission, and you can either turn it in for the appropriate faction hit, or destroy it if you're not on that side, to avoid penalizing players for helping friends with opposing faction missions.
