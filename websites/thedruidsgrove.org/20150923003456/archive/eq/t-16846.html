These are the missions I have completed and I believe are the only ones you can complete until the expansion comes out.

Available missions and info.

What Evil Lurks in the Heart of Gnolls?
Received from Fripp Darkpaw located in the South West part of the zone in a camp of gnolls that won't aggro you, say get supplies.
All you have to do is kill a Blackburrow Lurker until one drops a supply bag (Fits in bags) then turn it in to Fripp.

Man the Defenses
Received from Sergeant Bronal Cadran in the South East part of zone at some stone of a building being built, say useful.
Kill Magus Wentior that is south from Bronal, not snareable and summons then return to Bronal.

No Cure for Death
Received from Sergeant Bronal Cadran, say useful. (Must do Man the Defenses)
Go to Oceangreen Village and then head SW to a green/black spire (Zone in to Temple of Bert.) and kill 15 cultists (Kiteable).

Cure the Crud
Received from Kerrin the Unkempt located in the caves in the northern part of the zone (Go to where Surefall Glade will be in the present, then go past the
plants *They don't see undead invis* into the waterfall and make a left then follow the path until you reach water then go left and you will see some rock hanging down ahead of you where you will find her behind them), say what aid.
Forage 1 Oceangreen Grass, 1 Pinecone, 4 Maggots (Combine the 4 maggots first in the Stone Vial you get which is a container). Then when you have the first 2 pieces and the final combine of maggots combine again in the Stone Vial to get Vial of Curative Elixer. Now go back to the glade with the plants, take any of them down to 50% then use the elixer (must be real near) and then do it 5 more times. Finally go hail Kerrin.

Poach the Poachers
Received from Kerrin the Unkempt, say I can do this. (DO NOT have to have done Cure the Crud)
Kill 6 poachers in the zone, they are uncommon (a tracker would help). Hail Kerrin.

Stop the Spreading
Received from Kerrin the Unkempt, I cannot recall the phrase. (Must do Cure the Crud)
Kill 10 a blackbriar in the glade with plants.
Kill 10 a desiccated treeling in the glade with plants.
Kill 5 a tottering treant in the glade with plants.
Hail Kerrin.

Reducing the Risk
Received from Garvin Windrunner in the fort next to the entrance from Plane of Time and to The Void, say what test.
Kill 15 infected animals.
Hail Garvin.

Names for the Faceless
Received from Garvin Windrunner, say work. (Must do Reducing the Risk)
Kill undead mobs and retrieve Irregular's Signet Ring (CAN be prelooted) and deliver to Garvin.

Follow your Nose
Received from Jeneca Orlofen in the fort next to the entrance from Plane of Time and to The Void, say vengeance.
Kill Migglethorpe the Putremancer that is roaming south of the fort (goes from south of the fort to west up to the valley in front of Surefall Glade entrance), cannot be snared and summons.
Hail Jeneca.

Does this Look Infected?
Received from Tabron Caulria in the fort next to the entrance from Plane of Time and to The Void, say plague.
Loot 5 infected organs from animals with infected in its name. Then find a dead animal corpse (when you target it is dead and has corpse tagged at the end of the name) and use the open button, loot an undamaged organ *These pop from an infected animal, just leave them up and they will turn into a corpse*. Give them to Tabron.

Salving Grace
Received from Tabron Caulria, say plague. (Must do Does this Look Infected?)
Your receive a container, then find a mushroom that is no drop (can be dropped or pick up from the ground *looks like a mushroom on the ground with a tanish color*), then find a A snake's venom sack looted from a snake that is no drop, then find a fetid skin looted from a zombie type mob that is no drop, then find a skeleton's reanimating dust that is no drop from a skeleton. When done combine then find an infected animal and use it (it will die) but you must be near it. Hail Tabron.

Test the Waters
Received from Tabron Caulria, say plague. (Must do Salving Grace)
Go down to Oceangreen Village zone then head to the village that is at the South West part of the zone. Then say Tabron sent me to the Morris Olberson. You will receive a container with 4 vials. Now go the the ocean to the West of the village and click the vial that says Sea. Then go the pond East of the village and click the vial that says Pond. Then go to the Oceangreen Hills zone and go to the lake on the North West part of the zone and click the vial that says Lake. Then go to the glade with plants in the northern part of the zone and click the vial that says Vial. Deliver the vials to Tabron when done.

Familiarity is Key
Received from Captain Hiran Tillin in the fort next to the entrance from Plane of Time and to The Void, say work.
Head to Oceangreen Village zone to start finding locations.
Find "The Ruin", it is East of the village looking like a building being built.
Find "Pit One", It is the first pit of sand Southeast of the village.
Find "Pit Two", It is the second pit of sand Southeast of the village.
Find "The Square", middle of the village where the clock tower is.
Find "The Inn", the southwest building in the village.
Find "The Smithy", the northernmost building in the village.
Find "Southwest One", the southernmost building in the village.
Find "Southeast One", the southeast building in the village.
Find "Northwest One", the northwest building in the village.
Find "Northeast One", the second building north from the southeast building in the village.
Return to Captain Hiran Tillin.

Plague from Above
Received from Archon Darianna Althus in the fort next to the entrance from Plane of Time and to The Void, say willing to serve.
Destroy 3 of the catapult that are hanging around the fort, you can either kill the bombardiers, nuke from a far distance or pull them away wittingly and then destroy.
Return to Archon Darianna Althus.

Thinning the Rank
Received from Archon Darianna Althus, say other work.
Destroy 6 of those corpses of zombies you see around the fort, a few nukes will do it.
Kill 5 of the bombardier zombies that are around those catapults outside the fort.
Return to Archon Darianna Althus.

Stop the Contamination
Received from Captain Hiran Tillin, say work. (Must do Familiarity is Key) *Group Mission - YOU NEED A TRACKER*
Speak to the white robed NPC next to him to be transported to the village in an instance Oceangreen Village.
When your group is ready hail Billik and the event will begin. Mobs will begin to pop in 1-3 at random locations, usually in the shrub northwest of the village, over the ridge north of the village, northeast of the village, under the docks west of the village, behind the rock south of the village, and inside the buildings of the villages. Kill everything before anything is corruption (trackers get a failure in track window if this happens). Eventually the waves end and you get credit, then there is a non-interactive event. When that is done go hail Billik and then Captain to finish the mission.

The Dread Challenger
Received from Captain Hiran Tillin, say work. (Must do Stop the Contamination) *Group Mission - YOU NEED A TRACKER*
Speak to the white robed NPC next to him to be transported to the village in an instance Oceangreen Village.
You will be rushed by 9 mobs from the North that you must kill and also have a mob pop like it did in the last mission. If you don't kill the 9 you will have to distract the mobs that pop around the village as well as kill the 9 because you will have to deal with waves again like in Stop the Contamination, same spawn points. At some point in the waves you will get a named called Nacakehoth the Reviled (He popped behind the rock south of village) who at some point summon a group of mobs to help him on low health. After him a few more waves and you are done. Then you get again a non-interactive event. Then hail Billik to exit the mission and hail Captain to finish it.

If I find anymore I will post here but I'm fairly sure this are all of the missions from this zone that you can do for now.
