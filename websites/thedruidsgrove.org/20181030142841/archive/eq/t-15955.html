Here are some ideas:

Can solo in using snare/root/dot for decent EXp in:

Vxed
Steppes
Icefall Glacier
Arcstone

Good solo quests that I did at these levels:

Wanderlust stone
Collect pages from gnolls in steppes
Orcs ring quest in Eldarr forest (is hard to solo but I did - dangerous)
Artisans charm (collect the drops you need from TSS zones)
Bear hides for great wrist item in Icefall
Slipgear's gem in Corathus Creep
very easy cloak quest (turn in only) in Eldarr

Just a start - but lots of good stuff out there.

I know this is a late post and moreover you may have better now anyway but my 66 Druid solo'ed the 'Shopkeepers Delight' quest.

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=47410

Most mobs summon in Relic but the supply runners dont. I found that I could solo three of the four supply runner types. The little fire types messed me up.
Anyway I snare pulled to the zone with Mire thorns, zoned if I got adds, reapplied the longer snare and dot rooted them. It took some time, but I did it.
