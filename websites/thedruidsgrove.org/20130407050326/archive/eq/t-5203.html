Here's info on what drops in the revamped PoM. You decide if it was worth the wait.



Grand Cape of Fortune (http://pub57.ezboard.com/ftheplaneofmischieffrm10.showMessage?topicID=95.to pic) or Soft Leather Pants of Vitality (http://pub57.ezboard.com/ftheplaneofmischieffrm10.showMessage?topicID=100.t opic) drop from a buried treasure chest in the forest. You spawn the chest by turning in 4 Mischief Mushroom Spores to Nahas the Treasure Hunter also in the forest. Note that Nahas can also fail to spawn the chest for you.

Fetid Hide Boots (www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=62345) drops from a funky skunk, a blue-con named in the forest.

Fungus Covered Wristband (www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=62342) drops from an irritating mushroom, a blue-con named in the forest.

Amusing Bauble (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=62346) drops from a named bee in the forest. Name is something like Bzzibidana. Bee sees invis.

Cloak of the Jester (pub57.ezboard.com/ftheplaneofmischieffrm10.showMessage?topicID=94.to pic) drops from The Grand Gardener, a named mini in the garden where Ferjeneror used to be.

Robe of Dissolution (pub57.ezboard.com/ftheplaneofmischieffrm10.showMessage?topicID=99.to pic) drops from an ample ape in AiW 3's old Brenn and Grenn room.

Mask of the Performer (pub57.ezboard.com/ftheplaneofmischieffrm10.showMessage?topicID=101.t opic) drops from a non-named performer behind the stage.

The Bunny Behemoth reportedly drops similar junk in Stomples' old room.

There is also a ring event in the big chessboard room. No info on what drops there. No one seems interested in the event.

Bristlebane spawns in his throne room when you kill the Mischievous Jester. Again, few if any seem interested. No word on what drops.

Well, 12~ of my guildies and myself (Ele/vt equipped) went over to PoM and see what was up with this jester and Bristlebane.

We ported into jester and dropped that fool like he was a sack of potatoes. PoV mobs have put up more of a fight than him...

Anyways, I checked track, noticed BB was up - hurray! We ported into his hallway by clicking the SW painting, and began prepping. 5 minutes later we engaged and were just a bit surprised by some of his AE's/proc.

Here's the skinny: Proc is a 180% aggro reducer, with chromadic resist check - need to have MT with highest resists possible in everything. Bard is a must. The curse is cured with two casts of RCG. The ae, to put it bluntly, sucks. Illusion:Halfling, 80% snare and HP/Mana DoT. Like I said, sucks. Resists at -250 mr...

We went again, but with only two clerics, weren't able to keep it up long enough to put a serious dent in him. Got him down to 89% best try. I would estimate 200k hps+.

Might go back one of these days and finish him off with some more. In short, doable with VT/ele gear, but need at least 24/36 for a good shot at killing him.
