Epic Slot 4 Augment - Replace with Tipt Aug, VP Key Aug, or BiC Augment

Shield: Hanvar Shield, YL Shield, Kessdona Shield, Vish Shield, Emp Draygun Shield

Range: Jelvan Orb, Endurance Range, YL Range

Boots: Keep them if your planning on Last Blood, otherwise pick up Anguish Boots

Legs: Tunat Legs, Rikkukin Legs, Hatred Legs, Anguish Legs

Cloak: YL Cloak, Hanvar Cloak

Shoulders: Tacvi Shoulders, AMV Shoulders

Soul-Embuded Ring: Replace it with nearly single group ring for now. Upgrade to AMV or Ture Ring.

Bracers: Upgrade to Ashenback Bracer and Anguish Bracer

Tunic: Upgrade to Anguish Tunic

Gloves: Keep if your planning on last blood, otherwise upgrade to Shyra or Anguish gloves

Arms: Upgrade to Tacvi or Anguish Arms

Band of Dark Auras - Upgrade to Ture, Harrier or AMV Ring

Earrings: Jelvan Earring, Harrier Earring, Kessdona Earring

Helm: Anguish Helm

Necklace: Circle of Drakes neck.. I am sure ther eis a anguish neck item but the name escapes me.

All visable slots may also be upgraded with Katta Ally Armor.

All Augments can be upgraded to Anguish / Ashengate augments.
