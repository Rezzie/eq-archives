There are a series of quests for some nice No-Drop items in the Plane of Knowledge.

Check out these posts on "The Runes" PoK Quests (http://therunes.proboards6.com/index.cgi?board=popquest&action=display&num=1035395058) and feel free to correct or add the info here.

I've done all but two if the collections quests, and survived several trips to Droga both alone and with company between levels 5(druid) - 24 (ranger ) to get this:

Fine Cut, Diamond Inlaid Mask (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16394)
NO DROP/LORE/MAGIC
AC/STA/WIS/INT/SvM +3 HP/Mana +10
Focus Effect : Spell haste II

Weight: 0.6
All Races/Classes
Slot: Face



In my opinion some of the nicer and easiest to acquire items VI has offered in some time.
Each has a focus effect that effects spells of level 44 and under.
All, except the mask above, can be done at 1-65 because the quest items are purchaseable non-no-drop.
