Really easy actually

1) Mem the spell lineup you want
2) Right click on spell book in your spell bar, it will bring down some options such as save spell lineup, delete spell lineup...in your case choose save spell lineup.
3) A small window will pop up asking what you want to call that spell lineup. In my case I memed a soloing spell lineup. I simply named it "Solo lineup".
4) Now that you have named it, mem another spell lineup etc etc save it, name it and viola.

To bring up your spell lineup, simply right click on spell book icon in your spell bar and choose which lineup you saved. Hope that helps. Sounds confusing, but actually quite simple once ya get the hang of it. Good Luck!
