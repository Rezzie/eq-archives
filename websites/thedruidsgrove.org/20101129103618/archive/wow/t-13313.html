04-14-2009, 10:46 AM
I use:

+16 spellpower gem in red slots (Runed Scarlet Ruby)
+6 MP5 or +16 Spirit gem in Blue
+16 Crit gem in Yellow

I tend to use hybrid gems that include spellpower for the "non-red" slots. So for Blues I ussually use the purple +sp/stam, and for yellow I use Orange +SP/Crit.

Enchants:
Head = Wyrmrest Temple Rep Head enchant (+SP/MP5)
Shoulder = SoH rep or Inscrip (+SP/MP5)
Chest = (+8/10 Stats)
Bracer = +SP
Weapon = +SP
Legs = +SP/Spirit or the Crit one is nice too!
Boots = +Spirit
Gloves = +SP
