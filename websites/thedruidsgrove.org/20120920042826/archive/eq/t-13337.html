Unless my math is wrong, you will spend 63AAs to max out nuke crits vs. 53 to max out dot crits. When soloing, I always tend to lay down several dots and mix in some nukes here and there, so I tend to do more damage with dots (and use more mana). I saw a huge increase in DPS maxing out dot crits. Watching big (for us) nuke crits is cool and helps too, but you will get more bang for your buck going with the Critical Affliction line.
