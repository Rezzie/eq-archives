Ironically, it seems all of the + hit on good gear, is available only from T7.5 raiding. I wish I had a good explanation why.

Emblem gear is meant to suck. Blizz suggested that this would be the case, in order to leave room for growth with the release of new raid etc. They will be adding to the badge vendor loot slowly as they release new content (kind of like the Sunwell Isle badge vendor). Blizz made the mistake of making the first badge gear on par with end game raid gear, and that made end game raiding less important. Currently Naxx/Mag is the top raid, so badge gear is equal to enterance level gear for those raids (it is). The reason the gear seems so inferior atm is because Heroic Loot is on par with it. (as heroic loot is also set to be entry level raid gear for Naxx)

Take note of your recount data, I don't think we are currently missing as much as the EJ nerds have projected us to. My kitty has very low hit (I am Bear primarily) and unbuffed my kitty has like 110 hit or somthing. With buffs (Extreme Snapper + Flask of Accuracy = +85 hit, I am around 200. On the Heigan fight in Naxx-10, I missed .78% according to recount. That is less than the 1% that is standard with hit cap.

My point, just do your best with flasks and food and I think you will be ok.

Also, if you are seeing a large % of miss, you can re-gem to +hit. +16 hit gems are quite inexpensive.

FYI, With 1 Billion hit rating, you will still have a 1% chance to miss. You cannot gear to hit 100% of the time. There will always be at least a 1% chance to miss. Now, in practice, you may miss more/less than that, but the game mechanics are designed to cap out at 99% chance to hit. So you are looking at 3400 DPS x 1.005 (.5%) = 3417. So an increase of ~17 DPS. Any increase in DPS is a good thing, but weight the increase appropriatley.
