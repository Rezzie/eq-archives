To forage from a macro, you need to use the "doability" command. Check the abilities tab and note in which spot forage is (this is the tab with 6 buttons). With this info, your macro will be something like:

/sit off (makes you stand)
/doability 1 (forages)
/pause 2
/autoinventory
/sit on

I've found I need a slight pause between forage and autoinventory for it to work properly. Alternatively, if you don't need the auto sit/stand commands, you could fit multiple forages into one macro:

/pause 600, /doability 1
/pause 500
/pause 600, /doability 1
/pause 500
/doability 1

Then have another hotkey to autoinventory everything. /pause gets unreliable at high numbers - I've heard 600, 1000, etc, as the upper limit. 600 seems to work for me.
