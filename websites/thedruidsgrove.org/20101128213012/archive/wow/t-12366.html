Nice one with the plant parts :> If only I had had that kind of foresight..

Are you sure you have done all the CE quests?

There are some in hellfire peninsula, blade's edge mountains, netherstorm and terrokar forest too..

Also, you can gain rep by handing in Coilfang Armaments, but I wouldn't recommend this as they sell for loads of money - money you will need for Swift Flight Form (obtaining this should your overriding priority; screw earthwarden tbh).


I doubt you would have any problems persuading people to do Steam Vaults. It's a popular instance with players who are still too undergeared to field heroic instances with ease.


As for heroic mode...

Heroic Slave Pens is probably the easiest instance - in fact maybe the easiest heroic in the game. The hardest part of the instance is the trash leading up to the first boss, but with some deft sneakiness you can actually skip most of it. A shaman with tremor totem will help a lot with the fears. Be aware that you might be feared for a couple of seconds, and don't fight too close to other packs of mobs.
On the first boss, kill all totems except Fire Nova totems. When you see a corrupt fire nova totem, have your tank pull the boss away from it, then kill it from range. (it explodes for 6k aoe fire damage when destroyed)

Heroic Underbog is also very easy, no particular classes needed but an offhealer can be useful for the last boss, and an offtank for the 2nd last boss. (Feral druid!)

Heroic Steam Vaults is the hardest of the three. The trash leading up the the first boss is particularly challenging. However, after that the instance becomes a lot easier.
