Forgive the really n00b question.....is VoA and Eye the same thing ? as someone said we should try VoA 10 man but said you cant really plan as because you can only do it when you control WG ????

No, Eye of Eternity is the immediate follow on from Naxx and is above Nexus. As long as one of your group has Downed Sapphiron and has the key/quest from that boss you can do EoE.

VoA is an instance in the Fort doodah in Wintergrasp so you can only do it when your factions is in control of WG. Not run it for ages, but think there's now three bosses in there.


Edit : best place to find a really good tactics / video / map etc of where to flippin run to in order to avoid the flame wall things ? is there some secret to it ? do they come in a pattern and you simply need to remember it ?
It's pretty straight forward once you realise that there's only two gaps in the wall and hence only two areas that you need to learn to run to. If the wall spawns to the right of the boss then you need to stand more or less where the boss is. If it spawns to the left of the boss you need to run away from the boss towards the top end of the island.

Having someone call "Left" or "right" when the wall spawns and maybe putting a flare or two down to mark where you need to run to can be helpful for the first couple of runs.
