Quadding in EW, as has already been suggested, is probably the best bet.

Quadding Dire Wolves in GD is also very doable (I've been doing this recently with my "baby" Druid, who is 39). I'd stay away from the Giants, though - too many HPs.

Zelniaks in Dawnshroud also works, but Velious is better XP. Spectres in Oasis are also pretty good XP but they're very hard to quad effectively, mainly due to other players rather than the mobs themselves.

That's my take on it, at least.

Accretion
I went from 38-42 mostly quadding Dire Wolves in Iceclad. It's rarely camped and has a couple of "safe" icebergs where you can med up between quads. With a KEI and Kitty Krack you should be able to pull at least 8-10 at a time and the xp should go pretty fast. Respawns are pretty quick and you shouldn't have any trouble finding plenty to kill.

They start to go light blue at 42, I believe, but are still pretty good xp until 43, at which point you can group with your BST.
