Just for reference:

(and these are all slot 7 augs available pre-70 from DoN vendors)

You can get a total of 6 FT augs, for a total of FT12.
You can get augs with SH5, ER5, BA5, and ExtEnh5 foci. And a shield with MP4.
You can get 6 stat augs totalling +80 to each of the 7 main stats.
You can get 8 resist augs totalling +84 to each of the 5 resists.
You can get 4 augs with 10 AC each.

There are lots of great augs to get from DoN to fill out your kit. Just pick the ones that interest you the most. If you click on the three profiles below, you will see most, if not all, of the augs mentioned above ;)

NOTE: For LORE augs (FT, stat, resist) that can go in (almost) any slot, you can get both the good side and bad side augs, even if they have the same name. Even with the same name, they have different IDs in the DB, and do not count as duplicates.
