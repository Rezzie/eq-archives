Sorry, I can't be more helpful, but there are not really any solid base lines for a bear for heroics or 10-man at them moment.

The overall impact of the patch is hard to understand, at least for me, at this point. It seems to have hit the high end tanks much harder than the entry level tanks, so If we assume the standards haven't changed since 3.1 (not a great assumption), you should be shooting for:

Entry Level heroics (Nx, UK, VH, CoH)
25k Health (shifted)
20k Armor+ (shifted)
25-30% dodge (shifted)

Harder Heroics:
30k+ Health (shifted)
25k+ Armor (shifted)
30%-40% dodge (shifted)

10 mans need about the same as the harder heroics. A good goal to shoot for is all i187+ gear before 10 manning, and all i200+ stuff for 25 man.

I strongly reccomend avoiding Uldaur all together until you are in mostly i213. (Blizzard claims i200 is adeqate for ulduar10, but I think they have over tuned the content abit at the moment for that)
