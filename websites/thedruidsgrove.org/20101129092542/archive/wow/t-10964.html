You can DE to 40 or 50 skill.

As Swear said, you can Enchant the same piece over and over. Beyond that the piece doesn't have to be green. I'd just buy a vendor/white lowbie bracer/chest/shield etc.

I did a mix of buying/farming. Part of the Auctioneer package is Enchantrix (I think, if not it's it own addon) that lets you know the % chance of what an item will DE too. Some items sell on the AH for more then it'll DE into... so sell it and buy mats. If you don't want to get an addon, a good rule of thumb is alway sell cloth "of the Eagle" or leather "of the Monkey".
