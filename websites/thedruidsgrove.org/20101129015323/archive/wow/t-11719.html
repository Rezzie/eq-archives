If you're using the Blizzard Downloader (which is a lot better now than it used to be) make sure you have your firewalls disabled or follow the guide on the WoW FAQ to open up some ports.

I usually tend to manually download the patches.
Here's the link: http://www.blizzard.com/support/wow/?id=aww01810p

Once you download it, make sure you place it in your C:/Program Files/World of Warcraft directory and double click to patch it.

Good luck!.
