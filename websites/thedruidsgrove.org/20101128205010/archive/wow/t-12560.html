Idol of the Terror (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=33509) (mitigation idol -- some benefit for threat via improved crits while activated)

Idol of Ursac (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=27744) (threat idol -- Many feel it is worthless. For single target scenarios, it does improve threat more than the first two; however, with a single target, threat isn't typically an issue after the first few seconds.)

The idol of terror gives your Mangle ability an 85% chance to grant you 65 agility for 10 seconds. The idol has a hidden internal 10 second cooldown which is only triggered upon a successful proc. It works for both Bear and Cat form Mangles.

Idol of Terror is available from G'eras in central Shattrath City, priced at 20 x [Badge of Justice].
