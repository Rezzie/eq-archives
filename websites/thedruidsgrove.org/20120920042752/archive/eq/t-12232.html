Trial of Subversion requires you to successfully sense the trap, can sometimes take more than 1 cast if the RNG fails you or you are too far away. After sensing the trap, you have to disarm the trap. After you disarm the trap, you have to use the 3rd spell to unlock the chest. Then, you can /open the chest like normal and loot it.

If any step in that process fails, you should repeat it until you get a success. And yes, remember to bring some greater scryer stones with you.
