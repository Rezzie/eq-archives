Stupid Tat tricks:

This goes WAY back, probably fall of 2000. I had just started playing EQ, and was probably in my teens at the time. There was a BotB server qualifier happening at the Arena one evening, so I decided to go see a new zone, and check out the action. Nobody was there yet when I arrived, so I climbed up to the top of one of those giant spires that was there. Then I set my camera angle down towards the arena floor, and started reading a book, occasionally checking the screen to see when the action started.

Well, quite a while went by, and I was seeing plenty of zone chat, but no action. More time goes by, and still no action. So I type an ooc asking when it would start, and several people state that it's almost over. WTH?!!?!

So I float down, and see all kinds of toons around, and then I realize what happened. I had a lame-o PC that required lots of options turned off/down to play well. One of these was.. the clip plane :o So I couldn't see any people in the arena because I was too high up off the ground, LOL.
