I don't want to play my cleric, though.
Everquest right now is full of folks who are going to give you lots of advice on what you should do to get to 75, because that's where the action is at, in the so-called Endgame. I have to say that, except for raiding (which also has it's bad side) I didn't get much more enjoyment grinding AA's at 70 and 75, than I did soloing levels at 35, or grouping for xp at 60.

I suggest you just play your druid, and enjoy playing EQ. You can solo effectively up to level 60 Quad kiting or Root Rotting,. With the new expansions, like TSS, you can actually solo all the way up to 75, rooting and dotting, or kiting singles. It just takes time, since the quad kite kind of dies at that point. It's up to you to figure out what your time is worth to you.

If the Endgame is where you want to be, then group up and grind xp. But as a cleric or a druid, I think you understand that healers are seen by others as a necessary evil. They need one to keep the tank up so that others can do the real work of killing the mob. It's not the job of a star, because no one is going to be wow'd by your ability to heal without going OOM.

There are quiet a few threads talking about soloing, I usually link them, but the search feature works well now. Search for Timorous Deep for one that I've posted on before.

As far as gear, I don't know how much 60K gets in the bazaar right now, but DON radiant crystals used to be cheap, and there are probably a few upgrades in the DON camps that you can grab up.

Good luck.:)

60k will go a long way in the bazaar if you spend it wisely. The only thing to take care of is required levels on items. Most of what I name below will work for the cleric but not the druid.

What you need to look for is high quality/high-supply items - they end up being priced well below their real/objective value.

The easiest way to do this is use the bazaar search window - look for items between say 0-5000p, and sort by mana. You'll find every now and then there are some exceptional value items.

Try Direwrought Leather Sleeves. They may be in lower supply with people having moved out of Direwind, but these were selling for 100p on CT for a while. An Iceborn Diamond Necklace from Icefall still goes cheaply on CT, also.

Good luck :)
