Neither A nor B is correct the way you wrote them. When it says 'increases armor by 360%' it means it adds 360% to your base, so the multiplier is 4.6. Also, you will always at the very least have a small contribution from Agility, so your entire base will never be multiplied.

http://wow.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=10366&page=2

Thick hides will change the multiplier on the armor value listed for an item from 4.6 to 5.06, not 4.7. (base*1.1*4.6)

Caster/Cat AC = (Sum.of.armor * 1.1) + 2 * Agility + Enchants/Kits/SetBonuses

Bear/oomkin AC = ((Sum.of.armor * 1.1) * 4.6) + 2 * Agility + Enchants/Kits/SetBonuses

If you had 3,000 armor as a caster and 10,000 armor as a bear, which seems way out of proportion, you'd have 10,900 armor as a bear with thickhides.
