I have found a great website that lists all the new zone collection quests, details new zone progression which unlocks sellable gear around the 400+ hp/mana range from zeb, most of the faction vendors and what they have for sale at what faction levels, and more.

http://home.comcast.net/~mathadon/Guides/SoD/index.html

For Progression, look under the void tab in the top left corner, it also tells what zeb has for sale at the different levels of the void. You can then click the progression link under each zone tab on the left hand margin to get information on progression for each particular time theme.

For collection quests, click the appropriate tab on the left, these include a solo'able 32 ac, 75 hp/mana, 10 all saves aug and a 20 ac, 140 hp/mana aug that i dont know if its solo'able, among others.

For the faction merchants in the different zones, click on the zone tabs and click the faction tabs.
