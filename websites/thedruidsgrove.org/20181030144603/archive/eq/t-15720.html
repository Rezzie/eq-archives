I did this quest inside of 10 minutes. Its very simple. This quest is supposed to be completely soloable - I just find this way a lot easier. I want you to read the write up to get a basic idea on how to get the quest. This all takes place at zone in - there will be no adds.


http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3939


You can only have 1 quested person at a time get a shield. Oddly - if more then 1 person has this quest in your group and someone fails - you all fail. No matter - you can just get it again.


Once you have the quest - Wraithguard Hunter Grethmar (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=24161) will spawn right outside of Frostcrypt. When you hail him he will poof. You then zone into Frostcrypt.

Set up 2 hotkeys in /say ... STOP and MOVE - Only the quested person who hailed Grethmar - can control Grethmar.

When you zone in you will see a few guards and him at the zone in. Target Grethmar. Spam STOP until Warlord Vongar, the Relentless (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=24160) spawns. This can take a few minutes... Take 1 or 2 people with you to assist Grethmar until Warlord Vongar, the Relentless (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=24160) is dead. The person doing the quest always keeps Grethmar targeted, let your friends beat the warlord down. He is not hard and never summoned the 3 times I did the quest. I like to use casters - They can just cast away from a distance. Melee classes will steal argo from Warlord - so avoid using them unless you have a cleric with you - I am not sure if Warlord is slowable - never had a slow class with me. Someone might want to confirm this...
