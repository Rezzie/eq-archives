Laeg,

I will share the "Secrets of the Spiroc/Lumi Masters" with you.

Normally when you quad, you tried to target the one in the middle, right?? So you got all 4 hits.

Don't for Spirocs, target the one closest to you(ie the one that just hit ya). Don't worry about missing the straggler just yet, it will soon bunch up.

You will get hit, but you have cloro up, right? Just say you are still working on Defense;)

Alternately getting hit a couple of times, actually bunches up the stragglers.

Only 3 can hit you at a time, the others will just start to walk away. They don't hit that hard really.

Face them when you cast, Provens BS for 300ish. Use your keyboard to turn, not mouselook, it will save you interrupts in addition. BTW, I use first person view, the other school on this says to use 3rd person.

If ensnare wears off, the spirocs will usually(if you are out of casting range) just morph back to spawn spot. Finish off the ones still chasing ya, lev, camo, and go get the one that gated on you asap, it will heal itself.
