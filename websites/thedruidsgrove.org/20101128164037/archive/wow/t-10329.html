FULL QUEST LINE (Alliance)

Posted in Test server forums,

This is the order in which you do tier.5, i am going off memory so i may forget somethings.
also this is alliance version (sorry hordies)

First you go to IF and talk to the human in the throne room, she sends you to winterspring to gather blood samples off bears and cats ( the brown bears didn't seem to drop for me) when you return to IF you get the bracer.

(this part is a bit fuzzy as i did it really early in the morning, i know a lot of people have done this part so someone can correct me if i am wrong)

She sends you to gadgetzan to talk to a goblin, he is in the same room as the master goblin engineer, he sends you to gather ashes off the ground in burning stepes, when you return w/ the ashes he takes a few of the items from your bag (that bliz gave you) and sends you to gather ecroplas, from ghosts in (silithus, EPL and winterspring)

tip* while in winterspring, go to darkwisper gorge and get the rod that costs 50g off the imp in the first cave...you will need it


After you gather the ectoplasma return to goblin, he sends you to kill a named magma elemental in steppes when you return he sends you to get the rod in winterspring (but you should already have that) he sends you back to IF to talk to the human in throne room, she gives you belt/gloves and sends you to talk to a ghost outside strath w/ the ghost revealer you made.

The ghost outside strath is the ghost that gives you the 45 min baron run quest (this is the blocking point of the quest) get a 5man group and try your best...

After the 45 min baron run you return to the ghost and he sends you to DM library. the librarian in DM gives you a quest to gather ogre warbeads off the ogres in DM and BRS. we did not do the ones in BRS, the ogres outside DM north have a very low droprate 1:30 or so, the ogres inside DM north are about 1:5.
once you have your 25 beads you return to the librarian, he sends you to gather an essence off a ghost in DM (plus other items bliz gave you) the essence is a group drop and you do not need the quest to have it drop, everyone in our party got it and 2 did not have quest. when you return to the librarian he gives you a banar to take to BRD...

In BRD you need to clear to the arena, once inside you drop the banner as a challenge, you fight ba wave of nasties (normal arena thing)
then out of the second door you have a pvp type fight.. the group was a warrior, 2 rogues and 2 mages, they are immune to taunt the act smarter then regular mobs.
once you beat them you get a BOP blue from a chest, (this event is also farmable as you keep the banner)

And now you return to the ghost outside strath, he sends you to Ironforge to get your legs, shoulders boots.

The chick in IF sends you to BRS to talk to a ghost (he is on the ledge near BRS entrence outside the instance)the ghost gives you a RAID quest to slay incendrius (now immune to stuns) emberseer in UBRS (the fire elemental with the 8 orcs keeping him restrained) and duke of flame in silithus. then you return to ghost.

The ghost sends you to do a random task based off your class. as a warrior i was sent to silithus to gather the druids remains off a silithid was a 59 elite bug ( all the quests were at least duoable if not soloable)

Once you get your item you return to ghost and he sends you to an instance to summon a boss based off your previous quest. mine was in LBRS in voones room, after voon is dead you summon the boss (in this case a tauren druid) once you slay the druid everyone on the quest gets the quest drop, even if they did a different prequest.

Then you return to the ghost who sends you to gather blood kelp, it drops off the nagas in duskwallow marsh (59-60 elites) also ground spawns on the island w/ the nagas common drop.

When you return to the ghost you get another random class quest mine was to kill giants in winterspring, (paly friend had the same one as my first one) after we were both done we went back and did voones boss again.

Then you return to ghost and wants you to gather bracers off orcs in BRS, the orcs in UBRS had a much higher drop rate than the LBRS ones.

You turn in your bracers (from the orcs) and flask that blizzard gave you and now summon and fight lord valethak (in the beasts room)
*Raid encounter*
