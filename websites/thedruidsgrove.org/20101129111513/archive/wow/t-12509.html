I evaluate gear based solely on my mana regen performance in raids. As long as you can finish every fight in your raids with a little mana left over, stack +heal as much as you can.

If you find yourself running out of mana, add spirit/intelligence or pure mp5 to get to the point that you don't run out of mana, then go back to +heal.

Keep in mind that when I say "mana left over" I mean after using pots on every cooldown and your innervate on yourself.

Recently I noticed that I always ended boss fights at 30% or more mana left, so I regemmed all of my +spirit and +mp5 gems to +heal gems. Unfortunately, I still end fights with 30% mana left 8/
