Funny how my first level 70 5man tank job came after this patch. Had no problems holding aggro in Shadow Labs except when the Overseers would fear and I would lose complete aggro.

It's just a temporary loss of aggro since it is considered an immobilizing effect ( like being netted or frost nova'd, though we get out of these ). When the fear wore off you may have had to get 1 hit in, but should have been able to re-establish aggro with minimal effort.

I've run a number of 5 mans with druid tanks ( heroics, and this weekend I think a druid will be MT in Kara ) and really have no issues with them holding aggro at all. I prefer having a druid tank over a warrior in multi-mob pulls as the druids tend to know how to gather them up and keep them under control better than more warriors than not ( or perhaps that is just b/c we feel we have more to prove ).
