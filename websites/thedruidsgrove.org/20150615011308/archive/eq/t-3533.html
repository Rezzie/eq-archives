Tatankawd
05-10-2002, 03:08 PM
Elan,

They did raise the cap, but at half the rate.

So at level 60, 1 WIS = 6 mana for WIS over 200, compared to 1 WIS = 12 mana for WIS up to 200.

So, assuming level 60, and assuming all WIS added by items is between 201 and 255 (in other words, equipping items doesn't put you over 255, unequipping doesn't put you below 201):

18 WIS = 108 mana

9 WIS + 40 mana = 94 mana

The differnce at level 60 is 14 mana, less than that at lower levels (only 5 mana difference at level 50). 14 mana is pretty much meaningless, at higher levels, mana regen is MUCH more important than total mana.
