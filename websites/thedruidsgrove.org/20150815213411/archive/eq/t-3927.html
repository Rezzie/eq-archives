Othmire Prexus Totem
Magic, Lore
Range, Primary, Secondary slots
AC 5, Sta +20, Wis +18, Cha -8
Wt. 0.8
All/All

Give Bloogy Shellcrusher (an otter) in CS the following:

Sea Dragon Meat (drop off Kelorek'Dar, the sea dragon in CS, or Faydedar in OT)
2 Saltwater Seaweed (fished up in OOT) - unstacked
2 Fish Eggs (create in tackle box by combining skinning knife and Thunder Salmon, fished up in the Karanas) - these need to be stacked

The faction hits are + Othmir, - Ulthork.

(Note. Some people dispute whether 2 Fish Eggs are required, and say they have completed it with one. )
