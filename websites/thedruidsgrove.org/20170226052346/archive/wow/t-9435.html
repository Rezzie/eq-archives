Depending on who you talk to, and which guides you read you will get a different answer on weapon skill. Some say it uses your weapon skill the same way you would in caster, but you cannot gain skill points so it is better off using the weapon you have the highest skill in. Others say that in forms you are considered at max weapon skill for your level and will always act accordingly so you should use the best weapon you have even if you haven't trained up your skill yet. Its your choice which to believe. Personally I follow the latter school of thinking, as I've noticed the same thing you have. That super cool 2hander I just got, but have only 5 skill in misses constantly, but when I change forms there are no problems hitting unarmed.

On a side note, it would take care of this discussion completely if we just had our own weapon type while in forms, claws or something that skilled up only while in weapon form. You would then know exactly what the skill is.
