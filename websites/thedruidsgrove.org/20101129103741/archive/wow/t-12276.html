Ya, get to the BG's -

You will discover that level 70 PvP is a whole different ballgame (two level 70's toe to toe with different sets of gear? - good stuff.) And if you start now, you will probably have accumulated a decent amount of honor points and marks to start getting some of that nice gear.

Mind you, if PvP is not your cup of tea, then you don't HAVE to do it. I've found that it IS possible to get bored during the game and a good bout of PvP can cure that. Not to mention how cool it feels the first time you take down 2 opponents at once. (self-healing WILL be involved - hee hee)

Run 5 mans whenever you can. Rep is important. And there are a gazillion keys out there to get. You will want those later.
