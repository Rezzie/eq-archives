Yes.

Its used when you pull aggro, or are offtanking something for a few seconds becuase aggro isn't on the tank yet.

It reduces all incoming melee by 75 percent (making a 1k hit 250) and has a chance to proc a effect which lowers your hate on that mob by 400.

The spell will last 18 seconds, or until its mitigated 5000 points of damage.

The Recycle time is 15 minutes.

-Fenier

I keep this spell constantly memmed and hotkeyed. It's just like our own version of Divine Aura in that the 1 second it takes to cast can save you.
