Since the gear sets aren't the only druid gear in Anguish:

Keldovan the Harrier:
-Stud of Chilling Precision (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32588)
--Fire focus is <3... that is all. ^.^
-Infernal Staff of Fiery Fate (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33684)
--Not really a great druid item unless you like the stats
-Mask of Crackling Energy (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32920)
--Again, not the best druid item, but if you like the stats go for it
-Rigid Ring of Prowess (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=36031)
--The last dru usable item on Keldovan, and the trend continues... good for stats, not so great for focii


Jelvan Event
-Bazu Claw Hammer (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33346)
--Just like the staff from keldo... not worth it unless you need a secondary with solid stats and some useful effects
-Earring of Dark Conflict (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33347)
--Very nice earring with a great focus... effects aren't the best, but can be made up for with other gear
-Globe of Voltage (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33746)
--Only thing wrong with this item is a lack of focus... still a nice piece of gear though

Ture
-Aegis of the Dragorn Elders (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33012)
--Nice shield if you use magic based nukes a lot, solid stats and good effects
-Belt of Contempt (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32972)
--Good stats, not horrible effect... utterly worthless focus for druids =/
-Hanvar's Hoop (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33024)
--Fun clicky if you're a collector or into AC, not-so-great effects... probably designed as a melee ear
-Ring of Disdain (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32562)
--Great ring for stats and effects, a focus would make this one nigh irreplaceable for a good long while

Warden Hanvar
-Belt of the Stagnant (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33071)
--Not a common focus in my experience, and pretty decent effects and good stats make this one the 2nd best belt in anguish for druids imo.
-Cloak of Regretful Transgressions (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33091)
--Good cape as far as stats and effects go, but Yar'lir back slot would be a better pick imo.
-Shield of the Planar Assassin (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33070)
--I like the graphic on it, but that's just me. Would be a decent shield for druids that like the melee/porcupine mobs. Prolly won't be a favored shield for druids unless you really want it for the effects and pretty decent stats.
-Totem of the Chimera (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=32971)
--Seems to be a favored item amond some casters I know. Good stats and decent effects, would be a lot better with a focus

Arch Magus Vangl
-Amice of Ill-Will (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=35007)
--Nice focus, good boost to avoidance if you're not capped, good stats.. most of the druids in guild want one but he stopped dropping it for us.
-Mask of Forbidden Rites (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33405)
--Not a horrible mask as far as stats go, lacks on the focus and effects though
-Ring of Deterrance (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33404)
--Great ring all around, well worth getting
-Warbeads of the Arch Magus (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33406)
--Another really good item for focus and hp.. nice if you need shielding too to take the edge off those 1k hits ><

Overlord Mata Muram
-Chains of Anguish (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=35795)
--Great focus, awesome effects and really nice stats... best belt for druids in anguish imo
-Earring of the Dragonkin (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=36687)
--Very nice earring if only for the clicky. Best mana regen clicky in the game that's been found so far with nice stats to boost.
-Plagueborn Cape (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=37838)
--If you want a back slot from Anguish this would be the one to get!
-Ring of Persecution (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=35158)
--Good focus, pretty useful effects, good stats... solid ring and worth getting
