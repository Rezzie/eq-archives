well first off let me say that i find someone coming here and posting and just saying "hey tell me how to do this" kind of crappy.
Most of us, who have played for 2+ years figued out this for ourselves. Or had a more experienced druid to fall back on.
+ this is a subject on any druid discussion you go look at.

That being said, cause i am a nice guy

34- 40 Iceclad Ocean and Eastern Waste. Cougars, Ulthorks.

42- 52 ish. Tim deep, Spirocs

52-56 Cobalt scar, CoV faction required. Wyverns. Pull them down to beach and kill.

56-60 Tim Deep, Raptors.

HOW TO QUAD KITE.

Find a mob that you want to kill in a outdoor area.

Make sure you always have Sow or SoE up at all times.

Load your quadding spell, Some variation at all times of lighting blast.

Snare the mob in a outdoor area with little to no "roamers"

Go get another mob
Repeat.

When you get 4, Run in a big Circle till it looks like they are all on top of each other.

Run to maxx casting range." For me i count to 8 seconds"

NUKE

Run 8 more seconds.

NUKE.

Repeat till dead.

Sit and med when LOM, With Kei this is not a problem.

Thats about it, basic strategy, that i use.
