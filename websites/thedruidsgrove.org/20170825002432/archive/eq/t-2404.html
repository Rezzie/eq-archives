With 199 wis i think you definitely need to do elysians at least until you get the tunic, crown, and legs. You should get some other pieces by then also. The horse is really key for elysians as with rotting anything. Because at 55 elysians will break root constantly, i would also suggest getting the warden symbol of tunare if you can. With it, you can chain cast root while still getting med benefits on the horse. After that, I would suggest raptors at 55 and 56. All are still blue at 55 and only a few go lightblue at 56. You can either charm them (disposable pets) or quad them.
Most of the explanations of charm tactics i've read are for 58+ charming (ie after TR). For raptors, the only thing you need to do is snare, charm, set them on other raptors, and break charm with camouflage when your pet is low on hp to kill it. The same would apply to ravenous nighstalker in pon also i would imagine. The key is that you need a steady stream of charmable animals without any that are too high for your charm spell. This makes blood ravens in pon and rats in pod impractical. I think it would also make ravenous beasts in ME a bad idea. Once you get TR at 58 you can start keeping your pets around effectively and then can start looking at pets that might be too high for charm.
The other spot i used when raptors was taken was toilers, but with only 199 wis i doubt you could finish a quad. With 255 wis at level 57, toilers become a better option. By that time, a good number of the raptors are lightblue.

01-29-2003, 07:53 AM
Port to Dawnshroud Peaks. Go east a short distance to the zone wall, then head south for another short run. There will be a tunnel in the zone wall that leads to Maiden's Eye.
From the zonein to maiden, camo and run east. There will be a large stone enclosure with xi's (guys in black robes). Go around it and don't get too close. Look out for wandering xi's nearby, and con them to make sure they don't see invis. Go east until you hit the zone wall, then follow it south. There will be another cave in the wall, and this one leads to umbral. At the entrance to the cave will be 3 xi's. If any of these see invis (check by conning) you may have to be adventurous. Otherwise, run right past them. If one does see invis, you can try to run past anyway, but some of the xi's are sk's and will harmtouch you, which would be bad. Otherwise, you can try to pull the one out that sees invis and kite it around, but the xi's cast and the nearby area is usually filled with unpleasant things.
Once you zone into umbral, make sure you remain camouflaged, and turn right when you leave the cave at zonein. Follow this wall around until you get to a temple with stone guardians outside it. Skirt around this temple (the guardians don't see invis) and stick to the wall afterwards. The only things that see invis along this path are dark stalkers (more guys in robes) and they only rarely see invis. Once you pass the temple and follow the zone wall, you will reach a corner shortly. This is the safe valley to which you will pull elysians.

www.eqmaps.com is a good place for maps to find general directions.
