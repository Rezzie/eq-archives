I use a UI called ArkiveUI. Contained within it are two addons (amongst countless others) - Mapster (a map replacement) and Gathermate (has a database of known herb/mining locations and shows them on both the map and the mini map). They work well and make gathering a breeze. One other addon I use for gathering is called Routes - it allows you to create the best route for visiting all these herb or mining nodes and shows it both on the map and mini map (lines joining all the nodes). These three addons make gathering a breeze and can all be downloaded individually. This doesn't help with your current problem but is at least one possible solution.
