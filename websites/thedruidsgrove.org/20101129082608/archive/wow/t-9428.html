You have to pick up the quest again from Dendrite Starblaze (http://thottbot.com/c11802). The NPC is at 56,30 in Moonglade (upstairs in the building).

If you have abandoned the quest and you cannot get it again from this NPC, try going to the original quest giver. For Alliance that is Mathrengyl Bearwalker (http://thottbot.com/c4217) (34,8 in Darnassus), for Horde it is Turak Runetotem (http://thottbot.com/c3033) (76,28 in Thunder Bluff).

If you cannot get the quest there either, there may be a bug and you have to write a GM ticket to get it resolved.
