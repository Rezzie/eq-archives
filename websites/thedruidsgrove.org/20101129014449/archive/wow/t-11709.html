With 2.0 the WDB folder moved, it is now in ( for example )
C:\Program Files\World Of Warcraft\Cache\

Just delete the entire WDB folder and see if that works for you.

Another way to find your video card:
- Right click on any empty space on your desktop
- Choose "Properties"
- Go the "Settings" tab
Under "Display:" it should list your video card. If you are having problems figuring out what type you have just post what is displayed there and we can help you further.
