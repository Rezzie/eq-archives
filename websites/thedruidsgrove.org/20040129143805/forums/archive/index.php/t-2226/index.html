Purestone
The quest is for the signet of grisk (check my Goal profile in my magelo at the bottom for stats). You turn it into Taxonomist Darwin Grisk in West Freeport. First of all you need the regular luggald flesh which drops in Dulak's Harbour (i think!) fairly often. Collect 3 of the flesh.

You talk to the taxonomist guy and he gives you a box to combine them in, you hand in the combined box back, and get nothing. However, when you then prompt him again, he gives you another box, you then need to collect Fecund Luggald flesh from the Crypt of Nadrox. This flesh is slightly rarer. You combine 3 of them in the box and hand it back to the taxonomist.

After doing this, you get nothing, but he asks you to seek out a dark elf in Tox. You find Assistant T`os who, on hailing, summons a skeleton you must kill. I haven't gotten to this part yet, since I need one more Fecund Luggald flesh which I am too lazy to get atm. However, I believe the skeleton requires the assistance of a group. Loot the "cursed heart" from the skele's corpse and run over to WFP again and hand it to the Taxonomist, and he will give you the signet. (or a melee item if you are a melee)
