Expertise is about twice as good as hit on a per-point basis until you get enough expertise to remove 6% of parries and dodges. Then it becomes about as good as hit is. The only exception is that hit improves your taunt's resist chance. Expertise is the best stat to improve your threat on a per-point basis. It's also one of the best ways to improve your survivability, thanks to removing parries from the boss's table.
