Depends. You could try Redridge first, and then hit Duskwood once you're into level 20. Ashenvale works around the lower 20s until about level 30. Wetlands could work as well, but that's level 20ish as well. I'd say Redridge and then Duskwood or Wetlands or Ashenvale. You might be better off with Duskwood after Redridge.

Generally, it'll go like this, I think (I haven't actually played a gnome or dwarf yet):
Humans: Elwynn, Westfall, Redridge, Duskwood
Night Elves: Teldrassil, Darkshore, Ashenvale
Dwarves/Gnomes: Dun Morogh, Loch Modan, Wetlands

Problem with the human lands is that Westfall ends at a point that you should ideally go to Redridge. Loch Modan and Darkshore would be bad as you'd have to do the lesser quests to get the better ones, and by that point you're better off going to Ashenvale and Wetlands. Or so I think, at least.

Er, long explanation short: Just go to Redridge. :P
