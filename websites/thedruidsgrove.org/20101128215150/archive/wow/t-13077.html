I would definatley but 2 in Imow if you are going to be spending a lot of time solo DPS.

I would also strive for ILoP too. This sounds silly, but you will crit so much that you will only need to pop out and top off when you pulled too much. (Can now bandage while shifted, so I do that more than healing myself)

As for the shapeshifter...that is up to you. Its really more of a playstyle than anything else. I leveled all the way to 80 as kitty, and almost never shifted in combat, nor sat to drink because of low mana. That being said I was before and am now back to being a full time Bear. Kitty isn't my specialty.

I would dump some points in R&T to pickup IlotP and Imangle. R&T is really only viable in groups. By the time you get debuffs up and target stunned for positioning in order to shred, it will be dead unless you are in an instance.
