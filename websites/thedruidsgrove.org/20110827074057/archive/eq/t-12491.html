Mental Clarity AA (Archetype) is 1 mana regen per level.

Base FT (worn on gear/tribute) is 1 per point up to 15.

In OoW, they added an AA, Expansive Mind, that allows you to raise the worn FT cap by 1 point per level with 5 levels taking the total worn/tribute FT to 20.

It is confusing sometimes how people list their FT as different numbers based on whether they include the MC aa.
