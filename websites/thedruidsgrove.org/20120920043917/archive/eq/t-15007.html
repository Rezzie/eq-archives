You need to do all 22 missions, hard or normal doesn't matter as long as your flagged for having done the mission on either setting.
You need to do 22 missions for the mask. However, you can get a clicky that increases one resist by doing 4 or 5 missions. Then you hand in all 5 clickies to get the mask.

You need to do 22 missions for the mask. However, you can get a clicky that increases one resist by doing 4 or 5 missions. Then you hand in all 5 clickies to get the mask.

Fenier is correct that you can do the missions on either normal or hard and still get the clickies / mask.

You can also re-do the missions after receiving your mask to re-obtain the individual resist clickies (aka do the whole quest twice) and not do the final turn in. I am not sure if this has been adjusted but the individual clickies and the "all resist" clicky did stack.
