Burn it down to the point where he is really low health, below 20% for example. Deal with adds accordingly. After he's belowe 20 or 10%, start clicking your stone. You do NOT have to kill him, simply "sleep" him with your stone that only works with him on low health.

Believe the way the script works is at 80 60 40 and 20% he switches off his mental defenses or the other emote and you click the rune on him x times, spawning an add ea. time (at this pt you want all melee off of him -put them on the adds- and until you figure out what x is probably have the tank off attack as well), until he gets knocked out.

He'll stop attacking for a while (at this pt consider him mezzed, if someone hits him he'll wake up early and the script won't advance) then give an emote about waking up... tank re-engages as he comes live and you repeat the above for the next increment of 20% iirc.

At 20% or a bit under he stays asleep / depops when you mez him again and a chest pops.

Kinda a bunk raid to do though, it's not needed for the clicky (only the first 4 tasks for that one) and unless it got changed all you get from it is a discordant stone that doesn't even have the $$ anguish focuses =/
