Taldiwen
No, it makes perfect sense. Disease-based dots add disease counters. Poison-based dots add poison counters. So, a curse-based dot adds *gasp* curse counters! If one has the appropriate cure spell (RGC, abolish poison, etc) then you can remove the effect before its full duration has elapsed...the number of casts of said cure spell will depend on the number of counters added by the particular malady.

Just because our swarm-based and fire-based dots don't add counters doesn't mean that ALL dots behave this way. And I guess technically even those dots sort of do in a way since they can be "cured" with cancel-magic series spells.
