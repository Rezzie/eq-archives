Well aside from the zones being packed a few good spots i found so far:

Gray Legion dude to the right of zone in - Direwind
Griffons at Druid Rings in Direwind - Or altenatively the gnolls just around the corner
Bears in Direwind, around the corner from the "Direwind Current" marked on map

Mammoths/Kobolds near the Wiz spires in Icefall - Note that Mammoths have around 100k hp, but are white/yellow to 75 so yield more exp.

They're the only places i've tried so far - Exp ranged from 10% - 18% per kill with a 90aa/10normal exp split.

I never tested the pathing of a quad in Icefall, so can't give you a definite answer - but if the pathing is ok, the spiders -among other mobs in the zone- would be quadable.

Another soloable zone is Valdeholm. You can kite the blobs and spiders by frostcrypt ent - however, be careful pulling anything past FC ent, because most of that summons. The tigers on the hill summon as well. Mammoths in the zone are soloable as well, but I've never kited them without the giants (who are not soloable) in front of them cleared out. The mammoths here run extremely slow. All mobs in Vald have a LOT of hp and a small XP hit, but a chance at high level spells.
