Quote:

With ft15, kei, self mana regen, potg and mental clarity, mana shouldn't be that big of a deal.


Hmm...that is the kind of thinking I worry about a little. I worry Verant thinks about it this way too.

There are broadly speaking two very different problems going on at the same time. 1) Casters don't get the same benefit from new gear and equipment that tanks do (this is the big raid guild problem). This is what the Ancient spells and some of the higher, harder to get Focus Items are for. 2) Casters are not in balances with melee overall in the game (and not in balance with each other). This second is a problem that affects most caster classes at most levels (certainly post 50).

If we approach the second problem assuming that everyone has FT15, KEI, all levels of mental clarity, etc then we will not really be balancing casters. We will be tuning the casters in about one or two guilds per server. I think that perhaps 3 to 5 druids on my server have FT15. I am certainly not one of them.

Admitedly any changes made to balance casters must take into consideration the theoretical maximum benefits, but I really don't think that the appropriate platform for caster balancing should start with the maximum equipped character profiles. They have to look more at Joe Average, but be careful not to improve things for casters in such a way that the best equipped casters in the game become overpowered.
