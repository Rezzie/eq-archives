These are the 3 debuffs I cast on just about every boss mob:

Hand of Ro
7: Decrease Fire Resist by 72
10: Decrease ATK by 100
12: Decrease AC by 15

Skin to Flora Rk. II
1: Increase Incoming Spell Damage by 9%
2: Limit: Max Level(85) (lose 10% per level over cap)
3: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)
4: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
5: Limit: Resist(Fire allowed)
6: Limit: Combat Skills Not Allowed
7: Unknown #367 (100/25/0)

Blanched Frost Rk. II
5: Decrease ATK by 136
6: Decrease AC by 36

Which gives a total debuff of:
-72 Fire Resist
-236 Attack
-51 AC
+9% damage from Fire nukes

I keep these spells in my raid lineup all the time so that I don't have to flip back and forth between spell sets, and they're the most effective ones for accomplishing my goals - I basically want to make the mob not hit as hard / often, and raise both melee and caster DPS.
