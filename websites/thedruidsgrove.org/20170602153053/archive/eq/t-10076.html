Con Ella Foodcrafter in Misty. If she doesn't like you, the problem is your Guardians of the Vale faction (you killed too many moss snakes). Guardians of the Vale faction can be fixed with the bandage quest (Yay, one of the two cities where you can buy faction). If Ella likes you, the problem is your Storm Reapers faction (you been killing treants in SK or NK ?). The easiest way to fix Storm Reapers faction is the "exterminate the vermin" quest in RV (have fun killing lots of level 1 mobs). If your problem is Storm Reapers faction it may be faster to kill bandits in WK until the druid trainers in Surefall like you. Note that if you have been killing treants you may be KoS in Surefall.
