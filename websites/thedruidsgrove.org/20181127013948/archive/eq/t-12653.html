Mission selection is tied to faction level.

Doing Progression missions unlocks progression single group missions, progression raid encounters, and All the Dragon Encounters.

The progression missions you may be able to three box. The Progression raids go 24, 42, or 54 people.

Missions reward crystals, tho there are random items which can drop from trash in missions. I have yet to ever see a item from a named, reguardless of mission or area.

A checklist is always handy:

Go to the Good Camp in Lava- hail chieftain, get flag

[ ] Do the 3 tasks you get
[ ] --- Kill 11 laborers (Goblins - Stillmoon)
[ ] --- Loot 5 boiling bloods (Spiders -Lavaspinners)
[ ] --- Loot 5 teeth (Goblins - Tirranun's)

[ ] "Do the ""Children of Grimblax"" mission - hail chieftan, get flag, Gift of the Keepers AA (+10 to all stats, not resists), 2 aa points"

[ ] Get Amiable faction by doing more missions (I hear its 35 faction hits to amiable)

[ ] Do the next 3 tasks
[ ] --- Loot 7 sandy bloods (Goblins - Stillmoon)
[ ] --- Loot 8 shredded maps (Cats - Ascent)
[ ] --- Kill 9 kirins (Kirin - Ascent)

[ ] "Do the ""Sickness of Spirit"" mission"

[ ] "Do the ""Calling Emoush"" raid"

[ ] Turn in Glowing Rock Fragment from Emoush to the Chieftain. - get flag, Valor of the Keepers AA (~+300hps/mana), 2 aa points, 69 Spell / Disc.

[ ] Get Kindly Faction by doing more missions
[ ] ---Loot 5 Purified Nest Water (Goblins - Stillmoon)
[ ] ---Kill 10 Snowfoot Goblins (Snowfoot Goblins, very top of the Ascent)
[ ] ---Collect the Scrolls from Thundercrest (Senties Drop Scrolls)

[ ] Complete Trial of Perservance Mini Raid (Stillmoon 24 Man Event)

[ ] Complete Volkaras Bite (Lavaspinner's 42 Man Event)

[ ] Complete History of the Isle (Thundercrest Isle, 1 Group Mission)

[ ] Get Warmly Faction

[ ] Complete Guardian of the Sands (Stillmoon Temple, 42 Man Event), Loot Quintessence of Sand

[ ] Complete Goblin Dojo (Thundercrest Isles, 24 Man Event) Meditation stone

[ ] Give Prrevious two Items to the Chief

[ ] Complete A End to the Storms (Thundercrest Isles, 54 Man Event) Loot Yar'Lirs Fang

[ ] Complete: Origins of the Curse (The Nest, Single Group)

[ ] Complete: The Curse of Ju'rek (The Nest, 54 Man Event)
[ ] Reply With Quote
