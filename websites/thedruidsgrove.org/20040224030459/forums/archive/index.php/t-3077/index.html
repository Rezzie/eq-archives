Look for a Bellows Crafted Cap. The WIS isn't higher, but I recommend it because of the raw HP and mana benefit. They drop like candy in Plane of Innovation, so they're usually really cheap in the Bazaar.

It does have a "Recommended level 50" on it, but you're only one level away anyway. :p

Edit: There's also the Elysian Crown, it drops in Umbral Plains off "disturbed elysian remains." It has more WIS than both the Othmir and Bellows caps. You can solo them in a few levels, but usually Druids will shout if something dropped that they don't need. Elysian armor rocks.
