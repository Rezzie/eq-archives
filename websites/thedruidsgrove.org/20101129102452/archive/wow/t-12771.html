Hi Druid,

It does not, I'm afraid. Instead, druids get a trinket (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=32481) as a reward from one of the final quests (http://www.wowhead.com/?quest=10992) in the Swift Flight Form quest chain.
To start the quest chain you need 5000g to purchase your swift flying mount skill from the trainer NPC in Shadowmoon Valley.

Once you have that, you can begin the questline by speaking to Loganaar (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=12042), our dear trainer in Moonglade, who first taught us the way of the drood. :>
