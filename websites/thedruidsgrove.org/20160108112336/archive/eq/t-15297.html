In Torg Mines at the "bridge room" there are trolls which should be white/yellow at L51. Maybe slightly red. All single pulls, nothing there sees invis, easy for root rot or kiting as it's a large room. Not far from the Nadox zone line if you need it. It's about 12 mobs or so with a 20ish minute respawn time so you should have enough there to kill.

Nothing special in the cash department but the AA exp is decent.
