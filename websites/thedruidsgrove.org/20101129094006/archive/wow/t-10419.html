You didn't mention the level of your alt.

Either way I wouldn't recoment either EPL or Winterspring. Truesilver is a rare spawn for Mithril OR Rich Thorium Veins (I'm not sure if lesser Thor veins can spawn TS). In EPL/WS it's going to be Thorium if it's not True Silver and you won't be able to mine that at all.

Depending on your level, I'd recomend Un'goro, Fellwood, Badlands. They'll have plenty of Mithril, even tho it's green it'll still skill you up... and the occasional True Silver.

You could always get the mining enchant on your gloves, and if your alt can wear mail and is an engineer, you can make the miner's helm... the two bonuses will make Thorium aviable that much sooner.
