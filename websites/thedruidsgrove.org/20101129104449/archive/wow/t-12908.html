Another thing you have to consider is that even though the numbers maybe bigger/smaller, the spell coefficients have changed as well. So, you may have +600 heal on your druid and +1200 heal on your pally, but their effective healing maybe be identical (assuming similar level gear).

In short, it is now even more difficult to compare +heal and +spell between classes any more. Futhermore, you can't compare pre-3.0 +heal to 3.0 +heal due to the major coefficient changes.
