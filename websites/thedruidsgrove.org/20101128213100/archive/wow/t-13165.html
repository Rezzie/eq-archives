The best crafted blues you are going to find are the "Eviserate" set of items. They are lvl 78 rares, crafted by your friendly LW. They are actually PvP items, but have many of the good tanking stats you will need too. Cost: ~10 heavy Borean, and an eternal or two. (Depending which piece)

You could easily tank entry level heroics in that set once its enchanted. Keep your eye out for the Trollwoven Shoulders on the AH too! They can be pricey, but you never know when a LW is going to need cash fast. They are the best shoulders pre-Naxx-25 atm (better than T7).
Goose
01-20-2009, 08:05 AM
If you're looking for cheap, then most of the quest blues are actually good enough to start tanking heroics. Of course if you're just starting to tank heroics, stick to UK, nexus and COT (nontimed runz) to start with. If you're gonna farm up an item, don't be a cheap ass and get the trollwoven set, it takes like a day to farm up the eternals yourself, i sold extra eternals to pay for the leather i couldn't skin myself. Trollwoven are top tier till you get to naxx, and are maybe top 3 in game so its worth the *same* amount of time to farm up the epics as it is to farm up the blues.
