<img src="http://www.eqmaps.com/m3/povalor3.gif"/>

ww = west wall (also left castle)
ew = east wall (also right castle)
bc = back caves (also inner caves) <--best exp
fc = front caves (also outer caves)
ne = northeast (along zone edge, NE corner of zone)

you might also see groups at the AD door, whatever that's called on your server - though groups at WW, EW, NE, and the door make killing in the desert a bit slow~


There are also a lot of mobs noone ever seems to kill. They aren't as good exp as the above camps, but are certainly viable. The big human-looking soldiers of Marr are tough but rewarding. There are also unaggro frogs and larvae. And don't forget the undead.. This is all still quite fun and all still better exp than tier 1 mobs~
