kineada
Only three parts of the epic can't be solo'd by a nekkid druid ... Frog, leezard and dragon. Pretty much everything else is soloable.

When I did my epic, I used the bind camp method. Not the zek version of bind camping ;)

Bind in everfrost (or hunt ice giants for cash) and track for the tainted mammy. Hell, massacre mammies and sell their tusks for 7pp each! Bind in rathe mnts (or hunt hill giants for cash) and keep track up for the tainted hill giant. For the non-cash generating camps, just bind there and gate over before logging off. Hit track, if tainted isn't up, log out. Log back on the next day and hit track. If tainted still isn't up, gate elsewhere and hunt.

Can do the same with elysian armour.

After getting the mammy and hill giant, you should have enough farmed cash to get Xanthe and Solstice earrings. You said you had some friends in Torv so you'll easily be able to get frog, VSR and dragon for your epic. Hell, make them help with seahorse as well and maybe farm phinny for bard/rogue/mage/wizzy epics.

Help a friend with their epic piece and you can pretty much guarantee help with yours.

Heh ... I realize that druids are a bit timid about soloing summoners. But with Hand and Illumination low 50's summoners aren't all that tough.

Just load up magic nukes and scoriae (fast cast) as well as chloroblast and nature's infusion.

Although not solo nazi solo, getting symbol, WoG, brells, focus and KEI in PoK before heading off to OOT or LFay would certainly help.

Porcupine old world summoners. They don't hit especially hard with ATK debuffs. Snare them, root them, DoT them (Fire DoTs then swarm), then nuke the bejeezus out of them. Heal as required.

The tainted mobs don't summon so root rot until they are in flee mode. snare them then med up to full mana. Kill and enage the corrupted.
