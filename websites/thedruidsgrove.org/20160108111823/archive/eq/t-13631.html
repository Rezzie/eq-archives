For some time, my primary soloing spot has been the static Accursed Nest. The mobs at the top of the ramp are always yellow cons, and none put up much of a fight. There's a huge area to kite, but be warned if you're going to bring mobs down the ramp towards the zone-in. I generally snare pull and walk the mobs down the ramp, hugging the ground as I go. Put too much distance between you and the mob, or too much variance in the Z-axis and rest assured the mob WILL warp onto your head. Slow controlled pull ftw ~ Beware the chanter mobs and cleric mobs will gate on occasion, just kill them close to spawn point to avoid that mess.

Oh ... there's also loads of tunnels to kite through up the ramp beyond the door. Invis vs Undead potions fool most of the mobs, although some do see through everything. In the tunnels you'll find a mix of yellow white blue cons, not everything is yellow beyond the door. Nothing summons except the named, might want to avoid those solo, ouchie.
