11-01-2006, 09:00 AM
If you are feral with HotW, most people prefer strength, especially if it is straight AP (rather than feral AP) since rogues will get just as much benefit from straight AP while HotW gives druids 2.4 AP per point of str. Its a scaling thing, if you want to try to do competive dps against rogues, ferals need much more AP than them, and generally HotW is how they do it.

One of the big concerns for itemization in the expansion is so far we have seen many upgrades to +AP weapons, but not many with significantly higher str.

If you do not have HotW, I don't suppose it makes a difference.
