01-04-2005, 11:15 PM
There are no "complete" heals. Clerics can heal up to 7500 with "Complete Healing". Druids can heal up to 2925 (Tunare's Renewal / level 58) or 4680 (Karana's Renewal / level 64), up to a maximum of 75% of the target's hit points. The druid percentage limitation is only relevant if the target has less than 3,900 hp (for Tunare's Renewal) or 6,240 hp (for Karana's Renewal), almost never the case today.

Therefore both clerics and druids have a "big 10 second heal," it's just that clerics can heal more in one shot and the cleric spell is much more mana efficient.
