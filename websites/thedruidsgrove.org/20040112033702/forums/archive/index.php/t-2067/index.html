<blockquote>Nothar says:<hr>As far as i know, there is no way to split the King/PH and the 2 other Shrooms beside him on the platform with him by a Druid.[/quote]It's actually pretty easy to pull the king without his guards if you're level 60+, because the king's reaction radius is much bigger than an average myconid. I have never tried to kill him solo as a druid, but I pulled him several times in Kunark at level 60.

Approaching from the ilis jail side, kill the two myconids on the left. Now as I recall there are either one or two roamers that walk by the king spot. Wait until one of the roamers is close to the platform, within the king's reaction radius, but far enough so you don't aggro the other two, and pull, the king will drop down and come at you with the roamer. If you're unlucky the myconids will path and bring another roamer or two, but at least you won't get the two myconids at the enterance (which you just killed). If the frog roamer was a shaman, you might want to pull with dispel because the roamer might have haste on him.

If the exit room doesn't have a priest/adept myconid up, you can kill near the enterance there in the hallway, since you've killed the frog roamer. If you're lucky, the myconid roamer you pulled is also a low level warrior and you can root it outside of melee range, so it serves its purpose without hurting you. Keep stuns up if it's a priest.

Now, Hand of Ro and nuke the bejesus out of the king! You want to nuke him down to 30-40% health as fast as possible, then he starts wasting time trying to heal himself and you're home free. Keep your back to a wall so you get interrupted the least.

This is how I did it many times in Kunark in small groups, and while pulls can easily go wrong, it is the easiest way to kill with minimum crowd control and the least worries about myconids CHing through walls.
