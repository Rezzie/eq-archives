BriennaMonk
Before I quit, I did a lot of charm soloing. This is going from memory here, so hope I remember right :)

Tunare's request lasts longer, but is limited to a lower level of animal max than CoK.

So you can get a higher level pet with CoK than with TR, but TR is less unpredictable.

As for Charisma, there are people who swear that it helps. There are others who swear just as vehemently that it doesn't help. Personally, what I found most helpful was AC/HP gear. That way when your charm broke (and it most definitely will), you can take a few hits. This is especially important in PoP zones where the mobs can hit very very hard. Of course the harder they hit, the better pets they make :)

It's ok to sacrifice wisdom for ac and hitpoints when charm soloing. If you do it right and are careful, you don't use much mana anyway.
Mendan
Tunare's Request is a piece of trash. Max pet level is 35 (which makes for a useless pet at 55), it costs 130 more mana than CoK, and takes 3 more seconds to cast. It's higher level than CoK because the pet won't aggro when charm breaks, but if you can't handle a charm break you really shouldn't be charming. To charm after level 51 you really need CoK or CoT, and as for charisma it does make a difference but it's so small I don't bother trading out gear to boost it.
