IF you watch the zone emotes, there is a "Changing of the guard" you have to watch your timing to avoid this event for your particular area or you get lots - o - mobs. At the changing of the guard for your area, the Hero Shrine Guardians can respawn even if out of time/sequence.

Audo
Have fought a bit in the area you are talking about. The person above is correct. When you see the zone shout "changing of the guards" 2 hero shrine guardians will come to "relieve" the 2 already posted.

So if the 2 Hero Guardians are spawned and the 2 are on the way to relieve them, you have the potential for quite a few mobs. And the Lieutenants (sp?) are roamers, expect to see them at any time. (also - the Liue's give 3% aa per, rather then 2% in my time there, so if things are clear pull em they are not difficult)

You can avoid aggro on the wanderers by staying in the corner there right accross from the 2 Hero Guardians.

Anyone can single pull the Hero's or the mobs in the halls in the shrine itself with harmony.

Hope that helps a bit
