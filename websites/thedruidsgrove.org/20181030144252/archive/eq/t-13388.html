THE DRUIDS GROVE
Mini Guide to Barkspur



This task pops up with you looting a skin from red-con mobs in Devastation, named in Sverag or any mob in Ragethorn. As soon as you loot the skin a task will pop up in your screen (Saga Skins) - you get it automatically. There is no npc to request this task from.

This will grant you a single task -NOT a group one- to collect skin samples from different model mobs from Plane of Rage. You need to collect a total of NINE skins. You can do your farming either in Devastation or Sverag... as long as you do red-con Named (in Sverag) or red-con Captains/leaders (In devastation), these are what drop the skins. They also drop from just about everything in Razorthorn if you have access.


I. Where to get skins from?

The Devastation

In Devastation look for red cons called "an elite", "an elite soldier", "a centurian", "a dark initiate". etc. Watch the camps and troops in Devastation as they wipe out other camps, they will spawn new races. Sometimes these red-con will pop up. They spawn at a higher rate close to the Stronghold and around it. They can be soloed, the tedious part is getting them alone. Stay out of range, cast snare on the red-con... then run as far as you can and egress or exodus. Then go back and see if he's solo - rinse and repeat if he isn't. You can also try and cast Nature's Serenity on his friends and then pull him from a distance as quickly as you can, but this technique didn't work for me well enough (they warp all around and end up agroing others). Pull them to the Stronghold walls so you don't get adds. They break root often so keep on alert, and also watch for trains - don't stick particularly close to the Stronghold door (because of trains), though the side camps are fine for soloing.

You can also check the outer walls. Sometimes lines of mobs stand there and red-cons pop. The red con eye was particularly abundant here. I did the harmony thing, ensnare the red come... if friends followed i ran and evac'ed. Then you can go back and get the red con alone.

Some of these mobs can summon. A list of these mobs include Red con Werewolves "A Ravager" and "An Elder", Red-con Drachnids and "a basher" which should be a troll red con. Red con Shiliskin, Kobolds, Orcs and Evil Eyes do not summon.

For a complete list of mobs' names see Fenier's thread here (http://eq.forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=13460).


Sverag, Stronghold of Rage

In Sverag (the stronghold)look for easy named such as "The Subjugator". The best camp area is near the instance door. If you can get a group around that area they can pull "the subjugator" as well as a white con that spawns right beside the door. Both of these drop skins. Even if they aren't the race you need, they mostly drop Bolvirk skins but can also drop others that aren't of their particular race.


Razorthorn

Like I mentioned earlier, if you have a group with access to these instances, you can hunt here for the skins. The skins have a 80% chance of dropping of trash here. NOTE: The task will not pop up here if you do not have any skin, you can only get the task from looting skins in Devastation or Sverag.


II. Complete List of Skins

The complete list of skins you need is as follows:

1) Bolvirk - Scarred Bolvirk Skin (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88069)
2) Undead - Mummified Pigmented Skin (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88070)
3) Kobold - Patch of Dyed Kobold Fur (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88071)
4) Orc - Torn and Tattooed Orc Flesh (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88072)
5) Evil Eye - Inked Evil Eye Hide (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88073)
6) Wurines - Decorated Wurine Hide (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88074)
7) Drachnid - Etched Drachnid Carapace (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88075)
8) Shilliskin - Tattooed Shiliskin Skin (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88076)
9) Berserkers/Vah Shir - Scarred and Tattooed Skin (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=88077)



III. Who to give the skins to

After you got all the skins, take them to Grand Librarian Maelin in the top of the library in Plane of Knowledge.

He will give you pages for each skin sample you give him. Combine the total 9 skins in a Book Binding and you should get a book. Turn in this book for your spell. You need an open top inventory slot for the Book Binding, you can get it on East Freeport, Corathus, and most home cities. Here's a list with buying locations (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=19421&menustr=035000000000)

He then tells you,
Grand Librarian Maelin says 'Well, these are very interesting! I've learned a lot from the things you have brought me, so I wanted you to have this.'

You get Barkspur.

Cleric: Elixir if Divinity
Ranger: Scorched Earth

If you don't care about the tob flag part the alternative Advenia mentioned is a lot easier.

The mob at the entrence in stronghold of rage (right behind the door) gives out a number of collect 4 quests. Doing any of those will get you your spell. Basically ever type of mob that drops a skin also has a chance to drop another no drop item, typically a body part. You get 4 of those and turn them in for the spell (they made it so you can get the parts first and task 2nd now too).

I got all the parts for mine from devestation, not sure if they drop in stronghold of rage as well (edit I did get an undead drop in stronghold - if you farm there though you'll probably have to do the turn in after the next control change). The drops are;

Bolvirk - Bolvirk ear
Undead - Undead legionaire's skull
Kobald - Kobald paw
Orc - Bloody orc head
Evil Eye - Evil eye central oculus
Wurine - Wurine paw
Drachnid - Drachnid head
Shiliskin - Shiliskin feeding tentacle
Berserkers - Stone war axe

The berserkers seemed like the best ones to go after. Troll bashers summoned but were still soloable, otherwise it was easy to pull packs of zerkers to the wall snaring them along the way then root dot them. Drop rate seemed good, especially for the vah shirs. Ended up pulling like 3 or 4 packs of them total maybe to get 4 axes.
