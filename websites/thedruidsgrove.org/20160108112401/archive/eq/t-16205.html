In gathering swirling shadows last night, I found a way to help prevent interrupts. The island with the shadow mobs in Twilight doesn't really have a nice corner to back into for casting while tanking a train. I found out that I could Vinelash cascade the entire train, then back out of melee range and still be able to damage the pack with my PBAoE spells. It broke the root often, but with mobs that have so few HP, one AoE is all you really need.
