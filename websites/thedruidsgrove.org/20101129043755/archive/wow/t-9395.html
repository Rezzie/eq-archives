Hey everyone I just thought I'd share something with you all :).

There's a new MMORPG in the making called Rising Force Online (RFO). The game looks great! What's really cool is that there are three factions, instead of two. Check out www.rfbase.com (http://www.rfbase.com) for more detailed info.

For those of you who are looking for a cool new game, this might be what you're looking for. This game has the whole
"space-age" attire to it, but you still get to kick some ass with swords/shields. Looks great, check out the screenshots on the site.

The three races (factions) are:

The Cora - Elvish Magic Users, Spiritual.

The Accretians - Androids, 100% Mechanical. Big guns and big armor!

The Bellato - A combination of the above, the Bellato are Cyborgs, although they also use magic.

But anyhow, check out the link to find out more!!
