Make sure you do the 3 dain missions as you level up. There is a seal aug that you can get by winning the drop from all 3 missions that is just amazing. The best thing is that there are three levels of this aug, one for level 45ish, one for 55ish and top one ofr 65ish.

The top aug gives 14ac, 70hp, 70mana plus other stats, the lower augs are 10/50/50 and 12/60/60. This is definitely a set of Mm that lower levels will want to do over and over again.

Also, dain 2 and 3 there are hp augs as possible loot drops as well, again these scale up/ down to the level of the player....so doing dain missions from 45+ you can easily get all the visible armor aug slots taken care of for a long time.
