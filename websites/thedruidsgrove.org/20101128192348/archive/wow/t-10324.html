Hey Onyko,
I have 2 lvl 60 horde that are both 300skill fishing.

Make sure you use lures when your fishing skill is not very high. This will help a huge amount.

I would fish mainly in the barrens, ratchet and south of ratchet. Try to fish in the oilyblack mouth fishing holes. You can make a lot of money while you lvl your fishing. **Your fishin will continue to go up even if you fish in lower lvl areas. I.e., you don't have to find higher lvl zones to increase your skill because it moves at the same slow pace regardless.

I personally would que for WSG, fly to barrens and fish for Oily Blackmouth, then go WSG, then fish, WSG, fish, etc. Free action pots rule.

The link that Yrys provided is great for Stonescale eels. I have always fished for them in the Bay of Storms (Aszhara). However, you need a skill of 400+ to consistantly catch fish - vice "your fish got away". Only fish there at night: midnight or later when the %catch is the best.

Hope this helps!
