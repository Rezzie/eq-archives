I dont think I have seen anything posted concerning rank caps per lvl.

So far you have done very well getting your MS rank at only lvl 33 however its only going to get harder and harder. Competition for rank is based off of the cp's( contribution points) you get for the week compared to every other MS no matter what lvl they are. So say there are 10 Master Sergeants, 1 being you and the other 9 are all lvl 60. It dosent matter how many cp's you get if they all get more than you, you will not rank up. At lower level ranks this dosent come in to play as much but as you get higher the competition gets very tough. One missed day can put you way behind the pack.
If im wrong here on anything dont hesitate to correct me.

I worked my way to Lt. Commander and decided that was far enough. My highest cp week was 325k i think when alot of others were getting 500-800k. I simply could not put in that kind of time so i gave up heh.
