Lowbie quests:
1. Gnoll fangs to the captain in tillin in south qeynos
2. Bone chips to the cleric guild in felwith.
3. Collect orc pawn hatchets and return to ranger guild in kelethin.
4. Rusty weapons to the ranger guild in kelethin.
5. Talk to your respective class armor quest NPC to get a free bag to carry stuff.
6. Spider fungus to Walter in Halas for shaman spells.
7. Collect ice goblin necklaces for supplies from Lysbith in Halas warrior guild.
8. Collect bone chips for the paladin guild in kaladim.
9. Goblin beads to warrior guild in kaladim.
10. Orc belts in kaladim warrior guild.
11. Orc shoulderpads to the kaladim warrior guild.
12. Tumpy tonics to the bar keep in kaladim.
13. Moonstones to the barkeep next to the dock in Qeynos.
14. Do the deliver supplies quest for the barbarian in shadowhaven to the gnome in the bazaar. (also gives faction with shadowhaven)
15. Postal notes to various bards, easiest one is kelethin to felwith.
16. Pick up a present with a reciept from Vesteri in surefall glade to give to the woodworker in qeynos named Nesiff, bring back statue to verteri.
17. Bring polar bear pelts to Cindl in halas (the white bear pelts) for pieces of patchwork armor and faction.
18. Bring 4 skunk glands to the cleric guild in kaladim for a spell and some money.
Thats about all the low level quests that i have used over the years.
