The timer is Very RNG dependant though...I have cleared it with 7+ mins to spare and only seconds with the same group of people pulling at nearly the same pace! (had a group doing it everyday to get us all a mount) The issue is where the next group spawns. Sometimes you have to run the lenth of the city, other times its right next to you. Chain pulling is key, and Resto druids make the run cake. Its definatley beneficial to have instant casts and HOTs. Nothing hits real hard, but many things hit soft and often. A good tank/healer combo will make the timer almost everytime. Bonus boss takes ~1 min to kill. (DPS depending)

The gauntlet is the killer for most PUGs because your tant needs mean/mobile AoE threat (Pally/Bear FTW). Also, DPS has to manage their aggro here, because the elite mobs are mixed in with the regular trash, and over excited AOE dps ends up dead quick. Fun run imho. Done it way too many times though.
