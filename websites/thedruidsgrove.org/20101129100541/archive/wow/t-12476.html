It's just preference, but I would take Natural Shapeshifter, especially if you love NS+HT and/or don't have a mage in your regular 10-man group.

Also, if you're going to go with the points in balance, you might as well get Natural Perfection to buff your crit chance (as it affects all spells).

I think it's more of a style choice when it comes to balance vs. feral for solo. Personally, I found it very difficult to go from soloing as a cat while feral to soloing as a cat while resto. Your call.

I leveled feral, so I always kept my extra points in my feral tree and carried around my feral set and threw that on while I was soloing or doing dailies.

As my resto set was building quite nicely, I decided to re-spec and put my extra points into Balance. I figured that way I'd at least have more room in my bags (no feral set to carry around), plus I'd never really tried my balance abilities.

Gotta tell ya, I was quite pleasantly surprised! I'm loving it! Balance skills are fun, I can solo quite efficiently, and I very rarely run out of mana.

I never thought I'd say this, but I miss my paws less and less every day. Mind you, I still upgrade my feral set if the opportunity presents itself. :wink:
