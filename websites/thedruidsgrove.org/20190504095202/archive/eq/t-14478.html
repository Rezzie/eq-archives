Give stuff like Broken Nokk Insignia to Librarian Hemfar on level 1 of Crescent Reach. Charm powers up the more artifacts you give him. (If you go back and give him an artifact you've already given him, he rejects it.)

You must turn in 5-6 items before he will give you the charm. If you are not being offered the charm, check EQ->Quests->Rewards Buffer off the EQ Button. It may be in there.

Some of these items are ground spawns, others drop from mobs and some a rewards from different quests.
