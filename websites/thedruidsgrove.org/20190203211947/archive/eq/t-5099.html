Plane of air
Nothing in here is soloable all mobs summon, even a single group can easely get into trouble here, really sux zone to much lag stupid patching etc..
Everything see invis.

Plane of earth

Nothing in here is soloable, all mobs summon. Can get very good exp with a single group camping zone in, or pull to a wall spot, some decent trade skill loot also, if you are into that stuff.
Everything see invis

Plane of water

Some mob are soloable, Regrua, can be snared dont summon. They are very deep in, and you will have to pass see invis mobs to get down to them. Can make some very good cash or food with their claws.
Triluin champion, Regrua sentry, and all named mobs see invis. all other mobs can be passed with invis.

Plane of fire

In general 3 spots very easy to solo.

First field (zone in) All mobs before castle, are pretty easy to solo. Some mobs here is snare imune, so you must be able to handle mobs without snaring them.
Bird spot (between first castle and the bridge) If both birds are dead u can single kite mobs here, magma guardians and what ever u like to pull from second field. all mobs in second field is snareable, can be quite resistant though, so be prepared to have a lot of early root breaks, and resist.
Picnic table (Fennin ro trigger spot) Can kite this spot very easy, tons of room, if Fennin ro is dead can kite all way into his house. All mobs snareable somewhat resistant though.
Named mobs see invis, most normal mobs dont see invis, although a few does, so con mobs before you move.
