The naggy monster mission can drop an earring with 100ish mana/ hp and its just a blast to play. We did it with 5 people, one playing naggy, one the king and 3 healers. The key was to get naggy set up just at the start of the bridge so his cone of fire and fear hit as many mobs as possible...and the healers staying ready to cheal. Good exp, ok loot and a ton of fun.
