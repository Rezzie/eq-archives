This following bit does not have to be done, but it's the lore on how we found the rest of the story: Your Location is -2198.29, 1476.26, -409.16

You say, 'Hail, Finrin Talister' (he can be tracked near BW zone, he's the old soulbinder type guy)

Finrin Talister says 'Well met, Wood Elf. What brings you to this [legendary site]? I hope that you're here to do something about all these pesky [goblins].'

You say, 'goblins'

Finrin Talister says 'They've been a real problem lately. Not that they aren't always a problem, but there was all this commotion about some sort of contest with a prize a while ago. It seems that they've [forgotten] it again for the moment, thank goodness.'

You say, 'forgotten'

Finrin Talister says 'Yes, forgotten. They forget about this contest for a while, then suddenly one of them remembers. Fights break out for a while over who will win the [prize] then they forget again until the next one remembers it. I wish they'd have this contest already and get it over with.'

You say, 'prize'

Finrin Talister says 'I don't know for sure what the prize is, I don't speak goblin all that well. All I know is that they discovered something on some poor [victim] that they desire. I'm not certain that they even know what it is from the way they talk about it.'

You say, 'victim'

Finrin Talister says 'I don't know who it was, just someone that they caught sneaking into or out of Droga. Guide to fighting the Goblin Hider -

After spawning the group of Goblin Hiders there is a very simple way to kill them, i'd suggest just getting a few dots out and keep epic 1 handy incase you get adds (giants and stuff).

After they pop on track, run to loc -3000, 75. They all hit for around 150ish, double hitting at most. So be careful if you arent buffed! Now run onto a hill adjacent to the building with SoE on for lev. Run up to the top and stay on the top of the wall, pull it using snare and run back to where you were. Keep in mind: One will spawn when you first find them, pull that then a few seconds later a few more will drop from the sky, then a few more. If you evac and go back, just one will be standing there

If it is the real goblin it will say, "Your dead corpse will soon line my pockets with gold!"

If its the fake goblin it will say, "Hah! You stupid! Me not real hider!"

This might sound kind of weird...if it is a fake goblin, root the one you just pulled and keep pulling more til you get a message for a real one. If you can indeed pick out which one is the real one, root him far away then any of the others and evac to zone line.

Now run back to the building, lev up, and run to the top. The one sitting there is all alone and will be the real one! Pull and nuke away, it'll take about 10 nukes or so to kill him using Winter's Frost. Good luck!
