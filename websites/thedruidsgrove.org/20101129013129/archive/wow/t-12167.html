Found this site recently..kinda interestin'.

http://www.freerice.com/totals.html

Basically, they give you a word and a choice of definitions, you guess it right, and the advertisers on their site send the UN 10 grains of rice per word to be sent out to the hungry and those who can't afford the food.

Sure, 10 grains doesn't seem like much, but every little thing means a lot, when you're starving.

Just in case anyone cares :o, also a nice time waster when you're waiting for something.
