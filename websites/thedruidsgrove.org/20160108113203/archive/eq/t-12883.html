12-17-2005, 03:21 PM
Kurn's Tower from PoK.
Find and use the Cabilis Stone / book.
Which will take you to THE FIELD OF BONE.

Map found here.
http://www.eqatlas.com/kunark/fieldofbonemap.html
Book is at about the letter K on the bottom of the map.

Go north until you run into the edge of the Pit.
Skirt around the pit keeping it to your right.
Keep going aound the pit until you see a Tall building
with a ramp goin up the side. Go up the ramp and
at the top you will zone into Kurn's Tower.


Paludal Caverns from PoK.
Find and use the Nexas Stone / book.
Go East up and across the middle until you get to the other side.
This will take you to Shadow Haven.

Map found here.
http://www.eqatlas.com/luclin/shadowhavenmap.html
You will zone on the little circle between 20 and 22.

You will go east. (stay out of the pool)
Up 2 sets of stairs. (by #31 on map)
Go south / east through the garden and under neath the bar. (#9)
You should run into an old wooden bridge going south.
Now don't worry you will not fall in between the boards on the bridge.
Keep going around the 2 bend and that should put you into PALUDAL CAVERNS.
