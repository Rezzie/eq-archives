06-25-2005, 05:18 AM
Several options:

Raptors in TD. I tried several methods, the fastest was to charm the 3 vicious ones to kill the others. While your pet (hasted and DSed) is fighting, you ride a horse (to save mana) and root+snare 3-4 raptors one by one, dot them with level 44 spell + mana free DOD. After 3 or so fights, kill the pet, grab another.

Plane of Mischief yard junk (do not touch the named), and the rascals on the bridge (better exp but worse pathing). At 57 the fastest method is agro-kiting with a charmed skunk doing damage. Note that not all skunks are chamable with level 52 spell. Root-dot also works but slower. Bonus exp in this zone since you are 55+ and the mobs are mostly within 5 levels of you (51-55). DEFINITELY come here once you get to level 58 -- now you find a skunk to charm and you can almost wipe the entire forest (You got level 58 heal to keep your pet alive).

PON raven/werewolf. Charm kite. Raven gives better exp per kill, but for werewolf you can kill both the pet and another mob (no worry of running out of pets), so I prefer werewolves. Use harmony of nature a lot.

POD charm a disease rat and kill the other mobs. Rats are tougher than other mobs so you kill fast. However the ground is hilly so I don't like it. Finding a rat is also not very easy (but not difficult either).

Blackpaw gnoll cave (deep inside) in Japperedpine. Root-doting while on horseback make it a nobrainer. Exp is moderate. Some casters see through invis, making it trickier to get in.
