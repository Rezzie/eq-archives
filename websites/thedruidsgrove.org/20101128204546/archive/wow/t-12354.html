Basically, Nordrassil is better for stats. No matter how you look at it, it's inarguably better in every way, although admittedly not by a huge amount.

What it comes down to for me, is this:

Malorne:
2 pieces: Your melee attacks in Cat Form have a chance to generate 20 additional energy.

**VS**

Nordrassil:
4 pieces: Your Shred ability deals an additional 75 damage




We can show that if your damage was extremely low, say shreds were hitting for 20 damage, the nordrassil bonus would be better.
But if my shreds are critting for say, 25,000 damage, I'd be better off with the extra energy! Instead of 25,075 shreds, I'd rather get an extra shred in every now and then, for 50,000 damage!

So. It stands to reason that at some point, somewhere along the line, the 2pc Malorne bonus will become better than the 4pc nordrassil bonus. I have to choose between them because I can't have both.
