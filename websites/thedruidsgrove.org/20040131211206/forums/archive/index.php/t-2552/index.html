I like never use Legacy of Bracken, had it for a while, but I see other druids using group damage shields all the time, so I actually looked at it.

The description says it lasts for 16 minutes, for 380 mana. Thats better than the single target one that lasts for 6.5 for 180 mana. So I figure what the heck, I'll try it out for a while.

Well it seemed like a short 16 minutes, so I timed it... It's not, it's 6.5 minutes (unadjusted.) Lucy agrees. So, question is, is the spell bugged, or the ingame description?

Group damage shields (heck, ds's in general in PoP) are of questionable value at best, more of a convenience in knowing when it wears off, but with such a short duration this spell is simply not worth casting, unlike Ancient: Legacy of Blades which has the long duration.

TeriMoon
I am getting lazy in my old age. I have always been dead set against using group damage shields in favor of single target ones. There have been reasons like mana efficiency, better single target DSs vs group, not wanting to waste buff slots, etc. But it seems to me these days it makes no sense not to use it. I mean its not really any more mana, the amount of the DS is exactly the same, and its a LOT more convenient. I say its not more mana because when using single target I always time it for the tank by casting it on myself first then the tank. It annoys me to have to make the tank call out for a DS every 6.5 minutes. Its part of my job to keep up my buffs, IMO. So, I always end up casting single target twice anyhow. These days, I have plenty of mana, so that's not really a consideration, and I can be a little extravagant for the sake of convenience.
