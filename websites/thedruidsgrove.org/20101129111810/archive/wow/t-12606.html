Your Lifebloom issue is really, really weird. If what you're saying is true, it sounds like you're getting two stacks no matter what in your healing gear (if it's healing for 300-something per tick at your bonus healing level), whereas your Lifeblooms are stacking properly in your PVP gear. Your idol is certainly not going to dictate whether or not Lifebloom stacks. If this problem persists I'd contact a GM.

As for coefficient information, this is quoted from http://druid.wikispaces.com/Druid_Game_Mechanics:

Lifebloom:

- Approximately 51.8% of your +healing is added the the HoT over all 7 ticks (7.4% per tick);
- Approximately 42.8% of your +healing is added the end heal;
- The +healing bonus of Lifebloom is, as of patch 2.1, added to all 3 stacks of Lifebloom, so stacking lifebloom 3 times will add a total of 155.4% of your +healing to the HoT (22.2% per tick).
- The end heal of lifebloom is unaffected by the amount of stacks of Lifebloom. It will always heal for 600 + 42.8% of your +healing regardless of 1, 2 or 3 stacks.
- The end bomb of lifebloom doesn't count as your own healing. Therefore, all agro generated by the end bomb of lifebloom is attributed to the person healed, not to yourself.
