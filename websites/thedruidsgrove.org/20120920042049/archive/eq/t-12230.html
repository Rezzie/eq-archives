09-30-2005, 11:29 AM
I believe 3 seconds. But in being unable to heal for 3 seconds usually means tank splat in the kinds of situations I'm talking about using it for.

Casting time is 2.5. Recast time on our heals is 2.25 seconds. Delaying heals 0.3 seconds is worth it for the benefit of the aa's in situations that call for it -- you just need to learn how to get it off fast between spell casts. Personaly, I have it on slot 5 hot button page 9 -- so as I cast a spell I'll hit shift+9, than when it's down casting I hit 5, than while SC is casting I put my mouse over my heal's spell gem, than while I cast a manual heal I hit shift+1 to get back to main page. Very little time lost if your quick about it.

Anyways, most practical purposes for SC the casting time doesn't matter much. If you see a lot of mobs come into camp, if you cast heals right off the bat you'll most likely die anyways, so that window of time is when you want to cast SC so you can blast heals left and right w/o having to worry about mobs being locked down.
