The best answer to your first two questions is wait till your 60. Duke's require atleast 5 level 60's if your all geared decently (you can get prob away with 4 if your BWL pimp'n)...

This is how it works. You kill the twilight cultist, they drop cloths (robe/shoulders/hood), if you have friendly faction with Cen Ciricle you can equip those cloths and activate a Lesser Windstone that you see around the twilight camps. That summons up a Templar that's either lightning/Fire/Water/Earth... listed from hardest to easiest. The thing of it is - you have to be level 60 to equip the cloths to summon templar. I solo'm regularly at 60... so they're druidable.

Everytime you kill a templar, they drop 1 crest. With 3 crests and a Large Brilliant Shard you can get a medallion of station. You equip the medallion and cultist robe/hood/shoulders and then activate the 'Windstone' and summon a Duke. As mentioned you want atleast 5 people (prob 7-10 if your actually using this as a guide :shuffle: ). I actually like to bring in some mid 50's/new 60's guildies to get some of the gear that drops from these guys.

Duke drop Signets like templar's drop crests... 3 signets and 5 large brilliant shards will get you a Ring that allows you to summon one of the royalty.... when you have revered faction. These are summoned from the "greater windstones" and can drop epic loots.

The only reward I'm aware of for faction other then the summoning is enchanter reciepes that require faction to buy...

*edit* oh yea, Bonecrusher from is a quest from DMwest that starts and ends in the instance itself. DMwest is the hardest of the DM's and for the best to be 60... DM east is much more friendly to a 55. I personally prefer the Fist of Ommok (drop in LBRS) to the Bonecrusher for the hp's, but it's a tough call. (29str 12sta vs. 30str 1%crit)... Fist def' looks cooler imho.
