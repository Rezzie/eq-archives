No note (on alt) - yes
Note on cursor - no
Note on top inventory - no
Note in bag - no
Note in bank, top level - no
Note in bank, in bag - no
Note in shared bank - yes

Conclusion... the new "quest item checker" is capable of knowing whether you do (or don't) have a specific quest item regardless of whether that item is on your toon (in bags or not) or in your bank (in bags or not, but not shared bank). It probably uses the same logic as the Lore Item checker.

Which means if the Coven spawner is looking for your Hasty Note, it can be in your bank, in your bags, etc and the spawner will still see it.

It also means if the Coven spawner is looking for you NOT to have previous crystals, then they are not safe in your bags, in the bank, etc.
