Welcome back, Kerech!

Venril is solable today, perhaps as soon as level 70. Before that his lifetap will make him very hard to kill. With a merc it should be quite easy actually.

The frog is a joke, he hits for 200 or so. The problem is, he runs when low on health and is (nearly) immune to magic - very hard to snare him. Fire sometimes lands on him. He is extremely resistent against spells, so you will have to make him die to thorns and/or mellee.

Good luck!
