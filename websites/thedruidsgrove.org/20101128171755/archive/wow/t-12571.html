Does anyone have tips-n-tricks for grinding Cenarion Expedition rep? I'm currently at Honored, need to get to Exalted so I can make Nethercleft Leg Armor.

Before you do a single CE quest in HFP, ZM, BEM or NS, turn in 360 Unitendified Plant Parts (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=24401) to get to honored (360 can be purchased off the AH for 100-150g, do it!)
Once you reach honored, turn in any uncatalogued species (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=24407) you get from the above turn-ins.
Do all quests in HFP, ZM, BEM and NS. Once this step is done, you will be well into Revered.
Consider getting epic flight form as that quest chain gives quite a bit of honor.
Run regular SV, heroic UB (IMO, quite easy) or heroic SP (also very easy)
Turn in any Coilfang Armaments that drop from SV or hSP.

Step 5 should be done after you reach honored.
Step 6 cannot be done until you complete a quest that starts from a random drop in SV. Don't turn in armaments until you have reached honored.

You can also run regular SP and UB until you are honored in lieu of turning in plant parts.

For Step 1 and Step 6, I had my banking alt purchase cheap-o parts and armaments off the AH the whole time I was leveling. I had all 360 plant parts by the time I was lvl 50 for well less than 125g (which is just about "free" in terms of BC economy). From levels 60-70+ I purchased 40+ armaments.
