Any current content won't be great xp at 75, the highest level xp mobs are L73, which is 2 levels under 75.

Kiting? 69.1 hard will be ideal -- during double xp weekend we got ~12 aa per LotD in a kite group, so will be 5-6 aa/hour w/o xp mods @ L70. Since every mod there is L73, it'll be decent xp up til 75.

However, the instance server is going to overloaded the first day or 2 of expac. Non-instanaced zones w/ large amounts of L71+ mobs are illsalin, relic, sverag, elddar, hive (bridges/cacoons/orc room), and dreadspire. Non-instanced nest has a few yellows as well.

And best druid solo spot atm is likely trusik caves in qvic, but at 75 the xp there will be horrid. The xp hits seem to be mostly 10%, sometimes 12% @ 70 - but since they have very low xp it's ideal for druid soloing. 69.1 hard will give 15% solo per mob, but a druid could solo 2 trusiks before 1 spider. Yellows generally give 12% (in non-instanced hive at least.)
