Basically find another druid that wants to grind the faction and its hell of alot easier...specially if you are both feral. You have the tank/healer/dps slot filled already. If you find a tank, now one of you isnt a tank, you are dps., if you find a healer, one of you are dps, one is tank. etc.

I ran my instances in one weekend with another druid, we were flying through it, you get roughly 1800ish rep per clear + armaments looted.

Basically you want to kill everything...you are there for rep so there isnt a reason to skip stuff, the bog giants are harder, sometimes groups dont even kill them, but they give 25rep per kill. Also pull the slaves solo, they give rep too, but not if they run with the master dies.

Generally if you can get people that are getting up to revered for heroic keys and once you get a solid group going and able to repeat, you start flying through the clears

But yea, alot of people dont "need" exalted here, so stop after revered, I llinked the nice healing exalted offhand to some healers and got them to come for that hehe.
