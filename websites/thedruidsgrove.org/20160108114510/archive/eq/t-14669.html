12-18-2006, 06:24 PM
His dps does ramp up if he remains aggro'd on one person for too long. Weaponshield or a riposite (have them use a big dmg big dly weap if they have one) will build up aggro fast b/c of the extra amount of swings it produces. If a tank is building up aggro during that time it might not matter if he ends up taunting off of the ranger so probably better to have them save aggro tools with a timer until after ae taunting off the ranger (just be sure not to let the ranger die though or the aggro they built up goes to waste).

Other than that there are a few ways to help with building aggro better than usual. Sk/enc aggro buffs are the usual ones, you can also try to stack on as many proc buffs as possible... panther, stormblade, sloth, skin of the repitle and that mage ds style one will all attribute aggro to the person they're procing off of.
