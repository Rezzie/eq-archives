If I were to level today, I'd go down the Feral tree until I was level 30 and had travel form (and/or purchased my land mount). At that point, I'd pick-up 3/5 Furor for 75 energy every time I shift into cat (so that you'd have plenty of energy to start a fight as soon as you dismounted, shifted from travel form, shifted back from a self-heal, etc.). Then continue back in the Feral tree (focusing on DPS talents) until I pick-ed up mangle. Then I'd take 4/5 Furor, 1/2 iMotW, 5/5 Intensity and 1/1 OoC (at level 61). Then focus on taking 5/5 RnT as it awesome while leveling.

After that, well? Call us when you make it that far and we'll talk.
