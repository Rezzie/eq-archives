Claritondeus
02-09-2007, 04:41 PM
Pounce, Mangle, FF, Shred. You can get this all off in 4 seconds. Most likely you will have 4 cp up, if not 5. If 4, Mangle again, then rip, and quickly shift to bear.

If they are a caster, make sure to keep feral charging them whenever you can for the cast interruption. Mangle is instant, and Maul is on the next attack. Always have a maul queue'd up, and mangle whenever you can. Save your bash for when you are low on health.

At 64 with Maim, sometimes I'll build 5cp, Maim, shift out, HoT myself, root them, drop a MF, then shif into bear. Depends on whether or not I want to start the fight with a few HoT's ticking (good to do to mitigate damage against high dps classes, especially warlocks that will fear you and burn you while you are running around).

Also against high dps classes, let your rage build up, and hit frenzied regen. Thats 2500 points in health without having to lose a ton of health and armor shifting out of bear. Add that to the 250 - 350 health that you will be getting from iLoTP every 6 seconds, and you are good.

Druids right now can take any class. *edit: except maybe for demonology speccd warlocks with Soul Link and a Felguard. Fel just hits too hard, and with SL, the warlock takes 20% less damage.*
