This quest has nothing to do with Tier 3 aside from name. The quest is for the reward tokens. Get enough tokens and you turn them in to the quartermaster at Light's Hope and you can choose from various rewards. There are both blue and purple loot. You may do the quests repeatedly to get multiple rewards. The higher your rep with AD the less tokens you will need to get the rewards.
