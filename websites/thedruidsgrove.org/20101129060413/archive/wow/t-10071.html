Ok... as some of you remember I posted a while back about being totally owned by a warrior 3 levels below me. He was 24 I was 27. I thought it would be an easy kill and made several tactical errors and I was killed.


After listening to the advice of many of you here and getting a bit more experience I was able to test my skill again last night in a similar setting.

While in Wetlands a lvl 29 Warrior challenged me to a duel. He was a Private in rank so I knew he had at least some PvP experience. I have no rank as I rarely PvP. I am lvl 32 currently.

We go into the street and I accept. As soon as the counter counts down I root him and cast a starfire. It hit him pretty hard and took a nice chunk of his hp. Roots break and he charges me and I was stunned for a sec. I cast regrowth on myself and re root him. After healing to full I drop to bear and go toe to toe with him. Bashing when its available. When I needed a heal I switch out, root him, heal, and at this point he was fairly low health so I go into cat and tear him down.

When I won he was very surprised. He apparently doesnt lose often.

I accredit the knowlege that I have largely to this forum.

Just for fun I switched out of my thick murloc chest to my Green Whelp armor and asked him to duel me again and told him I would not fight back.

I dropped to bear and he attacked. He was napping in about 20 seconds. lol.. he was pissed.

I let him kill me after he woke up just to be a good sport.
