Mission is from the Gnome camp to the east of the zoneline with Steamfront, in the Dragonscale hills zone.

Get both the instance and the explore task, and the easier of the two collect tasks. Take a group. Start exploring.

The further you get (it is ~5 hours from start to finish) the more of the explore task you finish.

As your explore task progresses, you seem to get access to instances that start part-way into the instance.
