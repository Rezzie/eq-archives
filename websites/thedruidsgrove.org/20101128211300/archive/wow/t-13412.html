Your equipped weapon skill will not matter unless you are melee attacking in caster form.

Easy way to level a weaon skill: Find a low level (15-20 levels below you) start your auto attack, and alt tab out. They won't hit you often enough, or hard enough to matter. Until your weapon skill gets up, you won't hit hard or often enough to matter either. I had to do this for my hunter once he was allowed to use staves.
