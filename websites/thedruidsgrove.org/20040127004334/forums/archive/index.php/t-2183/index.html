Donaden
I actually had much luck soloing down in the jail. I know the tricky agro you mean, but the location you decide to charm at can prevent that.

At the last staircase leading down into the jail, where you can go right to enter the jail or left to go toward the caves with worms, rats path frequently past the closed door at the top of the stairs. Simply wait there and DC one as it walks by, then walk him down the stairs to the jail. If you do get agro deal with it on the staircase and you won't have any adds, just don't go to the base of the stairs or the high guardian inside the door will come rearrange your face. (It should be noted that some of the pestilence rats that path by that door are too high to charm, they are few and far between however, I have rarely had more than 1 DC fail per trip there).

Once you have your rat past the high guardians it's easy. Use Harmony of Nature to split and let your buffed up rat chomp through enforcers and punishers. For me it was a 1 CH per fight deal, with the lvl 58 DS and potc on the rat, and only the epic and bracer click to get me some damage credit on the kill. I set up my camp in the corner to the right as you enter, next to Dreamthief's cell, to avoid the wraith which spawns every so often when someone is in the room.

For the difficulty of the area the reward is very good. Only rarely was I in any danger, and if I was it was a group of Mauvin seekers plowing by to get their flag done. Without Dire Charm this could be a problematic camp, as you don't have a lot of room to be running around in the jail trying to recharm. The rampages and flurries add up and can quickly drain your hp away if you have 3 or more punishers/enforcers chasing you. I first tried this camp alone at 61, and my gear on my magelo profile hasn't changed much since, in case you want a reference for your stats. Hardest part about this camp is finding it open on Tunare. :)
