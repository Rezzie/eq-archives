If I were you, I'd consider the Onyxia questline. It starts with a quest in Burning Steppes called Dragonkin Menace. You can obtain the quest from an NPC in the camp in the South East of the zone.

Onyxia differs from MC/ZG/BWL in that there is very little in the way of trash mobs, and the entire instance will be over in about 30 minutes or less. It can take a while to learn the fight if your guild has never done it before, so you'll have to get used to wiping whilst everyone figures out what to do, but once you're attuned and you can get into her lair, killing her will provide some awesome loots, including the Stormrage Hat, which looks like a pair of antlers! :)
