I'd start by solo kiting in Tipt. You can also do well in the Steppes and Sunderrock. There are a variety of missions to go around that enable you to pick up assorted, and fairly powerful, nonvisible slot items. DoN missions (with crystal rewards) yield very nice groupable augments, especially for resists. You can make considerable plat with tradeskill drops in 65-70 level zones, and save up for the new cultural armor. Once geared up a bit, TBS group gear (or, again, faction grinding + tradeskill component farming + buying currency in the bazaar for Katta gear) will be viable.

Pix, Halls of Honor is hot zone for 65+ (or something like that) so this is a good place to go if you want to group. Glowing Hoop of the Silverwing (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=70803) which drops there, was a welcome upgrade for me, but you might have better =)

Also, be sure to visit the Librarian Hemfar (1st floor) and Master Vanguard Regan (2nd floor) in Crescent Reach. Regan hands out a 18 step augment quest, most of it soloable, and resulting in a +75 hp/mana (plus other stats) augm, the Wanderlust Guildstone (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3680).

The librarian has a hand in quest: Charm of Lore (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52354). Basically, throughout TSS zones there will drop various items that he wants. After handing in 5 of them, you will get a charm. The following hand ins will increase the stats. Lots of the items are soloable at our lvl, the last items requires TSS raids. Maxed, the charm is +495 hp/mana with hp/mana regen and other stats too.

DoD has some good things as well, for example the DoD Resist Clickies (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3264), results in +50 DR/PR/FR/CR/MR instant clickies which can be combined into one clicky. It's 4-5 missions for each clicky and you will need a group for each mission.

Good luck!
