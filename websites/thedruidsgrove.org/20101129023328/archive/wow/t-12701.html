Blizzard has announced a keyfob authenticator (http://www.blizzard.com/us/press/080626-auth.html) for added World of Warcraft security. After purchasing the authenticator and linking it to your account, anyone logging in would need to supply the code generated by the authenticator, in addition to password, to log in.

This is intended to increase the security of your account, presumably due to all the account hacking recently.
