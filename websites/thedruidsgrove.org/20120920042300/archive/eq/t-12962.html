A Wizard friend of mine has been running logs with various focuses while testing his new Manaweave Spell (the Wizard version of Dawnstrike).

Basically, what he has found is the following:

No Fire of Dawn Proc:

ActualDamage = (Base) + (Base*ActualFocus)/100

Where ActualFocus = the effective percent of the focus effect your wearing.

This is expanded with Fire of Dawn to be:

ActualDamage = (Base) + (Base*ActualFocus)/100 + Songfocus

Where Songfocus = Bard Song or Fire of Dawn Proc

So for a critical (assuming DF3) with Fire of Dawn Running would be:

ActualDamage = (Base) + (Base*1.25) + (Base*ActualFocus)/100 + SongFocus

Which *should* make the final max number as the following:

ActualDamage = ((Base) + (Base*1.25) + (Base*ActualFocus)/100 + SongFocus * 1.30 (1.5 effect) OR 1.5 (2.0 Effect).

We're looking for help testing the Last Statement, on where exactly the Druid Epic Click falls in to the order. We believe it is placed last and is calucated off the final number.
