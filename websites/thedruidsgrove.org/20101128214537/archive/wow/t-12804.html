Spamming Lacerate works out at more threat than spamming Swipe, even if you already have 5 stacks of Lacerate up on your tank target.

It is only better to spam Swipe if the mob has some kind of modifier on it that increases Damage Taken by a great deal - for example, during The Curator's "Evocation" ability you should already have 5 stacks of Lacerate up, and it's better damage and aggro to spam Swipe instead of Lacerate for the duration of the Evocation.
