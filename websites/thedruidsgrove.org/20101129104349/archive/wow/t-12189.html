am wondering what stas are most important out of stam or spirit?

Spirit is the more healing-focused stat. However, you will have a rough time of it if your max health is too low, it really helps your raid survivability if you can at least take 1 hit from a mob without instantly dying.

If your raid buffed health is at least 9k, you're fine imho. Pile the rest into spirit.

You didn't ask about intellect, +healing, or mp5; I assume you know these stats are also very useful for healing druids. Let us know if you want some more details on what these do.


Credit (http://thedruidsgrove.org/wow/forums/showpost.php?p=146856&postcount=4) Abies for this:
- stamina: increases your total health by 10 per point of stamina
- intellect: increases your total mana by 15 per point of intellect
- spirit: increases your mana regeneration while not casting by 0.56 every 5 seconds (0 while casting, unless you have the talent Intensity, then it is 0.08 every 5 seconds while casting)


Spirit also affects the tree of life Aura - the more spirit, the better your aura will be.

It does that by proxy; for each member of your group, the tree of life aura increases healing taken by 25% of your spirit.

Suppose a warrior in your group is the main tank.
You have 1000 spirit.

25% of 1000 = 250. So the warrior (and everyone else in your group) will receive up to 250 extra healing from spells.


When I lead raids we normally have 2 warrior tanks and 1 paladin tank. I put these 3 in group 1, along with a warlock for the imp aura, and whichever tree druid has the most spirit. That way, all 3 tanks receive some extra healing.

Blizzard have also been talking about making spirit more useful somehow, although I'm not certain on exactly what they plan to do with it.
