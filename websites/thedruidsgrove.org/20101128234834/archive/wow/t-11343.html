The way Omen of Clarity works is that, for 30 minutes, each of your melee attacks has a chance for you to get a 10 second buff called Clearcasting. Clearcasting means the next skill you use, if you use it by the time the buff runs out, costs no rage, energy, or mana.

30 minutes of free skills would be nice :) but this is pretty nice, too and it can make all the difference in a fight.
