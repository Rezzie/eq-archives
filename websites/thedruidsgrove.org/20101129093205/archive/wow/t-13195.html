At level 80; 41.6667 Agility = 1% Dodge.
At level 80; 39.34798813 Dodge Rating = 1% Dodge.


So basically it is nearly the same conversion for gem, so you are better for agi GEMS instead of dodge rating since agi gives you dodge, crit for extra threat and AC for mitigation.

So if i were you i'd go :
16 agi
16 +Hit
8 agi - 8 +Hit

Depending if you really need that much +hit
