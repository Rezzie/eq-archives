Just to sort out a couple of slight misconceptions in this thread:

Yaulp is extended by the Spellcasting Reinforcement Mastery AA. This extends it to 6 ticks. If you're fortunate with client/server synch you can occasionally get a 7th tick, but this is luck.

You cannot use Yaulp to its full effectiveness in any environment when you're casting a lot of spells. This is because Yaulping requires precise timing (check the duration -v the refresh time on it.) What you usually do is Yaulp the moment the gem refreshes and you aren't doing anything else... but in a tight CH ring, the loss of ~3.5 seconds (cast time + gem refresh time) to a Yaulp can wipe the whole raid.
