You can get a starter set of Boom gear pretty cheap...buy the Boomkin rep set http://www.wowhead.com/?itemset=743#comments for 85 gold, then go to the ah to fill in the gaps. You can go to Kara in that gear, and get yourself nicely geared in time for LK's release.

As far as a one-size-fits-all feral build, I would drop Thick Hide and pick up Primal Fury (a must have talent for bear and cat pve/pvp, imo), drop Primal Tenacity in favor of Infected Wounds (again excellent for bear and cat, plus pvp), drop Predatory Instincts for Protector of the Pack (PI only works in cat now, the tooltip is wrong; and PotP is a must have for tanking), and finally drop Improved Mangle for King of the Jungle. KotJ is a far larger dps bump than IM, great burst for pvp, and you want the extra threat in bear.

I skipped Shredding Attacks and took Improved MotW because I rarely just dps anymore, and if I do Rake has been buffed enough that I can do quite well without SA.
