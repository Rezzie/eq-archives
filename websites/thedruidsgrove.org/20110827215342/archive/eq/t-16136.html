Are you referring to Second Chance? That tribute option is more like Second Life than Delay Death. It appears to provide 2%, 4%, and 6% chances of Touch of the Divine firing at ranks 1, 2, and 3 respectively:

http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5612&source=Live

http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5614&source=Live

(Second Life gives you a 25% and 29% chance at rank 1 and 2.)

As for whether it stacks with Second Life, in theory it should since tribute effects are treated as worn effects, and worn effects generally stack with spells. Setting up a test to confirm this would be very difficult, though.
