can't tank without gear. It's not like being "kinda" dps. You have to have the gear to tank it. Regular instances fine, heroic and up you need real tank gear.

Go for the leather crafted crap if you want a good starter set. get your defence maxed for being uncritable or you will die...alot. Get at least 20% dodge, and 12k+hp or dont bother with heroics. Yes you can save it just going bear and having a good healer, but that's the healer not you. Any spec takes a good gear set. Your spec means you need even more gear, not less to tank.
