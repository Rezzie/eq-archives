Class Names: Based on Character Level: 1 - 51 - 55 - 60 - 65 - 70
General AA Titles: for having 6 General AAs
Archetype AA Titles: for having above, + 12 Archetype AAs
Class Specific AA Titles: above, + 24 Class AAs
Class Specific Epic Titles: Awarded for Epics: 1.0 - 1.5 - 2.0
