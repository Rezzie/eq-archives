Question 1:
In your opinion, what is the best weapon obtainable via PvP rewards for a feral druid?
PvP doesn't offer much in the way of feral weapons. The cost of the rank 14 HWL/GM weapons doesn't justify the return, IMO. Not sure how much The Unstoppable Force from AV costs, but it's probably significantly less, and still a good feral weapon once enchanted.

Question 2 (bit of a noob question, sry):
What stat(s) contribute to increasing the amount of damage you crit for (e.g. my FB usually crits for about 1400ish; what do I do to get 2000ish)?
AP, and the easiest way to raise that is go for strength (and have HotW talent maxed).

Question 3:
In your opinion, is it really worth seriously pursuing new PvP lootz now, given that we'll be ditching them for greens by lvl 63 or so?
You won't be ditching them for level 63 greens. If you had the entire warlord's/marshal's epic set, you might jettison a few pieces, but I have no doubts you would still be wearing a few when you hit level 70. What I've seen of the expansion loot, some has been really nice, but even the level 70 arena gear isn't much of an upgrade from the current epic pvp set. Yeah, it has a lot more stamina and it has the upgradeable slots, but that's about it.
