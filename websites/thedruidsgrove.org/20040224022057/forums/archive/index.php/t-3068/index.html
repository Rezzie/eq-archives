Hp is much more important than AC for a druid. It helps keep you alive against any type of attack, not just melee. For me it's a slightly higher priority than mana pool even...

Resists are important too. If you can't get gear that has good resists and good stats, at least work on a set of resist gear--things like a crown of the froglok kings, for example.
