At level 61, I think the quadding opportunities are limited. Once you reach level 62, you can enter HoH and try to quad the basements. But I had a hard time doing that with 5.5k mana iirc. The PoP mobs and later start to have over 30k HP and quadding them takes some decent gear, mana preservation, mana regen, and perhaps nuke crits would help as well.

Other options for experience include: Root rotting / charming the Lorok frogs in PoS on the forrest side (need lvl 63 Command of Tunare to charm frogs). Root Rotting Golems or Razorfiends in Plane of Valor or Murkgliders in Nobles Causeway.

Ah, one I can answer :)

To get to the PoN gargs (which are very good XP until you ding 63, at which time it drops off a lot due to the 5 level bonus XP range), you can do this:

Zone into PoN, and head north high up on the zone wall. When you hit the corner of the zone, turn and keep going east, until you get near the big rift valley. Don't get too close or you'll agro the (forgot their names). Now go south, 50 feet or so west of the cliff dropoff, for a little ways, and then look for a good spot to cross the chasm. Lev across, and then keep going east, aiming basically for the hedge. Once at the NE corner of the zone, go south high up on the wall. You will have to come down off the wall and out a lil bit to get past the tunnel leading to PoNB. Then keep going south, wrap around the hill that comes up in front of you, and there you are at Garg city :)
