Zack
11-02-2006, 11:32 AM
Feral charge works on all mobs/bosses that can have their spells interrupted (including Lor'khan and bat boss -- I've used it there myself). They're immune to the rooting effect of FC, but not the spell interruption effect. :)

...and bash isn't really a spell interrupt -- it's a stun. If they get stunned they naturally stop casting, but a lot more mobs are immune to stuns (and therefore bash) than mobs that are immune to spell interrupts (kick, pummel, shield bash, counterspell, feral charge, etc).

I've yet to find a boss that can't be feral charged to interrupt a spellcast. :)
