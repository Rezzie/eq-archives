The most fun one that i have tried is the group Power trial in the NE corner of map.

You have 15 minutes. You have to kill 20 mobs. The pop randomly below a center platform and you have to pull em in or they run down a ramp and depop, much like poj trials.

Once 15 minutes is up. Yellow named in center attacks and becomes targetable ( if you have killed 20 ) He isnt too hard.

BTW. dont get off the platform. you'll take a deathtouch. losing results in a lockout timer of 1hour 45mins on all trials. winning results in 1 day lockout for trial you did and 1hour 45min for rest.

Some nice loot including a belt that can drop that has right clicky Maelin's Methodical Mind ( 8mana, 5regen / tick )
