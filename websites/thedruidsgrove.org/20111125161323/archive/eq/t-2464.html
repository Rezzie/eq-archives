Believe it or not kael is the best exp hands down for a level 60 druid. I farmed / exped there constantly with minimal risk until PoP came out... which in effect killed all my velious exping... but anyway there isn't much to it. Charm a wolf and keep it under control... SotW is very nice for something such as this. The max any wolf will hit that is charmable with Call of Karana is 233... not too much but if it is hasted it could mean trouble. Charm breaking, while it may seem like death, really isn't. If you want to play it safe only do the bank area and up to the arena for some nice exp, or if you want to do exp I reccomend starting off with the Tmax ramp before going to arena. It is much easier to do CR in bank area lol. The wolves and giants only summon every 10 seconds. That is more than enough time to run away and recharm... that is always first priority unless you at 1 bub of hp lol. After that just root the giant and let your pet eat it. If you want to just farm armor then I reccomend grouping with a similar level shaman and going to arena. After shaman slow the pet regens faster than the damage given out by even Veterans. Its just a matter of getting the hang of killing summoning mobs. Most druids are afraid of such a thing, but in the end you will find yourself much richer for the experience. Enjoy kael as I once did... for it looks like the whole secret to doing it was already released lol. Try not to die and enjoy the exp.

Oh yeah there used to be an awesome bug that no longer works. /pet back off used to regen wolf to 100 percent hp. This was very nice but got nerfed really fast. I did 5 aa in around 6.5 hours doing that... which was very good at the time.

Best of luck!
