Bloodfield Ports

Rolled Discordant Parchment (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=77766) & Discordant Crystal Shard (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=77765) x3 to any regular Priest of Discord or the Elder. Both items are supposed to drop in Dranik's Scar and Bloodfields


Walls of Slaughter Ports

Dark Enchanted Scroll (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=77768) & Pure Discordant Blood (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=77767) x3 to Elder Priest of discord in Dranik's Scar. Both items are supposed to drop in Walls of Slaughter, Muramite Proving Grounds, and Nobles Causeway

You will recieve both ring and circle versions when you do the turn ins. Wizards use the same items for their ports. All the quets items are dropable and look like regular vt so might be wise to check the bazaar and also vendors.


Other Spells

Minor Muramite Rune - level 66 spells
Lesser Muramite Rune - level 67 spells
Muramite Rune - level 68 spells
Greater Muramite Rune - level 69 spells
Glowing Muramite Rune - level 70 spells

Minor, Lesser and regular Muramite Runes (lvl 66 to 68) are tradable. All runes are turned in to Karsor the Mad in Dranik's scar (sw corner of the zone -1650,-40). The spell you recieve should not be a dupe but will be no drop.
