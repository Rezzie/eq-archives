I have used DruidPartyHeal (DPH) for a while, the old (http://ui.worldofwar.net/ui.php?id=1384) and outdated version even worked with the latest 1.9.x, or 1.10 version (can't remember), I see there's a new (http://ui.worldofwar.net/ui.php?id=2396) revamped beta version out now.

Also handy are the events in this mod: when I mount I automatically put on my boots with Mithril Spurs and Carrot on a Stick (make a set first with these items), and when I'm gonna drink a pot of mana it will put on my neckless and dagger with +mana/5 sec (another set). Also when I dismount/stop drinking the original items will be put back.

Don't know the other mods here, but they look interesting.
