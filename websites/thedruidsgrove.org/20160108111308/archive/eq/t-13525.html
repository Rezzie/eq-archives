PoR The Trouble with Dirt

Get task from Borso in Relic Head Servant Hithorin in relic
kill the following in Relic
kill Xanthrin the Ring Leader "blue to 70", 900 hitter. very easy mob
Kill 10 Earth Elementals "even cons to yellow cons"

loots are

and

Melee Range

PoR The Needy
In Relic speak to Borso

Go to Arcstone and:
Kill 10 shrubs
loot 4 sacs
Kill 10 Mistwalkers
loot 4 vials
Kill 10 firewalkers
loot 4 hearts
Turn in sacs vials and hearts to Borso

Rewards are

caster Earring - http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39706
Melee Ring - http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39705


PoR Crossfire

Get quest from G'kil Zon in Relic
Kill 15 Fallen Spirits (0/15) - Arcstone
Loot 4 Arcstone Spirit Dust from Fallen Spirits (0/4) - Arcstone
Kill 15 Spirit Stags (0/15) - Arcstone
Loot 4 Spirit Stag Hoof from Spirit Stags (0/4) - Arcstone
Kill 1 the Master of the Herd (0/1) - Arcstone
Deliver 4 Arcstone Spirit Dust to G'kil Zon (0/4) - Relic
Deliver 4 Spirit Stag Hoof to G'kil Zon (0/4) - Relic

Reward is a 55/55/55 aug
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/equipment/fpics2/relic_shopkeepers_charm.jpg
