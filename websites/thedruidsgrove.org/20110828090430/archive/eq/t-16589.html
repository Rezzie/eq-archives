Hi Kett,

my main is a 76 druid with 1200 AA. I also have an 68 necro with 100 AA.
Here is how I see them:

Druid:
- versatile
- ports are very nice (but not as necessary after POK, GH, potions)
- FOE is nice, but modern mobs are often faster
- OK at root/rotting
- Quadding possible with good gear, but not very time efficient
- Charming is the best exp out there solo, but restricted to animals
- ok buffs, but mostly can be substituted by potions now
- Great Powerlevelers



Necroes:
- pets are weak - ok
- incredible dots
- nice selfhealing
- not equipment dependent at all (unless summoners)
- best kiters
- pretty good groupers
- FD still great pulling tool
- probably best exp/time of any class

Summary

Druid:
Solo: Good to very good
Group: Rarely wanted, ok sec. healer, lousy DPS
Raid: 1 is enough for debuffs, low desirebility

Necro:
Solo: Gods
Group: Always wanted
Raids: Always wanted

If you do nothing but solo, a charm killing druid can equal a necroes killing/exp rate. This is very situational however. Druids need to med more, necroes can kill constantly.

I hope this helps. Enjoy EQ again - especially with the new AA exp system, which is in my mind the best change to happen to EQ for years:
