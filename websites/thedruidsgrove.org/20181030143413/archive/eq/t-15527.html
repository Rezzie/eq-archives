Powdwar
03-09-2007, 11:02 AM
Infusible means that equipping a power source in the new slot will have an impact on the stats of that item. Be warned though that some adjustments from power sources will be positive and some negative. For example you may lose hp to gain mana. The purity number on an infusible item determines how much of an impact the power source will have on it.
