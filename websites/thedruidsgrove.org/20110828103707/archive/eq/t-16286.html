Note that there are actually a few different places your EQ folder might be found, depending on when you initially installed EQ.

\Program Files\EverQuest
\Program Files\Sony\EverQuest
\Program Files\Sony\EverQuest Trilogy

etc.

Regardless of where that folder is, there should be a "Screenshots" subfolder inside it.

If you took screenshots a number of years ago, they might be in the base EverQuest folder.
