iegil
Get flagged/levels for bastion of thunder and start looking for some ornate leather boots, sleeves, gloves, bracers etc.

Those items have excellent affects that will greatly enhance your druid.

Boots = Mana Preservation
Sleeves = Improved Heals
Gloves = Mana off on Summers Flame
Bracers = FT2

Plus overall better stats that UP or SS gear.

BoT is also an excellent source of level 63 to 65 spells.

You might want to compare a few other items such as:

Large Smoothmetal Torque
Bellows Crafted Cap
Veil of the Executioner

These are pretty common PoP loot drops that have surprisingly good stats and can be very cheap upgrades.

In addition, I'd recommend checking out some of the new Storm Rider gear (crafted). A couple of pieces are decent upgrades from Elysian and are often available in the Bazaar.
