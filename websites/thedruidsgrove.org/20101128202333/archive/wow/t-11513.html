There's no bank in the moonglade, but there is a druid trainer. He's over by one of the moonwells (not the one near where we port in).

How long is your druid flight? The alliance one takes us to darn and it's only 2:30. That's a quick way to a bank. I suspect the horde flight is much longer based on the distances between the moonglade and the horde capital cities.

In any case, Bryne is correct, Shattrah is the bank in the outlands... Once you leave HFP, start harthing to Shattrah. It's one hop to any of the areas you'll be playing in for the next few levels (Zangermarsh, Tekkar forest, zangrand, etc).
