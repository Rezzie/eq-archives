Hallo druid :D

I would suggest working up to farming Cenarion Expedition rep over to the west, in Zangarmarsh...

but first, make sure you get this idol:

Idol of the Wild (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=28064)
Binds when picked up
Unique
Relic Idol
Equip: Increases the damage dealt by Mangle
(Cat) by 24 and the damage dealt by Mangle
(Bear) by 51.75.

The quest to obtain this is in west Hellfire Peninsula, here:
http://www.wowhead.com/?quest=10132



Ok so after you've got your idol, I'd also suggest getting Bladefist's Breadth:
http://www.wowhead.com/?item=28041

Also a Hellfire Peninsula quest.


At this point, I personally would start going to the Slave Pens instance in Zangarmarsh. Keep any unidentified plant parts you find, and hand them into the npc at the Cenarion Expedition camp in east Zangarmarsh - just across the border from Hellfire.

Once you reach honored, you can't hand them in for rep anymore. At this point, do all the CE quests in Zangarmarsh, and also make sure you also do the Sporregar quest for the Underspore Frond (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=28111).

The idea is to try to get as good a rep as possible with the Cenarion Expedition - this is important because the CE quartermaster sells a lot of extremely nice druid items, such as Earthwarden (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=29171), which needs Exalted.

If you only focus on one faction while levelling, make sure it's the Cenarion Expedition. :>


-Annikk

Assuming you want to be a Feral tank or DPS...

1. Do every quest in the first 2 zones. They give you quite a nice base of gear. Especially the group[2] and group[3] quests.
2. Buy, or farm, 360 unidentified plants and turn them in at the CE town in Zangarmarsh. This will get you to honored with CE.
3. Do all CE quests in HFP, Zang, Terrok Forest, BEM and the 3 in Netherstorm.
4. Run SV a few times 'till you are exalted.
5. Buy Earthwarden for tanking
6. Get a group of 5+ lvl 65+ and run the Ring of Blood quest for a great Kitty staff.
