This quest is obtained from the same NPC as the bear form quest - Mathrengyl Bearwalker in Darnassus, and can be started at level 14. I'm going off memory for a lot of this, so please post corrections if you see something wrong.

Things to be sure to get before starting the quest: 5 earthroot.

Mathrengyl will send you to Moonglade to talk to Dendrite Starblaze. Dendrite will then send you to Darkshore, to fill a bottle with water from the cave mouth above Cliffspring Falls. There are level 16-17ish naga guarding this cave; you'll probably have to kill a few of them.

After filling the bottle, go talk to Alanndarian Nightsong in Auberdine (she's in the northernmost building). She will ask you to collect 5 earthroot and 12 lunar fungus. Earthroot you should already have, hopefully. The fungus is inside the same caves as the bear form quest, east of Auberdine; you'll probably have to go through 4-5 caves before you get enough fungus.

Returning to Alanndarian, she will want you to apply the cure to the sickly deer in Darkshore. As mentioned in the quest description, these are pretty much all over the zone, south of the river. Roam around until you find them all.

After curing 10 deer, teleport back to Moonglade, talk to Dendrite, then return to Darnassus for your new spell and a decent offhand item (http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=15866)!
