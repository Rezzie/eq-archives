Which epic .. 1.0 or 1.5? Almost all of the 1.0 can be soloed by a 63 driud. Grab a tank partner for dark elves in EK and black reaver in CoM, and a single group of 65+ folks for Faydedar (or less than a group, really just depends on their gear and classes).

For the 1.5 druid epic, you have to be at least 65th level to equip the final item. As for the mobs themselves, at least six of the encounters would be practically impossible for a 63rd level (of any class) to solo. At minimum I'd suggest a tank partner for ulthork raider in EW, swamp terror in Feerrott, piranhas in CT .. and tank+slower for the NPCs in Harbingers and the ritual conduit / juggernaut in Natimbi. You'll need at least a few well-equipped groups for earthshaker in RCoD and the final fights (blackened treant, branches, and blackened dryad) in Kith.

I don't know if the speak/hail NPCs will respond to a pre-65 with the proper quest dialogue for the 1.5... maybe someone else here knows.

Since your lvl 60, I assume your not prolly high end geared or have many friends who are, so heres my best recommendations to your needs-

Ulump is cake, just that hes 100% resistant to magic. He has the hp of a PoS mob and less dps. You should be able to take him with a tank and a melee dps (2 melee dps preferibally.)


For VS, bring a tank, cleric, a dps class (or more), and a slower. Even when I did it , with mostly people wielding epic 1's =p, i was able to do it with a enc / ranger / war / rog. I believe VS no longer LT's , or runes wont stop, tho i cant confirm.

Fay is a very overhyped mob, imo. Hes not very hard at all, bring a similar Setup as i recommended for VS and you should have it. Just keep in mind you have to be within melee range to land spells, and fay casts an AEdispell.

Corrupted gorilla spawns from a certain PH in a corner of the zone, i believe its NW corner - was an easy camp for me once i found the right PH. Perhaps if you ask your server boardsfor a MQ you could get one.
