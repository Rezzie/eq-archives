spending and bidding are different

each item in our system has a "base" or minimum price, but you are allowed to "bid" your DKP (only we dont call it that, since our system is older than DKP).

this means the more hot an item is or the more classes that can use it the more expensive it will be, because more people will "bid" the price up from "base"

we dont bid against each other for druid only loots, we just go with straight random and buy for base, so we can save our DWP for bidding against those pesky clerics and shamans and wizzies that are so annoying.
