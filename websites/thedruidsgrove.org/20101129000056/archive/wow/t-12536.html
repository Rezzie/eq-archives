One other thing to realize is that as a healer you are going to take a few hits unless you are running with a good tank, and even then stuff happens. If you do catch aggro while healing for a group, make it easy for the tank to pull the mob back off you. I run to the tank so he knows I'm getting beat on right away and can taunt the mob off me. Obviously there are situations where this would be a bad strategy, but at this level it's usually fine on non-boss fights. If the tank fails at this and lets you die more than twice...time to find a different tank or a different group.

I usually heal in groups, but the huge majority of my time is spent solo on a PvP server so my spec has evolved to 44/0/13 at 65. By focusing on +heal and +dam gear, I so far have been able to heal and solo very effectively. Self buffed my +heal is over 550 and +dam is over 350, can't remember exact numbers atm.

I think all of your listed pros are accurate, although with Moonkin Aura pulling aggro seems to be a fact of life. I also don't think any of your cons will actually be true. I tank when I duo with my brother's shadow priest with quite a bit of cloth gear, and you won't see any change in your damage output with the points you take out of balance.
