Well, I didnt see any discussion of the forage AA, so here's a start.

It adds +50 to your limit, but not to your skill. You need to skill up to actually get a skill increase.

Even if you are at the 200 max, the AA will add 50 to your forage limit and raise it to a whopping 250.

I've only had mine a little while but I spent few days specifically foraging and running around waiting for my skill to go up and all those misty acorns to fall into my hands. It didn't happen.

My skill has gone up a mere 2 or 3, and while it was probably only a few hours of foraging, I can tell you it's going to be a long wait to skill up to 250.

It took a while just to get that 1st skillup to 201 but it eventually came and I could let my breath out.

I was in Misty Thicket this morning for half an hour before work, and I actually got 2 ground forages of misty acorn, I've never had more than one in a day it seems to me, and I had no real forages of misty acorn /sigh. This was even with my foraging machette. But that's another problem
