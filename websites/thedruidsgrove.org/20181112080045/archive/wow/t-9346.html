here's a quick jist from one of the posts (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.aspx?fn=wow-druid&t=432129&p=1&tmp=1#post432129) on the forums: 1.8 Druid talent viewer in korean official homepage.

Hasn't this new talent not yet publicated in English? I searched for a while but couldnt.
I guess most of you guys can grasp roughly what our new talent will be like by icons and numbers..

Hmm.. Then I will review most interesting features in the new tree, for you guys =3

1.Moonkin form - 31th of balance tree, gives druid 360% increased armor and kind of buff aura that increases chance to get a critical strike of spells of party members within a radius of 30 yards. can use only spells of balance school in this form.

2. Leader of the pack(may be wrong) - 31th of feral tree, gives druid a kind of buff aura that increases chance to get a critical strike of melee or ranged attaks of party members within a radius of 45 yards, when druid is in cat form, bear form or dire bear form.

3. Furor and intensity are integrated into one talent. The same for fairy fire(cat, bear).

4. Omen of clarity and weapon of the nature(<-balance of the nature) are reformed. It will affect all kinds of melee attacks. Omen will get rid of not only mana cost but also rage and energy required for next attack(and healing).

5. Natural shapeshifter(<- swiftshifting) changed to 10,20,30 % instead of 20,40,60% but not require 'within 6 seconds'. In other words, this talent reduces mana cost of shapeshifts up to 30%.

6. Essence of the wild - great. intelligence increses 4%~20%. In addition stamina increases 4%~20% in bear form or dire bear form, strength increases in catform.

7. Improved healing touch - reduces the cast time of healing touch 0.1 ~ 0.5 sec.

8. Insect swarm - 11th of restoration tree. decreses target enemy's chance to hit and causes nature damage.

Other features are more understandable with ease, by icons and numbers, i guess.

And one more important information in case you dont know, hurricane will not be a talent skill any more. It can be bought by skill trainers.

Here's a comparison of the old and new restoration trees. My personal opinion is that it's sad people are complaining. The changes are overall positive, and you will get an increase in the effect of the tree even if you spend the same amount of talent points in it as previously. The only real negative change is that it will cost slightly more mana to cast HT.

Old (40 points invested):
35% extra to MoTW effects
60% chance to avoid heal interrupts
15% less mana for Healing Touch
5% extra healed by healing spells
15% extra healed by Rejuvenation
20% less threat from heals
15% mana regen while casting
80% chance to avoid Tranquility interrupt
50% extra chance to crit Regrowth
Nature's Swiftness
Innervate

New (46 points invested):
35% extra to MoTW effects
70% chance to avoid heal interrupts
15% mana regen while casting
20% less threat from heals
15% extra healed by Rejuvenation
10% less mana for Healing Touch and Tranquility
10% extra healed by healing spells
80% less threat from Tranquility
50% extra chance to crit Regrowth
Insect Swarm
Nature's Swiftness
Innervate

Changes
10% higher chance to avoid heal interrupt
5% more mana for Healing Touch
10% less mana for Tranquility
5% extra healed by all healing spells
80% higher chance for Tranquility interrupt (doesn't matter with Barkskin)
80% less threat from Tranquility
Gained Insect Swarm, a DOT/debuff
