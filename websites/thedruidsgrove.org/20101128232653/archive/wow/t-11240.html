The ranks are harder to achieve as you go higher... significantly harder. The grind from 4 to 8 is difficult. You basically need to be PvPing as much as you can to get as much honor as you can.... and you're on a deadline. If you miss rank 8 before the next patch, you're screwed. All ranks go away and you're back at ground zero.

If you think you can make rank 8 in the next 4-6 weeks, go for it... if not, wait for the patch and grind honor points from there.

Now with all that said, I _LOVE_ my PvP gear... especally the 4 piece bonus: 15% speed increase in form is bloody amazing. With FS you now stealth faster than people walk.

I definitely suggest you should go for it... just decide on which side of the patch you'll try for it.
