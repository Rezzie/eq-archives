11-03-2006, 01:01 PM
I did almost all my signets duo'ing (with my warrior) and it was a cakewalk. The only one I got in a group was RSS and that was while camping RS for AC augs for my tank.

A couple suggestions/advices -

[1] When you get the catacombs instance, get Catacombs C - the instance giver will mention a named Pixxt. In the spawn room, there's a noc against the wall with a little cubby-like area in the wall. Kill him in there, park yourself in there and that'll be the only mob you'll agro or have to kill until the Insidious Dragorn spawns.

[2] For the Sewers, you need Sewers C - again, the instance giver will mention a named Ukun. Make sure you bring a stack of invis to undead pots with you... you can make it through a lot of the zone invis until you get to the undead murkgliders. Sewers signet PH is "a twisted stoneworker". He's just past the undead murks. Once you get to him, you'll just have to kill him and one roaming undead murk.

[3] For Hollows, due to SoE's screwup, you'll have to shroud down to 70 to get the Historian to give you the quest. Once you have the instance though, you can gate back and unshroud and then go in and slaughter. I found that the easiest to get was the Fire Pits, but I've also heard the Watering Hole is another easy one. I was able to invis all the way in to the camp without a single problem in Fire Pits.

As for time - it took me about 4 hours for Hollows, 2 for Catacombs and 2 for sewers. It was just kill PH, wait 18 minutes, get ready, kill, rinse + repeat.
