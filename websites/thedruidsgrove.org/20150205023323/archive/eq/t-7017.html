Hmm, I'm level 41, KEI??? Who needs it (can't get it anyway)? As far as quadding, there are several posts here, but to put in my two cp worth, the first question I guess would be how much wisdom/mana you have? If you don’t have at least good wisdom, I’m guessing around 180 or more then I might be concerned with running out of mana before the quad is successfully finished. And how good your gear is is also a good question, but at level 32, you should have enough stats/gear to be successful. Get your Solstice Earring (FT1) as that will help.<BR>
I started quadding in Iceclad by the igloos (just northwest of the dock). You can run cougars back and forth just south of the igloos for 3 or 4 levels at least. They resist snare occasionally and also will draw an add if not careful, but good EXP. No real loot except the cougar claw earring (Mama Pres I), which sells for 100-200pp in the bazaar. After that I moved to the big Island in Iceclad where I did wolves and Giants (you’d better have LOT’S of mana for Giants,). Giants can also be root/rotted there as well for soloing. Giants have good loot and occasionally some drops for the Prayer Shawl quests if you’re interested.<BR>
If you’re not sure, try Gnolls in SK first. They are real easy, drop some loot and weapons and are just plane fun to learn to quad. Aviaks as well in SK for quadding.<BR>
After that try EW…lot’s of solo and quad mobs there (be careful of the Mystic of Ry’Gor if quadding them, he’s a caster and rooted me yesterday with 3 mobs in my pull…VERY UGLY. He hangs out in a hut just off the bridge in EW). Anyway, good luck.
