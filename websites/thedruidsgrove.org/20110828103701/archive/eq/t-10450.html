Do not worry about doing those faction quests. They have been said to be pathetic compared to faction from killing. Also faction is the same no matter the level of the mob you kill. Fighting in RCoD or Dranikscar will not matter except in dranikscar you will get faster kills. Two best ways of getting faction are this:

1) Group in Ruined City of Dranik. There are nice drops there for quests you can complete when you get the faction as well as armor you can sell for a nice price in the bazaar. NW Camp is the best place for faction / exp and this camp is arguably better exp than most MPG groups (Unless you are in a really great group). Mobs are easier here so they die faster. I would recommend factioning this way...levels / aa and faction all at the same time.

2) Go To Draniksscar and form up an AE Group. Have a puller pull a lot of the newb mobs here and just AE them down. I have noticed an AE group isn't even needed for this. As a druid, grouping with a ranger, beast, and cleric. I pulled mobs in waves of 8 per pull, cleared camp, then pulled again. We set up near the lookout place where bloodfields zone is out. mobs here are easy and fast kills which = best faction.

Good luck in factioning..it takes forever..grouping in RCoD from level 65-70 will almost get you to the faction you need for tier1.
