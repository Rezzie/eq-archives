I would vote in favor of OOW over DON for solo play. Nobles causeway is a very good zone for the solo druid, there are two zone wide random drops there that are excellent, plus a quest cloak can be done very easily solo (once you kill enough dragorn to get faction). Tier one armor is also a possibility, altho not solo at your level.

The aug drops are mostly very rare random drops, but are a nice plus. There are a couple augs in dranic scar that you could solo, one is 5 to all stats, other is saves.

OOW definitely offers more for the solo druid, especially one not at 70 yet.
