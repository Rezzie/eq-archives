For the moment only enchants that boost stats work in forms, not any that are chance on hit or occasionally procs.

There is some debate regarding the patch-notes reference to being able to use items in forms after 1.10, but that will not likely include weapon procs, but "use" items such as trinkets or helm of fire.

All procs work for druids while actually using you melee weapon of course, such as in caster or moonkin form, and mana regen enchants work even when in feral forms.
