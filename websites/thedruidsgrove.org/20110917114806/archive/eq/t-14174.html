I did a combination of Vxed and PoStorms at 63-66. The Loroks (The frogs you want to be charming) in PoS are not very mana or gear intensive. Vxed will be very slow for you with the resists and low Mana pool and regen, but doable. HoH Quadding will be very difficult.

Something to always keep in mind is that many suggestions on soloing spots should come with footnote saying "Assuming XX level of gear and YY AAs".
Work on getting your mana regen and mana pool up. Make use of Tribute for Focus Effects that you don't have, namely Burning Affliction.

There are dozens of other threads for soloing past 65ish on this forum. Sadly, RootRot is king outside of a few Charming and Quadding spots. I've been reading through them for myself. Good luck! :D

I have soloed from level 1 to 68. From 63-65 definitely go to POS and charm loroks. You charm one and let it fight another, then break charm (innate camo AA) to get exp on both mobs. (This I heard was nerfed later and you need to do 1/3 of the damage in order to get exp. But I cannot confirm this).
Spell memed: best quick heal, glamour, level 63 charm, root, ensnare (or level 61 mine thorn from DON merchants. Less resists but only lasts for 3 min and need refresh) , dots, egress.
Buff: poison resists (loroks proc poison dots), SOE, skin, regen.
Stay in 63 and get important AAs before advancing. AA is faster at 63 than 64/65. Exodus, SOTW1 are very good.

After 65 the loroks drop out of the 5-level range so go to HOH to root-dot (or quad if you have enough mana). The humans at the basements are level 62 and give better exp. Black armors are level 61 so don't bother with them. Only kill valorian guards/guardians. Do not try sentries since they cannot be snared -- unless you want to run and kite it for many minutes.
