I don't play eq anymore, but here's an idea: Let your high level druid pull 5 mobs, group them together, AE (spiral spore) root them (not sure if you can root 4 or 5 at a time, I think it's 5) and they will beat on you. Meanwhile let your warrior damage them a bit and he will get the exp.

Low level shroud form has penalty, so don't shroud for too low. I think the best level is the highest one with mobs still con red. So let's say your druid can take 5 level 55 mobs at a time, then shroud the war to level 50.
