Do you just have one set of gear, or do you have a set for dps as well as tanking?

I'm going to go ahead and assume that the gear I see you in now is your tank set.

A few notes about your existing stats.

1. You are above the crit cap. Even without that elixir of ironskin. You can afford to drop some resilience or defense skill.

2. Honestly this is easily geared enough to do lower karazhan, if not all of karazhan. It's also sufficient as an offtank in early 25 man instances.

3. Your stats are generally well balanced, however your agility is very low. Agility is a very important stat for feral druids, it gives armor, dodge, crit, attack power, and in patch 2.4 if specced for it will also give you +healing and +healing received (in cat form). Go for upgrades to your gear that feature increases in agility.

4. Earthwarden, as you say, is the logical next step. However, you also might like to consider this ring (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30834#sold-by) available from exalted Lower City faction, which is extremely nice for tanking (and for dps too if you have nothing better).

5. If you have a dps set of gear, once you have 2800+ attack power and about 32% crit, start stacking hit rating until you reach the cap, which is 142. Continue to stack stats after that but be on the lookout for items that give expertise - the expertise rating cap is 89 and this will significantly increase your dps once you're in the further reaches of SSC/TK. It's also important for tanking as it reduces the mob's chance to dodge and parry. When a mob parries your attack, its next swing comes faster, and this creates a damage spike. If you reach the cap, mobs will never dodge and never parry. This also serves to increase your threat generation.

6. And yea... make sure you have multiple sets of gear for different roles. Pvp, healing, tanking, dps... all require different types of gear. You don't need defense skill on dps gear, for example. Don't be one of those druids who is "only bear" or "only cat". Embrace the chromacity my furry friend.. :>

7. The Idol of Terror is available from G'eras in Shattrath City for 15 heroic tokens. At the time of writing this is the best tanking idol. Everbloom idol is sold by the same NPC, also for 15 tokens, and is the best dps idol.
