Deiffin, a lot of DON good equipments require level 70 to wear. Think about it: you don't want to spend a lot of money for an upgrade, only find out later that you need upgrades again. So level to 70 fast (doing the HHK mm will get you there in several days).

You can skip most of the level 63-65 spells and buy level 68 ones, they are better -- except for mask of the forest I think.

For the focus effects you need the most for soloing:

Mana preservation V (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/spell.html?spell=6419)
Muram's Anger(http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/spell.html?spell=6426)
Extended range V (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/spell.html?spell=6413)

There is a list of items under each of the focus effects, so you have a choice -- make sure they are dru useable :).
