You will receive 1 talent point for each level you gain. Once you hit 40, you should have a total of 31 points. However, since you're deep into the feral tree right now, you will have to respec your talents at a druid trainer (at a cost). Basically, you will be refunded 31 talent points and from there you will be able to put them in the balance tree and get Moonkin.
