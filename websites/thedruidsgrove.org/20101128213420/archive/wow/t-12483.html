I looked at your armory, it looks likes you logged in a mix of tanking & dps gear.

First few questions:
- Are you uncrittable?
- What's your armor at?

Bear tanks excel at taking consistent and predictable damage, with very little spike damage. You should have somewhere between 20-25k AC at this point, and probably around 10 - 11k HP.

Lower than 10k HP and you're really risking spike damage issues, especially in heroics.

Personally I geared myself like this:
Def to uncrittable
stam
agi / dodge
