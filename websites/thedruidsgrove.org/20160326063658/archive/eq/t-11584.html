I am on Cazic Thule and I just wanted to say a HUGE thank you to the guild "The Relentless". They are the top guild on this server, yet they have helped me out many times even though I am only lvl 56. They dont know me at all, and each time they assist me it is different folks from the guild.
They have patience and are always eager to help. Many think that a top guild would have "stuck up" attitudes, but these folks are the nicest and they are what keeps EQ alive.
On many occasions they have reached out to me to join them in a hunt, even though i am only lvl 56 and i am not equipped to fight the mobs they do. But they also included me in their loot. They taught me how to be a Druid and how to fight.
Basically, these folks are what EQ is all about. Being there for each other.
When I lose faith in this game and get frustrated and wish to quit, I just think of how there are folks out there like "The Relentless" and it keeps my spirits up. I know this game will survive for a long time as long as these folks are around.
What makes them special is that they Volunteer their services, I have never reached out to them first. They reach out to me first and other lower level folks also. That's what makes them so great.
Thank you "The Relentless" for everything :)
