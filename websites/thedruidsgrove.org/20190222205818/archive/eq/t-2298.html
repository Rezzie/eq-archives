Talk to Trep Thilcan he is sitting a little ways from Williamenia in PoK. His quest is he wants to set up shop where he’s sitting but is afraid Williamenia might take his spot so he wants you to go get supplies for him. After a conversation he gives you a six slot box to fill with his supplies and sends you on your way. You travel to FV, Qeynos, Thurgadin, Shadweaver, Freeport, and Bazaar to buy the supplies (cost around 6 plat a piece, wear CHA gear and price will go down a plat) and supplies are no drop. Combine in a box and bring it back to Trep. He gives you 65 plat as a reward and I got at level 61 2% alternate experience for about 20 minutes work. Check Alakazam for in depth quest details.
