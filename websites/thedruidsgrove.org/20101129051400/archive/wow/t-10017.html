The arena is great fun!

Not long ago when I was at a (warcraft) party, we had 3 computers all set up in the same room. It was late at night and we got a bunch of guildies to come down to the Arena. I invited everyone to a raid and then we had some awesome team battles... the biggest was a 5v5v5. Everyone heads into a corner of the arena with their group, then they get a few minutes planning time... I'd do a countdown then yell GO! and then everyone tries to pillage each other. We had a priest standing off to one side to res people who died, and 2 lvl 60 Shamans appeared (played by people on the other 2 computers in the room I was in), and they agreed to be "bodyguards", killing anyone who tried to interfere during the battle.

We did get a few people arriving, who decided to join in.. after the shamans had disposed of them, I invited them to the raid and included them in the next battle. It was really awesome, there was a lot of frenzied shouting in the room I was in, hehe..

Also, sometimes I go to the arena without having any interest in the contents of the chest - I go there simply for the pvp aspect of it. Sometimes people see me waiting there and I'll get whispers from lower level players asking if I need the chest, or if they can group with me and take it if we win. I always say yes, on the condition that they leave at least 1 item in the chest so it doesn't disappear.

My advice to anyone who is looking to get the chest is to go there 20 minutes early, find the toughest looking player, and whisper them to ask if they need the chest.. if you are polite, and they don't need it, you may well get invited to their group and wind up with one or more extremely powerful allies.. :)
