Ya. You're fine. Off to a good start actually.

I don't know your Kara group and / or their experience, but the more practice with the mechanics of the fights you and your party have the easier it will get.

As for drops, after several successful 1st half of Kara runs, you should be very well geared. I recommend you save your honor points / badges to replace gear that simply won't drop for you. (Prince's Mace, for example).

And +Healing is the ticket. Mana will follow. Use whatever gadgets you can to improve both. Superior Mana Oil is one of my faves (cheaper than Flasks of Mighty Restoration - although they simply rock as well). You can also use Elixirs and Fishsticks for extra boost, but depending on how many times you wipe, it can get expensive (if you're not an alchemist). I prefer to use mine for boss fights only.

Anyway...soon you'll have it on farm...and you're gonna love it.
