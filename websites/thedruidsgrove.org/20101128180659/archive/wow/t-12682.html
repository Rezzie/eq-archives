There are still groups of elites on the way, 8 total I think? You'll have at least 1 group of birds + 1 or 2 groups of the other adds. If you're having trouble with the gauntlet as it is I'm not sure your group could really handle all that.

If you have a prot pally, gathering the birds is easy. Add a mage + lock to aoe and it's over. Bring a hunter for the frost trap to slow things down a bit more if the tanks need more time to pick things up.

If you don't, just make sure you kill the adds fast and move to the elites. You want to chain pull, constantly trying to move forward so as the 2 are dying you're pulling the next group.

Ok... its almost dirty telling this trick, but there's a way to pull the tempest first.

If you look at the waterfall to the right, you go up by the gauntlet on the right. There will be some tall rocks at the base of the steep hill. If you have a hunter, you have them stand by the rocks and do a /target Amani'shi Tempest. They send their pet and then back up. The tempest will kill the pet and agro on the hunter. The tanks intercept the Tempest (he may come with one of the groups of casters and protectors.) The you can go up the guantlet with no risk to the group for birds etc. Just move quickly.

Yes I'm aware this isn't the way the guantlet is meant to work and yes I'm sure Blizzard never intended people to be able to do this.
