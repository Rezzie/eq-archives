From what i can tell by your specc/gear its a +hit rating issue. your currently at an effective +7% spell hit and in raids (specifically on boss level mobs) you going to need 17% to cap it. in fact i believe 7% is a full 1% lower then the hit cap on +1 mobs (correct me if i'm wrong) and 4-5% lower then +2 mobs. That believe it or not is an absolutely HUGE factor to your dps. Such a large factor, in fact, that you would do well to focus solely on acquiring +hit gear and or echantments. there are two specific trinkets that come to mind which would give you an additional 140 hit rating (or thereabotus), one from Heroic VH and the other is a quest out of wyrmrest temple. These two trinkets alone would bring you up about a full 5-6%. from there you can try speccing into IFF for the +3% hit. Even losing spellpower and crit from your trinkets would still yield you a greater dps return based on the +hit.
