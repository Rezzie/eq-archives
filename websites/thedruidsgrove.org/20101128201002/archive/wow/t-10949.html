Black Fathom Deeps, I believe it is, wouldn't be too bad for your level, either. And, if you ever want to get ahold of a nice +stamina and +intellect belt, the final boss drops the "Moss Cinch." The guy before that drops a good staff, as well.

To make it easier on you (I may have massacred some of the spellings):

Black Fathom Deeps is in Northwestern Ashenvale.

Shadow Fang Keep is in Mid-Western Silverpine Forest (Take the boat from Auberdine to Menethil, then follow the road north to Arathi Highlands. From there, take the road west to Hilsbrad, pick up the flight path at Southshore. Continue West/North-west to Silverpine)

Gnomeregan is in the Mid-Western area of Dun Morogh. I'm sure you've been to Ironforge for your Aqua Form quest, so take the gryphon from Menethil to IF, then walk out into the snowy land, and travel West.

Razorfen Downs... I'm not sure of the exact, having never done it, but I believe it is in Southern Barrens (South of Eastern Ashenvale)--Do NOT mistake for Razorfen Kraul.

Scarlet Monastery I would not advise, personally. It's lowest leveled section is the Graveyard, and that's levels 29-34ish. I suggest going to Scarlet Monastery at level 33+. Anyway, to get there it's basically the same drill as Shadowfang... except now head north to Trisifal Glades. Scarlet Monastery is in the top North-Eastern corner... Be careful to avoid Undercity and Bree, as well as the Deathguards. AKA, try to avoid the road. Also, there is a shortcut to Trisifal. In Southshore, head due north to Alterac Mountains. Once there, head West to Dalaran; do not enter the snowy regions--stick to the grass. Follow the lake's side until you reach the far northern area, and then swim across the lake, in aqua form, past the castle. This puts you in Trisifal Glade.

Hope that was of some help.
