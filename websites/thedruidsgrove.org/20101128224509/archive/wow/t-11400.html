I don't know if you WANT all 6 pieces. The breastplate, hat, and hands there's no competition for, so there's your 3 piece bonus. With the recent bump to armor on the epics, the boots are now, I think, clearly better than the AB boots, but if you can't do better than the defiler's belt, you may want those boots anyway for the +run speed and 3 piece +1% crit set bonus.

The shoulders are a hybrid piece with all 5 stats and a SP bonus, whereas AB has awesome leather shoulders for less honor that are clearly better for physical damage. Going to go pick those up tonight and finally ditch my freaking Atal'ai Spaulders.

It's also a toss-up as to whether the pants are better than the WSG pants. The rank pants give 24 STR and some int/spi, but the WSG pants have 10 more AGI, 9 more STA, and 15 more armor. It's really a tank/dps thing, but I'd consider the Outrider ones carefully, given that they're cheaper.

While I'm on the subject, don't overlook the epic bracers from WSG, they're very nice as well, and it's hard to get good feral bracers.

BTW, if you go with the rank pants, and you're looking at a potential 6/6 bonus for the rank set, then it's a lot closer between the AB and rank pieces.

If you can get into and through ZG/AQ20, it changes the picture entirely. I went with the Belt of Preserved Heads from ZG, Chitonous Shoulderguards & Thick Silithid Chestguard from AQ20. Grab the bracers and the pants from WSG (get the druid pants. the rogue ones aren't worth the sacrifice in secondary stats. no need to be one-dimensional).

That leaves gloves, helm, and boots from the General's/Warlord's set. This gets you the speed bonus and the 20 stam bonus. Nice =]

Grab the Amulet of the Darkmoon for a nice solid neck (or the ZG neck if you're exalted) Throw on a ZHM and a couple PvP rings and you're looking at a solid set.

Granted, this is much easier to pull off when you have a Mace of Unending Life. But even with a Warden Staff, this set comes out pretty wicked, offering you more than just a little survivability. You can use rings, trinkets, and cloak to change from bear to cat (stam to attack pwr/agi/str).

As for enchants? Honestly, I have stam on boots, agi on gloves, dodge on helm, stealth on cloak, HP on chest, (here i cheat) stam/str on bracers. Yes, I bought two pairs and gave them different enchants. I also put the +30 attack power on the shoulders. I put str on my one hander and my Warden Staff has agi.

I find that I have a ton of viability in this set. I can deal a great deal of damage, run flags, soak up damage in bear form. . . very multi-faceted. I even have some mana to heal a few times.
