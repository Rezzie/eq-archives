So in just over 3 hours 3 bards could take 100 mobs providing they don't summon.

Near the south end of the zone, about 1/2 the mobs summon. Eventually a yellow con (i.e. not harmony-able) summoning mob will spawn and you're screwed.

Cragbeasts, hynids, and pookas do not summon.

Stonemites, ikaavs, golems and most (but not all) of the humanoid things summon.

Near the north end of the zone (where the hail mob is) I would say 3/4 or more of the mobs summon. They max hit for 1050 or so, some run and bard speed, and some snare, so there's really no realistic chance of training to the hail mob.

Basically, if you want to win, you gotta have a tank. Although with some finesse and a little bit of luck, an enchanter pet can work as your tank.

Oh and mobs have in the neighborhood of 22k hps.
