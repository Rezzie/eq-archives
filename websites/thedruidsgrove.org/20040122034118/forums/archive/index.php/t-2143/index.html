Quelm
There are some nice solo spots, but most of my "Must-See" areas are more fun with an appropriately levelled group of friends. These include:

Guk 20-45
Kaesora 35-45
Howling Stones 45-60
Dragon Necropolis 50-65
Veksar 55-65

Some of these solo spots are also fun with a group:

Stonebrunt Mountains
Emerald Jungle
Chardok
Western Wastes
The Grey
Umbral Plains
Plane of Nightmares
Plane of Tactics

Most of these areas won't be available until after level 40 or so, I'm not sure what to recommend before then. Paludal is interesting, and has a nice xp modifier. If I were you, I'd try to find folks for a group if at all possible. Good luck!

As for quests, I strongly recommend:
Thurg/Kael/SS bracer (even if you have to MQ it) :)
Circlet of the Falinkan
Epic!
Entrance to Plane of Tactics
Rallic Pack
Amulet of the Grey Wastes
