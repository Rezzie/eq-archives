06-01-2008, 02:31 PM
I prioritize stats this way:

1. +heal ~ 10
2. int/spirit ~ 5 each

and ignore the rest as they tend to take care of themselves. Spirit is better than raw mp5 because of Innervate, and Int is dual purpose since 2.4 because it boosts mp5 quite a bit as well as increasing your mana pool.

With the use of potions and innervate, unbuffed mp5 > 100 while casting (from mousing over the mana regen stat in your character screen) is enough to start healing Kara and heroics.

Most fights (95% +) are too short for mp5 to be a factor. In those fights the size of your mana pool is far more important, especially if you have to throw some big heals. You could patch heal Kara (without dps'ing) with only 6k mana and +1000 heals, but you would have a very difficult time healing heroics unless the pulls were very controlled to limit incoming damage on the tank and/or the party.

As far as spec I ended up deciding to go 0/0/61 because I am a noob and found that I really could not afford to start tree healing without every healing tool I could carry. I don't think my spec is unique at all but FWIW. (http://www.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Kel%27Thuzad&n=Oiysters)

Soloing is rather hellish but as I get better at my job I may be able to respec to something more utilitarian later. Until then I just group for dailies with friends or work on an alt.

Hope this helps!
