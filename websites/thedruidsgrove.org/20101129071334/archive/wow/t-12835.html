I've done a fair bit of Moonkin pvp, and although my playstyle may differ from that of others, I've found the best tactic is to play like a sniper.

The main nuke spell I use is Starfire, for the simple reason that it comes out of the sky - as opposed to Wrath, which comes from the Moonkin, and instantly shows the enemy your location.

Try hiding behind a tree or some other natural cover, and sniping. Half the time I find people simply twirl about on the spot, trying to work out where the hell the Moonkin is. Then they die. :>
