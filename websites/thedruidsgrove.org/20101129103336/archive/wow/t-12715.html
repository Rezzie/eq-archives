Maiden is a really simple fight as a healer/dispeller when you have SmartDebuff (cleansing addon) and BigWigs or DeadlyBossMods running. My original main is a holy priest that can dispell magic just as a pally can, just by clicking on a little highlighted icon, which means no worries about her holy fire, cos it's dispelled even b4 it can hit if you are quick. DBM will warn you when the repetance is gonna take place to 1/10 of a sec. Place yourself on the first step of the round stair around her, and 1 sec b4 repetance is going off , step forward, take 1 hit of her AOE, repetance will break, take a step back and continue healing as if nothing happend :thumbsup: , your MT will not even loose much HP, as you should HoT him up b4 repentance starts.
As a side note, we once did Maiden with 10 druids, 3 Feral, 3 resto and 4 boomkin, we had an "omg" fight, cos noone could dispell the holy fire and we had to heal through it, fight lasted over 5 mins, which is really long, but TS was filled with laughter and joy, cos our cuddly bears needed almost no healing :grin: druids ftw
