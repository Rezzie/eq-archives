You are confusing the Dire Maul class trinket (level 58-60) with level 50 Class quests. The DM class trinket (10 Fire resist +44 healing for Druids - all classes get 10 Fire Resist +something) is obtained by getting the appropriate class book (Greatest Race of Hunters, etc.) from either a Dusty Tome or random boss drop in DM. Simply take the item to the library in DM and turn it in for the trinket.

The level 50 quests start at your trainer, and most refer you to Azshara for some additional questing, culminating with the final step taking place in Sunken Temple (Temple of Atal'Hakkar). Some final steps are to obtain drops from trolls, others involve killing one of the drakes (e.g. Morphaz).

There is no class quest for druids at level 50.
