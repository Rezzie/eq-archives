Moonglade gear is fine. You'll be low on intellect but that's normal for the early raiding druid healer. Just make sure your gear is fully buffed with gems and glyphs and enchants.

Exalted rep will is your friend:
-Exalted Sha'tar for the healing mace
-Exalted CE rep for the healing off hand
-Exalted Keepers of Time for the cloth healing bracers
(Yes they're cloth but they're decent)
-Exalted Scryer or Aldor rep for the +healing shoulder enchant

Also get the +35 healing glyph for your helm from Honor Hold rep. And get the +70 healing trinkets from Lower City Rep. And there is at least one decent +heal ring you can get through rep faction I think.

For gems go with the [+28% healing -2% threat] meta in your helm. Use +18 healing red gems where you can, and hybrid (+healing +spirit +int +mp5) gems elsewhere to help fulfill the meta gem requirements.

For enchants go with +heal on your gloves, bracers, and weapon, +mp5 on your chest, vitality (+mp5) to boots, and subtlety (-2% threat) to cloak.

For other gear, don't forget the healing leather belt from Arcatraz.
