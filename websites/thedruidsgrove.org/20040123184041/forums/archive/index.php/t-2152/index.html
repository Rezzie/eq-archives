ccLothar
Good start on spells. That 63 heal is nice. Use that and TR as your healing one, two punch. Groups in PoP need a slower, tank, and usually crowd control plus random other. You can be the healer or an other. If you are the healer, start your CHs really early(like 75%), until the mob is slowed. This of course depends on what class and how good your tank is. As far as surviving, play and have fun. Most mobs run, so remember to snare. A horse would be a good thing. When you do zone into PoV invis up and run around. Load the map and learn it, and where all the camps are. If you get a choice, Inner Cave is the best XP in the zone. Try really hard not to get agro. Stuff in this zone can remove 1k hipoints in a blink. Just use your standard grouping instincts and you'll do fine.

P.S. Most of the ubers have moved on to other zones; and don't let the people you do find intimidate you.

Good luck and have fun.

Usually depends on the Plane.

Please note that if the holy trinity isn't mentioned, they will still apply.

Valor:
Can holy trinity, aggro kite, charm tank, ping-pong ... It's DL, KC and Velks all rolled into one.

Storms:
Same as Valor. Kiters are limited to forest frogs. Holy trinity for everything else on account of summoning mobs.

Torment:
Don't go there. Just ... DON'T. If you don't go crazy from the static comming out of your speakers ... Saryn will find some other way.

CoD:
Standard Holy Trinity.
Charmed tankers + rangers/nukers (non-melee) groups are possible

Bastion of Thunder:
Holy Trinity for melee type giants (summoners)
Can aggro kite elementals and casters as they don't summon

HoH:
Kiters paradise. Aggro kiting snareables. Ping-pong unsnareables.
