Humming Finsternacht Rune are the turn in runes for Rank III level 76 Spells. Order is as follows:

1: Torrid Sunray Rank III

Whirring Clockwork Rune are the turn in runes for Rank III Level 77 and 78 Spells. Order is as follows:

1: Puravida Rank III

4. Chillvapor Breath Rank III
5. Viridifloral Bulwark Rank III
6. Ironwood Skin Rank III
7. Forbear Corruption Rank III (Note you progress to 78 spells here)
8. Skin to Mulch Rank III
9. Horde of Fireants Rank III
10. Viridithorn Coat Rank III

Level 79 & 80 spells from Glowing Prismatic Rune (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=52679)

1. Tectonic Upheaval Rank III
