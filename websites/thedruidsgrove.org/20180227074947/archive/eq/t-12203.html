At that level (back in early 2002), I quadded the tro jegs in Scarlet Desert (up on the plateau). There are plenty to pull from, and you can root DoT the casters singly. Also, there is a new concept in EQ of "hot spots" (zones with an extra large ZEM, for better XP), and Scarlet Desert is one of them :)

Once you get 58 and Fist of Karana (I think that's the name), you could head to ME to quad the toilers, but stay in SD and do the Tro Jegs as long as they're all dark blue.

The other thing worth mentioning is that you might be better off root/rotting or charm-fighting in the PoP zones. With the higher ZEMs and bonus XP (you get bonus XP when you are 51+ and kill mobs that are 51+ and within 5 levels of you.

Oh, that reminds me of another thing! ;)

Another hot spot right now is the Grey. In the far NW corner of the zone is a small lake with giant skeletons (forget the names). They are easy single pulls, and pretty easy to root/rot, and they are all level 55. So you would get bonus XP, extra hot spot XP, and a relatively safe zone to hunt in.

Better yet, try them all out, and see which you like best :)
