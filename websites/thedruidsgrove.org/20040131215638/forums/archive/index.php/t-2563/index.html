Palarran
No conflicting stories here...

It used to be that focus damage was doubled on a crit. If you had a 1000 point nuke, and a focus item added 200 to it (for the moment forget that it's random), the nuke would normally do 1000+200=1200 damage, and on a crit it would do 2*(1000+200)=2400 damage. As posted above, this changed early on in PoP. Now a crit does (2*1000)+200=2200 damage. Without crit damage being doubled, or the possible upcoming 45% focus effect, it's impossible for a druid to nuke for 4k, while it was briefly when PoP first came out.
