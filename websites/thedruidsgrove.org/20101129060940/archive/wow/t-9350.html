I had problems with them until I got Feral Charge. Now in duels, it's rare if I lose them. In normal PvP, whoever gets the drop on the other has a big advantage.

Improved Bash can take the hunter out of the fight long enough to Hibernate their pet. Root the hunter, and do what you will. Worst comes to worst, you can always root the pet with Nature's Grasp, and concentrate on the hunter.

Beast Master specd hunters have a new ability - Pet stun - available at 21 points in Beastmastery tree. This is very hard to counter in a duel, as it interrupts casting and is on a 30 second cooldown iirc.

Any hunter that is NOT Beastmaster spec I find quite easy. Just mez the pet, then root them and get in the "dead zone". The zone between their Ranged and Melee ranges.

The main thing I find is to not let them drain you of your mana with Viper Sting or you're in trouble.

Even Beastmater specced Hunters are not that hard, it just means you're going to take a little more punishment than the other specs. I rarely go into feral forms at all when fighting Hunters. Mez, Root, Moonfire, Wrath, Starfire is all I really need.
