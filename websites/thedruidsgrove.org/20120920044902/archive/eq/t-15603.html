The pit in Tactics is a good source for SP. There is an alternative task to get you flagged to that area.

First go to POStorm to kill giants to get your faction up (if a group is killing named for flags you can tag along to get faction hits). Then go to the sand area of POS to kill diaku's. There are 4 of them, each carry one corner of the turn in piece (mobs have the same name but are different calsses, so you may need to kill more than 4 to get the whole set). The killing and factioning don't need the whole group, just enough people to get the job done (druids can even solo them). Once you collected the whole set, gather your group to do turn in. The whole group gets the flag to Tactics. Only the turn in person needs a good faction (enough to get the task from Wembly the Forlorn ). If MQ by two people then both need good faction.

Ordinary mobs in the pit should be an easy kill. Named are difficult at your levels, and be careful of the stempede.
