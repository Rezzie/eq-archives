I'd say the biggest druid-specific change has been the addition of the spell Skin of the Reptile. It is a short duration targetted buff that adds a "defensive proc"--a proc that has a chance of firing every time a mob swings at that player. On average the proc fires about once (per mob) every six seconds. The proc heals the player for 600 with all hate going to that player instead of the druid.

We also got Moonshadow, a group heal (and its upgrade, Lunarlight). It's crippled by an 18 sec recast time, but it's still a significant improvement for times when a druid needs to heal against AE damage.

Our damage output is still on the weak side, though there have been a couple relatively recent improvements in this area.
