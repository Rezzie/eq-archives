I have read a few times about turning on name plates for multi-mob tanking situations and can certainly see how that can help. What I am not a fan of though is how big the frame itself is. Does anyone know of any mods that manipulates this frame?

For those that don't know, name plates hover over mobs/toons heads and displays their name and health. I believe the default on/off toggle is shift+v or maybe just v. The interface allows three options under the targeting section: show all, show friendly, show enemy. At least if you turn on enemy you don't get "spammed" by seeing everyone's.
