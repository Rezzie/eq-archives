I am recently returned myself, and have been leveling up a Druid, just got 51. From reading this forum and others, I have managed to get a few key pieces of advice:

1: With upgraded bazaar gear, you will do better than you think in PoI. 1a: Skip the warrior mercenary - I have done extremely will with a healer merc and playing the tank mage (DS potion stacks with druid ds, pull, dot, nuke away while the merc heals.)

2: Grinding out AA along the way, seems to be the best idea. AA > Level these days. If you get too high a level without any AA it's much more difficult to kill anything that gives you xp.

1) Same group role as before. Healer usually in PUG's while DPS/Utility/Back up healer in groups with friends. Nothing really has changed
2) Wild Growth (Level 81+ spell) and Lion's Str line are the only ones I can think of that you didn't include.
3) Their all crucial. Focus on one line or focus on all of em. In the end you need em all.
4) For Druids, AA > Level as we need many AA's to do either of our jobs reliably
5) I would grab the Tank Merc. Make sure its the Journeyman level one though.
6) If by pets your referring to our lvl 55 one (Boo-Boo) then yup, he was a joke by Sony/Verant and nothing more. We get a decent Swarm Pet and Charming seems to be where the Druid is heading for a class solo wise.

Extra info -
AA level's. 55, 59, 62, or 65. Most of our "core" AA's start at 55, the rest start at 59. Also at 59 you can wear Elaborate Armour, very over powered vs. content you will most likely xp in. 62 Provides you with Charm so you can master that for later levels. It is also possible to charm before this in PoDesease and Nightmare but I didn't personally. At 65, I would stay here until you have ALL the AA's you think you will need. 66-70 can be skipped over and you can begin AA'ing at 74/75 again. If you where able to get your spells in the 66-68 range. Staying 71 to gain max AA xp per kill solo is advisable.
Other things to consider. You gain a nice snare at level 61 in DoN expansion. At 61 you gain the ability to recieve the best Cleric, Chanty, and Shaman Buffs. These will make your Merc vs. DB's godly :thumbsup:

First option comes into mind is to shroud down to level 45 bear warrior and use shaman made DS potion and some POK bought buff potions (HOT, +AC) to solo some level 35ish undead mobs. Kills will be very fast and non-stop. Gulf of Gunthak is good, lower Guk maybe good too (not sure about this one since I only PL'ed people here).

If you like to charm kite, go with PON werewolves once you reached level 53 (spell: glamour of tunare will greatly enhance charm success rate). This place is good in that you have multiple charm target (read: you can kill your pet and gain experience), not like other similar spots where only limited animals are available. Stay there till 58 when you get your percentage heal spell, then you can try other areas with only one fully buffed pet (skin, DS, regen, strength, pet haste), and use the percentage heal to keep it alive.
