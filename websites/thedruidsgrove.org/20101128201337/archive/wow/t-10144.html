That's what you were looking for, although, getting in the habit of using a selfcast modifier key is probably better. I don't think running around with CT's self smart casting on is such a hot idea. I only turn it on in duels/BGs. It wastes a ton of mana because when someone is LoS or OOR it casts the spell on you instead of telling you so.
