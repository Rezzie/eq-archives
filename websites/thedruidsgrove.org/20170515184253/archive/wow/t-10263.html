Level 25-29
-Stonetalon Mountains: Stonetalon Peak - Antlered Courser, Cenarion Botanist, Corrosive Sap Beast, Daughter of Cenarius, Fey Dragon, Great Courser, Sap Beast, Son of Cenarius, Treant Ally, Twilight Runner, Wily Fey Dragon


Level 28-32
-Ashenvale: West side at exit to Felwood - Terrowulve


Level 30-34
-Alterac Mountains: Lordamere Lake(south edge) - Snapjaw


Level 30-33
-Thousand Needles: North Shimmering Flats - Salt Flats Scavenger, Salt Flats Vulture, Saltstone Basilisk, Saltstone Crystalhide, Scorpid Reaver, Scorpid Terror


Level 32-34
-Alterac Mountains: Southside Alterac Mountain Range - Mountain Yeti


Level 34-38
-Thousand Needles: North Shimmering Flats - Sparkleshell Snapper, Sparkleshell Tortoise


Level 35-38
-Swamp of Sorrows: Shifting Mire - Sawtooth Crocolisk, Sorrow Spinner, Swamp Jaguar, Swampwalker, Tangled Horror, Young Sawtooth Crocolisk
