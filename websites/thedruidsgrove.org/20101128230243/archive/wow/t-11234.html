what I have found that works well is to gather up the Shadowform sp? (Rogue T0) gear and Wildheart gear and switch out to get the best benefits. In cat form I wear the chest, bracers, legs and belt from the Shadow set to get the 2/4 and 4/4 benefits, plus the boost to my crit, dodge and attack power. For the Wildheart set I wear the head, shoulders, gloves and boots to gain an additional 2/4 and 4/4 benefits (gain +200 armor from Wild and +200 Shadow and the rage/energy bonus from both sets). I switch to full Wildheart when in caster form for the obvious boost to casting.
