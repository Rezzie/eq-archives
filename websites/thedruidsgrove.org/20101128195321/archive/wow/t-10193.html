The first benefit is speed at lower levels. In the 20-29 bracket, cat form with feline swiftness rules, and dash for getting out of the flag room is awesome. In 30-39 nothing but a shaman in ghost wolf is gonna catch cheetah form.

The next is that shifting forms breaks most forms of movement slowing effects, so while that frost nove might immobilize some other class, the druid cheetah shifts out and back and barely slows down.

Of course stealth is great for getting into their flag room in the first place.

Once you get the flag back to your base you can go bear for heavy defense and self healing with frenzied regen.

So to sum up, stealth into their base, cat dash out of flag room, cheetah across the field, shift out of most cc, bear up if you have to wait for your team to recapture their flag, no class is better.

To the OP... Druids are indeed amongst the best flag carriers, particularily in a 1 or 2 man flag cap team. The best is really a defense spec'ed warrior who's supported by his team. Lucky for us we're also excellent flag carrier support.

More then any single talent, the secret to being an uber WSG flag carrying machine is Free Action Pots. The mats for which are easily fished up (oily black mouths) or cheap enough on the AH usually.

Talents are debatable. Personally I love innervate... no need to worry about drinking up in situations where you either are being chased up the field, or need to swap forms alot. Once inside the base, HoTW for the extra hp's would help for protecting the flag. Nature's Grasp is good to have as is the Mana reducing shifting talent in Balance. Feral Charge is great for getting up/down the tunnel and for stopping enemy flag carriers.

To the off-topic people, I think the patch notes are reasonably clear that we'll be able to use equip'ed items... so no procs or consumables.... someone who's been on test should be able to clarify.

I also think Blizzard very much cares about the economy. Look at some of the Silthius quests... items like Powerful Mojo finally have some demand... I used to vendor stack's of them. Nature resits rings are another example of some new flavor for old stale items. FR rings have been going for good gold for awhile, while other resistance rings are DE bait... now there's more demand.
