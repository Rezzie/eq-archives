For tanking I use a stopform macro for my healthstones and health injectors. (be careful though if the mob is quick you might get stuck in cowform)
#showtooltip Master Healthstone
/cancelform
/use Master Healthstone
/cast Dire Bear Form
Add a healing injector and health pot and any healing restoration devices you might have in there.

I recently killed all my macros but I have a macro for spamming mangle and lacerate in 1 button too. Can't recall it will have to search for it.

For healing I use grid but do not use cliq because then I cannot target anything for trading because it trys to heal them instead.

For various plug ins I use Fubar. I have a plugin for atlas loot and my durability on my gear.

Recount is great too it is a healing meter/damage meter that will also tell who breaks cc and who is taken most damage etc.

Of course Omen to see your threat per second. I look at this all the time cause i tickle myself when I hit 1200 tps.
