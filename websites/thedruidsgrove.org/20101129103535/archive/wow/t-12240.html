HT is one of the list spells used in almost all cases. In a full Rejuv/Lifebloom stack, on a single target, I can heal more in the course of HT's cast time. If I crit, I can come about even.

My HT Crits at ~5.5k, I get ~900 rejuv a tick and ~650 a tick on Lifebloom stack 3 -- x 3.5 and you have ~5.5k.

As far as rejuv vs lifebloom. For me, it depends on if I need quicker heals or efficient heals. During raid, I often shoot Rejuv on a lot of people, to get them a heal faster (~900 a tick, over 1k raid buffed), but for efficiency (lotta healers in raid), I'll shoot out Lifebloom. I also always have the mob targeted, so if I see him change targets, I can quickly shoot a life loom on the new target, to compensate for some dmg they may soon be taking.
