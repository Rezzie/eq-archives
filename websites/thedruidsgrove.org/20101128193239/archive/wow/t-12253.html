Its much more bearable (pun intended) than it used to be. With the speed up of leveling post 20 and particularly after 30 its much much easier to do. I level my balance druid with my wife's character. She's a feral druid so its a very nice combination for us. The biggest hassle about it is buying all of the water that I need. I've taken to swinging out my mage prior to working with my wife... giving her a few stacks of food and water and then loading up my balance druid.

The down time due to drinking is the biggest complaint about balance druids. They do lots of good damage. They are certainly viable, but people whine about how much time they sit down to drink or heal. If combined with a secondary melee class its much better for mana use etc.
