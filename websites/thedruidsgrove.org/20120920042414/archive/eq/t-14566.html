Tenielle

It does indeed, however it works like any other damage dealing focus, it diminishes 10% per level. Also, it only affects the first nuke component of Winter's Flame (which essentially makes it not worth using a Fire of Dawn proc on).

To be specific, Fire of Dawn has the following affect:
Rime Crystals - 50%
Equinox Burn - 60%
Winter's Flame (Fire Component) - 70%
Dawnstrike - 100%

In essence, if you're going to continue to use Dawnstrike, your best bet is to just use Dawnstrike again for the chance of triggering Fire of Dawn again.
