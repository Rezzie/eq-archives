It is important to understand his assumptions (first page). As some of them don't make much sense (IMO)...

Both Bear and Cat mitigation lists assume that no healing is available. This emphasizes the value of Stamina as it makes you last longer. However, there is no meaningful way of representing healing, so it is better to have a clear understandable model (that you can then easily compare against) than a complicated one. However, until you have enough Stamina, the value is appropriate as it represents the buffer Health gives you against damage spikes.I can understand making this assumption on kitty gear (maybe), but not on bear gear.
