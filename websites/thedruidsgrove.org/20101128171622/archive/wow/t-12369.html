Different druid specs are strong or weak against different things.

For example, frost mage vs feral druid is heavily stacked against the feral druid.
Feral druid vs rogue is heavily stacked against the rogue.
Rogue vs boomkin would usually see the rogue winning.
And warlocks generally eat restoration druids for breakfast.


To answer your original question, imho resto is considered the best spec for group pvp, but in a 1v1 situation feral druid is the stronger spec.
