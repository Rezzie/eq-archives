AFAIK, STR (with HotW) increases your AP by 2.4 per strength. Can't remember if that is all STR increases. AGI will increase your AP by 1 per agi point but it also adds a bit of crit, dodge (and armor) as well. Attack power well, adds attack power.

The formula goes (for maximum AP) like this:
Focus on STR until 1200 AP and then focus on AGI because at that point AGI becomes equal to STR in terms of AP and it also includes bonuses such as dodge and crit.
