Get the +66 healing spellthread for your pants. (http://thottbot.com/i24276)

Get some +heal / +spirit gems (http://thottbot.com/i32836)

Get the Glyph of Renewal from Thrallmar revered (http://thottbot.com/i29190) - looks like you only need 1 more run to get Revered.

Get the Essence of the Martyr Trinket for 41 badges (http://thottbot.com/i29376)

If you can't get the hammer from Prince, the S2 PvP healing mace with a +heal enchant is nice. Total healing on it with the enchant is +504 (no spirit or mp5 tho :( )

Just getting the helm enchant, mace (and enchant) and the spellthread for your pants will give you an extra +328 healing, putting you at a respectable 1560. With the trinket you will be up to +1648 healing. With some re-gemming you should be able to get close to +1700
