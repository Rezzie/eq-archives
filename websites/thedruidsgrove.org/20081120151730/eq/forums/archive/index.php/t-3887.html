Spell Haste Focus Items
-Do not affect Complete Healing
-Do not affect spells with less than a 3-second cast time
-Do not affect spells that last less than 72 seconds


Direct Damage Focus Items
-Personal area-of-effect spells are not affected
-Target area-of-effect spells are not affected
-Damage-over-time spells are not affected
-Dragonbane and Giantbane are not affected


Affliction (DoT) Focus Items
-More ticks of damage done for standard amount of DoT per tick
-Do not affect spells that last less than 36 seconds


Duration Focus Items
-Do not affect invulnerability spells
-Do not affect Complete Healing


Summoning and Reanimation Focus Items
-Only work on summoned pets
-Do not work for charmed pets
