I don't have any exciting gear, but almost everything I have is agi & sta based. I've never really played a restoration build or a balance build so I can't comment on that side of life. I think it really irritates casters when I'm going in circles around them and they can't get a spell off.
I think feral has improved dramatically, but to have the most fun, you really have to change your gear for it. I have found the AGI gear to be much more common at my levels right now, but not sure about later on. At low levels, I felt I was forced into a feral spec due to lack of +int based leather armor.
Overall, I can't get enough of my druid in PvP.
In a PvE environment, I'm completely amazed at dire bear form. I am now taking on up to 2-3 of any mob. Most usually my level or one under. It's quite sad when I compare my 46 druid to my 53 Warrior on another server. I can tank more as a bear at 46 than I can with that 53.

Have fun with your druids,
