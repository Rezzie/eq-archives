Ok, here goes:

1) Get yourself to Nadox and camp the undead trolls for a Coral Ring to replace one of your Velium Rings.
2) Head to CoM and camp the goos till the named goo spawns and get a Green Slime Ring to replace the other Velium Ring.
3) If you're guilded, head to Kael and do Vindi. Get his head to start you on your PoG quest for the Gloves of the Earthcrafter.
4) GAH! Ditch the MRT for Elysian. I'm assuming you just haven't had the luck with this drop in UP since I see you have other Elysian gear.
5) Do some LDoN Takish adventures and pick up a Dusty Girdle of Reformed Sand.
6) Perhaps do the Shield of Bane Warding quest or save up for a Tactician's Bulwark or Tae Ew Shield

Have fun!
