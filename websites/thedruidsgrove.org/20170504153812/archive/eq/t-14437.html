Head to Icefall Glacier.

In the North west area, there is a set of Kobold caves in the center hill.

Find Malgen


You say, "Hail, Melgan!"

Malgen says 'Me need to talk so you do things for me. Me need you to go kill orc wolves. You [do that]?

You say, "Do that"

Malgen says 'Bark! Good!'

Assigned to task (Thinning the wolves?)

Locate the sacred cove of the Wire Wolves (NE corner of map north from the small orc fort)
Locate the sacred cove of the Eldar Dire wolves (NE corner of map, broken spires)
Kill 4 Dire wolves
Kill 3 Eldar Dire Wolves
Kill 3 Coldeye wolf tenders
Talk to Malgen

Reward:
Direwolf Totem of Spirit (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=53688)
Effect:
Arcane Wolf Familiar (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=11518)

Increase mana by 6 per tick
Increase Chance to Dodge by 20%

Does not stack with any charmed pet, or boo boo. (AA pet is fine)
Does not stack with HP/Mana regen clickies:
Chaotic enlightenment
Refyin's
Mealin's
Lunar whispers
etc...

Note that this also means you don't have to choose your reward right away.

However, you can only have 5 rewards pending in the reward window. If you get a 6th, it will overwrite the oldest reward that you have waiting.

The familiar will NOT stack with Maelin's Methodical Mind, Chaotic Enlightenment, or any other mana regen buff in that line. It also prevents you from having a pet (charmed or otherwise).

While the familiar does last for hours, it will disappear when you zone. Not a big deal, but just something to remember if you zone frequently.

The dodge modifier appears to be equivalent to Improved Dodge II. Given that it is a spell rather than a worn modifier, I would expect it to stack, but would have to do a lot of parsing to confirm it.
