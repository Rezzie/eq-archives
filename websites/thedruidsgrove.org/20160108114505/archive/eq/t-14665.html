Fire Dots definitely won't work in Doomfire. Use Winged Death and Swarming Death (orb from Time works very well if you have one). I also use the Drones of Doom from ES Vambraces and epic on occasion. Once you hit 68, Wasp Swarm is a huge upgrade for our magic dots.

Oh - and bring BOO BOO out for a little extra DPS - can't hurt.

Quadding advice in Fire - Once you hit 66, use the cold rain Tempest Wind instead of Karana's Rage. The magic AE nuke gets partially / fully resisted where as the cold Rain nuke won't get resisted at all, even w/out debuffing.
