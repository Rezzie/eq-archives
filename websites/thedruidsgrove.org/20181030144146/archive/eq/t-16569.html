He is on the rooftop of the ship, Go up Elevator at zone in with Clockwork Illusion on from Disrupting the Workshop task. Proceed up all 3 spiral ramps. At the top of the third spiral ramp there is a small room with a ladder. Go up ladder then up the next elevator, set up camp against the north wall of the cylinder you exited from below the steam vent. The Ph is any of the mobs in the middle circle around the cylinder you exited from. The mobs on the edge are good xp, the mobs immediatly against the cylinder same, ph can be any in the middle circle. Again he's on the tip top rooftop, middle ring of mobs.
