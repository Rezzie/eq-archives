A short guide I wrote for my guild (information acquired with the help of my guildmates Zenne & Wrenn):

<blockquote><strong><em>Quote:</em></strong><hr>How to farm the PoDecay keys

After about 2 days of doing this and figuring it out (along with help from Zenne and Wrenn) I have acquired the following information:

You need 4 items for this key. These drop ALL the time off rare wurms in PoD. The mobs are on a 22 mins timer. The 4 wurms are:

1. Harrior. Pher for this wurm is "a malarian larva". When you zone into PoD look out towards the lake and follow the zonewall to your right. When you hit the shore thats your camp spot. A tracker from here can track these mobs and single pull them to you.

2. Scavian. Pher for this wurm is "a tsetsian pupa". Your camp spot for this is approx +1200 +550. Get a tracker and track these wurms .. or alternatively kill all the wurms you see if you have a full grp (slower preferred).

3. Genesian. Pher for this wurm is " a planarian larva". Camp spot same as Scavian above.

4. Sengion. Pher for this is "a tachian catterpillar" but there are several of these. After some research alongside Zenne we believe we have found one that is actually the Ph. +1250 neg 575 is where we believe the PHer for this spawns. You can camp right near here alongside the zonewall. Alternative theory is there are multiple PHers in which case you kill ALL the tachian catterpillars near you.
[/quote]

For point 4 both me and zenne found the named spawn at the loc we listed above. But since we never came back to pod after getting the keys we cannot confirm it 100 percent. But I am pretty sure thats where named spawns. So far I have never seen it spawn anywhere else.
