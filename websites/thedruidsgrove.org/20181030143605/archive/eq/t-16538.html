01-23-2008, 03:44 PM
I haven't personally seen burning affliction on anything since DoN. As far as other DoT focii go, the ones that are extremely useful would be affliction efficiency and affliction haste, both of which offer DoTs a vastly better effect than anything you can find on raid gear. Unfortunately, in many cases this has historically meant that raiders would have to sacrifice an aug slot or actually wear lesser gear to get the effect.

Both were introduced into tribute as twinge of suffering and vision of suffering up to level 6. Level 5 (to level 70) was the last that was made available to gear. Level 7 has not been updated on tribute (nor has anything else; I posted on the SOE boards last weeks and haven't heard a peep from the devs on an ETA).

The long and short is, if you're a dotter, level 6 trib is your best option for DoT focus right now.
