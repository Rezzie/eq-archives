Been on 30+ runs of UBRS. I've failed on Drakk only about 6 times. And I have to note this...In my 30+ runs I've never seen a WH chest drop.

You can do drakk many different ways.

1. Hunter kite drakk, with the MT + DPS killing one guard while a lock fears the other or a druid sleeps.(A druid CAN sleep the guards if they are the level 59 ones). I've done this just today.

2. Old school way, rush them all. You can kill in any order you want. I've done it where the guards died first or Drakk has. It all works. FR will not save you from 1k hits or 2.5k crits. If you get full aggro your as good as dead in most cases.

3. With two group locks fear both guards and burn down drakk.

4. With two tanks, have one OT Drakk while the group kills one guard and the lock fears the other. Druid can sleep if the guard is level 59.

Group make up wise i've always be one of the main healers. Normally it's just me, another druid or priest. Once in awhile we'll have a pally. But really two healers will work just fine, and the rest is DPS.

FR is not needed for this encounter by everyone. Only the tank really needs FR.

Well not seen it mentioned but there is another options for takeing down drak and his adds. Druid kite :)

Basicly it works just like hunter kite but more effective least my what I have seen as our hunters always died. Get Max range from drak. Moonfire/Starfire Drak. Turn and run to beast room do look back dont stop. Drop to cat form ASAP and sprint. You will easily out run drak to the beast room. By the time you reach the beast's room drak will be just bit out of visual range turn and watch for him. Soon as he appears move to your right to the far corner of the beasts room. Drak will unagro off you just as he enters the beast room. If he does not you should have time to kite him around the room by staying close to the wall and running him around the room. Once you get back to the entrace to the beasts room return to draks room they should be ready for you by now. The Second guard should be down by the time drak returns and I've only died doing this by being stupid and turning around to look or run back for him. The nice thing about this is when you return the healer is ussually about ready for your innervate and you can DPS or heal at this point as well or even off tank drak. Was quite fun doing as well.
