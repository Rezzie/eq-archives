Seriena
Comm 1 - min group would be any melee + your druid (my monk tanked without slow)

Comm 2 - Basically a shm + any melee can duo here. Slow makes it simple, but without slow you should prob have a tank that is ranger or better. My monk did tank him and the elite ph's more than a few times though.

It goes a lot faster though with a high dps group and constant pulls.

For the pouch, you kill the taskmasters which can't be slowed (if I remember right). They can't be snared or rooted either. So, your best bet is a plate tank. Some tm's are harder than others...the flurry one for example requires pretty much constant healing. Some rampage which means you'll want a tank + another melee to get the rampage dmg so you can heal safely.

For the insignia you'll need a really good group or more (recommend atleast 2). They have a stupid amount of hps and even worse ac. You also need a rogue to get you through the door. Luckily the rares drop the insignia everytime and they're more common than the commanders.

Oldoaktree
Bear in mind that while he can be killed by a tank and you, the challenge will be actually getting him pulled and dealing wiht any adds. You would of course only be working a small camp with a trio or so, but be aware that many of the pull spots are hard to get without a FD splitter of some kind.

Commanders are unslowable, so you do need to do more healing on your tank for them. I know people who duoed him (and a few who soloed) but I would recommend at least 3 just because while the tricks are fun, his spawn is really rare and you need to do a lot of killing to get him.

Taskmasters are mostly slowable (2 are not). I have seen a shaman solo them...easy to get duo with a shaman. Probably soloable by a superhero druid but again, unless you are trying to prove something, why bother making it that hard on yourself.

Third floor is probably doable with a group, but honestly I have never known anyone to do it with less than 2, and normally you go as a raid. I have seen 3rd floor mobs pulled to 1st floor Ass room, but honestly there is a clean corner you can do the camp in so little reason to do special effect pulls. The 3rd floor mobs have beeelions of HP, and at least one of them has a neato 300yard gflux it does repeatedly during the fight. If you are in a guild working on emp best thing to do is:

* If no one has the piece, call a raid for 3rd floor and go and get 50 or 60 in a few hours. The nameds spawn regularly and you can grind through a lot in a few hours.

* If everyone has one, just have your raid stop on the way to emp and kill a few until you get a named for those for whom it is the last piece.

If you don't have key and are looking at this as an investment in a future guild decision you lose nothing by waiting. Any emp killing guild will stop to help someone get their missing piece if it is the last one....3rd floors are easy to come by.
