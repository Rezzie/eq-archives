Haste will quicken the spell cast ( or how quickly a channeled spell ticks ). For a resto druid, this quickens regrowth, healing touch and tranquility.

With Tranquility having a long CD, it's benefit of +haste is questionable.

Here's the formula for spell haste cast time modification:

New Casting Time = Base Casting Time / (1 + (Spell Haste Rating/1570))

Regrowth: 2 sec cast.

It takes 15.76 haste rating to reduce a spell by 1%. You'd need about 500 haste rating to get regrowth down to about a 1.5 second cast. Going to assume you don't use healing touch too much, so I won't even do the math on that.

Spell haste is nice, you can fit more heals into the same amount of time, but it takes a LOT of haste to make a marked difference in your cast times.
