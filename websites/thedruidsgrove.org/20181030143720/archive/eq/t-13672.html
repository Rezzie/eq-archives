"mini-nameds" drop these augments. If you're level 70 and hit track and it cons red, but starts with "a something-or-another", that's what I mean. There are some other red-cons in zone too, but they all have capitalized names (Arena Overseer, Ashenback, Nightshade, etc..).

So, when you find something like "a demented scrykin" in tracklist and it cons red, that's what you're looking for. They drop augs and occasionally grey orbs (for black orb of scrykin quest). I guess they all have placeholders and so on, but I haven't spent eneough time there to know. There's at least one on the upper level too. Probably at least one per "theme" - fire / ice / storm.

If you go farm these, would you care posting your information back here? :)

There is:
Gray shard of relic 10AC +5 to all stats and resists (a wandering bone pile)
Amethyst shard of relic 65/65 +15 avoidance
Opaline shard of relic 65/50 +10 accuracy +10 attack melee classes only
Blue shard of relic 50/50 +5 regen +5 damgae shield (a demented scrykin) in the alley of the dammed area
Emerald shard of relic 50/50 +1% shielding +5 avoidance (a lost spirit) wanders from porthio to ZI past bazaar area
Marbled shard of relic 40/40 +5 to resists (random in Arcstone)

Artic Scrykin pops on the Ice platform if the dragon is dead, loot unknown.

There are named up top and bleow, and at least 1 per platform that the dragons spawn on if the dragons are dead.
