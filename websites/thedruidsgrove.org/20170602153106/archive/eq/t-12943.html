Focuses don't stack...

So whatever focus is the better focus will fire when you use a spell that works with that focus.

Pyrelian Rage only works with fire based spells, so it will only fire on fire based spells.

Spells such as Wasp Swarm are magic based and thus some kind of focus is needed to have them tic for higher damage. The BA5 one will work for that as will http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=45717

That is a 25pct magic focus that will give you up to 5pct better on your dots and is an augment if you dont have some other item with a magic focus.

BA5 is only 20pct, so you can add a bit more magic damage by swapping out your Citrine Spell Stone for Ritual Nexus Opal.

Focus only works on the highest focus as far as I have seen.
