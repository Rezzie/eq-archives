Instead of compiling a list, get the auctioneer addon http://auctioneeraddon.com/ (hosted at sourceforge). It's made to keep track of the price you can get for items, but I find the most useful feature is that it tells you what profession uses each item when you mouse over it. It's very nice to be able to send items to my friends without having to ask if anyone needs something. So if you notice an item is used in leatherworking, then just keep it.

As far as leatherworking as a profession, I'd have to agree that it's hardly worth it, but I think most professions feel that way when they hit 60. I can't even make money off devilsaur as there is too much competion now. It amazes me that people will sell the pants for less then the leather sells for. When I asked why they would take a loss, they replied that they farmed the leather themselves, therefor, they were making money. Apparently they never thought about just selling the leather, because at their prices they could make more money.
