A well played restoration druid I think has the most survivability as a healer. A paladin is extremely mana efficient though and if you don't do something about his mana, he's likely to heal all day long. There's also nothing more frustrating than a paladin healing groupmates while he's immune, but it's really the only thing they have to heal under fire, whereas with swiftmend and NS you can dish out some heavy healing on the run if you have to.

That said, priests are nasty too but they make for prime targets by the other group.

I don't have a priest or a paladin, but if I were to pick one of the 3 specifically on healing ability in pvp I'd prolly take the druid, just because you can avoid being targeted first alot of times because of stealth etc. Can't really go wrong though they're all powerful imnsho
