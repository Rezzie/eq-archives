From 10 - 20, it's pretty obvious. I went this order:
- Furor 5/5 (gives great head-start when switching forms)
- Ferocity 5/5 (dunno if anyone ever uses Feral Aggression?)

From 20 - 30+, I'd take:
- Brutal Impact 2/2
- Feral Instinct 3/3
- Feral Charge 1/1 (you might wait with this if you don't PvP/group much)
- Sharpened Claws 3/3
- Predatory Strikes 3/3
