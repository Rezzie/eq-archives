iegil
There is no safe CoD/CoT duo'ing in the elemental planes. Mobs hit to hard.

Best thing a druid/chanter can do duo'ing is quadkite at the fennin trigger picnic tables. The chanter AE Tashing will improve your damage from nukes and their mana will allow you to finish off the stubborn mob or two in the quadkite.

Be sure and spend the 180p on the appropriate shaman focus potion too. Since you don't plan on dying and druids are mana intensive, every percentage saved helps.

One diamond drop and the potions paid for.
