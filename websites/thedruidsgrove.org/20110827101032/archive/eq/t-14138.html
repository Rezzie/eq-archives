There's some decent stuff with PoR including a few easy quests for some good slots as well as new spells including a group heal. PoR is pretty heavy on the grouping though, not a whole lot of solo spots that I've found. I'd personally go with the package now and hold off on PoR. It has nice quests but I think you'll have plenty to deal with by just adding DoN and DoD. Both have lots of accessible gear options - DoN via crystals at the merchants and DoD via mission rewards. DoN merchants are a quick way to patch armour slots and fill in a missing focus and you can sell all the items back to the merchant as you progress. It's fairly easy to get a group to do a creator and pick up a few crystals, or if you have plat they are tradeable and at least on BB very cheap in the bazaar.
