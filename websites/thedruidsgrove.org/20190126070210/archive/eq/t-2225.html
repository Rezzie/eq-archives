Lots of things summon. I think it'd be as easy to list the mobs that don't as those that do.

In PoN:
Vexed Banshees summon but the nettling wraiths don't.
A Tar'dak Hunter (crocodile) summons but slarghilugs and murkwater minnows don't.
An Agony Mephit summons but mephits and misery mephits don't.
Terror Matriarch and Infernal Consorts summon but little spiders like virulent arachnids don't.
All Hobgoblins summon but all the bats don't.
Gloom Nightmares and Foreboding Guardians summon but gargoyles don't.
Things on the top level near the graveyard, including nightstalkers, treants, blood ravens, and hatchlings don't summon.
