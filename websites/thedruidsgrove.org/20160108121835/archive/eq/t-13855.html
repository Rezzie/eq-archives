Its two parts really. Coldfang for the eye part (/say bound). And Friddlebrat for the Monocle. You can receive the piece (the lens) from Coldfang without already owning the other needed piece for Friddlebrat (the first monocle).

But you need both to get the Monocle of Blood for Demi flag/key from Friddlebrat.

Curse AAs only matter from a key/flag standpoint to allow you to loot in Demi. When doing Coldfang and Friddlebrat quest portions, no Curse AAs are needed, just the items (Vule's Eye --> lens + the first Monocle = Monocle of Blood).
