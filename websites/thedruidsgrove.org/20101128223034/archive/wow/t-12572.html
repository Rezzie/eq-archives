Grats! For arena chests pieces I would recommend

2 x Blessed Tanzanite (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30552#dropped-by)
1 x Mystic Dawnstone (or one of the Steady green res/stam gems if you prefer ... I use my yellow sockets to stack resilience)

if you want the socket bonus. Otherwise, just stick in a Steady green res/stam gem in each socket.

---

ETA: If you're socketing this for PvE, then definitely go with

2 x Teardrop Living Ruby
1 x Luminous Noble Topaz

Do not ... DO NOT ... stick purple gems in those red sockets for PvE. IMO in 2.4 no resto druid or healing priest should be putting purple gems into red sockets except for to satisfy meta requirements (which your boots will cover anyway, with the Chess and Leo boots each having two blue sockets).
