There are some decent items that drop in Dragonscale Hills that have level 80 focii. These, primarily, have type 7 aug slots, while the more rare versions have a type 3 as well. Most of these items can be bought in the bazaar for very little plat as they are extremely common, even the 2 slot items. The Tier 1 armor drops from various NPC's scattered throughout the SoF expansion, with the exception of Dragonscale Hills.

Type 3 augments can be bought from various faction vendors (with appropriate faction), but cost a good bit of plat. Most of these modify the damage and healing of the new spells. As for any other augments, it's pretty much the same as ever....zonewide rare drops, some from particular nameds, etc. If you're really hurting for augs you can solo trash in the hot zones, both old and new, and they will sometimes drops them, though they aren't spectacular.
