What is the hit max and expertise max?

1 Hit Rating = 0.030% Hit
32.79 Hit Rating = 1% Hit

Cap vs Level 80 mob = 5% Hit = 163.95 HitRating
Cap vs Level 81 mob = 5.5% Hit = 180.34 HitRating
Cap vs Level 82 mob = 6% Hit = 196.74 HitRating
Cap vs Level 83 mob = 9% Hit = 295.11 HitRating (Raid-Boss)

1 Expertise Rating = 0.030% reduction of dodge & parry chance
32.79 Expertise Rating = 1% reduction of dodge & parry chance

Cap vs Level 80 mob = 5%(Dodge) / ?%(Parry) = 163.95 / ? Expertise Rating
Cap vs Level 81 mob = 5.5%(Dodge) / ?%(Parry) = 180.34 / ? Expertise Rating
Cap vs Level 82 mob = 6%(Dodge) / ?%(Parry) = 196.74 / ? Expertise Rating
Cap vs Level 83 mob = 6.5%(Dodge) / 15%(Parry) = 213.13 / 491.85 Expertise Rating (Raid-Boss)
