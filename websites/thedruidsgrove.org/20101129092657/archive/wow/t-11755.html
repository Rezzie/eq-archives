I do have something useful to say though.
Oily blackmouths require low level fishing skill to catch - you can be catching them within about 30 mins of starting fishing.
A great place to catch them is Menthil Harbor, in the Wetlands.

The Canals of Stormwind have fishing trainers and fishing reagents vendors, and also contain suitably lowbie fish to get you started with levelling.
