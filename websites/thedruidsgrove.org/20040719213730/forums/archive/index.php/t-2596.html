Get SCM2 (and 3) before MC2, hands down.

The lizard lets you get "medding" mana regen, without needing to sit, as long as you're not moving or in auto-attack mode.

Assuming maxxed med skill (252), you regen 21 mana/tick sitting/medding. At 63, I believe you regen 2 mana/tick standing (not including buffs, FT, and MC AAs), so the lizard is effectively FT19 for you, so long as you're not moving. Miss half the ticks, and it's still like FT9.5, so as you can see, horses/lizards are very powerful items for casters.
