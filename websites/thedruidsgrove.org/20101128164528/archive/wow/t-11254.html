:physics: I'm not quite sure if this should go in here or in a different area, but here it is.

For a very good and extremely useful moveable mana bar for, your mana showing, well, your mana, while shapeshifted that will help you know when and when not to shift click the link below. After its opened just click download, wait a few seconds, and click save. Save it to Program Files>World of Warcraft>Interface>Addons.

Once saved you'll need to go to the addon section by going to My Computer. Once there double click on the file, open it in winzip, and high light, drag, everything in there out to the addons area.

There ya go! If anything comes up just post the problem and i'll help you fix it.
