Getting from 13 to 20 is probably the biggest challenge druids face.All you can do is root and wrath (1-3 times). When they come in close, hit moonfire and shift to bear (make sure you start your auto-attacks!!!!), enrage and swipe/maul when possible. Be prepared to drink often. Use bash to stop runners (or put moonfire on your bear bar).

13-20 really, really, sucks for Druids. I didn't have that many problems with my priest or hunter in that range. There is also a slightly painful point in the early 30s (seems like you have self-heal way too much), after that leveling Feral is quite easy (and fun).

Hello new druid ! :D

Welcome to our Druid's Grove :>

As other druids here have said, 13-20 is a very tricky period for young druids. I can sympathise as I have recently started a second druid, an alt who will also be resto. :>

I've found the best thing of all to speed up levelling is to find a nice mage to conjure you some water. Then at least you can drink all the time.
In terms of the actual spells and stuff I use, I normally open with a wrath, then immedietely entangle the mob while my wrath is still travelling towards it. Then I continue to spam wrath, and if the roots breaks and runs towards me, the last thing I do is Moonfire.
If that still isn't enough to kill the mob, I shift to bear form and kill it that way.

Don't worry, levelling gets much easier once you are level 20 :>
