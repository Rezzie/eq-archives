If you have done this quest from inside the ship as I did the first time you know the chest is at the stern or back of the ship on the bottom of the hull. It's difficult doing it that way. Now if you swim down to the bottom of the hull on the outside of the stern and get real close you will see the gear icon to loot the chest and you get the quest item from the outside of the ships. I did it today just to be sure it has not changed. I can't take credit for figuring this out because someone was kind enough to show it to me but it makes the quest an easy solo. Good Luck =)
