Thandril
Very very few coldain hits can be had in the arena as most Kromzek mobs only improve Skyshrine faction. Best place from my experience in Kael is the the "Riff house". Triggering the Coldain ring mobs there spawns some decent level giants and results in a fairly constant supply of decent faction hits. You will need a couple of others to kill all the mobs there so solo is not really an option. Tank, slower and druid is ideal but I'm sure many other groups would work too. The giants there drop armor pieces too but I have only seen plate.

The other alternative is to quad giants or wolves in GD but that's much slower.
