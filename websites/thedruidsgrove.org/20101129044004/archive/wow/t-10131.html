Nah, in my experience most Alliance types have a fairly ground-level opinion of pallies as well. It's a shame really, they are really awesome for PvE. The big problem most paladins face is trying to figure out what their class role is. The most poignant description I've heard is that basically a paladin is there to enhance all the other members of a party. They get awesome buffs, auras, they can heal and res, and tank at a pinch.. But if they try to specialise in any one of these areas they will always be outstripped by other classes. I guess it depends how you want to play the class, but imho the best paladins I know work as well as they do because they have embraced the idea that they are a hybrid of many classes.
