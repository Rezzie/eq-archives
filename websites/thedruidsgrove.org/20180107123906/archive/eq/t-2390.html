tenebrous mountains, 40-47: bind in katta at the TM zone. port to nexus, get kei, gate, run out, gather 4 wolves at first, use your ae blast. that should only take 60m.
Med to full, go out, grab 8 wolves, blast til all are dead, you should be at 20m.
Repeat.

Timorous deep:47-50/51: buy your level 49 ae spell first. Bind along the beach near the aviak spire. port to nexus get kei, gate back, and start with 4, see how low you get on mana, if you have 60m or so after 4, med to full, and grab 8 the next time.

thats what I did, except i grouped from 47-49...but I probobly should have done this for fast leveling. Just realise there is really no loot at all at these spots, so after a few levels KEI will really start to hurt the bank.
