Quelm
Average for me was somewhere around 2 hours. There were some lucky camps where I picked up the epic piece in under half an hour, but those were balanced by 8+ hour ordeals (FU barracuda!) The epic quest can be a good exercise in moving bind points. Just bind at your current camp, and leave when you get bored. Pop in occasionally, and you may get lucky.
