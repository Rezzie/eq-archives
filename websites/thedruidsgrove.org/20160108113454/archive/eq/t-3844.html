It's a healing elixir, similar to Ethereal Elixir, and similarly can be MGBd.

One of the most important aspects of the ability is it's instant casting time. When things are going wrong, adds are coming thick and fast (and hitting for 500), you're trying to root off two or three at once... an instant tap of the SoWood button and your group is healing.

SCRM makes this ability <strong>really</strong> worthwhile, increasing the duration from 5 ticks to 7 ticks.
