Well, in Stoneroot none of the regular mobs summon. Drachnids and shadowmanes there are around 8% AA per kill at level 70 solo. Not quite as good as the second castle in PoFire, but very relaxed exp, and with multiple spots to camp, rarely full. Since it's an outdoors zone, you can save (regen) a bunch of mana by sitting on your horse while doing it. Shadowmanes also can (rarely) drop hides, useful in the DoDH augment tradeskill quest, which sell for about 2kpp on my server.

You can also kill the orcs and the witherins around Stoneroot. Not sure of the exp hit on those, never really paid attention.
