I don't think there's really a specific overview post, but if you look back through some of the older posts, there are a lot of great talent and gear discussions.

Just for a really short overview...

For gear, I would try instances, such as the Temple of Atal'Hakkar, Maraudon, et cetera.

Talents really depend on how you want to play, and even then, talents only really enhance what you have. Balance is primarily spell damage; feral is tanking and melee damage; and restoration is healing.
