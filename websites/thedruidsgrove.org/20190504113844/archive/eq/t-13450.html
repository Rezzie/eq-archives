you don't NEED SI - if you are satisfied with your healing performance currently, then there's no need to purchase SI (I'm assuming that the spell is currently in the 20-30K range). it's just a bonus or a perk since it's an upgraded version of NI.

I would recommend SI more highly if you often found yourself in situations where more healing was necessary, i.e. raiding or higher-end content. I used Chlorotrope for big heals or hp/sec, then SI for any patch heals or when Chlorotrope was overkill (I used NI before I got SI).

keep an eye out if the price ever drops or perhaps work out a deal with the researcher (by providing farmed components).
