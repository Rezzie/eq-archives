From Allakazam (http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?wquest=5088)

Slay Arikara. Bring her remains and the Incendia powder to Motega Firemane in Whitereach Post as proof of your deed.

Game Description:
Take this refined and purified Incendia powder to Thousand Needles and toss it on the sacred fire of life. It is located in the Grimtotem Compound on an isolated needle where they bury their dead.

The enchantment that I have weaved into the Incendia powder will summon Arikara to you, <Name>.

I fear she may be stalking Cairne Bloodhoof; too much Tauren lore has changed, and I fear this has angered Arikara. Move quickly before all is lost, <Class>!


Quest is given by the old crone Magatha Grimtotem on the elders rise in Thunder Bluff. The quest itself is done in thousand needels. From what I remember in 1K there's a Grimtotem Outpost that is up on the rises and you can find the sacred fire there.

The whole chain:

Get Alien Egg quest from Freewind post, turn in

Sent to Whitereach post, get gather Agave quest

Take Agave to Thunderbluff and the Elder Crone, turn in

Take Incendia Powder to the top of the grimtotem rise in Needles, right click on frame, spawn and kill Arikara (loot scale) return to Whitereach Post and get reward
