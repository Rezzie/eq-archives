Claritondeus
09-11-2008, 01:22 PM
Spell penetration is a worthless stat for PvE.

It effectivley negates spell resistances (e.g. the buff to all resist that druids get from MoTW, or a priests prayer of shadow protection, etc.) There are very few mobs in the game that have any kind of built in resists. (none that I am aware of in heroics, only ones I can think of are the fel hunters in Hyjal and maybe 1 or 2 in BT)

Spell cap is 16% for lv 73 mobs (bosses in heroics), which works out to 203 hit without any talents. Boomkins get a talent that increases their chance to hit by 4% or so (Balance of Power), so you'll only need in the neighborhood of 120 hit. I'll look around for the exact numbers.

Spell haste is great and will increase your dps, though it will definitley make you oom faster. OOMing shouldnt be a problem on heroic trash, but some of the longer boss fights might be tough. Also, you will need a lot of haste to notice a difference, and unless you are farming ZA I don't know where you'd get it.

I'd go:

+spell hit
+spell dmg
+spell crit

Yeah so generally on trash you can spam Wrath because the wind up on Starfire is just TOOO long. You'd be lucky to get more than one or two hits with Starfire. The down side is that Spamming Wrath will typically cause you to go OOM really fast. So you have to plan for sitting down and drinking a lot in between trash pulls. For quick burn down situations you want to use Wrath if you can. For boss fights typically because they are longer you need to be spamming Starfire. If you don't use Starfire you'll go OOM about half way through the fight.

I levelled alot as boomking...wrath is better dps, starfire is better dpm. For longer boss fights IS, SF x 3, repeat is your most efficient rotation, IS, MF, Wrath is your highest dps rotation but OOM for sure on bosses.

Wear leather caster gear, seriously. Mages can aoe crowd control, locks have pets, you have high armor. Wear cloth armor and you give away your built in survivability. Itemization in BC was horrible for Boomkings, but at level 70 there is enough available for you to run around in high quality leather caster gear.

Intensity and Subtlety are must haves. Boomking spell efficiency is pretty much teh suck, and they all seem to be high aggro spells so you must talent for those things.

This is all pre patch, of course.

My guild set 600 spell damage as the minimum for Kara runs, and that works pretty well as a minimum for heroic mgt, too.
