Not really.

A raid orinitated person is more apt to have their spells which would tirgger the AA in the first place. They are also more apt to be in the higher end content where they are casting those spells more consistantly.

If your a raider, you are apt to be using SI, CT or Anicent Heals, which are all 65 + and you will notice the differance more readily.

If your nuking, then you'd probly be casting Sol Strike, Dawnstrike, Glitter or Glacier - again, all 65 + spells.

The bonus is far more noticable in a raid enviroment, becuase you are more apt to be chain casting then in a exp group. This is not always true, but it is enough to the point you need to consider it.

If your however, using Summer's Flame as your main Nuke and healing with NI, then you'll never see the bonus reguardless of your level.

The AA is awesome, but its not for everyone and it really depends on your playstyle and what you cast. The fact it requires a 65+ spell to fire is a major limitation to some people, who have yet to recieve those spells.

This is less of a cause for concern for a raider, for the simple reason they are more apt to have their spells and to be in content which requires then more often.
