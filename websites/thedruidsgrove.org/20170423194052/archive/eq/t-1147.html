Any color chart is not of much use since the color of the regular armor texture is always what the tint is overlaying.

That means if you have a dark chain, the same color RGB value will look very different on it than a dark leather.

So really what you end up doing, if you want several pieces to look the same, is pick a color look for one then keep moving around with the other ones until it is similar.
