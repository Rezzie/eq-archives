FR is not a must, but if your party's gear is kind of subpar for blowing through these heroics, then some FR on your tank will help by decreasing the healing he needs (therefore allowing you to spend more time healing the damage the party will take).

Really, the fireballs and the fire patches they spawn shouldn't be a problem for anyone. If one lands on you, just move. While you're moving you can still keep the HoTs up on the tank and put a stack or two of Lifebloom on yourself to heal the damage. Tell DPS to bandage, pot, or healthstone when they can so that you can keep your focus keeping yourself and the tank alive.

As for the "too much melee" concern, as long as your melee aren't idiots and stand in the fire (your tank has to be good at moving the boss around if a fireball gets shot at one of the melee players as well) then this shouldn't be a problem for you, either.
