Why would we use this spell instead of Karanas Rage, Maybe its just listed wrong but damage is 1105 when KR is 1100

Also, KR is outdoor only, whereas this spell is castable anywhere.

As far as mana efficiency, one needs to consider time as well. You may get more damage per mana point using another spell, but if getting the damage done quickly is key, then you want the less efficient/higher damage output spell. For example, in many higher end raids where the named casts mana drain spells, you want to burn thru your mana as fast as possible, or else lose it to the manadrain. In other cases where the mob is regenning at higher rates or your are under a time limit (again more raid-like scenarios), you want fast/high dps spells... even if they are not mana efficient.

If you are just a soloer, and time is not an issue, then mana efficiency is probably more important to you.

I always wondered why we don't have an Ice debuff primer spell like we do for our Ro debuff line. I also ponder why mobs in PoFire resist our Ice debuff spells (I am talking about named... Eci sticks to trash pretty easy).
