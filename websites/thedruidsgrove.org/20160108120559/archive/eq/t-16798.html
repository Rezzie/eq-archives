The reason it has stayed in my primary over Crystallos priest 1hander (grouped one) is because of the slot 8 aug. All grouped 1handers from SoF have a type 4 aug slot which has only a handful of mana/hp augs to go in it, however our 2.0 has 2 aug slots, type 4 and 8. If you factor in the hp/mana granted by the type 8 aug (90/90 from CoA lets say) it will get very close to Wyvern-Priests Runed Censure off by like 20 or 30 hp/mana. Also the click effect is nice to have on hand and the focus is invaluable for not grabbing aggro too fast or with a bad tank. That is my longhanded way of agreeing with Tanom, yes it is worth getting your 2.0.
