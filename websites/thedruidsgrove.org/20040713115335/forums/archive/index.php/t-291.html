SoE in this situation is the one to use. In many cases it has 3 noticeable effects:

1. Reduces the times that you get "You can't see your target", because you are hovering above the virtual geometry.

2. Creating distance by using elevation is useful in lots of places.

SoE is my running spell of choice in all situations because of its versatility. So it stays permanently memmed for me.

I hunted otters for months while working on Tailoring. Harmony of Nature is the spell you want to use and it works very well. I used SoE exclusively and Harmony of Natured until I reached 60 and was able to single pull.

The Harmony you are using must be the lower level version.

Once everything is harmonized it is just a matter of snare pulling one at a time until you get 4. I then would run to top of cliff (ally CoV faction so Drakes and Wyverns leave me alone) and round them up and AoE. Rinse and repeat.

I would definitely make sure you have the level 29 version of Harmony of Nature. In over 3 months of hunting these things I think I had one resist of Harmony.
