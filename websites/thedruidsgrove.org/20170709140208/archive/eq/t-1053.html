I have yet to do a Miragul dungeon so maybe I shouldn't pass judgement yet but out of the ones I have done I would have to rank them like this:

1. Deepest Guk

2. Mistmoore

3. Tak Hiz

4. Rujarkian

Deepest Guk has the best looking mobs(although MM gives it a run for it's money) and the best dungeon architecture. It has the most 'dungeony' ambience to me: dark, dank, and mysterious.

Mistmoore's architecture isn't anything real special(although it has some cool rooms/objects) but the mobs are what really make it shine. The mummies, shadows, robed figures, and dust devil thingies get a thumbs up.

Tak Hiz has a couple cool mob models(phosphorescent golems come to mind, the sand elves also look good) and the architecture isn't bad but it just seems to be missing something. It also has the most old world mob models in it which makes it seem less than original.

Rujarkian seems kind of uninspired. The new models for some older mobs look good but it's till orcs and goblins. The architecture also leaves something to be desired. Kind of plain and without a lot of atmosphere, it has kind of a boring feel.

Mistmoore is great. I've always loved the undead dungeons (like Unrest and the original Mistmoore). I really like how some of the mobs are actually sleeping in the coffins.

I've only been to two of the Guk zones, but I was a little disappointed with them. They didn't really have the same feel as Guk and were more like Innothule.
