kalbear
05-20-2008, 02:44 PM
It's very good for tanking. It absolutely makes a difference in any fight where mobility is an issue. For example, with boar's speed you can get up to Al'ar in phase 1 without a single flame buffet triggering. It makes a difference when closing in on mobs without charge, it makes a difference when being nailed across the room by Thaladred or Al'ar's adds, or trying to get to one of Vashj's lackeys. Think about all the fights where you're running around.

I wouldn't recommend the agility for bears, but boar's speed is only 3 stam, and it comes in handy all the time.
