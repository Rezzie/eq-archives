Get your main tank to pull the snake boss off to the left of the stairs (facing the altar) and well away from the raid. Have a couple of healers assigned to keep the MT up while the rest of the raid deals with the guards. Reason we pull him to the side is he casts an arcane explosion AE, so you don't want people taking any more damage than you need to at the start.

The guards are all pulled down the stairs. Druids and mages hibernate or sheep all the guards but one; have an off tank assigned to that one ahead of time. All your DPS needs to be concentrated on that first guard and the other healers in the raid keep the off tank alive. Once the first guard is dead, the off tank moves to the next. Rinse and repeat until all the guards are dead.

At this point, all your DPS can focus on the snake boss. We do ranged dps on him until 50% to avoid the arcane explosion. Once he gets to 50% he transforms into a snake and no longer does the AE arcane explosion, now he does an AE posion cloud. The MT can wait til he does his AE and then pulll him out of the cloud so that melee don't take AE poison damage. Just burn him down, he's not too tough.
