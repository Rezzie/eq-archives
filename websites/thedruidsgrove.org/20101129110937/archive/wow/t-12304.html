Shift out to Caster Form to avoid the Arcane Explosion.

When he casts Blizzard, shift out into Caster Form, Run to the Back of the Blizzard (so you are following it around without getting hit by it) and shift back to Tree once it starts moving.

To avoid the Blizzard, don't run in a circle...try to cut angles by running straight ahead and stay behind the Blizzard.

Staying behind is a bit tougher than staying ahead...but if you can get it down, you should only be shifting a few times a fight.

When resto I always healed it in caster, never in tree.

Shade has no aggro table, so it is random who he targets ( otherwise the tank would take all the damage ).

When he calls out blizzard, run towards the center and find the back of it. Then move towards where it was. It overlaps a bit, so you'll have to follow it for a bit, but you should be fine after about 1/4 circle.

Don't move during flame wreath ( though you can spin in place, but absolutely no forward, back or side to side.

Arcane explosion, just move to the edge.

There's too much movement in this fight where Tree becomes more of a problem than it is worth.

Most the fights in Kara are tree friendly with only a few exceptions:
- Aran
- Big Bad Wolf ( if you get little red riding hood too many times )
- Netherspite can be rough as tree
- Prince can be rough as tree ( depending on how you fight him )
- Nightbane requires some movement but not much
