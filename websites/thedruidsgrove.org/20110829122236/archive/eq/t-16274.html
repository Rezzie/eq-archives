It's completely lame with the starter decks. They give you some really crappy cards. I bought 3 booster packs with the smallest of hope I would get a mount. AA horse speed, very nice. I want a cat.

It is amusing if you are really bored and just sick of soloing and waiting for something. Also if raid is getting ready but going to take quite a while. You can still keep an eye on chat windows to make sure you aren't needed. One way to really piss everyone off is to play LoN during a raid and miss stuff

The only real good thing about LoN is the chance to get a loot card. You can get 1 of 4 mounts (kirin, chimera, snow leopard, and panther), a few items (the only real good one I saw gives you 7K endurance and 10K mana every 20 hours), 1 of 4 weapon cards that let you permanently change the graphic of it's item type (1hb, 1hs, 1h piercing, etc), or a potion pack (things like 10K mana or 10K hp fillers, exp potions which are way overpowered. There is no real recast time. I know someone who has a lot of money and has gotten 55 of these).

If you don't want to spend money for a pretty worthless thing in hopes of getting a useful but rare loot card you just have to loot booster decks if you are lucky enough to see one drop. They are supposed to drop in any zone from any mob that cons light blue or higher (1 in 300 mob drop rate my ass.)
