GL, bored so I feel really helpful this morning :P

Hukku attack sequence;
full mana/health/thorns
SF/FF/MF/Rejuv drop to bear and take him to far side of center (depending on where he spawns (as viewed as a clock in the instance) there is a chance the little imp can bring adds, the far corner from the hall prevents this)

Build up rage to about 80,(demoralizing roar as needed) swipe (you'll have the succubus/blue guy in range) holding 80 rage till hit about 2500hp, then I bear regen.

stun hukku/SS now it get's busy, pop off a regrowth/rejuv, refresh FF/MF hukku. Now I shift to his lock adds, I DoT the blue guy, the succubus and the imp if he's not ran down the hall where I can't see him. I also pop off my Rammstiens at this point. Hit innervate, drop another MF on Hukku Rejuv and back to bear.

Now the succubus will drop pretty quickly after this so I usually start spending my rage points on focused attacks on hukku, bout over now.

Hukku drops, I SS back to caster MF the blue guy (imp again if I can catch him) rejuv then melee down the blue fellow (he's pretty hurt now) A wrath or MF will usually drop the imp at this point. Do not chase him if you head to close to the hall you will allow him to go further out and possibly catch adds.

Rest up peek to the balconies left and right and find which way to go next :)

BTW, I've ran this and pulled nothing but a few coin and 3 troll sweat, and I've run it and pulled 3 blues.
