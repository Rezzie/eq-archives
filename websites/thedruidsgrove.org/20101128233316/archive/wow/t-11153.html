10-10-2006, 01:29 AM
Tier gear is basically a set that blizzard has put together, that when equipped has bonus stats for wearing like 3,5, and 8 pieces. Tier 0 is gear found in the 5 and 10 man instances such as LBRS, UBRS, DM, Scholo, etc. Tier 1 is gear that is found within the Molten Core. Tier 2 is found off of Onyxia, Rag, and the majority in Blackwing Lair. Tier 2.5 comes from AQ40 and Tier 3 comes from Naxxaramas.

Everything except for Tier 0 and Tier 0.5 requires 40 man raids to obtain.
