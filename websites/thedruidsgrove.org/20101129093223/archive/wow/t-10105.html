Sell the bars...not the ore. Most of the metals sell well as long as your server isn't overloaded with miners. There are always people who pick up blacksmithing/engineering after they hit 60 and need the resources for their goods.

I've found that iron is the easiest and most profitable on my server. A stack of 20 sells for 4g+ (usually 4g 75s).

Either way....keep mining. Keep smelting. You'll level your mining as you level your toon. Once you get to iron, the money will roll in. Just don't go on a spending binge at the AH for gear.

Btw, Arathi Basin is great for iron. There is some silver, tin and mithril deposits sprinkled in there, but mostly iron. The caves to the southeast with the ogres and trolls are great. Just run a big circle around the map.
