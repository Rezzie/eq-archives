I will assume these are new Class AA abilities because of the format they take is the same as other AA class abilities.

Most have three forms corresponding to 1, 2 or 3 levels of the ability.

Druid :

Wrath of the Wild. Gives a target 350, 500, or 650 damage shield for ONE hit. This is accomplished by including a 1 point rune as part of the effect which otherwise lasts 36 mins.
Because the Rune effect is in slot 1 it only conflicts with the Enchanter AA rune and so if you can somehow make sure that the target is already Runed and KEEPS the rune up through a fight (continually renewing the rune like Bedlam or Uproar) then you can keep the damage shield up all 36.0 minutes...

Spirit of the Wood. Lasts 5 ticks, give +75 hp/tick 25 damage shield 30 AC at level 1. Level 2 = 100 / 40 / 39 and Level 3 = 125 / 55/ 48

Superior Cure. Removes 9/12/16 Poison Disease and Curse counters.

Enchanter:

Eldritch Rune. Gives a target 350, 500, or 650 Rune at the cost of one cat's eye agate duration 36 mins, casting time 0.5 seconds. Sort of an emergency rune.

Paladin:

Hand of Piety. 750, 1000, 1250 LoH type ability. 72 min refresh

Wizard:

Allegiant familiar. + all resist bv 30, + 9 casting level, see invis, +300 max mana + 8 mana regen A new planar familiar for the wizard class.

Call of Xuzl. Three levels of calling forth "the might of Xuzl". /shrugs. Putting under wizard since Xuzl was a wiz.

Magician:

Servant of Ro. Three levels of new uber Fire pet.

Host of the Elements. Three levels of something. Looks like it might summon multiple elemental pets to come and kick @#%$ for awhile.

Necro(?):

Wake the Dead. Three levels of maybe new uber undead pet.

The above effect might instead be a Rez AA ability for clerics.
Ranger:

Guardian of the Forest. Illusion Black wolf for 8 ticks. does 50%/75%/99% Haste and +100/+120/+140 ATK

Shaman:

Virulent Paralysis. 1.2 / 1.6 / 2.0 minute disease-based root.

Superior Cure (as druid above)

Bard :

Boastfull Below. up to 50 point damage and 0 second stun. Might not be an AA but its in the middle of the others and kinda looks like one.

Beastlord:

Frenzy of Spirits : 99% Haste and +250 ATK for 8 ticks.

Hobble of Spirits: adds Hobble of Spirits proc which is a snare proc.

Paragon of Spirit : 3 levels which adds 100 hp 50 mana / 150 hp 65 mana / 200 hp 80 mana per tick for 6 ticks.

Spirit Call : Might be 3 levels of AA warder pet. <-- very uncertain
