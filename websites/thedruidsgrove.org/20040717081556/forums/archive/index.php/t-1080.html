From what I have gathered about Augments are....

<strong>Type 1</strong> - these are going to be your stat upgrades to items such as +hp, +mana, gains in saves, wis/int/sta etc.

<strong>Type 3</strong> - this is just a guess, but I would imagine something such as focus effects like mana pres, improved healing, etc

<strong>Type 4</strong> - found on weapons, these will add procs
