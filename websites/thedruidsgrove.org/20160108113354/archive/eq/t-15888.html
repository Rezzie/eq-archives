LoY zones (ie Torigan mines), Veksar, or whatever hot zone for your level.

All LoY zones have a high XP modifier.

Keep in mind, that XP hits are capped 3 levels into red cons, so no reason to waste your time doing super high level mobs.

As far as technique, pull a train mobs with druid to a corner, use reptile + hungary vines + heals to keep the mobs on druid, and damage every mob in the train at least once with the PL char (use cycle nearest npc.) While you're doing all of that, use fernspur + barkspur to melt the mobs.

Edit: Don't forget to buy DS potions.
