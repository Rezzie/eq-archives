The South Shrine in HoH is a nice spot to solo with the chance of 2 named spawning. I root/rot everything but Sentries and the Named. Those are more of a high speed dot/nuke type deal.

When you zone in you just take all lefts until you get to the shrine then you take a right into the shrine. This is not the Hero Shrine. There are 2 rooms you can pull from with a chance to spawn named in each. Each room has 2 guards at entrance and 3 in the actual room. Facing this room past the 2 guards you will see a guard on the left and one on the right. The one in the middle that you would be looking at is either the named or his PH.

Avendesoral
Actually youre making it harder than it has to be ;) You can go through the Wise room whether hes up or not, just make sure to hug the right hand wall and loop out when you get to the tunnel where the champ sometimes wanders.

From there down that tunnel you have only the one champ to deal with, and if youre used to it, their reaction time isnt that great, and you can cut the corner if hes near the mouth of the intersection and swim right behind him. Or wait the 10mins for him to path out of the way if you have lag problems or cant get the knack.

Down the tube and left or right as a matter of your choosing to whichever drop off you prefer. From there you stay on the ledge and you can pull the giant middle cave, with Offaasa in the far rear, up to the ledges. From the ledge if the first snare doesnt land, and they are fairly MR, you run back through the cave tunnel and make loops (must have innate camo to avoid aggroing the cudda's and squids), at each sharp turn you should have time to cast a snare or root. Once snared proceed to kite/root dot/nuke your happy way to thier demise. Regrua tend to be less FR than feesh and on the whole easier but I kill them all as a matter of course.

Nothing down there summons, aside from named, the sentry is not named, he will not summon. Once you get used to it you can clear your way into the caves you spoke of and pull them all the way out to the loop if youre hunting for namers (Overlord or Protector). You can kite down there without soe but I dont recommend it. Some spots tend to warp them as well, youll just have to learn where to avoid. When they warp you can take up to 4k when they slap you if youre stunned so depending on gear expect to die a bit before you learn. Regrua/Feesh and Squids/Cudda's are the two sets. Regrua/Feesh will assist each other but not the other set, hence you can kite through the tunnels with no worries.

Happy Hunting
