You need IS, getting a new instant damage spell in moonkin form is the biggest buff we've seen recently, drop some thorn damage for it or drop a rank from iFF.

Generally if you are going to take furor, you want it full. Whne you need to drop and charge someone you need it definitely, not likely.

I would say think about what you want to be doing as your playstyle. If you are a cloth feather and like to attack from range, all those points in feral are pretty much wasted. If you are a panzerkin and intend to slug it out, you'd probably still be better off putting the points in resto for more melee damage from naturalist and free spells from OOC.

The only way I see moonkin investing in feral would be if you really wanted to try to max the meleekin thing by getting moonkin, predatory strikes, and OOC; or 32/18/11.

Frankly, I anticipate 90% of moonkins will go 44/0/17 or something close to it.
