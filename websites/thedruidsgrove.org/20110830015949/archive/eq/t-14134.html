Best place to farm rare trash drops is in the basement. Connected to first basement room are 3 smaller rooms full of stonemites, these stonemites can drop any of the 3 rare items. Druid (with reptile skin) + another PBAE class + any melee class can farm rare drops easily although it may take a while. Just slap reptile skin on melee and have him/her none dmg pull (this is important, these stonemites have very little hp, any dmg to them will result in summoning), then PBAE whole room of stonemites at once. Rinse and repeat for 2 other rooms, if puller grabbed anything other than just stonemites, evac and start over. Once all 3 rooms have been cleared just drop task and get a new one, any Skylance mission works. Takes about 5 minutes per run, great for leveling evolving items too. It took me 20 some odd hours total to get 3 augs to drop, during the time I had 2 eyes and 4? vials rot. It sucks that you can't add anyone to task once task has been received.
