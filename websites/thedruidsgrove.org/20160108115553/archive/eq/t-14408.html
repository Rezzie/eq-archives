L71+ TSS Druid Armor Quest (Tier 1 Group armor)

STARTS WITH: Tenish in Crescent Reach (Loc: -1399.79, -1439.05, -55.47). He is in the citybuilding, SW upstairs (corner with the Research Suppliers-Vendor 'Research Layla'), the stairs behind him.

There are two parts to this quest.

PART I

To get the first armor reward, (the Inlaid armor series with stats, but no effects), turn into Tenish the following:

CHEST - 4 Frosted Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Chest Pattern
Pattern drops from Tarnuk Farwalker (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22709), a named orc in Icefall Glacier, paths in and out of fort in NW of zone.
LEGS - 4 Ash Covered Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Leggings Pattern
Pattern drops from Son of Severan (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22667) an undead gnoll in Direwind Cliffs. He hits 1300, not snareable/rootable, summons, easy groupable mob.
ARMS - 3 Frosted Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Armwrap Pattern.
Pattern drops from Chef Gudez (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22704) in The Steppes.
HEAD - 3 Ash Covered Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Skullcap Pattern
Pattern drops from Deathshed (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22867) in Direwind Cliffs and Sulfrog (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=23395) in Sunderock Springs.
BOOT - 2 Frosted Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Boot Pattern
Pattern drops from Taruskor (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22439) a named Mammoth in Icefall Glacier. He hits for 4k max.
GLOVES - 2 Ash Covered Hides & 1 Ancient Leather Glove Pattern
Pattern drops from Whitewater (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22440) a named water elemental in Sunderock Springs.
WRIST - 1 Ash Covered Hide & 1 Ancient Leather Wristguard Pattern
Pattern drops from Vinelord Molder (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22810) in Direwind Cliffs and Darkmist (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=22460) in Sunderock Springs.

Frosted Hides drop rarely in The Steppes and uncommonly from cats, wolves and bears in Icefall.
Ash Covered Hides drop rarely from drakes, lizards and elementals in Sunderrock and uncommonly from Direwind Cliffs.

Valdehome drop sources:

- Named in The Pit (The Void and named bear)
- The Animal Trainer
- The Hero in the arena
- The named guard in the back left corner on the map
- The named smith
- The named near the smith

If anyone has exact names or /locs, please post them below.
