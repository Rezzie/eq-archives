i'm also down on spirit which i assume is needed for oomkin?



I never looked specifically for +spirit stuff as a moonkin, I would just focus on +intel as that also gives you +damage.

If you're looking to switch to moonkin for solo quests and stuff, remember that you have your innervate that restores your mana over time (though its a six min cooldown). I never had mana issues leveling back then. I think I would pull with starfire, root, insect swarm and moonfirex1 wrath until dead. That gave me at least 6-7 kills before I needed to innervate or drink..

Raiding moonkin have more mana issues just to the duration of boss fights, so ask to group with shadow priests if possible (they wont mind due to the +crit you'll bring the group)

Priest mp5, spirit trinkets alway help if you're damage is good enough

hope that helps (I think I went a bit off topic, sorry) :-)
