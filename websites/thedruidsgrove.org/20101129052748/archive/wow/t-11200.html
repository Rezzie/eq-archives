While Feragho and Rubix are legends from the early days, their tactics probably won't work very well nowadays. ^^

Probably the closest current "legend" that relies on outlasting the opponent, is Azgaz with the "Nerf Druids" series. Azgaz uses the pretty common 30/21 and 1/29/21 builds. (http://www.warcraftmovies.com/viewauthor.php?id=2299)

If you prefer a full feral spec, the guy behind "Druid of the Claw" series is my favourite -- he plays his druid really agressive, and it's a joy to watch if you're into feral stuff. :) (http://www.warcraftmovies.com/movieview.php?id=24394 is the fourth one in the siries -- the others can be found via the search function on that page.)

Both of these druids are well-known both amongst druids and outside the druid community. Dunno if I'd call them legends though...
