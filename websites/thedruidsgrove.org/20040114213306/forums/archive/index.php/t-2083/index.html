Tatankawd
Don't think BW giants will be any good at 58, but maybe I'm wrong.

If you enjoy quadding, and ME toilers aren't camped much on your server, you can run through lots of quick XP there. It's even good for multi-root-rotting, if you have a horse and epic (assuming you have ES arms or Velious bracer).

Getting a DoT group together in UP at the Elysians is also good, especially if the Elysian armor would be an upgrade for you.

And the Sun Revenants in NW corner of the grey are good.

At 58, the Elysians and Sun Revs will all be within 5 levels, so you get the XP bonus. UP has a better ZEM, but Sun Revs have less HPs, so it balances out.
