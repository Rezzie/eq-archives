The number one item I would recommend is the Idol of Terror.

This is an idol available for 15 badges from Gher'as in central Shattrath City. It is generally considered the best druid tanking idol in all but a very few theoretical situations.

It gives your mangle ability (for both bear and cat form) an 85% chance to grant you a +65 agility buff which lasts for 10 seconds. There is a hidden internal cooldown of 10 seconds which will be triggered even if your mangle misses or does not proc the buff. Realistically the buff is up about 30% - 40% of the time.

This would give you the most possible bang for your (15) buck(s). The S2 gear is totally fine for tanking all of Karazhan. Regular Karazhan runs will also help your gear a lot. If you use dkp I would recommend saving it for rings and cloaks. The number one boss for feral druids in Karazhan is Terrestian Ilihoof.
