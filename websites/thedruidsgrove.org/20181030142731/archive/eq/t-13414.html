There are a couple solo quests that you get in Relic, but are completed in Arcstone easily with a small group.

In Relic, there are 2 mission NPCs in the merchant area to the left from the Zone in. Invis and go through the left doorway, then stay to the left through the door. You will see tents and stuff. Go to the back lefthand corner to find to "Missions" NPCs. This area is NON-KOS, so feel free to drop invis once you are passed the 2 KOS MOBs guarding the area.

One mission is called "crossfire" and results in a 55 hp / 55 mana Augment.

All you have to do is kill 15 Spirit Stags and 15 Fallen Spirits in Arcstone, loot 4 of their respective No Drop items, and kill Master of the Herd. Master of the Herd is easy if you have some CC and a tank. The 3 spirit stags with MotH are rootable too, but they do summon.

The other mission is called "the needy." I have no completed that yet, but it's similar. Kill X mob Y times, loot Z...but this one doen't tell you what the reward is beforehand.

Also in Relic, in the arena area (right from the ZI) there is a mission called something like "Champions of the Circles" (can't remember exactly off-hand). You have to kill 6 named mobs that spawn at certain times of the day in the area. 3 at a time, all within a game day. They get slightly progressively tougher, but all pretty easy. All blue cons except final was even to 70. We did all six without breaking a sweat with a Mage earth pet tanking, a cleric healing pet, me debuffing and nuking, and enchanter slowing. All level 70 casters, 4 of us. And the result was a 200 hp/mana necklace with no effects.

I'll post more later if I think of em.
