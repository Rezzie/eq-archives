Late as I may be and assuming you haven't chosen yet (probably have?)... I happen to be on Aerie Peak US (short-hand AP for reference)

Quick facts.

We have 9 guilds in T6 (http://www.wowwiki.com/Server:Aerie_Peak_US/Level_70_raid_Progression) (though, don't let that fool you; to be honest, AP isn't that great on quality).

Any BG has less than a minute queue.

We win AV 50-60%.
AB, WSG, and EotS are toss ups, usually not as frequent on wins. My experience with WSG is biased as I can escape the horde base with seven on me and capture the flag. So, I can't give you average results there.

It is a few months shy of two years old. But, it used to be one of the most populated US realms. Transfers off have fixed that (several times, we hit queues in the 600s-900s... painful). Queues to get in are 100-200 tops on peak hours, usually not all the time.

With the abundance of guilds on AP, it is should be alright finding a guild if you put effort into it. However, I don't raid, so I can't give you accuracies there. But there are a lot of raiders.

90% of the guilds on AP raid at PST, to my better knowledge. I know many of them raid at times that accomodate working and school hours. I'm EST myself, so it was painful at first (raiding until 2:30 AM, but then again, I am homeschooled).

As far as PvP overall, Nightfall (Aerie Peak's Battlegroup), it's okay... Not as great as the others, and Aerie Peak only has about, what, less than ten teams above 2000 arena rating in all brackets?

Anyway, hoped that helped, assuming you hadn't chosen yet.



Also, to Eldrynn. As others have said, stay safe. Hope all goes well over there.
