Vault25, OS25, and hell, even Naxx25 are ALL very easily PUG-able. Hell, the first time i ran through Vault25 and Naxx25 were with a PUG. I had to shout all over my guild's forum to get an OS25 group going. Since we've gotten all the gear we need from OS25, we've moved on, so if i want easy badges, i start up an OS25 group myself.
Getting an OS25 group up and running should take about 10 MAYBE 15 minutes. 2 tanks, 4 decent healers (maybe 5 to be safe), and the rest dps.
The trick to burning down OS with a quickness: go counter clockwise. One the first drake, everyone but one tank and one healer go through the portal and down the add. On the second and third drake, everyone ignore the portals and just burn the drake. Watch a video on Sarth. A video is worth 10,000 words... lol... and <bang> free lewts, and a shot at t7.5 gloves! There's also a wicked set of Boomkin shoulders if i remember correctly.

Raiders remember people like that, and when the time comes that they're short a person or 2, you'll get a tell and most likely a raid invite.
