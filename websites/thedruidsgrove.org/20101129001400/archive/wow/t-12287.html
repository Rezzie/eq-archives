As I understand it boomkins wear only cloth. I've seen enchant/tailor boomkins. There's just more available for casters in cloth than leather. You'll find them wearing the occassional leather piece, usually set pieces, but there's no need to bias yourself toward leather if you're a caster.

This isn't as true for holy paladins. To solo, they need plate. They might find some cloth that offers an upgrade to their healing, but can be forgiven to bank it in favor of plate for levelling.

Whereas boomkins don't need high damage mitigation to solo. A holy paladin solos in melee range. A boomkin shouldn't find herself in melee.
