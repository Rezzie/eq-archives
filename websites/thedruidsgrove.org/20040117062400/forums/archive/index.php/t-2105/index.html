Demina21
I usually set up in that Coterie castle just south of the DSP zone line. I grab the three static Coterie and then a Bat to make it 4 and kill them. Med up and then invis and run almost straight out and grab the 3 Goranga that are out there. Usually get at least 1 add but no big deal, if a mob is snared it is owned. Med again and then run out and pull Bats. You can pull 8 bats and do them with one bar of mana (well depending on level and mana pool). Med again and the Coterie should respawn by now.. rinse and repeat. At 62 and going 50/50 on reg xp/aa xp i would average 3-5% reg exp and 7-10% aa, depending if there were any light blues involved. Easy and fun and refines quadding skills.

The area to pull to is just west of the little castle, it's a nice long flat area. Run between the big rock to the south and the tree on the other end. It's perfect and no adds unless someone trains you coming form AR.

TryonB
I found that charming a ravenous beast was FAR better EXP in ME then quad kiting. Not all rav beasts are charmable with Call of Karana, about one in 3 is low enough. My best camp was the 3 coterie closest to the DSP zone. 2 buildings with one in each, then one pop in center.

Grab a beast and lead him over to the zone wall. Pull, root, send in pet. Rav beasts are some of the toughest mobs in the zone, lucky for you. Don't forget to Savage Spirit and DS your pet. Also, if you hit with Glamour of Tunare, your charm will last longer. There's plenty of room to kite and re-root or charm if needed. Once you clear the 3 coterie, there's a whole field of bats, toilers, and skellies in the area just beyond. Literally endless pulling. You don't even really need c3.
