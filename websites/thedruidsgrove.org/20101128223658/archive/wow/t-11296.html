I kept a good cat set and a good healer set from the mid 20's on. It's always much easier to keep the healer set updated since there's less competition for caster leather. Bear pieces other than the Warden Staff and Tortoise Ring don't really show up until 55-60. That's when you can get the Ring of Protection, Mark of Tyranny, Smoking Heart of the Mountain, Cloak of Warding, etc. You still aren't going to have completely separate bear and cat sets until you start running at least ZG and AQ20.

Oh, and being the main healer while wearing feral gear because you can "still heal just fine" is like being the MT that refuses to use a shield because he can "hold aggro better with his 2H". Sure, you can heal just fine, but you're OOM about 3 times faster which at best means a lot more down time and probably means a lot more wipes. Crap like that is what is what gives ferals a bad name.
