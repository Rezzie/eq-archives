As people have posted many times before, STR is great up until a certain DPS than AGI begins to have a better effect.

AGI adds only 1 AP per point but it also adds crit%, armor, and dodge%. I like to keep my AP over 1k and aim for high AGI.

+25 AGI is so much better than +15 STR.

15 STR = 30 AP
25 AGI = 25 AP, 1.25% crit, 1.25% dodge, 50 armor (not sure about calcs on dodge/armor, taking the ratios from another post on these forums)

5 AP versus 1.25% Crit, 1.25% dodge, 50 armor.....hmm seems pretty obvious to me
