I'm sure a lot of people already know about this but there's a head slot item with IH4 that is pretty easy to get and is a semi-common drop. For druids in high end PoP guilds or VT, this is trivial but for the casual player this item is awesome:


Polished Smoothmetal Torque
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
Slot: HEAD
AC: 12
STA: +8 WIS: +8 HP: +65 MANA: +65
SV MAGIC: +10 SV POISON: +10
Recommended level of 50.
Focus: Improved Healing IV
WT: 1.0 Size: MEDIUM
Class: CLR DRU SHM
Race: ALL


Drops off PoI Manaetic Prototypes, which take about 18 so so equipped people (give or take) to kill. I *think* they are a 3 day spawn (although could be 5), banish and rampage but are otherwise very easy.

They have 2 ae's: <a href="http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1076&source=Live" target="top">Oil Spray</a> (ae snare - unresistable) and <a href="http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1074&source=Live" target="top">Steam Blast</a> (ae 800 dd - fire check).

They are slowable and have relatively low resist checks (fire and cold both land with debuffs)

The biggest issue is that they banish the person on the top of their hate list to the other prototype rooms so healers need to do some /assist healing. It helps to have a SOS rogue along to CR those that get banished.

We get 1-2 of the helms per clearing along with other minor loot which usually rots. But for the effort, you really can't go wrong. IH4 makes our already great spell Nature's Infusion basically equal to TR without the 10s casting time.

Anyway, like I said, this is trivial for high end druids but thought I'd get the word out for those casual druids that want to improve their healing.

Good luck!
