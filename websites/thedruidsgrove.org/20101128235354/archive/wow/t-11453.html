Well with the expansion just on the horizon, you would probably want to stay a feral spec. Although most feral druids have some points to put in the Restoration tree. Namely Furor which is an awesome talent. And also Naturalist. It improves both HT and physical damage. Also you'd want to get Omen of Clarity.
