Looks like the Lifebloom nerf has been changed to still be halfway decent for PvE.

Lifebloom: The bonus coefficient on the final bloom effect has been reduced by 20%. This spell will no longer cause error messages when interacting with Spellsteal or while the Druid is under the effects of Mind Control.

So instead of 8% less per tick now we get 20% less on the Bloom portion, which is good since in PvE a resto druid would normally keep it stacked ticking away.

It also looks like Regrowth might get a slight buff to compensate....though I'm not sure I see it that way.
Regrowth: The mana cost of this spell has been reduced by approximately 20%.IMHO really to make Regrowth compensate for the reduction in Lifebloom's Bloom they should have reduced the casting time of Regrowth so that it would be easier to keep LB rolling while putting in a Regrowth now and then.
