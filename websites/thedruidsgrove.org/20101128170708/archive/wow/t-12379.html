As a resto Druid MP5 is one of your primary stats (in no particular order, +int, +spr, +heal, +mp5). The more you have the longer you can last during boss fights without having to use innervate and/or mana pots.

In short +1 int < 1MP5 and +1 heal <<< 1MP5 and +1 spr << 1MP5. So an item with +10 int vs one with +10 MP5, I'd take the +10 MP5. Of course this depends on your talent spec, what you are doing (raid v 5-man v PvP) and the relationship between the stats. Ex. +1000 healing does you no good if you only have 3 int. ;D

For the exact ratios, read http://www.resto4life.com/ Great blog. In particular read the "VS Series" entries in this post (about half-way down), http://www.resto4life.com/2008/01/03/resto4life-or-at-least-2007/. Don't get hung-up with the math and/or charts (unless you want to). Read the supporting text and follow-up comments.
