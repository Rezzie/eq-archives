Ok, some things here:

You need Wildheart to get Feralheart. They are all turn-in quests. Expensive, and difficult ( 45 min Barron run?!?! *curses* ) but quests none the less.

Ok upping you armor in bear - if you're tanking you may want the thick hide, short of that it is all equip and enchants. So here's a quick breakdown.

- Weapon: Warden Staff ( 260 AC +11 stam, get a +str enchant ) http://www.thottbot.com/?i=12314

- Cloak: Cloak of Warding ( 214 AC, get the +70 ac enchant or the +5 al resists ) http://www.thottbot.com/?i=35977

- Shoulders: Atal'Ai Spaulders of the Bear ( 193 ac 12-13 str 12-13 stam ) http://www.thottbot.com/?i=3866

- trinket: Mark of Tyranny ( 180 AC +10 Arcane Resist ) http://www.thottbot.com/?i=13103
