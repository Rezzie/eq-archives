My 2's partner plays a dreamstate restokin spec, and he is going with the dual 35 resil bonuses. As a druid it's dangerous to actually cast a spell, as a good mage, rogue or warrior can lock you out of your nature school. Also, you don't have swiftmend to make up for spike damage if their dps is focusing on you, so maxing resil asap to reduce the amount you are crit helps.

A lot of resil is very key, and typically its better to gem +healing than +resil, considering how easy it is to get resil capped just through gear, and the more +heal you can have the better.

Also, if you are playing restokin, ideally you will be looking to do some damage as well as just heal (i.e. help coordinate cc on one target and burst on another with your dps partner). And a little extra damage and spell crit helps when throwing out a MF, IS and some quick wraths at the opposing team's dps while their healer is chain cc'd.

If you are looking to play a pure healer arena role, having a 8/11/42 spec and Kodohide is the way to go.
