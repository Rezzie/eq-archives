Feral druids, to be good, should be prepared to be both cat and bear when the required situations arise.

You should collect, at the least, two sets of gear: Bear gear which focuses on armor, stamina, defense (until 415) and dodge rating; and cat gear which focuses on agi, tohit, attack power and crit rating.

I, too, am leveling a Druid (lvl 54.5). Until recently, I focused entirely on +str gear (taking +agi as needed) as pre-2.3 1STR=2.4AP. That said, I have read several articles which state that with 2.3 patch agi>>>>str.

Since you will be duo'ing I suggest putting your first 41 points in the Feral tree. There is no point wasting a point on Nature's Graps. I don't have it and I solo. There has been maybe 4-6 times I wish I had it. But, honestly, high Kitty DPS combined with Dash work just as well when dealing with multiple mobs. And since you will have 2 players multiple mobs will NEVER be an issue while questing.

Once you get Cat form, you should never need bear form again while questing until you try a group[4] type quest. I have also learned that until your Kitty AP is above 1K, FFF-->Mangle*3 is faster and does more damage than the Pounce->Shred->Shred.

I'd beg to differ with the above post. Buy or make your gear. The pre-BC non-instance quest rewards are total crap. Thru lvl 54 I have used maybe 4-6 quest reward items. Everything else is from the AH and/or LW (which I dropped once I hit lvl 53). Sure, once you reach the Outlands this will no longer be true, but Old World quest rewards are a complete joke.

Is that the way to go? switching from cat to Bear? and without sounding stupid, why?

Effective shapeshifting comes when you know how to react to the situation you are in.

For example, if you have aggro from a mob or know you're about to get aggro, then going bear form is a good idea. If you're trying not to get aggro and just want to do lots of damage, cat form is better.

Literally that's all there is to it. Go bear form when you need to be tough. Cat form when you need to do damage. And caster form to heal your dudes :> Everything else is just detail.


-Annikk
