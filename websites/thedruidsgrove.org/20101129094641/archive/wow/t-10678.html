Well, the quest you are referring to is the one that uses the Libram of Voracity. The items needed are:

Black Diamond x1
Libram of Voracity x1
Crystal Force x4
Whipper Root Tubers x4

Depends on your server, but on Bloodhoof the Black Diamonds go for 20-30g. The Crystal Force is BoP, but super easy to get, will take you 30 mins tops to get the crystals necessary for it. Whipper Root Tubers are gonna take some time to get, but not too bad. I carry a stack with me all the time since they are on a seperate timer from healing pots. The Libram itself will probably be the biggest expense (not counting the 30g needed to do the turn-in) so it just depends on your server's prices. Of all the libram quests this is probably the easiest though, so really it's just a question of convenience. If I had spare gold I would probably pay 100g, but certainly no more than that.
