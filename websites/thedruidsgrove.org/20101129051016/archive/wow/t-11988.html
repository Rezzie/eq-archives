Probably not quite the advice you're looking for, but...

That bracer enchant has to go :P I see druids with the AP enchants all the time and can't for the life of me figure out why. Get the +12 str bracer enchant, same mats, and you get so much more out of it...plus it scales with Blessing of Kings.

If you're teamed with a resto shaman, then you're the only source of offense really, not sure if you mean to link tanking gear, but alot of the stuff you're using has no offensive benefit at all. Clefthoof stuff etc...

Especially if you're fighting caster groups, try to target something in cloth and take it down. Anything the shaman can do to interrupt the other groups healer(if they have one) is gravy..or if they're slow to heal for whatever reason, bust up your target, cyclone the healer and try to finish off the other.

2v2 without some form of reliable CC(fear, poly, etc) is pretty reliant on creative ways to "CC" someone.

If your Shaman respecc'd Ele, then it's your job to stunlock the healer while he destroys the squishiest opponent.

In kitty toss a pounce, get another combo point, when they come out hit them with Maim, pop out to caster form and hit them with a Cyclone.

By the end of the cyclone the other opponent should (and I stress "should") be dead and then you sit back and heal the Shaman and toss on a couple Moonfires and Wraths when you can.

Against most teams this should work as an Elemental Shaman should be able to take most classes down pretty quickly.
