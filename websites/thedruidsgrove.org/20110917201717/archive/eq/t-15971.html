The Purity is on the armour itself. Higher levels of armour have more Purity. The higher purity of an item, the more a powersource will affect it.

ie. If you have the above item, you would get 50% of whatever benefit is listed on the PS (eg. if it says +100hp, you would only get +50. If you had, say, arms with 75 Purity, you would get +50hp on BP AND +75hp on arms etc.)

Slot 2, Type 13 augs are not currently in the game, but apparently the purpose of those will to increase the base purity of armour.

Also - The number on the PS is just how much of it's "charge" is left. It's like a battery.. So at 400k out of 1mill means you have used 60% of it's "energy" and only have 40% left.. This doesn't affect the stats of the PS in any way (unless it hits zero, cus then it's a dead battery).
