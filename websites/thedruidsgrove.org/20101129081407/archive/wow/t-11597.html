Arena Head and Shoulders:

Head: http://www.thottbot.com/?i=54970
Shoulders: http://www.thottbot.com/?i=54971

Crit immunity. Yeah, it's within our grasp now. But strictly through PvE content, our option to get to it, well, stinks (Heavy Clefthoof Set, high stam, high +defense, and NOTHING else).

However, if we venture into the realm of PvP we can become crit immune with 2 pieces of gear. How? Well, we need 154 +defense to become crit-immune (this assuming you already have -3% to be critted via talents, which you damn well better!). Each point of Resilience is the equivalent of 1.5 +defense for the purposes of reducing crit chance by mobs. So, lets do some simple math:

20 res: Head
15 res: Shoulders
35 res: 2-piece set bonus
5 res: Socket Bonuses
12 def: defense gems in the yellow sockets
24 def: Earthwarden (assumed you'll get this from CE rep)

For a total of 75 resilience and 36 +defense. Convert the resilience over with that 1:1.5 conversion, and we get the equivalent of 148.5 defense. Make up the remaining 5.5 with another yellow gem socket, a vindicator's armor kit, or your favorite tanking necklace, and you're golden.
