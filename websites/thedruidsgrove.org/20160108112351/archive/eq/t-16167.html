Okay, Harbringers spire is a zone off of Dranik Scar, this can be reached via saying "wish go" to any Priest of discord namely the one in PoK(easiest to get to for everyone). NC = Nobles' Causeway another zone off of Dranik Scar, I'm partial to NC, more kiting room then again I didn't spend much time in Harbringers.

RCoD= Ruined City of Dranik, this is off of the Bloodfields which we get a port to at level 66 I believe if you cant port there you will go through Dranik Scar->Bloodfields->RCoD again.

Regular version of the nest means the static version, ie it isn't an instanced zone just a normal one.

HoH basement = 1 of 3 basement areas in Halls of Honor off of Plane of Tranquility.
