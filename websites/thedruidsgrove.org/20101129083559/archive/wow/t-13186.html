yeah, talk to all the elders.
From wowhead:
Complete the Lunar Festival achievements listed below. Elders of Eastern Kingdoms
Elders of Kalimdor
Elders of the Alliance
Elders of the Horde
Elders of the Dungeons
Elders of Northrend
Lunar Festival Finery - Purchase a festive pant suit or festive dress with Coins of Ancestry. You only need 5 coins to purchase one.
Frenzied Firecracker - Shoot off 10 Festival Firecrackers in 30 seconds or less. You can buy these off the Lunar Festival Vendor in any major faction city (Not in Shattrath or Dalaran) and Moonglade.
50 Coins of Ancestry - Obtain 50 Coins of Ancestry, easy enough to complete with all the Elders you need to visit.
The Rocket's Red Glare - Shoot off 10 Red Rocket Clusters in 25 seconds or less. You can buy these off the Lunar Festival Vendor in any major faction city (Not in Shattrath or Dalaran) and Moonglade.
Elune's Blessing - Defeat Omen in Moonglade, the quest you need to pick up is Elune's Blessing


I read one estimate that it would take 6 in-game hours to complete the achievement. Most folks in my guild report around 8-10, up to 13 hours. Then again, I don't know how co-operatively they've been working it, summoning folks in once a dungeon is cleared, etc.

I'm just over halfway done with the first 5 achievements, and have probably spent 2-3 hours, but haven't done any of the Northrend Elders yet.

Note that one of the dungeon elders is only available in heroic Gundrak, so only LvL 80s can complete the whole achievement and get the title. Also, the Elders of the Horde/Alliance require you to go into the opposing factions cities to visit the elders. I have had no real problem on my care bear server, but even there I've seen folks camp the elders waiting for the Horde to show.
