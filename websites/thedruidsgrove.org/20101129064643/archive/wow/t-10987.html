Well I suppose we should address the fact your interested in "sick damage". Choose your angle. You say you have done moonkin and feral and not to go there. It is my opinion that in order to do one thing very good, in this case "sick damage", you need to focus your talent at damage. To do that, you can go balance for spells and focus on your crits and potentially the moonkin aura. Or if your still wanting to maintain healing efficiency you could go with the 24/0/27 build. It's not sick damage, but again I think your going to have to go either strong focus into feral damage or spell damage to find what your looking for. I don't think moonkin is essential to see some great damage. I have entertained a 30/21/0 build recently that would provide sopme versatility between casting and feral.

There have been some recent threads about how a good healer IS loved in BG's. If your entering the battle grounds with a regular group you'll be appreciated more and your probably going to get noticed more in wsg and AB over AV.

People love when druids heal. And I think you'll find that those who don't understand the damage potential of a druid will wonder why you aren't healing more if your a feral or balance build. I am feral and love it. I do some great damage and tank well and charge in for interrupts etc. But I also make sure I am healing every chance I get. I am known amongst the people I play with as a very good druid because I don't ignore things like healing just because I am feral.

I guess you just need ot decide how much you'll be in PVP if you should go with a more efficient build over a focused casting or feral one. Good luck and just make sure whatever you go with that it's fun for you. Don't spec for the admiration of others.
