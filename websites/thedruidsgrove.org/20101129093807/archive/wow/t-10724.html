My best experience with LNE and GNE has been lvl 45-49 green weapons/wands. You will get the occasional dream dust, but usually they tend to yield essences. ZF is right around that level for items, but since most drops are blue, it may not help.

Another idea, sell the dream dust on AH (since its used in a lot of enchants) and use the money to buy GNE cheap. Also, look for bargains on green weapons on AH. When I needed greater eternal essences, I used to buy wicked leather headbands and DE them. They cost me around 2g each and I had a decent DE rate for GEE (which on my server cost 6-8g each).
