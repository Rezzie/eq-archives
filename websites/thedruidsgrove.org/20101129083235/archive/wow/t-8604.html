At level 12 you can move on to Auberdine. Go to Darnassas and walk into the pink glowy behind the bank, this will teleport you down to Rutherian Village take the path down towards the docks, at the bottom of the hill will be a fishing supply vendor that should have a quest for you. Take the quest she will give you a box and send you to the Hippogriff master who will teach you about flying and give you your first path to Auberdine. You'll find alot of quests for your level there.

I don't remember which quests give really good rewards around that level, but when you get 16, I'd go to get the sea form quest for Moonglade. For that quest you'll have to find your way down to Westfall. To get there you'll head through both Iron Forge and Stormwind which are good to find. Down is westfall you'll want to pick up the quests for deadmines, especially starting with the defias brotherhood quest chain. You'll find them listed by going to Allakhazam and looking up quests by zone and then deadmines. You get a great staff for the killing Van Cleave quest in the deadmines, so do that as soon as you can find a good group and have a few hours to spend. Doing all that and questing on the way should help you level up for a while.

At the lower levels instead of trying the old RPG mentality of "grinding" through levels, stick with the quests, they'll level you much quicker. Plus, most of the lower level quests involve just killing multiple single mobs (instead of encounters with 2-3 at a time, like later on). Most likely, you'll end up killing what you're already killing while grinding - you just get an extra 500XP on top of it :) If I recall, the buzzbox quests in Auberdine result in a 10 or 12 slot backpack which is pretty nice at that level. Just hang around the city, you should get golden !'s quick enough.

...stay away from a quest from the dwarves named "Large Ocean, Deep Sea" (or something). Unless they've made it <b>a lot</b> easier, or you have a full group of five, it's just not worth it.

Actually there is a way to complete the "Deep Ocean, Vast Sea" quest quite quickly and solo. It's a cheap solution, but it works.
Swim down into the ship and straight at the footlocker in the bottom hold, ignoring the attacking murlocs. Use the hole in the side of the ship to get in. When you die, and you will, make sure to die right next to the foot locker. When you run back to your body you'll be able to open the box and get the loot without causing any aggro.
