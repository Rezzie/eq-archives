If they are for tanking, the choice is obvious that you should go with AGI/Stam or pure Stam gems. Which one should be obvious based upon your role in raids: If you MT, get the pure Stam. If you offtank, the AGI/Stam will probably give you more bang for your buck.

If you are using them for DPS, I'd say throw in one agi and one agi/hit gem.

If you're going to use it for both, *always* socket for tanking. If you fail as a tank, everyone notices. If you fail at DPS, you have several other people to make up the shortfall.

Red and yellow sockets. Set bonus: +3 str
So get:
[Shifting Nightseye] (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=24055) +4 Agility, +6 Stamina (counts as red and blue)
[Jagged Talasite] (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=24067) +4 Critical Strike Rating, +6 stamina (counts as yellow and blue)



This gives you:

12 Stamina
4 Critical Strike Rating
4 Agility
3 Strength.



Instead of:

24 stamina.



That's what I'd do, anyway...

Why did you get the footwraps of wild encroachment for kitty DPS as opposed to the Nyn'jah's Tabi Boots? 29 str/29 agi < 60 AP/29 AGI/22hit, IMO. I have mine slotted with 4agi/6 stam and an 8 agi gem and a 12 AGI enchantment, giving me:

54 AGI,
60 AP (plus the 54, yeilding 114 total AP or 125 with HotW),
25 to-hit.

Your's is giving:
37 str (74 base AP)
29 AGI (yeilding 29 AP, 103 total AP or 113 with HotW).
18 to-hit
+8% run speed

I'd consider using the wild encroachment for tanking and get the Tabi boots for DPS. You're giving up 1% crit, 7 to-hit and 12 AP. Even if you switch out the +12 AGI enchant for a swiftcat's, your're still gaining 1/2% of crit, 17 to-hit and break even AP. I'd say that 1/2% of crit makes the boots worth while all by itself.
