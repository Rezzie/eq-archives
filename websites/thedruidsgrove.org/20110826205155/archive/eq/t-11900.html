The Chaos Imbued Armor can come from almost any of the Epic 2.0 encounters that have a chest. It is just that the tunic is rarer, do not think it it restricted to just 1 or 2 encounters.

An alternative to the time tunic really depends heavily on where you raid when not raiding time and the progression you are making in GoD, OoW, and DoN.

If you are able to take on the dragons in DoN, Kess has the nice Supple White Tunic which is on par statwise with Yelnia's tunic from Queen in RS. Which is possibly still a little above what your guild farms/kills. The Gaelin armor mold from BP if I am not mistaken drops in Tacvi and Txevu encounters only in GoD so if your guild does not raid them or at that level, then you will not likely see that for a while either. It doesn't sound like the Everspring Jerkin is a possibility for you as it is the Anguish BP and only drops off OMM and AM in Anguish. If your guild is in Uqua, then the tunic from Barxt is a possibility. It really depends on what mobs you take down. If not would suggest just hold out for a Time BP as I think that took most druids to the top 3/4 tunics currently available, Anguish/Queen in RS/Kess/Muramite armor.
