Let me preface this by saying that I know things on test do not always go live and can change on an hour by hour basis.

I would ask my fellow Druids to respond to this with an "at-a-boy" or thanks for the continuing effort to address Druid concerns.

I would like to thank Verant for the "effort" they have put in thus far in an attempt to balance all classes and make the game fun for all.

In regard to Druids the following has been done on test or already live:

1. 10% healing penalty removed. (live)
2. Snare removed from epic. (test)
3. DOT's made to stack (test)
4. NPC's had effect slots upped from 15 to 30 (test)
5. Percentage Heal added (test)

Please keep in mind that some of these changes required some serious code changes/additions and were not just a few numbers in a database. These are the most positive changes "ever" for the Druid Class and it clearly shows that while the "new" Verant does not like to converse as much as the "old" they let their actions speak for them.

Verant is going to take alot of heat from some narrow minded people about any "real" changes to the Druid Class and we need to express our thanks and encourage Verant to do what is right and not just popular.

For sure we can list our personal "tweeks" to this proposal or that but I am now confident that Verant is doing some real testing and will come out with some significant changes that will alter the face of the game like Melee Balancing did for the Melee Classes.

A hardy THANK YOU VERANT and THREE CHEERS....
