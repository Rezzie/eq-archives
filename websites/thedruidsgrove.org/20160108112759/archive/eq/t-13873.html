Torg mines there are several named that you can farm from close to the zone in from nadox. The exp will however be fairly slow. From zone in you go through a fake wall into an area of tunnels, the first room on the right the center mob is pher for named, the second opening on the left the pher is the mob in the far right rear. Can easily snare-pull-zone these mobs solo.

Nadox would give you better exp, and there is there is one area with a drop that might be worthwhile farming for you. On the way to hates fury there is a large rectangular room full of undead, this is the first undead trolls above the dock area (next to the room where the broodmother spawns if that helps). In this area an undead weaponsmith will spawn who can drop as rare a druid neck item that has a clickie pet buff.

Realistically tho I would go to POP or perhaps OOW zones instead, both loot and exp will be better.

In Nadox at L65 I found the best targets for a charmed pig are the undead trolls. The exhausted bloodtusk is available a little over half the time in my experience.

At L65 I would get an AA in about 2 hours, not counting vet reward, and cash was around 800pp to 1200pp an hour depending on luck with the gem drops. The pig kills in about a minute per victim. At L66 the zombies begin to go light blue.

When the vehement and furious blackhands are still dark blue, in the 50s, they also make excellent targets using the lower level pigs.

The zone is very forgiving if you need time to run around and recharm your pig, just keep it snared.

Pretty easy and relaxing zone I always found.
