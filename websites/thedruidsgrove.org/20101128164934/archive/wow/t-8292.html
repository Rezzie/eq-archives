Again, Thanks to Boudicca for this:

Seal form (level 18 ):

This quest was a lot more time-consuming than the first, event though you
don't fight any big mobs or anything. Again, I recommend having a refreshed
Hearthstone to speed things up, and you'll probably have it refresh at least
once during this quest anyway.

Once you're eligible for the quest, the Turak in Thunder Bluff once again
tells you to go back to Dendrite in Moonglade to learn about the aquatic
form. So, you teleport once again to him, and Dendrite wants you to explore
the depths of Lake El'eune there in Moonglade for a shiny bauble, then open
it at the Shrine of Remulos over on the west side of the lake . It's a timed
quest, and you only have 30 minutes to find it and take it over.

Once you find the bauble (and it can be hard with the timer and if any
competition is around), get to the shrine. The shrine is on the western end
of the zone, and Remulos can be seen guarding it. You activate the bauble
next to the tree and then speak to the Night Elf NPC at the shrine. She
sends you back to Dendrite to do the second part of the quest.

Dendrite then tells you to find two halves of a pendant, but he doesn't tell
you where. If you want to see the clues, talk to the Orc Wind Rider trainer
to the South, close to the Timbermaw Pass zone.

I'll divulge the locations of the pendant halves here (OMG Spoilers):

Both halves of the medallion are inside black chests you have to open.

1. The first half is located in the Barrens, at the bottom of the Sludge
Fen - the pond northeast of the Crossroads. Really easy to get once you
clear your way to the water, killing the mobs in your way.

2. The second half is on the coast off Silverpine Forest, at North Tide's,
directly west of the beached shipwreck. You have to swim FAR and DEEP to get
it, so stop at the bubble crevice to refresh your breath, then keep going
west along the bottom of the ocean. Fatigue sets in before you reach the
chest, so prepare to die if you're not fast enough. I died trying to swim
back to shore once I got the medallion, but the important thing is I got it!
(BTW thanks a TON to Ahnn who guided me to the shipwreck, otherwise I would
have spent an additional hour trying to find the exact location in the
forest).

Once you have both halves, teleport back to Moonglade, go to the Shrine of
Remulos, and combine the pendant. Go back up to Dendrite, listen to him
blabber, then port back to Thunder Bluff and complete your quest. Hooray,
you can turn into a scary seal!

Seal form is not useful for anything other than swimming, but it gives you
enduring breath and you swim 50% faster than anyone else. It's great if
you're fighting underwater, you switch over to it instantly to reset breath
and switch back to continue fighting/casting/etc.
