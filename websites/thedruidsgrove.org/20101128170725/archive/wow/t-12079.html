A few things to bear in mind when healing low-level people.

First of all, tranquility will heal most lowbies to full health in a single tick. So barkskin + tranquility saves your entire party in an emergency, but then obviously you have to deal with mass aggro unless you have tranquility talented.

Second, you will notice that lifebloom doesn't work at all on anyone below level 56 (i think?), so make sure you don't rely on that only to be surprised when it doesn't work at a crucial moment. Trying to cast lifebloom on someone below level 56 returns the error message "target is too low level" or something similar.

I would say just downrank your heals. Rank 3 healing touch has a short cast time and in level 70 healing gear will still be significant enough an amount of healing to be useful. If you find rank 3 isn't enough, rank 4 also has a reduced cast time.

[edit - Just re-read the subject of your post. Just go for normal rank healing over times if you're healing 58-60]
