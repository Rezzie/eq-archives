If you already are in Tanaris you can also go the route from Gadgetzan. Follow the road to the north to get to Thousand Needles. Cross the plains and exit them north west. Then simply follow the road until you get to Feralas.

A good place to find such information is http://mapwow.com/
There you get a Google Earth-like map of Azeroth/Outlands made up of screenshots of the minimap.
