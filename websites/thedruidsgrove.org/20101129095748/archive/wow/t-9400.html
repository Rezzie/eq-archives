Greater Eternal Essence

This comes from disenchanting green items in the 51-60 level range. I obtained all mine (have gotten the +22 int enchant twice now) by farming leather, making Wicked Leather Headbands, and mailing them to an alt for disenchanting. You can also get shards from the headband, but only about 1/15 or 1/20.

For shards, farm Dire Maul with an enchanter, or simply visit the AH. Unfortunately the 1.8 content patch (with Slithus quest requiring a shard to spawn Dukes) have about doubled the price of Large Brilliant Shards on my server - from 3-4g each to 6-10g each.
