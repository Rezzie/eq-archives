Levels of fishing per zone is irrelevant with raising skill. Fishing "harder" areas doesn't raise your skill any faster.

All the secondary tradeskill quests require level 40 and 225 skill before you can even start them, so your skill will already be 225, and obviously will be of somewhat level require to complete the quests. Rarely, like some fishing, will you need mods to increase your skill above 225 to complete the quest.
(I used a +50 fishing mod in Feralas, and that's it. Rest I was able to do unbuffed)
