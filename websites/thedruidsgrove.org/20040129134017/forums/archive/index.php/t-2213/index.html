Kalsii
The birds are flagged as animals, but were too high for CoK (haven't tried CoT there). I did some solo here level 61 and 62 the front 3 spawns. The silence is very short range, so if you keep them rooted you're good (I Glamour'd them to make sure root kept). Zone out is very close if you do get in trouble, so it's a decent solo spot for early levels. Once you get to 63, they're just not worth it. They will pull single; their assist radius is smallish.

Just up the ramp is a spider trap: they don't snare ;(. Just beyond that, there is a koriz hunter wanderer that sees invis.
