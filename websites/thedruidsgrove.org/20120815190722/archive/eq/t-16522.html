Bolodo
01-13-2008, 03:00 PM
You do not need to buy Expansive Mind before Mental Clarity. Expansive Mind does not give you additional mana regen, it expands the cap for mana regen of worn items. This allows you to add more mana regen to your total through the gear you are wearing. Mental Clarity does give you additional mana regen as soon as it is purchased.

They're independant lines. Personally I'd always get mental clarity first since it's the same effect except cheaper and not gear dependant.

To tell how much mana regen you're getting from AA it's just 1 purchase per 1 point from either line.

To tell total worn your updated magelo there is handy. It says you have 54/25 mana regen. 25 is your cap (15 innate +10 expansive mind) add 25 + 8 (your mental clarity) = 33.

Beyond that there's innate +3 while standing just for being, so your worn would be 36.
