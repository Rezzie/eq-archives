Since each HoT would use the Global Coold Down, this "macro" would require a bunch of time.

Umm...do you want a full stack of Lifebloom as a part of the HoT'ing up? Cause that would make it take a lot more time.

If you don't need a full stack, try this.


/cast Regrowth (Rank 10)
/stopcasting
/cast Rejuvination (Rank 13)
/stopcasting
/cast Lifebloom (Rank 1)

And for the Shift/Pull

/cast Bearform
/cast Fairie Fire (Feral)


You have to have the mob targetted though for it to work.
