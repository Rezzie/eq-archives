(Assuming Feral leveling < lvl 50ish) Imho: Agi/Str. Both add to Attack power, and I've had an easier time levelling when I kill things faster, than when I can take more of a beating.

Caveat: if you plan on levelling Bear doing mostly instances with a group, then perhaps favor Agi/Sta. And from what I understand, the higher lvl you get, the more important Sta becomes.

But best of all is as AppleJax suggests and one set of gear that favors each so you have gear tailored to either situation. In my experience, there is a lot of overlap with the two sets at lower levels, and having 'two sets' mostly just means swapping out a few pieces, rather than an entire set.

Bear: Agi/Sta
Cat: Agi/Str
