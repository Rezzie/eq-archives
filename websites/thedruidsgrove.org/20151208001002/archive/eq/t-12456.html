Titles are gotten by certain achievements such as getting your Epic 1.0, 1.5 and 2.0, getting to certain levels, getting certain tradeskills to certain skill number, and so on.

However, there is no "Wanderer" title...

A list of titles (lots missing) is:

Earthkeeper (2.0 required)
Forest Scion (1.5 required)
Naturewalker (1.0 required)
Master Tailor, Brewer, Chef... etc (tradeskills)
Grandmaster Tailor, Brewer, Chef... etc (tradeskills)
Elder, Heirophant, Naturalist, Spiritist, Waymaker (24 or more AAs into Class tab in AA window)
Diviner, Prophet, Venerable - (12 AAs into Archetype tab in AA window)
Baron/Baroness, Master/Mistress, Lord/Lady, Duke/Duchess - (6 AAs into General tab in AA window)
