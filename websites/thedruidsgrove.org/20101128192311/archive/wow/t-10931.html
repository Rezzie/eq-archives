Trust me, feral druid leveling is the fastest EVER.

Up to level 30-35ish bear seems more reliable than cat, being as gimped as it is. Once you get some talents for cat, you should start leveling with cat.

The great thing is feral druids have relatively little downtime. You buff yourself, go cat/bear, fight the mob, shift out heal, shift in and go again. The mana regen is enough to keep you healed while you fight.

When I was leveling, I never got to play everyday unlike my mage and warrior friend, but once I got to about level 30ish, I literally blasted past them. I hit 60 1 and a half weeks before they did.
