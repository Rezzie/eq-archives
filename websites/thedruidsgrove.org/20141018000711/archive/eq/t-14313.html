To get to Undershore you can either find some other Druid or Wizard and ask for a port to Undershore or you can run it. To run there you will need to get to Neriak first. From there find the stream in the middle of the zone and follow it west to Corathus Creep. Once in Corathus you can get to Undershore by invissing and hugging the wall right until you find the stream. Follow it westish until you reach the end. The zone to Undershore is there.

Once in Undershore head to the NW corner and find Brother Hidden Path. He sells the ports to Undershore.
