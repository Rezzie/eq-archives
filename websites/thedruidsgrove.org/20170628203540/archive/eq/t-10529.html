You can either destroy the adventurer's stone and ask for a new one, or remove it using distillers sold by the gnome at the BB camp. You get a new adventurer's stone for free, distillers have a cost based on the type of distiller needed (from a few PP to over 1K PP).

The stats on the wooden charm reflect the current state of your pop progression flags. You can get the charm at any time or destroy and get a new charm without changing the stats on the charm. Note that the charm stats are based on pop-progression and not on zone access due to level or the alternate access quests.
