I did it recently with 57 monk, 57 druid, 55 monk, 53 druid as the group. The big advantage to an all-monk melee team for the group is that you can FD after killing the tainted to let the druids med back up before you kill the corrupted. Just make sure the druids are out of sight or out of agro range. We FD'd for this one, but we really didn't need to. The group was overkill by a lot.

BTW, the fight started poorly -- both druids LOM. We had planned to use pets, but our shark pet broke charm and the tainted killed our seahorse pet while we were dealing with the shark and med'ing the druids. That's when we moved the tainted out and decided to FD after killing it to let druids med up again. If you snag a seahorse for your pet, try not to get a cleric seahorse. It spent all it's time trying to heal itself and none of it doing damage. Get a squalsurge horse (wizzy horse).

As Accretion mentioned, though, be sure to bring someone that is an expert at KK. If you're all KK newbies, this will be a lot harder for you than I've made out. KK is my favorite under 50 xp zone, so I know it rather well.

The sea horse is a pain. I've been on 3 Sea Horse camps, not counting my own, and they involved a group or two of 50+'s clearing the whole Sea Horse cave area for 2 to 7 hours - very painful.

Rather than have my friends suffer through this for my epic, I just went down to Kedge every so often and kill 3 or 4 Sea Horses (I was level 59), and zone back out. Then I'd come back 20 or 30 minutes later and check track. Sometimes I'd go back and kill a few more, other times just forget about it. After a few trips (less than a week), the Tainted popped and I had a 60 Epic Mage come down there with me. His pet owned the Tainted and Corrupt, the pet only needed one or 2 heals from me.

As far as safe spots, I'd usually go into the Patriarch cave - I think there is only one horse there. You can also just Charm one of the horses in the caves where there are two and have them go at each other - root/dot/snare the un-charmed one in case charm breaks. If you get a cleric Horse, nuke and stun as much as you can to try and stop CH. I would also kill in the Grand Hall outside the caves, but you can get fish adds there and an occasional Mermaid.
