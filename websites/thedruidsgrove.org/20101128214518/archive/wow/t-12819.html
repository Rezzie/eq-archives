Your stats are pretty balanced at the moment already. I don't see any particular need to spend more money to buy a new head enchant.

Ive heard people say "Oh, Stamina, got to survive longer and need more in higher raids"

and Ive heard people say "Oh, dodge, its your only mitigation"

To take each point in turn.

1. You do need stamina for raids. However, many things contribute to the equation of "how much damage does it take to kill me". Stamina is only one of them; for physical damage you also have to factor in armor, and for spells your resistances come into play.

A bear druid who only stacks stamina will end up with more health than another druid who goes for a more balanced approach. However, they will be a lot squishier without an appropriate level of defense skill/resilience, dodge, and armor.


2. This is just plain incorrect, bear druids also rely heavily on armor for mitigation. Dodge is great but there's no point in dodging 9 blows and then getting 1-shotted by the 10th.


The best approach is to go with a balanced mix of tanking stats. Use your own judgement or ask questions here to get a feel for that balance.
