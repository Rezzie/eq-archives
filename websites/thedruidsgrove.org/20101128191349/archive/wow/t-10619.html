swearword

It Heals your party

Channel means that while you are casting you will see a channel bar where the casting bar usually is while that bar is counting down you will be casting the spell and therefore healing people. If you get hit while channeling you will stop channeling and stop healing. It is nice with barkskin since you can't be interrupted by taking damage.

And yes it does pull some decent aggro which is why barkskin is helpful.
