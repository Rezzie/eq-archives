Talent into the Balance tree: improved fairy fire (up to +3%) and balance of power (up to 4%).

So a moonking with improver fairy fire and balance of power maxed will need an + 263 to hit to have the max % to hit a level 83 boss that you have debuffed with fairy fire (if you are grouped with a dreani with heroic presence (+1%), you will need 237).

The + to hit need to be maxed, you need it maxed for raiding, but like defense once at the cap and for as long you are at or passed the cap, it worth nothing.

Grats on clearing up to Sapph - KT is a lot of fun too!

It'd be hard for me to say if the hit from an elixir would be worth more than the benefits of a flask for you. My only balance experience is < lvl 50. You'd have to consider the things flasks can give you - your mana longevity, spell power, etc - vs having that hit. As a gut instinct, I'd say that if you have a noticeable miss rate, then the hit would be good for your dps.

I know for kitty they say that on bosses, hit is extremely important (almost as much as Agi or Str) until you get up to the mid 200s, if not actually capped.

I assume it's similar for boomkin but substitute Int/Spi for Agi/Str.

As far as gear goes, the T7 set (http://www.wowhead.com/?itemset=800#comments)has a little (65), 36 on the helm and 29 on the shoulders. So it doesn't look like there's a lot of help there, and you'll get more hit from other pieces. I hear that the set bonuses are nice, but not earth-shattering.

Sapph and KT drop gear ilvl > 200 if I recall correctly, but regardless of ilvl, there are pieces with juicy amounts of hit that could drop from the wings of Naxx. And OS, and VoA.

I set up WoWHead searchs for +hit items that drop from regular Naxx, VoA and OS, ranked by their Balance Druid item weight scale, but couldn't find how to filter out the 25man items. At any rate, it gives a good summary of what's available in there.
