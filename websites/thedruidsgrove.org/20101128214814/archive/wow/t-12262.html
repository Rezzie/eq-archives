I must confirm you *do* gain mana while in Cat or Bear form.

This is fairly easy to confirm: blast some mobs with spells until almost oom, shift, kill a few mobs in fuzzy form for awhile, then shift out to heal. Suddenly, you have a full mana bar.

Can't report the exact numbers, because I haven't concentrated on this issue that much.

Wowwiki reports a reduced mana regeneration. Again I don't have exact numbers but I can confirm this too. Humanoid form regenerates mana faster than feral forms.

I use Spartan, a bundle of ui add-ons. It has an add-on that helps druids in bear or kitty form see what their mana bar status is. Problem is, its VERY approximate, prone to not just small differences but wild divergences from reality. Barely useful, more annoying than anything else.
