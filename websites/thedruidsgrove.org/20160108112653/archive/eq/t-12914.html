VS procs Deadly Lifetap: http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=993&source=Test

He hits around 235 and is very resistant so you need to debuff him before landing spells. If you perfer fire, Hand of Ro is good. If you perfer Magic, a Tash stick out of Sebilis helps withthe swarm DoTs (which he does resist fairly often).

My Personal Damage shield is 89 Base

+5 from Belt
+8 from 8th Ring
*4 for Cloak of Thorns
+ Nettlecoat and Nettleshield.

I also have Spirit of the Grove Maxed, with Full AA Extension.

I have Omens Avoidance AA and PoP Mitigation AA Completed.

The issue with the lifetap, is its partially resistable, so it may not land for the full 1500, but he might still regain say 600 hitpoints. This makes having very high MR nessercary. My Selfbuffed MR ranges from 540ish to 575 - depending on if I use infusion. (9 more missions till Mask clicky).

It took me awhile to figure out how to kill him, basically you need to live while taking hits (and possiably lifetaps) and Deal enough damage to him to kill him (He has appox 18k hp). This may seem simple but he procs the lifetap enough to make it fairly difficult.

The other thing I noticed, is he is fairly streaky. Sometimes when I fight him everything lands right off and other times he resists everything reguardless of how much I debuff him.

Typically when I fight him I pull with Hand of Ro and land a few DoTs (click, Vengance, etc) I will also click my Epic and Try to nuke him for the first 30 seconds. After that I tend to focus more on keeping him debuffed and DoTd. - I also use my Hammer of Hours, go go Melee Druid Time Laspe Procs!

This is the only one I can't kill consistantly the RNG seems really against me. I can tank him while over 10 minutes no problem, its dealing enough damage to offset his Hitpoints + Lifetap.

One minor note, he is MUCH harder then VSR. VSR casts the Lifetap, and VS procs it - making it a much more difficult fight.
