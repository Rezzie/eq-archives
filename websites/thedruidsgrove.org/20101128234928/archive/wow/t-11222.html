As far as I'm concerned, druids don't need NR for almost any boss fight in AQ40.

For the bug family, I go in with myself set to decurse priority, and just do my heals as usual. I'm not sure how it works on Alliance side, but we let the shamans drop their poison cleansing totems, and remove poison (more efficient mana cost for them). Druids help on poison removal, but mostly heals the raid etc etc.

For Huhuran, only the 15(mebbe less now?) closest targets needs NR and NR pots. The rest of us will not get hit. When we do get hit, it's probably game over by then. ;)

Twin Emps... we don't need NR. Frost and Fire pots ftw. ;)

Ouro... I think we'll need a bit of NR here, but he's an optional boss. We skipped him to work on C'thun, and then Naxx came out shortly after we dropped C'thun.

C'thun... I stopped using NR, although it helps a bit for low health ppl. Basically, if you have enuff HP to take a double eyebeam without any NR, than you know you're fine.

Don't need NR for Skeram, Satura and Fankriss.

I'm not sure about Viscidus though, because my guild skipped him, since he's kinda pointless.

So ya, that's what I do in AQ40 as a druid. The melee classes are the ones who needs NR the most, not us. :)

Like others have said, you don't need much NR unless you're a soaker on Huhuran (it's still the 15 closest, btw). If you are (like me) in melee on that fight, you'll need a lot of NR.

First, there are some few pieces that drop off the royal trio, Skeram and (I think?) Sartura, that ups your NR considerably. In addition to the other stuff mentioned earlier in the thread, you can also craft the Bramblewood items (from CC), and also the cloth items (from CC) if you don't care about armour (i.e. if you're not tanking).

Other decent items:
- Gloves from the trees in DM west, hard to grind for, but boe, so they occationally pop up on the AH. Expensive.
- Gloves from the spider boss in RFD, if you're not able to get the ones from DM west.
- Pythonskin shoulders and trinket from Maraudon (Razorlash and Noxxion respectively).
- Smoking heart of the mountain if you're enchanter (if not, get another trinket from Noxxion).
