A pallies shield looks just like every other shield, so when you see it, shift out and bandage or heal...Bandage is usually the way i go, because when they shield, theyre more concerned with healing themselves, than hitting you once to interrupt your bandaging, so you get a mana free heal. Only way to fight a pally is in bear form, their ac is way too high for cat, and you'll be oom by the time they use their shield if you just cast on them. Depending on who you're fighting, you can try and bash when they're low on hp and shift out to finish them off , but smart pallies will start healing at or around 50%, so that's hard to do sometimes. They have a 5 second stun on a 1 minute timer, so do not let your health fall below 50% if possible. That's it for now I guess, I'll post more if it comes to me.

heres what i do to TRY to win vs a paladin. 99% of my fights vs paladins end up draw's, so keep that in mind.

I will start out bear, whittle them down, when they are low and bubble to health, i will switch to cat form and build up some combo points. Switch to bear, whittle some more, switch to cat, get some more combo points (remember you KEEP combo points no matter what form you are in as long as you do not untarget the player/npc). Once i have 5 combo points, i begin the process of whittling them down. Once they get low enough, but not low enough to bubble, ill try to hit them with a 5 point full energy + tiger's fury ferocious bite and pray for a crit. If they let themselves get low enough, and it crits, its usually game over.
