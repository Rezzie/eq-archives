Palarran
The Amulet of Necropotence has a downside: the illusion undoes any shrink spell that has been cast on you, even if you're refreshing an existing illusion skeleton spell. In some places everyone is asked to shrink, making the AoN a bad thing.

I'd say the Sigil Earring of Veracity is the best instaclicky item (clicky self-only acumen, usable from inventory). Too bad it's not exactly easy to get. :P The spyglass is a good one too.

In any case, I'd add Necrotic Divining Device to the list of clickies to get. In addition to finding people's lost corpses, it's great for targetting corpses stuck in the wall, and for finding mob corpses that got left behind in LDoN (happens very frequently to me).

The manastone still has one major use: mass imbuing. A year ago it used to take me an hour just to imbue enough emeralds to make 20 attempts at the mistletoe cutting sickle...

Then there's the Green Flower of Functionality from PoM (or String of Beaded Slime from VT). They have a buff that adds 50 to poison resists and stacks with everything.

Hmm, there are a whole bunch of clicky toys from VT that are worth getting. Silver Skin Gauntlets has a clicky 150 point self rune. Bone Earring of Evasion has a fixed duration 27 minute self only IvU. Ring of Resistance has clicky +10 to all resists that stacks with just about everything. Armguard of Piety has clicky Abolish Poison, though I suppose with Pure Blood it's no longer very important. Bracelet of the Shadow Hive has clicky targettable shrink.
