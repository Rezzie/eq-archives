Just going to arrange your listed ones in what I would say are priorities.

1.) Planar Power 1-5
2.) Spell Casting Subtlety 1-3
3.) Natural Durability 1-3
4.) Resplendent cure 1-3
5.) Spirit of the Grove 1-3
6.) Secondary Forte
7.) Convergence of Spirits
8.) Increasing my saves (Magic, Poison and Disease need the most work)


PP is quick and easy, hence first. Gain a little more HP, some more mana and overall add some sturdiness to your toon. SCS is one of the best AA's a healing OR nuking druid can get. Less aggro you pull, the longer you live, the more you can heal and nuke. ND3 is fairly cheap and since you raid, your HPs keep you alive. Resp Cure and Grove can be done in either order really, but both are very handy ulitity AA's. Secondary Forte is nice for that extra innate mana pres (specially since you heal and nuke). Convergence... bleh steals grove timer. Innate saves are nice but more something I consider for the high-end game, where resisting most spells is rare and every last save counts even more.

Just my 2cp!
