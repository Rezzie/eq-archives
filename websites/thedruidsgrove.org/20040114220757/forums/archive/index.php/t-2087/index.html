Jocund
So far I have gotten 6 ornate drops soloing in Sol Ro. Cast SoE before you zone in and gate out periodicly to refresh.

You will need an extended range item (player made ceramic sword is what I use). Use the post so that you fly over the stairs while the mob goes around the long way (I don't think that is an exploit - I have lev and the mob does not). I nuke them down with Winter's Frost and I use E'ci to debuff. Forget Fire nukes.

It is close with a 6.3 cast time and I usually nuke at every other hop. I am working on getting Quick Damage to reduce my casting time.

It is true that you get double pulls all the time and then there are the two wanderers. I have Quick Evac so my Egress takes 2.5 seconds and that is how I loose aggro, loot, and med. I have not tried it but since you can cast a 6.3 nuke w/o getting hit, you should have no problem with a 5 second egress.

If I don't get trained, and I keep my eyes open for the wandering sentries, I generaly don't get hit at all.

Ornate drop rate is about 1 every 2 or 3 hours solo with KEI.

Edit: PS Duo is great - you each take one of the two mobs on each pull and basicely solo your mob same as above.
