Your pvp gear should differ from your pve gear in the following ways:

- You need lots and lots of stamina for pvp
- You need lots of resilience for pvp
- You do not need hit rating or expertise for pvp
- Crit chance becomes a lot more important in pvp than attack power (although this becomes debateable in arena)
- Being fast in pvp is very, very useful. Consider investing in the arena 4 piece bonus.
