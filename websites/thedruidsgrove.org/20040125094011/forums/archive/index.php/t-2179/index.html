Seriena
The well is near Western Wastes side of the zone, btw. There's 2 of those Uthork guys guarding it. Invis up and drop down. Sometimes there's a see invis mob at the bottom. If that happens you can charm kill usually - although it's generally pretty risky. Otherwise, drop down, swim right then left and up out of the water. Lots of sirens there so be careful. It's a fun place to hunt though - soloable but make sure you have high MR and gate mem'd ;p Would be easier/faster taking a friend along. The shells are pretty common.
Aluaeia
Yeah, I got 4 of each type of shell, 5 bp gems, an immaculate shell horn, and a neck with 5 charges of eb in about an hour down there.

One of the funny things about fighting sirens is that other than tashing you (which they all do nearly constantly, don't bother curing it, they'll just cast it again) they tend to have their own personality, some chain nuke, some chain mez, some chain charm, some chain annul. The ones that mostly mez are the easiest, mez is easy to resist and even if it lands they'll break it nearly instantly with something else, charm is just funny, though if you're using charm too it causes some odd things, the nukers are annoying, but not really dangerous, since all enc nukes have a stun component, it makes them a bit easier to resist. Worst type are the ones that will chain annul you, if you get one, don't bother rebuffing until after its dead (except for clickables anyways since if they annul you again there's no mana lost).

Oh, btw the elemental guys are immune to snare, bloody annoying, don't bother killing them without a DC pet unless you absolutely have to.
