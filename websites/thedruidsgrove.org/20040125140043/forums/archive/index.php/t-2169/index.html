Intentionally switching pets is a great tactic for efficiency.

Yes.

If it costs 220 for charm and 100 for SS, to keel both 70% faster.

Kinda makes sense, don't it.

Now, of course it is situational. But I am sure most druids are going to go through stalkers or raptors or ravens or rats at one time or another.

And it is judgement call when and when not to do it.

A lower level druid may not need to break charm intentionally, it may break on it's own. Use that to your advantage. When you can. And if you get a 'breaker', you won't want to keep him as a pet anyways.

SS does add str and ac, as well as haste.

So keep that in mind.
