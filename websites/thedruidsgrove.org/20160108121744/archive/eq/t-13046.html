Hi all.

Returned to EQ recently (albeit to focus on playing a mage this time around) and while updating myself on all the patch messages I noticed they finally added in a new drop to fix the Jaggedpine tunic quest. I immediately logged on my druid and proceeded to clear the river in Jaggedpine Forest. After clearing the rive of potameids and then some I finally got the drop I was after - whisps of potameid hair. I went through the other combines and hand ins and hey presto, Tunic of the Pine. Stats are nothing to write home about obviously but I would rate this quest actually somewhat easier to complete that some of the other pieces like the pants or bracers. Don't think there's an official link in any database yet except Sony's new eqplayers site. I'll submit it to Allakhazam.

Here is the remaining quest text for the tunic quest.

Hand in the Black Rawhide Tunic along with Pouch of Polished Jade Stones to Jhaya Wyndrunner.

Jhaya Wyndrunner says 'Excellent, my friend! Well done indeed. These are exactly what I needed to make the tunic Nolan has requested of you. And. . . thank you for avenging my dear, departed friend. She shall be missed, and it is in her memory that I give you the Jade Studded Tunic. Go now, my friend. Nolan awaits and I am sure that he will be most impressed with your success thus far'.'

Receive Jade Studded Rawhide Tunic. Hand same along with Silver Griffon Feather to Nolan Greenwood.

Nolan Greenwood says 'You have done well, Grel. I am impressed and most pleased to know that you are adapting and accepting our ways so easily. Take this and may it protect you in these troubling times.''

Receive Tunic of the Pine.
