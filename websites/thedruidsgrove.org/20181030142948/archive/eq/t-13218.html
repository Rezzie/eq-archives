Read this post at Eqtraders.
I have not tryed this yet and can in no way take responsibility if it farks up your game.

NOTE: if you are changing your gamefiles like this, take my advice and save a copy of the origional file somewhere so you can copy it back in if the changes dont work.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
from Graffe's:

The file is eqlsPlayerData.ini and should be located in your EQ directory.

Look for these lines (the numbers will vary depending on when you last saw the popup):

ProphecyFirstOffer=1138712259
ProphecyLastOffer=1138712259
ProphecyViews=1

and change them to this:

ProphecyFirstOffer=9999999999
ProphecyLastOffer=9999999999
ProphecyViews=1


(The numbers are a timestamp. The edit puts the whole thing so far into the future that you'll never be bugged again.)
