The quest to obtain Bear Form begins with Turak Runetotem, at the Elder Rise in Thunder Bluff (tloc is 76,27 if you have a map addon installed). Turak teaches you the Teleport: Moonglade spell, and sends you to talk to Dendrite Starblaze in Nighthaven. You can also see Turak's location on the attached Thunder Bluff map (http://www.thedruidsgrove.org/wow/forums/attachment.php?attachmentid=147).

Dendrite is in the building just north of the location you port in to (56,30), on the second floor. Dendrite sends you to talk to the Great Bear Spirit, which is just northwest of Nighthaven (39,27). You can find both these locations on the attached Moonglade map (http://www.thedruidsgrove.org/wow/forums/attachment.php?attachmentid=146) -- the red dot is Dendrite, and the green dot is the Bear Spirit.

After talking to the Great Bear Spirit, return to Dendrite. He'll send you back to Turak in Thunder Bluff. At this point, you can either hearthstone back, or talk to the Flight Master in the southwest part of Nighthaven (44,45) to get a ride back. The Flight Master is the blue dot on the Moonglade map (http://www.thedruidsgrove.org/wow/forums/attachment.php?attachmentid=146).

Turak will then send you to defeat Lunaclaw, near Mulgore in the Barrens. Seek out the Moonkin Stone in the Barrens (41,60), then use the Cenarion Lunardust Turak gave you to summon Lunaclaw. Be prepared; Lunaclaw ranges from level 12 to level 13, so you might want to bring a friend to help! Lunaclaw is marked on the attached Barrens map (http://www.thedruidsgrove.org/wow/forums/attachment.php?attachmentid=145).

After killing Lunaclaw, talk to the Lunaclaw Spirit, then return to Turak in Thunder Bluff. Turak will then teach you the Bear Form ability.

Whew! Enough running around for now.
