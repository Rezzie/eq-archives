You may only be saved to an instance once per cycle. Also you will not be saved to an instance till a boss is killed with your active help. This means you have to have been in the instance at the time.

MC & BWL - Tue-Mon (7days)
Ony - Every 5days
ZG & AQ - Every 3days

Thhis is only for the major instances with raid content. The other instances I dont know thier current rotations for. just be aware if your guild is doing or planning to do raids that you check with them first before you run with other guilds as some get quite peeved if you get your self saved to other instances for the current cycle. If you want to know if your saved to any instances you can type in /raidinfo in game and it will tell you what instances you are saved to and how long till the next cycle will start.
