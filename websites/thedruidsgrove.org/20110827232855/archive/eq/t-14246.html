you could upgrade most slots in the bazaar. You should consider getting GM gear, or if you can't afford it, masters gear would probably upgrade most slots.
POR has some great quests for casual players. You could get a 200hp/190mana earring (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39706), a 195hp, 200mana ring (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39708), a 250hp/mana cloak (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=85614), and a 55hp/mana augment (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39710)from simple quests.
DOD spell missions should sort out the rest of the slots, or the bazaar!
