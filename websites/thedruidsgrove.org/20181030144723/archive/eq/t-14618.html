The writeup on Alla's doesn't tell the full story... here's a comment made by someone that adds very important info:

Basically, there are three ways to do this quest. As noted above you have to collect the 4 "Torn Pages" that identify differently from one another. Another way is to collect the 3 "Tattered Scrolls" that identify differently from one another, OR, you can collect the 2 "Bundle of Brittle Parchment" that also identify differently from one another. All these parchments, Tattered and Brittle, drop from the same darkfell gnolls that drop the Torn Pages also. They combine in the book given to you by Fenegar, to make the Ancient Book, which you then hand to him for your reward.

So don't let those other parchments rot, cause in some cases it's easier to collect 2 than 4 ;).
