I've killed tainted/corrupted 6 or 7 times. The tainted always spawns a corrupted.. no let downs here =)

Tainted spawns in the Matriarch caves near Undertow and have heard from friend it has spawned in the Patriarch room too, but I haven't personally seen it there. Here's a map below I hope helps.

I see you are 52.. with Call of Karana you should be able to charm a seahorse (the cleric ones make decent self healing pets) and possibly solo him.. there are a lot of little bothersome fish in the room outside of Undertow's dome, hiding in cubbyholes in the ceiling.

To solo it at 52 will be quite a challenge not because of the fight with tainted/corrupted back to back.. but just dealing with all the other mobs around will be quite a drain on your mana.

I would recommend just popping in from time to time to see if Tainted is up and put out a call to a couple friends to come help if so.

If Estrella is up, hug the walls when passing by her room, she sees invis and has a rather large agro range it seems, she will make quick work of a single 52 druid with the adds you will get if the bitc... err... if she jumps you.

If the tainted isn't up yet and you and your friends get there, it's actually not that hard to avoid tough fights (adds) from that point on. If you and your friends can just get to that room where he usually spawns, it's easy to keep the room until you get him to spawn. You'll just have to take care of the two mermaids right outside the door every time they pop, otherwise just keep pulling the seahorses from the little alcoves in that cave over and over till he pops.

When I got my crystals, my group waited in the first and largest of those caves/chambers and I would pull the seahorses from each of the three little alcoves to the group. There wasn't much downtime and we were able to fight them one or two at a time. I'd recommend that strategy to you.
