In the case of a spell focus effect like Mana Preservation or Extended Enhancement, etc, the highest modifier will take precedence over any others. MP3 will overwrite MP1, so no use keeping the MP1 item unless you like the stats.

Also, just because you can melee a lot right now doesn't mean you should ignore your spell casting schools. If you wait too long, raising your skills is gonna be a pain in the ass.
