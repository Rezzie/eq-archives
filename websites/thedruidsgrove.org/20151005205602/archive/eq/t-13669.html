I soloed in POM, charmed a skunk and killed the rascals on the bridge. Need to buff the pet and CH it. Can do the break and recharm trick to get full exp.

Not all skunks are charmable at that level, some are too high. Which means it's a good idea to keep your pet alive.

Also the ramps to get up the bridge are hidden. So buff yourself with SOE.

Good exp except that you need to be very familiar with the zone in area to avoid add. Mobs hit for 300+ max, can be snared and rooted, are social however they are stationary and far apart so no add if you are careful (until you pass the bridge and get to the gate then you need harmony).

Suggest doing the zone in mobs first. When you know the place, move up to the bridge. Only the named mobs summon (each kind of mob in the zone in forest has a named).
