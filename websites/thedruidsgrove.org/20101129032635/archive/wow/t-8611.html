Blizzard has updated their "Under Development" page again with some nice screenshots which I'm sure started some players drooling.

http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/underdev/

<b>New Features</b>
We have implemented the following features in this patch.
Please note that the New Features list is a sneak peek. It does not contain every addition, change, or bug fix going into the next patch.

Player vs Player Honor System: Players will have access to items and equipment by achieving rank in the PvP Honor System:
<a href="http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/underdev/images/honor-system/ssmounts.jpg">New mounts</a>
<a href="http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/underdev/images/honor-system/ssarmor.jpg">New armor sets</a>
<a href="http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/underdev/images/honor-system/ssweapons.jpg">New weapons</a>

Gurubashi Arena Event: There is now a regular event in Gurubashi Arena in Stranglethorn Vale. Every three hours starting at midnight, a pirate will drop a chest in the center of the arena. The player who opens the chest first gets what's inside!

Elemental invasions: Elementals will invade different locations on Kalimdor every few days. Players can investigate Silithus, Un'Goro Crater, Azshara and Winterspring to seek for ways to counter these invasions.
