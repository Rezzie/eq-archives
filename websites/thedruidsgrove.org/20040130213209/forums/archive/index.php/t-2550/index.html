It's taken me awhile, but I think I've finally optimized my Destroy Summoned/Hand of Ro/Summer's Flame strategy.

In my opinion the most efficient spell strategy is,

Use Destroy Summoned in Takish. Only the whirlwind and tiny beetle-thingys are not summoned. I've never had it resisted. As such it's the most mana efficient.

For non-summoned mobs, use Hand of Ro/Summer's Flame in combination if you can get in at least two spells before the mob is dead. Hand of Ro is an AC and Attack debuff as well as a fire debuff. Your tanks will give more damage and take less damage when you use Hand of Ro. Let other nukers know you use Hand of Ro so that they can use fire based nukes.

If you are in an uber-group and can only get one spell in before mob is dead, then use Summer's Flame.

This strategy needs to be adjusted if you are also snaring and healing.
