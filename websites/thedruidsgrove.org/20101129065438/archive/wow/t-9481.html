Getting new gear with decent AC is always a plus with warriors. AC will help or +def/sta ofcrouse. For Priests Shadow Resist or HP's...

One thing you can always try with warriors is kiting. Travel Form + Moonfire till they die or run away... they don't intercept enough to be able to squash you as long as you keep rejuv up.

Do you have improved bash and are you healing with that stun window? do you have NG? As others have said... try not to heal while Mortal Strike is up, it cuts heals in half.

Priests are just a druid nightmare when played well... In AB I think it's best to avoid solo encounters with them... that's what I do 8) If I see a priest headed to a node I'm defending I call for help and go into stall for back-up mode... just getting one more person means dead priest.

NS really helps with these two classes....
