Interestingly, it seems last patch Prathun added the spell groups for the SoF spells, likely so that they work with the Slot 3 Focii being added.

What was not in the patch message however, was mana adjustments, very minor ones, to our DD spells. Rank 1s seemed to increased mana cost by 1 - 5 points, and Rank IIs decreased in cost by 10-25 points.

Example Winter's Blaze increased mana cost from 574 to 575, but Winter's Blaze Rank II went from 652 to 631.

The Following Spells were adjusted:

Tempest of the Stormborn
Nature's Burning Wrath
Winter's Blaze
Equinox Brand
Hoar Crystals
Annihilate the Aberrant
Reaping Inferno
Solarsliver

The change was not limited to our class alone, seems the change effected DDs for most caster classes.
