If you look on Allak or Thott, you'll see some items have a number next to them (1), (2), etc... that means the vendor only has that number of items, and you'll have to wait a while (how long depends on the item) before it shows up for sale again.

It is a bit of a pain, but checking during off-hours can help (late night, early morning).
