Not sure what level your gear is at but, I got a Glowing Stone of Expulsion with Muram's Anger focus, and dumped the BA focus. MA focuses all my DoTs but one, but is a higher percentage focus, so it's worth it.

And as Turik said, they are two completely separate things (DoT damage foci and DoT AA's), so there is no either-or argument. You want both. Similar to DDs, one adds a percentage to your base damage, and the other gives you a chance to double your base damage (crits).

You also want to add a DoT mana-saving focus. With all three, you will be amazed at the difference. Unfortunately, there is no easy way to get Affliction Efficiency 5 for a non-raider. The only way I know of is with a customized "flow" (OoW) aug ("of pure potency"), but I can't afford that. So I do AE5 focus through tribute. It's 216 tribute points per hour, but I can generally reload my tribute points for a 10-1 ratio with bazaar junk, so it only costs me about 20pp/hour, and I only have to reload it about once a month.

There is a fourth DoT helper, a focus to extend the duration of your DoTs. I have yet to find a way to obtain any of those. There is an aug in a LDoN camp, but that's not realistic for me, since nobody does LDoNs any more.
