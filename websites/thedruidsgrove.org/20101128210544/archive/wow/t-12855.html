Keep in mind Dest that yes we were nerfed, but only to bring us in line with other tanks' mitigation, not to make us worse than them. If we are in fact proven to mitigate worse than the other classes, it will be by a tiny amount. Blizzard will fix any major discrepancy.

In TBC pre-Sunwell you wanted to aim for being uncrittable, and have 40% dodge and 20k health unbuffed. After that you had some flexibility based on your preference and situation to stack more stam, more hit for better threat, strength for ap...whatever you wanted. You won't be able to get 40% dodge unbuffed until several levels beyond 70 anymore, but 20k health is totally achievable.

Baselines for LK have not been established yet, but the safe bet is to stack armor like crazy, once you are comfortable with your level of health.
