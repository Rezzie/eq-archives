Feel free to move this, but I wanted to get something going in general.

Background
I spent several hours in ChardokB last night. I was really excited because I used to hunt many nights in Chardok, one of my all-time favorite dungeons. Compared to CoD it’s an easy crawl. Nothing hit for more than 400’s that we saw. Resists weren’t too bad and there wasn’t a lot of chain agro. Usually you never pull more than two and prox agro works well for singling mobs. There is almost always a named on track somewhere --- even if it’s a quest giver. Remember to con :) . The zone is a cross between LoY and old Chardok. The mobs are all based on what you see deep in old Chardok and I’m pretty sure these mobs are KoS to everyone. The pathways are wide and varation of elevation is classic Chardok.

I saw three Quests:
1) Gather four staffs for a reward --- Priest Quest?
2) Gather four pages – Int caster quest?
3) Gather four blades – Melee Quest?

We picked up in our group one of each type.

The loot:
Saw several items that drop in old Chardok here off random mobs. Nothing special these days, but did include some level 60 spells. There were two really nice items I saw links to. One was was a pair of FT2, Imp Dam4 boots and a FT2 Neck with a few stats. I believe both were no drop. We killed a mob that dropped a high AC melee bracer and another mob dropped a haste mask for melees.

Getting there
It’s really not that hard to get to if you know your way around old Charkok. Take the lev tunnel back to the herbalist room. Stay left of the room and cross the small stream of water. Hug the bank on the other side and take it back around to the right. There will be a lever on the wall that you pull down (click) to zone into ChardokB. Its not more than a 20 second run from the herbalist shack.

<strong>On Death and Dying</strong>

Well, there's some risk here just getting to the zone. It's between the herbalist and bank area. On the Herbalist side of the waterfall. There's a lever you click and in you go. Looks like lots of people tried to run down with invis, unfortunately lots of things in Chardok can see through.

Once inside, you really just don't want to have a TPW. Getting a cleric back down to res you won't be a lot of fun unless someone has a better route to get there than I have.

I'd call the actual recovery from death: High Risk

But I'd rate the actual chance of dying, at level 65: Low Risk

We had one fatality, an enchanter. I was a bit rusty on botting my cleric. :)

<strong>Experience</strong>

Well, it was just very good! Things died very quickly. It seemed like mid-200's hits was the typical high for regular mobs. Certain mobs hit higher, but nothing like PoP. Was a nice place for a rusty, botted cleric. Got about 1% per kill and the kills were fast.

<strong>Mobs</strong>

Nothing seemed to run, so unmem snare.

Poisons seemed to work, although everything seemed immune to the stun component of my poisons.
