The exact thing of all equipment works like this. (And yes, I used the word "Thing" for lack of a better word.)

If the weapon, trinket, or piece of armor says "Chance On Hit: blah blah blah" then it does nothing for us in animal forms but works fine in humanoid forms.

If the weapon, trinket, or piece of armor says "Equip: blah blah blah" then it works just fine in all forms for us.

I heard a rumor from someone I trust that in BC "Chance On Hit:" effects will work for us in our forms, but that is un-confirmed.

And for Seadancer, based on what is currently in the game, all you have to worry about on weapons are the stats on the weapon for feral forms.
