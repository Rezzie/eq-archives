Decide per item whether it's worth it to get the socket bonus.

Consider 2 different scenarios; what stats would you get if you kept within the confines of the socket colours, and what stats you would get if you could just choose any gems you want.


Agility is a very important stat, to be sure. Stamina has a bit of a psychological effect though; healers who see a bear with 18,000 health feel reassured, even if that bear only has 25% dodge (which they don't see). In my view the stamina vs agility thing is like form vs function. It's easy to write things like this off but raid morale is extremely important. Motivated raiders can be absolute jedis, frequently belying their gear. Seeing a TUFF BARE can be enough, sometimes.
