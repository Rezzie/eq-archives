There is some problems with this method and one of them is cold based snares. Until the next patch, Free Action and Shapeshifting will not prevent coldbased spells from slowing you.

Also, if there is 5 guarding the flag, you will not get away even with a Free Action potion on. You'll just get mowed down. You need a rogue or another druid distracting them away.

I've seen rogues and druids feared over and over again that were trying to run for the flag. Until the patch comes out where you can shapeshift and use trinket you are an open target to spammed fears.

Not knocking this idea, because it is still a good idea to run with a free action potion on. As there is still tons of stuns and slows that it makes you immune to.

If your team is guarding the middle and successfully holding it, it makes it much easier as that's all the further you have to run.

If a solo shaman is guarding the flag, with his earthbind totem and frostshock it makes it almost impossible to get away. there is a tactic I use when a shaman is guarding.
I jump down in stealthed cat from the ledge, quickly shift to caster then to bear and feral charge, then bash, shift to caster grab the flag, and then cat with dash.
The shapeshifting should take care of the earthbind effect just long enough.
If you know a rogue or hunter is also guarding then you'll also want to throw in a free action potion and barkskin in the routine. Once outside, rooting is on your side. Even if you have someone rooted you'll want to root the closest to you. or if they are too close you'll want to bump them with natures grasp heh.

Go Bear from once you are with your party, enrage and regen yourself.

Once the new patch comes we wont have to worry about their overpowered slowing bugs.
