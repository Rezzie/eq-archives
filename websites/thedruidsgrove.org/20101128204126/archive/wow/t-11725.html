Moved your post to the help forum.

<WoW install directory>\Interface\Addons

Be sure to extract the addon extracts into its own subfolder, e.g.,

Interface\Addons\MetaMap

Under the individual addon's folder, you should see some .lua files, a .toc file, and some others if it extracted correctly.
