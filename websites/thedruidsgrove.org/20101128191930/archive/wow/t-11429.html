Assuming you are using the default interface do the following:
1) Click on your "main menu" button (button icon looks like a computer screen)
2) Select 'Key Bindings"
3) Scroll down until you find a binding labeled "special action key 1". This is the first form you learned (usually Bear).
4) Change the binding to what you want by selecting the first or second binding and pressing the key-combination you wish to use. (default is ctrl-F1 iirc).
5) Repeat steps 3 and 4 for "special action key 2...4". These will be the other forms you know in the order learned. The order most druids learn forms is bear, aquatic, cat, travel. From memory (I changed mine long ago), the default bindings are ctrl-F1, ctrl-F2, ctrl-F3, ctrl-F4.
