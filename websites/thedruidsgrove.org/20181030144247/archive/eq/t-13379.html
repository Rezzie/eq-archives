The thing is Rytan didn't want to make the aura a replacement to our curing, but to be useful in conjunction with our current cures. If we already cure curses in 1 cast then it doesn't help us much really if the cure is meant to be used in conjunction. An example would be 30 disease counter AE. We cast pureblood once on each member and let the aura tick 3ish times to finish off.

Example of a 30 counter curse or disease:
RGC each group member once - 3.6 + 2.25 + 3.6 + 2.25 + 3.6 + 2.25 + 3.6 + 2.25 + 3.6 + 2.25 + 3.6 = 33 seconds

Pure Blood each group member twice - 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + ..(imagine a bunch of + cast time and recasts).. + 2.25 + 2.5 = 55 seconds

30 Curse Counters (example of Aura of Life as a curse cure) - 6sec (4 cured) + 6 sec (8 cured) + 6 sec (12 cured) + 6 sec (16 cured) + 6 sec (20 cured) + 6 sec (24 cured) + 6 sec (28 cured) + 6 sec (32 cured) = 48 seconds to cure group

30 Disease Counters (let's assume Aura of Life cures 4 disease counters and we are casting pure blood) - 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 + 2.25 + 2.5 = 26.25 to cast pure blood 6 times
6 sec (4 cured) + 6 sec (8 cured) + 6 sec (12 cured) + 6 sec (16 cured) = 24 sec
Overall we cure in 26.25 seconds as disease.

Note: casting time on RGC was figured in with 30% bene haste (GET IT!~) and 10% spell haste buff.
