The walkthrough seems to be missing 1 tiny little bit of information. This info was keeping me from getting the books from Arelat (he said he didn't see honesty in my eyes)

The end of Part 1 of the walkthrough says to do:
Do the Misc 1 combination to get the Sealed Collection Bag (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62813). Take this back to Niera Farbreeze (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=5173) in Surefall Glade (http://eqatlas.com/easternkarana.html). She will hand you back a Wash Cloth (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62847) (do Misc 2 combination to get Wrapped Mangled Head (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62848)).

It's missing that you need to turn Wrapped Mangled Head (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62848) back in to Niera to get Mangled Head (container) (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=62827). As soon as I went back and turned the wrapped head in to get the container...Arelat gave me the books.
