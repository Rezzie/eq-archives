Bear form there are only really 3 attacks: Maul, Swipe and Bash. None really do much damage. Bear is all about high ac, and just wearing the mob(s) / opponent down. That said..

Maul repeatedly when fighting single mobs.

Swipe repeatedly, and occasionally maul on the main target when fighting lots of mobs. They are on different timers, so you can literally maul then swipe immediatley afterwards. Especially effective when you have the talent that generates rage when you crit in bear form. Almost guaranteed to crit on one or the hits from maul.

Bash when you need to heal, or if you know that you arent going to need to heal yourself during the fight, to interrupt spell casting. Feral charge is great for interrupting spell casting as well.
