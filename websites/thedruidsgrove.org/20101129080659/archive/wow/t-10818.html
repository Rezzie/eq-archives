Make sure you wearing all leather, don't worry about cloth +dmg gear at your level.

Use barkskin while in melee and renewing MF, its not just for channeling spells. The extra 20% damage mitigation can make a real difference, and it has no effect on your instant spells.

Dropping to bear to heal is a good strategy at your level, if you have good AC leather pieces on for moonki armor it will be the same bear armor, then enrage and get a frenzied regen as soon as you can. When you need to heal again bash and shift to heal then shift back up to moonkin, you will have regened mana in the meantime.

Don't forget to use all forms, your still a druid.
