Like that, to expand it a bit more sort of by catagory:

Ensnare - PoK merchant

Spore Spiral - dodh level 69

Hand of Ro - research/PoP ethereal parchment
Sun's Corona - research/OoW+DoDH lesser rune
Fixation of Ro - velious drop

Annul Magic - PoK merchant

Bind - PoK merchant
Gate - PoK merchant
Egress - Kunark spell turn in quest
Succor - research/Kunark drop
All the teleports you can get

Spirit of the Wolf - PoK merchant
Levitation - luclin drop(the deep)
Spirit of Eagle - research/luclin drop
Flight of Eagles - research/PoP ethereal parchement
Blessing of Oak - research/OoW+DoDH greater rune

Skin of the Reptile - dodh level 68

Protection of Seasons - research/PoP spectral parchment
Pure Blood - LoY quest
Remove Greater Curse - research/ldon merchant/luclin raid drop

It also depends on your playstyle and AA. For example you may not care much about the two attack debuffs after hand of ro. I reccomend never bothering with cast invis and just go straight to buying the AA. Also with regards to AA the second rank of entrap will save a world of trouble with cast snares, yet having Exodus doesn't mean skip cast evacs. If you like charm and don't box an enchanter get glamour of tunare. Some are obviously worth more so you may laugh at the others, but it's like they say: better to have and not need, than need and not have.
