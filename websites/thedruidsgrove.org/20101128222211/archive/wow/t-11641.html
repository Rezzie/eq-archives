I don't understand the point of TOL, personally. Our hots have less hps and cost more mps than healing touch. The main TOL heal ends up being regrowth, which is quite poor in mps, assuming you have good +healing gear. If you include the global cooldown time, hots take 1.5 seconds of counts-toward-fsr-time to cast versus 3.0 seconds of not-in-fsr-time for healing touch, but they do less healing. And casting more often reduces your fsr ratio substantially.

Sure, you can heal almost anything in TOL if you're a good player, but why would you want to?
