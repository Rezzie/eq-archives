Here's a neat trick you can use to escape with flight form.


Suppose you are next to a cliff in cat form, fighting mobs. You accidentally pull lots of adds and things are looking a tad shady, so you decide to bail.

You run for the cliff, jump off. Whilst falling you spam the flight form button. After you've fallen 50 yards or so you will leave combat and shapeshift into bird form. Just fly back up again :>



**NB: This doesn't always work; sometimes you just splat.

04-14-2008, 08:49 AM
There are very few options.

1) Cat + Dash, exit combat, fly.
2) Travel Form, exit combat, fly.
3) Jump (from cliff, zone edge, etc...), exit combat, fly.

Note, a mod like GoGoMount or something that allows you to bind a hotkey to mounts/forms is very handy.
