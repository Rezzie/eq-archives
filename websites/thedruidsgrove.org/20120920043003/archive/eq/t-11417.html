The mobs in the basement of HoH have a chance of dropping both Spectral and Etheral parchments. As far as soloing goes, you could try either root dotting or quadding (if you have enough mana). There are several good threads about strats for fighting down there. Harmony of Nature works great for pulling them away from each other and you can just run them along the wall of the room then (or root them there).

I personally bring a necro buddy along and agro kite (this works great). Just watch out for A Valorian Sentry (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10425) as they cannot be snared or rooted.
