Let's see, at 62, here are the good spots I know of:

PoN: the gargoyles in the far SE corner of the zone. They are all 57, so you get full bonus XP for all of them. About 13k HPs.

PoS: the frogs, all level 60, about 17K HPs.

HoH: suits: all level 61, about 25K HPs. Human guards, all level 62, about 24K HPs.

NC: Murks are 60-62ish, about 26K HPs.

These spots are all nice, easy, reasonably fast XP, and doable with even quite a gimp kit. Trust me, I know ;)
