I believe it goes like this.

All DoTs of the same name stack if cast by different players. In the case of stacking DoTs of different levels the lower must be cast first than the higher one. If the higher is cast first the lower one will get a "didn't take hold" message. Eg in the case of Drifting Death and Drones of Doom, Drones of Doom would need to be cast first, then Drifting Death (this is of course being cast by 2 different people, if cast by the one Drifting Death just overwrites Drones of Doom completely)

This works for all DoT whether they be Magic, Disease, Poison or Fire Based. Not sure if there is a cold based one out there.

This was all my understanding of the explanation given, although I don't know anyone low enough to test this with.
