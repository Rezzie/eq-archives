Quaras Shadowscythe
Ok...

Step 1, rebind yourself if you can inside the large room with the treasure chest in the castle. Its the most convenient spot and very safe. It is also centrally located in the zone.

At 58, the best xp would probably be the ratmen underneath the castle inside the maze. They are easy to reach through trap doors near where you are bound. For maps and instructions (and many other pieces of sage wisdom) try planeofmischief.com if it is still up and running.

If the ratmen underground are too tough, go back outside where the port in from NTOV was and work on the pansies/flowers. Clear the outside of the invis traps if you can and then herd them into a corner and avoid them.

I will try to post more when I can think of stuff.

Must... have... DC

Inside the secret NW wall there's a passage, take it all the way till the pain in the arse Ricococulous or whatever and stop, back up 10" and go in that door, on the left hand side is Teence the Naysayer, he's your DC man, charm him then run down and kill the gorilla king (for practice). With a 65 cleric and 65 druid you can clear all the puppets and all the audience before 5am.

Most of all LEAVE the treasure chests alone and avoid the dining room. Bit and Byte are PITA's as well and def not worth the hassle.
You can get a DC pet everywhere except AIW4 which I just use the summon/root method since they don't hit very hard.

KnightWard
Its important to note alot of stuff Summons.

Best exp at your level would like mentioned, probly be in the maze underneath the castle.

I recommand killing Dinner, then finding the Garden for your deck, so you can make Flowers. The Black and Green flowers are both better then our normal resists buffs, even if its only by 5 or 10 points.

Most of the zone is animal based, amazingly. Becuase of this, they can be charmed. Call of Karana works on most of the zone, but you'll need Command of Tunare for the back part of Alice.

The Monkeys summon - always, they drop a libary card which you can use to make Words after getting the container from the libarian.

The chests are a pain, they spawn halfings to assist them.

Exodus is god here, it lets you try stuff out and still be semi safe about it. Unless one of your clerics is bound here with you, I don't recommand dying, as rezzes are kinda hard to come by. So you'll probly end up eatting the death.

I personally don't have Dire Charm, but am working on it, and looking forward to using it in this zone.

If your looking to do the Tunare Ring quest, I would recommand a group for the Unicorn.

Been bound here personally for 8 months now, and I love it.

Emelia Sunheart
You can easily run to PoM without a raid.. I've done it twice, once with my druid and once with my chanter. Just get an Earring of the Frozen Skull (4 charges of 1sec-cast Divine Aura, from 10th ring war, or buy in bazaar for ~4kpp), run invis to Aary's spawn point, and cast DA and run. Just SoW was fine; you don't need Selo's at all, although I'm sure it would help significantly. Having at least dubious Claws of Veeshan faction will help tremendously. I used 3 charges of DA when I ran my druid (first run), and only 2 charges with my chanter (after i ran the druid there). I never took *any* damage... just run ahead until you're far enough away from any mob to cast a 1 sec cast time spell. If you find yourself in trouble and can't channel that, Exodus.

BE SURE you know the exact way to PoM entrance. My guild raided NToV a lot about a year ago, so I still knew the layout pretty well, but I still looked at some maps. Getting lost and/or DT'd by the Vulak`Aerr script is not good. Also, I've heard people say the zone-in looks like a miniature house or something - but I've never ever seen that. It looks like a small dot on the ground in the corner to me on this computer and my second computer, so I have no idea what happened.
