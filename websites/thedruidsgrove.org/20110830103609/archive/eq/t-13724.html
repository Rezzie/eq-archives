there's 2 kinds of mana regen clickes

1. straight mana regen (sometimes has hp regen as well)

2. hp runes (sometimes has mana regen as well)

You can get one of each and stack them.

type 1 are like maelins methodical mind, aura of eternity etc

type 2 are like shield of dreams, chromatic purity etc.

To get a complete list of either type. look up the spells listed above in lucy, then look at the bottom of the page for "items with this effect". Once you have that, click on the stacking link. The spells listed there will not stack with the one you recently looked up and likely have similar effects. Click on those to find out again which items have such an effect.

To clarify some things...

There are 3 lines of clicky mana regen buffs.

*Slot 8: Maelin's Methodical Mind line, etc
Druid usable items with line of effect: Many

*Slot 5: Beast buff line.
Druid usable items with line of effect:
Scornful's Chromatic Talisman (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=30390)
Shadowed Storm Sphere (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=26601)

*Slot 9: Jester wine line.
Druid usable items with line of effect:
Brazier of Endless Flame (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=47241)

However, the only druid usable slot 5 and slot 9 clicky items share a slot 1 rune, so they will not stack. But the slot 5 clicky items will stack with jester wine and brazier clicky will stack with SA.

---

Not really a clicky item since it's a potion, but shaman made mana regen potions (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2849&source=Live) has slot 7 mana regen, aka mind wrack line.

edit:

All slot 8 hybrid usable mana clickies have a hp regen componet that doesn't stack with clickie overhaste, so this statement depends on the hybrid's gear.
