Raging Epistaxis
01-31-2008, 03:04 PM
Bartender3 is the bar mod in SpartanUI.

I have used Bongos2 in the past with nearly the same setup, but since Bartender3 is Ace and more easily updated, I went with that.

And yes, the page changes with form shifts to show appropriate abilities.

Word of Warning: if you use Bartender3, make sure you do not have TheoryCraft2 installed - they do not play well together and TC2 will keep your paging from working correctly. I just wasted my whole lunch hour trying to figure out why when I was in cat form and tried to prowl, I instead shifted to caster and shadowmelded. Reason? When TC2 and Bartender3 are installed together, a button press somehow triggers the button at that position on your first bar, regardless of which stance/form bar is showing at that time. And on my setup, Prowl and Shadowmeld are both at the lower right position, but on different stance bars.

DrDamage works just fine with both Bongos2 and Bartender3 though (also it's an Ace mod).
