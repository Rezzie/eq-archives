To upgrade to feral heart, you have to trade in your Wildheart piece for the upgraded Feralheart along with other mats. The first step of this is the bracers. The quest leads you to silithus where you have to collect 15 venoms.

The following step takes you to Burning Steppes to get Volcanic Ash. Really easy to get...took me 5 minutes. They have to be turned in with other mats that are listed that can be bought on the AH.

The next quest isnt that difficult as it is long. It will lead you to Winterspring, Eastern Plaguelands, and Silithus where you have to drop an item and kill mobs within the aura. You have to collect, I believe, 12 of each of these from these mobs. You turn these in and it sends you out to Burning Steppes to kill a boss that is a joke. (Can be solo'd easily by a 55 I would guess) You go back to gadgetzan to turn it and the quest sends you to Winterspring where you have to buy an item off an Imp way down south for 50g. Once you turn this quest in, you will have gotten your gloves, belt, and bracers.

I am at this step in the quest chain and now have to head to UD strat for what I presume is the 45 min Baron run. After the second gate drops, you have 45 mins to clear the Abominations, Abom Boss, Skeles, 6 Elite Guards, and then the Baron.....all in 5 man. Should be the most difficult so far.

Quest Chain
Many thanks to Fuzzwuzz for this info. If anyone has any more specifics or additional information, please post as much in this topic.

Feralheart Bracers:
1) Talk to quest giver in Thrall's Chamber.
2) Go to Silithus and kill spiders/scorpions.
3) Receive Feralheart Bracers from quest giver.

Feralheart Belt and Gloves:
1) Go to quest giver in Gadgetzan.
2) Go to Burning Steppes, get ash.
3) Go back to Gadgetzan.
4) Gather 36 Ectoplasm (12 from Silithus, 12 from Winterspring, 12 from Eastern Plaguelands).
5) Go back to Gadgetzan.
6) Go to Burning Steppes, Kill Lork Bokk.
7) Go back to Gadgetzan.
8) Go to Winterspring and talk to an Imp; buy a 50g item.
9) Go back to Gadgetzan.
10) Receive Feralheart Belt and Feralheart Gloves from quest giver in Thrall's Chamber.

Feralheart Boots?:
1) Go to outside Stratholme and use special quest item.
2) Get quest from ghost to save his wife.
3) Run Stratholme from after the 2nd inside gate to Baron in less than 45 minutes.
