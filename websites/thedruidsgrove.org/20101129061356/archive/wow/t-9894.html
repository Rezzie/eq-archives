In my opinion Warlocks are just about the best duelers out there. Not to say best in PvP, just best in 1v1 dueling. Warlocks have enough options to take on any class, but it takes time. Time to farm shards, change pets, recharge their cooldowns, etc. depending on the class they will be fighting. In open world or group they loose a lot of that advantage. But don't take dueling a warlock as an estimation of their PvP skill vs your PvP skill. Real world and BG PvP you can't prepare for everything because you have no idea who exactly you'll be against. Still Warlocks are tough no matter what.
