I read thru the PoP Progression (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2638) on Allakhazam and this is what I got out of it:

Go to Seer Mal Nae`Shi in the PoK and 'unlock memories' and 'guided meditation".

If you get "You have bested Aerin`Dar and proven yourself honorable enough to pass into Mithaniel's Halls." then you are HoH flagged.

If you get "You have defeated Aerin`Dar but have yet to learn the true meaning of Justice." then you need to do a PoJ trial and zone in to HoH to get flagged.

Hope this helps. I don't know if a 66 Druid can solo a PoJ trial, but I know that a group of 70's fly thru them with no strain.

BTW, if anyone is doing PoP raids on Nameless, and you wouldn't mind a level 75 Druid running with you on a Saryn Raid sometime, send a PM or a tell.
