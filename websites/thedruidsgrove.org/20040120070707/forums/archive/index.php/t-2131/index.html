If your race has the "hide" skill, you can use that to go invisible for however long you are afk. You need to make sure your hide was successful first, but once successfully hidden you'll be invisible until you move.
This won't do anything against undead mobs or mobs that see invis, but if you know none will be wandering by (but mobs that don't see invis might be wandering by) hide may help.
