Regarding stats (for tanking), I'd gem for +sta until you have ~16K in bear form. Then gem +agi. Always enchant +agi (or +stats).

For your kitty set, just use...

http://www.emmerald.net/ (The Cat-DPS links, not the high-DPS links)

I use an addon called Pawn that allows you give items values based on their stats. I take the numbers from Emmerald's list (on the info tab) to create a bear and kitty value for each item. It helps to compare items in game. Though sometimes the lists values don't always reflect an item's true value if they have "on use" entries, weird procs, set bonuses, etc.

The most important thing for you right now is to get a weapon that gives "attack power in feral forms".
This will increase your damage output drastically. If I were you, I would focus on this task to the exclusion of all else.

If you can get a 5 man group together, there is a quest at the arena in north Nagrand which gives a blue reward called Staff of Beasts.

With regards to Hit Rating.
This only becomes truly important once you are raiding, and most of your gear is of Epic (purple) quality. At this point Hit Rating becomes a useful way to increase your damage output.
If you get 142 hit rating, your attacks will never miss - even against a raid boss.
142 hit rating = 9% "chance to hit".
