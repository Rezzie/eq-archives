Nytewalker
10-04-2005, 11:19 AM
/agree 100%

I built my first computer from scratch instead of upgrading an old one and went for the top of the line. Of course after 2 months it wasn't top of the line anymore so I felt bad about that $500 card I splurged on. It is capable of resolutions twice of what my monitor is capable so most of it is useless for me. In all honesty, unless you play a FPS and reaction time is the key to victory you won't even use the top of the line capabilities. In MMORPGs, even with an excellent connection, lag will reduce the gameplay edge you would get no matter what you do; so it just comes down to getting a card that will allow you to play in any area with any number of people without dragging your performance down to unplayable levels. I would say just about anything with 256MB will do it until the next wave of games come out that requires the next generation cards. I know its hard to resist, but keep that extra money, spend it on a better keyboard/mouse etc. Something that will reduce the carpal tunnel from constantly playing and you will thank yourself for it.
