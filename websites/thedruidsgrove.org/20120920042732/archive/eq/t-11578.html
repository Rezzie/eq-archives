I'm guessing the primary reason is because they're from different expansions, and there wasn't a level addition with the new expansion. So, TPTB had to come up with something that would feel like an upgrade and be worth wanting, at least a little without making it overpowering or something.

This is from the spell list on this very site.

60 - Mask of the Hunter - 100 - Kunark Drop
60 - Mask of the Stalker - 150 - Luclin Drop

I remember being REALLY glad to get Stalker because I could recast after I died on raids, all of which seem to be in indoor zones at that point.
