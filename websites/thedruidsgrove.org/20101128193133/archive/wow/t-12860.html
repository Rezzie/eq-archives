Welcome new druid!! Welcome to our section of the world.

For druids its not exactly that. I'm sure Annik will be along shortly with the equivalency numbers today, but in the expansion we'll want more rogue stats than we do today. Today a lot of times we stack more strength to get a lot of AP. Our base damage isn't that high so unlike Rogues who can up their damage by getting a higher DPS weapon we have to hit raw AP bonuses to up our DPS.

There are two schools of thought here. One is balance the stats and stack some decent Agility and Stength. We'll get more AP point for point out of Strength than agility, but arguably we lose a decent amount of crit as well. The second would involve stacking rogue gear for all of the extra Agility, but their AP would probably be a waste for us point for point... so its kind of a wash. We'll get more crit and in some cases damage out of the higher AGI on rogue gear, but we won't necessarily have the extra stamina and strength and armor that the feral druid gear has.

The good news is that our itemization closely aligns with rogues in the expansion so.. this may be an academic exercise at best right now.

Annikk
10-01-2008, 04:26 AM
As Kheldar says, provided you have all 3 points in the Survival of the Fittest talent, you need a further 2.6% crit reduction to become uncrittable even by bosses.

You can get that 2.6% from Defense Skill and Resilience. Just mouse over these stats and look at the tooltip - it directly tells you how much percent your resilience (or defense) is currently doing.
Just add the two numbers together - if they add up to more than 2.6%, you're sorted :>
