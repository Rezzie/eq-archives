AV is all about range. Stick with your group you are healing and back from the fighting and you will do great.

Don't miss out on the fun of dropping some nukes though. My personal favorite is when the hordies clump up on the flag defending a graveyard and then our melee rushes in. There is so much going on they don't even notice I have 8-10 of them in my hurricane.

Terrain can be huge factor as well. While the melee forces are advancing more or less along the road you can be climbing adjacent hills, etc and dropping heals while staying out of most players field of vision so you don't get targeted.

AV is my favorite thing to do with my moonkin right now, I just wish I could get in more nights (3-4 hour wait on my server).
