It's a proc from an item set - Thekal's and Arlokk's Grasps from ZG. It's something like 2-3% chance on atk to transform into Tony the Tiger (phase 2 Thekal). Buff is +300 atk power.

Since it requires both weapons, most people acquiring this set are Hunters - the proc works off of ranged attacks as well. Neither Fist weapon (they are pimp looking claws) is all that great as far as stats go. More of a vanity set than anything.

That said, my hunter has Arlokk's and is waiting for the next Thekal drop.
