It has been a week since I posted the Are Priest Classes Balanced? Poll (http://forums.thedruidsgrove.org/showthread.php?t=8248). So far, a large majority of 38 out of 42 (90.5%) says classes are not balanced. The remaining 9.5% says classes are balanced.

Based on the results, this poll will focus on what mechanisms the community believes will will bring about balanced priest classes. Obviously there are many ideas floating around at the moment so I can't possibly list every idea. What I will do instead is to group similiar ideas together and do a more detailed drill-down poll a few weeks later.

Personal plea: Try to vote for what you think is best for Everquest overall, rather than vote for what is best for yourself or your class. So please take into consideration grouping and raiding issues before you vote.
