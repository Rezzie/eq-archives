Um first off, attacking someone while they are fighting mobs and are low on health is pretty bastard. Thats how you get camped. Trust me, when people do that to me, assuming I can find them again they see me a LOT after that.

Secondly, Green Whelp Armor (you can wear at lv 30) helps bigtime (5% chance to put the attacker to sleep when hit), though this is getting nerfed for people past lv 50 in the next patch.

Third, root is a great form of crowd control. Root one of them (preferably the non ranged attacker.. no point in rooting a mage, lock, hunter, etc.) then go to town on the other. Root the melee, get away from it, cat form + dash and shred the cloth wearer. If you are fighting two heavy ac classes, root the non-ranged, then fearal charge the ranged attacker and maul ftw. Remember to use all of your healing abilities and bear form and you should be ok. Roo
