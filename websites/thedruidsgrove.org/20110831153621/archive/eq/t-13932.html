I remember Sonorous! I saw you in PoK and remembered you, too. I used to come to ToV raids, lol.

You'll find quite a few old Sonorous in Exemplar these days, if you want to look them up.

For an easy back item (if you still have PoG faction), check out the Tree Heaven quest.

There is a mission in Eldaar Forest that is a simple delivery quest. You hail Speaker Grayleaf which is a treant near the Arch Druid. After some dialog he will offer two missions. Select Tree Heaven and you will be given a seed to deliver to Plane of Growth.

Head to Tunare's Tree and up the ramp and back around the platform is the npc to turn it into.

Caster reward is: Crimson Cloak of Moonwaters (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39692)
