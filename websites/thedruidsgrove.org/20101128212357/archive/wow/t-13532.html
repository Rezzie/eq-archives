You can get away with ~55-60% raid buffed crit if your ArP is high. Pure agility stacking is pretty much win until you're at like 700 ArP. Then just gem straight ArP.

I'm going to disagree on gemmign ArP once you hit 62% crit though. Agility is still better until your ArP is at higher levels because ArP scales proportionally versus linearly.

The more ArP you have the more value a 20 ArP gem is going to be. So if you're at 64% crit and only for some reason have 200-400 ArP, just stay with Agi gemming. The safe level to switch to pure ArP gemming is generally around 650+ ArP through gear.

You should really never gem attack power. Just gem ArP. Or agility.

If you're going to gem straight ArP by the way, find more ArP gear. Gemming pure ArP is still going to only bring you up to about 550-ish. Ideally you want to hit soft cap with some sort of ArP trinket proc. That's around...1300? So basically like NES procs for 600-ish ArP, you want to aim for around 650 ArP with gear. Maybe replace your Dalaran ring with the triumph badge DPS ring.

The Herkumi War Token is not a really good trinket for DPS either. You'd be better off farming Heroic Forge of Souls for the Needle Encrusted Scorpion.

Also if you're in t10 content, I'd go for my 2pt10 first. The 2pt10 bonus is pretty awesome-sauce. Looking at your gear though 5.5-6k shouldn't be too hard if you smooth out your rotation.
