TristamDk
Once you master charming outside, I would recommend trying to charm using the chokdai in chardok. This was probably one of the most fun things I have ever done in the game, I got insane exp, and charming in confined spaces is probably one of the more fun and useful soloing techniques I use. Start off by getting a kei and using call of karana to take the ZI/ZO with the sniffers and eventually move to pulling the fort. Once you've trivialized this, go past the fort, fall down the pit, hang a left and stay left to a little hallway with bigger chokdais and some random slaves scattered about. This is where the fun starts because you get an insanely better pet than the sniffers and you can just rip through the sarnaks with a bit of room to run. The one tip I would give you is if you haven't cleared the whole hall or broken the spawn, keep the mob you're fighting and any adds rooted and snared and root your pet if you dont have room to get another charm off. Also, never hesitating to gate should save you plenty of deaths. It really gets the heart pumping, Id recommend it
