siamesecyborg asked:
What is the safest way to camp the arena?

Kill the 6 mobs that can see invisible leading to the arena ramp, kill two guards at top of ramp, kill the guard on wall across from the ramp. This is your camp. If you can't manage this part, you will not be able to handle the mobs inside the arena. Arena mobs see invisible and summon. Most of the arena mobs are melee, a few are cleric / enchanter / wizard. Best solo druid tactic is to charm one of the dire wolves near the bank. Avoid Derakor the Vindicator, he is level 70 and doesn't drop quest armor components.
