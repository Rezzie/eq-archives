10-01-2007, 08:56 AM
Efreeti in Atiki. Three to four AAs/hour, depending on the mix of mobs and your ability to maintain a rhythm. They can be snared and rooted and do not summon (even the named Hand of X mobs). A set of six dots (including clicky swarm) will take them roughly to half health. Start round #2 then pull another. Lather, rinse, repeat. You will need 2 junk buffs in front for sorcerors, who occasionally dispel. Enthrallers require 4 junk buffs in front. Both enthrallers and sorcerors can gate (lords are straight melee). However, as with all gating mobs, they do not gate if they don't have line of sight to you - so laying down enough dots to drop them to low health and ducking around the corner will remove this annoyance. There are pathing glitches if you climb up on hills, but aggro radius is very small - e.g. if you see 2 efreeti standing on both sides of a staircase you can pull one without drawing the other.

Animal charming in Direwind can be pretty good experience as well, but the roots are probably the fastest for soloers still.
