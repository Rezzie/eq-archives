Welcome back! I came back about a month ago after a 5 year hiatus. The change in AA's xp is amazing. I remember having to spend a full night in serious grind groups to get one or two. Now I can get 5+ an hour.

I dusted off my old warrior as well, transferred him to another account, got him to 65 (from 57) and have been plugging away at aa's on both while boxing with a tank and healer merc. Druid has gained almost 200 aa's and the warrior about 150.

Coming back has been kind of overwhelming. So much new content and so much missed. I finally gave up trying to absorb it all and have decided to work on my Valdeholm faction while killing stuff in the Iceroot Falls (70 hot zone).

I'm going to make the leap to 71/72 here this xp bonus weekend (or collapse trying lol) to get to the new charm and new gear i've stocked up for the level. Then its another grind to get at least another few hundred AA.

In any case, the zones (at least on AB) are fairly sparse. Not lots of competition like back in the day. Folks are still generally courteous and helpful. I might also suggest that you pick up the 'gate' aa and bind yourself next to the emissary in PoK to get access to the plane of time/void stuff for when higher level groups announce that items are rotting. At 65 (with innate camo aa) you can go a lot of places to pick up rotting augs and gear quickly to help you prep for the 80+ transition.

Also, don't forget about the LoN (legends of norrath) game. You'll see it as a link on your 'eq' interface buttons. You can pick up a familiar that'll help you with your mana pool and/or regen. Lots of different choices there. It only makes me sad that you can't be in illusion form and on a mount at the same time :-(

Anywho, enjoy the return. I smack myself for not keeping up with the game over the years.
