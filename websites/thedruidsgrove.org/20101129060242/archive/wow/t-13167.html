The big problem that no one has addressed yet is that DPS PVE gear scaled considerably better than Resil gear. So right not in arena's its not about staying alive longer, its about burst DPSing down one of their members first. I run with a 3v3 team of Resto Druid, Boomkin, DK and we are right around 1450, so not great. Pallies, Hunters, and DK's were hands down our biggest weakness. All of the above did get nerfed yesterday, so only time will tell if they are still an issue.

Basic stragegy, leave your pvp gear in the bank! The Resil doesn't make up for the lost DPS. When I put on my pvp gear, (mostly savage) I sit around 550 resil, but my spell power drops to about 1250. In PvE gear my spell power is ~2k.

Also, with the "Rating" system on the higher end arena gear, Blizz has created a cycle, where players can't get high end gear because their rating is too low, and they can't get rating, because their gear is too low.
