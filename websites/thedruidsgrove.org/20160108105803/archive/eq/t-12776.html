yeah, diving for lava rocks, from the dwarf on keepers faction.
Park BL at zone , have egress memmed, there are some see invis, just follow the lava and look for purple blobs sticking out of the lava, wack it once or so and get your rock. Be warned, some of the rocks have drakes in them, but they dont hit hard, and you can egress.
I can usually burn through the 15 drakes in 30 minutes (for lesson), takes 45min to 75 minutes in total, usually about an hour to do.
Ive noticed the lava rocks spawn in the same areas most of the time, so this is an easy, relatively safe way to get some crystals and exp.
You need 3 to get it, and having a dps in group really speeds the drake killing up at least, but its very doable with 2 people.
With a BL tanking hand of ro and suns corona are your friend..i cast both on every mob, every time (unless its light blue or green hehe).
Fire lands great on the drakes in lavaspinners, go figure :)
