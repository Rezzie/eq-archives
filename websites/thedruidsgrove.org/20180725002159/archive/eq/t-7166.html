Someone mention charming? Here are some options (leaving out DC'able mobs)

PoV: Charm a frog (some are snareable) and take it to kill golems, etc. I used one to get the sacred waters from the armored guys for the Aid Grimel quest (did it twice over, in fact).

PoS: Loroks are not limited to lorok v. lorok combat, although many folks think so. Take that lorok and go kill giants at any of the 3 camps. Or take it to the front cave and use it on the frogs there. Heck, I used a lorok to kill the bees, too.

Natimbi: Lots of people have been charming there. Too low level for me.

Tipt and Vxed: Some of the hynids are level 60 (many are not, though). Charm one of these little monsters, haste it, and go to town. In fact, the time I completed Tipt for the first time, I charmed the hynid right at the very beginning and took it all the way to the end.
