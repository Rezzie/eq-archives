There's no stat that increases it that I know. What I've done with skills like that on alts is to make a key for a skill that you use a lot (like tracking) and use the /doability command for both forage and tracking on the same key. Then every time you track, you forage if the ability is available. I used to throw sense heading in there too, but from what I hear you don't need to do that any more.

Since I quit I've heard there's a new command (/autoinv or something) that will automatically drop whatever you forage into your inventory. Add that to your key as well :)

Wisdom (if higher than intelligence) increases skill gain rate. At 177, it's not unusual at all to go 30 attempts (or even 50-100) without a skill gain, so nothing is wrong. Just keep trying.

I suggest the following hotkey:

/sit off
/doability X (where X = your forage #)
/pause 5 (you might need more than 5 on a slow system)
/autoinventory

Just press it every 2 minutes wherever you go and you'll get 200 forage in no time (along with a lifetime supply of water, rabbit meat, and roots).

You can also assign forage to a hotkey, go into the keyboard tab in options, and find the listing for the indivual hotkeys. Make the alternate for the hotkey forage is assigned to the same as one of your movement keys. Every time you move in that direction you'll forage if the button isn't greyed out.
