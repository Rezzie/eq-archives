EXP is far more important for a tank than +HIT.
Why?
- the boss cant dodge anymore when u have EXP at 6.5% (so it is a similar effect as HIT -> more aggro)
- the bosses chance to parry is reduced (cap is at 15% for lvl83 mobs), why is that good -> everytime a boss parries, his attack speed for the next hit is increased -> more damage from boss.
