Some things with mana regen stack--example, pot9 and KEI.

Others don't--example, spirit of oak and pot9.

It all depends on the spells--you can tell from the spell database (lucy.fnord.net if you really want to dig into that), but otherwise there's no surefire way to figure just from the spell description whether or not it will stack.
