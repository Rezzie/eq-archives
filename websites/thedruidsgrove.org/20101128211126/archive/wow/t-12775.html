You are WAY over the crit cap. You can afford to drop a lot of defense/resilience in favour of more stamina, armor, and agility.

Honestly right now the biggest need for improvement is neither your chest, legs, or boots - it's your weapon. Consider asking your guild to help you grind to Cenarion Expedition Exalted - the easiest way would be a few Steam Vaults (non-heroic) runs. This will allow you to use Earthwarden (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=29171), a much better tanking weapon (although not as good for dps - keep your S1 Mace as well).

Gladiator gear is the most obvious source of replacements for the 3 slots you mentioned. It's either that or try to muster enough people for an attempt on Magtheridon - he has been nerfed a lot and is now very easy to learn and kill. He drops your T4 chest, which would make an excellent replacement for Clefthoof stuff. There are probably other items you could replace stuff with, eg the badge items, but I cannot think of any off hand.

Also, just between the two of us, I STILL use the Clefthoof Boots for tanking.. ;)

Edit - Added the following explanation to dispel any uncertainty about the crit cap.

How to tell if you are at the crit cap

1. Bosses have a base 5.6% chance to crit you.
2. You can reduce that chance by 3% by speccing 3/3 Survival of the Fittest
3. Therefore, you require an ADDITIONAL 2.6% to reach the crit cap.

- Click the Defenses tab in your character sheet
- Mouse-over the Defense Skill tooltip - it will show your crit reduction
- Mouse-over the Resilience tooltip - it will show your crit reduction
- Add the two numbers together - if it's more than 2.6%, you are over the cap!

There was, I don't remember it offhand though and besides I heard rumours that it no longer works

/script DEFAULT_CHAT_FRAME:AddMessage(2.6-(GetCombatRatingBonus(CR_DEFENSE_SKILL)*.04+GetCom batRatingBonus(CR_CRIT_TAKEN_MELEE)),1,0.5,0)
If the number is 0 or negative, you are uncritable. If the number is above 0, you require more defense or resilience vs level 73 bosses.

This works, but it assumes you are lvl 70, have a base defense of 350 and have max SotF.

The "rumors" started because it often takes several weeks to reach 350 base defense. I, for one, was only at 341 (?), when I hit lvl 70 and it was 4-5 weeks later before I reached 350. And I had very little +def (except what the Clefthoof set provided) so the script gave a false positive result.

Today with free PvP gear everywhere (http://www.wowhead.com/?itemset=744), you can be uncritable the second you ding 70. Combined with the clefthoof set you'll be well beyond uncritable.

So, there is no reason to ever waste a gem or an enchant for +def. As +def and +res abound everywhere. Go back 8+ months, and it was a bit more difficult to achieve.
