Spell hit>spell dmg>spell crit.

Any gem slots I have I put in spell hit gems. Right now I have 3pieces of season 1 pvp gear as there isn't much caster leather to be found in raids.

I also highly suggest badge gear. The shoulders are awesome and so is the belt. Right now I'm saving up for the boots. Oh and don't forget the idol! It rocks and procs a lot.

Anything that is an upgrade from any leather that you're wearing (including cloth) is worth getting. Since there isn't caster leather in raids then wear cloth or badge gear until you can get your hands on tier pieces.
