A good number of people kite either in Icefall, Direwind or Katta Casturm.

Kitting in Icefall and Direwind will give you a random chance to obtain level 71-73 Rank II spells.

Kitting at the Greenhouse in Katta Casturm will give you access to Combine Merchant faction, and thus the Rank II spells from TBS. You can run the Serpent Slayer Quest and the Herb Retrival quest at the same time, and work faction that way. You will need to either purchase or do mission for the Orum to purchase the spells.

Groupwise, Valdehom, Ashengate and Frostcrypt are fairly popular, with other people doing TBS missions.

-Fenier
