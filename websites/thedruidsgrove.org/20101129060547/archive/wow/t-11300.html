The OP was asking about PvP, not raiding. In PvP against most classes, agi/crit is king because you only get a couple hits before you are dead.

You aren't going to outlast a warlock, shaman, shadow priest, stun lock rogue, or fire mage. If you can't kill them in the first few seconds, you are a dead druid.

You probably aren't going to outlast an arms/fury warrior or a PvP hunter.

You can't outlast a Paladin, but he can't kill you either.
