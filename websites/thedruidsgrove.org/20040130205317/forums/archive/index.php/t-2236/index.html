Accretion
I spent a lot of time in Dreadlands and Burning Woods at 52-54. In DL, I would usually track dark blues (Drolvarg Ragers, Ravaging Drolvargs and occasionally a spider or two) and cook them on the spot. Their HPs are low enough that it actually goes pretty fast. Burning Woods has a good quadding spot in the SW corner near the bee hive. Again you have to be selective (I usually could only find 2-3 dark blues per quad) but xp is decent and you get an occasional spell drop.

Alternatively, Gnolls in JP (Root/DoT) or Raptors in TD (charm or quad) are good options.

KrystofC
2 good quadding spots at that level are Eastern Wastes, SW corner, at the Ulthork Man-O-War camp (might have to clear blue/lt-blue walruses to get em to spawn) and Cobalt Scar wyverns (rather over-camped, depending on the time of day you're playing). Skyfire in the corner where Ragefire spawns is also a good place to root/cook -- as long as you know which mobs summon and avoid them. Chromodracs here have a nasty dispel, but you should be able to root/rot em well out of their dispel range and dance in and out occasionally to get HP updates.
