What happens is, when you kill one guard, his ability (Mindwarp, or Bodywarp, depending on which guard you chose to kill first) is transferred to the Commander, thus making him harder to kill. Here is the gist of the event.

You must kill one guard before engaging Commander. Each guard has a AE..Spiritlord Mind= Debuff AE...Spiritlord Body=Lifetap AE. Kill one and Commander is attackable but gets the AE the dead guard had. In Beta, the guards were tethered, not sure about live. The AE's were resistable, also (prismatic). Both Spiritlords hit for around 1500 and were splitable. Rahtiz (GM) said he might change this before Live.

We engaged Spiritlord Mind first. The Debuff AE landed sporadically...we noticed that it also slowed our cast time...not good, but RGC cured Mindwarp. Spiritlord Mind went down pretty fast.

Next, Mastruq Commander Gorkakt: While Spiritlord Body was offtanked (his lifetap was in the 500 range but expect it to be more in live encounter), we engaged.
Commander hit for about 2800 and using the AE Debuff made this event pretty tough. Rahtiz poofed the Commander midfight.

We then switched up for next encounter and chose Spiritlord Body to take down first figuring we could handle a 500 lifetap every 30 seconds from the Commander. This went real smooth as the Lifetap was interrupted quite alot so was pretty much ineffective.

Mastruq Commander next: Commander's inherited Lifetap (Bodywarp) appeared to be changed to a single target Lifetap (bug?...never found out...it it was, it will be fixed). He Rampages.
