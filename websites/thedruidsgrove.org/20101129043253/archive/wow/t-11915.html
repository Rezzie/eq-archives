Sometimes....

PvP quest just mean you have to be "flagged for PvP" in order to complete the quest. This isn't an issue on PvP servers since everyone is flagged. But on PvE servers you have to manually flag yourself in order to complete the quest (collect items, turn-in the quest and/or "take control" of a flag stations, etc.).

In many cases, when you complete a zone's PvP quest, your faction will see a benefit. Typically in the form of x% DPS increase, or some other buff.

Here's a description of the Silithus PvP quest...

http://www.wowwiki.com/The_Silithyst_Must_Flow

BTW, in the Outlands, many (all?) zones have some sort of World PvP quest that has a similar affect (boost your DPS, etc.)
