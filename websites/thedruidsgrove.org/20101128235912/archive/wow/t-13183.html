Also <3 Starfall. If your goal is to try and eek out as much damage as possible, why would you pass on this?

The only danger is unpredictable mob pulls. My rule of thumb is that if all mobs within sight are already pulled, pop that badboy. If there's a group on the other side of the room or whatnot, that you don't want pulled prematurely, don't.

I even use it in some of the longer single target boss fights - why not... Its an instant cast, and is not channelled, so there's really no downside to popping it whenever possible (unless mana conservation is an issue, but I never go oom, so no biggie).

But yeah... lol... starfall and hurricane goin at the same time on a crowd of 20 mobs in naxx is pure sickness (especially if you've specced gale winds which I also highly recommend)
