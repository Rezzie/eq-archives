Unrest MM yields a nice 65hp/m aug. We have trioed it at 75 down to 22mins but I am guessing 69 unless you bring friends you can do it but take your time and snare/egress pull.

The 65/65 augment is a rare drop, so it may take a dozen missions or more to get depending on the RNG.

The mission should be pretty easy in a group at 69, just not as fast as it is at 75. The mobs are 68ish (named mobs are 70), hit for 550 (?), don't summon and are fully slowable. It's an outdoors zone, so mounts work and level appropriate Harmony should work too. Make sure to get rid of the pets before you charge the ghost or else you may be fighting the whole sub-basement.

Solo-wise, well, a level 69 (70+ by now, I assume) Druid should be able to kill any single mob in the zone except perhaps for the CHers. If you could get the 3 named mobs to the zone line by themselves, you would probably kill them quickly, loot and scoot. However, even with snare/root/egress/IVU potions, I can't think of a way to get the ghost out of the basement single and if you have to kill your way down there, well, you will be 75 before you are done :-\

There is a 3 monster mission arc regarding Venril Sathir's rise to power that begins in the Emerald Jungle. The NPC to begin the missions is Tulok (an Iksar) who spawns in the NW corner of the zone (where the tainter gorrilla and Serverillous spawn). All the augs are type 7/8.

First mission has common reward for 20ac 30mana aug

Second mission has common reward for 65hp / mana aug (similar to unrest)
.
Third mission (which starts in Trak's Teeth) has common rewards of 65hp / 75 mana aug or 75hp / 65 mana aug both of which have small resists to them.

There's also that nice random drop one from DSK with the beneficial range (Ikaav Gaze). It's called Ancient Eggshell Fragment or something.
