You can go to the quest NPC and hail them. They should offer you the next mission that you have not completed. i.e. if you have done 69.1 and 69.4, if you hail the NPC in CC he will offer you 69.2.

Not ideal but at least you can get some idea of which mission you need next in that particular arc.
