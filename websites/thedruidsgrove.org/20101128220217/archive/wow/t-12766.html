The bear mount drops when you complete the 'four chest event' in ZA.

You have to kill the first four bosses within a certain timeframe (i.e. quick as hell). Each boss that you kill within the alotted time leaves behind a chest which contains an extra epic. The fourth chest contains a Bear Mount. In order to get the four chests, you have to be chain-pulling and not stop at all. One wipe will pretty much ruin the chances of getting to the fourth boss in time.

Its a fun challenge, and definitley worth it - Bear mount looks awesome :)
