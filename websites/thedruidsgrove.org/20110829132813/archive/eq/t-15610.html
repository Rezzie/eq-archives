Assuming you have all current expansions, yes, there are several tunics you can get that are better than the Plane of Time tunic. Off the top of my head:

Praetorian's Energeiac Leather Tunic (from the Buried Sea expansion):
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=80454

Bark-Inlaid Leather Tunic (from the Serpent's Spine expansion):
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=53281

Both are single groupable, though they have required levels above 70.

Oh, if you're willing to consider a mini-raid, there are additional options, such as:
Everliving Bramble Tunic (from the Prophecy of Ro expansion):
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=39921
