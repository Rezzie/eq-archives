Well the Ogri'la quests open based on your Reputation level.

Fastest way to do it is to kill all the Sons of Gruul. After finishing the chain you can automatically get the "The Apex Resonation" or something like that once you're Friendly with Ogri'la.

Quick side note....the items from killing the Sons Of Gruul can be looted by anyone who has the quest. You don't have to be in the killing party to loot it.

It might be worth your time to check and see if anyone else is killing the sons and just tag along.

When I did the quests initially, there were 2 other groups killing as well so we just took turns racing to the next objective and smashing the buggers.

Then once you hit Honored with Ogri'la you unlock the "Banish Demons" quest with can get you the Crystalforged Darkrune which is used in the Shartuul Event that can get you a Depleted Badge.
