Yes indeed, there is a troll guy at the top of a pyramid in southern Hinterlands who drops the mallet of zul'farrak. You need this hammer to bang the gong by the pool in ZF. When you use hammer > gong, Gahz'rilla spawns. He's a giant hydra and he's not as nails as he looks. Once you've killed Gahz'rilla, everyone who has the quest from teh shimmering flats can loot [Gahzrilla's Electrified Scale] or whatever it is. Turning this into the quest guy in Shimmering Flats will give you your carrot.
