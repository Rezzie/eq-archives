You can start by getting your 4 unique forages out of the way. I believe the Corrupted Mammoth is do-able around 35, although that's probably the only one you should try before your 40s. You could maybe get the Cyclops and HG drop if you hang around those zones long enough, as many higher level folks farm there for pp and usually let the epic pieces rot.

If you're really bored, you could also do some scouting of the various areas that you'll need to camp (e.g. LOIO for Barracuda, etc.)

Also, try making a few friends now that will be able to help you do some of the tougher fights later on.
