1. Get book from drop (random in Dire Maul), AH, or guild bank and right click.

2. Go to Dire Maul North, zone in, cat form, stealth. Go down the ramp and to the left. Be careful because the dogs there see through stealth. Open the door with your crescent key (obtained in DM East).

3. Go down the hall and enter the library. Straight ahead on the left next to the last librarian is a skeleton on the floor. Right click.

4. Talk to librarian. Receive Royal Seal of Eldre'Thalas (http://www.thottbot.com/?i=38431)

Note: I received mine before the DM North revamp. It may be more difficult (or impossible) to solo stealth into the library door.
