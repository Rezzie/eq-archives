If you have TSS, you might consider getting 71 and the level 2 charm animal spell. Just to collect mammoth tusks when I was doing Wraithguard faction, I charm fought the roaming mammoths south of the glacier, that are charmable with beast's beckoning. Was no trouble to clear every roaming mammoth before repop, that's a lot more than you could kill using root/DOT or nuke kiting. Experience was mediocre since I was doing it at L75, and they were all blue, but I'd bet that spot would be about the best solo exp spot around for a druid at L71.
