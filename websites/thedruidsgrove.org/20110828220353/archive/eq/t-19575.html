Yrys, you should come check it out for yourself sometime. There is a free key a & download here: EQMac (http://www.eqmac.com/)

In the past year we've had a substantial influx of new players on Al'Kabor that is the result of some posts concerning our server on the SOE forums and a popular thread on the Fires of Heaven site (http://www.fohguild.org/forums/mmorpg-general-discussion/38234-eqmac-those-starting-up-mac-server.html/) too.

More recently, I've noticed a lot of old toons that left when WoW hit that are coming back to Al'Kabor for a few reasons:

1). We have an older player base, so there isn't a lot of immaturity and stupid stuff going on.

2). There is no mudflation or Show EQ. There are no in-game maps, shared bank or targeting rings. This is truly old-school EQ.

3). The realization that what made EQ great is the people you play with day-to-day. We've got those in ample supply.

I'm not saying other games aren't awesome, they definitely are. But I am saying that if you want an authentic, throwback EQ server, here it is, primed and ready to explore.

All are welcome.
