Vxed, yes. Yxxta and Qvic, maybe. Those are tricky and I think most of them summon? I group 5x more than solo, therefore others would have better grasp of it. I have solo'd orcs and shiliskins in Undershore and Stoneroot while in between DoDH missions. Real good spot for kiting is at the Drachnid Candle mission. During the double aaxp weekend we had a couple months back, I spent alot of time kiting in the Hive near the zone in from Stoneroot. In a nutshell; it rocked.

You want Reptile Skin asap. It's a level 68 DoDH spell, 4 or 5 missions for it. Will be great for you and/or group members. While working on those missions for the 68, 69, and 70 spells you could also pickup nice armor upgrades which appears mostly PoP/PoTime. When get groups together, work on DoN expansion factions and missions. Nice augments you can buy from the merchants and 100% weight reduction bags, but most importantly is the spell Serpent Vines; a very difficult to resist snare.

Secondary Forte is a good aa, just ensure you train it through utilizing the correct specialization ;) Finish up Spirit of the Grove and also train Resplendent cure; both are valuable in group, alone, and raids. Also, Call of the Wild and Shared Camoflauge are standard AA's to have when you hit level 70 and quite useful.
