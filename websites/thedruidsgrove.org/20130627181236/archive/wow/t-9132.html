All my info comes from reading other posts, so don't take my word as absolute. As I understand it, and please correct me if I am wrong, it is obsolete for anyone as long as they are in combat. So anyone else would have to switch before/after combat too. You can set an auto switch for the weapon, and then create a macro to use after you change forms. In equipmanager, create a custom equip set, say bearform2, then load the bearform2 after you have changed forms. But like I said earlier, if you are actually in combat, this won't do anything but activate the global weapon cooldown for switching weapons. Its really easier to just decide before hand whether you will be mostly casting/healing and want the int and spi bonuses, or will be using the feral forms and want the AC/STA/STR/AGI and +Crit bonuses.
