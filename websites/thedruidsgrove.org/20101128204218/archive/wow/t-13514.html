Unfortunatley most of the decent gear lists for bears have not been updated since Naxx. Tankspot and EJ both have them but its all i213 stuff.

If you have questions about specific slot, I would be happy to point you the right direction or explain the thinking/conventional wisdom.

For the most part, just take the highest iLvL you can get your hands on for AP Leather, then gem/enchant for EH (stam > agi in gear +i226).

The "jewelry" slots are a much more gray area. Depending on content, you will need to swap out your trinkets pretty regualarly. I typically run with one EH trinket, and one Mit/Avoidance trinket.

In TotGC, I have to slap on two stam trinkets in an effort to buy my healers more time.
