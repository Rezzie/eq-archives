Aye, the masks spells don't stack with Form of the Howler, but if you get Mask of the Stalker you'll be able to use it indoors where you couldn't cast Form of the Howler. Also if you get a horse or a drogmor to help with sit aggro for healing you'll find it especially useful as you can't be in wolform and sitting a mount at the same time. I have Mask of the Stalker and love it, and don't miss wolfform one bit!

One thing to keep in mind though: do you plan on reaching 65 soon and do you think you might be able to get some 65 spells? If that's the case you might want to wait and get Mask of the Forest which is 4 mana tick. A druid guildmate of mine got his copy for 5k, but I think he was especially lucky as I usually see it for much higher than that, but it is an option you can consider if you don't want to spend the money on the these spells that might someday find themselves at the very back of your spellbook.
