Firstly by not callng yourself gimp with feral talents.

And keeping the warrior away from you initially helps, those melee abilities of theirs come through in a pinch when you least expect em too. I had a Warrior one-shot me down to less than a quarter heal from full. Rooting initially (thanks to diminishing returns it wont be as effective), then HoT yourself and tank em with Bear. I might throw up Barkskin first then switch and tank em with Frenzied Regen as well. If you need to heal again stun them, shift out and heal up (with the 4 second stun if you spec for it helps a lot when you need to heal midfight.).

I never really have problems with Warriors. Don't ever use Catform against them outside of opener otherwise you are asking them to kill you.
