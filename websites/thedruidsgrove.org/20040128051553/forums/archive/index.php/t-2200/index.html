Wiggume
I think most everyone knows where to powerlevel the low levels. Pauladal Caverns -> Mera Seru will get you to level 30 in a day easily.

Starting friday evening I took my level 30 bard to the Temple of Droga. I snare pulled 5-15 then let them beat on my DS. All of them beat themselves to death on my DS except for the last one who tried to run, but being snared he just stood there. With my bard I played an AE DoT, which is all he had to do the whole time, well and melee the last mob as it stood there.

I went to Droga because the same strategy in DsP just didnt seem fast enough and I figured there must be a better place. The mobs in DsP seem to hit harder and have more HP than similar level mobs in Droga, plus being a dungeon there is an EXP bonus. A dark blue mob will give 2% EXP at level 30.

Buffs are the key here, I will list what I had on at all times.

1) Grim Aura (my feeble attempt to save my buffs from being dispelled, but the spell the mobs are casting remove buffs in random slots.)
2) Resist Magic
3) PoTG
4) Brackencoat
5) Mask of the Stalker
6) KEI
7) Replenishment
8) Shield of Bracken
9) DS Potion
10) Natures Recovery

Thats a 86 point DS on me.

With that I would pull 5-10 mobs at a time just depending on what the rooms looked like mobwise. 5 is easy, 10 is a more efficient use of Buff time but you will have to channel a Heal or 2 during the fight. Whenever SoTW was up I would pull 15 or so, use that and my DS would be 111 points with crazy regen.

Like I said I started Friday evening at level 30, I hit level 46 on Sunday at about noon. Thats 16 levels in about 2 days. It was pretty constant playing, I took a break Friday to help a rogue friend in Hate and another on Sunday to help a druid in Iceclad.

I zoned in from FM and fought my way towards the area with the Goblin Traitor then ran around in a loop for levels 30-41. After that I started fighting in the Inner Sanctum area fighting my way back towards the Chief.

I picked up prolly 5 stacks of Green Goblin Skins and 12 stacks of Goblin Salts. So if you have a char working on chardok faction thats a good way to get them. Or sell them in the bazaar. Just the loot/plat you pick up in there easily pays for the DS potions and KEI's. Welp thats about it, just thought I would share what I have found to be by far the best PL spot for levels 30-46.
