Oldoaktree
And worth it at any time.

As others noted, it does not have the reuse restrictions of Radiant Cure. I also find RC is buggy in regards to differences in Z axis. ie, you can use it on someone and they are on a hill and it doesn't work but then it is used and you have to wait.

Pureblood is a better castable remove poison and remove disease in one spell than we have in individual spells (our best remove poison and disease spells are 8 counters each. If I read Lucy right, Pure Blood is 18 counters of both poison and disease. (9 counter of poison x 2 slots, 9 counters of disease x 2 slots).

So...you can mem just the one spell, and you can deal with slows and poisons pretty easily.

I find it invaluable in VT in particular...mobs use both poisons and diseases there, and it is nice to just have the one spell up all the time.
