Wolves in Dragonscale appeared to be immune to run speed changes as were the brownies in the southern part of the zone. The wolves were light blue I think but the brownies were 75-78 level mobs. Not the most fun when 1200 hitters are chasing you.

Be careful if you try and kite in the open area in Fortress Mechanotus to avoid the packs of wandering gnomes that can sneak up on you. Also, they wandered in sets of 5 and even with the new harmony, I was unable to split them single (too close...turned and pathed over each other.. type of issues).

Would probably be a very good spot for a kite group though.
