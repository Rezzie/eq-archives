There are some quest items for feral druids that are MUST HAVES. These are some off the top of my head.

Manimal's Cinch (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30942) - Tanking Belt. Very good for pre-kara. Lots of armor.

Ash Tempered Legguards (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30941) - Tanking leggings. Very VERY good for pre-kara. Lots of armor.

Cleansed Fel Pauldrons (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30369) - Should you be aldor these are one of the best kitty dps shoulders for a while.

Mark of the Ravenguard (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=29336#00zc) - Awesome tanking neckpiece. Should last you until you can get the neck piece from the heroic badges.

Verdant Gloves (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30943) - Another great tanking glove piece. From a lengthy quest line in SMV. Might get this finished after hitting 70.

Umberhowl's Collar (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=30944) - One of the best tanking wrists (since theres no set wrist armor). Another long questline in SMV.

That's all the quest items that come to my mind right now that are definite must haves.
