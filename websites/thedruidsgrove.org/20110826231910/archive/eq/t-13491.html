Here's the 3 scenerios where I use it most often:

-Big pull is incoming, I click BP and SC so I can lay down heals fast.
-Hard named is about to be engaged in a group, I click bp so I can spam heal til it's slowed.
-When not much healing is needed, I click bp before I click epic so I can get a few more dawnfire casts in before epic fades (bp click really cuts down dawnfire cast time.)

IMO, it's not anything amazing. If they removed the max hit limit, it'ld kick ass. But with 2 / 4 spells, it's really not all that. Ang bp as a total package is well worth the wait. But I wouldn't sweat over trying to get the tier 1 bp.
