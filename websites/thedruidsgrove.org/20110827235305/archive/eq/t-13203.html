Heh... these are some "expensive" aa I am gonna suggest and would probably take a bit to collect them all for a very casual player, but.

Shared Camo. Get it. Its Great. Everyone who has it loves it. Fixed duration group instant cast invis.

I would recommend finishing off some things as well, Healing Gift Mastery, Radiant Cure, Chaotic Potential, Convergence of Spirit, Expansive Mind, Destructive Fury in that order.

If you heal more than you nuke, definitely finish HGM first, if you nuke a lot more than you heal, then I would say DF first but just switch those 2.

Others to look at, Persistant Casting, Mystical Attuning (if you ever have problems with needing more buff slots or just are laxy like me and don't want to keep clicking off haste and sense). Also, a couple fun ones just to have to play with, not really all that useful are Nature's Guardian and Wrath of the Wild. But only if you want the toys.

Thats a pretty long list I know, but others I am sure will suggest a different order for those.
