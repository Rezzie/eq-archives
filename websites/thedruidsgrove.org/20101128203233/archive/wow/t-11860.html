I can't help you out in terms of balance ufortunately, but resto and feral, here goes.

If you are JUST starting out feral here goes.

Feral:
16k+ Armor
10k HP in bear
3/3 Survival of the fittest
415 Defense ( or combo of Def & Resilience to be crit immune )
20%+ dodge

Resto:
9k mana can be fine depending on how far resto you spec. You'll want about 1k + healing and I loved to stack mp5. If you go full blown tree, stacking spirit is good too, but you need quite a bit for it to really make an impact on your +healing.

Generally yes. I have currently something like the following unbuffed:

22k armor
14k Hp
3/3 Survival of the Fittest
410 def (+30 Resilience)
