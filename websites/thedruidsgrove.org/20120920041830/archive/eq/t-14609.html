There is a way to get the pet to attack multiple mobs, but the situations may be limited:

1) Target the priority mob being tanked by someone else and cast pet.
2) Sit down and wait for another mob to hit you.
3) Hope someone else gets agro on that mob quickly.
4) When the priority mob dies, your pet will start hitting the mob that hit you.
