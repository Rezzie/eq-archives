Have a look at the following quests :-

Back - Crimson Cloak of Moonwaters (soloable)
very simple quest http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=50811

Wrist - Fieldwarden's Bracer
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52392, nice mana regen and sometimes people are letting the hides rot

Ear - Relic Shopkeeper's Earring
Kill stuff & loot in Arcstone http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=48059

Aug - Relic Shopkeeper's Charm
Kill various mobs in arcstone - http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=47370

Aug - Runed Diamond (has Mana Preservation 6)
Kill & loot stuff in Steppes http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52701

If you have have no clicky mana regen item then consider getting a Direwolf Totem of Spirit - http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=51937 . This summed pet gives +6 mana regen

Most of these quests will need a group but are fairly easy / quick to do (except Runed Diamond which may require a lot of killing to get required drops).


Also head to the bazaar and look for cheap gear, if you check every so often you should see some nice bargins. Quick check on AB showed the following decent buys :-
Arms - Direwrought Leather Sleeves 175mana/hp - 2k
Waist - Sunderspring Belt 150 mana/hp - 2k


As I mentioned before check out DoN merchants for missing Focus Effects (according to your Magelo you are missing all focus effects) and Master's Runeseeker / symbols (should be pretty cheap these days).


As you are grouping in TSS zones you will be interested in Tenish's Leather Armor (Part 1) - http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3595. The arm molds can drop in the steppes and helm / bracers / gloves molds can drop in Sunderock so it's worth keeping an eye out for these (especially once you can hunt for power sources and upgrade any pieces you have). Spirit Mark armour from PoR will be easier to get but not as good http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3375.


For charm slot buy a tainted heartstone (need to improve language skills to get it to max) or buy a charm from DoN merchant, do Wanderlust quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=52451) and then you can have both Adventurer's Stone & Wanderlust Guild Loadstone in the same charm.

p.s. get Spell Casting Mastery AAs as soon as you can, will save a nice chunk of mana.
