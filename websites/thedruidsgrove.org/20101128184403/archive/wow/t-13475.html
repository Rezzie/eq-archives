from wowwiki:
For Sartharion + one drake learning purposes, Sartharion + Tenebron is probably the easiest step up, since you have the option of just burning the drake and off-tanking/AOE'ing the whelps without taking the portal. With the others, you either take the portal and waste a bunch of time, or don't take the portal and eat a nasty debuff for a while.

The main trouble will be the adds, you will need someone that can get aggro from the adds without geting aggro from Sarth (hunter misdirection can work miracle here).

Tenebron need to be killed ASAP (dps need to blow every cooldown). Get the agro from the add asap too or all your healers will be killed. A good number of players that can aoe dommage make it less painfull. Ranged dps help too as they need to move a little less.

This fun part is everyone need to give attention to what is happening, and they are a lot that can happen at the same time (fire elemental adds (that you you don't want to go inside a firewall), drakes adds, circle of dead, firewall and changing target often).

You will need 2 good tanks, 2-3 healers and very good dps for the rest (you realy want to have killed Tenebron before the second drakes add wave). Make sure that everyone understand the firewall, and be ready to have a good number of wipe.

This is what my guild is tryoing to do, but as we have a good turn over for our guild raiding evening and some players with low dps, we are trying 3-5 times then go kill Tenebron, and kill Sart solo.

When Tenebron is up all shadow damage done to the raid is increased by 100%. Tenebron does primarily shadow damage, and therefor hits much harder than when you just kill him on his own.

When Shadron is up all fire damage is increased by 100% This is a much bigger challenge because the vast majority of damage done to the raid is fire damage.

When Vesperon is up he reduces the health of all raid members by 25%. He is very difficult to deal with because he also casts a shield that does around 2k damage per hit to anyone that hits him so essentially players beat themselves to death if they are not paying attention.

If you leave all three up and can't kill them fast enough you have to deal with all their abilities at the same time. It's a fun fight!
