Color doesn't necessarily deem how "good" an item is, just the rarity of it ( or the effort needed to obtain it ).

While it is usually the case where Orange > Purple > Blue > Green, it is not ALWAYS the case.

Take this list of belts that is "rated" in terms of how good it is for tanking. Notice there are greens that out perform blues and purples.
