Don't worry about the zone-wide emotes.

As for the kyv emotes, audio triggers are invaluable. Create a set and insist that everyone in the guild use it. Sure, you can win without them but the audio triggers took us from struggling with Foresight to winning it easily.

Most people don't understand that every missed emote results in all emotes coming faster. Some people figure it's no big deal because they'll get a heal or heal themselves. Stress this point with your guild... If you miss an emote you are putting the win in jeopardy.
