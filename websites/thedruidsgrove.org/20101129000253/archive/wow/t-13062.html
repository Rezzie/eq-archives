I think the optimal DPS rotation depends on your theory. Really right now they've opened it up for two possible ways of DPSing. One emphasizes crit and counts on earth and moon. The other focuses on what I'd deem the "warlock theory" where you DoT the crap out of stuff and watch it die.

So for instance for leveling I've gone with a theory of DoT. So I open up with a Moonfire, lay down an Entangling Roots, Insect Swarm and then Starfire, Starfire. The DoTs alone are enough to do about 50% of a mobs health in damage.

I'm not sure math wise though which will come out ahead on longer boss fights. I suspect the DoT based might, but that's just based on gut instinct and has no basis in numbers. I suspect the DoT theory will turn out to be better mana wise.
