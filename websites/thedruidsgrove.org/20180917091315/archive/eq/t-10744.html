Is it the zone right at the druid port in spot to Barindu?

No, but somewhat close. You can get there invis. It is however a lot closer than porting to natimbi though.

What's the best way to fight here, kiting or root/rotting?

too many adds to kite, you can root/dot in the coliseum just fine. Named can be nasty if you're not prepared though, and most named summon.

Can I expect summoners or non snareable/rootable mobs?

Yes. The named. :) I think Kyvs are immune to runspeed debuffs. If not others... has been a while.

Btw Firefox rox :)

If you're looking for that BP, then you can just kill trash mobs for it... however if you're looking for named drops.. prepare for a beating. Named can hit as hard as Elem mobs but a whole lot faster. Pulling named that are outside the Colliseum is hard because of the adds, not saying impossible because it isnt :D but it is hard for an inexperienced puller. The named inside the colliseum are mostly casters, but still hit hard and summon and not to mention have nasty spells.
