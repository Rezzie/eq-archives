Myself, I use Cosmos, Titanpanel, and Auctioner. I am thinking of picking up Ctmod--from what I have heard it allows one to see everyone elses healthbars? Or something. I don't know if I want it right now, but as I always main-heal I might get it.

With Cosmos comes many things. I particularly like the following:
Feralskills (very helpful. What it does is combine all of your feral attacks into 4 buttons)
Health/Mana bar displays (this is the reason why I do so well in healing, especially the percentage displays)
Quickloot
Arcane-casting bars
Better tooltips
Alphamap (Able to view the map while I run and fight? I love it)
All-in-one inventory
And many other things.

With Titanpanel comes:
Co-ords
Clock
Honor counter
Bag space
Durability %
Item Bonuses
And other things.

If you're interested, cosmos can be found here:
http://www.cosmosui.org/
I highly recommend it. It'll take a little while to configure everything... But the number of helpful add-ons it has is amazing.

And I believe this is the right link for titanpanel:
http://ui.worldofwar.net/ui.php?id=1797

Hope you enjoy them if you take them up.
