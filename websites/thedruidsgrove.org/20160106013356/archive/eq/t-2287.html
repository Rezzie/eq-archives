Best place I have found for quick/easy PP was the Grey. What I did was...:

1.) Go to Ssra Temple. Find the West and South walls. Kill both of the Golems standing at them.

2.) Cast snare on "a Shissar Revenant". They do not assist and are not very resistant to anything. Just root/rot like normal. They drop "A Polished Serpent Scale" which sells for around 9pp to merchants and is stackable.

I'm sure this would be very nice experience for a level 51 druid but my advice would be not to try this until level 54 when you get Winged Death and Breath of Ro (53). The shissar go down by stacking Breath of Ro, Winged Death, and Drifting Death about two or three times.

Also you can quad kite these for nice experience and of course loot. I found it best to quad kite them with the level 58 AE of course, as you may run out of mana trying the level 49 AE.

Another thing, the golems repop on the walls once about every 15 minutes. On occasion you will find named golems such as "A Shattered Golem". He is just about as strong as the eternal golems and he drops Grey Flesh Gloves which are EXCELLENT for druids. The +6 wis helps tremendously and Affliction Effenciency II increases the length of Drifting Death, putting it at around ~720 damage.

Hope this helped.

Try JaggedPine Forest.

I have a simliar situation to you only in addition to only playing a couple hours at a time I frequently need to go afk. I have found JPF to be an incredibly easy zone to hunt in since very little is aggro to a druid.

With tracking you never run out of mobs and as long as you avoid killing faction mobs (black panthers, treants) nothing there will aggro you. Track down timber griffons (which drop a nice secondary item rarely) and root/rot them or aggro charm once you have the magic debuff.

Loot here will not be great, but a lot of the willowisps are still dark blue to me at 52 and they drop a greater lightstone every time. You can sell them in the bazaar for 12pp or do the concordance quest in NK for 10pp.

Good luck
