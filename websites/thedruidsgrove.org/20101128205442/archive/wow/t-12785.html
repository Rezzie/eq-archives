Generally you will want to go with agility gear, as the crit chance you get from this will help you to grind mobs faster more than extra attack power would do.

So yea.. agility and crit chance are both good ones.
Secondary to that, strength/attack power - it's roughly 2 attack power per point of strength - and a reasonable amount of stamina.

Hit rating has very little bearing on your levelling speed, so don't go out of your way to get hit rating.

One of the best things to get is the 4 piece set bonus from any pvp feral set - that increases your outdoor movement speed in cat, bear, and travel form - invaluable for running around levelling quickly, not to mention exploring a new, exciting zone :>
