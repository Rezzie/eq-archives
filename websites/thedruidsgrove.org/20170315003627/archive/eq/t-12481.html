Skyfire is Ring of Scale faction. If you want to do any of the quests out of Veeshan's Peak you'll need ally and it's a pita to get, trust me. You hurt faction by killing the wurms in SF. You can check your RoS faction by conning Esorpa in WW. There's probably other places as well, but this is the one I know of.

CoV faction is gained by killing in Kael. You can check your faction by conning the wyverns in Cobalt Scar. You can do this safely from the zone in area. You do NOT hurt your faction by killing wyverns in CS as long as you don't kill the named.

Skyfire is Ring of Scale faction. If you want to do any of the quests out of Veeshan's Peak you'll need ally and it's a pita to get, trust me. You hurt faction by killing the wurms in SF. You can check your RoS faction by conning Esorpa in WW. There's probably other places as well, but this is the one I know of.

If you should find them up, you can also con Gore, Talendor, Sev, Faydedar, or anything actually in Veeshan's Peak. Escorpa Roams near the Temple of Veeshan Entrance, Does not See Invis, Hits just under 300, and casts a AE Silance. She also may gate.

CoV faction is gained by killing in Kael. You can check your faction by conning the wyverns in Cobalt Scar. You can do this safely from the zone in area. You do NOT hurt your faction by killing wyverns in CS as long as you don't kill the named.

Other places to check CoV Faction:

Named Wurm in GD, Wuoshi in WL, Ronadil (white dragon, north wall of WL), Skyshrine, Dragons in West Wastes, Temple of Veeshan (tho I would not recommend the later).

Places to gain Faction:

Giants in:

East Wastes
Iceclad Ocean
Great Divide
Kael
Wakening Lands
West Wastes
Kiling Zlandicar in DN

Statue and AoW in Kael are the largest hits you can get.
