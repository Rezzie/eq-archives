The increase in threat is largely due to the increased periodic damage of Lacerate.


Lacerate has 2 components. The first is a tiny bit of direct damage, and it is this part that contains the threat payload. There is also the periodic bleed effect which does not (as far as I can tell) add any extra threat beyond what that amount of damage would generate anyway.

If you have 5 stacks of Lacerate ticking all the time you can expect quite a lot more aggro now, because with 5 stacks the amount of damage it churns out is really quite considerable.
