I think that is something that is too server dependant. It is also something you might never see on the AH.

This is one of the biggest drawbacks to the WoW AH system imo. There is a market for this belt but it is rather small. You have to have a seller and buyer meet w/in a 48 hr period. I would love to see a separate system like they have in EQ where you can list items you would like to buy and for what price. Even if someone's buy price is lower than what you would like to sell for, you at least know someone is looking and can touch base with them. I started emailing people who have items I would be interested in buying but who are wanting to sell for more than I would like to pay. More often then not I don't hear back from them but it has resulted in 3 purchases to include a badge of tenacity for less than the "going rate." I think it also helps if you mention the item is something you are wanting to use vs resell.
