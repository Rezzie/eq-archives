For me, Destructive Fury 3 increases the critical multiplier by 24%; i.e., with a 45% damage focus, the critical is multiplied by 2.7 max instead of 2.45 max, for a 10% damage increase. Since the chance of a critical is less than 20% with all other skills maxed, this works out to around a 2% overall DPS increase.

The tests I've seen on FoMM (one is linked in the sticky above) place it at a 2-4% increase in criticals. Therefore, without Destructive Fury, it is a 2-4% increase in DPS, and with Destructive Fury, it's a fraction of a % greater. I certainly can't casually notice it working.

Both skills are only minor DPS boosts, but make for nice eye candy. If you really want to boost your burst DPS with nukes, get Quick Damage first for a solid 10% boost, then work on FoMM and Destructive Fury for the extra edge.
