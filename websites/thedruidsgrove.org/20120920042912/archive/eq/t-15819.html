AtU works somewhat differently then the previous spells in the line.

AtU works on Summoned and Constructs.

If mobs are flagged correctly (which is fairly hit and miss), AtU will land on:

Summoned: Elementals, Summoned Creatures, so forth
Constructs: Golems, Gargoyles and the like

If you think a mob should be summoned, and it's not - /bug it.
