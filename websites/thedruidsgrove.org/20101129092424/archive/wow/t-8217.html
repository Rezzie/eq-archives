I hate to give away "my spot" but I can farm a stack or two in about an hour and a half/two hours of Stranglekelp off the western coast of the Wetlands... there's a large area, and in aquatic form, you can travel it pretty quickly...

There's a few sets of murlocs that wander, but they're easily killed/evaded depending on yer mood... I generally start right north of the GY (which is just north of Menethil) and swim all the way north till I zone... I go in a zig-zag pattern so that I catch anything on my radar

Once I get all the way up north, I get on the shore, travel form, and run back south to Menethil. I pick up usually 5-6 Liferoot along the way (sometimes more, sometimes less). Once I get back at the GY, I hop in the water and start my way north again... usually most everything has respawned in different locations but I can still get about 9-10 per pass (south to north)
