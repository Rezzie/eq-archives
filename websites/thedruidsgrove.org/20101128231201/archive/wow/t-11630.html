The best way to quickly boost your stats as feral is a better weapon.
The third boss in Botanica drops Dreamer's Dragonstaff (http://www.thottbot.com/i28325), which is the best cat weapon in the game you get outside of raidcontent and heroic instances.

I would wait with enchants until you have the really good pieces, because you will need your money for epic flight form (or epic mount).

A good place to find better equip for kitty damage is http://www.gurgleblaster.net/emmerald/Cat_Sustained_DPS/Head.html (formerly maintained here http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=104460524&sid=1, also found here http://druid.wikispaces.com/DPS+Gear+List)

Your current stats are quite good already (assuming they are your stats when unbuffed). You should be leading the damage meters in five mans all the time (I have 2,2k AP and 30% crit and do).
So just keep playing, do all the quests in Netherstorm and Shadowmoon Valley (some good rewards there) and try to get into heroic instances, because all the really good stuff that is within easy reach drops there.

As for enchants: Everything with AP, str or agi is great. Do not forget that with Heart of the Wild you get 2,4 AP per point of str. so 12 str is better than 24 ap for ferals.

Also: the more AP you have, the better 1% crit gets. So with 1000 AP I would take 100 AP over 1% crit. With 2000 AP I would definitely take 1% crit over 100 AP. Try to keep your +hit rating somewhere around 70-100.
Also never forget your dodge value. A dead kitty does no damage ^^. You should be at least around 30% here as well.


Hope that helps you a bit.

best regards

Here is a good breakdown of the enchants that will yield the best results for your kitty. Keep in mind some are pretty pricey so you want to make sure you'll keep the gear for a while.

Back: Enchant Cloak - Greater Agility (+12 Agi)
Chest: Enchant Chest - Exceptional Stats (+6 All Stats) ((All the +all stats are better than any other single increase here))
Feet: Enchant Boots - Dexterity (+12 Agi)
Finger: Enchant Ring - Stats (+4 All Stats)
Hands: Enchant Gloves - Major Strength (+15 Str)
Head: Glyph of Ferocity (+34 AP, +16 Hit)((FYI:Need faction rep to purchase))
Legs: Nethercobra Armor (+45 AP, +12 Crit)
One-hand: Enchant Weapon - Potency (+15 Str)
Shoulder: Greater Inscription of Vengeance (+30 AP, +10 Crit)((FYI:Need faction rep to purchase))
Two-hand: Enchant 2H Weapon - Major Agility (+35 Agi)
Wrist: Enchant Bracer - Brawn (+12 Str)

These are a combination of player provided enchants, armor kits and vendor (faction) enchants. In the combination above give the best overall DPS/Dodge/Crit.
