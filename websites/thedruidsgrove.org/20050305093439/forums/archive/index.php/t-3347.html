09-18-2002, 10:45 AM
With a few Druid friends with epics you can do the Elysians easy. I first went there with some Druid guildmates at 53. There was 3 of us there on avg, usually 2 or more had epics, most of them were 55 to 60. They die fast. We all (about 7 total got full Eylsian in about 2 weeks of about 4 hrs a day camping them. I just went back after I got my epic at 54 and since the DOT stacking changes. They are MUCH easier now that you can stack your dots with other druids. Grouped about 4 of us, loaded them with Epic, Winged, DoD from each of us and they were dead in about 2 rounds. Long as you got a high level friend to root them they are easy. Fire based dots don't work well, so safe your mana and just go with Drifting line.

So, you can do it with 2 people, having an epic or more is nice cause of the no mana. If you get 1 group of druids, load them up on DOTS and they will die much faster. Don' t worry about having a tank less you have a cleric cause they hit hard, have about 30k HP's and keeping a tank healed will use more mana then it's worth. DOT group is the way to go on these :-).

Good Luck! I Love my Elysian, and I'm sure you will to.
