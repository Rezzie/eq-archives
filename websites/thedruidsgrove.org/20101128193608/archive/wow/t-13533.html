It's a little outdated actually. With 3.3 and all the ICC plate gear with bonus armor, we lost our edge to armor. I find myself actually trailing behind plate tanks now in armor (wtf Blizzard).

Also please ignore the threat in caster form spiel. That was useful back in BC when swipe only hit three targets, maul only hit one, and everyone did 10x more threat so AoE tanking was actually difficult. Pre-Hotting yourself before pulls will not generate nearly the amount of aggro as a single swipe or maul will do. Rage is hardly an issue anymore either.

Reading this guide actually, it seems like it was written in BC with some Wrath updates. I'm actually in the middle of writing an uber bear guide for my guildies but here are some quick tips:

If you're having problems with threat, you need more hit/expertise

Stamina is your friend

Agility is your friend

Dodge gems are bad

Armor is your friend.

For AoE trash it's pretty much swipe/maul. If you're tanking anything that involves trash, Glyph of Maul is pretty win/must have. It only hurts us in fights like Saurfang really. A lot of people still tab lacerate, but I usually just maul and swipe spam and tab around because I don't feel like wasting the GCDs finding each mob to lacerate.

Single target tank rotation is pretty much: FFF off CD, Mangle off CD, lacerate at 5 stacks, swipe in between and make sure that you're hitting maul every time it's up.
