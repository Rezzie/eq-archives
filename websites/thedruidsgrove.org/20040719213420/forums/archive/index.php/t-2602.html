To give you soem insight into Spirit of the wood and MGB. I got lvl 3 SOTW because I powerlevel ALOT. I could cast replenishment on myself, natures renweal, brackencoat, and shield of bracken, pull about 25 lvl 40-45 mobs, cast aoe debuff on them twice, and pop wood, taking my damage shield, (with shamy potions) to over 130, and the mobs woudl shred themselves. the toon to be powerleveled would aoe the mob, or tap each one with a weapon, and get the exp for 25 red mobs at once. Used every 20 minutes in addition to regular DS powerleveling, it was a noticable improvement, and I was happy. Then I got MGB. The first time I used it on a Sontalak raid, I knew it was a BIG improvement over my pre MGB/sotw raid contributions. To give you an idea of how large an improvement we are talking about, we went from losing 6-8 people on a sont kill, to losing none. And I mean none, ever, to the AOE, since I got the ability, on any Sont raid I've been on. It makes that much of a difference. For mobs like Theris Thule, you pop that aoe wood when the butterflies spawn, and a. noone dies, and b. the butterflies shred themselves on a raid wide 100+ damage shield.
There just isn't anything else that I can do during a raid, that is anywhere near as valuable as MGBing 40-60 people and trivializing an AOE. It is like asking, how hard would (pick the dragon of your choice, sans sleeper) be without an AOE?
We use a 3 part chain on any aoe mob, one MGB wood, then an MGB Paragon, then another MGB wood. Once you have experienced what an MGB Wood/paragon chain can do, you will be astounded.
