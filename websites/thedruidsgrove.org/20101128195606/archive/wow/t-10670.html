Same on the second link, and same confusion...

Most of the other stuff can be chocked up to quirky choices, but you MUST have Moonglow in any balance build, whether you take moonkin or not. It is simply the most important talent for casters (even healers). Mana consumption and the management of it is the key to playing moonkin, make your spells cheaper man.

The furor is okay, if you take the natural weapons and OOC, with decent AC panzerkin gear you can alternately be a pretty good bear, otherwise drop furor for improved mark.

I have IS for when I am MH PuGs, but with this build you won't use it.

Improved roots is not necessary, especially if you are spending all those point in NG, that is what you will use if you are getting hit.

Natural Shapeshifter will save you tons of mana, and make shifting to moonkin much more cost effective, and other forms too.

Because of our mana issues, I don't mind the reflection (I have it as well), but you might want to consider splitting points between focus and iHT. None of those points in resto really help your healing (unless you are getting hit at the time).
