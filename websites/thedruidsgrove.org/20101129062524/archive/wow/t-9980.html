Hmmm without giving advice on fighting a specific class its kinda tough to say.

Alot of your approach really depends on how you want to play. Do you play "Red = Dead" or do you only attack when attacked? Do you only attack when its a fair fight or do you attack people when they are fighting mobs or half dead or sitting and drinking ect. ect. ? Do you kill people lower level than you? Do you solo alot or run with a group most of the time?

Other than those basic self guidelines it really boils down to what class you are facing and what the situation is. In my opinion you really have to be able to think quick on your feet, fight or run, because usually he who hesitates ends up face down in the dirt.
