First off, I'd advise waiting until after level 10 to make the run... you have the Moonglade teleport then, so you can hop back to Darnassus fairly easily.

Optional: Bank all your gear in Darnassus. This will save you repair costs if you die while running over. Next, take the boat to Menethil from Auberdine. In Menethil, grab the flight point, then you can mostly just follow the road through the Wetlands. Keep going east, then south, then west into the tunnel. Watch out for the orcs and keep running through to Loch Modan. Follow the road, optionally stopping by Thelsamar to pick up the flight point. Follow the road south again, and take the west fork into Dun Morogh. From then, it's pretty easy going into Ironforge.
