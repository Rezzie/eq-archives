I made these two keybinds to save on the number of keypresses required to shift between running, hovering and flying. Hope this helps you guys too.

/bind R "powexec_name sprint$$powexec_name hover$$powexec_name fly$$third 0"
/bind T "powexec_name hover$$powexec_name fly$$powexec_name sprint$$third 1"

With these two keybinds, pressing R will trigger flying and switches to first person camera mode. Further pressing of R will toggle between flying and hovering.

Pressing T will switch from flying or hovering to running mode as well as shift back to the third person camera mode. Further pressing of T will toggle between hovering and running while staying in third person mode.

I have 3 keys to control things:

F "powexec_name Fly$$powexec_name Sprint" - Toggles flying / sprinting
V "powexec_name Hover$$powexec_name Sprint" - Toggles hover / sprint
G "powexec_name Fly$$powexec_name Hover" - Toggles fly / hover

I like the flexibility.. a lot of times in combat I'll be hovering and need to get moving quickly (to run or chase someone), so pressing G just gets me going. Otherwise, you dont need sprint on during flight/hover, so it saves that little bit of endurance by turning it off.

I have one just for Sprint/Hover, since I use Teleport for my transportation power. It's very useful and I use it all the time. I've actually started to use similar things on my other characters to insure that I have spring off when I have other toggle powers on.

Here's something for you teleporters:

/bind lshift+lbutton "powexec_name Teleport"

This will basically just by-pass the Target ring allows you teleport with a single click.
