Group with someone, make a hotkey.

Mine is something like:

/g You love me
/g You worship me
/g You will send me money
/g You will buy me presents
/g You will be my slave

Change your language to whichever you're trying to teach, and spam the heck out of your group. Takes about 10 minutes to max (100).

If the person you're teaching also selects that language and spams back (ie trying to speak it) they'll skill up slightly faster.

But they can also teach you, say, ogre, while you teach them...vah shir.

You can teach a language to someone up to 1 skill point higher than your current skill.

So if your elder dragon is at 10, you can teach someone up to 11. They can then teach you elder dragon up to 12, and so on.
