Besides the old standbys of FFF, hurricane, starfire, moonfire, wrath we now have a couple of new ways to pull.

I have used the entangling root pull a couple of times, but last night I learned a new trick. The Nature's Grasp pull.


Mark a melee mob you want to CC with Nature's Grasp
Shift to bear
Start Nature's Grasp
Enrage if you want
Pull the above mob with FFF
Might even want to run toward the mob
Once he is rooted, target skull and turn around and backup a bit so you can swipe/maul (glyph) the rest of the pack.


Not only does this allow you to CC a mob while in bear form, it also gives you a bit of extra rage from the hit or two the CC mob does before you start swiping the rest of the pack. Use barkskin if you are afraid of too much initial damage.
