I got Kalua'ak exalted first ... for the robes and healy mace, then for the fishing pole.

Just got Wyrmrest exalted today... for belt, bracers, head enchant, sandals, but ultimately for the mount.

Next comes Sons of Hodir for their shoulder inscription.

My next vanity rep will be Oracles for the chance at the green protodrake mount.

As far as gear goes, it's been my experience that I'll get a rep piece, then replace within a few dungeon runs with a drop or emblem piece that's better, so it really hasn't been a big deal to focus on one tabard rep over the other.

QMs that I know of off the top of my head:
Wyrmrest Accord - Wyrmrest temple in central Dragonblight. Championable. But you know that one already. :-)
Kirin Tor - Violet citadel, SW corner of Dalaran, second floor at the top of the stairs. Championable.
Kalua'ak - in the tuskar villages of Moaki Harbor or Kamauga. Dailies for rep.
Sons of Hodir - Lillehof, in eastern Storm Peaks. Dailies for rep.
Oracles - Geen, in central Sholazar Basin. Dailies for rep.
