the thing is... the tier sets aren't dropped, they are bought from tokens from dungeons (don't know what they are but o well :) )

Tier tokens 4 drop from Kharazan (Gloves and Helm), Gruul's Lair (Shoulders and Legs), and Magtheridon's Lair (chest).

Tier 5 tokens drop from The Eye and Serpentshrine Cavern.

Each token can be used to buy one of three different sets (feral, balance, resto). What I was referring to is that there there are three tokens, 1 for Mage / Warlock / Hunter, 1 for Pally / Rogue / Shaman, and 1 for Priest / Druid / Warrior.

So if a chest token dropped, and you as a druid already had your t4 piece (for whatever you specialize in), it would be pretty greedy to roll for another one if either the warrior or priest didn't have it. I can't see a guild letting a druid have 2 chest pieces when a priest or warrior still needs his 1st. Thus the crazy endless grinding to get everyone their pieces. I guess if guilds are still using dkp, whoever has the most can still get it. /shrug
