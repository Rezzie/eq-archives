Nagafen died 2000-09-09

 

The strangest thing happened today...

I was going into norrath to get me a shiny new level in the left room in Solb. When I logged on Annanas told that we where going to do and Efreeti raid. Great I though, GEBS for Drawmjil to trade for a wurmslayer. I went and got myself a glass of wine and some snacks, setteling down for a nice IMP-slaying evening. When I get back, the Efreetiraid had suddenly turned into a firegiant raid.

Well, ok, lets rock, and alot of player from Old Continent gathered in the corridor. Hapi was warlord and giving commands with great authority. He also managed to recruit an very nice and competent (and Pretty) Monk, Danodare, to help us. Well, with Danodare in the lead, we followed and killed all the baddies without a single dead. Skarlon and another named giant gave Funk and Falloz nice stuff to.

When we where almost done, Danodare anounces that if we want, we can setup a Naggy raid, and she offered help from Grey Council too. Great, everyone was very excited, and people from GC started pouring in. KEYSER, who was to high, but still voulonteered to take the role as warlord did a good job trying to get everything put togheter.

Buffing, groupforming and all that started, which in my opinion took a lillte bit to long. I had to rebuff 3 times before the action started. I cannot say how this could have been done faster, maybe putting all the tanks in line and let them go by the clerics, shamen and so on, eventually landing on the 'ready-spot'. Well, after that it was bashing time. All tanks (and Rangers to ;-) Charged the beast, the pets went in too. When at two boubbles of health, run out to get heal, and then back in. The dungeon echoed with the usually pathetic 'You will not evade me Drawmjil....' wich in the end. happened to be true. I think I was one of the last standing, but suddenly, I woke up in Oggok again.

What supprised me then, was that before I even had time to tell the group I died, Malimas said 'Consent me' 2 min later I was back in business. GREAT corpse recovery, I think all that got it appriciated the efficency, thanks to everyone helping out. That is how it is suppose to work ;-)

Well, what happened now was that Grey Council called in more tanks, and everything was setup again, charge and finally the beast was slewn.

I had a GREAT time, thanks Hapi, Danodare, Keyser and all the rest that was there, I think everyone appriciated it, except maybe Funk, who got Linkdead before everyone ported out ;-) (He is back in business now, dont worry, meaner than ever).

Some thoughts:

  1. Try to speed up the buffing process, dont know how, but everyone try to think of something.
  2. Dicipline is VERY important, listen to the warlord and the more experienced players.
  3. Fast corpse recovery if good, for morale, and of course, for tactical reasons.
  4. When fighting dragons as a tank, RUN when you have two bubs or less and get a heal, if you can stay alive for another rush, you will do more dmg than dead in Oggok.
  5. Bring TANKS, first attempt was 10 tanks, not enough, at least not the level of that tanks that was there, 15 level 45+ less if they are higher levels....
