Drawmjils Guide to Sebelis

This guide is FAR from finished, 
but I will add more info as I get over it
 /Draw
Thanks to ZATO and NENE from the safehouse.

Sebilis cannot be left in the same way you came in. Fairly close to the entrance of the dungeon is a teleporter that takes out directly outside of the dungeon, next to the large red teleporter you used to enter. Hunting the zoneline isn't a bad option for a small, lower level group that can't penetrate deeper in the dungeon, as it gives them the oportunity to immediately flee.

To reach zoneout: From the entrance, you go past the first set of doors, and down the sloping tunnel. Turning left to approach the bridge area, you proceed forward, turn left and come to a large structure on your left - outside of it are several spawns. Directly inside of the structure are two more frogs. Through the door, you come to a room with a door at the end and three alcoves.
In each of these alcoves is a golem. Their assist and aggro radiuses are fairly small - they can be single-pulled by a level 51 character (possibly lower than that, haven't tested it). Through the door is a very small room with a frog in it and the exit out. The exit is a small, transulent ring that hovers a bit off the ground. By moving a bit around in the room, you can break up the spawn very easily, and hopefully get a spell drop or two for your trouble. While not a rewarding camp in Sebilis, this is one of the safest.
