How you can make useful hotkeys

 Useful Commands

Some of you may or may not have come across these commands. I'm sure you can see where these will or will not be useful.

A word of advice is to turn off as much text as you can deem crap. E.g. I'm not interested in anyone else's hits and misses so these are turned off. That way you can keep an easy track on what people r saying during battle.

DOABILITY.

This is a very useful command. What it does is do one of the ability u have assigned under your abilities screen. I think they go like this

1 2
3 4
5 6

If I have Sense Heading in slot 1 I have a macro that not only does my sense heading but also my location. So assign a macro key with the following two lines:

/doability 1 (line 1)
/Loc (line 2)

/CAST & %T
The /cast command is used to cast spells 1 through to 8. The %T variable can be used in text and will state what the current target is. Now these have uses for a number of people. For pulling you might have a macro that reads:

/G INC INC INC Its ............. %T

That would then inform the group that not only you're coming back but also what you have pulled (well on singles anyhow).

Now combine this with the cast command. As an enchanter this has uses when perhaps I'm just about to try and charm something. So I would have a macro set-up to read (assume the charm spell is in spell slot 1)

/Cast 1 (line1)
/g Attempting to Charm ....... %t (line2)

These command also have uses when two of the same caster classes r either grouped or within the team. That way they can inform the other caster what spell your casting on what mob.
