New Members FAQ

I put this page togheter so that everyone know how to utilize this website,
read this before you ask questions about not having access to boards and stuff.

 

So... you have become a member of the bets guild on Povar have you? What do you need to do to be able to get access to the inner secrets?
  1. First, go HERE and sign up with EZBOARD. Ezboard is the provider wich runs our forums, and they are the one who register you userid and password to be able to access the member only forums. Please try to pick a name that matches you playcharacter, that way, everyone will know that it is YOU that are doing the posting.

  2. Post a message in the General OPEN forum wich can be found HERE using the name you registered. Say that you want access to the member forums, and also, dont forget to say if you have registered a GLOBAL or a LOCAL name. If you used the form about, you have registered a GLOBAL account. An officer will give you access asap.

  3. Be sure to read the Guildrules, to make sure you know what to expect from the guild, and what the guild expects from YOU.

  4. Welcome aboard!

 

Ok, I think that is about it, any futher questions? Just send Drawmjil a mail and I will try to help you as best I can.
