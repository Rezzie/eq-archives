Drawmjil guide to camping The Giant Beaver

Before you start the actual camping, and the higly saught after break of the camp, you must find the beaver. You should also take GREAT care assuring that IT is not allready camped. Ignoring this important part will probably give you lost factions, both with the beaver and, more important, the campholder, wich will probably challenge you to a duel.

Once you have made sure that the beaver is not camped, set up you campspot well. A supply of alochol would be recomended here, as well as a high level of alchohol tollerance, since the Beaver usually dont approve of someone to drunk to do the actual break.

Start by hailing the beaver, and then start pulling slowly. A slow but steady pull is of the essance here, no chargin in type of breaks have ever become succesfull to my knowledge. This will acutally raise your faction with the beaver. When you have pulled for a while you know when it is time for the big BREAK of the camp. You will know that when you hear:
A giant beaver_00 says: 'You will not evade me Drawmjil!'

Now, the actual break, when you hear the words above you will be summoned to the beaver, a word of advice here: DONT PANIC. There are a couple of breakins methods here, some like to just charge the beaver with everything the got and just Nuke the living hell out of it. Even though this method actually might give you experience, will, I promise, lower you faction with beaver drastically. Soo, how do I do it you might ask? Well, the simple answer is kiting, the longer you can kite it around (around, up and down), and wait for the final big Ice-coment to go off, the better you faction will become, the better experience you will get. There is also rumoured that the kiting time affects the loottables, but that has yet to be confirmed.

Ok, once the beaver looks ready for that last and very important nuke; DO IT, if you are not a wizzard and got that fancy Icecommet (wich, by the way, impresses the living daylight out of most beavers), use the most powerfull nuke you have. Getting the beaver with a DOT will not impress anyone, especially not the beaver.

Once the nuke is off and the beaver is laying there panting. Get some rest, you will need it for the looting, and for the next breakin. And also, get that alcohol, preferably Elven red wine and train some alcohol tollerance.

Now, for the afterwork, if you have done this correctly, you faction with the beaver have gone up, probably alot depending of how succesfull the kiting was. You will now have a better chance for your next camp, especially of you intend to camp the same beaver again. A god tip is to wait for the next spawn again as soon as you feel that you are ready for it, the respawntime is about 28 minutes. If you are a cleric, you can speed that up by actually ressing the beaver.

Now, a couple of words of advice, CONSIDER the beaver before you start camping (/con), if the beaver cons green (A giant beaver looks at you warmly - looks like you would win this fight), it WILL indeed be a easy camp. The problem with it is, obviusly you will not get any XP. The other things that have been implemented is that the easy-to-get beavers have a disease proc than they cast. The spell will make you regret camping that beaver, I can promise you that. No! I say, go for the blue, reds instead. The best thing you can do is to find a white (even con) beaver, the will get you the most out of the camp.

Well, one last note: When you have succesfully camped the beaver and gotten whatever loot or experince from it. DONT GO BRAG ABOUT IT IN OOC OR SHOUT (Falloz?). I would not even recomend guildsay or grouptell. You can use occational tell to you closest friends, but I would leave it to that.

Good luck to all of you, if you need any help, give Daddy Drawmjil a tell on povar!
