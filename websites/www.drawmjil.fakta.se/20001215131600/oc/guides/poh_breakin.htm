Guide to Plane of Hate SW Corner Break

Breaking the Plane of Hate
SW Corner Method
Drawmjil Rahl

Well, there are several ways to do a break on the Plane oh Hate. What I am going to describe here is the so called SW corner. What will happen is that everyone gather at the wizzard spires in SRO. Groups are formed, 5 people in the groups that dont have a wizzard for permanent grouping. The first group to go up consists of only the rouge and wizzard (eventually monk and/or sk that will immideatly feign death). The rouge will scout for the zonepatrol to go by and depop. When this happens, the rouge will tell the warlord to start the real porting. I will not go into how the groups are put togheter or anything. This guide is simpy ment go get evryone safely to the safespot (B3).

Ok, if you are reading this guide, it is probably because you have never been to Plane of hate before, and want to know what to do, not to screw you other fellow raidmemebers. Note, that this guide is only applicable if there will be a so called SW corner break. Well, look at the picture bellow.

  

 

 

 

 

You will popup at the red '1', start running as the green lines indicates, keep close to the walls on the house where you spawned no to aggro mobs in house 3. Keep as high up on the zonewall as you possible can. When you get to approx point B1, stay for a short while and look around to make sure that you have'nt aggroed anything. Continue to B2, same as B1 here, a quick look around to make sure nothing is aggroed. Then proceed to B3 and wait here, as high up on the wall as possible (there should be several other waiting here).

If you happen to catch a mob on the way, just stay and die, DONT try to get help or anything, if you can, try to camp (dont quit /q, CAMP). The rest of the raid will get you and rezz you. If you have the time, make a small note on OOC to let the rest of the raid know that you die (/ooc got aggro, waiting for death, trying to camp)

Once on B3, stay there untill the warlord orders the charge, be sure you know who to assist, charge along the wall (with the rest of the people) along the wall and then down to the B2 corner. Use assist and take out the mobs. Someone will surely start telling stuff on the OOC, probably the rouge ('add lich', 'add cleric' and stuff), that means new creature that got aggroed from the charge, this is normal, just keep you head cool and assist untill all creatures are dead.

After this, just follow instructions from the warlord... to be able to do this, shut everything off your chatfilter except says, tells, guildsay, ooc and shout... you NEED to know whats going on.... not if you hit the mob for 5 or 20 dmg...

When there is loot dropped, the warlord will call the classes down to the mob who have them to ditribute the loot. He will do that when everything is safe, dont run there untill you are requested to.

Happy hunt, and good luck on the rolls for armour (If you are not a warrior on the same raid as Drawmjil, in wich case I wish you VERY bad luck on the rolls ;-)
