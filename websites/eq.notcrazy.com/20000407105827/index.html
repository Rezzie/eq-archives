Povar: Twinkie Bash
Thursday, April 6 15:9:17 PST -- epic

l337 d00ds!

Nightmares Asylum (NA) will sponsor their first BASH THE TWINK Event in The Arena in Lake Rathe.

Date: Monday, April 17, 2000

Time: 5 PM Pacific time

NA will create a Level 1 Ogre Warrior and equip it with the finest armor and weapons and the very best buffs available to the Norrathian masters of their arts. All "newbies" of Levels 1-5 are invited to appear at Wizard or Druid teleport locations and obtain a ride and escort to the Arena for this event. All "newbies" gathered in the Arena will commence the battle with the Twink at the same time. The "newbie" who slays the Twink will receive an item with immense magical properties depending on his class. Fallen "newbies" will be ressurected at which time they can resume the battle. We will have several of these battles until our Prizes are exhausted or our brains are riddled with boredom.

Miscellaneous Rules:

1. All "newbies" will be stripped of all buffs prior to the battle. They can re-apply their own buffs afterwards if they wish.

2. Any newbie caught receiving buffs from anyone other than themselves will be removed from the competition.

3. Nobody can heal the Twink. Anyone caught healing the Twink will be removed from the competition.

4. Newbies can use any skill, spell or ability they wish to enhance their chances at slaying the Twink.

For more information, visit the Nightmares Asylum message board at http://pub5.ezboard.com/bnightmares.html or contact a NA Officer in-game.

Natascot Ashbringer
Officer of Nightmares Asylum

Lanys T'Vyl: Oboo
Thursday, April 6 15:6:50 PST -- epic

This is the weirdest thing I have ever seen!

Rally for Justice on Lanys T'vyl

Calling all Barbarians, Erudites, Dark Elves, Trolls and Ogres who wish to continue the fight for justice! The Bears of Oboo, newly re-united with the Reach of Oboo are holding a rally in Qeynos Hills during the final hours of the Guard Hezlan murder trial. For those of you who haven't followed the proceedings to date, Guard Hezlan, while stationed outside the Qeynos gate last month, allegedly murdered Oboo, a Wolf of the North and a founding member of the Bears of Oboo guild. Hezlan pled not guilty by reason of self defense, and the trial has been proceeding over the last two weeks.

The murder trial is scheduled to finish up Saturday evening at the Qeynos courthouse, and the rally will be held to coincide with the jury deliberations. The Bears of Oboo, a guild made up of barbarians and erudites, and the Reach of Oboo, a guild made up of Dark Elves, Ogres and Trolls, have decided to merge and join forces and voices in this rally for justice.

We are calling all members of the oppressed races of Norrath to join us in our protest rally. This trial is not about the murder of one martyr, it is about equality and freedom throughout the land.

Join us on Saturday, April 8 at 6:00 pm EDT in Qeynos Hills and let your voice be heard. We will not accept anything less than justice.

For the background on the murder and the trial, visit the Bears of Oboo website at http://oboo.tripod.com

Announcing Ask the Arch Mage

In cooperation with Verant Interactive, the staff of the EverQuest Vault is pleased to announce the grand opening of Ask the Arch Mage, a special column dedicated to all things magic in EverQuest. This unique column will give players the opportunity to ask the Arch Mage of Norrath himself, Verant Game Designer Geoffrey Zatkin, questions regarding spells and magic in EverQuest.

You can email your questions in to the column at archmage@vaultnetwork.com. Writing your questions in a respectful and polite manner will definitely increase your chances of having your question answered. Please also remember use proper spelling and grammar skills when forming your questions.

Updates to Ask Arch Mage will occur about once every other week, so there will always be a new update looming on the horizon.

I would also like to thank Geoffrey publicly for taking even more time out of his busy schedule to answer more of the community's questions. Lets give him a big round of applause! :)

Karana: Micro Servo Hunt
Wednesday, April 5 15:7:20 PST -- epic

When: Sunday, April 9th, 3pm - 6pm CST
Where: Steamfont
What: A hunt for micro servos
Why: Because we need them and we are giving out cash and prizes for them. Xps for lowlevels too!
Who: Open to all! Guildies & nonguildies. Lvls 1-50.
How: You kill clockworks. If you find a micro servo, you stay in the loot screen but DON'T LOOT. Give me a tell (Ceinwein) with your general location and coordinates. I run over, loot the micro servo, pay you 10pp (!), and randomly generate a prize for you. At the end of the event, I'll be giving out extra prizes for the people who found me the most micro servos. First prize is a TRUESHOT LONGBOW!

Runaway Clockworks are Lvl 1-5. They can be found in the newbie area near the druid ring. Watch out for the spiderlings ganging up. They can also be found near the dragon bones. Watch out for skeletons. The can occassionally be found on the path to Lower Faydark.

Small Rogue Clockworks are Lvl 5-9. They can occassionally be found on the path to Lower Faydark. They can also be found in the valley and on the ridges north of the windmills. Watch out for kobolds and skellies.

Large Rogue Clockworks are Lvl 8-12. They can be found exclusively in the area outside of the mino caves. Lots of nasties to watch out for here!

Different clockworks drop cash, blackboxes, blackbox fragments, cogs, and scrap metal. We are not interested in these. Either loot them to sell or destroy them. Clean up your corpses! It makes it easier to find you.

Feel free to team up. Just make sure you agree who gets credit for what kills. I have no problem with people splitting the cash rewards or trading prizes,etc.

But I need to know who gets "credit" for the running total towards the big prizes.

I have alot of small prizes and try to make them appropriate to the winner. Not all of them are great. You could win everything from a torch to a thick leather apron to a piece of cured silk or bronze armour to forged weapons to bows and much much more. And that automatically 10pp doesn't suck either.
