Brad McQuaid Interview - Day 1, Page 2 (11-8-99)

Arr: What possible classes can the Iksar be, or has that been decided yet?

Brad: It has been decided, but we're not releasing the details at this time.

---------------

Arr: Since the Iksar are a neutral/evil race, as stated in the Ruins of Kunark web site, are they going to be killed on site in the "good" towns?

Brad: Yes.

---------------

Arr: How are the Iksar going to fit into the race war (team pvp) server? Which team will they fit into, or will they be their own, separate team?

Brad: That is undecided.

---------------

Arr: We hear that you decided to "spice up" the boat rides? Can you give us any more information on that?

Brad: There will be new boats, new ships, that travel between Antonica and Faydwer to Kunark, but no more details other than that.

Arr: Nothing other than that?

Brad: There will be a couple of different ways to get there.

Arr: What I mean is, are any NPCs going to be added onto boats to like sell booze or things like that?

Brad: No, probably not.

---------------

Arr: About how many cities are on Kunark? I know we have to assume at least one for the Iksar.

Brad: There will be the one starting city, and there are all sorts of what I call "Adventure Zones". A lot of those adventure zones are dungeons, or ancient, abandoned cities. Do keep in mind that the story of the Iksar is that they once had this empire that dominated all of Kunark and it has since crumbled and is much, much smaller in terms of its influence than it was once. You know, there's a lot of different adventure areas and things that you'll run into where are ruins or relics of the once great Iksar Empire.

---------------

Arr: What kind of problems do you think will result in adding this new race in? I know there's still ongoing debates saying that a lot of classes really aren't balances or that some classes are too weak

Brad: Those will never end. Those are inherent in a class based system. I mean, when I played text based MUDs years ago we were constantly complaining this class is over powered, this class is underpowered. So that stuffs never going to go away. Certainly we've got to balance the Iksar and what classes they're going to be able to be. But its not going to be any greater of a challenge than we already have facing us.

---------------

Arr: Back in beta, before the game had even been release, you said that necromancers may have the possibility of becoming a lichi at some point. Any idea if this is going to be implemented at all?

Brad: That is one of the ideas that we're tossing around that might relate, -might- relate, to the increased level limit. That's all I'll say at this point.

---------------

Arr: What about additional classes, any ideas that you might add any additional classes?

Brad: We don't have any short term plans for additional classes. You know, back to the balance issue, that would be more of a significant balance situation than just adding a new race. Because the Iksar will be classes that already exist its not a huge balancing thing. So we don't have any short term plans to add more classes, but I wouldn't rule out long-term plans. Right now, what were focusing on is getting the message out to the public as to whats going on with EverQuest between now and the expansion and with the expansion itself. Beyond the expansion, who knows with this genre, you just keep going, and going.

---------------

Arr: About what kind of stats. do you expect the Iksar to have? I assume they have less strength than a dwarf but more than a human, for example. Where do you think the stats. will fall?

Brad: We have some initial stats worked out for them. I expect those to significantly change in the first part of beta. So just as with EQ, we were hesitant to release classes and stats and things like that until very close to release. Its the same with this because I don't want to talk about it now and we find out in beta that those are going to change drastically then people are going to be like oh, "why did this change, why are they weaker now, or stronger?"

---------------
