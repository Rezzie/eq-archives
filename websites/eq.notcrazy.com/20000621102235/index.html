Wednesday, April 19

New Patch Message
Wednesday, April 19 15:9:30 PST -- epic


April 17th, 4:00pm
-------------------

EverQuest: The Ruins of Kunark (upgrade) should now be arriving on doorsteps around the world. Please note that attempting to create an Iksar character will not work until the expansion zones are officially opened within the next week.

We ask that if you attempt to create an Iksar character out of curiosity that you do not proceed past the "History" page (the page that speaks of your lifestyle) in the character creation process. Once at this page, please use the "Back" button to get back to the character creation screen. Pressing "Next" on this screen will cause the name you have selected to go into the "used name" list, making it unavailable for future use.

We appreciate your cooperation.

- The EverQuest Team

*Today's Patch*

Today's patch consisted of preparations for the launch of Kunark, as well as some new art. The following spell changes were also included:

- Corrected a bug with a new bard song that allowed them to charm anything, such as deities and dragons.
- Added a lesser included wisdom effect with "Brilliance" as well as a lesser included intelligence effect with "Insight". These spells will no longer stack.


- The EverQuest Team

Roleplaying Servers?

Hail friends and well met!

I have decided to lend my aid in the collective movement to discuss, design and implement a roleplaying server design we can submit to Verant.

Below, you will find the address to the bulletin board set up that will assist our efforts until I can get a site up. This is no small effort friends, but I believe with the collective pool of resources, imagination and desire that the roleplayers of Norrath harbor, we can come up with a

Sunday, April 16

NotCrazy Downtime
Sunday, April 16 21:32:32 PST -- epic

Sorry about the long delays in between news posts this week. I promise to try to make up for it. I've been attending quite a few events in game this week including a heart-felt funeral for a great hero on my home server of Quellious. Ozzmar (Main GM) was kind enough to attend and pay his respects as almost 50 of the greatest players on our server attended.

Also there was a clash in Kelethin between the King of the Dwarves and the King of the Elves about who brews the best beer. I got disconnected early but from what I saw it look to be a fun event.

Lastly, there was an undead invasion of Qeynos. Many players died in a great attempt to stop the festering hordes that martched forth towards the city.

It seems like there has been more events in the past week than I have seen in my entire life in EQ. I hope this trend continues as we move forth to a new era in EQ (Kunark).
