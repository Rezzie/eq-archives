EverQuest - Cleric Spells

The Healers of Norrath, there is no one better at the art of Healing than a Cleric. Not only do they possess an amazing ability at healing, but they are decent at melee and can even wear plate armour. Unsurpassed excellence against Undead, and good direct damage casters make this an almost must have in every group. Not to mention their +HP and +AC buffs are some of the best in the game.
