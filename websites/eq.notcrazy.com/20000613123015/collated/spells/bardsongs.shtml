EverQuest - Bard Songs

Bard's, the jacks of all trades, and mystical song composers of Norrath. A Bard is at home in a party and one known to 'Twist' his/her songs is quite an asset to any group. Similar fighting abilities of a Rouge, able to wear plate armour, and their unique spells make them a very fun class to play, and a very useful one at that.
