Plane of Fear lookout

As I mentioned before, the Plane of Fear is a zone dedicated to high level players.  It is definetely the first of its kind.  I paid it a visit earlier today with a fellow necromancer, Obidian, and it looks great.  To get there, either find a wizard that can simply teleport you there (don't know how dangerous that would be) or go through the Spectre cave in Feerott.  Once in Feerott, head to loc -1450 +2680.  You will come up to a mointain side with a small tree nearby.  Go through the fake wall and you are in the Spectre cave.  Now be very careful since the Spectres hit very hard and fast and are level 34-37.  Keep following the right path until you reach a wall. It is also a fake wall so go through it.  You will now see the entrance to the Plane of Fear but be careful with the real tough necromancer that is guarding the portal.  Once you go through the portal, you will find this real strange land filled with nasty creatures.  The portal that takes you back to Feerott is very interesting.  The first thing you see is this weird looking Hourglass and a red sky.  My friend mentioned that this Hourglass supposedly traps you or sends you some place you just can't get out.  I have no idea what it does, but when I selected it from far away, it said I was out of my reach.  Thus, it does something.  Looking over the hill you are taken when zoning to the Plane of Fear, you can see many creatures walking around.  The majority resembles a creature from the normal plane, but has a different name and is a lot more powerful.  Some examples are Spinechiller Spider (Spiders), Scareling (Fire Fiend), Fetid Fiend (Zombies), Worry Wraith (Willowisps) and Shiverback (Gorillas).  But there are some different creatures such as the Dracoliche, a fairly large dragon that could be seen even from the entrance.

Heading down the hill, you can see a few houses on most sides you turn your eyes to as well as some strange ruins scatered all over the place.  Next to one of this "houses" and roughly below the Hourglass, a large fire wall surrounds what looks like a large castle.  Even though there was something that looked like an entrance, I couldn't get to it since it had too many creatures guarding it.  I did get close enough to the fire wall to see the castle, but the only picture I got does not show it very well.  Anyway, that may or may not be Cazic-Thule's home.  It seems so.

From what I have seen in the roughlt 1 hour we spent there, the Plane of Fear doesn't really look like a big area.  Of course, this may be a totally wrong impression, specially if you consider the many secret areas that may be found.  It is a very crowded area though, with many creatures spawning all over the place and in no apparent pattern.  The main prize in this altered plains seems to be the castle.  From wherever you are you can hear Cazic-Thule shouting he won't allow any trespassers.  I wonder what level this "thing" would be.

A few last comments.  If you plan on paying the Plane of Fear a visit, I believe there are two ways to get there.  First, you can find a good party (levels 30+) and fight your way to the portal since there are a few Spectres on the way.  The other thing you can do is get SoW'd and run till you zone.  Spectres are slow and will be easily outrunned.  The other important thing you should know is that Gather Shadows is pretty much useless there.  All creatures can see you.  And finally, make sure you have gate memorized.  It does work and the last thing you want is zone out of the Plane of Fear to fall on the lap of Cyndreela, the necromancer guarding the other side of the portal.

I hope that gives you an idea of the Plane of Fear.  If you are looking for some adrenaline rush, just head that way.
