How to Sneak into Kunark


I took my level 17 (well, 16 now) Gnome necro to Kunark, and I hope that my experiences may help others to avoid the problems I had on the journey. I took the ferries from the Butcherblock docks. They come to the South-most dock there. You may want to use Gather Shadows to invis yourself until you are on the ferries. I had problems with the dwarves here, who were just not happy to see me. Once on the ferry, you are safe (they will con indifferent to you as long as you stay on). Ride the ferry to the main boat (which is huge and beautiful). The ferries don't stop, so jump right off onto the boat and go up the ramp. The boat will set sail shortly, and it's quite fast.

When the boat zones into Firiona Vie, I had a problem whereby instead of remaining on the boat, I was deposited on the land near the docks. I think that normally you will have the chance to jump off the boat and swim west. This proved to be unfortunate, because the "Pilgrims" here can see through Gather Shadows, and I was KOS to them. I managed to bind there before having trouble, but I died several times from the Pilgrims before I figured out a safe route. Basically, what you want to do is swim around the docks, at a safe distance, to the SW corner, then go a little west to a bit of land there. This small section of land was deserted, and I was able to rebind there. I had to get a friend to retrieve my corpse from near the docks where I died the first time (thanks again from the bottom of my heart, Purity!). I lost 1/2 level dying over and over here. Sometimes I'd reappear and die immediately. Finally I escaped the loop. So, be sure to stay away from the land area by the docks! I hope your swim is nice and high. At 85 I was all tuckered out and barely able to keep my head above water when I reached the land.

Once on the bit of land, you can use Gather Shadows again, which does work against the drixies and various huge spiders (thank goodness). Run North along the mountain border here until you find the stream. Follow the stream West and Northwest, it will go up a ways, and then you'll zone into The Lake of Ill Omen. The loc is approximately 1290,3500. The zone border was free of any creatures, and a safe binding spot if you plan to stay a while. Follow the stream farther along. Eventually you'll come out onto a large plain. The Iksar city is to the NE. I did not go close enough to the city to con the guards there, but there's plenty to hunt all around this zone, from level 10 on up. Bring lots of food and water :)

I hope this will help others who are planning to make the trip.

- Lumyna of Innoruuk
