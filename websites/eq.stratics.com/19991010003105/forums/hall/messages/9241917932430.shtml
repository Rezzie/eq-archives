Do Ii need to spend points from the guildhall to start skills? ...

Some skills you have to put at least one practice point in, some you don't. For instance, I started baking with zero points, and without putting any points in raised my skill up solely by baking different recipes.

On the other hand, you can't use Meditation until you put a practice point into it. So if you're a spell caster, it's extremely important to put at least one point into Meditation (unless you like sitting around for hours recovering mana and rememorizing spells.)
