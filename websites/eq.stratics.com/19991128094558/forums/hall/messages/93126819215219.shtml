How do I loot? Free loot except on stuff like fine steel (which at level 30 is still good to collect and sell), which goes alpha if everyone wants it. I have been in plenty of groups though where no one really had any particular interest in FS, so it was just free loot all around.

The good stuff? That goes to who needs it the most. Every now and then there's a debate on who needs it more, but those don't last long. Usually if more than one person needs it, it goes to the highest level person, unless a lower level player has really crappy equipment.

The really, really good stuff? That goes to the person who got their friends together to camp for it. In situations where you pull someone in from the zone that you don't really know, and they insist that the group loot the good spawn alpha, that person is full of BS. I gladly spend hours helping my friends get a good piece of equipment even if the only thing I get out of it is experience. Most of my friends gladly do the same. If you and your friends start worrying about splitting all loot you get evenly, I garauntee you that you will not be friends for long.

Sure, you can complain that what you need is already being camped. I can tell you this though. There is always, positively SOME thing, SOMEwhere, that you can use, that is NOT being camped. Try Najena (ok, Jboots are always camped. Bonecracker is free 50% of the time I go in there, as is the ogre captain... Rathyl is almost always free). Try Solusek if you're 25+. Yes, it's a 20+ zone, but I would stick near a zone line if you're under 25. Try Permafrost. Try The Gorge of King Xrobb (beholder maze). The BIBS in Runnyeye is free more often than it's camped. Just get out there and explore and adventure. If your main concern is that you get the same cash as everyone else in your party, you're playing for the wrong reason.
