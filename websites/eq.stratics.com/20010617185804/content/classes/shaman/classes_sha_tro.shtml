Playing a Troll Shaman

 Introduction   Creation   Skills   Spells 

Introduction
Welcome to Bohica's troll shaman guide. The path you choose is a most interesting and inherently difficult one. It has, however, been travelled before. Learn from Bohica. Strengthen yourself for the battle ahead. The hordes of light come. Like the waves of the sea they will throw themselves against us. Prepare, young one. Together we will feast upon their fallen. So Cazic Thule has said. So shall it be.

This guide is a work in progress. Many experienced shaman have already contributed to make it as accurate and thorough as possible. Shaman, feel free to e-mail (boz@tyler.net) me information that I need to correct or that I have not yet obtained.

Creation

ATTRIBUTES

STRENGTH
  • Strength determines the weight you can carry before becoming encumbered.
  • The higher your strength, the larger the weapon you can wield in combat without losing your stamina.
  • Strength has a direct impact on your attack value. Increase your strength, and you increase your ability to force that blow through your enemy's defences.
STAMINA
  • Stamina impacts directly on the number of your initial hit points and the number of hit points you will gain upon reaching each new level.
  • A high stamina allows you to continue a tiring activity for a longer period of time. You can run farther without a break. You will be able to swing that overweight lead staff of thumping more times without becoming exhausted.
AGILITY
  • Agility influences armor class. An agile troll will lead a longer life than a slog-footed one.
DEXTERITY
  • Dexterity is said to influence your chance of striking an opponent in melee combat.
  • Dexterity should increase your chances with ranged weapon attacks as well.
WISDOM
  • Wisdom determines the shaman's mana and shaman spell success.
  • Wisdom increase a character's ability to save versus spells that impact the mind.
  • Wisdom cannot be raised with any shaman spells. All the physical attributes and even charisma can be raised this way. Because of this, I recommend the starting shaman place as many bonus attribute points into wisdom as is allowed.
INTELLIGENCE
  • A high intellingence allows a troll to learn skills at a faster rate this his less-gifted peers.
CHARISMA
  • Charisma effects how NPC's react to your character.
  • A higher charisma will lower prices at merchants.
  • Everyone hates a troll anyway, so why worry about it?
SKILL POINT USAGE

STARTING OUT
  • Purchase one point in hand-to-hand. This will double the damage you can do with a punch. You may have to resort to hand-to-hand combat should you die without an extra weapon in the bank and no money to purchase another.
  • Save all other points at this time.
  • Remember to set up a hot key for sense heading and practice this skill while adventuring.
EARLY LIFE
  • Channel: Spend a point on this at 4th level. It will raise itself automatically after this initial purchase.
  • Weapon Skills: Choose a single weapon skill for your character early in life. The two basic choices for shaman will be one hand blunt or two hand blunt. Try to stick with this choice exclusively. At fifth level, I would spend the points necessary to increase your first choice to at least (20). This should not take many points as combat alone should have increased the skill prior to this. Fifth level is an important one in that you will now lose experience upon death.
  • Tailoring: At 5th level, after purchasing the above weapon skill points, try to spend 15 points on tailoring. A party of 5th level trolls can now kill brown bears. The tailoring skill will allow you to create patchwork armor from the bear hides. This makes passable armor until you can afford better. After you are outfitted with full patchwork, you can sell the excess for a hefty profit to your fellow trolls.
  • Meditate: Purchase a single point of this at level 8. This will increase quickly on its own after the initial purchase.
MID AND UPPER LEVELS
  • I'll let you know about it when I get there.
DIETY CHOICE

CAZIC-THULE: Followers of Cazic-Thule, The Faceless, fear their Lord and believe that only by causing terror in others will they be spared his vengeful wrath. They strive to beat down and suppress all hope. Fear rules their lives and through fear they rule the lives of others. Pain, misery, violence, torture, living sacrifice; these are the tools of a Cazicite. Many Lizardman tribes are devout followers of Cazic-Thule, but his number of humanoid followers grows daily, a cold shadow slowly engulfing the bright spots of Norrath in a nightmare of horror and pain.
  • Trolls and Ogres can both be followers of this Diety.
  • The Lost Temple of Cazic-Thule would be an ideal place for the Faceless One's followers to hold player ran quests and role-playing events.
INNORUUK: Followers of Innoruuk, The Prince of Hate, include nearly the entire Dark Elven race who regard him as their "Father." They believe that Hate is a creative force, or rather "THE" creative force int the universe. Creativity born of destruction. Love and kindness are tools for those too ignorant to know what they want or too cowardly to do what is necessary to obtain it. It is only through the total disdain of your enemies that you can gain true power over them. Pity and mercy have no power when confronted with contempt and viciousness. It is the honest belief of the followers of Innoruuk that if they were to hate strong enough they could destroy all of Norrath.
  • Trolls and Dark Elves can both worship Innoruuk.
  • If you are a troll and plan on spending a lot of time in the dark elf city, Innoruuk would be an optimal choice of diety. The native dark elves will be more lenient and loose of purse with fellow worshipers.

PVP SWITCH

Many trolls may choose to be born on servers other than Rallos Zek (+pvp). At some point in a troll's life, the decision may be made to charge down the path of discord and engage the enemy at will. This switch cannot be made in the troll hometown of Grobb. Strange as it may seem, no trolls have risen to the status of Priest of Discord here. The troll seeking to switch must travel far north to the dark elf city of Neriak. Here one will find, near the entrance to the Commons, a dark elf Priest of Discord willing to accomodate this dire wish.

Skill Descriptions

ABJURE
Magical skill used to determine the effectiveness of the following spells: Endure Cold, Inner Fire, Endure Fire, Scale Skin, Endure Disease, Spirit of the Bear, Endure Poison, Burst of Strength, Turtle Skin, Cancel Magic, Endure Magic, Frenzy, Resist Cold, Resist Fire, Resist Poison, Resist Disease, Fury, Shifting Shield , Wall of Fire, Resist Magic, Nullify Magic, and Abolish Disease.

ALCHEMY
No first hand knowledge of skill usage. Potion making skill.

ALCOHOL TOLERANCE
The greater your skill level, the more alcohol you can consume before blurred vision, slurred speech, and staggering hits your character. Five short beers totally wiped out my character with no skill in this.

ALTER
Magical skill used to determine the effectiveness of the following spells: Strengthen, Minor Healing, Cure disease, Dexterous Aura, Gate, Cure Poison, Feet Like Cat, Drowsy, Fleeting Fury, Light Healing, Cure Blindness, Spirit of Wolf, Bind Affinity, Enduring Breath, Root, Levitate, Disempower, Spirit of Snake, Walking Sleep, Healing, Malise, Spirit of Monkey, Spirit Strength, Regeneration, Counteract Disease, Listless Power, Greater Healing, Counteract Poison, Quickness, Alluring Aura, Raging Strength, Befriend Animal, Rising Dexterity, Tagar's Insects, Malisement, Enstill, Nimble, Health, Talisman of Tnarg, Charm, Extinguish Fatigue, Chloroplast, Dexterity, Furious Strength, Glamour, Togor's Insects, Malosi, Charisma, Dexterity II, and Strength.

BAKING
No personal experience with this skill.

BEG
The only thing I have ever gotten from begging is slaughtered from the NPC I tried to beg from. I have never used this skill higher than skill level 15, so perhaps it is more successful at higher levels. I'm sure charisma and character class impact on this in some way also.

BIND WOUND
If you have bandages (6 silver each), you can apply these to a wounded character, healing a very small amount of damage. I have little experience in using this skill.

BREWING
Fer dem trolls wan make tastie drinkie b'fore bashem frogs, de brew materials be behind de bar in Grobb. De still be in de bar of Oggok to mixem. But, dont go past de Oggok bar into de warrior guild or Cranik killem troll on sight. A troll build up a mighty thirst after weez bashem gud all night. - Kraak, troll warrior

CHANNEL
Competence in this skill is doubly important for a shaman. Channelling determines how long it takes to cast a spell. Channelling also determines the chance of a melee attack against the caster interrupting his chance to finish casting. Channelling goes up quickly. I recommend purchasing one skill point in it at fourth level. After that, channelling will go up by itself and stay near maximum as your character ages.

COMMON TOUNGE
This is the disgusting bird-chirpy like tounge used by the hummies. All races are proficient in this tounge. Trolls learned it from the screams of tortured prisoners ringing through Grobb.

CONJURE
Magical skill used to determine the success of the following spells: Sicken, Summon Drink, Spirit Pouch, Summon Food, Winged Courier, Tainted Breath, Infectious Cloud, Affliction, Insidious Fever, Scourge, Companion Spirit, Venom of the Snake, Insidious Malady, Vigilant Spirit, Envenomed Bolt, Plague, and Frenzied Spirit.

DARK SPEECH
This tounge was developed to aid communication between Drow, Ogres, and Trolls during joint raids on the weak and prideful humies and their allies.

DEFENSE
Defense, agility, and armor are all factors combined to determine your character's armor class. Defense is not trainable. Only experience gained in combat will increase this skill.

DIVINATE
Magical skill used to determine the success of the following spells: Flash of Light, Sense Magic, Spirit Sight, Sense Animals, Serpent Sight, Invisibility to Animals, Vision, Ultravision, Blinding Luminance, and Assiduous Vision.

DODGE
The higher one's skill in dodge, the greater the chance that they can sidestep a foe's melee attack.

EVOKE
Magical skill used to determine the success of the following spells: Burst of Flame, Frost Rift, Spirit Strike, and Winter's Roar.

FLETCHING
No personal experience with this skill.

HAND TO HAND
When you are caught weaponless, this skill determines your chance of punching your foe. I highly recommend spending one beginning point at level one on Hand to Hand. That one point of skill will double the maximum damage you can cause with a punch. Hand to hand rises very slowly.

JEWEL CRAFT
No personal experience with this skill.

MEDITATE
Meditation restores mana at an increased rate. Meditation shortens spell memorization time. Spend one point on meditation at eighth level. Meditation raises very quickly and will stay near maximum as you raise in levels.

OFFENSE
Offense, strength, and weapon skill level combine to determine a character's attack ability. Offense is not trainable. Only experience gained in combat will raise offense.

ONE HAND BLUNT
This skill determines your success when wielding a club, hammer, mace or staff with one hand. Weapon skills such as one hand blunt combine with strength and offense to determine your attack value.

PIERCING
I have never encountered or even seen mentioned a piercing weapon that is wieldable by a Shaman. If one is out there, this skill would determine your success with it. I would love to wield a razor sharp, gem encrusted, magical sacrificial shaman dagger. Perhaps there is one out there just waiting to be found.

POTTERY
No personal experience with this skill.

SENSE HEADING
Success with this skill will give you your facing to the nearest of eight compass directions. When you fail, you will get a message telling you that you cannot determine which direction you are facing. No false headings are given from failure.

SMITHING
Fer dem troll wanna be u smith. Der be a forge in Oggok metal armor store. De troll kin buy de ore an de sharpening stone from a troll on de path to de Shadowknight Guild in Grobb. Den troll buy moldies an book from Ogre (if u stand with yer back to the entrance of Okkrog, turn right and look for some caveman huts, she be there). Den de forge it in de Ogre metal armor shop. - Kraak, troll warrior

SPECIALIZE
No personal experience with this skill.

SWIMMING
As this skill increases, so does the speed at which you move through deep water.

TAILORING
This skill allows the character to craft patchwork and/or leather armor using the hides of animals. A skill of (15) allows the user to craft a patchwork tunic. Troll armor requires the hides of large creatures, namely bears. To make the armor, the character must purchase a sewing kit and a pattern for the specific piece of armor wanted. The hide and the pattern are placed into the sewing kit and "combined". If the skill succeeds, the piece of armor is made. If it fails, both the hide and the pattern are lost. The sewing kit is reusable. Selling patchwork armor to fellow trolls and ogres is much more profitable than selling to NPC merchants.
Tailoring, when combined with blacksmithing, will allow the tailor to create studded leather and banded armor. The tailor will need a large sewing kit, medium and high quality pelts plus studs or braces made by a blacksmith. - Detritus, troll shaman.

TROLL TOUNGE
Bohica's native language. This gutteral collection of growls, grunts, and groans insures secrecy when trolls prepare their excursions against the shorties.

TWO HAND BLUNT
For sheer damage potential in melee combat, the shaman chooses to fight two handed with a blunt weapon. A two-handed war hammer is a prized possession indeed for the up and coming shaman. Weapon skill, strength, and offense ratings combine to determine a character's attack rating.

Spell Descriptions
Some spell descriptions are from sources outside Bohica's life experiences. Each definition from an outside source will be followed by its source.

Level 1

Strengthen
This spell adds 10 strength points to the target of the spell.

Burst of Flame
Burst of Flame does direct fire damage to a single target. Maximum damage is 5 pts.

Minor Healing
Heals 10 points of damage on a single target.

Flash of Light
Momentarily blinds a single target. While this works on other +PvP players, its effects are so short term as to be negligible. No effect on party member if targetted.

Cure Disease
Removes disease from spell target.

Endure Cold
Adds a maximum of +20 to cold resistance. The maximum effect is reached at the 10th level of casting. - Detritus, troll shaman

Dexterous Aura
Increases target's dexterity. Maximum addition is 10 pts and reached at 6th level.

Inner Fire
Added 15 hit points and 1 ac point to target when cast by 5th level shaman. Adds 16 hp and 2 ac pt when cast by 6th level shaman. Adds 17 hp and 2 ac pt at 7th level. Added 19 hp and 2 ac at 9th. Further tests pending.

Sense Magic
Supposed to be used to detect magic on items. Most magic items clearly state function without this spell. I have yet to determine this spell's usefulness.
Sense Magic is most useful in practicing divination. At level 14 the shaman has access to Serpent Sight, which is quite appreciated when touring with humans. Casting a level 14 divination spell when your divination skill is around 5 means a lot of fizzling. - Detritus, troll shaman

Level 5

Gate
Returns caster to home point. This cannot be cast on others.

Sicken
Short term disease with a small amount of initial direct damage to target. Causes 8 pts of damage at 7th level. At 9th level, 8 pts were still the maximum. This spell reduces the troll regeneration rate. If this is consitent with other races, it has a minor damage-over-time effect. Further tests pending.

Cure poison
Removes poison from spell's target.

Summon Drink

Endure Fire
Adds a maximum of +20 to fire resistance. The maximum effect is reached at the 10th level of casting. - Detritus, troll shaman

Feet Like Cat

Drowsy

Fleeting Fury

Spirit Pouch
Creates a magical pouch that stores up to four items of any size (tested with 2 handed staff). This bag can be traded and banked. It cannot be sold to merchants. The bag and all of its contents will dissappear upon death or disconnection.

Scale Skin
Snake scales are a necessary reagent to cast this spell. Adds +5 ac at 6th level. Adds +6 ac at 9th. Tests pending.

Frost Rift
Causes direct cold damage to a single target. Maximum damage potential is 15 pts. - Quwem
