Roleplaying Stories

We here at EverCrest fully support roleplaying in Norrath (even though we occasionally make fun of roleplayers, we're all roleplayers ourselves, so it's ok), and we applaud all efforts to put together roleplaying events. To support roleplaying, we've decided to put this section online, where we'll feature RP stories written not only by members of the EverCrest staff, but also submissions from all of our readers. Just email us your story and we'll put it up here for everyone to read. Please read the submission guidelines before submitting a story.
