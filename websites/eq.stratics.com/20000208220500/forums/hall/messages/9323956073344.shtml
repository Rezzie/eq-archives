This is a Random spawn in Castle Mistmoore behind the Cemetary at the hidden entrance.

The Caitiff is a Dark Elf, and friendly to All PC's. He is a level 35ish mob, and does not carry the Wakizashi on his person. It is a Quest Item, and in order to complete the quest, you have to free his Mother and Father's Souls from the service of Lord Mistmoore. They are his Maid and Butler, and I suspect you must get items off their corpses / Kill Lord Mistmoore / Talk to them, say you'll free them / etc.

Haven't done the quest, so I dont know., but its a HIGH level quest which will take you into the bowels of Castle Mistmoore
