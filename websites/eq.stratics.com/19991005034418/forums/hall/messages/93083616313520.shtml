> I hope your plan was to increase the effectiveness of these spells, not
just
> remove/replace them. Most 44+ necros I know only have 4 level 44 spells
and
> 3 level 49 spells :(

The spells were removed because Necromancers (and Shamans) could not
charm anything near their level - the pets that they can summon are almost
always better than anything they could hope to charm (because of the charm
level limit). Because of this, and because of some of the inherant trouble
that can come with the charm spells, I decided to remove them from the
Shaman and Necro. I did not remove the undead charming, which works very
well. On a similar note, Shamans did not lose their animal charming. I
will make sure to replace it with something interesting.

As for the 'few spells' - most of the high level ones are researched.
They are a bit harder to find - but still very nice.
