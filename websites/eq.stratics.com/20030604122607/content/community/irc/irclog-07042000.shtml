IRC: Conversation with Aradune 07/04/2000

 Introduction   Conversation with Brad 

Editor's Note:
 Recently a few people chatting on the #everquest IRC channel were pleasantly suprised when EverQuest Creator Brad McQuaid (Aradune) dropped in for a bit.

They had a nice conversation regarding the state of things in EQ: class balance, Ruins of Kunark, enhancements, nerfs and other miscellaneous topics. Now for your reading pleasure, we present a partial chat log from this conversation.

Enjoy.
Eskavar DeWinter
Technical Lead - EQ Stratics

 top
