Well, there are severall places on Antonica to get Ring / Chain / Plate.

First off you can get Ringmail pieces that will fit you in Highpass Hold off Orc Scouts, Orc Fanatics, Orc Medics, and Orc Mercenaries. this would be appropriate for your level .... just get some backup it can get rough

For Chain, im not sure where to find that, but most higher level dungeons should have it (aka Guk, Cazic) but I'd stick to Banded mail, its better AC than Ring or chain, and less weight.

For PLatemail, Im not sure where you can find FS, but you can get bronze in many places. Lg Bronze is in Najena off Ogre Guard Captain. Med Bronze is in Lake Rathe off Dry Boned Skeletons. Sm Bronze is in Unrest off Various Undead.

Just some pointers on where to find armor in Antonica. :)
