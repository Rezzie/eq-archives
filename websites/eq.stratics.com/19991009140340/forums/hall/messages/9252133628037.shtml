When I read that someone saw a message from Brad that the skill wouldn't be implimented, my heart sunk.

I wrote a letter to Verant in hopes that it could sway them if this is indeed true. I've enclosed it below for all to see.

----

To whom it may concern.

First off, let me say that I LOVE EverQuest.

It's just grown on me. You've all done a WONDERFUL job.

I chose to play a Rogue. They've always been my favorite characters. I must say
however, I was VERY perturbed to hear a rumor that the poison skill will not be
implemented. I started this character with the hopes that I would be able to use Backstab
and Poison at some point. This news really made me disappointed and angry.

The poison skill is SORELY needed to round out the character class. Right now the Rogue
is just a lesser Warrior with backstab. I always try to fight and adventure in a group but
there are sometimes that I'd like to solo also. It is simply not possible to do so with this
character. Oh, sure, you can solo lesser blues and only if it's a true one on one fight and
nothing else jumps in. We have no ability to run away or strengthen ourselves. We're
simply not powerful enough to stand it alone. Backstab is wonderful but Poison is
something that the character simply isn't complete without.

Every class has some aspect to even it up to the other classes. I assume that you guys
think that Backstab evens it up for the Rogue. It does not.

I've been loyal to Verant and this game the whole way through. During the beginning, I
tried to calm people. When I'm killed by a game bug, I understand. I'm polite to other
players. I keep a website open for players to communicate. I consider myself to be a good
contributor to the EQ community.

I ask you to PLEASE, PLEASE not give up on the poison skill. I speak for ALL the Rogues
on the Safehouse (a Rogue only message board, most all Rogues in the game frequent it)
site. DO NOT let us down on this. We've all kept with the character in hopes that we'd
eventually get this skill as promised. We've been kept in the dark about it.

Don't think that not implementing this skill will be a good thing. It will be then, and only
then, that my faith in this game will waver. It may be hard, it may be difficult to implement,
it may take weeks of work, but it's NECESSARY. Please, don't make this the reason I loose
my faith in the game.

Sincerely,

Wolvinsbane the Rogue
