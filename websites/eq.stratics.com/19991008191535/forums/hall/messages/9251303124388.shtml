I think you misunderstand the way monster frenzy works. If a monster is attacking you, and you drop below half health, there is nothing that can get it off you until you are dead, or a healer returns your life to above half. Actually, I believe the cutoff to be 3 bubbles, but others have argued 2 bubbles, so I compromised. The moral? If you are in a party situation, always have people step back anytime their health is about to drop below 3 bubbles, or half, or whatever you find works, and let other tanks take the beating until they get near 3 bubbles, etc. Always have a way to do damage while you are stepped back; this is one of the bow's greatest strengths in the game. Anyway, I digress. My point is simply that when a Mob wont get off you, its usually not because the people helping you aren't taunting.
