Freeport
Bustling with activity, the city of Freeport is the stony sentinal of humanity lying on the Eastern coast of Antonica, and serves as a gateway to the continent Faydwer. Traveler's Guide to Freeport, Maps of Freeport
 top


Erudin
Located just across the sea from the human settlement of Qeynos, the marble and gold city of Erudin is the magical, island-bound city of the High Men, otherwise known as Erudites. Traveler's Guide to Erudin
 top


Grobb
We low on Ant-onikk. Below humies and tiny humies we crush. Slime and dark is home! We no like bright light in sky or ugly green things on ground! Traveler's Guide to Grobb, Map of Grobb
 top


Halas
Located at the Northernmost point of Antonica and atop the snowiest of ridges, Halas is the ice-laden home of the Barbarian folk of Norrath. Traveler's Guide to Halas, Map of Halas
 top


Rivervale
Rivervale is home to the stout Halflings and is nestled within the heart of Antonica, between both the highest of mountain ranges and the deepest of valleys. Traveler's Guide to Rivervale
