Getting Drunk in EQ

To Drink a Drink So Fine
Upon a single beer you will not notice the effects of inebriation much, although ye do become less intelligent. After around 2 to 3 drinks you begin to stumble, waving back and forward, making it impossible to cast spells. And after that your speech becomes impaired; the more you drink the more your words become garbled.

'Tis after the fourth drink that your vision becomes distorted, making the simple task of walking very difficult. The more you drink after that, the more your vision gets distorted.

But the major joy of getting 'drunk off one's arse' is not the process of drinking with your mates... 'tis what you do after getting drunk that you have the most fun. When people stare at you and mutter obscenities as you slur every word and stumble about. Ye must beware of cliffs and drops and monsters for you surely will be crazed in your drunken stupor.

Drinking can make a mage useless and a rogue will lose his edge. But warriors can revel in great quantities drink, since doing so will give them strength and stamina, although take away agility and dexterity.

So, I welcome ye all to take some of your well earned gold and squander it on a good amount of drink in the times ye feel bored and your life shall surely liven up.

One story borne of drink is that of a nameless bard who spent no less than 5 platinum on drink, then, making his way to the top of the Temple of Marr, he played a song of dissonance to aggravate the paladins and jumped off... Ye can probably guess his fate but by it all he enjoyed it!

And while I be not nameless, I be a minstrel in my own right, not having enacted many adventures in a drunken stupor as of yet, but determined to have more than a few in the near future.
