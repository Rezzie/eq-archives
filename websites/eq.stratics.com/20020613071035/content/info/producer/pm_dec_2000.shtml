EverQuest Stratics Project Manager's Letter

Hail Norrath!

The Holidays are upon us, and so things here at EQSS may seem a little slower and more erratic than usual (if thats possible). Still, the previous month was another good one for us here at Stratics, especially the early part of it because of the EQ Gathering 2000 which was attended by a large number of our staff! Moonshadow, Reijas, Ogamok, Onnie and myself had a rip-roaring good time and spent far too much money at the Texas Ren Fest. We also had the opportunity to meet and talk with Geoffry Zatkin as well as one of the EverQuest Lead Designers, plus people from RPGWear.com and MaximumEQ.com. The entire event was a huge success and a great time was had by all!!! (Just look at the pictures!)

Other major news here at EQSS is that at long last, we have a calendar system available for use by all visitors here at our site. We've provided a calendar for every server and you can request that your event be listed on your server calendar by posting in our Server Forums. Just look for the post titled Event Request Instructions in each server forum for details. This calendar is open for posting Bazaars, Quests, Raids and anything else you can schedule in Norrath, so feel free to use it as much as you want! (Moderators for the server forums to help maintain the calendars and promote more active discussion are always wanted, as well!)

We've also gone through a pretty significant upgrade to our forum software in the last month and they are running better than ever, so we think you will find them more responsive than ever.

Oh lets see, what else...what else...Oh YES! Onnie is back!!! Yaaay! Onnie returned to our staff as Lead Editor actually in late October and has been slaying the bixies that have gathered in the Editorial Staff and starting the machine of Editorial Processing moving again. You should see more regular updates to our existing content, as well as new content, start to pick up again very soon! Got something you'ld like to see posted? Learn how by checking out our Submission Guidelines!

Finally, let me wish all of you the very best for this coming Holiday Season. May the spirit and joy of this holiday season reach out to all Races and Classes in Norrath.

All the best!

Greebo
EQSS Project Manager
