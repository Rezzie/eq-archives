Basic Fletching

When the new fletcher is first starting out, and indeed as they continue down their path of progression, they need to keep one thing in mind: As a arrow becomes trivial, upgrade to the next expensive component, and revert back to the cheapest components for the other parts.

For instance, when first starting, the new fletcher will choose the Basic Point, the Wood shart, the round fletch, and the large nock. When these become trivial, the fletcher should look and see what component is the next level up in cost. Always increase your arrow creation based on component cost.

In this case, the next step up is the medium nock which I believe costs 3 Copper. So, the fletcher will start making the next level arrows with the following components: Basic Tip, Basic Wood Shaft, Basic Fletch, Medium Nock.

When this becomes trivial, he or she will look for the component that is more expensive than the medium nock, I believe it is the Parabolic Fletch (Level 2 Fletch). So at this point his components become: Basic Tip, Basic Wood Shaft, Parabolic Fletch, Basic Large Nock.

Can you see the progression here? Always revert back to the basics for all components except the one being upgraded. I believe this holds true throughout the proccess. Make sure you compare the prices of all the components, as the cost increase does not follow any specific pattern.

FLETCHING TIP

Getting tired of buying 20 pieces of each component and moving that slider to select one of them and then add them to the kit? Those days are over. Follow these simple steps to make it so when you purchase an item, it goes directly into your kit!

Fill all of your main inventory slots with items other than fletching components. Personally I purchase one set of compents and make one of the arrows first and put it in one of my inventory slots. If you can, fletch a different arrow and put it in your ammo section, Ill explain why in a moment.

At this point, you have made one arrow of the type you are shooting for, and put it in your inventory, and filled the other slots up. And there is a different type arrow in your ammo slot.

Right click on the merchant that sells the components. When it opens up the components you can buy, right click on your fletching kit.

Select and purchase 1 component of the arrow. It will automatically put it in your fletching kit! After you buy all 4 components, hit combine. If you are successfull, just move your mouse over your image on the right and click on it. It will drop the arrow you made into the inventory and you will see the number of the first arrow you put there increase to 2. I personally always put a different type arrow in my ammo slot because if you do not, when you click on your image, it will drop it into the ammo slot.

Then it is a simple process to buy 4 more pieces and hit combine. As you progress up in level, you will find yourself selling the arrows back to continue your training. Since the merchant is already open, you just click on the arrows and you can sell them and not interrupt your fletching. I personally sell all but one so it will continue to drop arrows into that slot and not fletching components.
