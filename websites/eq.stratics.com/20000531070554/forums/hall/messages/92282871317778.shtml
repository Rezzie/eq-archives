They start at 0, so first thing to do is train once in each spell type. But your channeling, if you are a paladin, will be nearly maxxed (LoH is good for more than just healing it seems!) so your spells will come out quick. Then just work em up. A level 9 char casting lvl 1 spells will gain in his/her spell types (abjuration etc) really quickly, almost every second time I cast it seems. Yes, you fail alot at first but by the time you are tenth level, if you have been using them alot, you will be casting very consitently.
