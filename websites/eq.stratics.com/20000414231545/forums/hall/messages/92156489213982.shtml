---Before you even take a step, go to the "Options" screen. Then choose the "Keyboard" Options. There, you can scroll through the keyboard commands. You will see that "auto-attack" is defaulted to "a"-- presuming they didn't change it. Click in the space next to the "a" and replace it with a non-letter key. I used the "[" key just because I knew I would not likely accidentally hit it. You cannot choose keys such as "Ctrl" and "Alt."
