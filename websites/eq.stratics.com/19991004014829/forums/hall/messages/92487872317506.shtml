Greeting friend,

Everquest skills are broken down into 2 major categories: Combat and Non-Combat. Skills range from 1 handed slash to Brewing. It is generally true that you have a max skill gain of 5 per level.

On top of this are modifiers, however. As an example of one modification. At level 12, Palidans gain Meditation skill. For one train in Meditation (freebe points) you start at a 12 skill. From there one train is = to one gain.

Skill Maxes are (generally speaking) 5*Level. Therefore, at 15th level a Palidan could have 75 2 Handed Sword and 0 1 Handed Sword. If the Palidan were willing to put the time in, he could get 1 H Slashing up to 75 also.

The problem with letting combat skills go to long is that I have noticed skill gains in combat occur more frequently with like leveled creatures and less with others. So while I can swing 29 points of damage with my 2 handed Dwarven Axe, I may only swing 9 with a One handed Slasher(Most 1 handed slashers with skill can swing 13-19). Try beating a creature swinging 1/2 ur normal damage... so most ppl try to raise skill on lesser creatures. This also does not work well.

Trade skills on the other hand seem to be workable at any times, as do spell categories (Evocation, Divination, etc...).

Hope this helps,

Lyshan, Weaponmaster Palidan of Karana

PS. You can save your 5 free trains per level as long as u like... most combat skills never require more than 1 train to be effective.
