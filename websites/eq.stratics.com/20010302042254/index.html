New Producers Letter Posted Today
Official_EverQuest_Letter [ 8:51 PM EST ] - Ogamok
As we approach the two-year anniversary of EverQuest, we can’t help but look back at the changes that have taken place in the world of Norrath. In the beginning our Customer Service Department was small and the rules both we and the players were expected to follow were still being hammered out on a day-to-day basis. We then grew quickly; as did our expectations, and as time went on we developed and refined a number of rules providing varying levels of communication between us and the player community. Now, as we move into the third year of our lives in Norrath, we plan to focus strongly on this aspect of communication and dialogue. Henceforth, we will strive to even better communicate our Customer Service policies to the player community at large.

Paladin Best of the Best Rules
[ 8:44 PM EST ] - Ogamok
Paladin Best of the Best Rule Sheet
Paladins level 54+

Single Elimination 1 vs. 1 competition for all rounds but final round.

Final round (to determine 1st and 2nd place on both Test and live) will be a best 2 out of 3 match.


Dates: March 1st - March 8th on Live Servers, times will be posted at least 48 hours in advance.

Final is on Test Server, March 10th at 12:00pm PST, all capable servers will be represented. (Sign up for finals to start at 11:30am PST)


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Spectator and General Rulings


Guides and GMs are considered judges and therefore restricted from being attacked. Anyone found attacking a GM or Guide may be banished from the Arena or death touched without warning.

The Arena is off limits to unauthorized PvP 15 minutes before and 15 minutes after the event. This is to ensure that the contestants and spectators are safe.

Spectators shall remain seated and not casting during the match. Any spell casting shall be considered aimed at a participant, and either harmful or helpful to someone in the match. Anyone who casts any spells (including Bard songs) during a match can be death touched and removed from the Arena.

Spectators shall not use /shout for any reason, but use /ooc instead. This is so that those involved in the match can use /shout, and the channel can be turned off to reduce distraction to the contestants involved. Use of /shout at anytime can result in a silence command being issued, rendering the player unable to use any form of communication, or banishment from the Arena.

Spectators shall not interfere with this event. Severe penalties are used only when directly attacking a player involved in the match, attacking a judge during the match, or healing a player involved in a match.

VI may limit the amount of people in the zone to control conditions of lag or frame rate for the participants. The event shall start open seating, but may close off at any time. The Event Staff may use any sort of decision on who stays and who leaves to ensure the event is a success.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Combatant Rules


The owner of the account must play the contestant, no "Ringers" will be allowed. Any account found with someone besides the registered and original owner of the account playing that character is breaking the EULA as set forth by Verant Interactive.

There is no entry fee for this event, and the only prizes will be titles awarded to the winners. Title for winning at the server level will be the level 51 title for Paladins "the Cavalier" The title for the 3 runners up will be the level 56 title "the Knight". The grand champion shall have his level 60 title given to him on his own server "the Crusader".

Both contestants once in position will be removed of all buffs, once debuffed, they shall be asked if they are ready for the match to start. Upon confirmation, an explosion and a verbal command will start the start the match.

Both contestants shall stay on the tiled area at all times. Standing on or behind the firepots is disallowed. Standing on or in the throne is disallowed.

All Armor and Weapons available to the contestant's class will be allowed. Right click-able effects have been disallowed in this event.

Any use of exploits or bugs will result in immediate disqualification from the event, and is a break in the rules of conduct as set forth on www.everquest.com


Rallos Zek Specific Rule: There shall be no looting of any item at an event, this includes spectators. Anyone found player killing for profit at an event will have action taken against his or her account.

Tallon Zek and Vallon Zek Rule: You may request to have your faction restored to the condition it was in at the last available save. This is to ensure that no unnecessary faction hits are taken at an event.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Test Server Finals


Those champions who go to Test are bound by additional rules: The character used for the event cannot adventure on Test for any reason. Handing off even the smallest items to a player on Test is considered duplication, resulting in a warning against the account, and loss of that item on your home server. Those found extremely abusing this rule could have further actions taken against the account at the discretion of the EQ development team.

The transfer of characters to Test is midnight (give or take an hour) PST the night before the main event.

Lake Rathetear will be shut down several hours before the main event. The only characters allowed in the Arena on Test for the main event will be the champions of server level event, the judges of the event and the event staff, other members of Verant Interactive, and members of the press who signed up with Verant Interactive Event Staff at least three days in advance via email.

All other characters on test found in the Arena during the main event will be summoned out of the zone and bound so they cannot return. If a second character is found to enter the Arena for any reason, it could be considered disruption and subject to action taken against the account.



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Druzzil Ro Guild Event For All
EverQuest_Server_Specific [ 9:07 AM EST ] - Ogamok

The Ordo Equitas Umbra is pleased to announce its first official server-wide
faire on Druzzil Ro. The Genesis Faire will be held on March 3rd at 1pm CST at Kelethin in the Greater Faydark area. Genesis Faire will be comprised of a number of events aimed towards low-level characters -ages 1 to 15- including an in-game trivia contest and a scavenger hunt. Prizes will vary from platinum rewards to valuable items and equipment. We will also be sponsoring a trade far during the events, so bring your wares as well as come peruse ours.

In addition, guildmembers will be providing assistance as needed to fledgling warriors and casters during the faire. We will also be accepting applicants for the guild throughout the day. Anyone interested in this event or the guild in general, may view out website at http://oeuguild.virtualave.net or may contact the High Counsel at seain@oeu.i-p.com. So, please, tell your young guild friends and allies to venture to the Genesis Faire and enjoy the hospitality and fellowship of the Ordo Equitas Umbra.
