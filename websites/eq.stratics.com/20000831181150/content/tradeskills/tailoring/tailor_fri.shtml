Raw Silk Armor

 

Necessary Items:
A large (not small) sewing kit
Pattern Dependant, 1 - 2 Silk Swatches
To make silk swatches:
Combine 2 Spider Silks (not Spiderling Silks)

Raw-Silk Armour:
Items that take 1 Silk Swatch + the appropriate pattern:
Raw-Silk Mask - AC: 2
Raw-Silk Headband - AC: 3
Raw-Silk Collar - AC: 2
Raw-Silk Mantle - AC: 2
Raw-Silk Cloak - AC: 3
Raw-Silk Sleeves - AC: 3
Raw-Silk Gloves - AC: 3
Raw-Silk Sandals - AC: 3
Raw-Silk Sash - AC: 2

Items that take 2 Silk Swatches + the appropriate pattern:
Raw-Silk Robe - AC: 6 (using the tunic pattern)
Raw-Silk Leggings - AC: 4

All the Raw-Silk items weigh: 0.4
CLASS : MNK NEC WIZ MAG ENC
RACE : ALL

Cured Silk Armour:
(in addition to the above 'recipies, add)
Heady Kiola, in varying amounts

To make Heady Kiola:
2 Kiola Saps
1 Water Flask (not summoned/foraged water)
1 Bottle
OR
Consult your local brewer

Cured Silk Mask - AC: 2
(1 Silk Swatch + 1 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Headband - AC:
Cured Silk Collar - AC:
(1 Silk Swatch + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Mantle - AC: 3
(2 Silk Swatches + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Cloak - AC: 4
(2 Silk Swatches + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Sleeves - AC: 4
(2 Silk Swatches+ 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Gloves - AC: 4
(2 Silk Swatches + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Sandals - AC: 4
(1 Silk Swatches + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Sash - AC:
Cured Silk Tunic - AC: 8
(3 Silk Swatches + 2 Heady Kiola + Pattern)
Cured Silk Leggings - AC: 5
(2 Silk Swatches + 3 Heady Kiola + Pattern)

All the Cured Silk items weigh: 0.1
CLASS: MNK (Overall, the Cured Silk items are 1 AC higher than Raw Silk, but is class restricted)

*Please note, when combining these items, make sure that they are not stacked, but each put into the kit as a seperate item.

Whereas making silk swatches easily becomes trivial around a skill level of 15, the Raw Silk seems to be the interim segue between Patchwork Armour and Studded/Reinforced Armour, and Cured Silk is on par with Mastery levels. Enchanted Silk Armour is yet a rumour.

Silk Armour - Speciality Items:

Wu's Fighting Gauntlets
(1 Silk Swatch + 4 Heady Kiola + Pattern + Greater Lightstone + Vial of Viscous Mana)

    To make Viscous Mana:
    1 Pearl + 1 Poison Vial (from a Master Potter) + LV12+ Enchanter with the spell 'Thicken Mana'

Shadow Silk
(1 Silk Swatch + Shadow Wolf Pelt + Spell Scroll 'Gather Shadows')

Inky Shadow Silk
(1 Silk Swatch + Shadow Wolf Pelt + 2 Spell Scroll 'Gather Shadows')
Add extra Shadow Wolf Pelt for larger sizes
