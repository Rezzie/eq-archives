If you are in the 30s or up, you should be fine in this dungeon. In that case you can do what I do - jump down the well on the first level so that you don't need any keys. This puts you right near where the troll SK spawns. From where you come out, you just exit your little cul de sac and go left to the end. When you are ready to leave, just beat on a monster, being careful not to kill it, and then follow it out - it will open doors as it goes. If you are a finger waggler type with eye of Zomm, that works on locked doors too. Also an enchanter can charm a pet and have it walk through the door.

If you need keys, then you have to beg, but, hunt for them off of the easier SKs. Once you are ready for the final one, you go down the stairs where the dry bones are, through the door to where the elf skel is, and it is a secret door on the right wall - stay to the right after entering and you will be in the roundish room where the troll SK spawns.
