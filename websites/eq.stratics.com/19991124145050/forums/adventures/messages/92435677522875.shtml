Getting to Faydwer is quite fun actually. You'll need to go to Freeport to accomplish this though. As you leave the Kithicor forest and enter the commonlands, head east keeping close to the edge of the mountains at all times and watch for guards. Next you'll zone into the west Freeport zone. Head towards the west gate but skirt around it a good distance from the guards and check the wall east of the west gate for a secret passage that leads through the Freeport sewers to the docks- just run along the wall at an angle until you go through the wall. There is a guard that patrols the docks sometimes so watch for him and when he is out of site get onto the dock and go to the far end where the ferry comes and wait for it. When you get on the ferry go below deck and hide as on one of the stops to Butcherblock there is a guard standing on the dock waiting to kill you. When you are nearing the third stop which is butcherblock jump into the water and swim to shore outside of the walls which surround the dock. There are two guards at the entrance to the docks- they stand at the arch which allows access to the docks. From then on I'd keep close to the mountain walls as there are guards everywhere in Butcherblock. Good luck.
