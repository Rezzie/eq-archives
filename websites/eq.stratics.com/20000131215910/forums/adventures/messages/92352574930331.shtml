Well Befallen is west of freeport which is on the complete other side of Qeynos, you have to go to the west karanas, you then follow the water line, i dont know if it is south or north my snese heading isnt that good, but if you enter the karanas from the qeynos hills and take a right when you hit the water you will go left, you follow the shoreline until you get the the second bridge. Cross the bridge and go straight and follow the path, you'll come to a wide open space surrounded by tall mountains and that will be the beholders maze or high pass hold, i'm not sure which it is called, but you take your frist right after entering that zone and then go straight until you see a cave, that is a goblin dungeon, you stay to the left in there and zone out in no time, then you have to take a right and go through rivervale and then throught the kithicor woods, and then you want to stay along the far right wall and you will zone, befallen is a cave entrance on that wall you should see it, it is accross from some houses, a lot of hill giants are in the area, but if you stay on the wall they wont bother you, if you stay on the wall for two more zones you'll get to west freeport, you can get bound there fairly quickly, thats usually the best place to bind if you not at a high enough level to do it yourself, but if your not level 15 or higher i wouldn't even go to befallen, i'm 11 now and it was BAD!! well as you can see this isn't an easy trip either so good luck!
