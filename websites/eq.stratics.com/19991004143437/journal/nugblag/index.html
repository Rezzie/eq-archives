The Adventures of Nugjar and Blaga (One Troll, One Ogre, No Clothing)

Day One (The Journy Begins)
Journal Visions
 
Blaga n Nugjar
 
Orc Meatpuppet
 
Arm-raisin' Fun
 
Helo der, me called Blaga n me buddy der is Nugjar (I be da gud loking one on da right).

Dis is de shurt stoory bout when I an Nug make r way to da Daerk Elf town to get awey frum Nug's muther. She always tell Nug that he no polish his club or he go blind! Nug like to polish his club aloot so it best for him we leave da swamp and see da wurld.

(luk at picture above dis on top) Here we be saying buy buy to de swamp. Shortly after dis picture wuz taken de flash make orcs behind us testy an dey ripped me head off fer an beer mug, den dey use a branch of that big tree for da desert delicacy, Nug on a stick.

After getting out o da swamp again, we return da favor by treatin dis orc to a past time we like tu cal:

"Da Happy Orc Meat Puppit Schew"

Dis is where Nug uses a orc as a Puppit n I pertend lik he tells me how fun it is to shave halfling women.

His mouth don't muve so gud but you tust me... it fuuny!

When we done Nug pulls out his arm an holds it like dat til we find gud clean river.

Whut we no think of is dat in da desert der aint no water, n so Nug had to hold arm like dis for a gud too days.

On de gud side no orc boderd us for long time after dis.

On de bad side Nug no polish his club for too days neither (he can get vury angry if he not polish fur dat long.

 top


Day 2 (Hitching a Ride)
Journal Visions
 
In da Desert
 
Here wee see me n Nug n da midel ofe da desert luking for da nearest camel ride. We try to hail a carevan but wit da flies dat begun too gather on Nugs arm no one want to stop fer us.

Note too meself: Nug no longer alowed ti plat wit puppits beefor long trips no mor.

 top


Day 3 (After da wash down)
Journal Visions
 
Uhh... Lost
 
Me want to ask derectiuns but Nug tink his sense o derection wurk beeter. Dat is how we end up in dis place. Me go winder shoppin fer some leather pants (der are sooo maeny here dat dey are much cheeper den in da swamp). But when me turn round me loose Nug!

Me no see Nug for long time, den I find out dat fer 20 gould he culd get his club waxed by nice lady.

Funny, me no tink ladiys have adams apple...

 top


Da Wumen of Nug n Blagas Life
Nug n me get sad sumtime tu tink of all dat we leave behind so we bring picture uf dose girls dat we proud to call our own. My big Ogre wuman is a big head trip but she give gud luvin (she cost me aloot to keep thouu, she eat more raw meet and I can bring her). Nug found a gud girl who is just his type, a big girl wif good teeth an boy can she polish a club!
 top
