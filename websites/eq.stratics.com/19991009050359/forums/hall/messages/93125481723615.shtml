'Sick and tired' still plagues this server. When you die for any reason at all you come back with the 'sick and tired' effect on you. This means you have about 25% health and mana and your stats are severely nerfed for about a minute or so. I reported this earlier and Brad said it is a BUG and you are only supposed to have this effect when you are resurected or if you are killed in PvP combat. Well, we have had 2-3 patches since then and we still have sick and tired.

New thing that has been added recently. I dont know if it exists on the rest of the servers so I will list it anyway. When you target something, its name flashes. This is wonderfull. Now I know for SURE which creature I am attacking before it either dies or a spell effect goes off.

Thats really about it for the noticable changes to the test server lately.
