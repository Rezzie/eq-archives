Mari on Twisting

 Introduction   First things first   What next?   Putting this to use   Conclusion 

Introduction
 Hail fair traveler!
If you wish, I shall pass on the wisdom I gained from my Teacher, the bard Ricola, who most graciously took this young wood elf and taught her what being a bard means.
 top

First things first
 Take you the hot button pad. Choose a number to which you can switch easily. You have, surely, already equipped one such pad with basic socials and skills, like forage, should you be a wood elf, and sense heading, and consider, and walk/run, and sit, and such.
On this new hot button pad, place you the songs you wish to sing. Remember you the numbers upon which you place each song, as when you are singing one of them, the others do not show up. This effect is just like the effect in transparency when the bar with your songs is along the lefthand side of the screen.
Take you another hot button pad. In this one, equip your inventory/equipment slots 5,6,7 and 8. Also equip your melee boxes.
 top

What next?
 When you are questing, store you your instruments in equipment slots in these four boxes, along with any spare weapon you alternate with your main weapon. (You should alternate 1H slashing and Piercing regularly to keep both skills ready to use against evil mobs.) You do not want to store loot in these boxes, for you want to be able to equip strings for heal or drum for Selo's most rapidly in emergency situations! Get you a back pack so soon as ever you can to store loot and food; in fact, get TWO backpacks.
When you enter melee, switch you to the hotbuttons with your songs. While you are slashing or piercing the evil mob, you should be "twisting" several songs.
For example, you could twist your healing song with Discord (BE CAREFUL NEVER TO SING DISCORD IN CIVILIZED REGIONS!! You will be KOS by the first NPC you approach. Marithea remembers with shame her mistake in her very own guild hall!) Before you get Hymn of Restoration, you can twist Chant and Discord. Afterward, you can even twist all three.
 top

Putting this to use
 Now, how twist you songs? You need to be alert. When you sing, as you must already know, you see on the top right an icon that shows what song you are singing, and you also see in blue in your text box what the song is doing to you (or, with Discord, to the mob). The next part requires that you give much attention, because you also may be exchanging essential information with group mates.
As SOON as you see that one song has taken effect (as when you read "bat winces"), you hit the hot button for the other song TWICE. First hit cancels the first song. Second hit starts the second. As soon as THAT song takes effect, double hit the first hot button, and over and over until you and your group mates have slain all the evil mobs.
Remember always to put Selo's Accelerando in your hotbutton pad. You will find that Selo's gives you more life-saving than even Restoration. So soon as you see your health reaching the two bubble level, you must run for the nearest guard, so fast as ever you can. Selo's, like Chant and later songs, affects your group mates as well as yourself, so you can save the whole party this way!
 top

Conclusion
 I have learned to twist Anthem, healing and Bellow; last evening, my Elder Brother Leithreas the Ranger and I took out many dwarven bandits in ButcherBlock who were attacking us in groups with simple melee skills combined with skillful use of songs and spells. Never could either of us have done so alone! And never could I have healed us without being able to equip mandolin and then again dirk and shield with the hot button box.
May you fare well in your questing, young bard!
Marithea (newly level 14!) hopes that her song of twisting has enlightened you with understanding!
