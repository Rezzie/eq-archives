Well there definately should be a noticeable difference between running and walking. Do you run as fast as everyone else when your say following teamates to a new location. One thing to check is if your crouching. Crouch and uncrouch is with the "d" key and accidently hitting it slows you to a crawl. Remember getting hurt slows you down alot thats why its best to run early if a battle doesnt seem to be going your way. If you wait youll be hurt and cant run very fast, then youll get hit alot from behind, stunned, knocked unconcious and usually killed. If your agility suddenly goes down that means your hurt and will be running slower.

Another thing is that jumping moves you faster so jumping a couple times when makeing a break for it can really help put some distance between you and the monster. Just watch your stamina jumping eats up alot. Take it from me when it comes to fleeing combat im usually leading the pack to the next zone.
