There was someone here who said they had a dup of their body, dunno if its true or not, but I'll cater to the idea.

If you had just the right set of events happen, this could be possible.

First off, there's that Save every few minutes of your character. This stores your info on the server, so that if it crashes, you're put back to that spot with that amount of life, and that amount of exp, etc.

Now, This would be one of the only situations where I could see a body getting duplicated :: Server's going down, IE Crash or something else, and there's a Save of all IN-Game objects.

Thus if your character had just been saved, and you got yourself killed, then right when you're re-spawning the servers go down, what happens?

Well, the Servers boot you for one, so you'll go back to your last save with full Health and HP (Had this happen once) and your corpse will be saved (If you're lucky).

In that situation, when the servers came back up, youd have all your equip and a corpse to boot.

NOW, the hard part would be the Psychic ability you'd have to posess to know they were taking the servers down on an emergency basis. That or you'd have to have HELLA timing.

The way it could be possible would be get the save, then go get yourself killed RIGHT as the servers are comming down. This way you'd be in "Limbo" when the servers came down, and put right back at your save point. VERY difficult, but you'd end up with a dup of all your stuff.

*grins at the idea of 2 Exe Axes*

Just rambling about it.
