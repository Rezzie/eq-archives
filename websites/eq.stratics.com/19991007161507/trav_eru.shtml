Traveler's Guide to Erudin

 Overview   Prominent Places 

Overview
Erudin is the home city of the Erudites. These people excel at the use of magic. Their lack of strength is offset with intelligence. The teleporters to the Tuxxolia Forest are found down the stairs by Blue Hawks and the Vasty Deep Inn.
 top


Prominent Places

Erudin City

Temple of Divine Light
This is home to the cleric guild in Erudin. Inside you will find Leraena Shelakyrak, Lumi Stergnon, Depnar Bulrious, Rem Knarjon, Ayth Niphiria, and Stella Breezemoon. Rem and Stella are weapons vendors. There is a balcony outside the Temple. There you will find: Cipse Tospyr, Cleric Spells 4 (sells level 4 and 5 cleric spells), Cleric Spells 1 (sells level 1 Cleric spells), Reklon Gnallen, and Rarnan Lapice. Just outside the Temple Jras Solsier can be found.

City Armory

For the Paladin, the armory deals in steel and chain armor. Inside you will find Jagin Kindler and Dunare Steelfinger.

Fountain Area

This is an extension to the Temple. Here you will find: Cleric Spells 2 (sells level 2 Cleric spells), Vynon Estaliun, Galbass Weaver, Demicia Tanner, and Cleric Spells 3 (sells level 3 Cleric Spells). The teleporters to the palace and docks areas are found here.

Vendor

This little shop deals in storage devices from pouches to large chests. Reihan Wilhemson is your contact. There are rooms upstairs which are empty. Just outside this shop you will find Monita Weaver.

Erudin Surplus

This store deals in provisions of all kinds. Borge and Terla Finleather are the proprietors.

City Library

You will find Illith Nollith and Onissa Glarn inside. Onisa sells a variety of spells. There are staircases which lead up to the third floor where you will find Sinnkin Highbrow.

Deepwater Knights

This is the home of the Paladin guild. Inside you will find Laoni Reista, Ghemna Nyjuss, Paladin Spells (sells all Paladin spells), Weligon Steelherder, and Fhania Aserdon. Ghemna amd Fhania are weapons dealers. Upstairs you will find Oleria Mausrel, Mikeana Tolstaub, Gans Paust, and Breya Nostulia. The side door on the first level leads to a courtyard. Here you will find Zure Bavuior and Tiam Khonsir.

Blue Hawks

Geoard and Helia are the proprietors of the local tavern. Geoard sells spirits while Helia sells food.

The Vasty Deep Inn

Weary travellers will find accomodations here. It is a friendly inn where Luren Renthalis sells pouches. The comfortable rooms are found on the second floor.

Erudin Docks

Dock Building
Here you will find the teleporter back to the city proper. Inside the building you will also find the local priest of discord.

Vendors

At the inside end of the docks you will find Beth Breadmakers peddling food and weapons. You will also find Nerth Sailwind selling pouches of all kinds. There are stairs which lead down to a beach where the fishing is rumored to be fantastic. But then we must account for the fisher tales.

Erudin Port Authority

The trade in and out of Erudin from the other continents is inspected here. Inside you will find Reko Saltamer and Kisrin Breezeshadow, both of whom deal in pouches.

Barge

Here you will find transport. The barge will take you to a masted ship for a pleasant sail to Kerra Isle.

Erudin Palace

The Second Level of the Palace
The second level of the palace is where you will find the teleporters to and from the city. Down the stairs you will find some shops.

Vials of Vitality

This is the jeweler of Erudin. Here you will find Myrein Denven to outfit you with rings and amulets. Or perhaps help you on your way to become a jewler yourself. You will also find Elbsin and Slansin Denven in the shop.

Sothure's Fine Gems

If you want to find that special something for a gift, the best gems of the land can be found for sale here by Anite Gemcutter.

Erudin City Office

No respectable city can be efficiently run without the local government. Here you will find Tan and Judge Monosty. Judge also sells pouches on the side.

Bank of Erudin

All your banking needs can be handled here by Agryn Moonfield, Sahana Heartlyn or Wick Ashfield.

The Third Level of the Palace

The third level of the palace is where you will find the entrances to the guilds of magic. In the courtyard of this level you will find Raskena Djor and Toresian Phadel. The magic users aren't very friendly to those who are not members of their guild.

Craft Keepers

This is the enchanter guild entrance. The stairs lead you up to a shop area. Here you will find Nolusia Finhain, Nikina Sparlek, Enchanter Spells 1 (sells level 1 and 2 spells), Enchanter Spells 2 (sells level 3 and 4 spells) and Enchanter Spells 3 (sells level 5 and 6 spells). Continuing up the stairs you will find Gwynorna Armista. Finally the stairs lead to the top floor where you will find Lanken Rjarn, Jeliala Glimmercharm, and Mystin Shalan.

Gate Callers

This is the magician guild entrance. The stairs lead you up to a shop area. Here you will find Dellas Cekus, Mage Spells 1 (sells level 1 and 2 spells), Mage Spells 2 (sells level 3 and 4 spells), and Mage Spells 3 (sells level 5 and 6 spells). Continuing up the stairs you will find Warban Ranflash, who sells armor, weapons, and gems. Finally the stairs lead to the top floor where you will find Vasile Jahnir, Frena Runeflash, Markus Jaevins, and Reba Garus. Frena sells gems.

Crimson Hands

This is the wizard guild entrance. The stairs lead you up to a shop area. Here you will find Jesper Kenshed, Wizard Spells 1 (sells level 1 and 2 spells), Wizard Spells 2 (sells level 3 and 4 spells), and Wizard Spells 3 (sells level 5 and 6 spells). Continuing up the stairs you will find Andane Starinen. Finally the stairs lead to the top floor where you will find Ghanlin Skyphire and Tika Shockstep. Tika sells gems.
