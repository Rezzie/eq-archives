Printer Friendly Zone Map of Norrath, by Grant Kushida
This is a map of the zones in Antonica, which I created because none of the other zone maps fit my criteria:
  • Printable - single page, no colors, backgrounds or images
  • Indicates non-caster bind points
  • Shows approximate dungeon levels (<15, 15-25, 25+)
  • Shows towns
  • Shows size of zone connections (small tunnels or entire zone borders)
  • Shows location of zone connections - all shown connections should all be on the proper side of the zone
It's not the prettiest map out there; it's intended to be printed, scribbled on and used as a reference while in-game. It was created in Visio and exported to GIF - I'd love to provide it in Adobe Acrobat format but I don't have the full version of Acrobat.

Please let me know at gkushida@kickasscomputer.com if there are any cartographical errors or other problems with this zone map. And of course, thanks to all the map sites out there that do great work, without whom this map would not exist.
