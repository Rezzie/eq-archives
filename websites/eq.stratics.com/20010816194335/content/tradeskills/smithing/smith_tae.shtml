Taerdal SHOW YOU HOW

 

OK, first off, you have nothing on How to Smith as a Troll or Ogre, so now Taerdal SHOW YOU HOW =):

Taerdal started da smithing at lvl 0 with int of 84 and wis of 60. I put 1 point into smithing, yup 1 point. den I buy Small bits of ore from vender outside MY SK guild in Grobb, yup MY SK guild, it belong to ME. anyway, I buy LOTS n LOTS of bits of Ore, 2 bags full, with 2 bags stocked full of Ore bits I had uhh.... hmm 2 times da 8 is..... ummm.... LOTSA ore bits!!!

den I buy 1 bag oh water, cause da handbook to da smithing said 2 ore bits per 1 water (I had trouble reading that, took a good hour to read sentnce, but me is da Smarterest troll cause me is 84int) so den I venture to da Smelly Ogre city of Oggok and I go to da Forge dat deys have. Dem stupid Ogres put da forge in da building of da Armor sellers, stupid Ogres, When I accidentily drop a flaming ore bit on da Rug, a bige fire starteted, and me gots in trouble....

but me run fast. So after cleering up dat insident I went back to smithing, I made da bits till 21 skill. dat was fun! I gots lots n lots of Metal Bits, but me sold em cause me stupid, YOU NO sell mettle bits, deys be inva... invalua... deys be realy importamt for da later practicises. Den me be sad cause dem stinky Ogres no sell no molds before da lanter molds, dumb Ogres. So I gotteted my self to Nerriak, it nice and shiny there. and I bought da steel boning molds from da stinky Ogre vendor people in da Foreign Quarter. I bought Steel Boning molds cause dey was da cheapest make.

So den I haul my encumberded but to Nerriak Commons, cause there is where da forge is, neer da Nerriak Underground building. and I makeded da Steel boning with a file. Oh yea!! afores ya can make steel boning ya gots ta make a file, but dem stinky ogres sell file molds and dey is trivial after yas maked all dem Mettle bits. so I made da Steel Boning. I forgots how to do it at da moment but da Smithing book tell you how.

So den I gots my skill to 41 and da bonings became trivial. So den I decided to make Lanterns. yas can also get da lantern molds from dem Ugly Ogre vendors in Nerriak Foreign Quarter, and dats where I am now. With a Skill of 56, hunting in Befallen to make some cash to further my career as da BlackSmith type person. AND DATS HOW WE EVIL n' LARGE RACES SMITH!!!
