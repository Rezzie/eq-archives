Quest for the Bloodforge Hammer
Fiction by Lamoorn Bugbasher

One day, while slaying Giant Snakes in Black Burrow, my friend Tygron the Magician said he helped a man kill the dwarf Trumpy Irontoe in Qeynos. I asked him "Why did he want to kill Trumpy Irontoe?", and he said "If you turn in his head to Byzar Bloodforge, he will give you the Bloodforge Hammer, worth up to 100 platinum." Impressed by the price I ran off to kill Trumpy Irontoe for myself. I killed Trumpy Irontoe myself and took his head, and ran from Bruno Barstomper and a few of the Corrupt guards. After outrunning the guards with Trumpy Irontoe's head in my hand, I slowly crept into Sneeds for food, fearing he would kill me, but he suprised me with a discount on all products. After stocking up on products, I started my quest along with my friend Kaeden the bard. When we got to North Karana, I casted Invisibility on us as well as See invisible so we could see each other. On our way, my friend scolded me for leading him inbetween a Giant. In the valley, we were running up when we appeard and a Mountain Cat was stroling by. He stopped, and we feared for our lives, and he sat down, licked his foot, and walked on. We sighed in releif that we didn't die, and than we herd a hungry cat, the same one. My friend repeatedly drummed on his drum to make us run faster. And we ran all the way to Highpass. We were strolling along when a Gnoll jumped us, we managed to kill it and we ran. Kaeden stayed at the inn while i traveld on. On my way, I got lost and ran into a Gnoll Soothsayer. I had a near death experiance with one of those, and ran like crazy hoping I would run out of range so he can't cast root on me, but he did. I turned around to see him charging at me, so I casted Root on him. We stood there, staring, when suddenly a Barbarian charged and killed the gnoll. I thanked him and asked him if he could guide me to the path to Freeport, and he did. I traveled to Freeport and waited for the boat, waited and waited and waited. The boat finaly came and I boarded. I arrived in Kaladim and gave the head to Byzar Bloodforge, he was happy to see Trumpet's (my nickname for Trumpy) head and gave me a Bloodforge Hammer. I gated back to Qeynos and auctioned it off, with the best offer of 22 pp. Extreamly disapointed, I ran to black burrow and killed all of the snakes.
