EverQuest Stratics Lead Editor's Letter

EQ Stratics Visitors,

I would like to take this opportunity introduce myself to those of you who have recently joined our site in the past few months that I havent had the chance of meeting while I was away. I am Onnie and once again EverQuest Stratics Lead Editor.

My involvement with EverQuest began before the game was ever ready for players to beta test it. Once the game went commercial and Stratics started hosting the forums we have now, I became a regular visitor of the site. It was only within this year that Jhariden and Greebo managed to talk me into moderating the Westwood Tavern. After becoming a staff member a call went out for someone to take over the Editorial Team. Having far too many things on my plate, I half jokingly offered to take the position.

At the time Stratics did not have an Editorial Team. My first priority was trying to rebuild the Editorial Team and crawling through all the current documents for the site to find those in need of updating. Shortly after I accepted the position I had a family crisis occur in my life that left me unable to fulfill my duties. In short, I emailed Greebo I needed to step down. While I was gone my previous position was not filled and I have once again accepted the role of Lead Editor for EverQuest Stratics.

I wish all of Stratics participants to know what you can expect from the team and myself. It is our goal to bring our guests the highest quality material regarding a game you enjoying playing. In addition, EverQuest Stratics hopes to bring you entertainment and information while visiting our site when you cannot actually be in EverQuest. EverQuest is a game that constantly changes and previous information can obviously become prehistoric in a short amount of time. My first priority since my four month absence will be to re-examine the sites documents and make note of items that are either incomplete or in need of updating. The Editorial Team is currently compiling a list of items to present to our readers that we feel are a priority for you. We hope you, our visitors, will share with others your knowledge and gaming experience through a guide or article.

Also, I would like to invite you to email me regarding any documents on the site either new or old at eqeditors@stratics.com . The Team would love to hear from you should you wish to say thank you, want to see various other types of written work from interactive to static, or you have criticism with something the site has newly published. I would like to recommend that in addition to emailing the Editorial Team you bring any issue(s) you might have regarding an article or articles to either the Westwood Tavern or our various specific boards. This enables all our visitors to enjoy the debate or your point of view on a given topic. However, I wish to stress that you are certainly more than welcome to send your comments, concerns, or issues privately without posting to either the Editorial Team, eqeditors@stratics.com, or myself at Onnie@stratics.com .

Finally, I would like to say it is good to be back and active again on Stratics. Interacting with all of you always made the EverQuest experience for me be it styles of game play issues to the latest Verant decision. The ability to communicate and share your enjoyments, role-playing or problems about the games world is what makes EverQuest so enjoyable from this end of the keyboard. If I had to distill all my time spent in EverQuest to its essence (be it from the articles, debates, or drivel), I look at all of the people EverQuest brought me into contact with and can only say, It truly has been my pleasure.

Onnie, Lead Editor
