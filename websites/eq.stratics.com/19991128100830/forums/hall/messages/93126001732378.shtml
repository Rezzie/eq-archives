Im just thouroughly impressed with AC. I saw some SERIOUS crap in it.

We walked into a Dungeon the other day, called Crater Cave. (Whoo hoo, NDA LIfted, I can talk about it!)

Well, My friend's character is a Blademaster. He's one of the higher level players in game, Level 18. (Highest is mid 20's right now) This is cause there was a P-Wipe 2 weeks ago. He's currently a Duke, having Vassals, who have Vassals, who have Vassals.

Anyhow, he walks into this dungeon, and what greets him? Granite Golems. These things are HUGE! Inspecting these huge beasts, He finds them to be level 33! So, delving deeper, hoping to find something easier to kill, He comes upon Copper Golems! They are level 43! Taking a mis-step, he comes too close to one of the Golems, which kills him in one hit. DOH! Respawning to the Life-Stone outside (These are placed in towns and distant places, where you "bind" by using the stone. Looks like a bigass Crystal) He delved back into the cave to retrieve half his gold and his least expensive item. (When you die, you come back with all your equipment on you, minus one half your gold, and your least expensive EQUIPPED item. Thus those are Lootable by the populace) Now, he is more careful, and delves even DEEPER into the dungeon. Determined to find some treasure chests, or item spawns (Yes, they have Random Spawns of items in random places, as well as Chests which respawn items in dungeons) He braves the next level. At this point, he can narely see anything it is so dank and dark. Lo, on his radar, he see's a monster, clicking on the blip, he find it is a Lava Golem! This bugger is Level 104!!


All-In-All, he didn't find any treasure, but had a fun time exploring. This game is different in that, if you enter a high-level dungeon, you dont Die Instantly.

Plus, Spellcasting is real fun. One of the ways to find better spells is to research by combining random Components. I've seen ppl Blow their Arses up and its sooo funny! Hehe.
