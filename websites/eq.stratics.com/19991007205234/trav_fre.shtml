Traveler's Guide to Freeport

 West Freeport   North Freeport   East Freeport   Under Freeport   How to Get From Here to There 

West Freeport
We begin our tour just outside Freeport's West Gate. Why? Because, if Freeport is your home town, you will be re-entering Freeport through that gate time and time again, because the Freeport resurrection spot is located just outside the gate. This area is a great area for low level characters to work on their fighting skills and gain experience. Among the monsters found here are Large Rats, Stonesnakes, Deathfist Pawns, Fire Beetles, and, at night, Bats. The occasion Orc Apprentice and Orc Centurion can be found here as well.

As you enter the West Gate, the first building you will see is the headquarters of the Freeport Militia. Right now there does not appear to be anything interesting inside the building.

For the rest of the tour we will, unless otherwise indicated, follow the right hand rule. We will stick to the right wall and always make right turns when they are available. Proceeding along, the next building is the Smithson's Smithy where you can buy high quality weapons. Across from the Smithy is the Hogcaller's Inn where you can buy alcoholic products.

Continuing along to right we come to the Arena. The WARRIOR'S GUILD is located in the building across from the Arena. There is a jail, of sorts, located under and in back of the Arena. Strangely, the jail contains only one prisoner whose cell is not locked. However, instead of escaping he just stands there begging for copper.

Moving along following the right wall, we come to a passageway to East Freeport. However, as we are not yet finished with our tour of West Freeport, we will skip that passageway for now and continue back the way we came along the right wall.

This will take us to the Theatre of the Tranquil, better known as the MONK'S GUILD. Aside from the various Monk trainers to be found here, there are also merchants selling a variety of items including lockpicks, backpacks, and musical instruments. Beyond the Theatre is another passageway to East Freeport which we will again ignore for now.

Continuing along the right wall we come to Torlig's Herbs & Medicines. Here you can find a variety of grossly overpriced magic items and potions including, a chipped bone rod for the ridiculous price of 5419 platinum pieces. Supposedly this item is useful in locating corpses.

Next we come to the Academy of Arcane Science which includes the WIZARD'S, MAGICIAN'S, and ENCHANTER'S GUILDS. The merchants here sell a variety of magical scrolls, arcane books, and gems. The floating headquarters can be entered by stepping on the teleporter pad. It is exited the same way.

Finally, continuing along to the right we come to a passageway to North Freeport. As we have finished our tour of West Freeport, it is time to take this passage.

North Freeport

We arrive in North Freeport following a passage way that ends with us facing an unnamed blue building. This is a jewelry store. Inside, you can buy rings, jeweler's kits, metal bars, and related books. For now, however, we need not enter this store. Instead, we will proceed to the right.

The first opening is the entrance to the Freeport Market. This market contains a variety of shops as well as The Vault and the Freeport BANK. Taking the shops in order, there is an unnamed inn, an unnamed blue building, and an unnamed sewing shop wherein you can buy a complete set of cloth armor, as well as a small sewing kit, and instructions for its use. Next is The Vault. Beyond that is The Emporium, which also sells cloth armor and sewing supplies. Beyond that are Tassel's Tavern and The Jade Tiger's Den. The Den is not as exotic as it sounds. The remaining buildings in the market are currently empty.

Leaving the market, we continue until we come to the Freeport Coalition of Trades Folk, another source of miscellaneous goods including food, water, and fishing equipment.

Next up is the Hall of Truth, or the PALADIN'S GUILD. Naturally, weapons and Paladin spells are available here. A word of warning here, the leader of the guild may not have exactly the same name as given on your paladin beginning quest note.

Continuing along, we come to the Freeport Office of the People--yet another source of food and other basics. Returning the way we came, we continue along the right wall until we reach a small market place which contains the Freeport City Hall, Marsheart's Chords (the BARD'S GUILD), and Groflah's Forge. There are two exits from this area. One leads to West Freeport. We take the other. Straight ahead is a guard house. Turning left we follow the wall to the entrance to the Temple of Marr or the CLERIC'S GUILD. This concludes our tour of North Freeport.

East Freeport
Entering East Freeport from the passage way opposite the entrance to the Arena, the first building we see is Armor by Ikthar. Here you can buy lots of overpriced metal armor. Continuing along, the next shop is Trader's Holiday, a source of alcohol. Down the alley is an unnamed tavern. Skip the alley and continue past Felith & Bard's Imported Goods, yet another alcohol source. Continuing straight you come to the Leather & Hide, where, surprisingly, you can buy moderately overpriced leather armor.

We now turn right and go down the alley between the Leather and Hide and Felith & Bard's. After making a second right turn, we come to an opening in the wall which leads us to the slums of Freeport. The first shop in the slums is Hallard's Resales, a fine source of rusty weapons. Following along the wall to the right we come to an empty building and then an unnamed inn. The second door to the inn leads to the NECROMANCER'S and SHADOW KNIGHT'S GUILD. These guilds are located deep within the building, down several flights of stairs. It is worth noting that there are evil trainers here for Magicians, Enchanters, Wizards, and Clerics as well.

Returning to Hallard's shop, we can continue to a downward sloping path. This path, eventually, brings us out into a dirt covered plaza. The condemned building contains the NECROMANCER'S and SHADOW KNIGHT'S GUILD. Also located off this plaza are the Chops-N-Hops, a poor quality food shop and a weapon shop.

Now we must retrace out steps straight past the Leather & Hide until we turn right and come to a plaza with three additional exits. To the left is a passage way to West Freeport; Straight ahead is the port, and to the right is the South Gate which we will visit first.

There are two shops out this way. The first is the Grub & Grog Tavern. Their clientele appears to be somewhat exclusive. They serve guards, but not Paladins. Next is the Freeport Inn which is the first place to sell loot on the way into town and, in my experience, offers very good prices. The Freeport Inn sells the basics, including food and water.

Beyond the gate is an open field suitable for low level characters. Beyond that is the Northern Desert of Ro. Beware the Sand Giant.

Returning to the port, the first building is the Port Authority which sells fishing supplies and other ocean related goods. Exiting the Port Authority by its back door we arrive on the docks. This is a bad area of town, so be careful. You may be attacked for no good reason. To the left is a long pier at which the boat to other continent docks. Do not beg from the fisherwoman at the end of the pier. Straight ahead is the Seafarer's Roost. The tavern keeper here sells alcohol while his patrons have been know to kill strangers. Outside the Seafarer's Roost are stairs down. If we go through the door beyond those stairs we will find the ROGUE'S GUILD. This concludes our tour of Freeport's surface streets.

Under Freeport
Unlike the Qeynos Aqueduct System, the Freeport underground is not particularly dangerous. If anything it is particularly safe because it offers the evil races a way to reach their guilds without facing potentially hostile and deadly human guards.

There are at least three exterior entrances to the Freeport underground. One is an underwater entrance in East Freeport. To find either of the other two exits, face the city gate, then to the left, and follow the wall. The secret entrance is located just where the wall turns. The East Freeport secret entrance is more useful because it leads to the ROGUE'S, NECROMANCER'S GUILD, and SHADOW KNIGHT'S GUILDs. The West Freeport entrances appears to lead only to the Freeport Office of the People. However, I cannot claim that I have explored the entire underground.

How to Get From Here to There
West Gate to South Gate: Follow around the Freeport Militia Building taking the last left turn. Run between the smithy on your right and the Hogcaller's Inn on your left. Continue to your left and follow the sign to East Freeport. When you get to East Freeport follow the left wall past the shops and into the plaza. The South Gate is to your right.

West Gate to Bank: Enter the city and take the first right passage way to North Freeport. Once in North Freeport, follow the right wall passed a passage way to West Freeport. The next opening leads to the Freeport Market. The bank is straight ahead.

South Gate to West Gate: Come straight into the city and, at the plaza with four exits, take the passage way directly ahead to West Freeport. Upon arrival in West Freeport follow the passage way and continue straight into the area of the MONK'S GUILD. Continue through this area moving around to the right. You are now facing the entrance to The Arena. Do not enter the arena. Instead, take the second left and head straight to the Freeport Militia building. Turn right and follow the wall around. The gate is directly ahead and to the left.

South Gate to Bank: Come straight into the city and, at the plaza with four exits, take the passage way directly ahead to West Freeport. Upon arrival in West Freeport follow the passage way and continue straight into the area of the MONK'S GUILD. Continue through this area moving around to the right. You are now facing the entrance to The Arena. Do not enter The Arena. Instead, take the second left. Then take the first right. The passage way to North Freeport is ahead and to your right. Once in North Freeport take the first right turn and follow the right wall. The next opening leads to the Freeport Market. The bank is straight ahead.

Docks to South Gate: Go to the right through the Port Authority Building. Come out the other door of the building and continue the only way you can until you reach the plaza with four exits. Take the left exit out of the city.

South Gate to Docks: Head straight into the city. When you get to the plaza with four exits, the docks are to the right.

West Gate to Docks: Follow around the Freeport Militia Building taking the last left turn. Run between the smithy on your right and the Hogcaller's Inn on your left. Continue to your left and follow the sign to East Freeport. When you get to East Freeport follow the left wall past the shops and into the plaza. The docks are straight ahead.
