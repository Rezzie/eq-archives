First and foremost is STR. But note that even though you will hear rumors of high str at high levels mattering, I have not noticed a thing. I have three characters with three totally different STRs at 20th level.

Ogre 150 STR
Half Elf 100 STR
Dark Elf 70 STR

The biggest concern is armor weight, and also loot weight.

Damage? I have never, ever seen a difference of more then 1 to 3 pts. And I think it was more of a skill thing then STR.

Now at higher STRs I have noticed doing max damage more often. But doing max damage consistently is highly related to DEX also. Make sure DEX is at least 80, I have all my characters at 85 which seems to be a nice point.

Anything higher then 85 in DEX seems to make NO difference at all. But from 80 to 85 through 4 levels it made a good enough difference.

STA if you have it keep it, if you dont dont put any points in it. The whole point of a warrior in this game is to AVOID damage. All warriors from 80 STA to 150 STA will have similiar HPs till about 30th level. By then though youll have good armor, and be good enough 10 to 40hps wont really matter.

AGI Is a very important Stat. It seems from my experience the higher your AGI the more of a bonus you get from the AC of armor.

Example:

Banded Tunic AC 15:
Ogre with 80 AGI - 17
Half Elf with 100 AGI - 22
Dark Elf with 95 AGI - 21

So as you can see it can make a HUGE difference with a FULL suit of armor.

Always remember that not being hit is way better in this game then being able to take it.

I always on a personal level keep my str up cuz I love the metal armors. I always, and I mean always put at least 5 to 15 pts into agility. Although anything above 100 I have not seen a big enough difference to go any higher then this.

I hope this helps.
