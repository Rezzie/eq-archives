Your best bet is to do two things.

1. Visit the sites server listing page and mark down all the server IP addresses.

2. Ping to each of the servers. Use 3 pings and average them out.

3. Run a trace route to each of the servers and make note on how many Hops you go through to get there.

OK so that's 3.

Anyway you want a combo of the least amount of hops and lowest ping for best performance. Now this doesn't always work. On occasion I have had over 15 hops come out faster in ping then a 6 hope through a different provider. So remember Ping first Trace second.

Also for best enjoyment I would recommend the server with the least amount of people on it as well. But that's just my opinion.

There also may be a utility you can use to do this.. I know UO has one. I'm sure there is one for EQ. Perhaps some kind soul will let you know.
