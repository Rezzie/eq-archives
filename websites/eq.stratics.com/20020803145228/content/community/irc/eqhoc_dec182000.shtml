EverQuest House of Commons - Developer Chat with Abashi and the Team

Members of the Verant Staff Present were: Abashi, Absor, Hobart, Kayvman, Kendric, Mrylokar, Narris, Rowyl, Solist, and Tolan.

Special thanks go to Reijas, Moonshadow, Ogamok and Thundolfe for managing the questions throughout the session!

Here now is the transcript from the Second EQSS House of Commons!
 
-#eqhoc- WELCOME!
   
Greebo Thank you all for coming to the EverQuest House of Commons! This is, the second Stratics EverQuest House of Commons and so I would like to start off by extending a special thanks to Gordon "Abashi" Wrinn for his help in helping us get this set up.
Greebo I need to go over a few of the groundrules very quickly before we get things started, so please bear with me.
Greebo This is a moderated chat session. I will be moderating this session but I will not be doing much of the actual speaking. Public discussion during this session is not allowed.
Greebo Public chat during this session can go on in the #EverQuest channel.
Greebo We will be taking questions privately and relaying them publicly to the developers. We have a few question takers assigned who will be taking your questions and relaying them to the developers.
Greebo I know you all will have lots of questions - please only ask them your question once and only send one question taker your question.
Greebo Sending your question to all of our takers will not increase your chance of getting your question answered and spamming will result in you being removed from the channel, and possibly from the Stratics IRC network.
Greebo The Question Takers tonight are, in no order of preference, Ogamok, Thundolfe, Reijas, and Moonshadow - Say Hi Guys!
* Thundolfe is #1 and you know it
Ogamok Hola
Reijas Hiya all
Moonshadow Hi hi!
* Ogamok vetos Thundolfe's idea
Greebo Send one of them your question by typing: /msg Your Question
Greebo Where is the name of the Question taker. If you have any administrative type questions, please /msg me, but do not send your EverQuest Questions to me, as I will not be participating in the actual Q&A.
Greebo While we are happy to invite non Stratics web sites to reproduce transcripts of this House of Commons, the transcript of this Chat is copyrighted by Stratics, and anyone wishing to post a transcript of this chat must provide links back to http://eq.stratics.com both on the page where the transcript is posted as well as in any news announcements presented on the hosting site, or sent to other sites.
Greebo Now that we've got the basic rules out of the way, lets go ahead and get things started!
Greebo With us tonight are our special guests from Verant. At this time I ask them each to take a moment to introduce themselves...
Abashi Hello all. I'm Gordon Wrinn, Associate Producer of the EverQuest Live and Velious Teams. Welcome to the chat!
Kendric-VI Howdy folks. John Capozzi, Assoc. Game Designer, EQLive
Solist Ello, Bill Coyle, Assistant Producer of EQLive and Game Designer on Velious.
Absor HI all. I'm Alan VanCouvering, Customer Relations Manager for EverQuest.
Mrylokar Hihi Lawrence Poe, Game Designer EQ Live.
Kayvman Hello all, I am Kayvan Simantob, I am a fairly new addition to the dev team =)
Narris Hi! Tyler Sargent, Assoc. Game Designer EQ Live
Hobart Hiya, Hobart here. Assoc. Programmer on EQ Live
Absor Oh, heck... I'm the Community Relations Manager.... but who's keeping track anyhow...
Greebo Is that everyone?
Greebo OK then!
Greebo Ok then - while the Question Takers get busy organizing the questions coming in, how about we take a few minutes to talk about Velious in general...
Greebo First off - how has user response been? Would you say its been up to your expectations? Higher? Lower?
Solist From all I've seen they love it:) The response on the boards have been huge and the support has turned out to be even better than we expected
Abashi We're currently well over 100K registered velious users with sales in the 150K range. That's not bad for a week or two :)
Abashi Of course it's more than sales... It's also the comments we've received which as Bill said, have been more than encouraging.
Greebo Now that Velious is out - are there any that, prior to release, you could not say, that you feel you can share with us - say regarding the general theme of the new world or the mindset of the designers for what the players would be doing?
Kendric-VI Well, we wanted to break up the mold a bit with Velious, and I think we've done a good job of that
Abashi I think that people have noticed that we've done a few things with Velious that we hadn't tried before. As for the [secrets], which I think is what you are asking about, there's still a good number of them out there.
Greebo Where are the secrets?
Greebo Who's secrets/
Greebo Who's Secrets?
Abashi Rofl :)

Have you forgotten your scaley friends in Kunark? Is this quest really functioning
Hobart I had every intention to work out the problems with those quests. But along came this Velious thing. Now my specific duties have changed regarding bug fixing. We have apprentice game developers who are going through those broken quests and re-writing them.
Tolan I just wanted to step in here and say hi. Im the spell guy for Everquest in case you have any spell questions ;)
Hobart done - sorry
Ogamok *Zurren* is it intentional that Skyshrine armour quests, which require parts from Veeshans Temple, have the same results as the armour from Thudagrim and the plane of growth?
Narris The quests and the drops from those zones result in armor but they are slightly different from each other stat wise.
Narris Done

Thundolfe yay Rowyl
Abashi I think that it is important here to mention what our goals were with these quests: We wanted everyone, regardless of which path they choose, to have the option of questing for some of the nicer armor sets in game. Certainly some things will be restricted and will be out of reach if you choose to support the "wrong" faction, but some of them we want to be available regardless. Now, as Narris mentioned, there are indeed differences, but
Tolan Same reason the animals in PoG arent classified animals. Its another plane of existence. They look like plants but they arent. Yes, I know that means defoliation isnt useful there but there are alot of other good necro spells ;)
Abashi they aren't night and day nor is one set of armor meant to be a logical upgrade to the next.
Rowyl <-- playing with windows

Thundolfe (Azz) There has been some concern expressed on the Gameplay board over recent reduced effectiveness of wizard Lure spells...with tine for a long, reasoned response by Absor regarding enchanter illusions of all things, why is it nobody has looked at this question, even if only to say "we'll check it out?"<
Absor Any posts that I don't respond like that it's usually because I don't have an answer, or I missed the post. I'm still trying to get an answer that I understand for the Lure question. I, honestly, don't like to just respond with a, I read this....
Rowyl Hi I'm Rowyl, I am a Senior Programmer on the EQ Live team, I mostly work on graphics and UI stuff. And I am a freak!
Absor Hehe, I guess that means, we'll look into it... :)
Abashi I'll be happy to answer "The Lure question" everyone would like
Abashi Ok... As long as no one posts another question while I'm typing :)
* Greebo slaps Ogamok's wrist. Bad druid! Stop training!
* Ogamok shouts d00d stfu
Abashi Basically: The Lure spells were meant to save like lifetaps. Unfortuantely they were saving like dragon breath (dragons get better saves). They were fixed, they now save like lifetaps as was intended. It should have been preannounced, but was missed due to its low game impact. It really only affects raids on things that are immune to certain types of magic.
Abashi done.
Thundolfe (Xanthus|AtWork) Question: What is in store for the future of EverQuest? After the latest Velious expansion, will there be anything more added on to the game?
Solist We, that is the EQLive team, are committed to constantly add to and improve existing everquests. This includes everything from more planned download zones, new quests, more content in old zones and revisiting old dungeons.
Thundolfe (Shalmeneza) There are supposed to be many 35+ quests in Velious, but the majority are 50+. Is this true or just there are more to be found?
Kendrick Shalmenza, the 50+ ones are higher profile, but there are lots of lower level quests yet to be found.
Tolan There definitely are level 35 quests, I wrote more than a couple ;)
Solist The eastern side of Velious has quite a few quests for levels 35-50, you just have to ask the residents of those areas.
Greebo Follow up on that if I may..
Greebo It has been my impression from researching a number of those quests in the eastern areas that they are intended to extend over along period of time...in one particular quest (cont...)
Greebo I was told "Come back in a month"
Greebo Are you actually using real dates in your quests as flags for ability to continue now?
Greebo And if so - are you sure they're working? :) Its been three game months since I was told that and, well, he's keeping me hanging. :)
Solist Hehe, there are actually a few tags in there that are slated for future quests. You might want to check back with him after this patch =)
Abashi interjecting....
Greebo You're not going to answer my technical part of the question about dates, are you? :)
* Thundolfe tapes Greebo's mouth shut
Greebo Ok ok - Abashi go ahead then back to the queue :)
Abashi Basically we wanted to have NPCs have dialog and have story even if we didn't necessarily have a quest for them right away based on it. It's also nice to keep people checking back with these guys after every qeust :) And no, we don't have any special flags that are generating that response.
Abashi done...
Greebo Ok - next up?
Ogamok *Xerexs* The lifetap line of spells is unresistable in normal encounters but often completely resisted in "uber encounters" Originally lifetap was completely unresistable but this was changed to prevent low lvl characters from killing mobs of much higher level. It seems perfectly balanced to restore post lvl 50 lifetaps to unresistable versus any mob. Any chance of a change like this in the future?
Abashi Yeah, that'll happen....
Abashi Seriously though...
Abashi Do you not think that it's reasonable that necro's should have to pay *sometime* for their immense power in all other portions of the game?
Abashi Nothing comes for free.. A gentleman came up to me at the launch party and asked me the same question. He complained that he couldn't "solo much" beyond 57th level and wanted to know why that was. My answer was that he soloed up to 57th level without a problem. It's not the answer people want, but there has to be a downside to the class: An area where they are not as powerful as they are in another.
Abashi done.
Thundolfe (Strifei) question for rowyl... why is trade window or tracking or the box to change language isnt same resolution of screen like the rest of windows in velious
Rowyl The plan is to make EVERYTHING work in "fullview" mode and I mean EVERYTHING. It's just gonna take some time to get there.
* Thundolfe grovels
Rowyl Tracking will be working in fullview in January
Rowyl I do also plan to make the "loot" "bank" and "merchant" portions of the inventory mode real windows also
Rowyl done.
Reijas *MilamberX* Do you think that you may add more quests in the future which involve thinking, and not camping? There are quests like that now, but the reward is SMALL.
Kendrick MilamberX, we've done more than a few "thinking" quests..some of them are epics, where the reward ain't small..;) And there will be more.
Greebo Please bear with us folks, we're wading through the questions just as fast as we can. :)
Reijas *MatoQuelliousHierophant* Will there be a higher level limit in the future? The game is getting stale because of no forward progress at 60.

Greebo the questions go from the Question takers to the developers in advance so they can write up the answers with as little delay as possible.
Greebo However even then, they all get busy writing at times and so it slows down a bit in here for a minute.
Greebo We do appreciate your patience and are moving as fast as we can.
Thundolfe *Radek* Two part question. Greetings, I would like to know if the Burning Ember drop in Burning Woods is still bugged till next patch. There have been a FEW confirmed drops of it.
Moonshadow *Kazz|EQ* Will there be any thought as to including more spell diamonds for caster to memorize more spells at any given time?
Greebo oops
Kendrick Radek, the Embers still drop, verified on Saturday by yours truly on a live server..they are just rare.
Reijas *Radek* Greetings, I would like to know if the Burning Ember drop in Burning Woods is still bugged till next patch.

It is a big part of the casters game putting together what they consider ot be the "best" spell lineup, so we're not going to change that.

Greebo One other quick reminder:
Greebo Please only submit your question to ONE Question taker ONE time - if the question isn't accepted the first time it won't the subsequent times.
Greebo And it just slows things down for everyone else. :)
Ogamok *Assandir* question: we just got two necro only robes in plane of growth,both people who got them cant equip them. is that robe broke or is there now some lvl requirement to equip some items too(would make sense as both necros dont have the spell thats the effect on the robe yet)
Absor We'll look at those robes. For now, that's all I can say.
Abashi interjecting....
Abashi We haven't purposely put any level restrictions on equipping items... so it would mean that it's a bug. We'll just have to check into it as Absor said.
Abashi er. done
Ogamok *|Reddog|* Will we ever get more bank slots and or the abiity to purchase or tinker boxes that only fit in the bank which have more than 10 slots?
Abashi Simply, no. Current bank space and container size is something that we've frozen and have decided not to pursue expanding.
Abashi done.
Rowyl Because of the way items are stored on the server and how the code is structured to deal with them, it's VERY unlikely we will ever see a bag with more then 10 slots or more bank or gen inventory slots
