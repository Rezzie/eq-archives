They went and screwed the Shamans' DOTs. Wonderful.

You noticed when you download the patch, how it says EXACTLY what they did to the Necro DOTs? Here is what they said they did to Shaman spells:

"Necromancer and Shaman disease spells have had their duration reduced. Their damage has been upped to compensate."

Interesting.

What actually happened to Shaman disease DOTs is that the time the target is affected by the spell has been reduced greatly, without any corresponding increase to the initial damage, or the per tick damage. Ergo, the spells now do less damage, total. Same damage per tick, but the spell doesn't last as long.

So, did Verant decide that since no one was complaining about Shamans being too weak, that they were obviously too powerful? Why do this? All it does is contribute to the amount of complaints that that Verant will receive now. I definately will let my displeasure be known. Not that it will do any good.
