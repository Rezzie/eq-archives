THEY 'RE FIXIN WARRIORS!! WHOO!

Here's the Low-Down on what we get. They're not puttin in a higher level skill then Disarm. Thats to be expected.

They ARE adding a new reference for Warriors. Like that WORTHLESS skill Berzerk, we get another "random" skill. CRITICAL HIT! Yeah. LIke if you roll a natural 20 in AD&D, if you get a Critical Hit, you do DOUBLE DAMAGE. There will NOT be a skill for this, its gonna be Random.

Also, we get Weapon Specialization!!!! This happens at Level 20, and works much like Caster's Specialize. We get to dump points into base specialize, and it'll go up. Then we choose a "Primary" for our Specialize skill at 50, and dump one more point into it. That's our primary specialization.

Rumors are that Specialization will see an Increase in Damage and Decrease in Delay for the skill you choose as your primary. (To a Lesser extent for the rest of the specialized skills)
