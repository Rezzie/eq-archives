I'm a smith on the Rathe server, but unfortunately I have no large molds readily available where i live.

I'm a Dwarf from Kaladim. If you come on the Faydwer continent and are willing to bring me molds i can make you all the banded you want, at a good price.

My skill is 115, and although i still fail a lot on capes and leggings, i'm very proficient on most items and most likely would require no more than 3 molds of each items you wish made.

As far as AC and weight is concerned i do not know them all by heart, but the general rule is:

Banded offers one AC more than chainmail, for the same weight.
The exceptions to this are then tunic AC15 vs AC12 for chain, and gorget AC5 vs AC5 for chain.


Here are those i know for sure:

Legs AC8
Helm AC8
Mask AC4
Bracers AC6
Gorget AC5
Tunic AC15
Boots AC6


And those i'm not too sure:

Arms AC7
Mantle AC6
Belt AC6
