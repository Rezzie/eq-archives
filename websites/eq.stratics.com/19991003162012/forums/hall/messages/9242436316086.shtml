As a 'tank' 12th level Dwarven Warrior, I spend a great deal of my time sitting and resting, occationally calling out if there is a healer in the area. I always thank them profusely when they do heal me, and in many cases offer coin, only to be turned down with a 'Tis my pleasure'

When I am adventuring/hunting I would give a great deal to have a cleric in my party, and would insure to the best of me ability their defense.

I have grouped with other warriors, and we get a lot of small talk in while waiting to heal, with Wizards, which make short work of monsters (sometimes to short) and with Paladins and Clerics. Paladins while on the low side for healing make up for that fact with the ability to also fight. But NOTHING beats a good cleric by your side!

Hail to the Clerics and Healers of Norrath.
