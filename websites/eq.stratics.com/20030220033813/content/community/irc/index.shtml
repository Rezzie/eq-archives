EverQuest House of Commons - Developer Chat with Absor and the Team

On October 2nd, 2001 in the #EQHoC channel of irc.stratics.com, Verant Interactive Developers joined with IRC Regulars to hold the third EQSS House of Commons!

Members of the Verant Staff present were: Abashi, Absor, Akkirus, Aradune, Auric, Ester the Tester, Jahaar, Kayvman, N0chulus, Rowyl, Solist, and Yakatizma.

Special thanks go to Anci, AnnaT, NickyD, Moonshadow, and Reijas for managing the questions throughout the session!

Here now is the transcript from the Third EQSS House of Commons!
 
-#eqhoc- WELCOME!
   
Thundolfe Hi folks and welcome to another EverQuest Stratics House of Commons Chat with Verant. I am super pleased with the turnout and the amount of developers that have joined us today!
Thundolfe Before we begin, I want to cover the ground rules.
Thundolfe This is a moderated chat.. only those with an @ or a + beside their names may talk
Thundolfe In order to get your question in correctly, please message the following people. Question takers are: Anci, AnnaT, Moonshadow, NickyD, Reijas. Please send your questions to these people ONLY
Thundolfe DO NOT send them to me or the devs as they will not be answered.
Thundolfe Please be patient. We have a huge turnout and a lot of questions, so yours may not be answered immediatley.
Thundolfe The EQ Devs are those who are voiced (+ beside their names)
Thundolfe Verant people, if you would please introduce yourselves :)
Solist Hi, I'm Solist the wizard aka Bill Coyle, Assistant Producer. Has anyone seen my Icy wand...I keep losing the stupid thing
Abashi Hello all, I'm Gordon Wrinn, Associate Producer of the EverQuest Live and Expansions team. Welcome to the chat!
Absor Hi all! I'm the Community Relations Manager for EverQuest. Thanks for showing up, and thanks to the kind folks at Stratics for hosting this chat.
Auric Hello all. My job title is Community Relations Representative (Message board moderator) Basically, I am the go between for the players to the developers!
EstertheTester Hey! I'm Ester the ummm .... Tester!!
Jahaar Hi all, my name's Jahaar and I'm a Designer on the EQ Live team.
Yakatizma Greetings, Dan Enright game designer on everquest live and expansions..
Akkirus Steve Burke...Assoc Designer EQlive
Aradune Hullo, I'm Brad McQuaid, Chief Creative Officer and VP, Executive Producer on all EQ products, original Producer of EQ, original co-designer of EQ, etc. etc. :)
N0chulus Hola, I'm Lawrence Poe, Game Designer on the EQLive/Luclin team.
Rowyl_The_Freak Hi there, I'm the lead programmer... I lead all our programmers off the deep end. Aside from that I am doing the DirectX 8 engine code
Kayvman Hello all. Its good to see all of you here. I am Kayvan Simantob, A game designer for EQLive/Luclin
Auric Oh yeah. Forgot my name heh. <==Brian De Marco.)
* Rowyl_The_Freak is also known as Roy Eltham
Thundolfe We are trying to focus this chat on the new expansion, Shadows of Luclin. Preference will be given to those questions. I don't think they're going to answer any more questions about melee binding, sorry :)
Thundolfe I'd like to lead off with a question to the devs if I may.
Thundolfe I'm very keen on some of the new SoL models, but not so much on others. My question is: If I decide to use the new models in SoL, but later decide I don't like them, can I freely switch back and forth between old and new?
Solist Yes, players will have the option of choosing to see either all of the new models or all of the old ones as a customization feature. Unfortunately you won't be able to see new gnomes and old trolls for example, but we figured we'd give people the option for sentimental and subjective purposes.
Thundolfe Excellent, thank you. :)
NickyD *Jerrith* Can we expect high level content for Beastlords (planar armor, thurg, kael, and SS armor quest npcs), to be in place right away on SoL's release, or will we face a long delay? (Similar to the iksar planar armor.)
N0chulus We have already created several sets of Armor for the Beastlords that will match the planar, Kunark, Velios, and Quest armors that the other classes currently have. We will also be putting together new armors and equipment for them in Luclin as well. You can expect these to be in place on release.
Reijas *Houseofthedead2* "What exactly are the price ranges for horses? And what are the highest and lowest prices?"
Abashi It's too early for us to put a price tag on them. To tell you the truth, we probably won't announce it prior to the first people discovering where they might be purchasable in game. We do like to save *some* stuff for those who go exploring :)
Anci *Tuuv* There are reports from test server that epics have not been subject to the 2hs enhancements. This could mean that the hard earned epics, specially paladins, could at the end be sub-par when compared to those receiving the upgrade. Are the epics not going to be subject to the 2hs dly/dmg ratio enhancements? and if not, why not? or are there going to be quest in SOL to furhter enhance our epics that will place them above the ones now being enhanced?,/b>
Jahaar While it's true that epic two-handed weapons won't be receiving an increase in damage and delay stats with this melee balance patch, it's not accurate to say they weren't enhanced. We made a change to the way two-handed weapon damage is calculated after level 50, which is going to mean a significant increase in damage done by two-handers.
Jahaar All epic two-handers will benefit from this change, and I think you'll find they compare quite well to epic one-handers. Only the very highest end, post-epic two-handers really needed a change in stats in addition to this new damage formula.
AnnaT Playing a ranger, I was wondering if any of the new spells would help to define the ranger class more, such as archery related spells or maybe something to do with tracking
N0chulus Indeed, we will be introducing a new line of Birds eye spells in Luclin, these spells will modify your chance to hit with Archery. Currently there are three spells in the line, which will start at the lower levels and run to the post 50 levels.
Reijas *Bihlbo* Are they [bird's eye spells] self-only?
N0chulus The Ranger Birds eye spells will be self only
Reijas *Espio_Terris* Question for the devs: Most of the other spell classes have gotten a hint, albit unoffical, as to what thier new spells are going to be like, but bards are currently hanging dry. Any chance you could give us a little hint as to what new songs we might be getting, if any?
N0chulus ok, in response to the bard question posted above, first let me apologize for not getting songs in sooner, but please don't feel like you have been forgotten, I assure you, you haven't. I'm still waiting on coding for some of the new effects, though I do hope to add some of the bard songs later this week. Some of the things that are being considered are area (not group) Resistance songs, and haste songs that will exceed the normal haste cap. Going beyond the current haste cap will only be possible using one of these bard songs.
NickyD *Tuuv* Can you explain the reason for horses if it is true as rumored that they will provide no speed advantage over sow/selo's perhaps not to upset those classes that view those abilities as "defining"? Just eye candy to keep people around? I mean besides being able to say hey I had 14k to burn on a horse; what good would they be vs. the much cheaper and available jboots for example?
Aradune Well, there are several reasons really. First, both the players and the dev team has always wanted horses in the game, ever since pre-beta really. So we've always wanted to add them, but needed both the time and opportunity to do so. But we also needed to figure out a way that wouldn't be unbalancing, and that wouldn't require re-working combat or other major systems in the game. So we dicided on a way to add them that hopefully won't interfere much, and it's really our desire that they do the following:
Aradune 1. provide an alternate means of transportation -- as the EQ universe grows and grows, this becomes more and more important
Aradune 2. provide for a new way to look -- I think being mounted on a horse is pretty impressive visually
Aradune and 3. provide a solid money sink, to help keep platinum valuable within the player driven economy
Aradune and we were able to do all of that with the current design for horses without risking changing too much else in the game
Aradune as for the prices, as mentioned, it's too early to announce them, but while they'll be expensive, they won't be unobtainable, or only obtainable by the very few -- that's not the point at all. Nor do I think they'll interfere much with druids and rangers being asked for SOWs and such.
Anci *Elena* my question: Will you be able to see the new models in the new world as well as the old?
Solist Anyone who owns Luclin will be able to see all of the new character models in every zone, old ones included.
Moonshadow *Lyron* I don't meet the miinimum system requirements for Luclin. Obviously, I can't play a Kerran or visit the new zones. Will I still be able to buy a copy and take advantage of some of the other content, like non-Kerran Beasmasters, for example?
Yakatizma Even if you can't visit the new zones due to graphical requirements you can still choose to display old world character models and play a non-Vah Shir Beastlord. There are four other races besides the Vah Shir that can be beastlords. Admittedly some of the beastlord content such as quests and no drop items that are found in the new zones will be unavailable.
Yakatizma You will also still be able to take advantage of the horse feature and the post 50 alternative advancement.
Anci *Nanaki_Ikanan* Here is my question: Will there be any new high end zones like Temple of Veeshan in SoL? New planes, or the like? Or will it be more focused on smaller encounters?
Abashi There will be a good deal of high end content, as well as raid encounters. However Luclin focuses *primarily* on content aimed at at single, to two-to-three groups working together. So we're not including entire zones geared to raids-only as was present with a few zones in Velious. Don't worry though, there will still be tons of things to do :)
AnnaT Jahaar: You're new to the team.. what role are you filling and what kind of experience do you have in everquest. Have you played a play character all the way to the top?
Jahaar I joined the live team a few months ago, and have been working in the game industry for about 9 years now. My role on the live team is working as a content designer for new zones and expansions, mostly. I've recently been heavily involved in re-vamping Chardok, and working on the melee changes on the test server. As far as my knowledge of the game, I've been playing EQ for about 2 and a half years now, and I have levelled 5 characters over level 52 on a play server, including one level 60 character.
Reijas *HuweyII* Could you give some clues about the, "Additional non-level-based advancement options for those at level 60
Solist Basically, high level players will be able to use experience gained towards new skills and traits rather than leveling...which of course isn't important once you've reached 60. These traits will contain many options from reduced casting time for wizards to the ability for a paladin to increase his lay on hands skill to heal completely. We're still playing around with the finalized list, but I'm sure it'll be a much desired feature for all.
Moonshadow *Ikeya|Graffes* While riding on our ponies, will 2 handed weapons suffer an increased dely on them, since from a roleplay point, you need to swing it with one arm while using the other to keep from following off the pony?
Abashi We're not planning on including any advantages or disadvantages to gameplay based upon being mounted with possible exception to giving the horse some "mass" from a physics perspective. Adding another balance component of that magnitude (e.g. affecting all of melee) would have made doing horses difficult in the first place. As Brad said earlier, Horses are being included primarily for Travel, and the players desire to futher differentiate themselves from others. :)

Reijas *leick* any chance of sneaking in a way to change cursor/pointer cause at higher resolutions i keep losing where my cursor is?
Rowyl_The_Freak Yes, with the new UI you will be able to change the graphics for UI elements including the mouse cursor.
AnnaT Why was there a name change on Mrylokar's Dagger along with lowered stats and damage/delay? Is there any plans to un-nerf this or another added to Vulaks Loot Table?
Jahaar As part of this melee balancing patch, I'm looking over the quality of post-epic items and trying to guarantee each class has equal quality weapons available across the board. I'll be looking over the items on Vulak, along with the rest of Temple of Veeshan and I think it's safe to say a few items will be getting some stat changes.
AnnaT Are beastlord casters, tanks, meele casters? What are their stregths?
Yakatizma Beastlords are a melee/shaman hybrid with the melee aspect leaning more towards a monk than a warrior. They have some of the special properties of a monk such as an unencumbered armor class bonus, better ac bonus for high agility and are second only to monks in unarmed combat damage. Like a monk they will have to rely on tanks or their pet to soak up a large percentage of the damage that would otherwise be directed at them.. In addition to their pet enhancement spells they will also receive some unique non-pet oriented beastlord spells and spells from their Shaman parent class. You can think of them as primitive spiritual warriors with close ties to the wild and the shamanistic cultures they emerge from.
NickyD *Vino-TMO* Question: As a wizard, i've often been concerned by the relative decline in spell damage vs. melee damage over time (melee tend to get faster weapons, while damage spell damage is basically static). I've heard some things about SoL that sound promising on this issue, can you elaborate a bit on it?
N0chulus We are currently addressing some of the problems that casters are having in the higher end encounters as part of our rebalancing on the test server. I really can't elaborate any more on that right now, but we will be releasing more information sometime soon.
AnnaT Being a warrior and loving the class and having loged 520 played days in EQ I am seeing a trend towards bring all mele class up to par with warrior the only skill a warrior has that other mele dont have as of yet is defencive skill what if any skill will new warrior have to look forward to I have seen a lot of talk about the other class but none of mine also can u put up a link to a pic of the new Oger looks comeing out
Thundolfe That's a lot of days
AnnaT we all have our crosses to bear :)
* Supercow has udders to cross
Jahaar As a class, the warrior is incredibly useful in both group and raid situations, and in fact is often thought of as crucial to making an efficient group. The warrior does have a lot of things going for him now - the highest quality armor, excellent damage output, a large advantage in hit points and actual tanking ability, taunts, stuns & slams, a vast selection of weapons with interesting and useful procs, as well as many useful disciplines. We're quite happy with the role a warrior currently plays in EverQuest, happy enough that we used the warrior as a baseline to measure the performance of other classes as we were balancing. With this round of changes, we will be upping the warrior's taunt skill at level 60 to a cap of 230, to help deal with other classes gain in melee damage. We'll keep an eye on future changes, to guarantee a warrior always has an important role in the game, but there aren't any immediate class changes we feel are needed to make warriors more effective than they are.
NickyD *Matthi* Hail, I have hoping for years that you will implement "chat groups" - where a message will be automatically sent to multiple people - will that be implemented in SoL ?
Abashi Not as part of Luclin, but yes, it remains one of the many things that we'd like to do at some point.
Reijas *Ynyr* will monster models(for old world) be redone as well? (in addition to player models)
Solist A few of the old monsters will receive new models, particularly the ones that players can summon or turn into such as skeletons and elementals. Most of the old world critters will remain the same however as there just isn't the time or resources to do that much remodelling.
NickyD *FrisLFG* I'm sure it's too late, but..."Q: What do familiars do???"
N0chulus Familiars will follow the Wizards around. They do not attack, and can recieve no commands other then /pet get lost. They will zone with the Wizards, and be there waiting for them when they log in (they do not stay in the world when the wizard is gone though)... They will grant added resistances to the Wizard, as well as some other benefits that will aid in casting efficiency, as well their mana pool, and mana regen. That being said, they currently have not gone through final balancing and are still very much in the process of being worked on.
Moonshadow Have they looked into changing the faction level required for the upper end quests so that sneak will not be an option?
Jahaar We've already made one pass through some high end quests, making the top level Velious quest armor un-sneakable in most cases, as an example. We're likely to go through again to make sure we're happy with the faction requirements of high end quests, and will be keeping that in mind as we develop the Luclin quests.
Anci *Oogly* Question: Regarding horses, if you die, does your horse die with you?
Yakatizma You have an item that you can keep in your inventory such as a bridle or whistle or something along those lines that will allow you to summon your horse again should you die or dismiss it, whether or not these will have charges is undecided at this time.
AnnaT question to the fine folks from verant: will gnomes get clockwork horses? we gnomes demand clockwork
Solist Unfortunately there was an accident in the Akanon clockwork horse manufacturing plant last weekend that resulted in..well...a big mess. Due to this unfortunate turn of events the Gnomish Horse Crafters Union has sadly announced that their people will have to use real horses for the next several years. Sad..but its the truth ;)

Anci *Rylandkov* In the new expansion will those who have existing characters beable to select a new face for their character?
Jahaar We're definitely planning to make some accomodation for people with existing characters to choose a face they like when the new character models become available.
Reijas *GreyK* are there any plans to change the way some of the highend mobs spawn in Luclin and/or EQ in general? Server down campers don't give anyone else a shot at anything
Yakatizma You can expect to see some unconventional methods of spawning both standard and high-end unique mobs in Luclin. Can't elaborate any further than that or it would spoil the fun.
NickyD *HuweyII* Will the clipping distance be increased in the new graphics engine? I'd love to be able to see the shore from the boat and I'm confident my computer can handle it.
Abashi The new graphics engine doesn't limit the distances we can set the clipping plane out to, at least not in the sense of what you're experienced with. So in general, I expect that you'll have more control over the clipping plane, with exception to cases where we limit how far you can see as purely a gameplay feature.
Moonshadow *Nanaki_Ikanan* What are some of the things we can expect in the revamped chardok? Harder zone? New Loot?
Jahaar Chardok was a dungeon that wasn't seeing as much use as we'd like. People would go there to raid the Royals for epic pieces, and occasionally you'd see a group camping the zone in, but overall it just wasn't getting many people coming in. I've always been impressed with the architecture of the zone, and it seemed like a perfect zone to get a bit of a revamp.
Jahaar The amount of roaming mobs has come down quite a bit, things still roam but they don't wander from the very back of the dungeon to the front. You'll find a higher amount of named mobs in the zone, some very interesting new treasures dropping, some quests and conveniences for people with good Sarnak faction, as well as a lot of fun encounters there.
Jahaar Another guy on the team, Azaliil, has worked in some incredible story elements and flavor that really make the zone stand out. It's been very well received on test server, and I think people will be really pleased when they get a chance to adventure in new Chardok.
Anci *Makswel* question: any chance of getting a "keychain" or someting with SOL? would be nice to consolidate all of my soul bound keys in one spot. getting a 9th one is a current impossiblity.
Abashi We're working on something for that, essentially marking your character as having completed a particular quest and then having doors and such check that flag rather than requiring an item. I hope to see it in by Luclin.
