It is my solemn duty to bring life to those who are near death. But then why should I do it for those Rangers (Sil), or warriors who SEEK it?

Think of this, a few peasants diseased, or molested by animals. I go and heal them, this is all well and good, it is my duty. But my prayer book, my guide to my way of life says nothing about following around some smelly warrior to heal him when he gets into trouble.

I am sorry if this offends, and my conscience does make me heal the few in trouble I see. I dont always get a thank you, and dont expect one.

But I dont see it, My Duty, to heal those that have gone to far. Those warriors, rangers, and other arms masters who go out of there way to get themselves in trouble. If you get diseased, poisoned or hurt horribley, I will heal you for a price.

Again some cases are different, pleading always works. It is the power given to me by my God Rodcet Nife. It is mine to use how I see fit. If Im in trouble I will of course return any help given.

And if Im not asked to heal, and your in trouble do not give me sarcasm. Saying, thank you for the help oh grand cleric. That reallly does offend me.

Now the charges for my services, since so many have been rude. Once I was offered 2cp to cure disease! BAH! That was worse then asking for free.

Light Healing - 5cp
Courage - 3cp
Holy Armor - 1sp
Center - 2sp
Cure Disease - 2sp
Cure Poison - 5cp

Is this asking to much? Im a cleric and have need of both Arms & Spells. My profession is indeed an expensive one.

Yes it was my choice to become one of the healers of Norrath, but it is also my choice on how I use it.
