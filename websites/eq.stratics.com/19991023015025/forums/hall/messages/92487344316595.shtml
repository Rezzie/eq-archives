It's funny, I have been all over and find Cutthroat rings as one of the items easy to acquire. Yet, when I attempted to sell them for 1pp (outrageous since I had 9 on me) each at the Woodelf town, I had to limit 1 to customer to keep them from going beserk. They told me I could have gotten 2pp for them. Well, I thought this was strange, but after talking to a few I found out something strange. These characters were around 10 - 13 lv and had never left the Faydark. They didn't even know where the port to Freeport was. Well I was surprised at my little backwoods High Elf relatives. For being as high minded as we are, they have chosen more of a hermit type existence.

I took an opportunity to go visit my old haunts and was as disgusted with Crushbone as ever. I had forgotten why I had left to begin with. Crushbone is overhunted and the hunters fight over the poor outnumbered Orcs like carrion birds fighting over the last scarp of meat on an old dry bone. It wasn't long before I was back on the boat to Freeport. I wish my race would learn to control its population by either pushing the birds out of the nest once they are grown or practice safe sex. We may have to put spray some chemicals on the forrest where the Woodelves forage, they seem to flourish like rabbits and are incapable of controlling their instincts.
