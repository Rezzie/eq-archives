When you fight anything that will poison or disease you, right before they die, there is a hell of a good chance you are gonna get nailed with it. Solo, what do you do? When you pull the snake, your animation will taunt it. Don't do root and blast it before your animation locks on. Just hit it with either Tashan or a Choking spell to pull it. Now your animation has taunted, you weaken it and enter melee. This will save your animation and allow him to beat on the snake. When you see the snake getting low, or you are getting near half health, back out and let the animation taunt. (if it is locked on, color flux then continue.) One key tactic for you is "fear", when the snake, spider, scarab gets to about half health, hit it with fear. It will turn and run while your animation is beating the hell out of it. IF it makes it through "fear" then finish with CF. In open areas, fear is a very useful spell, especially solo or with one other tank to smack it as it runs.
