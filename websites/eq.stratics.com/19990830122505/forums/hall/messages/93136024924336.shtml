In Reply to: Me and my Questions. Here are a few. posted by Gilgamel on July 07, 1999 at 10:34:28 AM CDT:

1) Telchar is correct... *1* point is all you need for DW to start it off... it goes up fairly quickly and remember that it will start at 17 since that is the level you learned it.

2) Maybe this is just because I am a rogue... but I would much rather be using 2 weapons instead of a 2 hander... yes the BIBS is nice with 20 base damage but still... a barbed whip and PGT makes for a nicer combo IMHO.

3) Blackened iron crown... 5 more str and since you are headed to RE anyway I would look for one. I would also give up the rusty spiked pads for a BS Mantles... you'll lose 2 str but gain 20 magic res in the process over using the spiked pads. Other str items you may want to look for in the future: Jagged Band, Dwarven wookboots, and Bracer of Erollsi... also I believe Fire Emerald jewelry gives STR and AGI or DEX...but I don't own any yet.

4) You WILL need a full & well rounded party like Telch sed at level 17... and even then I would suggest having someone with SoW or JBoots charge up outside then zone back in and pull elites/warlord/king to the Beholder zone and fight there....and also remember that while blackened armor does look cool (even I couldn't resist giving up my barbed arms and legs for it), it has worse AC than banded and it also MUCH heavier. Your choice I guess on what you feel your needs are.
