Would it be possible for many of the quest that should only be done one to have a single bit flag that show if the player has completed the quest or not. The explanation that I have heard to why the quests have such a small reward in loot and exp is do to the fact that they are repeatable an infinate number of times. (hiltop and the turnip quest in rivervale come to mind) If the quest was only do able once the rewards could be much greater and more realistic, now simple quests like "bring me a deathfist belt" should have the small reward and should be do able unlimited times.

Advanates for a flag for each quest
1) Bigger rewards making them more worth wild. Not saying super weapons and armor but atleast more than what you could have sold them to a vendor for.

2) After the completion the Npc could say a differnt line of text when the same player hails him to increase the illusion of a real world. Just a simple "Thanks for your help, but I require nothing more at this time" would really help vs. "Hi, I need 4 bone chips" regaurdless of the thousands you gave them already.

3) A personal monk issue. If the guild knows you completed the orange head band quest, you then should always beable to get anouther orange head band. Nothing is worse than a bugged headband quest and you have to start ALL over with the white headband. This should maybe also work with the guild tunic, and even the ablity to get a new "newbie" weapon as long as you just go back to your guild if you turned your note in.

4) Remove the level restrictions, I have found many quests that are "Not worth my effort" when I just enter a new town or area, I would like to be able judge if its not worth my effort or not.

Dissavantages

1) 1800 people 600 quests ~5 characters per player even at one byte a flag thats 5,400,000 flags. I don't know how long it would take to check that info, but if the quests already check your faction and level, to replace the level check with a flag check wouln't be to much worse

2) A "gliched" charater that either has all the quests reset or have a few seen as already done would cause problems

All feedback is welcome, as long as its not in ALL CAPS =)
