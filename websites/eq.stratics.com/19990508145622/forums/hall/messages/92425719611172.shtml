It depends on the class - as a shaman I hung around the main room downstairs and
occasionally went below the bridge. The area gets pretty crowded, so it is tough to
find a gnoll all to yourself. One good spawn is the gnoll out on the platform - you can
hit it with a spell from the bridge area - it usually has a bow and arrows so the loot
is good. At the upper end 11-12 I partnered with a similar level fighter to take down
elites and the commander. Of course, there are always the giant snakes...
