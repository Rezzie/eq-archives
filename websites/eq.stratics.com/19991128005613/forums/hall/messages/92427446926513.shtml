Being new the the game and at 5th leve Wizard, I decided to venture across the globe from Felwith to Erudin. I had not even seen the sea before. What an adventure. I am currently higher level but after reading all the complaints and sniffles everyday, I feel it needs to be said that nice people help nice people and the game is what you make of it.
I had no business running through Gorge of King Xorbb, runnyeye or wherever lvl 5 Wizard should not be but found everyone to be very helpful and willing to guide me in the right direction or cast a nice spell on me to aid me run fast.
Yes I had the false impression I could hide in houses from the Griffins and hill giants and was very lucky, but that did not ruin my experience. This game can be much more than just kill and level but make your own quest. I met many funny and amazing people and stories on the way. I took lots of screen shots and have a wonderful screen saver of my personal quest. For having never seen the ocean or a halfling, it was great! You should have seen the stares and comments I got when I got to Quenos. BTW, I got to Erudin and bought me a red Wizards cloak to make me stand out on my island.
Base point is, stop trying to pick out the bad parts of the game but make the best adventure and game experience you can with what you have and you might have fun.
