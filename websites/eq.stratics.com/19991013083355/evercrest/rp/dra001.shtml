Quick Save? or Quick Death?
Fiction by Draciar

Draciar, noble paladin of Tunare awoke. As he looked around, he was in unfamiliar territory. A branch cracked from above him and it fell into a muddy pool next to him. He had no clue how he got here, or where this was. As he became more awake he realized his armor, and face was encased in dirt.

Draciar took a quick swim to clean the mud off his armor, and jumped back out. Somehow he remembered running... coming to save something. He couldn't remember what that something was. He shrugged and walked along a muddy path. He was very cautious, since he had no idea what kind of monsters dwelled in these muddy crevices. Suddenly something jumped out of the water! Draciar's first instinct was to draw his sword. He looked at the creature and saw a frogman holding a spear. He slashed at it, and cut it in half neatly, killing it. "hmm.. easy." He remarked.

He continued down the path, and stopped suddenly. Footsteps... and very deep, booming voices. Draciar slowly approched and saw a drow or inkie scum, as he liked to call it, struting away, laughing evily. He glared at the thing daring to kill it, but the inkie wasn't the real threat, he saw two trolls, tormenting an innocent female, that was trapped by a hand. Draciar remembered his dreams 3 or 4 days ago again. Tunare somehow spoke to him to come to a swamp... the Innothule Swamp. That explains where he was! He looked at the trolls and saw them doing...

BAM! Draciar bashed one troll on the side of the head with his shield. The another troll swung carelessly in rage, but Draciar ducked, and the troll hit his companion troll standing next to him. "Ha, hey troll that just got hit by your "friend," it looks like he isn't really, your friend, he just wants to fight over the beautiful girl in the hand! I'd watch your back if I were you..." The first troll that Draciar bashed came from behind him and slamed him in the back, crushing him. Draciar tumbled but managed to stand upright.

"Want to play that way, eh? You're no match for me!" Draciar delivered a blow to the trolls stomach with his steel sword, and followed up with a bash. The young troll was enraged and swung his club in an arch motion. Draciar sidestepped and slashed again, this time in the throat. Draciar laughed and said mockingly, "Didn't you learn at your guild that anger doesnt win battles, but skill and patience? Oh yeah, I forgot... the inkie was stupid and left 3 YOUNG trolls in charge." The troll charged after him, but Draciar rolled to the side, and the troll hit the other two, that were provoked into fighting each other.

All three of them began to fight. Draciar looked to the woman in the hand, that was trying to wriggle free. "That was brave of you, but i fear you can't cant help me in this situation of freeing me, noble paladin" She said solemnly.

Draciar replied, "At least i got the trolls off ye." He looked back to see one of them was bleeding signifigantly.

"Stop it, my young trolls! why are you fighting each other, but not the enemy? Help kill this pitiful High Elf!" Draciar spun around to see the drow. But the drow was too late in warningthe trolls... a troll was on the ground dying. The other two trolls looked at Draciar and began to advance... too many of them, i could take the drow one on one, and... the next thing he knew was pain, and then darkness...
