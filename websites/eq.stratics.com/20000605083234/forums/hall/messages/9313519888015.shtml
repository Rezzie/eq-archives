Enchanters can be a good deal of fun. I have a high-elf enchanter (lvl 11) and it has been very enjoyable. Here are the minuses:

1) Your damage spells are not as powerful as the wizards are magicians.
2) Your pets are not as strong as magician pets.
3) Because of points 1 and 2 you are more difficult to solo beyond lvl 9 or so.

Pluses:
1) You have some unique spells: Fear, color flux, mesmerize, lull, sooth to use in combat to help save the group.
2) Becuase of (1), you must be able to think and use strategy as an enchanter or you and the group will die.
3) Enchant metal spells: Learn jewelcraft and you have the only class with a useful trade skill.

Good luck.
