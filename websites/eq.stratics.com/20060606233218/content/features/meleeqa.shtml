Melee Enhancement Q&A


 

We received a few questions answered by Alan VanCouvering while he was still the Community Relations Manager about the latest changes to the melee system!

Now on to the questions!

Q:Any plans on endurance regeneration coming from gear, AA's or spells? (People are hoping it won't be a buff. No /ooc Selling Endurance buff_01 for donations at the bank!)

A:We are not planning any endurance regeneration at this time. Hopefully folks won't be too upset, because buffs are all we have in mind. Rather than buying buffs in PoK, maybe you could invite that character to group with you...

Q:Will Hybrid abilities be as effective as pure melee, and if so will they get them at the same time or later in the level progression?

A:Hybrids will not be getting any of the melee skills. These skills are being introduced specifically to address a discrepancy between melee characters and hybrids abilities, as well as introducing a way to make playing a melee character a bit more fun.

Q:Are warrior NPC's going to receive the new 5% melee mitigation or just player characters?

A:Only player characters will be getting the mitigation boost.
