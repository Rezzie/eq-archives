I've found that I don't have too many problems in Befallen with an elemental. I think this is due to two reasons. One is that undead don't run away and encourage your pet to chase them and irritate a whole load of other monsters before you use the "Back off" command. The second is that Befallen is pretty open in some areas allowing lots of elemental manuverability and in others it seems well sectioned off where you can deal with the bad guys in bits and pieces.

Are there other dungeon-type areas similar to Befallen? I've been told that Crushbone is pretty pet-friendly. From personal experience I know that Runnyeye is not.
