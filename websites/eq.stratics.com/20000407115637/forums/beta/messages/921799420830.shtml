I found out yesterday that trade skills such as fishing are bound to your lvl. the general formula that most agree on is 5 x lvl + 5. During the beta, there was no level based skill cap on trade skills but i guess Verant didn't like a newbie (lvl wise) to have a skill of say.. 150 in fishing. personaly, i think this is not good, for what if someone is only interested in doing trades and NOT hunting? i suppose that's one negative thing about EQ, it's very one dimmentional. I hate to use a UO example (seems so taboo to mention UO these days) but in UO you could be logger or smithy or any other trade person if that was all you wanted to be.

with the current structure in EQ, you have no choice but to be a hunter because thats the only way to gain exp that is needed for increasing skills.

I suggest you send fedback to Verant and ask them to remove the lvl based skill cap from trade skills.
