There is a specialize for each type of casting. You can take each one to the cap (50). One, of course, can go over 50.

So, since I do cast the other types of spells on occasion, I have points in all the different specializations.

Heres what I did. I had 80 training points saved up. I put one point in each availible specialize. If you do this at level 20, the first point put in brings you to a skill of 20. I did it at 22 so my skill in each specialize was 22. That left me with about 75 points, and I then put 70 points into specialize evoke. That brought specialize evoke up to 90 and ensured that it would be my main specialize (first one over 50).

Now, over time and use, my specialize evoke has risen to 116 and my other specializes are around 45. They others will soon max at 50.

So to answer your question, I picked ALL other specializes. Just made sure my specialize evoke went over 50 first.
