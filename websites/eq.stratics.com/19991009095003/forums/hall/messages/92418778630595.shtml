I am very open about what a merchant asks for and sells an item for. I have no problem with it. but mainly, it is up to you if that item is worth that price to you. Is it worth your time to try and find it elsewhere for cheaper (which it may or may not be). Or is there something better to buy with your money at an NPC. All trading I have done (and I was never in beta, So I have learned this over a couple of weeks) was based on what NPC's sell items for. Not always the same item I am selling, but just any items that benifit your character. Then I say, ok is this item someone is selling worth the price, what can I buy from an NPC for this money?

Some of us are trying to be merchants here. To add a little more to the game. All I am saying is that watch what things are buying and selling for in that area. Learn and make your own profit. Th same item will sell for a different price in a diffrent area (which is just like real life too). So don't just blurt out that that price is to high. See if someone buys it then remember that this place is good for this item.
