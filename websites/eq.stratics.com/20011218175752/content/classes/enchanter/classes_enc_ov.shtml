Overview of the Enchanter

 Introduction 

An Introduction to the Enchanter Class

Enchanters, as our Arch Magi Geoff puts it, are the masters of 'messing with monster's minds'. Enchanters have the most spells of any class and can do some very interesting and effective things with them. When it comes to fighting, enchanters can hold their own solo and thrive in groups.

Enchanters are not the easiest class to solo with. Enchanter's damage spells tend to be resisted more than other classes, making it difficult at times to kill monsters using only those spells when solo. Enchanters can get by solo using only damage spells, but a wise enchanter will discover many other ways to kill monsters. One example is Charm. It is dangerous, as charm is not very reliable, but experienced enchanters can charm a monster, have it kill another, then kill the mostly dead charmed pet when the charm spell wears off. It is far from impossible to solo all the way with an enchanter, enchanters just have to be smarter than other classes.

Groups are where enchanters thrive. With the ability to Rune all players and with the best haste spells available, it pays for enchanters to play with others. Rune makes players untouchable for a certain amount of damage and haste spells make any player attack faster. Besides rune and haste, enchanters have endless options in group battles. Before a fight, enchanters can rune the whole group, haste the fighters, and use some other very powerful 'buffs'. During a fight, enchanters can lower a monster's magic resistance, lower a monster's strength, slow a monster's attack speed, stun a monster or two for most of the fight, charm one of the monsters to help kill all the others, rune players as needed, use plain old damage spells, and much more.

Outside of combat enchanters have a ton of useful spells. They have illusion spells that can turn them into any race and several monsters, thus giving them access to any town and several other places that would otherwise be hostile. With the high elf illusion, dark elves have free reign in the high elf town. With the skeleton illusion any enchanter has free run of 90% of the undead zones. Enchanters can even use the troll illusion and get the trolls health regeneration bonus. Enchanters will be the only class that can enchant metals that can be used with trade skills to make magic items. Enchanters that build up trade skills as they grow will be set. Enchanters that wish to will be able to make a fortune selling enchanted metals. Our Arch Magi even hinted that enchanters may be close to discovering the secret of enchanting weapons directly, without the need for any trade skills.

With the most spells of any class, enchanters are the go anywhere, do anything class. Enchanters are not only extremely competent in combat, they are very fun to play no matter where you are or what you are doing.
