As he said, Skills max at 5*lvl + 5 pts

Most things you just need to dump 1pt into and then let them raise naturally.

I suggest doing this unless you feel somethings not going up quick enough in the beginning. I save most of my points and use them as needed on things that don't go up quickly or cost you a lot of money to improve (such as Bind Wound do to bandage costs, and smithing do to failure rates.)

As far as his suggestion on 1H slashing it's not quite the way he states it. The fact is that while 1H do less damage per hit. The hit much quicker (lower delay speed.) A good rule to test whats the best weapon on doing damges is.

(2*Damage Mod value + 1)/(Delay Speed)

Examples his 2H halberd doing 29 pts of damage has a damage mod value of 14 and delay of 50

So he paid 35 plat for 29/50 = 0.580 damage, pretty damn good.

I paid 10 plat for a Minotaur Axe (from a player) this is 1H slashing weapon. Stats are 8 and delay of 37.
17/37 = 0.459 It's a bit behind but I'll be lvl 15 in a short while then I'll be able to carry two of these bad boys. Note that the delay speed on a secondary weapon is slower than primary weapon My understanding is it's ~100.

So I'll have 17/37 + 17/100 = 0.629 damage for 20 plat spent on weapons. I'll be doing more damage.

Note that I don't advocate either 1H or 2H specialties. I advocate keeping both at high levels, unfortunatley I've let my 1H Slash slump so I'm working it back up from the mid 40s to where it should be (in the 70s with my 2H slash.) And I plan on switching back and forth. Because there will be points in time where 2H slash will out perform my 1H slash and vica versa do to weapon availablity.

Good Luck to all yeah warriors
