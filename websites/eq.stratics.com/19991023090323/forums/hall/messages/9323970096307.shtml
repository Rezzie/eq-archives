I think AC's magic system will fall flat on it's face...

Ok, so here is what I gather from the magic system:

You get to "create" your own spells. This is a good thing. You draw mana from a pool. The more people that draw from this pool with a particular spell, the less effective it will be. So it is best to keep the new spells that you research a secret. Now is this really possible?

Ok, look a EQ. A person finds a new item on a monster. They tell a friend or two and "SWEAR" to secrecy. Perhaps one person submitts it a spolier site WITHOUT giving the location or MOB the item is on. Well, now people will atleast know it is out there and will be looking for it. Maybe they will submit it to a spoiler site WITH the location and MOB. Maybe the one or two friends also tell one or two other friends who also "SWEAR" to secrecy. Within a few weeks, the MOB is camped by 30 people and everyone wonders why.

Same thing with this magic system for AC except it will be more fatal. With word on new spells spreading and the quest for more knowledge by spoiler sites, spell recipies will be out an about in no time. Everyone will know what the BEST spell to do damage is and use it. Thus making the damage and effecitiveness useless. Lets say there are more then one spell that does decent damage. Everyone will suddenly use the spell that looks the "coolest" when cast. Unless new spells keep popping up and there are a dozen spells per level with similiar effects, this magic system will eventually suck. In the beginning you may kick ass, but in time you will be just an average joe like everyone else. Doing the same damage as everyone else.

In EQ I like to rely that my spells can do the same damage everytime I cast it. I know that my Expulse Undead will hit for 94 damage UNLESS it gets somewhat resisted and I end up doing lower (yeah, sometimes as low as 1 damage). But in AC you will never know. I would wager my Expulse Undead spell does max damage about 90% - 95% of the time. What will the percentages be in AC when everyone is using that spell? My suggestion to spellcasters in AC, play during off peak times ONLY!
