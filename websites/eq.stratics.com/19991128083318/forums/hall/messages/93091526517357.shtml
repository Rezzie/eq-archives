I think your misunderstanding specialize a little.

There are 5 specialize skills, one for each type of casting (conjuring, evokation, divination, etc). These are each independent of other skills.

For example, you CANT specialize in smithing or any other skill. You CAN put points in the skill "specialize evokation" or "specialize divination".

I dont think any trade skills have a level cap.

You can raise all the different specialize skills, but ONLY ONE can ever go over 50. This is determined by the FIRST one to go over 50, so make sure thats the one you really want and that it gets there first.

You pick your main specialize (the one that you want to go over 50), by looking at what type of spells (evokation, divination, etc) that you want to specialize in. You can tell this by right? clicking on them in your spell book.

Each specialize skill will reduce mana usage and lower fizzle rate for the casting of that type of spell, based on the level in the skill.

I am a Wizard myself. Wizard damage spells use evokation, so I put my points into "specialize evokation". I now have 116 specialize evoke, and my other specializes are around 45. It really helps lower fizzle rates, but the change in mana usage is pretty small.

Dont expect specialize to change your life :)
