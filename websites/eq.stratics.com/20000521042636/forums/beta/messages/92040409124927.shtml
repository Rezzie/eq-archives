Serious list

The idea behind the serious list is that the transformation that a character undergoes should cause them to become something that has importance to those of the same class. It should be something that other class members call upon in a time of need, a minor diety of sorts, or a revered and famous founder of the order. In all cases, I'd assume that the character be 'retired' so that it becomes part of the game rather than still being under player control.

Bard

Cleric/Paladin/Shadow Knight
Avatar. Becomes the hand of whatever diety they pledged their lives to.
Druid
Forest spirit/guardian. Becomes some force behind certain woodlands & other natural areas. Vague, but I think you get my picture.
Monk
Teacher
Necromancer
Lich
Shaman
Tribal Diety
Ranger/Warrior
Hero (sorry the best I could do). Perhaps magical arms & armor be created & named after the character.
Enchanter/Magician/Wizard
Also hard to come up with something (like Warrior/Ranger). Could be immortalized with the creation & naming of a spell or two.
Rogue
Hardest of all. The nature of the character is that they don't aspire to anything, they just live for wealth & adventure. What do thieves do when they 'retire'? Settle down & become repectable?
