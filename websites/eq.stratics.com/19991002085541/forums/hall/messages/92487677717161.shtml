...watch your stamina. I swing the Screaming Mace about and if it's a long fight with many mobs then by the end I've zero stamina left. Even fighting a single mob I can watch my Stamina decrease with each swing. I tend to switch out my mace for another piercing weapon when it looks like my Stamina will get too low. I've also noticed that at about 66% Stamina loss you start to swing weapons noticably slower.

So with a melee type character I would believe a high Stamina might be a good thing for these reasons. I've not used 1H Slash, 2H Slash, or 2H Blunt so do not know if the Stamina is effected similarly as when using the 1H Blunt.
