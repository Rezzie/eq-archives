I've been playing EQ for about the last month and a half or so an I have gotten bored with it now. After you explore many areas and create some characters of different races and classes,it just gets boring. Once you get a high level their are no challenges in the game. No huge meeting place where you can meet friends and no broadcasting so you can get to know people. All this game really is is a Nintendo game played online. Verant is also the most unreliable company I have ever seen with the worst customer support. Their are better games out there where you can actually get to know people and not just play the game but be a part of it. Go to the address below (cut and paste) to see a game where you are a part of it. It may not look like EQs shaped oup of plastic 3d look but its a much better game. The price is a little steep now but it'll change soon.

www.3do.com/meridian
