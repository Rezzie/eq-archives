We usually assume all loot is party loot, whether it be a rusty dagger or a Holy Sword. I always expect the party leader to make sure that each and every member of the party gets a fair share of the loot. I have found that the master looting system works far better than alpha loot.

If we are camping for a major item, we usually know who is going to get it before we ever start to camp for the item. We often times have already determined what compensation he will pay the party in return for helping get the item. Proper communication and a strong party leader are usually the best remedy for any loot problems.
