Half-elf and Follower of Tunare

Author's Note: I am not the best player by far to write a guide. I still play and learn more everyday about the game of Everquest. After having tried a character with every race and every class (honestly it took me FOREVER), I liked my Half Elf the best.

Here is a brief guide for early level players. I recommend starting in Qeynos. The Qeynos Hills and North Qeynos are excellent "newbie gardens," and when you want you can always just shout for a quick teleport to Greater Faydark.

First off, you will want to kill bats, large rats, and decaying skeletons whenever you see them. Always try to be within running distance of the guards for the times when things get rough. Getting to level two takes about ten to twenty minutes if you fight everything you see.

After reaching level two, you stand a better chance against decaying skeletons with weapons (and decent loot). If you have a good two-hand slashing weapon as a ranger or warrior, kill scarabs.

Getting to level three should also take very little time, and getting to level four is a little tougher, but not by much.

Con everything you see by simply right clicking them. I stuck to scarabs myself, but if you play smart you can try to kill large bats.

At level five its time to head to Blackburrow. You can stay at Blackburrow for a long time collecting gnoll teeth and turning them in to the Warrior Trainer in the Arena of Qeynos for cash and exp. (YAY). By now you should be able to obtain Patchwork Armor. There is almost always a kind tailor that will make you armor if you supply him with pelts. Now I personally try to stay away from wolves because it is against our nature as a Half Elf but if you must slay them, I'm sure Tunare can forgive you just this once. : )

By level eight, I was sufficiently bored with Blackburrow and needed more excitement, so I decided to head over to the ruins. I was definitely too weak to take on the monsters there, but there is always a high level character waiting there for the elusive Pysjin and you could take advantage of this by asking for a couple high level buffs. Pysjin is a 16th level monster, and I always found a shaman, druid, and sometimes a paladin willing to buff me. Then, I would kill real skeletons, and if could find a kind healer I would go after the Putrid Skeletons. This was quite hard and I did this until level 10.

I felt that it was time for me to leave my home in search of better things. I would send a nice tell to every high level wizard I saw for a teleport to Greater Faydark. At Greater Faydark, I would go to Crush Bone (a place very similar to Black Burrow), Butcher Block, and Steamfront. All of these are very good zones for hunting.

That is about where I am up to, and I hope I have helped in your journey to become a great Half Elf!

GOOD LUCK!
