I'm sick of this 25% exp bonus BS that gets tossed in our faces ever damn time the subject about a warrior is brought up.

The fact of the matter is that even if we got a 50% exp bonus to solo it wouldn't be enough to make up for our down time.

Two senarios. A paladin fights a MOB and warrior fights an equivalent MOB. Both get out at half health, paladin with most Mana gone. The sit down (at lvl 20 after we get to heal at 30pts a min instead of 20), Warrior sits for 15mins to heal up, paladin sits for a couple mins gets mana up, heals himself mostly, sits down another few mins and is up in under 5 mins. Thus he gets to do 3 times as much fighting do to the down time of soloing and nobody healing, (this is similar for most comparisons of warrior to other races.) Thus if you want to even it out we need to 3 times the exp or a 200% exp bonus for soloing. I know this sounds absurd but the fact of the matter is we do suck soloing do to down time and no kiting or root and Nuke or Pet take all the damage stuff and no healing abilities.

Note I'm not asking for any of these as a warrior. Warriors are suppose to be played as a group member and I'm happy with that fact. I don't want the stupid extra HP they claim they're going to implement (wonder how cheap they're going to be.) I wish verant would listen to our requests and not try to fix it with things that don't exactly make sense, Why the hell should a warrior have good magic resistance, I didn't expect to have good magic resistance going into the game as a warrior.

Warriors really just asked for speciallization of weapons as a main request, thats not an unreasonable request.
