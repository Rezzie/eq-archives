Will o' Wisp

Zones:
Qeynos Hills
East Commonlands
West Commonlands
Kelethin
Nektulos Forest
Toxulia
Greater Faydark

Level: 10-12

Faction:
No Faction - Pack

Abilities:
Invulnerable to Normal Weapons, Parry

Loot:
Cash, Burnout Lightstones, Lightstones, Greater Lightstones
Description:
Looking for all the world like a harmless little ball of blue light, these guys pretty much define the 'small but deadly' creature class. Anyway, if you want to go after one get your magic weapon ready, cause you are gonna need it. These critters are pretty much spellcaster only otherwise. While wisps are sought after for their lightstones, they also have burned out lightstones, which is the most common loot. They act as a normal lightstone but vanish when you log off, also they can't be sold or traded.
