------Dawn breaks onto a new land. A dawn so bright and full of hope as if magnified through the wings of the crystalline dragon that brought the seeds of life to a once barren planet. Standing tall in this brave new world, bold men and women devoted to the codex they live by and would die protecting. JUSTICE, so that all lawful persons may one day walk the lands of Norrath without fear. UNITY, kinship between races and religions, joining in a common cause to rid our lands of the denizens of darkness and the will to sacrifice all that they are for the sake of others. NOBILITY, having the utmost respect and compassion for others, not because of their station in life but for their actions which define their lives. TRUTH, the never ending persuit of wisdom to guide our lives along the path of goodness to the betterment of our fellow citizens. HONOR, conducting oneself with bravery in the face of battle and integrity in one's dealings with others.

-----Sacred Haven is dedicated not to itself but to these ideals which we hold dear. Striving to control the destiny of Norrath so we may provide a haven of virtue in a sea of discord. If you'd like to be a part of this ever lasting quest, please check out the web page at:
