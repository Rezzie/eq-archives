Greetings Erudite fellows and brothers of magic !

I'm Artac, a Wizard from Erudin and a follower of Solusek Ro. We, the High Men, are a race inclined towards the use of magic. We are the masters of the arcane arts. Magic flows in our blood since our birth. Yes, it's true, others races have magic users. But they are only children dabbling with powers they don't understand. WE ARE THE ONLY TRUE MAGES in Norrath, we have the willpower to master all the secrets and powers magic has to offer us. We should not tolerate other races to boast about their so called mastery of magic. We should strive to become as powerful and as knowledgeable in magic as we can. It is an holy duty to us. None shall stand in our way to the true source of power in Norrath. I suggest we join in a Guild, the Brotherhood of High Magic, to improve our skills in magic and to teach the other races who are the true masters of magic. Here's the charter we shall defend.

- Each Brother must strive to become a master of magic, whatever the odds.
- Each Brother must help and support other guildmembers to become better mages.
- Each Brother must share his knowledge with all guild members. We shall all work together like one.
- Each Brother must investigate every opportunity to learn a new spell, to gain new magic powers and to get new magical items.
- Each Brother should prevent other races from stealing or learning magic secrets not designed for them to know.
- Each Brother should confront in a duel magic-users of others races to teach them who are the true masters of magci. Let them live to tell the others about their defeat. Kill them if can't understand the lesson.
- Each Brother should protect by all means Erudin and the High Men from the lesser races.

If you're a Erudite Magic-User from Bristlebane, join us. We could create a powerful Guild which will inspire respect in all other races of Norrath. This charter is only a proposition. So Brothers, if you're interested in becoming a future member of the Brotherhood of High Magic, contact me with magic internet communication (email) or meet me in Erudin on Bristlebane.

Artac, Mage and Follower of Solusek Ro
