Just make sure you put one point into meditate at level four, and in every skill that you gain at later levels.
If you plan on being an adventurer, and not picking up a trade at all, then apply the points to whatever of the skills you use while fighting that go up the least(IE: 2H Blunt for your greater staff, which you should have by now)
