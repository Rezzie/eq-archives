EQ locks all other programs out while its running, therefore you cannot Alt-Tab out to any other programs (in an effort to abate 3rd party cheat programs)
ICQ is also a semi-CPU hog..therefore its in your best interest to close ICQ before running EQ, or atleast put yourself in NA mode since when you receive messages while playing EQ your HD will probably thrash around for a bit causeing major slowdowns

EQ's habit of locking out all other programs also caused me to litteraly remove those damn annoying Win95 hotkeys from my keyboard (who uses those anyways?) , as you cannot alt-tab out to the desktop, but it seems your computer will still accept keyboard commands outside of EQ...so if i hit the Start menu shortcut key and the down arrow a few times then enter..itll launch whatever item was highlighted, though i cannot see what is happening or even close what was just launched since im locked in EQ...

summary : close ICQ before running EQ (IMO)
