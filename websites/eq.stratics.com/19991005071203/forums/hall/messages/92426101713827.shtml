All the "inns" have a guard, or most of em do. They are usually somewhere in the area killing all the little critters around the building. The simple answer to this is put a guard inside the inn. Let me back up a sec.

The reason Verante doesn't want to do anything about this is because the inns would be flooded with PC's sitting and resting safely. That is not what they want, right or wrong.

So, all they need to do is post a guard inside each inn, or inside each merchant shop, etc. All that guard is for is to usher out anyone that stays inside longer than a given amount of time. If anyone has been to Halas, the bank is a guy in an inn, with two guards around him. Do the same thing. If someone comes in and sits down, have the guard warn him once, then kill him. Problem solved. If you are truely there to sell and buy stuff, you would not be bothered. If you are trying to heal safely, the guard will kill you.

My only problem with the innkeepers, is how sometimes they back up into the wall, literally. You can't buy or sell, even from either side of the wall. Then sometimes they are on the roof, or just standing in place just outside the walls. They just need to tweak that a bit, shouldn't be tough fix. Neither would the guard thing, just keep the guard inside, he doesn't have to have faction or race prejudice, just purely a bouncer. WEll thats it.
