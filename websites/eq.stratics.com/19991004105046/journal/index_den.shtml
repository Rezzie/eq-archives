A Week of Norrathian Wine - Introduction

Seven Days Playing EverQuest
During Phase 3 of the EverQuest closed beta I engaged in a week-long frenzy of EQ-induced insanity. I set everything aside and bowed down before the next big massively-multiplayer online RPG... my job, my girlfriend, my regular living schedule--all normality was sacrificed in order to allow me to devote seven days towards EverQuest. During this week I played the game for the first time using a standard beta account, on a standard computer, with a standard video card, over a standard ISP, in order to present to those people without beta accounts the closest possible alternative to actually playing the game.

Over these seven days playing EverQuest I logged over 50 in-game hours and snapped 100+ screenshots while exploring all three of the continents available to players. Due to the sheer time spent journeying the lands and searching for new sights, I spent less time than normal trying to gain levels. As a result, this journal is not an accurate indication of the average amount of time needed to gain levels, although it is completely reliable in all other regards.

The account of these journeys are contained within multiple entries made to this journal and organized as follows:

  1. Day One
  2. Day Two
  3. Day Three
  4. Day Four
  5. Day Five
  6. Day Six
  7. Day Seven
  8. Afterword
It was a cold Monday evening when this all began, and Midnight meant the beginning of my seven day EverQuest marathon. For the remainder of that week I spent nearly every single waking hour staring at my monitor and performing an activity somehow related to EQ at all possible moments. Work? Friends? Family? Relationship? Food? Sleep? A life? Who needs 'em! Okay, well, maybe food and sleep... but the rest had to wait until the following Monday.

As the inaugural act of me becoming the first EQ player to dive into the depths of true EverQuest madness (at least under the all-consuming public eye), I presented to the readers of the EQSS the first entry of my journal. During those seven days I devoted my waking life towards playing the game and reporting all of what I found back to the readers of Stratics through this log of my first seven days playing EverQuest, which was updated several times each day. Here, presented to you, is the totality of my week of Norrathian wine.
