Fight monsters. It's the best way. Fighting monsters while using trade skills can work too, but just plain fishing is a waste of time, really. Lets see, while you're fishing you make 2 silver for each fish you catch, and you might catch a fish each 30 seconds.. if you go hunting monsters, though, you can make at least that much money per kill around level 6 or 7 by fighting humanoid foes, and while you won't get 1 kill each 30 seconds, you will gain experience. Now, while you're hunting these monsters, you can use your trade skills, like Tailoring to make armour out of cat or wolf pelts you get. That'll let you make even more money. But just fishing is a waste of time.. the only way fishing should be used is to keep a pole and bait in your bank, and use them to make a nest egg should you ever die and lose all your equipment without a chance of getting it back. In all other cases, you'll make more money by killing monsters.

I'm not dissing the trade skills, I'm a practicing fletcher myself, just that as stand-alone skills, you're not likely to make as much money as you would by just fighting. So if you want to make the most money, fight. You can't do like you can in UO and buy a full set of platemail before fighting a single monster.
