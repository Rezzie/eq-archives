Well both have their advantages I guess .

However I remember the days of being a minor in UO . Pking was a real problem as hordes of cowardess PKers would take out the mines and kill all those wishing to make an honest living . EQ does not have a PK issue as you have a choice to be a pker or not . Even if you choose to be a pker you can't kill anyone who has not turned on their PK switch . Of course once the PK switch is turned on its not like you can turn it off easily .

Battle-- fighting monster critters etc is way better than UO . Not to mention the graphics which absolutley kill UO . If you have a decent system P166MMX 64MB and a good 3d Card and 33.6 Modem than you have the minimum requirements . Like I said if u have the system then the graphics will blow your mind with no increase lag .

One draw back is that you cannot buy houses etc - YET . I heard they would incorporate this into the game . however if they do man I hope they dont make it to easy to get a house or something .

In all aspects EQ is superior to UO . However UO does have some flexibilty IE able to make your own clothes and color them the way you want . Becoming a Tailor or some kind of merchant trade is alot easier in UO than it is in EQ . Granted EQ will probably improve this somewhat . Also if you are into merchanting in EQ , it is geared towards you selling to other players because the prices you get from the NPC's are usually below what it costs you to make the item in the first place . So no making a 100 small sheilds and then selling it to the merchants in EQ thats for sure .

Of course there are some issues with EQ . For example the camping of respawn spots , that is a real issue currently .
