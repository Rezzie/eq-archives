Shaman Spells Analysis

 Offensive   Altering 

Offensive Magic
Many people in our lands underestimate the role a Shaman plays in the overall scope of life. Some consider them tribal witch doctors, while others consider them second rate casters. This could not be further from the truth.Shamans, being from mostly underdeveloped races, tend to rely more on spell components than other casters. This however is a minor concern when one looks at the true power of Shamans.

The Shaman stands out in two ways. First is in their ability to deal damage to their enemies. While not as powerful as the Wizard, Shamans should not be overlooked in this area of power. Not only do they posses spells to damage specific targets, but they can also do damage over a wider area at once, as well as wield spells that hurt their enemies slowly over time.Their second area of expertise is in enhancing the abilities of those around them, and hampering the abilities of their enemies.

Let us first look at the Shaman's power in dealing magical damage.

Rank I

Burst of Flame

Your basic direct damage spell. Burst of flame allows the Shaman to hurl a small fireball at his or her target. Not the most devastating spell, but effective nonetheless.

Flash of Light

Blinds your target momentarily, making them unable to combat effectively.

Rank II

Frost Rift

Frost Rift will be your mainstay direct damage spell, only replaced by Burst of Flame when mana runs low.

Sicken

Your basic disease attack. Sicken will do two things. It will damage them slowly over a period of time, and it will make them diseased thereby slowing their ability to heal.

Rank III

Tainted Breath

Tainted breath is a nasty spell that poisons your target, causing them to be slowly destroyed from the inside out over time.

Rank V

Affliction

Affliction is basically a stronger, more efficient version of the spell Sicken. You will want top throw this at them right at the start of battle. This way they will be suffering its effects while you regain mana.

Infectious Cloud

Nearly as deadly as Affliction, but this spell's effects are spread out of an area around the target. Very useful for multiple engagements.

Altering Magic
Next, lets analyze the Shaman's ability to alter the behaviors of others around them, both for the good and bad of the target.

Rank I

Strengthen

Right away you have the power to make others exceed the limits of their mortal shell. Strengthen makes the target of this spell even stronger than nature intended.

Endure Cold

Increases the target's capability to withstand cold and cold based attacks. This includes the occasional blizzard that sweeps through the Everfrost Plains.

Dexterous

Increases the target's dexterity. Spells such as this, Strengthen, Feet like Cat, and others that increase the target's natural attributes are highly prized and especially useful to Monks who strive to make their bodies pure weapons.

Inner Fire

Adds to the targets Armor Class, maximum hit Points and Stamina.

Rank II

Endure Fire

Increases the target's ability to withstand fire and fire based attacks. Never jump into Lava without this one. On second though, never jump into lava period.

Feet Like Cat

Further increases the target's natural Agility.

Scale Skin

This spell will increase the target's armor class by causing them to actually grow scales over their skin.

Drowsy

Makes the target drowsy, thereby reducing his or her attack speed significantly.

Rank III

Spirit Sight

Allows you and others to be able to see invisible beings.

Endure Disease

Increases the target's ability to withstand disease and disease related attacks.

Serpent Sight

This spell is treasured by Humans, Erudites, and Barbarians as it allows them to see in the dark without use of a light source.

Spirit of Wolf

A highly prized enhancement, Spirit of Wolf allows the target to run even faster than normal, and boosts his or her ability to withstand fatigue as well.

Spirit of Bear

Increases the target's stamina beyond normal means.

Rank IV

Enduring Breath

A must have for underwater travel, Enduring Breath allows the target to no longer require oxygen.

Endure Poison

Increases the target's ability to withstand poison and poison based attacks.

Burst of Strength

Grants your target a momentary burst of strength and armor class. This burst lasts for about 18 seconds.

Turtle Skin

Increases your target's armor class by making them grow a magical shell.

Spirit of Snake

Increases the target's Charisma.

Disempower

A devastating spell to be cast on your enemies. Disempower will lower their strength, armor class, and stamina.

Walking Sleep

Walking Sleep is a more powerful version of the Drowsy spell. It will even further slow the target's attack speed.

Rank V

Spirit of Monkey

Increases the target's Dexterity to superhuman levels. Cast this on a powerful monk and watch them go crazy with love for you.

Spirit Strength

Increase the target's Strength even more so than the strengthen spell. Again a favorite of monks and warriors.

Endure Magic

Increases the target's ability to withstand magical attacks.

Malice

Lowers the target's resistances all across the board. Hit them with this first and they will be ripe for your disease and poison attacks.

Insidious Fever

Makes the target more susceptible to disease, and hits them with a bit of damage.
