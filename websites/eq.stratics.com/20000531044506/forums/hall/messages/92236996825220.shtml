Yes, there are ore blocks and other stuff for sale in Neriak, but your going to be suprised at where you find all your stuff. Almost the complete smithing gear seleciton is there.

The books are sold in the Foreign Quarter, specifically in the Ogre/Troll slum by two Ogre women on the bottom of the entrance ramp.

The ore is sold...in the Blind Fish bar? Yup, they're downstairs by the dartboards. So are the sharpening stones.

Check around. You find the weirdest stuff in the wierdest places. A bartender upstairs in the toadstool bar/inn sells banded armor molds (don't try forging banded armor till 70-80 skill).

...And the rock dervishes in the Lavastorm mountains drop ore as loot. Level 10 monsters. Lavastorm is very close to Neriak's door. It is also cut off fromt the rest of the world since Nektelos forest has the only entrance. Little competition.
