Yup you get it at 9. You do need a shield. You must go to the warriors guild and train for at least 1 pt before you can use it. It is mutually exclusive from your kick (i.e. you can only do one or the other in a given time period). I believe (but have not been able to validate) that you can stun with this skill.

I have made hot buttons of my kick next to my bash, depending on the situation I alternate (groups really like it if you bash and sword strike...it slows the critter down and makes their lives easier....also if you are saving someone else the old smash/taunt combo seems to get the offending monsters attention).
