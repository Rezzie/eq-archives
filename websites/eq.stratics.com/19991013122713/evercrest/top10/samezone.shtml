Top 10 Signs That You're in the Same Zone Too Much

10. You've started to ask others how much they are paying in rent.

9. You're getting ready to hang pest-control strips at the local spawn points.

8. You refer to the MOB-in-charge as The Landlord.

7. You complain that you can't get enough swimming practice without a larger water feature.

6. The NPC's are beginning to respond to your hails.

5. You think things would be better if there was a Zone Tenants Association.

4. The trains used to bother you but now you don't seem to notice them so much.

3. Just the other day you were complaining about how the zone has gone all to hell since those newbies moved in.

2. You've petitioned to get a Bank and few Merchants put in to cut down on your "commute time".

And the number one sign you're in the same zone too much...

1. You often shout "COULD YOU HOLD IT DOWN OVER THERE!? I'M TRYING TO MED!"
