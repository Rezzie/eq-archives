Hi - I run a dwarf called Angaar on the Cazic Thule server. At 06.30 GMT 04/29, (about 10.30 PST 04/28?) Almost instantly it happens again - only this time when I eventually get back, I am at the bottom of the Ocean of Tears (how aptly named).

So if anybody from the GM's or Verant reads this, the way I see it there are three possible ways you could help me out of this.

1) Restore Angaar as of prior to 10:00 pm PST 04/28. (Don't know how feasible this is).
2) A GM loot or recover the corpse on my behalf.
or
3) Most of what I had could be fairly easily replaced - credit Angaar with 60 plat. (Mostly banded armour, a 1h Dwarven Axe and a Mino Axe).

Like I say I'm not a whiner, but I've invested some 250 hours in this character, and I hope the staffies can help me
