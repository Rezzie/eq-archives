As far as INT vs Mana, its better to have INT with a mage. This will do several things ::
1. Make your skill in a given area go up faster
2. Give you Mana, most likely more than adding a mana bonus of same amount
3. Give you bragging rights to all your friends. :)

As far as wisdom goes, there's some speculation that higher wisdom gives you an advantage at getting more exp off monsters, but it remains to be proven.. So go for INT and screw WIS.

For a Mage, good item to have is the Blk Iron Medallion (From Gobbie King in Runnyeye) This gives a bonus to your INT and WIS of +5. It is this way to make it useful to other classes aside from magi such as clerics, druids, and shamans.
