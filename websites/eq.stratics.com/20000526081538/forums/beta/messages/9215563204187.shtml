In Reply to: The opening of Everquest is being VERY poorly handled. (txt) posted by Yzen Danek on March 15, 1999 at 10:03:42 PM EST:

It won't be a race as long as you don't make it one. Take your time and have fun with it. You will never be the most powerful/highest level character around, there's always somebody who has more time to play, trust me on that :)

You can even still use the race analogy, but instead of treating it like the 100 yard dash, treat it like a marathon. Most everybody starts in a big clump, though some are even at a disadvantage because they start in the back. Once the race is underway the crowd spreads out, and each person proceeds at the pace which best suits their training/style.

The greater thing about EQ though is that you don't need to even to worry about the 15 miles you have left, but then fun you'll have along the way. Remember "Its not the destination, its the Journey." I saw that on a poster somewhere.

-Fyndlorn,
PS- Have fun and take it slow :)
