The whole economy in Norrath is totally contrived in order to fit the progression that Verant has in mind, rather than being based on some realistic model. For example, for people who make bows, the difference between a piece of hickory and a piece of ash (no pun intended) is like 16pp or so. This is done so that the nicer bows made with the ash will be affordable by high level characters only, but has no realistic basis whatsoever. This kind of thing is present throughout the game; the price of items reflects only Verant's opinion of how far into the game a character should be before they can afford them, not the difficulty of making them. Heres another good example: if you've worked metals before, you know that making something that is light, intricate and strong like chainmail is actually harder than banging out sheets into plate. Yet, the price difference between these armor types in astronomical. Bah.
