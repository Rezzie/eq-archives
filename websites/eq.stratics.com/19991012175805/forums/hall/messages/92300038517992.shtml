If you Hail Tumpy, he will say something like "ask if you don't find what you need". If you ask him for Tumpy Tonics. He will tell you the ingredients. One ingredient you need to get by taking the boat to the island (of women) in the Ocean of Tears. If you are quick, you can make your purchase and race back to the boat if you want to continue on to Freeport, or wait another 15 minutes to catch it on the way back. The other ingredient can be found a lot of shops.

Also, pay attention to exactly what Everhot wants.
