Rabid Wolf

Zones:
Qeynos Hills

Behavior:
Aggressive

Abilities:
Disease

Loot:
Pelts of varying quality
If you can bitten and can't resist you will be diseased. I think it takes about five to ten minutes for it to wear off, but it feels like an eternity. For the duration of the disease you will heal at an extremely slow rate, so slow you probably won't even notice your health increasing.

It should be noted that these creatures are under the protection of the wandering Rangers of Qeynos Hill. These rangers will consider you a poacher and kill you onsite despite the fact that you are putting a diseased creature out of its misery.
