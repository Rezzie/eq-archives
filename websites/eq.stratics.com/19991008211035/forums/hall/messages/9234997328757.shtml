Last week I came down with a cold and my job didn't want me in the office because I would get everyone else sick. Actually, in the 1/2 hour I was at work on that Monday I was contagious enough to get 1 other person sick and they weren't very happy with me when they made it back to work.

Anyways, from Monday through Thursday I had nothing to do but play the game and sleep, I could drink fluids while playing. During those 4 days I went from a level 6 dwarven paladin to a level 9 dwarven paladin. That is an advancement from 1 bubble into 6 all the way to 1 1/2 bubbles from level 10. That's an average of a level a day.

If those people are playing 24/7 I can understand how they have advanced to level 30 especially if they are playing characters that don't have experience penalties (paladins, rangers). A level a day should be possible. Although, they have no like, but that is their choice.


In regards to having full chain at level 12, that is another story. I have only completed my 2 quests once. Although, If I am in a group and some members of the group would also like that item I will stay part of the group until they accomplish it too. The thing I hate most is when someone is part of a group, gets a great piece of loot and then disbands or worse yet they then auction it off to the highest bidder.
