Mana is not nearly important for a magician as it is for Wizards -- most of our mana should be spent before fighting begins, then put into shields during initial fight, then as finishing -- with some reserved for emergencies. Stamina -- HPS-- are the one thing that might keeps us alive should a mob attack us while the fighters pry us off them. Agility is of little use to magicians, as if we're meleeing that's a bad sign. Strenghth, Dex, dito. We don't need much money, summoners that we are (I'm lvl 16 and i've already enough to pay for all my lvl 20 spells -- and no spell cost more than 30 plat, and by the time I reach that stage I'll have plenty of cash), so charisma is not important.

Thus, primary stats for magicians: intelligence and stamina. I threw some into wisdom as well for saves and spell resistance.
