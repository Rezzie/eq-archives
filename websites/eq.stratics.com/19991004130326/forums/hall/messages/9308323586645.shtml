I'm sure there are more, but here are three map exploits that I'm aware of where you can attack a mob and not be hit in return. (and it's taken me a long time to get to lv15, so I ain't been usin' these here cheats myself :)

1. S. Ro: first hill after you zone into S Ro from Oasis. if you attack a mob down the south side of the hill, it cannot get to you after you climb up a certain height.

2. Misty Thicket: Runnyeye cave entrance, on the side. goblins will run at the wall, and you can cast at them.

3. Highkeep: this one's a whopper. you get up on a balcony of the castle entrance, and (at least I've seen this work for a wizard) you can zap the high elf NPC from a safe distance.
