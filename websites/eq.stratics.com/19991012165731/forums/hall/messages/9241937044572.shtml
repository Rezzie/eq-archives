While I play a paladin and if we ever cross paths I shall slay you out of hand, I have to say that your harm touch is lacking. Even at 50th level it is going to do a measly 500 points of damage, I know that sounds like alot, (and it is) but mobs at 50th level have like 2000 hit points and stuff (maybe more), and 500 points might win the day if its close, but mobs hitting you for 140 points a pop (double attacking you for another 140) and your toast pretty quick. The harm touch is, IMHO, not comparable to my Lay Hands. Lay hands heals 1000 points of damage, and I dont have to worry about having more that that till level 30 or so. I think what Harm Touch Should do is act like a vampiric embrace spell where it transfers the HP to you, that way it would make it more viable. What do you think?
