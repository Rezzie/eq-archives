I'll apologize now for the nature of this note. I'm just very upset at what I've seen over the last 2 weeks in EQ, and need to get it off my shoulders, unload it.

Level 30+ characters- How can this be? In beta leveling didn't occur at near this rate. I'm near twenty myself, but have admittedly been playing in all my spare time since release. The fact that there are 30+ level characters tells me something is wrong with the game. There are loopholes allowing easy exp at low risk. We aren't talking about 1 or 2 freaks with out lives..I mean hundreds of people are in or very the 30's in level.

Level 15 characters in full ring/chain? What the hell? These guys weren't given this armor by others, I know how they got it, and its flat out ridiculous. I cry when I see when I see a group of characters in their teens strutting into town /shouting how bad they are, decked out head to toe in ring mail. Its sad, truly sad. The games only a month old for Christs sake.

Questing allowing duplcable rewards. Someone sold me a ring this morning, then chuckled and said he could get another by simply talking to the npc who gave it to him as a reward. He didn't have to complete the quest again. This is a quest in RiverVale..enough said.


I'm not pointing the finger particularly in any direction. I'm not saying I hate this game, cause I don't, I LOVE this game. But it really cheapens the rewards for hard work to me, when I know someone is selling the item duped for didly squat, that I've been questing all week for.
