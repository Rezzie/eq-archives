Just a couple of things to remember:
1. Spells last longer with higher levels. The person that cast center on you, was this person higher level than you? If so, that could account for some of the extra duration.
2. New spells never seem to last as long as one you have had for a while, I think it has something to do with you getting better at casting the spell.
3. This is more for the 14th level cleric below, but- you WILL fizzle many times more often with a new spell. Every time I get new spells I fail at LEAST 3 or 4 times more often than I cast successfully until I have used it enough. The more you use the spell, the more often you cast it successfully. It will also cast more quickly the more often you cast it.
Now these things may or may not have affected your situations, I don't really know. But those are just a couple of observations I have made that I think are worth keeping in mind.
