Moonshadow - Assistant Project Manager


Real Name
Monica - No jokes please, I heard them all!

Age and Gender
Female, and old enough to know better

City, State/Region, Country
Toronto, Ontario, Canada

Hobies/Interests
Not listed.

How long have you been playing EverQuest?
I started playing Everquest March 26,1999

How long have you been volunteering for EQSS?
I began volunteering at Stratics in July or August of 1999

What do you do for EQSS?
I moderate the Westwood Tavern, and the Rallos Zek Forums.

Note: When I asked Moonshadow for her Bio information, I hadn't talked to her about the APM role yet. The day before her bio came up in the queue, she became the EQSS APM. - Greebo

Why do you continue to play EverQuest?
I continue to play EQ because I simply love the game and the people who play it. It's as simple and as complex as that.

What one thing would you change about EverQuest if you could?
If I could change EverQuest, I think I would make the "Zek's" more different from the rest of Norrath than it is now. I realize that this would be a horrid decision for Verant to make (lots of resources expended for minmal return) but I think having PvP work differently from the Blue world would enchance the Zek world enhance the "different experience".

Name one thing about you that makes you unique.
Good lord, I'm a 32yr old mother of two and I've played RPG's all my life, isn't that different enough? ;-)

*Personal information such as name, gender, age, address disclosed only with permission. If information in an EQSS Bio is not complete, this is by request of the staff member.

Greebo's Remarks :

Always charming, graceful and elegant, I can't name anyone who "gets" what we're about at EQSS better than she does, and I'm absolutely thrilled that she's agreed to be the Assistant Project Manager. Thank you for being such a fantastic part of EQSS, Moon!
