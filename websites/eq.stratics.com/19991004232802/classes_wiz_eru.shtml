Playing an Erudite Wizard

 Introduction   About   Creation   Levels 1 & 2   Levels 3 & 4   Levels 5 & 6 

Introduction
Hello all! During phase 4, the first character I started out as was the Erudite wizard. I have made this little guide to help all you new people who may want to be a wizard. This help document is based on my Erudite wizard (who starts in Erudin). This is all a suggestion and by no means the only way.
 top


About
The wizard is a master of the arcane arts of damage (don't expect to get healing spells). With a few waves of a wizards hand, he/she can do startling amounts of damage. But a word of caution, wizards are one of the most anti-melee characters around and are not for close combat.
 top


Creation
The one thing that a wizard uses the most is his mana, which comes from his intelligence. I started my character as an Erudite, (Erudite wizards gain levels at a very SLOW rate) with maximum intelligence, which gives you the most mana and the ability to learn skills the fastest. I then added my additional points to stamina.
 top


Levels 1 & 2
The first thing you want to do is to enter your guild, which is located at the pond near the Erudin armory. Go through the teleporter to the palace. (Its the one over the small water fountain with the steps leading up to it.) Get your guild robe and put it on (one of the most important thing a wizard needs at low levels is some armor because he starts with very limited life.) Next you have to memorize your spells in the hotkey spells slot. I would advise not to buy any first level 1 spells, because they are a waste of money. Now your ready to adventure into the forest! The Erudin Forest is very large and you can become easily lost (trust me on that, I know from experience). I would suggest attacking snakes and decaying skeletons because anything else is too hard until level 2. When you attack something, always use your Shock of Frost spell, and make sure you have your shielding spell on. After reaching level 2, start attacking kobolt runts. Word of caution: You are not a tank. It is hard for wizard to attack 2 things at once at low levels. Make sure when attacking kobolts, that no other kobolts are in the immediate area, or you will end up getting overwhelmed (same goes for skeletons). One other thing: DO NOT CROSS THE BRIDGE TO THE SECOND ISLAND! YOU WILL HAVE "A CAN OF WHOOP ASS" OPENED ON YOU!
 top


Levels 3 & 4
When you are level 3, continue to fight things that are white and blue to you when you use /con. Around this level I started buying my cloth armor, so I didn't have to mess with it later on. When you reach level 4, a whole new HUGE world opens up to you. The second you gain level 4, run straight to the guild house and learn the skill "Meditate." I would suggest putting 1 skill point towards this skill, due to how you learn skills fast. After learning this skill, buy Shock of Fire, Root, and Gate as soon as possible (in that order)! You now have the skill and experience to take on more than one creature at a time, but not much more.
 top


Levels 5 & 6
Ahh level 5. This is when you need to start fishing (I would advise to have at least 100 fishing by level 8) to get money for spells and the rest of your cloth armor (that is if you don't have a full set yet), because the next set of spells at level 8 costs around 5 platinum and can get expensive. Also, you might want to buy a staff soon so you can do more hand to hand damage. When you arrive at level 6, it is now time to cross the bridge! Don't you feel special? I still warn you that it can get pretty dangerous, and hope you have another person grouping with you. Remember to attack what is blue to you. If you have a friend in your group, then you could attack things that are probably white.

This is as far as I go. Good luck to you wizards and may you kick some serious monster butt !
