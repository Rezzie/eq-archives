Welcome to EverQuest Tricks of the Trades! This site is devoted to the trade skills and professions one can learn and master in the world of EverQuest. Simply follow one of the links to the left and you can learn how make your own armor, or weapons, or food, or almost anything you can think of! If you know how to make something that isn't listed here, please tell us how! Submit your knowledge, so all may benefit!

This site is newly renovated, and we're in the process of reviewing submissions. You'll see a lot more added to the site in the coming weeks, so bookmark us and keep checking back!

Also, to get the best possible viewing experience, make sure to use Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher, as we make use of some newer technologies such as frames and layers. It works fine with Netscape, but at some resoltions you may see the frames not matching up perfectly :(
