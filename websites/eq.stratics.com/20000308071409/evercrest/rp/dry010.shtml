Discovery
Fiction by Drysart

Days turned into weeks, which in turn passed into months. The guild all learned of Drysart and Vinalrii. Instead of being cold and unaccepting, as Drysart had originally feared, they accepted the dark elf with open arms, allowing her to sit in their tavern.

Drysart was happy that she was accepted. Being among the guild was the safest place for her to be. But, at the same time, he was deeply concerned. Vinalrii had fallen in love with the wizard, but his feelings were not the same for her. He would take care of both the forthcoming child and it's mother, but would he love her? He could never love a dark elf. It was just a matter of time before the issue came to a head...

*****

Rubicant sat alone in his bedchamber. Neither Drysart nor the page he carried were in his thoughts at the time. It was late, and he was preparing to retire for the evening when there was a knock at his door.

Vinalrii stepped through the door and into his bedchamber. By this time, her pregnancy was quite apparent. She spoke in an almost reverential tone, "Good evening, m'lord."

Rubicant glanced up at her and smiled warmly. He wrapped his arms around her and held her in a warm embrace. "How is the little one?" he asked, pulling himself away to look at Vinalrii's growing stomach.

She laughed slightly, "she's doing well, although I wish she would walk herself around at times, she's getting rather heavy."

Rubicant kneeled down and pressed his ear up to the child. He listened for a moment before standing. "What of the father? When will you bring him here?"

Vinalrii smiled devilishly, "soon enough."

*****

Drysart sat with Vinalrii at a table in the tavern. She looked at him, her eyes filling with tears, and a shocked expression on her face. He had just told her that he didn't share the love that she had for him. He knew what was coming.

She stood and stepped back. Drysart stood as well. The other patrons of the tavern took notice. He stepped forward and put his hand on her arm, trying to comfort her, "I will still take care of you. I've promised that, haven't I?"

Her eyes filled with anger. She felt cheated. Her voice raised to a yell as she pushed him to the ground, "Don't touch me! Leave me alone!" she stormed out the tavern door and ran right into the armored elves who had been about the enter the tavern.

*****

Drysart stood up from the floor and sat back down at the table. He put his head in his hands and wondered what he had just done.

Four high elves, outfitted in platemail, burst into the tavern. One of them held Vinalrii who was struggling fruitlessly to get away. Another one stepped forward and addressed the tavern.

"Good people, we are from the Royal Guard of the great city of Felwithe. It has come to our attention that one of you, Drysart Pennoble, has begat a child with this dark elf." He gestured to Vinalrii and looked over everyone in the tavern. "I suggest, Drysart, that you step forward."

Drysart looked over at the guard. Taking a deep breath, he stood and stepped toward the guard.

The guard looked at Drysart for a moment, then turned to two of the other guards. "Seize him."

The guards moved forward and grabbed Drysart. The guards with Drysart and Vinalrii left the tavern as the last guard apologized to the tavern for the disturbance, before returning with their prisoners to Felwithe.
