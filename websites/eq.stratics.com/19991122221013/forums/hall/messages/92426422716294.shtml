I've explored most of the zones of both Faydwer and Antonica. Here is a run down on my observations about which zones are the most and least popular.

Most Popular Wilderness Zone: Greater Faydark
Least Popular Wilderness Zone: East Karana.
Most Popular Dungeon: Toss up between Blackburrow and Crushbone
Least Popular Dungeon: Permafrost
Most Popular City Zone: Qeynos with Felwithe a close 2nd.
Least Popular City Zone: Difficult to say, but among the least popular are High Hold, Rivervale and Freeport

Other not so crowded zones include: Runnyeye, Oasis, Norther Ro, E/W Commons, Karanas, Beholder, Splitpaw, High Hold Pass. Some of the most popular zones are Butcherblock Mts, Steamfont, Lesser Faydark, E.Commons, Qeynos Hills, Misty Thicket.
