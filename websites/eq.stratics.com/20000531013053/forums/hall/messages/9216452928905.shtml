---One day, I was sitting at work, and I opened my browser and tried to go to eqss.stratics.com (true story). This was several months ago. I got the old "server could not be found" message. Within a moment I was flooded with "Why can't I get on?" "Where did the Internet go?" "What did you guys do?", etc. etc. ad nuseum.
---My Network Administrator told me he was getting absolutley NO pong from ANYTHING. Nothing. We had no idea what was going on. About an hour later (our customers were livid) a guy from MCI calls and said that a major phone line south of Chicago (we are in Southern Kentucky) had been severed. He told me that most of all Dial-up activity were dead. He gave NO ETR...:(
---About three hours later we were back up. I had a customer (a renowned idiot) yell at me on the phone that it was our fault. I gave him the truth, but he didn't believe me. He said he never had a problem before, but nothing would work now. Even though he couldn't even connect to AOL either, he insisted it was our fault.
---Now, I didn't walk up to Chicago and cut a phone line. So, it's not my fault. Things happen beyond our control. If you cannot accept that, then... well... It's your own fault you can't accept it.
