I myself have a full set of bronze plate, but it is easy to get for us Lg People, you just have to go to Najena and kill the Ogre Guard Captain several dozen times. Most of my plate is secondhand however, handed down by persons who cannot wear it or have fine steel plate.

Personally I feel that if you're so hung up on armor, start smithing!! For a mere 400 plat you can have a skill of 95 to 100 and be making your own banded mail which is only -1 AC per piece vs Platemail. It has the added bonus of not weighing a ton, which actually allows you to carry loot. It really sux when i have a weight of 118 with my full bronze, and my Shiny brass Hally. This allows me to carry food / water, a lightsource for my party, and the assorted plat / gold i find.. Its gotten that bad i drop silver and copper when i get it.

So go out, get some banded, and be happy. Its really cheap so if you dont want to make it, you can always buy it. I've seen full sets go for under 150 PP. and unlike leathermaking, you can get every single piece you can wear.. :)
