Let me first say that I totally agree with all your points. But this is what gets me through the day without bitching and moaning about how someone must have cheated or exploited.

1) Level 30??? So what? Are you in a race? Are you jealous? Worry about yourself and not others. There will always be powergamers, exploiters and cheaters. Deal with it and be true to yourself. Or else, become one of them. Either way, stop whining.

2) Who cares if other people have chain mail already? Good for them. This does not affect me. Why not worry about yourself instead of what others have? Do I detect envy? Jealousy? I hope not. I am level 12. I got a full set of Leather armor, a regular Halbard and 2 mino axes. ALL of which I saved up for or fought to get on my own or in a group. The only item handed to me was the Trainer shiled in CB by a high level Guildmember. I do not have Crushes armor, I do not have full ring mail, I do not have COMBINE weapon and I also do not whine about it. All those people near my level and below that posses these items, good for you! Personally, I have the satisfaction of knowing I earned my items (99% of my items) the hard way.

3) If there are exploits on the game then that is for Sony/Verant to deal with. If someone wants to abuse this in the meantime, who cares? Again, it does not affect me. When all the level 50 players are wondering around saying "What do I do now?" and I am level 25 or whatever, I'll still be having a ball. I'll enjoy that rare item I had to quest for, fight for and outright earn. I'll be laughing at the level 50's as they get bored to tears and make new characters.

This is not a flame, this is my mentality on how to deal with people who have exceedingly better items then they should have for their level, as well as all the high level characters that powergame to rise soo quickly. Doesn't affect me, doesn't bother, I will most likely not associate with them, and that is the bottom line!
