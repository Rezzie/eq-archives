Event on Vallon Zek
Vallon_Zek [ 2:55 AM PDT ] - Alethal

My good friend Diggles sent this one in
-Alethal

Event on Vallon Zek pvp teams server, Saturday May 20th 10pm EST

Flyin’ Fatties: An Iggles Production, Designed by Diggles
It is time for us to put a temporary end to the in team fighting and squabbling for a night blessed by the Father of Hate himself. On the eve of Saturday May 20th, 10 pm EST, Da Bashin’ Iggles shall bring forth a contest of skill and ability like no other: The Flyin’ Fatties. (This is an evil team only event. If you are a goodie and found you will be KOS)

We will meet at 10est at the outpost in Overthere. Casters will bind all melees and we shall march forth to the Gorge of the Scorpikis. Upon reaching the gorge all casters will bind themselves close by and the event shall begin!

Event 1: Single Fatty Distance Jump

This is only avaible to shaman above level 24. Each race troll and ogre will select 6 members to represent their race. The contestants will then soc (spirit of cheetah spell) up a designated ramp and attempt to hurl themselves the furthest into the gorge. The jumps will go in heats separated by a short period (1st troll in group vs. 1st ogre in group etc)

Event 2: Look at that blubber fly!

Again, 6 members of each race, troll and ogre, will be selected to represent their race. Contestants will be given levitate and on command hover out into the gorge, they will then cancel their levitate (via pumice) and plummet to the earth. The fatty remaining with the fewest amount of HP after the fall shall be deemed the winner. If no fatties survive the fall then…we will all have a good laugh!

Intermission

Gnome kabobs and the finest brews of Innoruuk shall be served for all to enjoy. 2 storytellers shall be selected to share some sort of interesting tale.

Event 3: Free Style

This event is open to all that come! Buffers will be available at the top of gorge to provide contestants with requested buffs. Contestants are then given a chance to freestyle to the bottom of the gorge. Judges will observe both take off, midair, and landing. Judging is completely subjective and high biased =).

Event 4: Drunken Stooper Race

12 contestants will be chosen of any race to park in the binge drinking contest/race. All contestants will be gathered at point A and required to drink X amount of a provided alcoholic beverage. They will then attempt to reach point B and /poke the judge there. They then race to the gorge and the first one to land shall be deemed the “Drunken Stooper King”.

Event 5: Bikini/Swimsuit Contest

9+ female contestants will be put through a series of trials to choose the “Queen of Hate” award winner. The judging panel will be made up of 9 males, 3 from each race. Female contestants my bribe, seduce, or otherwise manipulate the judges anyway they see fit. =)

Closing

Prizes will be awarded to the winning contestants and the remaining food and beverages shall be handed out. (need suggestion, possible another story telling). We will then conduct the duel of the millennium. Vllad vs. Tipp. The winner shall take home the title of the “THE FAT” and force to plunge to his death and “leave his mark” on Norrath, while the loser will retain “THE OBESE” title. The only rule is that outside buffing & healing IS NOT allowed, and the 2 contestants must not leave the circle of onlookers. Weapons, armor and items may be thrown or dropped into the ring from supporting factions.

Lastly there will be an open forum on any subjects to be address by the evil team. People can give their feedback on the event and/or split into groups to exp/pk/etc

===============================================================
Required positions!

Caterer and brewer! Expertise needed in lightie meat cooking and preparation. Brewing of Strong liquor a plus.
Head Priest(ess) and team of 2 or more revivers. Need to provide revives at the bottom of the gorge and heals.
Head enchanter(ess) and 2 assistants needed to clarity the cleric revivers and buffs for freestyle and look at that blubber fly contests. SoW men: 2 Lvl 9+ shaman. Need to sow respawned melees at outpost and non jboots contestants in the gorge. Great tips!
6 Judges to run event and judge contests!
9 Males to judge swimsuit contest. 3 of each race.
2 Story tellers
===============================================================
Contestant positions!

6 Troll shaman / 6 Ogre shaman (must be lvl 24 or higher)
6 Troll / 6 Ogre
12 drunkards
9+ Female models

If you would like to sign up for any positions drop by our webpage and reply to my thread on our msgboard. I can't guarntee you the position but it will help alot for planning. I'm hoping to have alot of fun!
