Playing a Human Wizard II

 Making your character   Starting out   Levels 1 - 3   Levels 5 - 13   Level 15 - 23   Level 23 - 38   Levels 38 - 40   Levels 40+ 

Making your character
A wizard's true powers lies in his ability to use magic, and to use magic, one must have mana. To acquire a sufficient amounts of mana, you must have intelligence. Since humans don't get nearly as much intelligence as erudites, dark elves, or the other races, I suggest you max out your intelligence and put 15 bonus points on your intelligence, making it 110. You should have 5 bonus points left over, I used these last 5 on stamina to boost my hit points a little bit.
 top


Starting out
I suggest you start out in the fair city of, and my hometown, freeport. You will start in west freeport, where the guild is located. Find Opal Darkbriar in the Academy of Arcane Sciences and give her your guild note. Put on your robe and wear it proudly, this is your first step in becoming a true wizard! Now you should get some training from your guild leader, put 3 points on evocation and 2 points on channeling. Before you start hunting, you must scribe and memmorize shock of frost and minor sheilding, the 2 spells that are given to you at the beginning. Now equip your dagger if you already haven't done so, and get ready to start hunting!

Levels 1 - 3
During my first 3 levels, I hunted in East Freeport. At level 1, you should hunt large rats, stone snakes, and bats. At level 2 and 3, you should hunt fire beetles and deathfist pawns. Beefore you begin fighting, check you health and mana to see if they are sufficient enough to fight another battle. Also, it is good to cast minor sheilding on yourself, this will give you a boost in health and armor class. Once you have checked everything, you must venture through the gates and find something to kill. To do this, you must target the enemy and cast your spell shock of frost. The enemy will run towards you, when it is within range, turn on your auto attack and hit it with your dagger. During the fight, you may want to cast a few more shock of frost's for good measure. You should have bought some extra level 1 spells by now, I bought frost bolt, numbing cold, and sphere of light.
 top


Levels 5 - 13
Now you have gotten the hang of casting and attacking! It is time to move out of east freeport and go to north ro. To go to north ro, just head straight out from the east freeport gates until you zone. As soon as you enter north ro, you will see a grassy area, this is where you want to hunt. From lvls 3 to 4, kill coyotes, fire beetles, spiderlings, armadillos, and decaying skeletons. Congratulations! once you get to level 4, you get your new spells! Buy shock of fire, root and gate first, shock of fire being your new attack spell, root which I used until level 20, and gate which I still use now at level 40! You may also want to buy okeil's radiation, a sheild where when the opponent hits you, he will take some damage too. root will keep your opponent in place for a certain amount of time which allows you to cast attack spells from a safe distance or to flee for your life. Now that you have your new spells, you're ready to move into the desert part of north ro. There you will want to hunt jackals and orc raiders until level 7. At level 7 you must group with other people and hunt giant tarantulas. These tarantulas will get you to level 10, but wait! At level 8 you will get even more spells! Buy shock of ice and fire bolt, your standard attack spell upgrade, lesser sheilding, an uprade to minor sheilding, and shadow step, this spell will teleport you to a random spot in the zone.You are now level 10 and ready to hunt new things. Look for groups to hunt shriveled mummies and madmen until lvl 13. But wait again! At lvl 12 you will get even more new spells! These spells are really starting to pile up now! You will want to buy shock of lighting, a very good attack spell, bind affinity, this spell will change the location of where you appear when you die or gate, cancel magic, cancel's a spell on a groupmate, halo of light, this will summon a little halo for you to wear, and resitant skin, which will improve your poison and disease resistances. From level 13 to 15, you will want to group and hunt Dervish Cutthroats.

Level 15 - 23
Now you have the hang of things so i won't go into much detail. You will want to hunt caiman, crocodiles, deepwater caimans, and deepwater crocodiles. Advance in this order, you should know what to hunt by "conning" your enemy. At lvl 16 buy lightning bolt and flame shock, standard attack spell upgrade, heat sight, will give you infravision, invisibility, sheilding, upgrade to lesser sheilding, and sheildskin, this spell will absorb some damage (requires cat's eye agate). At level 20 you will want to buy enstill, and upgrade to root, elemental sheild, which raises your fire and ice resistances, force shock, standard attack spell upgrade, tishan's clash, a spell which stuns your opponent, tox gate, teleports you to tox forest, north gate, teleports you to north karana, and fay gate, teleport you to greater faydark.

Level 23 - 38
Now you are feeling the true power of a wizard and have mastered how to cast your spells and attack. You should now use your spell north gate and than go to south karana (south karana is right across the bridge after you teleport into north karana). Here you should hunt aviaks until lvl 35, than tesch mas gnolls at the entrance of Splitpaw until lvl 38. You will now have good knowledge of your spells, so now the decision is up to you, you can buy any spell you want, money depending, there are no penalties for buying the right or wrong spells.

Levels 38 - 40
Use Nek Gate to teleport yourself to Nek forest. From here go to lavastorm and than the dungeon known as Solusek's Eye. There are 2 zones with the same name, ask the people around there to show you to Sol B. You MUST group in here. There is no other way to kill the monsters in here.

Levels 40+
Congratulations if you get this far =). I cannot give you anymore advice, you are almost a master wizard now, you hold your fate, you must make the decisions now for you have surpassed even my powers. Good luck to you!
