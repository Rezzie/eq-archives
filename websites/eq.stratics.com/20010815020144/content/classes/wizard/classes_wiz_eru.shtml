Playing an Erudite Wizard

 Introduction   About   Creation   Levels 1 & 2   Levels 3 & 4   Levels 5 & 6 

Introduction
Hello all! During phase 4, the first character I started out as was theErudite wizard. I have made this little guide to help all you new people whomay want to be a wizard. This help document is based on my Erudite wizard(who starts in Erudin). This is all a suggestion and by no means the only way.
 top


About
The wizard is a master of the arcane arts of damage (don't expect to gethealing spells). With a few waves of a wizards hand, he/she can do startlingamounts of damage. But a word of caution, wizards are one of the mostanti-melee characters around and are not for close combat.
 top


Creation
The one thing that a wizard uses the most is his mana, which comes from hisintelligence. I started my character as an Erudite, (Erudite wizards gainlevels at a very SLOW rate) with maximum intelligence, which gives you the most mana and the ability to learn skills the fastest. I then added my additional pointsto stamina.
 top


Levels 1 & 2
The first thing you want to do is to enter your guild, which is located atthe pond near the Erudin armory. Go through the teleporter to the palace.(Its the one over the small water fountain with the steps leading up to it.)Get your guild robe and put it on (one of the most important thing a wizardneeds at low levels is some armor because he starts with very limited life.)Next you have to memorize your spells in the hotkey spells slot. I wouldadvise not to buy any first level 1 spells, because they are a waste ofmoney. Now your ready to adventure into the forest! The Erudin Forest isvery large and you can become easily lost (trust me on that, I know fromexperience). I would suggest attacking snakes and decaying skeletons becauseanything else is too hard until level 2. When you attack something, always useyour Shock of Frost spell, and make sure you have your shielding spell on.After reaching level 2, start attacking kobolt runts. Word of caution: Youare not a tank. It is hard for wizard to attack 2 things at once at lowlevels. Make sure when attacking kobolts, that no other kobolts are in theimmediate area, or you will end up getting overwhelmed (same goes forskeletons). One other thing: DO NOT CROSS THE BRIDGE TO THE SECOND ISLAND! YOU WILL HAVE "A CAN OF WHOOP ASS" OPENED ON YOU!
 top


Levels 3 & 4
When you are level 3, continue to fight things that are white and blue to youwhen you use /con. Around this level I started buying my cloth armor, so Ididn't have to mess with it later on. When you reach level 4, a whole newHUGE world opens up to you. The second you gain level 4, run straight to theguild house and learn the skill "Meditate." I would suggest putting 1 skillpoint towards this skill, due to how you learn skills fast. After learningthis skill, buy Shock of Fire, Root, and Gate as soon as possible (in thatorder)! You now have the skill and experience to take on more than onecreature at a time, but not much more.
 top


Levels 5 & 6
Ahh level 5. This is when you need to start fishing (I would advise to haveat least 100 fishing by level 8) to get money for spells and the rest ofyour cloth armor (that is if you don't have a full set yet), because the nextset of spells at level 8 costs around 5 platinum and can get expensive. Also,you might want to buy a staff soon so you can do more hand to hand damage.When you arrive at level 6, it is now time to cross the bridge! Don't you feelspecial? I still warn you that it can get pretty dangerous, and hope youhave another person grouping with you. Remember to attack what is blue to you.If you have a friend in your group, then you could attack things that areprobably white.

This is as far as I go. Good luck to you wizards and may you kick someserious monster butt !

 top
