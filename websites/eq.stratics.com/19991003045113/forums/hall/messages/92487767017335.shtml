In Reply to: A boring boat ride that turned into an adventure. posted by Syll of Bristlebane on April 23, 1999 at 10:02:08 AM CDT:

I fell off he cursed boat too one day going from Freeport to Kaladim. Fishing from the boat can be precarious!

Well we'd JUST passed the rocks with all the aqua gobs and sirens on it so I decided to ... SWIM to Kaladim. I took off after the boat, at that time I was considered to be a Feeble swimmer. Well after swimming for a very very very long time, stamina basically all gone and I've floating along on me side contemplating my loses when a shout rings out...and then the boat goes by the OTHER way *sigh*

Well I shout out to the boat and one shouts back that the only way to 'zone' is on the boat, it's not possible to do it swimming. Well ain't that just swell! In a few minutes I get another shout letting me know the general location of an island with a dock....soooo, off I swim.

I visited a couple of islands, very interesting they be. I will return, should be fun. Finally I too found my way to the Island of the Sisterhood. Running around and talking to all I find while I wait for the boat wasn't a bad thing.

All in all not an aweful experience though it wasn't the way I'd planned on spending 2 hours.

Cheers,

Quixotica, Freak on a Leash

and now an Expert Swimmer! look out fish!
