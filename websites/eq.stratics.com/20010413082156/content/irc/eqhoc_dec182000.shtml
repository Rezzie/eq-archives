EverQuest House of Commons - Developer Chat with Abashi and the Team

Read the First House of Commons Transcript!

On December 18, 2000 in the #EQHoC channel of irc.stratics.com, Verant Interactive Developers joined with IRC Regulars to hold the second EQSS House of Commons!

Members of the Verant Staff Present were: Abashi, Absor, Hobart, Kayvman, Kendric, Mrylokar, Narris, Rowyl, Solist, and Tolan.

Special thanks go to Reijas, Moonshadow, Ogamok and Thundolfe for managing the questions throughout the session!

Here now is the transcript from the Second EQSS House of Commons!
