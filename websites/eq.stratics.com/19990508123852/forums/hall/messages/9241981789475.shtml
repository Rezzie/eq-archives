The main advantage to being able to buy a combine weapon, is mainly for the magical properties.. Many players dont have the knowlege or means to acquire some of the better weapons... I believe combines are meant to provide an interim weapon for use against magical monsters, as well as some speed advantages over normal weapons, on a temporary basis until the character has the opportunity to acquire a more suitable magic weapon either thru a PC who has one for sale or to go quest for the weapon themselves.. Another reason for Combines is that they are widespread, and there is not much journey involved in acquiring them. Most characters must only travel a few zones to get a combine, whereas if a Cleric in Queynos wanted a screaming mace, its quite a hike to go get one.. Just my opinion. ;)
