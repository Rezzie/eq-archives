Silent Fist Headband Series


The following quests are found in the Silent Fist Clan House, across the pond from Crow's Tavern in North Qeynos.
I suggest looking at the excellent EQS Maps section, before attempting the later stages of the Headband series.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The White Headband

Speak with Phin Esrinap to begin this quest, and he's not always easy to find. He likes to take a little stroll every so often and leaves the Clan house. If he's not meditiating outside the front door, you can usually find him standing near the Priest of Discord. The PoD is located behind Crow's Tavern, near the South Qeynos zoneline. Once in awhile he tends to fall into the pond between Crow's Tavern and the Silent Fist Clan House, and gets stuck. Silly Phin.

Khamon says, 'Hail, Phin Esrinap.'

Phin Esrinap says 'Hello, my name is Phin. Practice, practice, practice.. that's my motto. It is my responsibility to train our members, and help them to advance their skills and abilities. I also reward our members with colored [headbands] for completing certain tasks.'

Khamon says, 'What headbands, Master?'

Phin Esrinap says 'Yes, I give out [white, yellow, and orange headbands] to our newer members, and Togahn gives out [red, purple, and blue headbands] to our elder members.'

Khamon says, 'What white headband, Master?'

Phin Esrinap says 'That is our training headband.. Beginning students can earn this by slaying four [gnoll] pups, and bringing their scalps back to me.'

Khamon says, 'What are gnoll, Master?'

Phin Esrinap says 'Those vile creatures are constantly attacking our city.. and often killing innocent citizens. It is our duty to help defend Qeynos from their vicious raids.'

All of the quest components for this Headband can be found within the North Qeynos newbie hunting grounds. He asks for the following:

  • Gnoll Pup Scalps (4) - These can be found on the Gnoll Pups that torment the young adventurers outside the North Qeynos Gate. Most of the time you can find these on the local merchants for a few Silver pieces, they aren't very rare.

    Return to Phin Esrinap with your four quest items.

    Phin Esrinap says 'Good job, Khamon, keep up the good work! Here is your white training headband. Wear it with honor, and make Lu'Sun proud.'

    White Training Headband
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +1 Sta, +1 Cha, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Yellow Headband

    Speak with Phin Esrinap, once again.

    Khamon says, 'What yellow headband, Master?'

    Phin Esrinap says 'This is awarded to our students for helping clear out the evil that inhabits the old ruins of the Qeynos Hills. Just bring me back three putrid rib bones as proof of your good deeds and turn in your [training headband], and then I will give you the yellow headband.'

    All of the quest components for this Headband can be found within the Qeynos Hills zone. He asks for the following:
     

  • White Headband - See previous quest.
     
  • Putrid Rib Bones (3) - You can find these on Putrid Skeletons. The skeletons are located in the Qeynos Hills zone, at the Haunted Ruins. You are probably familiar with the location of the Blackburrow zone by the time you start this quest. Head north from its cave opening and you'll find your target. Heading southwest from the Surefall Glade tunnel will also start you in the right direction.

    Return to Phin Esrinap with your four quest items.

    Phin Esrinap says 'Good work.. and, as promised, here is your reward. It is an honor to present this yellow headband to Khamon, for recent acts of courage and heroism, on behalf of the Silent Fist Clan.'

    Yellow Headband
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +1 Sta, +1 Cha, +1 Agi, +1 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Orange Headband

    Speak with Phin Esrinap, yet again.

    Khamon says, 'What orange headband, Master?'

    Phin Esrinap says 'This is a difficult award to obtain. The city of Qeynos is, as you know, under constant attack by the gnolls of Blackburrow. The Silent Fist Clan rewards its members who venture deep into this gnoll stronghold and bring swift justice to these vile creatures. Please return two Blackburrow gnoll pelts - make sure they aren't ruined - and a Blackburrow gnoll skin as proof of your noble actions. Also, turn in your [yellow headband], and then I shall give you the orange one. Good luck!'

    All of the quest components for this Headband can be found within the vile tunnels of Blackburrow. He asks for the following:
     

  • Yellow Headband - See previous quest.
     
  • Blackburrow Gnoll Skin (1) - These are found on the Gnoll Commanders and Elite Gnolls of Blackburrow. These are very rare, so it could take a few hours before you see one. Do not confuse these with Blackburrow Gnoll Pelts, Ruined Blackburrow Gnoll Pelts, Gnoll Scalps, or Patches of Gnoll Fur. Only a Blackburrow Gnoll Skin will work.
     
  • Blackburrow Gnoll Pelt (2) - These are found on most of the Gnoll types within Blackburrow, and the Gnolls in Qeynos Hills. They do not drop from Scrawny Gnolls, or Gnoll Pups. They're a lot more common than the Gnoll Skin, but make sure they aren't ruined!

    Return to Phin Esrinap with your four quest items.

    Phin Esrinap says 'It is an honor to present the orange headband of the Silent Fist Clan to one of our finest members, Khamon the mighty!'

    Khamon says, 'Pffshaw.. Thanks, Master.'

    Orange Headband
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +2 Sta, +1 Cha, +1 Agi, +2 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Red Headband

    Speak with Togahn Sorast, who can be found meditiating just outside the back door of the Silent Fist Clan House.

    Khamon says, 'Hail, Togahn Sorast.'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Greetings.. I am Second Master Togahn Sorast. I am here to teach the ways of our guild. For our more advanced members, I will give out [headbands] as a reward for their contribution to the clan.'

    Khamon says, 'What headbands, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'The Silent Fist Clan gives out colored headbands for various deeds completed by our students. [Phin] gives out the [white, yellow, and orange headbands].. and I give out the [red, purple, and blue headbands].'

    Khamon says, 'What red headband, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Ah, you think you are skilled enough to earn the red headband of the Silent Fist Clan, Khamon? Hmmm.. Well, if you can help us find [Brother Dareb], you would certainly deserve the honor of wearing the red headband.'

    Khamon says, 'Who is Brother Dareb, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Yes, brother Dareb was last heard from over two weeks ago. He was out in the Plains of South Karana, and then just disappeared. We fear that those vile gnolls fleeing Splitpaw may have captured him or worse. We sent brother Shen to go search the Karanas and look for Dareb over three days ago, but haven't heard from him, either. I fear the worst for both of them, especially if the [Splitpaws] are involved. Please, Khamon, find and return our lost brothers to us. If they are not alive, bring back their remains so we may give them a proper burial.. and, if it is that evil [Ghanex Drah]'s doing, bring me his head as well, for the honor of the clan. You do this for us, Khamon, and turn in your orange headband, and I will reward you with the red headband of the Silent Fist. Good luck.'

    Khamon says, 'Quellious, guide them.. I shall leave at once! ..but, who are the Splitpaws, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'The Splitpaws were a large clan of gnolls that lived in a burrow out in South Karana, They have recently been ousted from their home by a new clan of gnolls that are very powerful. We try to stay away from them if we can, for we do not know much about this new breed. The Splitpaws are on the move and now is the only time to strike. If we wait too long, there will be no way to find our brother. Go now my friend, we haven't much time.'

    Khamons says, 'I must ask one more question before I take my leave, Master.. Who is Ghanex Drah?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Ghanex Drah is ruler of the now fleeing gnolls of Splitpaw. I've heard rumors that he has been dabbling in the dark art of necromancy. Ghanex is looking for more test subjects, no doubt to rebuild his stronghold. The good people of Antonica must put an end to his evil reign soon, or I fear he could grow much more powerful.'

    All of the quest components for this Headband can be found in the Southern Plains of Karana zone. He asks for the following:
     

  • Orange Headband - See previous quest.
     
  • Head of Brother Shen - The remains of this poor Monk can be found on the rare wandering Gnoll, High Shaman Grisok, who is located in the Southern Plains of Karana zone.
    • He seems to wander due east of the Aviak Treehouse, for the most part. You will probably have better luck finding someone selling this item, then finding Grisok himself.
  • Brother Dareb's Skull - The remains of this poor Monk can be found on the rare wandering Gnoll, High Shaman Phido, who is also found the Southern Plains of Karana zone.
    • Rumor has it that the best place to spot this target is a bit south of the Hermit spawn point. I stumbled into him during a thunder storm at night and forgot to get a /location in all the excitment. By the time my night blinded self found my bearings, back tracking was a lost cause. You will probably have better luck finding someone selling this item, then finding Phido himself.
  • Head of Ghanex Drah - The leader of the homeless Splitpaw is also found in the Southern Plains of Karana, and what's great about this part is.. he doesn't wander around. Yay! Head south from the Northern Karana Bridge, to the Skeleton camp. It's a large circular area with small wooden towers. It's really hard to miss. Avenge our fallen Brethren and take his head!

    Return to Togahn Sorast with your four quest items.

    Togahn Sorast says 'We are deeply honored to have such great warrior as part of our Clan. With a proper burial, Shen and Dareb's souls will finally be at peace, and with the death of that vile Ghanex, the plains will be much safer for travelers. It is a great honor to present you, Khamon, with the red headband of the Silent Fist Clan.'

    Red Headband
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +2 Sta, +2 Agi, +1 Dex, +2 Cha, +2 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Purple Headband

    Speak with Togahn Sorast, once again.

    Khamon says, 'What purple headband?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Ah, you think you are now skilled enough to deserve the purple headband of the Silent Fist Clan? Very well, Khamon, I have a task for you that will prove whether or not you are worthy of this honor. I have heard recent rumors of ancient evils surfacing once again throughout Antonica. These evil groups have discovered some items that are giving them mystical and dangerous powers. These items must be destroyed, or else the evils of Norrath may gain control of the world's future. Bring me these [evil items], and turn in your red headband, and you will have proven yourself a mighty spirit and dedicated member of the Silent Fist.'

    Khamon says, 'What evil items, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'These evil creations have been found all over Antonica, and no one knows, or at least no one admits to knowing, where exactly they were found, but we do know that they are currently in the wrong hands and must be destroyed. Please, Khamon, seek out the [Skull of Jhen'Tra], the [Dagger of Marnek], and the [Coronet of Buried Blood], and bring them to me.'

    Khamon says, 'What is the Skull of Jhen'Tra, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'I don't know much about the Skull of Jhen'Tra. I've heard that it has resurfaced in the gnoll burial grounds of Lake Rathetear. Apparently, this skull is helping to give these gnolls the power to raise an undead army of their fallen ancestors. This evil must be stopped before their army becomes too powerful.'

    Khamon says, 'What is the Dagger of Marnek, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'The Dagger of Marnek is a diabolical weapon created by the ancient necromancer Marnek Jaull. Marnek was the leader of a small cult of necromancers who worshipped Solusek Ro. Marnek used this dagger in his ceremonial sacrifices to the Burning Prince, to increase his own power and show his loyalty to his god. His cult grew stronger and word of the evil acts spread all over Antonica. Legend has it that a group of paladins from Erudin, followers of Prexus, spent over 10 years searching out Marnek and his followers, and finally destroyed them. Though Marnek's evil reign was put to an end, many of his books and items are still being uncovered to this day. The Dagger of Marnek is now being used by the evil inhabitants who control the depths of Befallen. Bring this dagger to me, so that we may dispose of it and banish its evil powers forever.'

    Khamon says, 'What is the Coronet of Buried Blood, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'The Coronet of Buried Blood is an ancient headdress which is said to have belonged to the High Priest of the Plexant Temple. The Plexant were known to be devout followers of Innoruuk and the coronet is supposed to have been blessed by the Prince of Hate himself. Apparently, the coronet is now in the hands of a clan of goblins that dwell in the caverns of Permafrost.'

    The quest components for this Headband can be found in three locations. They are the dungeon of Befallen, Permafrost Keep, and in the Lake Rathetear zone. He asks for the following:
     

  • Red Headband - See previous quest.
     
  • The Skull of Jhen'Tra - This item takes you to the vast waters of Lake Rathetear. Head down the path from the Southern Plains of Karana zoneline and you'll soon see a Gnoll camp set up around old ruins and a dock. The Gnoll Embalmer carries the Skull. A word of caution though.. At night the Gnolls you kill will respawn as higher level Undead and Skeletons.
     
  • The Dagger of Marnek - This item takes you to the dungeon of Befallen. It is found on a rare NPC called the Thaumaturgist. To reach her you must make your way to the bottom level of the dungeon, using a series of keys dropped by Shadowknights along the way. Upon opening the locked door to the bottom level, turn right and continue down the hall. Hug the wall on the right hand side until you stumble though a hidden doorway. You'll see a large wooden chair in front of you. Go beyond it and stick to the wall on the left hand side. You'll see three tunnels branch off ahead of you, choose the left most tunnel and continue on until you reach the end. In this room there are three spawn points. The Thaumaturgist is the center one and has place holders. While here, stick around for Priest Amiaz, who is located on the left most spawn point of the same room. His wand will be needed to complete one of the Ashen Order Sash quests in Freeport.
     
  • The Coronet of Buried Blood - This item can be found on High Priest Zaharn, in Permafrost Keep. You can find him in the room marked "7" on EQS' Permafrost map, otherwise known as the "King Room." When looted, the item goes by the name of "Zaharn's Coronet."

    Return to Togahn Sorast with your four quest items.

    Togahn Sorast says 'In honor of your recent acts of courage and dedication, I reward you, Khamon, with the purple headband of the Silent Fist Clan.'

    Purple Headband
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +1 Ac, +2 Sta, +2 Agi, +1 Dex, +2 Cha, +3 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Blue and Black Headbands

    These items can't be quested for, and are not required for any Monk quest you may come across down the road, like the Robe of the Lost Circle for example. Rumor has it that the various Monk NPC's will drop these headbands should they be killed. This is simply some bonus material.

    Speak with Togahn Sorast, once again.

    Khamon says, 'What blue headband, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Sorry, we are currently out of blue headbands. Please check back later.'

    Khamon says, 'What black headband, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'The black headband is one of the highest honors of our guild.. [Lu'Sun] only gives those out for great acts of heroism and devotion to the clan.'

    Khamon says, 'Who is Lu'Sun, Master?'

    Togahn Sorast says 'Ahhh, Lu'Sun is a master of many skills. He has traveled all of Norrath, studying various techniques and disciplines. Now, he runs this small guild and passes on his knowledge to those willing to devote their lives to our cause.'

    Khamon says, 'Hail, LuSun.'

    LuSun says 'Ah, greetings! I am Lu'Sun, Guild Master of the Silent Fist Clan. Here, our goal is to train the body, mind, and soul to the peak of perfection.'

    When asked about either the Blue, or Black Headbands, Master Lu'Sun offered no comment.
