EQ just plain isn't a good pvp game. The whole combat/spell system with all of its combinations is just too complex to be able to balance the classes in that sense. I think that the balancing Verant has done with the classes has mostly been in terms of contribution to a party, in which your wizard is actually a bit stronger than the ranger you fought. But, one on one, your wizard is going to have trouble with any of the hybrid classes (forget about it against a paladin; you'll blow all your mana, he will LoH, and then you will be manaless fighting a fully healthy tank in full plate). Just think of it this way; there are classes which can solo monsters that /con blue fairly easily at level 25; while in my experience soloing /blue monsters at level 25 with any mage class that doesn't have a pet is nigh impossible. You are trying to solo an "even fight" mob, so to speak (the ranger) with your wizard and you should expect difficulty. The only mage class I know that has an easy time beating hybrids is the necro, who gets powerful DoT's and the benefit of having all of their DD spells heal themselves at the same time as they damage their opponent.

If you go to the pvp server and fight other characters as a group, your wizard would be able to live up to his/her full potential.
