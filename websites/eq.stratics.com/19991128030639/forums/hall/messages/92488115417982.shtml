Every time you level you get 5 training points.

The trainers of you4r guild will only train you so far in any given skill.
I beleive the limit is 21 or 26, or there abouts.

The skill cap for most skills is level * 5 + 5.
All weapon skills, casting skills, fishing, sense heading, swiming and others fall in this category.

Other skills like Offence and defence are caped at level * 5.

Finally, some trade skills are not caped at all.
Smithing and tailoring fall in this category.
But these are very hard to raise anyhow.

A skill that has a level requirement will require you to spend at least 1 training point in it before it can be used.
Paladin gets Meditate at level 14 (level 12 is bash by the way) and he will need to train at least 1 training to learn the basics of this skill. And that will put your skill level at the same level that was required to get the skill.
Which means you get Meditate 14 when you train your first training.
You get Bashing 12, when you train it.

But until you learn that first point in those skill, they remain at level 0.


It does seem like it takes longer to raise a skill that you neglected for a few levels.
Although i was told this is not true, i still maintain that you're better off maxing all your skills every level before you gain a level.


Hope this helps
