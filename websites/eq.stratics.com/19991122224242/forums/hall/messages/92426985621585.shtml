Funniest thing that I have experienced in EQ since playing the beta since phase three happened to me last nite. I was wandering non-chalantly through the west freeport gate, when all of the sudden a dark elf necro NPC walks up and starts to cast a spell. I look warily her way, not sure what is happening, then all of the sudden "You lose control of your self." Well, being a hapless victim of her wiles and charms, I go on a slaughter rampage, killing newbies in the area, and even chasing a guy though town trying to kill him. I am sorry to all of those that I killed last nite, as it wasnt my intention to harm you, but the vile witch had me in her power. Anyway, this lasted about 10 minutes, where I finally was lead back into the necro hideout under freeport, and was promptly removed from my mortal coil when she no longer had use for me. Alas, I guess the corruption of the Militia has finally shown itself to the populous when Dark Elven Necromancers can walk into town Charm a Paladin of 20th level, and lead him on a killing rampage. *sigh* what to do?

Tiniden
The now less-than-pure Paladin
