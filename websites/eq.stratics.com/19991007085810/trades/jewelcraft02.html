Enchanted Jewelry Tips

When using enchanted silver you are limited to 3 jems that will make magic items: Jade for mana, Topaz for Disease, and Pearl for poison.

After trying most other gems, like Onxy for strength, I found they only made normal items. If you wish to make something like that you must have Enchant Electrum, a level 16 spell.
