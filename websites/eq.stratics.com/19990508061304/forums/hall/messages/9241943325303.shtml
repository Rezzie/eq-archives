In Reply to: How do you know if your Skill is Maxed out? posted by Zubalea on April 15, 1999 at 12:28:08 PM CDT:

Usually, the max for a skill is (level x 5)+5. So at level 5, the max is 30. The way to tell how far you are from maxing a skill is to first get one skill to the max. I usually use sense heading, it goes up fast at later level. Now, go to your guildmaster and look at the cost to train your maxed out skill. You can then tell how far the other skills are from being maxed by comparing the price to train them.
