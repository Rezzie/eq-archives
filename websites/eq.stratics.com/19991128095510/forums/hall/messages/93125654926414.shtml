Well kids, its been a fun ride.

I Thoroughly enjoyed Beta 3 and 4, and all the months of Final. But now is the time when Oggog is put to rest.

I've gotten into the Beta 1 of Asheron's Call, which starts soon. Im hangin up my hat in EQ, takin a break before I start the AC beta.

I think the defining moment where I decided to quit EQ was this past friday. First off, I got the AC email. (Yahoo!) Secondly, I had a big encounter with the guild FoH.

Lets just say, they showed their true colors, and went off when I said something that hit a nerve. ALOT of their High Level players showed up, and followed my group around, stealing kills. Now, I got some screenshots, and reported them to a GM. What did they say? "We'll look into it" And what happened to FoH? Nothing.

So, Im moving on to greener pastures, toward uninterrupted fields of pure heaven. Asheron's Call. I played my friend's beta account the other day, and I'll never go back to EQ. I'd sooner go back to UO then EQ. If you really get down to it, the graphics in EQ are the pull. Look beyond, and you'll find a poorly implemented game system, and an underdeveloped game.

So, this is it. Im committing the ultimate sin, selling my account on EBay. I'll be happy to get the $150 I've spent on the game back. If it goes more, so be it, but once that thing hits $150, Its gone.

Good luck to you, I wish you well. Telchar, keep on it, you'll get a character high up. :P Riga, keep on being your sarcastic, Sinical self. MLE, You keep on guidin those newbies, for you are their king. ;P

I do reccommend everyone to go to the following link, and click on the beta tab. The Beta 4 for Asherons Call is going to be 45000 people. So, If you want in, you'll most likely be in. Soon as I create my characters, I'll post their names here. Best way to get ahold of me is thru Gannan in AC, or email me at ZSmith_7@hotmail.com.

Take care friends, and do try the beta out. I guarentee you'll love AC as much as I do, and never look back to EQ.

--Oggog Goggrobbok
