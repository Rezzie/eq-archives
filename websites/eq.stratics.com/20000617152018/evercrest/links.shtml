Links
The Big Boys - Must-visit sites

  • Everquest - The Offical Site
    Everquest is the online, multiplayer RPG that EverCrest is based on. If you're not already playing, buy the game and start. You'll thank me.

  • Everquest Strategies and Statistics (EQSS)
    The largest library of technical information about Everquest. Must reading for every Everquest player. Knowing is half the battle. Home of EverCrest!

  • NotCrazy
    Another "so-big-it's-not-funny" news site.... or would it be, so-big-it's-not-crazy?

  • Dr. TwisTer's EverQuest Alert
    Agree or disagree with it, this is a must-read for every EverQuest player. You'll find bits of information here that none of the major news sites will touch with a ten foot pole.

The Best Guilds in Everquest

  • The Crimson Rose
    Server: Karana
    Organized and successful guilds are few and far between, but they add so much value to the game. The Crimson Rose is one of those guilds.

  • The Syndicate
    Server: Tarew Marr
    The Syndicate is an extremely large, extremely established, and extremely well-known guild. If you play on Tarew Marr, you'll want to check these guys out.


The Best of the Rest

  • Everquest Express
    The weekly newspaper of, for, and about EverQuest. The quality material they can turn out week after week makes this site one to add to your bookmarks. Plus, it's the home of Lil Archy, the first computer-drawn comic strip based on EQ. ;-)

  • Forever Quest
    Dedicated to bringing epic roleplaying to Everquest.

  • The Rathe Rhapsody
    Humor, info, the best source of information on things happening on the Rathe server.

  • Cazic-thule.com
    The home of the community of the Cazic-Thule server. If you play on Cazic-Thule, definately stop by this site for news and events occuring on your server.

  • The Cackling Klaknak
    A great collection of assorted EQ-related humor. I guarantee you'll get at least a few laughs out of this site.

  • Faydwer's Frolickers
    A different kind of EQ fansite, with it's own unique brand of mature roleplaying. Not for the faint of heart. Parents, you don't want your children following this link.

Other Web-Comics

  • Penny Arcade
    A very impressive strip based on gaming in general (Drysart's favorite is the "Software, and like, etc." strip). Updated twice weekly.

  • PvP
    A professional strip covering life in the office of a gaming magazine. Updated Monday through Friday.

  • Sluggy Freelance
    A profesionally done online comic strip, based on a couple of guys and their sadistic rabbit. Updated daily.
