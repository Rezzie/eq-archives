Our goal...a simple one to say, but never ending. We are the seekers of knowledge, the keepers of secrets, the understanders of all Arcane. We are the choniclers of all things arcane.

Join us and help unearth all the mystical items, artifacts and such. Gain an understanding of all the magics of Norrath.

Become a Mystic Sage...unearth all the mystical mysteries that surrounds us...

The only requirement is that you share your knowledge with all guildmembers, and that you assist your brethern whenever possible..
