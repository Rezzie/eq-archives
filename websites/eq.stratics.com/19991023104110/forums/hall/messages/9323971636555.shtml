For example: I a lvl 19 warrior can solo emperor crush. He takes me down to about 2 bubbles of energy. I now have to heal for about 20minutes AT THE LEAST. This is no lie. And If one of his guards pops up I will probably die, I cannot run. I cannot heal. I cannot gate. I cannot super blast. I miss more times in beserker mode than I do in regular mode. Warriors have NO escape route like the other classes. (besides some other melee types but there other skills make up for it such as speed song or hiding or Feign death)

A shaman same lvl goes to solo crush. Before he leaves he can cast SOW on himself just incase he gets in trouble he can run. Another guard appears and he decides to try and take it because he can heal himself a few times before actually giving up and running. If he does run he will have no problem getting away. If he does get in trouble he can jog away and gate. Or if a cleric is soloing crush. He gets in trouble so he heals himself, or he gates.

I have 588 hp at lvl 19. He (the shaman) has about 470 BUT he also has heals. This is like having 650 hp. because when he drops he can regain them. When I drop I just drop and my hp are GONE. Which brings me to the next rant.

Bind wound is the most useless skill in the game. To use bind wound I have to be in a spot were there is nothing to attack me. That IS NOT A HELP. When I need a heal I need one NOW before I die. Its not gonna help me if Im alrdy dead or a big train is on my ass. If I am lucky enough to make it(which I have yet to have been) then I have to BUY bandages. So I have to use 20 bandages to get me to half. The bandages cost me around half of a plat. That gets me to half. Hmm now I only have to wait about 10-25min to get to full.Why would I do that when I could ask a cleric or shaman or paladin to heal me? Or offer him 5gp and heal you ALL THE WAY.

Taunt will not work when I want it to. I want it to work to tank. And It does. I REALLY WANT IT TO WORK when my caster is getting rdy to die because he accidently over blasted and now the monster is on him. His hit points are almost gone and Im taunting and taunting at skill of 100 and the monster ignores me and continues to kill him. HOW CAN I BE USEFUL IF TAUNT DOES NOT WORK???? It doesnt even tell me if it was successful or not. It makes it seem like it worked(You taunt a ghoul to ignore others and attack you) but it didnt.

So they try to fix it by giving us critical hit. The way it is now I will do a little bit more damage every 5-6fights. Great!!!! s/r. The only way this can be helpful is if I am about rdy to die and the creature has a tad bit more energy than me and BAM critical hit goes off and it saves me. Now I have to go heal for about 10-25min. Are we not warriors? We are strong and brutal!! Our hit points should heal at an incredible amount of speed. I mean INCREDIBLE.

The warrior is sopposed to be one of the best solo classes in the game. But they fall short by about a mile. The monk is the true solo class with mend and there awesome ability to solo yellows all the time. The warrior at higher lvls cannot function without others. You will work your way backwards if you try. And the truth is the others dont need you to group with them. theyd just assume have a monk or rogue or bard. There is no spot were you need a warrior. People on my server are starting to find out that monks are ALOT better pullers and tankers than warriors. Warriors ARE NOT a solo class. They may be a little better at soloing then wizards but if a wizard gets in trouble he can gate. I get in trouble i have to die. Thats why I now claim that warriors ARE THE hardest class to play at higher lvls.

I dont have trouble finding a group because I group with the same people every single night and they only doit cause they are guild friends and good friends. ANd I am helpful for them. They have another punching bag for the group.
