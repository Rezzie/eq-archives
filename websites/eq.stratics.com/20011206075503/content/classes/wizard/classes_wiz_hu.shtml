Playing a Human Wizard

 Making Your Character   Starting Out   Your Hot Buttons   Levels 1 & 2   Levels 3   Levels 4   Levels 5 & 6   Levels 7 & 8   Levels 9 & 10 

Making Your Character
Wizard's main power is mana, which is acquire through intelligence. This means that when you are creating your character use all of your extra points in intelligence so you will be able to cast as many spells as possible. After you do that, then pick the deity you want. Finally, you pick Qeynos as you're starting point.
 top


Starting Out
Once you have landed in Qeynos, go directly to your Guildmaster. Give him your note and then put your Guild Tunic on. After you do this, get 3 training in Evocation and 2 training in Channeling. Equip your dagger and now you are ready to go adventuring!
 top


Setting Up Your Hot Button Keypad
The most important thing you need in your hot button keypad is Melee Attack. On the main screen, click on combat and then click on Melee Attack. Hold that button down and when the button duplicates, click in one of your empty slots. Now click on socials and do the same thing with consider. After you do all this, then it is time for you to memorize your spells. Click on the spell book on the left-hand side of your screen. Take one of your spells and click in one of the square boxes. One of the poles should fill up with red and be going down. Once the red is completely gone, you have finished scribing the spell. Now scrib the other. Then you have to memorize them. Hold down the mouse on one of the spells and click in the little knotches on the left-hand side of the screen. Do that to the other. Now exit the spell menu and hold down on one of the spells. Move it to the hotbutton menu and now you are ready!
 top


Levels 1 & 2
Once you get into North Qeynos, go to the gate and head down the road a little bit. Not too far or else you will find yourself in Qeynos Hills. Look for large rats, snakes, and gnoll pups. Once you have one targeted, use Shock of Frost on it. It will come to you. You hit Melee Attack when it's right next to you, and then look in your chat box to see what is happening. If your life is very far down then run to the guards, but if you are fine then keep on attacking it. Once you kill the monster, right click on it to loot it. Keep on doing this until level three.
 top


Levels 3
You've probably got the hang of everything now, so I won't be as detailed. Once at level 3, you will probably have enough to buy at least one piece of cloth armor. Go inside the town and right click on Nerissa Clothspinner once you find her. Select one and hit purchase. Now you have a piece of armor. Once you get more money then you can get more armor.
 top


Levels 4
Now the challenges start. If you want to gain experience fast, then I suggest killing Klicniks. Careful, the Queens will whoop you! Once you have gotten to level 4, it is also time to buy some new spells. Buy them in this order: Shock on Fire, Root, and Gate. Once you have those spells you will have no problem with Klicniks and you will still get experience.
 top


Levels 5 & 6
OK, you are now ready for the Qeynos Hills. WARNING: Stay fairly close to the guards and know where you are when in these hills.This is when you use your consider button. When you enter the hills, look around so you are familiar with the territory. Then target something and consider it. If it says "kind of risky but you might win" or "looks like you would have the upper hand", then Shock of Fire it. If it says "Looks like quite a gamble" or "what would you like your tombstone to say", then run as fast as you can! Mostly try and kill wolves and other things. When you are attacking animals be 100% sure that Cros Treewind is not around. He is the protector of animals. I think you can guess the rest. When you reach level 6, you are ready for brown bears. Remember: If something does tons and tons of damage, run as fast as possible to the guards.
 top


Levels 7 & 8
Aah, these are very hard to get, but if you do, good for you. You have entered the level where you are ready to go to Karanas. Be extremely careful in this place for it is easy to get lost, and never go hunting in the dark. When you are level 7, then kill young lionesses, young lions, mist wolves, and brown bears. Those are pretty much the only things you should kill. If you see anything big, RUN! Also at level eight it is time to buy some more spells (if you have any left over money from buying armor). At level 8, you should invest in fishing. This is the best way to make money, and most spells will now cost around 5 plat. You should buy these spells in this order: Fire bolt then Lesser Shielding. Once you have those two, then keep on killing the monsters I told you. At level 8, you should start to attack giant beetles, but still stay close to the guards while in Karanas.
 top


Levels 9 & 10
OK, now you have the big fun. Start killing giant beetles and spiders. You will make lots of money. And keep on fishing until you get that skill up. If you ever get to level 9 and 10, I congratulate you deeply for this is a very difficult level to achieve.

This is all I know so keep on adventuring and send me some more information if you have any. Thank you for spending the time for reading this.

 top
