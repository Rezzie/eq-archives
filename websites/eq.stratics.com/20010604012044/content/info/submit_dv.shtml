How to make a screenshot?
In EverQuest you can make screenshots by pressing the '-' (minus) key on your keypad. When you press that button your computer will show a short hiccup, depending on your resolution and the speed of your computer. Once this is done EverQuest saved a copy of the state of your screen as a file in your EverQuest directory. The name of the file is in the following format: 'EQxxxxxx.bmp' where the xxxxxx represents a number. EverQuest creates the files in numerical order, starting with 'EQ000000.bmp'. If you want to view the screenshots you have just made, you can easily go to Windows Explorer, go to the directory you installed EverQuest in (usually c:\everquest\) and double click on the screenshot you want to view.

Why should I make a screenshot?
Some people might wonder why they should ever take a screenshot. The only answer I can give is by returning a question: Why would you ever take a photograph? The reasons should be obvious, however I would like to add a little something to that. I have been playing EverQuest for quite some time now, and thus collected a pretty large amount of screenshots. Every time I look back at them I enjoy it again, because all previous memories are stored in them. That excellent group that took out deep reds, or the first time you got a lastname, or the time you solo'ed Vox... Please, make screenshots of everything you think is worth it. You can always delete them if you do not like them, but from my experience, it is really fun to look back at them later. Now that is why I think you should make lots of screenshots, however not all screenshots are intresting enough to be viewed by thousands of people. Do not let this keep you from making them though...

Which screenshot(s) should I send to EQSS?
First of all, we would like to thank you for even considering this option. We are very glad with any sort of user input and submitting a screenshot for our Daily Vision is no exception. However, to save time for both of us, there are some minor things we would like you to do before sending your screenshots in:

First of all, which screenshots are suited for posting? Well this is no easy question. The best question that describes it is "What would be interesting / funny for a large number of people that play EverQuest, ranging from level 1 to level 50?". For instance a picture of the first fire beetle you killed might be great fun for you to store and look back on later, but it would be futile to send it in to us for reasons hopefully you can understand. However a picture of a brave group of high level players standing in front of a dragon they have just slain, would have a bit better chance of being posted =)

The second point is something more technical, however, not any less important. If you make a screenshot in EverQuest, the game will save the data automatically in the so called BMP format. This is a way to store image data. The problem is that EverQuest does not compress the images. By compressing the image the size of the file greatly decreases at the cost of image quality. A well-known format, which is frequently used for Internet graphics, is JPG. This is a format that supports compression and is preferred by us. Please do -NOT- send us BMP files, you would help us a lot if you would first convert it to JPG. I have got some 10mb+ emails in the past, which will simply be deleted because I can not download them. A good size for a picture is about 50 - 100kb. If your picture is too large, try increasing the amount of compression with your favorite program. A freeware program I always use to convert pictures is InfranView which can be downloaded from this page.
