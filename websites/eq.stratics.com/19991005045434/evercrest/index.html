Monday, October 4, 1999
  • The 1,000,000th Hit Competition
    If you'll notice, at the bottom of each page, there's a LinkExchange page counter. It counts how many unique page views are generated throughout the entire EverCrest website.
    That counter is on track to top 1,000,000 this week. To celebrate, if you happen to have lucky page #1,000,000, take a screenshot of it and send it to me. As a prize, I'll feature a character of your choice (most likely your character in game, although you can pick anything you want) in a series of EverCrest strips (or maybe I'll come up with some better prize. It's not like those sponsors at the top of the page are MINE, I don't have money to spend on REAL prizes).
    I've added a unique identifier that prints beneath each counter hit, be sure to include that in your screenshot.

    Hey, and while you're here, click on that annoying little Top 100 Homepages button on the left of the page and write in EverCrest. You know I'd do it for you. ;-)

    Cheers!
Drysart - 2:18 AM EST

Monday, September 27, 1999
  • EverCrest: Elseworlds will run Monday and Tuesday this week, with regular EverCrest returning on Wednesday. I have enough strips pre-drawn to last a few weeks, and by then I'll have more strips ready.
    Again, I apologize for letting the site rot the past couple weeks, but the factors that went into that are gone now, so the site will be back up to regular update schedule, EverCrest on Monday through Friday, and ElseWorlds on Saturday and Sunday.

    In addition, I have a bunch of RP stories that need to be added to the RP section of the site, so if you've sent in a story and it hasn't been posted yet, it will be within the next couple days.
Drysart - 2:32 AM EST

Wednesday, September 1, 1999
  • Today we have a guest artist doing the EverCrest strip, none other than Piper, the recently introduced druid character. She took the liberty to tweak her character's look a bit and came up with something that actually quite good when you consider she did it with a mouse (EverCrest is hand drawn and scanned in normally).
    On another bright note, EverCrest will hopefully be returning with a normal schedule before the week is through. If not, then look for new strips starting Monday. In addition, I have quite a few RP stories waiting in my inbox to be posted in the Roleplaying Stories section of the website. If you've sent something in and haven't heard back from me yet, don't worry, I'm getting around to posting everything (I have to format each and every story by hand... it's quite tedious).
    Oh yeah, September 1st, 1999 is exactly 730,000 days after January 1st, 1. Yes. That's totally irrelevant, and yes, I'm easily amused.
Drysart - 12:39 AM EST

Saturday, August 14, 1999
  • Apparently while EverCrest was away, a few parodies were done in the EQ Stratics Gamers Forum, called EverText. I wish I had time to read the forums more regularly, I just discovered EverText today (even though Greebo sent me email about it a few days ago, I had no idea what he was talking about at the time).
    I'll probably copy off the EverText threads and post them here in the next couple days, I got quite a few laughs reading them. Until then, you can view them by going to the Gamers Forum search page and searching for "EverText".
Drysart - 3:54 AM EST

Friday, August 13, 1999
  • EverCrest: ElseWorlds premieres today, and will be appearing on the EverCrest site every Saturday and Sunday. All of the writing and artwork for EC:EW is done by Ryoma, and I must say, what he's shown me so far has impressed me, I'm sure you'll be impressed as well.
    As far as EverCrest (classic), I'm going to do my best to keep it up on full schedule, but I'm not going to promise anything. It will all depend on how much time I have this weekend to put together some strips (I'm hoping I'll be able to get out at least a week's worth.)
Drysart - 3:54 AM EST

Sunday, June 6, 1999
  • The brand-new page layout is here, finally getting rid of those annoying frames from the old layout. I've made the page a lot cleaner looking, and now it works under 640x480 again.
    Not all of the sections of the page are completed under the new style yet, but those that aren't will be up and running within the next couple days.
    Let me just do a quick overview of the main changes:
    • Today's strip is now on the main page, saving everyone a mouse click.
    • The What's New Bar - Along the right edge of the main page is a bar labelled What's New. On this bar is a recent history of each of the major features of EverCrest. Items you haven't seen yet should appear red on the bar, so with one glance you can see if there's anything new on EverCrest that you haven't seen yet.
    • Games and Downloads - In the next few weeks, these new sections will come to life with all sorts of fun stuff.
    • Link Icons - If you want to link to EverCrest with a graphic, we have a selection of them here for your linking pleasure.
    • Saraiah's Top 10 - Saraiah comes up with some good top 10 lists, and we're featuring the best of her work here.
    • Roleplaying Stories - Since we actively support good roleplaying in Everquest, we added a section for RP stories. We're always accepting new stories to add to this page.
Drysart - 1:46 PM EST
