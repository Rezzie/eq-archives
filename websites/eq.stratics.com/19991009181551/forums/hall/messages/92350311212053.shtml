One thing that kind of annoys me, and I think could be fixed kind of easily, is that your guildmaster (or anybody for that matter) always gives you the same quest. How about instead of that, they give you a more difficult quest for a better reward? Such as, my guildmaster wants me to get a carcass of some animal or another. I bring it back to him, he says thanks, gives me stuff, and says "Now that you have proven yourself, Sparker, I have a slightly more difficult task for you. A fellow wizard requires (an item from a different zone)" or something to that effect. They should take a cue from 'Daggerfall'. That was one of the best parts of the game. Your quests would get better and better, but would never be predictable, so spoilers would also be tougher. Just MHO.
