Site Background

I worked on another EverQuest (which will remain nameless for the time being) site originally, but didn't like where it was headed - so I left. I looked around at all the different EQ fan sites, trying to think of some unique EQ-material. Then it hit me - zone-by-zone analyses. None of the sites I've visited (as of 3/13/99 anyways) have complete zone guides. Many have city zone guides, but not the wilderness zone guides. Seeing this omission, I build this site from scratch in less than four hours to fill this niche. What you see before you is the result. I truly hope you find the information presented here to be both informative and entertaining. Feedback is always welcome, especially if it may help to improve the site :-).

Well, that's about all I can think of for now. If you have any other questions you'd like to see answered here, drop me a line.
