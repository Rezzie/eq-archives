Ok let me clear things up here.

The best place to get HQ pelts from is from Higher level animals. Such as adult Kodiaks in East Commonlands.
Using a different type of weapon does have an effect, blunt weapons don't ruin the pelts as often. Get a mage to kill some kodiaks with a Runed Totem staff (no spells, that ruins the pelt too) and you attack the same type of animal with a BBC or other slashing/piercing weapon and see who gets more HQ pelts. Also, If you kill something with one hit you are most likely NOT to get a pelt at all. Hitting something a lot will also ruin or not give you a pelt. Finally, I have found that killing things at higher levels does not matter. (I found some ringmail sleeves on a gnoll and a rawhide belt and I am only level 7)
