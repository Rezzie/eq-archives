Here is an attempt at summarizing my notes at Pottery. Hope someone can make good use of it.

EQ Pottery Guide
June 30, 1999

Mixing Bowl Recipe (corrected):
2 large clay
1 Large Bowl Sketch
1 Flask of Water – Combine
Fire with a Firing Sheet (NOT Quality or High Quality, just Firing Sheet)

Clay Conversion Corrections:
If you mix a large block of clay with water, you get a block of clay.
If you mix a block of clay with water, you get a small block of clay.
If you mix 3 small blocks of clay with water, you get a block of clay
If you mix 3 blocks of clay with water, you get a large block of clay.

Bad Recipes in Book:
Warning: The small bowl recipe does not make anything.

Sources of materials:
The closest place to Freeport to get firing sheets is in the East Commons. There's a small hut next to the first in that has a clay seller. But he's not always in.

The zone called the Gorge (off Runnyeye) has MOB’s called Muddites. Kill them for free clay!

In HighHold Pass, Min Greyeagle sells every sketch including mixing bowl, except pot.

Tinkerers need pie tins for their trade skill. Can sell for more than NPC offers.

Pie Tins:
Pie Tin Sketch: 1.7 silver
Flask of Water: 1.3 silver
2 Blocks of Clay: 1.4 Gold (2 x 5.2 silver)
High Quality Firing Sheet: 5 silver
Merchant Pays for finished Pie Tin: 5.4.2 Gold (twice what you paid!)
Merchant Sells for 9+ Gold

Triviality:
Firing in Kiln – Trivial at 17
Small Jars – Trival at 27
Med Jars – Trivial at 35
Pie Tins – Trivial at 115
Mixing Bowls – Trivial at 128
Large Bowls – Trivial at 148

Tips:
Never use water when firing objects in the kiln! Just the firing sheets.

I maxed out through a slightly different path:
Medium Jar went trivial at 25 for me (this was a while back, perhaps they fiddled with the numbers?)
Then I found Medium Bowl, which was cheap, and took me to 122 in a hurry.
And then Large Bowl to 148.

That may not be the optimal route, or the cheapest, but it's fast, with fewer components needed. Doing Medium Bowls at mid level skill results in an increase with almost every combination. I didn't even bother to fire the bowls, just dropped em back in the wheel and combined to destroy. Got to 148 on 12 plat.

Many thanks for the Mixing Bowl recipe! I've been wanting to do a few of those..
