Evercrest occasionally has serial strips, spanning a week or two. Those strips are listed here, along with the date range the series spanned.

Login Server Woes (3/29/99 through 4/2/99)
Drysart, Artur, Fluffy, and Mystiana get kicked off Fennin Ro for various reasons, and have a heck of a time getting onto Innoruuk.

Mystiana's Love Potion (4/19/99 through 4/23/99)
Drysart falls in love with Mystiana after accidentally drinking a love potion.... or so he thinks.

Orcish Violence (4/26/99 through 4/28/99)
Three strips dealing with the violence orcs face every day.

Galliwyn at Level 99 (5/1/99 through 5/7/99)
Galliwyn thinks he's become level 99 through a character corruption bug. He finds out the truth the hard way.

The UO PK A classic! (5/10/99 through 5/17/99)
A PK from UO accidentally recalls into Norrath, and finds that things don't work the same way in Everquest as they did in UO.

Aradune's Missing Goatee (6/2/99 through 6/4/99)
One very drunk Aradune minus one very cool looking goatee equals one very angry Aradune.

The PK Goes RP (6/21/99 through 6/24/99)
The PK turns his /roleplay switch on and becomes his friends' worst nightmare.

Enter the BFG9000 (7/4/99 through 7/9/99)
Sony Management forces Aradune to implement Quake's killer weapon, the BFG9000, into Everquest.

Galliwyn's Student (7/20/99 through 7/23/99)
Is it possible that someone looks up to Galliwyn? Find out what happens when Barishi, the hapless halfling, does exactly that.

Wrath of the Druids (7/26/99 through now)
Read the current series in progress!
