Traveler's Guide to Grobb

 Overview   Prominent Places   Legends of the Land   Neighboring Zones 

Overview
The home of the Trolls festers like a boil on the fetid underbelly of the continent Antonica. Screams of tortured frogloks resound throughout Grobb's three principal hollows and the many grottos to which they lead. The wary shopkeepers' and savage bouncers' eyes follow the infrequent visitors as they make their way throughout the city. Severed limbs and vivisected torso's of Cazic Thule's or Innoruuk's latest sacrifice litter the few open areas found in this blight on the world. While not well-versed in manners, hygiene, charity, or mercantile, trolls are resourceful. Armor, weapons, spells, and other necessities for equipping the adventurous anti-hero exist in abundance. From this secluded pit of malignancy, the race of troll's project their evil intent first on the frogloks of Innothule, and then onto the rest of Norrath.

Prominent Places

1st Bank of Grobb - The bank grotto opens onto the third hollow of Grobb, sharing this hollow with the Temple of the Dark Ones and the Sacrificial Alter.

Da Bashin Place - The Warrior's Guild in takes up the better portion of the first hollow in Grobb. Here young bashers can hone their skills at the feet of their masters.

Gunthak's Beltch - After a long day of misery-making and mayhem, the weary troll can kick back here for a tasty plate of Dwarf Pickles washed down with some Ogre Swill. Conveniently located just across the bridge from Da Bashin Place.

Krung's Clubs & Junk - This single shop has a near monopoly on sales of armor and weapons in Grobb. Krung's shares the second hollow with Nightkeep.

Nightkeep - Here the Shadowknights of Grobb plan their never ending campaign against the Greenblood Knights of Oggok.

Temple of the Dark Ones - The home of the Troll Shamen occupies the third hollow. This temple contains a seemingly bottomless pit of lava. Troll followers of Innoruuk claim the burning hate of their deity radiates from this cauldron.

Legends of the Land

Carver Cagrek - Master Torturer, Cagrek can be found awaiting his next guest atop the sacrificial alter outside the Temple of the Dark Ones.

Kaglari - This heartless She-Troll guides the shamen in their sadistic pursuits. Kaglari occupies a throne overlooking the "Heart of Innoruuk" pool of lava in the Temple of the Dark Ones.

Ranjor - Champion Basher and leader of the Da Basher's Warriors' Guild.

The troll slayers - These solitary trolls rampage through the swamp of Innothule in an attempt to prove themselves worthy of Basher status.

Neighboring Zones

Feerrott - The native land of the ogres can be found just west of Innothule Swamp. The dark forrest of Feerrott holds adventure appropriate for the starting troll or ogre. Through the olive fog, one can find lizardman camps, skeleton and zombie ruins, lizardman temples, and finally, the dread Lost Temple of Cazic Thule. Monster Difficulty: Low to Intermediate

Guk: Fortress of the Froglok - An underground metropolis populated by the reclusive frog-like race native to Innothule Swamp. Monster Difficulty: Intermediate to High

Innothule Swamp - Foreboding and confounding, travelers to the native land of the Trolls must take head or lose their way amongst the bogs and islets of this swamp. Monster Difficulty: Low to Intermediate

Desert of Southern Ro - The southern edge of this vast desert lies to the direct North of Innothule Swamp. Orc bands and undead populate this harsh landscape. Monster Difficulty: Intermediate
