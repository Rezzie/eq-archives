The three big stats for a hybrid are:

Strength, for carrying things more than anything.

Agility, for not getting hit!

Wisdom, for mana points later on.

If you want heavy armor and the ability make money by selling treasure from monsters, you better put some points into Strength.

Human Paladin Agility is very low for the punishment ahead of them.

Your stock Wisdom could always be better, but I think it's the least of your worries given your situation.

So, I would either go 5STR/15AGI or 10STR/10AGI.

Also, is it me, or did they change it so that you start with 85WIS and 80CHA rather than 80WIS and 85CHA?
