The Ashen Order Sash Quests

The following quests are found in the Ashen Order House, near the East Freeport zoneline, in West Freeport. Some noteable landmarks in the area are a large water canal and a 'mysterious' pile of 'something' on the ground near the Theater of Tranquility sign. I suggest looking at the excellent EQS Maps section, before attempting the later stages of the Sash series.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The White Training Sash

Speak with Velan Torresk, on the second floor of the Guild House.

Khamon says, 'Hail, Velan Torresk.'

Velan Torresk says 'Greetings, Khamon ! I am Velan Torresk of the Ashen Order. I am in charge of training the newest members of our clan, and helping them advance their skills and rank. When members perform certain tasks on behalf of the Order, they advance to a higher rank in our clan, and are awarded a special [sash].

Khamon says, 'What Sash, Master?'

Velan Torresk says 'The Sashes of Order are given out by the various trainers of our clan. I give out the [white training sash] and the [yellow Sash of Order], and [Reyia] is in charge of the [orange and red sashes].

Khamon says, 'What is the white training sash, Master?'

Velan Torresk says 'The white training sash of the Ashen Order is awarded to our new members for completing a few simple tasks to prove their devotion to our clan. As you know, Freeport is a very hostile place, under constant attack from [orcs], wild beasts, and even the undead. To help keep this city and its citizens relatively safe, we must help the Militia protect the main gates. Bring me two Deathfist pawn scalps from those vile [Deathfist orcs], a snake fang, and some bone chips from a skeleton. Good luck, Khamon , represent us well!

All of the quest components for this Sash can be found within the West, or East, Freeport newbie hunting grounds. He asks for the following:

  • Deathfist Pawn Scalps (2) - These can be found on Deathfist Pawns, usually seen near the West Freeport Gate, and surrounding zones.
     
  • Snake Fang (1) - These can be found on low level snakes near the West Freeport Gate, and surrounding zones.
     
  • Bone Chips (1) - These can be found on Skeletons all over Norrath. The Decaying Skeletons found near the West Freeport Gate, and surrounding zones, are probably your easiest target.

    Return to Velan Torresk with your four quest items.

    Velan Torresk says 'Good work, Khamon , you've worked hard and proven yourself a valuable addition to the Ashen Order. Here's your white sash, wear it with pride.

    White Training Sash
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +1 Sta, +1 Cha, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Yellow Sash of Order

    Speak with Velan Torresk, once again.

    Khamon says, 'What yellow sash, Master?'

    Velan Torresk says 'To earn the yellow sash, you must prove yourself to be very skilled in the art of fighting. The lands to the west and south of Freeport are filled with dangerous beasts that often prey upon innocent travelers. Help protect our merchant caravans and traveling citizens, while at the same time practicing your defensive skills, and eliminating these deadly creatures from the surrounding landscape. Bring me a giant snake rattle, a Deathfist slashed belt, the chitin of a desert tarantula, and turn in your white training sash, and I shall reward your noble work with our yellow Sash of Order. Good luck, Khamon !

    All of the components needed for this Sash can be found throughout the Commonlands and the Northern Desert of Ro. He asks for the following:
     

  • White Training Sash - See previous quest.
     
  • Deathfist Slashed Belt (1) - You can find these on Deathfist Orcs. The Orc Pawns do not drop the belts. Orc Centurions are the most common target, and can be found in the various camps throughout the Commonlands.
     
  • Giant Snake Rattle (1) - These can be found on snakes, such as an Asp or Darkweed, throughout the Commonlands and the Northern Desert of Ro.
     
  • Desert Tarantula Chitin (1) - The best chance to get one of these is in the Northern Desert of Ro. A Desert Tarantula, or a Dune Tarantula being your best target. Make sure that the Chitin is not cracked.

    Return to Velan Torresk with your four quest items.

    Velan Torresk says 'Ah, well done, Khamon. You have proven that you are a very skillful fighter and it is a honor to have you as a member of the Ashen Order. On behalf of Master Closk, and under the watchful eyes of Quellious, I present you, Khamon, with this, the yellow Sash of Order. Go out and make us proud.'

    Yellow Sash of Order
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +1 Sta, +1 Cha, +1 Agi, +1 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Orange Sash of Order

    Speak with Reyia Beslin, she's found just outside of the Guild House near the sparring Monks.

    Khamon says, 'Hail, Reyia Beslin.'

    Reyia Beslin says 'Greetings, Khamon . I am Reyia Beslin of the Ashen Order. I help to train our members and I am in charge of handing out the orange and red Sashes of Order. Would you like to know [how you can get an orange sash]? Or maybe you think you are ready to earn the [red sash]?

    Khamon says, 'How can I get an orange sash, Master?'

    Reyia Beslin says 'To prove your skills and earn the orange Sash of Order, you must bring me the following items - a greater lightstone, a cutthroat insignia ring, a legionnaire's bracer, and, of course, your yellow sash. I will leave it to you to figure out exactly where to get these items. Good luck, Khamon .

    All of the components needed for this Sash can be found throughout the Commonlands. She asks for the following:
     

  • Yellow Sash of Order - See previous quest.
     
  • Legionnaire's Bracer (1) - You can find these on Orc Legionnaires. The bracer can come from either the Deathfist Orcs of Antonica, or the Crushbone Orcs of Faydwer. These are a lot more rare than the Deathfist Slashed Belts. It may be faster to take the boat in East Freeport to the Butcherblock Mountains, and buy one from the High Elven merchant who is located near the dock you arrive at. They cost around one platinum and he seems to carry an endless supply.
     
  • Greater Lightstone (1) - These can be found on all forms of Willowisp. They can be found throughout the Commonlands, as well as many other zones across Norrath. Don't confuse this a regular Light Stone, or Burned Out Light Stone. Only the Greater will work. Willowisps are magical creatures, so remember to equip a magical weapon, or wear magical gloves before picking a fight!
     
  • Cutthroat Insignia Ring (1) - These can be found on Dervish Cutthroats and Dervish Thugs. There are many camps throughout the Northern and Southern Desert of Ro zones, and the Oasis of Marr. There is also one camp in the Western Commonlands.

    Return to Reyia Beslin with your four quest items.

    Reyia Beslin says 'You have proven yourself a mighty warrior. I am honored to present you, Khamon , with the orange Sash of Order.

    Orange Sash of Order
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +2 Sta, +1 Cha, +1 Agi, +2 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Red Sash of Order

    Speak with Reyia Beslin, once again.

    Khamon says, 'I am ready for the red sash, Master.'

    Reyia Beslin says 'So, you think you are skilled enough to earn the red Sash of Order, Khamon ? I have just the [task] for you then. But be warned, this will be the toughest challenge you have faced yet. There are many legends and myths of great heroes and ancient evils from all over Norrath. Here in Freeport, we get travelers from all over the world, and we get their tales as well. The point is that most of these stories are just that, stories and fairy tales. Told to amuse people and frighten children. I also thought the tales I'd heard about the [Marnek Jaull] were only legends, but now I know better.

    Khamon says, 'Who is Marnek Jaull, Master?'

    Reyia Beslin says 'Marnek Jaull led a cult of necromancers who followed the Burning Prince, Solusek Ro. He built an army, known as the Burning Dead, and spread destruction and fire throughout Antonica. Over the years, the Burning Dead fought many fierce and bloody battles with the paladins of Prexus, the Oceanlord. Though Marnek achieved many victories over the paladins, ultimately they were able to defeat him and destroy the Burning Dead. Legends say that before his defeat, Marnek created many powerful weapons and items, and wrote many books on his findings in the areas of magic. Many of these items were hidden away from the paladin forces, and remain untouched, waiting for Marnek's return, as the legends go.

    Khamon says, 'What task, Master?'

    Reyia Beslin says 'I've heard a few legends of a powerful wand that Marnek himself used to wield. This was known as the wand of the Burning Dead, and was rumored to instill its owner with mystical energies. The legends I have heard all differed on the wand's creation and powers, but the basic story was always the same. I will [tell you the legend] if you like. The task I have for you is to bring me the two parts that make up the wand of the Burning Dead, before they can be assembled and used for evil once again. Bring me [Marnek's wand] and the [Sapphire of Souls], along with your orange sash, and you will have proven yourself a loyal member of the Ashen Order, worthy of receiving the red sash.

    Khamon says, 'I would like you to tell me the legend, Master.'

    Reyia Beslin says 'The legend of the wand of the Burning Dead goes basically like this, though you may hear slighty different versions from others. Marnek did a lot of experiments and created an unknown number of diabolical artifacts and weapons. One of his creations was this wand. Marnek fashioned a charred wand from the scorched bone of a paladin of Prexus, and Solusek Ro himself created the wretched Sapphire of Souls. Then, Marnek and his priests combined these, with the Burning Prince's blessing, to create the wand of the Burning Dead. Marnek used this Wand and its powers in his evil and destructive reign over Antonica. Years later, as Marnek starting losing battles to the paladins of Prexus, one of his own followers, a necromancer named Kenox, was able to steal the wand. Sensing Marnek's defeat, Kenox made his way toward Odus, hoping to strike a deal with the paladins for the wand. Would you like me to [tell you about Kenox]?

    Khamon says, 'I would like you to tell me about Kenox, Master.'

    Reyia Beslin says 'Well, Kenox was not as sly as he liked to think he was. Marnek knew full well what he was up to, but let him continue with his plans. Kenox had arranged to meet with two paladins late one nght on a small island in Erud's Crossing, a place that would be considered neutral ground. Sir Toran Neshal, leader of the paladins, knowing the power of the wand, was certainly not going to make any deals with a servant of Solusek Ro, and definitely not foolish enough to take any chances with this mission. Imagine the look on Kenox's face when a ship carrying more than 25 paladins, including Sir Toran, arrived at the small island! The paladins immediately captured Kenox and the wand, and headed back to Erudin. Would like me to [tell you what happened to Toran]?

    Khamon says, 'I would like you to tell me what happened to Toran, Master.'

    Reyia Beslin says 'Below the deck of the ship, Toran and his men questioned Kenox and carefully inspected the wand. Toran ordered one of his men to pick up the wand and try to identify its true powers. The man picked up the wand, but was unable to decipher its magic. He handed it to Toran, just as Marnek and Solusek had hoped, and the wand exploded, instantly incinerating the ship and everyone aboard, and lighting up the night sky with a giant fireball. Marnek's plan had succeeded. With the help and blessing of the Burning Prince, they had destroyed Sir Toran and an entire squad of Prexus' finest paladins. Years later, though, the paladins were able to regroup and ultimately defeat Marnek. The wand of the Burning Dead was destroyed and gone forever. Or least, that's what we thought. This wand must never be allowed to be made whole again.

    Khamon says, 'What is Marnek's wand, Master?'

    Reyia Beslin says 'Marnek's wand is rumored to be found in the depths of Befallen. Those who currently hold Befallen are said to be new followers of the Burning Dead. Probably believing that they may one day be able to awaken the evil Marnek again. This group must be stopped, and these evil items destroyed, before their wicked armies grow powerful enough to leave their horrible dungeon and spread throughout Antonica.'

    Khamon says, 'What is the Sapphire of Souls, Master?'

    Reyia Beslin says 'The Sapphire of Souls is rumored to be held by a thief in Najena. No one knows how this guy obtained the sapphire, but we believe he is trying to strike a bargain with those who are currently holding Befallen. Apparently, we aren't the only ones who know of the wand's powers, and this thief thinks he can make a profit from it. Sadly, his greed will surely lead to his death.'

    The components needed for this Sash can be found in the dungeons of Befallen and Najena. She asks for the following:
     

  • Orange Sash of Order - See previous quest.
     
  • The Sapphire of Souls - You must head to the Najena zone for this item. It is found on a rare NPC named Ekeros. His room is right next to the room marked "C" on EQS' Najena map. When looted, it's called "A Blackened Sapphire." Najena is set up so that you need a series of keys to get deeper into the dungeon. The first three keys are needed to reach Ekeros' jail cell.
     
    • The first key drops from Ogre Guards found throughout many rooms in Najena. Be sure to get this key before venturing too deep into the dungeon because there are two pitfalls that drop you into locked jail cells. The Guards Keyring will allow you to get out.
       
    • The second key can be found on Bonecracker, an Undead Froglok with two Necromancer bodyguards. His room is marked "A" on the Najena map.
       
    • The third key can be found on the Ogre Captain, who is surrounded with Ogre Guards. His room is marked "B" on the Najena map.
  • The Wand of Marnek - This item takes you to the Befallen zone. It is found on a rare NPC named Priest Amiaz. To reach him you must make your way to the bottom level of the dungeon, using a series of keys dropped by Shadowknights along the way. Upon opening the locked door to the bottom level, turn right and continue down the hall. Hug the wall on the right hand side until you stumble though a hidden doorway. You'll see a large wooden chair in front of you. Go beyond it and stick to the wall on the left hand side. You'll see three tunnels branch off ahead of you, choose the left most tunnel and continue on until you reach the end. In this room there are three spawn points. Priest Amiaz is the left most one and has place holders. When looted, it's called "A Blackened Wand." While here, stick around for The Thaumaturgist, who is located in the center spawn point of the same room. Her dagger will be needed to complete one of the Silent Fist Headband quests in Qeynos.

    Return to Reyia Beslin with your three quest items.

    Reyia Beslin says 'Great job, Khamon ! Congratulations. With the destruction of these evil items, the wand of the Burning Dead will never bring harm to anyone on Norrath again. It is my honor to present to you, on behalf of Master Closk and the Ashen Order, the red sash. May Quellious be with you always.

    Khamon says, "Aww, shucks.. It was nothing."

    Red Sash of Order
    (Magic, Lore, 0.0wt, +2 Sta, +2 Agi, +1 Dex, +2 Cha, +2 HP, Class: Mnk, Race: Hum Iks)
