Are you tired of the "this class is better" or "I want my class to have..." you hear all of the time? Me too! Therefore I've come up with the perfect, all around kickass character class that everyone will want to play: the Drunken Bastard (DBA)

Skills: the DBA is the master of Alcohol Tolerance. While it may not be useful for other classes, a high AT makes the DBA almost godlike. Here are some situations, and how a DBA would handle them:

Traveling across Norrath - while most people would really like to have SoW, the DBA scoffs at this weakness. Solution - Drink your butt off. After that, who cares how fast you're moving? You'll never get where you want to go anyway.

Fighting single MOBS - most classes fall into two schools of fighting here: bash them to death or throw mojo at them until they stop breathing. DBA's don't fall into either class. Solution - Drink your butt off. After the third time you throw up on yourself and trip over your own footprints, most MOBs will die laughing at you.

Fighting a group of MOBS - usually the answer is to group up. This is not the DBA way. Solution - Drink your butt off. Then hand out more alcohol to the MOBS and hope their AT is lower than yours. When they fall down drunk, whack them over the head with the nearest (empty) brew bottle.

Healing - most people want a cleric around to heal their wounds. You guessed it...DBA's do not need clerics. Solution - Drink your butt off. No pain, no pain.

Splitting the loot - DBA's are great for grouping with, because they usually can't remember when their turn is in the loot rotation. Be careful though...if they don't have enough money to buy their next round of brew, they will be VERY cranky when they sober up.

DBA's will never complain that they can't do things other classes can do. Generally, they don't even have a clue what the other classes can do anyway. They never have to worry about picking a trade skill...they are created with a brew cask in their inventory. They never have to worry about armor or spells. Heck, they rarely even have to worry about buying food. You will never see a twinked DBA walking around. Heck, you will be lucky to even see ANY DBA walking around.

So in closing, I think this would be a great addition to an already good game.
