***** I want to know which idiot at Verant decided on the prices of staffs. Does this guy have a wood fetish or something? I've seen blatant stupidity (cough cough Bola cough cough hehe), and this goes way beyond blatant stupidity.

Lets look at a sword shall we....how long does it take to make one? what kind of skill is required, the materials needed? Swords cannot be made by the comman man...you must have a skilled blacksmith to make one. You can sell a rusty long sword for about 1.5 gold pieces in Norrath.

Lets look at a staff shall we....its a branch....or a small trunk....it might be bound with some leather. It isn't hard to make....almost anyone would be able to fashion one. You can sell a splintered staff for over a platinum piece.

HELLLOOOOOOOOOOO?????? MCFLY.....ANYONE HOME????

A two handed staff is over 22pp in cost. Why? its a big stick! 12-15 pp for a quarter staff! why?!? its a slightly smaller stick! You can sell a worn club for over 2 gp! its a club....its not even a worked stick...you picked it off the ground..and you hit someone with it...ta da! its a club. But those clubs sell more than a rusted long sword.

Does this really effect me in the game? No...I don't play a character that uses staffs or clubs....but its just plain dumb. Its wrong, stupid, idiotic, and needs to be fixed. Sure I like being able to make a pp off of a decaying skeleton..its fun....but its wrong.
