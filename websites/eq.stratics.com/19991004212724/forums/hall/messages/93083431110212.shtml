I roleplay alot, I love to and its fun and takes me away from my horrible days at my job.

Yeah i dont like my job, but dont give me "find another one line", just like saying you dont like one thing in EQ "Stop playing".

Anyways.

Ok, Im a Warrior, honorable bladesman and all that. My wife is a Druid, wood elf GODESS...hehe..ok.

Now Im not to slay the unicorn for the slain unicorn shield thing, warriors only.

How can I roleplay that with others who i think are wrong with attacking/killing NPCs/MOBs who are good?

Aviaks for isntance. I would love to stuff my sword down those stupid druids throats who kill these amiable creatures.

Centaurs, they dont like/dislike anyone.
So why the hell kill them?

Becuase you roleplay an evil character? Well then how can I roleplay a good character?

I cant heal the monster, buff them or help them in anyway. You can easily just kill them.

I cant attack you without your consent, and even then it wont do any good if I win. I cant take anything from you although your body is laying right in front of me.

Non PVP is fine, I enjoy walking around in my nice items and even being vain sometimes.

I am all for a non PVP server and a PVP server.

But on the PVP server it should be all or nothing. Your win over this or that person should spell doom for the other person.

This is all just my OPINION Eberon. Please no flames, just comments from everyone.

Why cant I pry that sword out of his hands, or rip his armor off his corpse?

Its truly redicilous. You cant really roleplay a racial war in this game. Even when the race war servers come up. Yeah I killed that Inky bastard, didnt get squat for the half hour long fight.

Sorry but for me, even as a good guy, rewards should be more then pride.
