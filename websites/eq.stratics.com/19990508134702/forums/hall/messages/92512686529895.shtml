I was running solo when I was jumped by a couple of mobs, one of which was well above my ability to dispatch. I was well away from a zone border or any guards. I knew there were a couple of people nearby on the road, a tank and a wizard. I was dead without help, so I backed up to within the wizards spell range and stood my ground. I saw a few range spells hit the tough mob and it was down to 1/2 health before I was killed. When I went back to pick up my gear, I saw the corpses of the two on the road.

My question is, should I have approached these two? I didn't ask for help and they had plenty of time to escape. Perhaps I should have stood where I was jumped and taken my medicine.
