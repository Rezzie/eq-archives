Personally I like the last few statements. Don't treat it as a race. The main problem with EQ is that people want more more more for doing less less less. Give me more of this, more of that, then I don't have to work so hard and I can level quicker and have more cool stuff than others. If that happens, then everyone has the same cools stuff as you, you don't appreciate it as much, and you level quicker, hit high levels faster and then stop playing since you don't want to start all over again killing bats after killing dragons. Just enjoy the game as is. If it was SO bad, it would have been adjusted in Beta. The real problem is that we have all these Doom, hexen etc shootem up playeres that want to blast their way through fast and get to the next level quickly. Whine whine whine, I want more more more. Live your character, be your character, love your character, act out your character, do the quests for the enjoyment of doing it, work for your experience instead of it handed to you, earn your money instead of hoping for random spawns etc etc etc. Just live with the game as is and enjoy it instead of wishing it was easier.
