Traveler's Guide to Rivervale

 Overview   Prominent Places   Legends of the Land   Neighboring Zones 

Overview
The home of the Halflings is a quaint, small town located near the center of the continent Antonica, and is one of the smallest of all races' starting cities. Resembling the Shire from the classic Lord of the Rings, this hamlet is surrounded with an aura of cheer. However, don't mistake the attitude of the Halflings as oblivious contentment--the stout folk are quite aware and ready to deal with the problems stemming from the nearby camps of Orcs and Goblins. The Deputies (guard force) and adventurers of Rivervale are constantly venturing into the nearby Misty Thicket in order to curb this constant threat to their beloved community.

Prominent Places

Church of Mischief (Clerics' Guild) - The Clerics' Guild in Rivervale. Also a haven for the wounded adventurer in need of healing.

Druids' Guild - Go behind the Fool's Gold, follow the path and turn left. There stands the Druids' Guild of Rivervale. There is also a foodstand nearby, from which you can purchase foodstuffs and drink.

The Fool's Gold (Tavern and Rogues' Guild) - This is a three-storied tavern as well as the Rogues' Guild in Rivervale. Probably the largest building in the 'Vale, its first floor is dedicated to the consumption of drinks, the second for cards and gambling, and the top floor to Lendal Deeppockets, the leader of the Rogues' Guild, who makes his home there.

Town Hall (Bank and Warriors' Guild) - The Rivervale Town Hall is large, castle-like building that serves as a courthouse, bank, weapons store, and Warriors' Guild. On the first floor there is a merchant who is always willing to sell weapons of high quality, as well as a banker who handles deposits and withdrawels of valuables. On the third floor is where Sheriff Raglio and Mayor Gibbon may be found.

Will Tagglefoot's Farmstead (Druids' Guild) - Run by the Tagglefoot family and located in the Eastern corner of the city, this farm is home to both Shakey the Scarecrow and the Storm Keeper Druids.

Legends of the Land

Ember - A baby firebeetle who follows her master. If attacked, her aforementioned master will make minced meat out of even the most seasoned adventurer.

Lendal Deeppockets - Leader of the Deeppocket Rogues' Guild of Rivervale.

Mangler - A wolf that dwells within the local Blacksmith's shop. He will attack if you come near, but is easily dispatched by most adventurers.

Mayor Gibbon - A cheery little man who puts the needs of Rivervale above anything else.

The Ogre of the Tower - A lone Ogre warrior that stands sentinal over a ruined tower just outside of Rivervale and within Misty Thicket. His intentions are unknown, as he attacks no monster or man.

Nillipuss - A very tiny person who goes running around Rivervale taunting visitors and inhabitants alike. Either an imp or golem of some sort, Nillipus is known to recite a rhyme of warning to those he passes: "Nillipus, Nillipus is my name. Stealing Jumjum is my game. Think you can catch me? Let's see if you can. I'll always run faster than you've ever ran."

Shakey - A living scarecrow stationed within a small patch of crops in Will Tagglefoot's Farmstead. Magical in nature, Shakey laughs all day and night long, weaving his magic.

Sheriff Raglio - Leader of the Warriors' Guild and head of the Deputy guard force within Rivervale. A callous individual if approached by a non-fighter class.

Neighboring Zones

Highpass Hold - The precipitous manmade gateway between the Eastern and Western halves of Antonica. Reached from Rivervale by trekking through Kithicor Woods. Monster difficulty: Intermediate to High

Kithicor Woods - A dark and ancient forest which, in addition to Rivervale, borders both Highpass Hold and the Western Commonlands. Monster difficulty: Low to Intermediate

Misty Thicket - Characterized by rolling green hills, Halfling mounds and age-old stonework, the Thicket is the first place that most low level Halflings venture to gain experience. Monster difficulty: Low to Intermediate

Runneye Citadel - The evil home of the Goblin raiders that live in and around Misty Thicket. Monster difficulty: Intermediate to High

Western Commonlands - The desolate barrens that fill a good portion of the continent and marks the beginning of the journey to Freeport, the largest Human city in Norrath. Reached from Rivervale through Kithicor Woods. Monster difficulty: Intermediate to High
