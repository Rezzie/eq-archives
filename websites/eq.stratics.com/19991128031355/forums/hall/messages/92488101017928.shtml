This may seem a bit too simple to be true, but the /who command will tell you where people in your level range are hanging out the most. For instance if you are say level 17 you can do /who 15 17 all and it will tell you the most popular spots for your level. Then goto sites like EQ'lizer and find out what zones you can handle at your level. Then get a group of friends and go hunt at the least popular zones. Which would you rather do? Goto overcrowded places like Unrest or find some nice spot where you and your friends will level nicely without have to worry about a big crowd.
