Join the EverQuest Stratics Team

Dedicated? Committed? Want to see your name in lights? Join us!

EverQuest Stratics is expanding and growing. There are many exciting things planned, and we want you to be a part of it!

EQSS is looking for dedicated, committed, creative people to join us and fill out positions in various departments. If you're interested in joining up with one of the top gaming networks in the industry, read on to find out how to submit your application.

NEWS

We have several News Team positions open. If you're interested, send us an email.  Specifically, the following is needed:

  • General News Reporter: The primary responsibility of this department is to be a "board troll." By that we mean, to poke around the net every day looking for Verant staff (Absor, Abashi, etc) postings. Most of these will be found on the Gameplay boards, of course, but there are many other community boards where they stop by, including our own. The people in this department are responsible for digging up these replies and posting them under our General News category, as well as Patch messages, downtime notices, and anything else directly gameplay - related. Being able to post during the day is a must for this position.
  • Community News Reporter: This department covers the roleplaying part of EverQuest. All events (such as weddings, open raids, bazaars, etc) go to this department. These events are then posted under our Community News category so those submitting them can have their events publicized. Also, reporters in this department must learn to seek out events/happenings around the various game-related sites/servers and include them in their daily postings. Being able to post during the day is a must for this position.

CONTENT

  • Class Section Leaders: Class Section Leaders are responsible for the content in an individual class section here at EQSS. This will include:
    • Have a great deal of experience with the class in question. Someone who has been there who has advanced to the later stages of the class (50+). Now, an important distinction needs to be made here. We need someone who has PLAYED this class, not just power leveled their way to 50.
    • Be excited about the role and have a willingness to encourage discussion about the class through feedback, forum use, etc. We want whoever oversees these areas to really over see them and be INVOLVED. Not just type up some guides and upload them to the site. We want people, who are excited about their class, want to help others and be involved in an ACTIVE way with the area. This should be considered a very KEY and CRITICAL prerequisite for these positions.
    • Have a clear understanding of what it is we are trying to do with these areas.
    • Have the time available to commit an average of four hours or so a week to the staff.
    • An ability to write well. They will be expected to write guides, a weekly or bi-weekly column and also post a lot in their respective class forum.
  • Trade Skill Section Leaders: These sections will be very similar to the class sections, however will be dealing with trade skills. All of the same requirements apply towards this section as those that apply towards the Class Section Leaders, just with various changes in terminology towards trade skills rather than classes.
  • Content Editors: Content Editors are responsible for pouring over the existing content on the site and making sure it is up-to-date and current/correct. If it is not, they correct it and submit it to the Lead Editor for submission. They are also welcome to submit their own guides/databases if they so wish.
  • Other Submissions: You don't have to be an active EQSS staff member to submit content. Simply send it to our Lead Editor. This includes guides, articles, editorials, spell/item database updates, artwork, and anything else creative you might have thought up.

If you wish to become are interested in any of these positions, please send a 250 word document to our editorial staff, explaining why you love Everquest!

FORUMS

  • Moderators: If you see a Forum that currently has no Moderator (or that is dormant) that you think you can revitalize, contact the EQSS Forum Administrator Team. We are looking for new moderators who will not only come in and moderate a forum, but who will come in and revitalize that forum.  We want people who are willing to be active on that community, who will go out and advertise that community, and who will oversee that community as it develops.  Being already active on our current forums is a plus but not required.

You are not required to submit a writing sample or anything of that sort with your application. We are looking for interesting, dedicated people and do not want to scare anyone off with a critique. If you are unsure how to word a news post, for example, we will show you. The main qualities we are looking for are dedication and commitment, not the ability to write a novel.

 Questions? Email Lord Of Dreams.
