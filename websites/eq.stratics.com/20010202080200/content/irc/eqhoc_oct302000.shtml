EverQuest House of Commons - Developer Chat with Abashi and the Team

On October 30, 2000 in the #EQHoC channel of irc.stratics.com, Verant Interactive Developers joined with IRC Regulars to hold the first EQSS House of Commons!

Members of the Verant Staff Present were: Abashi, Absor, Dovasirr, Hobart, Mrylokar, Rowyl, and Tolan.

Special thanks go to draft, Moonshadow, NickyD, Ogamok and Thundolfe for managing the questions throughout the session!

Here now is the transcript from the first EQSS House of Commons!
 
 
-#eqhoc- WELCOME!
   
Greebo Thank you all for coming to the EverQuest House of Commons! This is, to my knowledge, the first Stratics EverQuest House of Commons and so I would like to start off by extending a special thanks to Gordon "Abashi" Wrinn for his help in helping us get this set up.
 
Greebo I need to go over a few of the groundrules very quickly before we get things started, so please bear with me.
  This is a moderated chat session. I will be moderating this session but I will not be doing much of the actual speaking. Public discussion during this session is not allowed.
  We will be taking questions privately and relaying them publicly to the developers. We have a few question takers assigned who will be taking your questions and relaying them to the developers.
  I know you all will have lots of questions - please only ask them your question once and only send one question taker your question.
  Sending your question to all of our takers will not increase your chance of getting your question answered and spamming will result in you being removed from the channel, and possibly from the Stratics IRC network.
   
Greebo The Question Takers tonight are: Moonshadow, Ogamok, Thundolfe, draft and NickyD - Say Hi Guys!
Ogamok Good Evening everyone
draft Hiyas
Greebo Send one of them your question by typing: /msg <nick> Your Question
  Where <nick> is the name of the Question taker. If you have any administrative type questions, please /msg me, but do not send your EverQuest Questions to me, as I will not be participating in the actual Q&A.
  Now that we've got the basic rules out of the way, lets go ahead and get things started!
 
Greebo With us tonight are our special guests from Verant. At this time I ask them each to take a moment to introduce themselves...
Abashi Hello all. If you don't already know me, I'm Gordon Wrinn, Associate Producer of the EQLive and Scars of Velious Teams. I'm far less important that virtually everyone else on the team so make no mistake that they are the ones who do all of the *real* work. I just talk about it and take all the credit :)
Rowyl Hiya folks
Absor Hello. I'm Alan 'Absor' VanCouvering. I'm the new Community Relations Manager.
Dovasirr Dovasirr here. I'm Jason Polk, game designer, artist, and coder.
Hobart I am Hobart, Associate Programmer on EQLive, Scars of Velious. You can catch me on the QA board or on Test as Hobart.
Dovasirr thats about it =) all in all
Mrylokar Hi all, Lawrence Poe Aka Mrylokar Associate Game Deisnger, mainly I do pathing.
Tolan Hello everyone, Im Javier Jimenez. Im an associate game designer on Velious and EQ Live. One of my duties, but not the only one, includes all spell issues.
 
draft *Forensic|MC* What do you think about the new games coming out soon like Shadowbane? How do you plan on competing with these new games with better graphics/gameplay/features/etc?
Abashi ShAdOwBaNe R0xx0rs! But seriously, we are all looking forward to taking a look at that game, and think that it will be a lot of fun. That said, we still think that a primarily PvE environment will always have a place in the online gaming world. New games will come out all of the time with better graphics and gameplay, and it's our task as game designers to keep up. We'll continue doing that in new and interesting ways,
Abashi but I don't want to give away all our secrets.... done
Greebo Any other commentary?
Tolan I think Shadowbane will be interesting for its niche. Im looking forward to killing a few of my friends ;)
Mag lol
Dovasirr I know you're on Mrylokar's List.
 
Greebo Ok - next question?
Ogamok *GD|ykz* Question: Gordon how do you think Verant is protrayed amongst the gaming populace of today?
Abashi Well I suppose you mean from the standpoint of reputation, and that's an area where we, and every other company can continue to improve. Overall, I think that we have a good reputation as witnessed by the fact that we keep growing. In fact, we hit 300K subscribers today. EQ's growth has always been driven by word of mouth more than anything... (cont)
  ...and that shows that we're doing something right. I think that a certain amount of the reputation is deserved, and those are the areas that we need to improve, but a lot of it is due to being on top. We'll continue to improve, just hang in there with us, and remember that honey attracts more flies than vinegar :) done
 
Falkieri Question: Whenever are you going to fix up those awful helmet armour graphics, especially High Elf helmets?
Rowyl Part of the new armor enhancement includes a slew of new helms, I added some code to allow us to attach helms similar the way we attach weapons and shields so we can have many new styles of helms. Not just new textures but geometry.
Abashi So in other words, we've heard the call of the bucket-heads
Tolan And the bird cage heads, and the samurai helmet heads, etc ;)
 
Ogamok *Beldukil* My question is for Abashi: Many months ago you agreed there was a lack of paladin specific weapons in Kunark especially since most 1h weapons are meant for duel wielders. Can you comment on the lack of any new paladin weapons in the game in the last 4 months since you made that statement?
Hobart With this upcomming patch Paladins will be given the piercing skill. Off of which they will be able to use 2handed piercing weapons. These weapons will be introduced before and during the release of SoV.
 
Greebo General Scars of Vellious for all devs: What would each of you say is the main thing customers can expect to get out of the upcoming expansion to EverQuest?
Dovasirr Its different for us all, since we all have a specific interest....For me, I would say higher poly npc models...but, thats just me =)
  helps the old, immersive feeling =)
Greebo How about everyone else on the Dev team?
Abashi In a word: more. More of the things that people have said that they enjoy. We've gone to great lengths to adopt some of the suggestions that we've received, and you'll see a lot of that in Velious, even some thing that we've said "wasnt' possible given the current game engine". Velious not only includes more, but opens the door for *much more* going forward.
Mrylokar I think the level of immersion in Velious will be much higher than previous installments. Players should enter Velious feeling as if they are playing an active role in shaping the new continent. And of course the pathing wil be great :)
Rowyl The main thing I am working on is a revamped fullview GUI (for when you play in 800x600 or greater, we will support up to 1600x1200 also), which will remove the need to switch back and forth to the "windowed" mode.
  Also, the new Dx7 interfaces allow us to turn on features that previously would not work.
  So, even thought the engine is a little old, we are giving it a little bit of a face lift.
 
Ogamok *Catalyst-Everlore* Is there any chance we'll get a sneak preview of the new User Interface? So we could offer our own suggestions on improving it and making it more functional?
Abashi Well, you've been seeing a bit of that every week. After the patch tomorrow, you'll notice that we've added a number of additional options, such as 108 new programable socials, and far more customizable keyboard options. It just continues from there. Rowyl isn't stopping any time soon.
  done. :)
 
Greebo Follow ups on the Graphics, Question takers?
Greebo Thundolfe?
Thundolfe (Tanilin) Is there any chance of the erudite plate helm issue being fixed? (The issue is that our helms have no texture of their own, they rely on the graphic from the gloves you are wearing)
Dovasirr I'll go ahead and fix that erudite helm problem tonight =)
  it will go on the new CD.
 
Greebo Ask and ye shall receive :)
  I also have a follow up on the Scars expansion...
Dovasirr it will eventually hit patch, but its a big file....done
 
Greebo More polygons were mentioned - will there be any support to limit the number of polygon extras - like trees - other than the clipping plane in SoV for those people with lower end platforms?
  For example something to turn off moving trees, and such?
Rowyl Moving trees has NO EFFECT at all on the framerate. It's the number of trees in the scene not that they are moving that slows things down.
 
Greebo So then clipping plane will be the only way to control that?
  Sorry I'm hogging the questions - moving on :)
Rowyl One thing that will now work better in D3D is mip mapping, plus Gamma controls. Some new things we can add support for now are vertex buffers (which helps with performance on many cards), more LODs for models besides the characters, better lighting controls, and maybe even HW T&L.
  A small thing I am also planning to do is allow you to control particles better, setting environment, spell, and item particle density independantly
Greebo Ok - thanks - I know thats a BIG question among a lot of lower end pc users
 
Greebo Hobart had a follow up on the piercing question from earlier.
Rowyl Dx7 also has better Texture management, and support for more Texture formats, so we can take advantage of DXTC and gain performance that way too.
Hobart I want to clarify one thing on the Paladin Piercing. We will not be making any one hand weapons for Paladins that use the skill piercing. We will be making 2 handed weapons that try and cover the lance and polearm weapons that go off of one hand piercing. Cont..
  That being said, anyone who had the piercing skill will be able to use a 2handed piercing weapon. However, right now we are limiting that to only Paladins, SK's and Warriors. As we evaluate how these weapons perform, we may introduce 2handed piercing weapons for other classes. Cont..
  Also, wanted to say that piercing for Paladins won't be in in this patch. But will be the next one. Sorry.
 
Beldukil Will existing helms be changed or ust new ones we have to buy veloius for?
Abashi There will be some graphics enhancements that will only be viewable by people who purchase Velious, due to its higher system requirements. Exactly what will be visible by those who don't versus those who do is still being worked out.
 
Xanthus-Everlore I have read that there will be new planes added with the Velious expansion. What additional information can you give us about those, such as level range and will the level 47 plane cap be raised for them?
Tolan Planes in Velious will be handled like the old planes. The level requirement for entering the planes will stay the same but the level range will be better balanced to accomodate groups up to level 60. Unlike our old planes which were balanced for groups up to 50.
Greebo Any comments or followups on the planes question?
Abashi Not here, that about covers it :)
 
Ogamok *Thornwind|Combine* Question: Will SoV be just another expansion a la Kunark, that will, provide new things to do (they became quite boring after a little while, you know), but nothing really new when it comes to gameplay and such?
Abashi Well it is an expansion, but we've searched out new ways to make it exciting, both in way similar to the existing world while expanding into new areas and ideas. I think it's fair to say that it will be a new experience for everyone who goes, while still retaining the best facets of EQ.
Abashi As mentioned earlier, we've taken a lot of the suggestions that we've heard from the players and have tried to make them a reality wherever possible, and wherever consistant with the EverQuest world. .. done.
Smegal I'm wondering if it was the intention to make Gorenaire one of the 3 hardest mobs in all off everquest? Right up there with Hoshkar and Phara Dar.
Hobart The outdoor dragons were balanced for level 60 groups and for outdoor areas. Gorenaire may be more difficult because she is in an outdoor zone that is frequented by many people. Thus, there is no difficulty in getting a large group of people to fight her. (done)
 
Ogamok *Beldukil* Can you comment on what is being done to make underused and unused zones such as chardok and the hole to be more popular for normal groups (ie other than those getting epic quest pieces). its a shame to see these zones go to waste
Dovasirr Well, we ARE aware of what zones are underused. Every so often (when we are not in crunch), we re-evaluate the zones that are underused, and see what we can do with them. I imagine after Velious ships, we can take a good look at those underused zones and have at them with a surgeons knife.
 
Thundolfe (Desbarolis) Rowyl: Any chance the hotkey box could be resizeable, similar to the spell box? I play with a key config that would benefit more from a 3x3 hotkey box than a 2x5. Very low priority I know, but since your working on related stuff already ...
Rowyl Resizing the hotbutton box and reorienting the spells gems are on my list to do as low priority items. I can't promise they will be in SoV, but they are planned eventually.
 
cyglorx Will races that are currently not able to wear robes be able to in the future? Such as dwarves, half elves, trolls, etc.?
Dovasirr I presume the question is, will they wear them in Velious. No, unfortunately. It doesn't mean that robes for the above races are out of the question, and in all honesty, something I want to see in the game. Unfortunately, we haven't the resources. As you know, we make a habit of adding little things here and there. I'm not making any promises, but they may show up in the future.
Tolan Something Id like to see alot too. I have a wood elf druid who's jonesing for a robe =)
Rowyl Robes GOOOD!
 
Aadyn When will druids be able to get a full set of Kunark armor...ES boots still dont drop?
Tolan Thats something Ill look into tonight Aadyn. Ill fix that one up and make sure they drop appropriately.
Abashi But it won't be in tomorrow's patch, as we've locked data for that :)
 
Aran_Quendalieva Will SoV come with new quests, especially for druids?
Dovasirr Druids do quests? =)
Ogamok BAH!
Rowyl plays a druid
Tolan Yes, lots of quests. All of us working on content have put alot of time into quests
Tolan There should be some druid specific ones too. Theres a couple of appropriate places for them in Velious ;)
 
Ogamok *Thornwind|Combine* Question: EQ was a game with a very weak story line, especially if you consider that is is a game geared mostly towards PvE and RolePlaying. Original EQ had like no backstory at all, and with Kunark a new continent just magically popped out of the sea, will SoV change any of this? Any other comments on my statement?
Tolan As Jeff Butler has said in many of the Velious previews the storyline, background, and history for Velious is something very important to us and also something that plays a large role in the way Velious flows as a game.
Dovasirr Actually, EQ doesn't have a weak storyline. Not all of it is available to every player. Many pieces can be put together to form a background story. There is also a great deal of storyline that we have left out. It is the goal of the Quest Troupe to make sure that storline is realized.
Greebo If I may interject a comment on story line...
Rowyl Kunark didn't just pop out of the sea! it was undiscovered land until recently
Dovasirr by all means =)
Greebo Many people know I was a former Guide and had the honour of being on the "inside" of several of the epic storyline quests. (Dont worry I wont give away anything)
Greebo Its easy, I'm afraid, for people to MISS the storyline
Abashi There are websites that make a point of putting together storyline for mass consumption. EQ's story line was meant to be discovered by people in game rather than just being printed up for all to read. It's certainly there for people who wish to seek it out.
Greebo Because nothing is pre-announced - take the Halloween event last year - total surprize, major change to the game came from it, but if you werent' in kithicor at the time
Greebo well...you missed it. :)
Greebo Sort of like real life...
Rowyl You can't learn the story camping the Ghoul lord in guk
Abashi that's one of the reasons we've opened the Harpy's Head Tavern.
Dovasirr one thing we didn't want to do is hand everyone the story of the world. We want players to explore every nook and cranny and get what they can out the npc's and world.
Ogamok but but... you mean I have to stop camping the ghoul lord?
Rowyl Ogamok: only if you want to experience more story
Rowyl You have to explore and do quests and interact with the game to experience the story...
 
Dakuel I understand Barbarians will have a new city (hopefully more than the hovel of Halas) in Velious, so it's assumed that other good races can go there. Will being an evil race in Velious present a great deal more trouble than if one were a good race?
Greebo I'm sorry to say this will be the *last* question - we're over an hour now.
Dovasirr Grr....This is just another example of why people shouldn't believe everything they see on web pages. There is NO Barbarian city. Will good and evil be able to go to Velious...Of course! Can I give you the specifics about all the races in Velious? No, we want you to explore. =)
Tolan Dont worry, anyone who wants to get into Velious will be able to, regardless of faction or alignment.
 
Greebo Ok - thank you all very very much for being here - are there any final comments from the developers before we wrap this up?
Dovasirr We have to do this more often =)
Thundolfe Absor: SAY SOMETHING
Thundolfe :P
Tolan Thanks for letting me drop by. It was fun. Sorry for not answering everyones questions
Absor Something?
Thundolfe There you go =)
Greebo Hey! Absor speaks!
Ogamok Woot! He can speak!
Rowyl I hope you all enjoy playing Velious as much as we enjoy making it
Abashi Yes! Thanks to everyone who came and thanks to the moderators for getting rid of everyone who shouldn't have :) As long as these are plesant, I think that we can start doing chats on a more regular basis.
Greebo Thank you all very much for being here. A transcript of this session will be posted on eq.stratics.com. Information about the next EQHoC will be coming soon!
 
-#eqhoc- This concludes this EverQuest House of Commons! Thanks to all for coming!
