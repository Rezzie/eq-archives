Editorial: Wood Elves Make The Best Warriors!

Granted, most of the people who are reading this probably think I'm nuts.  But give me some time, and I'll give you a good argument for why I believe Wood Elves make great warriors.

Sure, we're not the strongest and we're by no means the most intimidating, but that's not all there is to a warrior, contrary to popular belief.  Lets say you make an Ogre Warrior.  You're big, scary, strong, and about as intelligent as the plate you wear.  You got brawn, that nifty slam skill, and that's about it for ya.  Trolls are pretty much the same, only you have regeneration (which puts Trolls as my second choice for a warrior. That is beside the point right now).  Again, however, you're not too bright.  Same with a Barbarian: not as strong, a little smarter, but not all that agile either.  You see, the thing is that smarts count.  Intelligence makes your skills move. I don't care how big and scary you are. If you don't have the intelligence to get the skills, size isn't gonna make a difference.

Granted Barbarians, Ogres, and Trolls have that Slam ability that'll have them 'bashing' even with dual wield.  That's a big plus, but really now, how effective is that gonna be when you're fighting Nagafen?  Not very.  Stamina's another big thing, as a Wood Elf you won't have as much health as a Troll, Ogre, or Barbarian.

By now you must be thinkin I've totally blown my case.  But here's the good parts!  Agility!  Agility is HUGE!  Agility defines your armor class, and how much you can dodge attacks.  Okay so you're a big Ogre, you're as agile as a boulder going uphill.  So you can take more damage than a Wood Elf, but the thing is, the Wood Elf won't be taking as much! Wood Elves have 100 base agility!  People always bring up the arguement, "but without high strength, you won't be able to wear all that armor!".  This is where many people forget how armor really works.  As an Ogre, Barbarian, or Troll, you need to wear Large sized armor.  Large armor is much heavier than small armor, which Wood Elves can wear!  Also, large armor is much harder to find than small armor.  Small armor is all over the place!  Besides, wood elves look pretty nice in their platemail.


Now the main reason I think Wood Elves are better?  Check out those starting abilities!  First off they have infravision, so that's always a plus, but look at the rest!  While most warriors don't get Bind Wound until level 5, Wood Elves start with it!  Wood Elves also start with the ability to hide, and while not always effective, when you're trapped in a dangerous area, with nowhere else to go, trying to hide can't do any harm.  And finally, the one skill that really sets them apart: Foraging!

While the rest of you warriors go and spend gold after gold after gold on food and water, we can just forage for it!  Saving multiple plat over the Wood Elves lifetime, plat that can be spent buying new weapons, buying new armor, practicing a trade skill, or even buying more alcohol!

So before you think "Oh only Trolls, Ogres, and Barbarians make good Warriors" look to the Wood Elves, what you find make surprise you!
