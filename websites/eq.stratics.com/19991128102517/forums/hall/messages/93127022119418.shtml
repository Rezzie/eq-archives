Oh boy...some prognosticator. I predict that the players that move to AC will move again when the next game comes out...that is the natural evolution of on-line gaming. This is my 5th game in the past 15 years, surely I will move to another in a year or two. Of course, I dont hang around the web-sites of the games I leave to irritate other people but that is a personality (or lack of) issue. Question is: what is better out there today?

Personally, I think the group combat system in EQ is pretty good and I really enjoy the tactics of it. I have never been much for graphics so they don't faze me much...still rather play Ultima 3 than Ultima 7...Civilization has very low end graphics but it was easily the best game I ever played.

Best environment for Play Acting (the term Role-Playing is inaccruate because everyone who logs on is automatically a RP'er) I have ever seen yet no one seems to know how to take advantage of it. The level advancement system and equipment system is decent and the interface is fine.

EQ is not very good for solo play, actually downright boring. There are alot of player policing issues that need to be addressed. Some of the skills and classes still need work. I really have no interest in doing quests, even GM moderated but that area does need to be addressed. I have never played in an On-line game that didn't have the same issues. I will bet anyone a months salary that AC has atleast one patch within the first week of commerical...anyone want to take that bet?
