You have to shoot at something that will give you xp for killing it. No wasps, or bats, or orc pawns.
The more red it is the better it seems to be for raising it. There are also some melee weapons that will raise archery skill in melee combat. Pine Needles and Tiny Daggers are the 2 that I know of.
What kind of Bow are you using? If it's one of those substandard short or long bows from the merchants, get a Fletcher to make you at least a Hickory Recurve, if not Elm.
