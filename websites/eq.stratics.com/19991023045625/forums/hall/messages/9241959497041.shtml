This is not the real way it works, but what I have found useful in trying different chars.

on spell related skills: if you start with it, it is fine, if you get it at a later level (like med for me a cleric) then spend one point in it.
On combat skills, I point at least one point in each one I started with. With my monk, dodge didn't go up until I put 1 point in. But I already knew the basics. On higher combat skills I put more training in... anywhere from 5 to 15 points.
trade skills: depends on the skill. easy skills (fishing and baking) don't need to (maybe a couple on baking). With darder skills (blacksmithing and jewel craft) put at least 15 points in it. otherwise it costs you a lot.

For me, any skill I want I will point at least 1 point in to be on the safe side.
