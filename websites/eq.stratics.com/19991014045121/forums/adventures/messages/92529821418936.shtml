1.I am still using my hands and kicks for fighting. Is that ok for a monk or should i start training 1hblunt or soemthing else?

You are a monk, your hands and feet will always be your true weapons, grasshopper.

2. I have saved 15 skillpoints but ave no clue what to spent them on. Should i power up my roundkick with
them or should i wait till i get another new skill (but this seems so far away now *sniff*)?

When in doubt, don't use your skillpoints, you can always find a use for them down the road. Combat skills will only need **1** point to initially train and then will go up very fast as you use them. I believe that Safefall is extremely difficult to raise, for instance, so this sort of skill you should train a bit more in. Or save many points and train a lot into a trade skill.

3. I spent a couple of points to mend and i have this skill now at about 35. Will mend be available faster as
higher my skill is? Is it a skill i should continue training?

No. There is a cap in place, so that you will only naturally raise the skill to a certain point depending on your level. Just use the skill a lot, and when you next level you should notice it improve again.

4. is there a place in freeport where i can buy better armor then just cloth for my Monk? i was looking
EVERYWHERE but couldnt find a shopkeeeper selling leather or something like that....

Monks need to travel light, keep this in mind. You can certainly purchase leather, but I once found a bit of mesh clothing down in the Qeynos aqueducts! Simply dive in the pool near the bank, go down, if you explore you may find (a few, hidden) frog beings down there, they have it! Mesh or better: Woven armor should be your goal. General rule of thumb, you'll always get armor cheaper from players, not NPCs. Pick up some patchwork from a tailor, simply listen for an auction, or ask yourself.

5. Do you have any other tips or hints for an upcoming friendly monk?

Best experience things, or ask other nearby monks for advice, do a /who monk. You'd be surprised how a more adept monk will help you with answers.

6. Can i get any Healing abilities with my Monk?

Mend?
