Why not do all three?
I have a 10th level half elf paladin, a 7th level dark elf shadow knight, a 5th level halfing, a 4th level half elf rogue, a human magician and a wood elf warrior. There are plenty of slots, why just have one type of character? Try a bit of variety, makes the game more appealing. Especially when roleplaying, it's good to go from a paladin, the holiest of the holy, to a shadow knight, the vilest of the vile :)
