The following tables are presented in order to assist players in choosing the race and class most suitable for their desired character(s). Data highlighted yellow represents the best in a particular category. Red numbers indicate a class's primary attributes (those which are most important to that class). The red figure directly following each class's listing is that race/class pair's class index. This number is the combined average of each of the class's primary attributes within that particular race. In theory, the higher its class index, the more effective a character will be within a its chosen profession. Following each race's attribute table is a box containing further information which is applicable to that race (only advantages and disadvantages that are not immediately obvious from the ability statistics will be listed).

Please use the following numbers wisely and bear in mind that they do not necessarily represent the best in a particular class or race. EverQuest's character choices are extremely well balanced and provide a wide range of choices to players. Numbers aren't the only aspect of a character that effects performance, even from a purely statistical point of view. Powers, abilities, NPC reactions... all of this and more will influence which type of character is right for you.
