If you've been testing for a good time, you'd know that this is by no means final.

Trade skills and Rogue abilities are being added in Phase 2 (Or Phase 1, depends if you call this Phase 0 or Phase 1)

Anyhow, this is a PRE-Beta. Thus things are not as developed. Right now they're testing the basic combat system, server latency, etc.

This is just the fundamentals of the game, expect to see alot more in the future. Phase 2 will see a New Graphics engine as well.

In your post, you treat this as if it is AC how it will be in final, you should go read the official newsgroups and see what they still have to implement.

Again, this game is by NO MEANS final, or even near a final beta. You have to look at what they plan as well as what they have.

Also, I disagree with the statement "to Get exp you have to hack and slash"

That is not the case. I have an Enchanter, and All I do is buff people in town, and research. Got money from an Allegiance, so I can research. When I find new spells, I try them out on other PC's. Buff em up for combat, even go along on a dungeon romp and heal. Haven't hacked nor slashed a thing, and got alot of exp.

For Example :: Level II spells. Researched many, and went out and buffed fellow Vassals. Buffed one, Recharged mana, Buffed second one. How much EXP did I get? About 4K for buffing etc.

So, you dont HAVE to hack and slash, you can play a Enchanter, or a Healer, and not even pick up a weapon. You'll just have to Specialize in Running for when the monsters come after you for healing the fighter. :p

That and Magic Users seem to be Monster Magnets. I've been laying down and monsters will spawn, run by me, and hack on the caster standing behind me. Hehe.
