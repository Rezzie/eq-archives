Treant Fists Quest
 
The Treant Fists are stylish crimson gauntlets useable by Human Monks only. Iksar Monks can quest for them if their faction is high enough, but they can not wear them because of the plate restictions their Race has. A group is suggested if trying to complete this quest before the early twenties.

This quest begins with the ending of another, but it isn't required. Grim Oakfist can be found in Kithicore Forest and initiates the Collar of Nekisha quest. Once you complete his tasks, he tells you that he gave his last pair of Treant Fists to Master Paub.

Master Puab is the head of the Ashen Order in West Freeport. It's near a large stage, called the Theater of Tranquility, and the East Freeport zoneline. Puab Closk can be found on the second floor of the House.

Khamon says, 'What Treant Fists, Master?'

Puab Closk says 'I'll give them to a skilled monk of the Ashen House.'

Khamon says, 'Well, Master.. I am a skilled Monk of the Ashen House.'

Puab Closk says 'Then you shall aid our family. My former pupil [Clawfist] has been banished by his people. You will go to him and hand him this token as proof of your origin. He shall be expecting you.'

At this point you will recieve Puab's Token.

Khamon says, 'Who is Clawfist, Master?'

Puab Closk says 'Clawfist is a Kerran, a catman. He braved the dangers of Norrath to reach the Ashen Order. He sought knowledge of our disiples. He learned well.'

Khamon says, 'Where is Clawfist, Master?'

Puab Closk says 'Clawfist has been banished by the Kerrans of Odus. Where they have sent him I am unsure.'

Now you can travel from Freeport to Kerra Isle and speak with Clawfist, but you'll save some time if you skip to the next step. You'll have to go to Kerra Isle eventually anyways.
As Master Puab said, Clawfist was exiled by the Kerrans of Odus, but for some reason he still is found within the Village. He is called "a banished kerran" and is located on the bottom floor of Priestess Mitty's temple.

Khamon says, ''Hail, a banished kerran.'

a banished kerran says ''Rrrrrr.. Away frrrom this isle of death and.. prrrr.. punishment.'

After handing him Puab's Token you will receive the following text:

a banished kerran says 'Ashen Order!! Prrr.. My order. I have been expecting one of us. Prrr.. Help me rejoin my native land. Take this box. Combine all the [remains of Thipt] within the box and return it to me. This shall aid me in my redemption.'

Khamon says, 'What remains of Thipt, Brother?'

a banished kerran says 'Clawknight Thipt wished to learn the ways of Ashen. Prrr. Together we go into green Goblin's lair to train. Prrr.. We find too many and he is too weak. Seperated and unable to find him, I hear his death cry. Prr.. I cannot make it back, the eyes are upon me. Now I must find one sent by Ashen to aid in the recoverrry of his remains.'

At this point Clawfist will hand you a four slotted box with a combine button, called the Box with Paw Prints. Once again, you can save yourself a trip to Odus by doing this part of the quest after you've collected the remains.

There are a total of four pieces remaining of poor Thipt. They have become scattered throughout Runnyeye and the neighboring zones of Misty Thicket and the Gorge of King Xrobb. The Goblins who now posses them are as follows:
 
  • Pickclaw Bonemender - This Goblin is a Cleric of the low twenties and is located in Runnyeye. He carries the Fractured Femur. It can usually be seen in his hand.
    • The most common area to spot him is on the second floor. If you zone in from the Gorge of King Xorbb zone, take your first right hand turn and you'll come to a stairway with a wooden door at the bottom. When facing the wooden door, turn right, and you'll see a short tunnel leading to a water canal. He sometimes wanders from the canal area to the base of the stairs.
  • Pickclaw Mindripper - This Goblin is an Enchanter of the low twenties. He is also found in Runnyeye and carries the Catskull Cap.
    • The most common area to spot him is on the second floor. If you zone in from the Gorge of King Xorbb zone, take your first right hand turn and you'll come to a stairway with a wooden door at the bottom. When facing the wooden door, turn right, and you'll see a short tunnel leading to a water canal. Just like the Bonemender, he sometimes wanders from the canal area to the base of the stairs.
  • Goblin Alchemist - This Goblin is a Shaman of the mid teens and is located in the Misty Thicket. He carries the Ribcage Chest Armor.
    • He can be found in the Goblin camp with one tent and three spawn points. It's the farthest to your left when facing the cave to Runnyeye. The Alchemists spawn point is inside the tent and on an 18 minute timer. There are a few place holders that can spawn in his spot. They can vary from a Goblin Warrior, to a Shaman, or to the infamous Mooto
  • Goblin Alchemist - This Goblin is a Shaman of the high teens and can be found in the Gorge of King Xorbb. He's a rare spawn and carries the Humorous Handled Mace.
    • With your back to the Runnyeye enterance, you'll see several large rocks and a fork in the path. Disregard the right path. The left path has three goblin spawn points. One on the left hand side, one of the right hand side, and one just slightly closer to the middle on the right hand side. The two spawn points on the left and right are on a 6 minute timer. The middle one, near the right hand side and where the Alchemist is located, is on a 72 minute timer. This is where many Monks begin their Trials of Patience, or slowly loose their sanity. Be prepared to wait a long time. There are a number of place holders that can spawn in the Alchemists spot.

    After collecting the remains of poor Brother Thipt, it's time to return to Kerra Isle. Be sure to press combine before handing your box back to Clawfist! Handing in the Box of Cat Bones produces the following text:

    a banished kerran says 'Prrr.. Thank you brother of Ashen. I can now spend my time upon this island in peace, until my penance is serrrved. (He dabs his paw into the mud and places it upon a tattered parchment). Take this message to Puab. Farrrwell.'

    At this point you receive a Paw Print Tattered Parchment.

    Khamon says, 'Glad to be of assistance, I hope to see you again once your penance is through. May Quellious guide your way, Brother Clawfist.'

    Now it's time to head back to Freeport. If you are an Agnostic Monk of the Silent Fist Clan, you must do some faction work before handing in this note. If you don't, all your hard work will be for nothing! Slaughtering Orcs in the Commonlands is a good way to raise your Ashen Order faction.

    Visit Master Puab in the Ashen House and hand him Clawfist's note. He responds with the following text:

    Puab Closk says 'You have performed a great service to one who is your brother. Your loyalty shines brightly, as does your skill. Take the treant fists. They are yours now.'

    He then hands you:

    Treant Fists
    (Magic, Lore, No Drop, 8ac, +3 Dex, +5 Hp, 0.7wt, Class: Mnk, Race: Hmn)

    Khamon says, 'Thank you Master. I shall wear them with honor and pride.'
