Okay during beta there were exploits which involved using buildings/ obstacles to be able to attack npc/monsters from a distance and have them be helpless to attck back. The game was changed so that if you try this now.. the monster can reach you.. whether you are on a ledge high up a cliff... in a building... levitating etc. Okay fine they stopped an explitable situation. However, in the process the drastically altered the "realism" of the game(yes I know its a fantasy role-playing game, but there is a limit). As things are now you can be in an INN.. which is the worst name for it ever.. it should be called a 1 way house of mirrors. Creatures can SEE in and attack you from outside.. but you cant see out.. so you never know when a big critter is gonna start hacking away at you while your doing business with a shopkeeper. This is extremely aggravating.. and even though in beta it was a known bug that was being worked on. Now when asked about it all the GM's say its NOT a BUG! I know a bug when I see one and this is a bug.. if the developers meant for people to get screwed over like this then they shouldnt have been working on this game. There have to be ways to differentiate (at least a little) between someone who is under attack and is trying to hide in an unguarded inn or shop and someone that the monsters should not know is there.. I mean lets be real maybe a Kodiak could smell you or somehting.. maybe all the critters in the game have ways of knowing your there.. but at least make them use the damn door like the rest of us! Allowing creature to attack THROUGH walls needs to changed! Make them bust down the door to get in or whatever... that would be perfectly acceptable. If I can't initiate an attack on creature through a wall then they shouldnt be able to attack through one either. Verant is so far REFUSING to aknowledge that ther is a problem. I suggest that EVERYtime this happens to anyone that feedback and a bug report be filed. This is a serious flaw in the game and Verant is trying to just "glaze over" the truth and has instructed GM's to do so as well. Fight to make Norrath a better place!

Zem Abane
