Well... I did ask a GM about the rampant high level monsters in any given area, and they said that Verant had planned to put one "super creature" of high level in each and every zone. I know this is true for many dungeons.. Befallen has Gynor Mitok, and Runnyeye has Borxx, Unrest has the Hag and assorted other meanies, Mistmoore with Tanara Vispin, the list goes on and on. They did this to encourage higher level characters to be in zones that are frequented by "newbies" and lower level characters. I think this is to avoid level segregation. How many times have you been saved by a Higher level character that is just passing thru?
Personally I'd like to see all the Sand Giants in Oasis go to hell, but thats just personal spite because they've killed me so often.

What fun would it be if you knew you were safe in a certain zone aside from town? There's always that element of danger looming overhead. ... You never know whats gonna be over that next rise so deal with it and WATCH OUT!
