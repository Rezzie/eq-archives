EQSS Forums - Posting Guidelines

Posting Messages
The most important aspect of creating a new message is staying on topic. Each Discussion Hall--with the exception of the QuestHall, which is considered general discussion--has a set topic that must be adhered to. Off-topic posts will be removed. If you have doubts as to whether or not your EQ-related message is fit for a board with a specific topic, post it on the QuestHall.

Constructive posts are the heart and soul of the EQSS message boards. While we at Stratics frown upon censorship, we will take any necessary measure in order to ensure the happiness of our readers. Site and/or guild announcements are tolerated as long they are not repeated, contain intelligent information, and are appropriate for the board that they have been posted to. Spamming (the act repeating the same post over and over) will not be tolerated. Regardless of subject matter, explicit racially or sexually offensive posts will be removed without warning.

Please try and post message that are beneficial to others reading the boards. If you feel that you must post a 'No Text' message, please include "NT" in the subject of the message. Lastly, do not post in ALL CAPS--this tends to lower your standing in other peoples' eyes and will accomplish nothing in getting your message noticed.

A Guide to Colored Names

You may notice that some peoples' posts include names that are highlighted with either a red, dark blue or bright blue color. Individuals who are highlighted in red are administrative representatives of the EQSS, those highlighted in dark blue are EQSS staff who are particularly knowledgeable about the game or our message boards (for specific information, please visit the EQSS Staff Page).

Those names that are highlighted in a bright blue color work for Verant Interacrtive, the makers of EverQuest. Here are some people from Verant who periodically post to our message boards:

  • Brad McQuaid: Sometimes known as "Aradune", Brad is the Lead Designer of EverQuest.
  • John Smedley: "Smed" is the President and CEO of Verant Interactive.
  • Dave Rickey: Assistant Head GM of EverQuest.

What Do You Think?

The EQStratics Forums are constantly evolving message boards that will grow in order to fit your needs. If at any time you feel that any of our boards could be better, please inform us and we'll try to adjust to your desires. In order to submit a compliment, complaint or suggestion, please e-mail den@stratics.com.
