I practice it almost all the time. Usually as a first shot, bringing the creature towards me. Then while my party is attacking it I can usually hit it with some arrows before returning to the heat of battle. The monster has forgotten how pissed he was at me at first by then. However the cost of arrows, the major slow skill gain, and the cheap damage makes me wonder why I do it. The two things that really piss me off. 1)Fletching supplies in only two places
2)Fletching only one arrow at a time.

If you are going to use archery, at least work your skill up to be able to fletch the cheapest arrow. You will save yourself half the cost on each arrow. For now though it isn't worthwhile doing one arrow at a time. But hopefully they will change it.... I am praying for it
