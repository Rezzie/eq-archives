Rubies are what are known as rare gems, and as far as I know those may only be bought from a vendor if someone has sold one to the vendor. ( I think, not positive on that ) Otherwise, the only way to get a Ruby is to kill a high level mob and hope it drops one. If you find one at a vendor, the price will be in the neighborhood of 130 platinum pieces, depending on your charisma and faction with that particular vendor.
