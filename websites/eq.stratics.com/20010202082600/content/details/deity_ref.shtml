Deity Reference Chart

 Explanations Key   Deity by City/Race/Class 
The following chart details which Deities are available to every Race/Class pair, as well as the starting cities that are available to each. Deities are listed in order as presented in game. See below for explanations of abbreviations:

Deity Abbreviations:
  • AGN - Agnostic
  • BER - Bertoxxulous, The Plaguebringer
  • BRI - Bristlebane, The King of Thieves
  • CAZ - Cazic-Thule, The Faceless
  • INN - Innoruuk, The Prince of Hate
  • KAR - Karana, The Rainkeeper
  • ERO - Erollisi Marr, The Queen of Love
  • MIT - Mithaniel Marr, The Lightbearer
  • ROD - Rodcet Nife, The Prime Healer
  • PRE - Prexus, The Oceanlord
  • QUE - Quellious, The Tranquil
  • SOL - Solusek Ro, The Burning Prince
  • BRE - Brell Serilis, The Duke of Below
  • TRI - The Tribunal, The Six Hammers
  • TUN - Tunare, The Mother of All
  • VEE - Veeshan, The Wurmqueen
  • RAL - Rallos Zek, The Warlord
City Abbreviations:
  • Any - Any starting city available to this Race*
  • Ak' - Ak'Anon (Gnomes)
  • Eru - Erudin (Erudites)
  • Fel - Felwithe (High and Half Elves)
  • Fre - Freeport (Humans and Half Elves)
  • Gro - Grobb (Trolls)
  • Hal - Halas (Barbarians)
  • Kal - Kaladim (Dwarves)
  • Ogg - Oggok (Ogres)
  • Ner - Neriak (Dark Elves)
  • Qey - Qeynos (Humans and Half Elves)
  • Kel - Kelethin (Wood and Half Elves)
  • Riv - Rivervale (Halflings)
  • Q/K - Qeynos or Kelethin (Half Elves)
