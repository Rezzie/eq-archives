I have played with a Magician and a Shaman, both of which make excellent soloing classes. For Shaman, a troll is a good choice because they heal quickly. A barbarian is also good: they don't have the disadvantages of being evil, and have the bonus of being able to use gear for both large and medium sizes.

My Magician is an Erudite, which I enjoy. But I think high elves may be a slightly better choice. While the mage can summon most of what he needs, they need to do a fair amout of purchasing. The high elf's charisma helps a lot here.

I have not played a Necromancer, but I hear they have problems getting killed because of their "evil" disposition. They're also a little more reliant on their pets.
