EverQuest Festival 2000, November 3 - 5, Houston Texas - Greebo's Travelogue
  top
(Click on any picture to see it full size)
 

Baltimore Washington International Airport (BWI)

Waiting for Continental Flight 1695. The plane just pulled in. Watching them load the luggage, sitting in the overpriced bar, as I finish loading the overpriced batteries from the overpriced store into my camera. Why didn't I stop at the overpriced 7-11 before I got here?

LSG - our Sky Chefs - providing our meals for the next flight...uh, is that legal?

Reijas just called me. (I love cellular phones) Confirmed time to meet at George Bush Airport (I'm the Taxi this weekend.) I'd be a lot more nervous about flying into "Al Gore" airport. I'd worry about "No controlling Air Traffic Authority"

Somewhere at 36,000 Feet

Lunch, if you can call it that, was a roast beef and some unidentifyable cheese with a single slice of wilted lettuce and (upon request), Helman's Mayonaise.

Weather Conditions in the Mid-West (I think) are partly cloudy. Rumour has it of a hurricane off the coast of Texas meaning Houston may be wet. I forgot my umbrella.

In Houston at the Hotel

Thank goodness, the T-Shirts are here! Landed in Houston (Yay) and things went smoothly. I dont like the Mercury Marquis I rented - too small for me. The Hotel is a tad on the cheap side but clean and the staff is fantastic!

Just about to head back to the Airport to get Ogamok and Reijas, then we're going down to Rice University to see stevec, Naldain and Canien. We went out to Benigans and closed it getting nice and toasty. *GRIN*
