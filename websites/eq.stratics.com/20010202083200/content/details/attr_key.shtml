Attribute Table
Attribute: Determines:
STR - Strength Melee Damage, Weight Allowance
STA - Stamina Hit Points, Fatigue
AGI - Agility Dodge Percentage, Starting AC
DEX - Dexterity Chance to Hit
WIS - Wisdom Mana for Clerics, Resistance to Mind-Effecting Spells, Determines Chance to Ignore Failed Skill Rolls
INT - Intelligence Mana for Magic Users, Skill Gain
CHA - Charisma NPC Reactions, Merchant Prices
The 'ADD' column represents additional bonus points, which may be distributed to any attribute during character creation.
