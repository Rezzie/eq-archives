Top 10 Reasons why visiting Rivervale is just like visiting a Disney theme park

10.
Disney: You can visit Sleeping Beauty's castle.
Rivervale: You can visit the Mayor's castle.

9.
Disney: Everything seems like it was made for children.
Rivervale: Everything seems like it was made for children.

8.
Disney: That big Mickey guy is really annoying but if you beat him up the locals get annoyed.
Rivervale: Mangler is really annoying but if you beat him up the locals get annoyed.

7.
Disney: Loud carnival music at the venues.
Rivervale: Loud carnival music all over.

6.
Disney: Have to be careful you don't trip over all the little ones wandering around.
Rivervale: Have to be careful you don't trip over Nillipuss while wandering around.

5.
Disney: Lots of cute dwarves running around.
Rivervale: Lots of cute halflings running around.

4.
Disney: Vendors charge ridiculous prices.
Rivervale: Vendors charge ridiculous prices.

3.
Disney: Pirate Cove.
Rivervale: Piranha Lake.

2.
Disney: Eagle Pines Golf Course nearby to get out and relax.
Rivervale: Misty Thicket Hunting Ground nearby to get out and relax.

And the number one reason why visiting Rivervale is just like visiting a Disney theme park...

1.
Disney: The whole time you're there you feel like you're in some kind of crazy cartoon.
Rivervale: The whole time you're there you feel like you're in some kind of crazy cartoon.
