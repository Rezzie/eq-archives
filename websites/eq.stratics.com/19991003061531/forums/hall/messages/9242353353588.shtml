(205.199.141.33)

I hereby Declare that starting Saturday, April 17, it is Be Nice to Healers Week.

Too often people will literally demand that a healer attend them. I can not even begin to tell you the number of times that someone has messaged me for heal or cure.

I also, can not begin to tell you how often it has been done without even a "thank you" or a coin. I have ventured often into the depths of Blackburrow and just wandered around healing those that were injured and bloody. Not once was I ever gifted with coin or item. I have stood along side a person fighting for their lives and healed them so that they were victorious, but when the vicious gnolls turned on me, the warrior had better things to do. And my corpse fell by the wayside.

People, riddled with disease have begged me for succor. Then, cured of their disfiguring disease, they walk away with a wave and a smile. Not thinking of the poor healer's time and effort. Time that, perhaps, could have been better spent in making some coin to fund her studies.

Do not demand of the healer's time and skills. Rather ask nicely. Do not cheapen the effort and the effect by walking away with nary another thought. Rather contribute to the cause. For as a healer's skill grows, so does their effectiveness. Help to fund that growth of skill. Then, you can look at the more powerful healers in the land and say -- I Helped them become that way.

For one week -- show the healers how much you appreciate them. Some of us need to be reminded.

Dharbrin
Acolyte Healer
The Dragon Order
