So You Want to be an Adventurer
So you wanna be adventurer, living with only the minerals that surround you, grasping on to what berry bush you can for food, and fighting as if it would take you years to find your corpse. The first thing you need to do is establish a good solo fighter, for solo fighting is what you'll do the most while adventuring. You may encounter many friends and odd races as you adventure to odd and exotic zones. You should know that once out and adventuring, you are most certain to never recover your body and all its belongings. So if you do adventure and expect to die, be sure to know your way back and forth through out all of Norrath. Being one of the number one adventurers out there, I have been to the wonderful waterfalls of Surefall Glades, to the smelly swamps of Freerrott, to the marble city of Erudin and to the mechanical monstrosity known as Ak'Anon. To be an adventurer is to be known as the most daring profession out there. No other profession is as exciting as the adventurer/traveler.

How do I Become an Adventurer?
You don't really become an adventurer, you evolve into one. By this I mean, once out in the wild, there is no way of turning back into a zone monger(one who stays in one zone for levels x-y, then moves to the next zone to advance to levels z-n). To be an adventurer is to be daring and charismatic. To be knowledgeable of all zones and know where you are at all times. You must have an incredibly high "sense heading" skill level. And the number one rule to an adventurer is keep to the roads. Never wander off track away from a road, if you do, be sure to know how to get back, or you might be lost forever in a hypnotic landscape and a never-ending sea of hills. Always be sure to steer clear of "What do you want your tombstone to say?" targets, for they may be your end to the adventure. If you die, well, you can chose to become a "zone monger" or an adventurer again.

Traveler's Tip: Do you ever get lost? Are you tired of hitting the sense heading hotkey for a half hour just to have your skill raise only one point? Never fear. Just as in real life where moss always grows on the north side of a tree, mother nature (Tunare in this case) can help you out. Look to the sky. Clouds in Norrath always travel from East to West! -Tinker

Why be an Adventurer?
Why not be an adventurer. With the excitement with in every zone to the beautiful landscapes you'll discover and capture in your screenshots. Traveling to the local taverns of every foreign town, you can trade stories with local adventurers, or even trade screenshots of your daring adventures, known as your life. When you reach a new city, you can absorb a part of the culture and add it to your own personality. Wander through the town, marveling in the architecture of each town and the different buildings. At the end of your life, you can share stories with the young children(newbies) and laugh at the lack of knowledge of some high level characters. You can even make a photo album of the beautiful sites you admire as you wonder the ancient grounds of Norrath. Take photographs of eccentric humanoids to villainous monsters and comrades from afar. Remember a picture is worth a thousand words.
