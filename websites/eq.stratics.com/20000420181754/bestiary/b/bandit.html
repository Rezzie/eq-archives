Description:
Bandits vary a great deal from zone to zone, they come in all shapes and sizes. Basically they are just like player characters, they can be any race and any class. So one could be back stabbing you while another is casting a spell. They also have unusal factions. I haven't been in the Karana's since beta, but i know that the guards wouldn't attack bandits. Though i saw a bandit killing a giant beetle once.

Greater Faydark: There is a small bandit camp in the greater of the faydarks. It usually contains three elven female bandits that i believe to be low level rogues. Levels about 5-8.
