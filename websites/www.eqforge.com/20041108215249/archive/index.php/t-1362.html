Fall of the Western Civilization tells the story of the First World from the outbreak of the war August 1914 to the armistice November 1918. All major theatres of the war are simulated, including the Middle East and colonial Africa.
There are six playable tribes:

Russia (includes Romania)

Italy

Germany (includes Austria-Hungary and Bulgaria)

France

Turkey

Britain (includes Albania, Belgium, Greece, Portugal and Serbia)
The scenario will have five scn-files starting at different years.

At the start of WWI Willem, Poland did not exist as a seperate nation. It was controlled by The Geramn Empire on one side, Imperial Russia on another, and The Austro-Hungarian Empire on the thrid. Danzig was a German city at this time. Warsaw was a Russian city, the Austrians controlled Gallicia. This arrangement went back to the Napolenoic wars. After the Great war, a Poland was created by the allies, and it fought a successful war with the Soviets (circa 1920). Danzig was called a 'free city', to enable the Poles to have an outlet to the Baltic sea. WWII began here, as the German navy started bombarding the Polish garrison on September 1, 1939.
