I don't think the AI has a stronger tendency to demand $$ after you have built MPE unless you are weak, meaning you have few military units. This is the reason it may be a good idea to build 10-15 warriors soon. OTOH I have often demanded and received tribute (sometimes a total of several thousand gold over a few dozen turns) after building MPE, in fact MPE makes this easier because you have contact with every civ, know their attitude, how much $$ they have etc.

The funniest sequence of events that can happen is this (the numbers are arbitrary turn numbers):

(1) Demand tribute => you get some gold
(2) Demand tribute => war
(3) Contact AI civ, it offers a cease fire. You refuse, get offered $$ for it and say yes. Then you refuse a peace treaty, get offered $$ and only then sign the treaty.
(4) Demand tribute => war
(5) See (3) !
etc.

Sometimes more than one turn passes between the above events. Needless to say this can also happen when you don't have MPE.

Why earn money yourself when you can let others do it for you ? :D.
