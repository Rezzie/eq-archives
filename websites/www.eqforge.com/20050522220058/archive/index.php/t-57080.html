Greetings fellow citizens. Welcome to the official Term 3 campaign thread for the Executive.
This thread is an opportunity for the people seeking election to any of the following positions to state their case:
President
Domestic Leader
Military Leader
Foreign Affairs Leader
Culture Leader
Trade Leader
Science Leader.

It is also an opportunity for you the citizens to ask them questions and in general make them work hard for your vote.

The election office has a number of questions it would like the candidates to answer in order to help the citizens decide who is the most worthy.


1).Why are you standing for this position?
2).Why are you standing for other positions too?
3).What are your objectives for the first 7 days of office?
4).What qualities can you bring to this position that your fellow candidates cant?
5).How will you get more people involved in the game?
6).If you have held office before, what was your greatest triumph and worst disaster?
7).Which method of victory do you want to pursue and why?
8). How have you contributed to this game?

Please take the time to answer these questions and have fun debating. :)

@boots

If a member of the judiciary proposes a law, and then there is a legal question about the new proposed law or the procedure used to propose or approve it, how should the leader handle that situation?

@peri

Your opinion seems to stray away from the principle that all leaders have dual roles, as leader and citizen (and therefor member of congress). The concept that the judiciary should not legislate is quite valid in RL where there are sufficient people to write laws, but we don't have a separate lawmakeing body. Do you see any alternatives which would still allow the most qualified individuals to write laws, but avoid ethical entanglements?
