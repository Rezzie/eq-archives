I tried it out, and I found, that Jorrit Vermeirens MapEdit util actually will allow for gigantic maps (32000+ sq sized) -the important thing is to set the executive directory of Civ2 correctly in the options menu, as this determines how large loaded maps can be.

As far as I can tell from my small test, the gigantic maps won't work on classic Civ2 (or they will, but with strange bugs), but works fine with MGE, also without the no-limits patch installed! GREAT NEWS!

NO NEED TO COMPROMISE! Now we can make a monster map of Europe! (or any other part of the world) GREAT! -this allows for even more opportunities in scenarios!
