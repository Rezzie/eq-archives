I've built colonies on the coast of otherwise uninhabitable islands because it was the only place I could find oil, or rubber. I found them useful because I a: didn't want to waste time trying to keep a population happy in a crappy environment and b: didn't want to waste money on the improvements such a city would need to be successful, or c: the resource, for instance diamonds, was in the middle of a mountain range that would take many many turns to be inside my cultural border and since you can't build a city on mountain tiles the colony worked out great! Make sure you station a defensive unit withing the colony though otherwise roaming barbarians will destroy it. Love them colonies!:p
