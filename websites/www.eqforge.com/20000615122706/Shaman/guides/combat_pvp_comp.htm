Taking a Shaman into PvP - a comparison by Rumbul Wolfkin

Shaman vs Warrior
- Shaman win, no prob. Only exception would be if the warrior is 4-5 levels higher due to spell resistance. Warriors especially dread root.

Shaman vs Rogue
- I duelled one of equal level at level 26 and the Rogue could not lay a glove on me. Keep your back against something solid and just fire away. If you are really mean, root and DoT the sucker.

Shaman vs Cleric
- I duelled a cleric 3 levels higher than me and once again slaughtered the poor fool. The only thing you need beware of from a cleric is blinding and fear spells. Their damage spells are really mana inefficient so they aren't much of a concern.

Shaman vs Shadowknight
- A fairly tough class to fight for one simple reason: Harm Touch. Root and blast them and you can still handle an SK several levels higher than you.

Shaman vs. Magician
- Depends on whether or not they have their pet (Rallos Zek) or not, but so long as the Shaman can avoid the pet and go straight at the mage there isn't going to be a huge problem. Pop a DoT (or two) on the mage and just heal off the damage they inflict. They will be hurt and out of mana and you will be fine and able to melee them to death. Same works for Enchanters and Wizards. Endure Magic is extremely handy against an enchanter whereas against a wizard you want to stay on top of them and disrupt as many spells as you can.

Shaman vs Necromancer
- A tricky fight because they have many potent DoTs and drain spells. Burn them down fast, as they have few hit points and no true healing spells.

Shaman vs Paladin
- A tricky fight because they are essentially a warrior you have to do double damage to (LoH). Use your mana sparingly to DoT the paladin and then stay out of reach of their melee attacks. Disempower and similar spells are good to be used against them.

Shaman vs Druid
- If the druid is higher level you are most likely going to lose as they have a greater mana reserve than your average shaman, and better direct damage. They are not as good at melee however - root them, get close, slam and blast away. A very very close fight.

Shaman vs. Bard
- Bards are essentially the same as warriors but tricky because you cannot root them. Pop a DoT (or two) on them and then blast away. They will try to trade blows with you but you can burn them down quickly enough.
