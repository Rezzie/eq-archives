Well, after writing my treatise the Science of Alchemy, I decided to test some of my theories out...

While driving home from work I was pondering the Rejuvenation potion. If Str + Sta + Catalyst gives Regen, maybe Dex + Agi + Catalyst would give SoW... Afterall, it is a mobility effect.

When I got home I logged in and ran from Oggok to Halas to try it out, and sure enough, success on the first try! Okay, you've read this far. The formula for the Spirit of Wolf potion is:

Birthwart (Agility)
Fenugreek (Dexterity)
Wolfblood (Catalyst)

Cost? About 11 platinum. I plan on selling these for 25 plat each at first, though price will probably settle to 20 once enough people start making it. To all you alchemists out there, I would ask that you NOT charge less than 20 plat, a very fair price for a great potion. Anything less is selling ourselves short and hurts the shaman community as a whole. Enjoy, and now study up on my Science of Alchemy if you haven't already!
