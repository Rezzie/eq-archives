Many years ago a young boy named Maximus was born into the barbarian tribes of the 'Dividing Peaks'. His father who is only known as Biggus, was killed by a raiding party of warriors from the civilisation of Fanaticus.

While his village still burned to the ground, Maximus fled, arriving into the Great city of Gorina. Without much he led a life of poverty, until a rich man caught him trying to steal a plate of food from his table.

With the lack of laws, he saw it only fit to adopt the young boy and name him his own. Maximus became Maximus Desmond Neverilis.

Many years passed and he became more and more aquainted with his new life in the Lower Gentry. Ten years after his arrival in Fanaticus, he married a gorgeous commoner, Livia Nigelias Neverilis. Unfortuantley his adoptive father died three weeks before the wedding, that saw barbarian boy become loyal and secure Fanticus citizen.

With the birth of their first child, Maximus Desmond Neverilis II
the line became secured. Long live the line of Neverlis! After Maximus II came his brother Juluis Desmond Neverlis, and his twin sister, Agrippa Livia Neverilis.
