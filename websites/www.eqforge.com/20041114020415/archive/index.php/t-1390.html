oryx
Apr 21, 2001, 07:12 PM
Include a game.txt file with your scenario and modify it as follows:
Find this section in game.txt
@REALLYCHEAT
Remove the "Yes" answer to the question "Do you really want to enable cheat mode?" When you remove the "Yes", be sure to leave a line feed there, otherwise the dialog boxes after that section will be fouled up.

Having said all that, anybody who knows this technique will be able to re-enable cheat mode by restoring that section of the game.txt. You cannot absolutely prevent someone from cheating.
