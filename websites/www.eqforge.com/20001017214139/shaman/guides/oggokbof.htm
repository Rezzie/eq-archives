I just started a Shammie and have completed this one twice:

     You get the quest from Zulort in the Shaman guild area.  Hail him and he tells you blah blah [gonna help].  So, respond with something like, "me gonna help".  He'll then tell you that he'll reward you if you bring four froglok tadpole fleshes to him.  Run out into the Feerott and whack froglok tadpoles until you accumulate four fleshes and return to Oggok to deliver them to Zulort.  You will notice that the fleshes "stack".  Peel off one and drag it over Zulort's handsome mug and left-click.  Then when the trade window comes up, put three more into the slots one at a time.  You should see four separate fleshes in the trade window now.  Click on "Give" and you'll hear a horn blow, woohoo!, you just got some faction and a nifty new Burst of Flame spell.  I don't think you can get anything other than a Burst of Flame spell from Zulort.  I did the quest twice (and don't feel like wasting any more time on it) and have gotten the same Burst of Flame spell.
