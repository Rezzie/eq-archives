Tlaxcala prevents us from connecting Africa and Woodstock with railroad and geographically it also belongs to us. We should immediately try to get the Aztecs to declare war and then capture Tlaxcala, regardless of whether we decide to totally eliminate the Aztecs now or not.

Blenheim Palace: SE (IRB now !!), SM, CH. Needs irrigation/farmland, maybe build engineer ?
Electrical: Engineer, MP, aqueduct. Build railroad towards Tlaxcala now.
Superstorms: Rehome cavalry in Rhodes. IRB cavalry now to help capture Tlaxcala. Then build aqueduct and bank. Bribe the Aztec caravan and send it to Doughnutia.

The biggest news here is Tlaxcala. The Ministry of Domestic Affairs sees this city as a major obstacle to its railroad plans and strongly recommends capturing this city immediately if we can get the Aztecs to declare war.
