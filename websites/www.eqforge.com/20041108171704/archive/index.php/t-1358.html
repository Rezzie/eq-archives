Thanks for the info on the Trek...keep it comin' because I am adding it into the events to tighten it up...

As for the science ship, Kobayashi, I had to increase its defenses for two reasons:

One, on the series it seems that these ships lasted forever in a distress call situation...

Two, the first reason never had anything to do with it...Klingons and Romulans kept trashing the science vessels so I increased their defense strength just you could have a chance to get a colony ship to another local space system. Otherwise, you'd never expand anywhere.

I raised their cost too...they are basically naval units anyway so they can't do much good to enemy cities...you really need the other ships which are actually air units for attack.

hmm...let's see...oh...and you could actually use one or two of the terrains to make space fabric if you want to....I've only used the "desert" and "grassland" positions in my map making for these scenarios if I remember right...that would make it possible to make some changes. I don't believe I made any appreciable changes per se to the terrains parameters in the Rules.txt (although I may have and forgot since I've done too many mods).

Keep me posted.

Thanks for the help on this.
