You go into your User CP, then select Edit Profile. In the signature section you can paste something like:

[*color=blue][*url=http://forums.civfanatics.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=94]Be part of the Civ II Democracy Game[*/color][*/url]

Just take out the *'s for it to work.

You can change the text to say what you want, and change the color as well.
