Shaman Quests



One quest is very simple, well known to many (but not listed): By killing polar bear cubs and returning one white polar bear pelt to Cindl in Mac's Kilts, one will receive a free piece of patchwork armor, a few gold, and some xp.  Great for new barbarians of any class.

The Delius' Theme Diary quest is a little more complex but still simple. By killing all three types of vengeful skeletons (Soloist, Composer, and Lyricst), whose levels range from 8/9 to 13/14, their loot yields 3 different pages. Take these 3 pages and turn them in to Thadres Thyme in one of the brewer shops in Halas. She will yield xp and a book called Garsen's Notes, which provides some useful recipes for brewing. (Personal note: the tougher skels are not hard to kill as they only have one attack and spells are easy to cast once you figure out the timing)
