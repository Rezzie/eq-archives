To properly update your drivers:

1. Download the drivers to your hard drive and save them. Also make sure that the folder C:\ATI is deleted or cleaned of all prior driver versions. You have to do that manually.
2. Download and install DriverCleaner. (Google)
3. Uninstall all current ATI drivers.
4. Restart when prompted to restart. Hit F8 after the BIOS screen flashes and choose SafeMode.
5. Cancel out of any hardware wizard or "new device found" installation prompts.
6. Run DriverCleaner and remove all of the ATI driver files.
7. Restart your computer and boot normally.
8. Cancel out of any hardware wizards again.
9. Install new ATI drivers.

Make sure you have the latest DirectX version and Windows is fully updated. Make sure your games are fully patched.
