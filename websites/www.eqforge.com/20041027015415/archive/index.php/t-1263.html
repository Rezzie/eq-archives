You can´t use the graphics from harlan´s collection directly, because the file has not the same layout as the standard civ2 cities.gif.

You need a graphics software to copy the city graphics you like from Harlan´s collection, then paste them into the standard civ2 cities.gif (or in the cities.gif of a scenario). If you are not familiar with such things, most graphics editors have detailed helpfiles which should explain these things...http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/wink.gif

I'm not an archer here, I see.

Concordia es parvae crescunt means something like Union makes Power. Translated in to Dutch it is:
Eendracht maakt macht.
That translated in to English:
Eendracht=Unity
maakt=makes
macht=power

"United we stand."

It means, "When we stand together, we are strong"

It was used during the 80-year war, the independence war of the Dutch against the Spanish. All the provinces had to stand together so they could resist the Spanish and be free.
