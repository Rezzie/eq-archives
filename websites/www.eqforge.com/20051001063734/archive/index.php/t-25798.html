Nick

If it is zipped, unzip it using Winzip (download from www.winzip.com); right click the iconon your desktop and click "Extract Here" to do so. Load up Civ 3 then click Load Game then hit the Up icon (just above Auto) a couple of times until you find Windows, click that then click Desktop and the load game should be there. Click on that, confirm and you are away!
