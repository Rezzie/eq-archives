Triremes have a 33% (or is it 50%???) chance of being lost at sea each turn, so it is certainly possible to cross vast stretches of ocean with a Trireme - but you have to be prepared to sacrifice Triremes to do it. It took me 6 tries to cross - and the third Trireme foundered in site of land before I could make contact!

Even after the patch, the Lighthouse gives Triremes an extra movement point and allows safe Sea travel. Both are useful for increasing the chances of surviving long crossings.
