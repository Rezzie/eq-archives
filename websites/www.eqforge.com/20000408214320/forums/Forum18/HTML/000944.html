I've been trying to figure this one out for some time now...every night for the past two weeks I go through Paw and kill all of the named gnolls (4 total + the Ishva Mal).

The Ishva is rarely up, but the others seem to spawn on a 3hr cycle (though sometimes it doesnt work right).

Each of these gnolls drops an item VERY rarely. In fact I've only gotten one drop of each in two weeks (actually two drops on one).

Here's what I know:

The Named Gnoll in the old Captain/Shaman area drops a 1HS sword -- 10/38 +5dex, Effect: Strong Poison. It's No Drop.

The Named Gnoll near the old Ghanex spot drops the Gnoll Hide Tome -- +10int, +5wis, -10str and some poison resist and hp. Nice item.

The Named Gnoll just around the corner from the old Ghanex spot drops a Shield -- 12ac, 5dex and some poison resist. It has a unique graphic - brown with a eagle looking emblem.

The Named Gnoll down where the commanders and shaman used to spawn drops another 1HS sword - can't remember stats, but nothing too special - again No Drop.

I can only assume that one of these gnolls has a secondary drop - the shaman spear. Time will tell.
