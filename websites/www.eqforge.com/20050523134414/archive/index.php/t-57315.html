As we all know, there is a Maximum number of characters one can have. This number is Two. Having two characters is fine for some storylines, but not for others. Having many characters, like five, is sometimes needed to make a succesful story line.

I am standing (really sitting...) here today asking for a repeal of the Character Maximum. I feel it is an unfair bar which can stall creativity. If we had whatever amount we needed, that would be fair. I, as Game Manager, would step in if someone were creating characters for money (besides, the amount of income goes to the player)

I am using for my defense, as always, the Sir SaaMs. I call upon "The Birth Of Cool" (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=49043&perpage=20&display=&pagenumber=1) as my defense. This... odd... story line could have never occured with just two characters. It took several to get the job done.

In conclusion, Im just asking for more characters.

SaaM

well, maybe i got an idea for how you can still limit it:

2 characters is the standard maximum for all players. those are the 2 permanent characters of the player.

every player can have as many additional "temporary" characters as he wishes, but he/she MUST get approval by the rpg manager himself for them.

to get approoval, the player must pm a request for those characters with their names, stats and their purpose (maybe a short outline of the planned storyline or other purposes) to the rpg manager. he will then receive official approval in the rpg management thread.

those temporary characters should be removed during the game. the main roleplaying of the player should take place within his 2 permanent characters.

a maximum of 10 temporary characters is set.

the rpg manager can decide at any time to "kill off" any temporary character in the rpg if he/she thinks they are not used for roleplay or are used to "cheat".

a player can "kill off" any of his permanent or temporary characters at any time. but he must roleplay that!

it must clearly be stated in the character registry post of the player which characters are permanent and which are temporary.

any permanent character may be changed into a temporary character at any time. same for temporary characters. a "status change" of a character may only apply ONE TIME during each term. for all of a player's characters.

one addition maybe: we should allow people to let their characters have children though not forcing them to. the children could then be the temporary characters for example and later during the rpg become the permanent characters (for example if the player lets one of his permanent characters die).
example:
lucentio and victoria could have a child, called vendetto. as lucentio and victoria are my permanent characters atm, vendetto would be a temporary character. after some tc's, i could kill off lucentio during a roleplay and let vendetto be a permanent character...
