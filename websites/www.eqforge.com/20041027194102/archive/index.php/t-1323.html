Hakan,
I recommend the following 3 scenarios:

Verne (from the CD)

Midgard (also from the CD)

T4srgold (a Scenario by Paul Hanti available from the Downloads Section of this Website)

The Scenarios that appeal to you will obviously depend on your interests and tastes. If you have an interest in Medieval history 'T4srgold' will appeal. The Scenario starts in the year 1000 AD and involves the struggle for supremacy between the 4 Italian trading/maritime Republics (hence the unusual title), Venice, Genoa, Pisa and Amalfi(initially belongs to the Byzantines), Byzantines, French\Holy RomanEmpire, Normans and Turks. There is a degree of historical latitude, however I played as the Byzantines and this Scenario is THE BEST at recreating the predicament the Byzantines faced - caught between East (the Turks) and West (Normans, Venetians etc.)

The units and graphics are good, but most importantly the way the Scenario plays is very good (first time I played, I thought Paul Hanti must have improved the AI, however it was the excellent way he has stuctured the Scenario that gave that impression). There are ways this Scenario could be improved however that is for a different thread - I strongly recommend 'T4srgold'. http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/goodwork.gif
