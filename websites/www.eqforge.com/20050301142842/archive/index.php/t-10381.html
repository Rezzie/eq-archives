I think the best way to increase your odds is to always make sure you're sending your elite troops into battle, even if it means waiting for them to heal up. On a side note, I think the first GL should always go towards an army (since it lets you build three small wonders), and all the rest towards rushing. Armies are nice and all, but there's a second way to get your second army but only one way to rush a wonder.

I think I remember seeing that the percentage boost for getting a GL under militaristic wasn't all that great, less than 4% increase? Though having more elite troops would definately help.

Definately nice to have, but going games between them isn't too suprising or too much of a problem, IMNSHO.
