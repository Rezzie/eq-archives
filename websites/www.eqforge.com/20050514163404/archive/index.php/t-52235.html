Before it gets any worse, let me say...

PLEASE STAY ON THE CURRENT TECH!!!!

We have NO techs to work with, so any sense of organization, defined groups, etc. is unrealistic. Orders as seen in one thread, is more along the lines of Code of Laws, if not Fuedalism.

Remember, we're in a village, surrounded by other small tribes. We haven't settled yet, so we don't have a nation. We only have access to maybe a couple hundred items of weaponry, if any at all. Please, no 1,000 racoon berrets (where do they come from? We only exist on an acre or so of land). If you need to get those items, have a character with a tracking trait join one of yours to hunt for racoons. Even still, there aren't going to be 1,000 racoons in a few acres. More like 50-100 at the most. You're going to have to barter with other tribes for tools and weapons. Not only that, you have the despot to content with.

Another example is recruits. In ancient societies (looks like my Anthropology class paid off!), children were trained as fighters from the time they could stand. Recruits imply conscription, which is at Nationalism. It's FAR, FAR too early for recruits.
