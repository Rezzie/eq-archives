Some of the mods (like mine) are too extensive to play as scenarios. They change graphics files, unit files, civilopedia files, etc. Many of the established mods (like mine ;)) have install and uninstall files so you can just pop the mod in and out when you want to play it. For the others you need to back up the original files and then restore them manually.
