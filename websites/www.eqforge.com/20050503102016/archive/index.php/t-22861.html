Upon reflection, I believe the defining strategy in "Iroquois Nation" was intensive trading coupled with diplomacy.

http://www.crowncity.net/civ3/gotm7/ad1000-trade.jpg

http://www.crowncity.net/civ3/gotm7/

I analysed the trade situation as of 1000 AD. Basically, no one could afford to go to war with the Iroquois. If they did declare before the trade agreement expired, then their reputation would be affected; and if they waited for the end of the trade agreement, I would be prepared to renegotiate or at least anticipate any breakdown in diplomatic relations. Everybody's making a buck.

Rome
25g per turn --> Furs and spices
Dye --> 13g per turn

Persia
10g per turn --> Spice
Wine --> 30g per turn

Germany
Peace --> Horses
(Helping the poor oppressed Germans fight Persian agression by providing horses.)

Japan
Ivory --> Spice and gems

England
Incense --> 25g per turn and spices
