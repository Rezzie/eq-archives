This phenomenon is known as the "Future Tech Rollover." In essense, the program has only 254 or 255 slots available for scientific research. When you reach future tech 167 (or 168), the scientific research paradigm resets from 32,000 beakers per advance back to the original ten beakers per advance. Consequently, when attempting to complete the 255 FT maximum, the real challenge is to get to FT 167; past that, the remaining techs can be finished off in as few as 3 or turns.

(And by the way, if you make to 255, make sure you don't get FT 256, because this will wipe out all points for Future Techs.)
