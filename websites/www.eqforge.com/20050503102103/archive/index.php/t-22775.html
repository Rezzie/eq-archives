How's this for a proposal:

Every couple of months, the top ten or twenty players in the GOTM rankings play a GOTM against the top five from Apolyton.

It could be played as a game where the players write-up what their moves etc are, and the rest of us could "watch" from the sidelines. We would obviously need some form honour system to stop the players reading about each others moves. Perhaps the players have a tighter schedule to work (say two weeks) and they postpone the write-up so that it lags the actual game by quite a period. If the top players can't make the time for it, then the next players can step in.

This idea needs development, but the aim is to 1.) make it a "spectator" type event, and 2.) give players more incentive to try and get into the top 20.
