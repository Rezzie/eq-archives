I stopped Friday's chat prematurely due to a possible conflict between the Military and Domestic departments. It seems that in order to make the best possible showing in or current war with Egypt, we may have to send our treasury well below what the Domestic Department sees fit.

-We currently have 146g in our coffers and are making 22 gold per turn.

-We have immediate upgrade opportunites for 2 Warriors in Aureus, as well as another coming from Lake Asphinxia in a few turns. Besides that, we have 8 other warriors , mostly defending our mainland.

-Domestic had once advised that our treasury limit not dip below 150g. However, there were no instructions from Domestic for Friday's chat.


As I see it right now, there is no reason for us to keep that much gold on hand during this war, and so I would ask that we have no spending limit at this time.

Here is my reasoning:

1. I want this war to be a slam dunk. I believe that upgrading these three units(as well as others along the way) can get us very close to that point.

2. Both Noshuret and Gorina are set to grow within 2 turns. This will enhance our gpt, especially during our Golden Age.

3. There is nothing to really use the extra gold for right now except Military. We don't really need embassies with America or the Aztecs right now, and any possible tech deals for gold are way out of our price range anyway. Plus, we can't use gold to rush improvements since we are still in Despotism.


Believe me, I am not usually a proponent for redlining our treasury but I believe that this situation warrants it. Let's drive Cleo back to Thebes and beyond with the power of our mighty Legions. Then, once the dust has settled, we can build a strong commercial base with the help of our newly acquired cities and a properly placed Forbidden Palace.
