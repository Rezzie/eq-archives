I would like to see the moderator(or moderators) take a quick look at some of the key event polls for the GOTMs and then link them in near the beginning of the results page.

This is not because I posted most of the key events polls to start with, but because I feel these polls make a great summary of some of the spects of the game that would not be available in other forms without a great deal of human effort.

For GOTM8, the key event polls were:

How did you make contact with the civs on the second continent? (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=24687)

and

What was the final score for Russia in your game? (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=24686)

The fact that 38 people responded to the contact poll and almost 50% of those respondents indicated that they traded for contact with the the other continent is a very interesting result.

The poll about Russia's score only had 34 respondents but it also contained a good distribution of responses that showed people did not necessarily just wail the tar out of Cathy to be successful.

It would be nice to urge better participation in these types of polls to get the coverage of the data up to about 60 to 80% of the submitted game files. This would be very educational.

I should also acknowledge that I was not 100% ignoring Phillipe's poll attempts here, but I do not think he really grasped the objective of the polls since one of the examples was just a blind repeat of the victory type submission.

Putting together functional polls for the special events is a little bit tough to do, but I think we should encourage this process by perhaps making this task one of the perks/pains or winning a prior GOTM or winning a medal in a prior GOTM. Definately do not want to put more work on Matrix since he does so much already, but I see some real opportunity to expand the group discussion potential of the GOTMs by using these key event polls as a basis for thought.
