This is a Domestic sponsered wonder thread. ;)

Based on the costs of techs, and our current research, our research time now is 3x the cost of the tech (minus devaluation).

In other words, Writing's cost is 8, we get it in 24. Masonry is 4, and we get it in 12. Lit's is 10, so we can expect it in 30 turns.

So, that means we have about 54 turns to prepare for The Great Library. This is why Masonry is so important - the palace prebuild.

TGL is 400 shields, which means we'd need a city with an average of 7spt. This is why we need a granary for fast growth, and a palace prebuild. Gorina or Penguin would be good for this. Gorina is the better of the two, as it can go above size 6.
