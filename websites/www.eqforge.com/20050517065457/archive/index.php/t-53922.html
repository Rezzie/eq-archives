Now, as reminder, here are the clasifications:

A Carthage : A Carthage-esque civilization is a bitter enemy of ours. We will not stop until we destroy this civ. They are our sworn enemy, and they deserved it. They have either attacked us or done something horrible against us.

A Jerusalem: A Jerusalem type civilization is a civ which has become victim of our might. We take them over, sometimes without reason.

A Gaul : A long term enemy. Similar to a Carthage, but this involves advance plans with multiple fronts and a larger amounts of units. We may not necessarily destroy this civ.

An Italia - A constant ally. We just seem to like these guys. We would, normally, help them out. We get them on our side in most wars.

A Persia - We are yet to kill these guys. We are at peace with them, but who knows when it may break?

A Goth- This nation we get along with, now. We dont know what will happen if we turn the other way on them, but we hope it wont result in the Middle Ages.
