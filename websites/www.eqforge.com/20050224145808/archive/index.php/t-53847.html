We now have the opportunity to expand east and west. The included maps show IMO the best city sites but feel free to suggest other sites. In particular I think we should we should found C5 ASAP; it's on the same landmass as the Aztecs and probably Zulus and thus enables us to demand (and probably get) tribute from the Aztecs and Zulus before republic. The probability of getting tribute increases if we build a few military units before demanding tribute.

Of the sites in the west I think C3b and C3c are better than C3a. In theory C3a might be better but quick early growth is better and the forest makes that difficult (the city would either grow slowly or founding it would be delayed by the need to chop down some trees before founding a city).
