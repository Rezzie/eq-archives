Here is all the info on this quest that I have gotten from going on test and doing alot of work.

After doing the first newbie quest that Oxyn asks of you (2 mosquito wings, bone chips, mud crab), he tells you to go talk to the Furscales.
Shezlie and Zazlan Furscale are on the second floor of the northwest ruined building just south and east of the shaman guild. If you just hail them they say something about broodlings being careful. Say "Oxyn sent me" and they both say something about you needing to get something for them for payment.
Tell Shezlie "I will collect some more bundles" and she tells you of the armor she will give.
HELM - banded helm, sapphire, Bargynn's Digger, Sythrax Building Plans
GAUNTLETS - banded gauntlets, ruby, Venomous Parchment, Warlord Drawing Plans
LEGGINGS - banded leggings, fire emerald, Recruitment Letter, Exiled Iksar Head
TUNIC - banded tunic, star ruby, Watchman's Spectacles, Nectar of Isolation

If you tell Zazlan "I will collect rare supplies" he tells you of his armor.
SLEEVES - banded sleeves, sapphire, Frenzied Gnawer Tail, Froglok Treated Planks
CLOAK - banded cloak, star ruby, Giant Hammer, Worn Charbone
BOOTS - banded boots, fire emerald, Spirit Caller Beads, Rhino Hoof
BRACER - banded bracer, ruby, Coercion Implant, Gooey Adhesive

All you do is collect the items, buy the gems and armor, and hand it in.

REMEMBER THIS IS ONLY ON TEST RIGHT NOW AND SUBJECT TO CHANGE......

but...... on my regular server I have collected some interesting items as of late. Such as.....
Sarnak recruitment letter from Sarnak Recruits in LoIO
Exiled Iksar Head from Iksar Exiles in WW, LoIO, SoNH
Forest Giant Hammer from Forest Giant Greenwood in WW
Rhino Hoof from lower level rhinos in OT
Coercian Implants from Coerced mobs in Dalnirs
Gooey Adhesive from goos in Dalnirs
Worn Charbone from Charbone skeletons in LoIO, SoNH
Spirit Caller Beads from Spirit Caller Goblins in WW, LoIO

Now thats all I have found and all were Lore Item No Drop If anyone else has found any more items please tell on who and where. Especially the Nectar of Isolation and Watchman's Spectacles (there is a watchman in Timorous Deep btw)
Oh and all the mobs are killable so far by 25+ for sure maybe need help in Dalnirs

The following is a list of what we have and what we don't know a damn thing about If I haven't gotten the item myself I've noted that. No clue on the nectar, went around the Swamp of No Hope devasting Frogloks running loose and all through their town, no planks. Sigh.

Abdiel Al`Azrad - 45 Shaman - Bertox

Seems like this is what we have so far:

Helm: Bargynn's Digger (Loot off of Bargynn, named burynai at the bottom of Kurn's Tower), Sythrax Building Plans (Loot off of Gharg Oberbord - Troll SK in the Sythrax Cave In the Field of Bone)

Gauntlets: Venemous Parchment (Loot off of Venemous Lampreys in Firiona Vie), Warlord Drawing (Quest giver says warlord drawing PLANS, but the warlords are dropping a Warlord Drawing Quill. Probably a typo on Verant's part? Loot quill off of Goblin Warlords in The Lake of Ill Omen)

Leggings: Sarnak Recruitment Letter (Loot off of Sarnak Recruits in The Lake of Ill Omen), Exiled Iksar Head (Loot off of Iksar Exiles, found in The Lake of Ill Omen and Warsliks Wood)

Tunic: Watchman Spectacles: (Loot off of Raider Watchman in ogre town on the ogre island in Timorous Deep), Nectar of Isolation (Location unknown)

Sleeves: Frenzied Gnawer Tail (Heard both Dalnir and Kaesora nibbler types for this one, haven't tested personally), Treated Froglok Planks (Location Unknown)

Cloak: Forest Giant Hammer (Loot off of Forest Giants in Warsliks Wood), Worn Charbone (Supposed to loot off of Lake of Ill Omen or Swamp of No Hope charbone skeletons, haven't been able to find any yet personally)

Boots: Spirit Caller Beads (Loots off of Goblin Spirit Callers in The Lake of Ill Omen or Warsliks Wood), Rhino Hoof (Loot off of the lowest level rhinos in The Overthere)

Bracer: Coercion Implant (Loot off of any "coerced" mob in Dalnir), Gooey Adhesive (Loot off of any "goo" type mob in Dalnir)
