Method One:

1. Sit
2. Wait for the mana bar to move or "tick"
3. Stand
4. Cast Canibalize
5. Sit
6. Repeat Steps 2-5

Also buffing yourself with regen/chloro/regrowth helps too.

Method two:

Make a hot key with the following lines

/sit off

/cast # (the # being the spell slot from the top that cannibalize is in...if cannibalize is the fourth spell from the top then this would read /cast 4)

/pause 13

/sit on

after the hot key is made then its easier to just.

1. Sit
2. Wait for the mana bar to tick
3. Hit hot key
4. Repeat steps 2 and 3

Basically the way it works is this.

You sit until you see the med tick(when your mana bars moves), then you stand cast cann and sit right away. If you can get this accomplished before the time for the next med tick you will gain the mana from medding.(the computer will think that you have been sitting the whole time)

Some people have even set up social keys with the commands to do this.

What I do is keep cann hotkeyed to my number 5 key and sit hotkeyed to my number 6 key.

So I hit 6 and wait until I see the bar move, then I hit 6,5 right away and as soon as the casting bar goes I hit 6 again. Then I wait for the bar to move and repeat.

Remember to always keep regen or chloro up to maximize effeciency?sp?

Hope this helps.

Tips for watching the "tick"

1. The "dream catcher" icon is very lag sensitive..so don't rely on it.

2. The health bar and mana bar "tick" at the same time..sometimes you will see the health bar tick and not the mana bar but that's okay..its safe to dance when health bar ticks.

3. where the bubs on the mana bar connect there a blind spots..you can't see two or so ticks..you have to rely on your timing during these gaps.

4. After you cast, your whole spell selection goes blank..it usually comes back right at the tick of the mana bar..if you're looking for a visual trigger to rely on this is much more reliable than the spinning dream catcher icon.

5. The most effective way is to press your nose up to the monitor and watch that silly little mana bar until your eyes bug out.

The cannidance works because you gain mana from a tick worth of medding and also a cast of cannibalise.

There is a simple way to prove that the cannidance works.

Remove any wis gear you have on (to maximise the effect of meditate on your mana bar). Drain your mana to zero. Now try standing up and then sitting back down within a tick cycle (ie. the same as a cannidance but without casting cannibalise).

You will see your mana go up. This is not due to cannibalise as you are not casting it. Therefore it is due to the dance part.
