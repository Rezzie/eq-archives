What the hell is Dugru?

Anyway, Hobart clearly stated that the second skull was obtained through another quest, NOT a drop.

I took my ranger to Burning Woods. Here's what I've discovered so far.

YOU can get to the quest NPC. *I* cannot. Therefore, I cannot verify that he is indeed the quest NPC nor can I tell you what you need. It's gonna take a shaman of at least 24th level to get up there. Here's how you do it:

If you could get an invis beforehand, it will me much easier. You zone to Burning Wood from Frontier Mountains like you did to forage your Mendglow Clay (ranger foraged one while he was there....) From there follow the right hand wall north up to around 5500. Have levi on during the trip and run like the devil was after you. Everything that my ranger saw was blue, even, and yellow at 39th level. Nothing was green. If something happens to agro on you, keep running. You'll outpace it with SoW. You can get to the 5500 area, which has no spawns or roamers as far as I could tell. You will then have outrun it far enough to be able to log and clear agro. Log back in. Here's where it gets fun. With levi on, creap forward until you can see the walls of the sarnak fort. Stay ON the right (east) wall, turn around, and head south (keeping the wall to your left now). Use your up pitch button to be looking up as you go and climb as high as you can on the wall. As soon as you've gotten as high as you can, as far north as you can get at that altitude, do what only you can do. Cast Cheetah on yourself. I've been told by a reliable source that it's MUCH faster than SoW. Once you have Cheetah on, with levi, from as high as you can get, run straight north towards the walls. Head for the top of the tower and slip through the space between the overhang and the walls of the tower. Once inside, you can speak with the NPC. I didn't quite make it that far with SoW, but I DID manage to get on top of the wall east of the tower, which I could find no other way to do.

OK. Somebody with Cheetah go test this out. I've done all I can do for you at the moment, and my iksar shaman may never get high enough level to do this quest. I only play him for a few hours once a week.

When using levitate, you can climb the side walls near where the trees hit the walls. The zone edge between the trees and the sarnak towers is before the cliff wall, so you can't "climb" the edge there. You'll have to just follow the zone edge south until you get to a place where you can climb up the cliff or a tree. It's almost vertical, so you'll have to pitch up and just keep moving forward facing the cliff or trees. It will barely look like you're moving, but you should notice yourself getting slightly higher in the air. You may have to adjust your direction left or right slightly if you get stuck. Work at it for a few minutes until you're absolutely SURE you're as high as you can get. Then Cheetah and run yer butt off towards the sarnak tower. The extra elevation should make up for the extra distance using Cheetah. You're probably right. You may need to Shrink in order to fit through the gap between the roof and the top of the tower wall.

"Mystic Donovan sent me to recover the lost Skull of Dai Nozok."
"Mystic Donovan sent me to recover the lost Skull of Die Nozok."
"Mystic Donovan sent me to recover the lost Skulls of Di Nozok."
"Dai Nozok wishes for his(her) skull to be recovered."
"Die Nozok wishes for his(her) skull to be recovered."
"What conversationalist?"
"How have you heard of me?"
"How have you heard of you?"
"Who are your weaker minions?"

"I wish to speak of the Skulls of Di Nozok."
"I wish to speak of the Skull of Die Nozok."
"I wish to speak of the Skull of Dai Nozok."
"Will you speak to me of the Skull of Die Nozok?"
"Will you speak to me of the Skull of Dai Nozok?"
