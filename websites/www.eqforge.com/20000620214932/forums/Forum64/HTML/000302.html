You should be able to break solo the Butler room (just the Butler PH and the garg) at about level 43. You should be able to break and solo both rooms and the hall at about level 47. You will need to use Snare, Harmony, and the fact that some mobs will be green. You must NEVER allow them to get to the doors of the kitchen. If you do, you die.

Vials of Vampiric Blood are good to have if you plan to solo here. It helps a lot with the casters.

Levels are about right from Wadin's post and I'd start with the Butler room to get the hang of it. There are only two spawns in the Butler room to worry about.

This is a situation where high AC isn't as important as high MR so drag out that MR gear and put the runed bone fork in your range slot as you'll have no need of a bow in here.

Iuvas and I kept the spawn broken for a couple of hours at 40th and 45th respectively. Had to take the Maid down solo since she popped right when Iuvas was going to the zone to pick up a druid and warrior we'd asked to come over. Snare and mana dumping CL took her down and melee finished it.
