Still had mentioned this was his/her first GOTM, so I thought I would respond in an encouraging way. This month's game is on Deity, and as a result few people are winning the game. As yet, only 4 people have indicated on the spoilers thread that they have won the game, although a few other players are coming close. My point was that even the most elite players are having some difficulties with the game (though probably less than I did). Do I normally expect a lot of losses? No, I don't, but on a Deity-level game something would be wrong if the majority of the people were winning without much trouble. Diety should be the realm where only the most elite players can consistently win games, and Civ3 delivers admirably in this respect.

My goal was to make players who may be struggling feel better about themselves and realize how difficult this month's game will be to win. Although I did manage to win, things looked exceptionally bleak at times. I hope this will help those who are struggling in the game stay with it to the end, because even if you don't win you should still learn something from the game (I know I did!) :goodjob:
