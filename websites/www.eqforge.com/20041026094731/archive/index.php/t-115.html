Gentlemen, civkies and dodgy Brazilians,

I am a little bemused as to why someone should spend any amount of time trying to build a HOF score on a custom map that had no "enemies" in the game.

I am currently playing a Custom HOF game and am thoroughly enjoying having to compete with two other Civs with 60+cities and having major wars.

My point is anyone with the knowledge can set up a custom game with and put Civs where they want and alter other aspects, but I cannot see the point.

The map that I am playing on was created by a fellow Civkie and all I knew was that it was one continent.

Although you cannot dictate(or can you) what makes a valid game for a HOF purpose, surely you must have some sort of guilt in submitting obviously constructed(cheat) games.

Perhaps the only option lies in supplying worlds for people to play and relying on honesty in playing them.
