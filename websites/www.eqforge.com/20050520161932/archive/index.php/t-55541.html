I was reading through the Gorina thread, and was pleasently suprised by the level of participation that Gorinan citizens acted at.

This put the idea for political workings on the provincial level in my head, but I swear dis proposed this before. :)

I would imagine such a system would work this way:

Once a defined province reached a certain number of citizens, say ten, the population of the province would have the option of developing a provincial charter. The charter would define a provincial government, with the only mandatory items being a termly gubenatorial election conducted in a certain fashion (see below), a democratic government, and recognition of the Fanatican constitution as the supreme law of the land.

A big piece of this political proposal would be allowing people in a province to vote for their governor. As I see it, this system would work if national votes were factored with the local one. That is, a regular election poll should be posted, along with a poll only for citizens of the province. When the vote is over, the results of both would be averaged.

Thoughts?
