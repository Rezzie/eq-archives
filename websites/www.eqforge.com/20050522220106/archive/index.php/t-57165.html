I personally find the debates are annoying & useless. For several reasons:

1) None of the debate questions refer to the candidates ability to run the office they are running for.

2) Some debate questions have listed the candadates goals. This is a democracy game. It should matter very little what the candidate wants to do or not. He is just one voice out of many. It is what the people want to do and the candidate has very little say in the matter.

3) Most people vote on who they like better anyway. No debate is going to make a differance in that. The demogame over time has formed several say... political parties. Those people that agree with eachother often tend to vote for eachother in polls.

I am not suggesting getting rid of the debates overall, but rather to address the "problems" the debates have. Which I listed above. So does anyone have any thoughts? Idea's?
