If you intend on participating in the RPG's Economic Aspect, you must post in this thread. If your info changes, edit your post in this thread, lest it be lost.

Salary is calculated by caste level, as such:
Common: 10
Lower Gentry: 12
Upper Gentry: 14
Noble: 16
High Noble: 18
Royal: 20
Imperial: 22

You may also recieve turn chat-ly from land income.

Please format all posts in the following manner:

Character Name: (you character's name)
Caste: (character's caste) - (Link to verify character's caste) (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=55326) - (gold from land info)
Land income: (gold from land income) - (link to verify land income from land office (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51788) - (gold deducted for troop upkeep) - (link to verify gold deducted for troop upkeep) (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=55255) - (Net gold income)
Total Gold Income - (add salary gold and net land gold income)

Replace all text in () with correct info.

THIS IS NOT A DISCUSSION THREAD.

If your post contains incorrect info, you will notified by myself via PM or through the #civfanatics chat room.
