All that I can say about day 3 is wow. I had to meet a friend in Gfay and could not get a teleport to save my life. Well the boat was at the dock so I jumped on to ride it back. Start going back we stop and I get on a shuttle. Bad Move!! I guess on the way back it makes an extra stop. So I am now stuck in the middle of Timorus deep with 2 poor suckers who followed me, and probably a wait of 45 min for the next boat to come around. I logged off and visited my favorite site and then came back on.

Well I took the two new guys to the most wonderfull place in the world. The teleportation center. One was a caster and bound himself there, I did also. I now have the one thing I was jelous of. The ability to go anywhere I want to. I figure I am about 10-15 min from most places in the game. No more begging for teleports. No more paying 50-100 plat to a money grubbing druid or wizard. If you have not bound yourself here then get a going.

Well after running errands all over the world I jumped into the Lizard city. I was invis and you walk 2 feet to zone into Lake of Ill omen. Its a 5 min run from there following the left hand wall to firona vie. From there I ran to the dreadlands. For anyones interest go to -200, 3600 there is along tunnel (I do mean long) run straight through to 300, 6000 and you will zone into the dreadlands. From there its a quick straight run to the castle.

Well I got into the castle, OOC'd my services and got into a group. There were between 30 and 50 in the zone. Most people pulling to the zone line. The monsters were tough but not that bad. The hard part was all the trains. There was a Stupid!! Barbarian warrior who kept bringing trains through making everyone zone. By the way if you see a massive train tell your group to zone and run to zone. Do not do anything else. Well besides the trains I got some exp. Our group only got one good item that went to the paladin. It was a 37 48 Axe with -10 to all resists. Man our paladin felt like a warrior and was hitting in the 140s a lot. Well I camped out just outside the castle and tomorrow I will be doing more of the same.
