Comments:

Science has been shut down (it is 0%) as had been decided, everything in the science plan has been discovered. Despite this we are making discoveries every 17 turns because of the Einsteins. This is not a problem at the moment, we are researching Atomic Theory and it is Industrialization we want to avoid until we have built our infrastructure.

We have considerable cash ($1548) despite some rush buys in 1670 AD (and lots of them in 1660 AD).

Despite emphasis on military stuff as was voted for in the Coresux build queue polls building the infrastructure called for in MODALTSIR has started but at a relatively modest pace. It will soon get much faster because military buildup for invading Africa is finished and military buildup for invading Hell is almost complete should we decide to do so.

As mentined military buildup for invading Africa is complete but just in case we might want to build an extra veteran cavalry in Suggestions and send it to Zimbabwe - the military advisor has a lot of goodies to play with in Suggestions ;).

Military buildup for invading Hell (should we decide to do so) is almost complete. Naervana is our base of operations in this case. I'd add one more unit of alpine troops, a diplomat and maybe a cavalry or two if we go for it. Exploration has revealed that we could use fortresses to completely block Greek access to Knossos, Corinth, Athens and Argos.

The Dou hides caravan near Naervana can be sent to Thermopylae (!) which demands hides.

No AI civ has discovered or is researching gunpowder but it is only a matter of time before this happens - some of them are researching invention (a prereq for GP). The same is true of metallurgy (which would enable the AI civs to build coastal fortresses to defend against our mighty destroyers).
