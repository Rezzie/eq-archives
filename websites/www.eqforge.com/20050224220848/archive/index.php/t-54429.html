This is an overview/recommendations of what to do in the following 4 turns assuming no major upsets (barbarian/AI invasions etc.) but playing more than 4 turns should easily be possible. It is based on the poll results and preceding discussion. The poll results may change from the publication of this list until funxus plays but hardly drastically.

There is one modification as a result of the discussion, I recommend building two caravans in Dou before aqueduct. These will yield lots of cash and science when delivered and probably delay Dou's growth beyond size 8 (because of the aqueduct delay) but only slightly (1 or 2 turns) so this hardly contradicts the poll result.

Important: No caravans should be delivered in Dou until it has built its three caravans in 520 AD. On the turn caravans are delivered maximize trade and eliminate possible Elvii in the source and destination cities, deliver the caravan(s) and then reset production/Elvii to what it was. This maximizes the delivery bonuses.

I'm leaving the decision of exactly where to deliver Dou's caravans to our president. Everyone demands dye, Delhi demands hides, Ulundi and Orleans demand beads. Cities far away get us higher delivery bonuses but OTOH $500 10 turns from now may be better than $1000 20 turns from now.

In addition to the list below we maybe should rush buy defensive units in BP and FJ, either warriors, phalanxes, diplomats or horsemen.

The entries preceded by an asterisk are some events that happen, for example something that gets built in a city.

460 AD
Set taxes to 80%, science to 20%.
Rush buy caravans in VM and Dou
Switch to harbor in LP, FW, EF, Ipec, Igloria
Switch to marketplace in Nae, Neu

480 AD
*VM and Dou build caravans.
*Dou reaches size 6
Rush buy caravans in LC, PB
Start harbor in VM.
Start another caravan in Dou

500 AD
*Dou may reach size 7
*RF, LC, PB build caravans. These should be delivered in Dou.
Start harbor in PB
Start marketplace in LC and RF.
Rush buy caravan in Dou if possible.

520 AD
*Dou probably builds caravan, may reach size 8.
Start aqueduct in Dou.

Continue until we have $400 or more, then set science to 0% and luxuries as high as needed to get all size 3+ cities celebrating.

PS Apologies if there are any errors in the above plan, it's rather likely :D
