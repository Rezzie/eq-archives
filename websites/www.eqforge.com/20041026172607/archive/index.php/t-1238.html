Sounds like your having the traditional GIF formatting problems that plague all scenario and modpack writers. Don't worry, everything you are experiencing is normal...you have to be patient with terrains and use the "save" function on your graphics editor as opposed to "save as". For some reason, not all editors save in exactly the same gif format...I haven't figured out why yet, but it is supposed to be saved as a GIF89a format with 8 bits and 256 color palette (I think). Otherwise, if one thing is off...you get a bunch of black junk, or you might just get it anyway, even if you do it right.

Also, the black square boarders are because your sprites (the little diamonds) are not big enough. make 'em bigger or add more terrain to the edge and that should fix it up.
