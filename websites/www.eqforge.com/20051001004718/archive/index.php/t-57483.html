The citizens have chosen a switch to Monarchy, but there is an urgent remaining question. When should the switch occur? Settlers need to be produced as soon as possible to claim some territory while we still have the chance, and going into anarchy would stop us dead in our tracks for 5-6 turns. The seafaring nations might settle our borders if we don't hurry.

This discussion will run for 24 hours. I call upon our Domestic Deputy (if I win the runoff) or Leader to post the following poll tomorrow, as I'll be on the road then and therefore without access.

When shall we switch governments?

Immediately, next turn chat
Wait until the current crop of settlers is done
Wait until our space is filled
Other (please specify)
Abstain


The first 2 choices (now, current crop) are gradated vs (space filled, other). If they get more votes combined than (space filled) combined, then that should be seen as a vote for as soon as possible. See the Polling Standards for a description of a gradated poll.

Let the discussion begin ;)
