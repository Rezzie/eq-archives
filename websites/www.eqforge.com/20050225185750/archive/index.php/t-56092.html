The Domestic Ministry asked for when we can complete research on Democracy. The science ministry tried to assess our situation and layout a few plans that we can follow to complete research on Democracy.

I’ve tried to calculate these plans out as accurately as I can, however, we are dealing with so many interrelated variables, it’s hard to know if I’ve got everything right. There is the celebration growth of Doughnutia and its impact on trade (including corruption), the value of new trade routes created, trying to project the cost of our next tech after invention. The further out in time I try to project, the likelihood of error increases exponentially.

With that in mind I have three possible plans laid out. The first two are designed to learn Democracy going into the 1180 oedo turn so that we can immediately revolt and go into Democracy government without subjecting all our cities to a turn of Anarchy. The difference between the first and second plan is that the first should provide considerable margin for error on achieving democracy in 1180. The second provides slightly more gold for rush buys in the early turns, but cuts things very close for completion of Democracy in time for the 1180 revolution. The president may need to make some last minute adjustments to reach this in time. The third plan delays the discovery of Democracy by reducing science to 10% again, and does not learn Democracy in an Oedo year. However, this plan provides a couple hundred gold per turn extra for spending on rush builds and more caravans are delivered before Invention is learned.

Please vote for one of the three plans. Also, please vote on the desirability of an instant revolution to Democracy. The Science ministry strongly urges we take advantage of this opportunity for several reasons:
-Our best city, Doughnutia should not have to suffer through Anarchy if we can avoid it
- Democracy will alleviate corruption in our more distant cities and will thus actually allow for bigger delivery payments than I have calculated for the 1180 and 1220 turns (I couldn’t get that fancy!)
- We only have 6 units (5 triremes, 1 elephant) that are currently in position to cause unhappiness and no city supports more than one of these, so the unhappiness should be manageable until we get either Theology+Bach or Magnetism+Leo

Situation summary:
- We are currently researching Invention (~30% complete), which will result in our caravan delivery payments being cut in half once it is complete.
- Democracy will be available as the next tech and should be chosen for our next research.
- We have 16 caravans, 15 of which can be delivered to their destinations by 1200. 9 can reach their destinations by 1140; the other 6 will not arrive until at least 1180. Only three are currently demanded by their destinations: EF Coal to Dou (1180), Dou Hides to Tlaxcala (1200), Dou Gold to Delhi/Bangalore (later). Payoff calculations assume all three are still demanded when delivered.
- Doughnutia is size 13 and growing from celebrations. I project the city should be able to grow uninterrupted to at least size 20 without a change in the tax rate. Delivery payoffs assume an increase in population each turn at least until all trade supporting squares are occupied (i.e., get the road done on the plains SW of Dou ASAP)
- I did not assume any of the in-progress camels would be rushbuilt in time to be delivered by 1200. If some aggressive RBs are made, we might be able to deliver a couple in 1180-1200, but it didn’t seem likely, so I did not deal with them at this time.

Originally posted by TheViking
Hmmm... we must revolt in 1160 meaning that science drops to zero. Will we actually be able to discover democracy in 1180 despite this ?

NO, that's not how it works. The whole point is that you don't have to revolt in 1160.

When you discover a new Government tech, you are always asked if you want to start a revolution RIGHT AWAY. ("Yes, start a revolution now", or "No, Republic is working fine for now")

You select "Yes, start the revolution" and then only the cities that have yet to be processed that turn experience the Anarchy. Because 1180 is an oedo year, when all cities have been processed, you get the option to choose the new government. Pick Democracy...all is good.

In either of the first two plans, we stay in Republic until at least the processing of Doughnutia. It's beakers are necessary to complete Democracy in either case. It is likely a few of the cities processed after it will be needed as well (Especially in the "Riskier" plan). Once the system has processed the city that throws us over the top on Democracy, we get the pop up and start the revolution. Then only those cities yet to be processed (founded earlier) experience the anarchy. This is why it is so valuable to do it this way if we can.
