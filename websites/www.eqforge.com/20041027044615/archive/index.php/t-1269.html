Håkan, of course you can make scenarios for the original civ, but the options for scenario design are very limited there. They became much better with Fantastic Worlds, so a player who likes scns should have that add-on or the MGE version, which allows to play FW scns. Several online shops still sell MGE, sometimes even FW at very low prices http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/wink.gif

Test of Time has even more and better options for scenario design, but since there were no editors included, it is a lot more work to make scns than for FW or MGE
