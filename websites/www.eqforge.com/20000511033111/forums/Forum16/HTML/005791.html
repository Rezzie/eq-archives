How I found the TD teleport room. (good race)

Got CD, loaded, got a lucky TP to BB, get shuttle at docks, zone to Timorous Deep. Jump off moving shuttle (did not stop) onto the big boat (Ship really). Ship is fast, try /loc a few times. If you want to try to go direct to spires, don't ride the Ship long, it is a quick trip at the speed it moves. I waited for the return trip.

Zone into Firona Via. Don't get off. On return trip, wait till after 1st stop. When you see a long flat island on left (neg 7000, neg 1000?), jump off right side and head SW to neg 12000, pos 4360. (SOW and Levitate make this easier)

Spires don't really stick out of water much. Swim down into the hole in the middle of them. Halas pot is 11'oclock. Looks like a wooden box. 12 o'clock is Freeport (blue symbol on pot) and 4 o'clock is Rivervale. Looks like a torch. (you have to click this one, unlike others you can walk on.)

I bound and then proceeded to TP to Halas, Freeport and RV in quick sucession. All I can say is WOW! Kind of made the game seem different.

I think Baelish got the exclusive from GZ on this, which makes me think it won't change soon.
