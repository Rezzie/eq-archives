Civ3 Game of the Month #4: February 2002

After two Regent and one Monarch level GOTMs, it's time for us to play on a more difficult level: Emperor! This GOTM is more challenging than last month's not just in difficulty levels -- the English's special unit Man-O-War is commonly regarded as a not so useful unit. The good news is that the starting location for this month is excellent and you may be able to use the Man-O-War to good effect on this archipelago map... :)

Here are the detailed Game Parameters:

<IMG SRC="http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/gotm_images/gotm4.jpg" align=right>Civilization: English
Leader: Elizabeth
Difficulty Level: Emperor
Land Form: Archipelago.1
Map Size: Medium
Rivals: 5 Random Rivals
Climate: Random
Temperature: Random
Earth: Random
Barbarians: Random
Victory Conditions: All

Download Links:

- ZIP format (29 KB) (http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/games/GOTM4_Civ3.zip)
- SAV format (0.97 MB) (http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/games/GOTM4_Civ3.SAV)

Playing the Game:

- You must use the DEFAULT RULES and the v1.16f patch to play the game.
- Use of any cheats/trainers and editing of saved games are not allowed.

Submissions:

- Saved games are due March 2nd, 2002.
- Be sure to save around 1 AD and at the turn before the game ends.
- When submitting the game, please include all the info asked in the submission page and zip your saved games. Submissions with incomplete info will not be added.

Have fun playing the GOTM and good luck (you'll need it)! :king:
