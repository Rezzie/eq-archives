Rik Meleet
Jun 16, 2003, 05:12 AM
Our Western Province is going to be an important province. It has a diversity of land-types and recources and has a lot of coast. It will also be close to yet unknown seafaring savages who call themselves German, Babylon, Persia, China and Japan. I predict the Western Province will be the meeting ground of several of them, if not on land than certainly off our coasts. The geographical placement of the province will make other Civs view it as a landing-ground. I will make sure the will not view it that way for a long time.

It is important to settle these lands quickly, but with brains. I intend to not blindly follow a dotmap or an "optimal city placement", but use our ever increasing knowledge to gain the highest benefits from our lands.

If we get lucky enough to claim all of the land I will make this in a cultural and economic region with harbors, markets and libraries.It's geographical location makes it ideal for and vulnerable to invasions. I will make it impenetrable for landing troops and if they do get an invasion force on our shores will throw them into our sea. When it's safe I will make it a jumping board for boats with settlers and troops.

If we won't be lucky enough to claim all of the land I will make sure the savages are removed from our backdoor, either by force or by culture. the Western province must become our backdoor and I will keep it properly locked and flourishing. Our "backgarden" will be a region of great achievements and wealth. This will allow the other provinces focus on military, production and Wonders.

The Western Province cannot really be a production-powerhouse region as it's cities are at best 2nd ring and most likely 3rd or 4th. But it can function as a military training ground for future wars as well. It's best designation is of as our cultural and economic heartland.

Vote Rik Meleet for Governor of the Western Province and you will not be disappointed.
