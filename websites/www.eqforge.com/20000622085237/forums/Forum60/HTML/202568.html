The rubicite armor was never removed from the game - it just doesn't drop any more. Not because it was overpowered but because it was at the time the only decent armor available for PCs from the high 20's to mid 40's

There were no class specific armor quests... no Crafted, Totemic, Darkforge or Etched Ivy quests and so every class that could wear rubicite was camping the same place (Cazic-Thule). It was hell. You think getting into a Frenzied Ghoul camp was hard? You basically had to join a queue for armor that started in Cazic and backed out into The Feerot somewhere near the zone to Rathe Mountains

Most players (those not able to camp 24/7) were still wearing plate (or in the case of rangers, banded mail) up to the mid 40's because the spawns were being controlled by a few guilds and most of the armor was being won by casters who couldn't even wear it and who would then sell it at exorbitant prices.

Rubicite was replaced by the class specific quest armor because if they'd left it dropping nothing much would have changed (i.e. people would still have camped the rubicite in preference to the class specific quests). The rubi drops were replaced by the rogue ravenscale drops. The legacy pieces of rubicite on each server are now 'antiques' and command quite a good price... its a quest in itself to assemble a full outfit It's good to see that rubicite is being reintroduced... perhaps this time it will be scattered all over the world rather than all in one zone.
