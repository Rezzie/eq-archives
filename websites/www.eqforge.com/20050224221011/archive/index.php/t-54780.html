Ministry of Domestic Affairs (MoDA) 560 AD recommendations

The following list is based on the 560 AD poll and the rather limited 560 AD discussion.
The main result is that WLTP will be started now. The way things are looking we should probably celebrate for 4-7 turns, depending on when the celebrations run out of steam.
The goal is to get Dou to size 12 and as many other cities as possible to size 8. We can hardly get them all to size 8 so Neu, LC, RF, LP, EF and FW have priority (in roughly this order).

560 AD
Set tax=60%, lux=40%
Change production in Dou so it builds aqueduct next turn, then set Dou's production to maximum trade.
Maximize trade in Neu and rush buy marketplace there.
Rush buy harbor in Ipec, work ocean instead of grassland.
Rush buy harbor in VM, work goldmine instead of forest.
Remaining cities: Maximize trade but try to have at least one shield.

580 AD and later
LP and EF may need harbor soon.
Increase luxuries if necessary to keep celebrating.
Rush buy stuff as needed, harbors for enough food and marketplaces to keep cities celebrating by increasing trade. Possibly also temples.
Possibly rush buy a courthouse in at least one eastern city if we can afford it but the cities near Dou have priority.
Dou should start a University next (switch to harbor and rush buy it if necessary but I doubt this will happen).
LC may need irrigation soon.
In the unlikely event that we have the $$ to do so, rush buy aqueducts in cities like Neu, LC and RF if/when they reach size 8.
Deliver Dou's caravans to foreign cities but don't deliver until Dou has reached size 12 (this shouldn't be a problem). IIRC we need to be generating at least some beakers when they are delivered to get a science bonus.
