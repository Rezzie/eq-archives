And here they are: the results of the Game of the Month of March (http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/civ3gotm_results_05.shtml). It was clearly a very difficult game, because of the map. One third of the participants has lost the game. Another third has reached victory by space ship and just a handful of people had the game completely under their control.

The game has been won by SirPleb (cultural). Congratulations! He's followed by Aeson (also cultural) and Lucky (the only victory by conquest and fastest finish). SirPleb has taken the lead in the Global Ranking (http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/civ3gotm_ranking.shtml).

Other awards
Domination: Trash
Space race: Dirty Clint (with lots of rivalry)
Diplomatic: falcon512
Histographic: Beard Rinker
