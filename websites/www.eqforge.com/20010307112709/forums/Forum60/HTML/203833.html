As the trumpets fade, all eyes turn skyward and see the sky twisting a hue of crimson. Frightened, people began to scatter as a low moan arises from points unknown.

It seems a blood red cloud has formed over the fortress of Crushbone and a glimpse through a telescope reveals an abundance of strange shapes terrorizing any near.

The Ranger, crumpled from his latest battle, raises his hand to his side and finds his swords have vanished. Painfully reaching around, he notices his bow splintered and rendered useless by some unseen force. He calls upon his magics, yet finds his mind empty of the power needed to evoke the mystic powers required to heal his wounds or harm his foes.

Undaunted, the wiry form rises to his feet to faces this new challenge. Startled, the Ranger whirls to face a sound behind him.

Ahhh, he smiles, it seems that others have heard the call and have come to his side. He sees the forest of Kelethin come alive as Rangers detach themselves from the trees and stumble forward. He painfully gazes upon the countless scars and wounds his comrades bear and cringes as his own body tries to comply with his will.

For ages Rangers have come to the call of many battles and the hundreds of faces of Brothers and Sisters among the now-swelling crowds brings a lump into his throat. He has often heard their shout throughout the woods, "Ranger, looking for a fight!" and yet sadly go unanswered.

Solitary as wolf, yet at home among his kin, the Ranger begins to snarl at the impending forces amassed against them. He motions for the others to follow as he picks up a steady trot towards the gaping maw of the unknown.

Each Ranger knows this will be the fight of their lives, yet they have laid down their lives so many times in the pursuit of adventure that they know their tenacity will be not only seen, but feared!

The Rangers run towards Crushbone, many being cut down by the now swarming legions of Orcs. Hundreds of elves are fighting for their lives and the rest of the populace stops and watches the battle, slowly shaking their heads at the overwhelming odds.

The grass takes on a fiery red as the blood of so many stain its blades. Strange drops of blood start to spatter the land as the clouds burst their seams and spill their essence. The moaning becomes louder as the howling of the orcs strike an eerie chord.

Somewhere in Crushbone lies the answer...

How will this battle turn out? You'll just have to participate to see.


(OOC)

The time has come to face this strange, unseen force crippling Rangers in their prime. The time is now to act! We will storm the Fortress of Crushbone and we will not desist until the land is covered in the blood of our foes, or us.

What: Wrath of the Ranger
Where: The Nameless
Zone: Kelethin to Crushbone
When: Saturday, July 1st
Time: 1PM EST
Levels: Please make NEW Rangers and only equip your newbie sword.
Restrictions: Please do not use the zone-wide channels to communicate. We had a great turnout at the last "sit-in" and it was peaceful. Although we are at war, we do not need to act immature here.

This is an in-game, roleplaying event to dispute the drastic role changes Rangers are forced to endure post level 45. with a serious lack of defensive skills necessary for a melee class, and a declining role in groups at this stage, the life of Rangers everywhere are coming to an end.

If we are going to die, we are going to do it with dignity and honor, not to some political in-fighting, misinformation and disorganization within Verant!

To arms Rangers.. to arms!
