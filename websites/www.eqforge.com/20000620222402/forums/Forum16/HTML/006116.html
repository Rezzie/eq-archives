I just made that choice myself. One thing to consider as well as the AC and the WIS is that the Chitin shield isn't Magic while the CGS is. When you have a shield equipped, your slam skill use it and not your shoulder item to determine if it can bash something magical. So, when equipping the Chitin (or any other non-magical shield) you can't bash creatures immune to non-magical attack.

It really too bad the CGS has such a lousy texture. The Chitin Shield look so much cooler, especially with a spear equipped.

Charred Guardian
