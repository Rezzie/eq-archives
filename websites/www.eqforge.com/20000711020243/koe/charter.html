Members

1. Guild members will not attack another player unless attacked first. Guild members may kill another player only in self defense. The guild does not support Player vs Player combat.

2. Guild members will not cheat other players out of gold, items, or experience. Guild members may sell items and equipment, but they will not provide false information to the buyer. (i.e. selling of empty potions or items that look similar)

3. Guild members will not actively steal kills from other players. While hunting, if a guild member comes across another player the guild member will not actively attack monsters on the other players square unless asked for assistance or the guild member asks for and receives permission first.

4. Guild members will strive to be examples to the player community. If another player asks for assistance due to stun, death, blindness, poison, or death pile recovery, assistance will be rendered, if the guild member feels they are able to do so without suffering death themselves.

5. Guild members will not interrupt another parties hunt or lair battle unless permission to join has been asked and granted or the guild member is asked for assistance.

6. Bounty hunting is a community violation and lynch mobs will not be formed to track down player killers. If a guild member is killed, the guild member will contact the guild leadership and ask for assistance. Revenge killings and lynch mobs give a guild a bad image.

7. Guild members should always strive to keep peace within the community and should offer moderation in a conflict instead of taking sides.

8. Guild members will always evenly distribute gold and treasure earned during a hunt after the hunt or lair battle has been concluded and the party returns to town. Guild members may refuse a share of treasure, but a share will always be offered first. Rare items will be distributed among the guild members on an equal basis. Each member will be given a chance to receive a rare piece of treasure.

9. Each guild member will have a voice within the guild and no decisions that affect the members will be made without a majority vote. A time frame will be determined by the Guild Captain at the time of the vote. If a member does not vote within that time frame the member will forfeit their right to a vote on that issue.
