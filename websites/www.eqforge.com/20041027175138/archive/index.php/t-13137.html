It is the end of the year 1939. For years a crisis has driven Europe -and the rest of the world- in a state of despair. Especially in Germany wich has been struck twice, once by the unfair and unbalanced treaty of Versailles and once by the crisis itself. People are desperate, they are poor, and they are unhappy. Making clever use of this, the NSDAP took power. But their plans were not only to help the people; they had greater ambitions.

In September 1939 Germany invaded Poland, yet the leader of the NSDAP and the head of state Adolf Hitler died quickly thereafter because of a stroke. Rudolf Hess, leader of the dreaded SS, took command and is about to continue the campaign...
