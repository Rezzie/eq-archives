Wooly Fungus Quest

The first quest you should attempt is the wooly fungus quest. Wooly spiderlings can be killed by shaman level 1-4 for experience, so you need to start this quest early in your shaman life.
Realistically, you should initiate this quest immediately after you start your shaman. You recieve a jar to store the fungus in, and you can either use it as your first backpack, or store it in the bank to add to your storage capacity.

To receive this quest, you need to speak to Waltor Felligan, who is the shaman to the left, as you look at the Shaman guild.

Walter will lament that he is too busy to go out and find reagents. Speak to him and follow his cues, until he asks you to gather the fungus.

Once he gives you the jar, make sure that you put it in your lower right hand inventory slot. Wooly fungus is also food, and nothing is worse than spending 3 days gathering fungus, only to lose it as you eat it as food. Always make sure to have food above or to the left of the fungus.

Another good way to get fungus is to buy it off of other players - many do not know it is food, and will sell it cheaply. Two silver per piece is more than adequate. (Though this may be more than your reward)

After you gather enough fungus, (8 pieces) place one piece in each slot of the jar, and hit the combine button.

Return it to Waltor for your reward - some minor items and coppers (He gave me a torch)

You should repeat this quest as often as you can until you reach 5th level and spiders give no experience.
