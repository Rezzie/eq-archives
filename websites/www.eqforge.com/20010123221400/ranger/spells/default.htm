Ranger spells are based off the Druid spell line. Like the Paladin and Shadow Knight, the Ranger doesn't get access to spells until 9th level. Because of the low amount of mana, and the significant disparity between 9th level enemies and 1st level spells, the Rangers don't really become effective with spells until rank three or four.
