You put the .sav file, which is in the zip, into your Civ3 savegame folder which should be something like .../Games/Civ3/Saves. You can also use any other folder but this is the default Civ3 save folder. :p
Then you start Civilization 3, go to Load Game and load the save.
Now you can simply start playing, just like any other Civ3 game, but read the rules first and act accordingly, of course. That means no cheating, reloading or replaying and no reading of the Spoiler thread here without knowing most of the map. :scan:

Try to achieve a high score or finish the game quickly to place high in the ranking but most important of all, HAVE FUN! :yeah:

Note that you need the v1.17f patch (second version) to play this GOTM. The patch can be downloaded at http://www.civ3.com/patches.cfm .

No problem, have fun playing the game. ;)

Concerning your other questions:
1. Yes, you can become head of the UN anytime. It does NOT depend on any wars you waged. But you will need a majority of votes of the other civs. And to get these votes, these civs have to like you more than any other competitor. And waging wars damages your reputation with other civs. Read the manual about reputation and diplomacy to learn how to get those votes. :scan:

2. No.
