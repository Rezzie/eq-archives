The one thing to be careful with the airbase ring is that with cruise missiles and nukes, the AI cheats. AI Missiles don't move through tiles the way human controled ones do. They just suddenly appear in the square next to your city and boom! Thus you must make the airbase in the tiles directly next to your city, if you leave space between the ring and your city you will gain no defence against nukes and CMs.
