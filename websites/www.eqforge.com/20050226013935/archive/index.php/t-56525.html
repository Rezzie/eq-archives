I've been checking what needs to be built/rush bought to achieve the following goals:

(1) Three trade routes, marketplaces, temples in all of Coresux' cities + harbors in those cities that can build them. Ditto for one city in Woodstock, Jayneland and Barbaria. Exception: No trade routes in BBF (what happens if you send a caravan to a barbarian city ??)

(2) Banks and sewers in at least 2 cities and possibly as many as 7 cities in Coresux.

(3) Stock exchange in Dou.

(4) A few crusaders to strengthen our military in Coresux and Woodstock.

Once this is achieved we can achieve the next goal:

(5) Get all of Coresux' cities to size 12 or more the next time we celebrate, as well as one city in Woodstock, Jayneland and Barbaria.

There is also one additional goal:

(6) Build three wonders, AS, Bach and Leo in this priority order. The reason AS is #1 is that some AI civ already has economics. Bach must be built before we celebrate, otherwise our triremes must stay in a city while we celebrate which is a bad idea.

And once this has been done invade Greece by first building a few barracks and then 6-10 more crusaders/dragoons.

Goals 1-4 require the following to be built (most of it rush bought in fact):

4 crusaders
16 caravans (4 food caravans since they are already being built, the other 12 will be delivered in Dou)
6 temples
3 harbors
9 marketplaces
10 aqueducts
2-7 banks
2-7 sewers
1 stock exchange
3 wonders

I haven't worked out roughly how many turns this is going to take but probably not much more than 10. The wonders are hardest and things are further complicated by the fact there are some interesting techs ahead.
