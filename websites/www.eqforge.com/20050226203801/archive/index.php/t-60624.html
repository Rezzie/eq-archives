Jayneland Status Report 1752 AD

:rocket2: Lahore delenda est !! :rocket:

The Ministry of Domestic Affairs views the presence of the city of Lahore in our lands as a declaration of war by the Indians and demands the immediate destruction of this city of infidels. We should get the Indians to declare war and then our holy troops should burn this city and raze it.

Fort Jayne: Bank, SE, SM, CH. Then stockpile caravans. This city should be able to reach size 20+ when we celebrate next.
Trollhättan: Finish MP, caravan (send to Dou), aqueduct. Need more roads ASAP.
Nemoville: Engineer is premature, want the city to be able to celebrate. Build temple, marketpalce, aqueduct.
Suggestions: Temple, marketplace, aqueduct. Sell barracks ?
Mozzarella: Harbor, temple. Irrigate ASAP SW and W of the city so it can grow.
Duke's Creek: Engineer (IRB now !!), temple. Build railroad to Morpheus ASAP. Then found city.
Trinity: AT not needed, switch to engineer. Then harbor (IRB), temple.
Morpheus: IRB diplomat and bribe the ironclad. See if it can raze Lahore. Otherwise use the alpine troops to capture it. Following this, build harbor, temple in Morpheus and possibly coastal fortress. If the Indians cannot be made to declare war build coastal fortress and probably city walls since the Indians will sooner or later sneak attack.

All cities should be connected with railroad ASAP, especially Morpheus which is vulnerable. Irrigate and build roads around size 3+ cities.

Comments and criticism wanted !
