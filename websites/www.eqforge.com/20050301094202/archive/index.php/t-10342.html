The AI tries to win in any possible way, so if you remove a particular victory condition (Spaceship, like I did) it will not build spaceship components. Also, the AI will directly try to stop other civs from winning. In one game, the French built the UN. The English, who were at war with everyone, and obviously feared a diplomatic victory if an election was held, immediately declared war, seized Paris and held it. Needless to say, an election never took place. I was impressed.
