This thread will serve as a registry for new citizen groups as well as an index of all registered groups. Register a new group by posting in this thread. The information posted should include the name of the group, a link to the group's thread, and a short description of the group.

This thread is not for discussions.

INDEX

Demogame Mac user group (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51564) - A group for anyone who has something to say about the Mac.

Brotherhood of Steel (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51736) - Militarists from all over the empire can gather to discuss war, peace, the military and how it affects our civilization. we are a group of devoted warmongers who wish the government to maintain a strong army at all times.

The Children of Peace (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51776) - A group to oppose the warmongers. Peace is our goal.

The Will To Power (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51955) - A group founded on the principle that no act, however repugnant, should be ruled out if its use might benefit our nation.

The Conservative Association (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51958) - A citizen group dedicated to preserving a minimalist ruleset, protecting the power of the people and encouraging a cooperative demogame environment.

The Polling Standards Commission (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=52260) - A group committed to the creation & maintenance of polling guidelines, and encouraging high quality polling from officials and citizens alike.

The Tech Freaks (http://forums.civfanatics.com/showthread.php?s=&threadid=51867) - A citizen groups for all people who would like to see that sciense should be promoted this demogame. We want to ensure that the government doesnt try to be cheap towards technology. We want to get the upperhand of our opponents by technology instead of war.
