goods: 250@ (from timber or heavy timber)
garbage: 0@ (from industry makeing goods)
plastic: 0@ (from weird liquid)

@ is amount of money gained by the player that produces the goods

accumulated resources works like this: with 5000* we can build a black smith/factory/forge and weird heavy metals, with 2500* and robotics we build a manufactureing plant. you can work at a factory in addition to your normal work (you must post a short storie every day you work in a factory). the goods are sold to the goverment, selling gives me more money. once you start making goods you must wright a story for evrey day the goods take to complete. idustry chart:

factory: base days to complete goods: 10

manufactureing plant: base days to complete goods when linked to factory: 7

manufactureing plant part 2: base days to complete goods when linked to both factory and super industry: 5

work camp (comunisim) super industry same affect as manufactureing plant, see manufactureing plant part 2 for details.

slave camp (despotism) same as work camp.

paid labour (republic) same as work camp.

free labour (democracy) same as work camp.

royal servants camp (monarchy) same as work camp.

super industrys cost 10,000* and are replaced by the super industry of the new form of goverment after a revoultion, and are temporaily deactived in anarchy

things with the *weird* prefix are future resorces

you must submit 7 short stories about your day with a list what you mined that day, you can only submit 1 report a day. every day i add up the value of the companie as a whole (all resorces ever mined).
