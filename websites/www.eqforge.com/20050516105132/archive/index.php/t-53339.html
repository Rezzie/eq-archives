Antonius and Aurelia are walking, and on the side of the road they see a baby.

Aurelia: Oh my! A Baby! She runs over and picks up the baby. Antonius is holdin Antony.

Aurelia: Someone abandoned it! We must keep it and care for him. Please?

Antonius: Oh alright. But mind you we need to prepare for that suspected attack.

Aurelia: Don't worry so much. If we stay near the palace the guard will protect us.

Antonius: So what are we going to name him?

Aurelia: Achilles, for it means unknown.

So Antonius and Aurelia adopt Achilles, a newborn baby abandoned on the side of the road.
