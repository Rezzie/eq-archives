posted 04-17-2000 06:11            
Extra regenerative ability is still a huge advantage. Why do you think Verant gave Trolls the huge xp penalty and a massive fire resistance penalty to boot?

Hint: It wasn't because of their good looks.

2 hp per tick is still 2 hp per tick. This helps Trolls easily and quickly smash their way to level 20, despite the penalty. At higher levels, look at it this way:

Regeneration at 24 (5 hp/tick) is made 40% more effective by Troll regen (+2 hp/tick).

Chloroplast at 39 is made 20% more effective.

Any kind of edge in regeneration is advantageous with Cannibalize. If this ability is so negligible "at higher levels", why are Rubicite Breastplates worth so much, even before they quit dropping?

And which would YOU pick? Rubicite BP or Totemic Breastplate? Pretty much a no-brainer, if you ask me.

Bottom line is, though, play whatever race you like. Each has significant advantages and disadvantages. Personally, I like having a regeneration advantage, and +2 hp per tick is a BIG advantage in my and many others' opinions.
