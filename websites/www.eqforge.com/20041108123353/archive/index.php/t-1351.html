Civ uses only its music from the CD, so unfortunately you can´t change it. You can use own Wave-Sounds, so you can convert short songs into .wav files, but be in mind that this may not be much useful, because these .wav files can be very large (=long up/downloads when spreading the scn, also much RAM needed) and they are only played once ordered via events, so they can´t serve as background music (they are fine for sound FX http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/wink.gif)
