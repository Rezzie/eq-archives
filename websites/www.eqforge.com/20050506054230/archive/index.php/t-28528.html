skaternate: if you've left Regent difficulty behind for keeps in terms of your skill level, and you would only be snoring through and milking this month's GOTM to boost your "global" ranking, there is an alternative. There's a steady diet of challenging scenerios offered at Realms Beyond (http://realmsbeyond.net/civ). There are about three Epics played per month, on average. The next game opens Monday August 5, a Deity conquest scenerio, which might be more entertaining for a player of your experience level.

Regent games are great for including more players, but some folks have definitely graduated beyond that stage, through more experience. I enjoyed playing GOTM7 but don't intend to play here every month. I pretty much stick to Deity games and/or "variants". The GOTM offering a Regent game is probably a good thing, as that will be fun for a lot of folks. For those with time for more than one game per month, or in search of a steady supply of rigorous scenerios, there is an alternative.

Best wishes to all GOTM10 participants.
