New Apple history date to remember: April 28th, 2003

At the special event organaised today by apple, the corporation launched it's very own online music service, new iPods, iTunes 4 and Quicktime 6.2.

The new music service is, basically, a server with 200 000 tunes online. You can download each tune for 0.99$ [99¢].

The iPos was granted it's first major update today. The form factor of the iPod has been changed. While still having the same scroll-ring, the buttons are now place in a row above it. The buttons are also backlit. The iPod is availible in 10, 15 and 30 GB formats.
