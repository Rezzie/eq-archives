Just copied the post I made on another board to put some info out for you all.


Well just a small tid bit on this zone.

Today we went in with 6 players and basically decided that we would kill anything that moved. This zoneis located near The Overthere. If you zone in from The Overthere then hang a right on the zone wall and it is only about a minute run from the zone. It is guarded by about 6 goblins in front.

We knew the stuff up front would be green to use b/c everyone in the group was L51. When zoning in you are met by a 1-2 goblins that hang out at the zone. After this you take a short right and you fall down into a pit. Here you are met by 3 globlins. Once you have disposed of those them then you can start
making your way down the tunnel. You head down for a long way. This zone is basically in a U shape pattern and there is no exit unless you reach the very end and make it back to where you started from. Once down a ways you will enter a large cavern that has alot of tunnel leading off from it. Most of these lead to a larger dead in cavern at the end. However one tunnel leads to the mining colony and the graphics are pretty nice. Here you will find alot of goblins to kill in the mini-houses (Sort of looks like hate just less houses and they are brown). Here you can go off in several directions or go into the large house and go up the ramp which will take you to another part of the dungeon. Basically I would say the mining colony is the place to hunt. We did not find anything special in our group but Kerkoc and his group found a SK dagger that was 9/27 +4 str which I guess is not to bad. (We got a whip at the very end that was not to impressive but I guess it was another weapon that is out there). Once you are done here you start
heading up along the "rail road" tracks and it eventually will take you back to the zone. THere is a switch on top of the first hill but we did not figure out what is was used for.

Over all the mobs in there I would say are probably on the scale of a greater koblold in Sol B. Most I was ever hit for was in the high 70's think it was 77 points. However many of the goblins in the zone are casters and can cause a good amt of dmg if you get to many of them.

So I would say if you are in the 40-45(7) this might be another alternative than Sol B.

Things to remember: Keep nullify or cancel memed at all times. Someone should be on this duty b/c justabout everything we fought had a dmg shield of some sort. If you want to resist then Magic Fire and Cold are what you need.

Pet casters keep an eye on your pets b/c they will warp on you in several places. Lost my pet at the end of our journey through the dungeon, I have no idea where he went off to but he was fighting so I inviso'ed to kill him off.

I am sure there are other named mobs in there with some stuff so I guess just nothing was up when we made our way through, we just kept moving for the most part to see the sights.
