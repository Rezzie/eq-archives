I was wrong.

Freely admitted.

The suggestions I made and ideas I had won't work. Mind you, I'm not really qualified to say that as I haven't tried them post 50 , but there it is. Even some of the assumptions I made were wrong.

I was also right.

I told you that even if there was a problem, Verant would not fix it until the hubbub died down for a few months, but you wouldn't listen. Abashi has now stated exactly that. In fact, it is my opinion that what he said effectively admits there is a problem. ("I will be completely honest though, even if we did find a glaring issue with the class at this point, we'd be hesitant to change it, as many people would think that we were doing it just to throw them a bone, thus encouraging the wide-spread complaints that we are seeing now.") From what I've read on other boards, there are "glaring issues" with just about every class, not just rangers.

Fortunately, some of you have taken the spirit of my arguements for what they were and have run with it. There are several good threads going now on possible alternatives, and they are even being supported by people who continually shot my ideas down, dispite some of those ideas being the same.

I have continually defended Verant against baseless (and sometimes not-so-baseless) accusations. In this instance, it's just plain wrong, both from a PR perspective and a business perspective, for this kind of attitude from Gordon. I grant you that a significant portion of the player base are teenagers and a lot of the posts at the Sony boards are immature to say the least, but that is no excuse for a grade-school playground attitude in response.

For what it's worth, I never believed that the majority of what I've been suggesting re:alternative strategies would actually work. Posting them and defending them is very similar to a very effective problem-solving tool I've used professionally for over 10 years now, although the effectiveness isn't as great on a discussion board as it is in personal conversation. The bottom line is that prior to my original posting, there was nothing but rants and attacks on Verant being posted, and the prevailing attitude was one of "our problem, they have to fix it". Now there are people trying to come up with intelligent alternatives.

It was not my intention to pull some of the heat off of Verant onto myself, nor do I get paid for doing so. Nor do I believe that my advice and experience regarding rangers and EQ in general is automatically baseless and false just because of this one issue as some of you now seem to think. I am retiring from The Rangers Glade, at least for now. I am always available in-game or via E-Mail to answer questions, offer advice, etc. should anyone care to contact me.
