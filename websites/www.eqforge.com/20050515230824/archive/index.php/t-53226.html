I'm going to appoint one (or more) citizens to a new official position. This position will be primarily tasked with welcoming people to the game and helping them to get into the swing of it. None of the duties are particularly onerous or difficult but if done well this position will do a lot towards getting people into the game quickly and painlessly as well as retaining those folk who drop in and then fade away.

Duties:
When a new citizen registers, send a PM or email with a welcome message including links to the Welcome Center, current save game and rule set. Anything else that would help but not overload (frighten away ;) ) the new citizen is also encouraged and up to the official. This can be a standard write up that is sent to each new citizen, with a current link for the save game.
Help new citizens to get into government. Find out what aspect (department, judicial, governor) they are most interested in and serve as a liason to get them involved in that office.
Serve as a help desk source. In the welcome PM make sure that the new citizen knows he/she can PM you for advice and help with the game.
Follow up when new citizens fade away. Keep a hot list of the most recent citizens and check once in a while to see if they are still active in the game. If not, send them a friendly PM encouraging them to come back or at least determine their reason for leaving.

The person appointed to this position will need to have a good familiarity with the rules, the layout of our forums and a good grasp on the game as a whole, including keeping up with current events.

Please let me know if you are interested! :D
