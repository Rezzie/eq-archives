Exactly. RR= +1 food if through irrigation and +1 shield if through a mine.

Therefore every forest that is on a terrain type OTHER than tundra should be cleared, then irrigated or mined, and rail roaded. Tundra however is the same mined and railroaded as it is with a forest (1/2/x) with or without a railroad, so it's not worth the effort to clear it unless you want the one time 10 shields.
