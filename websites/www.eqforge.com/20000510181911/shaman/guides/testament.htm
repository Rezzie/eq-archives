Gothakmithan's Testament Of Vanear Guide

This is a simple two part quest for a great +10 wisdom item : 

1. Seek out Moodoro Finharn, who is at Crows in North Qeynos. Ask him about The Testament of
Vanear. Give him 2 gold and you will receive a book which has to inventory slots and a combine
button in it. Then, give him 4 gold until you get the Jester Card from him. [Aside : Keep the Knights
and give them to the dwarf on Erollisi Island for a reward of some kind (forget what it is but it isn't
relevant to the quest). Sell all the other cards. ] Give the Jester Card back to him and he will give you page 34 of the Testament. You now need page 30. 

2. You need to go to Highpass now. I will assume you are coming from East Karana, head toward the Kithikor zone. You will see water on your right with a bridge spanning it. Swim under the bridge and go underwater. You will see an underwater tunnel on your right. Swim through there and follow the right wall until you reach another underwater passage. Swim through this short passage [note: being a barbarian I had to duck a few times while swimming to get through this tiny hole] and you will come to dry land on the other side. Run up and into the big open area. At the back of the big open area on the right you will see a forge where the NPC with page 30 spawns, Dyllin Starsine [note: he is wearing a Highhold Pass guard's uniform], a -20 mob. He has a 7 1/2 hour spawn cycle (learned by camping him). Killing him is easy but the smugglers, who were green and light blue to me at 24, will aid him if they are close by. I just rooted him and used direct damage to put him down quickly. Get page 30 from his corpse. Place page 30 (from Dyllin) and page 34 (which you won betting with the quest giver Moodoro) in the Testament of Vanear container and hit combine. You now have a Testament of Vanear. 

That is the nitty gritty on how to get it. There are some other things Moodoro says and a couple of
steps I left out because they do not affect the quest. 

Gothakmithan 
28th Shaman, The Tribunal

- Editor's note:  Moodoro is most often found very close to Lt. Dagarok, a near 50, ill-tempered SK whose Harm Touch alone will insta-kill any shaman below 24th level.  Know your exits   -  Thantu
