This group is founded on a single principle: that no tactic, strategy or course of action (excepting only genuine exploits) should be ruled out if it could potentially further the the cause of the ascendency of our nation.

We support use of pop-rushing, city razing, extortion, treaty breaking, RoP rape and all other such maligned practices wherever these will prove genuinely advantageous. This does not mean that we are actively seeking opportunities to put all these into practice, but simply that we will not balk at doing so where the potential for gain outweighs the negative repercussions.
