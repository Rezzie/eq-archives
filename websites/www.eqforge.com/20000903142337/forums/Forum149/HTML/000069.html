Novalight,

I am about to turn 50 tonight and have managed to solo something every level since lvl 1. Granted post 45 soloing is not very profitable and the down time is long but it can be done. Mgeryle, is correct regarding the RF that is a mid 30's weapon at best, however I would suggest that for solo work you should DW yeks. The argo of the proc is irrelevant if your solo and the extra 72 to 300 plus damage that the procs make are significant. Mgeryle, also said to get COF, minor problem that is a lvl 50 spell. I don't know the specifics of your armor, but what ever you do max AC don't keep anything for any other stat. Get a group to hunt the giants in the castle in Dreadlands and get a nice Treeweave tunic for 25 AC. Oh wait, of course you have to have haste sash for solo, just keep a good AC belt for those times when you are grouped and need the AC.

48 is probably one of the most difficult levels for a ranger, at 49 you will get new damage shields and coats that help as well as wolf form and immolate but still you can find mobs at 48. That is the key, find the right mob. Ash hornets are great in BW because they are summoned and let us use our best DD. I also soloed a few spiders outside Kanors at 48. Once dispelled and appropriately arrow kited the spiders will go down. There may also be some blues left in Frontier Mountains that can be rooted and shot at the BW zone, although that was best pre 47 as I remember. At 49 I have found Emerald Jungle at the TT zone to be fairly good although the down time can be long. I have had success with Gorillas and Soulsuckers as well as the occasional Tatterback. Being right up against the zone to fight lets you press that last bub of health hoping for that one last proc, yuck.

Good Luck,
