You cannot build the Military Academy until AFTER you have researched the "Military Tradition" tech that also enables Cavalry. The victorious army is just one of the prerequisites for the MA.


If pressed to switch your accumulated wonder shields to something before losing them, then analyse your choices:

If there is another Great Wonder that you could use, then switch your shields there before building small wonders like the Heroic Epic. Small wonders are always available to you but you are in a "get there first" race for Great Wonders with all the other CIVs.
F7 can reveal the other wonders being built by known civs.

If you have no other Great Wonder choices, and these shields are in your capital, then perhaps your best option would be to switch the Heroic epic shields to apply to something else (or a temporary copy of your palace) and then convert the shields in your capital to apply to the HE once it is freed up by the first move.
