Now to the Jade!

The Jade chokidai Prod is a 1H piercing weapon with Dmg11 dly30 Sta+3 Wis+6 (awesome for a shaman)

Go to Lake of Ill Omen in Kunark, then go to the Sarnak castle, can't miss it, once there you must Gather a full group around lvl40 to get to the throne room, because sarnaks Train like hell and you have to clear the way for alot of greenys!
once in the throne room you'll see the throne where Emperor 'Di Zok spawns ( he will spawn every 1 hour) he's a lvl 40 mob with some nasty AOE spells, so better have some protection buffs on you! when he is killed he drops the Jade chokidai prod!
and remember that the way out is as tough as the way in! =) Good Luck, and have fun! i had!
