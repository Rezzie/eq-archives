It's the time of the month for a new Civ3 GOTM! We are very pleased with the response to our first Civ3 GOTM -- Matrix told me he has received more than 100 submissions already! Due to the huge number of submissions and that this is the first time we need to work on results for BOTH Civ2 and Civ3 GOTMs, the results will be posted a bit
later than before.

<img border="0" src="http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/gotm_images/civ3gotm02.jpg" align="right" width="325" height="165">For Civ3 GOTM #2, we will have a Monarch level game on a large size map.

Here are the detailed <b> Game Parameters:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Civilization: Greeks</li>
<li>Leader: Alexander</li>
<li>Difficulty Level: Monarch</li>
<li>Land Form: Pagaea_2</li>
<li>Number of Civs: 10</li>
<li>Map Size: Large</li>
<li>Climate: Wet</li>
<li>Temperature: Warm</li>
<li>Earth: 5 Billion Years</li>
<li>Barbarians: Raging</li>
<li>Rivals: 9 Random Rivals</li>
<li>Victory Conditions: Domination victory is disabled.</li>
</ul>

<b>Download Links:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/games/GOTM2_Civ3.zip">ZIP format (55 KB)</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/games/GOTM2_Civ3.SAV">SAV format (1.73 MB)</a></li>
</ul>

<b>Playing the Game:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You must use the DEFAULT RULES to play the game.</li>
<li>Use of any cheats and editing of saved games are not allowed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Submissions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Saved games are due <i> January 2nd, 2002</i>.</li>
<li>Be sure to save at 0 AD and at the end of your game (We no longer require you to submit the 1000 AD saved game).</li>
<li>IMPORTANT: When submitting the game, please include all the info asked in the
<a href="http://www.civfanatics.com/civ3gotm/civ3gotm_submit.shtml">submission page</a>. Submissions with incomplete info will not be added.</li>
</ul>

Have fun playing GOTM! Don't forget to discuss your progress with your fellow GOTMers in the
<a href="http://forums.civfanatics.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=49"> Civ3 GOTM forum</a>...
