I was thinking about a Citizen of the Term award. This would go to a very involved citizen and active. This could encourage New-comers to increase activity.

Nominations would start at the End of a term, and self-nominations are not allowed. The nominations would last as long as election nominations.

After Nominations, citizens vote for their choice ofor the Citizen Award. Elections would last as long as leader elections. In the event of a tie, the players who tied will be recognized as Citizen of the Term.

For Citizen of the Game Award, a week before the last turnchat, Nominations will start. No Self-nominations are allowed, and maybe nominations should be given by the leaders/governors and deputies. The process would run like Citizen of the Term, except if there is a tie, the people who tied are recognized as The Citizens of the Game.


What do you think about this?
