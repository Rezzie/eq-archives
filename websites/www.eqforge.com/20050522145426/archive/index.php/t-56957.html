This is the Character registry. Here is where you state your characters. You must include:
Name
Gender
Relation to Any Other Characters

Also, you must include their skill level in the following areas:
Intelligence: How quickly a character can learn new things. Their ability to solve things. If a character were to learn a new skill, it would take them different amounts of time based on their logic amount.
Chartered Accountancy: A characters skills with money. A character who has high skills in Chartered Accountancy looses less to corruption in his/her business. People with a Chartered Accountancy rating of 6+ gain 1 gold per caste.
Charisma: How well a character presents him/herself. A character with a high charisma rating can command more troops then one with a lower rating. The exact equation is(C^3*I^2*W*A)/30,000=Troops to Command, where C is Charisma, I is Intelligence, W is Work Ethic and A is an age factor, with Middle Ages giving a 1.25 bonus, Military Tradition giving a 1.5 bonus, Radio a 1.75 bonus and Computers a 2.5 bonus. (You WILL need a calculator)
Work Ethic: A character with a high Work Ethic is able to complete tasks faster. The higher the work ethic, the less time a job takes, with each point being -5%.

These are the MAIN SKILLS. They dictate how your other skills go. The Conflict Skills are determined by the ammount of skills you have here. You are given 32 points to distribute among these 4 skills. Use them wisely, because they CANNOT be changed.
