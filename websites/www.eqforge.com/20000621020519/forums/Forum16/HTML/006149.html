Hey Tryg,

I think I saw you in Karnors Castle a day or so ago. There are a couple of pieces there that you might want to try and get for upgrades.

Jarsath Scale Legs are just fantastic, AC 15 +5 Int/Wis

Jarsath Scale Tunic AC 20 +5 Dex +10 HP

The Prayer Shawl is much better than the Lizard Skin Mantle, tough to get. Not just the difficulty of the mermaid it drops from but the farmers are there all the time.

Theres a planar belt that isn't no drop, Flayed Turmoil Skin, nice AC, Wis and Dex, I usually see a lot of them at the bazzaar.

I prefer the Golden Efreeti Boots for the Feet. SolB has really cleared out since the expansion and the Efreeti is left alone quite often.
