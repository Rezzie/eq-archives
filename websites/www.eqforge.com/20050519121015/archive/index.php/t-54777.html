IT SO HAPPENED on one day that Octavian the Tenth, a peasent known for his wisedom, went to sleep. During his slumber, he had a dream in which he saw a man die. This certain man was placed in a box during a ceremony, and was buried. He then saw the man's body go to an abundant land where milk and honey ran, where the grass was green, and with fat cattle.

The next day when he awoke from this slumber, a local nobleman loved by the local populace of his village outside Noshuret died. Octavian noticed that the man looked like the person from his dream. As the peasents mourned for the loss of the noble, Octavian spoke.

"My friends. There is no need to mourn this man. I had glourious vision last night. If we have a ceremony to celebrate the life of this man, and bury him approiately afterward, he will enjoy a life after this one, in a great abundany land."

The villagers said, "Come, let us do as this man says! He is a wise man, and surely his words are true." They held a ceremony, remembered his life, and buried him in a field outside the village.

Then, as others of the village died, they were given the same ceremonial burial as the noble.

Octavian, seeing how comforted people were after the deaths of others, left the village to carry on this message to other Fanaticans.

"In the beginning, there was void in the universe. Nothing existed except the power that moves all things…
Into this nothingness came Alpha. He was alone but not alone. He was with the other. Her name was Terra. She was his wife and he was her husband. Alpha was the first one, and Terra was the second made from Alpha but now apart.

Alpha and Terra were together for a time beyond our ken. In their love and devotion to each other, they had a son. He became known as Pyra, for he was a power of fire. Aqua was next to be born, and finally Aria, a beautiful daughter. From one, there now were five. Alpha crafted a wondrous place for the Spirits to live. It was light were there was darkness. It was substance where there was oblivion. It was the Place of the Spirits.

Alpha looked out into the void of the universe and knew that it could not continue thusly. He raised a hand, and the sun, the stars and the planets were born. Such was Alpha’s power, with one gesture, he created the universe out of nothing. The universe was full of many different worlds, one such world was a perfect globe, another was a flat square, one was circled by a giant snake that held its own tail in its mouth, and one was a disc, held atop four giant elephants, who in turn were on the back of a even more gigantic cosmic turtle. Alpha was nothing if not inventive.

But the universe was chaotic, without order. The act of creation had left Alpha tired and in need of rest, so he sent his wife and child down to the world to create order from the chaos. The winds howled, the seas churned and the earth quaked. Terra looked at the chaos of the world below and wept with sorrow at the task ahead. We cannot allow this pandemonium to continue,'' she said to her children, ''Come, we will remake the world.''

Aqua went to the sea and claimed the waters as his responsibility. Aria rose to the sky and calmed the howling winds with a gesture. Pyra alone stood without dominion.
