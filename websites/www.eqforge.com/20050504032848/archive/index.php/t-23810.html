Difficulty: Monarch

Game: All victories; Restarting players off
Opponents: 6 Rivals

JohnnyReb
Jun 03, 2002, 02:03 AM
It means civilizations of similiar cultures will tend to start near you. For example if you play the Japanese and enable the cultural starting positions then there is a good chance the Chinese, Indians, and..er..I forget other asian ones at the moment...will start near you.

You can view the civs in the editor to see what culture they are a part of.

Actually, I think the culturally linked starting has always been around. The patch just gave the option to disable it (though I could be wrong here).
