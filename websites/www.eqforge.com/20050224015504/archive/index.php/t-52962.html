I'm splitting the poll into two parts since 'only' 20 options are possible.

I strongly recommend a marketplace in Dou ahead of other improvements. It increases trade more than a courthouse since corruption isn't huge. A library only affects science whereas a marketplace affects both money and luxuries and makes it easier to celebrate. I also recommend to IPRB the MP 4 turns from now. The reason is that roughly 10 turns from now we may have Astronomy which means starting Copernicus. At that time I'd like MP to be finished and preferrably CH too which would mean to IPRB CH the moment we discover Astronomy or shortly thereafter.

Option C refers to Funxus' plan from the 100 BC thread for enabling Neumatic to grow by rearranging terrain utilization in that area:

"It needs 12 more shields. Either we switch to fish (or other) directly to finish it in 4 turns, or we play one more turn with 5s, next turn switch to rivered plain, and then to fish in two turns to finish it in 3 turns.
I think it should have priority for the fish, meaning we'd have to rearrange the serfs in that area."

This is a good plan IMO.

I think we should start irrigating near Neu once the city square is irrigated. If you disagree please specify what to do. Ditto for Dou.
