The tech calculator says that with 8 civs in the game and 5 know Poly, on Monarch and standard map size, the value is 205g. If they would give us GPT it would be a no-brainer, but nobody is ready to offer that yet. With general apathy in the forum threads and only 9 total votes for the research poll, the will of the people is open to interpretation. We can

let it slide, not enough is on the table to make it worthwhile, and without clear positive indication that the trade is wanted we shouldn't do it
take the trade, gold in our hands is better than in theirs, and there is not enough clamor against it to conclude that the people don't want it
use a spot poll, which at least has the formality of getting the voice of the people who are able to / bother to attend the chat

There are arguments for and against any of the above and I have no prior DemoGame experience, so will leave it open for input from Trade and for the President or DP to handle interpreting the "will of the people". :rolleyes:
