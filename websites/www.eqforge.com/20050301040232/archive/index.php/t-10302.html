You didn't mention the size of the map. If you have half of a tiny world, don't expect your area to be large (actually, it would be half-tiny).

The total population is the sum of all your cities. If you count all cities and the population is different than 45 million, then it's really a bug. If not, then admit, you have a small country.

The familiy size isn't an accurate statistic, it's just proportional to the growth of your cities. If it's 2, it means your cities are growing at an average rate (guess what, not a bug too).

Just be sure you're dealing with a bug (not something you just don't like) before saying "Damn another bug !"
