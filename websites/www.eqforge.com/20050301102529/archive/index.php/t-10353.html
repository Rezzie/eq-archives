Build the forbiddon palace. In my experience it doesn't really matter where you build it. Build it in a place you'll have it quickly.

The manual says it reduces corruption in nearby cities but in my experience it just seems to raise the number of cities you can have before corruption gets bad.

Distance does seems to play a minor role but maybe it's just my play style. In my games I take out one maybe two civs at most and this keeps corruption at a managable level. It is more than enough to sin the game (unless you like to maim and kill everything in sight).
