1. I remember having two leaders at the same time. I do remember it because it was the only time I got leaders at all (not a big surprise, being as peaceful as I am)...

2. Nothing - the prerequisite is to have the two resources at the time you begin to build the Iron Works. It's the same as with disappearing strategic resources - you always get to finish the troops/improvements you started to produce before the resource disappeared. But if you are afraid that a resource will disappear before you start building the Iron Works, don't connect to that resource until you start building...
