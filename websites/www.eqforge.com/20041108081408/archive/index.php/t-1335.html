I also made an Assyrian and Hittite tribe for my modpack (along with Phoenicians, Ethiopians and many others). The way I did it was to edit the rules.txt and city.txt with notepad. This gives you more control I feel. Also, by editing city.txt you can control the names of cities founded in the game, so you won't have Washington appearing in the desert! http://forums.civfanatics.com/ubb/wink.gif

Another thing to note is that you can only have 1 tribe of each color at the same time, so Hittites and Assyrians must have different colors. I had to change some of the colors for my modpack, or else the Romans and the Celts would never be able to fight, as they are both white!

Can you send Units the way you want them to go?

There is a command to do this in the events. However, it is quite limited and only units not doing anything at that moment will respnd.

And as player or AI you can't change it's order/direction?

It works only for AI and you can't change it during the game.

By the Way; how Do I can change they turns
not to pass a year, but a week or month?)

You can adjust the turn interval to whatever you want and also the starting year - in cheat/scenario parameters.
