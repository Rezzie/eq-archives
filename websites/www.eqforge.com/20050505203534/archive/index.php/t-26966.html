Please read this info before answering the Poll.

Read all the choices carefully before selecting the option that best fits your game. BE PATIENT, most of the choices above will not apply to any typical game.

For the purposes of the question, we will sort of assume that you somehow made direct contact with Japan in the July 02 GOTM9 because of their close proximity to our start position acroos a narrow coastal body of water. Beyond that first contact with the japanese there are a number of interesting options.

THE KEY QUESTION HERE IS: Who did you make contact with next after Japan?

Just like last month's contact poll, you need to interpret where you were in the game and what you were doing in order to answer the poll correctly:

If you were moving a unit across a body of water separated from your towns and cities when you made contact, then you discovered the other guy.

If the other civ was moving a unit on land or sea and the contact occurred near your towns or cities, then they discovered you.

In particular there are three choices (marked with *) in the poll that relate to making contact between eqypt and the other civs by using a landmass that was sort of centrally located neutral ground. Depending on how your expansionist strategy played out, this landmass could have belonged to many different people.
