Our primary research goal is set (Monarchy), and Writing is winning out (at the moment) as our alternate tech. I got the Research Planner utility working, and it has provided some new information we need to consider and decide about. We are currently researching Ceremonial Burial. Our next list of choices will be: code of Laws, Horseback Riding, Map Making, and Writing. Even if we research Code of Laws next, Monarchy will NOT be available the following round, no matter what we research. That means we either take our alternate tech next round, or we research Code of Laws next round and take our alternate the round after that. As Code of Laws doesn't really give us anything useful in the game, I would suggest going after our alternate tech next round, Code of Laws after that, then Monarchy. Vote and discuss (or maybe the other way around :crazyeye: )
