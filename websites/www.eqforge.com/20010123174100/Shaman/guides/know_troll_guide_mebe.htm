Levels 1-4

Remember, dis is EverQUEST, ya shuld look around in yur Troll's Shaman guild fer a real gud first quest. (HINT: BEFORE you buy any spells)

Ya also needta git ready fer a higher level spell dat needs snake scales as a component, so stash em all in da bank. Yull dank Mebe later! If ya come across any Fish Scales or Bat Wings, save dem too.

Snake eggs can be used as food, so keep em instead of buying food from Vendor.

Try ta use yur mana wisely. Yer a TROLL so ya regenerate health perty quickly, try ta make it so ya get back ta full mana and full health at da same time. Don't be afraid ta barsh dings, it fun too! Har Har Har

When ya can take on skellys, luk fer ones wit weapons. Dis a gud way ta get a better barshin tool dan da one ya start out wit.

Use yur majik ta help yur Troll brothers n sisters. Dey like Inner Fire, and course dey like ta be healed too. If yur not stingy, yull make lotsa friends. Friends come in handy now, and as ya grow in experience!

Stay perty close to da Guards, dieing at dese levels not dat bad, but ya hafta rememorize all yur spells. Mebe rather barsh dan sit around memming.

Levels 5-8

Nuttin ta add dat hasn't already been said on yur site. Except dat at level 8, it seems like ferever before ya get level 9 and Spirit of Wolf! Tho, meditate is very welcome at 8.

Levels 9-13

Now ya got Spirit of Wolf(SoW)! Mebe started ta travel more now. See da sites, meet new tings and Barsh dem. Har Har Har ALWAYS keep SoW on ya and everyone in yur group, ALWAYS! Save yur life and ders many times. BUT, be careful not ta train tings into areas others rest in, dey really don't like dat. Go away from newbie areas when ya zone and around people ya meet. Chances are, if ya have long way ta run, yull lose da critters attacking ya before ya zone. Zone anyway, dey remember ya otherwise and ya don't want em attacking ya while yur busy wit someting else. Oh ya, don't wait too long before running, if yur health get too low, yull be slow enough fer dem ta catch ya.

Two tings ya can do here, ya can sell SoW fer a fixed price to passerbys, OR ya can just ask fer donations. Mebe always SoW'ed people recovering bodies fer free, no donation asked fer. Mebe get better results wit asking fer a gift after Mebe cast it. Anyting from a snake fang (1 copper) ta over 2 platinum! Plus ya get interesting tings too (rings[majik and other], earrings, weapons, etc) and lotsa friends!

Tainted breath is GUD! Mebe like it alot. Cast it first ta lure it in, and den barsh away while throwing in a Frost Rift evrynow and den depending on how yur health is doing. If yur in a group, might wanta wait abit before casting it. Dose creatures really don't like dat spell and ya might find yurself in da center of too much attention. Experiment and be carefull.

Its slow going when ya get ta level 11. Hang in der tho, level 14 is great!

Mebe, 14th level Troll Shaman
