Deepwater Harpoon:

 

4.3.1 Q - Where does this quest start?

A. – Talk to Serna Taskon in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport about the ‘Potion of Marr’.  You will then have to hand the potion to 8 different Sentries, in alpha order.  The sentries are: Andlin, Boris, Gallius, Janeal, Meighan, Theo, Warren, and Xyrni.  All sentries roam or are static within the grounds of the temple.  Sentry Xyrin is lost somewhere in the Ocean of Tears. 

 

Talk to a lady next to a lone hut, to the right of the docks on Sister Isle.  Ask her “Where is Sentry Xyrin” to spawn Sentry Xyrin on Spectre Isle.

 

People have said Xyrin wields a weapon that looks like the Harpoon (Halberd graphic), killing her reveals that it is only a rusty halberd in her hands.  Do not kill her. 

 

This quest may or may not be broken at this time, it looks as if portions of it are broken/disabled, while there is another quest in Erudin that looks like the new start of this quest.
