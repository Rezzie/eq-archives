Latest News:

 

01/20/99, Version 1.01 – A great month for Paladins, as we have finally got some acknowledgements by Verant that our class was one that needed an upgrade, and an upgrade we got.  We were given a slight inrease in Taunt, Bash, more hitpoints, Armor of Ro being easier to quest, and promises of better weapons.  Thanks to hints given out by Ratatosk on PON, Paladins were finally able to solve a quest that has stumped us for a long time: The Princess Lenya/Silent Watch/Tolon Nurbyte quest, which ends with a Silent Watch Shield being given to the noble Paladin completing it.  I’ll be putting in information for Plane of Sky Paladin-related quests and drops once I get a hold of for information and evidence.

 

01/10/99, Version 1.00 – Added questions relating to Strength, Dexterity, and Yaulp.  Looking to add a section dedicated to editorials relating to Paladins, and also a zone guide section with the Paladin perspective in mind.  The Fiery Avenger quest should be revamped and released by month’s end, lets hope it’s worth the wait and reasonably acquirable.

 

12/27/99, Version 0.99 - Did a slight update of the Paladin FAQ.  Cleaned up the appearance of the FAQ by linking topics under separate pages.  Added some quotes from Verant employees regarding the Fiery Avenger and Lay on Hands. Answered questions of: ‘Where can I get some freebie No Drop gear?’ and ‘What are some good uses for Root?’ Corrected or edited other answers.
