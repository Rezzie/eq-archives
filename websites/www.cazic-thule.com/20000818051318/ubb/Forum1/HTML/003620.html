Kill the Miller and loot a Sack of Hay. "Give" the sack of Hay to the named Aviak in the Aviak City of South Karana. If he says "thank you", then target him and say "What cows". He will then give you instructions for entering the secret "Cow zone".

Once there, kill as many cows as you can until Betsy, the named cow, spawns. Kill Betsy and loot a Cow Bell (effect: summon food). Every once in awhile El Toro, a named bull, will spawn and aggro on anyone in the zone, and he drops a Bull Horn (effect: Spirit of the Ox).
