Valorium:

 

1.2.1 Q – Where do I find this Paladin-only armor?

A. – Valorium armor pieces are rare drops off mobs located in the Plane of Fear.  All pieces are Magic Items, and No Drop.  They are light blue in color, and a Paladin of any race can wear it.

 

1.2.2 Q – What are the stats of the pieces?

A. –

Valorium Helm: AC 17 WIS 7 MANA 15 SV MAGIC 13 WT 4.5

Valorium Gauntlets: AC 13 STR 5 WIS 5 SV DISEASE 10 WT 3.8

Valorium Bracers: AC 10 STR 5 DEX 5 MANA 10 SV POISON 5 WT 3.0

Valorium Greaves: AC 17 INT 5 AGI 9 SV FIRE 10 WT 5.6

Valorium Chestplate: AC 30 STR 13 STA 9 WIS 9 WT 7.5

Valorium Boots: AC 17 STA 5 CHA 9 INT 5 WT 4.9
