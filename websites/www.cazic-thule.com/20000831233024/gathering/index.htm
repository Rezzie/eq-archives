Finally, after much planning it's time to make your reservations for the Cazic-Thule Bash! Meet all the people that you live and play with here on our great server!

Where: Las Vegas, Nevada

When: July 7 and 8, 2000

Hotel: The Wizzie Portal (Luxor) - You must register at the hotel on your own. For Details on how to get the group discount click HERE. I highly encourage everyone to stay at the Luxor as being in the bash hotel is always much more fun then staying near the bash hotel.

Registration: $75.00 per person prior to June 1, 2000. (After June 1st the registration fee is $100.00 per person) (Note: if you will be attending one of the nights instead of both, there will be a discount)
Registration fees cover the price of the Hors D'oeuvres Friday Night & Dinner with Verant on Saturday Night. For details on where to send your registration fee click HERE. Registration is necessary to attend the bash functions and registration fees must be received by June 1, 2000 for the early registration discount.

EverQuest Gathering III: The EverQuest Gathering III is happening in Las Vegas on July 9, 2000. This is a totally separate event but we planned the CT Bash on the same weekend so that if you decide, you can attend both. For complete details on this separate event go to the EverQuest Gathering III Page.

How to Book your Hotel Room

Where: Wizzie Portal (LUXOR)

How: Call 1-800-288-1000

What to Say: I am calling to make my room reservations for the Cazic-Thule Group July 7 and 8, 2000. My reservation code is TCAZIC.

How much: Rooms are $99.00 per night with a local 9% hotel tax. This is for double occupancy rooms; if you need a room for 3 people it is an extra $15.00, for 4 people it is $30.00. They have suites too but are not available in our room block; if you need/want one let them know and it will be arranged with them.

Check In: 3:00pm Check Out: 11:00am.

We have 75 rooms reserved at this rate. Once the 75 rooms are filled or after June 7th (which ever comes first), rooms and rates are based on availability and prices at that time.

Registration

Registration fees cover the costs of the events which will be taking place at the bash. It's encouraged that everyone attend these events for maximum fun.

Registration Fee: $75.00 per person prior to June 1, 2000. After June 1, the registration fee will be $100.00 per person. (Registration details are below)

  • Early Registration for Friday Only: $35.00 ($45.00 after June 1)
  • Early Registration for Saturday Only: $40.00 ($55.00 after June 1)

Also, please complete the registration form here on the CTC so that we can keep a running list of who is attending. You may complete this form prior to sending in your registration fee.

To register you may mail me a check or money order or use your credit card.

To mail me a check: Make Check or Money Order payable to Donna Kemper and mail it to the following address (Be sure to put your Registration Screen Name somewhere on the check!):

Donna Kemper
5252 Dolores Drive
Pleasanton, CA 94566

To Use Your Credit Card: Go to PayPal's Free Website - after you have registered with them, select the SEND MONEY link. Please list my e-mail address as webmaster@cornerzone.com when completing the transaction form. (If you are having problems figuring this out, please send me an e-mail and I will arrange for PayPal to bill you - whatever is easiest for you) Be sure to list your screen name in the NOTES area within PayPal

A list of all attendees can be found on our bash list. Up to the moment bash details can be found on the CTC Message Boards - Forum CT Bash. All questions regarding the bash can be posted on the boards, Bash Chat Forum.
