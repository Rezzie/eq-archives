Man nobody knows what piggybacking is when asked on this board, but yet at every calendar meeting people acknowledge that they do. Anyway here is what is "considered" piggybacking, but I am no guru.

If a guild with planes time invites others to join them then they can do all of the following things without being accused of piggybacking...

1. Invite as many non-guilded people as they want.

2. Invite as many people from a guild that has no alloted planes time.

3. Invite up to 6 people from a guild with planes time, but anymore than that is considered piggybacking. However some people consider 6 to even be too much, but the consensus has agreed to 6 from past talks.

What people generaly complain about in regards to piggybacking is this...

(using hypothetical guilds for reference) SS has 2 days in fear this month as well as EA. Now when SS does fear they invite EA members up and vice versa. People consider this piggybacking because EA and SS now have 4 days in fear this month instead of 2 each...get it?

Like I said I am not the guru of piggybacking, I am just here to give you an idea on what it is.
