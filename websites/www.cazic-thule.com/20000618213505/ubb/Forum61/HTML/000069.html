Let me elaborate on this story - it's worth relating. I was camping the bartender in Sol A one night with a group new to the area. We got trained and had to make a run for the zone. Unfortunately, a ranger in the group fell into the lava near the LavaStorm Mountains zone & died. I immediately offered to get his corpse for him, as I knew some people in the zone and was quite confident in my ability to retrieve it. A friendly necro cast Dead Man Floating on me and away I went.

I dove in and out of the lava, found the rangers corpse, and started dragging it around looking for a way out. I looked and looked, and lo and behold there was *no* way out. Here I was, floating above deadly lava, with 30 - 40 minutes remaining until my levitation spell wore off. No one in the zone was able to help, not even /duel would save me.

In a last ditch effort, I used /who all to find a wizard near me. THe first name I spotted was Kanbien - a level 36 entirely unknown to me (and me to him). I sent him a tell explaining my situation and begging for help - he immediately came to Sol A to teleport me out. He grouped with me, cast levitate on himself, and teleported. Lo and behold, he disappears! I was still in /duel mode and the TP didnt' stick .

Kanbien realized what had happened and immediately ran from the Neriak wizzie pyramid to Sol A - again! This time I wasn't /dueled and the teleport worked - I was safe.

Not only did he save my arse, he then proceeded to invite me into his guild - Lord knows why, I felt like the stupidest monk alive at that point, but I'm certainly glad he did.

If anyone deserves a nomination for selflessness and generosity, it's this particular dwarf.

p.s. Kanbien is Kanbyen's alter ego, if you weren't aware.
