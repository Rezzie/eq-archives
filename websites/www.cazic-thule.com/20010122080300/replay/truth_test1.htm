Test of Truth:

 

4.1.1 Q – How do I begin the Test of Truth to obtain my Testimony?

A. – This is a very important Paladin quest, as it is part of the process to become a full-fledged Knight and also a component to acquire Soulfire with.  Talk to Merko Quetalis in the Hall of Truth in North Freeport to start the quest.  There is no level restriction on this quest.

 

4.1.2 Q – Where is Altunic Jartin located?

A. – Altunic can be found in the East Commons building across from the Inn nearest the West Commons zone line, most often called 3rd Inn.  If he is not in his hut selling items, then he is out wandering around in East Commons and will be back shortly.  You will have to wait until he returns to his shop before continuing the quest.

 

4.1.3 Q – What level is Squire Narls?

A. – Squire Narls is about level 6, although he is very difficult to hit even if you are a few levels above him.  Narls also likes to run when he is near death, so be prepared for that.

 

4.1.4 Q – What level is Guard Willia?

A. – Guard Willia is about level 12.  If you hesitate to kill Willia, she will run, and you may not be able to catch her in time, forcing you to start the quest over.
