General Factions:

 

3.2.1 Q – How many faction (consider) levels are there?

A. – Scowling ready to attack, Threateningly, Dubiously, Apprehensively, Indifferently, Amiably, Kindly Consider, and Warmly.  There is also rumor of a ‘Fondly’ faction for the highest faction level, but that has not been seen in quite awhile.
