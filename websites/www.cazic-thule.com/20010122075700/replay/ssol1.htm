Shining Star of Light:

 

4.2.1 Q – How do I begin the Shining Star of Light quest?

A. – With the proper factions, talk to Astaed Wemor near the Temple of Life in North Qeynos.  Tell him you are ‘traveling afar’ to learn more about the quest.

 

4.2.2 Q – How can I recharge the Shining Star of Light?

A. – Talk to Whysia Flock in North Qeynos, she will require your Shining Star of Light and 20 gold pieces to recharge the weapon.  Hand in the SsoL and 20gp and she will immediately hand back to you a recharged SsoL.
