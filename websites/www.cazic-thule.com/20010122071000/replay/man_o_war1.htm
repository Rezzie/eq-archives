Man-O-War:

 

4.4.1 Q – How do I acquire the Man-O-War?

A. – An Erudite Paladin by the name of Quid Rhilestone is said to wander around the Paladin camp in Mount Rathe from time to time.  Killing him will either land you his common drop, or the rare drop, the Man-O-War.  He does not aggro the other Paladins, and he has no faction tied to him.
