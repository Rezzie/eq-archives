Q - Where in Lake Rathe is the Royal Fish?

A. – The Royal Fish can usually be found anywhere between the island with the flames (also known as 3-bird island) and the cove housing the guard tower and two human NPCs in full steel plate (due North of the zone line heading to Arena zone).  Be careful when you hand in the fish food to the Royal Fish, due to pathing, you stand a good chance of making the NPC kill itself (suicide).  Some recommend giving the food to the Royal Fish in the water, then handing in the key and the bracer to Princess Lenya also in the water.  Others recommend making sure the Royal Fish is near land, before giving the food, so that the Princess can easily get on land and you handing in the components from there.
