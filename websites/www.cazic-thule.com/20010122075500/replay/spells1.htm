2.1.2 Q – What are some good uses for Flash of Light?

A. – It is a good taunting tool and is mana efficient.  It is also used in duels/PvP to blind the opponent to set up for your next move.  One instance you do not want to use it in is when a mob is running away from you while in a dungeon.  Doing so will make him continue in his last set motion, and quiet possibly either fall off a ledge, or bring back more mobs to you.  FOL acts almost like a Fear spell.  If you flash, stop attacking and back away. Caster or not, the monster will turn around and run away.  When it comes to it’s senses, it should come running back at you provided no one did any more taunting or damage to him after FOL.

 

2.1.3 Q – Can I tell if the undead sees through Invis to Undead?

A. – After you cast Invisibility to Undead on yourself, consider the mob, and see the message.  If the mob originally considered something other than Indifferent, and now considers you Indifferent, then he does not see through your spell.  Any other (lower) faction means the mob sees through your spell.

 

2.1.4 Q – How many hit points does each heal spell do?

A. – Minor Healing = 10, Light Healing = 30, Healing = 80, Greater Healing = 270.

 

2.1.5 Q – What gems do I need for the Symbols Paladins receive?

A. –

Level 30, Symbol of Transal requires 1 Cat’s Eye Agate and gives 100hp.

Level 39, Symbol of Rylthan requires 1 Bloodstone and gives 150hp.

Level 49, Symbol of Pinzam requires 1 Jasper and gives 200hp.

 

2.1.6 Q – What are some good uses for Root?

A. – Proper use of Root can allow for some great crowd control when other methods aren’t available to you, such as Bards, Enchanters, or Monks.  Root can be used during the initial pull to keep one mob in place, while the other linked mobs lunge at you.  If more than the desired amount still comes at you, you can root them in halls/rooms enroute to the groups camping spot.

 

Root can be also used to space out spawns, when you do not want to kill spawns within seconds of each other.  It will immobilize them while you wait for a proper amount of time to pass by. 

 

Rooting casting mobs away from the group is not effective use of root.  Rooting mobs next to other players will not work either.  When mobs are ready to run due to excessive injury, make sure you do not interfere with someone who has snare, engulfing darkness, or chain song, as they are more effective in that situation.

 

A quote from Gordon Wrinn about roots being resisted, “The Root series has always been highly resistable, just like any movement affecting spell. No changes have been made since beta to change how resistable it is.”

 

2.1.7 Q - What are some good uses for Sense Magic?

A. – It is an inexpensive way, mana wise, to get your Divination skill up.

 

2.1.8 Q – What are some good uses for the Yaulp line?

A. – If you use Yaulp throughout the course of combat, you will notice no stamina loss.  This may be a good alternative to having to use Invigor when you run out of Stamina.  Try and cast it right after you swing, not right before you were about to swing, otherwise you stand a good chance of losing that swing to get your spell off.  Think of Yaulp as a temporary AC, STR, and STAMINA bonus.  It has a short cast time and uses very little mana.  Although Yaulp does not cancel Celerity or Swift Like the Wind haste buffs, it does cancel Augmentation.

Jasper can be found in: West Freeport - caster guild, East Commons – Erudite Vendor, High Hold Keep – upstairs, North Qeynos – The Jewel Box, Kelethin, Felwithe A – Shop of All Holds, Felwithe B – building nearest zone, Rathe Mountains
