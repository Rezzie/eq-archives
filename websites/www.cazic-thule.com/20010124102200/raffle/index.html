The Raffle Rules are as follows:

  • Tickets for the raffle are 10 plat each
  • Each character may enter the current raffle no more then 5 times for a total of 50 plat.
  • The only two people selling raffle entries (at this time) will be Aracnakat & Delrachnid.
  • Raffle drawings will include all items donated prior to the day of the raffle and the total amount of money collected for that raffle.
  • All items donated for the raffle on the day of the raffle will be held until the next raffle.
  • Winners do not need to be present at the time of the raffle drawing. When the winner is selected via /random roll their name will be shouted throughout the North Ro zone and a /tell will be given. If the person is not on-line at the time, they will see their name listed with all winners on the CTC.
  • Winner's names will remain in the raffle until all /random rolls are done. In other words, everyone can win more then once.
  • The Grand Prize of the raffle will be all the money collected for the raffle. The Grand Prize will always be the last /random roll.
  • If at anytime the raffle will interfere with a GM Run Event or Quest, the Raffle will be postponed until the following week.
  • All prize winners will have exactly one month to claim their prizes (or at least to contact us). Any prizes not picked up by the next raffle will be forfeited and entered back into the raffle as prizes.
  • This is a "live" document and the rules can change at any time as we learn how to make the raffle run more smoothly.

If you have any questions about the raffle feel free to write to me directly. Aracnakat
