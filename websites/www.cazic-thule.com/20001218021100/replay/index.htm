Paladins of Norrath FAQ     version 1.01

 

 

 

This FAQ is a compilation of many different questions and answers from web boards, talks with other role-players, editorials and stat sheets, among other things.

 

This FAQ is to help answer some of the more commonly asked questions related to the Paladin class in the online role-playing game, Everquest.  This is not meant to be a guide on how to play the game, Everquest, and on a lesser note, the Paladin class.  Some questions, because of differing views, will include multiple answers – opinions.  As is with opinions, there is no one right answer; you will have to let your heart and mind decide which route is the best for you.

 

Understand that there may be typographical mistakes, errors, omissions, inconsistencies, and that the world of Norrath is ever changing.  Should some of these things come to light, let the author(s) of this FAQ know so that it may continue to be updated.
