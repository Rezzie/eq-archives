Falchion of the Koada’Vie:

 

4.8.1 Q – How do I start the Falchion of the Koada’Vie quest?

A. – After you complete some of the lower level quests including the Orc Runner quest, and given proper faction, go to General Jyleel in the Paladin Guild of Felwithe.  Hail him and tell him you will ‘Prove yourself a faithful Paladin of the court’ in order for him to speak to you.

 

4.8.2 Q – How do I acquire the components General Jyleel requires?

A. – Gen. Jyleel requires 3 items to prove your faith.  You will need the Black Heart that comes off Ambassador Dvinn in Crushbone castle, the Blue Orc Head from the Orc Prophet in Crushbone castle, and finally, the supply crate off Brarm in the Ocean of Tears.  Hand in all 3 items to Gen. Jyleel at the same time to receive your new Falchion.

 

4.8.3 Q – Where can I find Brarm?

A. – Brarm is located in the Ocean of Tears, on the first set of islands you pass by, when taking the boat going from Butcherblock to Freeport.  It is the set of islands to your right, assuming you are facing the front of the ship.  Brarm only spawns once per game day, right at the break of dawn, and is flanked by two constant-aggro Teir’dal Dragoons.  Their placeholders are the Alluring Sirens, who disappear right around 4am each game day.
