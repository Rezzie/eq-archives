Games.Net Interview, Part 3, July 11th, 1999
Games.Net has published the 3rd and final segment of their interview with Brad McQuaid. Topics talked about in this segment of the interview include class balance, rare items, artifacts and more. Here's an excerpt:

Jason: Fiery Avenger. Will there only be one of those per server?
Brad: Fiery Avenger is not an artifact. An artifact is by definition is an item of which there will only be one per server or perhaps one in the entire game.
Jason: Are there artifacts in the game now?
Brad: There are no artifacts in the game right now but there are artifacts. Artifacts will be awarded after huge high level dynamic quests created by GMs. You will never get artifacts off natural spawn. The Fiery Avenger is a very high level complicated quest that no one is even close to right now.
