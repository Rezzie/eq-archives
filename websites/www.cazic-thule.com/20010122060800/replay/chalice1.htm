Chalice of Conquest:

 

4.7.1 Q – How do I begin the Chalice of Conquest?

A. – Talk to Dru Razbind who is located just North of the docks in Butcherblock.  He will not give you the quest if you are not considered Amiably or higher.  Currently, the quest only seems to work for Dwarves, and no other race.  After talking with Dru and receiving the container, head to Rathe Mountains and ask the Dwarf Paladin, Nicholas, about the Chaclice of Conquest to further the story. 

 

4.7.2 Q – Where do I find this odd druid?

A. – The odd druid is a rare spawn located around the druid camp in Rathe Mountains.  The druid is labeled as a ‘unkempt preserver’, but when attacked, will shout “Long live the green!!”  You must continually clear other unkempt druids and animals around this area to get the odd druid to spawn.

 

4.7.3 Q – What do I give Dok to receive the Candle of Bravery?

A. – You have to give Dok of Halas: one honeycombo piece, one Soil of Underfoot, candle stem and candle base.  Unless you’re KOS, faction with him is not essential.
