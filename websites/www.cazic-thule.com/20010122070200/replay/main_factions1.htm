Essential Paladin Factions:

 

These are just some ways to fix your factions, not a complete listing.  Listed methods are the ones deemed easier than most, or the only ones available to fix a certain faction group.

 

3.1.1 Q – How do I better faction with the Knights of Thunder?

A. –

Hand in bone chips 2 at a time to Lashun Novashine who is found wandering in North Qeynos.

Talk to Captain Tilin in South Qeynos to start the Gnoll fang quest.  Handing in fangs gives you some faction adjustments with other groups and more importantly, you receive moonstones for each fang you hand in.  Hand in the moonstones to Mcneal Jocub in Fish’s Ale for Priests of Life faction adjustments. 

Kill putrid skeletons in the northern-most undead camp in South Karana.

 

3.1.2 Q – How do I better faction with the Priests of Life?

A. –

Hand in bone chips 2 at a time to Lashun Novashine, who is found in wandering in North Qeynos. 

Talk to Captain Tilin in South Qeynos to start the Gnoll fang quest. 

Handing in fangs gives you some faction adjustments with other groups and more importantly, you receive moonstones for each fang you hand in. 

Hand in the moonstones to Mcneal Jocub in Fish’s Ale for Priests of Life faction adjustments. 

Killing putrid skeletons in the undead camp in South Karana will also help raise faction.

 

3.1.3 Q – How do I better faction with the Priests of Marr?

A. –

You can kill Militia out of West Commons, located near the tollbooth, or hand in milk to Mojax Hhikspin, located near the Inns and Lake in West Commons. 

You can kill piranhas swimming next to the Hall of Marr in North Freeport, or kill orcs in East Commons. 

Talk to Serna Tasknon located in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport and do the Potion of Marr quest. 

Kill corrupt guards, and then hand in the damaged militia helms that come off of them to Theron in the Hall of Marr.

 

3.1.4 Q – How do I better faction with the Priests of Marr?

A. –

You can kill Militia members in West Commons located near the tollbooth, or hand in milk to Mojax Hhikspin, located near the Inns and Lake in West Commons. 

Kill the piranhas swimming next to the Hall of Marr in North Freeport.

Kill orcs in East Commons. 

Talk to Serna Tasknon located in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport and do the Potion of Marr quest. 

Kill corrupt Freeport guards, and then hand in the damaged militia helms that come off of them to Theron in the Hall of Marr.

 

3.1.5 Q – How do I better faction with the Clerics of Tunare?

A. –

Clerics and Paladins of Felwithe belong to the Clerics of Tunare faction group. 

Hand in 4 bone chips at a time to Yeolarn Bronzeleaf located in the cleric guildhall. 

Give muffins to Pandos Flintside on the 2nd floor of the Hogcaller’s Inn in West Freeport.  Muffins can be purchased at the unlabeled building right next to the monk guild.

 

3.1.6 Q – How do I better faction with the Deepwater Knights?

A. –

No known way of raising faction with the DWK at this time.

 

3.1.7 Q – How do I better faction with Miners Guild 249?

A. –

Hand in bone chips 4 at a time to Paladin Gunlok Jure in North Kaladim.

 

3.1.8 Q – How do I better faction with the Clerics of Underfoot?

A. –

Hand in bone chips 4 at a time to Paladin Gunlok Jure in North Kaladim.

 

3.1.9 Q - How do I better faction with the Temple of Solusek Ro?

A. – One way is to kill Shadowed Men, lots of them.  You can also give Ssynthi in Mistmoore Castle the flower in the burial chamber.  If you have money to spend, you can give money to an Erudite NPC named Rathamana in South Ro.  Give him 3 plats worth of coins each time for a faction adjustment, experience, and a low level prize – usually a spell.  Consider Rathamana, since he has the same factions as the NPCs in the temple.
