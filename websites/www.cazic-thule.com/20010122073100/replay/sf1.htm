Soulfire:

 

4.5.1 Q – How do I start the Soulfire quest?

A. – Talk to Kalatrina Plossen in the Hall of Marr in North Freeport to begin this quest.  She will have you deliver a note to a spy in the militia house.

 

4.5.2 Q – Where is Ariska Zimel located, and what do I give him?

A. – Ariska Zimel of Zimel’s Blades is a prisoner located below the Arena in West Freeport.  He needs Edible Goo, Bog Juice, and the Blanket.  Upon receiving these 3 items, he then gives you a banker’s key labeled “H.K. 102”, the name of a High Keep safe deposit.  The Edible Goo is made from baking, the Bog Juice by brewing, and the blanket from the beggar, Tykar Renlin.

 

4.5.3 Q – What do I do with the key labeled H.K. 102?

A. – You need to take this key and give it to the bank clerk in High Keep.  Upon doing so, you will receive a Note, which you are to deliver to Brother Hayle.

 

4.5.4 Q – What factions do I need to check and fix?

A. – Priests of Life and Knights of Truth factions are the ones that matter: Priests of Life because Brother Hayle’s faction is grouped with them and Knights of Truth because Valeron Dushire’s faction is grouped with them.  Look under the factions thread for more information on how to fix these factions.

 

4.5.5 Q – Where is the spy in the West Freeport Militia house?

A. – He is located in the backyard, below the balcony.  Hail Guard Alayle and then say “Truth is good!” for him to respond.  Hand him the note Kalatrina Plossen gave you to deliver.  It is inevitable that Guard Alayle will die, so do not risk your life trying to save him.

 

4.5.6 Q – How are the Freeport portions done if I’m KOS to the militia?

A. – If you are deep KOS, and spending a reasonable amount of time questing and killing NPCs will not fix it, then there are other ways around it.  To get to the Seafarer’s Roost in EFP to talk with the beggar and Groflah, you need someone to cast Invisibility on you, either that, or purchasing Invis potions.  The same can be done for the jailhouse under the Arena where Zimel is being kept.

 

4.5.7 Q – Where is Xicotl and the Glowing Hilt located?

A. – Xicotl is located on the 2nd floor of Mistmoore castle in the spa room right before the master bedroom.  One way to get there is to enter through the hidden room in the graveyard, travel up the stairs and take the left route – opposite of heading to tower.  Xicotl’s placeholder is a Mistmoore Guard.  Xicotl is a shadowknight, so beware of his Harm Touch.  Best place to pull to would be the room right outside the pool, where two gargoyles spawn.

 

4.5.8 Q – Does the note come off Lucan’s human or skeletal corpse?

A. – The note, if it drops, will be found on Lucan’s skeletal corpse.  Be careful no stranger just walks along and grabs the note if the corpse goes public.

 

4.5.9 Q – Who do I hand Lucan’s Testimony to?

A. – Valeron Dushire, Paladin GM located on the second floor of the Hall of Marr.  Giving him the note with an acceptable faction will give you he Brilliant Sword of Faith (BSOF).

 

4.5.10 Q – Where can I find Brother Hayle?

A. – Brother Hayle is a human cleric NPC located in SplitPaw, a dungeon located in South Karana on the continent of Antonica.  He is locked up in the prison area.  In order to talk to him, you will have to flip a lever outside his cell.  It is recommended you take a friend to control the level while you speak to Brother Hayle or you may find yourself locked up with him.

 

4.5.11 Q – What order do I hand them items in to Brother Hayle?

A. – If you have the faction of Kindly Consider or higher: you hand in the Note separately, (Returned) Note separately again, then the Glowing Hilt, BSOF, and Testimony of Truth all at the same time to receive Soulfire.

 

4.5.12 Q – Who is Laken Boorheez and why does he mysteriously appear?

A. - Laken Boorheez is a barbarian NPC that shows up during parts of the Soulfire quest.  He appears when you ask Rashinda in the Office of the People “What do you know of Zimel’s Blades?”  He appears again outside when you go to Groflah’s Forge and hand Groflah the note.  He makes a final known appearance near Seafarer’s Inn when you hand the note to Groflah. 

 

Killing him only hurts your good factions with Freeport groups, and there is nothing to loot.  No known text has been known to get him talking.  Whatever his importance, he is not a necessary piece in acquiring Soulfire.
