May Contest:   Everyone in Cazic-Thule is invited to participate in this contest.  All that you need to do is post on the message boards daily.  Every day that you post will enter your name into a drawing for the prize.  Therefore you could have your name entered 31 times (each day in May).   (Posting multiple times per day will not get you entered more times on that day).

May Contest Prize:  Small Bronze Chest Plate (17 AC) (WAR, CLR, PAL, SHD, BRD) (ELF, HIE, DEF, DWF, HFL, GNM)  Although not all are able to utilize this fine bronze plate, all would be able to sell it .. and they do sell nicely.  Prize submitted by Felonius Grasshopper!  (Thanks Felonius!)

All people are invited to participate but only people within Cazic-Thule will be eligible for the prize.
