CLOSING AND CREDITS

 

Even with some obvious and not-so-obvious drawbacks of the Paladin class, they continue to be an enjoyable class for many, and I hope you give it a try.  But, if you’re not ready to handle the problems of being a Paladin, constantly complain yet have nothing to offer, only in it for some flaming sword, or not willing to put in the time, there are much more enjoyable classes to play.
