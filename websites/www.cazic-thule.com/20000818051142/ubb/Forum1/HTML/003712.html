When I first got my ports, did I charge for em? Hell yeah! I had to pay for the rest of my spells somehow. Not all of us knew about wisp stones and HQ bear pelts when we were young...

Do I still charge for ports? Not unless you call me... Free rides whenever I gotta get any place myself, and I offer almost every time unless I'm needed in a hurry at the other end. Will I accept a donation? Every time. Do I donate for SOW and Clarity? Sometimes. I offer EVERY time, and am glad to give it when it will be accepted. I'm purchasing a service that I'm glad (and not entitled damnit!) to get. I'm glad to pay 30 plat+ for a res too, its a service that I need, and its worth it to me to buy. I sure do love it when Clerics turn down their fee/donation, but that's THEIR choice.

Will I EVER TP some lame ass under lvl 10 who sends me a blind tell asking for a free ride? Nope. Enjoy the boat ride for 20 lvls and learn the lay of the land like I did. Make friends with Wizzys who'll port ya for free when they can. If you want the priveledge of a port that's gonna save you a half hour of play, at least be prepared for some MINOR compensation.

I dunno, I think once you're past 35 or so you start forgetting how tough it was back in the 20s to make some cash. Let the poor guy earn a few plat, who's it gonna hurt? If he's not charging outrageous rates he'll earn a few plat, certianly not enough for a SMR but enough to buy his spells and a few trinkets with. If he's outrageous nobody will pay his prices.
