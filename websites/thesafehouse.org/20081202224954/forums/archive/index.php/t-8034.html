Here it is Felonious:

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Go to Thurg and Bazaar and buy the stuff needed for the Artisan's seal:
- 8 x Velium Bar, 2100pp (Thurg, vendor bought)
- Blue Diamond*, 1200pp (player)
- Diamond*, 200pp (player)
- Black Sapphire*, 170pp (player)
- Jacinth*, 150pp (player)
- 4 x Ruby, 540pp (vendor)
Total: 4360pp (my price of course)

Now you’ll need help from a Jeweller to make the 4 mounted gems (2 x velium bar + 1 gem* = mounted gem*) and an enc to make a Vial of Purified Mana (Poison Vial + 4 Ruby as spell components). Triv 176 on Mounted Gem.

After this you use Pottery (I bet every rogue has somewhere close to 188 in this skill) to make the Seal (4 x mounted gem* + Purified Vial of Mana + Small Block of Clay + Flask of Water = Artican's Seal) (triv 107). Fire it with a HQ sheet.

Finally combine Artisan’s Seal with a Sealed Poison Vial in Mortar and Pestle (triv 251) for the Grandmaster Assassin’s Vial.

Make sure you have a Decanter of the Apothecary for the +3% in Make Posion (for the last combine) and a Geerlok Sculpturing Tool for +5% on Pottery for the Artisan’s Seal combine.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference link at: www.eqtraders.com.

Good luck.

And oh.. dont for get to turn in one of your Grandmaster's Satchel to Jolum (in Bazaar) for a more permanent Grandmaster's Carry-All.

Enjoy.
