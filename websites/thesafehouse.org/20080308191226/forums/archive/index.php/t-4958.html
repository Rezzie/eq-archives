Huff's Formulae works out to a Max cap of 63% of time dual attacking.

Base 25%
50 spec +25%
1/4 Level 13%


Since weapon, CS, stealth are all MANDATORY I believe your choice is between envenom and DW/CD.

In 15 seconds at Skill 50 an Envenom Assassin will do 370 extra points damage.

In 15 seconds a fully specc'ed DW/CD will do and extra 306 points damage. (Note thats extra 63% dual vs 38% Duel Wield). Thats allowing for FAST weapons. If you prime hand a slower weapon this will fall of dramatically (158 extra points damage with a Guarded Rapier for instance).

As the above poster says, it's close enough to be your call. But the Poisons also allow disease, enervating , and some day mebbe the slow poison won't be a useless waste of coin.
