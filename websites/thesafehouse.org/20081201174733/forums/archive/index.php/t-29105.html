There are pages you have to collect to complete a quest to get the book.


Seek out Olkurk in Khal outside of the tavern on the east half of Khal


page 1: Khal - off the skeletons around the Fates
page 2: Qa Riverbank - Turpin named cyclopse
Page 3: Skawlra Rock - Inside the island carnivorous clams
Page 4: Sundering Wastelands- off the sentinals
Page 5: Jalens Crossing - Taint quest area off the fire eye's occulus (not off the elemental islands just the main island
Page 6: Magi Hold--off ice wardens
Page 7: Three Rivers - Off ants at the fallen star lake
Page 8: Ruins of Trengal Keep - off mutated spiders part3 of the trengal quest
Page 9: Misthaven Crossing-- hillsbury mansion off the wasps in the vineyard
Page 10: Ruins of Vol Tuniel --whisps on the top floor of the ruins
