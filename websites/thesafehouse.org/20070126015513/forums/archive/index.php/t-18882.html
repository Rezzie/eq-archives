Well, this is what I normally do: Stunlock. Normally, if a paladin is stucked in a stun throughout the entire battle, he can't even hope to Bubble heal.

But of course, stunlocking is not foolproof. In case stunlocking ****** up, remember this: A paladin with no mana is a dead paladin. Thus, I keep forcing them to heal and heal and heal. However, this is risky, obviously. There is a rather high chance that you will die first, though there was once I survived long enough to see him without mana, and that pretty much meant game over for the paladin.

And one thing to note, when the paladin bubbles, this is what you should do, stay back and first aid. The paladin can't come to you, and with you first aiding, you are resetting the fight and once again, you have a higher chance of surviving that encounter.

Then there is the most dishonorable method for us rogues to use, something we are amazingly good at: Ganking. Wait till the paladin's health is very low, though you are going to have to wait a long time, and you have to be quick or else he 'Lays on Hands'. A critical ambush will finish the job.

And finally, don't **** up on your stunlock or the battle will be hell.

And remember, always use Crippling Poison.
