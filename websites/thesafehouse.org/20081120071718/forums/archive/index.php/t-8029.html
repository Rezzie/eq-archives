1. You do NOT need to be holding the weapon on your mouse. I have NEVER held my weapon on my mouse and apply has always worked.

2. You can take your poison out of your bag, place it in an open inventory and right click to apply. You can then put the poison into an open slot in a bag, close the bag, open another bag, make some new poison, etc ... as long as you don't stand, the poison will apply.

3. While you are applying the poison, if you stand up, the apply is cancelled and you still have your poison.

Tips:

1. If you have a free inventory slot, you can place a vial of poison there and then hotkey that slot. Then, hit a key to apply the poison (very handy when you have an "add")

2. If you don't have an open inventory slot, you can still hotkey the container. Then you hit one key to open to bag, grab the poison, swap it with the bag in the hotkey location ... etc....
