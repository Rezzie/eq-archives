Well, I've tried a shield on a few occassions and it never performs as well as my DW, and at level 42 my DW is only at 10. When I solo a blue or yellow mob I take significantly more damage when I don't use DW because the fight takes longer. In group situations you'd better not be drawing agro anyway, unless you've got a pulser, so there's no point in using a shield and forsaking the extra damage you'd add to the group with DW.

I don't block regularly enough to make it worthwhile, and if I put the beartooth style on my hotbar I'd have to ditch one of my other styles. I prefer to leave my evade reactionary styles up instead if I'm soloing. I'm much more likely to be able to use them during a fight. Usually several times.

Generally the only time I equip my shield is during RvR when I'm being shot at by an archer.
