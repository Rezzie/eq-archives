If you need some extra money early on in the game I recommend doing some wholesaler tasks for whatever tradeskill organization you joined. Make sure you raise your tradeskill harvesting abilities on the Island of Refuge (much much easier to do there) and once they are above 40 you will be able to harvest resources in Antonica and the Commonlands.

The wholesaler will typically ask you to harvest 15 of a resource, and give you 12 silver for each quest (you can do them indefinately). In addition, most of the tier 2 resources are in high demand and will sell very quickly for 50 to 20 copper a piece.

12-01-04, 02:45 PM
Freeport stinks for gathering. All the Tier 1 zones are full of highly aggressive mobs and trains from dumbasses, so it can be difficult to gather until you get high enough to grey the agressive mobs out, or join up with a group that'll protect you while you gather. The Graveyard is probably the easiest zone to gather in, since the front of that zone is kind of a low level, and the undead have a very small aggro radius.

Qeynos, on the other hand, has 3 zones, Oakmyst, the Bog and the Ruins, where gathering is fairly easy. The aggressive mobs are easily avoided, and the only problem is competition from other players. The Caves aren't really even a bad zone to gather in, as the pit area and the front of the ramp area have few aggressive mobs, and quite a few nodes to gather from.
