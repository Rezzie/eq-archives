In RvR it works the same; however, if another player is far enough away from you they will not see you as a "ghost" but they won't even see you at all.

Very good point...just pointing out however that for the high levels stealth becomes "capped" in a way.

If you're a lvl 40 stealther and with items that have +stealth on them your stealth is 45, a lvl 43 searching for you will have the same chance as a lvl 45.

i.e. the game compares your total stealth lvl with the character lvl of the searcher. At lvl 50 the most +skill you can have on any one skill is +11 so if your stealth is 39+11(50total), you're as hidden as you can be. A lot of people rely on the +11 so they can stop training stealth at 39 and put the points into other things.
