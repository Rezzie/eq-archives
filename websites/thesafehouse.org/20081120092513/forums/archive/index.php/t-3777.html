EZ_Defy Logic
11-05-03, 06:15 AM
I've done the event numerous times and have come to find that the event getting bugged has something to do with having copies of VZ still at their spawn points for the next wave. As a monk, I've tried several methods of splitting/pulling the event from tagging a corner &amp; just trying to get 2-3 at a time to playing with their leashes &amp; FD to split them to singles or doubles. Most of these splitting tactics is what leads to the bugging of the event.

The surefire way that has never failed me with this event is to set up 5 tanks with dedicated healers &amp; have 1 target person to get a different target for each tank then just pull all 5 of them at the same time. Its easy to pick out which VZ of the 5 is the real one once you know what to look for. Just make sure you have a couple monks on DP duty making sure they're all pushed into the corner well &amp; the MA will assist off the other tanks that know their mob is a fake. Even if you dont know how to pick out the real VZ quickly it doesnt really matter if the next wave pops as long as there are no other fakes at their spawns &amp; they're all being killed off in the corner. Just have the raid in the left corner if you were facing VZ's balcony as there is no repop there &amp; it will give you plenty of room to kill the fakes without aggroing an early wave. Some fakes are mezzable some are not, the partially slowable ones are mezzable, fully slowable ones are Non-Mezzable. If your raid is in sync &amp; arent mindless lemmings you can get a couple mezzes down, if not just keep all 5 tanked &amp; dps them down, they dont have very much HP anyway.
