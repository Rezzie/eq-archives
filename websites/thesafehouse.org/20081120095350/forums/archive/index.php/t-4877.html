I'm only 24, mind you, but I autotrained stealth through that level. I found that perforate artery (which I got at 23) is a nice thing to have because backstab II is pretty excruciating to use when your stealth is low and you move so slowly. I would say if I had to keep stealth autotrained until 28, I would give up playing, because it's pretty hard to hold out.
