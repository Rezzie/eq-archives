Well.. depends on what ya want outta your Infil... Speccing Slash makes you more PvE deadly, Thrust more RvR deadly.

Slash has mainly 3 targets in RvR: Casters, Rogues, and Archers. But a lot of mobs in PvE are weak to Slash.

Thrust has all Chain wearing targets at a disadvantage. This includes (I believe) Warriors, Thanes, Clerics (For PvP server btw), Mercs, Minstrels, Healers, Heroes, Champions, and Skalds. I'm not too sure if Thrust gets a bonus to Scale or not, so someone please correct me if I'm wrong. This offers more targets overall as it's pretty easy to beat down casters with Thrust or Slash, just that Slash makes it quicker.
