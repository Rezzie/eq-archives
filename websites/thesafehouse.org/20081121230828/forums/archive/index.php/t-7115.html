Agil affects AC (but really really minor effect)
Sta affects HP (20hp at 60 = 100 or 120hp)
Str affects ATK and how much you can carry (1 str = 1 ATK at 60, give or take a little)
Dex affects proc rate

Just put all you can in stamina, and the rest probably in Str.
