If you are a Hover User, or a Hover/Fly User, I would especially recommend the post in the second link by Meraxes at 04/27/04 12:42 PM

A little tricky to setup, but Its an incredibly useful set of binds, that lets you hit F to 1) Jump up and start hovering upward at max speed 2) Stop rising and hover level and 3) stop hovering and drop to the ground.

It also has stuff to reset the three modes, in case it gets out of synch, and you can use shift+F to toggle from Hover mode to Fly mode and back again.
