SAN D'ORIA

The Batwing Quest
Given: Guy on the platform near the Blacksmith's shop, closest to the Ronsperre Gate.
Desc: He simply asks that you bring him two bat wings.
Reward: 200 Gil!
Level: 1+

Flint Stone Quest:
Given: Lady in the Stone Lion Inn.
Desc: She asks you to bring her 4 Flint Stones
Reward: 100 Gil
Level: 1+

Rabbit Hide Quest:
Given: An old man beside the tanner shop.
Desc: He asks you to bring him 3 Rabbit Hides.
Reward: 120 Gil.
Level: 1+

PickPocket Quest:
Given: Small girl in the large Airship bay, nearest the cargo rooms.
Desc: Speaking to her will trigger a scene. A pickpocket named will rob a guard standing nearest to the wall overlooking the bay. You must persue her by following her trail. But first! speak to the little girl that initiated the quest again, she will give you an 'Eagle Button', you need this to finish the quest.
Now, speak to guards and civilians as you go back. The route she takes is down the passage from the bay to the room that splits to the auction house and the bay, then back to Northern San D'Oria. From there, follow her trail up on the walkways and towards Ronsperre Gate, the gate guard will say that she ran past. Go out the gate into West Ronfaure, Esca will be waiting in a tower along the west wall, in the northern part of West Ronfaure. She will demand that you bring evidence of her crime. Trade her the Eagle Button. She will give you the Gilt Glasses, return these to the guard in the Airship Bay.
Reward: Light Axe
Level: 1+

The Ointment Quest:
Given: Women who walks slowly in front of a house on Pikeman's way, just passed the West Gate.
Desc. : Her husband is a guard, and he forget his ointment this morning. Take the bottle of ointment she gives you, and go out the west gate. Head west along the road til you reach a sign post, at the T-intersection of the path. Give the ointment to the guard standing there. He will thank you, and give you an Ointment Case to take back to his wife. Return to San D'Oria and give her the ointment case.
Reward: Bronze Subligar
Level: 1+

The Flyer Quest
This quest is obtained in the magic shop. You need to deliver fliers to the following people:
Fliers for Regine
from: Regine (Regine's Magicmart)
reqs: --
gets: 440 gil
soln: Give out 15 fliers to townsfolk around the town, not adventurers. The following are the people you need to give them to and their locations:
Answald, front of moghouse (Port J10)
Miene (Port I9)
Portaure (Port H9)
Prietta (Port H9 2nd level)

Guilberdrier (North SanD F3)

Boncort (North SanD F8
Coullene (North SanD M6)
Leuveret (South SanD F8 level 2)
Blendare (South SanD J9)
Maugie (South SanD L9)
Rosel (South SanD K8
Adaunel (South SanD K9)
Reward: 400 Gil!
Level: 1+ "They will come back, come back again, as long as the Red Earth rolls. He never wasted a tree or a leaf, why should he squander souls?"
