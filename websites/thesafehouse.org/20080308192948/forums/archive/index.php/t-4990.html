Skill cap is 11 + realm points.
L39 should do ya fine.

How stealth works,

Non Stealthers as Detector;
Detecter Character Level - Stealth Train Level * 1/2 =
# paces you can be seen at.

Stealthers as Detector
Detecter Character Level- Stealth Train Level =
# Paces you can be seen at.

So if your training is Skill 30 and a L50 tries to detect you (Non Stealther) about 10 Paces Stealther about 20 Paces.

Be aware it takes some Lag for your comp tp transmit position, other players comp to send location, Game to compare, make y/n, and display. In those (assume) 3 seconds a player will cover about 6 paces. So a player you CAN see coming right for you enters your visible envelope 10 paces away and is actually on your screen at 4 paces. By the same token a player who crosses inside your envelope moving away will recieve a "Yes" command and pop visible 16 paces away. Same player. There is supposed to be a "2 Pace" envelope where you are ALWAYS visible. It still takes 3 seconds to pop visible.

Jammer

ps; I use the "Pace" because it's far easier to understand. A "pace" in DAoC is about 50-55 "clics". The actual formulae above per Mythic is 25 and 50, hence 1/2 a pace and 1 Pace. Most players have a good sense of how far they move in 10 paces; while "500 clics" means next to nothing to many.

Okay, reason why people say 29 base stealth is that you can "supposedly" get:
(+11 stealth from items [by lvl50 ] ) + (another +10 stealth from realm rank 11) equalling +21 to stealth.

29+21=50 stealth
Most people say go 39 to base stealth +11 in items 'cause they know it takes a long time to attain realm rank and they want to reap some rewards early on. Valid point.

realize that you will have a very hard life as an assassin with a base 29 stealth before you can aquire +11 worth in items AND especially the +10bonus for realm rank11 RPs. Also, since you can only have item buff equal to LVL/5+1 to any skill, you will have to be lvl50 to get full item effect. BUT, in the loooooonnnng run, you will have saved enough specs for other skills from leaving your stealth at 29 or 39 that you will "likely" be a superior assassin in the end.
