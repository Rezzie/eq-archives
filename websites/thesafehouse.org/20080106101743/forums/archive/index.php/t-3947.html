(**OLD** Guide I made for out guild earlier this year)

Grummus, boss of Plane of Disease. Killing him flags you for the Crypt of Decay. CoD is a tier two zone which houses Bertox, along with several other minibosses

He is located inside the castle of PoD. Access to him requires killing Gryme the Cryptmaster. Gryme drops a no-rent key that lets you move past a locked door where Grummus is found.
He is unslowable and used the Stench of Decay AE descibed below. Paladin nife procs and nukes land on him. Quads for 700 and has around 100k hp.
Gryme is a one day respawn

After killing Gryme, you need to kill the Aramin the Spider Guardian. Aramin is level 68, perma rooted and maxes for 785, slowable. Has aprox 150k hp. After Aramin dies, 8 mini spiders spawn that hit for 300ish, and are slowable, but not mezzable. Suggested to camp a rezzer in case the minispiders wipe raid. They despawn faster then the corpse rots. Aramin drops a some decent items

You cannot pull Grummus out of the castle anymore. He has 4-6 level 61 guards that are not mezzable. They can be charmed and used as RT's if needed.

Grummus Level: 66

Max melee damage: 1179 quad + RAMPAGE

Not Slowable.

Could not find his HP, but a medium sized guild can kill him in under 5 minutes.

Grummus is on a leash. He cannot be pulled past the top of the ramp. Once he reaches the top, he poofs back to spawn point and loses all agro.

Area effects used:

Stench of Decay (36 disease counters)
radius: 100
resist: disease -300
dot: 200hp a tick for 1.8mins (3600 total)
Also has a slow affect with it (not sure on percent)

Plasma Decay (9 curse counters)
radius: 100
resist: disease -1000
dot: 1600 damage over 3.3mins (not sure)

Radiant cure will cure stench.

Shamans (49) and Beastlords (63) Abolish disease will remove stench in one cast.
Disinfecting aura will remove in two casts.
Counteract disease in five casts.

Plasma decay is only casted once every 4 mins.

Bard song + Shaman/Druid regen + AE healer in each group will overcome DOT damage.

Battle strategy:

Clear castle until we reach the room before the ramp down to Grummus. Finish all buffing and be prepared for Grummus engagement. A bard DA puller will pull his guards to the raid to be dealt with / charmed. It's ok if Grummus is agro'd while pulling guards, he will leash at the top of the stairs and poof to his spawn point with all agro lost.

Once guards are down, ranger WS or someone DA on Grummus to take the first plasma decay AE (4 min refresh).

After AE, the MT, RTs, and CoD pets are sent in, closely followed by the rest of the raid.

Rampage quad 1k is brutal, especially for non-planar guilds, so it is essential that agro is on the proper people.

Conclusion:

We have 7 radiant cures so far (at least), and hopefully more soon. This is an encounter we can complete with our normal full raid force (50ish people). We've beaten AoW finally, and this guy has less damage output to the MT. As long as we are smart about dealing with the AE dot damage we can do this. Healers keep your groups alive, melees &amp; nukers burn him down hard and fast.
