Unlike EQ, Stealth or Invisibility doesn't cause /con to change. All Stealths are successful, "failure" is when a mob unhides you or a player sees you (ghosty). High Stealth spec will decrease the chance of both, see Squink's Detect Hidden FAQ for details.

#1: "You are now hidden!" See above.
#2: The only timer is for the time between combat and when you can restealth, which is 10 seconds. You may remain stealthed as long as you wish.
#3: I don't know what F5 defaults to, it's mapped different for me. Ground target? That works with Distraction.
#4: Higher Stealth will increase your movement speed slightly while Stealthed, decrease your "You are too close to an enemy to hide!" radius, and make you generally harder to detect. Read the FAQ.
#5: You may want to head on over to the Ranger's Glade, though I don't have the URL. Archers frequently run into hostility here
