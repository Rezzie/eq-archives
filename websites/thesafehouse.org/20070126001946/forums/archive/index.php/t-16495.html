shattered bones can be found in Oakmyre forest, along with butterflies
unscathed/weathered bones can be found in Stormhold, along with spiders
bone chits and gnoll bones can be found in blackburrow

that's all I know right now. I find you can make a pretty gold piece if you go to SH or Oakmyre late at night, run around the zone, and find all the question marks when no one is online. Oakmyre is relatively safe, unless you're very low level. SH, the three places I've ever seen question marks are the Atrium, the Entrance Hall, and the Library. The Atrium they spawn more commonly, and it's completely safe there. I would spend the majority of my time in the atrium if I went ? hunting.
