I didn't feel like waiting, so here's a few answers. After you finish the very bginning of the quest, you're citizenship with Freeport is gone. The guards outside the gate aren't KoS. EDIT AGAIN: Some locs.. to reach the docks in Nektulos, head to pos 800 neg 200. I HIGHLY recommend following every path you can to get there. It should be in south west corner of the zone. I did the betrayal in the reverse direction to become a swashbuckler, so I can probably help in general terms on mechanics.

One very important thing to note though is that although your bank items will be waiting for you in your new city, your shared slots wont be. The shared slots are tied to alignment, not character, so if you leave anything important in those slots, only an alt of your old alignment will have access to them.

I found this out the hard way sadly. I thought those spots would change alignment with me...
