Ok, Rise of Kunark changed these a lot. You get one every 10 lvls, and each race also starts with 2. I'm going to break them down by race including only the ones I see being useful to the brigand, assassin, ranger and swashbuckler. Cobbled list together from several sources, lots of outdated info out there.

Arasai
Glide, a built in hover (I haven't tried this, but it would seem to negate safefall and prevent you from raising it or quickly dropping when needed)

Traditions:
Escaping Dive -hate
Aerial Dodging - Melee: +melee crit and defense
Dark Envy -5 min timer, 24 second +5crit and dam/dps, -defense and parry

Barbarian
Can summon beer

Traditions:
Herbology +5 Alchemy
Defender of Everfrost +Melee crit, parry, chance of double attack
Tundra Slam - dd + 2.5sec stun

Dark Elf
Master of the Shadows - 15% speed boost while stealth/invis
Hover - slow fall toggleable buff

Traditions:
Poison Crafter - 5% increased alchemy success chance
Poison Expertise - 25% increased poison trigger count
Shadow Slip -hate
Innoruks' Gift chance of double attack and piercing skill +
Fury of Innoruuk 5min +melee crit, dam/dps, -parry and defenses

Dwarf
Summons beer

Traditions:
Aggressive Strike +melee crit and +hate (might be bad)
Fury of Blades -AOE melee damage
Short but Strong - 3% max health

Erudite

Chemist +5 chemistry
