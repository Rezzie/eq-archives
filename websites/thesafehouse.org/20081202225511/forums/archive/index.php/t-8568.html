Sneak/Hide has worked as follows for me... When I go to turn in to lets say Stanos or the General since I'm KoS to both I get behind or on the side of them and unhide. I make sure I don't have aggro. I do a little jig. I click on Stanos/General. Yay, trade box open! I chill. I click trade. I put hide back up really quick. Now I think the only thing people are doing different is that they're clicking hide before they click trade (but the trade window is still open) in case clicking trade makes the mob face you. Then you're assured the mob won't aggro. Either way there's a delay.

For instance, when I spawn stanos I have to stand to the side of Anson and unhide to say "I need to see stanos" because he doesn't listen if he can't see you. Then I have to rehide really fast because Stanos spawns FACING you. There's a delay. Its possible.

And yes! That's the wonderful part about spawning Stanos you can have Stanos Parties. He's instantly spawned whenever someone says, "I need to see stanos."
