none that shadowblades can use


&lt;&lt;Is it worth it to spec in left axe?>>

LA is going through nerfs/bugs,whatever. its personal choice in the end,you can either spec la,or spec for 2h weapons. with 2h weapons you can fall back to 1h and a shield. whichever you like more,should be how you plan on speccing.

&lt;&lt;Can a shadowblade survive well just by using 2handed weapons only?>>

yes,although pve combat is a bit harder overall through your level range. left axe,is very good pve,while 2h has its ups and downs

&lt;&lt;Is this a good template?
Stealth-41, CS-39, Axes-37, Envenom-35, Left axe-25>>

stealth to high- 35-40 is fine,personal choice,at 35 you will get seen more often till you get lots of rp points. rr4 or better,but more points to invest into weapon/poisons

cs-fine,higher or lower as you like,dont fall below 34 if you want the pa/cd combo

envenom-35 is perfect with items/realm ranks can get to high 40s easy,and eventually 50 for lifebane.

left axe-nope,you either spec left axe or you dont. anything below 34 left axe is a waste,as skill determines your damge with 2 weapons. low skill means you will suffer a penality to damage.
