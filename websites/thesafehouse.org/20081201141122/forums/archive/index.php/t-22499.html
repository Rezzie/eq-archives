Today I went to the store looking for GitS:SAC 2nd Gig Vol. 3, and previously I had told myself that even though I do have a home theater capable of DTS I was only going to buy the regular versions of 2nd Gig, not the special editions (which includes a DTS version as well as some cool extras; last year I got three T-shirts and a couple CDs from the Special Editions).

So I get to Futureshop, ready to buy both Volume 3 of 2ng Gig and the Official Log 2 for the first season. I can't find the Log 2, but when I take a look at the new releases I see both the regular version and special edition of the DVD I want. Curious as to what separates the two versions (aside from $17cdn extra for the SE) I picked up the SE and read the back.

The Special Edition comes with the DTS disc in addition to the regular disc, both in a nice metal tin case, but it also came with two figurines, one of Major Motoko Kusanagi and one of a Tachikoma. I ended up buying the Special Edition for the figurines, though with the size of the box I figured they would be about the size of the small heroclix figure I got with my copy of City of Villains; I was wrong.

I got home and opened the box to find each model consisted of several pieces to be assembled, and the Major stands in at just over 3 1/4" high. The Tachikoma is smaller in height, but still a nice sized model and both have good detail. Below is an uploaded pic of the two models:
