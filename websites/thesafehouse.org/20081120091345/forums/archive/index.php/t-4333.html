-- How much energy do you use per fight? (As a Rogue, that is)

Energy is a constantly replenishing resource. It goes up 10 every second, roughly, no matter what you're doing. Usually, I use about 250 energy per fight.

-- Do you try to use it all because you regain it quickly, or is it something that should really be conserved strategically?

It's all about timing and sequences. For example, Backstab takes 65 energy, Sinister Strike (frontstab) takes 40, and Gouge (stun) takes 30. If I sinister strike, then try to pull a gouge-backstab combo, I can't, because the stun on gouge is very short - roughly 3 seconds. not enough time to regenerate the energy i used in the first attack. However, if I gouge/backstab to start and then use sinister strike when I regain enough energy, I complete all 3 attacks. You can burn down mobs by unleashing a flurry of high-energy attacks, but that draws aggro like mad in groups - thus, a bad idea.

-- Are there any spells that help to regenerate it?

No.

-- Are there items that increase your energy potential?

No.

-- Do different classes or stats affect how much energy you have?
