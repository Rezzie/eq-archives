If you're wondering if items affect stealth the same as spec points do, the answer is yes.

I posted, some time ago when detect hidden was changed, extensive data testing that I had done to determine the formula (which includes item and realm rank bonuses when comparing hider stealth skill to seeker level).

It was in the main hall, surely long since scrolled off.

But I can assure you that the game uses your stealth skill including items and realm rank bonuses when calculating stealth movement speed and detection radius of people looking for you.

One thing to note is that if you stealth, then change out items and increase your stealth spec, you have to unstealth then restealth to have the benefit of the (slightly) higher movement rate. Movement rate is calculated based on the stealth spec when you hit the stealth button, it doesn't change "on the fly". -- Huff McTuff, 41 Nightshade, Pendragon
