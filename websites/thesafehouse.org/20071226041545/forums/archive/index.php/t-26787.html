I seem to remember someone in a discussion thread on another forum mentioning its pacing being slower than 'normal', whatever that's supposed to mean. I don't mind the pacing of a story being unusually slow or fast (.hack/sign is a slower paced anime I liked, Excel Saga is a fast paced anime I liked) so long as it fits the way the story it being told. I think of it as being taken for a journey. Whether they're going to step on the gas and cover a lot of ground in a short time, or whether they'll slowly cruise through the main plot to build up suspense or curiousity and give me a good amount of time to soak up all the points is less important to me; I can be entertained both ways.

The only thing I don't like is when an anime goes fast through something then adds filler (they may as well have just taken their time through the important points and not even done the filler), and I also don't like those insanely long Dragonball type fights.
