I'll tell my very favorite PvP story.

This is an old one, back in the days when Vallon was item-loot and you still lost experience for DoTs and falls. My best friend at the time was a Gnome rogue, and she ran up to do some banking in Kelethin. But there were a bunch of elves camping the bank, and they rooted her, blinded her, and started blasting her. She just /e taps her foot. When the blindness broke she started bagging her stuff, but her health bar was dropping too fast to get all her banded in a sack. So as soon as the root broke she ran out the door and off the platform.

The bank in Kelethin is high...third level. She hit the ground and died, losing experience, but she was young and a fraction of experience was worth less to her than a nice piece of banded.

Then the server crashed. When she gets back on an hour later, she's returned to her last save point, which in this case was in MID-AIR, in the MIDDLE OF HER FALL. So the server thinks she only falls half-way, and her safe-fall is good enough for that. She hits the ground and runs off! No death, no experience loss.

I tell you, she was full of herself for a month.

I have some pretty exciting PK stories in Kelethin myself, when I was learning my fletching. My little Gnome got to be expert at jumping from one level down to another...knew all the places to do that. And I was a warrior, not a rogue, so I always hit with a crunch. I really wanted to get away, too, because I'd be running from the bank to the fletching vendor with lots of cash. It's astonishing that not one single elf ever followed me when I jumped between levels. I guess elves running around that city are just never in enough fear for their lives to do that.

Ye gads, I could go on all day =)
