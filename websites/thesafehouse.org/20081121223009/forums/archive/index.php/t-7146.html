Recently have had good xp grinidings with myself ( 65 druid) and a friend (65 necro). Halls of honor zone in / courtyard is easy enough. Harmony a few mobs, go in, Dire Charm a Wrulon Youngling.

Buff the pet up ( we have a spell that hastes the pet, and adds other melee stats, etc.) The pet now is hasted and hits for 149 max.

Pull from zone in and courtyard. Black armored guys normally ( Guardian of Marr, An Animated Guardian, A Protector of Marr)

Snare X2- 3 times. Lay about 3-5 DoTs if you wish. Sic your DC pet, 65 Necromancer friend lays on his DoTs and sics his pet as well. Kite the mob along the back wall in the graveyard to avoid a few pathing issues in the zone. Run around fast in wolf form, keeping aggro, sit and med between dots, and let the pets kick the mobs ass. With KEI, and necro mana regen, pulls should be non stop.

With 100% AA turned on, its about 3% AA xp per kill duo, and can solo just as easily for about 6% AA xp per kill. No idea on the regular xp one would receive on these mobs. Venerable Wickedneck
