Well there was a patch about mid way through Beta 5 that totally changed the stealth mechanics. Prior to the patch, it worked much as it always did in most MMOs; you would invis, then move around only having to be leery of see invis mobs. Your skills would go up as you walked around, regardless if mobs were present or not. During this time, very few mobs saw through it, I was only ever seen by mobs a few levels above me.

After the revamp, you only get skill ups if you're in the mob's frontal cone (it works out to be just shy of 180 degrees. The obvious problem here is that you get seen a lot more and it takes forever to get your skill up. However, the other change was that if you're behind the mob, it can not sense you at all, your skill and level difference to the mob is irrelevant. They also tweaked the mechanic so that the longer you manage to stay invis while attacking, the more damage you do. Post the revamp, I was able to kill things w/o getting hit in return or dropping invis significantly more often. This is a hell of a lot of fun when taking out group mobs, drop one w/o the others being any the wiser, rinse/repeat.

Hopefully they will modify the skill up mechanic so that you are given some credit outside of the mob's frontal cone, otherwise it will be very common to see a max level rogue w/ less than 100 skill in hide or sneak. Otherwise, I think this was a good fix overall, it may be annoying at times, but it does make it more challenging.

O yea, the other thing I hope they fix is how fast we move while invis. I'm continually having to drop it to keep up w/ groups. Then often end up using flash power to reinvis in order to do max damage because they had already pulled before I managed to keep up.
