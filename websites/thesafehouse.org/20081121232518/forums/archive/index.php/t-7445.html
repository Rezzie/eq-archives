Don't overlook Gale.

I know, you are thinking... Who needs that much knockback?

Since you are getting hover, try this out for size.

Hover directly over your tank, and the swarm of baddies. Cast Gale at one in the swarm.

It knocks them all down for the same amount of time they'd normally be knocked back, but they don't move anywhere. They all jsut lay down for several seconds.

Plus, you can use it in the normal way to get the badguys far away when the @#$% hits the fan, or to pile em up in the corner.

Great addition for Crowd Control.
