If you look at the armor it will have a line for Max Dex Bonus. On average, the heavier the armor the less your dex bonus could be. For example, if you have a dex of 16 and a bonus of 3 (I think thats right) and you put on a set of Full Plate, you bonus will now be 1 because that is the max for that set of armor, hence the Red 1 on your character screen.
