Well let's be fair, and give the whole situation. It may or may not make a difference, but at least you'll have all the info at hand.

First, the region 2 version of Haruhi did not get the broadcast order. When Bandai was trying to work with the distributors, they refused to let us have the broadcast order version. After a hell of a lot of debate, they allowed the broadcast order to come as a bonus, but did not allow it to be dubbed.

So the blame for this lies entirely with the Japanese company. It is somewhat unfair to rob Bandai of what they tried their hardest go bring over.

Of course, I am speaking solely from the perspective that you are boycotting the release solely on this issue. There's more to it, and ultimately, it's your money, you can do buy or not buy it for any reason.

Though, to be honest, not being able to spend $40 per DVD (after discounts) is completely understandable. However, why not buy the regular edition? The only benefit the broadcast order DVD's offer is not having to change DVD's occassionally.

By volume two, you can watch episodes 2 to five broadcast order. You'll have to skip episode one, but it is an extremely disposable (albeit, awesome) episode.

I'll be buying the limited edition myself, because the collector in me won't allow otherwise.
