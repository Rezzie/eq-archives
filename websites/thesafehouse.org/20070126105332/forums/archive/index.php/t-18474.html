Spoiler Inc.

















Sathir the Ancient is actually a bit off of the map. Follow the Wellspring River until you get to the Elder treefolk, then bear to the north west up a little rise and keep going north until you hit the tree wall. Follow the tree wall west and there will be a break in the wall on your right (north) where you can step out onto a large branch of the world tree. Sathir may be found on that branch. There will also be spiders that path along near the tree wall and the entry to the branch.
