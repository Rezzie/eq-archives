You can train herbalism at a place that is on the right after you enter the main gate.

You can train alchemy in the Gnome section, on the right after you go down the elevator.

I'm at 75 in cooking and probably won't go beyond that, since I find alchemy much more helpful on a daily basis. However, having to restock on herbs every other day is a PITA.

I wouldn't pick up too many trades as skill points do get a bit hard to come by later on...no, nevermind that. What I should say is to try out wahtever you want, but once you take one or two into Journeyman (75 SPs for alchemy alone), you'll probably be unlearning other tradeskills.

RIght now (I'm 24) I've got journeyman herbalism and alchemy, and apprentice cooking. Maybe later on when I have extra SPs (somehow) I'll take on more.
