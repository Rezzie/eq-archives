It can be used in either situation

as second chain after hamstring on an Evade and
When YOUR target evades you.

PvE most often situation #1 (though some higher mobs like Far Dorachas do evade also)

RvR most often when YOUR target, example another assassin, evades you.


As an aside it gives a nice defense bonus too.
