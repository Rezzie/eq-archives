If you want to be on a PvP server just to kill people and don't want to mess with spells, I think I'd say that monks seem to overall have better success at PvP in general than rogues, possibly because of their Mend ability. If you are willing to plow through to lvl 50 as a rogue, I think you will have an advantage over the monks. Rogues in general seem like much more fun to play, though, if you want to have fun at stuff other than PvP in the 1-50 levels, I'd go for a rogue. Running past some lighty fighting a hill giant, and instead of attacking and possibly xp killing them, instead targeting the giant and taking 30 pp of the loot the lightie was about to get, has got to be one of the most rewarding EQ experiences I've ever had.
