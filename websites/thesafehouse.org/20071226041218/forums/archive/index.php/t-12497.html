ADV announced that there won't be any new licenses announced (no suprise- they announced 15 or so titles at Anime Expo.

Personally, the only ones I'm looking forward to:

PlanetES - A space drama. It's an amazing anime about life in space. Something of a "slice of life" drama. (5/5 rating from me)

Le Portrait de Petit Cossette - A very surreal show, lots of symbolical image. I haven't watched the 2nd episode yet so I'm not sure what it's all about yet, will probably update soon as I watch the 2nd episode.

Koi Kaze - Man, I can't say enough about this series. This is one of my favorite series of all time. Mainly because it's so REAL. It is easily the most controversial anime to come to USA, ever. The artwork is beautiful. The character designs are odd, but will grow on you.

It is a story about a 22 year old man that works at a dead-end job. When he was 10 years old, he was separated from his 2 year old sister (divorce). He never saw his mother and sister again afterwards. 12 years later, his sister moves to a nearby school. In order to reduce the commute time, she moves in with her brother and father. (She is a 14 year old high school girl).

Without getting too much into the story, they fall in love with each other. They must deal with the turbulent emotions within them and find an answer on what to do about their relationship. I'll let you know this now- don't expect a, "suprise! they're not blood related!". There's no good convinent excuse to make this relationship "okay" by moral standards.

They must fight against their feelings and the people around them and come upon a decision.

It is truly a great anime that only comes once a decade. Most people will not watch it due to what is being explored in the anime. It was quite an emotional roller-coaster that put Kimi ga Nozomu Ein to shame.

Can you tell I'm highly anticipating it?! :p

Not sure what the other licensing companies announced. There's rumors of Asagiri no Miko (Maidens of the Morning Mist), and Da Capo being licensed. All in all, a decent announcement.
