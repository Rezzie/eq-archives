Reverenced Ire is a pre-release guild for the game Vanguard Saga of Heroes. Our vision is to bring together a guild comprised of mature, competent, skilled, and respectable veteran MMO Gamers that will ultimately dominate high end content.

Upon Release of the game we will be focusing on character development, progression, and the journey to get us to our end goal of high end content domination. We will be accomplishing this through teamwork, support of one another, our past experiences and knowledge, our ability to skillfully play, and our determination to succeed. We intend on providing a fun environment, one that builds teamwork, trust, and camaraderie. To sum it up we are a core to hardcore raiding guild that has a laid back casual tone to it.

We will not be doing excessive grinding and treadmilling just to get to max level as soon as possible. Rather we are going to make the effort to fully develop our characters, explore the world, get a firm understanding of the game mechanics and dynamics all the while continuing our progression and outfitting our selves in preparation for the end game and our goal.

Ten things to know about Reverenced Ire:
1. We are a Team.
2. We are Active.
3. We Support Each other
4. Attitudes are Not Wanted
5. Greed is Unwelcome
6. We Raid
7. We will take on the most challenging
8. We may die several times to accomplish our goal
9. We will never quit or give up
10. We will succeed

If we sound like a guild that you may be interested in we invite you to drop by our site and have a look around. Please be sure to read the Creed and Charter. We may be found at http://www.reverenced-ire.com

We also host the weekly Sunday night Vanguard Community Vent Discussion, so if you want to talk to any of our members first hand about the guild you can usually catch at lest one of us every Sunday night on Ventrilo @ 8pm EST (-5 GMT).
