You can repair the item as many time as you like...until the durability drops too low. Then the smith will give some message along the lines of "I cannot repair that."

Each time you repair, the durability drops (well I've seen exceptions). Based on my experience, the quality of the items is a factor in how large a durability hit the item recieves when repaired. Dropped items (80-89% qual) can take as much as 7-10% hit per repair, while quested items (usually 100%), may only take 2-3%, or none at all.

Once durability reaches 0%, the item can no longer be repaired.
