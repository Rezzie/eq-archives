Got hit by this a little bit earlier, and while thankfully, he only got away with one bag of items that were just trade stuff from myself and two friends, he also snagged two items I was selling for Shadowcross, which sucks

Anyhow, here's how it works. You're selling some stuff in few bags. The person comes up, asks to see the bags. Ok, you show em. They then cancel, and say ok, I want A, B, C, X, Y, and Z (if they're smart, as this person was, they ask for the least valuable items). You stick em in the window. Then they ask to see the other bag (usually the one with the most valuable stuff in it) again to check something. So you put that in the window. Then they push cancel. Ooops! You've just been scammed EQ drops items into open inventory spaces first - this of course means that the bag(s) will drop, and they'll be spamming click to grab em.
