Once you clear out the jails of the normal mobs you can start the ring.

I would suggest a well balanced group in each jail and a Paladi or SK between the two which I will explain later.

Once you start the event 3 spell jammers will spawn in each jail. You need to kill these as fast as possible and mez the skellies while you kill them. When they are dead in your jail then you kill the skeletons. I think there are 3 waves of spell jammers in each jail so a total of 18 to kill. When you started the even a 'Warder of Life' spawned between the jails and is untargettable. The Paladin/SK/Warrior needs to watch that for the second it is targettable and get aggro from it. If you do not it will go in the cells and kill the two NPCs. When that happens both jails repop instantly with the normal grimlings that were in there and you have failed. I don't remember the exact strategies on the warder but he was lvl 65 and didn't hit all that hard and fast and was slowable if I remember.
