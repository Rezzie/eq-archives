Level 41, I've run out of mana twice. Ever.

Situations where we should be chain-casting our DD instead of getting into melee are freakishly rare. They happen, or they can if you remember that you can do it, but it's not something to base your character choice around.

I don't believe that dex is figured into our chance for hitting. That might have something to do with bolts, but mostly it's the creatures resistance and level. Higher dex does decrease casting time, though, so we can get our spell off relatively quickly.

You won't ever run out of mana unless you are purposely standing back and nuking nonstop for about a minute.

When in a group pulling or solo, at best (barring pathing wierdness) you can get off 4 casts before the mob is in melee range, and you usually have full mana again by the time fight is over and your endurance is back.
