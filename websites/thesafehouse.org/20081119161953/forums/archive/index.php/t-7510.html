2) Land a Backstab and Poison at the same time

Easy, eh?

You're already way ahead.

Da Boss's Guide to killing Dr00ds:

1. Get your MR to 90+

2. Get your FR as high as possible (70+ is great, 50+ will do)

3. Bring a sow potion with a couple charges. Make sure you drink one before the fight when he isn't looking

4. Get the best bow you can, and some nice arrows (I hope your archery skill is good.)

5. Have a fully charged pupmice stone hot-keyed in inventory
If you get snared, target self and hit that baby till you get it off you, timing is key with the increased cast time. Also, try to get a couple of pumices in on him right off the bat, hopefully you'll kill his damage sheild.

6. Open with Muscle Lock (Snare) poison, this will kill his sow, and give you a HUGE advantage.

7. Stay on him like white on rice, and land dem backstabs.

8. If his damage shield is owning you, back off and bow kite him while you wait for your backstab to rececyle, before moving in to melee again, backstab, back off, rinse and repeat.

9. Try to kill him before his DoT chews away your HP's, if you're DoTed, without any buffs above it, then pumice it off ASAP.
