It's a story of a spanishi biker who is working on one of the three european grand tours. He is a second (or whatever you call it, the person who helps the lead win). But he is presented with an opportunity to win it for himself.

It's a heartwarming and very interesting story. The storytelling style was very unique and refreshing, and there was a very nostilagic feel about it that I could not pinpoint.

I really recommend it, it's 45 minutes long stand-alone OVA.
