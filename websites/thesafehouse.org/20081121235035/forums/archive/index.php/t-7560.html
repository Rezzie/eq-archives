Well I've only played on RZ primarily, so can't tell you much about the team servers, other then that they are an entirely different world :)

On Rallos, yes, past about level 15-20 or so, the random PK'ing drops off quickly, but it's never completely gone. There are plenty of unwritten rules, and I could probably write a book on them, and when they do/don't apply, the variations, etc. you pretty much have to play and just learn them hehe. Never played on a blue server, I can't be more then vague about the differences there either. Generally, random PK'ing is frowned on, but there are guilds who have that as their primary goal. These people are often despised, especially those who use less then honorable tactics to accomplish their goals. There are guilds who oppose these people (like mine).

On occasion people will form groups to hunt other people, but on RZ it's not really that common. Because of only a +-4 level range, you can't always find people to hunt, and tracking them down is hard as well. Plus there are many ways to escape, especially if your a caster.

Rogues are very good at PvP, moreso if they are the attacker. With a good backstab, a quad, poison, and a successful disarm you can really knock an opponent off their feet if they aren't paying attention. Lag kills ya though :/

Anyway I suggest trying it out on Rallos, play to at least 20 before deciding... if you can handle it ;p
