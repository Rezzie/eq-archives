That's not bad my friend - you kill yellows probably 90% of the time it sounds like. That's very good actually, but not good enough to solo because you are right - one death wipes out an hour's worth of work.

IMHO, you have specced fine, you do very well, and that's just how it is. Other classes may solo better. Also, remember there are high yellows and low yellows, and no way to tell the difference. Also, pick mobs that you get a plus against and they get a minus against you. This makes a *huge* difference.

Finally, I will add there is one way to tell if you should run that works pretty well. I was soloing blues and yellows last night, and I noticed that if a mob hit me for over 100, it was going to be a very close fight. If I PAed for less than 180, same deal. So with yellow con mobs, I would PA and the mob would swing at me. If my PA did less than 180 and he hit me for over 100, I would hit Creeping Death and sprint my ass off during the stun. Then I could come back later and kill him when he was wounded. I had no deaths using that guideline.
