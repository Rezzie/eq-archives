Inside Dargun's Tomb there are some decently good Rogue armor drops. The only ones I suggest going after are the chest and legs.

Legs:
Greaves of the Slayer
Item level 36
207 Armor Class
+26 Dexterity
+13 Strength
+52 Hit Points
+1 Melee Accuracy
+1 Melee Damage




Chest:

Tunic of the Slayer
Item level 38
274 Armor Class
+34 Strength
+28 Physical Resistance
+17 Dexterity
+69 Hit Points
+1 Melee Accuracy
+1 Melee Damage




The legs are a rare drop from a named called Blackmoon. The chest piece drops from a named (can't remember) right outside the hall where Dargun is.

Happy hunting. =)
