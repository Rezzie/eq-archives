Below is the complete post on PvP changes taken from the darkenbane web site..

PVP News from Minneapolis Fan Faire

NOTE: All of this information is from my notes during the pvp discussion SIG
at the Fan Faire and may or may not be Lady Daegarmo's exact words or
meaning. Take special care that some of the more important rule changes
are not yet in effect and violating existing policy can still get you banned
until the new rules are officially announced.

First, general news:

There were approximately 1200 attendees (according to the hotel staff).
Nine folks from Rallos, 25 or so from TZ and an unknown number (at least to
me) from VZ. The RZ and TZ teams combined to do the "real life quest."

ALL ZEKS (RZ/TZ/VZ)

* In approximately one week, pvp "rules" will be as on Sullon Zek.
In other words, there are no rules with respect to PVP. Standard
blue server rules apply and sexual/racial harassment will not be
tolerated. Everything else (training/bind point camping/exp killing,
etc) will be completely legal. No pvp related petitions will be
handled by the CS staff. The standard blue server type problems
such as lost corpses, bugs, lost items, etc will still be handled.

* showeq is actively being pursued to be "fixed"

* Classes are being re-examined for viability in a pvp environment.
LD specifically mentioned rangers as being one of the classes that
is woeful in pvp.

* LD is pushing for the return of egg shaped pumice on vendors for
approx 200P each.

* It was noted that Harm Touch and Lay Hands are on the same spell
function with opposite effects so there's no way to give LH back
to paladins on death while restricting the same recycle on HT.


PVP Race War Servers (TZ/VZ)

* Cross teaming is coming to an end. Teams (see later notes) will be
LOCKED DOWN. There will be no ability to crossteam at all. No
grouping, guilding, buffing, etc with non-team members. This
lockdown is probably 60 days out.

* The lockdown will entail a guild wipe. All guilds will be deleted
and must be reformed. There will be extra staff for the first few
days after the wipe to assist in this endeavor.

* It is highly likely that common (the language) will be removed from
the racewar servers for general communication use (ie, public
channels). Training in non-team languages will also receive very
low caps.

* Strong possibility of adding a long (40 or so hours) quest to change
your team. Certain classes/races would not be allowed to join certain
teams (such as a Paladin joining the evil team).

* TZ likely to be changed to a 2 team server (Good vs Evil).

* VZ may be changed to a 3 team server.

* statistics that are interesting: 45% of the players on TZ are evil
races. 39-40% on VZ are darkies. A mere 13% of TZ is shorty and 95%
of the epic weapons on these servers have been obtained by cross-
teaming guilds.

* It is not part of the Vision to allow a 1 time team change without
the previously mentioned quest.

Rallos Zek News:

* Rallos will always remain coin + 1 item loot.

* Code for rain spells is currently being changed to not effect your pet
or your immediate group.

* Big item is the player enforced pvp rules mentioned above.

Other PVP News:

* Sullon Zek will be live within 3 weeks.

* There's discussion about adding a new Guildwar server with no "teams"
but lockdowns based on your guild. Sounds like a very interesting
extension/refinement of what RZ currently is.

* On Sullon, the new summon corpse mechanism for good races will be
obtained via a quest which likely involves PVP/crests of fallen
opponents.

That's all I took notes on. This was the first time I met LD and I was
impressed by his enthusiasm for PVP. I certainly don't agree with everything
he proposes or campaigns for but it's nice having a pvp advocate at
Verant. My thanks for the time and patience.
