Love the instanced dungeons, there's lots of opportunity for quests and exp in them, and you don't have to worry about someone else 'camping' the instance. VC wasn't bad, lots of fun to do the first time through, after that, its fun to walk n00bs through it &lt;grins>.

(( STOP READING HERE IF YOU DON'T WANT TIPS FOR VC INSTANCE ))

The trick to Mr. Smite is to first kill all of the stealther mobs on the platform where the chest is (2, I believe), then pull him back onto the bridge as far as possible, and kill all of the guys that come with him before you start to kill him... at 2 or 3 points during the battle (I believe 75%, 50%, and 25% life) he stomps (semi-AE stun that hits all the party members despite range) and runs back to the chest to change weapons. Pulling him well back onto the dock means that it takes enough time for him to run back to the chest for his weapons that by the time he gets back, you're not stunned any more, and can be ready to fight again.

As far as VC himself goes, have your highest level rogue go into the hut where he is, and sap (or ambush) one of the stealther mobs to the sides of the doorway to start the fight, and kill the rogues before you start on VC. When he hits 50% health, he calls two more stealther buddies into the fight, it's best to have a tank ready to pull them off of whoever they aggro, and then kill them before finishing VC off, as they have much less health, even in his weakened state, and combined, do more damage than him.

(( END HINTS ))

Also, in Gnomeregan, at the bottom of the instanced section is a mob which drops (possibly every time) a unique blue named dagger, called Toxic Ravager, that is 20+ dps 1.9? spd, and has an AE poison (5% strike chance) effect. A high level guildie killed him while I was outside the instance, and all I had to do was join the party, go inside, stealth up and jump off the edge to where he was in order to pick up the dagger (I did this at 27 or so, which is about the level of the dagger).
