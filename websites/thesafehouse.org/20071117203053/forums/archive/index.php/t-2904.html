Call of Cthulhu is based on the horror novels of H.P. Lovecraft. It is set in a universe where mankind is an ephemeral incident in an ancient universe. The Gods are an evil, hostile set of TTMWNMTKs, and there are a load of ancient powerful near unstoppable beings called Great Old Ones. Who are sleeping or imprisioned about the place. Cthulhu is one of these.

One of the premises of the game is that humanity exists in a sort of cosy delusion of importance, and as the truth becomes known the human mind is unable cope and goes mad.

Magic involves calling upon these beings and their servitor races, and is essentially demon summoning and tapping of power. You can play a magic user, but you will have a relativly short career. And that's relative to a bunch of people with the life expectancy of a soap herring.
