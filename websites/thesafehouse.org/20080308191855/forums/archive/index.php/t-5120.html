Couldn't really say about mastery of arms, but I strongly suggest getting mastery of pain.

As an infiltrator I have mop 3 and have noticed a dramatic increase in critical hits. Not only am I getting more crits in, but they seem to be higher damage on the crits. With mop 3 I would guess that I crit on about 25-30% of my attacks and for pretty good damage.

I normally use fast weapons, so when I crit it is not that high, but I can count on 3-4 crts per fight. As a 2-hand critblade I would definately take this ability. You are basing your character to do big damage per hit, having a 25% chance to do up to double damage seems like it would go along with your character profile pretty well. Also, you would have many of those oh so fun 1700 damage perfs that piss people off so much(against non-epic armor opponents of course)

I think the 2-hand critblade is still a very viable template, but be warned, that you will be hurting at high level against opponents with epic armor.
