"Detect Hidden," the ability you get by training stealth, does not actually do much -- it increases the minimum range at which you see archer/minstrels from 125 to 250. Of course, 250 == next to nothing, so this won't help you much.

"See Hidden," the realm ability, is part of the 1.50 patch and allows you to see archer/minstrels at all times.
