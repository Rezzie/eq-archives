ひぐらしのなく頃に - Higurashi no naku koro ni, can be written in kanji as well, 蜩
Higurashi - Cicadas(or one of the species of them)
no - Particle 「の」 is often called the "possessive" indicator. 「の」 follows a noun or a noun substitute to indicate a thing, person, place, or category that the following noun belongs to.
naku - to cry
koro - 頃 (n,n-adv,n-suf,uk) time, about, toward, approximately (time)
ni - Particle 「に」 follows a noun or a noun substitute to indicate a point or a very small area of location. English translation can be "on", "in", "at" , "to", and "from".

in case anyone wanted some literal stuff.

there is more to "no" and "ni" but there is no need to bring it up here.
