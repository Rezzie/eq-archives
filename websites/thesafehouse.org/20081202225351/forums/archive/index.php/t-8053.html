Likewise, Im not sure what the GMAV has to do with the merchants (but after reading this thread I tried various chit-chat brandishing my GMAV too --- in vain )

ATM, I am of the opinion that the Sketch Book will contain 2-4 lost pages wherein our recipes are contained.

A seperate issue is the creation of our Advanced Poison Vials, if indeed the Advanced Vial Sketch is for poison making. I have been working on this problem with my friend a GM potter but we havent had any failures or successful combines yet using pre-PoP supplies. Our best guess is that there is an additional POP ingrediant still unknown which we have yet to try.

The Recipe for a Poison Vial is simple:
Small Block of Clay
Sealed | Lined | Poison Vial Sketch
Water Flask
HQ | MQ Pelt

creates in a Pottery Wheel an Unfired Vial which combines with a HQ Sheet in a Kiln to create the Vial.

The Recipe for an Advanced Vial probably works similarly:
Small Block of Clay or Block of Tanaan Clay
Advanced Vial Sketch
Water Flask
HQ | MQ Pelt

but with the addition of some unknown PoP ingrediant. Right now we are tracking down some Tainted Planar Essence to try some different combinations, possibly with celestial solvent as well.
