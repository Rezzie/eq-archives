On the FV server we (Fire and Fury) have beaten this raid twice now. We failed the first time figuring out how it works. It is simple in theory, but a bit more difficult to carry out. The first step is to clear most of the dungeon up to the three boss mobs. When they are the only ones remaining, split your army into three parts and send each to a boss. You will want to distribute your raid force into relatively equal dps output. There are a few catches, however. The shadow boss, for example, has considerably higher armor class than the other two, so you will want to use mainly caster dps on this one. The other two also have weaknesses/immunities. After engaging the three at the same time, one person from each section would call the health percentage of their boss mob. If one group is killing too quickly, have that section lighten up on the dps. However, there is a minimum dps that must be maintained to keep the boss hp from reseting. So don't stop attacking if you are way ahead of the other groups... just slow down a bit. It takes a bit of time to get your groups right, and we had to send members back and forth to control dps accordingly. Try to keep the bosses within 10% health of each other. We haven't determined the exact threshold, but it is somewhere between 10% and 19%. If at any time their health is beyond this threshold, they will imediately reset to full life. Fortunately, the mobs don't do too much damage and you can leave the rooms to regroup if need be. It takes some patience but after a few tries you should be able to take them all down.

Good luck
