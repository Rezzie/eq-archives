This is a pretty old post, but i'll stick my two cents in anyhow...
first off, lvl 19 is pretty late to start grabbin gear for a 19 bg twink, i started on my rogue at lvl 10...being Horde, imo you should have been running Wailing Caverns, very nice pair of leggings (Legging of the Fang), a decent cloak (Lizardscale Cloak) and a nice bashy weapon with a poison proc serpent something cant recall the name.

as for a rogue, i'd be on the lookout for anything with an Agi/Sta stat and slapping that on, Forest leather bracers are nice, Scouting gloves of the monky or agility are also good. i also fished my ass off and entered the Fishing tournament in booty bay, got lucky and got my hands on a very nice hat with +15 stamina(Lucky Fishing Hat). if your not into fishing, i'd take up engineering, get to 150 and make yourself some Green Tinted Goggles.

my rogue is alliance so i got lucky and got me a Tunic of Westfall, pretty nice piece of chest armor, as for boots i went with the Feet of the Lynx, belt i went deviate scale belt which was pretty cheap considering i got the mats for the belt myself and had a guildie slap it together for me. well anyhow, good luck with your rogue and if your still running in the 10-19 bg's im sure we'll run into each other somewhere out there.

BTW, a very nice site with names and locations of twink rogue gear is on www.shadowpanther.net nice site and easy reference to thottbot.
