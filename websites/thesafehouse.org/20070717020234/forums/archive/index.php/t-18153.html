Try 21/30, or 20/31, which has become semi popular in my guild since the seal fate fix.

You can and should include riposte in your combat skills. Also grab improved kick and a weapon spec (your choice, mace/dagger/sword, each has their own advantages). The difference between the two is adrenaline rush vs cold blood. I've heard good things about adren. rush (ss spam for the win) and cold blood is a game over button usually, although if you miss it hurts. I'd say take adren rush if you are going maces or swords, cold blood if daggers.
