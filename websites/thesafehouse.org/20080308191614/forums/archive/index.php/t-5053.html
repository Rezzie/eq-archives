" I dont think you can perform evade style indefinitely when the target is stunned"

If the last thing they did before getting stunned was attack and get evaded by you, then yes, you can perform evade styles until their next swing. It's not indefinite, though, since diminishing returns code halves the duration of any sleep effect every time it is used on the same target after the first, and players can only be stunned once per minute.

Here is purely fictional situation situation:

You are soloing. There is nothing else for the mob to aggro/hit. You evade. The mob suddenly stops hitting for the rest of the battle. You can use the weapon style that chains off of evade as many times as you want until that mob does something.

Another purely fictional situation:

You are in a group. The mob your group is fighting turns to you and you evade, but then turns back to the main tank. You can use the style that chains off of evade as long as the mob has not done anything else. Point: just because your evade was the last action the mob performed on you, does not mean that it was the last action performed by the mob. "Here is a hand full of warnings..
