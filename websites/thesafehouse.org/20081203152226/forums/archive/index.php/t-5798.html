June 15-16, 2001
Minneapolis Marriott City Center
The Safehouse's Trivia ContestSaturday, June 16, 2001.
Starts at 9:00 p.m.
Contest RulesThe 'quiz' consists of 27 general knowledge multiple-choice questions
Teams of 1-4 people allowed per quiz.
Participants have 30 minutes to complete the quiz
You may not leave the room once the contest has started unless you are done
The team that has the highest score will advance to the final round
An answer sheet is available after the contest

Final RoundThe final round consists of 10 oral questions.
The individuals on the winning team(s) will complete solo
Each individual will answer by writing the answer in a notebook and all participants will show the answer simultaneously
If no clear winner is received at the end of the 10 questions, two more questions have been prepared to break the tie

PrizesFirst Prize: Original Artwork by Keith Parkinson, Special Edition Safehouse T-Shirt, mug, mousepad
Second Prize: Special Edition Safehouse T-Shirt, mug, mousepad
Third Prize: Special Edition Safehouse T-Shirt, mousepad
Fourth Prize: Special Edition Safehouse T-Shirt

I'd say most of the questions are quite challenging, and the players who are familiar with the entire world of Norrath will definitely have an advantage. There are only a couple of rogue specific questions since there is certainly a plethora of player types come here to the safehouse.

Of the administrators, Glip did quite well, getting over 80% of the questions right. Zato on the otherhand... well, he's a dwarf.

I will be posting the quiz on the Safehouse soon after I return. Oh, and no, several of the questions are <u>not</u> in the compendium.
