This quest was just added last patch to change the bind point of Call of Qeynos and Call of the Overlord spells that port you back to town. I didn't do the Freeport quest, but did do the Qeynos quest. Hopefully, someone can add the Freeport quest.

There is a new npc, erudite, standing by the lighthouse. When you talk to him, he'll give ya a bracelet looking object. You'll have to solve 4 riddles, visiting those areas in Antonica.

First one is about water, so go to the aqueduct between Oracle Tower and Windstalker village. There is a tree right by the aqueduct that you can "climb" to get up on the aqueduct. On the aqueduct, you'll see an orb. Touch it, get the update and you'll auto pop back on the ground near the tree. I believe the loc is -1400, -360.

Second one is about air. Go on a griffon ride. Don't know if it makes any difference, but I took the Oracle Tower to Steppes Station.

Third one is about fire. Touch the campfire at Sir Jeager's camp. He is near the ocean by Blackburrow. Paladin in shiny armor with a little squire boy at a camp.

Fourth one is about earth. Just walk on Qeynos Highway. This is the main path/road through Antonica. As soon as you get to the road, it will update.

After this is all done, you'll learn a new skill called Set Bind Point. It is in your abilities tab under knowledge. Apprentice II level.

I set my bind point in Starcrest Commune, in the bank/inn, right next to my door to my room. :)
