here's a sure-fire template for skillchain macro:

/wait 3
/ws "Weapon Skill" &lt;bt>(or &lt;t>)

press as soon as you see "Soandso readies Weaponskill"

If you can't keep up with the battle spam, try:

/wait 2
/ws "Weapon Skill" &lt;bt>(or &lt;t>)

press as soon as you see your buddy's weaponskill animation

Example:

For Skillchain: Detonation
A warrior readies Raging Axe, you hit your macro
/wait 3
/ws "Gust Slash" &lt;bt>
/party Using Gust Slash on &lt;bt>!

In addition, if your party has a monk that can perform One Inch Punch, you get to have Gravitation as your 2nd chain.....annnnnd, if your party has a mage that can cast Earth or Dark spells, you get a Magic Burst for even more damage.
