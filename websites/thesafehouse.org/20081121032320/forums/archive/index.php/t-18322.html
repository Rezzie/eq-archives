This quest starts in Nektulos forest, occasionally while killing the undead (and unconfirmed - mist grinns) you'll get charred bones. Right and Left tibia/femur, and ribcage. (chest drops.)

Upon examination, each bone will go into your quest journal separately. Once you have all 5 collected, visit Private Doopy (outside Gul'thex Citadel, northern portion) He's a wee little halfling ghost hiding behind a stone.

For each bone you bring him, you recieve 15-17 silver and a bit of experience. After you've brought him all 5 bones, he sends you on a quest to deliver his skull to his war chest. The chest is located in a halfling camp. from the top of N'marr's Ascent, take the western road until you reach a gate with some undead halflings. In the back you'll find his chest.

The reward for this step is 8gold,30ish silver and a minor item.
all told the quest brings in roughly 9.5 gold :) very good for a mid 20's quest doable by an exp group
