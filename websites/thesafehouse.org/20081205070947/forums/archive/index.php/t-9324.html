For enhancements, you're looking at /manage

The keybinding would be /bind p manage

I've got no idea on mission list, but I'll look into it.

One other binding you might want to consider is the ones for changing chat channels. I'm proud of this one: "/bind alt+r chat_set r" goes to Request channel. Rinse and repeat for alt+t, f, g, v, and b -- and you now have an easy way to switch between channels.

Two things about this:
1. alt+g and alt+v are to supergroup (chat_set s) and local (chat_set l), respectively, but it's easier to have them all bunched up into 2 nice rows.
2. As of now, when you zone, the channel resets to the one you've last clicked on w/ your mouse. Don't know why. Just keep this in mind, or you'll entertain the Request channel for a while.

I know this is a lot of work, but it ends up being worth it in the long run. At least if you want to talk about how much you hate your team to your SG, you can cycle the chats easily. Also, if you set it up for one character, you can easily load it into the rest.
