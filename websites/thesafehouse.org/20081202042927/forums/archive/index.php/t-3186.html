Here's my character (sorry it's long, just thought I'd lay out all the details in case people wanted to know how choices worked out)

Class: Pure Cleric (level 8 )
Race: Human
Starting Stats...
Str: 14 (16-19 w/ buffs)
Con: 14 (16-19 w/ buffs)
Dex: 12
Int: 10
Wis: 14 (added 2 points at level ups, so it's 16 now)
Cha: 14 (16-19 w/ buffs)

Domains: Healing, Sun
Feats: Martial Weapons, Extra Turning, Extend Spell, Toughness

Spell setups:
level 0: All Light, but usually get cast as cure minor.
Level 1: Bless &amp; Protection from evil, but usually get cast as cure light.
level 2: If I'm in a group, bull's strength &amp; endurance. When soloing, all cure serious (searing light if fighting tough undead).
level 3: Extended Bull's strength, Endurance, Eagle's Grace. Other two slots for healing or more buffs depending.
level 4: All summon creature IV (I play on a server w/ summon durations much closer to PHB values)

Skills: Mainly focused on Concentration &amp; Lore. A little bit in Healing, and 1 point in open locks &amp; remove traps.

With the extended duration buffs, my cleric ends up with more HP than some fighters I group with, and is able to put out similar damage. She doesn't have the feats to go crazy w/ cleave or anything, but it allows her to hold her ground in a fight. Concentration is high enough that she can still get off her healing spells while in the thick of combat.

She freaking obliterates undead. The stuff she can't turn (ie: skeletal warriors) get lasered with searing light spells (1d8/level, no save) while her pet keeps them occupied. With the high charisma, and sun domain, she can destroy screens full of lesser undead, which aside from being really funny, is also great XP. She gets 12-13 turns per day depending on how high she rolls on the charisma buff, which is way plenty (I've never run out).
