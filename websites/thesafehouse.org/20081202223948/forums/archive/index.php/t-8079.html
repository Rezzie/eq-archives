Nobody knows yet, but according to:
pub146.ezboard.com/fthesa...2461.topic
you are only told the recipe for celestial suspensions if you have a Grandmaster Assassin's Seal. To get the seal requires that you make a Grandmaster Assassin's Vial:
pub146.ezboard.com/fthesa...5768.topic
To make the vial requires a minimum skill of 225 (don't know if that's confirmed or not, but I have seen reports that it cannot be done at 201, and it can be done at 240).

So, in order for the NPC to tell you the recipe, you must have at least 225 skill, have spent the roughly 5kpp required to make a vial, and done the seal quest. You should probably work on those steps before trying the Advanced Poisons.
