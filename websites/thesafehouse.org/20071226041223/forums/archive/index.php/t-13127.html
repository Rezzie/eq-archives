Gunslinger Girl.

Cute name, right? Makes you think of some cheesy sappy 'girls with guns' fanservice show, right?

Umm... How do I put this...
.....
.....
Okay, I cant figure out how to tell you what I'm thinking about this hauntingly beautiful yet manic-depressive series that dug itself into my brain and refuses to let go. Thankfully, I managed upon a site that pretty much says what I have to say:

Review (NOT WORK SAFE) (http://www.therossman.com/rrr/anime/gunslinger.html)

Okay, you read that and are thinking 'it can't be THAT messed up'. Oh, Evangelion only wishes they would have thought of something like this. I'll shut up before I ramble, HOWEVER, if you are tired of seeing anime where everyone ends up happy, even if said people all have no limbs and live in a cave full of hentai monsters... This is your ticket. Want an analogy? Fine! 'Grave of the Fireflies' meets 'Evangelion'. Now go watch. Now. Preferably with someone you love. With the lights on. And lots of comfort food. And the number to a mental health clinic nearby.

I kind of accidentally DL'd it knowing nothing about the series, and I'm convinced it's one of the greatest accidents I've ever had, minus the self-doubt and nightmares. Oh well! ^_^
