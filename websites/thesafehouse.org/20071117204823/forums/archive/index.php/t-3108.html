Hmm .. a Welsh coastal town that is supposed to relate to desolation and loss? Well .. Port Talbot springs to mind

Actually a lot of Welsh coastal towns are at river mouths, so just name a river something gutteral and add 'Aber' (pro. ab-er) to the start which means 'mouth of ..'.
Of, if it's a fortified town, just name it something gutteral and add 'Caer' (pro. care) to the start which means 'fort ..' (I think, or is it town?). E.g Caerdydd (pro. care-Deeth) (capital of Wales, called Cardiff in English)
