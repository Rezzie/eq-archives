Snare poison is probably your best choice, if you can kill him before it wears off. If you can't, though...well, you're in for a long, hard fight.

If you can't kill him before the poison wears off, or he resists, the first thing you have to do is make sure you kill his pet. It will interrupt your "spells" every single time unless you do, since you have no channeling skill. This is the single most important piece of advice I can give you.

Get pumice or a golem wand to cancel his malo and epic dot.

Get cure poison and disease potions. You'll need them, and probably need them badly.

Get a fungi, and get your MR/PR/DR up as much as you can, at the expense of AC if necessary. Wear as much HP gear as you can, too.
