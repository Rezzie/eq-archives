>>If you don't keep env up... does it eventually become useless? Like will a *lvl 10 env skill w/ lvl 10 poison* NOT have any affect on a lvl 35 guy? Or will it still do the same (albeit very weak) dmg / str debuff / snare time / etc?

Resists are based on your character level, as stated by Iriki.

However, in relative terms, it will become steadily weaker. With that I mean if you are level 10 with a 10 envenom vs a yellow con (level 10 mob), it'll do full. At 50 with 10 envenom vs a 50 target... 10 envenom vs 50 target level means you'll be doing less. The poison didn't really get weaker... if you managed to stick it on a level 50 at level 10, it would have done the same amount. But don't expect a poison that does 18 per tick to yellows when you are level 10 to do 18 per tick to yellows when you are 40. Won't happen.

A low level strength debuff won't bother a level 35 guy unless you've got a high level of envenom (in which case you'd probably be using a higher level str debuff). The guy will also have better gear (upping his str), better buffs (if buffed) and stat gains (assuming his class gains str as he levels). So, yah, some things like str debuffs become useless. Damage poison... well, doing 10 per tick for 5 ticks isn't much compared to a level 50's hp. Others like disease will continue to last long enough and so remain effective.
