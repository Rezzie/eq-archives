One easy thing to do in order to see if you really care if the gloves are blue/green/grey. Notice how much AF you have on the stats page, take off the gloves, check how much AF went down. An even better test is if you have orange gloves, check out the difference between your grey ones and those. The first thing you will notice is that gloves contribute very little to effective AF. IMHO they contribute so little to effective AF that you shouldn't look at the color at all, only at the stats they give you. The same goes for boots and to a lesser degree, hats.
