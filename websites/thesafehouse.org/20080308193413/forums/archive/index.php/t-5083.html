EZ_Ciardha Sidhe
05-25-02, 07:23 AM
PA does significantly more damage than BS2.

BS2 only has one follow-up move, Thigh Cut. PA has two follow-up moves, Creeping Death and Stunning Stab. Both Creeping Death and Stunning Stab do significantly more damage than Thigh Cut.

BS2 stun is very short. PA has no stun, but Creeping Death has a much better stun, around 6 seconds or so.

BS2 and Thigh Cut both have defense penalties. PA has a defense penalty, but Stunning Stab has a defense bonus.

PA is stealthed from the front. BS2 is stealthed from the back. Sometimes it's more convenient to use one or the other rather than try to get on the other side of someone/something. Ciardha Sidhe, Elven Nightshade of Guinevere
