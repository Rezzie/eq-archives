It is NOT true that you will pick all coin first, then some item (be it the epic pieces or anything else off a mob). It's purely a function of random chance.

The first check is to see if you successfully pickpocket the mob. If that check is successful, the next check is to see what "item" you pick. That can be any non-magic, non-nodrop item in the mob's inventory, or one of the coin types (platinum, gold, silver, or copper). If it's one of the items, you get that item; if it's one of the coin types, you get 50% of the remaining coin of that type. It's as simple as that...

I think why you may have been under the impression that it worked the way you described, is that if the mob only has one item, but coins of all 4 types, you are much more likely to pick some coins than pick the item. And even After you've picked a particular coin type, you only got 50% of that coin, so you can still pick from that coin type again.

The resaon most epic-guides tell you to "pick the mob until all the coin is gone and then pick him 30 more times" is simply to tell you should be persistant in picking until you are fairly certain that the mob doesn't have the epic piece. Not that you have to first empty the mob of all coin and the "good stuff" will get picked...

Gratz on the notes, and good luck on the rest of the epic quest !!
