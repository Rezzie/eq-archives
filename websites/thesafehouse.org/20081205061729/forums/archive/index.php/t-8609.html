what faction do you need to spawn him, or can you spawn him while sneaking?

You can spawn him KoS. What I did was clear the whole smuggler camp before spawning Anson, which is possible when Anson's PH is up. It'd be a bit tougher once he's up since you'd probably aggro him while clearing the smugglers near him. If the human chick is up, those mobs around her wouldn't be killable unless you try lull. Just drop hide with sneak on and stay behind the smugglers, say the phrase to spawn him, then re hide. You shouldn't get aggro if you're quick.

Here's the log I posted a few months back. We did 58k in melee damage. 2 rogues, 1 warrior, 1 cleric, and a 2boxed druid for DS and spot heals. He has some insane resists so melee heavy is better. My 100dd proc was landing for 20-30 when it did land. On our other Stanos head farmings we brought some wizards and they were getting numerous resists, even with lure spells. Our elemental equipped warrior was using evasive and Stanos still did 320 dps which is probably one of the highest "old world" mob damage dealers.
