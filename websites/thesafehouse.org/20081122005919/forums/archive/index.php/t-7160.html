Taken from this thread at Paladins of Norrath:

No one at PoN has provided any information about what the "key" conversation thread means. The slightly easier one is from the "Clerics" thread. This one suggests that Millius wants a Deepwater Emblem. As it turns out, a Deepwater Symbol is apparently dropped in Gunthak (haven't found one myself, but others have seen it). Unfortunately, turning it in to Millius apparently causes him to take it without saying anything (this has apparently happened even when paladins hand it in)...
There's also a generic female Erudite ghost that is a rare spawn on the beach. I've seen exactly one myself, and others have reported it as well. It cons indifferent, and does not respond to hails. After hailing it multiple times, I killed the one I found to see if it would drop anything, but didn't find anything. Its possible that its tied to the quest, although since it doesn't respond to hails, and doesn't appear to drop anything, I'm clueless.

Millius Darkwater does indeed start the quest with the above dialogue.

Head east off the rock to the beach. There is a tunnel leading up from the back of the beach which has a fork left to a fort. Take that fork left, then kill the multi race mauraders, pillages and other piratey sounding names until a nodrop "deepwater emblem" drops.

Take this emblem back to Millius, he hands over "Alina's emblem" and will say to find Alina's spirit in the zone somewhere.

She is actually "erudite spirit" (female form) and spawns at neg 840, neg 675, beside a shipwreck on the beach. She is a random spawn on a 20 min timer. Her PH is a savage pirate skeleton (I think, I posted this on allakhazam, but I can't access the page to check)

My 48 cleric duoed with a 44 pally to kill the mobs for the DW emblem, and the PH is green to 48. 2 banish undeads and change dispatches the PH.

Hand Alina's emblem to theerudite spirit, she speaks and hands over Spell: Temperance.

I have heard rumours, but havent checked myself yet, but if you do back and speak to Millius after getting bgeo, he'll speak of the other cleric spell.
