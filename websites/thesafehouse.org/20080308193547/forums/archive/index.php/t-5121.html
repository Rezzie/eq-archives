You will have the flexibility to use different weapons to your advantage, but with that low of weapon spec your damage output will be pretty low. I would suggest lowering stealth to 38-39 and also dropping your dual-wield in order to bring up your weapon specs.

Your weapon spec determines your base damage for all your attacks including critical strikes.

Not saying that you couldn't make it work, but I think you would find the damage output a little disappointing.

EZ_Landril77
07-17-02, 01:25 PM
That's really up to you. In my opinion dual-wield has the least return of any skills we have. The dual-wield styles are pretty worthless for an infiltrator, so the only thing you gain from it is .5% chance for your off-hand to attack per point you spend in it. This is nice at low levels when it does not cost many points, but as it gets higher it gets very costly for little benefit.

My dual-wield is only 7, but I know many infiltrators that have gone higher and been happy with it. It's really a matter of preference. I only mentioned it because you need to salvage points from somewhere if you are going to spend them on both weapon specs.
