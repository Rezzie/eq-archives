Huzzah! After much preperation, me and the guys who are staying for summer quarter @ RIT started our summer campaign last night. There was even roleplaying, and with our "miniatures" combat was much more interesting. I made 2' x 2' sheet of 1" graph paper, and we used various dice and large capacitors (does it get any geekier) for our miniatures. All monsters were D10's with the pointy side indicating facing. Occurance of the night was the Fighter/Wizard convincing the NPC that resuced the party when their caravan got attacked that the bald, female wizard had married her cousin. He thought they were all wierd and slept under one of the wagons for the night.

He's really an evil guy who is just going to try to manipulate the party for his own ends. Actually, most of the campaign is driven by evil guys, and the PCs think they are doing good...ahahahaha. Krimzan Anklestabber
