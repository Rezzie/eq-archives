So I just got done watching this.

Animation/art-wise, it looks EXACTLY like the second Vampire Hunter D movie.

I liked the story allright. The "present" in the movie is dystopian future instead of the actual present. But it's definatly set in the timeline of the TV series, not the cartoon, and seems to have nothing to do with Highlander 2. In fact, they pretty much don't get into the Highlander mythos much at all. It's kinda assumed you already know it. Which is good. It's just a story about one particular highlander, doing his thing.

Colins: "backstory"
Colin isn't actually born a MacLeod. He's the leader of his own clan during the time of the Roman invasion of Scotland. Later, he returns to Scotland during the time of the English invading Scotland and joins the clan MacLeod and ends up keeping the name.

I will say that the action in Highlander lends itself really well to anime style. They have alot of superhuman phyical actions but no outright magic powers or anything wierd like that.

The story was decent. But Colin is a little bit of a one-dimensional, typical anime badass loner type(not unlike D, or even Afro Samurai! Complete with wierd supernatural sidekick thats always bothering him).

All-in-all, I did not hate it. And I would totally watch a series of this.
