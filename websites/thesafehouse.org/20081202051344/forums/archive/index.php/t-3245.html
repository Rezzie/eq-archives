braincandy99
07-10-02, 08:16 AM
If it is a drop the easiest way to do it is on the creature who drops it.

1. Bring up the Edit of the creature in question.

2. Click on the Inventory Button.

3. Click on the standard list and open the heavy armor and select the half-plate +1.

4. Right Click and Edit Copy

5. Give it a name, and a blueprint name and tag

6. Give it the added skill property on the property page

That should do it for ya

For armor, you change both the look and the AC value by changing things on the Appearance tab. The look of the torso sets the armor's base AC.

Honestly, the item wizard doesn't do much; its easier to go to the palette, find a base item you want to change, and right click 'Edit Copy'.
