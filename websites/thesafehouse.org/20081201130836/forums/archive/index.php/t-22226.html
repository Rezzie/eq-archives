Six new screenshots have gone up on Vanguard's Screenshots Gallery (http://www.vanguardsagaofheroes.com/screenshots.php). There's a gnoll fight, an undead fight, an ogre battle, and a couple pics of mounts.

One thing I'd like to draw attention to that others may miss is that I appreciate the attention to detail that's going into Vanguard's sky. The stars hiding behind wispy clouds, and the nice use of colours (not just a standard blue sky). Here (http://www.vanguardsagaofheroes.com/screenshots/01-03-06_horse_1024.jpg), here (http://www.vanguardsagaofheroes.com/screenshots/11-14-05_orc_1024.jpg) , and here (http://www.vanguardsagaofheroes.com/screenshots/11-14-05_qaliatower_1024.jpg) (absolutely love the sky in this last pic) are a few examples of what I mean.

The terrain looks impressive, but just as the landscape has its trees and grass and sand, the skyscape has its blending of colours fading into the horizon, varying styles of clouds, and a backdrop of stars shining through at night.
