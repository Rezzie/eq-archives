You walk happily through the valleys of the Steamfont Mountains. You know that any Kobold that attacked you in this area would end up as a stain upon your dagger... but perhaps you are a bit careless.
Failing to look brings you upon a horde of Kobolds, you know not from where they came or where they go; only that they hate you.
At first the fighting is easy, your daggers rip apart the dogs quickly. You take only minimal wounds with each you slay... yet more continue to come. Gradually you realize they are becoming more powerful, as if you were nearing someone/ something that needed guarding.

BOOOM!! A huge fireball sends you hurtling backwards.
