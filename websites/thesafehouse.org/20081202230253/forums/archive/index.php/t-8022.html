The short version:

You have a 5% chance of making it pre-200


The longer version:

Mind melt is not trivial at 250. Based on my rough experiences, it still fails a good bit of the time. From this, and some work people have done approximating the success rate formula, you can work out some basic info, like an approximate trivial level. Knowing that you can work backwards to a success rate at 200ish.

Now this is VERY crude, but figure for each point below 250, your success chance drops about 1%. (Slightly more actually.) This is an estimate based on the success rate formulas, which themsleves are approximate.

So by the time you drop down 50-60% in success rate, you're about at the minimum success rate of 5%.

So if you really need one and just want to brute force it, you will succeed sooner or later. But it's going to be a major hassle.
