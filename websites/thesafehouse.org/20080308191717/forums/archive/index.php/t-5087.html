It depends on what you plan on doing with your character. Ultimately I think thrust is more powerful once you reach 50 if you spec all the way to 50 for dragonfang. I believe this gives you the most powerful character possible. however, thrust styles are very weak, and you will be sacrificing a lot of damage to gain the benefit of dragonfang and a greater variety of targets which are vulnerable to your weapon.

Slash is nice for battling other assassins and archers, and has slower weapons available to increase your PA damage cap. The styles in the slash line are also better than the thrust styles. Primarily amelthyst slash which is the most damaging no-opening attack available to an infiltrator and gives a bonus to chance to hit.

Overall I think thrust is the better way to go because of dragonfang being the ultimate weapon in an infiltrators arsenal. But before level 50 or with a lower thrust spec I think slash is probably more effective.
