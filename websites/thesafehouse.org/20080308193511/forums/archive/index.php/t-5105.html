You want weapon skill max, period. Get it to 50 and be done. Styles in most weapons aren't that bad anyway, but it does not matter cause you will be using critical strike styles all the time anyway. Just get the weapon skill up there for better damage.

Get stealth to 35 and envenom to 36. Those will hit 50 with items and realm points. No reason to even chance those going over 50, as having then over 50 does not benefit like weapon skill and critical strike.

Dump the rest into critical strike.

As you level get weapon and critical strike max, and envenom to 5 for the second DOT. Leave stealth and other skills alone.

Stealth will auto-train to stay at 25% of your level, and for the first 20 or 24 levels you don't really need it, until you hit the battle grounds. So save points but not bumping up stealth early on.

You do not need to put anypoints into Celtic dual or dualwield to be able to use it. It will just go off every now and then on its own. More points mean more styles and higher percentage to go off. But it goes off alot anyway, so don't bother until you hit 50 and put extra points into it.
