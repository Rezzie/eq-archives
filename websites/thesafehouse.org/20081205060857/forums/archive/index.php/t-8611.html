Why not do Grimror's Bugs quest in PoD?

everquest.allakhazam.com/...quest=2213

It will give you xp. I happen to like the direct reward (Bangle of Disease Warding) because I play on a PvP server - but the insta-cast is useful when fighting any MOB that casts dispell.

The best reason to do the quest tho, is that it gives you a flag that can be used in the Storytime quest:

everquest.allakhazam.com/...quest=2469

I completed Grimror's Bugs on Saturday and my Intricate Wooden Figurine charm gained an immediate stat boost. On talking to the Storytime quest NPC I gained an AAXP, and the other members of my group all gained their charm and the AAXP as well. Level 52 Ogres (old models, of course) all bouncing up and down with glee.

For what it's worth I essentially did the whole quest without a cleric or an enchanter. SK and necro aggro-kiting did the trick... and I think that rogues are a great help in such situations.
