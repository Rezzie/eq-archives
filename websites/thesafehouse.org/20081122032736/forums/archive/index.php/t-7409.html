Lets say for example, you have 2 Damage Training enhancements in your power, and you want to upgrade one to a Dual Origin Enhancment. You buy a new DO enhancment, and you have to eliminate one of the existing 2, right?

Well, did you know you can combine enhancments that are already in a power?

Take the 2 Damage training ones currently in the power, Combine the two of them, and then drop in the DO in the newly empty slot.

Hey, Its not much, but its better than overwriting it completely.
