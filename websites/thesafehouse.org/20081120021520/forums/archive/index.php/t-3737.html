if you get fully raid buffed you might be able to solo her with the gear you have, that is if she's still the same difficulty as before.

the golems in the room were dblue for the most part before, so i dunno if she was changed other than drops.

what i've found that works best for soloing her (assuming you have SoS), is to sneak back into the hall BEHIND her room, kill the 2 roamers, heal up, load up an old world snare poison.

either pull her with a dispell item or throwing dagger (easier with dagger cause she roams the back and dispell won't land without line of sight. by the end of the hall, smack her once to snare her, then run for zone out. run back in, heal up, repoison (if she went too far past and is in big room before zone out just sit in hall and wait for snare to break).

/disc duelist as she rounds the corner and have at it. she casts a lot, so use that to your advantage and backstab her, if your resists are weak, i'd recommend using some scoriae bite proc poison for the chance to interrupt her. last time i did her, she didn't even attempt to run, but if she does she paths back TOWARD her room, not zone out, so position her as far back in hall as possible from the get go.

gluck with it, i bit the dust quite a few times with another rogue friend of mine before i figured this out. =)
