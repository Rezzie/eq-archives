Well Phase 2 is off to a game changing start. Rest states, hearthstones, sap changes, etc. are big changes to the game.

I'm only level 7 as my highest character presently (they locked our Alpha and Beta 1 characters). So far the biggest change that's affected me has been the way skill point are earned. You gain 1 point for every 300 unmodified xp points gained by killing mobs.

It now takes around 10 skill points instead of 1 or 2 to begin apprentice level of a trade skill (actual points required varies). Certain attributes are also increased by skill points now (agility costs me 1 skill point to raise as a rogue).

I'll be posting more as I test and will do a new rogue review down the line.
