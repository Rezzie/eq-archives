Viz Announces "Bleach" Dub Cast; Several Manga Titles (http://news.toonzone.net/article.php?ID=11724)

Viz drew out quite a crowd to all of their panels, and they announced plenty of news to match. For starters, the dub cast of Bleach is as follows:

Ichigo - Johnny Yong Bosch (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=1283)
Rukia - Michelle Ruff (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=7125)
Isshin - Patrick Seitz (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=7108)
Karin - Kate Higgins (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=20166)
Orihime - Stephanie Sheh (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=3419)
Tatsuki - Wendee Lee (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=1184)
Ishida - Derek Stephen Prince (http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=4036)

Additionally, at their main panel they announced Shokugan No Shana (both the original novel by Yashichiro Takahashi and the manga by Ayato Sasakura), The Cain Saga by Kaori Yuki and the Dragon Drive manga by Ken-ichi Sakura. At their Shojo Beat panel they announced the manga Love Com (aka Lovely Complex) by Aya Nakahara, and they stated that the color printing used on their 1st anniversary issue is a permanent addition to the magazine. Last but not least, at their Shonen Jump panel they announced the Houshin Engi manga by Ryu Fujisaki, and they confirmed that Gintama by Hideaki Sorachi will be serialized in the Shonen Jump magazine. During the main panel's Q&A, it was mentioned that by giving retailers advance information about their mature titles, Viz has had no complaints about content, even in the case of very mature titles like Saikano.

Toon Zone's coverage live from the San Diego Comic-Con will continue throughout the weekend.
