Quote:These same mobs are charmable regardless if you do normal or hard and, yes, they are immune to snare in both.

Yup, although there is one important difference. These mobs can be 65 on hard, which means uncharmable. So you've got to find the model and it can't be 65. Makes a critter to charm harder to find. Of course, if you do find one, it does serious damage. But first you have to find it.

Quote:And how does all this effect rogues trying to get groups for hard adventures as oppose to easy ones?

Due to the nastier nature of hard mobs, more support classes are desirable. Combine that with the uncertainty of charm and it makes support classes even more desirable. More slots dedicated to support means less to pure DPS. However, that also makes high DPS in those slots more important.

But much of it has to do with the people you're doing hard with. The better geared and AA'd they are, the less important support is, the more DPS you can pack in.

Anyway, to sum it up, it's who you know that that has the biggest impact on doing hard groups. If they know you're good enough, you'll get them, but most likely only if you're in their guild.
