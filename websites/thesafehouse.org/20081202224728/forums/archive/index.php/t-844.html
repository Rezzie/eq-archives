The beta they're talking about now is closed beta opened only to those who signed up on their message boards from a long time ago, and maybe promotions ran by affiliates or marketing partners.

According to CoH's site pertaining to beta testing:

Quote:Within the next couple of weeks, the first batch of Public Beta invites will go out to community members who previously registered on the City of Heroes Forums. These invitations are based on a number of factors, beginning with original signup date. A chunk of those first registered for the forums will be plucked from our database, checked for recent activity, and then invited randomly in groups of 100. The process will start slowly and ramp up, but eventually, everyone registered on the City of Heroes forums prior to November 1st 2003 will be considered. While we appreciate and welcome interest in the City of Heroes community and are happy to see new members on our forums, it is important to note that signing up for the forums at this point will have no effect on being chosen for beta testing.

It's my understanding that GameSpot's list of beta testers is different than the ones Cryptic Studios picked. The ones on the list above are GameSpot fans picked by them for their own promotional purposes. Cryptic picked their own crop of beta testers based on the message board sign up list. So in other words, even if your name isn't listed above, you could still be selected for beta assuming you signed up on CoH boards a while ago.
