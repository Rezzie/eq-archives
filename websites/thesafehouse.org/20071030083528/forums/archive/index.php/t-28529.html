Jet Lag sucks. I don't even know what time zone my brain is in right now.

Oh, and if you are ever considering a trans-pacific flight, Just kip right over China Southern's selection of flights.

15 hour flight.

Had to board via bus, starting at 10:30 for a 11:50 flight out of LAX. (Of course, to my brain, that was 2:50 am, coming from EST.) So, we finally take off at 12:55. That's 4am by my brain. So, we settle in for a long sleep. Finally get cozy, and about the time the pilot takes the seat belt sign off, It's time for a meal! Yeah, That's exactly what I want at 4:30 in the morning. Anyway, we go from LAX to Guangzhou, and my last cigarette was at 9:30 Pacific time. We land, and have to wait in line for customs part 1. Repeat for customs parts 2 and 3. We barely make the connecting flight at Guangzhou, 4 families from our adoption agency were literally sprinting through the terminal to get there in time. So that's another 1 1/2 hrs, plus getting baggage, etc.

At this point, I'm about to die for a cigarette, and I finally get outside. Light up, and the group's guide/translater says hurry, we are going to miss the bus!

So, 1 single solitary puff on a cigarette, and back on the bus for another 50 minutes.

I damn near couldn't walk after smoking that first one.

It's 8 pm here, but it feels like 7 in the morning, with no sleep. Gonna crash.
