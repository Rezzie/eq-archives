Another chanter here, so not 100% sure on melee stats...but I believe he hits 900ish, dunno if it's triple or quads. Partially slowable, but must be in melee range to land any spells (except tash/malo). I'd recommend at least two tanks, one MT and a rampage tank. Doesn't rampage a lot, but enough that you'll want to land a cripple and have a dedicated healer (druid etc if no extra clerics) for the rampage tank until slowed.

As far as I know Inny has the two different procs. One is an AE grav flux/dispel of one slot (always gets first buff). The second is a much bigger dispel targeted on MT which will very quickly strip all buffs off them.

If you setup correctly you can get all your healers hidden behind a wall, but still in healing range of MT. Best spot we've found is to pull Inny to the corner immediately to the right as you enter his room, and then the clerics can sit out in the hall in the corner and still reach him. You need to make sure that the rampage tank gets his aggro position, because from this setup the clerics are in rampage range and that can get ugly.

EZ_Daminy7th
11-11-03, 08:01 AM
The Nadox Inny spawns in the room just behind the broodmother trigger mob.

All you do is look for the "Luggald High Priest". He is the trigger mob. He is indifferent and you dont have to kill him. To spawn Inny is easy. If High Priest is up just clear his room. Thats it. There are about 6 mobs in his room after you kill the 4th or 5th one the High Priest will say something about Inny, he will say it not zone wide shout. Few minutes after that Inny will pop in the center of the room.

Best way to trigger him is to set up in the hall about half way. Pull at least half the room then pull the other half. When you have the second half dead Inny should be up or about to pop. Just make sure you dont agro Inny =).
