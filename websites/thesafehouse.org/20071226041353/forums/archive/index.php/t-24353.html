Ouran High School Host Club

Series Info:

Plot Summary:

Haruhi goes to Ouran High School, which is a school that exists only for rich people. She did not pay to enter, rather, she won a scholarship to enroll into this prestigous High School. Due to having to study hard to become a honor student to get in, she had no sense of fashion and acted very tomboyish.

While searching for a quiet place to study, she entered Music Club room 3, which happened to be where the Host Club is. The Host Club is managed by six young men, and it's sole purpose of existence is to graitfy the wishes of girls. In other words, girls come to this club in order to have the bishounens make them feel special.

While suprised at the surroundings, Haruhi accidently breaks a vase worth 8,000,000 yen (approx. $80,000). In order to pay off the loan, the club makes her work it off. Finding out Haruhi looks like a perfect bishounen, they make her into a Host to pay off her loans. What they did not realize was that she was an girl.

Genres:

Comedy, Romance, Shoujo
