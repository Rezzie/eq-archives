You might be crazy, sure... but what the heck. Live crazy.

Some things to consider...

You're going to be relying on your weapons and styles for damage. High level poisons are nice, but they don't contribute anywhere near as much as high level weapon spec. You want to take thrust to 50, because - whether you know it or not - you want Dragonfang.

Also, if you're more-or-less ignoring Critical Strike, you don't need anything like 40 stealth. Stealth for you will be a convenience, not a necessity.

Think about something like 50 Thrusting (styles + maximum weapon damage), 50 Dual Wield (styles + maximum chance for double swing), 40 Envenom (very good poisons, items + rank may get you to 50), 25 Stealth (enough to climb walls), and leftovers get you 5 Critical Strike (enough to pop bladeturn from behind).

You're basically a full offensive mercenary. You lose chain armor, protect skills, parry, and the better fighter tables for HP and melee. You get really good evade, poison, and basic hiding. It should work. Ciardha Sidhe, Elven Nightshade of Guinevere
