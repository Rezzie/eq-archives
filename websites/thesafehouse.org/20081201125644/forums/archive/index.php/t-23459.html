Dai Mahou Touge - B-

So, I've watched the first two episodes of Dai Mahou Touge. I'm immediately reminded of Bokusatsu Tenshi Dokuro-chan. It's a comedy and a parody of mahou shoujo genre. People who watch Mahou Shoujo are more likely to be entertained by this, or at least, those familiar with elements that makes up your typical mahou shoujo show.

It's got some raunchy humor and ecchi scenes, but nothing too extreme. It's not as in-your-face as Dokuro-chan was, it draws it's humor from being a parody more than it does from over-the-top violence.

Typically I'd give it an A, but i decieded to step it down as I'm sure the next OVA won't be as funny, however I've been suprised before.

I promise you'll love it, no matter what. If it doesn't have you in tears, you need a sense of humor.

Watch it, if you watch only shonen and not interested in expanding your horizons, it's not for you, but if you ever step outside of shonen, even once in a blue moon, watch this. It's so awesome.

In order to get off the planet and solve the murder of their parents, they have to fight their way up to become Jyu Oh, the Beast King.
