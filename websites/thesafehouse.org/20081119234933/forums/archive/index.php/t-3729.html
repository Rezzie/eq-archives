EZ_Sarai of Grove
11-20-03, 03:09 AM
Ahvi is permarooted, it makes building agro against him easy. Set up in the room before Ahvi and have your puller take the skeleton guards that are currently with him to the raid and MEZ them. If the skeletons are killed, they will just split, so they must be mezed and held for the duration of the fight (they can be safely killed after Ahvi is dead). Ahvi rampages so make sure no clerics heal your pullers, no buff songs are going, etc. Have your main tank and rampage tanks go forward and start plinking Ahvi with arrows for a couple minutes till they have solid agro. Then start your CH rotation on your MT (have some druids or alt healers watching the ramp tank). I believe we use a 3 second rotation using an 8k HP tank but that may be overkill. Ahvi should die pretty rapidly, after he's dead, have the raid back up to the previous room where the skeletons are mezed and kill. Move into Ahvi's room and prepare for the next stage.

Enjoy!

- Raidn

EZ_Walish
11-21-03, 05:30 AM
Fastest we have ever done the carprin cycle was 1hour from zonein to completion.

Carprin:
Make sure you get his 3 pets without carprin, once the last dies carprin warps down on corpse - generally just have people use assisthealing, as carprin memblurs self - and then just have all your tanks taunting..

Avhi:
Skellie hordes are fun.... but not all the time.
Clearing the room is easy, and the person doing so will most likely get on rampage - use this - have a sk pull the room, since Avhi is rooted its no problem.
On engage make sure all your healers spamheal MT - have 2 healers assigned for rampage tank - and just have people NOT use pets or FD - fd WILL spawn skellies, so will pets that despawn (like wizzy swords or cleric hammers).

Bishop:
Rooted and right through easy - have a mt, have people watch his health and their agro - then just kill him.

Vindor and Raex:
Vindor is slowable, tank with high resists and lots of healing, and just burn him down...
Raex - we do this guy in a fun fun way! - we sacrifice rangers, pull him in - have a chain of rangers set up, they fire weaponshield and then just keep snaring the guy till they die.... thats 18secs per ranger approximate - have everyone do all their dmg - even clerics nuking, and he goes down and you loose 5-7ish peeps.

HighPriest:
Have someone ready to cure MT from the slow - and else just watch agro, he is easykill
