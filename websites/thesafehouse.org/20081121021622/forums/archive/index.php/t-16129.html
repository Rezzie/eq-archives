The first stage of the Vanguard Fantasy Fiction contest has ended and the Safehouse is well represented with 10 entrants, as well as 3 staff submissions sent to other sites (staff was not allowed to submit to their own site).

Next stage is public voting. Each of our entries has been posted in The Fireplace forum as a locked and stickied post. After the voting ends we will unlock them for comments and authors notes. Stories are listed by title and author. In most cases their Safehouse name is the same as their Vanguard forum name. In the cases where they differ, the Vanguard forum name is listed first, and the Safehouse name is listed as an "aka".

Voting will be open until December 17th and you only get one vote, so please make your decision carefully, alot of nervous writers are counting on you!
