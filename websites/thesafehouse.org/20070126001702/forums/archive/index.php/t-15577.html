Graystone

1. Finding Bait --- Moyna

She's a merchant on the docks. Talk to her and she'll ask for you to go get her more bait. If you do, then she'll send you to get centipedes from the Forest Ruins. After you get them, come back and give them to her. She'll give you money and either a "Paring Knife" or a "Fishing Spear." I took the knife

Stats: + 2 health, +2 power, dual wield, dmg 1-4, delay 1.2

2. Snakeskin for Soulforge

He's the blacksmith in the armory. Talk to him and he'll ask you to get snakeskins for a weapon or something. If you agree, he'll send you to the Caves near Baubbleshire. You get 10 and then come back. He'll mumble something about not being able to pay you much because they are damaged, but will give you money and a choice of weapons. This quest is good for anyone cause he has a wide variety of weapons. I chose the blackened rapier.

Stats: +1 health, +2 power, dual wield, dmg 2-6 (3-8 at lvl 14), delay 1.5

Oakmyst Forest

1. Proof of Poachers

Talk to the Steward something or other. He can be found up a hill where the Great bears spawn. I talked to him and he told me to get proof there are poachers in the forest. After I killed two poachers (they spawn at night near the deer paths), I took the proof to Tracker Galera. She gave me a 2H staff.

Stats:+1 int, +1 str, +2 health, +2 power, two-handed, dmg 4-12 (6-17 ay lvl 13), delay 1.7

Forest Ruins

1. Vaughn's Stuff (Part 1)

Talk to the Outlander Vaughn near the pond. He's lost his satchel, letter, journal, and ring to the Ruin Stalkers. He asks for you to get them for him. After you've retrieved his items, he asks you to take a letter to Hunter Forest Deep

Letter for Hunter Forest Deep (Part II)

Take the letter from Vaughn to Hunter Forest Deep in the Willow Woods. He is on a hilltop where he has cornered a bear. After you hand him the letter, he thanks you by giving you a knife (isn't that sweet of him?)

Stats: +1 agi, +3 health, dual wield, dmg 2-6 (2-7 @ lvl 14), delay 1.2 sec


Elddar Grove

1. Hawk Hunt

You can find Gregor Earthstrider in the bower shop in the corner. Hail him and he starts talking to you about how he can't seem to find the hawks needed to make arrows (a ranger ... who can't find hawks ... yup, must be EQ). He will ask you to do it for him and tells you to go to the Forest Ruins. You need to kill 5 Antonican Hawks for their feathers and then return to him. After you've handed in the feathers, he'll give you his old bow.

Starcrest Commune

1. Essences for Obidudyn

He's a scholar near the fountain in the middle of town. He keeps muttering about how he wants to be left alone, but yet is still willing to talk to total strangers. Must be an erudite thing. Anyway, talk to him and he will tell you he wants to be able to summon pets, but is unable to without essences. He then asks if you want to go to the Forest Ruins and kill 6 forgotten guardians for the essences. When you return triumphantly, he will reward you with your choice of weapons. Again I chose the Smouldering Dagger

Stats: +1 int, +3 power, one-handed, dmg 5-14 (base 3-9), delay 1.7 sec
