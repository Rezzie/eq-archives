If you want to do the Shield Quest, you need to raise the Concillium Universus faction that Lcea and Phenic are on. To raise that faction, kill Heart of Seru mobs in Sanctus Seru:
**From Alla's**
A Castrorum of the Heart - lvl 55
A Castrorum Seniori - lvl 60
A Lictor of the Heart - lvl 60
A Praetor of the Heart - lvl 55
A Quaestor Guard - lvl 53
