I pulled it off solo a couple days back. Pretty much you gotta sneak up to the top of the tower, when you get to the top of the tower, Sap the guy guarding the chest, loot it, jump down the stairs and sprint out to lose agro.

Once that's done, you just have to pp the malformed drone (hits for 250ish), MAKE SURE YOU ARE BEHIND HIM OR HE WILL GANK YOU

after that bring the stuff to the man, turn it in to get the touch of zanzil. When that's done talk to the gnome downstairs, then you have to find some thread, wild flowers, a leaded vial, and a bronze tube.

Thread is in Finer threads on the ground. Wild Flowers are at the gardener (i believe it's near stormwind keep), leaded vials from the poison supplier, and the bronze tube either get it made or get lucky and buy it from an engineer supplier.

(if you want to get rid of the poison sooner, you can either die or get a high level cure ( I think ) )

But if you do the quest, it's worth like 1600 xp.

Not sure if it was luck or the fact that I snuck up close, stopped moving then picked (instead of attemping to pick while moving).

Once you start your PP, stop. If you move while the pick pocket window is open, it will close automatically. However, he can move as far away as he wants, and the window won't close.

I died or ran away at least a dozen times trying to PP the drone. I was trying to keep up with him. As soon as I re-read it, I got just close enough for PP to work, hit the PP key, and stopped moving. The drone wandered around the corner, and I had the key in the window waiting to be looted.

So those are my two tips. Get just in range of PP, and stop moving when you hit the PP key.

I actually snuck up to the top of the tower on two separate occasions, just to check it out. Just as I got to the top the second time, some valiant paladin and a few others came clearing up through the tower. They pulled Klaven, and I wandered over and opened the unguarded chest.
