Ssra:

The MAGISTARTE can be found going straight from the zone through the door past the 4 snakes in their alcoves. At the T-intersection turn right und follow the hall which ends at a door to your left. Behind it is a 3-spawn of snakes, the magistrate will always appear at the middle spawn; it does have PHs. Seen him there 4x, never at another spot.

The ASSASSIN can be found when turning left at the T-intersection, going straight until you you reach two doors, the 1st to your right und 2nd one a bit down the hall to your left. Take the left door, you'll see another 3-spawn of snakes, behind them is a stone which covers an entrance, you have to pick that door/stone. Follow the tunnel (there's a trap right there a few feet down the tunnel) until you reach a pretty big room, filled with snakes.
If you look into the room from the door you just walked through, you'll see a small ramp going down to the lower floor und another ramp at the far side of the room. The assassin will spawn to the left of that ramp at the far side of the room (south I believe). Never seen him spawn anywhere else.

Hope that was of some help to you. Edited by: Pieksy Kenderkin at: 10/26/03 11:57 pm
