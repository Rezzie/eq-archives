Quote:(Orange, California-AP) -- Three 12-year-old California girls have been sentenced to serve from 30 to 45 days' detention for falsely accusing a homeless man of attacking them.

Each girl must perform 60 hours of community service, pay unspecified restitution and remain on probation until age 21.

Nordmark was arrested last May and charged with attacking the girls in a suburban park. His trial ended abruptly in January after one girl admitted the three friends concocted the story as a cover for coming home late from school.
