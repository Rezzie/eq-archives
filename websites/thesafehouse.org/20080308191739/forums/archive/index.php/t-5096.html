I personally feel envenom is a crucial and fun part of being an assassin. It allows you to do 4 spell like effects. Sure as hell a lot more friendly and useful than a little extra damage from using two axes.

Also at level 21 you should have perforated artery. @#%$ that up, but you will get it eventually. =)

Also at level 20 your auto trained stealth will be 5.

I figure if you are going for melee damage, which is why people go high LA, then they mind as well go berzerker since you are forgoing envenom.

As for tips, stealth, crit and chew up with left axe =)...and use what little venom you have...its like free damage. And the snare is so crucial.
