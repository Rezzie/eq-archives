05-21-04, 08:31 AM
Although, you may not want to assist the main tank.

In EQ, The Main tank tends to stay on one target for a long time, until they are dead.

In COH, the Main tank's job in groups is not damage, but Taunting. Thus, they will be rapidly switching targets to taunt anyone who has turned his attention to the squishies. (Defenders/Controllers/Blasters)

If you have a secondary tank or a Scrapper, it may be more effective to target them instead. They will likely not be switching targets as rapidly, and will stay on one target until it is down.

Doing this gives you much better odds of avoiding aggro.

Also, Alturick, you can bind any keys that you want to quickly target group members. For example, if you wanted it to be F1 - Fwhatever, you would use the following command:

/bind f1 "team_select 1"
/bind f2 "team_select 2"
etc. etc.

However, I'm not sure if binding the F-keys to targetting will also use your inspirations in the case of F1 through F5... Although I doubt that. However, if you still want to be able to use your keyboard to use inspirations, the commands are as follows:

Assuming you want to use Y through P to use them:

/bind y "inspexec_slot 1"
/bind u "inspexec_slot 2"
etc. etc.
