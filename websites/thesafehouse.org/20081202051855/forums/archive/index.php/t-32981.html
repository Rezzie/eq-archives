Looks like some posts at the start, but none really recently.

I've been playing since alpha 2 and still really enjoy the game. The constant addition of content and new features has really been a great thing... not to mention the stability and beautiful world.

At any rate, I'd love to start a little theorycrafting here as I'm interested in other Burglar's experiences. Obviously, there's really two separate roles, as a solo player and as a fellowed player. Seems in a fellowship Agility would play a much more significant role than might, since the more free conjunctions, the better. But as solo I find a much closer blend of might and agility is best. For weapons I tend to choose a mace for my primary (slower speed, bigger damage range, chance to stun) and a nice fast high "dps" weapon for my offhand.

I've not done any math or anything like that, just kind of makes sense to me. My skills are mostly mainhand based and are a significant source of my damage, so it would seem that a high max damage weapon would out-damage a faster, but lower max damage weapon.

Well, I hope there's some Burgs hangin about. Also interested in what you guys think of the current Legendary traits and the new ones coming in Book 12.
