Add Tower of Sol Ro to your list of AA's and you are done (max is 7)

As for the charm, each flag grants 1/10 of the full stats:

PoV/S - trials
BoT - key quest
HoH - AD or box of souls
CoD - grummus or part collection
Torment - terris or poison cure quest
Tactics - Behemoth or symbol quest
Tower of Sol Ro - Vallon and Tallon
PoEarth/Air/Water - Rallos
PoFire - Sol Ro
PoTime - 4 elemental gods

note: even with time access, you won't get the stats from that unless the other 9 are done. A friend has everything but PoFire, and she only gets 8/10 of full stats.
