I got a few messages when I revealed that I ran the soulblade template. So I thought I would post a bit on my experiences on it to answer peoples questions.

Level 46 Shadowblade
My template
33 (+12) Stealth
34 (+6) Leftaxe
35 (+9) Axe
39 (+9) Crit Strike
7 (+5) Envenom

My realm rank is 3 (+2 to all skills)

Tentitively, My level 50 should look something close to -
37 (+13) Stealth
34 (+6) Leftaxe
43 (+9) Axe
43 (+9) Crit Strike
7 (+5) Envenom


What do I get out of this template?? Damage. Pure steel damage. I don't have to worry about resists, I don't have to worry about reapplying poisons, I just hit my style buttons and hit hard EVERY swing.

Do I regret the envenom loss? Not at all. My crit strike and Axe skill will ensure I have a nice opening perf/creeping death. Close to that of a pure critblade. I use envenom for it's secondary abilities. I LOVE the new snare. It makes my styles hit over and over, they never leave my range. I keep infectious on the second blade. With that my damage is fast enough that I can overcome most heals. I will not move envenom up one more drop. The enervate line is nice, and the dot is much better, but that's just not how I do my damage. I hit you hard and don't stop hitting you hard until you are dead. And you WILL be dead!

How's doublefrost in RVR? Oh... my.... god. You will love this style. It is the "stealther killer". It's sometimes tough to setup a perf on an assassin or high stealth archer. You lose them from target nearest enemy too fast to setup the front facing. You don't need it. Doublefrost is all you need. It hits SO hard. Against yellow con assassins/archers, you're looking at +120 to +150 on your style damage. That makes my hits around 230-250, plus my leftaxe hit. 300ish damage a swing, they just have nothing to defend against it.

What's the best thing about this template? Pure and simple. I can kill things that other shadowblades can't. Where other shadowblades are dumping damage in the first few hits and praying. I have a nice dump of damage in those hits and then I have something to push through the rest of their hitpoints... doublefrost. I don't shy away from armored opponents. In fact, I do some stuff that most people think are pretty insane!

Suggestions to those following the soulblade path? Hold the line. Find the path you like and follow it. It truly shines as you reach the 45ish area. You'll truly shine. It's hard to lay off envenom, it really is. But in my opinion, getting your spec to 34cs/34la is your primary goal. Once you hit it... you're golden. Work on hitpoints!!! Con and +hitpoint items are your friends. You are going to be staying in fights where other assassins would be running. You can't do this without alot of hitpoints and if possible Slash/Thrust/Crush resistance items.

At 46 as a kobold I have 1047 hitpoints with zero buffs. My thrust and crush resists are beautiful.... I struggle against slashers, but the others die nice, I hope to up my slash resist next. Leaves me in the fight a 2 or 3 more rounds, in which i get 3 more doublefrosts off!!


Hope this helps!

Stick with it, soulblades, you'll love it!!!
