What you'll find as a 2h user as you go up against people with evade or block or what have you is when you miss it will go very poorly for you. That means up to 11 seconds with a 5.5 delay weapon of NO damage to the target. A infil with a 2.0 speed weapon hits you 5 times in that span. I have roughly the same specs I think and if I go up against something that isn't an archer or a mage, I switch to LA. Also at 39 LA you get a stun style that chains off Comeback, named Frosty Glaze that gives 6 secs worth of stun. So if you don't get the PA/CD chain you can still get the stun effect off.

General consensu is if you don't go 39 in LA then don't bother. Hope this helps.
