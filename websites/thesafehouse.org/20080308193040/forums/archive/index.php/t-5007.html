Are the weapons orange? WeapDam is capped per-level, and orange weapons will exceed the cap (and thus display the same number when you switch them).

To help you read WeapDam, an explanation of the display: the zero second-from-right is supposed to be a period. For example, 906 WeapDam = 9.6 DPS; 1406 WeapDam = 14.6 DPS.

Also, I believe WeapDam and WeapSkill only display for your mainhand, but the damage of your offhand is definitely affected by your spec the same way your main hand is. Also keep in mind that the main thing affected by weapon spec is damage variance (because it gives you a higher Min damage).

In Midgard, offhand damage is determined by Left Axe spec. Dual Wield and Celtic Dual are different in that they only affect your chance to swing with both weapons.

Using a 16.2 Blade when you are speced in Pierce is like... like... well like using a very low-DPS Pierce weapon, except you'll get some high hits now and then (but overall damage the same).

Glimmering Stilettos are gold with blue lightning particles. Edited by: Valarauco at: 4/2/02 2:57:28 am
