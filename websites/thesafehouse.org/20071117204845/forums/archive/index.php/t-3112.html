PCs are involved in a fairly major roof top combat right now. One of my newbie PCs decided to use one of the random magic items I gave him, the Bag of Beans!, to help the situation. If you don't know, the beans basically sprout into ANYTHING, be it creature, item, spell effect, what have you.

The first one he threw produced a 60+' version of the plant from little shop of horrors, which proceeded to eat some of the Goblyns they were fighting, and to take some shots at the PCs. So my newbie decides he needs to throw another one.

Now, it's not that I have any shortage of ideas for what this next bean could be, but I figured, seeing as it can be ANYTHING, I thought I'd tap the SH pool of ideas for something really wacky. It can be ANYTHING, a Wyvern, Elder Dragon, bunny, 60X60 ice storm effect, a portal door, a bastard sword +1, whatever your twisted little minds can come up with. Need to know for Sunday 8/31.

edit

Quick view of the situation, the PCs are on top of the main keep, with a steeped roof in the middle, an a ~10' walkway around the roof. Goblyns are pouring in the from 2 of the 3 trap doors on the south side of the roof, each in an opposite corner. There is one additional trap door in the middle of the southern walkway, directly under where the party is fighting. While battling a werewolf, the wizard threw out a web spell to fence them off from the ever increasing numbers of goblyns, basically cornering them in the middle of the south wall. Web spell will hold for at least 3 more TURNS. There are at least 12 goblyns either stuck in the webbing, or out on the roof attempting to get to the PCs.
