Confirmed Lockpicking Information: (READ THIS)
1. When you want to pick lock a door, if you PP a junkbox between successes (not just skillups) at the door, it will allow multiple skillups at the door. (ie. pick door (get it open), pick junkbox (skillup/noskillup), pick door(possible skillup again).) (this is very useful in scarlet monastery)

2.

Quick lockpick skillup guide:
(or how Melgrave got so high on Lockpicking and Laenis copy/pasted his guide and modified it =P)
NOTE: You get the Pick Lock skill at level 16 from a rogue trainer high enough to give it. You get 5 points of lockpicking per level. (ie. 225 lockpicking is achieved when you hit level 45)

1-103 on the Insta-chests from lockpicking quest.

I recomend running through a "private" Stockades instance when level 30 or so (less with MoD) and PPing 2 bags full of lockboxes.

103-175 very tediously between pickpocketted lockboxes and Klaven's tower.

Desolace is good at late 30s for the undead ravagers also drop mageweave (yummy!)

175-178 on the Gnomeregan doors (I suggest you learn how to reset an instance, see notes 2)

179-184 on Scarlet Monastery doors/chests

Haunted recomends that at 175 skill, the best thing to do is wait until you're level 45-50 and pickpocket either the ogres in Tanaris while grinding, or pickpocket Zul'Farrak and resetting the instance (about 5-6 junkboxes per run average.)


Notes:
1. I just did this at 39, go to Westfall to where the poison quest is at, top floor, Klaven can take me down to half health at 39 if I don't dual-ambush so he kinda hurts. To do this just go behind his table, there should be two chairs, jump over the chair closest to the chest and from behind the chair pick the lock (you MUST click on your lockpicking icon then the chest). Don't worry about Touch of Zanzil, a death(*) or Jungle Remedy from STV or cure poison from a high enough player should make it all good.
You must be of a high enough level not to aggro Klaven!
*: for extra points see how many mobs you can pull and tank at the same time in Westfall without dying or killing any, my record is 25 (without using evasion)

*BIG CAP WARNING* ***DANGER*** ***ALERT*** ****TOLD YOU SO**** DO NOT LEAVE GROUP BEFORE LEAVING INSTANCE, THIS WILL BUG YOUR INSTANCE AND YOU WILL NOT BE ABLE TO WALK BACK IN FOR ABOUT 30 MINUTES.

For anyone who's in the mid 30s or beyond, I've found a pretty good way to pick up some skill points, since I didn't know of any particular places or tricks for lockpicking. Just head to the north western part of Stranglethorn, where there are three sets of ruins full of Bloodscalps, ranging from around 33 up to the high 30s, with two boss types at 40 and 41. You can avoid anything higher than 37 by not going up the ramp to the top level at the northwestern part of the large ruins.

Just wander around picking pockets. You can clean em all out in about 15 minutes, and get around 10-20 worn junkboxes each run, depending on your luck. I just do it about once per day, since I'm too lazy to kill the things. I started doing it at 39 and had no trouble. At 42 even the bosses don't spot me when I'm sneaking around.

The worn junkboxes can take you up to 175, the skill necessary to open sturdys and steel. Also, with each run you'll make about 1g in coin, usually get around 5 greater healing potions, a few gems (I usually get 2-3 citrines, something the guild smith is always pleased about), plenty of pocket watches for another 25-30s, and occasionally some nice recipes or items. Last two runs I found smithing plans for reasonably high end weapons (200 and 225 skill, I thnk) in some of the junk boxes, made 3.5g for the two.
