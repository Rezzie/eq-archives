Actually I do have one that I use for them all. I cant say its the best one out there but it does what I like it to do.

Typically three sheets. First is Stats, Feats, Levels, a Random Die Roller, and basically everything on a paper sheet except the skills.
Second is Skills List that figures all the mods based upon how many ranks you put in it, plus has a "Result" column that works in conjunction with the Random Die Roller.

I have seen some out there that calculate the cross class skills based upon your classes. Also calculating the max HP and how many attribute points you add and such. I just didnt want to mess with anything that big for every character I had. (I think I have 15-20 characters on my HD for AD&amp;D alone, and thats not counting NPC's for my campaign either. Those are actual PC's that ive played at one time or another)

If you want mine I could probably get it to you. But if you do a google search for "Excel AD&amp;D Character Sheet 3ed" you may find a better variety.
