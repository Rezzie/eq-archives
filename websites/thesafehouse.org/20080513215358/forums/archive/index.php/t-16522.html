I went gnome, now level 52 on a PvP server.

Imo, undead rogues have it the best, whether they go daggers, swords, or maces. There's only one skill in the game that totally wrecks me as a dagger rogue, and it's pretty much the same for sword and mace rogues: FEAR :(

It takes us out of fight and lets dots eat up our low HP before we can kill our targets. Since the undead racial ability allows them immunity to fear it's damn near essential for rogues. If you're not partial to a particular faction, go horde and play an undead rogue.

If you really want to go alliance, each race has some neat things going for it. Imo, escape artist > perception, since its useful against more classes. The major reason to go human is for the +1 level to sword and mace attacks. So if you're going for a combat build, human is probably the better choice.
