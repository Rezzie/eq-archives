Heya:

If you leave a chapter before finishing a henchman's quest, I'm pretty sure you're out of luck forever on that henchman's series of quests. Basically, at the end of chapter 1, before continuing on, you should hire _all_ the henchpeople, one by one, and go through all the "tell me more" conversations with them until you get all their quests completed. Level 8 should be sufficient for this. Before advancing past chapter 2, do this again. Etc.
