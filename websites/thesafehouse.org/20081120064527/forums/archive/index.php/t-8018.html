09-20-02, 11:16 AM
Spirit of Sloth is in the Muscle Lock family (snare without slow). Spine Break is in the Paralyze family (snare with slow, can wear off prematurely). I don't know if it is a bug or not when Paralyze poisons break early.

Spine Break is a great poison if you just want to test slowing some mob, but if you want to actually keep a mob slowed, you might want to upgrade to using a Paralyze level 2 poison, named Kinetic Suppressant. You mix two delphinium with one basilisk eyestalk, in a lined vial with constrict suspension. You need to farm only the basilisk eyestalks. They are extremely easy to obtain in the basilisk caves in Jaggedpine (they drop in other places also, but that is the best spot I know).

The slight extra work of gathering the basilisk sacs is offset by almost doubled max duration.

Paralyze III is not worth the added trouble of farming the heartsting venom sacs, in my opinion. They seem very rare, and you do not gain much in duration over Paralyze II.

Paralyze I lasts a max of 7 ticks, 42 seconds.
Paralyze II lasts a max of 12 ticks, 72 seconds.
Paralyze III lasts a max of 17 ticks, 102 seconds.

They all slow the mob 50 percent.
