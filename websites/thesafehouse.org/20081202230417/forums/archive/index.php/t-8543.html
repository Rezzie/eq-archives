Quick first note, Korocust and his courier are spelled that way, notes are spelled with Korucust for
some reason. Unless there have been changes to one or the other since the info I have.

Okay, Vrasyk reported to Druushk (dragon in VP), who reported to Phara Dar(leader of RoS in VP). He was
on the council of the Di'Zok, presumeably one that advises the royalty of Chardok.

'A Captured Message' seems to be the precusor to the Blood Stained Note. It's from Vraysk and warns his
sleep is being disturbed by visions and he worried Korucust is working to expose him. Unknown where it drops.

Interrogator Gi'mok has the 'Blood Stained Note' which is Vrasyk -> Druushk. He spawns in Korucust's room.

Korocust (spelled Korucust also) has the 'Message to Phara Dar' which might be a fake Vrasyk note. It
somewhat seems to fit after the other two notes in trying to bypass Druushk who he has no replies
from. However the fact that the Interrogator has the 'Blood Stained Note' which makes me wonder if Vrasyk's
dead and Korocust made up a note that doesn't mention him but still links Vrasyk with the Ring of Scale.

Prince Selrach Di'Zok drops 'Korucust's report on Vrasyk' which makes it clear that Vrasyk is exposed and
that he and his couriers/attendants were disposed of.

'Korocust's report to the Di'Zok' seems to be set after 'Korucust's report on Vrasyk' in that he is trying
to link a traitor he found to a conspiracy of traitors he's tracking down. He's thankful of being elevated
to the Council, likely in the spot Vrasyk had. Unknown where it drops.

Overking Balthezid drops 'Note from Prince Selrach' warning about Korocust gaining power through hunting
traitors.

Courier of Korocust has 'Korucust's note to Venril' which says all elements are in place, he's ready,
and that Venril Sathir is Korocust's master. He has a potion with an effect of gate to Overthere. He spawns
somewhere in Korocust's room.

Accused Traitor spawns in Korocust's room on 20min? timer but is killed by others. He was KoS to someone
on good Chardok faction I believe. No faction hits for killing but might only be receptive to Ring of
Scale or Minnons of Scale faction (god I hope not Minnons). He doesn't seem to have any drops.
