"Tales from Earthsea" Takes Worst Director, Worst Film at Japanese Razzies (http://news.toonzone.net/article.php?ID=14395)

Tales from Earthsea, the latest feature film from Studio Ghibli, directed by Goro Miyazaki, Hayao Miyazaki's son, took home the dubious Worst Director and Worst Film honors (http://hogacentral.blogs.com/hoganews/2007/01/bunshun_raspber.html) at the Japanese Bunshun Raspberry Awards.

The "awards" are voted on by 32 Japanese movie critics, a group that had no particular affection for the younger Miyazaki's work when it was released there in July.
