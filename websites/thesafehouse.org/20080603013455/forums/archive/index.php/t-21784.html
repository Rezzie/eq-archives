The speed of your offhand weapon is mostly irrelevant, since all special attacks use your mainhand anyway. The only things that really matter for an offhand are the stats. However, the one exception is that if you use dual poisons all the time, then you want a fast offhand - the faster it is, the more chances for your poisons to proc.

Unlike normal procs, rogue poisons are a flat percentage chance to proc, instead of a weighted chance scaling by speed; therefore, a fast offhand will proc poisons more often than a slow offhand. Keep in mind, tho, that non-poison procs (Crusader, Lifesteal, any innate procs, etc) will have the same number of procs-per-minute regardless of the weapon speed.
