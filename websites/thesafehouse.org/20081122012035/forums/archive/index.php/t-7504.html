------------------------------------------------------------

Ok, I know the biggest concerns in killing others in PvP is faction. Some of us recently heard of a certain Mr. Sneakypants, slayer of darkelves, hanging out in Neriak recently. People that saw him were like, "WTF? how does he do that?" Well, here is goes, lets try to start a complete list of all the quests you can go, to become non-KoS in all cities again.

Ak'Anon and Gnome Faction:

I was KoS to all gnome NPCs in the game, now I managed to raise it to dubious, to merchants, guards, NPCs, and maybe the banker.

What to do:

1) Head to the mino caves and kill every Mino in sight, each time you do this, you'll get better faction with the Guards.

2) Kill Every Runaway and Rogue Clockworks that you see in the Valley outside of the caves, while waiting for the mino's to spawn. Collect all "Scap Metal" and "Rusted Black Boxes that drop from them. The boxes are heavy, so if you have a wieght reducing bag, put them in it.

3) When you get non KoS to the guards, and are encumbered, head to Ak'Anon with all your junk

4) Stop at the Warriors (Gem Choppers) guild first. Get a map, because Ak'Anon is confusing as hell (as are most cities in the blasted game). Use hide and sneak when you go in, and sneak behind (this gives you indifferent faction) the NPC named Manik Compolten. Hand him all your boxes and get a bunch of good faction.

5) Head to the necro guild and look for an NPC named Sanfyrd. He should be standing right outside the maze that the Evil Necro and Warriors guild is in. Use hide/sneak behind him, and hand him all your srap metal pieces 4 at a time, for a crapload more of good factions.

Rinse and reapeat until Indifferent or more. This should give ya quite a faction cushion to go out and punt some more gnomes around in your spare time.

Ok, who's next. I'd like for Wiggo to post spoilers like this for his Red Wine (Neriak) and Muffin (Elf/Human?) quests. Also looking for easy ways to all good human cities faction.
