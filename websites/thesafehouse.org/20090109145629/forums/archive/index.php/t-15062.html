SWG has one character per server, so over 20 characters per account. Even WoW will have eight characters per account and they have fewer races and professions/classes.

Character slots is the online equivalent to an offline game's in-game save system. Games such as the original Hitman and Oni were criticized by their save system (Oni's virtually ruined the game for me, forcing me to redo amost entire levels several times), and online games should not be exempt from that kind of criticism. Forcing me to delete a character is forcing me to redo levels should I want to get the character back, and on a much greater time scale than any offline game.

The fact remains that a player who doesn't mind only having four characters per account isn't going to refuse to buy the game if more character slots were offered, but a player who wants more will refuse to buy the game. Sorry SOE, but four characters per account is a dealbreaker for me. Crunch your numbers and get back to me with a better offer and I'll reconsider, but as is I'll stick with the games which offer me the freedom to play them the way I want to, not the way a beancounter wanting to save a few bucks on hard drive space wants me to. Four characters per account just seems stingy to me, which makes me wonder where else SOE is going to choose to be stingy.
