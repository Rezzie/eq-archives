You are correct you need keeper, you already have the preflag for it ("Tylis is being tortured by Saryrn...").

So, kill Keeper, return to seer unlock/guided should get you "Tylis has been removed from his agony."

Then unlock/guided again to resolve Saryn kill. should get "Saryrn has been bested. An aura surrounds the mystical symbols that have appeared on your arm."

Go to the librarian, Hail. That should complete the cypher. Then tell the librarian 'new lore', that should get you "The words of Maelin echo in your mind, 'The Zeks and Solusek are planning an invasion in Norrath, find out more from Rallos if you can." And open of Tower of Sol Ro.

Then back to seer for unlock/guided to resolve the Rallos kill, back to Libraian with "new lore". That should combine the agnar and rz kills to open the elementals.

You lack 3 of the sol ro mini's, so you won't be able to zone to sol ro's chambers, but you should be able to enter PoFire anway.

edit: some "new lore" are "new information" or "what lore". As I personally haven't been there, I can't swear either way. Edited by: Javan Transient at: 4/20/04 8:38 am
