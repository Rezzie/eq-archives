I just started an EQRPG game about 2 months ago, first time DMing in about 4 years, I started them off in Freeport working for the Militia getting Orc belts now they've moved on to Befallen doing the quest in the Player's Handbook (The ghost guy). The magic system works much better for longer fights then the D&amp;D spell system does. Only 1 other person in the group has played EQ and they are really enjoying the game alot. The other 5 members of the group (Yeah I made it 6 people just like EQ hehe) all play D&amp;D and Star Wars RPGs about 3-4 times a week and they have said they love EQs system over D&amp;Ds.
