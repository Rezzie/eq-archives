02-12-02, 04:43 PM
You don't need envenom.

2h provides a higher base damage for opening CS styles (the bonus remains the same) and has a higher cap (due to being slower and 2h's 30% cap bonus). To optimally take advantage of that front loaded damage, you'd want very high weapon and CS spec.

At low levels, you're really best off focusing on 2 skills. Either weapon + CS with 2h backstabs or else weapon + left axe with left axe styles. You can dabble a bit in a 3rd skill, but focusing on keeping 2 near level will help you the most in early PvE. Eventually, you'll want to up stealth more than just letting it auto-train, but early on, it works fine.
