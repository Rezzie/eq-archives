One problem some people have with BitTorrent is being firewalled, and the incoming ports are blocked or something like that (I forget exactly...). If you're behind a router, I suggest going into the settings for it and opening up the ports that BitTorrent uses, as this fixes quite a few download to upload ratio problems. Also, since you're using DSL, you might want to drop your max upload speed to around 13 to 15 or so, as often with DSL, if you're maxing out your available upload bandwidth, your download bandwidth takes a crap.
