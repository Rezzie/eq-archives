09-10-03, 06:28 PM
For those of you who can't see the maps, let me give you a step by step walk thru.

At zone in there is a fountain, you are in a u shaped area that extends out pretty far. just ahead and to the right of the zone in is a bridge of sorts with 4 mobs standing on it.

Cross over this bridge. Directly in front of you is a building, skirt just to the left of this building and follow this corridor till it opens up into a large area with a large building with HUGE steps kitty corner to your right. Go to these steps. Stand in the very middle of them and face away from the building they lead into. You should be staring in the general direction of the zone in.
Directly ahead of you is a small little platform with 4 colums rising from each corner. Sometimes there is a mob standing in the middle of this. Just behind it is a small building. Go into this building. Ahead are some steps. Up the steps and turn right. ANother small set of steps leads into a bedroom with a dresser on the back wall. The Book of Souls spawns on this dresser.

So to recap and make it easy.
1) zone in
2) face out of the zone in and proceed to the steps on the right that lead up over a small bridge with 4 mobs.
3) Go straight ahead off the bridge and skirt to the left a little bit to pass by the building directly in front of the bridge.
4) continue to the large area with the large building and steps off kitty corner to the right.
5) get on the steps and face away from the large building
6) go straight ahead over the small raised platform with the colums and enter the building jsut behind it.
7) up 5 steps, turn right up 5 more steps
loot book off of dresser in room.
