Thats not 100% true shankd.

It really depends on what server you are on and how large the population is.

Im on Daggerspine and have been questing in Stranglethorn recently. Sure ive been ganked but a lot of the time both sides just ignore each other. Around Grom Gol and the Expedition Camp can sometimes get a little hairy but if you are questing its not too bad if you are solo. Just make sure you are always aware of whats going on around you - especially in an area like Stranglethorn where there are A LOT of the opposite faction.

As for tips...I play a hunter so I cant give you anything other than what has worked on me when facing a rogue. If you get the jump on the other person you should be in pretty good shape if you open with a stun move. Thats the only time I have a problem with rogues. Against a hunter you should always ignore the pet and hope that he doesnt have his "Track Hidden" ability active.

And a little side-note. You can play however you want but I recommend not attacking someone while they are mid-fight or directly after the fight when they are low on HPs. There are plenty of people that do it but I think its just a common courtesy. I dont mind getting ganked ten times in a row but if I get jumped while im mid-fight it pisses me off to no end.
