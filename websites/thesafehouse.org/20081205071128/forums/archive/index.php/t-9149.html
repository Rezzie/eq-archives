Maneuvers version adds 6.25% Defense to all members of the group in range. Fully slotted with SO's, this can go as high as 13.75%. This stacks with other buffs as well.

If a group of 8 all have Maneuvers, with no enhancers, that is 50% Defense. Minions that con even have a 50% base chance to hit, Lt's have a base 75% chance to hit. Thus, minions would be dropped to minimal (5%) chance to hit and Lts would be reduced to only 25% chance -- and potentially much more if the power is enhanced.
Nymm
05-19-04, 02:15 PM
Defense modifies chance to hit as % of their base, not directly. so a 10% defense bonus is 50% chance to hit - 10% of the 50%, ie 45% not 40%.

50% defense would be a 25% hit chance for even con minion, not a 0% hit chance. for Lts. 50% defense would be a 37.5% hit chance not a 25% hit chance.

Also there are some claims that stacking is applied progressively. If this is true, then 5 10% buffs wouldn't really be 50% reduction. They would be roughly 41% reduction. This last bit is purely speculation with nothing from the devs either way as far as I know.
