I think its time we created a list of weapons up and coming rogues should shoot for. Please post quested and dropped, daggers, swords, and maces.


-- Daggers --
Blade of Cunning (http://www.thottbot.com/?i=1304) - Quest - Rogue - Level 10 - Horde/Alliance
Bite of Serra'kis (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=9688) - Drop - Blackfathom Deeps - Level 23 - Horde/Alliance - Duo able with 2 rogues at around level 23. Old Serra'kis (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=13418) (Level 26+) is just underneath the Twilight Lord Kelris's room (under water).
Meteor Shard (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=9472) - Drop - Shadowfang Keep - Level 24 - Horde/Alliance - Drops off Archmage Arugal.
Toxic Revenger (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=9007) - Drop - Level 30 - Horde/Alliance - Solo able at level 30 - I did this in the Gnomergan instance that doesn't require a key/picking. Stealth in, follow the path to the right. Drop down to a clear opening, took about 900dmg on the fall. Clear trash around your spot. I killed about 3 or 4 mobs before I engaged the named Viscous Fallout (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=48356). Viscous Fallout is a Green Water elemental, you don't want add's with him.
Hypnotic Blade (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=4015) - Drop - Level 34 - Horde/Alliance - Dagger drops off Arcanist Doan (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=246734) level 37+ in Scarlet Monistary. This dagger has 8int on it so you will have to battle casters for it.
Black Menace (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=3148) - Quest - Obtain quest at level 34, Quest is level 40 - Alliance - You have to kill all 4 bosses in Scarlet Monistary. Quest starts in Stormwind City. You can also get a sword instead, see Sword of Serenity.
Coldrage Dagger (http://www.thottbot.com/?i=Coldrage%20Dagger) - Drop - Level 39 - Horde/Alliance - Drops off Amnennar the Coldbringer (http://www.thottbot.com/?n=329358) in Razorfen Downs. Amnennar is an undead caster. He will summon 2 waves of adds, non-elite but will be 3 or 4 mobs. You can just kill Amnennar and the add's will disappear on his death. I got this dagger 1 out of 3 runs. He was not doable with 2 40ish rogues.
Widowmaker (http://www.thottbot.com/?i=9247) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 42 - Random World Drop. Check link in name to view possible droppers.
Barman Shanker (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=14900) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 50 - Drops off Plugger Spazzring in BRD.
Dire Nail of XXXX (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?s=dire+nail) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 51 - For XXXX put in your animal of choice, mainly Monkey for us rogues. These daggers drop off Shade of Eranikus (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=323361)(55+ Dragonkin) located in The Temple of Atal'Hakkar.
Lifeforce Dirk (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=3725) - Quest - Quest chain starts at 40, Level 44 to get quest, Quest level is 53 - Horde/Alliance - Quest starts in Tanaris with Yeh'kinya. You have to kill Hakkar and loot Filled Egg of Hakkar.
Keris of Zul'Serak (http://thottbot.com/?i=3780) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 55 - Drops in LBRS off War Master Voone.
Frightalon (http://thottbot.com/?i=19394) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 56 - Drops in Scholomance off Kirtonos the Herald.
Bonescraper (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=24339) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 56 - Drops off Baron Rivendare in Stratholme.
Witchblade (http://thottbot.com/?i=19083) - Drop - Horde/ Alliance - Level 57 - Drops in Scholomance off Darkmaster Gandling.
Deathstriker (http://thottbot.com/?i=20470) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Drops in UBRS of Warchief Rend Blackhand.
Heartseeker (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=25585) - Weaponsmithing - Horde/Alliance - Level 58 - Made by Weapon Smiths.
Finkle's Skinner (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=13374) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 58 - Drops off The Beast in UBRS.
Darrowspike (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=20684) - Quest - Horde/Alliance - Level 63 Quest - Actual Quest is Here (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?q=4937). Requires you to obtain Human Head of Ras Frostwhisper from in Scholomance.


-- Swords --
Wingblade (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=2827) - Quest - Horde - Level 13 to start first in the series, but Thottbot says you have to be 21 to get the final quest. - Quest starts in Thunderbluff by Hamuul Runetotem. This is the first Blue sword for a rogue.
Outlaw Sabre (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=4122) - Quest - Horde - Level 25+ - Side quest from "Amongst the Ruins" quest offered to horde which sends you to the Blackfathom Deeps instance. The Original quest giver is Je'neu Sancrea at Zoram'gar Outpost. When you get the fathom core from the alter a level 30 Elite water elemental (Baron Aquanis) pops. He drops a Strange Water Globe which starts the quest to get the sword. All in all it was a very easy quest to do for a level 30 rogue.
Sword of Omen (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=4139) - Quest (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?q=1957) - Level 33 to get quest, quest level is 42 - Horde - You have to kill all 4 bosses in Scarlet Monastery. Quest starts in Under City.
Sword of Serenity (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=3934) - Quest - Obtain quest at level 34, Quest is level 40 - Alliance - You have to kill all 4 bosses in Scarlet Monistary. Quest starts in Hillsbrad Foothils by Raleigh the Devout. You can also get a dagger instead, see Black Menace.
Vanquisher's Sword (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?q=1125) - Quest - Alliance - Quest is obtainable at level 39, Quest level is 42. The quest starts and ends with Archbishop Benedictus in Stormwind City. He asks you to kill Amnennar the Coldbringer in Razorfen Downs. The same guy that drops the Coldrage Dagger mentioned above.
Shortsword of Vengeance (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=13871) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 42 - Drops of alot of mobs, check link to see which ones. Looks random at best. It is bind on equip so one might check their local AH.
Phantom Blade (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=4664) - Weaponsmithing - Level 44 - Horde/Alliance - The hard part about this is that it requires six (6) Breath of Wind (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=Breath%20of%20Wind) which drops off Gusting Vortex (Level 43 - 45) and Living Storm (Level 47 - 49) both of which are in Tanaris.
Tharsh Blade (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=19490) - Quest - Level 45 to get quest, Quest is level 51 - Horde/Alliance - Both Factions have this quest. Both start in Desolace. You have to kill Princess Theradras in Maraudon.
Blade of the Wretched (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=4772) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 49 - Drops off Ogom the Wretched (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=316625)(53+ Undead). He is found in The Temple of Atal'Hakkar.
Krol Blade (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=8407) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 50 - Random World Drop. Check link in name for list of possible droppers.
Dal'Rend's Sacred Charge (http://thottbot.com/?i=23785) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 58 - Drops off Warchief Rend Blackhand in BRS. Also see Dal'Rend's Tribal Guardian for other half of the set. Complete set = +50 attack power.
Dal'Rend's Tribal Guardian (http://thottbot.com/?i=18260) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 58 - Drops off Warchief Rend Blackhand in BRS. Also see Dal'Rend's Sacred Charge for other half of the set. Complete set = +50 attack power.
Skullforge Reaver (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=19910) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 58 - Drops off Baron Rivendale in Stratholme.
Argent Avenger (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=22062) - Quest - Horde/Alliance - Level ?? - Quest chain. Starts from Light's Hope Chapel where you kill the Archivist in live side Stratholme, then get the head off Balnazarr, then Baron Rivendare's head from deadside. Looks like a lightsaber.
Mirah's Song (http://thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=18561) - Quest - Horde/Alliance - Level 60 - Quest chain in Scholomance. Quest giver is just outside the instance and requires you to Kill 3 people. Starting with the Butcher.


-- Maces --
Midnight Mace (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=3738) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 33 - Drops off random mobs. Check info on link to item name.
Mug O' Hurt (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=9436) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Level 41 - Drops randomly off mobs. Check info on link to item name.
Might of Hakkar (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=5049) - Drop - Horde/Alliance - Drops off Avatar of Hakkar (http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=10024) Level 50+ in The Temple of Atal'Hakkar.
