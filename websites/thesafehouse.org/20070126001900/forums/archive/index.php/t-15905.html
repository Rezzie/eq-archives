Since our energy fills at increments of 20, and nearly all the skills i use cost 60, it doesn't actually help that much.

Example:
110-60 (Cheap Shot)=50
50+20=70 (First Tick)
70-60 (Backstab)=10
10+20 (Second Tick)=30 (Note, still too low for Evis even)
30+20(Third Tick)=50
50-35 (Evis)=15
15+20(Fourth Tick)=35
35+20(Fifth Tick)=55 (And still too low for another BS)
55+20(Sixth Tick)=75
75-60 (Backstab)=15
15+20(7th Tick)=35
35-35 (Evis)=0 (Ching!)

Basically, due to the skills i find myself using, the extra 10 finally caught up to me at the 7th tick of energy (and only if i got consistant crits on BS, or i would have to wait even longer due to not having the cp for the 2nd Evis), or roughly 14 seconds of combat. Considering that Thistle Teas reset the whole thing also, i never really found Vigor that helpful.

Note: While this example is theorycraft i have had extensive use of Vigor in CB. (Up to level 50 at least) In addition, my math might be wrong, and if it is, any corrections will be greatly appreciated. Personally, i wouldn't mind adding Vigor to my talent tree if i found out it was crazy.
