A balanced Rogue is a happy Rogue

A Rogue Uber in one/two skills and idiot in rest is a poster on the VN Boards "My class is broken waaaa waaa Mythic won't fix waaa waaa".

Try to keep Weapon... CS... Envenom... Stealth all roughly about equal.

Keep in mind that it's EASY to find +stealth stuff (my char would be +12 but my cap is +9 at 41).

It's a bit harder to find +Envenom stuff but doable. (Oi am +8 envenom)

It's harder yet to find + Piercing stuff. Until the L50 Reward (Shard of Light Pierce +5%) most is +2 or +3 I've seen.

Crit Strike + stuff is damn near impossible after a few Backstab +s in the 20's.

Suggested formulae EFFECTIVE skill till L40;
Pierce/CS near 80% of Level
Envenom near 75% of Level
Stealth near 80% of Level AFTER L25-28.

After L40 and the 1/2 ding trains start getting all skills nearer 90%.
