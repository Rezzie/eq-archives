The Praxium (http://www.vanguardrpg.org/vanguard/site/index.php) is now recruiting individual members and companies. The Praxium will be based on the RP preferred server of Florendyl. If your building your own guild and only have a few people then bring your entire guild to the Praxium. You will be given a charter within the Praxium as your own governing Company. Your company is your guild.

Each company will be given its own company forums at www.vanguardrpg.org as well as its own company room on the vanguardrpg Ventrillo server. Each company room can also be password protected for that company if they so wish.

Company Advantages Include:
• Access to every crafter within the Praxium
• Access to more adventuring teams so finding a group will be easier
• Access to more people gathering you materials to build your crafting skill
• Access to ongoing storylines if you wish to get involved with it
• Access to your own Ventrillo room for free (passwords available)
• Access to your own forums for free (private for each company)
• The ability to govern your own company (guild)

If you’re flying solo and looking for a good home the Praxium is for you too.

Individual Advantages Include:
• Talk to every company within the Praxium to find a Company that fits your play style
• Enjoy relations with growing guilds without being a member
• Access to crafters for your crafting needs
• Access to people forming adventuring groups so you can avoid bad PUG’s
• Access to the Praxium story arch should you wish to get involved
• Access to Company story arches should you wish to get involved
• Access to the vanguardrpg Ventrillo server general areas

The advantages don’t stop there. Visit the Praxium at www.vanguardrpg.org today to find out more.
