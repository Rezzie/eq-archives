I did mine less than two weeks ago and had no problems with adds, nor had I heard of any.

Faction in Steamfont is a little weird, I've found, because it's easy to get bad caster faction without ruining guard faction or merchant faction, and the guards are, like many guards, hostile to those in wolf form. My guess would be that if this happened, it would be because you ended up too close to some of the gnome caster/merchants or merchants who just happened to have bad faction with one of the party members.

I did my Renux battle in half-elf form to eliminate any KoS DE issues due to a few errant gnome caster kills a long time ago, and we avoided anyone using wolf form as much as possible.

Do know that even if you die, Renux might be there when you recover. She was for us, and I was astonished, since I hadn't seen that fact mentioned elsewhere. In fact, she wandered between the PoK book and her spawn point, and around to the dragon bones, and regardless of where we went at her again, nothing added.

Ideally, find a nice open spot to spawn her. Yendar shouldn't interfere. (After all, she's just robbed him as much as you.) And he despawns at some point shortly after she spawns.
