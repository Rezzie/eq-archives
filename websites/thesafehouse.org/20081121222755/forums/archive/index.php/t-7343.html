Quote Statesman
Cryptic Studios Lead Designer

As we work to get the bugs out of the current patch, I wanted to comment on a couple of issues.

First, we will have a test server, where the public can sample upcoming changes. As Gaffer said, we were a victim of our own success here and needed everything we had for live operations.

Second, the changes in the last patch are still going in. We've playtested through the changes thoroughly. Why did we make the changes? The #1 reason is always balance. We don't want to make any power - or power set - absolutely vital for "maximum" leveling speed. The power set Super Speed should offer overall no greater advantage than Super Leap or Leadership or...well, you get the point. Hasten had become "the" power to get because it was just too darned powerful. Of course, there's lots of other issues that go into balance, but that's a major factor.

Thirdly, respec is coming. As some have already noted, it's mentioned as part of one of our "trials" in the Strategy guide. That didn't make it into release, as we'd hoped, but it will be in a soonish update. There's no switch that can just add respec into the next update; some work needs to be done on it.

Changes to powers, villains, rewards are inevitable in a MMORPG. We try to minimize the impact of these changes, but sometimes certain things need to happen. This time, too many communication errors occurred; I deeply apologize for the lack of patch notes (that's not going to happen again). No one should suffer a stealth nerf. And when a Test server is up, some things will be disseminated somewhat earlier.

Thanks for playing and making City of Heroes the success that it is!
