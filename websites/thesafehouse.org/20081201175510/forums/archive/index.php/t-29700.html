There are several broken aspects to these quest lines, though in most cases it benifets the player.

Curse of Vampire: acquired by fighting undead in manor.

Curse currently only activates if you die or log in game at night. It gives you a 10% runspeed buff that stacks with just about all movement effects, including mount and stealth. There is a duration timer of 5mins but when it hits 1s it remains and does not go away. Well eventually it goes away I have had it last over an hour. "Theory it seems to go away when the next game night cycles, so I just camp out and back in and i have it again."

It is also supposed to go away when you complete either Keeping the Curse or Curing the Curse quest, which it does not. Now this is not what the curse is supposed to do but this is what it does.

If you do Keeping the Curse quest you obtain Form of the Vampire, which currently lets you turn into a batform with levitate. Power is only activatable during the night game time, last 5 mins 15 min refresh(as long as its still night).

If you do Curing the Curse you get a fairy pet, only castable during the day 5 min duration 15 min refresh(as long as its daytime). Currently does nothing, it used to fight but was way overpowered, so they just basically shut him down.

Its worth gettting the curse of the vampire now even if you haven't decided which way to go, just for the runspeed buff.

I personally went with the Form of the Vampire, I don't ever see them allowing the pet to tank much at all, and even if it did its for only 5 mins every 15mins during daylight. I much prefer being able to levitate, which has come in handy quite a few times.
