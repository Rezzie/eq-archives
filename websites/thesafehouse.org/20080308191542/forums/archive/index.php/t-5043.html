Stealth 40

40 in stealth is a good number. It gives you safe fall 4 which is pretty useful and it cna be maxed with items

Slashing 29

I would raise this just because every point spent increases your damage.

Envenom 50

No need to take it that high, spec in the low 40s and you can get it to 50 with items. Envenom when raised with items allows you to use the higher level poisons anyway.

Critical 44

Good number. Infils get the 2.5 spec which allows them to raise Crit that high.

Dual Wield 19

Don't really need it. I would raise all the other stats higher and put left over points here

With that spec (DW=3) - isn't his DW chance:

25% + 0.5%*3 + 0.25%*50 = 36.5% (hope my math is right)

That seems a pretty good number - over 1 swing in 3 will be a double swing?

Note that the vast majority of this percentage comes from the base DW chance (25%) and from the level of your character (10%). That's why Dual Wield is the "leftover" bucket to put points in when you don't have anywhere else to put them.
