I'm a follower of Innoruuk myself and I'm also a Tier'dal. Head to Warsliks Wood or Lake Omen and kill as many Gobbos as you can. If you are high enough, sit at the Gobbo camp in WW and camp Grachnist. When your faction is maxed like mine, you will con indifferent to about 99% of Firiona Vie. Once you don a light race illusion, you will con warmly. Be warned on the docks near the Soul Binder, there are some barbarians that are not on the FV faction, and are still scowling without the illusion. If you REALLY want to do it, that's the way I did it. Also, if you get your faction high enough with an illusion, you can turn in Drolvarg Fangs to help speed up the positive faction hits. Good luck!
