Same here Llabak, I couldn't find Windowed mode either.


Skinning is really easy, after find a trainer, spend I think 5 or 10 Skill Points to train in it. You then need to purchase a skinning knife and keep it in your inventory. After you kill and loot an animal, you have the ability to skin it, you right click and go through a tradeskill animation. If you succeeded, a new inventory window pops up with what you were able to skin. You can also skin other people's kills after they've looted em.
