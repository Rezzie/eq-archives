The character dynamics in School Rumble are absoluely wonderful. There have been many cases of "he likes her, she likes that other guy, that guy likes this other girl" type of thing in anime, but I felt it was well-done in this series.

My absolutely favorite character is Harima. I really love Yakumo too, especially when she's around Harima. It's so sweet to watch her.
