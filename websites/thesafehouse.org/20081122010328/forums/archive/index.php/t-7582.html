Hey Sindrachh, this will probably be moved to the arena, hope you find it... anyway, a couple months ago I left Bertoxx to play on Vallon. I had gotten up to level 9 on Rallos as well. Apparently Vallon was more of a purist team server in the beginning. Now it is fairly evenly divided between purists and light vs. dark players and random gankers.

Roleplaying on Vallon is not as much as I had hoped for, but it is definitely better than Bertoxx. There's a real sense of comradery when you fight alongside members of your team, and you will likely never forget the first time you are attacked or the first victory against another player. Another interesting thing is there are a lot more European players, as well as a lot more intelligence casters.
Twinking on Vallon is definitely present and is more discouraging than on Bertoxx, especially when it's your enemy--be glad that deleveling is gone now. Don't be discouraged though, there are plenty of fresh penniless characters, veterans of bluebies or just newbies, on Vallon though. And if you are a skilled player, you will know how where to hunt to get money fast and you will know the best buys that will make you well suited for both P v E and P v P (i.e. bloodstained mantle, DRT, cloak of the ice bear, etc.... if you saw my SK you'd think I was twinked, but I just bought judiciously). The Warrens is truly fantastic for low levels. Prices on Vallon are not as messed up as on Bertoxx (items are generally the same price as the average low prices on websites with less deflation to the low end ones), though Velious items are slower coming into the economy due to the server population. (You'll notice that Vallon has many more exotic items than Bertoxx--lots of masks of obernation around). The human team has no real economy however, so if you are on that team, I recommended you get by as long as you can and then create a mule to trade in Faydark.
I have played up to level 18, and made an enchanter who is now up to 9, both on the human team. If you start in the Qenyos area, P v P is quite rare except for the random raider (or unless you are not human team, then you will get plenty of P v P). The Commons and WFP has plenty of P v P and I have killed many, many dark elves there. There are plenty of high level teammates that are generous and will help out nontwinks with fine steel weapons, split paw tunics, or bronze weapons for selling. Three of my characters have received gifts more generous than what you'd get in Bertoxx, without requesting freebies.

As for Rallos, I played an ogre shaman there and got into P v P encounters only three times. Basically how much newbie P v P you encounter depends on where you start. Joining a guild is probably more important there. Rallos had less twinking, as i remember, but that was a long time ago.
