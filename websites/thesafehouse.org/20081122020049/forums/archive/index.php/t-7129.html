In the end, I'd say with Luclin gear, but PoP spells, a SK can get pretty close to any rogue, higher than a lot of monks (all this for the limited timespan that the mana lasts, about 25 minutes for me with best mana regen buffs and FT 11) and certainly higher than any dual wielding ranger.

Then again, if we're talking about a 2hander using ranger, thats a different league altogether.

the big advantage of SKs over pure melee, at this time, is the fact that the above mentioned spells from PoP were a BIG boost in DPS, while so far superior weapons havent really turned up.

Btw, IMO thats the reason for this whole problem with casters outdamaging melee etc. Every class with spells operates at lvl 64/65 performance since those are easy to come by, while pure melee still use the gear they used at lvl 60 for the most part.

I'd expect the "ranking" of DPS to change substantially for elemental planes / plane of time.
