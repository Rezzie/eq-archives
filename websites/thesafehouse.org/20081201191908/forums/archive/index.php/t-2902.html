We recently started an advanced campaign - all characters begin at 12th level, with 3 minor magic items and 2 major magic items. DM rolled up 24 minors and 18 majors, straight out of the book's random loot table, then let us pick what our characters wanted.

One of the party is a human Barbarian/Fighter with Weapon Master prestige class, who started at 17 strength. Upon picking his gear, he somehow ended up with Gauntlets of Ogre Power (+2 str while worn) and a Manual of Gainful Exercise +5 (+5 permanent strength once read).

This puts him at 24 strength, which is a +7 bonus. However, he's also a barbarian, which allows him to rage twice per day to jack his strength up 5 more points.

This boy will be running around at 29 strength, a +9 bonus, using a two-handed masterwork weapon, with weapon feats out the wazoo. He is at +10 to hit, +16 to damage.

WITH A MASTERWORK WEAPON.

Insane!
