I completed this event with 48 priest,49 hunter, 51 rogue, 52 paladin, 48 warrior.

First thing is go /pvp if your on a blue server. It is believed the AE heal from the ncp will hit your group members.

We then assisted the paladin in killing one mob at a time until they all died. When an Elite showed up we all jumped on it. If the priest pulled aggro he would fade. If the priest held aggro the hunter would distracting shot and taunt with pet. Consentrated DPS is the key. Get them dead one at a time.

When we completed the waves we walked downt he steps and fought the NPC we had defended. Kill the dwarf first as it is the healer.
