I like to write fiction, and I like to develop campaign worlds, so with that in mind....

I'm working on a setting of a mostly oceanic setting, with a couple distant landmasses, but tons of smaller islands. It will be a pirate type setting, but with mystical elements (my main character is married into a family of witches). The central portion of the seas is a area prone to dimensional irregularities, so that many times people from other worlds stumble through, and several powerful entities/nations know how to use it to travel worlds, trade with this world. So the pirates who infest the waters have many choices of victim, but must also watch out.

So the point of the post, I will be typing up the setting, and would be more than willing to share it with others who are interested, so if you are, send me a ezmessage and I will send you the initial work. If you would like to contribute a pirate shipname, crew or or other cool idea, you never know, it could end up in a book someday (with due credit given natch).
