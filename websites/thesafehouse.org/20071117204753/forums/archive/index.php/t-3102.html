the books I recommend (mind you, the older books I have are first edition, but I own the second edition rulebooks. there weren't too many changes, so it's not a big deal):

legend of the five rings core book (second edition has a samurai with an eyepatch). might be called player's guide.

legend of the five rings dm guide (good cultural and background info)

winter court books (kyuden kakita, kyuden asako, not sure what the third is)

those're good books for a general feel of rokugan, politics, culture, etc.

all of the d20/original system mix books are based off of second edition, and I don't own any of them so can't say how they are. the OLD clan books are:

way of the crab/crane/dragon/lion/phoenix/scorpion/unicorn
those books take place in the older timeline, which is about 10 years previous to the current one. the new books are called "secrets of.." and take place in the current one.

so basically, if you're going to play the original l5r system, which I recommend, just go with the second edition core book, and maybe the gm guide and the first winter court: kyuden kakita. then if your players like the system and gain more interest in specific clans, find some old "way of" or newer "secrets of" books.
