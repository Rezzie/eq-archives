If you examine your spells/abilities you'll see each one has a second icon. Those are for heroic oportunities. When an oportunity pops up it will have one or more icons around the outside of the ring. If the next spell/ability that goes off is one of those icons then it will start the chain. The chain will show a larger ring with one or more icons on it and a countdown timer (the outside of the ring). If you manage to activate all the icons then the special effect (extra damage, group buff, group heal, etc) will occur.

Caveats: The first ring will be cancelled if any spell or ability other than the one shown is used. When you manage to get a chain started, your hotkey bar icons will flash if they are required for the chain. If the chain has a green coin next to it, scouts can "reroll" the chain and possibly get a better chain.

As I recall, the icons are all color coded, red for mages, gold for clerics, blue for fighters, and ummm some other color for scouts.

Hope this is what you were asking about, otherwise ignore me.
