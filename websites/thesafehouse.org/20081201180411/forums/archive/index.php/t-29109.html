This is a Heroic dagger from the Beranid's chunk just NW of Southwatch in Thestra. It is a decently long and semi-annoying quest line. The mobs in the area are lvl 39-43 4 dots.

To complete you have to gather symbols (similar to Arena) and summon certain mobs. After defeating all the mobs you can recieve the item.

Stats:

Damage 98 - 119
Speed 2.25
+17 Strength
+4 Constitution
+4 Dexterity
+1 Melee Accuracy
+1 Critical Melee Hit Chance


Quest line details: The Purification of Greystone (http://vg-en.curse-gaming.com/database/quests/details/5885/the-purification-of-graystone/)
