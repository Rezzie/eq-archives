On another note...

If you're looking for Legionnaire Dalini in Netherbian, he can spawn at either of the two static guard posts right next to the Marus Seru zoneline. There are 3-4 other guards who can spawn there, also, and there's no faction hit for killing these guards -- they respawn in about 12 minutes, so if Dalini's not up, just start smacking them down until he respawns. (The wandering guard who paths from Marus Seru down toward the central pillar is not a placeholder for Dalini.) Giving the items to Reothe in Marus Seru does NOT trigger Dalini or have any apparent affect on him, so you can skip that part of the quest completely, if you're so inclined.

Also, Bregun Dorey does not despawn when you give him the Bixie Crunchies or the Note to Bregun, so once Bregun is up, you can have any number of people finish that part of the quest. Killing Bregun is not hard, but it's also unfriendly, especially since Bixie Crunchies are so easy to get (triv at 46, and bixie parts drop commonly -- any vendor in Misty Thicket is likely to have some on them that they bought from a player).
