Quote: The schedule for the new Saturday Toonami block has been released, and tons of new-to-Toonami stuff is headed your way! It'll be airing from 7PM-2AM. That's right, 2AM, not midnight! That gives us 2 extra hours! Whoo! (NOW are you mad that Adult Swim lost its Saturday block? ;-)~ ) Not only that, but there's also a whole bunch of other surprises on the way, too!

- .hack//SIGN, Duel Masters, Cyborg 009, and Samurai Jack return!

-Gundam SEED premieres on opening night!

-New episodes of Transformers: Energon, Duel Masters, and Knights of the Zodiac will be premiering on this block! (And, if we're lucky, we just might get Cyborg 009's banned episode after all!)

-Teen Titans and Dragonball GT will have dual airings on both this block AND Miguzi!

-Movies continue at 8.0PM, with a repeat at 10.0PM, and we get some NEW movies, too, including the skipped Dragonball Z movie "Lord Slug"!

-When Megas XLR premieres on May 1, it will be double-stripped, meaning we get a full hour instead of 30 minutes on its premiere night! Yeah, baby! Also, Rave Master premieres on the same night! Sweeeeeeeeeeeeet!

But the surprises don't stop there! It turns out that Miguzi will be more than just 2 hours a day. It'll also have a weeknight block from 9:30-11PM!
