Creator and voice of top anime 'Conan' tie the knot


Gosho Aoyama, creator and writer of popular TV anime "Detective Conan" and Minami Takayama, the woman who provides the voice for the boy gumshoe, have married, it was learned Thursday.

Aoyama, 41, and Takayama officially married on May 5.

The couple had known each other for about 10 years, meeting while working on an anime created from one of Aoyama's works.

They began dating last year.

"Detective Conan" is known in Japanese as "Meitantei Konan." (Mainichi)
