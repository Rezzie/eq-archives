The 3 Major debuffs for Radiation are,

Radiation Infection
Enervating Field
Lingering Radiation

I personally opted for Radiation Infection as my main debuff until I get Lingering at 12. Infection is a targeted AE toggle power. You pick a mob, infect him, and everyone around the target is infected as well (you see a nifty little graphic effect for all those infected). As soon as the targeted mob is dead the infection dies off and the rest of the mobs are OK to beat you down once again. Infection is easy to keep going (very low END cost) and severely decreases the Defense and Accuracy of all mobs infected. Very, very nice.

Enervating Field looks very handy but the END cost of this toggle power is much higher than Infection. Its a hog and not something to be used while soloing or even on minions, save it for the big boys. It decreases the resists of the mob as well as decreases the overall damage they can do.

Lingering Radation is NOT a toggle power and thus very useful as long as you can keep the mobs in one place (Provoke tanker, controller, etc). It slows down the enemy's attack speed and movement speed by a lot from what I've heard.

Rad/Rad is a very fun build. I never got involved in the Hasten/Accelerate Metabolism thing so I'm just enjoying him as it. In a group such a defender is very useful and quite a bit of fun to play. Just don't get involved with min/maxers who want you to debuff and stand around.
