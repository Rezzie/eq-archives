The second part, where he is magically held from stepping on a boat or being in the ocean for more than 5 minutes...

Does that mean that he CAN step on a boat or into the ocean, and only after 5 minutes comes under the effects of a Hold Person type spell? If so, thats doable. Having the curse make him deathly afraid of large bodies of water, to the point that he needs to make a will save or go catatonic (or berzerk, or act as if he is under the effects of a confusion type spell) might be more fun, though.

Or does it mean that, after 5 minutes in one of those conditions, that he is forcibly ejected from it my the power of the curse? That would be problematic for the GM, as it would basically prevent the character from ever travelling by sea.

The trick on curses is to have them be inconvenient to the character, but not so much to the GM.
