What talents you use are really level and weaopn dependant. I find it amusing for example that you state you think you are loosing DPS by going to the build you are going to over a combat build because at 60 with a good dagger like shanker the build you are going to or maybe a 21/8/22 for the imp BS will get more dps then a combat build any day of the week in a group or raid.

Before you hit 60 though when you are still soloing up to quest and level combat DOES rock because your fights are typically toe to toe battles and combat shines for that. However if you want your role in life as a rogue to be as an offtank then I would suggest making a warrior cause they tank better and can have nearly the same dps as you will with a combat build.

In a group or raid (lesat in higher levels) your are going to have fights tha simply don't last very long. The burst dmg you can get from ambush is really nice then with the benefit of Cheap shot available to you if the group needs some stuns due to a weak healer. However a great dagger, imp BS, Opportunity, and Imp ambush will do higher dps then anything else at 60 cause to be honest you are NOT going to be doing much solonig then.

Till 60 though play with all your skills. Combat rocks then, but I would personally have a hard time setting my entire build around PvP snice I don't really dfind myself PvPing as the majority of my online time.
