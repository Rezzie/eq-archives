05-20-04, 03:29 PM
Build-up and spine burst are VERY important for a spines scrapper. Get 'em at 4 and 6, Reconstruction at 10, QR at 16. You can put off Integration until your 20s, and I doubt health/stamina will be all that hot for you as you'll have to dump a lot of slots into them to make them even noticeable- but by the time you've got room to get them, you'll know if you need them or not.

As for slots, 6 in barb swipe/lunge and either spine burst or impale, whichever you find yourself using more, 3-4ish in the one you use less. 5-6 in Fast Healing and QR, and 3-4ish in Build Up (for recharge reduction enhancements). The rest is mostly a personal preference, but you'll probably want to slot up Ripper a lot too.

Agree on Build Up, it's essential for when something needs to get dead fast. I haven't slotted it significantly though, and don't feel the need to. YMMV if you mind downtime more than I do.

I find Impale is my bread and butter, and I hardly used Spine Burst through my teens (it was handy early when I needed another attack, and it's making a comeback post-Quills, as I take on large groups more). With regen secondary it may be easier to keep up with Burst's significant end drain, though, and it's slow can be quite helpful. Impale is nice simply because you can play blaster on many bosses and lts who you don't want to close with. Either because they've got sick melee damage (warriors, freakshow) or crippling but short range debuffs (sorcerors, storm shamans).
