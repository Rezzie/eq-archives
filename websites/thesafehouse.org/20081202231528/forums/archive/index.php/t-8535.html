This quest starts out with Iramai Sacredsoul in Plane of Tranquility at n250 n1350 in a building next to an NPC named Phanti laying on the ground. Hail Iramai and she mentions something about Phanti. Ask her about Phanti and she'll say they have their suspicions. Ask what suspicions and she asks you to bring a bile from one of the rodents in Plane of Disease.

The bile you need drops off of a named rodent in the caves in Plane of Disease, his name is Rythak. He does not drop it every time. He is located in the spider/rat cave. Enter the cave, at the first big room hook a left, and he's the first rat spawn on the left as you exit the small tunnel. Loot the bile, and turn it into Iramai Sacredsoul in PoT.



The Indigo Orc she speaks of in the preceeding text resides in the Plane of Justice. To get to him, go like you're going to Mavuin, or the deathrow area, and instead of going right at the T junction at the bottom of the stairs, go left, thru the door and right. Brugga is his name, and he is in the third room (I think?) on the way down, the door is on the right.

For the next part you have to be non kos and have 100 in Orc Language. I taught myself Orc, actually not too hard, just takes a bit of time. Spend one practice point at your class trainer into the Orc language, and then just change to it with someone else in the group, doesn't matter if they're in the same zone or not, and spam it until you get a skillup. Zone, spam it again, etc. You can only get one skillup per time zoned if you teach yourself. Took me about an hour to get it up to 100.

I don't know what faction you have to have with Brugga in PoJ in order to have him respond to you. I am an enchanter and charmed him, and that worked, so you may want to bring an enchanter along to charm, as that will take your faction up to indifferent.



Hand Brugga the purified bile.



The 'gnomemade crawlerpoison' is mechanical clockwork venom from the mechanical spiders in the Plane of Innovation. The stinger from a Nettling Wraith is a fairly common drop in the Plane of Nightmare off of Nettling Wraiths. The venom sack drops off of the Terror Matriarch at the spider camp (spawns in between the two consorts) in the Plane of Nightmare. She is a 3 day spawn, hits in the mid 600's, unslowable, spawns a baby spider bout every 30 seconds, or at 66% and 33% life. Turn all three of those in to Brugga and you get the vial of opaque fluid.





Turn the fluid and the bile into Iramai and she will give you the Symbol of Torment, which allows you access into the Plane of Torment. I don't have the exact quest text she gives you on the final turn in, because EQ crashed (go figure).

01-21-03, 08:36 AM
To answer a previous question, Yes this does "screw" up your flagging for progression to the higher planes in a way.

The single player flag quests for CoD, PoTorment, HoH, and PoTactics all allow you to enter the zone. Which if you are not in a guild that can do a raid to drop the requisite mob, this is a perfectly viable option to gain access. However they do not give you the required flag for progression onto the next plane in the line as you would if you completed the flag via killing Grummus, TT, AD, or Behemoth respectively. Therefore, if you do the single player quest to get access to CoD, even though you are able to later go and kill Bert, you won't be able to receive the necessary flag unless you had previously gone back and killed Grummus again to gain his flag for CoD.

So although you are able to enter one of these planes, you can only really expect it to be another exp type zone until you are able to get the real flag from the intended raid encounter.

Quote:When I do "/key" it only lists non-flag keys. How do you get it to list flags and stuff?
Find Seer Mal Nae`Shi in Plane of Knowledge, she is in the building closest to the Plane of Tranquillity stone. Sit down in front of her and say, 'Why guided meditation?' and she will give you a list of all of your flags. If you used an access key (like this one) and then killed a boss mob in the zone (ie Saryn) you still couldn't get further flags without killing the boss mobs usually required to get into the zone you quested for (in this case you quested for Plane of Torment where you would normally have to Terris Thule). Saying to the Seer 'Retrieve my memories?' (or something like that, she'll say exact phrase in hail text) to see which mobs you still need to get flag from to advance (in this case she would say that you needed to go back and kill Terris Thule). I know it was probably confusing, but hope that helps
