Ok, I really was interested in the Stealth powers, for RP reasons for my Scrapper. But really, is there any need for stealth in this game? And it seems like the defensive bonus it gives is a bit weak.

Sure, stealth has lots of uses. For my blaster, it lets me run around the city without getting shot at in passing. And I can scout. As a scrapper, it is nice as it lets you close on mobs without them nailing you, a big issue with scrappers.

I also use it for the bonus to defense, 7.5% lowering of their hit chance. That takes most even con minions from 50% to 42.5%, unenhanced. If I were to fully enhance it with 6 SO defense, that'd modification would go up to 16.5%. Obviously, has the greatest impact on things that have a lower chance to hit already, but even so, not a bad adjustment. If you are a super reflexes scrapper though, not as much reason to take it for that.

Next I was looking at the Speed series. Flurry and Hastening looked interesting. Any thoughts?

Hasten is sweet, drops the re-use time on abilities to 2/3rds of what it is. Fully enhanced with 6 SO recharge, it'll recharge in 2 minutes, 40 seconds. The effect lasts 2 minutes, so 40 seconds without it. Lets you use build up more, as well as attacks you have less of, say, if you only have one AE attack, with hasten, you get a lot more use out of it. If you go for it, I'd advise having some form of endurance regen (either from the scrapper regen secondary or stamina from fitness) as well as slotting it out pretty heavily to gain maximum benefit from it.
