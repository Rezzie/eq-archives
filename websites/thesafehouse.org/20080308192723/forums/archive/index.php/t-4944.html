1. Yes - sort of.
Stealth - and most skills - give a bonus by overspeccing. It's a VERY small edge - not easily noticeable. You can spot it by parsing damage logs sometimes.

So if you have two L50 infils, one with 39 stealth and the other with 50 stealth, both with +10 in bonuses, the one with 50 base stealth will move faster and be harder to see.

As a side note: it's very possible to get over +11 bonuses right now; it was recently revealed that item and RP bonuses to not add up to the item +11 cap. A person can have +9 from RP bonuses (in theory, anyway - that would be a LOT of RPs!!!) AND still get a +11 from items - for a max total of +20.
