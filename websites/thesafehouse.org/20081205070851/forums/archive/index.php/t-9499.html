It takes about 6 or 7 seconds for a rogue to fill the energy bar. Casters don't use energy, but their mana replenishes more slowly (which is probably where the 20 second number comes from).

But the problem with trying to calculate dps is that the energy regains in blocks (of I'd guess around 20 energy/tick) instead of a constant flow so actual dps would be less than theoretical dps. In addition, every move has a replenishment timer of its own before it can be used again, so for example you can't just repeatedly sinister strike.

And just to discourage you even more, remember this is beta - it's not guaranteed to be the same for release, and all the classes are going to be getting their fair share of attention before the game goes live.
