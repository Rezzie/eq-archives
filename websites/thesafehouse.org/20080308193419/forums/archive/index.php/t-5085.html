I can't remember where I hunted at that lvl, but check out a dungeon called The Tomb of Mithra. I believe you may be around the right lvl for it. Around lvl 12 check out Keltoi.

As for equipment, these early lvl's will go by so fast that I wouldn't bother spending cash on anything with the exception of a good player made weap(s).

On the other hand, a player made set of armor at this lvl will hardly cost anything and most of the time people will make it for free. Check the merchant's items before you buy to see whats available.

If you plan on RVR in the battle grounds at lvls 20-24, work towards building your char for this.
