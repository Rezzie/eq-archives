Rule #1 is never go into fight without flash powder and an available vanish.

Rule #2 is pick your enemies wisely. Avoid mages like the plague, but most other classes are fair game. I was always fond of killing warriors. If you have more than two enemies, sap one of them out (and make sure the one left is one that you can kill under the appropriate time).

Rule #3 is use your abilities and items wisely. If you're fighting a paladin, get ready to blast his ass with a crystal before he can cast his shield, and it's the same with priests. If you're fighting a warrior, make sure to use evasion before he does his dang-friggin-mother-effing-painful crit ability on you.

Rule #4 -- and this is for PVE Server -- is choose your area wisely. Don't go picking fights in gadgetzan where there is a couple level 60 alliance mages sitting around. I find it best to go /pvp, and then draw them outside the city. Remember, once you attack somebody and go PVP, anybody can attack you and do the same for themselves. Although most are conservative and would rather not risk it, some people are just plain crazy and love to fight (I was one of them).

It takes practice, but eventually get it down. When in doubt, VANISH AND RUN!

Rule #5, use the right poison for the right foe. I like wound poison vs paladins (less bang for their buck), snaring poisons for mages (blink-run-blink-frost makes it hard to chase them down), ect.

RUle #6, chainstun as much as you can versus casters. I will pop kidney shot before a eviscerate many times and let my natural dps work them over. Thistle tea if definatley your friend vs mage and priests.
