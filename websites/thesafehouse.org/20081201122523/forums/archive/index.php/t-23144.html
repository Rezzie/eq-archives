Well this is the first mmo I've bothered with since I quit EQ(shortly after POP)

Short list of observations
The combat style swinging your weapon,dodging,tumbleing etc is chaotic but very fun.
Traps are creative and very cool A+.
Taverns nice in theory but messy lag fests in reality.
Better LFG system.
Being able to multiclass,triple class,pure class=more individualty in toons.
Group orientaited game however less downtime means less social interactions among players your grouping with ie less likely to make gaming friends in ddo than eq.
Well designed dungeons your not camping your dungeon crawling.

My friends talked me into this I got in cause they where so excited all and all been fun sofar.

Game is pretty much gonna live or die depending on their ability to release new content and quests at a steady rate.
