grats on your fish! what kind of cichlids are they? you could go out and get a 95qt (about 25 gallons) rubbermaid container at walmart or something for like 10 bucks to raise your little fish in and then move them back when they're big enough. use a turkey baster to suck them out and transfer to the container. the biggest pain in the ass though is feeding them because i think they will only take baby brine shrimp since they are egglayers which means you will probably have to start up a mini brine shrimp hatchery. it only takes about 30 mins to do but it gets annoying once you have to do it for a month or so until you can get the babies onto flake food. once they are decent size, you can make a little cash by selling them to the local pet stores. good luck with the fish.
