Guild Name: Lux Arcana

Guild Website: http://vanguard.luxarcana.org.uk

Main Time Zone: European (English speaking)

Guild Leader & Recruiter: Belteyn

A short bit about us:

We are a live guild for Vanguard Saga of Heroes on the Halgar server. Our focus is to provide a solid long-term home for the 'dedicated casual' player based in the UK and Europe. By 'casual' we mean players that have other commitments in real life such as their job, family and other interests, and whose play time can be fairly random. By 'dedicated' we mean players that are willing to immerse themselves fully in the game and give 110% to the guild.

We accept all races and classes, and encourage an equal balance of the Spheres to support each other. We currently have an internal Work Order system where our Crafters make sub-components and finished items that we use as member rewards or sell for profit. In addition members regularly take part in Harvesting nights to provide the raw materials we need, and more recently our Diplomats get involved in raising the City Buffs for Crafting nights.

With a growing number of level-cap players we are currently assembling a casual raid force, and although we will never be a hardcore raiding guild we do hope to raid at least once a week.

As a guild we are now working towards the construction of a Guild Hall. We also have a number of Safehouses throughout Telon that can act a temporary HQ's when required for guild events, and that can be used as bases for raiding. We also operate a Subsidised Housing scheme where the guild helps guild members fund and build their houses. It is also our intention to get involved in building player-run settlements when the game allows.

We hold weekly guild meetings where members share equipments and materials with each other, and where we present awards to members climbing up our internal ranking system. At least once a month after a weekly meeting we will also hold an all-guild event with various prizes.

Our recruitment is nearly always open for the right players, but I must stress that we want players that are willing to make an effort to get involved with the guild and contribute to our ongoing projects. We also want players that are mature, helpful, friendly and outgoing. Above all we like to have fun, make friends, and try out everything that the game has to offer.

Guild category: PvE, Mature, Dedicated-Casual, RP Friendly, Fun

Recruiting members status: Actively Recruiting
