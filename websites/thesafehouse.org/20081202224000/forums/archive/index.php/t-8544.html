The junkbeast is fine. Here's what you do and why it's confusing to folks about placement.

Zone in and go left all the way to the large intersection (or right and past the place with the vendor gnome). When you go left you will need to pull from the right hand side through the doors. There is one mob in there that cons dark blue to 65 (the rest of the zone is green/light blue). That mob is the PH. Kill it over and over and you will get the JB.

It always spawns right there, everytime. The issue is, the thing has a HUGE aggro area. It can see you when fighting down the valley to the cross section through the wall and it will rush you. It also does still camp corpses. This is why it's sometimes out in the valley instead of in him room. He wipes a group and sits there while the bodies pop back at the graveyard.
