05-19-04, 11:59 PM
The beautiful thing is, just about any combination of characters works well together. Sure, sometimes you'll have your team say "a healer would be nice", or "we could use a bit of crows control", but I've yet to find a group makeup that didn't at least in some way work. Healers are not nearly as indispensible as they are in DAoC or EQ, at least at the level I've gotten to (16). They're damn nice, but if you don't have one, you don't have to wait until you can find one before killing anything.

They've done a really good job on balance. You can play whatever you like, and you'll be good at something. t just changes what you're good at and how you go about it.

As far as I've heard, the only pure healers are Empathy Defenders, so you don't have to worry about doing nothing but healing at higher levels with the character you have.
