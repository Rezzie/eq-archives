On one-shotting:

I am not sure what Sanya was referring to and I hope that she was referring to no one-shot kills in the new Battlegrounds only where there is only a 5 level spread. Otherwise, how in the heck would you prevent one-shots in the frontier where there will inevitably be level 10 players and level 50 players side by side. Albeit, level 10 players may be exp-ing in their frontier at their own risk. Still, you don’t have to be an assassin to one shot anybody. Any level 50 will take down any level 10. So this talk about preventing one-shotting really makes no sense to me. You will NEVER be able to prevent it, and you may do a lot of damage to the game balance trying to do so.

About stealth taking endurance:

What a dumb idea. I bet that the persons in favor of that idea had never gotten their stealth characters to even level 20, not to mention doing any meaningful RvR with their stealth characters. Face it, no classes are meant to be effective against all other classes. Archers get killed by assassins and other stealthers. Assassins get killed by tanks and insta-mez characters and other stealthers. It is a rock-scissors-paper arrangement that is the basis for a strategic RvR play. What we see here is “paper” guys whining about “scissors” guys.

Assassins do not have an easy life. Sure they can one-shot grays and remain unstealthed, but hey, if you are a level 30- player dinking around in the frontier, understand that there are some risks. You would be dead the same way from a level 45 assassin and from a level 45 character with speed and mezz. You just won’t see the assassin coming. If you don’t like the risk of being one-shotted, then go to BG or level in the safety of your own realm like everyone else.

I challenge all who posted in favor of this silly “stealth should take down end” suggestion to try to get some assassin kills in RvR. I am a level 45 NS with 32.5K RPs and I am just getting the hang of the strategy. Only then you will realize the dangers from other stealthers popping after you take down your victim, insta mezzes that stop you while running away, the 10 sec restealth timer from last attack (including arrows that archers keep shooting at you), roaming guards that happen to just walk over you as you unstealth, etc. When you get to take down 10 victims per portal run (even if gray), come back here and post exactly how you think stealth should be nerfed.

Bah!
