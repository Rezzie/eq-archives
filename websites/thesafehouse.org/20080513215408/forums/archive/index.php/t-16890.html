Problem fighting pallies is a few things.

1. Their immune clears all effects, so you lose Dots, poison effects, warrior loses rend and deep wounds, ect

2. They can fire off the immune while stunned. So unless your lucky they can use it to avoid a finish blow for a while.

3. While they have range deficieincies, most hardcore pvp server pallies are using engineering to compensate (being turned into a gimpy leper gnome will ruin your escape).

4. One of the shields does allow attacking, but at a 50 percent slower rate IIRC.

5. Hvy Rune bandaids can be used while immune for a good heal and no mana expenditure

A couple things I use to my advantage

1. Blind poison. works great for giving me a second (or with prep 3rd) chance to bandage in a longish fight.

2. A good hard hitting ranged weapon. When he immunes, get some distance and hit him with a 200-300 pt shot to whittle him some.

3. Gouge, cheapshot and kidney shot. Anything that pauses him and lets you get a good solid shot in (I use daggers), thus wearing down his number of immunes,LOH, heals ect.

4. I will always drag the fight into 1 or more mobs and then vanish to drop them onto him. More damage and chance of them finishing him off if he kills me.
