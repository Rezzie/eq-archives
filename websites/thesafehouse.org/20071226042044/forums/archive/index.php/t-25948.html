Mushishi, while heartwarming at times, is often very heartwrenching. Fullmetal Alchemist is also often very depressing. One REALLY sad show is Koi Kaze, very few shows have ever put me through such emotional turmoil. Haibane Renemi, Voices of a Distant Star, The Place Promised In Our Early Days, Binchou-tan, and Saikano all come to mind.

Yomigaeru Sora -Rescue Wings- made me cry. It was one of the most incredible anime I've seen. Too bad nobody takes notice of it.

Kino's Journey (Kino no Tabi, Kino's Travels, take your pick) is great. Air is recent, and sad, but I think you already saw it. Full Moon wo Sagashite is another tearjerker. Now and Then, Here and There is brutal. Samurai X: Trust and Betrayal are powerful. Kousetsu Hyaku Monogatari (Requiem from the Darkness) is horrific and depressing.

Le Portrait de Petit Cossette is another favorite. Symbolic, depressing, and brutal. Perfect Blue is a psychological thriller that will get you really ****** up. Jin-Roh The Wolf Brigade is disturbing.
