I'm usually on for a few hours every weekday. Send me a tell, name's cylant.
I'll just sum up a few things, if you have any other questions ask me in game.
1)Steamfont is one of the worst newbie zones as far as pks. I leveled a gnome rogue and a gnome necro there, so i know. 50% of the people you meet there will be pks. 99% of the magicians you meet there will try to pk you. 99% of the naked warriors will try to pk you. I advise you to solo in a far off corner, or at odd hours of the day until you work up to about level 8. At around that level you can go to butcherblock, where you can basicly level without being bothered.
2)Pk heavy zones
-levels 1-10
--any newbie zone/newbie dungeon
-levels 10-20
--areas just outside of newbie zones(commons)
-levels 30+
--oasis, sro, the dreadlands.
3)PK guilds
-low levels
--flowers of happiness
--shortenbane
--reign of pain
--malificus sect(they rock.. really nice people... but they do pk with heavy roleplaying restrictions)
-higher levels
--darkenbane
--house in'visus
--reign of pain
--malificus sect
4)Respected/good guilds you could join
-silent decree(canbik's guild)
-warriors of wit(my guild)
-true alliance
5)Warnings
-don't join some large guild where you don't know anyone. Guilds like this generally have one or two pks that ruin the reputation for all of you
-gnome casters are evil, avoid them at all costs =)


The server is a blast. It manages to basicly preserve the PvE part of the game, and leave PvP as a passtime or a solution to a problem.
Don't be discouraged by the newbie zones!
