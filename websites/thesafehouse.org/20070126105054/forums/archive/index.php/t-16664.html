Not sure there are any real strategies,except the usual "don't suck" strategy.
Two things come to mind right away:
1) Get shadow protection potions from your local alchemist(and any other potions that would help)
2) If you have a rogue, let him/her sneak up to Arugal before fight starts, there is no aggro when stealthed, so rogue can be ready to Ambush as soon as fight starts, instead of having to stealth up stairs while tank is already fighting for a few seconds.

I think success can be vastly affected by wherever Arugal decides to jump to the ledge you can't get to. Thats a few seconds of no melee that really hurts.

I think its important to start moving towards Arugal right away when he jumps, don't stand around for a few seconds wondering where he went. Look while moving.
