If you get him single it shouldn't be any problem.

Get a SoW and an Aego/Virtue.

Give 5th ring to Korrigan, follow Icefang across EW or just book it to the Orc Hut near the Iceclad bridge.

Probably best bet is to sneak into the hut since you can't track to find out when they pop.

Wait for the guy to pop, run out back, un-hide a fair ways off and slowly approach to hut.

He will agro on you (hopefully before Icefang gets there).

Back up a ways and take him down, shouldn't be too difficult.

As a ranger I've done this 4-5 times solo, but the advantage of track and being able to root/snare off extra mobs make it a lot easier.
