Don't think Adept 2 is in game yet. App 2 is bought from the NPC trainer. Adept 1 and Master 1 are drops. App 3 and Adept 3 are PC crafted, but the reagent for the ink used in Adept 3 crafting is an extremely rare harvested component. To obtain Adept 3s, you're best bet is to acquire the rare ingredient then find someone to make the skill upgrade. You'll need an alchemist for the ink and a jeweler for the skill itself.
