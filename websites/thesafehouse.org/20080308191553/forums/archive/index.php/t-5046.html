EZ_Valarauco
04-29-02, 09:57 AM
"but I'd rather see my damage increase every time I attack than 43.5% of the time."

The flaw in this logic is that you only PA once per battle, whereas CD is checked "every time you attack". The damage increase on non-hidden CS styles for 2 pts of CS is probably pretty minimal. There are other intangibles to CD, such as landing two venoms early or right away.

As I said, though, I'm not saying you're wrong. I'm also not saying high CS isn't good, just that IMONSHO*, the return on investment is probably better raising one skill many points from a low spec than another skill a few points at a high spec.

My only suggestion is to go with 44/15 CS/CD instead of 46/7. Same styles and general flavor of your current template. I don't think you'll regret it, but feel free to ignore me.
