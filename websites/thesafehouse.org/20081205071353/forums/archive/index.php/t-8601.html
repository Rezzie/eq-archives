Camp in the tower until Heratius Grolden spawns on the balcony. When he does, kill him. This will spawn Valdanov Zevfeer, the man you need, in the center of the balcony. Stays up for about 15 minutes and then poofs if nobody aggros him.
As for resisting his lifetap, it's probably possible since you can resist the normal vampyre tap, but given that it's 1k per tap on Valdanov you may not want to go experimenting either. He's killable with one good group without going to the trouble of getting your MR up.
