i never really had a problem with both poisons going off relatively early in a fight, only rarely it didn't. some advice i was given is to put your debuffer poison on your main hand weapon and your DOT on the offhand. because you want your debuff to go off ASAP so the creature cant hurt you as much. the DOT can go off at any time and still do damage, as long as the creature is alive.

oh yah. raise your weap skill to 1/2 or 2/3 of your level soon as you can, it will help you a lot. i wouldn't raise celtic dual again until you do that.
