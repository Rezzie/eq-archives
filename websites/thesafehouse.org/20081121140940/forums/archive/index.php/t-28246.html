Ok after much guesswork I finally figured it all out up till getting the quest inside.

You should to be level 16+ to do the first 5 steps and 19+ for the rest. All the mobs involved passed the 2nd step are 3 - 4 dots 19 - 22. If you go even further into the tomb it becomes 25+ level mobs.

1) Talk to Master D inside the house at Lotus Hold SE of the Jin'Ka Forest (ouside the forest)

2) Accept quest "Ulvari Weakness" and complete and turn in to him

3) Accept quest "Into Jin'Ka Forest" and complete and turn in to him

4) Talk to Da'lar in the forest (near entrance, head straight east and down hill)

5) Do quests from other 3 wardens in the forest to be "blessed"

6) Turn in to Da'lar once Blessings complete and head to the temple with the key it gives

7) Head up the stairs, take a left at the top and go up the next set of stairs

8) Head up the stairs against the back wall and go up to the glowy orange colum on your right in the middle of the platform area

9) Go to the side facing outwards from the temple and open the door then click the crystal inside to teleport in

10) Once inside clear the first room (has the axe) then the next room you come to has the dagger quest

11) Farm all the mobs inside the tomb and in the Jin'Ka forest ((only the 4 dots in the forest)) for the random gem drops (symbols ARE tradeable)

And thats as far as I got tonight. Took about 5-7 hours to do all that x_X

If you want the longsword instead of the dagger like I did it's a bit more work. You have to go passed the first few rooms then take the elevator UP (should be on your right after the dagger room). A few rooms in you'll come to another split where you need to go UP again. Few more rooms back you'll find a named golem and the altar for the longsword behind it. You've gone too far up if you see level 25 mobs.

More uptades as I do the rest this weekend : P
