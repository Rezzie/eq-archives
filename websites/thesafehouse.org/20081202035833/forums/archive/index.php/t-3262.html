The Safehouse -NWN web site opens its doors
07-14-2002 - Aidden
The Safehouse is proud to present the opening of the Safehouse – Neverwinter Nights Web site.

The Safehouse is known for being one of the largest MMORPG fan sites in existence. Offering its members quest assistance, community support, and general game information, the staff of the Safehouse continues its commitment to the community by offering a new site dedicated to Neverwinter Nights.

Features include a character tracking database, event calendar, and a module distribution library. Members will be able to join the “Virtual Safehouse”, a persistent NWN module modeled after the Safehouse Forum community and although the Safehouse is a Rogue dedicated community, we will accept all classes of players.

Come on by and check us out, and sign up for an account today!

The Safehouse - Neverwinter Nights
-------------------

Before you ask, the Game server is not open as of yet..Feel free to get your aco***** created, and check out the site..

Those with Memberships will be receiving game server info in the mail.

Have fun!!

P.S. If you have any problems with the website, please use the bug report link listed in the Staff section on the website, please dont use the forums, as they may not get the attention they need.
