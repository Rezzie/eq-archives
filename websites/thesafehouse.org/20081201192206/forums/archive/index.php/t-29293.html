Me and a guildie have been working on this one for a few, and we managed to locate the one in River Palace yesterday. If you go up the stairs you'll come across a room with doors to the sides, most of them leading to bedrooms. The guy you're looking for is in one of the bedrooms to the right as you come up the stairs. If the door is locked, try moving into another bedroom and using the back door from the balcony.
