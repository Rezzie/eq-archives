I personally would not have picked a PVP server if you wanted to chill and have fun. With the honor system in place PVP servers are hell for leveling up. It doesn't bother us level 60 folks, but I feel for the lowbies.

But anyway, on to your questions. A good build for a leveling rogue is to go combat heavy and use swords/maces. However, when you don't have a dagger equipped you lose the use of backstab and ambush if you did not know that already. Sub-60 a combat heavy rogue is also perfectly viable in PVP, as backstab and ambush don't really start to shine until the end game when you have a nice dagger.

Spec down through combat to Blade Furry at least. DW Spec and Sword or Mace Spec are nice leveling up talents. Make sure you at least pick up Malice and Imp Evis from assassination tree and maybe some MoD from subtlety which makes life easier to sneak around.
