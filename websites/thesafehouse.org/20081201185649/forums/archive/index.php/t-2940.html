A little background. Our adventuring party is the remains from a military force that was sent to investigate a temple. We were attacked by a dragon, and all of the troops were killed. Now, me...the lovable undercover military police...aka rogue...is stuck in the middle of the situation.

During our struggle to find food, shelter (a big storm hit us hard), and to just survive...we befriended a group of refugee orcs (not evil on this world) and attacked a dragon. Due to the lack of food, the orcs informed us that trolls make good eating. Usually, you cook it first, but if you eat it raw, you "absorb its power, and become a true warrior!"

To make a good impression on our orc allies, we all ate some (well, except for the annoying elf wizard, and...um...she ended up being the only intelligent one!). The results are...interesting. Those who survived a fortitude check gained max hps for their next level on the roll. However, the unlucky thief missed his by one point. Along with several other allies, we now have a small troll growing in our stomachs. It is too late to induce vomiting.

Next game session is in a week. Any ideas on what to do?

Supplies:
None

Magic:
1st lvl cleric, 1st lvl druid, 1st lvl wizard
