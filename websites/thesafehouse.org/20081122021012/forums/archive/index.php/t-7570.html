FBSS = 8-10K on VZ

First off you wanna get your standar Wolf-eye chanter made stuff.

Plat Wolf-eye Neck- 150-200pp

2 Wolf Eye Bracelets- 50pp each

BI Crown or Ry`gorr helm- 100-150/500pp

Dwarven Ringmail tunic (if you can wear it)- 20pp

Sarnak Emblazened Tabard (for a medium/large race) - 250pp

Ravenscale or Rygorr arms/legs/tunic/gloves- about 200-500 each

RS gloves are really nice if you can find them.

Cloak of the Icebear- 100pp

Troll Hide belt- 200pp

Blood stained Mantle (A MUST for all rogues)- 35-50pp

Mask of Deception (good luck)- No drop

Split Paw hide mask- 30pp

Ry`gorr Collar- 500pp


MR is key, try to get that to 100+ and you'll be all set. After that, worry about FR and CR, then PR at higher levels. ALWAYS keep your MR gear on unless you are fighting mobs, or 1 on 1 with a pure melee, then go for AC and STR.

When you get some extra plat, try to get these items:

Plat Jacinth Rings (+13 MR)- 2K
Earring of essance (+10 MR)- 3K
Crown of Froglock Kings (+20 sv Fire/Cold)- 2-3K [b]Ceescrit Bossalini
