A very easy one, in the erudite ghetto in Qeynos (forgot the name). There's a kitty and a little girl, and all you have to do is talk to one or two people in the zone (I'm sorry most of the details escape me) and you get a cage for a pet as a reward to place in your home.

Another is the "Know Your Gnolls" gnoll mastery quest. Fairly easy, it starts in Antoinca, at gnollslayer keep. Click a glowly chest and it gives you the know your gnolls book. Simply kill a fuckton of gnolls and collect tail/tooth/paw/eye .etc. Reward is a ton of exp, a book to place in your house, and a halbred to place in your house. It's called the gnollslayer or something similar, the description is cute "Yip! Yip! Thunk".

One more book quest, "The Flood of Blackburrow". I believe it starts with a dropped book called, you guessed it, the flood of blackburrow. Read it and complete the tasks, it basically requires you to kill 5-10 of almost every type of mob in blackburrow, but the exp is nice. Final reward, exp, coin(?) and a book to place in your house "The Flood of Blackburrow", detailing one barbarians treck accross bb in ye olden days.
