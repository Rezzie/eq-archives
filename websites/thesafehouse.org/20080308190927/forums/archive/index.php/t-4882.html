In large scale RVR, it's best to coordinate with other stealth players to attack the rear or side of the prey.

For example, most casters and healers will stick to the back of the army. So one day in big RVR, me and a minstrel circled around an opposing army and took out a few casters. One was medding so he got my perf artery. The minstrel helped finish him off, even with heals we killed him fast. Then we took out a few more.

By then our army had advanced. I'm convinced our coordinated stealthed attack in the back helped our side a great deal.

Also having 5+ high level assasins helps a great deal for keep attacks. We can take out npc archers or take out or at least distract enemy forces. Actually just sneaking up and hitting one with disease and jumping back to the ground outside is often enough. It's dangerous and if you die in the keep you will likely have to release, so hit quick and jump away fast.

You'll cause enough chaos up there to weaken their defenses, and likely cause a few deaths as well. Instead of attacking the army at the door, they will be watching their backs for more assasins.

Large Scale RvR try this:

Wait for troops to engage. There will constantly be small skirmishes breaking out, with tanks running at each other in the "no man's land". When one of these occurs, an enemy nuker will run forward and try to nuke your tanks.

Stealth up to him and beat on him - wait until he has begun casting to get real close, and make sure you are picking one near the front, NOT the back (this is for large scale RvR fights that last awhile). Hit your PA, your stun, and maybe 2 normal attacks and sprint like hell if he's not dead for your side.

90% of the time you will kill him if orange con or less, and get away. If you don't get away, you will at least get to sprint for your side and be within rez range. Stalking to the back and dying/releasing sucks. If at any point after you attack you start getting hit, turn and sprint for your side, even if he's reeeeeeal close to dying. If you get away, that guy will die eventually, and you willl get rps. I almost soloed a red-con caster once, but he got away, and got healed. 5 minutes later someone killed him and I got 350 rps.

Almost everything we can do is covered by others in a large scale fight.

You have archers/casters safely take down other archers/casters from long range. There are abundant of these classes so you dont really have to worry about not enough of them.
