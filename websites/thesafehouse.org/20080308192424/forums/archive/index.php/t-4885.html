As a nightshade turned ranger, I can tell you the order of power of archers from a purely class design standpoint: 1) Hunter, 2) Ranger, 3) Scout. What I *can't* comment on is the availability of good gear, quests, or Realm content specific issues.

Check daoc.catacombs.com for exact skills and abilities but here's a quick summary:

Midgard Hunter:
2H Spear melee (slow, but high damage)
Beastcraft (Self buffs, PET spell, Not as many self buffs as a Ranger[missing STR I think..])
Bows (lowest delay out of the 3 archer classes, this means best chance at interruptions)
Stealth
Archery
Commentary: Beastcraft is superior to Ranger Pathfinding because while a Ranger is sacraficing spec points they could spend in melee into pathfinding and a hunter does the same, their PET makes up for their lack of melee ability. So they're really not losing a thing! Additionally, they can hide and retain a pet. Also, they can sick the pet on a caster or archer to cause interruptions. Additionally, a Ranger has 2 lines to spec melee in: Melee type (piercing, blades) and Celtic Dual. Hunters need only worry about 1. And you may argue if 2H or dual weilding is better damage, but the pet will make up for any perceived lack. I feel these factors makes them the best Archer class out of the 3.

Hibernian Ranger:
Melee: Piercing or Blades, plus a Dual Weild skill called "Celtic Dual". At 1 CD, you have a 25% chance for offhand weapon to go off. Can weild a shield, but it's not a trainable skill like Scouts.
Spells: Pathfinding line of self buffs (Str, Dex/Qui buff, AF buff, Damage add buff, getaway spell)
Stealth
Archery, bows in the middle speed, but slower then hunters.
Comments: Middle range power archer. See Hunter vs. Ranger comments above.

Albion Scout:
NO spells (why?)
1H melee plus Shield ability (trainable, I believe, but dont quote me)
Stealth
Archery, slowest bows mean largest crits and larger chance of 1-shotting targets however this also means less chance to interrupt spells and other archers, so its a trade off, they are almost more geared towards a life of sniping.
Comments: Least powered of all 3 archer types in my opinion. One realm specific issue in the favor of Albions, is that they are the only realm with a frontier that acts like a frontier. Their first 2 frontier realms are extremely dangerous to travel to. Snowdonia isn't all that dangerous, but it's filled to the brim with realm guards. Forest Sauvage isn't really used, and most mobs are gray at the level you can head there to PK so there aren't many targets since noone is there XPing. Not many go there to Alb to PK because of these factors, so you will not have to worry about leveling being interrupted as much as if you were a Mid or Hib.
