Actually axes are superior to swords if you use left axe. The reason is your left axe is always a drop/quest after level 20 which always add +axe, and so you can get up to +11 axe fairly easily wielding two axes. By comparison if you use a sword and an axe, you end up with maybe +6 sword and a +5 axe which is useless. Plus there are nearly ZERO good sword drops in Midgard from 25-35, but many more axe drops.
