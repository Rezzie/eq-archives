Some clarifications:

The Book of Ultimate Feats is from Mongoose Publishing
Some may be unbalanced, but it is a compilation of feats from a multitude of open sources, not just their own.
Additionally, feats are like chinese buffet, take what you want and leave the rest.

Gygax is primary with Troll Lord games, though he does do other stuff for other companies on occasion.
Necropolis from Sword and Sorcery games for example.

Sword and Sorcery games is a part of White Wolf. Which also has Necromancer Games
(Necromancer Games products are published by Sword &amp; Sorcery Studios and distributed by White Wolf.)
Sword and Sorcery is the one who put out "Relics and Rituals"

Traps and Treachery is actually from Fantasy Flight Games.

Freezing the Lifeblood is a feat from Oriental Adventures.

Dirty Fighting is from Sword and Fist, therefore not open content, therefor not in the book of Ultimate Feats

More information:

There is also a book called "Feats" from Alderac Entertainment Group which is another compilation of feats.
