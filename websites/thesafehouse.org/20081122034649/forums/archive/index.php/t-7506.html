My in game sister is a dark elf shadowknight, (on Tallon Zek), and now, at level 50, she is terrifying. However, the road to where she is now was long and frustrating. Shadowknights tend to get owned until they get their lvl 29 (?) spells. I'm not sure how it is with other classes, but the difference between attacking a shadowknight a level before and a level after their next spells is immense. If you are willing to do some hard painful work, being handicapped while the other classes go through their sweet spots in their 20's and 30's, in relation to PvP, it will be worth it. I'd say the same goes for rogues. You never get enough money or good equipment to be really devastating until your mid 40's.
