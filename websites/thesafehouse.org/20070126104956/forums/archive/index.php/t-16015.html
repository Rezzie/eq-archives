The boss should be soloable at 22 (dagger spec, imp gouge/backstab &amp; malice5), if:

1) you land both ambushes (ambush, vanish, ambush)
2) you pop a greater healing potion (the 400ish one) at some point during the fight
3) you burn evasion early
4) you have something in the way of buffs (armor potion, mark of the wild), or much better gear than I have

I got him SO CLOSE (down to one or two hits) twice and gave up. I'll try to solo him again tomorrow, before I call in the wrecking crew, and see if maybe using expose armor instead of evisc on the first 5cp move helps (at 22 a decent backstab is almost as good as a 5pt evisc, and a crit BS is almost double it). Also I'll bring better potions than the lesser healing I had on me at the time ;)

Oh and FYI, the patrollers don't seem to be weak to anything (haven't gotten rupture yet :oops ), but the lookouts do some funky morph-into-a-slime thing when you stick 'em with an evisc, even a 1cp. So far the hardest part (besides Gillywix the Humongous Bastard) has been getting the 2 mutated drudges on the bottom floor solo, since you don't get credit for killing them if you use their weakness to garrote you've got to duke it out with a 24-25 mob. It's not the hardest thing in the world (I didn't even use potions on them, just gougedanced them one at a time) but it took me a few aborted pulls to get them to play fair.
