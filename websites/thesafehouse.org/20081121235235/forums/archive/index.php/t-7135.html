Reason why it doesnt stack....

Skin like Wood and the Courage line share the same stacking components (HP/AC). This goes all the way up to Aego/Virtue which is a combination of Courage, Holy Armor and Symbol line.

As for why they dont stack... The ability to add another 470HP and 24AC to a tank in a raid situation where Shaman buff is already adding 544 HP (and some STR/DEX). Add all that HP up...

470 + 544 + 1500 = 2514 HP in buffs compared to 2044. an extra ~500 hp could mean an extra hit taken by your tank and Verant wants you to lose encounters Slowmotionz Unstopable
