What I'm personally looking forward to is Honey & Clover the second season, Binbou Shimai Monogatari, Bokura ga Ita, Mamotte! Lollipop, Chokotto Sister, NHK ni Youkoso, and Tsuyokisu.

Do you all remember Tohsaka Rin from Fate/Stay Night? Her behavior pattern, where she is very strict at times, and when Shirou says something that gets to her, she blushes and becomes somewhat vulnerable. This behavior is tsundere, the basis of the whole game of Tsuyokisu (Strong Kiss directly translated, but makes no sense. It's kind of a pun on Tsundere, you could say. An act of strength, and a act of passion, individually.

I'm suprised the eroge is not based around the male lead, but one of the girls. It's unknown as of yet whether the male lead will be there or not, but if he's not it'll be quite interesting.

I'm also looking forward to Honey and Clover which should be more serious this season.

And man, will Chokotto Sister fill my little niche :p I can't wait! Those extremely averse to things that might seem close to lolicon would want to stay away from this.

I'm definitely looking forward to NHK. It's a little more hardcore and over-the-top version of Genshiken. It almost goes without saying that if you enjoyed Genshiken, you'll enjoy NHK. On the topic of Genshiken.... the previously rumored second season turned out to be a season of Kujianbiki Unbalance, not Genshiken S2. I'm half disappointed.
