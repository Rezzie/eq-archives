I was very, very impressed with this. The animation quality is on par with the best I've ever seen in a series. Very well drawn, and very fluid especially in the action scenes. I can't comment much on the story yet, but the characters are all engaging. The cuts between scenes were a little wierd at times, as was the non-sequential edditing, so Im sure some people will complain about it, though I thought it worked well stylistically. Overall its very promising, and may turn out to be one of the best series done in a long time.
