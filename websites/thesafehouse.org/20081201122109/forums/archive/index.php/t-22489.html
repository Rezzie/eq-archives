Guild Recruiting

Seren Llys Du is open for new members. We are a casual guild that enjoys having fun in a family type atmosphere. As we wait for Vanguard Saga of Heroes to release we schedule chats to get to know each other and just chat about Vanguard. At this time our plans will be on building up trade centers and routes throughout Telon. Crafters, Diplomats and Adventurers will all be welcome. Honor, Loyalty, Integrity, Chivalry, Knowledge, and Excellence are the foundation of Seren Llys Du. If you are interested in finding out more about us, please feel free to visit our website at http://seren.guildportal.com or contact the guild leader at ladyS@tentonhammer.com.
