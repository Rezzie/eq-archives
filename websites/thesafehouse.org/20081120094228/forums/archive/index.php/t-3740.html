SEER INFORMATION

Find Seer Mal Nei`Shi at Neg 40 / Neg 200 in PoKnowledge.
Say 'Unlock memories' until they repeat or you see:
You manage to recover some images from your childhood, but no recent events spark a memory.
If her replies are the same after multiple 'unlock memories' and does not have the childhood quote, that is a clue to some flag(s) you are missing. Find the quote below for help on what to do.

Now say, 'guided meditation'. .... here is what they mean!
Cyan means you have finished a flag and are ready for more
Orange means something is not finished for the final flag.

Tier 1 - JUSTICE
The evidence of Mavuin is the only thing that can save him now.
- Preflagged for Mavuin
Having endured the trials, the Tribunal has agreed to reconsider Mavuin's case.
- You have the preflag, completed the trial, and talked to the Tribunal, but have yet to go back and hail Mavuin

- Received Grummus preflag by saying "What ward" to Alder Fuirstel next to PoDisease entrance. Grummus not defeated.

- Grummus chain complete and have access to Crypt of Decay
