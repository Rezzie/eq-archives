03-04-04, 10:50 AM
Kingdom Hospital is loosely based on the story of Waverly Hills Tuberculosis Hospital, off Dixie Highway in Louisville, KY. It was a place where they sent people for treatment for tuberculosis and mental illnesses in the late 1800s and early 1900s. The place obviously had an extremely high fatality rate, and ghost stories started circulating.

If I recall correctly, the original hospital burnt down in about the 1920s, and another hospital was rebuilt around the 1960s. However, patients wouldn't go to the place, because it was supposedly haunted. One especially freaky thing there was that within two or three years of being built, the building had deteriorated so badly that it looked like it was 100 years old. I think it ended up being a nursing home, but was shut down because the staff were beating the people living there.

Kids from my high school used to go there on Halloween and sneak around the building. If I had known this story then, there's no way I would have gone.
