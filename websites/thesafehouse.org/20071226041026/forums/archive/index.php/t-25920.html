Well its easy to spot bootlegs. More than 5 episodes per dvd (as a general rule) is bootleg. Chinese subs is bootleg. Region-free is bootleg.

That aside, Full Metal Panic? Fumoffu! Which is 2nd season of the FMP series is great. 100% comedy. You may want to watch the first season, Full metal Panic! Which is 70/30 mecha/drama and comedy respectively.

Excel saga is along the lines of abenobashi, but different enough that they are not that closely related. Its a comedy.

Gankutsuou is one of the best anime Gonzo has ever producted, and oddly is not getting much attention. Its an adaptation of The Count of Monte Cristo, and incredible one at that. The art style is an eyesore for 2 episodes before you get used to it. By eyesore I don't mean bad, its unique and awesome, just takes a bit getting used to.

Ghost in the shell is great too. Fits your 15+ rating.

Now for something off the wall, how about giving Hajime no Ippo (the first step in usa) an chance? Its an excellent boxing anime. Very engaging and entertaining even if you're not a sports fan. I don't watch any sports, but I ended up loving the series and rooting for him the entire way almost fully released in the us now.
