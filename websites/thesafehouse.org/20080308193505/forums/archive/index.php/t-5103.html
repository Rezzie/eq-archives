First of all. 35 stealth, 36 envenom is all you need for base to get to 50 with DF and epic armor and realm rank. Most people will never to get rr4 or 5, but more items will surely come out in the future and spellcrafting which should eliviate the burden of getting to rr5.

Then you must have max weapon, which would be 50. Gives you great damage varience.

Then get critical strike as high as possible after that.

I have been looking at 50 cs and 50 axe. Thinkiing about dropping cs down to 39 since the last two styles are good damage but 3 and 4 in an evade chain.

As you level, keep weapon max and cs right up there for the styles. Toss 5 into envenom and let stealth auto-train until 24, which will give you 20 extra points, and just intime for the first battlegrounds.

Since you are already up there, get your weapon and CS high as possible, leading with sword (50 style is kewl), and dump rest into envenom and stealth evenly.
