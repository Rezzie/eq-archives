Check out the Weapon List thread (http://www.thesafehouse.org/viewtopic.php?t=18160). It lists a lot of daggers, swords, and maces that are good for rogues, along with the level for it and details of how to get it. Just as something to compare to, I got a pair of Tigerbane (http://wow.allakhazam.com/item.html?witem=1465)s for 2g and change (for both, not each), and had them waiting for me when I hit 33. It's not a whole lot more DPS, but the higher damage on the weapon makes for higher special attacks. Toxic Revenger from Gnomer is the same damage/delay, but a different effect, and usable at 27. You should really do the SM quest (starts in Desolace by killing undead) for Sword of Serenity (http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6829) or Black Menace (http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6831). Both are quite good, depending on how you want to do things. Hypnotic Blade from Arcanist Doan (he guards the key to the other instances, but you don't have to kill him for the quest) in SM is good too, but you'll have to fight the casters over it because of the stats. =) If you can get another rogue to help you out, you should be able to sneak back there and kill him without bothering with anything else.

I was doing SM at 40 for my mount money. Even the regular junk mobs outside the building carry a lot of coin, and everything can be pickpocketed for even more money. I could get 2-3g per run.
