Well the trap goes as follows:

An altar has an iron dragon hanging above it, that apparently was used as initiation for some cult.

On the altar is a riddle~

The Poor have it
The Rich need it
Eating it will bring death.

If you read the riddle thats when the magic takes effect. You try to leave the room , the dragon breathes 8d6 or something on you.

Now heres the fun part, if you answer incorrectly it also blasts you for 8d6, but if you dont give an answer *or* give the correct answer the altar shoots a green ray which makes you save or die. You make your save you take 6d6.

The answer to the riddle is ~ nothing.

So if you say nothing or dont issue a sound you have come up with a correct answer.

Congratulations and welcome to the cult.
