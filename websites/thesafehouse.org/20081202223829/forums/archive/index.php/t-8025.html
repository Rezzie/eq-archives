Unfortunately +% items dont stack, and they dont always take the greatest modifier.

Stick a lower +% backstab item in mainhand, and a higher in offhand, and it will use the lower +%. Stick a lower in mainhand and a higher in ranged, and it will use the higher.

So it actually checks Ranged => Primary => Secondary and takes the first it comes to. Not sure how it checks with other slots. Edited by: MarronD at: 9/30/02 9:25:52 am
