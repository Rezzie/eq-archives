Hakkar fight is hard. You need a group that can handle back-to-back-to-back mobs.

As for Jammal, there's a trick. Jammal CHARMS not mezzes. He turns his target into an orgre and sicks them on the rest of the group. Now if Jammal is fighting a pet, he will charm that. While the pet becomes an ogre, it stays fighting Jammal. So send your group agains ogrom but send your pet against Jammal. As an ogre, your pet will tank well.

Shade is pretty strait up as far as fights go. He will summon the type dragon mobs, (drakes and 4-legged dragon things, not the wind serpents). Clear all of them. From then he's just a High damage fight. He stuns and sleeps but only 1 at a time so an offtank is helpful, but if your tank is holding agro, he'll stay on them after he stuns/sleeps and so it won't be a problem. Make sure your healers conserve mana though. I've had a 49 shaman heal the whole thing w/ a high dmg group. On the other hand, I had a 49 druid and 53 shaman not be able to handle it. (druid was feral and I had this conversation.)
