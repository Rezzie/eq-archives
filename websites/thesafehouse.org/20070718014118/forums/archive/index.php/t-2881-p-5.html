I read this whole string out of curiosity. I see a lot of people asking exactly how to spawn the named quest aliens in Twilight Sea, but I don't recall having read the exact way it is done. Here is exactly how you do it.

You go to the hidden area.

You kill warders and spiritwards that roam of the type that you are looking for. ie, killing a grol baku warder that roams will spawn either a grol baku warder, a grol baku spiritward, or a grol baku named quest mob. The same goes for the other two types.

You do not kill any static spawns.

You do not kill any warders or spiritwards that are static.

You do not kill any roaming guardians or roaming workers. This only spawns other guardians and workers of all three types.

Also, the ph are ph for more than one type of named. I do not know if each ph can spawn any named of its type or only a few. But I do know they spawn at least a few as I got the same ones to spawn a few each. Using this method it will take anyone that can clear the entire pool of mobs they are looking for before the respawn about 2 to 3 hours to spawn their named unless they are having some real bad luck. Using this method you can see that you do not have to wipe out the whole zone and that your odds are not 1 in 70 or so.

Don't let anything else lead you astray. I assure you this is exactly how it is done.

Also, not that it will help, cause it won't. But to answer a couple questions for you people asking.

When I spawned the painter he was exactly halfway between the bowl structure and the first tent to the left as you enter the hidden area.

When I spawned the jeweler he was a few steps across the river from the bowl structure.
