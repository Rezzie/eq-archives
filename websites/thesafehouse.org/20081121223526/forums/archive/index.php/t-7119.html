Dial-up.

Satellite is really fast, and nice for general internet use and file downloads. The inherent latency, though, makes it horrible for games. The signal between systems literally has farther to travel: up into space, then back down again, THEN across normal landlines from Hughes or whoever the provider is to the host -- AND BACK AGAIN. So even though it may be travelling at the speed of light, and the bandwidth isn't limited by the medium (as much), it takes longer to initiate communication.

I hope that makes sense.
