Japanese Classic Anime Collection Coming in April from Digital Meme (http://news.toonzone.net/article.php?ID=14302)

Digital Meme has announced the Japanese Classic Anime Collection (http://www.digital-meme.com/e_mail_e.html), a four-disc DVD set of anime made between 1928 and 1936. The classic anime will be restored and remastered, with nothing added except integrated music on some of the silent anime titles.

The estimated price for the set is approximately $110 and the release is scheduled for April 30, 2007.
