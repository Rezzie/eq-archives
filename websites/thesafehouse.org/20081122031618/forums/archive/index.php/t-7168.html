Killing giants in Great Divide will give ya faction for both Dwarves and Dragons. Problem is, while it takes 100 kills to get ally to the dorfs, it takes about 5 times that to get ally to the dragons.

Best way I can think of is inside Kael. The giants up in the arena and tormax arch area don't give coldain faction, but they do give some nice dragon faction; especially the named ones. Move out of those 2 areas into the rest of Kael (such as the manor, etc.) and you'll get + coldain faction as well.

I always thought it was rather amusing that you have to fight inside the arena to get most of the armor drops for the coldain quests, but you get no faction for those same coldain in doing it.

Bottom line, most (not all) of the Frost giants you kill will give Coldain faction and the Storm Giants will give Dragon faction. Sometimes ya get luck and one of em will give ya both kinds of faction, sometimes not. Get a hit on Vindi or AoW to get a good faction boost as well.
