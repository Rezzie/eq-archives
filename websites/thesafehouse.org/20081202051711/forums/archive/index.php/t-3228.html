- Initially locked doors will automatically re-lock after the door closes after a period of time.

- Players respawn at beginning of module (will work on adding alternate bind positions in the near future)

My high level monsters have been designed with the "augmented" high level Players in mind. I.e. Solo, you will NOT be able to kill them, no matter how good your home-made gear might be. The high level players that have come have stayed for hours because of this challenge.
