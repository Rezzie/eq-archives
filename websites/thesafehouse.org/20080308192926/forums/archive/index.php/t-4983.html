My perspective as a casual player (36 healer, 30 shadowblade)...

Albion--most well developed content in the game. Lack of dropped thrusters.
Midgard--good item content, lack of high level content which makes leveling in the 40s significantly harder.
Hibernia--reasonable high level content, spottier item content.

Comparing the classes:
Albion--most spec points, most complete assasin. Playing the Maxi-min game, a slash using infiltrator briton is on paper probably the best assasin in the game.

Midgard--benefits: use of left axe (superior to the other dual wields in effectiveness) or two-handers, 5% more HP but most difficulty time soloing with their inability to pull and restealth. Playing maxi-min, the Norse shadowblade may be the second best assasin in the game. The lack of high level content is a significant demerit to me though, from what I hear from those in their 40s in my guild.

Hibernia--a couple magic spells, but I don't see too many benefits. Their shout spell doesn't do very much damage and takes up endurance to cast, so it's not free damage. Their other spell has a casting time. Lowest strength and con among races.

That said, the classes don't vary that much that any of their differences should be close to a deciding factor. Just play what you want, and a good assasin will be better than an uber-race mediocre one.

45 Saracen thrust Infiltrator, 31 Briton slash Infiltrator here. Gonna be my last post for Slash vs. Thrust.

-Slash have slow weapons = huge PA = more chance of one shot kill.

-Slash have fast weapons, some are as fast as 2.2 speed.

-Slash have 15% bonus vs. leather, the armor which your arch enemy wears. Midgard assassins are all slashers, if you pick thrust, you'll be fighting 30% less effective compared to the Shadowblade. You will not win! A smart Shadowblade won't dance around with you lining up PA, they will just smack you when they see you. You will die because thrust is crap vs. leather.

-Slash have 0% bonus vs. studded, while thrust have 10% penalty vs. studded. Always nice to hit harder on archers, espcially when you miss PA. A High spear spec hunter can sometimes kill you if you miss PA. With slashing bonus, they wont.

-Slash have 15% penalty vs. chain, while thrust have 15% bonus vs. chain. Chain wearer = Hero, Warrior, Thane, Healer, Skald. These classes dont solo, sorry. So one on one with these classes are a rare occasion. Very risky as well, even if you drop PA, CD, SS on them. With my Saracen infil, I will bring a tank down to 50-60% after opening combos, sometimes I'm lucky, I will be able to finish them off, sometimes I die. And usually those kills are on dumb tanks or healer who dont use thier ability to the fullest. When a fight is not in thier favor, a healer will just insta mez-stun you and run away, a skald will insta-mez you and run away, a warrior/thane will Slam stun you for 9 secs and run away or chop you up, a Hero will turn into Antelope mode and smack you upside down.

-Amethyst Slash > Garotte, my lvl 31 Briton slasher have both Garotte and Amethyst Slash, vs. yellow, garotte bonus is about +20, while ammy slash is about +35. I expect the difference to only go bigger as I lvl more and put more points into slash. Ammy slash uses way less endurance, and no penalty to defense. On my Saracen Infil, i usually have little to no endurance left when i kill someone, because Garotte takes away huge chunks of stamina. It will kill you some times because you cant sprint away.

-Do not worry about not being able to use thrusting frenzy, just equip it, apply poison, and swap back your slasher in. There is also a +3 envenom slasher from llyn.

-Slash is str based, thrust is dex/str based. You will have higher weaponskill with thrust. Thus more accurate and more damage. But slash more than make up for this with superior styles that does tons more damage and gives better bonus to hit. Garotte is Medium bonus to hit, Ammy slash is Large bonus to hit. PA always hit.

Go with slash.

Shadowblade is the best.
2 handed weapons do more damage on the first hit. No higher one shot weapon in the game but a 2h axe.
Slashing is all strength. When you get items to up your stats its 1 for 1. If you are a thruster you are effectively getting half the value of your items, as you are on dx/str.
Left axe is superior to the other dual styles. You always swing with both weapons, so even having a lower leftaxe value you can drop both poisons. Left axe styles are also suppose to be very effective (but I havent done a comparison).
Racewise. Norse has the highest strength of any assasin and will do more damage per hit and on the one shot. Also they have the lowest quickness in the game. Since quickness reduces the damage done per hit on styles, it reduces our one shot abilitity. Dx is nice for evades, but does nothing for hitting, and is only half a stat on thrusters. Plus most dx items in the game come with quickness - so I dont want any. Norse also have the highest con in the game, so they have more hps, and more endurance for styles - given that our heavy 2h weapons take alot out its a good thing, hehe.
