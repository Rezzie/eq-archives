A lot of people are critical of the no level PK limit, but consider the possibilities for a moment.

In the past, players could not enforce their beliefs on others about honor and order. Cruel sadistic and twinked level 5s could kill level 1s nonstop at bind points, because no one who wanted to do anything about it would be a twinked level 9 in range of fighting the level 5; the same thing with characters deleveled to a lower level. If they were a healer, they could provide some protection for as long as they had patience or mana.

Now, they're creating a society where the players will be completely capable of enforcing their codes of conduct. If some level 20 enjoys killing newbies, someone can kill him. In fact, if newbie killing is so odious, he might be hunted down again and again and deleveled.

Moreover, the fact that there can only be one character on the server will have the biggest impact. Who will trade with the newbie killer if this behavior is condemned? Will the newbie killer band with other newbie killers and become an organized criminal society?

Anyway, I see this as really interesting, a chance to create a society and police it. Of course it might fail if there is no team unity, but whether it succeeds or fails will be entertaining in itself. And if one team consistently fails to protect their newbie members while another team does, will they lose members to other teams and strengthen the other teams as well?

Hopefully when they hardcode the teams they make the neutrals capable of buffing and being buffed by either evil or good, but keep evil and good hardcoded. Who knows whether there might be diplomacy between the teams to capture the neutral support?
