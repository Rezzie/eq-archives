Got it! First time in Hate, and it is amazing. There was a raid party camped at the zone-in which probably greatly contributed to my success, but anyway. Take the bridge on your immediate right...and just keeeeeeeep going. You'll bounce off buildings, but keep creeping south and or southwest. Just when you think you are way off and start to retrace your steps, keep going S/SW and you'll come to the staircase leading up to the Maestro's house, it is unmistakeable. Directly in front of the steps is another house, with a goofy entrance. You have to walk around a wall to get to the door, if that makes sense. Inside the building "foyer", were clerics, revenants, and a drake. I nearly pissed myself when I opened the door. Just inside on the right, there is a short flight of stairs leading to the target room. From the doorway, the Book was there on the second table to the left, as an actual book, opened to the "centerfold". Inside that room was a sole revenant. Through the entire crawl, I passed by mobs in doorways and alleys, none of them Lichs or Banshees mind you, but plenty of others (clerics, chests, golems, revenants, abhorrents, ire ghasts, kiraikuei, rats) and NONE of them saw through h/s. When I first zoned in, the raid was fighting a Loathling Lich. I'd swear I was facing it, or perhaps it was preoccupied with dying, but it DID NOT see me. I was neither invised nor ITU'd, merely hidden and sneaking. My opologies for the lack of loc's, but I was preoccupied with my own death at the time. Hope this helps a bit; good luck!
