Quote: Im planning on going solo sometime this week. All of my friends say that its impossible since lots of stuff wanders there that sees invis. Is this true, and how hard is it, really?

There are mobs that see invis in hate. I wouldn't call it "lots" though.

There are two types of routine mobs that see invis: loathing liches and scorn banshees. Note that they see invis - they still can't see through sneak so if you're behing them, you're fine.

Scorn Banshees do not roam around the streets. Ever. The only place you'll see them is in the Maestro's house. You may have to maneuver around one or two or wait til they move out of the way to sneak by.

Loathing liches populate the roamer paths quite a bit so you'll surely see some. There may be a couple in the Maestro's house also. The thing about them is that they have a VERY small agro radius. No lie - I've run right by them or almost ran into them coming around a corner and not gotten agro. If you're lower level or if you linger at all, they will agro to be sure. But it's not as bad as it could be.

So how hard is it really? That's relative.

These days I know hate like the back of my hand. I get ported up and start scouting around checking all the mini bosses. Sure there are see invis mobs, but if you know how things work, you may as well be a shadow. If I needed the book today, I would go up fully dressed and be out in under five minutes.

If you don't know hate well, it's going to be a lot more tense. And frankly a lot more fun. The risk is the thrill, and it makes the reward all the sweeter. If you want to cut your risk down, duel someone and go up naked with just the evac potion. (Frankly this is the smarter way to do it - even on the best day, wierd things can happen.)

But it's perfectly do-able and you don't need l33t knowledge or amazing luck. If you succeed, you'll know what it means to be a rogue. And if you failed, it's still worth the trip.

2.) Loathing liches can see invis, but cannot hear a sneaking rogue. I snuck right by one... came within a few virtual feet of it while it's back was turned and went happily on my way. I almost ran into a lich, but I guess the aggro radius was so small it didn't aggro on me.

4.)The entire top level consists of mobs that cannot see invis!
5.) If you want to drag corpses to a safe spot, there is one right up the ramp to the second level. Just spend a few minutes watching mobs; path routines before leading a cleric to that safe spot. Dont have a map so cannot tell you exact loc, but the safespot was used in excess of half and hour for CRs. Rezz the bards and the rest of the clerics, and have the bards and rogues pull the corpses if your raid was wiped on top level.

Note: Safe spot does have mobs around there during full repop, but this is assuming your raid killed them all already, leaving that safe spot effectively safe.
