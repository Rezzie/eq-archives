I like the game fine as it is now (other than the crashy stutter bug 1.20 introduced for me) but these are a few things I would like to have in the inevitable expansions.. feel free to add your own ideas!

1.More PC customization... more heads, body types, sound sets(maybe racial soundsets) sub races (elven vampire, human werewolf etc) that give bonuses/penalties and other abilities or restrictions.

2. Prestige classes (no brainer)

3. Change the level limit - 15 would have been ideal and then up it to 20 for an expansion.. people dont seem to realize how powerful level 20 is. Half the people playing NWN would not believe that Drizzt is only 15th level..

4. Maybe a city pack with several cities all over faerun that could be added easily into custom mods

5. A character pack with famous (and lesser known) npcs that can be placed in mods, uniforms for some of the various factions, generic NPC's (red wizards of thay!) and items from some of the other places in the realms

6. An Evermeet campaign! Underdark, Icewind Dale, Mithril Hall are all expected as well.
