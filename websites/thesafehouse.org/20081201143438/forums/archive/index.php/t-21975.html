Itsudatte My Santa - B+

I recently watched this 30 minute long OVA. It's by Ken Akamatsu, so Love Hina, Negima, AI Love You, etc fans will enjoy this show almost a given. The OAV is very, very fast paced. Someone's always talking, something's always going on the screen, never a moment to relax. While I'm not a big Akamatsu fan, this was an exception. It's high pace and constant insanity was a fun thing to see.

The story itself is your typical whiny boy. Santa (in the form of a cute girl) visists him to give him good luck and make him believe in Santa again. The process from not believing in Santa to believing is irrational and takes a lot for the granted, but I enjoyed it anyway.

The ending when they talked about his parents was a little touching, though. There's a LOT of fanservice in this show (panty shots, nude t&a, etc.) as typical of Akamatsu.

Fate//Stay Night - A

Ok, probably the show I am most looking forward to, so I'm happy it's here now. Fate//Stay Night is based on the ero-game of the same name, and in the same type of world as Shingetsutan Tsukihime (Lunar Legend Tsukihime in the US).

The anime seems to follow the game. I noticed a lot of things were rushed through, and understandably so. There was a lot of conversation between Archer and Rin, but they didn't even talk this time around.

Some of the events are not in the order as the game, instead jumping around a bit. However, these are all very understandable. It's blindingly obvious that the anime will revolve around Shirou and Saber, so they will likely skip over a lot of backstory of the other magicians and servants, which is pretty disappointing. But Saber is my favorite character so it's all good in the end, I suppose.

The animation is pretty good, and I like the character designs. I like the pace of the story. It looks like this will be an anime to watch out for.

By the way, even though it's based on a ero-game, it's not like KimiNozo, Kanon, etc. It's very different, so don't come expecting such. The only similarity is that the girls are pretty.
