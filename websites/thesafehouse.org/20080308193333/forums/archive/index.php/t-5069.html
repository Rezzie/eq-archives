Mythic has stated you must be 50% weapon trained to be "effective" as an Assassin.

Most rogues will tell ya 66%, 75%, 80%

Think of it this way; CS affects Critical Strike Moves,
Weapon Training affects EVERYTHING, CS, Pierce/Thrust/Slash, Offhand, Everything.

Untrained Variance 25%-100%; average 66%
50% Trained Variance 50%-125% average 88%
75% Trained Variance 75%-125% average 100%
100% Trained Variance 100%-150% average 125%

So being specced in weapon basically DOUBLES your damage over being untrained in weapon. You will be crippled if you ignore this.

(((spec/level)*.75)+.75)*100%
