Greetings!

Ex Umbris (Out of the Shadows) is very excited by the launch of beta 3!

We are a “core” guild that will play the game for fun. We are not casuals or hardcore. We will be raiding on a regular basis. We will have members on all three continents in-game. Our members are currently located from GMT -8 (PST) to GMT +2, (we will not be limiting recruitment to these time zones). We are looking for mature, quality players from the pre-release community. Our guild’s interest run the gamut: Adventuring, raiding, crafting, diplomacy, harvesting, trading, building a guild town, and ships.

We are working very hard on our web site, and lore.

Ex Umbris Portal (http://www.ex-umbris.com/)

Ex Umbris Charter (http://ex-umbris.com/phpBB/viewtopic.php?t=15)

Ex Umbris - Rules (http://ex-umbris.com/phpBB/viewtopic.php?t=30)

Ex Umbris Raid Loot Rules (http://ex-umbris.com/phpBB/viewtopic.php?t=344)

The basic tenants of eXu lore include:

-There is a Spiritual Force at work that selects people to have "the call"
-Ex Umbris is thousands of years old and has existed under a great myriad of leaders and has built up an eccentric feel after all this time
-The Ex Umbris "Vaults" were built thousands of years ago when a Dwarf ran eXu.
-Most of the previous generation are finding and selecting our characters and trying to continue the Order.
-Most members are spread out around the world doing what they can to make the "world a better place".
-"The Call" manifests itself as a driving need to get out into the world and make a difference.

We are a role-play friendly guild and have a role-play background that we encourage members to add to, however we do not require any form of role-play from our members.

We are still recruiting and now have a formalized application process. We require the following information when you apply:

1. How would you describe yourself?
2. What is your favorite race to play? Why?
3. What is your favorite class to play? Why?
4. What spheres do you plan to participate in? (adventure, crafting, diplomacy, harvesting, trading.)
5. Describe your play style in Massive Multiplayer On-line Games.
6. Character names and classes?:
7. Main character?:
8. Starting location?:
9. What brought you to VG?:
10. How did you hear about Ex Umbris?:
11. Do you have an ExU sponsor, if so who?
12. Closest time zone for playtimes (GMT ?)?:
13. Have you ever been in a guild before?
14. Describe your experience with your guild(s).
15. Have you ever served as a guild officer? Other positions of responsibility?
16. Member of any other guilds in VG?:
17. Vanguard is a group-centric game and we plan to group a lot. Does that fit your play style?
18. We are not a hardcore raid guild. Do you like to raid?
19. How much do you plan on raiding a month?
20. Do you role-play?
21. Have you read the guild charter?
22. Do you understand the loot system we will use for guild raids?
23. What are your requirements for a guild?:
24. What are your dislikes in a guild?:
25. Do you have friends and family you are hoping will come join this guild at a later date?
26. Do you have any questions for us?

If you are interested in applying for guild membership, click here to get started. (http://ex-umbris.com/phpBB/viewtopic.php?t=133)
