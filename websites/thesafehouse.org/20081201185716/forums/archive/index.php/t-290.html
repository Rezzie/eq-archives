Ok, guy sends me a tell saying he's the brother of a guy I grouped with twice and then tells me he needs some help killing desert madmen and dervishes in N Ro. So I figure what the heck, I'll help him out with 1-2 of them. Get there, group with him and what does he say? "I don't want the xp, I just want (all) the money and the gems they have". Um, and what am I getting from this partnership? Anything? Well, there comes the desert madman, attack, oops, there comes the mummy too, um, make that two mummies. Tell little idjut to run, idjut starts screaming for help, ok, get lucky and an 18th lvl paladin steps in and saves my sorry behind. Results? I'm diseased at 1 and half bubble, idjut monk is at 4 bubbles, paladin is perfectly fine and we have killed one mummy, which the idjut promptly loots (no auto-loot on for him of course). Then I tell him I'm not that rich. "Ok, will you kill lockjaw and get me the tunic then?" Um, me? Kill lockjaw at 16th? When I can hardly find groups in less than 3 hours and LJ is camped to hell? I think not. Ok, so he keeps going on and on about how he desperately needs the tunic so he can play his character. Yeah, right, just like I absolutely need an SBD to play my character. After a few (in character) /tells I usually load the buggers up with a few items of loot (ABSOLUTELY NOTHING ALL THAT VALUABLE.) Typical set will include:

1 backpack
2-3 pieces of rawhide armor
1 decent weapon (nothing better than a normal steel rapier for instance -- and only given two of these away)
