It's summer, meaning that for half of every day, it's too goddamn hot to do anything but sit in the dark and watch anime. I've finished Berserk (rented, not downloaded), Naruto, Bleach, and Kimi ga Nozomu Eien are on my other computer, and my brother keeps knocking Farscape down on the Blockbuster queue because he wants to watch Citizen Kane or some dumb ****.

There's a lot of series out there. There's also a lot of crap. Fame doesn't always mean good, either; having loved Evangelion, and seeing that it was near the top of AnimeNfo.com's Top 200 list, I gave RahXephon a try (that it was cheap at GameStop didn't hurt). Ugggh. :sick

Here's the rundown on what I'm looking for:

-To give you an idea, some of my favorites are Eva, Cowboy Bebop, Trigun, and Fullmetal Alchemist.

-I tend to prefer shows with a limited run (26 episodes, or 51, or whatever) to those that go on forever, but it's not hard to get me to make exceptions.

-Anything that continuously takes itself way too goddamn seriously (Witch Hunter Robin, the aforementioned RahXephon) gets on my nerves.

-So does anything that continually hints that there's something really interesting going on, but refuses to tell you any of the interesting parts until long after I've stopped caring (WHR and RX again).

-I'm not much into girly stuff.

-A heavy romantic focus is annoying. Unless, of course, the ai in question is shounen. Or shoujo. :naughty

-Humor is good.

-Straightfoward action is fine, but my holy grail is anything that can **** with my head.
