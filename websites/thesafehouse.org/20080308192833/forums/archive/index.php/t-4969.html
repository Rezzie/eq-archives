I think I would personally shoot for 34 to get CD. It is a great skill. Your piercing is high enough for now and so are your poisons. Hit 34 with CS then start to raise your poisons and piercing again if ya want.

Here is why I say this....

In battlezone you will find archers or casters off to the side. If you just perf them they will run. However, if you perf them with a minor dot so they can't jet out and then hit them with CD.....they are dead in the water and before they come outa CD they are most likely dead. I loved it when I was in battlezone and you will too. You must have fast type of weapons for this. I say anything below 2.5 speed is good. There is a fine line between high damage weapon and a good speed weapon. I find a weapon around 2.3 is a nice balance. Lohengrin
