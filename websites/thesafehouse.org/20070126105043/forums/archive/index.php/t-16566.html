Gnomeregan is in Dun Morogh, the dwarf/gnome starting area off Ironforge. Outside the tunnel to the instance are newbie mobs (level 10ish) and two of these guard the tunnel.

Once in the tunnel, you encounter mid20s gnomes (horde may also be attacked by the fleeing exiles, no clue how tough they are?) You go down the tunnel, take a U turn and take the elevator thing down. Below are more mid 20s gnomes, go past them and up the ramp to the "train" area, you will see Invaders/Pillagers mid20s elite mobs. If stealthed, give the green glowing ones a wide berth because they can area poison you which will drop your stealth. Take a left and you will pass an open area (this is where hordies get teleported in from Ratchet FYI) and keep going along the upper path or lower path until you see the instance line.

Once inside the instance, move up till you can see a MoB, all of this first hall is safe from static spawns or roamers. Up here are more invaders/pillagers, again some are irradiated so be wary if stealthing past them and don't waste time trying to PP these green glowing ones unless you also want to fight them. You should be within sight of a big circular hole, there are a few options from here depending on your time/luck/skill:

The simple option: go stealth, wait for wanderers to go away and walk straight off the ledge. if you aggro something, don't move and either vanish or kill it on the spot, re-stealth and proceed to step F below.

The semi-simple option: stealth up, walk left to the "clean room" (you are still avoiding the green ones right?) and proceed to step C below.

The long haul: kill your way to the "clean room." Have slowing poison or item because they do run. Watch for the fast moving roamers to add, a couple of the normal spawns roam slowly as well. If sapping grouped pairs, try to sap the non irradiated one. Right before the clean room are 3 tied mobs; You can stealth past, kill all 3 at once, sap one or get lucky and use evade/return/vanish tactics to seperate them.

Step C: Once in the clean room, the NPCs are friendly to alliance (hordies go back to The Simple Option above) and there is a mailbox and a vendor on gnomer exile faction to sell to, but he doesn't repair. Walk to the mailbox and look down, you will see a 2 spawn on your left. There is also a roamer, so if killing the 2-spawn wait and pull the roamer up to the clean room first. Tip: if you pull one of these back into the clean room, near the vendor, and fight it there one of the NPCs usually casts on you a [priest's stamina buff.] Take the left and you are in the fallout room.

Step F: In the fallout room, the area leading to the clean room is safe. If you jumped but didn't aggro anything then freeze, wait for stealth to recycle and sneak to the clean room entrance. If you aggro something, fight it on the spot, pull it to the clean room side or vanish. From the clean room opening facing in, start to pull the MoBs on the right side. ***IF*** there is a **Corrosive Lurker** DO NOT fight these without either a good 15 levels on them or damage poison(s) because they can drop your melee damage down to 1 and then you can only kill them with combo moves or poisons (non-rogue spellcasters will have an obviously smaller problem but this is a rogue forum.) After pulling two or 3 of the spawns on the right to the clean room entrance and dispatching them, you can move up and wait for the oversized water elemental, "Vicious Fallout" to wander over to be dispatched. He isn't noticably harder than anything else you have fought so far.

Hint: Do not solo past fallout room unless you can handle 10+ of these MoBs by yourself because you **will** set off an alarm-o-bot!
