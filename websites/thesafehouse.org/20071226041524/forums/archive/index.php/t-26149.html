The final volume of Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex 2nd Gig was released yesterday and I must say it's the best looking DVD box I've ever seen. It's similar to the case that came with the first season's volume 7 SE, but 'kicked up a notch', as the saying goes.

If you've been following the releases you know that the special edition tins have a circular window in the front which shows off the DVD. The tin box uses the circular window to display the box art on the back of the volume 7 DVD tin, and inside it has dividers for each volume. Also coming with the volume is a CD (Stand Alone Complex OST 3) and a Motoko Kusanagi figurine.

The content of the DVD is good as well, and the DTS sound is great during the battle scenes. Overall I liked the first season a bit better, but as a whole I thought the 2nd Gig is a very well done anime series and well worth watching.
