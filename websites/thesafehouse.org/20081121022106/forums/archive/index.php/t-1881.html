16.) Backstab dmg softened

17.) 82 new piercing weapons have been introduced. Anything with 10+ dmg will be caster only, have 15str and 30sta, and look killer. Anything below 10 will be rogue inclusive, but will have about 100 mana, 6int or wis, have mana regen LXVII, and look like a butterknife.

18.) Sneak/Hide "fixed" again. Since the button kept going gray, we've decided to just leave it that way...now's it's less distracting.

19.) Run-speed no longer affects sneaking.

20.) If it rains, snows, or someone farts...hide will no longer function.

21.) Added 72 minute timer to hide function.

22.) Frogloks can now be rogues! Instead of backstabbing, they have the unique ability to back-lick. Works the same, though, unless a fly gets in the way.

23.) Worn ATK cap reduced to 15. That zero at the end was never intended.

24.) Items can no longer be pickpocketted from mobs without pockets.

25.) Items that are geometric, shiny, weigh more than 0.1 stones, make sound when dropped, follow the laws of physics, or have a name starting with a consonant can no longer be pickpocketted.

26.) Sensing traps in LDoN can now set them off, so be careful out there.

27.) Rangers have a new AA ability called "Track self". This allows them to never get lost, even if they try. Requires mapping, sense heading skill of 200, and a tracking skill of 250.

28.) Monk FD now works without fail while dragging corpses. This should help monks when they help out during raid wipes.

29.) Rogues lose Virtue or any other clerical or paladin buff when pickpocketting, applying poison, or backstabbing. Makes no sense to have "virtue" whilst swiping something or stabbing someone in the back now, does it?

30.) As a result of all the melee "balancing", we're now focussing all our attention to rebalance casters. These changes will be made within the next 72 hours, and will be implemented in a patch scheduled for two weeks from today. If that patch doesn't go on time, we'll credit all caster accounts with 10k platinum.
