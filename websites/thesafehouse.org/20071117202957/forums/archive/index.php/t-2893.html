Actually, what I thought was interesting...

1st edition had weapon length tables...they never explained what they were used for, as they did not match the actual size of the weapons. In the original books, there were hit location charts. Against larger then man sized creatures, the size was added to the player size, and would modify the hit location. We used it in 2nd edition...and it worked nicely!
