7th Sea kicks ass! I love the system, the world, the character creation and balance are all done well. And as for rogues, its got that. Its a point based skills system. Attributes are a little overpowered but expensive enough to get that it doesn't interfere too much. The system rewards cinematic and dramatic scenes! Chandelier swinging is practically a skill, and intrigue fits in perfectly. The entire world is based on Renaissance Europe, but with sorcery and quirks. My personal favorite is a Vodacce Swordsman that for all intents and purposes is a rogue. Vodacce is Italy, where the power structure is from 7 princes all competing against each other for power. Assassinations and poisoning is fairly common and every shadow has someone hiding in it. If you are looking for a new system and world I would highly recommend it.
