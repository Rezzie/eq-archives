On the damage issue, there are lots of variables. You have your sword skill vs. left axe skills. You have bonuses on the weapons (do they add points to the skill?). You have the quality of the weapons which effects their actual damage.

Lots of stuff besides base DPS and speed, in other words, so it's really hard to determine just from that data exactly why your output is how it is.

On the macro question, Nostromo's N-50 and MS's Strategic Commander are left-hand devices that let you assign keystroke strings to a single button. I've found them a little frustrating to use for combat, though, as the timing of the fight changes dramatically each encounter.

This time your PA goes off just right, so you continue to CD and then SS. But this time your PA doesn't go off, so you're either running or switching to Garotte. This time you get AE'd while just about to PA, etc. etc.

The problem with repeating keystrokes by rote becomes when the situation alters and your stupid macro keeps plodding along and you're now trying to do something else.
