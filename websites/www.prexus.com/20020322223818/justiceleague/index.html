Welcome to our site we are very honored that you have chosen to visit us. We are a family of RPG Gamers who share a love of the game Everquest. We play on the Prexus server and are always looking to meet new friends. If ever you need us feel free to look us up. We are also always open to new family members. We ask only that you group with us once or twice so that we might better know your way of playing prior to your joining the family.

 

The web site is currently under construction but, please feel free to browse the pages which are up and running. We are adding and making changes to the site almost daily so please check in regularly. Also please be sure to visit the message board and drop us a note in the general forum. If you are a JL member and do not yet have a members password for the members area please contact Laadyhawk, Zmax or Ambrozia for your password. If you don't see any of us on line just drop a note to zmaximuz@yahoo.com

 

Members please help us to grow by sending your screen shots, RL photo and bio of your self along with your character info via E-mail or a post on the EZ Board in the members & Officers area.
