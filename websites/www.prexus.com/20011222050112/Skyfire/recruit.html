Things to think about before jumping into joining.

1) Basically if you are joining just for the fact of getting loot we unfortunately are not the guild you are looking for. Try joining a bigger, stronger guild who do raids everyday.

3) Again we don't raid daily but when someone needs something done in our guild we can more than likely or with a little help from our friends get the job done. It just takes a lot more time than the bigger guilds.

4) You have to have a good sense of humor and be able to be called bad bad ego blowing names left and right by modew.

5) AND you have to feed him shit back!

If you understand and can live by these 5 easy rules come on and apply in our Application to SkyFire forum section.. 

Be sure to post "Want to join", along with your class race and name.
