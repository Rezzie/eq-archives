Genesis Alliance

The alliance was formed offically formed around September 1st of the earth year 2000.  It was done to help the smaller guilds band together to make a single voice for all of Prexus to hear.  That one voice said, "We are here to stay and wish to join in the battles on the planes, and the dagons."  With that announcement the alliance has seen many new found friendships and have been to the planes, dragon runs, even high level Kunark dungeons.  Thanks needs to go out to Cuts of Crimson Stiletto, Stormtower of Twilight Council and many others.  With out this holy alliance the "little" guild would not have had a chance to go to the planes....
