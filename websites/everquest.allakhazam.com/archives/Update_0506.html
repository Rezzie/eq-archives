This probably caught your eye before reading this update: We have added a new feature to the site: Beginning today (and what we anticipate as every Friday from here on out), we will feature a Player of the Week. This feature has replaced the retiring Thought for the Day (the archives for which will remain here, linked through our history section).

Our first featured Player of the Week is a Wood Elf Ranger named Saraban, from Darkwind Guild on the Antonius Bayle Server. You may already be familiar with this name as it is tagged with credit on countless information pages across the site. Saraban is a regular contributer to the site's content (especially for the high end game), so we thought who better to be our guinea pig..err...first featured player. You can check out Saraban's profile page and gallery here.

OK, another week is down (woo, it's Friday), and we are due a new poll of the week. Last week the question was asked, what was the best Bazaar deal you have ever received? One of the many interesting answers was techlemist's preparation for the Prophecy of Ro expansion's eight additional bank slots. He stocked up on Hand Made Backpack tokens after PoR was announced, only to sell them for more than double the platinum upon release. Now, that's smart business.

For this week's poll we go to something we all have in common: Computer monitors. We ask: What type and size is your (main or best) computer monitor? You can find this poll on our EverQuest Forum.

Victims of Murphy's Law take note: EverQuest live technical support will be closed on Monday for the Memorial Day holiday (if tech support is closed, something will go wrong!)
