March 30, 2006

We have a tip for those of you who are seeing human character models in place of regular NPC models. While this fix may not work for everyone (depending on the cause of your problem), it usually does the trick:

  • Go into your EverQuest folder (typically C:\Program Files\EverQuest)
  • Delete the zone files associated with the zone in which you are experiencing the problem ("akanon_chr.s3d," "akanon_obj.s3d," for example -- there are 5-10 files per zone depending on which one it is).
  • Run the patcher.
  • When it's done, click on "Advanced" and then "Complete File Check."

The patcher will pick up the missing zone files and download the appropriate fixes.

This message was inspired by the number of Media Box submissions featuring these naked human models in place of regular NPCs. I hope this helps.
