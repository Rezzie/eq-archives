RULES

Guild Standards
 
  • All members are expected to treat their fellow guild members with respect and dignity at all times. Answer guild chat even if it is IDK, or no. Should personal quarrels arise over any matter, the use of weapons is not permitted for the resolving of disputes. Unless a guild duel has been approved. :)
  • Bad mouthing ANY member or the guild itself in guildchat or on the boards is forbidden. If you have a complaint, take it first to that player and if you feel it is serious enough, take it to the leadership.
  • No harassing, belittling, stalking or any other behavior that disrupts the guild’s continuity will be allowed. Petty arguments (exemption for Valiek and Badmad) and disagreements will not be tolerated.
  • All Members should also have the same civility toward other non-guild members, unless of course they are worthy of your contempt.
  • Ninja Looting any item for any reason will not be tolerated.
  • Guild bank, only accessable by officer. Currently costs 10G p/week to access. If officer sees fit to charge for items, cost will be at 1/2 of reasonble AH price.
Required Addons
 

It is a requirement to have the following game addons/mods and to make sure you have the updated versions.

  • Ventrilo
  • Group Calendar
  • Deadly Boss
  • Omen
