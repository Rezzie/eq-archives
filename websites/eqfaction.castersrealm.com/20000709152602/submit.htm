Submit Faction Information

If you have any information regarding factions, and changes of due to battle with NPCs or MOBs, please send it in! Any new factions that are not listed here, or changes or additions (due to patches most likely) are more than welcome.

I do ask, however, that you be very thorough in gathering your information. Only send something if you yourself have seen the faction adjustment after the death of an NPC or MOB. Please don't go by heresay, or by what others tell you because they might be wrong or lying. If you have faction information that you would like put up here, send it to me at eqfaction@castersrealm.com.
