DaBashers

The warrior guild of Grobb, Da Bashers have a large camp set up in Innothule Swamp right outside their city. They are a tough crowd, attacking everyone except Trolls, Ogres and Dark Elves, and even then oftimes attacking them. Troll Slayers and the Slayer Captain that roam Innothule also belong to this group.
