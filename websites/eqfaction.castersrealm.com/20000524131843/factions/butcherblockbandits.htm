ButcherblockBandits

The bandits that make their camps in Butcherblock Mountains and raid the hapless adventurers that wander their way. They include Qued, Stump Rundl, Crytil Dunfire, Margyl Darkin and their leader, Pegleg. There may be more, but none that I have come across.
