CracknekWarriors

The warrior guild in Oggok, led by Guntrik. It is found inside the large mushroom-looking building surrounded by water deep within Oggok. They are constantly at war with the Greenblood Shadowknights, and if not for The Bouncers created by Clurg, the two groups would have destroyed each other long ago.
