  • 12-24-99 - Wow, the info is coming in quite steadily, giving me little time to update everything!! Anyway, here's a few faction tips sent in to me...

    - Erudites should not get teleports where there are treants or other druids, as they are KOS, unless you have done something to improve your faction with them.
    - Don't kill Trumpy Irontoe in Qeynos twice - without honey mead he gets cranky.
    - Even if Barstomper at Fish's Ale is nice to you, he will help kill you if you engage Trumpy or his buddies.
    - Killing Blixkin Entopop's fire beetle pet Amber in Misty Thickit will destroy your faction with the mercnahts of Rivervale.
    - Never help anyone kill something if you don't want the faction hit. Even if it's healing or buffing them, if the MOB goes down, you get the hit.

    Also, here is a tip for easily ensuring that the faction info you are collecting is correct: While playing, if you want to record what factions are affected by a certain MOB, turn your log on before you fight it, and then turn it off again after it is dead. This will output all text scrolling through your textbar to a file called eqlog.txt in your EverQuest directory. You can then open it in Wordpad or Notepad and do a search for the phrase Your faction standing with and it will proceed to run through all the faction changes that you have logged. To toggle your log on and off, just use the command /log in-game. I usually just turn mine on when I first connect to EQ and it will automatically turn off when I disconnect.

    Also, I have received word that there have been numerous ogres to make it successfully into Rivervale. Agmyles of the Karana server has accomplished this feat long ago. Screenshots can be found at: http://www.alliedgiants.com/EQ/shots/ogre.htm. Not to make this a contest or anything, but it has come to my attention that Mungalung of E'Ci can now walk the streets (or bridges) of Kelethin safely, as long as he stays away from the banker who seems to not belong to the MerchantsofKelethin faction group, and has sent him diving from the platforms and charging for the nearest zone. He can also walk the streets of Felwithe, paying close attention to shy away from the cleric, paladin and caster guilds
