This quest is given by a male High Elf named Neasin Leornic within the Plane of Sky. The quest says it will reward you with a cloak, but in fact it is a mask that fits in the face slot.

You say, 'hail Neasin Leornic!'

You say, 'Concentration'

Neasin Leornic says ' Concentration it is. Proceed upward through the sky and return to me an Azure Tessera, an Augmentor's Gem, and a Grey Cloak. This will prove your ability to concentrate and I will reward you with an Augmentors Cloak.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Items Needed:

  • Azure Tessera (droppable): Found on island one in Sky from the Thunder Spirits (they all drop various Tessera for different classes)
  • Augmentor's Gem (droppable): A rare drop from the Protector of Sky on the Azarack Island in Sky.
  • Grey Damask Cloak (NO DROP): Found upon the Gorgalosk (the named gorgalosk inside the building on the 3rd island).
