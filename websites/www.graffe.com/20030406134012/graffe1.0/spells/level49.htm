Level Forty Nine

 

Evac: West
Group evacuate to West Karanas.

Ice Comet
Mana Required: 400
Casting Time: 6.30 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Description: Oh, yes. This is the one that everyone hears about and then plays a wizard in hopes of getting. This spell is quite literally the ultimate destructive magic, at over 1100 pts of damage per cast. In order to acquire this spell you will need to either purchase it from another player, or have the research skill to combine the runes yourself. The runes are found on high level mobs scattered throughout Norrath. Also acquirable by doing the Ice Comet Quest. Ice Comet does 1110 at level 49 and 1120 damage at level 50, using 400 mana per cast.

Markar's Clash
This is an upgrade to Tishan's Clash, and stuns opponents for a lengthy amount of time. This spell is a necessity if you plan to hunt over-level-50 mobs like dragons or guildmasters.

Paralyzing Earth
An upgrade from the root line of spells. Costs 100 mana to cast and will supposedly hold enemies for a longer period of time compared to Immobilize.

Rend
Magical based DD spell. This was added to our spell line to combat the Magician's ability to have a purchasable spell that rivaled ours. Rend does 780 damage for 280 mana.

Supernova
Personal-based Area effect direct damage spell. This area effect is somewhat useful, for a swarm of monsters around level 25. 875 mana, 854 damage to every mob within range. This is one serious area effect spell, but will use close to 2 bars of mana at level 50. (stats submit by Gos of Mithaniel Marr)

Wrath of Al'Kabor
Area effect spell that is not very useful for your level. Costing 500 mana, it will produce 448 points of cold dmg to all around your target. (stat submit by Eldriash of Povar)
