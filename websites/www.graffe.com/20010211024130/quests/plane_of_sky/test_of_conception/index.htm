This quest is given by Abec Ianor within the Plane of Sky. This quest will reward you with a ring.

You say, 'I wish to test my powers'

You say, 'Conception'

Abec Ianor says 'Conception is vital. Travel beyond and bring forth an Efreeti Statuette, a Mithril Air Ring, and a Box of Winds. From these I will produce a ring that will protect you wherever you go.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Items Needed:

  • Efreeti Statuette: DROPPABLE LORE ITEM. Drops off of Soul Carriers or Essence Harvesters on the 4th isle.
  • Mithril Air Ring: DROPPABLE. Found upon Spiroco Guardians as a rare drop upon the sixth isle.
  • Box of Winds: NO DROP. Found upon a Bzazzt Zazzt from the bee isle.

Rumors say these items are dropped on the third island from a sprited harpie as well as the Efreeti Noble upon Efreeti Island.
