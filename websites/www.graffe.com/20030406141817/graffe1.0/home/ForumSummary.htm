This is a summary of the Wizard Forum that was held on Monday night, the 14th.

We all started out at the gate point within Greater Faydark between the four pillars. One GM named Arian showed in a timely fashion with an explosive entrance and the meeting got underway. Indalamar took the floor and brought up many issues that concerned all of us. It seemed as if the GM did not want to hear our pleas. Another GM showed and then another. At this time the meeting in the forest started to get a bit unmoderated so we moved it to the chat server. Once in chat, the following GM's and designers showed up:
Aradune (Brad McQuaid)
Geoffrey Z (Lead Magic / Spell Creator)
Arian
Daegarmo
Lokkison
rpalacio (the guy with ALOT of statistics)

The subject of Damage:

 During the first hour within chat, it seemed as if whatever the speaker said, the GM's did not want to listen at all. The topic of our damage was brought up and they assured us that in later levels (MUCH later levels), we become the masters of destruction. A high level spoke up and tried to explain that this is simply not true. Verant came back with their facts that our damage to mana ratio far outweighs any other class and they would not tweak us in any way.
   
The subject of Root: We let the GM's know that root in no way works as we would like it to. The programmers let us know that every class that uses Root, uses the same exact spell and there is no difference between any of them. Once again, they threw the statistics of all four spells at us and said nothing would be done to rework it. One submitted the idea of a 'push' spell that would throw the enemy away much like a Tentacle Terror does. GZ said this would in no way be possible for the way the AI of the mobs works. Apparently they move on invisible 'tracks' and this push spell would in no way work.
   

The subject of Area Effect Spells:

 This subject was based on the fact that area effect spells are nothing but a call for suicide. They backed themselves up stating that area effect spells have their place and time for use. Again, nothing is to be done about them.
   
The subject of Research: Many areas of research were brought up. From the spawn rates of runes to the spells that are not researchable. Geoffrey Z stated that he again is upping the spawn rate of runes and that researching of spells is still being fixed. He stated he was to look into the rate of which the research skill andvances also. We are to expect some fixes in the next patch. Hooray! Finally something is going our way.
   
The subject of Toy Spells: Geoffrey Z stated that his best work comes from creating 'Toy Spells' for casters. Some Wizards stated that a morph spell would be a nice addition and he agreed to the idea. Although he cannot create a new graphic for use to morph into, he said it was feasible to create a spell for us to morph into a creature that is already on the servers. It seems our sight line of spells were to be our 'Toy Spells' and he said they were implemented at the last moment in beta and really haven't been dealt with too much. One wizard spoke up and stated the truth, all they seem to do is 'magnify' your screen.. they don't really let you see farther at all. GZ quickly stated that he uses it to target certain mobs in camps before the fight and also uses them to make sure there is no extra mobs in a camp they can't see. GZ also relayed that our 'Eye of Zomm' is being reworked to not train monsters and we should see a fix in the next patch.
   
There was more things talked about but these were the main issues brought up. Although many were upset at the outcome of the meeting, there were many that were happy with it. I for one was just simply surprised at the turnout of all of Verants people and their willingness to take time out for us. This shows that they do care about their game and their paying customers.
