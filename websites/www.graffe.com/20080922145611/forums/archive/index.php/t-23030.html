the write up tells you how many mobs and how many waves. just read a bit harder.

I apologize if I didn't waste enough of my time, I'll hold your hand a bit more:

From: http://www.andponcho.com/wizard/#fires

Zone in from Sol B side, fight south until you get to the room just north of the room where the bridges form a lambda symbol (upside down Y).

The room in question is a circular room with lava in the center. There will be 8 lava elementals in the area. 4 Lava Vindicators and 4 Lava Champions. Clear these.

The room grows warmer as the lava pool begins to steam heavily.

One minute later the next wave will spawn. This time, four Furious Magma Guardians will spawn, one on each of the 4 platforms. Clear these.

The lava starts to bubble and churn. Dark shapes move beneath the molten rock.

One minute later the next wave spawn. This time, two Inflamed Champions will spawn: one on the east ledge, one on the west. Clear these.

The pool of lava begins to boil furiously. Something large is growing within.

One minute later the final wave spawns. This time, the Pure Flame Elemental pops. He will spawn on the middle rock.

Pure Flame Elemental says, 'Why have you awakened me mortals?'

Kill him. He doesn't hit hard, but mitigates slow. Was a very short fight and didn't need any heals, except for the enchanter.

You receive: Wisp of Pure Flame. Head back to Talwyn and hand it to him.

Thats 8 in wave 1, 4 in wave 2, 2 in wave 3 and 1 in the final wave
8+4+2+1 = 15.

Yes its trioable. It is duoable. I have witnessed a shaman solo it. I'd repost the map showing exactly where the room is, but quoting pictures is lame.

Also to save you from your inevitable second post: the respawn isn't definately known, its less than 8 hours and more than 2...
