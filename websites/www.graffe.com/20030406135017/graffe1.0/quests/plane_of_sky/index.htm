All of the quests found upon these links are initiated and completed from within the Plane of Sky. The Plane of Sky is a zone for high level players, level 46 and up. We wizards hold the only key (portal) to visit this plane.
To initiate all of these quests, you must speak to Wizard Schrock. He will ask if you wish to speak to Neasin or Abec. Asking for either quest giver, Schrock will hand you a book that you can read and then hand back. Once you hand Schrock back the book, the appropriate quest giver, Neasin or Abec, will appear.
