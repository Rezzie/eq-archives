DPS

DPS stands for Damage Per Second. DPS is measured in a few different ways.

[edit] Theoretical DPS

Theoretical DPS is often used to calculate the base damages of classes. Often the more advanced calculations are done with spreadsheeds or sometimes home-made scripts such as the scripts used on shadowpriest.com.

[edit] Burst DPS

First, it can refer to Burst DPS. This is how much damage can be done within a short period of time. An example of this would be the damage of a spell divided by the cast time of the spell.

[edit] Sustained DPS

Sustained DPS is a calculation (or sometimes a parse) of the average damage done over a potentially infinite period of time.

[edit] Parsed DPS

Parsed DPS is calculated based directly on someones actual log of a battle. Because they rely on the person doing them to accurately parse the information, these logs are almost never perfectly accurate.
