Disintegrate
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 650
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 3.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 120.0 seconds
Descriptoin: Costing one opal gem to cast, this spell will completely obliterate the targeted mob. As well as destroying the mob, it also destroys the corpse. This means zero loot. Speaking to Geoff Zatkin of Verant, he verified that this spell has a level cap and it's either a full hit or a no hit. No info is verified as to what level of mobs it will destroy, but it is guessed that at level 60, you could probably kill a level 45 to 50 mob with one cast.
