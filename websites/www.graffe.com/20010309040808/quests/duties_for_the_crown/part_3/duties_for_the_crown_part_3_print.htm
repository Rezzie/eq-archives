This quest starts by speaking to Loremaster Borannin in Thurgadin. You can find him in the hall by the library. You must complete part two of the quest before you can continue with this.

::SPOILER::

Once again, go find Loremaster Borannin in the Thurgadin library (-303.00, 812.00).

You say, 'what chore'

Loremaster Borannin says 'The council agrees that you have demonstrated a disdain for our enemy, the Kromrif, that rivals our own. They now ask that you demonstrate your loyalty to the Dain by humbling yourself and serving our people. Give this, my seal, to Mordrin. He will instruct you further.

You now have what looks like a silver coin in your inventory called Bornannin's Seal. You can find Mordin Frostcleaver inside Mordin's Meats at -221, -220. Note that his name is Mordin and not Mordrin.

You say, 'hail Mordin Frostcleaver'

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'Ah, hello there, and welcome to me shop. Ye'll have to excuse the mess but I've been havin a bloody good time here lately. . . HAR HAR, ye get it? Anyways, thanks to you offlanders I now have a selection of some exotic meats never afore seen here on Velious. In addition to supplyin' the freshest meat in Thurgadin I also offer tasks to would-be hunters in the wastes.

Now hand Mordin the Bornannin's Seal.

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'Hullo, I see you've signed on for a bit of
food service eh? Well we can always use an extra pair a hands here dat's fer sure. First things first, warsh yer grubby mitts an cook up dinner fer good ol Leif. Shouldn be too tough fer ye, he has simple enough taste. Make him a Tundrabear sandwich an lemme know when he's been fed. Dontcha ferget ta give him this with his meal.

You say, 'What tundrabear sandwich?'

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'Dat's easy, ye just take a slab a dat Tundra
Kodiak meat and a jug a sauce, put it in da oven, den combine the product with a loaf a bread in a mixin bowl an there ye be.

Now go out and kill a Tundra Kodiak to loot the Tundra Kodiak Meat. The closest place to find them is in the SouthWest part of Great Divide or roaming in Eastern Wastes near the Orc Fort. The jug a sauce can be purchased from Perkins in the Exchange. The recipe for the Tundrabear Sandwich is -

  • Step One. 1 Tundra Kodiak Meat, 1 Jug of Sauce. Put them in an oven and hit combine. You now have some Saucy Bearmeat.
  • Step Two. 1 Saucy Bearmeat from step one, 1 loaf of bread. Put these in a mixing bowl and hit combine. You should now have a Tundrabear Sandwich.

Now go find Guard Leif located outside the guard barracks (-285.00, +500.00) near the library and hand him the Tundrabear Sandwich sandwich along with Leif's Napkin that you got from Mordin.

Guard Leif says 'Well I'll be derned. An outlander that can make a good
Bearmeat 'n Bread! Thank ye.

You say, 'What snow bunny stew'

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'Simple enuff, getcherself two hunksa Bunny meat and a jug a sauces. Stir 'em in a mixin bowl, then put spices and two flasks a water with it and cook it in a pot. Mmmm, zesty!

To find bunnies, head to Eastern Wastes. You may have to kill a lot of bunnies for the meat... or you may have good luck and get two right off the bat. Again, jug of sauce are at Perkins and a mixing bowl is neccessary. To make Snow Bunny Stew, use the following recipe -

  • Step One. 2 Snow Bunny Meats, 1 Jug of Sauce. Put these in a mixing bowl and hit combine. You now have some Saucy Bunnymeat.
  • Step Two. 1 Saucy Bunnymeat from step one, 1 pot, 1 spices, 2 waters (not summoned or foraged). Put these in an oven and hit combine. You should now have a Snow Bunny Stew.

Keep in mind that in order to make all of these recipes, your baking skill must be sufficient enough to do so. One submitter's baking was at 67 and making the meat was trivial. Now hand this stew and Trita's Napkin to Trita Coldheart. She can be found in an unnamed building, in the back and down a bit (-115.00, +317.00).

You say, 'what ulthork meat pie'

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'Hmm, lemme look that one up. Been awhile since I made one. Here we are, two Ulthork meats, a vinegar and a spices in a mixin bowl. Put yer meat mix, a clump a dough, an a pie tin in da oven an yer good ta go.

Ulthrok Meat Pie Recipe

  • Step One is 1 Milk, 1 Flour, 1 Snake Egg. Put in mixing bowl and hit combine. You now have 'a clump of dough'.
  • Step Two is 2 Ulthork Meats, 1 vinegar, 1 spices in a mixing bowl and hit combine. You now have some Spicy Ulthork Meat.
  • Step Three is 1 clump of dough, 1 Spicy Ulthork Meat (from step two), 1 pie tin. Put these three items in an oven and hit combine. You now have an Ulthork Meat Pie.

Give the Ulthork Meat Pie to Lorekeeper Brita in the library (-184.00, 810.00).

You say, 'what snow griffin soufle'

Mordin Frostcleaver says 'This one's a bit tricky, ya toss four snow griffin
eggs and a pack of spices in de oven. Ye must bake it just right or it's all
gunna be wasted. When yer done bring it to Thoridain and if ye did it right he'll give ye his special seal. Just show Borannin that seal and yer shawl and he'll know that yer finished in the kitchen.

Snow griffin eggs can be found on snow griffins in Eastern Wastes. A pack of spices can once again be found for sale on Perkins in the Exchange. The recipe for Snow Griffin Soufle is -

  • 4 Snow Griffin Eggs, 1 spices. Put these items in an oven and hit combine. You should now have a Snow Griffin Soufle.

One submitter tried to combine the items with a baking skill of 67 and failed. They worked on their skill until it was 135 and it was trivial. Now give the snow griffin soufle to Grand Historian Thoridain. Thoridain can be found in the library also (-317.00, +805.00)
