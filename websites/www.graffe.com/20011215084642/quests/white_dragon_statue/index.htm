This quest, also known as the 'First Arcane Test', takes place in the Temple of Veeshan by talking to Gozzrem.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


First find Gozzrem (picture & loc needed) in the West wing in the Temple of Veeshan -

You say, 'hail Gozzrem'

Gozzrem says 'Greetings. If you seek wisdom or the arcane I have tasks in the halls of testing you may complete to gain what you seek.

You say, 'I seek the arcane'

Gozzrem says 'To garner a reward fit for one who walks the arcane path return to me the poison tear and the poison symbol. Along with these return the serrated symbol and the runed symbol. If this task is not hard enough for you, I have a second quest for you.

The symbols he is looking for are -

  • Poison Symbol - Found upon various dragons within the Halls of Testing of the Temple of Veeshan
  • Poison Tear - Found upon Dozekar the cursed within the Temple of Veeshan (picture needed)..
  • Runed Symbol - Found upon various dragons within the Halls of Testing of the Temple of Veeshan.
  • Serrated Symbol - Found upon various dragons within the Halls of Testing of the Temple of Veeshan.

Once you have obtained all four items, head back to Gozzrem and hand them to him. In return, you will get the White Dragon Statue -
