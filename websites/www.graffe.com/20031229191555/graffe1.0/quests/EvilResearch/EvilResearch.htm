Within the Desert of South Ro (loc +100, +500. Straight South from the Oasis of Marr zoneline), there is a camp with a female Teir'Dal, named Smith Tv'ysa, selling research assistance items. In exchange for a Greater Lightstone, you get a Concordance of Research Tome, which has spaces to combine items much like the Lexicon. If you give her a few lightstones, she will give you the Runes and Research Tomes Volumes one and two.
