This quest will make it easier for the good aligned researchers gain experience in Research rather than wasting precious runes that would be of value to create real spells. The quest begins (and ends, really) with Mrysila of the Gypsy camp in North Karanas (Loc -672, -162). Hail her, she will talk to you about the [Concordance of Research] and it's sister books Runes and Research Volumes one and two. Then tell her you are [Interested] in researching spells. If you bring her a lightstone, she will give you whichever book she has more of at the moment. If you bring her a Greater Lightstone, she will give you the Concordance of Research, which is the book used to combine the items much like the Lexicon.
