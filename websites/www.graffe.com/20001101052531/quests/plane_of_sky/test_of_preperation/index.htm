This quest is given by Abec Ianor within the Plane of Sky. This quest will reward you with the Staff of the Storms.

You say, 'Preparation'

Abec Ianor says 'Always be prepared. Adventure and return an Efreeti War Staff, some Lush Nectar, a Copper Air Band, and a large Sky Sapphire. And you shall be rewarded with the Staff of Storms.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Items Needed:

  • Efreeti War Staff: Droppable. Found upon the Efreeti Noble Dojorn. Both Dojorn and the Overseer drop the staff
  • Lush Nectar: Droppable. Found upon Bzzazzt upon the bee isle.
  • Copper Air Band: Droppable. Found upon Fatestealer Drakes on the Sister of the Spire Isle.
  • Large Sky Sapphire: Need drop info and drop/no drop info.
