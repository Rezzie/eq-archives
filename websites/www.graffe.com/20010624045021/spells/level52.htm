Lure of Frost
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 320
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Description: Drops target's charisma by 5 and damages for 640.This is the first of the lure line of spells. Lure spells are a great new addition to our arsenal in that they allow us to hit mobs that would normally resist everything we threw at them, such as Spite Golems in Hate. They may not hit with as much damage and efficiency as we are accustomed to, but at least we can be a strong part to any group.
