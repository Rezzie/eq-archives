If you wish to send in information on an item, this is what I ask:
1. Place the item within a backpack (this is so the icon has no words behind it).
2. Open the backpack and drag the window away from the statistic window.
3. Right click on the item icon and pull your pointer away from the icon (so when you do the next step, your cursor doesn't shroud the icon).
4. Press the minus (-) key on your keypad to take a screenshot. (This is saved in your Everquest root directory as a bitmap file. Example: C:\games\everquest\EQ000001.bmp)
5. You can email the full bitmap if you wish or you can convert it to a JPEG if you have the knowledge. I only ask that if you convert it, please leave the resolution at 100%
6. Along with the picture, send any information on what MOB, NPC, Quest or any other vital information to what enabled you to get the item.
7. If you wish to recieve credit for the information, please include your characters name and server you play on and I will be more than happy to give you the credit you deserve.
8. EMAIL it to me! =)
