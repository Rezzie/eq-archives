::: Wizards with Manaburn :::

Manaburn gives high level wizards the ability to convert all of their available mana into unresistable damage, but at a reduced mana/damage ratio. In organized teams, manaburning wizards can devistate powerful monsters in seconds. If you have manaburn, record your name here... and add channel manaburn to your /autojoin.
