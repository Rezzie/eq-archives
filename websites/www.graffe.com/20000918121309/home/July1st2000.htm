Area Effect Rain Spell Update:
Before I call it a night, I thought I'd relay a bit of info to all of you. If you remember correctly, I spoke to GZ about them a while back and he said he would try to get the issue resolved before the release of Kunark. So, I asked him this morning on the status of them.
