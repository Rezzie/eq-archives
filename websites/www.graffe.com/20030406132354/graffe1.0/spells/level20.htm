Gem Descriptions | Mana Calculator

Level Twenty

 

Elemental Shield
Raises your resistance to cold and heat. Lavastorm anyone?

Enstill
Same as the Root spell but with a longer hold. Also like most of our spells at this level, it gets resisted quite often.

Fire Spiral Of Al'Kabor
Area Effect fire spell. Fire this upon the center target to do damage to it and all mobs surrounding it.

Force Shock
I get many conflicting reports upon this spell. Many people, including myself, will not use it but then again, many people say it works just fine for them. All I can say is purchase it and judge it for yourself. Following is my thoughts upon this Direct Damage spell: When I first got this spell, I was elated. It hit with 171 damage per hit. I have now come to the realization that this spell gets resisted more than it hits for. I have spoken to a few Wizards that wont even use it. They stay with the level 16 damage spells. Rather annoying to spend 11 platinum on something and have it not work the way you hoped it to. On a side note, a reader stated that he uses it for a spell interrupter while going up against casters. Guess you just get that extra edge when it does hit for damage. Thanks to Muera Silvenmoon of Solusek Ro for the interrupt idea!

Gate to Toxxula Forest
This is a personal gate spell that will transport you to the Toxxula Forest. It drops you at -1510, -916 on the coordinates inbetween 4 large stone pillars. All of the gates drop you into safe spots, but if you are evil and venture far, I suggest watching your back.

Gate to North Karana
Another personal gate spell that will transport you to the Southern zone line of North Karana. It puts you right at the bridge river crossing to South Karana. Again, in the middle of four large stone pillars and safe place. If you are of an evil race (like myself) be sure to cast invisibility upon thyself before running by the guards to South Karana. You can swim through the zone but just be sure that you are directly under the bridge in order to zone.

Gate to Greater Faydark
The last of the personal gates for level 20, this drops you at -2023, -441 inbetween stone pillars once again.

Sight
An enhanced version of Gaze.

Tishan's Clash
A stun spell. Great for interrupting NPC casters from beating on the tank of your group.

Track Corpse
This spell is not available to wizards. This is an error in the Kunark Strategy Guide. The spell was wrongly placed as a wizard spell when it is, in fact, a level 20 necromancer spell.
