Chron X announces Expansion Set 4:
Chron X announces Expansion Set 4, "Whiteout". Check it out at http://www.station.sony.com/chronx
- Whiteout includes 74 new cards which fully compliment the latest battleground, "Antarctica". Whiteout introduces a new keyword, "Arctic", on some assets. These assets, like the existing Mawson assets, do not suffer from the usual penalties associated with the Antarctica map - they do not take damage if unprotected by a base and they do not have their firepower reduced. Arctic assets are more expensive to move, but a whole range of decks specialized to the Antarctica map can be built around these assets. With their immunity to cold, they will be able to survive in an enemy's Headquarters city, where a non-Arctic defender will not have the firepower to dislodge them. Because of their higher movement costs, Arctic assets are less likely to be seen on other maps, with one exception. Whiteout introduces the Freak Blizzard intervention. With this in play, anyone can create "blizzard" conditions in any city on any map. The result is the same as a city on the Antarctica map. Imagine the enemy's surprise when his assets suddenly start freezing to death in the Pacific!
- Whiteout also introduces a powerful new enhancement, "Double Agent". Double Agent can be used on your own assets to untap them. It's the only non-program that can do so, but you run the risk of the card being used against you next turn.
- Take advantage of the new Player Referral program to get FREE cards! For every registered player you refer you receive a FREE 15-card Booster Pack from Chron X.
Sony-Gregg
