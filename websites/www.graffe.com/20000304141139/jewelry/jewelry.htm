Jewelry is split into two sections

All of these pieces of jewelry have been fabricated by jewelers. Jewelers are real character players just like you and me. If you are after one of the items listed upon this page, get in contact with your friends or search for an enchanter / jeweler that can create them for you. I will not list pricing upon these pages due to the fact that it varies greatly server to server and day to day.

All of these pieces of jewelry can be found upon different mobs scattered throughout Norrath. Some more easier to acquire than others.
If you wish to submit jewelry statistic pictures, please keep in mind that what we as Wizards really need, are Mana, Intelligence, Hit Points, and Agility. So if you wish to submit new jewelry, I ask that you keep it with those four attributes. Thanks!
