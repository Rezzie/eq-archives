This quest, also known as the 'Test of the Emerald Tear', takes place in the Temple of Veeshan by talking to Lendiniara.

Talk to Lendiniara the Keeper (-1065, +1125) within the West wing of the Temple of Veeshan.

You say, 'what test of the emerald tear'

Lendiniara the Keeper says 'The emerald tear of Veeshan entrusted to the cursed one. Return this tear to me so I may return the powers of an ancient shield. Three symbols you must also seek. Emerald like the tear, one kissed by the flames of the elder wurms and one made of the ancient wyverns venomous poison.

The tears are obtained from Dozekar the Cursed (picture, location & text needed), the boss of the Halls of Testing in the Temple of Veeshan. Once you have all of the items (stat pictures needed), go back to Lendiniara and hand her all four items. You will be rewarded with the Buckler of Insight.
