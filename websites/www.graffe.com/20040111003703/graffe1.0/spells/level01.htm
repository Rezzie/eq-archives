Frost Bolt
Damage: 4 (L1) to 7 (L6)
Mana Required: 10
Damage to Mana Ratio: 0.7:1
Range to Target: 300 feet
Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Projectile Aim
Resistance Check: Cold + 25
Casting Time: 1.80 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Description: Being a bolt spell, this will generally do more damage than any other offensive spells. However, the drawback is that you must keep line of sight with your target whilst you cast. This means that if a person, tree, wall or any other object gets in the way, it will be cancelled.
Location:

  • Erudin Wizard Guild, Gissa Brist
  • Neriak Commons Wizard Guild, Mak Ixtaz
  • Qeynos South Wizard Guild, Rosdaria Sepria
  • Felwithe Wizard Guild, Celent Newmist

Minor Shielding
Increase Total Hit Points from 6 (L1) to 10 (L5)
Increase Armor Class (AC) from 3 (L1) to 5 (L5)
Mana Required: 10
Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: Self
Spell Duration: 270 ticks (27.0 minutes)
Casting Time: 2.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Description: This can only be cast upon yourself and adds armor class and hit points. Not much added, but invaluable for a wizard at this level where melee is part of the equasion for battles.
Location:

  • Born with this spell in your inventory
  • Qeynos South Wizard Guild, Pai Berenis
  • Neriak Commons Wizard Guild, Tkaz H`Rugla
  • Felwithe South (not in guildhall), Srell Tumblebrook
  • Erudin Palace Main Floor, Telor Beteria
