Abashi on the 51+ Spell Drops:
Found on the Everquest Gameplay Forum, Gordon addresses the issue about the lack of 51+ spell drops and if it had been patched:

I've no problem admitting that it was messed up, I just didn't want to announce in the patch message that the spell drops were adjusted until I had a chance to get in and test it out myself.

The spell drops were addressed in this patch. I still feel a bit uneasy about the frequency of those drops, but we'll wait to see what happens before taking further action. Just a reminder to everyone searching for spells, you'll want to be in dungeons, in Kunark, killing things that are generally over level 45 to get a chance of getting those spells.

-Gordon
