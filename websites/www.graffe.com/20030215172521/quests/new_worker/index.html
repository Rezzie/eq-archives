This quest takes place in the Overthere Outpost and is for evil races only. You will need amiable faction with Venril Sathir in order to start or complete this quest. In order to get your faction up to amiable, you can do the Sarnak Warbraid quest that is detailed at the bottom of this page.

Once you are amiable with the Undead Foreman, hail him:

You say, 'hail an undead foreman'

an undead foreman turns to face you. You can see a faint green glow emanating from his vacant eye sockets. 'Are you a [new worker]? Huh? Speak or go!!'

You say, 'I am a new worker.'

an undead foreman says 'Hmmph!! Too much flesh!! If you want to work, you must first fill my eye sockets with my favorite gem. This shall be your payment for your Sledgehammer.

Now, go to the gnome merchant (Tin Merchant IV) nearby and purchase a Jade gem. This should cost just a bit over 7 platinum. Go back to the undead foreman and hand him the gem.

an undead foreman places the gem deep into his hollow eye socket. He pulls a giant sledgehammer from thin air and hands it to you. 'Here!! You shall be assigned to the lower decks of the Scaled Trident.'


Damage Ratio: 8.33

This sledgemallet has the effect of 'Gate Overthere' attached to it. This is a proc, meaning you must be in melee combat for it to work (duels work as well). The effect only works for players level 50+. Once the sledgemallet casts it's proc, it will drop you inside the ship being constructed at the Overthere Outpost shipyard. In order to get out of the ship, just run towards the bow and you will clip up onto the top deck. Then simply jump off to the ground. Although it says it only has 1 charge, I have used it three times so far and it still works perfectly. A little trick to using it, is to duel a friend until it procs.

To raise your faction with Venril Sathir, you can do the Sarnak Warbraid quest. This involves speaking to the Captain Rottgrime in the Overthere Outpost.

you say, hail Captain Rottgrime!

Captain Rottgrime says, 'we [shall not allow those sarnak to overtake the Danak Shipyards]. Is that clear marine?!!"

He wants you to kill the Sarnak Berzerkers and return the war braids they carry. Hand him four war braids and he will say

Captain Rottgrime says, 'Great job marine!! Word of your heroics shall be passed on to the Admiral. If you don't have a shield then take one, if you do then get back to the frontlines. There is no time for R n' R!! Move it marine!! Or you'll be pushing Danak till the cockatrice crow!!"

Your faction standing with VenrilSathir gets better

You will get a bit of coin, experience and a shield called the Guard of the Marines. This shield is only usable by melee classes and is also NO DROP. So you will just have to destroy it. It took me 10 quest completions to get from apprehensive to indifferent, and 12 quest completions to get from indifferent to amiable.
