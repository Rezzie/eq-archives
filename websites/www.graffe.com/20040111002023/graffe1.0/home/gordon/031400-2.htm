>>>What about all the player expectations?

Many of the complaints I've seen quote a line from the Wizard description. Your's is no exception: "when it comes to dishing out damage, wizards have no rival."

I'm beginning to think that the problem here is that Wizards had this expectation that they'd be much more powerful than the other casting classes. But that line you've quoted speaks of Direct Damage spells, and is true in that application.

The Wizard is the most straightforward casting class in the game, in the same fashion that the warrior is the most straightforward melee class. There's not much strategy to them, they do one thing and they do that one thing well. The addition of travel spells does add some diversity, but they are still primarily the same. We really have no plans to redefine the class.
