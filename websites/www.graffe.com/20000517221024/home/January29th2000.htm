Staff of Temperate Flux Warning:
This was sent in by an unnamed viewer: "I found what appears to be a bug last night. I was at the bank in Felwithe. I got my Staff of Temperate Flux recently, and decided to cast it on myself to see if it would affect me, and it did. A friend of mine was next to me, and I decided to see if it would affect him. I targeted him, and right clicked on my staff. Well, then next thing I know, I am dead as every guard in the area beat me to a pulp. After getting my body back, I decided to try again, a little safer, to make sure I didn't have a guard targeted. I targeted a newbie near the entrance to the city and right clicked. I got ran out of Felwithe with guards beating on me as I went. Just letting you know to be careful who you cast that on."
Although the Staff does no damage, it does cause aggravation (aggro) on any mob. Therefore, it is considered a hostile act and will attract guards within the vicinity. Be careful with your toys... you might shoot someone's eye out. -Graffe.
