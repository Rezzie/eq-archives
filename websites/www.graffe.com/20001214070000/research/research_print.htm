These research spells are from all four books. Note that you cannot simply take what is given here and try to create the spells. Your research skill still has to be high enough to create the spells. So in all actuality, these will do you no good except let you know what you can create with research and what you must buy.

To create a spell, you will need only the Lexicon and the correct runes for the spell you wish to create. Open the Lexicon and drop in the required runes. You will not need a piece of parchment or a quill pen, only the runes. Once you put the runes within the Lexicon, take a deep breath and hit 'Combine'. If all goes well, you will now have the correct spell in your inventory. Thats all there is to it!

If you wish to take the time to learn all of the correct languages for each book, these are what you are going to need to know:

Wizards Canon - Old Erudian
Wizards Doctrine - Combine Tongue (or Giant)
Ipsor's Enlightenment IIV - Dragon
Ipsor's Enlightenment IV - Elder Dragon

I constantly receive e-mail from people wanting to know what mobs carry the certain runes. Being that I have had to answer this at least 100 times via e-mail, I will post it here in hopes that most will see:

There is no certain place to loot a certain rune. Runes are dropped randomly on random mobs. The only thing that you can count on is that you will not loot a rune that is needed for a level 49 spell off of a mob that is only level 20.

IMPORTANT concerning the Rune of Fulguration: There is actually two different Runes of Fulguration within the game. The two runes are NOT stackable. If you try to create a spell with the wrong rune, you will fail and lose your other runes. The way to figure out which rune you have is to take both runes to a vendor and try to sell them. The vendor will give you different pricing on each rune (Note: this only works with the Attraction Rune now). The lower priced rune is for the level 16 spell, Project Lighting and the higher priced rune is for the level 29 spell, Energy Storm.

IMPORTANT concerning the Rune of Attraction: There is also two different Runes of Attraction within the game. The two runes are NOT stackable. If you try to create a spell with the wrong rune, you will fail and lose your other runes. The way to figure out which Attraction rune you have is to take both runes to a vendor and try to sell them. The vendor will give you different pricing on each rune. The lower price rune is for the level 24 spell, Levitate and the higher priced rune is for the level 44 spell, Gravity Flux.
