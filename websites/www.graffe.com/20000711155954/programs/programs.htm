Upon this page, I will put programs that will help you along within Norrath. You will not find programs that will enable you to become 'godlike' or the such. I frown upon cheating highly and plus.. Verant is on top of things when it comes to cheating. All of the programs here have been scanned with AVP and do not contain viruses. I in no way created these programs so please do not email me asking how to work them. Enjoy.
 
Program
Description
EQ Trade & Research
 Created by Xekl Xephyre of Xegony, this spreadsheet is a download must. Simply loaded with all kinds of trade and research info as well as wizard spell damage info.
EQ Icons
 Created and submitted by Luizito, these are icons based on some Everquest Spell Icons and the such to replace your computer's icons anywhere you wish to.
Research Spreadsheet
 Created by Boots at the Crossroads Gaming Network, this Microsoft Exel Spreadsheet will help you keep track of your runes and also tell you what spells you can create with the runes you have. Highly Recommended.
Practice Spreadsheet
 Also created by Boots at the Crossroads Gaming Network, this Microsoft Exel Spreadsheet will help you keep track of your Practice components for research.
Mana Calulator Executable
 An executable program made for Windows that will calculate your current mana points. Fill in the blanks with your current information and have it calculate how much mana you have to spend. This program requires you to have the virtual basic dynamic link file msvbvm60.dll within your windows\system directory.
Item Bonus Calculator
 This spreadsheet is in MS Excel 97/95 format. It is used to calculate item bonuses to stats, AC, mana, and HP. Very nice little spreadsheet. Thanks to Baks of Veeshan for creating this and sending it in.
EQ Monitor
 The ever popular Everquest Server Status Monitor. This keeps an eye on the servers and zones and will automatically start Everquest when your selected server comes back online. I personally have not used it since beta. Unlike that Godforsaken game Ultima Online, Everquest's servers are rarely down. They frequently update this program so rather than putting it upon my server, I simply added a URL to Hydra Computers Everquest page.
Mana / Int Spreadsheet
 Itzokae Blastenburn of Cazic-Thule made an Excel spreadsheet which calculates the mana bonuses gained from intelligence bonuses by level and a chart which tells how much of an intelligence increase is needed to equal the mana bonus of an item by level. The latter chart makes it a bit easier to determine the point at which to switch from mana+ items to intelligence+ items.
Mana / Damage Spreadsheet
 This spreadsheet can make a comparison of mana available and damage possible with that mana, for two different Wizard's Intelligence. Changing cells B4 and D4, you can compare your abilities with your current and your intended ( or you friend's :) Intelligence. This spreadsheet requires you to have Microsoft Excel upon your system and an unzipping program such as WinZip to unpack it. Spreadsheet compiled by Nenad of Cazic Thule.
EQ Mana and Weapon Damage Calculator 3.5
Very well written executable for Mana Calculations with the added benefeit of calculating our melee damage! If you have any problems getting it to run, you may be needing system files. Visit the author's page for more info and DLL downloads!
Inventory INT/Mana Spreadsheet
 Simple Excel spreadsheet with a unique feature of allowing you to edit each slot for each item you may have (or not have) that adds to INT or mana. A good way to figure out what is the best combination of items once you hit 200 INT. Thanks to Zypher of Rodcet Nife for creating this.
SFManaCalc
 This is a simple mana calculator which lets you calculate your mana, be it on an INT or WIS base calculation, without having to do anything special. The calculator will also display mana for every level from 1 to 50, and depending if your character is a pure caster, or a hybrid class, you will get the appropriate results. Thanks to Rindelk Darkcastyr of Fennnin Ro for creating this great little program.
