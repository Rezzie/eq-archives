GM Event on Veeshan:

Yesterday afternoon, a few GMs got together and set up a match between "Ahkim Sandstorm" and "Daldrum Oakbreak" in Oasis.. A sand giant and a hill giant, who were fighting over "Granny's" will.. There was constant bickering over who was Granny's favorite and what-not, while "The Referee", a female halfling, directed the fight.. Players were allowed to buff and heal the two Giants, but not to interfere directly with the fight (a few people were summoned a way).. After several minutes of boxing, and occaisional feign deaths, Ahkim fell, and the players supporting Daldrum cheered.. Those who supposedly roleplayed the best were awarded with cash, and one particular cleric in his teens was given a "Sandal Shield": 15 AC, +5 STA, -9 CHA.. After thanking everyone for their support, Daldrum ran off into the Ocean.. Only to return when some Wizard that had been summoned away came back with Ice Comet loaded.. Won't say his name, but he dispatched the Giant quickly, and I believe he dropped 1 copper, as did Ahkim. Thanks to the GMs of Veeshan for setting this up.. We never have events =P

Flaming Sword of Xuzl on Povar:
Cylus of Povar stopped by Xegony last night and paid me a visit to give me some details on the level 59 spell, Flaming Sword of Xuzl. Cylus is the first level 59 I know of to scribe and use the spell. By the way the spell was described to me, it sounds a lot more like a toy spell than anything useful. The 'pet' is an animated two hand sword that is not flaming. It has very low hit points (dies by one hit from a blue mob) and slashes for around 50 damage. Once cast, the sword will only attack the targeted mob and once that mob is dead, the sword dies as well (if it lasts that long).
Thanks to Cylus for stopping by with the info!
