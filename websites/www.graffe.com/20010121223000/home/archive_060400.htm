Harvest Spell Info:
A few things all should know about Harvest -

  • Only found for sale within the Third Gate of Neriak. Just like the enchanter spells.
  • Besides using the Jboot / Staff of Temp Flux trick to get the mana to register, you can cast ANY spell in your current list and just 'duck' to cancel it.
  • Keep it loaded at all times if you intend to use it. It will take 10 minutes for it to even register on your spell buttons after you memorize it.

Harvest is Working:
In order to get Harvest to register correctly on your mana bar, you need to fire off a small spell (I used my Journeyman Boots because it uses no mana. You could also use a Staff of Temperate Flux). This is the same bug that plagued us when Verant incorporated the ability to meditate after 35 without having to stare at your book. The spell gave me approximately 3/4's of a bubble of mana when cast.
A very large thanks to Ignigena of Xegony for helping me with the debug.

Out of Body Experience?:

I'll start with an introduction. I am Nebuchadnezzar a gnomish wizard.. I was bored one day so I was running around. thinking of a quest to do. What i'm a human?!!? (my char is a gnome). I look at my icon on the right hand side of my screen. magician? what's going on. The other guy who owned the magician I was playing was switched to my wizard. Anyways I'm in the sage room with some necro. Since the name was Nebuchadnezzar and the letters in the name were maxed, it couldn't add an X at the end of the name which it usually does if there is a name clash.
