Sulgar and the Ice Comet Quest: A bit ago Kryogen Katspaw posted some of this info to the General Message Board. I have confirmed it so I will post it here so all can read: If you are still not able to speak to Sulgar within Everfrost Peaks concerning the Ice Comet Quest, there is only one thing you can do... and that is petition so that a full GM can help you. There is faction that Sulgar is based on called 'beta-neutral'. Everyone that cannot talk to Sulgar is trying to find a way to raise this faction. This is a faction in game that is not supposed to change so that anybody can speak to NPC's with this type of faction.. no matter what they have killed in the past. Apparently, there was a mob within Mistmoore (a werewolf) that did effect this faction when killed. This mob has been fixed but unfortunately, before it was, it effected a few Wizards out there. So, if you wish to have this corrected... write a nice petition stating your problem with Sulgar and maybe.. just maybe if your server's GM is online and feeling kind, they will help you out. There is no promises and don't say "Graffë said you would change it for me!" because I didn't say it. =) Good luck!

Quests for Wizards within the Temple of Solusek Ro: I've seen a few upset people on the message boards, within my e-mails, and over on the Wizard Mailing List concerning the lack of Wizard quests within the upcoming zone of the Temple of Solusek Ro. I posted to the mailing list saying not to get upset yet because the zone is not completed. In saying that, it seems some changes have already taken place within the new zone. Tirion of Povar sent in this e-mail:

" After I heard there wasn't any wizard quests in the temple I decided I had to take a look for myself before I believed it. I found two npc's on the balcony opposite of the mage's quest. Gardern gives the quests for Staff of Temperate Flux and Weeping Wand and Vilissia gives quests for Runescale Cloak, Tishans Kilt and Acumen Mask. Gardern seems a bit bugged... I can't get past the first hail, but Vilissia is working fine.

Vilissia will make the Runescale Cloak if you give her a Lizardscale Cloak and three Runes of Scale. The runes can be found on saltwater crocs in Guk, deepwater caiman in Oasis and firescale crocs in Rath Mountains. For Tishans Kilt, which I am assuming is going to be a lower magic resist effect on it, you need to take a Shadowed Ball, from shadow men, to Trankia in Everfrost (I am assuming it continues from there.) And for the Acumen Mask she wants a Glowing Mask from a Skel Monk in Guk, Patch of Shadow, Darkbone Skull from Unrest and a Bonechipped Mask from a Goblin Headmaster in OOT. "

Thank you Tirion!

Qenelin Bu'Khma mailed in some information and pics of the new upcoming zone, Temple of Solusek Ro. Here is an exerpt of his info and the pics to follow:

"....The zone is very small. I've found one secret passage, leading me to Tazgar (a cool looking NPC who did not answer Hails) and barring more, the whole zone is the size of Northern Felwithe, if not smaller. I must admit, it was beautiful to behold. The furniture and buildings looked great . So why was I disappointed? Well, first of all there are NO WIZARD QUESTS, that I could find. Not that I'm against, but Wizards got the shaft, once again, and that angers me. Nothing to think about, nothing to explore, just straightforward "go camp that mob" quests. Finally, there are just not that many quests. Qeynos has more quests than this, I am sure. Oh yeah, there is one guy who will (assumingly) erase your specialization. This is for those who went over 50 on the wrong skill and want to change it. No big deal."
