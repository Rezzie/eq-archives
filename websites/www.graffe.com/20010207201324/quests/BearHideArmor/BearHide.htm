In the Qeynos Hills, near the Surefall Glade zoneline, you can find an NPC named Chanda Mller. She can often be found near the campfire. Chanda can make you bear hide armor if you provide her with a bear skin and some coin. First, Hail Chanda and after it fails tell her, "Your Brother sent me.". Ask her about her bear hide armor and she will fill you in on details regarding the quality and cash requirements for each piece. If your faction is not good enough with Chanda, you will have to do each item in order (belt, then boots) before you can receive the Cape. Thanks to Jadce Mysticast of Xegony for the added info.

 

Pricing is as follows:
Belt - 5 GP and a Low quality Bear pelt
Boots - 15 GP and a Medium quality bear pelt
Cape - 21 GP and High quality bear pelt
