SPELL GEM DESCRIPTIONS

YELLOW:

These spells will be cast on you.

RED:

These spells will be cast on who you have targeted. Mostly for Direct Damage (DD) type spells.

GREEN:

These spells are area effects (AE), and will radiate out from who you have targeted. You can take damage from these if you are too close. Great for kiting multiple mob kiting.

BLUE:

The spell is an area effect (AE) that will radiate out from you. You will not take damage from this type of AE.

ORANGE:

These spells are area effects (AE), and will radiate out from who you have targeted. It will also effect everybody (usually for spells like Rain of Fire). You will take damage from these if you are too close.

PURPLE:

These spells are area effects, but will only affect people in your party. Mostly a portal classed spell.

 

 

 

 

Level One

 

Frost Bolt
Being a bolt spell, this will generally do more damage than any other offensive spells. However, the drawback is that you must keep line of sight with your target whilst you cast. This means that if a person, tree, wall or any other object gets in the way, it will be cancelled.

Minor Shielding
This can only be cast upon yourself and adds armor class and hit points. Not much added, but invaluable for a Wizard at this level.

Numbing Cold
Being an area effect (AE) spell, this will damage all monsters in a close area when casted upon one. In my journeys, I have found all AE spells to be nothing but mere suicide.


Shock Of Frost
This is the first in the line of direct damage (DD) shock spells. Shock spells generally do not do as much damage as a bolt spell of the same level will do however shock spells are attained frequently and do not need to be aimed.

Sphere Of Light
Creates a sphere of light that only you can keep within your inventory. It cannot be dropped, sold, or traded. It will create a lightsource equal to that of a normal lightstone. Funny, I don't need this one (Dark Elf...<grin>)

True North
Once cast, it turns the caster to face directly North.

 

Level Four

Fade
This is a small teleport spell that will take you to a random spot to a maximum of about 5 to 10 seconds away. Uses for this include impressing your friends and giving you a bit of time to cast Gate to get away from a mob.

Gate
Gate will take you to your home point or to wherever you were last bound.

Glimpse
Enhances your vision to about 1-1/2 times normal. Only lasts for approximately 20 seconds and is cancellable once casted.

Ice Strike
Another area effect spell based on cold rain. This should be casted on the tank within your group as it works 'around' him or her.

O'Kiels Radiation
Casting this fire shield upon you or a friend, it will cause a small amount of damage to the enemy every time it hits you for the duration of the shield it also gives you +10 versus fire. This is a rare shield in that it can be casted upon others, not only yourself. Great way to make friends.

Root
This is probably the most valuable spell in your arsenal. It will hold a NPC in place as you blast upon it. Be weary and get a good distance away from your enemy while it is rooted because they can become unrooted at the worst times without warning (casting will usually cause the mob to become unrooted). In addition to this root and blast, you can also root and gate to safety.

See Invisible
Can be casted upon yourself or another. Allows you to see anything which is invisible otherwise. Invaluable to cast before casting invisibility upon someone so the both of you can travel invisible, yet see eachother.

Shock Of Fire
Direct Damage spell based on fire. This will be your best DD spell at this level.

 

Level Eight

Column Of Frost
Area effect cold based spell. Cast this on the center enemy to radiate out from it.

Eye Of Zomm
Casting this creates a small remotely moveable eye that you can use to check out areas you are not sure of venturing into. Be warned, my eye has been attacked and after being killed, the monsters came straight for me and my party.

Fingers Of Fire
Area effect fire based spell. Cast upon yourself and it will damage any mob within 25 feet. Great spell to use while collecting bat wings for a later spell, Levitate.

Firebolt
Another bolt spell. Remember they give higher damage, but often miss their target due to line of sight.

Lesser Shielding
An upgraded shielding spell.

Shadow Step
Much like Fade, it will teleport you. This one works a bit better. I do wish to warn you not to use this in an area such as Lavastorm. I once landed right in a pool of lava.

Shock Of Ice
A better Direct Damage spell based on cold. This is the offensive spell you should be using at this level.

Sense Summoned
Casting this will point you to the nearest summoned pet. Rather unusable by us Wizards.

 

Level Twelve

Bind Affinity
This spell will bind you to anyplace within the world of Norrath. It can be casted upon another but they must be in your party and also they must be in a town. This is an invaluable spell for all of us and can also make you rich. Although I bind people for free depending on thier race (roleplaying is the best!) and stature amongst other players, I have seen other casters easily get one platinum per bind.

Cancel Magic
The best use for this is to undo a party member that has been charmed or rooted. Hmm.. Lady Shae visiting Freeport from Quenos comes to mind. (two consecutive deaths..grrr)

Fire Storm
Fire storm is a fire based rain spell area effect.

Frost Spiral Of Al'Kabor
AE spell which is fired upon the center target. Looks very nice but not too much damage.

Gaze
Simply an upgraded version of Glimpse.

Halo Of Light
Conjures a glowing headband that lights as well as a greater lightstone.

Resistant Skin
Raises your resistance to disease. Great for hunting zombies and mummies.

Shock Of Lightning
Shock of lightning is a direct damage spell. If you are in this level range, this is the DD spell that you should be using.

 

Level Sixteen

Bind Sight
Cast this on any animate object, be it a friend, a monster, an NPC vendor... anything. The will not have any inclination that it is casted upon them and whatever they see.... YOU now see. I love casting it upon an angry Sand Giant and watch it roam the dunes destroying everything in its path.
More useful uses for this spell include:
Cast on your tank as he pulls from within the dungeons. You can explore the dungeon with his eyes.
Cast on a newbie and give him pointers as he fights in a zone.
Cast on a mob in a new zone and recast onto a mob further out (note you cant get too far for it has its distance limit) to get a feel for what the zone is like. thanks to Wraithe of Innoruuk for the extra uses.

Flame Shock
This is a fire based direct damage spell. In this level range, you should be using this most often for offensive damage. Upwards to 110 damage per cast.

Heat Sight
Looks as if you are peering through a infravision telescope.

Identify
Used like sense magic. Casting this on books will inform you what language is needed to read. Casting on items such as the Spokes of Tartan's Wheel from the Ice Comet Rune Quest will inform you of the rune's name.

Invisibility
Will make you or anybody you cast it upon within your group invisible for a random length of time. Be careful with it, although it will give you warning as to when it is about to fail, the time between the warning and when it does fail is actually quite short (approx 5 seconds). Undead see right through it also. When a party member is looting his or her corpse you can cast upon them and they will be invisible until the spell wears off or when they get up from looting.

Lightning Bolt
Lighting based bolt spell. Again, high damage but harder to pull off than a DD.

Pillar Of Fire
AE spell based on fire.

Project Lightning
Player Based Area Effect spell based on electricity. It will hit up to three mobs with on casting on an average of 60 damage to each mob. Should be called 'Instant Suicide'. Only use this spell as a last resort to a mob beating upon you.

Shielding
This shielding adds to your armor class and also raises your resistance to magic attacks upon you.

Shield Skin
Although this takes one Cat's Eye Agate to cast (over 2 gold each if store bought), It is an excellent shield for your level. It can absorb from 27 to 55 hit points of damage before it ends.

 

Level Twenty

Elemental Shield
Raises your resistance to cold and heat. Lavastorm anyone?

Enstill
Same as the Root spell but with a longer hold. Also like most of our spells at this level, it gets resisted quite often.

Fire Spiral Of Al'Kabor
Area Effect fire spell. Fire this upon the center target to do damage to it and all mobs surrounding it.

Force Shock
I get many conflicting reports upon this spell. Many people, including myself, will not use it but then again, many people say it works just fine for them. All I can say is purchase it and judge it for yourself. Following is my thoughts upon this Direct Damage spell: When I first got this spell, I was elated. It hit with 171 damage per hit. I have now come to the realization that this spell gets resisted more than it hits for. I have spoken to a few Wizards that wont even use it. They stay with the level 16 damage spells. Rather annoying to spend 11 platinum on something and have it not work the way you hoped it to. On a side note, a reader stated that he uses it for a spell interrupter while going up against casters. Guess you just get that extra edge when it does hit for damage. Thanks to Muera Silvenmoon of Solusek Ro for the interrupt idea!

Gate to Toxxula Forest
This is a personal gate spell that will transport you to the Toxxula Forest. It drops you at -1510, -916 on the coordinates inbetween 4 large stone pillars. All of the gates drop you into safe spots, but if you are evil and venture far, I suggest watching your back.

Gate to North Karana
Another personal gate spell that will transport you to the Southern zone line of North Karana. It puts you right at the bridge river crossing to South Karana. Again, in the middle of four large stone pillars and safe place. If you are of an evil race (like myself) be sure to cast invisibility upon thyself before running by the guards to South Karana. You can swim through the zone but just be sure that you are directly under the bridge in order to zone.

Gate to Greater Faydark
The last of the personal gates for level 20, this drops you at -2023, -441 inbetween stone pillars once again.

Sight
An enhanced version of Gaze.

Tishan's Clash
A stun spell. Great for interrupting NPC casters from beating on the tank of your group.

Track Corpse
This spell is not available to wizards. This is an error in the Kunark Strategy Guide. The spell was wrongly placed as a wizard spell when it is, in fact, a level 20 necromancer spell.

 

Level Twentyfour

Cast Force
Point Blank Area Effect spell that does nearly 100 damage to all things near you. Be sure to have a very good graphics accelerator before casting.... this is the one that drops everyone's framerate to about 5fps. Additional spell information sent in by Quil of Bertoxxulous

Gate to Cazic Thule
Personal Gate to the Temple of Cazic Thule. Be very weary when gating with this one. It drops you atop the combine ruins in an extremely dangerous area known as the 'maze'. I will not gate to this point unless I have a level 30 tank waiting for me upon the other side.

Gate to Nektulos Forest
Personal Gate to the Dark Elven forest of Nektulos.Drops at the Combine Ruins at 432, -772. It is safe for good alignment being to gate in. I would suggest casting invisible upon yourself though. There is a chance of a guard being near due to it going after a spiderling or some other small creature.

Gate to Northern Ro
Personal Gate to the desert of North Ro. Drops you on the combine ruins near the Ro tunnel.

Gate to West Commonlands
Personal Gate to the West Commonlands. Drops you on top of the combine ruins at approximately 0, +1900. Just East of the lake. Light races should be warned that there are two dark elven NPC's that may be nearby, Dragoon Zytel and Kizdean Gix.

Gate to West Karana
Personal Gate to the Plains of West Karana. Drops you in the Southeast part of the zone on the pyramid at -3569, -14815.

Frost Shock
Another direct damage spell based on cold. Easier on the mana than all the rest. Hits for approximately 180 damage.

Leatherskin
Works like Shield Skin but uses one Bloodstone per cast. Can absorb from 71 to 118 hit points of damage before it ends.

Levitate
Allows you to 'float' over anything. Be it water, land, even lava. Great for use in Lavastorm or Highpass Hold on the Gnoll side. Castable upon non party members also. Uses 1 Batwing with each cast. Although the wizard version of this spell is 'research only', you can purchase the versions available to the other classes and they will scribe to your spellbook with no problems.

Lightning Storm
Lightning based area effect rain spell. 75 damage per hit to each creature (up to 3 at one cast).

Major Shielding
The awaited upgrade to shielding. Adds AC and magical resistance.

Column of Lightning
This is a research only, target-based, area effect spell based on lightning yet looks like fire.

 

Level Twentynine

Bonds of Force
A direct descendant of the Druids spell, 'Ensnare'.. this will slow your enemy down to walking speed (reduces movement by 55%). Take note that this is an Area Effect spell and can grasp up to 3 mobs in it's radius. Do not cast in melee range for risk of rooting yourself. Also, if there is a couple of mobs in the vicinity of where you cast and it hits them, you will be added to thier aggro list.
Extra info submit by Nataesha of Innoruuk Server

Energy Storm
This area effect spell has the added bonus of sapping the effected creature's stamina.

Evacuate North
The Evacuation series of spells works exactly like the Portal series, in that they will transport your entire group to the specified location. However, there is a greater chance of leaving a group member behind in the cast. The upside of these spells is that they cast much faster than the portal spells (once you've cast a portal, you'll realize quickly why you don't want to try it in an emergency). This one transports to North Karana.

Inferno Shock
This direct damage spell based is based on fire, the usual upgrade to your shock spells. 237 damage while using 135 mana.

Imbue Fire Opal
This spell can only be scribed and used by wizards that are followers of the diety, Solusek Ro. You can only purchase this spell from a vendor in the very bottom of Solusek B named Zordak Ragefire (who is part of the cleric's epic quest). This spell is used to imbue fire opals with magical properties so that they may be used to create player made armor.

Magnify
This is the continuation of the Glimpse/Gaze line, offers an extended range of vision and a longer duration. Also gives Ultravision when used.

Portal Fay
This transports an entire group to the same location as Fay Gate. When portaling, all group members should be within 10 feet of the wizard. Some myth holds that having each member click on the wizard as his current target decreases the chance of leaving anyone behind there is no proof behind this but it wouldn't hurt. Portal spells cast very slowly (in upwards of 15 second casting time).

Portal North
This transports an entire group to the same location as North Gate.

Portal Tox
This transports an entire group to the same location of the Tox Gate.

Shock Spiral of Al'Kabor
Like the other Al'Kabor spells, this is an area effect spell that radiates out from the center targeted mob. Also like all of the other area effect spells, it brings alot of attention to yourself from the mobs.

Thunder Strike
Continuing the tradition of bringing you two forms of direct damage spells each level, Thunder Strike offers electrical damage to offset Inferno Shock's heat damage. Note that heat spells function with decreased effectiveness in rain while electrical spells function with increased effectiveness. 247 damage at 150 mana used. Unfortunately, it gets resistes like the level 20 Force Shock spell. Good for disrupting spellcasters.

Yonder
A spell that was taken away from us after beta and only given to Druids, this has once again been added to our spell line. Yonder works much in the way that Fade and Shadow Step do only transporting you a bit further then the others. Been told a great way to get out of a sticky situation is to Fade, Shadow Step, Yonder, Gate. Although this sounds great, I cant imagine having all of those loaded upon my list at once.

 

Level Thirtyfour

Circle of Force
This is a target based area effect spell based on magic.

Combine Portal
This portal will take you and your group to the Dreadlands of Kunark. The drop point is at +3050, +9650 and is a safe place to portal into. I have yet to see any mobs path nearby. To use this portal you will have to own the Kunark expansion and have it loaded on your system. To purchase this spell you must visit the Gypsy vendors (South Ro for evil races and North Karana for good races).

Evacuate Fay
Functions like Portal Fay but faster and with a higher chance of leaving someone behind.

Greater Shielding
More Armor Class and more Hit Points.

Harvest
A self only alteration skill spell that will increase your mana points from 171 (L34) to 301 (L60). Casting this on yourself will stun you for 10 seconds and then cannot be recast for a full 10 minutes. It only uses 1 mana point to cast.

Ice Shock
A hard hitting Direct Damage spell based on ice.

Iceclad Gate
A personal gate to the continent of Velious within the zone of Iceclad Ocean. This will take you to the loc of -630, +4925. There are roaming mobs in the area that can be outrun with SOW or Journeyman Boots.

Iceclad Portal
A group portal to the continent of Velious within the zone of Iceclad Ocean. This will take you to the loc of -630, +4925. There are roaming mobs in the area that can be outrun with SOW or Journeyman Boots.

Lava Storm
Like the earlier Fire Storm spell, Lavastorm will rain down fireballs in an area surrounding a certain target, causing multiple hits to everything in the area.

Nullify Magic
This upgrade to Cancel Magic works on more powerful spells and can remove more effects at once.

O'Keils Flickering Flame
5 point damage shield as well as an increase in fire resistance by 10. This is stackable with our low level O'Keils Radiation as well as other class fire shield/buffs. This costs 115 mana to cast and can be cast on all targets, not just yourself. This spell can be purchased from many merchants.

Portal: Cazic
Functioning like the personal Gate Cazic, this will take your group to Cazic Thule.

Portal: Nek
Functioning like the personal Gate Nektulos , this will take your group to the Nektulos Forest.

Steelskin
Protecting you like Shieldskin and Leatherskin comes the new evolution! This spell will absorb even more damage than ever before. This spell will absorb from 168 to 230 hit points of damage before ending.

Thunderclap
This is a radius spell eminating from the caster, doing electrical damage.

 

Level Thirtynine

Chill Sight
This spell will provide us with the vision of Dark Elves, Ultravision.

Concussion
This spell is only available by completing the Brain Bite Quest. Costing only 25 mana to cast and only taking 2 seconds to get the cast off, this spell will lower a mob's aggro to you by approximately 400 aggro points. Therefore, if you overburn on a mob, you cast this upon that mob and it will turn back to your group tank (in theory). Sometimes it takes more than one cast but from all the wizards that have it, they say it is an invaluable spell.

Evac: Ro
Evacuation series spell to Ro desert. It will drop your party to the combine ruins within the desert of South Ro. Many use this for personal travel to get closer to Guk and the surrounding area.

Force Spiral of Al'Kabor
Another area effect spell for us to commit suicide with.

Immobilize
This is the much needed upgrade to Root and Enstill. Unfortunately, by the time you get to this level, most of the mobs can shoot back.

Invisibility to Undead
This is a self-only spell that will make us invisible to undead creatures. This is not the same spell that you can purchase from vendors for other classes such as clerics. This spell is mob-dropped ONLY. Costing 100 mana and lasting 50 ticks (5 minutes), you will only be able to use this spell or the level 16 spell Invisibility, not both at the same time. Even if another player casts Invisibility on you, it will make this spell drop.

Lightning Shock
Standard direct damage upgrade.

Markars Relocation
This group evac will only work from within a dungeon in the lands of Kunark. When cast, this will take you and your party to the Emerald Jungle (-1260, +3504). Note: this evac point might be changed soon due to the danger of it's drop point.

Portal: Commons
Group portal to West Commonlands. Light races should be warned that there are two dark elven NPC's that may be nearby, Dragoon Zytel and Kizdean Gix.

Portal: Ro
Group portal to the desert of North Ro.

Portal: West
Group portal to West Karanas.

Shifting Sight
This is just like Bind Sight yet it has the added ability of infravision.

Tishan's Relocation
This group evac will only work from within a dungeon in the lands of Kunark. When cast, this will take you and your party to the zone of Skyfire (-3094, +795). Note: this evac point might be changed soon due to the danger of it's drop point.

 

Level Fortyfour

Arch Shielding
This is the final upgrade to your shielding line of spells. More armor class and more hit points. This will add 100 to 150 Hit points, 42-90 Armor class, and 1 to 20 Resist magic.

Cobalt Scar Portal
This will take your party to the dragon ring portal in the Velious zone of Cobalt Scar (-1065,-1634).

Conflagration
Standard direct damage upgrade.

Diamondskin
This is the final upgrade to the damage shield spells such as Shieldskin and Leatherskin. If you are attacting the attention to use it, you should probably change your tactics. A wizard of your power should not need it. It's nice to have just in case though. It will absorb from 305 to 394 hit points of damage before ending.

Elemental Armor
Resistance boost spell.

Enticement of Flame
This is the lowest level Lure type spell we have in our arsenal and it is based on fire. Lures are used to do damage to mobs that would normally resist our spells.

Gravity Flux
Alteration, target Area Effect spell using 337 mana and doing damage from 170 to 220 hit points, plus whatever damage the mobs take from the fall. Great Divide Portal Teleport
This is a group portal spell that will take you to the dragon rings in the Great Divide of Velious (-3766, +3651). Every person in your party that is to be portaled will need to have one in their inventory. They are no drop items.

Wakening Lands Portal
This is a group portal spell that will take you to the dragon rings in the Wakening Lands of Velious (-3040, -3032). In order to portal to this destination, you will first need to travel by foot to the dragon ring and pick up a dragon's tooth that spawns in the middle, every 30 seconds. Every person in your party that is to be portaled will need to have one in their inventory. They are no drop items. Be warned, there is a level 65 dragon by the name of Wuoshi that literally sits at the zone in point. If you do not have good 'clawsofveeshan' faction, you will be KOS. In order to raise this faction, you will need to kill Frost Giants. Make sure that this is the faction path you want your wizard to take.

 

Level Fortysix

Welcome to the next level. These spells, once completed, will allow a wizard to take his group where none have gone before, the alter planes. The planes are the farthest characters will ever travel in EverQuest, well beyond the world of Norrath itself, and wizards are the gatekeepers to these realms.

Alter Plane: Fear **(no longer in game)**
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Fear, home of Cazic-Thule himself. (This zone is very buggy. Plagued with lag and Link Dead happenings. Enter at your own risk.) This zone can also be accessed through a hidden cave within the Feeroott once you are of level 46.

Alter Plane: Hate
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Hate, home of Dark Elf lord Innoruuk. Component needed: Fuligan Soulstone of Innoruuk. You can only purchase the Soulstone from within the Cleric guildhall in Neriak's Third Gate. The Souldstones cost approximately 250pp. Depending on your god/diety, most light races are KOS in this area of Neriak.

Alter Plane: Sky
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Air. Component needed: Cloudy Stone of Veeshan. It's in Erudin in the Great Library upon the 3rd floor. Sornita Eltern has the stone for sale at approxmiately 250 platinum.
