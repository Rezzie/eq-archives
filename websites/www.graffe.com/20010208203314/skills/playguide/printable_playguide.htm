Wizard Play Guide
by Lindiien Lightbringer of Xegony

In this guide I will discuss three topics:

  1. How to nuke meaning what type of spells.
  2. When to nuke.
  3. What to do when you get attacked.

So, you want to make a wizzie eh? Good choice! I'm Lindiien Lightbringer of the Xegony server. In the last few weeks I've gotten many tells from wizzies who are having trouble with their class and need advice, so I decided to make a wizard play guide to help everyone out.

Note- in this guide I will set forth a series of situations and explain what to do in each case.
Note- all of these scenarios are group combat

Ok, a point about wizards. Most people think we are just nuke, nuke, nuke. But what defines the nuker from the wizard are these strategies which I will teach you in this guide.

1. To nuke or not to nuke? That is the question. (doh, too much Shakespeare class sorry)

This part of the guide is about what type of spells to use, which is only a decision between our two types: Nuke or Stun.

ONLY use stuns on critters that dish out massive amounts of damage like spellcasters. Or maybe the Ghoul Lord who triple hits for 120 and hopefully, if ya get lucky, has his wonderful sword which procs for 70 damage. :P The reason for this lack of usage of stun is because it is very in-efficient mana wise. Markar's Clash costs exactly half the mana that Ice Comet costs while it does 200 damage to Ice Comet's 1120 damage.

For example, if you stun a Greater Kobold ( warrior type creature ) you will stop it for 5 or so seconds preventing it from doing MAYBE 100 damage to your tank while causing you to lose 200 mana and only do fairly insignificant damage to the kobold. But, in the case of a casting creature like the Ghoul Arch Magi, you may prevent a nuke or Gravity Flux which has the potential to do 2400 damage total to the group ( 400 or so per group member)

Hopefully this explanation shows why stunning a warrior type of creature is fairly worthless, while stunning a magic caster can save the group. Therefore, don't stun melee stuff because it's a complete waste of mana.

2- When to Nuke

Note- the key to being a good wizard is NOT being afraid to take damage.

Scenario 1- One creature pull, group is full health and mana:
In this case the group is ready to kick this things ass so don't waste too much mana on it, nuke it once or twice then meditate. If you over nuke it and use 60% of your mana the rest of your group will be ready for another fight while you are LOM and need to med which will cause them to have to sit around and wait for you. Whereas if you nuke it once or twice and med, the mana usage should be distributed fairly equally throughout the group causing a uniform downtime between all the mana users which will lead to less downtime

Scenario 2 - Multi creature pull:
In a pull where you get 3 or so creatures the key is to drop one of these creatures ASAP. If you sit and wait for the tanks to do some damage to the critters to avoid getting hit yourself, the tanks will get mauled while you wait. If you do this, the group will most likely end up dead pretty damn quickly. As I said at the beginning of the guide, don't be afraid to take damage. Right when the tanks start to fight assist the main tank and nuke the creature until it's dead. You probably will get hit but don't be afraid, the tank will taunt it off of ya pretty fast and then you can nuke it again :P just make sure you get that first creature down ASAP.

Another tactic which I use along with the above tactic is this: Pick a target NONE of the tanks are fighting. Nuke it until it charges you. Run out of the room, root it, run back in and employ the above strategy, DONT NUKE THE ROOTED ONE, concentrate all damage on the target the tanks are on like I stated above in order to get that one down ASAP.

Note- some locations will be too dangerous to do this like if there are several creatures outside.

Note- if you get a crappy root and it breaks quickly just run back out and do it again :P

Note- only employ the root strategy on tank creatures, if you do it to a caster it can just sit there and nuke you making your effort pointless

This strategy eliminates one tank creature from the fight completely until you are done with the others and are ready to give the rooted creature a proper beating.

3- What to do when critters get really pissed at you/ when sompin beats on ya.

Whatever you do, DON'T run around like a chicken with no head screaming GET IT OFF. It just makes it harder for the tanks to get a hit in on the creature so it will be on you longer. Either stand in one place and tell the tanks sompin is trying to ruin your beautiful face, or run TO the tanks so they don't have to search around for your creature in the midst of the fight.

Also, I see a lot of casters who start to melee attack the creature when they get attacked… umm WHY? You may inflict a whole 9 damage to the beast which I assure you ain't gonna matter. But, in inflicting this 9 damage you have managed to piss it off even more due to all your misses which will make it that much harder for a tank to get it off. So, don't go melee cuz it just leads to getting pounded even longer.

4- General Notes

  • Don't trust inviso, it always wears off at bad times :P
