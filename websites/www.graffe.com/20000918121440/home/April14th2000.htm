Wizard Bind Portal:
This once shot down idea of being able to take a group to a wizard's bind point seems to have surfaced once again. This time around it's getting a bit more attention. Almost a year ago I brought up the idea to GZ and it was shot down with good reason: the ability to easily farm a mob/item. Picture this ability with a group of known kill stealers/ninja looters. Now with Guides and GM's ability to enforce the Play Nice Policy and reading more about ninja looters being suspended, it seems somewhat feasable. Let's see what Abashi can get accomplished. Good thought. We just gained this ability (a cast-on-others "go home" spell) recently. I've also raised the idea of a "Group Gate to the Wizard's Bind Point". I'm not winning that argument though, so please don't hold me to it

Carmine Drops on Veeshan:
Plaid of Veeshan sent in the news of the rare item dropping on Veeshan. Here is his tale:

Two nights ago I was in PoF with members from various guilds. To name a few: Celestial Tomb, Advocates of Honor, Impact, Dragon Lords of Norrath, Guardians of Veeshan, and United We Stand. Our puller, Arutemisu (whom i was begging ALL night to pull the fingers) finally pulled all three frightfingers and a boogeyman all at once. It was a tough battle till the end to say the least. The boogeyman was the first to fall. Then the next two fingers quickly came face to face with death. Finally the last finger was pulled back in from our kiter, ready to be vanquashed. Lo and behold, to my disbelief of carmine even existing, the last finger drops the infamous carmine robe! I just wanted to give a big THANK YOU to CT, AoH and everyone who fought and died with me that night. Bang Bang!

Plaid - wizard of Veeshan
