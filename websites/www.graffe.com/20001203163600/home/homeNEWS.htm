Staff of the Four Revisited:
I just got back from visiting Occy on the Quellious server. I thought a good, large picture of Occy holding the staff would be nice for all of you to see. Occy was very kind and even offered to come visit me in Nektulos. Here is another picture for you to view -

  • Occy with the Staff of the Four (1024x768, 140kb jpeg)

I'll try to explain the look of the staff's graphic. If you notice the little sphere at the point of the four spires (spires resemble our wizard spires), the sphere has small 'clouds', if you will, that encircle and constantly move. Inside that sphere is a flickering flame. There is also a straight beam of energy that comes from the top of the wooden part of the staff and meets the bottom of the sphere. Personally, I think the staff is simply beautiful and Verant did a great job with it's design. However, I also feel that the resistance stats of it need to have a look over (if you were to compare it to other classes) and possibly tweaked a bit. Also, Occy made it clear that the staff's effect does not stack with Manaskin, but does stack with Manasink.

Thanks for taking the time out of your play to visit me for the chat and picture Occy.

Staff of the Four:
Earlier today I mentioned that Occy <Vis Maior> of Quellious was the first to complete the wizard epic quest and obtain the item. Occy sent in the pics of it's stats and of it being held. Here you go -

  • Staff of the Four Statistics
  • Staff of the Four Being Held

For the staff effect of Barrier of Force, it is a damage absorbsion spell. Right clicking on the staff will cause a 12 second cast of a spell that will give you a 796hp absorbing shield as well as a slight effect of Clarity. This effect can be stacked with the normal Clarity, Manasink (not Manaskin though), and Harvest. It doesn't cost a reagent and will last for 1 hour 40 minutes or until shield is brought down by damage.

Congratulations Occy. Great work by you and your guild!
