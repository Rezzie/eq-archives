Seems that the original poster of the "Camp Banning" to EQVault had his wired crossed just a bit. You can read more on it over at EQVault, but what Verant is doing is politely telling the high level players that are camping a low level item, to move on if there is another lower level group there that wishes to take the spawn. This is a far cry from "GMs are now warning High level people (40+) that are camping for Jboots, that they are exploiting". The GM's will only step in and ask the high level players to move on if they do not yield to a lower level group that wishes the spawn. Now.. if you argue with the GM, you bet your hiney you'll get a warning. =)
