The Quest starts in Najena, in the jail cells that are guarded by Moosh the Ogre Guard. In the cell that has the injured Halfling is a High Elf enchanter named Linara Parlone. When you hail her she speaks of being released and says that the key she needs is on a Priest that is now in an estate. [/ooc She is speaking about the 'a priest of najena' in Unrest (loc +504, +65 on the third floor). He is a rare spawn (a normal ghoul spawns in his place and the spawn rate is once every hour), and was blue to me at Lv 37. Three shots of 318 and he was dead. He drops the Tarnished Bronze Key.

Once you get the key from the Priest go back and give it to her. She will give you a sealed note. She then tells you to bring it to her father. [/ooc her father is one of the fishermen near the lake in Felwithe. His name is Tolkar Parlone. His loc is -60 + 680.] He gives you a Faded cloak and tells you to bring it to his wife in Erudin so that she can enchance it for you.]

Bring the Faded Cloak to the Enchanters guild in Erudin and on the second floor of the Enchanters guild you will find her mother, his wife, Trilani Parlone [loc +530 +730]. Give her the Faded Cloak and she will then give you the Mystic Cloak [8 AC, + 5 int, + 5 Magic Resist. 10 charges of Steelskin.

ALL parts are NO DROP, So once someone gets the key, they must do the rest of the quest.

This quest is geared for light races. If you are a Dark Elf and wish to obtain this cloak, click here.
