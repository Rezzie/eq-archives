Looking to find how much mana you have? Use the following equation:
Level ((Intelligence / 5) +2) + mana items
If you have graduated and forgot algebra like most of us, this is what you do:
1.
 Find your Intelligence and divide it by 5
2. Take the subtotal from step 1 and add 2 to it.
3. Take the subtotal from step 2 and multiply it by your current character level
4. Now count up all of your mana points from items such as a Runed Totem Staff and add it to the total from step 3.
5. Now you have your total mana points!
