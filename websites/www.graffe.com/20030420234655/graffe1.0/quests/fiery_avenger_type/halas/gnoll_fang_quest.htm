Halas KOS Issues - Gnoll Fang Quest

IMPORTANT: Before even trying this quest, be sure to con the NPC, Lysbith McNaff. If she is 'scowls/attack' to you, you cannot do the quest!

In the back of Halas, there is an area in the warriors guild called 'The Pit' (see area #10 on the map). You will find a female warrior NPC named Lysbith McNaff that has three quests she will give. The one that will give you the fastest faction increase is the one she does not speak of. This quest is is the Gnoll Fang Quest. Giving her three gnoll fangs at once will result in some coin, a ration, and the following faction adjustments -

Positive faction:

  • Merchants of Halas
  • Shamen of Justice
  • Steel Warriors
  • Wolves of the North

For a quick way of getting the gnoll fangs, goto Blackburrow and use your area effect spells to wipe out hordes of the little dogs. The fangs are NO DROP, so you can't purchase them from people in the zone.
