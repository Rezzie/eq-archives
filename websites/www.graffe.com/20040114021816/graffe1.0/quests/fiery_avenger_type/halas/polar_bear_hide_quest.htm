Halas KOS Issues - Polar Bear Hide Quest

This quest starts in Halas, at Mac's Kilts (building #5 on the map). Step inside and speak to Cindl.

You say, 'hail Cindl'

Cindl says 'Welcome t' me humble shop, then! I offer fine items fashioned from the skins o' the beasts o' Everfrrost, I do. Leather is me specialty and I can always use the [extra hides], ye know.

You say, 'what extra hides?'

Cindl says 'I'll offer any hunter some used tattered armor fer every polar bear skin I'm brought. I'm sure even ye can wrestle the skins from the backs o' the polar bear cubs, no?

Now go out to Everfrost and start killing those poor, defenseless, polar bear cubs. Collect only the polar bear skins that appear white with the icon. I was able to get one with about every fourth cub I killed. A good place to hunt them is right in front of Halas, down the ramp in the newbie killing area. There, you get two paths of low level mobs crossing constantly. Just inform the newbies in the area that you are there getting the skins so you can increase your faction and they will usually let you have all the cubs. Once you have enough skins, invis yourself, run back to Cindl and hand her one skin at a time. She will reward you with coin, patchwork armor (be good and give this to the newbies that allowed you to slay their precious cubs), and experience.

Factions Raised:

  • Merchants of Halas
  • Shamen of Justice
  • Wolves of the North

Be careful when handing in the skins because there are three guards that path close enough to aggro when you are inside at Cindl (I've been chased out 3 times now). At the time of writing this, I am still dubious to Cindl and I have turned in over 16 skins total. You can only purchase and sell with a vendor if you are apprehensive or higher.
