Tuesday March 19th, 2002 Patch Message:

Here it is, in all its glory...

* Alternative Advancement Abilities *

- Most targeting issues with Alternate Advancement abilities should be fixed

* Spell Changes *

- The new Evacuate spells are now available on vendors
- Egress and Abscond have a reduced mana cost and a lesser chance to fizzle
- Translocate spells no longer require that the target be grouped with the caster. Also, the dialog box for this spell will remain longer
- Levitation spells now check to see if you have a second Levitation type spell when it wears off before hurling you to the ground
- Eye of Tallon has a longer duration
- Spell memorization speed should now take +Meditate% skill items into account

* Zone Changes *

- Changed safe spot in Kedge Keep to actually be a safe spot

* Combat *

- Pets are now more likely to avoid NPC ripostes
- Wizards now have an inherent chance to score a critical blast with their damage spells after 11th level. This ability does not lessen the chance of scoring a critical hit with Alternate Experience abilities that grant criticals
 

* Miscellaneous Changes *

- Critical hit messages from spells now take into account PvP damage calculations. The actual damage was calculated correctly, it was just displaying wrong on the critical message

If you want to check out the entire patch message and/or comment on the changes made in todays patch, check out this thread currently in progress. :)

Wizard Changes on Test:

It appears that some new changes for Wizards have made it onto the test server.  Rich Waters was kind enough to share them with us on the forums...

Hi all, just wanted to mention a change I put up on Test tonight and get some feedback -

Now on Test Server - Wizard's have had a new Evacuate spell added, and the existing one has been lowered in mana cost.

Lesser Evacuate costs 150 mana and is available at level 12. It drains 300 mana from the party when it is cast, and takes 12 seconds to cast.

Evacuate now costs 100 mana to cast and only drains 150 mana from the party. This is changed from 300 mana casting cost, and a 750 mana drain on the party.

The goal of this change is to allow lower level groups to have a good escape option when adventuring into dungeons. It should also have the benefit of adding value to taking an evac caster in your group at earlier levels.

I'll check back later for feedback, so please post if you have an opinion and get some time.

Thanks,

Rich Waters - Lead Designer, Systems
EverQuest Live and Expansion Team

Please keep in mind that these changes are of course only on test and that they may never make it to the live servers.

If you wish to join the discussion currently in progress you can check it out in this thread on our forums.

Perhaps this will aid the younger generation of Wizards in traveling through the newly revamped Cazic-Thule. :)
