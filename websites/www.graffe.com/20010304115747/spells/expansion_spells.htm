Expansion Spell Listing

This spell listing was taken from the spell data file downloaded from the Test Server. This list is more than likely subject to change greatly. Keep in mind that this list is not final and many things may be deciphered wrongly to preserve the secrecy behind the Ruins of Kunark.

Level 51

  • Atol's Spectral Shackles - Decrease Movement by 60% (Much like Bonds of Force)
  • Tishan's Discord - Stuns target for 5 seconds, as well as doing 260 damage.
  • Draught of Fire - Fire based DD ranging from 630 to 675 damage.
  • Pillar of Frost - Cold based AoE ranging from 725 to 750 damage per mob.

Level 52

  • Manaskin - Personal damage absorbtion from 521 to 591 points. Mana increase by 1 (per tick I suppose)
  • Abscond - Evacuate to an area within the same zone.
  • Lure of Frost - Cold based DD hitting for 600. Decrease in CHA -5 (this could be an error)
  • Tears of Druzzil - AoE rain damage hitting for 600 per mob.

Level 53

  • Annul Magic - Cancel Magic +9 levels.
  • Inferno of Al'Kabor - Fire based AoE damage ranging from 690 to 770 per mob.
  • Jyll's Static Pulse - Point Blank AoE damaging for 495 points.

Level 54

  • Shield of the Magi - Personal damage absorbtion from 232 to 250 points, AC +30 to 32, Magic Resist unknown.
  • Voltaic Draugh - Lightning based DD ranging from 645 to 675.
  • Pillar of Lightning - Lightning based AoE ranging from 775 to 795 damage to each mob.
  • Thunderbold - Stuns target for 3 seconds and Increases HP by 600.

Level 55

  • Plainsight - Self based Ultravision and See Invisible.
  • Lure of Flame - Fire based DD hitting for 600 with a CHA -5 (may be an error).
  • Draught of Jiva - Magic based DD ranging from 650 to 675.
  • Tears of Solusek - Fire based AoE damaging each mob by 645.

Level 56

  • Markar's Discord - Stuns target for 8 seconds with a damage of 750.
  • Retribution of Al'Kabor - Cold based AoE ranging damage from 781 to 821.
    Jyll's Zephyr of Ice - Point Blank AoE based on cold, causing 561 damage to each mob.

Level 57

  • Evacuate - Group evacuate to another spot within same zone.
  • Draught of Ice - Cold based DD hitting for 660 to 675.
  • Pillar of Flame - Fire based AoE ranging from 825 to 840 damage to each mob.

Level 58

  • Fetter - Root type spell.
  • Lure of Lightning - Lightning based DD hitting for 600.
  • Tears of Prexus - Cold based AoE hitting for 690 to each mob.

Level 59

  • Invert Gravity - AoE spell pushing mobs back 3 feet and throwing up 14 feet while damaging for 250.
  • Vengeance of Al'Kabor - Large AoE hitting for 858 to 868 to each mob.
  • Jyll's Wave of Heat - Point Blank AoE ranging from 592 to 612 damage per mob.

Level 60

  • Winds of Gelid - Point Blank cold AoE hitting for 854 to each mob. Large radius.
  • Sunstrike - Fire based DD hitting for 1600.
