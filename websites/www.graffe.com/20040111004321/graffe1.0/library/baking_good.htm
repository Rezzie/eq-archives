By Aline Griffonfeather of Povar

Here is a guide on how you can raise your baking skill quickly and cheaply. Be warned that this can get boring and you won't make any interesting food. This guide is really only usable by Good Races unless for some reason you have faction with Erudite vendors and High Elf vendors. There are probably other vendors for Evil Races but I do not know of them. Another way to get around this problem is to have an alternate buy the items and transfer them to your character.

The key to raising your baking skill is fish rolls. Fish Rolls will take you all the way to become a master cook for the Shawl Quests. When making fish rolls, you must combine one fresh fish with one batwing. The stacks of batwings are purchasable from a vendor in a small shack in East Commons. If you zone into East Commons from the road in West Commons, and look directly to your left, there will be a group of small houses. Walk towards these houses and the single one on the right has an Erudite vendor that sells stacks of batwings. I suggest buying as many of these except for one slot of your inventory. This should be at least 250-300 batwings. Next, teleport to Greater Faydark and run to Felwithe. Head towards the South Felwithe zone, but right before you zone in, there is a small Bait shop with a nice lady that sells fresh fish. Buy as many of these as you can. Then, to the right of this shop, behind a femal jewelry NPC, is an oven. In this oven, combine your fresh fish with batwings, 1 by 1. When you run out of fresh fish, continue to restock them at the baits shop. And when you run out of batwings, just run back to the Erudite vendor that sells them. Doing this repeatedly will bring your baking skill all the way up to 135, but you can stop at 122 if you are just doing the Shawl Quests.
