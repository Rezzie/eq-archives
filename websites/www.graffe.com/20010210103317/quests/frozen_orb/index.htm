This quest takes place in the Eastern Wastes on the continent of Velious. You must kill a Ry`Gorr Messenger near the orc fort.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Outside of the Ry`Gorr Orc fort in the Eastern Wastes there is a tree. Every three hours or so (you may have better luck), a Ry`Gorr Messenger shows his face at the top of the hill behind the tree (-3150, +400). Once the messenger spawns, kill him and upon his corpse you will find a set of Ry`Gorr Invasion Plans.

Once you have the plans, take them to the 'a coldain lookout' a couple hundred feet away (-2600, -500). Note that the lookout cannot be hailed. Just simply hand him the plans. Upon doing so, many Ry`Gorr Oracles will spawn as well as the named Ry`Gorr called 'Scarbrow Ga`Hruk'. He is a shadow knight, hits for about 130 damage, uses harmtouch, engulfing darkness, and fear. Scarbrow is approximately level 47. After killing Scarbrow, you should find a Frozen Orb on his corpse. Note: Since the patch on 1/18/01, the orb has become a rare drop from Scarbrow. He will not have it every time. So far it seems to be about a 1 in 5 chance of dropping.

There is a way to get only Scarbrow to spawn without the oracles backing him up. In order to do so, you must have a friend stand inbetween two trees at the location of -2689, -50. Once they are at that location, hand over the plans. Scarbrow should be the only orc to spawn.
