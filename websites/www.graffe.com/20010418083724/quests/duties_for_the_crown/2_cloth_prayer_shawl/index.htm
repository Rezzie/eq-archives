This quest starts by speaking to Loremaster Borannin in Thurgadin. You can find him in the hall by the library. You must complete part one of the quest before you can continue with this.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


After giving the four toes from part one, recieving the burlap prayer shawl, and being with warmly faction to the coldains, you can proceed with the following -

You say, 'what task'
Loremaster Borannin says 'Every year the Dain replaces the trophies in his trophy room. There is a reward given for the best new trophy obtained. I intend to win this year and I will upgrade your prayer shawl for assisting me. Fill this box with ten Kromrif heads and bring me the combined contents along with the burlap shawl. I will submit the best one for the Dains consideration.

You get a 10-slot box with a combine button to fill. Kromrif heads are somewhat rare drops off of scattered giants within the Great Divide. Fill the box with ten heads and hit combine. Once doing so, you will have a box of Preserved Kromrif Heads. Put this box and the Burlap Coldain Prayer Shawl (from the first quest) in a trade window with Loremaster Borannin and hit trade -

Loremaster Borannin says 'Ahh, some fine specimens indeed. I will have the best of these mounted at once, wish me luck in the contest! Before I forget, here is the Cloth Prayer Shawl of our people. Before long I will have yet another [chore] for you to complete. Be sure to keep checking.

Your faction standing with Coldain got better
Your faction standing with DainFrostreaverIV got better
Your faction standing with Kromzek got worse
Your faction standing with Kromrif got worse
You gain party experience!!

The Lormaster gives you a cloth prayer shawl that is one point better in all areas vs. the burlap shawl from the first quest -

You say, 'what chore?'
Loremaster Borannin says 'Sorry, the Dain hasnt yet given me more work for you to complete, but rest assured more is coming. Please come back soon and ask me to assign your next [chore].

You say, 'give me the next chore you ugly little smurf!'
Loremaster Borannin says 'Sorry, the Dain hasnt yet given me more work for you to complete, but rest assured more is coming. Please come back soon and ask me to assign your next [chore]. I'm assuming this part is not yet in game.
