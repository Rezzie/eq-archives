Lure of Lightning
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 320
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Definition: Direct damage lure spell based on magic drops your target's charisma by 5 and damages for 680. Lure spells are a great new addition to our arsenal in that they allow us to hit mobs that would normally resist everything we threw at them, such as Spite Golems in Hate. They may not hit with as much damage and efficiency as we are accustomed to, but at least we can be a strong part to any group.

Manasink
Skill: Abjuration
Mana Required: 200
Spell Duration: 720 ticks (1 hour and 12 seconds)
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 9.0 seconds
Description: Using one jasper to be cast, this personal shield will absorb 265 points of magical damage only. This spell is stackable with all of our personal shields. Geoff Zatkin of Verant describes it here, "manasink - think of it as another diamondskin, but it ONLY absorbs damage from spells".
