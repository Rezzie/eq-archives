Level Forty Six

 

Alter Plane: Fear **(no longer in game)**
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Fear, home of Cazic-Thule himself. (This zone is very buggy. Plagued with lag and Link Dead happenings. Enter at your own risk.) This zone can also be accessed through a hidden cave within the Feeroott once you are of level 46.

Alter Plane: Hate
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Hate, home of Dark Elf lord Innoruuk. The landing area is not a safe spot. Mobs can aggro through the walls as well as roamers are near. Zone in, invis, and move out.
Component needed: Fuligan Soulstone of Innoruuk. You can only purchase the Soulstone from within the Cleric guildhall in Neriak's Third Gate. The Souldstones cost approximately 250pp. Depending on your god/diety, most light races are KOS in this area of Neriak.

Alter Plane: Sky
This spell will transport your party to the Plane of Air. The landing is a safe area with NPC's nearby.
Component needed: Cloudy Stone of Veeshan. It's in Erudin in the Great Library upon the 3rd floor. Sornita Eltern has the stone for sale at approxmiately 250 platinum.
