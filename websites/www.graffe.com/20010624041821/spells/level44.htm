Cobalt Scar Portal
Mana Required: 300
Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group
Area Effect Range: 100 feet
Casting Time: 16.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 10.0 seconds
Description: This will take your party to the dragon ring portal in the Velious zone of Cobalt Scar (-1065,-1634). The zone in point is patrolled by a few cobalt drakes. These drakes cast (at close range) an ice-based attack which can also dispell two buffs. The drakes are blind to invis but may see sneak or hide. In order to portal to this destination, you will first need to travel by foot to the dragon ring and pick up a dragon's tooth that spawns in the middle, every 30 seconds. Every person in your party that is to be portaled will need to have one in their inventory. They are no drop items. Once you have obtained the portal tooth key for Cobalt Scar, you can destroy the teeth to Great Divide and Wakening Lands.
Vendors (??pp):

  • Skyshrine, Guardian Trekolz

Mob Dropped:

  • Velious Mobs

Wakening Lands Portal
Mana Required: 300
Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group
Area Effect Range: 100 feet
Casting Time: 16.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 10.0 seconds
Description: This is a group portal spell that will take you to the dragon rings in the Wakening Lands of Velious (-3040, -3032). In order to portal to this destination, you will first need to travel by foot to the dragon ring and pick up a dragon's tooth that spawns in the middle, every 30 seconds. Every person in your party that is to be portaled will need to have one in their inventory. They are no drop items. Once you have obtained the portal tooth key for Wakening Lands, you can destroy the tooth to the Great Divide. Be warned, there is a level 65 dragon by the name of Wuoshi that literally sits at the zone in point. If you do not have good 'clawsofveeshan' faction, you will be KOS. In order to raise this faction, you will need to kill Frost Giants. Make sure that this is the faction path you want your wizard to take.

  • Skyshrine, Guardian Trekolz (vendor) ??pp
  • Velious Mob Drop
