The issue is really quite simple, why don't Wizards get the spell Enduring Breath?

Abashi:

There are more utility spells out there then Enduring Breath and levitate, and though they are indeed utility spells, they aren't of the same line.

Utility spells are any spells that are not combat related. Examples of this, while including EB and Levitate, also include Shrink, most of the mages non-pet summons (even if the result of the summoned item is an attack/defense spell), and a good portion of the enchanter spells (such as metal enchantments, alliance, etc). There are many others as well, including travel spells which would also fall under this definition.

Off the top of my head, I don't believe that clerics get either of those spells. I might be wrong about that though.

It's not an oversight, it's by design.
