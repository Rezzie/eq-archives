PVP AoE Issues:
If you haven't been keeping up on the news lately, there is a growing concern on the PVP servers about the usage of AoE's while in group. On the pure PVP (free for all) servers, the majority of our AoE spells will damage anyone in it's wake. GZ commented to this growing concern a few days ago (click here for that interview). Now with that being said, Tannista Daemonsyre has done a bit of research for me and has found out that on the PVP Teams server, this is not the issue. Example: a Wood Elf, a High Elf, and a Half Elf are grouped together. The High Elf is a wizard and casts an AoE in the middle of the group. None of the group members are damaged by his AoE because they are on the appropriate team. Now if a Gnome joins the group and that same High Elf casts an AoE, everyone in the group is safe except the Gnome. Now the question remains as to if Verant would even think to implement a code to make group members immune on the other Zek servers. It's doubtful, but we'll see.

Talks with GZ:
Yesterday, Geoffrey Zatkin took a bit of time out so that I could go over a few things with him concerning our class as wizards. I'm not going to quote him because our chat was very relaxed and not based on facts or numbers, so to speak. I will enlighten you as to a couple key points.

  1. Our value in a group - Personally, I feel our class could be made more useful in a group. Verant thinks we are fine and no drastic changes are going to happen. I brought up the point that our AoE's are beautiful examples of the wizard's true power but so many groups are afraid to bring more than one mob in to fight. GZ made a valid comment that we as wizards, need to educate our groups much like enchanters have to with mesmerize. End result - start using your AoE's guys and gals. With the expansion coming, we are getting some very nifty high level AoE's.. start learning how to use them effectively. GZ's end comment was that we are getting some cool high level expansion stuff, but Verant is not going to be adding or taking away much stuff in the future as to how our class is played.

  2. Rain Spells - What is up with the number of cycles per the number of mobs? If you haven't noticed, with more mobs in the area effect of a rain spell, the less times it 'rains'. GZ said that all rain spells (not just wizards) need to be looked at and they will before Kunark is released.

Closing statement on all of this. I love being a wizard, please don't e-mail me begging me to start a petition or start throwing rocks at Verant. I do think our class is one of the best or I would not continue to play it or run this site. But just like any class, we have our downfalls. Keep in mind that the game is HUGE and it is constantly evolving. If you have comments about the class, e-mail them to Verant or use their Forums. If you want to be heard, be constructive. Don't cuss, don't whine.. much more will become of it.
10:45am -Graffe
