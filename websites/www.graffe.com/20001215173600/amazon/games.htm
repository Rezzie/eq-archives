:: GAMES ::
EverQuest: The Scars of Velious
The second expansion to Everquest. Due for release on December 5th, 2000. This is the expansion only. You must have Everquest installed on your machine in order to install this. Pre-order your copy!
Everquest - Ruins of Kunark
This is the full version of Everquest and the first expansion called 'Ruins of Kunark' all on one CD. See the Strategy Guide as well.
Ground Control
A great 3D real time strategy war game. The graphics are stunning as well as some pretty impressive AI.
Baldur's Gate II - Shadows of Amn
The sequel to the original Baldurs Gate, this roleplaying game has stunning graphics as well as a huge wealth of quests. A must have for any RPG fan. One great feature is once you have finished, you can use your characters to play Icewind Dale! See the Strategy Guide as well.
Icewind Dale
Much like Baldur's Gate in the way of graphics and layout, this RPG takes place in Icewind Dale, the trilogy by Robert Salvatore. See the Strategy Guide as well.
Diablo II
Not really an RPG, more of a hack and slash. Still, it's got a huge fan following and it's loaded with hours of fun.

This guide won't answer all of your questions, but it's a fairly decent guide to have next to you while playing. Most of the info inside can be found upon the web, but heck... this site is listed in it so I guess it can't be that bad.
Versus Books Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn Official Perfect Guide

Icewind Dale Official Strategies & Secrets
Written by Chris Avellone, a member of the game's development team, and features comprehensive maps and walkthroughs, inside stats and strategies, plus an exclusive full-color poster.
