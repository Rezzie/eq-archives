Within Qeynos at the bridge near the bank, there is an NPC named Zamel. Speak to him about the aqueducts, beasts and children. He will then give you the information you need to start the quest. Go down into the water where the bridge is and find the spawn point within the sewer (132, -63). Please note that there is a way down in the water right next to Zamel but it has been blocked off for downward travel. You must use the other entrances to get down. Kill everything that spawns and at some point Cuburt, a gelatinous cube, will come out. Kill him and he will sometimes drop a tattered dress. Kill Cuburt quickly or he will sometimes kill himself within the water as he runs away. Take the tattered dress back to Zamel and he will in return give you a +3 intelligence earring (the booby prize is a piece of hemitite). Getting the earring in return is actually quite rare. Be prepared to do this quest over and over.
