Pillar of Lightning
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 344
Area Effect Duration: 5.0 seconds
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds
Description: Area effect spell based on magic. When cast, this spell will do from 828 (L54) to 848 (L58) damage to each mob within it's radius (4 allowable targets).

Shield of the Magi
Skill: Abjuration
Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: 900 ticks (1 hour and 30 minutes)
Casting Time: 12.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Description: When cast, this personal shield will increase add to your total hit points from 232 (L54) to 250 (L60) as well as increase your Armor Class (AC) from 30 (L54) to 32 (L60). Also thought to give a bonus to magical resists.

Thunderbold
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 300
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds

Recast Delay: 18.0 seconds
Description: This is an area effect stun spell that will keep your targets stunned for 3.0 seconds (if not resisted), do 600 damage to every mob in it's radius (4 allowable), as well as push each target back 3 feet.
