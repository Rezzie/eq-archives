Flaming Sword of Xuzl - Words from Geoffrey Zatkin.
May 31st, 2000

Graffe: Can you explain the thought behind the Flaming Sword of Xuzl? Lots of mana (700), hits for 50, has very, very, low hp (dies in one hit from a blue mob), and also dies after the mob is dead.

GZ: Think of it as some of the ultimate indirect damage. It is a "pet" that hits pretty damn hard. But you can't treat it as a pet because it really isn't a pet. The sword won't tank, it won't taunt, etc. What it will do is hit really hard against creatures with massive resistances. If you send it in to a fight first, it will die. If you send it in to a fight once there are 5-6 people hitting the monster, then it should last the entire fight and do some serious damage. If people are thinking of it as a pet, they are going to be seriously disappointed - wizards don't get pets in the EQ sense of the word. This is another way to do damage that just happens to involve a npc.
