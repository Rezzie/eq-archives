Area Effect Spells on PVP Servers - Words from Geoffrey Zatkin.
March 15th, 2000

If you don't play on the PVP servers and don't understand where this is going, I'll explain it here:

Picture a nice day in the bottom of Guk. Your group's tank runs out to bring back something to kill. Upon his return with the one Dar Ghoul Knight, the room spawns as well. Now you have 4 mobs instead of one. Those of us on the non-PVP servers, would probably fire off an AoE upon the mob that the tank is fighting and then watch the surrounding mobs writhe in pain as their HP gets pulled from them. Well, this isn't the same scenario for PVP players. If they are to cast an AoE, everything in the AoE's radius takes damage.. including players. Because of this, it is not too wise to use your AoE's.

Now I spoke to Geoffrey Zatkin again concerning this subject and here is some of what he said:

GZ: it has always been that way - in the same way that a caster can hit themself with their own AoE. Because AoE's are more deadly on PvP servers, they are more deadly to everybody.

Graffe: So no change in the future then? Not even to exclude just your party members?

GZ: AoE spells were balanced damage wise vs. NPC's. NPC's have more hp (by far) then PC's. Beings able to nuke with impunity all hostile pc's but not your own people, especially if they are all close together, would be very, very unbalanced.

Now with our true damage being in certain AoE's, this is a downside to playing on the PVP servers if you do not play there to do combat with others. There are a few ways this could be resolved but it would be up to Verant and the players on the PVP servers. One, make AoE spells to be effective to mob's only (much like Fear and Charm now). Two, reduce the damage AoE's do to player characters (much like root has been nerfed on pc's). Keep in mind that not everyone on the PVP servers would want this to happen. Some play there to do battle and player kill, others play there to adventure with the risk of player killing.

GZ: One of the problems is that *if* we change anything, we will have unhappy players. Lowering damage on AoE's will make unhappy wizards. Making AoE's not wax your own party will make Wizards too powerful in PvP - unhappy players again. This is an issue where no matter what we chance, we will have unhappy players. In a situation like this, we will stick to the original design of the game.

Graffe: I totally understand that point. I just keep going round and round trying to find a viable way to allow PVP'ers a way to use them. I use them, and with thier flaws, I still love them if they are used at the right time.

Geoffrey had a suggestion to help, but not solve, the issue and it's a fairly simple one.

GZ: tell them not to have their buddies stand there - or to, if they are going to use fire AoE (as an example) in combat, to make sure their buddies are immune to fire.

After looking all of this over, I had to think about something. This is also the way it is on the regular servers if you choose to go PVP by visiting the Priest of Discord. Or, picture your guild heading out to 'The Arena' (granted there is no mobs there) to do battle with another guild. If everyone is up on the battle platform duking it out and a wizard casts an AoE, it is going to hit everyone, including his guildmates and party members. I doubt that is any consolation to the wizards on the PVP servers, but I just wanted to you to know that it can happen on the regular servers if you choose to go PVP.
