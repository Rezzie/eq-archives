1)Why does mana not have a numeric value when everything else does:

Negative argument I've seen for this: It would take away some of the mysticism of the game.

My reply: But we have exact casting times, stat values, hp and ac value.

We've spoken about the mysticism before, and in regards to your argument, we actually publicize far more numbers in game than we originally would have liked. The fact that we did publicize those additional numbers doesn't necessarily mean that we should publicize the next thing that seems "logical" given that argument. At some point we either have to stop releasing more numbers, or just release them all. Though there are certainly people who would like to see numbers on everything, we're of the mind that the game shouldn't be reduced to pure mathematics in the eyes of the players.
