Halas Help for KOS Wizards

If you are an evil raced wizard or if you have killed a few barbarians in your upbringing, you are more than likely not too welcomed in Halas. Fret not, it's not as bad as it seems.

The shaman guild area in Halas that we evil or KOS wizards need to do our transactions with Dargon/Arantir in is patrolled by two guards. One named Cian McMarrin and his brother, Alec McMarrin. Both of the guards stop right in front of the shaman guild during their patrol and this stopping point is what I will refer to as their starting spot. You can use this map that I have edited (original created by Aghzul of Solusek Ro) so you can get a better understanding of the guard pathing and the safe spot.

Using a stopwatch, I have timed their patrol -

Cian McMarrin

  • 00:00 minutes - Leaves the starting point and heads to the rear houses of Halas.
  • 06:30 minutes - Returns and walks by the shaman guild, headed towards the bank.
  • 09:00 minutes - Stops in front of the shaman guild. (the end of his patrol)
  • 09:50 minutes - Begins his patrol route again.

Alec McMarrin

  • 00:00 minutes - Leaves the starting point and heads to the rear houses of Halas.
  • 09:45 minutes - Returns and walks by the shaman guild, headed towards the bank.
  • 11:10 minutes - Stops in front of the shaman guild. (the end of his patrol)
  • 11:50 minutes - Begins his patrol route again.

Keep in mind that their patrol times do not match. This means that the times they will cross paths in front of the shaman guild will vary. If you have the time to wait or are really paranoid about getting attacked, wait for them to leave the shaman guild at the same time. This way you will have a total of 6 minutes and 30 seconds to do your business.

I reccomend going into Halas invisible and head to the safe spot. Once you have the guard's timing down and it looks safe, run to the shaman guild area and stand behind Dargon, next to the tree. Do not worry about the guild trainer, Shamus Felligan or his brother Waltor. Even though they con 'scowl/attack' they will not attack (you have to trust me on this.. I have personally done it as have many others). Now, drop your invisibility and try to purchase the Ro's Breath potion from Dargon. You can only purchase it yourself if you are apprehensive or higher. Many report that wolf from will not change your faction to Dargon but using a Ball of Golem clay and turning into an earth elemental will work (see the Miscellaneous Equipment page for more info on the Ball of Golem Clay) or if you have an Amulet of Necropotence (see the Mob Dropped Necklaces page for more info), skeleton form will work as well.

If you don't have any luck getting the Golem Clay or an Amulet of Necropotence is out of your reach, you can always get a friend with good faction to Dargon do the potion part of the quest for you. Click below -

  • Having a Friend or Mule Get Your Ring

If you want to try to raise your faction you can try either of the following two quests. Keep in mind that this will take a LONG time and depending on how bad your faction was to begin with, it may never change -

  • Polar Bear Hide Quest
  • Gnoll Fang Quest
