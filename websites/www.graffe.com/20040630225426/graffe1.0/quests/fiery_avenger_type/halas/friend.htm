Halas Help for KOS Wizards - Having a Friend Get Your Ring

This majority of the info on this page was taken from the forum. This just goes to show how much you can learn from simply reading the posts there.

  1. Hand your friend or mule the 1000pp (faction depending) so they can purchase the potion of Ro's Breath from Dargon.
  2. Have your friend goto Erudin and give the potion to Camin and bring back the used potion.
  3. Once the guards are not in the area, stay invisible and run behind Dargon. Stand next to the tree and behind Dargon.
  4. Have your friend hand Dargon the potion which will spawn Arantir.
  5. Drop your invisibility and hail Arantir (I have dropped invisibility here with the two shaman trainers being scowl/attack. So long as you are behind Dargon, they will not attack).
  6. Make sure you have your ring and go visit Challice in Felwithe.

Now you can have your friend continue to speak to Arantir to keep him spawned while you goto Felwithe and deal with Challice so that you do not have to purchase another potion to get him to spawn. NOTE: Some have had Arantir despawn even while talking to him. I suggest doing this multiple times if your Truespirit faction is low.
