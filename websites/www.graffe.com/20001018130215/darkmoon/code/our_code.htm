The above is simply humor. That is not what we stand for. I apologize for being so out of character but recent events have provoked me to find humor in it all.

We in Darkmoon are very strict in the people we call family. We will not tolerate in house squabbling. If you squabble, you must settle the situation immediately with said person or look to a long time member to help you resolve your issues. If you do not, you will be promptly removed.

We have come to the realization that Everquest is not a true roleplaying game and we must change to make the play style more enjoyable for our members. Take note that being out of character in public is still not tolerated. There are times to roleplay and there are times to relax. Our family members can judge for themselves when these times are (only with another family member).

Darkmoon is made up of friends. True friends. Friends that do not backstab. Friends that do not put loot above everything else. Friends that would do anything to make each other smile. Being in Darkmoon is a privilege. You will not seek us out. We will seek you.
