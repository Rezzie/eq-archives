WoW: Class: Warrior: Battle Stance

From GraffeWiki

(Redirected from Battle Stance)
Jump to: navigation, search
Gained: Level 1, innate
Bonuses: -20% threat generation
Penalties: None


A good solid footing suitable for all types of day-to-day fighting. It conferrs no bonuses, and no penalties, and provides access to a wide range of Abilities. It is suitable for most types of weapons.

Abilities usable only in Battle Stance: Charge, Thunder Clap, Overpower, Mocking Blow, and Retaliation. However, Battle Stance shares many further Warrior Abilities with the other two Stances, making it a very flexible stance for general use.

Best used for Soloing or Main Assist, in unfamiliar situations, or against enemies of unknown capabilities and similar, or slightly higher levels.

Useful in PvP for the access to the Charge Ability, allowing a lot of ground to be covered, very quickly. Once engaged, a Stance swap to Berserker Stance may be more appropriate.

(See also: Tactical Mastery Talent)
