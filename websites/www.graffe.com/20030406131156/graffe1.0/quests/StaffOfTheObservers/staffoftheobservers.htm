In Steamfont theres is an Observatory past the windmills (see the map I edited). There you will find a female Gnome lenscrafter named Brona Frugrin that wants the retina (lens) of an Evil Eye in Runnyeye named Borxx (lvl 30) bring the retina (lens) to her and in turn, you will get the staff. The staff carries charges of the spell 'Sentinel', which warns you of incoming creatures while meditating. It also acts as a lightsource when equipped. The amount of charges is directly subject to your faction with Brona. If you are a Gnome that is amiable towards her, you will be rewarded with a staff that has 50 charges of Sentinal, but if your indifferent or so you will get a staff with less charges. Also, the staff defaults to one charge once it is traded or dropped. Be sure to con Brona before you attempt to get near her. You will need good faction to get the staff.
