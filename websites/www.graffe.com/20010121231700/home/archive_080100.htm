The Abscond/Evacuate Project:
The Abscond and Evacuate spells are very nice additions to any Wizard's spell book.

These spells can be used as both a means of escape within a zone, and as a way to cut down travel time while journeying across Norrath.

In keeping with our role as Master Seekers of Knowledge, we have decided to launch The Abscond/Evacuate Project to help locate and document the destinations for these spells within each zone.

If you are a wizard with either of these spells, and would like to join the project, then follow these steps:

  1. Choose a zone at random that has not already had its safe spot documented. (Currently no zones have been documented. As new zones are documented, they will be posted on this page)
  2. Take note of the zone you are in.
  3. Take note of the coordinates of the location the spell drops you, (X, Y, Z).
  4. Take note of any landmarks near the location the spell drops you.
  5. Comment on any possible danger or travel benefits that could result from teleporting to this safe spot.
  6. Add any other comments that might be relevant.
  7. Email this information, along with your name and server to merloc@graffe.com
  8. Give yourself a pat on the back!

Let's see how fast we can document all the safe spots in Norrath!

Concussion Unresistible:
Well, it looks like the annoyance of the "taunt" from resisted spells is working to our advantage for once.

It seems that even when Concussion is resisted, mob aggro is still lowered by the same degree as when the spell is not resisted.

After quite a bit of testing, we found that mobs seemed to back off from attacking the wizard after a concussion spell was cast, whether or not the spell was resisted... Which makes sense, when you consider that other attack spells still carry their positive aggro component when they are resisted.

This should be good news for all those wizards out there who rely on Concussion to keep them alive.

Thanks to Ichron of The Rathe for noticing this in the first place, and testing it out extensively.

Markar's Discord Stun Time:
Zarkon of E'ci was able to get his hands on the level 56 spell, Markar's Discord. All of the websites have the spell listed as an 8 second stun doing 750 damage. Zarkon informed me that the stun actually carries a duration of only 5 seconds. I contacted Geoff Zatkin to see if the spell data was wrong or if the spell was actually not doing what it was intended to do. Once again he told me not to trust the spell data. End result: I'm changing the site to reflect a 5 second stun, not 8.

Thanks for the info Zarkon!
