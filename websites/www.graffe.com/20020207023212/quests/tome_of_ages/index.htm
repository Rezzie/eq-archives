This quest is started by speaking to the Oracle of K'Arnon within the Ocean of Tears.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

You can find the oracle on the Goblin Isle in the Ocean of Tears, directly across from the boat dock on the Sisters Isle. -

You say, 'hail Oracle of K`Arnon'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'Greetings, I am the Oracle of K'Arnon. Do you quest for [knowledge] yourself, or do you offer that which I [seek]?

You say, 'I quest for knowledge'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I have lived long and have insight into many matters, yet I despise charity. To learn from me, you must first bring me that which I [seek].

You say, 'What do you seek?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I seek knowledge, which is superior to all things. And no knowledge is greater than that which is contained within the [Tome of Ages].

You say, 'What is the Tome of Ages?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'The Tome of Ages has been compiled over the millennia by the Scribes of [Dal]. It was divided into seven distinct books by the scribes to keep the knowledge from one such as me. That said, with aid, I shall unite these books and the Tome of Ages shall be mine.

You say, 'Who are the Scribes of Dal?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'They were elves once, but now are Teir'Dal, and as evil as they are wise. Many tomes have they written, but none greater than the Tome of Ages.

You say, 'What books?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'The seven books that make up the Tome of Ages are those of enlightenment, scale, turmoil, monuments, elders, blood, and the lost book. Each of these great books is an important chapter in the history of Norrath. If ever you find one of these books, bring it to me.

You say, 'What is the Book of Turmoil?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'Ah, yes! The Book of Turmoil. I seek this book, and should you find it and return it to me, I will reward you with secret knowledge.

The Tome of Ages was written by the Scribes of Dal. The Scribes of Dal are a group of dark elves that broke the tomb into seven separate pieces and are keeping those pieces hidden from the Oracle. The first piece he seeks is the Book of Turmoil.

To get the Book of Turmoil, you must head to Neriak's Third Gate and go inside the library. Stay left once inside the library and you will find a young dark elven necromancer named Lokar To`Biath and his pet.

You say, 'hail Lokar ToBiath'
Lokar ToBiath says 'I am the Scribe of Innoruuk. If you do not have business with me, begone!
You say, 'What vow?'
Lokar ToBiath says 'Break the vow? She might. Were you to tithe 70 gold to your god, Innoruuk, he might aid you.

Hand him 70 gold (there is a lot more before this but you can skip it)

Lokar ToBiath says 'Take this note to the Scribe of Dal, and perhaps she will break her vow.

Your faction standing with DarkBargainers got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardOuter got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardInner got better
You gain experience!!

Now you have a note in your inventory. Take this note to Lokar's sister, Rysva To`Biath, which is a barmaid in the bottom of the Blind Fish found in Neriak Commons.

You say, 'hail Rysva To`Biath'
Rysva To`Biath says 'Hello. Let me know if I can get you a drink.

Hand her the note -

Rysva To`Biath says 'Take this note and be gone with you.

Your faction standing with DarkBargainers got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardOuter got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardInner got better
You gain experience!!

Rysva has given you a note that tells you to kill her brother Lokar and bring his head as well as this note back to her. So.. go visit him and throw some mana his way -

You have slain Lokar ToBiath!
Your faction standing with DarkBargainers got worse
Your faction standing with DreadguardOuter got worse
Your faction standing with DreadguardInner got worse
Lokar ToBiath says 'The death of a Dark Bargainer never goes unnoticed!

Now grab his head (and his robe if you want it) and run back to Rysva in the Blind Fish. At the same time, hand her the note she gave you as well as his head -

Rysva To`Biath says 'Take this book and be gone with you.

Your faction standing with DarkBargainers got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardOuter got better
Your faction standing with DreadguardInner got better
You gain experience!!

You now own the Book of Turmoil (picture of pages 1 & 2 - picture of pages 3 & 4) Taking this book to the Oracle of K'Arnon will result in a Glowing Torch (weak item for an enchanter) and the following faction adjustments -

Oracle of K`Arnon says 'Thank thee for this tome. Be sure to check back with me later, as I might have another task for thee.

Your faction standing with OracleOfKarnon got better
Your faction standing with OracleOfMarud got worse
You gain experience!!

You say, 'What is the Book of Scale?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'Ah, the Book of Scale.. It would be wondrous indeed if you could recover the Book of Scale for me. If you do manage to procure the Book of Scale, I will gladly part with an artifact of my own that may be of interest to you.

The book of scale drops in two pieces. One piece from the dragon Lord Nagafen in Solusek's Lair and the other from the dragon Lady Vox in Permafrost. Once you have both halves, bring them to Rysva To`Biath in the Blind Fish of Neriak Commons. Hand both halves as well as 1000pp and she will combine them for you (remember the note? She can restore the other books as well).

This is what the Oracle will tell you if you ask about any of the other books -

You say, 'What is the Book of Enlightenment?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I do not seek the Book of Enlightenment at this time, but I might have need of it in the future.

You say, 'What is the Book of Monuments?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I do not seek the Book of Monuments at this time, but I might have need of it in the future.

You say, 'What is the Book of Elders?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I do not seek the Book of Elders at this time, but I might have need of it in the future.

You say, 'What is the Book of Blood?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I do not seek the Book of Blood at this time, but I might have need of it in the future.

You say, 'What is the Lost Book?'
Oracle of K`Arnon says 'I do not seek the Lost Book at this time, but I might have need of it in the future.
