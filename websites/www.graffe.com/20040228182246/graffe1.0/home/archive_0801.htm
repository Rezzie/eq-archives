Staff of Temperate Flux Confirmation:
For some odd reason, I was not able to log in yesterday to confirm if the change to the Staff of Temperate Flux was one that would have nerfed it to only work if wielded. Luckily, Glinlor noticed I didn't follow up with news so he informed me that it does still work from a main inventory slot. The only change is that if we wish to use it as a weapon, it must be held in the Primary Melee slot.

Thanks Glinlor and everyone that posted the confirmation on the forum.

Patch Day Message:
Today's patch has a few things that directly effect us as wizards -

  • Staff of Temperate Flux has been changed to main hand only.
  • Ring of Frost Spiders can now be worn.
  • Robe of the Seacaller should now be equipable.
  • Changed the way resists are calculated when a Player Character casts a spell on another Player Character on Sullon Zek. Resist modifiers based on level will now be more smoothly distributed and will allow lower level characters a slightly better chance to land spells on higher level characters. At the same time giving lower level characters a slightly better chance to resist spells cast on them by higher level characters.

I'm checking to see if the Staff of Temperate Flux was fully nerfed to only work while equipped.

Oracle/Tome of Ages Quest:
I got an e-mail today from an individual that was able to speak to Brad McQuaid (Aradune) about the Tome of Ages quest while they were at the Fan Faire -

"I asked him specifically about the Oracle quest and he said that only 2 parts are active. The Torch quest and the pally quest. That's it. I mentioned that someone posted that the full oracle quest had been finished and you had to travel to lots of places including the Hole. He shook his head and said, 'No, that quest is definitely not done'."
So as we all expected, those items sent in last year that have been labeled "Possible Hoax", are indeed a hoax.

Thanks to Colossusa Forbin for the e-mail.
