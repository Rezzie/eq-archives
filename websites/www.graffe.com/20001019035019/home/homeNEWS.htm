Phiningel Poofs:
Pandur of Xegony sent in a little warning on the corpse rotting time of Phinigel Autropos -

"I am just comming from a Kedge Keep raid, and I don't want this to happen to other people :-) After we sucessfully killed Phinigel Autropos, we began to rez people and prepare lotto. Maybe you could warn the fellow wizards out there :-)

Pandur Volucris, 48 wiz of Xegony"
