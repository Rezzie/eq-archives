Level Fifty Nine

 

Flaming Sword of Xuzl
Skill: Conjuration
Mana Required: 700
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 60.0 seconds
Description: This spell will summon a very low level (under 100 hps), animated two hand sword to do melee on the targeted mob. This 'pet' will attack your target but will not transfer to another target or stop fighting until the target is dead. Once the targeted mob has died, the flaming sword dissapears. The pet hits for approximately 50 damage per slash.

Invert Gravity
Skill: Alteration
Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 40 feet
Pushes Target Back: 3 feet
Throws Target Up: 14 feet
Description: This is an upgrade to the level 44 Gravity Flux. This area effect has a huge radius and will throw an unknown amount (assuming 3) of mobs into the air doing 250 damage on the initial hit with 350 additional damage from the fall.

Jyll's Wave of Heat
Skill: Evocation
Mana Required: 342
Casting Time: 2.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds
Description: Fire based, point blank area effect doing from 638 (L59) to 648 (L60) damage to each mob within it's radius (unlimited mobs allowable).
