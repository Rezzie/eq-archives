Hi Brent. It's been a long time since I've seen your posts on the boards. I'm glad to see that you are still around.

As for your poll, we try to keep the focus of polls to something that affects most, if not all, of the players in the game. Class-specific issues don't belong in a login poll, when only a percentage of the people would have any frame of reference. The poll itself would only serve to identify how many people may have misunderstood our intent, and I don't want to give the impression that the issue is being put up for some type of majority-driven redesign of the core game engine.

Also, in the case of definitions, people would tend to vote for what they feel it should be, rather than what it is. I've stated our stance on the wizard issue, and as the creator of the game, it is our right and responsibility to establish the definitions for the various things that we published. If some people misunderstood, I apologize, but in this case the perception of those players is out of line with the vision, not a case where the vision is out of line with the perception of the players.
