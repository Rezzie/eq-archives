Lure Line of Spells:
There is a few lure spells in our arsenal after level 50 but nobody really knew what the deal with them was. Yesterday I asked Geoff Zatkin if they are just less resisted or if the name 'lure' meant anything special. He said they are "..much, much harder to resist". Along with this, I have had a few e-mail confirmations from people saying that they have complete success with these spells against mobs like Cazic Thule. Not only are they not getting resisted, but you can actually cast and hit for full damage just out of melee range.

Brain Bite (Concussion) Spell Quest:
There has been some news found stating that one of the mobs needed in the Brain Bite quest cannot be approached due to the fact that it is stuck in a void (null) part of a zone (picture a mob surrounded by a brick wall). A guide was able to verify this and added that there was no way a player was able to interract with it in it's current location. This may be a single server issue but none the less, I sent off a message to Verant to please look into it.

I also spoke to Geoff Zatkin this morning about the difficulty of the quest asking if it was an oversight and wrongly submitted to the servers or is the spell really that good? He informed me that it's a good spell. I guess time will tell once someone get's ahold of it and uses for the first time as to how good it really is.
