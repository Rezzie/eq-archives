Ultima Online:The Second Age
By Bowmeister

I opened the door, not without some concern as to what might wait on the other side.

"Hail Sir"

I looked up at a man, garbed in a strange suit of finely tailored material, marred only with a strange symbol "UPS" on the breast.

"Hail to thee, and what might I do for thee this fine day?"

"Sir, I bring thee great tidings from none other than Lord British himself! He hath instructed me to let thee know that thy card of many chargings hath been accepted, and to bring thee thy passport unto the new realms!"

I stepped out of the doorway now, eager to recieve this wonderful bounty provided by my Liege Lord British, and equally eager to step forth unto this newly discovered world. The messenger thrust a most non-descript bundle into my awaiting arms.

"Wait sir, " he cried out, as I turned to close the portal. "I have need for thee to place thy mark upon this receipt, so that all may know I failed not in my duty..."

Impatiently obliging the man, I turned and hurried up the stairs...suddenly I thought to myself...

"God damn...I think this stupid game is starting to get to me..." :)
