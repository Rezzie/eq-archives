-What's New-

Altered Worlds Quick Link is an easy way to visit you favorite site on the net.  With one click of the mouse you can check out any page on available.

AWQL just sits in your system tray awaiting your command.  Never again do you have to weed through your Internet Explorer Favorites or Netscape Navigator Bookmarks to get to our site.. Just move your mouse down on the little purple square on your system tray right click and your on your way to the best site on the net.  And as more pages and features are added to the site AWQL will be updated accordingly.  Just click on the menu item that says "Check for Updates" and you will be brought right to this page.
