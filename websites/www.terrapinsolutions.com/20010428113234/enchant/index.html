    Welcome to the Enchanted Circle!

    The Enchanted Circle is an EverQuest guild dedicated to the Enchanter class. Our guild is cross-server and admits enchanters of all races, transcending racial hatred in guild matters. We are dedicated to the four cornerstone virtues of our guild, which are enlightenment, exploration, education and entertainment. If you are an enchanter and are interested in joining our guild, please read the charter and the information about how to join by clicking on the appropriate link on the left side of your screen.

    All existing guild members can gain access to the members-only portion of this website by clicking LOGIN at the left.

    We welcome you to our guild! Enjoy your stay.
