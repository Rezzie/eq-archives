A_Evil_Guy

My blood runs cold when I think of them.

I've spent decades here… not months or years. I've dwelt in this place when I could have been out enjoying myself. For forty-one years I've hired followers, built shrines, loaded tombs with powerful corpses. I've taken every care that I could ever imagine and put it into the placement of my enchantments and the positioning of my traps. I've lured the most magnificent and malicious beasts in the known world and a few that are not from this world into my abode and given them places of prominence and placated them with food or treasure. And it seemed a labor of love.

    Tale by Archanalia


Leg Work

Recently something exciting has happened on my server Karana. In fact I wish I had thought of it. A person from the server called a meeting of guild leaders to discuss the atmosphere of the server.

That about sums up the general intent. Basically the guild leaders who attended, or those like myself who had to go chase down a tart blond greyhound who got out of the fence and had someone log it to read it later, got together with no other interest but to bring more entertainment to the server.

Vol. V

Smoke swirled around my head, and the acrid stench of sulfur filled my nostrils. I looked around to see that I was in a rough-hewn maze of caverns sprawling under the earth. The heat was stifling, and sweat poured down my brow. I heard shouts and footsteps echoing in every direction, and Darnack emerged from the passage ahead. He spoke, but his words were garbled and unintelligible. As he stood there, I noticed that the lustrous bronze of his armor seemed somehow tainted. I glanced at his hands to see that they were discolored...
