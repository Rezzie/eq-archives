Qeynos Hills -

Expectations are high for the three day festival of bards, jesters and all around freaks called "Bardstock".

Several days in advance people have shown up at the standing stones in Qeynos Hills and camped out in anticipation of the great string of acts who are set to perform. It's even rumored that our esteemed editor, Absor, will appear on stage to perform a set alongside Bergin and the Firedrakes.

When campers at the site were polled to see which act they were waiting for, all said they were waiting for someone called "Putrid skeleton", an act which is not even listed on the bill. Nonetheless, anticipation is high.
