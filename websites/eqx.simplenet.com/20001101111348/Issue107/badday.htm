January 1st - January 7th, 2000


A Bad Day in Butcherblock


"Killed by Dwarves and chased by Gnomes in this fair land that I call home.

Sadly they see no wrong in this as they break my teeth with their angry fists.

I run and dodge to no avail, to have my head smashed open with iron flails.

If only they could see across the blue. They'd find a Teir'Dal army... but they have no clue.

Its time for me to go, away I fly. Knowing that one day, they'll all die."

This song got me no sympathy (didnt expect it to) only more disdain as the foul dwarves ang gnomes of butcherblock put prices on my head. I laughed and headed to Kelethin, my home. I rested in one of the tree hammocks, knowing it would be my last time here in a while. Tomorrow, I'll say goodbye to Faydwer.

Kyodai the Bardic Legend

AKA
Maki "Sir Maximus" Naro
