ISSUE # 17Apr. 11th - Apr. 17th

The Gnome’s Secret Plan

The snake sensing the attacks moving with the sword, which reduced my expected amputation to mere scratches. Eventually tiring, the giant snake recoiled and hissed, trying to recover. At that moment, with quick thinking, I lunged at the snake with my shield, distracting him, as I came down with my sword. This time amputating his head, as it rolled across the floor. As I’m catching my breath I hear screams coming from the north. I looked around, finding my comrades had abandon me. Hearing the screams again, I start for the stairs. As I top the stairs I see the source of the cries. The two gnomes were there. The first lay in two pieces on the ground. Attached only by his entrails. The second, one hundred feet off the ground being shook like a toy. You see the snake I killed was a mere child. This was mommy! She stood over one hundred feet tall, her head at least ten-foot wide. Her fangs looked like swords.

Thinking for a second of what my battle plan might be.

I ran.   I’m no idiot

Tale by Razor.
