ISSUE # 57

January 16th - January 22nd


Sonet's Lament

Note: Verse is sung to the tune of O' Holy Night. The refrain is sung to a double beat ( think metalica )

Oh foggy night, the moon was barely shining, and in the wood you could hear the kobolds howl.
Alone in the woods, two adventurers wander, and in the heart of one was a crime most foul.
Etar... oh Etar.... Why did you slay my humble soul?... Etar.. oh Etar, That which you stole shall forever condemn you noooow!


Your soul will be devoured
You will ever be my enemy
Forever will you regret
Dogs of War... (shouts ) to Me!
