Magic

As many of you who read the EverQuest message boards and news group will know that there has been a lot of talk whether there should be Moongates or a type of Recall spell in the game. There have been posts where people would not like them in Everquest at all and posts where people would like them in a game but with some differences than the way Ultima's Moongates were used. Also there have been posts about the good and bad parts of recall type spells.

Moongates

Some people have been posting that Moongates should be in the game but with some modifications. For example, there was a post on a message board that said you should only be able to use then maybe once every two hours. Another thought was to only have them available to certain mage classes that would be able to cast a spell for it. So people would still like to have them in Everquest just not the same way Ultima had them. 		However, some people just would not like Moongates in EverQuest at all. One person posted that Moongates should not be in EverQuest because they take away from the adventuring. Instead of being able to adventure into the wilderness between each city fighting your way through you would only have to walk through a Moongate. Another post said it would take care of the all of the PKs hanging out around the Moongates waiting to kill and loot. Well that won't really be a problem with the PK switch though but it should be considered. Also people were getting mad with the area being over crowded by tame animals, furniture and useless items along with the frustration of usually not being able to go to the right town you wanted to but instead going somewhere else or sometimes actually staying at the same Moongate.

 Recall

In Ultima the Recall spell was thought by some to be a bad spell to have in the game. They thought this because it was a sort of abused spell. It was sometimes used by PKs so that they could kill someone, loot their body, and then Recall the hell out of there. Also it was used by the weaker players to loot something like a lich, drake, or even a dragon that was killed by a stronger player and was therefore left with no reward. This made people come to hate the spell or for some, love it.

Some people would prefer to have a Recall spell in EverQuest or a similar spell for a few reasons. One person said that if they wanted to adventure with some friends that were across the map they would rather recall to a nearby town instead of wasting 2 hours of playing time having to walk to them. Also people just want it for the sake of not having to walk and instead going to where they want to go.
