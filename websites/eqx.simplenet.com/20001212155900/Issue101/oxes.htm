Welpers I thinky jist bout everything except da giants a lil'un to ya's, which means der's lots and lots o lil'uns. So thinky bout dat jist a sec. Okay done thinky about dat? Good... Now why they smack da snott outta him. See.. I's know dis FlooterTooter, Tarell. Now he comes a sing'n in da jungle. He's not doin a thingy, jist sing'n in da jungle. Dunt matter where he comes in ta da jungle, faster than ya's can swing two mino axes der's Bouncer a try'n to thwap him. So dat's why all da lil'uns ya see a thwap'n yer Bouncer, cause der's no music while da blood and guts are a flyin.
