Hail!

First, I want to let you know that I enjoy EQ Express! I am new to the EQ world and find it very enlightening. It is really helping me get a feel for the world.

Second, in Oghma's article, "Dwarven Clans" he stated,

"This was an attempt to build a dwarven ethos and history, without any Norrath background. None of it is official!"

Anyways, keep up the good work! I look forward to your next issue.

Dain Ironhand
