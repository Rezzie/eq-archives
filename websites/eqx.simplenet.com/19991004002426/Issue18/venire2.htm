Venire's Promise

"I will never practice magic," I often told my father. He would always look at me with an appalling glare and chuckle like I was just some stupid kid. Then with a sweep from his enormous hand, I would be cast to the side. You could here him laughing to himself as he walked away. I’ve lived with my grandparents in Qeynos since my father’s death. "Venire"

"VENIRE!!" Gosling barked. He knew I often daydreamed during lessons.

Turning my attention towards my instructor, I walked over towards him.

"I want you to practice this one here" 		He pointed to a paragraph in his ancient book. The book was immense, and the once white pages were cinnamon color, stained with old age. The thick leather binding was flawlessly crafted and a credit to its surviving this long. As usual Gosling read the phrase, and demonstrated the correct gestures to cast the spell. I watched as his energy collected in his hands. His pupils turned up into his head, leaving a white empty stare. It was actually frightening to watch.

Tale by Razor.
