ISSUE # 34August 8th - August 14th

Blood

Trickle red sweet ribbon candy
On satin skin
On white linen
Larynx crush and pale flesh yields
To a precision kiss
like chirurgeon's scalpel

Flesh is bondage
Our souls dragged under
Weighted down through atmosphere
And time a mere feather I brush aside
In whirlwind
Of black down wings and gripping hands

Did we once scale ancient landscapes
Before standing stones
Before pyramids
When blood was wine
And teeth all we needed
To get drunk
To feel
…And to be young again

    Prose by Archanalia
