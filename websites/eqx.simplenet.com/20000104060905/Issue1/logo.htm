EverQuest Logo Competition

 

  EQX is running a competition to design a logo for EverQuest.  The logo should be designed so that it looks good when shrunk to the size of a Windows icon, made into a graphics link, or turned into a badge.  We hope that the contest will be judged by Brad McQuaid, producer of EverQuest, but it will depend on if we can barter him down from his demands for a green tea plantation.
  To enter the competition, have your logo designed and submitted by Christmas Eve (Dec 24th), and send it in .jpg or .gif form to the editor.
