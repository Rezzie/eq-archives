The Life of an Elven Enchanter

Darkness had never been my friend. Throughout the course of my short but exciting life, the night had always seemed to bring far too much danger and mystery - things that could jump out and eat me alive if they felt the need - and that thought was never too comforting. Being an Elf, my infravision had only helped slightly, as I could see my enemies… but not the land surrounding me. I stroll over the wrong hill, and suddenly I'm running for my life. Magic didn't help much either. I had neither the bright flash of fire and ice that my Wizard kindred had nor the unique ball of light that Magicians could throw in front of them. I was, and still am, an Enchanter, a unique class that most people seem to disregard as trivial.

    Tale by Diesnin
