ISSUE # 24June 1st - June 5th

A View to Temptation

The smaller warrior, one of the companions to Serat, hesitated but only a second before bringing it to mention. "See there oh mighty Serat, wouldst that be fear or love?" The men burst to laughing and even the shrouded figure appeared to move.

I must admit I could but barely contain my laughter.

Slowly Serat moved within full sight of Hameil. It was easy to see Serat was wealthy as well as beautiful by her exotic adornments and weaponry. 		"You there, man-child. Do you like what you see before you? Be truthful and be not afraid to speak your mind. After all you are certainly protected from any harm by the spirits of peace" she said calmly. "Settle this argument and perhaps we might reward you" she smiled. Hameil began to move his lips as if to answer but could yet form speech. "You might have a bit of gold if it were my wish, but this weapon is for the warriors hands alone" as she placed the hilt of the black sword on Hameils knee. "Your destiny is only to wonder at my glory as I defeat my enemies and become rich beyond these merchants imaginations!" she laughed as she motioned to the half empty tavern. I could hear the merchants as they began to openly appraise the weaponry of the adventurers. "It is true, we receive many of our more exotic items from those following the path of war" said a voice.

Her companions joined in with her laughter. "Bring to us food befit warriors!" said another loudly. "This is peasant food and would not see us through a days travel." A large mace smashing a fire stone sent the proprietor scampering for his best cuts of meat.
