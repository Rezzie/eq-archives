Who can be a reseller?
In reseller web hosting, SimpleNet provides high-quality web hosting services to resellers that, in turn, act as web hosts. The benefit to you lies in the ability to purchase reliable, wholesale services and sell them for a retail profit. With SimpleNet's expertise on the backend you merely need to sell the product, not maintain the store. Plus, we're available 24x7x365 to support your reseller business.

  • Webmasters and designers. Those with multiple clients can use the SimpleNet Reseller Program as the home for clients' web sites. This maximizes performance and reliability without having to maintain your own set of dedicated servers.
  • The Technically Saavy. If you are the the go-to person for all questions about web hosting and email, you can take advantage of the reseller program. You may help your parents and friends figure out how to make the most of the Internet, why not make money from your recommendations?
  • Existing resellers. Give your customers the reliability they deserve. Existing resellers with clients hosted elsewhere will find unmatched support, flexibility and anonymity with SimpleNet.
  • Organizations, Corporations or Hobbyists. Entities that need to provide some form of hosting or email to their clients and employees, but with special limitations or applications woven into the offering.
