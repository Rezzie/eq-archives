The Plague

There was some laughter and then quiet. I turned my attention to the television.

A few minutes later the pace in the channel had again picked up. The topic was, of all things, religion. Different habits and traits of each persuasion were being discussed. I turned back to the television. 		After an hour, the channel didn't seem to be getting any better. More silly comments, caused by general boredom, plagued the channel.

I left #everquest and entered #eqtavern. EQTavern was besieged with the "away" disease. 		Later that night I was watching television, and monitoring IRC with one eye on #EQTavern. Someone typed.

"Brad is on #everquest."

I joined.
