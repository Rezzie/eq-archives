Letters to the Editors

Great and mighty Absor:

okay, I was going to write this completely in character, but then I decided that that would be going COMPLETELY over the top; so plain, Pacific Northwest English it is:

I've found that the best way to make people "get it" on a server isn't to duel them, isn't to chide them for being out-of-character, etc. All of these responses are completely OUT of character for the role-player. I fnid that responding IN-CHARACTER to out-of-character requests is the best way to gently nudge others to conform ... for instance, when someone /shouts "Can someone give me the /loc to the entrance for Crushbone?," I - as Serona of Felwithe, cleric in the service of Tunare - respond with "I know not of this loc of which you speak - but if you head due north of the city, you will find the fortress of the orcs." I've responded in character. If someone asks "what level are you?" (I'm /anon), I respond in character with "I am of the fourth circle of my order." Foolish of them if they think I'm only fourth level - but if they think IN CHARACTER they'll figure out I'm between 14th and 19th level.

You can never FORCE someone to behave a certain way in a freeform environment; you can only affect your own behavior. But if your behavior and lack of response to THEIR behavior can nudge them - that is the best.

Serona of Felwithe

I myself really enjoy Everquest, but I have a plea. I have long ago shut off OOC, and everything else, till I am down to just shouting and saying. The problem is, as much as I'm interested in the roleplaying of the game, i still am bombarded by loads of shouts and messages that seriously detract from roleplaying integrity. I really love your articles about roleplaying the different races, but I would really love something more general. I think it would be great if you could write an article that encourages Roleplaying...staying in character, keeping the world of Norrath it's own. I think that a push for people to roleplay better would be well worth the trouble to improve an already fantastic gaming environment. Let me know what you think.
