The Politics of War - Role Playing an Ogre

South of the Rathe Mountains set into a foreboding wasteland is Oggok, City of Ogres. Bred for war and ultimately the destruction of all Norrath, Ogres are one of the most fearsome races you can play. While they are notoriously stupid, they are unlike their Troll neighbors in that they have a strong militant culture honed in the very likeness of their creator, Rallos Zek. All other life on Norrath are either cattle or enemies to Ogres, and both are there to be eaten.

    Article by Ian Ash

The Adventures of Glabrin and Breandor - Loss of Control

It was a warm spring day, and I was daydreaming by the main lake in Felwithe. Actually I was supposed to be studying, but instead I was trying to figure out where the large bass that I had almost caught yesterday might be hiding. It was fairly warm, so he might be under the bridge. But on second thought, he could be deep in the middle of the lake. I had just about decided that I could forget about the rest of the days studies, when I heard a voice from over the wall.

Role-Playing in Norrath

I think that EE is definitely on to something, here. I really like when people care about what they're doing, even if it is a glorified Internet game. I myself really enjoy Everquest, but I have a plea. I have long ago shut off OOC, and everything else, till I am down to just shouting and saying. The problem is, as much as I'm interested in the roleplaying of the game, I still am bombarded by loads of shouts and messages that seriously detract from roleplaying integrity...

- Daedhel
