For many years the Aqua goblins of Dagnor's Cauldron have held a virtual monopoly over the amusement park industry with the construction of the "Cauldron Water and Theme Park". However, in the last year, the Aqua Goblins have had their lofty position as kings of the Amusement parks toppled by the ingenious minds and creative works of the Gnolls of Black Burrow. As reported several weeks ago, the Gnolls of Black Burrow opened their Black Burrow Water Park and introduced what is now being called the revolutionary interactive water pool. Black Burrow owners turned a simple, and mostly boring, swimming pool into a nail biting and blood pumping experience by adding live razorgills to their pool. The addition of the razorgills met with unprecedented success, drawing untold numbers of visitors from miles around just to see the wondrous pool.
