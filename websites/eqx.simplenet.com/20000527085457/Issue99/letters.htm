Lil Archy (Issue 97)

While I understand the humor behind the cartoon, I might consider pulling it after what happened in California over Halloween. A 12 year old was beaten nearly to death with a golf club and then stabbed multiple times with the shaft when it broke. He had simply gone to the house trick-or-treating. Some people might really be upset after reading this comic. Trust me, I know that he had no idea something like this would happen when the comic strip was gone. Just wanted to make you aware of the situation.

Sincerely,
Edward Myers

[Ed. Absor - Well, I certainly understand the concern. And we're not at all insensitive to the fate of that child and the coincidence of that particular Lil' Archy. However, I do think that we all need to be a little less worried about coincidental relationships between our entertainment and the horrible things that happen in this world, and a lot more concern in finding out why these things happen in the first place. If I for one instant thought that Lil' Archy was anything but just plain funny, I would pull it. But I just can't see it the way you do...

Beyond anything else, our sympathies are with the family of the victim.]
