ISSUE # 147

 October 28th - November 3rd, 2000


Bard's Log Talk

In a quiet café away from the noise and wanton insanity that permeates the continent of Velious (and anywhere else the “Bard’s Log” group travels), Kyodai, Lanys, Garg, and Sanotaro sit down to a cup of Joe and a good conversation. Let’s see what they’re up to…

Sanotaro: So what’s the deal with Bard’s Log? After months of grueling work, production comes to a halt and I haven’t seen ANYTHING.

Kyodai: Well, there were some technicalities beyond anybody’s control.

Sanotaro: Like?

Kyodai: Well…Like script problems, design delays, mass confusion, college, and eventually an utter shut down of a lot of websites.

Lanys: Not to mention hardware difficulties on the writer’s part.

Garg: Yah, dats why Garg’s log not be arownd.

Sanotaro: Forget Garg’s Log! I’m talking about Bard’s Log here. I want to see results!! After all, I’m a main character now.

Lanys: Sadly…

Sanotaro: Hmph, you’re jealous because Kyodai, Garg, and I were the ORIGINAL Bard’s Log trio.

Lanys: Before they left you drunk in a gutter in West Freeport.

Sanotaro: …That has nothing to do with anything.

Kyodai: Oh who cares? After many trials, technical details have been worked out, hardware problems solved, and the writer knows what he’s doing. We hope…

Garg: An Garg’s Log be winnun awards!!

Kyodai: Yeah! That- huh?? Where?

Garg: Sowwy, me jus wanted ta say dat…

Sanotaro: Three issues and the whole thing gets to his head…

That’s all the time we have today, be on the lookout for more Bard’s Log stuff here or on their upcoming website.
