The Care and Feeding of Your Elemental

- - - -

Good day, magicians of Norrath! My name is Alisai. You may remember me from such classic lectures as “Pole-vaulting for Fun and Profit” and “Vinegar: The Silent Terror”. I’ve come to give you a few pointers on the grooming and care of your best friend: the elemental.

What’s that? Your elemental doesn’t need any care? Oh, but you’re mistaken…elementals may not ask to be cared for, but believe me, if you learn to properly feed, clothe, and clean your elemental, you will unlock it’s true potential.

    Instructions by Alisai

An Old Friend

“Oghma!” Posy almost shouted.

“Posy, my friend, how are you!” Oghma replied, smiling. He waved from the path.

“Oh, Oghma, I’ve been banished from the Wizard’s Guild! It’s horrible!” Posy burst into tears. It was such a relief to see her good friend Oghma’s friendly face! All the past week’s events, and her resolve to not show anyone how hurt she was, tumbled out of her in a rush, and she was unable to restrain herself anymore. She raced down the hill, threw herself into his arms and sobbed.

Editorial - Absor

E-mail ate my editorial!

I spent a lot of quality time at work writing up an editorial for this week. I e-mail it to myself at home, blindly trusting in the Internet to handle my data with respect.

I should know better by now.

And, as much as I like you guys, and as much as I like to see my words in print, there’s no way I’m going back to work just to copy it onto a disk and drive it back here. I’m sorry, that’s just the way it has to be.
