ISSUE # 27June 20th - June 26th

Crossing Paths

The drill is simple but effective: Angaram drives into the group, chopping wildly and maiming every gnoll in his path... Janell skirts the edges and uses her longer stride to chase down and kill those that choose to flee. The gnolls, though far outnumbering the pair, panic and scatter to the winds, forcing the adventurers to chase them through the heavy snow banks. After a few moments of heated melee, the foes are defeated. Angaram loudly hums a bawdy tune as he sorts through the bodies, looking for anything of value while Janell examines the cart they were pulling, having no taste for touching the dead beasts. "Bah, nothing as usual...worthless vermin" mumbles the dwarf as he approaches the carts. "Anything there?"

"Just the usual...meat, hides, some trinkets. Still, it will more than cover our expenses with some beer money for you later, though not enough for women." Janell chuckles.
