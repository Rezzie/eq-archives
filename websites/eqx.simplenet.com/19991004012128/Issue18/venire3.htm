ISSUE # 18Apr. 18th - Apr. 24th

Venire's Promise

Across the room the dummy raised off the floor. His appendages started to sway about, and his legs jumped.

"He’s dancing" I laughed.

Gosling concentrated on his spell. I watched as the dummy moved towards the table in front of us. The dummy, which was a shirt sewn to a pair of pants and filled with orange-yellow straw, picked up Gosling’s quaterstaff and readied it, then started towards me. As he approached he rotated the staff. 		"Hey stop that!" I laughed.

The dummy moved close to me. Then with a quick twist of his body, rotated the staff down and upward, cutting out my legs from beneath me. I landed in a heap on the floor. Gosling laughed as the dummy’s dropped lifeless to the floor. Embarrassed, I picked myself off the floor, and regained my posture.

"You see my son, the element of surprise can save your life." 		I nodded my head in acknowledgment.

"Red said he heard of powerful mages raising the dead. Can I learn this grandfather?"

Red was my best friend. We had met at the Qeynos stables where I have worked since I was fifteen. Gosling’s face hardened, and his thick black eyebrows lowered.

Tale by Razor.
