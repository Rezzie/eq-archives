Interview - Explorers of Norrath   

EQX: When was Explorers of Norrath first created?
Saturnino: My guild was first created at the beginning of November under the name Cryptic Adventurers.... we changed our name around the middle of December officially our first member joined November 13.
EQX: What specific types of characters are you hoping to attract into your guild?
Saturnino: Well we are open to all races and classes (except those intrepid halflings) as long as they role play.
EQX: Are you going to accept only PK or only Non-PK characters?
Saturnino: It is entirely up to our members if the turn the PvP switch on or off we do not however accept any random PKing.
EQX: What guild related activities do your members get up to now (pre-beta)?
Saturnino: Oh occasionally we have official guild meetings but almost everyday any member that are on the net get on our channel and chat... most of our members are very active on all the message boards along with many different EQ IRC channels one of the biggest requirements for getting into our guild is that you have to be very active.
EQX: Do you have any other pre-beta activities planned for your members?
Saturnino: I am currently looking into other online RPGs that we could participate in other than that there isn't much to do now.
EQX: How many members would make your guild the ideal size?
Saturnino: Before beta starts we have a 25 member limit but after the open beta and final get underway we hope to expand to around 35 seeing as to they are good RPers we don't want 200 members just enough to be able to have a couple members online at a given time so members have other to adventure with.
EQX: Finally, tell us a little about what your guild will get up to in the game.
Saturnino: Well....   the main purpose of our guild will to go on huge quests all over Norrath and the many other dimensions beyond that... we will most likely set up a fort or 2 (seeing as to that possible) and also have tavern located in several towns to make money and to hang out other than that we will be doing anything else that is fun (in a non annoying way) hopefully there will be many uncharted waters for us to sail and map and many a quest to buy our time and of course many a brew to quench our thirst.
