Dear EverQuest Express,

I write to you as a concerned roleplayer. I don't want to complain about Verant and the GMs after all they've gone through to make the game playable, but the most recent GM event on Rallos Zek has thrown quite a monkey wrench into the plans of the people down here at the Straylight Saga, the most well-known roleplayer group on the infamous Rallos Zek server. We have been carefully crafting a storyline involving a child said to be the daughter of Innoruuk, and have been preparing for a major event that will attempt to summon Innoruuk into the world of Norrath for an hour or so.

Verant has said it wants to foster roleplaying, yet suddenly here comes a Teir'dal claiming to be the daugher of Innoruuk, giving a quest that can only be fulfilled by powergamers (since it involves killing high-level spawns, and 95% of our members are below level 30). Also, I've spoken with a GM, and the chances of actually getting Innoruuk to appear at the aforementioned ceremony is very slim. Even on other servers, the most prevalent GM events involve them hopping into high-level MOBs and causing havoc. Some roleplaying, eh?

If Verant wants to foster roleplaying so much, then why do they give events that cater to powergamers and ignore these wonderful, legitimate roleplaying storylines?

Leexa "Cliffjumper" Koroshyuki Erudite Cleric, Level 17
Ambiguous Slayers Guild
