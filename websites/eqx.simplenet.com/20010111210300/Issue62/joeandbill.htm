ISSUE # 62

January 20th - February 26th


EQ - a short performance by Joe and Bill

My interpretation of role playing the first few levels of ANY character in EQ:

"Scene: JOE the noble high elf Paladin and his companion BILL the high elf Wizard are standing at the gates of Felwithe. They have long prepared for this moment. They have honed their role playing skills. They have created extensive histories. They are ready."

JOE: Hail fair Bill! I have awaited this time since I was a mere child and we played together in fountains of Felwithe. Finally! To test our skills against the evil denizens of Norrath.

BILL: Aye my friend Joe. I have studied many a long night perfecting my spells. I feel the energy of my magic pulsing in my veins waiting to be released through my hands. Shall we away?

JOE: Aye Bill, let us adventure, and let NONE stand in out path.

"At this point JOE and BILL move a short way away from the gate. Enter a GIANT WASP DRONE."

GIANT WASP DRONE: Bzzzzzzzzzzzzz.

JOE: This insect's noisy ways are bothersome to me Joe, let us end its simple life, and of course give thanks to our god for whatever bounty it may provide.

BILL: Aye, Joe. This will be a poor test of our mighty skills, but a test nonetheless. You engage the creature and I will strike it with my mysterious magic.

"JOE swings at GIANT WASP DRONE. Melee ensues."

GIANT WASP DRONE: BzzzzbzzztBZZZZZZZZZZT!

"JOE is STUNG repeatedly, and is consistently missing with his swings. BILL has casted twice on himself, and twice on JOE, and is very low on mana"

JOE: This is a fearsome creature Bill! Perhaps a demon of the forest disguised as a simple wasp!

BILL: My magic is nearly taxed, Joe. I will...

GIANT WASP DRONE: BZZZZZZZZZZT!

JOE: Aiiiiiiiie! I am slain! Escape while you can precious BILL, let it not be said I died anything but....

"JOE is STUNG to DEATH. BILL runs to guards."

"30 seconds later"

BILL: Joe, how unfortunate. I wept for your death.

JOE: SCREW this dude! I'm grouping with some POWERGAMERS!

JOE (in shout): Anyone wanna group with a lvl 1 pally...I'll make a good tank!

    Tale by Celestak, Wood Elf Druid
