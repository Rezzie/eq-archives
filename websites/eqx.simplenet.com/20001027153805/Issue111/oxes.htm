Dear Oxesiess,

I am, as my race is so crudely referred to, an "Inkie", or a Teir'Dal. This has presented many, many problems for me, a struggling wizard, across the lands of Norrath. It seems wherever I go, people are more concerned with trying to kill me then to understand my dilemma. I worship no gods of an evil nature, as I see it only encourages them. And yet, though I persist in making the local gnoll or orc populations relatively extinct, the citizens of nearly every city I come to are hell-bent on putting my head on a pike. I can tell you, this is a discouragement to a growing wizard, just trying to make a living, like everybody else. Now, barring the act of rooting everyone in a city at once, so they are less inclined to run up and take a slash at me, can you suggest any other methods I may be able to win the trust of my fellow Norrathian, or at least get them to stop attacking me?

Sincerely, Azrien
