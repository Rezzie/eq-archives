Amnesia

'Twas not four score but seven long and tragic years ago
I chanced to bump my head in Freeport; how I do not know.
Was it a roguish fiend who struck me just to gain my purse?
Or was it some adventurer I failed to reimburse?
It could have been the influence of too much gypsy wine,
Or it could be a vengeful old associate of mine.

    Tale by Calthaer
