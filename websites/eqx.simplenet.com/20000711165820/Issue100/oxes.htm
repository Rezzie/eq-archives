Dear Oxesiess,

What is this obsession with races in Everquest? Isn't judging critters by the color of their skin wrong? How can Paladins get away with it or any creatures that deem themselves "good?" Why isn't there an outcry from players about this? And don't *even* get me started about the racial scores! What about the merchents who refuse to sell to players because they're dark elves? If Trolls can be ShadowKnights and cast Necro spells, why couldn't a young Troll choose to be an enchanter--or to be different to be a true Paladin--one who realizes it's whats in a person's heart that's important, not the color of their skin! Peace!
