Greetings. I am Adicus, a grand ol' mage from Freeport, and I fear that I have a problem. It is like this , lass, I'm a Mage , and part of being a Mage is the pride you get from all the skills you have apart from most of the population, such as my favorite, Gate. Aye , but alot of that pride is gone now , for ye see , a problem has developed, it first happened some time ago , and it keped happening. It first happened when i was traveling with a druid friend of mine. Our journey was taking longer than expected , so he offered to use his Gate skill, he started casting , and i started feeling quite ill. I felt ill untill we reached the other side of the portal. Aye , it was very unpleasant from that monent after , and it seems the only thing that can calm down my illness is a good , tall, frosty mug of ale. I thaught this was isolated at first , but it hapened again , and again , and again. Often enough my meals revisit me, making me very unpopular with my friends. The effect is even starting to spread to my gate skill, and yet , the only thing that helps me is ale. I have visited all of the great clerics and shamans of the world , and they do not know what my illness is. I have heard you are a wise and grand lass , who is wise beyond your years, and that you might be able to help me.
Adicus Spellbinder, magi of the 20th circle
