ISSUE # 20May 2nd - May 8th

Venire's Promise - part 2

The shutters flung open from an abrupt gust of wind. Dust from its resting place, filled the room like a viscous mist. To the left and front of Solace, particles began to take form in the shape of a semi transparent swirling sphere. As the ball grew larger it transformed into a ghastly looking aberration. "Your magic is stronger" the Lich hissed. His gray flesh cracked and hanging from his torso, rotten and decayed.

"Yes your Excellency" Solace said, as he bowed before him. 		"Good" "Gooooooood" The Lich muttered as he walked toward Solace, moving his cane and planting it near his foot with each step. "Soon we shall rally our allies and bring death to Norrath." The once powerful mortal mage slammed his fist down hard on the wood table. His undead eyes burned red with abhorrence.
