The Dark Season

I've found the purest form of hatred is hatred of oneself.

I'm known to dawdle in fits of dizzying self-pity. I read back over many of my writings and they seem a testament to an image of myself as a victim. As anyone who knows Tier'dal Society would certainly note, victims and dark elves do not mix.

If you chipped away the veneer of indifference, the disregard for rules of behavior, the outright irreverence, one finds at the core of me a hatred of what I have become and a desire to abandon the criticisms of others.

    Tale by Archanalia
