ISSUE # 79

June 19th - June 25th


Beneath Befallen

Beneath Befallen's putrid mound
Amidst the boulder'd tombs
Where undead creatures slink and moan
A treasure may be found

Yet seek ye not this thing of lore
Until your blood is warmed
For finding it shall test thee true
And lead through thrice locked doors

Just gathering the mystic keys
Shall prove your life is dear
For foul rats and skeletons black
Mere fodder do appear

Past scores of ghouls and wisps descend
Amidst the necromancers whims
Delve deeper if you seek to own
A claymore sword of bladed bone

And Borgon did since just thirteen
A Northern stalwart bred on dreams
Of how with sinew strong and lean
He'd wrest it from the undead fiends

And yes, tis true I ventured too
With friends both bold and true
Fore t'was the getting, not the having
Which made the beasties stew

Shadow Knights did bar the way
At many a crucial turn
And besting them was our first test
Of mettle yet unused

An undead elf did seek our death
Down within the deepest depths
A vile guardian sent to purge
Those who trod the secret steps

So none shall grasp the bon'ed blade
One wall hides well the fabled cave
Yet seek it still with blinded eye
Both hand by hand and foot by foot

But finding it won't end the task
Still greater things this sword does ask
For in it's darkened chamber hiding
Greater fears are still abiding

Past the throne of tortured past
All round about are beings of night
And besting them we near the nest
Where lays the sword of Borgon's quest

But lo, the crypt is even empty
Have others gained the prize afore we
Wait a brightening all engulfing
Brings forth the keepers final army

Strike and parry, cast and wheel
Knight of Darkness aided fully
Undead minions feel our wrath
We shall have your ward at last

And now we stand and cheer aloud
As Borgon grasps the storied sword
Evil beings your fate is sealed
A righteous man this blade does wield

Befallen may be bested true
Yet only venture there forewarned
Take those you trust who trust in you
Or call Befallen home

    Tale by Remido of Rathe
