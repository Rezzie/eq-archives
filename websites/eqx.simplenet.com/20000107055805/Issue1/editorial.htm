Editorial


  Welcome to the first issue of EverQuest Express.  I would like to take a little time to explain the aims and objectives of this newspaper over the coming months and years.
  You might think that we at EQX are jumping the gun somewhat in releasing an on-line paper at this point in time.  Why not wait until beta? you may ask.  Well, the reason we are not waiting until beta is that writing good articles and publishing them in an interesting way is a skill that is acquired over time.  When we hit beta there will be a so many things to do, and so little time to organize them all, so we plan to have an efficient staff used to working on a weekly publication by that time.

  The format of EQX will change once beta starts, the newspaper will then be based within the world of EverQuest.  All news articles will then be written as if the EQX newsroom existed within the world itself (who knows, perhaps we will manage to purchase such a building).

  We have a lot planned for EQX over the coming weeks, a new section is planned for Issue 2, along with other features such as interviews conducted with well known EQ players and staff.  Thank-you for visiting our newspaper.
