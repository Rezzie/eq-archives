As for latency screwing with the system, we have an auto-combat feature that alows users to just select a target and let the server take over. The difference between AC and EQ in this regard, is that we give you the choice as to weather you want to use this auto-combat system or not - it's up to the player if they want to control everything or not. The magic system in Asheron's Call is as much of a joke as its combat system. It is a recipe system, where mages spend a significant amount of time sitting around with lots of spell components mixing and testing, mixing and testing. If the mage gets lucky, she could discover a brand new and powerful spell that no one else knows. Apparently there is a system to the madness, where a particular ingredient is always associated with Fire, for example, which could help in figuring out the spells. It is the developers' hope that mages will keep their findings secret, since they are more powerful if they are the only one with a particular spell.
