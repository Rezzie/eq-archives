Editorial


Welcome to Issue 3. Kit is taking some time off. That leaves me here to mess up all his good work. He may not be available for Issue 4 either. So please direct any comments to me, Absor. I would like to thank all those that have submitted articles for this issue, as well as those that are currently writing for the next issue.

I also figure that the editorial section is the place where the editor gets to spout or rant a bit. And since Kit is not here to stop me...

I just wanted to talk about the hoaxer that hit the Avault EverQuest news group, and maybe a few others as well. Recently someone decided to post a false message from Brad McQuaid to the news group. This person attempted to convince us that EverQuest had been cancelled. He also posted the the Tanarus group with a similar message about the cancellation of Tanarus. Okay, so not too many people believed it (well... one good friend of mine was too ill to recognize the hoax. :) ). So I am not going to say that he was a good hoaxer. But I come to question the reason why someone would do this. Rumor has it that this person was ill treated in an IRC chat. I have also heard that he has been banned from Tanarus. So he chose to strike back at Sony Interactive Studios of America (SISA - what a long name!) by trying to anger and confuse potential SISA customers. All I really want to say is: grow up. Only thoughtful and intelligence discussion can actually influence people. The only thing that the hoaxer has convinced me about is his own immaturity. I guess nobody is surprised that I feel this way. I suspect you all agree with me.

That leads me to the rest of us. Brad has mentioned to us that he likes to read the newsgroup. I appreciate that. He also told us that our endless rants make it hard for him to spend his time reading the news group. So far I think the posters have handled it pretty well. We have been mature and have held down the rantings. I wanted to congratulate and thank you all for that.

Well, please enjoy this issue of EQX!
