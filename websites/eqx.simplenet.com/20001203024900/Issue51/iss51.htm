Mechanical Role-Playing

Now we have charts of statistics, hints of spoilers, lists of lists and all sorts of other mechanical data, some players wonder where we stand on role-playing. I can attest to the fact that role-playing is contagious, and the environment for role-playing is being built. We start to see more of the vast background for Norrath during our daily quests and journeys to new places, and even just the world itself has some classic role-playing value.

A Woman's Point of View

A professor of mine once told me that men have their thoughts concentrated in a narrow cone of view, while woman have their thoughts concentrated within a bubble around their head. Meaning that men concentrate on one thing, very intently, while women have many things circling around. Basically, women multitask better than men.

This could not have applied better to the previous article Women Vs. Internet gaming. Men with their intense concentration on games, (such as quake II) or on sports, as most women know, give bad examples to women for the love of that sport or game. Of course if we are watching you play quake so intensely that you drown out everything around you and become furious when you get killed because your teammate does not cover your ass, we laugh. Quake is not the game to introduce any female to Internet gaming.

    Article by Saskia
