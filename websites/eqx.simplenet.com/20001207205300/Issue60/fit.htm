FREEPORT:

Laevra Starleaf was assassinated in her room at the Freeport Inn last night. Laevra, a noted Magician and leading supporter of the Elven Crusade, a proposed war to destroy the Dark Elves, was in Freeport trying to get support from the local government. Details are sketchy at this time, but eyewitness reports say that a dark figure was seen leaving the back ally of the Inn near the time of the incident.

The Freeport Milita, first on the scene, reported finding nothing but a dagger of Dark Elven make. It was plain except of the addition of two glyphs on the pommel. Experts conclude that the Glyphs are Dark Elven, One For Evil or Harm, and the Other one for Blossom or Flower. With no other leads, the Freeport Militia are offering a reward of 5 Plat. for information leading to the execution of the person or persons responsible.
