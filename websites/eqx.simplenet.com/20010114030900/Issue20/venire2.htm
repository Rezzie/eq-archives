ISSUE # 20May 2nd - May 8th

Venire's Promise - part 2

In the center of the dark shanty stood a robed figure. His outer garment tattered and decomposed. His face barely visible, illuminated by small beams of light infiltrating the room through cracks in the damaged and decaying shutters concealing the windows. Particles of dust danced about in the beams of light. Solace raised his hands and grasped a blue crystal orb with his bony fingers. He whisked away an assortment of scrolls and books of various types that cluttered the desk surrounding the sphere. Holding the sphere with both hands palmed, Solace began to chant in an unfamiliar tongue. "Sol et port vas ragaus" he chanted as his right hand waved over the crystal orb.

"Sol et port…. Sol et port vas ragaus… Sol et port….. " He said with a hauntingly deep voice.
