Crossing Paths

"Nae any women about worth my time anyway.... all too tall, gangly, and nae any meat on their bones" he scoffs.

The pair pull up the cart and begin heading down the track when Angaram pauses, his nose to the wind. "D'ye smell that?" 		As Janell forms a reply, she spots something on the path ahead. Completely out of place in this snowy landscape, in his gawdy clothing and armour, walks a dark elf. He strides about as if he owns the place, moving towards the pair with complete confidence. Before Janell could reach her volatile friend, Angaram draws steel and launches himself at the intruder, shouting obscenities that she'd never heard before. The dark elf pauses in his stride and stands his ground, smiling enigmatically as the armoured dwarf barrels at him like an avalanche of hair and chain mail. Just before contact is made, however, the dwarf collides with an invisible obstacle and rebounds into a snow bank, disappearing from view. The ensuing string of epithets put his prior tirade to shame and Janell blushes as she strides forward.
