Jason Booth: Lead Technical Artist for Asheron's Call 		Response:

Thank you for your clarifications. I agree that most information published about a game before it has even reached beta is a "crap shoot", as I stated in the article. It has been stated in earlier articles about EverQuest that anything and everything about the game as we know it could change before we ever get our hands on it. The same applies to Asheron's Call, I'm sure. The EverQuest vs Asheron's Call article was not meant to be a review, but merely a comparison of what I saw and was told at E3. It doesn't surprise me if I misinterpreted something or the person giving a demo misstated something. But the point was to get across an overall impression of the two games. I stand by my opinion that of the E3 versions of both games, EverQuest has a clear advantage. I will continue to watch both games very carefully to see if this continues, and I'm sure everyone else will as well.
