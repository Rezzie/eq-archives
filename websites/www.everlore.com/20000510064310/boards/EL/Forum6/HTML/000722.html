We have just received word of a developing story, down at the docks in East Freeport. Luckily, our monk in the field has been waiting there for the past three days, in hopes of catching a boat. So now, we’ll turn you over to him. Byessed are you there?

< Poof! The scene in the crystal ball changes to the familiar one of the docks in East Freeport. In the background, heavy black clouds hover ominously over a tumultuous sea, driven by fierce winds. In the foreground, the reporter, who bears a striking resemblance to most of the other monks in Norrath, stands steadily in the face of the storm and smiles for the audience. Meanwhile, a halfling holds on for dear life, clinging precariously to one of the monk’s sturdy legs, in an effort not to be blown away. >

“Hello, this is Byessed Posticuss, and I’m here by the docks in lovely East Freeport. As you can see behind me,” the monk gestures over his shoulder, then continues. “One of our frequent storms has arrived without warning again, and is lashing the city with torrential rains . . .”

“But it’s not raining,” the halfling chimes in, while trying to go through the monk’s coin pouch, and hold on at the same time.

Byessed sighs, he’s clearly disappointed

“Well, it will be raining you little beggar, as soon as the lag passes.”

< A flash of lightning fills the sky, followed closely by a clap of thunder, and then the rain begins to fall >

“There, happy now? Now give me my money back. And no, I don’t have a spare Fiery Avenger for you, even if you could use it.” Frowning, the halfling lets go of the monk’s leg to hand back the pouch, and is promptly blown out of the picture by a strong gust of wind.

AHALFLING SHOUTS: HELLLLLLPPPPP!

BOOGABOOGA SHOUTS: Use /yell you dumba$$ noobie

< There is a muffled impact in the background, sounding exactly like a noobie beggar being blown into a solid wooden structure >

Smiling even brighter now, the monk continues. “Well, as I was saying, another horrible storm has arrived here in Freeport, but that’s not all that will be arriving. Earlier, I received a /Tell from Flatulence the ogre. And before he logged, he told me that there had been a monster sighting in OOT, and that it was purported to be heading this way. Unconfirmed sources say that it may be the dreaded Nerfzilla!”

< For a few moments, the live scene is replaced with a horribly out of focus picture, showing either a large monster or a huge blob of green gelatin. Then the scene returns to Byessed >

“Unfortunately, that is the only available picture we have of this dreaded beast, and we’d like to take a moment to thank our anonymous source, as well as to urge him to get a better video board. If it is true that Nerfzilla is heading this way, we can only wonder what horrors he will bring. And if the sightings are correct, he should be arriving here shortly. So, let’s just see what happens.”

< Byessed then walks calmly out of the picture, as a gnome goes tumbling by, driven by the wind >

AGNOME SHOUTS: HEEELLLLLPPP!

BOOGABOOGA SHOUTS: For the last time you sfdg, use /yell

< A few moments pass, and the storm suddenly disappears, leaving sunshine and calm waters in its wake >

BLACKBART SHOUTS: HAS ANYONE SEEN MY CORPSE!

From off camera, Byessed says, “I’m sure he will be arriving anytime now”

< A RL hour passes >

BLACKBART SHOUTS: HAS ANYONE SEEN MY CORPSE!

BOOGABOOGA SHOUTS: For the last time no, and stop using caps you fsdg!

< A sheepish Byessed walks back into view >

“Well folks, I’m sorry but it would seem that this story just isn’t going to happen today. So . . .”

< Byessed is suddenly interrupted by a huge commotion, as a giant monster erupts from the sea behind him. Standing waist deep in the ocean, the evil looking monstrosity towers over the nearby Seafarer’s Roost. The picture quickly moves to center on the creature >

TAKING A SCREENSHOT

“Heh, I hope this one turns out better than the one we had.” Byessed says from off to one side.

NERFZILLA EMITS A LOW RUMBLE, THEN ROARS MIGHTILY

NEWBIE SHOUTS: Whats a Nerfzilla?

< The horrible beast proceeds to rip the roof from the Seafarer’s Roost, and then peer inside. >

OTHERNEWBIE SHOUTS: I don’t know but he’s red to me

BYESSED SHOUTS: WTF Nerfzilla, I thought you were going to be here an hour ago

< The giant monster drops the roof into the ocean, then looks sheepishly at Byessed. >

“Yah, sorry bout that. I dropped when I was zoning from OOT.”

“Oh, np. Happens to me all the time. Go ahead and Nerf.”

“Ok thanks”

NERFZILLA WAVES AT BYESSED

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: FEAR ME, FOR I AM NERFZILLA AND I HAVE COME TO NERF!

< Nerfzilla peers again into the interior of the Seafarer’s Roost >

NEWBIE SHOUTS: What does nerf mean?

BOOGABOOGA SHOUTS: WTF you stoopid noobie, go play Barbie Dressup or something and stop asking stoopid questions

MILICENT SHOUTS: Oh, I had that game, it was fun.

CHRISTINE SAYS OOC: Me too, he he

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: AARRRGG! SHUTUP! I’M TRYING TO NERF SOMETHING HERE!

CHRISTINE SHOUTS: Well sorry, Mr I woke up on the wrong side of the ocean floor this morning.

NERFZILLA SHRUGS.

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: Groflah, I have come to nerf you, you experience giving quest NPC. You are mine! BWAAHAHAHAHAHAHAHHAAAA!

< A few seconds pass, as the view bobs wildly while Byessed and the cameraman run into the Seafarer’s Roost. The scene then stabilizes centered on Groflah’s usual spot, which is currently empty. >

NERFZILLA SAYS OOC: Hey where’s Groflah?

OTHERNEWBIE SHOUTS: I think he’s in the little dwarf’s room

MILICENT SHOUTS: LOL

NEWBIE SHOUTS: Hehehehehe

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: ARRRGG! That’s it! I’d like you all to welcome the new Nerfed Groflah!

< The monster waggles his fingers, and Groflah magically appears in his usual spot, and hurriedly pulls up his trousers >

“Hey! What happened? I was just in the little dwarfs room an now I’m back out ere?”

“I am Nerfzilla, and I have nerfed you! Ha! I know we said your adjusted quest was fine, but now we have changed our minds”

Nerfzilla sticks out his tongue at Groflah

“What?” asks Groflah in disbelief.

Nerfzilla grins evilly at Groflah

“You shall no longer give any experience for those tonics Groflah, it has been decided. People have been taking the easiest most boring route to . . . blah blah blah . . .”

< A collective NO goes up from the gathered Tumpy Tonic fiends >

“Now who in the world will be bringing me drinks, ye big green lump? How am I supposed to survive with no more of Tumpy’s Tonics?”

Looking concerned, Nerzilla says, “Have you considered rehab?”

“Ack, a life without tonics! What’s a dwarf to do?”

GROFLAH AUCTIONS: Wtb: Shared room in rehab with Sandra Bullock, send tells.

BOOGABOOGA SHOUTS: That was a movie you asfg!

GROFLAH SHOUTS: ACK!

< Byessed steps back into the picture >

“Well there you have it folks, Nerfzilla has struck again. This time claiming poor Groflah as his latest victim. Who will be next? Let’s see if we can get him to comment.”

< Byessed quickly runs out and to the end of the dock, with the cameraman in tow. Where they catch Nerfzilla as he is turning to leave. >

“Excuse me Mr. Zilla, or can I call you Nerf?”

Smiling, Nerfzilla says “Aw shucks, Nerf would be fine.”

“Ok Nerf, can you make any comments on future nerfs, at this time?”

“I’m sorry Byessed, but at this time you will just have to discover future nerfs on your own. That way, I won’t be taking all the adventure out of the game.”

NERFZILLA BEAMS A SMILE AT BYESSED

“There you have it folks, straight from the monster’s mouth. We’ll just have to wait and see what will be nerfed next. Thanks again Nerf, and have a good day.”

BYESSED WAVES AT NERFZILLA

NERFZILLA WAVES AT BYESSED

< The giant beast turns to leave >

Looking surprised, Byessed points at the departing monster and says, “is that a zipper I see on your back, Nerf?”

NERFZILLA BLUSHES

“No it’s just a video glitch caused by your poor graphics card.”

“Well it certainly looks like a zipper. You know what, I don’t think you’re a monster at all. In fact, I think you’re a Verant employee dressed up in a giant rubber monster suit.”

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: ARRG! NO I’M NOT! I’M A MONSTER!

BYESSED SHOUTS: ARE TOO!

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: AM NOT!

BYESSED SHOUTS: HEH! ARE TOO . . .

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: AM NOT!

BYESSED SHOUTS: IT’S BRAD ISN’T IT? YEP, I BET IT’S BRAD IN A MONSTER OUTFIT!

NERFZILLA SHOUTS: ARRG! TAKE THIS!

< A giant green rubber monster hand, suddenly fills the view, accompanied by screams and a tremendous crash. Then the screen goes blank. >

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT . . .

< Suddenly, the view returns to the newsroom and a dapper looking newsman. >

“Well, we seem to be experiencing technical difficulties out in the field. But to recap, Nerfzilla has struck again. Stay tuned for future broadcasts where we will be covering his upcoming nerfs. Until then, this is Norrathian News Nine wishing you a good evening.”

We interrupt our regularly scheduled broadcast of "The revenge of the 50ft tall halfling woman," to bring you an important news bulletin.

< The scene changes to the Norrathian News Nine news room, and the usual dapper looking anchorman >

"Hello, this is Dan Idrather, and I'm here to bring you a special update. It was only just days ago that we witnessed the apparent demise of one of our ace reporters, Byessed Posticus."

< A video clip is run, showing the last seconds of film where a giant green monster hand can be seen descending toward the camera, followed by darkness and then the message "Loading, please wait . . ." >

"However, we've just learned that Byessed is indeed alive, and still on location in East Freeport. Apparently though, we only have an audio feed, and no video. So, let's see if we can get an update from him."

< The view zooms out a little, and a still photo of Byessed is placed on the screen next to Mr. Idrather >

"Byessed, can you hear me?"

"Yes Dan, I can."

"Can you tell us what happened, we thought for sure you were dead."

"Well Dan, I did as well. But luckily, I managed to dodge at the last second and then feign death."

"So Byessed, you've been feigning death for the last three days?"

"Yes, I have Dan. With the past nerfs to FD, I thought I'd give Nerfzilla enough time to get far far away, so he wouldn't aggro on me again. Unfortunately, Bob wasn't so lucky"

"I'm sorry Byessed, who is Bob?"

"Oh, Bob is my camera-troll. He kind of resembles a large troll pancake at this time, and I'm trying to get him res'd."

"Well Byessed, we're sorry to hear about Bob. But, I'm sure I speak for all our viewers, when I say that we're glad to hear that you're ok."

"Thanks Dan, I appreciate the sentiment. And I look forward to seeing you all when I return to the news room, in a couple of days."

"Take care Byessed, and we'll be looking forward to seeing you as well."

< The picture of Byessed disappears from the screen, and the shot centers again on the anchorman >

"There you have it folks, Byessed is alive and well. And we hope to be able to bring you further details when he returns from the field. Until then, this is Dan Idrather at Norrathian News Nine wishing you a good evening."

< The scene fades, and is replaced by the ending credits of "The revenge of the 50ft tall halfling woman." While simultaneously across Norrath, several rabid fans of the cult hit begin to bludgeon their televisions in anger, over having missed the best part of the movie. >

< Off camera, the story continues with Byessed at the Freeport docks >

BYESSED AUCTIONS: WTB Exp Res down by the docks.
