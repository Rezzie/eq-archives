Trade Skills
Brewing
This skill is simular to the Baking/Pottery line of trades. It is rumored that drinks can increase your regen stats, but not confirmed. This skill is basically for fun and roleplaying. 		- - -
Baking
Not as profitable as it is fun. This is more of a entertaining skill rather then money making. One good thing about this Baking is that you never need to buy food again. 		- - -
Alchemy
This is a very interesting Shaman only skill. All kinds of neat potions can be made like Invisibility, Extra Ac, Water Breathing and much more. Whether this skill is profitable is not known. 		- - -
Tinkering
This skill is only for Gnomes who are the masters of Tinkering. They can build interesting devices like Compass's, Telecopes and more. Very little is know about this skill and whether it can be profitable. 		- - -
Apply Poison
A rogue skill of applying poision to weapons. This might not be considered a trade skill. 		- - -
Jewel Crafting
This is one of the most profitable trade skills in the game. It is better to be an Enchanter doing this trade since making Magic Jewery requires enchanted metals. This skill is soo great becuase even high level players will dish out tons of money to buy your fine items. For example, Platinum Ruby Veil can go for over 1,000Platinum. It is a costly trade to learn though. 		- - -
General Skills
This is the General Board where can come to chat about anything relating to Making Money Via Trade Skills/Future of Trade Skills...Etc 		- - -
Fletching
This skill can be very profitable. It is the art of making Bows of various types. One drawback is that is can be very hard to train in this skill. 		- - -
Tailoring
Tailing can make decent money. Not as much as blacksmithing though. Master Tailers can make 10 Slot back packs, renforced Leather armor which is a plus for Monks, and other fine Armor Pieces. 		- - -
Smithing
Smithing is the art of making Items and Armor. This field of one of the second most profitable trade skills. A Master Smith can easly make lots of money on a daily basis selling various armor componets. 		- - -
Pottery
This is much like Brewing/Cooking. Although it can make some money on the side. It is said that a recent patch made this skill comepletly useless. Making Pie Tins and other items can sell to other Bakers around Norrath. 		- - -

Monk
Monks are the most powerful fighting class in the game. They are limited to Hand to hand combat which makes it so they cannot use many great weapons. They are very limited on armor (Leather) which makes the monk take more damage when hit. They can counter this by having the ability to avoid many hits during combat.
