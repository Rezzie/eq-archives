A dread bone is similar to a dread corpse except it doesn't have the dread touch a dread corpse has. It is however just as deadly, if not more so. They are the same oozy, sickly, gooey red that makes you think of a freshly removed skeleton. They are good experience, but most likely they will end up being an annoyance and obstacle in the face of a larger task.
