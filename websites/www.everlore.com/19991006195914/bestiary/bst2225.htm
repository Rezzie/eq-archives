Guards are one of the mainstay NPC's of Norrath. They aren't technically monsters, but they CAN be killed and they are dangerous at times. Their levels vary widely; some guards are level 14 (at the least) while others are level 30. They attack all evil races on sight, unless a member of one of these races manages to raise his factions with the guards, usually by killing certain monsters such as Split Paw and Black Burrow gnolls. They have guard shields for loot I believe, although I cannot confirm any loot at this time.
