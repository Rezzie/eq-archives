New Site and Guild for Rodcet Nife
Lozur [5/1/99 5:17:50 PM PST]
Greetings My dark elven brother , Brorna and I , Lozur ,would like to announce that we have began our own website at http://www.rodcetnife.com . This is in its VERY early stages as you can tell if you visit it but we are working on getting it up and with more material quickly . We want it to be a general website for all Rodcet Nife role-players and it will soon also have a private section of it for a guild we are forming name House Mallar Phalar which means "honored grave" in dark elf speech . We welcome anyone to visit it and send emails for general suggestions and for guild applications as we are looking to find 8 more dedicated players to form the leaders in our family . Again please remember the site is brand new and should expand dramatically in the next few days . Lozur (Necromancer)
