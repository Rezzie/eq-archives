These are found in great numbers in the Desert of Ro. The give you disease, but they look pretty damn cool. They offer a variety of weapons and rawhide armor, so they are good experience if you are a healer type or teamed up with a healer type. If you are not a healer or teamed with one, don't bother, since the rabies give you way too much downtime between battles. Plus you do not heal with rabies so you could be overwhelmed by an easy enemy after fighting a mummy.
