The LA EQ-Event   #1
Saturday - October 2cnd 1999

The first ever Everquest player gathering was a complete success! 

The Everlore team (Stryder, Eldwin and Xanthus)  set out at about 11 from Orange County and headed for LA.  Xanthus and Eldwin had already begun selling shirts when I happened to arrive, I managed to get quite lost and cruise around LA for about 45 minutes. 

We all had a great time meeting everyone, the food was good and the caffeine never ceased to flow.  The Everlore shirts were quite successful as well as the Event shirts.  If you were unable to make it to the event, your definitely going to want to make it to the next one!  

Thanks go out to Kevin Scott for putting the event together, the whole Verant team, they are all great people, and full of information, Gregg who was master of ceremonies, and everyone who attended and contributed to the event, I and the rest of the Everlore team had a great time and we are looking forward to the next gathering.

Show me the pictures!

-Stryder
