P E R S O N A

In the world of EverQuest you assume the Persona of mythical figure traveling across a magical world in search of fame and riches. Which Race will you choose? Will you be a noble enigmatic High Elf or a despicable Troll? Which Class will you choose? A heroic Paladin or a villainous Necromancer? What Skills will you practice? Will you spend your time honing your skill at the fine art of Bashing or make a reputation as the finest Brewer Norrath has ever seen? The decisions you make when you create your Persona have lasting and drastic effects on how you will perceive the world of Norrath and how its inhabitants will perceive you. The skills you choose to master will make you either a fearsome foe or a valuable ally. What Equipment should you carry? How you equip yourself could mean the difference between a legendary journey of life and a short brutal trip into the arms of death.
