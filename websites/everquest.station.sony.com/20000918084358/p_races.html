R A C E S

When you play EverQuest you'll first create a character. This creation of yours can be any one of 12 races and 14 classes (keep in mind that not all races can be all classes), male or female, and you even have the opportunity to adjust your attributes (for instance, how strong you are, or how intelligent you aren't). You'll then immerse this character of yours into our virtual world and make of him or her whatever you wish.

Select a race to view from the index to the left to see descriptions of each of the races you can play. Click on their pictures to see them animate.
