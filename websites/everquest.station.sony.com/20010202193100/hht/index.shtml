Donal's Breastplate -- some clarifications

Absor on the Boards

Monk Talk
Monks are one of those interesting types of warrior that, as a class, we have tried to balance a rather large volume of damage dealing with a lesser type of armor. It's not an easy task to build a fighting class that is 'balanced' with a warrior but doesn't wear armor…

Warrior Talk
So this is the forum to talk about what is right and what is wrong with warriors. Go ahead and tell me about bugs you've seen too. I'm not focusing on them, I'm concentrating on class balance here. But I will also take note of non-balance concerns as well.

Bard Talk
So let's have it. Tell me what needs balancing for bards. You may as well give me bugs and broken stuff too. But my focus is going to be on things that are needed to move bards closer to 'balanced' in the game. This may include a nerf or two, I want to warn you of that. I am just as willing to suggest a negative change that helps to achieve balance as a positive one.

Absor, say it ant so, plz
Again, the usual reminder. I am primarily looking for balance concerns here. But I am certainly willing to put bugs on the list. Also, though I really do understand the fact that you want to emphasize and back up statements by others, please, please try to keep the repeat information to a minimum. I'm finding that by page three or four on these threads I'm rereading the same suggestions by people that start their posts with "I didn't read this thread, so I don't know if this had been said…" I don't want to stifle any creativity. But I have to admit that I tend to get bleary eyed reading the same suggestions over and over again, and I know I'm missing good new ideas late in threads when they are buried among things that others have covered several times before.

Gnomish Games - The Tribunal
On Sunday, February 4th at 4pm Eastern (1pm Pacific), the Steamfont rodents will be released to cause havoc and mayhem. Tuatha de Danann of The Tribunal server is sponsoring a few Gnomish activities that should prove to be fun for all of us, and annoying for everyone else! I mean, who isn't annoyed by a gnome?

First event: classic gnomish gnaked gnewbie run starting at Ak'anon. Alcohol provided and mandatory (of course), prizes awarded.

Second event: the Gnomish Inquizition, hosted by our own Kainazzo. Match wits and beat our quizmaster.. while drunk of course.. to win more prizes.

Third event: Drunken newbie "test-of-tactics" type brawling in the lawn in front of Ak'anon. Need we say more?

Registration:

1. Create a level 1 gnome of whichever class you choose - remember the third event, so if your friends sign up with you - coordinate class choices.

2. IMPORTANT: Email me at gnomerun@usa.net with the following information: The name and class of the gnome you created, as well as the name, level, and class of your "main" character that will receive any prizes that you might win. As an option, send me a tell in-game to register.

3. Be online with your gnome out in front of Ak'Anon by 3:30pm Eastern/ 12:30pm Pacific for briefing, debriefing, debuffing, and distribution of alcoholic substances. Anyone showing up who did not register via email or contact me in game will not be allowed to participate.

Prizes are geared for low to mid levels. We have a "cash" pot that will be divvied up, and a whole lot(!) of items valued upwards of 1000pp. If you have more than one character, register your lowbie as the char being represented by your gnome. For added fun, we will not be announcing who is who until the end.

Good luck!
