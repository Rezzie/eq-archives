Billing FAQ

 

1.0 Billing Questions

1.1 How much does it cost to play Everquest?

EverQuest has three pricing plan options:

$9.89 per Month

$25.89 for 3 Months

$49.89 for 6 Months

All pricing plans are recurring, meaning you will continue to be billed at the appropriate intervals unless you cancel your subscription. Note that everyone receives a Free Month of game play the first time they subscribe. If you cancel your subscription during the Free Month, no charges will be made to your credit card.

New York State residents will be charged sales tax.

All charges on your credit card will appear under "The Station".

The subscription charges are in addition to the cost of the game. You will need to purchase a copy of the game from a retail store. The price of the game will vary depending on the store.

 

1.2 Forms of Payment

Currently, to play EverQuest you will need to pay with a credit card or compatible debit card. The Station will only be accepting Visa, MasterCard, or American Express. No other forms of payment will be accepted.

 

1.3 How do I cancel my EverQuest Subscription?

You can do this by going into the EverQuest game interface (client) and clicking "Account" and then "Cancel Account". All subscription cancellation requests become effective at the end of your current subscription cycle (e.g. if you've chosen a 3 month subscription, it will be effective at the end of your 3 month subscription). You will still have access to EverQuest until your current subscription expires. Our policy is NOT to give refunds for subscriptions you have already purchased.

If you cannot do this (cancel your EverQuest subscription) online or are having problems, you can contact The Station Customer Service team at accounts@station.sony.com or call 1-888-STATION.

 

1.4 How do I switch to a different pricing plan?

You will need to cancel your current subscription and wait for it to expire. (See the FAQ above on how to cancel your subscription). Note you will still have access to EverQuest during this time. Once your current subscription is expired, you will be able to re-subscribe to any pricing plan you wish. NOTE: YOUR GAME CHARACTERS WILL NOT BE AFFECTED IN ANYWAY IF YOU SWITCH TO ANOTHER PRICING PLAN.

 

1.5 Will I lose my game characters if I cancel and then re-subscribe to Everquest?

No. If you cancel and then re-subscribe to EverQuest within 6 months, you will still have access to your game characters. You will need to use the same Station Name that you used though when you initially subscribed to Everquest.
