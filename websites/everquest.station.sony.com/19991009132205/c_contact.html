Technical Support
We're here to help you. For technical issues dealing with EverQuest, please use one of the easy-to-use technical support forms:

Problems with video – EverQuest Video Technical Support
Problems with sound – EverQuest Sound Technical Support
Other technical problems – EverQuest Technical Support

Additional technical phone support is also available Monday – Friday from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM PT (11:30 AM to 8:30 PM ET), except holidays. Phone 1-858-554-0144. For better service, please be online and at your computer when calling.

Billing Inquiries/Account Issues
Your problem is our problem, too. For billing issues or problems with your Station account, you can e-mail The Station using an easy-to-use form from Account Services. You can also contact The Station customer service department by phone at the numbers below. Customer service reps are available weekdays from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM PT (11:30 AM to 8:30 PM ET) and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM PT (12:00 AM to 5:00 PM ET), except holidays. Toll-free in the US and Canada: 1-888-STATION Outside the US and Canada: 1-801-579-2300.
