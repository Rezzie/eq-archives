December 30, 1998 - Wednesday
 
EQSS Troll Guide  10:30 PM
The EverQuest Strategies and Statistics page continues their line of racially-oriented guidebooks with "Thugthug's Guide ta Barshin" covering the strategies for starting and building up a Troll in EverQuest.

 EQ Vault Exclusive Interview  10:00 PM
The EQ Vault has scooped the other sites by scheduling an exclusive interview with Geoffrey Zatkin (EQ's Master of Magic) and they are letting you pick the questions. Submit your questions here. In a week they will be compiled and the interview will take place. While you are at EQ Vault, check out Saturnino Idalen's editorial entitled "Building a Community."

 Screenshots, Screenshots, Screenshots  9:30 PM
Fourteen new screenshots have been posted for your enjoyment. Taken with a Diamond Monster 3D, they cover a variety of subjects including some "live" shots of players.

Everworld.net has posted 20 spectacular shots entitled Visions of Norrath featuring magic, scenery, and some very photogenic monsters.

Jason Montes of PCFan updated the EverQuest Photo Album on December 23rd. While this news is old, the screenshots are still hot.

 EverQuest News Network9:00 PM
Come check out the EverQuest News Network. This relatively new site has been working hard to catch up with the "better established" EverQuest sites. In the nine days they have been up and running, they have added Guild Listings, a Player's Guide, and editorials covering EverQuest and computer gaming. There is a Java-Enhanced and a non-Java Lite version of the page. Keep up the great work guys!
