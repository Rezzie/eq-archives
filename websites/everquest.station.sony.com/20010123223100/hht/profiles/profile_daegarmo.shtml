Name: Lady Daegarmo

Race: High Elf

Class: Paladin

I was born in the first High Elven City of Takish Hiz, a city destroyed long before those who call themselves elders were born. I remember the migration to Greater Faydark, and the creation of Felwithe. I too served my time guarding the city from Orcs, and those who would cause our loved ones harm.

Over the span of hundreds of years, I became the eldest of the Elven Paladins, and earned the right to wield "Faithbringer", a sword created by the first guildmaster of Takish Hiz. I refused the position of guild master of the Paladins, as well as an offer to join my people in the Plane of Growth, the home of Tunare herself.

I faithfully continue my duties as any noble paladin would, to be the mediator of disputes, the investigator of those accused, and the judge of the largest tournaments to ever see Norrathian soil. Often I will volunteer myself to fill in for a sick guard outside the city of Felwithe, to show good example to those who follow my path.

If you see me there, do not hesitate to come introduce yourself. I have tales from many moons past that even the most avid adventurer has yet to hear.

May faith in whatever god you so chose guide your path!

Lady Daegarmo "The Faithful"
