8/2/99

All right, let's talk about Gen Con! Verant will be showing off both the Ruins of Kunark™ (the EverQuest expansion) and our massively multiplayer real-time strategy game Sovereign™. We highly encourage anyone who can make it to come by and check this new stuff out. Here are the details:

GEN CON is at the Midwest Express Center in Milwaukee August 5th - 8th.

Verant is working together with Sony Online Entertainment and we will be at booth #847.

The following products will be shown, EverQuest, EverQuest: The Ruins of Kunark, Sovereign, The Station web site, Chron-X, and Fantasy War.

I also encourage you to stop by Keith Parkinson's booth (#422) and check out what he's working on. Keith was the artist for the EverQuest box cover and posters. He is also Artist Guest of Honor this year at Gen Con.

Lastly, you are all invited to attend the EverQuest Player's Party: Please join us Thursday from 7-11pm at Buck Bradley's Saloon located at 1019 N. Old World Third St. (3 blocks from the Midwest Express Center). Verant, SOE, and 989 Studios will host food and beverage to the public to celebrate EverQuest.

Hope to see you there,

–Brad McQuaid
Producer, EverQuest
Vice President, Verant Interactive
