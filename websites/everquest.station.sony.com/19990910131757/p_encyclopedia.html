E N C Y C L O P E D I A

 
Welcome to the Encyclopedia Antonica! Here you will find information that will greatly help you on your journeys and adventures through the land of EverQuest. If questions like "Where did the Dark Elves come from?" haunt you, our History section is just for you. Norrath is a big place but the Atlas will help you plot your trek from Odus to Ak'Anon. The gods of Norrath are described in our Deities section; of particular interest to those who wish to be a Cleric, Paladin, or Shadowknight. In the Beastiary you will find pictures and descriptions of some of the hundreds of different creatures you will encounter in your travels.
