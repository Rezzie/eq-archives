P R O F I L E S

    

Name:Delanus The Doubtful

Race: Human

Class: Paladin

Pride and foolishness cost me my sword. Once a great warrior, I traveled the lands slaying the weak. I strove to make Rallos Zek proud. Yet, I doubted. I yearned for a visit from my God, yet he never found me worthy enough. Thus, I began to loose faith. Not only in Rallos Zek, I lost faith in all the gods. I began to believe they did not exist, and I spread this belief to all that would listen. For my foolishness, I gained the wrath of the Gods, and they punished me.

They enacted their vengeance upon my mortal soul by making me a slave of Rodcet Nife, the Prime Healer. Under His command, I am forever doomed to heal the wounds of the land. Denied the use of a sword, I am cursed!
