February 23, 1999 - Tuesday
 

Pricing News 4:15 PM
We are pleased to announce the pricing for EverQuest. At launch, we will have three pricing plans,

  • 1 Month - $9.89
  • 3 Months - $25.89
  • 6 Months - $49.89

This is in addition to the free month you will get upon signing up. Please note that these charges will be in addition to purchasing the game at retail, where prices will vary depending on the store.

Alterd Logix ORPG Finder 6:40 PM
Alterd Logix is proud to present The Online RPG Finder. The ORPG Finder is a resource for a wide range of online role-playing games, dedicated to being the #1 source for locating fellow players in your city, state, or from all over the world, to play online games with. Information which can be obtained at the ORPG finder include player names, character names, email addresses, ICQ UIN's, favored servers, and more, for a wide range of online roleplaying games. Whether you explore the lands of Norrath, brave the dungeons of Brittainnia, or make your home in any of a multitude of other worlds, go and submit your information at the ORPG Finder. And when you desperately need that brutish warrior to aid you on a perilous quest, or a much-needed cleric to heal your party as you traverse the plainscapes before you, head on over to the ORPG Finder to locate and contact a fellow player quickly and easily!!!

New Banshee Drivers 5:30 PM
For those of you testers with Voodoo Banshee based cards, specially modified drivers for EverQuest are now available. There is a Windows 95 and a Windows 98 version. Be sure to download the correct one. Please note these drivers are specifically modified for EverQuest, 3Dfx does not support their use. Also, only the TEST server has Glide enabled for Banshee cards at this time. Patches for the other servers should appear within a day or two.

Special Poem 4:00 PM
Here's a little something from an anxioulsy awaiting fan, Lee Sale. Thank you, Lee!

TWAS THE NIGHT BEFORE THE RELEASE OF EQ

Twas the night before the release of EQ,

and it was silent through the house,

all but the clicking,

the clicking of the mouse.

Over and over I read reviews of everquest,

screenshots and interviews,

as a say farewell to my CD of beta test.

I ran upstairs and climbed into bed,

as 3DFX cards danced in my head.

I entered a medieval world filled with beauty,

and me walking around making fun of a newbie.

I still remember that ultima online,

a pking world and dying all the time.

I woke up on that special day,

March 16th the releasing day.

I rushed to the computer store dancing like a little schoolgirl,

as I picked up the eq box and said "I think I'm gonna hurl!"

This day I have been waiting for,

the day of eq,

I have been waiting for weeks and weeks,

and now its finally here.

I drove home going 300miles an hour down the terrain,

I signal of speed rushed through my brain.

I jumped out of my car and ran for the computer,

and opened the CD drive frantically without any control,

I through the UO CD like a Frisbee,

and dropped the EQ CD in as a moment of pleasure ran right through me.

It began to install and my dreams were true,

"I can see it now and its pretty cool!"

Waiting patiently it slowly installed,

showing pictures of EQ Land,

as I waited by the computer crossing my legs,

"I really gotta go to the washroom bad!"

I ran back downstairs as it completed at the 100% mark,

and I turned off the lights so it would be completely dark.

I watched the intro and just about ready to play,

and to let nothing disturb me this day.

I saw a GM appear on my screen,

" HAPPY GAMING TO ALL, AND TO ALL A GOOD NIGHT"

("THATS IF YOU GO TO BED")

-story by Lee Sale

February 14, 1999 - Sunday
 
Forever Quest Project  9:00 PM
Those of you who enjoy RolePlaying with a purpose should check out the Forever Quest Project. Here is a summary of their goals:

"Forever Quest will be gathering a sizable group of roleplayers on each game server. These groups will work together to provide a positive roleplaying experience through frequent quests. Each group will be sub-divided into different factions who will be working for different in character goals. Freelance players who are outside these factions will also be accepted. The first of these quest will center around the Heartstone."
