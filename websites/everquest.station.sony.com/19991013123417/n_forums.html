F O R U M S / N E W S G R O U P S

 

The world of EverQuest is always evolving, due in part to discussion groups and forums devoted to EverQuest. If you would like to get involved with an active EverQuest discussion group, open up your news reader and head on over to the EverQuest forum at news://news.avault.com/forum.everquest hosted by Avault. You can also participate in live EverQuest chat on EFNet, channel #everquest. We're also on usenet under alt.games.everquest.
