January 16, 1999 - Saturday
 
Confirmation and Clarification  5:30 PM
Confirmations were sent out to Phase 4 testers this weekend. People who recieve the confirmation must order their Phase 4 CD thru http://www.station.sony.com/everquest/beta by January 22nd. You must use the Authorization Code given at the bottom of the confimation email, to validate your order. The following clarification has also been sent:

"1. International Customers - We value all of our customers, regardless of their location. We realize that by restricting Phase 4 of the EverQuest Beta test to US and Canadian residents, people outside this region are frustrated. Unfortunately, due to timing and cost limitations we simply were unable to accommodate you. We do apologize for this, and wanted to assure everyone that EverQuest WILL indeed be available internationally.

2. Timing Issues - In order to ensure that all Phase 4 CDs are sent out in sufficient time, it is necessary that you sign up by January 22nd. After that date, we will no longer be able to accept orders for the Phase 4 CD and the page will be removed. We will begin to process orders immediately and shipping of CDs should commence on January 27th. The test itself will begin February 10th and will continue until shortly before commercial release of the game on March 16th."

Anyone who receives extra authorization codes (multiple registration entries) may feel free to give them away to those without. Keep in mind, Authorization Codes will only be good for one CD order.

Irreverant Preview  11:00 PM
The RPG E-zine, Altered Worlds, has posted a rather irreverant preview of EverQuest written by Sacrates. Who was it that said there is no such thing as bad press? Actually, despite the warning that reads like a pack of cigarettes, Sacrates really seems to like the game.

"If your a Crack dealer read this article, you've got a new Drug to deal out. If you read magazines like Cat Fancy often then you don't want to read this article. If you play computer games often then it's time to sit up in your chair, perk up your ears and read on. If you have any form of Heart disease, high blood pressure, or often have spastic convulsions when you get too excited you may not want to read this either."
