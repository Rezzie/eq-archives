Froglok  

These swamp loving creatures would like no more than to be left alone but the Trolls of Grobb will have no such thing. Deep within the ancient Trollish city of Guk, which the Frogloks reclaimed from the Innothule, they fight a never ending battle against legions of Trolls and other humaniods of ill morals who, in their quests for lost riches, seem intent on exterminating every living Froglok. The Frogloks are also cursed by the fact that it is only within the swampland of the Innothule where they can deposit and hatch their young. Few Froglok Tadpoles can avoid the ever-stomping Trollish boots long enough to reach adulthood.
