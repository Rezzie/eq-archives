The known world of EverQuest is referred to by scholars as Norrath, although very few are aware of its entirety. Four continents fill the hemisphere, and whether other lands lay beyond the oceans to the east and west, no one knows, or at least admits to such knowledge. Rumors of different planes and dimensions even surface occasionally, but most believe these to be mere ramblings of madmen.
