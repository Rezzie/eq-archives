NEWS FLASH: We've put up a sneak peek at the in-game prizes that will be awarded to the winners of the contests at the Baltimore Fan Faire! Winners of the Real Life Quest and the Trivia Quest will be awarded either the Staff of the Gathering or the Sword of the Gathering! The stats on these items will be revealed at the Fan Faire. But you can follow this link to see the preliminary sketches!

The Baltimore Fan Faire is sold out! If your name is not on the Attendees page, you are not registered! There will be no walk in registrations at this event so you must be successfully registered to attend!

We are planning future events, so if you missed your chance this time, watch for announcements of the next Fan Faire!

The EQ Fan Faire is heading East!! Join us in Baltimore for great fun, awesome activities, prizes and the chance to meet the people that work behind the scenes of EQ! We will be hosting panel discussions with the development team, artists and other well known personalities from the world of Norrath! In addition, you will get a limited edition Fan Faire T-shirt and a chance to win some truly phenomenal and unique in game prizes! And most importantly, meet your fellow Norrathians! This is your chance to actually meet the people in the community you have formed in EQ!

Cost of attendance will be $85. As we expect this event to sell out, there may be no room for walk in registrations. Please register early to make sure you don't miss out.

You must book your own travel and hotel arrangements. We have made arrangements with BWI Marriott for a special rate.
