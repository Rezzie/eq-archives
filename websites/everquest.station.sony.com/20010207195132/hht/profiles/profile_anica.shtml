Name: Anica

Race: Wood Elf

Class: Warrior

Anjil and Shica were crusaders. They married young and traveled far from their native home in Kelethin. In a recent battle Anjil had slain a fellow elf and they were no longer welcome in there home. Shica was with child and they feared for the safety of the unborn child. Anica was born during their journey and they were forced to find shelter. They settled in Halas where the Barbarians of the north took them in.

Not long after Anica was born, the elves found the hideout and killed both of her parents leaving her at the mercy of the generous warriors who took her in. They protected her and began to teach her to be a powerful fighter at a very young age. She fought along side them until she was a young adult. With the beauty and grace of the woodland elves and the strength and endurance of the barbarians, Anica set out into the world intent on avenging the death of the family she never got to know.
