Stranger in Qeynos

Loyal and Goodly Citizens of Qeynos,

To our dismay Mordavin Telase, the Arch Priest of Bertoxxulous who has plagued our city with the veiled evil these past weeks, has escaped the custody of our guards and city's mandate. We have called out a full force in effort to apprehend this criminal guilty of the most vile and evil acts to scorch this city since the erecting of its first building. However, all citizens have been called forth to give assistance within this, our darkest hour.

Despite our misfortune at having our suspect escape our grasps and justice, we have been given a rather important piece of information that may lead to our apprehending this criminal. Rykonis Sw`Vaye has given to us a description of the locations where he had met with Mordavin over the past year. To assure ourselves of the validity of this man's claims, we have employed the use of the Rangers within Surefall Glade. The rangers of the glade were able to verify the bandit's claim. On orders of Antonious Bayle, I will reveal the locations that Mordvain is sure to be found depending upon the month in which we search.

For Qeynos, for Antonica, for Bayle,

~Vegalys Keldrane
Magistrate of his Majesty,
Antonious Bayle

During the months that end our year, center our autumn, and peek of our spring, Mordavin was found to have ventured into the Eastern Plains of our Kingdom. Look for him there during the months of December, October and April.

For the fifth month of our year, the month that scorches our summer, and the month that centers our winter, Mordavin was found to have ventured into the Southern Plains of our Kingdom. Look for him in this location in May, August and February.

For the seventh month of our year, the month that marks the beginning of our spring, and the month that rings autumn into the lands, Mordavin was found to have inhabited the Mountainous Pass that lies within our Kingdom's control. He will reside in this locale during the months of July, March and September.

For the months that begin our year, light the first fires of our summer, and rains down the first chills of our winter, Mordavin was found to have ventured into the Western Plains of our Kingdom. Mordavin calls this place home during the months of January, June and November.
