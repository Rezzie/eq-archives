The East Coast Fan Faire will be in Baltimore, Md. on February 2-3, 2001! Join the people from Verant that have made the game what it is today! Not only will you meet the artists, producers, GMs and other Verant representatives, you will meet and get to know the most important people in the EverQuest family… THE PLAYERS!

Meet and get to know some of the people you have known as hunting partners (or maybe sparring partners)! Or use this opportunity to meet others from your server that you might want to group with in Norrath. Or… make a new real life friend that can share the adventure of Norrath!
