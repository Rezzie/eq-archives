November 20, 1998 - Friday
 
Phase 4 Confirmations  2:40 PM
An initial batch of Phase 4 confirmations were sent out last week on November 13th, which basically confirmed entry into the beta testing (the subject read "EverQuest Phase 4 Beta Announcement"). A number of you received this particular email, which prompted you to await further details on Phase 4 participation. Shortly after sending out this initial round of emails, we realized that a lot of people were NOT confirmed because they had outdated information, so we gave these people the opportunity to resubmit a brand new beta application. This application was available for only 24 hours on Thursday, November 19 at 10 am Pacific and lasting exactly 24 hours into the following day, at which time it was taken down. As of this time, we are processing all the beta applications and will send out another round of initial Phase 4 confirmations (the same ones we sent out on November 13th) to accomodate all the new beta applications. We will then follow-up shortly with the promised email that details the next step in Phase 4 participation.

 24 Hour Beta Application  10:00 AM
The beta application for Phase 4 testing is now available. Please fill this out in its entirety if you have not received a confirmation for Phase 4 beta testing as of this time. This form will only be available for the next 24 hours, and will be taken down on Friday, November 20th at 10 am Pacific time. This is your only opportunity to ensure a place in Phase 4 testing of EverQuest. No other exceptions will be made, so don't delay in filling out the form. Check to make sure all information is entered correctly. Future corrections will not be accepted.
