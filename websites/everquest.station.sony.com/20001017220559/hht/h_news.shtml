Karana Player Event 12:30 PM

The Karana Council of Guilds held a meeting on the Karana server in Lake Rathe on Wednesday, October 11, 2000. The meeting was well-attended, and many of the things the Council has accomplished were discussed, including our recent successful raid on Lord Nagafen, and our plane raids. Also discussed were the upcoming Mixer Hunt, entry level arena tourney, and other events already done or planned.

The Karana Council of Guilds is a group of more than 50 guilds on the Karana server that seeks to further the player community. We do this by sponsoring player-driven events, such as guildwars, tourneys, roleplaying storylines, large scale raids, and any other player-driven event. For more information on the Council, as well as how to join, go to our Web site at http://go.to/karanacouncil or to join, email Haemish at haemish@mail.com with your guild name, the names of your 2 representatives and their email addresses, and your guild Web site if applicable.

October 10, 2000 - Tuesday

 

Lady Vox Slain - Tribunal Server 12:30 PM

While many await anxiously and gather power in an attempt to take on the current High-Powered Dragons within the depths of Veeshan's Peaks, many of the citizens of Norrath remember that there are still two other Dragons out there that need to die, and as often as possible. That is why the Tribunal Council, an alliance of guilds on the Tribunal Server, decided that it was time for us to tackle the Self-styled Queen of the Northern Regions, Lady Vox.

The adventurers assembled and after clearing Vox's lair we moved in to begin preparations. The instructions were given and parties were formed. And then, the attack call was sounded! In a furious battle that lasted a mere two minutes but which seemed like an eternity, we felled the beast. Vox attempted to delay her death with the beginning of a Complete Heal but the combined might of the parties and the power of the Magicks we wielded were just too much. Her spell was interrupted, and then she fell.

People who participated were: Horammer, Shermann, Nives, Phisst, Gandain, Snoop, Sanain, Ancil, Baguk, Sholto, Bladewind, Elfesse, Kaizo, Kalaz, Splintercat, Lauvien, Raidian, Aindaen, Locyta, Brisance, Phunkbass, Qanamar, Cyanide, Chaox, Lyphe, Sivad, Dini, Lile, Falaris, Galendril, Sisley, Anault, Kaynn, and the assault was lead by me, Ignitz.

Loot was 2 Ice Comet spells. 2 Crystalline Spears, and McVaxius' Horn of War. Winners of the loot were Ancil and Kalaz for the spears, Lauvien for the horn, and the guilds of Caretakers of Norrath and Clan Omega for the spells. Congratulations to all!

As a side note this was the first raid for Tribunal Council and it was hugely successful considering some of the horror stories I have heard surrounding Vox Raids. It was also my first time leading a Raid of this magnitude and I have to say everyone did a fantastic job. Thanks!

- Ignitz Gemcaller
Public Relations Spokesperson for Tribunal Council
http://www.tribunalcouncil.org
