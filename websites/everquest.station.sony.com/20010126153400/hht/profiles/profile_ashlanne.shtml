Name: Ashlanne The Keeper of Morell-Thule

Race: Halfling

Class: Druid

Ashlanne was born in the small but sweet city of Rivervale. This is where all halflings call home. Ashlanne was born to two loving halfling parents that brought her up to be kind, caring and helpful to all that come in her presnce. She is a good and kind person always willing to lend a hand. She worships the beautiful goddess Karana. Ashlanne wears mutli-colored armor with varities of green, yellow, and black and oh yes she never wears shoes. She feels her feet are too beautiful to hide. Instead of shoes Ashlanne wears Fuzzknuckle Foothairs. Overall Ashlanne is a great halfling who loves to help others and is always willing to smile at anyone who crosses her path.
