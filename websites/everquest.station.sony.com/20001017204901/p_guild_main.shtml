G U I L D S

As you adventure throughout the lands of Norrath, it is certain that you will meet and befriend many people of like mind and similar goals. EverQuest allows you to establish a lasting relationship with these people through its guild system.

When you join a guild, your membership will be announced to the world by the addition of your guild's name to your name in both the 3D view and in inquiries made via the /who command. In addition, as a regular guild member you will be able to communicate with all members of your guild who are online via the /guildsay command. Other players can also use the /guildstatus command to determine if you are a regular member, an officer, or the leader of your guild.
