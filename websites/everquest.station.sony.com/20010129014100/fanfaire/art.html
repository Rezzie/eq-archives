Announcing the EverQuest Fan Faire Art Contest!

We are now accepting submissions of art work from registered attendees of the upcoming Fan Faire for display at the upcoming Fan Faire in Baltimore. Only registered attendees of Fan Faire may participate. The winner of this contest will receive a free year of EverQuest gameplay, and EverQuest Lead Artist Scott McDaniel will accept a portfolio submitted by the winner. He will review, evaluate and offer feedback to the artist on his or her work!
