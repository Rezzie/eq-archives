Minotaur  

While the identity of the dark sorcerer who first created these monstrous creatures is buried in time, it is Meldrath the Malignant, an evil gnomish outcast, who has refined these half-man / half-bull abominations into the lethal fighting machines they are today. Used mainly as slave labor, minotaurs are valued members of any nefarious legion due to their great strength and ferocity.
