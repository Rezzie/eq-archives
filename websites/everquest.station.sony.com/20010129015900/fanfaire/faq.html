FAQ

What do I get for my registration fee?
You will be attending a private reception with other players Friday evening. Then on Saturday, you will be able to meet representatives from Verant. There will be contests and drawings for prizes. You will be able to attend discussion panels with EQ artists, developers, producers and GM's. You will get a limited edition T-shirt and be served dinner Saturday evening. You will also get the unique experience of meeting hundreds of other Norrathian citizens!

Can children attend this event?
Children can attend this event but all minors must be accompanied by an adult. The cost for children is the same as for adults.

My spouse does not play EQ but wants to come with me. What is the fee for someone that does not play?
The fee for someone that does not play EQ is the same for those attending the event that do. We still have to meet all of our expenses for all that attend. Just list them as your guest on the registration form.

What about my hotel accommodations?
You are responsible for making your own hotel and travel arrangements. You might want to take advantage of the arrangements we have made with The BWI Airport Marriott for our group.

What kinds of prizes will be awarded?
In addition to lots of great EQ stuff, there will be a number of prizes awarded in game! More information on those prizes will be released later!

I am a guide. Do I register under my guide name or my player name?
Please refer to the guide board periodically for information on this.
