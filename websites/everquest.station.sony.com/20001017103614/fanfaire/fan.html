Coming soon to a zone near you!!
The only official real life event sponsored by Verant, EverQuest Fan Faire is the hot spot for everyone involved with EverQuest! Cindy Archuleta, the person who organized the Saint Louis and Las Vegas real life events, is now working directly with Verant. If you have never been to one of these parties, you can check out some of the pictures here!

Whether you want to meet the developers, artists, and producers behind the game, or the person behind that hunting partner you have known in Norrath for so long, this is the place to do it!

There are activities designed to help you find and get to know other players in your area, or from your server. You can also win great prizes! Many new hunting parties have formed as a result of these meetings!

Also at Fan Faire, learn the latest plans and developments ahead for EverQuest and talk with the people who will make them happen!

The next Fan Faire will be on the East Coast in February. We will announce the date and location soon!
