Technical Support
We're here to help you. For technical issues dealing with EverQuest, please use one of the easy-to-use technical support forms:

Problems with video – EverQuest Video Technical Support
Problems with sound – EverQuest Sound Technical Support
Other technical problems – EverQuest Technical Support

Additional technical phone support is also available from 9:00 AM to 12 midnight PT (12:00 PM to 3:00 AM ET), seven days a week except holidays. Phone 1-619-554-0144. For better service, please be online and at your computer when calling.

Billing Inquiries/Account Issues
Your problem is our problem, too. For billing issues or problems with your Station account, you can e-mail The Station using an easy-to-use form from Account Services. You can also contact The Station customer service department by phone at the numbers below. Customer service reps are available weekdays from 12:00 PM to 12 midnight ET (9:00 AM to 9:00 PM PT), Saturday from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM ET (11:00 AM to 7:00 PM PT) and Sunday from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM ET (11:00 AM to 5:00 PM PT). Toll-free in the US and Canada: 1-888-STATION Outside the US and Canada: 1-801-579-2300.
