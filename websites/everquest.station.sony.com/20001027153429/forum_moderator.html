M O D E R A T O R
 

 

If you wish to report a thread in violation of the message board rules, please email the following information to EQModerator@verant.com:

  • URL of the thread to which the post belongs (Right-click on the message window, choose "Properties")
  • Subject of the thread.
  • Name of the Poster
  • A short description of how the post violates the message board rules.

The moderator will attend to your report as soon as possible, however, please be aware that you will not receive a direct response to this mail.
