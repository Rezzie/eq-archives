25 things I have learned in 60 levels.
(not an official, nor endorsed, SOE/Verant list)

  1. Never trust the /con text.
  2. People who use sewing kits as containers deserve to combine their most precious items.
  3. A copper piece saved just weighs you down.
  4. Boat travel sucks.
  5. Those who believe the person they are trading with really will return with the item or money on their other character should stick to killing rats and bats for their income.
  6. No matter how many improvements are made, Necromancers will still feel shafted.
  7. GMs are not gods.
  8. Nothing is wrong with the female dark elf thong.
  9. At times, Barbara is not really controlling that hot wood elf chick... Bubba is.
  10. Plat can not buy happiness, but it sure as hell makes misery more fun.
  11. If someone shows up just as a rare spawn pops, they will try to steal it.
  12. Verant is not a different way to spell Satan.
  13. D3wd sp33k is not a real language.
  14. No matter what kick-ass armors are put in game, nothing will ever compare to Rubicite.
  15. It is understood there is a logical reason for downtime, but we still don’t have to like it.
  16. The test server can actually be wiped.
  17. It was best, and funnier, when Mooto charmed people.
  18. Kithicor is a nasty place to visit after sun down.
  19. Hell is too light of a word to describe hell levels.
  20. Beggars can’t be choosers and they are also annoying.
  21. Absor is the Community Relations Manager, he can not actually make design changes to the game.
  22. Never give your pet a rare weapon.
  23. When in doubt, zone.
  24. Logging out in the Efreeti camp is not a good idea.
  25. Trains will happen.
- Steve Fuller
