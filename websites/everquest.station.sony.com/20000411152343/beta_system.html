System Requirements

Minimum Required

Windows®; 95/98
Pentium 166+
32MB RAM
Direct3D or Glide compliant accelerator card w/ 4MB RAM
500MB* Hard drive space
28.8k+ internet connection
Windows compatible sound card
Microsoft compatible mouse
6x CD-ROM drive

Recommended

Pentium 200+
64MB RAM

*This number includes the amount swap space(virtual memory) required. We recommend having a minimum of 200MB free disk space after the installation of EverQuest.
