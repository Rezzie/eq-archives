Joining and Quitting Guilds

When you have decided that it is time for you to join a guild, your best source for information will be your fellow citizens of Norrath. You'll be able to see which guilds they belong to by the guild tag that they display over their head. Ask them about their guild, delve deep and see if the guild fits your personality and style of play. Most guilds have websites you can visit to gather more detailed information.

Not all guilds accept new members. Guild membership, and who it is offered to, is solely at the discretion of the guild and its members.

If you are accepted into a guild, the guild leader or an officer of the guild will invite you to join using the /guildinvite command. When the invitation is extended you may either accept or decline.

If you decide that you wish to leave your guild, you may do so at anytime by using the /guildremove command.
