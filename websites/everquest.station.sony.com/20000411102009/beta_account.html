Creating an Account

Please make sure that you have your registration code and e-mail address ready before creating an account.

After running the EverQuest executable and performing the patch process, you will be taken to the EverQuest front-end screen. Select "Account", then left-click on the "New Account" button, this will bring up the account registration screen.

  1. Enter your name, address, e-mail address, and date of birth by left-clicking in the field, then typing in the information. The TAB key may be used to move the cursor to the next field. Once all of the information on each registration screen has been filled out, click on the "Next" key located at the bottom right side of the screen.
  2. Enter your PC specifications and answer the other general interest questions. Please note that the PC and general interest questions are not required to create the account. After you have completed this section of the registration click the "Next" key to continue.
  3. Select a Chat Handle and enter your EverQuest account key. An account key is included with EverQuest. The login name that you select will be used to connect to the EverQuest servers, and will also be seen while you are in the EverQuest chat rooms.
  4. Enter your Sony Station login and password. If you do not have a current Sony Station account, you may create one by simply entering a desired login name and password. After selecting a Station name and password, left-click on the "Finish" button located at the bottom right side of the screen. Your registration will then be sent to the server and processed. An e-mail with your password and the chat handle that you selected will be sent to you within 48 hours. If you do not receive a confirmation e-mail regarding your account information within 48 hours, please contact our Customer Service department immediately.
  5. Once you have received your confirmation e-mail regarding your new EverQuest account, return to the EverQuest front-end and select "Change Account". You will then be required to enter your Station name and password, do so, then left-click on the "Connect" button. After you enter your Station name information, a window will appear asking you to subscribe. Left-click the "Now" button to begin a subscription. Select a method of payment and fill-in all required billing information, then left-click on the "Finish" button located at the bottom left side of the screen. Your billing information will then be processed, this may take a few minutes. Once accepted, you may then return to the front-end screen and select "Connect" to begin playing EverQuest. If you encounter any problems with the billing process, please contact our Customer Service department immediately.
