Dragon Dreams

In that instant his chest heaved upward and outward. Like a skin diver taking his last gasp of air before submerging, he filled his lungs. His scales cracked and popped as they shifted to accommodate the transformation. Then he exhaled. At first it was a hot, nauseating breath that blasted my face, but within seconds the temperature increased and the air filled with fire. Tree branches ignited around me. I dove to the ground. Waiving my arms around to ward off the flames. I woke up still waiving my arms around, I swear for a second I could still see that damn dragon above me. My wife sat up and grabbed my arms. She asked me what was wrong. In that moment I realized I was not engulfed in flames, and nothing was attacking me. I sat there for a second and laughed at myself.

"Nothing Honey" I said, still laughing under my breath. 		Wow what an experience. I gotta stop drinking coffee before I go to bed!

The rest of the night I continued to have weird dreams. I can remember an encounter with the Budweiser frogs, saving them from the lizard.

But I won't go there.
