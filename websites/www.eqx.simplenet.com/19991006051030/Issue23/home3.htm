Home Alone

Magic or other special abilities

I really love magic. I love experimenting with magic, but how about a magic system that does not have you mastering a spell after one attempt? Magic should strengthen with experience. How about a teleport spell. When you first learn the spell you find yourself transporting players into uncharted areas, and in the middle of oceans, or worse you stick them in the side of a wall, severing their leg.

Huge Maps!! A good game has to have lots of areas. Areas that don’t look alike! I would like to spend days trying to get to a secluded location that I heard about in a tavern. Traveling across oceans should also be fun. Climbing aboard a huge freighter and wandering the lengths of the ship. Just please do a better job than SSI did! 		3Dfx!!!

And first person views: Nuff said!

Multi Player

You know if I’m telling you about a game, it has to support multi player. I just can’t find solitaire games that keep me interested longer than a few days. That’s why I gave up on MM6 so fast. Just wasn’t there, you know? Online games are the way of the future. You just can’t create a game that can replace human nature and error. 		So here I sit.
