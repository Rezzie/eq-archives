Can I wear what? I thought confusedly.

I had just made my way to the gate of Qeynos, new guild tunic proudly displayed upon my torso and trusty - or was it rusty? - short sword clutched gingerly in my sweaty hand. Well, to be completely accurate, I had just made my way back to the gate, having barely escaped a mean and vicious rat by reaching the protection of the stalwart Qeynos Guards.
