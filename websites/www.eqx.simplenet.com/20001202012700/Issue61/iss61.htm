Religion and Race: The Dark Elves

What is religion? One can say that religion is the organized structure of worship. A religion in that sense would include the hierarchy of the priesthood, the rituals and sacred texts, the edifices in which worship took place, and the lay worshippers. It does not, however, speak about the purpose behind all that structure. Why do people worship? Why are there priesthoods and churches and odd rituals that appear quasi-magical in nature?

The Ranger

The tale you are about to hear is not my own, nor in truth that of any friend of mine, it is a tale I heard once a long while ago while I was still plying my wares along the old trade routes between Qeynos and the mountains of Rathe.

It was a crisp autumn day and I still had a full cart and at least three weeks on the road to look forward to when I happened by a small town that looked as if it was on its last legs or just beginning to walk. Rather than spend another wretched night camping along side the road I decided to call my day early and spend the night here. It was with no difficulty that I determined the Tavern to be the moderate sized building next to the town's well...

Chat boards are flooded. Newsgroups overflow. The torrent of questions just keeps coming, and there is no damn large enough or sturdy enough to withstand the pressure.
