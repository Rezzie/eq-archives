SimpleNet Resources: Online Marketing Tools

The following links have assisted our customers with the success of their web site. Please contact us if you'd like to let us know about a site that has helped you achieve your online goals.

Meta Tag Generator

FreeWebSubmission.com
Meta Tags are placed inside the <HEAD> section of your web page to provide information to search engines. This site has a form that will, once submitted, provide meta information that you can cut-and-paste into your site.

Keyword Density Measurement

GoRank.com
Visit Gorank.com to determine your keyword density (seek 3-4% of your page). Avoid creating pages with extremely high keyword density. They may be regarded by the search engines as an attempt to keyword stuff, or seek higher ranking by placing too many keywords (hidden or visible) on the page.

Search Engine Friendliness

1-Hit.com
This site supplies a search engine spider simulator that verifies that your site can be "spidered" by search engines. The simulator will give you an idea on how search engines will see your page. If there are problems found, the site will give you suggestions or additional comments to help you resolve any issues.

StarGeek.com
This Search Engine Optimization (SEO) tool will show you what search engine spiders see when they index your page.This Search Engine Optimization (SEO) tool will show you what search engine spiders see when they index your page.

Search Engine Site Rank Tools

FreeWebSubmission.com
This free application will tell you where your site is naturally listed, for phrases you specify, if your site happens to be in the top 100 of the twelve search engines it accesses.

Rankwhere.com
This free application will tell you where your site is naturally listed, for phrases you specify, if your site happens to be in the top 200 of the seven search engines it accesses.
