Dragon Dreams

You know its time for a game to be released when you find yourself dreaming about it. I was surfing EQ sites on the web the other night, reading everyone's E3 reports. I remember reading a quote from Brad about dragon sightings being rare, and that it would be a chronicle moment just to spot one, and a major accomplishment to survive the encounter. That must have been in my subconscious when I went to sleep that night, as it inspired quite a weird dream. I was running down a freshly cut path in the middle of a jungle. Vines dangled, severed, outlining the contour of the footpath. The sound of screaming monkeys could be heard, echoing throughout the area. The jungle was alive. On my exposed skin mosquitoes gourmandized on the delicacy of my blood. My breathing was labored, and my clothes were drenched in sweat. I must have been running for a while. At the end of the path I found a clearing. Actually it was a cliff overlooking a huge valley. A five hundred-foot waterfall could be viewed to the right of the clearing. Enormous amounts of clear blue water gushed out its orifice, and into the lowland. The high ground was made up of granite. Its glass-like appearance distinguished it as impossible to climb. The sun beamed down above me, warming my sweaty wet clothes.
