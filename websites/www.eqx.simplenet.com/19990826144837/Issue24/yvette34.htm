ISSUE # 24June 1st - June 5th

Yvette's Tower - Part 3

The four began the words for the different spells they chose to cast as the pirates turned and rushed the group in a panic. The surprise on the faces of their opponents was quite visible in the failing moonlight as Sabrina's spell hit first, slowing the movements of the crew two-fold. Then followed the quick blasts from Marissa's fingers, sending blasts of magical energy down the length of the ship into the chests of her targets, who fell to the deck near dead. Montgomery called forth the fury of the seas, bringing a wave over the ship that sent the sailors to the far end of the ship, and a few over the rails. Last came the terrible magic from Morgan the aged Erudite in the form of great bolts of lightning that tore through the pirate men, killing all but the captain. The Warrior Captain seemed to be rather resilient to magical attacks, being only slightly burnt by the lightning. He made charge for the group with his cutlass. No one saw the drowned sailor climb over the rail save Yvette, who was standing like a shadow near the rear of the ship eyes set on the Warrior Captain like a hawk. Montgomery made use of a broken rail trying to defend against the captains slightly slowed attacks as the others made ready spells to strike at him. The undead spoke, Yvette's lips moving his.

Tale by Ian Ash, Fraterni Lux.
