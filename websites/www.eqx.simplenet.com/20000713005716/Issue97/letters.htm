I was wondering if you would be able to do something for new readers....

I just started reading Everquest Express about a week ago and am very interested in the fictional stories that people are writing. However I have found it quite difficult to find the other parts in a multi-part tale. I was wondering if it would be possible to put links to previous parts of the tale at the beginning of the story. I know this would be VERY hard to go back through and do with all of the old ones, but maybe if you start now it would make it easier for people who have just started reading the site. It's very daunting work to have to try and find the first 4 parts of the tale that you are reading when it's on part 5! Thanks for the consideration.

Andrew Barnhart

[Ed. - We already have something like that. At the end of the story, click on the author's name. That will take you to a listing of the author's work. It's a start. I'm thinking about setting up downloadable text and Word versions of some of the longer tales. But I'm a big fan of not doing too much work... :)]
