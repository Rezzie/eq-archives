Innothule- Deep in Innothule swamp, more has been stirring than just the Trolls. Frogloks and Kobolds have been seen gathering in large groups. The campfires blaze until dawn, and the Trolls are starting to become a tad uneasy. Grobb is well protected, but an invasion by both the Frogloks and the Kobolds would put a strain on the Troll defenses. With hopes of fortifying the entrance to Grobb even more, messengers have been sent out to deliver word to hunting parties that have traveled to various points in Antonica. Word has also been sent to Oggok and Neriak, with hopes of uniting the dark races against these foes.

Oggok- The outbreak of humans, elves and various other races in the Feerrott has spread the Ogre bouncers thin. Rumor has it that the good races are trying to take over Cazic Thule's temple, in an effort to reduce the evil that shrouds the southern portion of Antonica. Not only are the ogres having constant lizard problems, now they have to deal with warriors from abroad trying to tear down their temples. Having recieved word from Grobb that the Frogloks and Kobolds are gathering, the Ogres are becoming frightened that their live's may be changing for the worst.

Made Fit by Elaan - South Antonica Division

NERIAK, FOREIGN QUARTER - Today the group known as the F'leurs DuMal was again responsible for aggression against the residents of the Foreign Quater. With the aid of their necromancer, Fuerlespit, the group let loose over 30 undead skeletons which rampaged through the Quarter, killing visitors and human residents alike.

Tdaven H'yonith, the infamous poet and spokesman for the group, said "It will take ages for us to clean the stink of the animal races from Neriak. Next time, we'll give our skeleton minions mops and buckets." The group has gained adherents in the last week, allegedly numbering seven, including Tdaven, his sister Tdanye, Fuerlespit, the cleric Gainam, Nuuin the warrior, and Ezmend the enchanter.
