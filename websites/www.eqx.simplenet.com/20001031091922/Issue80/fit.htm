EAST KARANA -

In a move to boost sagging ticket sales, the league of Antonican bards will be sending many bards out on tour together. The hope is that there will be strength in numbers, with a wider range of bards appealing to a wider audience, and hopefully reflecting more money.

When asked about the flagging attendance numbers, Guild Spokesman Furlin Etherode blamed the trend toward gladiatorial duels. However, he also stated his opinion that arena battles are a fad. "We've all seen blood and there's nothing creative there. They'll get disenchanted and come back," Furlin claimed.

The Anotnicans posted part of their schedule for Summer...
