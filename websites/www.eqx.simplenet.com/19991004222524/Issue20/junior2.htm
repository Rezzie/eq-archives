ISSUE # 20May 2nd - May 8th

The Test

Thyvald walked with a light step towards the meeting with his superior. The Bothin crack had gone really well, and there was no way that the Thieves Guild could know of the three extra first quality gems and five second value gems he had secreted before writing his report. "I’m going to go up quickly in rank, I just know it!" thought Thyvald. "No thief of my level has ever done such a clean job!"

As he walked around the corner, he bumped into a dwarf, and heard the jangle of harp strings from the dwarf’s pack. 		"Watch it, human," grumbled the dwarf, "or you’ll be hurrying to your funeral."

"Sorry."

Thyvald didn’t want to risk losing his anticipated take from Bothrin. Besides, the dwarf hadn’t budged when Thyvald ran into him, but Thvyald had rebounded off him like an ball striking a wall.

Finally, Thyvald turned the corner and saw his superior at the end of the alley, under a street light. 		Report of Raffles, Master Thief on Thyvald, Junior Thief

1. Raided Bothrins after third watch, before Thyvald.
2. Took count of strongbox contents: 653 gold 8 gems first quality, 5 second quality, 20 gems third quality.
3. Thyvald failed.

As he listened from the shadows near the alley entrance, the dwarf heard a sudden scream and gargling gasp. "Hmpf," Oghma snorted, "I guess he was hurrying to his funeral."
