ISSUE # 27June 20th - June 26th

Crossing Paths

"I'm cold, Uncle" mumbles Janell.

"Yer a bloody barbarian for Brell's sake! Now be quiet, we're almost there...and stop calling me Uncle" The dwarf continues his ascent of the snowy peak, the winds tearing at his mail with a vengeance. Once to the top, he settles into a crouch and glances about, surveying the merchant track below. 		"Angaram? How can you stand it up here? Is it all that hair?" Janell asks, crouching down herself and huddling deeply within her bear hide cloak in a desperate attempt to ward off the chill. Being born in this climate did not mean she had to like it, she thinks quietly. "Great. A snowy waste full o'barbarians and I get saddled w'the whiniest one." Turning to the young barbarian, "I only brought you along at your fathers insistance, so be quiet or I'll toss you off the cliff m'self."

Tale by Mac.
