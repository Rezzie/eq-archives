Anyway, it appears that there is a new and dangerous hobby afoot in Norrath. Sentient creatures of all races seem to be participating in this foolishness. Here's a part of the letter that came with these drawings:

... last night while battling the fierce Aviak guards, we decided to have a diving competition. Certainly the troll got a 8.5 on his gargantuan cannon ball. Even the birds wanted to join in the fun!

That's right. These 'adventurers' are throwing themselves into lakes from disgusting heights.

As a responsible member of the society of Norrath, again despite rumors to the contrary, I must advise you all to stay away from these freaks. I suspect that some of them aren't bright enough to land in the water at all. I would hate to lose a loyal reader to the heavy squishing sound of a Troll landing on hard ground, only cushioned by a few feet of intelligent reader...

And you certainly don't want your children seeing this. Imagine if they were to decide to try this out at home! If you find yourself in a situation where this sort of foolishness is going on, get out.
