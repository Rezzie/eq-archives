Readers Choice

Well, we asked you all to tell us what you liked most about the last two years of EverQuest Express, and you did. We wanted to be able to point newer readers to the best of EQX. And it's the readers that know what was best. Thanks for responding to our poll. The results were wonderful to read. You've given us a good sense of what sort of things we did right during our first two years. And we were very pleased to get all of your comments, despite one's like this:

Favorite Stuff - Absor

The task for our staff this week was to come up with a few favorite things from EQX past. Not an easy bit of work, that's for sure. The basic idea was to keep it to two or three things that you liked best about EQX, something that you did, something someone else did, and your favorite EQX moment (meaning nearly anything else). It's one tough job, to find just a few things over the last two years of wonderful goofiness that has gone on here.

Favorite Stuff - Ian

Anticipating writing this "Best of EQX" article I thought back over the history of EverQuest Express and EverQuest itself. I thought of how the community has grown and changed, of the various fan web sites that started and disappeared, and of the legends I have come to meet in this online world. During this break I tried to remember what it was like before stat modifications, weapon efficiency ratios or class balance petitions.
