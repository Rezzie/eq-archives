Venire's Promise

I felt the energy within me. My fingertips tingled as the electricity filled out the joints of my fingers. Then in an instant the energy leaped from my index fingers. Like a bolt of lighting twisting and turning, the magic raced towards my victim, smashing it in the chest, lifting him off the ground, and slammed to the floor ten feet away.

"You did it!" 		The old mans withered face formed a smile, as he clapped both hands together applauding himself for his great teaching. I looked down at the target dummy still smoking on the ground.

"Did you see that!" I said gasping for air.

"You see you do have your Father’s power and more." 		But Gosling’s words didn’t find me. The events that just occurred confused me. I couldn’t believe I had that in me. I use to think magicians were evil. My father practiced magic for years. Passed down to him from his father and his father’s father passing it down to him.
