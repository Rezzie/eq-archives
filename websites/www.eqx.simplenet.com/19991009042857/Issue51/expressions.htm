Jora Wrote,

I would like to see a section on terminology. I mean there are a lot of words and abbreviations online. What do they all mean?

So you just received you copy of EverQuest and your running around the world of Norrath. A small elf runs up to you and says, "Hey are you a newbie?" You see all types of scroll on your screen. People shouting, commenting OOC, and selling things in auctions. What does it all mean??

Well here is but a small list of terms and abbreviations that I could come up with. There are many many more…

Channel Auction: This is a channel to sell items you have found on your profitable quests. The proper method to use auction is to first make an offer.

/auction I have a War Hammer for sale. Min bid 10g
(This means this person is selling a war hammer and will not take less than 10 gold)

The correct response he is looking for would be for someone to say;
Thrash auctions "I bid 10 gold"

Now the auctioneer will say "10 gold heard Thrash"

If no one else bids the auctioneer will say "10 gold going once.. twice.. sold.. to Thrash for 10 Gold"

They will then find a meeting place to make the trade.

AFK:   Away from the keyboard
BRB:   Be right back
BUG:   A response from the program that was not desired output by the author
BTW:   By the way
Camper:   Someone who hides in one spot and ambushes other players
<g>   Grin
<eg>   Evil grin
Flooding:   The process of flooding everyone's screen with text.. Also called "Spam"
GameMasters:   Same as a dungeon master. One who runs the game. Abbreviated GM
Guide:   Someone who helps you with the game. This person can also help you if you're killed by a bug.
Guild:   A group of players who choose to play together. A brotherhood.
IMHO:   In my humble opinion
Lag:   Connection problems. The game is moving slow.
Lamer:   Someone who is lame.
Llama:   Same as lamer
LOL:   Laugh out loud
MUD:   Multi user dungeon. An old text game played online.
Newbie:   A player who is new to a particular game.
OOC:   Out of character. This is for saying something out loud that is non-roleplaying in nature.
PK:   Player killer. One who kills other players
PvP:   Player vs Player. Someone who roleplays killing other players.
Shouting:   Someone who types all in caps.. I CAN'T HEAR YOU. Note this is very annoying for other players to deal with. You wont make many friends doing this.
