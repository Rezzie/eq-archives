ISSUE # 108

Ask Oxesiess































 Dear Oxesiess,

(I apologise for the length of this missive..I am of elven descent and as you may know, we do not like to rush things...)

When first entering Norrath, I befriended a fellow high elf, a cleric, outside Felwithe. For many months we hunted together, she being more worldly wise that I and able to save this Paladins life when he bit off more than he could chew, and I have to say those formative weeks were some of the most treasured times of my life. We grew close, and I knew that one day I would propose to her - I decided to do so after reaching my 20th season. She had travelled to Antonica before I reached my 20th season, and when Tunare saw fit to grant me it, I was heartbroken to find that she had met a barbarian warrior stomping around the Qeynos hills and they were married. I know not if it was the horned helm, the facial tattoos or the vaugue cattle-scent that attracted her to him, but that is neither here nor there..

..anyway, from there I travelled to the Runnyeye Citadel alone and vented my frustration on the goblin inhabitants for a while. Some weeks later I again met the lady and we again began to hunt together on a regular basis.

I recently admitted that there could be no other in my life save for her, and was dismayed to be told that had she not married the man she is now with - in a past life she was also married to him - she would have asked for my hand.

To cut an already overly long story short, she has recently voiced a desire to try an alternate plane of existance in Norrath, something called a nue servor? I have meditated long on what this may be, alas I have drawn a blank. I only fear that on her entry to this other-plane, I will never see my lady again. If this were to happen, may Tunare forgive me, I do not think I could go on. How should I go about convincing her to stay in this plane of existance, or would you advocate the old addage, "if you love something, let it go."?

I thank you in advance for your undoubtedly cryptic although honest advice.

Githnaur
Distraught Paladin, 33rd season, Erollisi Marr
