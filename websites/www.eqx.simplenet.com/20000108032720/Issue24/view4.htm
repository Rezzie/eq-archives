ISSUE # 24June 1st - June 5th

A View to Temptation

"Please, everything I have is for your needs. There will be no reason for weaponsplay!" urged Turlas Delk.

"Can you speak man-child? Tell me your name at least that I should know who admires me if not the reason" as she sheathed her sword.

"I, I, Ha-Hameil, I am Hameil." 		Her hard features seemed to soften as she spoke. "Hameil, that is a strong name and I see that you indeed have power in the flesh beneath these robes." She moved her hands over his shoulders and felt his muscles as he relaxed beneath her touch. "So tell me," she whispered. He could feel her sweet breath on his face. "Tell me what it is that caused you to look upon me as you did." Hameil would tell her. There was nothing he would conceal from her. Never had he felt so alive. "Your movement it was that first caught my eye my lady. So fast, so fluid and powerful. I lay froze awaiting instruction from my training and instinct of which I seem to have little. Further I admit I was trapped by your beauty. Even if I had wanted to move I doubt if my heart would have let me."

Tale by Dirge.
