ISSUE # 27June 20th - June 26th

Crossing Paths

As the dwarf turns back to view the trail, he spots his quarry: A train of gnolls moving goods from the field towards the Black Burrows. Shifting his shield onto his arm and drawing his sword, he shifts his position and motions for the human to do the same. Janel sighs quietly and draws the claymore from her back. Also crouching, she moves towards the edge of the cliff, preparing to slide down at her short mentor's cue. Angaram roars like the bear whose skin he now wears and launches himself down the incline towards the now panicked gnolls. Following behind quietly and with more grace and skill than her age would indicate, Janell follows.
