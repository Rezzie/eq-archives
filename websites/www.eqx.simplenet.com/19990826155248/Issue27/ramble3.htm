A Rambling about Roleplaying in Everquest

Ironically, even though I wished everyone would talk like a Britannian, and less like an American, I too used plain old English. Hey, everyone else was. There was no point to roleplaying, since the atmosphere of the game had already been put through a garbage disposal, sent down the sewer, and had rotted away into nothingness. So what's my point? It's that I don't want EQ's already well-formed atmosphere to go down the tubes like UO's did. I'd personally love it if everyone could at least talk sensibly, try to say things understandably, spell things right, and, God forbid, roleplay a little and speak like an ogre or mage or halfling or whatever they are. It makes the game a lot more interesting if everyone does that. Really. Honestly. Besides, wouldn't you rather go online and into Norrath than online and into just another giant graphical chatroom? I know I would. Regardless of what you think about what I've said, thank you for your time. If you couldn't understand anything I said here, then I've turned into my own pet peeve. :)
