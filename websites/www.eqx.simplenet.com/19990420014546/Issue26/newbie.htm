Remember when you were a newbie?

Remember when you were a newbie?

The first time someone gave you a few gold coins, or a morsel of bread, how grateful you were. You would thank that person repeatedly. Hanging out in donation waiting for gifts from the gods to drop down. Standing for hours at the fountain begging everyone in sight to take you leveling. Remember playing with socials for the first time? Fart, Burp, greet, sneeze, pout, just to name a few. 		I can remember when I started playing my first MUD. It was my first experience with multiplayer games. I was in college and a friend of mine took me to the computer lab on a Saturday afternoon. He said he had been playing this game for several months, and that I should give it a try. We arrived at the lab and logged on the server. First we rolled me up a character, and then entered the game. After a few minutes in donation, loading up with newbie equipment, we took off for some island. Grouped with him, we started killing things for experience. I maxed out in experience and we headed back to town for leveling. I trained with my instructor and gained my levels I had earned. Then I was able to practice a few new spells. Holy cow! I could now create food, and water. We continued to adventure. All I remember is looking at my watch sometime later, and it was Sunday afternoon. We had been playing for 19 hours straight.

I played that MUD almost every day for the next year, and I was still only half way from maxing out in abilities and levels. I can remember living that MUD for that year. It was usually on my mind throughout the day at school. I'd plan what I was going to do that night when I got online. It was very addictive.
