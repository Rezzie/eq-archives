The Slaves of Crushbone

Kerys fidgeted uncomfortably as he stood in the marble hallway. The door in front of him led to trouble. He knew that, with as much certainty as he knew the sun would rise the next day. Being a wood elf, he didn't like being cooped up in any building, but the marble city of Felwithe was especially unpleasant for him. He could feel the weight of the heavy stone walls and ceilings upon him, and longed to be out in the Greater Faydark forest again. Given a choice he would be there at that moment, but he had been summoned to the white city, and he didn't dare refuse the one who had summoned him.

    Tale by Snicker

Breaking and Entering

Razor removed a small thin metal rod from his black leather leg pouch. There was not a lock made in all of Norrath that he couldn't pick. He took pride in his work. He carefully and quietly worked the intricacies of the lock mechanism and within seconds a slight metallic "click" could be heard. From his leather pouch he removed a second item, a small jar of gnome grease. After rubbing the grease on the hinges, Razor quietly opened the door.

    Tale by Razor
