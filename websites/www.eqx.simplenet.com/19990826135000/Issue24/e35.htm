EverQuest E3 Report

The Good Things

There are plenty of nifty features that are pretty cool. When you go down to 0 HP, you are knocked unconscious. You still have time for your party to heal you up or get that monster to quit attacking you. While you lay there staring at your helpless body, its lifeforce slowly drains away as you bleed to death. If you are healed, then you will wake back up and can retreat and go heal up. 		There are various menus for skills, socials, inventories, and so on. You can take any icon or any inventory slot and drag it into a macro box. This box is always available, even in full screen mode, so you can have easy access to your inventory, skills, or anything else without having to search through the menus. Of course, the graphics are stunning. We've all seen the screenshots and had to mop the drool off our keyboards. Well, even the movies don't do it justice. To see the smoothness of the animation and response to your movements is truly amazing. The graphical artists deserve a commendation.

Article by Wynn Orill.
