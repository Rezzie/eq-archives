Ask Oxesiess

Dear Oxesiess,

I am a High Elven woman with... well... feelings... feelings for other women... Everywhere I look I see beauty, but I am not noticing the correct sex... Nay, not even the right race! Predominantly I am attracted to wood elf women, however I dare not bring to light my forbidden feelings, not only for fear of rejection but of the disgrace I would bring about on my families name. Please help, I fear my feelings shall soon tear me apart if my dagger does not do it first.

Glamcrist

Well firsted o all, make sure when ya's usin yer dagger dat ya's always thrust forward from right in front o yer body, not sideways. Have yer grip on da handle wiff a frim grasp. Dis way ya's won't glance a blow and maybe have it comes back at ya's. On da other problem.

Ya know once me family had two sled Doggie Woof-woofs named Drift and well I ferget da other's name. Any how, they both were she Doggie woof-woofs, but Drift would sometimes act like a he Doggie Woof-woof wiff da other one. Did da barbs all stand round worry bout findin Drift a he sled doggie she liked? Heck no! When ya's gots more dan 14 polars ta skin, are knee in da icky process, it's below... well it's friggen cold out, and yer not allowed ta go ta bed near da fire afore ya's finish, no one cares what da Drift is doin and who's she a hurtin? Let lone take'n time outta da hunt fer food and furs jist ta walkin her round so she can sniff tails wiff another clan's doggies. I thinky iffen yer family gots enough time layin round ta be worry'n bout dis stuff, send em on up ta Halas fer a bit. Dat's da problem wiff Norrath Glamcrist, to many dunt gots enough polar bears ta clean.
