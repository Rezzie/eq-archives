Dear Auntie Moog,

For some reason, I have an overwhelming attraction to High Elf women, whilst I am a human. Other humans think I'm totally nuts, and while I am nuts, they think it's wrong for me to be attracted to a race other than mine. Auntie Moog, you're the smartest person I respect, please tell me what I can do about this, or should I just slice and dice all the nay-sayers with my minotaur axe?

Estocles

Moog always been big fan of interras…interrra…luv between peoples, no matter what or who dey be. Anyboddy tell you different, you give em big whack! Solve argument, and impress some girlies, too.

It hard, when used to seeing Troll and Ogre wimmins with great big…assets, to see same on Inkies, but dey are dere. Just don't get too close, or could make Inkies mad. Dat never a good ting.


Moog want to say someting. Aklar be goin way and she ask Moog to go wit her cause she be lonely. Moog was getting tired of dis tiny desk and always writing all de time anyways, so I going. If you have more problems, and don't know answer, just ask yorself. What Moog do? And if you don't know what Moog do, I tell you. Moog bash. Solve most problems.
