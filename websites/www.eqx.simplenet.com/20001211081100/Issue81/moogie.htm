Ask Auntie Moog

Dear Auntie Moog,

I was wondering if there was some sort of a mysterious cross-breeding experiment going on in the Grobb that we should know about?

Aunti Moog,

But when me try to bash to get dem, sometimes me duh one get bashed. Dat not bodder me too much cuz me udderstand dat some odder peoples might be bedder at bashing den me right now. But ting I no like iz de tree hugger derooids dat no fight fair. Dey make tangly tings grab me feet or run too fast away for me to catch. Den day do sparkly tings like Shamen and it hurt till I go sleep. How u ting I should top dis frum happenin' ta me?

Next time you want to go barsh stuffs, go ask her to come wit you. You do fine, make troll momma proud of you! It always segnif…sicnaf…big when girl asks you home to meet the folks. So think good before you go! On odder hand, if you get second thoughts, can always barsh her on head and then run away. Moog had this happen to her once…


Dear Auntie Moog,

I have fallen in love with another High Elf, but I cannot help wondering if he is the one for me. I have never left my forests, and think that I may be over eager in my youth in wanting to settle down. The other day, a nice human said something about my legs, and was wondering if other races are as gracious in their compliments? Should I experiment with inter-racial relationships before setting down? Would it help my love life?

- Confused.

Dear Confused

Best way to have good love live is to have big one. You still be young! Live life big, travel, meet peoples all over. If dis High Elfie guy is the one, you will find him at end of travels, and all will be happy.
