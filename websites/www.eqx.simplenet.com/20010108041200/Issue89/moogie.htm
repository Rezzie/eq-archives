Dear Auntie Moog,

Allow me to introduce myself. I am a Dark Elven rogue residing in the picturesque city of Neriak. In an attempt to broaden my horizons, I packed up my belongings and headed south to lovely Grobb, hoping to stay a few nights in the marshland village. Rather than the 'romp in the swamp' I had desired, however, I was promptly bashed by the guards. In my apparently naive mind, I had thought the sophisticated trolls of Grobb would welcome a traveler of similar 'ideals' to their own (many refer to both of our races as being evil, but moral values are relative, of course). Would you, kind and knowledgable Moog, please clear the air between the Trollish guards and myself!
