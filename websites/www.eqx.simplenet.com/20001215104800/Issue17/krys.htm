ISSUE # 17Apr. 11th - Apr. 17th

Krysthanir's Return

"Die, evil beast!" Raylend Kinsar raised his sword and stared into the gaping maw of the thirty foot long snake poised in front of him. Sweat dripped from his brow, blurring his vision slightly, but the knowledge that this was a life or death situation kept him focused on the serpent. Its teeth were razor sharp, and its scaly skin rippled as it slithered its way ever closer. For a brief instant, his thoughts went to how he came to find himself in this situation. It had been not two moons ago that he arrived in Arendakor, a relatively small town on the banks of a winding river at the center of Antonica. It was no coincidence that the river was named The Serpent, and ran through a mountain range named Serpent's Spine. Raylend needed only pause in the Rusty Ale tavern for a moment before he overheard the townsfolk voicing their worries. A ragged, bearded man sat at a table with a smaller, balding man. "Aye, if that slithering abomination comes near my house, you can bet it will have my axe buried in its skull," he boasted. "If it doesn't get you first," his companion retorted. Raylend noticed a hesitation in their voices that betrayed their fear.

Tale by Wynn Orill.
