ISSUE # 113

February 12th - February 18th, 2000


To the End


Out in the blackness, with the moon in full flight
Cast your gaze skyward;
hear the stars' plight.
The west stars are passive,
the east stars love fight,
The north shines alone dear,
and I fear the light.
You've taken my heart dear,
beyond the night's reach,
You've taken my soul dear,
Alas, I have need of each.

Out in the blackness, with the mist swirling white,
Cast your faith outward;
feel the fog's might.
The haze knows its power,
the murk is twilight,
My soul wanders out there,
and my love I recite.
You've stolen my life dear,
hid where I dare not go,
You've touched deep my heart dear,
Can you feel my love flow?

Out in the blackness, where wind seems to freeze
Let your soul free now;
let it ride the breeze.
The wind howls for freedom,
It screams only wheeze.
the crisp air blows truth dear,
I hear a mere tease.
My soul rides the winds dear,
beyond the world's breach,
It searches for yours dear,
On the clouds, on the beach.

Down on the seashore, where the waters foam white,
Hang your head over;
hear the winds flight.
I asked you to dance dear,
right from the start.
I wanted your love dear,
with all of my heart.
Oft times you hurt me,
and oft times we fought.
But if you should leave me,
My life, will been for naught.

Under the mountains, where dwarves often call
Throw your hands upward,
hear my love's call.
Your smile warms me full dear,
Heals me from the fall
Your eyes sparkle life dear,
I love you all.
I'll fight to the end dear,
For you go through hell,
I'll not let you down dear,
I love, Camrielle.
So cast your gaze skyward' hear the stars' plight
Cast your gaze skyward; hear the stars' plight.

    Tale by Bouldershoulder Thunderpuncher
