ISSUE # 89

August 28th - September 3rd


Wedding Song for t'Ceira and Darvinn

From beneath the free city
comes a dark maiden of elven beauty
She walks in the shadows with such grace
an impish smile she keeps on her face

She met an ogre warrior
that made his way past her heart's barrier
She fell in love with his soul
for countless ages foretold

But another also loved the maiden
a human wizard whose expressions harden
He would not accept that she'd love another
his jealousy pushed him even further

He cursed the ogre and the elf
so for all time, in sickness and health
Never would they touch, embrace or kiss
so their physical emotions she would miss

But their love for each other, their gentle stare
Their eyes held the flame that always would flare
She would not give the wizard what he wants
for her heart belongs to a man he couldn't taunt

Today their love grows even more
As they search for a cure among the lore
As they exchange their vows and their rings....
At this wedding I wish you both all the happiness the gods can bring.

"Your love is deep. How could we ignore it? We should all help you find the way to finally hold each other."

Written, sung, and spoken at the wedding of t'Ceira and Darvinn Wrathchild by;

    Tale by Lisanarathalasa Feanor'Silverleaf The Wandering Bard
