Yvette's Tower - Part 2

"You know, that will kill you," Yvette answered in a serious manner.

Marissa swallowed the wine hard. "What? What are you talking about? The wine?" Marissa gave Yvette a stony glare. "You aren't trying to tell me how to live are you?" 		Yvette lowered her voice, and glanced at the sailors who had looked up to listen, and then turned back to Marissa. "We learn how to live by learning what makes us die, you know that, just that your teacher calls it something different." She turned to face the ocean. "We examine the dead for many reasons, much like those who heal the wounded we must understand what makes a mortal what he is. Haven't you ever wondered?" Yvette stared into the black water below them. Sabrina cleared her throat. "I have wondered about that, and we were taught that the magics behind mortality were mirrored in nature. We examined the aura from a nymphs tree bark and compared it to the weaving aura around an enchanted shield. The same can be said about our own skin, and life's blood as well." Sabrina looked at Yvette who was still looking into the water, but more likely seeing only memories. "Are you ok?"
