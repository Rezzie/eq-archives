ISSUE # 106

December 25th - December 31st


THE BALLAD OF THE TASTY BARD


The group was joined, five stalwarts all,
A cleric and a wizard tall,
Two paladins for close melee.
What's that? Oh yeah. The bard was me.

To Blackburrow our leader led.
Down ramps, round turns, our leader sped.
This speed, thought I, is passing rash.
He zigged, I zagged. Look OUT! Ker-SPLASH.

The water here is deep and cold.
At least there are no pesky gnolls
My tender skin to rip and slash.
Yee-OUCH! Some FISH just bit my ash!

Drag, drag, drag my corpse, slowly back to shore.
Rejoice, rearm, rejoin my group. I'm ready for some more.

Now follow me! our leader said.
Down ramps, cross spans, our leader sped.
You know, this speed's not so appalling.
Oops! Spoke too soon. Now here's me falling.

Ker-SPLASH. Again I've hit the drink.
Which way is out? I've got to think.
But wait, I see no fish to rend me.
I can relax, need not defend me.

Ah, here's a ledge. My search is ended.
Uh-oh, this ledge is gnoll-defended.
Quick. Jump back in the water cold.
Yee-OUCH! What's this? Aquatic gnolls?

Drag, drag, drag my corpse, slowly back to shore.
Rejoice, rearm, rejoin my group. Let's even up the score.

Beneath the bridge my party stands
Their weapons ready in their hands.
I sing my song and then start strumming.
Our leader gives a shout, "Incoming!"

All at once, there's gnolls aplenty.
Maybe ten or maybe twenty.
I think our leader's gone insane.
He went and pulled a freaking train!

Gnoll commander, elite guardsman,
Patrolling gnoll, and gnoll shaman.
This fight's a riot, not a melee!
Hey! How come these gnolls all pick on me?

Loot, loot, loot my corpse. This story must be told.
Return, rearm, rejoin my group. The price of being bold.

Beneath the bridge my party fights,
Headlong, I rush to aid their plight.
They're only fighting three or four...
By Bristle's balls! Here come some more!

My blood is spilling on the ground.
Can it be wise to stick around?
"RUN," someone yells, "without delay!"
That's fine, I think, but - uh - which way?

Blackburrow is a twisty maze
Of tunnels and dark narrow ways.
Headlong, I race around a bend.
Headlong... into a dark DEAD end.

Loot, loot, loot my corpse. It's really getting old.
Repent, rearm, rejoin my group. I'm tired of being bold.

'Neath the bridge my party paces,
Eager smiles upon their faces.
While I've respawned they've had to wait.
You can't pull gnolls without the bait.

This time, though, you can be certain
With death no more will I be flirtin'.
This time I've learned the fastest route
Through Blackburrow for getting OUT!

So now I know my way around,
My corpses shall not ere be found.
As sure as my name's Kianel,
When things get tough, I'll run like HELL!

    Tale by Kianel of Qeynos
