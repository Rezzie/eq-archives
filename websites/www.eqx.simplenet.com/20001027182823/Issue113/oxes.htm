Hello hummie, me is Dawnrose. Sum call me da rose of Grobb, udders call me da weed. But me don't mind, a rose by any udder name stil tastes as gud.

But me is very upset about sumting diferent.

Me a gud troll, me makes tings scared so Cazic luk down an be happy wit me. Wen me was a small troll me cut out one eye an gav it to Cazic so him be always seeing da tings me be bashin! In return he givs me all dis nice majik dat maks me his bestest troll!

Well me was in da swamp wen dis small blue frogie runs up an says hello. Well dis da first tim a frogie talk to me (well dey do say Frooooook a lot but me not kno wot dat means) so I think, wot would Cazic do?

Ah! Me casts da big bad mojo to posion da blue froggie. But my majik dont do anyting! Da froggie den tell me dat hes not a froggie hes a dark elf and dat Innoruuk protects him from my majik.

Well me tinks dat Innoruuk is stoopid and me tells him dat, den me shouts it so dat all da trolls in da swamp could agree.

Dis wer tings get not gud. Da udder trolls, dey say dat Cazic is not as gud as Innoruuk! Yes, udder green skinned trolls! Me started shouting nasty tings at these not-real-trolls. Den me ran home to Grobb to tell da udder shamans wot des fake trolls been saying.

<dis bit may upset delicat trolls so don't listen if you is one>

Here da biggest troll shaman tells me dats rit! Innoruuk better dan Cazic Thule! Oh me very angry an me about to bash da shaman but me remember dat me promise in blud never to bash udder trolls. Well me left in disgust crying at dis betrayal.

Why me so upset? Becos wen Cazuic learns wot his trolls are doing he goiing to be very angry. First he be bashing dat weakling Innoruuk den he com and teech da trolls whos da bestest god.

An dats not going to be nice, so dats why I got my stuf from da bank an moved away. Me ben visiting da hummies in Qeynos now, bashing da nasty gnolls. But me miss da swamp…. Me wants to go home an hear the froggies go Frooook and taste nice gud green swamp water.

But most me sad cos Cazicis is da dady of da trolls, he maks us from da nice green stuf in da swamp. Him is da bestest!

So me wants to kno wot to do? Wen can I go home?

Dawnrose
