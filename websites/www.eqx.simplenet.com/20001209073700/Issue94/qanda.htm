Q:  What's the diffrence between the PvP and the PvP teams server? I would like to create a character on one but I need to know the diffrence first, which one tends to have more Role-Playing?
Silimar the 3rdHigh elf, City of Felwithe, Brell Serilis Server
A:  Essentially I think you will see a lot of roleplaying whichever pvp route you choose to follow, especially if you are roleplaying yourself. The pvp server, Rallos Zek, is the most established as it has been in the game since early in release. This is a server where any player can kill or attempt to kill another other player, regardless of race or class. It is not totally a free-for-all and basic rules are enforced by the GMs and guides. The pvp teams servers, Tallon Zek and Rallon Zek, were created for pvp roleplay and smiting by race. Thus, when you create a character there, you will find you automatically are part of a team with two other races. As part of that team you cannot kill any other member of it, i.e. a half elf may not kill either a wood elf or a high elf. However, you may kill any other race in Norrath and you'll probably find it healthy to band together with those of your team for protection and for adventure. These servers also have a basic set of rules, different from either Rallos Zek or the non-pvp servers, which must be followed in game play. I can't honestly say which of these servers has more roleplay so I'd suggest you try them out and see what you think!

Q:  In a previous issue, a young Tier'Dal was asking for a good date locale. After much thought, I'd have to suggest Dangor's Cauldron, particularly at night. It's a bit tough to get to as you'll need to get through Freeport and Butcherblock, but I think it would be worth the trip.

You have cold, majestic peaks overlooking deep blue waters, with only a few adventurers to distract you. If you wish solitude, head to the east side near (but not too near) the Orc camps, or if you would prefer a spectacle, mount the eastern peaks overlooking the Goblin camp. There will be a steady trickle of traffic moving to and from Unrest, and the occasional party harrassing the Goblin encampment. If you are patient, such parties almost inevitably meet their end when one of the Aqua Goblin Lords bestir themselves to patrol the area. You may also find it amusing to watch clumsy or unwary travelers hurl themselves to their deaths as they run off of the cliffs, particularly after "blessing" them with the boon of a Spirit of the Wolf or other speed-enhancing enchantment.
Troas
A:  Ah, it sounds like you have spent many a romantic night there and certainly understand what will make a dark elf's heart flutter and then beat erratically and wildly. Thank you for your advice.
