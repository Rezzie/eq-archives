ISSUE # 24June 1st - June 5th

Yvette's Tower - Part 3

First Mate Holfanon let out a defiant yell as he plummeted into the chilling waters with the remaining crew of the cargo ship, with only a sliver of a moon shining on the midnight waters. Most of them would try to swim to shore, seeing the city lanterns as a point of refuge from the pirates who now had their ship. The rest of the passengers were tied up below deck. Last to be thrown below deck was Yvette, and she hit the hard floor with a smack and bloodied her nose. "I told you," the rogue Warrior Captain yelled after her, "women should not be involved with war." He laughed as he closed and blocked the door. There was no light below deck, only the sounds of the five captives breathing and struggling against the ropes. For the first time since they left Erud the frilled older Erudite man spoke. "Th- they took my box," he murmured. "I need to get that box back." Yvette could hear him searching about, and the others seemed to stop and listen as well.

"Aren't you tied up as well?" Marissa asked, struggling against her ropes. "What was in the box?" Sabrina added, "Money?"

Tale by Ian Ash, Fraterni Lux.
