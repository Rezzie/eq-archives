ISSUE # 143

 September 30th - October 6th, 2000

A Murder in Misty

Each day is a mad rush to invent a new way to die. Many days our kind succeed.

The group of us hovered in the bushes, watching the road. Things were silent as they always seemed to be on the difficult nights. Many of us made ourselves busy by tending to the flora of the area, but I was much too restless to do anything but pace.

Something moved. Everyone turned and looked at the shrubbery and within seconds a giant came bounding out of the shrubbery. His eyes alight with madness, an enormous weapon hefted in his mailed hands, he began to attack us.

    Tale by Archanalia


All for the Lack of a Dress!

The trolls are growing wings and the pixies hairy toes,
Firiona is chasing Fippy, and in Qeynos anything goes.
The world is topsy-turvy, and an ever growing mess
And so you want to know the reason? The lack of a dress!

The dragons are crooning lovesongs and inviting you to tea,
The skeletons are growing skin these days, just like you and me
The sea is full of cornstalks and halflings all confess
That the world is going crazy because Nep wants a dress

    Poem by Nepenthia
