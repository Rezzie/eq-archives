Evil Games

The dark elf prophet Saphyre and her friend Lapys sit at the edge of the giant slave pit playing one of their favorite games.

"Okay... that's six points for me, Lapys... and you have seven points," she says. The slaves at the bottom moan and yell at the two dark elves above, too high up to reach. Lapys spits another watermellon seed, but misses. "Now its my turn... I am gonna go for Obert the dwarf, his left leg," says Saphyre. The dark elf maiden takes her slingshot and shoots a pebble at the dwarf who tries to dodge her attack.

    Tale by Saphyre

Editorial - Absor

EverQuest was not designed to be a place of business. It was built to be an entertainment that makes money for those providing the entertainment. In the same way that sporting events and concerts are designed to be a simple transaction, the customer pays the entertainers so that they will entertain. Somehow someone threw a monkey wrench into the machinery. For sports events it's scalpers (and in a small way the ticket agencies), gathering up tickets and reselling them to the audience at inflated prices. In EverQuest, it's the eBay sellers.

    Editorial by Absor
