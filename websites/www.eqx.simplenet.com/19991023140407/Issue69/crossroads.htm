ISSUE # 69

April 10th - April 16th


Song of the Crossroads

Amidst the stony Butcherblock Range
T'ween Kaladim royal and fair Felwithe
A road foreboding and passing strange
Wends it's frightful gore strewn way

Many brave men, a woeful sight
Broken and beat about the valley
Their fate t'was sealed by ogre might
Rapacious Corflunk and grave Zarchoomi

Whilst many another other folk
Wouldst stay at home abed aloof
Four stepped forth as strong as oak
From neath the cities sheltering roof

To challenge the reign of foul death
Brave Bascome and Koranek joined
With Legdien and Remido both
To rid the path of life purloined

Not without a share of daunting
Did the fellows go a'hunting
Ogres have a way of pouncing
To make you prey in ways surprising

But lo upon the road they 'peared
Corflunk first and then Zarchoomi
Huge hammer blows as back they reared
Rained upon us monumentally

By taunt and tease we tested them
By song and steel we bested them
By heart and soul we rested then
For by and by they'll rise again

Yet even through eternal worry
A bard in truth can sing the story
For when in Norrath heroes foray
Anyone might rise to glory

About the author:
      Remido was born in Qeynos of a human mother and a elven father. Both were bards and they travelled Antonica together. At an early age, Remido was sent to Kelethin to be raised by his father's family. A few years ago, he received word that his parents had been killed on the road by anti-elven humans. Remido now prepares himself to continue his family heritage as entertainer, historian and social critic.

    Article by Remido
