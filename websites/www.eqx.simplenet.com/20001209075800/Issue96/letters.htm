I play on Karana. Sorry for the long wait for a reply, but I wanted to think about what I wrote back to you. Sometimes it's more about how you act or roleplay than anything else. I was playing a funny character and wasn't asking for anything or doing anything in a begging fashion. Attitude is probably most of it. I ran across someone who ws emptying their bank account of "junk" - misc bronze and stuff. I bought a few (for 5pp each!) and was given the rest.

Mostly don't let the jerks get you down. I find that I have a better time if I leave monsters as soon as they are camped, and don't get involved in interactions with bad players. You'll find if you have fun, Role-play a little, and don't sweat the small stuff it goes ok. Seek out good players and play with them.

Good luck!
Oghma
