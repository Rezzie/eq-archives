One or two things...

It's 2 o'clock in the morning, my eyelids are so damn heavy that I can barely keep my eyes open with the help of a pair of matchsticks ,and I still can't get enough of EQ stuff (that is reading EQX and EQSS).

It all began about 5 years ago, when a fourteen year old kid picked up the first book of the Fellowship of the Ring trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien. This is when I started to get into the fantasy and sci-fi stuff. From then on most of the time of my somewhat meaningless life I spend fantasizing and dreaming up my adventures in imaginary worlds. And here is where EQ steps in.

EverQuest may be the answer to my dreams. Although I did not get the chance to play any of the betas (result of an inability to meet the requirements with my current excuse for a computer), I am impatiently awaiting the time I will set up my newly bought PC equipment (which is probably 4 or 5 months into the future), install the software and log on into the world of EverQuest.

This letter may seem pretty pathetic (actually, I know it is) but I just had to give my inspiration a go. I've been devouring any news about EQ for almost a month now (three weeks and 4 days to be accurate) and still can't get enough of it. I would give worlds to play the beta and see what's it like, to experience it. But I think I will have to settle for the screenshots and the info I can get from you guys, at least for those couple of months that is. I sure am trying to hook my friend up with an idea of him owning the "ultimate experience of fantasy" starting from March 16 since he has much more better hardware than myself.

Overall I wanted to congratulate you all on doing a great job of keeping goofs like me informed about this particular and wonderful event (sniff, sniff, I always got all emotional at the endings : ). Thank you for reading this (a slim chance as it is...) and for making all this possible (sniff). I hope I will see you all in Norrath when my time comes.

(furiously waving and sniffing)

Kufel

P.S. Looking forward to all new tales of brave (and not so brave) adventures to more detailed information about the game itself.
