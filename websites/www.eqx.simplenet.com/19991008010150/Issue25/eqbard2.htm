Bards in Everquest

Since we don't have a list of songs that the EQ bard can learn I won't travel too far with a song list. But from recent posts on EQvault and Wynn Orill's excellent article in Issue 24, we can look at some possibilities. Songs will likely be like spells: once found, a bard can learn them and scribe them in his/her "songbook". S/he can then memorize them for singing (which Wynn reports takes about 40 seconds) and use mana to sing (cast) them. Once sung, a song would need to be re-memorized. This system is pretty unrealistic for what bards were really capable of (one Irish bard could sing over 500 songs and tell over 2000 stories, verbatim, each time, some stories taking over a week to tell), it is likely a game balance issue. Perhaps a high Intelligence would allow a bard or mage to memorize faster. I very much like the idea of songs, like spells, being searched out. While you will learn certain songs at level gain, the truly wonderful/interesting ones will be hard to find, rumors being tracked down, fierce guardians defeated. This gives the EQ team a chance to continue expanding the song/spells list helping them balance a class with more songs, or weaken it by releasing useless/highly specific songs/spells.

What determines a song's effects, besides the nature of the song? The skill of the bard. Everquest lists some of the skills a bard uses on their site: 		· Singing
Bards (one can at least hope) have great singing voices. And the better they are at this art, the more effective their songs, which are themselves often magical in nature.
· Stringed Instruments
Certain Bard songs require certain instruments, and the better the minstrel is at playing Stringed Instruments, the more effective he or she will be at playing those songs.
· Wind Instruments
Certain Bard songs require certain instruments, and the better the minstrel is at playing Wind Instruments, the more effective he or she will be at playing those songs.
