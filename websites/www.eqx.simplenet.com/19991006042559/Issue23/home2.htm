Home Alone

I have an opinion of what I look for in a game. Keep in mind taste in games could differ from yours.

Plot / Story

I like a good plot. I don’t like to run around mindlessly through a virtual world with no purpose. I like to pickup clues as to why I’m there as I play along. I also tend to enjoy either being in the future on sometime during the days of King Arthur. I like a story that just keeps getting better and better the longer you play. My god, when I was descending into the depths of Diablo my stomach was in knots. I feared death at every corner of darkness I uncovered. 		Items

I like tons and tons of items. "Named" items. Remember that Sword of Ancient you found. The one you would kill yourself if you ever lost it. That sword you had to fight fifteen hours in a dungeon, almost loosing your life, to get? 		Creatures with Superior AI

It needs to have creatures that have uniqueness from each other. A Creature that can follow you around corners, up stairs and other obstacles. I hate when a creature gets stuck behind a wall allowing you to shoot it without effort. And for God sakes, I hate when you’re aggressed by fifty thousand enemies in one room. Come on. How about one or two creatures in one room that are hard as hell to defeat, and chase your ass all over the level, possibly leading you into more creatures.
