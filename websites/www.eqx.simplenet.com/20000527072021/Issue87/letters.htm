My reply:

The obvious answer is to be a bard! At level 5 you can go almost anywhere you want on Norrath. With your drum equipped and playing Selo's beat, you can outrun griffons and giants and travel to any place you want to see. Bards also are quite challenging to play once you get some higher level skills and songs. We have the dual wield of a warrior but the spell effects of casters, and we don't have to wait for mana.

Did you know bards can eventually track? charm and direct their pets? Also as a half-elf you can look great in plate. Spend a few hours and get a bard to level 5 and see if you can cure your wanderlust.
