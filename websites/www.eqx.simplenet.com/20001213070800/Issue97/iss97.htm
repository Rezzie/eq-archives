We Are Our Stories

I've begun my life as a scholar in the past few months spurred on by the activities of my beloved Ezmend. A Tier'dal born into the current world can find waiting for him or her little in the way of assistance toward becoming a functioning member of the underground society. There are no Tier'dal schools, only apprenticeships; Their helpfulness to the student varying widely with the skill of the teacher. There are no epics to read and understand and model ones self after; Instead we have those sermons spoken aloud in church but which are not carried far. Few Tier'dal faithfully attend church these days. Me included.

    Tale by Snowvayn


Auctions 101

I've had some experience running auctions, and I wanted to share some of the things I've learned. It's often better to be organized in an auction than to be constantly spamming the auction channel with twelve items in a single message, along with every other auctioneer in the zone. A good auction will get higher prices for the people selling, and more items out for those interested in buying.

    Article by Razor


Event Procedures

The description came from a friend of mine that, as I just recently discovered, is a guide, and participated as an NPC in the event. There were several interesting things that this guide mentioned in his re-telling of the event that got me thinking about what, exactly, does it take to make an event work in EverQuest...
