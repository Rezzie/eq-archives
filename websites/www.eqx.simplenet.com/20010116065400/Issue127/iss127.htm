The Pen is Mightier than the Cursor

I’ve been on a long hiatus from online RPGs. It has lasted coming close to six months now. Ill log on to EQ once every few weeks or so just to play a nasty Dark Elf cleric and keep looking for something I could have sworn was there. New developments in my personal life have a good deal to do with my absence but an underlying dissatisfaction is equally to blame. I really haven’t run into an online game lately that makes, what I consider to be role playing easy or even really gives it much support. Of course, lately, I’ve been playing those huge commercial MMORPGs such as EQ.

    Article by Russell Linton

A Different Kind of Evil

The cry for help reaches the young gnome’s ears as he stands alone upon the searing heat of the midnight oasis desert. Quickly, his small and wiry body makes a 180 degree spin, clouds of dust rising from the ground as his eyes lock upon the waylaid. He staggers backward, toppling onto the azure sands and painfully scraping his wrist. He watches as an elf, bronze armor glistening dark gold in the moonlight, do battle with a lumbering sand giant, grim visage intent upon slaying the smaller inferior elf. His small and nimble mind whirls, searching the chaos that is his thoughts for some way to help the elf.

    Tale by Bouldershoulder

The Rescue

Igag woke wearily in a place vaguely familiar, his sword lying in front of him on the ground. As he grabbed his sword and felt the comfort of its grip, he saw the bottles of kalish, mead, ale, brandy and wine line one wall behind a counter. He knew he was in a familiar place… except… a strange red haze was everywhere. The air was putrid as he gasped for a breath. He grabbed a bottle of Brandy and took a swig when he heard strange sounds behind him, he turned suddenly and thrust his sword totally by instinct.

    Tale by Igag

Letters to the Editors

Ahtenret's letter questioning the lack of consequences of "roleplaying" may sound reasonable, but does not make any more sense than Greyfreth's perspective.

Say it were possible for a DE SK to "renounce" his faith. If his powers could be so easily stripped, why even start out as a DE SK in the first place? It would save the player a lot of time and effort to simply start out as a paladin.
