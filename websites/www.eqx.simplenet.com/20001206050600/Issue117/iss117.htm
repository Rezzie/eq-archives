Common to Barbarian

This guide was designed to introduce you to the rudimentary's of the Barbarian's native tongue. Like many things in this world climate and society has a grand impact over time not only on our person but on our languages as well. Despite the Erudin scholar Shietzar's firm assertions that Barbarians are most likely a different race entirely and cannot even remotely be considered cousin to humans, there can be no doubt that their language is firmly rooted in our own Common tongue.

    Tale by Onnie

Editorial - Oghma

Well, I took a few weeks off EverQuest and EQX. I had a big run with Posy and sort of hit a rut, taking her from 6th to 28th and then kind of flaming out. Today I logged in for the first time in ages, and started where I left off, fighting out by the Split Paw Teeth again. Now, some of my previous friends were a few levels higher than I was, so I couldn't group with them, but I found a group right away and got half a level in about 4 hours. It was as if I had never left.

    Editorial by Oghma
