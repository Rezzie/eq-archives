ISSUE # 26June 13th - June 19th

EverQuest vs Asheron's Call

Intangibles

It's all fine and good that there are these games to play, but what is really going to happen with them? EverQuest is backed by a huge company with lots of resources and experience. Even though SISA changed their name to a number (what's next, Prince?), you can bet Sony has strict standards of quality. They know they have a potential landslide here with EverQuest, and they will be sure to do their best to keep it that way in the long term as well. There is no doubt EQ will be a winner in the end. 		Asheron's Call is also associated with a huge company with lots of resources and experience. But, the company is named Microsoft. How many times has your Windows crashed? Hmmm. What happened to Bill Gates at his big debut of Windows 98? Hmmm. I can only guess that AC realizes they have a short term game here, and will be trying to push it out the door before EQ sees the light. They will hope to get some addicts before the players have a chance to compare it to EverQuest.

Advantage: EverQuest 		Summary

Asheron's Call: A nice pat on the back and thanks for playing.

EverQuest: The new heavyweight champion of the world!
