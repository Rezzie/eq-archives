No Experience Required

"What exactly are you typing?" his voice started to crack with anger.

"RUN" I snapped back. What a total idiot I was dealing with.

"You’re just typing R U N. Right?"

"Yea. Open quote R U N close quote." 		There was a long pause and a short chuckle in the background. After a brief moment a voice comes back on the phone.

"Um.. Just type RUN without the quotes." More laughter.

I hung up the phone. 		I then focused my attention on the program, which I might add was then running perfectly. The screen read the opening comments and comments about the author. At the bottom of the screen was a flashing "Hit any key to continue".

After about five minutes of scanning over the keyboard I shouted "Any key? Where the heck is that".

I called Radio Shack.
