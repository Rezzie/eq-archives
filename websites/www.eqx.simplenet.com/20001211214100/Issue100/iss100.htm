A Reader Poll

In a few weeks EverQuest Express will be two. Yup, our lil' baby will be two years old. And we thought that for Issue 104 we should do a best of issue. This serves a few purposes, really. First, it gives new readers a chance to see what we've done that people think is worth reading, without forcing them to plow through a hundred issues to find it. It also serves as a chance for long time readers to re-read some of the stuff that they liked. And, maybe more importantly, it means a sort of vacation for the staff. They don't have to pound their heads on their keyboards trying to be clever for that week.

Editorial - Oghma

During a recent visit home my father gave me a slim volume of philosophy entitled Finite and Infinite Games: A vision of Life as Play and Possibility, by James P. Carse (Ballantine, 1986). In it are a list of principles of what the author believes are the two kinds of games that exist, and rules and conclusions about them. I was so struck by these and their application to EverQuest and our styles of play that I decided that it would be perfect for our 100th Issue...
