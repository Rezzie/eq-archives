Bards in Everquest

Some races which have that sort of tradition would be the high and wood elves, dwarves, humans, and erudites. Gnomes, halflings and dark elves have the necessary long culture or history, but the nature of those races may preclude a bard class evolving. Dark elves tend to be power seekers and grabbers, and anything not directly related to the obtaining of future power may be disdained. Halflings and gnomes may be too flighty to do more than write and perform songs, the gnomes are less so than halflings. Trolls and ogres don't have much in the way of culture. Barbarians are very likely to have bards, since although the culture may be pre-literate, the Celtic bards relied on memory alone and did not write down any of their lore, yet were highly developed as a class. One nice thing about the Bard class description is that EverQuest did not make their bards glorified thieves, as some RPG's have done. Bards could be roguish, but those who were not completely dedicated to their art were more often warrior-types than they were thieves. Bards will hopefully not get the bad reputation that evolved from those RPG's. My namesake, Oghma, the Celtic God of Poetry and Eloquence was also the God of Strength and Wrestling. (Which is why Forgotten Realms emphasized his "Binder" aspect). It will also be nice to be able to defend oneself without an instrument :). In summary, it looks as if a bard is a warrior/mage type in everquest, with more emphasise on song as a magical tool than on the skills of the warrior. I hope that those who choose to play one feel the urge to play it to the hilt, and add a rich lore to the history that Sony has been leaking to us, bit by bit. I wonder if I'll be able to dual-wield? :)
