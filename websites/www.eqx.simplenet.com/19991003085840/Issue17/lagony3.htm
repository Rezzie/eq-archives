ISSUE # 17Apr. 11th - Apr. 17th

Lagony: A Fable

And could be heard the clatter of armor against stone walls. And they knew that the dwarf was dead. In fact, the Sony gods credited Archanalia with this great victory in one-on-one combat.

Archanalia collected the Dwarf’s goods, but found no magic supplies among them. How then did he do such witchery?

Later A&A returned to town. There they stood in the courtyard and listened to the minstrels play while Absor contemplated his epic poem "Archanalia’s Butt and The Tossing of Dwarves". When suddenly something bumped against Absor. He turned to see a young girl moving away with his magical lute. 		Absor shouted, "Stop! Thief!" and the minstrels stopped playing and the crowd turned to watch as Archanalia tackled the thief.

"I would have had the pipes and been away if not for your cursed demonic lag spells!"

"We are not laggerts."

The girl turned to the crowd and shouted "Laggers!! They use Lag!!" Whereupon the crowd picked up stones and began to shower the two, causing much pain.

"That’s enough for me for the night," said Archanalia. 		"Me too," said Absor. And both disappeared within split seconds of each other, thus escaping death by seconds.

"Loggers!! Cursed loggers!!" The crowd cried.

Hearing these cries, just up the road a group of lumberjacks made a sharp u-turn in their carriage and headed for another town where their kind is welcome.

Fable by Archanalia.
