The Spirit Caller
Chapter 7
STRING DRAWN AND ARROW NOCKED

Obsession is the only way I can describe my desire to re-create the circumstances of my father’s death. Since my near demise in the clutches of bad weather, the Spirit of Wolves has dogged my steps. After learning from my mother that the Wolf Spirit appeared on the day of my father’s death, I became convinced that she bore some unhealthy interest in my family. This notion eventually led me to the Surefall Glade, where I hoped to make more sense of my involvement with the spirit.

So far my efforts had been in vain. No one left alive knows why Robert Ross went to Surefall, when his family was imperiled by plague. My father’s death still seems to be the work of illness.

    Tale by Daniel Madryga

The Price of Harsh Words

Lamia had been staring at it for quite some time. She couldn't tell how long it had been, and she didn't care. It hypnotized her, the delicate cuts; someone had spent a long time on it. The blood was still fresh. She leaned closer and smelled. The wonderful smell of death filled her nostrils, oh yes this had been done recently. It was a corpse, or what was left of one. The eyes were gouged out, the tongue removed from the mouth, the throat slit, and the arms and legs were nowhere to be found. It was the remains of what appeared to be a priest of life. But what made this particular carcass so appealing was the incisions made on the chest. It was writing, carved into the rotting flesh.

    Tale by Dystopias

Editorial - Absor

I’ve had reason recently to think back on the bad things that can happen to us in our lifetimes.

I’m not going to list the things that I regret in my life, not because you might not be entertained by them, but because I just don’t want to air all that in public.

But earlier this week I received a call from a good friend. We talk briefly about how we wasted our money placing football bets the week before. Then he tells me that a friend of ours was attacked by her ex-husband.

Letters to the Editors

About Hassyrtiko's question regarding calculating the birthdate and hour of his EQ character - based on what I know about how the time and date are handled in the game, gleaned from many discussions with knowledgable players on Brell Serilis and from my (and my husband's) own observations, what he's looking for would be very difficult.

Firstly, time does indeed stop whenever the servers go down. So, any program to do this calculation would have to have included a database of all server outages, including how long they lasted.
