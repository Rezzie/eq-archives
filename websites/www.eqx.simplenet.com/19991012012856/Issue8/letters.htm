Letter from Silverlock

 I like the new sites "front page" much better than the old one. Please keep the newspaper look when doing any changes in the future. My idea of "newspaper look" is one that is simple, easy on the eye, and very accessible. One that looks like it may have been run off an old mechanical printing press, or at least holds with the philosophy of simplicity and good taste. I think the new EQX form fits the bill nicely.

The Everquest title on the old form, the swirly squishy wiggly thingy, whatever it is called, just does not carry the idea that this is a newspaper. I guess I am saying it was out of character to use a rpg term. And the big colorful type that varied in size and font was a bit much too. I'm glad to see both gone from the new front page. Good job!

In the forum it was said that this project might be carried over to an in-game newspaper. I think this is a wonderful Idea and hope to see it done.
