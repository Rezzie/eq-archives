ISSUE # 126

 May 27th - June 2nd, 2000


Commentary - Bard's Log

Wow… 50 pages of a comic that I thought wouldn’t be any good. That’s right. Bard’s Log’s first pages were somewhat of a “pilot” I sent it in to see how the reader would like it. I had no idea how popular my mixed up comic would become! Mixed up may be an understatement, writing Bard’s Log was quite the roller coaster ride, if you noticed no inconsistencies in the plot, I must be the master of improvisation! ^_^

But where to begin… the characters I suppose.

Kyodai: The name is from the Batman cartoon. Kyodai was Bruce Wayne’s ninja adversary. What does this have to do with a bard? Nothing, Kyodai was the name of my monk, then my ranger, and it stuck with my Bard. His character development was rather shaky. He started off as loving Lanys, then wanting nothing to do with her (to the point where I wrote him as the self serving, selfish type). Once I had an idea of where I was going, he flowed a lot better. His heroics later are sort of a redeeming factor.

Gargatox: The big hilarious troll that doesn’t get much on time because he takes up a lot of room (No joke!). With every good hero comes a dumb troll to lighten the mood. Garg is the only character that I’m happy with. He’s simple, hides nothing, and says what’s on his mind, even if it is just “Eet bugz”.

Lanys: Another shaky character, she went from Evil Princess to cute, fun-loving girlfriend pretty quickly. She’s probably the smartest of the three. She’s some sort of mage/wizard/necro combo, but classes really don’t matter in this comic.

The Guard and the Guard Who Loses His Helmet: ‘Nuff said really. Just guards. Why do I include them here? I don’t know, they were fun to work with, especially the one that keeps his helmet. He’s sarcastic, and probably a bit disgruntled with his job.

Innoruuk: Ah the God of Hate. He actually was gonna play a big role in this. Too bad for him. ^_^

Dorn B’dynn: I actually had somebody ask who he was. I didn’t think anybody didn’t know. For those who don’t, Dorn is the scourge of North Ro. I put him in there because my bard was able to kite him to death at level 12 (and we haven’t been nerfed yet! GO BARDS)

The Warrior Lady with the Scar: Just a filler character, expect to see her sometime later, though ;)

The Charmed Froglok: Also originally had a lot to do in the plot. Keep your eyes peeled.

Damos B’vrnn and the Smiley Faced Orcs: I liked these guys. Damos was my scary, incompetent general who ran around with a band of orcs. Yes, he only has one arm. I have no idea what spawned that, actually. He too originally was going to play a huge part in the comic.

Donkey Face and the Crazy Troll: Just more filler characters. Probably knew Garg at some time or another.

Auntie Moog: I loved Auntie Moog. I was saddened to see her go, and this was my tribute. I personally like the “AUNTIE MOOG!” intro she gets whenever she appears.

The Gnomes: They’re only in there because I liked the way “Kiss the gnome, Lanys! Kiss the gnome!” sounded. Really! :D

Garg in the Deputy Mask: Fans loved it, I loved it, Garg at his best.

Mooto and Fatty: The Runny Nosed Goblins. Mooto is an actual goblin that spawns in Misty Thicket. Fatty is my own creation, always wanted to know what a fat goblin looked like.

The Nosy Evil Eye: He got his own interview, that’s enough for him.

Fury T’vyl and Zeruel N’tok: Fury is Lanys’ jealous older sister. I thought the long-range morning star thing was cool. Wish I had more time to do cooler stuff with that. Zeruel was supposed to be the kind of handsome bad guy that you wanted to hate but couldn’t because of his charm… that didn’t happen. Time constraints made it hard to develop characters like him. His name comes from one of the Angels in Neon Genesis Evangelion. Expect to see more of Zeruel.

Qeynos Guards: I haven’t been to Qeynos in a while so I had no idea what their guards looked like. Oh well. All I can remember are those goofy shields.

The Council: Cameo’s galore. I love cameos, there are a lot in Bard’s Log, but you’ll probably never catch them.
The Cleric: Just a high elf, thought it fitting.
The Magician: Slayn from Record of Loddoss War
The Enchanter: Didn’t get any lines because I don’t like enchanters, haha.
The Necro: Scull, an old character of mine.
The Shaman: Just some crazy barb. I should’ve made it Oxie…
The Druid: I love doggies…
The Wizzie: I love Lil’ Archy even more. He will be sorely missed (sob)

Other fun things:
-Kyodai’s clothes are based on what my Bard on Rodcet Nife is wearing (Banded)
-Zeruel’s clothes and weapon choice are heavily based on that of Kenshin from Rurouni Kenshin
-Lanys’ party dress is based on the one Deedlit wears in Record of Lodoss War. Kyodai’s tux is totally original.
-Lanys, despite being a caster, uses magic twice in the whole comic.
-Kyodai, despite being a bard, sings twice.
-Gargatox, despite being a shaman, never casts anything.

Well, I hope you sorta enjoyed my rambling. I’ll probably think of more stuff to write later, hahahahahahaha… nah. Anyway, I’m taking a break for a bit. Don’t worry, Bard’s Log is gonna keep going for a while!

    Commentary by Maki "Sir Maximus" Naro
