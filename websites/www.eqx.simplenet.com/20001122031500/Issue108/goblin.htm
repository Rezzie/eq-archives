ISSUE # 108

January 8th - January 14th, 2000


Goblin Drums


Goblin drums, goblin drums beat loudly,
Rousing us from our sleep.
Goblin drums, goblin drums pound doo-oom
To our homes and our keep.
So we rise, so we rise to confront the foe,
Wanting peace but daring war.
Goblin drums, goblin drums must be silenced
Forevermore.

Dwarven hearts, dwarven hearts beat strongly
With the life of our land.
Dwarven hearts, dwarven hearts draw courage
>From the strength of our band.
So we march, so we march with eyes lifted high
To the bright light of our star.
Goblin drums, goblin drums must be silenced
Forevermore.

And the blades of our swords glisten
Keenly in the clean light
Of the moon,
So bright, so bright.

Goblin drums, goblin drums like thunder
Echo loud in the night.
Goblin drums, goblin drums beat danger
As we gird for the fight.
So we go, so we go with a steady tread,
Marching grimly off to war.
Goblin drums, goblin drums must be silenced
Forevermore.

Dwarven hearts, dwarven hearts like drum beats
Pound so loud in our chests.
Dwarven hearts, dwarven hearts beat faster,
No more thought now of rest.
So we strike, so we strike in a mighty wave
'Neath the cold light of our star.
Goblin drums, goblin drums must be silenced
Forevermore.

And their blood on our swords glistens
Blackly in the bleak light
Of the moon,
So bright, so bright.

Goblin drums, goblin drums fall silent,
No more sound do they make.
Dwarven hearts, dwarven hearts sleep soundly
No more drums to awake.
So we come, so we come to our hearth and home,
Marching gladly back from war.
Goblin drums, goblin drums will be silent
Forevermore.
Goblin drums, goblin drums will be silent
Forevermore.

    Tale by Kianel of Qeynos
