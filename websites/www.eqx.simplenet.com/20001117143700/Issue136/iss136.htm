 August 12th - August 18th, 2000

Midnight
Part III

It was dark. Pitch dark. A blackness so deep and sorrowful… How long had he lain here? How long had he slept? Gods, it felt like ages.

He tried to take a deep breath to calm himself. He felt his mouth open, but nothing else. No air flowed in to fill his lungs. His stomach clenched as his throat desperately struggled to contract.

In a panic, he thrust his arms out, only to meet resistance.

    Tale by Qestlian

The Spirit Caller
Chapter 5
FIGHT OR FLIGHT

I left Halas with turmoil at my back and a miasma of unanswered questions ahead. Little did I realize that this departure would set the tone for my life. There would always be trouble at my heels and shadowy menace ahead.

Those uncertain days also gave me a glimpse of the meaning behind the strange visions that came to me on death’s door. When one of the people from my fevered dreams stepped forth and unmasked himself in the light of day, I was struck with a profound revelation. As I languished in death’s clutches, my life did not flash before my eyes. It was my future that I saw.

    Tale by Daniel Madryga

Where’s Archy?

Online gaming is dead!

Okay, did I get your attention with that incredulous lie? In high school, my writing teachers always told me that the most important part of the story was the opening, because if you didn’t get people’s attention, they’d never read it.

It’s no surprise to anyone who has read my articles that sometimes I write to express an idea, but most of the time I’m writing as therapy. I spill all the thoughts I have about life and “the game” onto the paper in an attempt to gather a little perspective. This is definitely an article about ME.

Editorial - Oghma

A while back I mentioned the Karana split and my thoughts on the issue. This week finds us looking at the issue again. It appears that some GMs helped their guild move en masse to the new server from another server. Verant has researched it and has issued the appropriate punishments to its wayward staff members. The issue isn’t that it was wrong for a guild to get preferential treatment. Preferential treatment would not have happened if there was a guild moving mechanism for server moves to be effective. It appears more than ever that a guild moving mechanism is needed.
