Religion in EQTNG

EQ has a good pantheon of Gods, and the magic system takes into account the ‘mana’ theory of religions. I’d like to look at some things that could change about religions which would hopefully not be huge changes, yet make religion interesting, worthwhile, and useful in a game sense. I want to make religion matter for the NON-cleric.

Religion is primarily based on fear - fear of the unknown, of the things that can’t be explained or anticipated, or of death or destiny. Religion was created by man to attempt to control and appease those forces that appear to be affecting mankind.

    Article by Oghma
