Letters to the Editors

Well I just finished reading the latest Issue of EQX and I might add it is the best issue yet. The one story by Razor "The ultimate geeking experience" certainly amused me as I sat here and though "LOSER" but then I realized that my experience was almost identical during the Realm 3.x beta. I work night slight so I'd wake up at about 1pm and then get pissed off when the server was still down for updates uttering some comment about incompetent programmers then heading off to work. While at work my mind would be solely concentrated on what new thing I would try out tonight and what changes they should make. When I finally got home I'd turn on my P200mmx with 64 RAM loggin, then marvel at the brilliance of the programmers for the updates. Then I would play for hours always pushing my sleep back. Then I'd find some bug that would cause a wipe, cus and swear for a while about incompetent programmers and fall into a three hour coma dreaming about the next night of gaming. This continues until two or three months after the release then I decide to lay-off Realm and console my wife for a while, little does she know I've found another pet called EverQuest and I've already got my storage of 100 2L bottles of Pepsi waiting for that magic e-mail.
