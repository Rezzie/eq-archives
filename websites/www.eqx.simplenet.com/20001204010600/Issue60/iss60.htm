The Creation of a Sinner

A long while ago, Oghma wrote a pair of articles about creating a character for a roleplaying game. One was called Author, Author! How to write a character biography, in which he gives some very good tips about creating a character biography as a tool for playing your character. In The Nuts and Bolts: telling others about your character, he not only proved his penchant for long titles, but he tells us how to express your character background to the rest of the players in the game without just flat out reading it to them. If you haven’t read them, I highly recommend them.
