SimpleNet Resources: Promote Us

Much of our business comes from customers who refer us to their friends, family, and associates. We've created several ways for you to get the word out. There is no better way to choose a web host than to have someone you know make a recommendation, therefore we appreciate your referrals!

SimpleNet Referral Program

It's never been any easier to save money than now!

The SimpleNet Referral Program allows you to earn credit towards your current account balance by referring your family, friends and associates to SimpleNet. For each new web site account that is referred to SimpleNet, you will receive one FREE month of service on your existing account (see below for more details)! The more new accounts that you refer, the more FREE months you will receive.

SimpleNet Shared Hosting Referrals
After the referred account completes the sign up process, they must visit the "Referral Program" page of their Control Panel (under the Billing menu) and input the referring account name into the field provided.

IMPORTANT PROGRAM INFORMATION

  • Referred accounts must be active and in good standing for one month before credit is applied to your account.
  • A confirmation email will be sent to the primary email address of the referring account.
  • Credit will be for one month's base fee of your account, up to $35.00.
  • Failure to enter proper domain name in referral field will negate all claims to referring party for free service.
  • This is a limited time offer and may be revoked at anytime without notice.
  • Referred "Domain Only" and "Starter" accounts are not eligible for this offer.
  • Credit will be applied within 6-8 weeks.

SimpleNet Dedicated Server Referrals
Just make sure your SimpleNet account name (yourdomain.com) is entered into the referral field on the order form whenever someone signs up for their new account.

IMPORTANT PROGRAM INFORMATION

  • Referrals must sign up for a Dedicated Server account.
  • Accounts will be credited within 48 hours of new account sign up.
  • A confirmation email will be sent to the primary email address of the referring account.
  • Credit will be equal to your current monthly charges or $250.00 (whichever is less).
  • Credit card charge-backs will negate benefits of referral.
  • Failure to enter proper domain name in referral field at the time of sign up will negate all claims to referring party for free service.
  • This is a limited time offer and may be revoked at anytime without notice.
