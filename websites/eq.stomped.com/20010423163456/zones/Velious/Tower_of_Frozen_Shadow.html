    Tower of Frozen Shadow

    Level Range:  30 to 45
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:
    • Tower of Frozen Shadow Bestiary
    • Tower of Frozen Shadow Equipment Drop List
    • Tower of Frozen Shadow Quest List
    • Tower of Frozen Shadow Strategy Discussions
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to the Tower of Frozen Shadow
    Description:   Located just off of the Icy Fingers, this is a huge, multi-story tower.  The tower consists of at least seven floors, and in order to advance to the next floor, you need to get a key from a boss mob on the previous floor.  These keys are not soulbound, so if you die in an upper floor, you will have to fight your way back up to your corpse, or tag along with a party that has a key.  There is a lot of very nice loot in this tower, especially for its levels.

    Level 1:  You zone into a small room, with a larger room in front. There is a larger room beyond that, with a slippery floor, and a pit with a few shadowbones.  This floor mostly holds shadowbone skeletons and other undead, which are all around level 30 in range.  The key to the second floor (known as the crystal key) is found on "A Large Undead Gnoll" who seems to spawn after enough skeletons are slain.

    Level 2:  This is the Library.  As you approach the portal in the back of the slippery room (the one to the right) a distant voice says, 'Please, you must turn back. They no longer know, they no longer recognize..' Its triggered as a trap (not disarmable) and "respawns" every minute or so. Use the crystal key you
    got from the gnoll and gather your party at the portal. when everyone is close enough to be off the stairs (heh, shrink helps here) person holding the key clicks, and you all pop in. (Keys are no drop.)  This is a very large room, with bookcases, "a ghostly student", "a ghostly scholar", "Librarian", "a ghostly teacher" and "Librarian assistant".  Killing enough spawns the Angry Librarian, who holds the key to the next level.  In the very back of the room, there is a hall with a portal at the end of it. The key from the librarian opens this. Dont forget to gather your party before you go there.  The Mobs here seems to range from 30 to about 34.

    Level 3:  This is the kitchen/staff level.  There are skeleton maids and butlers, shadowbeast cooks, an angry cook and an enraged cook, as well as skeleton sleepers and armored shadows.  The enraged cook drops the key you need to get to level 4.  Mobs here range from the low 30's up to around level 40.

    Level 4:  The Crypt area.  This area consists of shadows and various mummies, and looks like an Egyptian crypt.  The key to level 5 is on a named witch.  She will spawn after killing the named mummy.

    Level 5:  There is a wedding going on here.  Feel free to crash the party.  Several nice items are dropped by members of the wedding party.  The Mob levels seem to range from the mid 30's to the mid 40's.  The key to level 6 drops off of an Enraged Relative.

    Level 6:  The level is full of guardians.  The key to level 7 is dropped by a Djinn named Vhal'Sera..

    Level 7:  This level consists of a a large room plus a set of stairs to a mirrored room.  The boss of the tower is named Tserrina.  She resides here. All but one of the mirrors will teleport you outside.  One of them is locked, and is supposed to be the way to level 8.

    Level 8:  This is the top level of the Tower.  Nobody has figured out how to get here yet.
