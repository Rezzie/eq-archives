    Everfrost Peaks

    Level Range:      1 - 40
    Resources:

    • Everfrost Bestiary
    • Everfrost Equipment List
    • Everfrost Quest List
    • Everfrost Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map  and Guide to Everfrost

    Description:   The Barbarian newbie zone.  Cold and snowy, and home to many creatures of the snow and frost.  Also the entrance to the mid to high level dungeon Permafrost.   If you go down the ice hole and then swim north under the ice (use water breathing), you will come to a ice goblin diver guarding the entrance to an underground cavern.  It is relatively empty, but is probably going to be an entrance to one of the planes someday.  There are a couple or low level ice goblin divers guarding at various places, a room with 3 ice bone skels which has runes all over the place (which I am told are are Erudinite markings), and another room with 2 ice bone skels and a level 45 plus necromance named Sulon McMoor around some round platform, where I would assume you will eventually be able to gate to a plane.  This is the cavern where you get one of the ice comet rods.
