The Emerald Jungle
Level Range:  30 - 50
Typical Creatures:  Tigers, Gorillas, Giant Leeches, Man eating Flowers.
NPC's and Quests:
Connections:  Trakanon's Teeth; Field of Bones; City of Mist
Description:   There is a large cliff that drops into a thick jungle full of the types of creatures you might expect in a Norrath jungle.   The vegetation here may be more deadly than the animal life.
