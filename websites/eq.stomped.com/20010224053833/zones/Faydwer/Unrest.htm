    The Estate of Unrest

    Level Range:  20 - 40
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Unrest Bestiary List
    • Unrest Equipment List
    • Unrest Quest List
    • Unrest Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to the Estate of Unrest
    Description:   A clerics dream dungeon, this is the home to a large population of undead.  It starts out fairly safe, and gets harder as you work your way towards the center.  The outside of the house has monsters ranging from 13-18 or so.  The inside of the house has monsters ranging from about 20-25, and the basement of the house has monsters ranging from about 28-32.  Very popular dungeon, especially for parties with one or more clerics.  Groups tend to hunt here in two areas.  Out in the yard, you can stay near the wall and send a runner into the mansion to lure out Mobs for the party to kill.  Inside, there are a number of rooms that can be camped by a strong party.  Be aware that this dungeon is well known for large, dangerous trains.  It is not unusual for someone to pull the entire mansion out into the yard to run around and slaughter everyone there.
