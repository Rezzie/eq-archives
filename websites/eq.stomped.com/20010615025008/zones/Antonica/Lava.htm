    Lavastorm Mountains

    Level Range:      12 - 20
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Lavastorm Bestiary
    • Lavastorm Equipment List
    • Lavastorm Quest List
    • Lavastorm Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide of Lavastorm

    Description:   This zone is one of the more underutilized zones in the game.  It is a rugged, jagged rocky area with large and small pools of burning lava, along with many steam vents.  The steam vents won't hurt you.  The lava pools will.  This zone seems to have been designed with pure spellcasters (i.e. Enchanters, Magicians, Necromancers, and Wizards) in mind.  Most of the beasts in this zone will have noticably fewer hit points than similar level beasts in other zones.  Which means that they don't take as long to kill.  They make up for this lack by having either offensive spells or fire shields (or both).  While these fire shields may be a Monk's worst nightmare, for a spellcaster (all of whom have Cancel Magic by 12th level) it's pretty easy to remove these shields.  The spells cast by these beasts are not too potent, and while they're casting their spells, you don't have to worry about YOUR spells being interrupted.  Finally, only the Fire Elementals are aggressive.  Everything else will leave you alone if you leave it alone, and even if you attack one, others won't join in (except for Fire Imps).   As if that weren't enough (and you'd certainly think it would be), the monsters drop gemstones half the time as loot, as well as dropping upwards of 4 gold and change.  Depending on your race, you may be able to get better prices for your loot at the Gypsy Merchant camp in the zone than you could get at the nearest friendly town.  (For Erudites this is definitely true.)  The best places to hunt from are:
    1) Right at the entrance to the zone as you leave the pass and enter
    the zone proper.
    2) Near the entrance to Najena.
    3) The Northeast and Northwest corners of the zone.
    The biggest danger in this zone is getting jumped by multiple fire elementals, which are everywhere.  If you can avoid that, this is a great zone to stay in while working from 12th to 16th level.  After 16th, it's still a good place to get loot but you won't find many things to give you XP.  One final note.  When it rains many of the fire-based creatures seem to head for shelter and the hunting drops off significantly.  (Thanks to Desdinova for scouting Lavastorm)
