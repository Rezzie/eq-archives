The Arena
Level Range:     Variable
Typical Creatures:  Other Players.  There are no NPCs in this zone.
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   This zone holds a very large PvP arena.  There is a circular
track around the arena that is non-PvP, so you can enter the zone safely without fear of being jumped.  The entrance to the PvP arena is on the opposite side of the arena from the entrance to the zone proper.  So you won't be considered PvP unless you enter the zone and then take the trouble to run halfway around the circular track and THEN step into the arena. This zone is treated like a city zone for purposes of binding, and this is a good place for melee and hybrid characters to get bound if they're hunting in South Karana or in the Rathe Mountains.  And, obviously, it's the perfect place to get bound if you're hunting in Lake Rathe.
