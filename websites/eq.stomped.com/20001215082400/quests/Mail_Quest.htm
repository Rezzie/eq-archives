Going Postal

"I am interested"
"I will deliver mail to ..."

This quest covers every town in Norrath, as well as some non-town areas.  Listed below are all the towns and contacts I know of.  Kelethin, Freeport, Highhold, and Qeynos are international mail hubs. Delivering mail between hubs will always result in carrying a mailbag. All other mail origination sites are satellite postal offices and feed into the nearest major hub.  The reward varies (at whim) between 1gp and 12gp, with values in the middle more usual. With a couple of deliveries, low-level characters could get a few platinum easy.  Bards with Selo's Accelerando are really in good shape, as some of these routes can be done in only a few minutes.  The quest is sponsored by the League of Antonican Bards. Faction hits are League of Antonican Bards, Knights of Truth, and Guards of Qeynos get better, RingofScale and Mayong Mistmoore get worse. So remember, if you see a wood elf bard slouching in the shadows in your home town (and it's not Kelethin), it's just the local postal worker.

The numbers after the City name refer to the general level of critters in the area, so you know not to stop at all. Take care that you don't stop in difficult places like Steamfont, until you KNOW you are safe. Pay attention to what the NPC's say. In Freeport, Felisity is for letters, and Ton is for Mailbags (in general - it has been said that there is an error on these, btw.) but there are times when you will simply get a Thank You.

Also check a few other quests to see if they are going your way. For example, there is a Qeynos/South Karana Lute quest, that goes both ways.
