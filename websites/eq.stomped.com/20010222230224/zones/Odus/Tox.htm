    Toxxulia Forest

    Level Range:    1 - 8
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Tox Bestiary
    • Tox Equipment List
    • Tox Quest List
    • Tox Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to Toxxulia Forest

    Description:   Erudite newbie zone.  As an Erudite, you generally only want to only hunt here for a few levels before taking the boat to Qeynos for a better and safer hunting zone.  This is basically broken into two areas separated by a bridge.  On the near side of the bridge are the typical newbie monsters, while over the bridge you can find two kobald camps, skeletons and briar snakes.  Also across the bridge, you will find elementals, which are about level 20 and can see through invisibility, making this area very dangerous for lower levels.  And you will occasionally find a Heretic Prophet.  Because of the elementals and the prophet, it is very dangerous to wander over the bridge if you are less than level 20 or so. This is also where you find the entrance to Paineel, home of the Heretics and The Hole.  The bridge itself is no longer a safe zone.  There is a troll NPC named Rungupp who wanders the shore yelling obscenities at the Erudites.  He is at least level 6 and very tough to kill without a group since he casts blindness on his attackers.  Be wary of several poachers who wander into the newbie part of the forest and kill unsuspecting newbies.  To reach Kerra you need to swim or take a boat straight out from the pier.
