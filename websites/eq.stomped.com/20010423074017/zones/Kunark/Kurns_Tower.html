    Kurn's Tower

    Level Range:  10 - 25
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Kurn's Tower Bestiary
    • Kurn's Tower Equipment List
    • Kurn's Tower Quest List
    • Kurn's Tower Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to Kurn's Tower

    Description:   A low level dungeon located in the midst of the Field of Bones.  It is mostly full of undead and burynai.  This is basically the next step up for the Iksar after they get tired of fighting in the field of bones.  There is some nice loot in this dungeon for the level, including fine steel weapons.  You enter on what is pretty much the main floor, has mostly greater skeletons and a few scalebones.  This can be hunted even below level 10. There are 3 levels above the main level, with the Dining Room on the top level.   The upper floors have nothing but lesser icebones, greater skeletons, greater scalebones, a few lesser charbones, and a few other various similar level skeletons.  From the floor you zone into, if you go right, you will find some stairs leading down. This leads to an area that has Burynai excavaters and burrowers. There are another 2 floors with a similar make up below this. They are in short just a square corridor with rooms on each side. In the stables, there are 2 wandering spawns that are placeholders for all of the following: Forager, Fingerboned,and Thick Boned.  Near the other end of the satables is a placeholder for the elusive Undead Jester.  On the lowest floor, one of these rooms has a hole in the back wall, you can enter this hole, and there is a very long drop, enough to do 150 to 230 damage to some one with out safe fall. Once you fall down there is a cave and tunnel area with Burynai excavaters, sappers, and burrows, and a few skeletons.  There is also anther zone down here, which simply takes you back to the Field of Bone, right at the entrance to Kurn's Tower.  Anyone looking for the Forager, Finger Bone, Jester or Thick Bone will need to LIVE in the stable levels and be prepared for a LONG wait - the placeholder wanders and can be ANY creature found in Kurns, from a scalebone up to a lesser charbone, and any Buryani except Bargrynn.  In addition, there are 4 potential locations for the mob to spawn - but in a controlable area.
