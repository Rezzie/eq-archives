    EQ Links  


    General Everquest Links

    Affiliate Pages(Become an Affiliate)
     

    Mythiran Tower The definitive resource for EQ research information.  If you are a magic user and need to know how and when to research your new spells, this is the place to go.  It has lists of information for each class and several well used message boards where you can discuss your problems and concerns.

    Neriak.com For those who choose the path of the dark side, this is your site.  Written totally from the perspective of the evil races, this is where you can go to gather information, meet friends and swap tales with others of evil bent.
     
     

     Illia's Beastiary  A compilation of all of the Creatures and NPC's in the game, with information on where they spawn, what they drop, what level they are and more, collected into a fast and easy to use database.

    Hall of Fame Sites

    Everquest Home Page
        The Home Page for Everquest.  Now that the final release is here, they are doing a lot with this site.  Verrant keeps adding to this site, and it is starting to be a very useful resource.  There are now a whole set of message boards where you can occasionally get technical and other help from Sony employees.  They also post information about server downtime and potential changes to the game.  They promise to try to keep everyone informed through this site.

    Verrant's Official EQ Development Page
        Lists skills, spells, etc that are in development for the game.
    Verrant's Official Message Boards
        Definitely the most popular game related message boards.
    Verrant's Official EQ News Page
    Verrant's Technical Support Page
    Official Ruins of Kunark Website
        Check out what is happening on the upcoming expansion
    EverQuest Stratics
        The best resource for Everquest news.  There is a lot of information on this site.  It is kept up to date and updated several times each day.  The general news section is very useful for keeping up on game updates and events.  This is a very good place for playing and strategy guides, maps and other information. It also has a very popular message board which is frequented by some very knowledgable players who are likely to answer legitimate questions.

    EQ'Lizer
        Probably the best spoiler site out there.  Very good site with some excellent information on classes, races, areas and spells, zones, quests and equipment.  If you need information you can't find on this site, this is probably the first place you should go.

    Quester of Norrath (Japanese)
        This is definitely the best map site out there.  Unfortunately, it is mostly in Japanese. There is enough English to figure it out and the work is excellent.  Also, he has done an admirable job in trying to translate some of the information into English.

    Everlore
        This site keeps getting better and better and has grown into one of the more popular EQ sites.  A nicely designed site where you can add in your own information to the growing database.  This has a lot of good information.

    The Casters Realm
        Formerly known as Everquest Enchanters.  Originally devoted exclusively to enchanters, it now includes information for wizards, magicians, necromancers, druids, clerics, bards and shamen as well.  Very well done.  Nice site with a lot of good information and updated very regularly.

    EQ Atlas
        You may have noticed that most of my zones contain links to the EQ Atlas information.  This is one of the best sites to go for information and maps on what you can expect to find in each zone.
