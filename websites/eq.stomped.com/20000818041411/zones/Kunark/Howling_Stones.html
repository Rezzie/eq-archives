Howling Stones
Level Range:  45 - 60?
Typical Creatures:
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   This dungeon (which is also referred to in game as Charisis) requires a key to get in, which is obtained through a Swamp of No Hope Quest.  There are undead creatures right at the point where you zone in after using the key.  If you have invisibility vrs. undead, you should be OK if you just stand there and wait for the rest of your party to zone in as well.  A thief can pick the lock, but only he will get transported in, not the rest of the party.
