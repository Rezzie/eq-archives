Karnor's Castle
Level Range:  40 - 60
Typical Creatures:  Lots of Drolvarg's, decayed prisoners, skeletal guards, skeletal berserkers, Venril sathir, Skeletal Warlord and Drolvarg Warlord
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   This castle is located in the middle of the Dreadlands, making it convient to the teleport location and to Firiona Vie.  Of the various Kunark dungeons, this is one of the better in producing high level items, especially the class-specific items.  The items mostly seem to drop randomly, rather than from any particular spawn (which is true in much of Kunark).
