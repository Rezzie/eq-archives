Game Lore

There are various pieces of information to be found in the game that generally serve more as background than anything else.  They come in the form of books and scrolls and notes and other documents that can be found or purchased, or through long conversations with NPC's.  While mainly background, they are interesting, add depth to the game, and can be useful in figuring out quests and factions.  Below is a library of these stories.  If you have any texts or other game lore or other similar information, please e-mail them to me at Allakhazam@allakhazam.com to add to the list.
 

  • Before Green:  Description of the beginnings of the Iksar Race from the Iksar lore books in Cabilis.
  • The Blasphemere: The Story of Dag of the Forbidden Circle.  Obtained from the Forbidden Scribe in West Cabilis.
  • The Book of Turmoil:  The contents of this Book describe the beginning of the age of Turmoil.
  • Captain of the Sea:  The story of the Iksar Captain Vlzk Irontail, founder of the ironguard as described in the Iksar books of Lore in Cabilis.
  • The Crusades of 1312 a.g.:  The story of the founding of the Crusaders of Greenmist as described in the Iksar Books of Lore in Cabilis.
  • The First Ogre-Dwarven War:  The history of this war which lead to the founding of Oggok, as distributed by Verant during beta.
  • The Founding of Ak'Anon:  The history of the founding of the Gnomish Kingdom, as distributed by Verant during beta.
  • The Founding of Crushbone:  The story of the orcs in Faydwer and the founding of the crushbone empire.
  • The Genesis of Fist and Tail:  The story of the founding of the Iksar Monk's Guild from the Iksar Lore Books in Cabilis.
  • The History of Charasis:  A background on Charasis (The Howling Stones) from a tome looted from the Dark Elf Spy in Northern Ro.
  • The History of the Kerrans:  The history of the Kerran People as told by Falthrik Lothoro in Kerra Ridge.
  • The History of Norrath :  A basic background on the creation of the world copied from the official site.
  • The History of the Verishe Mal:  History of the Gnoll inhabitants of Splitpaw, taken from four scrolls that drop in the dungeon.
  • Miragul's Story :  The story of the founding of the Erudites and Heretics, and of Miragul, the world's foremost Necromancer taken from the official site.
  • The Story of the Ankexfen:  The story of the creation of the four goblin tribes
  • The Story of Edril:  How the Elven hero Edril discovered the Dragon Trakanon and learned of the end of the world, as distributed by Verant during beta.
  • The Story of The Hole:  The background on The Hole as told by Talloth Vara in Paineel.
  • Talon Southpaw:  The story of the greatest Iksar Monk as described in the Iksar lore books in Cabilis.
  • The Troll Migration:  The story of the Wars between the Troll People and the foundation of Grobb.
  • Venril the Chief:  The story of the famous Iksar Warrior Venril Sathir as descrived in the Iksar books of lore in Cabilis.
  • The War of the Broken Crown:  The Story of the War that broke the elves into three separate peoples, as distributed by Verant during beta.
