    Ocean of Tears

    Level Range:      10-30
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Ocean of Tears Bestiary
    • Ocean of Tears Equipment List
    • Ocean of Tears Quest List
    • Ocean of Tears Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to the Ocean of Tears

    Description:   The ocean you cross when travelling between Antonica and Faydwer.  Mostly water, but there are several islands with various nasties on them. Karean of Karana, the seafearing dwarf, scouted out this zone as follows:  Monsters by Island:  Erollisi Island: You will find 5-6 high level named NPC's near the dock area. There is also a Dwarven Blacksmith on the west side of the Island with one of his friends. Scattered on the island are some Sisters of Erollisi.  They wil sell to you and are involved in several quests.   Pirate Island: There is a single Pirate on an island right west of the the Erollisi Island.  He was red at level 17 and aggressive. One of the Mudtoe Ogres can sometimes be found here. On the Island there is also a shopkeeper selling Magician spells. Cyclops Island: North of the Pirate Island is an island filled with Sea Fury Cyclops. I didn't get near one because they coned red and were aggressives. There seems to be a circle of teleportation for druids on the island as well, but if I was a druid, I wouldn't want to teleport into the middle of 4 cyclops. On the north shore of the Island is a named Giant. I didn't approach him since I'm used to Oasis, but he seemed to be peaceful enough. The second Mudtoe Ogre spawns on this Island. Death Island: Very dangerous.  There are the standard four spectres guarding a spire at the top of this  rocky hill of an island.  There are also a series of spawn points where either greater skeletons or gargoyles appear.  The gargoyles are the same as  grinning gargoyles in Castle Mistmoore, and occasionally drop gargoyle eyes. The gargoyles, when left to their own, will destroy the skeletons, so if  you camp at this island, it's wise to leave with one gargoyle roaming, so when you return there will be all gargs and no skellies. Goblin Island: South of the pirate Island, this one is full of Isle Goblins. They range in levels from about 17 to 23. Beware. Also on the Island are 2 NPCs including an Oracle of some sort.  He is involved in a couple quests and also drops the oracle robe if killed. First Island from Kaladim to Freeport: This island is devoid of everything except an Inn and a Shopkeeper and is southwest of the Aviak Island. Aviak Island: An island full of parrot-looking aviak.  There are 4 types of aviaks.  Starlings are around level 10, Turacos are level 15ish, Tanagers are around level 20, and Quetzels are around level 25.
