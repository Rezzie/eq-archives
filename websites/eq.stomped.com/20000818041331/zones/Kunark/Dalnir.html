Dalnir
Level Range:  30 - 45
Typical Creatures:  Ravenous Nibblers; Coerced Erudites, Iksar, Dwarves, Tier'dal, and Gnomes, Smoldering Goo; Gyrating Goo, Kly (cohorts, followers, acolytes, evokers)
NPC's and Quests:   Dalnir Quests
Description:   The location to the cave entrance is in the nw corner of Warslik's Woods up the hill from some water.  Once you enter into the cave tunnel, it splits off a couple of times.  The tunnel that leads to the zone is filled with illusonary water, so you can just walk through it.  This is a huge maze, consisting of several levels.  There are notes to be found in various places in the zone that seem to indicate one huge quest involving the zone.  There are also some very nice bard items to be found here.  The second level is full of pit traps, so levitate is suggested.  In the Room next to the Overseer room at the Bottom of Dalnir is a Forge which is labeled "Always Works".  I'm not sure if this means you can use this and not risk a failure, but it seemed worth mentioning.
