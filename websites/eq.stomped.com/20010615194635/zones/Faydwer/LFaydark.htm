    Lesser Faydark
    Level Range:    5 to 40
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:
    • Lesser Faydark Bestiary
    • Lesser Faydark Equipment List
    • Lesser Faydark Quest List
    • Lesser Faydark Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to Lesser Faydark
    Description:   Mostly low to mid level area, with some high level monsters as well.  The entrance to the dungeon of Castle Mistmoore is found here.  If you kill enough Fae Drakes at their ruins near the entrance to Castle Mistmoor, sometimes an Emporer Fae Drake will show up, who is very high level and a magic user as well.  There are 2 bandit camps here that can give some good experience.  Both camps are also needed for quests.  The Nybright camp has four bandit sisters that are pretty tough (around level 10 - 15), and have the backstab skill.  If you attack one, they will all come after you unless you have lulled them, so you need a good group to hunt them.  They often drop good  loot and are needed for a nice quest, so they are camped quite frequently.  The other bandit camp is also a good place for loot, expecially bronze weapons, so it is also camped a lot.  Be careful in this zone.  There are a number of high level Mobs that wander around attacking at will.
