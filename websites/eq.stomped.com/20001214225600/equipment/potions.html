Many potions can be bought at vendors in most major cities.  Costs range from 10 to 15 pp for the minor potions to over 1000 pp for the rare ones.  These costs are significantly lower than when the game first came out, and make the use of potions for such things as healing, underwater breathing, etc. much more viable.  Other Potions can be made by shamen through the alchemy skill.  Some only drop from certain creatures.  Unless otherwise noted, all potions are expendable and disappear upon one use.  Also, all effects are temporary.
