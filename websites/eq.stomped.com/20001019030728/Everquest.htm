And the next epic quest to be solved is ... The Necromancer.  I know it was actually solved yesterday, but it seems fitting that I announce it on Friday the 13th (and only a few weeks from Halloween too).  Congratulations to Zrazzt D'ethshade for completing the Necromancer Particle Weapon Quest and getting the first Scythe of the Shadowed Soul.  I updated the quest to reflect the fact that it is solved, but I don't have the details yet.  By my count, that is 6 of the quests that are solved now.  You have to give Verant credit.  Considering how many people have pooled their resources and shared information on each of these quests, they have still posed quite a challenge to the community.
