    East Commonlands

    Level Range:     4 - 12
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • East Commonlands Bestiary
    • East Commonlands Equipment List
    • East Commonlands Quest List
    • East Commonlands Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to East Commonlands

    Description:   Next to the newbie zone of Freeport.  Also a pass through to the western part of the continent including Qeynos.  This zone connects to W Commonlands to the west, Freeport to the east, North Ro to the south and Nektulos Forest to the north.  Hunting areas include a couple of orc camps and a cemetery full of ghouls, zombies and skeletons.  The orc camps are often heavily camped, but if you are a high enough level, you can often hunt the ruins.  Monsters split mostly north and south of the main road.  South of the road, along the mountains are the undead such as zombies, skeletons and lesser mummies, as well as the undead ruins.  Beware of the occasional air elemental, which can only be hit with magic.  Also south are asps, known for dropping giant snake fangs and pumas, which are good hunting for lower levels.  North of the road are various animals such as young kodiaks, lions, giant spiders, giant scarabs and darkweed snakes (which drop giant snake fangs, but can cause poison that will drop a person pretty quickly).  The two orc camps are located north of the road.

     The biggest worry in this zone are the several griffins which periodically run around and slaughter everyone in sight.  The griffins generally patrol the area along the road leading from the first to second inn.  There are four inns which are referred to by the players as follows:  1) First Inn is the one closest to Freeport, 2) Second Inn is the next in down the road from the first inn, 3)  Third Inn is the one nearest the Western Commonland border, and 4) Fourth inn is the one along the mountain between the orc camps. The first and second inns both have guards stationed nearby for protection, but are sometimes visited by Griffins, especially the second inn.  Be very careful if you decide to meditate there.  Your best bet is to meditate next to the third inn, which rarely ever sees a griffin.  You can buy food at either the first or second inn, depending on which innkeepers are selling at the time.  If you can afford his prices, there is a good weapons merchant in the tunnel to N Ro.
