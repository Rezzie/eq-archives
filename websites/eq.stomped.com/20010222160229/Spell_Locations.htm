    ALL SPELLS' LOCATIONS

    BY YLDAL VARGHEDIN, BARBARIAN WARRIOR, WORLD OF INNORUUK




    Key:

    Ak = Ak Anon: Mage Guild, Cleric Guild and in the Mines of Malfunction.
    Bu = Butcherblock Druid Ring
    Er = Erudin: Deepwater Knights, Temple of Light, Mage Guilds and the Erudin Library
    Ev = Everfrost: Starn Bearjumper in Temple
    Fe = Felwithe Paladin, Cleric, Mage Guilds and Srell Tumblebrook in lone shop.
    Fr = Freeport: Hall of Truth, Temple of Marr and Academy of Arcane Science
    Fs = Freeport Necromancer and Shadowknight Guild, the Dismal Rage.
    Gr = Grobb Shaman and Shadowknight Guilds
    Ha = Halas Shaman Guild
    HK = The High Keep Library
    In = Innothule Sawmp: Troll Merchant on North Stone Hand
    Ka = Kaladim Cleric and Paladin Guilds
    Ke = Kelethin Druid and Ranger Guilds
    La = Lavastorm Druid Ring
    Oa = Oasis Gypsy Camp
    Og = Oggok Shaman and Shadowknight Guilds
    Qe = Qeynos: Hall of Sorcery, Herb Jar, Temple of Life and the Temple of the The Rainkeeper.
    Qs = Qeynos Sewers, the Shrine of Bertoluxxous.
    Ne = Neriak: Shadowknight, Necro, Cleric, Mage Guild and the Neriak Library.
    NK = North Karanas Druid Ring and Gypsy Camp
    Ri = Rivervale Druid and Cleric Guilds
    St = Steamfont Druid Ring
    Su = Surefall Glade Ranger and Druid Guilds
    To = Toxxulia Forest Necro Guild and Druid Ring
    WC = West Commons Druid Ring
    WK = West Karanas Barbarian Fishing Village
     

    Notes:

    1) Spells may exist in other classes guilds in the same zone. If you cannot locate a spell in your guild even though the guide says it exists in the zone, try another spell casting guild in that zone

    2) Freeport, Qeynos, Erudin, Neriak and Felwithe all contain more than 1 zone with caster guilds. All zones within these cities are listed as one.

    3) The spells that do not contain any zones are either only researable or no longer in the game.

    4) The guide does not list which classes get what spells. Nor does it explain how to research spells. The sole purpose for this guide is to show where to buy spells sold by vendors.

    5) This list does not contain songs for Bards.

    6) Send any updates or information to Wyskize@hotmail.com.
