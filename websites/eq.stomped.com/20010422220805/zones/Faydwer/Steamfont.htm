    Steamfont Mountains
    Level Range:    1 - 20
    Resources:
    • Steamfont Bestiary
    • Steamfont Equipment List
    • Steamfont Quest List
    • Steamfont Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to Steamfont
    Description:   Gnome newbie zone.  Hunting here consists of two kobald camps, plenty of wandering monsters and a mine full of minotaurs which are good hunting for those in their low teens.  The zone is basically divided by the windmills, with newbies up to level 10 or so able to safely hunt in the areas before the windmills, and characters above level 10 or so able to hunt after the windmills.  For level 4 to 6, there is a kobald camp way in the back corner that is often not camped even on busy nights.  You just have to be careful of the higher level Mobs you can encounter on the way there.  Higher level players often like to camp the gnome guards.  This means that you usually play your low level character here with no chance of help from the guards, so be prepared to run to the city or to the Lesser Faydark zone when you get in trouble.  The minotaur mines are a very good area to hunt for characters in their low teens.  Minotaurs give good xp and drop decent loot for their levels, and the Ebon Drakes and Harpies are also good hunting in this area.
