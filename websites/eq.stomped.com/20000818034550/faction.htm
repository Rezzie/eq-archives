Factions

    Faction is very important in the game.  Your faction with various groups affects the prices they offer in buying and selling and the availability of certain quests.  Also, if you have a low enough faction with a individual or group, you can often get attacked on site.  It is definitely to your advantage to work on developing a good faction with your individual guild, and to build up faction when you get to a new city.  For magic users, good faction can mean large savings on spells and the like.  The following tables list what the various faction groups are in Norrath, along with which monster kills affect faction, arranged alphabetically by both monster and group.
