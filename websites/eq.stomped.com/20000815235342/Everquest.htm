August 14, 2000

Multiple people have reported to me and on other eq sites that Najena has been changed (called Najena_alt1) on some servers (but not apparantly every server) and has new mobs which are dropping rubicite armor and enchanted fine steel weapons (same damage/delay as fine steel, but with stat adjustments).  The drops seem to be random off every Mob in Najena.  It has also already switched back to the old version on some, if not all of the servers.   It is still not clear it this is a limited GM event, or a permanant change to the zone.  There was a post by Brad McQuaid a while back that mentioned that they were looking into redoing Najena to make it a more useful dungeon, so it is possible that this will become permanant, but right now your guess on this is as good as mine.   I have also heard that the zone is a madhouse on the servers where the change took place.  Check it out at your own risk.
