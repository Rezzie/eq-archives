The Burning Woods
Level Range:  35 - 55
Typical Creatures:  Mountain Giants, Forest Giants, Wurms, Sarnaks, apes, hornets
NPC's and Quests:   Burning Woods Quest Section
Connections: Dreadlands; Skyfire Mountains; Chardok; Frontier Mountains
Description:   There is a Sarnak fortress known as Chardok in the middle of this zone, which is a high level dungeon.  There is also a Forest Giant city here.
