    Brewing Recipes  

    Brewing Recipes


    If you wish to contribute recipes for creating beverages, please e-mail me

    Note:  Tempers are used to create armor

    • Acorn Oil = Misty Acorn + Water Flask
    • Ale = barley + malt +  water + a cask.
    • Bleeding Brain = Vodka + Spice + Shotglass + Fire Beetle Eye
    • Blood Temper = Ogre Swill + Elf Blood + Human Blood + Dwarf Blood
    • Bog Juice = snake skales + water flask + bottle.
    • Brandy = 2 red wine + vegetables + spice + shotglass
    • Earthen Temper = Dwarven Ale + Lava Rock + Couple of Drops o Mercury
    • Elven wine = Morning dew + foraged berries + wine yeast + bottle
    • Embalming Fluid = Rubbing Alcohol + 2 bog juice + granite powder
    • Fish Wine = Water, Grapes, Fish, Bottle
    • Flask of Berry juice = 2 berries(foraged) + 1 flask of water  (produces 5)
    • Flask of Fruit juice = 2 Fruit(foraged) + 1 flask of water  (produces 5)
    • Flask of Karana Brown Tea = 2 tea leaves(foraged) + 1 flask of water
    • Frost Temper = Brandy + Ice Goblin Blood + 2 Essence of Winter
    • Ginesh = Vegetables (a druid or ranger can help here) + Water + Shotglass + Short of beer
    • Gnomish Spirits = Rice, Spider Legs, Rat Ears, Bottle
    • Gypsy Wine = Grapes + Wine Yeast + Firebeetle Eye + Bottle
    • Halas Heaters = Spiderlegs (not spiderling) + Vodka + cask -- yields 3
    • Halfling Stouter = Vodka + Spice + Shotglass + Berries (foraged)
    • Heady Kiola = 2 kiola sap + 1 water + 1 bottle  [this is used for tailoring skill]
    • Honey mead = hops + royal jelly (found on giant wasps) + water + cask
    • Hulgarsh = 2 Vodkas + Flask of Water + Fishing Bait + Cask
    • Kalish = foraged fruit + foraged veg + water + bottle
    • Legion Lager = bottle + giant blood sac + bloodwater (buyable) + deadbone barley.
    • Mead = Hops + Malt + Yeasts + a cask.
    • Moonlight Temper = Morning Dew + "swirling mists of dusk" + "moonlight of two nights"
    • Ogre Swill =  Cask + Froglock meat + Malt + Yeast.
    • Ol'Tujim's Fierce Brew = Barley + Hops + Yeast + Malt + Cask.  Combine, and remember not to stack the ingredients.
    • Red Wine = Grapes + Wine yeast + Foraged berries + Bottle
    • Royal Temper = Rain Water + Griffene's Blood + 2 drops of sunshine
    • Scale Temper = Vial of Sarnak Blood + Vial of Froglok Blood + Vial of Iksar Blood + flask of Blood Water
    • Sea Temper  =  2 Mugs of Sea Foam + Saltwater Seaweed + Bottle of FishWine
    • Shadow Temper = 1 neriak nectar + 1 elven blood + 2 essence of shadow
    • Short Ale = Barley + Hops + Water + a cask.
    • Short Beer = Barley + hops + malt + a cask.
    • Tumpy Tonic = 1 Kiola Nut + 1 Flask of water.  Combine in brew kettle. No cask needed!
    • Vodka = Water Flask + Vegetables + 2 Short Beers + Bottle.
    • Water flask = 4 pods of water(foraged) + 1 bottle
    • White Wine = Grapes + Wine yeast + Foraged fruit + Bottle
