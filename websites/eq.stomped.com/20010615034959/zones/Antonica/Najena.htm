    Najena

    Level Range:      15 - 35
    Experience Bonus:  The Creatures in this zone give an additional 13% experience.
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:

    • Najena Bestiary
    • Najena Equipment List
    • Najena Quest List
    • Najena Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to Najena

    Description:   Located off of Lavastorm, this is a zone where a group of goblins, ogres and dark elves have formed an alliance with the Tentacle Terrors, skeletons, and elementals which inhabit the dungeon.  This is one of the smaller dungeons in the game and this, combined with the slow spawn rate (everything in the dungeon has an 18.5 minute respawn time), means that the dungeon has difficulty supporting more than  approximately 15 people unless they're spread out over the level range listed above, or unless some of the folks are camping one particular spot.

    On the lower end of the scale, there are a number of large skeletons, greater  skeletons, and low-level Ogre guards near the front of the dungeon.  These will be good for parties of 10th-13th level characters.  Further back you encounter the Elementals, which are mostly high green to a 21st level character.  There are also four Tentacle Terrors near the middle of the dungeon in various spots that
    could prove to be quite a challenge to a low-level player.  (Hint: Some of these Tentacle Terrors are still blue to a 29th level character.)

    Near the spot where you first encounter the elementals is a doorway leading to a room with a fake wall.  Beyond this wall are some passages leading to darkboned skeletons (blue to a 24th level character), and, deeper down, to higher level ogre guards, ogre officers, the Ogre Captain, BoneCracker, and Trazdon. Note that ogre guards with the headbands are the toughest of the guards and many of them were blue to a 26th level character, long after the other ogre guards had gone
    green.

    If you keep going through the main cavern, you'll eventually come to a big archway with the word "Najena" engraved into it.  Beyond this archway are magicians, more ogres, two camps filled with goblin magicians, a couple giant black widow spiders, a group of jail cells being guarded by Moosh the Ogre, a couple of invisible floors leading to said cells, and, of course, the Big 3:  Rathyl, Drelzna, and
    Najena.

    This is an excellent dungeon for soloing between 23rd and 29th levels.  The level range above goes up to 35, but at that level you're pretty much restricted to soloing Najena herself, which makes the dungeon pretty useless for XP purposes.  If you have the dungeon all to yourself, you can probably still solo effectively up to 32nd level by taking on the Ogre Captain, the Ogre Officers, BoneCracker, Trazdon, and maybe Froglok Ghouls.  If there's anybody else in the dungeon your level and they're NOT camping Najena, you might as well go somewhere else if you're after XP. 18.5 minutes between kills is a long time to wait.

    If you're looking for loot, however, this is a spiff dungeon.  Here are some of the items available to the perserverant and the lucky:  Tentacle Whip, Flowing Black Robe, Ashenwood Short Spear, Stiletto of the Bloodclaw, Barbed Whip, Dark Circlet (for Crafted greaves), Terror Spines (for Totemic boots), the Ogre War Maul, and large bronze armor. (Dungeon scouted by Afterburner)
