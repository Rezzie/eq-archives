Packs and Bags

If you have an addition or correction to this list
or if you have an item image not included on the list
please e-mail me at allakhazam@allakhazam.com

Packs and bags fit in your inventory and allow you to carry addition items.  Many magical packs reduce the weight of the items stored in them.  There is often a size limit of small, medium or large to the items that can be placed in a particular pack.

A note on getting packs.  Many low level quests result in your getting a bag or box to collect items in.  These can easily double for a pack, and can be obtained by a level 1 player for nothing.  Just be careful not to hit the combine button if you use them.
