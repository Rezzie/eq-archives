    The Hole

    Level Range:    40 - 60
    Experience Bonus:  The Creatures in this zone give an additional 25% experience.
    AllaKhazam Site Resources:
    • The Hole Bestiary
    • The Hole Equipment List
    • The Hole Quest List
    • The Hole Strategy Discussion
    • EQ Atlas Map and Guide to The Hole
    Description:   The former home of the Heretics, which has been invaded by a horde of elementals.  This is a high level dungeon.  This is a very nice dungeon for Druids and others with spells aimed at summoned creatures, since the majority of the Mobs here are summoned. Don't jump in the hole located in Paineel.  You will receive 10,000 points of damage and the GM will not recover your corpse for you.  Instead, buy a key for about 100 pp from Superior Supplies shop, below the Poison Petal, next to the bank in Paineel.  Then go into the tunnel in the back of the newbie area in Paineel and go down it until you get to a shallow pool of water with a pile of rocks by it.  The key works on the rocks. If you can't get the key (for example if you are one of the many who are kos in Paineel), you can sqeeze though.  Look for the highest crack in the rock, and go up to the crack while holding forward,  looking a little into the rock, and keep hitting Duck. You will magically fit through the crack (watch the shins cause those things hurt when you scratch them).  You will have to hurry through the tunnel unless you have enduring breath. After zoned into the hole and now you surfaced for air you'll notice that there is a totem sticking out of the water.  That spot is safe. You can explore from there.
