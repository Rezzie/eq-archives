Chardok
Level Range:  45 - 60
Typical Creatures:  Sardoks, Golems
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   This zone is located in the Burning Wods.  The entrance to the zone is one way, and you have to travel around a path to get back out, so make sure you actually want in before entering.  The zone is home to a tribe of Sarnaks.  This is a very high level zone, so be careful going here without a good group.  The Sardoks include casters that can hit for a lot of damage.
