Lake Rathetear
Level Range:      10 - 25

Description:   A collection of various camps and towers make this a good hunting area.  There are very few wandering monsters, so you are pretty safe sitting and resting in most areas of the zone.  This is an excellent area to hunt for players in their mid teens to low 20's.  It has the major advantage of being right next to the arena zone, where binding is allowed.   The most popular hunting area is probably the gnoll camp near the Southern Karana zone which spawns darkbone skeletons (level 20 - 23) and various gnolls, including the gnoll embalmer who drops the skull of jen'tra,  an occasional high shaman wielding a runed totem staff, and undead gnolls at night.  Also popular are the various aviak towers around the lake.  The nice thing about these towers is that the aviaks,which are relatively easy to kill and their spawn is predictable, if a bit slow.  Aviak guards range from levels 17 to 20 and there are also regular aviaks that are in the low teens.  The bad thing about the towers is that they are almost always camped.  There is also a bandit camp and an orc camp that can be hunted.  Be aware that the lake is now full of deepwater goblins, who are a much higher level than the aqua goblins who used to frequent the lake.  Their home is a tower under the island in the center of the lake, but they also roam the rest of the zone. There is a tunnel under water which leads to the underwater tower and there are several chests in the tower, but I don't know what, if anything, is in them.
