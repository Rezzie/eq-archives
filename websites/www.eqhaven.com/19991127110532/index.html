 UPDATED PLAYER GUIDES  12:42PM PST    Fenaorn  
  This came in from Dragons:

The Syndicate is proud to announce a whole series of new and updated player guides on our website. From this page you can learn about various races, learn how to solve a number of the armor quests as well as learn about some of the harder zones and where to find the best items and armor. In addition, due to many requests for it, we have published our "PvP Ladder" and all of its rules and such so that other guilds who wish to implement one can use it as a basis for theirs. Take a look! There is alot of information there to absorb. If there is a particular guide you wish to see written, let us know and we'll get it put up for you.

Thanks Dragons!

  
 FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 19, 1999  
 A GUIDE TO ROLE-PLAYING IN ONLINE RPGS - PART I  3:26PM PST    Nebac  
  Thought this was worth posting. Fargo @ GameSpy cooked up a rather funny look at Roleplaying.. check it out
This week on GameSpy's "PlanetFargo" column, I've started part I of a
two-part series presenting a helpful guide to roleplaying in online RPGs.

"It may seem difficult at first to come up with a good fantasy RPG name, but
never fear. It's easier than you'd think. Throw in some extra apostrophes,
and use letter combinations you never see in real life -- lots of "ae"s, for
instance, or the liberal use of the letter Y to replace any vowels. This
handy chart should help:

Good Names: Bad Names:
Trynn K'tar Try'an HumpMe
Sir Hargrove Sir Mixalot
Tanniel the Grey Commander Taco"

There's more, including handy illustrations using such games as EverQuest,
Ultima Online, and my new addiction: Asheron's Call.
