EUROPEAN SERVER PETITION

 I don't even know how feesable a European server is however, I'm posting this petition to give the European players a chance to show their numbers and will to Verant. Non-US EQers, this is your chance to have a voice, show your support and signe the petition. In the guild slot put your country first. **Adendum:From Beowulfe Spiritlight

1.) There are not enough servers at the moment, anyone playing at prime time will see that upon logging in. Verant stated that Everquest was designed to handle 1000 players AT MAXIMUM per server. So any new servers are a good thing.

2.)
a.)You are playing from Europe: The lag and packet loss (pl) are just plain unbearable before night time. Most nights, a decent game before 1 am is not possible. High lag and pl means you get dropped by the server a lot (up to 3 times an hour if it goes bad), MOBs pop out of nowhere and attack you (like Hill Giants :o for example) Once you have established a reputation of having an unstable or laggy connection your will have a hard time finding a party.

b.)You are playing from the US: Do you really want lagged and unstable player connections? It is in your own interest, if there is a set of european servers. If your are beyond 20th level chances are good you have experienced what it means when a key party member (the healer for example) goes linkdead in a fight. And since you can't tell from where a person is playing this is quite a risk to bear when adventuring. The number of high-lag/pl players on YOUR server could be greatly reduced by introducing european servers.

Please vote for this petition, not just if you are actually playing from Europe. The benefits are numerous and Verant, backed by all that Sony muscle, should not have a hard time setting up some european infrastructure.
