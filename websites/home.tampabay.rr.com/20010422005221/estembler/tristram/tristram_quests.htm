Tandan Nybright
Tynkale says 'Hail noble <your name>. Can you be of [service to the Clerics of Tunare] or are you not from our order?'

You say 'I can be of service to the Clerics of Tunare.'

Tynkale says 'That is good. Prove yourself then. Are you [young to Felwithe] or have I been conversing with a [verteran of the good fight]?'

You say 'I am young to Felwithe.'

Tynkale says 'Then stand at attention when I speak. I want you to venture to Kelethin and seek out Tandan Nybright. He is an old member of ours. He had some hardships and he fell from Tunare's grace, yet he is still a member of our family. He will be the one who wreaks of Elven Wine. Greet him by name.  We are worried for him.'

Jojongua's Hide
Tynkale says 'Hail noble <your name>. Can you be of [service to the Clerics of Tunare] or are you not from our order?'

You say 'I can be of service to the Clerics of Tunare.'

Tynkale says 'That is good. Prove yourself then. Are you [young to Felwithe] or have I been conversing with a [verteran of the good fight]?'

You say 'I am a veteran of the good fight.'

Tynkale says 'I command you to venture to Innothule and seek a beast called Jojongua, a name penned by the Trolls. It is said to be ten feet tall and nothing but fury. Some say it resembles a kobold, but how can it be. You bring this beast Hide to me and then I will believe you to be a veteran. Beware. Innothule is a very dangerous place for a noble veteran.'

Destroy the Krag Chicks

Bumle Reminjar says 'Welcome <your name>. The Cathedral of Underfoot is welcome to all good persons. May you find peace from the dangers of Butcherblock within these walls. The paladins of this holy place are very much aware of the evils outside Kaladim. The threat of Goblins and the [Birdmen] have increased.'

You say 'What Birdmen?'

Bumle Reminjar says 'The Birdmen I speak of are the Aviak Krags. These vile creatures have desecrated our land. They have dared to perch upon our great statue. The King has instructed all noble paladins within this order to [destroy the Krag Chicks].'

You say 'I will destroy the Krag Chicks.'

Bumle Reminjar says 'Yes. You are know to have aided our cause. You shall continue by returning any Aviak Chick Talons to me. I will reward you for the return of no less than four. Go and serve the wills of the King.'
Battle the Undead

Gunlok Jure is found on the left side in front of the balcony in Underfoot Hall.

Gunlok Jure says 'Hail <your name>. Bow before the greatness of the Clerics of Underfoot. It is good to be a paladin of such an order as ours. To fight the good fight and defend Kaladim from the evil and undead. If you be a paladin then I pray you find the [courage to battle the undead].'

You say 'I have the courage to battle the undead.'

Gunlok Jure says 'Yes!! To battle the undead is our greatest call. There has been a rise in dwarven skeletons with Butcherblock. If you are a true member of this order then I shall reward you for the return of four bone chips. We shall defend our land from evil.'

Upon turning in four bone chips, this is what Gunlok Jure says:

Gunlok Jure says 'You have done well. We thank you for your deed with this humble reward. The power behind the raising of our dead shall soon be found. You will earn more respect with more bone chips. I only wish you could assist in the return of the [remains of Cromil].'

The reward was 3sp, 12cp plus a random item.  The faction hits were:  +ClericsofUnderfoot, +KazonStormhammer, +MinersGuild249.
Remains of Cromil
After turning in four bone chips to Gunlok Jure, for the Battle the Undead quest, this quest is given:

Gunlok Jure says 'You have done well. We thank you for your deed with this humble reward. The power behind the raising of our dead shall soon be found. You will earn more respect with more bone chips. I only wish you could assist in the return of the [remains of Cromil].'

You say 'What remains of Cromil?'

Gunlok Jure says 'My brother Cromil ventured to the Plains of Karana on the continent of Antonica. He never returned. Rumor has is that his undead skeleton now is part of an undead army within the plains. To end this curse I ask all good paladins of this temple to return his bones to me. Nothing less than a spell is my reward for such a deed.'
Orc Oracle Scrolls

Lord Jyleel, is a Command in Defenders Hall in Felwithe.

Lord Jyleel says 'Hail. I am Lord Jyleel, Commander of all noble Defenders of Felwithe. Do you [follow Tunare], the Mother of All or do you still [seek your enlightenment]?'

You say 'I follow Tunare.'

Lord Jyleel says 'Then you are wise indeed. Would you like to [assist the Defenders] in our conflicts or have you other business to tend to?'

You say 'I will assist the Defenders.'

Lord Jyleel says 'Seek out the Crushbone Orcs of the Faydarks. We must have their Oracle Scrolls. They are illegible to you, but we will study them here in Felwithe. Only the Orc Oracles will carry them so be very careful. There is also the problem with the [Crushbone Runners].

Upon giving Lord Jyleel an Orc Oracle scroll, he says:

Lord Jyleel says 'Very fine work. A pity you are not a Defender. Here is a small reward for you. Anytime you come upon an Oracle remember to return it's Scroll to me. Go and find your fate in the field of battle.'

The reward was 8cp plus a random item.  The faction hits were:  +ClericsofTunare, +KingTearisthex, +Anti-Mage.  Perhaps he says something different if your faction is higher with ClericsofTunare.
Crushbone Runners

You say 'What Crushbone Runners?'

Lord Jyleel says 'The Crushbone Orcs are sending messages across the Ocean of Tears to Antonica. Why, we do not know. The Runner is usually spotted on the open pathways of Butcherblock, running towards the docks. Find him, kill him and return his Note Pouch.
Collect the pearls of Odus

Weligon Steelherder says 'Hail <your name>. This is the Deepwater Temple. Here you shall find the wisdom and courage of Prexus, the Ocean Lord. I am glad to see you have an interest. Forgive me if I cut our conversation short. I have many [Deepwater tasks] to attend to.

You say 'What Deepwater tasks?'

Weligon Steelherder says 'We here at the Deepwater Temple must tend to the [Peacekeeper Battlestaff] as well as other duties such as [ocean protection]. There is always something we must do.

You say 'What Peacekeeper Battlestaff?'

Weligon Steelherder says 'Every Sentinel within Erudin carries a High Guard Battlestaff. The creation of this weapon is the duty of the Deepwater Temple and the Temple of Divine Light. Our portion of the task is to send young members to [collect the Pearls of Odus]. These are imbedded into the staff and used to store mystical power.

You say 'I can collect the Pearls of Odus.'

Weligon Steelherder says 'Then ventuer to the harbor of Erudin. There you shall dive into the shark infested water and search for the Pearls of Odus. They lie upon the grounds of our waters. Fill the bad I have given you, combine it and return it to me. Good luck.
Hunt the Diseased Shark

You say 'What ocean protection?

Weligon Steelherder says 'In the name of Prexus we are sworn to protect all ocean creatures. We have heard reports of a shark carrying a deadly malady. We believe she is pregnant. If she delivers her young to the ocean, it will endanger all other creatures. We need to find a young paladin to [hunt the diseased shark].

You say 'I will hunt the diseased shark.'

Weligon Steelherder says 'Yes. Take this bag with you. When you have collected the remains of the Diseased shark and no less than three of her young, you will combine it and return it to me. Then you shall get your reward.

The reward is the Barnacle Breastplate.
Cleansing of the Ocean

Laoni Reista says 'Very good to make your acquaintance <your name>. If you are a [new knight], then let it be known, for I am seeking youg knights of Deepwater.

You say 'I am a new knight.'

Laoni Reista says 'You do seem a bit young. We shall test your mettle. Within this Temple you shall learn to swim as fast as the swordfish and attack with the bravery and skill of the shark. Are you ready to [assist with the cleansing of the ocean]?

You say 'I will assist with the cleansing of the ocean.'

Laoni Reista says 'You will go to the waters near the harbor. We have heard of Qeynos' rogue guild attempting to smuggle our valuable Vasty Deep Water from Odus by way of swimmers. We require proof of their involvement. It is said they often carry special coins. Return one of these coins to me.'
Order of Thunder

Kasine Paegra says 'May the blessings of the Rainkeeper protect you in your travels. I am Kasine Paegra, keeper of the [Order of Thunder] medallions.

You say 'What Order of Thunder?'

Kasine Paegra says 'The Order of Thunder is the Medal which is awarded to a Knight of Thunder who has proven himself superior to the average warrior. All Knights and Priest of Thunder have one, save for the youngest members. Along with the medal comes great respect. You might say the wearer becomes more Charismatic. Should you find one, please return it to Kasine Paegre, unless told other wise.

At this point, if you give her the Order of Thunder medallion found on Drosco, this is what she says:

Kasine Paegra says 'You have returned an Order of Thunder which has been drained of all it's power. I wonder how this could have happened. No doubt evil is behind this. Thank you for it's return. I give you the blessing of Karana. Go and serve the Rainkeeper.

You then receive the follow faction hits:  +KinghtsofThunder, -BloodSabers, +PriestsofLife and +GuardsofQeynos.  No reward is given.
Bayle List

This quest starts in South Qeynos at the Knights of Thunder guild with Runethar Hamest, their leader. 

Runethar Hamest says 'Welcome to the Temple of Thunder. We are home to paladins and clerics who follow the ways of Karana. Do you need guidance or are you already a follower of Karana?'

You say 'I am already a follower of Karana.'

Runethar Hamest says 'I need assistance from you then. The Temple of Thunder was asked by Antonius Bayle to retrieve a list. He does not trust his own militia as of late. He asked me to send a knight to the Jaggedpine to find a man named Frenway Marthank. When you find him you are to tell him that Toe needs the Bayle List. Return the list to me. Be aware that the shadowknights of Bertoxxulous are after the list. Be careful.

I never found Frenway Marthank, however, by coincidence I obtained the list from the corrupt Qeynos guard Dyllin Starshine, who is found in the hidden smuggler's den of Highpass Hold.  Upon giving the list to Runethar Hamest, this is what he says:

Runethar Hamest says 'Antonius Bayle will be pleased. The Temple is also pleased. Here is a token of our appreciation. We need more disciples as yourself. Go forth to tell the world of the Rainkeeper.

The reward was the "Spook the Dead" spell and 2sp.  Faction hits were:  +KinghtsofThunder, -BloodSabers, +PriestsofLife +GuardsofQeynos.
Shining Star of Light
This quest starts in North Qeynos at the Temple of Life with the paladin Astaed Wemor.

Astaed Wemor says 'Greetings. I am Astaed Wemor of the Paladins of the Temple of Life. We are the defenders of the all giving Prime Healer. Are you [here to pray] or are you [here to assist the temple]?'

You say 'I am here to assist the temple.'

Astaed Wemor says 'Good. There are a few in the congregation who require assistance. Will you be [travelling afar] or are you [staying close to Qeynos]?'

You say 'I will be travelling afar.'

Astaed Wemor says 'Then outfit yourself well. We will require you to visit the village of the halflings called Rivervale. There you shall find a herb merchant named Kizzie Mintopp. She has an ingredient which we require. Tell her that you are from the Temple of Life.'

Venture to Rivervale and find the herbalist Kizzie Mintopp.  This is what she says:

You say 'I am from the Temple of Life.'

Kizzie Mintopp says 'Oh !! Yes. They always come for Kizzie's [Honey Jum]. They should have sent you next week. There is a [problem].

You say 'What problem?'

Kizzie Mintopp says 'You see.. Joogl Honeybugger is hurt. He usually collects the [Honeycombs]. Only he know where to get them. I need Honeycombs to make the Honey Jum. Go and speak with Joogl. He is at his burrow outside of Rivervale. He says he cannot work. Tell him you need the Honeycombs.'

You say 'Where are the Honeycombs?'

Kizzie Mintopp says 'If Kizzie new that then she would not hire Joggl Honeybugger!! Go talk to him in the Misty Thicket.'

You say 'What is Honey Jum?'

Kizzie Mintopp says 'It's a secret!! I get the honeycombs and combine them with the secret sauce and..presto.. Honey Jum!! It is used by only the wisest as a component. And you can only get it from Kizzie. It is also good on Batwing Crunchies. If you like.. Kizzie can [make Honey Jum].'

You say 'Please make Honey Jum.'

Kizzie Mintopp says 'No no no!! You will not get Honey Jum untill you give Kizzie three Honeycombs and five gold coins. That is how it works.'

Joggl Honeybugger lives with his sister, Lil Honeybugger in a burrow in Misty Thicket, behind the druid ruins.  Once you hail him, this is what they say:

Lil Honeybugger says 'Hello. My brother Joggle is a great bixie hunter. No one can smash the bixies like my brother. He is the best. He works for [Kizzie Mintopp].

Joogl Honeybugger says 'Hello. I would shake your hand, but it would [hurt]. Ooooh! Owwy!'

You say 'Why does your hand hurt?'

Joogl Honeybugger says 'I was attacked by the bixies. They swarmed on me !! I think I got near their queen. I need that honey to make a living. Get me a bandage and I will tell you where all their worker bees buzz around.'

Once you give Joogl Honeybugger a bandage, this is what he says:

Joogl Honeybugger says 'Oh thank you, <your name>. If you are ever going to gather bixie honeycombs pray you do not run into the queen. The only way I know of collecting the honey is by intercepting the drones and taking their honeycombs which they may be carrying. Good luck !!'

Faction hits were: +MerchantsofRiverVale, +Deeppockets, +GuardiansoftheVale, +MayorGubbin and -CoalitionofTradeFolkIII.

After hunting down a few bixies and collecting three honeycombs, return to Rivervale and give them, along with 5gp, to Kizzie Mintopp.  She then says:

Kizzie Mintopp says 'You are one lucky bixie buster. I just make a batch of Honey Jum. Here. No waiting for you. One jar and a little silver for your good work. Bye now.

She gives you the Honey Jum and faction hits: +MerchantsofRiverVale, +Deeppockets, +GuardiansoftheVale, +MayorGubbin and -CoalitionofTradeFolkIII.

Return to the Temple of Life in Qeynos and give the Honey Jum to Astaed Wemor.  He then says:

Astaed Wemor says 'I pray to Rodcet Nife that you have made it back in time. Let's add a small amount of this honey jum to this and.. here is the potion. This potion must be taken to a sick member of the congregation. The man is Lempeck Hargrin. He lives in the west plains of Karana between the river and the crop fields. He is in dire need of this potion. He has an odd disease. We have tried everything to cure him and this is his last chance. Run to him.'

Faction hits were: +PriestsofLife, KinghtsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.

Give the potion to Lempeck Hargrin in West Karana and this is what he says:

Lempeck Hargrin says 'Greet.. cough.. ings. How are you? Don't get too close to me. I have come down with something bad.'

Lempeck Hargrin says 'Thank you!! I felt the madness nearing my brain, but I feel much better. Please take this as thanks, it is all I have to donate to Astaed Wemor. Be sure he gets it. I shall thank Rodcet Nife everyday for sending help.'

He gives you a Rusty Scythe and more faction: +PriestsofLife, KinghtsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.

Return to Qeynos once more and give the Rusty Scythe to Astaed Wemor.  He then says:

Astaed Wemor says 'It is good to know that we saved Lempeck. He has given us his scythe as a donation to the temple. We shall find a use for it. As for your fine work at preserving the life of another,.. I reward you with The Shining Star of Life. Should you ever desire more strength in battle, call upon it to give you strength, but let it be known that at battles end you shall feel weaker than before you called upon the power. Just for a short time. When the power is drained, go to our temple store house and ask Whysia to [recharge the Shining Star of Life]. Go and serve life.'

The last faction hits of the quest: +PriestsofLife, KinghtsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.  You are then rewarded the Shining Star of Light, not Life.  The weapon is not charged either, as Astaed Wemor wrongfully informs you.  The Shining Star of Light's statistics can be found here.
