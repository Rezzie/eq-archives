LEVEL 1

Hand to Hand - Your primary offensive skill. A monks bare hands have a damage of your 4 + (Level / 5) dropping the decimal place. Plus, at higher levels your Strength adds to your maximum damage. Wear a pair of Netted Gloves and you can hit magic only creatures.

1H Blunt/2H Blunt - The ability to use blunt weapons. As of now, there are no weapons that give an advantage to a monk over their hands. Weapons weigh a lot, and a 2H Blunt is simply out of the question since monks are the Masters of Duel Wield. Hopefully in the future a fast, light weight, monk-only weapon will appear.

Duel Wield - Monks are the only class to get this from the start. It allows you to punch with both hands, or use two weapons (which is not a good idea at this time). Starting monks must remember not to carry a light source in their hands or you with lose a possible attack and Duel Wield will not increase.

Throwing - Used with throwing knifes, axes, and stars to pull a MOB. No real reason to keep this skill maxxed.

Dodge - The ability to miss a blow. This skill raises automatically during combat.

Kick - A secondary attack. This is the same kick that many other melee classes get and will damage magic only creatures.

Bind Wound - This is an important skill to use to reduce downtime when soloing. Bandages cost about 5sp, but magician can summon them -- which will cost you less. Remember that summoned items disappear when you log.

Mend - This skill when successful with heal about 25% of your hit points. Not only will this skill sometimes fail and do nothing until level 20, but once in a great while it will actually worsen your wounds. Self only.

 

LEVEL 3

Safe Fall - An automatic skill that fall from heights with little or no damage. Very useful when running with Sprit of the Wolf through hilly areas.

 

LEVEL 5

Round Kick - This is the first of your special attacks. Slower than kick but does more damage. The special attacks will damage magic only creatures.

 

LEVEL 8

Sneak - Sneak cuts your speed to 1/4 and allows you walk behind aggressive MOBS without being detected. Most useful for selling to apprehensive merchants because they become indifferent to you (i.e. Aviak City).

 

LEVEL 10

Tiger Claw - Another special attack. Tiger claw does damage like round kick but recycles as fast as kick.

 

LEVEL 12

Block - The ability to block a blow. This is an automatic skill during battle. Block will not work on creatures that are behind you.

 

LEVEL 15

Double Attack - This allows you to hit with the same hand/weapon twice per round. This combined with duel wield makes it possible to get 4 attacks per round, plus a special.

 

LEVEL 17

Feign Death - This important skill allows you to fake your death causing the attacking creature to walk away. It is important to remember that most of the aggressive creatures will come right back to you as soon as you stand again. To be safe you should wipe the creature's hit list by zoning after Feigning, otherwise you may get jumped by him and friends when you least expect it. This is a skill you may want to save practice points for and use 30 or more to start it.

 

LEVEL 18

Instill Doubt - This skill attempts to scare a creature into running away. This skill raises very slowly, so if you are interested in this skill you should put many practice points into it to start. You can practice this skill with anything but yourself targeted. Beware, if you are close enough to an NPC and you are successful it is consider an attack and you will start a fight. One way to practice is to feign then use instill doubt, this way you avoid the animations and sounds.

 

LEVEL 20

Eagle Strike - Another special attack. As fast as Tiger Claw with more damage.

 

LEVEL 25

Dragon Punch - Another special attack. As fast a Tiger Claw and Eagle Strike with even more damage.

 

LEVEL 27

Disarm - This allows you to remove the weapon from the creature's hand. This is only an advantage when the creature is using a weapon with a lower delay then its bare hands. This will not remove magic weapons.

 

LEVEL 30

Flying Kick - The last special attack. As fast as Round Kick, but capable of doing over 100hp of damage.

 

LEVEL 35

Riposte - Allows you to block a blow a deliver a bonus one back.
