Monk Quests

Monks from Freeport will be able to obtain both the Sashes and Headbands without increasing faction. Qeynos monks will have to raise their Freeport faction, by various Qeynos quests or killing Deathfist Orcs in the commonlands etc. before they can get the sash quests.

Sashes (from the Guild Masters in Freeport) Sash Stats

White - Bone Chips, (2) Orc Scalps, and Snake Fang. Give them to the guild master unstacked for the white sash of order.

Yellow - White Sash, Gaint Snake Rattle, Chitin, and Deathfist Slashed Belt.

Orange - Yellow Sash, Legionnaire Bracer, Greater Lightstone, and Cutthroat Insignia Ring.

Red - Orange Sash, Blackened Wand (Marnek's Wand) from the 3rd level of Befallen Necro Thaumateurgist, and Sapphire of Souls (Blackened Sapphire) from a thief named Ekeros in Najena, located in the room in front of Rathyl's room. He looks like a monk.

Purple - Broken.

Blue - Dropped by Guild Masters when you kill them.

Black - Dropped by Guild Master when you kill him.

 

Headbands (from the guild masters in Qeynos) Headband Stats

White - (4) Gnoll Scalps from Gnoll pups. Give them to the guild master unstacked for the White Headband.

Yellow - White Headband, and (3) Putrid Rib Bones from Putrid Skeletons. Putrid Skeletons can be found at the ruins in Qeynos Hills.

Orange - Yellow Headband, (2) Gnoll pelts, and Gnoll Skin. Gnoll pelts are found in Blackburrow. Gnoll Skins are dropped by Elites and Commanders in Blackburrow.

Red - Orange Headband, Skull of Brother Dareb, Brother Shen, and the head of Gahnex Drah. Brother Dareb's skull is got by killing High Shaman Phido. Brother Shen's head is gotten by killing High Shaman Grisok. Paw has been upgraded so these no longer drop there, but are found through out South Karana instead. Gahnex Drah can be found in the undead camp by the stone bridge.

Purple - Red Headband, Skull of Jhen'Tra, Marnek's Dagger, and Coronet of Buried Blood (Zaharn's Coronet). Jhen'Tra is dropped by Gnoll Embalmers in Lake RatheTear. The Dagger is Dropped by Necro Thaumateurgists on the 3rd level of Befallen, and the Coronet is dropped by the High Priest Zaharn in the king's room at Permafrost.

Blue - Kill the Guild Masters.

Black - Kill the Guild Masters.

 

Collar of Neshika - Go to Kithicor Woods and talk to Grim Oakfist, who is a random spawn in place of Lozani. Kithicor is clawing with level 30+ undead from 8pm to 7am. He is located at the inn #2 on the map. You need to have a high faction with AshenOrder to obtain the quest. He wants you to kill three named tigers (Eenot, Reggit, and Kobb) and bring back their skins. Talking to Grim triggers the spawn. All items in this quest are No Drop so you have to be there to loot. You will need a tracker and SoW to get these tigers. Turn in the skins and you will get Collar of Neshika.

 

Treant Fists - Talk to Puab Closk in the Freeport guild, tell him "I am a monk of great skill". He will give you a token to give to a "banished kerran". The banished kerran is located in a pit on a island with necromancer heretics in Kerra Ridge. Kerra Ridge is a zone next the city of Erudian. Tell him "Puab sent me", then give him the token. He will give you a box with 4 slots to put remains of a catman in. You will find the remains in and around Runnyeye. At Mooto's camp in Misty Thicket is where an ultra rare goblin alchemist spawns that drops 'rib cage armor'. Near the entrance to Runnyeye in Beholder's Maze you will find another ultra rare goblin alchemist that drops a 'humerous handed mace'. If you swim down through the star shaped object near the bank in Runneye you will commonly find a gelatinous cube that drops a 'catman skull cap'. If you camp the room behind the goblin king, approxamately every couple hours a goblin captain will spawn in place of a elite goblin guard. The captain drops a 'fractured femur'. Combine those four items in the box and give it back to the banished kerran, he will give you a note to give to Puab to get the Treant Fists.

 

Brother Zephyl - Located in the Rathe Mountains offers you a reward if you return the Idol of Zan Fi and your red sash. He states that a monk named Raster in Innothule swamp has the idol. Raster is located in lower guk, mino side, in the elder room. When you give him the Idol and your red sash he will give you a Rare Robe Pattern.

 

Brother Qwinn - Located in South Karana wants you to find the Code of Zan Fi, which was stolen by Targin the Rock. Targin the Rock is a rare spawn found in the King Room of Solusek B. When you give him the Code of Zan Fi and your Purple Headband he will give you back a sewing needle and a recipe for a robe.

 

Robe of the Lost Circle - The recipe is Brother Zephyl's Rare Robe Pattern, Brother Qwinn's sewing needle, Shadow Silk, and Jonathan's Whistling Warsong Scroll (bard scroll). Shadow Silk is made by combining a Shadow Wolf Pelt, Silk Swatch, and Scroll of Gather Shadows (Necro/SK Spell) in a sewing kit. The Robe of the Lost Circle is dark red with AC9, Wt. 0.7, STA +5, AGI +5, +15 HP.
