Because of the AC, many Paladins would like to express their displeasure to Verant. Namely, that Rangers and Bards have higher AC than Paladins do? It is my understanding that we as Paladins, sacrificed offensive power to gain a higher AC(through Plate) and the ability to Heal. Rangers, unable to wear Plate, and capable of dealing more damage, prefer offense over defense. This is no longer the case when comparing Quest armour. It is either equivalent or greater than the Armour of Ro. Brad McQuaid visited our site the other day and scans many sites daily. We could put together a petition but I am fairly certain he is getting a good idea of the situation now. Maybe in a couple of days, after he has been given time to respond, we can decide on a course of action. I have posted a Comments post on the General Board for your comments.
