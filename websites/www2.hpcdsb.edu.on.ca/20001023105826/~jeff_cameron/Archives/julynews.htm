July 23 - THE AFTERMATH - I learned a valuable lesson today. *smile* I should have prefaced the proposed ideas with this comment : I did not make these suggestions up. They came to me in emails from various posters on the site. I received a few immature and childish emails calling my intelligence into question? I posted the Petition so that the Paladins of Norrath could be heard. There are many differing opinions. Some valid, some not. I wish I could share everything with all of you but I cannot. The only idea that came personally from me was the idea of a ranged Taunt or similar spell that allowed us to tag mobs from a distance. If you would like to comment on the Petition, remember, I am reiterating ideas from all of YOU. A few valid points came out though. I think the Petition has served its purpose in garnering discussion on our Class. I think we are doing well when most requests are based on improving our Class as Characters and not as "They are better, we want more." Some call it nitpicking, I call it a game that should suit the people that play it.
