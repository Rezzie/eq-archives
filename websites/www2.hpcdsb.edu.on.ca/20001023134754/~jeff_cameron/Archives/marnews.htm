March, 2000 News

March 27 - Have some good news from Aradune about Fiery Avenger and Bashing.   Thanks to Fenar for telling me in-game.  The Forums are already talking about it! 

After some thought and consideration, we've made it such that one can bash when wielding the FA, even though it remains 2H.

We're also contemplating adding FA type weapons for all the classes, including special 'particle' effects for those weapons, but it's going to take us a while to get them all in (meaning the quests, graphics, and such), so please be patient.

Also, making some 2h weapons 'bash ok' is something we're going to limit to FA type weapons, so I'm going to go ahead and say 'no' to all the requests to make other 2h weapons the same -- sorry in advance.

- Brad

March 2 - I have received numerous emails about the need for a moderator for our Fiery Avenger Knowledge Pool.  Interslayer, Bephsi, Romidar and Hooloo have been nominated or volunteered their services.  I will post a discussion on the FA forum and you can decide on who would like to do it.  With that out of the way, I just thought I would explain why I did not move quickly on picking a moderator.  First of all, we really haven't found the Quest, so to me, almost any information is still relevant.  Even though Nennon has been heavily criticized, it is possible that his information is the best we have.  I haven't had the time to keep up with everyone and I haven't even been able to play much.  Even though the majority of all the Nennon-talk going on has been flames, there is still some information that may prove valuable to some.  I have a hard time believing that you end up paying some obscure NPC a lot of money to start the Quest but remember, a human wrote the Quest and is bound by human capabilities(read illogical).   So, when you decide who will moderate the Forum via the posts, I will contact the lucky people(2) and we will be VERY careful on what we delete so you can decide what is valid and what is not for yourself.
