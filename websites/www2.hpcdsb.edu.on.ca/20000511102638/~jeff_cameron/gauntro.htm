Gauntlets of Ro

Quest given by Joanna, Human Cleric

Quest

Joanna tells you of a Sentry named Alechin who roams the land of North Karana.  He is a werewolf that poses a threat to many.  Slay him and return his locket to Joanna. Once you return the locket, Joanna grants you the mold for the Gauntlets of Ro.

Faction

Joanna is a Cleric of Marr, based in Freeport.  Ask Gygus Remnara, the Cleric Guildleader if you are an honoured member.  To raise faction with the Clerics of Marr, purchase shark skins from the Port Authority in Freeport and give them to Theron, a Paladin in the Hall of Truth in North Freeport.  This increases faction with both the Paladins and Clerics of Freeport.
