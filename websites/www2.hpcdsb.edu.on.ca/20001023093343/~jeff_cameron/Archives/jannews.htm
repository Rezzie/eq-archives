January 22 - Greetings all! The Paladins of Norrath event was a great success(screenshots are coming)! About 80 Paladins attended and met at the Tunnel in East Commons at about 9:20(after Xegony came back up). We all zoned in to West Freeport and pushed the population to 104 before the zone crashed. Someone pulled 5 Militia to us and they were all quickly dispatched. Only Waylan, a level 14 Paladin was a casualty. Valor Fireheart pulled Sir Lucan D'Lere and his Guards outside the city. Many came to Hail Sir Lucan. A Dark Elf Shadowknight attacked Sir Lucan but quickly ran for his life. Valor Fireheart came back and brought Sir Lucan to Romidar and his Companions. The fight was mighty! Over 50 Paladins jumped into the fray and destroyed the Corrupt Militia's Leader. Sir Lucan arose from his corpse and once more attacked our Fine Paladins. After another short disconnect, Sir Lucan had fallen, again! Romidar was barely able to swing his blade in the fray, but knelt down and pulled the Testament from the Skeleton! We all headed back to the tunnel and by 11:30, Romidar returned wielding SoulFire! It was truly superb! All in all, I had a great time and really appreciate everyone's participation and enthusiasm. To sum up the night, a Paladin made a noble statement that went something like this, "What other class would come together across ALL of the Servers to assist one of their own?" I am proud to be a Paladin of Norrath!

January 17 - Good news!  In case any of you have missed it, Abashi gave Paladins a major overhaul.  They have basically touched on ALL of our weaknesses and put us on the road to happiness. :-)  Romidar, I am sure everyone here thanks you wholeheartedly, you are one who deserves his title.  Here is the news, clipped from the Gameplay Forum :

 

Hello all, I said I'd stop in tonight and let you know what was decided. As of the next patch, Paladins and SKs will receive additional hitpoints, and increased caps on bash and taunt.
The additional hitpoints will be noticeable for Paladins and SKs over level 25. This was done because the Paladins, SKs, and Rangers had the same HP base, which was incorrect. This change will basically put Paladin and SK HPs midway between rangers and warriors of the same race.

The taunt skill was increased because SKs, and in particular, Paladins could not taunt opponents off of Rangers, Monks, Rogues, and Warriors due to their lower damage output. Though the Paladin and SK will never equal the warrior in their ability to taunt, this change in addition to some of the taunting spells should help them fulfill their role as a tank.

Bash was increased because Paladins typically aren't switching to two handed weapons, choosing instead of using the bash skill. Most warriors, being ogres, trolls, or barbarians for the most part, do not worry about bash and instead opt for slam, which uses the same skill. Paladins are typically the only characters who use a shield throughout their lives.

The problem of an AC disparity between Paladins/SKs and rangers still exists due to the ranger class-specific armor typically having an AC that is too high. We'll be further examining the AC disparity over the coming weeks, and will address it with items.

Additionally, the need for Paladin/SK 1H weapons above that of the Ghoulbane has been brought to the attention of our Live team (the item/quest people). Quite frankly, if you are still wielding a GB in the high 40s, you aren't doing the damage you should be doing. However it's apparent that Paladins (and the smaller SKs) prefer to retain their bash.

Also, the Armor of Ro should be more obtainable after the next patch. The class-specific mid-level armor in general is still being analyzed and will improve over the next few patches.

Finally, Fear will drop more items. I was able to obtain several pieces for each class upon testing it last night.

-Gordon
