Vambraces of Ro

Quest given by Marianna, Human Cleric

Quest

Marianna asks you to slay the diseased sharks that are in the waters of Erud's Crossing.  Once you return two pieces of their rotten flesh, Marianna will grant you the mold for the Vambraces of Ro.

Faction

Marianna belongs to the Temple of Life in Qeynos.   Speak with High Priestess Jhanda to find out if you are honoured member.  To raise faction with the Temple of Life and the Knights of Thunder, give Chesgard, who is standing outside of the Paladin Hall, bandit sashes from West Karanas.
