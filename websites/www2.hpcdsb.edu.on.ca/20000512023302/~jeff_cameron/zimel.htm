Zimel's Blade(Soulfire!)

 

Chapter 1 : Kalatrina Plossen is one of the greatest Paladins in the Land. Her duty is to train new, young knights and keep an eye on the Hated Freeport Militia. She can be found at the Hall of Truth in North Freeport. She is directly above the entrance on the second floor. When you Hail her, she informs you she has need of young knights. After further investigation, you learn of a spy within the Militia. He is in danger and Kalatrina hands you a note to give to him. He can be found in the Freeport Militia House.

 

Chapter 2 : The beginnings of your Quest! Rush over to the Militia House. The spy is in the middle of the Militia House in the Courtyard on the first floor. You give him the code and he warns you of danger. You hand him the note. Suddenly, Militia swarm to the spy and begin attacking him! He hands you an old piece of burned parchment and yells to return to the Hall of Truth. He falls to the Evil Guards. Run to the Hall of Truth! Upon further inspection of the note you read: Zimel's Blades with some prices. Give the note to Kalatrina.

 

Chapter 3 : Kalatrina is excited by the note you have been given. DO NOT give Kalatrina Plossen the Note. If you do, restart the Quest and get it again. This is where the Quest is counter-intuitive. If you give her the Note, she tells you Zimel was a great Blacksmith who knew the secret of enchanting blades and making superior weapons. She tells you to go and talk to Rashinda Elore at the Office of the People. Rashinda is difficult to get any information out of but once you do, you determine that Groflah Steadirt of Groflah's Forge, was a friend of Zimel's and may have more information. When you ask Rashinda of Zimel's Blades, Lakeen Boorheez wanders in, saying he is lost. He quickly exits and heads to the tunnels.

Chapter 4 : Run over to Groflah's Forge and ask him about Zimel. He is on the second floor of the Forge and is not very helpful. He is nervous when you ask him of Zimel and tells you he will not speak here. Hand Groflah the Note you received from the Spy. Groflah tells you many things and asks you to meet him at the Seafarer's Roost. He can study the Note and try to decode the information. Groflah hands you the Note back.

Chapter 5 :  Wandering over to the Seafarer's Roost, buy a drink, relax and watch Freeport go about its business. Groflah SHOULD show up between 1am and 3am. When he arrives, ask who Zimel is. He will go into a long story about how Zimel has disappeared and the Blade, Soulfire has been lost. Hand Groflah the Note and he attempt to decipher it. Groflah tells you that he thinks Sir Lucan D'Lere, Head of the Freeport Militia, and Pietro Zarn, Head of the ShadowKnight Guild in Freeport are in cahoots and searching for Soulfire as well.

Chapter 6 : Thanking him for his knowledge, look about the Seafarer's Roost. Who is wandering around? A beggar named Tykar Renlin should be close by. Asking Tykar for information about Zimel, Tykar responds by saying he is thirsty. Purchase a Drom's Champagne from the Bartender and give one to Tykar. Repeat this three more times. Tykar informs you that Zimel has been thrown into Prison by the Militia near the Arena. Feeling guilty, Tykar hands you a blanket to give to Zimel if you should meet him.

Chapter 7 : Alas! It is true! Zimel is locked in a cell and has been driven crazy. After asking Zimel various Questions, he responds by saying he wants some items. After deciphering his unintelligible speech, you determine he wants Bog Juice, Edible Goo and a Blanket. Well, Tykar gave you the Blanket. You have seen Edible Goo in the various Bakeries and know it is the most basic of delicacies. Bog Juice, wait! You know a Brewer who has the recipe and seem to recall even drinking some before! Hand each of these items to Zimel. Zimel will give you a key simply marked H.K.102

Chapter 8 : H.K.102? 'tis a Chest key. Ah! In Highhold Pass, Humans have built a bank there to guard their riches from the barbaric Orcs and Gnolls. Visit the bank in Highhold Pass and hand the Bank Clerk the key. She thanks you for the key and hands you a Note to deliver to Brother Hayle. Alas, she is certain he is being held captive in a cell in Splitpaw, a Gnoll stronghold.

Chapter 9 : Journeying into Splitpaw, you find Brother Hayle and hand him the Note. Brother Hayle hands you the Note once again and asks you to retrieve a number of items for him. He would like you to prove your nobility to him. He asks you to bring the Hilt of Soulfire back to him. The Hilt is being held by an Evil ShadowKnight named Xicotl in MistMoore Castle! As well, bring him the Brilliant Sword of Faith(Quest info here). Finally, he asks you to bring your Testimony(Quest info here).

Chapter 10 : You have destroyed and defeated many Evil Creatures that wander Norrath. You have lived up to your name and go back to Brother Hayle, truly bringing proof of your Nobility. Brother Hayle will thank you and grant you the greatest blade in the land! Soulfire!
