Boots of Ro

Quest given by Nicholas, Dwarven Cleric

Quest

Nicholas asks you to retrieve Brass Brazen Kilij Plans that are being carried around by a Sand Giant in the Desert of Southern Ro.  Find the Sand Giant and slay him, return the Plans and Nicholas will reward you with the mold for the Boots of Ro.

Faction

Nicholas worships Brell Serilis and belongs to the Clerics of Underfoot.  To raise faction with the Clerics of Underfoot, go to Priestess Ghalea in Kaladim and ask her if you are an honoured member.  There is a Paladin in the back of the Paladin Guild house, on the left side.  He will accept bone chips and increase your faction.  Once Priestess Ghalea tells you you are an honoured member, you will con warmly and be able to complete the Quest.
