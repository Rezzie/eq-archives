Helm of Ro

Quest given by Thomas, Human Paladin

Quest

Thomas tells you of an Evil that is roaming the lands of South Karana.  Two Undead Cyclops have been spotted there, causing havoc.  You must slay the two Undead Cyclops and return their skulls to Thomas.  If you do, Thomas will grant you the mold for the Helm of Ro.

Faction

Thomas is a Knight of Thunder, of the Temple of Thunder in Qeynos.  To increase faction with the Knights of Thunder, go into the West Karanas, slay bandits and hand in their sashes to Chesgard, a Paladin trainer near the Temple of Thunder in Qeynos.
