February 6 - There are many people complaining about the recent "upgrade" to our Lay on Hands.  LoH was supposedly increased from level*25 to level*27.  Many have tested it and it doesn't seem to work.  Why? Oh Why, do they not just check your current hps and make that LoH's value?  If I am level 50 with 1800 hps including Cleric and Shaman buffs, just make LoH heal 1800hps.  That solves the disposable Paladin problem and makes us heal up to our own hps.  If we used it on a Warrior it would not heal to full, but players of our level or less would be healed.   If they want to get picky, even base LoH on natural hps.  Making an easy solution difficult.  Working on Armour pages!  Posting them soon!
