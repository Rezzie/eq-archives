What is a 3D pen?

It’s much like a big texter or highlighter and instead of drawing with ink it draws with plastic to create 3D objects.

You’ll need a steady hand to create the 3D objects like those above, but with practise you should be able to create all types of objects.

How does the 3D printing pen work?

A 3D pen is a bit like a 3D printer in that you can create 3D objects at home, however that’s about where the similarity ends. With a 3D printer you load drawings and it automatically creates your object, with a 3D printing pen you are the machine, in that you have to guide the pen to get it to go where you want it to.

So plastic filaments are inserted into the pen and it gets heated inside the pen, making it melt it into liquid. As soon as this liquid plastic is released into the air again it cools and sets, making it solid.

You can also draw on just about any surface with a 3D pen, which makes it perfect for personalizing your belongings and for fixing small household items like remote controls where you’ve broken the back off.
