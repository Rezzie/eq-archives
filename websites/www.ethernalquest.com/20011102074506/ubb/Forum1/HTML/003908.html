regarding keyboard loggers...

ever 20ms, read port 0x60
that gets you the last scancode (make or break)

if it was me making a keylogger, I'd just add one scancode to the end of each item DB. unless you know the exact packet data format, you can't be sure what's in the packet. it'd be stupid to put the account and password in plaintext and send it out in one packet. on the other hand, pull account name out of equname.txt, record keystrokes for password, encrypt account+password, append one byte of info to each item packet sent to server.

the fix is simple. change your password after you use the program. *be* paranoid, that's never a bad thing.
