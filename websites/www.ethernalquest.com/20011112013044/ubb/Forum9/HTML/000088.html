When they first changed the token system getting token had to do with how much of the mission you completed (which means what percentage of MoBs you killed), how much of the map had been been revealed, and how much time you sped in the mission.
Now it looks more like it only has to do with time spent in the mission. I would get the small 5 or 6 room cave missions at the easiest setting and run through them, kill everything quickly (usually all grey), open every chect and door, and never get a token. Then I noticed I was getting tokens a lot more than normal when I was soloing in missions normally for the XP and would pick up the reward whenever I happened across it. So I tried getting the same type of easy mission, ran through and killed everything, got the chests and doors, and then waited 10 minutes or so and I almost always got a token. 9 of 10 times. So time seems to be a big factor. But on occasion I've gotten a token when I only killed 1 or 2 things in no time and grabbed the reward. So some missions might be predisposed to giving out tokens.
Oh, and I've never gotten a token when I've snuck past things or while something was agro on me.
