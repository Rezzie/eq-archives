Drop the keyboard macro into your EverQuest directory.

If you're running Windows 9x...

Go into your Windows/System directory, and copy dinput.dll to dinput2.dll.

If you're running Windows NT...

Go into your Windows/System32 directory, and copy dinput.dll to dinput2.dll


Please note, I said COPY, not just rename the old file. The Keyboard Macro references dinput2.dll is why. This is the method that I use.
