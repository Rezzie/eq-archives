However, ever since I've gotten my new account, four months ago, I've been using ShowEQ, and the trainers, without being banned at all.

I am extremely careful though. I use VERY few hacks. The only ones I've used are:
*-Fall with no damage
*-No anon
*-NPC and Corpse to /who
*-Breathe Underwater
*-Sense Heading always works
*-No Rain
*-Hide while running
*-Show Spell Names

and Sow Hack. Yes, Sow Hack. Well, I am VERY careful about the last, and NO hacks are on until the very moment I need them, then they are immediatly disabled. I also use a few other unreleased ones, but never anything I know is flagged. Especially /zone. No way in hell.

As to the Sow Hack, well, I use it VERY rarely. Only when ShowEQ tells me no GMs in zone, only when I absolutely HAVE to, (like running from something quick), and turn it off ASAP. and NEVER zone with it. Even when I do use it to run from something dangerous, I use it in spurts. On for a sec to get a small headstart, then off until it's close to catching up, etc...

Therefore, I can reasonably conclude that very careful use and monitoring of hacking can go undetected and therefore unpunished. In fact, that rule applies to alot more then RAM hexing... be very very careful, slick, and think very fast, (and of course don't go in above your head), and monitor EVERYTHING, and you can get away with all sorts of things. In fact almost anything.

I don't use a trainer however. I build myself a small retarded C++ application long ago, to be able to enter a found offset (address in RAM), read it's current value, edit it, and switch it back with a simple button-press. Ironically I made it with the help of various people from this board, (adenine!, casey, etc..) and some people from the ShowEQ and HackersQuest IRC rooms, (andon, and (gasp) patrick!).

Anyway, back to the point, Just a reminder and warning to anyone who wants to use cheats: Don't get cocky. In other words, just because you ran by a GM with Sow Hack and zoned, and am not banned a month later, doesn't mean jack shit.
