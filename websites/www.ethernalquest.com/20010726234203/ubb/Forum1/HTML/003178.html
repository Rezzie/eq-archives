"Can any of you ex-GM's out there let us in on the process for how it is determined whether an account is banned or suspended?"

Uber Saussage I was once a GM, Here is how banned accounts are determined versus suspended accounts.

There is a meeting twice a week for cirtain SONY/VI employees that work within the game (GMS/TESTERS mainly). Not all of the people have to be present, it has some sort of rotation where you only have to be there every other week or so, during this meeting, all instances of pending banable/suspendable accounts are brought forward, and all the information is presented, all attending then vote on what they feel would be proper punishment for such behavior, depending on how it is voted, is if you are banned/suspended. If it is your second or more offense, you are automatically banned, no vote made.

Hope that clears things up for ya.
