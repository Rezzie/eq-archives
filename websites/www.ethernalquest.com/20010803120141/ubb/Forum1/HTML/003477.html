It depends who you ask.

Necromancers and Druids are generally regarded as the best solo class, with race not generally being an issue (except for Half-elf druids and a very low base wisdom, which is the stat they use to calculate how much mana they have).

Personally, I think you miss out on a lot of the game if all you do is solo, so if you want to be popular with groups, Warriors and Clerics are almost always wanted, with Rogues coming in a close second once they get backstab, and monks in outdoor zones for pulling once they get Feign Death. Lemme make this easy for you. Be a Troll Shaman who worships Innoruuk. Pump all your points into wisdom, then the last 5 should go into stamina.

Here's the deal. Shamans are an awesome soloing class (My barb shaman can solo effectively at any level). And they are ALWAYS needed in a group (healing and buffing at lower levels, then slowing and buffing past 35).

Trolls have another advantage. They get an item later in the game (around level 30) that allows them to Snare. A HUGE advantage when soloing. Trust me on this. The BEST char to start out with is a troll shaman because you're needed in both Groups AND you can solo very effectively.
