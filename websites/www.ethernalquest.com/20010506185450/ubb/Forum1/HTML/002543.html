If you're playing with the button art, here's a little trick I used to do on my C64 to simulate semi-transparent. Make every other pixel "transparent"... you know, that ungodly ugly purple that's treated as clear... the one that was just recently fixed on dropped coins ... Just like a checkerboard drop it on the spell gem. I'm fairly sure one could get PSP5+ to do some paper tricks to make it easier to paint.

It'll look like ass at low resolutions (unless you've got a cheap monitor, heh heh) but should be pretty acceptable at 1024x768.

The only problem would be if clicking on a "clear" pixel wouldn't count as clicking on the gem. Hmmm... it's about time I start contributing... I've got a weekend project.
