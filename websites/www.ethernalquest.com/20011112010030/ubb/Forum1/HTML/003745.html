As I said in another post just a few minutes ago.

Make sure to minimize the first window before openning the second. Once in the game with the second unminimize the first. If one is Really sluggish escape your mouse pointer and drag both around the screen, then realign them. This should balance their priority.

Also if you are having really wierd graphics bugs, upgrade your drivers.

If you are having really wierd sound bugs in echoy places, or zoning, try /reverb off, if that works then you need to disable EAX. /reverb only works till you zone. What you have to do, and will probably have to do more than once to make it stick is open your Speaker setting from you SBLive menu and set the task to Play EAX game, then set the preset to Disable EAX. I had to play with it, but finally it stuck and had no more REALLY loud(not depending on the volume) annoying tone from the sound card.
