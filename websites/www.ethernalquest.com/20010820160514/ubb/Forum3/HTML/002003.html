The problem most people have (other than drawing lines that they expect to still be readable later) is that they're acutally using a SWITCH instead of a HUB.

Even some recent Linksys HUBS are actually SWITCHES. The ports on the Linksys cable/DSL routers... ...you guessed it... SWITCHED.

Go to the store. Get the cheapest hub you can buy that has an uplink port. Put your SEQ machine and your Windows machine on this hub and connect it to your Linksys.
