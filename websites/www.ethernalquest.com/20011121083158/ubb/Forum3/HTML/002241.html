You should be able to use the IP addresses that you would normally use going straight into your router. Just be sure to set your default gateway to the internal address of the router. (most likely 192.168.0.1). I don't know about the SMC router, but it's likely that it is DHCP enabled, so you can pull the information on your windows box, and use that information to configure your Linux machine.

be SURE you are wiring between the hub and the switch correctly!

a straight through wire should be plugged into the UPLINK port on ONE device, and a NORMAL port on the other!

don't plug the straight wire into the uplink port on BOTH devices.

also, a straight through wire won't work between two NORMAL ports, you need a cross over cable for that.
