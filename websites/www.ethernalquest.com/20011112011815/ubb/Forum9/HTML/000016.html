Decaying Skeleton 		posted 07-31-2001 01:50 PM               
tokens increase rank. rank is marked on the neck bar you start with. the neck bars also increase stats with increased rank.

after you collect 10 tokens right click your neck and a new one appears in your inventory. the next one is at 30, the next at 60.

the first one gives hp, the second one gives hp and nano. the third one gives hp, nano, and an xp bonus to every kill.

right clicking the neck bar will also display your rank to others by hovering it above your shoulder until you zone.

higher level rank bars require you to obtain certain levels, for example the 3rd rank neck bar requires lvl 15 or higher to wear (though it does not stop you from collecting as many tokens as you can at any level, you just cant upgrade till your also high enough level)

theres more after 3, but i dont know thier requirements or thier bonuses, im not high enough to use them yet.

with the recent patch you no longer can "see" if a mission gives a token as a reward. ive found the best way to obtain tokens now is to take "official" missions (read the mission description carefully), and the closer you follow the instructions the better your chances of being awarded a token for your services. clearing a mission completely will also increase your chances of getting them as a reward, its even possible to get 2 tokens if you complete the mission quickly in addition to following the mission insctructions.

also be sure your using omni-tek mission booths if you want tokens, neutral mission booths cannot give "official" missions as they are not run by omni-tek. omni mission booths are black, neutrals are brown and green. (ie harrys mission booths are neutral, where as booths in omni-1 are official)

if your a clan all of the above applys except you would need to use clan booths for tokens.

if your neutral, you cant gain rank.

Ok some news on how the token system now works.

When you get a mission there it either
1) will not award a token
or
2) May/Will award a token

Let me explain #2
If a mission May/Will award a token you have can guarentee that you will get the token by killing everthing, going into every room, unlocking every locked door and chest (note that this doesn't include killing your target if it is a spy mission). Some things may be marked as necessary to do to get a token others just may increase your chance to get a token.

Take a simple 10 room mission with 3 mobs 4 chests (2 locked) 2 locked doors and the pick up item (i'm using the describe an item mission as an example)

Behind the scene the mission is set to give a token. It requires Box B to be unlocked before the possibility to get the token will happen. The 2 locked doors and 2 of the mobs have been set to increase the chance to get the token.

If you unlock Box B, unlock the 2 doors and kill the correct 2 mobs you WILL get the token.
If you just unlock Box B you may still get the token just less of a chance.

Doing everything in the mission just increases your chance to get a token to 100% IF the mission could give one.

I have personally got a token without killing a single mob in the mission...I was level 24 doing a 44 mission (describe and item) and just ran through the mission found the item and picked it up...I didn't go through every room though.

My advice if you want tokens faster do the easiest mission you can and just clear the whole level. Do the "Describe a item" mission as you can leave the item where it sits until you finish killing everything. This can also be used to build up some cash. I'm level 25 and get my missions in Tir. I grab the missions that give 4418 credits (3 at a time) and go do them. normally about 1/2 my missions give tokens (including the missions the playfield doesn't initiate)

115 tokens and 390,000credits and a fair amount of wasted money too.

Good luck...if you haven't got many tokens I wouldn't worry about it to much. I got 60 of them at low level because I was told that it would let me get passed some of the caps earlier...but it didn't.
