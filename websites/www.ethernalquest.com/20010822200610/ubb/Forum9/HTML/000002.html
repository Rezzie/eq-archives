Possible Item Dupe here.
(disclaimer: trying this may result in lose of items, don't flame me for trying this.. if it works kudos.. if it doesn't.. well I warned ya)

If you have a bag (big bag pref) you might try this.

1)Fill the bag with random items..
2)open both your 'bank' and 'bag'
3)remove item from your bag and place directly into the 'bank'
4)take the bag itself and place into the bank.(this should still leave the 'bag' window open with items inside)
5) mess/remove items from both bank and bag.. at any rate ... until you start to notice items are taking 1-10 seconds to reappear. at this point there's the possiblity of losing the item. or if you're lucking it will randomly replace itself with an item in your possession.

Trying this myself I ended up with 18 floating torches in about 20 mins. -- I did find that If I logged or crashed.. all the items would revert back to the normal items, and sometimes would be missing 1-5. I recommend trying this with monster/robot parts.. etc.. so you don't go and lose your armor/weapons etc.
