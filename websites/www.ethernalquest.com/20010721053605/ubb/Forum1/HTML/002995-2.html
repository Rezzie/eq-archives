I did a LOT of spell hacking the day I was banned (I basically dicked with every single value, figured out what each offset did, etc, and if I find my notes, I'll post them)

I was dicking around with spell values, changed all of them to FF, and found out I had a LONG recover time for Minor Healing, along with being teleported beneath the world, moving a lot faster than I used to, etc. But, what interested me was (besides the intra-zone teleporting) was the recover time. It's really hard to explain how to add spell effects, I point you to the spdat.eff thread and hope you figure it out

Basically there is room for 4 'effects' per spell, then 8 values, a minimum/maximum for each 'effect'. You can overwrite an AC effect or something that's already there with ultravision and you'll still get AC since it's serverside. For values on Yes/No True/False spells, just set the minimum value for that effect to 01, don't need a max.
