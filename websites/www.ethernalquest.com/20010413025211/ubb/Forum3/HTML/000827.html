Since no-one else wanted to field this one, the simple fact is that there is no data about a MOBs loot sent to the client until you go to loot the creature. The only exception would be items that are actually viewable while looking at the MOB in game. Which is why you see data about other players, because you can see what weapon they have in-hand, armor worn, etc.

Only one creature pops into my mind where this is NOT the case, and that is the Wisps. For some reason LS and GLS are distingushed differently, I can only imagine that the lighting levels around the wisps with GLSs is slightly higher (Like my perception is picking up that one! Not to mention my lame video card!) and the ones with LS and BOLS must have the same lighting level as they are always reported the same by ShowEQ.
