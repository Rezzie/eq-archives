Hence if you have answers to these question feel free to reply.

1. Items, do they have a specific number that makes them unique.
I.e. A blue diamond has number 30000004 and another blue diamond has 30000012? If no, does that apply for all items, or do the more rare or ueber items have unique numbers? In a very recent post somebody said that was not the case, but again, it was only one person who said that.

2. How are items kept track of?
Is there a record per character or per item? ( Without a specific number system it would make more sense that it was kept per person)
I.e. Record “KING” entry 1. Looted a “diamond”
Record “ FRIEND” entry 1. Received a “diamond from character “KING”
Record “KING” entry 2. Traded diamond to “FRIEND”

3. Is the zone and time items were traded/dropped part of the record?

4. There are no individual numbers or tracking associated with the items.

A blue diamond is item number 1998382 for example. ALL blue diamonds are that number. If anyone has a blue diamond on them, it is the database representation of the master item.

I am not sure if it is per person or per zone, but it is definately not per item.

The reason they do not go back and check the logs is it is difficult and would take "too much time" (IMO) even though it might take 30 minutes to find out the details, that is 30 minutes that they could have told another 20 people that "I am sorry, I cannot help you in this matter, there is nothign I can do"

If they REALLY want to, just about anything can be found out that happened in the game.
