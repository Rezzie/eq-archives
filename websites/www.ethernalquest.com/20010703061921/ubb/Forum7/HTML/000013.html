AI is _A_rtificial _I_ntelligence.

It's a fancy term for all automated action taken by someone other than the user or a human.

In this particular context, people want to make the MOBs (NPCs or monsters) able to interact according to some rules.


This interation would likely consist of, but not be limited to :
MOBs responding to Keywords and Hails.
Merchants selling items.
MOBs responding to attacks (this would trigger or invoke the monsters COMBAT AI, which is typically a seperate script, and would be run the the COMBAT flag is still active. So, COMBAT would end when a timer reached a certain point, when the monster dies, the last person on the HATE-LIST dies or disappears (MEM-BLUR, zones), and so on).
Pathing -- this would be how the MOB moves. His path over the map. Usually it would consist of a series of points. When the monster is forced to "deviate" from their path, then at their earliest convenience, they quickly either return to the divergence point OR find the closest part of the path to their current position and generate a traversable path to that point (this would take into account falls (like not falling off a platform in Kelethin to get to the Spires), water, lava and the like.
Questing logic (similar to the dialogue).
Faction logic.
Agro logic (related to faction).

In any case, these are usually "scripts" held in a database and loaded when the character's are initialized (like a zone RePop) or when the character is killed.
