Megaton
Innoruuk 		posted 03-23-2001 10:33 AM            
Actually you can't see the stats on items if you inspect someone. This has been like this since EQ's release. The itemcollector can't get the items this way either.

When you inspect someone it doesn't send you the items, it only sends you the name of the items, their location and the icon.

Here are the places you do get the complete item packet:

Looting from a corpse (a mob, or your own)
Viewing items at a vendor (you don't have to buy them, just opening the interface)
Summoning an item (or anywhere you create a new item, such as a tradeskill, etc)
Viewing an item in a trade window (don't need to complete the trade)

Also, when you zone the following items are sent: Items in your inventory, and items in your bank. These are currently encryted and thats why the itemcollector is not picking them up.

Some places where you won't get the item packets:
Wearing and removing gear
Taking stuff in and out of the bank
Inspecting someone.


Hope that clears up some things
