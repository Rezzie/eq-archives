1) Locate mission terminals

2) right click the mission term for individuals. When you find the terms, there will be 2 of them next to eachother, on the front one will have a thing representing a head and body, just one. The other will have 3 overlapping eachother partly. You want the one with a single person, the other term is for group missions.

3) Adjust the sliders to your preference. I play 50% difficulty, 100% bad, 100% money, everything else default.

4) Hit the request mission button

5) look at the 5 missions that it generates for you, (shift + click the icon to look at it. hopefully this has already been ingrained on you if you took the time to click those helpful little boxes in the noob area that say "SHIFT + CLICK ME" and actually raed them)

6) Click the one you want and hit accept mission, or adjust the silders a bit and hit generate mission to get a new set.

7) Travel to the playfiled your mission is in (you get this by reading the mission description)

8) hold-right click the mission icon in your mission display and pick the option that looks like a little grid. This puts an x on the map and a yellow indicator on your compass.

9) go to your mission site and zone in

10) complete the objectives for your mission

11) get reward

12) lather, rinse and repeat


Missions come in the following basic flavors

Radar Mission: you get some device, and must find the radar terminal in the mission area and while holding the device, right click the radar.

Inspect Object mission: Go to mission area and pick up the object.

Return Object Mission: Go to mission area, get object, and then while holding it, right click the mission terminal that gave you the mission (only mission requiring a round trip back to terminal)

Assasination Mission: Kill the specified target

Observation mission: Find the specified target and target him or click him a couple times until you get mission completed.


Note: assasination and observation missions have a chance for not being indoors like the other missions, in these cases you do not get a mission key. Get to your mission locs quickly, as as soon as you get the mission, your target is already walkign away from that location.
