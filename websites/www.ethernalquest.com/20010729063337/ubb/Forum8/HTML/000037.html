if you like beta's you'll love WWIIo. Its ok.. not my favorite game in the world. Very slow paced... it basically works like this.. you wait 4-6 minutes for it to load to the theater select screen. Finally it comes up, you select if you are german or french/british, then area of battle, you then select a vehicle (tank, truck, infantry, airplane or AT gun.) and wait for about 1 min for it to load up. Now you are in game. If you have a good map, you should know where to go. but unless you are driving a truck, it takes about 15 minutes to get to the action unless you are defending a base. Then as soon as you get there, you get shot and killed and you get to spend another 10-20 minutes getting back to the action to get shot again since you have to start all over.

maybe some poeple like to waste time with that. Not me. I give the game 1 star of 5.

I will give it credit for the flight sim portion of the game.. pretty cool.
