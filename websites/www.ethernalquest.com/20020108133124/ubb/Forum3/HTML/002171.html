Any kind of router will work, and any kind of hub. Just make sure it's a hub and not a switch. If it's a switch (like linksys routers) then it won't work.

If you want to use the linksys router get a Hub and connect it like this:

Cable/DSL -- Router -- Hub -- Computers

So both the linux box and EQ computer are connected to the hub, and the router is also connected to the hub.

I also have the LinkSys 4 port cable switch/router. Just purchase an additional hub that sits between your linksys and your computers. The kind of hub to get is a single speed hub, not a switchable one. I currently use an Addtron Technology 8 port Fast Ethernet Hub AEF 1008T. Did a search online for 100 speed hubs. This came up and it cost less than $40.00. No programs to load just plug it into the wall, bridge between the LinkSys and the new hub, plug the other computers into the new hub and boom, everything running A-OK.
