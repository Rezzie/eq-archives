Curious Necro
Zombie 		posted 09-03-2001 11:22 PM            
Pre-Velious game developers (not designers) were limited by a 32k cap for mob HP. They primarily used high-regen to make mobs seem bigger. Try Tarbul Earthstrider if you want to see impressive pre-Velious regen in action from a constantly available mob. Other mobs, like the outdoor Kunark dragons, and Ven Sath, would employ lifegaining or AE slows to the melee players to effectivly increase their hit points.

In Velious the hard limit of 32k was removed by the game developers (not the designers) and they probably went a bit overboard on making mobs with insane HP. Try Taskmaster Abbyot if you want a mob with insane HP.
