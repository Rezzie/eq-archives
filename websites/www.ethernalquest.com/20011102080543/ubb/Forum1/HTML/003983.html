image - you are wrong. it IS a hack.

It's not a hack in the way you are thinking though. Hack is a word, like many, that has many different definitions. Hack means many things at the same time. Yes, I can 'hack' my way into Verant's server and do stuff, or I can hack the head off of your grandmother. I can hack up your living room furniture, and I can hack a paragraph I typed in a Word Document. I can also hack a program to get it working, also called cracking. I can hack a game to give me endless life, and I can hack a game to let me play without an account.

Just because the word is the same, doesn't give it the same meaning. When I say I am 'hacking' everquest, I'm not saying it to sound elite or awesome to skript kiddies. I'm saying it because that is basically what I am doing. There are other terms to discribe it, hexing for example, but used in this way the two words mean the same thing.

I can turn off my alarm. My alarm can go off. I can run across the street. My computer is running. etc etc etc.
