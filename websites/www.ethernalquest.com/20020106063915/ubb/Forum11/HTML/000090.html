Reaver, This may sound stupid, but I did this a few times :
Make sure you are typing
/usr/lib/qt-3.0.1/bin/qmake odinseye.pro
and not
/usr/lib/qt-3.0.1/qmake odinseye.pro

in other words, don't forget the /bin/ part like I did, because /usr/lib/qt-3.0.1/qmake is actually a directory.
