QT 3.0.1 was just released, and all testers (and would be testers) are expected to upgrade. I'll update the INSTALL instructions shortly.

Since it seems the con color thing will never be fixed (Of all the testers only 2 people have emailed me con colors), we'll likely go beta soon.

The goals of the beta will be twofold:
- Increase the number of build platforms and clearly map the build requirements on each of these platforms. Win32 will NOT be one such platform, but other distributions of Linux will, and possibly a few other OS.
- Limited feature adding. Limited meaning small things like an option to make the map turn when you turn ingame, or an option to not show invaders in the spawn lists. Big things (map editing) will probably have to wait a little bit.
