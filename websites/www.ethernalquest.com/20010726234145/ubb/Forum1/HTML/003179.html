I wanted to know where we stood on this before doing anything further, but as I recall the best exploit I've found is the one in Paineel, the Erudite necro city. Basically, you start a level 1 newby in the city and go to the head librarian. Ask him something (I forget what now.) like "What history of Paineel?" or "What history of ____" or something, and he'll give you a book. Fill your bags with these books, and then run to the nearest merchant. Each book sells for like 1.2 gold apiece! With 8 bags you can make 10pp per very short trip, and with a macro program like Xylobot you can leave your char running all night and make quite a bit. I don't do it these days as I have better ways to waste time, but it was fun while I was lower lvl.

Note: I found this exploit on a msg board several weeks ago. While it worked at the time, it might be patched by now. Go try it and tell us the results, someone!

Zombie 		posted 07-09-2001 09:41 PM            
Plat making methods the hard way. (ie. no exploit/trainers)

Kindle in sol a is a good camp. drops impskin gloves as rare and sell for 2-3kpp depending on server. You will probably see a pair after 3-5 hours of camping. Plus you can walk away with several hundred plat in loots and FS. And camp a few other rares in the dungeon while camping kindle (platinum armbands, charred boots/guants etc..)
If you are high enough, camp idol of thorned, can take a while to get, but worth anywhere from 12-20kpp. The idol will take a while to camp. but shouldn't be longer than say 8-10 hours. for ~15kpp that's not that bad.
HGs can net you 250-500pp an hour depending on your level, number in zone etc....
The easiest method tho, is really to buy low and sell high. If you can get your hands on an fbss this is probably one of the better items for doing this. You can make an easy 1kpp per sale on these. just buy and sell em in fp. A lot of the velious 2 handers have extreme price ranges and you can sometimes make easy cash off of these. Find someone selling a 30/37 2 hander for like 5k and turn around and sell it for 10k. Get to know your server prices and item rarity and you won't have problems with cash.
I don't think you're going to find a magic pp generator. If they exist, the creater would be a moron to post it on this site.

Anyway, these are a few of my old faithful revenue generating methods. I'm not aware of any velious things, but it's mostly because of lack of hunting there as opposed to them not existing.
