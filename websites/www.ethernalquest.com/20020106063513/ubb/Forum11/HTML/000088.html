make sure you hub is really a hub. one of the problems for most seq users is that most linksys hubs are really hub/switches. they will only behave has hubs in VERY certain situations.

one 10mbit device hooked to a hub with 100mbit devices turns it into a real hub. if its all 100mbit, or all 10mbit... it is a switch. and the 10mbit and 100mbit parts are switched as well, so the seq/oe box can't be the one hooked up with 10mbit. great eh?

Most home routers act as a switch. Packets intended for port 1 are not seen by ports 2 through X (where X=4 in your case).

Buy a cheapy hub and connect it to one of the ports on your router. Then connect your Linux and DAOC PCs to the hub and set them both up for DHCP connections.

Voila!
