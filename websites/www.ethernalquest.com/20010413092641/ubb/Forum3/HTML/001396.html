Yes you need 2 machines to use EQ. One that you play EQ on and one running Linux with ShowEQ.

Here are the 2 most common setups.

Linux Gateway:
Net -> NIC1->Linux/ShowEQ Box->NIC2 -> To Hub /EQ Computer

In this setup all packets are comming through the Linux box, so ShowEQ can sniff them.

Broadcasting Packets w/ a Hub:
Net -> Hub -> Both EQ and Linux boxes

Packets sent to a Hub get broadcast to all ports, usualy the computer only looks at packets addressed to it, but showeq tells the computer to look at the packets with the same ip address as your eq computer.
