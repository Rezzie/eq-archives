The general rule is do nothing that does any kind of accelerated video or anything to the monitor.

Examples:

Let monitor go into power saving mode
Let a screensaver come on
Play mpgs/avis with Window Media Player, etc


The steps I use to make DXWindows work.

1. Make sure EQ already has Same Res on
2. Go in and turn it off
3. Go in turn it back on
4. ctrl-alt-d for full size

It sucks to have to login twice everytime, but for me seems to be the only way.
