From the XU page. . .

New in this upgrade: Basic Macros Support.

To bring up the macro window, press CTRL-INS-TILDE (~)

Currently supported are:
1. Single/Looped Playback of macros.
2. Record up to 100 macros (saved to the Macros folder)
3. Record Mouse Only, Keyboard Only, or Both

You must press F12 to stop a macro from playing (if looped) or to end recording.

The macro window can be closed and the macro can still be active. Again, press F12 to stop.

The macro code is still experimental, so please report any bugs to the forums.
