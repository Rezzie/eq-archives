All you have to do is simply do missions with the slider set all the way to the money side (you have to open the extended sliders with the down arrow next to the main slider). Just slide it all the way over to money since even all the way to XP gets you nothing for XP from the mission, just kill the stuff inside and you'll get plenty of XP. I set the main slider all the way to easy early on and do those missions and as missions begin to fill up with greys, I slowly move the slider up until I end up leaving it at the default position. But I always set it for maximum money and also get missions as close to my position as possible to reduce travel time.
At level 15, using no exploits at all, I have spent well over 100,000 credits (tried buying trade skill items from shops to build stuff, a bad idea) and have over 40,000 left on me. Item duping doesn't make crap for cash since everything sells back for about 10 creds. A 20 minute mission at level 15 makes me about 1800 credits plus whatever drops from the MoBs I kill (from humanoids I get 110-180 creds each).
