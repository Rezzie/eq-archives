its not hammer specific

when you attack in the game there are 2 functions of speed

one is attack, one is recharge

attack is the timer bar filling up, recharge is the timer bar going down prior to filling up again

however if you push "Q" at any time, your attack/recharge bar is auto shut off.

thus you can:
1: push Q to attack
2: wait for weapon to swing
3: immediatly push Q and voila your recharge time is gone
4: push Q to attack again

a large portion of the weapons in the game are fast enough for this not to matter, and a great deal of them have the same or similar attack and recharge and shutting off the recharge bar will only save you 1 perhaps 2 seconds on most average weapons

however this problem is exagerated vastly on weapons with high recharges of which there are a handful. the single most efficient weapon to use this exploit with is the 2hb hammer which has somewhere around a 7 second recharge and only a 3 second attack rate

thus you end up with a weapon that is "intended" to only swing one time every 10 seconds, but if you shut off the recharge your able to swing every 3 seconds, nearly trippling your damage rate

as said, this can be used to save a second or two here and there on most weps and thus usualy double your damage; however, the massive damage per swing on the hammer and than being able to tripple its rate of swing, is the uber version of this exploit

just a note, funcom is aware of this bug and is working on a fix.

i was contacted recently by a GM. if your not aware they are in the process of re evaluating the nano tech class and possibly putting in new spells and tweaking a few details about the class in general.

anyway, they have a tracking system in place that logs damage per second and per minute. if your damage per second or per minute exceeds thier rating for your class at your lvl it is red flagged and dropped to a log for a GM to evaluate.

well, apparantly i am have one of the highest damage per second and per minute of any nano tech my level and they wanted to discuss how and why i was able to achieve this and how this may affect other nano techs when the changes to the class are made

well, during the course of our conversation part of what we talked about was that (in his opinion) our appearing to be out damaged by other classes comes alot from them being able to pulse thier weapons and "cheat" the system out of its recharge rate

anyway along with a host of fixes coming for nano techs he assured me this bug is one of thier primary jobs at the moment, as that will cut exploiters damage by anywhere from 1/2 to 2/3s

btw if you want to know how im scoring highest in my lvl range for an NT. im not exploiting, im using 2he instead of pistol. currently a pure katana that only requires 2he, brawl, and dimach. the IP requirements of raising those 3 skills is LOWER than tryinng to use a pistol with any secondary attacks (fling/burst/full auto etc all cost MORE than brawl and dimach) and the minimal increase in IP over the pistol (2he is 12.5, pistol is 8 cost) is no where near the IP cost of raising dual ranged(20). an NT would need to dual wield pistols, and have at least flingshot, to even attempt to reach the damage potential of an NT using a good 2he with brawl/dimach. most NTs are using either a single pistol(about half my weapon damage output) or dual wielding weapons way under thier lvl(due to the massive cost of multi range).

so if your an NT, i highly recomend 2he

ps, if even half the changes the GM discussed with me are put in, NTs should be quite happy, and theres gonna be alot of disapointed exploiters when they find themselves at higher lvl suddenly without thier favorite exploits and no idea how to adapt and play the game as intended
