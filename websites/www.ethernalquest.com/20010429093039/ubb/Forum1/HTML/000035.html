A friend of mine got banned while in game, as the GM told him, "for using Winhack." Period. This person did indeed have Winhack running on his machine as I use its API for the GUI version of an EQHack program. This tells us one of 2 things...

1) Someone who knew he was "using Winhack" (and only 4 of his close online friends knew) ratted him out,

OR

2) Verant has a list of "proscribed" programs that it checks for. While its true no directory listing is sent back to the server, it could be a simple true/false flag if one of the proscribed programs was detected.

I am nearly 100% certain it is #2, above. Regardless, if you are using any widely used "hack" type program, including any of the programs available here, you should definatley rename any executables to be safe -- and I can gaurantee you Verant doesn't give a rat's ass if it is legal for them to check for other running executables on your machine or not.

Hopefully Ashran, Loony and the others will give this some consideration and start allowing for changed names on such things as .dlls -- as in you choose a custom name when you install the program.

One thing no one thinks of is the fact that at patch the patching software grinds through all of the files in your EQ directories and then compares them to a list on the server to determine if you need the patch, You give permission for this in the agreement you say "accept" on everytime you log on.

It only follows that they would have also listed "Bad" files in this check and all they have to do is flag an account for whatever they want when it shows up... its a simple file list and compare.

Actually busting the person is easy .. all they do is flag the account that has the "illegal" proggy in it and then check that accounts logs for discrepancies... they dont even have to say you have it all they have to do is say "We saw wierd activity in a log and so we looked deeper... So sorry your gone." end of story.

The moral... as any "Un-caught" hacker or cracker will tell you ....

"Don't use your primary account or any machine that can be traced to you for purposes of experimentation, or some time no matter how careful you are you will get nailed for it."

Why do you think there are so few Hackers that are well known.??. Its because the "REALLY" good ones don't ever make a mistake that can be traced to them. Hence they are not known by anyone except maybe a close friend.

Be smarter than the software, after all it only does what we tell it to.
