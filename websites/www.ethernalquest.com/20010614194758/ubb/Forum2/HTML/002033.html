Zombie 		posted 06-06-2001 09:30 PM         
Yes, they are all the same models. It's like in Quake or Half-Life where you can change your skins, Verant did the same thing with the dragons in the game. A model is the actual 3D polygons and the skin is the textures that go on top of the polygons. #type lets you change the skins, however, I'm not sure of the mechanics of the command, so you may not be able to use #type for skins that weren't discovered at the time Ashran was actually working on it. In that case you'd be SOL, there's no way to add models or types other than hacking the exes but you'd have to be very good at assembly.
