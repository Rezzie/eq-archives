Hmmm....I'll try to field this question sans flames as you don't directly ask for a hack or something...
At Level 23 you aren't supposed to have a lot of money. You will gain money with levels. As you get up in the levels the money starts to flow. I get average about 4 pp per kill now and the cash flows reasonably well.
You can look at some of the spoiler sites to find out easily campable items that will net you alot of cash.
THERE IS NO FREE MONEY TRAINER IN EQ.
Just so you know that. Please don't ask for one, they don't exist and you will be flamed up tha wazoo for it.
As a first character, SKs are relatively difficult as they are KOS to alot of mobs and they don't really become powerful until the later levels. You might want to switch to a druid or something. they are fast leveling and can make good money easily.
Maybe try doing the bandit sash quest. It made me pretty good money for that level range if I remember correctly.
As well, 2 months isn't that long to be playing the game. Level 23 is reasonable for that time I think. Enjoy the game, don't worry about what everyone else has. Get up in levels and the money will come. Pretty soon that 1kpp that you have been seeking will look like peanuts.

Here's some tried and true money makers for someone in your level range:

WIllowisps - take Greater Light Stones to the Dark Elves in S.Ro and turn them in to the Smith for a book worth 8 plat.

High Quality Bear Skins. Go to Everfrost and whack bear cubs, or Black bears in Karanas, Commons..
Tailors pay around 20-35 plat each for them for backpacks.

Leather Padding. Get a skinning knife from a smith. combine high and med quality pelts with the skinning knife in a tailor kit to make low quality pelts.
Combine two spiderLING silks in a tailor kit to make silk thread. Combine Low quality skin and silk to make leather padding. Used in smithing.
People power-leveling smithing will pay 100pp per stack of padding.

Silk swatches - With the new Coldian Shawl quest requiring trade skills, lots of high level players are taking up tailoring. Silks are in big demand for skilling up. Hunt spiders in Karans, Guk, FoB, commons and combine two spider silks in a tailor kit for a Silk Swatch.
Sell stacks of silk swatches for 100pp each.

and finally: LEVEL!
The FASTEST way to make Plat in the game is to Level up.

EDIT: that was "smithing", not "smiting"...
