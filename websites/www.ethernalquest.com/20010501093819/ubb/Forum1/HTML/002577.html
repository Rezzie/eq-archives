Items purchased from a vendor are NOT logged. If you found a source or a way to buy items without cost, then you found a gold mine. Now using the items in a world container (oven) is tracked, the moment you put em in there it is logged. Also the moment you give an item away it is tracked, however the bigtrade logs do not track normal items, only expensive, plat, magic, lore or no drop items are tracked. If VI tracked everyone handing in fire beetle eyes, the log would be too large to handle.

Be forewarned, while what you are buying is not tracked, others will turn you in and you will eventually get a warning (a VI version of a cease & desist) once you get that, DO NOT do it again as they will be watching
