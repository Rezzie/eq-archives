On the spere site, in the board area, there used to be a single world file you could use. Had critters npcs etc (not many but some) I think it may even have had the doors for the buildings. Makeing your own world is a lot of work. I'd suggest anyone interested to check out some of the registered sites (also listed on the spere location).

You have very little to change (just an IP address I believe) and all the various boards have there own little rule sets. Some no Pking, some you start out with skills at 50, some with 1000 gold pieces. And some are open to general public and some you have to send an email requesting to be allowed to play on their boards.

I started my own little shard, wass ok but just a lot of work. I liked having GM power and ability. But creating every little thing kind of dragged me down. There are a lot of support routines on the spere board as well but you will have to do some reading to get set up and running (as well as having a copy of UO hehe)

Good luck. I quit UO originally because I was PKd thru the side of a house, looted and lost four houses worth of property. That convinced to me switch to EQ and I haven't been online with UO (proper) since.
