Yew leaves spawn randomly through out the zone. I've never counted but the maximum number is around 30 or 40 I think. The respawn rate is about 10 minutes I think.

The problem is that when a server has been up for a while they get collected and respawn. Every time one spawns there is a chance (pretty good one too) that it might spawn under a tree or rock. That means that as time goes on the maximum number of leaves obtainable goes down.
They aren't that bad to find if give time. I've found quite a few without SEQ just by running through the zone.
