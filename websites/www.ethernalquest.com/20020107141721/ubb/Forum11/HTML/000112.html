When your running OE, open up another console and run top. You'll be able to see how much memory OE and others are taking up with totals at the top of the screen.

OE itself shouldn't be taking up that much memory. You did make a swap file right? Depending on what your running you may want to increase it(which would involve reparitioning, go elsewhere for help on that). Just close anything else you have open that your not really using. GUI junk can take up a lot of memory. Scale back your window manger or Gnome/KDE to get more memory back.
