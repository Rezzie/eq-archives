- Go in a laggy dungeon, dont open chests.
- Kill all the mobs.

- Open it from you back.
- For example, pick up bullets from the backpack.
- Split the stack in your inventory so it becomes full.
- A friend have to do the same.

- Go to a closed chest that suppose to be full of items.
- Open it.
- When clicking an item, the game display a message that you are full.
- You have to free a slot. Dont make so.
- Your friend has to click on the chest too.

Miracle ! He can open it too and view all the objects inside !

- Both of you, free a slot in dropping a bullet on the floor and in picking up an item in the chest.

- Both of you have got the item.
