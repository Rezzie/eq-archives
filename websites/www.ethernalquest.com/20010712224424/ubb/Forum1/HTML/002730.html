Zombie 		posted 05-20-2001 09:29 AM         
If you want to get the complete models, you'll have to figure out Verant's 3D model file format, unless it is the same as the same as the zone 3D format. If it is you can just rip the code from the ZoneConverter that does the actual converting and make a program that turns it into a common 3D model format. It's probably not the exact same thing, but it may be close. Let's just say you'd have quite a bit of work cut out for you, and if you're not a programmer, forget about doing it yourself and try to convince someone who is to do it

The emulator allows you to connect to a non-Verant server, but it's not even close to a production server at the moment. You can summon NPCs (who just stand around) and items, but there is no combat or spellcasting or anything fancy. Summoning NPCs with the emulator to get screenshots of them *would* be a hell of a lot easier than figuring out the 3D file format though Someone once made a program to convert the output files of ZoneConverter to the Quake 2 map format, but it was never available for download and I forgot who was making it.
