Hey welcome to another one of my long dumb posts.
What does everyone like and dislike about Everquest?
Ya I know this topic doesnt really have anything to do with EthernalQuest but I figured if Verant_hater can post about ghostbuster mods for halflife then why cant I post this right?

I've played UO for 2+ years,EQ for 6 months, AC for 6 months, and I plan on trying out AO, Horizons, and Atriarch when they come out.(hopefully I'll have money by then .)

These are some things I like about EQ compared to the other games:

1)Easy to Roleplay, I think EQ is the easiest game to roleplay in because of all the different races.

2)Best Graphics, I know graphics arent the most important feature in a game but they are nice to have .

3)Different Races, Finally I can play something other then human!(although my main EQ character was a human monk...)

4)World, I like EQ's world best. I'm tired of walking around UO/AC and just seeing forest and dungeons that look the same.

5)Lots of classes, Instead of one simple mage class we have cleric, wizard, enchater, etc.... Instead of one type of fighter we have Warrior, monk, barbarian, etc....

6)Long time to lvl up, It takes a long time to get high lvl(unless you play 24/7 and have no life like my friend who had a 50lvl druid. No i'm not jealous! ........). In UO you can become Grandmaster in a couple days or less.

And now some of the things I dislike about EQ:

1)Camping, probably the biggest reason I quit EQ is because of camping. Its hard to get experience when everyone else is hogging up the monsters! In UO and AC its easy to get experience because you could just walk in the forest and find a monster or animal to fight.

2)Exp loss, the 2nd biggest reason why I quit EQ. I wouldnt mind Exp loss too much if it were easy to get my exp back but because of camping its tough to find something to fight.

3)Monsters are hard, For example in UO I could fight 4 Ettins at the same time easily but try fighting 4 monsters your lvl at the same time in EQ, you will be eaten alive unless your twinked . This isnt really a bad thing because its good to have a challenge in games.

4)Monsters follow you till you zone. you mean I have to run all the way through the karanas just to get this monster off my back, sigh...

5)I can only get up to lvl 50? Well its 60 now(I stopped playing EQ before the first add on). Ya I know it takes a long time to get to 50/60th lvl but wouldnt it be nice if the lvl limit was 100 or if there was no lvl limit like AC. A boy can have his dreams right....

Ok thats all for now. And no i'm not trying to bash any of the games listed above I think they are all good in their own way.
