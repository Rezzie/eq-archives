Heya cornflakes. I have not compiled or used ShowEQ as of yet so I am taking a stab in the dark here. But it looks as if you have installed the source rpm and not the binary for the zlib compression library. The RPM you are looking for is zlib-1.1.3.i386.rpm. You may want to check out http://www.rpmfind.com. It is a good resource for tracking down rpm's. Hope this information helps.

Oh on another note it is not neccasary to copy the rpm to any directory other than the one you are installing it from. Once it is installed it can be deleted or archived. Think of a RPM as tarball or zip.
