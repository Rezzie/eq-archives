I tried it on 3 different system with different specs.

First i tried is a Pentium 75 with 64meg . The installation and compilation took an entire day and SEQ did run on it but it was extremely painful to use and you would have alot of slow down and locking up for a couple of second when you tried to scroll in the list. Not very usuable.

Second i installed it on a P3 600mhz with 128meg ram. This was a perfect system for it and it worked like a charm.
