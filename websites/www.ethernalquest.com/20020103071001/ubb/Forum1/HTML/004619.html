When it comes to processors, it's best to get them in the retail box, and not OEM. The stuff of pricewatch is usually OEM. Most of the time, that's fine, but there is always that one in a million chance that you're going to get a defective processor.

The processors in the retail box come with a fan and other goodies that you normally wouldn't get, as well. There is also a manufacturer warranty instead of a dealer warranty, so you can go directly to AMD if there is a problem.

The XP 1700s are $195 with a retail box.
