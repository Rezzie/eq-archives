Just for info, HP is completely stored SERVER SIDE... meaning you could easily make a spell full heal you, and it would show full HP client side, but once you get hit again, and the server side hp hit's 0, you are dead... even if client says you have 900hp left.

So with you example, Call of Bones would LOOK like it was regening hp as well as mana client side, but in reality your hp is going down on the server.
