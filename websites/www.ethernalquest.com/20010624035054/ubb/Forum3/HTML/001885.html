You can see what is on a mob IF the item is a "visible" item on the mob. In other words, you can see usually see wielded weapons, shields, visible armor pieces (BP, Legs, etc). You cannot see things like earrings, rings, belts, etc.

As far as I know, you have to be in the same zone to have ShowEQ inform you a mob has popped.
