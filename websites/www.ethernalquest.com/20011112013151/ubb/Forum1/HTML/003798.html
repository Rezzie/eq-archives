There is a tea I make when I have to stay up long hours and I don't want to be jittery from too much caffeine.
Requires going to an herb store.

Pick up some Cramp Bark.
It's a low level relaxant. Add Cinnamon and maybe mint.
The cinnamon acts as a stimulant (mint is a stimulant, too) and the cramp bark keeps you from getting nervous or jittery. Fresh ground cinnamon works best. You could probably use cinnamon oil as well.
You could add Ephedra type herbs (Ma Huang) but I find that makes me more jittery than caffeine.
