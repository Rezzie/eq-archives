Active development on ShowEQ continues at http://sourceforge.net/projects/seq. Although the development crew seems to have changed quite a bit from the original group, that's not all that unusual for Open Source projects. People tend to come and go as their interest level and time permit.

ShowEQ continues to be a very useful addition to Everquest despite the broken encryption. Version 2.3 is simply nonfunctional, version 2.4.1 is barely functional, and version 2.4.3 seems to work pretty well. The latest version has working experience calculations, improved/updated maps, and (as always) accurate player positioning on the map.

No sense writing it off just yet.
