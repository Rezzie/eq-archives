What you are more than likely experiencing (pardon the pun) is that the graphical bars don't accurately reflect the actual numbers in a linear way - at least in my experience. Basically, the middle of the bubble looks like it moves considerably faster than the "scrunched up" parts of the bubble. If you wanna see what half a bubble looks like, try jumping a bunch and looking at your stamina bar. A jump looks to take 3/4 of a bubble, then the next jump looks to take about 1/4. Its an optical illusion because of the little "scrunchies" in the bar. At least, as far as I can tell.

I'm not so sure about the accuracy of the bars being the problem. This isn't the first time someone has posted that a person below them in level is actually getting more xp than they are.

I recently sat killing mobs with a monk quite a few levels below me. He just wanted to get his skills up and I was really just killing time, so I didn't care that I wasn't getting all the xp for it. Turns out that, even though all mobs were xp-giving for me and I always did the majority of the damage, he was getting much more xp (bubble-wise, and hell was not an issue) than I was.

This is at odds with Verant's statement that they tried to tune it so people who group level at the same rate. Perhaps they also tuned it so that people who group are more likely to converge in levels, as the lower levels get more xp and can catch up?

I do know they did some sort of tuning. I usually group with my wife, who has a much higher modifier than I do, but we stay matched, bubble-for-bubble, until we level, whereas I should have 5 bubbles for every 4 of hers.

Someone with ShowEQ should carry out the following tests:

- Two people, same level, vastly different modifiers. What's the xp division when one or the other does the most damage?

- Two people, same modifier (preferably exactly same race/class), different levels. What's the xp division when one or the other does the most damage? Repeat for 1,2,3 levels difference, preferably up to the point where the lower stops getting xp.

Anyone have the time & resources to do those?
