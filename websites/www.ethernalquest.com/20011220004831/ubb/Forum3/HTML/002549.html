I made a fix to my own copy of ShowEQ over the weekend, and it seemed to work, albeit with a large degree of inaccuracy (this inaccuracy will never be fixed, unfortunately).

In players.cpp, where it handles an Experience packet, exp->exp used to contain the actual experience amount. It now contains a number between 0 and 330 which tells the EQ client how much of the experience bar to fill. Since 1 blue = 4% and 1 orange = 20%, then 66 points = 1 orange and about 13 points = 1 blue.

You can estimate the total experience (what you used to receive in the packet) as follows:

Total exp. to beginning of this level + (amount of exp needed to complete this level * exp->exp / 330)

So, I did that formula, then just did exp->exp = (the result), and let the rest of the code continue on.
