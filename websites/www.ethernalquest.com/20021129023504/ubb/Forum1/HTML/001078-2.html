Two more to add to the list:

In Unrest: Pull mobs from the house via the porch area and run along the wall towards the hedge. As soon as you cross the hedge line the mob (if not too close) will immediately turn and run back to the house. Take a step towards the house and it comes back. Great fun on red mobs.

In Cabalis East: There is the left overs of a building behind Klok Wiga that will allow you to get behind the invisible "Player Retention Barrier" and cruise the outer wall all around town. Needless to say the npc's are also subject to the "Barrier" and unless they cast, are helpless.

two quick ones, you listed most of mine

Dalnirs, dunno if this still works, but after you zone in there's a small rectangular room. Stand against the wall right wall there and mobs can't get you.

Go down a set of stairs and run to your right in the hallway if you're not already on that side. Then just stand against that wall and blast on either of the 2 guides. I think there's 3 guides you can pull here, and if you let one of em run, they will run away and you can then pretty much pull the whole building this way. Great exp after you're finished with the mechanics, just watch out for getting suspended =)

my 2 cents....

This is merely an example, since there are probably hundreds of similar ones in almost every zone.

In North Qeynos, at the Jewel Box / Ironforges, the little fence, jump on it, use it to get to roof, jump rooftops or get lev, and get to the wall, it's pretty easy. once on top of the wall, you can walk anywhere within the city, and if you cast on something, the mob will run about 10 feet, then stop and sit there.

The exception is mobs that cast, like Corporal SomethingOrOther, if he sees you beating up his friend, he will cast you to hell.

Rogester Thurston (or something lihe that) and Lashun Novashine both cast also, but there are very few others that cast, and the corporal is the only one that wanders throughout the entire zone.

Just thought I'd post one of the only two i've ever figured out on my own.
(The other one being the hill/wall behind Millers in QHIlls)
