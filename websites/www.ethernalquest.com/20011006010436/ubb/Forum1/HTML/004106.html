If you want to do foraging and save the special items, here's what you do.

First, you need a few of the items to begin with. Then spread them out so that there is only 1 in each slot. So instead of having 8 drake eggs in one slot, have 1 drake egg in 8 slots. Now do your macro so that you forage and drop in your auto-equip slot. The drake eggs will fall into the slots that already have eggs until there are 20 in the stack. Then it will drop on the next one and so on until all stacks have 20 in them.
