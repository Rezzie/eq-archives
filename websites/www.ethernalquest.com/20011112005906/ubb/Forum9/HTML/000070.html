From what i have seen this is what happens
All spells that are not of the same line (ie just a upgrade of another spell will stack)

For example you can not stack Augments with expertice spells since the expertice is just an upgrade to the other spell But Expertice will stack with any other spell that effects the same stat. I'd imagine that the DoTs work the same way.
