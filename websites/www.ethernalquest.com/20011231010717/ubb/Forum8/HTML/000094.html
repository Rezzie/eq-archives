Zombie 		posted 10-28-2001 08:21 PM            
Well, I plan to have no classes. Everything will be determined by skills. As you advance in skills, such as 1-Handed Slashing weapons, you will have access to special attacks, such as a Blur Strike which fully ignores your target's AC. Their will be some skills that raise your magic resistance. To determine your magic resist, it is in this formula:Level + Resist + Charisma Bonus

To determine how well your spell will hit, it is in this formula:
Level + Spell Hit Chance (sometimes it is d100) + Wisdom Modifier
