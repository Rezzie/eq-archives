My primary development system took 6 hours to compile QT3 with full debugging and gcc3. (P3-450)

My memory-leak test platform took 20 minutes to compile QT3 will full debugging and gcc3.

All the files take too long to compile, but no one file should take more than a few minutes.

Yes it picks up reliably

It can be compiled either in or out of XWin.

64 MB RAM and 128MB swap is almost certainly not enough Compiling large c++ template code eats RAM, and if you're compiling under X, you already used those 64 MB on your windowing system. I'd say 128 MB RAM and 256 MB swap is minimum.

I haven't found anywhere that has QT3 compiled in binary form in the way that it's needed for OE.

Yes, there are plenty of IRC channels about #linux, but they usually aren't that helpfull.

No, different versions of QT can coexist.
