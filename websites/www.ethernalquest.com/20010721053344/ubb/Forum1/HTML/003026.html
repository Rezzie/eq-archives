You can't just generate packets and throw them at the server when you want, A) it won't work and B) it will get you banned. Including other security measures, all the packets include a sequence number, you'd have to both send the right number in your packet, and fool the client into thinking that it had already sent that packet#, so it wouldn't send a duplicate sequence#.
