What iz this werld...? What has it for me?
What iz the untold...? What iz there to see...?
Who makes all the rules?... Who dares break free...
Why iz there no God?... Why must it all be?...

All this and more... Shall be answered right here...
Time iz a whore... Death iz too near....
Life is unseen... The unseen iz fear...
This werld has been "screened"... It iz God's career...

The God you all worship iz simply a digit...
Not all of this bullshit religeous and frigit...
A balance of minds... A balance of orders...
Ironic rewinds... and mental-like borderz...

This depth that I speak of... Few have yet sought....
This stench that we reek of... Iz what we are tought...
You know nothing.... Try to remember...
You read it while waiting... Break and dissmember...

Why can't people see...? Why can't they just leap...?
These false memories... It's all getting too deep...
Why must we all drown... In the unknown abyss...?
Perhaps just a frown... Or perhaps just a kiss...

This makes no sense... you think to yourself...
It's all but a dance... A toy on a shelf...
But no I tell you... It's all very real...
In your short existance... It should be a big deal...

Reynold Hollins (Atrox) of the Advin Clam in Athens
