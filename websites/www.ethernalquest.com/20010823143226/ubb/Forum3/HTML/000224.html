For what it's worth, I solved my problem.

Upon clicking 'connect' windows was kicking up a dialer 'connect to isp' dialog box. Since the dialog was behind the un-alt-tabable EQ it appeared to hang. I went into control panel/internet in 98 and changed it from 'I connect to the internet via a modem' to 'I connect to the internet via a lan'. Thereafter it works great.

There is no notable difference in lag going this way although it appears as if I zone slower. Maybe they did something that increased zone load times in this last patch.

Zombie 		posted 08-20-2001 10:09 PM         
To resolve hostnames your machines that are behind the firewall need to know the IPs of the DNS servers for your ISP.

ipchains -A -i ppp0 -s <home network> -d !<home network> -j MASQ

<home network> == the network all your machines are on. In my case that would be 10.0.0.0/24
