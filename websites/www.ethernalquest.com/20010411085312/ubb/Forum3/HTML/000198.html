CyberTech
Decaying Skeleton 		posted 04-08-2000 12:28 AM         
You can't sniff a 100base network from a 10base card because, as mentioned above, on a hub that has 10/100 autoswitching, the 100base and 10base ports end up on different segments.

The only way to do this would be to have

EQ machine->10/100 hub->10base hub

Then into the 10base hub you plug your sniffer and router. Many variations on this of course, but I hope you get the idea.

My own configuration is:

EQ->10/100 switch->firewall->10base hub w/sniffer attached->router/firewall2
