Things to check:

Make sure your IP address of your linux box is on the same subnet that you are sniffing.

If you are using a switch you have to configure it to allow monitoring of traffic from all ports.

Regarding the 10/100 speed differences if it's a switch it doesn't matter the switch will convert the packet data to be readable by both 10mb and 100mb links.

If it's a hub, then it has to be a Dual-Speed hub with share channels (ie: 10mb can see 100mb and vice versa) otherwise the 10mb and 100mb channels will be independant of one another.

What type of hub/switch are you using? I can give you the further details if you can get me a model number.
