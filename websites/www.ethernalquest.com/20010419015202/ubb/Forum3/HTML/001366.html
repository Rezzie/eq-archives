Zombie 		posted 03-31-2001 01:16 AM            
Use the "Dump Drops <ctrl>-D" menu option to print the identity and loc of every ground item in the zone to stdout.

The following info may have changed since it's been a while since I looked at this, but it was accurate as of about four months ago. Maybe someone else can confirm.

Ground items don't seem to spawn in exactly the same location every time, but often they spawn in almost the same location.

Unfortunately, they also spawn underneath immovable objects (trees, structures, etc.) effectively removing that drop from the game until the server is rebooted. They do not ever seem to despawn if not picked up.
