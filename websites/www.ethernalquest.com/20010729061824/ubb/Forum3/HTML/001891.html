I found this on the Monk boards..

Faction is on a point system in the game. Ever faction hit gives you anywhere from 1 to 50ish points ( some up to 100, but that's extreamly rare ). Most points are given in a 1-5 point scale, and every 5 points from there. For example:
Skyshrine Frost Giant toes quest gives you 5 points to CoV, while the Giant Warrior helm quest gives you 15 points.

Here's then numbers for ya:

p1100+ - Ally
p800+ - warmly
p500+ - kindly
p100+ - amiable
0 - indifferent
n100+ - apprehensive
n500+ - dubious
n800+ - threatening
>n800 - Ready to Attack

Not sure if its accurate but something we can try and validate.
