No, an emulated server has nothing whatsoever to do with Verant's servers.

Here is a simplified explanation:

When you launch EQ you log into one of Verant's servers where your characters and stuff are stored. You play a bit, get stuff and log out. The server remembers what you did and got and saves that (none of this exists on your PC only the server).

An emulated server lets you log into it in place of Verant's servers. Since you control the emulated world you can do anything you want (not just anything yet as the emulator isn't actually fully functional yet). Obviously none of this has any impact on the real servers and your characters stored there. In fact right now the emulator doesn't even save your character info at all as that part hasn't been done yet, but even a fully functional emulated server would have zero impact on the real ones.
