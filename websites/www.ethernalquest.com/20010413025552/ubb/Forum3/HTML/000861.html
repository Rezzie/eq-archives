"I know EQ needs real IP's "
I have my linux box connected to my ISP via a 56k modem and all works fine for both my 95 machines. My 95 machines ips are 192.168.0.131 & .132

First thing you need to do is get ppp working.

Go into linuxconf and configure PPP. Its not hard....only mistake I made was that I picked the wrong port the first time. Once you get your linux box dialing in so it can talk to the internet make sure that your 95 boxes can see the linux box (ping them)

Next make sure your 95 boxes have the linux box set up as their gateway. Easiest thing to do is set the DNS servers manually on your 95 boxes to your ISPs primary and secondary DNS also.

There was 2 lines I had to put in the
firewall.conf
(I think thats the file) script to get ip forwarding enabled...I'll get and post that info tonight.

I didn't have to recompile the kernal. But I'm using RH 6.2 with the .14 kernal.
