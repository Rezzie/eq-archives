Making Treatment kits involves the following steps:

1. Convert monster parts into blood plasma with Bio Comminutor (sp?)
2. put blood plasma into antiseptic jar (bought in store)
3. put a special oil (bought in shop, forgot exact name) into the converted jar
4. put the result into a empty treatment kit (the black ones, not from medic booth !)

voila, new Treatment Kit !

It's name differs from the store-bought ones, has lower requirements and higher HP numbers. But they have it's downsides: They are expensive to make and i couldn't stack them (even with same QL). A stack of 50 store bought treatment kits is WAY cheaper.

As a side note: every conversion step gives you QL*5 experience points, which seems to apply to all the trade skills.

The exact description of the steps can be found in a book at vendor machine. You need to right-click it in your inventory to get the detailed informations, so you actually have to buy it. But for less then 2000 credits it won't hurt most people.

There seems to be another recipe in the book, but i never tried it ...
