Here's an example...

1). Open "Qeynos.s3d" with EQInside.
2). Scroll down the right-hand column until you get to "Qeynos.wld" and single-click on it
3). Choose "Archive" from the menu above, then under that, choose "Save Data ..."
4). You'll now see a standard Windows save box... Just proceed naturally from there.

Hope this helps.
