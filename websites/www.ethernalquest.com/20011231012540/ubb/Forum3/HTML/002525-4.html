Hey Knighttime,
If you are really using a Linksys 5 port hub, check and see if it is either a 10Mb or 10/100Mb. I have a Linksys 5 port 10Mb and a 3com 16port 10Mb hub and they work just fine. A hub, be it passive or active doesn't look at the packet type, it is only concerned with amplifying, retiming and re-transmitting the bit stream. A switch on the other hand will look at the MAC layer of the packet and could feasibly forward all broadcst packets to the uplink port of the switch. However a switch usually sends broadcast packets to all ports on the switch unless some type of frame tagging is taking place. Let me know what you find out.
