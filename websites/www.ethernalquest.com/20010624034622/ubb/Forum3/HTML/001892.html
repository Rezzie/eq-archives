a routers ethernet ports are 99% of the time switched ports.

there is not really such thing as a 'step up from a switch'. Ethernet ports are either switched ports or they arent, no other options there. A routger generally provides more functionality than a common switch, and that is prob what the rep you talked to meant.

Put the EQ and SEQ boxes on a hub together, or if you can manage that router, see if you can configure it to broadcast traffic to a port.
