Anyone that wants to share info on quests that either give great exp or a high reward for the level of it, post away. I'll start off with a couple I can think of off the top of my head.

Level 3 - 6: Kaladim - In the cleric area, the bone chip quest. Turn in four chips (stacked ok) to Gunlok Jure, get quite a bit of exp. I've gone from 3 to 6 (killing NPCs is easy enough to get through 1 and 2) with about two backpacks of bone chips. Once you turn 7, the amount of exp dwindles down significantly.

Level 3 - 7?: Qeynos - Turn in rabid wolf pelts to Caulria (I think?). Again, these come up with a lot of exp. The wolves are level 3-5. Later (10 or so) you can do the same with rabid bears for another significant exp boost.

Level 8 - 12: North Karana (good)/ South Ro (evil) - The old stand-by money-maker in the early part of the game, willowisps. They are about level 8-11. So it's reasonable exp for making the kill, then turn in GLSes to the gypsy camps for the 8-10p book. I had a corpse decay on one young character I didn't play for a few months, and used this to reoutfit him in a relatively short time.

Level 1 - ?: Paineel - I haven't done this one in a long time, but it is the history books quest. One of the three books sells for around one gold (the other two only sell for a few copper). Just say the phrase to the NPC, collect a billion books, cash them in to any vendor. As you can imagine, it is quite tedious.

Level 30ish rogues, the Burning Rapier quest. I did this one just after hitting 30 (cursed hell levels!) and wound up getting half of level 30's exp for it all. There were about 3 pieces of the quest that each gave around 500k exp. I don't know if others can go through the quest, or if you could repeat one of those pieces over and over for the easy 500k. It's been too long for my memory.

Of course with anything like these, you will have to spend as much time working on skills as you do repeating the quests.

Others?

Duh, there are a few thousand web pages that list all quests. But they don't tell ya exactly how much exp you get for them, like we can here with SEQ.

For instance, working from memory (haven't sat down and did the quests in a while), the rabid wolf quests give about 4000 exp points. Considiring you get about 1500 for killing the wolf, that's an extra 2+ kills for free.

The GLS quest (when you're in appropriate level range) is about 7500 exp points per try; the equivilent of about 1 extra kill.

Many of the quests I've done have server-side level limits. As you advance, you get more exp points until some peak, then it quickly diminishes. I suppose it's their attempt to prevent level 1's from doing something like the Burning Rapier and making several levels in one shot.

Speaking of the BR quest, after pondering about it since the last post, I believe the part of killing Lynada and turning in her purse was the quickest to one of the big exp chunks. My rogue didn't need to do any faction building, but I don't know (yet) if there is anything to prevent other classes from doing it. Of course the downside is Lynada is like a 4 or 8 hour spawn, so no power-levelling here, but it may be a nice bit of exp if you just are near SolA, first time logging on for the day, etc.
