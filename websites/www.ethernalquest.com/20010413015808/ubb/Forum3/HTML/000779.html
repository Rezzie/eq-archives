I suspect your router/hub is acting like a switch and not a hub. Showeq is a passive monitoring tool. It does not do any sort of probing. What this means it needs to see the broadcast packets, but a switch/router will not broadcast, instead it does what the name implies, it routes packets to the appropriate ports, hence, your Linux box has no direct visibility to the packets coming accross.

A switch routes packets to specific ports, where as a hub broadcasts packets to all ports. You may need to research the information for your equipment to see if it directs packets to specific ports, or if it indeed is a hub, which broadcasts all packets to all ports. If you discover it does not broadcast to all ports, you can use a hub. Connect the hub to one of the ports on the Router/switch, and connect your Win9X machine and your Linux machine to the newly installed hub. This will allow your Linux box to passively sniff your Win9X packets, and will allow connectivity to the net.

I hope this helps!
