Best way to PL?

Get a high lvl enchanter. No one's better for do'n it.

Tips for PL'ing druid - go to any newbie zone til around lvl 5. Go to lavastorm (best source for summoned npc's at that level). Fight elementals + other summoned mobs - nuke + mindblur. At lvl 16, shadowed men CAN be hit with spells. Nuke (or use pet / chain-charm) shadowed men; mindblur before ded then nuke with druid.
You have level 20 within 1 hr played.
--Stay with shadowed men until approximately lvl 29 or so. Port to NK, go to Paw. Camp Ishva or anywhere else down there that you feel comfortable. Use pet, or chain-charming meathod. Mindblur, nuke with druid.
--With great timing, and adequate spawns (something you get plenty of in splitpaw) - Level 40 can be reached within 24 hrs game play. I did this with my 2nd account, however I got bored camping the ishva so I went to rack up cash from HG's. This is slower xp, 'cause there's tons of people pulling HG's in Rathe mtns, but the cash further funded my blacksmithing.
Optional areas for high 30's to low 40's is the Overthere and Dreadlands. Once obtaining lvl 40, move to the Hole. This is semi-tricky for one person to do 2 accounts, only because the mobs have higher hps / lvl compared to the previous. Use same techniques of chain-charming. Mobs drop easy and fast. XP moves as smoothely at 40 - 45 as it had with lvl 30 - 35. (not really, but seems to with hard PL'ing.)
Takes about another 36hrs play time to get to lvl 50. I've done this with 3 druids (various times per, but same meathods.)

I still wanna PL a rogue up, 'cause in my opinion, it's one of the funest classes to play - because of how it's played - Evade really turned the stratagey around long ago

I've heard people talk about taking static spawns, like the barbarian camp in WK - but if you don't want to worry about factions, Splitpaw, Guk, and Perma work well - for those in pre-kunark dungeons. I haven't spent much time in Kunark yet alone Velious.
