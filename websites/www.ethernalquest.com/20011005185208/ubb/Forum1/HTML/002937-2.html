Kinkyfellow
Decaying Skeleton 		posted 06-27-2001 10:26 PM         
Part of hacking is learning how to restrict actions that allow for you to keep looking for the same 'offsets' over and over. Once you find certain things you'll notice alot of the time they use similar settings for similar things. After that you just learn to think like a programmer (if you do programming you should think like one) and you find out that they keep things constant when possible to make finding bugs easier.

Each person goes about finding these offsets differently too. Some work alone some work in teams. 4 people searching for the same offset can cover alot more ground faster then 1 but only if they work as a team and discuss what they find.

Most of what i found was from self searching and i had praticed mem hacking on many other games and kind of 'knew' what i was looking for. It takes pratice and i'll tell ya tho if your ever in doubt and dont mind possibly getting caught, crashing your comp, etc do what you said and just start changing stuff randomly 8p~ (not very effective at all tho 8p~)
