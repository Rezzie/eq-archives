Your group members do not see any change to your information because you are in a state of disconnect (LD). The server will not "update" your info because it is not receiving any info from your client PC.

The reason you "get back in" and then lose the 700hps is due to the server handling the "combat". Once you get back in, the server updates your client PC. Once the server and client are in sync, the server "updates" your info to the group members as well.
