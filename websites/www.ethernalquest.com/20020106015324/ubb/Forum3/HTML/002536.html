You can tap the info from the linux machine without even having an IP address. A similar technique is used for programs like the Snort NIDS.

Just config the device far enough that the kernel knows what driver to load for it and bring it up with:

ifconfig eth0 up

Then if you truly have a hub (and not a switch in disguise) SEQ will be able to watch all the traffic flowing down the wire to it.
