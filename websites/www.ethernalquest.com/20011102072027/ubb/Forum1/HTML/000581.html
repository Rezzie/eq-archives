the only way i know to do this in seq is to med to full and then cast a spell. seq will show you how much mana you have AFTER each spell you cast (it doesnt refresh like hp's and mobs). so, what I did was to med to full, cast a measly litte spell that takes 10 mana and came up with my max mana for the lvl (seq mana + 10 after casting). now, I just canni/med to full, then cast an alteration spell (specialization in alt = 120ish) and see how much mana it costs me. If you did this enough times, you could figure out if specialization really lowers mana cost (anybody with seq could do this).

edit: seq doesn't show max mana either like it does hp's... only show current mana (as of last spell cast).
