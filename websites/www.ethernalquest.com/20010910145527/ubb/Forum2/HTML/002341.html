Heya. .crp is short for .crap, hehe I think

Yes, there's an indescrpency and I'm not sure why. The format used by ItemEdit is the same as what ItemDB.exe outputs. The .crp is encrypted, ItemDB.exe will decrypt it but the format is off, not sure why yet, but I was considering compiling itemedit twice, once for the current showeq format and the other for the existing itemedit format. This could be due to an old change in the item structure, not sure...

Anyway, the difference is that unknown1 in showeq is a 24 byte field, in itemedit.exe and itemdb.exe it's a 20 byte field. There are no other differences. Lots of the fields are filled with uninitilzied garbage BTW (like "WINBOOT=C:\WINDOW"... etc).

Send me an email and I'll send you a compiled version of itemedit.exe for the 24 byte field if you want (the same format as showeq. Sorry for the inconsistency
