Hi all! If you are having problems running EthernalQuest please read this post, it might be able to help you.

[Credits]
Thank you to HackersQuest for a great EverQuest zone viewer and all the people in this forum who have helped me. Also thank you to Turbo, EQMatrix, and HackAttack for the alternate links, these are much appreciated! =)

[EthernalQuest newbie scenario]
-Download EthernalQuest from this link.
http://www.hackersquest.gomp.ch/downloads/ethernalquest/EthernalQuest.zip

-Download and read SIPandFAQ1.1.doc (Thanks HackAttack)
http://www.gamersworld.4t.com/downloads/faq.doc

-Either make a duplicate copy of your EverQuest directory (you could rename the copy to EthernalQuest) OR you could reinstall all of your EverQuest CD's to a new directory (you could also call this directory EthernalQuest). I personally did the reinstall of the EverQuest classic, Ruins of Kunark, and Scars of Velius (recomended).

-note: If you make a copy of your EverQuest directory, please remove .oct and .ini files (I assume that some .ini files may be causing the client to hang). Most of these files will be automatically recreated when EverQuest is executed.

-note: The second EQ directory will take up extra space, please make sure you have the hard drive space to spare. Each directory is about one to two gigabytes of hard drive space.

-Unzip/extract the EthernalQuest.zip to this new directory (EthernalQuest) and overwrite all files.

-Unzip/extract the EmuLog.zip and place it in the root EthernalQuest directory (same directory as the EverQuest.exe).

-note: Please make sure your server.exe files are also in the same directory as the EverQuest executable files.

-Change the "IP.ini" file to your IP address.

-note: If you need help finding your IP address, click start, run, and type WINIPCFG and look at your "IP Address" (in the form of ###.###.###.###). Copy that number in to the "IP.ini" file.

[execute EMULOG.exe]
EMULOG!
ERROR: file = 1839104 | cmp = 2140160
WRONG VERSION -- Please get the newest version of EverQuest!

-Download eqfiles.zip from this link. (Thanks EQMatrix)
http://www.avalon.net/~cyberdoc/eqfiles.zip

-Unzip/extract the eqmain.dll and eqgame.exe in to your EthernalQuest directory.

[execute EMULOG.exe again]
EQGAME!
ERROR: Eqgame has caused an error in Eqmain.dll.
Eqgame will now close. If you continue to experience problems, try restarting your computer.

-note: WARNING! Idiot loops are easily caused here. Thanks to lazy Verant programmers, you may be stuck in a restart computer, execute EUMLOG again, and get this error and never get it fixed. Theres nothing wrong with your Eqmain.dll or Eqgame.exe either. The issue here is you need a new Eqtext.txt file.

-Download eqtext&zonefix.zip from this link. (Thanks Turbo)
http://razk.netfirms.com/eqtext&zonefix.zip

-Extract/unzip the eqtext.txt file to your EthernalQuest directory. There is also an updated zoneserver.exe that has fixed some crash/lockup problems that people have been experiencing with the emulator.

Start your LoginServer, WorldServer, and ZoneServer executables then hit your EmuLog executable and let the fun begin =) .

OK! I got all my files, everything works great! Now I can get to the character creation screen and make a character and zone in to... South qeynos? How lame... How do I get to the other zones? Conventional zoning does not work in this emulator. Well, its in the instructions somewhere, I forgot... but read it hehe...

Don't know how to zone?
http://www.ethernalquest.com/ubb/Forum2/HTML/002651.html

SIPandFAQ1.1.doc section 3.3
SYNTAX
#zone qeynos2

Got stuck in a room and cant get out?
http://www.ethernalquest.com/ubb/Forum6/HTML/000100.html

#goto x y z (coordinate values)

-note: Please add an extra 0 at the end of the z coordinate (altitude). So the following disired coordinate...
#goto 40 1680 32

... should read as...
#goto 40 1680 320

... with the extra 0 at the end.

[in game scenario]
So lets say you wanted to check out Veeshan's Peaks. All you have to do is start EthernalQuest, make your character, then type the following...

enter game: assuming South Qeynos
#zone veeshan
/q

remake character
#goto 40 1680 320

and from there you can explore part of Veeshan's Peak =)

Some other helpful things I like to do is...
#summonitem 10400
#summonitem 12260
#summonitem 13015

Those items should help a little. It's just a GreaterLightstone (light source), food and drink.

The latest database for the emulated items can be downloaded here (note: no epics, not many velius armors, and only half the kunark armors are in this DB, sorry!)
http://www.ethernalquest.org/db/emuitems.cgi

The developers of EthernalQuest have not implimented all of the features of the real EverQuest. The only real application to this game, in it's current condition, is to use it as a zone trainer/viewer.
