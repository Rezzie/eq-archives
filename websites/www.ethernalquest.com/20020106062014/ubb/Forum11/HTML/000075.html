In a nutshell...
Odin's Eye is a packet sniffer. It listens to the traffic between the DAOC client and server from a seperate computer. As of the last time I used it it had a very limited featureset. I havent used it in about 2 weeks so I am sure someone will add to this explanation. OE shows you a list of all PCs/NPCs that the server is telling the client about, which is a radius about double the normal in-game visibl distance. It tells you their name, level, and location. Soon to show HP and Race. It then takes all that information, along with a map of the zone and your location, and plots a graphical "radar" of the area you are in.
