You don't need to zone, just use one of your blur spells. the "zone" strategy would work for anyone I would think.

Here's how I would do it (and have done it). I'm assuming a level 50 enchanter since you want a level 50 cleric.

Clarity, Gift of Magic, Augmentation. Start with those. Ignore the morons telling you to use celerity. Celerity is pure haste and doesn't benefit casters the way aug does (it has an AGI buff and some others).

Next cast Shadow, the only AC buff (others) chanters have at that level and berzerker strength or rampage, whichever is higher, I forget. Yes, it lowers your AGI while it's in effect, but it's also a damage buffer so you don't need your AGI until it's gone anyway.

Don't mess with feedback. the minimal damage it does doesn't compensate for the pain in the butt recasting of it.

Make SURE you're in an area where you know the mobs well or are using SEQ. Make the cleric stay put in a nearly safe spot and go grab something that's barely red to it. Pull it with Tashania. I can't say that enough. You need to lower the MR of the mob so the cleric can cast on it. run to the cleric and then root it. Color skew repeatedly if necessary so it can't hit the cleric. Nukes, dies.

The key for powerlevelling a cleric is undead. Kurn's tower is good at low levels, then unrest, befallen isn't too bad either. Just make sure that you have your chat filter so it shows damage, that way you'll know when the rampage/berzerker wears off and can recast it. It works like a rune but without the component.

Lastly, I can't recommend enough taking a druid. nobody can PL like a druid. SLN, chloroplast, nice damage shields, and heals when necessary. The druid has decent AE nukes in case of trains and can sow as well. The druid can also take you from place to place if the powerlevelling spot is camped.

And don't underestimate the value of a monk as well. pound, pound pound pound (monster runs away) Feign Death, nuke nuke, cleric gets XP.

My PLing ways (vary slightly depending on class i'm PLing): I use a 44 bard, 52 monk, 52 cleric or a 52 druid.

--------------------------------

1-12 Runnyeye with druid and cleric.. simple Heroic Bond, Thorns, Chloroplast. (group cast the spells, if you don't know, your 51+ spells are too "powerful" for intended targets, so group up, click on yourself and cast group buff, at 52 I can put Heroic Bond on a L1)

L12 /played = 2 hours
-----------------------------
13-35 SolA.. move in, at about 17 or 18 i go into the Bar and stay there until 35.

I either:
Beat it to nothing, have Bard charm it (pre L35 this works like a charm, his charm works nicely, stop him from hitting your dmg shields, and wipes agro 99.9% of the time)

Beat it to nothing, have Monk FD. Less reliable, but still effective on mobs < L35

Or pull with druid (cleric always hotkeyed in corner using Celestial Healing. Have Heroic Bond, Shield of Words, Lower level Symbol, Druid STR, Thorns and Chloro. Pull like 3, tap them all with my PL-EE and then let 'em all die on the druid.

Downsides are your Defense is crap afterwards. But if you duel a necro's pet that's blue to you, your melee skills go up.

Level 35 /played 10 hours =)

---------------------------------
at 35 it slows down for me. You can either mass-kill in SolA, head to Cazic Thule and pull the sewers (by the little AoF room) for decent XP or head over to Kaesora and start in there. PLing sucks in Kunark because mobs have too damn many HP. But Kaesora has a nice ZEM so it pays off until about L43.

then I head over to Dreadlands, by Burningwood zone, there's a nice highway of mobs walking there. You can get to 50 No problem there. After that, just slit your wrists because 51+ game sucks.
