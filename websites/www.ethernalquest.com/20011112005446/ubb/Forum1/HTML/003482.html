Ok, I'm sick of replying to the same problem over and over again so I'll put it in a topic for everyone to read.

The first major problem with EQw is getting it fullscreen. The majority of people complaining about this are users of Voodoo based cards.

Voodoo 1 and 2 based cards:

Sorry, not much can be done, try hunting for something called WinGlide but I doubt it'll run very well at all.

Voodoo 3 and above based cards:

Go into EQ, when you get to the screen with Connect and other options, pick Options and set your display driver to Primary Display.

NVidia Geforce/TNT based cards:

These should work fine, if they don't make sure your desktop resolution is higher than the resolution you run EQ at, and that you have the latest Detonator drivers from www.nvidia.com

Other cards:

Generally if it doesn't work try the methods listed for Voodoo 3 and above cards, and GeForce/TNT cards.

Secondly, I'd advice anyone running EQw from their EQ directory to move it somewhere safer so VI doesn't have a chance of scanning it (better to be safe than lose your lvl 60 over a prog like this ) and follow the instructions on how to make a shortcut for it.

Lastly, use CTRL+SHIFT+R (EQw v1) or CTRL+ALT+R (EQw v2) to get out of the EQ window, don't use Alt+Tab as it screws things up.

If anyone else makes a new post regarding any of the problems I've fixed I'll scream and tear clumps of hair out, ok? :P
