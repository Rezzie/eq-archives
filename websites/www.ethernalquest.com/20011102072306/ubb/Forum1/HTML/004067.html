With Win98 SE, im 99.99% sure that it would support 384mb of ram as long as you still have free DIMM slots to support it in your motherboard (dimm slots are the ones your ram goes in).

however, if youre intending to buy the extra ram (as opposed to say, if you had some extra lying around) i would suggest you upgrade your processor and/or vid card first to get the performance jump youre probably looking for.

oh, and make sure that the ram youre getting is similar. its not really advisable to mix ram speeds and or some brands (i.e. you dont wanna mix your badass corsair ram with the generic crap you can pick up at Fry's) because the seek time will be different for each.
