First of all Gornit isn't level 24, he spawns between level 33 and 37. Second of all he's not a Cyclops, he's a Hill Giant. Thirdly he doesn't typically drop "upwards of 250pp per hour". He drops as much as 45pp (from my personal experience)each spawn and spawns every 6 minutes. However, 35+pp drops are fairly rare. His "good" plat drops are typically in the mid 20's and he tends to go in streaks. So you may get several 20+pp drops in a row or several 4pp drops in a row.

While it is entirely possible for him to drop 250pp in an hour it is more typical for him to drop around 100pp per hour or a little less.

I don't mind seeing posts about certain mobs, but I do think it's important to be as accurate as possible. I'd hate to see some level 25 try to go solo him and get waxed for their efforts. Gornit is tough to solo even at 40 as he tends to resist frequently. He is a great place to make money non-the-less.
