From the EverQuest "Forums":
All servers will be coming down at 3AM PST Friday March 30th. The estimated downtime is 3 hours. This is to correct a number of video card related issues.
- The EverQuest Team

And a bit more info:
I have a little more information for you on the emergency patch tomorrow.
It should fix a bug that is causing people with video cards that require square textures to have trouble with garbled textures and crashing.

We are still looking into a problem that is causing Win 2K users to crash when changing resolutions. We haven't nailed that down yet, so I have no estimate as to when we'll get it fixed.

Alan

Note that they could also change encryption in this patch (Verant's been showing signs that they might change encryption). Please be aware of this fact when you start up ShowEQ post patch.
-Scruffy
