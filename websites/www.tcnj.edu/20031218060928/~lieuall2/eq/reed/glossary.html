Skills:
B - Brass
P - Percussion
S - Stringed
V - Voice
W - Wind

Targets:
AE - Area effect, centered around the bard
Group - Effects your group
Self - Effects only the bard
Target - Effects only your target
Targeted AE - An area effect, but centered around your target

Other terms:
Burning Affliction - Improved Damage that applies to damage-over-time spells Improved Damage - A focus effect that adds randomly from 1 to maximum effect of the focus's % damage to direct damage spells (I.E. a 15% improved damage can add anywhere from 1% to 15% of the spell's base damage) Overhaste - Stacking haste that will also exceed the haste cap
Pulse - Beneficial spells that have an instant duration "pulse" when the song finishes singing, and then every tick until the song is stopped
Stacking haste - haste that will stack on top of another spell/song haste
Tick - The EverQuest unit of time, six real world seconds
