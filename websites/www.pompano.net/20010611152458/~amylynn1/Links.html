Links
EQ Lizer
The best EQ spoiler site out there and companion site to EQ Items!

EQ Stratics
One of the best news sites. Also has good statistics info and city maps.

NotCrazy.com
Updating a bit slow these days, but has great maps and news.

EQ Haven
Formerly Everworld.net, this site has great news articles and message boards.

EverLore
A lot of unusual items here, also good news content and a lot of screenshots available.

Quester of Norrath
A mainly Japanese site, but it has the best maps of any map site.

Enigma's Maps
A compilation of maps from all over the 'net. If it's been mapped, it's here.

Everquest Weapons Database
A neat site that compares weapons in a variety of ways.

EQ Online
Had a long dry spell, but is being updated regularly now. Lots of great spell and trade skill info here.

EQ Vault
The main news source for Everquest, also has a large amount of server-specific and class-specific message boards.

Crossroads of Norrath
Very up-to-date news, with many well written player articles.

Casters Realm
Formerly EQ Enchanters, this site now has great news/spell lists/trade skills for all four spellcaster classes.

EQ Druids
Great site for Druids, also has tons of information on items and battle tactics.

Everquest Monks
Great site for monks. Has great armor comparisons and monk quest info.

The Ranger's Glade
Spell lists and great map lists, along with a good deal of ranger-specific info.
