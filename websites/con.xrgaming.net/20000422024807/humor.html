Twas The Night Before The Release of EQ
by Lee Sale
Twas the night before the release of EQ,

and it was silent through the house,

all but the clicking,

the clicking of the mouse.

Over and over I read reviews of everquest,

screenshots and interviews,

as a say farewell to my CD of beta test.

I ran upstairs and climbed into bed,

as 3DFX cards danced in my head.

I entered a medieval world filled with beauty,

and me walking around making fun of a newbie.

I still remember that ultima online,

a pking world and dying all the time.

I woke up on that special day,

March 16th the releasing day.

I rushed to the computer store dancing like a little schoolgirl,

as I picked up the eq box and said "I think I’m gonna hurl!"

This day I have been waiting for,

the day of eq,

I have been waiting for weeks and weeks,

and now its finally here.

I drove home going 300miles an hour down the terrain,

I signal of speed rushed through my brain.

I jumped out of my car and ran for the computer,

and opened the CD drive frantically without any control,

I through the UO CD like a Frisbee,

and dropped the EQ CD in as a moment of pleasure ran right through me.

It began to install and my dreams were true,

"I can see it now and its pretty cool!"

Waiting patiently it slowly installed,

showing pictures of EQ Land,

as I waited by the computer crossing my legs,

"I really gotta go to the washroom bad!"

I ran back downstairs as it completed at the 100% mark,

and I turned off the lights so it would be completely dark.

I watched the intro and just about ready to play,

and to let nothing disturb me this day.

I saw a GM appear on my screen,

" HAPPY GAMING TO ALL, AND TO ALL A GOOD NIGHT"

("THATS IF YOU GO TO BED")
