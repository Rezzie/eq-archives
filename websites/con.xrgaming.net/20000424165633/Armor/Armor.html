Norrathian Armor

Below you will find a listing of all commonly found armor available in the lands of Norrath. By "commonly found" we mean that it can easily be purchased off of a vendor, we list only these item's to avoid listing spoilers. Note: The prices seen below are approximate. These were the prices charged for agnostic characters with a Charisma of 75. The price that your character is charged will vary somewhat depending on race, class, faction, God, and Charisma. If you have any suggestions/comments or find any errors in these charts, please e-mail us.
