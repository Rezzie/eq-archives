Subject:Have you considered Cazic?

Most folks I know swear by Cazic for exping in the mid 20s.


That said, I did all my serious exping in that level range in the Keep. That was quite a while back, but even then it was a bit of a pain to get into a group. Ultimately, it was worth the wait. Once you are into a group, you'll move much faster then you ever could soloing.


About the only thing I can imagine a mid-20s ranger soloing would be Aviaks and Centaurs in South Karana using Snare and Archery/Spells. But you'll be competing for them with Robes and Druids and will probably lose half your targets to kill stealers (intentional and accidental)


So, anyhow, take a look at Cazic and High Keep.
