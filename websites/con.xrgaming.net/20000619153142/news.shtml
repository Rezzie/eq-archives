Where are the Coins?
One player was upset about Verant lowering the coin loot in Kunark. Abashi had this to say:
>>>You have reduced coin-loot to lower than typical levels on most mobs in Kunark as part of your effort to prop up the value of plat?

The idea was to redefine, worldwide, the definition of good coin loot. The problem is that certain static costs in game remain just that, static.

We're going to discuss the coin drop issues, and other things related to the economy (trade skill costs, component costs, etc.) in the tuning meeting next Monday.

-Gordon
