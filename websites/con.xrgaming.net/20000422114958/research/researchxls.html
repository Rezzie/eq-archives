Research Component Tracking Worksheets

 

To use the worksheets, download the worksheet that corresponds to your class. Open the worksheet in Excel. The first sheet, known as the Component Sheet, allows you to track the number and type of research components you have. The second sheet, know as the Research Sheet, will show you the list of spells that are researchable and how many of any given spell you can create given your current component count. When you research a spell click on the corresponding research button and the components will automatically be removed from your inventory and will update the list.

Click on the icon below to download the worksheet for your class.  These spreadsheets were done using Office 97, and in order to use the Research Sheet you MUST have Macros Enabled.
