More intelligence is always good, but do not neglect getting your charisma up. It is *very* important for an enchanter, possibly even more important than the intelligence. Charm durations as well as resists for our mesmerize and color flux lines are all charisma based.


You might even think about going with some kind of cats eye bracer along with the polished bone rather than sticking with the ivory.


And don't forget the AC..it really helps us when we are getting beaten up on when we *do* get resisted. We don't always have the choice to hide behind the tanks in combat as do other casters.


And what you wear and concentrate on depends on where you are fighting. Have different equipment in the bank and switch it around as is appropriate to the situation. Carry some of it around. If you are getting more than the normal resists on your stuns one day, switch out some of the intelligence for charisma. Or if you are always running out of mana, switch the intelligence items back in.
