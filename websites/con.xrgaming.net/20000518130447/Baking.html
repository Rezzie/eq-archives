Baking: A beginner’s guide

 

Greetings young baker! Within this tome you’ll find basic recipes and cooking instructions for the delight of your audience- regardless of race or class! As a great Baker, you have the power to make food so revolting…so utterly inedible, that your poor victim just may black out from the taste of it! Let us begin.

To start, you must have your ingredients, of course. Once you have them, and in the right measures, you must mix them up, and cook them in an oven, steamer, or whichever sort of cooking device is required by the recipe. Let’s begin with a basic disk, Bixie Crunchies- a favorite of many, revulsed by few…

Bixie Crunchies

Ingredients:

1. Bixie Parts

2. Frosting

Directions:

1. Carefully spread frosting to completely cover the bixie parts.

2. Place your uncooked snack into the oven, and cook until frosting becomes hard.

Easynotes: Take the Bixie Parts and Frosting, place in the oven, click "combine".

 

Smoked Wood Elf

Ingredients:

1. Wood Elf Parts

2. Spices

Directions:

1. Smother Wood Elf parts with spices.

2. Place the raw, spiced meat into a smoker, and cook until brown.

Easynotes: Place the wood elf parts and spices into a SMOKER (not an oven), and click "combine".

 

Note: Many items required by the baker can be purchased from a potter. It would be advisable (note: CHEAPER) to buy the items from another player who has taken up the pottery skill, than to purchase what you need from an NPC vendor.
