Fletching for the Apprentice

 

What do I need?

There are four basic parts to each arrow, no matter the quality or type. The Fletcher must have a fletching kit to utilize these parts. They are:

1. A Groove Nock

2. A shaft

3. A tip

4. A fletch

The apprentice should place all four items into a fletching kit, and combine them. If his skill is high enough, an arrow will be produced. In order to raise the success of creating an arrow, the apprentice may want to purchase a book, and keep it in his backpack where it is a handy reference while fletching. (unconfirmed note: Your chances of success may be higher if you have a training manual in your inventory.)
