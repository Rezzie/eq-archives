Basic Blacksmithing

 

The art of Blacksmithing. It is dirty and tedious, backbreaking and difficult to learn.But the product, should one have the skill to complete it, is a work of wonder, andinspires awe to all who lay eyes upon it. (note: Do not attempt to construct an itemwithout having at least 25 skill points in Blacksmithing…unless you are very rich,that is.)

There is one basic item which every blacksmith requires- a forge in which to shape andhammer his work. Once you are near a forge, you may begin to construct the armor which youhave only dreamt about until now. This tutorial will provide you only the most basic ofinformation, as becoming a truly excellent blacksmith takes years of work- and a lot offailures and successes.

To Create a Gorget

1. Purchase a sheet of metal from any vendor which sells them.

2. Purchase a gorget mold, which is available in most any city.

3. Be sure you have a flask of water, with which to cool your product.

4. Place each of these items into the forge…

5. Combine them.

Voila! If you succeeded, you will now have a gorget in your possession! Please note:Any vendor that sells the components needed to create your gorget should alsoprovide a training manual. Refer to this for instruction on what components are needed forother items, and what to make at higher levels of skill.
