Pottery for the Young Artist

The potter is the tradesman who leads the tranquil life- for it requires much patience and skill to be successful. He or she is bound to make money…for the products created by the potter are necessary for the baker to do his work. Enclosed within this tome is an overview of what items are most common for a potter to create, what items he needs to create those items, and additionally, how to create a few of the basic items.

*Firing Sheets of all qualities are NOT combined in the pottery wheel. Once you’ve made the raw product, place it, with the firing sheet, into a kiln and THEN combine them.

 

Example: I wish to make a Mixing Bowl. Assuming I have the skill to complete the task, I take two large blocks of clay and a mixing bowl sketch, and place them into the pottery wheel. Click on "combine". Once the raw product is produced, I take it, with the firing sheet, find a kiln, and place both of them within it. Again, click on "combine". Voila, I’ve just made a Mixing bowl.
