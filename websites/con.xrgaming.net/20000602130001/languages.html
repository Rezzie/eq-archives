What language are we speaking?

It seems pretty clear from where I'm sitting that the majority has spoken. I a poll conducted at the CoN over the last few weeks we've taken in over 350 votes.

75% of us made it clear!

66% want to: "Greatly reduce the initial understanding of the common tongue for non human races"
9% are more dramatic and want to: "Eliminate the common tongue from the initial understanding of the non human races"
22% want to: "Keep it as it is."
1% want to: "Give all characters a basic understanding of all languages."

I've felt for some time, that the language aspect of EQ provides on of the greatest roleplaying opportunities in a long time, but as every race begins with such an easy way of communicating with each other, it is utterly lost. Please Verant, here our prayer!

There is so much opportunity for roleplaying & racial unity that can come from this opportunity. We implore you! 71% of us say that the increased chances for roleplay outweigh the added burden of losing the easy common tongue... you don't have maps or 'recall o rama' either, as convient as they may be. Please... Verant, here our prayer!

Just imagine... Having reduced the initial understanding of the common tongue- the female wood elf who travels to a distant land known as freeport, will have to speak with her lost half brother (a half elf ) - in hopes of finding Boots® the Human Necromancer who has been secretly sending her love tokens for so very long.

And that's just the beginning!
