Kedge is really a lot like Lower Guk in level range. The Lancers are like the Shin/Vis/Wan part of the frog spectrum. But Kedge ramps up much faster then LGuk - there is a single pop point in the first room (but far from the entrance) that spawns those piercers and impalers who are more like Zols and Boks. By the time you are into the second room, most stuff is into the low to mid 40s and it just gets tougher from there.


But, the Lancers by the zone are like Shins in Lower and are even better exp! Before they all went green to me (around 42nd level), those lancers were one of the only places I could solo as a warrior, thanks to my Fishbone earring.


You'll want a healer or two to try them: they hit very hard for mobs of their level - into the 60s and low 70s sometimes. It will probably be difficult to talk enough folks into trying it. But, if you do, it'll only take one or two kills before you'll start hearing "wow, awsome exp" coming through /group.


And, unfortunately, it only takes one or two drownings to break a groups morale. Ah well, risk is commensurate with reward.
