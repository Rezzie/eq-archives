General Guidelines

  • No personal attacks. This includes, but is not limited to, insulting others for their comments and ideas, language skills, age, sex and religious beliefs.
  • No offensive racial or sexist remarks.1
  • No offensive religious slurs.1
  • No offensive ancestral remarks.
  • No spamming. This includes, but is not limited to, comments not contributing to the board, advertisements and messages posted repeatedly.
  • No explicit content of sexual or violent nature.
  • Impersonating game officials or developers, or site admins is not tolerated.
Sage Tower Guidelines
This board is dedicated to acquiring and providing help and tips for game play. Do not post guild news, advertisements or dev-related issues here.

Tavern Guidelines
The Tavern is a location where characters can come together and interact. It is intended for roleplaying and discussions relating to in-game issues only.

  • Do not post dev-related messages or bug reports.
  • Keep questions about game mechanics to the Sage Tower or Dev Board.
  • Keep out-of-character topics to a minimum.
  • Sexually explicit posts are not tolerated. Sexually suggestive posts are only tolerated in moderation and when done in a tasteful manner.
  • In-character conflict is welcome as long as it remains in-character. If the discussion turns to a heated debate between players, it should immediately be resolved on the Discussions board.

Dev Board
The Dev Board is reserved for discussions related to game mechanics, suggestions and bug reports.

  • Do not post bugs that can be exploited for personal gain or that may prevent others from playing the game as it was intended. This includes the posting of site locations that advertise such bugs.
  • Do not post complaints about bugs or Sony Interactive policy. These discussions belong on the Discussions board. Try to keep your messages constructive and civil.

Discussions Board
This board is unmoderated and is reserved for off-topic posts, complaints, debates, flame wars and any other discussion not appropriate for the other boards.

Penalties
Board admins reserve the right to ban individuals from posting without warning, depending on the severity of the offense. In most cases, a general warning will be issued to all posters. Then, if that fails to correct the problem, a warning to the specific offender may be given. After that, bannings may result. Also, bannings may or may not be permanant, depending on the severity of the offense. If you would like to discuss the reasons behind a banning, or would like to appeal your banning, feel free to contact an admin.

 

1 Some comments may be acceptable when used in-character and during roleplaying. Use extreme caution when posting comments on these subjects.
