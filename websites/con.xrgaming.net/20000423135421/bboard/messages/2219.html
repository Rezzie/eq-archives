I did a /who just before we advanced to Vox's room and there were 53 people in the zone. I don't really know how many of those were part of the operation and how many were just normal Permafrost groups.


Really, there were more then we needed. During the course of the Vox fight itself, a third of the folks there were LD at any given time. In fact, I don't think anyone made it all the way through the main fight without losing con.


For the most part, tanks and their support teams held up really well. The caster groups were massacred. I'm not sure, but I think only one wizard survived. I was surveying the wreckage afterwards and all the dead I saw were robes and the healers that had been assigned to their groups.


People under 45th level absolutely withered from the breath weapon - it was devastating to them. I'm 47th and had my CR at 131 during the fight. It was painful, but no one in my group died, even though both our clerics (group was 4 tanks and 2 clerics) were LD for most of it.


For the most part, it was a donnybrook - no real tactics or finesse. We just swarmed her. Oh well, we won.
