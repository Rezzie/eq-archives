Fishing Basics

 

Fishing is perhaps the simplest skill one can learn. Anyone can doit (I even saw an ogre fishing by a lake once!). Here are the basics:

1. Place your fishing pole in your hand.

2. Be sure you’ve got bait in your backpack.

3. Fish. (Click on "Fishing" under your Abilities list on the right-hand sideof your screen)

 

Simple, no?

Bait can either be bought at any fisherman’s store, as can a fishing pole, or manya skilled outdoorsman can "Forage" for bait. It is a relatively cheapinvestment, considering the amount of profit one can make in a single day’s work!
