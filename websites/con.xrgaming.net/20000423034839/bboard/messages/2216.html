Subject:So, I helped kill a dragon last night...

That was pretty exciting - we bagged Vox.


It went like this:


2 hours sitting around in the kings room while a couple monks pulled the giants and other junk. Except for one pull that had 3 giants and the Preist of Nagafen, that was such a bore I didn't even bother to run over to enter combat - the bored casters just blew the pulls away.


Next, the far more challenging Grouping/Buffing/Countdown. Trying to maintain some sort of discipline during a 20 minute countdown while bored people run around /poke-ing and /cheer-ing each other.


Finally, the charge into Vox's room. That was pretty thrilling at first, then it because just a sorta tedious, greuling 4 minutes of hitting her with my sword and watching the relentless spam from Vox's breath weapon.


And my group, a TANK group, got the exp!


Loot: Hammer of Divine Might, 2 x Staff of Forbidden Rites, Dragonbone Bracer, Dragon Tooth.


Pretty cool, eh?
