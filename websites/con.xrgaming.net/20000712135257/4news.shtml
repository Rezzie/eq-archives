Crossroads Undergoing Changes
Hello Crossroads of Norrath Readers!!! We are currently undergoing a series of changes to redo/revamp the Crossroads of Norrath. Among those changes are:
  • A revised staff of news hounds and managers. They are: Tiger, DMWolf, Nythane, Tenwen and Delar. Welcome all of you!! Expect to see more news from them soon!
  • A new and improved layout for the pages with new graphics. Marduk and the graphics team will have this assembled for us soon.
  • A new overall Site Manager (Dragons) who is moving here from our main Xroads pages in order to try and help get the pages back on track.
  • New additions to the pages like a stories page, guild listings/news and more! Coming Soon!
We'd love to hear from YOU with news items. Did your guild kill Nagafen? Is someone getting married? A new guild just form up? Are you hosting an event for players on your server? Drop us an email so we can get your news out!! Keep checking back and watch for many new changes coming soon!

-Dragons - 8:07 AM MST
