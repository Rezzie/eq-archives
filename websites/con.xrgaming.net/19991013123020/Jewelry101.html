Additionally, the trainee will need precious stones to set within the bands of high-quality metals. Any stone will work- but the higher the quality, the more precious and rare the piece of jewelry will be produced. Gems range from a simple 6 silver, 3 copper, to a hefty 25 platinum, 3 gold. As you may guess, there is a wide variety of gems, and thus, items which you may create if your skill is high enough.

The actual act of creating a piece of fine jewelry seems simple enough, but in truth, only a highly skilled artist should attempt even the simplest of tasks. (note: Don’t even try using this skill until you have at LEAST 20 points in it. It’s a highly expensive skill to use, and if you don’t have at least this minimal amount, you will lose an incredible amount of money through your multiple failures…)

To create a simple band of silver, with a small stone set within:

1. Place the bar of silver within the jeweler’s kit.

2. Do the same for the gem.

3. Combine the two.

That’s it! If you succeeded, you now have a dazzling ring in the palm of your hand.
