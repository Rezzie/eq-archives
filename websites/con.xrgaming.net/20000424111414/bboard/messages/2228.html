You should take a look at Kedge Keep, too. The fish right by the zone line are around 30th level (32 tops) and are pretty easy to pull singly with archery. You're right by a zone so the risks arent that severe.


Obviously, you can't solo those. But I bet a properly balanced 4 or 5 group in the mid 20s could make a bubble an hour there without breaking a sweat.


Just one warning: if you see a Piercer or Impaler, do NOT pull it. Those are mid 30s and 40-ish respectively and would tack your hide to the wall in seconds. Just have some patience and they'll move away from the zone area after a couple minutes.
