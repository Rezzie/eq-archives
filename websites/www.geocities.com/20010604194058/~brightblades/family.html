Our daugter Megan was born on June 25th, 1996. I just got stationed at Offutt AFB, in Omaha, Nebraska. Megan is starting pre-kindergarten now at St. Mary's School. Brooke also got a part time job there as a school secretary, so that works out great for Megan. The cool thing about Megan going to that school is that she's already getting a great education. We're not even catholic!
