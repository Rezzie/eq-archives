Dark Age of Camelot

Okay, I just started this page, so bear with me for a bit. I just got into DAoC Beta 4, and all I am doing at this point is trying to figure out how the game works. I have collected a few links that should help in that regard.

As far as playing, I am on the Guinevere Server (a Role Playing Server!). I got the name Larian, and I am a Ranger (of course!). Now the big challange will be trying to split my time between this and EQ. Once I figure out which one I like best, I may pick one over the other, but we'll see. Anyways, enjoy the links, and hope to see you on line somewhere.

I am also trying to put together a little something for my guild The Harpers at Twilight. If any of you know any good graphics from the Forgotten Realms Harper series that I could "borrow" to put together a web site, I'd really appreciate it.


Hey, my first screenshot in DAoC! I'll try to get a better one up here soon, but for now this will do.

  • Mythic's Dark Age of Camelot Page - Start here to find out about the game.

  • Camelot Live - The official link between the folks at Mythic and the player base. Now Live!

  • My Ranger Guide! - Just a guide that I wrote on how I plan on playing my ranger. You can play your ranger any way you want. UPDATED 12 Dec 01! Since I just wrote this guide, I am getting a lot of feedback (thanks, I want it!), and I am continually updating this document. Please check back often.

  • The Harpers at Twilight - My guild in Dark Age of Camelot. Look us up!

  • The Classes of Camelot - A listing of all the classes, races, skills, spells, etc. Great place to go to figure out what you want to play.

  • Ranger's Glade in Camelot - The friendliest Web Community there is! Rangers rule!

  • Allakhazam's Dark Age of Camelot Site - Hopefully a spoiler site of the same quality as his EQ site.

  • Camelot Lore - A Great general news and information site (They have also been driving a lot of hits to my page!).

  • Mellodies Dark Age of Camelot Manual - The first place you should go once the game is installed. Sort of a "setting up Dark Age of Camelot for dummies" kind of thing (I needed it for sure!).

  • DAoC Character Projection - A program to download that let's you optimize your skill points for all classes.

  • Camelot Stratics - Another general news and information site.

  • Camelot Vault - Another general news and information site. Lots of EBBs there too.

Well, that's all I have for now. Hopefully I can get more soon.
