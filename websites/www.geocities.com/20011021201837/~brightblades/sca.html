What I Wish I Could do For a Living


A Great Way to Work Out Agression and Stress!

Now these are true weekend warriors. Read on to find out more!

What is the S.C.A. you may ask. Well, to put it simply, it's a bunch of people interested in History, who like to recreate certian aspects of it. Granted, it's a more idylic version of History than those days really were. Most people who are in the S.C.A. are fighters. There are a lot of different aspects of mideival society that they focus on. Some people are artisans, and recreate objects using the same methods they used in the middle ages. Some people are musicians, cooks, smiths, or any other vocation that existed in those days. The thing that most people know about the S.C.A. is the recreation of combat and battles. This is the thing I like to do. Nothing gives me more satisfaction than making my enemies helm ring like a bell! There are a lot of rules that the S.C.A. follows to make sure the combat is safe (so you can go back to the modern day world and go back to work). Even though you aren't killing you opponent, you still inflict bruises and such. There are a lot of different S.C.A. chapters all over the world. Look them up in the phone book or on the web, and go check them out. Most of the people are "different", but I find them all to be friendly and willing to do just about anything for another S.C.A. member.

This is what I look like when I get ready to go out on the field of battle. I like to be prepared for anybody and anything! In the S.C.A. you pick a time period and a name for yourself. I selected Scandinavia, circa 1200. This gives me an opportunity to get dressed up in full plate armour, and have some viking raider characteristics in me too. I picked the name Gunnar Kungalvson based on an old family that lived in the area where I grew up in Sweden. They had a really nice (for those days) castle on a river in the province of Kungalv. And of course, there are the many jokes about Gunnar the Barbarian and such. My friend Jason is Lauchlin MacCool, and we fight each other all the time. It's actually quite good excercise!
