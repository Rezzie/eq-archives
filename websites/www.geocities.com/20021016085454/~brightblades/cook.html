Recepies and Exercise From Brooke

Well, I'm finally letting my husband do some work on this page again. I enjoy easy to cook but impressive recipes. Here are a few that my husband and I like. You'll notice that we use a lot of fattening ingredients. Sure, why not, at least they're natural. I'd rather use butter than margarine any day, because I trust cows more than I do chemists.

Mexican Chicken Kiev - Not really a Mexican dish, but yummy.

Skillet Tomato Chicken - One of my husband's favorites.

Peaches and Cream Pie - Something else Steve loves.

These are just a few of the recipes that we have a lot of. Keep coming back to see what I've let Steve add to the list. Recipes to look for:

  • Lemon Pepper Chicken
  • Lemon Dill Sauce for Fish
  • A Variety of Cream Sauce Recipes
  • Swedish Style Lobster

So how can we stay in any shape except for round after eating rich foods like those? Well, first of all, we don't eat huge portions! (It's that whole eat less and exercise thing!) We also watch our total calorie intake. Normally we eat smaller meals during the day, and then have the big family meal together. As soon as I actually write down my exercise routine, I'll have Steve post it here.
