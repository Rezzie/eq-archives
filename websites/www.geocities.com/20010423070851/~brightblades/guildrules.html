Our History
I have compiled a small history of our guild, taken from the journals of my ancestors. It is my hope that you learn from their words the meaning of our brotherhood.
... It was once said that my very ancestors were the first people to set foot on the shores of Norrath, whether this is true of not doesn't matter. The truth is that my ancestors were great explorers, and kind people. My father has told me many stories of their journeys and for as long as I can remember I have dreamt of the day when I can journey to the far reaches of this great land.
Before I could grow up to see the wonders and joys of Norrath, tragedy struck. Many of my ancestors and friends were killed in the last rampage of the orcs, including my own father. Our endless struggle with them has gone on long enough, and I have learned the meaning of hate from the Orcs.
Now many moons from that terrible day, I have finally set out on my journey. At first I set out on my path alone, with no one to turn to. The struggles were many, and the way was hard. Here I learned the meaning of lonliness. With the birth of many new days, and the wonders of Norrath to greet each dawn, I continue on my trek. Recently I found a small handfull of travellers that share my point of view and my desire to explore Norrath. My hope is that we will meet many others on our way... and together we shall set out on this long journey, and together we will finish it, thus learning the meaning of brotherhood....

Larian of "The Blade and Stars"

Rules Of Conduct
All members of "The Blade and Stars" are bound to follow these rules set by my ancestors:

  • Blade and Stars shall show respect to his/her brothers and sisters
  • Blade and Stars shall respect the ranks of the Order
  • Blade and Stars shall obey a direct order from a commanding rank:
    a. It must be clear that the order is such,
    b. The order shall not compromise any other conditions within the Rule of the Order
  • A Blade and Stars shall attend all gatherings of the Order when it can be done:
    a. This includes but is not limited to meetings, events, and wars
  • A Blade and Stars shall lend aid to his/her brothers and sisters when it is requested:
    a. Such aid must be within the abilities of the member,
    b. Such aid must not compromise any other condition within the Rule of the Order
  • A Blade and Stars shall not take advantage of the generosity of his/her brothers and sisters
  • A Blade and Stars shall not keep from being known any offenders to the Rules of the Order which he/she has witnessed
    a. It will rest upon the determination of the Guild Leader if violators shall be removed or not
  • Proof must be presented with any allegations in the form of material witnesses or screenshots
  • A Blade and Stars shall remain active within the Order:
    a. If an absence is planned spanning more than a 30 day period it must be made known to a Guild Leader, who shall notify other guild members,
    b. Any unforetold absence spanning more than 30 days will be interpreted as a death or disappearance resulting in removal from the Member's list
  • In order to receive an award, proof must be shown in the form of a screenshot or material witness. The Guild leader may choose to assign awards, or not assign awards, at his discression.
    a. A guild member is free to do as they chose with any material awards given to them.

    The Rules of Conduct Around Others in Norrath

  • A Blade and Stars shall lend aid to all citizens of Norrath:
    a. Such aid must not compromise any other conditions within the Rule of the Order
  • A Blade and Stars shall act as a guide when requested:
    a. Such guidance must be within the abilities of the member
  • A Blade and Stars shall defend those in need of protection:
    a. This can mean the sacrifice of the Blade and Stars life if it would successfully save those under his/her protection,
    b. This will mean the sacrifice of the Blade and Stars life if those under their protection will die without help
  • A Blade and Stars shall not interfere in the combat of another without permission:
    a. A Blade and Stars may not group with those that engage in this sort of action,
    b. Upon the best judgment of the Blade and Stars, he/she may intervene if one's life is in dire peril
  • A Blade and Stars shall not make use of public channels (ooc, auction, shout, guildsay) in a manner that is abusive, thereby detracting from a role-playing environment
  • A Blade and Stars shall not take vengeful or retributive action against those who have wronged him/her:
    a. Disputes shall be settled by dueling, or by a verbal challenge in an honorable and respectable fashion
  • A Blade and Stars shall volunteer information regarding the Guild and it's purpose to all interested persons, or shall direct them to a commanding officer to do so
  • A Blade and Stars shall not willfully endanger the lives of others
  • A Blade and Stars shall not ask an unfair price, nor defraud another person in a sale, purchase or trade of goods and services

Now that you have read the rules, the question you must ask yourself is, "Am I a worthy candidate for the Blade and Stars?" If you can answer yes to that question, then E-Mail me at brightblades@msn.com. Please tell me about your character. Tell me the race, class, level, trade skill, and give a brief history of the character or just fill out this simple Guild Application Form. Once the form is filled out, you will be contacted by a member of the guild, and assigned a quest to prove your dedication to the guild.
