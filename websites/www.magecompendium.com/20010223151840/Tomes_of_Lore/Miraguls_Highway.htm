Miragul's Highway

 

 

Note : The background history I have written in here is what I have been able to put together from various sources,

and may not be accurate.

 

 

During the time when the Arch Wizard Miragul was roaming the lands in search of artifacts of great power, to horde them away in his icy fortress in the northern lands of Antonica, he created a system of teleporters to enable his safe journey across the land.

 

These teleporters were known by others as Miragul's Highway. They enable anyone willing to travel to the far of cities of distant lands in the blink of an eye.

 

The room containing this great transport system is hidden amidst an ancient Combine ruin located in The Timorous Deep.

 

 

Note : By hovering your cursor over the artifacts shown below you can reveal the their destination.

 

 

Note : Once you enter the chamber the only way out is through the use of the artifacts.

 

Each artifact will take you to the listed town simply by clicking on it, however it is a one way trip and if you are not favored by the towns guards you are likely to die on arrival.

 

Due to the ancient powers employed by these artifacts you will be unable to bind yourself anywhere near them, also I would not recommend it as there have been rumors of a giant serpent dwelling near these ruins.
