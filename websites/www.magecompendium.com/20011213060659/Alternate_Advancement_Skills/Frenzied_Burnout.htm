Frenzied Burnout

 

 

This ability give the Magician the power to drive their pet to extreme aggressiveness, so much so that the pet will literally explode after a short time at such a frenzied pace.  The explosion will cause damage to anyone near. The forceful expulsion of the elemental pet will disrupt magic in the area as well. The Refresh Timer for this ability is 72 minutes (One day in game time.).
