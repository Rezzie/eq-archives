Air Pet

View the Forums for extra details.

Below 50, Air is a defensive pet, sporting high AGI but low HP. Below level 50 it casts the stun Air Elemental Attack. Above level 50 its stun Air Elemental Strike gains a DD component. Air also features a near-immunity to cold based attacks.
