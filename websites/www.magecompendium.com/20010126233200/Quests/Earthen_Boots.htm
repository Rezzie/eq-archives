Earthen Boots

 

 

Note : This guide is written on the personal experiences of the Mages involved in maintaining this site.

 

 Written and Compiled by Shaidar Haran.

 

 

Joyce in the Temple of Solusek Ro needs the following components to make the earthen boots for you :


Note : All components are No Drop, except the Cats's Eye Agate.


A Stone Marker off a Glyphed Guard in Mistmoore Graveyard

 

Note : This is a nasty place to be if you are alone as it is very easy to train lots of monsters getting there and leaving (And while camping the spawns), I do not recommend doing this alone. However if you are lucky an Imp may spawn and he drops the Sacrificial Dagger, +5 Intelligence and Piercing.


A Heart of Stone off a Stone Skeleton in Lake Rathetear (South East corner)

 

Note : These skeletons are a nasty piece of work, it took me two attempts with another Mage helping me (Both level 30) before we managed to get two hearts.


Soiled Boots off a Tonta Knight in Upper Guk, very easy to obtain if you are level 30. Just wander into Guk and slay lots of frogs.

 


Cat's Eye Agate, available anywhere that sells the lesser gems, not Jewelry stores.

 


- Shaidar Haran
