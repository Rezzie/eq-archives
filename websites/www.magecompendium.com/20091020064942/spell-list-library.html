    Magician Spell List

    Spell List

    Special Thanks to Biggerin for adding drop/purchace information

    Key

    • Guild - Magician Guild Vendors (Ak'anon, Neriak Commons, West Freeport, Erudin Palace, South Felwithe, South Qeynos)
    • Caster Guild - Magician, Wizard, Enchanter shared Venders (Ak'anon, Neriak Commons, Neriak Third Gate, West Freeport, Erudin Palace, South Felwithe, South Qeynos, Qeynos Catacombs)
    • Library - Magician Libraries (Erudin, Neriak Third Gate, Ak'anon)
    • OOT - Ocean of Tears, from Elesseryl Terussar
    • FV/OT - Firiona Vie and Overthere Magician Guilds
    • Kunark, Velious, Luclin - Kunark Drop, Velious Drop, Luclin Drop from NPCs, respectively
    • Ancient - Ancient Drop from Lord Vulak in Temple of Veeshan, Lord Inquisitor Seru, and various bosses in Vex Thal
    • SH - Shadowhaven Magician Guild, The Twilight Sea Castle (on Elemental Island)
    • Bazaar - The Bazaar, sold by Jolum (balcony above and between the banks)
    • EC - East Commonlands
    • PoK - Plane of Knowledge Grand Library
    • Eth, Spec, Rune - Ethereal Parchment, Spectral Parchment, Glyphed Rune Word quest, respectively
    • Research - See Magician Research
    • GoD - Taelosian Geomancy Stone, or Chaos Runes. The exact one is listed. Yiktu and Eril drop prior to Kod`Taz, Jelki only drops in Kod`Taz and beyond. Chaos Runes only drop in Qvic+ zones.
    • Guk, Mir, Mmc, Ruj, Tak - Deepest Guk, Miragul's Menagerie, Mistmoore Catacombs, Rujarkian, Takish-Hiz Wayfarer Camps, respectively
