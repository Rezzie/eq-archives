Testament of Vanear

 

 

In Highpass Hold, there is a secret area under the pond near the Orc side.

 

This hidden area is a smugglers den.

 

Once through, walk around until you find Dyllin Starsine (Only person in there that looks like a Qeynos Guard).

 

Kill him and loot page 30.

 

Note : The other smugglers will attack you as soon as attack Dyllin.

 

Now you will need to travel to Qeynos and find the Crow's Tavern.

 

Moodoro Finharm is in the Crow's Tavern but you can wait until he comes out of the tavern to puke in the pond.

 

Give Moodoro Finharn 2 gold and he will give you the book to combine Pages 30 and 34 in.

 

Then give him 4 gold and you will receive a card. If it is a Jester, give it back to him in return for page 34.

 

You may get a lot of different cards but you need the Jester so be prepared to spend a bit of money here.

 

Once you have both Pages 30 and 34 in your possession, combine them and create the Testament of Vanear.
