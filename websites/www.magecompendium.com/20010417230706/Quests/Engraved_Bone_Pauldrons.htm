Engraved Bone Pauldrons

 

This quest begins with with one of the noblemen in Kael Drakkal. He can be found in a room in the same building as the food vendor.

This NPC has three different tasks which he will assign to you depending on your faction. The firsts of these assignments will reward you with Engraved Bone Pauldrons. If you tell him that you will accept his assignment he will give you an order for a toolset. You must take this order to an NPC who is standing outside of the room. If your faction is not high enough the NPC will not give you the toolset.

Upon attaining the toolset you will need to travel to the Iceclad Ocean. There is an NPC by the name of Sojon who spawns in one of the igloos at the gnome camp there. Hand him the toolset and he will hand you a note to give back to the noble who sent you there. Return him the note and you will be rewarded with the Engraved Bone Pauldrons.
