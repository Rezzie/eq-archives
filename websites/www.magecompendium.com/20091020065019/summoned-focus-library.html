    Magician Summoned Focus Items

    Courtesy Khysanth for the original submission

    Gallenite's Sapphire Bracer

    Focus Effect: Gallenite's Chaos
    Actual Effects: 1: Increase Spell Damage by 20%
    2: Limit: Max Level(65)
    3: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)
    4: Limit: Instant spells only
    5: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
    6: Limit: Target(AE PC v1 excluded)
    7: Limit: Target(PB AE excluded)
    8: Limit: Target(Targeted AE excluded)
    9: Limit: Target(Uber Giants excluded)
    10: Limit: Target(Uber Dragons excluded)
    Most Comparable To: Improved Damage IV

    Jolum's Brilliant Bauble

    Focus Effect: Jolum's Superior Abatement
    Actual Effects: 1: Decrease Spell Mana Cost by 15%
    2: Limit: Max Level(65)
    3: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
    4: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)
    Most Comparable To: Mana Preservation IV (although this is only for damage spells, not all spells).
    **note** This earring has often been thought to be an agro reducer - It is not.

    Naki's Spiked Ring

    Focus Effect: Naki's Superior Pernicity
    Actual Effects: 1: Increase Spell Haste by 15%
    2: Limit: Max Level(65)
    3: Limit: Spell(Complete Healing excluded)
    4: Limit: Min Casting Time(3.00 sec)
    5: Limit: Effect(Percentage Heal excluded)
    Most Comparable To: Spell Haste IV

    Tavee's Runed Mantle

    Focus Effect: Tavee's Superior Diuturnity
    Actual Effects: 1: Increase Spell Duration by 15%
    2: Limit: Max Level(65)
    3: Limit: Spell Type(Beneficial only)
    4: Limit: Effect(Complete Heal (with duration) excluded)
    5: Limit: Effect(Invunerability excluded)
    6: Limit: Min Duration(48.00 sec)
    Most Comparable To: Extended Enhancement IV

    Jedah's Platinum Choker

    Focus Effect: Jedah's Superior Conservation
    Actual Effects: 1: Decrease Chance of Using Reagent by 15%
    2: Limit: Max Level(65)
    3: Limit: Effect(Summon Item excluded)
    Most Comparable To: Reagent Conservation III

    Please note only the level 65 versions of the focus items are detailed here. The reason for this is that all the focus items regardless of the level of spell used have the same effect. The limitation is simply the maximum level spell the focus item can affect.

    For example the Level 12 spell Summon: Tiny Ring will create a ring that has the same effect as the one listed above with one exception, it will only effect spells up to level 29.
