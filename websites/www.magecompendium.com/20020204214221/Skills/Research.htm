Magician Research

 

 

Research can be carried out by all pure spell casters Wizards, Necromancers and Magicians. Magicians require a few basic things before they can start their research into the arcane arts.

 

    An Elemental Grimoire - Available at all Mage guilds.

 

    Spells Books - These are optional, but I have heard rumors that they increase success rate.

 

Also available to buy are four spells books containing the required formula for each spell you can research. These books are written in four different Languages (Old Erudian, Giant Tongue, Dragon and Elder Dragon) which you can either learn at you guild or from other players.

 

To make the spell you require place the components listed in the spell book into your Elemental Grimoire. Hit Combine, if you have the required skill you should now have the spell listed.

 

As with any skill you have to start off with the easier items first (Lower level spells) and work you way to the harder items improving you skill as you go.
