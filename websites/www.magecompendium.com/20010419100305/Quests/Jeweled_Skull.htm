Jeweled Skull

 

 

Yiz Pon in West Karana will ask you to deliver a note to his brother in Freeport named Hyrill Pon.

 

Yiz Pon is running from the Dungeon of Splitpaw and Splitpaw Assassin

 

After you talk to Yiz Pon, a Splitpaw assassin will show himself.

 

Kill the assassin and you will loot a hollow skull container from him.

 

Yiz Pon stole this skull from within Splitpaw and tried to escape, but then had to give it to the assassin so that it would stop hunting him. However, the assassin is still trying to kill him.

 

Take a note from Yiz Pon to his brother Hyrill Pon in West Freeport.

 

Hyrill will tell you that his brother was searching for some powerful skull which should give great power to a wizard.

 

To continue with the quest from Hyrill, you must find two Eyes made of rubies. One ruby is found on Lyunga in Innothule Swamp. She is a level 32 Troll that sometimes casts and sometimes dual wields.

 

Note : Killing her will give you bad faction within Grobb.

 

The second ruby is called a "Ruby Pendant" and can be looted from a Gnoll Shaman named Kurrpok who spawns in the Dungeon of Splitpaw.

 

Note : He still spawns but is killed very fast, by the mobs near him. You have to camp his old spawn spot carefully.

 

Once you have acquired both +1 Intelligence Ruby Eyes, put them in the Hollowed Skull and press "combine". After they have all been combined, you will now have a Jeweled skull.
