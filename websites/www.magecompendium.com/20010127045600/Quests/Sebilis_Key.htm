Sebilis Key

 

All high level characters should attempt this quest to obtain the key to Sebilis.

Emperor Ganak will grant you a key to Old Sebilis if you bring him two sacred medallions. He needs the Medallion of Kunzar from the froglok forager and the Medallion of Nathsar from the froglok hunter. The two frogloks are both in Trakanon's Teeth. Give him the two medallions for your Trakanon Idol (The Key to Sebilis).

First kill the froglok forager for the Medallion of Kunzar.

Second kill the froglok hunter for the Medallion of Nathsar.

Last of all give both medallions to Ganak for your Trakanon Idol.
