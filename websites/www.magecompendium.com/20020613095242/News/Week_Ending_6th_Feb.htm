Date :    03/02/2000

 

Topic :   Patch

 

February 3rd, 8:00am

The following changes have been implemented on the live servers:


*Spells*

- Lay On Hands: Enhanced to heal more hitpoints per level.
- Enveloping Roots: Casting time reduced to match Immobilize
- Skin Like Nature: Small Damage Shield added. This will stack with other shields.
- Insidious Fever: Casting time reduced.
- Insidious Malady: Casting time Reduced.
- Burnout series: Negative HP component removed to bring it in line with the Necromancer's Intensify/Augment death series.


*Songs*

- Syvelian's Anti-magic Aura: Potency increased.
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding: Now also grants See Invisible. Party members can now see each other while under the effect of this song.

Additionally, the following songs have been given a slightly larger radius:

- Selo's Accelerando
- Agilmente's Aria of Eagles
- Shauri's Sonorous Clouding
- Tarew's Aquatic Ayre


*PvP-Teams Change*

Customers on the PvP-Teams servers have been plagued by a tactic known as "Bind Rushing". Casters would bind themselves in a hostile area or dungeon, and attack everyone of an opposing race in the area. Sooner or later the caster would die. They would then respawn, memorize a few spells, then jump back in to attack their wounded opponents. 

As a first step in combating this practice and improving life for those on the PvP-Servers, casters will now respawn with zero mana after being killed by another player in a duel or open combat.

This code is new for those on Tallon and Vallon Zek. It has been in place for some time on Rallos Zek and works very well there.


*The Plane of Sky*

All spells that grant levitation will no longer function in the Plane of Sky. This affects spells cast by Wizards, Enchanters, Shamans, Druids, Necromancers, and Bards. The Plane of Sky is designed to be completed in certain fashion, and the existence of these spells allowed the islands to be completed out-of-order.

A teleporter will be placed on the first island in the next patch to allow people access to the room below.

- The EverQuest Team

Date :    01/02/2000

 

Topic :   Duel Wield

 

Make all applicable mobs capable of dual wielding. Making mobs tougher is no problem.

 

Making all the NPCs more powerful would drastically change the dynamics of the game. Their damages and attack speeds were all adjusted *assuming* that they were properly dual wielding. The outcome of this is that they are as powerful as they should be, even though they are only using one attack. People notice and voice objection when we up the damage on a mob by 2pts. If we were to turn on dual wield for all of these NPCs, I can assure you that it would be a problem.

 

- Gordon

Pathing is bugged

 

There are areas where pathing could be improved/fixed -- we need to know about them so the Game Designer in charge of pathing can fix it.  He's been adjusting and fixing pathing for over a year now, and most areas are fixed though, but I'm sure there are some that could be worked on.

Not sure what you mean here.  Mobs can teleport when they get stuck, and they can also teleport at times due to lag.  There is also an obscure bug that can cause them to teleport for no discernible reason we've been trying to hunt down for months, but this is fairly rare.

Login server is puking up a storm

The login server works fine unless more than like a thousand people try to log on simultaneously.  We have no intention of throwing additional hardware at the Login Server to make that number 2,000 or some such, in that that many people trying to log on doesn't happen often and always clears up in a relatively short period of time (as more and more people get into the game).

As of the Plane of Sky, ALL zones are itemized.  I'm also not aware of any entire zone that is 'broken'.  Could zones be improved? Absolutely, and that's one of the big tasks the EQ Live team has ahead of them.  In addition to populating Paineel, they will revamp Kerra Ridge and then probably Najena, and perhaps Mistmoore then as well, although they'll also be responsible for populating and tuning the Hole.

And you're working to add "reverb" in caves..
