Circlet of Mist

 

 

Note : This guide is written on the personal experiences of the Mages involved in maintaining this site.

 

Written and Compiled by Shaidar Haran.

 

 

Joyce in the Temple of Solusek Ro requires the following components to make the circlet of mist for you :


Note : All components are No Drop except for the Sapphire.


A Globe of Mist is dropped by a Festering Hag in the Estate of Unrest, you will find many of these creatures in the basement and it is well advised to tackle them with at least 3 people level 30 or more for complete safety.


A Watery Ring off an Aqua Goblin Tidal Lord in Dagnor's Cauldron, these creatures appear on the shores of the lake past the Aqua goblin camp and will prove an easy kill for a Mage level 25 or more.


A Ring of Evoluoy off a Alligator in Cazic-Thule, this is probably the hardest item to obtain as it is a very rare drop, any Aligator in the Green or Blue pools will drop this item.

 

Note : Do not attempt an Alligator solo unless you are at least level 30 or more.


A Sapphire, available in any good Jewelry store, I got mine in Freeport at the Blue shop in North Freeport.

 

 

- Shaidar Haran

 

Festering Hags of Unrest : Festering hags can be a real disaster. They spawn deep in the heart of Unrest, which means if anything goes bad you'll be tempted to flee through the house to escape. Don't. If you get in over your head and can't Gate out, look for the open window and jump. The hags and other nastiness will start going through the house to hunt you down, but this should give you enough time to Gate or run for the zone.

 

I've had some success with picking off the hags when they were part of another person's train. Just camp out at the back door and wait... it'll happen unless there's a good group above camping the hags.

 

Tidal Lords : I didn't have too many problems with the tidal lords, though the biggest threat out here at level 29 is that they'll wear you out through sheer numbers. The tidal lords are around level 32 or so, much tougher than the mariners and bosons who spawn on the island. Other than the goblins, there's nothing of consequence in the waters surrounding the island. Keep in mind that the tidal lord you seek might be swimming (and I've also seen one shore-side near the Heroes Camp). Use your Waterstones and swim around the island with your pet. If you get attacked by two tidal lords at once, you're probably not going to make it unless you're level 34 or higher.

 

Alligators of Cazic Thule : The best thing to do for these is to swim down the pool and camp with a good group in the hallway between the two pools. It is a rare drop, so be prepared to camp for a while. On the plus side, experience is very good during your late 20s to 30. Don't try to solo here, as there are times you'll find yourself fighting three alligators at once. You'll also find a lot of the mage focus item quest components on the gators (stein, torch, broom, shovel) and a few words.
