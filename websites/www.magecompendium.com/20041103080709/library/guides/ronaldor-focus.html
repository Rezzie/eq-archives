Ronaldor's Guide to Temple of Solusek Ro Focus Items

Written By: Ronaldor Vladimir

Note : All of the giant toes are dropped by Gornit, a hill giant in the Ocean of Tears

Deliver the items listed below to obtain the four mage Focus Items to Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro.

Broom of Trilon

The Broom of Trilon was held by the infamous Mistress of Air of the same name. It will need the following components to make it :

A Star Ruby.
A Feather from a Griffon.
A Broom from a Magician in Najena or Alligators in the Feerott.
Toes of a Cyclops.

Shovel of Ponz

The Shovel of Ponz was crafted by the Earthen Master of the same name. It will need the following components to make it :

A Ruby.
The Eyes of a Gargoyle in Castle Mistmoore.
A Shovel from a Magician in Najena or Alligators in Feerott.
Toes of a Hill Giant.

Torch of Alna

The Torch of Alna was constructed and held by the Fire Mistress of the same name. It will need the following items to make it :

A Fire Emerald.
A Scale from a Fire Drake.
A Torch from a Magician in Najena or Alligators in Feerott.
Toes of a Fire Giant.

Stein of Ulissa

The Stein of Ulissa was found and used by the Mistress of Water of the same name. It will need the following items to make it :

A Sapphire.
A Scale from a Mermaid in The Ocean of Tears.
A Stein from a Magician in Najena or Alligators in Feerott.
Toes of an Ice Giant.
