The Story of a New Mage

 

I have a few different characters on the game, but in all honestly I love playing the Magician. I am not one for many words really so I will get right to the point. I started a Gnome Mage, dumped all my points into intelligence (of course) and the remaining into Agility, don’t ask me why I just like to do that. Anyhow...

I wacked everything in sight in The Steamfont Mountains until level 4, WhooHoo Pets! Level 1 - 4 are kind of tedious but can be done in a sitting or two.

When you get your first pet spells the wait will be worth it. By level 4 u should have collected enough coin to buy most of your spells and hopefully as much cloth armor as possible). If you do not have enough coin, at least by the Fire Elemental for Steamfont, The 2nd pet I summoned coned yellow to me. The pets range 1-3 levels from your own. If you are lucky enough to get a con yellow pet (and you will) that means you can take on yellow monsters and more often than not, red... Great Experience. With a high intelligence and a pet you will level faster than any other class in the game (almost anyway). Just explore your surrounding area and get loot and experience until level 8, get a bubble or 2 into 8 though.

What I did is got the invisibility spell and high tailed it to the lovely human city of Qeynos (sonyeq) I got bound there (not hard to do just shout it out as you know) and head to Blackburrow. Great Experience. Never hard to group here and Humans love Gnomes, especially the ladies hehe. KILL, LOOT, KILL, LOOT… get the idea, you can stay here until level 16 - 20 depending on how patient you are.

Also to change things up a bit take the boat to Erud’s Crossing. The boats first stop is in Erud’s Crossing. Wisps… CHA CHANG is all I got to say about these little bad boy light bulbs. You can get almost 8pp for a single Greater Light stone and about a plat for a regular light stones. A couple of days here and you will be able to afford a hot tub in Steamfront :-). B.T.W. I started killing Wisps with a friend same level at level 10. Get killed sometimes but well worth the coin.

That’s it for now, check back in a few weeks and I’ll give you a run down when I have progressed further :-).
