Golden Ear Stud

 

 

Note : Cubert is normally camped by farmers alot.

 

 

Within Qeynos at the bridge near the bank, there is an NPC named Zamel.

 

Speak to him about the aqueducts, beasts and children. He will then give you the information you need to start the quest.

 

Go down into the water where the bridge is and find the spawn point within the sewer (132, -63).

 

The Entrance is between the bank and the Mage guild in Qeynos. Just swim down there. In the catacombs follow the path, when you reach the first crossroad (One path goes to the left). Head left and after a few steps is a huge patch of mud at the end. This is the place where Cuburt spawns.

 

He has three placeholders :

 

Giant Rat,

Mangy Rat,

and a Gelatinous Cube.

 

Note : That there is a way down in the water right next to Zamel but it has been blocked off for downward travel.

 

You must use the other entrances to get down.

 

Kill everything that spawns and at some point Cubert, a gelatinous cube, will come out.

 

Kill him and he will sometimes drop a tattered dress.

 

Note : Kill Cuburt quickly or he will sometimes kill himself within the water as he runs away.

 

Take the tattered dress back to Zamel and he will in return give you a +3 Intelligence Earring (Golden Ear Stud).

 

Note : The booby prize is a piece of hematite. Getting the earring in return is actually quite rare. Be prepared to do this quest over and over.
