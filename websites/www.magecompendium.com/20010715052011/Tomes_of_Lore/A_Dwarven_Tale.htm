A Dwarven Tale

 

Note : This tale is completely fictitious and does not relate in any way to Norrathian history or any events past or present taking place in Norrath.

Aileena says, 'Where is something interesting....'

Aileena says, 'Hmm.. History is so dull at times. Perhaps a story my father told me...'

Aileena closes her book and puts it away.

Aileena says, 'There now...'

Aileena clears her throat a bit.

Aileena says, 'Where to begin....'

Aileena says, 'Once, a long time ago, when the world was young, and the gods' presence could still be felt by mere mortals...'

Aileena says, 'there was a King who ruled over the race of Dwarves deep in the earth, over the city of Kaladim.'

Aileena says, 'He was a good king. Strong and powerful, but also fair and just.'

Aileena says, 'He had trained with the Paladins as a young lad, and was well known for his good deeds about the land.'

Aileena says, 'His name was Darkathan.'

Aileena says, 'In his youth, Darkathan wandered the lands far and wide....'

Aileena says, 'He traveled o'er the Ocean of Tears to the continent now known as Antonica....'

Aileena says, 'and spread the word of the Paladins, meting out justice to the wicked and evil of heart.'

Aileena says, 'One day, he came upon a group of Dark Elves in the forests of Nektulos...'

Aileena says, 'He crept up to them silently, watching what they were doing....'

Aileena says, 'The appeared to be sitting around a fire, discussing some evil plan as their skeleton pets watched the surrounding area,.'

Aileena says, 'Intent on ridding the land of these heretics, Darkathan crept closer, until he was almost on top of the camp...'

Aileena says, 'Then he heard what they were saying....'

Aileena says, '"You can't be serious" said one. "Aye, but I am. The Queen has ordered it!" said another.'

Aileena says, '"But to cross the Ocean? To the lands of those peace loving elves and dwarves? But why??" the first asked.'Aileena says, 'Then he heard what they were
saying....'

Aileena says, '"The Queen wants us to cause some kind of trouble over there in Kaladim.  She'll give us more information when we go to see her."'

Aileena says, 'Darkathan had heard enough.  With a cry he leapt up and charged at the Necromancers, quickly cutting them down before they were even able to call to their pets for protection.'

Aileena says, 'The necromancer's pets, not having a chance to react, crumbled into piles of dust and bones at the death of their masters.  Darkathan took a moment to survey the scene.'

Aileena says, '"Bah" he spat. "They must have been new to the Necromancers to fall that easily."'

Aileena says, 'He picked through their remains and found a note....'

Aileena says, 'It was scribed in a language he didn't understand, a spidery scrawl that was evil even to look at.'

Aileena says, 'Unsure of what to do next, he wandered out of the forest, and went to a small village nearby, that would one day become the city of Freeport.'

Aileena says, 'He went to the village elder, and asked him to translate the letter...'

Aileena says, '"Ah, most curious indeed" said the Elder. "You have stumbled upon a rather rare find here..."'

Aileena says, '"This letter contains specific instructions for a group of Shadowknights to make their way to Kaladim, and includes a detailed plan of the city."'

Aileena says, '"With this, they could easily sneak in and cause whatever mischief they would want before anyone would know about it!"'

Aileena says, 'Darkathan thought about the issue at hand, and asked what the best course of action would be.'

Aileena says, 'The Elder replied "Go back to your fellows in Kaladim. Warn them of this danger, and work to prevent it. Surely this is not the only copy of this map around."'

Aileena says, 'Taking the Elder's advice, Darkathan returned to his city, and showed his fellow Paladins what he had found.'

Aileena says, 'They then took steps to seal off the secret entrances, and lay traps to throw off any of the evil ones that might try to attack.'

Aileena says, 'A week later, a group of two dozen Dark Elf shadowknights crept silently through the forests and mountains of Butcherblock, intent on finding the entrances and carrying out their heinous tasks....'

Aileena says, 'But the Paladins were waiting for them, and a great battle ensued !'

Aileena says, 'The light magic of the Paladins was equal to that of the Shadowknights...'

Aileena says, 'And although the numbers were small, the battle raged for almost an hour.'

Aileena says, 'Finally, in the end, Darkathan managed to seriously wound the last of the Shadowknights, a strong and evil warrior who had taken down many of the Paladins there....'

Aileena says, 'But the Shadowknight suddenly disappeared, stepping lightly into the shadows, and disappearing with the night...'

Aileena says, 'The king at the time awarded Darkathan for his good deeds, and made him the next in line to take the throne of Kaladim....'

Aileena says, 'It is several years later that the main part of this story takes place....'

Aileena says, 'The king of Kaladim had died, and Darkathan took over as the ruler of the city.'

Aileena says, 'And far across the see, a Dark Elf... a shadowknight, with the scars of battle across his chest, slowly fueled his hatred of the Dwarf....'

Aileena says, 'This Shadowknight who was almost killed by Darkathan was named Ty'Vallan....'

Aileena says, 'Even after 15 years he still remembered the night that he almost died at the hands of that Paladin who now sat upon his throne and played king of the dwarves.'

Aileena says, 'And one day, he set his revenge into motion....'

Aileena says, 'He went to the rogues guild, and learned some of the art of disguise....'

Aileena says, 'Using these techniques, he once again made his way across the Ocean of Tears to the mountains of Butcherblock...'

Aileena says, 'On the day of his arrival on Faydwer, he saw a pair of young dwarves sparring near the waters...'

Aileena says, 'As he looked closer, he saw that these were two Paladins, of the same guild as the King.'

Aileena says, 'As he stared evilly at the two dwarves, a final formation of his plan of vengeance manifested in his twisted mind...'

Aileena says, 'The two dwarves were named Kalgath and Gathral. They grew up together and were best friends, both joining the Paladins of Kaladim and training together.'

Aileena says, 'After a few hours of practice with the hot sun beating down on them, they decided to take a break.'

Aileena says, '"I will head back to Kaladim and get us some fresh water and food while you rest" offered Kalgath.'

Aileena says, '"Aye, that would be great." the Gathral responded.'

Aileena says, 'And so Kalgath made his way back to the city, as Gathral found some shade under a nearby tree, letting the light ocean breeze cool his face.'

Aileena says, 'At this moment, Ty'Vallan decided to implement his plan...'

Aileena says, 'Donning a guise to make him look like a human, he walked up to the dwarf, and hailed him.'

Aileena says, '"Greetings, good Dwarf" he said.'

Aileena says, '"Greetings friend" replied Gathral.'

Aileena says, '"What might I do for you this fine day?"'

Aileena says, 'Ty'Vallan smiled to himself, but was careful not to let it show...'

Aileena says, '"My name is Merdill. I am a messenger from the Elder of the small town of Freeport...'

Aileena says, '"I have a message to give to your king, but I am afraid I need to get back home as soon as I can... you see, my wife is with child.... and... "'

Aileena says, 'Gathral stood up and said "Say no more friend. Go back, and tend to your family. I will take care of your message for you."'

Aileena says, '"My thanks!" responded Ty'Vallan, sensing his victory near...'

Aileena says, '"Here, take this note, and this potion. It is infused with a Spirit of the Wolf, and will speed your journey back to your King" he told the Paladin.'

Aileena says, '"My thanks" replied Gathral. "Now go, I will see that this message is safely delivered directly to King Darkathan."'

Aileena says, 'The disguised Dark Elf beamed a smile at the Dwarf, and waved goodbye, turning as if to walk back to his ship...'

Aileena says, 'But once he was out of site, he used a spell to make himself invisible, and crept back towards the Paladin.'

Aileena says, 'Gathral said to himself "I wonder what can be of such importance. Ah well, I'll not break my word, for my word is my life!"'

Aileena says, 'And with that, he gulped down the potion, and began to run towards Kaladim as fast as he could....'

Aileena says, 'Ty'Vallan laughed evilly, knowing the true nature of the potion the Paladin had just imbibed...'

Aileena says, 'For it did contain the Spirit of the Wolf... of this there was no doubt...'

Aileena says, 'But it also contained an evil spell of Suggestion....'

Aileena says, 'And before handing it over, Ty'Vallan had made very sure what suggestion was used with the potion....'

Aileena says, 'And as Gathral ran back to Kaladim a fast as his magic infused short legs could carry him, one thought ran through his head.....'

Aileena says, '"Kill the king!!!!!"'

Aileena says, 'And so he ran, determination spreading across his face.. to find and kill the King. And after that, it didn't matter...'

Aileena says, 'Now it so happened that Gathral's friend, Kalgath, was coming out of the city as Gathral was passing by...'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath, wandering why Gathral was moving so quickly, and in such an obvious hurry, turned around, dropping his backpack of supplies, and chased after the Paladin.'

Aileena says, 'He managed to catch up with him as Gathral was going through the Guard Checkpoint....'

Aileena says, '"My friend, " he started... "Why in such a hurry? I thought we were going to continue to spar until the evening came?"'

Aileena says, 'Gathral glared at him, and said in a harsh voice "Back off! I have important business to tend to!"'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath was stunned at this... His friend had never spoken so harshly to him before....'

Aileena says, 'He sensed that something was wrong, but didn't know what...'

Aileena says, 'So he focused, calling upon his god and focusing his powers of good....'

Aileena says, 'Stop!!" cried Kalgath!'

Aileena says, 'But Gathral paid him no heed...'

Aileena says, '"Stop! Stop! Go no further!"'

Aileena says, 'But again Gathral would not listen...'

Aileena says, 'And so Kalgath pulled out his sword, and put himself in front of the path of Gathral....'

Aileena says, '"I see that aura around you. You are possed by evil! What has done this to you my friend?"'

Aileena says, 'But no answer came from Gathral... instead, his sword whistled from it's scabbard, straight for the head of Kalgath!'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath parried the blow, and backed away...'

Aileena says, 'But Gathral lunged again, this time aiming for the heart...'

Aileena says, 'The fight ensued, Kalgath trying not to hurt his friend, and Gathral using all in his power to force Kalgath out of his way...'

Aileena says, 'All this commotion soon brought forth the Kaladim Guard....'

Aileena says, 'And they charged in, grabbing both the Paladins, and restraining them...'

Aileena says, 'The Guard took their weapons, and lead them to the King....'

Aileena says, '"What is the meaning of this?" asked Darkathan of the two Paladins before him, stripped of their weapons and held by the Guards.'

Aileena says, '"I will NOT have fighting in MY city, especially not between to respected and honorable Paladins!'

Aileena says, 'Gathral's face lightened, and he bowed down on one knee...'

Aileena says, '"Please forgive us, oh great King." he said. I meant no offense to you or to these good Guards here. I was overtaken by a madness. I know not why. If you will grant it of me, I would ask my weapon back, and leave to visit the Clerics for healing."'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath tried to break in, but the King stopped him. "Very well" said the King. "Guards, give him his weapon, and take him to the Clerics Guild."'

Aileena says, 'And so it was done....'

Aileena says, 'But as soon as the sword was back into Gathral's hands, he cried out, darkness spreading over his face, and ran straight at the shocked King!'

Aileena says, 'Before he could react, Gathral had ran his sword through the chest of the King...'

Aileena says, 'Screaming in pain and anger, Kalgath grabbed his sword from the Guard, and charged his friend...'

Aileena says, 'The fight was short, and in the few seconds it took, Kalgath ran the sword through his friends stomach, then brought it down over his head....'

Aileena says, 'And as he died, Gathral's face lightened, and he whispered "I'm sorry"......'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath let out a cry so mournful it echoed down the halls of Kaladim....'

Aileena says, '"Wait!" shouted a Guard, leaning over the King... " He is still alive! But faintly..."'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath momentarily shoved his sorrow to the back of his mind, and bent over the King...'

Aileena says, 'He focused hard, calling once again on his god, and put his hands over the gaping wound....'

Aileena says, 'A sudden burst of light filled the chamber, and a shiver was sent down the spine of both Kalgath and the King, and as the light faded, the Guards could see the wound on the King was healed....'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath, exhausted by the strain of using his Lay on Hands, collapsed, and began to weep over the loss of his friend....'

Aileena says, 'King Darkathan slowly stood up, and looked down at his newly healed chest...'

Aileena says, 'Breathing a sigh of relief, he helped Kalgath to his feet....'

Aileena says, 'You saved me, " said the King. "Whatever can I do to repay you?"'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath looked at the King, his eyes wet with tears, and his lips trembling...'

Aileena says, '"My liege" he began...'

Aileena says, '"My friend is dead. And I do not even know why?? And I was the one that killed him! Oh what have I done!?"'

Aileena says, '"Please, my liege, allow me to find the person who did this... Surely Gathral was under the influence of some dark magic! Please, let me avenge my friends death! And atone for my sin of killing him...'

Aileena says, 'The King looked at him with saddened eyes, and said "Aye, you may do so. But be careful not to let your anger take the form of vengeance. Do this to rid evil of the land.. not because you seek to punish."'

Aileena says, 'Kalgath nodded at the king. "You are right, my liege. And I will rid the evil of this land! That will be my atonement."'

Aileena says, 'King Darkathan nodded, and led Kalgath into a side chamber by his shoulder.'

Aileena says, 'He pulled out a key, and unlocked the door, revealing the Kaladim Treasury.'

Aileena says, '"Take whatever you need, my young friend. Buy your supplies, equip yourself, and start your journey when you see fit."'

Aileena says, '"Aye, I thank you, my liege" replied Kalgath, bowing before the King.'

Aileena says, 'And so Kalgath took some gold and bought the necessary supplies. And the King ordered the best Smiths to create special armor and a sword for him, of the finest dwarven metals...'

Aileena says, 'And thus, with a heavy but determined heart, Kalgath left his home, and started a journey that would take him many years... '

Aileena says, 'But that, my friends, is another story.'

Aileena smiles.
