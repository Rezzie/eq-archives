The Staff of The Ankexfen

Note : I have tried this quest myself and have got as far as I have detailed here.

This quest starts in Najena with the Visiting Priestess.

The Visiting Priestess spawns near the Ogre Captain.

If evil, you can hail her and she asks "Are you the champion that Najena sent for ?".

You have to say "I will champion for Najena." and she will give you her quest.

She wishes to put together the The Staff of The Ankexfen.

Tell her "I have not heard of the staff."

Then tell her "What happened to the Ankexfen ?"

She will then reveal that the staff has been broken into four parts which were given to various goblin tribes. She wants you to collect these pieces and states that you will be rewarded by the Priests of Innoruuk for their return.

These pieces are the four Crystal Staves :

The Ice Crystal Staff : Scryer in Permafrost

The Water Crystal Staff : Webclaw Murkwave in Lake Rathe

The Slime Crystal Staff : King Xorbb in The Gorge of King Xorbb

The Fire Crystal Staff : Goblin Drunkard in Solusek's Eye

Collect these and pass them to her and in return you will receive an unreadable note. As you pass her each piece, she grins evilly and adds it to the staff she is carrying until she wields the Staff of The Ankexfen.

Note : I do not know if anyone has killed her when she is wielding the staff to obtain it. Or even what the staff does.

Once she has all four pieces, a scroll called the Scroll of Flayed Goblin Skin will appear in your inventory.

It is written in Dark Elf, it says "Some illegible writings about Neriak and blue flames".

Note : Nobody is sure who you give the scroll to. A priest will spawn in the Temple Steps in Neriak and immediately kill herself, so it may be that she is the one, but that it is broken. However seeing as the first part works fine, it is likely that the quest is solvable.
