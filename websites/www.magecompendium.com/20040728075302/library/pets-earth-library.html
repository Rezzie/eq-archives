Earth Pet

View the Forums for extra details.

Earth is the workhorse of the elementals. It sports the most HP, highest AC, and highest ATK, but the lowest AGI and DEX. From levels 4 to 49 it casts Earth Elemental Attack, a root. At 51 and 57 it casts Earth Elemental Strike, and Rathe's Son casts Earth Elemental Strike 2, both which are root with a DD component. Earth also sports a near-immunity to Disease-based spells.
