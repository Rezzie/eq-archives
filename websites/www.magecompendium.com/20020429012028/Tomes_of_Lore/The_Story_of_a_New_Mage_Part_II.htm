The Story of a New Mage Part II

 

So you made it to level 12 huh. Probably bored with your surrounding area as well... no fear, here's where we are going to get you to level 16 in about 4 days of sitting. Kerra Isle, just outside of Toxxulia Forest is a great area to level and level fast. The kitty factions here only effect what's on the island so no need to worry about getting killed on sight anywhere else but here.

Before you zone into Kerra Island from Toxxyoulia Forest make sure you bind before the zone, trust me, bind before the zone. Good you are zoned now... there are two Kerran Awrats guarding the entrance to the little kitty city, there yours. They should con yellow to black/white to you by now, sometimes blue. What you need to do now is break up the spawn. I used an earth pet for these, because you need to attack and root one and then pull your pet off and work on the other, you just want to kill one now, might take you a few tries. After succeeding in killing one zone. When you zone back in you will no longer be aggroed and can work on one Awrat at a time... great experience, also collect all the fish and milk you can, because if your like me at level 12 you hate summoning food and water at the startup of a game day hehe.

When you progress a level I recommend 2 levels try the Mamluks. After splitting the spawn, once you level once here you will have your own system of kerran slaughter. The rest is history, and if you can tell me what these damn dolls are for that the Kerrans drop, you will get a ton please let me know. I've deleted over 80 at least I'm sure.

You can stay here and camp it, the most people I've seen here is four others and I've never seen them they are just hunting Jojo, don't worry about Jojo for a while. If you bring a friend, I had an enchanter friend with me who charms the kittys and we watch them fight betting plate who will win, you will have a ball as both of you level fast make a little gold and watch the kittys fall.

I cannot express how important it is to use earth pet on this one, if they run they will bring friends and the trains here although rare are the toughest I've seen in the game at this level, but the zone is right there and you will be fine. Do not stop if you fail a few times you will get a system down and you will be able to do it in your sleep.
