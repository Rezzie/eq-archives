Veeshan's Peak Key

 

Emperor Ganak will grant you a key to Old Sebilis if you bring him two sacred medallions.

He needs the Medallion of Kunzar from the froglok forager and the Medallion of Nathsar from the froglok hunter. The two frogloks are both in Trakanon's Teeth.

Give him the two medallions for Trakanon's Idol (Sebilis key).

After receiving your key to Sebilis, go in and kill Trakanon.

Recover one of Trakanon's fangs and bring it to Emperor Ganak along with the three remaining tribal medallions.

The Medallion of Obdulus, The Medallion of Kylong, and the Medallion of Jarsath.
