Rain Spells

 

 

It seems that removing the pets from the equation would be the best way to make rain spells useful without making them too useful. And it might also just eliminate the bug where some pets attack other PCs when they are caught in a rain or AoE spell. It might also be a good idea to look at the resist rates on these spells, which seem to be higher than with most spells. Some people would also like there to be one or two waves to these spell types.
