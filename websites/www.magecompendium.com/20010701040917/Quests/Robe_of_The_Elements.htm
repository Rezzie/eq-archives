Robe of The Elements

 

 

Note : This guide is written on the personal experiences of the Mages involved in maintaining this site.

 

 

Note : I managed to do this quest solo at Level 30, it is a lot easier if you offer a small amount of Platinum for the looting rights to obtain these items especially the Twice-Woven Cloak. 

 

Joyce in the Temple of Solusek Ro will make a Robe of the Elements if you bring her the following :

 

Fire Goblin skin from Solusek's Eye.

 

 

A Frost Goblin skin, available from Ice Goblin Elite guards in Permafrost.

 

 

Note : I have heard of these items being dropped by normal goblins in Everfrost.

 

A "Twice-Woven Cloak", a semi-rare drop from undead sprite guards in The Lesser Faydark.

 

A scroll of Elemental Armor, available in any Mage guild store.

 

Note : Give her all the items at once as she does not remember who has given her what items and someone else could obtain the robe without handing in all the relevant items.
