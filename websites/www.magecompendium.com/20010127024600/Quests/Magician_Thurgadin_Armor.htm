Magician Thurgadin Armor

 

 

Note : If you hail the Ogres in Lake Rathtear (At the Rathtear Mountains zone) each one will offer you a different service. One will say "Gimme stone I will crush them...", One will say "Gimme stone I will grind them..." and the last one says "Gimmie stone I will shatter them...".

 

Note : I have not tested this information yet it has been sent to me by a reader.

 

Note : Gems seem to be dropping in Cobalt Scar.

 

 

You say, 'hail Kyla Frostbeard'


Kyla Frostbeard says 'Greetings to you.  I seek those who call themselves magicians.  Are you a magician, little one?'


You say, 'I am a magician'


Kyla Frostbeard says 'I thought so.  I have several tasks for you accomplish.  Once you have completed them I will have a cap, a robe, sleeves, wristbands, gloves, leggings and boots to reward you with.'

 

To obtain the Armor you need to turn in a piece of Torn Enchanted Silk clothing and the required Gems.

Leggings

You say, 'I am interested in leggings'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'Three nephrite and a pair of torn enchanted silk leggings will net you a fine set of pantaloons.

Cap

You say, 'I am interested in a cap'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'For an enchanted head guard bring me a torn enchanted silk turban as well as three pieces of crushed flame opal.'

Robe

You say, 'I am interested in a robe'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'If you wish to have a fine robe, bring unto me a torn enchanted silk robe and three pristine emeralds.  Then you will receive a robe beyond your wildest dreams.'

Sleeves

You say, 'I am interested in a sleeves'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'Bring unto me a set of torn enchanted silk sleeves as well as three flawed topaz.  With these in my possession I can create a set of enchanted sleeves the likes of which spellcasters like you dream of.'

Wristguard

You say, 'I am interested in a wristband'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'An enchanted silk wristguard and three crushed onyx sapphires is all that I require to create an imbued wrist wrap of great power.  Seek out these items and return to me when you have acquired them.'

Gloves

You say, 'I am interested in a glove'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'If you seek gloves of great power, bring to me three crushed topaz and a pair of torn enchanted silk gloves.  I will use the power of the gems to imbue the gloves.'

Boots

You say, 'I am interested in boots'

Kyla Frostbeard says 'Three crushed jaundice gems combined with torn enchanted silk boots would make a most interesting set of footwear.  The latent powers of the gems can be harnessed to create magical effects.'
