Horses

 

 

Available for sale at any reputable stable are sixteen types of bridle, these are split into four categories Rope, Leather, Silk and Chain (These correspond to the speed of the horses, with Rope being roughly equivalent to the speed of Journeyman's Boots and Chain being slightly slower than Spirit of The Cheetah.). The colour of each bridle corresponds to the colour of the horse Tan, Brown, White and Black. The prices can range from 10,000pp being the cheapest to 113,750pp for the most expensive (Depending on you faction with the merchants.).


Now once you have purchased a bridle you will need to place it one of your main inventory slots and summon your magical steed by right clicking on the bridle. After a few seconds your mount will arrive and you will be placed upon his or her back. If you no longer require the services of your fine steed you only need click the icon in your buff window and your mount will disappear to be called again when you need him.

Horses can only be summoned while outside.

While riding a horse you can perform all the actions you could under normal circumstances e.g. Spell casting.

 

Note : At present pets can be summoned while riding a horse.

Before you say what is the point of owning a horse when you can quest for Journeyman's Boots or buy a Spirit of the Wolf potion ? Well they are a status symbol for the rich and famous and look wonderful.
