Mass Group Buff

 

This ability gives the Magician the power to make his next group buff spell into an area of affect spell. The spell would benefit everyone in the area, not just the caster's group. This Ability causes the spell to use up to as much as twice as much Mana. It will require at least the normal casting cost, and will use up to as much as twice the Mana if the caster has that much available. The Refresh Timer for this ability is 72 minutes (One day in game time.).
