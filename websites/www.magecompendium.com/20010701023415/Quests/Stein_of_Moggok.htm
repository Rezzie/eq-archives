Stein of Moggok

 

This quest to most will begin in Freeport East, but it actually begins in Oggok.

You can, if you wish to begin in Oggok, but it is totally unnecessary.

If you do decide to begin in Oggok, then go up to Clurg, the bartender in the only tavern of Oggok.

He will say he is looking for his book of drinks.

Now for most of you, you won't go down to Oggok just to hear that so you will begin in Freeport East.

Go to the docks, and into the Seafarer's Roost.

Hail the bartender, Gregor Nasin, then talk to him about exotic ingredients.

The ingredients he is looking for are :

Kiola Nuts - Immediately in front of the docks in The Ocean of Tears on Sisters Island is a group of huts. Go to the one that is right in front of the docks and there should be a Elven woman named Cleonae Kalen behind a bar, she will sell you the Kiola nut.

Erud's Tonic (No Drop) - Go to the Blue Hawk's Inn in Erudin and buy an Innoruk's Kiss of Death (Lore Item) from Geord BlueHawk. Take the Kiss of Death to Sinnkin Highbrow at the top of the Library in Erudin, and she will give you Erud's Tonic.

Honey Jum - Give the three Honeycombs and 30gp to Kizzie Mintopp in Rivervale and she will give you the Honey Jum.

Koalindl Fish (No Drop) - There's lots of the Koalindl fish swimming around near the Temple of Life in Qeynos, so take one out. Be warned that the Koalindl fish are social, so you may be fighting more than one at a time.

Note : You can also get a Koalindl fish by killing Frostbite, a wolf in the Barbarian Fishing village in the South West corner of The Western  Plaines of Karana. Also note that none of the NPCs near the fish like you killing them so if they see you they will attack. You will also take a pretty heavy faction hit for the worse with the good people of Qeynos.

If you get all the components, give them to Gregor Nasin in Freeport, get the Bartenders Compendium.

Travel to Oggok and give the Bartenders Compendium to Clurg, and finally get the Stein of Moggok.

Note : You have to have an Indifferent faction or above with Clurg to obtain the Stein of Moggok.
