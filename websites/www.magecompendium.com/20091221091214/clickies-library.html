    Magician Usable Clickies

    Debuffs and Detrimental Clickies

    Pendant of Malosini
    Found: Diabo Xi Xin (Vex Thal)
    Effect: Malosini (Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 5 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Mages almost always cast Malosini on every monster they fight. This clicky does two things: One, it reduces the need to memorize the spell, freeing up a valuable spell gem. Two, it allows to debuff the mob an infinite number of mobs infinite number of times without expending any mana. The instant recast on this clicky allows one to chain cast Malosini over and over should there be multiple resists.

    Veil of Lost Hopes
    Found: Saryrn (Plane of Time)
    Effect: Malosinia (Required Level: 65. Must Equip. Cast Time 3.3 seconds)
    Why its Useful: Mages almost always cast Malosinia on every monster they fight. This clicky does two things: One, it reduces the need to memorize the spell, freeing up a valuable spell gem. Two, it allows to debuff the mob an infinite number of mobs infinite number of times without expending any mana. The instant recast on this clicky allows one to chain cast Malosinia over and over should there be multiple resists.

    Earring of Living Slime
    Found: No longer drops
    Effect: Instill (Required Level: 51. Must Equip. Cast Time 4 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Arguably the most powerful magician clicyy in the game, the earring grants magicians one powerful spell they do not already have: A form of Root.

    Buffs

    Orb of Infinite Terror
    Found: Saryrn (Plane of Torment)
    Effect: Burnout V (Required Level: 55. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 7.5 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: The most important buff for our pet is Burnout V. Unfoctunately the need to keep this spell memorized uses a valuable slot in our spell lineup. This item removes the need to have it constantly memorized, reeing up a slot for other valuable spells.

    Bangle of Disease Warding
    Found: Quested (Plane of Disease)
    Effect: Resist Disease (Required Level: 55. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time: Instant)
    Why Its Useful: This clicky grants magicians another spell they don't normally have. In situations where shaman buffs or other equivalent buffs are not available, this clicky fills the void. It allows you to increase your disease resistance.

    Earring of the Frozen Skull
    Found: Narandi the Wretched (Great Divide)
    Effect: Divine Aura (4 Charges. Must Equip. Cast Time 1 second)
    Why Its Useful: This clicky is for emergency usage only. It grants the user 18 second invulnerability to damage.

    Earthen Boots
    Found: Quest (Temple of Solusek Ro)
    Effect: Invisibility Versus Animals (Effect Level 20. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time Instant)
    Why Its Useful: This clicky grants the user instant invisibility to animal mobs. While on the surface this seems useless, mages that choose to charm pets with Call of the Arch Mage value it for its ability to instantly break the spell.

    Shield of Auras
    Found: Arch Lich Rhag`Zadune (Ssraeshza Temple)
    Effect: Shield of Auras (Required level 65. Must Equip. Cast Time 7 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This is one of the few items in the game that grants magicians a right click rune ability, absorbing up to 300 HP of incoming damage. This also acts as a form of mana regeneration, stacking with almost all forms of regen.

    Envenomed Sleeves
    Found: Cazic Thule
    Effect: Voice Graft (Required Level 51. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 4 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This grants magicians an ability they normally don't have: Voice Graft. With this, Magicians are able to use /say commands through their pet. A very useful toy. :)

    Item Summon Clickies

    Magi`Kot's Pants of Convergence
    Found: Elemental Silk Pants Pattern, off of:
    Fennin Ro the Tyrant of Fire (Doomfire, the Burning Lands)
    Xegony the Queen of Air (Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind)
    Coirnav the Avatar of Water (Reef of Coirnav)
    Avatar of Earth (Ragrax, Stronghold of the Twelve)
    Plane of Time, Phase 3
    Effect: Rod of Mystical Transvergence (Required Level 65. Must Equip. Cast Time 2 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: One of a Magician's most common and useful duties is the summoning of Rods. Over the course of a single day, though, the act of summoning rods can use up tens or even hundreds of thousands of mana. This item saves that mana, and at the same time increases the gain per mod rod to its maximum.

    Koadic's Robe of Heightened Focus
    Found: Emperor Ssraeshza (Ssraeshza Temple)
    Effect: Koadic's Heightened Focus (Required Level 55. Must Equip. Cast Time 6.5 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This grants a mana regeneration buff that stacks with almost all other buffs, including the entire Clarity line. In this case, it's 5 mana/tic. In long fights this will make a very large difference in damage output.

    Aegis of Discordant Matter
    Found: Weapon Master Vtiink Vzann (Ferubi - Forgotten Temple of Taelosia)
    Effect: Koadic's Heightened Focus (Required Level 63. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 2 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This grants a mana regeneration buff that stacks with almost all other buffs, including the entire Clarity line. In this case, it's 5 mana/tic. In long fights this will make a very large difference in damage output.

    Direct Damage Clickies

    Boots of Bladecalling
    Found: Velketor the Sorceror (Velketor's Labyrinth)
    Effect: Shock of Swords (Effect Level: 40. Must Equip. Cast Time 17 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Like all direct damage clickies, this one lets you inflict nuke damage for no mana. In this case, it provides 35 damage/second mana free. In situations where mana is an issue (especially in long extended fights) these are extremely valuable.

    Burning Scepter of Elements
    Found:Rhag`Mozdezh (Ssraeshza Temple)
    Effect: Blazing Heat (Effect Level: 50. Must Equip. Cast Time 8 seconds)
    Why Its Useful:Like all direct damage clickies, this one lets you inflict nuke damage for no mana. In this case, it provides 31 damage/second mana free. In situations where mana is an issue (especially in long extended fights) these are extremely valuable.

    Burnt Wood Staff
    Found: Chardok
    Effect: Cinder Bolt (Effect Level: 46. Must Equip. Cast Time 13.5 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Like all direct damage clickies, this one lets you inflict nuke damage for no mana. In this case, it provides 24.6 damage/second mana free. In situations where mana is an issue (especially in long extended fights) these are extremely valuable.

    Miscellaneous Clickies

    Gloves of Control
    Found: Rhag`Mozdeth (Ssraeshza Temple)
    Effect: Summon: Companion (Effect Level: 39. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 7.5 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Like all utility clickies, this one is nice to have. It saves you the hassle of searching your spell book to use this utility spell, and it also saves you from spending mana.

    Armguard of Purity
    Found: Diabo Xi Va Temariel (Vex Thal)
    Effect: Abolish Poison (Usable from Inventory. Cast Time: 15 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This clicky grants an ability normally not usable to Intelligence casters: The ability to cure poisons. This clicky can cure very strong poisons with a single cast, including poisons cast on others (including the pet).

    Clay Bracelet
    Found: Quested (Temple of Solusek Ro)
    Effect: Eye of Zomm (Effect Level: 20. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time: Instant)
    Why Its Useful: Like all utility clickies, this one is nice to have. It saves you the hassle of searching your spell book to use this utility spell, and it also saves you from spending mana. In this spell's case, it lets you have an always-available scout handy for scouting around without the need to sit down to memorize the spell.

    Attuned Spire Shard
    Found: Grieg Veneficus (Grieg's End)
    Effect: Combine Gate (Effect Level 46. Must Equip. Cast Time 32 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Like many clickies, this grants an ability not normally usable to Magicians. In this case, it grants Magicians the ability to port himself to The Dreadlands wizard spires. In an emergency situation where a Magician might not be able to safely gate (such as a bad bind point) this clicky will allow the Magician to reach a fairly safe location and make his way to a safer destination.

    Silverflank Belt
    Found: A Silverflank Shiverback (Cazic Thule)
    Effect: Summon: Companion (Required level 51. Usable from Inventory. Cast Time 2 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: Like all utility clickies, this one is nice to have. It saves you the hassle of searching your spell book to use this utility spell, and it also saves you from spending mana.

    Wand of Imperceptibility
    Found: Koxiux the Imperceptible (The Deep)
    Effect: Imperceptibility (Effect Level: 45. Must Equip. Cast Time 8 seconds)
    Why Its Useful: This grants magicians the ability to cast a shrink spell on himself and his or her group members. For large races, this can be a necessity for simple navigation in some areas. For smaller races, this is almost useless.
