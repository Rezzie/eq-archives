Navigation Guide

 

 

There is nothing easier in EverQuest than getting lost. Nothing more frustrating than using sense direction when it is low. Here is a very easy way to know exactly where you are going without the aggravation of 20 consecutive failed attempts at sense direction.

 

First modify a social that you don't need with the following : 

 

Name : Loc 

Command: /loc

 

Second, add the social as a hotkey. This will be your new nicely priced compass and greatly facilitate navigation.

How to use the command effectively

Location provides three pieces of data - X,Y.

 

Oddly enough, the X value is for the Y axis (North - South), the Y value is for the X axis (West - East).

 

Direction


Traveling west raises your Y value (X axis)

Traveling east lowers your Y value (X axis)
Traveling north raises your X value (Y axis)
Traveling south lowers your X value (Y axis)

Strategy

 

When you travel, hit your hotkey continuously. It keeps you updated as to what direction  you are heading as well as your current coordinates. When finding an interesting location take down the coordinates (Or right before you die to find your corpse). This makes it very easy to return to the site afterwards, no searching around anymore, just a straight path to where you want to go.
