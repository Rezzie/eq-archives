Servant's Staff

 

 

Josper Kenshed, of the Erudin Wizard guild is in need of spell components.

 

Note : If Josper Kenshed will not talk to you, raise your faction with the Crimson Hand by killing Elial Brook. Elial Brook is the Necromancer that is near the docks in the Tox Forest.

 

Once you can speak to Josper tell him you will help get his spell components.

 

He will give you a bag that you are to fill with Ice Goblin beads.

 

Note : You can find the Ice Goblin beads off of Ice Goblins within Everforst Mountains.

 

Once you fill the pack, combine them and hand them back to Josper Kenshed.

 

In return, he will give you the Servant's Staff.
