Consider Messages for Monsters and NPC's

 

The /con message varies depending upon your level and the Monster/NPC that you consider.

Social Scale

Throughout Norrath you will meet Monsters and NPC's who will view you with different reactions. Here is a list of the varying degrees with which they will react to you.

Monsters/NPC's that fall into the green section will interact with you to varying degrees, those marked in red will attack you on sight. That is unless you are sufficient level above them for them to consider attacking you futile.
