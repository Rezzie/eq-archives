Magician Pet Weapons, Armor, and Buffs

View the Forums for extra details.

Starting at level 1, Magicians have the potential to summon weapons and armor for their pets. Magicians also have the ability to damage shield their pets for additional damage towards enemies and monsters.

Pets will use almost all droppable items given to them, with few restrictions. Pets also do not follow recommended levels, but they do follow effect levels for weapon procs and such.

Lower level pets that do not dual wield will equip two-handed weapons; pets that dual wield will not equip two-handed weapons but will equip two one-handed weapons. Any weapon given to a pet with a higher damage than the pet's innate melee will boost the pet's attack power to that level. Also, weapons that are Magical in nature will imbue lower level pets with the ability to hit Magical creatures (and on a side note, all Magician summoned weapons are Magical). Pets are unaffected by the weapon type of weapons, be it hand-to-hand, slashing, blunt, or piercing.

Pets are affected by required levels for spells. Higher level spells that are cast on the pet will only affect the pet if it is also castable on the master.
