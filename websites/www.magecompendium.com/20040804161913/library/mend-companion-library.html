Mend Companion AAs

View the Forums for extra details

Ability: Mend Companion
Cost: 5 AA points in the Class section
Pre-req: requires 6 General and 12 Archtype AA points spent
Description: This ability allows you to instantly cast a Lay on Hands type of spell on your pet.
Reuse Timer: 36 minutes base, 27 minutes with Hastened Mending I (2 AA), 18 minutes with HM2 (2 AA), 9 minutes with HM3 (2 AA)

Ability: Hasted Mend
Cost: 2 AA points per level in the PoP Abilities section
Pre-req: Mend Companion, level 61 for the first rank, level 63 for the second rank, level 65 for the third rank.
Description: Decreases the reuse time of Mend Companion by 25% per rank, for a final reuse time of 9 minutes at level 3.

This does (Level x 33 + 1) HP of direct healing to your pet. At level 65, it does 2146 HP instantly. It has no cast time.

Improved Healing focuses do not work on this AA.
