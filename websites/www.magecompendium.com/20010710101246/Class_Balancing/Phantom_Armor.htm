Phantom Armor

 

 

Two concerns here. The first is that it uses 4 components! That’s an awful lot of components to carry around. It would be nice to make the component a single item that costs 4 (or even more) times as much. The second concern is that it is overwritten by Shield of Words. It seems logical that the 52nd level personal magician spell should hold over the 49th level cleric spell. Though the cleric spell seems to have more AC, the mage spell also gives additional regeneration.
