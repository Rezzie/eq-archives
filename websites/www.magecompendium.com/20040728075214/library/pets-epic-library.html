Epic Pet

View the Forums for extra details.

Manifest Elements, more commonly known as the Epic pet, is the reward for the Magician Epic Quest. It's summoned by equipping the Orb of Mastery and right clicking the orb. When summoned it takes the appearance of an Air pet, but does not render itself invisible.

The pet itself is a beast. It's got massive hit points, very high melee damage, casts the damage shield Aura of Elemental Mastery (50 points of damage), procs thru melee Elemental Mastery Blast (143 points of damage), and casts the DD/stun Elemental Mastery Strike (84 points of damage).

You can summon the Orb of Mastery as early as level 46, but you cannot use Manifest Elements until level 50.
