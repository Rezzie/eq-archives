Bear Hide Armor

 

In the Qeynos Hills, near the Surefall Glade, you can find an NPC named Chanda Miller.

Note : She can often be found near the campfire.

Chanda can make you bear hide armor if you provide her with a bear skin and some coin.

First, Hail Chanda and after it fails tell her, "Your Brother sent me.".

Ask her about her bear hide armor and she will fill you in on details regarding the quality and cash requirements for each piece.

Note : If your faction is not good enough with Chanda Miller, you will have to do each item in order (belt, then boots) before you can receive the Cloak.

Pricing is as follows :

Belt - 5 GP and a Low quality Bear pelt.


Boots - 15 GP and a Medium quality bear pelt.

 

Cloak - 21 GP and High quality bear pelt.
