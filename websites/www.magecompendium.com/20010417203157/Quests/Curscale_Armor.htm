Curscale Armor

 

 

Find the Iksar named Klok Mugrok, in a store in Cabalis named The Haggle Baron. It is near the Court of Pain.

 

Note : I am not sure of the correct spelling of Klok Mugrok.

 

Hail him, and tell him you will perform a small service.

 

He will then give you a pack to fill with 8 scaled cur hides.

 

Note : They are found on Scaled Wolf Curs in The Field of Bone.

 

Once you have filled the pack and combined the 8 hides.

 

Return the pack to Klok Mugrok and he will give you a random piece of curscale armor and a nice amount of experience.
