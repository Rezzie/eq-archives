Mystic Cloak

 

 

This quest starts in  Najena.

 

In the same cell that has the injured Halfling is a High Elf enchanter named Linara Parlone.

 

Hail her and she will tell you that she need a key from a Priest of Najena that is in the Estate of Unrest.

 

Note : The Priest of Najena in Unrest at location +504, +65, on the top floor of the South West tower. He is also a rare spawn and took at least two days real time of camping. He died after three hits for 333 Dmg.

 

Get the Tarnished Bronze Key.

 

Return to the Enchanter and give her the key.

 

Take the note she gives you to her father father.

 

Note : Her father's name is Tolkar Parlone in Felwithe. His location is -60 + 680.

 

Take the Faded Cloak he gives you to the Enchanters guild in Erudin, on the second floor of the Enchanters guild you will find his wife, give her the cloak. 

 

Note : Trilani Parlone is at location  +530 +730.

 

You will then get the Mystic Cloak.

 

Note : All the quest components are no drop including the cloak so once you get the key you have to do the quest or waste it.
