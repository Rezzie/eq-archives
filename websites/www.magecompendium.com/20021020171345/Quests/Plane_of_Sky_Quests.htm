Plane of Sky Quests

 

 

Note : Most quest items are no drop, and are not no rent.

Information obtained form the EverQuest Casters Realm quest pages and reader submissions.

 

 

There are six quests in the Plane of Sky, and from what I understand about them they are very different from normal quests. First you have to undertake a test, then obtain several items dropped by monsters throughout the zone. Below are list the six quests the items required and the item obtained from that quest.

 

Note : The only exit from the quest room is by jumping off island one and zoning into East Freeport.
