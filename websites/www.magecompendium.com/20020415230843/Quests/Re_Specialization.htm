Skill Re-Specialization

 

 

This quest is located in the Temple of Solusek Ro.

 

Talk to Ostorm and he will offer to change your specialization if you give him a Ruby and 50 gold pieces. 

 

Note : All your specialized skills will be reset back to a skill level of 49, make sure the skill you want to improve beyond 50 is the first one to pass a skill level of 50.
