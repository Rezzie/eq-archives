Kerran Fishing Pole

 

 

This quest begins on Kerra Island by speaking to Feren.

 

Feren on Kerra Island is seeking someone who can kill a Razortooth.

 

They can be found in the water surrounding Kerra Island.

 

Kill them until you loot a tooth.

 

Take it back to Feren and you will be rewarded with the Kerran Fishing Pole
