A Quick Guide to Research

 

 

1. Practice research is a waste of time.  Unless you wasted huge numbers of practice points at your guild, you should easily be able to train 5 points in Research at every level.  This will cost a lot of money later on, but no more than it would cost to acquire all the practice components (not to mention the time it takes to run all over the world getting the components, or the fact that combining practice runes will NOT always give you an improvement in Research).

 

2. Don't initially train research until you HIT level 24.  There are no research-only spells below level 24.


3. The research cap is equal to (Level - 15) * 5.  At Level 24, your cap is (24 - 15) = 9 * 5 = 45.


4. Once you hit level 24, train at your guild up to your cap of 45 before attempting any of your level 24 research-only spells.


5. Do not waste time trying to research spells that you can purchase.


6. For the research-only spells, there are only SEVEN TYPES OF WORDS that are used.  They are: Bondage, Coercion, Convocation, Dimension, Duress, Incarceration, and Transcendence.


7. Don't bother doing research on a spell if you can't use it yet.  i.e. Don't research your level 34 research-only spells until you are at LEAST level 34.


8. Leave all your research crap in the bank.  This includes your Elemental Grimoire, words, components, etc.

9. The Elemental Grimoire is the book you need to do research with.  Put the components in the book and click Combine.  If you succeed, you get a spell in your pointer.  Scribe it immediately (unless you foolishly tried to research a spell below the level you can use it at :).  The Grimoire is purchasable from any mage guild for around 11pp.  Don't bother buying it until level 24.


10. Like I said before, practicing research at your guild trainer can get mighty expensive later on.  At a skill of 135, it costs around 20pp to train ONE POINT.  And you have to train five points per level!  However, when your skill is 135, you will be level 42 or so, and you should be earning FAR more than enough money to support the costs.  Every time I've spent at least 2 hours hunting Mountain Giants at the fort in the Frontier Mountains, I've made at least 50pp per trip, and I'm only level 32.  At higher levels, you might need to earn 500pp to buy five practice points, but that's a night's work in some places, and you'll take far longer than one night to level.
