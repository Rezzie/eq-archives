Jewel Craft

 

All components listed below should be combined in a Jeweler's Kit.

Note : To aid you in your chosen craft there is also a Guide to Jewel Craft available in the same shops as the Jewelers Kit.

They can be found in basically every town across Norrath, but some of the gems, however cannot.

You may need to slay some high level creatures to obtain the rarer gems, but there are a few available in remote shops :

Western Commonlands, the hut near the entrance to Befallen is one.
