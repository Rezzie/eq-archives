Lake of Ill Omen
Location: To the southwest of the city of Cabilis, in the center of Kunark Adjacent Zones: West Cabilis, Firiona Vie, Frontier Mountains, Warsliks Woods, Veksar
Level of Monsters:1-30+
Decaying Skeletons, Skeletons, Scalebone Skeletons, Greater Skeletons, Lesser Icebone Skeletons, Lesser Charbone Skeletons, Charbone Skeletons, Goblin Whelp, Goblin Scout, Goblin Watcher, Goblin Outrider, Goblin Hunter, Goblin Brawler, Goblin Skirmisher, Goblin Spirit Caller, Goblin Soothsayer, Goblin Agressor, Goblin Warrior, Goblin Warlord, Bloodgill Goblin, Sabretooth Kitten, Sabretooth Cub, Sabretooth Kit, Sabretooth Cat, Sabretooth Grimalkin, Sabretooth Tigress, Sabretooth Tiger, Sarnak Hatchling, Sarnak Broodling, Sarnak Recruit, Sarnak Legionnaire, Sarnak Adherant, Iksar Footpad, Iksar Bandit, Iksar Brigand, Iksar Manslayer, Iksar Marauder, Iksar Exile, Iksar Pariah, Deepwater Barracuda

Description:  
A huge zone with a mix of creatures ranging from newbie decaying skeletons and such up to very tough goblins.  At the bottom of the lake is the entrance to the ruined city of Varnek, a high level dungeon.  There is a huge fortress of Sarnak in the southwest corner and a tribe of goblins called the Brood of Kotiz in the north.  These two don't like each other.  There are also plenty of undead and wandering cats to play with.
