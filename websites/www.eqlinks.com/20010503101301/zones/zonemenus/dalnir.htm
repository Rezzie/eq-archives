Dalnir
Location:Located hidden with Warsliks Woods Adjacent Zones: Warsliks Woods  
Level of Monsters: 25-35+
 		 Ravenous Nibbler, Coerced Erudites, Iksar, Dwarves, Tier'Dal, Gnomes, Smoldering Goo, Gyrating Goo, Kly Acolyte, Kly Believer, Kly Invoker  

Description:
   The location to the cave entrance is in the nw corner of Warslik's Woods up the hill from some water.  Once you enter into the cave tunnel, it splits off a couple of times.  The tunnel that leads to the zone is filled with illusonary water, so you can just walk through it.  This is a huge maze, consisting of several levels.  There are notes to be found in various places in the zone that seem to indicate one huge quest involving the zone.  There are also some very nice bard items to be found here.  The second level is full of pit traps, so levitate is suggested
