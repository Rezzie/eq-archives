Warslik's Woods
Location: To the northeast of the city of Cabilis, in the heart of Kunark Adjacent Zones: West Cabilis, Field of Bone, Lake of Ill Omen, The Overthere, Crypt of Dalnir
   
    

Level of Monsters:1-30+
Decaying Skeleton, Goblin Scout, Goblin Watcher, Goblin Hextracer, Goblin Hunter, Goblin Outrider, Goblin Witchdoctor, Goblin Bonecaster, Goblin Aggressor, Goblin Skirmisher, Goblin Bloodtracer, Goblin Brawler, Goblin Soothsayer, Goblin Spirit Caller, Goblin Warlord, Skulking Runtling, Iksar Footpad, Iksar Bandit, Iksar Brigand, Iksar Marauder, Iksar Manslayer, Iksar Pariah, Pygmy Skulking Brute, Skulking Brute, Forest Giant Sapling, Forest Giant Greenwood, Forest Giant Evergreen

Description:
An enormous forested zone, this is home to the Forest Giants and a number of other creatures of varying levels.  Within the forest are goblin and a giant fortresses to hunt.  Most creatures in the zone are in established camps, but there are some wandering ones as well.
