Upper Guk
Location: In the center of the continent of Antonica Adjacent Zones: Innothule Swamp, Lower Guk Level of Mobs: 6-25
     

Types of Mob:
Frogloks of all types, including Tads, Guards, Sentries at the front end, then to more advanced frogloks, including Warriors, Necromancers, Magicians, of many different varieties, Swamp Alligators, Heart Spiders, Giant Heart Spider
