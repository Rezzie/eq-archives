The Dreadlands
Location:A blasted frozen crater on the southwest of the continent of Kunark   Adjacent Zones: Burning Woods
Firiona Vie
Frontier Mountains
Level Range:  35 - 50

  		Mountain Giants, Dracnids, Plaguebone Skeletons, Sarnaks  

Description:
The Druid and Wizard gate spells take you to this zone.  If you are lower than level 30 or so, you may be better off taking the boat to Firiona Vie than teleporting here, as you can run into some nasty creatures pretty easily.  The zone is home to quite a bit of Mountain Giants.  In the center of the zone is a castle full of Sarnaks.  There are also a lot of wandering Mobs to contend with.
