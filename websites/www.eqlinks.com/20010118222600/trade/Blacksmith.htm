Blacksmithing

The ability to shape ore into valuable metal items. These items are of better weight and utility than those sold in stores.

Examples are Lockpicks, Muffin Tins, and Iron Boots.

Requires: Ore and Forge

Additional info:

  • Small sized armor - Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings, Wood Elves, High elves, Dark elves.
  • Medium sized armor - Humans, Half Elves, High elves, Wood elves, Dark elves, Barbarians, Erudite
  • Large sized armor - Troll, Ogre, Barbarian
