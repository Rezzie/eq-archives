EQ'Lizer's

Reference Guide

Alcohol Tolerance 1 200 How much alcohol you can drink without feeling the effects of it.  (blurred vision etc...)
Baking 1 200 A trade skill used to bake food.
Brewing 1 200 A trade skill used for making alcoholic beverages, and consequently, lots of friends.  New recipes have been added to get your skill all the way to 200 now.
Fishing 1 200 How well you fish, considered to be a trade skill.
Fletching 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning bow and arrow components into bows and arrows.
Jewel Craft 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning gems and precious metals into jewelry, sometimes even magic jewelery.
Pottery 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pots (amongst other things) out of clay.
Smithing 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning ore into armor and other metal items.
Tailoring 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pelts into types of leather armor.
