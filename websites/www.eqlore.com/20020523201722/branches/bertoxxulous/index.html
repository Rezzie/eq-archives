Welcome to the Seekers of Lore, Bertoxxulous Branch. We are a guild dedicated to searching out all the unknown, to finding that lost tome, or forgotten sword, and to having a good time. Together we can take on any task that is thrown our way. We like to share information and group and help one another accomplish that quest, slay a mythical beast or get that exciting new item. Together the Seekers of Lore have been striding the plains of Norrath since the beginning. We are one of the oldest guilds on Bertoxxulous now, with members old and new. Come, join us in the search!
