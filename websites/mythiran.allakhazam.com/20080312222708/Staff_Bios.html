About the Site

Originally started as an information site for the game Everquest, Allakhazam.com now covers all of the major Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games.  We currently do over 5 million page views a day, have over half a million registered users, and reach millions of online gaming enthusiasts every month.  We run on 35 dedicated servers and use up to 50 mb/sec of bandwidth.  Players rely upon us to give them the most complete and accurate information possible to help them get an edge in their game play.

  • SOE Worlds Magazine has called us "The Unbeatable Allakhazam.com", describing us at "one of the most thorough and up-to-date site[s] on the internet for players who are searching for information about quests, in-game items, or NPC's."
-- Spring 2005, SOE Worlds Magazine
  • Baseball Pitcher Curt Schilling has listed Allakhazam.com as his favorite site on the internet.
-- ESPN, the Magazine 4/15/02
  • DMOZ internet guide has Allakhazam.com sites listed as highly recommended for Everquest, Final Fantasy XI and World of Warcraft.
