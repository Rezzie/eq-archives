Zu Online Mourns Earthquake Victims

Posted By amandasaurus, 2008-05-15 19:16:34 for Zu Online
In the wake of a devestating tragedy, people all over the world are coming together to acknowledge the victims. The Zu Online community joins in the tributes with a prayer and memorial event.

This week, a devastating earthquake hit southwest China’s Sichuan Province. The death toll continues to rise with over 12000 confirmed dead and almost 10000 people thought to be still trapped in the rubble. 

As a show of support for those victims of this disaster, players in Zu Online spontaneously came together to mourn the deceased and extend prayers to their families and those still fighting to survive. You can watch a video of this special event here.
