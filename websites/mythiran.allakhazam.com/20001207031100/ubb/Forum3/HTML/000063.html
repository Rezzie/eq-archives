I'm afraid many of the research components are very random in location. I recommend trying to:

1) /auction for it, offering to trade as well. Sometimes other caster classes will happily do a straight trade for their components.

2) Look carefully at the bottom of vendors' inventories, particularly in/near zones with level 34+ beasties (HighKeep, etc). Druid ring vendors are a good source, too. One trick is to buy cheapy player sold stuff like fishing grubs or whatever, then re-access the vendor to see what else pops up from the unseen inventory.

3) Join a guild. Many times the higher levels will be happy to help you out and have the components already collected.

Good luck.
