The rawhide armour is very easy to get. It requires a tailor, but also requires a low skill in tailoring. All you need is a ruined pelt (any type of animal but it has to be ruined) and then combine it with a pattern in a sewing kit. Any sewing kit will do. If you don't need any specific type of rawhide armour, I would just buy a simple pattern (wrist or belt) and then hunt wolves or bears or cats. That will give you your rawhide armour. Hope it helps.

The Only place you really need to go for both components is to Eruds Crossing. On whisp island, get off the both, and then run to the other side of the island where the sunken boat is. Swim into the water, and go towards the right end of the boat and you will find 3 zombie sailors. Zombie sailors have a chance of dropping both zombie skins, and raw hide. In a 3 hours period from these guys i remeber getting 40 Zombie skins and about 20 pieces of raw hide armor. Other Places for Raw Hide are the Orcs in Oasis.
