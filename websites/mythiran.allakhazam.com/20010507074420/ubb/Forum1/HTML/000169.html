Let me qualify this with the fact I do not know at what skill level, various spells are "trivial".

This most likely works like trade skills. To have even a slight chance to succed, you would need to have your research skill maxed at the level of spell you are making. A total _rough_ guess:
20% chance at entry spell circle level.
50% chance at next spell circle skill level.
80% chance at +2 spell circle level.

So, take a level 24 spell. With research skill maxed at level 24 cap:
- you have a 20% chance to make a level 24 spell.
- you have a 50% chance to make a level 20 spell.
- you have a 80% chance to make a level 16 spell.

I'm sure this in not accurate, only gives you an idea. Also note that spell research starts lower and stays lower than most skills.
Most are (lvl X 5) + 5 = skill cap.
Research is (lvl X 5) - 65 = skill cap.

So, even though one might be 24th level and have research skill "maxed", it is still only a skil of 55, with a 55's sucess rate.
