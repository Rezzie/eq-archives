To work out your max research skill, take you level and subtract 15, and multiply by 5.
So for level 24, this is (25 - 15) * 5, or 45. In this case you should be using Training Runes.

Anon

Well Froglock Eyes come from any frogloks in Upper and Lower Guk. Froglok Tad Eyes will not work. The Froglok Eyes are Random loot off any of the higher level frogs.

When I completed the 2nd Tier Runes, I used to following strategy.

1) Check every vendor in North Freeport, and then every vendor from there to Innothule Swamp. I did this about once every 10 days. Sometime I got lucky and would get 10-15, other times 1.

2) Buy them. Use your trusty SHOUT or OOC to buy them.

3) Kill them yourself. First warning here - if your sensitive about factions, then this isnt the way for you. Buy killing the frogs yourself, you become KOS Very quickly Live Frogs in Upper, as well as the entire Live Side in Lower Guk. That being Said, When I was harvesting the eyes, I was often walking out of Upper Guk with 100+pp aqfter a few hours and about 4 back packs full of FS Weapons.

4) Make friends with some higher level people who frequent Upper Guk, and Lower Guk on Live Side, and ask them to save the eyes for you.

So thats the strategy I took, and after about 4 weeks playing 4 hours 4 days a week, I had acquired about 45 eyes, and had trivialled the 2nd Tier.

Looking back now, I probably should have started an Troll Mule, and used it to check all the vendors in Grobb, as its a close town to Guk.

Hope that helps.
