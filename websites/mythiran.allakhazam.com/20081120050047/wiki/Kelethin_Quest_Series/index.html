Kelethin Quest Series

Quest Series
 
Kelethin Quest Series
Starting ZoneGreater Faydark
Expansion Echoes of Faydwer
Recommended Levels1 to 10
NextGreater Faydark Quest Series

The following are quest series that take place in the Nursery and Kelethin vicinity in Greater Faydark. They are aimed at characters of levels 1-10, and they flow naturally into the Greater Faydark Quest Series at level 10.

  • Note: Evil characters will find that most of these quests are inaccessible without faction work. See the Citizenship series for repeatable quests to raise your The City of Kelethin. However, the faction quests are all level 20, making them undoable at the level for the rest of this series, so Evil characters are best off just staying home.
