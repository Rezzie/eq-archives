SOE Station Launcher Update Nov 24 - Beta 1.0.4

Posted By Calthine, 2008-11-23 00:42:50 for General News

Dan Kinney posted on the Station Forums:

We will be deploying the next Station Launcher beta update on Monday, November 24, 2008.  This deployment is primarily focused on providing a fix for a crash bug in the application, first reported by NeillM.

This deployment will happen around 10:00 am PDT, after which you will notice your application updating the next time it starts.  There may be a short outage during the transition period, which should not last more than 30 minutes.  This topic will be updated just before and immediately after the maintenance period.
