I am up to skill 55 in reserach, all though the practice research quests. The froglock eyes drop in UGuk of Tuk or stronger Froglocks (that means no generic froglocks, they must have a clan name). If you are around 20 a good place to hunt is the room just before the the ramp room (squire's room). There are 4 spawns here, 2 wimpies and 2 Tuks and there is a safe alcove to rest in. If you are a little stronger, you can camp the squire room and maybe get yourself a runed bone fork also. I camped the scryer room for an hour yesterday for his braclet and one of his PHs dropped an eye.
