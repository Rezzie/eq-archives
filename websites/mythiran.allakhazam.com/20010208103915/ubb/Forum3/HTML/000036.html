I found these words Recluse, Absorption, Possession, and Allure off of the Named orcs in Kithicor. They sometimes dropped off of the Chief shralock..or something close to that. If you come out of the valley to HHpass, you take a right and follow the wall until it turns...there you will find the orcs. They spawn in a group of four, sometimes they have placeholders of a bear, wolf, giant bat, and fire beetle.
If you can find the words of Detachment, and Sentient (Beza) would appreciate an E-mail or response.
