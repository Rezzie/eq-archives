Hey guys

All Components for the Concordance Runes do exist. I have personally gone from 0 - 163 in skill using them.

In any case to your questions

Giant Batwings - Go to Misty Thicket. Go through the hole in the wall, and then turn left 45 degrees, and head upto the top of the hill. A lot of Giant Bats Pop there. Also, you can get the Giant Bat Wings in Sol B (aka Nagafens Lair), Erud's Crossing off the Vampire Bats, probably Unrest as well, and also from Dragon Necropolis. There used to be Giant Bats in Butcherblock as well, but I never seen any there

Giant Scarab Eyes - Well this took a *little* while, but if you kill scarabs in North Ro, and then the 2 Types of scarabs in Oasis, and then the Scarabs in South Ro, they have the eyes. West and East Commons also have the Scarabs.

Suggestion is to also check most of the zone vendors in North Ro (North end of zoone), Oasis (Gypsies), and South Ro (the DE Merchants... just get someone to check for you) as these are not very expensive, and people at lower levels usually sell them, as they give decent money at that level

Hope that helps
