Staff Bios  

Originally started as Allakhazam, an information site for the game EverQuest, ZAM now covers all of the major Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games. We currently do over 15 million page views a day, have nearly a million and a half registered users, and reach millions of online gaming enthusiasts every month. Players rely upon us to give them the most complete and accurate information possible to help them get an edge in their game play.

  • Best Website for Gaming News and Information, 2007 Readers Choice Awards: "Allakhazam.com, known for extensive item and quest guides for numerous MMOs takes home this award with 33% of the votes."
-- Beckett MASSIVE ONLINE GAMER issue #11

  • SOE Worlds Magazine has called us "The Unbeatable Allakhazam.com", describing us as "one of the most thorough and up-to-date sites on the internet for players who are searching for information about quests, in-game items, or NPC's."
-- Spring 2005, SOE Worlds Magazine

  • Baseball Pitcher Curt Schilling has listed Allakhazam.com as his favorite site on the internet.
-- ESPN, the Magazine April 15, 2002
