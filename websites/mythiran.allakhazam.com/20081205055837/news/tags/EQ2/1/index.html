Housing Zone Issues

Posted By Calthine, 2008-12-02 10:55:41 for EverQuest II

Locked out of your house?  Kiara posted:

Any zone with housing or with access to housing may not be loading at this time (eg. South or North Qeynos, any of the city villages, etc).  Antonica and the Commonlands should not be affected as they lead to Guildhalls.

The zoning problem should be all fixed now.

Please note that if you have a character who hasn't zoned into their house in six months, they will not be able to return home until tomorrow

EQII In-Game Survey

Posted By Calthine, 2008-11-26 13:05:39 for EverQuest II

SOE has implimented an in-game survey.  Kiara tells us:

Your experience with Everquest II is very important to us and we would greatly appreciate your feedback. As such, we’ve implemented live surveys in-game to hear what you have to say about EQII. To access the survey, log in to EQII and use the /survey command in-game. Your responses will help us enhance gameplay, which we hope will provide you with a more enjoyable EQII in the future.
Your responses will be kept confidential and will be used solely for the purpose of helping to improve SOE’s products and services. Participation through the Survey Analytics site is governed by Survey Analytics’ Privacy Policy.

Thank you for your time – we appreciate your feedback.

source:  EQII Official Forums
