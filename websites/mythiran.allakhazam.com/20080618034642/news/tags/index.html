Wonderland Online: One Dollar Control!

Posted By tovin, 2008-06-17 14:12:50 for Wonderland Online
All of us have been leery of buying items from an ingame mall before, so if you haven't done it yet in Wonderland Online, this is your chance, and there's no excuse not to!

In return for players’ constant support as well as to offer more players the chance to experience the wonderful items in the Wonderland Online Item Mall, the Wonderland Online (http://wl.igg.com/) team will sell the Electron Control at a huge discount from June 17th to June 23rd (GMT-5). During that period, players can buy an Electron Control for only 1 dollar (18 Item Mall points)!

Compared with the Remote Control, the Electron Control has many more advanced functions.
1.       Perfect Design—Both the character and the pet’s skills can be set.
2.       More choices—Two physical skills or magic skills can be set by default. Players can also change the skills in accordance with their requirements.
3.       More convenience—The skills for the first three bouts can be set and the assistant skills are also available. Auto controls replace human controls.

Read on to find even more reasons why the control is worth not passing up for only a buck!

WoW TCG Event: Ogre Smash & Bash

Posted By tovin, 2008-06-17 14:09:22 for World of Warcraft
June 28th hobby stores everywhere will host the Two-Headed Ogre Smash & Bash WoW TCG team tournament.

Two-Headed Ogre Sealed Pack—Each team will receive 8 booster packs from the March of the Legion block, from which they will need to construct two 30-card-minimum decks. Players may work together during deck construction and while playing their matches, meaning that this is a great opportunity to bring a friend along that’s new to the World of Warcraft TCG!

Prizes

Every player that attends the Two-Headed Ogre Smash & Bash will receive a code redeemable for a Fireworks Trinket that they can use on their favorite online character! After playing in the event, participants will be e-mailed the code that they can take to Blizzard’s Promotion Redemption site to redeem for their character.

 Both members of the duo that comes out on top when the dust settles will be able to show Azeroth their accomplishment with highly sought after Ogre Trinket that allows any online character to turn into an Ogre. Previously only available as one of the high-end items up for grabs on the UDE Points store, the Ogre Suit not only makes you look cool, but it allows you to do the Ogre dance!

Entries for the U.S. contest is $40, and you can find a participating store near you by using this nifty Upper Deck page!
