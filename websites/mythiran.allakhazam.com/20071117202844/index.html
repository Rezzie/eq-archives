Kickin' it Grab Bag Style

Posted By Maia, 2007-11-16 22:42:46 for Dark Age of Camelot
Grab Bag time! Check out this weeks edition:
Q. I have a question about Heretic spell piercing buff. Does it stack with the 10 % passive spell piercing from artifacts like Atlantis Tablet 5 % piercing and Jacinas sash?

A. Spell piercing is a hot topic lately. I went to the Balancinator who offered this reply: No, it will not let you exceed 10% spell piercing. It may not take you over 10% spell piercing, but it does open up alternative templating options. If you were to spec high in Enhancements and utilize the piercing buff spell, it would allow you to template out of one or both of the items, granting you more freedom while templating. The options are endless! Well, sort of.

Fan Festival Podcast, Day1

Posted By Exodus, 2007-11-16 19:09:03 for Final Fantasy XI
We SO wanted to have done this live, but due to one technical issue after the next, it just truly wasn't meant to be. Instead, we decided to just go directly to a podcast instead of going on air. I'm not sure if we'll go live tomorrow, but at least you get a taste of Fan Festival.

From our booth we set up two towers and a couple of laptops. There, people were able to use the towers to log into Allakhazam to not only post pictures and post on the forums, but to use us to finish the Live Quest (which from what I know, grants you three BCNM orbs in-game; Heros, Heroine, and Mercenary Combat.)

I ran around doing interviews from a few people at the booth! That was fun. The other juicy tidbit is that Pikko, IN, and I talk about the 30 minute interview we had with the devs briefly. Pikko is still compiling the transcript for the whole ordeal, but you will pretty much hear the the specifics in the podcast.

You can download the podcast at onlinegamingradio.com and clicking on Podcast. I'll also go ahead and post a direct link here.

Hope you enjoy!
