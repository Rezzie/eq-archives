Hot Fix Notes

Posted By Maia, 2008-04-17 17:14:52 for Dark Age of Camelot
Here are the latest hot fixes to hit the live servers:
- Thanes will once again be awarded the ability to utilize Large Shields at level 10.  This fixes an issue where any new Thanes would not receive the Large Shield ability upon hitting level 10.  Note: This doesn’t fix any Thanes created and leveled past level 10 since the last hotfix that are missing the Large Shield ability.  A fix for those characters will be forthcoming.

- The bounty quest for Shiny Shells available in the Tutorial zones can now be completed by players up to level 10.  This increases the level limit to allow more characters time to gather shells for the bounty quest before it becomes ineligible.
 
- [Dye Another Day] – This quest is now given and completed by the NPC Maidh.

- The Armor Redemption Token item given at the end of the some of the higher level content in the tutorial zone, now has information on it regarding how and who to turn the token into for a reward.

SOE Fan Faire Announced!

Posted By Calthine, 2008-04-17 15:01:00 for General News
Fan Faire Returns to the Las Vegas Hilton, August 14 - 17, 2008!


If you've never been to an SOE Fan Faire before, you've been missing out! Fan Faire is a great place for SOE gamers to mix and mingle with each other and meet some of the faces behind their favorite SOE games. Here's just a taste of some of the great stuff we're planning:

  • In-game tournaments
  • Live, game-themed tournaments and quests
  • A costume contest
  • Lots of social activities, including a Kickoff Reception, a Grand Banquet and a VIP Party with live entertainment
  • Sneak peaks at SOE titles currently in development
  • The 2nd Annual Community Address from SOE President John Smedley
  • Developer forums and roundtables

 
And that's just the beginning! No matter which SOE game you like to play, Fan Faire will be a blast! Even if you're not an SOE gamer, we encourage you to join us and bring your friends!

Passes will go on sale later this Spring, but you can book your hotel stay now! The Las Vegas Hilton is currently accepting reservations at the discounted Fan Faire rate of just $139 per night. Just call (800) 635-7711 and tell them you're with Sony Online Entertainment, booking code SSOE8 or click here to make online reservations. Space is limited - so get your room soon, in case they sell out!

Hope to see you in Las Vegas!

Watch for passes to go on sale and other Fan Faire announcements on the Fan Faire site.
