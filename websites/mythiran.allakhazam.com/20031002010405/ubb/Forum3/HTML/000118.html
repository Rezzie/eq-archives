Ahh Yes Words of Detachment, Allure, Absorption, and Sentient (Beza) are all fairly Rare. Since they are level 24 spells the words always drop off of Level 24 (and higher) mobs which makes High Hold Keep one for the few old world zones with imbeded vendors, that has enough hunting in it to produce cache's of these words on the vendors. To find rare words I concentrate on old world (original EQ) zones, they just don't seem to drop as often in new world zones. And I have made thousands from selling these spells on my server.
