Gathering Tempest World Event Rewards

Game Update #46
Gathering Tempest
Live: June 17, 2008
  • Good players take your Concordium Note of Appreciation to Mage Fulcrum Gearshift at the Concordium Tower in South Qeynos (red teleporter).
  • Evil players take your Academy of Arcane Science's Note of Service to Scientist Dally Scrumplecog at the upper floor of the Academy of Arcane Science in North Freeport.
  • The rewards are mainly cool-looking furniture or gear intended for your appearance slots, but there are a few decent, usable weapons and some fun thrown in!
