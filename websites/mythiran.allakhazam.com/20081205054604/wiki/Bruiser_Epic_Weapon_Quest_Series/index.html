Bruiser Epic Weapon Quest Series

This quest series is for the level 80 Bruiser-only Epic Weapon.

  • Pre-requisites:
    • Command of the Oggish language is required to start or complete this quest.
  • Hint:
  • Starter:
    1. Go to Maj'Dul, to The Golden Scepter, outside on the west side talk to Alim Gulan of the Horizon at 122, 140, -122. To hear his story, pay him 2 
    2. Go to South Freeport, by the door to West Freeport, and hail a Juggernaut taskmaster ( 70, -17, 126 ). You need to speak Oggish to talk to this guy (which can be bought in Big Bend from Papilius Ahala).
    3. Go to the docks in South Freeport and hail a roughhouser champion at -268, -56, 202
    4. collect the gambling chips from the bars:
      • 3 in West Freeport:
        • The Blood Haze Inn, at 217.42, 3.84, 151.60
        • The bar next to the Blood Haze Inn, at 297.18, -1.88, 84.54
        • [[eq2 zone:The Coalition of Tradesfolke, at 32.41, -11.10, 97.28
      • 1 in South Freeport, in a bar at -211.79, -55.79, 151.21
      • 1 in North Freeport, inside The Jade Tiger's Den, at -79.47, -10.37, -67.93
      • 1 in East Freeport, inside the Seafarer's Roost, on a table upstairs at -233.27 -47.91 13.09, 0, 
    5. Now hail a Juggernaut taskmaster again to begin the quests
  • Heroic Quests:
    1. The Fist of War
    2. The Broken Fist
  • Fabled Reward: Gorynn's Fist
  • Epic Quests:
    1. A Taste of Past Glory
  • Mythical Reward: Gorynn's Fist
