Butcherblock Mountains Quest Series

Quest Series
 
Butcherblock Mountains Quest Series
Starting ZoneButcherblock Mountains
Expansion Echoes of Faydwer
Recommended Levels20 to 35
PreviousThundering Steppes

NextSteamfont Mountains

The following are quests that take place in or around Butcherblock Mountains. The Solo Quests are aimed at characters of levels 20 to 35, with a Heroic Quest series from 60 to 70.

  • NOTE: The Dockmaster Waulon series can be skipped, starting directly with Brulten Hiltstill, if you are coming from Greater Faydark and wish to get started immediately. You can still pick up the Dockmaster Waulon quests later when Jonedorn Kilnkor sends you to the docks for Axing a Favor.

Factions

The Irontoe Brigade

This is actually the only faction you need to get in this zone. There is, however, no faction merchant and the mender, Mender Horon Bronzethumb, at West Fort Irontoe does not require faction tomend your armor. However, you will need +10,000 with The Irontoe Brigade in order to buy the spool of dwarven wire from him, required by the Heritage Quest, Casualties of the War of the Fay.
