Greetings fellow Magicians!!
I just wanted to post some findings so everyone can share in the rewards.
I have found that words of Duress (great for using to practice with) drop a lot from the Yetis in the Dreadlands (Kunark). I have also found that Gloves of Rallos Zek drop often in Eastern Wastes (Velious). Essence of Rathe and Tears of Prexus (lvl 44 & 49 pet components) dropped in Burning Woods off of giants at the fort(Kunark). I also got some Words of Bondage that were found in Sebilis (Kunark lvl 50+ zone). I have been told that component drops are normally found in zones that are either equal to or higher than the lvl of spell you are looking for. I don't quite belive this totally since I have found several copies of Words of Duress in the yeti caves in Dreadlands and that zone is MUCH higher I believe than the spell you need the words for. Though I do believe that the lvl 44 and 49 components are only found in zones that are equal to that or greater. I hope that this helps you all. Please post any other places where you have found any high lvl components.
