Angels Online: Warming-Up for the New Expansion

Posted By amandasaurus, 2008-08-26 13:40:22 for Angels Online

The upcoming expansion for Angels Online , Lost Atlantis, is almost here. To get you ready, the GMs have cooked up a little pre-expansion warm up event. During the period between August 28th and September 1st, tems that have2x exp effects will be enhanced to 3x exp. In addition, a new training ground, Flame Arena, will be available to all players to help them gain strength and level up to defeat Evil Lyceum!

1. From August 28th to the server maintenance on September 1st, all items with the double exp effect such as Double Exp Cards, Double Skill Exp Cards, and Pet's Double Exp Cards will deliver 3X exp effect.

2. All items with speedup effects such as Collecting Speedup Cards and Facture Speedup Cards will offer players quicker effects.

3. From August 28th to the server maintenance on September 1st, players can obtain a Flame Area Ticket if they buy one Lucky Bag. With the ticket, players can go to find the Guide Angel in the Angel Lyceum (157, 78) and enter the [Flame Area] for 24 hours.

4. From August 28th to September 1st, the upper limit of exp pet's Exp Stones can deposit will be double.

Guild Highlights: Keeps

Posted By Berek, 2008-08-25 16:19:13 for Warhammer Online

In Warhammer Online, Keeps are one of the main components in RvR battles.  Just one of the many benefits of controlling a Keep is additional over-time experience your guild gains.  If a Keep is under siege, you'll also see a convenient warning letting you know.

In WAR, guilds play an integral part in claiming keeps for their side. Not only can you fight together with your banner held high, but those banners can be used to benefit everyone in the area in keep defense once you’ve claimed the keep as your own. Not only that, but your guild will gain certain benefits and advantages just for owning a keep. Imagine your colors flying high for the masses to see. Stir passion in your allies and instill fear into the hearts of your enemies as they come to recognize your colors!
