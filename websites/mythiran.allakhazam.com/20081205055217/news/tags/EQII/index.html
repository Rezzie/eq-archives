Clearing the Bonus XP Confusion

Posted By Calthine, 2008-08-27 18:11:02 for EverQuest II

Did you get Bonus XP?  How much?  Did that potion stack?  Kirstie, EverQuest II Associate Producer, posted this today on the official forums :

In what may fall into those "Wow they said they'd never do that" type of moments for EQII, we've made a change to hopefully alieviate almost all of the questions and uncertainty about bonus xp weekends.

In the hotfix I just scheduled for tomorrow morning there is a change to how we display you experience bar. Specifically, Autenil has added a display for the percentage of bonus experience your are gaining from potions and server sidemodifiers to the tooltip that pops up if you hover over the experience bar.

Min/Max'ers everywhere rejoice!
