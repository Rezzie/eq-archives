It's summer time! Celebrate with a new event from Elf Online.

Note: Players who are already in the Lvl 30 + range will not receive a reward. They must reach Lvl 40 in order to obtain a prize. Lvl 40, must also reach the next tier in order to receive prizes

Scholar Series Job Build

Posted By Tamat, 2008-05-08 15:47:41 for Elf Online
Play Elf Online? Want to know the proper build for a scholar? Check out the following release from the Elf Online staff!

So it seems to me that people have been asking on a consistent basis about the proper build for a scholar. Generally speaking, it really depends on what you will be choosing as your second job. Currently, although I only have experience in buildiing the stats for a doctor, I will try to explain a short summary of the other 2 jobs as well: Poet and Alchemist.

First off, it is very important that each of the 3 jobs has enough stamina to meet the requirements for wearing dream, hero and legendary armor. You can easily check by going to the NPC in Dragon City that exchanges armor for gold powder and hover over the correct respective armor which for a scholar, should be leather. After meeting the stamina requirements, the builds start to branch off a bit.

Doctor

Since your main job will be designated as the healer vs. an attacker, it very important to have the correct balance of mp and hp. The AOE (area of effect) heals and single heals can start to become quite pricey at higher levels and therefore, a substantial amount of mp is needed. However, your most important job is to keep yourself from dying, therefore as important as MP can be, a higher level of HP can become crutcial. I have personally pumped 2 Wis, 3 Stam or 1 Wis, 4 Stam into my doctor in order to ensure that by lvl 60 my HP will be over 4000.

Elf Online Guild Wars

Posted By tovin, 2008-04-23 09:38:24 for Elf Online
Have you ever wondered how guild wars proceed in Elf Online?  Today's press release from them shows you!
Even if your guild only has low lvl members or weak members it's alright, a guild like that can still win the war. First of all, you make a few teams, maybe 10 -15 team should be enough if the members are weak, then tell them all to go to the town you want to win and wait for your enemies while you are hiding your teams.

When the war starts, first get your weakest team to fight them and of course that team will  lose, then the 2nd team will go and fight them and make sure try not to let them have a chance to heal after each battle. After the 2nd team fights if the enemy is still there use the 3rd team and continue until you win. Using this method I can say you will win for sure.
