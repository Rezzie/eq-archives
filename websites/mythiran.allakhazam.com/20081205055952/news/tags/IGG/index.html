Moonmaiden Screenshots

Posted By Tamat, 2008-12-02 09:15:43 for Zu Online

Here at Allakhazam we do the best we can to give you guys, our community, as much information on every MMORPG on the market. Over the next couple of weeks we're going to be looking at Zu Online, one of the F2P (Free-to-Play) games under the IGG umbrella. We'll give you a glance at the five classes within the game. This week, we're taking a look at the Moonmaiden.

In Zu Online , Jingje, the Grand Master of the Moonmaiden clan, practiced in the South Sea for years. As a result, she left countless treasures in Zin Palace. However, the clan fought among itself for the treasures and the palace was filled with turbulence.

The Zin Twins, disciples of Jingje, made every effort to calm the clan and eventually the Moonmaiden Clan was reformed. However, the peace did not last long. Controlled by Blood Demon, the devil Yuan-Ti attacked Zin Palace. Realizing the danger, the Zin Twins ordered Wushan to lead the other disciples to refuge in the Desert City. Later, the disciples of Moonmaiden will lead all of the clans to return to Zin Palace to fight against the Blood Demon together.

Moonmaiden is quick and skillful in the secondary world. They can gain the initiatives using special magic abilities, such as trapping enemies and turning invisible to sneak up on their enemies.

Celebrate the Season with Wonderland Online Events

Posted By Togikagi, 2008-12-01 06:27:40 for Wonderland Online

It's Dec. 1, which means the holiday season is officially upon us (even though most stores have been selling Christmas merchandise since before Halloween). IGG is celebrating Christmas in style with presents and events in Wonderland Online.

Starting today, players who purchase a card worth 1,000 points or more can get a Christmas Gift Box which contains a Christmas Cap and 3,000 gold. You have until Dec. 10 to purchase the gift, and they'll be given out Dec. 16. Men and women will experience different effects when they wear the cap. All the information can be found here.

Also, a Christmas system event preview will be held in-game from Dec. 13-25. Players who get three different bells from killing 10+ monsters can redeem a sock from Santa Claus in Welling Village that is full of suprise rewards. You can even send Christmas wishes to your friends through this page on the Wonderland Online Web site.

Quadruple Skill Exp Event Offered Tomorrow

Posted By Togikagi, 2008-11-28 11:09:24 for Angels Online

The first ever quadruple experience event is being held in Angels Online tomorrow to celebrate the holiday weekend.

Players will be able to obtain double skill experience during the event, which runs from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. To get the quadruple bonus, all you need to do is use a "Double Skill Exp Card."

In addition, many items are on sale in the Item Mall both today and tomorrow. You can find the entire list here. While people in the U.S. battle their fellow shoppers today as part of Black Friday, you can enjoy the Angels Online sales from the comfort of your computer chair.
