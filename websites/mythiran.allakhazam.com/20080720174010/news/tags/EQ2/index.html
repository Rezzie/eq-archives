New EQII Community Manager

Posted By Calthine, 2008-07-15 15:13:01 for EverQuest II

Have you been wondering "Hey, what happened to Kiara?" In a classic "local girl goes on to great things" story, it was just announced on EQII Players :

Today we welcome Christie Renzetti (Kiara) as the new Community Manager for the EverQuest II community! Kiara has been with EQII since beta, and has been an active member of the fansite community that supports the game. She joins the existing Community Relations team (Amnerys, Gnobrin, and Grimwell) in the support of EQII and the EQII community.

We are very excited to have her with us, and hope you share our excitement in this opportunity to increase our support for the EQII community! Please join us in welcoming her to the team in the forums, where you can learn a bit about her!

Congratulations, Kiara!
