At level 16. Locate your Guild trainer and Train 1 Point in Research (assuming all classes/your class gets research at 16.

Doing that places your skill in research at 16. (or 15, not sure really)

DO NOT attempt to concordanse Practice Rune quests yet.

you are ABOVE your skill cap and cannot learn any more about research until you are Level 19.

I wasted hours trying to do practice runes over and over again. Cussing that my skill never went up

Go ahead and combine words for your level 16 spells, you will have a success rate of 80-90%. Ifr you can live without a spell or two till 19, then wait till then to make them, you may get a skill increse doing the spells at that level.

Once you reach 19. you can work research upto 20 skill ponts
(level-15)*2
(19-15)*2 = 20
DO NOT waste any attempts making spells. Just do the first set of Practice Runes tests until you have a 20 skill.

Research rises very quick, you should not have to make very many runes, less than 10 I would guess.
( Note, always remember that the Practice runes require 5 Ingredients. 3 of which are the components listed. Then there is the Quill and the Blank Parchment.
Also, never combine runes in anything except your classes special book. Never use Concordance to combine the runes.
Quills and Parchemnts are sold by your guilds merchants as well as some herbalists)

At level 20, your skill cap is at 25.
Again do the first set of practice rune tests till your skill is 21, then the runes are trivial to you.

Now you can either choose to combine words for your level 20 spells or do the third set of Practice rune tests to get your research up to 25.
I think skipping the second set of practice runes is a wise move considering that 2 of the components are hard if not impossible to find. " froglok eyes and bixie wings"

Using this method of maxing your research then trying to combine words is good way of making sure that Words/paes/runes are hardly ever get wasted.

Orkonico

the formula for working out the Research Skill Cap is as follows

(Current Level - 15) * 5.

So I'll give a small table of research caps from 16-22.

Level....................Research Cap
16...........................5
17...........................10
18...........................15
19...........................20
20...........................25
21...........................30
22...........................35


So Once you reach level 16, and go to the trainer, and train a single point, your skill in Research goes straight to 16. So if you look at the table above, you see you have a Research skill equivalent to someone at level 19. You can not use Concordance, or Train at the Guild Trainer any further until you reach level 19. This is a common mistake. People often start down the path of Concordnace and say that they created 100 Practice Runes (Azia and Beza) and combined then all without any skill increase. Also, you can not train at your Guild Trainer if your skill is maxed at the level cap.

My biggest suggestion I can give people who want to use concordance is once you have trained your single point at level 16, spend the Levels 16, 17, and 18 collecting components for the Practice and Apprentice Runes, and make the Azia and Beza Runes. Do not combine them into the Completed Runes, as its a waste of time.

So theoretiocally (and this is EQ of Course )You have I'd say 75% chance of creating your level 16 spells with just the base research skill. I can't say with certainty, as I didnt research my level 16 spells until I was level 24. I guess also, if you can hold off a few extra levels, and raise your research skill just that bit higher, you can almost certainly get your lower level spells almost 100% of the time. I have researched all my Wizard spells from 16-44, without single failure, but each creation was trivial. As for researching your level 24 Spells at 16, you would more than likely fail 90%+ of the time.

Bones Answer: Once you have an Azia and Beza rune created from the Concordance of Research Book, you place the runes into your Research Book and Hit Combine. That's it. When you press combine in your Research Book, you get a chance of increasing your Research Skill, as well as a chance of getting back a "Completed Rune". You just sell the Completed Runes for some money.

As for Trivial Lists for Real Spells, Search through the Forums here and I remeber that someone had posted a guide for Trivial Levels for Spells.

Hope that Helps. If you are on Veeshan, and a Wizard and need spells created, let me know. My Skill is at 155 (all increases from Concordance )
