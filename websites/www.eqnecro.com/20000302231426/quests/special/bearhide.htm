Bear-Hide Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Qeynos Hills

Who: Chanda Miller

Walkthrough:

 

1. Go to the campfire near the zone to Surefall Glade and look for Chanda (make sure your faction standing is good enough to approach her)

 

2. Hail Chanda and ask her about Bear-Hide Armor.  She will tell you the price and requirements for each piece she can tailor (Belt, Boots, and Cape)

 

3. To get the Bear-Hide Belt you need the following: 5 GP and a low quality bear pelt

 

4. To get the Bear-Hide Boots you need the following: 15 GP and a medium quality bear pelt

 

5. To get the Bear-Hide Cape you need the following: 21 GP and a high quality bear pelt 

 

6. Return the correct items to Chandra and she will give you the Bear-Hide Armor
