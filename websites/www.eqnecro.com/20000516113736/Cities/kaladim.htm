The City of Kaladim.  Home to the Dwarves.

Dwarves are short, but extremely strong (much more so than Humans) and dexterous. They appear to be small, burly humans, and are never seen without beards and mustaches.

Pro's: Strong, Sense direction skill, Very good stamina and dexterity

Con's: Very small legs

Available Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Rogue, and Warrior
