Boil Blood

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 150

Speed : 4.0

Damage : 27 points of damage per round for a max of 15 rounds

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : The upgrade to Heat Blood, Boil Blood deals damage over time

Comments:

Skyshade - Excellent spell 
Lifebane - Most potent DOT yet. Use it to good effect with things like Cyclops and such. 



Dismiss Undead

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 90

Speed : 3.3

Damage : 162

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Direct damage against undead creatures 

Comments:

Skyshade - I have to go back and test this spell again... 
Lifebane - Any of the anti-undead series of spells are good. This upgrade is still more mana efficient then your other spells against undead.



Dooming Darkness

Skill : Conjuration 

Mana : 120

Speed : 4.0

Damage : 18 points of damage per round for a max of 15 rounds

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : The upgrade to Engulfing Darkness, Dooming Darkness deals damage over time and slows down your opponent

Comments:

Skyshade - Like its predecessor, Dooming Darkness gets resisted quite often. Great spell nonetheless. Very important when going solo
Lifebane - I actually still use engulfing darkness or clinging darkness at level 50. The high resist and slower casting time of Dooming Darkness makes the faster casting and less mana costing spells more desirable. Note: damage is greater, the effect lasts longer, and dooming may be slower then others.  This difference may prove crucial to those without jboots.



Panic the Dead

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 50

Speed : 3.5

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Fears an undead creature

Comments:

Skyshade - Good spell since both fear and Spook the Dead are often resisted by level 30+ mobs
Lifebane - This is similar to Invoke Fear but against undead only. Note it is cheaper then Invoke Fear. Again, I prefer using spook the dead, shorter duration usually but at 10 mana per casting and with the very quick casting time, I find I can get the spell off before getting interrupted more often with same effects. 



Renew Bones

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 125

Speed : 4.5

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Acquisition (azia), Purification, and Incorporeal

Effect : The upgrade to Mend Bones, Renew Bones heals your pet

Comments:

Skyshade - Good to speed up your downtime, but you could live without it
Lifebane - Definitely could be better, but it is still useful for keeping pet alive, especially after fighting something that uses DOT's like Treants who have a habit of killing your pet even after fight is over.



Spirit Tap

Mana : 144

Speed : 4.0

Damage : Drains 127 points at level 29 and maxes at 150 points at level 35

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : The upgrade to Siphon Life, Spirit Tap drains life from target to self.

Comments:

Skyshade - Real good one
Lifebane - As always, slow casting but definitely a necromancer favorite. Get it and heal yourself as needed.



Summon Dead

Skill : Conjuration 

Mana : 290

Speed : 12.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research and purchase

Research Material : Words of Possession, Haunting, and Rupturing

Effect : The level 29 pet spell

Comments:

Skyshade - Can be found either at the Shadow Knight's guild in Oggok or Paineel
Lifebane - This pet spell is similar to all the previous ones. The thing that makes this particular spell noteworthy is that it is the last spell which you can still acquire in shops. All after this require researching! It is also of interest that even the one place that sells this level 29 pet spell technically should not have it! It will likely be removed very soon by Verant.  So if you know where to find it, get it quickly or end up researching this one. On the brighter side, by the time you can cast this pet, expect the following abilities from it: kick attack, bash, 2 swings, and now Dual Wield! Truly a scary skeleton.



Vampiric Curse

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 144

Speed : 4.0

Damage : Drains 11 HP from your target per round for a max of 15 rounds

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Dissemination, Parasitism, and Possession

Effect : Drains life over time, even if your target is dead (as long as you got it off before the mob died)

Comments:

Skyshade - Quite an interesting DOT spell. It doesn't deal a lot of damage, but the fact that it stays on even after your target is dead is very nice. Great to use with Call of Bones/Allure of Death so you can med faster.
Lifebane - Hard to be resisted by your enemies compared to your other spells and the DOT healing is very useful in mana recovery as Skyshade stated. A good DOT since it does no DD damage so less chance of getting aggressive critter on your butt.



Word of Spirit

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 133

Speed : 3.5

Damage : 75 points of damage to each creature within range

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : he upgrade to Word of Shadow, Word of Spirit deals damage to creatures within an area. It works the same way Word of Shadow do, you must be within melee range to the creatures you want to damage. In other words, they
must be hitting you

Comments:

Skyshade - Quite an useless spell since at level 29 you do not want more than one creature attacking you at the same time. You should let your pet tank as much as he can. And there are so few slots you can put your spells that having this one memorized does not sound like a good thing. It might speed up your bone chip hunt though.
Lifebane - Agree with Skyshade, good for killing a bunch of baby greens at one time or getting all creatures within range to go aggro on you.
