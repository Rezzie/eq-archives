Strategy

I was exploring HK (you need to raise faction to dubious to do this) when I walked into a High Elf that decided to try and kill me.  I had no problem soloing him at 18 (I love killing High Elves).

 

There are other NPC's all around the area but they do not assist him. There is a door next to the bank that leads to the prison area.  Make a left after going through the door and just keep going (Only one way to go).  At the very last room is a cell that has the High Elf in it.  Nothing special you need to do to kill him.

 

He spawns approximately every 5 minutes. He drops anything from a couple silver to 5 gold (max I got) and also occasionally drops some bronze weapon.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Faydark Champions, Clerics of Tunare, Soldiers of Tunare, and King Tearis Thex.  Raises faction standing with Crushbone Orcs.
