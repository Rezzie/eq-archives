Seafury Cyclops

Mob Level

39 - 42

Location

+1000  -6500

Spells Used

Bond of death, Lich, 49th level pet, augment death, assorted other dots as needed

Strategy

 Camp your pet on the coast and pull a Seafury with your level 1 lifetap spell (yes, level 1).  The low damage of the level 1 spell cause almost no agro on you.  The best technique to use on these is to pull, run to your pet, and then stand behind your pet a little bit. When the Cyclops hits you your pet will be right under him (this will take some work, but it pays off big if you have melee classes with you).  Once your pet engages the Cyclops all you have to do is use pact on your pet to keep him alive, Lich to keep your mana up, and bond of death to keep you healed.  The Cyclops will never agro on you if your pet has augment on him because he will be doing enough damage to keep the Cyclops busy.

The reason for getting your pet right underneath the Cyclops in a group is because they have a huge melee range, so if you have any melee members in your group they can swing from behind your pet and never get hit themselves.

I can constantly pull Seafuries with absolutely no downtime with this technique.  I net about a bubble of XP every 30 minutes and the Cyclops' drop gems that vary in resale value from 50 to 80pp every once in a while.  They also load about 5pp per kill.

Additional Comments

Matamkin

You can do Seafuries in the 44-48 Level range if you have a good 44 pet (45 or 47 hitter).

 

The tactics are much the same as at 49.  Park your pet at the coast and augment. I pull using Vampiric Curse as it's damage is low enough that pet will get its attention right away.  After that just Pact of Shadow to keep pets health up and keep VC on the clops always.  Use drain as necessary to maintain health, I also usually put an Asystole in when the Seafury is down about 1/4 health.  The extra damage helps and the debuff saves your pet some health. (If you do it right away he'll aggro on you).

 

I also recommend keeping renew bones mem'd for the higher level clops (levels 41-42 that hit for 74-76 respectively), as you may need it once or twice.  And of course, use Call of Bones during the fight to help with mana.

 

Using this method I used 2.5 to 4 bubbles of mana, so you had the associated downtime.  The XP isn't great but decent and the loot is fairly good.
