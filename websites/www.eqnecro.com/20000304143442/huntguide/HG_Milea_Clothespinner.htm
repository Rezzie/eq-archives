Mob

Milea Clothespinner

Mob Level

15

Location

Deep in gorge leading to High Hold Pass from East Karana

Spells Used

Clinging Darkness, Poison Bolt, Vampiric Embrace, Bone Walk

Strategy

She is located under a manmade archway deep in the gorge leading to HHP from East Karana. Head east to HHP but instead of getting on the ledge, stay on the ground and you'll go a long ways into a gorge. You'll go very far into this gorge but eventually you'll come to a carved archway and you'll see Milea standing under that archway.

 

At level 18 she cons blue to me. I go past here invisible until I am on the HHP side of the archway just in case I need to zone. The zone isn't real close but err on the side of caution.

 

At 18 I'm hunting her solo (no pet) and have had no problems so far.  I hit her with darkness, back up until my spells are refreshed, then put the other two level 16 dots on her while she's slowly walking up to me. Then I melee her while I steal life and let the dot spells do their work.  Most times I'm down less than a bubble of health and 3 bubbles of mana.  She interrupts quite a bit since she hits, bashes and kicks.  I've had her resist darkness a few times but it's always stuck the second time.

 

She drops up to 5gp but usually it's sp and cp.  Every time she drops a lore, no drop cloth mask of some sort (large races only) and a rusty sword.  So the loot isn't much and the swords will encumber you very quickly so I just destroy them.  The money is ok but nothing special.  I have gotten one word off her so far.

 

Pros: She's completely alone so there's no danger at all from wandering mobs. She isn't a hard mob at all to fight at 18 without a pet, you get all the experience. She drops terrible to decent loot. The real benefit to hunting Milea is the safety factor.

 

Cons: Loot is relatively low compared to other mobs necros can kill. A very long walk from anywhere (bring lots of food and water).

 

Conclusion: If you're going to make the long and dangerous trip to North Karana to hunt Cordelia, head out early and hit this mob at maybe 15 to 20.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Steel Warriors, Guards of Qeynos, and Knights of Truth.  Raises faction standing with Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia
