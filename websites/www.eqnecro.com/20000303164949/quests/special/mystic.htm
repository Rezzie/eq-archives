Mystic Cloak Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Najena, near the jail cells guarded by Moosh (Ogre Guard) and close to Rathyl's spawning place


Who: Linara Parlone, a High Elf enchantress locked in one of the cells

Walkthrough:

 

1. You may not be able to get this quest if your faction standing with the elves is bad (likely).  Thus, your only option is to raise your faction standing with the elves.  Regan, a Gnome Necromancer,  got this quest with a dubious faction standing (thanks for the info Regan!).  And the same person must complete all parts of the quest since all items described below (including the Mystic Cloak) are No Drop

 

2. Head to Najena and go downstairs to the jail area.  Find and hail Linara.  She tells you that to be released, she needs a key that can be found with a Priest that dwells in a estate

 

3. Head to Unrest and look for the Priest of Najena that spawns in loc +500 +50.  The Priest is a ghoul-like creature around level 30.  He is a rare spawn and a Ghould spawns in his place most of the times

 

4. When he does, kill him and loot the Tarnished Bronze Key that he drops

 

5. Head back to Najena and give the key to Linara.  She will then give you a sealed note that you must take to her father in Felwithe

 

6. Go to the lake in Felwithe and look for a fisherman called Tolkar Parlone (loc -50 +670).  Give him the note and he will reward you with a Faded Cloak.  He will then tell you that you should take the cloak to his wife in the Erudin Palace so that she can have the cloak's powers restored

 

7. Head to Erudin Palace and look for the Enchanter's Guild.  On the second floor you will find Linara's mother, Trilani Parlone.  Give her the cloak and she will reward you with the Mystic Cloak
