Mob

Two named Druids

Spells Used

Darkness, Invoke Fear, Venom of the Snake

Strategy

Send pet in after one of the Druids and Fear the other.  Follow with darkness the one your pet is fighting and Fear it then cast venom of the snake.  Let you pet finish him off but keep an eye on it.  Sometimes they break Fear and heal.

 

Wait for other druid to come back, cast Darkness and Fear.  Have pet attack, do not DoT if not needed this way you can space the 2 spawns out.

 

Spawn time is 8 minutes.  Fast exp can take you to level 42.

Additional Comments

Karath Nae'blis

Much safer tactic here is to split the spawn using screaming terror.  Summon your pet and have it guard a point near the West commons zone (the last inn is a good landmark).  Screaming terror one druid and then drop it as a taret and run back to your pet.  The second druid will cast on you, allowing you to target it. When your pet moves to attack the second druid, cast darkness, fear, and VoS. At lvl 39 add Scourge for this first druid.  Once the first is dead meditate to 3 bubbles and kill the second druid with fear/dark/VoS combo.  Splitting the spawns is safer but the fast spaawn rate will require frequent use of Call of Bones/Siphon Life to keep health and mana above 1/2 before each fight.

 

Great loot and XP here, though mobs spawn with greater than normal speed, making them fast even when darknessed. Pathing errors with druids at West commons druid ring makes them easier kills as darkness is not needed.
Chris (Prexus)

First, you start with summoning highest level you can summon, you have higher chance of getting high level pet when you have full mana.  Then, park your pet near Befallen zone or Kithicor zone.  Now go to the Druid ring.  Use 3rd person view to see Druids and Rangers.  Females usually are rangers and males are usually Druids.  The only to way to tell them apart is if they are dual wielding or not. Those dual wielding are Rangers.  Now try to Screaming Terror the Druid because Rangers are easier to kill since their spells are weak.  Druids' DDs will take at least one bubble of your HP so be careful.  If resisted, run like hell and Feign Death and try again.  If not resisted still run like hell to your pet then only 1 Druid/Ranger should be on you.

 

When you are at your pet, start with Dooming Darkness.  If resisted, Fear quick because your pet won't last long against these guys then use Engulfing Darkness.  Start up Call of Bones then Venom of the Snake.  They should go down in no time.

 

The spawn time is around 7 minutes.  I timed it.  Med for 3 minutes then go kill the one that is standing on the Druid ring.  Start up with Invoke Fear.  You don't need to Darkness because there is a pathing bug there so it will just run around same spot over and over.  Sick your pet in, VoS, then Lifetap and Fear as necessary.

 

Note: You have to keep Call of Bones up until level 39 to keep up with spawn.

 

Loot is FS weapons every time.  You can make 1kpp in no time if you hunt here and XP is great for level 34 to 39.
