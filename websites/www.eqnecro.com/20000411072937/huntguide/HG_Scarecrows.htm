Strategy

The Scarecrows are pretty tough, and hit hard.  They also try to Fear you during battle but you should resist it most of the time.

 

For the 13, 14 levels you should start by finding one of the weaker Scarecrows and Darkness them.  If the Darkness succeeds run out of the Miller field (the Millers KoSd me a few time, and most likely will KoS you if they see you). Once out of the field hit them with heat blood and send in your pet.  Once your pet encounters the Scarecrow, Spook it and start pouring it one them with Drain Life and Ward Undead.  When the Spook wears off either use it again or just run backwards because they will usually come straight at you, and watch your pet finish it off.

 

For the other levels just do the same but pull the harder ones and throw in Heart Flutter to weaken them a little and also give your pet an easier target to hit.  Also do not attack Furball Miller.  He is one of the Millers but looks like a Black Burrow Gnoll.  He is very weak but you will lose faction and the other Millers such as Cleet and Tiny will be very mad.
