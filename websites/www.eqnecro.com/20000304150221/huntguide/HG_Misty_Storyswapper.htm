Spells Used

Lifedraw, Heat Blood, Engulfing Darkness, Fear

Strategy

You have to use a bit of savvy at level 12 to solo her correctly because she can be as many as 4 levels red to you.

 

First off...summon a GOOD pet.  I don't care if you have to spend 20 bone chips...you NEED it.  Start a good distance away and cast Engulfing Darkness then send in your pet.  If the darkness doesn't hold, don't recast just cast your heat blood.  After a couple of seconds, fear her.  This works most of the time even on deep reds.  Then to help out your pet, start sucking her dry.  If she comes for you, use what mana you have to heat her blood back up and hope you don't die..=)  This is where you use your knowledge...Don't be stupid and just sit there and take the blows or else you will die quickly.  She hits in the high 30's and at level 12, you can't take many of those...but once you get a pattern down...she is WELL worth the XP.

 

I have been bugged about this spot for awhile now so I'll tell you.  It is NEVER camped and she always drops a Mandolin and upwards of 3 gold.  The mandolin can be sold for about 12 gold (depending on your charisma) and they weigh absolutely NOTHING. (0.0).  So grab some backpacks and bring a sack lunch with you and sit on her for a day.  Once my Necro hits level 12, I go and sit on her from the morning to the afternoon...one day I went from 12 to 16 in 8 hours of gameplay so she is WELL worth it.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with League of Antonican Bards and Guards of Qeynos.  Raises faction standing with Mayong Mistmoore and Ring of Scale.

Additional Comments

Saduja (Bristlebane)

I found that at level 12, Engulfing Darkness and Fear were not a good idea. They were resisted FAR too much. In fact, 6 out of 7 Fears were resisted. Here is what I did. If she is yellow, go ahead and try to pull with Engulfing and Fear. If this does not affect her, revert to "If she is red". Then Heat Blood and let the pet eat at her from behind. Throw in an occasional Lifetap. If she is red, DO NOT try to Fear or Engulfing Darkness her. Just Leach her to pull, Heat Blood her, and use the level 12 Lifetap on her. Yes, it is mana intensive. Yes, you will probably have to jump in and share damage with your pet. But she is about a 7 minute spawn, more than enough time to fully recover. After switching to this, she was very easy for a deep red.
