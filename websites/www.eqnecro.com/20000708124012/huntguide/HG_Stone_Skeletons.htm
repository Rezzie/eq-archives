Strategy

The worst part of this spawn in breaking it.  The spawn has 2 Stone "Skeletons", the name is a lie they are NOT undead, they are in fact Mages.  I started on this spot at level 32 with a level 28 Mage friend, and this is how we did it...

 

Summon good pets and buff them, choose different caster targets (ignore their pets for now).  Cast Screaming Terror to put one of them of.  This will make the other go aggro on you and you get the added bonus of 2 pets hitting you.  At this point, your partner should start blasting.  Aim at his target and use Shock Of Poison to hurt it (I used Vampiric Cure to start getting some health back first).  Before the first is dead, the second will start casting.  Leave him till next concentrate on one at a time.  Once they are both dead, clean up on the pets.

 

They spawn quick so sit and med fast sitting in the tower.  When they respawn, blast the first fast and for the second, let pets try and hold it for a while.  Then finish by getting some health back.  Now the spawn is all yours.

 

I got 2 bubbles of XP in 4 hours here and only one death (trying different ways to split the spawn).  Loot is OK around 1pp plus gold earrings.
