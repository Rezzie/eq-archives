Wednesday, June 21, 2000

News from Brad on Test Server Wipe
Despite the Pwipe, more than 95% of the average population on the test server were playing tonight anyway. They were there, starting anew, despite the huge time loss due to the PWipe.

We also received a lot of heartfelt emails (and very few flames). So, after discussing this issue with 180 or so players on Test tonight, we've decided to seriously consider the following 2 options:

1. Do an item wipe on Test (i.e. restore the characters but without items).
2. Move on a voluntary basis characters from Test to Live servers. The characters would NOT go across with items.

It is also possible we will do both.

I got a lot of good feedback and passionate opinions and responses tonight, and I really appreciate it. The spirit of the vast majority of the players on Test is truly amazing and I admire you all.

While a total player wipe with select individuals being buffed to test mid and higher level zones might be clinically best for the Test Server, I think it's more fair to the players and testers there to do one or both of the options above.

In closing, if we choose option 2 (or both 1 and 2) I would really encourage those players on Test who are there more for playing and less for testing to seriously consider moving. Another wipe, player or item, is not necessarily unlikely some time in the future.

Your comments and feedback are welcome,

-Brad
