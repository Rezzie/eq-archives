Level 51+ Spells Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: West Cabilis or The Overthere


Who: Izarod Fristan (Cabilis) or Vaean the Night (Overthere)

Walkthrough:

 

There are two different quests to obtain a four different spells.  The first quest is obtained in Cabilis with an NPC named Izarod Fristan who is in the Necro guild.

 

Hail Izarod Fristan

 

Besides all the ones you will find, there are some that are only in my possession. To show that you have been successful on your journey, return either one of Splurt, Defoliation, Convergence, or Thrall of Bones. You then will be rewarded with one of my scrolls.

 

 

The second quest is obtained in Overthere with a guy in the vendor circle not too far from the docks.  This same vendors sell spells, including some level 51+ spells and others that cannot be bought in Necromancer guilds.

Hail Vaean the Night

I see it as a win win situation for me. If you succeed I'll gain more power from knowledge you bring back to me. If you fail, you become another addition to my undead minions. So thus you cannot fail me in returning the scroll of Splurt, Defoliation, Covergence, and Thrall of Bones. In return I will part with a scroll of mine.

As you can see, both quests ask for the same spells:

 

- Splurt (level 51)

- Defoliation (level 52)

- Convergence (level 53)

- Thrall of Bones (level 54)

 

All of the above spells can be obtained as loot from level 45+ mobs.  Turning one of them in to one of these NPCs will result in a random spell from the following list:

 

- Sacrifice (level 51)

- Scent of Terris (level 52)

- Minion of Shadown (level 53)

- Shadowbond (level 54)

 

So, turn one spell at a time and good luck!
