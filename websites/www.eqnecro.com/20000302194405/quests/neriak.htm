Neriak Quest Guide

 

 

 

 

Where: Neriak's necro guild
Who: Necro on top floor of Neriak's necro guild
What: Collect 10 Fire Beetle eyes
How: Head to any zone with Fire Beetles and kill them until you collect ten Eyes. Put the eyes inside the box given to you when you started the quest. Once you have 10 eyes, hit combine
Reward: It can be either a low level spell such as locate corpse or items such as lanterns in addition to experience

Update info submitted by Kalentas

 


Where: Neriak's necro guild
Who: Necro on top floor of Neriak's necro guild
What: Seek three individuals and them if they have messages for Neriak
How: The three individuals are found in West Commonlands, East Commonlands, and Highpass Hold
Reward: You will get money and experience for each successfully completed quest

 

 

Where: Neriak's necro guild
Who: Lovehill
What: Bring Sir Lindeal's back his prayer book
How: Sir Lindeal spawns in Lavastorm and is around level 15.  He walks all over Lavastorm so it might be hard to track him down. He does go by the entrance to Najena and near the gypsy camp
Reward: Random item such as robes and spells

 


Where: Neriak's necro guild
Who: Lovehill
What: Second part of the above quest, you must find him the Thex Mallet
How: Once you find Kizdean, say the phrase Lovehill told you.  Kizdean will then give you a note you must give to his apprentice, Lrodd.  Lrodd can be found in the first level of Befallen but is now dead.  Give the note to his apprentice and he will tell you that to get the Mallet you must seek a level 20+ zombie called Windstream. He will spawn in the third level of Befallen.  You will now need to find three pieces for the Mallet, the stem (from killing Windstream), the head and the hilt (from killing elf skeletons).  Once you have all three pieces, put the head and the hilt in the stem, hit combine and you get yourself the Thex Mallet
Reward: Random spell, which can range from mid to low level spell

 


Where: Neriak's necro guild
Who: Noxhil V'Sek, a necromancer on top floor of Neriak's necro guild
What: Get him a bull elephant tusk
How: Head to South Karana and kill bull elephants till you get the tusk he is looking for
Reward: Experience and a small item
