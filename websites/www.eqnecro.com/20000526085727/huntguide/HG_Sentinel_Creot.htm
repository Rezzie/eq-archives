Strategy

Sentinel Creot is a paladin, which means he is a very easy kill PROVIDED you can keep him from using Lay Hands. He spawns right next to the shop on the north side of the beach, west of the Erudin entrance (the shop marked Shop 1 on Quester of Norrath's map). Summon pet, give pet 2 weapons, buff pet, make sure /pet taunt is ON in case something goes wrong and pet needs to tank while you run. Pull Creot with Dooming Darkness, which he will resist about 25% of the time; don't worry, just send pet and recast until it sticks. If he resists more than 4 times you may not have enough mana to kill him and should probably gate or feign death, but this is rare. Now cast VoS, which is almost never resisted. He should be good and pissed at you by now, so just back away until his health is below 50% (this won't take long, he has few HP for his level). Now the important part, and the reason he is very difficult to kill before level 34: cast Invoke Fear, repeating as needed until it sticks. Get him Feared before he is at 25% health or he will use the dreaded Lay Hands complete-heal ability, essentially starting the battle over. Once he is Feared, start casting Shock of Poison or Spirit Tap to drop him as quickly as possible. Generally 2-3 shots will do the trick.

 

Creot always drops 0-5pp and a Battle Staff, 2HB +10 charisma item that sells for 3.5pp to the vendor or about 10pp to players, provided you can find an Erudite cleric or paladin in a newbie zone who has 10pp =). They are lore items, so you will need to sell after every fight; this is not a problem, since the druid vendor is just a short walk away at +1050,-380. He also occasionally drops Fine Steel weapons, medium ringmail armor, jewelry, and level 29 research components. XP is comparable to Ulrich, who I have virtually given up on (he's camped 24/7 even on Rallos Zek, one of the least crowded servers). 6 minute spawn time, which you will most likely not be able to keep up with at 34th level. Be sure to med a safe distance from his spawn point, because he might get within aggro range of you while valiantly defending Erudin from skunks and fire beetles. He does wander a bit, patrolling down the beach to the docks and then back. I've rarely had trouble finding him.

 

Creot is a good kill because he doesn't have the ridiculous magic resistance Verant has given to some guards. Also, he raises your Heretic faction. Dark elves will be able to enter Paineel after seven or eight kills. Unfortunately he stops giving faction once you get them to apprehensive (which is fine, because it allows you to buy/sell and bank there). If you really want higher faction so you can train in the necro guild or do quests, kill Shintar Vinlail (low level Erudite wizard who wanders the Paineel side of the bridge) or the palace courier (who can be found running aimlessly into the wall at the Erudin zone line). Your escape plan should be either Feign Death or Gate, although in a serious pinch you can run for the Erudin zone and Harmshield to get past the two sentinels there. You will rarely die here, but may be forced to zone more than average when Creot gets on one of his resist binges the current record is resisting darkness 7 times and fear 6 times =P
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Erudin High Guards, Erudin Merchants, and Erudin High Council.  Raises faction standing with Heretics.
