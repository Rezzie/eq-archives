Strategy

Ok well there are a few different ways to do this, depending on the set up of the spawn.

 

First lets say there is a frenzy in the middle, a blood thirsty on the left, and a dar on the right.  Okay now sometimes (all the time maybe) when you charm the one on either side the middle one will attack you but NOT the one on the opposite side (charm the blood thirsty on the left, frenzied in middle will come but dar on the right won't).

 

Clearing a frenzy room solo pretty much relies on this fact.  So to prepare pull out the spells: Root, Harmshield, Feign Death, Bond of Death, Ignite Blood, Drain Life, Banish Undead.  Also since you will be charming its best to have charisma items on instead of INT/mana items. I highly recommend having SOW on and Diamondskin as well.

 

Ok now onto the battle...start by charming the bloodthirsty (or whatever happens to  be there) and the frenzy should make his way toward you. Start with ignite blood since it takes longer to work, then do bond of death.  Then its pretty simple...root and  banish away.  The bloodthirsty can take a lot and dish it out also.  If its not a bloodthirsty then replace one spell ,drain life id say, with invoke death (you might even have an  extra spell slot open, didn't count).  So assume you charmed the dar instead (only do  that if there's something smaller on the other side, like a wan) now the frenzy will slaughter that dar.  Once you see the dar almost dead, re-root the frenzy and begin casting invoke death, with luck you'll get it off and be able to arm him.  Then pet and  nukes should finish the job.  Be sure you're ready to root though in case he starts to run.

 

Now if your charm fails...give up.  Accept the fact you either have to feign run or die,  and you are NOT going to kill that frenzy (note-if frenzy is almost dead, simply feign  let dots finish it, but make SURE to get up before he dies or he doesn't leave a corpse nor give exp). Just Harmshield and run away, with sow you should make it to zone, or  you can feign and /quit. Once your char leaves Norrath you can come back in and frenzy will have forgotten you.

 

Finally: soloing frenzied.  This is for that rare case where you find the frenzied alone (happened to me twice so far).  This is very tricky because you don't have an over- powered ghoul knight by your side, so of course make your pet as usual and get out  the pet speed buff augment death.  Equip your pet with 2 FS weapons and augment once your full. Now for this fight pull out invoke fear and cascading darkness as well as your normal spells.  Now start the fight with bond of death, since it takes so long to cast and you get 1 free spell it might as well be that (also the frenzy is going to have 1  good round of beating you so this will restore your hp).  Now put on Cascading Darkness and Ignite, followed by Invoke Fear.  This only works once because if he fear a 2nd time he will go too far away and bring friends.  Once he is near the room with the other froggies root him if fear hasn't broken yet, that bit of time is all your pet needs to get an edge.  Now you should be at full again with BoD still going strong...get in there and take a few hits for your pet, back off and let the BoD work and toss in a nuke or 2...then take a few more hits once your full health. Now if the fight ends up a bit close, take quite a few hits, bring yourself around half, then cast Harmshield...this is 15 seconds of pure beatings that your pet will be administering, with one or 2 dots still going on him. This should finish him, feel free to renew BoD once or twice and nuke  whenever possible (when he's feared). Also a variation to this...instead of cascading and fear, pull up drain and tank even more for your pet, and just drain the health  back. That along with BoD will do quite a bit of damage and save your pet a lot of hits from frenzied.
