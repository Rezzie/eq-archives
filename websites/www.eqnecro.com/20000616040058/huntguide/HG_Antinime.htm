Mob

Antinime

Mob Level

22 - 24

Location

Sister Island shoreline

Spells Used

Engulfing Darkness, Heart Flutter, Heat Blood, Life Tap, Fear, Feign Death, Gate

Strategy

This lady is one tough cookie.  To get to her, ride the boat from Freeport to Butcherblock, jumping off the boat before you get to the dock at the Sister island.  The sister at the end of the dock will pound you flat.  Once you are on the island, stay near the shore to avoid the attention of the lower level sisters.  You will find her standing on the shoreline by herself.

 

Make sure you have a good pet, and pull her away from where she spawns.  Pull all the way back to where there is a small piece of island sticking out.  Make sure you pull with Darkness to slow her down, so you have a chance to get your other DOTs on her.  Get your pet on her and get away from her.  She is fast.  Use Life Tap on her, it casts faster than Siphon Life, so you have a slightly better chance to get it off.  Use Fear with the utmost caution, she will wander into the center of the island and bring all the lower level sisters to you.  Be sure you know what is around you.  The lower level sisters, if they get too close, will join in.  If this happens, Gate or Feign.  They may also try to heal her.

 

Her loot can range from several silver to 2 - 3 platinum.  She almost always carries a Purity Belt too, worth another plat at a friendly vendor.  She is a very tough mob, so make sure you have full buffs loaded and you are paying attention.  If it gets too bad, try to run away from her some and see if your pet can get her off.  She will usually go straight for you, no matter how hard your pet tries to taunt her off.  Expect to finish most fights real low on health.

Additional Comments

Thara

Antinime is a 6 minute spawn.  I would not advise soloing Antinime before level 23 because she will be red at times and will resist enough to beat you into submission.  She is very good experience at level 23 but still very dangerous.  When she spawns as a level 24, she hits for 50 and double attacks!  Another problem is the wandering MOBS and the high level MOBs very close to the spawn point.

 

I would advise never using fear! The lower level sisters will ignore you but they will attack your pet. Also, you might get some of the other named MOBs which means feign and recast pet.

 

Here is how I handle her at level 23: Cast your best pet and fully buff.  If she is yellow, cast Shieldskin on yourself, you will be meleeing for a bit.  I know the Shieldskin costs a reagent but the purity belt loot will offset the expense.  I move down the coastline toward her with my pet.  I have pet attack first!  This is crucial!  She will stay on your pet MUCH longer this way and I am able to get all DOT's to stick and several casts of Siphon Life.  If she doesn't come after you at this point you will want to move in and melee a bit to save your pet as he will be around 25% health.  Cast Lifedraw during the melee since it is a faster cast than Siphon Life.  You will bet beat up a bit but the Lifedraws will keep your health up.

 

That's it.  The reason I like this tactic is that it keeps the battle on the shoreline away from the other MOB's and she doesn't aggro on you at the beginning of the battle.  After the battle, move down the shoreline to the point sticking out, med and get ready for another fight in 6 minutes.
