November 1999

 

Welcome. When we had the idea of holding a contest, I never knew it would be so hard to figure out the winner. Not only that, but we got so many good stories we simply couldn't select only two winners. That is why you will see we selected not 2, but 5 winning stories. Ok, so you don't want to read this, all you want is see who won and read their stories. Here we go:

First Prize - US$ 100.00


Thanateros by Achad, Innoruuk

Second Prizes - US$ 50.00


The Recluse's Testimony by Humwawa D'Estain, Brell Serillis
Revenge by Mourtis Darkmoure, Rodcet Nife
Bizarre Love Triangle by Niande Boneshaper, Xegony
Trickery by Shai'tan, Rathe

Just a quick and final comment. Thanateros is a really well written story. I am not giving anything away, but the way the story is setup makes you want to read more and more. Very good job Achad. The other four stories are also very well written and could have well gone for the first prize. Great job Humwawa, Mourtis, Niande, and Shai'Tan. Overall, I think you will agree that these are 5 stories that deserved recognition. Some are darker and some are funnier but they all have one thing in common, a roleplay narrative. Enjoy your reading and let us know what you think. For those who won, we will be getting in touch with you guys tomorrow. Or just send us an email :)
