Strategy

Cynthia, the female Mage spawns every 7 minutes near the fire at the center of the Gypsy camp.  With a pet she is quite easy.

 

First, buff your pet and yourself with a Shield spell.  Then, send pet and cast Cancel Magic.  It is important to send the pet first because if you don't do it, she will cast on you and she is a good nuker.  After that, cast Shock of Poison and Spirit Tap.  After 4 or 5 Shock of Poisons, she is dead.

 

Important:  do not Fear!  Cyclops are nearby and will complicate the situation a whole lot!

 

Loot is up to 4 plat and some Pearls. You can sell the stuff in the Gypsy camp.

 

Other things to note:

 

1. You get no bad faction when you kill Cynthia

 

2. The other NPCs won't attack you when you attack Cynthia

 

Enjoy!

Additional Comments

Sandian Dcarupt (Ec'i)

At level 39 Cynthia is easy prey and still good experience, and for an added bonus you can knock off Cyclops' while you wait for her to respawn.  At level 39 your new pet will tear Cynthia to shreds.  Just target her and send in pet after casting augment death on him.  She'll probably get one nuke off before she dies. If so just cast drain spirit on her to regain the lost health . Cast vamp curse before she dies to negate the loss of life when you have call of bones on.  She seems to have at least a 7min respawn rate, I'm not sure but it seems a little longer to me. Whatever the length of her respawn rate is it is irrelevant because while you wait you have a nice supply of Cyclops' to tear up in the meantime.
