ShadowBound Boots Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro

Who: Syllina

Walkthrough:

 

1. Head to the Temple of Solusek Ro and talk to Syllina.  She will tell you that she makes three different kinds of ShadowBound armor, Boots, Gloves, and Robes.  You will need a different set of items to get each of these items

 

2. To get the boots, you need the following items: a Shadow Silk, the Scepter of Sorrow, an Eye of Shadows, and a Skeletal Toe

 

3. You can get these items in any order, but remember that items gotten from the Shadow Men disappear once you logout

 

3.1. The Shadow Silk must either be tailored or bought from a PC.  To tailor one, you will need 1 Shadow Wolf Pelt (sometimes dropped by Shadow Wolves), 1 Gather Shadows scroll (bought in any necro guild), and 1 Silk Swatch (tailored from 2 Spider Silks, sometimes dropped by Giant Spiders/Large Spiders)

 

3.2. The Scepter of Sorrow can be found in Mistmoore.  Go to the building in the middle, the Crematorium.  You will see a coffin with a bag on top of it.  Get it and you will see it is a flower.  Take it and leave the building.  Go to the back of the Crematorium and you will see a Shadowed Man standing there.  Give him the flower and, in return, he will give you the Scepter.  That is the one you are looking for.  Cast Identify on it to make sure you have the right one.  If you want to, kill the Shadowed Man to loot the Robe of the Keeper

 

3.3. The Eye of Shadow is found in any Shadowed Man camp.  It is sometimes dropped by them.  Keep killing them until you get one

 

3.4. Finally, the Skeletal Tow can be found with Giant Skeletons.  Head to an area where Giant Skeletons spawn (Rathe Mountains for example) and keep killing them until you get one.  It can be quite a rare loot.

 

4. Once you have all four items, head back to the Temple of Solusek Ro and give all four items to Syllina.  In return, she will give you the ShadowBound Boots
