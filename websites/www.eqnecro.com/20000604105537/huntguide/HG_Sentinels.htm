Strategy

Inside Erudin itself is actually a great place to hunt.  Invis past the guards in Toxx forest, killing the courtier at the zone :)  When you zone in you will see two teleporters.  Jump into either of them, this will take you to the 'balcony.'  This is where you will pull to, so begin by summoning your pet.  Note that to escape from here, you can either jump into a teleporter or jump off the balcony (200 damage) and zone out.  I have never been chased/hit by guards zoning.

 

Once you have armed and buffed your pet, set it to guard and run along the circular corridor leading to the city.  You will come to a set of stairs leading to what I presume is the main courtyard.  Wait there for a couple of minutes, standing half way up (not in the courtyard) the stairs.  If you are on the stairs and not in the courtyard you will not be aggroed by the guards.  You cannot hit them with spells either... Waiting on the stairs allows you to watch the guards movements.  There are two 'blocks' of buildings, one near, one further in.  One guard circles each block.

 

The best plan is to wait until the nearest guard comes and stands at the top of the stairs.  If you pull him when he is walking in the courtyard, you sometimes bring both. T o pull him, run up the stairs and straight back down, he will aggro instantly.  Run to your pet and root the guard. Let your pet tank while you apply DoTs.  After you have applied DoT's, keep him rooted until he is under 50% HPs.  Then when root has broken, hit him with Dooming Darkness and Fear him. Nuke him as he runs for Erudin.  You will get the timing right with a little practice and to be honest he'd have to be lucky to get into Erudin DoT'd and being nuked.  If he gets close to Erudin, root him again and prepare for a long fight (he will LoH).  Usually though he will not LoH and die whilst still feared.

 

Once the first Sentinel is dead, you can pull the 2nd. Pull her from the right hand side of the courtyard, as there is an enchanter on the left (standing in front of the building). I haven't pulled the enchanter, but it might be possible, as she aggroed me once and came alone.  The loot is 0-4pp, with Battlestaffs and FS weapons.  The nearest shop is the heretics in Toxx.  XP is good and the place is never camped.  And the best thing is getting a picture of yourself standing at the 'Welcome to Erudin' sign ;)

 

Well worth the visit, good hunting!
