Strategy

Ok, I started doing this at level 18 for very good experience.  It is possible at 16 but extremely hard to do.

 

First, it is important to get a high level pet and cast Spirit Armor on it.  Med to full and cast Dark Pact on your self.  Now you have two choices, Lars or Juhn.  I pick Lars because loot is good and safety is good.  Lars hangs out by the dock.  If you pull him right along the shore line to the zone wall, you should be safe.  Have your pet guard by the zone wall for extra safety.  I pull with engulfing darkness to slow the mob.  If it is resisted, I run until I can cast again and then I cast and it usually sticks the second time.  Next, keep running and sort of kite him towards your pet.  Don't use your Lifetap, use your DoTs, and once you get to your pet, have the pet attack and then  Fear FAST!  Lars hits for 35-45 and doubles along with a 2 second stun bash that works a lot.  Once he is feared, sit down and med.  Yeah, it is only half a bubble of mana tops, but that is half a bubble you will need.  By now, I usually have two bubble of mana and he didn't resist.  Now he will break Fear.  Don't re-fear but cast Darkness again until it sticks.  This will put you in no mana range and a very large aggro.  So, basically your pet is almost dead and he wants to kill you now.  Just run like hell and stop after you've run pretty dang far so you can re-fear.  Just Lifetap him to death and you should end with no mana left and at full health.  You probably got resisted 2 or 3 times and maybe your pet died.  Yeah it is hard to do.

 

Do the same thing with Juhn Mcmannus.  Pull him by opening his door and running like hell to your pet.  You can't target Juhn because he is in a house and will aggro if you step around the house or open the door.  Juhn is a little bit easier I think.  A lot slower I am sure, but his loot is just a Fishing Spear while Lars is level 20 and drops spell components and FS weapons.

 

Careful with the Barbarian named Ulrich McMannus.  He often runs around and will aggro on you if you touch the Barbarians so keep a close eye out for him.

 

Usually high levels are around hunting Ulrich and Einhorst McMannus but this is to your advantage.  Usually the hunters are druids and/or shamans, can you say SoW =) and when fellow high level necros come to hunt kindly ask if you can borrow the pet incase you get in trouble.  Their pets can take Lars and or Juhn easy.

 

Well hope you have as much fun as I am Having with these guys.
