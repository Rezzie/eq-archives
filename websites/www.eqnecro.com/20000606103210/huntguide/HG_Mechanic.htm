Mob

A Mechanic

Mob Level

23 - 27

Location

Waterfall room

Spells Used

Screaming Terror, Darkness, Shock of Poison, Leatherskin, Feign Death

Strategy

Not sure on the loc, but they spawn in the room with the huge waterfall (they spawn right in front of a vendor you can sell your loot to).

 

Always keep Leatherskin up, just trust me on this one, and unless you took no damage, refresh if after every battle.  Start with Screaming terror, you'll get three chances to darkness the creature, which is imperative, because if it runs too far off, you will get whacked by either a Guide or a Clockwork.  After you get darkness to stick, immediately sic your pet on the Mechanic and nuke it with Shock of Poison.  You should be able to get 2 SoPs off on the Mechanic.  This should either bring the mechanic to the point of fleeing or just about there.  Usually, if you didn't get darkness to stick, the mechanic will flee directly into the water.  It is imperative that you jump in, wait a few seconds to stop moving, and take it out.  The Mechanic could, if you are too slow, make it within the aggro radius of a high level Clockwork, thereby getting you killed.

 

Mechanics are great XP, and their loot is comprised of Small Ringmail, Fine Steel weapons, up to 5.5pp cash, Words, Pages, Runes, Mage research items, and rare drops of gems (I've gotten 6 Black Pearls in 3 levels, which I've had fashioned into rings and sold at great profit for more than enough cash to buy myself an Oracle Robe and a Nightshade wreath, and have 2000pp to spare).

 

The spawn timer on these guys is between 5 and 6 minutes, not quite sure, never timed it.  I would REALLY recommend these guys.  They are a very profitable, and much quicker alternative to Treants (approximately 1 bubble an hour, and 100pp/hour loot unless you get lucky and loot a Black Pearl).

 

I am not sure whether this is a viable camp for dark elves.  I bet the vendor the Mechanic stands next to will be naturally aggro to you, rendering this camp useless without extreme faction work.  But I see no problems with Gnomes, Humans, and Erudites camping them, unless you are against kin-slaying (as a gnome that is).
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Gemchoppers, Eldritch Collective, and King of Ak'Anon.  Raises faction standing with Dark Reflection and Meldrath.

Madjake Shadybone

The spawn rate is 7 minutes.  No need for any mana conversion here as you will still have time to work on your swimming skills (haha).  The loot is the best I have encountered so far.  Faction is a concern since I am Gnome.  Just watch you cons and when they drop, go kill some Minos.  Faction jumps back up pretty quick.  I don't know how to get the Collective faction back up, but who cares about them anyway.  Also, you can kill Rogue Clockworks and turn in the scrap the the guy past the waterwheel.  During fighting, I have found that if you wait till they are off of the area by the broken bridge they tend not to jump in the water so much.  I set up about half way between the spawn point and a cart.  I put my pet on him after he runs down off the ramp and usally get one shock of poison, before he gets to me.  Now run up and get in that cart (hehe) and cast SoP again.  By now the battle is pretty much over and time to do some fishing or swimming till the next one comes along.

Ok, here is another secret we have been keeping from you.  Not everyone is going to like me giving out this secret Mechanic spot. Oh well, another faction hit with the commoners.  In king Ak'Anon's house there are three more mechanics that can be camped. Stay in the last room where the third one is. You can kill him first. Then move on to the second. Most likely the third will come after you too. If you park you pet in the door of the last room he will hold both of them up usually, but not always. Kill the second one fast as possible and toy with the third to break the spawn. Not very hard to do, but well worth the loot and exp you will get. If you keep allure on you should be able to keep the exp and loot flowing non-stop.
