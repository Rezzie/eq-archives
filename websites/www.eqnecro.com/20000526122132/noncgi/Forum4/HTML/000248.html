All level 44 words can be obtained at the docks of Odus, as well as all 44 research components for other classes. Simply go to Odus, kill the diseased shark(s) that spawn there and kill the young sharks that spawn immediately after the death of the diseases shark. Fish scales can be looted off many of the other fishes in the waters there. It can take some time as the young sharks also drop all other 44 research components for other classes. I did this for about 16 hours before i obtained all my components as well as enchantor and wiz components. Oh, by the way, the guards (sentinals) there don't like you and even though they will be green, they hit hard. The rock to the far left of the docks seems to be a safe haven and NPC's have a hard time tracking to it.

Note that the diseased shark spawns mostly after the shark in the cove wanders off, but also at other times.
