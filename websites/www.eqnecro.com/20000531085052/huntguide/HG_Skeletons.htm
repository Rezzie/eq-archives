Strategy

This is very good hunting if the zone (Nektulos) is relatively empty and you can grab the pyramid nearest to Neriak.

 

First off, there are up to three skeletons that spawn there plus a Zombie or Lesser Mummy every once in a while.

 

1> What I do first is use "Numb the Dead" on the mobs I don't want to come to me, and pray it holds.

 

2> Next I use Siphon Strength on the one I want and pull it to my location (a good distance away from the spawn point).

 

*note* If numb the dead doesn't hold, and all three (or 4) mobs come after you, run to the guards at Neriak which isn't very far; that way breaking the spawn up and being able to start fresh.

 

3> I then allow my pet to wack on it while I cast Poison Bolt.

 

4> I then jump in and melee it, all the while checking my pets health with /pet report health.

 

5> When I start getting low on hp's I usually back off and cast Lifespike to boost my hp's back to a reasonable range.

 

6> By this time the skeleton is dead and I have time to med back up to full mana before repeating the process all over again.
