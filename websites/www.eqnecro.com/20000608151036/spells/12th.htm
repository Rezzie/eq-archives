Banish Undead

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 225

Speed : 5.5

Damage : 585 points of damage

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Direct damage against undead creatures

Comments:

Skyshade - At level 49, you have more than one spell that can deal the same amount of damage Banish Undead deals. Banish Undead is still a useful spell since it costs little mana for the damage it deals. 
Lifebane - Go to Lower Guk Dead side.... keep this spell memorized. You will be well loved by many in your group when helping to take down a wizard quickly or a quick nuke to finish off a mean ghoul frog



Bond of Death

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 360

Speed : 7.0

Damage : Drains 800 points from target to self over a 1 minute period

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Odus and Grappling

Effect : The upgrade to Vampiric Curse, Bond of Death is a drain life type of DoT

Comments:

Skyshade - Awesome spell. Works the same way Vampiric Curse does, but drains a lot more points 
Lifebane - Always have it memorized, an 800 point DOT spell with no initial DD damage means that you are 95% unlikely to get aggro monster on your butt. And when you combine it with the lvl 44 Pact Of Shadow spell, you can heal one or two targets for about 350-400 each without taking any damage if you cast Bond of Death first. And do recall, your PET is a valid target *grin*. 



Cajole Undead

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 245

Speed : 9.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Last in the Charm Undead spell line

Comments:

Skyshade - Once again, this might have some uses, but only in certain situations. You should often prefer a pet over a charmed mob. 
Lifebane - Well now. Are you a smart Necro? Strategies? If in undead area, this is a good spawn breaker spell.  Used it frequently in Frenzy and Lord rooms in Lower Guk. Cast it on a MEAN ghoul frog and make a pet out of him while you take out one of your enemy. Nothing like a Bok knight pet. Basically grab him and throw him at the other frogs. Do make sure you have high charisma or an item or two to boost it since success and duration are rumored to be dependent on this. 



Cascading Darkness

Skill : Conjuration 

Mana : 300

Speed : 7.0

Damage : 800 points of damage over time

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Final upgrade to the Darkness line of spells, Cascading Darkness slows your target down and deals damage over time

Comments:

Skyshade - You should know by now that you need this spell :) 
Lifebane - This is the final upgrade to our Darkness line of spells. Does respectable damage over an approximately 2 minute period of time. A bit slow for dungeon battles and still just as resistable as lower level versions of this spell. I normally use clinging darkness or engulfing darkness at lvl 50, my logic.... too expensive and slow to cast for a high resist spell. Better off with cheap fast casting spell that gives same slow effects. You decide. 



Drain Soul

Mana : 225

Speed : 6.1

Damage : Drains a maximum of 338 points from your target

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material :

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Final upgrade to the Blood line of spells, Ignite Blood deals damage over time

Invoke Death

Skill : Conjuration 

Mana : 490

Speed : 16.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Requisition and Acquisition (beza)

Effect : The level 49 pet spell

Comments:

Skyshade - This is as good as it gets. Your skeleton can hit for a maximum of 57 points of damage (as long as you have the focus item).
Lifebane - Ahh yes. What you have been waiting for.  The pet is mean, hard hitting, and can take quite a bit of abuse. Bear in mind though, a pet is as tough as it's master is smart. To throw this pet at a large foe is still suicide. But, augment the pet, give it 2 daggers, and then use Pact of Shadow to keep it alive and kicking. You will surprise yourself sometimes at the effectiveness of a well managed pet.



Lich

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 5

Speed : 3.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of the Ethereal

Effect : Final upgrade to the Allure of Death series, Lich will turn the caster into a skeleton and exchange 32 HPs for 20 points of mana per tick

Comments:

Skyshade - Lots of uses for Lich. Reduce downtime, use in conjunction with Bond of Death, or just scare a newbie..
Lifebane - The amount of mana being regained is as scary as the amount of health you are losing! This spell works particularly well in conjunction with chloroplast version of regeneration if you can get it cast on you and of course for short periods with the level 49 Necromancer spell "Bond of Death". 



Paralyzing Earth

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 100

Speed : 2.5

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of the Crippling Force

Effect : Upgrade to root, but with four times its potential duration

Comments:

Skyshade - Paralyzing Earth is no longer broken.
Lifebane - This is a 100 mana version of our level 34 ROOT spell. Resistance seems to be about the same, only real difference is that there is the "potential" for the root effect to last much longer with this version of the spell. I personally still use lvl 34 root at lvl 50, less mana for a high resist spell.
