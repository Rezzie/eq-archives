Strategy

This mob is supposed to be Kithicor's personal pet, and as such you can find the great bear grazing in front of Kithicor's chalet-style hut, about -500, 2100.

 

Just hit him with a DoT to draw toward the zone border, then send in the pet, cast Darkness and Fear him... That's it -)

 

Even with the long spawn, he's never camped, so easy to rotate in and kill right before sundown.  I have heard of a rare drop, but after several kills, have seen no evidence as yet.

 

Kithicor, I'm coming for you soon -)
