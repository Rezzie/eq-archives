Strategy

Five minutes spawn time.  Have pet guard at Hermit's hut. When Hermit engages your pet, cast Darkness, Heat blood, Heart flutter, Infectious Cloud. That's it.  IF, and that's a big if, he gets to you, he will do about 100 hp's damage before the DoTs kill him.

 

Sit and med with Dark Pact. By the time you are back to full and pet is healed to 100%, Johan will show up again.  Next time around, skip two DoTs and cast Life drain to get the hp's back.

 

Easiest spot I have ever been to.  Got a full bubble in one hour without EVER having to cast Fear.
