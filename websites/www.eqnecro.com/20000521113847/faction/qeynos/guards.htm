Where: Black Burrow
Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? When you kill a gnoll, you will often loot gnoll teeth.  Keep those and give them, 4 at a time to the Captain of the Qeynos guard that can be found in the Arena area (second floor).  Your faction standing will be raised and you will get a moonstone.  If you wish, take this moonstone to a bartender in the Fish Ale for a stout per moonstone.  You will not get a faction adjustment, but the stouts are worth a little more than the moonstones

Faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing

Where: South Qeynos

Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? Find Captain Tillin in South Qeynos and hail him.  Go through the threads and in the end he will say the following: "You fancy yourself a Gnoll Slayer huh? Very well. We can use all the help we can get against those blood thirsty dogs. Right now we offer a bounty of one moonstone per adult gnoll. You don't get paid for killing the pups. Bring back their fangs as proof but be sure to hand them to me one at a time so I don't lose count and short change you".  You can find the Gnolls in Black Burrow.
