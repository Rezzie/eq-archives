Robe of Enshroudment Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro

Who: Syllina

Walkthrough:

 

1. Head to the Temple of Solusek Ro and talk to Syllina.  She will tell you that she makes three different kinds of ShadowBound armor, Boots, Gloves, and Robes.  You will need a different set of items to get each of these items

 

2. To get the robe, you need the following items: a Large Shadow Silk, the Globe of Shadow, a Mask of Shadow, and a Werebat Wing

 

3. You can get these items in any order, but remember that items gotten from the Shadow Men disappear once you logout

 

3.1. The Large Shadow Silk must either be tailored or bought from a PC.  To tailor one, you will need 2 Shadow Wolf Pelts (sometimes dropped by Shadow Wolves), 1 Gather Shadows scroll (bought in any necro guild), and 1 Silk Swatch (tailored from 2 Spider Silk sometimes dropped by Giant Spiders/Large Spiders)

 

3.2. The Globe of Shadow can be found in Guk, with the Nokta Shamans.  Go to Upper Guk and head to the zone to Lower Guk with water.  Look for an island with a fire burning.  You will see three Frogloks on this island.  The room where you can find the Nokta Shaman is guarded by 2 Shins and 1 Vis Shaman.  Kill them to loot the Globe of Shadows from the Nokta Shaman (note that Guk also has Nok Shamans but these do not drop the Globe of Shadows)

 

3.3. The Mask of Shadow is found in any Shadowed Man camp.  It is sometimes dropped by them.  Keep killing them until you get one

 

3.4. Finally, the Werebat Wing can be found with Werebats in Unrest.  Keep killing the Werebats until you get a Werebat Wing.  Note that the Wing is No Drop, so you must go to Unrest and loot it yourself

 

4. Once you have all four items, head back to the Temple of Solusek Ro and give all four items to Syllina.  In return, she will give you the Robe of Enshroudment
