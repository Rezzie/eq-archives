At level 50 or so you can hunt the following dungeons without regards to faction hits:

City of Mist: some mobs are green, and if you venture deep there's pathing issues that will more than likely dump a train of 5+ mobs on your group.
Chardok: Bring a good group/or two. Yard trash are high 40s and casters WILL put a beating on you. Bring clerics and enchanter to stun them or they'll gate and bring friends.
Sebilis: Need key(s) for everyone, haven't been there myself.

Not many outdoor mobs give decent experience past level 50 with the exception of Skyfire. Wurms and wyverns have lots of HP, the chromadracs AE dispell and are a pain in the butt. Oh and pulling is tricky due to all the warping in Kunark.
