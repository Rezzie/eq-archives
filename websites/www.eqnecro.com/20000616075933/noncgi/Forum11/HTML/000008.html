This was my experience to OVERTHERE, I am a Gnome Necro that reached level 51 before I started my journey.

I boarded the Barrel Barge in Oasis at the docks which was to start my journey. After a short time we zoned into Timmorous Deep. Timmorous Deep is just a layover island to get to the OVERTHERE. Once the raft stops jump off and head out over the sand. There are two points of interest here. One is the Aviak city (They are not Aviaks but I forgot the name already). The other is a Fred Flintstone looking village full of Ogres. I was not KOS to anything here, nor did I want to be so no hunting yet. In the Fred Flintstone village there is a dancing skeleton, pay the guy the gold and watch him dance while you wait for the boat. The boat to OVERTHERE arrives on the dock where the shunken ship is, or the dock that looks like it is tied together with rope. You will notice the boat when it arrives, it looks like a shipwreck ontop of a barge. Once boarded, this boat hauls ass. DO NOT jump or fall off, it's a LONG rin back to the dock. The first point of interest on the boat is the Ship graveyard, I was able to con some green bandits in this area. The boat did not stop and continued on, the second point of interest will be on the right side and looks like an old abandoned castle. I was not able to con anything there and nor did the boat stop there to investigate more. After a short time more the boat will zone into OVERTHERE.

OVERTHERE is a evil aligned zoned, Gnome necros are NOT KOS here. You will see some Skeletons working thougout the city, these hit for about 140 and up (I was training mobs into the city to test this). The city has vendors of every sort here and also banking can be done here. Vendors are listed in other posts so I wont go into details about them. There are 2 Clay Golumns here that appear to guard the city but they do not attack any mobs that come running in. When in trouble you need to find the Gargoyles at the City entrance, the Guards or the Skeletons.

I didnt fight much higher level mobs, I wanted to be careful and progress slowly. I started out by hunting the yard trash in front of the city. All MOBs con's green to me, though I was able to get experience off a Scarlac (Spelling) when I didnt have my pet up. Scarlac's are wht you need to watch for here. There are several flavors of Scarlacs with the highest being Scarlac Knights, these things hit for just under 80 points. I think the lowest Scarlac is a Berzerker which you find right by the city. There is a quest involving the Berzerker, but when I turned in the Braids, I got nothing but a thank you. Verant has mentioned that some quests are messed up still in Kunark so no biggie.

If you follow the path out of the city, it will take you to the next zone, being the W???? Woods (I forgot the name of this also). Be careful on the path! There are many Scarlac Knights that roam around and though there are also wandering guards that will assist you, these things do not drop very fast. My theory so far (of what little I have seen)is that the mobs here are the same difficulty as a normal mob somewhere else, they just hit harder.

You need to turn in four braids to Captain Rotgrime. He in turn gives you a few gold and an AC 8, +10 sv_magic shield that is no-drop and is also no-rent. I gave it to my pet and it didn't show up. When equipped, it looks like a wooden kite shield with a flower on it. This zone is level 30-40 necro paradise. There are plenty of mobs, they are well spaced out and the pathing is screwy enough that when feared, mobs will run back and forth in a small area. I have also feared some mobs that ran away and never came back. You do have to be careful of the guard patrols. They kill steal just as well as their Neriak brethren.
