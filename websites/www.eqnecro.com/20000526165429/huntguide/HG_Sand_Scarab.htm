Sand Scarabs

Mob Level

9 - 11

Location

All over the zone

Spells Used

Clinging Darkness, Siphon Strength, and Lifespike

Strategy

Find a Sand Scarab that cons yellow, cast Clinging Darkness, step back a little and send in your pet. Then Siphon strength and melee it.  Cast Lifespike as necessary to regain HP.

 

I tend to melee more often than other Necros I see, speeds up the killing.  Spend my Entire 8th and 9th levels on these.  They can hit for 20 but they don't poison or anything so it is not difficult.  I completed 9th level in just under two and one half hours on these (late at night, no competition).  Only problem is everyone about 7th or 8th level hunts these, so be prepared.
