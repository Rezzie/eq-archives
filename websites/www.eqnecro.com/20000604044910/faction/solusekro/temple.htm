Where: South Ro
Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? You must find Rathmana first.  She is in a camp to the right of the zone once you zone from the Oasis of Marr.  When you find her, hail her and she will ask you if you are there at business or traveling.  Tell her that you are traveling and she will tell you about Ortallius.  Ask about Ortallius and she will tell you about him.  Once she stops, ask "What offer?" and she will make a proposal.  For 30 gold (also works with 3 plat) you can get a random spell.  Pay her the money and you get either a random spell (not necessarily a necromancer spell) or a rolled up scroll (HH quest) and experience

Faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing

Other faction standing modifiers: Lowers faction standing with the Shadowed Men
