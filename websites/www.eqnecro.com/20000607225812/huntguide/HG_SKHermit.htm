Strategy

The hermit is a good NPC to camp mainly because of the spawn rate of around 3 minutes.  Before entering the door to the hermit's house you will want to summon pet and buff yourself because of being KOS by him.

 

When he attacks cast fear this will keep him running straight into the wall which makes him a easy target for your pet. While he is feared cast dots sit back and let your pet do the work. You will want to make sure to keep fear on him, he hits for about 40+ damage and casts some minor spells.  Just be sure to keep him off of you and in the corner, because he hits fast.

 

The loot is usually around 1pp and sometimes he will be carrying fine steel weapons which sells for around 3 to 4pp depending on what kind it is.  He can also be taken at level 19 if grouped with one other person preferably a tank.

Additional Comments

Azkirte

You can go after the hermit with a pet or by yourself.

 

With pet:

Go to the back of the house near the chimney and say "Kill you."  The hermit will accept your challenge and attack or will babble about hating necros and attack.  Sic your pet on him and then throw Engulfing Darkness and Heat Blood on him.  If your pet starts to take a beating, simply Fear the hermit. He will sometimes teleport out of the house and start whacking you, so just back off and Lifetap him.


Without pet:

Get the hermit to attack you and then run outside of the compound he lives in.  Hit him with Engulfing Darkness and then Fear him.  Through all of your other DoTs on him and keep Fearing him.  Lifetap if he beats you down a little bit.
Karlov I always go in with a pet because sometimes he has a weapon, a fine steel weapon.  Since I can't see whether he is armed or not, the pet is a must.  I hit him with Darkness first, then Fear. Fear will cause him to run into the compound wall and stay  there until it wears off.  Anyway, Darkness, Fear, Heart flutter, Heat blood, and then I join my pet, back off and Siphon Life.  The hermit has a damage shield on him, but Cancel magic has yet to remove that damage shield.  The compound is the safest place to hunt.  No lions or wolves to disturb your meditating.  No Cyclops or Treants will wander over the top of you.
Madjake Just wanted to add that when fighting the Hermit, I found that if you first cast Cancel Magic on him it really helps.  I see fewer resist this way.  I hear he may have a Harmshield so this would help kill that.  I Cancel Magic first, Fear, Darkness, Heat Blood, Fear, Heart Flutter and then Fear again.

Chris

I tried this spawn and ran into a number of differences from what this hunting guide indicates:

  • I could not enter the hermit's door.  It just wouldn't open.
  • I had heard that if you go to the back of the chimney, you can say "Kill you" and he would exit.  This does work.  However, the hermit moves around inside, and he needs to be near the chimney for him to hear you there.  Running around the building and saying "I will kill you" at different spots will get him out.
  • I killed the hermit quite a few times.  He casts some minor spells -- root, flame dd, a minor dot.  He hits for 25-40+, and he hits fast.  You have to keep him feared or rooted, because he will slip your pet and go right after you.  I ended up at half hp's quite a few times.
  • At 18th level, he resisted very few spells for me.
  • The hermit drops anywhere from <1gp to over 2pp.  Usually it's around 5gp-10gp.  He also drops "Winds of Karana, page 1" (almost always, lore item), cracked flute (usually, lore item), words (usually), and a FS short sword (I only saw short swords, uncommon drop, about 1 out of 6-7 spawns).
  • He spawns every 3.5 minutes.  If you loot and immediately med, you will be full mana by the time he's ready to spawn again.  Make sure you either heal your pet, or make him sit down.  You can hold this spawn indefinitely if keep focused.
  • He always casts a damage shield as his first action upon spawning.  If you time things correctly, you can pull him out before he does this.  If he does cast the damage shield, don't bother doing a cancel magic -- I could never get it off of him (on one pull, I cancelled 6 times in a row, no resists, and he still had it on).
  • Best tactic I found (solo) was to 1) have pet attack, 2) cast darkness, 3) cast fear, 4) heart flutter, med and recast fear as necessary.  Adding heat blood will cause him to aggro on you pretty quick, and wasn't really needed.  The pet and the other two dots are all that's needed, as long as you keep fear on.
  • I was playing around with Voice Graft :-), and was able to get the hermit to be pulled by my pet and focus on him initially a couple of times.  First use I've found for this spell.
  • Running from him, and expecting darkness to work, isn't the best tactic.  He seems to cast SoW or something, because he was moving at my speed.  Darkness wasn't resisted, however.
  • I could easily solo him at 19th.  For some reason, I found it difficult at 18th -- not sure why.
  • XP is good.  Just under 1 bubble per 8 kills at 19th.  Given the spawn rate, you should be pulling in slightly less than 2 bubbles per hour.  I was averaging about 12pp in loot per hour.
  • You'll find quite a few people of 20th-25th level camping this spot.  XP (per their experience) is good all the way to 28th.
  • Watch out for the gnolls!  If one comes by and attacks, send your pet in and run for the zone.  Don't stop to look back -- he definitely is following you :-)
