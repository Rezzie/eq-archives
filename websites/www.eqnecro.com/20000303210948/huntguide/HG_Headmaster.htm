Goblin Headmaster

Mob Level

25 - 27

Location

On the Oracle Island

Spells Used

Screaming Terror, Darkness spell, Blood spell

Strategy

This is the guy you need to get a Shadowed Knife from to complete the Harvester quest.  He also drops a rune for a Magician quest.  He stands in front of a tent on the opposite side of the island from the Oracle.

 

If you are at least level 29, you can sometimes pull him without any of the surrounding Isle Goblins going aggro on you, just look out so there is no Goblin Headhunters close by.  I usually park my pet down by the water, pull with Screaming, Darkness and Boil.  Then run down and sink my pet on him when he arrive, blasting him with Poison Shock and Siphon Life..  He spawns every few minutes, and if you manage to med up in between, you can do the same thing with any Headhunter who happens to be nearby.

 

The Headmaster usually drops a few plat!  This is a good alternative if the dwarfs on Sister Island are camped!  It might even be faster..
