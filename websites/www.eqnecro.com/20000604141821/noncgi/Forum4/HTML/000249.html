The entire process will require 6 skill points.

First, you need to visit your Guildmaster as soon as you reach level 20. Put 1 point of skill into the Specialize of your choice. I would suggest Alteration as that is the most common type of spells you will be casting and includes your life-drain spells.

(For the sake of argument, I assume you pick Alteration)

Once this is done, run out and cast your lowest mana costing Alteration spell repeatedly until you see:

You have become better at Alteration (50)!

Once you reach 50 you can NOT progress until you put 1 skill point into the remaining Specialize categories. Once you have put 1 point into each of the others you will need to put 1 additional point into Specialize Alteration to get it up to 51. This makes Alteration your MAIN Specialization and when it kicks in you will spend less mana to cast these types of spells.

There is never any kind of notification that the specialize has kicked in, so don't look for one...
