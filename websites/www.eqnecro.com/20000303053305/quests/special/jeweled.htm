Jeweled Skull Quest Walkthrough

 

-> This quest still works, even after the Paw upgrade

 

 

 

Where: West Karana (map#1 - map#2)

 

Who: Yiz Pon

 

Walkthrough:

1. You must find Yiz Pon.  He wanders around the zone, but seems to be more usually found in the northeastern part of West Karana.  Having a ranger track him down for you really helps

 

2. Once you find Yiz, talk to him and he will tell you that he is running from an Assassin from Splitpaw.  He will then ask you to deliver a note to his brother in Freeport.  Just as he says that, a level 16 Assassin will appear and try to hit you

 

3. Kill the Assassin and loot the Hollowed Skull container

 

4. Take this container and the note from Yiz to his brother, Hyrill Pon in Westr Freeport.  Hyrill will ask you for 2 Ruby Eyes, one for each slot of your Hollowed Skull container

 

5. The first Ruby Eye is found in Splitpaw.  After the Paw upgrade, things might have changed a bit.  But the ruby is still here.  This is an email from a fellow Necro: "Well, one day with a fellow Necro we were clearing a path into Paw in hopes to kill some named Gnolls and get some nice loots.  We came across the Executioner, the Judges (which really like to heal each other and the Executioner) so we killed them and to my surprise, Kurpox popped a bit after we had killed Executioner and some Judges.  He pops in place of the center Judge I believe.  And there is no placeholder.   I just think he pops when you kill a certain Judge or all of the spawn.  So we killed that dirty Gnoll and looted the gem"

 

6. The second Ruby Eye is found in the Innothule Swamp with a level 32 Troll spellcaster called Lyunga.  Once again, having someone track her down can be a good idea

 

7. Once you find Lyunga, kill her and loot the second Ruby Eye (confirmed as such with Identify)

 

8. You now have both Ruby Eyes. Put them in your Hollowed Skull container and hit combine to get the Jeweled Skull
