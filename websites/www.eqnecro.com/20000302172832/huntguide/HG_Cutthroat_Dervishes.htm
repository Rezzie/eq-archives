Strategy

The Dervish Cutthroats of North Ro reside in three different camps throughout the zone commonly referred to as camps 1, 2 & 3. I will break the strategy into two parts: Solo and Group.

 

Solo: Camp #3 or Dorn's camp (DE npc who resides at the camp) is the way to go for those solo necros who have outgrown the scarabs and tarantulas in the zone. You probably don't want to try the camp solo until at least 12 as you would have a hard time taking a derv by yourself without your level 12 pet. If you are around 12 or lower I would suggest getting a partner as you will not likely be able to break the derv spawn by yourself. Two necros at level 12 can easily take this camp. The camp consists of 3 dervs and Dorn. Dorn is amiable to dark elves and will not attack those of evil alignment and will not assist the dervs either. Dorn is about level 14-17 but fights at least 5 levels above what he cons. He is very tough so don't even bother with him. The first step is to take the derv standing near the flag pole (facing the ocean) as this derv is not linked to the rest of the camp so you can safely take it alone. Just cast darkness, Heat Blood and an occasional Lifedraw and just let your pet whack away. Once you have finished of the derv, rest up and get ready for the main camp. The spawn rate is about 10 minutes so you have plenty of time to rest between kills. The two dervs who reside in the camp facing the fire are linked so if you pull one you pull the other. If you are not high enough to take both (about 14) you will need a partner to break the spawn. Once broken, you can just kill each derv as it spawns for as long as you want and rake in the experience. If you are high enough to take both, send your pet in to attack, drawing both dervs to  it. Fear one of them and concentrate on the other. You should be able to get the one derv near death before the other returns. This will help in avoiding taking both at once. Once the spawn is broken, just rest, kill, rest, kill.....

 

Group: I recommend camp #2 for grouping (Camp near undead ruins) as it contains 5 dervs and is much harder to break the spawn due to the fact that if you pull one, you pull all five of them. That could get messy if you don't have a large group. If you can get a druid to cast harmony on the camp, it could be taken by a lesser group as the other dervs won't assist. I have seen this many times and I believe harmony was the spell they were using. Once the spawn is broken, the dervs spawn rather quickly so you can get a lot of good xp here and some decent loot in a group. Camp # 3 has three dervs but unlike camp # 1, they will all assist each other. You will need at least a partner to take this camp. The loot is good at any of the camps as dervs drop bronze weapons that sell for 1-4pp(fairly rare), insignia rings that sell for 1-2 gp (common) and an occasional miscellaneous item (gemstones, raw-hide/cloth armor and beer!). The derv camps should easily get you to level 16 where, at that point, you will not find much else worth while in North Ro.

 

Good luck!
