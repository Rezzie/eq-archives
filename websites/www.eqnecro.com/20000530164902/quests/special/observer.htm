Staff of the Observers Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Observatory past the Wiindmills in Steamfont, near Ak'Anon


Who: Female gnome called Brona Frugrin, a telescope lens crafter

Walkthrough:

 

1. Look for Brona and talk to her.  You will need a good faction standing with the gnomes to obtain this quest.  Brona will ask for you to obtain the retina of an Evil Eye named Borxx that can be found in Runnyeye

 

2. Head to Runnyeye and look for Borxx, a level 30 Evil Eye.  You might have some problems finding him since he wanders around the zone

 

3. Kill Borxx and loot the lens (retina) that he drops

 

4. Head back to Steamfont and hand over the lens to Brona.  She will then give you the Staff of the Observers
