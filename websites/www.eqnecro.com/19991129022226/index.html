Welcome to the Dark Side.  The purpose of this site is to share information and advice with those who come here, and who knows, unite to kill a dragon or two.

 

The Dark Side is open for players of all races and classes, but is focused to necromancer characters.  Necromancers can find answers to the most commonly asked questions such as guild locations, spell list, and how to improve your faction standing.

 

Please enjoy this site and be aware that you might not want to see some of the information contained here.  One of the great things about EverQuest is the thrill of discovery.  But if you are like me, a little help every once in a while cannot hurt.

 

Comments, suggestions and contributions are greatly appreciated and I will make sure that you get credit for it.  I am willing to make this the best reference site for Necromancers and I can do it with your help.  Thank you for stopping by and may Norrath treat you well.  Walk in darkness my friend.

November 22, 1999

 

Greetings all.

 

Well, how can I say this?  I am no longer called Skinner.  After thinking about this for awhile, I decided to change my main character's name to Skyshade.  It just sounds nicer I guess...  So, once again I introduce myself, Skyshade Lusaris, at your service!

 

I updated the Spell List, Research Guide, and Research Tracking Sheet, both at the site and the printable versions.  There were some minor inaccuracies that had to be fixed (e.g. Suffering was described as having 2 versions and there is only one, and the level 49 spell Expel Summoned existed only in Beta).  I also made a few other changes to Spell List.  Msew sent me some corrections and I added them to the list.  As you will see, the format is a little messy right now.  I am working on a new format for that list and simply don't know what happened to the nearly done version.. Guess I will have to work on it again..  Anyway, thanks Msew!

 

Regan Rymes sent me a screenshot of him proudly using his ShadowBound items.  Looks real cool on a Gnome.  Thanks Regan!

 

Added a few new faction information.  Look for the updated factions below.

 

And I got a reply from an email I sent to GZ a few days ago.  Here is what he had to say:

"Skyshade (me): What really happened to our Drain Life spells?  Do they have a cap now?  Are they supposed to be resisted by blue/green creatures?

GZ: They should be pretty much unresistable by anything within the lvl cap 5-12 levels above you (depending on your level). There will be exceptions (creatures with high MR), but other then that, they are good.


Skyshade: Is Dead Man Floating working according to your plans? Will it work underwater in zones such as Kedge Keep?

GZ: No spell with lev will work in Kedge. Other then that, it is a fun multipurpose spell. Almost everything else in that spell can be done with another necromancer spell - but this one combines them all into one.


Skyshade: Is there a cap to your character statistics such as INT? Once it is at 200, can you still increase it?


GZ: Stats do not cap, but once you hit 200, anything over that is much less useful. (increases MUCH more slowly then the increase from 75-200).

 

Skyshade: One necromancer complaint that I tend to agree with is that your God will attack you on sight. Shouldn't he be at least indifferent to you at first?  Of course, once you attack one of his minions or himself, your faction would be greatly affected. Do you have any plans to change this?


GZ: Nope. If you walk into chat with the god of Hate, don't expect him to do anything but Hate you. I mean, if you get there, how many of his guards did you just kill (or help kill)?


Skyshade: Can you comment on the two new zones that are supposed to be implemented in Toxxulia Forest?Will we, necromancers, finally have a place where NPCs actually like us?

GZ: No comments yet - sorry. But... well, somebody might like you there.

Skyshade: What is it like to have such a cool job?


GZ: Most days it kicks ass - but, just like anything you do for a while, it is still work. That being said, I recommend it if you can get it. :)"

 

No major news as you can see.  Interesting to note that the level cap for the Drain Life spells is not a fixed 6.  I don't know if this has been changed with the latest patch or if it has always been like that.  If you ask me, Verant made the level cap more flexible to deal with the high level necro against big mobs issue.  In any case, it is good to know that a 6 level cap is not true.  And oh well, I tried to get some info about the upcoming zones.. But it looks promising.
