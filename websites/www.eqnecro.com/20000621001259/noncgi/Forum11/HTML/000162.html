I think I figured out how these guys work. I found my two froggies on an island very close to the city. Hug the right wall after you leave the city, and follow the water edge until you can see the island off to the right. It has some pillar ruins on it which you can see from the shore. There is a set of pillars at one end of the island, and I didn't go to the other end of the island, as mobs started to con yellow scowling to my level 14. Stay in the area with the pillars and a pathlike road. Near the 2nd set of pillars on the island (not the ones on the end) I found a mob called a foglock Escort. Way green, maybe level 5-6. He had a bixle pie and a flute looking thing. When killed they say something along the lines of "No! Let me finish my mission!"

So their mission must be to Escort the escaped froglocks to there base. Soon after the Escort was killed, I spotted an escaped Frog. This happened two more times (I let a monk have one of the frogs), and I got my shackles. I'd guess that the escorts are the placeholders for the escaped frogs, and the escorts are random. So I killed everything on the island to speed up their spawn. Didn't take too long to get them.

I have done the escaped frogloc thing 3 times. It took me so long because I botched the smithing I accidently stacked the bone chips (do not stack them when making bone granite powder), and then botched the embalming fluid (I added an extra rubbing alchol by accident). This is one of the most tedious quests I've done but I'm sure eveyone else wont do what I did. I did want to mention I have hunted these criters for alot of hours and I know the general place they spawn. Take the path past the newbe area until you hit the water then cross over to the next trail. Go down the trail till you reach the lone piller standing on the right side of the trail. Now this is the general area, and I have noticed some coincedences that I will note. They spawn about every 2 hours RL after killing every thing within sight. When you start see Frogloc escorts running by get ready they're about to spawn. I have only seen 2 spawn at one time one then about fifteen minutes later the other. So takem down fast and kill every frog you see. Good luck
