Darkness spell, Blood spell, Drain Life Spell, Intensify Death

Strategy

Once zoning into Mistmoore, head down the ramp to lake and go to west (area furthest from tower side against the wall.  The train action here can prove fatal if you hunt in other locations other than the west side.  From here, set your pet to guard here and then pull the various spawns around the lake back to your pet using Engulfing/Dooming Darkness.  Once your pet has engaged, use either Siphon Life if you are low on HPs or Shock of Poison to finish the off really quick.  Make sure you make use of Allure of Death as well to minimize downtime.

 

If you play smart here you can average a bubble an hour at level 29.  Make sure you bring lots of food/water/bone chips with you as there is nowhere on the island to sell for most Necromancers.

 

One final note, you may go through a lot of pets here as sometimes your pet will accumulate multiple mobs during a combat.  When this happens, dash to zone and jump all the way.  Jumping to zone is the key to good survival rate here.  You will find you can jump all the way there from your location without running out of stamina and the mobs won't catch you.

 

Good hunting!
