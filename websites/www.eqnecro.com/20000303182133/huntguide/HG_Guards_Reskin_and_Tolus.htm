Strategy

Not many people I know of kill the guards in East Commons, although it's pretty easy to do so.  You can solo them from 27 upwards if you play it safe, and I have seen a group of three level 23s killing them before with little difficulty.

 

The guards are located at the first two Inns, Reskin at the first Tolus at the second.  I personally like killing Tolus myself because it's an easier pull.  Go behind the Inn Tolus is at to the ruins at summon/park your pet there and give him a shot of Intensify Death.  Once your full of mana and ready to go, sneak up on the left or right sides of the Inn and look for Tolus.  Pull him with either Engulfing or Dooming Darkness (depends on your level) and bring him back to the ruins and your pet.  You can stop on the way to ruins and cast another DoT on him and anywhere from 2 to 4 casts of Shock of Poison.  Send your pet after him as soon as you can. He will probably still come after you because of the DoTs, but all you have to do is run out to casting range and give him a SoP or Life Drain spell until he dies.

 

With Reskin you have different options on where to park your pet, it can be in the tunnel, but you'd probably have the guard dead before then.  On a hill around the Inn, which is good.  Or at the zone into Freeport, which will give you time to kill or severely injure the guard before you reach your pet.

 

Both guards have been known to resist darkness, but just train them to your pet and then cast till it sticks.  They also resist SoP maybe one out of every 7 casts.

 

DANGER: You absolutely HAVE to make sure Sergeant Slate is nowhere around the guards when you pull them.  If by some chance you DO get Slate on your tail, run for all your worth to the nearest zone for he will kill you real fast.  Slate is level 41 and can easily take two Griffs at a time, so AVOID HIM AT ALL COSTS.
