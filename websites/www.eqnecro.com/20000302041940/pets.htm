Pet Commands

 

/pet attack - Pet will attack targeted creature

 

/pet guard me - Pet will follow you around and attack anything not friendly around you

 

/pet guard here - Pet will attack anything not friendly around his guarding post

 

/pet as you were - Pet will forget any previous commands

 

/pet back off - Pet will stop attacking targeted creature as long as it is not engaged in melee combat

 

/pet follow me - Pet will follow you around

 

/pet health report - Pet will tell you the percentage of HPs it has left

 

/pet who leader - Pet will tell you who summoned it

 

/pet sit down - Pet will sit down and heal faster

 

/pet stand up - Pet will stand up and guard the spot

 

/pet target - You will target your pet

 

/pet taunt / no taunt - Turns taunt on or off in battle (taunt is on by default)

 

/pet get lost - Pet will commit suicide

 

**Note You can press F1 twice (first selects yourself and second your pet) to select your pet. Easier than typing /pet target or wasting a hot key slot for this command.
