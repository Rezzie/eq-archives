Strategy

<EQN Note: This Hunting Guide no longer works.  Both guards have been buffed to protect newbie players>

 

These guards are hard, they have massive resistance so trying to land darkness or fear is futile, its all about pets and drains.  The good thing about these, at least on Rallos Zek is that they almost never are camped, mainly due to there massive MR.

 

First you need to be at least 2, preferably Necro/Necro or Necro/Mage.  Set up base camp on the sand, make pets (good ones), give them daggers and Augment them.  The puller watches the 2 guards until one of them runs after one of the many critters in the area (happens a lot), then he pulls the other back to his partner and the waiting pets.  Send pets and start to drain, pact your pet (he is going to need it), continue until dead.  The trick here is mana management so fight with Call of Bones running.  If you have a manastone, use it before every drain to minimize downtime, balance your downtime with how much damage the pet takes.

 

If you pull both guards by mistake, run to the nearby zone to East Freeport.  If you are more than 2 or very good there is a 3rd guard named Brendyl a bit down the road with exact the same stats, so basically there is never a shortage of kills.
