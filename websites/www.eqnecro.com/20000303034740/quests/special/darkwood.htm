Darkwood Staff Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro


Who: Sultan

Walkthrough:

 

1. This is a pretty straightforward quest.  The person who gives this quest is an Enchanter, but a Necromancer can still talk to him

 

2. Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro and look for Sultan

 

3. Once you find Sultan, talk to him and he will tell you that to get the staff, you must 

bring him a Lambent Stone from a Hill Giant, a Sand Giant, or a Griffin

 

4. These creatures can be found in several different places, including West Commonlands, Oasis of Marr, South Karana, North Karana, etc..

 

5. Lambent Stones are a rare drop, so you might have to kill a couple of them to get yourself one.  Once you do, return to the Temple of Solusek Ro and give it to Sultan for your Darkwood Staff
