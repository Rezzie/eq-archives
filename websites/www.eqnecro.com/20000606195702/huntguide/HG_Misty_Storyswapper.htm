Spells Used

Lifedraw, Heat Blood, Engulfing Darkness, Fear

Strategy

You have to use a bit of savvy at level 12 to solo her correctly because she can be as many as 4 levels red to you.

 

First off...summon a GOOD pet.  I don't care if you have to spend 20 bone chips...you NEED it.  Start a good distance away and cast Engulfing Darkness then send in your pet.  If the darkness doesn't hold, don't recast just cast your heat blood.  After a couple of seconds, fear her.  This works most of the time even on deep reds.  Then to help out your pet, start sucking her dry.  If she comes for you, use what mana you have to heat her blood back up and hope you don't die..=)  This is where you use your knowledge...Don't be stupid and just sit there and take the blows or else you will die quickly.  She hits in the high 30's and at level 12, you can't take many of those...but once you get a pattern down...she is WELL worth the XP.

 

I have been bugged about this spot for awhile now so I'll tell you.  It is NEVER camped and she always drops a Mandolin and upwards of 3 gold.  The mandolin can be sold for about 12 gold (depending on your charisma) and they weigh absolutely NOTHING. (0.0).  So grab some backpacks and bring a sack lunch with you and sit on her for a day.  Once my Necro hits level 12, I go and sit on her from the morning to the afternoon...one day I went from 12 to 16 in 8 hours of gameplay so she is WELL worth it.

Saduja (Bristlebane)

I found that at level 12, Engulfing Darkness and Fear were not a good idea. They were resisted FAR too much. In fact, 6 out of 7 Fears were resisted. Here is what I did. If she is yellow, go ahead and try to pull with Engulfing and Fear. If this does not affect her, revert to "If she is red". Then Heat Blood and let the pet eat at her from behind. Throw in an occasional Lifetap. If she is red, DO NOT try to Fear or Engulfing Darkness her. Just Leach her to pull, Heat Blood her, and use the level 12 Lifetap on her. Yes, it is mana intensive. Yes, you will probably have to jump in and share damage with your pet. But she is about a 7 minute spawn, more than enough time to fully recover. After switching to this, she was very easy for a deep red.

Gnuggalumpus

I have hunted Misty now for far longer than I care to admit. When you are a level 12 hunting Misty, don't even think about casting fear or darkness. The thing to remember is that when she cons red, and you are a mighty bright green level 12, a failed darkness spell can basically screw you for the entire battle since heat blood gobbles up so much mana (and is indispensable). Even if darkness fails only 1 out of 3 times, that is one battle you stand a very good chance of losing. Also, fear is just a little too unstable for my taste. First, the spell has to take hold and then it has to take hold long enough to make any kind of difference. The few times fear actually worked on her, she took a few steps in panic before returning to me quickly with renewed fury.

 

When I was 12, I did the following. I walked out far enough so that I could cast spells on her. I then sent my pet even further back. I hit ole Misty with HB and then ran like hell passed my pet. I had the pet suitably distanced so that once she was really closing in on me, he stepped in and interrupted her pursuit. Even though pets may not be able to continually taunt a critter, the first attack always seems to work and further serves to slow critter down for a few more valuable seconds (heat blood!). I then fire off leach, poison, and weaken (!) one right after the other and close in to fight (very important note: I had a runed totem staff but with no armor to speak of).

 

I have no idea how much poison and weaken contributes, but I always use them (as a matter of good taste) and I never died to a red Misty at 12. My pet rarely died. It is certainly possibly I would have defeated her without them, but they are just too much fun to use. Also, weaken is a perfect timer for when she will spawn next. As soon as your stolen strength disappears, she pops up.

 

Once you get past 13 (and especially in the later levels), this bard is extremely easy. When I hit level 14, I used engulfing darkness as a pull, but it was more out of boredom than anything else.

 

One final point: I use strafing a lot in EQ. I have no idea how much difference this makes (if any at all), but I circle strafed Misty quite a bit which, combined with my pets attacks and taunts, seemed to freak her out a bit, making her constantly jump around and change directions. Again, this could have ZERO to do with my success with Misty, but you might want to experiment on your own.

Aegean
I started hunting Misty at level 12 thanks to the posts on this board.  The first time it did not go very well, but once I established a pattern it was extremely easy.  I went from level 12 to 15 in one day at this spawn.
 
Here is how I did it.  Turn off Pets Taunts.  Pull with Heat Blood and run back to top of hill.  Send Pet in.  Cast Leach.  Back up a couple steps if she getting close and cast Heat Blood again.  Run along hill a few more steps to keep her away from you and cast Leach again.
 
Then charge because if you dont your pet is going to die soon.  So take some hits and try to lifedraw.  At level 12 you have no mana left for lifedraw..so just cast poison or something and swing your staff at her.  You will likely win.
