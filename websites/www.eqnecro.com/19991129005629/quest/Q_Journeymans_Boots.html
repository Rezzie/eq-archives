Where: Rathe Mountains
Who: Hasten Bootstrutter
Walkthrough:

1. You must find Hasten.  He walks around Rathe Mountain with no specific location

2. Once you find Hasten, talk to him and he will tell you that he could give you his extra pair of Journeyman's Boots if you gave him a Ring of the Ancients, a Shadowed Rapier, and 3,250 GP

3. The Ring of the Ancients is found in South Karana, the Ocean of Tears, and in the desert are of South Ro (possibly among other areas) with an Ancient Cyclops.  The Ancient Cyclops is a rare spawn and is around level 32.  If you find and kill him, he will drop the ring

4. The Shadowed Rapier can be found in any Shadowed Man camp.  Hunt the Shadowed Men until one drops the rapier

5. Finally, the 3,250 GP can be found... err... at your bank account.  I am not sure if it has to be gold pieces or if you can use platinum pieces instead

6. Give all three items to Hasten and you get the Journeyman's Boots
