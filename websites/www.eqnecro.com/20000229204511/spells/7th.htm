Lifebane - Well with Kedge Keep being opened now this spell finally has real usage. Was mainly corpse recovery in Lake Rathe or Dagnor's Cauldron before. Just keep them Fish Scales Handy. Note: Like most of our spells, this can be cast on self only.

Resist Cold

Skill : Abjuration 

Mana : 50

Speed : 4.5

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Adds 16 points to target's resistance versus cold attacks

Comments:

Skyshade - A good spell in areas such as Permafrost and a spell that makes you quite popular over there as you can cast Resist Cold on other players. 
Lifebane - This spell is an upgrade to the level 4 spell Endure Cold. It provides the recipient with a sizable amount of protection vs cold using creatures and cold based attack spells. This is one of the few Necromancer spells that can be cast upon other people and most are very appreciative of it when they see the effects. This is a must have for traveling to places such as Permafrost or when fighting casters that like to toss cold based spells.  



Rest the Dead

Skill : Abjuration 

Mana : 75

Speed : 3.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Lowers an undead creature's reaction

Comments:

Skyshade - The upgrade to the level 4 Numb the Dead spell, Rest the Dead will reduce the chances that an undead creature will attack you. You can cast it to, for example, head deeper inside an undead dungeon such as Unrest, but I would not recommend it. The last thing you want is to meet with all those undead critters when running back to a zone. What you can and should do is cast this spell when soloing 2 undead creatures at the same time. By lowering the reaction of one, you can safely kill the second undead and be ready for the one you just calmed once the effects of Rest the Dead wear off.
Lifebane - Well Skyshade pretty much summed it up for me as well. Good spell for breaking an undead camp spawn up.
