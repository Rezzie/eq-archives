quote:
Originally posted by Raz:
As someone mentioned, head to Sister Isle in OOT. Bring all the backpacks you can carry and fill em up.


I have to echo that sentiment. You will make some *nice* coin for your lvl at Sister Isle. I lived there form lvl 13 through mid lvl 16 and made at least 300pp. Not much exp at lvl 16 but I couldn't tear myself away from the money machine.

Being there no safe and convenient zone line to run to, it is risky. Gate is the only thing that will save you if you get in over your head and can get it off in time. Be careful. They will assist each other. You can use Fear if you are careful. Park your pet as close to the water as possible and pull to there. The Sisters will take off up the hill when they are hurt or Feared, so kill them quick.

They almost always drop a Purity Belt and, if Verant hasn't changed it, Words for lvl 16, 20 and 24 lvl Spells. Lvl 24 Words? From a lvl 13(max) MOB? Oh yah...
