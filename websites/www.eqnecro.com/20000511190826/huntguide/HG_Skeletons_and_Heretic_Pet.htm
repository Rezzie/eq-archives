Strategy

First take out the Heretic Pet that spawns just off the platform as it comes to check on 'its' miners from time to time and will attack you if you are fighting them.  Then sit your pet on the porch.  I found mine had real trouble going into the tunnel from outside, and if you are in there and try to make it attack it will often run in the exact opposite direction leaving you stranded.  Then walk in and pull the lowest conning skeleton.  Both will come, but with you and your pet working together on one skellie (only tell your pet to attack once you run onto the porch) and tanking one each, you should be able to take both of them no probs, and if you can't there is a zone just past where they spawn!

 

Then med and rest until the Heretic spawns again and do it all again.

 

Using this strategy levels 8 and 9 will pass quickly, then you can move off to BB or Kerra to make money before new spells at level 12.
