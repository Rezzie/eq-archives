Strategy

This lady is one tough cookie.  To get to her, ride the boat from Freeport to Butcherblock, jumping off the boat before you get to the dock at the Sister island.  The sister at the end of the dock will pound you flat.  Once you are on the island, stay near the shore to avoid the attention of the lower level sisters.  You will find her standing on the shoreline by herself.

 

Make sure you have a good pet, and pull her away from where she spawns.  Pull all the way back to where there is a small piece of island sticking out.  Make sure you pull with Darkness to slow her down, so you have a chance to get your other DOTs on her.  Get your pet on her and get away from her.  She is fast.  Use Life Tap on her, it casts faster than Siphon Life, so you have a slightly better chance to get it off.  Use Fear with the utmost caution, she will wander into the center of the island and bring all the lower level sisters to you.  Be sure you know what is around you.  The lower level sisters, if they get too close, will join in.  If this happens, Gate or Feign.  They may also try to heal her.

 

Her loot can range from several silver to 2 - 3 platinum.  She almost always carries a Purity Belt too, worth another plat at a friendly vendor.  She is a very tough mob, so make sure you have full buffs loaded and you are paying attention.  If it gets too bad, try to run away from her some and see if your pet can get her off.  She will usually go straight for you, no matter how hard your pet tries to taunt her off.  Expect to finish most fights real low on health.
