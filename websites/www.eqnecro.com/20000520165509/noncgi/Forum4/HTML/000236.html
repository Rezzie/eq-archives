Avocets are probably more than 6 levels higher than you. Any MOB that much higher will resist any spell you throw at it.

Drain type spells (Shadow Vortex, Siphon Strength and the Lifetap series) are unresistable as long as they are within level range. If any of those spells are resisted, feign, zone, or hope that your group can kill it because there is NOTHING you can do to help except maybe your pet. (which will probably hit for 5 damage every 10 seconds. heh.)
