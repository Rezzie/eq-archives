Strategy

You will need another necro buddy to wait down by the bridge, you go near the HighPass Hold marker, (closest one to the bridge), and wait for Sir Morgan to leave Wimbley. Pull Wimbley down to the bridge where your buddy and you send your pets on him and you nuke, make sure your buddy saves his mana. Keep allure of death on so you regenerate mana fast cause Wimbley is a 1 minute spawn and he will run back up to Sir Morgan once he spawns. Take turns killing him, with you both sending pets on him every time to save mana. Good Luck!
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Karana Residents, Guards of Qeynos, Priests of Life, and Knights of Thunder
