Strategy

Soloing a Tentacle Terror is not a such a hard task at level 20+.  You might even be able to do it before level 20, but the problem is two Tentacle Terrors spawn in the same area most of the times.  So, to hunt them solo, you will have to break the spawn.  Don't worry though, it is not a difficult thing to do.  Tentacle Terrors have a very long spawn rate (20 minutes) and don't spawn every time.  Every now and then, a Skeleton takes his place and you must kill it to restart the spawn.

 

So, how does one breaks the spawn, kills the Tentacle Terror and lives to tell the tale?  To answer that, I will divide this Guide into "Hunting in Small Room" and "Hunting in Large Room".

 

Hunting in Small Room:

Breaking the spawn in the small room is not as complicated as you might imagine.  Granted, it is not easy but since it is close to the exit, you have time to run in case things don't go as smooth as planned.

 

The best way to break any two-spawn is to take one of them as fast as possible while keeping the second one out of the battle.  Here is how you do it.  Go invisible to small room (if you are level 20+, the Skeletons on your way shouldn't bother with your presence).  Just before you walk to the room is the spawning place of an Ogre Guard.  He usually sits there, guarding both the entrance to small room and passage to the rest of the dungeon.  But he does walk around a bit, going to large room and back.  So, make sure you kill him before going to small room.

 

Once you are done with it, go to small room and cast a pet if you don't have one yet.  You can cast it close to wall, opposite to the first Tentacle Terror.  He won't mind your presence if you are close to the wall.  Med up and cast Darkness on the first one (the one you can see while meding at wall) and send your pet to the second one (inside a little room).  Your pet should hold one of the Terrors while you fight the first one.  As soon as you cast, run to zone.  The first Terror will start casting a funny burn spell that throws you around when hit.  Keep going to zone and stop at the opening close to zone to cast DoTs.  By the time you get close to zone, the Terror should be a little hurt but you still have a long way to go.  Now is the hard part of the battle.  You will have to fight it without your pet.  That is not impossible though as the Terror stops every now and then to cast a spell, giving you a chance to cast.  Try as much as you can to stay at a safe distance and cast Drain Life spells as fast as you can.  Also, don't try to melee the Terror as it hits hard.  Whenever you can't get away from it, Feign Death and let it take some DoT damage.  Get up and Drain.  Keep doing that until the first Terror is dead.  Make sure you fight close to zone as you might need to run.  Also, the second Terror will kill your pet so you have to kill the first one fast or zone.

 

Once the spawn is broken, it is an easy ride.  Simply pull with Darkness, stack your DoTs and Drain.  Your pet will do a good job taunting the Terror and you shouldn't be hit until the Terror is nearly dead.  Now you have the room all for yourself and with a 20 minutes spawn, you have plenty of time to kill the Terrors and meditate.

 

Hunting in Large Room:

Breaking the spawn in the large room is a little different.  The two Tentacle Terrors in this room are not next to each other and you can hit one without pulling the other.

 

To get to large room, go invisible and head to room that leads to small room.  Take the left way and head past the Elementals.  The corridor will open into a large room with one Tentacle Terror on each side and the gate with Najena's saying.  Another Ogre Guard roams this room and you should kill it before attempting to take the Terrors.  Now head to this gate but do not cross it.  Instead, turn left or right  and you will get to one of the room's corner.  Cast a pet, med and send your pet to the Terror closest to you.  Do not cast before you send the pet.  Once your pet is fighting the Terror, move in closer to the battle and make sure it stays where it spawns. Whenever the Terror gets low on life he starts to run to gate.  When that happens, you either kill it fast or he will pull a train.  So, Drain him fast and if you see he is about to die but a train is on the way, go invulnerable and head to zone.  In any case, you should have broken the spawn and can return to take the second one.

 

Just like the small room, once the spawn is broken it is easy XP.  Pull the Terror with Darkness, send in your pet, and Dot it.  About halfway through the battle, the Terror will come after you.  This is actually good since you can get it far from the gate guarded by the Magician.  Pull him to the back of the room and Drain, Drain Drain.  Remember, Terrors run when low on life so make sure you kill it before he gets too close to gate or the Magician will come after you with her pet.

 

That's about it.  Hunting Terrors is pretty good if you get a room for yourself.  Since it is a long spawn, sharing slows down the XP too much.  Note that I am not saying you shouldn't share.  You should but it is sometimes better to look for something else to kill.  Terrors drop up to 3 or 4 plat as well as gems and the Tentacle Whip, which can be sold for around 15/20 plat.  Good hunting!
