Character Level

6 to 7

Solo/Group

Solo

Zone

East Commons

XP

8

Loot

3

Danger

5

Mob

Puma

Mob Level

5 - 6

Location

Desert side of zone

Spells Used

Clinging Darkness, Blood, Poison Bolt, Fear, Lifespike

Strategy

1) Summon a pet (At least blue)

2) Find a puma

3) Cast Clinging Darkness on it

4) Tell the pet to attack it as it runs for you

5) Cast Poison Bolt

You can then choose the next step:

 

6a) Cast fear on it, and you and your pet chase it round the zone, laying the smack down on it.

 

6b) My favorite (and thus more detailed). Once both the dots have been cast, just into Melee. make sure the puma is attack you, as the pet won't last long. Casting Lifespike when you health starts getting down, you and the pet should easily be able to melee the puma, and do it pretty quickly with the DoTs help. The best thing is, that there isn't too much downtime if you do it well.
