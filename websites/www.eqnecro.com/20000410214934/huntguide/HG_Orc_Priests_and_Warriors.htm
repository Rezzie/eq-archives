Strategy

From North Ro zone into Oasis along the Mountains. As soon as you zone in move a couple of steps forward. Summon pet and make him guard that position. Just over the ridge ahead of you is where numerous Orcs wander.

 

Just pick one of these Orcs and apply Darkness.  Go back to pet and get the pet on him.  Heart Flutter, Leach, Vampiric Embrace then go in and melee.  If you get low on health back, away and apply Lifedraw.  For the pesky Priests save enough for 2 Lifedraws.  They heal themselves pretty good and seem to have ALOT of mana.
