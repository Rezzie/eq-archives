Mob

Teschene Gnolls

Mob Level

28 - 30

Location

-3200  +800

Spells Used

Dooming Darkness, Boil Blood, Vampiric Curse, Spirit Tap, Fear, Intensify Death

Strategy

Find one of the 3 claws (if you can find a claw that isn't camped) or just behind the entrance to Paw on the North/northwest side to camp.  This place is occupied by groups of people but no nearly as camped as the Treants.

 

Cast pet and med till full.  I have found that Screaming Terror is heavily resisted here, so I no longer use it.  Cast Intensify Death buffing your pet, Allure yourself in you prefer, and pull with Dooming Darkness.  Send pet, cast Boil Blood from a distance and then cast Fear.  If Fear is resisted you have about one more chance before the Gnoll will forget about your pet and attack you.  If Fear holds, Tap him till he is dead or let the pet beat him and give him a Tap to get the kill and replenish what little life Allure has taken.  If Fear doesn't hold, turn and run....the pet will get his attention again.  Then try Fear again.  I cast Boil Blood before Fear most of the time, because it can really get him as he is trying to chase you and your pet and DoT's are getting him since he won't reach you because of Darkness.  You may get a resist on the Darkness.  If so, send pet and recast.  It rarely is resisted more than once here.

 

Again, watch out of groups forcing soloist out of their camping spots. It sucks and they may try (speaking from experience).  You can out damage a 3 person, non-nuking, group (druid, Paladin, and cleric in my case).  But, in the long run, will outlast you when it comes to downtime.  Also watch out for multiple spawns, up to 3 at a time here, but they will walk around and out of range soon enough.  I currently do not have Vampiric Curse, thus cannot cast it here, but I would use after Fearing the mob.

 

Good luck and safe hunting.

Additional Comments
Siphonz D`rkPain (Rallos Zek) I have my 2 cents worth on camping these Gnolls that I would like to share. The way that is presented here does not work as advertised, or at least not unless you have massive mana to waste on resists. The following is about the best strategy I have found for killing these pesky furballs.

First of all let's set up our spells. I use siphon life because of the faster cast time, (when Darkness wears off these Gnolls can hit really hard. It is nice to have a quick and easy way to heal up. Also its cheaper and casts faster when you have one mighty pissed Gnoll in your face). I use Engulfing  darkness mostly for quicker cast time and because dooming darkness is resisted a lot and it burns up mana really quick. Last but not least is shock of poison. At 29 its doing full damage so fire at will. Optional spells I used were Vampiric Curse, ( in case I got beat down and needed some quick health), and either siphon strength or shadow vortex.

Ok on to the fighting strategy. Turn pets taunt to no taunt.  Find a nasty Gnoll and cast darkness, if resisted send in the pet. Cast until darkness sticks. Once darkness takes, blast with shock of poison, that will get the Gnolls attention. Proceed to run in a straight line, all the while your skelly is ass kiting the Gnoll. Once you can get a little distance between you and the Gnoll blast em again with SoP, repeat this process until dead. NOTE> I have noticed that after about the 2nd to 4th cast your original darkness has worn off. Recasting another darkness can be the difference in life or death or at minimal longer down to heal as well as med. I have found this system to work very well indeed, although I wouldn't suggest trying solo until 29.
