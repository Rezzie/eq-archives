Concordance of Research Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: South Ro

Who: Smith Tv'ysa

Walkthrough:

 

1. Before heading to South Ro, make sure you have with you a Greater Lightstone and a few Lightstones (4 or 5 should do it).

 

2. Now head to South Ro.  If zoning from Oasis of Marr, you should run South and look for Smith Tv'ysa.  She is not very far from zone and you should have no problem finding her.  She is inside a small tent.  Once you find her, hail and this is the conversation that follows:

 

Tv'ysa: I am Tv'ysa, guardian of the [Concordance of Research].

You:  What Concordance of Research?

Tv'ysa: The Concordance of Research is an aid to those who follow the path academia. It, and its sister books Runes and Research Volumes I and II help those who are [Interested] in researching spells.

You: I am interested

Tv'ysa: I have several copies of each book. If you want a copy of Runes and Research Volume I or II, bring me a Lightstone and I will give you whichever book I have more of at the moment. Bring me a Greater Lightstone, and I will give you a copy of the Concordance of Research.

 

2. Give her a Greater Lightstone and you will get the Concordance of Research in return.

 

3. Now you must give her the Lightstones, one at a time.  She will randomly give you one of the two Research Volumes.  Keep giving her the Lightstones until you get both books.

 

4. Once you get the Concordance of Research, you will improve your faction standing with Dread Guard Outer, Dread Guard Inner, and Dark Bargainers.  You will also gain a little XP.
