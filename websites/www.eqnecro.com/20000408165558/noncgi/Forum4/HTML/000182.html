Zombie 		posted 02-22-2000 15:52            
The tower in Lake Rathe has a mage with earth pet and a wizard. They spawn pretty fast. The mage has around 400hp and the wizard around 300. Soloing them is tough though because they are hard to interrupt and the spells the use hit hard. The wizard stops giving exp at level 28 but the mage is a little higher. These were pretty good exp from 26 to 28 though. I camped this spot with a ranger and at level 26 we had little problem. Use harmony to break the spawn. Pull the mage and her pet then kill the mage fast. After that I terrored the pet to erase the ranger from its list. Then I attacked it and feigned death to get it to suicide. Then run into the tower and kill the wizard. Now med and heal. If you did this correctly you have them broken up and from now on you can camp inside the tower and kill them as they spawn (no pet) easily. The book drops off the mage and is very common. I would say you get it 1 out of every 2 on average. Btw, no heretic faction for killing these :-( Oh well.

When could you solo them? Hhhmm. You might be able to do it at 29 but it would be risky. I would try to send pet in after the mage. Streaming terror the Wizard. Now if the mage pet is after you terror or fear it. If not then just Shock of Poison the mage. With pet hitting her 2 Shock of Poisons might be enough to finish her. Now leave the pets to fight and take out the wizard. At 29 you will resist his spell a lot (I did a 28) and 2 SoP should take him. Then help your pet take her pet and the spawn will be your. It sounds easy but 3 or 4 blasts from the mage will easily kill you if you don't resist them so be careful.

Zombie 		posted 02-23-2000 07:18         
You CAN solo this tower at 24. Screaming Terror one, sick your pet on the mage pet (alternately, fear the mage pet), and nuke the other with SoP. I wouldn't suggest it though. In the best case scenario, its extremely painful (I finished with 1 bubble of health and oom), and the risk of death is very high. I tried it once just to see if I could, got cocky and died 2 times trying to repeat it.

Came back with a buddy necro (also 24) and the tower was cake. You'll take some hits and may have to feign, but you should easily take them down. They have very low HPs.

The book however, will drop of either caster and is far from a common drop. We had to clear the tower 11 times to get my book. Then had 3 books drop in the next 5 spawns. We were shouting for people to come get them before they decayed.

Of all the books for the Words of Darkness, this is the easiest to get. I'm guessing the above caster is correct that at 29 you could easily punk both these guys.

FYI: at 24 in a duo they are EXCEPTIONAL xp.

--Nergal
