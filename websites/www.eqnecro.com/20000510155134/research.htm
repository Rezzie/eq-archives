Greetings Fellow Necromancer,

 

In order to start researching, you will have to be at least level 16 and own a Book of Dark Bindings.  This book works just like a jewelry kit for example.  You add the words (scroll like piece of papers) and click combine.  There you go, you either have a new spell or you lost the words you have been looking for in the last few weeks.  The Diablerie books have a description for the spell components, but in order to read it you must learn the language the book was written in.  Below is the translation of these books.  For a description and location of each spell please refer to the Spell List section.  

 

Enjoy.
