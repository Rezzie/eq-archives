Boil Blood, Fear, Drain Life spell

Strategy

I suggest before you do this, decide if you want to mindlessly camp this spot - bring lots of food/water, and bind yourself anywhere on the spires, they don't KOS.

 

Jump off the boat coming from Freeport when you see two spires that you go between on the boat.  Swim to either one of them, avoiding the Aqua Goblins, what a nuisance (who do KOS if you're too close)!

 

If you are level 12 try the island that has only one siren (the island with the Aqua Goblins) before you take on two at a time.  The XP is great all the time and almost no chance of dying once you figure the strategy out.  I couldn't get enough kills doing what I was doing before so I came here at level 12 and got to 16 before the experience was bad.  The catch is that you have to boringly camp them, with only about a 5 minute spawn time maybe less, and you can swim from island to island to kill all three of them.

 

Taking on one: start with Heat blood, then /pet attack right away.  That should do it, but if your pet is hurt give him a hand at first and mend him or Drain the siren after fighting it (don't do it too long they hit for 25 or so damage).  If you really want to be safe, back away then try Fear, it usually works fine.

 

Getting back on the boat:

Since you might be bound on the island the trip is a lengthy one back.  You have to watch for the boat to come to the spires and go underneath it - not directly underneath at the spine but at a slight angle, and swim up through the bottom of the boat.  That's how you get back on it.  Or if you don't think you will die much, bind yourself on the dock in east Freeport.  Then you will just have to wait for the one-way boat trip when you die.

Additional Comments
Fistandatilus, Rathe

There are 2 locations in OOT, with different levels of sirens.

 

The first group is on the tall spire rocks you pass between on the boat from FP to BB, near aviak island. These sirens are level 12.

 

The second group is on the rocks you pass between on the boat from BB to FP, near BB zone edge. There are 2 groups of sirens here, 2 level 12s, and 3 level 13s.

 

The level 12 sirens have about 200 hit points, and are blue until level 17.  At level 18 they are green (but still give minor XP).  At level 19, they no longer give XP. 

 

The level 13 sirens have about 300 hit points, and are blue until level 18. At level 19 they are green (but still give minor XP).  At level 20, they no longer give XP.

 

The level 13 sirens cannot be hit with normal weapons, however the level 12
sirens can be. (I had to switch to using my Dagger of Marnek to hit the level 13s.)
Naalina, Prexus My tatics were as follows.

Set my pet to guard beside me just within casting range. I'd then cast Clinging Darkness and send in my pet. Now I would cast poision bolt and step into melee myself. 

This is the hard part. The sirens hit for 20+ damage. You need to time the sirens attacks and cast LifeSpike as quick as you can. If you get in trouble, back up and let your pet tank again while casting LifeSpike. After the fight, cast Dark Pact and med to full. If your still low on health, you can drag the aqua goblin at the top of the hill and just use LifeSpike to heal.

The only real problem you will fun into is if you try to use the DoT Fear tatic. Your pet will chase the siren and if he gets near the aqua goblins you'll have quite a fight on
your hands. Seeing as how the goblins can still be red at the time.
