25

Location

Spells Used

Intensify Death, Darkness spell, Blood spell, Fear, Shadow Vortex, Shock of Poison, Drain Life spell

Strategy

This is how I do kill him.

1. Darkness (THIS IS A MUST!!)

2. Send in pet (pet taunt is on)

(regardless of darkness taking hold on 1st cast)

3. Back off to max cast range

4. Heat/Boil Blood (wait 5 sec)

5. Shadow Vortex (if resisted cast once more)

6. Fear (as necessary) DO NOT TRY TO TANK THIS GUY!!

7. Shock of Poison (as necessary)

8. Life Drain (as necessary)

9. 1 dead ranger = minimum 11pp and good XP

 

Glyndur dual wields Long Sword and an Axe. He can hit 4 times in one round for max of 54 each, bashes and kicks. DO NOT TRY TO TANK THIS GUY!!  He can drop Leatherskin in one round and still do some damage to you if you let him get to close.

**=dropped everytime


My pet (level 24) pet is normally at one quarter health, and I have approx 2 bubbles of mana and full bar of health after the fight.  My 29 pet normally finishes the fight at half health and I have about 2 to 2 1/2 bubbles of mana left.  Allure of death to help mana regeneration, and get ready to fight again.  Spawn time is Approx. 6 minutes.

 

Another good thing about this hunting area is that Thistle Underbrush passes close by and is a fairly easy kill if you are careful at lower levels.  Bind by one of the Druid rings, either Butcher Block or Steamfont and sell there about every 6 to 10 kills depending on your strength/weight carrying capacity.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Merchants of Kelethin, Merchants of Felwithe, and Emerald Warriors.  Raises faction standing with Indigo Brotherhood.

Disorder De'Enraged He is a level 25 Wood Elf Ranger that spawns every 6 min. He spawns at Location- 264, 1267.  Don't sit right on his spawn, its best to sit back up on the path.  He spawns in a little ditch.  He does not cast but he does have double attack and hits from on average 30-55 (ouch for a caster).  I could take him with a partner at level 20 (a fellow Necromancer, and once or twice with a druid) ALWAYS send the pets in first, then cast your Dots then FEAR him... he will run up the hill behind you, occasionally over the hill but not often.  He will often Agro on you after 5 casts on him, without much delay in between casts. He won't agro if u delay 5 seconds between casts. After you DOT him just Drain Drain Drain until his little Wood Elven ass falls to the ground.  He drops a sword that sells for 10pp (with good faction) and an axe that sells for 2pp. You can begin to solo him at level 25. (level 24 he resists to much) Always have feign death memorized. There is a cool trick that once u feign death get back up, walk back real quick and he is already off Agro on you. He gives great EXP and Loot... don't group on this guy, just partner or solo. And if you are a Necro you are already KOS with dirty elves... I have been camping Glyndur Tindel in LFay for the last 2 levels for XP mostly, but also for the loot he drops.  Every time you kill him he used to drop a long sword that sells for approx 9 or 10 pp, an axe that sells for 2 to 3 pp, a bead necklace that sells for 3 silver, and wood elf meat.  In total, you would get a minimum of 12 or 13 pp in loot for every single kill.  Add to this he drops anywhere from a few gold to 3 or more plat in change too.  Well, after this latest patch he now drops the necklace and meat ONLY.  He no longer drops weapons and the change he gives now ranges from 1 silver (common) to 2 plat max (very rarely).  Obviously, Verant decided to kill this cash cow for reasons akin to the Butcherblock dwarf loot nerf, but who knows.  One weird thing is that he still hits just as hard with his fists as he ever did when dual wielding weapons - so I guess the notion of being "disarmed" doesn't apply to him hehe.
