Agility

  This stat helps in a few different ways.  First it helps you dodge more often in combat ( you get the dodge skill at level 22).  It also raises your armor class as well as making you harder to hit in melee combat.
   

Charisma

  This will help the way most NPC merchants in the game view you.  The more they like you the cheaper your buying prices will be and the higher you will be able to sell you items for.  There is also a rumor that this skill directly effects how long charm based spells last when casted upon on a monster (i.e screaming terror, charm undead, dominate undead)
   

Dexterity

  This stat helps a weapon's effect to be casted while in combat.  If you use the stiletto of the bloodclaw, this helps the spell go off in combat.  This stat also helps you be more accurate in melee combat.  There is a rumor that says that this helps you recover while casting.  That is not true.
   

Intelligence

  Intelligence has several functions.  First, this is where all of your mana will come from.  The higher this stat the more mana you will have. Once you get your Intelligence to 200 your mana ratio drops to about 1/1.  So its pretty useless to go higher than that. Second, this along with your casting skill will determine how often your spell fizzles. Third, this attribute helps you progress faster in practicing skills.  Wisdom will determine how successful you are at a skill but Intelligence will determine the rate that you progress.
   

Stamina

  Main function of this stat determines your hit points.  It also helps with your physical stamina.  I.E running, swimming, jumping.  The higher this stat the longer you can do these activities.
   

Strength

  Strength has two main functions.  First, this will add to your max damage dealt with a melee weapon.  The higher this is the higher your max damage.  Now we really have no need for higher melee damage b.c you shouldn't be in melee that much.  Second, this is what sets your max encumbrance.  Encumbrance is the weight allowance of your character.  The higher this stat, the more weight you can carry.
   

Wisdom

  This stats main use to us, is how often you are successful on a skill practice.  This also helps us resist spells like fear and the like.
