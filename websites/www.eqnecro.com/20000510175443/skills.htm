Greetings!

 

Just a reminder.  Once you attain a skill at a given level, you must spend one training point on that skill to make it useable.  For example, once you attain 4th level you must put one training point into meditation in order to be able to use that skill.

Level Skill Description
1 Abjuration One of the several skills related to the art of magic, abjuration deals with defensive magic. 

(example spell :  Lesser Shielding)
1 Alcohol  Tolerance The ability to consume alcohol without the drunken effects.  Now you are probably asking yourself why should I work on this.  Well besides staggering around Norrath making an ass of yourself, drinking alcohol temporarily raises your strength and stamina.

(example of skill : Drinking Ale)
1 Alteration Another of the several skills related to the art of magic, alteration deals with changing things, whether it be illusion or an actual physical change.

(example spell : Gate)
1 Baking

Allows you to make a wide array of different edible treats.

(example item : Rat Sandwich)

Necessary Item : Oven
1 Bind Wound Gives you the ability to bandage your wounds.  This can take quite a few seconds to do and you can only heal yourself to 50% health. This is quite a useful skill to have know.  The best thing to do is to practice with summoning bandages.

Necessary Item : Bandages
1 Blacksmithing

This allows you to make all kinds of different weapons and armor.  

(example item: Banded armor)

Necessary Item : water, forge, 
1 Brewing

Allows you to produce potent alcoholic beverages.  You will be the life of all events by distributing free alcohol.  Also you need this skill to make an item for Cured silk armor.

(Examples of skill :  Mead)

Necessary Item : Still
1 Channeling One of the several skills related to the art of magic, channeling allows the caster to decrease the amount of time necessary to cast a spell, regardless of its nature. 
1 Conjuration Another of the several skills related to the art of magic, conjuration brings something from elsewhere into the world, whether they be elemental creatures or powerful clouds of energy. 
1 Divination Another of the several skills related to the art of magic, spells of divination allow the caster to both detect things previously concealed, as well as to conceal things previously visible.
1 Evocation Another of the several skills related to the art of magic, evocation deals with things that go boom. Fireballs, force spells, etc. all fall under the auspices of this offensive skill. 
1 Fishing Allows you to pull items from the water.  You will pretty much only find fish, fish scales, daggers, and an occasional pair of sandals.

(Necessary Item : Fishing and Fishing Bait)
1 Fletching

Allow you to make bows and arrows.

(Example Items : Elm Linen Bow)

Necessary Item : Fletching Kit
1 Hand-to-Hand Although this skill can be mastered by only the Monks, most every other class can at least throw the occasional punch. Besides, one never knows when one might be relieved of one's weapon, both during combat and at other times.
1 Jewel Craft

The skill allows you to make different types of jewelry.  I wouldn't suggest doing this skill unless you are a banker or an enchanter.

(Example of Item : Silver Jade Ring)

Necessary Item : Jewelry Kit
4 Meditate Another of the several skills related to the art of magic, meditation allows the caster to memorize spells more quickly and regenerate mana faster.  Don't waste any training points in here because trust me you will practice this often.
1 One-Hand Blunt Ability to use a blunt object that only requires one hand to wield.

(Example of Skill : Cracked Staff)
1 Piercing Ability to use piercing weapons.

(Example of Skill : Stiletto of the Bloodclaw)
1 Pottery

Allows people to craft clay into pottery. 

(Example Item : Small Clay Container) 

Necessary Item: Clay, Pottery Wheel and Kiln
1 Sense Heading Use this skill often.  I normally practice it while meditating.  It allows you to figure out which direction you are facing.  This can come in handy when you get turned around in a zone. 
1 Swimming Ok the ability to swim.  Enough said.  It also determines how long you can hold your breath underneath the water.
1 Tailoring

Allows you to make different types of armor.  You also need this skill to make items necessary for the necromancer quest armors.

(Examples Item : Raw Silk Tunic)

Requires: Tailoring kit (different sizes), Pattern and a Pelt (different for each size)
1 Throwing Ability to use throwing weapons. 

(Example of skill : Throwing Dagger)
1 Two-Handed Blunt Ability to use two-handed blunt weapons.

(Example of Skill : Runed Totem Staff)
16 Research Permits the magic using classes to assemble spells from component pieces. 

Requires: Book of Dark Bindings
20 Specialization This allows the user to specialize in a spellcasting skill of his or her choice. Once specialization gets to 50 you become specialized in that skill.  This allows you to cast spells of the circle with less mana and at a faster speed.
22 Dodge This allows you to dodge out of the way of attackers physical blows.  Don't bother training this one because it will go up on its own.
