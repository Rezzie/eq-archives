Black Burrow Zone Guide

 


Welcome to train town ! That should be the sign outside the entrance to Black Burrow. Black Burrow is located a short walk from Qeynos, to the southeast of Qeynos Hills of Qeynos Hills.


You can find all levels of characters spread all over this large underground area. As soon as you zone into BB, you will meet the lower level characters. Levels 5 or 6 would be the minimum level you should to come here. If not greeted by a unwelcome Gnoll train, you can walk around and scout the area. A small Gnoll camp is located straight ahead from the entrance and you can gain some experience here. An occasional bear or large rat will roam the entrance as well.   When you first zone into BB, be careful not to fall on the fake tree holes located near the entrance.  It appears as if the tree has a door excavated in it but the ground is fake. If you fall into one of these fake holes, all I can say is pray as the dungeon downstairs will most certainly be full of Gnolls, mainly the Patrolling kind.

 

From the entrance you can go two ways, either down to the BB dungeon or move to a different zone, Everfrost. The dungeon is a much tougher place and it is not advisable to come here until you reach at least level 9. Even though you should be able to handle most of the Gnolls in this part, they tend to gang up and getting run over by a train becomes so common it is infuriating. There are several different sections in this part and I will not spend too much time describing all of them.  Nevertheless, it is important to note that the farther inside you go, the stronger the Gnolls become. Some of the sections are the snake pit, where you can fight Giant Snakes and occasional Patrolling Gnolls (level 9 or 10 recommended), the Guardsman room, a small room filled with all sorts of Gnolls including the tough Gnoll Guardsman (level 10 or 11 recommended), and the Commander room (level 12 or 13 recommended). The BB dungeon is a dark and large maze so take some time to familiarize yourself with the surroundings. After all, you should know where to run when that train comes your way.  Be aware that no zones nearby exist and thus, running from a train that has you on its mind is fairly useless.

 

I still have not learned of any good and safe tactic that can be used in BB.  My advice is to party with as many friends as you can and make sure you all know what everyone else is doing.  A good spot for levels 9 and 10 is the area below the door that leads to the Commander room.  Three Giant Snakes spawn here and even though they are fairly easy to kill, they provide good XP and Giant Snake Fangs, a nice weapon that sells for about 6G.  Trains will come your way quite often though but you can avoid them by hiding away from the ramp they follow downstairs. Another good spot are one of the three Commander rooms.  Once you reach level 14 or so you can party with some friends and take out some Gnoll Commanders.  They are the toughest rank among Gnolls in BB and always spawn with company.  Thus, be prepared to take some damage.  The loot is the best in BB as you can find quite a few different magical items.

 

Most common creatures in BB are Gnolls of all kinds and Giant Snakes.  Gnolls vary from levels 3 all the way to 15 while Giant Snakes vary from levels 6 to 9.


If you are looking for some quick XP, BB is not your place. Sure, you will get XP but you will also die often. I still don't understand why BB is such a popular place.
