Firstly, I would suggest before posting stuff you look further down the list, you will find many posts asking the same questions.

Ok, you get specialize in all 5 disciplines of spells but there is only one that you can take past skill level of 50. The usual for Necromancers is Alteration.

At level 20 I just put 1 point into each specialization skill, they will go up as you use each type of spell but the first to hit 51 will be the one you will the bonuses for. It will go up to 150 max and the others will stay at 50. You start your specialization in each one at the level you put the pracs into it.

Because I used Alteration for most spells at level 20+ it hit 51 first easily. Some say to just put the point into the one you want to speciazlize in then wait for it to hit a decent level but I would suggest don't bother, you by far use Alteration the most at that level so it will go up fast.

You will see messages such as:

You have become better at Specialize Alteration skill! (51) etc that is the messages you are looking for.

In the skills list there is some confusion as they all just say "Specialize" in the box. You can tell which is which by going down in Alpha order i.e. Abjuration, Alteration, Conjuration, Divination, Evocation.
