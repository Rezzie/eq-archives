Strategy

Here is a fun spot to hunt!  Lots of creatures to kill so you won't be killing the same mob over and over again.  But the fact that lots of mobs spawn here makes it a little harder than other places.  Nonetheless, once the spawn is broken, the XP gain can be quite profitable.  And you will be fighting close to the zone border so if anything goes wrong, run as fast as you can.  The closest zone is Upper Guk and you will zone close to the room where the Nokta Shaman spawns.

 

To better explain this spot, I created a little map with spawn locations.  Click here to take a look at it.  Note that this is only a zoom to the part of the Live Side you will be fighting in.

 

Here is the setting.  Zone to Live Side and head down the ramp.  Once you are down, you will see a small bridge in front of you.  Set camp here and cast your pet.  Now let the fun begin!

 

Start with the Froglok in front of you, the one close to the water on your left side.  Cast Screaming Terror on it and go back to your pet.  If Screaming Terror is not resisted, the Froglok will stay there but another one within the aggro range will come after you.  So target it and root close to ramp.  Then send your pet and cast Venom of the Snake and Drain away.  If the second one joins, Scream it till you kill the first Froglok.  Otherwise, finish the first and head back to take the one you initially Screamed.  Pull with Venom, root close to ramp and Drain away.

 

Two of them are down and you will need to med.  Once at full mana, head to the spot where you did your initial pull.  The wall close to here is fake.  Go through it and climb the little stairs to the bridge.  Across the bridge is a Froglok.  Repeat the process.  Cast Screaming Terror and jump down towards your pet.  If Scream is not resisted, another Froglok will come after you.  So, just repeat the same process.  Root, VoS and Drain.  If the Scream is resisted, you might be in trouble.  The Froglok hit you and your pet will rush to combat, which may mean a train is coming.  If so, Harmshield and run to zone.

 

If everything went well, you killed the two Frogloks and only have one more to go.  Med up and to the bridge that crosses the water.  You can see a Froglok up this small place to your right.  Check to see if no roaming mobs are nearby and cast away.  Standard procedure, pull with VoS, root close to ramp and Drain away.

 

Now the area is cleared up and you can start pulling the roaming mobs from the tunnel.  Both Frogloks and Greater Minotaurs walk around the tunnel and you can pull them at will.  Just make sure only one is around so you don't pull a train.  And it is not a good idea to pull with Root.  If you Root the creature, another mob might show up and join the fight.  VoS is a good puling spell.  Pull and head back to your pet, close to ramp.

 

That's it.  Once you learn your way around, this is quite a safe place and XP rewarding.

 

A few notes.

 

1. Greater Minotaurs hit harder and faster than Frogloks so you will have to let your pet take the hits.  Either Root it or Feign Death to get it off you.

 

2. You will find yourself dealing with two mobs at the same time quite often.  You can deal with it easily if you Scream or Root it.

 

3. A wandering mob often spawns up the ramp and comes down to your camping spot.  This mob can either be a common Froglok (either a Shaman or a Knight) or a Zol Ghoul.  Zols are a lot harder and can be all the way up to level 40.  If it cons anything but blue or white, you are in trouble.  Harmshield your way out of there.

 

4. Sometimes, things don't go too well.  Especially before the spawn is broken.  If you find yourself dealing with more than two mobs, play it safe and zone.

 

5. Use Call of Bones to reduce your downtime.  It greatly increases your XP gain.

 

Loot is not bad.  Frogloks and Greater Minotaurs drop from a few copper up to 5 plat per kill.  It is often on the 1 to 2 plat range.  Frogloks also commonly drop Fine Steel weapons, which you can sell for 5 plat or so or give them to your pet for better fighting results.  Have fun!
