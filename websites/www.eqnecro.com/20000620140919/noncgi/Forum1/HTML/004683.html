Best two I'm aware of are Treants and Ratraz.

Also, you could try the heart spiders in upper guk. If you kill the giant one, he drops a black chitin shield that's saleable for about 50plat.

Another good cash maker is that bard in the karanas, the one by qeynos hills. She drops a lute every time that sells for about 3pp.

Giants! If you have Kunark, go to warslik woods! Kill the giants. Go in the fort and there's a hill. Set up camp there (the giants in that zone don't see invis, so summon a pet after you've entered) and kill. You might need a group at your level until about level 25 or 26. Then you can solo them.

Sell some bone chips. I know, ruthless, but it works. I did this a while ago to pad my bank holdings. Go to nektulos, buy/kill to get as many chips as you can, like, 2-3 bags full. Go to the overthere and auc that you are selling bone chips to the highest bidder(s). Make them use the /auc line. Most of the necros in the overthere gain enough money to easily afford this. I'm just a tightwad when it comes to buying my own chips.

Try camping the GBS. The more I think about doing this, the more I want to. You can get a lot of bone chips in the process and the item sells for huge, vast, enormous, humongous, grossly insane amounts of cash.

While camping giants, you might also get a forest loop. Those are a +3 dex/wis/agi earring and I've had some phenomenal trades for this item. I've gotten 2 and for one, I got a runed cowl, a +4 int, +4 str necklace and 2 plat engagement rings (+4 int each). For the second, I got black chiten legs (valued at 1300pp). I've seen these [the loops] traded for ykeshas.
