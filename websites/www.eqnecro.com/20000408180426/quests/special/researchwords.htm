Research Words Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Neriak

Who: X'Ta Timpi, X'Ta Tompi, and X'Ta Tempi

Walkthrough:

 

1. First you must climb the stairs to the top of the guard ramparts in Neriak Foreign Quarter, the first thing in the city. On the top you can find three Necromancer sisters, X'Ta Timpi, X'Ta Tompi, and X'Ta Tempi.

 

2. Hail one and follow the threads until they ask,"What [word]s are you interested in?", followed by a list of pet spell words. Then ask, "What word of [wordname]? and they will disclose the items you need for the quest.  Each quest is only on one of the sisters, so you may need to ask around between them.  Now for the quests:
