Strategy

The Chain Mail guards in East Karanas were recently twinked to a high MR and better stats.  Before the twink, this was a highly competitive camp fought over by Druids, Wizards, Mages and Necros.  These days they are a ghost town; it is *very* hard to get a root, snare, darkness etc to stick on the "new" guards.  With this impossible casting situation in mind, allow me to share with you the "brute force" method of handling Barbarian Fish Camp Guards.

 

Note: there are two guard camps in East Karanas that I know of, the first is nearest the bridge (to the right of the wood bridge as you come from North Karanas) and has regular Human guards that will be green at 47.  The second camp is farther right (keeping water on your right) as you come out of the bridge, at the very dead end where the waterfall is.  All of the Chain Mail guard spawns are 5 min spawns, three per location, one of the three patrols.  Take some time to study the patrol route : )

 

Now that you have all that, the rest is fairly simple.  Since casting is completely unreliable (and the run to zone from the barbarian camp is annoyingly long), you have to beat and drain the guards.  Bring up the 44 pet, and give it Spirit Armor for the hell of it, as well as some Daggers if you care (it's a marginal difference).  One note: at the Barbarian camp, do not pull along the shore, pull inland.  The shore is mildly buggy. When you are ready, and the patroller is far, far away, Augment the pet and pull the nearest guard with Vampiric Curse.  Run backwards a bit to get him away from the patroller's path and then send in the pet.  With rare exception (Leon) the guard will stay on the pet like glue.  When the guard and pet are on each other, immediately Pact of Shadow the pet.  Trust me on this; the guards hit for occasional 110's and a lucky run can be very bad.  When the Pact is started, Drain Spirit the guard twice, and reapply the pact.  Continue to drain until you are *certain* (using /assist and F1F1) that the pet/guard ratio is safe enough for you to back off and let the pet finish the guard.  Start up Call of Bones and watch Jibekn chase Guard Mercyl slowly away from the camp.  You will be keeping Call on almost continuously from here on out.  When the guard is dead, sit down and med up.  You should be able to take at least one more guard spot before the first one spawns.  Just keep Mr. Pet alive and everything will be juuuust fine : )
