Cazic Thule Zone Guide

 


Cazic Thule is a temple-like dungeon located next to Feerott. Next to the entrance to Cazic there are a few pyramids guarded by Lizardmen. The largest pyramid, at loc -1200 -70, is the entrance to Cazic Thule.

 

Cazic Thule has a large number of creatures tailored for a wide range of levels.  It is also a dangerous place since trains are found started by one of the many many players that come here in search of experience and fortune.  Furthermore, Cazic is not the place to go if you are looking for some quick solo XP. But do not be discouraged.  Cazic Thule has many rewards for those who decide to take its challenge.

 

Lower level necromancers (around 20-24) should stay close to the entrance.  The corridor before the real entrance to Cazic Thule and the courtyard are home to a good number of lower level Lizardmen.  Broodlings, Warriors, and Guards are commonly found wandering around the Cazic entrance. In this case, the best strategy is to team up with some friends and stay as close to the zone as possible, but outside the entrance corridor (that way you will not be in the train path).  Have someone pull the Lizardmen to a safe spot in the courtyard and cast away.  Be aware that, while in Cazic Thule, you will rarely pull a single creature.  Casting on one will most certainly mean a battle against two, three or even more creatures.


Once you have made it past level 24, you can start pulling from a little deeper inside Cazic Thule.  The area known as maze (accessed through a door to the right when you enter the courtyard) spawns a good number of mid-level Lizardmen, including Defenders and Sentinels. Use the same strategy mentioned before.  Have someone pull the Lizardmen to the patio and cast away.  Keep in mind that if you are the one pulling, you should pay attention to your pet.  The maze is indeed a maze and it is quite easy to lose your pet in its corridors.  Having your pet guard an area in the courtyard, next to your group, might be a good idea. And make sure you get to know the corridors of the maze before you attempt to pull from the deep end.


Necros in their low 30s can start hunting in one of the many areas with mid and high level Lizardmen. The most popular area is known as the Throne room.  This area spawns a large number of mid and high level Lizardmen as well as a few Gorillas. To get there, head to the courtyard and go through the door on the left side and then turn right. You will need a fairly large team to hunt in the Throne room and a very good ISP connection.. The battles are almost non-stop and creatures always come in teams. Another good hunting target for necros in their 30s are the Alligators. They can be found in quite a few different places.  The most common can be accessed by going up the ladders either in the right or left side of the courtyard. Once again, be careful when pulling in Cazic. These Alligators will rarely come alone and can bite pretty hard. Finally, a third hunting option is the area known as Arena. You can get there by going through the same door you use to get to the Throne room. But instead of turn right past the door, turn left and keep going down. There are several mid level Lizardmen on your way downstairs, but those shouldn't be a problem. The arena spawns several high level Lizardmen, including Heralds, Prosletypes, Zealots, and Justicars.  Make sure you have a good and large party when coming to the Arena. If anything goes wrong, zoning is often not an option (you do remember those Lizardmen you fought to get to the Arena right?).


Necros past level 34 can hunt pretty much anything in Cazic Thule. There are plenty of targets for you here.

 

Level 35 characters can hunt pretty much anything in Cazic Thule. That does not mean you can go on a solo rampage since you still need a group. So, get yourself a team and look for one of Cazic Thule's tough opponents. The Taew Eew Lizards are a good example. There are a few different of them and they spawn every now and then in different locations. The Taew Eew Diviner and the Taew Eew Templar can be found on the first floor of the Throne Room. The Diviner drops the Lizardscale Mantle and the Templar drops the Lizardscale Cloak. Be careful with the Diviner. Even though he is not a very tough opponent, Diviners can and probably will charm. Make sure your party knows about this before heading into battle. A third Taew Eew, the Archon, can be found in the Temple at the end of maze. Go through the courtyard door that leads to the maze and take the first left. Go through the room with four pillars, take the second left turn and keep going until you see a Temple. The Archon can be found in the room at the topmost part of the Temple. He is the one that drops the Lizardscale Belt.

 

Another option for level 35+ necromancers are Golems. There are three types of Golems in Cazic Thule, Clay, Stone, and Steel.  Any of these three Golems will not go down very easily and are always protected by high level Lizardmen such as Justicars, Heralds, Zealots, and Judicators.  First, the Clay Golem can be found in the Temple area and is the easiest one of the three Golems.  He commonly drops a Lizardscale Tribal Mask.  The second Golem, the Stone one, can be found in the Throne room area.  Go up to the second floor and look for a throne.  The Stone Golem room can be a very tough place and you should only try it with a full party.  The problem is the room you will be camping is surrounded by other rooms where the high level Lizardmen spawn.  Even though you might not want to, you will have to fight a lot of Lizards before, during, and after you take the Stone Golem.  Finally, the Steel Golem can be found in the Alligator Tunnels.  To get there, go to the Alligator tanks and swim until you get to a ladder. The Alligator Tunnel area is just up this ladder.  You must be very careful when hunting  in here since you will in the middle of a major Alligator spawn.  And Alligators will KOS anyone that crosses their way. My advice is this: come with a full party and make sure you don't have to zone. You simply won't be able to.


Well, I saved the best for last.  If your party is really up for the challenge, go after the Avatar of Fear. You can find him below the large greenish ball in the downstairs area of Cazic Thule.  A full level 35+ party can take him down, but not easily. He hits very hard and is often protected by a few high level Lizardmen.  The best strategy is to take him down as soon as possible.  Focus your party's efforts on him before you move on to the Lizardmen.

 

Well, that is pretty much it for Cazic Thule.  It is indeed a good hunting place, but also a very dangerous one.  There are far too many people, a clear invitation to trains. And they will happen very often.  Besides that, the only real bad thing about Cazic is the fact that you will need to be in a party to kill anything.  And it hurts a whole lot more to die when hunting with a party.  You can loose a whole day of Lizardmen killing after a single death. Thus, make sure you have a good team going.  Pay special attention to your tank/puller. The difference between a good and a bad one is easily the difference between your life and death.  Have fun!

Recommended Levels: 20 through 40


Creatures: All sorts of Lizardmen, Gorillas, Clay Golems, Stone Golems, Steel Golems, Alligators, and the Avatar of Fear


Common Loot: Common weapons, Fine Steel weapons, gems, and Research material


Train Likelihood: High


Merchants: None


Special Items: Lizardscale Cloak, Lizardscale Belt, Lizardscale Tribal Mask, Golem Metal Wand, Ball of Golem Clay

 

Linked Zones: Feerott

 

Things you should know: Cazic Thule can be described as a big big maze.  And there is no worst place for our low IQ pets than a maze.  Pay attention to him or he will be gone real fast. Also, be very careful when meding in the tunnel right outside Cazic.  The Ogre Bouncers that roam Feerott patrol this area and can kill you in a split second. So, if you have to med outside Cazic, try to do it while invisible or as close to the Cazic zone as possible.  Believe me, you will not have more than a few seconds to zone after a Bouncer attack.
