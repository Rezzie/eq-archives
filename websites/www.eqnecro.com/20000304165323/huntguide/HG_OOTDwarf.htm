Strategy

The strategy here is u want to jump off the right side of the boat when you see the island coming from Freeport, this is because you don't want the dock person attacking you.

 

Once on the island, head left till you see 2 Dwarfs.  You can kill them here. Just stand far enough away so that the opposite one you are killing does not see you.  Pull with Screaming Terror, this will give you up to 3 times for Darkness to stick.  Once Darkness is in, tell your pet not to taunt and send him in.  These hit hard :)  Then blast away with Shock of Poison.  Watch out for the Sisters on the island.  They will be green but will still attack until level 29.

 

This is awesome XP since there are 2.  Feign Death if you get into trouble.  These will kill your pet fast hitting for 50+ so turn his taunt off.  You will love this spot.

Mdar I may not be hunting them correctly, but I would not suggest hunting these dwarves solo until level 29.  I found it extremely difficult to kill them and they hit extremely hard
and fast as well as run very fast. Loot as far as I have seen sucks real bad, they drop a few silver and some copper mostly.  Well these are my comments about this spot.
Volemak Zexus (Cazic Thule) Two 20th level necros can take them one at a time. What we did is casted our pets, made them guard close to the shoreline about 15 steps away from the dwarfs. We both cast allure of death before casting Siphon Strength on him to pull (lowers his hit some) then run to pets and let the pets bash and taunt, while both of us get far enough to cast siphon life on him until dead. The trick is to stay far away enough for him to not chase you but with feign death that's a non issue really. They carry good loot, most of the time in the 1 to 2pp range, got some words and pages of them too. We had siphon life, siphon strength, mend bones, feign death, gate, allure of death, and another spell of your choice. They resist DoTs (at least at 20th). Good spot, and if you stay close to shoreline like we did, the sisters are not even an issue.

Noquar V'Ashar (Innoruuk)

I'm not sure if this has changed but the two dwarf spawn includes one merchant dwarf (con blue to my wizard associate of 47 seasons).  I made the mistake of attacking this one and I and a companion died a swift death.  After corpse retrieval and a shrewd lesson, the other dwarf (the one who spawns beside the anvil) proved great hunting solo.  The loot is poor at best but the XP is pretty good.  The spawn is slow enough one has plenty of time to meditate and is not often camped.  I gained about 3 levels in the mid to high 20's here.
