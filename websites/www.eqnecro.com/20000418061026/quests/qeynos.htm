Qeynos Quest Guide

 

 

 

 

Level: 4-6

Where: Qeynos Aqueducts
Who: Kane Bayle
What: Kill Paladins in the plains of Karana
How: At 8pm game time, Kane leaves his post at the North Gate and heads to the Aqueducts.  He will stop at the Clerics Guild house by the pool in the Bloodsaber compound.  Talk to him and he will ask you to kill Paladins in the plains of Karana.  Once you find and kill one of these Paladins, loot their Holy Symbol or Prayer Bids and bring it back to Kane.
Reward: Cash, XP, and a common weapon

Quest submitted by Gradel

 


Level: 6+

Where: Qeynos
Who: Zamel
What: Kill Cubert, the NPC that spawns in Qeynos Aqueducts
How: Go to Qeynos Aqueducts and start killing Mangy rats and some cubes that spawn one at a time in the location where Cubert spawns (check this map for his exact location, look for the red spot).  The spawns are fast, about 20 seconds after each kill and the highest level among all spawns is level 7.  Killing these will make Cubert spawn and once he does, kill him and hopefully he will drop a tattered dress that you must take to Zamel.  Zamel is near the pool close to the bank in Qeynos.  Once you find Zamel, give him the tattered dress.  A few notes for this quest.  Everything cons green to a level 13 and a level 6 is able to complete and gain XP.  Zamel kills a Dark Elf on sight but Humans are able to talk to him without a problem.

Reward: Goldean Ear Stud (+3 INT) and XP

Quest submitted by Baane, Fuego, Brelag, and Kirk

 

 

Level: 15-20

Where: Qeynos Aqueducts

Who: Rihtur Fullome

What: Kill Milea Clothspinner

How: First you get the quest by Rihtur Fullome he is standing at the entrance to the Bertoxx shrine, then you move towards EK and to the slope leading to HH. Take the road next to the ramp follow the road and find Milea and kill here.  You will get some bad faction but if you first talk to Melissa Clothspinner she will give you a note to give to Milea which will improve your faction.  So no harm done first give note then kill here and take the Veil to Rihtur Fullome.

Reward: He will reward you with a 8th level spell !!!! and some cash and XP
