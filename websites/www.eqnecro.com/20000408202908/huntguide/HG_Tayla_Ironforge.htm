Strategy

After making big ol' 16, head to Neriak 3rd gate and pick up your new spells.  Now it's time to try them out.  Head over the Maiden's Fancy.  Say hi to whomever is camping Ratraz.  If Ratraz is there, proceed to leave.  If Ratraz is camped, then you are in luck.  Downstairs in a couple rooms is Tayla Ironforge.  She is a Warrior type that is very similar to Trisha Whistlesong in West Freeport, with one major difference: she has multiple attacks.

 

First, if you have it, cast Banshee Aura.  This will be a high damage fight since neither side has many HPs.  Banshee Aura will make the difference everytime.  Open her door, avoid targeting Spice, and Engulf her.  Run upstairs and go behind a table.  Notice the odd pathing.  Use this to your advantage to get the DoT of your choice off (I suggest Leach for obvious reasons).  Once combat has begun, make sure you throw in a Poison Bolt between rounds to stack more damage.  It means a lot to the low HP Tayla).  It is important to try and time your Lifetaps in between her bashes/kicks.

 

Result? Dead Tayla with nice XP.

 

The real beauty of this camp is that if you have Banshee, you can do it without a pet. Huge XP.  Also, while you are camping Ratraz, you can pull Tayla as well. My only warning is that the character I did this on was 16 but well equipped.  I never ever had a close fight, though.  This becomes crazy XP if you can manage it without the pet.  Obviously, completely buff yourself as you will be meleeing.

 

Note: If you are using Piercing, throwing Vampiric Embrace on before the fight *will* save the battle a couple of times ;)

 

Tayla may have something to do with the Fiery Avenger quest ;) A quest involving her has just become active since last patch.  Be nice to your Paladins ;)
