Stein of Moggok Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Oggok

Who: Clurg, an Ogre Bartender

Walkthrough:

 

1. First the bad part.  This is a very large quest, involving a lot of traveling and item gathering.  And worst of all, it can't be completed by a necromancer.  You must have a friend tag along for the run.  And it has to be an Enchanter, since that is the only class that can complete this quest.  You could get all items, but they are no drop and you wouldn't be able to complete the quest

 

2. The premise for the quest is the following: Clurg has lost his Bartender's Compendium and really wants it back.  The Compendium is currently held by Bartender Gregor in Freeport.  Thus, you must go to Freeport and get the Compendium.  But Gregor will not simply give it to you.  Instead, he will ask for 4 ingredients for a special drink he wants to make.  The ingredients are an Erud's Tonic, a Koalindl fish, a Honey Jum,  and a Kiola Nut.  If you get these, the Compedium is yours.  Here is how you should do it

 

3. The Erud's Tonic can be found in Erudin.  Make sure you are able to walk around in Erudin before you do this part of the quest.  If not, ask a friend to do this for you (it is quite simple).  Head to Erudin and go to the Blue Hawk's Inn in Erudin.

 

4. Once you get there, buy an Innoruk's Kiss of Death, a Lore Item.  Then leave and go to the Library.  On the top floor you will find Sinnkin Highbrow.  Give her the item you just bought and she will give you the Erud's Tonic.  Note that the Erud's Tonic is a no drop/lore item

 

5. The Koalindl fish can be found in Qeynos.  Once again, make sure you can walk around in Qeynos.  If not, you can still complete this part of the quest but it will be a little riskier.  You will have to use Gather Shadows most of the times.  I will assume that you can't go to Qeynos without being KOS (damn those Treants...)

 

6.  Leave Erudin and take the boat to Qeynos.  Once you get there, leave the boat and immediately cast Bind Affinity.  Then cast Gather Shadows

 

7. Go to North Qeynos and find the Temple of Life.  Inside it you will see a pond with a few Koalindl fish.  All you have to do is kill one of them.  Easy isn't it?  Well, not really.  First, the Koalindl fish will group attack.  Second, they impose a severe faction penalty when killed (with Rodcet Nife/Clerics).  Once you kill one, anyone nearby will jump in and attack you.  That includes a Paladin that walks around the Temple and several guards.  And finally, the Koalindl fish don't drop their corpse every time so you might have to try again.

 

8. Once you get the corpse, immediately cast Gate.  If for some reason you died before gating, cast Gather Shadows and head back to the Temple of Life.  Loot your corpse and the Koalindl fish.  Note that the fish is also a no drop/lore item

 

9. The Honey Jum can be found in Misty Thicket.  Once again, your faction standing is important to complete this part of the quest.  You need an indifferent faction in Rivervale.  If you don't have it, you will have to ask a friend to complete this for you (a halfling to be sure).  If you do not know how to get to Misty Thicket, try the Antonica Zone Connection Map

 

10. Leave Qeynos and head to Qeynos Hill.  Then keep going to West Karana, North Karana, East Karana, Gorge of King Xorbb, Runnyeye, and finally Misty Thicket (sheesh!)

 

11. Once you get to Misty Thicket, look for a small hut with a male and a female halfling inside.  Once again, if you do not have a good faction standing don't attempt this.  Instead, ask a friend.  Go inside and talk to the female called Lil Honeybugger.  She will tell you that a girl named Kizzie Mintopp has a recipe to make Honey Jum

 

12. Kizzie Mintopp can be found in Rivervale, next to the Fool's Gold store.  But there is no need to go there.  What you need is 30 gold pieces and 3 honeycombs.  These honeycombs are dropped by Bixies, which can be found in Misty Thicket.  Ask around where you can find them or simply buy a few

 

13. Head to Rivervale and give Kizzie the money and the honeycombs.  In return, she will give you the Honey Jum

 

14. The final item, the Kiola Nut, can be found in the Ocean of Tears, at the Sisterhood Isle/Island of Erollisi (+1200 -9400).

 

15. To get there, leave Rivervale and head to Kithicor, West Commonlands, East Commonlands, and finally Freeport

 

16. Once in Freeport,  head to the docks in East Freeport and wait for the boat.  Cast Gather Shadows as soon as you see the boat approaching.  Take the boat to Butcherblock Mountains

 

17. At the first boat stop, look around and con whoever you see.  You should have no problem with faction, but it is better to be safe.  Remember to cancel Gather Shadows (if it is still active) before you con

 

18. If the con is not good, you might have to ask a friend to complete this part of this quest.  It is quite simple and you might even get someone in the boat to do it for you.  If the con is good enough, go ahead and leave the boat

 

19. The first hut to your right has a Female Elf that will sell you the Kiola Nut for about 2 gold pieces. Simple

 

20. Wait for the boat to come back and jump on it.  Don't worry, it is probably not broken.  It just takes a long time to complete the round trip

 

21. Now for the part of the quest that can't be accomplished by a Necromancer.  Go all the way back to Freeport and look for an Enchanter you can trust.  Not only that, but that Enchanter must have a few illusion spells to make this part of the quest work.  He must have illusion Human and benevolence or illusion Dark Elf and Alliance

 

22. Once you find someone you trust, give him the four items and tell him to go to the bar where Gregor is

 

23. Have the enchanter cast benevolence or alliance on Gregor and then give him the four items.  In return, he will give him the Bartender's Compendium and a few platinum pieces.  Note that the Compendium is a no drop item.  Thus, the same Enchanter must go all the way to Oggok and complete the quest for you

 

24. Once the Enchanter has the Compendium, go with him all the way to Oggok.  You should not go inside Oggok unless you are able to (Dark Elves should be able to, but not other necros)

 

25. Wait in Feerott while your friend goes to find Clurg.  He is at the bar near the bank.  Once inside the bank, have the Enchanter cast benevolence or alliance on Clurg and then give him the Compendium.  In return, he will receive the Stein of Moggok (a lore item)
