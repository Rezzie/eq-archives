Clinging Darkness, Heart flutter, Boil Blood, Drain Life spell, Spirit Armor (self & pet), other self buffs

Strategy

Make your way down the ramps in Upper Guk after entering the zone.  At the first branch, take a right (you should see a hallway with water in the bottom with a wooden plank laying over the hall - you will have to swim partially but good swim skill is not necessary).  Warning: if you begin to cross a round wooden bridge you have missed the hall!!

 

As you make your way through the water make sure the wall stays to your right.  Eventually you will come to another hallway and can climb out of the water.  Just remember to keep the zone wall to your right.  Easy way to tell if you are there is that an Alligator will be sitting in an alcove as you come out of the water.

 

Location: While standing next to the alcove you will see a burning cauldron.  Just past this will be a cave with 2 spiders.  This is where you will start your XP fountain.  There is another room (further back to your right from the entrance with 3 more spiders - don't worry, they will not agro with the first two).

 

Strategy: Haven't tried any variations, but this one worked without any problems for me over a 3 hour timeframe..... Start by sending your pet in at the nearest Spider.  Cast Darkness, Heart Flutter, then Boil Blood and then target the second Spider.  Cast Darkness, Heart Flutter, and Boil Blood again.  At this time, the second spider will break off and begin attacking you (if not, step in and start melee cause your pet is taking a whooping!!)  If you can, time casting draw to keep your health up.  The Spiders don't have a lot of HPs, so they should drop pretty fast after the DoTs kick in.  As soon as you dispatch the one on you, switch to the one on the pet (should be well under half HPs by then) and finish it off.

 

Only once did I come close to losing my pet only because i didn't notice he was poisoned... The poison from Spiders doesn't last long so don't worry.  Med up and rest your pet.... rebuff and get ready for more fun.  By the time you are fully healed, the spawn in that room should take about 3-5 more minutes.

 

Now here is the thing, I have killed 30-40 spiders and I still con indifferently to these guys... so you can walk freely amongst them.... so.... with that in mind start making your way over to the next 2 Spider cave (or you can hang out and wait/rest for the same 2 to spawn).  There will be a cave entrance on the opposite side of the room from where the 3- Spider cove is.  Enter that cave and you will see another 3 Spiders.  Hug the wall to the left and you and will see another entrance to another cave.  With 2 Spiders!!!  Rinse and repeat from the first encounter!!  It's that easy!!  Usually no one is there so you can run back and forth.  Your only constraint is keeping full health and mana!!!!

 

The other good thing about this is that if you do get 2 other people it is relatively safe to do all four caves! just pop back and forth between each one.  They are all independent aggro zones from each other!!

 

Sorry its so long but I wanted people who haven't traveled to Guk to understand where to go... =)

 

Enjoy my brothers and sisters!
