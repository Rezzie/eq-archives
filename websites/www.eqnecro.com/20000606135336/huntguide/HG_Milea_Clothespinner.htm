Mob

Milea Clothespinner

Mob Level

15

Location

Deep in gorge leading to High Hold Pass from East Karana

Spells Used

Clinging Darkness, Poison Bolt, Vampiric Embrace, Bone Walk

Strategy

She is located under a manmade archway deep in the gorge leading to HHP from East Karana. Head east to HHP but instead of getting on the ledge, stay on the ground and you'll go a long ways into a gorge. You'll go very far into this gorge but eventually you'll come to a carved archway and you'll see Milea standing under that archway.

 

At level 18 she cons blue to me. I go past here invisible until I am on the HHP side of the archway just in case I need to zone. The zone isn't real close but err on the side of caution.

 

At 18 I'm hunting her solo (no pet) and have had no problems so far.  I hit her with darkness, back up until my spells are refreshed, then put the other two level 16 dots on her while she's slowly walking up to me. Then I melee her while I steal life and let the dot spells do their work.  Most times I'm down less than a bubble of health and 3 bubbles of mana.  She interrupts quite a bit since she hits, bashes and kicks.  I've had her resist darkness a few times but it's always stuck the second time.

 

She drops up to 5gp but usually it's sp and cp.  Every time she drops a lore, no drop cloth mask of some sort (large races only) and a rusty sword.  So the loot isn't much and the swords will encumber you very quickly so I just destroy them.  The money is ok but nothing special.  I have gotten one word off her so far.

 

Pros: She's completely alone so there's no danger at all from wandering mobs. She isn't a hard mob at all to fight at 18 without a pet, you get all the experience. She drops terrible to decent loot. The real benefit to hunting Milea is the safety factor.

 

Cons: Loot is relatively low compared to other mobs necros can kill. A very long walk from anywhere (bring lots of food and water).

 

Conclusion: If you're going to make the long and dangerous trip to North Karana to hunt Cordelia, head out early and hit this mob at maybe 15 to 20.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Steel Warriors, Guards of Qeynos, and Knights of Truth.  Raises faction standing with Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia

Jarraxle (Cazic Thule)

I wish I found this mob before level 19!  Easiest experience I have ever made.  Far safer than Dry Bones Skeletons or Gargoyles and no where near as camped as the Deepwater Crocs/Caimans.  At 19, almost each kill gave at least a slightly noticeable increase in exp and the loot isn't too bad either.  She will always drop a Monogramed Cloth and a Rusty Broadsword.  Occasionally she'll drop runes or words (gotten about eight so far), and the cash ranges from a few copper to 9 gold (most I saw).

 

My tactic is as follows (really good if you don't want to solo without a pet):

 

- Set pet to guard right where she spawns

- Cast Darkness

- Cast Heart Flutter

- Fear her (again if/when it wears off)

- Lifetap to finish her off

 

I do this and my pet loses almost no life and I don't come anywhere near to being hit by her.  I made 2 bubbles of experience in about 3 hours doing this, and she spawns eight minutes after killing her.  As I said, I only wish I made the trip earlier.

I have been hunting Milea from level 14 onwards and I have gotten some amazing XP from her.  Here is how I was doing it...

 

WARNING: At 14, you cannot take too many of her sword slashes and 2-3 resists and you are dead.

 

1) Setup up shop at the entrance of the tunnel (Bridge). Summon pet (must be blue or higher)

 

2) Mem Engulfing Darkness, Heat Blood, Lifedraw, Leach and Vampiric Embrace.

 

3) Put them in your hotkeys.

 

4) Con her first...if she is Yellow she might or might not resist Darkness (1 out of 8 times she resists). If she is blue or white, she is NOT going to resist.

 

5) Cast Vampiric Embrace and then cast Darkness on her from the entrance to the tunnel.

 

6) As she is approaching you, you can cast Heat Blood and it will cast before she gets to you

 

7) Just before she gets to you, start back peddling and send in your pet.

 

8) Now keep back stepping , until you have enough distance to cast Leach and one LifeDraw. She should be on your pet right now.

 

9) At this point she is going to want a piece of you, but you don't really care because she is going to die anyway. Just step in and melee. You will take some big hits, but Leach and Embrace will keep you at roughly 90% health. If necessary Lifedraw her.

 

10) She takes approx 5 1/2 to 7 minutes to spawn again and that is ample time to Med and heal.

 

11) Just repeat as necessary

 

Sidenote: If she resists the Darkness, don't panic. Just send pet in, cast heat blood and make sure you cast leach (IMPORTANT) this way if it comes down you you meleeing her, you are making progress on you health bar even with her hits!

 

I went from 14 to 15 in 2 1/2 hours and I am now half way to 16. I will be camping her until I can't get a single drop of XP from her.

 

Good luck to you all.
