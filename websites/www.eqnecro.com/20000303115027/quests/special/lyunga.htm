Ivandyr's Hoop Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Innothule Swamp


Who: Lyunga, a level 32 female Troll NPC

Walkthrough:

 

1. Go to any gem store and buy one or more rubies

 

2. Go to the Innothule Swamp and look for Lyunga.  She usually stays close to the zone 

to Guk

 

3. Once you find her,  she will tell you that she needs a ruby for her gem collection.  If she doesn't, your level is not high enough (should be around 20 to get this quest)

 

4. Give Lyunga the ruby and she will give you one of three prizes, a Midnight Mallet, a Brutechopper, or Ivandyr's Hoop (this is the one you are looking for :))
