Beguile Undead 

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 170

Speed : 6.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : The upgrade to Charm Undead, Beguile Undead charms an undead creature

Comments:

Skyshade - Like Charm Undead, Beguile Undead can be useful when hunting with a group and you are fighting against more than one creature. You may choose to hunt without a pet so you can charm one of your opponents.  Nice strategy, but harder to use when hunting solo.
Lifebane - At times I would send in my pet while fighting with my group against something like the Ghoul Lord room in Lower Guk. I would have Beguile Undead memorized in case my pet died, this way I could try to charm a ghoul and use it to battle on or even to just keep it alive for a bit to split up room spawn.



Call of Bones

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 5

Speed : 3.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Motion, Neglect, and Endurance

Effect : Turns the caster into a Skeleton and trades hit points for mana at a rate of 16 HPs for 16 mana

Comments:

Skyshade - Nice spell to reduce downtime, specially when cast after a successful Vampiric Curse
Lifebane - Yep, finally a spell that turns us into a skeleton! Wheee! Useful combo with Vampiric Curse as Skyshade mentioned above. Beware the "COB BUG", you click on the icon to cancel it, icon is gone, yet you keep on draining yourself of health. When that happens, sit and camp quick! When you log back in, the effect will be gone.

Root

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 30

Speed : 2.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Reduces target creature's movement to zero

Comments:

Skyshade - We all know how awesome this spell is. It takes necromancers 34 levels to get it, but once you do, you will be very happy. Root makes battles so much easier, specially in those cases where you are not fighting with a pet.
Lifebane - Few words about root. If the Root sticks the target is held for a random amount of time. However, every time you damage the creature you have a chance of setting it free. So use non-DD DOT's on him like Vampiric Curse, Boil Blood, and Darkness. This way you are damaging him and not breaking the Root until it's time was meant to be over.

Venom of the Snake

Skill : Conjuration 

Mana : 160

Speed : 4.6

Damage : Approximately 500 points of damage over time and an initial 40 points of poison damage

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Poison damage over time

Comments:

Skyshade - Massive damage dealt quite fast (approximately 30 seconds) against your target. Best DoT spell you have so far, even though it is resisted a little more than Boil Blood.
