Erudin Quest Guide

 

 

 

 

Where: Necro Guild in Toxxulia Forest
Who: n/a
What: Kill the Troll known as Rungupp and bring her his head
How: Get a party together and wait for Rungupp to shout that he hates Erudites and then find him.  He commonly stays close to the bridge that divides Tox and often stays in the middle of this bridge.  Be careful as he can cast a spell that renders you totally powerless


Where: Necro Guild in Toxxulia Forest
Who: n/a
What: Kill the Palace Courier that runs all over Tox and bring him his head
How: The courier usually runs across Tox at night and he follows a path from somewhere close to the bridge all the way to the entrance to Erudin.  Kill him as far away from the entrance to Erudin as guards will kill you to prevent the courrier's death


Where: Entrance to the skeleton cave (with those 2 skeletons) on the side across the bridge in Toxxulia Forest
Who: Talgarim
What: Bring him two useless cloth caps
How: These caps can be found when killing the Kobold Sentries that spawn in across the bridge and in the two Kobold camps located here.  They are a quite common loot and cannot be sold.
