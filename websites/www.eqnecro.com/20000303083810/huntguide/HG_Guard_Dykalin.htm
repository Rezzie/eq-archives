Fear, Blood, Darkness, Drain Life spell

Strategy

Just use your Darkness, pull away from the huts, pet attack, DoT, Fear strategy and you'll be fine.

 

To find him, zone into the mountains from Feerott and take your first right.  Keep the wall on your right, but do not take the path down to the Sphinx.  You will come to a group of 3 huts and he spawns in front of them.  He is a 18 minute spawn approximately.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Guardians of Tunare, Clerics of Tunare, and Stormguard.  Raises faction standing with Crushbone Orcs.

Additional Comments
Kaalan Elgluth This guy is difficult to solo up to Level 21.  Its doable but not really worth it in my opinion. The spawn is slow and he only gives a few silver 90% of the time (got a couple of low words off of him too).  It is a relatively safe spot because it is out of the way. If you are going to solo, be careful of using Fear.  Don't fear too quickly or close to huts because the innkeepers (green at 21) will come out and waste your pet. Don't wait too late, because he hits for 40+ points a pop and the pet won't last long.

Again, the biggest problem is the slow spawn and the lack of loot that he drops. I would put his Loot at about a 2 for a level 20-22 NPC.

Dementia Doomweaver (Veeshan)

I have just been camping Guard Dykalin in the Rathe Mtns.  Loot was silver, a little gold and once, 2 plat and a Word. DoT, pet, DoT, Leach, Fear and he is done for. He hits hard so watch your pets hit points. Just don't let Dykalin head towards the huts as the Innkeepers will join in.  Experience was minimal for a 23rd level.
