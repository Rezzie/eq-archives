Greetings all.

Well, I have not yet received Kunark so I wasn't able to explore it

Bummer!

Anyway, I did gather a few interesting bits of information. Most of these are not yet confirmed while some are already widely known.

- Some of 'The Overthere' vendors sell previously research only spells. Ndaar pointed this out at this post: http://www.eqnecro.com/noncgi/Forum1/HTML/003597.html

- There are a few different vendors that will sell part of the expansion spells. As you should know, level 51+ spells can be obtained either through a vendor, a quest, or as loot. Zleski posted on the same thread a pic of the purchaseable spells along with the location of the vendor that sells them, Lithriss Frizen in West Cabalis at /loc 167.75, 665.51, 3.75.

- Lifebane pointed out a few other locations where you can buy some of our new spells also at this same thread.

- There are two boats leaving to Kunark, one for evil races and one for the good races, Oasis and BB.

- Some of our spell descriptions have changed. Splurt, as you probably saw, is a new type of DoT. Even though it is not clear yet how it works, I heard it is NOT purchaseable. It is actually dropped in the Plane of Sky! Oh well...

- Spawns in Kunark are different. Some spots spawn different creatures and at different intervals. I have several reports from people that spent hours killing several different mobs and even getting different items.

- Necros seem to get a new 'Trade Skill'. Not sure what it does, but it uses some pretty cool items

- Kunark mobs often see through invis

- Zones next to towns are not necessarily newbie zones

- Some zones in Kunark have only way in and one way out. Thats means cannot go back through the same way you came in! Sounds like you will have to clean the area in order to get back out. Fun!

- Some zones in Kunark are not accessible unless you complete a quest first! I don't know which zones are 'questable' yet. Will keep you guys posted

Well, that is it for now. I will keep adding any interesting information to this post today.

To answer one question above, 51 lvl Necros are now known as Heretics

My DE Necro conned an Iskar Trooper at the gate in to West Cabilis and was mostly definitely unwelcome.

ROK is truly impressive and might well be thought of as EQ 1.5 ! I have probs with Framerate/Video lag in some areas and adjust clipping to compensate. The easiest and quickest way to do that for those that might not know, is to hold Shift then hit Plus or Minus key; saves a lot of hassle and can be done on the fly.

The Skellie Mortician near the Necro Guild sells supplies to make Embalming Fluid which is required for some Quests. For example to turn in an Embalmed Brain

The zone transition areas from what I've seen are not nearly as well defined and easy to locate as on Antonica etc. The one from Over There into Warslik Wood is a good example; the path runs out far from the zone and you have to climb the hill and search through the trees.

ROK is full of surprises, something I really love. In Over There I ran into a statue of an Iskar as tall as the Statue of Liberty looming up from the hills; I just stood in amazement !

No doubt ROK will have its problems but I'm very impressed with what I've seen so far and how well it works. My Circlet is off to Verant on this one.

Newbie loot is much better then any other place I've seen, no doubt to encourage people to leave Antonica and releive the overcrowding we all hate there. It seems to be working, one of my Guildmates yesterday reported ...LGuk is DESERTED !!

I intend to explore and enjoy it slowly, both from the view of my baby Iskar Necro and my 51 lv DE Nec.
