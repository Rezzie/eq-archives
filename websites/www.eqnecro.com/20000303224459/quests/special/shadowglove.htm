ShadowBound Gloves Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro


Who: Syllina

Walkthrough:

 

1. Head to the Temple of Solusek Ro and talk to Syllina.  She will tell you that she makes three different kinds of ShadowBound armor, Boots, Gloves, and Robes.  You will need a different set of items to get each of these items

 

2. To get the gloves, you need the following items: an Inky Shadow Silk, the Scepter of Tears, a Hand of Shadow, and a Skeletal Finger

 

3. You can get these items in any order, but remember that items gotten from the Shadow Men disappear once you logout

 

3.1. The Inky Shadow Silk must either be tailored or bought from a PC.  To tailor one, you will need 1 Shadow Wolf Pelt (sometimes dropped by Shadow Wolves), 2 Gather Shadows scrolls (bought in any necro guild), and 1 Silk Swatch (tailored from 2 Spider Silk, sometimes dropped by Large Spiders/Giant Spiders)

 

3.2. The Scepter of Tears can be found in Guk.  Go to Guk and zone in the first zone to Lower Guk.  Turn left in the first intersection and look for a waterfall.  When you find it, look for a room with a carpet on the floor and you will see a Scepter.  That is the one you are looking for.  Cast Identify on it to make sure you have the right one

 

3.3. The Hand of Shadow is found in any Shadowed Man camp.  It is sometimes dropped by them.  Keep killing them until you get one

 

3.4. Finally, the Skeletal Finger can be found with Giant Skeletons.  Head to an area where Giant Skeletons spawn (Rathe Mountains for example) and keep killing them until you get one.  It is an uncommon drop

 

4. Once you have all four items, head back to the Temple of Solusek Ro and give all four items to Syllina.  In return, she will give you the ShadowBound Gloves
