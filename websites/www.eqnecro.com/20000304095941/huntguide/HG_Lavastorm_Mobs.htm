Strategy

At level 14 you can hunt most of the mobs found in Lavastorm.  Understand that all of them vary in level, especially Fire Elementals.  So consider before you pull.

 

Even though Danger rating for this zone is only 7, it is a pretty safe place since you will be doing a good part of the fighting right next to a zone border.

 

So, how should you do it?  Simply go to the tunnel entrance to Najena and cast a pet.  Med to full and make your pet guard a spot just outside the tunnel.  Now all you have to do is find a mob suitable for your level.  Rock Dervishes are bizarre creatures made of flying bricks.  They are bizarre but not too hard to kill as they have no special abilities or attacks.  Lava Basilisks can be a tough opponent.  They hit fairly hard and cast a nasty stun spell that can leave you helpless for around 10 to 15 seconds.  Fire Elementals are the strongest of all three mobs described here.  They can hit hard and will eat through your pet if you don't interfere.  They also tend to go aggro on you more often than other creatures and Elementals will help each other if they see you fighting.

 

The strategy for any of these three is the same.  Pull with Darkness and send your pet to battle.  Cast Heart Flutter and start draining.  Do not cast Blood DoTs as they will be resisted.  Remember to step into melee combat every now and then to help out your pet.  And keep Feign Death memorized at all times if you have it.  It will fool the mobs in Lavastorm and can save your life in a desperate situation.

 

XP is pretty nice, especially around level 15/16.  There is plenty to kill and you will only die if something out of the ordinary happens.  Loot isn't too good though.  Mobs only drop a few gold pieces or no cash at all.  Bloodstones are commonly found here though and can be sold for about 4 gold to vendors.  Or you can keep them to either practice jewelry or use for the Leatherskin spell (when you get it).

Ok, just have your pet guard the zone to Neriak or Najena.  Once your pet is there, run out and pull a mob with Darkness.  Lead back to your pet, have pet beat on it, cast Heart Flutter, then finish off as necessary.

 

I wouldn't advice here until level 16 because most mobs resist the Heat Blood spell and that is your main DoT until 16.  Also, if you are a Gnome or other race like me, you can also kill those stinky dark elf guards..

 

Profit ain't too great here, but if you took my advice before and killed Sisters of Erollisi you should have more than enough for level 20 spells already and then some!
