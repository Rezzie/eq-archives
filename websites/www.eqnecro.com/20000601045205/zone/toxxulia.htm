Toxxulia Forest Zone Guide

 


Toxxulia Forest is the starting place for Erudite Necromancers.  It is here that you will spend most if not the entire part of your early youth.  Toxxulia is located just outside of Erudin in the Odus continent.  If coming from the Antonica continent, you should take the boat from the docks at Qeynos.  You will head to Eruds Crossing and the next stop will be Erudin.

 

As mentioned before, if you are an Erudite, you will have to stay in Toxxulia Forest for quite some time.  The only way out of Odus is through Qeynos.  And let me tell you something, you do not want to go through Qeynos until you reach level 8 and have the ability to become invisible.  They will kill you on sight.  Thus, sadly as it might be, you are stuck in Toxxulia.  But do not despair my dark friend as Toxxulia has plenty to kill for level 1 characters all the way to level 6.

 

At first, concentrate on attacking Decaying Skeletons and Kobold Runts.  These are two nice creatures to kill as they provide very good loot.  Decaying Skeletons will give you bone chips, the required component to cast a pet while Kobold Runts can sometimes give bags such as boxes and backpacks.  Once you reach level 3, you can move to different creatures such as the Fire Beetle and Spiders.  One thing you should know is that you can equip the fire beetle eye and it will provide you with a better night vision.  And believe me, it can be helpful as Toxxulia is constantly under complete darkness at night. Levels 4 through 6 characters can start going to the other side of Toxxulia Forest.  There is no zone dividing these two parts, but a river separated them.  Cross the bridge (be careful with Rungupp since he usually stays at the bridge) and head to one of the two Kobold camps. The first one, the closest to the bridge is the larger one.  Four Kobolds spawn quite constantly here.  They are tougher then the Kobold Runts you have been fighting so far.  Thus, make sure you bring a friend along to avoid getting a Kobold train.  The second Kobold camp is farther inside the forest and spawns three Kobolds at a time.  They take a little longer to spawn but this camp is usually less crowded.


Be aware that once you cross the bridge, skeletons will become a lot more common and will often attack you on sight.  You will only start dealing with them more effectively once you reach level 6.  At this level you should start looking for them (use sense the dead) as Kobolds will provide you with little to no experience.  Once you hit level 7 or 8 you can start going to Kerra Isle, the only zone linked to Toxxulia Forest.

 

Well, if you are fighting in Toxxulia Forest chances are you are stuck here.  Thus, enjoy it and start saving those bone chips as you will need them throughout you life.

Linked Zones: Erudin and Kerra Isle

 

Things you should know: One of the necro quests you can complete in Toxxulia Forest requires that you kill a large Troll wizard, Rungupp. He usually stays on top of the bridge that separates both parts of Toxxulia.  So be careful when taking the bridge as you might bump into your death.  Also, close to the second Kobold camp you can find the entrance to a Skeleton cave.  An NPC can be sometimes found here (he gives a quest) and two skeletons guard the entrance and you can actually talk to them.  They will say something like "Can't you see we are working" or "Grab a pick and give us a hand".  If you walk further inside, you zone back to the entrance of the cave.  This will be the entrance to one of the two new zones to be added January/March of 2000 through an optional patch.  They are certainly welcome and I heard rumors that one is an ancient Necromancer town.
