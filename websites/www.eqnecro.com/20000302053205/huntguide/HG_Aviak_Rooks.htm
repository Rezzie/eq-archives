Strategy

The Aviak village is a great place for the entire of the 20 levels. There is a full spread of mobs over this level range from Egrets (18ish) to Avocets (28-32 I think). The XP is good and the loot is too, ranging from bronze and gems to Black Pearls... The great thing about Aviak hunting is that they have no faction and are initially dubious (to me anyway). This means you can pull in relative safety and return afterwards without being attacked. Centaurs are similar... Start with Engulfing Darkness, then place DoTs. I then send in the pet. By this time the Aviak should be so AGGRO he will ignore your pet! He just keeps coming, letting the pet attack him freely, without retaliation. If he turns and starts on the pet, Fear him. Keep going and perhaps finish with a Life Drain or DD. Easy!
