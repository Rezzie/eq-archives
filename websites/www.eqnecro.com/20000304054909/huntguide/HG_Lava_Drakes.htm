Strategy

Drakes are the must abundant creatures in Lavastorm and for a careful level 12 Necro they can easy make their way to level 15 without any trouble, pull them to the zone edges and stay away from elementals. The easiest way of dispatching these things is just the standard couple of dots with some melee and Lifetaps.

Additional Comments
Shinku This zone can be very dangerous...especially for a level 8 Necromancer.  I would advise having your pet guard at a certain sport near the zone entrance away from all the wandering fire elementals.  You first have to wander around and find a fire drake to kill.  If you have a level 8 pet with you then I would advise sticking to the fire drakes that con yellow to you. If you have a level 9 pet feel free to take on the fire drakes that con red.  The great thing about this mob is that they do group up against you and are the most abundant creature roaming the zone so they are easy to find.  Once you found your target you pull with clinging darkness and run towards your pet. While you are running back with the mob slowed you may be able to cast siphon strength without yet engaging in the battle.  Against the fire drake you need to use as many offensive spells as possible.  Once you reach your pet let them engage against each other while you pull off disease cloud and poison bolt. Heal your pet once with mend bones and enter the battle while using Lifespike to keep your HP up.  These drakes have a AE magical attack which takes at least half a bar of your health every time at level 8.  If done correctly you will have awesome exp up to level 14 and also decent loot.  By level 12 with your new pet and spells you should be to kill these fire drakes with ease.  They drop anywhere from a few copper up to about 4 gold.  The common loot found on them are fire drake scales which sell for about 4 silver each and or some form of bile which can be sold for about 2 gold.  They rarely drop bloodstones which are worth 4 gold.  Great money for a level 8 guy heheh.
