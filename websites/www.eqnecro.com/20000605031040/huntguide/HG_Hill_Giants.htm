Strategy

Okay first thing is to find some people who know what they are doing here.  VERY IMPORTANT! With this in mind it is quite safe here.  Get to know the area and where the hg's spawn generally and find a nice spot to leave your pet and group.  The Necro of the party should pull here.  Darkness the Giant and start heading home turning every so often to get off a DoT.  You should arrive at your group with mana in hand and the giant having taken about 1/4 -1/2 dam.

 

The moment you get back say /pet attack your pet will rush it when it gets within range and start whacking away but because of the DoT damage the Giant will likely ignore the pet and keep after you.  Now it is just a matter of keeping it Darked and wait for the group to get it off you or kills it.

 

IMPORTANT NOTES:

 

1) If your initial Darkness doesn't work bolt for your group and the safety of your pet tell the pet to attack and the Giant will likely stop to kill the pet and give you a chance to get off Darkness again.  If not this is the point where someone with snare in the party comes in extremely handy.

 

2) Get a code worked out with your group so they can tell you when it is safe to turn round and have another go at the Giant.  Otherwise that seconds pause as it breaks the Darkness effect could be fatal (more on this in a sec).  Simple one letter codes are good e.g.- "p" meaning pet has it, or "o" meaning it is now on the person saying it, and the one you really don't want to here if it is really pissed at you "oo" meaning it is loose of Dark and/or snare.

 

3) Do not use root on these guys ever!  It really makes them mad and removes the Darkness effect and Snare.  It also has lasted for only like 4 seconds and that is the longest I have seen before they break it.

 

4) And finally do not let these things get into close combat with you they are mean and fast I have been killed in about 6 seconds by one of these guys.  They hit for around 90 and have four attacks in most rounds of combat.  I find that the Shieldskin spells are great here especially Steelskin.  It gives you time to get the hell outta the way.  Also when they are running they are really quick.  Do not underestimate how fast they can go I have been caught by one of these things when I had sow and wasn't on my guard.

 

Well enjoy and don't let my warnings scare you off.  Once you get to know the area and which people to group with this is a great spot particularly loot wise.  Each giant generally has around 30+ pp on him though you get the occasional cheap one and they also drop lambent stones, Fine Steel weapons and good words.
