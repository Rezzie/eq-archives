Strategy

The Giant Skeletons are located at the bone tower and the ruins near the zone to The Feerrott.  If you zone in from Feerrott, as soon as you come to an intersection just take a left and go straight forward and then you'll see the place. There's a Dark Elf Necromancer walking around the area but I have yet to see him attack anyone, even a Dwarven Cleric so I guess he's safe. At the tower there are two other Necro NPCs that will instantly come after you with level 29 spells if you pull anything from the tower, so it's much safer to pull from the ruins.

 

There are about 10 spawn points at the ruins and Giant Skeletons spawn in all of them at different times, usually three Giants at the most at a time. The other baddies include Skeletons, Barbed Bone Skeletons, Iced Bone Skeletons, Dry Bone Skeletons, Zombies, and Skeletal Monks.  At level 29 the Barbed Bone, Dry Bone, and a few Iced Bones conned blue to me so they were decent XP as well.

 

Giant Skeletons are very easy to kill unless you get three of them on you, two is still a challenge but it can be done.  At level 28 or below just summon a pet, buff him, med to full, then try your best to pull ONE giant with Engulfing Darkness.  Send in pet to tank and continually cast your Undead spell until you win.  Level 29+, very easy, may not even need a pet, yet I haven't tried it.  Summon pet, buff him, med to full, pull one or two Giant Skeletons with Darkness.  Send pet, continually cast Dismiss Undead until the Giant falls.  If you get in trouble just run back the way you came (towards the intersection) and hang a right into Feerrott.

 

Loot is anywhere from 5 copper to 5 platinum. They sometimes drop a BloodBlack Wand (wiz only, lore, no drop), Skeletal Fingers/Toes (necro only, lore, no drop, used to get ShadowBound Boots/Gloves in Solusek Temple) and various Words for research. They also drop Warbone Chips (no you can't summon a pet using them :P )

Additional Comments
Toxyn, Bertoxxulous When you zone into Rathe Mountains from Lake Rathetear, turn right and run along the mountain wall for a distance.  Soon you will see a tower in the corner; from here to the Gypsy camp that's farther along the mountain is a good place to find giant Skeletons. Take care when running past the tower; Findlegrob lives there with his pet, and he is agro to my erudite necro (he's about 25th level).

The most important thing about doing these Skeletons is the pull, because the area is literally crawling with Cyclops. Don't fight them out in the open; pull them back to the edge of the mountains. The technique I've used with great success is: pull with darkness to the edge of the mountain, tell pet to attack, hit with flutter, then follow with spook the dead, and shock of poison until it's dead. These can hit for 40-50, so if you don't use fear it can kill your pet or turn on you and interrupt your spells.

The nice thing about these Skeletons for a necro is that you can get 1-2pp in coin, bone chips, words, and the skeletal finger/toe quest items, and you can sell stuff and buy food and water from the gypsy camp around the corner.

Melinaa Morsvita, Veeshan I was soloing those Giant Skeletons in the Rathe Mountains at level 24 with ease.  The only problem is that the Skeletons have placeholders, that being the Cyclops. I could send in my level 24 pet with Intensify on him, cast Darkness, then Spook the Dead or just plain Fear and nuke away with SoP.  I used about 50% of my mana and my pet was still at 75%.  Easiest thing to do is pull the Skeletons to the top of the ridge where they spawn and fear/nuke them.

The Skeletons are excellent XP, probably the best for levels 24-29 in the game for solo work.  Quick spawn (if someone is hunting the Cyclopses) and very little downtime. Plus, you are about 30 seconds running and jumping to the zone line in case something happens.
