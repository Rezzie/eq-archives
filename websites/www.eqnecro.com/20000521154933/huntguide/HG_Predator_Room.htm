Strategy

This can be a very risky at lower levels but the XP and loot is tremendous!  If you go down the stairs at the entrance to Solusek's Eye through the window and then hang a right a left and another left go across the little bridge and go more or less straight till you hit a hall going left which ends at a door you will have hit the predator room.  All this must be done invisible as these halls are rarely clear, also any goblin caster can see through invisibility so don't go any further if there is one in your way.  Inside the room are three spawn points, one at the top of the stairs and two on either side of the bottom of the stairs.  The top position is the spawn point of the Kobold Predator.  If the room is vacant then chances are the top point will be empty unless there is almost no one in the dungeon.  This spot is where you will set up camp, if the spot has the Predator or the goblin placeholder in it you will summon your pet outside in the middle of the long hall and then rush the top of the stairs.

 

Once you have situated yourself at the top of the stairs, med until full mana and then break up the spawn at the bottom.  I have been able to take down two of the goblins at once but I barely made it out alive, a better way is to cast Screaming Terror on one and pull the other up to you with Darkness.  One more thing, MAKE SURE DARKNESS STICKS!  If you let something get away you will have to start over as you will either be dead or driven out by a hideous train.  Once you split the goblins at the base you can pull from one of the adjoining rooms or wait for the respawn.

 

The spawn time is 20 minutes and make sure you keep careful track of the Predator spawn because if you are meding up from a recent fight and he pops it will be an iffy fight at best.  Sometimes, the predator will spawn a lot and other times he will be a rare show, just depends on how lucky you are.  The goblins consist of the whole range of common mobs found in Solusek, flame, fire, cinder, etc.  At the lower end (24) you may want to steer clear of the casters as they can really do some damage.

 

Loot consists of up to four plat off of the goblins along with Words and Fine Steel weapons.  The Predator drops the Kobold Boots, AC 5 +3agi +10hp all/all and the Obsidian Shard.  The XP is phenomenal at 24.  The Predator gives almost a fourth a bubble of XP and the goblins give noticeable boosts.  But even at 26, I have made two bubbles in an hour and a half.  Also, on my best night I came out of there with 6 pairs of boots one shard and 50 plat in change.  This place is awesome, however the danger is high as you are in the middle of a dungeon and the train problem is light but still real.  If you get to much to handle try to Feign.  I don't recommend trying to hot foot it to the zone as there is lava by it and a bad step can lead to an awful corpse recovery.  I have died while soloing but about 3 times in the whole time I've been there which I see as acceptable when you see how fast XP is gained while solo in there.

Additional Comments

Onamatopoeia (Tunare)

After you hit 29 you can begin to camp the Foremen Room also. It is kinda hard to break, and the 6 spawns at 20 min can be a bit much.  But you can also wait for a green mob to spawn in a separate place.  If you never pull it and don't go near it and it will never agro. The secret to breaking the Foremen Room is to ST the mob closest to the door from the Predator room.  Have your damage shield up and leatherskin and just nuke the one mob to hell.  The one behind the well won't come for some reason.

 

I camped here for 2 maybe 3 levels and the loot here is great.  12 pairs of boots 2 shards 2 tunics and a scimmy.
