Sisters Island (First boat stop if leaving from Freeport)

Spells Used

Darkness, Lifetap

Strategy

This one is easy folks.  See a Sister, Darkness her, run to the edge of the island so no other Sisters join in.  Melee for a couple of seconds while pet beats on her.  Run back a bit, Lifetap.  Melee until dead.

 

These women don't cast spells, but you gotta watch it when you get off the boat because on the docks you'll get KOS'd.  Just jump off before you get there.  Also the Purity Belts you will find on these ladies sell to merchants for 1pp at least and for 3pp usually to other players if you aren't as lazy as me.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with Faydark's Champions, King Tearis Thex, Soldiers of Tunare, and Clerics of Tunare.  Raises faction standing with Crushbone Orcs.

Additional Comments

Agarwaen

It pays to have Shadow Step and Gate memorized.  If things go sour, (3 sisters or an NPC turn up) you can shadow step, then gate.  Good idea to step twice though because sisters are fast, and will swim to get you before you gate and you need that time.  Will save you lost XP and a long corpse retrieval waiting for boat - you can sell items etc.

Obamen

Sisters of Erollisi do indeed cast spells.  They are rangers and if you see two sisters of Erollisi near each other one will buff the other with a low level druid spell.  Also, if your fighting a sister and their health gets below 50% and another sister come by, but only in range of the sister your fighting and not in range of you...then it will start to heal the sister your fighting.  But even if you kill the sister while the other one is healing it, the healing sister will just walk away...

Talis Et'Souls

As an aside, there is a dwarf in West Freeport in a two story Inn, his name is Pandos Flintside.  If you give him muffins, you can get opposite faction to all those above (i.e. increase with first 4 and decrease with Orcs).  The muffins can be bought close by.
