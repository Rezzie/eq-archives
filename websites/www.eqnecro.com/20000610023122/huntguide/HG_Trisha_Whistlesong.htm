Trisha Whistlesong

Mob Level

15

Location

Freeport Inn

Spells Used

Drain Life spell, Darkness spell, Blood spell

Strategy

She is a Bard who doesn't hit too hard.  Use pet and stack you are DoTs, be careful, Lady Shae, an easy to kill paladin will help her, she KOS Dark Elves.  Trissa drops a mandolin, and words occasionally, 1pp 1gp 9sp 9cp are what you can sell them to the necro guild in Freeport for.
Faction Standing Lowers faction standing with League of Antonican Bards, Knights of Truth, and Guards of Qeynos.  Raises faction standing with Ring of Scale and Mayong Mistmoore.

Additional Comments
Manren

She cons yellow everytime at level 14.
She cons even everytime at 15.
She drops from a few silver to 4 gold.
Drops a Mandolin everytime and about 25% of the time an additional instrument or words. One time I've gotten 2 mandolins for loot. That's awesome loot for a young Necro.

 

The patron at the counter near the entrance was KOS to my gnecro. But he was green at 14 and I had no problem clearing him out.

 

Lady Shae will come into the room and attack so keep an eye out for her if you've had a tough fight and are low on HP and mana. She drops a few silver or copper and a couple of bottles of booze. She was blue to my 14 gnecro.

 

Generally Trisha spawns around 6 minutes after killed.

 

The vendor in the same room will sell to my gnecro so if you don't want to make the trip to the necro guild you can sell to her. I'd imagine her prices won't be as good but it's convenient.

 

I spent about 1.5 levels hunting sisters on the island and felt rich after that. But I died about 5 times there and porting back to sell the purity belts, then taking the boat back was a real pain. But Trisha blows them away for loot and safety. May get a tad boring once you're at full health with 3+ minutes left before she spawns but no deaths is a big plus.

 

I also hunted Lavastorm at 14.  It can't even compare to Trisha in regards to loot and safety.

 

I'd have to say, IMO, Trisha is the premiere MOB for levels 14-16 and maybe a 13 with good equipment.

 

Hope this helps.

Mohdri Castillian This is the best place to hunt from 12 all the way to 18. She will con red at 12 but don't worry she can be taken.  For 12 to 16, try this, hit her with Heat Blood and then sick the pet on her.  Then shoot across the room and start hitting her with Lifedraw.  At the lower levels you will use a lot of mana, but don't worry, you wont be these levels for long.  Once you get Heart Flutter and your new pet, things get a lot easier.  I pulled with heart flutter and then sicked the pet on her, following up with some heat blood and then suck the life out of her.  I did make an interesting discovery by accident once however. If you send the pet in before you DoT her she tends to stick to him a lot more, so if you have faith in your skeleton friend give it a try.  Some people have told me that after 16 they didn't bother with the pet, and yes it is possible but quite risky at 16.  Needless to say after less than a week I am now 18 and am ready to go tackle her sister in North Karanas.  So stick it out, be patient, and watch the XP roll in.  Oh and did I mentioned I have accumulated well over 200p since level 12?  Trust me it makes a big difference when spell time rolls around.  Oh and another trick, sit against the far wall and have your pet sit near you.  When Lady Shea walks in (the High Elf Paladin) she won't attack you if you are there. This way you wont have any nasty surprises. Good luck and if your on Prexus let me know how it went.
Salbec She is the bard in the Hogcaller's Inn in West Freeport.  Also in the Inn is a drunk at the bar who was not KOS to me as a Gnome Necro and a female Paladin who was KOS and around level 10 or so.  The Paladin woman spawns upstairs and walks down about every 20 minutes or so, do not go upstairs as the female Paladin has a level 30 or so friend upstairs. Trissa is fairly easy, don't worry the guards don't care if you kill her. She drops a mandolin every kill which sells for over 1pp, at level 16 that's very good. She also drops words, runes and other instruments sometimes. You get bad faction with Qeynos and Antonican bards, better with rune of scale and Mistmoore.
Zambam (Luclin) Oh my goodness, this is the easiest XP and cash I have ever seen!  I'm level 12 right now and take Trisha easily every time. Here is the trick: back in the corner of the room there is a table (best place to sit for down time) and next to that there is a table up against the wall with a few chairs around it.  Pull Trisha with a darkness spell and then when she runs at you, you will notice she runs around the rug lying in the middle of the floor.  Pump off your table and strafe sideways over the table with the chairs around it. Hehe then the stupid bard turns around and runs back around the rug to the other side trying to reach you.  When she gets close just strafe back over the table and start unleashing the DoTs.  Continue strafing back and forth and she will be dead in no time and you will only have been hit a few times at most. Make sure the darkness sticks and recast it if it wears off.
