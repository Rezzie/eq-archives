Arch Shielding

Skill : Abjuration 

Mana : 200

Speed : 12.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Final upgrade to the Shielding line of spells, Arch Shielding increases your HP, AC, and magical resistance

Comments:

Skyshade - The HP and magical resistance buffs are almost a necessity at high levels, so buy it as soon as possible
Lifebane - A must have for survival as stated by Skyshade. Get it quickly and keep it active at all times.


Asystole

Skill : Alteration

Mana : 210

Speed : ?

Damage : Deals 690 points of damage over 1 minute 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : The upgrade to Heart Flutter, Asystole will deal DoT and will reduce the mob's strength by a max of 50 points and AC by a max of 28 points.  It will stack with all other DoTs with the exception of Heart Flutter.

Comments:

Skyshade - Not a bad spell, especially when in a group.  Damage is nice and the reduction in strength and AC will help your tanks deal more damage while taking less hits.  Since it is a Disease based type of spell, it cannot be removed with Cancel Magic, making it nicer.

Lifebane - Good damage, nice AC/STR debuff..... But, Major Aggro spell as well as being VERY resistable. Still, when you can land it on a mob while in a group or even solo, it does let pet and tanks be more effective.

Michael - It is the only useful thing that we can cast on the stone spider in solb. My take - a great spell. It combines the fast acting speed of cascading darkness with the fast casting speed and low mana cost of ignite blood. If only it had the ridiculously low save rate of ignite 8)

Mana : 450

Speed : 15.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research and purchase

Lifebane - Definitely the first spell to get if possible.

 

 

Covetous Subversion

Skill : Alteration

Mana : 300

Speed : ?

Damage : - 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : -

Effect : The upgrade to Rapacious Subversion, Covetous Subversion allows you to transfer 100 mana to your target while loosing 300 mana

Comments:

Skyshade - Same as Rapacious Subversion.  Covetous is a nice spell when hunting in a party that doesn't have an Enchanter.  But not that nice since you loose too much mana for what you give away to make it real useful.

Lifebane - Woohoo.... literally we are manastones now!  The same 3 to 1 Mana transfer ratio as Manastones 3 to 1 health to mana ratio.  Very useful between fights to help clerics get those resurrections done faster and for cutdown on all daowntime in groups.  Best when used with Lich, Chloro, and Bond of Death as needed to replenish your own mana.



Dead Man Floating

Skill : Abjuration 

Mana : 150

Speed : 6.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Research only

Research Material : Words of Psyche and Burnishing

Effect : Dead Man Floating will raise your target Poison resistance by 70 points, allow him to breath under water, see invisible, and levitate

Comments:

Skyshade - Who said Necros can't buff? Okay, so it took us 44 levels to get a good buffing spell, but it is a damn nice one. Only problem is it can't be used in one of the areas it would be most useful, Kedge Keep. Bummer.
Lifebane - Well a truly nice spell, castable on others even. The effect lasts for about a real time hour. Very useful in places like lavastorm and solusek where falling into Lava is a bad thing. Note: Can not be cast while in Kedge Keep or the Plane of Hate... no idea why not.



Diamondskin

Skill : Abjuration 

Mana : 236

Speed : 5.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Final upgrade to the Shieldskin line of spells, Diamondskin absorbs around 400 points of damage (requires a Peridot to cast)

Comments:

Skyshade - At 10 plat per cast, Diamondskin is a little too expensive to use all the time. But I would most certainly buy the spell and a stack of Peridots. You never know when you might need it. 
Lifebane - Keep using Steelskin as your regular buff. Use Diamondskin when you are about to tackle something very mean like the ghoul lord, frenzy, etc. since the cost does get to hurt the pocketbook rather quickly.



Ignite Bones

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 210

Speed : 6.1

Damage : 494 Damage (+1 point per level until 500 points of damage at lvl 50)

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Direct damage with an interesting side effect since it turns your target into a skeleton for about 10 seconds

Comments:

Skyshade - In some ways a better version of Shock of Poison, Ignite Bones is an excellent spell. Good damage but resists are a little too often. 
Lifebane - This spell is resisted more then it should be in my opinion. But what makes it worse is the fact that it is heat / fire based. What this means is that our most powerful DD spell and our most powerful DOT spell (Ignite Blood at level 49) are both heat based, in essence crippling our effectiveness against the denizens of Solusek B, most of which are at least partially resistant to heat based attacks. 



Pact of Shadow

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 10

Speed : 2.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Transfers about 350 HPs from caster to target over a 30 seconds period

Comments:

Skyshade - Another interesting spell, Pact of Shadow works in a similar manner to DOTs, but instead of dealing damage, you are healing someone at the expense of your HPs. Not a bad spell, good at reducing downtime
Lifebane - This spell has limited usage at level 44 through 48, but once level 49 is attained and you acquire the spell Bond of Death then do you feel the power of this spell. You can cast 2 Pacts of Shadow and one Bond of Death and lose no HP yourself. Good for keeping your pet alive and healing another player in group.
