Captain Gideen

Julak - Captain Gideen says ‘I hope you have come to assist in the defense of the gate. We have had numerous assaults by the goblins. No doubt it is due to those [goblin watchers].

 

You say, ‘What goblin watchers?’

 

Captain Gideen says ‘The Goblin Watcher are rumored to be the communication link between goblin outposts and patrols. We have found Goblin Watcher Signal Torches on a few of them. You bring me back no less than three and I will reward you.

 

These Goblin Signal Torches can be found easily in the Lake of Ill Omen from Goblin Watchers (they’re pretty common drops). I assume they could also be found in Warsliks Woods, however, it’s much safer to get them from the Lake. Although the Captain makes it sound like you could turn in more than three, turning in four acts as if you turned in three and then he says:

 

Captain Gideen shakes his head. ‘No!! No!! I asked for three torches.

 

Anyways, apon turning in three torches:

 

Captain Gideen says ‘Good work Julak. You are on your way to becoming a respected ally of the Iksar Empire. Keep up the good work. Take this reward for a job well done.

 

Your faction standing with Legion Of Cabilis got better

Your faction standing with Cabilis Residents got better

Your faction standing with Scaled Mystics got better

Your faction standing with Crusaders Of Greenmist got better

Your faction standing with Swifttails got better

You gain experience!!

You receive 6 silver from Captain Gideen.

 

I’ve got anywhere from 2 silver to 8 silver for three torches (and considering they’re only worth 4 copper, I think it’s well worth it) as well as half a block of XP at level 6 from doing the quest 3 times. You also get a bandage every time you turn in three torches too. This will definitely help with those spells =)

Outcome: A few silver, a bandage, and some XP
