Mob

Broon

Mob Level

23

Location

Along the water, to the right of bridge when coming from North Karanas

Spells Used

Intensify Death, Engulfing Darkness, Heat Blood, Shock of Poison, Fear and Siphon Life

Strategy

Start with Darkness followed by pet, then Heat blood, Fear and SOP.  Broon hits pretty hard, 48hp to me at 26, so try and stay just inside casting range and always be ready to Fear again if you it wears off and you are too close.  Siphon as needed.

 

When you kill Broon, loot fast and run like hell because big brother, Droon, or Dad, Proon will kill you fast.

 

Now for the hardest part, run across the zone to the Highhold ramp. That is where Broon spawns, in the canyon next to the path.  Spawn time is 6 minutes so that don't leave much time for meding, use mana wisely.  As you enter the canyon there are 2 trees, med by the first, Broon spawns by second farther in and walks out and heads back to the water.  As soon as you see him spawn cast Intensify.  By the time you are though casting he is within range for darkness and pet.  Best part is there are NO "I see you meding, think I will pester you" creatures around.

 

Happy hunting!
