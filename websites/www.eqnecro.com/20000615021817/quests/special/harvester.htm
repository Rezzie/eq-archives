Harvester Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro


Who: Vurgo

Walkthrough:

 

1. Please note that items needed for this quest disappear once you logout.  Don't logout in the middle of the quest!  And pray those lag spikes don't go beyond that...

 

2. Head to any Shadowed Men Camp and start killing until you get a Shadowed Scythe (not difficult)

 

3. Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro and look for Vurgo.  He is close to the Shadowbound armor NPC, next to some stairs.  Ask him about the Harvester and give him the Shadowed Scythe.  He will give you a note and ask for 3 more items, a Fungus Eye, a Shadowed Knife, and a Fire Opal

 

4. The three items can be obtained in any order, so take your pick

 

4.1. The Fungus Eye can be found in Unrest.  Go to the basement and hunt down the Mortuary Fungus until you get a Fungus Eye (not a rare drop)

 

4.2. The Shadowed Knife can be found in the Ocean of Tears.  Go to the Oracle Island (loc -600 -8200) and hunt down the Goblin Headmaster that spawns in the Goblin Camp in the south part of the island (loc -1222 -8594).  Kill the Headmaster and take his Shadowed Knife

 

4.3. The Fire Opal can also be found in the Ocean of Tears.  Go to the Pirate Isle (loc +1600 -6200) and hunt down the Seafury Cyclops.  They sometimes drop Fire Opals.  You can also get the Fire Opal in the Temple of Solusek Ro by giving the NPC named Genni 55 platinum pieces or 550 gold pieces (both ways do work).  She then gives you a Fire Opal

 

5. Once you have all three items, head back to the Temple of Solusek Ro and give them and the note to Vurgo.  In return, he will reward you the Harvester
