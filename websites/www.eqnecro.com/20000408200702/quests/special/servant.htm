Servant's Staff Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Erudin, in the Wizard Guild

Who: Josper Kenshed

Walkthrough:

 

1. Josper is a wizard, and as such, probably won't talk to a Necromancer.  To get his quest, you must either have a very good faction with the Crimson Hand (unlikely) or ask a friend to get this quest for you

 

2. In either case, tell Josper you are a servant of wizardry and he will let you choose between 2 quests

 

3. Choose to get the spell components and he will give you a bag that you must fill with 6 Ice Goblin Beads

 

4. Head to Everfrost and hunt Ice Goblins.  The beads are a common loot and should not be a problem

 

5. Once you fill the bag, hit combine and head back to Erudin

 

6. Give the bag to Josper and get your Servant's Staff
