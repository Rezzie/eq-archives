Words of Darkness Quest Walkthrough

 

 

 

 

Where: Temple of Solusek Ro


Who: Vurgo

Walkthrough:

 

1. Head to the Temple of Solusek Ro and look for Vurgo.  Hail him and follow threads of info until he tells you of the Words of Darkness.  He says that you need to bring him a Book of Shadow found on their arch enemies the Shadowed Men.  He will tell you no more until you bring him the book.

 

2. The Book of Shadow is a no drop/no rent item found randomly on Shadowed Men in their various camps across Norrath.  Be aware that the Book is a rare drop and you should expect a long camp.

 

3. Once you finally get the Book, run back to the Temple of Solusek Ro and hand it to Vurgo.  He will thank you for the book and give you a note.  Keep the note since it represents the fact that you already gave him the Book.  Vurgo will then tell you that if you bring him 4 things he will scribe you a copy of the Words of Darkness.  The items you need are the Book of Frost, the Book of Darkness, 300 gold pieces (not 30 platinum pieces), and the note Vurgo just gave you.

 

4. The Book of Frost is found with the Icy Goblin in Permafrost (map#1 - map#2).  If you are in Permafrost and enter the jail area (ramp down), turn left down the hall, and go to the first room to your right.  The Icy Goblin spawns in this room.  Note that the Book is an uncommon drop and the Icy Goblin has a 25 minute spawn rate.

 

5. The Book of Darkness is found in Lake Rathetear.  If you zone into Lake Rathe from Rathe Mountains, go to the docks and face the water.  You will see an island out in the lake with a tower on it.  Go there and you will find within the tower 2 Erudite casters.  The female Magician is the one that drops the Book of Darkness.  Yes, you must kill an Erudite and yes, it will affect your faction standing.  Here is what you get after killing her: lowers faction standing with Guards of Qeynos and Circle of Three; raises faction standing with Opal Darkbriar and Sabertooths.

 

6. By now you have 2 Books, the note, and need to hit the bank to get the 300 gold pieces (not 30 platinum pieces).

 

7. Take the 4 items back to Vurgo and hand them in all at one time.  He will reward you with the Words of Darkness.  It is the open book you carry in your hand that you may have seen some people carrying around.
