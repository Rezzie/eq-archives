Greetings!

 

The charts below should be used to rate XP, loot, and surrounding danger so that we can keep a consistent rating system in the Hunting Guide.  Thanks!

 

 

XP
Rating Explanation
   
0 No XP
1 Long spawn rate and long downtime
2 Long spawn rate and average downtime
3 Long spawn and little downtime
4 Average spawn rate and long downtime
5 Average spawn rate and average downtime
6 Average spawn rate and little downtime
7 Fast spawn rate and long downtime
8 Fast spawn rate and average downtime
9 Fast spawn rate and little downtime
10 Very fast spawn rate and little downtime

 

 

 

Loot (includes cash and items)
Rating Explanation
   
0 No cash or loot
1 Up to 1 silver
2 Up to 5 silver
3 Up to 1 gold
4 Up to 5 gold
5 Up to 1 platinum
6 Up to 5 platinum
7 Up to 10 platinum
8 Over 10 platinum but no special item(s)
9 Over 10 platinum and special item(s)
10 Over 10 plat and rare special item(s)

 

 

 

Danger (nearby creatures/environment)
Rating Explanation
   
0 Certain death
1 Death is very likely and happens frequently
2 Several mobs nearby will attack and you do not have control over environment (mobs appear at random intervals)
3 Several mobs nearby will attack but you have control over the environment (mobs attack if anything goes wrong)
4 Random mobs will often show up and KOS
5 A few creatures nearby will KOS if anything goes wrong
6 A few creatures nearby will KOS if something out of the ordinary must happen
7 There are creatures nearby but you often have time to run
8 There are creatures nearby but you often have time to run and time to respond to unexpected behavior
9 No creatures around but hunting spot is not close to zone
10 Very safe spot with a zone just nearby
