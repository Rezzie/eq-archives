Ahhh loot, it is such a sorry thing. If you want a cracked staff, the only place in the 4 newbie zones i have seen to get one is in the swamp of no hope. I took 2 in 5 minutes then didnt see another one in the remaining 2 hours at the spot i camped out at.

There is a spot to the left of the zone to Cabilis. (facing out of the zone) If you follow the path till you hit the water, follow the water line to the Left. After approximately 75 yards you will see a path on the other side of the water.(Water is only about 15 yards wide) I fought about 100-150 yards down this path. There are 2 trees on the side of the path nearest the water right before the ground opens up in to a small open field next to the water. It is at these trees that Frogloks spawn about every 2 minutes(maybe less). You can get skipping stones, Fifes, Staffs, but i have yet to get Shackles. Be wary those under lvl 10, there is one type of froglok that is kinda tough for lower level that spawns infrequently as well as wandering Iksar Outcast, some of them around lvl 13. I stood by the tree nearest the open field and they would pop out and skip across the path, i would drill them before they got into the heavy trees. Be careful using fear, it is easy to loose your pet and your prey in those trees on the other side of the path. You will so alot of heavy hitting critters that never aggroed on me. The occasional skeleton would appear, but overall it was pretty safe for lvl 11.

Hope this helps some,
