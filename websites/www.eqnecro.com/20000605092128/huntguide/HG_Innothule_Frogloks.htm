Strategy

If you're looking for easy money (especially if you're having trouble buying all your 8th level spells) and netted armor, you can't lose in Innothule. All Frogloks drop netted armor, even the little Tads. Regular ol' Frogloks drop netted, weapons, and decent coin (2-4sp). Fishermen drop less coin but will also drop containers. Best idea is to find someone to bind you in Grobb first, summon your pet and then head to the Guk entrance. Directly out from the entrance (do NOT go into Guk) there is an island. Go to the left end of the island and from there you can pull Frogloks with Disease Cloud (why waste mana?), Fishermen with Clinging Darkness, and when there's a spare minute you can sic your pet on the nearby Tads. When the mobs get low on HP, they'll run, but they always run in the same direction down the length of the island and don't get very far. It's best to melee alongside your pet, but sometimes the Fishermen can hit for 14hp of damage and you may want to back off and hit them with a Lifespike after you take a couple hits. At 8th level, only the Fishermen give XP, but in 45 minutes I made close to 5pp and was so encumbered that my 90 Agility was down to 60. At that point I just gated back to Grobb, sold, banked and repeated the process. Did I mention I also equipped myself with nearly a full set of netted armor in the process? In one session of EQ I was trotting back to Freeport to buy the other 5 8th level spells I couldn't afford earlier. Not bad for a day's work.
