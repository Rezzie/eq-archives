Where: Ak'Anon
Who should I kill? -

What quest should I complete? Go to the scrapyard area and look for an NPC named Sanfyrd Montop.  He will ask for three different items: scrap metal, micro servos, and blackbox fragments.  All of these can be found in Steamfont as loot from Rogue Clockworks and Rogue Cleaners.  Collect them and bring them back to Sanfyrd.

Faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing

Other faction standing modifiers: Raises faction standing with the King of Ak'Anon.  Lowers faction standing with Meldrath

Tip submitted by Dabs
