Level: 1-6

Where: Necro guild in East Freeport
Who: Xelha Nevagon
What: 2 fire beetle eyes, 4 spiderling webs, and a few skeleton bones
How: Just head outside Freeport and you should have no trouble finding these items

Reward: ?

 


Level: 7-14

Where: Warrior's Guild in East Freeport
Who: Captain Hazaran
What: Kill Orcs in East Commonlands and return Deathfist Slashed Belts to Captain Hazaran
How: Head to East Commonlands and kill Orcs that roam around the zone or that spawn in one of the Orc camps.  They sometimes drop Deathfist Slashed Belts.  Collect and return them in quantities to Captain Hazaran.
Reward: Cash, XP, and the following faction standing increases: Freeport Militia, Commons Residents, and Coalition of Tradesfolk III

Quest submitted by Derax, Bertoxxulous server

 


Level: 1-?

Where: Tunnels in Freeport
Who: Harkin Duskfoot
What: Deliver a note to Janam
How: Talk to Harkin and he will ask you to deliver a note to Janam Rekish in West Freeport.  Take the note and head to the Stage in West Freeport.  Find Janam and give him the note.  He will ask you to go back and give another note to Harkin.  Just go back and give the note to Harkin
Reward: Merchant's Gloves (+1 AGI) and the following faction standing increases: Corrupt Qeynos Guards, Freeport Militia, Carson McCabe, Coalition of Tradesfolk I, and Coalition of Tradesfolk III

Quest submitted by Derax, Bertoxxulous server

 


Level: 1-?

Where: Tunnels in Freeport
Who: Nestral T'Gaza
What: Deliver a note to Rebby
How: Talk to Nestral and he will ask you to deliver a note to Rebby in West Freeport.  Take the note and head to the Theater of Tranquil in West Freeport.  Find Rebby and give him the note.  In return, Rebby will give you a rat wiskers.  Just go back and give the wiskers to Nestral
Reward: Cash, XP, and the following faction increases: Corrupt Qeynos Guards, Freeport Militia, Carson McCabe, Coalition of Tradesfolk I, and Coalition of Tradesfolk III

Quest submitted by Derax, Bertoxxulous server
