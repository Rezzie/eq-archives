Augment Death

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 200

Speed : 6.0

Damage : 

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Increases your pet STR, AC, and attack strength for 15 minutes

Comments:

Skyshade - A necessary spell to have your pet fight at its full potential 
Lifebane - Put a dagger in each pets hand (one fine steel and one normal or summoned dagger) then watch it fight closely. Then battle same creature again but cast augment death on the pet before the battle.... you will notice a difference in speed and number of attacks. Always keep it memorized if you have a spell summoned.

Drain Spirit

Skill : Alteration 

Mana : 189

Speed : 5.3

Damage : Drains 207 points at level 39 and maxes at 240 points

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Drains life from target to self

Comments:

Skyshade - Not a major improvement from Spirit Tap, but it still drains a little more 
Lifebane - As always, a slow casting spell so never meant to be a wizard-like DD spell, but the healing can save you in a pinch and help finish off a weakened enemy. 



Expel Undead

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 130

Speed : 4.3

Damage : 270 points of damage

Research or Purchase : Purchase

Research Material : 

Effect : Direct damage against undead creatures

Comments:

Skyshade - Good damage for the amount of mana used to cast it. When fighting undead creatures, specially at low 40s, prefer Expel Undead over Drain Spirit. 
Lifebane - Much more mana efficient then life drain spells, so definitely do as skyshade says, use this spell against undead. And hey, faster casting time anyway!

Word of Souls

Skill : Evocation 

Mana : 171

Speed : 4.6

Damage : Deals a maximum of 155 points of damage to each creature within melee range

Research or Purchase : Research and purchase

Research Material : Words of Projection, Cazic-Thule, and Specter

Effect : Area effect direct damage spell

Comments:

Skyshade - At level 39, this spell is only useful against creatures that con green. Or you could pull two Sand Giants and start dealing 155 points of damage to them at a time. If you are lucky, you should last about 5 seconds :) Awful spell, but if you really want it, you can find it at a Cleric's guild.
Lifebane - Ahh... the best use for this spell that I have found so far. Run through the center of one or two skeleton ruins drawing a train of them.. then stand still and cast this... BOOM! restock the bone chips *grin*. Or if you feel particularly brave or dumb, you can use it to try and help your group... cast this in the middle of the attacking creatures and they will all likely turn on you... quickly cast Harmshield and hope your party drops them before Harmshield wears off.
