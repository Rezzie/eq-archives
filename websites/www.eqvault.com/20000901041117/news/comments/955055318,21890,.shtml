Thursday, April 6, 2000


International Kunark Orders
- Aurum @ 2:08 PM PDT
Wow, I knew international customers had it tough when it came to buying new software, but it seems for those trying to buy the Kunark upgrade have it even worse than they expected. Here's one of the emails I've received regarding this issue:

Well, it seems that there is a huge dispute about the cost of the game now. People outside of
the United States are having to pay as much as $50.00 and $60.00 in shipping and handling to
get a copy of the Kunark Update from Everquest Store.

It seems just for Australia, it will cost $58.00 for shipping and handling, making the cost of the upgrade $16.95 + $58.00 = $74.95,
while myself, who lives in the United States, only have to pay $16.95 + $7.00 = $23.95. This also means that people living over seas would be better off just waiting for the game to hit the markets and pay a lot less then the Shipping and Handling from EQ directly. This has been confirmed by myself and others from putting in different Over Seas address's in the EQ Online Store when you log into EverQuest. The shipping and handling is anywhere from $20.00 to $60.00 over the in store price at a regular store for Over Seas.

Here's a second email from another international player:
I was overjoyed to see the upgrade online today, and yet after filling in my details was blown away at the cost to get it to australia. it was $19 US for upgrade, and $58 for Shipping. How can they justify $58 for shipping. That comes to $115 Australian. Now the Game is retailing (or will retail at around $89-$99) australian. How can we be justified in paying a theoretically cheaper upgrade cost, to get it on the release date. When in fact, we are paying more than the entire game as a whole, not getting the extra account than we would get if we bought the retailversion, and will not get it before the release date of the expansion,(14th + 3 weeks minimum)which I thought was the Idea, so that we could all pay a cheap upgrade fee, and play at around the same time as the rest of the world.

I can understand a shipping fee. But i pay $10-$15 US (Shipping) for DVD's I import, and always get them within 2 and a half weeks.

Where, and why should we pay $58 Shipping if we can get it
sooner/cheaper buying the retail version. I would like to pay a cheaper upgrade fee, as the majority of your player base will be. I will also be reposting this message, and asking all other Overseas Everquest players to be checking there shipping costs, because I beleive it to be ludicrous.

Disgruntled (for the first time ever)
David Neighbour
