Real-Life Death - Cazic-Thule
- Aurum @ 10:05 AM PDT
Sad news from Qlayen:

Hammerone Arizin, Dwarf Warrior on Cazic-Thule, played by Terry, passed away the morning of April 7, 2000.

He lives now only in the memories of the many lives he touched.

Thank you in advance for your consideration.

Sincerely,

Qlayen and Axmire
