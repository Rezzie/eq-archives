#16   Name: Zier (SourceEQ@aol.com)   Date: 4/23 @ 12:02 PM PST
Thanks everyone for the feedback. We really enjoyed reading both the posts and the E-mails that were sent.
Just to reiterate, this was just a demo. It was conceived, filmed, and edited in about six hours. The props were just what we had lying around from previous plays and works some of us had been in. (And actually the sword was from a Zorro production). We made this short demo just to see what kind of support in general we would get from the EQ community.
If we do go ahead with production, we will be using props, make-up, and cloths very similar to what you actually see in EverQuest. Props are nice, however, we believe strongly that what makes a good movie is the story. So over the next month we will be working hard on the script, and setting for the movie. Filming would be, at the earliest, June.
Those who offered help we will keep updated on the status of the production.

Once again, Thank you for your time.
