There is a story, true, but you always need to consider something. This is a game. This is not the world. In a game, there are three considerations: fun/playability, consistency, and realism. And like everything, you can't get all three at the same time, you have to sacrifice one.

The problem with Everquest is that Everquest is made to be a SOCIAL game. You are supposed to group, create friendships, and adventure together.
