#3   Name: Souldrin (souldrin@hotmail.com)   Date: 4/12 @ 17:46 PM PST
Simple solutions to current problems: PvP Teams or just pure PvP. All things can be lootable save no drop. Things in bags should be lootable. Coin plus one item loot of corpse. Person doing highest amount of damage to taget is the only person allowed to loot. Death by dot/pet still gives looting rights and is added to total damage done by that individual. Basically no safety with anything you have on save for no-drop stuff. Easiest and fairest solution IMO.

#4   Name: Chasm ()   Date: 4/12 @ 18:35 PM PST
The flaws with your plan Aza are that you make assumptions then base your solutions on your assumptions. You seem to feel that a PVP environments punishment for death should have some sort of "anti twinking" component. Why? Some ppl don't like twinking (apparently you), but some ppl could really care less (me, and the makers of the game). The rules for PVP should not exist for the sole reason of preventing twinking to satisfy those that are annoyed by it, nor should the decision about what rules are the funnest and fairest be made with twinking in mind.

You went through great pains to suggest some pretty outrageous measures to try and keep item looing. Item loot, under the current system, breeds hatred and contempt. I have been on VZ for almost a year now, and EVERY DAY I see someone flaming someone else, be it in game or on a message board, for ganking them, risking nothing while taking something nice. You make no reasonable suggestions that a: have not already been suggested and dismissed by Verant, and b: would solve this problem. If someone has a method for keeping an item loot system that is both fair and implementable (by the designers), I have yet to hear about it.

"C: Experience.
problems: less numeous races are screwed bad (they stay low level, while the others go up) and become a target.
solutions: none that I can think of."

Hmmm, the problems with an xp system that you list are a: "a lot" (very weak), and b: Weaker races/teams would be kept weak.

One solution (for racewar): less teams (2).

Why wouldn't guilds or team mates protect their newbies in newbie zones? They do now, and would do even more if xp loss/gain were at stake. I don't see xp loss/gain preventing any race from getting strong, if the system is implemented correctly (see below).

The best way to implement an xp based race war PVP system is with 2 teams, good vs evil. That's pretty much the way it is on VZ now anyway, yet the evil team gets screwed by having less classes to choose from. Give the evil team a Druid/Ranger/Bard race and the Iksar, and an xp based 2 team environment would be balanced. It would also be a heck of a lot of fun.

I also feel that the xp gained/lost from a PVP encounter should be considerably less than that from a PVE encounter. PVP should be mostly for RP purposes, with a reasonable gain loss system to give ppl a reason to engage/avoid it, but not a replacement for PVE. The game mechanics are already xp oriented. One must gain xp to grow. PVP should be no different.

How long would stay in a zone if you kept getting killed over and over, losing xp every time? It works both ways. Zones could be controlled. Some ppl would hunt other ppl. Solo pkers would soon be ran out of zones. Groups of ppl would band together and defend/raid zones. Sort of a "race war". Hmmm...what a concept.

It surprises me that you will suggest outlandish measures to try and keep item loot, yet you can come up with NO solutions to your hypothetical xp loss "problem(s)". It's obvious you are not seeing the whole picture, and are taking a biased stance on the issue.

George...why post when you don't play EQ PVP, and don't care? How could you feel that there is no RP in PVP when you've NEVER PLAYED EQ PVP? Dumbass. PVP is ALL ABOUT RP. You obviously don't have a clue how to RP.

Ikyruss...good suggestion that has been made before. Unfortunately, Verant has stated that this is too difficult to code.
