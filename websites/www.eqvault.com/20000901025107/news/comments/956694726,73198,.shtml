#40   Name: Wraith ()   Date: 4/25 @ 15:00 PM PST
Hmmm read the stuff on low frame rates and i have a few suggestions.

1. Like the man/woman above said, turn of 3d audio. Unless you have 4 nice speakers its kinda useless anyhow.

2. If your card will run in 32-bit color make sure its not running in 32-bit color. This can realy cut into frame rates if they are already low. 5 fps isnt much when your running at 50 fps, but at 30 its a whole hell of alot.

3. Make sure you have NOTHING running in the back ground, no CD's, no WinAmp, No McAffe Vsheild, No Napster, NOTHING... every little program sucks up avalable resources.

4. Reduce your windows Desktop to 640x480x16 (if your Video card will run 32 bit color in games, this will usualy switch the game to 16 bit colot as well. Depends on your settings)

5. Dammit i forgot.... hold on i will rember... OH YEA! Turn your in game resolution down to 640x480, and reduce the clipping plane. ( yes im spoiled too but all ya gotta do is hold down on the (-) key to reduce it and the (+) key to increase it.

If your STILL getting crappy a$$ framerates I suggest upgrading your DirectX drivers, and your Video Card drivers. There are a few other settings you can mess around with, but if your a newbie in tweaking your computer your more likely to make it slower than faster. That means dont overclock your Voodoo 1 up to 200 mhz, unless you like your chips crispy.
