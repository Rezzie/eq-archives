Gordon Infobits
[3 Comments] - Aurum @ 6:56 PM PDT
Here are the latest quotes and excerpts made by Gordon Wrinn on the Gameplay Board:

  • Warriors Getting Gate?
    It's a fake.

    If we were going to put Gate on a non-caster item it would be deep in Veeshan's Peak and drop at a rate of like 5 per year.

  • Gordon Exploting?
    Though I really don't care to get involved in this thread, I should probably point out that falls not causing XP loss on the PvP servers isn't a bug. It's part of a ruleset specific change in design to reduce the impact of DOT deaths, bleeding to death, and Pet kills were causing XP loss in PvP combat.

  • What happened to Kunark refunds?
    George Scotto (gscotto@station.sony.com) is handling issues related to the Kunark shipping problems directly. Feel free to drop him an email.

    I apologize for the problems you've experienced.

  • More Armor Questions
    [Quote from original post: I would much rather be able to wear plate. We all know that it was supposedly the limit of texture memory that caused us not to be able to wear plate. You cripple a nice race/class combo for that reason?]

    That's not the reason at all. They can't wear plate because it chafes their scales. There are still 4 different texture sets for the Iksar just like any other race. The hardiest just isn't considered "plate".

    Of course the hardiest stuff will still be less AC than the plate in the game since Iksar get an innate AC bonus by virtue of having scales.

  • Class Testing
    [Quote from original post: Abashi - Why we don't think Verant does throrough class testing...]

    What's an issue here is the definition of "thorough". It's simple math, 250K players can find more issues in a given amount of real time than everyone here at Verant, or everyone on the Test Server combined.

    Mistakes happen. I apologize here on the board for them. I take every step possible to reduce the chance of their occuring. They still happen, and will *always* happen.

    EQ is not a linear game where there is a specific and small set of things that a player can do. It's a complex system where thousands upon thousands of people are doing thousands of different things, all at the same time.

  • Harvest
    [Quote from original post: I think that the recast on harvest is too long, because the small burst of mana every 10 minutes isn't a huge difference. (i love the spell effs btw)]

    Harvest is ok. It is something that you have now that you didn't have before. I realize that everyone wants more, but keep in mind that we have balance issues to consider. The extra mana every 10 minutes easily eliminates on period of meditation per hour. It's fair.

  • Gordon on Kunark
    [Quote from original post: 2) In Norrath proper these mobs will chase you all the way to zone to kill you because they are mad at you.]

    No, they don't. All over, NPCs will not pursue you if you are out of a certain range. Try rooting a fire beetle on the Northeast side of east commons, then run with SoW to the Nro Tunnel. If you get far enough away before the root breaks, it won't pursue.

    The zones being bigger in Kunark do probably exacerbate the problem, since the zones are bigger, and someone is more likely to be able to run a good distance without hitting a zone border.

    [Quote from original post: In Kunark the mobs won't follow once you get out of spell range, which is very short and very dumb. They should follow you until out of visual range, and maybe even then.]

    Are there specific mobs that only follow you for this short distance? I'd like to get a list of zones and specific critters that have this problem. I asked for it once before, but didn't get much of a response.

  • Melee Classes Getting Robbed?
    [Quote from original post: Non melee classes getting ROBBED, avoiding the PvP switch, ABASHI!!!! Its called Disarm. So what do casters have to do to make $$, camp, quest, KS!!!, and other undesireable actions.]

    Disarm is not at all reliable enough for this to be effective, so we wouldn't consider it a cheat. Thought I don't have the exact formula handy, I'd be surprised if one in twenty weapon-wielding critters could be disarmed on the first attempt before getting back to the group.

  • Server Listing Question
    [Quote from original post: WHen will the server list go back to server population rather than alphabetical?]

    It's back to being listed by population. New users will see it that way, however since the new program stores preferences, existing people will see it in whatever order they had it set to the last time they entered the game.

  • Getting Harassed
    There is absolutely no tolerance of character gender based harassment. It is handled with the same policies and with the same level of response as every other type of harassment possible.

    If there is something that offends you, please /report it and /petition to let a GM know that a /report has been filed.

  • How do you react to people telling you your job?
    [Quote from original post: Cleric, don't stun just heal. Druid, don't waste mana on thorns just heal. Bard, don't twist, just "play the mana song." Wizards, don't stun, just nuke.]

    Well I've always been of the feeling that a person who built the class up from scratch knows more about the class than someone who hasn't, but I have run across a cleric or two who wanted to be wizards. There's nothing worse than being the first person in the group to call for a heal and being met with an "OOM!".

    Not meaning to take this thread off topic, but this is one of the reasons that it wouldn't make much sense for me to endulge a common request on this board that I "Whip up a level 50 XXXX and see how well you do!". Unless I personally build the class from scratch, or at least get some serious calendar time playing it, I wouldn't exactly be a very good example. I certainly wouldn't even be an average example of the class.

  • Closing Rallos Zek Threads
    No, it's not censoring, it's spam control. Deleting the threads would have been censoring.

    Systematically bumping all threads of a specific issue to the front page is spam and is not something that we want to encourage. When this happened a few weeks ago with threads on another issue, I said at that time that we'd close the threads.

    I'm sorry that this was necessary, but artificially elevating the apparent importance in this topic doesn't help your case. It in fact hurts it, because supporters of the issue don't know where to post their followups. For instance, instead of getting 22 people posting in support under one thread, you end up with one person posting in support of 22 different threads.

  • Monks and Stun
    [Quote from original post: I just read something on a warriors kick being able to stun at a given level? My question, Abashi, is: If a warriors, oh say 15 damage kick, can stun a target...don't you think a monk's 100+ damage kick would knock the target out for a few minutes?]

    It's a good argument Mooo: If a warrior can stun a target with a Kick, and monks kick so much better, then monks should stun.

    However, this is one of those cases where gameplay supercedes realism. For balance reasons, we can't give monks stuns in this manner.

  • Few Armor Issues
    [Quote from original post: 1. You said a long time ago Verant wanted to increase Pally/SK quest armor AC without nerfing rangers. Did anything ever come of this?]

    I don't think that it was this that was said. What I believe that was said (and we're talking like 5 months ago now) was that it wasn't the Paly/SK mid-level quest armor that had AC that was too low, but rather the ranger armor AC that was too high, and that we didn't want to nerf rangers.

    I think it was also said at that point that deciding not to nerf in a particular case did not mean that we'd automatically upgrade everyone else. So by deciding not to nerf ranger armor we weren't saying that we'd up Paly and SK AC.

    [Quote from original post: 2. You also said a long time ago you'd look into increasing the crusader spawn (darkforge armor quest). Anything ever become of this?]

    Again 5 months ago, but as I recall we decided against it. Frequently when new items come into existance, there's a rush of people that want them. Since everyone wants them, a shortage *in relation to demand* is present. However, after a period of time goes by, supply and demand even out.

    We try to avoid upping spawn-times or item creation rates based upon info received within a short time after item availability as the data is skewed by the new-item rush.

  • Broken Quests
    Thanks for the post Qwinn. I'll send it to the test team. There's a couple that I can comment on now:

    [Quote from original post: 3) The Cannibalize 2 quest is broken. Dread Diamonds do not seem to be dropping anywhere.]

    It's not broken. It's been confirmed and completed by players.

    [Quote from original post: There is a quest in the Qeynos bard's guild that involves delivering lute strings to a npc named Fabian. No one's ever seen him.]

    I commented in a Bard thread the other day about this one saying that it was flavor/future expansion. This was apparently incorrect. I read the list wrong, and information on what the deal with this quest is is still pending.

    [Quote from original post: A special Bardic weapon" mentioned by one of the Bard NPCs in Temple of Sol Ro. May not count as 'broken', but merely unimplemented.]

    This one *is* unimplemented and for future expansion of the storyline. It doesn't count as "broken".

    [Quote from original post: Blued Two Handed Hammer]

    As I recall, this is one of those rumors that floats around

  • Naming Bug
    We are aware of the problem regarding the names showing for skeleton models as well as a couple of others. It's on the list, and we hope to fix it at some point.

  • PoS Question
    I'm not sure what, if anything, will happen with this particular item, but the Plane of Sky items in general are getting a second look, particularly the lower level items.

    I don't think anyone has really gone to the upper levels there. There are still a bunch of things that haven't been found.

  • Will there ever be a non-planar wizard only robe?
    Much like other cases where people are asking for items, quests, etc., of a certain nature, anything is possible but we wouldn't comment on any specific plans of this nature, one way or the other.

  • Why is Emerald Jungle so underitemized?
    I've asked the test team to look into the loot issues there. It was updated the other day but there's certainly a chance that something didn't get exported. If that's the case, we'll get it fixed ASAP.

  • Abusive Players
    I'm really sorry that Crystana had to hear the things that she did. We do not at all condone that sort of behavior, language filter or no language filter.

    When this happens you should /report the incident (with it in the chat window, scroll up if you have to), then /petition the issue to let use know that you've filed the report and who you've filed it about.

    The GM will look up the report and give the person a warning just like that and take further action when warranted.

  • PvP Update
    [Quote from original post: What I would like to know, and im sure many others from Tallon, is if players from RZ or VZ are going to be allowed to move to us, are we going to be offered the chance to move to them, thus theoretically moving all the people unhappy with coin loot on Tallon the chance to go to a community where they get to do what they want, and all the people on RZ and VZ get to move to us, hopefully keeping some semblance of balance on the populations .]

    I understand your concerns and agree with most of them. We absolutely dislike the idea of messing with an existing server community in a manner that those in the community would find offensive. In this particular case we're only allowing people unhappy with the current looting arrangements to switch between Vallon and Tallon in an effort to try and make people on both servers happy.

    We basically broke the rule above when we switch Tallon to Coin Only, but we needed try a few things with PvP to try and see what the players there *REALLY* want. As I'm sure you can see from the board here, some things aren't actually representative of the way the population really feels.

    As for Rallos, there are no plans for this server at present. In all likelihood, we'll leave it's loot rules the same, since there isn't a sister server to split people off to. We don't intend to allow people from Rallos to move to Tallon, as the rulesets and the way PvP is handled in code is *very* different. In addition, Rallos went live the same time EQ did, whereas TZ and VZ came up much later, but at the same time as each other.

    We'll be looking at how things turn out after the TZ/VZ/TZ move before making any further decisions.

Gordon Infobits
[18 Comments] - Aurum @ 4:47 PM PDT
Over 45 posts have been made by Verant's Gordon Wrinn over the course of the last 24 hours on the Gameplay Board. Here's the latest from him:

  • I lost my identity!
    These problems seem to run in spurts.

    As I mentioned in another thread today, any time we require human intervention and interpretation of a policy, there are bound to be mistakes.

    In this case, the GM should have made the least significant change possible to correct the violation of the policy. In this case, that would have been just changing the last name.

    I've coordinated with the Head GM on the issue and your first name will be changed back. Please accept our apologies for the incident.

  • Bugged Deaths
    There are a couple of issue. Please keep in mind that in order to do a rez for a bug death, the GM or Guide in question has to be able to verify that that is what occured, so something like this wouldn't result in a rez.

    Secondly, this bug itself wouldn't result in a rez even assuming that it was verified. NPCs attack. They attack without warning and provocation. The fact that an NPC somewhere had you on the hatelist, but was too far away to consider chasing you, and was propagating that hatelist to new NPCs that spawned who *were* in range to chace after you, is a design issue that players just have to deal with at the moment. Everything that is happening in this case is *intended*, it just *sucks*. Sometimes things that "suck" are described as bugs, but aren't bugs in sense of something that we can fix readily, or that we would give a rez for.

    For example, NPCs aggroing through walls/ceilings/ and floors in some cases suck. It's not a bug. It's not something that we can fix without breaking a ton of other things. It often becomes a known danger of an area, and therefore a risk that is assumed for fighting there. Yes, as I said it sucks. Death happens.

    In nearly 40 days of playtime that I've put in since August of last year, I died 3 times due to something out of my control. Twice was by disconnections, traced to my ISP, and once was to an honest to god bug, where I was feared by an NPC and promptly fell for 10K.

  • Hunting Solo Spawns
    [Quote from original post: What incentive is there not to hunt solo spawns?]

    There's no problem with "hunting" solo spawns. Camping the same spot, killing the same NPC over and over again, because it's faster than doing anything else, and provides little to no risk, is the problem.

    Solo stationary fast-spawns provide a really high reward for little risk. That is the core problem that we were addressing.

  • Overcrowding
    If a server had 2200 people on it at peak before Kunark launched, and has 2200 people on at peak after Kunark lauched, then the crowding situation has to be improved, even though the number of people on at peak is still a bit high.

    The expectation of course is that people have to spread out to the new zones. If that isn't happening, we need to come up with ways to encourage people to move into them.

  • I was suspended for a "bad trade."
    Giving everyone the benefit of the doubt, I'm happy to investigate issues like this, but I need the proper information. If you have the "ample proof" that you suggest, please email it to me at gwrinn@verant.com. Please also doublecheck the GMs name. We don't have a GM using that alias. We had a Guide a while back that had a name very close to that, but he resigned a few months ago.

  • Why is the loot in Kurn's Tower non-existant?
    Thank you for posting your observations. I'll forward them to the Live team and see if we can get them to check it out.

  • Why do I get hit by mobs attacking my pet a half zone away?
    Hrm. I thought that this bug was corrected several months back. I had a situation where I was fighting Ice Giants with a friend. He rooted the giant, but somehow got too close and got killed. The giant, from half-way across the zone was able to hit me. I was lucky enough to be able to dive off of a cliff with about 10 hitpoints left to avoid XP loss.

    If anyone can come up with a way to reproduce the NPCs hitting you from halfway across an outdoor zone, our testing team would love to hear about it at eqtesting@verant.com

  • Why aren't there natural disastors in Kunark as you promised?
    You're talking about earthquakes and manastorms and such?

    Some marketing guys read the design doc for Kunark and took some of the things in there a little too literally.

  • Ninja Looting
    [Quote from original post: Ninja Looting? Will Verant stop this madness. Pictures and links of what went on!!!!]

    Please, give us some credit. We do everything possible to stop this sort of antisocial behavior. We put in loot messages so people would know it is happening, and we put in a policy making it illegal. We get involved with it and try to take care of the issue as much as we can.

    That said, though we try, we can't be everywhere at once. If we can't respond to your particular case right away, unique names on the servers give you some recourse. Please note also that we won't dupe an item to fix a situation. Until the item can be removed from the person who did it, it's out of play.

    They are indeed intentionally set to levle 55. All of the hybrid spells put in today are level 55 spells.

    [Quote from original post: I saw a post about the mobs being able to walk thru doors thing.. Think you could dedicate some of that free time and not make mobs play by rules that we cant play by]

    [Quote from original post: Congratulations Verant! You fixed the clipping plane by COMPLETELY BREAKING IT....]

    We didn't make any changes to the clipping plane functionality, just made sure the tolerances were set to the maximum levels in zone headers (where zone-specific data is stored).

    Did it happen to be raining when you were testing it?

    [Quote from original post: Quick two questions: First off... why does a combine dagger cost less than a fine steel? A combine dagger in North Karana costs around 36pp, with a fine steel costing 48pp.... any comments on that?]

    Fine steel weapons sell at a large premium on vendors because we prefer that players trade amongst themselves for the weapons. It's basically to spur the economy on. We really wouldn't expect that anyone would actually pay for a fine steel weapon from a vendor considering the price.

    [Quote from original post: Second, is there any place to submit ideas for new quests? I've got a few ideas on them....]

    /feedback in game is a good idea at the moment. Soon we'll be publicizing some email addresses for quest ideas as well as one to help players coordinate events that require GM assistance.

  • PvP
    Player classes are not balanced in terms of PvP offensive power. What I mean by this is that we didn't throw each class in a pit and hold one on one battles in some effort to create equality. Classes are balanced primarily for Player versus Environment (PvE).

    We've had to make a few changes to the way things work in PvP in order to deal some balance issues that pop up. For example, in PvP, spells do 2/3rds damage versus another player. This is because the spells are designed to be effective versus NPCs, and are much too strong versus players who often have far less hitpoints than an equalently leveled NPC.

  • Patch Issues
    [Quote from original post: Ok, I have three small issues with the new patch. First, didn't you say the Druid root series would be fixed? We've been waiting FOREVER.]

    Yes, it's in today. I'll post it in the patch message addendum.

    [Quote from original post: Secondly you said, "Illusions are defined as any spell that makes a character look different than they actually are. This includes all racial illusions, as well as other illusions such as Treeform, Wolfform, and the Call of Bones series." How many times have you told us that wolfform/treeform ARE NOT illusions?]

    From an RP standpoint, they are not illusions. From a programming standpoint, they are handled the same way.

    [Quote from original post: And lastly, you said, "there are many cases where a solitary and stationary outdoor creature has a "fast spawn", yielding the reward of fast experience-gain without the commensurate risk." How do you figure? Any time you attack a creature there is risk, not to mention a single spawn will not give you the XP/loot that a good dungeon group will.]

    And example of a "fast spawn" is something that spawns much faster than a critter in a dungeon at a similar level would. For example, a level 40 critter spawning every 6 minutes would be a "fast spawn". Now if this spawn is stationary and solitary, the risk is reduced tremendously. That's what I was trying to point out.

    [Quote from original post: Quit trying to NERF everything]

    We've still made far more enhancements to the game that people would consider generally positive in nature than we have made ones that they'd consider negative. Regardless of how people view the change, we feel that they are in the best interest of the game overall.

    For example, in regards to those fast spawns, people will often sit there and camp them for hour after hour, doing an admitedly boring and repetitive task because they view it to be "faster". In the mean time, they are bored to death. Our goal in these cases is to make doing more exciting things, such as fighting in a dungeon, "faster". This way people are having fun while leveling up instead of being bored from camping one creature for hours. That's the goal anyway.

    Yes, kicks with an integral chance to stun are indeed an ability gained by warriors post-50. I have not though commented on what level the ability is gained.

    June 6th, 8:00am
    -----------------

    *Patch Day*

    Greetings! The live servers have been updated to reflect the newest data on the Test Server:

    *General*

    - Some new spells are available for the hybrid classes. Check your Kunark spell vendors.
    - Found and corrected a problem causing people to fall to their deaths upon zoning. The majority of these issues should no longer occur.

    - Upon review we've increased the drop-rate of level 51+ spells in Kunark. They should be more plentiful now.
    - Updated pathing information in many zones, making it easier for pets and other NPCs to get around the zones.

    *Items*

    - A problem with the spell effect for the Luminary Two-Handed Sword. Current owners of this weapon will need to contact a GM to trade their sword out for a working one.
    - The Gunthak Harpoon is now rentable. It will remain in your possession when you leave the game.
    - A couple pieces of Blood Ember armor have been reduced in weight.
    - Reduced the weight on several Monk weapons found in Kunark.

    *PvP Teams Servers (VZ/TZ) and Illusions*

    Due to an outstanding bug that allowed a player to permanently mesmerize or stun opposing team members when coupled with an illusion, illusions will now automatically cancel when the person wearing the illusion casts an offensive spell upon any player-character. Players should keep this in mind before attacking with spells in an area where their faction standing is less than desirable.

    Illusions are defined as any spell that makes a character look different than they actually are. This includes all racial illusions, as well as other illusions such as Treeform, Wolfform, and the Call of Bones series.

    *Risk and Reward*

    The concept of risk and reward is very important to the game of EverQuest. Ideally, situations that provide a greater reward should also provide substantial risk. Over the past several weeks, we've been identifying areas in game where risk and reward are at a disparity, and making necessary adjustments.

    In most cases, those adjustments are in favor of increasing the reward for a specific action. For example, we recently increased the experience awarded in many Kunark zones to offset the sometimes-considerable risk associated with adventuring there. We also do this quite frequently by increasing the reward associated with a quest by enhancing the item, as we did last week with the Burning Rapier quest.

    However, in some cases there are instances where reward is far in excess of the risk involved, and we will be correcting these issues like those above as we come across them. For example, there are many cases where a solitary and stationary outdoor creature has a "fast spawn", yielding the reward of fast experience-gain without the commensurate risk.

    Though it is not our policy to announce changes to individual NPCs, we did want to let you know that you will see risk/reward disparities corrected over the next several weeks.

    *PvP Teams (VZ/TZ) Character Transfers*

    As many people playing upon the PvP-Teams servers are aware, Vallon Zek and Tallon Zek have different rules regarding looting rights from PvP combat. Vallon Zek allows the victor to loot all coin plus one item from the fallen, whereas Tallon Zek allows only coin-loot with no right to loot items.

    Due to the fact that the looting rules were changed on Tallon Zek post-production, we've decided to allow characters on both servers a one-time character-move. Registration for the move will open at 6:00pm PDT on Friday, June 9th, 2000, on both servers, and will be open through 12:00pm PDT on Monday, June 12th, 2000. You can register for the move by executing the "/movelog yes" command during the registration window. There is no limit to the number of people that we will allow to move between Tallon and Vallon Zek, so there is no reason to rush to enter the command.

    Please remember that once you register for the move, your decision can not be rescinded. The actual character-move will take place sometime next week, at a time to be announced later. Please keep in mind that corpses are not moved in a character-move, so we'd recommend against leaving unclaimed corpses around after Monday of next week if you've asked to be moved.

    Questions about the move process will be addressed in the Gameplay Message Forum available via www.everquest.com.


    - The EverQuest Team
