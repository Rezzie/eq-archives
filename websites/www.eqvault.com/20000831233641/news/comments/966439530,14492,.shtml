#17   Name: Kithias (kithias@eqrants.com)   Date: 8/16 @ 12:38 PM PST
Just a few words on the 'drawn in MS Paint' comments (not to defend it, but to explain it).

I have always been artistically inclined. I can draw fairly well, and can ink even better. I did a few comic books in highschool. Superhero/dark age fantasy type stuff. Photocopied and passed them around, that sort of thing. I havent drawn daily (or even weekly) in about 3 years though. I still doodle when bored, but thats about it.

Eversticks was at first just to see if I could do it in Paint. As I played around with the program more and more, I kinda liked the childish, shoddy, simple, STICK figure look I could create. Plus, its quick. After a get a solid idea down for a strip, it takes 10 to 20 minutes to from start to finish (about the length it takes to download a couple of MP3s <g>).

For those who enjoy my work, cool. For those who dont, cool. :) I'm a big fan of Ding and EverCrest myself.

Kith
