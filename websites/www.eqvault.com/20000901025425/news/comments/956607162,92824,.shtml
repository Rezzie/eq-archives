#17   Name: Feiss ()   Date: 4/25 @ 1:13 AM PST
On the limited PvP aspect: I disagree with this, even though it is there with good intentions.

The reason that people don't like to play on PvP servers are 1) loss of equipment, and 2) unfair practices by PKers.

Your plan takes care of 1), but not 2). Basically, most roleplayers don't want to be attacked unless they feel that the combat is... how can I put this... "well staged".

In other words, if you're sitting around and a Dark Elf runs up and starts fighting you, you don't have much time to react in a roleplaying sense ("Halt! You shall pay dearly for this insurrections, you blue-skinned freak!") without greatly sacrificing their ability to do battle.
Likewise, you have to give people to opportunity to not fight. There may be a follower of Quellious who just won't harm a fellow intelligent being (to the point of not killing bandits or guards). The only option they would have would be to flee or die in a PvP situation. Hardly fair.
The best system to have is essentially the system they have in place now: duels. If someone slights your character, or if your character has a need to attack someone, challenge them to a duel. If it's a roleplay server, people would readily accept if there's a reason behind it. Just have an understood rule -- don't declare a duel without a good IC reason, and don't decline a duel without a good IC reason. I see no real advantage to having an RP server where you give up the benefit of being safe from PvP combat without consent. The only amendment that the duel system needs is an option to declare a sort of "group war" so that two opposing parties can duel.

I have a few other comments (negative and positive), but that can wait for another time.
