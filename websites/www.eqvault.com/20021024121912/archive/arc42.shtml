- Aurum @ 7:41 PM PDT
Check out the latest information from Alan he posted today on the Developer's Corner:

    /Roll Rigged?
    [Quote: I don't have any idea of the validity of this but since it's a pretty serious allegation I'd appreciate an official word.     Aeloryn.
    Thanks for sending this comment in. I spoke with Ryan Elam (one of our programmers) and he stated that there was a time in the past that with a lot of work, someone would be able to predict what their random rolls would be. So I don't believe you have anything to worry about in terms of random rolls being able to be predicted or players receiving high rolls regularly by changing anything in the eqclient.ini file.

    I'm certainly not saying that allowing a change of religion isn't possible (though there may very well be issues that I don't know about), I'm just saying that at the very least it's messy.

And even if we were to produce a set of perfectly balanced spells, there would be no end to the complaints from worshippers of one god that really would rather have the spell that we assigned to some other god…

    It's a time consuming nightmare. A great idea, but a total nightmare.

    [Quote: All right buster.. ;-) Since you brought it up again.. I previously submitted a question about bards, Veeshan, and the Claws of Veeshan faction group over in Velious.     Well, there is actually a reason for this, but you're not gonna like it… :)

    The main reason is for gameplay purposes. We wanted all player characters to have to choose one of the 'paths', not be forced into one (no matter how logical it might seem).

    As for the in-game reason, the residents of Skyshrine are very disconnected from the comings and goings of the lesser creatures. And, as with all residents of Velious, they have been cut off from contact with all other continents for long enough not to care much for, or fully understand, the way that politics and religion have developed outside of Velious. Their main concern has been survival and competition with their rivals, nothing much else matters to them.

    There's a ton of consistency there. But you'll still have to convince those big fellows that you're a friend, not a foe.

    Populations and Economies
    [Quote: Everyone talks about inflation, sorry, I do NOT see it.     Actually, what tends to happen is a reduction of the value of money and older items. Because these two things are rarely removed from the economy, they lose their value as more and more of them enter the world. New items, therefore, because they are more unique and because cash is more readily available, can sometimes have prices that seem very 'inflated'.

Riots in Qeynos - Tholuxe Paells
- Aurum @ 11:46 AM PDT
Kenarden Silknote brings us this report:

RIOTS IN QEYNOS AS PLAGUE SPREADS
by Kenarden Silknote
Tholuxe Paells server

QEYNOS: The Qeynos Guards were restoring order this morning after a night of
rioting that left six dead, including the Priest of Discord. The riots
started after rumours reached the city of plague in the Karanas.

A guard spokesman said: "A large number of heavily armed adventurers
assembled in front of the city gates, in the belief that the Blackburrow
Gnolls were about to attack the city.

"Some of them even claimed that a dragon had been sighted in the West
Karanas.

"The gnolls never arrived, and the adventurers rampaged through North
Qeynos, killing indiscriminately."

A local businessman, Sneed Galliway, said he had feared for his daughter's
life.

"I was ready to die in defence of my overpriced merchandise, but I was
terrified that the rioters would harm my daughter, Tanlyn. Those adventurers
should be ashamed of themselves. They were so full of talk about how they
would work together and defend the city, regardless of guild or race. And
then they turned on us, the people they were supposed to protect," he said.

Meanwhile, the threefold plague continues to spread unchecked in the North
and West Karanas. The trees and crops have developed a sickly green hue, and
rabid animals howl at the moon in pain and hunger. The road is littered with
the bodies of unwary travellers.

I spoke to one of the farmers, who said his crops were dying as a result of
a terrible green mist and swarms of small green beetles that ate everything
in their path. I was attacked by several of these 'swarmlings' on the road
to Qeynos. They could easily kill an inexperienced traveller.

The farmer said: "The followers of Bertoxxulous want revenge for the
execution of Mordavin Telase, one of their high ranking priests. Mark my
words, this plague has been released by the Bloodsabres, allied with the
Dark Reflection."

There could still be hope, however. There is a prophecy in Qeynos. It is
said that a small girl will step forward, and tell the beleaguered city how
to end the plague. But no-one knows when she will be appear, or if she is
just another bard's tale, like the gnoll invasion and the dragon of the
Karanas.

A great evil has befallen the Plains of Karana (well, at least on
Morell-Thule, it has)!

Slarti Tal`Krue has some ambitious plans for the Guk zones:

Greetings!

My name is Slarti Tal`Krue, a Leader of Defenders of Justice on
the Rodcet Nife Server. There is a call to all Rodcet Nife
guilds to do what has never been done before. This is no simple
raid that is planned.

Upper and Lower Guk is begging for a cleansing. The objectives will
be listed, the rules I ask all to read and agree to, and rules will
be needed as the scope of this will be on a huge scale. Once the
goals are all met, we will have done what no other has to this point.
I mentioned that before, didn't I? Ok, first the objectives, then the
rules. :)

Objectives:

1. Clear out Upper and Lower Guk completely.

2. Make Guk safe for once. For the two hour goal time, I would like
anyone to be able to go anywhere in Guk without fear of dying!
(non raid people)

3. Get some excellent loot from the evil ones!

Proceedure As Planned:

We will assemble in Inothule Swamp and setup three waves. Groups
will be formed as balanced as possible, and within the lvl requirements
for experience if also possible. Experience will not be a prime
goal, but will be high on the list.
Once groups are formed, the first wave, aimed at Upper
Guk, will secure the zone. (The rules will explain -secured-) Once
done, the Lower Guk group will zone in and secure Lower Guk. The
third wave, known as 'Guk Patrol' will split. Lower levels for
Upper Guk and Higher levels for Lower Guk, and police the random
spawns between all camps. It is important we all do not zone in at
the same time, to prevent zone crashing and lag over and beyond our
means.

Once this is completed, we hold the zone for two solid hours, reaping
the rewards.

Rules

*A note...the rules are extensive due to what we are attempting. I
would like to see Guk run by the players and make it as safe as a
friendly city!

The Breaking of Guk!

1. Existing camps are not to be taken over wrongly. We follow all
play nice rules. Your group WILL be assigned a camp, taken
or not. If camp is taken, the group will make sure the campers
or camper, are / is well protected and will announce camp
protected. Once the camp opens, then the group takes over
and will announce to the zone that camp is secured.

2. Need Before Greed!! Each camp drops an item of value, minor or
large, and this rule is to benefit all. Need before greed is
based on your character that you play for the raid. If an item
drops that you can and will use for your class or race, that
item goes to that person. If no one can use, then the group
lottos that item till everyone has it. Intial camps will be
setup according to the classes available. A group that is Druid
heavy would get Mutant Fungus camp and group that is Paladin
heavy would get Ghoulbane camp, etc. That was an example. I
will make sure the proper classes get assigned the proper camps
and based on how many people of each class we get, as balanced
as possible.

3. Camp Swapping. Once a camp has dropped items that all has had
looted and everyone has gotten, then they will announce they are
available for camp swapping. When another camp in that zone has
achieved the same status, then they are swapped during the respawn
time. This will hopefully ensure variety of loot. With the Guk
Patrol in place, this will not be dangerous to do.

4. -Securing- a zone. Once camps are protected or taken, and the Guk
Patrol is established and in place, then we hold the zone for two
hours. I will issue the zone shout -SECURED- for all to start the
two hour minimum timer. The event does not need to end after the
two hours is up however, if all Guildleaders there agree for another
hour for rare drops, then we do it. If we lose too many people to
keep a zone safe after the goal, then the event will be finished
for that zone.

5. All campers are to take off anon and roleplay for identification
purposes. This is to keep confusion down as the Guk Patrol wanders
and you are killing a mob for your group an may appear to be alone

The GOAL is to make Guk as safe as possible while reaping the items
for two hours. This means, BOTH zones will need to be secured.

Secured Guk

Mostly, when people go to camps, or find groups, they have to invis
to living and invis to undead. When traveling in some places, you have
to stay against one wall or the other. When you do get to a camp, you
have to deal with trains at times. This raid is intended to END that
for two hours.

1. All spawns will be exterminated. No invising past mobs. Use invis
as a strategy. Invis to undead, surround the Froglock Ghouls, then
pounce. Once spawn is destroyed, heal and med up, and move to the
next spawn, destroy them, etc, until you take your camp. This is
critical for the third wave (Guk Patrol) to move in and keep it
secured and safe.

2. Please plan on 5 hours for this event/raid. 1.5 hours to setup
groups s people arrive and organize. 1.5 hours to secure Guk.
Two hours goal time to keep the zones cleared and safe. The sooner
we secure it, the better chances for cool loot.

3. High level folks (50+) who prefer to solo are encouraged for
the Lower Guk Patrol! Casters with high lvl pets are highly
encouraged! We can not have too many people here doing patrol!

4. Setup yourself as if this was a Vox raid and lag is a critical
factor. Reduce Clip plane. Turn auctions off.
/showspelleffects off. Go to your options and turn
off all damage messages. Turn off OOC. The shout channel will
be used for Event communication. This is critical to lower the
amount of people going LD and control lag.

5. Group Leaders will assign a Master Looter. All items that drop
are Need before Greed as stated above. Lotto is /random 100.
There is a code bug. Group Leaders who see a /random 2 101 will
immediately count that person out of that lotto. The bug will
automatically select the higher range of numbers after a
normal roll 90% of the time. I have confirmed this. /random 2 101
is considered cheating and therefore disqualifies you for that
lotto. Same rules apply to those in Guk Patrol who are grouped.

Message to Guild Leaders and their Officers

This cannot be done with less than 80 people, not counting those
inside Guk the day of the Raid / Event. If we do not get enough
people in the alotted time, it will be postponed till the same day and
time in one week. This is to allow people to plan for it.

1. I will need each one of you to email me three days of the week
your guild can do this event / raid. The most guilds that pick
the same day will decide what day of the week we invade Guk. Send
to Droberts@Austin.rr.com

2. There is a reason for this multi-guild event. If we can establish
that we can come together, unite and achieve this goal, I feel that
a Unified Guild Alliance on Rodcet Nife would be possible. I would
encourage each guild to take turns doing a similar event we all
can attend.

3. All Guildleaders will use /anon to be easily identified as a GL
in case issues arise that authority needs to be used. For people
who do not know, /guildstatus Slarti will tell you which guild I am
leading. You have to be in the zone, but you do not have to target
the person.

Guk Patrol!!
There is a place for you in the Guk Patrol! Your powers, skills, and
if your class has them, Pet Power, are needed.

The job and function of Guk Patrol is critical. This is to
eliminate massive trains that have been known to kill campers.
Their safety and ability to destroy the camp for items without
the worry of adds will challange your skills. All classes are
needed! A few wandering Clerics doing random heals and buffs
and even an experience rez or two will be highly encouraged.
Be proud of this role. You will be rescuers, protectors and
the person the camp closest to you will depend on in a crisis!
You know how guards are everywhere in a city and come together
when danger hits? You get the idea!

1. Patrol a given area and stick to it if you can keep it cleared
easily. There are tons of places where spawns are cocky and
do not feel threatened. Teach them otherwise and keep that
area safe for others to pass through.

2. Guk Patrol will have to understand their role completely. There
is no such thing as KSing another Guk Patrol. You see another

immediately until all are cleared. It does not matter if they
are winning or not, kill the blasted critter and if you get the
kill, then loot. If you find your area heavily patrolled and
you cannot get in a kill edgewise, patrol another area or wander
and protect, that area is secured. Like any city, the Guards

3. You are not to attack spawns that are being fought in a camp

4. You do not have to group to be in Guk Patrol. However, if an
