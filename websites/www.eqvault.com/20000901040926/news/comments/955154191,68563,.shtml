Friday, April 7, 2000


Gordon Infobits
- Aurum @ 5:36 PM PDT
Gordon Wrinn has been on the boards responding to several posts today. Here's the latest round of infobits from the Gameplay Board:

  • Rod of Infinite Thought Broken?
    [Quote from original post: Rod of Infinite thought is a no-drop, lore, enchanter-only wand off of Innoruuk with dmg: 10, effect: clarity (infinite charges like jboots). The problem is that the effect has a 30-second casting time. In almost all situations, it is better to cast clarity the old fashioned way (3 seconds), and med for the next 27 seconds...gaining more mana than the cost of the clarity. Actually, the wand indirectly costs more mana to use than simply casting clarity because you can't med (or do anything else) while it is casting the effect. In fact, it is downright dangerous to use in a combat situation because you can't be interrupted (or do anything yourself), unlike normal spell casting/meditation.

    So my question is: Was this intentional, or just a typo in the database (since clarity has a 3-sec casting time, 30 seconds seems awfully harsh)? Since the wand has no ac or other stats, the long recast essentially makes it useless.

    Thanks for listening!]

    You win :) We'll drop the casting time on the item.

  • Robe of the Lost Circle Quest
    I would have *sworn* that I responded to this post sometime back, but as it turns out I didn't, I just responded to an email interview from The Dojo. Anyway, as I said there, I'm not familiar with the specifics of this quest beyond it requiring several linked quests and having a chance to fail at the end. I've referred the issue to the EQLive team for review, but it will probably be a while due to other pressing issues that are at hand.

  • RoK Purchase Options
    To clarify the different purchase options:

    If you buy the UPGRADE, you can use it to flag your CURRENT account for Kunark. This will allow you to access all Kunark-related content (including levels above 50) with all of your characters.

    If you buy the RETAIL package, you can do one of two things:

    1. You can use the key to create a NEW account, where all characters on that account will have access to Kunark. If you do this, you will receive a free month of play with the NEW account, but it does NOT authorize your CURRENT account to access Kunark content.

    OR

    2. You can use the key to upgrade your CURRENT to access Kunark content in the same fashion as the upgrade. If you choose to do this with the RETAIL package, you will NOT receive a free month of play, and you will not be able to use the account key to create a NEW account.

  • If i have two accounts, and registered with one do i need too buy kunark twice?
    Each upgrade purchase allows you to set one account to access the Ruins of Kunark content. You will receive one CD and Account Key for each one you order. At that time you can apply that account-key to whichever account you like.

  • Shadow Knights and Lifetaps
    Shadowknights weren't originally slated to get lifetaps. When we decided to enable lifetaps for them, they were too powerful. The increased mana cost was put in at that time. Don't forget that not only are you damaging the target, you are also healing yourself. So if you want to compare the lifetaps to the spells of the ranger and paladin, you have to take both healing and damage into account.

    Lastly, I noticed that you doubled the native listed mana compared to the necro as the SKs cost. The ratio for the SK gets better with each spell. It's not just doubled.

  • Online EverQuest Store Bug
    There are certain types of accounts that don't have access to the "Store" button. We'll be addressing this in the near future.

  • How do I take a screenshot?
    Hit the '-' key on the keypad. The screenshot will be saved to your EQ directory with a .BMP extension.

  • Trade Theft
    [Quote from original post: This afternoon, 2 of my guildmates were trading equipment and money, and this guy named Gondain jumped right up between them, and basically STOLE the trade. He then blatantly admitted that he does this all the time! This happened in front of the Freeport Bank. He was reported, and the incident was petitioned, not that there is much they can(or will)do about it.....]

    We would definitely recommend that everyone verify the name in the trade window to insure that they haven't clicked on the wrong person, as issues like these are pretty much impossible to deal with in code.

  • Kunark CD Key Question
    [Quote from original post: I purchased the upgade On-line.

    Now the server menu has a button to register ROK. Says something about being able to use your original CD key.

    So... do I put the original CD key in?

    OR

    Wait until the ROK Cd arrives and put that key in?

    I don't want 2 accounts.]

    The latter. Wait until the Ruins of Kunark CD arrives, then complete the "Kunark Registration" procedure using the key from that CD. This will result in your current account becoming active for Kunark.

  • Why is King Ak'anon of Ak'anon a Human?
    Maybe he's an enchanter and it's an illusion? hehe. Seriously though, I'll check into it. I'm sure that he's supposed to be a gnome.

  • Another Kunark Upgrade Question
    [Quote from original post: If one decides not to upgrade right away, how long will the upgrade option be available?]

    It will be there for quite a while. We have no short term plans to take it down, and I'm sure we'll put up a patch message announcing when it will no longer be available via the EverQuest Store.

  • Gordon's Weekend Plans
    [Quote from the original post: At any rate, kudos for the productive week, get very drunk this weekend and hit on a woman you think you would never get in a million years, it's good for ya!]

    The former looks like a possibility, but I'll have to talk to my wife about the latter :)
