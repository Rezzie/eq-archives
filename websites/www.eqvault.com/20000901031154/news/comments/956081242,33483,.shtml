This is a "play nice" rule and certainly doesn't take into account of roleplaying. The author needs to understand that the way deity/faction/race/class statuses are dealt with in EverQuest are seriously FUBAR. There are a lot of people that complain because of legitimate reasons. For example, having merchants that KOS you just because you chose to play say a Necro makes absolutely no sense: how in the world would ANYONE know what class you are just by looking at you? Are all necros walking with the word "Necromancer" stamped in the middle of their forehead? How in the world does say a paladin in Freeport, thousands of miles away from Odus, know what class a robe-wearin' Erudite is just by looking at him/her? That makes no sense and doesn't promote good roleplaying at all.

Twinks are unbalancing. The author needs to read Brad's comments on twinking and zone mob economy.
