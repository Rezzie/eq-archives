#30   Name: Iceheart ()   Date: 7/10 @ 10:57 AM PST
#26 Lag is not "internet latency". Lag is ANY slowdown caused in the game. Lag can be caused by poor net latency, little free ram, low disk space (windows needs free space for the swap file) and not enough free cpu cycles.

Everquest is a very ping tolerant game (I played on a 56k on the east coast with 5000+ ping for ages without a problem), it's packetloss that's the killer (ie: When you start to lose your connection).

Most people will see the biggest performance improvement with extra ram. I have 96 megs and it's not enough :)
