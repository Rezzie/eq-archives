The eBay Angle
- Aurum @ 8:02 PM PDT
Colors sent me an interesting report containing a response from eBay regarding the recent Verant announcement. Here's his letter:

Hello,

I have done a lot of business with eBay
selling EQ items from different servers. I was really concerned when I had
heard rumors that somehow eBay sellers and/or buyers would be banned from
EQ. I had even heard that Verant/Sony may actually have eBay start
retracting auctions, etc. I decided to write eBay and see what they had to
say. Here is their response...

"Thank you for contacting us about your concern in this situation.
The auctioning of Everquest items is currently being reviewed by Sony.
At this time such sales are not prohibited on eBay. We appreciate your
concern in helping to keep eBay a safe and reputable forum in which to
conduct business. As always, if you have a question or problem, please
contact us and we will gladly assist you."

So apparently, as of now, eBay will not stop the auctions of Everquest
items.

Thank You,

Colors!
Thanks for the report. It will be interesting to see what measures Verant will take with eBay concerning these auctions.
