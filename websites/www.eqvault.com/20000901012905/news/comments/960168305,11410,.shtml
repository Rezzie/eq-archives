#21   Name: A former Senior Guide ()   Date: 6/5 @ 8:50 AM PST
**Chuckles**

Unless you've been a guide you have no right to complain about the service they give. People every day post about how they get ignored by guides or happen to have a bad experience with 1 guide (and thus all guides MUST be horrible! EGAD!). And then there's the ever-recurring "Bad names" threads that pop up.

I'll throw up a short education on the Bad Name process since even some of our former guides don't seem to fully understand it:

1) Name is requested
2) Name goes through server filter (which is lousy by the way)
3) Name gets through filter
4) Name goes to any FULL guide, Senior Guide or GM online
4a) This is the last guide/gm to log in and/or zone
5) Full Guide or GM approves/deny's name

Now, for those who don't know, at least when I was still in the program, a busy server can get anywhere from 5 name requests a second to 1 every few minutes. Just depends on how busy it is that day. Add to that there are rarely more than 2 or 3 guides on (and often none) to approve the names. If a guide is zoning or away from his keyboard and a name request is on his screen for more than 3 seconds it's automatically approved.

If he's zoning it doesn't even ask. If none are online it doesn't ask.

Thus there are many times of the day that all a player has to do is be smart enough to outwit the name filter to get a name.


People complain about names like Menoly (which, yes, is from a book) and names like Mastwiz and names like Feculund and I have even gotten complaints from people on names like Melody and Citroen.

When you petition a bad name the GUIDE can't do anything about it but thank you, write the name down, and hope a Senior Guide or GM can get online BEFORE the guy with the offensive name logs on.

Why? Because the Guides/GM's won't change a name on a player when they are offline. There are many reasons for it but the biggest is that it is just plain cruel. We always try to let the player choose a new name (that's legal) when we change it.

Unfortunately Seniors and Server GM's are generally busy with abuse/harassment/exploitation situations. Bad names are pretty low priority and even when we do have time to do them there are SO MANY that we rarely get to them all. Remember that the guides, even seniors, are volunteers who only log in a couple hours a day. The GM's are paid and are online much more often but if it's a player that's only on at wierd times odds are it is going to be a while, if ever, before his name gets nailed.

Hope this helps clear up any confusion.

A Fomer Senior Guide of Xegony
