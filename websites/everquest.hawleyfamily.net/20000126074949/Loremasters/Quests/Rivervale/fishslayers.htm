The Fishslayers

Talk to the Female Marshall in the first hall before the Sheriff and she will send you down to the lake to talk to the shopkeeper there, who I believe is named Fiddy Bobek, about his problem. He tells you that there are piranha eating all of the fish in the lake. He wants you to kill the piranha and bring him their teeth as proof. He also mentions bringing one back to the Marshall to prove he's not crazy. Well he isn't crazy, and there are indeed piranha in there. Jump in and start killing piranha (be careful they tend to come in pairs - and keep an eye on your air meter). Each tooth gets you between one and two gold pieces -- real good money for a level 7 or 8 player. They also have fish and scales as loot, so you can conceivably get more than 2 gold per fish. Plus if you give him 4 at a time, you sometimes get an item. You can also take the teeth back to the marshall, but that seems to be broken since she says she is giving you a bag to put them in, but you don't get anything in return. She also is much stingier than Fiddy.  You get the following fation changes for each tooth: MayorGubbin, merchantsofrivervale, deeppockets, guardiansofthevale all go up, coalitionoftradesfolkIII goes down.
