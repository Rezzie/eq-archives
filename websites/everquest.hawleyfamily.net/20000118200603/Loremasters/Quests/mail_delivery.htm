Mail Delivery (1)

This quest may cover every town in Norrath, as well as some non-town areas. Listed below are all the towns and contacts I know of. The text to get the letters or bags is a little confusing. They say something like [deliver] mail to [Kelethin]. If you say 'I will deliver mail' or 'I will go to Kelethin' it won't work, only the form of ' I will deliver mail to Kelethin' seems to work to get the item to deliver. Kelethin, Freeport, Highhold, and Qeynos are international mail hubs. Delivering mail between hubs will always result in carrying a mailbag. All other mail origination sites are satellite postal offices and feed into the nearest major hub. Bards with Selo's Accelerando are really in good shape, as some of these routes can be done in only a few minutes. The quest is sponsored by the League of Antonican Bards. So remember, if you see a wood elf bard slouching in the shadows in your home town (and it's not Kelethin), it's just the local postal worker.
