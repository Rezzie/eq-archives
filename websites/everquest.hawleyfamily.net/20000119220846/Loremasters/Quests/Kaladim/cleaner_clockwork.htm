Cleaner Clockwork

Killing the Clockwork "CleanerVII" gives you two options. Jeet, one of the rogue trainers in Kaladim next to the bank, asks you to do the special task of "ridding him of that infernal Cleaner" and says he will give you a "Miner's Cap" if you do it. The Cleaner clockwork is sometimes found near the bank in Kaladim. Kill it and return the pieces for your Miner's Cap 628 (only wearable by Dwarf Rogues).

Alternatively, return the dead body (black box fragments) to Beno Tenarl for experience and faction and the Knight Card or Eye of Stormguard Quest.
