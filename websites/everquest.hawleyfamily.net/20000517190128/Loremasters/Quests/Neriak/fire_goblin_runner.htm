Fire Goblin Runner

Talk to someone in the Warrior's guild in Neriak. He will ask you to go to the Lavastorm Mountains and look for a Fire Goblin Runner who is supplying an Erudite. If you kill the Fire Goblin Runner and return his Runner Pouch to the warrior you will receive some experience, money and possibly a decent item. This quest will not be given to higher levels.
