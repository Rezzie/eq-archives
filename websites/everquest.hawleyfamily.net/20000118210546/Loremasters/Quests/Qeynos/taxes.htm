Taxes

Somewhere in South Queynos you will find on the 2nd floor of a building a man with a bad cough. After talking to him for a bit, he will tell you that he is a tax collecter and needs you to make his runs for him. He will give you a box, in which to place and combine the taxes, and a scroll with the names of the people. To get the taxes from the, simply hail them and say "Please pay your taxes." Doing this will give you a very small lowering of faction with Qeynos merchants. Upon going to Mira Sayer, In Qeynos Hills, and asking her to pay, she will begin to cry. When you ask her why she is crying, she will tell you a halfling named Flynn something or other stole her tax money. He is in Crow's Pub in Qeynos. Go to him, hail him, and ask him if he stole Mira's tax money. He will affirm this, and tell you to say "I am a gnoll loving weakling and am not worthy to comb your feet" <grin> After doing so, he will shout it, and the entire zone will hear what an idiot you are. He'll give you the money, combine it, and give it to the tax man.  You'll get a couple silver, and an item (sometimes a silver stud and/or a Brass Ring). A tip for those of you who need an extra way to hold items. If you keep the tax collection box, you will have a 10 slot box to hold items, and you can get this as low as level 1.
