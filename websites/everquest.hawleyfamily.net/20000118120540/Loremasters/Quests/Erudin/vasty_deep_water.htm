Vasty Deep Water

If you are a paladin, you can get Clear Water and/or a guild coin by talking to the Bank manager and asking him for vasty deep water. He says your guild must have sent you to get it and asks you to bring him a guild coin and he will give you the water. Go back to the temple and ask for a guild coin, which will be given with the message remember this is not currency. The coin is a lore item that gives +10 mana. Give this to the banker and he will give you the water and some experience and some faction. More than one quest requires Vasty Deep Water, so your reason for needing it will differ from person to person.
