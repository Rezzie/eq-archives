Monk Headbands

Monk White Headband: The monk guild master is willing to provide a special prize to young monks of promise.  Collect four gnoll pup scalps, which can be collected right outside the north gate, and return them to him to receive faction, experience and your white headband.

Monk Yellow Headband: The second monk headband quest. Once you have earned the white headband, go and collect three putrid rib bones. These can be gotten off of putrid skeletons which spawn in the ruins of Qeynos Hills.  Be aware that these tend to group with other critters and can be hard to kill at lower levels. Return them with the white headband and receive experience, faction and your brand new yellow headband.

Monk Orange Headband: The third monk headband quest. For this headband you need to collect 2 Black Burrow Gnoll pelts [cannot be ruined] & 1 BB Gnoll Skin. Pelts can be gotten from any of BB gnolls randomly. They can not be ruined or you may end up starting your whole cycle of quests over again. The BB Gnoll Skin may be obtained forom a gnoll shaman or elite..

Monk Red Headband: The fourth Monk Headband Quest. Go to Splitpaw, retrieve the Skulls of Brother Dareb and Brother Shen and the head of Gahnex Drah. Gahnex Drah's head is found by killing him (who also drops random magic items); Brother Dareb's skull is gotten by killing High Shaman Phido who is a random spawn in place of Ren Rexx in the lower level of Paw; Brother Shen's head is gotten by killing High Shaman Grisok who is a random spawn in place of Shaman Lenrel at the far NW end of Paw.

Monk Purple Headband: The fifth monk headband quest. This is obtained from monk trainer Togahn Sorast. After receiving the red headband, a monk can get the requirements for the purple headband. You need: Skull of Jhen'Tra (embalmer gnoll burial ground , lake rathetear); Marnek's Dagger (dropped by the the Necro Thaumateurgist in lvl 3 of Befallen behind the secret door); Coronet of Buried Blood (also called zahern's coronet - permafrost throne room); and the red headband. You will need a group in the 30's to get these items.
