Barnacle Breastplate

If you talk to the guild master of the deepwater knights in Erudin, he will talk about some deepwater tasks, then on to a diseased shark. The Guild master asks for you to kill a diseased shark that is endangering the life of the ocean, fill the bag with the sharks remains, and the remains of 3 of its young. If you go to the docks, and kill the diseased shark, you will get shark items, plus some shark skin that is a lore item. The Diseased Shark is about level 10. Save the shark skin lore item, and kill the young baby sharks that spawned. Each young shark has a baby shark remains no drop lore item, which you need for the quest. Place the diseased shark skin lore item, and three of the remains (separately in the last 3 slots of the bag), and combine. The bag becomes a no drop lore item, which you return to the guildmaster and receive a BLUE chestplate. The chestplate is only 8ac, however, it looks very spiffy, and is great for parties, gatherings, and weddings =) It's a darker blue than crafted armor too.
