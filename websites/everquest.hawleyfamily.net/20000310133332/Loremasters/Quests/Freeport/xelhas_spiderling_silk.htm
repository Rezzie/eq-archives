Xelha's Spiderling Silk

This is a very easy quest. Go find Xelha Nevagon and start a conversation. She will tell you to bring her 2 eyes of fire beetles, some skeleton bones and 4 spiderling webs. When you give her to 2 fire beetle eyes, she asks if the beetle had 2 eyes, as if you only gave her 1 eye, and she tells you to get more (is this broken?). When you give her the bone chips, she does nothing special, but when you give her the 4 spiderling silks, she give you another quest. You will get Experience Points, Money a quest for a Cyclops Eye.
