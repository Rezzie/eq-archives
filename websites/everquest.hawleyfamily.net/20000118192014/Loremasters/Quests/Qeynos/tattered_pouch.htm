The Tattered Pouch

In Southern Qeynos, there is occasionally a beggar walking around, I've seen him all of once. His name I've forgotten but seems like it had something to do with Rats. Anyhow, he begs you for 2gp's to spare his starving life as he's not eaten in days. Giving him this will Raise some factions while lowering yer merchants standings and it will open The Tattered Pouch quest. After offering him the 2gp (you can use any combination of money i.e. Gold, Silver and Coppers to make the 2gp) he will tell you about a mysterious pouch he happened to pick up off the street and for an additional 3gp's, he is starving after all, he will give you the pouch. At this point, once again, the same factions raise and lower, and he tells you that Crow is known for being a fencer, and quickly changes that to Crow being familiar with the local goings on and says he knows a good deal about "missing" items. After leaving the filthy bum, high tail it to Crow's bar. Hand him the Tattered pouch and this quest is done. You get experience, some money, a beer, and all good factions lower, all bad factions raise. Turns out "honorable" crow is really working for the bad guys.
