Note for Janam

This is a good quest to raise faction with the Freeport Militia and Corrupt Qeynos Guards and not lower any other factions. I would suggest you do it along with the Note for Rebby quest since both go to and from the same places. After you get his buddy a beer, Harkin Duskfoot, who is in the tunnels, will figure you for a real sucker and ask you to deliver a note for him to Janam Rekish in West Freeport. This note seems to reference certain not so legal activities. Deliver the note to Janam and he will give you a note to return to Harkin. When you bring it back to him you will gain xp, faction and a minor item (usually Used Merchants Gloves).
