Wooly Fungus

This quest is given to you from Waltor Felligan (he is outside the Shaman's guild). The activation sentence is "I will assist in gathering fungus". You get a jar with 8 slots. Kill wooly spiders and collect 8 of the wooly fungus. The fungus can be found on all the types of wooly spiders in Everfrost. When you combine the 8 fungus and give the jar to Waltor you receive guild faction, exp, some copper and a low level shaman spell. A side note Waltor will cure poison, disease for a donation to the shamans of justice.
