Letter to Brother Hayle

After you complete the Prayer Beads Quest, The High Priestess might then ask you to take a LETTER to Brother Hayle asking him to come home immediately. Brother Hayle is in the prison of the Splitpaw Gnoll Den, a dungeon in the center of South Karana. Be sure to have a friend outside the bars or a gate spell when you go in, because the bars will close behind you and the switch to raise them is too far off to hit from inside the cell. Giving the letter to Brother Hayle results in some experience and some faction adjustment, and he then gives you a key with a strange name "T.O.L. 2020", and tells you to take it to Tyokan, a vendor in the temple shop back in Qeynos, for a reward. He is a fairly rare spawn. Giving the key to the vendor and you may receive a decent spell scroll (I've been told as high as level 29).
