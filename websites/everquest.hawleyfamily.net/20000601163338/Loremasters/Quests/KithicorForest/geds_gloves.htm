Ged's Gloves

There is an man named Ged Twigborn who hangs around another man and they will wander the forest at times. I usually see them in one of two places: either along the same wall as the zone to the commonlands, or by turning on the path off the main road from the sign pointing towards Freeport. Ged will make you some gloves for a price. He makes ratskin, wolfskin, bearhide, or lionskin gloves. All require some money and a specific pelt. You need to be amiable with him to buy lionskin gloves. The Ratskin Gloves cost 6 gold and a giant rat pelt, the Bearskin Gloves cost 25 gold and a high quality bear skin (ac 5), the wolfskin gloves cost 15 gold and a medium quality wolf pelt and the lionskin gloves cost 95 gold and a high quality lion skin. I have been told that a tiger spawns some times in the south west corner of Kith forest near the orc spawn and gives a high quality skin. The gloves are good for druids who can't wear metal.
