Aenia and Behroe

In North Queynos there's a blue house which holds a daughter and her father. Only thing is they never leave. Why? Well first because the father hasn't gotten his shop up yet and more importantly because his daughter, Aenia, is not allowed to leave the house. This is because he doesn't want her around such rapscallions as those lowly bards, especially one certain one named Behroe Dlexon, who works nights at the docks compiling the watch report. Well it turns out that Aenia and Behroe are in love and if you go see him he tells his tale of woe. He is upset because Aenia's father will kill him if he goes near her. The lovesick fool has written a ballad that he will ask you to sing to her for him. You need to /say to Aenia the entire sonnet. Make sure you don't make a typo or it won't work. Aenia will swoon and gave you a love letter to take back to Behroe (note that Behroe only spawns at night - you might have to start the "watch reports" quest to spawn him). Take the love letter back to Behroe and he gives you a nice low-level item. (Usually a Shaw of the Wind Spirit). For completing the quest you get the following faction: LeagueofAntonicanBards, KnightsofTruth and GuardsofQeynos get better; RingofScale and MayongMistmoore get worse.
