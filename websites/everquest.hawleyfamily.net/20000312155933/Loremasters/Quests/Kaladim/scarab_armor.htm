Scarab Armor

A dwarf named Vacto Molunel in the stormguard hall will make Scarab armor for you. You have to get 2 scarab carapaces and 5 gold for a helm and one pristine carapace and 23 or 33 gold for a Scarab Breastplate.  The scarab carapaces can be found on the small worker scarabs in butcherblock and the pristine scarab carapace can be found on Death Beetles in Unrest or possibly on giant scarabs (rarely). Once you get your faction high enough, he will also create Scarab Boots for you. These take a Cracked Giant Scarab Shell, 2 Scarab Legs, and 17 gold.
