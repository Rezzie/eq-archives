Squire Narl

Every once in while a loug mouthed squire named Narl appears near the third inn. He is not a very high level, but can do some serious damage. Kill him and take his head to Altunic, in the hut nearby (Altunic sometimes wanders the zone too). This will increase your faction with the Knights of Truth and also get you a little money and exp. After that, he will ask you to get a cloth shirt (or some other similar item) for him. Buy the item and give it to him for more exp and a token of generosity (the token is needed fro the Freeport Test of Truth).
