Faren's Tacklebox

On the docks in Qeynos (not the boat dock, the other ones south of there) is a fisherman named Faren. Guard Beren will tell you that Trumpy, a dwarf, keeps pestering his poor brother Faren. Corshin thinks Trumpy is a friend of Commander Bayle. Fish with him for awhile & eventually Trumpy Irontoe will walk up and begin to taunt him. Faren will then say: "Hey! My [tacklebox]! You rotten dwarf!" or something similar. If you ask him about the tacklebox, he will explain that Trumpy just knocked it into the bay, he can't swim and could you please get it. Jump in (you have practiced swimming, right?) and look around. What fooled me the 1st time is the tacklebox doesn't look like a tacklebox, of course, it looks like dropped loot. Get it and return it to Faren for some exp, good faction bonuses some copper and a Tunic - which is a lore item. As a side note, you can also talk to Trumpy and he will give info on the Bloodsabres - for free, without buying any drinks.
