Peackeeper Staff

Speak to Lumi Stergnon in the clerics guild and offer to retrieve the peacekeeper staff. He will hand you a letter which you should take to Emil Parsini, a merchant in the first hut outside of Erudin in the Tox Forest. He will give you a staff to fill with treant resin and an odus pearl. In the harbor there is a place where you can at times find 2 Odus Pearls under 2 Sand Sharks fairly close together (on a side note sometimes there will be divers seen swimming here who I believe also collect the pearls, if you kill one of these swimmers they say the Circle of Unseen Hands will get revenge your faction with the Qeynos Guards goes up faction with corrupt guards and the circle of the unseen hands go down I also think faction with Qeynos merchants went down)... You want to get both of these because in order to get the treant chips you need to talk to a man in the upstairs of the cobbler shop in North Qeynos... he says he will give you some treant chips if you do a favor for him... the favor is to get him an Odus Pearl give him a pearl and he will give you the treant chips you need to get the treant resin made. Once you combine an odus pearl (from the harbor) with the resin, you get a peacekeeper staff (Damage 4/Speed 30--weapon for clerics and paladins). If you return the staff to the cleric, he will give you faction, xp, a random item and an additional quest, or  you can keep the staff. This quest will not be given after level 12.
