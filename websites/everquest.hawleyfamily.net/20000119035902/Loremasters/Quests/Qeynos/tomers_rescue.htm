Tomer's Rescue

You probably need high faction with the monks to do this quest. Tomer Instogle is a Monk hurt near the western walls outside of Qeynos city. He will tell you that he has been hurt and needs to get back to his guild in Qeynos. Leave him and go to the Silent Fist Guild in N. Qeynos City. Talk to Seta Bakindo in the back room. Ask her about Tomer and she will ask you to escort him back. Go back to Tomer and tell him "I will escort you back".  He will ask you to hold his backpack for him and will follow you. Take him back to Seta and tell her "I rescued Tomer". Give Tomer back his backpack for Faction and Exp.
