Shakey the Scarecrow's Stuffing

At the Tagglefoot farm, hail Shakey the Scarecrow, and one of the Leafsway NPCs will comment that Shakey isn't himself lately. There are two quests out of this: the head and the stuffing. It seems Shakey's stuffing is rather old, and his pumpkin head is beginning to crack. To fix his stuffing, you need magical hay. The Magical Hay that must be harvested can only be found in the Plains of Karana. If you travel to the corn fields of West Karana and kill some Scarecrows there, you may sometimes get some magic hay for loot. I have laso been told that Cleet Miller will drop bails of hay as he wanders his field. Next, you are required to obtain a Flask of the Blessed Oil of Life. How to obtain this is detailed in The Blessed Oil Quest in W Karana. Now comes the very hardest part. You must have the hay cursed by the High Priest of Cazic-Thule named Roror, who lives in the Feerrott. Hail him and say 'Bless my hay' or 'Curse my hay' he will tell you he wants 66 gold to bless the hay. Give him the 66 gold and hay at the same time, he will give you back a sack of cursed hay. After the hay is cursed, you must find a forge, such as you would use to make armor, and combine the cursed hay with the blessed oil of life and you will get a sack of writhing hay. You must have a good blacksmithing skill to so this. Then give the result to Shakey and you will receive a pretty nice item (Wee Harvester or Belt of the River).
