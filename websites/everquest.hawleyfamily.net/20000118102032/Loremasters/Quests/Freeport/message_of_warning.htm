Message of Warning

This quest is given after you complete the Message to a Spy quest. The Knights of Truth agent inside the militia (which you uncovered in the Message to a Spy quest) gives you a note which you are to take back to the hall of truth. The note says "Zimel's Blades", and a bunch of otherwise illegible writing. If you take it back to the knight who gave you the message to agent quest, she will give you xp and a faction adjustment. (Incidentally, though you shouldn't have to fight in this quest, there is a really good action sequence you might not want to hang around for).
