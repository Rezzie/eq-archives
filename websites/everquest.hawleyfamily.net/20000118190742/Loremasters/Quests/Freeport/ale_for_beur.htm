Ale for Beur

Beur Tenlah stands in the tunnels not far from the tavern by the dock. He is thirsty, but too lazy to go all the way up to the tavern for his favorite dwarven brew. Go buy him a Dwarven Ale and you will gain some experience, faction and his thanks, as well as a new quest from his pal Harkin.

Note: If you are an evil race the Ogre Paladin (How can this class exist?) will kill anyone evil who walks into the bar to buy the ale. So you need to buy it elsewhere before you go to Freeport. This quest no longer raises faction with the Freeport Militia.
