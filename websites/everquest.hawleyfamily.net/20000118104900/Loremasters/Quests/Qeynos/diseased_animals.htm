Diseased Animals

Priestess Caulria in the temple in North Qeynos (the one in leather near the back wall) gives the quest, which requires you to look into the occurrence of rabid wolves in the area. Rabid wolves and bears are found in the Qeynos Hills. Return a 'diseased wolf pelt' to her for faction bonuses, experience and a 'cure disease' spell that she casts on you to purge you of that annoying PlagueRatDisease and Rabies. There are also rabid grizzly bears in the Hills, and if you return one of the pelts to her, you get a spell scroll in addition to the faction bonuses. You do not get a cure for this quest, so make sure you keep a diseased wolf pelt to give to her after the bear pelts, as rabies is annoying and takes an age to wear off. Sometimes, fairly rarely, you get a 'fleshy orb' on one of the rabid creatures. Give this to her, you'll get faction bonuses, some cash, and she'll give you back a lore version and ask you to take it to someone in Freeport (Lorme Trethadore or something like that)(Note there is a Lorne Tredore in the academy, but this doesn't seem to be the right person).
