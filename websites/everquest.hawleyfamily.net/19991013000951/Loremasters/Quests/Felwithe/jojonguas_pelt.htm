Jojongua's Pelt

The Felwithe Paladin guild master will ask you if you are "ready to serve the Clerics of Tunare" if you hail him. Say "I am ready to serve the Clerics of Tunare" and he will ask if you are new to this or experienced (something to that degree). If you say you are inexperienced, you get the Bandit Sisters quest. However, if you say you have been with them for awhile (or whatever [xxxx] line equates to that) he will give you a quest to go to Innothule Swamp and slay Jojoojojgogogoguna, the long-named huge Kobold lord in the aptly named Jojongua's Tower. Take his pelt to the guildmaster. I have not been told of the reward for this quest or if it continues.
