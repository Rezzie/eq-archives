Halfling Raider Helm

A Guard in Foreign Quarter will announce that the indigo brotherhood is looking for some to kill halfling raiders. He will direct you to go talk to the brotherhood for more information. A warrior in the guild will tell you he will pay you 1 GP for every Halfing Raider cap you return to him. Get some gold, experience, and faction increase/decrease.
