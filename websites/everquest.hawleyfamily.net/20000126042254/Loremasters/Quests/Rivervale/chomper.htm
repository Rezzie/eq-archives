Chomper

A woman on the second floor in the rogues guild (Fool's Gold building) wants you to prove yourself as the new dishwasher by finding a missing jewel that is inside of a fish named Chomper. Chomper is a level 3 small aggressive fish that can be found in the water by the Rivervale docks. He always spawns with a Large Ruby that you return for some faction, experience, and some silver.
