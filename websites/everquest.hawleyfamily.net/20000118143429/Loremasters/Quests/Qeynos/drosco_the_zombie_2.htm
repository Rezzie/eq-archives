Drosco the Zombie #2

The amulet and letter that Drosco carries can also be taken to the Temple of Karana (Temple of Thunder) in South Qeynos. The amulet can be given to Wolten Grafe, the Keeper of the Medallions of the Order of Thunder, for xp, faction adjustments a minor item and a blessing. (There may be a female in the temple who also will reward you for the amulet). It could also lead to another quest. The Priest may decide to give you a scroll to Give To Eestyana Naestra, who is in the Hall of Truth in Freeport. The reward for this part of the quest is usually a nice item (sometimes a prayer bead necklace). These factions change when you kill Droscoe:  BloodSabers and Corrupt Guards of Queynos go down and Order of Thunder goes up. When you turn in the items, the faction change is the exact opposite, so faction is a wipe in this quest.
