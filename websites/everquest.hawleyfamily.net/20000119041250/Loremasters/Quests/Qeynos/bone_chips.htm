Bone Chips

There's an man named Lashun Novashine. He's found either on the runway leading up to the flying saucer, or out wandering around by the gates. You'll see him shouting out "Cease this endless conflict ... " If you give him two skeleton bones, he will give you exp and faction and sometimes cast a healing spell on you.
