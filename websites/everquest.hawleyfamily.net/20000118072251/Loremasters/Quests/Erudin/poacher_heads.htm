Poacher Hunter

Those nasty poachers wandering around Toxxulia haven't just annoyed you. They've apparantly enraged one of the townspeople who stands just outside the Temple of Quellios. His request is a little gruesome, but hey it's a barbaric world out there. Bring him head of a poacher and he will reward you with a little money, experience and faction. Also, after bring back several heads and bone boxes, the npc ask you to find out who rules the poachers. You are told that you must find a clue on the body of a poacher, and the one who summons the skeletons and bring back their head. I don't know the outcome of this part of the quest.
