The Bind

Llscent Tagglefoot by the druids guild is in a bind. He will say he needs someone to deliver a case of jumjum juice to Xanuusus a treant in the Northern Karanas Plains. Say "I will take the Jumjum Juice" and you will be provided with a case. Getting to Treant is a long walk. Go to North Karana and follow the water to the right. When you hit the mountains follow them and you will run right into him. I have been told you no longer have to levitate to give him the jum jum juice. Also, apparantly Xanuusus is not smart enough or just doesn't care to distinguish between the jum jum you get from Llscent and that you find from looting bixies. Who's going to argue with a Treant? So any case of jum jum juice will work. Factions go up with StormReapers, MayorGubbin, and MerchantsofRiverVale, and went down with the UnkemptDruids. You will also get some minor coin and a minor item. Considering that bixies are very low level creatures and drop jum jum quite frequently, this may be a good way for low level characters to make some money (Assuming you survive the trek through N Karana).
