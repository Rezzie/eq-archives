The Donations

One of the Knights of Thunder gives you a donation box and asks you to find six residents of Qeynos to donate to the temple. Here's a list of where to get the six donations: 1) Lanhern Firepride - Only at night when she is in Fish's bar. Give her an ogre swill for one; 2) Hansl Bigoon - Either at his house, or the office of the people; 3) Kane Bayle - Guard House; 4) Enil Runik - Temple of Life; 5) Guard Leopold - Qeynos Hills, wanders; 6) Guard Cheslin - Qeynos Hills, wanders - Frequently dead. The quest chest also requires your 'PK Note' and the 'PK Note' of another. There are other people who respond to "donation", but don't seem to cough one up: The cloth armor salesperson in front of the Lion Mane Inn and Tavern (she says to go away) in South Qeynos. The two gents who make bags in North Qeynos. A bard in the Bard's guild (says he donates to his own cause) in South Qeynos. Also in South Qeynos, a merchant by the Grounds of Fate who only makes three things, axes I believe. He laughs because of the taxes being so high. Oh, so do the bag makers.
