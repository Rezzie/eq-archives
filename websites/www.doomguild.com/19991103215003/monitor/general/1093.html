Ok, but you didn't give any yourself.

The advantage of a DE necro is they don't need to care about factions and can solo off of guards and quest npcs (Thanks for putting Tone Deaf into nek forest Verant).

Everybody knows about avocets so thats not much.

Karg Iceberg in everfrost, xp and nice loot, but spawns about every 8 hours.

The lone spectre in feerott, nice fast spawn, don't know if doable at 34 and is probably camped.

Griffins / HGs / Clops are all doable at this lvl but are they worth the effort? probably not.

There is one solo hag in unrest that you can do, she is under the waterfall accessible through a secret underwater tunnel next to the gnome spelunker. But it will be tricky as you have to be toe-to-toe with her, it's a small cave.

If your faction with the frogs is good you can find several lone undead frogs in LGuk that you can do, but it's risky.

Try grouping, at 34 you could do Basement of Unrest, Lower Guk, Cazic-Thule. Overall it's more fun and with the right group the xp flies. I got my druid through lvl 35 in 12 hours in a full group.

I do know what to kill to get from 48-50 fast, and evreyone will love you for it. The two rangers/druids in Quenos Hills. Fast spawns, walk around so easy to get them all alone, added bonus is you can camp for pyzjin in a vain attempt to get GBS. But getting xp at at the same time.

Maybe the barbs you're thinking of are Ulrich and Einhorst Mcmannus in the barbarian fishing village. Ulrich is low 30s and Einhorst is 35. The village is in the sw corner of the zone enxt to the river. Ulrich patrols and he is linked to all the other mcmannus's so u have to kill him first. Einhorst lives in a little hut, only pull him after ulrich is dead or both will come regardless of where ulrich is. They drop ok cash, and medium brz armour which you can sell for good price if you're patient, and fs weapons which you can sell to the vendor aliana who is right in the fishing camp and is always indifferent. The faction is with stuff in halas, warrior guild and merchants, so forget about entering halas if u camp these guys for a long time.
