3 . "dorfs in butcher"

Great place to solo is butcherblock, there are so many L35 NPC dwarves around its not funny. One house with I think 3 or so in or around, I never go there cause its always camped. The dock has, hmm, 7 I think, but noone (cept me =) kills the one where the boat comes cause of bad faction. The other 6 are usually camped by druids and high elves.

I usually go check the stone circle to see if its camped, there's 2 there. If its not, I fear one, me and my pet take out the one that comes, wait a few minutes for the first one to come back, kill it, then take out the paladin near the dock, then the one where the boat comes.

You should have no trouble killing them, their resist rate is reasonable, and at 44, assuming you have the pet, prolly wont spend more than a bar of mana using the darkness pet fear method. I've done it with only half a bar =)

Oh btw, I made 1100pp there last night from the 20pp axes they drop!

Good luck!
