1 . "Armor does help"
 Posted by Kefka Zinn on Nov-19-99 at 12:07 PM (EST)
Here's the skinny on how to equip Skinny:

1) Pets, in their natural state, are in what the game calls the "Unarmored" mode. This applies to all monster/NPC objects in the game when they are not equipped. Your pet has a base AC of next to zero.

2) As with any mob, giving your pet a single piece of armor, no matter what type, will switch it to "armored" status. It will experience a minimal, but still significant, increase in AC.

Now because the AC flag is activated just by the presence of any piece of armor, it would be a waste of your time and money to give your pet, say, a full suit of banded -- when a simple cloth cap will give it the same AC bonus. So basically, give your pets a cheap, single piece of armor along with their daggers to help them stand a bit more action.

Hope this helps. :)
