"research needs re-vamped"
 Posted by Kierlak on Sep-08-99 at 10:40 AM (EST)
Hello all.

/rant on

I like the idea of researching higher level spells because it fits well with the roleplaying aspect of the game. I like to feel like I've actually earned my spells and not just had them haded to me, but it need re-worked in a big way.

If I've hit 24, then I've earned the right to my level 24 spells. Likewise for 29, 34, etc. But in its current state, I've merely earned the right to try to track down the words for my spells, and also the right to fail miserably and get nothing for my efforts.

I've tried to research Intensify Death 4 times now. Once at 24, 25, 26 and 27. Not one time has it succeeded. I have finally acquired 2 more sets of the words, but I don't dare try it again until 29 just to try to give myself some more security. And I'll bet it fails at least once, if not both times. That's ridiculous.

Words should drop one circle earlier. 24th words should load on 20-23 mobs, 29 words on 24-28 mobs, and there should be a reasonable chance of success. If I want a level 24 spell, and I'm 27 or 28, then it should be trivial, or damn close to trivial, if my research skill is maxxed at the guild hall. Especially since I've never seen research go up on its own from trying to create a spell. Anything that I have ever successfully researched has either left my research skill as is, or has been trivial.

On a side note: is the blasted research quest fixed yet? If so, is it worthwhile? Will it raise research higher than you can train in the guild?

I want my spells!! Useful words are hard enough to find as it is without burning them to no good purpose. And I don't have the bank space to hold level 24 words until I've reached level 50 so that I can get my level 24 spells.

/rant off

Thank you.
Kierlak SoulReaver
level 28, The Rathe
