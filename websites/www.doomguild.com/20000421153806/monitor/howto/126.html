2 . "Actually..."
 Posted by Dumuzi En'Sabah'Nur on Nov-01-99 at 10:32 PM (EST)
I believe you lose faction with the Indigo Brotherhood (the Neriak guards) if you kill Crushbone orcs. It is possible to raise your faction with the Indigo Brotherhood by killing certain NPCs. There is a level 25 wood elf named Glyndir Tindel in Lesser Fay at 330, 1250. There is also a camp very close to this, so close that you should be careful, that has 2 wood elves. They are blue to me at level 30, Glyndir is too, but I think that they are closer to level 30.

I know that this doesn't help you much at your level but at least you know you'll be able to improve your faction later. I've also heard that there are some elf prisonners somewhere that you can kill to raise your Indigo Brotherhood faction. They might be in Butcher but I'm not sure.

Good luck.

Dumuzi En'Sabah'Nur.

5 . "another indigo brotherhood faction booster"
 Posted by Scull of Neriak on Dec-12-99 at 04:04 PM (EST)
there are a collection of NPCs that spawn one at a time in Innothule swamp just outside of the Guk entrance.

So far I've seen at least 3 different named NPCs, all around level 16 to 20. Two of the names are "Jyle Windstorm" and "Fandyl Arithin". Jyle looks like a wood elf to me, and Fandyl looks like a high elf I think.

All give the indigo brotherhood faction change but the other factions are more based on the race of the NPC.

The faction changes for Jyle Windstorm are:
Emerauld Warriors - worse
Indigo Brotherhood - better
Merchants of Felwithe - worse
Kelethin Merchants - worse

 
6 . "You need faction with Dreadguard Outer/Inner..."
 Posted by Gravedirt on Dec-15-99 at 08:13 PM (EST)
As these are the guards of Nektulos, and Neriak respectively.
Faction for both can be gained by killing the named halflings in the two leatherfoot raider camps, and the druid ring in Nek. The druid ring halflings are level 3-5, for newbie DEs to work out on, so they are pretty easy meat.
The leatherfoot camp ones are about level 8-12, and a variety of classes except for Leatherfoot Deputy (warrior, about level 20-21) and Leatherfoot Medic (cleric, level unsure, but higher than deputy) who wander between camps, and beat the snot out of unwary druid ring campers. As they are named, you take a large faction jump with Dreadguard, and a large faction hit with all Rivervale guards/merchants when you kill them. This is the most effecient way to gain access to Neriak, and once inside there are various quests you can do to raise your Dark Bargainers (merchant) faction to be able to buy and sell here. NOTE: if you are KOS in Oggok and Grobb, stay out of Ogre/trolltown in the foreign quarter of Neriak! they have a seperate faction over there.
