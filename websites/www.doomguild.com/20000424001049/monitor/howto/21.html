"How to make level 10-12 in no time"
 Posted by Jentiv of RZ on May-19-99 at 02:23 AM (EST)
Two words: Skeleton Lrodd. He's a level 11-13 mob that spawns in a corner of Befallen level one, and for some odd reason he gives absolutely INSANE experience for his level. He also respawns every six and a half minutes, which is pretty danged fast, and his respawn rate is independent of the number of players in the zone.

What you will need to use my strategy:
-- Maxed Evocation skill. You can't afford to fizzle.
-- Lots and lots of bone chips.
-- A good sense of timing and judgement.

What you do is this: kill the skeleton that spawns in the middle of Lrodd's hallway (Lrodd won't react to this). Do NOT attempt to kill any skels on either side of him, because you will pull him too. Have a pet summoned; don't worry too much about the quality of the pet, although having a good pet helps at level 10. Have full health and mana. Walk cautiously up to the point in the hallway right between the two doors. Target Lrodd, Ward him once, let him take a swing at you, then back off while your pet engages. Back off as far as you can and hit him with Wards as fast as possible.

Now for the part that takes timing and judgement. When he is down to about 10% of his health, stop Warding him and let him beat on your pet. What you really want is to time it so that he kills your pet and has just enough life left that one Ward will take him down. It takes a little practice to determine when that is, but it is definitely worth it, since you get almost twice as much XP without a pet. As soon as your pet dies start clicking on Ward. He may interrupt you a couple of times, but he hits fairly slowly so you can always get a spell off before he kills you. Once you've gotten good at fighting him, you will be able to realize when your pet is going to lose very quickly, and just zone rather than fight a lost cause.

I didn't start seriously using this technique until level 11 (when I finally got fed up with everything /conning either green or red), but I did successfully do it several times at level 10. It might be possible at level 9, assuming your Evo is maxed, but you'd probably die too often to make it worthwhile. Remember that avoidance of death is the most crucial thing at this level.

Anyway, I made it from half a bubble in L11 to L12 in less than four hours, which is very fast indeed.

(P.S. There is a path error in the Befallen lobby that allows you to kill Lrodd without taking a hit. However I consider this an exploit, and Verant undoubtedly would too. Be careful or you might find yourself banned.)

5 . "not an exploit"
 Posted by jelenator on May-26-99 at 00:06 AM (EST)
I actually petitioned a gm about this just to see what they thought about practicing Ward Undead on your pet. He felt it was just practicing a spell, not an exploit. His reasoning was that if you were out practicing it on hostile undead, you'd be getting exp and loot as well as raising your skill, while when you practice it on your pet you are basically just burning mana. Or something like that.

What I did to get evocation up some was to cast Cavorting bones to get the really weak pet, and I'd practice Ward Undead on it while I was fishing in Neriak. You can cast spells while you are fishing, and you can fish while you are meditating, if you have it hotkeyed and you click on the button. Now the fishing-while-medding is probably an oversight on Verant's part, but I won't tell if you won't.
