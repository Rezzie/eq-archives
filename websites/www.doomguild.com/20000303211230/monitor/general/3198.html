2 . "Okay detailed walkthrough..."
 Posted by Etadanik on Nov-27-99 at 01:59 AM (EST)
First check if there are any zols in the room... If there are pull them first; hopefully the executioner will not be there with a zol.

Second, facing the exec. room, to your left there is a small curve in that is virtually absent of monsters. You should kill any patrols(and there are at least two) that wanders near the executioner room, then pull the executioner spawn(the one in the middle, standing ontop the table) and kill it with a pet. Note not using a pet is okay but much harder, and requires more meditation which means more monster magnet time which defeats the purpose of not using a pet(wandering spawns won't aggro on pet) anyway.

Now the executioner is a weakling, I would say the weakest of all four spawns in the tower. He has no special abilities other than he has alot of hp's(but then so does cavalier), so all you have to do is root, send pet, DoT with fastest Dot's(Venom and BoD works nice, or you can use Ignite Blood if you don't mind wasting mana), and heal pet.
