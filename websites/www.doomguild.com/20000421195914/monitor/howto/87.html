3 . "You might try this. . ."
 Posted by Menil on Aug-04-99 at 12:03 PM (EST)
When you first start EverCrack, select the "Options" button, and change your driver from the Glide driver to the DirectX 6.x Voodoo2 Driver. I was having problems switching resolutions with the Glide driver -- it locked up every time. I switched to the DirectX driver, and everything has been perfect since.

BTW, I am using dual Voodoo2s, and most of my equipment is similar to yours (incl. cable modem), so post your results to see if you have the same problem with the Glide driver, and if the DirectX fixes it.
