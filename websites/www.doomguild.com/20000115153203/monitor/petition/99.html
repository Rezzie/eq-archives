6 . "necromancer problems"
 Posted by Nebtawy on Sep-06-99 at 03:44 AM (EST)
Yes, I agree fully with the notion that the designers of EQ are not familiar enough with the necromancer character. I think there has been a slant favoring the druid character (gee wonder why) while the necromancer has been what I would call a designer after-thought. After the high 20 levels the game got kind of lame for me, the new pet spells stunk and travel time was just too long. Guess I will have to camp for JBoots although I really don't have the patients to camp for 8 to 13 hours. I wish they would upgrade or do away with the silly spells like voice graft and the different vision spells (one good one would be fine). Here is an idea, as a true caster class necros should get teleport to undead areas or other areas where only the so called evil characters are accepted.

As for grouping, with the exception of the bug problem which I have experienced only once, I gather some necros feel useless when compaired to a wizard for grouping purposes. My response to this is when you get into the 20's and blue cons hit for 50 points repetitively, you will learn the value of a DOT. I will give an amusing example, I was level 27 and fighting with a 27 ranger at the aviak village in South Karana when a much lower level wizard tried to KS our prey. He casted as hard and fast as he could after I had already hit a Harrier Aviak with H Blood and the ranger was hitting in meelee. Well the aviak turned, attacked and killed the KSer wizard in short order (we ROFL of course).
Hope everyone gets the point, hitting harder isn't always better. My strategy when in a group and hunting a hard hitting monsters is to cast boil blood after the monster is pulled, let the tanks hold the monster and then after boil blood or other DOT's do their work let the wizard blast away or use your DD spells. Well, this strategy worked on avocet aviaks for me and hope it helps others.

One other Idea, I would like to see our casting times reduced. Of all the casters I think we may be the slowest.
