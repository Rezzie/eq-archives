I couldnt give you directions of how to get to the Croc, but I have been led to the spot before, it is a cave near lower Guk Entrance that can only be got to by swiming in what seems to be a large underground lake. The croc is on a weird spawn cycle, and may be protected by anticamping code. I have camped him for 6 hours and never had a spawn (luckily he was there when I arrived), and have randomly visited the cave during trips to Upper Guk and have found him there on a few occasions. Saltwater crocs (lvl29 to 31) spawn in his place if he isnt there.
