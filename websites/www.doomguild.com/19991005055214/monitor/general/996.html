4 . "Pet has nothing to do with it"
 Posted by Mantik Boneweaver on Jul-16-99 at 12:43 PM (EST)
Pet has no bearing on whether the bug strikes or not. It only happens if the DoT gets the last 'hit' on the mob (ie, it dies, instead of 'xx was slain by yy'). However, I have seen many times when the DoT killed the mob and I got xp and the loot.

Like I said, it's hard to nail down the cause.
