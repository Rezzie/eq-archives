3 . "The Tusk Required"

From what I remember, having completed this quest, is that the tusk you need is not from elephants at all, but it is from a mammoth in Perma or Everfrost. Go by the exact name the quest giver gives, this doesn't match anything dropped by an elephant, but matched exactly what was dropped from some size of mammoth. Not an exact answer, but it is the right direction. Hope it helps.
