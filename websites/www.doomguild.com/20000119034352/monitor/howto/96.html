4 . "tips"
 Posted by Pron O'Graffy on Aug-23-99 at 11:26 AM (EST)
I just finished this quest (finally) on Bertox. Unless you're prepared to kill Lynuga, or have the money to either hire someone to kill her or buy the ruby, I'd suggest waiting on this quest. Here's how I did it tho, from start to finish.

Go to Paw, go to the room with the stream running thru it. There's a hole in the floor underneath the stream. Swim down there, and go thru the door that leads into the larger chamber. In a small room to your right, you'll see aq bunch of splitpaw nobles and possibly Kurrpok Splitpaw. Kill him, get ruby pendant, gate out.

Start a ranger character. Get him/her up to lvl4 as fast as you can. Run to WKarana. From all reports, Yiz spawns in the Northeast corner of the zone, and that's where I found him, hanging near the road into NKarana. Before you go looking for him, get your "real" character in that area and log out. Log back in as your ranger, and run around for the next 3-4 hours checking to see if he shows up on your tracking list. When he does, talk to him, and log out. The Splitpaw Assassin that spawns then won't attack you while you camp out. At least he didn't attack me. Log back in with your "real" character and kill the Assassin. Loot the hollowed skull.

Now you have some options. Lynuga, a lvl32 or so caster that hangs out in Innothule Swamp, has the final ruby. If you have high level (36+) friends, ask them to kill her. I didn't have any friends that high. I resorted to running into Guk after I found her and offering 150p to anyone hwo could kill her. This didn't generate much interest tho. In the end, I went to one of the weekly bazaars on our server and auctioned that I was interested in lynuga's ruby, and a very nice bard sold me hers for 150p.

Best of luck!
