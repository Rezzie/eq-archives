3 . "The secret to Rivervale"
 Posted by Blakwidow Mournbringer on Aug-03-99 at 03:14 PM (EST)
Ok necro's listen up =)

Here are a few secrets to Rivervale.


Those stupid halfling clerics


The clerics guild will always be KOS to you, no matter what, you will never find a way to raise the faction with them, as it stands. So you MUST ALWAYS invis past them on the kithicor entrance side.


Starting factions for Necro's


At first a gnome necro is threatening to guards, and I beleive the merchants are dubious to start, but I might be wrong. The guards are definently threatening. Here is what you do to raise faction quickly.


Kill Mooto!


Mooto is a 20 to 40 point faction hit kill him ALOT!


Give that man a bandage!


There is a little halfling in the corner of Misty thicket near the Rivervale entrance, (go left from entrance to RIvervale from Misty, run to zone edge, hut is near corner). Look for a little halfling named Joogl Honneybugger. He has an owie and wants a bandage. This is where you raise ALL your factions (except the damned clerics). Get someone to get you 100 bandages, give all 100 to him one at a time, do not stack them, after you give 100 to him your faction will be good enough that you can buy and sell from the merchants.


Good luck...
