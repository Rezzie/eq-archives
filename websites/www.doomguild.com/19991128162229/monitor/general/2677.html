This is found in Najena, His name is Tajena or something similar to that. To find him go down to Bonecracker, instead of going in that door, go into to door to the right of it, down that hallway and to your left. You will need to fight, an elemental, magician and Tajena. A level 30 cleric, and 30 necro are able to take them out. Best to nuke to mage first is she is agro to either of you.

6 . "my experience"
 Posted by Zatren Nertaz on Oct-18-99 at 10:51 PM (EST)
ok, went to najena last night to get the circlet solo, I'm level30

found the room, it has a pool with 2 earth ele's, an officer at the top and 2 mages. Trazdon spawned at the 2nd mage spot.

To solo the place I summoned behind the fake wall and pulled to my pet. The earth ele's didn't attack me, or the wandering guards. You can pull the magicians to free up trazdon's spawn spot and the officer at the top won't come.

btw this guy was harder than I expected. He killed me once because my pet was busy w/ his pet and he just sop'ed the shit out of me and I had the level20 drain memm'ed :(.
