"Solusek's Temple: Necro quests"
 Posted by Soliel Delahaine on Sep-30-99 at 08:31 PM (EST)
I went over to the test server and looked around what will be the new zone opening up soon. I only found 3 items that appear to be Necromancer specific.

If you come to what looks like the main chamber (there are lava flows going of left and right with a door in the center) go through that door, turn left, and again, to the library, down the stairs and underneath the stairwell is the NPC to talk to.

Shadowbound Boots:
Shadow Silk
Sceptre of Tears (from Mistimoore)
Eye of Shadowman (now they have a purpose)
Skeletan toe (from giant skeletons from Rathe)

Shadowbound Gloves:
Shadow Silk
Sceptre of Tears (from MM)
Hand of Shadownman
Skeletan fingers (rathe)

Shadowbound Robe:
Shadow Silk
Werebat wings (from Unrest)
Mask of Shadowman
Globe of Shadow (from a Nokta Shaman in Guk... Another NPC needed something from a Tonta Shaman in Guk so I bet they are refering to a Nok Shaman and a Ton Shaman).

The secret of making shadow silk costs 50 gold... which this newbie didn't have and no one was willing to cough up a little cash.

So with any luck.. these robes will look different for all classes (there was a magician robe too).
