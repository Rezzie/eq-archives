"Dueling"
 Posted by Pharos on Jun-14-99 at 02:14 PM (EST)
Hello all. I decided, once encouraged somewhat by my lvl 30 necromancer friend, that I should try my hand at a duel or too. I am lvl 21 now. So, I dueled a couple people in my guild of same lvl (we were 20 at the time).

My strategy against tank types and half-casters: Engulfing Darkness (run off a bit), Heart Flutter, Heat Blood, Disease cloud (I think thats the one). Wait until the spells are done and try not to be hit. Drain till dead

My strategy against pure casters:
Drain Drain Drain etc, try to interupt with weapon


Here are some concerns I have about dueling and I'm looking for feedback:

-magic resistance: If the person you are fighting has boosted magic resist you will be limited to life drain type spells. This stinks, you might not have enough mana to kill them this way.
Ex: Enchanter I was with was able to boost to 64 or so magic resist. Good news I can kill him with life draining alone, except:
-charm: Damn, this sucks, the enchanter didn't realize, but we tested after, he was able to charm me. This is really bad, how can I avoid it? I only have about +3 to my base gnome magic resist. If he cast on me during a duel, I'm not sure what would happen.
-Lay hands: does any mage have enough mana to kill a paladin twice? me thinks not. What to do about it?
-Wizards: doing 150 dmg per spell at my level I think, wizard could kill me in 2 spells and 1 poke of his dagger. You only drain for 65. I start with 304 HP lets say, after two rounds, no fizzles, I have 134 HP, wizard has lost 130 HP, if he hits me again its game over right?

Basically I'm worried that some classes face certain, very quick death against other classes. I can have a very close fight with a paladin I now know (without lay hands), I can do pretty well smoke an enchanter. I think I could whup any no magic class. Yet wizards it would seem can cream most classes.

I think the only way to beat a wizard would be to have someone buff you with magic resistance before the battle, but thats fairly lame as well.

Pharos

1 . "yep"

wizards cream us. So do druids, shaman and clerics.

Enchanters are also really good.

We do beat every single melee and melee hybrid however, with the possible exceptions of bards (I havent fought too many in solo fights)..

The reasons are multiple

1) Poor pet resist
2) Cancel magic.. cheap way to get rid of DOTs
3) better DD than us
4) Healing that we DONT have, unless one uses lifetaps, which is a surefire way of death..

The way to go actually is to charm a pet, and use THAT as your main weapon. Charmed pets have funnily enough, far more resist than our regular ones, and make for a more interesting fight. Pray that it doesnt turn on you midfight.

-LG
