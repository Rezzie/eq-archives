I watched a rl friend of mine help two of his friends get the robe. He is a lvl 29 druid and the others were a lvl 30 wiz and a 33 necro. 4 . "Oracle Robe..."
 Posted by Balthazar on Aug-04-99 at 11:01 AM (EST)
The oracle is a mid 30s wizard that has a level 40 paladin guardian. The minimum contingent to kill the oracle is 3 level 30ish casters and a druid. The druid kites the guardian while the other 3 casters take care of the the Oracle. One of the casters on the oracle team is handy if they are a cleric. You'll want to have gate memorized. Once you drop and loot the oracle, everyone just gates out. You don't have to kill the paladin. Although it is great fun to kill them both when you have the right group do it.
