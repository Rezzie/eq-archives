I am not sure where you got that message but there is a certain limit where the trainers will tell you to go learn advanced pottery but it is well below 40. But at the same time I made a shit load of money. Now you will be spending a shit load of money. So unless you need pottery for something I would suggest giving it up and moving on to something else.
