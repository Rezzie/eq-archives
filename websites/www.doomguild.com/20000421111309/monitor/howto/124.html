2 . "Tailor Raw Silk Armor"
 Posted by Necro Tailor on Jan-27-00 at 12:33 PM (EST)
Take up tailoring and make your own armor. It's the same AC as Woven as far as I know. I'm level 6 and about half Raw Silk. Here's what I did:

1) Saved about 3 backpacks of ruined wolf pelts for practice later on.

2) Put one training point into tailoring at 4th (not sure if necessary) and bought a small sewing kit.

3) Killed large spiders in Nektoulos (I'm DE) which drop 2 spider silks about 1/3 time. Not spiderling silks. When you have 2 silks, put them into the sewing kit and hit combine...you get a swatch of silk from this. These swatches stack BTW so it's a good thing. I've never had a failure combineing them either I think. You will pick up some skill increases doing this.

4) When I hit 6th I added training at my guild to bring my skill up to 20. 16 points I think?

5) Use the previously saved pelts to make patch for sale to players or back to vendor. my skill was 25 or 26 at this point.

6) Start making raw silk armor. More failure than patch, but hey, it's "free".

I'm at 29 skill after about 12 tries at silk with 50-60% success. When I hit 8th I'm going to do Tarantulas in NoRo for fun and profit.

Good Luck
