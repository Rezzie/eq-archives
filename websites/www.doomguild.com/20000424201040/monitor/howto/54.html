1 . "It's not too hard."
 Posted by Hondo on Jun-22-99 at 04:14 PM (EST)

Cast invis undead on yourslef and keep this up whenver it fades.

Then go to the well area where you fell. Do shouts asking people if the bottom of the well is clear. If you get a YES, then drop down the well, loot your body, and gate out.

If no one will respond to your shouts, then make sure you are invis and drop down the well, and just wait for people to clear the room. There are always groups down at that level clearing out the rooms trying to get the goss robes and other high ticket items.

The first time I fell through the floor in Befallen it scared the crap out of me, I thought I'd never get my body back.

3 . "Getting your corpse ..."
 Posted by Darzoj on Jun-25-99 at 10:27 AM (EST)
First I would sugget studying one of the many maps posted on Paw's Everquest Map page or such on Befallen. The Hardest part is once you are invis to undead, you jump down the well yourself. Then use the /corpse command to drag it to the Locked door to take you to level 2. The thing is you are going to have to make sure someone is around to open that door or wait before you jump down there until you know someone has opened it. I made the mistake once of jumping down there and although no one attacked me (invis to undead) I could not leave until someone opened that locked door. Once you get past the locked door ... I would still drag my corpse until I was in a safe place.

Good Luck.
