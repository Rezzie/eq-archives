1 . "Save at least 4 plat"
 Posted by Allicia on Jul-15-99 at 12:49 PM (EST)
I'd make sure you have around 4 and a half plat by the time you hit 8. You will absolutely need that level 8 pet.

That said, no other spell at level 8 is really _necessary_.

You'll be using your level 4 attack spells until level 12, so your combat effectiveness won't be effected much if you don't buy anything other than the pet.

Level 6 and 7 are a pain. Maybe get some cloth to help a little in the fights, but be very sure you'll have the plat for that level 8 pet.

Around 4-6 I spent my cash on some armor and only bought my pet at 8. Did fine. I'm a kill or two from 11 and I have maybe half my level 8 spells now.

Figure on seeing more money rolling in around 12 when you and your new pet can handle firedrakes in lavastorm. They drop earrings worth 1 gp and gems worth 4 now and again. Also the rock dervs drop ignots worth 4 gp and silver rings worth a gp here and there too.

Around 12 you should be able to pull down most dervs too, and get bronze weapons that sell for around 3 plat.

But until then get used to being broke and not having all your spells.

Level 8, I'd recommend your pet, then get mend bones, ward(it's cheap), lesser shielding, and eventually pick up gather shadows, dark pact, dark empathy and maybe dead eye when you can afford it.

Dump training points into evocation, because you've never used it before and ward will depend on it. It's hard to train evoke by using ward when your ward spell never works in a fight.

3 . "Evokation and stuff."
 Posted by Serrin on Jul-15-99 at 07:16 PM (EST)
If you want to raise evokation the REALLY easy way without spending skill points, get yourself a bone chip (You do have some, right?) summon a pet (you should get this far on your own anyway) and *ward your pet*. He will take absolutely 0 damage, your skill WILL raise, and all you spend is some mana and a pair of bone chips (if you were going to USE that pet for something, say, killing bears, then you have 100% efficiency). For those of you who think this is unfair/cheap/exploiting, I have asked the GMs numerous times if this was a bug or if this was intentional and, yep you guessed it, no reply whatsoever. I would think it's as fair as summoning magician stuff lots of times to raise conjuring (they do it too, at least the ones I know). Sidenote: You pet can be the recipent of ANY targeted spell, in case you don't want to raise your alteration by heat blooding yourself. (It SHOULD raise quick enough on it's own, but hey, you never know)

Serrin

6 . "Innothule"

A good place to go and LOOT armor you can wear is Innothule Swamp. The kobolds and frogloks drop netted and occassionally woven armor. What you don't use sells for good money, too. So see if you can get a group up, or go there yourself... that's what I did and I have seen many DEs there at low levels (I was 10th when I first went there). It's also not a bad place to hunt, there and in S. Ro, or you can hop on over to the Feerott.

Good luck!
