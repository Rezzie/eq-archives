1 . "Travel(sewer spoiler like you dont know already)"
 Posted by Moswsda - Shaman Troll on Jul-07-99 at 09:40 AM (EST)
This assumes that everyone hates you, just con all cause you may not need to do all of this...
It's quite easy to do, first off dont go to dock thru gate, go to the south side of the dock, on other side of wall(past skel tower, bandit camp, guard tower). Then swim out and around the wall, con the dwarf on the first pier, if glares, or ready to attack, swim out to sea a bit. come back near middle dock and sit near it. When you see or hear boat, swim straight out and keep swiming near front of boat. when boat moves it will pick you up(if it doesnt swim under boat and up thru floor). now go below deck and hang out till you zone to Freeport (about 20 mins). Come up on deck, now you can jump off left side before you come to dock. Assuming that the barbarian under the building on the left is not KOS to you(glare or ready to attack, and cons blue or above) go to where he is and go thru the door behind him(if he is then u need to swim under water to left of building and get to sewers under water).

Once thru the door go down the stairs to under the
landing, back there you'll hear funny music, and you walk thru secret wall. go down those stairs, open door and go to the left. Make right at NPCs(no need to open door) then just keep making lefts, you'll end up at stairs going up. go up and thru wall, you are now outside South gate.

I dont know the way off top of head if you need to go to sewers under water. So good luck!

3 . "another entrance from the docks."
 Posted by Shokakon on Jul-07-99 at 11:57 AM (EST)
Faceing the docks on the left side you will see a lower level of dock..stairs going down to it. Go through the door, you'll be in a room...go down the stairs from the top platform, go under the platform and there is a walkthrough wall there. Easy entrance if you don't want to swim all the way around.

Unless you have somehow screwed up your faction you should be welcome in Freeport. In fact, the guards there are apprehensive to me while my own guards in Ak'Anon glower at me! You can even bank in Freeport - just be sure to Gather Shadows while in the North (Paladins) and walk behind the counter to do your banking. Then either Gather Shadows or Gate out of there. There is also a hidden Necro guild underneath Freeport. Just don't go wandering into too many buildings because it seems there are some people who REALLY don't like us (read: KOS).
