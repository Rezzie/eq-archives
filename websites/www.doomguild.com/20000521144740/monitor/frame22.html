New Spell: Summon Corpse

Recent rumors have confirmed that indeed there is a new spell slated to enter the Necromancer arsenal: Summon Corpse. Although it will have heavy drawbacks ( including high mana cost, relatively expensive and horrendously awkward reagents ), this spell will bring praise upon Necromancers for CR around the land. No longer will players have to wait when corpses are stuck in walls, no longer will you have to find a 'lost' corpse, and CR in High Level areas will become substantially easier. More news to come on this highly anticipated spell!

Further Update on Feign Death

With the most recent patch, some changes went into effect that further altered the effects of Feign Death. In general, any mob under Level 35 is supposed to 'forget' about you when you succesfully Feigned. On mobs over Level 35, the immediate effects are somewhat different. If the mob returns to its spawn/home point, it is supposed to forget you 90% of the time. The effects on mobs without a home point, namely roamers, are fairly less pronounced. Contrary to Verant's acknowledgements, mobs without a home point seem to always remember you when you cancel Feign Death. Caution is urged when using this spell!

~Sluggo

Spell Analysis

Screaming Terror

Screaming Terror, in its old form, was the first and only 'fear and forget' spell in the game. When originally released, it brought great praises among Necromacers. However, Verant began a series of 'nerfs', which eventually led to the spell not functioning at all. Once again, Verant has altered Screaming Terror, completely ridding it of the Fear aspect of the spell. It will currently mesmerise the target, the only general purpose spell available in the game that offers this outside of the Enchanter class.

At a first glance, the spell has awesome potential. Crowd control has now become one of the Necromancers forte's. The spell does have its drawbacks in regards to the Enchanter mes; as it takes 60 Mana, 2.6 Seconds to cast, and has a recast time of roughly 2 seconds. Nonetheless, this spell will see action in all forms in the battles to come!

~Sluggo
