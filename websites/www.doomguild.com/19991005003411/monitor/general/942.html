8 . "The Litap line has been fixed"
 Posted by CreepingSword on Jul-14-99 at 05:16 PM (EST)
Before you could lifetap anything regardless of level difference, this was regarded as too unbalancing and it was fixed. Now the lifetap line falls in line with the 6 lvl rule, where all spells are resisted by a MOB 6 or more lvls higher than you.

This is fair IMO.

In any group the casters have to be near the high end of the group lvl wise to be effective.
