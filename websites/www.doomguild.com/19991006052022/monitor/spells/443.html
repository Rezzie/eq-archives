4 . "Clinging Darkness not that Bad"
 Posted by Clinging Darkness not that Bad on Jul-26-99 at 09:54 AM (EST)
Although not a great damage spell, you are correct, it is still an important spell, and one still used by high level necros for it's lower resist percentage.

Why? The slow effect. You are incorrect that it is 12 seconds or some such. It has a duration that can extend up to at least one minute. The trick to this spell is that the creature gets a resist chance every tick. So, it can fail after 1 or 2 ticks, or last the full minute. It's resist rate is painfully high, but still very useful for many reason. So even clinging darkness can slow a creature for a full minute, giving you time to run, DoT, blast, and attack with pets and melee.
