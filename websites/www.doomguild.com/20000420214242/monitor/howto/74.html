6 . "Research"

You can only train one point in it at 16 and the next time you can train is at 19. you can only put 5 pts in each level. I had 50 points saved for research only to find they are useless. also if you get the skill up through research without training the you will only be able to train 4 for that level (the trainer only lets you train to a max skill number every level)

As for when lv 16 spells become trivial its at lv 20. I found that i have about a 50% failure rate when it is trivial.
