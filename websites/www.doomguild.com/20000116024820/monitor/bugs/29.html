"Fear Bug?"
 Posted by Menil on Aug-16-99 at 10:33 AM (EST)
Has anybody ever experienced this: sometimes when I am fighting a mob and I have used Fear, I will be chasing the Mob, who is being hit by my pet. All of a sudden, the pet will stop attacking and go back to his guard point, although I can still have the mob targeted and still see him running away (this has happened many times in Lava Storm with fire drakes). Then, a few seconds later, the mob disappears. He didn't die -- they usually have about half hit points left, and I got no experience/loot or even a message that it died. Then, sometimes, the mob will return while I am medding and attack! It's definitely the same mob -- drakes are not aggro, and it is never at full hit points.

This also happened when I was grouped fighting a fire imp. The other players had the imp targeted, but it just never came back.

1 . "Mob "teleport" bug"
 Posted by Obumaru on Aug-17-99 at 03:47 AM (EST)
Yah, there's a bug on this in some zones.

The mob still looks visible for a bit, but is actually far, far away. Sometimes they do a little spinning dance just to torment you! :P Happened quite a bit in Qeynos Hills to me. If you wait long enough they come back and Jebtik will get aggro on them as they come back in range. This is the real clue here: Jebtik loses the mob, not just you, then he picks it up again.

Do a /bug with the /loc if you can, seems to be pretty location specific.

However, sometimes it's just lag, like when you see a mob jump a bit, but can still catch up to them or cast on it. If your are right on it, then you are suddenly out of spell casting range, then it's not lag, but this bug. (That is, if you're not getting horrible packet loss, etc.) With lag cases, your pet should still stick on it better than you.
