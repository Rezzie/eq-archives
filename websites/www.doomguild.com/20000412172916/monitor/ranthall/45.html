Let's correct this, shall we?


> A lot is not spell

Should be "spelled."


> A lot is not spelled alot

Should use double quotes since words are taken as objects rather than as words. "A lot" is not spelled "alot".


> "A lot" is not spelled "alot"....

A period-based ellipsis is three periods, not four. If it is at the end of a sentence, one would not add a fourth period as the sentence's period.


> "A lot" is not spelled "alot."

One wouldn't use an ellipsis here, anyway. Its one possible use would be as a pause, which is strained because the two sentences' constuction doesn't seem to warrant it. It's debatable, though.


> "A lot" is not spelled "alot." Just thought i

So did I...

Should be "I'd like to" or "I would like to."
