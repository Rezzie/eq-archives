"Difficulties Don't Work"
 Posted by Etadanik on Jun-17-99 at 04:12 PM (EST)
Okay many of you are blaming our problems on the fact that we chose a difficult class well listen up: Difficulty systems DONT ******* work in online games! How many times this happened I don't know, but difficulty systems are only useful in single-person games if you want a challenge. In an online game especially a rpg game difficulty systems simply don't cut it. You want to be able to compete with others on a similar level not at a HIGHER level.

That's why no online game so far including UO has placed a difficulty counter, because they simply don't work. Got that? If not, this is something for you to think about.
