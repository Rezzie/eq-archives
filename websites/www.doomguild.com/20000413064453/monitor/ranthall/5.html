"What ticks me off about Varant & EQ"
 Posted by Myskyra on May-08-99 at 06:44 PM (EST)

First, I understand that some things should have a limit to it, or players shouldn't be able to do certain things least they exploit the situation.

But Varant's paranoid. I've noticed from day one that anything which you could actually use strategy and thought for, or especially use your spell-caster to her/his full potential, they lable it an exploit and stop you from doing it! To the the point of being absolutly absurd.

A spell-caster's _main_ means of survival is using their spells to the fullest and avoiding a hand-to-hand as much as possible. We all know this, and the caster's ability to do so is a major part of the beauty of the class. Take, for instance, casting from what should be an un-reacable location. Hmm.. let's use Black Burrow for example. Lots of us should know this area, but for those who don't...

The entrance to Black Burrow is a cave in what appears to be the shape of a large dog's jaw. There's four "teeth", which are the two in front. The "snout" is connected to a very large block of stone. All in all, I'd guess it goes up about 20 to 30 feet, in game distance.

A player is able to "climb" the hill and stand on top of this block, indeed, climb up even further. Now that's getting up there. Being a spell-caster class, Necromancer of course, I seen this as a prestine opportunity.

However, Varant apparently put the blocks to this, as I experianced in great shock! After nailing the creature with the spell, it didn't climb up after me -- it went _inside_ of the tunnel -- and started pummling me from below! Through tens of feet of stone between us! Could I hit back with my weapon? No! Of course not, which means that the MOBs are deliberatly given this ability.

There are also other redicules limitations, such as not being able to attack something that's in water when you're on land. Your spells have absolutly no effect on them, and your pet will you that it is an illegal target.

It seems varant would be oh-so much happier if they could get rid of all of the spell-casting classes (especially Necro) and only have fighters. After all, the _only_ thing they apparently don't see as an exploit, is charging head-on at something and beginning to swing. This is ludicris and suicide for a spell-caster. Sure, we get pets, but we all know they only go so far and we're also painfully limited in what we can do with them, as well.

This is redicules. Why even have a spell-casting class if all you're going to be able to do is stand there, swing, and hope to get the spell off between being treated as a rag doll. I swear, it's like varant is affraid to let their players innovate and think, and are _deliberately_ squashing one's ability to use strat and one's imagination & thought and forcing us all to basically be figher classes. It's absurd, what are they affraid of? Indeed, _paranoid_ of??

Talk about control freaks, give the players at least _SOME_ freedom. You put us (Necros especially) through enough MUD (heh) as it is without such extreme and absurd limitations.

Okay, well folks this is the rant place and I really needed to get that off of my chest. : ) Later,

~Myskyra
