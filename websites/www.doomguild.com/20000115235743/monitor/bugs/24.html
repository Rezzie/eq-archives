4 . "I have long since accepted it, or Pets 101 reprised"
 Posted by Malbusia on Aug-12-99 at 12:38 PM (EST)
I regularly leave for adventure trips with 40-60 bone chips on me, and just recast until I get a good pet. If it's during the day, I'll recast until I get a blue pet that hits at the level just below the top pet, if at night, keep going until I get a top pet, then hand it a dagger.

I just learned to accept this as one of the things we necromancers have to deal with -- I actually take quite a bit of pride in crafting a powerful pet who can almost do as much damage as a warrior of the same level as me. Remember, though, that that is only with a top pet and high speed dagger. It takes some work to get that going, though.

Yes, of course green pets are trash. Most of the problems people describe about pets sucking are because they are generating green or low-blue pets. OF COURSE they suck!

The general rule is:

When you get a new pet spell, re-cast at night in the dark until you find the one that hits the most. Then, forever more, if it's daytime and you cast, just recast until you get a pet that hits for 2 less than that (maybe you'll get lucky and get the top one during the day.) Then use that one until nighttime and recast until you get your top one. Then settle in for some decent adventure, and keep /pet back off hotkeyed. (My regular adventuring bank of 6 hot keys is Persona, Sit, Spells, /pet kill, /pet back off, /pet follow me.)

For dungeons, learn to lead your pet around, swing wide around corners and wait for it to catch up, and when standing, don't stand such that the pet is trying to walk into the walls -- that has a tendency to make the pet suicide. Yes, it's a bug, but you can work around it if careful.

From what I have heard of the higher-level pets, they are just fine (correct me if I'm wrong.) The only difficulty is in getting the words to make the spells (several copies of the words if necessary.) Yes, it's idiotic that level 45 necros are running around with a level 34 pet (which may be green to a level 34 caster already, keep in mind.) Of course that pet is worthless.

It seems to me the gripes are with the inability to get the current pet spells, or with them, to cast a top-end pet. That extra work, which can take upwards of 15 minutes, I take great in-game pride in.
