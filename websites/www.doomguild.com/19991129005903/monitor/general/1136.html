"Interesting experience in CT..."
 Posted by Cala on Jul-25-99 at 10:50 AM (EST)
Had a very interesting experience yesterday in the temple of Cazic-Thule on Solusek Ro. The zone is broken (is it broken on all servers?) on solusek ro so there aren't alot of lizardmen or alligators and only about 5 people in the zone when I got there. I was in a group of 5, all 24 or 25 and one level 49er. The level 49er disbanded and was going to pull for us. Well after about an hour (wasn't great exp, I always get alot more soloing than grouping but was just helping out), he wanted to pull the lizard champion to us. He said it was level 35 (I don't know) so we were sorta worried but heck he was there and could handle it. The group break down...me a level 25 necromancer, one level 25 druid, a level 24 shaman and a level 24 wizard. So he pulls the champion and you know what? It resisted everything we cast at it EXCEPT! Siphon life. Siphon life hit for full damage every single time it was cast. The wizard couldn't hit it, the shaman couldn't hit it, the druid couldn't hit it. The other three ended up meleing it while I cast about 16 siphon lives on it. It was very interesting to say the least. I knew all 4 of the other guys very well and the druid used to always say how much better his class was...well, I doubt I will ever hear that again.
