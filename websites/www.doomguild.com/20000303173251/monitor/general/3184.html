9 . "Clarification"
 Posted by Kefka Zinn on Nov-24-99 at 08:44 PM (EST)
There are three distinct guard camps in EK:

1) Human camp near the gorge leading to Beholder. This is a village of sorts, a cluster of houses around which 2 or 3 guards are posted at separate spots. Each can be pulled alone without interference from the others.

2) Human camp, up the road from the bridge and a little to the North. This camp is another human house cluster, again with 2 or 3 guards that can be pulled by themselves.

3) Barbarian fishing village, at the end of the coastline. You can reach this spot by turning right while on the bridge and facing into the zone. Run all the way down the beach until you encounter this series of barbarian huts. There are 3 guards here which can be pulled alone, but doing so requires a bit of careful timing (one of them roams the perimeter and must be cleared first).

Each of the guard spots is about as good for exp as the next, and the guards in each spot are the same in terms of loot and experience. They are level 35 or thereabouts, and they usually drop up to 4 platinum, with the occasional research word or piece of bronze armor/weaponry.

Hope this helps. :)
