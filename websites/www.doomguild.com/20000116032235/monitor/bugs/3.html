9 . "FD and pulling"
 Posted by Clawdia on Sep-06-99 at 10:32 AM (EST)
I have seen monks use Feign Death to great effect when pulling and wondered if the same tactic would work for us.

It goes like this:
You want to pull from a crowded camp or room, but cant handle all the mobs that will come, so you cast a DD spell on one of the Mobs, then just before the pack reaches you, Feign Death. The mobs all stop in there tracks, because they think you are dead. One by one they return to their spawn spots.When only one or two are left hanging around your corpse, you stand up and kill them at your leisure. Repeat until room/camp is empty and the respawns will be nicely spaced out from then on.

Anyone tried this with the necro version of FD?
maybe a nice soloing trick in places like unrest or CT
