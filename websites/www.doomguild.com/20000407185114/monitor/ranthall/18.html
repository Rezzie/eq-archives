"This whole necro thing isn't working"
 Posted by Dravynth on May-28-99 at 10:10 AM (EST)
The whole point of being a necromancer....you are hated by society. They fear the dark arts you practice, though they envy the power. That is why we are killed on sight throughout Norrath. That is why we are limited to where we can adventure, as it is difficult to find vendors and banks willing to deal with us.

The only problem....our powers are becoming less and less enviable. Our darkness line is almost laughable, since it generally takes 2 or 3 casts to make them stick. Our high-mana-cost-per-damage lifeleech line of spells can now be shrugged off easily by creatures more powerful than us. Our pets are our strength...as long as they don't run off or vanish. And as long as it doesn't take 5 attempts to get a reasonable one. Assuming of course you can even get the pet spell for your level.

Our powers of death and destruction are waning. The powers that be (Verant) sees that we can do things that no other classes can do (6 level 16 necros taking down a sand giant, or necros of any level soloing reds) so they feel the need to weaken our powers to bring us in line with everyone else. If we are brought in line with the other more mundane classes, what is the point of being hated and feared throughout all of Norrath?

NPCs attack or won't do business with necros because they fear us. In light of recent "adjustments" to the game, what do they have to fear? I'd think at this point they would just laugh at us. Merchants would feel sorry for us and give us a discount.

We all decided to study necromancy because we wanted the power. We understood that the price of this power was that most of Norrath would hate us, and kill us on sight. But in exchange for this, we would have abilities that no other class could match. Now, we are little better than any other class, but the stigma still remains. We might as well be wizards or magicians, at least then we wouldn't be attacked everywhere.

They punish us for doing better than other classes. We are supposed to be better. Necromancy, the power over life and death. If we can find and use any remaining abilities that we might have to make us seem more powerful than others, they will take those from us, too.

Pretty soon there will be little reason, if any, to study our dark arts.
