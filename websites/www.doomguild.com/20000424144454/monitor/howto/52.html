4 . "Easier way..."
 Posted by Psoio on Jun-17-99 at 10:16 PM (EST)
I know there is a secret hole in the wall outside the South and West Gates to FP that lead to the tunnels. The tunnels lead to the dark guilds. The only one I can give you a positive location on is the one from N Ro. As you're running toward the gate from the Ro zone, bank left and head for the corner. TAA-DAA, behind the tent is a false wall. There is a choice to go left or right a little ways in, go right or you'll zone and waste time. No need to drudge thru the tedious path thru the regular zone. There is a false wall outside the West Gate, but can't remember its location.

BTW, My gnome necro is only bothered by one NPC that I've found. It's a monk in the Inn in East FP. At level 7, though, the only reason I zoned was because he was bugging me. He's weak. Just don't venture to the little bar at the docks. I heard there is some bad Paladins there who don't appreciate the finer points of our Dark Magic.

Walk in Death,
Psoio
