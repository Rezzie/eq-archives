3 . "Qeynos Docks"
 Posted by Zharahk on Jun-14-99 at 01:27 PM (EST)
The best way I've found to get into Qeynos was to take the boat from Erudin so that you arrive in Qeynos after midnight, but before dawn. You'll zone out of Erudin and into Erudin's crossing, and then you'll zone into South Qeynos. As soon as you zone into South Qeynos, jump off the boat and swim over to the little dock to the right of the main dock. Under the dock (underwater) there is an entrance to the catacombs. Swim down there and, since the entrance is broke, you'll end up on the dock. At this time of the morning there aren't too many guards around, so if you're shadows dropped during the crossing then now would be a good time to cast them. From here run for the underground entrance that is southeast of the bank that will lead down to the guild. I would highly recommend anyone planning this crossing to create a character in Qeynos to explore the docks, tunnels, and town before bringing their Necromancer over. I hope this helps.

Zharahk

 
4 . "Entering Qeynos"
 Posted by Obumaru on Jun-14-99 at 07:50 PM (EST)
I wouldn't mess around with the broken sewer entrance.

Get off the boat, Gather Shadows, run to the Arena. There, follow the bones on the ground downstairs. There are two more guards, make sure Shadows is still on, run past them following the bones to zone into the sewers.

Follow the hallway to the Shrine of Bertoxxulous! Defile any goody corpses you find lying outside. ;)

Exit the sewers at the secret door near the Qeynos gates and get a bind there. Make a Gnoll mass grave and consider the guards from long range (with Gather Shadow OFF). Repeat until Guards are fooled as to your true intentions!
