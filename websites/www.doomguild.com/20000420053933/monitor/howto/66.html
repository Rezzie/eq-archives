9 . "Sacraficial Dagger..."
 Posted by Balthazar on Aug-04-99 at 11:26 AM (EST)
The dagger loads on the imp that spawns in the graveyard in mistmoore. I've seen the daggers sell in auction in west commons for up to 300 plat. The dagger can be either used for combat or placed in the ranged weapon slot as an added bonus to your stats.

The best combat weapon I've seen so far is a bloodclaw stilletto that does a 20 point direct damage and DOT effect. You can put the sacraficial dagger in the ranged slot and equip the stilletto for 1h piercing.

I've not been able to acquire a staff of writhing yet.
