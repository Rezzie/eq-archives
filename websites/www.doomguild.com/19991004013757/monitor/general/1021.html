Your chance of having vamp embrace go off has nothing to do with your level or the monster's level. it has *everything* to do with, as far as i can tell, Dexterity, just like any other spell effect that items conjure forth (bloodclaw from stiletto, chaotic feedback from writhing staff, etc.). So for all of you people whoa re saying it goes off once, mebbe 2 times a battle, mebbe none at all: get dexterity items. They aren't just useful for getting spell effects to go off on weapons, they *also* play an extremely important role in lowering the amount of interruptions when casting spells.
