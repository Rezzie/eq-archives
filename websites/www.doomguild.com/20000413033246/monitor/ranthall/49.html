If you can ever safely log in, get bound inside the secret entrance in the wall outside of Qeynos. No guards go there and there's a water zone into the catacombs to go to the Necro guild.

Kill or be a party to killing hundreds of Gnolls in BlackBurrow. Your faction will raise enough to be safe from guards and to deal with merchants. Jealous mages near the Qeynos vault will still try to kill you, so Gather Shadows, hop behind the counter before transacting with the banker -- far away from door as possible.

Criminally insane Holly and Cros will probably always try to kill you, so beware them (they wander Qeynos Hills looking for animal abuse).

If you can't log in without guards killing you, create another character, log in and petition on behalf of your Necro. Ask GM to move it somewhere for you... wait forever to see if they do. ;)
