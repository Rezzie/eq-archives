1 . "Killing spectres in Feerrott"
 Posted by Azuron on Dec-16-99 at 03:51 PM (EST)
The solo tree spectre is very easy with or without the pet at lvl 34 (without a pet assumes you have jboots). Spectres have very low resistances to every type of magic.

This is what worked for me...

With a pet:

Open with darkness, then have pet engage. Engulfing if mana is an issue and dooming if mana is not. Tell the pet to attack. Then cast panic the dead on the spectre (the 10 mana one). Now stack venom of the snake/boil blood/vampiric curse. I forget how many dots I stacked, probably only vos and curse because the spectre is not going to live long. Reapply panic as necessary. I recast panic after each dot was applied. Give it a few expluse undeads (whatever the 273dmg one is) and then you have a dead spectre.

If you fear the spectre too early it will head towards the spectre cave... This is not a problem in my experience because even though it heads towards the cave, it only goes a short distance then heads back without aggroing any spectres.

Without a pet:

I only did this with jboots I do not know how easy/difficult this is without them.

I just rooted and doted the spectre to death. First I cast call of bones on myself, before rooting the spectre at its spawn. Then I would stack the dots and recast root after each application of dot. Firts curse, then boil, then venom. Venom last because the 40DD component tends to increase the chance of root breaking.

It root broke (as it often will), I would just run a short distance and reroot. This is not hard because spectres stop a lot to drain you. When running to reroot do not run towards the spectre cave this will aggro the cave and you will have 5 spectres on you. I never aggroed them... but people who tried to KS the spectre from me when I was there pulled to the cave and then everyone was zoning to Rathe Mts.

My run pattern was from the tree norht towards the undead ruins, to the west zone wall, south perpendicular to the spectre tree, east to the spectre tree, then repeat the pattern. When first running north to the undead ruins do not get too close or the skeletons and zombies witll aggro on you -- very annoying.

If the solo spectre is camped:

If is even possible to pull singles and sometimes doubles from the cave. I would not recommend trying this unless grouped or you have enough mana to root and nuke/dot two spectres. Remember the undead ruins north, take a skeleton from there clobber but don't kill it. Bring the skeleton near the entrance to the spectre cave. Inch slowly towards the entrance and never go in. As you get close you should pull only one spectre because the skeleton triggers it. You'll figure out the exact radius by trial and zoning :)

This last tactic was shown to me by a cleric that I met there once and we killed a few spectres together... cannot remember his name though.

Hope that helps.
