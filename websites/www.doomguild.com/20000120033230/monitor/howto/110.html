1 . "Where we are KOS"
 Posted by Fallow Ground (Gnome Necro) on Aug-24-99 at 06:05 PM (EST)
As a gnome necro you can go into Akkanon, Neriak, Freeport, and Qeynos to start. You do have to be carefull however since there are merchants and NPC's in these cities that will kill you. Your faction will also be dubious in most of these cities, so don't expect to get great prices.

DO NOT kill the orcs in Crushbone as this will lower your faction with the Indigo Brotherhood and deny you access to Neriak.

When in Neriak the only place you have to really be carefull at is the Necro Guild (imagine that.) The Ghouls guarding the front gate will kill you (at least they killed me).

I am also tolerated in Rivervale. I believe this was from killing so many Gnolls in Lake Rathe and Paw.

The Dwarf guards and Elf guards also con dubious or tolerate me. I am not sure what this was from, maybe a small quest I did in Freeport. I was able to easily repeat the quest (give someone something you could buy) and I remember that it increased my faction standing somewhat.

So hope this helps. I am at the point where I can walk around most cities without being killed. However it is NEVER safe.

Method #1: Gather Shadows. Walk in. Wait by the merchants until the boat comes. Gather Shadows again. Run onto boat.

There's a guard who swims under the docks. So, if you wait on the docks and your shadows fade, he'll jump out of the water and kill you.

Method #2: If you don't have Gather Shadows, you can swim into the docks. Stay near the walls to avoid the swimming guard. Again, hang out near the merchants. when the boat comes, book ass onto the boat. Don't stop for anything. And pray that the swimming guard doesn't pounce on you.
