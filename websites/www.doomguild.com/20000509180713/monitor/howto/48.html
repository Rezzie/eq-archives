2 . "Research -how to-"
 Posted by Kuur Styx on Jun-14-99 at 10:46 AM (EST)
You need several things:

1: Dialbre (sp?) 1 and or 2. This is your recipe book that lists which word combo's create spells.

2: Languages. You need to be excellent in a certain language to read the recipe book. I've read several suggestions as to what or which langs need to be known to read these books, but so far none of these were correct. I think I have zeroed in on the correct one now. =)

3: Last but not least, words.

Put the correct combo of words in the Book of Dark Bindings and hit the combine button. If you read the recipe correctly and your skill lvl is high enough for the spell you're researching, you should produce a spell. The only other tip I can give is don't research a spell thats requires you to be a higher lvl to use.

Kuur Styx
