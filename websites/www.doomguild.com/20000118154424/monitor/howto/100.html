1 . "lavastorm"
 Posted by Torym on Aug-15-99 at 01:30 AM (EST)
Lavastorm is a goldmine and if you fight smart you won't get killed.

#1 fight near the entrance to najena so you can have a quick zone if needed.

#2 don't fight elements, first your pet has to be lvl12 I think to hit magical, second they hit hard and have a damage shield

#3 fight drakes for xp in the lower levels, rock drvs are good for xp, but their loot will weigh you down, basilisks are good for their eggs that sell for 5g each

#4 have your pet guard a spot, run and look for a creature dot it with engulf dark or clink dark, pull to your pet, make pet attack, lifedrain a bit, I usually than fear the monster while in darkness so my pet gets a backstab bonus

Hope that helps, I made about 25pp selling the loot in two days of casual play.

Torym

3 . "hunting options"
 Posted by Caelum on Aug-18-99 at 02:17 PM (EST)
I was in this same predicament until the last few days when I made a breakthrough. To zones work well, Oasis and Lavastorm. Oasis has the advantage of more soloable yellows, Lavastorm has better loot.

In Oasis look for caimans, and occasional yellow croc to a 12th leveler.

In Lavastorm fight at the nektulos or najena entrance and fight fire drakes and rock dervishes. Leave the elementals and fire imps alone.

In either case you need a good supply of bone chips, so you can summon a descent pet. Dont accept a green pet, you'll die guaranteed. Give the pet a rusty dagger. Use impart strength on the pet. Pull the creature with siphone strength. Send in pet. Hit creature with engulfing darkness (in lavastorm) or heatblood (in oasis). Trade tanking with pet if needed. Rinse and repeat. :)


Good luck!
