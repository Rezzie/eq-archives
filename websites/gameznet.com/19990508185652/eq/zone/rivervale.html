Everquest Zone Guide: Rivervale

Rivervale is the home of the halflings. This zone exits to Kithicor Forest and Misty Thicket.

Rivervale is important for two reasons. First and foremost it is the only city on the main continent where you can buy small armor. This is important for visiting gnomes or dwarves who probably don't want to sail all the way home to buy armor.

The second reason is that rivervale is more or less in the center of the continent. If you have to be bound to one place(and are not an evil character) this is probably the place to be bound to.

Rivervale features many interesting quests. A couple of the more popular ones include the pirahna tooth and squad ring quests.
