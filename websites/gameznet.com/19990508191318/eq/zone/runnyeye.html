Everquest Zone Guide: Runnyeye

Runnyeye is the home of the Runnyeye goblins. This zone is connected to Misty Thicket and Beholder. This dungeon can support players level 5-30 very well. In general the farther down you go the more difficult the dungeon becomes(it is this way with most dungeons isn't it?). This dungeon not only contains goblins but also evil eyes.

The items most sought after in this zone are the different forms of blackened iron armor. This armor can be found off the elite guards, the warlord and the king. The king is a level 27ish goblin, the warlord is around level 24. Elite guards are all 20+.

The king and warlord also sport several other magical items including the black iron girdle, black iron crown, black iron medallion and grotesque mask.
