Everquest Equipment Guide: Studded Collar

Studded collars an be found on Gnoll Elite Guards at Black Burrow from time to time. They are a leather classed item which prohibits them from being used by pure casters. The studded collars give a +3 AC bonus in addition to a +1 strength bonus and a +1 stamina bonus.
