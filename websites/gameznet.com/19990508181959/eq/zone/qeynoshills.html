Everquest Zone Guide: Qeynos Hills

Qeynos Hills borders with Qeynos, Surefall Glade(a tiny town of the druids and rangers in the region) and West Karana. It also serves as an entry point to the gnoll dungeon of Black Burrow.

This zone is targetted for players levels 4-10. You will find several species of bear, wolf, snake, bat and rat. You will also find wandering gnolls.

Beware however when fighting the wolves and bears because there are two high level NPC's by the name of Cros Treewind and Holly Windstalker who patrol the region and will attack anyone who dares to fight bears and wolves. This can be especially irritating since these monsters are agressive and you can be killed simply because a monster attacked you.

Also beware for infected and diseased animals as they can give you foul diseases which can be a large nuisance for meelee classes.
