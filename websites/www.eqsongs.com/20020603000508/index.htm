Farewell Friends

1/7/2002 10:00am - It is hard to believe it is finally over, but over the weekend I logged out of of EverQuest for the final time.  It has been great fun playing EQ for the last 3+ years (starting in Beta phase 3) and running EQ Songs for the last 2+ years.  My reason for leaving is very simple, I just do not have the time to devote to the game or my guild.  Between the demands of work, relationships, and owning a home I have little time to turn my computer, let alone devote the time EQ that I would like.

    There are far too many people to thank for helping to build this site, a short list can be found here.  One person who does deserve special mention is, of course, Kaearia.  Kae was instrumental in helping this site with hard data on particularly difficult songs to quantify (ie Selo's Accelerando).

    If there is anyone out there that wishes to continue the site please let know and I can send you the files.  Personally I feel the Kocho's site, EQDiva, and Reed's spreadsheet have things more then covered.  I will keep the site online in it's present state for a while still, but there will be no more updates. 

    The Bard community, IMHO, has always been the brightest spot in all of EQ.  And I have always been proud to be part of this community.  So my friends keep your lute's dry, your sword's sharp, and your wit's sharper.

Rokenn Swiftsong <Eternal Wrath>
Troubadour of Tunare on the Ayonae Ro server.

PS For those that wish to stay in touch my email address is rokenn@earthlink.net.
