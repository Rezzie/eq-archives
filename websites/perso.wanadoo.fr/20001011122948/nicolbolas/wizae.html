Introduction: purpose - definitions of AE and DD

1ST SECTION: AREA-EFFECT CONCEPTS

A) Benefits of AE: 1) putting on a good show - 2) variety in informal use - 3) creature control through mass taunt - 4) high payoff in high risk applications - 5) alleged effects other than damage

B) Drawbacks of AE: 1) damage dilution - 2) part-time ability - 3) built-in risk - 4) uneven catalogue - 5) overwhelming EFX - 6) game balance troublemaking
