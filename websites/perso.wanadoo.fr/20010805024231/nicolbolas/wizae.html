PAGES SPECIFIC TO SPELL LINES

Columns: the staple, all-purpose AE damage spells, with fair damaging performance.

Al'Kabor spells: the lousy cousins of Columns, with overpriced wider radius and no added strength.

Rains: early-fight hybrids between AE damage and DD, the only spells able to damage a mob more than DD (by means of stacking up to 3 hits on the same mob), but slow and tricky, with tight limitations.

PB spells: the risky melee-range AE damage spells, with uneven damaging performance versus Columns, but able to hit any number of mobs at once.

AE damage spells scattered in the book: Gravity Flux - AE Tishan's Clash - Damage shield.
