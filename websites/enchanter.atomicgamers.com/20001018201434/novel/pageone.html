Prelude
"All stories have a beginning and an end, but what of the story before the beginning"

The sun danced on the horizon making the water sparkle as if imbued with magic, Toorac could make out the docks of Qeynos if he squinted his eyes almost hard enough to hurt. He did not look much different than the other passengers upon the Seaking other than his bright yellow robes that marked him as an Enchanter and his slightly larger forehead. As the mighty vessel drifted in to make contact with the dock many of the sailors where shouting in what Toorac knew to be common tongue, but what they said was so filled with slang that he knew not a single word.
"ALL ASHORE WHATS GOING ASHORE" shouted a large burly sailor.
With that the passengers moved toward the ramp which lead to the dock, some more quickly than others and some holding their stomachs displaying a unique shade of green. Toorac moved without urgency toward the ramp keeping an impassive face to hide his disgust of being in proximity to so many low humans.

  Normally he would not refrain from his haughty Erudite attitude but his current mission called for him to blend into the crowd, even if the crowd was filled with low humans.
Toorac allowed the crowd to guide him through the busy streets of Qeynos, past all the vendors shouting their wares and the common people haggling for their next meal. The cobbled streets were small, unclean and the drab buildings were crowded together, he noticed, amused that it was as if the streets and buildings where a parity of their creators within the crowd.
Keeping careful watch on the street names Toorac slowly shifted his path, almost imperceivably manipulating his position within the crowd. Anyone watching him would have seen a lost erudite caught up in a sea of drably dressed humans.
But one set of eyes followed his every move as they drifted through the oblivious crowd not far from their target. The stranger seemed to read Toorac's mind shifting with him as he navigated the crowds.
