Chapter one - Of Friends and Foes
"The art of combat is the knowledge of friends and foes"

Moribund looked up from his crouched position at an area above his head, where seconds before a chair had meet its splintering fate. As Mori uncurled his body returning to a fighting stance he turned back toward the chaos that was Crow's Tavern. Not sure exactly how the brawl had began Mori glanced over at Megien, gleefully and agilely dodging a clumsy human's attempts at attacking him, and suddenly knew very well how it began. Suddenly without warning Mori's view of Megien's dance was blocked. It took a moment for Mori's brain to adjust his eyes to encompass the massive Ogre standing in front of him. His eyes beginning at what was undoubtedly a belt buckle at his head height, Mori slowly scanned the massive hulk until he, unfortunately, found the Ogre's face with its angered eyes glaring back at him. The Ogre slowly raised his fist cocking it back to deal a killing blow upon Mori's relatively small frame.
