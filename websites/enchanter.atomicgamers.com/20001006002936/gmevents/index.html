GM Event reported only sketchy reports so far Posted Friday, February 25, 2000 by draconal
Mayong Mistmoore appeared tonight on E'ci, he brought forth endless destruction to all who dared try and smite the King of Castle Mistmoore, his servant, Kharsa, told a tale and left clues of the upcoming war and the involvement of Veeshan. Her clue was that Mayong would once again appear in the city of fallen elves (A.K.A Neriak).
