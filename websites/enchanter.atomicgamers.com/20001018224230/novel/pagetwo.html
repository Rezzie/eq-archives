Finding the street he wanted Toorac slipped out of the crowd with the grace of a cat and found himself alone on the side street. For the first time since the Seaking docked Toorac noticed that the sun had crested and the torches lining the streets where causing the shadows to dance merrily upon the building walls. Toorac felt a shiver pass his spine and instantly scanned the deserted street, Nothing. After gathering his composure Toorac continued down the dimmly lit street and listening to the timed scrape of his shoes on the cobbles quickly lost himself in thought, never noticing the quiet shadow moving and dancing within the others upon the wall.
Before long Toorac found himself standing before a large home, bright blue banners hung from the third level portraying a family crest of a unicorn overlaped by a jewel encrusted sword. Toorac smiled, this was the emblem he was looking for. Casually shifting his position into the shadows he noticed the two guards standing watch within the recessed foyer.
"Now for the wait" whispered Toorac moving deeper into the solid shadows and adjusting his stance so as to lean against one of the cold brick walls of the sleeping buildings. He was almost to his breaking point when he heard someone walking in his direction whistleing an extremly annoying tune.
"Finnaly" Toorac moved his lips and the sound barely escaped.
The figure moved at a gingerly pace down the dim street, but even at this distance it was easy for Toorac to recognize the figure in the dark red robes as a wizard.
