Before you start, the 'h' factor isn't just 2 for the 30+n*5 levels, it's 1.1 for lvls 31-35, 1.2 for 36-40 etc. -- The XP you need to earn is the difference between the total XP at the higher level and the current level.

1. Soloing, most mobs give 75*((mob level)**2) xp. Some in dungeons etc. give 80*. If the creature is a high green, it can give you 1/2 or 1/4 this amount.


2. See above -- only difference is whether or not they are in some dungeons etc.

3. Pretty good.

4. Choosing fast-spawning low blues are the best way to minimize downtime and maximise XP per hour -- Reds can give more XP, but the difference between a red and a white is pretty small as you go up in levels (lvl 23 vs lvl 20 mob is 32% more, lvl 33 vs lvl 30 is 21% more) so it's much better to go for the easier fights -- fewer resists, less mana used, fewer HP to heal up etc.

Your ShowEQ questions:
1. No idea.
2. Possibly. Definitely, if you mention it in game channels (including tell and guildsay), or obviously act on information you get using it (e.g. just hunting wisps with GLS ignoring others, or running straight to the Rare Spawns)
3. You get banned.
4. Your decision! This lets you pick mobs that you can fight AND BEAT -- certainly can make the game more fun if soloing.
