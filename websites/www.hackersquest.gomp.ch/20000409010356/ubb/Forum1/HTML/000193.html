Give each person in the party a number at the beginning. After each loot, the numbers "cycle around", so whoever was person 1 for the last round is person 2 now.

And then have the last person that "WON" looting rights generate random numbers from 1-10 until you hit a person-number. Then that person wins.

It's like Russian Roulette and will prevent any cheater from gaining too many "wins"

Another way is to keep a log of all the loot items, THEN auction them at the end (or auction before hand using one of the fair-auction methods... no money.. everyone gets the same number of points and whoever bids the "next-highest" amount wins (not the highest bidder). Then you deduct from the auction winner the bid amount of the highest bidder (people that don't win the item don't lose any bid points).

That way, you won't have the same person getting EVERY piece of good monk armor cause they won't possibly be able to "afford" it. But Non-Monks won't bid because they might be forced to get it.
