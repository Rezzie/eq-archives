In a way you'll pretty much have to.

You'll need to tell the server what NPC's say, where the NPC's are, what the NPC's stats are, what items there are, what stats items have, what graphic items have, what spells there are, what spells do, etc... That's all server-side. Granted NPC's are generated client-side, the information about them are stored server-side.

hmm... put it this way. You found a way to load an EverQuest zone file, models, and artwork. What else would you need to make it more like EverQuest? The answer is what you can configure/customize. Now, how difficult it is to customize and what-not is a different story.
