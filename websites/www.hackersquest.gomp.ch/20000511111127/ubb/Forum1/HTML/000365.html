ShowEQ is most definetly a puller's dream tool.

This can work for any class, however, in any situation. Any time you're considering pulling a mob, it's invaluable to know what's nearby you and what may soon be nearby.

For people who kite, it's nice to know that the direction you're running won't plow you into something that will agro on you.

For more "normal" pullers (rangers and monks), it lets you see around corners or beyond normal vision range to guess whether another mob will join in the fight.

Everyone else sits somewhere in the middle. They can attack a mob with greater assurance that they only pull what they want to be pulled.

Granted, you can do this without ShowEQ (and I do do it a lot), but the proggie can vastly reduce the guesswork.

My primary usage for ShowEQ anymore is the exp gain info. There's a few camps out there I've started avoiding due the horrible XP rate you get out of it. ;)
