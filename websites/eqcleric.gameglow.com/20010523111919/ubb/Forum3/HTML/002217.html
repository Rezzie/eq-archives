It won't work. There is a case like this in the game already. (two different items with same name)

Look up Rune of Concussion for wizard research. There is a lower level one, (level 34) and a higher level one. (level 49) Vendor will pay different price for the two, yet they both have same name. And you will fail trying to make a spell if try to use one in the place of another.

One very likely explanation I can come up with is that Everquest items have characteristic item number associated with them. They might have the same name, may even identify with same lore, but if the characteristic item numbers are different, they are two separate items from the server's point of view.

If you are a cleric, you might remember that snafu with terror forged mask effect being "call of bones" instead of "fear." How could they mistype that you might ask. It's very likely that spells are identified by characteristic spell number, and a mistake was made in data entry. This probably applies to the item identification as well.

Shimmering meteors on Isle 3 may drop what you are looking for. My memory of Sky is old however...
