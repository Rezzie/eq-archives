Well, looking at what you have on, I'd say ya need to weigh out how much wis you want to go down. I assume ya think it's safe to go down to 200 on the dot. WIth that in mind, let's see what options you have on each body part and what they give you.

Head: I think Sebilite scale is chain-based, so the cap is not a bucket: 12AC, 4Str, 4Wis, 2MR, 2PR. You get a whole bunch of stuff for only losing 1wis

Neck: Obvious is Necklace of Superiority. More easily, You can also get a Chrysoberyl and actually INCREASE your mana total. You lose 2 wis, gain 20 mana, which is +18 mana, +5AC, -10MR (which hurts, but probably worth it). Of course, abalone is the best, but I'm guessing you probably can't pick one up, THAT easily =)

If you don't mind dropping down to Wis 199 or so, though, I would drop the PKT and get a strathbone shell shield: AC19, Wis8 mana5.

If you just replace the head and the PKT you get +30AC and still have a 199wis with +5 mana, which is just a 17 mana difference. from what you have now.
