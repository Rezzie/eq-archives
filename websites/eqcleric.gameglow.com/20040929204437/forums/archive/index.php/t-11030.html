Hi All,

Just thought I would share that after you stop getting XP from the NIDs at the Crystal Caverns. There are 4 Juicy Spectres in......... Wait for it...... The Qeynos Catacombes at the enterence to the bloodsabres guild. :p

Yep it is a NEWBIE zone and yep you do get the 15% NEWBIE XP bonus and yep they are UNDEAD ;)

Spent 7 hours there when I was level 38 with a level 38 Paladin, just the 2 of us, we died on the break, GRRR calm is such a waste of mana.

Next time we used Enstill and Atone but ended up with 3 To break the spawn, or survive 3 spectres, you can:

1. Fear the specs and run them back to the injured Rat area, once there you can use Panic the dead till your heart is content or you mana runs dry to keep the combat to one Spec at a time.

2. Run the Spectres (USING A SOW POTION) to the zone mouth near where Cubert Pops and zone out. For some reason they can't find their way home and you can pick them off at your leasure. DO NOT TRY THIS IF NEWBIES ARE CAMPING CUBERT :eek:

NEVER use fear at the bloodsabres on the Spectres that have already been spooked once before as they go back inside the guild and you end up with 2 level 60 NPC necros. Not much is worse than siting there with divine armour up knowing you can't run because you have been hit with Immobilise and you can't gate as won't live past the 1st nuke. :(

Once we had the spawn broken it was 3/4 of a blue bubble at 38 down to just below a 1/4 of a blue bubble about 7 hours later when we dung 44, it was so easy the paladin got her 1HS, 2HS and 2HP skills all maxed. The paladin was using a Centi War Spear and a FBSS in the break, later changed to a Ghoulbane and later a Nathsar 2HS, You have to love Instrument of Nife Paladins. I didn't have Turning of the Unnatural until after I dung 39 but the 400 dot made a difference too. We also took a breather to get her level 40 spells (which is why we know so much about how to break the spawn :rolleyes: ).

Money is OK, Scathes and gold Pendants go for about 12pp Bronze for about 3pp. You also get Peridots periodically too, over all we made about 700pp each. Take a Rallic pack, EE Bag or Burlap as Scathes weigh a lot I think I had about a 240 enc.

Now you do get bad faction with the Corrupt Qeynos Guard & Bloodsabres but you get good faction with the real Guards, Antonious and the Temple of Life. It made it really good for buying my 44th spells as my faction was warm which I think saved me about 30pp.

After that, off to the Dreadlands

PLer - 52 High Elf Cleric with crappy gear and no AAs.
