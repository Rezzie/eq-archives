Sol B and SkyFire can be done in any order. You can get the container first and the pearl after that. There's nothing wrong here, since you need BOTH those items to get your third Orb anyway, it only goes against the logical order of the Epic story.

If you camp your Monk there, expect others who are camping him to say you should bring your Cleric there instead.

SkyFire Human Zordak will be a static spawn, who will not wander around. If he does not find a target on his hate list anywhere near him (say within 300 feet?) and he is not at his spawnspot, he will poof back (not walk, he will simply vanish) to his spawnspot with a cleared Hatelist. This is actually "fineprint" but important to know.

The Sol B Pearl hand-in to Zordak Ragefire is totally and completely unrelated to the spawning of Zordak Ragefire in SkyFire. They are not connected in any other way than that they share the same name and the same looks.

This is quite easily proven when you consider that the Sol B Zordak spawns once every day, while the SkyFire Zordak spawns once a week (twice if you are lucky). Hence, they are unrelated. If they were related, they would have the same spawn timer. Though the prospect of having 7 SkyFire Zordaks would be promising ...
