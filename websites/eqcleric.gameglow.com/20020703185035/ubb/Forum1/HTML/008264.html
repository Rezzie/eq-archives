With a cleric a given, I'd definetly go with a monk. A cleric's biggest weakeness is offense. So a necro/cleric duo may well be fun, using a cleric for offense is not very efficient. An sk maybe nice, but the lower DPS too high a price to pay.

If you really want a cleric for offense, you're going to want clarity which leaves you with a bard or enchanter. A bard can actually be a good partner. As cheap as bard armor is, it's not hard to equip one well enough to tank up to level 50 at least. With a good drum, rooting and dotting can work very well.

But anyway, a monk will give you excellent offense and enough defense to be able to tank, especially with a cleric backing him up. CC is covered (FD and root parking), offense is covered, defense is covered, the only real lacking thing is snare. So you can either be an innoruk cleric (inky or human) for the snare necklace, or rely on stuns and roots. Shaman+monk is a better duo just fyi.

CH + mage pet is very effective too, but you start getting into the pet exp eating thing and the horrid pathing issues mages have.
