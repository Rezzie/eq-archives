Managed to find the epic armour NPCs last night. Has anyone done any progress on these? This is the quest starter info that I managed to find.

ALL classes' NPCs are in the Twilight Sea. They are on two islands, the one closest to Katta Castellum (the one with the Inn Jax's Rest), and the island with the Mage's Tower. The cleric armour NPCs are in the Mage's tower. If you head into the tower, and veer right, you'll find Derron Gramjon (loc -1101, 474). I hailed him, and got,
