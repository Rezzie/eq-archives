It has long been said by the majority of the "eq community" classes are not balanced. This is of course true, perfect balance will never be achieved, but many people mistake balance for total equality of ability. For instance clerics can not even come close to soloing as well as necro, they are not equal in this ability. But how many times do you see groups shouting "Group needs a necro" over and over? I don't know any necros that have gone in a zone and ooced Xlvl Necrco LFG and gotten 10 tells as I have as cleric (of course sometimes I get complete silence too, but usually there is at least one group that needs me).

Yes clerics have got the shaft on some things, especially MoK and especially recently. Necros were nerfed like mad back in the day but many claim they are still "overpowered" and they probably are to a degree. I do sometimes find it frustrating when I'll get the crap beating out of me by an IG, but my necromance friend will have me come up when he is soloing him so I can HP buff him and heal him so he will be able to constantly convert health to mana and have zero downtime on the 3 spawns outside perma, but at least I get exp and loot share.

Anyway, Verrant has largely ignored the cleric class balance issues. This is because clerics ability to find a group has not been hurt, groups still need them just as much as they always have so the cleric class is still balanced in Verrant's eyes. Yes I find it insulting that the issues the cleric community brings forth are largely ignored and we are flames by other classes for whining, especially when it is something stupid like "but you guys have resses." Res is a wonderful thing.. but we all know it rarely gets you a group. But I still like my cleric the best of all the classes I have played. Most never even entertained me enough for me to continue to play them up to 20th level. As long as I play this game I will always play my cleric. I enjoy grouping, it is what draws me to this game. If I just wanted to talk to myself I would play another game that actually has a dynamic story and quests that are more then running from place to place and camping a mob. I have soloed a good bit, at times where I had trouble finding a group (like the low 20s) I hated it. I was miserable soloing low blues to get through the levels to a better heal. But as a cleric, I am rarely without a group, so I continue to play my cleric.

Sorry for the long rant, jump up and down and scream at me if you want, but remember, this is a game... if it pisses you off, and you aren't having fun, then it isn't worth your ten dollars a month. If you are having fun, then why complain?

Then VI did other things to weaken clerics:
1) cant fear people and then later not even their pets!
2) root times drastically decreasted
3) magic resists boosted on characters and items. Root, stun, blind were now useless

Clerics didnt have the mana to even kill a warrior now but couldn't even root one to try to get away. The cleric is the ONLY class that has such high defense (in a group) with so little offense.
