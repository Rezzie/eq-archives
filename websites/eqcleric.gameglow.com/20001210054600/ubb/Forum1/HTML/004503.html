I've used lull line to pull the spectre cave in Feerot from time to time.

Sometimes it would stick and then I'd nail the Spectre and get only one.

Sometimes it would not stick and I'd get 1 spectre.

Sometimes it would not stick and I'd get 2-3 spectres.

Thusly I say the results of pulling with lull, even in a scenario where resists are taking place worked better for me than pulling with damage spells. Therefore it did have some use for me.

I've done plenty of CRs using these tools solo in dungeons. Not to mention doing things like getting down to Xalgoz in Kaesora solo...stoopid spiders/reavers.
