I figured I'd start a new thread, just for Guk. At 47 and up (maybe 46) you can pull stuff solo from a distance. There are several places this works great in. Killing the ritualist at 46 (and the 2 other wizzies spots) was pretty easy.

At 48 I'm now pulling the executioner, crusader, and sage to the savant room (keep that cleared). It's a blast when no one else is in Guk (early morning is best for this).

If you do get a zol- they have tons of hit points and a higher magic resistance that most of the other frogs up to that point, so you'll have some down time from this one.

Most Vis Knights or Wizzies take 2 Banish Undead, maybe an extra Force thrown in for good measure. Wans will take 3 Banish Undeads.

It's funny to see your experience bar move after all this time (solo is so different from groups).
