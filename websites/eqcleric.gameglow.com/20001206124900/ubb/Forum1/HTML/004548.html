I don't have one.... but I've used Mod Rods to good effect.... As I understand it, mana stones work nicely for clerics, because if you stone down to almost dead, then CHeal yourself, you will receive a net gain of mana. This works fine with Mod Rods, if you have a willing magician.

Stones were nerfed a while back so that they no longer operate in the planes, and they do not operate in Kunark. Presumably they won't operate in Velious.
