Kurn's is a super low level dungeon - think Befallen v 2.0, but cooler looking and much better loot.

There are no mid-level undeads in Warsliks. There, however, is a cave where two brutes spawn (I think they are about level 30) right outside Dalnir's. I *believe* they can be pulled solo because of low agro radius. The zone to Dalnir's is very close, which is always a plus when soloing. It's kind of camped though, because people pass by there all the time. I suppose you *could* go into Dalnir's and look for a group. Also, if you are extremely bored, camp iksar bandit lord. :P

Burning Woods and the Dreadlands both have 35+ undeads. Trakanon's Teeth, near Sebilis ruins, have mid level undeads - pick on spectral partisans, they are the lowest level there. Bad thing is that a lot of mobs there run much higher on levels, and casters will gate.

As for me... I grouped in Cazic Thule back in the days of rubicite craze. Then I moved to Lower Guk. So that probably doesn't help you that much.
