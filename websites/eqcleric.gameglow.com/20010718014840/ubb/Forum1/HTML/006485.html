I have been spending my time in Lower Guk lately. At 52 the Frenzy camp is easy and I get exp for over half the mobs.

I happened to get into an AWESOME group and stay in it all Sunday (940am to 940pm). Everyone had a great time, laughed, ate fresh baked cookies and cupcakes, joked, pulled the hand to the camp, cried over only one sash all day. (It dropped at 930pm, right after we talked about leaving in 10 min!) Warrior there won it, but I was so happy for my new friend it didnt matter I lost!

Now my whole problem was the corpses people kept bringing down. I would get a tell: deadperson01 tells you "Where you at?" and not 2 min later a corpse shows up at my feet.

Now I am a nice person, I try my hardest to help out and rarely demand payment. My standard is "Donations aprieciated". After the first dozen or so, half with not even a thank you, let alone donation!
I had one group be dragged into me 3 times! *sigh* At one point it got so bad the druid was healing the group and I was just resing! *pulls out her hair*

I begin charging 50pp a head.

I did /split at least 20-30% with my group. And after I earned about 500pp post split, I stopped feeling taken advantage of. The group started laughing and having a great time over it. Yes its a little sick to get pp and amusement over someones misfortune, but after 10 hours and over 2dozen (where i stopped counting) res's what else could we have done?

The druid joked about hanging a sign on my head "Reses R Us". But until I started charging, though I dont think that detered anyone, I had felt used and taken advantage of. I left that evening with 700pp in my pocket and a 4 new people on my friends list.
