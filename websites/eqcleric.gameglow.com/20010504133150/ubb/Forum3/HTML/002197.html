Definately Akkuris' over Templars..

Complete set of Templar Armor (Kael quest) =
237ac, 240hp, 255mana, 24str, 13sta, 37wis, 7cha, 5pr, 22mr, 8dr, 11fr, 9cr.

Complete set of Akkirus' Armor (Skyshrine Armor)=
209ac, 305hp, 315mana, 10str, 35sta, 5agi, 3dex, 51wis, 9cha, 14pr, 22mr, 17dr, 12fr, 25cr.

Skyshrine armor has much better resists, mana, health points and stamina. All things that will help a cleric stay alive longer than a bit extra AC and strength.
