I know my guild has had issues with shei as well but what I do know so far is this:

1/ Shei has 4 guard in room these are on a 2 - 4 minute timer so killing these guards is useless you need to have a bard kite them around outside room for the duration of the fight.

2/ Shei DT means a corpse for each corpse in the room a SK guard will spawn and Shei DT every 5 minutes so you need to have enough melee/nuke power to burn the guards within 4 minutes so that you have time to regroup for next guard spawn.

3/ You need to have constant damage output on shei at all times and there needs to be some form of agro control (AE Taunt is good) when you MT gets DT'd cause he will.

4/ You need to have a person elected to rez the dead MT's asap so a person also should be elected to drag the corpse for the rez.

5/ You need a team of Rez/Buffers only and a Team of Healers for MT who are fighting shei.

6/ You need the backup healers to cover the SK guard healing.

And to be honest knowing all this we have still been unable to take shei out ever since verant patched her she has become VERY hard to kill. Personally I think the Arch Lich and HP in SSRA are easier kills than this one.
