GLS- turn them in to the gypsy in nk (for the good races), receive a concordance of research. Sell concordance back for more than you can sell a gls to a pc for.

Some people get their gls on wisp island. I preferred hunting commons. There are also wisps in nk, but that seemed more dangerous to me, so didn't do it.

Regular ls can also be turned in but the book received is no more valuable than the ls, so not worth the trouble. I just sold the LS themselves to the merchants.
