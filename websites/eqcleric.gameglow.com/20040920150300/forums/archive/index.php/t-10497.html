HPH: Killing orcs with a small party at the Kithicor zone is a great way to get XP. I went from 20-21 in 2 days of playing a few hours a day. Plus you can get the Shiny Brass Halbert from the named Orc..cant use it but you can sell it for 150PP!
I went down into the jail area...some things down there are nasty...wait till your 30.

East Karana is great! You can hunt Crag Spiders by the Ramp to HPH. You can hunt Gnoll Reavers and Undead Gnoll Reavers by the water near the bridge. I got a lot of XP here and its easy to run if you get overwhelemed.

West Karana: This place is great for clerics 18-21/22. There is a farm with a good supply of Scarecrows (Jack O' Lanters from unrest). Root and expulse undead in 4 hits. I went from 18-19 here Solo.

01-17-2000, 09:50 AM
A good tank will taunt the orcs off you. If only there were any good tanks in those pick-up groups....

I have a hot-key that says "GIT THIS THIN' OFF ME SO I KIN HEAL YE!" It's usually ignored by the tanks in the group when I'm fighting Highhold Pass orcs.

Solution One: Just keep casting your Healing as needed. Between high AC and good channeling, I can still heal half the time with one orc on me. The odds get substantially worse with three (which I've had happen before at that location).

Solution Two: Cast Daring instead of Healing. It casts as fast, costs the same, and heals almost as many hit points. I've heard that it does still taunt, but less so than the Healing. I haven't fully tested this yet.

Solution Three: Get in a group that has another cleric. That way there's almost always someone available who can cast Healing.
