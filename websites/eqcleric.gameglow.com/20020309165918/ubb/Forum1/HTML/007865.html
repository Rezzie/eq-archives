Leaving the big losers, monks(although monks can FD at the first sight of danger), paladins, and clerics. There is currently no non-stackable, permacharged invis item in the game (that I am aware of) for either of us to use.

With everything the cleric class has going for it, lack of a permanant Invis item is a really really small price to pay.

39-48 we have half the xp loss of any class. 49 to 55, 90% less than any class. 56+ 96% less than any class. We get that for free, Necros pay a steep price for theirs and Paladins only get 90%. The only time we eat a death is if we loot everything on accident.

We have Plate armor and a decent defense. No one even comes close to the power of a cleric where healing is involved.

Our Undead DD's rival wizards in mana/damage ratios and our magic based ones are not to bad either. Our entire spell line up is pretty sweet overall.

We have mutiple tactics to use when travelling outdoors without invis. Fear, Root, Atone to name a few.

I'd say our single weakness is indoor travell. Not having Invis can make that a pain. It's the only time when I depend on Wizards, Druids, Mages, Enchanters and even invis equipped warriors at times, to get me where we need to go alive.

Personaly, I'll gladly except that one weakness with everything else the cleric get's.
