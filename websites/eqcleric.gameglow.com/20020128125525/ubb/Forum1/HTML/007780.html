There was also an interesting talk about tanking high mobs on monkly-business. Apparently, if you can get a monk to about 1750 ac (monk in question sits at over 4000hp and 1610ish ac unbuffed), they start to make decent tanks. He can't tank the AoW and 3 other mobs of similar caliber, but he's tanked pretty much everything else and in most cases does it better than a warrior with defensive. The insane ac gives him mitigation near that of a warrior, and a monks avoidance ac and defensive skills can apparently avoid enough damage to counteract not having defensive.
