Well it's a long story, but I will try to condense it.

Our cleric had been camping RF in SF with a few people (I know, not enough people to claim rights to kill it - but enough to give a shout to the guild to come help) for 3 days.

A wizard in our guild was helping her camp on this night. The cleric went to swamp of no hope to help a druid with the named frog there leaving the wizard to watch the camp. The wizard was actively kiting a couple mobs around in the general area that RF spawns.

The other guild - WOF - moved into the area with some people. When our cleric got back (about 15 minutes or so) to SF she saw the other guild there. She immediately felt this would be a problem and messaged one of the officer in our guild that was on line. He spoke to the Guild leader of the other guild and explained the situation.

The other GL refused to acknowledge that we were already there. He claimed 1 or 2 people in the spot didn't count as a camp. He also said that if RF popped they were going to kill it.

At that point, it became a race. As more and more of our guild logged on, they were requested to come to SF to help camp RF. Some of the people there left and other came. I think we had around 25 people in zone.

A couple hours later, our cleric decided she had enough of the competition, so she spoke with the senior officer present in our guild and between the 2 of them, they decided to call off the camp.

About 10 to 15 people from OG left the zone after the camp was called off.

I remained behind in SF to finish up a couple conversations I was having. Some of the other people stayed to do some xps.

About 10 minutes after half of the OG's present left, RF popped. I was the only remaining officer present in the zone, so I gave the order in GU for everybody to move back against the wall and not to interfere.

WOF engaged the dragon. A couple people in GU were reporting numbers of WOF and RF health.

A few minutes after WOF engaged, the number of their people in zone was down to 2. A couple seconds later, RF came running over to us. He attacked 2 of our people, so we killed it. Our cleric looted the heart.

WOF petitioned that we pre-agroed therefore they weren't able to finish killing RF. Then they had 3 or 4 naked people trying to sit on the corpse so we couldn't loot.

The petition was forwarded to the Server GM to review and he is supposed to make a decision this coming Saturday.
