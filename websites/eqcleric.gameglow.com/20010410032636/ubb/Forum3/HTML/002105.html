Well, I too am a non-Uber Guild Cleric, and I am on Giant faction. However, I chose the faction because it was the less popular at the time (the details of the quest armor were not yet known), and figured I'd go where the crowds weren't, as well as for RP reasons.

That said, I like the faction, Kael is a nice central base of operations, and I can kill both Coldain and Giants. And I don't really care that it is unlikely I will ever achieve Templar's armor without some major even in the future.

But, if you are interested in getting Velious quest armor, then you might wanna stick with Coldain faction. You start out Apprehensive. Thurgadin is the most developed of the cities with the largest variety of vendors and services. The Quest armor is reasonable to complete (requires hunting in Kael). Being on this faction will allow you to ally with the Dragons later in life if you desire (killing Giants earns points with both Coldain and Dragons).

Being on the Giant team whole hog requires alienating both Coldain and Dragons.

Hope this helps.

It depends.

Twelve people can hunt level 45+ giants inside Kael for Thurgadin armor quest. Twelve people can also hunt, with much greater risk and difficulty, while losing faction in Kelethin and Felwithe, in Plane of Growth and get the same armor. (different name, same stats) If I were going to Velious strictly for armor quest, I'd pick dwarves. Word of caution, these armor quests sometimes can take a long time to do.

But there is more to Velious than armor quests.

Kael has a lot of non-armor quests, many of them requiring hunting dragons. Other two factions, especially Skyshrine, suffer from lack of quests other than armor quests. A lot of Kael quest items are top tier items, and surplus you get from those quests will sell for a small fortune on open market as well. Hunting Western Waste dragons (and thus raising your giant faction) also have two advantages - dragonhide armor, which is poor man's version of Thurgadin/PoG armor for melee, and individual dragon's talismans, no drop version of NoS. And there are number of dragons in WW that can be taken down by two groups.

As for travel in Velious, dragon faction can't be beat. Wuoshi, dragon that sits on Wakening Land portal, will leave you alone. There will be very few KoS mobs near Cobalt Scar portal as +CoV, and relatively easy time traveling in Western Wastes. (which gives you easier access to Dragon Necropolis, level 55ish dungeon inside level 60 zone...) And Skyshrine is centrally located. (gem vendor/bank included)

If you are approaching 60, I would recommend allying with giants and kill everything in sight. If you are in low-mid 50's, then I would choose dwarves, and maybe keep dragon faction (easy to raise if you fight in Kael) for easy travels. Then start siding with giants as you finish armor quests in Thurgadin.
