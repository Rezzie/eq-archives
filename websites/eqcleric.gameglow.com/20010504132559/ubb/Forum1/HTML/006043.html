EQ has some serious problems at higher levels. Cooperation between high level guilds/groups has reached an all time low on many servers because of competition for limited high level spawns.

Many casting classes are poorly suited to Verant's high level game design and have become discouraged. Verant seems clueless as to how to address these issues and has recently cut themselves off from one of the few sources of interactive feedback they had - their forums.

Lower level characters today are often heavily twinked somewhat distorting the low level game.

EQ is in a state of evolution. The game was not designed with the thought that people would still be playing it for a year or more. Verant is desperately trying various ideas in an attempt to retain long time customers. Their recent efforts have only had limited success.
