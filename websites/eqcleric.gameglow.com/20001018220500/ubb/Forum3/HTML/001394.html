I finially got Grimrot's heart this morning at about 5:30am CST. I just wanted to recap what I learned from this camp.

The PH no longer despawns. You have to kill it.
And when you kill it is the important part. If you want to an undead Grimrot then he needs to spawn at night. If you want the live version then he must spawn in the day.

So decide on what game time you would like the Grim to spawn and add 9 game hours to that time and kill the PH then. For example I wanted the undead Grim, and I decided that I wanted him to spawn at around 8PM game time. So I kept the PH alive until 5AM norath time and then killed him. The spawn time is somewhat imprecise. It is best to come back in 3 RL hours and then wait for the spawn. The spawn is usually between 195 min to 216 min.

I had been doing the above for several RL days and I was still getting the PH. I read somewhere, dont remember where, to kill the two putrids( tolon and bozin) close to his spawn site. So I show up at three hours and start immediatly killing Tolon and Bozin, Woohoo undead Grimrot spawns, which is then KSed by an evil SK.

OK at this point the game time is around 9PM and Grim has been killed and the spawn is due to spawn again at noon game time. So I came back in 3 hours, kept the PH alive until 5AM game time and killed him. Came back in 3 hours started killing Tolon and Bozin and got a PH, One thing different about this time was that there was a low level necro harvesting bone chips from the camp so the camp was disturbed. Reset the ph again, came back 5:15AM rl time this morning. No one is around and started killin Tolon and Bozin. Lord Grimrot spawns and I now have his heart.

So does killing Tolon and Bozin and having an undisturbed camp make Grim spawn? Dont know, however for the previous 3 days I had been killing the ph to get him to spawn at 9PM game time and I always got the PH. And by only killing Tolon and Bozin I got Grimrot.
