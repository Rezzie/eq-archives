Yes, you are.

Every spec skill you put a point into, you "learned the basics". Then, that skill will cap at 50. Now, ONE skill can go past 50, but only IF you have at least "learned the basics" in the other 4 skills. Yes, this means you have to blow a couple skill points and drop one into spec:conjration and spec:evocation.

Once you make sure every spec skill has 1 point into it, cast nothing but heals until you see Alteration hit 51. That locks in alteration, and caps the rest at 50.

Something about that log bothers me.

From the first CH to the one before you used a mod rod, was FIVE full minutes. Hell, even taking into account 130 seconds spent casting, that still leaves 3 minutes to sit and med. With just C2, you could have meded up to FM in 3 minutes. Even standing you would have regened 50 ticks @ 14 mana per tick or 700 mana. Through the entire fight (6 minutes), that adds up to 840 mana.

I'll assume you stood the whole time to avoid aggro, and had C2, no FT items, no bard, no necro.

3700 mana pool + 600 mana from the mod rod + 840 mana regened while standing = 5140 mana total.

5140/16 casts of CH = 321 mana per cast of CH.

Now, 321 mana per cast is STILL a 20% savings per cast, and MUCH more realistic than 280 mana per cast which would be a 30% savings (I seriously doubt it's 30%)
