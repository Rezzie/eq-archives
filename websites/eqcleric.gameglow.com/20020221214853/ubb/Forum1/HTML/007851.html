Silent Redemption/Watchers got him on Brell a few weeks back. We had a CH rotation set up so that heals landed on the MT every three seconds or so. You really can't afford to wait any longer than that. Hence, mana is usually an issue. Make sure you have mages dropping rods near your clerics, because the clerics won't be able to cover the ground between where rods are and where they need to be to heal quick enough. Make sure druids/shamans/non-rotation clerics are backing up with nature's touch, chloroblast, and DL. That's about it on the healing side of things...I won't go into the melee and caster details on a cleric board
