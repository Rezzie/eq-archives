I completed the Dark Elf Cleric Initiate quest. Doing so yields you to a Disciple.


Disiple Symbol of Innoruuk
AC5 plus 2 wis plus 1 int plus 7 mana
Effect: Siphon Str (takes 10 str from target and gives to you)
Cleric Shaman
Human Dark Elf and Troll

Will run through the whole quest from start.


First you head down to a halfling camp and collect 4 of their necklaces. Then return to the Cleric Guild Master Perrir(?) on the first floor.
Then collect Master Whoopal's head, but slaying...Master Whoopal, go figure. Take this to Draxis. He is located at the Spurned Tower, which is the tower where all the non-necros go..mage/wiz/ench.
For giving him the head he returns you a mask, which looks like a head, that will give you 1 guise of halfling.
Now head down to West Commons and near the back a npc halfling called Wallin. He does wander a bit..but I waited on the path near Kitchor and he always wandered back there, I did this quest several times trying to figure out what the key phrase was.
Tell him that you will assist him and he will give you a letter that you return to Draxis. He then tells you to find the spy and return the spy's head and your Initiate symbol to Ivlor(?).
The spies are located in that very zone. Head back towards the priest of discord and your notice the waterfall. Climb up the waterfall and take a right. After going down the path your fall into a pit. Here two halfling leatherfoot spies spawn. Kill them and one or the other will have a head. Take this head and your intiate symbol to the clerics guild now.
Turn them in to Ivlor(?), who is located on the third floor next to two other guild masters, he is on the left side upon going up, and he will give you the Disciple symbol.
