Please refer to this link http://eq.castersrealm.com/archetype/Details.asp?Id=40


Basically Healing gift increases your chances of landing a critical heal ( critical heal gives twice as many hitpoints as its base.)

With healing gift 3 you have a 10% chance of landing a crit with each healing spell cast.

IE)Say a 438 hitpoint remedy spell will heal 876 hitpoints.

Less useful than criticals are to nukers, but I wouldn't call it "bad for clerics".
