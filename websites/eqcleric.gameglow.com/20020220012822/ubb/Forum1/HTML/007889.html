Official guild policy: kill everything, faction is nothing.

Personal policy: I am mostly just anti-giant. I like Coldain faction since Thurgadin is the most convenient city in all of velious. It's the only source of invis potions in velious as well. Plus lots of good trade skill vendors if you care about that sort of thing.

I'm a bit of a roleplayer and as a high elf, I had to stick to +COV/+Coldain. Giant faction is not a choice for a Tunare-worshipper. (Tunare and the COV are allies against Kael Drakkal. See the "Note to Eysa" quest for details of the alliance.)

So in my case, I really didn't have a choice.

I will mention these small advantages tho: +COV is easier travelling than +giant. Wuoshi and Cobalt Scar? np. Additionally, I like how SS armor looks. I hate how Kael stuff is purple. Of course, these are minor things.
