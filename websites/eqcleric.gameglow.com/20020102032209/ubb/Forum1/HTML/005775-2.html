As a necro, any heal received during downtime is fine with me :>

I think a lot of the resistance to heals comes from a sort of confidence -- you spend your played time getting really good at balancing your health and mana vs the situation, and then some cleric thinks he has to help. When you make your bones soloing giants, you get to the point that you know what you are doing, because if not you'd be paste. This is the same issue as people telling clerics "Shut up and med," as if getting mana back had never occured to you before and you thought you were supposed to being stunning mezzed mobs.

As a downtime reducer, sure. If you have someone angry that you healed them when you were at full and there was nothing going on, well, they need to step back from the game for a few minutes and maybe find another hobby.

I'm pretty good friends with my regular cleric, and we've worked out that he really shouldn't throw a combat heal on me unless I've got mobs on me and am dropping fast. Feign Death is pocket change, but the time he spends tossing a heal usually isn't in a crisis.

As a shaman, I'll often tell a cleric I haven't worked with before "don't worry about my HP unless I'm being actively beat on." Due to regrowth, fungi tunic, bearform, and my base healing, I get back 36 hp/tick standing, 2 more sitting down. So any minor bumps will quickly clear up.

It's just a lot of regen and they may not take that into account unless they actually look at my gear to see the fungi.

Apart from that, they can spend mana however they like, I'll assume they know what they're doing. As a cleric, I'll often tell a shaman "go ahead and cann down to 20%, I'll CH you up, I'm fm." if I'm ok with mana and see them sitting and medding while below fhp.

Just make sure the communication's open.
