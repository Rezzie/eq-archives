Taking Kaitlyn's advice (thanks http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif) I hunted the ruins there while waiting for Big Red to spawn. This is a great zone for us. I was taking on drybones, barbedbones, monks and GS. By myself and sometimes with a tank of 25-30 I could eaisly fight here. There are 9 spawn points and if you are solo just leave the greenies alone and they will not come when you pull. I did this and was pulling singles easy enough. The zone is close too just in case.

Big Red never did spawn for me but I got level 26 back (darn MM) and Barbedbones drop FS. This place is great xp at my level and the 32 level Necro was getting good xp off the GS's.
