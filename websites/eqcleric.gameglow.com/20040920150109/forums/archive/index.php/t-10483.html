Well it spent 16 Hours on the weekend camping the Eyes at the temple. It was a ball. If you get a Druid, 2 Wizzy's and of course a cleric you can make a killing.

Our strategy was to get the eyes before they made pets and buffed themselves. Therefore, the second the eye spawns, I would hit it with Holy Might and the Wizzy’s and the Druid would cast their best DD spells. If it is a regular eye it’s dead. If it’s a lord, you need to go the 2nd round, but this is OK as it’s stunned from your Holy Might spell http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif. Only about 1 in 5 eyes ever got a spell off before they died, these were the ones that resisted Holy Might.

In the 16 hours we killed about 150 Evil Eyes, but only picked up 3 PBBs http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/frown.gif, but we made 4.5 bubbles of XP each with about 180pp profit, putting me on 24th http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif

I strongly recommend this zone.
