Rather than wait for the complete info (personally I'd love to see partial info but I'm curious that way) I'll post what I learned so far.

As posted the quest starts with our trainer. You can either go round up four bone chips to get the next converation or use the trigger words "Initiate of Tunare".

He will want four ghast hearts as indicated. They're in lesser faydark.

Now the fun begins.

It seems the ghasts just spawn roaming around l-fay just like skeletons and mummies.

DO NOT do what I did, and sit there and say "Darn now I'm gong to need a ranger to find these things" Fortunately it only took me a couple of minutes to remember "sense the dead" and I was merrily off hunting undead.

I've found three things - skeletons, mummies, and rancorous ghasts. There is the small camp at the pillar which you'll have to clear routinely.

I'm level 53 and I loaded up my the anti-undead nuke that does 273, and the drop in one spell. So they're not much threat.

What IS a big threat is the brownies. There are brownie scouts that roam around lesser faydark. Not many, but enough. They con green to me, but they give me experience.

These things are mean. They are druids and move at SOW speeds, and they have an annoying way of making their spells stick even when they're green to you AND you're buffed for them.

Therefore I encourage you to go in with full buffs and to keep resist magic and fire up at all times. I also would melee the undead I would find so as to avoid wasting mana. I can kill a brownie but it takes about 80% of my mana to do so. If you are lower leve, I suggest root and run for the zone.

So far I've managed to find and kill 5-10 ghasts and no hearts yet. Most annoying.

More as it becomes available if someone else doesn't get it first.

If you remember nothing else, remember: fear the brownies.
