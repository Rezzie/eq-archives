I think both sides have valid viewpoints.

On the one hand, if there are many who want in and noone has been waiting longer than others, the group has the right to choose which class/level/person they feel is the greatest asset to the group.

On the other hand, if a person wants in and has waited patiently for a spot, they should be given a chance to show what they can do.

In situation two, if the person turns out to be a drain on the group, then they should be willing to step aside. If, for instance, this 38 warrior turns out of be a liability to the group, they should be willing to leave and allow a more suitable replacement to step in. More likely than not this would not be handled well, but it could be if we could all just respect each other.
