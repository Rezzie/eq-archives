Usually, as soon as root wears off, the atoned NPC will start pathing back to it's spot (assuming it is static). You need to atone 2x to be safe, it doesn't always work the first cast.

What I would do (if I was KoS to dorfs) is wait until I see the shuttles at the dock, then just run and get on them. NPCs, even if they are already pissed at you, can't usually hurt you once you are on a boat. To be extra safe, you could pull her down and atone her, then get on the boat - but it isn't safe to sit there visible on the docks.

Yipes, no, atone will make the mob forget you, but they will go right back to their spawn place when root breaks.

What I have always done, being a DE cleric, is wait somewhere safe, like on the other side of the wall...or even in the water, watching for the boat. Then when the boat comes I just swim up into it. Or ONTO it, if you are talking about the barges.

You can swim right INTO the boat, just line yourself up in front of it while it is parked at the dock, hit your page up key and swim forward right into the front of the boat. It will usually swim you right INSIDE the boat, I've done it a million times.

This works!

First, always carry invisi potions with you. (buy them at any potion vendor).

Second, stand at the dock for Fi Vie in the corner. (as you look down the dock at Magnus, on your left is the corner safe from her and the fisherman)
