MISTMOORE!!!

In the graveyard of mistmoore is a small building (tomb). Inside the tomb is a coffin. On top of this coffin spawns a flower (looks like a small bag, of course). Pick up the flower, run around to the back of the tomb and give the flower to the shadowman there. (this shadowman is indifferent) The flower respawns every 60 seconds. You gain Temple of Ro faction.

No mana, No medding, No killing, 1 faction hit a minute, and it's supposedly a pretty significant hit.
