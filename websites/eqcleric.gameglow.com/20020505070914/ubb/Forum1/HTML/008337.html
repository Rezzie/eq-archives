I felt that others may bennifite from my exsperiance.
So here is some information I gathered while trying to get to Alley CoV.

Just to put it in perspective ...
*** One of the druids I did this with started 3 Arena raids into Warmly
... it took him exactly 15 runs to hit Alley, and it took me exactly 24 ...
so faction for (1 Arena raid 3hr) = faction for (3 quests runs 12 minutes). ****

This is all first hand information.

If you are doubius , move to FAST part...
If you are not, go have fun in Kael till you are..
(collect all helms and toes, you can use them when you hit doubious)


FAST PART ....
There is a nice quests that give a VERY NICE faction hit for CoV + Yellinak

Each quest run takes about 4 minutes from start to finish.

Following the directions bellow will take you from
Start of Dubious - > Alley in about 6 hrs, and will cost ~3k.

Break down of Quest / Faction Rewarded
Duboius - ~ 11
Apprehensive - 7
Indifferent - 14
Amiably - 7
Kindly - 16
Warmly - 24

The number indicates how many times you do the quest to complete that faction level.
(I didn't start quest till I was 1 Arena raids into dubious,
so ~11 took me from where I was to Apprehensive.
If I had to guess, I would say 16 total for that level)


This quest can be done over and over till you get Alley.
But... (ok you new there was a but)
Non-gate classes will need TL Wiz.
Gate classes will need a Dru / Wiz.
If you are a Dru / Wiz, you need no one.

Velium Ore shipment
Zones SS, Thurg
SS - NPC Sentry Kael (in the kittchen)
Thurg - NPC ( enter mines , come to first junction with Left, Right, Streight options...
go strieght, Come to next junction , turn right, duck under rock, and drop down to NPC)

Quest , set up ...
Bind at Thurg NPC
Port to CS ( mobs here are on CoV, so no invis needed)
Run to SS, go to kitchen...

Quest Steps ...
Say to Sentry Kael.. "I am willing" , and get note
Gate (or TL to bind) back to Thurg NPC, give him note, and 50pp , get Veliumn Ore.
Port to CS, and run to SS.
Give Shipment to Sentry Kael get ... CoV , Yellinak, plat(~6pp), exp, and a bracer(1/3times).
..... Rinse and Repeate .... [get note, gate to bind, hand in w/50pp, gate back to CS ]


Notes:
Don't forget to get Note ... before you gate to bind
You will get a bracer ( most likely a dozen or so) they are no drop, so i just destryoed them.
Stats are not great, but effect is nice.... unlimited summons throwing hammers, that do not poof on log off.

GL to all
hope the information helps someone.
