Don't worry about how long it takes you to get planar armor. Currently at level 53 I only have 2 pieces of plane armor. I won the helm from hate after spending 2 1/2 weeks camping up there in the SW corner straight (prior to the patch where they added 3 things in that corner).

I had been level 50 for quite some time before I won that helm. I won the boots by default (only cleric out of 3 in fear that didn't have them already) when we killed a jello mob. Eventually I would like to go back to hate and get more armor, but i'm not overly concerned about it. Take your time and enjoy your game and get up there when you can, but don't make it a be all, end all to your armor quest type deal. There is a a lot of nice items coming out of Kunark, so don't think that armor from the planes is the only stuff you will need or want to get.
