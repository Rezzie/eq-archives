Snare on the other hand... in all it's forms, Snare/Ensnare, Bonds of Force, Darkness line of spells from Necro's/SKs. Isnt snare the exact anti-RvR spell in all of EQ? Consider...

With CH you have to have someone up close and personal with a mob. Wouldnt be needed in any other way. Someone is being attacked by the mob, thats Risk in RvR.

Seems very shortsighted of people to just say "oh, because of CH Verant broke the game and now everything has to be built around it with a bazillion hitpoints or AC" because when they say that they are referring to the singular boss mob at the end of a lot of killing to get to him. Complete Heal isn't the sole cause of the bazillion HP mobs.

It is the combination of Complete Heal + massive increase in the average tanks HP + massive increase in the quality of weapons available.

Pre-Kunark, CH really only healed 2k at the most (tanks just didn't have more HP than that). Thats a 5-1 HP/Mana ratio in the best possible case, and most of the time it was in the 3-1 to 4-1 range. A bit better than the fixed value heals, but not that extreme.... With 2000 mana a Cleric could reasonably expect to heal 6 to 8 thousand HP (assuming he lived long enough, the fight lasted that long, and not counting any mana regen). And, that tank he was healing might be lucky enough to be wielding a par of Yaks (you know - those 8 Damage 24 Delay weapons that even most newbie reject these days).

Fast forward to today : You can get your tanks to 6k+ HP. You can reasonably expect a high end raiding cleric to be healing 5k on each CH. That is a 12.5-1 ratio. 2.5x as much healing as was available from the exact same spell 2 years ago. At the same time, those tanks aren't using Yaks anymore. They have upgraded to truly sickening weapons with near 1:1 Dam/Delay ratios. They are doing more than twice the damage they did before (3 to 4 times as much if you factor in how much better the available haste is as well as the higher haste cap).

So, now we have clerics healing 2-3 times as wel as they used to and melees doing at least 3x the damage they did before...


If there was no CH, the increased tank HPs wouldn't matter much - all they would do is provide a buffer so you would have more time to get the 'normal' heal spells off.

If HP inflation hadn't happened, CH wouldn't be any more efficient that before.
