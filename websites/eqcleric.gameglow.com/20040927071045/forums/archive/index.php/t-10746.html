Burning Woods - good exp moderate chance of finding a group

City of Mist - great exp but small chance of finding a group (BYOG)

Trakanons Teeth - good exp but BYOG

Skyshrine - mediocre exp but guardian armor (best pre-planar/pre-velious quest armor IMHO)

Happ the ever-dead gnome in LFay - solo, but almost always permacamped

Oasis of Marr - Spectre island do the 4 corner specters solo

Kithacor forest - night time only, bring some friends

Rathe Mountains - solo HG's (think they are still blue at 45) for nice cash... buy reagent gems at merchant when you have too much cp/sp/gp

There are your non-typical answers

If you can grab a group from DL that is mid-high 40s try the outside of Chardok in the Burning Woods.

OMG we TOTALLY rocked this place and I got 2 yellow in 2 hours in 46...which was pretty cool.

Also the split from the MLer was 33 plat 155 gold each for the six of us, although the only thing good that dropped was a Runic Carver http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/frown.gif

Anyway, the pops by the gates are like on a SUPER fast timer, all conned blue at 46. Most were blue to a 50 as well. You can continually pull with no problems, and if you pull to the field you have zero chance of adds as no wanderers.

Its not far from the DL/BW zone in, so your group can be fighting outside of Chardok 10 minutes after leaving the north wall of KC.

Its great!
