Comparing Happ to the Spectres ...

I have messed around with both in my early and mid 40s. Happ is much easier than spectres - not only does she stay put and not roam about to find friends, but you can med without being interrupted by marsh bears and skellingtons every few minutes.

However, Equestrielle the corrupted does path near the Gnome camp, so you have to be attentive enough to run to the Steamfont zone before she can keel you. She is a mean horsie.

I find both forms of soloing dreadfully boring, but will do them when having a semi-afk kind of a day. You will get much better experience and much more fun grouping with friends and hunting things that are faster paced. Droga was greening out by 45, unfortunately, but you might try getting together a group and heading to City of Mist or grouping up and going to Howling Stones or many of the Luclin dungeons.
