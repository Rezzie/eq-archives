Prologue
The Celestial Rapture is a high end european raiding guild. We are the top guild on Kane Bayle with our maingoals of advancing in the storyline and get access to the new zones, killing the hardest mobs and beating the toughest scripts. We work together as a team.
Dont bother to apply if you like to achieve your personal goals! Everyone will benefit from the guild`s progress!
We raid 6 days a week from 6pm CET until midnight (saturday is our offday!).
Our current news can be found on http://www.tcr-guild.com

WARNING: WE ARE NOT A FAMILY GUILD!

Application Process
Usually, once you send your application througth the application form on http://www.tcr-guild.com/application.php you will get contacted within 1 week by the recruitment team (Mitch, Oculus).
We expect you to be online for questions we might have after you send in your application form.

If we accept you as an applicant you will be invited to several raids to see how the guild works and we can check you out if you do your job on raids. If you doing well you should get the fullmember status within 3 to 5 weeks.
