We get 4 resses currently.

29 - Revive, 0% exp returned.
39 - Resuscitate, 40% exp returned.
49 - Ressurection, 90% exp returned.
56 - Reviviscence, 96% exp returned.

The ONLY other ress available in the game is something like "Customer_Service_Resurrection". And that one is done by GM's. It gives 100% exp returned, AND has no res effect.
