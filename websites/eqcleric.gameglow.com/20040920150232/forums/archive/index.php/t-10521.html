If you can logon early in the evening for your server, try getting into a High Keep guard room group. Be prepared to play an alt for an hour or two while you wait, and once you get into the group you'll want to stick for 2-4 hours. Often clerics get bumped up the list since the group's usually prefer a cleric as the primary healer.

On a good night, you'll get 1 yellow per hour in high keep. On a bad night, it'll take longer.

The big advantage to most HK groups is that there is *usually* a master loot system in place meaning you don't have to suffer ninja looting players.
