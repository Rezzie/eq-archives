Ok, a couple of things you should know.


1. The cleric epic will make a HUGE difference to you and your guild if you do multiple group raids. Being able to res people during battle for no mana has made a HUGE difference for me and my guild.

2. Chardok sucks, and the lag is horrible. If the highest person in your guild is 52 the odds of you getting past this part are very slim. However, what you can do is tag along on other guilds raids and help them out. Go a few times and you should be able to get the note.

3. Ixblat, what a wuss. This guy is EASY. We killed him 6 times in a row in about a hour. The trick to him is tash, a level 60 shaman for slow, and a main tank with multiple clerics healing him. Fight him at the zone to skyfire.

4. The WORST part of the entire quest is Zordak. My recomendation is that you have a FD monk/necro/shadow knight and camp him here. Don't worry if you don't get Zordak right away. Be there and help out others that WILL beat you to the spawn. Helping them out will be appreciated and they'll probably return the favor. If you do get lucky and get the spawn solo make sure you have enough people to take him down. Basically if you have fought vox you'll know what you need.

In closing, I really can't emphasise how much of a difference my epic made for our guild (I'm the only cleric in our guild with an epic). It's made such a huge difference.

Don't give up, never surrender. It may take you a while but don't sweat that.

The one really important thing is don't sit down in Zordak's lair for 4 months like I did. Heh you'll go nuts.

Best of luck to you.
