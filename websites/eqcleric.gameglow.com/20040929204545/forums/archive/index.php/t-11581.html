Should killing one level 60 mob in Sanctus Seru (inset zone name you hunt here as I mentione Sanctus due to killing mobs in that zone for the reports for a quest) give the same exp as giving some gimp level 47ish spider in velks?

Level 60 mobs already gave more experience than level 47 mobs.

Using the widely accepted formula Experience = (Level ^ 2) * ZoneSpecificConstant, a 47 mob in the same zone as a 60 mob would only yield 61% as much experience. Of course, comparing a mob in Velk's to a mob in SSeru throws a wrench into the works.

What the patch did was increase the spread by giving a bonus at the high end and a penalty at the low end. Increasing risk vs. reward is perhaps appropriate, but there are some definite downsides, especially for casual players and people still climbing up to level 60. The changes also increased the value of efficient healing in experience groups, and since clerics still have a monopoly in that arena, clerics received a net boost at the expense of other healers.
