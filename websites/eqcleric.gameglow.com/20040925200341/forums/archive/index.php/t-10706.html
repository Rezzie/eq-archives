Best Places to regain exp or get through hell levels that are not camped- ever

Ice Giants in Everfrost- grab a 44 or above necro or mage

Seafury Cycs in OOT- grab necr/mage/tank

Guards- grab couple of melee folk and have fun. Great inside of cities, except lower level peeps tend to train you- especially in Neriak.

Dwarfs in BB- several of them are NOT very magic resistant and don't give faction hits against dwarves in the dwarven city (just Dwarf faction)

Problem with Guk or Sol B is over crowding- too many people to pull constantly unless you play at off hours. <sigh> Times of Hand/AM/Lord are over- same with Priest/King/Champ in Sol B.

Live Fort in Guk may still be uncamped on several servers, but that's getting crowded too.
