I'm not totally positive, but I believe the FG's respawn 8 hours from when they were killed. So if Magi Rokyl (sp) was killed last, there will be an extra period of time before he respawns from when the first one respawns.

Once the FG's spawn, no matter where you sit in the lair (unless FD) you will agro the FG or Magi that spawn in the area of Naggy's lair.

Ragefire spawns right in the middle of the back of the lair. There is that column that is part of the back wall between the fire pots, he spawns directly in front of the column.

firegiants pop every 7 hrs providing they were all cleared at once, this is from the death of the first one of course. In the lair magi pops near the back and you must not bind in there, also two FG's stand either side of the door to the lair as well but these cannot be agroed if you sit at the back.

Its best to bind at zone-in really then if you get in trouble you can gate and call in reinforcements. It takes just a good group at most to clear them and a chanter is very useful as they can be charmed

Sadly the camp can take anything from a few to 72 hours (heard of more as well) so make sure you take care of yourself during that time, get a friend to play you, take a nap etc etc you cannot do a long period of time alone and should not have to as your epic is for the benefit of the whole guild.

Yes he does spawn in the back of the lair is a trader wearing a red robe and will make you jump out of your skin when he pops lol One nice tip is to loot the robe he wears after him and the dragon are killed, its a nice keepsake.

Also remember when you go and do your final hand in don't do it while sleepy and unfocused or you may mess up

Camping ragefire is fairly easy (but boreing).

Get a necro to level 18 or so for FD.

Make sure when you create that necro that his name is very close to your clerics name.

Camp your cleric out in naggy's lair with invis on (use potions) and gate/invul mem'd.

Get your necro there and FD him there. If anyone asks if it's camped your there to say yes. If they give you the standard "FDers dont count" then camp your necro and log your cleric in.

ALWAYS leave ALL the FG's up to keep the annoying people out. If you know the fg's are being killed be ready to log your cleric back in.

This will let you camp this annoying mob while reading a book and not keeping your guild there 24/7.
