The boots are called "Boots of the Huntsman" and drop off a mob in a room to the left of the Dain's throne. Just invis up after the Dain goes to bed (he and the seneschal see through invis) and head to his room. Fight him in the corner, otherwise he'll run to the hallway and agro the nasty royal guards.

He drops the Huntsman boots, as well as the head needed to do the Coldain Boot quest (ac3 +8 wis +8 int +35 mana).

On the other side of the throne is the Royal Scribe, who's head can be turned in for the lovely Coldain Skin Gloves (ac12 +6 wis +more stats).

Both quests you need to have amiable or better Tormax faction I believe.

Icewell Keep can be fun!
