I go for AC/HP/Resists over +mana.

Two situations..

My group dies because I was out of mana - but 2 extra remedies might have saved the day (assuming I was fm to start with).

My group dies because I died.

Both happen, but IMO the second is much more likely. Since much of the time you will have pulls coming in before you are fm, and you will have chance to med in combat, I am not *too* worried about maxing total mana..the one time when failure due to lack of total mana is a significant possibility is in Dragon/God raids, where there's no question of what you should be maxing.

Also, if those HP/AC/Resists save you one heal on yourself every now and again, they have made up for some mana.
