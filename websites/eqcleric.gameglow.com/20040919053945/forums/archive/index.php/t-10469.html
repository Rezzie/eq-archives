heh, only against the bloodgills, but my level 25 warrior has raised from 20 to 25 here with only 1 death.
The trick is to team up with a cleric and then just do what I did in Oasis when I was a young cleric:
Pull a mob to where the cleric is medding, whack it senseless and grab the nessesary heals. Fast non-stop pulling and a steady xp.

Grouping is also nice, as you can kill many sarnaks and higher gobos here.

The level range here is very good starting at around 15, head to FM when you reach 30, come back when you are 44.
