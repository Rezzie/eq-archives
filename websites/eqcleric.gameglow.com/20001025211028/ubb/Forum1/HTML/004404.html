I've read time and time again that clerics can't solo after lvl 20 unless they solo undead, and about how our effeciency at soloing sucks. Well I have to strongly disagree. I am presently a lvl 36 cleric on rallos zek, I have a 735 buffed AC, 1120 hps, 150 wisdom. I have absolutely no problem soloing non-undead blues.

I sit in melee pounding on them with my cadecaus and casting wrath over and over. When I finish a fight I am usually left with 2-2.5 bulbs of mana, and only lose about .5 - 1.5 bulbs of health. This is more effecient than my wizard was using roots after each nuke.

So if you are a younger cleric don't listen to what everyone says, always take AC into consideration when you decide what to wear, don't worry about getting to 200 Wisdom, its highly overrated at these lvls, and as long as you can solo something without root you'll do just fine for effeciency.

My general rule of thumb, never drop more than 1 ac to pick up 1 wisdom. A skull shaped barbute is a lot better than a runed circlet, the chestplate of the constant is better than a bs tunic... you get the idea.

A lot also depends on where you are soloing. A lot of stuff is much harder to solo on in Kunark than say the old world. I can remember being level 50 and EASILY taking down spectres in oasis or OOT. Once Kunark opened, I went to trakanon's teeth and went to soloing on the spectral keepers. They were a tough fight. I would use 3 quarters of my mana bar and be down about 2 bubbles of health after healing during the fight.

Even at level 57 the last time I ran through there using my new undead nuke 695, I would use half a mana bar on them. Specs I can drop with 2 nukes and a sound of force (generally).

I have soloed the living stuff, but it's much harder as the damage output from our normal nukes are not efficient as our undead nukes.

So yes I do agree with you that those who say soloing as a cleric is impossible are incorrect. Although at higher level you WILL find that you will not be able to stand toe to toe with things and nuke and melee. The things at higher levels start hitting way too hard to take a beating for more than a couple hits. I have 900+ AC and still get the snot beat out of me in very rapid order.

I have to agree with Dalur, as with most of the other clerics who have learned this the hard way... Yes, with a high AC we can tank. But with a lower number of HP's than melee classes combined with the inability to hit the broad side of a forest giant, we can't stand toe-to-toe with a high-level mob (especially Kunark mobs) for very long.

For example, a wurm will make short work of me, as it hits for 134 consistently. Combine that with double and triple attacks, and the occasional stun, and you can die in a matter of seconds.

Can we solo past level 50? Absolutely. Is it done often? No. Can we solo non-undead mobs? Absolutely. Is it done often? Again, no.

The AC / Wisdom argument has been debated before, and probably will continue to be as long as people play the game. Just play how you wish to play, and don't pander to snooty players telling you how to play.
