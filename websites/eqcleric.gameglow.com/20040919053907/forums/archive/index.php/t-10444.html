I found this as a great spot foe these levels. It is almost always empty and the North side has mummies, ghouls, and DryBone Skels. The zone is close by. AND if you are an evil race (or a worshipper of Bertoxulous http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif) you can sell to the Dark Elf merchants riht there in the zone! Beware! The merchants are part of the Dervish Cutthroat faction. Killing Dervs may make you KOS to them. But healing those that are killing them does not. http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif There are two cutthroat camps in this zone as well. Most times there are 8-14 people in the zone. Great place for Dark Elf clerics! The other great thing is that this borders wiht the Troll and Ogre area. They don't get pure clerics there, so they welcome a healer type in the group.
