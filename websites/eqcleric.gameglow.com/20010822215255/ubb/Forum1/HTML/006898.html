Here's a hint I used when I realized I was level 53 and still missing 45 points of defense. :/

Up until Level 53, there's a gnome merchant out in the West Freeport newbie area - I totally forget the name now, either Happ or Fizz? Anyway, he's blue until 53, and while I don't suggest killing him, he hits pretty weak. What I did was load myself up with full buffs, full AC gear, and full mana, then found a spot on the far southern side of the newbie area, right next to the EC zoneline, and cast a nuke at him. He ran over, I moved around to get me against the wall and him directly in front of me, and just let him go to town. Since you're against the wall, you can usually channel any heals you want, and I was able to stand there for up to 5 minutes at a time with well-timed Complete Heals. I ended up getting a point every 15 minutes or so, but I think having a chanter friend cast Boon on you would help, as the 100 point bonus to Dex should increase the chance of a defense raise.

After 53? Heh, well, remember that you can still get defense raises while DA'ed/DB'ed. I've made a habit of being the 'last one out' on a train, and using DA/DB right at the zoneline, and having the whole train beat on me. Since you can't be stunned under DA anymore, the odds of getting an increase are pretty good if you have 15 mobs beating you senseless. Obviously, don't do this if you're evaccing, as DA will block ports.
