I can CONFIRM that this spell drops as of 3/29/01. I finally got this bugger to drop after camping the Sifaye's in Waking Lands for 4 days straight--approx 26 hrs total camping time. Drops in that time were as follows:

Sifaye Thane's:
(8)copies of the Druid spell Ro's Firey Sundering
(4) copies of the Shaman spell Tumultous Strength
(1) copy of Shadowknight spell Shroud of Hate (vendor spell /boggle)
(1) copy of the elusive TURNING OF THE UNNATURAL

Sifaye Troubadour's:
No spell drops

Sifaye Knight's:
(1) copy of bard song Cantana of Soothing (vender spell /boggle)

I was camping the (5) static sifaye's that spawn along the northern wall of the WL zone (loc approx p2200, n2600) and all mobs can pop in any of the 5 spawn points on a 20min 45 sec spawn cycle.

Good luck and thank u to Carna for tipping me off to this spawn!
