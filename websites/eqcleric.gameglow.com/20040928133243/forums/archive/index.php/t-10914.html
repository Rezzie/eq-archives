1. No, when you go with a giant aligned crew there are no faction issues at all as they are kos to everything too.

2. For the named fights in ToVN/W clerics dont need resist gear except for one where I put on max mr - other guilds may haev different tactics that require different gear though. For clearing trash moderate fr/cr is nice - I usually dont go overboard but settle for 160-170 of each buffed. At level 57 you might want to be in the 180-200 range buffed though.

3. Not really. I personally only use that for Hoshkar and Trakanon - not for Zlexak and never for fighting trash. Others mileage may vary.

4. Just stand up on inc to avoid needless ping pong - no really odd places I have seen.

1) no, going with a giant faction guild this won't be a problem.

2) resist stuff isnt a huge deal, you need fire/cold/maybe magic. The AEs off most of the trash are not too bad, wurms are a pita, but resists never seem to make much difference for me there anyways. Just dont piss off the blue ones, they proc a hp/mana drain dot that is quite nasty.

3) Should not be an issue, even if they use it for Zlex there are other things for you to do during the fight.

4) Dont open any doors you arent told to, other than that, no weird agro that I know of and if there is your pullers should be aware of it and you should be warned before it becomes an issue /shrug

Another note, if you have to camp, do not camp against a wall, you can end up back at zone in.
