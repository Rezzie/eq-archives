I have a growing guild of level 49-60 players, well balanced, most in the 54-55 range. We've gone to PoH probably 20 times now and have put most members in nearly full suits, and have also successfully cleared parts of the second floor. We've done PoF a couple of times and are doing our 3rd on Sunday (all without Draco or CT up) and have made decent progress in outfitting those classes as well. We were able to complete island 3 - azarack isle - in PoS.

Other achievements so far include making epics for a pair of monks and a druid, and are about to complete our first shaman epic as well. We're also close to a couple of ranger and enchanter epics. We've killed VS on our first and only try so far, and easily annihilated Faydaedar while doing the druid epic. We've killed all the fear plane golems twice. We were also able to kill Nortlav the Scalekeeper with about 17 people.

We've done all this in about 2 months since the guild was formed, at which time the best we could do was kill Phinigel - so we've been making good progress so far.

We're about to do our first PoG raid, get our second chance at VS, and soon we will do Severilous as well.

Our strengths:
-3 excellent monks levels 60, 58 and 54, all with epics
-3 level 60 clerics, 1 57 cleric, and 8 clerics level 51-53
-about 12 total level 55+ players including a SK, a ranger, 3 warriors, 3 enchanters, and a shaman
-players are all skilled at their classes and work together well in raid situations, listen to and follow instructions well
-a few senior and experienced people who know how to run raids well and successfully

Our weaknesses:
- lack of very high level warriors (we borrow often)
- generally insufficient resist gear
- serious lack of bards and magicians (again we borrow)
- little raid experience for most of the guild members outside of the past two months


Also as a FYI we are giant-aligned although we do have some people with good dragon and dorf faction.

Okay now that I've given all the background info, my question is: Where do we go from here? Is the progression from here (VS/Sev/PoG/2nd floor Hate) -> (Trak/PoS/PoG bosses/Draco/Innoruuk) -> Western Wastes/VP -> ToV/Cazic/Dain/Velk -> Sleepers Tomb? What other places can I take these folks where they will have fun, a good challenge, and be able to upgrade their gear enough to be ready for the next step? Do I have any steps out of order? Which ones should we do first to be able to progress the fastest and have the most experiences in the time left that we are playing the game?

What faction choice did you choose in Sov?

I'd recomend camping ToV or Keal for quest armors. Its a lot less stressful than PoG in most guilds. PoG has issues with people trying to stay non-kos but still wanting to help the guild and/or get loots.

Western Wastes is also good, but can be very frustrating for any class that has buffs. Almost every dragon in the zone has Vox or Nagafen disspell breaths. Some have Chaosflux so keep DMF up!

Dragon Necropolis is another great place to get some fairly nice items and the breastplate gems you will need if your doing Thurg/Keal/SS armor quests.

Based on that list your should certainly be able to hunt in WW, start exploring DN, and take down Lord Dojo and Chard King right now. The next mobs to try are Dracholiche, Chard Queen and Sev. If you can take them down its time to move on to the following list:

Trak
Talendor
Wuoshi
PoS (Spiroc Isle)
Vaniki
ToV farming

Moving past this list to some of the early "uber" mobs like Klandicar and Vindicator is gonna take some high level Warriors. Getting some and equiping them should be your number 1 priority.

Ok, if i'm getting this straight you have about 20 members 55+ atm. I would recommend getting 10 more 55+ asap. Use invites until you have a solid 45 or so 55+ players that will attend raids regularly. At least at first you will need numbers to make up what you lack in equipment/tactics.

First I would recommend going with +Dragon Faction in SOV, its alot easier than +Giant, and it allows you to take advantage of the new SS quest items in addition to the sick TOV quest items(eventually).

1. Practice Dragon technique on Sev, Trak, Talendor (wont hurt your Claws of Veeshan faction and are relatively easy).
2. Kill in Kael Arena for Thurg quest armor for your lowbies AND to build +CoV faction.
2.5 PoG armor is about the same as Thurg armor but drop rates suck really hard...not really worth it and will just bog you down.
3. Kill in Halls of Testing in TOV for your 55+ ppl to get nice SS quest armor (resists/stats will go up quite abit from this armor).
4. Fight in DN for ridiculous EXP and some decent items (willsapper, PSC etc) and make it your goal to take out Zlandicar eventually. He's got some nice drops and you can get ST keys too.
5. Once your COV faction is high enough, kill Klandicar too for more ST keys.
6. Kill Dojo, Velketor, Vindicator, Dracoliche and forget about VP. A. you need mass Trak killings to even get the keys B. It would take you FOREVER to get to the point where you can kill Phara Dar. (unless you wanna just kill SW and Hosh Xygoz and split)
7. Try to get as many Warrior and Cleric Epics as possible.
8. Once your ranks have swelled abit, and you can consistently take down Vindicator, Velketor, Trak etc. *Add* Dain, CT, and Dozekar to your To Do list and practice each one exclusively till you can keel em reliably.

There you have it. If you use this plan as a rough guide and pursue it aggressively with serious commitment from guild members you might be able to do all this in the next 8mo.
You should have 60+ active members by then if you REALLY want to cook with fire.
80+ if zerg is the name of the game.
