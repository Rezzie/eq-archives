Just to clarify - I do empathise with the situation you find yourself in.

Salina has expressed my opinion on people telling others how to play their classes.

Usually, the higher level cleric in a multi-cleric group is the one who should be stunning and nuking, for one simple reason - A higher level character will have less resists.

It is not right to criticize the choice of buffs used. Normally, I explain to the group if they are getting a lesser buff. (The cost of casting Shield of Words outweighs the benefits - lots of mana for +6 AC? bah!)

It would be better to ask why certain buffs are not used. Inquiring into tactics is always much better than being pushy.

So, to those who criticize fellow clerics, try this - learn if the group has worked together a lot. If they have, their tactics have been practised a lot and perfected. Don't just change their tactics because you want to.

I have learned to change group tactics without being pushy.

In high level parties, asking the question 'Who is the main tank?' can make the party re-organise very quickly. It signals your intention to have a main tank that you heal.

I hope this post has been some help.
