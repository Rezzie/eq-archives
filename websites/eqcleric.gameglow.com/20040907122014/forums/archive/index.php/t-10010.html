Sorry to correct you, Mag, but the reason why RF was moved to Skyfire had nothing to do with the clerics at all. It had to do with the GMs having to reimburse a KSed cleric with their pearl because of the screwy turn-in that happened in Naggy's lair. The GMs complained that they were doing way too much CS handling and too many pearls were getting reimbursed. They moved RF to Skyfire *without* a pearl turn-in so they could wipe the GM's hands clean of the situation.

What it comes down to is now the GM's decision on what to do when a random-spawn mob pops and is KSed or ninjaed. On apathetic servers such as Bertoxx, the GMs let whoever *looted* the heart keep it unless impossible proof is given in spades (multiple screen shots, /reports, /petitioning before and after the kill/engagement, etc.). On other servers, things are handled as the GM sees fit (reimbursing KSes, ninja-loots, etc.). Basically the response will be as random as the spawn-time itself. The rules are there are no set rules, so proceed at your own risk.
