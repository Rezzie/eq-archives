I hope I spelled your name right.

I remember being 19 and posting to this forum about where to hunt. Got several wonderful suggestions of areas- I don't recall them but I am sure the search engine would find it.

The reason I posted a separate message was to empathize- been there too http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif Some ideas, which you probably tried, but let me field them anyways:

1) Team up with a warrior. This will take a while, but start approaching warriors in the zone and ask via tell. I found one at about level 14 that took me to 32, and we were an awesome pair. At 19 we were in oasis and we zoomed along experience-wise.

2) Keep a list of people that you group with. Especially those that you like. On logging in, start searching for them and sending tells. ie- build your own group. Keep a list of their alts as well.

3) Approach existing groups and ask if a level 19 cleric would be of help. If they say no, then stand and watch. Heal as necessary. I have been invited into groups who say "he deserves it, he helped."

I am really a very shy person. These were very hard things for me to do. But they do work.

Best wishes, and good luck
