Saw a similar thread on Graffe's and thought I'd bring the question over here for kicks.

Now, as a Cleric, what fights are your favorites and what are your least favorites?

For me, I'm still fairly new to NToV but I think that Aaryonar is one of my favorite fights in the game. With Roy, there is just so much going on and so much to be aware of. For example, our last fight lasted over 20 minutes and we screwed up VERY badly when we cleared to him and this resulted in about 12 adds of various sorts (Hatchlings and Drakes) over the course of the ifght. In the end, everyone was totally OOM, low on health but we were able to say "Hail, Aaryonar's CORPSE!"

My least Favorite fights are probably most of the VP Dragons since they're just mean. I cast one or two heals, get summoned and die. They're just damage machines to compensate for their lack of hitpoints. I don't see a lot of skill nor strategy, it's just a series of burn fights.

My other least favorite fight is Lady Nev. I just HATE her. Lots of hitpoints, an AOE from hell, lack of a GOOD wall to hide behind, tashing and slowing the MA etc. My Guild has yet to kill her cleanly. Yesterday was our best effort in that we got her on the first try but still lost a lot of people and had a bunch of problems during the course of the fight.
