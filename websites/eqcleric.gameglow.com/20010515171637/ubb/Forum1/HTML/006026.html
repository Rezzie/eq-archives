Last night I was playing in a pickup group in Overthere. When I saw someone in /ooc advertise "selling celestial healing". I quickly asked him how much, Rutgar = Poor Cleric. I then, since I really didn’t know what they went for asked him "Im not sure what they are worth, but I am interested". A little time goes by and I thought I had lost out to another cleric. Then a few moments go by and then I get the tell " I am asking 500pp",. DOH... I look at my inventory and I have 73pp 135gp and misc copper, silver. I look at all the things I have that I would be willing to trade that is worth more than 500pp.

I then did a /w on the seller to see if he would want anything I have, seller <- 52lvl,. I offer him my +7 wis +7 str mask and he says he does not need that. I then tell him I might have enough in the bank. A few moments go by and I get the tell "ok ill sell it for 300pp" DOH... I quickly tell him that I will buy but need to run to the bank. I then find out that he is waiting for the boat and will leave when it arrives (I hate waiting for boats myself so I have no complaints). I quickly gate back to my bind spot run across LOIO and get to the Bank in FV,. look at my account (praying) and see 432pp woohoo i grab 300pp and start running back. When I reach the halfway point back to Overthere I get a tell from the seller saying "boat is almost here" noooo,.. I tell him im almost their and ask him what his final destination is. I then get a tell "I have an alt char here that I can transfer the item to" whew.. ok I tell him "thank you I appreciate any help". I keep running and finally get the SF zone in Overthere and after he gets off the boat he brings his alt char on and we make the transaction. So,. to make this long story short I bought Celestial Healing for 300pp at level 37 and im a very happy Cleric.

Veraphie

"can someone tell me the ratios on celestial healing vs divine light?
because i was told they are basically the same ..."

DL: 888-910 healing, 350 mana. 4.5 cast time
CH: 744-748 healing (over 24 seconds), 225 mana. 4.0 cast time.

(info from eqcasterrealm)

DL: 2.53-2.6 hps/mana
CH: 3.3-3.32 hps/mana

So yes, CH is technically more efficient by a noticable amount (in my opinion).

But it is still a HoT spell. So there are circumstances where DL would be a better option (or Remedy for that matter)

"didn't get my cel heal till this lvl, lvl 58, doesn't look like i'll be using it, almost sort of sorry i scribed it (got it from guild bank) because i saw an offer, paw of oppolla for cel heal O.o"

At 58th, one level from CE, you probably wont get much use out of it. Probably regulate it to after-battle heal for casters and light melee inorder to top them off.

Still though, it does have uses. I like to use in in conjuction with DL on light melees during large mob encounters like Kael giants. As soon as they take a bub of damage ill toss a Cel H on them. If they get super agroed, im ready with DL or Remedy depending on the sevearity of the damage being taken. It gives me a nice cushion. Plus, if agro gets directed back to a main tank, the Cel Heal will heal them up full or close to it.

Also Cel Healing is very low agro which is a good thing.

But all thats aside, you will probably toss it after you reach 59th and get CE.

quote:
Even more amazing - one of the higher-level clerics on our server a few days prior was offering to trade GEB's *and* a Ceno belt for Celestial. /boggle Why don't people just go to Icewell or PoG and get their own copy?

Several reasons...

1. The upsurge in cleric twinks. Cleric stuff is still a hot commodity (quest bracers still run up to 1k on CT) and nobody really wants to go sit through a boring camp to get themselves a copy when they can just unload that item that's been sitting in the bank for forever. As proof, head to EC and /auc Turning of the Unnatural and see what rolls in

2. Not everyone bough Velious. Kunark was a "must have" because if you wanted to go past 50 or hunt past 40 you didn't have an options really. But Velious didn't offer much to the pre-50s crowd so some just never bothered to buy it.

3. Nerfed drop rates. Just like Kunark, the best spells are the rarest and it's sometimes near impossible to find one of these spells. Verant told druids that Ro's Fiery Sundering (AC/FR debuff) was made slightly more common but that it was intentionally still rare.

Word to the wise... when SoL comes out, find those broken drops ASAP and farm them before they're nerfed
