Heh on my server Seb is also BYOG. I can't imagine what OS must be like with lguk-style mix-n-match groups. Though I guess if it's camped to the brim it isn't too tough to run down to a camp when there are no mobs there.

Chardok is probably at its best at 55. It really sucks at lower 50s because the mobs are too tough and you can't kill them fast enough. It's not too great at 58+ because you can do other things that are much better xp. But it's solid and rarely crowded.

If you have a really good group you can go deep in the Hole and try that, there are some excellent xp camps there and then ZEM is the best in the game.

Velk's Lab, kobold village or the pit with a very strong (avg. 57+) group will be terrific xp but it's not for the weak of heart (or for weak groups).

If your group is uber you can do DN, very interesting and fun zone, but you and everyone else had better play your best. Good place to try out Upheaval too =)

HS, south wing, is of course the best of all the options but only one group can do that place at a time.

If you don't mind the old world, the EL camp is good experience at your level and very easy, plus the GEB drops are nice loot to have. King area in Lguk should be all or mostly blue to you as well, was farming crowns with a 54 necro there and he got good xp in a group of 3 (60 cleric, 57 sk, 54 nec) with pretty much zero risk (I was meleeing).

One nice little secret xp spot that I'll share with ya that you'll never ever find camped - the bear pits in Permafrost keep. Clear out the giants for the xp then drop down into the bear pits. Bears, giant spiders, 'giant' wolves, all have zero MR and all will be low-mid blues to you with a nice spawn rate and plenty extra to fetch if you can handle it. Enchanter got literally zero mez resists, without tash. It's fun and different too, and good xp, though the loot is nothing great. And if your group is all 53+ you have an easy exit walking into Vox's lair and getting banished out of zone =)

BYOG is definitely the order of the day though. On Torv the highest level pickup dungeon is KC and sensible players avoid it like the plague. It's easy to create a group if you can find a good enchanter or bard, just search the lfg lists for players of appropriate class and level if you can't find enough in your guild and on your friends list.

Hand room in Karnor's is still pretty good experience at level 55, and a so-so chance at a pickup group there. Also, the Cappy room in KC will have a high percentage of blues at 55, and the xp pace is quite good in both locations with a middle-50s group.

Volume is everything. You will need like 1200 blues to get out of 55, so a fast pulling place like Cappy is what you need. (Howling Stones can be good experience and constant pulls, but the monsters are much more dangerous there, and you will probably blow a lot of cash symboling the party. Wipeouts can happen in HS, and there's simply no zone line. Cappy can be done easily without Marzin, and the zone is pretty close by in the unlikely event that things get out of hand.)

You can use Exile Undead to knock down blues in Burning Woods on your own -- tattering apes and the skeletons that wander near the Skyfire zone -- but it will take most of your mana and there will be lots of downtime, unless you can bum clarity off of a nearby party. I tried it and wrote it off as boring and slow. I did notice that each blue gave me perhaps a half-percent xp gain, which is wildly out of control for 55. If you can get a couple of wizard friends and an enchanter to powerlevel you for a few days with extra nuking/snare/clarity, you can atone the mobs to take the kills and get through 55 more efficiently.
