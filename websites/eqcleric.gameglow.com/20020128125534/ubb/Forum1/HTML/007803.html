I ground through 59 in Velketor's Labyrinth, primarily up on the Ledge. Volume is the key for clerics, since we aren't quad kiters, and the ledge can provide 40 kills an hour.

Advantages to a cleric of VL:

No dispelling mobs, virtually no trains on the ledge, easy fast kills, enchanter a bonus but not required, close to a town, one heroic bond and buffing is done, etc. A good number of quest gems will come your way as well.

It took me just over 2,000 mobs to get through 59, so I suggest you go where it's easy. VL might get pretty boring after several weeks of grinding away, but you can always vary your hunting spot occasionally to keep from going insane.
