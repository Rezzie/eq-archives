For the last 7 levels I have been buffing myself first to put the buffs on a timer.

Everyone gets valor, although sometimes it doesnt stick to them.

Tanks get Armor.

Enchanters and the main tank get Pinzarn.

And I get everything for the timer. But do I really need the AC or extra Hp's? Maybe later I will, but for right now I rarely get hit. 3 AoF's or Pinzarns take a lot of mana to refresh.
So the question is do I buff myself to the max for the over conservative protection or does the timer effect make it all worth it?

There is also the whole debate about who gets Naltron. Im thinking the tank I complete heal and the enchanter for a safety buffer, and maybe me. Nobody else really needs the extra Hp's.. they have enough of a safety buffer already or they should know how to control their aggro(ex. Wizards), I am not even sure if the cleric warrants a full Naltron. =)

Well, I usaully tell my group up front what my buff policy is. Primary Tanks (warriors and Monks, Rangers if they are pulling) get SoWords, Resolution and Naltron. Chanters get the same. Everyone else gets Reso, SoWords as I get the mana, and Pinzarn IF they ask for it. I give myself Reso, AoProtection and SoWords. The extra AC is worth it. Sometimes I pinzarn myself if I am getting hit alot.

Since I get alot of people who have Hero and such, which I can't cast over, and groups in KC are very fluid (people leave and new ones come in alot), I don't even try to time it. I tell the group THEY have to tell ME when it is flashing or gone.

Of course, I have a hot key that says "/g FM, Check your Buffs". That is all the reminder I can give them, and that only happens when I got full mana.

In a standard dungeon-chewing group...

HB goes off on all, naturally

AoP goes on myself, as well as Symbol of Marzin

Chanter gets all

Warriors, Paladins, SKs get SoWords, as they normally have the HP to soak hits. Marzin as well if I find myself using CH

Monks, Rangers, Shamen, and Rogues get SoWords and Marzin if they are tanking or pulling. Otherwise they normally just get SoWords

Casters get Marzin, if they ask for it, or are the taunting type. Normally just put it on them on breakin, once we get to camp, dont refresh.

How much I buff afterwords depends on how tough the dungeon is.. If I find were getting spanked, will refresh buffs if nessasary, but being the lazy cleric I am... Normally just keep HB up along with Marzin on the chanter and puller.

Try staggering your buffs a bit.

When I get into a group, I start off by casting Group Heroism - or you can start by casting a round of whatever you have that is AC&HP combined.

Sit and med back that mana. Then cast your AC buff on yourself (timer reasons). Sit and med back most of that mana, then buff the tank(s). Then sit and med the mana back. Buff the enchanter and med back the mana.

Then you can do HP buffs as you see fit.

Once you get to 58 and get group naltron, it doesn't become an issue any more. 1 cast and everybody get symbol.
