I am putting this in the rant section not because I want to write a rant but I'm pretty sure that is what is will end up being.

I have read ad nosium the druid cleric posts and I have come to one conclusion. Greed is destroying a fairly well designed game (considering the amont of variables in it).

Clerics are not needed in a 55+ EXP group, Yup(generally), but will your night end in disaster if you have a Cleric in your group, NO. Will you end your exp grind 1/6 of a blue exp bulb behind where you might have ended if you had some other chr, maybe.-No need to split hairs on this point btw.

5 Druids are not necessary at a raid, Yup(generally), but will your raid fall in to total disarray if 5 more druids showed up, NO. What will happen is a miniscule amount more lag, and you will have to split the loot with 5 more people. OMG your chance at that Black Diamond just dropped from 1-25 to 1-30.

If your in a guild that is not inviting any more druids, ask your self why. If your 5 person exp group is ignoring the LFG cleric ask your self why. If it is just greed ask your self if the opportunity to meet someone new, or make a friend, exceeds the value of plat and EXP gained.

30 seconds of thought and consideration and most of these posts will go away without a single change to the game.

Everquest is a journey disguised as a game. If you choose to make it a race feel free, but you do not realy win anything at the end.
