Go to crushbone - kill or help kill lots and lots and lot and even more orcs.

Go to Freeport - west I belive - in the hog caller inn, somewhere up the stairs is a little dwarf named pandos somethingorother.

Con him - if he is not KOS, then you can hand him 4 muffins at a time and that will give you the faction you need.

If he is KOS, then you need to kill more orcs.

This should repair your faction (from what I've heard - haven't verified it myself) with Kelethin/Felwithe and most of POG - but not the POGs themselves.


BTW you can buy muffins in WFP just on the other side of the hog caller inn.
