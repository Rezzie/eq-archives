Don't know if I'm the only one who didn't know about this place or not but in Timorous deep there is a colony of birdies called Spirocs which gave real good exp for me at 45.

I went there with a group of 52 war, 45 monk, 52 wiz, 40 sha and me 45 clr. We could kill these things real quick so I'm sure you could do it with less people. They hit pretty hard so a decent tank is a good idea to bring.. Also a monk for single pulls is a good idea..

Anyways, great exp and a major SK aviak flashback awaits you in TD http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif ..
