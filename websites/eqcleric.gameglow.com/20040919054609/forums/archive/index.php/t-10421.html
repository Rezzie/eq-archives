Skeletons around lev 8-10 have roughly 4-5 static spawns around the giant gears near the mino caves. Each spawn is far away from each other and thus can be solo'ed without too much difficulty. It's also a nice change from the evercamped chessboard.

And the cool part is that the mino lord never runs that direction, so you're relatively safe while you med and such.
