You also have to look at it like this. Many pieces for the warrior armor are random/rare drops from common mobs in the zone. Same thing for many of the pieces for SK armor, bard armor, ranger armor, shaman armor, etc. Then take a look at the cleric armor quests. They are either A) items which spawn on the ground and can be picked up by anyone or B) camp a rare named mob for the piece. From what I can remember, the only piece that doesn't fit this profile is a part for the arms, in which you give tumpy tonics to an npc and he hands you the item.
Cleric armor quests require you to camp a specific mob - not a specific type of mob ... i.e. cleric armor quests have mobs of which there are only ever one of them running around at once like Dwigus or Regis not like the griffenes or aviaks. So, non-clerics camping these mobs are either doing it for a friend or doing it to make a large profit.
