You were sitting out fights at level 5? Why?

Mana regenerates at a constant rate, sitting or standing, unless you are meditating--which you can't until level 8. Sitting out of fights will not get you mana faster; you might as well be in there mixing it up with the tanks.

After all, at level 5 we're practically as effective damage-dealers as most tanks.
