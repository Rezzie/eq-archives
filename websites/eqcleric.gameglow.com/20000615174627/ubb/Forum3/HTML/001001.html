Ok there are 2 parts to the quest

1: Items need for quest
2: Faction

1. You need a no drop heart and no drop scythe off lord grimrot who resides at the newbie undead camp near to the stone bridge in south karana. There are two versions of grimrot and two corresponding quest items. The no drop scythe is off the live version and the no drop heart is off the dead version. The approximate lvl of the dead version is 25 and the approximate lvl of the live version is 36.

Thee is one putrid skeleton that stands (as opposed to siting like the rest) I believe this is the placeholder (although in fits of pure boredom you might just clear the entire lot). It has been theorized that asking for the quest in qeynos triggers the spawn but noone has been able to verify this.

The live version is extremely resistant to magic so best idea is to have a pet tank about to kill him. If ya lvl 50 and want to try ya luck go ahead but a lvl 35-40 tank or 2 will make short work of grimrot (with a bit of healing).

(NOTE: Look at faction section)

Ok take said no drop scythe and no drop heart to camlend serbold in qeynos flying saucer (sorry temple of life). Give him the scythe if he says something strange but not directly bad (took me a while to figure out if he was giving me the finger or not) then give him the heart and woop with joy when you get shield.

2. Faction

Ok there are a number of ways of increasing faction with the temple of life assuming you are not kos (if you are then ya in trouble).

Bonechips to lashun novashine - waste of time
Money to lashun novashine - waste of time
diseased bear and wolf pelts to priestess caulria - not bad but very difficult to get lots
bandit sashes to Chesgard Sydwen - very good faction not bad loot either.
Koalindl fish to enic ruklin - absolutely amazing faction not bad exp ok coin.

Trick to koalindl fishes is to get a lvl 1 to kill them and then let you loot their corpses. Approximately 50% have a corpse you can hand in.

Wolf Form - used for finishing quest. Not required for all but it doesn't hurt. Unless you have a gm who can give you back your scythe get it before handing anything in.

Remember the key words are "I defend life" to camlend serbold. I believe I did approximately 13 fishes after getting the quest and I managed to get it (being a dwarf).

Hope this helps
