You need to attach yourself to people or a guild. There seem to be lots of lower level guilds out there.

The reason for this is that killing ANYWHERE is going to be boring unless it is just a diversion whilst you are with people.

Some places are more fun than others but in the end it is the people that make the hunting fun.

I emphasize this because the role of a cleric is often not perceived to be as exciting as some others. But if you perservere through the sitting, dependence on others and sense of guilt when fellow group members die, you will become one of the most powerful and sought after characters in the game.

Clerics are literally AWESOME ... but the trade-off for that is a fairly long apprenticeship. So find friends to assist you through the levels. They will keep you eager to log on and get another few yellows.
