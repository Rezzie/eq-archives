Sigh.

Reactivated my account after approx. 13 months of not playing, quit just as RoK came out. Log on, all my friends are 60, some are in guilds I could never picture them in, all of them are in places ive never heard of. Well... me being my usual optimistic self, I decide that i am 48 and I can get back into the swing of things easily. Boy I was wrong. Clericing, i found out the hard way, is a skill you must constantly keep up on or you lose it. Before I quit I hadn't lost a group member in 2 months. I had a good reputation. I lost a guy after being back for 2 days!!! and in retrospect i see it was my mistake. Decided to keep trying... I have really been struggling. This is all very disheartening. I really hope that by starting over with a new cleric that i can pick up on all the old things ive lost and all the new stuff I wasn't around to see. At this rate, im just going to make my existing cleric a pariah.

Being a cleric is not like riding a bike - as in once you learn it you can just pick it up and go again, at least not for me. Ive played 3 classes seriously - paladin, necro, and cleric - and I know that if I were to start over that being a cleric is the only way to go for me. So here I go again :P from level 1.

P.S. would it be unethical to res my new char with my old char??? (jk ^_^)

C'est la vie
