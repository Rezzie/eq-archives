Now, my one wish...

After discussions a while back I have revamped my suggestion for a mana boost (I think someone else suggested this as well)...

Divine Concentration. 20 second invincibility spell with DB-like mana regen rate (i.e. you regain ~500 mana in that time). Level 50+ spell.

Nothing new here except the mana regen. Remember, invincibility cuts both ways. Nothing can hurt you but nothing can help you AND you can't help anyone else. And with a recycle of, say, 15-20 min PLUS the fact you have to eat up a spell slot PLUS the fact that you'd have less invincibility afterwards... well, there's you're counterbalancing agents. And, of course, if you click it off you lose the mana regen.

Yes, it would probably be used a lot but I don't know a single wizard with Harvest that doesn't use it every chance they get.

Bottom line... we get a mana boost (don't really need regen. OOM is OOM and heals can't wait) This makes us choose when to use this spell and provides no unbalancing.
