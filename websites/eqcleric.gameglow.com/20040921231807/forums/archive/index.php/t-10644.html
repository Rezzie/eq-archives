I think you're out of luck. Theres a few nice mid 30s spots to solo in Kunark (Frontier Mountains, Overthere, Kaesora if you're ballsy).

Non-Kunark, things are a lot thinner on the ground. Low Guk near the dead zone is great, but can get crowded with people, plus spawns are slow.

You could try for soloing the tree spectre in Ferrott, but I'm finding even at 39 its a bit tricky - at 35 I couldn't do it, kept on going oom.

I can't think of anywhere else soloable. Griffons/Giants have too many hit points, Mistmoore is train ridden, and impossible to get in without invis (or wading through oceans of aggro greenies).
Maybe CT, I don't think green lizzies are aggro. A runner would spell disaster tho...

Really, if you are mid 30s, you either group up (where at 35 with Sup Heal you will be REAL popular), or get to Kunark...
