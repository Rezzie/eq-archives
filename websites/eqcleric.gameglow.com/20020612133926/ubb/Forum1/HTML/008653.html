The Ragebringer is considered so easy because of the shortcut.

The original intent of the epic was to acquire 4 rogue only weapons to hand in and get the Jagged Diamond Dagger. Now any rogue can tell you that the JDD drops every time from Renux in Steamfont. But if the JDD didn't drop every time (hence the workaround), then every rogue would be required to do or acquire:

Burning Rapier (long quest)
Eyerazzia (PoF)
Martune Rapier (PoH)
*something else*

just to get the JDD. Its the same situation with having an epic level 1 shaman who only did faction work. Verant just hasn't seen fit to change either "loophole".

The RB is an amazing weapon. Yes there are weapons in the high end game that can and will do more damage than the RB. But there are very few replacements for "Seething Fury".. the 41% haste + atk +ac effect on the RB. Unless your among the truely elite rogues in the uber guilds your not bagging your RB.... you may be putting it in your offhand more tho. It's really hard to give up the RB's effect.

While the rogue epic in it's current state may not be as difficult as other epics, it does give the rogue the opportunity to actually use "roguely" skills.... something other epics seem to fail to grasp. Think about it..

You have to pickpocket 2 Grandmaster NPC rogues. Any rogue worth their "mettle" (my opinion) will coerce a crazy wizard to port them up to the Plane of Hate alone and use their roguely skills to hide and sneak up to the second floor of Maestro's house to steal the book of souls. Renux is easy to do with an enchanter or druid + the rogue. The general is *slightly* more challenging now that they nerfed the "secret handshake" but a balanced group of 60's with NToV gear could probably still take him if he isn't dual wielding.

I got lucky when i killed my general.. he dropped the Cazic Quill for me. Another rogue in my guild wasn't so lucky and it took us 3 more clearings of the DE cabin in Kithacor to get him a Kedge robe. Yeah, if everything is up and planned out a rogue can get his epic in a single day. And I do think that all epics should be on comparable timers as for difficulty and time required. That won't happen until Verant see's more competition on the horizon and decides to do what should have been done originally with epics and their Vision(tm) of bottlenecks... and remove them.
