*thinks this sounds like the perfect opportunity for an amusing story*

In Hate the other week we'd cleared the entire zone, all except for one golem. Since the golems have insane magic resist the whole zone decides LETS ALL MELEE!!

Well my group was NOT the tank group by far. (We had a warrior, bard, 2 clerics, mage and enchanter.) Since we'd managed to get a decent number of the kills throughout the evening, when it was announced the last mob was a golem our opinion was "fun while it lasted".

So anyway, the golem is pulled and all 30 of us wade in and start wakking. I was actually amazed that I was fairly consistently hitting for upper 20s-low 30s.

You tell your party, 'Hey! I'm hitting for 32!'
WarriorX tells the group, 'Oooooh. You hit for 32 Aal! We're sure to get the xp now!'
You crush a spite golem for 32 points of damage!
You have slain a spite golem!
You gain party experience!
WarriorX tells the group, 'DOH!'

I think I literally fell off my chair in tears I was laughing so damn hard. I happened to have been on the phone with a friend at the time and it took a good 5 minutes before I could even tell him what was happening.
