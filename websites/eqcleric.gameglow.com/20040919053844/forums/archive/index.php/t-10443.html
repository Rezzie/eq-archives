I recently discovered a dervish cutthroat camp in North Ro that's right by the border to Oasis. And there are only 3 cutthroats there. If you hit the one on the left or the one in the middle, both of them will pull, however, the one on the right will not.
I haven't experimented with them too much yet, but I can consistently take one out, and almost the other. And if you can't take it, the zone's only a few steps away. Also the loot is great. Bronze weapons. I would imagine you'd do great if you had a better weapon and you are more geared towards meleeing. However, the higher teens will probably get less experience, if any. More for 14-16 I would imagine.

Also, there is usually an abundandt supply of blue shriveled mummies here. They're pretty good xp, with little hassle.
