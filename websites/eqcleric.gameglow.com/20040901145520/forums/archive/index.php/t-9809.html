Well it’s been a slow hard week and still, the urge to give in and come back to EQ is so very strong.

Thursday Night
Say goodbye, quit the guild, chat to my friend on the phone for 2 hours psyching myself up to be strong, have a restless night unable to sleep thinking “what have done”.

Friday
Have a lousy day at work, keep thinking of EQ, go out Friday night on a river cruise, dance, party on, meet some really nice people and have a lot of fun, “what was EQ again?”, sleep like a baby.

Saturday
The crunch hits, I want to log on, I stay busy in the house, play Star Trek - Birth of the Federation, go to a friends House warming party in the evening. Phew, made it through the day.

Sunday
Even worse than Saturday, have to get away from the PC EQ is calling to me, I keep dialling into the net “to read my mail of course”, Call a friend and go out for a 2 hour lunch, watch “The Talented Mr Ripley”. Gat back home about 7pm, win my game of BOF about 10pm. Find I can’t sleep.

Monday
Really busy day at work, put a flaming post up on the guild message board in response to something an EQ friend put up (yes I am really hurting by this time). Finish a 2nd game of BOF (so over that game now). Once again, bad nights sleep.

Tuesday
Read the Guild message board, get into a huge argument with two rather obnoxious guild members who have responded to my Monday post, manage to annoy a lot of people in the guild and burn many bridges. Take my flatmate out to Dinner, play Mechwarrior III. Sleep surprisingly well.

Wednesday
Get stuck in to work, ignore EQ, that night log on tell my friends (the ones that would still talk to me) that I’m selling my account. Some are really sorry to see me go some (after the message board affair) are happy to see me go. RL Friends come over, I play Magic the Gathering all night. Find once more that I can’t sleep.

Thursday
Start getting into my work, take a pause to get myself together, decide to post this rant to get my head around things.

-----------------------------------------------

What can I say, EQ had become such a large part of my life, about 170 hours a month, 35 days on Dom and a score of other secondary classes tried and played with. To go cold turkey is really hard, I miss my VR friends, I miss the game. When I first started playing EQ I was lucky to play 2 nights a week, slowly other things in my life slipped to maximise my EQ time to 6 nights a week and almost all the weekend.

First to go was Gym,
Then weekend activities like hitting the Beach or Bushwalking
Video Rentals and the movies
Partys and nightlife
The woman I was dating
Food shopping, replaced with take away food
House work, mowing the lawn etc
My friends stated to slip
I started missing sleep and getting sick more
My work started to slip

I finally decided to pull the plug, but it’s hard to give up something that has become your passion. But when your passion threatens to consume your life, it’s time to slap yourself around the head and get a grip on reality.

Two of my friends are heavily into EQ, I pulled them in with me, I only hope they don’t also slip over the line like I did, letting a game become an obsession.

It will take time to get my old life back, find the time to pull my friends back in and get out there living an active life again, until that happens the lure of EQ is just so strong.

I always thought that if I could get away from the game for a month or so, I could go back to playing a couple of nights a week again. However, I feel now, like any addiction, It would be much better for me, that I stay away once I get over it.

Anyhow, that’s my rant.
