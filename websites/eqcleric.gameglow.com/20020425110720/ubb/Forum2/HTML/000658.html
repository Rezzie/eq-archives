Just received my 'awe empowering' Unswerving Hammer of Faith spell and have been testing it out. Reading some of the related posts on the site, I see it might have some use in groups but haven't seen anything solo related. I was over soloing in Burning Woods when I got the spell, so I thought I'd play with it a few times with some light blues (plaguebones mostly).

The first time, I rooted the mob, backed off and cast...the hammer appeared, and disappeared, oops, very small range.

Second time, closer this time, cast, hammer appears, plaguebone swipes a few times, hammer gone (no time to hero the hammer).

Third time, this time I let the mob hit me while I cast to get aggro. Once the hammer appeared, I hero, and back off. I heal a few times but the hammer's hitpoints aren't updated very well and it dies.

Forth time, I try the same as before but try fear kiting, which works even less well.

So, ultimately, my first 4 tries end up wasting far more mana than I would have if I just used direct damage spells, and even this will light blue mobs let alone harder hitting deeper blues.

Just curious if anyone has had any success in using the hammer spell while soloing or if the extremly limited uses are narrowed to only while in groups.
