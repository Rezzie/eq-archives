Let me strongly urge you head go to Overthere. The loot is pretty poor. But by Brell is the experience insane. All across the zone, people are shouting, "Is anyone else's experience bar moving faster than a green mob in Guk?"

In a three-man group, consisting of a Warrior, a Rogue, & myself, I was seriously getting 1 non-hell bubble an hour. We were just doing single pulls to the Skyfire zone. The warrior was just shooting arrows, bring them in. 20 seconds later he was off again. I felt like I was being powerleveled.

Rogues, btw, are my new favorite class. They do insane backstab damage & with the Warrior taunting & themselves evading, I only had to throw about 2 greater heals an hour on them for those few times when evade didn't work. (Even then, I really didn't have to heal them, just felt like toping them back up to full health after the fight). It was nice only having to worry about the Warrior's health.

Just thought I would let you in to what I consider my new favorite zone. Good times.
