I have 64 skill training points saved up at 45. I will, when I get the excess cash, buy up my 1H blunt to 175, which is max (I need another 13 points there). Otherwise, I can't use them, because everything else is either maxed at 200 (swimming, sense heading, alcohol tolerance) or above 200 (alt, div, con, evo, abj). (Unfortunately, you can't spend the points on a skill that is at or beyond 200. 200 is the highest value your class trainer can train you to. Skills will only advance with use beyond that point, and advancement gets slower and s l o w e r.)

So unless I want to toss them away on a language, or become a master of fishing, or take up a trade skill (no interest in that), I will just let the points gather dust.
