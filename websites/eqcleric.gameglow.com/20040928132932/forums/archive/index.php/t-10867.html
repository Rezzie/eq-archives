If you had force sufficient to kill Zlandicar, naturally Vaniki is not going to be a problem. But with lesser force he can be a challenge.

From what I have seen, that 500 DD you're talking about is actually a lifetap. As with any high level mob, landing a slow is the difference between triviality and wipeout. Vaniki is a straight up fight. Only tricky part is he has alot of hps, seems to regen pretty quickly, flurries alot, and has a 500pt proc.

Get a high level warrior with defensive to tank him. Get all your clerics to focus on healing your main tank, the rest of your tanks go in once your main tank has sufficient aggro. Land tash, malo, turgurs and finally cripple on him. Cripple is big because Vaniki flurries alot, casting Cripple seems to keep the flurries down. Until you get slow to land, its good practice for multi cleric+single tank healing.
