ANY ideas would be extremely helpful. Can you imagine having to do your entire epic twice?! We are ready to start over, but this is my last ditch effort to see if anyone else has encountered this and found a way around it.

I messed up my epic on my first attempt after killing the plasmatic priest. So I needed to start all the way over. I'm currently one of 25+ clerics on emarr with a pearl, awaiting my shot at Zordak.

Problem with the Natasha spawns is that she is spawned usually after, you turn in an item to the first NPC. Then she will spawn, and you turn in the 2nd item to her. Depending on the orbs your friend has, there is a chance that some steps may be able to be side stepped, but it's unlikely. Due to the way each step is interwoven.

I'd guess you should probably go thru each step and determine based upon the globes you have and what he's missing to spawn Zordak again.
