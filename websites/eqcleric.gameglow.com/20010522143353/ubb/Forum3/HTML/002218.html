Well, if you had trouble with the Blood Wolf, then you will definitely need help with the final part of the quest.

First, have your group waiting at the giant fort in GD. Make sure an inviser goes with you down to the broodmother.

Head into the wurm caves and turn in the control harness to the broodmother. Make sure you get an invis and follow her out. She will leave the cave and head up to the giant fort. When the broodmother gets there, 2 giants will spawn outside the fort. One of them immediately starts to run away (towards EW zoneline), he is the one that has your leggings. Make sure you attack him IMMEDIATELY or he will get away and depop.

The broodmother will attack and kill the first giant, and then will become the pet of the 2nd one. So you have a few options:

1) Bring enough people to kill the 2nd giant before the broodmom can finish the first.

2) Get a bunch of people that can kite and just spend 20min-1 hour kiting the 2nd giant to death. I did this at 53 (druid) and it took me maybe 20-25 minutes solo.

Kill the 2nd giant (I think his name is Fergul) and loot your new leggings.

Notes:

I estimate both giants to be about lvl 50, the one you need to kill does not summon.

When the broodmother is a pet, she will only attack if you are within melee range. Once you step out of melee range, the broodmother will just ignore you and do the pet walk. The aggro is really odd.

Hope that helps. =)

I have done this quest in full once with my druid, and helped 4 other people with the final step.

1) Fergul is always up. Well, unless he was just killed through the quest.
2) Once the broodmom is handed the harness of control, the first fergul (green to me at 56) will despawn and the correct fergul will respawn (Blue at 56)
3) There is really no need to follow the broodmom out. She is pretty hardy- nothing will kill her. Bind at the EW zone and gate back- save yourself a ton of trouble.
4) Have your group waiting on the wall between the fort and EW zone- pull Fergul as he makes his dash for the EW zone. He has about 7000 hp.
5)Once the broodmom kills Graekl or whatever his name is- there is chance she will become Fergul's pet. Just stay out of melee range and it isnt any big deal. Sometimes she despawns after Fergul dies. Sometimes she wanders aimlessly around.
