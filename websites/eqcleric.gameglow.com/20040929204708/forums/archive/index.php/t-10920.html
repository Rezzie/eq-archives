Which spells: Well it depends on the dragon. If the dragon does an AoE damage, and depending on the amount of damage the AoE does or the effects, I will have either word of restoration (lvl 57 group heal, very useful for Trak or Hoskar and probably others) or word of redemption (lvl 60 group complete heal). In addition to that I will have Divine light, Celestial Elixir and regular single target complete heal. If the dragon doesn't do any damage AoE I don't have the group heals up. If the dragon does a stun (ala Phara Dar, Wooshi, Sev), I will load up remedy for really fast casting heals.

Where do I stand: Where I have to cast group heals, I stand with the melee to make sure we all get healed. If I don't need to group heal, I stand behind the melee farther from the dragon, and when appropriate I will sometimes be around corners and such to the dragons AoE effects.
