allakazam's is usually very good for quest info.. althoug sometimes they dont' have the specifics such as spawn timers and such

everquest.allakhazam.com I think is the address.. you should know it by now.

basically you have to camp gynok in befallen (spawns right at the zone but his PH will wander down to 3rd lvl if not killed so you might have to wipe out all 3rd lvl to spawn him) he's exceedingly rare and the only way you can get the plat ring that I know of.

You also have to camp the mutant fungus in Uguk to get various items that you turn in in kerra ridge to get the other 3 rings. I know you need degenerated guk weed to get the gold (the rarest of the 3)

You can also get the silver ring from the bottom of the razor gill pool in black burrow.. it spawn on teh ground.

Have fun, I know the mitty camp sucked like mad. Camped her for 9 hours straight on several occaisions and never even got the most common ring (bronze or copper), and she had a 18 minute spawn time with no PH, I'm sure it's just as bad now.
