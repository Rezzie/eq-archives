I duel a lot, and have yet to beat Warrior.

2 major problems with Warriors:

1) They have too many hp, very hard to really nuke them down.

2) They have high MR naturally, plus any smart person dueling a cleric will have high MR.

The second problem is less of a problem than the first =)

First off, The best place to duel is Arena. I like it because it's safe to die there, and it's pretty decent size for running around in.

Anyhow, with a warrior, the first problem to get through is the MR. Get a Journeymans Walking Stick and go to work, dancing around to try and avoid their melee, backing off to heal if need be. It procs a weak tash, but it's better than nothing (roughly -20 to MR).

Watch their weapons, often they'll have an array of proccing weapons with some nasty procs. Keep your resists high to try and resist them, the snare whip is a favorite.

Dispell them if they use sow potions or long casting item buffs. Have jboots to at least match their speed if they have them. They may try and pumice stone you to remove your buffs.

Next, you just gotta try and wear down the HP with nukes :\ This is hard and will require medding as you won't have enough mana to kill them with 1 bar. Of course, while you med they will be bandaging, or possibly even using items, etc to heal.

Warriors are generally a no win fight, unless the warrior you are dueling is a complete idiot.

Clerics are very good duelers against casters, and the lower hp melee. Only casters I have problems with are enchanters, and only because of those damn mana seive spells.

Edit : Read some of the other posts after I posted, and had some comments. It is generally a bad idea to try and use roots, stuns, etc in duels. They are easily resisted with even low MR. Our nukes, however, are rarely outright resisted, even with high mr, you will just get a partial resist. I was landing full on a level 60 warrior with 80 MR(after tash =b). Also be aware your nuke will NOT do full damage anyway in PVP (unless they are feared =D).
