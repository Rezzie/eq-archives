Bah Gair, don't give away the secret .

That spot is the place for plate. There is no better spot in Kael. The dorf only has spawned at the end for us. Say the phrase, 2 giants spawn. One of those 2 giants trigger the 3rd giant so kill the one that doesn't trigger the 3rd first. From there, it is like reavers until the lvl 60 giant. Then kill the dorf as indicated above. If he gets away, you can't retrigger it for a while. We waited over an hour the time we let him get away and we couldn't retrigger it.

As long as you kill the dorf, you can immediately say the phrase and start the cycle of 6 giants over. We usually fight right in the throne room and try to get 2-3 cycles before the 6 statics respawn in the area. Kill the statics and start the cycle again.

We have been getting about one plate drop a cycle. Arms/Boots/Gauntlets are very common. Helm/Bracer less common. BP/Legs are rare.

It's only plate drops. We have never seen anything else and have been there a lot. I suppose very rarely you might see other drops. So you need all the other classes willing to camp an area that they know only plate will drop.

Verant will nerf this once more people start doing it. It is way too easy for the rewards. It is a completely controllable camp where you can autospawn your plate drops.
