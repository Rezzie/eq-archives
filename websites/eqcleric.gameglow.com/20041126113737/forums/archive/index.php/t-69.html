Try Upper Guk, Permafrost, Runnyeye, Najena or Unrest. I hunted in Upper Guk and Runnyeye a lot from lvl 20 to mid 20s. For Upper Guk, you could try doing the Squire spawn area which isn't very far from the zone or head on to the scryer room. That kind of thing sets you up for things like Cazic and Sol A where you don't want to wait for your tanks to hike through the Inky woods or run all the way from Freeport to CT.

I didn't do Permafrost much, but I gather it's much like Runnyeye.

Solo gorge hounds in east karana. No loot, but pretty easy to root and smite a hound. If you get in trouble, just run back to the guards in the town near the gorge, or better yet, pull the hound in close to the town before you blast in earnest. Just root and shoot. Those hounds will give good experience while solo up through level 24. Boring, perhaps, but solo experience is better than group experience. You will probably have to smite the hounds five or six times to kill them. Med and repeat. Watch out for griffs, evil eyes, and cyclopses, all fairly rare near the gorge town. Lion Patriarchs are probably still blue at 20, but the guards won't kill lions.

When you get bored, go into the gorge and group up for evil eye hunting near the throne of King Xorbb. That's a lot of fun. Those evil eyes look nasty, but the unnamed ones have no more hps than a gorge hound. Watch out for charm, and those very fun push effects that throw you halfway across the chamber.
