We're seeking fun, involved, intelligent players whose characters are 45+. and who wish to be a part of a guild thats more than a social club, but not a full-time job. We are a US guild, on the Saryrn Server, with most of the leadership being in the Eastern time zone, so you will get the most out of our guild if you can play evenings with us. If you are interested in joining us, visit our website (http://www.eqnebula.com), please register and post into the "want to join" forum.
