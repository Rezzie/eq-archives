Its funny how even some Clerics are brainwashed. Little hint I dont care how much mana you have if you are dead. High Elves have very poor STR and the worst STA of any cleric. Poor str = crappy armor untwinked. Less STA = less hps. Could you wear a full suit of bronze, heavy weapon, qeynos kite shield at level 17, Mr. Elf? Certainly not. Any cleric that doesnt think AC is important as +wis +mana is going to gte his group killed. Simple as that. Things hit hard at high levels. That AC and hps will let me live. Mana and wis only affect the TOTAL mana pool. Not how fast you regen. Sure when the doodoo hits the fan a HIE might be able to get ONE more bravery off than me. Unfortunately when that happens the HIE cleric is the first to die with his low str, low AC, and low STA. Then your group dies. So lets just have a look at it shall we? Lets dispell this 'urban myth'.

Level 17
Human
Your Current Level Is: 17
The Base Wisdom For Your Char Is: 108
Your Base Mana At This Level Is: 401
Your Modified Wisdom Is Now: 108
Your Modified Mana At This Level Is: 401
Extra Mana As A Result Of Modifiers Is: 0

High Elf
Your Current Level Is: 17
The Base Wisdom For Your Char Is: 130
Your Base Mana At This Level Is: 476
Your Modified Wisdom Is Now: 130
Your Modified Mana At This Level Is: 476
Extra Mana As A Result Of Modifiers Is: 0

So the HIE can cast one more heal. He also cannot really wear any armor. Bronze is some heavy stuff! A full suit of banded + shield and hammer and the HIE wont even be able to carry any loot to upgrade his equip. The human can wear bronze and will also enjoy a few more hps. 10 STA is nothing to sneeze at.

Level 60
Human
Your Current Level Is: 60
The Base Wisdom For Your Char Is: 110
Your Base Mana At This Level Is: 1440
Your Modified Wisdom Is Now: 180
Your Modified Mana At This Level Is: 2280
Extra Mana As A Result Of Modifiers Is: 840

High Elf
Your Current Level Is: 60
The Base Wisdom For Your Char Is: 130
Your Base Mana At This Level Is: 1680
Your Modified Wisdom Is Now: 200
Your Modified Mana At This Level Is: 2520
Extra Mana As A Result Of Modifiers Is: 840

I left the humans wis at 180 as we will assume they have the exact same gear on. Obviously this wont be the case. More likely at 60 is both have 200 wis and the rest AC and mana. Guess what? Thats ONE Celestial Healing. Thats one remedy. This is also only if you stood up and completely emptied your bar of mana chain casting. How often does that happen? More likely you dump a bit and med. You will never notice the difference.

Hope this helps. Good luck to all you Human, Gnome, and Halfling cleric out there! We shall overcome!
