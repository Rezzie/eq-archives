some nice places to hunt in Kunark..

The Overthere : very good experience, watch out for goons tho

Burning Woods : group only. most mobs are in the late 30's on up, great experience

Temple of Droga (Frontier Mountains) : Nice loot, good experience... pain in the arse to get your non-gating classes out of

Velious (if you ever get it)

Frozen Tower: Very good loot, great experience, can be grand central train station if people don't know what they are doing. Has a very nice cleric equipeable +wis range slot item (Teachers Syllabus)

Crystal Caverns: your approaching too high a level at 37, all the orcs are green around 39 or so. Very good experience and great sellable loot. Camped there 8 hours one night at level 32 (with a group) and walked out with 10 yellow bubs of xp and a pack full of loot i sold for over 5k pp.

If your going to Kunark from NRo, make sure you jump off the boat immediately upon entering OT and just swim to your left as your facing land... unless your a Dark Elf of course.

Hope that helps
