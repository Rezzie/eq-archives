HS is my current playground. The xp is great.

We have been cavorting- 60 druid, 60 monk, 54 rogue. Sometimes add in a 55 enchanter.

You need a soul-bound key to enter the zone. Once inside- the wings require keys. When you get to the end of a wing, there is a teleporter to take you back to the zone in. Some of the side rooms require keys which are ground spawn. There are pit traps and many one-way doors. Check eqmaps, as many adventurers before you have documented where these traps are.

It is so much fun

Cant say about equipment. But I can say- start with the North wing. In fact, without a rogue you MUST start with the north wing.

The spermie things drop the candle which is the key to the north wing. Once you get to the end of the north wing- the crypt keeper drops the key to the west wing. I assume the key-holder for the west wing drops the one for the south and so on.

As we have switched to my playing the rogue and not the cleric- keys are not an issue. We have a druid to snare- so, runners arent an issue. But about 50% is undead. Evac is nice because frankly I dont know where the exit is.
