Just a tip-

I post alot of screenshots on my server ezboard, and I find that my screenies ALWAYS come out looking darker than my screen normally looks when I play.

I use Microsoft Photo Editor (comes with Office 97 and 2000, but you have to select it to install, it's not part of the default installation)

Photo Editor has nice Brightness/Gamma/Contrast sliders AND it has a Jpeg quality selector. When you do a Save As, you can set the jpeg compression rate for a small file/low quality or larger file/higher quality.

This is very useful if you want to take a screenie AND preserve the text, or just show a picture.
