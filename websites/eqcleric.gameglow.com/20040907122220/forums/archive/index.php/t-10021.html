Originally posted by Advert:
"This change actually benefits necros mostly"
Please explain this to me...


Talked to a nec friend about this. The DoT nerf never affected the necroes ability to fear kite, but it did affect their ability to *reverse* kite. That is, they snare and DoT the mob and then run around the zone while the pet beats the mob in the rear. The advantage of this over fear kiting is that you can control the mobs pathing and significantly reduce the possibility the mob will path into another mob in dangerous areas.

Druids, on the other hand, are usually better off quad kiting with AE's which were never affected by the DoT nerf.

>>Now that dot kiting has been unnerfed (after 2+ years), maybe they will stop asking for better heals?<<

I doubt it since Druids want heals more for the raid/Grouping part of the game where DOTs are rarely used and where the DOT nerf really didn't apply when they were used. This change helps Druids and Necros some in soloing but not at all as far as grouping goes.

In most EXP groups, Druids wouldn't use DOTs since MOBs go down too fast for the DOT to be worth the mana. Another reason against using DOTs in groups is that DOTs prevent Mez type spells from working for more than a tick so in groups with Bard/Ench/Necros the DOT damage wasn't worth the hassle. If they are used, the Nerf doesn't matter since these groups are based on Tanking MOBs, not kiting. Even in "ping-pong" situations, the DOT penalty wouldnt take effect since MOBs usually don't run around for long.

On raids, DOTs are efficiant but of limited use. Since DOTs can't stack, the only Druid DOT used on raids is Winged Death which means that any other Druids on the Raid would have to Nuke to do damage. In some Raids, Druids don't bother with DOTs anyway since they are frequntly on Heal duty anyway.

As far as how the DOT change will make a differance, we will see. It's been so long since Druids could DOT kite and at the highest levels, Nukes are almost as good DOTs anyway. Also, Root/Rott on MOBs does allow you to MED which in most cases, does regen more mana than the Root uses and the Root does some damage to boot.
