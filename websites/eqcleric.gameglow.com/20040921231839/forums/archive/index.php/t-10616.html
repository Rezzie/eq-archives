SolA would probably be a good choice, if you have non-evils in your group.

Hunt over near the gnomes, but fighting gobos and you have a convenient place to sell off all the FS and Bronze that drops. If they had a bank, it would be perfect.

High Hold Keep is another option to consider here-again because of the conveniently located bank and merchants.

Ringmail, though not insanely common, drops off the orcs and gnolls in HHP often enough to be worth "farming" it-and sells for as much as 20P or so on some pieces.

Or get together about 200P and head to your server's market area and become a trader. Just make sure ya spend some time learning the prices things go for so you are always trading up in value. It's boring, but it works.
