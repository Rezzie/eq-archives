Ok, this is what I picked up so far. The Bloodsabers are the enemy of the Temple of Life. These two are always doing things to piss each other off. Bloodsabers worship (or work for) Bertoxxulos AKA that Plague Bringer. I assume that the Bloodsabers are trying to plague the land for their god.

I found this on Sanderson's Quest web site:
"Plaguebringer Proof: Ask Roesager Thusten, Paladin of Rodcet Nife, who is located in North Qeynos, either in the temple of life's vendor building or walking out the gates and around the outer portion of the north qeynos zone "who is Bertossulous" and he will tell you that he is the lord of disease. Ask him "what disease" and he will tell you about a sect called the plaguebringers whom he fears in infesting Qeynos, but he requires proof of their presence. Ask him abaout "proof" and he will tell you that he has heard about a plaguebringer roaming Qeynos Hills, and he wants you to bring him proof of this. Here is the tricky part, which requires some teamwork and may be needed for both the good and evil branches of this quest. You need to have a friend create an evil newbie and do the gharins note quest. Take the fake note to Surefall (you'll be dubious to all, not KOS yet heh). Get your higher lvl main person in qeynos hills, at Tovax Vmar's spawnpoint. This is located at the wall behind the guard tower that is near the road to west karana, and down the wall a bit towards the qeynos zone. Once tovax has spawned, (the spawn time on the spawn point is 6min, and tovax only spawns at norrath night, between 8pm and 8am Norrath time. daytime spawns Scruffy the rat instead.) stick to him like glue, and make sure to Repeatedly (like once a min) ooc a message asking the other players not to kill tovax, as you are using him for a QUEST. Once you have done this, tell your newbie evil char friend to give the fake note to Te'Anara. She will KOS the newbie. This event triggers Tovax. He will keep walking until he circles back around to his spawnpoint behind the guard tower. The second he touches his spawn point, he will immediately turn towards the ruins and begin a non-stop walk towards them. Once he finally gets to the ruins, assuming the GBS campers don't nuke him, he will stop, wait for 30 seconds or so, then drop a note (looks like a bag) and sprint off to his spawnpoint again. When he drops the note, it triggers Varsoon to spawn at one of the three "mangy rat/pyzjn/varsoon" spawn points. If you open the letter, its from S'ragg (SK guildleader in qeynos bertoxx shrine) to Opal Darkbriar (necro guildleader in freeport sewers innoruuk shrine, and good guy in disguise in caster shrine in FP). Bring back this note and Roesager Thusten says 'Rodcet be praised!! This is what we have been searching for! On behalf of the Temple of Life let me thank you and offer you this small reward for your efforts to aid us in our war against the Plaguebringer. You get some minor coin and a minor item. It seems likely that this continues on from here. (Submitted by Jeskola Corpseshredder)"

If anyone has done this, I'd like to know what that note says. Perhaps there is a clue there.

To get Tolon to spawn go to the inn around the temple of tunare and ask the innkeeper "who/where is Tolon Nurbryte" WHAMMO he spawns. This is for the Thex dagger quest.

The fleshy orb quest has been broken for at least 6 months. I searched high and low in FP for this NPC when I was farming the SSoL quest and I got the fleshy orb quest. He cannot be found. If a GM told you it wasn't broken I don't believe them.

Also, on a side note, a easy way to get a diseased orb to try this quest without having to kill 50 rabid wolfs or bears. Go to the sick house in WK where the SSoL guy is. Occasionally a evil high level PC will be slaying him. He drops the fleshy orb. Thats why he needs that cure .

I never tried to ask NPCs about Lorme to try and make him spawn. But in the Thex quest it directs you to ask for him at the Inn. So it seems very unusual to have directions like that not be included in the fleshy orb quest.
