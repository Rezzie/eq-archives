I naltron all tanks and chanters, pinzarn anyone else who ask for it

Well, I guess I'm stingy in comparison to you. Personally, I think that symbols are over-used and over-demanded buffs. Since they're pure HP buffs, they don't increase your group's efficiency except insofar as they're used on a tank that you're healing with CH.

The only other function of additional hit points is to provide an additional margin of safety if people are routinely being beaten down to low health. I find that most of my day-to-day hunting simply doesn't occur under those circumstances. To the contrary, I usually try to seek out situations where we're getting controlled pulls that are challenging but not constantly life-threatenting.

My only "standard" symbol is the enchanter if we have one, and that may be either Pinzarn or Marzin depending on where we're hunting. If I have a heavy tank that I'm CH'ing a lot, I will usually (though not always) symbol him as well. And if the puller is getting beat up a lot on his way back to the group, I'll symbol him as well, so he's not stumbling back with 30% health all the time. That's it, really; everything else is situational, and for the most part other people (including myself) can do without.

Of course, there are situations were efficiency is not your primary concern, like raids, breaking a tough spawn, or perhaps venturing into a dungeon where your group is in a little over its head. But for everyday hunting in reasonably controlled circumstances, I think the "symbol everyone" approach is wasteful. If you can keep everyone healed safely with a given buff setup, adding more symbols to the mix just burns up mana and money without any return.
