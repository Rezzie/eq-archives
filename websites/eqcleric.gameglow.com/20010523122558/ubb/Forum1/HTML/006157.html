As long as your defence doesn't complete suck, the Mith BP is better then the constant. It will give you more AC (the AC on mith was better for me due to +agil around 35 with a defence of about 165 to 170) and will probably give you more hit points over all, depending on any other gear you have that gives you +stamina. I don't know if it gives more AC then the EB BP, I'm guessing it does for me at lvl 48 with a 200 defence, but I don't have an EB BP to test that out.

If you have the 5k or so (prices vary by server and seller of course) I would go with teh seb or froglock scale. If you can actually find a dwarven smith with a 220 or so smithing skill (enchanted imbued trivial at 229 I believe) then get a cultural BP. It's about 250-300 in material plus the temper (lava rocks are kind of rare), and you need a 49+ enchanter to enchant the brellium.

Outside of that, constant BP isn't bad.. I have the components to make another, and might wear it when I do since I'm ugly enough as is, and don't need more mismatched armor heh.

At level 49 I am wearing a Guardian BP from SkyShrine.

BP of the Constant is a nice piece for sure, but I put mine in the bank around level 40 when i got some Ry'Gorr Battlemail. I preferred the +10str from the Ry'Gorr because, well... i am a High Elf!

If your looking for good bp that you can camp at 40, i would recommend Guardian Armor in general. SkyShrine is a pretty easy area to hunt in and some of the pieces that drop are very nice (legs, helm, bp for clerics). The Guardian BP is only 2ac better than Constant, but it adds str, stam, mana, and resists. Guardian Helm is one of the better helms in the game for clerics that is campable at your level (helm is ac11 +4wis +otherstats and resists). And the Guardian Leggings are nice too (i believe ac13 +4wis +other stats and resists).

That is, of course, assuming that you are not trying to keep dragon faction. The Royal Frosted Velium in Icewell Keep is very nice stuff too, but you would really want to be 49+ to camp in there if your looking for a pickup group. RFV armor is much better AC wise, but i don't know about the RFV armors stats & resists.

Just some thoughts on stuff that is campable at your level and that may hold you over til you could get Sebilite Scale or Planar/Quest stuff.
