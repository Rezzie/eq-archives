Just a note with the use of DoT spells (for those that didnt know this already.)

If the mob you cast a DoT on MOVES, the amount of damage it takes from your DoT is SEVERLY reduced. This nerf was made ages ago to try and prevent druids from kiting. So, pulling with a DoT is not a great plan cuz it will nerf any real ability your spell has from doing dmg.
