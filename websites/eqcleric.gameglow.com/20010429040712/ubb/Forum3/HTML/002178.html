The quest givers in Skyshrine were recently changed from Yelniak to CoV faction, you only need to be indifferent to CoV to do the armor quests. Currently the only MOB in skyshrine that is Yelniak faction is Lord Yelniak himself.
Oh, and pay no attention to that gimp Erudite Boanerges, unless you like CR or something... =P
There are 2 seperate faction in ToV outside the Testing Halls. The main one is CoV, but there are also some wyverns that are on the Guardians of Veeshan faction. Thye guard the way to the entrance to the plane of mischif and are KOS to everyone. When you enter ToV and head in, you will come to a large room with a central pillar that has 4 bridges leading from it.
The zone in area is through the south doors, all MOBs around it are CoV faction.
The west door leads to a zone out and several named dragons that give quests requiring items from the testing halls. All MOBs past the west door are CoV faction.
The north door leads to an area that has the entrance to PoM and suposedly a shrine to Veeshan. The MOBs past this door include some that are on Guardians of Veeshan faction that are KOS to all, but is mostly CoV faction. Warning: IF you go through the North door, GoV faction MOBs will agro on you very shortly. If you train them out into the 4 way bridge area, they will NOT path home, they will stand there and will attack anyone coming into the area, regardless of faction.
The east door leads to the testing halls where those of us that are +CoV faction go to "test our strength" and get quest armor pieces for the Skyshrine quest armor and quest pieces for the quests given by the named dragons in the west wing.

Hope this helps some...
