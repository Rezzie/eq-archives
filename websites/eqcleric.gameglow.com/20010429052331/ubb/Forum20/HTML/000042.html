My rough stats are 990 AC and 2600 HP with Heroism, Aegis, and Naltron. I also have about +400 in mana, and WIS around 192-196. My unbuffed resists with normal exp gear are around 50 each, and can get magic and fire/cold to just shy of 100 with my resist gear. So while I consider myself fairly well-equipped, I am still shy of your stats, and I'm still missing 3 pieces of Blue Diamond armor, and 4 pieces of Thurgadin armor. Playing on a blue server helps a bit.

With regards to raids, well, I haven't done too many uber-raids, but I enjoy 1 group activities, and the occasional 2-4 group raid. So while I don't have the experience of slaying Trakanon, Velketor, or even going to ToV yet, I will say that I am pretty well-prepared. AC helps, but if you get aggroed by something, you are going to go down fast, so I'm thinking if I can get to 1000 AC unbuffed, I will go for HP/mana/resists, not sure which order yet, but will probably put mana last, since I have never really "needed" more mana in a big fight, yes it would have been nice, but I was able to adjust earlier in the fight.
