Normally, there is a short refresh time after you cast a spell. If you have an insta-click item, you can get rid of that refresh time.


Cast spell
Click Coldain ring
Cast next spell


If you didn't click the ring, you'd have to wait a couple of seconds between casts.

Forgot to add that it won't refresh spells that have a refresh time built in, such as the divines and reckoning. It does refresh the DA icon, but you won't be able to cast it.

Basically with MC 3 you will regain 1800 more mana per hour than you would without it.

Based on Mikars stats with CM 3 you will gain a 10% saving of mana over what you already have.

Therefore a simple sum 1800/0.1 = 18000 as in you need to use 18000 mana per hour for this to be as efficent.

This sounds alot but per minute it is 300 mana a minute.

In an average exp group this is probably about what you are doing, since you will regain mana in a standard group with either a bard for around 30-35 mana a tick (meaning you will regain 300-350 mana a minute, which most people will be using in some manner).

Now here is where it comes in useful. If you are on a big raid, say as part of the master chain. For example you are part of a 4 person CH chain timing a CH every 5 seconds. This means you will CH 3 times a minute at the cost of 1200 mana.

Since we already know that 300 mana = 3 mana per tick (from mental clarity), if you cast 3 CH a minute in a Chain then it 4x more effective than mental Clarity.

If you spend more mana it is better, the less you spend the less effective it is.

But given the fact that you have to use mana in order for mana regen to be of use, you should be at least using enough mana to put you on par with MC3.

Hope this helps :>
