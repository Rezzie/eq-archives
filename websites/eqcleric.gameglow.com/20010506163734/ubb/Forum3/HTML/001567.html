Facts to date:

First NPC we find, Named LAVAwalker. First impression by anyone meeting this NPC would be that this is a Mage Quest.

Upon returning the crown to him, we receive a potion that casts a Mage Spell. Also An NPC named WhiteWATER spawns.

When you kill him, a FIRE Elemental (spirit) spawns.

Some of the NPCs talk about Gods that clerics can not even worship, but Mages can.

Naxton DeepWATER in Burning woods.

RageFIRE in SolB.

All NPCs speak or are involved with the Plasmatic Priesthood who are a new group of folks bent on taking over the world.

The four elements (we are not talking modern science here) are Fire, Earth, Water, Air. Isn't it possible that the Priests have discovered a fifth element, i.e. Plasma. This would be the next logical step for an elemental, a plasma elemental, and these priests are using it for evil?

All of the items found to date are MAGE. Some are usable by Clerics, but ALL are usable by Mage. Some of these half-uber items are not even cleric usable, but all of them are Mage usable. (Rogues can use all the weapons they get on their quest)

Quest Text:
Omat Vastsea says 'The Triumvirate of Water are: E'ci, the mistress of ice; Tarew Marr, the lord of water; and Povar, the formless master of vapor and mist. We are talking Air, Water, Ice, Mist Vapor, these are all in the realm of Mages.


My conclusion:
We are doing the Mage quest.

I hope I am wrong, after all, a lot of these NPCs are clerics, but the facts speak for themselves.
