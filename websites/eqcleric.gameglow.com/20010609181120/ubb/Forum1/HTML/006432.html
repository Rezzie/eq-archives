Well last weekend we advanced two of our Clerics to the Ragefire stage of the Cleric Epic. Nagafen usually goes down right after patches on Erollisi Marr and we were counting on that. After the 2nd patch on the 31, Phoenix Crusaders moved a group into the lair to work on the Fire Giants and hold the camp. In the meantime, every single person in the guild was on "Ragefire" watch. Phone numbers were given out, resist gear backpacked etc.

Less than 24 hours after Naggy died, Ragefire popped for us. It was totally amazing as 30 guildmates came together in roughly 5 minutes and began flooding Sol B. I think that the camp was contested very briefly by one of the larger guilds on the server (for some reason they had an Ogre blocking the entrance to Sol B). However the rest of their guild was apparently busy in ToV and eventually stood down. I have no idea what happened but I personally believe it was just one person who got caught up in the heat of the moment and eventually realized that he alone could not do anything except trash a rep. From what it appears, he was the only person who even MIGHT have been trying to contest the Ragefire. Other than him we had no problems with other people or guilds. After that we were left alone to kill Ragefire in our time.

Anyway, about half an hour of the first Ragefire sighting, we engaged. The fight was over before we knew it and only lost a single member.

It was a neat experience and we now have our first Clicker in the guild.
