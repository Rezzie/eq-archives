ermm.. dunno how much agro you can build while DA is on..

First off, mobs will ignore anyone with DA/DB on unless they are the only person in agro range. Second you can not attack or cast while DA/DB is on.

So if the MT is the only one in aggro range they can build a little bit of aggro just from being the only valid target, but that's it.

If you want to think of an instance where this is really dangerous, it's DA/DB the main tank. Since the mob will ignore them and go for the player with the next highest aggro, chances are someone is going to get owned. Heh, do this to your MT just after he goes /defensive, see how much the tank, and the raid likes you then.

This skill will be useful, but it isn't high on my priority. Right now, none of them are since I'm only 53
