One important thing to relate is that for Skyshrine/Kael armor, you pretty much need to be in an uber guild. If you aren't in a "raid" guild, the odds of you seeing this armor is very low. Thurgadin armor is not easy to get, but MUCH easier to get than the other two. The stats aren't bad, slightly better than Donal's, usually much better than EM.

The arena in Kael (for Thurg armor drops) can be done with one good group, although it will be slow, and you won't be able to clear all the mobs, but it is doable. 2-3 groups enables you to clear the whole arena, and pull some nameds. For Skyshrine/Kael armor, the actual armor drops are probably about the same, but the mobs are MUCH tougher, and you will need around 3-4 groups minimum to kill these mobs.

So, if you are in a casual guild, or not a full-time raider, go Thurgadin armor. If you have the resources, go Kael or Skyshrine.
