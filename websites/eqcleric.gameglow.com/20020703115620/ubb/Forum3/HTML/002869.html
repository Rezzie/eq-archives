Lodi Shield does have mana for every cleric under 255 wisdom... If the 10 wisdom on the shield doesn't put you over the 255 cap (assuming level 60) then the shield gives you 60 mana.

Throw in 20FR 20CR and 10MR with enduring breath (nice convenient effect on an item you won't likely be switching in and out because of the nice ac, wis, and VERY nice resists.

And yes, the potential HP loss from other shields does hurt. Lets take an easily* obtainable popular cleric cloak and compare:

Heirphants Cloak - 10ac +4all stats 75hp 75mana

Lodizal Shield - 23ac 10wis 20fr 20cr 10mr effect: enduring breath

dex/agi/int/cha/str don't really matter in any case which leaves us with:

Heiro 10ac 4wis 75mana 87hp (75 + 3hp/1sta*4)

Lodi 23ac 10wis 20fr 20cr 10mr + useful effect

boiled down this gives us (assuming under 255 wis cap):

Heiro 10ac 99mana 87hp
Lodi 23ac 60mana 20fr 20cr 10mr + useful effect

It boils down to a personal choice, the Lodi Shield is +13ac -39mana -87hp +20fr +20cr +10mr + useful effect over the Heiro cloak. My personal choice would be the Lodi Shield over the Heiro, your choice was obviously different.

as for tons of better and easily obtainable cloaks (besides the heiro), i would certainly like to see your list and/or find out what you determine to be easily obtainable... those determinations may differ from my personal opinions of better and easily obtainable, then again.. they may not.

Seriously though, if I have to explain to you why Lodizal Shield sucks...you need to find a new class or a new game to play. HP, Mana, and Mana Regen are where it's at in the end game for clerics...PERIOD. Clerics very rarely need resists because of our ability to hide and heal. Besides, who can't get near 200 resists anyway with normal gear + the new spells + AE bard resists.

Here's my logic:

If you are 60, chances are you have been in a guild or two or a few. With the abundance of Thurgadin plate armor, new Luclin gear with high wis, KEI (25 wisdom right here), you are going to be at 255 wisdom. If you are 60 and you aren't at 255 wisdom, but can afford Lodizal Shield or Donal's Legs, YOU NEED TO RE-EVAULATE YOUR GEAR. As a side note, staying as low as 230 wisdom for use of hp/mana items is perfectly fine in my mind, since KEI bumps you to 255 anyway. It's very rare these days I'm anywhere doing anything important without KEI.

If you are low to mid 50s, I can understand that you might not have 255 wisdom. In that case, you are still looking for high wisdom items, and here are some that are cheaper and/or easier to get:

Acrylia Studded Cloak: 7ac 3str 5dex 10wis. same wis as shield, 1/20th the cost on my server (2k for this cloak vs 40k for lodi). Need EB? Buy a FB earring for 2k or do the Luclin quest...you are saving a ton.
Aegis of Life: 12ac 10wis 25svd. Granted, NOT an easy quest...but probably less time involved getting this than camping Lodi or raising the cash for the shield.
Matchless Dragonwing Cloak: 15ac 7str 11wis
Peerless Dragonwing Cloak: 14ac 5str 10wis

NOW - if you fall under that first category and have maxxed wisdom and are 60, here's what I think you should be going for. (BTW if you try and argue that AC + resists make Lodi better than these items, I feel sorry for your guild).

Heirophant's Cloak 10ac 4 all stats, 75hp 75mana. Relatively easy to get (shorter camp than Lodi anyway) or can be bought for about 1/4th the cost of Lodi shield.
White Dragonscale Cloak used to be another example but that changed with the focus added =/
Cultural made cloaks: New ones have 40hp 40mana and possibly 10wis depending on your class. STILL cheaper than Lodi shield

And chances are, *most* of you have a shot at these items as well depending on how good you guild is (no I won't argue that obtaining these following items are easier than lodi shield, but definitely more fun trying them than camping him or raising the cash)

Brewer's Mesh Cloak from Inny for dwarven clerics.
Cloak of Destruction from Rumblecrush.
Cloak of the Sky from Eashen.
Frostreaver's Embroidered Cloak from Dain.
Shroud of Dar Brood from Zland.
Shroud of Veeshan from Phara Dar. (one of the best cloaks in the game for clerics plus the focus is neat)
