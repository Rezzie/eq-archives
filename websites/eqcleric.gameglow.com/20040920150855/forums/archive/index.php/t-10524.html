Got into a group at the bandit camp 1(the one closest to shadowhaven) at 20, xp is very nice and the bandits drop armor that can be sold for some okey money, i used daring,healing,celestial remedy, spirit armor and stun here, didn't nuke much since my mana dropped fast enough anyway http://www.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif, not uncommon to fight 2 mobs at once sometimes 3(keeps you on your toes), a bad thing with this camp is that it is right infront of the exit to shadowhaven, so trains are not uncommon, the patog phlergs cause disease that lasts for hours so keep cure disease handy(takes 3 casts to cure it)

There is another bandit camp to the north and that one is probably better since there is no exits from zone around there, can be tricky to get to i guess unless you have invis.
