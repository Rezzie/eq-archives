The Buryniel Legion Gi (not sure of spelling) can be easily obtained fron the dungeon Nurga in the Frontier Mountains of Kunark. Stats are:

AC 14
WT 0.3

DEX +2
HP +15
MANA +25


To obtain item, just kill the miners in the pit (you will see pit shortly after waterfall). Our group got its limit (they are Lore) then started leaving them on the corpses

They are pretty common, dropping on about 1/4 of the mobs.

Just thought you would like to know
