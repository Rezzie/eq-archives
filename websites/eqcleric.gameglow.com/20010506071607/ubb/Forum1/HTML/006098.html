Tricky I'd say. I've spent a fair bit of time on CoM myself and I can't think how you'd pull singles.

Spawn time in CoM is ~13 minutes. The entrance section goes like this:
Starting at the entrance, there are two spawns this side of the bridge, and three just behind the gate. One at the near end of each balcony and one at the far.

Through the gate and to the right is the temple section. The pathing here is so bizarre that I've seen a *monk* have trouble pulling singles.

If you turn left after the gate you go to the stables. Once the spawn here is broken you can pull singles, but an initial full spawn will have 4+ mobs in it.

If you proceed sort-of-straight after the gate you eventually come to the yard. House in the middle with goos, buildings at the back. The stuff in the buildings *might* be soloable but personally I wouldn't want to try

It's a nice place but I have always been grouped there. It can be quiet at times but you only need one melee type to turn up, and you can hold the bridge with careful timing.

I've been leveling in the CoM for the past 9 levels, and since that zone is often empty I solo the first couple of mobs.

I actually dont use lull, just enstill and that works pretty well. The first two mobs you'll see are the "bridge" mobs. Just enstill one and pull the other to the zone and root & nuke. After you've cleared those 2 mobs, you can take on the three just behind the gate. I usually enstill one, then enstill a 2nd one in the middle of the bridge, and pull the third to the zone where i finish it off. The nice thing is that when they break root and you are at the zone they wont come after you.

Whenever there are multiple mobs to pull, make sure you root skeletons and golems first, since shadows/apparitions can break root very quickly.

Someone said they are on a 13 minute timer...I think its a little longer, maybe 20 minutes. Anyway, after you've cleared these 5 mobs, they should be ready to re-pop.

Great experience. Of course it helps if you have your lvl 44 nukes, but its not required. Good luck!

I have done alot of hunting in CoM.. both group and solo.

To solo effectively, you really need to be lvl 44+ . At that point it is a fairly simple process and you have 2 options:

1) Pacify the spawns (high cha REALLY seems to help for some reason) and pull singles

or

2) "Park" the spawns at the bridge light post. I don't know if this is considered a but or exploit, but if you run mobs around the "square" before the bridge they will get stuck on the lightpost. Bad pathing and all that. Once they are "stuck" you can pull singles out of the pile. Personally, i have never tried this... but some people swear by it.

I use the Pacify method. I carry my CHA gear (bought it for cheaper prices for smithing). I put on my CHA gear, pacify 1 spawn (works about 85% of the time), and pull the other to the zone and root-n-nuke. For the 3 spawn at the gate I Pacify the closest first when just barely in range... the other 2 have yet to aggro because of it. Then i move up and Pacify one of the other spawns and pull the 3rd back to the zone line.

Personally, at level 45 it was hard for me to take all 5 entrance spawns before they would start respawning. Although i could handle 4 of the 5 fairly simply.

My personal preference was to do the balcony + 2 of the gate spawns... balcony is EZ to pull singly, you have room to root-n-nuke. Then i would Pacify the Middle gate spawn which would aggro the closest gate spawn to me, but not the furthest. Root-n-nuke that one then pull the middle spawn from just inside casting range. If done right the 3rd gate spawn (furthest from which ever balcony your hunting at) will not aggro. By the time i finished i could med up to full, and rebuff just in time for the 1st balcony spawn to repop.

According to my clock, the CoM spawns are on a 20 minute timer give or take 30 seconds. Plus once you pass the bridge the drop rate for items seemed to increase.

At 44-45 my spell selection was:

Pacify
Panic the Dead
Banish Undead
Retribution
Enstill
Dismiss Undead
Celestial Healing (cast on self at 1/2 hp and you won't die)
Divine Aura

Hope that helps ya
