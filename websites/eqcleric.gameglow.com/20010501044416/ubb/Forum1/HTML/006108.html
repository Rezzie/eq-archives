Good choice! There can never be too many High Elven clerics in Norrath - we must outnumber those damn dorfs

As to advice: In the Cleric Guide/FAQ there is an excellent guide written by Renmorum Lifespinner. Read it. Learn it. Memorise it. It is fantastic - one of the best sources of information I've seen about any EQ class in the one spot.

Strength: In your mid levels strength can be a problem for a high elf - bronze armour is HEAVY. However, once you get your gold quest armour (either by questing or twinking) - that is much lighter, and has some nice +STR bonuses. With my hodgepodge of quest armour, some EM and a few other bits and pieces, I am at 68/95 or so. Also look to get a ruby veil (silver or plat etc) is very good, +STR and +WIS in the one package.

There are many other people out there with many points of advice - but just enjoy yourself - clericing can be repetitive, but that gives you plenty of time to indulge in /ooc arguments, abusing guildmates, and mocking monks as they get 35 point thunderkicks
