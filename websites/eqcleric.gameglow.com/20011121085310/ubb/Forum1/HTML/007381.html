Ciado, I've seen Nalox several times while running the left wall from SF to Chardok. The loc is really to help you find it if you are binding and setting up your forces at the Ixiblat spawn site. Naxot used to attack Ixiblat as part of the script, but Verant fixed that, because the strategy until the fix was to Charm Naxot and then do multiple turn ins on the note. This was never Verants intention as they want to have a 2 hour bottle neck there and at Royals to limit the number of clerics all needing Ragefire at the same time.

Ixiblat can be fought with a main tank being healed by several clerics with all the casters doing their finger wiggling. If your forces are primarily in the 50 to 55 range, I would recommend 4 groups or more, and go heavy on Tanks + Clerics and wizards. Oddly enough, Ixiblat is not immune to firespells, so mages and their firepets rock too! With half a dozen or so clerics primarily healing the Main tank and keeping the others alive, the fight should be over in about 60 seconds, if the battle goes to much longer that that it means you might be in trouble and could be looking at a second attempt.

Di Zok on the other hand is not going to be as easy as Ixiblat or for that matter, not as easy as Ragefire either. Snarks are mean, and your talking about killing their king, that upsets them. You will need a highly skilled raid force to do this, your average Hate Raid would be eaten alive in about 30 seconds. Best plan is find someone who's done the fight before a few times and is willing to lead it for you. Also research Chardok because since the zone overhaul there are lots of changes and the Safe Hall is no longer safe for instance.

Got Ragefire yesterday afternoon, 2 groups no deaths Wurm dropped Heart, BP, Gloves, Red Scale, FE Ring.


Good luck on your quest!
