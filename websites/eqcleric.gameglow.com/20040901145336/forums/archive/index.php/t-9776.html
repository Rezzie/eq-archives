Just going to comment on some of kitsinu's points... Please don't take this the wrong way.

"I have seen more than enough Clerics who nuked rather than the more dangerous healing also."

Nuking has its place. If s/he was over-nuking and constantly OOM, sure, call him/her on it, but if s/he only tossed 1 or 2 at the right time, all the more power to them.

"So far as a Pet class goes, I have to wonder why Druids were given an addition in their Tracking skill."

Because a skill level of 20 is well-nigh useless. At 20 skill you can see farther than you can track. Interestingly enough, Rangers have the opposite problem--At a skill of 200 you can track something across the zone, and your list will be so full it doesn't show the griffin coming up behind you.

"If you want to see how powerful Druids are, go to Ak’Anon and see a high level Druid kiting three gnome guards at a time. Could a Cleric do this even with Journeyman Boots?"

Not to my knowledge. Our DDs aren't mana-efficient enough. I imagine we could to the med-kite thing if we had JBoots, but why would you want to? You could get better experience fighting a constant stream of low blues in a 2- or 3-person group.

Look it it this way: No ther class gets Superior Heal. No other Class gets Complete Heal. No other class gets our Stuns. No other class gets Revives (Well, Paladins get Revive at 49, but who wants it at that level? Therefore, moot point).

They can do things that we can't, but we can also do things that they can't.
