Lower Guk dead side is not that bad, especially if you have 54+ enchanter farming that fbss all night long nearby, most would gladly trade c2 for hero or symbol. Dar frogs are all dark blue up to 54, and even up to 55 like 90% of them will be dark blue. After 55 it gets too slow and unefficient.
Spells you need: epitaph of life, our level 52 undead dot, and expel undead to finish them off, since most dar ghouls have about 1% of their hitpoints after 4 epitaphs.

Loot is not all that great, but if you get archmagus camp or frenzy, its doable solo after level 52 and you might get either SMR (which is still valiable as its required for chanter and rogue epics), or fbss.

You might consider pairing with some high damage melee, such as ranger or monk.
