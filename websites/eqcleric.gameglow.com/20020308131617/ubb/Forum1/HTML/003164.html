With regards to the CH post.. Sup Heal stops healing two bubbles on a base pally HP at lv 43, so I don't see how you could get three outta that.

CH is more mana efficient than Superior Heal anytime you can get it to heal more than 1050HP. So, if a tank has 1600 HP (Read: Buffed 36 warrior w/symbol, Buffed 43ish Pal w/o Symbol, 43ish warrior w/o symbol) then it is more mana efficient to toss a CH on them when they get to two bubbles of health remaining, assuming they're still taking damage. After the ten seconds, in a non-train situation, they've gotten down to around a bubble and a half and the CH will do about 1100-1200 HP, which saved you about 100 mana when compared to two Sup Heals.

Another good way to figure out when the warrior needs a CH is when they get their berserker frenzy message, since this is a percentage of HP and not a solid number.

My recommendation to you would be A: Symbol the Rogue for goodness sake, if he's taking damage then he needs more HP than you, and it's a safe bet he doesn't have it, and B: Buff the pet whenever you have the chance. He's the only tankish group member that will stay behind to die in a train, and the longer he stays alive the longer you have to get to the zone.
