I'm half a bub from 35th right now...the best places I've found so far for pretty fast experience are:

Mistmoore (CY, and almost anywhere in the castle/garden) - lots of trains, so have someone with evac with you.

Unrest (Tower/ghost/etc)

Kithicor (only at night, so downtime kinda sucks..although you can head to WC and fear-kite griffs and giants with a druid during the day)

Lower Guk (If you're lucky enough to find somewhere uncamped)

Those are the main places that have taken me through lvl 34 so far. I'll make 35 tomorrow I'm sure...probably in Kith.
