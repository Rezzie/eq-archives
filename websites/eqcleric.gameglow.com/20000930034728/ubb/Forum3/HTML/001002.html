The Glacier Bear is a seven hour spawn. Occasionally you get a placeholder of an Ice Boned Skeleton that wanders. Kill it if you see it, then wait for the next spawn. There is a small house with three Ice Boned Skeletons inside and a Rod for a Wizard quest on the floor. If you go from there to the first intersection and wait a bit towards the small house, you are out of range of the wandering lich. The Bear spawns at the intersection and is probably about level 25. The Lich cannot see through invisibility versus undead. The Lich does wander through the intersection, but does not go towards the house. You can watch him and his pet wander by in safety while waiting for the bear. The Bear does not always drop the Ingot. It is supposed to be a one in five chance. I got lucky and only killed two bears and one placeholder before I got the Ingot. I did the Totemic Helm quest before the second Bear, and was able to make two Shamans in my guild very happy. Good luck whatever you decide to do.
