Pickup groups don't exist as such for me anymore, and haven't since at least level 53 when I stopped going to KC. Since then I have always arranged groups through tells then headed out to hunt. If the zone is camped full, then port out to a different zone which isn't. OS is generally first choice, unless you can handle HS; After that is Chardok/Hole. Some groups can also do Velk's Lab, Dragon Necropolis, or Icewell Keep but they need to be solid 55+ groups to do these things.

The solid group formula: cleric, enchanter, dru or wiz, plate tank, 2 melee damage. Sadly there isn't much place left for shamans these days, they can't be main healer and they suck for damage output, and unless you have an awful puller any decent enchanter can handle slows. Ideally I go with clr/enc/dru/war/monk/rog as such a group can go anywhere, do anything, and operate more effectively than any other group composition. If I'm with hardcore players and we're already where we need to be, we even drop the evacer in favor of another rogue or a pet class.

So, as the cleric first thing I look for is an enchanter. Once I have one I look for transportation. Then we add the melee classes since they are far more plentiful than enc/clr and we have ports at the ready. You can get such a group together in 5-30 min depending on the time of day.
