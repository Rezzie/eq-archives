I was the monk who had the privledge of pulling Ixiblat (dang that thing is huge.. the mages with us were drooling!) to the Skyfire zone.

He spawns at neg 2000, pos 1300 when the shell is turned in (make sure the puller isn't standing too close to that loc heh). When agro'd he will still follow even if you get a fair distance ahead, unlike normal Kunark mobs, and he doesn't cause mobs he passes by to agro.

Any class could pull it honestly. Just make sure to use something with some range.. and don't let him get in melee range. The only real trick is trying not to agro other mobs as you run north to Skyfire.

Good luck clerics!
