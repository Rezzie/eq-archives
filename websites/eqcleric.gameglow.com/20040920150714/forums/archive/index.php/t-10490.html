After 3 or 4 weeks of ups and downs in unrest, I finally said enough. I was tired of playing russian roullete with grouping.

Sometimes I would group with smart people and get exp, but other times i would group with dumb or unreliable people and die.

Im level 21 and I decided to attempt soloing in unrest. I decided to keep sow on at all times and only go inside when i was near fom.

I invised myself and went in the house and looked around. I tried to find targets that conned blue and which were not too near to any other nasties.

I also promised myself if I felt threatened at all I would zone. I decided its better to be safe than sorry.

Well soloing went well (is that a good sentence? hehe). I would nuke and melee the target to death then leave house and med.

The last two night I've made 2 1/2 bubbles and Im not tweaked at all. Im poor and barely getting by in regards to money.

I had a druid friend message me and I joined with him last night some. I did this (1) because I liked him and (2) free sow. I would recommend grouping with a druid if the opportunity presents itself.

I also let another level 20 cleric group with me and the druid which also worked well. I would recommend that you never turn away a cleric and always have someone who can cast sow in your group.

The cleric later had a monk friend who he wanted to let join so he joined us as well.

When this group was formed they suggested we do the standard pull from the house routine. I said no to the idea and told them we go in together invised and sowed.

They said ok, but had they not agreed, I felt no pressure to conform since I knew I could solo.

In conclusion, I would recommend that you give soloing a try but also be open to grouping with people who you trust and are helpful to you.

I would recommend against the standard pull from the house routine unless you have alot of trust and confidence in the puller.
