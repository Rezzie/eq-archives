Versus druids...

First thing, invest in some resist gear, FR/MR gear like..

golden/plat wolfs eye jewelery
dorf ringmail tunic (serious )
MM shield or better
froggy crown
FR rings
ect ect

For druid DoT's, use reckless strenght (as mentioned before) as first buff slot, then maybe junk buffs like Jboots, CR/PR/DR, then HP AC Symbol MR FR (this is tailored specifically for druids though...)

The moment you have a dot land on you, dispell it... the only problem is when they have ES arms, but drones of doom is wimpy anyways...

If they try to chain cast nukes, just stand and heal yourself... their nukes are not as efficient as your heals.

Also be sure to dispell their buffs, like SoW dmg shield resists ect so you can root/nuke/whatever much easier.

Someone else described using fear to XP them... well since its been disabled on PvP servers forever I wouldnt know much of its use in PvP but I wouldn't try to intentionally exp your druid friend.. thats lame ;p

The basic problem with PVP combat in EQ is that it was a last-minute addition. EQ's PVP system is very bad and even Verant doesn't try to pretend otherwise.

EQ was meant to be a cuddly PVE game where everyone could all just get along. Instead, Verant has found out (and the other game companies thank them for it) that a full PVE world doesn't work. You need some element of PVP to satisfy some people, and to keep a certain amount of action-consequence in the game...eg allowing players to settle their own issues in the game, and dispense some justice when it's required. I know there's been plenty of times I wished I could just blast somebody (eg that quad-kiting druid who just dropped her mobs on my group, or the guy who tried to scam me in a trade).

Shadowbane is going to be a full-PVP world, but with PVP having potentially serious consequences in the form of bounties, bad reputation and becoming KOS to NPCs. Those who wish to be PKs can be PKs...but they can expect to be treated acordingly (eg unable to enter towns without risk, hunted down like dogs, etc.). DAOC is going to have similar features within its kingdoms, but PVP is going to be an important element in DAOC as kingdoms war against each other...I expect DAOC to be much like Tallon/Vallon Zek in that regard, and Shadowbane to be slightly more akin to Rallos Zek.

Hopefully these measures will help keep the grief PKs under control, and will also help cut down on the people trying to take unfair advantage...no one thinks anything about giving people grief in EQ because you know there's little they can do. In Shadowbane or DAOC, the person giving grief just may die for it. As it should be.
