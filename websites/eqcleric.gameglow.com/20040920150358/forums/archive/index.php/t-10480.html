I have found that I get the best experience when I group with people a couple of levels above me. Even though that means I may not get the most experience, I have found that staying alive and fighting a greater quantity of targets is a better method of gaining experience (especially in Unrest). I don't know if the lower level penalty is balanced by the higher XP mob, but I do know that fighting a large quantity of medium mob's beats a few high quality mob's 99.9% of the time. My opinion: group with people a few levels above and fight till you drop!
