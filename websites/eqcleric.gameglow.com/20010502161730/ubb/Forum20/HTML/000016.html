Ok, I ended up hunting in Temple of Veeshan's test wing Sunday. To sum it up, it was a blast! We went with 3 teams, no they
weren't completely balanced, we had no warriors, only 1 enchanter. We were able to clear quite a number of racnors, wyvverns,
fiery guardians, and guardian ice wurms. It felt like a mini VP raid. We did have one wipe out, but I was bound outside so it
made the CR take all of 5min =).

There is a close nit group of people on BB that are on friendly dragon faction so we have been forming together gathering Yelinak
faction and prepping for ToV. One nice feeling hunting the test wing is we wont be competing for resources. While the Kael
people are starting to make waiting lists and such, we are going to be hunting at our leisure.

I do strongly suggest for people wanting to go there, to get your fire and cold resists high. Everything there so far has an AE fire
or cold based breath. We even ran into a wyvvern that had a HP/mana dot. Some of our people would be sitting with C2 on,
and still not regaining any mana.

I can't wait to go back again. Was a great first time there!
