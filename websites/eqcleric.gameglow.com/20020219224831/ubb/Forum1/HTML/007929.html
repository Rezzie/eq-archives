quote:
4. Gnomish Vanishing Device (GVD) tinkered item. Does a fixed 20 min of both IvU and invis.

This unfortunately is incorrect. The Invisibility Cloak effect of the GVD is identical to your run of the mill Invis. It's been tested and discussed. The rumors are unfortunately false. Anyone feel free to visit the tinker's compendium and add any first-hand experiments.

Everything you wanted to know about the Gnomish Vanishing Device:

1. The Invisibility Cloak effect is a random-duration invis
2. The effect has a 5-second cast time
3. The effect is only regular invis, no invis undead component (unless a rogue uses it due to a quirk in coding)
4. You get 5 charges of Invisibility Cloak per class 5 mana battery
5. The mana batteries cost ~55pp with 105 charisma and indifferent+ faction. ergo, same cost of invis as cloudy potions.
6. Under 185 tinkering skill, there's about a 2% chance of making one; however there's not enough data to make a solid guess--add to this thread on the tinkerer's compendium if you've got any more data. Above 185 skill making a GVD is doable without burning stacks of haze panther eyes.
7. Anyone (all/all) can use and recharge the GVD. It's recharged by placing the GVD and a class 5 mana battery into a deluxe toolbox and clicking combine.
8. Deluxe toolboxes are sold in iceclad, class 5 mana batteries are sold in iceclad and on the first isle in sky from the keymaster.
