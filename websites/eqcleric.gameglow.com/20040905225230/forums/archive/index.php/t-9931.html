I've been helping a public raid every week for a couple of months now break and hold fear - many times at the request at the raid leader. I've had the cleric belt there forever, and I already have my EM boots, so no need for that - the only reason I am there is to help them. Now, I've never expected anything from the raids, but I feel slightly miffed that the raidleader (on a public raid mind you) decided that the ONE time I could have gotten a chance at loot he doesn't need me. I told him next time he needs me for fear to remember today... >(
