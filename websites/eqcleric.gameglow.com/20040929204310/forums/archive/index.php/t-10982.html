We also fought him on his bind. Zland and Velk are nice training mobs to get clerics used to functioning in a ch rotation type process of some sort. hardest thing is to not panic and to not start healing everyone except the MT when the people in your group start dying. To alleviate this, grouping the chain together with a shaman for cure/torpor is a good move and it keeps them from having a group of people when they are in the mentality of having to heal a group. Also makes others control their agro more because they are not with a cleric and expecting to be healed, IMO.

Good luck!
