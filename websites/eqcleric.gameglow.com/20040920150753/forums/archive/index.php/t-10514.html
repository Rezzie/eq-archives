I've been wandering around looking for places to hunt at level 19. I don't typically get a ton of time to play all at once, so I have to have a place where I can kill something now and then, but still be safe if I need to leave the computer for a few.

Dragnor's Cauldron has worked out pretty well so far. I'm bound at the mouth of the canyon to BB, and I pull blue aqua gobs and skeles to near the fab four. If I can't take them myself, the four will help me out. Note that the four don't hit very hard, so it can get pretty tight if a tidal lord decides to attack me after a fight.

If I need to leave the PC, I can just run up the canyon a little ways and med. Very low train risk, though not totally safe if someone picks something up on their way back from Unrest.

Just thought I'd throw this out. If anyone else has suggestions for a good place to hunt at 19, I'd love to hear it. (I've been to Lake of Ill Omen, too...but didn't explore too much there.)

Orcs in Kith.

Yes, I know, what about the nasty undead that wander? Well, from my personal experience (hunted with 3 characters there, pre and post Kith-upgrade), and a co-worker's, if you sit as close to the zone wall as possible, nothing will come even close enough to aggro you.

If you get a partner, or small group, you can really clean up. There are 4 spawns, the highest being level 19. 3 of the spawns have placeholders, which can provide you with time to med and additional safety (no need to lull/mez if all the placeholders are up).

They did nerf the loot a bit, so don't expect to come out of there with a fortune.

I generally use this spot as an alternative to HH, which (provided you get into a group) is also a great spot.
