Last night we had 3 clerics from our guild and another cleric from another guild that needed to do Ixiblat.

We gathered in BW near the SF zone. By the time everybody had gathered it was about 12 noon game time. One of our tanks went to pull Naxot to the SF zone where we had a enchanter waiting to do the charm/mes routine to keep Naxot from despawning.

Before our tank could get to the spawn point, somebody in BW apparently killed Naxot. So our tank goes to the spawn location and waits.

6 pm game time comes and no Naxot spawn. 7 pm and no spawn. Somebody else in the group thinks maybe the spawn time was change to 6 am. So we sit there at the spawn point and wait till 6 am - no spawn. 7 am no spawn.

We are thinking that something is messed up and that maybe we will call the raid off. Finally at 9 am game time, Naxot spawns. Our tank pulls Naxot to the SF zone and our enchanter does the mes/charm on it.

First cleric steps up and hands in the shell.

Poof - Naxot disappears - doesn't run off and try to suicide on Ixiblat - just poofs.

That puzzles us, but we pull Ixiblat and kill it.

Finish killing and looting, then we wait to see if Naxot will respawn at normal game time.

6 pm game time rolls around and no Naxot.

After waiting for about another 20 mins, we decide that we are going to cancel the rest of the raid for the night. Just then, somebody shouts that Naxot has respawned. The game time was somewhere about 8 pm.

We pulled Naxot and had a different enchanter charm it. After the 2nd cleric turned in the shell, Naxot again poofed.

Has anybody else noticed this happening?
