45 is the one level where you're better off soloing than grouping. Something screwy about the way exp is calculated in a group when you're 45...

My suggestion is to try one of the following:

Old lands: Feerrott swamp, spectre by the tree. At 45, I was able to keep up with the spawn, stay at full mana, sometimes snag someone else's snafu as they fled the fear portal area.

Kunark: Dreadlands. Base off the plaza surrounding karnor's castle. I found the southeast corner to be best, but use whatever works for you. Have to be careful of the kiting druids, but otherwise usually quite safe. DON'T use sow -- you outrun them and they go home. Find stonegazer chickens, gnashers, any of the spiders. Open with retribution, run back to the plaza, root, retribution or stun until dead. Just watch your path so you don't attract two instead of just one.

Kunark: Dreadlands. To your right as you exit the FV tunnel, is an undead ruins. Use the standard Banish / Root / Banish technique... just be careful, there is one named different from the rest, rare spawn (forget the name...) -- he is a lot tougher than he looks and will wipe the floor with you. The only thing to watch with these spawns is that you need to stay out of melee, it's tough to get spells off when they are quad attacking (darn monks!) You can generally med safely on the hill just southeast of the ruins -- plus the zone is close if something does aggro on you.

Hope this helps -- once 45 is behind you, it's clear sailing to 51 (and then the hell levels return) http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif
