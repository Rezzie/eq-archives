Revive does NOT restore any lost experience. It merely teleports the player to the corpse.

The player will receive a generic message with the revive that said you have gained some experience from resurrection, but you do not actually gain any lost exp.

Some *claim* to see their experience bar move back up after a revive, but that is just wishful thinking on their part.

Hope that clears it up some for you.
