I like being unique…

I was wandering around Firiona Vie last night, and let me tell you, an Erudite Cleric in Quest Armor, carrying a CGS and a Tome of Spirit, gets some second glances.

For those that don’t know, the Tome of Spirit has that “open book” appearance (not the “closed book” with the little handle, which I think is sort of silly looking) and can be equipped in the _weapon_ hand. It is +6 Wis and +6 Int. It actually belongs to my enchanter friend, but I have more use for it on a day to day basis, so he’s being kind enough to let me borrow it. (He has charisma items so only needs these particular stat boosts when he works on his jewel craft.) Does anyone know how rare this item is? I was only half paying attention when my friend got it from a library somewhere in Kunark and I don’t see it listed on Everlore yet.

Also, does anyone know anything definitive about the max Wisdom being 200? I have read that it is, in fact, the practical limit and there’s no point in going over it, and also that for Wis casters, there is no practical limit so it makes sense to get more Wis items even after you hit 200. I think the high level Wis casters on my server (Brell Serillis) are more inclined to believe that latter, since many of them have crazy amounts of Wis gear; far more than they would need just to get to 200.

While I am afraid that I do not have any information on the Tome, I do know about the wis cap. It is a hard cap where each +wis point over 200 gives you +1 mana. This means that going over 200 wis is more or less uselss - people usually try and swap out some items for either some good AC or +mana items. Its the same for caster and priest classes, and will likely not be changed in the near future.
