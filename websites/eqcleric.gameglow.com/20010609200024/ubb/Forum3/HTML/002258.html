That's a shame you can't wear it.

So...

If you want a BP, try to look for Emerald Dragonscale Tunic. (+20 wis) They are cheaper than the spaulders (they go for around 15k pp on the Rathe, while Imbued Granite Spaulders still command respectable 25~30k) so you should in theory have some money left over even after that. Cheaper alternatives are froglok scale BP and sebilite scale BP. Those alternatives run for much less than Spaulders - they do offer comparable AC, just that wis bonus on them isn't very good.

Hierophant's cloak is something very nice to purchase too, but I don't know your situation well enough to comment on it.
