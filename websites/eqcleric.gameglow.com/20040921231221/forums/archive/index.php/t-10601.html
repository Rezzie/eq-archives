Lower Guk is a great spot for low 30s. Yes, there are some badass mobs right down in the guts of the dungeon, but around the zone and the bedroom everything is 30ish level.

Shin ghoul knights are soloable with about 3 bubs of mana used at 32, and if you can grab a group and head to the bedroom (BR) the xp will just pour in.

Just a word of warning - you tend to get hit by every train, and they are ugly. So if you hear a train call, get the hell out of there.

But there is still great xp from about 30-31 onwards.
