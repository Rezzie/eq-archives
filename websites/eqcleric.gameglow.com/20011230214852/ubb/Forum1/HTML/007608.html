Unrest rocks as well. Good dungeon learning experience if you can get a few similarly minded friends with you.

Just pull stuff to the back left corner behind the back porch and you should be fine. The "Yard Trash" should give you experience up till 19 or so. If you decide to pull stuff from the house, stick to the first floor stuff. The pull order is counter clockwise around downstairs. Start in the nook just inside the side door and then pull things down the hallway to the right and around. All mobs on first floor path clockwise, so if you try to pull the stuff from the hallway in the middle of the house expect it to path back all the way around and past the front door bringing all of its friends with it. Another tidbit of advice for Unrest pulling, do NOT jump the rail on the back porch. If the puller jumps this rail, it causes the mobs to path back through the house and go through the round room and come out of the back door. This also brings a ton of friends so make sure to use the stairs on the side of the porch!

As long as you stay away from the trains near the zone area you should be OK. Unrest is also a great place for a cleric to learn the fine art of root parking to keep adds at bay until you are ready to take them out! Bind yourself just outside the zone and be ready to gate if you must. As a cleric, you can invis undead to scout the zone a little as well as keep your puller invis undead, but beware werebats that see through it. All in all, my favorite zone and a ton of fun!

Good luck and happy hunting,

Depends on how ambitious your group is and if any are Inky's but you could hunt in Neriak 3rd gate. A full group should be able to easily work the spawns, the only problem your going to have is that you have to invis moving through the city and then when moving past the guard in The Baubble, the shop that sells the gems.

The mobs to hunt are ...

3 ghouls that guard the Necro guild
1 Lokar To'Biath, necro
1 Ratraz - Troll warrior, in the bar Maidens Fancy
1 Rogue in Maidens Fance, forget his name.

By the time you kill Ratraz and the rogue the ghouls should be spawning again, except the guard in the Gem store the rest of the mobs are Vendors and wont attack you.

Loot - various junk on ghouls, no surprise there, I dont think the Rogue ever had anything, forget what Lokar had, Ratraz drops a warclub everytime that sells for 6pp if you have good faction with the vendor buying it and he's got a head and bracer for some junk quest that was never any good even when the world didnt have Kunark in it. Ratraz is on a 9min timer.


It's a nice quiet place to hunt, you get 6 spawns to your self that pop just about as fast as you can kill them and you would be surprised at the looks you get from passing Necro's when they see you slaughtering the guild ghouls.
