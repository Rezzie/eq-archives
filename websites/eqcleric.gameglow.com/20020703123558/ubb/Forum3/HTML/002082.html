My experience, am now Indifferent to Kromriff (Frost), Warmly to Kromzek (Storm).

I started out KOS to all Giants. I did not kill any Giants.

I hunted the Coldain camps in East Wastes. After ~30 Coldain I was KOS to Coldain, and Dubious to both Giant Factions.

From my Dwarf killing I had 4 Coldain Heads. I turned these into the Kromriff Recruiter at the Kael/EW zone line, since no one else would give me quests at that faction. The 4 Heads bumped me up to Indifferent with Kromriff, still Dubious to Kromzek.

I then went to Skyshrine, and hunted the Guardians/Sentries/Drakes there for 2-3 days, wound up Kindly with Kromzek.

My conclusion is that killing the Coldain in East Wastes/Great Divide gives fairly small faction hits for Kromriff, and only a token hit for Kromzek. The best way to get Kromriff up is turn in a few heads to the Recruiter. The faction hit is substantial.
