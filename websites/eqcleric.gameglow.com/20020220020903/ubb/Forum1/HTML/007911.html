From my experience there are four factions in Kael Kromzek (storm giant), Kromrif (frost giant), Tormax, and one other faction that's kos to everybody (statue, vindicator). I believe that killing dwarves will give the most amount of kromrif faction, and killing the dragons will give the most amount of kromzek faction. I worked up my faction by farming the dwarves for their heads. Their heads can be turned in to the recruiter for good kromzek kromrif faction. Then turn in dwarve heads to Captain Bvellos for kromrif kromzek and tormax faction. It is very important to get your tormax faction to at least dubious because you have to go past King Tormax's throne to get to the quest givers. The quest givers are on kromzek faction. And just like COV armor quest, you need to be ally to the kromzeks to be able to run the quests. I was able to get from kos to Tormax to amiable in one Dain kill So large targets would definitely help on factions.

1 Question:

Do you care about keeping your Coldain Faction (i.e. Thurg potions)?

If not, kill dwarves in GD for Kromriff faction (all you need is dubious so they don't attack you), and kill in Skyshrine for Kromzek Faction (These are the quest guys). Turning in Coldain Heads to Captain Bvellos in the Kael bank is the easiest way to get your King Tormax faction up. Turning in heads give + to all 3 giant factions, - CoV, and oddly, doesn't affect Coldain at all.

Now, if you don't want to wreck your Coldain faction, you can just kill in Skyshrine to raise Kromzek, then have a non-KOS mage CoH you into the quest giant room to do your quests. ALSO, you STILL can turn in Coldain heads to Captain Bvellos. And you can either buy the heads or kill "a lesser tar goo"'s in the mines of Thurgadin to get heads.

Trust me, Bvellos gives +kromrif, +kromzek, +tormax, and -CoV. The trainer guy gives +kromrif, +kromzek and NEGATIVE Coldain. And while the kromrif hit may be bigger with the trainer, if you want to keep Coldain faction (as the original poster stated), you don't want to do that.
