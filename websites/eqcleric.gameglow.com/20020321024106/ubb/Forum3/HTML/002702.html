Hmm when my cleric was 16 he wore mostly bronze so I guess you're a bit beyond par. Your necro could get a Charred Guardian Shield to replace the crystal webshield. ac and wis are a winning combo
You may have to group, but the rygorr battlemail from the chief in EW makes for a nice chest piece before sebilite scale/EM/whatever. Seb scale caps have ac/wis/str and minor saves and are very very cheap, at least on my server. If you do go to EW, the blacksmith in the same fort as the chief drops Gauntlets of the Black as his common loot (dark ember rare). These are nice ac15 gauntlets that you shouldnt have trouble acquiring. Runed Mithril Bracelets are decent, or if you feel like questing you can easily get 2 bracers of the reverent. These are cleric Temple of Sol Ro quest bracers, ac9 wis5. It sounds like you want to start raising your clerics wisdom just as you have undoubtedly increased your necros intelligence. Replace the moonstone rings with whatever metal jasper rings you can afford, velium of course being the best. Forest loops make good, long lasting earrings, and the Velium Ruby Veil is still the standard face piece.

Good luck

The quick and easy way to get you cleric some gear would be to go to KC one night and watch for rotting Jarcrap gear. It is NoDrop and rots all the time. It is also very light compared to most Cleric armor. Ask the RCY camp to pull the Undead Jailer by taunting him thru the wall. He will drop the legs. The BP seems to drop in the Well area. I have seen the Gloves and Helm drop in RCY & LCY. I think the Wrist, Arms and Boots drop near the Towers.
Also if you can make it to the WL room most likely a Supple Scale Armbands (NoDrop) will be rotting. If you ask nicely someone may even give you a Kunzar Cloak (Drop). Since the sell to vendors for 75pp they do not often rot. Just be ready to die a few times trying to get to the mobs.

Races: Humans, Erudites, Dark Elfs, High Elfs, Half Elfs, Barbarians, Iksar
