It is possible, indeed was sucessful for me to multi-quest the Overking Bathezed singed scroll portion of the Cleric Epic Quest. During the Fight with the Overking Bathezed, all but two died, another character did loot the scroll. We then both went to Omat Vastsea to do the turn in for the Orb of Vapor. She turned in the scroll, I turned in the Ixiblat Fer Staff. It is important to have the other character con the NPC, and then turn in their item first, and you must turn in your item last to obtain the item from Omat. Voila! I received the Orb of Vapor and then immediately turned in the Note to Natashia to Natashia Whitewater for my shimmering pearl to hand to Zordak Ragefire. My enduring gratitude to all who have made this possible.
