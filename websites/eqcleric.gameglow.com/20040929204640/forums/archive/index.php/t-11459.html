Get Turn undead at least 2... Get another person to make a huge undead train (Temple golems, Rocks and worm things are no good.. can't even have 1). Then he runs past you with the 40 undead mobs... make him run them in circles to group them tightly.... when they are like 6 seconds away from you start casting turn undead.... if they pass you, or if they are too far away just duck and try it again.

You can get like 3 blues easy a shot. Then you and the other PC solo till turn pops up again. I can get 7 blues an hour easy with my Paladin friend.

Might want to kill all the rocks around before you pull the train.

Remember to follow the mobs or you won't get exp.
