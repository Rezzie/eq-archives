I frequently visit Unrest, usually when I tire of a support role (such as High Keep -- bandaids, anyone?) and need to blow things to bits.

This morning there were eight people in the zone, made for quite an adventure pulling from the house. Most of the first floor would trail along behind my target, and the second floor pulls almost always ended in an evac.
