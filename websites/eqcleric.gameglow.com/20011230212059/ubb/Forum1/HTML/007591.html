I'm taking the liberty to X-post this on eqnecro, graffe.com and eqcleric, since it concerns us all.

When Kunark was released, it was expected that we'd stay there and level from 50-60, and most did this even though there are some spots in the old world that supports 50+ xp. Spell drops were balanced accordingly.

When Velious was release, casting classes got new spells, but most were not that cool. Now we suddenly had two continents for 50+ xp. And melee loot, as usual, got a huge boost in power in Velious, while casters still have to go to Kunark for spells.

Luclin introduces the same situation a third time. Now we get a 3rd expansion with even cooler loot, some new spells (most rather silly in my opinion but I'm sure some are quite nice). But we STILL need to go to Kunark for Kunark spells, and Velious for Velious spells, while the melees can happily get all the nifty stuff from Luclin, since they automagically get discs when they level.

The effect: its getting increasinly harder to obtain the Kunark dropped spells.

There are many solutions to this problem:

- Verant could simply label Kunark a legacy area and throw us a bone by putting all Kunark spells on merchants.

- Simply increase Kunark spell drops. Velious spells are, I might add, less of a problem since they generally drop more.

- Verant could put Kunark spells on merchants to make it a bit easier for us, and instead pull slightly improved Kunark spells on the Kunark dragons / bosses. I like this idea myself.

- Verant could random spells across several continents, so that we can get Velious spells in Kunark, Kunark spells on Luclin, etc.


Please feedback this issue if you agree.
