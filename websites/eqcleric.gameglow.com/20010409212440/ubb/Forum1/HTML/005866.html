Here are the top ten reasons I will not ally with the Giants and I don't think anyone else should. :P

1. With good dragon faction, you can port into Wakening Lands without trouble from Wuoshi.
2. With good dragon faction, you can port into Cobalt Scar without trouble from the Cobalt Drakes and Wyverns.
3. With good dragon faction, you can cross the Western Wastes to the Temple of Veeshan without trouble from the roving dragons (see a pattern here).
4. Half of Temple of Veeshan is on Claws of Veeshan faction, the other is not on any faction. Just fight in the un-factioned areas (this is where the parts for the Skyshrine quest armor drop anyway).
5. Easier trip through Temple of Veeshan to Plane of Mischief if allied with dragons.
6. Temple of Veeshan quest givers are on Claws of Veeshan faction. My guess would be that they give out some pretty nice stuff. As far as I have been able to determine, all the quest in the Temple of Veeshan are unsolved.
7. Bank and vendors in Skyshrine are more centrally located in my opinion.
8. If you do go on a dragon raid, you can avoid the faction hit by; getting killed (not an unlikely event), stay low on hate list (difficult depending on class), just heal (I have gone on a couple Wuoshi raids where I healed several people but did not get on the hate list), or /quit just before it dies.
9. If you do take a faction hit, there are lots of low level creatures to kill that can reverse the hit. Since it is usually only one dragon you are attacking, there are not several hits to fix.
10. And finally, Giants are Evil. Just ask Mother Tunare.

A few comments on your list;

3. With good dragon faction, you can cross the Western Wastes to the Temple of Veeshan without trouble from the roving dragons (see a pattern here).

Unfortunately, the ice burrowers and wolves aren't as nice, they usually end up being just as much of a pest as the dragons are (Easier to see big dragons than little wolves)

6. Temple of Veeshan quest givers are on Claws of Veeshan faction. My guess would be that they give out some pretty nice stuff. As far as I have been able to determine, all the quest in the Temple of Veeshan are unsolved.

Which leads me to believe they're either broken, and don't work at all, or there is not even an uber guild strong enough to take down the mobs needed (LoS is sided with dragons, and have yet to complete a quest given in ToV)

Also note, Sleepers Tomb is on CoV faction, or at least some of it, allying with the dragons will prevent you from killing almost everything in the zone.

Siding with the giants is a good idea because;
1) Kael is centrally located, without a port, you'll end up running through it once in a while.
2) The best armor in the game comes from the Kael quests (Can argue SS for stats, but I'll take the AC anyday)
3) There's some pretty nice quests from Kael, easily doable by one or two level 50's, including Girdle of Reflection.
4) Dragons drop some pretty nice loot =)

All kidding aside, I really think its best to do whatever your guild/group of friends decides, its not much use having all the options if you can't follow through with them.

1) Actually its alot easier to kill Woushi if you are +Dragon faction than if you are +gaint.

2) Yeap, number one issue that faces most guilds is getting people from point A to point B. Those portals make a huge difference.

3) Not only can you cross western wastes, but you can kill the dragons and a little time in Kael will repair the damage.

4) Kill everything in TOV and then repair faction damage in Kael. Or have a Non-KOS puller bring mobs from the Testing Hall.

5) +Dragon faction will certainly help get to POM but other factors also apply.

6) +Dragon faction allows you to complete all SOV quests with the exception of Kael quests assuming you are hunting in Kael. Even Kael quests can be completed with some effort.

7) Skyshrine is the most centrally located for people hunting in WW, DN, and SG. Not too mention that you get the side benefit of great faction in Thurgadin.

8) Two dragon slayings, two trips to Western Wastes where I killed roughly 40 dragons, one night in TOV and I am Kindly to dragons.

9) One night in Kael seems to make up for two or three nights in TOV or WW.

10) Killing gaints is fun especially for a cleric. Root parking works, Atone works, DA pulling is a blast. Its fun to kill level 50+ mobs that are not 100% MR for a change.
