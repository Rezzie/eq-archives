I would suggest not risking your faction with Opal since you've kept it thus far. Go to the bandit camps and kill bandits until the guards are ok with you. Then turn in gold to Lashun (a couple hundred at most) until you can turn in the sashes you've collected to the Temple of Thunder. Then do K-fish. It's really the best way. Make sure you can get the K-fish quest before you start trying to turn them in, though. If I remember correctly it's somewhere in the high indifferent range.

By the time you finish this you'll have destroyed your bloodsabers faction, but you knew this already. I can't remember in Opal Darkbriar faction is just her or all of her followers as well, but for us dark elves there's no sense in restricting ourselves any further; as I see it we've got it hard enough as it is.

Well anyway, I can get the quest from the Temple of Life now and Opal still looks at me amiably. All I need are the pieces now, hehehe.
