A few easy upgrades on this. =)

Depending on your level, the best helmet upgrade that's non planar is the guardian helm from Skyshrine. But be prepared for goofing up CoV faction (and *TRY* to find a group here sometimes, heh). It's got +5 wis, nice AC and some bonus stats as well.

You need to make friends with a jeweler, btw. Plat Ruby Veil for the face (str 7 wis 7), and the +6 wis rings for the hands. I'd say until you max out your wis you should pay a bit more attention to it, since 80% of our time is spent on our petards medding anyways. =)

Cheap back upgrade is a kunzar cloak for +2 wis and some fire resist, if you can't get a mammoth cloak.

Chest--either a tossup as to whether you want AC or if you want wis. If you want wis, get a bloodstained tunic. They're cheap, they're handy, and they give +4 wis. If you want AC, get an enamelled black breastplate (25 ac) or a Dusty bp (22 ac, but small races only).
