Some acronyms I have seen in this thread:

FT = Flowing Thought (gear with mana regen)
ME = Maiden's Eye (zone in Luclin)
AC = Acrylia Caverns (zone in Luclin)
BAoTR = Blessed Armor of the Risen (level 58 mana regen/ac/hp buff)
Full SS = Full Sky Shrine Armor (Velious Quest)
AA = Alternative Advancement (skills that you get by switching your exp from regular to this)
MGB = MultiGroup Buff (an AA skill)
HT = Harm Touch
FG = Fungus Grove (zone in Luclin)
TS = Twilight Sea (zone in Luclin)
KEI = Koadics Endless Intellect (also c3)

Since they fixed hammer of sun in the last patch, I have worked out a great way of getting exp here.

If you want just a yellow a hour have hammer and don't have FT, all you need is C3, Just cast Epitaph of Life and Hammer right after, sit and med till Epitaph is off, then Recast both. You will stay near FM, and be able to clear 5/6 of them before repops. I can keep 6 clear this way without C3.

If you have the FT/mana and C3, I would say do the same as above but after each hammer use Judgement once, then med till you have to recast Epitaph, hammer, judgement. Doing it this way I had to pull 2 out from near HP room to keep be busy before repops. That gives you 8 skeles in ~15mins which is 2 blues of exp.

Some will get less, if I feel like having fun I can get more, having 6 skeles rooted and doted at the same time is lots of fun, I have gotten up to 12 dead in 15mins, almost died 6 times doing it too ;p

Anyway, I am very happy with the change to the hammer makes soloing so much more fun.

On a side note for clerics, if they want to make it easier for the everyday 60 cleric to solo I think they should give us a lvl 60 Undead DoT, and a new lvl 60 Undead DD. Untill Judgement came out our undead DD was greater then our magic one, we should be able to do much more damage to undead and I hope they add it in.

Found another place where we CAN solo. To be blunt though, it's very similar to the other spots in that it's not efficient but it can be done.

Wakening Lands: Geonid Caves

Figured this one out while working on Shawl 8. I set myself up at the entrance rock and there is 1 spawn to the left and 2 spawns straigh ahead. Anyone who has spent some time here should know the area I'm talking about.

I would pull a Geo with Mark of Retribution and then just proceed to nuke them down in melee range while letting them beat on me. They don't hit, particularly, hard (max of 125) but they do hit fast so MoR really adds up. I just keep mashing Judgement until I channel through (I have Channeling Focus 1 and that seems to help a lot). When I drop to about 2 bubbles of life, I'd root the Geo, back off, and Complete Heal myself. Thing to watch out for, they run so at the end you might need to waste a bit of extra mana on roots before landing the killing shot.

This camp isn't any more efficient than anywhere else that we've discussed. Geo's have low hitpoints (I'd guess around 7 or 8k) so they're not too bad. About 75% of the geo's here are dark blue and the others are light blue. I have a horse and that helps a ton since I'm sitting there taking melee damage. I get an extra tick of med time between Judgements or so.

Experience here is decent. MUCH better on a per kill basis than the Grey. Can't really say what is better over a long period of time though. Loot is simply amazing though. Geo's can drop gems up to Diamonds and they also drop quest gems. Probably not better than hunting Hill Giants in this regard. But for some people, the combination of decent loot AND decent exp at the same time solo, is a great combo.
