There have been 2 major reasons behind the increase in Dawnfires...

The first is the proliferation of epics. Why use a Dawnfire over an epic? Yes, I like to melee a bit but it's rare to find areas to hunt that are filled with undead. PLUS you have to be in a group to use it. Even with 1k AC most exp giving undead mobs would own me long before I could get enough procs to kill a mob. On top of all of this is you need a low risk scenario to melee period.

The second reason is the revamp of Fear has given us more potential drops of Dawnfires more frequently. Pre-revamp, Dawnfires were rare as crap (maybe 1 CT in 20 would drop one).

My 2cp? If you don't have your epic or some equivalent (ie Sacrifice) then Dawnfire is THE best option for your weapon hand.
