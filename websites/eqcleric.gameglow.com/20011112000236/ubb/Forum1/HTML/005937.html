Went to Fear today after the servers came up. Was quite the group and we were plowing through the zone. Draco dies...and he has one of the Terror shields.

Now, in what is probably a sort of unusual thing, this particular guild I was with makes these shields Cleric only on the rolls. So...given one of the clerics there already had one, it was just between me and one other person. So we roll off. I roll an 84. /cheer!....then he rolls an 85. I was honestly laughing. Hail my kind of luck.

Well, we contine to clear the zone and are having a blast doing it. Get to Cazic. Take him down easy and....an Eye of Cazic Thule and Halo of the Enlightened drops! They roll the Eye first to all casters interested...I'm number 5 on the list. They loot master rolls a 6. *sigh*

So then she takes names for the Halo. And again, thanks to having the right clerics along I guess, and the fact that one cleric won something earlier, there are just two of us rolling. So we have to roll off. The other cleric rolls a 25. I'm standing there thinking ... Ya know, if I roll a 24 ...And I rolled a 58. I was stunned.

And lets just say....I'm glad my luck was just that little bit off with Draco. Never thought I'd be wearing such an amazing piece of gear.

And I never thought I'd be able to say "Nope, don't need See Invis"
