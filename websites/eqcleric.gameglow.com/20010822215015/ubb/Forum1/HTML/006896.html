Pretty simple. If a spell (any spell) does no damage to the target, it will not break mez. If damage is inflicted in any form, then the mez is broken. Dispell breaks mez, because well, mez was dispelled.

Only stun that will break the mez is the one with additional damage component. Level 5 stun will not break mez, and neither will stun command. Resists play no part in it either.
