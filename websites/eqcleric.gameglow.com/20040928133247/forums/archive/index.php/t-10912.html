Velketor is great for learning agro control and main tank healing strategies. He hits hard and has a lot of hp, but is slowable. You can make tons of mistakes in the fight and correct them on the fly.

If you do PoG much, you can try fighting a trifling focuser. They don't hit very hard or fast, but they have >1 million HP, so its a very long fight. Goal should be to keep agro on 1 person the entire fight.
You can also practice transfering agro from one tank to the next. Goal is to have it go from hitting one tank to the tank you want to pass agro too without hitting anyone else.

I recommended this to my guild some months ago, but they ignored me, so we learned agro control on Dozekar the Cursed and Vindicator...

If you want ST keys and decent loot, you can do Lendiniara the Keeper in ToV west. Its a very easy fight if you have good agro control and work out the healing on the MT. Its a total raid wipeout if you don't have good agro control or can't deal with the 300pt unresistable AE and dispell of top buff. =)

Different MOBs are different kinds of fights though. There are guilds that destroy Dain, but wipe out on Zlandicar and Velketor, or Destroy Yelinak, but level Dain and CT up repeatedly. My guilds current nemesis is Klandicar2. We are a +CoV guild but pharm Lendiniara the Keeper and Sontalak for ST keys. Klandicar just owns us for some reason though. =/

Roughly I'd class 'ubermobs' of Velious, starting from easiest and moving up:

~24man for those that know them (no huge heroics here either)
Cekenar -> Tukaarak the Warder -> Nanzata the Warder -> Hraashna the Warder -> Sevalak -> 350 Dain -> Velketor -> Telkorenar -> Gozzrem -> Zlandicar -> Dagarn the Destroyer -> Koi'doken -> Lord Feshlak -> Lady Mirinella -> Jorlleag -> Ikatiar

~30man
Statue of RZ -> Dozekar -> Lady Nevederia -> Vindicator -> Lendiniara -> Eashen -> Aaryonar -> Zlexak

~36man
Vulak -> Sontalak -> CT -> Kreizenn -> Klandicar 2.0 -> 425 Dain

~42man
(REAL hard for me to compare Tormax and Yelinak.. Tormax seems harder but maybe having ~3DBs at every Yelinak raid compared to an average of 0 GBs at Tormax can do that)
Tormax -> Yelinak -> Lord Vyemm -> Ventani the Warder

~lots
Avatar of War

Just a sort of estimate. The '24man' category basically means anyone should be able to take them out using ~40 people, and I do mean anyone (ok be 55+ preferably). If you fail it's not because you haven't farmed enough ToV armor, it's because you are doing something horribly and totally wrong.

So basically of those mobs you talked about reaching I'd say 350Dain -> Velketor -> Zland -> CT

Of course the issue is competition as well as difficulty. Dain is still spanked fast on RN for his head, even though pretty much everything else is rotting. Velketor is pretty much 0 competition thing. Zlandicar is probably pretty popular as the easiest source of ST keys and CT drops good stuff for those who haven't farmed the hell out of him already (on RN atleast we've grown tired of PoF already so it's not too rare for CT to live for more than 24h).
