I was in Kaesora last night(again) and I started a superior heal on the main tank. Well, so did the other cleric in the group. Then one of the mobs REALLY started hammering on the mage. I interrupted my own spell by ducking, but it still needed to finish before I could cast again. This led to wasted time, and an very nearly dead mage.

I personally think that casters should be able to "cancel" their casting midstream. This way, there would be less confusion with multiple healers, and, in the case of things like CH, one would be able to cancel the spell if the tank was going down too fast, and cast a remedy or two. There may be drawbacks to this, or features of it that people could exploit, but Im not sure what they would be.

What do you all think?
