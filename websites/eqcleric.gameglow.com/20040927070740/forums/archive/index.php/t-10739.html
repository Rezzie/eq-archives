Have been soloing in Wakening lands for a week or so now, fighting at the Skyshrine zone (nb: you can't bind yourself inside, although you can bind others...odd?).
Most 'raptors are blue, some are exp-green at 45.
Can finish a blue raptor off at 1/2 to 2/5 mana remaining (depending on resists, how often enstill breaks) and greens at about half.
The 'saurs are a bit tougher. Seems like about half of them are higher level/have an upped MR because they resist enstill frequently. I can still take 90% of them with a full load of mana. Six pack optional. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
