Wanted to toss out something other than "I just did it with myself and a level 3 bard" posts. If you haven't been to Chardok for the King yet, take your time. Personally I suggest about 4 or 5 groups (You need a Rogue, an Enchanter or two, a couple of Warriors, a couple of Clerics, and a Monk puller really helps).

The repop in Chardok is fairly fast (about 6 - 10 minutes before the castle and then it's a 25-30 minute repop) so you need to be able to kill things fast and move on.

Patience is a virtue here. Rushed pulls will, inevitably, train you. Let the pullers take there time and learn the pulls, especially if you haven't been here before.

Once you reach the Library, I suggest turning off all Bard songs. I've seen some crazy aggro here due to Bards and their songs. There is a false floor on the right side of the library hall leading to the old Herbalists room. If someone falls down here, they're dead. If they have Bard song on when they fall, you are all dead.

Chain aggro is a nightmare here. It is VERY possible to aggro something in the library and have the aggro pass along to every single Sarnak from the Library to the Queen's room, thus causing a 40+ Sarnak train which includes the King and Queen. Once again, patience is a virtue. Take your time to learn the pulls and learn the aggro. Once you know you have it, then you can go.
