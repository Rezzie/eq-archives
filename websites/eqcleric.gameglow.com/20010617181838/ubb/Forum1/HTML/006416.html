Odds are you will be KOS to Firiona Vie but it's very easy to raise faction. Once you can do so, head to Warsliks Woods and start to kill Goblins in huge quantities. Each Goblin gives positive faction with Firiona Vie but it's a small hit. It's a lot of work, a lot of time, and a lot of goblins. However, you sound like you're willing to put in the effort so you will be able to do it.

Personally it's a good idea to do. I assume that a Cazic worshipping Cleric is liked in the Overthere and eventually you might be trashing that faction (if you spend any time in Karnors Castle, Howling Stones, or Kaesora you will lose faction with Venril Sathir. Venril Sathir is the same faction as the Overthere Outpost). So just get Firiona Vie faction and you can safely bank and sell in Kunark.

I know for a fact anyone can do this since my guildmate is a Iksar Monk and is Warmly to Firiona Vie.

What's your level? If you are 18 - 30, you can get an early jump on getting the faction needed for Firiona Vie by fighting in the Lake of Ill Omen. To reach the Lake from the Overthere, head south to Frontier Mountains. From Frontier Mountains, go South East (just hug the zone wall) to the Lake of Ill Omen.

There are a series of Goblin Mines in the zone which offer very good hunting. Also the Lake is a zone that people tend to congregate into so it's never hard to get a group. Being a Cleric, you will never have a problem as every group feels they need one.
