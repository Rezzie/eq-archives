In West Karana at the Miller's farm, there are a ton of scarecrows. They are blue to me at level 18. I have no problem killing them solo. The loot is meager, but the exp is great and it's very safe.

Sense the dead, run, root, expulse 5 times, loot, med, repeat ad infinitum.
