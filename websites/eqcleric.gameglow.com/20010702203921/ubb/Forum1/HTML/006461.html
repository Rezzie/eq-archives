On most live servers today, ones that have highly developed economies, twinking in the classic sense isn't really necessary.

Some time ago when Fennin Ro was down for a while I created a gnome cleric on Tunare just to see if I could do it untwinked, and because they're adorable in patchwork *grin*. It's not bad at all. There are lots of ways to make fairly quick money if you know about them.

-- save all of your bone chips and sell them to necros fighting in Steamfont or Butcherblock
-- ditto on bat wings
-- become an apprentice tailor and create stacks and stacks of leather padding from the spiderling silks and pelts that you loot while leveling up (smiths pay good money for the convenience of not having to hunt in newbie zones)
-- run little errands for higher level players offering them as quests (but don't beg, blarg)

Only made it to level 11 or 12 I think, before I stopped ever playing her, but I had all of my level 14 spells in the bank, a bullsmasher that I quested for, a chitin shield, patchwork that I made, plus a few scattered banded and other pieces, along with a hundred pp or so in the bank. It was a good base, a heck of a lot better than my character was doing by that point on Fennin.

Gnome clerics are neat =).
