Well I may regret this as it's pretty uncamped right now.

But, Scarlet Desert is amazing! Its close to Katta Castellum if your a goodie, or an easy jaunt to Fungus grove if you have someone to invis you, where you can bank and sell or just a hop away from Shadowhaven.

The XP is mad here and reminds me very much of The Overthere because you can chain pull. Plus, there's some awesome loot to be had here!

My two sons play with me so I always have a group. We have a 45 Warrior, 44 Druid and of course me a 47 Cleric. We have no trouble fighting here. Sometimes we have a couple guild mates join us, another 45 warrior and a 45 Beastlord. When they join us it's amazing how fast we can pull!

Good luck!
