Had another attempt tonight. Got some very useful info. Breaks down as follows.

FD pulling out his gaurds is fairly simple. just have the monk FD on the ramp going up and pull off him there. You can usually get the king alone or with 1 gaurd. Problem is, the king casts Gflux. We were fighting him with 1 of his gaurds charmed (owning him in fact), when he set off a gflux. several ppl hit the ceiling. Immediately after, 5 to 6 more mobs run into his room and wax us. (overking was at the V in over when the last of us died to the train. sigh). My next attempt, we will pull him into the hall before his room after clearing the roamers and gaurds. This should work out better, there are no mobs directly above the hall section of the dungeon. That way a flux wont affect anything (other than your HP total).

his AOE is wuss. did about 2 bubs of damage to the average tank, and he only got it off twice the entire fight (every 30 secs i think, we were hammering him pretty good until the train). Not sure on his melee, never got close to him. Also, as mentioned casts Gflux.

Suggested buffs - GRM, RF, HP buffs, AC buffs. Hes harder than the prince, but deff killable. Just figuring out where to fight him and timing the spawns in the hall, foyer, and his room that makes it difficult. We go after him again next weekend probably. If anyone downs him though in the interm, pls post your strat so i can compare it to mine =)

Ive read of ppl getting to him with 18 ppl, but dont really see how thats feasable unless your all lvl 60 and have perfect group balance. Plan on taking 24 mid 55s. Not so much for the king (i can see 18 ppl owning him easily), but to clear quickly to your target. Getting caught in repop in certain areas can be really ugly if your trying to pull forward.
