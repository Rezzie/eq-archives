The best way to get a particular spell you need is to buy it. Camping Skyfire or Juggs or the various other camps for a *single* spell will more likely than not yield only frustration. Now, if you need lots of 55+ spells, then go for it, because your odds of a needed drop are better.

Naltron's Mark can be purchased for 10k or less on most servers. Given a 30 hours or so of xp grinding you'll net 10k in twink gear + gems + cash + rez $$. Then you can go auctioning for it. Besides, you'll get good xp during those hours.
