I maxed both mine out for a few reasons.. First off I got a staff of the waterwalker in Seb, and did not have an item that gave me enduring breath, and it was 2hb, and decent damage to delay, and I did not have my epic yet, so I wanted to use it for the enduring breath, the damage, and the wisdom increase. I only lost the ac from not having the shield, but with shammie buffs, I could hit 70 damage or so, which I didnt think was bad for a pos high elf.
Secondly I figured that IF I would not get my epic, that down the road Verant may introduce another weapon that could be 2hb, and I would have to start my training on it at a later date.
Lastly, it was fun to work on a skill for awhile, and to melee once and a great while instead of sitting and healing, sitting, etc.
Practically though for me and the equip. that I have ran into, and if you want to choose, then I would recommend maxing 1hb with shield first. Work on the 2hb for fun and possible later use. Any 2hb weapons out there will more and likely be at the high end of the game, and you will have some time to work on it as you progress thru the levels. Dusklar 59th cleric.
