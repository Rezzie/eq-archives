This is actually a fun raid to do - you have to move rather quickly through the Hole to get there, and there are some hairy moments in the city where you can have a few unmezzable golems on you, with a random selection of elementals. Fun

At any rate, if you are mostly low to mid 50s, it takes 3 decent groups to make it to Yael/Nortlav (it can be done with two, but much more risky).

There are two ways to reach him -- one is through the city and down the hall past the Ranger epic quest guy, and take a left just before the undead area; that tunnel leads right down to the Yael area. Alternatively, if you don't want to bother with that you can attempt to jump off the waterfall just off the city area. . .chanced are most of you will make it if you jump correctly Course, if all of your clerics die on the jump, you're pretty screwed.

I would use the maps on Belgar's site ([url]http://home.elp.rr.com/wolfclan/alchemy/alchemyindex.html[/url) -- check out the Shaman Epic Quest section, and read up on the part that details how to get to High Scale Kurn -- only instead of going through the undead area, you will go down the side tunnel. This is a pretty good guide, as the maps show all the spawn points and roamers, as well as the exact path to take to get there.

If you're not totally crazy the best way to get to him is to go into the city and look for the drop-off into the water, invis and take the plunge, then head off to the left and drag the rock golem into the hall with you out of aggro range of Yael. We like to run the whole route because we go down there primarily for ranger/shaman epics, and because it's fun. Just make sure you have a plan for dealing with 4 golems at once.

Fight him with a main tank getting about 15 sec of good aggro before other melees jump in. Heal the heck out of the tank - he doesn't aggro healers too much. I wouldn't count on slowing him, as his MR is very high; I wouldn't try to stun him either, as you'll more likely than not be resisted and you don't want to get aggroed by this guy. I don't know how effective runing is other than to say the only time we had an enchanter live through this fight she was OOM and couldn't rune I do believe the proc is a lifetap though so I would go the overwhelm-with-damage-output route.

He only drops red scales. The others are merely hopeful rumors and have no basis in fact.
