with my lvl 12 cleric, Oranoco, I often hang about newbie zones and heal/buff unsuspecting low-levels, and i've also saved much higher level characters than myself when they pull one mob too many or when they need buffs/heals. Lately it seems i've been getting items and uber-buffs from a _lot_ of random higher levels because "you helped my baby necro the other day" or "you helped my twink finish off that train". The other day i started a new cleric (Erudite of Prexus, again) on Morrel-Thule, where i have no other characters to help her along with. As soon as i had gotten off of the boat and walked through the north Qeynos gate, a player walked up to me and gave me 20pp right off the bat, saying that he loved clerics because they had saved him so often. A few days later, at level 3, I headed out into Qeynos hills with a stack of bat wings, intending to sell them and buy a peice or two of banded with the 20pp, and the lvl 56 druid i sold them to gave me over 1kpp's worth of equipment afterward, switching between all of his characters to find things i could use - and i didn't even know him. It was a "hey, wait here, i've got some things on my other characters that i'd like to give you" type of thing. Will I find this universal respect and appreciation of clerics everywhere? If so, i'm going to be playing this class for a loooong while...it feels good to be appreciated.

I very often give things away to lower levels, especially clerics. I do have a good story though, and here it goes;

I was in GF one day for some reason, sitting at the spires, could have been waiting for a port, but anyways. This level 3 gnome necro comes up to me and asks me to buy him a backpack in Kelethin, because the merchants won't sell to him, he then gives me the 7gp to buy it with. This was pre-kunark, so it was quite a while ago. Anyhow, I went to Kelethin, bought him 8 backpacks, filled one with food and drink, another with booze, and then went to the bank and found anything even remotely useful for a new necro. I went back and gave him all the stuff, I probably threw in a couple hundred plat even.

I was *so* happy for once to have a newbie asking me for a favor that was reasonable, and not "Gimme some plat?" "Got anything i can have?", that I did anything I could at that moment to help the guy out.

Anyhow, some time goes by, 6 or 8 months, and this level 50 gnome necro comes up to me in south ro one day. He gave me like 400plat, and told me how greatful he was that I had helped him when he was level 3.

Normally when I give items to new clerics, I tell them there's a catch, and that when they're done using it, they pass it on to another new cleric. Whether they do it or not, I don't really care, but if they do, that means my helping one person one time, is helping lots of people, all the time.

Just my .02
