MoK is sometimes worth it imho.

For removing dmgshields its only really worth it in a single group fighting easy mobs in my book. The resists on higher end mobs and/or the dmg done by an unchecked dmg shield on 20 melee at 100% haste means you want to recant the mobs fast anyway. But, in a single group with a couple of mobs mezzed and the situation fully under control its good to remove dmg shields from the mezzed mobs for sure.

For the high end game it also has uses - as an example it does stick on Yelinak - so you might as well have those 20-30 melee get a little healing.
