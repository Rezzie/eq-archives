Messy spellbooks kill me. I had to keep mine neat and continue to strive to improve it. I just about die each time I see my fiancee's spell book. (Oracle with no organizational skills! Tunare save me.)

I dont remember just exactly when I started to clean up my book. But this has worked for me:

Pages by numbers on the bottom of the page. Listed by left to right, top to bottom. New=close to my current level, Old=lower(st) level.

Page 1: Buffs I mem daily. (As a 58 cleric that is Heroism, BoF, Blessed Armor of the Risen, Symbol of Marzin, Symbol of Naltron)
Page 2: Heals I mem daily. (Heroic Bond, Celestial Heal, Remedy, Complete Heal, Divine Light)
Page 3: Utility spells I could need to mem in a hurry. (Judgement, Best Undead DD, revivicense, level 5 stun, level 9 root)
Page 4: More utility spells. (Bind, Gate, Revive, Divine Aura, Divine Barrier)

~Note: This makes 99% of the spells I need on a daily basis in the first or second view. Makes death less painful to be able to just mem, mem, mem, flip page, mem, mem, mem, mem, mem.~

Page 5: Resists. (Magic, poison, Fire, Cold, disease; ones capitalized are on the left/center closest to the spell spots.)
Page 6: Bah I forget!
Page 7 & 8: Heals. ALL HEALS not currently in use on a daily basis. (Group heals on top row over both pages, newest to oldest; regular heals bottom row, newest to oldest. In the middle 2 slots I have Death Pact and the oldest heal I have.
Page 9 & 10: Live nukes.
Page 11 & 12: Undead nukes.
Page 13: Summon spells (Abundant food, summon food, Hammer pet, Abundant water, summon water)
Page 14: Direct Fears
Page 15 & 16: Stuns & Roots
Page 17: AE ground spells
Page 18: AE other spells (I have never found those "Word" spells usefull though.)
Page 19: AE Fears
Page 20: Summoned DDs
Page 21 & 22: Misc scribing page.
Back of the book: Old buffs and dumb spells like Halo of Light

I never completely bury a heal or nuke as even the smallest could prove useful. Courage? Spirit armor? Ditched to page 47~50.

I use the pages closest to the front. As you can see I put little importance on the Summoned DDs. This way with just a few simple page flips I get to the group of what I need. Need to chain stun something? Flip to page 15 and mem 3 of them. Dead? Open book to page one, flip once to mem DA/DB/Rez, flip back mem heals.

When I get a new spell I scribe it on the 21st page and then move it to where it should be and adjust the old ones acordingly. I moved them so I filled up my book right to left, bottom to top. Leaving spots open in ("new spell land") upper left.

My spell set up typicaly looks like this:
1. DA
2. DB
3. Cel heal
4. Swap spot/stun/root/nuke/buff
5. Remedy
6. Divine Light
7. Complete Heal
8. Group heal (Heroic bond or Word of vigor) or root/stun.

Yes I dont have any offensive spells loaded. I used to warn people I was totally on defense. Now its just typical me and most people I group with know this. Only on rare occasions do I take down my heals, it has to be something out of the norm to get 4 stuns and a nuke up with only one heal up. The DA and DB NEVER get replaced. EVER. It has saved me enough to just live with 6 spells to swap.

I remapped my keyboard to fit me after 2 years of play.
C = sit/stand (yeah a DAoC habit)
B = spellbook
X = con

I free'd up 2 valuable hotkey pad spots by doing this (5 was sit and 6 was book).

Hotkey pad 1:
1. Remedy (/cast 5, /em bestows a remedy upon %T /g remedy on %T.)
2. Spell Icon 4
3. Spell Icon 8 or Group heal hotkey (/cast 8, /e casts a group heal and hopes all are near, /g group healing.)
4. Divine Light (/cast 6, /em bestows a divine light on %T, /g divine light on %T.)
5. Akkirus pants (Go go 105 group heal! hehe)
6. Shrunked Goblin Skull Earring (Go go clicky Dull Aura insta buff!)
7. Complete heal (/cast 7, /em attempts to completely heal %T, /g complete heal on %T!, /say Complete heal on %T.)
8. Akkirus BP (Free MoK baby! which is good as I dont have the spell.)
9. Celestial heal (/cast 3, /em bestows a celestial heal on %T, /g %T is now healing over time.)
10. Assist.

As a chatty female and a high level raid cleric I make lots of hotkeys. Things like /target chantername /cast 5 ~or~ even /target maintank01. These wind up on page 2 with other silly hotkeys like /ooc The Dragon Lair is camped thank you! (I swear I will never return to Sol B after this quest is over.)

Page 3 has my /loc and sense heading hotkeys as well as /corpse. I found it wise to keep these on a page with NO spells so should I be dragging bodies or lost and invis I dont foolishly uninvis myself by misclicking on a cast instead of /loc.

Not sure that this helps you any, but it works for me. Good luck and dont be afraid to rearrange on those long dull boat rides or rare spawn camps. Welcome to cleric life Abyssilar, welcome.

It will take some time, but goto CastersRealm, the cleric spell page. Systematically go through every spell. Jot down the name, type (rez, DD, PBAE, AE Heal, etc), and spell level. CastersRealm isn't completely accurate on some of the stats of the spells. But I haven't seen any errors for the effect type.

Once you get a rhythm going, shouldn't take more than 2 hours. If you used excel for doing this, now use the auto data sort function. There - catagorizing all done. Now pick which pages they should go on.

Like some others, all my spells are catagorized by level and type. The first 6 pages are the spells that I swap from the 'library' based on how much I use them.
