The 8th ring was so damned easy it was pathetic. Two players, four characters:

60 Monk
60 Cleric
59 SK
55 Druid

The Monk turned in the items. The Druid kited off all the mobs that spawned out front, leaving two giants, and two orcs (including the chief) inside the fort.

The SK and Cleric were parked in the hut. The Monk ran into the hut invis'd, then tagged the Chief and got him solo. We killed the chief in short order, Monk looted head, waited for Garadain to show up and turned in head. In the mean time we watched the neatest show of Dwarves versus Giants/Orcs (the Dwarves ruled!). It was funny watching the Dwarves line up their crossbowmen and take the Giants out one by one.

It was that simple, but admittedly levels are everything.
