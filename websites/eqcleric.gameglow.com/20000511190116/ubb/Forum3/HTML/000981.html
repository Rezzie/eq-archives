The skeletons are easy. The undead Grimrot is low twenties and cake if you have Expulse. The live version is something that the 50's can't solo. Too magic resistant.

I would do the Koalindl quest with a mule to kill the Koalindl fish, camp the mule on the ledge (but still in the water), and then loot with your Cleric. You need very high Warmly faction to do the quest. If you still get faction from giving Lashum Novashine two bone chips, your faction isn't even close. If you can't get faction that way, you are still not there. Do it some more. On the bright side, the cash and experience on the Koalindl fish is decent. The prayer beads you get are nice for low level gifts, and for an immediate heal for emergencies when you can't heal someone in time to save them. Two charges of Healing, but looks like one left after it has been used up.
