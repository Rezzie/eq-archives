Along the river edge in SK going east from the bridge is about 7 bandits. I used Earthquake on them and looted the sashes and money destroyed the rest except food and water. The Bandits are on a 6 min timer or so and with just 2 casts. 1 to get 4 of them and 1 to get the other 3.

I put my bind at Qeynos and would gate back then get a sow at the gates area. I was able to in about an 2 hours bring 3 hand made back packs full sashes.

The faction hit from sashes is great and you get bronze weapons in return. Sell for money or hand out to people at the gate while getting a sow.

Most druids or shaman when they see you giving out free weapons to newbies will give you a sow for free.

The SK camp is a good way to keep an eye on the Grimrot spawn too if you still need him.
