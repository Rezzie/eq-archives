Perhaps they are using the ress buff bug?
Commonly referred to as "Perma-SoW" or "Perma-Floaty", this bug permits you to keep the speed effect of Spirit of the Wolf and the levitate effect of levitate/DMF.

If you have those buffs up and are ressurected while in the same zone as your corpse the buff effects will remain until you zone.

But I think it is more likely that they believe they will get back some XP from the revive...

It's a common misconception that people just don't know what level clerics get certain spells or what spells are available and that revive doesn't bring back exp. I'm currently two bubbles away from 39 and the exp rez available at lvl 39. Until i get lvl 39, whenever i get a rez request. The first thing i say to them is this

"I'm only lvl XX, You won't get exp back, Do you still want a revive?"

More than half the time, the request is withdrawn and they say thank you. When they do want to do the revive it's because of not wanting to run to the corpse, past 3 hours, no exp rez available type of situation. Then I go ahead and do the revive. I end alot of requests for revive when i tell them that you won't get exp back from it.
