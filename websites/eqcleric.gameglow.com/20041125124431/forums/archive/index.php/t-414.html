Probably so, but you may be limited if there are a large number of other healers in the zone.

The Bedroom Loop area would be good for you in LGuk, great Xp. I would suggest basing your camp in the spider room however so as to avoid trains.

At 38, I was almost in the Frenzy room in Lguk as the only healer. It just so happens that a 41 cleric logged in as they were coming to get me, and I understand why they would rather use complete heal, since most people in that camp were 45-50.


Basically, at 35 you can get into groups there with people higher level than you. At 39, you can get into groups WAY higher than you.
