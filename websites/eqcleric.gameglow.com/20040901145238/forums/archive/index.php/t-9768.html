I've played a mage and a cleric both to level 31 so I think I can give some perspective here. (For the record, the mage was my first character, and my current character is the cleric.)

I think it's a good thing that mage pets are cheap as dirt. Unlike a cleric's symbol, a magician is utterly dependent on their pets in many situations throughout most of their levels. Symbols are icing on the cake for a great set of protective wards at the cleric's disposal.

Furthermore, pets path BADLY. No, thats a mistake. Pets path HORRIBLY. You want that pet to be cheap - that way the mage doesn't hesitate to let it go when it decides to path the long/wrong way around in a nasty dungeon. Symbols don't train green mobs all over your party. Symbols don't path. Maybe it would be equivalent if the extra symbol HP was randomly variable over time and decided to go negative on a whim, but it doesn't.

Furthermore, symbols are consistent. You'll always get the same amount of HP buff on a symbol, it will always last the same amount of time. Symbols don't disappear or commit suicide when the cleric dies or zones. Symbols don't commit suicide when the cleric goes invisible, or invisible to undead. Symbols don't decide to sometimes be way below the level of effectiveness it's supposed to be. The cost of the symbol pays for this predictibility and it's worth it. Maybe that top-level 12pp symbol is a little pricey, but the 1pp symbol is still quite useful at level 50 (I reckon, not having been to level 50) even if it's not the absolute best you can cast.

As long as you're not wasteful with your symbols, and use your judgement as to when is appropriate to use them, they won't bankrupt you. What else can you really spend money on at the higher levels? The only things I've spent cash on from level 24-31 are jewelery, bloodstones, food/water (since I'm too lazy to conjure), and newbies. The latter two don't cost very much. And I haven't gotten less jewelery because of the cost of symbol gems.

Symbol of Pinzarm (mid level symbol)= Dam good spell as good or better that Valor

I purchase 40 jaspers and take them to the dungeon of my choice. I always cast on the main puller, sometimes on the secondary tank, and when in very difficult areas everyone including my self.

Then u make sure u get ur share of the loot - this is hard in the L20's (when ur blind)but gets easier in L30's.
