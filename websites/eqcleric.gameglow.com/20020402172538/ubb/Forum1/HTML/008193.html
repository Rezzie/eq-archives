Just wanted to add my 2cp here regarding the roles in the list - specifically mage. I have a 50+ druid/cleric/ranger/mage/chanter/necro. I've played these characters in all situations - grind and raid. The mage list for me is very deceptive. Mages do not stack well in raid situations, so your list is only good for the 1st/primary mage. Pets are a significant issue with many players and are generally restricted in a numbers of places by a lot of raid leaders (Hate,, Chardok, TOV to name the top ones I know of). Until focus items, mages summons have been a joke - "ah, pardon me mr. mage, I just can't live without one of your no rent swords with the crappy stats pls". And finally, CofH has been seriously restricted in Luclin and it appears that policy will continue regarding new/revamped content (Cazic Thule, for example). DS are generally only effectively applied to the MA, so only need one mage for that. So from a raid perspective, your list should read: Modrods/Nukes (when not fighting a high MR/FR mob).

From a group/grind perspective I would agree with the list.
