barb rogue (the warrior)
gnome mage (the druid)
halfling cleric
wood elf bard see bard above)
half elf paladin
high elf enchanter

This group has always rocked -- we sushi the mobs and rip through levels. The key isn't the classes, it's the fact that we all play well together. Everyone has been playing for 2 years, multiple classes. We know what our classes do, we know what each other will do. There's some overlap, 3 of the players are in both groups, and that contributes to our anticipating what everyone will do.

The 2nd group is comprised entirely of real life friends (well, the bard is my husband
the first is 4 real life friends, and avatars of two in-game friends. That helps too.

So, my suggestion is that rather then try to design the 'perfect group' each of you think about what class/race combos you're interested in. Then if someone is vacilating between a couple, you can pick the one that seems to 'balance' things a bit, but any combo will work if you give it time to jell.

Ideal Group for 1-60

1. Cleric:

Best heals, best buffs, xp res. All around core class everyone can use.

2. Enchanter:

Sorry, there is no substitute for C2, a cleric with mana is a group that keeps pulling

Great damage and fast, evade to keep aggro lower and lockpick to get into some nicer areas.

6. Shaman:

Buffs, Slows, DoT's, debuffs, backup healing and SoW. What more could you want? The nifty pet is a bonus for out door zones.

All bases are covered for getting xp safely and quickly anywhere in Norrath, quickly.
