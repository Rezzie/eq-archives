From what I hear (and I haven't been up to sky since the "strip all buffs" patch), the tactic used it to send up one invisible rogue or monk, to scout things out. The spot chosen to fight is near the teleporter, I am told, but I don't know exactly where. The scout should be able to figut it out though.

When the scout thinks it is time, everyone ports up there, while invis, and then wait until an azerack aggros. NO pets until an azerack gets there. Kill the bird as fast as you can, make sure to have demob groups for extra birds (tank heavy groups that takes care of secondary/tertiary targets).

If you're lucky, you will get a few birds dead before geting wiped out and starting over from keymaster island. if you're unlucky you won't. If you're DAMN lucky you get a few, without having more aggros and have time to invis and med up for a second fight. :-)

Note: This is NOT something I have done myself, but it's what I've heard from what i consider to be fairly trustworthy sources.

From what I hear, their Sky runs cost them a LOT of platinum.
