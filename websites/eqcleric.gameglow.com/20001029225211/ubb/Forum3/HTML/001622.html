A few things I've noticed, Verant loves wordplays and to mix words around...

Let's start with the god's involved first
TAREW MARR TAREW = WATER MARR is a mispelling of Mar or sea, then Tarew MAR is Water Sea...

POVAR=VAPOR hence god of mists n vapors...hope this has nothing to do with what vapors issues from backsides of ogres n trolls everywhere....

E'ci is ICE hence mistress of ICE...

Fennin Ro.. Hmm stretch to ON FIRE? with two extra n's.

Omat Vastsea = O I'm at vast sea.... well Tim Deep is a vast sea no?

Ixblat Fer... X to S and FIRE BLAST

Zordak Rage Fire is a Drakoz (Drakes) Rage is Fire. He is a Dragon no doubt or at least a Drake of some sort.

I'm sure there are more out there in other quests too, but this seems mroe on the ends of someone trying to be clever for the sake of being clever instead of creative. Due mostly to the fact that once it's done more than a few times it gets damn predictable and kinda borish.
Ahh well what do we expect for the folks at VI, when one creative spell appears and we know we will enjoy it...it gets relegated to back pages along with True North.

feel free to add others notes like these found in game or quests...and yer thoughts
