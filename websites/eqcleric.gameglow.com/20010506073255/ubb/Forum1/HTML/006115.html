A balance of AC and Wis and then mana and HP.

I may never get to 200 Wis because as my Wis increases i trade out wis gear for AC gear.

Best way to level is hook up with a chanter and tank and, in my opinion, druid, and go to Nurga and Kaesora. When you outlevel kaesora go to Crystal caverns with one tank and do the Geonids. When you hit 46 go to 6th floor of Tower of Frozen shadow. You will level fast.

But leveling fast is not the be all end all of EQ. Having fun is, and you need to learn how to be a good cleric while you are playing. It is the opinion of some people that a pigeon trained to push the right buttons can do a good job as a cleric. I think most people reading these boards would disagree.

You do not bring much to your groupif you powerlevel past the important learning experiences that you need to play through, not read about, as you level.

Good luck.
