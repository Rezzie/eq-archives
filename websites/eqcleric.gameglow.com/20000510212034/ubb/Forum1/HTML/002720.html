Yes, you should explore.

No, it will not be safe.

Most people seem to have been dying a few times while exploring and then getting their experience back with interest once they settle down to hunt.

But how many chances do you get to go find things out for your self, to explore, to look around the corner and wonder what will try to eat your head? The feeling of wonder and peril is rare, and imho is the best part of the game.

My Ranger primary has been poking around for several days, and so far it appears that at our levels most things scowl and most things are fair targets.

I, meanwhile, have been left sitting in Freeport. And I am CRANKY at her for it.

Lynet on E'ci
