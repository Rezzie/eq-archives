This is a brief guide to obtaining your Howling Stones Key.

This quest results in a key labeled "Key to Charisis", which is actually the key to the Howling Stones.

There is a froglok named Durgaz in the Swamp of No Hope who wants a Jade Chodakai prod and a xalgozian fang, and in return will give you a key to his master's lair. He is near the Trakanon's Teeth zone line in a cave. You have to jump up to the ledge to get him. I'm doing this from memory so I don't know his EXACT loc.


The prod drops from the Chancellor of Di'Zok in the Sarnak compound in the Lake of Ill Omen. This is your target :

Go inside and find the large throne room. The Chancellor will spawn every 2 hours ±15 minutes. It is said that he is around level 40. He will drop the Jade prod every time. (refer to map) NOTE - the back door entrance is the upper right portion of the map, front in on the bottom right portion of the map, and the (1) is the location of the Chancellor:

The fang drops from a vampire located deep in the ruins of Kaesora from this guy :

He is located in the church (refer to map)

More detailed portion of tower:

The number (1) is the location of Xalgoz. His Spawn time is 30 minutes exactly. He has a place holder, but drops 2 fangs now or at least one fang with one of his other drops.

It is NOT true that the chancellor drops the prod every time. Seen friends go nuts because they had to sit 3+ spawns there (over 7 hours). Xalgoz is easy even with a 40-50's group, or even a tank gater and you (cleric).

Pre Nerf Prod is useable by all melee classes that have the Piercing Skill. Post Nerf Prod is Shaman only.

If you are going to turn in the two things, get a druid or ranger and wolf form. Also, there are two other frogs in the same room, you should kill them since they a) aggro you and b) he sometimes doesn't give the quest out if they are still alive (though that might be a bug).
