The freeport guards go for a much deserved coffee break and talk about all the silly things newbies did and they sort out all the loot from the kill stealing they did.

The Dwarven Guard sit and have a few beers and play chase the aviak.

In kelethin and Felwithe the wenches get nekkid and dance on the tables for the amusement of all the residents.

Imps are released and they run about counting all the plats in the banks and moving corpses to new places.

The druids chase each other around the rings in games of "Pop goes the Weasel"...how else do little druids get born?

Deep in the under dark of Neriak the Casinos open up and the Halflin Can can girls start dancing...or else.

The ogres and trolls try to get in a quick game of Kick the Frog before they have to get back to work.

The bards sit and write new love songs to each otehr that HAVE to get to the nearest city via FED-PC-Express. Usually the notes are just amusing observations of what they witnesed newbies doing in the town that day or something funny about the mail delivery volunteer.

Most of all The citizens of Norrath enjoy the peace and quiet of no shouts polluting the airwaves...
