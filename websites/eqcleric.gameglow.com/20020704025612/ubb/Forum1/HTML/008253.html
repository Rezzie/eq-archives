The following is a form letter sent to the leaders of the CT community. Please note the highlighted portion.

quote:
This message has been directed to you in order to provide clarity of the
Everquest "Play Nice Policy" regarding spawn disputes. These disputes have
unfortunately reached the point where they interfere with our ability to
provide service to the rest of our server community.
In all cases where a spawn or camp is contested by 2 or more groups,
customers are strongly encouraged and expected to reach an agreement that is
fair and equitable to all parties. Petitioning for a GM or Guide to enforce
a resolution should be a last resort.

Below we have outlined various points to better assist you in interpreting
the Play Nice Policy located at http://everquest.station.sony.com/support/policies/conduct.jsp

1. Length of time "camping" is a customer created rule and not enforced by
SOE.
2. If a GM or Guide is required to enforce a resolution to the dispute, then
all groups who make their presence known and have sufficient force to defeat
the NPC(s) will likely be asked to random, and the group that wins the roll
wins the right to the spawn.
3. All mandates arbitrated by CS Representatives are binding and final.
Failure to abide by the decision rendered at the time of dispute can result
in individuals and / or Guilds receiving a warning.
4. Customers are strongly encouraged to schedule rotations between All
Guilds / Alliances regardless of size that are interested in killing the
NPC(s)
5. Petitioning for a GM or Guide to attempt to "reserve" the next spawn of
the NPC(s) contested may be considered abuse of the petition queue and can
result in warnings being issued.

If customers / guilds continue to be disruptive by refusing to accept the
arrangements made by CS Representatives or not being able to work out
rotations amongst themselves then the source of the conflict can be removed,
if necessary.

It is hoped in the coming days and weeks that all guild leaders and
alliances can meet and work towards a permanent solution. Please relay this
information to all guildmates so they can benefit from the information
contained within as well. Thank you for your time.

Sincerely,

-----------------------------------------
Game Master *GM name removed*

Sony Online Entertainment
*e-mail address removed*
