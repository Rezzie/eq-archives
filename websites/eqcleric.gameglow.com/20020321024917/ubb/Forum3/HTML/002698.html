the best way to take on the overking (and queen believe it of not) is one of two people on charok faction to help scout for the puller.

I'd go for a Cleric - downtime healing and resing but do not buff anyone - Camp out and return before the next set of fights, and/or an enchanter - In the event of agro on the cleric during a detailed CR, the chanter can aoe stun to give the cleric tome to camp out.

Best tips I can give for getting in there is to move fast, rolling raid killing as you go seams to work best for me the 5 or 6 times I have been in there.

because of the complexities of the zone after the re-vamp it's quite important to get the spawn cycle close to the overking under controll. however once you have cleared his room once, you can set up camp in there and farm for ages.

Also once you have the OK under controll you can once again use your non KOS cleric or enchanter to make a solo pull for the Queen much easier.

Get yer puller to pull out the whole room and FD, have yer non KOS hail the queen while the others return to the room.

Once she is alone then have her taged. it's very important that the whole raid sit in the far corner of the room while you wait.

It's always nice to kill two birds with one stone, and the queen is a nasty stage for the wariors epic.

If you are doing the prince, it can be done with just a couple of groups, roll in, and get yer good monk to pull out the prince.

Hope this helps in some way.

Few things:

Non-KOS CotH works well, the top of the steps in the "safe hall" is completely safe, as long as people don't move more than one step down (they can overflow to the sides of the stairwell if needed). Mage goes down, CotH down a hiden/sneaked rogue to open the door and starts calling people down-preferably another CotH mage first to speed things up, followed by "holy trinity" + bard for mana. The people you are bringing down NEED to be disciplined enough to just sit their butts down and not move after they get called, or you're gonna be facing a possibly CR.

Non-KOS people do *not* gain agro from dragging or being hit by AoE-tested this personally.

Our plan for the queen (which we all feel stupid for not thinking of earlier) is to have FD puller agro her up to the secret wall by the old herby room and have the non-KOS person keep her hailed while the others return, then kill off her room and let her return to a welcoming party =) Reason for this is she likes to gate at low health and having her anywhere else but in her room can lead to significant chaos-she gated from King to Queen room and almost caused a wipe-out last time we tried her-me and the non-KOS mage had to jump in and take a couple faction hits to save the raid.
