Ding Again! I'm level 13 now. For those of you in the 11-13 range, check out the Undead Obelisk in Lesser Faydark. Pretty easy to pull one mummy or skele at a time, and good exp. Also, much less busy than Chessboard. Watch for wandering Orcs.

I'd recommend having at least these spells up: Spook Undead (or Fear, you'll mostly be fighting undead and spook casts faster), a healing spell, Root, Ward Undead, Strike, Cure Disease, whatever else seems important (D/A, Gate, etc.)

Good hunting!
