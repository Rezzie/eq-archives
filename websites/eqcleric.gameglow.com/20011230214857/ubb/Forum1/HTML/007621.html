How well I can relate!

I deguilded from my much loved guild when my best friend left because he was unhappy. I left to show support to him.

Honestly, it feels like the obsessive stranglehold that Everquest has had on me is letting up, and I couldn't be happier. I love my cleric but I hate racing home after work because the raid 'cant start without me' and staying up all hours so guildmate X can get Epic Piece Y that he needs. It's even worse now without a tag, because not only do you get the usual rez requests, you get "Hello there, I see you have no guild. May I direct you towards Uber_Guild01?" out of the blue.

I hate that after almost 2 years in the game, the only thing that the majority of players value about me in this game is my Rez-Stick. I wish it wasn't no drop, I'd gladly give it back to the guildmates that sat with me that long weekend.

Don't get me wrong, I love my guildmates (to me they are still guildmates)and they will always be my friends, but i'm tired of being a L60 Raid Cleric. The true happiness I find in EQ right now is playing an immensely twinked level 9 shaman with my friend, and chatting in tells with guildmates on raids.

Oh, and Luclin is yucky. /rude Verant for making an expansion who's zones look like a cross between a mini-golf course and a Dali painting. To me, it doesn't fit the Everquest theme.
