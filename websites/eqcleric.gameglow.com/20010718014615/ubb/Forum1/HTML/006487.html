I've never been on a PvP server, but everything I've heard tells me that we suck.

Stun & Run: We fall prey to the same thing as enchanters. It's easy to get MR equipment and MR spells are easy to resist. It may be better on a new server where people aren't twinked, but don't expect this to work much when you get up in levels.

"Against casters keep healing till they're oom then go in"

I'm sure that they will be in even more demand than normal, but your life will probably be even harder.

Then again, I don't know how many will freely attack clerics. Maybe they figure they will need a rez some day, even if you are on another team. VI probably didn't think to work around that one.

I play a lvl 60 cleric on vallon zek. Yes Clerics sux for 1 on 1 pvp, unless you have lvl advantage but in that case any class rule since lvls means a LOT.

In a group situation you usually run a lot as a cleric. We are an important target to take out early so that means you get a lot of attention from the enemy.

With the added 50% bonus on debuff spells. Getting one you really sux. Tash then malo and goodbye 150 mr. Meaning unless you get them off you you WILL be rooted.

Even though a lot of people now have mr over 200(unbuffed) this is still far from the norm. (on vallon dont know on "normal" servers ..even though we do some of the uber mobs they are still far from farmed in same degree as on most blue servers)


Anyway i will try to heal friends, dispell enemies. Rarely use any other offensive.

I have a few pvp kills mainly from situations where my side got the upper hand though.
