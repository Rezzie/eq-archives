Well I've only done one item quest and a number of exp quests. I was still getting almost a quarter bubble of experience plus 2pp and a endure disease scroll from turning in diseased grizzly pelts to Princess Caulria in Qeynos. This is an awesome quest for teens clerics! I would just hang out there on top of one of the bigger hills in Qeynos Hills and wait for them to spawn. Another one for decent xp and really good money is turning in GLS's to the gypsies in NK. I got 7 1/2pp for each one i turned in, and my total exp at lv 16 was over half a bubble for 7 GLS's. The weapons quest that I did was for the Shining Star of Light. It is mainly just a run around/faction quest, and you get a nice magic 6/28 weapon with an effect of cleanse. Hope this helps.
