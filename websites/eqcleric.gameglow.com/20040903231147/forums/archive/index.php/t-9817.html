This is not really a rant but this just seemed like the place to put this post.

For the last few weeks I have been playing a bard almost exclusively and have semi retired my level 36 cleric.

My bard is level 14, was 15 but died from a nasty train that jumped us while we were fighting a giant skelly. As usual it was just before I was going to log (Funny how that happens).

Anyway, Im having a blast playing the bard and love how the bard can do so many things. Kind of the opposite of my cleric which seems very focused and specialized in his purpose.

Also, there is alot less stress playing my bard than when I played my cleric. Everyone looks to the cleric to keep them alive and I always felt terrible and like a failure if anyone died in my group.

With my bard, Im pretty high on the agro list especially when there is multiple mobs so if anyone dies I will probably already be dead.

One thing Ive noticed while playing my bard is that clerics do command alot of respect when in a group. People usually listen to what they have to say and many times they act as group leader.

I still have a soft spot for clerics and I love to watch how they play their characters. On the whole clerics take healing far more seriously than druids or shamans.

When Im in a group without a cleric I pretty much dont expect heals from the experiences Ive had so far. But with a cleric Im pretty confident that I will get healed.

For the record I realize that many druids and shamans play their class well and do heal but Im a casual player and I dont have the luxury of grouping with knowledgeable people all the time.

In conclusion, for anyone who is maybe feeling a little burnout from the narrow focus of the cleric and not to mention the pressure that comes with the job, I recommend that you explore other classes.

When in groups I noticed how effective a bard can be and chose that class. I also hope to one day play an enchanter. EQ is now fun again and has the feeling of adventure again. Playing my cleric had begun to feel like a job (buff, med, stun, med, heal, so on).

To all the clerics out there I salute you and Im always thrilled when one of you are im my group : ).

P.S. My level 36 cleric is on bristlebane server (the high level overcrowding did contribute to my burnout). My level 14 bard is on Luclin and is very untwinked.
