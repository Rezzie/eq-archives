I was thinking about how one-dimensional clerics are and what possible role they could fill that wouldn't compete with an existing class.

In the DnD system I play in clerics have the ability to "bless" people. That gives you the chance to re-roll something that doesn't go the way you want it. EQ Clerics have that in an extremely limited capacity in terms of the spells that might save a character from death. But they're way underpowered and too expensive to make them remotely useful.

An extension on this might be blessings which could, randomly, prevent some damage up to a certain amount. Randomly go off to prevent a bad spell from affecting you. And don't make it based on charisma... sheesh.

Or... what if clerics had the ability to curse things? Or dispell spells as they are being cast? Or suck mana out of caster mobs? Granted, other classes can do that, but it isn't central to any specific class.

Or, if cheal is given to others priests, how about giving clerics some of their abilities? Like slow!

I don't know about that new-fangled D&D, but back in MY day... :)

Seriously, in AD&D first edition, and I think second, the Bless spell gave the party (it was AE) +1 to hit and saves. (I think it went up as you got higher in levels, but don't remember for sure.) Something like that in EQ would seem appropriate for Clerics. A line of spells that adds to Offense, basically. Maybe Defense, too, instead of saves (we already get endures and resists). It would be different and unique as far as I know... especially if you don't like wolf-ing out. (Mind you, I love wolfing out and wish there was an item that let me do it :)

I think our light spell should be castable on others. In this case, not making the Halo NO DROP.

We've all wanted Sanctuary to work like it does in D&D. You cast it, and things just dont' attack you. Would be easy, too, cause you could just make it work like invis, but players can see you.

Why don't we get see invisible, anyway? Shouldn't we get a high level true site spells that lets us see past illusions and invis?

I don't think cursing is the way to go. It doesn't seem in line for clerics in EQ. Unless...

There really should be some level of cross-over spells for clerics in the way of deity specific spells. Apart from Embue spells, there really is no difference between a cleric of the All Mother and a cleric of Hate. Not much, mind you, but enough that being a cleric of the deity give you something different. Curses for the evil gods, nature spells for Tunare worshipers, enchanting clay and steel for Brell worshippers, etc.

And how about a spell that lets you port back to your temple when you get to level 20... oh wait... we were slapped down hard with that one.

And one other thing. What about a special skill for clerics: Fasting. To train you have to go hungry. It should go up fairly quickly, though, like alcohol tolerance, or perhaps the speed of meditation. The skill would simply add to your meditation skill for gaining mana back. And when you were actually hungry, you could gain mana back at Fasting speed.

Just some thoughts.
