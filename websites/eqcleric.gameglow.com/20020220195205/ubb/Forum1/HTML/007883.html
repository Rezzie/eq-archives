Yes, you can camp without agroing the FG's, but you do have to be careful.

Once you are on the ledge where Ragefire spawns you'll see that at the far point from his spawn there is a slight overhag where you have to duck, that spot is pretty safe.

Danger times are when the FG's are spawning, and when they are being pulled.

*Don't* sit if the FG's are being pulled and watch for the wandering FG's that walk down to the front entrance, they can agro if you are sitting in the wrong spot, obviously level helps, but I just wouldn't sit for safety.
