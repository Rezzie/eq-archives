Its been said over and over-you do NOT need 4K mana to combine Mass Buff with Blessing of Aego.
The skill uses at a minimum the cost of the spell (2K mana) and at a *maximum* spell cost *2 (UP TO 4K mana). If you don't have the mana to support spell cost *2, it uses up whatever you have.
I'll repeat that, cause it's important. Minumum (as in, the absolute lowest amount of mana you can have for it to cast) = 2K mana, Maximum (as in, the MOST it will use, but not nessicarily how much you need to have for it to fire) = 4K.

So if you have exactly 2000 mana when you use it, it's going to use 2000 mana and fire, leaving you OOM. If you have 2500 mana when you cast it, it's going to use 2500 mana and fire, leaving you OOM. If you have 4500 mana when you cast it, it's going to use 4000 mana and fire, leaving you with 500 mana.
