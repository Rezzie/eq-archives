[skipping the fact that she would cast Greater Healing on my target after I had cast Complete Healing...not just cast it, but it was already moving the life bar up..was driving me nuts].

was way too expensive for this. Didn't matter one whit. That was her spell in 95% of the situtations.

Drove me nuts.

I use CH when my target is a tank who is between 2 and 3 bubbles of life. If it is a speed pounding mob I won't use it at all, unless I am full up and want to cast it early. I use superior healing on my casters, simply because I only want 3 heals up and greater just smacks of boring.;-)

Anyway, I love my celestial healing, but it ain't ALL THAT..at least not in every situation.
