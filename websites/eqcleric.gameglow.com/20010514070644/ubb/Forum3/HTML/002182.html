Reinforcements for the Tunarean Regiment is a Kael quest that results in one of three possible rewards redeemable by turning in a voucher to one of three giants. The rewards are Helssen's Prismatic Trinket for casters, Collar of the Storm for melee, and Girdle of Reflection for healers. The girdle is ac12 wis6 hp35, which makes it somewhat better than the Girdle of the Diligent from PoF.

For the last couple of weeks the quest has been disabled. It is possible to do the quest up to the point of getting the voucher, but attempting to redeem the voucher results in the three giants reporting that they are out of rewards and to come back another time. The voucher is returned to you.

I spoke with a GM who spoke with a lead GM. They have been deluged with questions about the status of this quest. They are hoping some announcement will be made at the time of the next patch.
