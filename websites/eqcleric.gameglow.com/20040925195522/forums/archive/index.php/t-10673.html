I solo Kithicor. Stay on the lower level (Warrior, like Wandering Warriors, Decaying Footman, Decaying Troopers) MOBS and you will do ok. Stay away from Decaying Commanders, Rotting Knights, Risen Commanders. The higher ones just take SO much mana. I root and nuke. Fight near the walls. Don't pull from very deep or you will be in for nasty suprise.

I like to keep Panic the Dead memmed. If you don't, you will be pissed off when another undead joins tha battle when you have the 1st MOB 80% dead.

The xp will not be as good as finding a group in LGuk or SolB.
