Without some rather uber upgrades from the likes of ToV, it will be very difficult to get your "Big 3" resists over 100, maintain 200 wis, AND keep your buffed AC at 1k+

Like others have suggested, sacrafice the AC. If a ToV mob is gonna beat on you, there isn't a whole lot of difference between 900+ AC and 1000+ AC.

Your goal should be to sacrafice yourself down to about 183 in wisdom as high level enchanter buffs (i believe) add a total of 17wis.

Some ideas (some already mentioned b4):

WW dragon talisman (take the hit and just repair the faction)
Lodizal Shield
Talisman of Benevolence (a must for dragon aligned people)
Paebela Fetish (range or primary)
Frog Crown (20 fr/cr) or Black Alloy Crown (5svAll)

Ditch the donals bracer and boots for 1 velium blue diamond bracer and either tribal war boots (chardok, stats listed above) or take a dwarf faction hit and get a pair of Boots of the Huntsman (15ac 5wis 5int 5cr).

In full resist gear I have these stats:
947ac (buffed)
100 dr
112 mr
100 pr
159 fr
188 cr

180 wis (+mana from the blue diamond items still give me an effective wisdom of over 100)

I am far from uber, infact in max AC gear i am only at 996 self buffed with 207wis at level 57. In several excursions to ToV if i got hit it was because I was purposely aggroing during a likely wipeout so an epic cleric could get camped (note: did not hit NToV, only Kael armor side 'west wing')

2cp for ya
