Hail Fellow Clerics,

I beseech you all to petition and feedback in game about how wrong Verant has made the cleric epic final part.

Basically it boils down to this...Verant split the final portion of the epic where you used to have to camp Sol B to get Zordak in Human form to spawn after Naggy had been put down...You would have to usually camp at least 2 to 3 days sometimes more just for your turn at Zordak as long as patch or Naggy didn't spawn and kick you out if you over 52...otherwise Naggy ate yas. This camp time did not include the camp time you had to spend with cleric before you in line. Prexus was honoring the camp as long as you were in zone and in line.

After the patch the Wednesday before last, they moved Zordak in Sol B to an easier location but didn't really change spawn time as they had stated in patch message. It took the cleric before me a little over 3 days and it took me 79 hours and 30 minutes. The Sol B Zordak was just Human merchant that trades old pearl (was old trigger piece) for a new swirling pearl. Zordak poofs as he gives you pearl and says he will return from Skyfire to kill blah blah blah...So then off to Skyfire where the new aggro version of 55 Zordak merchant spawns...He aggros on anyone that gets too close and has kinda large aggro range from reports. Once killed he spawns into Ragefire (fire dragon like Naggy with same loot). Kill Rage and get Naggy random loots plus new last piece for epic...Zordak's heart container that is just no drop. You must then place swirling pearl into container and combine for Zordak Heart which must then be turned in to Omat in TD. Omat give cleric final orb...3 orbs then make clicky stick...56 rezzer.

The ugliness has already started...Only three clerics had swirling pearl on Prexus when I wrote this message...Glorund, Dourgas and me. Dourgas managed to stay on camp after he completed Sol B part...Early Friday morning around 3am...Dourgas killed and got container...combined into heart...yeah Dourgas! Glorund and I weren't bothering to camp yet. Glorund had posted on Prexus that he was planning to camp this weekend and I was planning to be away this weekend so no prob there. Problem is another cleric decided since no cleric was there to claim camp, she would camp SF for Zordak even though she has no pearl ready to combine. Some might say well she is just gathering parts for her epic well let me put it this way...Anyone can loot this container in SF as is now. A level 1 cleric from an uber guild could plop is ass down and camp...call uber guild in for strike and loot container...as cleric is just collecting pieces for epic. This cleric told me she was camping because Verant's poor programming has allowed her to do this. What a weak minded answer...That really got me pissed after having spent 79+ hours to be told so sorry Verant's error allows me to do this...

This is so wrong to clerics who are doing raid in order and have spent countless hours in Sol B already. Verant has now made the one old hell camp into 2 hell camps with a FCFS setup in the making. This container must be made NO RENT as a start...Another better change would be to make Zordak triggerable like druids Fay... ie Give the swirling pearl to human Zordak in SF...he gives back and Ragefire spawns to try and eat you...like Fay. Lastly and least preferable would be to also remove all dragon loot except NO RENT container.

There is nothing wrong with having to camp in line with epic ready clerics on last piece. A no rent container would almost assure this to be the case. Please tell your friends and ask them to let thier guilds know we must stop this FCFS mentality before it makes for more chaos and hate in the game. Have them flood /feedback channels with demands that Verant make changes....Petition and ask GMs why they aren't fixing this problem before it really gets ugly.

Thanks for your time and I hope I didn't rant too much but yes Baggy is upset by this...
