Sounds a bit rough to me. At your level you can't expect to reliably channel through his hits. Monks have no bashes though so with high channeling they won't interrupt you that much.

Monks are weight limited but if this guy is really twinked he'll probably have rather high MR (plat. jacinth rings etc) and then you can't expect to win unless you can maintain a damage shield on yourself.

If he doesn't have that high MR, just root and nuke him. Make sure you have a lot of wisdom and AC. You can start with much wisdom and when you have burned away some mana, swap for AC gear. Monks have less HPs and thus this tactic should work. At 34-35, Wrath is still a decent nuke.

My tactics against monks is to let my necromancer friend cast splurt and envenomed bolt on them while I cast divine barrier :-).
