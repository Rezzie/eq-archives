My guild killed Gorenaire yesterday and, being at the Burning Woods zoneline, I decided it'd be logical to ask whether we could take down Ixiblat after she fell. It was 10pm at the time and I knew Naxot would despawn at 6am, so we had to hurry.

I went to Firiona Vie, nabbed the Ornate Sea Shell and headed back to the zone to see if she was up. At this point, it was now 1am and she was, in fact, still up. Yay! Now I just sit here and wait for the call and we'll take Ixiblat down...

Three AM comes and we're still buffing... "hurry up, we've only got 3 more game hours to do this." Then comes 4am.

Naxot left my target window completely.

You tell your guild, 'um... Naxot just left the target window. She's not at her spawn spot, either.'

Then come all the tells asking me where I was and whether I actually knew my own quest (this is almost as bad as asking someone for a heal in a group) so I explained to everyone that yes, she HAD despawned two game hours earlier than the walkthrough read and no, no one had killed her.

No worries, though. She'll spawn again in an hour and this will give us ample time to correctly buff up for the fight anyway. So I sit at her spawn spot. Since she despawned at 4am, I waited for 4pm. Nothing. Ok, so maybe she's back to her 6pm spawn... nothing.

We ended up waiting until 10pm that night for the scaley blue lady to spawn. Talk about misleading... if they changed her spawn time, we should update our walkthrough so people don't get thrown a curveball like I did.

Oh, and she despawned after I gave her the shell, and although this has been verified and I knew it would happen, it's not reflected on the walkthrough.
