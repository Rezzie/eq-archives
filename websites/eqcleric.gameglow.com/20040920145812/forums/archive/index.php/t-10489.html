I have been hunting Aviaks at Aviak Camp in South Karana from level 18 to level 23.

Even though Aviak Camp is always camped there always seems to be another room for a cleric and I have never had troubles finding a group to join and get some good experience.

At level 22 I could solo the Aviak Darters without trouble and they gave me fairly good experience (around 1/10th of a bub, I think). In a good group that gets lucky and keep the pulls coming you can get up to 1 bub of xp every hour - but normally its more like 1 bub of xp for every 1 1/2 hour - 2 hours.
