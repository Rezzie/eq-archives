Ok I guess I was a little terse. Here's the fuller version:

Zland - zerg fight, like Trak... too much damage output and too little HP for anything more organized. No point in CH rotation on this one. Just spam heal whoever is getting hit.

Kland - Stick 2 clerics on a rotation, have someone assigned to heal them if they get low health. Keep MT on one side and have everyone else hit from behind, if at all possible. Load up on MR gear to prevent fear.

Lendi - 3 cleric rotation, go straight HP/mana on this one. Longer fight than Kland/Zland but not too much more difficult. Group healing from clerics/paladins/necros is the way to keep everyone alive.

Yelinak - 4 cleric rotation with a healthy mod rod supply. Can be a pretty long fight, keep MT between Yelinak and the gate. Keep the gate closed. Position CH rot in between gate and MT, so when the knockback hits the clerics will stay in range. All other melee on the other side.

Sontalaak - Another spam heal fight. Like Lendi in that you'll take a good deal of AE damage so group healers are the way to go. Like Zland in that his damage output is absurd and you have to zerg him fast before AE takes you all out. Assist, spam, assist, spam, assist, spam. If this fight takes more than 2 minutes you will be doing CR. The difference between a total wipeout and killing him with no deaths can be tiny.

'spam heal' = chain cast Divine Light on whoever is getting hit, make an 'assist Zlandicar' key or something to that effect.

'zerg fight' = don't bother to try and control aggro, every moment you're not doing maximum damage is a moment closer to losing the fight. Just have your casters nuke from melee range so the mob doesn't jump around.

Also make sure everyone has right-clicky items to counteract the effect of AE dispels.
