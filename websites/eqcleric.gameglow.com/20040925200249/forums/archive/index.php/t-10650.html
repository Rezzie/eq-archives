Ok. I just have to tell you all this.

I absolutly flied through these lvls. I was getting a level per night (non hell) with my regular group.

Where: Overthere, park it in a nice spot to the left (with back to FM zone) of the 'ledge'.

Who: You group consists of ONLY 3 people. That is the importnant part, just 3. Yourself and two tanks. I know it's tempting to add a druid, but just use SoW potions. We were killing them too fast to have to worry about them running much. Now the two tanks i used were 2 warriors about 2 lvls above me, dont get much higher, as they will suck up more xp. These 2 really took the damage well, and get hit for alot less since thier AC is wonderful.

What: Anything, pull anything that moves, Unless it's a goon of course. then haul it to zone.

There you have it. The formula to speed through your mid to upper 30's

in the upper 30's move to the SF zoneline, we found higher lvl mobs there, but more of a chance for goon aggro's
