I was stuck on my quest for months thanks to a bug. Natasha killed herself when I spawned her with the priest robe to Omat /sigh

Last week I was helping a guildie get the first parts of the quest done and we both headed to TD. I had hoped that once my friend passed in her things that I would have Natasha to myself and could give her my ring and get back on track. Then I realized that she was only at the first stage and that she would not be spawning Natasha with what she had. Then we couldn't find the loc and she got jumped by something and gated out. I went on in anyway out of frustration more than anything else I guess; I get to the Inn and Natasha was just standing there. After I finished freaking out I gave her the ring and got my shell and now I'm back on track.

So I guess you have two options and for both you will need another ring and you will need to kill ixi again, which means there will be an extra scepter floating around.

1) Sit in TD and check in every now and again to see if you can get lucky.
2) Find a friend on the server that will be spawning Natasha and go to TD with them and hope that she doesn't despawn when they pass in there components (again luck).

fyi Natasha despawned as soon as I gave her my ring

Good luck.
