Given that the Best of the Best tournament is a PvP event, and not one focusing on normal play on most servers this would not likely be an idea they would implement, but here goes.

Setup: Cleric is grouped with 5 other GM controlled players of the following classes:

Warrior
Rogue
Monk
Enchanter
Wizard

All level 60, wearing standard gear for their levels. Cleric may buff before combat, once the cleric has indicated that buffs are done, their mana is put at 100% and 3 level 55 mobs are spawned. If the cleric survives, they are given 2 minutes, and then 2 level 60 mobs are spawned. Again if they survive, they have another 2 minutes and one 63rd level mob is spawned. All mobs are undead. If the cleric dies in any of these rounds, they cannot move on to the next round. Combat ends on the following 3 conditions:

1) The Mob(s) are dead.
2) The cleric dies.
3) The rest of the clerics group is dead.

Each round is scored on the follow criteria:

1) Number of group members standing
2) Total HP remaining in entire group, clerics hp are not counted.
3) Aggro management
4) Crowd Control
5) Damage Done by Cleric

I'd say thats a relatively fair weighting of cleric abilities.
