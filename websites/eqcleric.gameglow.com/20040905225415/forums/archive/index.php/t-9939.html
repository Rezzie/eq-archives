OK, I have a 25 cleric on Cazic-Thule server. I had her on the shelf for a few months while I gathered equipment for her. Finally I get her re-outfitted and head for SK.

I'm shouting in SK for a group, no takers. After about 20 minutes, I get invited into a Paw group. I was 23 at the time. Yes, that was very low for the dungeon, but they said that the 29 shammy would heal if I would chain-stun, so I thought that would be cool. Once I hit 24, they said, I could take over heals because I would have Greater Healing then.

So for about 3 hours we're killing gnolls in Paw. It's going great, no one even comes near to dying. I top off heals when we're resting. Finally we invite this 30 rogue into the group, and we're killing more stuff just fine.

The shammy has to leave, finally. I say "ok, I hit 24 in 3 blues, and then I'll have Greater Heal and everything will be fine. We'll be ok until then."

Next thing I know, I'm reading the rogue's words: "I'm not gonna pull cuz we don't have a good healer." Um, excuse me? I'm your ONLY healer, and I can keep you alive until I hit 24 in oh, about half an hour. But nope, he wasn't having it. So I disbanded and left, telling them they could put a "good healer" in my spot. An hour later I was in SK at the spires and still seeing one of them shouting for a healer for a Paw group.

Later that night, I'm grouped at North Spire. We're doing great, I've hit 24 and am Greater Healing with impunity. After about 2 hours, the main tank has to leave. We're getting ready to shout for a new tank, when the shammy (who was level 31) says, "I have a 35 cleric coming."

What the hell?? I'm keeping everyone healed quite nicely, thank you, and this putz wants a 35 cleric who will get crappy exp grouping with them? Needless to say I felt I was being kicked out of the group again and left.

What is up with these people??? I'm a kick-ass little cleric, I do my job well. They all saw that, and they still think I suck. /sigh

/rant off
