Well, to put it simply, there is a difference between abbreviating "rezzurection" to "rez" and "spirit of wolf" to sow and between abbrevating "please" to "plz" and "thanks" to "thx".

First, rezzurection is a pain to type, as is spirit of wolf. Lotsa letters, easy to misspell. Please and thanks are short words, and it isn't at all difficult to type 6 letters instead of 3.

Second, at least the way I see it, rez and sow are descriptions of what you need. If you can skimp on them and still get your point across, than I say go for it. Please and thanks, on the other hand, and courtesy words; skimping on them means something like skimping on the courtesy itself...

Besides, remember, when asking for a rez you're asking a stranger to do you a favor. You may as well put your best foot forward.
