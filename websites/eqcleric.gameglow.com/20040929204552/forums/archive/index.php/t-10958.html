Dont do Arena before doing Telk/Gozz http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif Do it after to fix faction, dont let people /q out to save faction hit, make them take the hit, all to often a raid will wipe because everyone wanted to avoid the hit and all of a sudden at the end you dont have enough people to finish off the dragon and then it regens faster than you can do damage.


Know the comparisons of the SS vs Thurg and use your gems accordingly. As long as your going to be working in the Arena it's safe to say that you'll be getting max ally to CoV and SS is often much nicer armor. It's light years ahead Kunark/Olde World. That doesnt mean you should let Thurg stuff rot, but since you might have people with much more limited play time, it's a lot easier for them to run armor quests at Kindly in Thurgadin then your die hard players to get Ally to Skyshrine and do the HoT armor quests. Takes a bit longer to get the SS, but it's worth it overall and everyone gets nice loots in the end.

Dropping VtS/Vindi/The Va`Dyn and doing the HP cycle are going to be immediate pay offs for you because the loot can be very nice for where your guild/alliance is right now.


Good luck!
