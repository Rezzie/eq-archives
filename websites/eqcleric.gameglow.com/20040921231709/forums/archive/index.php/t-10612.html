Soloing isnt that rough if you are a Dwarven Cleric. We start out with good strength and ac, and as you get equipped you can end up withstanding a ton of damage. My solo approach is generally Nuke and Bash. Melee them and add a nuke everytime it pops. If you are taking too much damage throw in a Stun and if it gets to looking really bad root and step outta swing range either to heal yourself, med, or gate out. Generally it isnt too rough to solo anything blue, but yellows at this lvl become a nightmare. If it is a caster have both of your stuns up and chain stun them.
