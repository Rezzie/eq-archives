I am in much the same situation. Until recently, I lived in Europe and pre-49 had only been on one planes raid. (most of these raids get started at 3am over there) I also have mostly gold quest armor, with Donal's arms. I am in the same small guild that I have been in since lvl 12.

Now that I am back in the states, I have taken to camping in SRo. I check the raid calendar on my server's web page frequently, and when I think there's a chance I go show up in SRo at about 7pm EST (or about 1/2 hour prior to whenever the raid is starting), find the raid leader, and ask if another cleric would be welcome. I also sign up on the host guild's web page whenever possible.

If no, I shrug and go on my merry way, hopefully to get more xp that night. No harm in asking, and you're not going to get anything without persistence in this game.

One thing about being picked up like that- if they are hurting for clerics to go to hate, they are probably hurting for people in general. That raid may not have the 'horses' to be successful. Then again, you can't win if you don't play. Might as well go up and learn a few lessons about wiping out.

I also try to go to PoF too- Not so much for the loot as for the contacts and getting to know people.

To get Armour, at level 51 and your warrior friend at 48, you can go to Karnors.

Some people love it, most people hate it, but the drops are nice.

Drolvarg Warlord drops Donals Boots.

Cursed Hands drop Donals Gaunts

As for your warrior friend

Cobalt Boots from Sentry of Sathir (or Caller of Sathir, cant remember)
