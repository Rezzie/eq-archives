Last night I was going to meet a friend and help him with part of a quest in Erud's Crossing. So, I went to hope the boat from Erudin...

I got on the skiff and went to the boat. The boat left the arbor at about the same time the skiff left the dock, so I had to wait for the next boat.

When the next boat came (an agony of waiting), as soon as it pulled up I steped forward... and was turned around and dumped into the harbor. There was *no* chance at all of my getting on the boat. And there was a shark in the harbor to boot.

I reported the bug (feedback form), petitioned, and logged out.

I was very upset. I had waited a very long time in the game for a boat, which annoyed me to no end. I avoid public transportation in real life becuase I don't like waiting 20 minutes for a bus/train. And it was completely broken, to boot, so all my waiting and I'm still trapped on that continent.

I wouldn't have minded so much if they had their message of the day when you log in be something useful: like "the boat from Odus is broken." Instead thay have a message whoring their new service of name changing. Or if they changed the message the mythical harbor master says to "You are out of luck, /join taxi:taxi and beg for a port in Toxxulia Forest." I might noy have noticed it, but then it woudl just have been my fault for not paying attention.

I'm growing to hate boat rides a lot in EverQuest. They can interesting the first time, but in general they are long, drawn out, and boring. Waiting 20 minutes or so for a slow boat is just way too painful. I'd rather more slowly swim the entire way -- unsow'd -- just to relieve the bordom, but then you can't zone while not in a boat. Aren't video games supposed to be interesting and fun without having to resort to /gems?
