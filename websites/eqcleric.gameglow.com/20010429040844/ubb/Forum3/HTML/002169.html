Three sharks sounds like the right room. It's the room above the "well" on the way to Phinny. Large, square room with four pillars and an exit you go through to the patriarch room and beyond to the right towards Phinny.

We hung out on the side farthest from the exit and one shark would spawn above, two below. The times the frenzied cauldron shark spawned (which was maybe 2-3 times out of a few weeks off and on hanging out there) he spawned as the top shark.

I never got it from outright camping it, just sheer luck during a Phinny raid. Been a while but I think it was the same day as a 10-hour session like yours where we didn't see him at all.
