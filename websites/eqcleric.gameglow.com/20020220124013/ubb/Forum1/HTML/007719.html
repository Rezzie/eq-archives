Was sitting at the fear portal killing the necro lass who respawns every 6 minutes and is pretty good exp until level 54, I was around level 52-53 at the time.

When a guild collects outside. Theres a fair amount of em, they all rush in, I don't hear anything for a while.

15 or so mins later, lots of naked people start collecting at the portal. I think ops, didn't go too well, hope they do better next time.

Well they rush in, and low and behold the guild gets wiped out again.

Now this was back in the days when there were no roamers on the north wall. So I ask em whats the problem, and they respond that none of their clerics managed to camp out.

I respond (as my guild has done it a few times), why don't they bind. And they say their clerics refuse to bind in fear.

So I figure what the heck. I ask for buff and invis. And tell em

'Smoke me a kipper, I'll be back for breakfast'

I rush into fear solo. I run all the way to the north wall with the hordes of fear closing in around me. I get to the wall, turn slightly so that it will register that I have stopped moving and start casting bind. I turn more and see a scareling almost on me with many many mobs behind him. Bind has about 2 seconds left when the first scareling arrives and starts pummeling me. My heart is pounding like anything when the immortal words came up that I channeled bind.

I immediately /quit out. Take the death, come back in 5 mins later and ress their entire party and get a port out. Feeling fairly happy that in a hero'less game, there was still room to save the day :>

Out of all the crazy stuff I have done and am proud of, this is the thing I will most remember.
