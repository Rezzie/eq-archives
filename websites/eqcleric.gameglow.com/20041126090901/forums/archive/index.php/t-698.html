Are you tired of all the higher level clerics getting to play with their hammer pet? Well tire no longer, because you to can have a pet.

Just use your magical hammer bestowed upon you by your deity, and tap the creature you want as a pet on the head, and presto! Instant pet.

Want to be a Necro? Tap a skeleton.
Want to be a Shaman? Tap a wolf.
Want to be a Mage? Tap an elemental.

While in the commons the other day I used this exact method to "charm" a wolf, and like a loyal puppy he would follow me wherever i went playfuly nipping at my back. He wasn't very obedient though, I'd tell him to attack something, and all he wanted to do was play.

Perhaps with further experimentation, or obedience school I'll be able to get my pet to attack another. Perhaps a natural enemy like orcs, and undead in North Ro perhaps.
