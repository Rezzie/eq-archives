It's obvious the servers vary on this issue. I don't want all the gems, I just want enough to cover my costs. If I got all the gems in a seb group I'd make a killing! I don't want to make a killing, I just want my fair share.

Sol Ro uses the first 3 gems to the cleric. I usually get in a group and stay there for 3 hours. This means 1 gem per hour. At level 54 that meant 18 symbols cast (200pp) for 150-300pp in gems (depending on if you got cheap fire opals or better gems). In a weeks time of xping each nite I usually came out slightly ahead (less that 500pp ahead).

Once I got Symbol of Naltron my symbol costs dropped slightly because I can save a few dots in that same 3 hours. Given the same scenario I net about 1k-1.5k per week over my dot costs. Keep in mind it cost me 10k to buy that spell though, so I have almost broken even.

Fighting in those horrible zones where symbols are needed but gems don't drop, I pass along the cost to my group. They either get cheaper symbols (Only naltron's mark, no Martzin) or they find another way to pay me back (more loots, a decent item, or they cough up the peridots themselves). Just like I don't try to take more than my share from a group, I don't feel it's necessary to give more than my share either.

The bottom line is that we each have a personal decision to make in this matter. Even the clerics (us) don't agree what is correct. What one cleric calls greed another cleric calls fair. Even if we did all agree, what the clerics called fair, some other class would call greed.

Let's continue to discuss and share, but not point fingers at each other. As always, our milage will vary.
