Ok everyone I know that every one of you has probably experienced some anger, frustration, stress, and/or homicidal thoughts from the actions of D.U.M.B clerics. But I just have a few things to say.
I've only been playing EverQuest for 3 months now, but I have learned many things over these months. (1st month played as a Ranger, 2nd and 3rd as a cleric)
One important fact is a good cleric, won't start out as one. Some may start as decent or OK, but everyone learns from mistakes. Boy I know I have. Another fact, no matter how twinked you are, you will die accept that, and as a cleric do everything in your power, including risking your own life to save a group member. If every cleric did this, people would erect statues in our names, and sweep the ground we walk on to make sure I nice bronze armor (not until lvl 20 for us non twinkers) doesn't get a smudge.
Never ever scream at team mates for not helping you when you yelled for help. It happens, they have a bunch of creatures on them too so just fight and fight more. (Yes I have gotten angry before and been an idiot, I blamed teammates for death and threatened to leave, but when I look back, everyone died because of bad luck, another person not in group trained on us when we were already outnumbered)

When someone asks for buffs, wait until after the battle (lvl 5 was when I learned that one)if I had waited poor ol' "lets call him joe" would have lived...longer.

The hot HELP OH PLEASE GOD MAKE THE BAD MOB STOP key is good unless you use it everytime an orc pawn gets on you. (lvl 13)

When someone asks for buffs, be generous but if your in a group make sure no one is fighting and there is no one dying from disease or poison. This is one point I didn't need to learn, but already knew.

When you're in a group that watches as you take on a yellow con mob and laughs and says oops I didn't know when you die, THERE IS NO SECOND CHANCE!!! Leave that group and move on.

Just a few more rants, I promise.

We are not tanks wait...one more time for effect...WE ARE NOT TANKS!!! You do a WHOLE lot more good medding for a heal then going melee when you run out of mana. Sure there are some situations where this is different but...over all when in doubt...med.

People saying they don't need a heal...will be needing one a WHOLE lot faster in battle than if you had healed them before hand.

Just because were clerics doesn't mean we are Undead stoppers. Raise your hand if you've ever heard of SPELL INTERRUPT /emote Raises his bloodied and beaten hand. Boy did verant ever trick me on that one. Can we say 2 deaths behind the locked door in befallen. 1 from thinking I'm a cleric I can kill undead, a second from corpse run boy that key is hard to use when you REALLY need it.

We are indeed a powerful class but don't abuse that power, please, I know I've encountered hostile people before who had a bad cleric experience.

There is a relatively simple answer to the buffs in battle question...

Any hp buff is effectively a heal so is okay to throw toss around in battle. Any AC buff is expensive both time and mana wise so is probably left to between fights so you can ask your puller to hold pulls or pull singles during med time.

Heroic bond as mentioned earlier is a great relatively cheap group heal

Symbol... if worn off on the primary tanks I would cast *before* complete healing. With marzin the extra 700 hp you heal with CH more than makes up for the time/mana cost.
