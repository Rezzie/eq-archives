My observation is that clerics generally are becoming more popular to play.

I play on a fairly new server, Luclin, and I would almost go as far as to say there's an abundance of clerics there.

It is not rare to have a group in Sol B and have three clerics in the group. Has happened to me several times. Two is almost the minimum. =) I'm a warrior. I LOVE clerics. I'm not complaining, I'd take five clerics in my group any day. Too much of a good thing is wonderful.

I think there are a couple reasons why there are many clerics nowadays. Firstly, all the druids from the established / older servers might get tired of not having all the healing skills when they often have to fill the group healer position in a group anyways. Maybe they enjoy healing but realise that they could be a better healer as a cleric.

Second, people realise that clerics are a necessity in higher level groups, and all sorts of raids. So they figure that they'll be popular and get to go on lots of raids and such as a cleric. Not a bad assertion, I think that's the same reason there are a lot of enchanters too.

Third, clerics look damn good in their Armor of Virtues. I wanted to make a cleric for that reason alone.

Clerics are a balanced class. They have their role in a group, and they are very good at it. They don't have any screaming problems with their class either. And from reading posts here, people are generally happy with the cleric situation today.

So all in all, clerics are cool.

Amanita
