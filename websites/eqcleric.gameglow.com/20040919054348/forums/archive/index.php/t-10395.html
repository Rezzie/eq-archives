As a Teir'Dal Cleric I camped the two undead spawn points in nektulos. These are the pyramid south of Neriak and the undead obelisk just east of the zone to East Commons. Pretty good exp and loot. Just watch out for the occasional mummy or zombie. Even then you can shout out and most times a higher level will come and pop the thing for you. If those two sopts get too heavily camped (or theres too much for you to break up) cross the road west from the newbie log and go into the woods. If you are careful and avoid young kodiaks, there are skeletons galore spawning out there. The stone guardians can sometimes KS your kills. Annoying!!!!
