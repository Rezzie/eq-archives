I spent a few levels solo in Rathe Mtns. There is an orc camp that is right next to a paly camp on a hill. If you accidentally pull too many orcs (stupid lull fails too much!), just run to the paladins and they will save you.

Great loot too..the orcs usually have leather/raw-hide armor and sometimes bronze weapons. The lizardmen that wander up there sometimes carry bronze weapons, too.

Main problem I had was with encumbrance. The closest bank is either Qeynos or Highkeep, and I would make a trip to either of these places after I got a buffer on the next level. By the time I got to the bank, I usually had about 20-30pp in change on me (20+lbs of loot!).

May Tunare bless you in your travels!
