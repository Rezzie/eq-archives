Last night I went to Sebilis for the first time! I've had my key for a while now (thanks guildleader) but I figured I'd stay in KC for a bit (hey what can I say, I like the place )

But anyway last night a friend of mine was forming a group there and I thought what the heck lets give it a go. Now, I have the eqatlas map of TT, so I got my port to EJ, ran to TT, and ran down into the southeast corner, only to find that a) TT gets REALLY DARK at night b) the whole place is filled with ruins c) the entrance to Seb is in a TUNNEL.

So I logged briefly to check maps again, found out the /loc to the tunnel entrance, and went back in.

Saw the 'Seb bubble' that I have heard mention of and clicked on it, zoned in...

I had kept the group waiting for me for some time so we headed in straight away. I of course had never seen so much as a MAP of Seb, but I kept quiet and followed them down. We fought some froggies on the way and I thought, Froooaaak!, but they didn't seem to say that Eventually we got to the iksar jail and set up camp.

And then it started - the LOOT. My god this place is FANTASTIC for cash loot. Admittedly the more experienced group members said this was an above-average night but still - in TWO hours we got (and this is just the cash and gems):

2 blue diamonds (/randomed at the end)
1 jacinth
3 sapphires
12 fire opals
1 sapphire necklace
1 fire emerald ring
about 200pp in coin

And you know what? Apart from the BDs, they told me to keep the rest of the gems! I had only cast 10 peridot symbols (melees for an hour, then everyone for an hour) in the group! I said, 'um jacinth? isn't that random?' and they said, Nah keep it for peridots

Sold up everything from the sapphires down at best prices and got just over 1k in plat. Less the 100 for the night's peridots and, well, suffice to say I now love Sebilis!

I was in such a good mood that not only did I respond to TWO '/shout need an xp res, send tell' and res them, but I also showed someone the way from KC to the FV bank with a running commentary

Just thought I'd share

btw something odd was happening at the camp. We seemed to be getting some sort of weird chain aggro - basically, kept getting adds from nowhere. I think I saw a couple froggies pop in, and certainly no-one saw where the golem came from. We wanted to camp to clear aggro but there was no time so we had to zone. I tell you, following someone to the zone out in a zone you've never been in or seen a map of, then zoning out with 41 hp, now THAT'S exciting

*edit* btw2, why are Disco 1 and Disco 2 called 'Disco' ?

Don't get TOO excited. As you probably noted, the iskar necros are the ones that primarily dropped the fire opals. They are only at that camp except for one or two more at the disco area. So in other places you will not get near so many.

But still, Seb is a grand place to make some cash.

Another thing, don't expect to get defaulted all the gems. Many players know the relative value of gems and how much symbols cost. Many pick-up groups will let you have the first few gems, then its fair game for everyone.

And beware of rogues you do not know. I hate to say it, but many will pickpocket as much as they possibly can, this includes rare spells and rare gems like blue diamonds. I know some rogues do not do this, but any thread about this matter brought up over at the safehouse (rogue site) shows that the majority do not see anything wrong with this practice. Hence, i no longer group with rogues i do not know if i am concerned with gems or spell drops.

(Note, rogues can not PP velious quest gems except for the black marble needed for priest BP. It seems the only one that was not tagged as "Magic item").
