Get our your summon hammer spell if you don't have a magical weapon yet and start hunting wisps. Blast and melee till dead. They have good xp and good loot. Occasionally you will find regular and greater lightstones. The regular lightstones will sell for close to 1 pp while the greaters will sell close to 4-5 pp depending on your cha. Also you can find groups to do derv & orc hunting.
