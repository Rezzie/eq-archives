Well, I think I've turned into a crusty, rotten old cleric, I must say.

I was playing my baby magician (lvl 6! How elite!) in Greater Faydark at the orc hill near Kelethin.

I was kind enough to get some buffs from a high level druid on my brave jobaber, and I started soloing a few orc centurions.

Now I must admit, the low level game is a whole new bag for me, I feel like GFay might as well be outer space. I had Alli at that level, umm.. over two years ago, so it seems like forever, heh.

First off, I was subjected to three idiots using /shout to argue over if Tinkerer's Bag's were 8 slot or 10 slot. In shout, no less. I offered my (I thought) my rather definitive opinion as my cleric has quite a few tinks bags now, and got a nice "STFU noob" in shout back at me.

Oh joy.

Next, I had to listen to some people shouting how "Dvinn was at the CB zone, RAPING everyone." There ensued an INCREDIBLY long /shout and /ooc match, over if it was ok to use words like "rape" to describe a monster beating you up.

While all this was going on, some lvl 7 Ranger is constanly KS'ing my orcs. No /tells, no "Pardon me, did you have this?" He would just go to whatever mob I was alreday fighting, and begin hacking on it until he KS'ed it. He did this no less than 5 times.

Finally, I had had enough.

I had to bring Alliliel on to teach our little Ranger friend how to get along and play well with others. Needless to say, he shortly logged. It's amazing how far you can fear an orc. Did you know that they never come back?

Blearg! I don't think I'll ever try to level another character ever again, I just can't take it.

My suggestion to you and anyone else thinking of making a light elf twink... move somewhere else. In your case, Ali, I'd move to Tox. Most of the serious twinks go to Qeynos to level up leaving mostly true newbies. There's TONS of room, lots to kill and as an extra bonus for your mage you have the benefit of the Erudin library to buy some of the harder to find mage spells (Heatstone, weapon summons, waterstone, etc).

One out of level 9 stuff, head to Steamfont near the mino caves. EVERYTHING is blue and soloing is a snap and you can sail to 15 or so. Then hit Unrest a bit or Lake Omen to work up to 30 (Lake not too bad on CT since it's wide open and I find my mage can solo or group at will). Be warned that Unrest has a level gap between 17 and 20 where yard trash greens but the house is still red.

In short, no, Alliliel, you're not short tempered, you just found a lot of short (brained) people
