Or if you have 2 clerics and 4 big tanks in a group in VP. One cleric casts word of redemption while the other spot heals and casts the faster group heals. It's usefulness has improved now that we can duck and immediately interrupt spells.

I used it last night in Velketor after a very long, drawn out fight when 3 tanks could've used a ch to top them off. The shaman and mage in a mana robe winced down to no life while I med'ed up to cast it. Lots of mana saved/gained.

In ToV when/if there aren't a lot of clerics, it makes sense to clump a lot of the heavy tanks into the cleric groups. Word of Redemption can be a life/mana saver there.

Word of redemption is, like all spells, a situational spell.
