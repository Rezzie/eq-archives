I went with the Chrysoberyl Talisman too, though I also carry a Goblin Soul Caller and White gold Necklace. The soul caller is good for magic resist situations, the chrys good for AC, and the necklace good for resists and buying reagents. I try to keep my AC up as I hate getting interrupted while healing someone. The Chrys talisman has 20 mana, so it is about the same mana as the soul caller until you get to 200 Wisdom wearing it, then it is better unless the resist is called for. It is also far lighter than the GSC, and it is nice to toss that in a weight reducing bag when you start getting heavy.
