There are a number of good hunting grounds 45+.

KC for all its problems is one.

Kedge Keep is another, if you haven't been here, it is definitely BYOG, but really a neat dungon.

Velks Lab is fun, very pretty and quite an adventure.

I was still getting pretty decent exp in the Church in Kaesora helping folks get fangs up to 51 or so, check it out.

CoM is perfect. Get some buddies, or try to LFG.

Traks Teeth is a good zone, pick up a seb key while you are there, doing the rares is very slow exp, but very nice loot drops. There are literally fields of undead there which are fairly easy to kill.

Kael is also an option, get some good faction pops if you want to be friends with dwarves and dragons.

Tower of Frozen Shadows has some floors which are fabulous exp for all levels ranging from 30 to 50+. It is also a really neat dungeon if you haven't been there much, the key system is a total pain in the ass, and if you die in there, your keys aren't soul bound, so you just might want to keep a coffin in the bank in case you get killed and you have the keys.

The whole world opens up for you at 49 when you get Ressurrection. I would rack up exp and get to 49, you will get constant tells from then on for both raids and resses.
