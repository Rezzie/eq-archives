Delheru:

I find your post curious, especially this statement: "I am appalled by the shortsighted greed that casual players think these zones should be for their pleasure too." I'm not sure you meant it the way I read it, but if you did, you prove my point.


You appear to suggest that certain zones are beyond the ability of casual gamers and should thus be "reserved" for the greater guilds. I assure you that, by saying "casual", I did not mean friendless and incompetent. I, and most other "casual" players, have a wide network of other such players willing to help each other.

The difference between such and the so-called "uber" guilds is that we don't engage in any sort of systematic (and by definition exclusionary) resource farming. Instead, we wander here, we wander there, we group with this person, we group with that person ... it's, well, it's *casual*.

Of course, we cannot compete with a guild camping and farming x dungeon or y plane 24 hours, 7 days a week, to equip everyone in the guilds and their twinks. But we can handle the zones themselves just fine. And even if we can't, we should have the opportunity to try.

Now, I understand that I am not in a position at 27th level to enjoy the planes, Trakanon, etc., but my experiences with such guilds in lower level zones -- at least those with anything worthwhile in them -- has been the same and it only gets worse as you level. I have also read extensively this board, the E'ci boards (general and cleric specific), and the boards of these guilds themselves. From this I have formed my opinion.

I don't mean to cast my aspersions on all large guilds or on you or your guild; some are merely large networks of friends enjoying the game in the same manner I do and are quite beneficial. But there are guilds that quite simply view the game as theirs to monopolize (or break) as they see fit. FoH and SotS are two of such guilds. Such guilds are not beneficial to the game and any effort to curb such activities, to me, is welcome.

Now, if you were talking about those persons who feel everything should be soloable and the game should cater to that end of the spectrum, here I would agree with you.
