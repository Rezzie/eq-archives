For all of you "He never had a placeholder cause I never saw one" people... learn that YOUR experience is not necessarily what happens to everyone.

The COMMONLY known facts gathered from LOTS of people about Gull and Babbinsbort are...

GULL
Pre-Patch: Rare Wandering Mob with a wandering Placeholder. 7 Hour spawn time. Normally 14 Aviaks on island, Gull or his PH will be the 15th Aviak shown on tracking.
Post-Patch: Appears to be a 7 hour (or less) static spawn with no placeholders. Not yet comfirmed.

BABBINSBORT
Pre-Patch: RARE Non-wandering Mob with a Placeholder. 7 Hour spawn time.
Post-Patch: Static 4 Hour spawn with no placeholder.

The Pre-patch stats are not my experiences, they're those of the hundreds of Clerics who hunted them when they were too hard to find in my opinion. So I don't care what any one person seems to think how they really were pre-patch. This is how hundreds of people confirmed they were.
