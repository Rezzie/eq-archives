Caleal
10-30-2001, 02:45 PM
We have a boatload of warriors with full Myrmidon armor, primal and SoD, so agro control and tanking is not a problem. Generally we have 1 warror that will keep agro for about 90% of the fight, only losing it when he is knocked back or feared. The rest of the warriors are able to tank during those times.

We pull CT to the SW corner and push him north allong the west wall. Rogues position behind him and keep moving backwards to maintain max melee range. Main Tank stands almost directly under him and the rest of the melee people position in front of him slightly inside of max melee range and at an angle to keep driving him north and into the wall. That is the part of PoF where the zone wall is an invisible barrier at ground level. Some of the clerics stay to the side of CT and move with him, some stay behind the main mass of melee people.
Everyone has DMF on to reduce the knockback. People with jboots should have it up in their first buff slot so that the AE snare takes its place. This is so that when you get hit by the AE dispell, it removes the snare and you can click your jboots again. Anyone with jboots can keep from losing any buffs.

Our last run, we only had 2 deaths. 1 cleric died to rampage after the first heal on the main tank, and a warrior in my group died cuz I got punted by the knockback while casting CH and she got summoned back before I could recover. =/

Actually, that's bind-rushing. Zerging is when everyone blindly rushes the mob (either all at once or in waves-ie: tanks, then 20 secs later casters) with no mind to strategies like MA/SA, CHeal rotations and the like. Taken from the game Starcraft, IIRC (may be wrong on the game, but the rest of this is accurate), where there was a race of aliens named the Zerg who built inferior ships, but could produce them in insane quantities, so they could afford the losses that this tactic causes. Most people get their first taste of this on the old world dragon (Vox/Naggy) raids.

On most higher-end raids, this is also known as "a quick way to die". =)
