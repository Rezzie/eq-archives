What I have learned, this being my second character. Find yourself a good friend (someone that plays same times / as much as you) and convince them to be an enchanter. Make friends with tanks.

Now you may journey to dungeons and get GREAT exp and have tons of fun! At 14 I would hit Najena.

We hit Sol A, Mistmoore, Crystal Caverns (that one is the BEST dungy in game imo) Lower Guk, and are now in City of Mist. Great fun, good loot, fast leveling!

Good luck! And have fun!

If you really cant find a good dungy group, you can always go to Lake of Ill Omen.
