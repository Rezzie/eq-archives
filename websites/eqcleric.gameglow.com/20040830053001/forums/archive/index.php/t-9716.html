I wouldn't have such a problem with the cleric armour if it wasn't so bloody heavy, if you're going to make the platemail offer less protection than the ranger's chainmail at least make the damn stuff lighter.

Just compare the breastplates

Ivy Etched Tunic: AC 19, +3 str, +3 wis, +30 hp. Wt. 6 -- This is the Ranger one

Chestplate of the Constant: AC 18. +8 stamina, +5 magic protection. Wt. 7.5. -- this is the Cleric one (just in case you didn't know http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif

Rubicite Breastplate: AC 19. Weight 6. Effect of Rubicite Aura -- The original.

Now, given the option, which of the above would you rather have ? the Ranger one, which not only is the same AC AND Weight of the original, but also gives 3 strength, 3 wisdom and adds 30hp, OR, the cleric one,
which is weaker AND heavier than the one it replaces, but gives you extra stamina (very handy in a class that can add 300 hitpoints and a bundle of armour class with just a couple of spells) and an amazing +5
magic protection (again, very useful to a class that can boost MR by 40 with 1 spell).

I mean come on, I ask you, the fact that our platemail is heavier than ranger chainmail I can live with(even though weight is a real problem with non-dwarven clerics, although by the looks of things, Verant are unaware that there are half a dozen other races
that can also be clerics), but it's also WEAKER.

Apparently the only things that distinguish this as being plate are the weight and the name.
