Since you have a set group, places like the City of Mist, the Hole, and Sol B are great for experience. They are generally underpopulated, give exp bonuses, and never run out of mobs. The downside is that once someone has to leave, the group usually breaks up due to a lack of a replacement.

For me, I generally look for pick-up groups. This means I am usually situated in Dreadlands. After I hit 46, I will try to find experience in Karnor's Castle. It has not been hard for me to find groups in DL due to the constant need for healers. If people leave, there are usually many ready to take their place.
