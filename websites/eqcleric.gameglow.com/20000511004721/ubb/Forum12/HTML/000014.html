Greetings!

I am amazed at the experience factory that has opened up in the middle of South Karana! The zone is still very empty, my group (34 warrior, 31 ranger, 31 enchanter, 28 druid, 28 magician, and myself) camped the front bedroom and controlled the entire front of the dungeon up to the double doors.

This provided twelve spawns on a fifteen minute cycle, so we had literally non-stop blues, whites, and the odd yellow for hours. I made all of 29, and a bubble into the first hell level, in four hours with about 45pp in cash and Fine Steel weapons.

A grand total of eight people entered the zone while we were there, and as we kept the front area completely swept of spawns they would inevitably attempt to pull beyond the double doors and train themselves out of the dungeon...

We offered much advice to the people who entered, but they all wanted to rush into the cell block area and ended up doing corpse recoveries. The front is a steady, controllable, profitable area to hunt until at least 34th.
