Well I've been KoS to live frogs since my mid 20s. Back then it was a bit of a problem, by 35th though it was merely an inconvience.

Occasionally I get aggroed in Uguk but usually I just root the frog rather then waste more mana killing it and leave. I don't even bother to atone it. (helps to have jboots or SoW on at the time though)

The only place it has been a real problem for me in Uguk is if I want to get to the ancient croc spawn. Running there through Uguk isn't option, I get agged by about 8 frogs right before the final swim to the AC alcove. The other option is to zone into Lguk at the normal spot then head left and swim back into Uguk through the underwater tunnel hugging the right edge into the AC area. This can also be a pain because of the several spawns of shins on the way through Lguk, but it is doable with root, and if your lucky they are all undead or all just plain dead.

I don't see a reason to perserve your live faction just so you don't get agged in Uguk. If you have no interest in hunting live side Lguk, I can see keeping your faction in order to roam freely around there though.
