Hello Jerycho

If I was you, I would solo either spectres in the south-west corner of Feerroot or Cyndreela (spelling probably not correct) in Feerroot by the Fear gate.

About 1/4 of the spectres drops scythes which sells for around 12 pp and another 1/4 of them drop bronze scythes which sells for 2-3 pp. You can sell them in the same zone to the barkeeper close to Oggok. I made 1600 pp there while I soloed through hell 45. Took me 17 hours to get that level from spectres alone.

I do not know what Cyndreela drops, but I have heard she drops pretty stones (emeralds, rubys, etc.) once in a while. These stones sells at 50 to 100 pp.

Good luck
