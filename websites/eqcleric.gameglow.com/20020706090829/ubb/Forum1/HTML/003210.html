So I log on Sunday afternoon and some allies of ours are going to work with us in Kedge. Not bad down there. Got the guild monk a SwirlSpine Belt but Phingle was a washout, having only the mage rod. Then people started to leave and the monk died in the pit. When we tried to summon him out we agroed the 4 guardians and without an enchanter we got plastered. Did CR and got everyone resed.

Then we decide to do FGs. I hit 46 finally (woohoo!). Got really lucky on drops as Tranix had the crown and Rokyl had the crystal.

We decided then to try Skyfire (here's where it got really wild). At first we went to the Overthere zone and pulled some wurms. Then our allies showed up and we went back to the Burning Woods zone to try some really high pulls.

First, we pulled an Ancient Wyvern. Had no clue what to expect. But after one CH it was clear I didn't need to be using that. The wyvern has a poison based DoT that makes Blood Boil from LDCs look like a love tap. I immediately memmed Counteract Poison (still not high enough for Remedy) and did my best to cure. Several people died, including the ranger in my group. But he fell and dropped a lvl 60 spell (think it was a necro spell). Next wyvern was much easier as I had tossed Resist Poison and had Counteract memmed. The Wyvern is a pushover if you keep your group poison free. Second round had 0 deaths and netted a level 60 enchanter spell.

Now for the biggie... we pulled the BIG guardian wurm from in from of the Veeshan's Peak door. Cons red to a 54. And I have to say I've seen dragon raids that were less messy than this one. This wurm double hits for upwards of 530 damage. CH wasn't much of an option here. Casters would get wasted in one double hit and it was a struggle to keep up on heals for the tanks. Everyone in my party except for one wizard died (this includes me). I see the tank drop followed by

A guardian wurm hits you for 530 damage
A guardian wurm hits you for 530 damage
You have been slain by a guardian wurm!

It was that fast too. Of course, I was sitting too so that made the damage worse. Most everyone died, but so did the wurm

The vile wurm had a level 60 mage spell on him.

Ouch. I too spent some time in Skyfire Mountains last night. I've been there many times, but it was the first time I've fought an Ancient Wyvern.
