03-02-2002, 12:00 AM
Have all hide in the cubbies near tov, and the MT stand right near the portal thing (in ch range though). Train Sont onto the MT, defensive, have paly take the first heal (loh, so 1st ch cleric doens't get eaten) and then use a CH chain of like 4 clerics, 3 sec heals.

Then do groups of 1 cleric/5 high dmg melee. And group heal when they get around 50% hp to keep them going. Try an unload dmg discs sooner rather than later too.

When learning him, we kept getting him to "S" and then dying. The best advice I could give ya is... group heal your big damagers (keep them above 30% hp) and don't let that first MT die. Fight lasts about a minute, so don't be afraid to waste mana.

The way we do it is similar to the DL style above. We don't really do any debuffing. The main tank needs to have 3-4 instant click buffs up and in first slots. Sont's melee is quite weak, but his procs for 700 DD and constant dispells can hurt. Just have all the clerics, druids, and shaman chain heal the MT with their best heals (DL type). MT gets aggro, melee move in and disc right away more or less. Melee should all have 1-2 instant click buffs on and we never heal anyone but the MT.
