Ring of Fire has been nerfed over and over again. As far as I know we are on Ring of Fire version 5.0 atm.

This one is a joke. The spawn count has been drastically reduced, mobs can be AE'd (although its not needed since theres so littel adds to deal with now), the best loot drops off a Possessed Underking (which has the lowest spawn rate, and btw all boss mobs are on a random spawn timer, any boss can spawn at any given moment).

If you have everyone exit the ring, the waves will end and the boss mobs will despawn. Also there is a warder that spawns (untargetable) near the entrance to the room that will summon you to lava if you try to exit. Occassionally he gets bugged and randomly selects people to summon.

Overall the recent nerfs made this even more accessible to medium level guilds, so I do think its latest revision was for the better )
