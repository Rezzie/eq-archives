There's two zones you can check out that have possibilities in finding pickup groups for your level.

One is Dawnshroud Peaks which is two zones away from ShadowHaven. It's an outdoor zone where you can fight wolves, fungus and rockhoppers.

The second zone is farther away. It's called Teneberous Mountains. It is four zones away from ShadowHaven, but it is one zone away from another town called Katta Consortium. The mobs in this zones are vampire bats, dire sonic wolves and coteries (vampires). This zone can take you all the way to 60. If you are an evil race, you cannot enter Katta until you have killed many vampires to increase your faction with the town.

Tenebrous Mountains is awesome hunting in the 40's. Grimlings in the caves in TM start to go light blue at 43, but are pushovers enough to be worthwhile. If there are others in the zone to group with, or you brought a melee partner, find a spot without roamers and kill bats and sonic wolves. Great xp for their difficulty.
