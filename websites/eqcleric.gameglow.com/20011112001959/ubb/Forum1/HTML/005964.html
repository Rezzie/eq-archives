sad news first...Lor Warfield Goblincleaver retired today, a finer Dwarven paladin I've never known, and likely never will again, and all I meet shall be measured by his memories and actions...
What's that mean?
On Terris Thule The Champions of Brell are no more...but On 7th Hammer and Bertoxxulous they are still thriving.
The CoB did something no other Uber guild has ever done, been a force of honor and valor on three separate servers. It was a first and probably the last time this will ever happen.
The Gms were sad to see CoB go away, we were a source of Amusement to them to skulk and watch in our raids, or in zones were were in.
If something was fun, it was more fun when the CoB showed, if it was boring, well not much can help that, but our drinking and feistiness made time pass faster.
My War cries shall remain the same, only I shall have no tag over my head, but the Honor and vlaor tha made us strong will ever be in me heart.
Many condolences came as the word spread of Warfield's retirement, but I reminded them that "hey, we are disbanding on our terms, on a high note, we found homes for them that wanted to move on, and others will reband as the champions of Norrath-with a similar charter, we died not from Politics, or poisons evolved from within the game, nor over arguements of Phat Lewts, we leave our guild because it was time. Lord Warfield wanted to retire. "
This way the game looses not a whole guild, but earns the memories of a famed paltoon of shorter folk, who fought like rabid wolverines against all odds and perservered where the game lacked, we brought to Norrath much that was lacking, we made it a place to have fun.
I myself am very near moving on, if my health doesn't improve that choice will be made for me. The game has to be an escape for me now, I will tolerate no tedium, or stress anymore.
To that end I am very lucky.
Today I got the call while CoB was in unrest making Mayhem, and taunting the evil ones there, RAGEFIRE's UP and UNCAMPED.
30 seconds later I was in SOL B (being bound there from earlier this month)
2 hours later we stood inside the lair all FGs dead, buffed and prepped.
I almost lost rage when Baaz the Bard who volunteered to lead the event to help me, said GO meaning me turn in the pearl and they started swinging... I clicked the trade even as the merchant version attacked...I got the exps the message and theymoved to attack...
5 seconds later I went LD...
45 seconds later I logged back in...
Ragefire and dragon were dead, I was told to loot heart...
And only 4 dead, one monk, both chanters (who kept chain RUNING tanks), and another cleric I think...or a ranger...I wasn't looking .
BP Vambraces and Fiery gloves dropped,
All were happy.
I was too.
Eventually I ended up in TD and did the deeds ...let me tell you Oceanson is nigh invis in the rain in green and grey armors...I walked past him at 10 feet several times.
then the water elemental...in rain...light fog...GOOD DUKE of BELOW, (that's brell fer ye unschooled) he's hard to see, and chase and make a trade with.
But I got me Water sprinkler...but somethings a bit odd with it...
I will post pictures and the changes later....hehehehehehehhe (as soon as photoshop arrives) I will make funnies later,
I'm still in pain and need to sleep.
I am facing more spinal taps this month, not fun at all.

Thanks again all who helped, and sat before me with and for me through all ragefire camps before...243.3 hours worth.
MAY I NEVER HAVE TO USE THIS EPIC UPON YOU!!!
