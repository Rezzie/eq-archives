If you can get all ya clerics there, then defensive running out shouldn't be a big deal, you shouldn't be out of mana anyhow. Just rearrange the delay at which you chain your CH's on your tank.

Velks is easy because even if you die he regens at such a bad rate that he will be below 50% for a second fight, just as long as you reduce buffs to warriors, and clerics only (mana buffs for clerics mind :>).

He is not slowable, but he tends to nuke alot at the start (which is good). Do not let anyone mana sieve him. Get ya magicians to drop lots of mod rods on the floor for clerics to pick up when they get the oppurtunity.

Also another little tip I found - turn trade off, if someone gives a cleric an item, be it a mod rod or a mistake, you will auto target them. If you don't notice the main tank is toast.

Also make sure that all of your tanks control their agro. Make sure warriors who are not at the moment tanking do not have primary hand epics, and the like. One unlucky proc and bang that tank is dead (heh one of our warriors picked up the rubbish 13/24 weapon off velks to use in fights before he was tanking :>).
