- Monk solo in FG (60 Iksar with Garns/Primal) = 1 yellow/38 mins (I think thats FAST and yes, I envy him too)

I still think HS is probably one of the better places you can do xp's at (south or east wing would be best bet).

I went to HS a couple days ago. I start off just slightly over 2 yellow bubbles of normal XP and 3 blue bubbles of alt xp. My ratio was set 60% normal xp and 40% alt xp.

After about 6 hours of xping I had filled 3 yellow bubbles and had 3 and 1 half blue bubbles filled. I turned my alt xp to 90% and my normal xp to 10% and continued to xp.

Somewhere about 3 to 4 hours later, I turned my alt xp to 100%. I was 4 yellow bubbles and half of a blue bubble of normal xp and 4 yellow bubbles with 4 blue bubbles of alt xp at that time.

After another couple hours (or less - couldn't remember the amount of time) I had finished my first alt xp point and then managed to get 1 more yellow bubble of alt xp.

At this rate I will be racking up the Alt xp points in no time.
