During my reflections on yesteryear, I remember fondly dinging 39.

I was in Burning Woods I do believe... fighting with the brave warrior Creolen. Well I thought he was brave... but he had never faced a Complete Healing cleric like me before.

I had never used CH in combat at this point. I cast it once I think, to try and see just how long 10s was. But never in combat. I had heard how amazing this spell was... how it was going to change my life... save lives and water my lawn. But now the time has come to test that theory.

Our brave band over thrill seekers headed to Burning Woods... there, we grabbed ourself a wurm to play with. Creo engaged the mob, and started "Start your heal now!". I was like "I cant yet... you're not around half... gotta make it efficent, this spell costs a lot of mana"

So then he's around 2 bubbles... and crying like a little baby.. HEAL HEAL HEAL!!!

I honestly don't remember if I pulled off a dang close CH... or he died that night... I just remember the warrior who showed no fear in the past (or in the future), crying.

Whats your first CH story?

Technically.. my first Complete Heal was on myself. I buffed myself and then wrathed myself down to one bubble. I then complete healed myself for about 1000 hp.

My first real CH experience was in Overthere during 39. My subject was a Dark Elf warrior, who had about 1800 hp. He was level 41 so he wasn't losing HP too fast, and I really had no problem CHing him. My first few CHs were at 2 bubs and went through fine. At the encouragement of the group I started doing my CHs at 1 1/2 and it was fine as well.

After a few more groups in OT I dinged 40 and spent some time in EC or Lower Guk. My CH experiences there were good as well, although I did much more trading then grouping. My first bad CH experience was in Dreadlands. Things hit harder in DL then in Overthere. I was doing fine healing our tank at 1 1/2 - 2 bubs. When we had two mobs on us, and the bard's charm was resisted.. well that 1 1/2 bubs of health goes down very fast. During the 10 seconds of my CH, I see the ogre fall down. Whoops.

Luckily he wasn't mad

After this, my CH motto became: You live, you learn, and then you rez.

The one thing I learned about this is that you must consider the number of mobs your tank has. When its two, its probably a good idea to heal closer to two, then one.
