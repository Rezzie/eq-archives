Another solid choice are Gladiator Chain Leggings which I'm using. They have the chain graphic but they're better than Ethereal Mist in my opinion.

AC 17, STR +2, CHR +4, WIS +6, HP +20, SV Magic +10, WT 0.0. NO DROP

They drop off of a named Giant in the Arena area of Kael. Also keep in mind that they're probably the best greaves for Druids and Shamans. They're not the best for Rangers and Rogues, but they are highly desired by them as well. The only reason that I got them was because I was the only one who needed them at one point when they dropped.

They're pretty common but you might not be able to get to them depending on your guild. It requires splitting and pulling about 3 or 4 Protectors of Zek to get to the "good" giants in there. But if you can get there, do so. Our Idols also drop in here as well for the Symbol. One day I walked out with these Leggings, a Shield of Battle, and 2 of the 4 Idols.
