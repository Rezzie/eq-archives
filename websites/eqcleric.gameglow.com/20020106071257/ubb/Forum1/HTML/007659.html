1) Find a new pull spot. I am not sure where you are hunting in Ill Omen but anywhere along the lake on the West side of the zone ro along the wall will let you pull without risk of adds during a fight. If you are fighting in the Fort then get more people.

2) You might have to lower your standers a bit and fight blue or even stuff it pulling a yellow one will cause adds.

When you root, move away from the mob. That way when root breaks it will give you some time to be ready to root again. If your ranger has a damage shield it will also keep the mob from getting extra chances to break root because the DS is doing damage.

The bottom line is that you are fighting yellow's. Root will almost never stick on a yellow con. If it does it isn't going to last long. Your best bet for all purpose xp grinding is to fight a steady stream of blues. If you get a blue add you can root park him and take him out later. Or, have time to make it to zone. Other than that I can't think of any other advice. Happy Hunting.

Root and Snare will stack so have your ranger snare as well as rooting them. Even if root breaks you can still DoT and nuke them and kite them around. Kiting is perfectly viable and, remember, you have undead DoTs and there are some undead in LOIO.
