Deepwater Helm nerfed
Posted by Rayne at 12:18 PM

The casting time for the Kunarkian Paladin helm was changed from 5 seconds to 10. While I certainly understand how many players of the Paladin class would be upset I feel this change was warranted. Clerics experienced this type of change previously when the casting time on Donal's Vambraces were changed from 6 seconds to 12. All this patch did was bring the Deepwater Helm into line with Donal's Vambraces (by doubling the casting time).

FWIW, I STILL use the vambraces for battle healing; I'm just very careful about it. Under certain circumstances (high AC tank, mob slowed, only one being fought) the vambraces CAN be used to heal even with a 12 second casting time.
