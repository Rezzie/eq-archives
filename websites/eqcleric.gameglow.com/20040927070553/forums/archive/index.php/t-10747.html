Ocean of Tears is a great hunting ground, as posted in the 47+ thread below. I figured that I'd create a seperate thread here.

The Sea Fury Cyc's are great exp and loot (just wait until you go to sell those wonderful jewelry!), and really quick respawn time. I'm taking a group there this weekend, in fact http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif

There is also the "dead" island where Sentry Xyrim is. This island has 4 (?) specters and several gargolyes.

On top of all this, there is the Dianosaur (Alisauras?). I can't recall his name, but he drops 49 level spell components, diamonds, and other high level jewels. You'll need a group or two for this bad boy, but he respawns pretty darn quick too. Outside the Planes, this is where you will get a LOT of casters their 49 level spells http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif
