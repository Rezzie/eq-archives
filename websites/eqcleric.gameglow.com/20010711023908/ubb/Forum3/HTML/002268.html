some things i have found out so far... most of this is not confirmed...

1) Ghasts spawn between 8pm and 6am... if you don't kill anything you will get 1-2 ghast spawns per "night"

2) the apparent PH's for the ghasts are the wandering undead mobs (skellies, mummies, zombies).. killing them between 6 and 7pm game time will yield a potential 2-5 ghast spawns during the "night"

3) ghasts drop the hearts about 1 in 3 kills approximately

4) I hate ghasts

5) For "best" results be near the northern bandit camp

On the second part........

1) the necro spawns on the bottom/first step of the undead spire. His PH is the skellie/mummy that spawns on that step

2) kill the PH around 8pm game time.. the necro only spawns at night. Since the PH is on a 15 minute RL timer (15min RL = about 4 hours game time) killing him then will give you the best chance of getting the necro to spawn.

3) the Courier runs from the Steamfont area to the DE camp.. he will despawn in 6 minutes if not killed.

4) There is a potential bug in this quest right now with the courier not spawning because of the recent Tunare/Cazic GM event that has happened on some servers (not all yet).. if it hasnt' happened on your server yet (red mist sky = GM event HAS happened)... go do this part ASAP.... it is unconfirmed yet as to whether or not the courier is bugged in the "new" LFay

Part 3... Warden Symbol

Easiest part of the series if your high level.. white hellbore from gnolls in SK or splitpaw, red dust from gobs in SolA, caustic substance from Dark Offerers in MM...


*NOTE* I have not completed any of these quests yet, spent one night in LFay with no necro spawn and no ghasts, got my caustic substance but haven't tried the other stuff yet.. however, i have been scouring the internet for info on these quests and this is what i was able to compile

I completed this quest.... here are the facts

Level 16 Cleric
Level 53 Ranger (used him to track, loc, camp)

First get LF out of the way
Wolf Form If u have it. HUGE HELP

Best place to find ghast is around the Farie Tower. Seen them pop as early as 8pm and late as 7am. This step took me the longest.
IT TOOK ME THE LONGEST BECAUSE I WAS FOLLOWING ADVICE FROM SOME WEB SITE THAT SAID THEY CAN POP HERE OR THERE OR WHERE EVER.
**** AFTER CAMPING DIFFERENT SECTIONS OF THE ZONE BEST PLACE IS FARRIE TOWER *****
I seen 4 pop in 3RL nights (1 heart), then camped around farie tower and seen 9 pop in 1RL night (3hearts).

KILLING UNDEAD DID NOTHING FOR ME...
My ranger tracked and killed all undead faster then they could spawn and it did not seem to help. In fact the last night I got 3 hearts, I didnt kill any undead, I just tracked for gahsts

Necro / Couurior - Keep the Undead camp clear
When the Necro POPS, kite him over to SF zone area. Kill lute, then get the Courior.
I got both Heads and Box with no problem. The Hearts took so long (4 RL nights) that I helped others get their Heads / Box. I even seen the Necro pop and stay on tracking for an hour. So if something goes wrong with getting the Courior, don't sweet it, you wil get another chance. Especially with needing 8 Hearts.

Caustic Substance - Will be easy for you 2.
Was a pain, because MM wasn't camped.
The two of u will have no problems what so ever. Just go clear the castle, leaving the main room untouched. I was almost able to clear it my self, but 2 people came and wanted to camping the paino room and they helped.

Sol A
Kill all gobs.
I camped the Large Fire Gob for 2 RL nights, then was killing stuff in the adjacent rooms... and BAM one droped. I was lucky I checked it, the ranger was just letting most corpses rott. So the Large Fire Gob does not have to be camped.

Hit SK, was on stone bridge said 100pp for White Halibed, had it there in less then 30 sec.

I worked my Brewing up to 55, and sorry can't recall if I got the trival message.

Best place to brew - NFP, you can auction stuff while u brew.
All ingrediants to go from 0 to 150 can be bought from the vendor in WFP as you zone to NFP. She stands in to corner.
