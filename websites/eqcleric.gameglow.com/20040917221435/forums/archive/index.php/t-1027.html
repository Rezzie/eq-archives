Well 2 weeks ago I was in a rut. I was not having much fun playing my cleric of 42 seasons. I was in OT and was playing the stun, heal, buff game. I found I was loggin in my alt more and more. The problem I had, was group dependency. I hadn't soloed since level 34 and I rarely traveled alone without SoW and or invis. So I got a nice buffer into 42 and decide to try something new, on my own.

I ran around the Frontier mountains by myself with no SoW. I learned the art of root/atone, root/run, fear/root/run, fear/ run and many other ways to save thy own arse. Then I went back to the mainland for a bit. I hit MM and Kith with some guildmates and while running back to BB to get the boat I saw a gnome magician who snarled at me. Hmm, blue to a 42 I buffed upped and mememed all my stuns. I rooted the pet and stun/nuked my foe to death. Now some may say that camping spawns is boring, but every fight ended up with me less than half health and Lom. A couple times I was at the zone edge praying to Marr that my nuke or heal would land before hers http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif Level 43 wasn't much easier but the xp was all mine from the kill and knowing that each fight had a darn good chance to end me was very thrilling. I only lost 2 fights, one I zoned with 25 hps and the other I didn't make it.

This all ended in me getting 44 and my new spells. This had to be the best level for spells I have ever had. 39 was nice but not as nice as the 44 buffs and nukes that are worthy of group play (500 pt nuke at my level is worthy). This was really a level full of spells for "me", they do help the party, but extend my options to solo or get offensive in groups a lot farther than ever before. I went back to that gnome with the new spells but after 2 nukes she was dead and I got too bored http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

So the point is?

Life IS what you focus on. If you have a bad morning and focus on that all day it will never get better. Same with EQ if you focus on the things that suck, EQ will suck. This is not to say you cant try to get your cleric improvements, I am all for that. Why I wanted to share this story is because you may not always be able to get loot or xp, but Fun and challanges are out there for whomever wishes to seek them.

Enjoy your cleric! this board is a great resource that will help you do just that. My helm off to thoes that moderate and post here in a productive way. If I could not share and learn here, there would be one less cleric in Norath.
