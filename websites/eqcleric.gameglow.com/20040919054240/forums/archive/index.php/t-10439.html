Of course, one of the largest problems that I found with Lavastorm was getting there. To get there you have to go through the Nektulos Forest which, if you're not an evil race, can be pretty tricky. I've got a mage friend who can inviso me to run through the zone... if you're careful, it's not too hard to avoid the DE guards and dragoons as they usually stay on the bridge and borders and if you stick around the Halfling camps, you should be safe. Keep an eye out for the Guardians, though, as they can be tough.

Lavastorm's a great level, though, and if you want to you can always stick your head into the entrance to Najena.
