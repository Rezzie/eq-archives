Loot is lame from them. Some quest item loot for Necros, enchanters, wizzies...

They're ok, just watch the ones with the paddles, those are the healers, and they're dangerous.

At 30, I've seen these guys wipe out a full group. They're faster than sow, so don't expect escaping to be easy.

The ones with the books are necros, and once they have a pet up, they can have alot of fun fearing you all over the zone while they shred you http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif

This isn't a horror story, ok? I've seen them camped very well, and I've seen them camped poorly. Try to find someone with harmony and pull singles. They have a large aggro range, so back up QUITE A BIT to fight them or all of them will be in the battle before you know it.
