Starting at about level 24 you can solo by the zone between Rathe Mountains and Lake Rathe. Hunt Giant Skels on the mountains side and when there are none spawned zone into the lake and kill deepwater goblin pets which usually run right by the zone there. Whats nice is if you are taking a beating the zone is right there. Good hunting!
