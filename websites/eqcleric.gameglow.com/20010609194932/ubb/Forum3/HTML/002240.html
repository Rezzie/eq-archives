Go to the undead camp in SK, which is just a short distance southwest of the bridge to NK.

The ph is always a putrid skellie that is standing up - as opposed to the other putrids in the area that are sitting down. It will spawn, or Grimrot will spawn, under a hut towards the middle of the camp. You can tell this hut because it has no 'lantern' on top and is not at the edge of the camp.
