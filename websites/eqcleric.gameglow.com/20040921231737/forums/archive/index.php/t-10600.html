There's a skeleton ruin in swamp of no hope. About half a dozen warbone/icebone/charbone skeletons spawn there, non-social so when you hit one, it always comes alone. I soloed from 27 to 32 there, although 31 was kinda slow, as lots were already green. Charbone skeletons range up to level 27, are and pretty tough, so I wouldn't recommend doing that spot solo before 26-27 though.
