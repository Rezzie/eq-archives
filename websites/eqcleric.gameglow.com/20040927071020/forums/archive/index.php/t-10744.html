We were waiting for a few friends for a Chardok trip outside the Sarnak Fort in BW.

was me (51cleric), a 51druid, and a 54 warrior.

We were killing the 4 "gate" spawns at the entrance to the fort, as well as 1 or 2 spawns from inside if time permitted. It was very easy with harmony pulling and was very good experience for all of us. We pulled thru about 4 spawn cycles (approx. 6 minutes on respawns) and in that time there was only 1 mob that conned green to our warrior.

It was refreshing to find that spot, we had ran thru it upon occasion on our way to chardok, and I have only ever seen it camped once. Of course there are other potential camping areas within the fort itself as well.

I am always looking for another spot to hunt that is rewarding and not contested... i simply deplore Karnage Castle as, IMHO, it seems to be Crushbone on steriods.

Just thought i would pass that along
