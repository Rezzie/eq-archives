In my experience, in battle rezzing just doesn't work very well. By the time the dead tank zones upon death, gets rezzed, and zones back in, at least two minutes or so have passed, and that's on a good connection. And once the tank is alive, he/she still has rez effects to contend with. I find it much easier to make sure the main tank doesn't die, by erring on the side of caution (read: overhealing and wasting mana rather than underhealing and letting the tank die).
