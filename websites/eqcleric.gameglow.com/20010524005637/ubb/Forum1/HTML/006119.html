Hmmmmmm, since I just REdid 49-51 recently, I can tell you all about spectres in Oasis. Argueably, this is the best exp from 45-50 for a cleric. Exp just flows and you can power through from 45 to 50 in a couple days.

At 48, 1/4-1/3 are green but still give exp
At 49, 1/2-2/3 are green, with approx 50% of the green ones still giving exp.
At 50, they were all green, but some still gave exp.

Since a 48 cleric can easily solo 3 specs at a time, the green ones don't really matter much. You can kill 3 green specs and get the same or more exp you would get killing 1 blue giant. Most specs die to 3 Banish undead, but load a lower level nuke for the odd one that just needs a bit more to kill.

All I did was use JBoots (or trace Resolution for a SoW with some low level druid), pull 3 from the island to the Inn near NRo. Root 1, run some, root 2nd, run some, root 3rd and burn it down. Then root 2nd, root first, and burn it down. Then go back and finish off the 2nd one. You may have to root and med for that one, depending on your wis and +mana and resists.

As a courtesy to the lower levels, if you pull 4 or more, try to kill as many as you can before zoning. Often, at 48/49, on a 4 pull, I could burn 3 without going OOM and would root and med the 4th.

Alternativly, if you have another cleric or a lower level group also doing specs, you can alternate spawns and just root and burn on the island. Once the spawn is broken up, this is easy exp. 8 spawns total means you get 4 every 15 minutes. Sit in front of the doorway (at edge of island, not on ramp), change your view, and you can see inside the doorway, the far side of the island, and all the tower spawns as well. When it's your turn, stand up, cast root, nuke 2 times, sit and wait until root breaks, then let it come to you. Then root again and burn it down. This saves you from having to run up the ladder to loot your kills.

This also is a great way to raise that pesky Defense skill. LET them hit you some. I maxed Defense by like level 48 if I remember corrrectly. Sometimes when I was bored and clarity was available so mana wasn't an issue, I would just tank one and nuke it as I meleed it.
