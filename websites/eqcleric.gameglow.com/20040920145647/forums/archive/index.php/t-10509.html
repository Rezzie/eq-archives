Another good place to hunt and rarely overcamped like the Aviaks of SK is the large undead camp in Rathe Mtns. Find you way to the feerott zone, then head back into the zone and take the first left. This area is Necro valley and has a bone tower on your left with two gnome necros. Unless you have a full group, stay away from them.

The undead camp is on the left and past the bone tower, you will see some ruins there. There are at least a dozen spawns in this area. I usually clear it from right to left. At this levell you need at least one other cleric/healer with you and a tank or two for the tougher skeletons. Avoid the skeletal monk unless you have a full group with at least 2 nukers. They spawn rate is fairly fast and includes zombies, skeletons, iceboned skeletons, dryboned skeletons, giant skeletons, barbed skeletons and skeletal monks. Recently a "dark boned" giant skeleton has been added here, it drops one of the items for the Caduceus of the Sacrament.
