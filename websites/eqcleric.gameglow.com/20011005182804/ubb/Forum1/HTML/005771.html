Cast time is none, they are an instantaneous cast. It's really not a shabby item, but for dragon loot, it's disappointing.

We had to kill Naggy 3 times over the last week, due to the patching and subsequent downtime. We killed him two sundays ago (4 prayers of life and two bard drums...yeah). Then the next Tuesday after the patch (CoF, prayers of life, some other stuff), and then again on last Friday night (more prayers of life and some other stuff). And then Monday night I got my ragefire, finished the epic out, woot!

But, we accumulated 7 prayers of life over this time, and most were just given out to folks as kinda a consolation prize. I was told to keep the last two that dropped, in case we needed them for anything, and they actually came in handy the other night in Karnors. No chanter, no bard, no mana regen, and a train got pulled. Used up all my mana, and we still had 3 things left beating on us, so I started to use the group heal from these. Saved the day, we won the fight. But, for the most part, useless.
