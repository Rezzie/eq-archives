Yea, that's right...those annoying schmucks that give you the message quests. They typically have a sorta slow respawn (10 or so mins but I haven't put a timer to it), but if you can't find anything better to do they're good exp. They vary in level, but the ones that I know of that are in this range are:

Tacar Tissleplay, lvl 25-ish, in Felwithe. He's right next to the weapons shop and bank, turn right when you get to the pally guild and follow that one around.
Silna Song(something), lvl 25-ish, in Hobbithell..uh, I mean Rivervale. She's hidden, sorta. When coming in from MT, turn left and go around by the inn there, turn right and there she is, just waiting for some lovin'.
I think the bard in Highkeep Hold is also a potential victim at this level...have not visited him yet though.
The bard in Erudin (Tacar <something>). Cons even to my 25.

Fac hits that I know of are: (I think there are a couple of others) You can reverse these hits (somewhat) by killing off the inhabitants of Mistmoore.
- for League of Antonican Bards (duh)
- for Guards of Qeynos
+ for Mahjongg(sic) Mistmoore

Loot: I've gotten as much as a plat and scattering of silver, to a gold and some copper off Silna. That's about all.

Observed exp: From my 25 half-elven rogue, about 1/5 to 1/4 bar per kill on Silna.
