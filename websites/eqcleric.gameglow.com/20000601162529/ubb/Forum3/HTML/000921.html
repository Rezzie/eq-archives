Yesterday started off pretty well for me. I ran to Runneye, stumbled around the dungeon for a while and ran right into Sludge. Few wraths, woohoo, half of the bp quest done. As I leave the Gorge, I run into a druid getting pounded on by Broon. I quickly root/fear and wrathed Broon. Turns out the druid had tried to kite him and had run out of mana. Very nice druid and very thankful for saving his butt. He offers to do me a favor. Woohoo I get a port Butcherblock and a quicked sowed run to Dagnors Cauldron.

This is where things went bad. I zone in, and ask /ooc Has seen anyone seen Dwigus? Get a tell from another nice druid (go figure ) that he had killed him a couple hours earlier, without knowing who he was. Turns out the piece rotted on the ground. Doh!! So I wander round and round that bathtub for the next couple hours. Buff and heal a few people along the way. I asked every 15-20 if anyone has seen him. No answer. Swimming around I run right into Dwigus. Follow him up the shore, my buffs still in place, full mana, I root and wrath. Then proceeded to get a butt whooping. I got a few more stuns and wraths off, but no roots. Got him to maybe half health before I died. Came back, searched desperatly for him, no one will tell me if he's on track or even around. Finally I gave up and looted my corpse.

All in all a very pathetic end to a day. So, here are my questions. What level do you need to be to solo Dwigus? I'm 37th. Seems he's a roamer, what's the best way to find him, without the ability to track? Is he a 4 hour spawn, or did I get lucky on the timing?

I thought I might try again tonight. Maybe I'll get lucky and run into a stranger willing to lend a poor cleric hand.
