I will tell you a little story about how factions can come back to bite you. =)

One part of the ranger/druid epic quest is to hand something into a druid gathering in E Karana. Then you follow a bunch of NPCs around and do the quest yadda yadda. Eventually, a DE corrupter spawns that you have to kill.

A friend of mine asked me and my roommate for some help, along with a few other guildmates of mine, so off we went. It NEVER occurred to me that my roommate would die long before we even attacked the DE Corrupter. =D

My roommate spent several levels killing treants back in the day. The first set of druids were just fine with him.. but the next guy to spawn was NOT happy about his presence at all, and killed him on the spot. =D

On a similar note, we have exactly ONE wizard in the guild who ISN'T KOS or dubious in Halas.. which is where the wizard epic quest happens to be centered around. =D

So choose the factions you want to destroy wisely - it can come back to bite you later on with content that isn't even in the game today.
