The only way to make CH not useful would be to have the mob hit HARDER than it does now (that would just mean double the number of clerics/frequency of heals).


Maybe a way to make CH less functional would be to add extremely high HP/High AC mobs with insane non removable DS and 100 + per tick regen. Make them hit for a normal damage amount.

CH wouldn't work all that well against this mob, too many people taking too much damage. It then becomes more about DPS control and balancing DPS with the amount of healing needed. Mana Burn would have a functional use, all other nukers have a roll. Pets, slowers, mana regeners... etc...

Make killing this 10,000,000 hp mob about combat control and not about how you can use a macro. Have the mob summon decent level pets in on a regular bases just to keep things interesting.
