My standard healing macro is as follows:

/stand
/cast 5
/pause 3,/g COMPLETE HEALING [ %T ] 10 SECONDS!
/pause 3,/cast 5
/cast 5

- gives me 3 shots at the spell, which catches both fizzles and when I try to heal before the spell gems refresh, I very rarely miss a heal with this setup

- the low pause value (3/10ths of a second) only locks me up for 2 seconds max, but is enough to keep the spam filter at bay. I may try to drop these pauses down to 1 or 2, but I'm used to the timing now

- it's difficult to duck to interrupt unless you wait for all 5 lines to fire, mob interrupts will still happen though

- I can always use the Alt-5 to cast the gem

- I have 4 different versions that do different calls, one does the /say, one /g, one for /gu, and one for /ooc

- I have to wait for all 5 lines to fire before typing something in the chat window (which only takes a second or two anyway).
