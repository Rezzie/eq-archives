You can also try here http://ultimatewedding.com/vows/ for some ideas. Whenever I perform a ceremony I do try to incorporate things that have to do with the characters classes, their personality etc in their vows also.

Some examples from ones I've done:

For a cleric:

quote:
.. to have and to hold, in quick spawns and slow... In times of full buffs or none, through 10,000 deaths and resurrections...

For a bard:

quote:
...To sooth his wounds, lift his spirits and replenish his strength with your songs

For the one marrying an enchanter:

quote:
...To cherish her in all her favorite forms

And the one marrying a monk:

quote:
...To suffer the pains of bad pulls and cheer the success of good ones

Personally I love officating at weddings. And I find it a real honor to be asked. And yes, I've been asked on the fly and come up with ceremonies in 15 minutes or less. Those are the fun ones *nod*
