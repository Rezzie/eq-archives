Talk to me about Dragon runs.

I went last night on a pickup group. THEY came to ME and practically begged me to come. They had 1 51 cleric (me), 1 50 cleric, 2 40 clerics and 1 who dinged to 38 off a FG.

This is what is refered to as a pick-up raid, correct?


So, I went.

We got the FG's, and I was told clerics couldn't roll for the mask. Well, that's all I really wanted anyways, but I carried on.

We got to Nagafen, and suddenly the raid leader changed the rules. All tanks under 49 couldn't stay, all other classes under 45 couldnt stay.

Well, I didn't really agree with this, but I could see the logic. Naggy conned even to a level 55 monk. He was gonna be tuff.

Well, after much arguing, they let the lower levels stay, but grouped them together and grouped the higher levels together. We charged. 2 clerics and a tank LD at the start. We got him to the "L" and lost.

CR, rebuff, go again. Second try went MUCH smoother, we had TWO deaths and none from my group.

Now, the drops were:
Diamond
2 Bladestoppers
Hunter's Sachel
Druid Staff (Hiero Crook?)
Bard Drums

They did the bag first. I have 4 tinker bags, a large soiled and 2 other weight reducing bags. So I wasn't really interested.

They did the Drums second.

Then the druid staff. Well, one druid asked to be in, but that raised a big stink cause he had some uber staff from the planes. They skipped the staff and did the shields. They eventually let him roll, because he participated, he died many times (lost 3 or 4 corpses too I think) and he did his part. They said everyone gets to roll on one item of their choice (with in class guidelines).

Now the shields. They said everyone got to roll one time. Equal risk, Equal opportunity for reward. EVERY Paladin and EVERY Cleric in the raid went in for those 2 shields. I won one.

I didn't expect to win, but I died 3 times the first try, and you guys KNOW how some of my previous raids went, and EVERYONE sent me tells saying I deserved it. Again, EQUAL RISK, EQUAL OPPORTUNITY FOR REWARD. So, I said thanks to everyone and took my "Prize". I offered it to one of the warriors who wanted to roll for it, but wasn't allowed, but he had won the Diamond. He was happy. I took my shield and left.

Now, I am sure some of you say I was wrong, but this was not a guild raid where certain items were supposed to go to certain people, and everyone knew it up front. This was a pickup raid and everyone rolls on one item.

I won a shield. I may use the shield, I may not. If I can trade it for a Medicin totem or other more useful item, you better believe I will. If I had picked the diamond and won, I would have sold the diamond too. Is that wrong?

/em remembers he still has all his Vs. Fire gear on from the raid. And still has Resist Fire.

/em figures if he could resist Naggy, he can resist anything here.
