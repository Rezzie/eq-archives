I know where the cave is... found the lich no problem

There's also about 6 ice bones throughout the area, 4 guarding the wizard rod for the staff of the wheel, 2 guarding a portal to a zone that isn't open yet.

And there's a oddball. Sulon McCoon or something. This guy is strange. He spawns every 8 minutes or so. When he spawns, he casts a fireshield on himself and any nearby gobbos (there's 1 or 2 gobbos in the caves also, very very green). He's red to a 40, blue to a 50. I'm suspecting around 45ish due to resistances from lower level people I brought with. A 50 mage, 40 cleric and 36 druid took this guy down using 80% of the mages mana, 70% of the clerics and 60% of the druids (mostly healing the mages pet). He hits like a freight train, double attack for 125. no kick, no bash, no quad hits. He doesn't cast any offensive spells at all other than the damage shield and he frequently casts nullify magic. No direct damages tho. He's human, dressed in a Sr Apprentice Robe which is his only loot. Factions are down with vox, up with naggy. My point is, if you're high enough, kill this guy over and over again while waiting for the bear.

The Lich is also about 45. he has about 9000hp, and loves to use fear. The druid and I practically solo'd this guy cuz he kept fearing the pet and mage. Use lotsa stuns if you don't wanna get those meganecro Dots on you, we're talking 800+ damage dots, ouch. He wanders the caves continuously, could be just about anywhere so watch out for him if you're medding after killing Sulon. He's a slow spawn, so kill him first and then go back to Sulon's rapid spawn. I was able to keep both the lich of miragul and sulon stunned continuously as a lvl 40 cleric (all 3 stuns loaded) and keep them from casting too much, even so, the fights are LONG, takes a long time to get those huge HP to go away.

All that said, I haven't yet seen the damn bear. Anyone know WHERE in the caves the bear spawns? or where it wanders? we had a druid tracking for it and we never saw it once.
