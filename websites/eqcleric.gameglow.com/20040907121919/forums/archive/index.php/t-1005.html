Couple of tips for buying peridots in Firiona. First, most people I know started off apprehensive there. But fixing faction is surprisingly easy. Killing the goblins in the Lake of Ill Omen will give positive faction hits. (Not the bloodgills, but the lower level "a goblin scout" and "a goblin warlord" types.)

You can hunt these for experience if a low enough level, but they are very green to me (and we all know how efficiently clerics can mow through greens... LOL). So I cheated a bit. I went to the goblin mines in the NW corner of the zone and found a level 20-27 group camping there. I made a deal with them - offered to buff them, keep them out of trouble, and res any of them who died, if I could get one stun in per goblin. They were only too happy to oblige. So I spent a lazy evening helping them out and having a conversation in tells with their cleric, who had all sorts of questions about our class, and getting faction for everything they killed. Prior to this, my price for peridots in Firiona was 12.1 pp even with my best charisma gear on. After three hours at the camp, I had gone from apprehensive to indifferent, and my price had dropped to 10.8 pp.

My charisma stinks too (base 70), but I carry a few items that can get it up to 97. It's still not enough to get the lowest prices anywhere but in my hometown of Erudin. So if I'm hunting somewhere with an enchanter or shaman, I ask them for a charisma buff right before the group breaks up for the night. I then go immediately to town, sell any cash items I picked up, and restock my gem supply. With my items and the buff, I always get the lowest prices.

Just a couple of suggestions, hope it helps http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
