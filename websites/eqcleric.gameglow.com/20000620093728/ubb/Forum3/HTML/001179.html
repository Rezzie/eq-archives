Well for a mid level cleric (20s to 30s) here is what is best and it is EVEN attainable solo or at most with a small group or you can probably shop around and buy. (Caution spoilers may be here, and I am posting this off the top of my head while servers down so some things may be wrong)
Head: Runed Circlet from perma or buy it for 600pp (4wis, 4AC)
Neck: Black Iron Medalion from Runny Eye or buy it for 20 pp (3 wis)
Face, ear, rings: call a good jeweler shop around to several, save up and buy all at once if you can for a big deal and discount silver ruby veil for the wis and str, jasper ear and rings, you should be able to buy all FIVE items for about 1000pp to 1500pp.
Arms, hands, chest, boots: do the sol temple cleric quests. Or you can buy all those pieces for about 2500 to 3000pp or do em yourself for about 700pp and 20 hours work if you are lucky.
Wrist: Cleric armor still great but always camped and farmed, go with polished bone off evil eyes in the gorge, chipped bone bracer off ogres in west karana, or runed mithril from solb off king (need a big group). You can buy them for about 75pp PBB, 600 CBB, 800 RMB, 800 cleric only bracers.
Shoulders: ademantium epols buy em for 400pp
Waist: just take your pick lots of good belts
legs: silver plated legs from lower guk buy em for 1000pp. Or gator leggings from upper guk for 500pp if you need the wis.
For the shield go with charred out of sola, paw of opalla is hard to beat for main hand or cleric quest mace or summon yourself a hammer, for range slot do the kerren toy quest for some extra ac and agi.
You may want to substitute dwarven work boots from unrest for cleric only ones.
