For first time, recommend the following...

Clear king room, and DA pull Queen room right to the King spawn spot. Then, pick off all the room spawns as they roam back (Queen goes back last). Wait for Queen to go back, and move into hallway to quickly buff up, med, etc. Then everyone move down near door, and have someone with DA run in right upto the Queen. When she AE's (makes an animation the DA person can see), have all the tanks rush in and use damage disciplines right away. Clerics follow the tanks closely, out of AE range if possible though. Nukers don't nuke at all till 50% imo.. pong will most definetly kill you on your first try.

Now you can do 2 things.

Either you can have 1 cleric per big tank, chain CH'ing that 1 tank so he never dies, but you'll have mass deaths from people not getting healed if you don't kill her before she AE's again. Or you can chain group heal till you're oom, so no one dies. First choice is safer, 2nd is cleaner. Upto you.

She dies in about 7 seconds now these days... and AE's every 10 seconds, so try to kill her in under 20.
