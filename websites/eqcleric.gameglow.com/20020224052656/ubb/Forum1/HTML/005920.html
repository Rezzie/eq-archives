Well, in scenario A, yes the druid has spent that time camping him... but the druid can't kill Ragefire. The druid can't even spawn Ragefire. The reason he's down there is to let people know when he's up to go kill him.

Like it or not, you can't camp one major spawn like that and "claim" it. Just like a group at the zone of Sebilis can't "claim" a camp. Otherwise you could just park 1 person anywhere in the world and just start claiming stuff left and right.

There are no "good" solutions here. Unlike the gods, you can't plan for Ragefire, you must camp him. And unless you have a guild willing to sit down there or can mobilize faster than anyone it's pretty much pointless to even start your quest now.

Depends alot on your server, but my opinion...

The only way to "camp" Ragefire is to have a cleric with a shimmering pearl sitting on the spawn point. A monk FD in the lair has about as much right to claim the spawn as a ranger in the SS room that can catch him on tracking, IMO.

If the spawn is camped by a cleric, they have a reasonable amount of time to get the people there to kill it. I would think that there should be a sizable showing (at least 2 groups there) within 30 minutes. If they can muster 2 groups in 30 minutes and a full force within 90 minutes or so then everyone else should back off.

If Ragefire is not camped by a cleric, then it's a matter of who can get a capable force in the lair fastest. Whoever deems themselves ready and hands in the pearl first gets the spawn.
