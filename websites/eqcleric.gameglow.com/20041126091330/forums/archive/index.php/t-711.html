I started in Firiona Vie just barely into level 31, and was almost level 34 by the end of the weekend. I fought the spiders very close to the Outpost, down on the beach near the tunnel that leads to the outpost. This is a good spot to pull to because 1) It's close to a guard (down in the tunnel) 2) It has no wandering spiders, and 3) It has very minimal wandering KSers (drixies, pilgrims). Every once in a while one of the scouts will KS you, but it's rare.

At 30 you should also be able to get in a Forest Giant group there with no problem, you just have to be careful when travelling up there, lots of drolvargs around.
