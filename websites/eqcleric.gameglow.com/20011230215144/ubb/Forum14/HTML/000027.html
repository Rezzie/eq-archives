I successfully soloed spectres at around level 39 all the way up to level 44 (although at 39 there was *lots* of down time and running to the zone if I got a particularly nasty one). Also, until level 42 or 43 I seldom used Root/Enstill. Expel Undead casts amazingly fast. Also, you run faster than spectres. So, what I did (hopefully the latter).

You *really* need a lot of mana to do this at level 39. My wisdom was something like 182 at the time which gave me enough mana to do this.

Of course, at level 44, Spectres are cake. I still use the same technique, however, although I use Entill in there; often it will last until the spectre is dead.

I see a lot of people suggesting using spook. This might be a good idea after the initial Stun/Blast. Stun, then Blast, then Spook. Then start over. I just never found a need to do that.
