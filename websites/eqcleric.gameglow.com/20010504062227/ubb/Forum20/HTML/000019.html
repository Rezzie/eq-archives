Greetings!

Captain Bevellos (at the bank) gives Tormax faction for coldain heads (can be turned in singly, not just 4 at once) and Icepaw kobold paws (4 at once). The paws also yield a nice mask with right-click Identify, a handy little utility spell.

Coldain Royal Guards in Icewell give Tormax faction.
