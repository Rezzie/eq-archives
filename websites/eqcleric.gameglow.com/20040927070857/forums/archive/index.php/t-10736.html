Speaking from point of view of my ranger. Attacking her will not agro all the DB's around her. Not unless you are in their agro range, no matter what level you are them DB's will attack you if your in their agro range even if they are green beyond belief. The pet is a possible problem of course because he will disrupt your spells when your attempting to cast them. You will have to root him and then deal with her. If your lucky when she pops you can attack her even before she casts her pet to begin with. Other than that, go for it. Just don't fall into the fear gate http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif
