Three areas I have had good luck in would be Mistmoore (The front area for a steady blue stream, and the Grave Yard for a semi-steady stream... while there *are* trains, they are usually NOT your fault http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif Rathe Mountains (soloing Giant Skeletons, or grouping for Skeletons and Cyclops....slow exp, but very safe.. even if you accidently pull 2 or 3 cyclops, the whole group can usually get to zone safely), and Cazic Thule (Courtyard area... WONDERFUL exp...but I generally died every few hours.. the problem isn't trains [blues and greens usually], it's figuring out when to zone...because some of the time the courtyard can be packed with alligators and lizards...and yet the people CAN kill them all, and reap exp as well.. however, by the time you know you can't make it, the area is usually too packed TO make it to an exit -_-; ]
Hope that this helps someone later on =)
