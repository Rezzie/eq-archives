My suspicion is that if this goes Live, they will remove JBoots from the game. I think removing JBoots is the primary motivator for this action. The new boots have a cast time of 3 seconds or so. Everyone and their brother uses JBoots (at 50+ anyhow) to beat debuffing mobs like WW Dragons. Adding the cast time will prevent that.

For example, hunting in the West Wastes, I was the only one in two groups with no JBoots. In one fight with one Dragon, I would lose every buff I had, about 16. Most other people would lose 1-3 buffs per fight due to refreshing JBoots.

I started buffing myself with Holy Armor in the first slot, but it was only partly effective, as I had to target myself, not always easy when trying to heal others, and still had the 2 second cast time. I would sometimes be able to save 4-6 buffs, but would still lose at least 10, often all.

I think letting us buy Travellers Boots is a way to make this Nerf more palatable. I don't necessarily think its a bad game decision, but it will make Debuffing encounters more difficult for those without JBoots.
