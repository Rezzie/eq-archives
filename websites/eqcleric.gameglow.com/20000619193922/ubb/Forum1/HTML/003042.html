Saturday I was killing aviaks then raptors in Timorous and got 48. Woot!

Mid day I got an invite to go to Hate so heck, why not. That raid was fairly uneventful. Had just gotten to the top of the loot list when we called it a night (figures huh?). During the night I again had a couple of chances to get that 'you're not 49 so you're not good enough' frustration...including one point where I got rezzed just in time to hear "You shouldnt have rezzed her yet, she cant help."

*sigh*

Sunday went back to Hate and this time it was quite different as I was the cleric for the pull group. (And yes a few people commented on why was the 48 cleric in the pull group..to which my entire group replied "because we want her". Plus one other cleric pointed out "Pre Kunark having a 48 cleric was always good enough, why isn't it now?" Made me feel good ) Died 7 times throughout the night, regaining 48 each time through rezzes, but had an absolute blast. It was fun to know what was happening every step of the way and learning to time the heals and buffs on our FD puller so as not to aggro anything. Got the chance to fight Maestro for the first time also. Unfortunately that was a failed attempt, but still I had fun anyway.

And as is true to my luck...:
It will take 5 more seconds to prepare your camp.
YOU have been slain by Maestro of Rancor!

Had to laugh at that one.

Yesterday I was playing my SK twink and was close to logging for the night when I get a tell from a friend: "Could you PLEASE come rez Fear? We had a major wipeout and no clerics."

ACK!

So, I logged on Aal, got a port over to Feerrott and ran into Fear. I find a hill full of bodies and a couple of people milling about. I couldn't resist doing a quick OOC "Did someone call for a medic?"

I then ask if there are any enchanters to rez first. The answer was "No, we didnt have any enchanters." Double ack! So instead I first rezzed the single bard and the necros. Followed by the lone pally since he could cover buffs. So there I am grouped with a bard and paladin and getting mana dumped on by 2 necros. I actually managed to do all 20 rezzes in a fairly short period of time.

Unfortunately a couple of wandering mobs decided that a camp full of newly rezzed people would be nice for lunch and I ended up having to rerez quite a few of the people. And in a bit of irony, my bard and pally managed to get us the xp for both mobs. I ended up being there about an hour and a half doing rezzes, rerezzing, buffing people...

Before I left I was overwhelmed by the thanks of the people there (not to mention the amount of begging to stay!). Not a single person made any comment about it being only a 39 rez.

The whole Fear experience reaffirmed for me why I love being a cleric.

I needed that

Fear is a strange place due to the experience or lack there of, of your puller. If you have a really good puller and makes sure you don't agro CT, you should be fine. But if you don't do a good job pulling and CT gets agroed, you are going to have problems.

Definitely wait till you have a decent amount of padding in 47 to do anything. I went on my first hate run when I was 47. Granted if things go bad you will be asked to help with healing and buffing, but generally you won't do much of the ressing unless they are in a crunch and needed. If you are going and the planes have been already broke, I think like others have stated, you should be fine to go at 47.
