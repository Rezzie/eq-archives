Has anyone else come across this undead beast in the Field of Bone? He meanders around the ruins that lead to Kaesora... he cons even and blue to me at 35, and he can be solo'ed!!

Root, he likes to resist. What I did was hit him with Panic the Dead, and the pathing on him is so good, that he runs in an L shaped pattern for the entire Fear duration. Blast him with Expel Undead, and Panic him again if needed... very nice experience.

The problem is that his spawn is about 1/2 hour or so... http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/frown.gif
