Not sure how many of us have hunted these stumbling brutes, but at 50 they're blue to me, and with some finagling can be done solo.. Solo'd for about an hour our there and then a mage friend came out and we were constant pulling these guys.. The exp flowed like fine elven wine.. I told my guild about this as well, and someone said they were good till 54th... so have a blast guys..

Seafuries have a lot of hp for their level - but their sub-par melee abilities make them perfect for duoing with tank / pet classes. I personally went through 51 and 52 in OoT. You'll start seeing greens at 52, but it's tolerable until 54 - that's when they really start greening out. The only drawback is finding a group... it's bring your own group type of place.
