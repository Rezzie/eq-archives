Prologue
The Celestial Rapture is a high end european raiding guild. We are the top guild on Kane Bayle with our maingoals of advancing in the storyline and get access to the new zones, killing the hardest mobs and beating the toughest scripts. We work together as a team. Dont bother to apply if you like to achieve your personal goals! Everyone will benefit from the guild`s progress!

Application Process
Usually, once you send your application to oculus@kanebayle.org, you ll get contacted within 1 week
by the recruitment team (Yesod, Oculus, Zwelgje). Please fill out the following application form and include it in your mail to Oculus.


Your application MUST have at least the following information:

*******
APPLICATION FORM

Equipment:
With a Magelo link. We require at least 100 in each resist with standard gear. Instant clicky and
EB item is mandatory

Keys:
List all keys you have

PoP Flags:
Time flag (the better your progression status in GoD is the higher the chance is!)

Experience:
Experience with high end mobs or/and willing to learn new encounters

Playtimes:
As mentioned above, TCR is an european raiding guild. You should be able to play from 6 pm untill midnight on an almost daily basis (Central European Time - CET). We only look for hardcore raiding people! Dont bother to apply if you consider yourself as a casual gamer

Connection:
Your inet connection must be able to handle 50 or more people raids

Past Guilds:
Which ones and why you left them

Why TCR?:
Tell us what you like about us and what you can offer to the guild

Played Time:
We wanna know how long you play this game

XServer Applicants:
All requirements that are listed here count for cross-server applicants as well. We dont accept naked xmovers applicants since its possible to move with gear
Tell Oculus if you have more questions (;t kane.Oculus)

AA`s:
List us your aa`s

All additional information is a plus too! Tell us everything you think its important to know about you.

*******

The recruitment team will look into the applications and discuss it

DONT servermove and expect to get an invite before you got our decission if we like or dont like you as an applicant!

If we see in you a potential member you will become an applicant of TCR. This will grant you a guildinvite and you
are able to join our raid, so that our members can get to know you and further evaluate your performance and disposition.
For a duration of around 3 weeks

During the applicant period you are part of TCR and able to earn eqdkp attendance.
(Loot is distributed by Velho and we only use eqdkp to see your attendance)

If all goes well, you will be notified by an officer that you have been accepted as a fullmember of TCR

As applicant member, it is unlikely that you will receive loot on guild raids
