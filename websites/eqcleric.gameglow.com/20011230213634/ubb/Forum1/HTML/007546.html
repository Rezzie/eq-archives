Here is my machines stats

P3 500mhz
512mb sdram
win 98
GeForce2MX400 64mb video card

Everything runs fine even though I am on a phone-line modem. I upgraded my video card(if you are on a budget I would recommend this card, it is down to 99 dollars and is fully compatable with SoL) and my ram, I got two 256mb sdram upgrades for 30 bucks..man that stuff is cheap nowadays.

Some more tips, if your video card is supported...SUPPOSEDLY, you can view 15 character models with 256mb of main computer ram, and all 30 models with 512mb of ram. I have verified this with a friend of mine who has the identical card and only 256mb of main ram. The bad news of course, if you have 128 mb of ram you can't enable any of the models.

Last thing..I noticed you listed your ram as 384. Never mix and match ram upgrades, especially now that they are so cheap. Two 256 mb chips will perform better than one 256 mb chip and two 128's. Sounds wierd I know, but my advice would be to buy the two large chips since the price difference is basically nil.

My machine started out about 3 years behind the times, I had never upgraded anything on it until now and started with 128mb ram and an old riva tnt/voodoo2 accelerator combo With the cost of buying Luclin I ended up spending almost 180 dollars(U.S.).
