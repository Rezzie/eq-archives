Hmm not sure here if this is advanced tactics or what, but my guild has killed Severlous multiple times without any trouble, I think that the following tactics are general knowledge on our server, but I'm more than happy to share them. The key to fighting any dragons , I mean ANY dragons is a little thing called bark potions. These can be bought at druid vendors in FV and Qeynos, perhaps other citys , but these are the only 2 I know of. Every participant in the dragon fight should have 5-6 bark potions. Basically what these potions do is root you in place and give negligible regen. The root basically nulls dragonfear, so you can wear gear keyed to 1 major resist. Don't bother with MR. Then you find a good pull place , buff, 30-36 people level 52-60 is suggested for Kunark dragons, and Wooshi. Then you send out puller, just before the target gets in AE range everyone uses bark potions, tanks planting in front, casters and healers behind and pets further behind so they don't run up and stop target short. My guild usually forms a semicircle to hit Severlous from all sides. We usually have atleast 5 balanced groups , sometimes 6, minimum of 5 cleric, lots of mages, necros for pets, and lots of melee's, basically only wizards can nuke with lures, all other nukes resisted, cannot be slowed, but 60 shaman to Malo is big help. With this tactic we have killed Severlous 10+ times in the last 6 months, our only problem now is getting Severlous before another guild does , as Terris Thule has quite a few guilds large enough to take him. As far as Wooshi is concerned he is basically identical to Severlous in his AE spells, but he is slightly higher level, as Severlous is 60, I think he is somewhere between 61-63.
Severlous does AE slow by the way, but doesn't Complete heal as far as I've seen. He does have a very odd bug where if his pet is killed he sometimes charms like 5-10 people, really wierd, we only had this happen once, so leave his pet wolfie alone when fighting him.
