Don't feel you need to join a guild just to be in a guild. A good guild is a great thing, a poor guild is a pain in the butt.

Don't join a raiding guild (one that focuses primarily on large scale raids) until you are happy with your current level, because you will stay that level for a long, long time.

Best bet is to tag along on other guilds raids to get a feel for how you like raiding and how you like the people in the guild. When the time is right to join one, the choice should be obvious.

On Velious factions, people are far more important than faction. Choose the faction that the people you want to hunt with are on, not the other way around.
