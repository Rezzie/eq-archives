Once you get to Iceclad, skip right over them silly cougars-after level 30, the exp is MUCH better in Eastern Wastes and (especially) Crystal Caverns and ToFS. If ya can squeeze into a stalag terrors group (I got into them starting at 32), the loot is passable and the exp flows like mercury (note that mercury flows bettah than water ;P) Did ToFS from 35-45 and OMG was it insane exp-I was getting a level or more a day, including hell levels (did 35 in about 5 hours on 2nd floor). Once I hit 39 I could solo the specs on 1st floor while waiting for my usual group to show up and once I hit 44 I could solo 5th floor wedding party for a lil exp and INSANE loot-I could pull 5K worth of auction gear out in 2 hours if that stupid enraged didn't spawn-if he did, I popped divine and ran the 60 game feet to the zone mirror and came back later.

As for breastplate, at 30 I think I was wearing a mithril-runed tunic, but I had more money than most from tradeskills and alot of time spent buying/selling in NFP (the auction zone on Drinal). Saw alot of bloodstained tunics, bronze, plate and even a few banded on my fellow clerics, if I remember right....
