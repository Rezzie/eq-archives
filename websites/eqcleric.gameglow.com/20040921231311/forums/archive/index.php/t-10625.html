At 34-35, you can solo the bookcase in Unrest. Two spawns at the base of the stairs, and a 3rd MOB that spawns up at the first turn after the two MOBs, (about 5 feet away), I get a bub of exp at 35 in about 3-4 hrs. The best thing is that I get experience from virtually everything, including low green monks for some bizarre reason. Just root/nuke, except for hags, where you should mem two stuns, and stun/nuke/stun/nuke/dead them. Boring as hell, but just fire up the ole TV...
