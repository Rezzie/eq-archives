I only *slightly* feel bad for killing the guards. We only kill evil guards, because we belong to a goodly aligned guild. (We even removed one person for killing Dwarf Guards.)

For places to solo, when you can't get a group: I'm able to to the single tree Spectre at Feerrott now (without a SOW) I can just sit there and duke it out with my spells, Fear and my HPs. And with a 6 minute spawn it's okay... but incrediably boring.

Kithicor is great... a clerics paradise, but it has a long downtime... even more so, becuase you'll exhaust your mana very fast and have to med at night also.

PAW is unique and challenging. I jump of the first bridge and very patiently solo blues. Bad thing is that you'll pull 2 sometimes. So I'll use fear and gate outside the PAW entrance and start over.. not worth dying. But jumping off the first bridge to the left is great. There are not that many clumped up Gnolls. Very very easy to fight singles. And a nice bonfire to rest up at that no roamers even go near.
