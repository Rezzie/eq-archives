Greetings!

The outside of Chardok castle in Burning Woods is a fantastic XP spot for 44+. The castle is in the Northeast section of the zone, along the right zone wall from frontier Mountains.

This is a Sarnak dungeon, and the outside of it has lots of fast respawns (6 minutes) for continuous pulling.

The entry gate is a 4 spawn. Inside the courtyard are four doors, each with a 2 spawn. The second door from the left has a false wall with an additional spawn behind it, so two will come on a pull from the left hand Sarnak there.

There are two divisions of outside Sarnaks, casters and melee. There are three roughly equivalant ranks within each division.

approx lvl melee caster
34-37 Knight Enthusiast
37-39 Champion Extremist
40-44 Avenger Zealot

The casters are gate-capable and will try to gate back to their spawn when below 25%. Extremists like to cast Scourge, and the Zealots like to cast an AoE poison rain, so keep RD/RP up or better yet get a necro to cast DMF.

There is a named lvl 48 Shadowknight who is a random spawn in any of the Sarnak spots. He has a 600 point Harm Touch. Of the twenty-two times we have killed him, he has had the same drop every time. Runic Carver, MAGIC LORE 2HS 18/37 WAR/PAL/RNG/SHD. Thing looks HUGE, like the sword from Final Fantasy 7.

Drops from the regular Sarnaks are about 5pp cash, with words/pages/runes and occaisional opals/emeralds/peridot.

Best technique so far has been a harmony pull to the corners of the courtyard, with two people designated to snare/root if they start running. A runner that makes it to the gate means an evac.

Our group has been 40-51 with about 20 hours pulling here, one death from a caster running out of heal range. On the other hand, I have done about 30 resses here for other groups. Have a good plan and do not die *grins*
