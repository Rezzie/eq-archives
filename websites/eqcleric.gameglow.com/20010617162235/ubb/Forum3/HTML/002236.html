Hard thing about these guys isn't really the mobs themselves - although they are kinda tough. Its the 3 protectors of zek that you have to pull before you get to the Prophets and clerics. It is almost impossible to get these guys single. I've had a couple of very good lvl 59/60 monks try it and what happens is they will pull a single and then another (or both) one will port on top of them. The protectors hit for 360 and they hit VERY fast. It IS possible for one group to kill them all but it involves kiting. If you're in the actual arena, there's not much room (its doable, I've been in a group where we've done it). If you're on the other side, where the adjudants, troopers, legionnaires and veterans spawn you MUST keep this area cleared. I've been in single groups that have done it both ways. In my case, we had a totally insane druid kiting both times. The protectors are not mezzable (like all giants) and they are not charmable except by Dictate.

Two idols seem to always drop from the Prophets. The other two idols should drop randomly from the clerics. The idols do NOT drop from Kallis.

I'm not sure how easy it would be to do with people who are on +Kael faction. I'm on Dragon faction myself. You will take negative faction hits with Kromzek, Kromriff, King Tormax and you will get a positive faction hit with Claws of Veeshan. Again I'm not sure how bad a hit you will get.

You don't have to have a shaman for slowing, the ench can do that just fine, although most ench I know don't like to slow, so having a shaman would be good. The group I was with the first time (when we killed them in the arena) was myself, a druid, an ench, a shaman, a paladin and a necro. I've also done it with a wizard, an sk, a necro, an ench and a monk.
