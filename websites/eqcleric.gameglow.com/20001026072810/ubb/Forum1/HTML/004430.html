At level 8, your melee power is almost equal to that of any hybrid. Aside from the fact that your weapon skills are capped at ((level + 1) * 4) instead of ((level + 1) * 5) like melee classes, they still do not have double attack or dual wield. Therefore, aside from missing a little more often, you are almost as viable as many other tanks of that level.

Certainly in the pre-meditation levels melee is better than sitting. Actually, melee is a viable strategy for clerics all the way to level 20ish.

I still get up and swing my hammer whenever I'm full mana and don't feel like blowing mana on nukes.
