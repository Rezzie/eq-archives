Now this isn't really a rant or anything...just a funny conversation I had today.

Let me set the background. My 10 month old female cat went into season this weekend. By Sunday she was making enough noise to make you think she was on her death bed. This goes constantly without relenting for 24 hours...it is now Wednesday. 3 nights no proper sleep. So I am in the sleep deprived nutty mood that makes me think everything is funny, and if it's not then I will find something to amuse myself. Me and my daily chanter duo partner head to FG for some experience as I am rushing for 56.

And then I get it....my side splitting laughing fun for the day...


Anon_01 : Hi
Mocca : Hello...
Anon_01 : Where are you?
Mocca : FG...
Anon_01 : I know, but where in FG?
Mocca : With my group...
Anon_01 : Grrr...where in the zone are you fighting? Killing what?
Mocca : Why?
Anon_01 : I'm comming for the rez...
Mocca : What rez?
Anon_01 : Your rez...
Mocca : My rez?
Anon_01 : Yes, your rez...your a cleric aren't you?
Mocca : /flex yes I am...almost level 56 too woot!
Anon_01 : So where are you?
Mocca : With my group still...having fun!!
Anon_01 : Grrr....where do I bring my body?
Mocca : I guess to a cleric for a rez...
Anon_01 : Yeah, you!
Mocca : Who me?
Anon_01 : Yeah, your a cleric!
Mocca : I know. But I won't rez you...
Anon_01 : What? WTF not???
Mocca : Because you didn't ask...
Anon_01 : Fine! Can I have a rez pls???
Mocca : Nope
Anon_01: WTF! Why not now??
Mocca : Because I am busy fighting in a group..
Anon_01 : Busy?! WTF! You just spent all this time talking to me...you can't be busy!!
Mocca : I am...
Anon_01 : Then wtf did you waste all my time talking to me...
Mocca : Coz it was fun...happy hunting!!
