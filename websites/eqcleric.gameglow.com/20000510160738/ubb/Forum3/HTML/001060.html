This weekend I had just finished a couple hour session at the goblin camp near unrest. I sit down near unrest to camp and do as I always do when camping in dagnors, I click around the horizon just in case I pick up a rare spawn. Get to about 10 seconds and I somehow click in Bilge. He's on the middle island heading to my right toward that orc camp. I about my camp and move to where I think he'll go. It takes 60 seconds for Bilge to get to shore before I started attacking him.

I'm lvl 29, full mana, with a couple buffs still active (including sow) and see that he con's yellow to me. I figure best to at least attempt him. I enstill, wrath, run, repeat, etc... Eventually I'm getting near death and Bilge is about 50%, I'm about 2m, and I've picked up a tidal lord along the way. I hit DA, and head back toward the unrest tunnel and yell for help. I get to the mouth of the unrest tunnel and turn around to see a druid and another casting on him. I drop invuln and fire off one more blast. I get the kill and experience for. With my health at less than a bubble I quickly run over loot Bilge then high tail it into unrest to clear my aggro list, the tidal was still on my tail.

I was told that I owe this druid the loot for saving my life. He offered my a rubi helm and 300pp as compensation for it. That's almost insulting, I'd rather he let me die and taken the kill that way. He say's it's the honorable thing to do. Also earlier he had been seeking a rez and I gave him two direct tells offering revives that went unanswered by him.

This druid has contacted my guild leader and wants to roll for the item.

I see it much differently. When someone yells for help around me I always kick off a heal and not try to kill what they were attacking. I believe that Bilge is a FFA and that I won the contest (majority of the damage, plus saw him and attacked him first and for 30+ seconds). My guild leader won't tell me the druid's story, nor any witness story. I think the druid is pissed he wasn't able to KS Bilge from me. For once a 29th cleric beats out a 45th druid on Bilge. If Bilge was undamaged and both the druid and I cast on him from the start I would have lost easily by DD amount and by the druids lower casting/recast times.

Question:
Is Bilge a FFA? I was in the zone for at least two hours without seeing a shout/ooc indicating anyone was looking for him. I had an easy 90 seconds before first seeing him to when I needed to hit invuln. Thats more than enough time for any waiting druid to track him and kill him.

Reguardless of how my guild decides, I'm keeping the Pearl Kedge Totem.
