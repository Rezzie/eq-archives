The one skel tower near the xroads is a good spot to start practicing your undead spells. There is a skel that is not linked to the right side of the tower which you can pick off and blast with undead and melee. Once you get better at it or if they are seperated, you can start picking on the skel on the left side of the tower which is linked to the one inside of the tower. Also there is a skel ruins not too far from the tower. Then if you can find a group you could probably go to the chessboard around 10 or so.
