Get a group of your friends together and sneak past the spiders and raveners at the front, head down to the library area.
Some very good exp and loot down there fighting unfactioned (so far as I can remember) undead. If you don't mind the faction hits with Overthere Outpost, take out some spiders too...see if you can get one of the rare Strathbone shields...very nice replacement for your CGS; get 9 more ac and 10 poison resistance for 1 less wisdom.
