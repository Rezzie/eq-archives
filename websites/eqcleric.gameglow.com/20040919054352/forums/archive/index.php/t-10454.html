From level 16 - 18 the Orcs at High Hold Pass have been a pleasure to kill. I rarely have to search for a group. Just walk over avial yourself as a Cleric in need of a group and usually you are fought over for your services.

The loot can be very nice, with many rust weapons, ring mail armor, words, and such falling. With a vendor less than 2 minutes away, turning the these around brings almost no down time. Be warned that the looting isn't every 6th, alpha, or such but more of just grab a loot when you get a chance with the over greedy being kicked out of the group.

The spawn rate is very nice. I usually sit and med, heal and buff ad watch the exp and auto loot come in.

Also be warned at most times the Orcs are a free for all. When there are two full groups running the Orcs are down in seconds and victor goes the spoils.

From level 14- 16 the Gnolls at the other end provide some very nice exp. They spawn is much slower than the Orcs, but also giving you more time to heal up and rest.

The City itself provides all you can wish for in supplies, bank, merchants. Non-self binding classes can bind anywhere in the zone, so traveling to your corpse after a death is low down time.

Enjoy!

Well, I've spent a little time there, in different pickup groups. Experience is great, as everyone says. However, I cannot recommend a pickup group at the orcs if you need cash.

If there's only one group at the orcs, the situations I was in, it gets pretty hectic if you're the healer. I was constantly on the verge of being OOM, casting a Heal as soon as I got enough mana for a single spell, with 2-3 people usually below 3 bubbles of health. I had to med during the brief interlude between fights, of course. Somehow, nobody died (even though after I called OOM and one tank was below 2 bubbles of health and still fighting, I had to specifically ask the Shaman to heal).

This means that I was completely left out of the looting process, as the melees were looting as soon as the corpses hit the ground (even if the fight was still going on). When I complained that I needed money, too, I was given a ring mail bracer and a bronze rapier. Over 4 bubbles of experience at level 18, I think I got about 5pp of loot, and spent about 2pp or so on agates for Symbol.

In a group of friends, it would be a lot better. Or, if you don't need money, the experience is great. But, if you need cash, I'd recommend the gnolls - it's less experience, but also less hectic, so you at least have a CHANCE to get some of the dropped loot for cash, and scalps for faction.
