Last night I was killing some time in Sol B while waiting for a friend to log on and I heard the people gearing up for a raid on Naggy (it was primarily a guild raid). As a joke at one point, I ooc'd "Nah, you guys aren't ready, you need another cleric!" Immediately after I get a tell from a friend (*hugs Lythander* http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif) saying "WE DO! Where are you and do you know the way down?"

Woot! Seems that 54 druid didn't want to be playing cleric to two warriors. Can't imagine why. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif Five minutes later I'm with a group fighting my first fire giant. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Soon after we killed the first few giants, they did a preroll for some upcoming potential no drop items (mithral mask, Naggy's monk book, Skarlon's sword). They automaticallly included all INT and WIS casters on the mask (their rules, and no, I'm not opening this up to arguments :P) and to my great shock, I won the preroll! The other two preroll winners happened to be in my group too...talk about an excited group! Now not only was I excited about getting to be on the raid, but I was really hoping that Rokyl remembered to get dressed right. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif

Well to make a long story short (too late I know):

-Rokyl DID have the mask (woohoo!) so I am now very happy to have a higher AC in a slot I didn't think I'd be able to without giving up WIS. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

-Only one of my two warriors died (got summoned back just he was headed back for a heal of course) but I lost both my druid and my shaman when the casters rushed in. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/frown.gif

-I didn't get to see Naggy alive since the clerics stayed back in the prep room so I still really haven't seen a dragon http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/frown.gif but I did get a great screenshot of him dead. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

-My group thanked me for coming by giving me 40 jasper and 20 peridots. Completely unnecessary, totally unexpected, but very much appreciated.

All in all, I had a complete blast! Now I can't wait for my first chance to try Vox. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
