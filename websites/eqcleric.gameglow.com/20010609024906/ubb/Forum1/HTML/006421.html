To enlighten you of the known rules by which a pickpocket lives, they cannot steal magic items, nor can they steal from things which are not humanoid and alive. /shrug.
I guess in closing I'll say that if you don't trust the rogue in your group then by all means exclude them. It's really your loss. If that gem is truely that important to you, it's your choice.

Very VERY long ago there used to be a separate coin loot pool for pickpocketing. When I started in Beta 3 I had a cleric and a rogue, and my rogue would regularly pick handfuls of 120, 60, 80 copper off of orc pawns. This was also when you got pickpockets at level 4 I believe.
