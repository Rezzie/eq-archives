I spam Remedy when the puller is out getting mobs until I start to cast. Mobs in general are higher and hence resist much more making 2 enchanters in luclin AE groups a definite bonus. Combine that with the fact that the puller has run speed 3, means that there is much less damage to be healed so swopping a healer out for an enchanter is a good move.

Who's pulling? If it's you, have all your Divine Auras up, a fast casting heal, a better heal, and if you have room, Upheaval (why not? might as well nuke too, not much healing needed after the pull). If someone else is pulling, lose some of the Divines, load a stun or two just in case, and perhaps a root.

Things to remember. AoE groups are virtually always Pure Caster only, save for the Cleric and/or Bard.

Self buffed, I have about 1200hp, not very much.

FAST Heals are critical. You'll probably never need Complete Heal in an AoE group, what you need is to be able to pop those heals off on the chanters quickly when a few of the mobs resist the stun. Unless pulling, the AoE damage casters will rarely take any hits, but the chanters will take some lumps while they get them stun locked, and looking stupid.


Once the pull is completed, and the heals done, cut loose on your damage yourselves, have a little fun.
