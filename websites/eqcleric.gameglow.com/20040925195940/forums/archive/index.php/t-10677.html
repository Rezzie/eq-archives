I'd had friends of mine telling me how great the XP was in this zone, but once I got there this weekend, I realized they were being modest.

THIS ZONE ROCKS!!!

I was there in 2 different groups this weekend at the Frontier Mountains zone/plateau thing:
1. 42 cleric (me), 36 monk, 39 druid. We were nonstop pulling. 1 bub xp in 2 hours. I was chain-stunning, buffing, and laying down the occ. heal (never over 40 mana), and the druid was primary healer and SOWer. This was a fun, fast-paced way to play over the traditional heal-med-buff-heal-med-buff way of life.
2. 42 cleric (me), 38 enchanter, 37 wizard, 36 druid, 35 druid, and 44 paladin. Paladin was pulling nonstop, i was chain-stunning (oh, how i love these spells against the hard-hitting MOBs), one druid was primary healer, other druid was DOTting, wizard was just a mana cannon, and the enchanter was performing crack duties and mana cannoning. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif 2 bubs of experience in about 4 hours.

I am bound at this location for at least the next two levels. 44 doesn't seem that far away now...

Only trauma associated with this zone are the wandering Dragoons. Quad attack for 137 damage, and really just don't like us wee halflings... So when you hear your neighborhood tracker /ooc "Dragoon to FM Zone" - be SOWed or Jbooted, cuz if they hit a member of your party, be prepared to boogie.
