If you can avoid the constant trains within Karnors, the drolvarg sentries are farily easy to solo. Even before you get Reconning, you can take out the sentries. Just pull them to close to the zone and root/nuke them till they drop. Sit down near the zone and med up. I do this a lot and quite often I get a drive by clarity from enchanters. At 53, the ragers that spawn at the entrance to the caste in the DL are mainly blue. Most of hte petrified cockatrices in DL will be blue. Some of the widow spiders will be blue. Some of the big ape things will be blue. Skyfire is not soloable by a cleric. I tried at 54 and at 55 on the easiest mobs in the job and was not able to finish them. Even with hitting for full damage I ran out of mana before I finished them - this is with 200 wis and no +mana items at the time.

The spectral keepers near the EJ zone will be mixing high greens with blues. They are a bit tougher than specs were as there are a variety of diffent types. Most all of them have some sort of spell nuke. The spectral champions are the easiest to solo in my opinion as they have no spells. They do pack quite the melee damage though, so be sure to stun them if they break your root.
