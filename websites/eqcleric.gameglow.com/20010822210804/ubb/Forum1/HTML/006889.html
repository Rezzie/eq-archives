Actually... the benefit of DA/Quit is that you only have to take a beating for roughly 15 seconds tops in order to log, and unless you are the only thing there the agro won't be on you as much during that 15 secs. If you try to camp with DA on, you cast it, its lasts 15 sec, but you have to be SITTING for an additional 15 and if you get hit you blow the whole thing and now have to start over the 30 sec process with one less DA spell.

If you DA and then /q, your LD NPC has 15 sec of no fear just to stand there and then after that if the mobs come back they just have to live for 15 more sec and will be logged out regardless of the pounding they are taking. If you can live for 15 secs you are safe using this method. If all clerics did this the probability of at least one of them logging out before dying is pretty high. Remember if everyone tries to camp, all mobs have to do is run around and hit each of the sitting clerics to make sure that no one lives. Everyone doing DA/q means they have to kill every single one of you in 15 secs or someone will make it out.
