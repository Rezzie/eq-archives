I would not use a long /pause block because even with a fizzle cather there is the chance you will be standing there for the entire time unable to do anything. Remember, as long as /pause is in effect your client is basically locked up.

To do an auto sit however, do the following AT YOUR OWN RISK

/cast #
/g your CH message...
/pause 100
/sit

My 2cp is I'd rather spam them twice if I fizzle than run the risk of standing there unable to do anything.
