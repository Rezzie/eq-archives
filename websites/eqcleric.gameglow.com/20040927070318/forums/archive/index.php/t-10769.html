Just FYI for anyone looking for some solo XP. Lower Guk is certainly the place to hang out. At 44+, you can start working your way through the entrance, the bedroom, and down to the assasin/supplier room. Once you hit 50 and get Improved ITU, you need to make your way down to the safe hall near the AM room. There's tons of stuff to pull from both the jail hall and the Lord's hall. Even with competition for the spawns I was getting a blue every 30-35 minutes at level 50. Root/nuke/stun. And be ready to gate if you get adds http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
