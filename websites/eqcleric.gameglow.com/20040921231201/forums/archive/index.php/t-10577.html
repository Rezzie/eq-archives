It should be possible to solo here at this level, but I think it could be counter productive, with regards to down/zone time.

The dead side zone into lower guk leads is a passage way which forks into two passages in a small room.

The passage way and room contain live frog spawns (warrior and shaman types).

The left passage from this room is live as well.

The right side is undead. About 4 - 5 undead type spawns (warriors and wizards - wizards hit VERY hard but dont have many hp).

At you level you could possibly take either live or dead one-on-one, but two+ will more than likly mean you have to zone. Unless other people are camping the same spot you or have already cleared it out, you will most likely get two or more most of the time.

Personaly i'd leave lower guk till the next level range. At that level you will get more out of it. Also you will be spending a lot of time here (although not at the zone) for the rest of remaining life as you charicter. It can start to get a bit repetative after a while http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/wink.gif

I personally think that Lower guk is a nightmare at level 32. I got to level 33 but thanks to a long string of deaths I am 32 again.

The problem is that there is always a group camping the first room (where you go right for deadside and left for live side). And my problem is i got bored. I got into a good group, and we camped the bedroom and everything we could pull to there (except greater Icebones). The problem is that it is Train Central.

Four Greater Icebones spawn a short ways in and tend to get trained. now these four will make short work of most groups not in their 40s my group was 40th paladin, 40th shadowknight, 35th cleric, 34th druid, 33rd Cleric and 31 paladin. Keep in mind we were all nicely equipped (ghoulbanes for the pallys, dark reaver for SK, etc). the entire group was dead within 1 minute or so (me being the first killed in 10 seconds).

If you are going to hunt in lower guk in the low 30s then stay near the zone. If someone yells train, invis to undead and run. going deeper into lower guk seriouly lessens your chances of making it to the zone (don't get me started on the Re-Animated Hand incident or the Mino Patriarch. Also, be patient. If you get bored, stay there. Even with the good group i had it was a nightmare.
