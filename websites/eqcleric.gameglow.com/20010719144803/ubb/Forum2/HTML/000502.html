It's a trade-out spell from Fistun, in the hut close to the ramp leading to the cleric spell merchant in FV.

He gives a quest where turning in some spells (Reckoning, Upheaval, Death Pact or Yaulp IV) returns a random spell (Sunskin, Heroic Bond, Unswerving Hammer of Faith or Word of Vigor).

If you can't find someone selling the spell, you can keep turning in spells and gamble on getting WoV from the quest.
