I just want to say that I ran into Zahal the day before yesterday and immediately shouted for help from my guild. Two Druids were the only ones online and immediately came to help. I tried to solo Zahal and kept telling the first druid to hold Regis's spot so we wouldnt get KS'd. Luckily for me, she ignored me and saved my butt. . We then proceeded to find Regis and the were wolf hunters were out, but no farmers were there. We decided to engage him even though our mana was at 40 % each because we were sure our luck wouldn't hold. We lit into him and about the time our mana ran out he was nearly dead so I began casting wrath to finish him off. Doh! He healed himself to just above half life. We were both dumped so I started medding furiously and one druid melee'd (Hah!) while the other cast heal on her. Every ounce of my new found mana went back into healing the fighting druid. I told her to run several times but she wouldn't go (Same one who insisted on saving me instead of holding Regis's spawn spot). When Regis was almost out of mana, he began casting smite of all things with the last of it and killed her. The cadacus of the sacrament began to exact our revenge and pitifully slowly I beat him to death.

A couple weeks ago a Bard and Druid friend of mine ran into those two and killed them both and gave me my first bracer. Now I have two. I still think they should be no drop, but I am glad that I got these myself (with a little help from my friends).
