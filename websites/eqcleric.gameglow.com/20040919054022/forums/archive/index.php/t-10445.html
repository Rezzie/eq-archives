Something to note about Befallen is that it's incredibly dangerous trying to solo anything beyond the first floor. So in the larger levels, you're going to need a group... and finding one inside Befallen can be tough.

Also keep in mind that getting your corpse back if you die is a real pain. The different sections of Befallen are all hidden behind locked doors that need keys to get through. Since the keys are now on your corpse and not on you, it gets tricky. If you beat a rat or something small up that will run, they will run through the locked doors and -- lo and behold! -- the rats can open the locked doors without a key. So just follow that sucker through.

Another warning? There are necromancers in the dungeon. This means that Invisibility vs. Undead is risky at best for something like corpse retrieval. Necros will see straight through it and, after they poke you once, everything nearby that's aggro will jump on you.

So! Befallen's not a bad dungeon at all, but try to keep a healthy group with you and keep in mind that dying here is very, very painful.
