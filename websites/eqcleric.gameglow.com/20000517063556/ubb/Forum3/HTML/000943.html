I found the best way to kill Maugrim is to spook or root her pet, hit her with a stun, and then make a mad dash for the shore. She'll follow, and without the other necros to heal her or her pet to interrupt your spells, she goes down fairly quickly.

The pet may come back during the fight, just reapply the spook/root. She was an easy kill at 35.

I believe she's either a random or timed spawn - when I arrived at Kerra, she was already up. The one other person in the zone, a druid who had been camping Mitty for several hours, told me there'd been no one else in the zone during that time.
