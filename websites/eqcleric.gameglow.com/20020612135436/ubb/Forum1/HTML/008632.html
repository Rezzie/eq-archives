Actually, no, he's not fine. When you first put a point into a skill, it starts off at your current level. And with Specialization, you will not get one over 50, until each are at least 1.

So he's got alteration at 50. Which is where it stayed. Then at 54, he put a point into the rest... the first one got to be 54, the rest are 50.

You need to do the reset specialization quest in the Temple of Ro. Details here

I bet it didnt credit Specialize in Abjuration, Divination, Conjuration and Evocation at 54, I'll bet it put them at 30, the level we get the ability to specialize. You can test this by looking at your skills menu. I forget the exact ranges but the terms are like "feeble" "very bad" "awful" and so-on. If I am remembering correctly those run in 10 point blocks, so to check and see if your in trouble or not, look at all your specialize fields.

Alteration SHOULD be the highest because it's at 50.

Evo, Div, Conj, Abj should all be tied at whatever term is for 30 points.

If one of them is different, and above the level of Alteration, then it's like Kero said, you got credited with 54 points the first time you added a point into whichever skill it was and now you have to go do the reset quest in Temple of Sol.
