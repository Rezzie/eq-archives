A couple changes in the Epic seem to have occurred. Perhaps more, but these are the two steps I just completed ... LOL

1) Natasha Whitewater does NOT get involved in killing the Spirit of Flame. The Spirit conned green to me and was easily taken with just myself and a lvl 55 DRU. No need to worry about KSing Natasha.

2) The NPC's in the Temple of Solusek Ro doe NOT get involved with killing the Plasmatic Priest. Your group must be ready to do it all! The Priest was a level 55 fight -- highly magic resistant . The DoD cast by a 54 SHM did 'stick'. A Lvl 52 MNK was able to be very effective in damage as well.
