Hi there,

Add DA (lvl 1) and stun (lvl 5) to your line up. They are still part of my regular lineup at 57. DA will save your life many times over your career. Stun is useful for interrupting casters, giving the tanks a little time to kill runners and stopping things beating on the enchanter until it gets taunted off.

Root is always useful for parking adds out of mellee range.

You have a necro in the group so fear may be useful. Have the necro cast engulfing darkness on the mob and you fear it. The mob will then run away slowly and everyone can chase after it and beat it up without taking damage.

Always try and keep you HP and AC buffs up on the party. Cast on yourself 1st, then work through the others in the group. This way you don't have to keep them memoorized, when they start to flash, you know that they are about to need refreshed on everyone and can sit down and mem them.

Hope these help.
