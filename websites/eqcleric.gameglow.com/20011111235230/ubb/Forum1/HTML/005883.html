Tower of Frozen Shadow, 6th floor is great exp.

You need to bring your group though and second floor key is hard to get. 5th floor is probably green, but has some nice gear drops.

Trakanon's Teeth has lots of undead to kill.

Kaesora still gives experience I think and down by the church is nearly always camped, but they might very likely be happy to hang with a cleric down there. Not much in the way of loot down by the church though

City of Mist is a very nice zone to get lots of exp and you can get a pickup group there.
