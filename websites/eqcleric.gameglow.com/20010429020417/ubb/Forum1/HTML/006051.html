Kaesora is a great dungeon. It's particularly good to pick a night and go with your guild/friends. Things about Kaesora in no particular order:

1. It's in FOB very close to the swamp zone. Stop by EQAtlas and get a map of FOB and I'm sure it's on there.

2. You can't go out the way you came in - you'll have to fight your way around to the exit, or gate out.

3. Melee folks can be bound right outside

4. The experience modifier is big.

5. There are two basic types of creatures - the spiders and the undead. Each have an assortment of drops. Each have healer-types among them.

It's a great place to hunt and is not often inhabited let alone camped (at least on my server). Make sure you bring a good map - it takes a little time to get the hang of it.

The library area (where spirit tome drops) is particularly nasty. Roamers outside the library are clerics, and will heal like clerics. :P

If I remember correctly (and it's been a while since I went there) there is a sharp demarcation line between undead and spider sections of the dungeon. Once you get to the last spider room (it's the second to last room on the map from the ramp down to the library) kill those mobs, and cast invis vs. undead. Go down the ramp to the start of the library and pull roamers, then pull statics. At least that's the way I did it - but your mileage may vary, as I was playing an enchanter.

As you go up the step of the library, there is a porch that wraps all the way around the building. There are two roamers there, both cleric mobs. (maybe three? it's been a while.) They will agro on you if you are fighting inside, and they most certainly will heal the mobs inside even if they aren't agro. It's really annoying when the mob running gets healed and start bolting at a full gallop.

The library is probably the second hardest part of the zone. (only second to the church) I would try doing spiders first to get a hold of the dungeon, and get acquainted with the exit. Oh, and your melee friends can get bound in Kaesora, so CR should be a breeze should something go wrong.

When you zone in and go down the ramp there is an option to go right left or straight. To your right is where you come when you zone out. Straight ahead is two drops. If you lev down to level 2 there is only one mob there. If you fall all the way down you will be surrounded by about 5 spiders. One possible named spider. My suggestion is to make a left and fall one level. When you hit bottom do not open any doors. You will be standing in a doorway one mob and pit to your left and the frenzy room to your right and a door straight ahead.

Pull the one on your left (watch out for the pit) and then pull the three from the frenzy room. After those 4 are done move into the frenzy room. There will be one closed door and one door way. There will be one you can pull from past the doorway and two that are behind the door.

Kill the two from behind the door and move into that room. In there will be a false wall that leads to the library. Past the wall is all undead. At the bottom of the first ramp will be three undead mobs. Head past the other ramp and there will be another three mobs. Turn the corner and down the hall is the library. Avoid camping in the library itself. Three good spots to camp is 1. Right at end of hall where only one mobs pops, 2. Pull to the fountain area in front of the library, 3. After you clear library head through the library to the back stairs behind it.

Try and bring an evacer to get out. If you need to work way out then go back up to the frenzy room and go through the one doorway with the one mob. Make a left and you will see a huge open pit. Drop down to find 2-3 more mobs. If you continue straight, that will be the way out. If you turn around, then you are headed towards the church.

Most green at 44. Shield most likely drops in Frenzy room. Undead mobs with loot will run. Good luck.
