I think I've figured it out after sitting in front of the undead camp for SIX DAYS. Yes... after 100+ continuous hours and much hard work, I have figured out the following.

If you KILL the standing putrid skeleton spawn in the hut withuot the yellow cone (whether it be PH or not), the next spawn will not happen for another 3.5 hours (210 minutes).

If you leave it alone and let it despawn at 8pm EQ time, (this usually takes 70 minutes) then another will spawn 5 minutes after.

If I "protect" him and let him live, I have a chance at Lord Grimrot in 70 minutes. If he gets killed by a PC or NPC, the next spawn is in 210 minutes.

So... please explain to me how it makes sense for a holy cleric (or paladin) to have to beg others NOT to kill a vile undead? Heck, I even have to kill wandering Aviak egrets and darters to protect my precious PH.

Not only that, how do you suggest I prevent others from killing the PH? This is ridiculous! All it takes is one idiot to kill my PH, and I'm going to have to wait 210 minutes for the next spawn... How fair is that?

Basically, the way the spawn is now, it is WAY too easy for others to come and disrupt it. Personally, I think it's the same logic of someone being able to cast SCoW (SOW upgrade) on someone else who is kiting to screw them up. What? You fixed that? Well... maybe you'll consider fixing this, then.

Someone killing the PH should make him spawn FASTER, by normal logic... Honestly, I think it's a typo... 210 minutes should be 21 minutes... 21 minutes if you kill the PH, 70 minutes if you don't. That sounds about right, in my most humble opinion...

I've petitioned numerous times, and put in bug reports... the answers I've gotten are "Lord Grimrot is rare" (along the lines of "deal with it") and "that seems odd - please fill out a /bug report"

Well, I'm posting here as well for help. If it is a bug, PLEASE, for the love of EQ, fix it. I'm left to frantically protecting a NPC that I can't even heal, and the penalty for him dying is a 3.5 hour spawn time?! WAY TOO big.

As for evidence... Well, from 5/16 to 5/17, the spawn was utterly broken, and nothing came up for 32 consecutive hours. Once the server reset on 5/18, the spawn seemed to be fixed.

The next two days, we killed every spawn in the hut, and found that nothing would spawn there for 210 minutes. Basically, we would only get 6-7 spawn chances a day. An impossible camp, really, considereing he has PH's too.

For the other two days, we figured out that letting him despawn is better, and protected the spawn as best we could, and he promptly despawned at 8pm. By this method, we got 18-20 spawns a day.

STILL... all it took was one lv.10 newbie with SoW to agro the PH, and we were asessed a 3.5 hour next-spawn penalty. Sigh.

To all the clerics and paladins out there who seek the Aegis... Please try this out for yourself, or simply take the word of a friend who has spent the last 6 days of his life figuring this out. For the rest of our friends out there, please show your support as well.
