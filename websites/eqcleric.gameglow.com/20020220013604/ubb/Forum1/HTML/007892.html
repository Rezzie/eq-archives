Paladins and sk's make great main tanks in xp groups. They both can outtaunt a warrior, taunting isn't an issue. Their damage output may not be as high, but a warrior's role isn't damage output, it's damage absorption. This is a role that paladins and sk's are more than sufficient at for normal mobs. Your new guildmate sounds like one of those clerics shouting at the zone-in to sebilis: "group of 5 looking for an enchanter to move".

The other night we did a chardok dungeon crawl with only one plate wearing tank: a 55 paladin. 7 of us made it to the king and killed him without effort. No deaths at all at the king fight, it was laughably trivial compared to the early days. (group was cleric enc paladin monk monk rogue nec)

When a warrior becomes a main consideration for an xp group is when mobs are hitting around 250+ often, like ssra temple. They have discs for when it gets hairy and they have the extra hp to minimize downtime due to less frequent ch. For normal mobs, even mobs like juggs, a paladin or sk is fine. Your new guildmate is overreacting a wee bit too much.
