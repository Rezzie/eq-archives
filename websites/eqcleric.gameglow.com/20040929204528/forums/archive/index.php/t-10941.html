Need for CH rotation isn't the point at all. It works, it works well, and it
isn't cheating. So why not use it? The fact that you are going to use a
perfectly viable method that keeps the most people alive at the end (quite
often everyone) is not a thing that one should be ashamed of.

Some battles are easier done without it (I mean, except for pre-slowed
Dozekar, who needs a rotation with him?), but when you are first doing something
doing it easy and making it work is better than doing it hard and failing.
Any combat where the main tank will evaporate before a single CH can
hit (and there are many) from full health begs for a rotation. And if you
say 'well, use several main tanks and have 2-3 clerics on each' that is
a rotation, just a smaller one. Our guild, mostly primal-less (but should
start getting some soon), quite often does battles with only 3 clerics in
their own personal rotation on the MT, while all the other clerics heal
their group. Much more interesting, and less prone to battle spam, than
a full-fledge rotation between 6-7 clerics.

When you are trying things with small numbers, keeping all attention
on the MT is kinda key, dontcha know..
