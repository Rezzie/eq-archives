Are you 50+? Not sure (you didn't mention) but you're running around with a 60 enchanter, so if you are....

I like the following zones as a 58th level cleric:

1. Maidens Eye. The exp is so/so, but the loot is quite good, although rare. There's an ac15 wis9 visor that drops here, along with an ac / wis ranged idol thats good. Be careful of the wolves - they have too many hps, and they aggro from clear across the field. Lotsa wolves are a pain.

2. Fungus Grove. Loot is pretty much nonexistant, except for the bandit rings, stuff from caller rares, and the occasional random drop from plants (from what I've seen). However there are almost always people here so you can LFG pretty easily. It's not too far from Shadowhaven, and there's a little town there to restock your supplies. Exp is pretty good.

3. The Grey. My favorite so far. You can do well here with a duo (mage and I did this for hours on end), or with a group. You don't need an enchanter or be successful, certainly. Cash loot is pretty good (scales drop pretty fast) and the random loot from rares can be an upgrade for you depending on your equipment situation. Exp can be pretty good if you pull fast. With a full group near the temple, you can pretty much pull non-stop and get a kill every 20 seconds or so (Epitaph of Life doesn't even have time to run out). 3 kills / minute is pretty fast exp. This is dependant on no-one else camping temple, however...odd hours help here.

I haven't been back to Sebilis or Velks for weeks.
