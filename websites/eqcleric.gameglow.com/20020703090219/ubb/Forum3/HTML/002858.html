Our guild along with a few others, made a day of killing Ixiblat. Naxton Deepwater despawns after turn-in. Ixiblat then spawns at the other end of the zone. Naxton Deepwater spawns once per EQ day, approximately 72 minutes between spawns.

We set up our groups near the Burning Woods/Skyfire zone line. Cleric doing the turn-in was bound at our camp spot. Cleric would go do the turn in and gate back to camp. We had a ranger at Ixi's spawn point to invite Ixi back to our party.

It took us almost 7 hours, but we did Ixiblat 6 times that day, and an allied guild did Ixi twice that day ahead of us.
