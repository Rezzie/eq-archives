The best way we have found is to tank him low, while everyone but tanks stays out of AE range. Make sure no casters agro him, as that will drag him into the caster/healer area, which should be out of the ae range. When he gets down to 40% or even a bit lower the casters rush in and nuke as fast as they can. Last time we did this we had no deaths, and rarely do we lose more than 2 or 3 if things go well.

The main thing to remember is to keep as few people in the ae life tap range as you can. He can be nasty if he starts healing as fast as your hurting him because he life taps the casters.

Get necros to mana feed clerics, and make sure all healers have a rod at the start of the fight, this should give your tanks enough healing to get mystro low enough that the castes can hit him.
