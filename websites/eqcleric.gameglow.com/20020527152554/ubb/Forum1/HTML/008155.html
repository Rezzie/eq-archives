I tried these today, on test during a HOT farming. This is what I found.


Conservation (mana cost reduction?)

Noticable. I can normally crank out 10 complete heals on FM. Today I was cranking out a few extra. Some complete heals seemed to actually use around 1/4 a bubble. That'd be 20 complete heals if they all did that... (they dont though)


Diuturnity (spell duration)
Never noticed it as i wasnt buffer. most our buffs lasted forever anyways. Druid casting the short duration circles didnt notice either. If its 10% like the other 50+ items are, then likely its a minimal change. 10% of an hour is 6 minutes. yay.

Destruction (nuking damage)
Noticable change. Wizard was happy, sunstrike doing an extra 100 damage. Reckoning was doing 769 max.

Pernicity (spell casting haste)
Wow. Right clicking complete heal informed me that 'Casting time is 10.0. YOUR casting time is 9.0' man that's nice. Divine light faster is nice too. reckoning faster is nice tooooo. =p Some minor bugs we found with heals + this, but they're in the tester database and will probably be fixed before going live.

Abatement (lower aggro?)
No clue. All the focus items 'twinkle' or something when they work. This item never twinkled for me. Broken, or always on, I cant say for certain. Whether it works for heals, i cannot say for certain. I hope this becomes a more obvious effect. Definately the weaker of all the above.

wearing this item, my first heal on our tank vs klandicar got me ate, so... =p probably not working.

Otherwise, these are all great changes. The summoned items have + ac, and some mana and hp. I think very few uberguilds would trade these items out for their HP/MANA gear from NTOV, but for group hunting + limited use on raiding for medium sized guilds, the mage items are pretty nice. Basically if you're not wearing something uber in that slot, no reason not to put the focus item there.

This also opens the possiblity of uber items for us casters. Warriors get hatebringer? +700 hate proc? Clerics get peacebringer, a shield that has 50% less agro. Melee get 40% haste on their melee? Casters might get 40% haste on their spells.

This is a nice shot in the arm for casters, and will sure as hell hang the proverbial carrot back in front of our noses. ^_^
