>with the two CHA items I have I can raise it to 66

Slightly off-topic - a serious priority might be to raise your charisma. This could be accomplished by buying a set of golden cats eye bracelets (+7 cha per, and can buy from a chanter for under 100 plat) followed by a set of opalline earrings (+5 cha per, 50 plat or less in Unrest - drop off the undead barkeep). This would give you 90 charisma, and you'd seriously see yourself spending less for spells/reagents while selling for a far greater value than currently. If you fight in the right places (FS drops or better) you'll see the cha items pay for themselves within a few days. 100 cha is all you'd ever need, prices increase/drop in increments of 10. And don't ever neglect faction...

You should be able to get a base cost for each of the spells from Casters Realm. I believe, with the exception of Earthquake, they cost around 21-22pp each. So 200pp should cover the cost of all your 44 spells.

The reagents are a different matter entirely... If you're tight on money now, make it a point to do three things:

1) hunt things that have good cash or loot drops
2) get yourself as much charisma gear as possible
3) work on your faction with your home city (get it up to at least kindly)
