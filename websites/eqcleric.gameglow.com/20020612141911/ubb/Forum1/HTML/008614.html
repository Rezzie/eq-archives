Yes, I have soiled the plane of growth. As such, this nifty toy (spirit stone) seemed a rather poor investment since I might die upon landing in felwithe at the hands of my trainer.

Here is how I fixed it in about 2 1/2 hours.
I'm a level 60 cleric btw...(how many other high elf tunare worshippers are going to hunt pog?)

MUFFINS (of course)

The muffin eating dwarf in west freeport in the hogcaller inn 2nd floor (near the umm..dirt spot on the road) is super ultra green at 60, and won't attack even if you get up close and dance with him.

I filled up with muffins and brought an enchanter with me, but any inviser (with high level) will do.

Key:
Walk up with 4 muffins, hand them to him, and then have your friend cast invis on you when the trade window is open. Hand him 16 muffins at a time (4 in each box) and he'll accept as long as you are invis when you click trade.

After about 2 hours of doing this, I was dubious to the dwarf and could tell my invising friend to leave.

Muffins are sold nearby in west freeport at the theatre of the tranqil.

Note that 2 times while I did this, the dwarf agroed and a simple root atone calmed him down and let me continue.

I will kill Tunare again, but I'm going to make sure I don't take any faction hits while doing it

Good luck,
