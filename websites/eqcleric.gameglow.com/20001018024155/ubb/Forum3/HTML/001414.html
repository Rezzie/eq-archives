Well as you all know from prior posts I was very fortunate. I logged in once last week after a server reboot to find the live Lord Grimrot spawned in SK. This morning I logged in to check and the dead Lord Grimrot was standing there keeping watch over his minions.. 3 Retributions later and his heart was mine.. (yeah I know, I could have Banished. I was so excited i did not even bother to sit and mem undead spells)

I completed the quest today after a bit of precationary faction improvements and now have my Aegis of Life shield (err cape).

I wanted to post some info I got today from a guide concerning this quest. I petitioned a GM to come and watch as I turned the items in knowing that some people had problems and had lost items when trying to complete the quest. This is the info I got from the GM.

A NPC can hold 4 items in there "quest" inventory. If there are currently three items given to him and you give him a 4th when you click trade if he does not have "ALL" of the correct items all items in his slots will be erased and he will be reset.

What does this mean to you.. Well lets say that I go to him and give him one item per trade in 3 seperate trades and then leave. Then some Cleric/paly comes along to do the Aegis quest and turns in the heart to test faction. The NPC says please give me the other item, but since he had 4 items in his "Quest" inventory he will destroty them. Now the Cleric/Paly gives the Scythe and lo and behold he gets no shield.. This is because the heart was already item 4 and gets destroyed.

The GM suggested that before attempting any quest where I am turning in multiple items (not in one trade window) that you open a trade window and place one bottle of water in each of the 4 boxes then trade.. After doing this the NPC will be in a reset state and ready to accept all items... I thought this was a usefull bit of info and figured I would pass it along here..
