The issue is what do I say when I'm in a group. I've had more then one occassion I've said yes, and we've ended up with a train, and if I had not used my mana on the res, it could have been used during that train, and someone in my group wouldn't have died. I won't even go into the response I've gotten from doing that.

I've tried charging... and if anyone in my group dies because I don't have enough mana to heal them cuz I ressed, well they get the cash. I've asked for buffs, or clarity, but this doesn't always work, as the person isn't even in the zone, and isn't going to have mana when they are ressed.

I guess my way of thinking is that I don't run up to the shaman in the middle of a battle and say "SOW ME" when they aren't even in my group. I don't run up to the warrior and say PULL FOR ME when they aren't in my group. I have requested clarity from enchanters... ONLY if they are able, and I always always offer to pay, either with PP or the pages (or is it words) that they need (they always accept the words). And I never insult them when they say no.
