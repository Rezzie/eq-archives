Quotison I understand what you are saying, I like my Cleric too but like the others here I am just plain disappointed by VI's abandonment of our Class. I'll try and suggest some stuff that may help equipment wise.

I found the best thing I ever did was go for Wisdom and AC. Ac is your friend especially on larger raid type mobs who will rampage heh, and getting hit less or at least for less damage means you can heal for longer without being interrupted.

Try and go for Thurgadin quest armour to start off - either hunt the Arena with a couple of groups/guild or hunt the giants at the start of where they begin to see invisible with a well balanced single group. Buy multiquests if you can afford it - its good ac and stat gear with some nice effects.

Get a shadel bandit ring. They are great, Look out for a lotus vine necklace (ac7 +9 wis +45 mana) they are great, get an earring of the solstice!

If money allows buy some cultural armour for your race/deity or even make your own (smithing).

Dragon bracers are good - good ac and charisma and wisdom (worked through to matchelss) see if you can find a bracer of sense.

Donals is.....sucky! I keep boots and arms in my bag - boots for sheer fun or AoE groups, arms for free heals or pet buffs (not wasting aego on a pet!)

Primary hand doesn't have to be a weapon - I use Writ of Dragonkind because I don't have my epic (Sleepers Tomb drop) Secondary I used sarnak batle shield for ages (still do for resists) or crystalline shield. Range slot a Gantru Charm (Umbral Plains) is fantastic (neck or range slot too), currently I am using a frozen orb because of the 40 mana (well over 255 wis unbuffed I am lucky)

Get the best you can afford to get but make sure you really invest in good stuff. 1 really good item is much better than 5 mediocre ones in my opinion.

I hope this helped a bit. Try making a Magelo profile and 'equipping' yourself in stuff that looks good. it's a great way to see what areas to improve and drool over some dream items.
