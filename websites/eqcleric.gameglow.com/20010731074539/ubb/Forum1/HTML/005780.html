I don't have any target numbers in mind for my equipment, I just use the best item for the slot I happen to have available.

I have a sebilite scale neckguard, necklace of superiority and a velium black sapphire necklace available for my neck slot.

If wis is #1, then obviously I want the NoS. If AC is #1, then it's the sebilite scale neckguard. If mana is #1 then velium bs necklace.

Right now I'm using the NoS. I net 60 mana from it, 5 ac, and a bunch of other stats and saves. Strictly speaking, the sebilite neckguard is probably better if ac and mana are all that matter. Likewise, if it's all about mana and hps, then you want the velium bs necklace.

Similarly, I have a bunch of masks available to me. Velium Ruby Veil, Sebilite Scale Mask, Bone Mask of the Jarsath, Enshrouded Veil, Polished Mith Mask and Gold/Electrum Diamond masks.

The Velium Ruby Veil has the best wis, the Polished Mith Mask the best ac/wis combo, the Bone Mask the best other stats/wis combo, the enshrouded veil the most mana added and the diamond masks the best saves.

I'm currently using the sebilite scale mask, which has the best balance of what I want. It's the highest ac piece, has 4 wis and 10 mana.

I think it's pretty stupid to obsess over target numbers for each stat at the expense of evaluating each slot on an individual basis. This doesn't mean I'll end up with 273 wis, I still don't want to exceed 200, but I'm not going to go with 20 more mana or 2 more ac on an item at the expense of saves, hps, sta, str, agi, cha and other stats just because I don't need the wisdom on something.

Use the best item, don't worry about hitting caps in any certain order.

Aiien.
