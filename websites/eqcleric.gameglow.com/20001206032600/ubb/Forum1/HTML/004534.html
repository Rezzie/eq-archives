Generally speaking, if you have 200 wis without planes armor (EM) then odds are your AC will be very, very low.

Also, you can't really say "You need X AC and Y wis by the time you're level Z". I'm 52 and I still lack full EM. Part of your AC is based on your defense (2AC per 1 point of defense I think).

Part is based on your agility. I would work on getting your agi to 75 (the first AC boost level) if it's not already there (quest gauntlets and forest loops are good sources).

The rest comes from equipment. Generally speaking, it's rather unwise to focus solely upon wisdom. The mana gain just isn't offset by the damage absorbtion. It's often been said that a dead healer is useless. It doesn't matter how much mana you have when you die.

I'd say get rid of some wis items (a good place to start would be ditching the BS tunic for a quest BP) and get some AC. Yes, quest armor is low in AC but it's fashionable and keeps you alive.

Just my 2cp...
