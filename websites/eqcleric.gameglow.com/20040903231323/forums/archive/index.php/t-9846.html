Taken from a topic on the general board here at EQCleric:
If everyone is rolling on cleric spell scrolls, so that they can make a hideous profit out of the clerics, why shouldn't we do the same with the other classes?

What is Norrath coming to? I remember that people used to help each other, perform random acts of kindness, and just be nice in general - on boards and in the game. Now it seems like the WarBringer! attitude has infected everyone - ME FIRST, SCREW YOU. I've already taken Juul and the horse she rode in on and exited stage left because I was sick of the pissing, moaning, bickering and backstabbing going on in my guild, on my server, at my level. I started over - new server, new character (well, she's a DE cleric instead of a HiE cleric - how's that for a complete change of pace?!!?), and new play style (helllllllo PvP teams!) - but just reading stuff on these, and other, boards depresses the hell out of me.

EverQuest (NeverQuest, EverNerf, etc) is a game! Say it with me now. Close your eyes, let the mantra take over your soul. Allow memories of the good old days - the first time you saved someone with a split second heal, your first piece of wisdom gear, being chased across a city by an NPC who didn't like you just because you had hairy feet - whatever the good memories are, remember them. Now, open your eyes (hopefully you read this entirely before closing them!) and consider how you feel. Now compare that to how you felt after the last time you logged off.

This is what I did, and I ended up starting completely fresh on a new server. I have diddly and squat to my level 16 name. It has been driven home to me that Oasis is a good place for exp, but a bad place to save up for spells. My greatest accomplishments to date are a Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk and pk'ing a mage who was at least 4 levels above me after she attacked a friend and I. Ya know what? That symbol, and the stupid excuse from the mage after she died ("I didn't have my hotkeys set up!") bring a far greater sense of pride and accomplishment to me than Juul's full set of EM, Dawnfire, Tobrin's, White Dragonscale Cloak, and Aegis of Life put together.

I actually have fun when I log in now - I'm on a fresh server, untwinked, no higher level cleric char for people to bug me with res requests. How many of you can honestly say the same? I don't care about my exp, I don't care about leveling, and equipment is the least of my concerns - the less I have, the less I have to lose.

I encourage everyone to take a look at what they are doing and why. If the game isn't fun for you any more, try changing a few things. If it still isn't fun, cut the cord. I have the feeling that I am gradually weaning myself off right now - I'm having fun, enjoying the nostalgia of 25hp heals and buffs that wear off in 15 minutes. Once I've walked far enough down memory lane, it'll be time to call it a night. If playing EQ is giving you pains in the stomach, I submit that it might be time for you to do the same. End the game on a good note, don't keep beating the dead horse. Level 60 isn't a pile of dog doo if you aren't having a good time every step of the way.
