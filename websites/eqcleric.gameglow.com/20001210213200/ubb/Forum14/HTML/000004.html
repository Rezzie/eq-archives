Mistmoore castle is a good xp, good loot, superfun deathtrap.

Died three times in hell 40, but still had a good time. got a nightshade wreath and such, was fun... Kitchen seemed to be the best camp spot and pull into there.

Really is best to have an enchanter for crowd control, and a good smart wizzy for evac. And two clerics are a must, if youve got that, mistmoore can be a blast. dont be too surprised when you die a lot. Its a good training lesson for the Planes i guess... hehe

Really dangerous, but really fun. And... INVISIBILITY is a MUST there.
