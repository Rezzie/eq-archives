Last night I resigned as guildleader, officer, and member of the Mithril Heart Brigade on Tribunal server. I had been the second person invited into the guild, standing alongside the founder in Unrest when the lead server GM showed up to create it.

Our guild had a very strict admission policy and was founded on a rigid ethic: Selfless aid to others regardless of personal risk.

We developed a reputation on the server for helping those in need and upholding high standards of conduct.

But our high level players did not group together, and we spent our time more apart from each other than together. I often saw those of my level only at guild-wide events.

The demands of the guild were never balanced by benefits. I rarely saw my friends, almost never went into dungeons, passed on lots of invitations to groups/raids/planes etc. to be available for my guild.

So there was no support for me as a player. We had a fine reputation but the guild left me isolated, lonely, and drained.

Then a series of unending flamewars among several of the guild members started to tear down the reputation of the guild. They did not see that their actions reflected upon all of us. My calls for moderation went unheeded.

The other officers of the guild would not censure the members for conducting personal attacks on other guild message boards. I saw our good name, the only redeeming feature of the guild that remained, being shredded.

Ultimately the apathy, indifference, and vitriol suffocated me beneath their weight.

I miss my friends, I miss our times together. I miss my fine guild.

I weep.

Thistleburr Soulbalm, Vicar of Bristlebane
