Ive been going over the best possible combinations for a raiding cleric to have in AA points So far ive come up with this(done by AA points not lvl of skill):


3: runspeed
3: Any stat you are lacking or want more of. If none, make regen

2:mental clarity
6:casting mastery
2:spell casting reinforcement
2:channeling focus

9:mass group buff

6:casting mastery
4:mental clarity
10:spell casting reinforcement

8:spell casting reinforcement mastery

6:Mental Clarity
------------------------------------------------------------

Runspeed + stat or regen- Lets face it these are probably the best 6 AA points you'll use ever. Get perma jboots indoors and be able to fix a screw up ya made when creating your priest.

Mental clarity(1)- Mental clarity is probably the quickest skill you can put AA points into and see a change. Read farther to see why only 1 skill at this point

Casting Mastery(2)- So far the wizards have figured out that with 3 points of Casting mastery you can save 15% of your mana avg. Throwing 2 skill points into this skill gives you like a 10% increase on the size of your mana pool. Raiding clerics always neeeed mooore mana and less fizzles!

Spell casting Reinforcement(1)- reason for puting 1 point here is because the first class skill (cleric) is going to be Mass group buff and this will help keep those 40+ Aegos stuck for a longer peridot of time.

Channeling Focus(1)- Needed to stick 2AA points somewhere and what better place. With Luclin, it has been my EXP from grinding there that the mobs tend to interrupt me 2x more often than anywhere else. This will help stop that a bit

MASS GROUP BUFF(1) - OMG YOU MADE IT HALF WAY!! Now your able to buff your whole raid for 4 dots and 2000 mana. Just as soon as you spend 20k for group aego

Casting Mastery(3)- RAR 15% increase to mana pool!

Mental Clarity(2)- Lets increase your mana regen rate a little

Spell casting Reinforcement(3)- Wooot! 3 hour Aego!!

Spell casting Reinforcement mastery(1)- R0xx0r 4 hour aego! whole raid is now buffed for 4 hours only using 4 dots and 2000 mana. *huggles guildfund* I promise to never spend all your money again on those stupid dots.

Mental Clarity(3)- last skill

Quickest way to being a monster raiding cleric for 61AA points!

The reason i didnt go for more points of Mental Clarity is because during a raid you should already be regening 40 or so per tick and wasting 10 points in a skill you will hardly notice when a bard or chanter is around isnt worth it. And lets face it... if you dont have BAoR 3 months from now something is really wrong with ya. The spell drops often and should be the first thing you buy before you upgrade any piece of gear.

So guys what ya think?

Why waste points to extend the duration of a buff that already lasts 2.5 hours? Most raids don't last more than 4-6 hours, so you're not really saving that much time/effort. As for MGB, yeah, it would be nice to be able to give Aego to an entire raid, but then again, why? Being a raid cleric is all about being able to heal, not buff. Buffing is secondary in a raid. I find myself rebuffing Aegolism, not from it wearing off, but to people dying. Alot can happen in 2.5 hours.

IMHO, for a "Raid Cleric", you want 3x Runspeed, 3x whatever, then go for Casting Mastery, Channeling Focus and Mental Clarity. These 3 skills maximizes your avalable mana and helps regain lost mana faster. Then go back and increase your stats/resists.

If you wish, go for the critical heals and other skills. I'm not really impressed with them. A 100% res that can only be used every week or 2? A waste of points.

Critical Heals? Really, I use CH and CE so much, I wouldn't even see a critical heal much at all.

Purify Soul? Yeah, 72 minute refresh one a skill that clears everything but fear, charm and res effects. You would forget you even had it until it's too late.

I'd spend the first 12 archetype points on casting mastery. Here's why: reinforcement-1 only adds 7.5 minutes to Aego, channeling focus-1 is only 5% and I'm not sure it works against a bash, mental clarity-1 is only 1 mana/tick, a raiding cleric will have c3 and AE bard song. One more mana/tick won't be as noticeable as SCM-3 will be. Then 9 on mass group buff, then go back and get mental clarity-3. After that, it doesn't matter too much.

For most situations, it's all about mana pool and mana regen. SCM and MC are the two abilities that affect that the most. The other abilities will be nice for some fights, but mana pool/regen is nice for every fight.

I think you should wait on reinforcement, it's really just a convenience. For xp groups, reinforcement is great, but you did say a raiding cleric. I usually don't rebuff Aego because it wears off during a raid.

The fights aren't quick and dirty like they used to be. That's why I'd choose SCM and MC.
