Heh Ofbac, I split too if I am in a group and I used their time. I am usually pretty flexible with rezes when I am solo (and I solo quite a bit), but very stingy when grouped. If grouped, I tell the requester that I have to get clearance from the group and give the requestor a rough estimate of the total down time in person-minutes that the rez will take, then ask for an offer to present to the group. If the group accepts, I do the rez and split the charge.

The key is to estimate the down-time correctly. You have to account for the travel, casting, healing, buffing and
medding time and then multiply by the number
of people in the group. The total is
usually prohibitively large. However, sometimes the group will be swayed by a particularly sad story and someone can get a rez cheap or free. You can also reduce the cost by dragging the body to me and refusing the post-rez heals and buffs.

Anyhow, while I almost always get an initial surprised response, once I explain the calculation and say that it is really up to the group, I have never received a negative or unfriendly response. Some day I will.

To answer the original question, I definitely end up on people's request list if I rez them. Happens all the time. Heck, I even had someone ask me if I minded if he put me on his friends list just because I helped him out in a fight (level 41 too - how can you not have a full friends list by then?)
