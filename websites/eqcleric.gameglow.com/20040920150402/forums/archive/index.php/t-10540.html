Early to mid-twenties, the courtyard is good. After that you move to the graveyard. If these areas are not crowded, you'll get great exp from them.

The problem with Mistmoore is the frequent ultra-high level trains that higher level types are always leading to the zone. Often the trains are so frequent that you can't even get into this zone.

So, if you are the sole party in the CY or GY and there aren't any higher level types in the castle, this zone is awesome. Other times it can be a major pain in the butt.

I am of the 26th lvl of learning, and have found that, in a good trustworthy group, the secret room offers the best xp! In this room , deathly heralds, ushers, scribes, and will sappers and pillagers spawn. They are chained to the wall, so you can kill them one at a time (unless one of them is a caster, thenyou have no choice). In order to keep from getting trained from castle and attacked by wandering mobs, just head into the basement to meditate and rest. Also, when there is nothing to do after the SR is clear, you can pull nearby glyphed from the CY into the SR. An additional tip: get an enchanter who can mez, they make the fighting SO much easier. Another benefit to camping the SR is that the Icon of Sacrament (required for Caduceus of Sacrament) spawns in the middle room. It's no drop, so don't let anybody else loot it =). Lots of nice FS and loot can be found here, I made 2 bubs of xp in under 3 hours camping here! Good Luck!
