At this level, you have a ton of options.

You can kill gnolls and undead reavers, along with assorted other stuff, in East Karana.

You can kill dervs in North and South Ro, the Commonlands, and the Oasis of Marr.

You can kill gnolls in Highhold Pass.

You can kill crocs in the Oasis of Marr.

You can kill orcs on the orc highway in the Oasis of Marr.

Those are the places I hunted from 14-18.
