I personally think it's almost luck. I've been in bard groups with 200+ and 250+ resists and resisted things fine in VP, and I've been in non-bard groups with my resists between 190-220 and resisted things fine. This last VP run for Phara Dar I was at 217 MR and I resisted all the stuns. But at 217 for Druushk I only resisted the AE 1 of 3. I personally don't think it hurts to be over 250, but I also don't think it helps at all either. I'd almost say that much over 190 doesn't seem to help much, but I also don't think it hurts. I certainly haven't run much tests, but these are my observations from the 4 times I've done VP. It would be hard to test since you need a dragon type AE and I think more then 2 meetings of SW to get a good idea if 250+ resists hurt or not. Your first SW encounter could have been just bad luck. *shrug*
