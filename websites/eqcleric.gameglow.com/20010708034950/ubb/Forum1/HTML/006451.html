How to Get Armour in Velious.

1. Decide which faction you want to get armour off (D0rf/Giant (boo! hiss!)/Dragon).
2. Get faction with the appropriate faction up to Ally (ie better than Warmly). Best way to do this is
D0rf: Kill Frost Giants in Eastern Wastes/Great Divide
Giant(boo hiss): Kill any D0rfs you can get your hands on - Royal guard D0rfs in Icewell keep are extra good, coz they give faction bonuses to King Tormax
Dragon: Kill Storm Giants in Kael/Wakening Lands.
Repeat killing until you con Ally.
3. Each piece of armour requires a piece of nodrop armour plus 3 gems. The gems are different depending on the piece and your class (ie cleric arms have different gems to warrior arms) - see the links for which gems you require.
4. To get the nodrop armour, you have to:
For D0rf armour: Hunt in Kael, and get the corroded armour that drops (seems to average 1.5 pieces per hour for my guild)
For Giant armour: Hunt in Temple of Veeshan (not a minor task) and pick up the armour that drops there.
For Dragon armour: Hunt in Temple of Veeshan Halls of Testing (not a minor task) and pick up the armour that drops there.
5. Once you have Ally, a piece of armour, and its appropriate 3 gems, then head to the city of your choice, hunt down the person who gives the armour, and Hail them, then follow the prompts. Finally you just hand em the armour and 3 gems, and you get back your spiffy new armour.

Happy SoV questing!
