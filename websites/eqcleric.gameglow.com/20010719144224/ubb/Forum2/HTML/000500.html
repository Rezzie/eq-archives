I keep gate memmed. If you are looking at a situation where everyone else is getting wiped out anyway, gate means one less corpse to summon or one more person in full gear helping fight your way back to the group. Look at it this way, one way or another you are going back to your bind point.

There is the old DA/mem gate trick, but by that time everyone else could be dead or gone leaving all of the mobs focused on you. If you gate while the warrior is still on his last bubble you have a better chance of getting out.

Usually I only gate when I am OOM, I figure at that point I'm not going to be adding much, especially since sitting down will inevitable get you beat on.
