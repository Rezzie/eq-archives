get yourself buffed up with AOF reso and protection.

Have invis to undead mem'd. Root. Fear the undead. and various others.

goto the east corner of the island itself. and stand your feet right in the water.

Target the spec that is right on the corner. Dont be afraid to peek over the ledge to target him. he wont bite you.

Blast him with 585. And swim to the shore.
Strafe run and jump a bit till you are OOR of casting.

Stop. Turn a bit. Then madly click the button until you cast banish undead. boom 585.

Run some more until you are OOR repeat above.
585 and he should be dead. ( Keep a lesser DD incase he is a high lvl spec.)

If your lucky you should have just pulled 2 and now you can finish off the other spec and still have 20 mana left.

i was able to do this with 198 wis and no sow, no jboots.
At level 44.
Exp = about 1/2 of a blue bubble. per kill
