For the sake of experimentation, and the fact that three of our useless class abilities require Healing Gift-3, I put 2 points in it. Don't worry though, I stuck a couple of points in Mental Clarity as well. Not all of my points will be wasted.

Anyway, it works exactly like you'd expect. You have a very small chance of doubling your healing power. Totally useless considering most of my heals are either Complete or Elixir. But....

It works well with group heals, including the effect on my Akkirus' Greaves. And even with a 3% chance of it working, I did get two critical heals during one cast from my greaves. Each heal has a chance to crit, so a group heal has 6 chances (although you're lucky if you even get 1 crit out of those 6). I feel kinda sorry for my group of guildmates the night I got this. I was spamming my greaves even when they didn't need any healing.

So it's nothing special really, but I still use the level 57 group heal from time to time. That doesn't justify putting any points in the skill though. Group CH is usually a better choice unless you need the cure component from the 57 group heal, or you think you might get interrupted on the 12 second casting time of group CH. But like I said, I just did it to experiment. It doesn't take long to earn 2 points and I need to spend 12 in Archetypes anyway. And three of the class abilities do require Healing Gift-3, so if I ever want any of those then I have to spend the points there regardless.

Oh yeah, it's really lame when you crit a CH. People tend to laugh at you. Thx 20000hp heal!
