With those numbers you should be able to smoke him every time.

***********SPOILER************

Enter DN. Make groups.

MT group
3 clerics -> CH rotation @ pause 35
MT, backup MT
Bard

Form the rest of your groups around clerics, paladins, shamans, and druids, so everyone has some source of healing.

Equip MR gear (AE damage is fairly unresistable) then HP gear.

Right click items go up top of the buff order (if jboots, zone out to click them). Buff everyone with Focus, STA, Aego, GMR, haste or KEI as appropriate, BMB, CotP. Add regen too for good measure if you desire.

When buffed, exit DN.

If you have a +CoV druid or ranger, harmony Klandicar and set up in a circle around him; otherwise you'll have to charge him.

Start of fight - all healers target MT, everyone else targets Kland and has their finger on their clicky item hotkey.

MT attacks, first AE hits. Everyone hits their clicky.

CH rotation starts, all other healers spam heal (DL, chloroblast) the MT until the rotation kicks in... so for 10 sec. MT group bard has a DS going on MT, other bard is singing MR.

Debuffing should start after about 10 seconds since your MT will likely need time to build sufficient aggro. At the same time, everyone else attacks Kland.

From here just keep the MT alive. When AE goes off, everyone hits their clicky to stay buffed. Clerics group heal their groups, paladins use their wave spells (perfect for AE dragons), druids and shamans have to do it with torpor and chloroblast.

Follow these instructions and you'll never lose another Kland fight again.

You easily have the force to kill him.

Here is what we do.

We have our force sit up against the wall. We get 4 crap buffs before we start any other buffs. This is important.

We have the enchanter tash him and then run into DN. After he wanders back to his spot we have our shaman Malo him and zone into DN. After he returns to the spot and the debuffers are back at the group we go down and the clerics rolling heal the MT at 4 second intervals until the shaman/enchanters are able to get him slowed. We always try and put a secondary healer in each group as well as the cleric. Druid, Shaman, Pally. It helps the clerics to be able to watch the MTs health and not use all their mana healing their own group.

Bard AOE resist song is a huge plus in this battle as well. You wont resist the AOE damage but it helps to resist the fear so you will keep more consistent damage on the mob and the clerics wont get interuppted as much.

We have done this fight with 24 people and 2 clerics and won. So you definately have the force to do it. Good luck!
