Cleric drops in Hate seem to be pretty good. You have to understand that I used to play a Paladin and pretty much all Fear classes get hosed on Planar Armor in some way, shape or form. In about 8 Fear raids I had a total of 3 pieces of Valorium (Bracer, Vambraces, and Greaves)that that was VERY good.

With my Cleric, my luck was far better. I had all the EM I wanted after 3 trips to Hate. The first trip I got a Bracer and the Vambraces. The second trip I got my BP and a second Bracer. The third trip I got the Greaves. I've been back in Hate quite a few times and have passed on gaunts, boots, helms, and shields numerous times as they are not upgrades for me (well the Helmet is but I won't wear the Graphic). So all in all, 3 trips to Hate netted me 5 pieces of EM. However the only piece that I still wear for everyday wear, is the BP as I haven't found anything better to replace it just yet. I still use the Bracers for the CHA, the Greaves for the extra point of WIS over my Donals when Tradeskiling (EVERY point helps) and the Vambs for Magic Resist Encounters.

With a decent raid, you have a good chance of getting something. There are lots of Clerics of Inny so there is the potential for a lot of dropped EM.
