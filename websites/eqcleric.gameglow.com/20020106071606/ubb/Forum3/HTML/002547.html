Well after turning in my Shissar organs for my EB necklace (highly recommend that quest, btw) I decided it was high time I started the dreaded Gurgle camp. I was astonished to find the old bastard up and ready to kill! No camping necessary! I quickly enlisted the help of a 55 shaman friend of mine (57 cleric here) and once at fm and buffed, we pulled him out of the tower.

Wow, is all I can say. His melee was pretty laughable, and he did try to complete heal, which I prevented by stunned...but the shaman and I swear to high heaven that he his mana tapping. Has anyone else seen something similar? Was this just a freak bug? Has he been changed to manatap, making a tank buddy almost required for this camp? Was I drunk and just imagined all of this ?
