Guys, this weapon is better than any of us even imagined, why in the name of everything holy are we bitching it sucks???

Its got by far the best 1hand blunt ratio we can use. Its got very good +wis, even if you are already at 200 wis you can drop other +wis items for resists, HP, AC,and Mana items. Its got killer +mana, that never hurts. Nice sta for hit points. Charisma for pacifies, atones, cheaper peridot, maybe not the best stat but not that bad. The effect is amazingly useful in a large raid after a wipe out, anyone who has ever spent time trying to kill any new dragon knows how long a total wipe out can take to recover, this helps a lot. Frankly what would you expect here? A 110 point fire based DD proc like Pallies? A self only haste clickable effect? It may not be Group Marzin with no regent but it still rocks in my opinion.

Bottom Line: The cleric FA is everything I expected and more. I'm very happy with it.
