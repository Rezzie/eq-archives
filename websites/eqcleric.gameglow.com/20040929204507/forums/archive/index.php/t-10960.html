04-18-2002, 01:20 PM
Heal hard and fast and have a mod factory. Defensive tanks die on any significant healing gap > 4 seconds.

Your mod factory will more than likely decide the outcome of this fight.

Just to give you some perspective.

1 mod per minute = 660 mana per minute
C3+Bard+BlessedA+Ft3+MC3+Standing = 420 mana per minute

Mods can double (and more than double your mana pools if managed well).
