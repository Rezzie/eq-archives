The quest starts by hailing Trakanon.

He tells you you need

Caduceus of the Sacrament
Cloak of Flames
White Dragonscale cloak

Once you have the 2 cloaks you must turn both of them into Sirrus the Mad on the 8th island in Plane of Sky. Sirrus then tells you you must defeat him and the Keymaster at the same time. This requires no less than 40 level 60 players.

Once you have done this you loot the 2 rusty maces from each (lore no drop). Then, you turn all 3 into Mayong Mistmore. He forges them into one but goes insane so you must kill him as well. Once this is done you loot your FA weapon!

Dragon's staff of holiness
2 hand blunt
15 damage 62 delay
+4 wis +8 str +4 sta +2 dex
Process: Minor Healing

Looks like a regular staff with this blue sparkle on the end. Also had a faint laughter sound associated with it...

All told, it shouldn't take you more than, say, 100 hours or so. FoH did it with only 3 total wipeouts on the Sirrus/Keymaster battle.

All I got to say is it better be REALLY good and 1hB or else I'm not doing it.
