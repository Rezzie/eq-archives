My partner and I have a lot of experience with this guy http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif The Overseer is the place holder for The Kly, which is the npc the drops the Necklace of Superiority. Both of them have some very nasty attacks and if you get close to either one of them you may find yourself dying very quickly indeed.

Our practice has been to chain-stun him while the other member(s) go about doing what they normally do in any situation. Having 4 stuns up, I rarely had to heal anyone in my group during the fight.

It turned out to be an extremely long and tedious camp but the reward was well worth it! (I had to refuse a trade offer for a PKT since I didn't want to do the camp again lol http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/biggrin.gif )

Just so you know, my partner is a necro and we were both around 45 when we started this camp. I've also heard that mage pets that stun and/or root work well.

Good luck if you decide to persue The Kly http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif
