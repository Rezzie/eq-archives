Enstill is not a good spell to cast on a mob that you will be damaging with either melee or spells. Enstill is the spell that you (theoreticly) cast on a mob that you want to stay well away from the fight.

Enstill has a longer potential duration but a higher casting time and higher mana cost than Root - I believe it also has a greater incremental percentage to be broken when the mob takes dmg.


Root (and Immobilize, its upgrade) is the spell you want to be casting when you're going to be damaging the mob. Its resisted less, has a faster casting time, and a lower mana cost.


GZ posted an article about the different types of Root spells and what they do. Its in the archives of the Arch Mage Answers area of the EverQuest web site. You should go read it and see that Enstill really isn't an upgrade to root and its really not the better spell of the two.
