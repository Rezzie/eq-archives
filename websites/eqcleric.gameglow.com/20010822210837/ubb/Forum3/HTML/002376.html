First off, that is not a super twink but is good for your level. I would recommend getting the cleric quest bp which would add ac. Yes this will drop your wisdom a little but u can fix that other places. Small upgrades include 7 wis rings, and velium ruby veil 8 wis 8 str. The goblin soul caller is not bad as it has 10 mr. Another belt u can get is the crystaline belt from eastern wastes i think. You are not in bad shape at all for your level. I started a long time ago and had much the same stuff at your age. If you are real worried about wis then you can try for golden efreeti boots in a few levels like after 45 and then you will be hitting the planes. There are also many other velous armors that you can hunt pretty soon in skyshrine and other places. If u need strength for your high elf you can camp the thick banded belt in l guk also.
