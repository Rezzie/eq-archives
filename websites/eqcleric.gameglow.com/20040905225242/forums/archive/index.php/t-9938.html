I got my buddy from my place of employment started on Everquest a few weeks ago and he's got a level 9 magician now. I semi-twinked his Mage so that he can have a little fun and avoid some trouble at the same time. A nice robe, jewelry and two good held items make him look like some main's twink. But he's not.. he's a true-blue newb. And he has the scars to prove it...

I get a tell from him one night, asking if I was near Butcherblock because he lost his body and didn't know where to start looking for it. I asked him what killed him and he said "some big skeleton". Immediately I think "Chessboard". I've got nothing especially daunting on my plate - so I get a lift over to Butcherblock from a kindly druid who demands 20 plat in advance for the ride. And we depart.

/scribbles down a name on my "Overcharge These Folks for Rezzes" list.

I locate my buddy sitting naked at the BB/GF zoneline, looking very sad that he can't get his corpse back. I tell him don't worry.. I'll find it. I click my jboots and take off for the Chessboard. I also turn on ANON because I don't want the million or so folks in the area pleading with me about how badly the game has mistreated them, etc. I hear more sad stories than God.

As I'm tooling about, looking for his corpse I spot a 30-something near the chessboard and wonder "hmm... I wonder what he is doing there?". The chessboard is for folks up to 16 or so. He sure isn't making XPs.. and loot there sucks so....

/ponders that for a moment and moves on.

I finally spot the body after 10 minutes of an expanding spiral search and drag it back to my buddy who is jumping up and down with joy. He loots up and I buff him and his pet up pretty good. Then I ask him "what exactly killed you?"

"Some skeleton king or something" he says.

"Was it about 14 feet tall and brown?" I ask.

"Yup. That's him" he replies.

Strange. I found his corpse near the first merchant along the path. There's no WAY he made it that far with a level 14 Skeletal King following him. He should have died near the chessboard. So I prod him on a bit more, looking for the whole story.

He tells me that he went to the chessboard but it was already camped by a 30ish person, so he left and was heading down to the path to hunt basilisks. He was sitting down medding when "bam-bam" that big bad Skeleton starts punching his lights out. LOADING, PLEASE WAIT.

Hmm... That skeleton does NOT roam. And there's no way he aggroed it. So... I check back with the 30-something person near the Chessboard and spot him. A level 38 DE Necro. Immediately I'm thinking - he's charming the Skeletons and messing with other players. I figure out the rest of the story on my own. Necro charms the skel... has it follow him to my buddy sitting down.. then gates out of the zone or FDs, breaking the charm and leaving it to aggro the nearby mage. Sure, it's amusing to him, not so much to my buddy. What the necro DOESN'T know is that my buddy has a SERIOUSLY devious friend who does NOT put up with stupid sh*t like that.

Okay. The necro likes to play? Let's play.

/gets jiggy with it.

I figure since that Dark Elf is here on a hunting tour, he's probably ganked more than his fair share of dwarven guards and his faction is worth ****. I make an educated gamble and enact my plan. I bind myself just inside Greater Faydark zoneline and then bolt over to the guards near the 2nd hut. I target one of the guards and /CON him. Apparently, I've done well in my early years and I'm WARMLY to this guy. Great! I throw a Fear spell which sends Guard Norin fleeing for his life. I do a little typing to prepare for the onslaught to come and setup a few hotkeys.

I sit down and watch the little guard pump his miniature legs quickly, propelling him in far away from me. Then I see the message "your fear spell has been broken" and I watch as he makes a beeline directly for me. I quickly con him and he's THREATENING now. Fantastic!! I now have his complete and undivided attention. I stand up, tap my Jboots once and head for the Chessboard.

Wheeee!!!!!

I pause to see if the little Guard is following me every few hundred feet - I don't want to lose him in the hills and traffic. He's angry as all heck at me and stays on my heels, trying to get close enough to hurt me. I hug the left mountain base as I get close to the Greater Fay zoneline and swing left towards the Chessboard, staying high on the wall of the mountain so that no one sees me. There are a few trees there and they hide my movement fairly well. I spot the necro sitting on the side of the board with a Undead Knight as a pet. Wow.

I check my calendar. Hmm.. it doesn't have a "This Is Your Lucky Day!" note marked on it so I'll take this as a sign from the gods that I must be living a good life.

I zip down from the necro's blind side and get far enough ahead of the guard so that I have time to sit down next to the necro.

"Hiya! How are you?" I ask him as I sense the impending guard coming up on my rear.

Before the necro has time to answer, I hit my Divine Barrier spell and go invulnerable for 18 seconds. I then sit back down as the dwarven guard closes the distance. I hit the hotkey which I had previously prepared...

/Say BTW.. ya gotta be careful when you mess with people.

The Guard by this point had reached me but because I am Invulnerable, switches targets and starts *pummelling* the evil necro. The necro is completely in shock and doesn't even do anything for a moment. Bam, Bam, Bam. The guard is all fists and fury. The skeletal pet jumps into the fight and tries to defend its master but is nearly useless against the level 40-something guard. I walk backwards, still invulnerable and watch the guard hitting the necro mercilessly as the necro tries to get a spell off. It's at this point where I start to grin, knowing that payback is *indeed* a bitch.

Once more, the gods smile upon me as I notice the charm on the pet breaking - - the Knight starts pounding on the necromancer, too!! I see that my Divine Barrier is about to expire so I hit my Divine Aura. This provides me with an additional 18 seconds to enjoy this deadly show. The necro is down to less than half health and he starts shouting for help. I hit my second previously-prepared hotkey...

/Say There is no one here to help you, Evil One.

Unable to get a spell off, the necro tries running for the zone, but the two enemies are destroying him a blue bubble at a time. I watch the necro fall lifeless to the ground and give a little "/grins." emote. The Guard then turns back towards me, it's original target and charges. I click off the effects of Divine Aura and hit my Gate button. The Guard manages to reach me before the spell finishes and hits me three times before I Channel right through his flurry of attacks and create a portal out of Butcherblock. Mission complete.

I zone into Greater Faydark and send a tell to my buddy still sitting in Butcherblock.

"Hey bud.. it's all clear now. You can safely hunt again" I tell him as I sit and "/camp desktop".

"What happened?" he asks.

I do a quick /who necro 38 all and see that the bad guy is now naked in Neriak. Heheheh.

"I swatted a *very* evil fly.. enjoy the night!!" I respond before logging off.


Moral to the Story: Do NOT f*ck with my friends. <grin>
