Turn in coldain heads to Captain Bvellos at the bank.

If you're kos to the kromzek (bvellos is on kromzek faction), turn in coldain heads to the kromrif recruiter near the EW zoneline until dubious to kromzek. (stuff in skyshrine will also give +kromzek, if you want to save those heads for +tormax faction)

If you're kos to the kromrif, kill dwarves until dubious to the kromrif recruiter then turn in coldain heads until dubious the kromzek, then give all heads to Bvellos.

It is possible to be kos to kromrif and turn in heads at Bvellos, but if you're not careful one wandering kromrif can ruin your day.
