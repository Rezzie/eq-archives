Tooling about in Dreadlands this evening - found a most excellent solo spot for clerics around lv40 or so (I'm lv40).

I don't have the exact loc, but if you zone in from FV, turn right and follow the zone wall. You will go past the skel ruins (which are a good spot, but I find the greater spurbones spawn regularly there, and they are too tough for me at lv40. But plaguebones are semi-regular spawns, and if you pull with a spell its 100% single pulls http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif)

But keep going, you'll hit the north wall, turn left (west) and continue along only for about a minute or so. If you come to the zone to Frontier Mountains you've gone too far.

Here you will find a solo plaguebone skel, who spawns in one spot, and just stands there. At lv40 I can root/nuke and kill it with 1-2 bubs of mana left, and noticeable xp bar movement in Hell 40. Sit, and med up.

As soon as you get up with fm (ie 5-6 mins later) the plaguebone will have respawned. Root/nuke/med.

Continue until you get bored http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif This is an excellent way to level if you have other stuff you can do in 5 minute chunks (housework etc). And I think it is far better than screwing around with the perma camped, buggy, lifetapping spectres of Ferrott. And of course, you are close to Frontier Mountains, where you can try for a Belt of the Cenobite.

So try it out, loot is good without being great (averaging 2pp each), but the xp is nicely moving along, and not having to chase around to find kills is great http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif.

Nem (1.5 bubs to get out of hell)
