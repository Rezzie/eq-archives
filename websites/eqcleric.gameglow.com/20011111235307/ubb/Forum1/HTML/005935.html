Welp, I participated in my last Nagafen raid this past Monday. Cause I hit 53 yesterday

And I wanna thank all you guys for pointing out how profitable Sebilis is. At 51 I was told not to be there. At 52 I couldn't get a group at first. Now I have no problems finding groups, and people are starting to get to know me (again). Exp is good, but the cash flow is GREAT.

Every group I have been in has been wonderful in regards to gems. They don't hesitate. They loot a gem, they pass it to me. I even offered to let them keep some when I start to get alot, but they haven't wanted any. I have been leaving Sebilis with an average of 15-20 gems a night. Enough for me to (finally) move Pinzarn to the back of my book, and dump all those Jaspers I was carrying. Everyone gets Naltron, tanks and chanters first of course, but everyone else as mana permits. My gem bag used to be a large soiled sack, with 20 pearls, 80 jasper, 80 peridots and my Seb Idol. Now I use my EE Bag, with 20 pearls, 120 peridots and the Idol Started using the large soiled to carry potions. 6 invis, 4 EB

Only had 2 deaths so far, one was myself, as our puller somehow managed to get 10 froggies (we never did figure out how), and I got run over while the rest managed to succor. The other was a warrior who had 4 frogs on him while 2 were beating on me and 1 on the chanter. I had to make a choice between healing the warrior or healing the chanter. Not a hard decision in my book. Chanter lives.
