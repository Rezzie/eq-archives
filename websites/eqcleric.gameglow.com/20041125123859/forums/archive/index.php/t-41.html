Best advice I can give is to print out Questor of Norrath's maps of Guk, and study the route you will be taking to deadside lguk. If you make all the right turnings, it takes about 40 seconds each way. The live frogs are agro to me so I knew all about this when I started going to lguk, back in my low 30s.

On the way down, nothing will agro til the scryer room in uguk. As long as you don't fall off the plank bridge over the water, you can get from there to deadside zone real fast, so if you do get in trouble just divine aura and leggit.

Do the run down a few times, gating back out to the swamp. And then once you feel comfortable with the directions, run it the other way, coming out. From deadside back to the surface, the first few frogs are spread out, so I usually just rooted em and ran away, not bothering with atone. The only tricky bit coming out is the last bit by the crocs, but the frogs there are super low level so you shouldn't have any difficulties.

Hope this helps.

At 34th level, only the Ton Warriors aggro'ed me on the live side - at least on the route I take towards the dead side.
(The scryer room is about half way there, on that route.)

The biggest key to success: Stop, root, pass by and atone.

1. Stop. Don't run! You'll probably start picking up shamans, or other Tons, or who knows what. One at a time is the key.

2. Root. You *did* have root memorized, right? I find Root works *very* nicely on these buggers. If I'm close to the zone, I just root and skip the rest of these steps.

3. Pass by. Out of aggro range. If you forgot to memorize atone, here is your opportunity. Just stay away from anything else that'll poke you - they're much more aggressive when you're sitting...

4. Atone. Never had this fail. You can even stand next to another baddie, as long as he's not aggro too.

And you're off... You can even do this for things like Corpse Recoveries to the spider room - root the spider in the hall, pass by, Atone, move on...
