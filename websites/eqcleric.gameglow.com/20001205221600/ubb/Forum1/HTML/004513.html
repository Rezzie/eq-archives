Greetings fellas-

If you guys are not familiar with the way I do ressurects, then please refer to: http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/Forum1/HTML/004406.html

Anyhow-

I was in Rathe Mountains soloing some Hill Giants (yeah, gotta love that necklace =D), and I get a tell from a local Druid, and he's asking for a ressurect. We all know also- how Druids love to be in anonymous mode or roleplay mode so that they don't get bothered for teleports.

a_druid00 tells you, 'Hey, can u rez me plz? I died in hell and lost my lvl, can u plz rez me? thx! I pay 150pp'

You tell a_druid00, 'May I ask who u is? And what's plz? I also need to know one more thing; what's thx?'

(I'm NOT making this up)

a_druid00 tells you, 'come on man, plz rez me d00d! If U rez me, I will tp you anywhere u want to go! thx!

You tell a_druid00, 'Could you please enlighten me? I want to know what plz is, and what's d00d? That some kind of insult? Who is this u person you keep reffering to?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'plz, I don't have time to explain, can u plz plz plz plz come to wc and rez me?'

You tell a_druid00, 'Obviously if you really want a ressurect, you will tell me who you're trying to talk to, and what your abbreviations mean.'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Listen man, I don't have time to explain, get your ass down to WC and res me NOW PLEASE!'

You tell a_druid00, 'Well, that's better than saying plz and thx. Now, about calling me an ass? What's that about?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'You're trying to provoke me man, don't screw with me, I really need this ressurect badly.'

/who all a_druid00

[ANONYMOUS] a_druid00

You tell a_druid00, 'May I ask you why you're anonymous?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'So I don't get bothered for TPs and SoW and everything else.'

BZZZZZZZZT, wrong answer!

You tell a_druid00, 'If you're anonymous so you don't get begged for SoWs and teleports, does that give you the right to bother me for a ressurect?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Yes it does you moron.'

You tell a_druid00, 'Okay, so I'm a moron now. Could you please explain, in your best terminology, why I'm a moron now.'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Because you won't come down to WC and rez me.'

You tell a_druid00, 'Hold a sec. Let's try this again. Do a /who all Jaldarith now, and tell me what you see.'

a_druid00 tells you, 'I see you as anonymous. So? What's this have to do with anything?! I'm in a hurry man, can you PLEASE help me?'

You tell a_druid00, 'No. Think harder man. If I was anonymous, would you be chatting with me right now?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Uhh..No, that's a stupid question.'

You tell a_druid00, 'Well then, pretend I am not a 49 Cleric, and you don't even know I can ressurect you. I'm just somebody you ran across named Jaldarith.'

a_druid00 tells you, 'That's stupid, how do I get my level back then?'

/sigh

You tell a_druid00, 'Think harder son. Obviously- you're anonymous so you don't get bothered for teleports. What if I don't want to get bothered for ressurects? Do I have the right to go anonymous?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Yes.'

You tell a_druid00, 'If you want to hide behind helping your fellow man, does this mean that you can bother me, because I enjoy helping my fellow man?'

You tell a_druid00, 'You there?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'Fine. Don't even worry about it, I'll just go get exp. back and finish the rest of my level.'

You tell a_druid00, 'You backing off because you know I'm right?'

a_druid00 tells you, 'What's it to you? Of course I know you're right, and that's why I'm leaving you alone, as per your request, I'm leaving you alone.'

You tell a_druid00, 'Good. At least you realize that now. Let's start all over. Turn of anonymous, and ask me without dude speak, and try to make it as clean as possible. I know I'm not an English teacher, but I'm teaching you a lesson...'

You tell a_druid00, 'You will usually get a YES response to very polite, very "roleplayish" requests, if the Cleric is not currently busy.'

This COMPLETELY blew me away!

a_druid00 tells you, 'Hi Jaldarith. I died in hell, and I lost my level. If you were so kind to come down to West Commons and ressurect me, I would be willing to pay you for your travels. I can even provide you transportation, and in hopes that Tunare will bless you.'

I was astonished, really.

/who all a_druid00

He turned out to be a level 44 Druid who lost his hell level while kiting *something* in West Commons....Perhaps a Hill Giant.

You tell a_druid00, 'Why yes my friend. If you could provide me transportation, I would gladly come your way to ressurect your lost experience.'

*yaddayadda, blah blah, port from Feerott to WC......*

After I ressurected him, he tried to hand me 150pp- I refused the offer and said, "Next time, try a request like that, and try not to be anonymous. Most clerics like to know what level and class they are ressurecting."

He gave me some flack about treating him like a child, and then ported to Lavastorm.
