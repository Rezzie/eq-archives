Hello...O.k. I am a level 45 cleric so I understand your situation. Right now a necro friend is with me in Thurgadin and we are hunting the wurms across from the waterfall where you zone into the city. The experience is very decent, coin drops are good 4 to 7 plat usually, and the item drops are decent too. This ia a place where drop only spells happen and you can resell back home.They sell like hotcakes on my server. Charge like 500 to 1000 per spell lol. Only tricky part is getting to the Great Divide, need invisible and SoW if possible. Remember Thurgadin is a non kos city as long as you didnt kill any Coldain. You can bind your tank friends in there and as a city it has a bank. From the wurm spawn I plan to spread out a bit later and see what else is in the zone.

Another good spot for ex and loot is the City of Mist in the Dreadlands. There are no banks too close unless you are Iksar but at least again this is a bindable area for tanks.

Also dont count out Sol B for exp. Some people exclusively level there to get through the 40s. Plus you just may win a roll for a good iem.

Thats all I got for now...good luck.
