I have gone on multiple and i do mean multiple efreeti raids that last for hours. The best combination is 3 groups for break in or a minimum of 2 groups for the break in. If you really have a good mix of players you can do the single group but it will take you twice as long to get there due to down time. The best puller is a Monk or a class that can FD.

Once you get into the room, a choice must made at this time.

If you intend to just be in the efreeti room and not go out and pull monsters into the efreeti room. Then only one group is needed to hold the room.

If you intend to pull monsters into the efreeti room, accounting for the down time on mana. It is recommended you have two or more groups available to pick up the slack if the efreeti makes an appearance.
