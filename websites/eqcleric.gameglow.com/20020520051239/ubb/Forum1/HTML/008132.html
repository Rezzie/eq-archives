Read my post again about MoR - please.

I was with a monk (Gharns Rock, Primal, 41% haste item + epic) and a Mage (Epic pet and Velk boots).

I cast MoR on inc or as part of me pulling with others hits on to specifically count how often the monk got hit with only the monk and mage killing and with me not nuking at all to get as possitive a result as possible.

Now consider the following:
The ratio would have been worse
- If I had not cast right away
- if the group had been larger
- if the mobs had been slowed
The ratio would have been better:
+ if the mobs had a higher hit percentage (monks in NToV gear dont get hit much)
+ If the mobs had had more hp and hence lasted longer
