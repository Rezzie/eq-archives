Kerran Toy, AC3 AGI2 all/all, lore, no drop, range slot equipable.

It sure beats the Preserved Splitpaw Eye or the Thex Mallet. Here's how you get one:

Kill a Nilt's Clockwork outside the Mino caves in Steamfont and loot the Gnomish Toy. There were several roaming around when I was there, no waiting, dropped with one blast of retribution.

Talk to a Kerran Tseq near the docks on Kerra Isle. There are two of them in a pen. They are highly amusing, and will give you the Kerran Toy in exchange for the Gnomish Toy.

This quest was lots of fun and completely painless. It was also neat to see all of the new and fascinating cat life on the island.

Go! Rush! Do it now! Clerics trampled, film at 11.

The AC 3 on the item translates directly to +5 AC for a level 50 druid (yes, you do get the stat and AC boost from your ranged slot).

The +2 Agi on the item may or may not add to your overall AC depending on whether it kicks you up to the next Agi "step." If you are 1 or 2 points away from the threshhold, you can see another +1 TAC from the item, for a total of +6 TAC (total armor class).

Some lower levels were seeing only +4 to +5 (the item multiplier is lower for lower levels). Results thus will vary based on class and level.
