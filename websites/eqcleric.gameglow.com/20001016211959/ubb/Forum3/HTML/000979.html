I've found Zahal twice. I was amusing myself by killing the undead camp just southeast of the druid ring (and clearing the zone so sense the dead worked). Boring, but I made a few plat from ghoul loot while waiting for Zahal to appear.

On both occasions, eventually I picked up Zahal roughly halfway between the undead camp and the guard tower, heading toward the guard tower (i.e. westerly direction). If you don't want to fight him, and preserve your mana for Regis, run him to the guards and let them finish the job.

No ideas on his spawn time, though.
