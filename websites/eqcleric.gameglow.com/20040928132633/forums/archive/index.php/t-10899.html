Dozekar isn't too rough. He has a 400 dmg lure AE, so you don't bother with resists.

My guild generally kills him with about six-seven groups; your mileage may vary. The key factor to Dozekar is your aggro control. The main tank MUST have aggro the entire fight. If he/she doesn't, the other melee will start dying, and you won't have the damage output to counter his insane regen. Dot/debuff him up, just like you do for statue. Have your casters avoid the AE; there are several spots to do it near/in his room. You should be able to find them without too much trouble. Have a few clerics (2-3) spacing cheals on the main tank; I'll leave you to figure out how to time the heals. Have other clerics maintain their tank groups, and just keep up the damage until he dies. It's a six-seven minute fight.
