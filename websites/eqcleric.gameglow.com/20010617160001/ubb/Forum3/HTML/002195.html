We just did him this past Sunday. We did get some small aggro from temple mobs at the zoneline (a couple of stuns failed) but they were easily controlled with atone/mem blur and that's what I'd recommend if your people don't want the faction hits. Also to make sure he doesn't AE on you while still upstairs (a lot of mobs up there), do not damage him in any way until you get him to the zoneline. He will wrath you, so be sure to have your magic resist up and have a great sense of direction (mine sucks and I took 4 wraths before diving off a platform to get back to the zoneline for the fight). It helps if you have someone escort you up to his room and have the doors opened for you - makes it easier to get out when you're running for your life. =)

In the end, he went down easy but he took a relatively long time. We had 13 people between level 47 and 55 whomping on him (or healing the whompers) and it still took nearly 2.5 minutes -- a very long time for a non-planar EQ fight.

Good luck!

I love when people talk about things they know nothing about, like they wrote the book.

Bring someone who can tank a 55 mob and a bunch of people who can do enough damage to a 55 mob to kill it comfortably before your heal mana runs out. Killed him yesterday with a 60 monk, 55 monk, 59 cleric (me), 55 cleric (the beneficiary), a 52 warrior, a 54 druid, and a 51 enchanter. This was overkill but we were on our way to kill Eejag so we figured we'd do this first.

The easy way to do this is to pull the mob to the zone, which is very simple. Take a monk, point out the mob to him, have him whack it and run to the zone where he should FD. Have someone say (preferably hotkey if you are still preparing, but shift up arrow will do) 'what plasmatic priest' which will make him hang around. Say it repeatedly so he doesn't leave until you're ready, then hand him the proper item and give him his beatdown.

He's a 55 cleric. You can expect him to hit like an average 55 mob and occasionally cast a laughable DD as well as CH. For humor value he also buffs himself with Fortitude, which you might want to dispel first if you're a perfectionist (like me). The only thing you have to worry about is the CH. Don't let him do it. If you were to believe some of the statements above you might get intimidated by this but don't worry. He is in fact quite stunnable (or by amazing coincidence his casting was interrupted four times in a row at the very moment my stuns landed on him) and not particularly resistant. Just stun him like anything else that CHes and he dies quite easily.
