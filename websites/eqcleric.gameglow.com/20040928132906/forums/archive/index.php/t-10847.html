I saw Arturo's post on the safehouse about the Captain getting CH's from Von the Dragon.

We did this quest last night (for me!) and we set up shop on the shoreline directly to the LEFT of the tunnel to SG. (As you exit tunnel from SG to WW, turn left, go to water.)

We had no wanderers, no dragons, no problems with staying there.

The giants were easily pulled from further down the shore, and the captain was tagged off the train while our expert kiter took the rest of the giants on a lovely tour of the western wastes.

The only problem we had at all was this (and boy, did this stink) Scout Charisa sees through invis at a roughly 100% chance. (I think this was done to prevent any possible faction exploits? I seem to recall a rogue telling me he couldn't sneak/hide this quest.)

Anyways, the Scout spawns RIGHT on top of the tunnel as you enter WW. If you have -COV people, they will can get aggroed in the tunnel and then you will be royally screwed, as Charisa will camp the zone and seems to have NO ability to reset properly. I sat there and stared at her for 10 minutes, she didn't move. Finally we had to get a shaman to train her away so the rest of my friends (who are -COV) could zone in.

This quest is insanely easy for the reward and I encourage every +COV person to do it.

Just make sure your -COV friends don't get killed!
