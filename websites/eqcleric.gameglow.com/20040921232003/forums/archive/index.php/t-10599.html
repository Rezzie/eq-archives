I was having a very slow time at level 30 in Rathe Mountains even though I was soloing Giant Skeletons and grouping for Cyclopses and Hill Giants.

So on a whim I took someone's suggestion that I try the basement of Highhold Keep. The experience just blazed through. Took me about 5 of my normal play sessions (2-4 hours each) hours each with only 1 death down there to not only get the remaining 4 bubbles of 30 I needed, but 1.5 bubbles into 31.

At level 30 you can solo stray pickclaw guards and lookouts. In a group pulling from "the hole", a cleric is a beloved friend and you will definitely find a group.

I hear that experience dries up a lot after 32, but there are plenty of people level 35 and up still here (grouped with a level 39 yesterday) getting experience off pickclaw raiders.

The only downside is that the loot is not as good here as other places. I average about 25 plat a session, nothing compared to other places. But hey, I'm not going to complain =)
