Ragefire spawned on Luclin today, ending the long wait for Kealoha, who was the top cleric for Luclin's Ragefire Rotation List, which has so far migrated to Skyfire without incident. Ragefire spawns right in front of the orange-globe dragon statue, cons blue in human form, and is kos with a large aggro radius. I do not believe a solo necro could kill the human rage, so no fear of a clueless soloer wasting a spawn, but a decent group easily could. (However, the dragon would then eat them)

We used about 36 people for him, and the dragon now is indeed 60th level, thus allowing for more time on the corpse. The dragon Ragefire is a little harder than the old one. He has in my opinion, been upgraded from "pathetic joke" up to "kind of a joke". I recommend 3-4 prepared groups for an easy no-sweat kill, 2 might not cut it anymore, unless they are all solid 60's.

In case your curious, Luclin's list is still alive and well. Today's Cleric, who had gone through a week of pain on the new camp, was in a teeny tiny guild, with no one in her guild online this morning. No matter, 3 of the "uber guilds" sent about a group apiece, and a couple smaller guilds showed up to kill him to move the list along. (and for a shot at naggy loot, of course!) Yes, that's 5+ guilds killing Rage on behalf of a cleric who belonged to none of them ; ) (she was nice and well-liked tho)
