Well it depends. Most of the PvP I see at high levels (50+) is massive group combat of one more more groups vs. another.

In that case, I'll put up these spells (ideally)

Remedy (Heal spell)
Word of Health (Group Heal spell, important vs. those nasty AE's that high level mages/wizzies throw)
Divine Aura (if timed right, to catch an incoming Ice Comet)
Divine Barrier (same as Divine Aura)
Retribution (hardly ever used, most mana is gonna be spent healing yourself since everyone always goes for the cleric first, bleh)
Immobilize (hardly ever stick cuz of high MR, but hey...)
Force
Holy Might

When solo, a cleric is basically defensive only at high levels. We lack the mana to really kill anyone outright unless we catch someone at a bubble or two of health.

Now, at LOW levels, clerics can be awesome. Most low level chars you'll face won't be decked out in high Magic Resistance so your blindness/roots/smites will stick, making you a pretty lethal opponent.
