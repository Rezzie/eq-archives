i found its at Al's after i finshed reading here.

Level Range: 30 - 45
Typical Creatures: Various undead. Spiders.
NPC's and Quests:
Description: This ancient ruin, located in the Field of Bone, is the home to mobs of undead. The entrance is down in a couple of pits. You should probably get levitate to be safe if you aren't familiar with the area. Drop down the left pit and turn right to get to the meat of the dungeon. You will be forced to fight three mobs, one of which may be the Frenzied spider. The other pit gains access to both the middle level (where the scryer is) and, even furthur down, the Lord spider pit. To reach the middle level (do this if you don't want to fight the Frenzied room), get a running jump and it will carry you to this level. If you choose to be bold, step off the ledge and you will be on the lowest level, amist approximately 5-6 mobs, one of which may be the lord (use levitate if you don't want to take a big damage hit). The first section of the middle and lower level includes various spiders and the floating things. When you reach the first temple, everything from there on out is undead. A good strategy for travel is regular invis till first temple(be prepared to fight though) and invis to undead for the remainder. Eventually you will reach what appears to be the church of the kaesora. The area includes the bottom of a very lond tube with 4 mobs, the outside area of the church, with approximately 5 mobs, the ruined tower, another 6 mobs, the upper church about 5 mobs and underneath the church another 4 or so mobs (the under church is accessed behind the altar). The under church is what appears to be the spawn point of Xalgoz, the leader of the remnants of Kaesora. The most important thing to find when adventuring here is the exit ramp. Find this first and become very familiar with it. You know you are in the right spot when you are in the room with the broken floor. The broken floor can be accessed on the middle level. Facing away from the entrance there are two ways to go. The left is a one way ticket to the zoneline. You will loop around, going up the entire way, eventually dropping off a ledge at the original starting point. The right leads down down down to the undead meat of the dungeon. Some things to note: There are lots of pits in this zone. If you fall, do not panic. Running even furthur into the zone while being chewed on can result in a nasty corpse recovery mission(ask kamaltion on eci about this). The spiders are casters. Most being necros. Some root, and the roots seem to last forever.
