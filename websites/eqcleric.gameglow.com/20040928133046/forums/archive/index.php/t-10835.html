It's possible that you MIGHT be able to kill things at the zone in with one other person, but in my opinion, it's far more trouble than it's worth.

To reach Dragon Necropolis we'll start in Cobalt Scar:

1. Cobalt Scar, you need Invis, SOW, and Endure Breath. You swim to the Northern Cave and zone into Siren's Grotto hugging the LEFT wall (more on this in number 2). Guarding Siren's Grotto are several Siren's that can see invis, dispell, and charm. You're only hope is to find the zone line and keep going past them while they beat on you and pray they don't kill you.

2. Siren's Grotto. There are two ways through this zone. The first way is to fight your way through which is extremely difficult. The Second way is to use Druid Succor or the Wizard version which takes the party all the way to the other end of the dungeon. Anyway, the reason you hugged the LEFT wall on zone in, is because there are about 3 very tough mobs at the entrance. They will not aggro if you're above level 50 so long asyou hug the left wall. They're very close to the zone and the 3 are packed tight. Anyway, you somehow got to the end and zoned out.

3. Western Wastes is the easy one so far. Here you just need to worry about a bunch of high level mobs and Dragons (if you've destroyed your COV faction). The nice thing is that only one or two of the dragons see invis. Nothing else does. Just make your way to the ravine and make your way to the back of it. Slowly work down to the Portal (you can't miss it). Touch the portal and you're inside. Note that Levi, makes it easier to get down the ravine.

4. You're now inside Dragon Necropolis. Congrats.

The zone in is guarded by Phase Spiders. There are not many and you have a lot of room to work with. They hit moderately hard and have the same annoying ability as Scarelings from Fear, they can randomly port people around the zone.

Also, don't touch the bones scattered on the ground. It will summon a horde of little spiderlings, summon Constructs, do DD, or dispell buffs.

Getting to DN is the hard part and will be impossible for you and a tank partner. The zone itself is very unforgiving. If you screw up, it's a long and messy CR (the one time my guild wiped out here, we had to Necro Summon our corpses. There was no other way).

My advice, forget the partner idea. It's just not worth it. Instead get yourself a good solid group (including a Druid/Wizzy with the Succor Spell) and just camp the zone line. You can make decent experience here, it's uncrowded, and some decent things CAN drop off of the spiders.

First off, I've never had the Sirens outside SG see invis, and been by there numerous times =b

Anyhow, getting through Siren's grotto without an in zone evac is a snap, I've done it with 2 invis potions and DA/DB, and with 3 folks fighting through.

If you know the way, just invis past the 3 mobs near the zone, leap in the water, and DA/DB your way past the horses (can get unlucky with stuns though :\) and then invis past the rest of the mobs to the zone out.

Fighting through, you need to kill 4 mobs. 4 seahorses to be exact. They have some hp, hit a lil hard, and kinda MR. Just bring a tank and slower and all is well.

Some people have this vision that Sirens is a nasty zone. It's not really. Kinda annoying to get through without the evac, but definately not something to worry about.

Hunting here can be rough, hehe, but passing through is no problem.

Dragon Necropolis is a damn fun zone, IMO.
I'd bring a group though. Make sure to bring a rogue to disarm traps, and a good enchanter if you plan on killing deep inside. I explored this zone without knowing anything about it, so was a LOT of fun. Died a few times, but there is something really cool about exploring something new, with no prior knowledge.

Some nice drops there, I would recomend having high levels (56-60) if you plan on doing anything but the spiders, however.

Going further on this thread, my guild took a solid stab at Dragon Necropolis over the weekend and we stayed for a good 6 hours or so. We took 3 groups here and just had an amazing time killing Rats, Bats, Spiders, and Snakes (ahhhh, just like the old Freeport Newbie zone...).

We had a couple of hairy situations but we never wiped out and our worst fight only left us with 2 or 3 dead.

Rogues make this zone SO much easier with their ability to disarm traps. We were able to move through the zone at will (though we did spring a few of the Dragon Construct traps just for the fun of it). The drops were decent, we got a ton of gems, spells, and a few weapons including 2 of these insane 2HB Caster weapons with a billions stats/saves on it.

Experience was amazing. I made 3 blue bubbles at level 52 while here. Loot was great and it was a fun zone.

Tips:
1. Bring a Rogue!
2. Come prepared with Fish Scales, Bat Wings, Peridots and all other caster components. You'll use it all at one time or another http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
3. A level 59 Chanter was NOT able to mez everything. Some of the rats were mez proof.
4. If you wipe out, CR is a pain in the butt. Unless you have a secondary raid force handy, you're going to need Coffins and a Necro.
5. Go in and have fun. This is a zone that not everyone will get to see. Enjoy it. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
