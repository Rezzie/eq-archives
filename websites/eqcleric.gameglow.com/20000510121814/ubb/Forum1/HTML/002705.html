I had a similar encounter in the hills of Firiona Vie.

My group and I were camping the Drolvarg temple (about 35% were blue at 40) when this nasty undead critter name an Ancient Jacinth jumped us. I threw a S Heal on the tank which was quickly eaten up. The druid of the group then went to healing so I conned the mob. He was yellow at 40. I had 2 choices: futilly heal the tank until he died or try and nuke the thing down. I decided to nuke since the heals were in hand. Took the rest of my mana to Expel him down but we did it!

He dropped a medallion piece. The piece is part of a HUGE quest to get the key to Veeshan's Peak (someplace I don't even want to think about going to until I'm well into my 50s and part of a huge horde of people).

Anyways, if you're in the FV area be wary of this guy because he was doubling for about 100 hp.
