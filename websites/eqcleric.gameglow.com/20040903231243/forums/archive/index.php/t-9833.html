In real life you NEED to protect yourself as best possible. You want as much health and wealth as possible. You don't want life to be challenging, for most people it's challenging enough already.

In a game you want to be challenged. If the game is not challenging, it's boring.

In EQ, people want to gain levels and equipment and generally become powerful enough that they are "safe". People go looking for "safe" experience, easy loot, the best equipment possible. Why? To reduce the challenge, die less.

Sometimes I think people get confused and pursue real life goals in a game. The social atmosphere of a MMORPG adds status elements into the mix in a very RL fashion, further confusing the issue.

Just something to think about...
