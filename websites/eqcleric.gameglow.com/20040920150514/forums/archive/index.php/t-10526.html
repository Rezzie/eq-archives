Ooops, I forgot to mention this wonderful zone! With a group, I went from level 24 to level 28 in just a few days hunting (do not do this dungeon without at least a partner except during peak hours or you *will* die LOL). The only downer is the trip through Nektulos Forest, where you must be careful to avoid the guards (solution -- travel high on the left wall all the way to Lavastorm zone line) and Iron/Stone Guardians until level 27, but once you hit Najena, you've got it made. Plus a merchant is nearby (gypsies and druid in Lavastorm) and the Temple of Solusek. Hard to go wrong with that.
