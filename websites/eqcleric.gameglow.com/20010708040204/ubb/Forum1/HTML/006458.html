It is true that you only need the Cobalt Scar tooth to go to all 3 dragon rings. If more confirmation is needed, info can also be found in the updated manual file in your Everquest directory.

As far as how to get the teeth, it is mainly a matter of travelling to each ring and picking them up off the ground there.

The Great Divide tooth is the easiest because no major mob spawns there.

In Wakening Lands, the dragon ring is where Wuoshi hangs out so the dragon will either need to not be up or you must be non-KOS with the dragons.

The Cobalt Scar tooth requires someone to have the Cobalt Scar key to be able to go from Skyshrine to Cobalt Scar.

Of course, invis will likely be needed to go through Kael and/or Skyshrine depending on your faction.
