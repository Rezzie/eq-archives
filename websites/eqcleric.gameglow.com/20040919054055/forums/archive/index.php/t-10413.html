The outer area of Najena is also good for a careful 10/11, and pretty easy for 12/13. Just watch for trains. Just don't get in over your head. Even if they're only green to you at 12/13, having 4 or 5 skellies all bashing & stunning you can be quickly fatal.

I usually kill the outer 3 skellies, then go hunt in Lavastorm for a few kills, then come back in and kill the respawned skellies.
