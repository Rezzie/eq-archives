I've done the plasmatic priest a number of different ways now, and he's a relatively easy pull (any way you choose). I'd say here are the main options, listed in order of safe to more potentially 'dangerous' (imo).

1. Have an FD-capable player tag the priest, baby him to the zone and then let puller FD. Hail the priest, make sure he's lost aggro and FD worked (or have puller zone), hand-in, and kill.

2. Have any kind of player pull him to zone, then depending on what class they are, memblur, zone, or whatever it takes to clear the priests' aggro. Hail him, check to make sure he's lost aggro, hand in, kill.

3. Go up to his room, hail him, hand in, kill. The only pesky bit here is *if* he AoEs. I've done this where he didn't AoE at all. He is stunnable (if you have levels, tash, etc) so you can stop him casting that way.

Personally, when I did this fight he was pulled to the zone (first time the myself and the group of folks helping me had done it) - you never know if he might just get off that one AoE at a critical moment and you might lose the kill to a temple resident.

One thing to note is that if your team is lower in level, or perhaps relatively low damage output, he *will* cast a lot more during the fight (obviously). The times where I've not seen him *not* cast his AoE is when a group of 55+, mostly 60s killed him, so he was trying to heal himself rather than damage us.

Good luck!
