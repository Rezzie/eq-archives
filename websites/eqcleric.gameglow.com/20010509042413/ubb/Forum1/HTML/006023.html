Dispel in a nutshell:

If you dispell another person, or if another person dispels you, it will remove a random buff. If you dispel yourself via spell, or dispel stone/rod, it will remove your buffs in a linear order.

Dispel will remove x many buffs according to how many buffs you have on you, and what level the buffs are. A low level mob cannot remove my Fortitude buff, because Jboots are lower level, it will remove that. Higher level dispels will remove pretty much anything, randomly.

At least, this is how I think it works. I know that when I was camping Bergurgle, and beggin' Enduring Breaths off level 22 shamen/druids, it was ALWAYS the first thing to get dispelled... I had Jboots, levi, etc on as well, and those didnt seem to be touched.
