Sit ins worked once because it was novel. After that it became trivial, then ignored, then banned under the lie of "server lag".

My proposal is to do something completely different. But it needs some fleshing out.

Verant nerfs things to slow people down. Verant nerfs us because we give TIME back to people, thus taking time (and money) from Verant. We need to come up with a way to do our job, but in such a way as to totally turn it in their faces.

For instance....3 groups of 30+ clerics take over Befallen. Kill every undead in sight and give the loot and coins to the noobs. Powerlevel people.

3 Clerics take over each of the undead towers in the world. Kill every thing and give the loot to the noobs.

Teams of clerics take over Lower Guk-Dead Side. Same routine. Kill everything *easily* with our combine powers of root, stun, undead blast. And give everything away so that the lower folks don't have to work.

Give to the players...and give LOTS of it.

3 groups of 40+ can rule Najena, Cazic, Upper Guk.

Meanwhile studiously avoid assisting in the tougher areas like Sebilis, Chardok, Howling Stones. Places where we're needed. Hopefully, those players will howl that there's no clerics, while the ones we're helping will be howling about how great we are.

When/If Gm's show up to bitch, we can calmly state: "Our job is to help. Due to the constant nerfing, this is the only way we can help. We're helping."

Needs work...but I think it's a different method to use their nerfs against them.
