As of lately, I have found a really great place to hunt at level 39. Find yourself a really good tank (preferrably somebody at least your level or above) and go to either Oggok and do the 2 ogre guards outside the city, or go to Ocean of Tears and do the seafury cyclops. Either one of them will be good xp. The loot on the ogre bouncers is better and the zone line is closer (about 20 feet away to the safety of the inside city (no bouncers near the zone line) but the spawn is slower than the sea furies. The sea furies generally have about 10-15 cyclopses on the island most of the time.
