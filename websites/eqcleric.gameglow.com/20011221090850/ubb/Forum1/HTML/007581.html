No. It just changes your normal running speed. It's not a buff or anything, you just move faster than normal.

For example (these numbers are just made up btw):

Normal Runspeed = 5
Increased Runspeed +3 = 8
Spirit of Wolf = 10

Wolf doesn't add anything to your base speed, it just sets it to a higher number. So everyone with SoW will still run at 10, regardless of their original speed.

People are debating on whether it's faster than jboots or not. Honestly, it's kinda hard to tell. At runspeed x2, when I clicked my boots, I would run just a little faster. When I got runspeed x3, I didn't see a change in speed after clicking my boots. I did try racing a few people who had jboots on, but video lag messes that up.

It's great. You have to spend 6 points in the first tier anyway. Putting 3 of them on runspeed is good idea.

To run faster by straffing, hit the auto-run key, hold down your Stafe key (Alt on my machine, but don't remember if that's default or if I did it cause Unreal Tournament was setup like that-heh) and quickly hit your left and right arrows as you move. EQ adds your horizontal movement to your vertical (or something like that) and, with a little bit of practice, it's marginally faster than flat-out running. Good for a quick escape, but too annoying for most everyday travel. For instance, I can personally verify that strafe-running is *just* fast enough to outdistance the Kromrif Death Squad, whereas normal running will get you squashed (had to do this pre-Jboots for 4th shawl quest before I learned the no drop item, click everywhere trick).
