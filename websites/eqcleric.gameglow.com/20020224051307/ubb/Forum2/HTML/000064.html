I don't use CH on just warriors. Any buffed melee class over 1800 hp can benefit. SH heals around 700, from what I've read. So if a player at 1800 hp receives a CH at 2 bubbles, that's 1080 hp healed, which saves you an additional cast of a GH, which with the SH would end up costing you the same as CH. Also, casting one healing spell instead of two, will taunt mobs less. Most melee types once they reach the high 40's or 50 will have at least 2000hp when buffed with symbol.

I don't use CH on casters because they take damage much more quickly. AC, stats, caps on things such as dodge, and the absence of skills such as parry, etc. contribute to this. In fact, I usually still use GH on most casters since I want something fast.

I use CH a lot on my main tank, who just happens to be a ranger. I even use it when fighting hard hitting mobs such as nox. There's a limit though. I would never use it if he was being attacked by multiple mobs at once or if we were fighting a hard-hitting creature like the ones in Fear or Hate.

It's a great spell if you have the sense to know when to use it.
