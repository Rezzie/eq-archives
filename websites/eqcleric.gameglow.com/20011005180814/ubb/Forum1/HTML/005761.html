This is your problem. You must put 1 point into each specialization to be able to get 1 of them over 50. I suggest you go put 1 point into the ones that you haven't done yet, then either cast heal over and over OR put a few points into Specialization:Alteration to get it to Average (which is over 51). More than likely Spec:Alteration and Spec:Evocation are sitting at 50 now, so make sure you only cast alteration spells or put that 1 point into Spec:Alteration to bump it to 51 first.
