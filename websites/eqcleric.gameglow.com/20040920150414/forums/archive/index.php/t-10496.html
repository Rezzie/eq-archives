To me the best experience that I have received from mobs has been from the Borer and Scythe beetles in NK. These guys are easy to solo too! Root, smite, smite, smite, melee, med, ad nauseaum. All were blue with the occasional white/black at lev 19. Every kill I received noticable exp. I went through 19 almost entirely on these guys. It is best to wait until the later hours because NK does get rather crowded. hope this helps.
