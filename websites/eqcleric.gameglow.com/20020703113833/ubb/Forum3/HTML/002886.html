Some of the rewards listed may be incorrect now btw. Verant redid the risk vs reward of many items in PoS. These all look like the original rewards to me... Speaks volumes that nobody has gone back and completed them a second time.

If you are looking to complete a quest in PoS you can pretty much forget about it unless you have a guild that is willing to raid PoS over and over and over JUST to get you missing items because that will likely be what it takes. Take the Truewind earring. Only takes 3 items from islands 1-3. Should be easy, right? Wrong. One item comes from the boss of 3. Let me explain bosses... there are 4-6 quests per class (not sure if Verant added Beastlord quests to PoS yet) so you've got around 5 quests average per class and 14 classes (assuming no BST quests) and if we factor in one rare boss dropped item per quest (some have 2 rare boss drops) then we arrive at ~70 rare items. Excluding Island 1, that's about 12 rare items per boss and each boss drops 1-2 rare items per kill. As if this wasn't frustrating enough, most of the bosses have 24 hour spawn timers. Meaning you can kill most only once a day. Djorn, Overseer and Hand/Eye of Veeshan are nothing more than bonus chances at rare drops.

Imagine the frustration of necromancers who had a PoS quest reward as part of their epic. For a long time, a fellow guildie was the only necro on CT with an epic because he lucked out on the rare drop when we were clearing up for warrior blades on 5. I know of one determined necro who went 14 kills without getting that particular drop. Took the drop rate patch to finally get her finished.

The real killer for this zone is the crap loot. Look at the posted list. Would you dedicate yourself to this zone for most of it? Nobody is in any rush to go and find out what the "improvements" are and I'm willing to bet that if/when they are done you could have taken that same effort and done half a dozen more profitable things for better rewards. I myself have 2 of the 3 items necessary for the necklace, including a rare drop from 6. Have had these for over a year and no end in sight for it. I need a rare drop from the boss of 3 to finish.

So have fun on your PoS raid. I hope you get a WoG. It is the only real reason to go to PoS.
