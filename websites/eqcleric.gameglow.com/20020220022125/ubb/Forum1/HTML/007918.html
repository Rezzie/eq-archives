If I were faced with needing to recruit a specific class, I would keep lists from pickup groups of clerics that were fun to group with and did the job well. From there I would keep in contact with them, invites to xp groups, raids, guild parties and such.

You might find a cleric right away that is looking for a guild to join you or you might get some return later but either way it's a good way to make some friends and if you stick with it you should meet enough wandering clerics that you'll have 5 to 10 that know you well enough to go hunting with you guys even if they are in another guild.

The best way to get someone to join your guild is let them spend some time with you and see how they fit in. If they feel welcome, chances are good they're gonna look for you when they log in.
