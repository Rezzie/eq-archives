I would definitely try to outfit him with some inexpensive jewelry and armor. Here are some rambling ideas off the top of my head.

Go for +mana and +hp items for younger levels when mana is much better than wis and the amount of HP added actually makes a huge difference.

Moonstone rings Str+3 mana+30 25-50pp each

Jade Bracelets hp and man 25pp

Lots of Bronze armor pieces preferably small if he is Dwarf or HiElf. Probably looking at a plat per AC point.

Gotta get a piece or two of Cleric gold if ya can afford it

Collar of undead protection is decent. AC5 with sv disease and poison. Remember he will be starting with snakes and mummies. 25pp or so here. Also, maybe a Black Iron medallion with Wis+3 on it but I think the AC is more important during younger times.

Ulthork Arms might be a good arm slot cheap. Prob less than 100pp here and can be looted for free in EW. They have AC8 and 3wis dex-3.

Orc fang earrings are also awesome for newbs because they have ac3 str3 hp15 Three things a newb needs! These are 25-50pp each

Admontite Eps are a definite must have I still wear mine at 56 and they sell for 100pp or so

Zaharns Coronet is cheap for the head ac5 str3 mana25 Another Head item is a Skull Shaped Barbute it is AC13 HP35 SVM10. Again AC and HP are great and ghouls root so the SVM is nice when he hits his teens. Both these about 100pp or less.

Split Paw gloves are cheap and they have some wis on them. Also common drop in Paw if you want to loot for free. Lots of the Paw armor is good for newbies so if you want to spend some time getting free stuff just go farm this place.

Weapon choice is not important really A Velium morning Star looted in Velious is great. Free loot. If you want a little bit nicer hammer, I recently saw Sharkbone WarHammers for 300pp These are dam8 del26 wis+5. Very nice hammer. Before level 20 there is a damage cap so speed of weapon should be most important thing.

One of those Icewell Shields is good. AC15 svc10 and they are ultra common drop. They rot everywhere in Icewell. Free loot.

Bottom line, try to get him as much AC and Mana/HP gear as possible. Don't blow your wad all on one big super item. Lots of little things will be better in the long run because he will stay alive longer. Sorry I have Rambled on without much structure or organization but it is late and I am deliriously tired. I used my Revivisence spell a few to many times tonight and my brain is Frazzled. At least you can get the jist of this post.

If you can, try getting the cleric gold bracers. They are really great!
I didn't skip mine until I got my hate bp and the Donals bracers (hey, I'm an AC freak).
Expect to pay somewhere around 300pp for one, but they will stay with him for a long time.
The rest of the gold armor isn't really that much worth imho when you can get alot of nice things without doing the whole quest and spending pp on platbars.
That's just speaking in gameterms - I loved the way it looked on me and it had some decent stats back in my younger days.

As others said, try not to focus too much on wis - mana is way better at lower levels.

Blackjack is a nice weapon as it's fast and he will get 1hb skill faster.

For a lowlevel, banded isn't that bad either and it comes cheap. You can get a full suit for around 75pp. If you can find a smith...

If you are in for some fun, perhaps try making a trip to Lower Guk (don't know your level, he he). Epolets, enamelled black bp, moonstone rings and skull shaped barbute a.o. drops there.
