I tend to get this too...or I used to get this...

I now take matters into my own hands..

I don't go ahead and loot whatever is left for more than a few seconds, I call loot order.

I started to run my own groups of regular people here there and everywhere...I have 3 main tanks (war, Pal and Monk), a Druid and a wizard (plus me the cleric). All in all pretty balanced.

I got tired of being left out of loot and what not. I also know VERY WELL that my party appreciates me..

I am very fair with loot, and try to get the concept of 'He who needs the special item most should get it'....Those that loot out of cycle of rip the group off, are left hanging in the cold. I soon remove them from the group and get a replacement, and my groups are good...we go entire evenings getting lots of loot and experience with no deaths....we click well and trust one another.

A couple of bad ones come to mind:

We were grouped with a Paladin in Unrest named Dammed. Well we were on a 2pp alpha loot, and it turned out that Dammed ONLY looted bronze items. He was taking pieces of ringmail that he found and destroying them, and not counting them in his loot tally. Now if I'm waiting for a BS tunic and I loot a BS mantel...I'm NOT going to destroy the mantel simply because I am still waiting for a tunic. I left that group and the remaining group kicked Dammed out and asked me to rejoin. Needless to say.. I now get tells from Dammed asking to be invited to my group...he's still soloing or trying other groups as far as I know...

The other incident was a druid...Eleron. Seems like a nice fellow, but I think that hemay have his bad moments. Eleron loots the Barkeep, and we kill the Magi...I was next in loot, and I ASSUMED he had gotten 2pp from the Barkeep (the rapier alone)...

Well I looted the magi, and was going to give the Savants Cap to another party member..who didn't have one (I had one), when Eleron said that it was his loot. We all questioned this as he had looted the barkeep. He stated that NOTHING (not even money) was on the barkeep, no apron, no ear ring, and the Weapon was Rusty. Long rant, but the point is to take control...Yer never left out if you take control. Also make it a point to be CRUPULOUSLY HONEST.
