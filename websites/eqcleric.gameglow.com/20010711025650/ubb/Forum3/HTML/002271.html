Snaring the wurms before charm with ensnare (10 minute duration) will help your enchanters immensely. Of course, this could become a problem if you are in need to kite Ixiblat.

Wurms are nice for charm tactics, because they have tons of hp and high ATK. On the flip side, their fast running speed and large attack radius (can hit you yards away) make them require some careful use of spells. Have level 53+ enchanter with Boltran's Agacerie charm those - because wurms hit fast and far, you want a fast casting charm spell. This would be different unless you are dictating - but I have had wurms charmed for more than two minutes with Boltran's at level 58, and I really don't see a need for dictate. Snare really helps when charming wurms, and I sometimes haste my pets if they are snared. And make sure your enchanters has sow. Jboots just doesn't cut it against unsnared wurm.

Anyway, I digress. I'd suggest some low agro spell to pull Ixiblat instead of snare.
