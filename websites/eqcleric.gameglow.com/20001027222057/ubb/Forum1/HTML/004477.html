These are the relevent spells I keep memmed for trak.

Word of Restoration
Word of Vigor
Divine Light
Abolish Poison
Cure Blindness

Basically I wait until that first breath hits and cast a WoR. That heals em all plus might get rid of the poison.
After that use DL and WoV at your discretion.

Unfortunately with all the PR gear on you don't have much mana. Make sure someone throws you an insight before the fight. CH would be great, but its just too slow for the rate at which someone could take damage in this fight.

Have all you group members make a hotkey like '/t shandara BLIND!' for when they get blinded. Keep cure blindness in a hotkey spot and cure yourself or anyone else that catches it.

When the fight is over, if your still alive, spend the rest of your mana casting abolish poison on everyone standing. Nothing is worse than being alive to kill Trak but dropping dead on the spot 20 seconds later.

As for the fight itself, I usually try to stay behind trak, right on his tail. I almost never get hit with his breath that way.
