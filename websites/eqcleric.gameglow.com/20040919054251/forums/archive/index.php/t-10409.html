There is a spire of some kind, sand coloured with a black symbol on it. On the base, and on two spots around it, skeletons and lesser mummies spawn. It's relatively easy to pull just one, if you do it with a spell. If you're able to do these solo, you can get quite good xp here. Loot is not that good though, if you want more money you can group up with some tank and do bandit camps.
