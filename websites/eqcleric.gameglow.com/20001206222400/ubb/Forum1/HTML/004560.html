I kept Gheal up until level 51, when I got Remedy. Gheal is tiny and not very mana efficient, but its quick cast time can mean the difference between life and death for a caster, especially an enchanter. With impeccable timing you can keep a chanter alive with Sheal in the upper levels, but I never trusted my timing enough to do that. I do know clerics who used Sheal exclusively on casters however.

When I got Remedy, Gheal went to the back of the spellbook. I kept Sheal around for a bit, but used it less and less; it doesn't heal much more than Remedy and is less mana efficient. Eventually Sheal went to the spell junkyard too. I subsisted on Remedy and Cheal for a couple of levels, till I got Divine Light.
