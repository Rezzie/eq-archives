Crushbone is famous for it's various cool items like the screaming mace, shiny brass shield, and the dragoon dirk. Thus, it's also commonly crowded.

Nonetheless, there is experience and loot to be made here. An appropriately armored cleric can solo the various centurion spawns all over the zone. The best bet, however, is to join a group and take on the BIGGER centurions and slavers. As you get higher in levels, you can then take legionnaires outside the castle...and eventually, the Throne Room...

Remember, you're a cleric. Groups will want you here since the lego's hit pretty darn hard...
