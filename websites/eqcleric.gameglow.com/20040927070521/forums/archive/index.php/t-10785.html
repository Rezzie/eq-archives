Well, this is probably coming too late for you, but for the benefit of others, I'll share what I've learned.

Spectral keepers/champions in Trak are good exp for 51 and 52. At 53, champs are still blue, while all the keepers are now green. However, several still give exp at 53.

Last night, I tried moving to Dreadlands, primarily to be closer to friends who like to hunt BW and Skyfires when they log on. I found that many voracious brutes, drolvarg ragers, and petrified cockatrices are still blue and easily soloable at level 53. One of the giant types, mountain steep something or other, was also blue and soloable, but a bit tougher than the others. Spiders are all green now, but when one decided to pick a fight with me, I still got exp for it. I was hunting near the BW zone, and found plenty of mobs there, the biggest problem being wanderers, but rooting or fearing took care of them.
