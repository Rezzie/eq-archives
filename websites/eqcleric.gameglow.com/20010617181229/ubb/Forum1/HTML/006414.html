Pro dwarf Lots of tradeskill intensive quest to do here (Ring of the Dain quest). Stick it out and get some nice rewards... if you can survive.

Quest armor: PoG/Hate equivalent armor quests that require items from killing in Kael.

Opposing factions: Tormax, Kromrif (Frost giants), Kromzek (Storm giants)

Benefits from killing here: Royal Velium armor (VERY nice armor set), various uber Dain drops, Kael quest stuff

Pro Giant Some very nice quests to do here. Also allows you easy access to WL and beyond.

Quest armor: AC rich armor obtained from killing in ToV

Opposing factions: Yelinak, Claws of Veeshan, Coldain, Dain

Benefits from killing here: Thunder etched armor. Thurgadin quest armor pieces. Uber drops from Tormax, Vidnicator, Statue of Rallos Zek.

Pro Dragon Walk around WW, Skyshrine and ToV freely (for the most part anyways). Lots of nice quests and faction meshes with dwarves.

Quest armor: Akkirus set is lower in AC than Kael but had more +stats.

Opposing factions: Tormax, Kromzek

Benefits from killing here: Skyshrine offers some so-so armor. Lots of nice twink things but not much for the 50+ character. ToV has TONS of nice drops (including Kael quest pieces) and many, many dragons to kill for uber items. WW offers an exceptional non-planar armor set and the various NoS clones that are common drops.

I agree with the poster on doing whatever faction your guild and friends can deal with. I belong to a mid size guild, that has mostly decided to side with the dorfs, and quest for the Thurg armor. I keep reading that the Thurg armor is the easiest to obtain, but don't think that this is a walk in the park either.. I have quested for it for almost 2 months, and am close to having a full set. (need marbles for bp). The drop rate for corroded plate is somewhat weird, as it seems to go from somewhat common to almost non existant. Plus the drops seem to be swayed to the less important pieces first. (bracers, arms).
Add to the fact the gems, which are the same for all the quest armor, are not always easy to get, makes obtaining a full set of Forbidden Rites armor a somewhat long ordeal. I do not think it is equivalent to Hate armor though as many have posted. It is slightly better in AC, and the legs effect of group heal, and bp effect of Mark of Karn make it superior to Hate imo. My AC self buffed is over 1k without the Thurg bp, and only is around 980ish with all eth mist. Don't get me wrong, I'm not tossing my purple stuff, but the Thurg armor is definately a step above eth mist.

I hope to later start the SS armor, but for now the Thurg was the way to go for me and my friends. You can pretty much port into anyplace with good dragon and dorf faction, and the only hassels you have is getting that invis to go across Kael.
