I agree. Kunark sucks for grouping experience. However, at the undead ruins in Dreadlands, I am able to solo, and I am roughly estimating that I get twice as much experience soloing, as opposed to grouping.

The way I figure it, I can kill one blue MOB every 10min or so (give or take). In a group of 4-6, I would have to kill from 4-6 MOB's in the same time period. That is basically impossible. It often takes 1-2min just to kill a single MOB (in Burning Woods and Karnor's for example). For getting experience, solo is the way to go. Course, I recommend picking up a good book.
