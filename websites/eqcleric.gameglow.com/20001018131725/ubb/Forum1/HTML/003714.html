Olanthe, you may be able to get one anyway. Here's some suggestions for how you may be able to land one, if you want one badly enough to hazard a bit of risk:

1) a high level enchanter may be able to charm the kerran kiddo and bring him to zone for you. While charmed, the NPC will be indifferent to you.

2) Buff fully, get invisibility, and head to kerra. Find the children in question, and I believe there is a corner you can back yourself into (to stop interruptions) right next to them. Give the gnome toy, and gate out. I remember someone else commenting they were able to do the paw ring quests while being beat upon by angry kerrans, so faction may not be checked on the turn in.

3) just say "heck with it", and start camping frontier mountains for a totem...
