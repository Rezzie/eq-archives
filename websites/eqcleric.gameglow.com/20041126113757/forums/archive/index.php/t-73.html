Welcome to the Cleric world, Medtakk!

I still melee at certain times when things are calm and no threat of death. There are times when you're group is simply overmatched for the mobs and you could be pulling 3 instead of one. At your level you could be standing right next to the warriors as your melee is not much worse than theirs. They can taunt, so the chances of you aggroing the mob is slight. During the battle if healing is required, then it's time to decide whether to sit and med or finish off the mob. Yes, later in life you will be out of the melee loop for entire levels at a time, but for now enjoy it.

As for our lull, calm, pacify line of spells.... I would say worthless. Wait, maybe they're worse than worthless as more often than not the spell is resisted and the whole camp comes and quick death can result. Best to rely on the Ranger or Druids harmony line or a Chanter with mezz.

We have to make many judgement calls during our careers. Some may even involve who lives and who dies for the better of the group. It can be stressful at times, but its the rewards of success and chance of failure that gets my adrenaline flowing like no other class and I've played most of them. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Soloing is fun (less stressful) and you can be successful at every level. You just have to pick and choose your targets. Our spells are more efficient against undead, but we can kill live stuff and level up without support.

To level fast stay grouped. Preferrably with good company who's main goal is to keep you free from damage. Let them know early that when you say "ON ME", it means you are now occupied by a mob and heals are no longer guarranteed and medding is impossible. A good group will dispatch help, a bad one will ignore you. If you can root and park that mob, fine, but it's always an easier battle if you can just be left alone for the most part. Your nukes can be resisted, but I've never seen a groupmate resist a heal. So with that in mind our mana can be 100% efficient.

This board is full of experience players that helped me tremendously when I first started my cleric. Our class really stands out a little later in life, but the rewards are worth it. So have fun and stick with it.
