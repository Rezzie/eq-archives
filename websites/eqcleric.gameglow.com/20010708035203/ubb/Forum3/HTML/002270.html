Last night I was showing my RL friend around the game. He had just bought EQ and I was over his house helping him get acclimated to the game. As part of my showing him around I was demonstrating to him how to buy and sell to NPC merchants.

I was showing him how to scroll down the the merchants inventory and how to use the new ALT key function to get item names... when... I suddenly realized that the merchant I was at was selling Diamonds! Not diamond dust or any "pseudo" diamonds.. but real life resist gear making diamonds... AND they were only 230pp per!

Needless to say, i went to the bank, withdrew all 4.4kpp of my life's savings and stocked up on 19 of them bad boys... I told my guild mates about it and some of them came and bought up another 40ish of them.

I had never seen diamonds on a vendor before, and I would have assumed that if they were.. that they had been sold to that vendor by a PC. I know that atleast 80 diamonds were bought by either me, my guildmates, or other PC's in the zone. Furthermore this merchant was selling Tinkerer's bag(s) too... didn't have enough to buy one to begin with so I don't know if there were multiples or not.

Has anybody ever seen this before?
