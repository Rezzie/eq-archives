Not only will you be KOS in Qeynos, but also in parts of the Karanas. Anywhere you see Jaggedpine treefolk. Be especially sure that you do not get a teleport from a Druid to North Karana (I was lucky enough to run far enough away that I could get my body back unmolsted). You can raise your faction with the Guards of Qeynos by hunting in Splitpaw which is usually unhunted (I think the max number of people I saw was 6 in this zone). Once you get your faction up high enough, you can hunt BB to raise your faction with the Jaggedpine Treefolk again, but this will take a good bit of time to do I think. I saw Holly in Qeynos hills, and while she was deep blue, she was still treatening. And this was after hunting for about 3-4 hours in BB killing all of the Gnolls I could. You also need to be careful of Druids in your group. Should they happen to need to evac your party from a tight situation, make sure it isn't to N. Karana, or you will be in worse shape, or as I had happen to me one day, it was decided that we were going to new hunting grounds, and the druid started the teleport before anyone could make me invisible so that I would survive the teleport. Luckily, I backed far enough away that I didn't teleport also.

Just some things to think about should you decide to do this. And the loot is GREAT there. Lots of plat, gold, and silver, and sometimes Great Staffs which sell to vendors for around 12pp a piece.
