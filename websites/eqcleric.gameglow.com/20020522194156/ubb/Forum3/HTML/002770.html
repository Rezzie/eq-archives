The necro only spawns at night. So time your ph kill at 2pm game time. He or his PH will then spawn at 7pm, midnight, and 5am game time. Its on a 5 hour in-game or 15 minutes RL timer. That way you get 3 chances per game night.

Other than that, its just luck.
