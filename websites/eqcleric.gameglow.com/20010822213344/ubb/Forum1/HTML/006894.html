Hello Euboa

If you really want to solo to some cash and do not care about exp at all, you have some possibilities:

Hill giants in Rathe Mountains
They drop easily when you start nuking. They drop up to 40 pp + often a FS weapon. There are merchants in the zone where you can sell weapons and buy peridots for the gold. If you do not get rid of the gold, you will be heavily overweight very fast.
I made over 100 plat an hour in Rathe, while doing my homework when meditating.

Lower Guk
The money here does not come from the mobs - it comes from the people you res.
When I have done this, I never require payment. About half the people usually offer donations on their own
If you have a principle of refusing payment for ressing, this is of course not an option.

If you also want exp, my only suggestion would be giants in Burning Wood. Again - a lot of the money comes from ressing people in BW, SF, DL and KC, so be prepared for some running around.

The only of these suggestion that guarantee money is Rathe Mountains. The other two depends on how many resses you are asked to do, and whether people pay - which can go from 0 plat to thousands of plat in a day - completely unpredictable.
