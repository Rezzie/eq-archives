Get on the boat in East Freeport and hop off when you get to "Sister Isle". From here swim to loc -1000, -7000. This will be Undead Isle....Greater Skels spawn here, but more importantly so do Gargoryles. The GSkels are placeholders for the Gargs so just clear them out and hope Gargs spawn. Then whip out Wrath and wipe those Gargs off Norrath.

Gargs drop Gargoryle Eyes on occasion, which are light, stackable and sell for about 8pp.
Spend a few days here and you'll have a few hundred pp.

Two things to note: one, this is the solo method to get good money (the other way is to get a partner or a group of friends and go camp a dungeon spawn that drops a good item...Avenging Caitiff in MM comes to mind at your level...good xp too). And two, DO NOT go to the top of the isle....Spectres spawn there =( .

Well, good luck.

*Oh, and GSkels are about lvl 13 (wimps) and Gargs are about 20 (just root/nuke'em).
