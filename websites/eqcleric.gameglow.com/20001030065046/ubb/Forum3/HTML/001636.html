Well, in terms of quests, there are a few that you can do. You can certainly do the cleric temple boots (if you have enough plat). If your faction with qeynos is good enough, you can try to get a shining star of light, or a thunder staff. And in a few levels, you can go to HighPass Hold and try for the testament of Vanear.

If you want to get armor/weapons from drops, then do some research into what you would like to be wearing, what drops it, what zone the mob is in, and the appropriate level. Then try to get into a group, letting the group know that you are trying to get that item.

However, the easiest way to upgrade your armor and weapons is to buy them from other players.
