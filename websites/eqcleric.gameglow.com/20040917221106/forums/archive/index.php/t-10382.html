Con your targets before fighting; at 2 you still want to stick to evens(black/white) or blue cons. Con by targeting and hitting the 'c' key.

Use strike to pull, you get some free damage. You can also use flash of light to sortof stun the mob a bit.

divine aura can give you time to run, but it's short and refreshes every 15 minutes.

don't try to heal yourself midfight; odds are you wont succeed and will lose several rounds where you could have been hitting the mob.

Train your 1hb if you haven't already, it gets started slowly.

Guards are your friend.

Good luck and enjoy the game! http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
