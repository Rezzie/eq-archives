I just got through camping Gynok. These are the facts :

This was on test server, so anyone killing the Dark Elves in the commons would be pretty unlikely.

Gynok's placeholder IS right by the door. Just med at the door, and when it spawns, it will aggro you. Turn the volume up, and go about your RL while you wait for the sounds of battle. The PH is a 16 minute spawn. The PH is the only roaming mob in the whole dungeon. So if you see something wandering around, kill it (unless it's just a mob going back to it's home after a train). Things to spawn in Gynok's place are a Large Skeleton, a ghoul, an Ice Boned Skeleton, and a Giant Plague Rat. There may be others, but these are the most common.

All times are ET (US)
