-> Put the item in Luclin or some re-vamped zone
-> Make it a semi-common drop off a mob doable with one good or two ok groups

Anyway, a flood (say 7 per week) would truly remove the Ragefire problem for Verant and would cut down on the traffic count of this board =P.

1) Guild KS horror stories. This item is not particularly desired by Clerics as much as it is for the Cleric's guild. Two per week will never satisfy the demand of the player base for a mana-free Revivisence. Therefore, the amount of pressure on the camp is just insane.

2) The time issue is rather unfair to a fairly large percentage of the playerbase. 3) From what I've read the problem for GMs still exists although it may be somewhat reduced. Seems like a GM is getting involved constantly anyway. My solution, while diluting the game (but Verant does that all the time anyway), would most definitely make life easier for Verant.
