I did Hap for half of level 45. Got through the last half in about 10 hours.

My tactic when breaking the camp is to root the pet, then kick off a stun, nuke, stun, nuke. Then I atone her pet -- poof.

After that, I grab her before she pets up. Lead in with a nuke, stun, nuke. Sometimes, I have to throw a root in there to keep her from running up and hitting me. She hits pretty hard, and can interrupt your spells easily.

When going into the first fight I always buff with MR, FR, symbol, reso and AoF. She casts both fire spells and magic spells. As stated above there isn't really any need to refresh symbol. She dies pretty quick usually.

I used Happ Dremblenod to get through 44, 45, 46, 47, and 48, off and on. She did not succeed in killing me once (got close a couple of times, but the Steamfont zone is reachable in 18 seconds while invulnerable).

To break the spawn, nuke Happ (always start out with a nuke, Happ only has 900 hps). The pet will come at you. Cast fear on the pet to make it run off. Happ will most likely throw a nuke of some kind and then run up to do her dual 70s on you (pretty good punching for a mage). Root her, back up, then throw a nuke to drop her. The pet will suicide when fear wears off. (Feared pets always suicide when fear wears off if their master is dead.)

From then on just pay attention and nuke Happ before she gets her pet up. It's the second or third spell she throws after she pops.

With spawns every six minutes, a cleric can crank steady, safe experience solo and do stuff in RL in five minute stretches. It'll be almost like having a life again.
