If you absolutely feel that you must melee for the damage you deal, out of those two choices I'd go with the Yak Warclub.

Dark Ember, while nice on stats, just won't dish out the damage; not due to a bad ratio, but due to the crappiness of cleric melee skills. Yak Warclub will still deal damage, even if you're missing constantly, due to the fact you can proc on misses.

Other options for damage dealing weapons include:

Dawnfire (against Undead, with a dex proc you're almost helpfull )
Staff of the Undead Legions (can clerics use this?) similar undead proc, looks cool.
Poison Wind Censer - buff the PR and go to town, fun little weapon with great ratio.

I know there are more weapons like this. Come on, think of them.

If you want stats, go with something like a Paw of Oppola, Cone of the Mystics, etc. These aren't weapons, but there are very few cleric weapons with good stats (AC, HP, Wis, Mana, Resists).

Of course, you could just go do your epic and have the best of both worlds.
