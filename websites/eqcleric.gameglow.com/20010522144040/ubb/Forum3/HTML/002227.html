Grimrot (and his ph) spawn every 3 hours and 30 min I think (it was a while ago, there are more details on the spawn time, just do a little searching). At that spawn time 1 of 3 mob's may spawn, the live version of grimrot, the dead version of grimrot, or the putrid skeleton PH. All three spawn in the same location. You need the no drop heart from the undead version and the no drop scythe from the live version.

I had a friend hold the camp for me between spawns, so I could run to town and increase my factions. I would gate back after about an hour of turning in 2gp's and bone chips. A much quicker way of increasing your Priest of Life faction would be to turn in the fishy things from the pool under the guild. Have a mule in the pool to kill the fish & let you loot them.

The only timing that is involved is that you need it to spawn at night for dead and during the day for a chance at the live one. I always killed it at 9AM when I was trying to get the dead one in order to ensure that the next spawn would be in the middle of the night. Killing it at 9AM doesn't make Grimrot spawn, but it does make it so that the spawn happens at night so that if Grimrot spawns you get the dead one and not the live one

Conversely, you could kill it at 9PM to make sure that the next spawn happened in the middle of the day if you were looking specifically for the live one.

Does that make more sense?
