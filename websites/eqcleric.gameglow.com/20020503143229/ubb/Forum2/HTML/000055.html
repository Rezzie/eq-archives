IF you have a mana stone, and you are fully buffed, think about this:

Mana stone takes 60hp for 20mana. A fully buffed Cleric can have about 1300hp at level 39. (This is a guess, since I have around 900 at level 31). If you mana stone yourself down to 100hp, you net 400mana. Complete heal takes 400mana (unless your specialize alteration processes). You break even.

But every level you gain past 39 allows more and more usage of a manastone with complete heal. With the level 44 buffs, I am sure you could have around 1600hp at level 44, netting yourself 500mana from your manastone. After a complete heal, this is 100mana more than you started with.

Now, what I am wondering: Is this really worth it? I mean, in the ammount of time you manastone yourself to death, then cast complete heal, couldn't you med back the 100mana and then some? I know that at level 31, I can cast a 90mana bravery and med it back in just a few seconds. As your meditation skill goes up, your mana comes back faster, right?

If these #'s are accurate (and please tell me if they are not), why do so many clerics swear by the manastone? I can see useing it here and there when you are full health and meding anyways, you might as well regain some HP's and get some free mana out of it. But to stone yourself down and cast complete heal just doesn't add up.

First of all, sorry for bringing up this ancient thread once again. However there is a chance that all the new expansions and equipment nowadays might alter the conclusion from 99.

Question: Is manastone really worth it?

Facts:
- Rightclick effect that converts 60hp into 20 mana (3 sec casting time)
- You can only use it in old world but not in any planes or the revamped Cazic-Thule

The alternative way of getting back mana is to meditate. With maxed meditate skill you will get approx 20 mana/tick. Some people argue that youll get back mana much quicker by stoning your health down and then CH yourself. I cant see how tho. Lets say I am fully buffed with Heroism and Marzin and self buff.

Start HP: 2500
Stone yourself down to 100hp. This will take 40*3 = 120 seconds.
Complete Heal yourself. This will take 10 seconds.

So in 130 seconds you get 40*20 - 400(CH) = 400 mana back. Thats a ratio of 18 mana/tick. Thats even worse than just sitting down. I know some of you have a lot more hp lets say 3580. It will still not be more than maybe 25 mana/tick. Thats less than meditating with normal Clarity or manasong. I can see a difference if you got a Donals BP or someone else casting CH on you. But without them is it really worth the money to get a manastone? Im on a old server so it is actually possible to get one.

I'm a heavy user of the manastone when in old world zone. and let me tell you what I feel is the best way to use it.

The "common" thinking is stone down to 100hp then CH, then repeat. I'll agree this is a complete waste. The return for doing this.. as seen above is very small. The only way it's worth it is if you have a donel's BP and can CH yourself for no mana every 8 mins... OR have someone else heal you.

The better use of the stone is to use it as you go. By that I mean.. cast a spell then tap the stone a few times to get yourself close to FM and sit and get the rest back via med. What your doing there is getting twice as much power by regening your hp and medding at the same time. Using a fungi tunic and/or other regen items REALLY makes a hug diffrance.

Doing it this way I'm ALWAYS at 90% mana or better and dont' run the risk od stoning myself to death OR getting aggro off something when I'm at 100hp and dieing. Plus is scares my group members less.

If your a manastone user and have been doing the stone down and CH method.. try it the way I discribe. I think you will find your stone alot more helpful then you already do.
