Nice on Phara Dar. For us Hoshkar goes down easily (just a handful of deaths), but Phara Dar is still giving us fits.

A couple notes on the group heals:

49 Word of Healing (http://eq.castersrealm.com/spells/spell.asp?Id=779): 4sec 600 mana for 500 hp = 1.20 mana per hp, 125 hp/sec
52 Heroic Bond (http://eq.castersrealm.com/spells/spell.asp?Id=785): 3.5sec 420 mana for 400 hp = 1.05 mana per hp, 114 hp/sec
52 Word of Vigor (http://eq.castersrealm.com/spells/spell.asp?Id=783): 4.25sec 750 mana for 620 hp = 1.21 mana per hp, 146 hp/sec
57 Word of Restoration (http://eq.castersrealm.com/spells/spell.asp?Id=802): 4.5sec 900 mana for 750 hp = 1.20 mana per hp, 167 hp/sec

- Word of Healing, Vigor and Restoration have the same mana/hp.
- hp/sec: In an AE situation where you are chain-casting group heals (which shouldn't be often if your group has good resists), and you don't have time for Word of Redemption (or can't cast it like me :P), Word of Restoration provides the biggest bang for the buck. If you're forced to chain cast the group heal then it's probably because the group is dropping faster than you can heal them. hp/sec is a huge consideration.

At 58, I use Word of Healing and Word of Restoration for group heals on ae targets. I tend to use WoR as my staple group heal. It gives me a more leisurely pace to keep everyone alive. I can spot heal those falling faster than the rest in between. Also, because of its higher mana cost, if specialization kicks in I'm saving more mana. Word of Healing I keep up for when my mana starts to thin and my goal is to keep as many in the group alive as possible, not necessarily healthy.

Far too often groupmates at raids (especially VP) get Aegolism or get swapped in from a group with a higher level cleric and heroic bond won't stick on at least one person. With specialization and the hp/sec I don't feel too guilty tossing some extra mana for a 100hp boost in a group heal. But yeah in an idea situation heroic bond is an incredible group heal.

But every cleric heals differently.
