Just finished a raid there last night. Our findings were that sometimes the regular Clerics of Inny were dropping 2 pieces instead of one. This is good because there were many that were empty. (I got sleeves and a bracer).

So, first floor seemed pretty much the same. We accidently aggro'd Maestro once and he wiped us out pretty handily where we might've been able to take him before.

Second floor was weird. Pathing is just awful. Some roamer would aggro on you, knock you about a bit, when run off for awhile. They seem pretty much MR up there, so you've got to split up your raid on multiple mobs, however when they're running around like that, it's difficult to do it. And by the time the confusion was sorted out, the clerics and spell casters would be dead...

However, if you got past a bad spot like that and found an area with relatively little aggro, you could sort of pull some of the things there a short distance. Other times you need to go from house to house.
We didn't do any mini-bosses.

We didn't see much of anything drop off the Sages, Diciples, Elite Dragons and Travel Agents. But a huge amount of pally gear dropped. No PoG gear for clerics... whaah! Not fair, I can't hunt there.

Oh! The spam is JUST awful! Every few minutes every Innorruk follower gets into a religious ferment and shout hallelujah or something to that effect. If you get too close to a static it'll spam you with some sort of message... over and over and over. You can't turn it off.

But the one thing that is fun is that the mobs have "last words" that they utter when you kill them, or they have some smart-ass comment if they kill you.

And the zone has emotes too which were kind of fun and were not overdone.
