Until your friend gets into planes levels You should concentrate on Wis and AC. If you can add some resists in along the way it's always handy.

Ears: Forest Loops AC2 DEX+3 AGI+3 WIS+3
Face: Velium Ruby Veil +8str +8wis
Head: Sebelite Scvale Cap AC12 STR+4 INT+4 WIS+4 SVPOISON+2 SVMAGIC+2
Neck: Blackened Alloy Medallion AC6 INT+5 WIS+5 This one comes from runnyeye
Shoulders: Adamantite Epolets AC9 WIS+7
Arms: Donals Vambraces AC21
Forearms: Bracers of the Reverent AC9 WIS+5
Chest: Sebelite Scale Coat AC22 STR+6 CHA+6 WIS+6 SVDISEASE+2 SVCOLD+2 SVMAGIC+2
Back: Mammoth Hide Cloak AC:7 WIS+4 SVCOLD+5
Fingers: +7 WIS rings, i think they're about 4-500 plat each
Feet: Royal Frosted boots, just have him sit around icewell until a pair drops
Legs: Sebilite Scale Leggings AC16 STR+4 CHA+4 WIS+4 SVPOISON+2 SVFIRE+2
Waist: Belt of the Cenobite AC5 WIS+7 SVCOLD+10
Main: Iksar Berzerkers Club 7/18 STR+4 WIS+4 this one is just for fun
Off Hand: Charred Guardian Shield AC10 WIS+9
Ranged Slot: Medicine Totem AC:5 WIS+5

This is just a list to get an idea of what to look for. Most of this stuff you could buy for decent prices, or even go get them yourselves. A few nights in Sebilis getting some loot, you could sell the loot you get and buy this stuff and this will get him ready till well beyond the planes levels
