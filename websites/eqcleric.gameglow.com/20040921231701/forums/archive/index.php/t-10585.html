The cyclopses near the Lake zone can be easily taken by a small group. I was killing ones yellow to me at 31 teamed with my 30 paladin companion. Merpa - Cyclops loot is rather poor, once as low as 8cp (must have been the town beggar). I kept the tanks symboled, and the loot covered the cost of the stones (bloodstones available at the inn in the zone, BTW).

The lizardmen are the placeholder spawns for cyclopses, so if you have a larger group and need to pull quicker be sure to clear out the lizzies.

For outstanding loot in the zone, move over to the Hill Giant area. Average loot for a Giant was about 50pp in coin and usually an FS weapon.

We took down HG's with a 31 Paladin, 31 Magician, and a 36 Shaman. No snare in that group so the pally and the dog and earth pets just stood and tanked with me chain healing. This is a risky way of fighting them, so buff everyone fully before attempting it. Have a plan for what to do when the Giant switches off the tank. We lost the shaman one time when he ran out of my healing range, since that was his reflex action... http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/frown.gif
