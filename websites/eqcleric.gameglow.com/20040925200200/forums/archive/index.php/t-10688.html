I was wondering if there is any clerics out there that actually melee solo?

HPs and AC aren't the problem - we are a plate-wearing class after all. The problem is it takes FOREVER for us to melee something to death because:

Our weapon skills are capped at 175
We cannot Double Attack
We cannot Dual Wield
We cannot Kick
We cannot Bash

And getting in melee range is a bad idea because:

We cannot Parry
We cannot Riposte
We cannot Dodge (yeah we get the skill but when was the last time YOU dodged something? http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/redface.gif)

In general, it's far more efficient to med and nuke.
