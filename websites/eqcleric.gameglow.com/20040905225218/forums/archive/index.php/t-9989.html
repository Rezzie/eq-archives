You may call me a n00b cleric or a cleric that doesn't understand raiding at all but nevertheless I have to get this from my chest:

Calling out heals: I hate to be on raids where every cleric calls out ever single heal they make!

Imagine a raid with 6 groups - every group has 1 cleric - one cleric probably heals 3-6 times (aoe-fight) means about 20 heal-messages per fight. Not only they may increase lag, but also they make me nervous, because I start to think we are in big troubles.

It makes sense to me to call out Complete Healings, so the other healers can help to keep the maintank up with underheals and show the people on the raid that you are not sleeping http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Healing messages - please if you are on raids and decided to call out every heal, keep it small and simple. Messages like: The voices told me to heal you %t with my remedy/CE/CH or Pumping %t with celestial healing or OMG OMG %t is in trouble - wait Complete Healing is incoming. 10 sec casting time may be funny for groups but when I have a busy time healing the MA and have to "filter" every message 1. who heals 2. who got healed 3. what spell is used I go nuts.

Heal adequately - Raid PoF - break in. My group is MA, one enchanter, lvl 60 druid, rogue and a monk. Nice break in - no problems, no deaths. But one cleric kept healing the chanter in my group - even tho he was not even hit (this makes me nervous too - I don't suck that much as a cleric that I would need back up healing of a druid and a cleric on a PoF raid /cry!) http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif . At the end he was oom or lom after fights (I was fm because I not even had to heal the Chanter ;b) and we had to wait till he recovered. How do you say this your fellow cleric whithout sounding arrogant or without making him feel bad?

Typically in our raids we have hot keys to call out which heals we are using when we are healing someone not in our group or the main cleric healing the main tank.

This way everyone assumes you are taking care of your own group (cuts down on some excess noise) but lets you know if your heal is needed on the main tank or when you will not need to heal a specific person because someone else did.
