Solb zordak is only an item exchange. You give him one pearl, he gives you another pearl and despawns.

The skyfire zordak is KoS. You kill him, and zordak dragon spawns out of corpse. Kill zordak dragon, he has a heart (which is a container). Loot heart, put pearl from solb exchange into heart. Hit combine button.

Take all your parts to tim deep, do item exchanges. Voila, sprinkler.
