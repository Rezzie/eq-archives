The shield is the common drop from the Clockwork EXG in Solusek A.

From what i've heard, it is around lvl 28-30 in level, but the big problem is that the sucker is 100% magic resistant.

So no root n shoot on that one - you'll need a couple of melee friends to help you take it out. Cause if she gets away, yer in for a world of pain.

If she carries the shield, you will see it on her. The rare drop is the Charred Guardian Breastplate (dun know the stats).

I've heard that the respawn time is upwards of 6 hours (cant confirm this myself).

I paid 800pp for mine, but our server is decently young (Quellious), and i've heard that they go for 500-600 on other servers.

1) it's dropped by the gnome exg in solusek A. It is a rare spawn, there are 2 different clockwork placeholders that can spawn in its place. It is often camped. Atone is a very usefull spell here.
