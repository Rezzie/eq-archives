Wizards would still be the kings of DD vs live mobs (and vs undead in many cases), shaman/necros/mages would still have the edge on us in damage due to having useable pets. Druids/wizards would still be the masters of quad-kiting and transportation.

In other words, you would be expanding the role of all the priest classes (since druids and shaman also get heals) and opening up some more options for both soloing and grouping for clerics.

All without needing to add new spells, abilities, etc-you would just need to add an extra bit to the spells that currently exist.

Not to attack you Mikar, but rather to respond to what you said, coming here and posting "that would be nice, but will never happen" only hurts our case for things being changed and acts only as criticism. If I were VI and read that, I'd think "they can't even decide amongst themselves what they want, so why bother paying attention to what they're saying?". Better to either post your own ideas, ignore the ideas you don't agree with or at least be constructive in your criticisms. Constructive criticism opens the door for healthy discussion and debate, whereas pure criticism only closes doors for everyone involved.
