Best maps I had seen were on EQmaps, but they are not free anymore now

Velketor is a zone anyway that´s hard to cartograph, so better to just go there and explore the zone by yourself and learn from your own experience - if you just go for the Kobold-Camp, one good group is enough. No need for several 50+-groups, that´s just overkill.

If you only want to go up to Kobold-Village, you can invis up and walk there (Cancel SoW and walk instead of run if you head there the first time, because most of the ramps are slippery). Just be careful not to fall down, because the Hunter-Spiders see invis and will attack.

Several Kobolds are able to see invis. Bards or Enchanters with faction-spell/song can pass them easily tho. The Gargoyles behind the camp don´t see invis either, except the Icy-ones.

Be sure to watch out for the healer-Kobolds, since otherwise you are going to fight one of them for several times.

Don´t destroy their scalps or paws since they are good money-loots (Paws around 21pp, Scalps 17pp each and stackable).

Feel free to ask for more specific things. Velk is one of my favourite zones
