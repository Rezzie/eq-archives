Another side note: guides are not paid. They are players who volunteer their time to try to help others and are on a very short leash as to what they are actually allowed to do (they can watch things and do minor corrections of very known, provable bugs).

The $10 a month probably goes toward server maintance and the payroll for not just the gm's but all of the eq live team for new content, bug fixes, etc. I'm also sure a large portion of that is going to server maintance and isp fees.

For specific cases you stated:

Falling into water going to kunark: Don't know. I've never had this happen on any trip on zone. I have missed the boat transfer as mentioned above though and had to ride on the front also...

Death loop: You can only be bound by another person in a city zone. A caster can only bind THEMSELF anywhere (except kunark dungeons). It's a bad bad bug if someone else managed to do it in oasis.

For falling though the lift in kelethin, the only players who have a problem with that are gnomes for the most part. And I do believe it is related to lag and posision updating. Getting a rez for this is very gm dependent. Most won't help at all if body has been moved.

As far as customer service goes, Verant has better customer service than any other company I've ever dealt with in person (Try calling Time Warner some time if your cable modem breaks. Good luck.). It helps a lot to know what is possibly a fixable problem for guides and what isn't since they handle any petition before a gm is called usually. Personally, I've never had a problem with any of my petitions and the longest I've ever had to wait was about an hour. It takes longer than that to get into most of my schedualed doctor or dentist appointments. Concidering EQ is just a game, I think they do extremely well. Sure they could use improvement in some areas, but that's true of anything. Most of the time they do ok.
