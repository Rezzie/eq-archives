27th: Started of badly with a 4 hour chickin xenocide camp. The only camping Ive did for any of the items so far.

28th: Got Gull, sludge, Dwingus, and a trumpy tonic.

1st: got Decayed heart of the dead Grimrot(ok not rearly quest armour but the quest shield doesnt excite me to much ). found the live one as well, must remember to take a tank next time !

2nd: got Babbinshot & Diary

3rd: Got Ingot of the Reliant from the stump, traded in diary for the Icon of the Reliant, Ingot of the Ardent from the icy river, traded in tonic for the Icon of the Fervent, Ingot of the Penitent from Guk, and killed the Heretic in Kerra Ridge.
Finished off the night getting an enchanter freind to buy and enchant 6 plat bars and I finishing 5 of the armour quests.

With the exception of the initial Gull camp im happy to say that I got all of these icons and ignots (and heart) by stopping in on the spawns on the way through or by travelling to the spawn loc and finding the mob/icon ready for the taking.

Doing the quests this way was actualy fairly entertaining & made a plesent break from the normal exp hunting (much needed at 45). Looking at the quests I rearly think that this was how there were intended to be compleated.

Unfortunatly this all leads to the Bracer quest which seems painfull in comparsm to the others (I guess people actualy think the bracers are worth the trouble of camping).

At various points during this questing I bound myself to NK, Gating back once and a while to see if the spawn was up. Yesterday I found somthing intresting. A very green cleric (at least I think it was a cleric) was camped out at the spot almost all day. He was the only one there over about a 6 hour time period.

The last time I gated back there the area had suddenly got very busy, I guess the spawn was due. Two green Clerics (as in no chance of taking out the mob) two blue clerics, and one 49/50th level Druid (I guess we know how the green clerics were going to kill the mob).

From my experiances here I guess I have a few options:

1. Camp a stupid hillside for 6 hours+ and hope that anyone else who comes along with there high level Druid respects that I am there.

2. Join in what seems to be a free for all, with all the other people who have the mob timed. Getting my guilds Druids in to help with the kill.

3. or foget about the camp totaly and either buy the Bracers (encouraging camping) or ruined mith bracers (yep Im still using +3 wis ones at 45 ).

Getting lucky and just checking the spawn every so offten does not look like it's going to work this time.

ps. this is not a rant about Druids
