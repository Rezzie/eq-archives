As bad as I hate to admit it, I always go to HighKeep when I need to level. I have been in lvl 30 for a month because of the toughness of lvl 30. In 30, you have to kill a hundred things to get a tinch of exp. One death and you loose half a bubble. I went to the keep just 3 bubbles into 30, got lucky on 2 occasions and got in the Raider room when I logged on. In 2 evenings I am now a half a bubble from 31. The last couple of nights there has been a list with 6 or 7 ahead of me so I camped and played my other character, but if you're fortunate enough to get in a group early on then the Keep is an excellent place to level on up.

There are two things in this game I love;

1) Halflings and (more to the point),

2) Cazic Thule

Don't take this as a flame but .


Cazic Thule at 30 should still be excellent xp. In fact even just pulling from the maze will be very good xp. Green pool and blue pool gators are fantastic xp. Sewars is wonderful. Throne room lower and Upper are very good, bowl is average. The zig with the 4 corners (towers) is outstanding. The Pyrimid where the silver guards is, is also fairly average.

At 30th Cazic has so many options available, the majority of players are between 30th and 35th. Those that hit 35 seem to move on to greener pastures ie Guk.

I can not disagree with you more, Cazic is a truly wonderful zone for your level, and IMHO is a powerlevelers dream zone.
