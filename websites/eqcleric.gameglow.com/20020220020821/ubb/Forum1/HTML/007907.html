quote:

I THINK you meant to ask "which RACE to pick". If so, to put it bluntly, pick WHAT EVER RACE YOU WANT, IT SIMPLY DOESN'T MATTER.

Seriously. There is NO racial advantage or disadvantage that cannot be overcome with proper equipment or faction work.

Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings, High-elves and Dark Elves can see in the dark. Humans and Erudites can not.

Halflings, Gnomes and to some degree Dwarves suffer from being short, so in some places (Kael for example), you have to do some jumping to get around.

Dark Elves suffer from some faction issues, but nothing that can't be avoided or fixed.

Gnomes, Erudites, Dark Elves and High Elves tend to suffer from a lack of STR, but as I said, that can be made up for with the right equipment.

In other words, play whatever race you want to, it wont make a difference.

Who cares about stats? They don't matter.

Who cares about Faction? It can be changed.


That said.... What do you see yourself roleplaying more?

Grumpy, but with a sly wit? Dwarf.

Loves to watch others suffer, heals to extend the time you endure pain? Dark Elf.

Fastidious, but willing to get dirty to help another? High elf.

Quick witted, but misunderstood? Halfling.

Average, but out to make the world a bit better? Human, (Good/neutral.)
Average, but out to make the world a bit worse? Human, (Nuetral/evil.)

Highly intellegent, Better then everyone else, but with a kind heart? Erudite (good)
Highly intellegent, Better then everyone else, and cold hearted? Erudite (Evil)

Absentminded, Obsessed, Funny, Missunderstood, kind hearted? Gnome (neutral)
Absentminded, Obsessed, Funny, Missunderstood, cold hearted? Gnome (evil)


These characterizations are the BASIC personalities of the races, based on Mythology and game information of the history of the world. Drawing from these, you can and will develop your own personality in the game, which may or may not be totally different from the basic build of your race.

In truth, you see more Dark elves with the personas of high elves, and vice versa, then you see the basic personalities.
