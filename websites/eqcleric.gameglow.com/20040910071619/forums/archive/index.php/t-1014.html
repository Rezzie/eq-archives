I have a warhammer of divine grace, a staff of forbidden rights and a truesight hammer all taking up bank space atm.

I use a Paw and a strathbone shield over them all unless I have a shaman around and we are fighting summoned. I outdamage paladins against summoned critters if I get speed and a dex buff with the truesight. I also equip the truesight when not grouped with a tank travelling. at 44+, clerics are generally more efficient casting retribution, and medding back the mana rather than melee'ing if they are wanting to add damage in a fight. I haven't really melee'd since about level 25.

Equiped the paw/strathbone give 45 ish ac and 17 wisdom. Even a 4 wis club and a cgs is barely eclipsed IMO by the very high end clr 2hb's

As soon as verant gives clerics a 2hb with a significantly better combination than the comparably available held items, I'll be happy holding my dolly rather than melee'ing. Nothing 2h'd comes even close to a paw/shield combo IMO.
