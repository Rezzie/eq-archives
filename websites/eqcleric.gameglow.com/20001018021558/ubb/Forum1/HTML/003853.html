I see alot of people post to this board who are genuinely looking for a guide to being a great cleric. There are many posts that go into great detail on certain aspects of being a good cleric, yet it's easy to get lost in the details.
I think some ppl are looking for a simple overview of what makes a good healer. To this end I have broken down what i believe are the 3 MOST important aspects of our jobs. Please remember that the best way to learn is through experience!

3 Keys to Becoming a Great Cleric

1. UNDERSTAND AGGRO
2. Understand the timing and effects of spells and class abilities(both yours and everyone elses)
3. AGGRESSIVELY strive to be as mana efficient as possible.

That's it. Deceptively simple, since there are a thousand finer points to each of these 3 basics. The more you know about each one, the better you will be able to play your cleric.
Have A Good Game
