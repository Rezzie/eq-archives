East Karana - Gorgehounds are pretty good, the upper basement of Highkeep is pretty good, but usually over camped, Runnyeye is good with a group, but can get very trainy, Beholder is good - Mino's and evil eyes...

For quests: Screaming mace - You're of a level where you can pretty much solo the warlord and emporer crush (Did this at 17 and almost died to the warlord then my group found me....) Halfway decent weapon, works for a few levels..

Oh! Unrest! Just beware the trains! There's MANY good places to hunt at your level. If Unrest trains are killing you too much, step out to Dagnor's Cauldron and work on aqua goblins..

Experienced more from my rogue's standpoint than that of a cleric, but none the less I still met quite a few others my level there.
Mistmoore, although it's become crowded there lately. Great exp, good loot. Just have to be careful of the Queen Cristianos Thex fachits if you're a schmurph. (Not sure if she's actually an important DE NPC or not, better to be cautious.) Some notes about hunting there, tho...if you hear a train announced, find a corner and hide (or zone if you're on the ramp); the walls of the pool area are good, and the cove beside the tower is also good.

The aviak village in SK should be a hoot. (IMO not worth the time due to the campedness of the place, but lots of people still seem to stay.)

Najena...wander in with a few friends, go smack bonecracker around, set up camp and pull the ogres and elementals. (This is a good one IMO, it is rarely a crowded zone.)
