Well I feel very fortunate to be on Erollisi Marr. We don't have a list but we do have a respected camp. On Erollisi Marr, if a Cleric is camping Ragefire and keeping the Fire Giants clear, it's a respected camp. The catch is that it MUST be a Cleric of appropriate level holding the camp. No Monks using Feign Death and no Rangers/Druids sitting at the zone line hitting track every 5 minutes.

My guild has finally completed our first two Cleric Epics. The first time we had Ragefire pop roughly 12 hours after Naggy's death. In 5 minutes we had 30 people in the zone. The second Ragefire popped ~80 hours after the first Ragefire but it was literally 5 minutes before the servers went down for this weeks patch... After Naggy died, our Ragefire popped roughly 28 hours later. 15 minutes later we had 40 people in the zone and he dropped like a brick (and he dropped a Cloak of Flames! Woo Hoo!).

Both times the camp was respected. We were not challanged and we never had to fight off another guild to maintain the camp or keep our Ragefire.
