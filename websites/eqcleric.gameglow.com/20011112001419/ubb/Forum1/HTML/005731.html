Yes, Naltron can provide a necro with an additional 320 mana in a minute and a half, but only IF they are can top off mana and hps before the pull.

Here's my take on buffing and it roughly translates into 'Bankrupt Cleric' =)

I symbol everyone in my group. Before L58, I cast marzin on everyone. It's a safety net, for me as much as for them. It means that if I'm just a little bit slow on that heal, the caster that finally did aggro something has enough hitpoints to withstand a couple of hits while I'm healing.

After 58, group symbol everyone and give Marzin to the enchanter and primary tank/puller in normal, easy experience grinds - everyone gets Marzin (now aego) in more difficult areas.

For a necro, hitpoints are God. A good necro isn't just liching, tapping and nuking - they are mezzing in a tight situation, healing when necessary to help me out, feeding me mana... a good necro in the party is a cleric's best friend. I notice a huge increase in efficiency when I have a good necro with me. Those extra hps give the necro more of a cushion for liching and tapping, allowing them more freedom in the choices they make during battle.

Aggro happens, trains happen, bad pulls happen - giving people the hp they need to survive them, even if they are rare, is part of our job.
