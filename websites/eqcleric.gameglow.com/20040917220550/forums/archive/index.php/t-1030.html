Gnomish cleric here. One day in the Frontier Mountians myself and a Barbarian Rogue were looking to join a party. On the way to meeting our new partners the rouge goes LD. I get there and they ask "where's the cleric?" http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Not as often now, but I used to get this alot "Wow I didn't know Gnomes could be clerics."

Just last week I was at the Bazaar in the Commonlands sitting at the tunnel entrance listening to the auctions for anything interesting. When all of the sudden a wood elf druid that was near me says "Gnome Cleric!" wich draws some stares, and remarks from surrounding buyers, and sellers. Some even expressing pity for me, as if I am some cripple! I found this most amusing since these beings were druids and necromancers, and their opinions for the most part mean little to me.

Being a bit of an... ahmem... oddity does have its benifits. People tend to remember you more readily then say Dwarf Cleric X or High Elf Cleric Y. Not that there's any thing wrong with those clerics, espicially fellow followers of the mighty Duke of the Underfoot. Oh here I go rambleing again, but needless to say being a Gnomish Cleric is not the life of a cripple as some would have you think, but rather a challenge that the Duke has set before us Gnomes that I have answered.
