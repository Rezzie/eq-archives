Well, I like my Dark Ember for a few reasons.

It's useful against healing MOBs for interrupting their casting because it has a fairly low delay.

If you solo (which I do half the time) it's better to finish off the MOB with melee than overkill with a DD spell. Trying to sit and med as the MOB flees is dangerous in some areas (such as DL) and risks the MOB getting so far out of range that it begins to heal fast.

Also, soloing blue healers such as spectral curates is quite a challenge and you need a weapon like the DE, because once it starts to heal you have to DD, stun, AND melee toe to toe (a lot of fun) for interrupting to win.

If any cleric knows of a faster weapon with a proc at least as good as the DE I'd like to hear about it (though I doubt I could afford it). Good huntin!
