Yestrday I hit 54 and finally got to scribe the Unswerving Hammer.

We were in Skyfire and using snare/fear tactics.

Got the wurm feared and launched the pet. It took one step forward, started doing the gate or invis spell graphic and suicided. I tried this twice more and every time it just suicided. Yes, I did have the mob targetted.

A while later we went to Burning Woods to hunt. This was with a shaman and magician, and we just let the pets melee. I launched the pet twice and it fought ok against these unfeared mobs. It was hitting from in the teens up to double 48s against tatterback gorillas. The first time it did about 150 damage (for 600 mana) before the rilla took a swipe at it and killed it.

The second time I let the other pets build up some aggro. This time the hammer did 300 damage (for 600 mana) before it was taken out with a small lovetap.

One thing to try might be to launch the pet and then fear the mob; maybe the hammer won't suicide that way.

But in a nutshell, I don't think I'll be memming this spell too often.

Here is a copy from my post on the general forum.

Just a little observation over the weekend on my new pet. First, our pet CAN last longer than one mob. I had him swinging up to 3 mobs max so far. How you say? Well..... I think I got it figured out. After he is done killing one mob, he either will come back to me and die or stay around the area he fought and die. I have noticed that if a mob is aggro right by where he finished, he will continue fighting. As for staying ability, I have also learned that if the tanks chain taunt, he will live (most of the time) through the entire fight. Our pet works great against high HP mobs. For example, we were fighting the emperor on Sebilis. I cast after 10 sec into the fight, tell the tanks to start chain taunting. He has made it through every emperor fight so far. He swings at 40 to 50 a pop and put that against a uber mob like
the emperor and you got about 1500 to 2500 damage over the entire fight while you are sitting down medding. As for the multiple mob situation I have been experimenting with. He worked extremely well in the Pickler room, he helped kill 3 mobs there. I have been thinking that if the tanks go to their next target right away and get the mob agro, the pet will stick around. Also, he CAN be buffed. I use my sleeves and gloves, it helps TONS. Now there is a down side, it is a crap shoot. I have sicked him against beetles and he got nailed right away and there was 600 mana down the drain. But over all I am extremely impressed with our pet. Also, these are just my observations from
experimenting with himover the weekend. Anyone care to add on, to what they have observed?


Also, new finding. His max mob kill count is now 9. I was in perma and went to town with him.
