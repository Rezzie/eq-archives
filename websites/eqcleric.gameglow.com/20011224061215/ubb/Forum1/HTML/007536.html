Welp, I got nerfed. Or more accurately Erudites did. Our foreheads are HUGE. My maroon has been nerfed to dark purple. For those who missed out last night... you didn't. Most of it was people logging in, going "OMG! NOOOOOOOO" and sobbing uncontrollably.

ALL the Erudites look like this at the start. Even the women. Moreover, (I rather liked this term a friend used) all the women got visited by the boob fairy. There must be some magic spell Luclin casts because not only were all the women unnaturally HUGE, they should have been topheavy and falling over.

The upside? Armor looks like armor. Yes, that's right. Armor is now different from robe models. And it looks sweet!

Faces are now comprised of 4 pieces instead of one. Hair (forehead in my case), face, eyes and beard. Yep, I can now have a beard. On this aspect, I tip my hat to Verant.

Not all the "improvements" were bad. Human females turned out ok. Barb females did too and they're the only ones not disproportionately large. They actually look BETTER now (unless they opt for the spikey hair look). Human guys got a good makeover (no more manboobs). High elf females got the best by far. The baseball cap is now a kitty helmet (just like the male cultural helm). There are more hair color options and in probably one of the smarest ideas yet, High Elf females can now opt to wear their hair down instead of in a pony tail. The look is simply stunning.

Need to buy more RAM to see the rest of the models but it looks like it's 50-50 so far for good and bad things in the new models.
