After a couple of nights in Kunark finding zones that were mostly green to me (Firiona Vie, Lake of Ill Omen, Frontier Forest) I finally found one with mobs of an appropriate level for me to hunt in.

My party and I were searching the Frontier Forest for a rumored dungeon and thought we had found it, but instead zoned into the Burning Wood.

Mobs were high level Sarnaks (champion), Tatterback and Tottering Apes (undead, though they don't look it), plaguebringer skeleton, Wurm (cool, looks like a little dragon, though we didn't fight it. And a red wasp of some sort.

Everything was blue at 45. Note that mobs seemed to have a strong tendency to attack the weakest member of the party.
