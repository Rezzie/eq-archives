Killing him in the room where he spawns is best. Have done this two times, he has never cast an AoE spell to agro those around him either time. Just take 6 peeps into the room, buff up and when ready hand in the statue and whack him. I had an enchanter there just to be safe but never have really needed them.

Pulling him to the zone can be a disaster. While some are successful like binaa some try and do this, he casts an AoE spell on the way to zone and the crap hits the fan. People running around trying to kill him as he agros more mobs, etc... Why bother with the chance of this.
