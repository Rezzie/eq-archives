The lower level rezzes are really a question of time. At 56 now, I tend to avoid taking a 39 rez, since the XP difference between 50% and 90% represents probably a couple nights of XPing for me. I have taken one, notably when I died in an out-of-the way zone that was pretty empty (Velk's Lab) and needed to move on... it would have cost more time.

However, the difference from 90% to 96% is tiny... what, 15 or 20 minutes tops of XPing for me. Unless there's a 56+ cleric standing there or on the way there for me and very close, I'll always accept a 49 no questions. I don't understand the people who get picky over a lousy 6%.

And as a cleric, if someone doesn't want my rez (at 39), up to them. I still get my boy scout medal for the day
