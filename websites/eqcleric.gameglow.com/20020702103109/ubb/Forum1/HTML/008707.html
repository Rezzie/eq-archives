First 3 should definitely be put into run speed.

After that - you need 3 more in general abilities before you can open the arch abilities.

Once you start into your arch abilities, you can choose to put into skills depending on your play style.

If you are a soloing cleric, you will want some into mental clarity, casting mastery and casting fury.

If you are a grouping cleric, you will probably want to put into healing adept rather than casting fury.

Although I don't have any AA points yet, I've read a lot of discussions on it.

In Archetype, the general consensus is that the best skill is Spell Casting Mastery (SCM). It will reduce the mana cost for all your spells, particularly the one you specilized in. Mental Clarity (MC) is a good one, especially if you do a lot of XP groups or soloing. SCM is best for XP groups or Raiding.

I'll give you my route for AAXP points, I want to get Mass Group Buff (MGB) first, then go back for other things:

General:
Run 3 (3 AA)
Wis 3 (3 AA)
Archtype:
SCM 2 (6 AA)
MC 2 (6 AA)
Class:
MGB (9 AA)
------ 27 AA to get MGB ------

At this point, I would probably go back for SCM 3, then MC 3.

I chose to work on Healing Gift, because this gives a cleric more sheer raw power with which to heal, and that's what it's all about. Three levels of this archetype (12 AA points) gives you a 10% chance to double heal on every heal cast, including group heals. (Mana efficiency is doubled when you get a critical, of course.)

This is the equivalent for us of the wizard's spell casting fury enhancement, and they always go for those huge critical blasts.

There is nothing like being in an AoE group and having mana for just one more group heal, and pulling off a critical to save the day. Nothing wrong with momentarily taking all the aggro, either, for the enchanter will stop everything in its tracks next round.

Trying to save the enchanter with fast heals? It's a lot easier when some of them hit for double value.

(Those who scoff at those 21000 point complete heals shouldn't, there is a Greater Curse that reduces healing effect by 75%, and that means that that 21000 point CH will heal only about 5000 on someone so afflicted.)

Besides, it's very impressive, and people will remember and want you in their group because of the added measure of safety Healing Gift provides.
