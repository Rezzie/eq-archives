Sol B finally will get it's own thread, but not because I just wanna keep everything neat. I had an absolute blast there last night, and one of the hardest and longest fights I've ever been in.

Right before the fire giants there is a room with a spider design on the wall. It looks like it's a lava painting, for lack of a better term, and their are several bodies encased in silk (victims of the bugs). Just around the right corner (when facing the lava painting) from this room spawns the stone spider, one the left is where the noxious spider spawns. In the room there are 3 LDCs that spawn and there are 2 wanderers that can come to the entrance of the room (if you are not fighting they generally will not come in unless you are close to the entry).

Now the group consisted of a 44 ranger (Kolen), a 44 warrior (Banzeye), a 50 Mage (Nim.- I can't remember his full name), a 50 enchanter (Goldius), a 50 cleric (Massieus), and a 49 cleric (me).

We'd fought to this room and killed the stone spider twice, the nox once, about 7 LDCs and about 4 laval beetles. Things were going great with an enchanter and 2 clerics. In fact we are pulling so fast I have to memorize Extinguish Fatigue (kinda like being back in Kedge heheh)

We are all between 50% to 70% mana when the nox spawns. We jump him and start whack'n away, but low and behold a wanderer coming to join, no biggy. Opps, there's the other wanderer (a lava beetle this time) and now the fight is 3. Enchanter charms the LDC and sicks it on the nox. The enchanter is a blur running all over the room messing, slowing, etc. The clerics are casting complete heals on the tanks, everything is fine. The Mage's pet dies (darn those earth ele pets have TONS of hp!), so he makes another and is getting low on mana- as is the enchanter.

When the nox is 1/4 life, the LDC breaks charm and as it hits Goldius I stun it once and it dies. Just as this happens, the room starts to spawn. 3 more LDCs join the fight. Both clerics are "almost" chain casting complete heal on the tanks at this point, the nox dies. Both tanks are poisoned and have boiling blood on them (ouchies), the enchanter is almost OOM but she manages to charm on LDC. The other cleric goes OOM, I stone down to 2 bubs of life and cast back to back complete heals on both our tanks (barely making the last one). This puts me totally out of mana.

As I sit down to med I start thinging of the corpse recovery process and think, my GOD this is a great fight. The Mage's pet dies again. We start saying "Don't loot the corpses, I've got to get a screen shot of this.", quickly responded to with, "Ya, if we live."

Everyone is taking some damage at this point, then mage and enchanter are about 1/2 health, the other cleric got jumped so he couldn't use his manastone and is about 1/2 health. The tanks are getting to about 1/2 health when the other cleric says, "Enought for CH". About 20 seconds later I say "me too".

Just after I said this, the first wanderer spawns and comes in to join the fight- this time a lava beetle. And it brings one from the other room. With a little mana the mage casts another pet and lets the pet tank one after the Enchanter charms one of the LDCs and sicks it on the remaining untargeted LDC.
We all sit to med while the ranger and warrior are trying to not get hit. The ranger starts healing himself and then jumps back into the fight. The last ones went down pretty easy after this.

All in all to toll was: 7 LDCs, 3 Lava Beetles, 1 Sonic bat (I know I didn't mention it, I forget where in the battle he came in), and one Noxious Spider. The battle took so long that the room was just about ready to spawn again. That night we killed 2 Noxious and 2 Stone spiders. 2 BCPs and 2 Drapes, a bunch of silk and tons of research stuff (only one that anyone could use), and both the tanks got to 45. After the above fight, my experience bar moved almost a half bubble.

Even though we thought we were toast, no one panaiced, and no one died. I know a druid or 2 that would REALLY wants this BCP I won in the random. I just have to decide if I wan't to make a friend for life, or trade this thing for some stuff I want http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif I can finally get that Paw <grins evilly>
