There are a number of battles in SoV where the breastplate is very useful. The best example is a dragon named Aaryonar, he guards the Halls of the Forbidden in Temple of Veeshan. Now this dragon has an AE dot that sucks 500 hit points over 10 ticks, and sucks 500 mana a tick for 10 ticks. Which means once the dot hits you, it will suck 5000 mana in 60 seconds. Now most us clerics have around 3000 mana +/- 350, which means we are OOM in 36 seconds even if we don't cast a single spell. You can't med back, since every tick you get +21 mana from medding and -500 from the dot. This is where Donals Breastplate and Shissar Apothic Staff come in. Despite its 30 second cast, I've managed to use it 7 times in one fight with this dragon.

The Donals Leg effect is nice, but the peridot cost will eat you alive. If I'm gonna use a single target base symbol I use Marzin or Aegolism. If Naltron will suffice then I use Naltron's Mark and save 52.5 pp.
