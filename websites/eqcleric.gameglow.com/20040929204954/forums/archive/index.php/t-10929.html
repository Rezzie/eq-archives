My guild failed also the first 2 times. His rampage and agro is very excessive and control agro is very hard if you have to heal the damage he puts out.

The way we got round it, and eventually won was fairly simple.

Sacrifice a slower.

Get whoever pulls him, do it in front of a slower. The resists on it are pretty minimul at best. Pull to camp, start with a CH chain with a nice delay such as 60, and boom the critter is dead. The difference between agro control and agro all over the place is simply put amazing. I immediately saw that we had won from about 15 seconds into the fight.

He has exceedingly high ac so make sure you have another slower as dispite his low hps, he can take a while to kill.
