At 37, neither Gimblox nor Burgurgle camp is trivial. You'll need a decent group (with crowd control) to do them and they are the easiest parts of our epic.

I'd say no need to rush. When you are ready to start in your low to mid 50s, grab one tank and you can finish the easy parts (Gimblox and Burgurgle) in a week. One group to do the Priest in SolA, a large raid to do Chardok, a lesser raid to do Ixi, and a long boring cammp for ragefire.

Now go level up and get yourself cheal, celes heal, and your rezzes. Those are definitely more important.
