Just a note that the longer quest druids were doing for their PotC spell gives 2 earrings as a reward. The first earring is druid only, but can be handed in for the second one, which is All/All and has FT1:

Earring of the Solstice
AC 5
8 wis/int
5 stam
55 Hp/mana
5 fire/cold/magic
Effect FT 1

All/all

There are nine tradeskill items to make or have made to give Rebecca Cabbageleaf in FG for this very nice item.
