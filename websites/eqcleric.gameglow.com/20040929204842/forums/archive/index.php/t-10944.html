Since you are a young guild I would start with small epic fights for those that are around 50. This would include some fun runs on ToFS, Phinny, and possibly trying some planes.

There are also some personal quests your guild could work on like Chardok faction and then you can go after very low bosses like Drussila.

Your best bet is to xp most of the time and raid once a week or twice. Getting epics will make you stronger, but xp will be needed to get those epics.

Good luck!
