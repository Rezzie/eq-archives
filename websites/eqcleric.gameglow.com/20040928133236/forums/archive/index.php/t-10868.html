HoT (Halls of Testing)- east wing is where you are going then.

1. Getting to ToV will be easy as you are not KoS to CoV - so you dont have to worry about Sontalak. Have all casters bind right outside.

2. Move everyone down into ToV - down the picture hall and setup in the hub room facing Zemm.

3. Have your monks open the door behind Zemm and jump into the pit just behind. Have those figure out when they can pull a single wurm. Dont be afraid to try a few times to get it right.

4. Pull the wurm back up from the pit staying beyond the door and have everyone rush and kill it. Be buffed with cr and fr (used to be only cr, but I think they fixed that).

5. Apply beatdown.

6. Repeat 4 and 5.

7. Once pit is clear - move your raid down there and pull from either side. Look into the east door at your own risk http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif

8. Thats the hardest part of HoT really. From here just have your monks scout well, pull singles and keep a watch on the roamers.

9. The named (drops parts for quests) can hit hard. One particular place seemed to often yield 2 of those - but always be prepared to split tank, you have the crew for it.

From a clerics viewpoint - buff hp, cr, fr - and have ae heals ready. Also, have chanters sieve more often than not until you learn which mobs need that and which dont.

It shouldn't be terribly hard to do HoT with a ProGiant crew - you'd need to clear the Hub first so that you don't aggro CoV aligned NPCs during the first few pulls of HoT, though. If you've been farming Kael armor, you'd have no problems from there. Just be aware of respawn and remind the ProDragon guys not to take faction hits when you have to kill the CoV Drakes http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Few notes to clear up my answers:

HoT NPCs have no faction and are KoS to everyone.

There are CoV aligned Drakes near the entrance to HoT (at the Hub).

There's no way to my knowledge to avoid these CoV Drakes while breaking into HoT.
