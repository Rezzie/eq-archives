I've played a lot of different classes out to the high teens and in this range there are only 3 uber-classes.

1) Druid

2) Cleric

3) Shaman

It's the downtime, or lack of it, and the buffs along with direct damage and healing that make these classes the best.

People haven't really clued into it yet but these are the tank-mages of EQ.

Druids are the best because they gain ALL of the major caster abilities (invis, bind, gate, DD, heal, speed, buffs) early on.

Clerics are next, because once they hit 14 and get Smite and Expulse they have almost no downtime and can solo blues and white or yellow undead. In addition of course they get most of the other caster abilities (bind, gate, heal, buffs),

Shamans are third, only because their downtime is higher due to the need to continually buff themselves or party members. Other than that they gain the same nice mix of abilities that the Druid and Cleric do.

I haven't been to upper levels yet (highest is L27 Shaman) but I'm guessing that Cleric will be best after 30, followed by Shaman and then Druid.

So, if I had to switch classes before L30 I would go with Druid. After L30 I would go with Shaman.
