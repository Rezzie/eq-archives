I did some testing a few months back after I found that some very low skels didn't see through regular invis as they normally would have. It turned out they were flagged undead AND summoned. I know it sounds strange that they were flagged as 2 types and I was suprised to find that out myself. I was casting undead only and summoned only DD's on them and both landed just fine. So my theory is that VI somehow has messed up in some tables that list what spells/effects various mobs are affected by and which ones not.
