OK, this is my first post, and I just have to boast about a recent achievement me and my two friends made.

I’m a level 56 cleric and my two friends, a 55 monk and a 55 chanter started a guild recently. We often like to putt around together in Skyshrine after a successful Kael raid and lose some of the faction that we have capped out in search of the elusive oiled greaves my monk friend wants so badly.

Well, with EQ running on one comp and EQMaps up on the other, I started looking at all the possible mobs and loot that we may find, and came across the profile of “A Large Velium Statue”. This giant-sized golem was listed as a level 54 mob with 23k hps, and doubles for 190. So my friends and I figured, “What the hell? Lets give it a try. He’s Blue…”

Buffs and buffs and buffs began, and we waltzed right up to that big blue bugger and he smiled back at us like any ally would… then the monk started to pummel him like there was no tomorrow. The chanter followed up with a quick tash, followed by slow after slow after slow, all resisted. Meanwhile, the monk was happily having his ass handed to him, while I was attempting to med between ComHeals. Every once in a while the statue would turn and beat the tar out of the gnome in his pretty blue robe, and sooner than later, the gnome was dead (no big loss).

Finally after somewhere between 15 and 20 complete heals, (bringing me down to 15% mana) the big guy dropped. Our reward? A medium coin purse and a scroll of Improved IVU… whoopity-friggin-doo. But was it funn, challenging, and a hell of a confidence booster? Hellz yeah. The monk and I were 10ft tall after that… and sooner or later I got around to rezzing the gnome.
