I recently took my non-cleric twink ( kitty bard ) through the Luclin newbie cycle. An AWESOME cycle is this:

1-15 Shadeweaver Thicket, towards the higher end of that level head into the tunnels and kill aliens.

15-20 Paludal right at Hollowshade zoneline. after about 17 or so, fight through to the next cave to the right looking at the lake right there near the zone. There's Shik'nar back there that take you to 20 or so real easy.

21-23 Move over to Shadowhaven side and kill Bandits.

23-35 Marus Seru. Kill rockhoppers to start, then move on to Stonegrabbers from there. At the higher ends with a good group you can try. . .

35+ Dawnshroud Peaks The xp here is just insane. We kill pretty much anything that moves. Usually set up near Grieg zoneline and pull the neandrathal looking guys, and if we run out we move over and kill zelniaks and shrooms until they repop.

Hope this helps. It's definitely more fun than the same old stuff you went through with your mains.

Unrest is awesome fun. Set up behind the porch along the back left wall of the zone. You can pull from inside the house if you want and also kill any and all of the yard trash in the area.

When pulling from the house a few things to remember:

The pathing is clockwise around the 1st floor. Anything you pull will path clockwise and pull anything else in the way. When pulling, pull the nook in front of the door and then go counter clockwise around the first floor.

Do NOT jump over the railing of the porch when pulling. This will cause the mobs to path back through the house and come out of the back door pulling the round room with it.

Practice root parking adds. Most pulls can be done either single or double. The practice root parking will pay off in later levels!

IF you decide to go inside and pull things to the round room, stay off of any furniture you might see. It will aggro the stuff upstairs and create a hell of a train.

Good luck and above all things, have fun with it!
