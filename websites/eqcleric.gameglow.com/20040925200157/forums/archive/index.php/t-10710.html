abashi's reply:
The difference in loot drops is due to the fact that the zones are designed to drop X pieces of armor per day. Since spawn frequency in Hate is higher than Fear, the percentage is less. We may have underestimated the amount of time necessary to clear Hate, which is having the effect of giving less of a reward for similar effort.


I've mentioned this to a few of the zone tuners, so we'll look at it. However, I can tell you that we haven't yet decided whether or not the loot difference is an imbalance at this point. As you may recall, a few weeks ago people were complaining that Fear was useless and people were camping Hate, now they complain the opposite. It's possible that we let the pendulum swing a bit too far in the other direction.
