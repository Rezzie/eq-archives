Rosieana - King in SolB is too low for a 45 in a full group. Though you and another 45 tank could hold it just fine. At 45, though, that is a loot camp not an xp camp. You need to be doing BnB or LDC in SolB for solid xps at 45.

Tundar, I can't believe that I forgot about the Bloodgills! They are mad xps! The ZEM (zone experience multiplier) for LOIO is 100 - same as all newbie zones, this makes it VERY profitable to kill in and you're right, the Bloodgills are a great xp train. Basic tactics - EB everyone and go to the top of the underwater dome. Go down one of the sides and start swimming around the ridge at the base of the dome (not the base of the building). At each corner there will be a spawn of 2 bloodgills. Slaughter those two, then move to the next corner. Easy, relatively safe, increadible xps!!
