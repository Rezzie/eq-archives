9 blue can be done in 4 hours in NEW chardok with a full group...
Start at zone in...clear tunnel to fort- pull front of fort and stuff in middle...Clear exit ramp- then clear fort- if you're lucky Captain of watch is up or watch sgt, two named good drops. Then leve up into tunnel (I recomment one and only one good group at a time take this path)
Spawn rate on commons is 11 minutes so the trick is KEEP MOVING at all times.
Work pulls down tunnel if something gates everyone sits immediately to bring it back. Mez or charm adds or root park.
Nearing captains and T to bridge or to mines (bank area) go up that left ramp before ye reach that area. Clear that lil back room 2-3 mobs lotta slaves (kill em too they can randomly drop some nice gear esp boots for healers)
Now pulling captains can be hard or easy. I've seen both.
Pulling em to the lil niche yer in is okay tactics Plenty of room to move about and root park and room for pets. Some folks starting to set up solo camps in that room and just farm that one sarnak or undead that spawns there and the one a bit up the ramp.
(BTW this is the only sarnak I've seen that drops the rare SOS, bith of theones I' lost a lotto on came from this guy) SoS and the rare staff of flames will show in hand unlike the water sprinkler or burnished wood staff- but I understand if it can drop a VP stave it can drop any of the other ones.
Pull capatain room (3 mobs) ph is a reg cptn. Once cleared pull Bridge keeper and his paired (nearest dog-or sarnak).
The Bridge keeper's PH is ancient guardian, btw. The BK is a Beeg Fooker btw. Real Big.
Sarnaks down here rival Giants for size now.
Closer to their dragon Blood(remember this fact folks-VI has more in store).
Once BK and othe mobs pulled you can move up onto the flat of the bridge area...3 more mobs up here you can fight.
Now you have 3 directions to head.
Across wood bridge towards waterfall and royal area- and I do recomend that you have two clerics in a group if going here single and a chanter and a bard (59-60 best on these two) Pulling royal area for exps is nigh impossible now due to traps and plethora of caster types (sucks for FD guys).
I recomend clering far reaches of the bridges (3) via pulls. Then resting until Captains and BK respawn *(11 minutes isn't much)- or simply moving on.
Facing waterfall move to bridge on left.
Right there on the actual bridge is another left over another bridge- go that way. Should have cleared far side already.
*( for monks Betrayers spawns opposite the BK and slightly to the left).
Fight up this path via normal pulls and move up and up. You should eventually find an OBSERVER or the named Observer. Up here there are two directions you can go- Usually at this time we move back to bridge to begin clearin it again. Speed and constant movement is the deciding factor.
11 minute spawn time. Remember this.
Some PH's for named do take 17 minutes BUT do not count on it.
Clear bridge area again (BK-captain camp) and then captains...you will have to once again clear the spawns outside the captains hall first before clearing captains room, unless you rush into the cptns room mez(glitter or dazzle one's better and cheaper mana wise- and yes your chanters can tap caster types to regain mana too).
Now you can either fight up and out or fight deeper. Depending on time available choose accordingly.
ALWAYS work with and around groups who are following your same path or have decided to risk and settled down on a single camp-the Bridge keeper is popular due to two named and monk epic guy being spawns.
OKAY let's assume you have the time and mana and decide to GO down...to the mines.
Down you go...skeleton miners are 55+ here and hard hitters.
BEWARE THE HERBALIST HOUSE- it's a AGRO factory. Pulling them can and often leads to wipeouts. Even with a huge aoe group I'd be afraid here....
Work down tunnel basically going straight where left turns are available.
plenty to hunt below, but be careful that your highest lvl does pulls, bard LULL works to help pull mobs sometimes event through another with little or no agro.
There are traps down here, and agro bugs especially if you are pulling when a bard's playing a song other than Replentishment Cantata.

I spent almost 2 solid weeks in Chardok after the upgrade. I knew the layout of the zone real well as I had camped several places in there prior to the upgrade.

I have avoided all the front camps, and spent most of my time in the deeper ones. Brodder described how to get to the Captain spawn well enough, follow that then at the bottom of that first tunnel, instead of going right to the Captain/Bridgekeeper, go left, downhill. Stay to the left until you get to a T intersection with a single mob facing you. This is where I "start". To the Left from here is the bank, to the right is herbalists and named foreman.

Bank:
There are 3 named spawns in the bank area:
Foreman Ku`Lul - Mask of Secrets (caster mask), Sarnak Prayer Beads (5ac 15str 35hp/mana 10sv cold, Range slot for CLE/DRU/SHM), and Boneshear (necro dagger that procs Summon Bone Chip)
Drill Master Dih`roul - Etched Steel Baton (14/28+saves all/all), Sarnak War Bow (no drop), and Sarnak Summoners Dagger (Mag dagger/Range with DD proc)
Sarnak Collective Auditor - Darksight Dagger (24/40 caster only dagger), Iskar Hide Manual (5ac 5int 45mana 10sv poi/dis range), and Girdle of Rapidity (6ac 7Cha/Int/Wis 50mana effect: Haste, all non-melee classes)

Other than the 2 spawns on the lower level, you really need a monk to break the spawn here, and even then you are going to get 2-3 at a time on the first break, so an enchanter is pretty much a must also. We form up in the hall before the door to the lower part of the bank and pull to there.

If you go right from the T intersection, there are 2 mobs and another Y split. The spot on the right side of the Y is a safe area with no spawns or roamers. Going to the "safe spot" you will see a 4 way intersection ahead, going left will take you back toward bank, straight is herbalist/alchemist, and right is Niblik (VP Key subquest mob).

Niblik Area:
This is a good camp spot for a smaller group. Sitting right where Niblik spawns, you only have 1 mob that will aggro. To the left are 2 undead and 1 iskar. The iskar will either be "an iskar collaborator" or "an Iskar Trustee", the collaborator is non-aggro and drops nothing, the trustee is aggro and drops one of 2 nice CLE/SHM earrings: 2ac 1wis 35hp/mana and right click Counteract Disease or Counteract Poison. Casting time on these items is same as the spell. The next little aclove has an undead and either "a sarnak foreman" or "Foreman Mirt`akk". The latter drops a Lore, 10 charge instant cast lev item as common, Girdle of Flayed Iskar (6ac 8stam/wis 30mana, priests) as uncommon, and Fester (12/23 50hp 10sv poi, proc Flesh Rot II, Rog only) as Rare. Each of these rooms will all aggro at once so you will need to root/park or mez to split the spawns.

Herbalist:
There are 2 spawns at the entrance to this tunnel that are on the fast repop, pulling these will get both and 1 from in the tunnel which is on normal repop. Kill these then move into the tunnel and fight the roamer and 1 static near the exit to the short tunnel. Next pull is tricky, but can be done with a level 60 (not tried lower levels). Hug the left wall and go ovre to the one mob standing outside the building. Aggo it (NOT with range weapon or spell), and walk backwards along left wall back to group, this will bring the one aggroes mob and one of the 3 from inside the building. The outside one can be "Alchemist
Acolyte", "a Wizened Herb Collector", or a placeholder. Once these are dead, move to the wall at the base of the ramp to the building and pull the remaining 2 from inside. One will be "a Di'zok Herbalist" or "Grand Herbalist Makha". Next, start clearing the tunnel that is ahead and to your right. **WARNING** Don't get too far ahead of mobs when pulling this tunnel or they will path around the back of the building and aggro the 4 mobs there giving you a small train to deal with.

There are several nice drops at this camp, including Ring of Di'Zok (6ac 25hp 50mana), Dizok Escape Staff (right click evacuate for Druids/Wizards), 2 Range slot food items, herb for necro epic (manisi herb), a small manisi plant (range slot item, -10FR/CR +10PR/DR, right click gather leaves, which summons 20 no rent, no drop bandages), and Sarnak Parrying Blade (16/30 10ac).

At the end of the tunnel ahead is another T intersection. Going right is another named spawn Overseer Dal'goor who has a PH of "a scarred overseer" or "a vetran overseer". He drops a nice ring (8ac/dex/cha 6sv magic), Iskar Hide Cape, Sarnak Backstabber (10/20+stats, either hand), and a no drop halfling illusion mask for Rog/Brd/Enc). If you have a strong group, you can camp the alchemists and this mob at same time.

Going left down this tunnel takes you to "the hard part". Here almost all the mobs are 55+, enrage, and need GoK to mez. This leads to the Kennel area with named spawns "Deathfang", "Kennel Master", and "Underboss Myli`ki". It is pretty tough for a single group to keep up with the spawn down in the kennels. We have done it, but there is basically no margin for error.
