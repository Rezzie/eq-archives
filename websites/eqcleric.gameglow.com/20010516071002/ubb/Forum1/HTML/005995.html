It varies and a lot depends on what you can afford or get.

To start things like the Bone Talisman (WIS and MR) is good, but I then upgraded that to my Hate Shield (all resists) and then look for the Sarnak Battle Shield (all resists and AC). The SSB is cheap and good 10 MR for the head.

It is usually not the big +20 item that builds you up, it is the overall use of +5s. For a cheap, easy way to get ready for Nagafen, there are the +5 FR earrings you can buy on the vendors in Lava, You can get the Kobolod Hide Boots (another +5 fire) and then just adding simple resist rings (+10 FR rings and +6 FR bracelents - total should cost you 400P or less if you know the jeweler) and you have +47 FR. You can get more and add join pieces, but it gets to be a balancing act and a constant quest to upgrade.

I don't alwasy use the *most* optimal. I follow Mithaniel Marr and so have the Imbued Plat Diamond Ring. Blue Diamond gives me 3 more to all resists, but with the Imbued ring I get more HP and keep 5 WIS.

Just start slowly accumulating it and over time it will build up.
