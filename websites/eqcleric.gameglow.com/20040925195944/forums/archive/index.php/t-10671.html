Just a word of warning about Kedge, keep buffs going full time. The Mermaids are druids and pack some hefty damage spells and the usual DoT's. At one point, we had a pull of 3 of them and I began casting full heal on our SK puller when she was a 60% life remaining, and she died before it went off. Also, I'm not sure where the critter came from in the dungeon, but we killed Cauldronbubble and I was lucky enough to get the sharkbone warhammer (8/26 +5wis). It appears to be the rare drop though.

Another word of warning: An unfriendly caster in the area can get your whole party killed as we found out in a fight at the zone when some jerk started randomly dispelling us to get our EB. Having a mage or bard along is a huge boost because of the water stones or water breathing song. Good luck to you.
