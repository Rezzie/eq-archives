I'm a 14 Cleric and just started Unrest. WOW. the trains are really a sight to see. I was really spooked seeing about 25 people outside the zone and heal spells going for about 15 minutes straight. It takes a little getting used to, but you start to get a feeling for the place. I know where the trains don't touch me now, and I know to cast Invis vs Undead when I med. Invis vs Undead is my best friend now and I use it all the time.

I bound myself right outside the zone so if I die, it's no big deal. My group got trained on, and I got just about the full assult. I cast divine auora, but could not run. 2 Gouls, dark bone, brabed bone and about 10 death beetles were all over me. The Divine Auora allowed me to take the hits while my group ran away. I was rooted by the ghouls and died, but wasn't a problem being bound so close. It's better that I die than have to wait for a tank to come all the way from G. Fay.

I didn't know Tormented Dead dropped Bronze! Nice.
