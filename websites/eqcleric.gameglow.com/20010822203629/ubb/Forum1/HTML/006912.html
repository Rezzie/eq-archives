I've pulled many times with my cleric...

Its usually quite a blast, but it is definately risky....If the monk dies, you send a rogue out and pull him to camp and res the monk...If the cleric dies, well, its time to head back to the zone out. Of course this doesn't apply in multi-group situations The issue is that oftentimes in more crowded dungeons you'll randomly run into a fd monk splitting 5 mobs, that as a cleric, you have no choice but to aggro all of them.

In multi-cleric groups, raids, or in groups where you have the levels to solo pull, it can be quite the right thing to have a cleric pull...or in outdoor zones where you can train (like fear) since no class has the surviveability of a cleric with jboots

I remember pulling the temple in fear one night and training around the mobs for so long that HT refreshed on the knights I was training....I was laughing so hard when I was burnt to the ground by 5ish HT's.

Darianne is a great puller.

The key to pulling for a cleric is knowing the zone inside and out and, unequivocally, having a chanter that rocks the world.

If a cleric can get singles because of level, then anyone can and it is no special thing.

I know Darianne and her chanter friend pull like clock work, cleric pulls, brings to camp, casts DA, chanter mezzes one, cleric moves, chanter mezzes another, cleric moves, chanter mezzes another, when only one is left, tanks dig in. It is super easy on the chanter, and it is a way that clerics get to really shine as a puller. Clerics can have high hp, high AC, and hte almighty DA. In certain situations, it works great.

I love to pull in HS, DA all those HT's and then let hte chanter and tanks take over. Never had a problem there, but many folks don't want clerics to pull, because even though they are the best class for it in some situations, CR is too frightful. And they are right. CR in HS is a serious pain if everyone gets wiped out because of a hot dog cleric. Thankfully, I work with excellent players and if it would have come to that, our evaccers have gotten everyone out safely.

Chanters make it all happen for cleric pullers though.
