If you're playing mostly during non-peak hours, I'd say this is the best place to be from 19-23.

There are three good single aviak guard spawns for soloing, two double spawns and one triple spawn in the zone. Taking a single spawn at 19 might be a bit tough, it's probably better to be two and try to split up a double spawn.

The best single spawns are the two near the Arena. You can pull one of the guards and not the other, and it's easy to zone if you're near death.

Also, during night, the gnoll camp next to the South Karana zone turns into an undead camp that is also interesting.

At level 24 and 25, aviak guards gets a bit green though, so then it's time to turn to bluer pastures.
