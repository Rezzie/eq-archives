Don't feel bad about needing to vent. You want something that other classes can have, but can't get it. Shamans do seem to have some of the easier armor quests though. I think that is because there are so few of them around. Do a "/who all count shaman" and see how many are around. You will find that there are a very high proportion of Clerics in the same way. Granted, it would take 1 in 6 players to be a Cleric to meet the demand at high levels (so I seem to gather from what I have heard), where Shamans are nice, but not a necessity.

The quest armor is supposed to have a 4 hour respawn time. Once you find out a spawn time, you can be there and ready for the next spawn. Don't take it from someone that is there waiting, that is the type of thing people will remember beyond their EQ days.
