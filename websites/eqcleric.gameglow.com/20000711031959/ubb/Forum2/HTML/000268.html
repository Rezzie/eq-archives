If you are just passing through the zone, then all you need to do is root/enstill - they won't follow you when it breaks as you will be out of range - however, they will pass their agro about you along. If you are staying in the zone, root and atone to clear the agro.

I love Kunark and travel there a LOT. It is very easy to travel without agroing mobs at all, but root and atone are wonderful for those you do agro.
