Actually, thats a darn good area for a group to go to get some nice things, and decent experience to boot.

Your friend didn't keep the arrow going forward to run onto the other ramp, so no problem. Take 5 of your friends, consisting of 1 chanter, 3 tanks (warrior, 2 rogues works nicely, though sub a bard out if possible for nice speedy mana), and 1 nuker and make sure they all know the keep running to get to the landing. Go forward until you get to a room with a broken floor. Standing there will agro the 1 spider below (no problem, need to clear it anyhow). While standing there you will note that across the broken floor there is a pathway (in the pit) that is the path to the exit! Remember that!

Now, fall down into the pit from the broken floor room, and look to your right. Thats the path to where your friends broken body is, and where several very nice things drop =)

Simply follow the path, eradicating everything in your way, and you'll finally come to a room with several doors, and your friends corpse. CAREFUL! One of these doors hides a Spider thats pretty difficult for most, so your party will need to be prepared. A group of 43-48's won't have much of a problem, but be warned that he likes to poison, so have the cure handy. He drops some nice stuff, as do the spiders in the surrounding area, as they have high chances of dropping that shammy/cleric only +wis shield that is ULTRA rare.

Clear the room, rez your friend, battle or gate out, and you're done!

Now, if you found THAT entertaining, you should meet the head honcho of the place =)
