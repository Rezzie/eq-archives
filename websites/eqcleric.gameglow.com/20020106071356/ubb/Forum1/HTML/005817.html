Talk about skewed stats, doing a /who is very misleading since /roleplay or /anon hides a lot of clerics and druids and wizards.

Actually, Therris Thule also has clerics as the number 3 class:
http://www.guildmagic.com/server_stats.cfm?ID=15

Lookup up your own server here: http://www.guildmagic.com/find_character.cfm

So far on every server I've checked clerics are #3. Druids always seem to be #1 and warriors #2. Interesting!

Oh, found stats for all the servers and breakdown by class/level. Interesting druids seem to be fairly evenly distributed between all level ranges, except the bottom levels. http://eq.guildmagic.com/site_stats.cfm
http://eq.guildmagic.com/class_stats.cfm?class_id=3
Clerics distributions are somewhat bumpier: http://eq.guildmagic.com/class_stats.cfm?class_id=2 Either there's a lot of new clerics being made, or else interest in them tends to drop off as they go up levels.

Warriors show even worse downward stair stepping than clerics. So that tends to argue they get left behind a lot in favor of other classes.

Interesting to look at the trends and conjecture.
