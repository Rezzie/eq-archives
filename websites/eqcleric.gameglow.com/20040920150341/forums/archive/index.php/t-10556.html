If you want to solo or partner(preferred) then pull sarnaks from the fort or gobs or skeletons(preferred) to the Frontier Mountain zone line and rake in some xp. There are enough mobs for steady pulls. When you get bored grab a group and head to the "back door" of the Sarnak fortress or even better go just inside the Frontier Mountain zone and hunt Giants for great xp and sometimes great loot. You can do this from 24 into your 30s no problem.
