There is a place out in TD that contains fallen iksars, this place is usually empty and you have the island all by your self

Mob level is between 12 - 16 and i stayed here until my necro dinged 20, slow in the end i admit.

The fallen iksars are nice undead so for a cleric it is a good place to be, they stand far enough apart to single pull, and trains never happen since you are alone also there is no easy escape from here since there are no zonelines around.

To get here take the raft from oasis to ogre island(first and only stop) run across the island heading east you will probably see some spiroc buildings on your right, when close to a mountain (should be on your right)
start swimming east, this will a take a little while but you should see the island after a short while, if it is raining then it can be very hard to spot the island untill you get rather close.

On this island are also some bandits, i for one never tried fighting them, they help out and are between lvl 16 - 20.

When you approach the island you will see some ruins, that contains a 2 spawn and i never bothered, going left from the ruins you will have 4 spawn along that shore line, that where the ones i hunted, also when swimming here make shure you spot the skellies before you run up on the shore, they don't have a huge aggro radius but still, they hit rather hard, low 30's max.

xp is good, loot not that great, from a few coppers to around 5gp, also research material for nec, wiz, enc, meaning runes, words and pages.

Seems an even con at lvl 16 has around 460hp or close and did hit me for 34
