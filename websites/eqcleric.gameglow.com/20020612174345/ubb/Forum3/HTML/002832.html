Won't work, Panteroo. Faithstones are restricted to the class/race for which they are intended (I say class, because Shammies get Spiritstones, which are essentially the same). So that Faithstone of Hate is CLR/DEF only, while the Faithstone of Tunare is CLR/HIE only. Not to mention that most (but not all) have a deity restriction on them too - the inkie one has Deity: Innoruuk, for instance.


Oh, and on being a follower of Tunare - when using the Faithstone while having killed Tunare, you'll indeed follow her ... you'll be killed too!
