Biggest hint: Move FAST!

Move through as fast as possible so you dont get pops while your pulling the next room.

Oh and make sure whomever is getting what knows ahead of time. (IE: Cleric01 gets first note, Cleric02 gets next) Helps clear up any confusion from the start!

AE to prince, main tank him, /grats chanters

AE to library, get lev, have monk pull the next room to library while you are buffing/levitating, AE everything. When you move from library to next room, STAY LEFT!! there is a trap rug that will drop you ontop of King on the right side of the hall.

Pull from king to the first room on left after library, AE everything. Pull king

King is easy, let one tank get agro and keep him healed, /grats clerics. move to king room, get everyone in the pit along the back wall. STAY IN PIT, when mobs come pull them into the pit, DO NOT LEAVE THE PIT.

Have a necro with a stone to rez FD out of AE range, or camp a cleric in the pit.

Let your monks pull the sarnaks out of the queens room, you can get all but 2 easily, The queen will come, let her go back and then bring the sarnaks to pit and kill. IF by accident you pull the queen to kings Pit, you are more than likely dead, wait for the masssacre, login the cleric or bring the necro to rez someone and start clearing queen room again.

At some point it will be the queen and a couple adds, buff up, move to door, have one person rush the queen and take the Initial AE, then all tanks go! Casters can move just inside the door (in the alcove), but stay out of the room! Have a chanter mez the adds, and casters debuff/light nukes, DO NOT pull agro from the tanks with casters... by this time you are gonna be close to second AE, once second AE hits, have casters move in, anyone with a nuke let fly, clerics start chaining group heals. Queen should drop rapidly. /grats warriors

If this is your first time to king/queen, might want to FD that necro at stairs for library pull, at library for king pull, and at corner just outside king for queen pull. I prefer to have a FD necro when possible, respawns are very fast around 10 mins I think, so as long as the room we are fighting in is clear, necro can FD his way up to rez the first cleric, then back to the hunt. If you dont FD the necro and get wiped, be ready to spend alot on coffins.

Last note is make sure you FD the necro in the room behind you, not yours, things go bad and I have seen queen pulled to library by mistake. you want him out of AE range all the time.
