LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...
Zone into kith, decide to cut that little corner of the zone, get aggroed by some kind of knight, think maybe I got lucky when he didn't immediately chase. I apparently got just far enough away to not be able to see the casting he was doing.
LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

remember - this is after playing the game for about 2 weeks.

It was late one night, and I was about to log. I was in Oasis, about to sit down and camp, when I get a tell from this druid, asking me to come to Karnors and rez a couple of her groupmates. I'm tired, but in a decently good mood, so I tell her I will and run to SRo druid rings for a pick up. A little bit later, she shows up, and ports me to the Dreadlands.

Once in DL, I make the long, incredibly boring run, from the hidden valley to KC and zone inside. Right at zone in are a couple dead corpses. I rez them, heal them up, and then wait for her to catch up with me (she zoned a little slower than I did).

As she zoned in, she drops me from her group, and goes over to her groupmates. I assumed that she had fumbled, and hit the wrong key or something, and fully expected her to regroup me and take me back where she found me. I sit down to med, and sit there a couple minutes without being regrouped, or hearing a word from her.

Finally, I send her a tell, and ask her if she could take me back to SRo. She tells me that she doesn't want to zone again, so she's not gonna take me back. Sorry.

Needless to say, I had to buy my own ride back to the civilized world, and to this day hold a grudge. This was several months ago, but I still remember her name, laugh whenever I see her corpse, and refuse her any type of aid if she's ever so shallow to ask me (she has asked me for a couple rezzes since then).

I agree with you about some of those guys are just complete idiots. But I avoid all of that with a set of rules. Some exceptions to rules will also be posted.
I am 56th so I have the 96% rez finally and no epic.

1) I *Never* leave my group to go rez someone
(exception of a guild member)

2) If I'm on free time and they are in my guild or allianced to my guild they get priority rez. (I wont leave group to rez a member of a guild allianced to mine, they should have their own clerics)

3) If it's a stranger I tell them I take donations but it isn't necessary. I will only go up to 2-3 zones away from me. (I will not go through a dungeon for someone)

4) I immediatly tell them they *MUST* have someone drag their body to zone-line or I will not rez them.

5) Whenever someone starts getting upset and curses me, I immediatly take a screenshot of text and send it to their guild's officers and leader, I also IMMEDIATLY let my guild know so they will no longer help that guild until the issue is resolved.

6) Sometimes I will take a day off and do rezzes for 100pp per body. The rules to this method is they must port me there and back when done, someone must drag their corpse to zone-line. Once again if they do not compensate you, repeat rule number 5.

I hope this helps you. I love being a cleric. I have ABSOLUTELY no problems anymore.
