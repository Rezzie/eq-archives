I've petitioned GM's about this at least 5 times. Overall the GM's talked about the server GM not being online and 'I'll tell him tomorrow when he's online'. In the end basically nothing has been done. They often log in ogres and block the castle entrances and get our pullers killed/almost killed and the GM's will move them to a different part of the zone, then they just walk back and stand in the same spot repeatedly.

Our only solution (which has worked quite well) is that the group who gets the kill can loot it. If no one on the raid needs it, then it is destroyed on the corpse before the corpse goes public. Since doing this we've cut down the number of 'camping plooter begger scum' by at least 70% and only had 1 set of arms plooted.

I really hate beggers, even nice ones. Go earn your equipment or go find another game.
