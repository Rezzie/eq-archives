We just did him this past Sunday. We did get some small aggro from temple mobs at the zoneline (a couple of stuns failed) but they were easily controlled with atone/mem blur and that's what I'd recommend if your people don't want the faction hits. Also to make sure he doesn't AE on you while still upstairs (a lot of mobs up there), do not damage him in any way until you get him to the zoneline. He will wrath you, so be sure to have your magic resist up and have a great sense of direction (mine sucks and I took 4 wraths before diving off a platform to get back to the zoneline for the fight). It helps if you have someone escort you up to his room and have the doors opened for you - makes it easier to get out when you're running for your life. =)

In the end, he went down easy but he took a relatively long time. We had 13 people between level 47 and 55 whomping on him (or healing the whompers) and it still took nearly 2.5 minutes -- a very long time for a non-planar EQ fight.

Good luck!
