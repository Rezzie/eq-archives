Tho this is rant forum you did ask for feedback:

Those familiars aren't pets any more than eye of zomm is. Ironically, wizards do get pets identical to ours at L59, except it's a black sword and not a hammer. It also dies when its hate list is empty just like the cleric pet.

Things that many with unswerving hammer on page 50 don't know:

- pets rarely get riposted now. You can cast them in a mob's face and not worry about it dying from a riposte.
- pets don't die when the initial target dies. They die when their hate list is empty. One pet cast on a multi-mob pull could last long enough to kill all mobs.
- more recently, pets can't break mez. So even with an enchanter you can safely send a pet in on a multi-mob pull.
- you can buff your pet. I've cast a few aegos on mine. Which will be nice to see pet hp with the new interface. I wouldn't suggest the pet become MT even if 1k+ hp, but you can make them pretty durable.

I won't bother saying the pet is useful or not. It's entirely how you use it like all spells.
