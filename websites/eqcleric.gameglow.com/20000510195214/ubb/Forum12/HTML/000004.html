The other good thing about druids is their Snare spell.

Snare is more reliable and longer lasting than root. Also, if a snared monster wants to run away then it turns its back and stops trying to hit you...a rooted critter will keep swinging until the end. A monster with 1 health point remaining hits just as hard as one with full health.

One final note, if things go bad and you are gonna die, always always always get a /loc because finding your corpse is a real bummer in the wide open plains. If you are running away and it doesnt look like your gonna make it then just be still and do the /loc and try to die on that spot.

1. No faction hits

2. Close to zone in case you get mor then you can handle.

3. A druid is a must for harmony. for the past 3 weeks we have been pulling directly outof the huts they reside in. People are just now catching on (on Xegony). target the MOB after harmony and pull from the backside of the tent. Safe to fight AWAY from the tents.

4. Decent loot (black pearls on Chargers, ringmail on alomost all of them, as well as pages etc...)

5. A vendor that will sell to EVERYONE (Ulan Meadowgreen)

At the same time, your druid can pick up on Aviak Avocets that run by, and the rare NPC's (Have killed 5 in 3 weeks). got the Crown of Leaves, Feathered Leggings and 2 bark shields.
