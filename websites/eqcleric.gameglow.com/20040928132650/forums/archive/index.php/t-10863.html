I agree with Dalain in regards to Doz vs Hoshkar, PD and Zlan.

As for vs some of the other Velious mobs...

Doz has his 400 dmg single dispell AE and similar hitpoints to Sontalak, but Sont's AE is much more fierce (650ish dmg, and AE pillage *waves goodbye to 4 buffs at once*). Plus, Doz is a rooted mob where Sont isn't. So I'd have to say that Sont is harder then Doz in a direct comparison.

Doz is probably a closer comparison fight wise to Yelinak...both being rooted mobs with tons o' hitpoints. Yelinak's mana drain dot is of course his biggest sticking point but with no dispell and about the same DD, but melee wise I'd say these are about the same. But by virtue of that darn mana drain alone, I still have to give Yel a slight edge on being harder. Or at least I'd say that Yel takes a bit more coordination on the part of casters.

It's really almost impossible to compare Doz to Vindi/Statue/Tormax/Dain though. Since those are pure melee fights with highly mobile mobs that bring friends along for the ride...makes them very different encounters.
