Try the specs in OOT or in Oasis. For some reason I have heard that specs in Ferrott are lower than the ones in OOT or Oasis.

OOT - Find the spot nearest to where the seafury island. You sit on a hill and there is a little downhill, then it goes back up hill to where the spectres spawn around the middle rock. At your level you will be able to pull them solo. Nuke it to bring it down the hill. Stop, root it and then nuke till it's dead.

Oasis - A lot depends if the tower is full. You should be able to pull these solo as well due to your level. If you are having to share the spawn, you could probably sit on the edge and then just fight them there.

Some people would suggest the spectral keepers in Trakanon - stay away from them. They are a lot tougher than the specs are and have quite a few more hp.

At 48, I would think that dreadlands would be a great place to hunt. The chickend (cockatrices) don't have too many hp and were blue to me until about 52 or 53. Sure you have to compete with a lot of other people hunting them, but that's nothing new.

The Drolvarg gnarlers should be blue to still, but might be a bit of a challenge until you get higher level and get reconning.

There are two undead camps which should work well for you. One of them is well know, the other isn't.

The first is the undead ruin in DL, in the NE corner of the zone. This ruin has four spawn points, each of which have a chance to spawn one or two plaguebones or greater spurbones, and occasionally a wraithbone champion. The secret to this camp is unless you're totally boneheaded, it's impossible to pull more than one at a time. A goodly number of plaguebones will still be blue to you, and all the spurbones will be as well. It's a great solo area for clerics.

The second one, which I have never found camped, is smack dab in the middle of the burning woods, almost directly north of the DL zone and a bit east of the FG fort. There are 5 or 6 spawns here, and this place actually spawns some pretty hairy stuff- up to level 44, i Believe. The pulling isn't too bad though- the skeletons have a really tiny aggro range, the gorillas have a larger one. Best thing is, there are no roamers which come around that camp. I've never seen anything even come close to it, so you can relax and pull at your leisure. this place is perfect for a mid to high 40's party.
