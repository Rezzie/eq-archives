Yeah,

That Guardian Armor looks really nice on DE's (silver design) and with some patience it isnt all that hard to get. Just realize that at your level only the occassional Gargoyle Guard will be blue... everything else will be green.

Get into Icewell on a busy saturday night (like tonight most likely will be). If your on dwarf faction just auction to the zone that you are buffing and post-fight healing for the rights to loot some RFV Boots... By doing this you will get first priority over any level 2 newbie who just ran up there for the loot. Chances are you won't be able to loot the legs and bp since they are quite rare and sought after (tis the reason most people in icewell are hunting) and the other easy to get items are not as good as what you already have (gaunts, arms, helm).

If you want to max your doorf faction AND get a sweet azz'd range slot item, do the thurgadin shawl quests:
1) 4 giant toes
2) 10 giant heads combined in a box (given)
3) "feed" the doorfs (requires baking and pottery, but can be multiquested)

Even after i have hunted in Icewell for 4 full days I am still amiable with the dwarfs (I am switching to Kael faction). So the faction hits you would possibly take in Icewell are small. I have killed approx 60 dwarfs and gone from allies to amiable.
