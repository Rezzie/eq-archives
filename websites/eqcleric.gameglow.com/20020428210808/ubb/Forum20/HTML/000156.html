Everyone needs to have a buffed MR around 180 or higher. With that you will be able to resist his Fear 90% of the time.

You need a Clicky Buff Item (JBoots, Gobby Earring are both perfect) as his AOE dispells a buff (usually the top one).

Our Klandicar fights are weird. Either we kill him EXTREMELY easily and make him look like a little girly dragon, or he totally demolishes us. What we do is keep everyone but the Main Tank, Clerics, and debuffers at Dragon Necropolis. The ones I just listed move down first and engage Klandi. NOBODY charges until a Slow is landed. Once you get slow on him, he's pathetic.
