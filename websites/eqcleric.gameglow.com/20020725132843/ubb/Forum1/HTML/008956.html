Today I managed to clear out two manastone looking things from my bank as well as that Shimmering Pearl that I've had for 18 months or so. And I no longer dual wield Prelate Lanterns =).

Interestingly enough Ragefire took 55 hours from server up to spawn on A-Ro. I had ZERO competition in the camp; a few people popped in for a few hours, but nobody contested the camp at all. When we took him down we spent 15-20 minutes getting people logged in and buffed during which the entire time I kept asking them to let me pull and tank him =P (my SK, not Cleric). There was only one person in the zone besides our guild so yes, I know I was incredibly fortunate. A-Ro has had it's share of nasty Ragefire incidents.

Don't ask me what he hit for, his AE damage, etc because I don't have a clue. The fight was a blur.

As is the norm for these the credit goes to my friends who stayed the course after I gave up, especially Dartanian and Zebulah who stayed up late last night with me. And I'd like to thank Dart for convincing my wife that he wasn't a salesperson and that I really did want to talk to him =).

Good luck to everyone going through this camp. I had a pretty easy time of it, yet do have some idea what it's like to watch a section of the screen for hours at a time (after 8 hours of pulling last night for super crap XP we made a pact that nothing would die that didn't have Ragefire in it's name). I will certainly continue to push for a better epic experience.

Rayne
