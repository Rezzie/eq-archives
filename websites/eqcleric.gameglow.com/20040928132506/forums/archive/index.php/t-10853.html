Hi, Rayne. Long time no see.

As a guildmate of Skrim's, I've been working on this one too. I think I've got a plan that will work, but it will take a 13th person.

All the MOB's named above have a quest. That means that when hail'ed by a non-KoS, tehy should stop and chat. So, all you need to do is this:

Pull the three to the end of the Ice bridge using normal FP techniques.
Have the non-KoS person hail the NPC.
Wait for the other NPC's to return to spawn points.
Pull the single.

Now, a MOB doesn't need to have a quest for this to work. Many SS MOB's without quests will respond to hails with a single chatty line. That's all you need for them to stop.

They might not stop if you aren't indiff or better. I kinda remember something about that in the back of my head. I know Yelinak (dubious) won't turn to face me when hailed, so I don't think the put-off comments will stop the NPC.
