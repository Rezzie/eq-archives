I must admit, I slipped. I neglected to mention it anywhere but on our guild message board.

Dikmer is dead.

My reasons were many.

#1: The whole Bladestopper thing. Many people who I thought were friends are now blacklisting me. I am now ignored by many people who at one time thought I was a good person to have in a group. I have been denied groups, and even thrown out of groups over it. If that is how friendship works at higher levels, I don't want to be level 50+ anymore.

#2: I aint level 50+ anymore. I lost 2 full levels trying to get my corpse from Fear a few weeks ago. Basically, a 50 person break never made it in. We all died at the portal. The roamers were aggroing even from across the zone. At one time, I managed to camp at safe hill, and was on the phone with a friend, and I logged in. TOTALLY non aggroed, I was alive for approx 90 seconds and SAW all the roamers come running from PAST the portal to the hill. That is an insane aggro radius, and because of that, I died over 30 times trying to get my gear. I ended up having to wait for a patch to get it all. I just don't have the heart to try to get back to level 52 from 49.

#3: I got hacked. Some prick hacked my account and took everything I had that could be traded, and dumped all my NO DROP stuff in the bank. All my cash, all my equipment. Left me 3 pieces of EM armor, my Aegis of Life and my Ruined Battleworn Morningstar. I petitioned, they told me to write an E-mail. I wrote an E-mail and the response was, "We're sorry, but we can only restore from back ups in cases where your character was deleted. We cannot verify that it wasn't YOU who gave your equipment away."

#4: I am now playing EQ exclusivly with my wife. We either play as level 9 necros (both), Her 14 chanter with my 14 paladin, or her 7 cleric with my 7 warrior. This promotes marital harmony, and fewer arguements over how I escape from her and don't play with her. (and no, she didn't hack me)

#5: What it boils down to, is Dikmer just wasn't fun anymore. I am now having fun being lower level. No more 15 hour camp sessions. I log in when I want, I log out when I want.

I guess I just wanted to clear the air. I had alot of fun here on EQ Clerics, and will continue to read every once in a while, but it will be a loooooong time (if ever) before I play Dik again.

Thank you all.
