Success of DI and DP is (or at least was) based on the target's CHA. You'll have a lot more luck casting it on non-evil warrior with a decent CHA. Ogres, and Trolls have an especially hard time getting success on it.

Some used to think that it checked the caster's CHA for success, but that would have to happen when the spell is cast and then put a "success" or "failure" icon on the tank cause the caster can log off before it activates.
