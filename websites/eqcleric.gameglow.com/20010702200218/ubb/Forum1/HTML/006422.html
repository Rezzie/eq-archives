Agility and Dexterity go in quarters, like 75, 100, 125, etc. Say for instance you're wearing a Fungi tunic, and you have 99agi with it on, 109 with it off, you're going to be missing a lot of extra AC.

If you get a hold of a +1 agi item (like one of those cockatrice feathers....Or is that +cha?), you'll see your AC boost a lot if you hit 100 or above it.

Just ask a jeweler to get you a +agi ring or earring or something and compare the AC sub-100 and post-100. There's also a bonus at 125, but I think that's the last of the bonuses.

I don't know the average for bonus over 100, but with my fungi tunic, I have 98 AGI, and I lose like 4 AC when I switch from my Froglok Scale Breastplate. I have 108 agility with my froggy BP on. When I put on my Forest Loop instead of my Electrum BS earring, my AC boosts like 12, when I'm wearing my fungi tunic.
