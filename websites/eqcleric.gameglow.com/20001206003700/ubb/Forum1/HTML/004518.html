When you see that same person camping a static 6 minute spawn for 18 levels, they have zero interest in grouping by definition. They want to level. That's perfectly ok. They want the maximum xp and sometimes the PP as well. All perfectly ok. However, when you solo, you want the rewards of maximum xp, you get the risk of having no one there to back you up. That's simply the way it is.

When you camp the Dwarf at the Butcherblock Mountain Docks for 5 levels, helping no one but yourself, then when you die, you expect others to come help you? Well.... I see that as somehow selfish. I know others would disagree with that, but that's just how I feel about it.

Personally, I have no idea why anyone would spend the cash to buy a multiplayer game and then go solo all the time, they'd be better off buying Baldur's Gate and not paying subscription fees to Verant. But that's another thread, lol.
