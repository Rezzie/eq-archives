I think the key to most all this is the fact that clerics want to feel useful again.. In many group situations now, clerics are not the "needed" and in many cases not the desired character. In the end game alot of our time is channeled into raid only situations.. This is fine to an extent, but raiding day in and day out does not help a cleric with experience, as most of the raid experience goes to one or two groups..

The other issue seems to be that we want to be able to defend ourselves against those pesky greens.. I think adjusting either our melee, or our casting, mana/damage ratio are not unreasonable requests. Almost every class, caster and melee alike have had some kind of boost, except clerics.. We still bring less to the table when it comes to damage output and defensive skills than any other class out there.. Uping the damage of judgement was a step in the right direction, but no where near what other casting classes have been given.
I don't think we should ever be a soloing type class only, but at least we should be as able bodied either thru our melee, or our casting abilities as other classes are to solo occasionally..even if it is against undead mobs only. Any change in that direction would be a welcomed improvement by me.
