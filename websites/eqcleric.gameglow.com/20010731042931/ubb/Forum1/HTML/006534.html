I went to Sol B last night to show off my new Walrus Bone Shield to the kobolds, and to grind out some XP before going to the planes. I got a group as soon as I zoned in, and we moved into the king room.

The group consisted of me (cleric-46), SK-42, paladin-38, ranger-38, rogue-44, and a mage-45 (with water pet). Not an ideal mix by any means, but with three taunters and a ton of melee damage, still very workable.

We had been in the king room for a bit, got the king to pop once, pulling stuff from out in the hall in between king pops, when the ranger noticed that the kobold noble was up. Given that the halls were being kept pretty clear by groups in the priest and champion rooms, we decided to chance pulling him. I refreshed everyone's Resolution and added Resist Magic prior to the fight, hoping to keep people from being charmed (the kobold noble is an enchanter). The mage handed me a mod rod to use as a panic button, and the SK went to pull while I medded up from 60% mana.

All hell promptly broke loose. The SK got charmed twice on the way back...turns out he had crap for resists. So the resulting pull was much longer, and much bigger, than anticipated. TRAIN TO KING ROOM...

As the train was coming in, the SK's health was dropping rapidly. I stood up from my seat by the throne, hit Celestial Heal hoping he was in range, and got lucky. It caught the SK at just under one bubble left, and held him there til my Complete Heal follow-up went off. A close save. Then the train hit.

As it turned out, the total pull included the noble and his pet, a mage's earth pet that the noble had charmed from somewhere, and at least 2 more kobolds. The earth pet aggroed on me immediately from the Complete Heal. The ranger had the misfortune to get the brunt of the noble's impact, so I just ignored the earth pet and started hitting Celestial Heal on the ranger as fast as I could...it finally channeled through on the third try, with the ranger at one bubble. Close save #2.

It was at this point, with all our melees heavily engaged and the mage nuking like crazy, that the king room started repopping. The first was a greater kobold that popped right on the throne beside me.

Left with few other options, I rooted the kobold on the throne, hit DA and shifted to the other side of the fight. The paladin got the earth pet off me, but another kobold got on me. At this point I couldn't see anything but the red bars dropping...too many arms and legs and weapons flailing around. I just started healing, going down the list with Greater Heal from one to the next (Celestial was too slow and I had to save mana) as fast as I could cast. Mobs were finally starting to drop, the first being the noble, followed by his two pets.

Fortunately, someone someplace got the kobold that was on me off, so I took a moment to look around. Three mobs still up, group at half health for the most part, me at two bubbles with 20% mana. I hit my mod rod, putting me to one bubble of HP left, and started rationing out heals from 30% mana. Fortunately, nothing else aggroed on me as the mobs went down one by one.

The fight finally ended with me at one bubble and OOM, the rest of the group at half health or above, no party deaths, and eight or nine dead mobs on the floor.

Geez, and here I was just wanting to show off my new shield.
