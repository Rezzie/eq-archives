There are many good places in Velious for a level 30 to hunt, really a solid group of high 20s could rock in quite a few spots.

The biggest problem you will find at level 30 in Velious is a lack of pickup groups. Just about anyplace you might go at that level will be BYOG.

I have been duoing very regularly with a necro at the Tizmak caves in Great Divide. It's only a short run from there to Thurgadin. Since you have no real experience with Velious, I'd recommend getting someone to show you the way to Thurgadin first. Now that you don't need teeth to port into Velious ports getting there should only take a few minutes if you get a port.
