I've played a druid to 50. then another druid to 58. and 3rd a cleric to 60 (well a necro to 53, a monk to 60, a pally to 56, a shaman, a war, and a few others to complete the list).


I can say this.


Clerics have CH. CH is over powered. we all know that. But forget about CH for a second. Druids are almost the same healers as clerics. Bekken brings up a good point about DA. but as far as heals go, almost the same. Clerics get a few situational heals that druids dont (group). and CE is better than natures recovery because of time(almost the same hps). But other than that, druids heal just like clerics. What i mean to say is, they're JUST behind us clerics in heals.

Now, druids also get forage, DSs, Ports, group ports, a pet(lol), Dots, quad spells, snare, atk buffs, str buffs, regen(a heal cleric doesnt get), evacs, debuffs, charms, pet buffs with haste, more weapon types, dire charm (=P), great nukes. Druids can solo with the best of them. Druids can quad kite. Druids up to 50 OWN clerics(very important when talking class balance).

Almost everything listed there is GREAT for soloing or exp groups. Clerics realistically dont get any of that. if you take away CH(rumored to happen in POP), the druids >>>>>>>>>>>> clerics. If you give CH to druids, druids >>>>>>>>>>> clerics.

However, i must say, a viable postion must be found for druids on raids. Druids may own clerics outside of raids, but druids need a job to do on raids other than backup healers, and backup nukers. This reason is why i quit my druid, Kireol.

In summary. Druids need raid potential, and clerics need better exp group/soloing ability.

Things i would like to see clerics get in POP:
--------------------------------------------------
AA skill to crit up to 5,000 damage vs undead like pallys get to, but with spells, on our best undead nuke
Improved Undead nuke (725 just dont cut it)
Improved Undead DOT(a 750 DOT that only works on undead is our best? ROFL)
Self only invis AA skill
fast heal for around 1500 hps. long repop(20 secs)
Upped melee = to 3/4 of what a pally can do.(we should be medding or healing, but would be nice to be able to melee once in a while. (The current 1/10th of a paladin is just unacceptable)
AA skill of AOE Manaburn CH(AOE CH that uses all mana)
Improved resurection. maybe spell cast with no rez effects, with 98% rez.(you wouldnt use this much, because of mana cost, but to have it would be nice)
Lastly, a cana, or gather mana would just be SWEET, but this one is dreaming here.

Those are my thoughts after playing both classes.
