In response to Hightop:

There are 2 mobs who will aggro as soon as you move from the zone-in point. They can be any of:sepulcher skeletons, undead oblations, bile golems, bottomless feasters (have I missed any?). In other words either live or undead.

If you zone in and don't move, you shouldn't get aggroed. But you will as soon as you move, and you will need to kill or crowd-control both mobs. Bottomless feasters drop the candle, which is a NO RENT key to the north wing - useful if you don't have a rogue and want to move beyond the zone-in.

Once they're clear, there are 2 pyre golems guarding the entrance to each wing - they're safely out of zone-in aggro range. They can be single-pulled reliably by anyone 57+ (56+ maybe, but don't count on it). Otherwise you'll get both on a pull.

Do NOT run up the east wing stairs to pull, there's a pit trap. If you do manage to fall into the basement below and are lucky enough to survive the fall & probable harm touches, dash for the portal pad in the centre - it'll take you back to the zone-in. Mobs in the basement will never follow you back up, there isn't a path.

There is no safe spot to camp the zone mobs. Respawn time in here is roughly 21 minutes though, so it's easy to kill the 2 zone mobs and pull golems until the 21-minute timer is about to run out, then stop & wait for zone mob respawn. Just watch the time.
