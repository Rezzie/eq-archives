ITEMS NEEDED
Some form of Enduring Breath
Rations & Water as camping take awhile

Step One: Obtain the Orb of Frozen Water
* Go to Lake Rathetear
* Go to Island with 3 Aviaks loc (2800, 150)
* Swim under Island
* Go to cave entrance loc (2831, -912, -221)
* Go to Aquagoblin Tower
(note: Wolfform = no aggro)
* Kill all the Aquagoblin on top of tower
Do not use AE – Deep is tough
* Kill Lord Begurgle in Lake Rathe
28 minute spawn timer
* Loot “Lord Bergurgle's Crown”
* Give it to Shmendrik Lavawalker 3600, 0
15 minute spawn timer
* Let Natasha Whitewater kill Shmendrik
* Kill “a spirit of flame”
* Loot "Damaged Goblin Crown"
* Give it to Natasha Whitewater
* Receive "Ornate Sea Shell (I)"

* Go to Timorous Deep
* Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227
* Swim through cave entrance at loc
(-11530, -3728)
* Follow wall with it on your left
* Swim through the other cave entrance at
(-11336, -1307
* Enter the Hidden Oasis
* Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
* Give "Ornate Sea Shell(I)" to Omat Vastsea
* Receive "Coral Statue of Tarew"

* Go to Temple of Solusek Ro
* Go through the big door and left
* Go to balcony and up some stairs
* Make a left there is a room with chair
* Go to, loc (326,38,29)
* Practice route to the zone
* Have puller come with you
* Announce you are pulling the Priest
* Give it to “a seeker” You want the guy one
Note:Spawns 9am,Despawns 9pm (game time)
Seeker is a guy
* Kill Plasmatic Priest
* Loot "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe"

* Go to Solusek A
* Go the room has pit in it loc (-796, -366)
* Kill Lord Gimblox
Note: 19 min. timer, PH "Large Fire Goblin" or "fire goblin shaman"
* Loot "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring"

* Go to Timorous Deep
* Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227
* Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
* Follow wall with it on your left
* Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307
* Enter the Hidden Oasis
* Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
* Give Omat "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe"
* Receive "Orb of Frozen Water"
* Find Natasha Whitewater in building behind Omat
* Give "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring" to Natasha Whitewater
* Receive "Ornate Sea Shell (II)"

Step Two: Obtain the Orb of Clear Water
* Go to Burning Woods
* Give "Ornate Sea Shell (II)" to Naxot Deepwater
* loc (3400, -2150)
* Receive "Message to Natasha".
* Kill Ixiblat Fer
* Loot "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer"

* Go to Chardok
* Kill Overking Bathezid Di'Zok

* Loot "Singed Scroll"

* Go to Timorous Deep
* Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227
* Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
* Follow wall with it on your left
* Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307
* Enter the Hidden Oasis
* Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
* Give "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer" and "Singed Scroll" to Omat Vastsea
* Receive "Orb of Clear Water"
* Find Natasha Whitewater in building behind Omat
* Give "Message to Natasha" to Natasha Whitewater
* Receive "Shimmering Pearl"

Step Three: Obtain the Orb of Vapor
* Go to Nagafen’s Lair
* Go towards the Fire Giants
Across the ramp up a ledge
Under that banner, Zordak Ragefire will spawn
* Give "Shimmering Pearl" to Zordak Ragefire
* Receive "Swirling Pearl"

* Go to Skyfire Mountains
* Go to LOC p3200 n3200
Big Dragon statue, he spawns in front
Under that banner, Zordak Ragefire will spawn
* Kill Zordak Ragefire, the human level 55
* Kill Zordakalicus Ragefire, Dragon Level 60
* Loot "Impure Heart of Ragefire" NO DROP Container
* Place the "Swirling Pearl" inside Heart
* Click Combine, Container becomes " Heart of Ragefire"

* Go to Timorous Deep
* Go to Island with Underwater Tunnel
* Swim through tunnel across to other wall
* Follow wall with it on your left
* Swim through the other tunnel
* Enter the Hidden Oasis
* Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
* Give "Heart of Zordak Ragefire" to Omat Vastsea
* Receive "Orb of Vapor"

Step Four: Obtain the Orb of the Triumvirate
* Find Jhassad Oceanson on the beach by a tree
* Give "Message to Natasha" to Natasha Whitewater
* Give "Orb of Frozen Water", "Orb of Clear Water",
and "Orb of Vapor" to Jhassad Oceanson

Step Five: Obtain the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
* Be patient, Avatar of Water end up beside Jhassad
* Give "Orb of the Triumvirate" to Avatar of Water
* Receiving the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh

I had a few problems with Fire Giants when I did this part. During the 3 days I was campign there had a few opportunities to examine this.

The idea is to stay at the far end of the ledge from where Ragefire will spawn. If you stay there until he spawns, you'll not get any giant visitors.

If you wander about half way up (about at the break in the ledge) you'll not activate the giants, but they've noticed you. The first time someone goes to pull the gate giants or the ones inside, a few that noticed you will come for YOU, not the puller.

If just one gets on you, you can try to root and atone him.. You can also try to run across the lava (presumably toward the guys pulling them) and get the giants to run around and back after you. Root to over there.

The safest course is to stay at the far end, watch the guys who pull the giants (and watch for any invisible visitors) and wait until ragefire spawns. Then - run up - turn in the pearl, get your pearl back and run back... if possible, head across or down in the lava if any are chasing you.
