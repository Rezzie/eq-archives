You can go from level 11 to 32 in unrest as a cleric pretty easily (I've done it.) At level 30 start camping the blood pool and ghost room and the experience is really good if you stick to a full group of 30 to 35s. Things con blue but with the repeative pulls from the pit below and up in the hall you get really excellent experience.
