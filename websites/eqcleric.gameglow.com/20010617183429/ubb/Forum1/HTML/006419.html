You can sell the canes of traquility for about 750-1000pp each.

Then buy:
2 forrest loops
2 Velium jaspers rings
velium ruby viel

total cost, 1500-2000pp

Additional purchases that will be cheap and easy (prices are approx):
Crysoberly Talisman, 500pp
Charred Guardian Shield, 200-300pp
Springwood Club, 750-1000pp
Adamantite Epolets, 200-300pp

This will put you at about 192wis.

For a belt, a Girdle of Diligent or a Belt of Cenobite will be fairly easy but a little more costly to come by.

I would also recommend you take the time to do the Aegis of Life quest (if you don't already have a cloak, it's a really good one to get. Plus it's free.)

If you have never been to Veliuos, then you should be at least dubious to dwarves there in thurgadin. If you have some baking skills, you can do the first 3 prayer shawl quests in an afternoon. That yeilds a range item that is ac3, 3 wis, 30mana and FREE!

hope this helps.
