Just being a level 45, I kind of know what mobs are 45+ in BW (I was pulling the other day for my group for this reason). The Scorcia Hornets, Tatterback Gorillas, Gullerback, Forest Giant Ancients, wurms, and Sarnak Avengers are all capable of being 45+ (though there were some of each that were blue at 44, so it isn't a sure thing). So, if you are looking specifically for spells, I don't think I would bother with Forest Giant Arbors or Verdants (both constant blue at 44), Ash, Ember, or Cinder Hornets, or the Moldering or Tottering Gorillas. The Sarnak are also pretty much out of it. Of all of the Sarnak that we killed the other day, only the one Avenger was yellow at 44. The Greater Warbone Skels should be 45+ I do believe also, but didn't see any the other day, but I remember seeing a few that were red when I was 43.

And if you are really looking for spells, you might want to consider getting a group together and hit Skyfire as there is a higher concentration of 45+ mobs. Not near the BW zone though. Most things there coned blue at 44 also. Go out a little ways and hit the mature anythings.

Hope this helps anyone shooting for the 51+ spells. I'm not quite that far yet (just one bubble into 45), so I'm not overly interested yet. But then if someone in my guild were to get a Cleric Spell, I'm sure I'd get it, and most of them are 50+. I'd be there with them, but I got all of my Cleric armor (minus one bracer) pre-patch, and spent a LOT of time sitting around waiting for stuff to spawn.
