We did it this weekend as well. I'm not exactly sure it's all that much harder than before. Anyhow, we pretty much fd pulled up to the bridge leading to the royals. From there we zerged each room and aoe'd. Had 4 enchanters stunning so there were no deaths and the sarnaks dropped fast. Once we got beyond the hidden wall, we fd pulled again and got the king with 2 of his friends. Easy. Then we moved into the king room to prep for the queen. Unfortunately, one individual chose this time "to go take a peek" and pulled the entire queen room down on the unprepared party. Almost everyone died to her 880/tick ae dot right away excluding the lvl 59's and 60's. One mage pet had her down to the Q in her name before she gated out and well...that's when the rest of us died.

We attempted to cr with a rogue and also had a cr team camped down in the old safe hall. Rogue died fast to see invis mobs as did the cr team. Summon corpses is the way to go here.

Anyway, overall from start to finish probably only took 2 hours if that. The longest part was getting everyone to chardok for a corpse summon.

Personally, I thought it was pretty easy but that's also do to the lvl's we had there I think. Most were 57+.

As far as classes you need. You need every class on this imo. Wizards, mages, druids for ae nukes, clr's of course, I'd take about 3 chanters atleast for ae'd stun, plenty of tanks and a necro or 2 for summon corpses, dmf, etc. Oh and mage pets for the queen.
