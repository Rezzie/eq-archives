I think that's where this whole thread has taken a wrong turn: everyone is applying a very broad (and VERY subjective) rule to a very specific case.

I agree that if 6 people are working on something and kill it that they should all have an equal shot. But you forget one thing: did they WANT an equal shot? We can't just say "Every must have an equal shot" and leave it at that. Obviously Dikmer had not one but 2 groups who did not feel that they HAD to have cleric boots. Specific case, specific rules.

If you want to apply this broad standard, yes Dikmer was greedy and shouldn't have gotten a second pair, much less sold the first one.

But when it comes down to the actual application of this, Dikmer was given 2 pairs of boots by 2 generous groups. What transpired was between them and him. To put it bluntly, NOBODY here has any right to judge him. Nobody was deceived, nobody was hurt. Yet I see people chewing him out over a pair of boots. I even sense jealousy int he voices of some (as a fyi, Donal's boots are RARE and most clerics will never see one pair, let alone two). What happened was between them and them alone and was offered up as an EXAMPLE for an academic study of NBG, not to be used as fuel for the "Death to Dikmer" fires I see burning here.

Everyone can get off your high horses now. You have a right to express your opinions here, you do NOT have the right to judge Dikmer. I am appalled at how this thread has gone and I'm ashamed to call myself a cleric with the sheer feeding frenzy that is going on here.

I wish I had the power to lock this thread right now because never has one needed locking as badly as this. This isn't even a discussion about NBG anymore really. It's a towering inferno.
