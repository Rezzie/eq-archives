24 is indeed where the cleric begins to break away from shamen and druids. Sure, they get G Heal at 29 but at 29 you get the always handy (and ever begged) Revive. Trust me, you should have no problems with groups after 24.

S Heal is another story. I found it to have usefulness for only tanks since few people got hit enough to bring them to death's door. But it does make you highly desirable. Another thing is at 34 you're high enough to group with high 40s and low 50s. I was able to play healer to my guild's 50s at 34 using nothing but S Heal.

Then there's Complete Heal, the last word in healing. When people complain about our healing I point to that spell. Contrary to popular belief, CH is our class defining spell. No other class can even touch us in the healing department. And you CANNOT do many areas without clerics because no other spell can match it. Remember, a level 60 tank, with basic cleric buffs (of equivalent level), has over 4k HPs. Not even Divine Light, a 53 healing spell (and best non-CH spell), can keep up. Complete Heal is one spell you will never outgrow.

Hang in there, people will be begging you to join them and one day your warrior friend will turn to that wood elf druid and say "Gee, I sure wish I had that high elf cleric in my group still because she heals in one shot... and she's cute too"
