Since all the loot isn't dropping like it used to, I might as well just mention a few things interesting about Karnor's if people haven't figured out already-

Although I have near full EM (Damn breastplate keeps getting randomed off to some newbie cleric everytime! This is not "fair" anymore....) Anyways, my point is-
In Karnor I have acquired:
Jasarth Scale Chestplate
Jasarth Scale Helm
Jasarth Scale Wristguards
Jasarth Scale Leggings
Jasarth Scale Gauntlets

Of all these, they seemed to have dropped consistently with a certain mob.

Chestplate-Skeletal Beserker

Helm-Sentry of Sathir

Wristguards-Skeletal Warrior

Leggings-Undead Jailer (Funny looking brown thing)

Gauntlets-Scryer

IMO- The chestplate and helm weren't worth it. The Wristguards are very nice (ac8 +4char/wis) but are lore=(

I don't know what Karnor is like on other servers, but on ours.....since the newbies have been rushing in, Karnor has been a training frenzy! You can't hunt in there anymore! =(

So how to pull deep if everyone can't even handle the small stuff? Easy, you can pull the undead jailer through the new Kunark Patented Bugged Walls.

It seems you can't stand near any wall ANYWHERE in Kunark without crap aggroing through 5 rooms and through a wall to you. So if you want to pull the undead jailer without going in deep, you just run next to the right wall and stand there.=) Be prepared for about 4 mobs with you, anything from skeletal guards, cursed hands, or an undead jailer.
