A comprehensive list of Twilight Sea armor can be found at http://www.taylir.com/ts_item.shtml.

IMHO, the quests are ridiculous. With a bazillion rares all sharing the same spawn points in each zone, acquiring the items for a specific piece is all but impossible, and that's assuming you can track. At least the quests don't involve purchased gems, in the infintesimal chance you should come across three or four quest items that are all for the same reward.

Worse, most of the quest items come from zones with notoriously poor mobs, like the Scarlet Desert and Dawnshroud Peaks. You'd be better off killing things that drop decent pp, and buying Donal's/ Sol Ro cleric armor. You'd get much better armor much faster (that's on a very old server like SolRo, but I'd think even on Kane Bayle it would be easier to buy armor then getting twilight. It's THAT hard to complete those quests.) Or loot Jarsath for free. Good AC at a good price.
