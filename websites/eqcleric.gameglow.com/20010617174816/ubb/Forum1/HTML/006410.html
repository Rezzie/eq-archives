kurns tower is super fast exp, and there are usually people in there paying top dollar for bone chips so you can make some cash there too. It is also an interesting dungeon design, with very low aggro radius and no real boss mobs.

It can get a bit boring in there. However, you should have 3 or 4 good levels in there and it will go fast.

Other then that, they have really shifted the dungeon focus to the 30+ area. At 18 you might take a look at Runnyeye, depending on the changes they made. Unrest is an option in a few levels. I forget what levels Najeena is for.

Thats all I can think of.

My Lizard warrior alt spent just about the entire time from 12 to 20 in Kurns tower.

It's not an empty zone like Runny, but usually plenty of stuff to hunt without much of a problem. I like the dual zone exit design- a little bit easier to avoid trains/training people. Also the agro radius on the mobs is pretty small.

The pathing isn't so great and loot ain't that hot and the rare spawns are insanly rare. But it is the bone chip factory...
