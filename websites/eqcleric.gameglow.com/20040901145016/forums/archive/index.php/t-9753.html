Granted I have only gotten my enchanter up to level 30, but I started to feel exactly the same way you were feeling.

If I wanted to do anything with my enchanter (except sit around the aviak tree for another five levels), I had to group up with more than just my ranger partner and deal with many different people every day, some who had absolutely no clue how to make the most of their enchanter. (Or even any other class, for that matter...some people just *are* clueless)

There are fun aspects of that kind of grouping. Meet lots of different people and make new friends. I really enjoyed those aspects of pick-up grouping.

But I just don't want to do it all the time!

And oftentimes, my partner and I would go into Cazic (our newest hunting ground) and at 4am EST, there were a total of eight people in the dungeon, including us, and no one wanted to group or they were already in a group, or something like that.

So what do we do now? Even if we could find something to solo,that wouldn't rip us to pieces in no time flat (just having ranger healing *sucks*) experience goes soooooo slow for rangers and enchanters, that we could kill something for five hours straight, maybe gain a half bubble or so if we are lucky, and then lose it all the very next moment in a death by uber-mob sneaking up while starting at the stupid book. Extremely frustrating. It's a wonder we have even made it to 30, much less 50.

I finally broke down and just made a newbie to play during times when we can't find a good group or we just can't group, and I've been having a lot of fun.

When we want to deal with the hassles of pick-up grouping, and the deaths and almost-deaths and never-ending overexcitement that comes with it, we can log in our other characters and fight in Cazic.

When we want to do things on our own, we play our newbies (and we've been playing these newbies a *lot* recently!) I started a cleric and she had started a newbie necro a while ago but never really played, so pulled the necro back out again.

It seems that clerics can solo a lot better than enchanters (and was what had inspired my question about solo clerics on the general board) and necros are supposed to be solo machines.

Unless Verant really nerfs both our classes, we should be able to group or solo with at least one of our characters anytime we please. Group goes bad? No problem, we are not stuck sitting around with nothing to do after hitting that disband button. We can go fight something on our own.

I will get my enchanter to level 50 eventually, but sometimes we just need to stop and do something different, when the other things we are doing become too frustrating.

It seems like you have already made a good start. You still like your cleric, but you aren't being a "traitor" playing something else along with the cleric. And you will eventually get to level 50 if that's what you want. Might not be fast, but you'll get there.

Just depends on what you want to get out of the game.
