Seb Scale Leggings are good too. Go for 2-3k on my server. 16ac 4wis if I remember correctly. Kromif Militia Leggings are similar (Thrug quest in Velious). Gladiator Chain Leggings (drops in Arena in Kael) are actually better than EM Legs if you have too much Wisdom.

I'm an AC Freak (and will be until the day comes that I stop tanking so darn much!), so I'd say Paw (Fire For Fun), Med Totem, Donals Helm, and then find other equipment to swap out a good 10 points of Wisdom or so

The different between 185 Wisdom and 200 Wisdom in a pickup group is rarely noticable. Usually you can get Insight/Enlightenment anyway.

My opinion is, and always will be.. 200 wis, then all AC. These days, 200 wis is pretty easy to attain, and from there it just comes down to where you can best lose wis to gain ac.

I like to use Allakhazam's site for reference, since you can sort each slot by AC, then figure out what is/isn't easily attainable in some of those odd slots.
