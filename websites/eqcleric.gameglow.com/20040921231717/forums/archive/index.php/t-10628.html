I went there yesterday at level 34 because I thought I'd upchuck if I killed one more pickclaw goblin in High Keep.

I made the last 2 bubbles of level 34 in a little over 3 hours. This is a fast furious action place. I was in a full group that was tank heavy and we were killing only lightweight mobs (Greater Kobolds and Greater Kobold Shamans). It is a LOT of fun if (a) you are not the type to panic (since stuff that kills level 50's sometimes makes its way to the zoneline (b) you are not claustrophobic (because lord there is no maneuver space in there whatsoever particularly at the zone line; and (c) you are good at managing your mana or persuading bards or enchanters to hunt with you.

I'm planning to stay for a while, I think. At least until I get up enough courage to try to visit Mistmoore again LOL.
