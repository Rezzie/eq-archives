Heh, the tug-o-war was funny. Mine isn't quite that elaborate, but none the less amusing.

We were in Karnor's at T2, and someone trained us. Well, being all good friends in RL we usually are ready for these kind of things as we grouo together quite often.

The chanter we had does a great job at mezzing everything, about 7 mobs to be precise. So we get somewhat relaxed. Me, the cleric, has 80 mana so we figure we are good. Had some nice tanks in the group to taunt and do the damage, not to mention a 55 monk that is very experieced in all of Karnor's.

We have it under control until the chanter starts getting hit. The cleric i am, predict when mez starts to break and i cast a celes heal on the chanter to keep him alive long enough to mez again, if needed i cast a quick remedy on him if he is being beaten on too hard to help with the celes healing.

At this point, our chanter is at 3 1/2 bubs of life with a celes heal on him. He begins to regen when we discover he is nowhere to be found.

A few seconds go by, and the chanter comes running back up to the group from down below. "I was knocked off" he says. No biggie, these things happen.

We kill 3 more mobs, one breaks and the chanter is getting beat on again. Once again i cast celes heal, and nothing happens. I cast remedy and nothing happens, his life does not move.

This boggles me as i do not know why i can cast on him and him not get life back.

We all have to zone as the rest of the mobs break Dazzle and commence to tear into us, me being the target seeing how is the chanter is not around.

We all jump off the tower and swim for our lives, and we make it to the zone with out any deaths.

I ask the chanter what happened? He replies "I was knocked off 2 more times and i had to run back to T2"

Well no wonder we had 7 or so mobs on us. He brought some friends everytime he came, not to mention when i tried to heal him, he was in the water, which not only wasted my mana, but it didn't heal him even though it was casted on him.

It was funny when i thought about it. Seeing a chanter fall off of a ledge, run back up to crowd control, fall off again, then repeat several times.

Reminded me of something off of Monty Python :P

Sorry if this was too long, I hope there is some humor in this for someone besides me.
