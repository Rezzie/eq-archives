From my distant memories hunting the spec caves in the Feerot.

At around 46-47 you can start to pull from the cave. Pulling the front spec will bring 1-3 specs depending on the level of the specs that are spawned there. 48+ you can pull singles almost every time.

Here's how I did levels 48 and 49. You can pull singles at this point, but alot of them are green. Luckily, the tree spawn is so popular that there are usually extra people there wanting to hunt it. Or maybe you have a necro there that can handle extra spawns. Go into the cave and pull the greens for these people. Kill the blues yourself. Everyone is happy ;)

Level 45 and under, with no help, I'd say you are pretty much stuck with the tree spawn, however, there is a camp of skeletons in the Dreadlands that have the same level and HP range as spectres and can be pulled solo...

I find that with the help of a druid (snare) the two of us can get great xp in oasis and even handle 3 or more specs.

For instance assume there are 3 specters, here is what we do:

1) The druid SNARES 2 of the 3, and casts a DD on the 3rd one. (now we have 2 slow movers and 1 fast mover)
2) Soon as #3 is within mele range he snares it and I fear it.
3) The druid then keeps attacking # 3 (feared and snared) and I watch his health.
4) The other 2 snared specs will arrive, I fear them both.
5) Once #3 is dead, the druid will switch to one of the other 2. Im still doing nothing but keeping them feared and healing druid if needed.

Doing this we can easily handle it provided they stay snared and I keep them feared.

If im solo I will zone if I pull 3. If I pull 2, I fear one and try to keep other 1 rooted while I nuke it till dead then switch to root/nuke the second one. I tried solo fear kiting but specters move too dang fast when feared without SNARE on them. So IMO root/shoot works better.
