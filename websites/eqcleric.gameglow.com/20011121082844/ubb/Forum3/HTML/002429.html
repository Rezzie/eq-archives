Some ideas for you...

1) Skyshrine... You have guardian arms.. you should want guardian leggings, guardian helmet, and guardian breastplate.... all three are very nice pieces and upgrades to your existing equipment... The leggings in particular because they have wisdom +5mr +15fr +15cr... The BP adds a few AC and some other useful stats (+7str +resists)....... Additionally.. if you can get a single group with a good tank, monk, druid, enchanter, and a rogue... all of around your level.. work your way past the CY and hunt everything but the big 3 cubes up to the 1st teleport pad. The equipment upgrades are HUGE (12ac 8wis cloak, 7wis 25mana earring, 13ac 6wis helm) and the others will want equipment from there too. Finding a monk who wants oiled greaves or a mantle of absorbtion, a rogue who wants a 12/23 peircer, a tank who wants an 11/21 or 13/23 weapon, or an int caster whose drops number too many shouldn't be hard to find. At 54 you will still get exp up there too.

2) Try to get in the rares group in Velks Lab.. great exp at your level and one of the drops is a 20ac 9wis shield.. big upgrade over your ToV.

3) A single balanced group will do well in chardok and you can get a Sarnak Battleshield which is also very nice

4) Buy a pair of coldain skin gloves.. 12ac 6wis +mana +cr

5) get your wis up enough to drop that other 7wis ring and get yourself a pair of 6ac 65hp rings and a pair of 5ac 30hp 30mana rings.. use whichever the situation calls for

6) Boots of the Huntsman are great, but your on dorf faction so your not likely to get them.. they drop off of Grand Hunstman Darryl in Icewell and are 15ac 5wis 5ing 5cr

those are some easy single group ideas for you... of course if you can get in on a Hate raid for some EM armor all the better too!

Hope that helps
