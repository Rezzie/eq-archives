Many of the names you suggested, and most EQ surnames, are either "nounverber" or "adjectivenoun".

You might want to choose a common noun or verb, and then vary the other part of the name.

So you could be Sweetbreeze and he could be Sweetsinger, or Lifebringer and Songbringer, and so on.

Is there a word you both share, that you could work with for surnames of this variety?

Once you have such a word, either play with it in English or grab some English-other dictionaries and see what you get in translation.
