You know, it's kind of a pity I did not know about this island earlier. It would have been a cool place to gain some xps.

The Aviaks are not aggro unless you are already fighting, and there are always numerous ones to pull.

Here is the range as near as I can figure. (I might be WAY off here, but it's the best I can do. When you're 34, they are all green.)

Aviak Starlings: Level 12 to 14ish? About 240 hit points.

Aviak Turacos: 15 to 17ish?

Aviak Tanagers: 18 to 20ish?

Avaik Quetzels: 21 to 25ish? (at 34, the very highest lvl Quetzels would still give xp, a very very insignificant amount)

There are no other mobs on the island of any kind, no snakes, no bats, nothing.

There are wanderers that move around the island all alone, so it's easy to pull. Little chance of getting 3 birds at once.

As for loots, the occasional Aviak will have a rusty, sometimes a bronze weapon. (Every Aviak I killed with a mace had a bronze one, not rusty. Swords were mostly rusty.)

Some Aviaks just had a few copper, right up to 6 gold per Aviak, sometimes the Quetzels would have up to 3 plat or so.

Hope this helps.
