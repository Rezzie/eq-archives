Well I am getting discouraged about playing everquest. I have made level 40 but all the excitement appears to be gone until I reach my mid to high 40's when I can do dragon and planes raids. Here is what I mean.

First: It seems that my only choices for hunting are Sol B, Lower Guk, Orge Guard Hunting, Kithcor Woods, or SG/Specter hunting.

a. Lower Guk. There are so many people camping the place that I can move about in almost total safety. When I get into a group I have the joy of waiting 30 minutes for spawns we kill the 3-5 spawns then wait some more. ASS/Supplier (nice loot, not many spawns) (I wont go into the others)

b. Sol B. I got into the King camp and stayed for 4 hours. No challenge at all. Stand cast a few heals, sit med, melee/stun a bit. No one in any danger at all. The bats/spiders are okay with a proper group.

b. Kithcor woods. Great hunting at night followed by 1 hour of surfing the web on my other computer until Everquest Night comes again.

c. Orge Guard Hunting. Very simular to Sol B. Nice exp, no excitement no danger at all.

d. SG Hunting. Not bad when you can get a group going.

I am probably just burning out on Everquest, but I have started playing Demise and that feels a whole lot better. Granted it does not have the world of Norrath but at least it's exciting. Is there a future? Or just more camping?

Yep, servers sure are crowded. ***IF*** you just want to level through 40 - 45 e-mail me and I'll let you know about a few spots that are just GREAT XP at this level. Of course, powerleveling is damned boring too, but it is a quick way to get through this stage to the more exciting moments.

Other than that, I can only suggest trying places off the well-beaten track. We got a lot of experience in LGuk, but didn't do it on the dead side at all (just too crowded). Instead we worked:

-&gt;Elder Room (Minos, drops the nice Chrysoberyl Talisman)
-&gt;Patriarch Room (Azure sleeves, Ebony Blade)
-&gt;Crusader/Priest, GREAT XP here, need a good high-30s, low 40s group (prob better w/ low 40s). Drops Silver Plated Legs (in high demand oddly enough, they arent that special), the Runed Falchion, and the Runed Mithril Tunic (you know, that druid tunic).
-&gt;Noble/Shaman area - further down there are some shamans and the noble (I think). Nothing special, but stuff to kill.
-&gt;Elemental/Herbalist/King area -- The big boys of the live side, but again, can be fun.
-&gt;We wandered one night all through the live side just killing whatever we saw. Was fun http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif.

Never did worth a crap in SolB.

Spending a lot of time in OOT and Kedge now, but those places require pre-set groups for the most part.

Regardless, good luck,
