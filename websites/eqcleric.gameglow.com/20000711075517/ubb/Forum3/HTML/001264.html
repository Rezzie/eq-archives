With Naltron, Shield of Words, Resolution, and Resist Magic/Poison/Disease up a 50 cleric should be just fine soloing the Live Grimrot assuming he had a damage shield potion from a shaman and just Shealed himself as needed in the fight.

Use a SoW potion by all means but don't depend on it since Grimrot might land a lucky Darkness spell that will slow you down and cancel SoW. So keep Gate up if you do decide to try him since you can probably get Gate off if things go badly.

With my 41 ranger who has 802 AC and 1334 hp self-buffed I got the Live Grimrot to about 60% health with just melee and my 8pt damage shield before I zoned with 40% health (can't risk staying too long since he might land a lucky Fear spell). Did this one day to save a low level Paladin who agro'd Grimrot, ate the harm touch and then bolted for zone with me chasing behind to taunt Grimrot off the poor 20's something Paladin.

As a fully buffed 50 cleric (with no planar gear at all) I have 832 AC, 198 wis, and 1800hp. More than enough to get off Sheals as I let my pitiful melee and the shaman damage shield potion do their work. A few Sheals would be all that's needed.
