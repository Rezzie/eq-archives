Go to Karnors Castle in the Dreadlands. You are a bit low for there, especially since you are naked (you could probably heal there at level 44th if you were geared up).

However, there is a full set of armor you can wear there called Jarsath Scale. It's not bad at all for your level. Since it is NO DROP, it rots ALL THE TIME, just ask in /OOC every 10 minutes or so that you are naked and looking to loot Jarsath.

Jarsath Scale Boots +10 AC +5 Sta +5 Agi +0.80 Wt
Jarsath Scale Chestplate +20 AC +5 Dex +10 HP +0.80 Wt
Jarsath Scale Gauntlets +10 AC +20 HP +20 Mana +0.80 Wt
Jarsath Scale Helm +10 AC +2 Wis +2 Int +5 vsMg +0.40 Wt
Jarsath Scale Leggings +15 AC +5 Wis +5 Int +0.80 Wt
Jarsath Scale Vambraces +10 AC +5 Str +5 Dex +0.80 Wt
Jarsath Scale Wrist Guards +8 AC +4 Wis +4 Cha +0.80 Wt

Also Supple Scale Arm Bands drop there (wrist item), they are good too and also No Drop, you might be able to loot one.

Supple Scale Armband +3 AC +5 Wis +5 Int +10 vsFire +25 Mana +0.10 Wt Effect: Extended Range III

Other then that, ask your cousins to help you out buying with some gear like: +7 WIS rings, a Velium Ruby Veil, Adamantite Eppauletts, and a Charred Guarding Shield.

I have no idea what to do about your Primary slot, there isn't a lot of choices in your level range.

There are various low level items that you can farm for the tradeskillers in your community and sell for decent amounts of plat.

A couple examples:
Iron Oxide
Leather Padding
Various spider silks or pelts
Temper ingrediants

You could try checking your server's board on eqtraders and see if there is anything people are looking to buy in your community.

Having some plat to help buy basic equipment will help. You may even be able to make some deals for tradeskillers where you collect them some items and they'll help you out with some gear.

I know there have been times I would pay near anything for certain items that dropped from low level mobs I just didn't have time to farm.

2 words.

Runnyeye Citadel.

Farming the upper mobs can net you quite a fair amount of usable equipment.

From there, get enough gear to move down and kill the mid level ones for the black alloy stuff.

In a night, as a solo cleric, should be enough coin and etc to get a good start.

Not to mention money making tradeskills, if you happen to have worked on those on your old server.

Fishroll making/selling comes to mind.

and as mindnumbing as it is, fishing also comes to mind... specifically the reagents for making those tradekill made idols.

Farming spider silks to sell in your tradezone is another great idea.

Trust me, just get killing SOMTEHING and you'll make money and gear in no time.
