I have one (no plans to sell it) we got off the giants in frontier mountain, and in poking around for another (you can only kill so many thousands before you need to move on), the price range appears to be around 2k on Karana. (some ask 3k, but my time ain't THAT valuable)

In the low to mid 20's (earlier the bigger the group), you can start hunting the forest giants in Warslik's Woods. Their village is a great hunting spot. And they drop the hammers and swords that sell for 30pp to vendors like their bigger cousins do. In the middle hut, a giant called A Forest Giant spawns sometimes. Once in a while he drops a forest loop. I'm told the evergreens also have a chance of dropping it, but haven't heard of it happening since the loot-table nerf a couple weeks after RoK was out.

They seem A LOT more common in frontier mountains, which your group can move to in the upper 20's. In one night two of them dropped for our group, tho we were probably real lucky.
