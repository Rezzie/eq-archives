The best spot to get Corroded Plate pieces is the Arena area of Kael. I have heard that it is POSSIBLE to get a corroded piece to drop at the zone but the chance of it happening is infinitely small. Personally I’ve never seen a piece drop off at the zone and I don’t believe it can myself. I’m pretty certain that the pieces only drop off of Giants that are level 51 and above.

Anyway, the best spot is the Arena. If you can field two good groups 55+ or three good groups 50+, it’s not too bad once you figure out how the Arena spawns work. My guild does Kael raids often and we’re able to keep the entire area clear. Drop rates can be extremely good or horrible. One day we hunted the Arena for 8 hours and only got two pieces to drop. Another day we only hunted for about 3 hours and ended up with ten pieces of Armor. Veterans have the best drop rates on armor pieces and they are usually level 57, Summon, Rampage, and Enrage. Adjutants also have a very nice drop rate as well and are typicall level 53ish Cleric types that can Complete Heal and Gate.

Kael is designed so that smaller guilds that are level 50+ can do it and get Thurgadin Armor. It still requires a couple of solid groups but the place isn’t that difficult.

Actually, as long as your Tank is 57+ (ie, can Taunt Veterans) then you can keep the upper Arena area (not the Guard/Senior Guard area) with one high level group.

2 groups can easily do it. You can get solo pulls almost every time once you learn the pathing (Hint: When going up the back ramps to the upper area around the arena, *charge* the first giant on the right - if you pull, you'll get 2 thanks to poor pathing).

Armor basically drops off any giant that can See Invis - so you can fight either below the "south see invis line" (this is near Toramx) or above the "north see invis line" (this is near the Arena)
