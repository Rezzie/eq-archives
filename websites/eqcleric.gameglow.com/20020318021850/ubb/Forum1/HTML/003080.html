There was a time when I never charged for resurrections and never turned anyone down who was reasonable about the request. Because I liked the new titles I went out of /roleplay for a week and started to get more and more abusive requests for resurrections from people I don't know so I've changed my policies a bit (perhaps the extremity of experience loss in the level 50+ hell levels has brought out the worst in everyone).

Initially I would be willing to travel to the corpse to do the resurrection. After getting burned several times by people saying "Oh I was resurrected already" when I arrived I decided against travelling to corpses except for friends.

I still would prefer not to charge for my services however there are a few circumstances I will accept money. My first case I will accept money is when I am in a group and the effort will actually put the group out...I ask them if they want to make some quick cash and when I receive payment I split it amongst all group members (Imagine the groups eyes when each person got 100pp from me resssing someone I couldn't stand - yes I asked for 100pp for each group member he was putting out thinking he would just say no thanks and he actually agreed). The other case is when the person insists that I take payment, which admittedly happens about 1 in 4 cases it seems.

While soloing I am also reluctant to do resurrections, simply because that mana spent will add another 3 or 4 minutes of boredom while I watch that little blue bar move. The only exception is if someone can provide mana (either through themselves or a friend)...many people know a necromancer, enchanter, or bard who is willing to help.

For me it has never been about making money and still isn't. But I also agree with many posters who feel their mana is for their party first. I never do a resurrection when I am in a party without first asking if they would mind. Sometimes it isn't an issue since I am in a party where my mana never drops below 60% (fear kiting parties come to mind).

Oh and here is my favorite recent experience with a resurrection. An enchanter sent me a tell requesting a resurrection. I said I would do it if she would cast clarity on me when she had enough mana. She responded "No it's alright then." Hmmmm she wanted me to spend 700 mana on her and couldn't be bothered to spend 75 mana on me.
