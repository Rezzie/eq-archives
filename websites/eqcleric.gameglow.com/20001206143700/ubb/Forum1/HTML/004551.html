And shut up and take a complement, okay? :-) Like I said, having reduced downtime is NOT due to having more mana, it's due to knowing WHEN you should spend mana. Look at some numbers:

Cleric A has a manapool of 2500
Cleric B has a manapool of 2000

Over the course of 30 minutes of fighting, they spend 20 minutes of that medding. Assuming you get roughly 300mana/minute medding,

Cleric A has an effective mana pool of 8500
Cleric B has an effective mana pool of 8000
A difference of about 6%

Over the course of 60 minutes of fighting, the spend about 45 minutes medding:

Cleric A has an effective mana pool of 16000
Cleric B has an effective mana pool of 16500
A difference of 3%

And I'm willing to bet that a lot of your camps were WAY longer than one hour (mine are most of the time, for sure), in which case the difference plummets to less than 1%. In other words, the reduced downtime that your groupmates are noticing is not because you have a greater manapool, but because you're a better cleric than the other ones they've grouped with. You do your job effectively and don't waste other people's time by flinging mana about indiscriminately.
