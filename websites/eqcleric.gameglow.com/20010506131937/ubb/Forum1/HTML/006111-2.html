"2) I can't buff members of my own group that i'm in charge of anymore. I've learned that the lvl 60 cleric in my guild will now buff who he wants in any party in any guild raid with full approval of anyone around due to the magic bullet that can do one spell for 3 of my buffs."

1. On a raid with a larger guild on my server (*waves at Omani*) I was "reduced" to just healing and buffs. I was 55th, and one of the clerics had a rez stick. The simple fact of the matter is, when I was 55th, I had lots of people turn me down, some quite rudely, because I didn't have the 56th rez. I had clerics overwriting my Marzins with theirs, etc. It happens. It's a little bit of a blow to the ego. You level up, and the problems evaporate.

I agree with the poster who said people who would not ever settle for a 49th rez when only one person was 56th rez-capable were selfish weiners. Sometimes you have to make that *horrible* 6% sacrifice in order to keep a raid going and get it back on track.

Anyway, 'nuff said. Maybe you need a break for a bit. Helped me a lot, I was getting pretty bitter Good luck with it. Try talking with folks - and accept that big raids like that are going to be different than single-group dungeon crawls.

getting in my 2cents worth.

Ok, cos I'm not lvl 50-some yet, and i'm not exactly sure how it works at that lvl yet, this is just based on what i've been seeing so far as i have gone through the life of a cleric.

First, stop bashing on the poor guy. Being in a relatively small guild with VERY FEW clerics, I, thankfully, don't have to deal with this stuff. In fact, my guildies love it when i come on cos it opens up so many places for them to hunt. Oh, sure, they do just fine without me, but I think they rest a little easier when I'm on and I certainly feel the love, even if it's "Nala, ya busy?" and shortly followed by a "one of our group members died in [fill in]. Can you come and res?" It's a nice feeling as opposed to sitting on your bum watching others do what you've always considered to be "your job." I'm greedy, I know. But we all like to feel important.

As a suggestion, I know this works at "lower" lvls... i don't know how many higher lvl clerics yer grouping/raiding with, but what about buffing the non-tank, non-high hate generators? Personally, I can completely understand giving my tanks and ench only the very best. They get the best buffs the group can afford because I know they'll be taking damage or are at a high risk of taking damage. The rest of us, however, who tend not to generate as much hate and won't be agroed unless something goes horrifically wrong, gets the "lesser" buffs. So... basically what I mean is that the enchanter, warrior/main tank/main hate generator, and maybe even the cleric gets the lvl 60 or whatever highest cleric buffs you got. And the lower lvl clerics take everyone else. I prefer to keep everyone buffed in one sense or another, and knowing that buffing sucks a ton of mana, splitting the buffing would work. Also, you'll feel a lot better that you're doing something n' contributing rather than sitting on your ass doing nothing.

Just my 2 cp... I don't know if it works at higher lvls and on big raids, but... if you can split the responsibilities, why not? No sense in having someone sit around at FM while the other goes oom on buffs.
