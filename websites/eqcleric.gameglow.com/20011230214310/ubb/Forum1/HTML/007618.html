I'm 48 too and you have about the same gear that I do. A few suggestions:

Arms: I have Ry'Gorr - the 11 AC is a bit nicer than the Ulthork they replaced. But it does suck to loose that wis.

Back: If you have the time to do it, the Aegis of Life quest will yield you a very nice back item. The best way to do this is to simply bind at the skel spot and do 15-20 minute camps at the start and end of your gaming day. If you're going up to the moon this is a fairly easy run from the Wiz spire.

Secondary: Grab a level 45 warrior from you guild. Take him to Kaesora. Buff him to death. Let him run around killing spiders till he's 47 (don't group with him so he gets all the xps). By the time he's 47 you should have a STrathbone shield +19 ac +8 wis.

Ranged: Either a) go to ToFS and get a teacher's syllabus or b) do the third prayer shawl quest. The ToFS way is much faster, but if you're going to go for the 7th shawl anyway, might as well get the nice thurgian seal thingy.

Feet: Go xp grind in KC - get donal's boots.

Arms: see feet
