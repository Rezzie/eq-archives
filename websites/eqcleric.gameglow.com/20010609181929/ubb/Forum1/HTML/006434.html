I have seen Black Marble drop in Dragon Negropolis off the Chetari rats. We usually go with a full group of lvl 60's, or 2 groups under lvl 60. A lvl 60 puller really really helps, as does a lvl 60 enchanter, umm as does a lvl 60 warrior (some of the rats enrage). It is pretty good experience here, but keep in mind that you are gonna die and get summoned most likely. =) The Chetari Dustxxxxx rats are unstunnable and will gate and summon you deep in to the rat area.
