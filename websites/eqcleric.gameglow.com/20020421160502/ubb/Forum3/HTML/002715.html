Verant pretty much knows that items with charges that are NOT no drop can be recharged. Actually with a pvp discussion sometime ago some verant employee said something to the degree they were comfortable with the high cost of recharging pumice/golem wands as a form of balance.

****SPOILER***


Anyway the way to recharge is simple. You first find a vendor with a space to sell stuff to. You put a fully charged on a mule...sell fully charged first. Than sell your empty one. Than log off your character and log your mule back on and buy back fully charged mask. Than log on your main character and buy back the recharged mask. Buying the mask back is like 1pp if that each time. The thing is you need to log your character...since when he sold the Lore item, the game wont let him buy it back until he relogs.
