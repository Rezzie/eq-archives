Increasingly I see bronze (and indeed a lot of other non-stat armor) going for 1pp per AC point. While that's less efficient for the BP it might help you in getting better prices.

Be sure to check the dwarf vendor on the island in Cauldron as players often sell bronze to him (you will probably pay him more than to another player, however).

Another option (if you have Kunark, that is) is to hop on the boat and ride to FV and /auction for bronze and ruined scale (it's equivalent to chainmail). Both drop commonly and most people are willing to sell them cheaply (or give them away since they only fetch a few pp at vendors)

Other places with bronze drops: Lguk (if you're not KOS on live side then you should be ok), SolA/B, Dreadlands (unsafe at 11), Karnor's (unsafe at 11)

Bottom line is that the bottom has fallen out of the bronze market pretty much.

Hope that helps
