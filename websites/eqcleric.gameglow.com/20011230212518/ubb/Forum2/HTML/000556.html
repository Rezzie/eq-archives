Two Armor of the Risen spell drops happened during our hunt of The Temple. I think we had about 14 spell drops all together.. maybe more. We cleared most of the first floor and cleared more than half of the second one, too.

Many of these mobs hit for mid to high 100's. There were ALL blue and higher to me (60) and good experience, too. The battles here remind me of the Lower Spider fights in Velkators.. same kinds of mob HPs and attack strengths. The difference here is that the snakemen Warlocks will Gravity Flux as well as deal some serious AE ice damage. Resist Cold/Magic is mandatory in here. There are some healers but not nearly as many as Velks. Their magical resistance is about normal, leaning towards above-normal. The deeper Shissars HT for upwards of 900+, so put a Rune on your puller.

The high level spells (54 to 58) were dropping about one in five mobs once you get a little deeper than the initial large room. There were ranged slot items which were VERY common drops - they had decent stats but nothing to drool over. Other than the Shissar Fangs which dropped like rain (Range slot item, all/all, Damage: 11 Delay: 15 Range: 170 Str: +5 Sta: +3 Agi: +5 Dex: +5 Poison Resist: +5) there were no other weapon/armor drops.

Our group was here with 16 people, ranging from 54 to 60. We only suffered the occasional puller deaths and hunted for almost 3 hours. Spawn times are fairly fast, so find a good spot and be ready for pops.
