Posted by SGoldenheal at 20:31 PM

>>>As any seasoned healer will tell you, the F1-F8 keys are a neccessity. Targeting your players with these keys are a must. Since we can heal pets i was wondering if it were possible for clerics to hit the Fkeys twice to target a mage's(or any pet class)pet. I also play a magician and i am able to target myself with F1 and then press F1 again and it targets my pet. I dont think this is to unreasonable a request. It would just make it alot easier to monitor the health of a pet in a group situation. =)
