I went into the crypt of Dalnir for the first time yesterday - and at 35 the hunting there was phenominal. Go to the second level and hunt the coerced, Kly, nibblers, and Goo. Some critters will aggro on you - and others will leave you be.

Lots of good drops down there, plus with a bigger group (40+ I am told) you can continue down to the third level and tackle the KLY himself for the Necklace of Superiority.
