Something for the younger clerics here...

There's a little known clerical problem called the "Rezz0rs Curse".

This curse makes it so that anytime you hit a rezzing level (39, 49, 56) and scribe your new rez, you *will* be killed. You'll lose your level, be unable to scribe your new rez, and there won't be another cleric within 10 zones in any direction.

Then you'll have to rez yourself back with your old rez spell.
