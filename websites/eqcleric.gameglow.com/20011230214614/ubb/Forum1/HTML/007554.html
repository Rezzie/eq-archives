I must disagree with most of the previous posters. While a lot of the skills are rather trivial, some could prove quite useful much before we reach 60. Excluding the class-specific skills, since you need to be 59, there are many of the Healer-specific skills that could greatly benefit a non-uber cleric.

Certainly Mental Clarity is of use to everyone, since unless I really miss it we still do not possess self mana regen. I realize there are many Flowing Thought items out there but why not have an innate abilty to regen, thus giving you more item flexibility? Even the Generic ability regen would be a nice addition, though definitely more for a tank than us. The critical heal thing is decent, at least in terms of healing anyone but a main tank (CH would waste it I imagine). The spell casting improvements could potentially help a lot, depending on how often they really work (only VI knows for sure). I can't remember the others right now, but as I have had some major PC problems lately those have been on my mind more than EQ .

In most cases I'd say 0% to alt XP until you're 60 (understand where you're coming from though, Goryn).
