My cleric's very first hero was Mordryn, high elf cleric of Tunare. This was on the Rathe server some time ago.

I don't know what level Mordryn was when I met him, but it was a LOT higher than me. I was level 5 at the time, hunting in the Butcherblock lowbie area. He buffed me as he passed by, then for some reason stopped to talk. After some discussion and roleplay, he thanked me for roleplaying with him and gifted me, a young dwarf in patchwork with a Cast Iron Mace, with...a Dwarf Ringmail Tunic!

That event was a catalyzing factor for several factors of my RP personality. I happily make fun of elves and their inability to drink or carry the weight of their armor, but at the same time I am very appreciative and respectful of the followers of Tunare. This is reflected in my faction choices...I'm Amiably or better in most of Kelethin and Felwithe, and also with the Tunarean Court. I may make fun of the treehuggers, but I like them nonetheless.

And as for that Dwarf Ringmail Tunic, it served me well for several levels, and then I passed it on to another young cleric in the same manner that it was given to me...for free, with buffs, as a random act of RP kindness. I don't think you can get much more heroic than that.
