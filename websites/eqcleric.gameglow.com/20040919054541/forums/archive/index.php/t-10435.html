You can solo Legionaires, Oracles and Emissaries while waiting for Emporer Crush, the Warlord and Dvinn to spawn.

The throneroom in the castle is perfect 14 to 18 hunting grounds.

Be careful to keep track of who else in in the room though. If the crowd thins out you should go stand on one of the windowsills so that you can make a quick exit if repop happens all at once.
