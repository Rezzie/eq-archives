Oi Oi Oi, Hail and well met goodly folks, and them not so goodly.
As we all are clerics of one deity or another here In Norrath, I seek yer aide.
How ye ask? lemme tell ye what's a buggin me... Someone noted before that the Temple o'Ro, them defilers of the elven home on Antonica, are seeking power by sending clerics of all faiths running about Norrath seeking hard to find items, and pushing the player economy into the ground(for us clerics that is)by making us purchase platinum bars to be twice enchanted to make some nifty golden armor.
Well I fer one have only sought the Cadecus of Sacrament, a mighty gobber smasher it is indeed. No other armor from them defilers shall I seek. A Dwarf's Promise on that.
Why? Tis not a Brell blessed armor, nor tis it beneficial to me people, or dem cousins of 'ers dah gnomies.
So without quests to do in this hidden temple to the Fire God what can I do until I earn the skill to penetrate the planes?
Well I sought me high Priestess Galetia to ask her about some quests...
Dah Soil of Underfoot...okay I can do that...What? No Drop/Lore? What's Fer me asks me guildmates...A Paladin quest for the Chalice and candle of bravery, to earn Dru Razbind's Cloak of underfoot back fer him so he may return to kaladim in honor and not shame...okay..what no drop? Give it to Dru...Thanks- here's me sword , yer a fine paladin...Paladin? Hoi I'm a Cleric...wait..no drop? cannae give de sword to me brother dah paladin or make a worthy quest fer guildmates...
Does she have any other tasks fer a Cleric of Brell? Silence...
So I goes and asks dah other trainers...skunk glands ? Yer Kidding right? Skunks is rare in BB, and dem glands even rarer, I knows, I hunted skunks with me mates to see if we could trick him into revealing a more secrative or daunting task... 16 glands and not smelling to great either, he gives me four spells of the first circle... . Nothing else forthcoming...
Okay, I remember there's a trainer tucked behind the altar to the right...I talk to him, Silence...Okay matey what gives...I try giving him everything from bone chips to dwarven ale , silence, not even a burp.
Bored I deck him out in tattered-patchwork armor earned again by doing the kill the undead quest in dah PALADIN GUILD...okay dey serve Brell, but what am I supposed to do?
What I am asking here is for all the Clerics of Norrath's Pantheon to talk to their leaders and see what tasks their leaders have fer them. As for dwarves, if'n yer a cleric, ye got nothing to do fer Brell, but heal yer mates, imbibe Holy Waters, and be an Honorable Dwarf.
As tha' is second nature to this Champion of Brell, I seek more to do.
Quests of me Own God, Brell Serilis, not some other god, though I am akin to aide tha' wily woods wench tha runs about scantily in Faydwer Forests...but tha's another story.
Fer now I am not happy with the number of dwarven rogue, warrior and paladin quests I have to do to keep busy, aye they do give a sliver of experience, but nothing useful to a cleric of Brell.

(OOC- Please list what deity ye follow and what and who gives quests in your temple and if it is for a cleric or instead another class to benefit from, I have a feeling that the Deity of choice to a character has no play whatsoever in EQ past the character creation and the faction ye earn at that point. It'd be nice to have a precise list of actual quests and rewards that you get from all temples in Norrath, that way we can easily address Verrant and see if they will address this issue ever.)
(OOC #2- I am the Dwarf that quested and successfully got Dwarven Ale returned to Kaladim this past summer, finding and defeating the smugglers in HPH and QEYNOS and FREEPORT, now only a few Gypsies still trade this item outside Kaladim Proper- there was to be more to this GM Quest, but alas Brathas is no longer with Verrant and I am left with a half completed GM quest and a bad taste in me mouth...)
