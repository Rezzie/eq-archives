How well have you explored the FV zone? I think most people underestimate this zone (even Allakhazam's lists it as capping at 35 which is quite incorrect).

First off, there are the Drachnids near the bridge to FV. You can solo them quite easily and you have the guards nearby. About a third still conned blue at 40.

Next is the Drolvarg temple. You'll probably need a tank or at least a decent caster group because these guys can hit hard but they are still doable without a tank IF you can keep the pulls down. (Harmony/Calm pulls work well). Again, about a third conned blue at 40.

Outside of FV... try the entrance to FV in the Dreadlands. Lots of Drachnids out there too. Also an occasional drolvarg. And the zone is close by.

The last suggestion is by far the most dangerous and I don't recommend you try it without JBoots or SoW. The City of Mists is a semi SolB-type zone but it's all undead Lots of blue undead critters. The only problem is finding a safe spot from the trains AND someplace where other stuff won't agro. Might want to try turning right after passing the gate.

The only problem is getting there. Swamp of No Hope is relatively safe as is Trakanon's Teeth. The Emerald Jungle is a nightmare, tho. Everything is blue and about half the stuff can see invis. Your best bet is to use your SoW/JBoots to run past it all. Head North and then turn East(right) at the wall.

Hope that helps.

Let me make some other suggestions, Meddik (the ones just before me are quite good as well).

1. Frontier Mountains. At your level, EVERYTHING (well, mostly) in this zone should be blue. You can probably solo the burynai digmasters if you're careful, but you shouldn't have TOO much of a time finding a group -- proably best to announce where you're going in FV and continue shouting through the lake. seriously, two people works wonders in here.

2. Burning woods -- once you get a higher group (35's or so), you can go to burning woods and kill LOTS of 37 or 38th level things. not great exp, but loads of fun. just wait until you kill your first Wurm.

3. The Overthere -- specifically, at the FM zone line. shouldn't ahve too hard of a time finding a partner or a small group, lots of stuff to kill that out to be blue to red here.

Those are the three places I've been hanging out recently. About to try moving farther east today, will post if I find worthwhile places.
