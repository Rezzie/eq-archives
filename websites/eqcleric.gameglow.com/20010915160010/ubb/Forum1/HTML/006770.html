I posted this on my guild's message board and it generated a thread that was, well, lively to say the least.

Anyway, I lurk here a ton but don't contribute all that much so hopefully this will help some people, especially ones in guilds like mine that are just starting to get mean.

-------------------

I've been getting a lot of tells recently after we kill something, saying "can I please get your rez, I just dinged, or am about to ding, etc. etc." In a situation like tonight where there were only 4 or 5 people needing rezzes to do, and we're in a safe area like that spot in burning woods, I'll happily do all of them.

But there are gonna be situations where it's not safe, and there's more than 4-5 people who need to be rezzed, and we need a fighting force ready to take care of wanderers. With that in mind I want to run some numbers for you. The math impaired can skip to the end of this post, I'll summarize there.

Let's take for example a level 56 dark elf enchanter (not singling you out Amer, just had to pick something). You need 39,647,959 xp to go from the beginning of that level to level 57. BTW this number is taken from the HackersQuest board, and even Verant has admitted that these numbers are accurate.
http://hackersquest.gomp.ch/html/xp/xpchart.html

Let's also assume worst case, and when you died you lost 2 blue bubbles of xp. For me at 56 it's around 1 blue, but I'll allow a bit more to account for people who are level 55 and are in post-hell level, etc. That would mean that you lost 2 blue bubbles out of 25 needed to level, or 2/25 of a level.

39,647,959 * 2 / 25 = 3,171,836 points of experience lost.

Let's say you get the lowly 90% rez, you would get:
3,171,836 *.90 = 2,854,653 points of experience back.

If you get the badass 56% rez, you'd get:
3,171,836 * .96 = 3,044,963 points of xp back, for a difference of:

3,044,963 - 2,854,653 = 190,310 xp.

If that seems like a lot, I'll put it in perspective. A decent (not even a good) xp group in someplace like Sebilis will give you 20,000 xp per MINUTE. A good one will push 30,000 per minute.

That means that by holding out for the lvl 56 rez you're saving yourself around 10 MINUTES of time in an xp group.

Again, I'm not trying to save myself any hassle. Hell, the level 56 rez is 100 less mana than the level 49 one, so in most circumstances it makes sense for me to cast it, and the clerics who don't have the spell to help heal/buff the incoming people. I just want people to have some perspective, and not hold up a raid waiting for a "better" rez that will save them 10 minutes.
