Might as well start this forum off with a bang, eh? http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

Trak is a poison dragon. The bulk of deaths from Trak are a result from the poison DoT killing people. Abolish poison will take the DoT off, but it's a long cast. Don't even bother with Complete Heal as you won't ahve time to get a cast off.

Make sure you give everyone full HP buffs (including Symbol). Resist Poison and DMF are standard for this raid.

There aren't any real tactics on the cleric end for the rush. Just have people call when poisoned and hit them with Abolish. Celestial Heal can keep them alive now if you have time to get it off.

Make sure people start calling out poisoned after the raid is over. Trakanon is the dragon who keeps killing long after he's dead. Watched 10 people fall down dead after Trak last time.

Enjoy!
