Presented with my view of the situation, I'd like to make a few observations about this "event". If Verant wants to have some GM events, that is fine. I have no problem with this what-so-ever. I myself do not participate in such things myself, and if I had known that it was coming, I would have logged in a different character. This to me is just completely and totally assinine. If there is an "event", then it should be geared to the level of people where the "event" is going to occur. Next, we have the loot. While it took a few groups of 50+ people to come in and finish up this "event", the loot itself was geared to the level of the people that were PKed. Dropped were a Lizard Mantle, a Golden Black Pearl Pendent, and a Jagged Band. I'm sure that these were wisked off to EC to be sold by the 50s that claimed the prizes while there were a number of people that were camping the fort could have used such items.

Worst, and I do mean the WORST of all, we have a GM show up in front of the fort. I forget his name, but his title was "the Oblivious". How is that for appropriate? He asked the people there if they enjoyed the "event". The unfortunate druid that was in my group that was killed 3 times by the Giants, and not by the GMs (thus, xp loss) voiced his disapproval about the whole entire thing, and I made sure that I told him that I did not have a "good time" during the "event". Neither did the vast number of people that I ressed for free back to their camp. The camp which the GMs stole from them all with this meaningless, assinine "event".

Words can not fully express the complete and utter contempt that I have for Verant for this "event" whose only purpose that I can figure was to give an ego boost to the GMs that were involved. And I sincerely hope that I am never again fortunate enough to be a part of a "GM event".
