I hunted in Upper Guk from level 30 to level 34 with a similar level Warrior and Wizard.

For level 30-32, there is a caskroom that spawns a Shin Warrior and a Ton Knight. We cleared that room, then pulled as much as we could. That is well out of the train pathways.

For level 32-34, we pulled from the water edge near the underwater zone and the swimming path to the crocs. We were pulling 12 spawn points at level 33. We did not get trained here either.

The xp was about 15% to 20% per hour and the loot was good since we had a wizard to sell the FS. We once overloaded the wizard with 30 FS weapons. He ported and was stuck, could not walk, very funny to imagine.

At level 33 I can run through upper guk to the first dead zone without being attacked by any frogs. Im not sure that they wouldnt attack if I lingered around but they dont attack when I run. The path that I take is via the scryer room, down the ramp, then head north. From memory I think you take a right turn whenever possible.

There are definately frogs near the live side zone that will attack. Plenty of them actually. You can hunt in upper guk near the live side zone and then zone into lower if things go bad. There are a lot of spawns points in this area so its not well suited for solo.
