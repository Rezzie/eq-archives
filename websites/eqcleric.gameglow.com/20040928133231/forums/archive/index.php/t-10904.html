Its been a long time since this came up, so I thought I would post on it again for the new people to the boards and people who have forgoten.

If you, or people you know, have a problem with getting booted to the black "You have been disconnected" screen, or otherwise lagging horribly once fights start on big raids, here is what you need to do.

Go to whatever directory you have everquest installed to and look for a file named DataRate.txt.

Open DataRate.txt with notepad, or whatever text editor you use.

The default number in the file is 5.0, change it to 7.0 if you have a modem connection, or 9.0 if you have a broadband connection, cable/adsl/t1, etc.

This file determins how many k of data EQ will send to your client at a time. If the number on the right of your lag meeter in EQ goes over the number of K in the file, you will likely get disconnected. 5.0 = 5000, 7.0 = 7000, 9.0 = 9000.

According to Verant, settings of over 9.0 are ignored and treated as 9.0, but I have seen conflicting reports of people having settings as high as 40.0 and actually recieving 30,000+ data packets during mass zonings, etc.

Hope this helps some of you. =)
