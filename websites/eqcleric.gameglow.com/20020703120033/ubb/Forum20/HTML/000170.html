The Grey is a good place to solo. Use the undead dot and root and you can pull nonstop. Course its slower killing with just the dot but no meddn since you do enough of that during the fight. Only thing is Grey is dangerous with Everyone that trains the temple. I always try to fight near the back to avoid them. Snakes are solo pullable a good 3 oranges into 58. But, if you duo with a necro/sk/paladin its amazing. You can clear around the whole temple with a good 58 plus of one of these classes. Now some of the stuff there is light blue but it dies so fast its not even funny. Duo in FG is good to, i like monks cause of their High DPS.

For group fights, CT is obviously the best exp wise and drops. 4c in CT is the most fun. Sewers and TR get pretty boring. You can solo group the Fire/Ice caves in UP. That is alot of fun since its a good dungeon crawl group and decent exp/loot. Another good exp is a dungeon crawl group in Chardok past the graveyard.
