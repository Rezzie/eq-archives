Earings- always a rough area since there is nothing really mid range obtainable between BS electrum --> vindicator, yelinak, PD earings. Fingerbone hoop isn't too bad, and you may want to consider a grim aura earing if you don't have an instant cast buff.

Bracer- if you value HPs>>AC and have SS faction you can get a bracer of hammerfall which has hps and wis (prob useless). Otherwise, Pog or Kael or SS is good since it has both decent ac, HPs and mana

Ring- 6ac, 65 hps unless your on dwarf faction hehe

Face- Mask of war is pretty good for this slot. If you are Allied giant, the lvl 60 rogue in the orb room drops a 3ac 9 sta, some fire/cold mask with ultravision that isn't too bad. Telk in ToV west wing also drops a 2ac 100mana face.

Neck- First brood talisman from zlandicar if you can. Otherwise the WW talismans (7ac, 6 to all) are nice. If you are on CoV, another alternative would be the rygor neck piece which has decent AC and hps.

Gloves- Thunder etched are nice but kinda rare.
