When my Guild goes to Kael, we head up several ramps that lead to the arena. We set up camp against the back wall. Looking straight ahead the Arena will be to your right and further back and to the left is a large Temple looking building. From this spot you should be able to see the Protectors of Zek on your left and kind of in front of the Temple looking structure.

From this spot you can pull a steady stream of giants fairly easily. Just have the tanks surround the giant so they don't push the Giant towards another one.

I've seen Corroded drop off of Adjutants and up with the most common drops being off of Veterans (though it's still rare as heck). On our last run through Kael we picked up:

1 Plate Boots (which I won and now my Paladin has some sweet new Boots).
1 Plate BP (One of our Clerics won this)
2 Chain Bracers
1 Silk Gloves

A few other pieces dropped as well but I can't remember what. We seem to average 3 or 4 pieces of armor in an hour but our Monk is a pulling machine and doesn't give us a chance to let up http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif

This seems to be the best spot to me since it's clean of roamers and very easy to hold. CR is a pain though as you need to go through several giants that See Invis so a Monk helping with CR is great.

One last thing, Adjutants are Clerics and LOVE to Complete Heal. Bring a few Enchanters along to Sieve them or you will have a long and drawn out fight since they cannot be mezzed or stunned.

Okay, there are a few decent drops in the arena, but they're rare. One is a rare sword off the protectors of zek (you can see them wielding it) called a Defiance. 9/19, str +6 dex +8 ac 10. The 10 ac on a 1hs is nice.
The circlet of tallon/vallon is a sweet monk head piece that drops off the prophets occasionally.
The rare idol (from our experience, and that's quite a bit) is the Idol of Decay. Get that one, and it's easy to get the cleric bracer.
And Kalis Stormcaller and his two buddies drop 3 of the 4 pieces for the ranger Tunare quest helm.
As for Stormslayer, the guy who drops the Cloak of the Maelstrom, his rare drop is Frostbringer (12/22, AC 7, effect: Frostbite (small DD with 15 ac reduction of target)). He is also the placeholder for a mob that drops the Mask of War, which is a great cleric/pally mask.
One of the mobs next to Tormax drops Coldain Skin Gloves. We don't know yet if he has a rare drop, the Gloves are either his only or his common drop.
If you pull his guards, you can squeeze by Tormax on the right and get back to clear the questgivers. First, they were green to most of my guild. This thread is almost half a year old http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/wink.gif I think Rayne is long done with Kael by now.

The way to max out what you get in armor drops in Kael is to hang out behind the arena (same area most are describing here, but left or right side doesn't matter) and pull all the static/wandering mobs above the level of Guard. If someone is doing SoTW quest then pull the temple mobs inside the building. If you're really mowing 'em down, pull the zeks and get some decent potential drops off of those too. Named gladiators have decent drops, and there are some very good random drops in this area as well.
