Velks spiders are ridiculously easy with the hammer and pet.

Pull with MoR, hit Yaulp V when mob is close, turn on attack(:P)cast pet after first round of hits, you can usually channel thru spider hits with ease.

Once pet is in action, strafe around to where you can see your pet, just incase you're not used to using pet hp bars etc. Heal with Ethereal remedy once/twice and by that time, spider is dead and you've used(after regening w/Y5) around 10-15% mana.

My hps/ac fully self buffed is real close to 4k and 1155AC. Just keep Y5 on the whole fight and you'll actually regen quite a bit. Each fight takes approx 2.5-3.0min depending on level/type of spider.
