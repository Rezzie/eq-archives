Hiya all fellow cleric friends,

When doing a rezz there are different ways to do it...all depending on the situation, and how you are rezzing.

If it is a guild event only I usually do 1 or 2 tells on guildchat telling everyone to sit tight and be ready for the rezz.

Doing single rezzes it is always best to ask the person first..."/tell Ready for rezz?"

After I got my Epic I now stick everyone on a raid 1 time without asking, but remember to still tell everyone in advance that they should NOT spend time doing lots of other things when they are waiting for their rezz.

Then I'll wait 1 minute and do a new round on all corpses that are left.
