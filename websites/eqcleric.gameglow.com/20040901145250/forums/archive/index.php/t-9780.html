Analysis shows that some pieces of the cleric armor will take nearly 40 hours to acquire. This is obviously unacceptable, and we are *greatly reducing* the time it should take. Spawn times, spawn percentages, and drop percentages are being adjusted across the board. This is not just for cleric armor, but for several, which I'll announce on patch-day.

Please keep in mind though that initial availability might appear to be less than it actually is due to the rush that occurs when new items are brought in game. As I recall, when class-specific stuff was first put in, *every* class complained about availability. After a few weeks, only a few were left with a problem, as the hoards of initial people finished getting theirs. Since cleric armor is so rare at the moment, I imagine there will be a similar rush on all the servers as it becomes available.

In response to some other posters, there are currently no plans to adjust any of the statistics (AC, Weight, Bonuses) on the armor. I do agree that AC and Weight needs to be discussed internally, just let us get the spawn times and such worked out before we move on to the next phase.
