Well, for the lower levels, wis isn't your main goal. AC, HP, +mana. You'll be doing alot of tanking for awhile, so may as well dress for it (besides, it's ALOT easier to keep your defense skill up throughout your life than get to 60 and have like 10 defense).

Now, server prices may vary, but here is a good, solid way to set up a new cleric with decent gear. No one item is "uber" nor is it even the "best" for any one slot. I am suggesting a nice, rounded setup based on the cash max you allow:

For all the bang for the buck, a full set of the cleric quest armor runs around 1k these days.

A Velium Fire Opal rings and a Shandel (sp?) Bandit ring (the one that drops off bandits in Fungus Grove). (about 600 for both)

A Pair of Forest Loops are a solid choice due to the +AC, +agi AND +wis. (about 400 for a pair)

For a shield, I would recommend a Clay Guardian Shield (about 300-500 these days)

For a neck item, you can get a Blackened Alloy Medallion (6 AC, 5wis) for like 50pp.

Adamantite Epolauts run like 100-150 tops. Hell, I still wear em at 60.

For a face item, you can't go wrong with a Platinum (+7wis/+7str) or Velium (+8wis/+8str) Ruby Veil. Especially if you go with a race with low STR.

Back and Belt items are alittle tricky. The decent ones run too much, or are NO DROP. If all else fails, a Fine Steel cloak will do for your back, and any cheap belt item (Straw Spun Belt for example) will do for starters.

There a LOT of good, cheap mace weapons that have + stats (Springwood Club for example). May as well get one for starters, since you WILL be tanking for awhile. Eventually, you will most likly switch to the old Paw of Opella, but not for awhile.

Take the rest of your cash, and get a couple weight reducing bags. Bag of Sewn Evil-eyes and Light Burlap sack both run about 200-300pp. You eventually will find yourself in zones where fine steel drops alot, and while 4-5pp per weapon doesn't sond like alot, it can add up when you are able to sell an extra 10-20 a night. I bought my first 2 Tinkerer bags off fine steel weapons.

By starting out as I am sugesting, your cleric will be very self sustaining for the rest of his/her life. Almost any future upgrade will be able to be gotten/purchased by your cleric, rather than relying on your main to supply him/her.

Dikmer's list is pretty nice, if you cant find the old Cleric quest armor you might think about Ry`Gorr battle mail, used to go for 1k, it's probably gone down some now. AC was comparable to the Cleric BP but it has 10 str and some other stats. Lot's of folks twink it on several classes.

Until you get to L20 your going to want to really focus on AC/+mana/HP gear. Resists will be icing on the cake. After 20 when mobs start getting harder to tank and mana bonuses on Wisdom are kicking in, switch to Wis/+mana/AC/HP/Resist.

If you use a tool like Magelo's profiler you can see what items will change your overall stats/mana pool and by how much, also the Allakhazam profiler does a neat search for you that you can specify what stat your looking to increase for what slot with other criteria as well.

Enjoy your cleric!
