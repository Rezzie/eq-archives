Since many bin d'partin' fer Kunark, I finally bin able t' git int' groups in Lower Guk. There be fewer than 40 people there mos' 'f th' time. Th' live side be c'mpletely empty.

'Stead 'f havin' t' git on a waitin' list, ye be havin' t' break int' empty rooms. This bin leadin' t' a number 'f deaths as people who bin 'ccustomed t' gittin' int' rooms with broken spawns now be breakin' int' rooms with full spawns.

I bin in a group that bin takin' th' Magi an' Hand. Our waitin' list bin havin' two people on it. Both bin findin' other groups b'fore we bin callin' 'em. Th' group that bin takin' on th' Executioner an' Sage bin shoutin' fer a healer fer at least two hours straight. I bin th' lowest member 'f me group fer th' 'ntire time I bin there. Fer a lot 'f th' time I bin 10 t' 15 levels lower that ever'body else. When I finally bin needin' t' leave, they bin puttin' lots 'f pressure on me t' be stayin'. Fortunately, they bin findin' 'nother cleric t' be r'placin' me jus' at th' las' minute.

Then me group went after th' Ghoul Lord b'cause nobody else bin takin' 'im.

An' t' be toppin' it off, I bin walkin' out with a Serpentine Bracer, a Skull Shaped Barbute, 8 fine steel weapons, an' a couple pieces 'f jewelry.

It be good t' be a cleric in Guk.

An' on me way out, th' someone bin shoutin' fer 'elp takin' on th' Shin Lord.

There now be room in Lower Guk fer those 'f us who never bin able t' git in b'fore.
