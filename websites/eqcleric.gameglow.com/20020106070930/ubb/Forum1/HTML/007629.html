I understood from your post that your guild is on Giant faction and you are ally to coldains. I gather you are trying to KEEP coldain faction and raise giant faction.

It can be done, I did it. Killing in SS will only raise your Kromzek (Storm Giat) faction, and (depending on what you kill) your Tormax faction. SS does not raise Kromrif (Frost Giant) faction. In order to raise Kromrif faction you have 2 choices.

#1, Kill dwarves, obviously.

#2, turn in coldain heads to Captain Bvellos in the Kael bank.

Now, it would appear that you would have to do #1 in order to do #2. This is not true. You can buy them, or you can go to the mines in Thurgadin and kill "A lesser tar goo". These guys drop Coldain heads (and beards and other stuff that normally drop off dwarves). And if you are KOS, try invis potions. This might have been patched, but they worked for me.

Unfortunatly, when I did it, I had not killed any giants, so my journey was relatively short (about 75 heads I believe). You have a much longer road to travel.

If you don't make an effort to get good Tormax faction, you will have difficulty getting in to do your armor quests.

You see, the quest giants are BEHIND the throne room.

It is POSSIBLE, but very difficult, for a monk (preferably non-KOS, but not required if he is good) and a non-KOS cleric to work together to drag your (suicided) corpse to the quest giant room and res you in to do your quests. I helped a 60 monk do it a couple times while I was still ally to giants. It basically required the monk to FD every 10-15 feet and have the cleric heal him up once he is FD. The giants in the throne room hit hard, hit fast and don't take too kindly to anyone dragging your corpse around.

Or you can just keep plugging away at the whole faction thing and eventually walk there yourself
