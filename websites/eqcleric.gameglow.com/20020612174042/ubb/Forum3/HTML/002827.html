Go to Umbral Plains, there are a lot of 2-3 group encounters there with some easy to obtain flowing thought items.

A word of caution though, don't overvalue flowing thought.

The first few flowing thought items to enter the game had flowing thought + AMAZING stats (ie eye/brain cazic thule, vulak robe, etc) and as such, anything with flowing thought was starting to get the idea that it must be an amzing item if it has flowing thought on it.

The items from Umbral Plains (and some other luclin minis) have neg stats and flowing thought, or no stats and flowing thought. Situational use only

With a mage, I can start with 400 mana and the mage with 260 mana and have the both of us to 90+ mana in about a minute... so figure 10 ticks, and I end up with a net gain of ~3000 mana for myself and about the same for the mage, hence even discounting the mage's mana profit that's about 300 mana/tick. Assuming me and the mage have to avoid low health aggro, that cuts our efficiency by a quarter, which still leaves with a net gain of 225 mana/tick. Adding 900hp to myself therefore is the equivalent of 75 mana/tick regen, so for the equivalent of FT15 I would only need ~180hp to match it.

I guess the real tradeoff one must consider is not FT vs. mana, but FT vs. HP. I favor +mana in most cases anyway because mana output is very rarely constant unless you are on a CH rotation, and what usually matters is how much mana I have to use at the moment when it is needed, not how much I will regen afterwards.
