Well, here are some shots of 3 resin gold helmets, about the closest we can get to the Armour of the Virtues colour.

Step 1 : Make the dye Extract
-----------------------------

Jack-O-Lantern fungus comes from three locations, but I can only confirm one.

-The Fairy Ring in Lesser Fay has some spawn on the ground that look like coin-sized mushrooms. These are very rare, and appear to be on a 22 to 24 hour real-life spawn.

-Jack-o-lanterns in Unrest, this may be bugged because they currently drop red dye. A Level 55 monk removes the population of unrest at a great rate of knots, and no fungus dropped.

-Jack-o-lanterns in West Karana - haven't had a chance to check yet, everytime I'm in West Karana I'm a bit tied up to check.

Once you have found a mushroom or two, go to the Windmills in Steamfont, the Armour Vendor in Highhold Pass or the Troll Vendor in Rathe mountains, and buy some of the 'Empty Vials' that are stackable.

Also, while you are there buy 3 x 'Resin' (5 plat each) and 1 flask of Acid and 1 flask of Resin. You'll need this in a little bit

Buy some ordinary store-bought water, and go looking for a Rogue or Shaman that owes you a favour. They should ideally have a poison making or alchemy skill of 40 or greater. Get them to combine the vial, the fungus and one flask of water (NOT summoned or foraged) in the poison kit / medicine bag. You should then get a 'Jack-o-lantern' extract.

Step 2 : Make the Dye itself
----------------------------

Now you need to practice pottery. This is a very easy skill to advance, and you basically get a skill point every attempt. You need :
- Medium Jar Sketch
- Clay Block
- Flask of Water.

This will make an Unfired Clay Jar. Make a few of these, they cost about 1 gold to attempt each one.

Buy some Quality Firing sheets, and take your clay masterpieces to a Kiln. Combine the jar and a firing sheet, and you get a four slot container with a combine button. As this is a container, you will need an inventory slot free, otherwise you will need to do some juggling.

Put the Jack-O-Lantern extract, and 3 resins into the clay container. Make sure the resins are *NOT* stacked, instead you should have the four slots each containing 1 item.

Hit combine. You should end up with single tube of 'Light Yellow Dye'.

Step 3 : Dying the armour
-------------------------

Obtain a undyed Fine Steel Helmet from your local blacksmith, or make it yourself. I recommend not attempting to make Fine Steel armour unless your blacksmithing skill is over 150. You should pay between 60 plat (cost) and 200 plat for your helmet.

Walk up to a forge, put your helmet, 1 vial of Acid, 1 vial of Laquer and the dye in. Hit combine.

It seems that there is no skill roll for this, but occasionally (possibly 5%) there is a failure. In this event, you get the helm back, but lose the dye, so that's why you may wish to have a backup dye made during the long walk to this step.

I did this with a set of three dyes earlier today, and I succeeded dying all three. If you are on Bertox and take a medium, send me a tell and try to catch me in the same zone, I'll donate the other two helms to clerics who can track me down.
