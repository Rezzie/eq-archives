Zellidon,

Yes you could do it. You need to have your Expel Undead and all your stun spells up. Keep him stunned and nuke him and you are fine. If he gets unstunned, he WILL heal himself, and you will not be happy.

You do NOT need to kill Zahal to force Regis to spawn. Other MOBS will kill Zahal if you wait long enough (plus SKs need him for a quest now, so I don't want to take away their chance).

Get yourself to 1500, neg 1500 and that is where he spawns. I recommend letting the entire zone know you are hunting Regis. (and get realy to deal with the 'is that your final answer' comments)

There is still some dispute over who has 'rights' to killing Regis (the one on his spawn point on a cleric that was chasing Zahal to off him).

So to avoid the issue - yell out you are camping him and the rest of the zone will know, and someone WILL yell out if they are camping Z., etc.
