No but I have a couple of interesting theories you might wish to try:

Suicide Pulling:

One person runs in and agros Trak AWAY from your group with the full knowledge they are about to DIE. Obviously use corpse dueling for res purposes and such here.

The main puller waits for the train to go away agros a single guard while all the other mobs head the other way. Feign , /q to clear agro 100%.

I'm sure this sounds extreme. But .. 1 death compared to everyone dying?

Gate Pulling:

I do this with my cleric sometimes.. used to do it in Oasis to pull spectres.

Pull mob of your choice. Run halfway to the fight point.. gate. Mobs now wander back to home point, pick off those that are left that you wish to kill ala normal chainpull techniques.


Obviously this techinque works best if you have amage who can summon you back to the party after you gate out of the zone.

Both techniques are specifically geared at 100% agro clearance ( which I have not been to Trak yet but Im guessing part of the problem separating the guards and always getting Trak is .. agro issues).

Good luck and keep us up to date with any succesfull techniques you develop.

Corpse Dueling (korps dool-ing)
1. Defeating a corpse in a duel to the death.
<table border="0">
<tr bgcolor="gray"><td>
<font color="yellow">Boanerges has defeated Lleh's corpse0 in a duel to the death!</font>
</td></tr></table>
2. Dueling anohter person and defeating them for the express purpose or resing that person.

Example... Your group need symbol but you're out of Peridots. The Warrior is bound in Firiona Vie so you duel him. He dies and returns to FV, gets money from the bank, buys Peridots. You then revive his corpse and he returns to your group.

Another example (happens in Hate a lot where you HAVE to Gate or get a TP out). Someone needs to get out of the zone and they can't gate. You duel a TPer. Defeat them. Revive them back in. They TP those people out. You Revive them again. *NOTE* Rumor has it that Verant made all duel corpses Revivable only once since multiple Revives for the purpose of summoning is considered an exploit. However, I have seen it done several times since the patch so I don't think it is real yet.

Hope that explains it. http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
