As a Cleric, I rarely see what gyrations our poor puller goes through to get that one lone monster all by itself. I didn't see the 12 feign deaths that the monk does to break up a full spawn, or the number of times the warrior dies trying to figure out the aggressive radius on his route back to the room.

Mostly, I just know: "Bad pull! Evac! Evac!"

On the other hand, most people know they're about to die, and yet miraculously don't... "Nice heal!"

For giggles, try learning to pull as a cleric. Root, Atone, Soothe, Invis to Undead, stun-pulling, fear-kiting, Divine Aura, cross-zone gating... It's exciting - and you get a whole new respect for that warrior that can pull a constant stream of single blues all night from a nearly empty zone.
