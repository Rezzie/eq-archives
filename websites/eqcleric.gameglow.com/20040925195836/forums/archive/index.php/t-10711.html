If you want good exp, never solo non-undead creatures. Yes, you can do it, but if you go for undead mobs you will always do more damage for less mana. My suggestion would be to hunt below the bridge in Lower guk dead side. When you look at the 3 wizards (1 of them being Ritualist/PH), just drop down and go left. You will end up in a "safe hall" which is a great place to hunt in. You can pull WAN's from one way of the hall and ZOL's and DAR's from the other way. Most of them you can get solo, at most you get 2, which isnt much of a problem. This place is still good exp at 50.

Retribution is 240 mana for 516 damage (probably even less on 45)
Banish undead is 225 mana for 585 damage

So soloing non-undead is just a waste of time and mana.
