There is no level you 'can't' solo at. The question becomes the effectiveness of soloing. I have solo'ed a lot (all my 40's, every hell level pre-50, every level from 1 to 20). At level 52 I can still solo for exp (currently trying to solo dwarfs in GD for giant faction, that Keal quest armor is awesome) but it takes forever. My fastested soloing was in my 40's with Happ (mentioned in previous post). I was the most boring camp I have ever had (6 minute spawn, dead in 2 nukes after lvl 44) but I was making a blue bubble every 3 kills in 45.
Now, post-50, I can still solo, when I feel like it (don't have time to group, busy doing other things in RL and don't want to be tied to computer) but I level soooo much faster in a grind group in Seb or KC. Hope this answers your question.
The trick to soloing is doing research and finding mobs that are high enough to be blue, but for some reason have fewer HP than other mobs their level. There are numerous web pages out there that can give you that info (including this one, check out the Hunting Zone's by level, and check all posts. There are a lot of nice camps that have been forgotten about in there). But I warn you, if you solo too much you could solo yourself into a corner, where you don 't trust yourself to group because of inexperience, and a cleric HAS to know how groups work and what tactics to use, if you ever plan on getting any planes armor or epic weapons.
