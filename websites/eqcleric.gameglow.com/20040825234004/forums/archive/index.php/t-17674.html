Crystal Dawn is a european raiding guild on the Tholuxe Paells server, currently in the Plane of Time.

Tholuxe Paells is a 3 year old server, not very populated : only 2 guilds are raiding the Plane of Time, including Crystal Dawn. The closest guild to enter the zone has not defeated Coirnav and the Rathe Council yet.

Also, there is only one other European raiding guild on the server, which has not entered the Elemental Planes yet and which gives Crystal Dawn a certain advantage in terms of time zone when we raid other zones than the Plane of Time.

We are looking for new clerics who are able to play a lot, with a good and nice team of players.

We require level 65 and a minimum of 80 AA points total (including SCM3, MGB, Divine Arbitration 3, Radiant Cure 1) and your epic.

The more flags you have the better it is, being elemental plane flaged or even time flaged is a big plus. But we will also consider good players if they miss flags as we reflag our applicants when needed.

We raid 6 days a week from 7:30 PM CET to 11:30PM CET (1:30 PM to 5:30 PM EST)
You shall not think of applying to us if you can't at least raid 4 evenings a week at those times.

For more informations and to post your application online, please check www.crystaldawn.info and this post in particular : http://www.crystaldawn.info/forum/viewtopic.php?t=6021

Feel free also to send me a tell in game (;tell tholuxe.Paoldara … across server for Tholuxe Paells) if you have any question, or to our (gnomish ;) ) guild leader Nomics if you don’t find me online. You can also send me an email at paoldara@crystaldawn.info if you have any additional question.

We are looking forward your application and raiding with you !
