Yall already know some of my Dwarven games....
1- HILL GIANT PING PONG. required lots of Beer..Root(for yerself)...FEAR...Smallest nuke ye have...heal and invigor... Find HG Root yerself...Drink...Nuke...fear...repeat. Seeing who wins is the best fun when really really bored. I had an audience of 15 newbies watching me one time in WC, they thought I was GOD. SIGH...

2- Gobber Wall Art....Find a wall any will do. Entice any gobber to come real close to said wall. (Oooo OI OI me foots broke! I must rest I cannae even move to defend myself) Root said Gobber in Place, stun him but good to make em stand still. Wind up yer favorite Gobber Kncoker and let fly at 'es 'ead. Invite friends over to drink and ale and make comments on yer latest work of Art>!

3- If'n ye hear some dwarf be yelling "FOR BEER FOR BLOOD FOR BRELLLLLLLLLLLL" Look Up. And then get out of the way. Likely ye'll be under a highplace where some drunken Dwarven Cleric is attempting a Bombing Run (while tottaly bombed) while Divinely protected upon some passing gobber, orc, or ogre. We get bonus points for splash damage to surrounding mobs and witnesses...ye've been warned.

Walking the Dog - Youve seen these people down the park walking huge packs of dogs for a few bucks. You too can enjoy this healthy exercise. Just go the nearest doggy dungeon, go barehanded up to a dog and give it a resounding slap and the command "Heel" and the dog will happily follow behind nipping harmlessly at your heels. Do this to as many doggies as you can. Once you feel you have given the poor things enough exercise find an impressionable group of newbies, stand in front of them and give the command "SIT!" at the same time casting Earthquake or your own faveourite AE. The doggies should all then fall exhausted at your feet.

This can also be a good excercise for gobbos and the poor troopers in crushbone who grow weary of standing at their posts for hours on end.
