Oh yes my friend, one of the greatest XP monsters in game are the DW goblin pets. For some reason, they have begun spawning in Oasis, i really don't know why, but i see lots and lots of red guys to me (lvl 27) fighting these freaking dogs!! The dogs are all blue to me, and from 21-25 I fought them at Rathe mountain zone in Lake rathe. The highest i saw in lake was lvl 34 magi soloing them, could only take like one in 5, so we got the rest. No loot what so ever since they are summoned, BUT they are summoned, which means Mages can use the handy ward summoned line. Clerics get that spell too, but at lvl 29 we are only at the second part. But still they are awesome exp, and in lake rathe there is a bandit camp near, you can clear that and get a few bronze weapons and bandit sashses, which turn in at qeynos for bronze weapons and 1-2pp in cash, plus faction. They make up for the no loot dogs.
