The entrance to Befallen and the Darkelf Shadowknight areas are easy experience for levels 10-13.
Plenty of skeletons and plague rats are here for the xp. The safest place is throught the second door on the right where Lrodd stabds gaurd over the well to level 3.
Becareful of Lrodd at levels 9 and 10 just kill skels until you stop getting xp then kill Lrodd he has the same stats as a greater skeleton. Root and nuke works very well with him. Becareful at lower levels because skeletons are all within agro radius of each other.
At the entrance higher level monsters will often spawn but if you stay near the Darkelf area you need not worry about trains or the occasional wondering ghoul.
GOOD LUCK and remember Divine Aura will give you enough time to make a run for the zone if things get stick!
