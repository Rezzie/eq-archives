A few comments on 1 on 1 battles:

1) Weapon choice will be crucial. We're not like the tank classes that have bonus damage which makes low delay critical in the primary hand. That being the case, you need to look for weapons with as good a ratio as possible. The two best that I could think of are the Sprinkler and the Velium Brawl stick.

2) Resist Magic Gear is critical. Once you get that to a good level (100+ prebuff), go for AC and HP.

3) Cancel Magic! Get rid of all the buffs on the other person.

3) Yaulp IV is going to be pretty important.

4) DD? Unlikely. Especially the level 5 one. 1 Remedy, costing 175 mana, balances 25 castings of furor, which cost 500 mana. Granted, if you can keep him permanently knocked back, that would be different. But I find it hard to believe that a char with 100+ MR wouldn't resist occisionally. And while you're casting furor, he's pounding you with a 20/32 or 32/39 weapon, likely with haste.
