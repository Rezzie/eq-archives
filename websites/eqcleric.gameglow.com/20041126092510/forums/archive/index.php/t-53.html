So you see, if you're willing to swap equipment every time you go shopping, these items can pay for themselves fairly fast. There are a lot of charisma items out there, and as charisma is a "non-important" stat they often have large bonuses.

A note about faction and price caps though. Your faction with the merchant affects your prices too, but if you have high charisma it doesn't matter. Me with 200 charisma have same prices at an apprehensive vendor as I do on a warm vendor. For warm/amiable faction it appears that after about 105 charisma that gives you best prices. Apprehensive add 10-20 to that number. After having good charisma for a little while you'll start learning prices and noticing what 'best' price for an item is, so you will know whether you get it or not depending on faction.

The only item I think on my list that not-everyone has heard of is the Siryn Hair Hood. Not wanting to post spoilers unprovoked, if anyone wants to know where the Siryn Hair Hood can be found, leave a message and I'll post spoilers.

One thing that was failed to mention, is for the higher level clerics, Ethereal Mist bracers provide 9 CHA each.

I use the SplitPaw Nacklace and a Crude Stien, but I am loathe to swap out all my equip for CHA cause when you get dressed again, you need to re-med all that mana. So with 1 EM bracer, the necklace and the stien, I am 74 CHA. Not great, but it does drop my cost of peridots from 14.5pp to 12.5pp. 2pp savings each. That really adds up.
