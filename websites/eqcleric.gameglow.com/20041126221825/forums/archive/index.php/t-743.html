Uhm... you need to make an Erudite cleric! Yeah, that's it http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/biggrin.gif

Mages are quite soloable. I get quite a bit of enjoyment from mine. And the best part is you'll be right next to the library where you can buy those hard-to-find spells (like Staff of Tracing, Summon Fang, etc).

I would also suggest you stick to Tox. I find that it's still one of the most underused zones. Most of the people who hunt there are genuine newbies because they don't know about the rest of the world. Twinks generally get taken somewhere else to level.

Tox will start to green out around 8 or so and by then you should both be accustomed enough to your classes to venture elsewhere. BB or WK by the QH zone are good places to do that.

Hope that helps.
