Thortak,

It seems to be built into the game. Whenever you lvl, the mobs do their damndest to !GNID you. Most visible and painful is when you just get a new spell lvl and lose your new toys http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/frown.gif (usually I get a spell lvl about 4 times or so until it sticks...)

What may help to keep this from happenening is the following (it's my strategie from now on...).
If you lvl to a new spell circle rejoice, tell everyone and then go on as if nothing happened. Keep using the old spells, keep fighting the same mobs as before the lvl. Scribe the new spells if you got them (I usually lug them around for the whole lvl before I can scribe them) BUT DO NOT USE THEM YET! Try to lock in the lvl until you have a tleast a bub into it before you leave the group, go sell, camp, pick up spells or whatever. That way if you die you won't lose the lvl (tho 3 consecutive deaths in Unrest in 10 minutes will defy any precautions http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/biggrin.gif).

Then take some time away from fights, max all skils you can, make sure your fizzles on the new spells are over (GHeal was wonderful, but a mana hog and fizzle city when I first started using it at 24).

Anyway, this works for me... try it for size and see if it does for you.

Anyone else have tips or strategies to counter the dreaded !GNID ?
