Tanks live and die by their equipment. They need AC, haste and high ratio weapons to do their job efficiently.

Casters (including clerics) are more dependant on the skills of the person behind the keyboard than equipment. Any equipment that gets you close to 200 wis with a reasonable ac and adds some HPs and mana is fine.

Sarnak battle shiled could be clay guardian/bladestopper/green dragon shield... each has slight differences in stats, but not enough to make one stand out.

Manastone. High end, you will be somewhere you can use it less than 1% of your play time. It's for twinks, nothing else. Post 50 with a good group you should be able to pretty much constant pull without going LoM anyway.

I could offer alternatives to most of the stuff on the list. If it has options that will replace it, it's not essential.

Resist jewelry is probably the only essential stuff for high end raids. Get your velium blue diamond bracelts, diamond velium wedding rings etc. These make a whole lot more difference than an extra 2 wis or 3 ac on a raid.

Once you have roughly EM equivelant equipment, everything else after that is a bonus. I stopped worrying about it a bit back and feel much better for it. If I get something new /cheer. If not, no big deal.

As was mentioned above, friends are a lot more imortant than equipment. I would have quit a long time ago if not for them. I get as much pleasure out of seeing them getting something as I do getting something myself.
