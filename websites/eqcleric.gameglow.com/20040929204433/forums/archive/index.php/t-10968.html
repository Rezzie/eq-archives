A few things you might want to try:

1. Make sure clerics are at pretty close to max casting range, proximity aggro is your friend. Be sure not to be right at the edge of casting range, or the gradual knockback of the MT could put you out of heal range.

2. Have another high AC melee engage shortly after the MT engages to eat up the first bit of Rampage

3. Start the Ch chain of with a lay hands from a paladin, they can take the hits and hopefully get a DA off, and the MT should have aggro by the time the DA wears off, and even if they die you dont lose much damage output.

Disclaimer: I've only done vindi once and it was a long time ago, so im not even sure these are valid strategies. Just going on things that have worked vs. other high rampage mobs.
