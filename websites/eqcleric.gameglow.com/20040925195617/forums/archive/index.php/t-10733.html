Here's how I soloed through 49.

At 49 (and 48 and to a lesser extent 47) you can pull singles from the Spec cave in the Feerot. The problem is that many of the spawns will be green to you, and it really slows the exp to have to clear them, so try to find a couple of lower level players that want to hunt specs. The tree spawn is so perma-camped that this is pretty easy to do. Then pull singles for them (I just feared them out of the cave).

Presto, not only do you get the greens cleared (keeping the choice blue spawns for yourself), but you are a hero for pulling for the other people. If you're lucky, the people you're pulling for can cast SOW or clarity.

And it's alot more fun than sitting there alone.
