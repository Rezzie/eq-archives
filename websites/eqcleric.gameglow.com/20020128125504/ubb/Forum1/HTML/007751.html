My favorite answer for the many requests and inquiries as to why I'm not in a guild is "When in a guild, you play somebody elses game - I prefer to play my own". Plus my schedule makes guild play and raids rare. Anyway, a guild asked me to go on a mission with them, in which I agreed. After killing several mobs, and receiving some ok loot, names were put into the hat as to who could lotto on items. When I asked was I able to participate, I was told no because "apps" were not eligible. I replied "ok np but I'm not an app". I didn't apply for memebership of this guild. I was asked to come on the raid, because IMO they were short healers.
