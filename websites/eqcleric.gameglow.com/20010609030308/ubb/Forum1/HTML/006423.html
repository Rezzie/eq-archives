Greetings! Figured some may be interested in an update from Luclin. As many may know, some servers are completely FFA on Ragefire, others have some form of nice arrangements, but the Luclin server is the only server in Everquest with a 100% respected Ragefire Rotation List. Our list has now turned about four months old, and the original creator of the list has retired from that task, which has now fallen to me. : )

Link: http://pub6.ezboard.com/fluclin71335dragonsandtheplanes.showMessage?topicID=791.topic

Tonight another Ragefire popped, and another cleric from a smaller guild, who would normally be sol on other servers, got her epic. : ) It truely was a neat little sight to see. This particular cleric was pretty well-known and well-liked. Her Ragefire popped, but it was kinda late at night. No biggie, we had a horde of over 54 people descend upon solb within a half hour or so for one simple pure motive, to help out the cleric on top. In attendance was a pretty strong showing from 2 of Luclin's four "uber-guilds", none of which the cleric on top belonged to. (Including mine, which had just finished off Trak for the 2nd time in two nights.)

It's gotten pretty nice now, where on other servers Ragefire has become a point of intense conflict, on Luclin, when Ragefire spawns, it is a social event, an opportunity to see lots of friends from other guilds, and meet others you may have barely heard about. All gathering from camps all around, maybe sick of LFG in seb to chat and joke around for a half hour, untill enough people show up all across guild lines for a massive overkill on Ragefire. In four months, we have had no KSing, no cheating, and no Ragefires lost. (well, except for one time when naggy killed Rage off :P)
