Last night, after a total wipeout in Howling Stones, I was sitting at the Overthere zone near Frontier Mountains. It looked like the Goons had been through the area again, and several corpses were lying about the valley. So while I was waiting, I offered resurrections to the clothed bodies.

"Would you like a Res?"

Dead guy answers, "ummmmmmm, exp res?"

Erz replies, "ummmmmm, rude way to respond to a favor."

The guy got really upset, called me a name, and I snapped back something about not bothering to try next time and threw him on ignore.

Now here's my question. I'm a very nice person, really, but that entire conversation got out of hand, and all I would have had to do to prevent it is say yes, 49th res. But, I'm 51st level, I'm not anon or roleplay, and a simple /who would have answered his question. He didn't even ask it nicely.

To top it off, this was a level 40 druid. 40! Not like he was 59th level. Not like 49 clerics run through Overthere every 30 seconds. Anyway, he may have had another account with a cleric or a friend at his beck and call, but he no longer has me.

Guess I should be less sensitive, but the way he asked was so irritating. And the way he whipped back at my comment was completely unnecessary.

Erz
