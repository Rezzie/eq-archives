I got Begurgle at 52, and was glad I had a SK of the same level with me. He conned dark blue and had a lot of HP and I'm not sure I could have soloed him too easily. For a duo he was no problem though.

When I got Gimblox I was 51, I had a few other guildmates with me of varying levels as we were powerleveling a couple who were in their high 20s. Sol A has a lot of green aggro and things won't leave you alone, so you'd run out of mana fairly quickly killing all the greens you encounter on the way there, so if you bring someone to keep you company it'll go much faster. Plus the respawn rate on Gimblox's spawn is mind numbing to say the least so company is always good.

Those are probably the only parts you could conceivably solo though, aside from the turn-ins of course, but I even dragged a druid friend with me for those I could have lev and sow for the run through Timorous.
