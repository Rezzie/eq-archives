Pet aggro is different from player aggro.

It used to be, way back in the day, that NPC's would mostly ignore pets totally.

Then, they changed some code and NPC's would automatically attack pets over any player of any class doing any amount of damage.

Last patch, VI said they fixed the "mob always attacks pet bug".

Then, LOS uses pets to great advantage during their AOW fights. (The AOW always attacked the pets it seems, over players. And BTW, big congrats to LOS for their victory!! WTG!!!)

VI decides that they really DIDN'T fix the "mob always attacks pet bug" and they felt that it "cheapened" the encounter with AOW. Pets made it too easy.

VI brings down Kael and nerfs charm for giants. Claims that they will fix the "mob always attacks pet" bug next patch and return Kael giants to charmable.

The above is just my understanding, I don't have a pet class, but I think that's right.
