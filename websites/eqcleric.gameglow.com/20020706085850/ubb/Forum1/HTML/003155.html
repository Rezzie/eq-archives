My friend and I were discussing things that irritate us about the way people play. I decided to post our number one reason here hoping that someone out there who plays like this will notice and become a better group member. I do not notice this happening with Clerics very often, it is usually wizards and mages, but I grouped with a 45th Cleric last night who had this problem.

People who use all of their mana and cast again as soon as they get enough for one spell irritate me. I don’t understand why someone always has to be at zero mana all of the time. What if there is an emergency? I chose 70% as my cutoff. When I get to 70% mana, unless it is a dire emergency, I do not waste mana on DD or stuns. I always have mana if the group gets in trouble. There is no difference between the time it takes to med back one spell from zero and the time it takes from 70%.

Going into why this is more of a problem with wizards and mages than clerics is a whole different thread.

What irritates all of you other Clerics? Maybe I am doing something that I don’t realize is annoying and you can enlighten me. Probably just being in my group is annoying enough for most people.

Chikung,

Yes, all our armor buffs that add HP's also do a heal for that amount. If you notice some caster armor buffs that add to max HP but don't do the heal bit, it looks they lose HP (an optical illusion)

So if someone already has the buff and you recast it, their AC doesn't change, their max HP doesn't change, but the spell does a "heal" for the buff amount. And of course the buff timer is reset.

It's a very nice advantage so you don't have to cast heals after armor buffing. And the side effect is some armor buffs are more efficient (mana and/or speed) than some heals.
