I *think* the only way to repair KOS faction with ClericsofTunare is a pretty rough road.
If you kill orcs in crushbone you recieve +Ranger faction, can't recall the exact name of it right now, but anyhow.
There is a muffin quest in freeport, the guy you turn the muffins in to is on the Ranger faction, but gives +ClericsofTunare for doing the quest.
So the answer, as best I can find, is to kill orcs until you're indifferent to the ranger guild (FaydarksChampions maybe?) Then proceed to turn in muffins in freeport until you have fixed your ClericsofTunare faction.

I haven't tried charming the quest npc in freeport, and of course, if you have sneak, dependant on your race, that makes this a whole lot easier, and you can skip the orc killing completely.

Just a note on the muffin guy. (Pandos Flintside, I think his name is, a dwarf)

He himself is on Clerics of Tunare faction, so if you are KOS in Felwithe, you'll be KOS to him.

If you do get the chance to give him some muffins, you can give him 4 at a time unstacked. (Never forget that when you hold down the CTRL key when clicking a stack, you only pick up one!)

Also, there is a muffin merchant in the SAME zone, I think her name is Merchant Brownloe or something like that, and she is in the house that is opposite the stage in the Theater of the Tranquil. (Near monk guild)

Invis will not work when handing over items to NPC. Hide works on same principle as invis, and won't work as well. (trading the item breaks the invis.) Mez will not work - it only stuns the mob. Wolf form used to set your faction to beta neutral, but that's been changed to animal faction. (yes, that can still be useful in certain instances.) Sneak will work 100%. Just make sure you con the mob.

But charm? That should work every single time... unless Pandos is level 53+, but I don't recall him being like that.
