Very familiar with Velks, and use it as my main group's one-stop shopping point for BP gems. Granted you will need a very good group, but it can be done.

Current group is a wizard, cleric, shaman, enchanter, ranger, and a 6th person that we pick up somewhere, any tank is good. Average level is 59, but we've been doing this for maybe two levels now. Any named dog can drop BP gems, as well as Icy Guardians (long respawn, as well as Lord Bob and Velketor's guards), Velium Hunter spiders (<--- mob of choice), large crystal shards (same area as spiders), and other mobs by the castle.

In our one group we drop to the lower kobold area, and fight our way to the spider area. Hunters have very high MR, and hit around 215 max. If you are L60 (maybe 59, but haven't tried it), if you pull from a spawn area, only velium stalkers and hunters will come (broodlings will stay). Since I'm the only L60 currently, I do all the pulling. The first spider room is the toughest, with 4 mobs, and is really a pain if all 4 are hunters or stalkers. If this is the case, I make sure everyone is buffed to the hilt (Kragg, Marzin, Rune V, Regrowth, etc). and I pull all four spiders. I will DA, and hopefully the chanter can get 1-2 mezzed while the tanks beat on one. I will DB a second time to make sure all of the extras get mezzed. Usually isn't too hard once everything is mezzed. We usually camp in the room near the 3 spectres (you can see them straight up from the room), which has no spawns, and only two dogs next door that are usually killed before we pull the first spider room.

All that being said, I've only had to use the DA/DB tactic maybe twice, as usually there are a couple of broodlings in there. The next spider room has two, and the third room has two as well, so if it's just our group, we'll just stagger the spider rooms really well, and pull dogs for experience.

I am estimating perhaps a 10-15% BP gem drop rate on hunters, as well as any other gem that can drop in the zone. Hunters can also very rarely drop L55 spells. They are around L51 themselves.

Long-winded I'm sorry, but holler if you have any more questions.
