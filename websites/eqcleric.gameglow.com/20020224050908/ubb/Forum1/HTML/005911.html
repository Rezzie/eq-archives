Actually, try for a Walrusbone Shield. Drops in the upper part of the maze in Skyshrine.

+5 wis, str, sv magic and only weighs 3.0 for a net gain of 9 carrying capacity.

Why ditch wis for str? Simple...

Velious armor is HEAVY. EM has +14 str. Stability only has +4. And Stability weighs 1-2 more per piece. Donal's is non-stat too.

If you can, ditch the EM bracer for Donal's. Not easy to find, tho. Drops in Disco2 camp of Sebilis.

As to the boots... tough call there. Donal's boots are RARE as heck but if Verant ever listens to us and ups the drop rate the price will fall. IMO, Donal's are not the best thing for clerics anymore, especially given Stability and Thurgadin quest stuff being easier to get for the stats.

What you really need to do is get in on a PoG raid (admit it, you never liked Tunare anyways ). It's a new standard in armor and you can pick up some major +wis and AC and even +mana.

Some other things...

Neck: go for a Goblin Soul Caller (+5 wis +10 MR) easy to get. Abalone Gorget is nice but good luck finding someone selling one. Saw one being auctioned off in trade for a VS stone the other day.

Face: Sebilite Scale Mask is good AC but you'll lose +str and some wis. Polished Mithril Mask is good AC and wis but no +str.

Melee: Obviously work on your epic but don't hold out much hope on finishing unless they change the quest. What are stats on that one you have now?

Dump the dark ember for a paw or the coveted ice cream cone of the mystics (ac 5 int 5 wis 5 mana 50), both sell for 3-5k these days.

A hiero cloak would be a nice upgrade for your back slot but that's something you'll probably have to camp yourself if you're on a budget. Luckily, the cone drops at the same camp as the cloak, so if you put in some time in sebilis crypt, you could probably pick up both.

I don't know if Dorfs can use them, but sebilite scale leggings are the best non-Velious, non-Donal's legs a cleric can get. They sell fairly cheap on my server.

The sebilite scale mask and neckguard are both upgrades to what you've got now, both drop in disco where, coincidentally, Donal's Bracer, Helm and Arms drop. The helm is terrible, but the bracer is nice, and you'll almost certainly get the mask and neckguard if you spend a bit of time in disco.

You'll want a sarnak shield eventually, but they're increasingly rare and the charred guardian shield is good enough until you can get one.
