Well finally got the computer up and running and went to SOL this weekend.

I decided to try out The Grey as this is the only zone I have heard that is decently able to solo for a cleric.

I ported into the Nexus and through my limited maps that I had printed off, found my way to the Bazaar.

Found the bank and deposited all my extra items and cash and then bound myself.

Zoned back to the Nexus (after taking a 5 min break to make a sandwhich while zoning - lol) and then proceeded to the Netherbian Lair. Conned everything as I was passing through. Dubbious to indifferent.

Zoned to Marus - nice greenie zone. Everything was indifferent to me. Kind of nice to pass through and not agro anything.

Zoned to Mons Lat. - VERY UGLY zone in my opinion. Almost everything was KOS. Passed and trigger some little trap thing and had about 20 or so of these little beetle things like in Dragon Necroplis nipping at my heels. Luckily they don't move very fast. Also got attacked by some greenie necro type thing. Kept trying to fear me and snare me but failed. Eventually ran out of it's range.

Finally found my way to the Grey. Interesting zone. Managed to solo a Snake thing and a few skeletons using Turning of the Unnatural (haven't managed to get a hold of the epitaph of life or the other one yet).

Found a small group of 3 other people (warrior, shaman and druid) and joined them. Killed quite a few things and was able to keep up really well with the mana (helps to have a level 60 warrior with epic and full TOV armor tanking with the shaman slowing). Did that for about 2 hours until the warrior went LD and didnt' come back.

Anyway - was a fairly nice experience in my first trip to SOL.

Where if any other zones are there that are soloable by a 60 cleric? I heard that bats in Maidens can be soloed, but they have a LOT of hp.

Would the Grey be the best place to try to solo as a cleric?
