I ran in Great Divide in kodiak cave 3.. unbelievable exp... and the groups are usually pretty good.... you can meet some good people in those caves... also meet some deadbeats.. but I found a tank, I will prolly hang out with for a long time in the future....

The kodiaks will all be blue to you in your 40's, but compared to DL, the exp just flows... now there isnt much loot in the kodiak caves, but if you are looking for a place to break through 40, and up to 43.. then those kodiak caves are phenomenal...

if you want loot, then hunt Crystal Caverns, or hunt the orc fort in Eastern Wastes... a cleric can get a group easily with these people.. but in my experiences, the exp itself is not very forthcoming....

Geonids in Crystal Caverns DO offer both good exp AND good loot though... but they are usually VERY camped...
