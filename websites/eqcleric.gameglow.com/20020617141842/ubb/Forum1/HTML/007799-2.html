Not quite sure what you are asking, Atabex.

We know the following from the information given though:

A Zordak Ragefire spawns in Sol B. He is supposed to spawn every 24 hours but seems to be broken on all servers and not doing that. This merchant you give a shimmering pearl and he returns a swirling pearl.

Independent of him, somewhere in Norrath, the former killable version of Zordak Ragefire that you would get when you turned in your pearl formerly in Sol B is supposed to spawn within 24 hours of a patch and within every 3 days thereafter. Killing him will spawn the dragon version, Zordakalicus Ragefire . He has yet to be spotted on any server. It is conjecture he spawns in Skyfire since the new Sol B text mentions his returning from his home in Skyfire, but this cannot be proven yet.

It is not really a triggered spawn concept like the druid Faydedar where there exists the regular Faydedar who can spawn independently and the triggered version and neither can co-exist. There are simply 2 different independent spawns of the merchant that accomplish different things. One changes your pearl and one you kill to get his true dragon form. Neither triggers the other and there is no reason both cannot be up at the same time.

No, there is no link between converting the pearl and the killable Ragefire spawning.

There are now two separate and unlinked camps.

Sol B has the Ragefire merchant who will convert your pearl into a Swirling pearl, that is all he does, he does not trigger the killable Ragefire he just converts the pearl.

Elsewhere, *not* in Sol B another ragefire will spawn which when killed will trasform into the dragon version, kill the dragon get the container heart, combine the swirling pearl in the heart to get the final heart.

No linkage between these two, each must be camped separately.
