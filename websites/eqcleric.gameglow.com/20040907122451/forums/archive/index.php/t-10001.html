Right now you are to low to notice. Rangers have fairly similar skill caps defensive/offensive to other melees up through level 40. Post 50 rangers get a large increase to their offensive caps but only marginal increases to their defensive caps. Also, the hitpoint differential grows much larger. Most warriors in the high end game will have 6500hp or so, maybe 6k at the lowerside and 7k+ at the very high side. Most rangers will have something around 4k hp's with similar buffs maybe 4500 max.

Also every other melee is both a plate class melee and has a defensive cap of 250. At lvl 60 rangers have a defensive cap of 220 and are still wearing "chain". Note the other chain melee class (rogue) has a higher defensive cap. Rangers caps for riposte, parry, and dodge are lower as well than the rest of the melees.

Rangers can tank but imho post 50 they either need both epics (esp earthcaller) or a sham/enc in the group doing slows. While AA does let rangers tank better ND, CS, CA, etc... they still don't tank as well as a similarly equipped and leveled war/pal/sk. The harder mobs hit the more noticeable this becomes.

This is kind of a funny issue because, of course there are well-played and badly-played examples in every class.

I don't think anyone is saying that rangers make the best tanks. That's ludicrous. However, it is true to say that SOME rangers are better tanks than SOME warriors just because they are better players.

A number of us have had the pleasure of playing with a well-played ranger. I heart my ranger tank. He's super =). I've played with him since my teens and he has consistently performed really well as our main (and sometimes ONLY) tank. He rocks as a puller, too.

Rangers tanking in Sebilis is a far cry from rangers tanking against huge boss mobs. Even then they can do it in fast fights with weaponshield, but usually that's the warrior's domain.

My point, for the original poster, is that if you're just talking about an exp group somewhere, a well-played and well-equipped ranger usually makes a darn good tank.
