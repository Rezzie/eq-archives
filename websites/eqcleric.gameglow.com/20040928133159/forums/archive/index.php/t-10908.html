Pulling Vind/Tormax to the zone with non KOS people is lame and we refuse to do it. =P

Generally we have a monk pull vind and his 2 guards out into the open area outside the areana. The monk FDs and a bard with fresh SoW on occludes Vind to agro him, then kites him and his guards around. A monk then pulls a guard off and we kill it, then the other. The bard then brings Vindicator home and the warrior playing main tank for the fight smacks him as the bard runs past. Main tank group for the fight usually consists of 3 clerics, 2 warriors and a bard. The 2 warriors are the 1st and 2nd main tanks. First heal on the main tank comes from 1-3 paladins using LoH. The clerics cast CH in sequence 7 seconds apart continuously untill the warriors defensive disciplin is down, then we tighten the sequence to land the CHs at 5 second intervals. Vendicator usually lives for less than 5 min.

We do the same general thing for the Statue, exept when it is pulled to camp, a ranger intercepts it off the bard and tanks it with his parry disciplin while the statue is debuffed and slowed. Once slowed, a warrior steps in and takes agro. We use a 10 second heal timing on the slowed statue. We are actually overhealing at 10 seconds but don't want to push the timing out any further due to possible lucky quads by the statue, slow breaks, etc. Last time we killed the statue the only death was the main tank who went down because his disciplin went down at the exact same instant that the statue got a lucky quad. It didn't help any that he is a Wood Elf warrior /tease Titanax. He was resed back in and fighting before the battle was over. Even the ranger and bard have lived the last 2 times. lol

3 cleric with c2, potg, GoB, a bard a steady supply of mod rods, and 3 necros to feed them mana can keep CHs landing on a main tank every 6-7 seconds for a very long time. Our last fight vs Lendairia the Keeper lasted 11 min and all 3 of us finished at 70-80% mana. At about 8 min into the fight we were yelling at the necros to feed other clerics because we were all at or near full mana. I cast CH 21 times in that fight and finished with about 75% mana.

One of our alliance members had a theory about how to kill the Avatar of War. Unfortunatly it would require about 100 level 60 rangers if his numbers are correct. If we can ever assemble 100 level 60 rangers to give it a whirl, I'll let you know how it goes. =)
