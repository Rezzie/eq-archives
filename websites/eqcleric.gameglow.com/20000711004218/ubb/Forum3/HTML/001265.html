1)What zone is the undead camp at? From what I gathered, it was one of the Karanas, South?

It's the undead camp directly south and a bit west of the stone bridge. It has broken-looking logs and stuff around and lots of putrid skeletons.

2)Does the live Grimrot spawn in the same place as the undead one?

Yes, the exact same spot. Several monsters spawn in the exact spot as well. The live and undead Grimmie can be stacked on top of each other, in fact.

3)If the answer to 2) is no, then where does he spawn?

4)I have seen differing posts on killing vs. not killing the live Grimrot. I would assume that you need to kill him, but do you need to kill them in a certain order?

Kill whichever one appears. Each will drop one item and you need both items.

By the way... yes, the zone is South Karana.

The posts about killing vs. not killing the Live Grimrot refer to the respawn time. If you need the part from the Live Grimmy, then it's perfectly legitimate to kill him. If you already have it, let him stand and despawn so that you have a reduced period of time to wait for the next PH or Grimrot to spawn. But there is no particular order in which they spawn... that I know of.
