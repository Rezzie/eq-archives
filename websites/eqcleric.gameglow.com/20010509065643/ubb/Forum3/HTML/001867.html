7 hour 5 minute spawn. There may also be a 7 hour 20 minute spawn that is the placeholder for the Lich of Miragul. Both placeholders are ice bone skeletons that wander. They seem to spawn at the T intersection near the house that has the Wizard rod in it on the floor. It is a long and boring camp, and it helps to get a timer and click it when you kill the placeholder. Making Shaman helms is a nice money maker though while you wait for the one with the Ingot.

Keep Invis versus Undead up in case the LIch of Miragul is alive and well. Also, consider any barbarians you see in there.
