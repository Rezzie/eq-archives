I camped for both Runed Circlets and my Mammoth cloak at level 30. ROOT EVERYTHING.

Once you get to the room, you have PLENTY of time between spawns to med and buff, so you can unload on each spawn. Root, nuke, root, nuke, root, nuke. If there are 2 gob's in the room, then root one after you kill the other, and leave it there for as long as possible. Try to break up the spawn so you have 10 minutes between each one instead of 20-30 minutes between each pair.

But the alchemist is a rare spawn and the cloak is the rare drop. It took me about a week to get my cloak. A week of green or zero exp.
