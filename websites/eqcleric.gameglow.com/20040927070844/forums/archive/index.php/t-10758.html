You can solo in the Grey at low 50s if you're careful. Just need to stay clear of the zonelines because people like to train, and you need to have an EB item. If you don't have an EB item, group up in the Grey for a bit until you can get 3 Shissar organs and you can turn that in for an EB neck. Though honestly I prefer grouping in Grey over soloing, since you can do both healing and nuking here.

ME, I don't recommend soloing in. Not enough undead, and too mean mobs.

Tenebrous Mountains are soloable, if you're careful. Vampyres on the north side.

Revanents in Scarlet Desert are also undead. Easy to pull from the west camp near the Grey. I haven't tried pulling from their fort in the North East corner though.

And the graveyard in Katta I'd recommend to L50 and below. Easy pulls, as you spawn each undead manually, and an unlimited number of pulls. The quest for the pieces are for a spear with AC, and it's not a very good one anyways. Clerics can't use it in any case, but the spear is tradable. http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1478
