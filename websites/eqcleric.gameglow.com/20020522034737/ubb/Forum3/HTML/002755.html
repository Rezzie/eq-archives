I am not a cleric. But, I have a very good friend that is one. I have been helping her camp RF when I can. Some things about the encounter have really rubbed em the wrong way. The spawn is set up in such a way to promote player conflict. And there is a lot of conflict. I have been chastized for mearly being in the zone with my friend....saying I am a KSer bla bla blah. Mind you RF wasn't even up.

This quest needs to be revised. I understand VI's point of view in that they want the epics entering the world at a certain rate and no more. So, here is what I would propose. Human RF is always up in SF. A cleric would hand him the item from Sol B RF. At this point one of two things would happen. One, RF would hand back the item from Sol B RF with an additional item. The additional item would be handed back to RF to spawn the final encounter. or two, he would say something to the effect of "I am sorry you are not ready. Come back at the next moon (30 EQ days or 36 hours) and I will tell you see if you are ready". The quest would be a random number generated reward. This would mean some clerics would get the first time and others not for a month or two. But what this would do is it would resolve the conflict. Yes, it would still be a pain but clerics could log in each day and check it before raiding or whatever.

My heart goes out to all those clerics doing the currently excruciating camp.

Please post any feedback you have on this idea.

Now this one is quite funny, it reminds me of going to Disneyland a couple of years ago for the first time after the speedpass thing was installed/instituted. So we'd get our pass saying your ragefire ticket is good from x to y 5-10-30+ days from now. Here is the problem with this.

There is a ENORMOUS flood of people on all servers with pearls (even swirling pearls) who haven't gotten ragefire. To get a valid estimate on ETA til YOUR ragefire for any given server (ie when your ticket would work.) With the sheer number of people at this step and their insistence of entering world about 1 every 5-6 days you could get something that said Your ragefire will be up in 290 days. Oh joy I now know I can't get my ragefire before almost a year passes anyway. No matter what I do am willing to do how patient I am etc....

The sad part is no matter where you move the bottleneck to without rewriting the entire quest you will just end up with a pile of people there camping and fighting over it, and if they move the bottleneck there will be a huge flood of cleric epics for a couple of weeks or more.

How much change would a server go through if all of a sudden the bottleneck was moved and that server had now another 300 epic clerics on it?

I think Rayne hit it on the head. They need to make another item have effect revivscience no matter it's weight/etc. The real reason most people want this item has more to do with its utility to a guild than with its utility to the cleric.

You don't see guilds pushing their necros to finish the necro epic...but you see clerics and enchanters pushed to finish theirs (especially clerics.) Without a cleric epic high level raids are either not doable (because of spawn times) or just plain no fun because of the length of time it takes to get a raid going again.

Basically having res sticks in your guild changes the raid game from unfun to fun or at least from unfun to tolerable.

I wouldn't mind them changing the bottleneck myself. I personally think the flood of epics would be interesting because it won't really change high end guild raiding to much (what high end raid guild doesnt have 4+ epic clerics on any given raid already.) What it would do is change the game immensely for those marginal guilds that can just barely raid now.

Nothing is going to make this quest any less painful unless they decide that they are willing to let more any 1.5 clerics get their epic in any given week.

I believe that they're reluctance is due to the effect. Let's get real here. If Ragefire turned into a 2 or 3 days spawn, getting your cleric epic would be really damn easy for a Level 51 cleric. Almost every 51+ cleric would be able to cast the 56 Rez. If the cleric epic effect was the 49 rez, would Verant care if most clerics had an epic? Probably not.

Personally, I always liked the idea of extending the quest. Several classes have intermediate weapons, why not clerics... Remove the Ragefire bottleneck, but make the reward for getting to that stage of the quest an intermediate weapon. Maybe the epic with the 49 rez...

Then extend the quest so that you can upgrade your epic to the 56 rez. For example, add 4 mobs each on a 3 to 4 day timer that you need to kill. Or make the cleric collect the heart of each Kunark Dragon. Or maybe, once you reach level 56, you can just turn in your epic for an upgraded epic.

Or maybe you change the quest so that the Sol Ragefire turns KOS when you hand in your pearl and requires 2 or 3 groups to kill. Killing him results in the Heart... Turning in the heart results in the gimped epic. If you want to upgrade your epic, camp the Skyfire Ragefire.

This all begs the question though. If your guild had a 56+ cleric who had a 49 Rez clicker, would you 56 rez the raid or 49 click the raid?
