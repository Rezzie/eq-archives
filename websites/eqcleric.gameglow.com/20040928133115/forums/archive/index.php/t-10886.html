Back when RoK was still brand new, I went on a dungeon crawl of Chardok with 5 of my closest and most skilled friends. We fought top to bottom and had only one death. It's one of the coolest looking dungeons in the game once you get down past the Castle area.

They could kill Woushi, and polish up their dragon encounter skills, or pull the dragons in the TOV valley of Western Wastes(they drop some pretty decent talismans).
