Hi, I have been soloing Frontier Mtns through 35 to 37. The gob casters are nasty and should be avoided, You should be able to solo most of the badgers forgot thier name.Gaints and brutes are ok too the gaints have a lot of hp but you can kill them.
My other hunting ground is Dalnir in the Warsilk woods. It is a little hard to find but worth the look. The first floor will yake you safely through 36. Just pull to the zone room, most mobs will be exp green to blue. One of the nice drops there is the Sap of Piety damage 13 1HB. There is a high lvl necklace at the bottom so you can find a few 50+ chacters there most of the time camping it. Makes getting a res possible http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif
You should also be able to find a partener pretty easy even during the off hours. That is in both locations. If you hunt Frontier be careful about medding there are a LOT of walkers and you want have much time to set before something comes by to fight. Best bet there is bind near a zone line and gate back to med, I know this doesn't sound good but getting jumped while medding hurts more. If you can get a range you are golden.
Good Luck
