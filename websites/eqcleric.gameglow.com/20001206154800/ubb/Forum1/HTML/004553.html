As most clerics hunting in Sebilis know, it is common habit in a pick up group to give all the money loots to the cleric, to help cover for reagent costs.

Once however, after about 1.5 hours of grouping, and after the cleric looted about 8 or 9 money loot (we got really lucky hehe:P) the rogue in our group looted a money loot. The cleric then asked him to give to him, and the rogue went extremly mad. I didn't speak out against the cleric, but the cleric had just casted about 12 naltrons, coming to an expense of 250 pp (why he used naltron on casters in first floor of sebilis just as an excuse to loot money loots I suspect) and had looted about 900 pp worth of gems/jewels etch. I am of the firm opinion that clerics shouldn't pay for the reagents (I always carry 40 jaspers, and magicians don't use jaspers) for my clerics, but I think that a cleric who insisists on having all the money loots handed to him is just being a greedy ass.

In an idea world, everyone should carry enough gems to cover their own symbol costs. I think that if the cleric is providing the cost of gems, he should be given enough money loots to cover the cost of gems, and then the remaining amount should be divided among the group.

What do you clerics think about this?

As a lvl 57 cleric I carry 9 stacks of peridots and 1 stack of jaspers (I'm not sure why I carry the jaspers since I've not used any in like 6 months but anyway).

On average I go through 8-9 stacks in a week easily.

Now if I'm hunting in OS I expect to get most if not all of the gem loot. Some may think this is greedy but let me explain the reasoning behind it.

OS is what I call a money item heavy zone. Lots of money items (gems/jewellery) drops here.. which is great.. but I don't hunt in OS ALL the time.. I hunt in Chardok, in Fear, on raids on Trak, Tal and other dragon raids, I hunt in HS.. Most if not all of these zone drop less to none money loot.

Now when raiding in these zones I'm still using up 6 peridots per hour (or more if the tanks are getting dispelled).

Yes I may look like I'm being greedy when I've cast 250pps worth of buffs but looted 1K plus worth of gems.. but what about all those other raids where I'm not getting any gems back.. those gems are coming out of my own cash.. my pocket.. to keep you alive..

Yes with 58 and Naltrons Mark the money situation should ease a lot but until then clerics are going to need to pay for these gems somehow.

Just a little something to think about before judging your cleric..

Jaspers for Symbol of Pinzarn cost me close to 9gp each. Like many clerics pre 39, I don't have a lot of money for reasons listed in previous posts.

I always buff myself with the Symbol, and make sure its at least the 3rd or 4th buff in my list to avoid dispels.

I ASK the other members of the group if they would like me to cast it on them as well and tell them the spell requires a gem that costs 9gp. I tell them what the spell does (adds approx 300 hp for 45 minutes) and let it be their choice.

In a 3 hour gaming session in a full group, that adds up to close to 20pp. (I'm not including myself in the equation) I'm not looking to make money on the deal, just to recoup some of the cost.

Add this to the fact that casters in general get screwed on loots, and if you don't charge for the cast, you're not coming out very far ahead of the game.

If I'm playing one of my Warriors or my Shadow Knight, I either pay the cleric for the gem used, or gives the cleric gems of the appropriate kind for compensation.

Its easy to be generous when you're rich. I'm trying to scrounge up enough money to buy my level 39 spells.

I follow exact same rule as Salina, with few modifications:
