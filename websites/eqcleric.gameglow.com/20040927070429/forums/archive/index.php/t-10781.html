Karnor and Skyfire are the only places I've found for casual exp. And once you bum a sow and know the route they're only 10 minutes or so apart.

CoM had too many greens at 52. And it's hard to just casually hunt reavers. I suppose you could pick on Goos.

Chardock and Sebilis are hard to just zone into and shout for a group.

At 55 I've been hanging out in Skyfire a lot lately. Once you get the pulling down it's easy to get singles to snare and fear. Snare/fear on a single mob is just relaxing compared to ROK dungeon pulls. And there are usually only a few people in the zone so you also get a break from Karnor's constant trains and res requests. And we get a spell drop now and then. Two nights ago a kind Skycinder Drake dropped Yaulp 4.
