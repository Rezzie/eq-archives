The problem with creating white plate armor is that they can only apply a tint to existing armor to change the color. So, untinted plate armor is the silver plate mail that guildmasters and others wear (the steel plate you buy from vendors).

There is no white tinting, white is just the absence of any other tinting, so silver/steel is it.
