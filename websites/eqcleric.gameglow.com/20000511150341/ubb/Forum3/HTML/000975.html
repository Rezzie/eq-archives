The fish trick is simple. Have a friend create a monk character that can start in qeynos. Have the monk meet you at the temple of life near the pool. Go to the side away from where the paladin guard stands and bind him there. Then disband the group, buff the monk and have the monk jump into the water and kill the kola fish 1 at a time. After the mule monk has killed the fish, you jump into the water and loot the corpse. Take the fish you get from the corpse and give it to the paladin and you get huge faction, a little bit of cash and sometimes an item. On my first fish I turned in I got the band of Rodcet Nife (sp?) +3 int +10 mana ring. Kinda nice for low level int casters.

The 2nd fish I turned in, I got prayer beads - item with 2 charges of healing.
