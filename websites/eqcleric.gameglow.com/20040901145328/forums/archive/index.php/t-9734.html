Greetings!

Like most in their teens/twenties who value wisdom, I decided to recover the fabled Testament of Vanear. I do not camp things, so whenever I pass through Highhold I check on the corrupt guard to get the final missing page. The four times I have seen him, there was a line of wolf-formed druids waiting to pounce upon him. So be it.

This past weekend, however, the server was down due to a power outage. I logged on with fewer than forty people in all of Norrath, and woke up in Rivervale. This was my chance! Cloaking myself from undead eyes, I ran through Kithicor and dragged several orcs to the guards in Highpass Hold. A quick swim and I was standing before Dyllin himself, with no other players in the zone.

Although he conned green to me, I had heard that the other smugglers would come to his aid. So I buffed up and followed him a bit until he was apart from the others. A quick smite, and I started swinging.

Then I see another cleric run into the smugglers cave from outside. I greeted him, and was shocked to see him cast twice against Dyllin. A few more thumps of my mace and he was dead, but I was unable to loot the corpse.

I was as mad as I have ever been in my clerical career. When I asked if he had seen me fighting Dyllin, his only reply was "Sorry, I need the page as well." The priest was anonymous, so I did not see his level, but he conned red to me, and was wearing a Bloodstained Tunic and carrying a Crested Mistmoore shield.

Kill stealed by another cleric -- Grrrrr!
