Ping Fuzzlecutter is a Merchant that sells hair-care products (no joke. hail him) just outside the West gates in West Freeport.

His NPC level was tagged as 63, and therefore RED CON to anyone, even level 50 players.

Ping Fuzzlecutter used to hit for single digits, and teens. Therefore making you able to raise your defense skill without any risk. (Since he was a con higher than blue, it would raise your defense skill while he beat on you)

So, Verant and their Vision™ states: "No risk, no donut!" - So, they took out the "easy" part, and added in the risk.

Now, if you attack Ping, he hits for 120s, and bashes for 60+.
