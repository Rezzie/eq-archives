Couple things:

Hall of testing AE's like "Rain of Molten Lava" is a Cold Check in the spdat.

300+ resistances only seam to improve resist rates versus lure spells. 200 MR and I resist 95% of old style dragon fears. 200 MR and I resist 40% of new style dragon fears. The difference is lure. My theory is Lure just treats your resist as if it where lower, and removes the level check. There are different levels of lure, shown in the spdat by the -cha line. A -5 cha is a medium lure, some get as high as -9 cha. A -5 cha is would treat 200 MR as if it where 100 from my estimates. So I would recomend about 300 resistance vs any lure spell.

In VP 200 MR is very important against mobs like Druusk and Phara Dar. Druusk's AE will eat you alive if your not careful and have low resist. Phara Dar will stop you from casting at all if you have too low of MR.

But as a general rule I shoot for 200 of the given resist in VP, and its kept me alive quite well.
