I haven't really seen this mentioned on here before but a GREAT spot I've been finding recently is camping at the POF Portal in Feerott..

Here there is a large open room, you can get sow etc if you are with a druid... On the portal spawns Cyndreela a level 40 necromancer, with a blue pet (if she has time to get it up that is before we kill her)... and right behind you a cave stocked with spectres...

Last night I brought a bunch of guild mates and we had a blast killing specs and Cyndreela.. however for best XP do it with a partner until 45 and then solo her after 45... She drops several platinum pieces each time as well I picked up a pearl bracelet off her that sold to a merchant for 65pp and I've heard she drops an 85pp ring as well from time to time..

To reach her enter the spectre cave (you may have to clear most of the specs first) and follow the right hand wall all the way down to you see a dead end. This is a fake wall, run though this wall and you enter the portal area. Large open room, some annoying drybones spawn here too, easy to clear...

So all in all a good, fairly safe spot.. Oh, and if you are under level 46 and run though the portal it teleports you near Ogguk, I never ran into any bouncers when I tried, but you might want to be careful.. so if you are getting beat, run through the portal http://www.kmrcs.com/eqcleric/ultbbs/ubb/smile.gif

Have fun!
