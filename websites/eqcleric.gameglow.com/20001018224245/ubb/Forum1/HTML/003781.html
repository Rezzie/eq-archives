I too have been trying to max my defense. It makes a huge difference in AC (I've raised my AC by about 20 in the last week without changing my equipment - not a bad deal at all.) I do get beat on in combat - clerics can't avoid that, especially in dungeons, and it seems the higher level I get the more I take the occasional hit.

A warrior friend says that all his combat skills slowed way down at about 175 and got even slower the closer he got to 200. The last 10 points, he said, took ages to get - and this from someone who gets beat on for a living. Also, a rogue acquaintance informs me that dexterity does affect the rate of combat skill gain, just as intelligence affects the rate of other skills. Not sure how true this is.

Maybe it's just me, but some creatures seem to raise combat skills faster than others. I first noticed this in the teens, whacking crocodiles in Oasis. I got skill ticks all the time when I was there, something that wasn't happening with the other stuff I was soloing.

I've made two efforts late in life to improve my combat skills. I had good luck in the low 40's with seafury cyclops. I was attempting to max my 1H blunt at the time, but I did manage to get some defense as well. This was in a party that was killing them so decisively that they seldom needed healing, so I figured I might as well melee a bit. At level 50 I teamed up with some friends to work on defense. A warrior buddy was pulling bloodgill goblins for a lower level team, and I'd "borrow" the goblins that were blue to me. I rooted them away from the others and let them beat on me for a while. They hit for about 75 max, and I was grouped with a bard who was twisting Cantana and another regen song, so I'd be able to go for quite some time before I handed the goblin back over to the others to be killed. I think I gained 6 points that night.

So yes it's slow, but repeated dedicated effort should pay off. (Only 11 more points to go here. )

Greetings!

AGI is tied to defensive skill gains (Defense, Dodge, Parry, Riposte). DEX is tied to offensive skill gains (Offense, weapon skills, Bash, Kick).

When working on defensive training I swapped out WIS gear for AGI gear (BP necklace, serpentine bracers, etc) and got shaman and enchanter stacked AGI buffs. With an AGI over 200 I saw dozens of skill increases per hour...
