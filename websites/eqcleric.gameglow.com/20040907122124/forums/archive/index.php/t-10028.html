Originally posted by Windfire
Why did they change the cleric epic? Here i do agree you got raped on the epic end of things.

As I heard it, the main reason for the change was that when someone handed in their pearl and someone else killed Rage and looted the heart, the cleric that handed in thier Pearl lost all their epic progress and had to start over (or /petition like mad to get the pearl recreated and given to them)

It also moved Ragefire to a location that was more accessable to people, so that you could get troops in place sooner and not lost him to a Nagafen pop (as happened more than once)

Now, they originally said that the long spawn times were a bug and that the Skyfire Ragefire should pop with the same frequency as the one in Nagafen's lair. However, they eventually admitted that the spawn times were set by design. They eventually threw us the bone of having one pop about 1 day after the zone was reset, but after that, the spawn can take as much as 8 days and couple of hours (by one recent report).

The no-loss-of-a-Pearl was good for the cleric, the move to Skyfire can been seen as good or bad, the change in the spawn time can only be seen as bad.

If they were counting on Nagafen pops to eat some Ragefires, and moving him removed that chance, then I can see why they extended the spawn (keeping the numbers of cleric epic's down is a primary goal of theirs). But clerics are NOT being treated equally to other classes - NO other class has a fixed ~1 week delay between epics.
