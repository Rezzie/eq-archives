Well, I haven't quite hit the cost of ~12pp per cast, but the way I think of things is this way.

I usually group with 1 main tank (warrior), 1 backup tank (bard, ranger), nuker (wiz/mage/druid/shaman), enchanter, and cleric.

When breaking spawn everyone gets full buffs with endures as appropriate, no matter what they may want. I tell the druid to never do their skin, as bravery (at the moment) is better, and I'd rather have it on them. If possible I also have everyone Runed, and Talismaned by the shaman.

That ensures that A) I don't have to worry too much about calling evac the moment we run into big nasties, B) It gives me enough time to finish off that gheal on the nuker and stun whatever the heck is hitting said nuker so the chanter can get the mez off, or C) to save my very bestest best friend, the Chanter, from getting hurt too much.

Same goes for once broken, however I only do the symbol for FREE on the pure casters, and the druid can do sls or whatever if they feel like. If I join a pickup group then I politely let them know that I'm always taking donations for symbol and a couple plat will ensure that I have the said stones to cast it. Never have I once heard of any tank thats worth the armor they're wearing complain about paying me for that. Nor casters at that.

I attribute it to the fact that paying for a little 6gp (soon to get worse I know) stone to save the trouble of them dying from lack of hitpoints is heavily worth it to people.

Occassionally I just toss random buffs out, and if I'm really in the mood I'll toss out the symbols.

Except, when someone says, out of nowhere, "buff me." No please, no excuse me ma'am, but just "buff me." Time to flip to page fifty in the trusty spellbook and pick out the low lvl stuff =)
