If you are having problems getting a group, here's a real good hint...

GET THE HELL OUT OF SEBILIS AND VELK'S!!

That's right. Sebilis is a cash cow, and clerics know it. So at any given time, there are no less than 5 clerics LFG in Sebilis. Sometimes as many as 20.

Velk's Lab has the other problem, Soloing Melee classes. At any given time, there are 5-8 Monks, 3-4 Warriors and even a couple Paladins and Shadow Knights SOLOING in Velk's Lab. About the only GROUPS in Velk's anymore are Upper and Lower dogs and Upper Spiders (Frenzy, whatever your server calls it). If you aren't in one of those 3 groups, good luck finding something to kill.

Now, as for other EXP zones...

Chardock is mostly light blue at 60, so you gotta go towards the Royal's area to get good exp. And Chardock is usually full of soloing shamans and AoE groups.

Fungus Grove is not a good place to go either, if there ARE any clerics there, they are 2-boxed. A group would rether have a shaman or druids here than a cleric.

Maiden's Eye has it's own issues. Most notably, it's BORING.

I haven't yet gone to the revamped Cazic Thule, but from what I hear, my server's top 5-6 guilds monopolize that zone and getting a group in CT is impossible if you aren't in one of those guilds.

My advice, is do what I do. Get together a group of guildmates or friends, and go to Umbral Plains or Ssra Temple. UP is usually a good choice since there is never more than 2 groups there, and there are ALOT of MOBs. Either Ganthru's pull to the zone in, or go to that "safe" valley past the castle and pull Elysian Remains. EXP simply flows there.
