Pre-Kurnak i set my 50th level cleric aside for the most part. I created a dwarf paladin and leveled him up to a decent level then join Clan Axepeak.

Clan Axepeak (an old NWN guild actually) is composed almost entirely of dwarves (there is a gnome and a halfling in the EQ ranks). We are in-character and RPing 99% of the time (even our web page/boards are RP). This was quite a change for me i must say. It brought a whole new challenge to the game for me (and so much more fun). When Kurnak came out i was fixing to hit 30 and was eager to get over there as the normal places i would be heading to would be the same-ol-same-ol. I quickly got to 38th.

One thing about Clan Axepeak is leveling and aquiring items is at the bottom of the list of priorities (drinking and torsting being at the top (torst=axepeak for adventuring with clan members). This was my 4th character of 30th or better level while many of my mates were playing their one and only characters. Only one member right now is planes capable. So i found that i was outpacing my new friends.

I would log on Badaf every now and then and look around for a pickup group. Went to Chardok once and had a blast. But was hard to find anything. So i made some more characters

Made a wood elf bard and got him to 12th in short order. Fulling twinked in all-steel armor hes a sight to behold indeed. Hes actually fun to roleplay (and challenging).

Also right at the start of RoK i made an Iskar necro to check out some of the new surroundings. Got him to 7th before i made my bard. Well, the other day, on a whim, i got him to 8th just to check out some of the next rank of spells. WOW! I was amazed at the difference. You cast this spell on you called Dark Pact and it trades your hps for mana. Well guess what? Iskar have troll-like regen naturally! So, in effect, i have constant clarity! Anyway, hes 10th now and is exploring Kurn's tower. Very fun!

Last night a Clan member died and so i logged on Badaf and ressed her (yes a REAL her. Our female members play females dwarves and males play males. hehe). After receiving some hugs i decided to run Badaf over to Karnors and see whats shaking, since i havent seen it yet.

First there was multiple groups in desperate need of a healer (So THIS is one of the places where everyone has been). I see many, many old friends there as well. It brought back alot of memories (mostly good but a few chumps were still around http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif). I joined a pickup group and commenced to shaking the rust of my etheral mist. Had alot of fun i must say. Big trains, close battles, etc. Well bed time rolled around and i hit my camp button. Then i stopped. I hadnt checked my exps all night (seems i dont do very often like a use to). I MIGHT be half a bubble into 51 but i didnt really care when i reflected back on the fun i had.

I will probably play my 51 cleric more than i have been. Some of my clan mates can catch up to my paladin (although some seem to have caught a touch of level fever lately http://eqcleric.gameglow.com/ubb/smile.gif). My first friend from Axepeak i met (Dhorwyn (cleric) in Unrest) is 30th now. PLus i can always jump on my bard or necro if i need soem exp gratification. Heck i even re-outfitted my 2 stripped warriors (27th level DE and 31st level ogre) and will probably play them some to.
