Etched Ivy Tunic - AC 19, +3 STR, +3 WIS, +30 HP

The tunic is an AWESOME item! Get it, keep it, treasure it... it's the third best tunic in the game for a ranger, after the brigadine tunic and thorny vine.

FIRST TUNIC COMPONENT: Skull of Meldrath, from Meldrath in Steamfont Mountains

Map of Steamfont:

http://www.everlore.com/geography/default.asp?mapID=1568

Map of Mino Caves:

http://www.3dnews.net/work/minocavespath.jpg

OK. Run on down to Steamfont, which is accessed through lesser faydark. The first map will get you to the mino caves easily, and the second will get you through the caves pretty easily. It ain't pretty, but I added some annotations that may be helpful.

Basically, you get your party and sit outside of the caves. Your puller should be the highest level character with the most magic resistance, and he should be SoW'd and invised. The puller runs into the caves, following the blue arrows, all the way to the end, when you can barely see the first mino guard. Then, he runs in and tags one of the guards and hightails it out. USUALLY, one or both of the guards will make it out with him... if you get XP from the guards (low 30's in level) kill 'em. Otherwise, run them to the windmills and let the gnome NPC's kill them.

After the guards are dead, the puller must IMMEDIATELY run back in (the minos spawn FAST), tag Meldrath, and basically walk him all the way back to the group... Meldrath stops to cast spells on you, so often he'll get left behind if you use SoW. After your party unloads on Meldrath and he dies, loot your skull and immediately med up for the next one. In fact, he should be respawned already. Rinse and repeat.

Some common mistakes which have killed level 45+ parties doing this:

1) The puller must be invisible. If he isn't, Meldrath and his guards (who can see through walls, apparently) will go aggro immediately and you're screwed.

2) If the little gnome merchant is around, DO NOT PULL UNTIL SHE LEAVES. She'll join in when you attack meldrath, and she's like level 45.

3) I know you're all wearing PoF armor and wielding Ykeshas and dragonspine claws, but do NOT rush meldrath. At least one of your people will invariably die. Take it slow.

SECOND TUNIC COMPONENT: Rune of Ivy from Thistle Underbrush, brownie extraordinaire

Some things to keep in mind:

1) The "brownie outcast" is NOT thistle's placeholder.

2) Thistle does not have a placeholder, his spawn is totally random.

3) There's no point to go around killing stuff in the zone at random, it doesn't help Thistle spawn. Then again, it doesn't hurt anything either.

4) If you're level 30 or lower, you need at least two rangers to cover the entire zone with your tracking skill. Set up at opposite ends of the zone.

This one is easy. You sit there and you hit your tracking button until Thistle comes up. When he does, you kill him. The problem is that his spawn is REALLY random... between 3 per hour and one every five hours. Hopefully you'll be lucky. Once Thistle does come up on radar, run to him, snare him, and start attacking. He isn't really hard (level 28, maybe?) but he DOES heal himself, and he does cast druid spells. If you happened to kill the brownie outcast, be assured that Thistle is much easier. You won't be able to solo him until your mid to late 30's at the earliest.
