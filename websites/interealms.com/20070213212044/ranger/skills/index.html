Attributes:

Statistics: Click to return to top

Strength:
Makes you hit more often for maximum damage. Weight allowance is directly tied to strength

Stamina:
Gives a few extra HP per level. Also allows you to fight longer and swim longer without getting tired.

Agility:
Helps defensive skills such as parry and riposte (theory) and slightly improves AC.

Dexterity:
This skill relates to how often a special weapon effect will trigger and how fast you learn combat skills.

Wisdom:
Determines mana store. Also improves the rate of advancing your noncombat skills and factors in skill success, if higher than your intelligence.

Intelligence:
Improves the rate of advancing your noncombat skills and factors in skill success, if higher than your wisdom.

Charisma:
Helps overcome resistance when "effect" style spells such as debuffs or charms are used by a critter. Also improves faction with NPCs and helps improve prices with vendors.
