Rangers Guide to Cazic-Thule

Editors note: Rubicite is no longer dropped in Cazic

After a ranger has passed the beginning levels and learned his strengths and weaknesses, it is time to go and adventure. One of the best places I have found to gain experience and loot is Cazic-Thule.

Cazic is located in the zone of Feerot, which can be accessed from the Rathe Mountains or Innothule Swamp. Cazic is on the South wall, approximately in the middle of the zone. Feerot has a few dangers you should be aware of. Firstly, Ogre Bouncers.. high level (40ish) ogres that walk around the zone. Occasionally, one will chase someone into the entrance to Cazic, causing a bit of a problem. Secondly, there is a spectre cave to the west of Cazic and these will occasionally be trained to the entrance as well.

Cazic is home to a race of lizardmen, and the only place in Norrath to acquire the much sought after Rubicite armor. There are several types of these lizards, varying greatly in difficulty. Cazic lizards are very social and will attack anyone, they also come in groups and will help out any of their kind that are in trouble. Note that your "con" is very misleading in Cazic because the lizards have A LOT of hitpoints, hit VERY hard, and will beat you to death before you can finish them off.

There are MANY trains in Cazic so resting by the zone is not a very wise choice unless you are invisible. Most people choose to rest in the courtyard where they can stay away from any train that is pulled to the entrance. Please note that lizard levels I give are approximate, there is a fairly wide level range for several of the lizards in Cazic.

COMMON SPAWNS:

Near the zone you will find Broodlings and Guards, which are great for mid-teen levels.

All across the zone you will find warders, proselytes, and disciples, these are lower level casters who can heal, will cast offensive spells and are very hard to kill as they heal themselves and any lizards around them. It is effective to use stun spells on these and all other caster classes. My advice is to take out any casters before you engage the melee class, as the casters will keep healing them anyway.

Next are the defenders and sentinels. These are melee fighters that hit very hard (up to 60 for the highest levels). I began to solo defenders in the mid-twenties and got beat up pretty badly. My level is now 35 and these are mainly green to me and still attack me on sight.

Lizard pages, justicars and judicator are the next casting types of lizards. These hit hard, cast some very powerful direct damage spells and have some impressive healing abilities. Again, these should be dealt with first in a fight. They range from 24-32 or so and will heal other lizards even if they are engaged in another fight.

Some of the toughest melee fighters in Cazic, listed in ascending order of difficulty, are protectors, fanatics and zealots. I began soloing protectors at level 33 and can just barely take a fanatic at 35 (if I am lucky). I have seen zealots hit for up to 74 points of damage and are very hard to bring down (they are about L33-34).

There are also three alligator pits in the zone that can be pulled from. These gators range from 24-33 or so. They bite VERY hard and have a tendency to swarm one person. Extreme caution should be used when fighting these. They drop no loot, but give very good experience.

UNIQUE SPAWNS:

There are many unique monsters in Cazic that drop sought after items. It is not my intention to make this a spoiler, but I will give you some names and locations. These items are fairly heavily camped and rare drops. You will need a group to get them and be at least L25+ to stand a chance against these mobs.

In the maze occasionally a Clay Golem will spawn. This is very hard to get to because of its spawn location, but if you kill it you will get either a golem potion (which turns you into an elemental for a while) or a lizardskin tribal mask.

In the throne room you will find the Tae Ew Templar and Tae Ew Diviner. These drop Rubicite pieces rarely and lizardscale items commonly. The Stone Golem also spawns in this area. It sometimes carries another piece of Rubicite.

Through he maze you will find a temple with fairly high level lizards. At the top spawns the Tae Ew Archon which also drops Rubicite or a lizardscale piece.

Down in the sewers you will find some alligator pits and the rare Steel Golem. This golem carries another Rubi piece or a rod that will cancel magic. When the golem does not spawn a placeholder Lizard Champion does. This lizard is level 35 and very hard to bring down.

In another part of the sewers you will find the Avatar of Fear room. This room spawns the two toughest (in my opinion) monsters in the zone. The Cazic Cenobyte spawns here. This Lizard drops another Rubi piece. Last but not least the Avatar of Fear spawns in this room. This beast is about level 38-39 and hits for 89 points. It is very resistant to magic and very quick, you will need a powerful group indeed to bring it down. The Avatar drops either the Thulian Bladed Claws or the Rubi Breastplate.

SUMMARY:

I hope that this guide will help you as you enter Cazic. It is really worthwhile to visit this dungeon as you can gain a lot of experience and loot here (I forgot to mention that many lizards carry fine steel weapons and quite a bit of cash). Make sure that you get bound in Freeport and it is a great idea to get on good terms with some L29+ clerics for resurrections. There are many places in the zone to hunt for experience and loot besides the heavily camped areas. Search one out and you can do very well.

One final note. ALWAYS, ALWAYS, ALWAYS, snare whatever you are fighting or it will come back with several nasty friends. For those of you with Ebony blades, be very careful. Right now the blade is bugged and you might not be able to re-snare the lizard if it tries to run. This has been the death of many a good Ranger (and group) in Cazic.

Good hunting and stay safe.
