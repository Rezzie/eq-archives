Etched Ivy Helm - AC 12, +5 Wisdom, +5 SV Disease

FIRST HELM COMPONENT: Top of rune of ivy, from deathly usher in Mistmoore

http://www.majestic-worlds.com/mistmoore/western_grounds_closeup.jpg

This is the best map out there for Mistmoore... it should be pretty easy to follow. Number one rule for Mistmoore: unless you're actually fighting, keep camo up. Always. Number two rule for Mistmoore: NEVER let a runner escape. Snare is your friend. Now, the Deathly Ushers *only* spawn inside the castle... but the castle is unbelievably trainy and just a general pain to go in the front door. So, we use the side entrance... voila, the illusionary wall in the back of the graveyard. You can see it on the map, it's a wavy line below the crypt.

So in the room marked "Jail", there are three doors, opened by the switch (which looks like a door) marked "2". First thing you do, is open that switch WITH CAMO ON, and see what you got. If one of em is a deathly usher, great. Harmony the rest and pull it. If not, you can either kill whatever's there for XP (no XP for me) or you can check behind secret wall number two, on the south wall. There should be stairs leading up.... this is incidentally where the Avenging Catliff spawns.... but if he isn't there (and he won't be) you can see if his placeholder is an Usher.

Now is the hard part... you see Mistmoore works differently than other zones. EVERYTHING actually *despawns* (32K's themself) every 30 minutes, unless you're actively fighting it. So, if you sit back and relax, in 30 minutes every spawn will be different.

While waiting for an usher to spawn in the jail area, a nice place to rest is underneath the trap. Basically, walk into the crypt to either side of the coffin, and fall down. The way out is obvious, and there's the second illusionary wall. It took me less than one spawn cycle to get the rune, the usher upstairs (catliff spot) had it.

SECOND HELM COMPONENT: Cyclops Charm, from Tarskuk in Rathe Mountains

http://www2s.biglobe.ne.jp/~hakata/eq/image/rathemountain/mape.htm

This map is the best for rathe mountains. Refer to it, let it become your friend. Basically, to kill this cyclops you need to either be level 39 and have SoW or own a pair of JBoots. Turskuk spawns pretty much where the map is labeled "cycropse" and once he's spawned he tends to STAY spawned... you can kill him four or more times in a row, and he'll instantly respawn after each time.

To kill Turskuk solo, you need to rangerkite him. SoW and levitate yourself, equip your bow and at least 3 full backpacks of arrows, snare him, and start plinking away. If you don't snare, he WILL kick your butt, since he runs faster than SoW. Eventually he'll die, and in my experience he ALWAYS has the cyclops charm.
