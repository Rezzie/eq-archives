Attributes (stats)
top
Attribute

Effect on Ranger

Strength Makes you hit more often for maximum damage. Weight allowance is directly tied to your strength.
Dexterity Increases the ability to hit. This skill also relates to how often a special weapon effect will trigger and how fast you learn combat skills.
Agility Helps defensive skills such as parry and riposte and improves AC.
Charisma Helps overcome resistance when "effect" style spells such as debuffs or charms are used by a critter. Also improves faction with NPCs and helps improve prices with vendors.
Stamina Gives a few extra HP per level. Also allows you to fight longer and swim longer without getting tired.
Intelligence Improves the rate of advancing your casting skills.
Wisdom Determines mana store. Also factors in a successful trade creation, i.e. fletching arrows.
