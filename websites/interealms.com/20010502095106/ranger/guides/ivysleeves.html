Etched Ivy Sleeves - AC 10, +4 STR

FIRST SLEEVES COMPONENT: Left rune of ivy, from Griffon Grimfeather in N.Karana

This is another one where you MUST be level 39+ to solo. Griffon Grimfeather, much like Tarskuk the cyclops, runs faster than SoW, and apparently even faster than a level 40 bard's accelerando song! He is also immune to normal weapons, so you need magic arrows.

The solo route: get lots (I mean LOTS) of summoned arrows from a magician. SoW up, levitate up, snare Griffon, and start plinking with arrows. Repeat until dead.

The group route: get a whole bunch (at least 4 or 5) level 35+ people, and attack. You will need a healer, you can't all be rangers. Griffon Grimfeather spawns at (-1400, -550). He is also a rare spawn, but not nearly as random as Thistle. You'll see at least one an hour.

SECOND SLEEVES COMPONENT: Heart of fire from an inferno gob wizard in Solusek A

http://165.90.193.58/images/soluseka.jpg

Above is the best map of Solusek A available.

You will need four or five people to do this at level 30, and two or three (one a healer) at level 40 plus. A level 50 ranger wearing FULL PoF gear and wielding dual ykeshas almost died trying to do it solo... so don't try.

Everything is green, but they all have damage shields and hit hard, and they're ALL aggro. Our object is to get to the "bartender room" and camp in there. Several inferno goblin wizards spawn in that room and the rooms adjacent to it. The map is excellent, follow it, get to the room, and set up camp. I got my heart on the second wizard we killed, but one other ranger had been there for two hours with no luck.
