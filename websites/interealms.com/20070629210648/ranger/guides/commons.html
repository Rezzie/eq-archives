West and East Commons (Bind in Freeport)

Level 9 to 11, East commons:

Not much to worry about except the very occasional griffon floating by, but they can easily be avoided. I have not had one attack me yet in any of the commons area. There are things to hunt here if you are lower level or don't have a magic weapon. Watch out for grouping with non ranger/druids as the wild life (wolves, big cats) will atack them on sight and as a ranger I don't even like to kill the big cats.

Level 11 and above, West commons: The wisps are more plentiful here but this zone is much more dangerous, however, experience and money will flow from this zone and can be done if careful. Things to watch for in this zone:

1) Hill Giants- don't think I need to say more here.

2) 2 wandering Drow elf MOBS, Big boys, 1 necro that tends to stay by the point they call the toll booth, right behind the inn. Watch your tracking for Kizdean Gix or Gix's pet. The other one is a Drow elf SK called Dragoon Zytl, watch out for this one as he wanders, although he tends to be by the lake. Both will knock you silly in no time flat.

3) Griffons- see hill giant note

4) Wildlife, same note as East commons, they will attack non rangers, full fledged adult kodiaks will attack anybody and do not con amiably to me.

5) "floating" wisps: If you see a wisp that looks like it hanging in the air (it will be about chest/head height) don't attack it. This will be a stack of three to 5 wisps (by the way these stacks mark the spawn points.) Wait until the stack breaks and they spread, follow one for a minute then attack. If you attack them while they are stacked or when they are to close together you will get them all attacking you.

Hunting in this zone:

1) WISPS - first and foremost, good experience at least to 15th and good loot (light stones and greater light stones.) Some light stones will be burned out. My partner and I usually make 15plat apiece for a couple of hours work.

2) Undead: zombies, skels, and mummies roam the area. By the way, the skeletons here are very small compared to steamfont skels)

3) Giant spiders, weak poison and good experience from 10th to 12th.

4) Big cats if you don't mind killing them, and I really hope that my fellow rangers are not hunting the young kodiaks and wolves that roam the area.

5) There is a dervish camp here for larger groups of 13+ levels.

Some other Items of note, there are pirannah in the lake, The guards will help if you get in trouble but not against the DE in the area or the griffons. There are ruins in the center of the zone that a bunch of druids stand around and they will attack the griffons.
