Rules

I know I said that all sites get added, so why are their rules for getting your site into the ring? Well, I just wanted to outline a few obvious things. First, if you want to get your site included, you must have a completed site up and running. I've had a few sites put into the queue in the past that were far from being complete. Second, I ask that you put the navigation HTML that I send you in the email, or if you don't want to put it somewhere on your site because you believe it will cramp your style, please add a link back to this site in your links section or somewhere. and remember, you can put the navigation HTML, or image map anywhere on your site, you can even have it on a separate page of it's own. Just make sure you let people know. If you refuse to put the HTML or the button (see link below) on your page, you are defeating the purpose of this ring and your site will be removed. That is all I ask for this great service. Thanks all!
