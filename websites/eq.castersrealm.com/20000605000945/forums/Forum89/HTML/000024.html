How we broke Azarack isle on Brell:

30 people, half tanks and about 6 clerics.
Buff up, invis, port over. The area we call the "safe spot" (which is by no means safe) is along the left ledge and straight ahead some when you zone over. Everyone huddles together and you have a warrior break invis. There you wait for 1 or 2 imps to start pounding on ya. Being a cleric I dont know what all spells the pure casters were using besides using runes on the tanks. After we killed what ended up to be 3 I think, we invised up, medded, ressed the dead, invised again and rested up. Break invis again, rinse repeat. I think there are 11 or so azaracks and the stationary Protector of Sky. Enjoy!
