Try Seafury homers in the OOT.

These are about level 43 and give good xp even at 50th level.

Also you could try Cindy, the necro that guards the Fear portal, she is about 40th.

The loot is a mixed bag, sometimes you will get high end gems, or nice sellable bits of jewelry, but more often then not you endup with just a few plat.

Cindy is probably the easiest to solo, she is on a fairly quick timer, but she gets camped a lot, especially by necros with Dark/Fear or stunners that can keep her from getting her spells off.

Being a necro she can really put the hurt on in a hurry, and likes to fear people, which makes them run back through the spec cave and aggro the specs.

Seafuries are usually in abundance, but have a crapload of HP and hit real hard. Occasionally you will also run into Queg Maelstrom, a level 45ish clops... nasty fellow. But usually it is not difficult to isolate one of them.
