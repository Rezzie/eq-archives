Greetings--

I'm back to answer my own question and then some. Not to overshadow Broog's coherant reply; all that he said has proved to be true in fact. I put 3 points into specialize alteration and spent 6 hours buffing and healing newbies at the Halas ramp, but the skill didn't seem to want to raise above 50. (Mind you, I hand't read any replies yet and also hadn't put points into any other specialize skills because I didn't think it necessary.) Well, 3 levels later I'm petitioning to find out why I'm a 33rd level shaman without a specialty -- needless to say, the guide who answered me didn't have a clue as to why this was happening. Alas and quite by accident I found that IT IS necessary to put at least one point into each of the specialize skills before any of them will go above 50. I still don't know why one thing has to do with the other, but the game isn't exactly flawless either. Hope this helps another like me, less than informed =)
