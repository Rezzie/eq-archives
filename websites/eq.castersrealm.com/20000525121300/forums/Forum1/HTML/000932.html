If you've never played D&D or any other "pen and paper" roleplaying game in person you probably won't get this so feel free to move on. For those of you who have, Galahad is a paladin, Cuthbert a cleric, and Gandalf is a magic-user. Without further ado...

DM: Okay, the three of you arrive at the Dungeon of Death, where the Holy Avenger which Galahad seeks is said to reside. As you step through the door, a demon stands, ready to attack...

Gandalf: Alright, I fry him with a lightning bolt, 10 dice of damage!!

DM: (rolls 5 dice)

Gandalf: Hey, what's going on? The rules say my LB does 10 dice, not 5!

DM: Sorry, but your lightning bolt was too powerful, I had to nerf it for game balance.

Gandalf: WHAT??!! But LB's are why I wanted to be a mage!! You can't do that!!!

DM: Sorry, there are plenty of other classes to play if you don't like being a mage. You inflict 2 points of damage on the demon.

Gandalf: Oh, great. Help me out guys.
Galahad: Cuthbert and I smite the demon!

DM: Okay, you and Cuthbert inflict 100 points of damage each. The demon then leaps over the two of you and attacks Gandalf...

Gandalf: WHAT??!! I do 2 damage and he's after me??!!!

DM: Yes, it seems your spell is a megataunt now... (grins evilly)

Gandalf: AAARRRRGGGGHHH!!!!

Later...
DM: Alright, the three of you have fought your way into the inner sanctum, and standing before you is the powerful Evilman.

Galahad: He will fall to me! Charge!

(Three men come out of a closet and bind and gag the player of Galahad's character with duct tape)

Cuthbert: What's going on?

DM: Oops, Galahad has gone linkdead. Evilman takes out a can o woop ass and sprays it on Galahad, then he...

Cuthbert and Gandalf: AAAARRRRGGGHH!!!

Still later...
DM: Okay on your fifth try you manage to slay Evilman.

Galahad YES!! The Holy Avenger is mine!!!

DM: You find 5 pp and a short beer.

Galahad: What? Where's the Avenger?

DM: It doesn't seem to be here.

Galahad: But the prophecy said the Holy Avenger is in the hands of Evilman! WHERE IS MY AVENGER!!??

DM: Sorry, I guess it's a rare drop. You'll have to wait until he respawns.

Galahad: Respawns??? AAARRRRGGHHH!!!

(1 hour passes)

DM: (Looking at his watch) Okay, Evilman pops up again...

(Two twelve-year-old kids bust down the door and throw dice on the table)

Galahad: Who the hell are you?

Elminster: I am Elminster, a 50th level sage, and this is my friend Drizzt Do'urden, a 50th level ranger. Drizzt hacks Evilman 6 times while I cast my Ice Comet.

DM: Okay, Evilman dies. Drizzt - You find a Holy Avenger.

Drizzt: Sweet, my level 2 paladin can really use this!

Elminster: See ya guys!!
Galahad: AAARRRRGGGHH!!!!

Even later...

DM: Okay, Evilman pops up again...
(6 guys walk in the door)

Squatter1: WhAsSuP dOoD? GoTtA sHaRe, MaN.

Galahad: What are you talking about?

Squatter2: GoTtA sHaRe ThE sPaWn MaN. pLaY nIcE dOoD oR wE pEtItIoN.

Galahad: Now look here...

Squatter3: HeY dM dOoD, tHeSe PhU*kHeAdS aRe HoGgIn ThE sPaWn..

DM: Alright, now you two groups have to share.

Galahad: But...

Squatter4: HoLd Up DoOd, wE gOt 3 gRoUpS hErE mAn. We'Ll ShArE, 1 SpAwN eAcH, dOoD.

DM: Sounds fair to me..

Galahad; Wait a minnit!!!! They get three spawns, we only get one!! that's not fair!!

DM: Hey, look I'm busy - There's four different groups here, 1 spawn per group!

Squatter5: KeWl. AvEnGeRs gEt $500 oN e-BaY.

Galahad: AAARRRRGGGHHH!!!!!

(Much much later)

DM: Alright, the other three groups have left after they got their three Holy Avengers, and Evilman pops up yet again.

Galahad: Joy. We attack.

DM: (Sits there, does nothing)

Gandalf: Now WTF?

Galahad: (Shakes DM violently) HELLOO!!! WE ATTACK!!!!

DM: (Gets up and turns on the TV) Sorry, but Cerfnet went down. No ETA at this time.

(The three players get up and take butcher knives out of the kitchen drawer, together they advance menacingly towards the DM.

DM: Hey wait, guys? Wait guys, don't do that! It's not my fault! It's a major backbone connection... AARRRRGGGGGHHH!!!!

(The End)

Galahad: Now look here...

Squatter3: Hey DM dood, these guys are hoggin the
