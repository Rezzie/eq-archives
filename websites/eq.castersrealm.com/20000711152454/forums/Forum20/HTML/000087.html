Well close to SK you can check out NK and fight borer beetles and scythe beetles thru level 20.

You can also go to EK and fight gnoll reavers, gorge hounds, dark stalkers, lion matriarchs and at level 20 you can fight the crag spiders.

High hold is a good place as well...sit on gnoll side and when 2 appear charm one and have it fight the other...great xp.

Lots of different tactics you can use. Most of the time its as simple as buffing your pet and debuffing the mob with an occasional blast or fear in there so your pet can smack it while its running.

Don't forget the gorge with minos but I have never done those solo so not sure how you will do there solo. Groups I know are great.

Well thats 4 other zones so hope it helps a bit.

The pickclaws in HK I haven't tried to solo as they are always camped anyway.

As far as the crag spiders at level 20 you can definately solo these. What I would do is pull them to an open area near the barb village. Mes them then deprep them especially with str items (they move slower and attack less with less damage). After you have depreped them make sure your pet is preped and blast the crag so your pet gets initiated in the attacks. The from there just fear the crag, blast, blast, fear, blast and its dead.

They aren't too hard to kill just make sure if you get poisoned that you don't get hit. If you get in trouble run to the barbarian village and let the guards kill it. Sow would be nice just incase.

My suggestion is The Aviak Isle in OOT around loc +1500 +4000. The isls has 4 types of aviak (and a very rare named spawn) giving a nice level progression.

The Birds:

STARLING lvl 9-11 loot upto 5gp
TURACO lvl 12-14 Loot upto 1pp
TANAGER lvl 18-19 Loot upto 2pp
QUETZAL lvl 21-23+ Loot upto 4pp

This isle is rarely camped or crowded...and the loot is pretty nice.
