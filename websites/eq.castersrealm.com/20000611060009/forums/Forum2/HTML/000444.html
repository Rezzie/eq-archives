By the time I was able to get him a LOW for 500 he could only get rid of the BBC for 120 and now that he has an exec axe we are not gonna get 500pp for the LOW. Point is that the higher ups made the money and will keep on making the money. The economy has and will continue to falter because of this.

Now they are gonna try and stop farming which many have done and taken advantage of.
So now I cant sit on my rear all day and get that good item for resale that others have done countless times before me. So again the rich stay rich and the lower downs dont.

Its a game and its fake money unless you go the Ebay route right? only thing that benefits the lower level players is the ease to get some decent items now. I wish verant would look at the actual PP dropped in the game as opposed to the actual PP in the game then they would see the problems that exist. As for a solution heh there probably isnt one. But something need be done to Balance things.
