However My Wizard is great in a group w/ his DDs but people will ALWAYS pick a Mage over a wizard. Why? Because a Mage can make a tank (pet) that rocks and his DDs do a fraction less damage than a Wizard. Having a Necro I KNOW how good pets are and so do all players at high lvls. No other class should have DDs that close to a Wizard in damage when they also have a powerful pet.

Here are the problems:

#1 Wizard DD damages need to be increased for low and mid level spell ranks (lvl 49 is the only level where thier DDs are unmatched - AS IT SHOULD BE). Example: a lvl 34 Mage casting DDs that do 15-20 or so less damage than a Wizard but w/ a pet that can hit for 35's - you tell me who becomes a MUCH more desirable choice for a group member(the mage bolt spell actually does MORE damage than a Wizard can do at that lvl). I think this could be ballanced if a Wizard's DDs at that level did 50-80 more damage than the Mages. Its a solid fact that pet damage adds up big time in a fight, hell, they do more melee damage than a cleric, druid, shaman, or bard (I know my Necro pet sure can). Since Wizards don't get pets, increase DD damages accross the board to compensate. Otherwise groups will continue to pass over Wizards for 'Wizards' that spawn thier on tank (pets).

#3 Resists rates are just plain too high
Nuff said, this one is a no-brainer
