I generally don't use my wand anymore. Though there are a few exceptions. I am 40th level.

First, if I have multiple monsters in the area, I do not use the wand because my primary focus at that point is crowd control. If I am in fighting, a monster is blocking a good 40-50% of my view of the area.. I have missed "controlling" monsters before because I chose to melee..

Second, if the group is down to one monster (or all others are very solidly under control), I tend to pull the (post-35) stun/med routine.. Stand behind a tank, Color Shift, sit, Color Shift, sit.. In Elemental form, you can do this without waiting for animations to sit you down.. effectively you GAIN mana while doing this and stun the critter more than 50% of the fight.

Third, Color Shift/Melee at 40th is PAINFUL. Resists aren't super uncommon, monsters taking a "liking" to you isn't either.. Being nearby when that happens and getting clobbered by Quad 70's won't let you last terribly long. It's better (and more efficient) to hang back and let your healer worry about the tanks instead of worrying about you..

Now, all that said.. I DO pull out my wand on occasion.. An enchanter who doesn't use every trick in the book to win and reduce downtime needs to become more creative.
