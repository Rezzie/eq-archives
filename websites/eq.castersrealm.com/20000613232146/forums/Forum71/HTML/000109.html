I am aware that that the spell list on the test servers are just for that : testing. But it is also getting rather close to the release of ruins of kunark, and I think it is fair to start brining up some observations about the spell list, that may not be apparent just by looking at the numbers and thinking "this looks good". This comparion, is about the two big daddy damage spells in the game : Ice comet, lvl 49, and sunstrike, lvl 60.
Ice comet : 1120 dmg for 400 mana
Sunstrike : 1600 dmg for 500 mana

At first, you think "eh, not bad, its more effienct". But lets dive down a bit futher into the numbers, to see why this spell is going to horribly fall short of what it should be. Lets take my gnome wizard for example. Currently, he has around 2400 mana, and can cast 6 ice comets before he goes OOM. Assuming that each int is still .2*int*lvl, I will have 400 more mana at lvl 60, allowing me to get off 1 more ice comet, and giving me 2800 mana. Allowing me to get off 7 ice comets. With this much mana, I will be able to off 5 sunstikes. Now, lets look at the math.

7*1120=7840 damage
5*1600=8000 damage

- Damage diff : 8000-7840 is 160

Now, for an upgrade from lvl 50 to lvl 60, only being able to do 160 more damage in a full load of mana is going to be a bit disapointing.

I think there are 2 options that Verant could do, to really make this spell shine, and make wizards the true supreem of magical direct damage.

Option 1: Increase the damage of the spell to 2000. Many wizards seem to like this option the best, for me. I dont =) but ohweell. I am only 1 wizard, this would make it

7*1120=7840
5*2000=10000
-- Damage diff: 2160

Which I think would be a nice set-apart for doing a full load of mana at lvl 60.

Option 2:

This is one of my fave ones. But, it is keeping the damage at 1600, but lowering the mana cost to a point of which a wizard could cast 6 or 7 of these spells in a row before going OOM.

7*1120=7840
6*1600=9600
7*1600=11200

So either a 1760 damage diff, or a 3360 damage diff.

I like the 7 cast one =) but that may be a bit to powerfull. So I'd like to see verant either do it so we could cast 6 of these spells for the avg wiz, or up the damage to 2000.
