You should be able to score 69 or 70 points with a gatorsmasher with 69 skill at lvl 45, assuming that your strength is 120 or so. If you buff yourself to 150 strength you should be able to get hits close to 77-78. As far as I can tell, skill, strength and level are equally important in calculating max damage.

And actually, the 2*dam+1 formula is only accurate in a rather narrow level/skill/strength range. Already at lvl 18 you should start getting hits exceeding 2*dam+1 assuming maxed skill and high strength.
