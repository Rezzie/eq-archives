I actually had great luck.

I camped OOT for about 4 to 5 hours. The next night I camped shadowmen and Hasten.

A piece of advice. After you get the rapier from the shadowmen and go to rathe mountains have someone kill you in a duel. Loot your corpse but leave the rapier on it.
