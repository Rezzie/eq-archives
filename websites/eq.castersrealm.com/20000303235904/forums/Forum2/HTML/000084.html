Master of damage? Never was.

Can we be? I think so.

Add one "Skill" into the wizard lineup. And that is "Syphon"(for a better name check your local library).

What in my head does Syphon entail? Basically, its the Wizard, standing completely still, focusing mind and body as one, accumulating mana from the environment and himself, then unleashing it on an enemy for either an increase of 25-75% extra dmg then normal and a decrease of resists.

Example: Ice Comet. 400mana. Takes about 6 secs normally to cast off and does 1100dmg. Recycle time of 5 secs.

Now with the new Syphon ability, add another 4-10 secs for the wizard to power up, then casting the IC spell.

Damage would now be anywhere from 1100-2000dmg. Mana used would be lessened to 350mana instead. Greater ability to hit anything over the Lvl cap and a greater ability NOT to get partially resisted ta boot. Recycle time is increased to 10-15 secs and you are dazed/stunned for 3-5 secs from over doing it

Frankly I would even be soo bold to increase it from 100%-150% extra damage for even longer down time, some extra mana and longer recycle time too.

This will make it more fun to do that last Knock out Blast and maybe make it worthwhile to have wizards around.

Add a new action graphic instead of my wizard flailing his arms around like he is trying to swim in mid air. Add something like shooting both arms infront of his person, outstretched at the mob, back bent, legs spread in a more dramatic pose.

Ugg I wish I would be working there. Boy would this game have some great animations

Just an idea. Oh ya and increase our mana!! Frankly the mana stays roughly the same for every new spell lvl for wizards from 39-49. THis means that you can roughly still only use your lvl 39 spell anywhere from 5-7 times before OOM, whereas you can use Ice Comet 4-6 times.
