I would suggest heading to the dungeon of Cazic Thule, in Feerott. It is a very good dungeon from 22-35 or so, and can support around 75 people comfortably with all of the camp spots. It is heavily camped, but you should be able to hang out long enough and advertise for a group and get in. At 26 you could hunt the maze, Steel Golem, Lower Tae, Crater, and maybe the Pyramid Courtyard. I would stay away from Upper Tae, Small Pyramid, Pyramid Top, AOF, and the sewers until you are a bit higher, around 29 or 30.

You and a partner could also move to Misty Thicket and do guard Felp. He is a solo spawn, around 27 or 30, and if you do it right no other guards will bother you. You have to kill him 30-40 time before you go kos in RiverVale or the guards in Misty. Around level 28 or so (maybe now?) you can solo him.

Unrest is also a good area for your level, if you can get into one of the good rooms with a good group.

All Cazic and Unrest will both require a good group that knows what is doing. No soloing in either of these places.

Safe travels!

Try the mechanic in Ak'Anon. He stands right next to Rylin Coil, who sells tinkering equipment. Rylin, however, will not attempt to help if you go after the mechanic. There are no roaming clockworks in the area and respawn is about 6 minutes. You can really start to solo him at 25 or below, and you can solo him to 31 for good experience AND loot. Sometimes camped, but worth it to wait. Don't worry too much about faction. I had killed lots of mino's and done the mino horn quest many times first, so I was still only 'dubious' in Ak'Anon after 4 levels of soloing this guy!!!
