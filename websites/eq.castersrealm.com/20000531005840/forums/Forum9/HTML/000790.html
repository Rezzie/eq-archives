To effectively powerlevel I have found you need to be about 10 levels higher than the person you are powerleveling. Once they get into this 10 level range it gets harder and harder to give all the exp to one person and not the other.

While you could charm your friend and have his character help yours there would be several problems that would come of this.
One, he doesn't hit as hard as a MOB of the same level so you might be better off Charming a MOB that cons White or High Blue to you.

Two, if charm breaks he won't agro on you but you will still be without a pet (A situation that quickly leads to death when soloing).
Three, it is highly likely that most of the time your charm will not last more than a few seconds on someone so many levels above you so you will go through ALOT of mana with recharms (possibly useing 2 or 3 charms per fight). This will lead to more downtime at best and actually slow the rate at which you level.
