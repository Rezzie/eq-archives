The only thing off the top of my head in Qeynos is Cubert. He (Cubert) is a named gelatinous cube in the Qeynos Sewers. He drops (a good chance) a robe. You take that robe to an NPC on the bridge near the bank and he gives you a) a hematite (oh boy) or b) a gold stud (+3 int earring).

At 7th you would be borderline on being able to solo. There is a ton of placeholder junk that you must kill. Spawn rate is very fast though.

Qubert is a good recommendation. If you get that +3 INT earring (you don't always) it's a very nice item. In fact I still wear mine at level 23.

Also in the Qeynos sewers in a named zombie called Drosco. He should be fairly easy to kill at your level and he drops a pretty nice necklace for an enchanter of your level. If memory serves it's AC 2, CHA +5 and acts as a lightsource when worn. Not to shabby for killing a level 5 or so zombie.

No quests that you cant do later on that you will miss out on. If you get the chance try to get hold of mana items as these will help you far more than int items until over lvl 20. Good items are any of the pc made jade jewellery, the skull of jhentra, polished bone hoops(plus 10 mana) and a runed totem staff 2hb. I doubt that you will be able to do any of the specific enchanter quests until you are about lvl 20 and in a group.

Oh dont worry too much about charisma at your lvl, i would go for mana and then int unless you charm a lot.
