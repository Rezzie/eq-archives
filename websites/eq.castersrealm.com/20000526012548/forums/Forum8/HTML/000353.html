I am not a person who is asking anyone to be nerfed. Personally i think if the programmers made one class too powerful in their opinion then they shouldn't be able to take that power away. Find another alternative. I just wanted to get that out of the way, once again "I do not want to see Necros nerfed and i think it sucks that you guys have been". However, I am active on the mage forum and have seen many necros complaining that mages should be nerfed.

I feel that both mages and necros are a very viable, soloing class. Mages have strong pets, perhaps now stronger then necro pets since they have been nerfed (i am not stating this as fact because i have done no tests). Mages also have terrific dd spells. This equals out to a class that can solo well and kill stuff fast, all in all a very powerful class.

Now necros. (please do not take everything i say as fact because i have never played a necro. some of my rl friends are necros and i have fought with many, but have never played one) Necromancers can summon up very strong pets. They have terrific dots, the best in the game. They also have direct damage spells, i think the best does in the 500's??? Not sure though. You guys are a very flexible class, having many useful spells such as a snare line, fear, root, magic skin, charm undead, self clarity (liche form), lifetap spells, brief invulnerability, and feign death. In my opinion Necros are the best soloing class in the game. They can solo a room better than anyone else.

Many necros complain that magicians are too powerful, we can nuke hard and we have strong pets. However, this is almost the extent of our abilities. True we can summon stuff, which i like very much, but is lacking in many areas. We have rain spells, which do not work very well at all. And our escape spells are gate. When a mage solos (without sow) he gets the mob on his pet. Nukes the mob, heal pet, nuke the mob, ect. If our pet dies and we cannot outrun the mob we are dead. We can try to gate but thats a very long casting time when every blow the mob lands is full damage. We cannot lifetap to get health back, have no magic skin, no invuln shield, no feign death.

The mages spellbook is probably one of the most boring out there. I truly love my class but to tell you the truth i do one thing my entire life. "pet kill" nuke. Thats it.

It sucks that your pets were nerfed. But in all honesty dont you think they were too powerful? I have many times seen a 49 necro pet solo a 50 warrior, in all his plane armor. With fine steel daggers they were insane, and if they lost duals to mage pets it is only because of our damage shields, your pets just killed themselves.

When i read a post from a Necro stating that mages should be nerfed, i wanna ask them How? Will you nerf our direct damage? (the only power we have besides our pets) Or will you nerf our pets? (once again making necro pets so much more powerful then ours) You cannot take away escape spells, because we have none.

I want to say again that i think it sucks that you guys were nerfed (i wanna state this alot because i'm gonna get burned for this post no matter what i say).

But even after all the nerfs Verant has screwed you with, You guys are still the most powerful class in the game. You are the best soloers. Plus you have a spellbook so much more unique and interesting then the mages. You have many many skills that can save your life.

Mages are not your enemies, most of us do not want to see you nerfed. We dont want your power taken away and we do not want alot more power givin to us. But when I hear necros complain i just don't see why.
