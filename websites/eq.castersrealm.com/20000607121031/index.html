ITEM VALUATION NOW ONLINE

As a continued development on the site we now have online our item valuation system.  Combined with our fast item searches you will be able to quickly get the latest pp values on items in Everquest.

Our valuation system will also track server pricing trends and once we have enough data collated we will be generating statistics on server economies if all goes well.

The system is however totally dependant on you submitting values for items on your server. Doing so is a fast and easy process - simply find the item in our database and follow the link.

You can access our item lists via the left menu or by clicking here.
