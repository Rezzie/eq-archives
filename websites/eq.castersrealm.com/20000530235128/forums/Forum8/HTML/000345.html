You have 2 options if you want to leave Tox/Paineel and both involve Qeynos(unless you know any high level wizzies/druids).

1. Go to Qeynos, stand on the dock and auction for an invisibility spell. Then book it to the north Gate and auction for a bind. Bind far away from the gate though.

2. Go to Qeynos and jump off the boat. Swim to the second dock (you normally get off at the first one) and go through the entance to the sewers (this entrance USED to be bugged but is now supposed to work). Make your way to N Qeynos via the aquaducts and then auction for a bind. If you can I would bind in the Necro guild in the aquaducts. It is nice to be able to gate back to a merchant when you are so loaded you cannot walk!
