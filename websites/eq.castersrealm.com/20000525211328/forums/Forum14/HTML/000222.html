Gloves you can always try for Impskin gloves. The spawn in Sol A is rare and the drop is rare from that spawn but they are probably the best caster gloves non-planar in the game. For the cloak you might try the Mystic Cloak quest. I wonder tho if it's worth camping that rare spawn in Unrest over waiting for Gabbie to show up without that quill in her hand. What I generally try and do is even out my int and my charisma so one stat is not that much higher than the other. It's pretty much general consensus that Charisma is a leading factor in our Mez and charm line of spells and at higher levels you become more of a mob controller than any type of damage dealer. With that in mind charisma becomes just as important as int / mana.

Personally I think of int as quantity and charisma as quality. Your not going to benefit from a higher int unless your going oom on a regular or at least semi regular basis. You do benefit from a higher charisma though since since it factors in on resists for charm, mezz and blur type spells. I try to get my charisma as high as possible without sacrificing too much on int. Having less resists on mezzes means you'll be using less mana also. Right now at level 41 I rarely go OOM because of a long fight. I rarely go below one bubble of mana for that matter and my int is 170 and charisma is 183. Back to your post though I think adding the Glamour Rod and charisma bracelets over the pbb and stein sounds like a good idea to me.

Don't bother with impskin gloves. They're about the same overall as incandescent gloves, and a pain in the butt to get.

Do the mystic cloak quest, it's a very nice item. Or at least get a cloak of the icebear, which has better AC than what you're wearing.

If you have some spare cash and aren't a HIE, go for Ivandyr's Hoop, great earring.

Bags of the tinkerers are great, buy 2 or 3 if you have the money.

Also consider losing the PBBs. Either get a nice high AC/+INT wrist item (GCB or ring of the ancients) or make golden cat eye bracers. PBBs are low AC, low INT = waste of a slot.

The rod of insidious glamour is nice because it offers 2 AC in addition to +12 CHA. It's only marginally better than a stein of moggok, but it looks nice and gives you the "enchanter look".
