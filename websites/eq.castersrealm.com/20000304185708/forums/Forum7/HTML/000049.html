I will state up front that I am not a wizard. However, I have a lot of wizard friends and guild members, and would like to see some enhancements made to the class. Please give me some feedback on if these ideas have been put in and what you all think.

Resist rates: Wizard spells should come with a lower resist rates then other classes. Warriors get crit hits, rogues get backstab. Then why shouldn't the masters of the dd be met with a less chance of MoB resists. Suggestion, lower MR FR and CR slightly for mobs when struck with a wizard spell.

Roots: Too many lvl 50 wizards I know still use root. Is it me or is there a problem with the higher lvl roots. Longer lasting, shorter cast times, less chance of breaking when hit, lower saves should all be products of enhanced root spells. Wizards should excel here over other classes.

Specializing and Mana Effieciency: 2.8 doesn't cut it. At higher lvls wizards should get a SIGNIFICANT advantage in mana effieciency compared to other casters. IE make IC 325-350 mana, Rend 250, Combust 225. Add a few more nukes can make a world of difference. Also give wizards a specializing advantage that further decreases fizzle rate and mana effieciency. You are the pure casters, warriors are the pure tanks. They got some buffs shouldn't wizards as well?

Casting times: You all do cast fastest of all damaging classes. However, this distance should be even greater then it is. Reduce cast times on all wizard damage spells by .2-.5 should yield a significant advantage. This would make the wizzys even more adept at stopping those annoying runners.

More stuns: Stuns are great to have when you don't have a pet and make it easier to help your groups keeping your tanks from getting hit. You all should get stun spells atleast every other rank. Enhanced damage and stun time as the ranks get higher.

Cancel Magic Line: Wizards should specialize in this area of "Debuffs" over other casters. Add some additional spells to this line and make them available at even an earlier level

DoT Spells: A line that kinda amazes me you all don't have. It would add great depth and variety to the class. Now I am not talking in the nature and power of a necro. However, Heat Armor, Radiant Sphere, Encompassing Frost are all ideas I have for this. Heat Armor does damage over time to a mob vs FR much the same way the D and D spell does. Radiant Sphere is a vs MR DoT spell that does radiation damage. Encompassing Frost is your vs CR DoT. As powerful as necro no way, but add some depth to the wizard specialty of doing damage. All of these spells would fall under the Verant area of what you all are dubbed "Pure Casters".

Just some ideas to enhance the wizzy class. Curious if these have come up before, or if you all like the ideas. I don't think they damage game balance. Rather they make you all what you are intended to be "Pure Casters and Fast damage potential". You guys do the best damage in the fastest way, but I would like to see a bit more of a significant difference in this area.

Just some ideas

Ok some of these ideas have merit however.

The better resist rates that I think should be improved accross the board not just for wizards. Trust me I would trade my Enchaters resist rates for a wizards any day. We all need a little help here at the later levels. The resist rates for mobs almost put the dedicated "nuker" either mage or wizard out of a job unless your lucky enoough to have someone to lower the magic resistance and even then your still missing nearly 1/2 of the time. And it makes the crowd control nearly impossible when you really really need it and cannot afford to try the same spell 3 times on the same mob.

As for the mana efficency issue I think this is already set just about right. The only real reason to increase mana efficency or actual amount of mana is to help soloing. As is the wizards are shy about 1/4 the amount of mana needed to root/nuke a low red solo as it should be. Of course if the resist rate was changed this might then become an issue. Maybe that is why the resists are as bad as they are. hmmmm! Something to think about.

As far as the cancel magic issue. I cannot even begin to figure out what your thinking. This is very much an enchanters area not a wizards. Sure it would be a nice additional speciality. But that doesn't mean it is approprate.

I kinda like the most of the rest of your ideas though although I think you have the wrong analogy for the Wizards equivilant melee class. Remember warriors are tanks not damage dealers although they seem to think they are damage dealers. Wizards are damage dealers not the casters version of tanks. So the analogy your looking for is the wizard is the caster version of the monk and the mage with/earth pet is the caster version of the warrior.
