Here are my new favorites that I charm.

1) Froglock Tsu or TAL ghoul wizzie in Lower guk. Oh my, chain nukes for probably 250 each time with ZERO downtime. Buff him and give him clarity then watch the fun. Stays charmed a LONG time and is so easy to recharm since he tries to hit you with his longest casting nuke when he breaks charm. When I see that charm has broken message I don't even color shift him, just cast beguile again.


2) Zombie commander or any of the zombie SKs in kithikor. Somebody mentioned that these are like cruise missiles. Charm one and watch how it keeps firing off harm touch over and over at the target. Kind of dangerous pet but fun to see them in action.

3) Decayed Healer. Heals itself, nukes, and dispells the enemy and seemed to stay charmed a long time.

4) Evil Eye in runneye. These are wizzies and can also Chain cast almost as good as the froglocks. The best part of this is to
Charm one and bring it upstairs and watch the newbies run. This is my most impressive looking pet so far.
