A lot of the things you listed are not bugs per se. They are simply the results of that ever present lag. FOr example teleporting mobs occurs when you client lags behind the server. The clients thinks (predicts) the NPC is still right in front of them when in fact it ran early and is now 1/2 a mile a away over the hill. When your client and the server finally sync up the mob is moved to its correct spot out of your range of view and thus appears to teleport.

Can this be improved? Sure, improving the network code and lowering server load would help a great deal.

Mobs teleporting to you is by design. If you attempt to exploit a pathing bug by getting a mob stuck in pathing it will eventually teleport to your location. This is a design decision on Verant's part.

Still a lot of the things on your list are true bugs.
