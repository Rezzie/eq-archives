Simple question.

Necro pets got nerfed badly, but they will get something to compensate, including lifetap spells on the level 44 and 49 pets, and new "group friendly" spells. I'm not saying this is fair compensation, but it's something.

Fine. Enchanters got the same exact nerf. Nothing to compensate. Nada. Seems to me that if the soloability and offensive power of a class get nerfed in a major way, there should be some compensation.

Examples of things Verant can do can compensate:

1) Make pets commandable. This would make them MUCH more group-friendly. I thought "group-friendly" was Verant's customary mantra, so how about it? Right now, the enchanter has to get hit for the pet to activate, which is OK for soloing but makes the pet a liability in a group.

2) Give pets more HP. This would enhance both soloing and grouping.

3) Enhance other enchanter spells to make them more "group friendly." Examples:

  • Remove or increase the damn haste caps that make celerity and swift like the wind 100% useless except for speeding up enchanter and shaman pets and twinking newbies. Haste items are dropping so often now you'd think they're going out of style, and they make these spells useless.
  • Un-nerf tashan{i,ia} so that it reduces MR like it used to. -33 MR for tashania doesn't cut it when all the new mobs have at least 100 magic resist. How can an enchanter be useful to a group taking on these uber-mobs if they can't land a single spell, and their primary haste buffs are rendered useless (see above)? Again, group friendly.
  • Fix whirl/DDD, and make them castable on uber-mobs. These spells should be alternatives to the color flux line, since color flux et al. are simply resisted way too often on uber-mbos.

Sadly, I'm beginning to think we'll never see any of these changes.
