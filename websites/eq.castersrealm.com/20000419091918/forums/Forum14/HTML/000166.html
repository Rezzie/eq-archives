Heh, twink is a sore point to some. But I understand. You are sitting on a mound of cash with nowhere to spend since high level items are often trade-only. Why not give it to someone who could use it.

Anyway, my suggestion is to go with low level magic items but lots of them. The thing is that a +3 stat item costs something like 10% to 20% of similar item with +4 or more.

I'd start with Opalline earrings for 20pp apiece. They are common as dirt on older servers and charisma isn't very useful to most players. Then maybe Savant cap for around 40pp or lower for head gear. You could get runed circlet for around 10 times that, but for +1 additional int and +2 AC. For the wrist slot go with golden cat's eye for around 20pp. Alternatively, you could go for PBB for around 100 plats apiece. Black Iron medallion for neck piece for around 30 pp. For waist slot, go with thick leather apron around 50 pp.

For legs slot, either drakescale leggings or mammoth leggings. The drakescale leggings will set you back by about 350 pp while the mammoth are around 70 pp. Personally, I think this is a place where you should spend a bit. Chances are the enchanter will be using the drakescale leggings for a long time and it's not all that expensive. Robe of the keeper is a pretty good choice for less than 100pp. FBR adds +1 int more but costs more than twice that. For ring slots, either electrum star ruby ring for around 100pp or platinum jade ring for around 150pp. These are expensive stuff. You can also go with jagged band for around 60pp. IMHO, trading stat for AC isn't so bad.

All these could add up to less than 1000pp. (More if you go with PBB for wrist and Plat Jade rings) The other slots either will cost a bundle or don't really have worthwhile magic items to fill in. You can throw in woven/raw silk to complete the set. The enchanter should be able to get stein of moggok on his own for one of the hand slots. The other, if you fancy carrying weapon, buy Water Crystal Staff for around 50pp. If not, look for crude stein for free if people are camping the bouncers and you can loot them. Sometimes, players sell the stein back to the vendor. In which case, it'll cost you around 10pp.
