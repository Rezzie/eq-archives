No, DoTs do not stop casting, however, Screaming Terror and Fear do it VERY well!

Need tips dueling a mage, heh, easy.

Pre Duel

Buff to hell
Skinspell
Banshee
Cold Buff
Heat Buff


Spells to have mem'ed
Fear
Root( or Screaming Terror if you have it)
Dark
Blood
Life Tap(top one you have)
HARMSHIELD
SHADOWSTEP
What ever you feel like for last, pet heal, Leech line if you have, or Dark Pact line spell, what ever.

Duel
Lead with Fearing his pet! His pet is his life line! A mage with no pet, is a dead mage. Sick your pet, but more often than not, he will lead with a DD spell, so your pet will agro right away. Then you simply Fear the Mage, and stack. Then it is time to keep the mage's pet running. A mage's pet will tear you to shreads if it gets ahold of you. If it does, since the skin spell will time to Shadowstep, do it. When you re-pop, you merrly re-fear his pet, and tap the mage till he dies. When the mage is dead, the pet dies.

The following is the way a warrior will beat a necro, ignore the pet, and kill the necro, dead necro=no pet. If things get hairy, use your life taps, and lay in a leech spell. Also, use your pact line, since the mage will go oom long before you. If it totally goes Fubar, use Harmshield to eat some spells. IF you REALLY want to be nasty, YOU pick the zone, and before hand, make sure to have bound your self somewhere WELL hidden in the zone. Then, you can gate to that spot, and the mage can not see you to target, and you can feel free to lay waste to the mage.

The final thing in all of this, it is the most important thing, keep the mage from casting, and his pet off of you! If you do both of these, you have one sweet easy kill.

Have fun

First of all, let me just say that I have never lost a duel to a necro. Never. The reason is in the armor I wear before I duel a necro. I have my magic resist at 100. 99% of the time your fears, dots, etc., do nothing. First, I send in an Air Pet with Burnout III and a flame shield. Then I dispell you with Nullify Magic, then I use malosi (yes the 49 shaman one that lowers all your stats by 60 except disease). Then I cast a few shock of swords if you're not already dead from the pet. Air pets hit very hard and fast, and because they stun it is very hard to get a spell off. Even if you fear my pet, it wont matter. It will come back or stay on your pet.

My point is this. If you are going to duel ANY caster, you better make sure your magic resistance is high. That is the key to killing any caster. For shamans I make sure my poison and Disease resists are high.

I want to also ad to the comment about a mage without a pet is a dead mage. That's not entirely true unless dueling a Melee class. The fact is a mage (or any other caster class) with low magic resistance is a dead mage.

If a mage or any class loses a duel against another caster it's mostly because your resistances are too low.

Oh one other thing in regards to binding and gating to another part of the zone. Dude, you as a level 50 necro should know that that is a cowardly and dishonorable way of dueling. Most people know that and don't do it because it's considered a cheap tactic. I could do that to but I don't. No offense. Just giving my .02
