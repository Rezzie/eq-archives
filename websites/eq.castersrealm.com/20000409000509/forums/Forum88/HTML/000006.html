First off, I have NOT been to PoF or PoH yet, so I am by no means an expert in this area. I have plenty of high level friends who attend and hold plane raids, however, so I feel I have enough knowledge to actively discuss the subject AND complain about it. Here I go...

From what I have heard, PoF and PoH are so completely different from one another. It's like night and day, apples and oranges, etc. PoF is no cake walk, don't get me wrong, but on a whole, considering drops, mobs, break techniques, etc, I think PoF is worlds easier and more enjoyable than Hate. Difficulty is not my main area of concern, however. My concern lies in the fact that the drop rates for PoF and PoH differ so greatly. My Ranger friend got five piece of Plane armor on two trips to PoF. Shaman friends of mine (and warrios as well) have been on up to ten Hate raids and have yet to even see a piece of their armor DROP! Now I'm not one to whine, but I do find this a bit... ridiculous to say the least. If for no other reason than the large class imbalance it creates at higher levels. Granted this may not be a problem right now when everyone has to stop at level 50, but imagine what it will do when the expansion comes out. All the classes who are decked out in Plane armor will have a great advantage over those who are not, and will forge onward up to whatever level we are able to obtain.

I am simply asking for this... I wish Verant would make the drop rates and spawns in PoF and PoH similar enough to where all classes have roughly the same chance of attaining a piece of their armor on any given run. I do not feel that this is too much to ask, rather I feel it is necessary to balance the playing field up in the higher levels. Each of the people I have spoken to in the game have the same feelings as me, even those classes who obtain their armor from PoF. Please let me know what you think, because I'm starting to feel very discouraged that I have worked for over forty days on my Shaman only to get him to level 50 and find that I cannot do what I have been wanting to do for so long: equip my character with high quality gear which will make him proficient now and in the expansion. Thanks so much for listening.
