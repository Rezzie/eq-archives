Actually the Level Cap will not be higher than Level 60. This is derived from the fact that if you use EQInside to look at the spell data, you will see that all classes can use all spells, its just that Superior Heal is set to Level 61 for the Necromancer class. This is true for all spells that a class cannot use and true for ALL total spells for warriors, rogues, and monks. Anything higher than 60 and the game recieves serious problems. PoF and PoH would not be super easy for Level 60s and I know as a fact that the Overseer of Aie in PoS is Level 63. This seems geared towards level 60s to kill. The last piece of evidence that leads me to believe level 60 is that if the camp were 55, some classes would be screwed over their level 54 spell level. For example, the druid class's Level 19, 29, 39, and 49 spell ranks are at least three times as useful as their 14, 24, 34, and 44th spell ranks. With Shamans, it's the exact opposite. Having the Level cap at 55 would be totally unfair for many caster classes.
