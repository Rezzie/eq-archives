You have it almost right.

AC is actually a combination of two values:
1) damage mitigation
2) damage avoidance.

Damage mitigation is the ability to absorb a blow. i.e. The higher this value the less damage you will take with each blow. Armor is an example of something that improves damage mitigation. If damage is reduced to 0 it is recorded as a miss so if you improve your armor you will be "technically" missed more.

Damage avoidance is the ability to miss an opponants strike . Increasing this value will cause more misses. Agility improves you damage avoidance.
