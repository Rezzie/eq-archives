As someone who plays both a bard, and a 'chanter, there is really no concern here.
Bard charm lasts 15 seconds MAX.
The new bard song REQUIRES a Flute. (No effective healing, no effective speed)
Bard Charms are resisted a LOT. (Remember, CHA is key for Charming.. Most bards in charming mode will have about a 130-160 CHA)
There's a cost of -5- mana for this song as well.

What this means (As I understand it):
The bards will have no weapons in hand, and not be able to assist the 'charmee' in beating on 'enemy'.
Charm will break FREQUENTLY, and resisted a lot trying to get it back up. Average INT for a bard is approx 75-100 (Int is NOT important), so the mana pool will be tiny meaning a limited number of attempts to charm.
No other songs can really be played effectivly while charming (Like the speed song, or healing song).

While this song is effective in it's role (Back up crowd control, and assisting an enchanter) it certainly has a lot of limitations to it as well.
