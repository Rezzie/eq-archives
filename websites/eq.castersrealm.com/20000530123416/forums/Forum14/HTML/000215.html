For high level casters, -10 STR is meaningless. At that level, most people will have a few bags of the tinkerers or other weight reducing bags, and caster armor tends to be pretty light. Also at that level people don't bother to loot coins or weapons. The tome is a really nice item because it can go in the primary melee slot, and offers +hp and +sv poison, easily on par (or better) than the GBS.

The problem with the GBS is that it is a HIGHLY overrated item and commands about 5 times the trade value it should. Don't ask me why, the stats are so-so and it looks pretty stupid (your hand looks like an answering machine with new messages).
