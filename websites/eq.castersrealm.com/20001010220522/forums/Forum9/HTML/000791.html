lol im fine on shaman buffs advice.

Right char and int are said to be equal. BUT charisma is much cheaper and easier to obtain. The general thing is to have plenty of int at early lvl ie pre clarity. You will solo quite a bit and char is not that much use but having plenty of mana will be. Charisma starts to be useful from around 20ish i would guess but i never realy worried myself until about 25 when i started grouping in dungeons seriously.

Staff of writhing is bad news as it is minus charisma. Try water/ice etc staffs, rod of insidious glamour, incandescent wand for 1hb, sac dagger, the dragoon dirk for piercing or rts, staff of the observers for 2hb. Armour well any thing that is int or charisma and fill the spots with mr or mana stuff. At your lvl you probably can twink as badly as you want to. The enchanter quest items are good though, crude stein, stein of mogguk, runed circlet, siryn hair hood, gator arms, drakehide legs, pc jewellery etc etc

I pretty much agree with Frygg.

I found that charisma does nothing till 20th lvl. The next few levels after that it will start becoming very important. Interestingly, I've found charisma to be pretty insignificant at high levels. The MR on high lvl mobs seems to completely negate the benefits of high cha.

My enchanter use to run around with a 196 cha at the expense of int. I have since dropped my charisma down to 156 and I have over 200 int. I've found that my mez works better now (in the planes) than when my cha was at 196. Go figure.
