Ok, it is obvious that GZ's latest email
snippet with NeuroS upset a lot of Wizards, including myself. Now that I have calmed down, I would like to compose a sane response. All I ask is that anyone who posts
a reply does the same. For brevity, I have summarized/paraphrased the Questions and Answers.

Q1. NeuroS proposes we get better mana efficiency out of our old spell ranks.

A1. GZ shoots this down, saying "Why would you ever want another flame spell after level 1?"

Rebuttal: I like NeuroS's idea alot. Raise the dmg caps on the old spells. This will help wizards take down "hall trash" without
a full burn and med cycle. Every other caster with a pet can handle low low greens with NO mana loss. I'm a wizard, I like to nuke, ALOT. This will let me do it, do ok dmg, and not get aggro'd and die in a group. Answer to GZ: Even with NeuroS "improved" mana efficiency, that lvl 1 spell will only do like 25 dmg at lvl 50. Say I root a Hill Giant. Which spell do you think I will use, Ice Comet for 1000+ or the "new" mana efficient frost bolt, for 25? nuff said


Q2. Resist rates for spells, sliding scale

A2. They are.

Rebuttal: No problems here


Q3. Why arent we the "Master's of Damage?"

A3. You just aren't.

Rebuttal: For the sake of game balance, I can
live with this. We can dump a whole lot of dmg in a very short time. On the flip side, often a few resists will leave me with a rooted blue down to a quarter bub of health, and no mana. Hope the zone is close, 'cause melee sure isnt an option, and I burned my "gate" mana.

Q4. Raise the "automatic" resist cap.

A4. Talks about resist rates in general.

Rebuttal: Ok, GZ either misread the question,
or is sidestepping it. I think the auto resist stinks. Apparently there is a powerleveling issue here that they aren't willing to bend on.

Q5. O'Keils is a joke.

A5. I know.

Rebuttal. This spell is actually useful/necessary for soloing to 8th or so, but i'm still not laughing. A precursor to
our shieldskin line would have been more apt.

Q6. Root got nerfed.

A6. No it didnt.

Rebuttal. Doesnt matter, nerfed or not, root stinks. Root is so unreliable I only use in emergency situations or when I am close to the zone. Every other
pure caster has a better chance of staying out of melee: enchanters have root + pet + stuns + wipe hate list + fear, mages have pet + pet root spell, necros have pet + fear + clinging darkness + root. Wizards sorely need spell options here.

Q7. Since root stinks, why can't we stun or something?

A7. Wizards stun great.

Rebuttal: No we dont. I assume GZ is trying
to placate us by telling us our DD spells
have a chance of interrupting a mob caster.
I have never seen this happen. It must be quite rare. Besides, I could care less about
mob casters. See rebuttal for Q6. (Wizards
need spell options to stay out of melee!)

Q8. Small rant, Wizards arent balanced.

A8. Small rant, Wizards are balanced.

Rebuttal: Fine, dont give us more damaging,
faster casting spells or lower the "auto-resist" cap. DO look into increasing
mana efficiency for lower rank spells, and
new spells to "keep mobs off us". A few
utility spells wouldnt hurt either, directional fade, useful sight and information spells.

Stormseer
