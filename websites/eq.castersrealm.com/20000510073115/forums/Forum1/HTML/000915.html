I have seen a lot of posts of international customers feeling very angry and hope Verant will take this seriously and give us a reply.
First of all, here are the facts:
RUK expansion 16.95$
Europe S&H: 25.39$ Total: 42.34$
Australia S&H 57.39$ Total: 74.34$

Many international customers feel this is extremely overpriced.
Now Verant would say „No, it isnt overpriced, go check UPS, they charge about 26$ for europe and 57$ for australia! It’s not our fault the prices are so high“
Agreed, Verant BUT:
These prices are for the best, fastest delivery available.
Have you ever considered that customers might want to wait 1 or 2 days longer und safe 40$?
So i went out and made some price check (oh Verant, please visit www.iship.com, they have a great price checking software) and these are my results:
Europe S&H via USPS:
Small packet airmal - arrival in 4 to 7 days 12$
Make that 3 to 5 days and 9$ if the package fits into a 9 and a half inch by 12 and a half inch envelope
(how about an international shipping version with just the CD and a small box around it – if the RUK manual is like the EQ manual you can keep it anyways since it is only useful to ppl who never played a RPG in their life)
Australia S&H via USPS
Small packet airmail arrival in 4 to 7 days 16.45$

That saves your australian customers 40$ per package!

Finally, some customers complained that they cannot order a bundle for them and their friends – common practice when you do international shipping. You might want to integrate that option into your ordering form.

So to wrap it up i have to conclude that Verants current S&H prices are extremely overpriced.

So what will happen now?
Well, nothing will happen, because:
1. S&H are costs the customers pay, not Verant
2. The customers that feel they are getting ripped off and thus won‘t order the upgrade are of no real importance to Verant, because RUK buyers already ARE paying the 8$ a month and they probably won’t stop playing EQ just because of that.
3. Changing planned shipping and looking for a better way to make their product available to international customers will cause work, thus increase costs.
4. Changing the electronic store in EQ to offer more shipping options will also cause more work and cost.

Finally, i won’t even get a response from Verant for this post, even though and probably because i stuck to facts the whole time and they know i’m right and they would have to agree that they are too lazy to look for a good way to ship, because S&H is the customers problem, not theirs.
This all makes me very sad. I wouldnt even mind paying 42.34$ for the upgrade, but knowing that 60% (almost 80% for the poor aussies) of the money i spend is for delivery because Verant is too lazy to look around for better ways to ship just makes me angry.
I will post this on a lot of newsgroups, because i think it’s based on facts, and adresses problems that are very relevant to many customers.
Oh, and before someone from Verant posts a reply like „we checked, that really is the best price, we cant use the rates you told us, because (enter some excuse here)“ – come on guys! You are a large enterprise! Other companies can ship for less, and so can you!

Now Verant, you can either reply to this post (and i’m very interested in the response)
Or you can chose to ignore it, proving my points 1 to 4.
