Hi all, I've been a lurker here for a while now and finally decided to step forward with a question.

I've recently hit level 47 and have been considering what it must be like to go to the planes, dragon runs and other such events.

Something else should be noted, I'm a Human. I realize that I will probably not see 200 cha and 200 int until the expansion hits, and then only maybe. I have pretty much the best non-planar equipment you can get, aside from freeti boots.

Now I have had other enchanters inspect me and inquire about my INT/CHA. When I created my character I balanced them out to 100/100. Nowadays I usually juggle equipment depending on whats going on, but I can achieve 202 CHA and 169 INT, which I think is not bad for a human.

What disturbs me is what other 'chanters' have said; that my stats are basically 'unsat' for a Planar run.

I'm a pretty reasonable person, but the idea that I might be rejected from a plane-run roster due to my stats really pisses me off.

Thats like telling a warrior 'u sux cuz ur HP's aint over 2k.'

I recall someone saying that CHA over 200 has little to no benefit, just like INT.

I'd really like to hear some opinions from the more seasoned Plane travelling Enchanters around. Whats it like in the planes?

Thanks for your time.

I highly doubt you'll be rejected for a Plane run, because you only have 200 Charisma - that's like saying a Cleric would be rejected because they didn't have a Manastone when they worked in Planes.

As for charisma over 200, it does make a difference, in my opinion. For every 5-10 points of charisma I pick up, I notice differences in resist rates. However as I get it higher, I think it is limited in how much it actually effects resist rates.

Typically I run Planes at 235 (240 now, thx Tranix Crown ) Charisma 179 Int, and have very few problems mez'ing most mobs without tash. There are a few 'problem' mobs that in general have very high resists (Scarelings, Clerics of Innoruuk), both of which require me to tash first, then attempt mez.

I'd suggest toying with Charisma items a bit, get a feel for what works and what doesn't. You may find that 202 charisma is plenty high for you, which is fine, so long as you are happy with it.

LOL...do you honestly think your charisma will get you anywhere in the planes?? With a 200 charisma you already have far more than you need. Having stunned/mezzed stuff many many times in the planes while completely naked, I can assure you that it doesn't do squat there. The benefit of charisma is base d on a sliding scale that is based on the MR of the target. Planes critters have higher MR than most things and therefore it doesn't buy you jack s&*t.

Just get your int to 200. Your int will get you a lot more than your charisma. At an int of 169 you're losing out on 310 mana. That's a lot. That's almost 3 dazzles you can't cast, and on a Fear break those 3 dazzles could save your life.
