Abashi on Clarity Items
The following post was made by Verant Internet Relations Manager Gordon Wrinn about the possibility of adding a clarity item to EverQuest:
Any possibility of a clarity item in game at least?
In all probability, no. This is one of the defining spells of the enchanter class, and makes them very useful to the group. I would suggest that if you find clarity that beneficial, you might want to group with an enchanter regularly.
We don't want to put items in game that eliminate the need for class interdependance. Class interdependance is one of the core concepts of EverQuest and one of the things that makes it so successful.

-Gordon

and then less then 2 weeks later this appears on EQLiser: http://www.gameznet.com/eq/equipment/rodunboundthought.html
So, why is it Wiz only, and are there more items out there with Clarity for other spell casters?

This bugs me on three different levels. The first is that the credibility of Verant and it's employees is starting down hill again. Clarity is one of the most sought after spells in the areas I am in, how can a Verant employee not know an item has been put in the game with this spell?

Second, as Gordon says, Clarity is the enchanters domain and should not really be on an item. I agree with him on this. If they do this, what stops them from give out a rod of undead summoning to summon our pets, but make it Druid only? Thay already have the wands in Befallen that locate corpses.

Lastly, that it is a Wiz only item. Something like clarity should remain an Enchanter only spell or, make the item avalible to all classes that need it.

The only redeeming thing is that it came from a god, so I don't think that we will be seeing to many more of them.
