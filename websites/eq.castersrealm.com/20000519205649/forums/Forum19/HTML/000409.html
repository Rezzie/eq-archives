Well my thoughts (if anyone is interested) on the spells are as follows:

Theft of Thought - Could be real useful for replaceing mana like Necros replace hit points. Great for PvP. All in all I am glad to see it.

Collaboration and Wake of Tranquility will probably not get used by me.

Boon of Clear Mind - Useful in some situations. Glad to see it.

Color Slant - Seems to lend credit to us taking a role as mana givers and mana takers in fights in addition to crowd control. Worried about resist rates though.

Fascination - Could be somewhat useful but will probably only be used in emergencie situations (Can we say Evac). During those situations it could come in real handy.

Rune V - Component cost is a little high but very useful spell. 700 hit points of protection combined with Beserker Spirit will keep you alive for a few more seconds. When you need the extra protection this will be nice to have.

Aanya'a Quickening and Boltran's Agacerie look like stand upgrades to Charm and Quickness.

Cripple - Great for PvP but probably not real useful elsewhere.

Recant Magic - Again useful for PvP but less so for actual combat because it will also wipe debuffs that your teammate cast on the MOB during it's duration.

Clarity II - ('nuff said?)

Dementia - Always nice to have a higher level finishing spell but hopefully I will not need it unless soloing.

Glamour of Kintaz - Since this is an upgrade to Mez one of two things will happen to this spell. Either it will last even longer than the current Mez or it will be resisted less. Either one works for me but I would prefer a lower resist rate.

Shield of the Magi - Nice upgrade to shielding.

Largarn's Lamentation - I think this is a single target stun spell that reduces mana. Could be useful when chain stunning and one MOB resists. Also has other uses including PvP.

Memory Flux - for 350 mana this has GOT to be an unresistable memblurr. No evidence of this though.

Winds of Tishani - Nice but dangerious. Stand far away from the fight when you use this. Would be great if it doesn't taunt much.

55th level Animation - Always nice to have a new pet.

Augment - Decent upgrade to Augmentation and with Clarity II the extra mana shouldn't hurt that much

Invert Gravity - Gravity Flux has limited uses so I imagine this will too.

Overwhelming Splendour - Always nice to have CHR buffs rather than having to wear the items.

Torment of Argli - HP and MANA DOT. I like the idea Combine this with all the other spells we get that suck mana and you will see a pattern here. Are new role....take out the casters.

Trepidation - Another Fear spell. I almost never use the other ones.

Enlightenment - Every caster is going to want this from us. Almost as bad as Clarity II

Forlorn Deeds - Slowing the targets attack speed is always nice. Works exceptionally well for making sure your charmed pet wins against the uncharmed MOB of equal level.

Tashanian - Considering the rate at which the taunt goes up compaired to the MR debuff I might not use this one

Umbra - Nice to have a little extra AC but I rarely use this line on myself.

Bedlam - Combine this with Rune V on you or your favorite Tank and then chain cast this with Beserker Spirit and you have a ton of protection that never gets resisted. Yet another role we can fill in battle. I like it.

Fetter - Another root is useful.....sorta.

Wonderous Rapidity - Haste spell but so far no time duration has been announced. If the time duration is really long this one will be worth it. If not it is back of the book with this one.

Asphyxiate - Not a big fan of our DOT's because it messes up Mez but I guess it has it's uses. Good for PvP.

Gift of Pure Thought - Group Clarity II for less mana than it costs to cast Clarity II 3 times? Works for me

Rapture - Another Mez spell. Could be good if it has something special to it.

Dictate - If it is only 30 secs I doubt I will use it (and I use Charm alot) but if it is a minimum of 30 secs this spell is awesome.

Visions of Grandeur - Rune III, Speed buff and attack buff all in one. I can see many uses for this spell.

Wind of Tashanian - If the taunt is the same as others this will get used by everyone once. You will die and then never use it again

All in all I see alot of good spells on the list. True they are geared towards a group but then we have always been geared that way. Think in terms of what you can do for your tank or to an enemy caster and you will start to see many roles for us on dungeon raids making us as desired as ever in groups that are going to all the coolest places

Now if I can just hit 50th in the next 3 weeks (LOL).
