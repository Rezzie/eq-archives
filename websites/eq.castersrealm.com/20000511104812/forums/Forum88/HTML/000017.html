Believe it or not, I actually believe they got it right this time. But truthfully. We set out to conquere hate with the idea that we'd be the first ones there. But there was another group and they had already broken the portal. So like decent players we waited an hour or so then the first group moved over to the SW corner and started pulling all the clerics and golems and the such. For a couple hours after that we just sat around the portal and pull the common trash till we could move forward. When we actually did move forward we ended up pulling most of the static rev's from the downstairs area. We got a total of 5 peices for enchanters and 2 mage peices.. which in my opinion totally rules compared to what drops use to be like. I know for a fact that the first group had better luck than we did due to their better positioning.. but all in all we did much better than we would have a couple days earlier. I think the other group got a total of 15 drops.. don't quote me on that.. could be more or less.. But I do know that everyone had fun since hate is no longer so totally boring with no drops.

If yer lookin for warrior/shaman/cleric stuff with little mage enchanter stuff.. head to the old safe corner.

But if yer whole group is willing to bite the bullet and get the mages/enchanters stuff for once.. head to the NE corner.. that's the left when you hit the wall from the portal area. You'll be able to pull the most static rev's from there and hit rev village alot sooner than you would if you started in the old corner.

Good luck y'all..
