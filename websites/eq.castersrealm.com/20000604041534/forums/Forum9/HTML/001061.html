Don't forget that the priest (non-research) classes have some of your spells.

Nullify Magic, for example, you can buy at any cleric/shaman guild and at one of the druid rings.

Other spells you can buy and don't have to research:

  • levitate (druid/shaman);
  • disempower (shaman);
  • endure magic (cleric/druid/shaman);
  • invigor (cleric/shaman);
  • ultravision (Oasis);
  • enstill (cleric/shaman);
  • immobilize (cleric);
  • resist magic (cleric/shaman/druid); and
  • extinguish fatigure (cleric/shaman).

I am currently at Level 35. I have not researched any of the level 34 spells yet. I have been collecting components to make research practice runes. I had a stack of Faerie Wings and a stack of Fire Drake Scales so I went to the Hall of Arcane Science in Freeport to get some parchment. I made practice runes until I ran out of Faerie Wings. Then I just went from vendor to vendor to see if any had some pages for sale. Sometimes players sell their pages to the vendor and you can buy them. I get to one vendor and notice that there are a couple extra spells. I check and the spells are Mana Sieve and Radiant Visage. I couldn't believe my luck. Bought both as fast as I could. Anyway, I would suggest whenever you are in town and have a little extra time go from vendor to vendor to see what they are selling. You can pick up a lot of pages and you never know when some high level Enchanter may have sold some spells that they have researched.
