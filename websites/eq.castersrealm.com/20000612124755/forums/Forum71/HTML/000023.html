5 DOTs doing 2000 pts of damage? You seem to have missed some of the numbers...

Necros end up with 7949 pts of stackable DOT between levels 51 and 60 (including the Shaman ones) with an additional 999 pts of it being Lifetap, a -21 AC, a -45 Str debuff and a 70% Snare effect and this DOESN'T include the DOT damage from the currently undefined Splurt spell, labeled a DOT in the file (which will probably add another 1000 or so pts to the table).

So we are talking *8000-9000* pts of DOT. By comparison, the other DOT class, the Shaman, gets 2 huge DOTs that do a combined total of 3138 pts with NO Lifetapping, Snaring or AC / Str debuffing side-effects for somewhere between a half of the solid numbers of the Necro, but more like a third of the true value, once the combat effectiveness of the 999 pt Lifetap is counted in, since that is 3x better than a Greater Heal. And that being said, I think the Shaman DOTs are awesome. I just think that the Necro ones are even more awesome in toto. (Although they are stupidly named IMO. Pyrocruor? Cessation of Blood? Splurt? Ack!)

I am seeing no reason for the Necro player above to be complaining. Especially comparing the proposed Necro Lifedraw upgrade of 825 pts to the Magicians 990 pt Bolt spell IMO. Do I want a spell that is very hard to cast, or one that is easy to cast and heals me and is rarely resisted?

In DD and DOT damage, the Necro is doing quite nicely for himself with the proposed spells.
