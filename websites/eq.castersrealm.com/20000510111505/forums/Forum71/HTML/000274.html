Well, I had heard it from several people, now I've heard it from a Verant employee...

The new necro res apparently kills the caster when cast. That is why it gives more exp than a similar level cleric res. The idea is as a rescue spell. Dying in kunark is gonna suck. There is only one group gate spot planned for each gg'er, yet the continent is huge. There will be places for melee to bind, but not many. This means that dying can lead to huge downtimes. In comes the necro res.

In a bad fight the necro can FD. Once aggro is clear the necro can res the cleric/paladin, whatever. The necro dies, losing experience, in doing so. The newly ressed cleric/paladin then resses the rest of the group, including the necro. If there is no cleric lvl 58 or above...the person the necro resses gets the best res, 93%, everyone else must settle for a revive/or 90% res.

I really hope this is in fact what spell does, I think this is a really innovative spell that will help tremendously as an emergency spell.

We have to wait and see.
