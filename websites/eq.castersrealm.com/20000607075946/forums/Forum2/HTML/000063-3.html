Well, everyone seems pretty well-thought out on this idea, after the initial flames died down. But I think I am going to have to side with the wizards on this one. Casting time and recasting time simply isn't important at level 50. They could have a spell that couldn't be cast for another 30 seconds and be completely happy with it, because they have to wait that long before recasting in the first place. Every comment made by GZ about casting time makes me wonder exactly how many group fights he has ever been in on dragon raids or planar fights. here's a clue... no one in the group cares that a wizzie can burn all their mana in less than a minute. Its a nice skill to be able to flout around, but for all common purposes, useless. Everything at high levels is about efficiency, and if the lead designer of EQ can't figure that out, then maybe I, too, will go try ShadowBane when it comes out.

ok, so everyone wants to say wizzies do all DD and all magik attacks. Fine, make a <b>MORE EFFICIENT</b> DD attack for them at high levels. The idea a previous poster had about being able to link-cast a lower damage spell at a more efficient rate sounded like the best idea to me. And another good idea I heard was the EXTREMELY large radius AE DD spells, though I would also add that those spells should be implemented in a way to make them agro the MOB a lot less of the time. And that AE line of spells or spell would have to be VERY mana efficient, or you're going to end up pissing off the bard/enchanter in your group that had all the MOBs standing still.

"But wait! That would make the Wizard a god class!"

So? What exactly do you think a Necro is?

"They could have a group of 5 wizards and a cleric just AE nuking everything to death!"

Wow, just like a group of 6 Necros killing everything they want at will? Simply put, nothing will make the Wizard a god class, because it will remain one-dimensional. But making their spells more efficient so that they can fill their role at higher levels isn't going to hurt the game. It will make the game better.

Those are the primary things for battle.

And on another note... Who cares if we kill dragons and gods? Its a game, and we want the exceptionally nice loot that those high level MOBs drop. Its the only way to get it, so we do it.

And if you believe that EQ is Duke Nukem in God-mode, you're playing it the wrong way. The way I see it, EQ is the longest treasure hunt you can play with a bunch of your friends. Some treasure is more valuable to you, some of it you can sell to others who want it. Duke Nukem God-mode makes it so you get a BFG and go kill everything that you want. Completely different premise from raising Wizard efficiency.

But on the side of the other classes, Wizards shouldn't compare their DD spells to other DD spells, because they are still different. Every Enchanter DD also casts a megataunt. Lots of Cleric DD spells cast a stun, which adds to the taunt. Magicians DD should be lowered at higher levels in my opinion, but their class should also gain more "focus" objects and spells to make up for it. And Finally, Wizards Root should be changed in SOME way to make it more desirable than a snare from a Druid/Ranger.

Other secondary issues that could make Wizards better at teleportation than Druids is to allow their evacs faster casting time than druids. like a full second, not some 0.2 balogna. The idea to summon players or MOBs sounded good to me, and another idea might be to allow wizards to cast a "shadow step" style spell on another team member, such as a Cleric or Enchanter that is getting banged up. That alone would make them desirable, but who knows... that type of spell could become more of a double-edged sword than Charm is for Enchanters.

Of course, these are all just secondary niceties that are thrown out. The primary issue is with the <i>efficiency</i> of Wizards in combat. Right now there is none.
