I've seen her outside Guk a number of times. Your best bet is to catch her late at night when the zone is empty, so no one will kill her. When I finally decided to do the quest, she was at -100, -300, just standing between two trees. I know because I had to make 17 trips between her and Stragak to sell the booby prizes.

P.S. -- you will need wolf form or an evil illusion to deal with her, and each time you pull the handle on the slot machine you lose quite a bit of faction with troll guards. Enjoy!
