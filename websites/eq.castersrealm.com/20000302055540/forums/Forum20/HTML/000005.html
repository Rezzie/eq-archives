20-24 was simple... just mind numbing...
Lake Rathetear.
I was able to solo the aviak guards even white ones all the way to 24.
med to full. Alac your BLUE pet..
Tashin to pull.. debuff like a fiend and step in when needed.. thats it..
could usually heal&med to full in time for the spawn(single guard on plank) OR have enough to do both birds(tower). Not to mention some ok loot. one night i came away with 117 gold.. thats was alot for me, not to mention words out the ass, and sometimes bronze weapons..

25-26 on gargoyle island or the island of the dead in the OOT. For those who dont know, to get there, take the boat from freeport to the sisters of erollsi(SP) island.. get on the dock.. go straight out from the dock to the island in front of you..Oracle island or goblin island as it may be called, make a right.. or whatever direction it may be, i never checked.. leave gob/orcale island and you should see a burnt out, dead looking place.. thats it The greater skeletons are nothing to worry about, but are rumored to affect the spawn rate of the gargoyles.. You want to solo a garg... pull him to the lil hill with a tree on it that you most likely passed when swimming over.. nothing ever seems to wonder by there... and avoid the specters.. If you lucky you get a eye as loot.. along with other things.. the lil babies sell for 9plat+ to a merchant.. not bad

at 26 your probably ready to commit suicide after being on the island long enough to level, with nothing but dull grey landscape and the undead for company.. i took off right after the ding.. sides most gargs went green at that point.. that about ended my solo career.. which i dont mind..

Have fun..

I haven't spent much time in Ocean of Tears, because it's so remote, but that's probably one of the reasons why it's good - less people to compete with.

I spent a bit of time in Lake Rathe also. There is a beggar named Jonas that is all by himself way back in a corner that you can just sit there and kill every 6 minutes up to about 23. You'll get a lot of water this way =)

Grouping with one other person in the 20ish range is great for the Lake also. It's not usually too crowded there and beats camping the Aviaks hands down. I'd say that at 24 you've outlived the Lake's usefulness with the exception of the froglock if you can find it (not the Prince)

It's MUCH more fun to get over to Cazic, Guk, Sol A, or Permafrost with a good group. And the experience of it all is way better. Typically in Cazic (at 23+ with a group) you'll be fighting gators for insane training and occasionally getting into a group for the throne room or other great places. That's the beauty of being an Enchanter. Even though our spells may not effect the monster because it is deep red, we can still turn the tide of battle with Quickness/Augmentation, Rune, mesmerise the blues that come along, animation... the list goes on.

Killing deep reds with a good group will always be the best and most fun way to adventure in Norrath. It's worth your while to take the time and find people who are smart enough to group with Enchanters. Sometimes we need to solo (and w***ver said Enchanters can't solo needs to rethink their strategies), but even in our 20's most smart groups are taking us in. If they don't then it is their loss and you're better off not in that group anyway.
