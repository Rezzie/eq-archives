The place is a bit of a maze, so it is hard to give directions. If you know where the Ancient Croc spawns, you are close. I will describe the room.

The walls look like rock covered with the egyptian type art that is of green men profiles. The center of the room is sunken in and 4 Froggies stand in the shallow water there. Two alcoves on either side have stairs leading up to them and a tougher Froglock stands before a seat on each one. There are only two exits to the room.

I hope that that is enough to let you recognize the room when you see it. There is a widening in the hall just outside the room that is a good place to pull to if you have a group going with you. The priest himself was green to me at 32 and one of the frogs in the room coned even to me, so a bit of a mixed crowd. I ended up bringing three groups of varying levels with me, and we just cleaned house till all three enchanters in the groups had a rod.

Good luck getting it.

If you know where the squire is then its easy. Goto the back of that long hall tht he is by, jump down the ladder. you will be in a small room. go out of the room and hang a left. At the next room hug the right wall until you get out of the room. Follow the hall. You will come to a big room with 3 levels. 2 spawns on each of the raised sides, 3 spawns in the pit and one wandering(i think). The priest is one of the spawns in the pit.
