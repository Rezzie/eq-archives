These days (40th level) I just simply say "/g Please use Assist" whenever my Mez is broken on something. Occassionally I have found the problem was the fighter switched targets just after the pull in an effort to take out the deadliest MOB first and at other times it has been me targetting the wrong MOB (too far away from it for it's HP bar to move when it takes damage. Everyone seems to know about Mez at the upper levels and if they do hit a mezzed mob it is purely by accident so the above statement is all I ever have to say (and I don't even have to say that that often). At lower levels I would keep Gate memmed and memblurr things as you Mez them (memblurr still works pretty good per 35th level). If someone does attack something you have Mezzed they will have it beating on them while you help the main tank fight whatever he is fighting. Let them take damage at first and then explain about Mez after the fight. If they still won't listen then let them die (just make sure the rest of the group doesn't.

Eventually even the most powerleveled, twinked, clueless individual will learn: Break Mez bad ting, not break mez gud ting.
