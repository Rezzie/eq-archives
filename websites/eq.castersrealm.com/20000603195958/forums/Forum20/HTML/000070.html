Here are my Hate plane observations.
(correct me if i'm wrong)

Most stuff CAN be mezzed if properly debuffed. (tashania, malosi, malisement).

Ditto for the Color series. Although some maybe be immune, like giants, you will get a msg saying they're stunned, but they'll continue to swing. Revulnant(sp?) Rats for example.

Spite Golem is immune to all spells. Only got tashania and pillage enchantment to work.

Some stuff are charmable, good targets are abhorrents and liches.

We were using Entrance instead of Dazzle, not really sure why. Since we're all 50, we have no problem overriding each other's mezzes. Big plus.

All the encs co-ordinate with each other whether to mez or charm.

My spell list at the moment was:
color shift, color skew, allure, SLTW/buff slot, clarity/buff slot, pillage enchantment, tashania, entrance.
