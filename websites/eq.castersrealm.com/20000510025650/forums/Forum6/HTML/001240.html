The reason that we don't get varying looks to our pets is that the video card memory requirements that are on the EQ box (4mb) don't allow them to load the new models in *every* zone.

The current pet models are loaded every time you zone, no matter what zone you're in, so that you can see a mage's pet. That takes up video card memory, and they've hit their current limit on the amount of stuff they can load to fill the 4mb requirement.

With Kunark though, they're upping that memory requirement, so hopefully there'll be plenty of new looks to pets and other stuff too
