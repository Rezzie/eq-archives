Try both. I've only played a magician to level 6, but an enchanter to 30 so far. Everything has it's ups and downs, but both are fun. The enchanter has the ability to get in almost all towns (all towns with agnostic and no ruined faction). They crowd control is great and so is clarity which is a great group friendly spell as we all know. They stink at damaging, but can make up for it with other skills.

If you like a little more soloability go with the mage. Decent DD, free edibles, bandages, throwing knives, a kickbutt pet, and damage shields make you both good in and out of a group. I may start one up again soon and see how good they can be.
