Nesuril here. Enchanter of the 25th season who happens to enjoy charming most of his pets. Has anyone noticed that charming is just really bugged in some zones? If so, has Verant ever addressed this potentially life-threatening concern? I've seen several things happen when I hit a monster with charm or beguile:

1. The mob charms fine and everything is peachy, save when it breaks 10 seconds after.

2. I hit the monster with charm and nothing happens! I don't get a resist message. Nothing. It just as if the mob didn't get charmed at all. One time I charmed a gargoyle in Mistmoore who proceeded to attack me while his intiate familiar friend beat on him. That sounds pretty bugged to me.

3. You charm your mob and it amazingly disappears! Goes off somewhere and vanishes from sight. You get the 'Your Charm' has broken message. I just tell my party to zone, because you know it pulled the whole damn place down around your head. Usually a minute later, the whole zone shows up in one mass mob, your screen goes red with damage and you die.

Now, I'm sure there are other instances of this happening and maybe its zone specific, but this is a problem of SERIOUS proportions. Namely because first off, its wasting your mana. Second of all, because you usually on get one or two chances to charm before your lifebar starts degrading, you need to make sure this works the first time and it works right. I've spent a lot of effort and time trying to get my tactics down, but it sucks when you train the zone with tons of stuff due to a computer bug. Does anyone know if this is old news or if they are gonna look into these problems? I petitioned a guide who said to mail Verant staff. Baelish, if you know anyone out there, can you check to see if they are aware of this stuff? I'd help a lot. Thanks.

/pet guard here has always worked for me, while /pet sit down always makes them wander off in a little while. The only time i use that one is when i want to see a specific creature type sit down. Giants don't sit but giant skeletons might be fun to see.

As far as pets attacking group members, /pet back off usually works, just make sure it's not on a mage pet. You and the mage just have to mash the back off key until they stop, it's not fun, probably the same for necro pets. Once you sic it on something, it will usually stay on it unless something hits it, so the pet is far from useless.

An interesting thing along the lines of bugged "you charm spell has worn off" messages. We had a pull of 3 seafuries the other day, so i charmed one and put him on the main target. Then i tashed the second, pulled him away a bit to root, but forgot to change hotkey sets. Root is in the same slot as charm, so i got the "you may only have one pet at a time" message, slapped myself in the forehead and rooted him. About 30 seconds later, I get the break message, and wait for the pet to come beat on me. But he doesn't, so i hit F1 twice, and sure enough, he still shows up as my pet. Later I got his break message. I'm guessing that even though the second charm didn't go through because I already had a pet, it still got counted, and so i got a break message for it.
