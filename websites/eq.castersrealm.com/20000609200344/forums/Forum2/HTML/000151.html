Necro pets are nasty, how nasty?

I heard the reason for the feign death nerf was because 6 necros got together, killed all the outlying things around vox(perhaps after a failed vox raid before the area respawned)

Summoned their pets, buffed them, equiped them, sent them in and all feign death.

Vox would kill the pets off one by one, because the necros were feign deathed out side of roar range, she would ignore them, fight would end, necros would stand, resummon pets, repeat.

End result, dead vox, via 6 necros alone, with their pets.

Some time later, feign death was nerfed.

Btw, all level 49 pets are powerfull, mage pets have obscene number of hitpoints, like 3000-4000 hits, and hit for 54 damage, while casting spells.

Necros hit the hardest, and have a good amount of hits.

Enchanters, enchanter 49th pet has been known to kill a necro 49th pet, both buffed by their respective classes, enchanter pets are NOT weak.

Buff then enchanter pet with beserker spirit, swift like the wind, give it a fs dagger(for a fast duel wielding effect), and watch it shred the necro pet.

It wont hit as hard as the necro, but it will hit insanely fast(at up to 52 damage a hit) Enchanters can also put up a decent(11 point) damage shield on their pets.

All 29+ pets will massacre the melee types of the same level as the caster.

Dunno why pets are so strong, I guess they would be worthless if not so, but they are nasty.

Of course, your looking at very specific cases, in a real world dungeon, a level 50 warrior is more usefull then a necros pet, the warrior is vastly smarter, and that makes a big difference, of course, if u can have both, thats a nice bonus.

Enchanter pets are almost worthless in most settings due to being uncontrollable, so, while are 49th pet(with 49 buffs) can out do a necro pet in a 1v1(it does in fact favor the necro pet, but its possible for the enchanters to win), they are fairly useless in dungeons.

But if your a bored 50th enchanter, and goto some super green dungeon, buff your pet, and watch it shred everything, it can be fun.
