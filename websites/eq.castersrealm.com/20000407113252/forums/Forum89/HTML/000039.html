Triton/Rising Ascension from Povar attemped pegasi island the past weekend, although we failed, there were a few things we learned.

1. They can be pulled down to temple(gorg) island.

2. They are impossible to kite now( faster then sow ). So seperate to 2 groups engaging each split is probably the best tactic.

3. The first non-KOS incarnation, essense harvester, is Mezzable. If the 2nd essense harvester joins in the the middle. Have a enchanter keep it dazzled for the entire duration. An enc can keep it mezzed indefinitely. So there isn't really a need to camp, just med, rebuff and start again with that particular enc camping.

4. When pulling the pegasi down to 3rd island, 1 flew back to 4th and the other got stuck in temple like the bladestorm bug. So this can be used to seperate the 2 essense harvesters.

5. The argo range of the soul harvester and soul tamer is ridiculously large. You will argo them from temple island when they are on pegasi island.

Once we regain our lost exp and make some more funds, our guild will attempt at them again Hopefully next time we will triumph

I believe the way to pull them to the third island would be by jumping. The fourth islands edge is very close to the third island. I'll probably find out tonight about this.

Also in reference to dealing with the two spawns. It seems to me the way to do this is after you kill the first spawn you devote all casters, damage dealing tanks (rogues/monks/rangers) and a good portion of true tanks to one, and a skeleton tank group to the second. You really don't want the second one to die quick since it's the tamer will start doing damage much quicker. Have shamans debuff both beasts but especially the second one with just about anything they have that will lower it's damage capacity. Nukers nuke the first one take it out quick to get to the tamer then med. DOT casters can use this time to load a few DOTs on the second soul harvester. Once the first tamer is dead the second harvester is near finished, nukers blast it quick and release the tamer and then all the tanks jump it.

If you can Kite it or use something like Eye of Confusion all the better but I don't know if those would work.
