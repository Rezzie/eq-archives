After seeing the changes made to the dmg numbers, it looks like no matter how they adjust the dmg of Wiz/Dru/Mag DDs, they intend to let Druids be a clear No. 2 in DDs ((((. I no longer have hopes of Mages being upgraded back to No. 2 as they have been from lvl 1 to 50 (taking into account alternate spell rank differences). Why are they changing the status quo?

Pet dmg should not be accumulated with nuke dmg to arrive at a balance. Mages get pets and Druids get buffs/utility spells and that is where the balance is. Mages get stronger pets and Druids get stronger bufs/utility spells at the same time. So changing the DD balance is a change in status quo no matter how much more dmg Mage pets can do.

Mage highest DD gets upgraded from 600 to 825 (and highest Bolt from 810 to 961) while Druid highest DD gets upgraded from 612 to 1037. When you see these numbers side by side, you will see how insane it looks and a very deliberate intention to change the status quo.

Although I was truly disappointed in seeing the new dmg numbers for Druid lvl 59 DD and Mage lvl 57 DD/lvl 59 Bolt, I must admit I was relieved to see that the Mage lvl 54 Bolt (2 sec cast/delay) was not changed. Not since lvl 8 have Mages got a nuke with such a low casting time. This one better remain unchanged if they are determined to make Mages No. 3 after Druids in DDs.
