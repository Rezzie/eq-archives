ON CLARITY ITEMS

Abashi said recently that items which have a Clarity effect would not be introduced into the game. I have had a few people email me about the Rod of Unbound Thought, wondering how this can be explained.

Abashi was referring to items which give permanent or unlimited charges of Clarity. The Wizard item in question has five charges only, and is a random drop off the God Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate. It has been in game since the zone went live at the end of October 1999, and only a few have been found in total across all the servers since then.

Since the item itself is quite rare, and there are only five charges of Clarity on it, I really don't think the item is anything for the Enchanter class to be concerned with. Five charges will only last a little over two hours of gameplay.

I hope this clears up the fact that this item does not give wizards a way to obtain Clarity on a permanent basis, and that Enchanters are still the only true dealers in this spell, with of course the exception being Bard's and their own method of boosting mana regeneration.

ABASHI ON AC BUG

Abashi mentioned on the gameplay forum that there is a known bug with AC. It is known to definitely affect Paldins, but other classes could be bugged also. A patch should be on the Test Server soon to fix it.

Basically the bug "caps" AC so that the AC number displayed in the information screen cannot raise past a certain point regardless of equipment or spells.
