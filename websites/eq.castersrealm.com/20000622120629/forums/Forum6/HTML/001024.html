OK, I feel that the current role of the magician class (the ONLY role we're truly able to play) is as a primary damage dealer. IMO, the current rankings for dealing dmg in a high level fight (lv45+) are...
  1. Necromancer
  2. Magician
  3. Wizard
  4. Shaman

If you look at the expansion spells, the new order of dmg dealing will be (without a doubt):
  1. Necromancer
  2. Wizard
  3. Druid
  4. Magician
  5. Shaman

This is regardless of the length of fight, number of targets, level of targets. We're being ousted, BADLY. Look at the list, and consider the following...

Necromancers: also heal, regain mana and life for themselves, debuff, buff a little, can shield themselves (less healing needed), and can perform miracle escape spells.

Wizards: Can also teleport, and now come with shieldskin that also helps regen mana.

Druids: Heal (big in expansion) and Regen, Teleport, Dmg Shield, Huge Buffs, Big Debuffs, Spirit of Wolf (uprgade in expansion), loads of group version spells.

Shaman: Heal (big in expansion) and Regen, SoW (and upgrade), HUGE buffs, HUGE debuffs (magic and slow).

Magicians: summon arrows, summon bread/water, summon arrows in a container, summon bread/water in a container, summon rod that takes away 225 of your HP, summon even more arrows!!

Wow, we're set! Who wouldn't want us in their group! Woohoo! :P
