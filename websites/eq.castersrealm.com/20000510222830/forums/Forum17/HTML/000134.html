I am KoS to the Neriak Guards as well.

As said invis up to the library and you can talk to Miz no problem. I have currently only presented my self to him as a Dark Elf though.

The rune of fortune or whatever its called from MM is pretty easy to get. Unlikely for a level 17 btw.
But you walk all the way through the ancille valley invis until you come to the back wall.
Once at the back wall you will see a ledge to your left if you look back the way you came. On this leddge will be Enyti (she drops rune) and 2 scree??? gargoyles (I forget the names) It appeared to me that I could buff no problem at the back wall, since I was far enough away not to agro them on the ledge. Be warned there is a roaming ancille as well.

Anyway once I created a pet Enyti agroed..since my pet walked down and got her attention. Well the fight was easy, I dropped a Discordant Mind and rooted her.. My pet just ate her up in about 10 secs after that.
The gargs then agroed as I looted, but I got the rune first time and gated out..

Basically Enyti is very easy to kill. I have no idea why I did not try this when I was younger..
