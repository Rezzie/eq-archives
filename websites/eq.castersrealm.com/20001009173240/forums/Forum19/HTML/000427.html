I have used this spell on Hill Giants over the past few weeks....

With Snare I think it is a little bit better than Fear for 2 reasons...

1) It costs 25 mana/vs. 40 for our lowest level fear spell...

2) You can control where the Hill Giant goes a little bit better than fear....

With snare/sow you usually just run around and cast..... hoping not to get hit all the time.....

With Fear you chase after the HG....Hoping to kill it before it runs near another HG/mob...

Eye of confusion allows you to stop the HG just by running up to it.....It also is pretty quick to cast, and has a good duration....

The way we do it.....I cast just after Snare.....Then we blast as it is running away....as soon as i see the Message I back up to our origional spot and recast again....
That way we keep the HG in our area....

I don't use fear that often, but Don't they mostly run streight away from you?....With Eye of Confusion they tend to Ping pong back an forth....mostly going in one direction but they keep turning around and back tracking a few steps so they are not totally running away from you.....
