With overcrowding being the issue that it is, Verant has said that one of the ways they plan on addressing the issue are by upgrading underutilized zones such as Kerra Ridge, Najena, etc. They've also stated in the past that if the loot is good enough, the players will come.

I disagree with their approach on improving zones. I feel that most of the issues of a zone go beyond simple loot, and that they should take a look at other things as well.

A good dungeon area has-

1) Good loot. Yep, Verant's right on the mark about that, Dungeons SHOULD contain loot (Kerra ridge anyone? Blech)

2) Good Exp. Even more important then loot, is the ability for players to gain exp from the mobs there. Gators in Oasis drop crummy loot, but Oasis is never short on players. Why? Exp.

3) Players. Much like a pick up game of basketball in the park, many dungeon romps are free-form, catchall, whomever can join affairs. Standard method is often to simply shout to fill openings of your group. Its hard to make a group if there isn't anyone else there. For me, Cazic Thule, LGuk, and SolB are such great zone because there are others there. Not because of the loot that can be had in each of those dungeons. Note that all of these zones are reasonably close to teleport locations, and bindable cities (for evil races and illusioned players at least..) That means to me that a good dungeon must be accessable.

4) Decent pathing. Mobs that attack though the roof (Mistmoore, Unrest), run all about before actually coming to you (Kerra ridge), get stuck (Permafrost, Unrest) make for an unenjoyable gaming experience. Nothing like wondering when the ol' train of death in Mistmoore was about to strike.

5) Manageable Aggro radius. One of the problems I had with the low level area of Kerra Ridge (who knows now with the updated version) was that the aggro radius for the VERY FIRST AREA was so big that if you attacked one mob, you'd wind up with 6-9 others as well. All they had to do was remove 2 spawn points from the bridge and the area would have been manageable. This same problem of aggro radii made the "improved" Paw a horrible dungeon as well. Not to mention the pathing problems...

6) An absense of Caster Mobs. While the ubercamped dungeons of SolB, LGuk, and Cazic all have casters, they don't have too many. Unlike Paw, where you have rooms of nothing BUT casters (complete with pets) or Permafrost with many casters close to one another.

Hmm, well thats all I can think of off the top of my head. What do you think should be changed to make dungeons worth visiting?

Heres hoping that Kerra Ridge wasn't screwed up as bad as Paw was. Come to think of it, it would be hard to make it worse then it already was...

I will responsed to your points in kind

1) Good Loot is required but PAW proves that making the loot carrying spawn every 8 to 36 hours does not work. Make sure the loot equals the risk and that the time requirements also equal both the risk and reward. Kedge Keep and Paw are both excellent examples of dungeons that dont reward the player for the risk.

2)Good experience is important, give the players to opportunity to established foot holds in the dungeon where they can get good exp even if they dont get loot (Lguk bedroom is a good example). Note : Oasis is heavily camped because it is easy, SRO has the same exp with better loot but rarely camped because it is more difficult.

3) The goal of a good dungeon is to attract and challenge players, cant really make it a requirement if it is also your goal.

4) Pathing fixes in Mistmoore and Kedge would greatly enhance the playability of those dungeons. Kedge has serious pathing problems whereas MM has Agrso radius problems that would need to be fixed also.

5) Mistmoore would be a great place if the agrso radius was reduced by 25%. Considering the dungeon has more mobs than any other dungeon and they are tightly packed to boot, it is easy to see the problems that causes.

6)Totally agree. Since NPC mobs are poorly balanced, a caster mob is much more difficult that it should be. Should a level 35 caster really be able to double attack for 80 as well as cast spells?

I would add:

7) Named spawns that offer good loot.

8) Gradual Increase in diffculty - Paw is a could example where the Mobs past the double doors are significantly more difficult that those before the door.
