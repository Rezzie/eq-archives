NECROMANCER RESEARCH

The true powers of the necromancer cannot be found by simply wandering into your nearby guild and begging for the pitiful scrolls they allow guild members to etch into their books.  No, the truly terrifying spells which make the necromancer one of the most feared adversaries in Norrath are scattered throughout Norrath waiting to be recombined into a form which the Necromancer can twist to their will.

The ancient words are the fundamentals of Necromancer research.  Once the necromancer has reached their fifth circle (level 16) they earn the right to learn the fundamentals of this essential skill.  The Skill of Researching rises very quickly.  Generally you will get a point of skill for every attempt, or every second attempt.  Pouring all your spare points into research isn't really a good idea early on.  You may wish to save these for trade skills or some other purpose. 

Researching for necromancers requires two things: the Book of Dark Bindings, which is purchased from the Necromancer Guild, and the appropriate Ancient Words.  The words themselves are scattered throughout Norrath on various NPC's.  The Words for a given spell will generally be dropped on monsters whose level is between that of the spell and five levels above the spell.  Some monsters in some zones will only drop a certain word, you will often have to travel and hunt a variety of Norrath's pitiful lifeforms in order to find your treasure.  Many of the Norrath's Undead Overlords prefer to trade other artifacts they may find in their travels for the words they need, and this can save a great deal of time.To combine the spell itself you will have one of three options.  

To attempt to create the spell once you have the Words, you simply place the two words into the Book of Dark Bindings, and hit combine them with your mystic power.  IF you are successful they will be bound into the form of a scroll which you can then inscribe into your spell book.  If you fail to focus enough whilst researching, your words will vaporize and you will be left with nothing but the dust.

Now that you know how to combine, you then have two choices.    You can either jump straight away into combining spells, or you can attempt to do the research quests first and raise your skill through these. 

To commence the research quest go to the Southern Desert of Ro.  Seek out an ancient Dark Elf sorceress now disguising her evil art through trade.  If you speak the right words to her she will aid you in learning the Dark Art of Research, without sacrificing the Ancient Words.  You can research the same spell more than once, and this is often a good way to raise your skill once you have learnt all which the ancient can teach you.

Keep in mind also that some research spells can be purchased from other casting class guilds.  A careful infiltration of the other mages scroll shops can often yield surprising results.
