When I heard Brad say that they tested drops in the new Hate on the test server and the report of drops was good I had to give a little chuckle. Not sure who he talked to, but I was asked to go to Hate with a group of non-GM testers. They probably did their own tests, and maybe cleared the zone and counted drops. The unfortunate thing is that the zone is still not cleared every time you go, and the new 'safe' spot is about 30 feet north of the old spot. Now that the patch is on live servers as well, I can talk about it.

With the amount of people we had, we never got to Revenants. Grand total when we gave the report to the GM was 7 cleric, 5 warrior, 3 bard, 1 shaman armor drops. We stayed around for a bit after that and got some more warrior and cleric items, but still no enchanter or mage. We went back on our own the next few nights with similar results. I think the puller was feeling my frustration a bit when he tried to bring us some female statics that ended up being a total party annihilation train. We did get one female rev there, but no enchanter drop.

On my 4th trip post loot fix I was breaking in a new cleric, helping him get to the camp out spot and letting him know what to expect. After one more trip, he is wearing 5 pieces of Barney armor. I am consistently one of two, or the only, enchanter present and normally we can have 4 to 6 clerics. Can't really hold a grudge against your friends that you go there with, but some veteran clerics now have no reason to go back, and I get to help the plane newcomers get decked out. Not sure how long before I have the items I want and will be able to move on, but at my current rate (0%) it may take a while. I guess it is not so bad, my real strength is in the new spells I will be getting after 50. Got all the pages for my level 49 spells that I did not already have on my first trip. Now I hand out researched level 49 spells like candy to any enchanter I see that is over level 40.

I did get one tease. Sunday there was a party there during the day, and they were adding members for Maestro when evening came and that means that Female revenants could be next. We all got up there and the server was taken down for a famous test server poorly timed patch. We got back in an had a respawn, so we started back at getting piles of cleric and warrior items.

I had a chat with GM after my 4th trip with the 'new and improved' drop rate and it seems they were aware of the discrepancy of items among the classes. According to him the mob placement has been mixed up some in hopes of remedying this. I am not the puller, so I could not verify this, but we were not getting more female revenants in the amount of area cleared as far as I could see. They also said they took out the drops on the roamers. Not sure that this helped much. I know that people were content to pull roamers for loot, but the real problem with the roamers is that they keep the party from being able to move in on areas that were cleared. It also slows progress on getting to the NE corner spawns since you have to fight the respawned roamers between pulls.

I know this all sounds like more "why me" whining, and well...I guess it is. I will be back in Hate again all this week so after a few more trips I will try to put together a good /feedback. I still think they are aware of the loot problem, so maybe there will be more changes to come.

Merilin Mistweaver
