Went to Plane of Hate last night again. We died alot but we killed alot too. A monk was pulling for us and we were doing well.

Up until the Monk fell asleep at his keyboard.

So here are 34 peeps with one asleep Monk. The event ended then. No more Hate.

Why??

Because without the Monk Fiend Death Ability, if a warrior tries to pull even one mob, the chain reaction on mobs would cause one seriously HUGE train.

I was able to see through the eyes of a Necromancer and see where all the mobs where located and how each one will cause a domino effect.

Now I pose the question to Verant: Why is this so? Why do places like Plane of Hate and Fear are so dependent on the Fiend Death class of the Monk??

I have looked far and wide and all I read about is that the best/only way to pull in those places is with a Monk. Many big guilds use this tactic. From what I read this is the best way.

If you go with a Warrior puller, expect the deaths of the team to be much greater. So anything less then a Monk means way harder odds and more chance of straight corpse retrivel all night.

Even Monks will pull 3-to-TRAIN lvl mobs.

All night my guild was saying things like, "This can't be the way Verant made things? There is no way that one zone should be so dependent on the Monk class!!"

I really really DOUBT that Verant actually plays in these zones. I doubt they realize how difficult they are.

I support them for making a challenging zone. Hell if it was that easy it would suck and be like Lower Guk then. But what I am talking about is Class dependence to the EXTREME!! This should not be.

My guild does not have a Monk. We can goto the Ghoul Lord room and back. We can go through the Imps in SolB, wave and the Efreet and head back. We can swim to Phingel in Kedge Keep and have tea(10-12 of us only). BUT from what I can tell, we don't stand a chance in Fear or Hate without a monk.

That my peers is bad design.
