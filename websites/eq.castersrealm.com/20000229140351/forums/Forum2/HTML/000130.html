I'm really tired of people whining that other people are better than them. From what I see, every class has strengths and weaknesses. There are times when, I as a magician thought I really sucked. However, this happens to every class.

Before you whine about magicians getting their focus objects, know this: pets were nerfed. a half a month or so ago, all mage pets from level 24 and up were changed so that there was a wider pet level range. For example, my level 20 pet spell can summon a pet in the 19-21 range. My level 24 spell can summon a pet in the 20-24 range. No longer can you summon a pet past the level of the spell once you hit 24. Also, 39+ pets were weakened. They did this so that when the focus objects were introduced, it would seem as if pets can be more powerful. In reality, the focus objects just make the pets what they used to be.

If anyone who likes to whine reads this, stop whining because I dont like it. Classes are pretty fairly balanced with the exception that not all skills are correctly implemented.
