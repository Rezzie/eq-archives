Copied directly from the Casters Realm news arcives:

October 22-
Some clarification on some statistic issues though:
The chance of specialisation kicking in for Wisdom based casters is effected by Wisdom.
The chance of specialisation kicking in for Intelligence based casters is effected by intelligence.
Dexterity does not effect chances of recovering and successfully casting. It does effect however how often a special procs if you have one on your weapon.
The only skill which effects recovery for casting when interrupted is Channelling. No other skill or statistic effects this.
If specialisation successfully rolls it will effect your chance of fizzling and the amount of mana used.
Higher Intelligence will reduce fizzles for Intelligence based casters.
Higher Wisdom will reduce fizzles for Wisdom Based casters.
Good alternatives for pure mages then are Agility to avoid being hit in the first place or Stamina for extra hitpoints.
October 24-
DEX AND RECOVERY
I have been doing a lot of tests on dexterity and spell casting recovery to see if I can work out the story. I am not sure precisely how it works, these are just my personal findings.

From my tests I didn't notice any difference from 100 dex until I got it up to 120! Quite a big gap. At 120 it seemed that I could handle another hit or so and recover. So my theory is that whilst channelling is the only skill which effects recovery, the channelling skill must make some reference to dexterity.

This means that in an fairly indirect way dexterity will effect your recovery, but you will need to raise it in fairly large amounts to notice a difference.

October 27-
DEX AND RECOVERY - CONTINUED
Okay. Can I just say for now that I am uncertain about the whole relationship. According to my talks with GZ it doesn't directly effect recovery. He is going to go and check the channelling code for me at some point and let me know if it somehow indirectly has a role.

From the emails I have received I can make no clear call either. Some say they think Agility is the key stat, other say Dexterity, others say neither makes a difference.

Then there is the EQ manual which says it does have a role in recovery....but then again it says enchanters can enchant items...

For now all I can say for sure is that Channelling is the major skill which will determine your recovery from interruption. How other stats intermingle we will have to wait and see. I will keep you posted.

Still nothing definate I guess, but maybe this will help a bit.
