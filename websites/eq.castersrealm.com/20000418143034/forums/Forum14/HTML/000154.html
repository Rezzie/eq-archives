Hmm I tried half-elf illusion last night with my SMR on and no purple shirt

Only robes that are tinted versions of other robes (using a palette swap) will show up as colored shirts when you turn into a non-robed race, because the tint carries over even though the graphic doesn't exist (bug #1). SMR, Oracle robe, FBR, and Cryosilk (I think, not sure) all have no tint to them, so nothing carried over. Insidious robe/Robe of the Ishva is a blue swap of the cryosilk, so you end up blue. Robe of the Keeper is a red swap, so you end up red. GSD is a green swap (same primary graphic as the Keeper), so you end up green.

If you turn into any creature, the tint will not show up unless someone zones in after you (bug #2). For those that zone in afterwards, your body section will be tinted. When you refresh the illusion, the tint will disappear for everyone inside the zone.

Actually, if you want to startle someone, go into werewolf illusion with either a red or blue tinted robe, and stand just inside the zone to a dungeon, facing the exit. Remain seated. Because of another illusion bug (bug #3), for everyone who enters the zone after you, you will appear to be standing in a "crucified" position with glazed over eyes. Scary.

P.S. Illusion bug #4: if you turn into a different race with a different height, everyone who zones in afterwards will see you as the new race, but with your original height (this is why you see so many "giant DEs" -- they're ogres/trolls/barbs with a guise).

Illusion bug #5: If you turn into a wolf, everyone who zones in after you will see you as a transluscent wolf.

Illusion bug #6: If you turn into an air elemental, everyone who zones in after you will see you as a bizarre part earth/part fire elemental.

Illusion bug #7: If you turn into a creature with a "dormant" animation (i.e., wolf howling), and you sit down during the animation, you will appear "frozen" to everyone else. Note that the dormant animations appear at different times for different people, because they're client-side.
