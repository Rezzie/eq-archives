Phoboplasm drops are bizarre.

You can get armor that normally drops in Hate, but it seems to be limited to certain pieces. In otherwords, I don't think they can drop ANY piece of armor from hate or fear.

I've also seen them drop ranger legs and cryosilk sleeves. I now wear the sleeves, they are similar in stats to Insidious but not quite as good. But considering that the sleeves are one of the good pieces of caster armor and insidious drops have been completely borked in Hate, I'm damn happy with them.

Jellos do not drop Insidious, nor do they drop Apothic. Basically, they can drop armors which originated in Fear (so they can drop Rune Etched and Woven Shadow). However, they cannot drop all pieces, from my experiences, ex: Rune Etched Boots and Woven Shadow Bracers seem to be the only drops off jellos for those classes.

Also, as for the Amulet of Necropotence, sure it is a nice piece, but, as you start going to Planes, you'll probably find the minus 100 hp from the Amulet to really be a pain.

Oh, and, Insidious Robe is far better than Crimson Robe.
