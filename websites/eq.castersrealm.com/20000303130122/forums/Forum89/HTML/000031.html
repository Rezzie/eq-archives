We cleared it by taking over people when there was a lull in the Azaraks patrol near the teleport point.

The Azaraks have a set pattern the fly in and if you use a scout to monitor their path and tell everyone else when to teleport over you can set up and be ready for them when they come. Tanks have to get the Azaraks on them because Azaraks dont hit that hard or that well and a good tank can have one beating on him for quite awhile while the magic users take em down.

Mes is useless and Mem-blus dont work but there are a couple of Enchanter spells that are ESSENTIAL for taking down Azaraks...and they are probably buried on page 50 of your spell book
