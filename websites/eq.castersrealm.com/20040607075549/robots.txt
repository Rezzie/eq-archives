	<td valign='top' align='left' class='news' width='90%'><p class='boldheader'>SOE POLL CAUSES STIR</p><font face='verdana' size='2pt'>As many of you may have noticed, SOE began a poll when you login to EverQuest today asking if players would like SOE to provide a secure/safe way to transact items for real currency.<P> The poll has caused quite a stir with some people asserting the poll as a claim that SOE will begin selling items and characters and others arguing it would ruin any chance of having a real in0game economy.<P> Alan Crosby posted a short clarification about SOE's intent with the poll on the EQ Live forums, explaining that the poll was released to obtain player opinions in such an option: <ul> Hey folks a couple of things here: <P>  1. It is just a poll.  We wanted your opinion.   <P>  2.  It did not ask if you wanted us to start selling items or characters.  It asked if you would like us to help facilitate these types of transactions and make them secure.  We are not considering selling items or characters.    <P> I know that this is a passionate issue and has many of you heated.
