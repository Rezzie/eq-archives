Just like any other research item, for a spell of level X you need to kill level X to X+4 mobs. At level 40 you'll never find it on your own beacuse you won't be killing level 24-28 mobs any more (I hope =P).

There are about a billion places where you can find level 24-28 pages, but to name a few of the better ones: South Karana, Highkeep, Solusek A, Permafrost, Runnyeye, Upper Guk. Basically any area that's targeted for level 25-30 players will have these pages.
