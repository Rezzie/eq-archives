Greetings --

I went after the rings in my 29th season. First off, I camped Gynok the elusive named mummy who spawns right at the entrance to befallen. 23 hours straight to be exact, and during which I passed the time killing everything that spawned on the 'platform' (the area before the first set of steps), and the first floor. Even so, it is a very tidious camp and I can not attest to anything regarding the first floor spawns that will make him appear any faster. All that is for sure is that you must kill all of Gynok's placeholders (i.e. the rats, ghouls, and skellys that come with the phrase "...you have disturbed my rest..." blah blah. If one of those ph's gets by you, it will wander down to the third floor and the spawn for Gynok will not proceed properly until it has been dealt with. At 29, Gynok conned blue when he finally showed up and took me down a bubb or two during the battle but wasn't difficut to solo. The timing on that spawn is about 16 min as I recall.

The camp for Mitty wasn't as tidious simply because between spawns I was quite busy killing all the Priestesses around the temple so they wouldn't heal her when she came. Her timing is 18 min. She has the smite spell which isn't a big deal, but what is very important is that she will run and run fast with SoW, and while doing so each Priestess she passes will heal her so that you've expended all your mana and she's still 3/4 full with health. During the 12 hours I camped her, she dropped the copper ring approx. 8 times, the silver ring 2 times and a 'fake' gold ring once as well. Just remember, if it doesn't have the +1 -1 adjustment on it, it's not the right ring. She cons high green to a level 29.

The container can be looted off of Vin Molter in the Qeynos sewers who also conned blue but was a push over to solo. I've also heard that giving him 4 kalish and a rat ear pie will also produce the container, but I am not sure about that.

And lastly, if Mitty doesn't provide you with a silver ring, it can be found lying at the bottom of Black Burrow pond. It I believe is a 2 hour 'spawn'.
>>>Anyway, the name of this quest is patience and with enough of it, you will recieve the final prize.

Good luck, and may The Tribunal find in your favor.
