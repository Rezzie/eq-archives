1) Way more roamers.
2) Way less mobs in houses.
3) Roamers seem to be exclusively revultants, ire ghasts and spectres.
4) Drops are HIGH. We got nearly 1 drop per 2 mobs.

All in all, for about 5 hours of clearing, we got (from memory):
2 shaman
2 rogue
3 warrior
2 cleric
3 mage
1 enchanter

A DEFINATE improvement. They were the first pieces of apothic and insidious ive ever seen drop, and the first pieces of woven shadow ive ever seen drop in hate (we got some off phobos in fear).

The haunted chests are awesome. Just like phobos except with a slightly higher drop rate I think.

All in all, I consider the zone FIXED. WOOHOO.
