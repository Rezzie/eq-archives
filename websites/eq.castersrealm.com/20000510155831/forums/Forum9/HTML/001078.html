Qaedaar, there's a sneaky way to get around the Colour spells in duels.. one so sneaky that most people who use it are completely unaware they're doing it. Due to problems the spell would otherwise cause on PvP servers, the Colour spells don't affect people in your party. Thus, if you were grouped with the wizard while duelling him, your Flux/Shift spells would still have no effect. Disband the party, then you'll be able to stun to your heart's content.
