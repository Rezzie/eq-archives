On area mez:
This spell shines from the mid 20s until the mid 40s. At higher levels (fighting LDCs and lava beetles and such) I tend to go with single target mez, because it lasts a *lot* longer, and if three of these critters wake up from mez and aggro on you at the same time you're in for a corpse run.

On DDs: these certainly have their uses, and in all but the largest groups I keep one memmed.
Trick I use: Once the "real nuker" (wiz usually) starts casting the "finisher" nuke, I start casting mine too.
If the wiz's nuke gets partially resisted, and doesn't kill the mob but rather makes it turn and run, my DD will hit it soon after and kill it.
If the wizies nuke kills it, I just interrupt my spell (use your 'duck' key for this btw).

Just about every spell in the enchanter spell line-up can be put to good use and ignoring any of your spell lines is a bad idea, imho.
