I think many of you are missing the reality of all of this.

First and foremost Verant does not ship the product to you. An Order Fulfillment company stores and ships the product to you. The fulfillment agency sets the shipping and handling charges. For handling only 200 000+ products for a limited time this is reasonable for them. After shipment of the upgrade there will be minimal ordering of that product again. International shipping discounts would require a large number of items shipped to a particular country at the same time. Volume discounts don't count on 150,000 items shipping to several different countries at varying times.

Those of you feeling abandoned in Australia with 50 USDollars less in your pockets... I feel for you. But, such is the cost of doing business. It may make sense for you to wait for the change up with the full version and acquire it for 50$ US total.

And Finally, Verant did not guarantee or otherwise promise that you would receive the expansion before 4-24. They did, however, promise that they would be shipped by that date. I would understand if it took them a little longer to get through customs and the like with non-domestic US orders.
