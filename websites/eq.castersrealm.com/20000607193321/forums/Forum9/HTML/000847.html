You forgot the most important thing:

C) No spell parser to date can decode what the "Mana" figure means in the spell data file. Clarity has an effect of "Mana" with a value of 1. Of course it doesn't give 1 mana, and it's also a variable effect, which depends on level.

What does this tell you? The effect is either coded in a way the current parsers don't understand, or the effect is calculated server-side. One of the new druid spells has a value of 3 for "Mana". I doubt this means it gives 3 mana, or triple the effect of clarity.

In any case, I wouldn't get all excited about it. Verant has learned some lessons and I doubt they would give wizards an ability that would obviate the enchanter spell.
