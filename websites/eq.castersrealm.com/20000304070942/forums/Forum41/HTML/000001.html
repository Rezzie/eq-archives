Without Fear, a necromancer's PvP abilities have been reduced significantly.

Fear provided both runner control and "interrupted" casters for ~30 secs to a minute, which given the lack of a stun spell, is vital.

The pet can interrupt, but a good caster will root the pet. At level 16 I doubt you have the money for a pumice stone, but I'd buy one asap.

Now, I'd think you'd open with engulfing, add other dots as desired (see how the fellow responds to the pet first--if he ignores the pet and tries to cast/attack you, shadow step away).

Do NOT lifedrain unless the target is fleeing, or until you personally are hurt. No reason to use lifedrain as a inefficient burn without even gaining the healing effect.

I am the infamous pk'ing wonder necro from Vallon Zek. Necros are hands down the best pvp class in the game, enchanters taking a close second. Mano e Mano a necro can lay waste to any opponent.

Before 29 dont even try to kill someone without a pet. You dont have enough mana to take them down and need your pet for interupts.

At level 34 and beyond you are simply a killing machine. Venom of the snake can pretty much kill any caster class with a pet on them too. Venom of the snake, steelskin and vampiric curse. I still lead off with a pumice shot and cling, then VC and then drop the VoS bomb. With your steelskin and VC...you will pretty much remain at full health against most casters. Melee types arent too hard especially if you have jboots. Dont even bother trying to dark a warrior either, their sv magic is insane by lvl 34.

You wanna learn own people hardcore past 34? The spell that will do that is screaming terror. Most necros dont understand what ST can do from a pvp aspect. ST is chr based (which means you wear +chr pvp gear) and is a mez style spell. What good is that you ask? The ability to interupt casts. At lvl 34+ I drop 2 powerful dots then sic pet. While you are dropping your dots your opponent has prob destroyed your steelskin. But now you can sit back and ST him in the middle of his casts to interupt. Awesome way to stop heals, gates, and long casting DD. Knowing your opponent, their spells, and casting times is key. And if you are waiting for ST to reload you can always lifetap once to keep near full health.

The only class that stands a chance against a lvl 34+ necro is an enchanter. Their DD/stuns and mez's to interupt are insane. Do not even attempt to kill an even or higher con enchanter past 34 without a pet and pumice. The only way you stand a chance is to keep your pet unmezed enough to give you time to drop 2 dots on them.

Hopes this helps some.
