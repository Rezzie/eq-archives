Taken from the main news site. Any Magicians on the test server completed this quest yet (i know its kinda early but you could probably do at least one of these quests if your high enough level in aday) and can give us some more info? Like how good it is etc.

MAGICIANS TO GET FOCUS OBJECTS, DECEMBER 1, 1999

New quests have emerged on the Test Server in the Temple of Solusek Ro for Magicians.
These quests allow for the obtaining of focus objects to be used when summoning elementals.

The focus objects work by increasing the particular specialist strengths of each elemental type. They also increase the hitpoints of the summoned elemental when used. They do not however change the chance of summoning a particular level elemental, they merely increase the power of the elemental summoned.

The Quests can be obtained from Vira in the Temple. Here are the details.

Broom of Trilon
The Broom of Trilon was held by the infamous Mistress of Air of the same name. It will need the following components to make it:

A Star Ruby.
A Feather from a Griffon.
A Broom from a Magician in Najena.
Toes of a Cyclops.
Shovel of Ponz
The Shovel of Ponz was crafted by the Earthen Master of the same name. It will need the following components to make it:

A Ruby.
The Eyes of a Gargoyle.
A Shovel from a Magician in Najena.
Toes of a Hill Giant.
Torch of Alna
The Torch of Alna was constructed and held by the Fire Mistress of the same name. It will need the following items to make it:

A Fire Emerald.
A Scale from a Fire Drake.
A Torch from a Magician in Najena.
Toes of a Fire Giant.
Stein of Ulissa
The Stein of Ulissa was found and used by the Mistress of Water of the same name. It will need the following items to make it:

A Sapphire.
A Scale from a Mermaid.
A Stein from a Magician in Najena.
Toes of a Frost Giant.
