Get a druid or wizard friend capable of group teleports. Create an ogre warrior. Have friend kill Ambassador. Teleport to GFay. Have friend kill D'vinn. Loot both hearts with Ogre Warrior. Go give hearts to Lork. Give new hammer to druid/teleport friend. Friend give hammer to barbarian.

Ok so it's not exactly you that's doing the quest, but if you just want the hammer and dont care about the RP factor of doing the quest for yourself. If that is a consideration for you in getting the hammer well then there's really only one way to do it. In Rathe Mtns there are the two lizard camps where the Mortificator is supposed to spawn. Spend a LONG time there killing the lizards and collecting as many tails and lizard meats as you possibly can carry. Once full head back to ogguk and use either the Ball of Clay trick, or shared wolfform to had them in to the ogre's around the warriors guild in Ogguk. That will give you the faction you need for Lork to give you the hammer, and with Ogre's in general. As a barbarian though, this process will most likely take you forever and a day. Yes I know that you can camp the lizard warriors around and in Cazic Thule, but Rathe Mountains is a lot more steady supply of lizards, and there are no trains to worry about. Only thing you need watch is the occasional Hill Giant Kiting Druid that runs over your position.
