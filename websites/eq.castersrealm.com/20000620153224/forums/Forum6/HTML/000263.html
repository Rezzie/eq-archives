A similar thing happened to me, and don't worry, there is yet hope.

DO NOT attempt the quest while the Temple cons apprehensively at you!

To fix your Temple faction, there are several things you can do.

* Kill Shadowmen

* Go to the graveyard in Mistmoore, go inside the little house and pick the flower that spawns near the coffin every 5 minutes or so. Give the flower to the named Shadowman behind the little house. I think (but I'm not sure) the shadowman will con aggressively but won't attack.

* Give Dervish Rings to the named Orc in South Ro. He is in a little camp with an Erudite in the corner (turn right if you are entering S Ro from Oasis). For every 4 derv rings you give him, he improves your faction with the Temple. I bought a bunch of rings from players in North Ro and took them to this orc. After doing this twice my faction was fixed.
