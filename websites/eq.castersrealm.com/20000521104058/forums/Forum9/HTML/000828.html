Chain casting pets is a common magician tactic to take out a really big mob, or an entire room. The idea is to wait until the pet is almost dead, type "/pet get lost" (no enter), start casting a new pet, wait until the casting time is almost over (i.e., 1 second left), and then press Enter. If you have the timing right, your new pet will appear 1/2 a second after your old pet vanishes, at which point you let the new one taunt, step back, then apply burnout and the fire shield, meditate, and repeat this process as much as you like. The only drawback to this technique is that now mage pets can dual wield, and I don't think (last I checked) you can equip a pet while it's fighting. So after the first pet, the magician will have to settle for no dual wielding.

The reason this works so well for magicians is that (1) their pets are commandable, (2) the earth pet especially has tons of HP, and (3) the pets take very little mana -- only 200 for the level 49 pet -- allow you to chain cast many pets before you run dry.

As for enchanters, you're out of luck because you can't do "/pet get lost". I suppose you could do it by pressing hide (hope it works), drinking a potion, or having a friend invis you at the right moment, but it's definitely not as effective a solo technique.
