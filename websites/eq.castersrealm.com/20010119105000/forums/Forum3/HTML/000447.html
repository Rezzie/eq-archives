As someone who plays a Paladin as my main character since the pet nerf, I have spent almost 3 months aquiring TWO pieces of the Armor of Woe as us paladins have begun to refer to it.

Prior to the "fixes" the two pieces I have aquired had perfect spawn times. The cooks in MM spawned regularly and the Kilij SG spawned regularly as well(he was hard to find as he was not named at the time).

The supposed "fixes" actually increased the spawn time on the cooks in MM, which is something that needs to be fixed back to what it was.

The Nightfall Giant is fine as it is, as well as the Kilij SG.

The Netmasters need the suicide issue to be fixed as well as the spawn time increased to under 3 hours, or make more of them spawn.

The plague sharks still have the issues of dealing with the killer sharks, although I hear they are spawning more often.

The undead cyclops spawn rate is just unacceptable!!! 3 days??? Who has 3 days to spend in SK waiting for an undead cyclops to spawn?

The Sentry used to have a decent respawn time, but apparently he has inherited the spawn time of the undead cyclops. Perhaps Verant meant to put them on the same spawn table, but on the Sentry's old spawn table, rather than putting them both on the cyclops' spawn table. I wouldn't be surprised if this was the case.

The BIGGEST problem of the Armor of Woe is melatite.

No other class has to dish out as much pp for their armour as us. Why? because most other components for other class armour is no drop. Want to by a heart of fire? tough it's no drop. Want to buy some decaying bracers? tough it's no drop. Want to buy the skull of Meldrath? tough it's no drop. Want to buy an Aviak Charm? tough it's no drop.

People can get many of the components of their amrour for FREE, but paladins are gouged at 200+ pp per bar of melatite for EACH piece of the Armour of Woe.

I have yet to see a piece of melatite drop off a clockwork and I have spent about 6 hours killing clockworks in Sol A since the supposed "fix".

The two pieces of the Armour of Woe that I have, I have because I bought the melatite or a friend gave me a piece because he got some Valorium.

Want to hear what's sad? A Bard asked a paladin why he didn't have all his class armour yet...the Paladin's responce? Valorium is easier to get, so I'll be waiting til I can get Valorium.

What other class finds their Plane armour easier to get than non-planar armour?

Something more than just doubling the melatite drop needs to be done...make melatite NO DROP!!! Rangers and SK's need it for some of their stuff too, so what does it hurt if people start giving it away? Apparently it doesn't hurt Verant to let people give aviak charms away, or hearts of fire, or skulls of meldrath.

Sorry for the rant, but I have a Greaves of Woe Mold sitting in the bank that is 3 months old waiting on a bar of melatite.
