On topic now.

I personally think recharging shouldn't be given to any class personally. It should have been from the beginning, and should be now an ability given to the various guild masters or Norrath. Which every guild master would have the spell that the charges come from, can recharge it for you. I was thinking a charge equal to what the particular spell scroll costs. So, the Stein of Mogguk could be recharged by a Cleric Guildmaster for 5 x (cost of Healing scroll).

Enchanters should NEVER be given the ability to charge something with spells that only WIS based casters know.

Enchanting something is slightly different from recharging something. The item enchantments that I like are ones that 1.) make an item magical in determining whether it hits magic monsters (low level), 2.) reduces dly/weight (mid level), 3.) maybe allows a weapon's effect to proc' more often (high level).

Another idea that I spat out a long time ago was a coming together of the pure casters to bring forth Orbs of incredible strength and power. Something along the lines of 1.) Mage summons Magic Orb, 2.) Enchanter enchants the Orb to prepare it for magic containment, 3.) Wizard shields and cases the Orb to protect against the sudden release of the magic that will be contained within. Once all have been done, ANY INT based spell can be cast upon the orb to charge it (put a mana cost cap; lots of small mana spells, or few high mana spells).
