On Quellious, near as I can tell, it's a special case.. despite being now a somewhat "old" server, it's taken our populace a comparatively long time to achieve any of the high-end goals. The Plane of Fear was always viewed as impossible and whatever groups tried it were slaughtered very badly.. finally after a patch when the zone was depopulated, a group went in and camped by the north wall, and logged in the next day to fight. That's how Fear was broken on my server, a bit ignomiously I know.. Hate was different, when enchanters/necromancers/dark elf clerics could get around in it without being killed on sight, there were always a good amount of people in Hate. Once the patch changed that, Hate was deemed "impossible" for almost 2 months and no one went. The dragons have only been slain once or twice each on the server as well.

I guess a reason for this is that we don't have one or two uberguilds on the server.. there's no lead guild that has the manpower or skill to consistently fight/break into planes or kill dragons. So generally, every trip to the Planes is a "pickup" trip.. new people will come in as others have to leave. Typical behaviour in Fear is to log in at the north camp, fight from there and start clearing the zone, and when you have to go return to the north camp and camp there.. then the next day when the plane is fully respawned, relog in and repeat. Cazic-Thule and the Dracoliche are generally avoided. It's a far cry from other servers where supposedly spawns in Fear are camped to the extent that people are waiting for creatures to respawn and killing them immediately.. each day in a "Fear raid", people deal with a fully spawned zone, they just log into it rather than break into it. The same would go for Hate, though I haven't been there much recently. So to answer the question, yes people can join pick-up groups in Planes, but they need to to be able to keep the raids going, due to the general lack of manpower and the lack of uberguilds who might be inclined to actively "farm" a Plane. That said, in the past month no god or dragon has been slain on the server.. maybe the Dracoliche or Maestro once, but that's it. It's a bit weird, I don't know why other servers seem to do so much more..
