To those that contend that all Enchanters should expect from their class is to be pure group support....

Look if all enchanters could ever do from day one was mezz, stun, charm, clarity, and cast minor buffs and debuffs and people were told this up front. Then you are correct in saying no one should complain or be surprised.

But this is not the case. I chose the Enchanters because I could chose to either play in a pure group support mode OR with cunning, guile and talent could play is a solo role as well.

In short the Enchanter when I started the class was a very versitile class. It had a broad range of skills and capability and people BRIGHT enough to recognize this have had a lot of fun exploring the various ways to use our limited damage dealing capabiliies to the fullest. Now after nearly 7 months (for me) and for some a year or more into playing the enchanter class varent is systematically redefining us to be purely group support. Those of us that have chosen to explore the full range of our classes capabilities and have seen more potential then simply passing gas (clarity) and mezzing and stunning PC's for OTHERs to have the fun of killing the MOB's have a right to be upset when now after in some cases a year of playing enchanters we are being told that we are to be FORCED into the mind numbingly boring role of passing gas (clarity) for hours on end so that others might enjoy the satisfaction of actually killing the creatures in the game.

Many have used the argument that they are being forced to group so why should Enchanters feel they have a right to complain? My issue has to with the role in the group that we are forced to play. There is a difference to be forced into a group and be able to actually deal damage and be forced into a group simply to hold things down while others damage them.
