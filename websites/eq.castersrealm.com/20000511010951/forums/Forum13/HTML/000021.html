From Allakhazam's site:

Lynuga's Gem Collection: In the Innothule swamp, you talk to Lynuga, a wandering NPC
troll female that can usually be found near the entrance to Guk. After level 10 or so she will tell you that she collects gems. By the upper teens, she will tell you about her gem collection and that she wants more. Ask her what gems and she will tell you that she wants a ruby. In return for the ruby, she gives one of several items. Ones I know of are the Brutechopper axe, Midnight Mallet and Ivandyr's Hoop. FYI, giving her chomper's ruby from Rivervale will not work. The criteria for what you get seems unclear and could just be random.

Good luck!
