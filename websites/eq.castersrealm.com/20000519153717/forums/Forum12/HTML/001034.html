I am gonna have to Say CT throne room with a good enchanter. At lvls less than 34 we are just as good a healer as a cleric, so plop down, pull out the book, and med, heal repeat. Grab the 1st spot in CT throne room that needs a healer, and leave if there is not a good enchanter =).

I did 30 in about 1 and 1/2 days there-- and FLEW to 35 there too. 30-34 in about 1 week.

It is pretty good loot, and , with an enchater that is good, not very dangerous at all.

Whatever you do at Cazic i would reccommend staying OUT of the courtyard. It really is not safe to hunt there much, unless you have a great puller. Once i stopped trying to hunt in the courtyard-- and moved to the throne room and 4 Corners-- I had MUCH more fun at Cazic.

Also Gators there in the Blue pool room-- great place to dust off that charisma spell, and that new befriend animal spell at 29 and charm gators. Kind of like hunting Oasis, but with higher lvl crocs. You miss all the trains down there, and get a taste for what you will have in 4 lvls-- your pet =). Hope this helps !
