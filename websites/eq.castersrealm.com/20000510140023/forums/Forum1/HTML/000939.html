I've seen people refuse cures to a diseased or poisoned character on basis of race alone. "I would sooner see you die than waste my mana upon a filthy Troll like yourself." Refusing to group with someone based on race or class seems equally snobbish.

GET A GRIP! The name of the game is, when you get down to it, cooperation. And sometimes you have to relax the RP stuff a bit in the name of everyone working together to advance in the game. Using "RP" as an excuse to be a complete TWIT is stupid.

I believe an example someone gave of not a good time to RP. Say you're in Oasis and you're around level 19, like my character. Say someone in the party spies a Sand Giant lumbering in your general direction while you and the other casters are medding.
Do you really want in the name of RP him to type out, "To your feet, my friends! Beware, for a sand giant approacheth anon!" as the sand giant closes to aggro range, or do you think it might be a bit safer if he targeted the SG and hit the H key? "Hail a sand giant" comes out, you know what's up, you stand up and you bolt out of the way before you become desert pancakes.

We type things in shorthand because time is often of the essence. If someone is in the middle of a fight and calls out "heal plz" it's due to urgency. By the time he types out "My wounds will soon overcome me! I need aid from the cleric!" he's toast.

Finally, dudes, let's put this bluntly. IT'S ONLY A GAME! We're here to have fun. Is it REALLY all that fun to play the superiority game in the name of Role Playing?
