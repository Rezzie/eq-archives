Seriously...
What are the illusions good for..besides the side effects I mean (Troll Regen, Air Ele Lev, Skel no eat/Drink).

I'm agnostic, and can go just about anywhere as a gnome, and still be liked/tolerated.

I put the illusion Skeleton on in Unrest...I still get pummelled...everything scowls at me there...meanwhile..all these wolves (which are alive, and not common to Unrest), run around completely indifferent to all the inhabitants.

Ever see a lvl 8 player with group wolfform run up and Hail the ghost? I have.

So if I stick this illusion of Skeleton on myself and go to Unrest...I get smacked...If I run to through Hero Camp with it on...I get smacked..those that should be kind to me by faction...now ignore faction to kill me...and those that should be fooled by the illusion (other undead), still kill me as well.

So what is the advantage?

Wouldn't it be easier...rather than getting that illusion Air Elemental spell...just to do the batwing and Levitate thing? At least if you're levitating by some normally friendly NPCs...they won't attack you. Try it in Elemental form..and you are KoS.

I realize that some illusions (Dark Elf, Troll, etc) allow you to go to cities that you would not normally be allowed to go to (but your choice of dieties affects this). It just seems to me that our illusions should grant some benefit against critters.

I dont know if its right that most MoBs can't see through Invis/ITU, but can see right through your illusion to rip you a new one.

Sure its nice to be able to water breath...Levitate...fire shield...etc...but its also nice to play with a MoBs mind..and not be KoS in an area that you would normally be.

We are enchanters after all...
