At about level 34 I think you can solo this spot. Here is what I did (died once trying).
Go up into the very corner of the canyon and wait while inviso watching the roamer. Make note of her time to complete her route (5min real time). When you know her last move before she goes back down the hill, move to a point where you can cast AOE mez on the 2 gargs and the DE. Here is where it gets interesting. If you cast AOE mez a few things could happen.

1) They all get mezzed and you tashan them all, charm one garg and have it attack garg #2 while you root/nuke DE girl and get the rune.

2) One or two resists AOE mez. If this happens, fire off color shift just before the 2 non mezed get to you and charm the garg and sic it on the other while you re- mez the one that got mezzed to begin with.
Then help your pet and gate after you get the rune if needed.

3)They all resist you. O O, you have 1 option here, Color shift and gate. You probably will die if this happens but the mask is worth it i think.

A few tips. Bind outside the MM entrance, Keep rune up with all buffs, and time the roamer (you have a 5 minute window before she comes back). Also, if any thing starts to run down that hill, GATE out asap. I died once when my pet garg had killed the DE and was in the process of killing the other garg, when he decided to run down the hill while I was attempting to loot the DE (no rune on first try). Yes, you guessed it, Train from "hell" before I could even see it. All i saw was so and so begins to cast and then loading please wait. so, if they run, and you fail to root the runner, get out.

good luck.

Actually, all four of the enchanter quest items are amazingly good. The mask is the most amazing, it's the type of item you'd expect to come from the planes, yet it comes from an easy-to-complete level 30 quest. The gloves dont "suck" at all, in fact I think they're excellent. 6 AC is a lot for a caster, and they also add 3 each of CHA, AGI, STR, MANA. The rod is probably the best primary melee item as it adds 2 AC in addition to +12 CHA. And the wand, while the least impressive of the bunch, is still nice for casters who like to melee in their 20s and early 30s.
