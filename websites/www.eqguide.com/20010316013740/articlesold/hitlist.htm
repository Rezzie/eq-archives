Ligge's Hit List

 

The people of Norrath I intend to go back for

 

One of the greatest advantages to being a druid is the aspect of traveling and meeting new people. Both friends and enemies. Eventually the difficulty becomes keeping tract of the myriad of enemies you need to follow up on and enact vengeance. Over time I have had, shall we say, incidents with quite a few folks across Norrath and have vowed payback to each and everyone of them. As the list grows and gets completed I will update this list.

As of March 1, 2000 there are only 4 people left on my original hit list. Zixx, Guard Huatian, Varsoon, and Hycallan. Huatian still has a little bit of luck on his side as of his location and what I will have to endure to beat him as he roams the middle of Felwithe like a coward. The other three seem to hide quite often, but are surely dead the second I see them. Due to this fact I have begun work on the second Hit List.

1. Nillipuss

That little obnoxious brownie that runs around Rivervale stealing jum jum from my beloved Stormreapers. Reebo Leefsway is interested in retrieving the jum jum stalks Nillipuss has taken and Deputy Hiltop has a well known fascination with the little thief, well he did until he heard the Ogre in the vale rumors... Nillipuss was annoying enough that I attacked him numerous times in my younger days just so he would know who I was when I came back to eliminate him. I am happy to say I tend to kill Nillipuss every other day now. If he happens to be run around Rivervale as I pass through, he can count himself dead. I began taking him near the end of my 15th season.

2. Ambassador D'Vinn

This man was a major thorn in my side when visiting my fellow druids near Crushbone, in Greater Faydark. During my visit we took a stroll into Crushbone, the hall of the orcs, and unhappily enough spent quite sometime tangling with the slimy Tier'Dal Ambassador D'Vinn. Rumor has it he is helping coordinate efforts between the orcs and the Tier'Dal against the fine people of Faydark. Soon I will be traveling back to Crushbone to repay that debt to both D'Vinn and his pal Crushbone. It is long overdue. What a wuss! It's scary to think one day I was scared of this guy. I took an afternoon off and sat in the tower and pop Crush and D'Vinn for a while letting the younger folks look the tunics and extra dirks.

3. Guard Hutian

This busy body needs a lesson. While casually fishing on the riverbank in the town of Felwithe I made a careless error. Maybe it was the new brew I was working on or just silly halfling antics, regardless I happened to target my partner and give her a little Flame Lick. Without hesitation Guard Hutian took me down and left me bleeding on the banks. No questions asked, no response required. How did he know she didn't like it? We all know what enchanters are like behind closed doors...

4. Mino Hero

If you have ever hunted the minotaur caves in Steamfont Mountains or know someone that has you know of this beast. Seemingly unstoppable he rampages out of the caves and slays everything he can touch without consideration or remorse. That is exactly how he will be treated when I get to him. There is a picture in the photo gallery of this huge beast. This beast is part of the Hero Bracers quest from the Steel Warriors.I am happy to say he has died both times I recently saw him.

5. Lord Shin Ree

He is related in some way to the Deathfist Orcs. I have yet to find their homebase, but one day I will and I will hunt him down. We met on what was to be a simple hunting expedition into West Commons to slay some orcs to assist my hometown of Rivervale and earn are squad rings. On our way from Freeport I got to close to the orc camping area, too curious as usual, and out of blue Lord Shin Ree arrived. It was a nastily quick battle and ever since then I have been looking for the orcish demon to get even. He is dead! WooHoo! What a glorious night it was! First Lord Shin Ree, then a Neriak guard, and THEN a werewolf. Of course the testosterone was high and Cailette and I went after Kizdean, bad move... the day before I would begin my 19th season I finished Ree off, there is a picture in gallery.

6. Kizdean Gixx

This undeserving Tier'Dal wanders through West Commons on a regular basis. The fine folks in my homeland have again asked me to assist in ridding the surrounding areas of vermin and he is one of the bugs that needs to be pushed back to Neriak. It is very obvious the Tier'Dal have only black and white. Either they are a great help and have been banished from their land or they are dark and deceitful like this one. Darkness will be the last thing Gixx sees I can promise you that. Another one bites the dust! On the night of June 22nd, after a raucous battle with a werewolf, Ligge enlisted the assistance of a roaming kodiak and finally taught Kizdean a lesson. I have an article on charming animals that includes a picture of the first time I killed Gixx with a kodiak partner.

7. Varsoon

He charmed me!!! That is one thing I will not stand for! I know nothing of this human necromancer's story, but one incident. He was guarding multiple corpses in the ruins of Qeynos Hills, prevent people from retrieving the bodies, so I offered to help. I was able to get his attention and make him chase me to the guards at Surefall Glade's entrance. The cowards did nothing! They stood and watched Varsoon and his pet beat on me and then charm me as his own little evil doer. It is unfortunate Varsoon did not have the sense to turn me on the worthless guards.  I have tried to kill this guy for a while now. He just spawns so infrequently it's an absolute waste of my time.

8. Hycallan

Women are normally not something I would include on this sort of list. I thoroughly enjoy the company of women, except this one did not return the affection. I was simply strolling through Lavastorm Mountains and found a goblin camp. Within one of their tents was an attractive female and I decided to free her from her confinement. As soon as I began to pummel the 3 goblin guards she cast a spell and summoned a pet... skeleton... a necro, and a Erudite at that. Not someone that looked at me too fondly as I tried to slay her guards...

9. Dorn B'Dynn

Another of the Tier'dal brethren. He hangs out with some Dervish Thugs and Cutthroats in Northern Desert of Oasis. As a young chap I took a stroll a little too close to his tent on my first adventure in the Northern Ro and he persuaded me to leave for quite a while. I am back and checking up on him daily. He will probably be the next to feel the wrath of Ligge. Well, I finally did go back and get this worthless drow, what a waste of mana! He was a wuss by the time I came back through here and Cailete and I took him with minimal effort. Next time he might think twice about picking on a little halfing. As he hung out eating scarab dung and cheating at cards with his cutthroat friends Ligge got stronger...

10. Broon

Big cyclops that bathes, continually as he should, in the river of East Karanas. He was a major nemesis for this fledgling druid trying to explore the world. Now he is a nuisance to be avoided. Soon he will be fish food. Last time I saw him he was fighting others and stopped to take a swing at me. Aye, Broon, I understand, you know I am coming back for you one day! Go ahead and get all the swings in now my friend. Come and get me big boy! We have had a blast running Broon all around East Karana lately. I am not very succesful solo yet, but with a partner or two we can ping pong him back and forth between us and have a ball. Snare, DOTs, and balst from all angle keeps this guys confused and disoriented.

11. Dragoon Zytl

Another evil Tier'Dal roaming the West Commons area. He seems to be most frequently found near the large pyramid structure and waters edge. Obviously I have no love for most Tier'Dal as Zytl makes the 4th on this hit list of mine. Maybe one day I can help lead the deserving Tier'dal in a raid upon Neriak and erase the evil that continues to spawn and fester in that dark place. Zytl has been paid back and will many times in the future. A word of advice, don't kill Zytl and sit around too long. We were low on mana and low health and Kizdean showed up and wiped the floor with me. Its almost funny. I guess ole greenie Kizdean has me on his hitlist too.

12. Lord Grimrot

A sorrowful shadowknight residing in South Karanas. I had no beef with this guy, I feel sorry for anyone that has to create his own girlfriends... (he has a quest for evil types to go bring her remains back so he can reanimate them) I only wanted a picture of him and his opal scythe. Well, I got it the picture, and he got me... this time! Next time he will not be so lucky. This smelly puke is deceased. I even kept his scythe as a memento of the triumphant victory.

13. Findlegrob

If anyone deserves the position of 13th it is this one. Some little twerpy gnome necro in the Rathe Mountains surprised me with his little gathering of groupies (2 iced bone skeletons, 2 dry boned skeletons, zombies, his pet and him - plus a Giant Skeleton nearby tagged me as I ran). I can assure you without the element of surprise he will not get another chance. Another mark in my belt!! When Cailette and I went back here I was determined to get even. We did. He did kill me as he got off a Heat Blood as I struck the final blow and neither cailette or myself had any opportunity to save me as my life drained away after Findle fell.

14. Zixx Nenix

What is the deal with Tier'dals and me? I have a few friends of that race that are wonderful, but it seems most are despicable. This annoyance roams Splitpaw when released from his hold and seems to always find me. He doesn't ever hurt much, but I can't seem to do much damage to his evil hide before I have to flee. Maybe he didn't like it when I offered to show him that black heart I carry around, or maybe it was the dark elf parts he smelled stinking up my bag... there is also a picture in the gallery.
