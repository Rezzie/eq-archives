Yeah yeah yeah. I have some  life again. Kis almost a year old and has been wearing me out, started a new job and all that fun stuff. As Ligge nears 55 and the face of Everquest has changed  dramatically I have some renewed vigor. For the longest time everytime I sat down to writ anything I had nothing positive to say, well that has changed finally and I hope to go back to some of what I was trying to do with this site again. Please bare with me as I sort through and probably create broken links and such for a short time and I decide what stays and what comes forward.

Rarely do I post pictures sent in by others, but this one is worth a look...

And now the beginning of a new era... I have gotten behind again on updates, but it is because of all the changes in Norrath for me. We all know I got married. Well, now we have a guild. It is called Changing Tide. Cailette and I are co-leaders and some of our closest friends have jumped on with us. More will hopefully be signed up real soon. Spliner is working on our website and we will be using the same message board for the guild we have been using for events. Recent weeks have seen a great run in Hate, we went with Sacred Fyre and killed Phini in Kedge, we got me Efreeti boots and will continue to go back for more for everyone else. I won the first pair on a roll of 95. Wooohoo! Lots more is brewing and the fun is returning.

Well, and end of an era for me. We lost our ranger a while back due to frustration and other issues and  slowly our team has been falling apart due to time issues mainly. Sacred Fyre has been nice enough to adopt Cailette on many of their excursions and has welcomed the rest of us into the events with open arms. Not really sure what would have happened if they hadn't. Today I got a nice letter from our former ranger and wanted to share. A Ranger's Dying Words.

I have a quick favor to ask. I get quite a bit of traffic here each and everyday and if just 1% of you would send Cailette an email asking her to write more articles it would be a big help to me! Thanks! Also congratulate her on finally marrying the only man in Norrath that sees her as the most valuable loot he ever found. Me! We eloped this last week and I am a happy halfling now. Ok I am gonna go run off now because I know I am in the doghouse when she sees this update...

I have added review on the Castle Mistmoore. Quite possibly my favorite zone in Norrath. I also added another the third page of Kunark shots.

Have you ever wondered just how bad that woman of yours could look? Well I know... egads, waking up to this would be a fright! Thankfully, I know its only an illusion and she has many more pleasant ones she does like to use from time to time. Hey I married her didn't I?

For many months we have discussed the validity of running raids in places like the Planes and some of the tougher multi-group spots as independents. Many have said it can't be done and we would need to be guilded. Bah! I say! Well, the first attempt was on the 30th and I am sorry to say it went very badly, no deaths, but a lot of fighting and issues with a few guilds and I had to go out of town on short notice so Cailette had to step in and fill both our shoes. The past is the past and I am not going to rake people over the coals on this, nor are we giving up. I set up a message board for this purpose so we can stay in contact and organize the next runs. Efreeti is next and Hate again in a few weeks. Wish us luck. I intend to prove unguilded and smaller guilds can survive Norrath events with a little patience, effort, and friends! Emalythe of Inner Peace Fellowship is still working with Cailette and I to make this happen. I would like to thank everyone that helped and supported us that night.
