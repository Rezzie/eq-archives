From Tolkien's Middle Earth saga The Lord of the Rings, and Anne McCaffrey wonderful world of Dragonriders of Pern, to the insufferable drow ranger Drizzt and Terry Brook's land of Shannara we have covered the best. Even a taste from the new Star Wars series written by Shannara's fantasy master Terry Brooks.

There are times when even Everquest can't totally fulfill your need for escape. These books help fill that void. A great way to past the time on long hunting expeditions that require lots of long healing and meditating down time. It's easy to pass the time with a good book in your backpack.
