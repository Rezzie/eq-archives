Ok ok ok ENOUGH IS ENOUGH!

First off let's start with SoW (Spirit of the Wolf)... It is not a right of every citizen in Norrath to have SoW. If it was a god given right then journeyman's boots would not be a NODROP item and every casting class would have SoW in their arsenal. Don't get me wrong the Jboots issue is a total mistake on Verant's part, but that is another debate. Why oh why would you make an item that encourages level 50s to come camp a level 40 spawn for 29 hours straight to help guild members get them? Ok off of that and on to SoW...

You people that demand SoW,  send tells for SoW, or just ask the question "who can SoW".. can all kiss my little arse!! Yes you heard me. I began druidic studies because I liked the abilities I would be trained in, not so I could make everyone run faster. What amazes me is people expect you to do it. I have never charged or asked for money. I have accepted donations and in many cases will reSoW those people that did donate everytime I see them without them asking. But for you to come up to me and say "Yo give me SoW" is ridiculous. It cost mana, it costs time. maybe not much, but don't you hate being in a group where your partners are always off healing, rezzing, buffing others while your team suffers? That's what you are asking druids to do. Be nice. Be polite. Ask for it!

I will go out of my way to assist somebody that gives a story or is polite.

"I am traveling from Qeynos to help save Rivervale from the goblin scum. Can anyone SoW me please at the stone bridge?"

I will be on my way immediately if I can. If I am in a group, they come first, but if not I will normally come running.

Asking "Who can SoW?" is simply inappropriate. Don't you people have manners? I won't even respond to it normally. Just because I can doesn't mean I will or that you deserve it. Especially you dark elven folk. With a few exceptions, of course, you are the most vile scum Norrath has to offer. I hunt your kind for fun. Why do you think I would SoW you? Didn't you spend your formative years hunting my race in your bleak forest area?? Now you want my help?? NO!

This applies just as much to "I NEED A HEAL" and "GIVE ME A CURE". Yeah so what? Why should I heal you?? "Can you heal me please?" will work much better for you rude people I promise. Try it.

Now... donations, begging, whining... whatever you worthless pieces of froglok dung want to call what you do... GET A LIFE. Those of us with nice equipment did not spend the hours to get it so that we could twink or powerlevel some unknown fool that comes begging for money. You are an embarrassment to us all and need to go earn your keep like those of us that have been in Norrath since its creation.

Now before anyone flames me for acting like this is a druid problem. No it isn't. This is a problem for pretty much every caster class in some way. Whether it be Courage, Valor, Group teleports, or Clarity we all have to deal with whiners expecting us to use our time and our mana to make your life better. Many of us will, and many of us don't mind most of the time, but think about what you are asking and by all means tip accordingly. I can assure you I rarely care how much people give me. I have received from 1 silver up to 10pp for binds, buffs, SoWs. It is always the thought that counts.
