June 25, 2002

Holy crap an update? Say it isn't so.

Where to begin... almost 3 months since the last update and we have done and been through a lot. The tyrant guild leader we have got fed up and completely pissed off and decided it was time for a major change. Some people left. Some people were kicked out. Some people will be kicked out soon. Some were promoted. Some old friends returned. I am actually starting to enjoy this world again.

The guidelines have been totally rewritten for members and applicants and along with raising the level to get in we also changed to a more raid focused environment. You know, right click stuff, have resists, have a clue... It's really kind of an odd approach for us considering that a large portion of us wanted to tone down some playing and raiding overall. So what we came up with was sort of a "part time raiding guild" approach.

Once a week we plan one big raid that everyone is expected to attend. Sometimes we have more raids, but its typically pretty obvious which is the mandatory one. This is akin to a weekly poker night. Do what you want during the week - work, experience, play with your kids, hang out with your spouse, go get drunk, keep yourself employed, work on trade skills, go make another crappy ass alt - whatever you want to do, I don't much care. However, once a week we have a poker night/day run that everyone is expected to be there and participate. This keep burn out down and it helps make people look forward to raids rather than the "oh god do we have to do that again?" problem we were having. Myself, the other officers, and a lot of our friends have no interest in the 24/7 raid lifestyle of some other guilds, but we want to do the bigger things and this approach helps keep us fresh and give us the manpower to do those things when we meet on those specific days.

In an extra effort to try and regain what CT stood for in the beginning and what I still feel we are about now I have returned to hosting open raids again. No longer the boring and mundane Hate/Fear type stuff that got us started, but Temple of Veeshan, Western Wastes, and a new World Tour concept I am working on. It should be a fun ride.

Oh well, attention span is probably past by now and I have proven the last few weeks that no one really reads all the crap I write anyway so this is for my own personal enjoyment. A few more epics completed, some cool toys here and there. If you want to see what we have been up to check out the calendar. In the meantime here are some pictures I took along the way.... some old stuff and some new.
