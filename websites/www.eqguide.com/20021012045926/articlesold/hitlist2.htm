Ligge's Hit List

 

The people of Norrath I intend to go back for

 

After 40+ seasons in Norrath I have developed quite a hate list. Both players and NPCs alike. There are quite a few hunters on the world of Fennin Ro I would like to see dead and one day that may be my new directive, but for now we will concentrate on the creature population.

1. Rosch Val L'Vlor

This guy is just annoying. It seems like every time we had him close during the early days of the new gnoll populations take over over he was healed by a newcomer gnoll or somehow managed to knock us off our feet long enough to get the upper hand. I am still looking forward to the day I can unmercifully torment him. Maybe he should ask Ghanex what that means...

2. Deserted House in Kithicor

Ok, so its not one person. Something about this house is unique in that our team has taken out a pull of 10-15 Kithicor brethren at one time, yet this house of 6-9 is able to stomp us. Even with the roamers it pulls we have still taken better than this bunch of soon to be morsels.

3. Temple of Life Freaks

I have tried to be nice, really I have... I needed some fish for a guy in Freeport and due to the Gods of Verant and their infinite lies I continued to capture this fish in the belief the mistake was on my side. However, as we all know the Stein quest was broken for ages and those of us that went and destroyed our Temple of Life faction due to a broken quest are just out in the cold. So I killed putrid skeletons for hours on end in South Karana to please them. They still hate me and now so do the Corrupt Guards of Qeynos. I intend to enact my revenge in this silly UFO worshipping center soon enough.

4. Ogre Bouncers

These fat wastes of flesh have sent me running plenty of times. They don't seem to take a snare and resist most over magic attacks. It amazes me that only the evil guards seem to have this sort of protection where as we halflings, along with gnomes and dwarves, are taken out rather routinely by people well below my ability.

5. Brownie Guards

Small pests. They have caused enough deaths of innocent people to deserve permanent annihilation. These guys are probably next on the hit list to fall. Plus I hear I can make chocolate with their brownie parts.
