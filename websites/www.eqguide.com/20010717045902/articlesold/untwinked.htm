Starting Over

living "untwinked", regaining the fear

There are a lot of unhappy people in the world, both ours and Norrath. As Verant representatives have said time and again not everyone will be happy with decisions that are made and patches that are put forth. However, it is just a game and you can quit at anytime. I know I have threatened to on a number of occasions and am still there everyday. Recently I came close again...

I could go on for pages on why I came close, and probably will over the next few weeks, but for now I just wanted to look at one issue. Boredom.

Maybe I have gotten to soft, maybe I have played to much, maybe I am just too good (snicker). I don't get the thrill I used to from walking into a new area and being genuinely scared of what was around the next bend. Remember when Splitpaw used to be frightening? It was this total adrenalin rush that made me love EQ to begin with. Ligge really has no fear. Actually most of my lives in Norrath are that way (5 characters over 10, Ligge at 31). I know what is around the next bend. I know what it will take to win most battles and I know when to run. At level 31 there is NOTHING in Norrath I feel I have to have. Ok, the Hierophant's Crook is one exception, but that's quite a ways away and I am sure many nights, and just as many deaths, of dragon slaying to get it. Anything else I can get anytime soon I can take it or leave it. There are a few lower level items (meaning the MoBs are green) I would like, but I hate camping places others get experience and tend not to do it. Plus many of those items don't sell to often or I have something so comparable that I can't see dropping 700pp on a bonus of 1 more wisdom... (i.e.. getting a chipped bone bracelet to replace a polished bone bracelet).

So how to get the thrill back? When have I had the most fun?

Questing and pure fear. Those two things seem to be my hot buttons.

So I started over again. I picked a class that I had no real interest in, no real desire to run through the paces with AND on a continent I hate! So was born my dwarven cleric. This also would allow me to work on the quests in that region.

Ligge is sitting with almost a full suit of small bronze, a fine steel morning star, extra polished bone bracer, savant's cap, etc in the bank. All intended to go to a new character one day. My partner and I are pretty much self sufficient amongst our many lives and she wanted a cleric badly to be around whenever she needed a resurrect and we couldn't find another available. So I knew inevitably I would be making one... however, I never passed a single item. I figured I would get bored with the no risk issues as a level 1 cleric running around Butcherblock in full bronze. Plus what would I have to look forward to?

First thing I did was run through Kaladim and hail everyone. Made notes and decided I could reasonably work on a couple of quests at a time if I only hunted things that were quest related (skunks, skeletons, Krag chicks, giant rats). I also have a distinct advantage that I know where most places and items are located in Norrath. My first lives were spent primarily on Faydwer so I have been all over, it has just been many months since then.

Let me just say I have had an absolute blast! There is fear again! There is immediate gratification since you actually see that experience bar go up after a kill. There is a certain joy from actually being able to complete quests in a reasonable amount of time. All issues that seem to disappear as you pass the 20 mark and beyond. One discussion we have had amongst ourselves is that life sucks after 20. I can't totally agree with that statement, but it's pretty close to the mark. As a druid the love began fading as I hit 30. I am sure each class is different.

Of course, Ligge will always be around, but I will always be starting new lives when I get bored. Untwinked. If you find yourself getting frustrated and bored, try it yourself. You might find you regain your prospective and maybe a little humility after you die at the hands of an orc pawn again.

Of course, as fate would have it... last night a frustrated 26th level cleric GAVE away everything he owned as he was giving up Norrath for good. As I was the only lower level dwarven cleric, untwinked, in the area he gave it all to me. One of the reasons I never passed all the stuff others had accumulated was I wanted to have a story on how I got my items. Well, he provided that in a big way. The whole exchange was done in /ooc and /shout so the whole zone knew what was going on. It was a most joyous occasion! So now of course I looked twinked to the hilt, but if anyone asks or criticizes I have a marvelous story to tell and a full zone of jealous witnesses that saw Kuen retire and pass on his equipment to a younger brethren in need. Thanks Kuen! It is unfortunate that someone like you has left Norrath. You are a rare exception to the greedy beggars and kill stealers I see too often!
