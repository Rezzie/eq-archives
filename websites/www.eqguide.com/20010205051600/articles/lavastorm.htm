LavaStorm Mountains

Right smack in the heart of evil central. Your closest town is Neriak, to get to the more pleasant areas of Norrath you have to run Nektulous Forst, but it is a haven for anyone that likes to find a home and hang out a while. You can feasibly live here as long as you desire, starting at about 10 in a group and all the way up to 50+ if you count the adjacent zones. The best levels for fighting within Lavastorm are probably 12-17. The only time you would need to leave would be training with a guild master and banking all your platinum. There is a gypsy camp that is a little risky for the youngest of Lavastorm hunters that you can sell off your looted items as well as buy food, water, and bandages to stay stocked as well as get rid of all the copper and silver you collect. At the gypsy camp there are many roamers that can catch and demolish younger people. You may also sell at the druid circle, its safer than the gypsy camp, however you will have to buy random items and resale them to convert the copper and silver to gold and platinum easily.

On to hunting...

There are 5 exits or zones from within Lavastorm. Nektulous Forest, Najena, Solusek A, Nagafen's Lair (SolB), and the Temple of Solusek Ro. Very quickly let's pull Sol A and B out of the mix as safe havens. If you are fighting near there and zone most things that might possibly be at either zone will kill the typical Lavastorm hunter. So I don't advise these as areas to be around. For the younger hunter you have Nektulous Forest, Najena, and Temple of Ro to hang out near for a safe meditating and healing spot. I do not in any way advocate training zones, but to sit and heal near one is always a good idea.

Nektulous Forest
I suggest a group here and it's easy to find one. Many druids show up here at 14 due to the first line of summoned spells they get for some nice blasting. Also the Swarm series works well here. There is an open area that you can pull to near the tunnel that heads to Nektulous. The only downside here is, like the Sol zones, there can be the guardians and guards in Nektulous Forest at this zone if you do need a safe exit. So beware. The youngest groups can fight in this area too, however things are fire resistant so beware what spells and buffs you have ready at all time.

Temple of Solusek Ro
Ok, time to give up a secret. This is the best place in the world to set up camp. Goblins are right above Sol B, harmony, lull, pull. They are the lower end of Lavastorm hunting. Probably 10-12 in levels. Druid circle is close and safe for selling. Back wall near Temple is a flat area to pull fire elementals, drakes, basilisks, and crawlers. This is a charm heaven for aspiring enchanters. My feeling is if you are an enchanter over 12 and still using your animation you are in for a world of hurt as an older enchanter. You get charm at 12, learn it, love it, live it. Things go bad mez and recon. Is it indifferent again? Go med somewhere. That's the big plus here for learning enchanters. Indifferent cons let you check for the memblur component of mez easily while you learn your skills. Again, I don't advocate zone hugging or running, but the Temple zone is almost always empty and the risk of you training anyone is pretty nil. As an enchanter I solo'd here very effectively with the occasional colors, root or mez trick to get me out of there for a run to zone. This place was a great area from 12-17. Nothing to fear inside Temple for resting periods if you need to go afk. At the Temple doors, inside Lavastorm, goblins do spawn and jump you if you are unaware. So becareful of that issue.

Najena
Another good place to set up shop, however there are trains and no vendors close. Great scenery and a nice open flat spot to hunt in front of the ramp up to Najena. Same issue applys here as Nektulous area. Beware of zoning as to what may be on the other side. The other issue here is there a lot of people typically sitting on the outside of Najena resting from a fleeing exit from inside. Be nice to your fellow hunters and dont risk their well being by being a reckless trainer.

All in all Lavastorm is one of the best midteen areas to fight in my eyes. A decent variety of creatures, stuff that cons indifferent so it allows you to roam somewhat freely (watch those fire imps and elementals), pretty easy to get one thing at a time, you can hunt - loot - sell - shop in the same zone, and IT'S NOT CROWDED! A premier place for druids to develop some variety in skills due to the lack of fire based spells being used as well as a great place for enchanters to work on learning charm tactics. Money isn't too bad either.

If you are learning the basics of being a Norrathian merchant there is an added bonus here. All 3 dungeon zones drop nice items and you could possibly buy them for much less here than elsewhere to resale yourself elsewhere for a tidy profit. Just an added bonus to a great zone.
