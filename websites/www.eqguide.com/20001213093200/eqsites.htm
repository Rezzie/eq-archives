Ligge's Top Everquest Web Sites Choices

This is not necessarily in any order. There are 100's of sites available to Norrathian adventurers on the web and in an effort to cut down on the fluff I have listed the sites I currently find most useful and why. There are other sites out there and as I find myself using them more often they will get posted here and others may be removed. Why list 50 sites when 6 do the job.

Everquest Player Photos:
This site offers no redeming value at all. It is actual player pictures of people on each server. Click at your own risk.

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm:
Here is something that Yahoo proved a long time ago.. you don't have to look good to be useful. Lots of quest information provided, I venture to say more than any other site. It is player submitted information so I do find some errors from time to time, but it is still very useful. Many other topics are covered, most are links to other sites that have the information. He also has a nice level chart on where is good to hunt based on your level. That's nice when you get bored and have run out of ideas on where to take your party tonight.

Everquest Reference Guide:
Other equipment sites: Caster's Realm
A very nice searchable guide to equipment. This comes to us as a partner of EQ'Lizer. As an all inclusive site EQ'Lizer does a good job and has more pictures of items than AllaKhazam's, however AllaKhazam seems to have the most quests available. With the inclusion of the item database this definitely is a good place to check. The quest database at EQ'Lizer is nice too, however I didn't find it any more useful than AllKhazam's "Yahoo" approach.

EQMaps
Other map sites: EQAtlas, Everlore, Maps by Hop
The best place for maps in my opinion. Yes, he did take them from everywhere else, but he deserves some credit for getting the best maps all in one place. He does give credit for to the creators also and has done a service to us by putting them all in one spot. The information provided on zone and other such stuff can really be overlooked, I find bad information here a lot as every newbie that wants to see their name on the web seems to post in each area.

Caster's Realm:
This used to be EQ Enchanters and was a great site for enchanters and jewelry information! Baelish keeps adding and expanding, now he even has equipment information. Some information is incomplete, like most web sites, but they do a very good job. An exceptional bonus is their wisdom and intelligence based mana calculators to see comparisons of what a wisdom or intelligence enhancing item really gives you in mana depending on your level. Great job! One of the 2-3 sites I will even bother looking at the message boards.

EQVault:
Why bother even reviewing this one.... If you play Everquest you have probably already been here. More daily news and information than any other site. They have a wonderful "server status" page to allow you to check if EQ is up and well before you even truly logging in. Warning this page is very slow when servers are down and we are all checking over and over again. The most important asset I feel EQVault brings to the Everquest community is their front page news center. They do keep us all up to date on things going on across the servers. Even with the nice redesign they still have legacy beta information in some areas.

Druid's Grove:
Druid one stop message board stop. Not only is it actually pretty darn good and informative, its also the only one I can find. Besides being full of knowledgeable druids I have to say without a doubt the best looking message board web page I have seen in a long time. Keep up the good work please! As any boards we have a few trolls, but it seems to be kept relatively on check. If anyone knows of a decent listserv that is Druid related; plus fairly active and not filled with powergamers, whiners, flamers, animal killers, and other blasphemers *smile* please let me know. Thanks! Ligge - For a druid specific site see EQ Druids.

EQ Necro:
Well, I have tried to steer away from class specific sites, but this one just deserves mentioning anyway. The hunting guide, geared towards necros, is still invaluable to other classes.  Lots of other good stuff here on quests and news. While I don't "necro" it around Norrath very much anymore I still check the site from time to time for information that helps all my lives out.
