Who are we to judge Everquest hints and news sites?

Absolutely no one and we offer up no credentials to say otherwise. Except we play the game daily and surf the web when the servers are down. Where do we look when we want answers and information?. Hopefully this guide will help narrow down your searches for Everquest information. These review are based on user experience only. If the sites don't seem to be being updated they don't get listed. If we missed something or you want us to take a look at yours tell Ligge.

We also don't plan to list web sites that are only server specific or entirely class specific - well except Druid stuff, after all a druid is writing this so a little bias is allowed, and possibly others that are specific to our team of travelers and contributors. That doesn't mean the others are bad pages we are just more interested in information that is specific to all servers and all adventurers journeys. If you feel I missed a GREAT area that should be recognized on any of these sites or another one that is even better, let me know! Opinions are always welcome, just usually ignored.

EQVault:
Why bother even reviewing this one.... If you play Everquest you have probably already been here. More daily news and information than any other site. They have a wonderful "server status" page to allow you to check if EQ is up and well before you even truly logging in. Warning this page is very slow when servers are down and we are all checking over and over again.

The most important asset I feel EQVault brings to the Everquest community is their message boards and their front page newscenter. Supporting 30 boards they are the most active I have seen and even garnish posts and responses from the Everquest producers. The closest thing to a true direct line of communication we all have to the people that make our world. The only problem is there have been a lot of infantile, flaming posts by the immature set. You guys know who you are. This page is still the best site for up to the minute happenings around Norrath!

Unfortunately, as with any large site some information gets out dated and stagnates. They would do well to take a walk through through their entire site and dump the old and invalid information that is still available, here is one from a front page link to get them started - Beta Information.... Too much sometimes can be too much.

EverLore:
Second best looking EQ web Site I can find. Now of course there is some bias there since I own one of the competitors. Looks good, and pretty useful. Information seems to be pretty accurate, whereas some of the others obviously don't check up on their submitters. I see great potential for this site due to the fact they seem to have better skills in web design than most others.

Unfortunately they suffer from the same problem most sites do. They are trying to do too much. Pick a topic and concentrate on that seems to work best, but no one seems to get this just yet. As with other sites a lot of the information is sparse or incomplete. Still a very worthwhile visit though.

EQ'Lizer:
Spoilers, Armor information, Spells, Weapons, Magic Goodies!! Can't find a quest in Norrath? They know where they are. Want to know what armor you have to look forward to at later levels, or what great weapon is around the next bend? They have it too.

The best thing EQ'Lizer offers is the item database they have. You want to find an item they are the onlyplace worth looking. If you start there for that sort of information you probably won't need anywhere else.

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm:
Another inclusive information site. Here is something that Yahoo proved a long time ago.. you don't have to look good to be useful. Lots of quest info, some faction info, and more. I found more quest information here than anyone else in one visit!

I found navigation easy and most pages with semi valid information, alas the brewer's guide needs some work (we know that's the first thing Ligge goes to on all sites), but the first listing I have easily found for all the commands, or most of them, and the first table on faction hits I have seen available. Hopefully this will always be updated and eventually completed as much as possible.

Forever Quest
For the true role-players among us! From their own mission statement: "Forever Quest will be gathering a sizable group of role-players on each game server. These groups will work together to provide a positive role-playing experience through frequent quests. Each group will be sub-divided into different factions who will be working for different in character goals. Freelance players who are outside these factions will also be accepted. The first of these quest will center around the Heartstone."

So if you are tired of hunt, meditate, heal, hunt expeditions; or are in need of a little more organization or community involvement this is your place. It's not for everyone, but I would suggest at least checking them out, they have some great ideas and I believe will do nothing but add to the experiences Norrath has to offer. Not for the Doom/Quake mentality people. Only for those that enjoy some true adventure, role-playing and strategy in their lives.

Of course I do blame them for Fennin Ro's overcrowding. Unfortunately the emphasis for a RPing server and the infancy of Forever Quest all began in beta on Fennin and, as is obvious, many people flocked there after release. Too bad there wasn't away to block the hack and slash mentality users from Fennin on day one and make it a role-playing only server... aye, but I digress.

EQ Stratics:
Lots and lots of stuff here for an inquisitive traveler. The best place for city maps available in my eyes. It is unfortunate that they do not have grayscale versions setup with printing in mind. Maybe one day they will. If you take their colored versions and convert them yourself it helps somewhat but it takes a lot of ink to print that new black and white map you converted when 80% of the map is gray or dark ink due to the original colors used. Also, don't bother with their "zone" are, most are dead links. Still well worth the stop!

Some of the guides are incomplete or inaccurate, but alas everyone has their own unique journey in Norrath so its hard to fault them for the discrepancies. One page comes immediately to mind though.. the brewers guide. The approximate cost listed is if purchased from a tavern not if supplies are purchased and the drinks made, the supplies aren't even listed that are required to make each drink or in what order a young brewer can make them. Not really sure if they can justify calling this a guide. Its more like a merchant or tavern menu reprinted. Granted I am reviewing this as a frustrated brewer...

Words of Enchantment:
Very active message boards with an obvious slant towards enchanters. There is some good discussions for every class and role-playing in general. One big advantage they share with EQVault is longevity, having been around a while they have some of the most experienced players posting to their boards. Some posters still having beta board access so a lot of the true "behind the doors skinny" can be found within their postings. Our resident enchanter spends her entire work day within these pages. Please don't tell her employer.

Official EverQuest Site:
The Sony/Verant home page for all that is Everquest. Not the most inclusive or best site available, but very nice just the same. Great Flash or text versions available and the news is straight from the horses mouth. You can also get that great poster and box art for your desktop wallpaper in their download section.

The redesign makes the site much more accessible and easier to use, especially in the text version. The Flash section is definitely full of more eye candy, but after the first couple of times the text version serves me better for speed and efficiency. One not that really bothers me is DUMP THAT TOP SONY STATION FRAME!! Please. The first thing I do when I enter their site is to right click inside the Everquest information frame and choose "Open Frame in New Window" from Internet Explorer options. I understand the value of branding and advertising, but there could be better ways to do it rather than annoy us with an out of place, off color-scheme, top frame that is not what we wanted when we came here. That top frame should only be viewed by people entering through the maze that is the Station. Hey we all have our own opinions. Taking nothing away from the Station of course, I am still a Jeopardy player too.

EverCrest:
Daily EverQuest comic Monday-Friday. Truly and EverQuest addict stop. There is really not much to say. If you like EverQuest and you have a sense of humor, which I find many people do not surprisingly, you will like this site. Just go take a look and judge for yourself.

Caster's Realm:
This used to be EQ Enchanters and was a great site for enchanters and jewelry information! Now they have added the rest of the pure casters to their entourage and expanded. Some information is incomplete, like most web sites, but they do a very good job. An exceptional bonus is their wisdom and intelligence based mana calculators to see comparisons of what a wisdom or intelligence enhancing item really gives you in mana depending on your level. Great job!

Everquest Enchanter's Discussion:
Another great place for enchanter discussions. It seems that recently many of the key people and posters have jumped ship and left Words of Enchantment for this site. As things progress we will let you know which one stays the best resource for an enchanters message board.

Mythiran Tower:
Spell Research. The magic users research tower. I have had 2 full fledged caster types tell me this is the absolute best web site related to magic research information. Ligge has to trust that as the only thing I research is the bottom of the brew machine. Research is such a necessary item for those finger waving types that a site like this is essential to their progress.

As we have been working more and more on areas of our site I find myself going back here more often. Ligge's alter ego is a gnomish Necromancer and could not have lived with out their help. Makes sense for a necro chasing druid to be a necro in his off hours doesn't it? Battling necros daily I had a great insight into the class beforehand and have had a blast! Thank you Mythiran for some great information.

EQ Druids:
This site just did a major facelift and added a huge amount of information. If you are a druid this is the spot to go look first. Between their various sections they cover pretty much all a druid would want to know, however Twylight and I disagree on quite a few things philosophically. That is probably a good thing. this way, between my articles and theirs you will see many different perspectives on things. Then you decide what kind of druid you want to be. Shame on you Twylight for hunting animals, even for pelts. =)

Druid's Grove:
Druid one stop message board stop. Not only is it actually pretty darn good and informative, its also the only one I can find. Besides being full of knowlegdeable druids I have to say without a doubt the best looking message board web page I have seen in a long time. Keep up the good work please! If anyone knows of a decent listserv that is Druid related; plus fairly active and not filled with powergamers, animal killers, and other blasphemers *smile* please let me know. Thanks! Ligge

EQ Trade Guide:
A newer addition to the EQ web pages. This covers all trade skills and is just getting better. Check it out and help him keep his information updated. They are a regular poster on the brewers list serv and not only has a good information on the web site, but is a nice person to boot. What a bonus.

Rangers Glade:
Having been a ranger at one point in my career, and still when I need a hack slash afternoon, this is the best site I ever found dedictaed specifically to this class. Looks great and has the information to go with the look. If you are looking for information on skills, trades, races, or even quests for yoru ranger look no further.
