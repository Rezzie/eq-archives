Simply Enchanting

 

Well it is official. Ligge and I have finally moved in together….. well, if you can call a blanket roll and fire on the beach outside Fironna Vie as living quarters. So not only do I get my own lil corner of the fire to call my very own, but I get a wee corner of his web site in lieu of a drawer in the bureau. So without further adieu, Welcome to Simply Enchanting.

Well, my intentions are to discuss some of the relevant issues of my chosen profession with anyone interested in hearing another opinionated 'chanter's opinion. In addition, I'll chatter a bit about some of the other things I do while not being utterly charming or pestering Ligge to bits with requests for this wee trinket or that to complete some quest or another.

So who is this 'chanter that is going to spout off about the Craft?
I was born in this incarnation the same time Ligge was, even though we did not meet until I had passed 5 tests of knowledge in my craft. So basically that means as a 'chanter, I have lived through both walk-outs, have know the early version of Whirl, and the current disappointment of Dynn's Dizzying Draught and many, many, many other changes in direction of my craft.

So, why a Keeper of the Arts you ask?
 Well actually a silly reason when looked at the skills I have had to master to learn my craft…… I wanted to make pretty sparklies to give to friends I met along my travels in Norrath. Naïve, huh? But remember this was way back when Norrath was first discovered and none of us had much of an idea of how the world would shape itself with all the bold adventurers. But I suppose, having been born with the mark of Erollisi, that wasn't such a surprising wish on my part.

So, how did I meet the Tree Hugger I share a camp with now?
 It was when I was a puny young thing. I was up on the hill outside Kelethin, desperately trying to do my part to rid the forests outside my home of the orc menace. And doing a poor job of it I might add. While I was resting after my sprint away from a particularly nasty centurion, I was chatting with another young enchantress about how I had hoped to begin my apprenticeship under a well known jeweler in Felwithe soon. Well, she and I were chattering away about what we had heard about jewel crafting, which stones were best used, and where they were to be found when a third voice piped up. Yes, it was my soon to be very own Tree Hugger. Well after we were rested, Ligge being the gallant halfling that he is asked us if we would like to band together to exact some revenge on the centurion who had sent us fleeing. Well, the rest is history.

And by the way if you do have more questions for me, the Anitonican Bard society and I have a nice agreement. They know the corner of the beach we have set up on, they will get the message to me….. just address to Cailette@eqguide.com, they will find us.
