（情報提供：Okota@Ayonae Ro）

　

　Mazeエリアの先のテレポート２回飛んだ先のQuestRoomにて。

　
　

>hail Crendatha Fe`Dhar.

What do we have here?
A cutthroat, perhaps? 
If you are a rogue I may have use for you.
I am in need of certain components and will reward you well if they are brought to me.
Do I have your services? 

もしあなたがROGであるなら、私はあなたにとって役立つかもしれません。
私はある特定の材料を必要としており、もし私に持って来てくれられるなら、あなたに報酬を与えるでしょう。
私はあなたのサービスを受ける事が出来ますか？

>yes.

Excellent.
Now, down to business.
I shall pay you in trade for the items I seek.
What I have for you are a coif, a breastplate, armplates, bracers, gauntlets, leggings and boots.
These should aid you in your skullduggery. 

よろしい。
では取引の話をしよう。
私が探している物それぞれに報酬を与えよう。
Coif、Breastplate、Armplates、Bracers、Gauntlets、LeggingsそしてBootsです。


>What coif?

I am in need of an unadorned chain coif and three crushed pieces of coral.
For your services I shall give you a coif suitable for one such as you.


>What breastplate?

For the breastplate you must seek out an unadorned chain tunic and three flawless diamonds.
Only then will you receive a breastplate of my crafting.


>What armplate?

For the armplates I shall give you, I require these: unadorned chain sleeves and three flawed emeralds.


>What bracer?

An unadorned bracer and three crushed flame emeralds are what I require of you.
A bracer of my making is your reward.

>What gauntlet ?

I need unadorned chain gauntlets and a set of three crushed topaz.
Once I have these, you shall have your reward. 


>What legging?

A pair of leggings will be yours once you complete this task for me, thief.
A pair of unadorned chain leggings and three flawed sea sapphires are all I require.


>What boot?

A fine pair of boots shall you have to skulk around in, once you bring me a pair of unadorned chain boots and three pieces of crushed black marble.
