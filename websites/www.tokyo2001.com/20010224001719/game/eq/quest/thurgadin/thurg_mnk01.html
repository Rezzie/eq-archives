　街の門の脇の部屋にて。

　
　


>hail Lorekeeper Einar.

Greetings to you.
I seek one tho has chosen the path of monkhood.
What do you call yourself? 

>I call myself a Monk.

Good. I had hoped you were one.
I have heard that as seeker of the way you are in constant training to push yourself to your physical and mental limits.
It is an admirable quality and I wish to aid you.
I will provide you with a cap, a tunic, sleeves, bracers, gloves, leggings and boots. 

>What is cap?

To create a piece of armor to protect your sensitive skull I will require three pieces of crushed coral as well as an eroded leather cap. 

>What is tunic?

To create a spectacular tunic fit for your kind I will require three flawless diamonds and an eroded leather tunic. With the gems and my skills I may be able to draw out some of its magical powers. 

>What is sleeves?

Protection for your arms will come at the price of three flawed emeralds and a set of eroded leather sleeves. 

>What is bracer?

For the bracers, I will require a eroded leather bracelet as well as three crushed flame emeralds.
Return to me when you find such things. 

>What is gloves?

Protecting your hands is very important.
I can create some excellent protection for your hands if you bring me three crushed topaz and a pair of eroded leather gloves. 

>What is leggings?

I may be able to craft a set of wondrous leggings from a set of old eroded leather leggings and three flawed sea sapphires.
It is amazing what you can do with a bit of magic. 

>What is boots?

We use our feet so often, should not they be protected as well as any other portion of our bodies? If you seek fine protection for your feet return to me with three pieces of crushed black marble and a pair of eroded leather boots.
