● Blaize the Radiant says 'Astrid the Penitent often visited the
frogloks of Upper Guk. She once told me her ingot was hidden
underwater there. I found that strange because she couldn't swim.
She was also a good friend to the Kerrans of Kerra Ridge, where
she lost her life fighting against the Erudite hereitic Maugarim.
Bring me the Ingot of the Penitent, the Icon the Penitent, and 1
Magnetized Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for
you.

以前まではEncahted Platinum Barが必要だといっていた個所が、
Magnetized Platinum Barなるものに置き換えられています。

○ You say, 'what is Magnet'

KeyWordsが少しわかりづらいですね。
MagnetizedではなくMagnetです。

● Blaize the Radiant says 'Encanted Platinum Bars can be Glavanized
by Genni, Vulcanized by Vilissia, and Magnetized by Ostorm.
Just give them the Encanted Platinum Bar and they will do the
rest.

Magnetized Platinum Barは、
Enchanted Platinum BarをOstormに渡せば手に入るようですね。

☆ OstormにEnchanted Platinum Barを渡す。

Enchanted Platinum Barは、
市販のPlatinum Barに高LVのEnchanterが魔法を付与することで作成できます。
OstormはTempleOfSolusekRoのZone内にいます。
彼に、Enchanted Platinum Barをそのまま渡しましょう。

● Ostorm says 'Here is your Magnetized Platinum Bar.

で、Maginetized Platinum Barが手に入ります。
