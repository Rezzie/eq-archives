The Forsaken

We have gained word that Cristanos Thex,Queen of the Teir'Dal,has deerned(deemed) Lanys T'Vyl as an enemy of the Teir'Dal nation.
Though The Dead remain out of the search for Lanys's apprehension,Cristanos has stated clearly that any whom are caught actively giving their support to the forsaken child shall be branded as traitors and served the proper punishments beneath Neriak Law.

New found hope that this rampant evil shall be placed to a quick end has erupted throughout the prayers and hearts of those seeking a final peace to this chapter of turmoil.

 　
Continuing Research 　
Al'Kabor is rumored to be continuing his research regarding the fusibility of minerals found throughout Norrath.
The exact purpose of his research is still unknown,though the council has taken an active interest in our champion wizard's work.
It has been rumored that Al'Kabor has recently retrieved minerals from The Hole,though whether or not he was seeking touches of the Underfoot of Brell or remnants of the explosive war between Erudin and the Heretics is unknown.
Whatever his cause mmay be,we hold no doubt that whatever comes from Al'Kabor's efforts will be nothing short of spectacular power.
