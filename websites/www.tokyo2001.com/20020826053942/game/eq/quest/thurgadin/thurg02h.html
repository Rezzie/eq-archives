Book #1 Forge of Icewell Arms
Royal Forge of Icewell Arms Set.
12-Royal Field Plate

The field plate of teh Coldain Royal Armory is lightweight and good for any expedition where battle may occur.
The following are teh summarized steps of the process:

1. To start you will need to first hammer out the folded sheets of velium that the armor is constructed from.
Place a Block of Velium in the forge, as it become more malleable begin to hammer it flat with a Velium Smithy Hammer.
When Flat pour some Codain Velium Temper onto the sheet.
Fold teh sheet over on top of itself and hammer it downflat once again.

2. Now begin to work on the Velium Rings that will link the pieces of the armor together.
To make a set of rings, places a large brick of velium into the forge and heat it up a bit.
Take a metal file and file off small pices and shape them into a circular shape.
Cool them in a viel of coldain velium temper to strengthen them.

3. Take a break from teh hot forge and prepare to fashion the soft fur padding which will be placed on the inside of teh armor.
The fiur will server as comfort and to keep the body warm.
The softest fur is found from velium hounds.
take a patch of their fur and sew it into patterns that will fit inside the armor, use silver thread to accomplish this.

4. The perparation are now complete.
You can now begin to fashion the armor.
Use a Coldain Smithing Hammer to hammer the folded velium sheets into a plate mold.
Once the sheet has been hammered into the mold.
begin attaching the velium rings to the outer areas of the armor that will connect to the other pieces.
The begin attaching the soft fur padding to the insides of the armor piece.
For creating Bracer and Boots you need 1 folded velium sheet.
for vambraces gauntles and helmet you will need 2 folded velium sheets.
for the breastplate and greaves you will need 3 folded velium sheets to fill the mold.

2冊目は宝石の加工の仕方。

Royal Forge of Icewell
Precious Gem Study v1

Now that the armor is created you can begin to set a gem within it.
We have found three gems that when faceted will seem to add an aura that radiates from the armor.
It is our firm belief that Brell himself is pleased with the fusion of Ore and Mineral and bestows powers unto the armor.
Three different gems are used in the process of creating this armor: Diamonds, Emeralds and Sapphires.
These are not normal gems that circulate through the economy; These are gems pulled fromthe raw ore and faceted to draw upon its powers.
To facet the unrefined gem you will first need a set of flethcing tools to chip away the raw edges.
Place the gem firmly in your jewerly kit and slowly chip away at all sides until a nice smooth edge is formed When you have finished with the lapidary place the piece of armor you wish to work ion into your jewerly kit.
Youse your etching tools to farm a base for the gem to fit in.
Then place the gem int the base you just created and you are finally done.
The optimum effect of gems has been found as follows: Diamonds for the Breastplate and Greaves, Sapphires for the Vambraces and bracers, and emeralds for teh boots gauntlets and helmets.
