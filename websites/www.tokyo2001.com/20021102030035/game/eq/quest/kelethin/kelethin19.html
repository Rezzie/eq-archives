>Hail, Tilania Shadowwalker

Greetings to you Xxxxx.
I welcome you to the Scouts of Tunare training halls.
I have seen many scouts pass through my halls with unimaginable abilities and daggers guided by Tunare herself.
I cannot say for certain what the future has in store for you.
But I can say that wherever your adventures may take you it is always wise to have the proper [training].

>What proper training

The training that I speak of is only intended for rogues of Tunare that wish to develop skills that will mold them into the most deadly assassins in all of the lands.
If you are a [rogue of Tunare] then I will be happy to explain the training exercises to you.
However, if you are not a rogue I suggest you seek training from another house.'

>I am a rogue of Tunare

Excellent Xxxxx, let us get started then.
Your training will test your fighting and gathering skills respectively, because of the items you will be required to gather all over the Faydark for [magical armor materials].

>What magical armor materials?

First things first, here is your Assassins Mail Assembly Kit.
Inside this kit you will combine different magical items, which in certain quantities in your mail kit can create armor materials for specific pieces of Tunarean Scouts Armor.
Once you have completed a specific armor material you can then place it in a forge with the applicable pattern to create your piece of armor.
Simply tell me what armor piece you [want] to craft and I can provide you with the material recipes and armor patterns for [Coifs], [Bracers], [Sleeves], [Boots], [Legplates], [Gauntlets] and [Tunics].

まず、ここにあなたのAssassins Mail Assembly Kitがある。
このキットの中にあなたは魔法の材料を詰める。そうすればTunarean Scouts Armorの元ができる。
そしてパターンと一緒にForgeで鍛冶をしなさい。
欲しい部分、[Coifs], [Bracers], [Sleeves], [Boots], [Legplates], [Gauntlets] , [Tunics]を言えばそのレシピとパターンを渡しましょう。

・Assassins Mail Assembly Kit

>I want to craft coifs

To create your coif material you will need to combine 2 Bricks of Crude Bronze, 1 Pixie Wing , 1 Ruined Wolf Pelt and 1 Bottle in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Coif of the Tunarean Scout.

あなたのCoifを作るためには、Bricks of Crude Bronzeを２つ、Pixie Wing、Ruined Wolf Pelt、BottleをAssembly KitでCombineしなさい。
そして出来た物をこのMoldと一緒にForgeで鍛冶をしなさい。
