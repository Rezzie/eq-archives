　QeynosのSilentFistClanのちょっとしたクエストです。Rikka嬢に協力いただき
ました。
　NorthQeynosを出ずに済み、またなかなか面白いので、初心者の方は兆戦してみ
ては？

　NorthQeynos の門外にいる Tomer Instogle に話しかけます。

You say, 'hail Tomer Instogle'
Tomer Instogle says 'Ahhhgggg.. Those mangy [dogs] put up a tough
fight.. If I don't make it back to the Clan House soon, it'll all be
over for me.

You say, 'What is dogs?'
Tomer Instogle says 'It's those Darkpaws.. They've beat me pretty
badly.. but they'll get theirs soon enough!
You say, 'What is the clan house?'
Tomer Instogle says 'I am a new member of the Silent Fist Clan.. I
need someone from my guild to help me find my way back.

　SilentFistClan に戻って、ギルドマスター LuSun に聞いてみます。

You say, 'hail LuSun'
LuSun says 'Ah.. Greetings.. I am Lu'Sun, Guild Master of the Silent
Fist Clan. Here, our goal is train our body, mind, and soul to the
peak of perfection.
You say, 'Who is tomer instogle?'
LuSun says 'Tomer? I think Seta needs someone to go find him.. Go
ask her.

　では言われたとおりに Setaに会いに行きましょう。
