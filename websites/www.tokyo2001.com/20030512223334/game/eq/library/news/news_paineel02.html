Runnyeye Citadel 'Liberated'From the Clutches of King Xorbb RunnyeyeがKing Xorbbの支配から開放される

Reports of a battle within the Citadel of Runnyeye have been intercepted from messengers across Highpass Keep and the Karana Plains.
These reports have given word that the goblins have gathered enough gall and feral distaste for the Evil Eyes that have held them as slaves and minters within the Gorge of King Xorbb to cause a successful uprising.

It has also been comfirmed that the former King of these goblin slaves has been executed by the leader of the rebellion who then took command of his abase subjects and the keep.
King Xorbb, however, remains alive and a possibility of a counter strike from him remains.

 Runnyeyeの内乱の報告がHighpassとKaranaの使者からもたらされた。
これらの報告によると、King Xorbbに対する不満からゴブリンの奴隷達が反乱を起こし、成功したというものだ。

これらゴブリン達はKingを処刑したとも伝えられる。
しかしながらKing Xorbbは生きている模様である。
そして反撃のチャンスをうかがっている。
The Plaguebringer's Blight Plaguebringerの悪影響
The kingdom of Qeynos felt the venomous touch of the Plaguebringer's hand several weeks past, but only now is this successful act of the Bloodsabres reaching the four corners of Odus.

Good Eva, the bakter of our darkened fair city, has reported that supplies obtained from the fields of Karana are few and those that rely on the bounty of those fields are now suffering as a result.
The citizens of Paineel shall, unfortunately, feel the touch of the Bloodsabre's success in bringing about the power of their divine lord, a success that we may find reason to give retaliation toward if our own city suffers at any greater length. 		Qeynosはこの数週間、Plaguebringerの手による災厄の影響を受けている。
しかしこのBloodsabresの成功も、Odusの辺境にまで影響が及びつつある。

我々の暗闇に閉ざされし街にあるパン屋のEvaは、Karanaの疫病の影響で材料の供給が滞っていると打ち明けた。
Paineelの市民に更にこれ以上の苦しみが続くならば、皮肉にもBloodsabreに対して何らかの報復も考えざるを得ない。
