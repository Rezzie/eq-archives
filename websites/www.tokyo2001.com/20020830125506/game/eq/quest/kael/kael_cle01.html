>hail Vylleam Vyaeltor.

Vylleam Vyaeltor bows Greetings to you.
You are far away from your homelands, stranger.
I am looking for someone.
Someone that the outlanders call a cleric.
Are you a cleric? 


>I am a cleric.

Ahhh... most pious one.
I sense the aura of your devotion and conviction and it is strong about you.
A shame you are so short lived, mortal.
But I see potential in you and for that I shall aid you in your calling.
Do you wish my aid? 

>I wish your aid.

Then I have a set of goals for you.
Once you have achieved them, you shall be rewarded with such as these; a helm, a breastplate, armplates, bracers, gauntlets, greaves and boots.
May they protect you from your enemies. 

>What helm?

I shall craft a helm for you but you must seek these items out for me first.
I have need of an ancient tarnished plate helmet and three crushed onyx sapphires.. 


>What breastplate?

Such as your faith protects you so shall this breastplate.
I must have an ancient tarnished breastplate and three pieces of black marble. 

>What armplate?

For the armplates, I shall require a pair of an ancient tarnished plate armplates as well as three jaundice gems.
Go forth and seek these out. 

>What bracer?

For the bracers, faithful one, I shall require the acquisition of an ancient tarnished plate bracer and three crushed opals. 


>What gauntlets?

I will need a pair of a ancient tarnished gauntlets three crushed lava rubys, so that I may make your reward.


>What greaves?

You shall acquire a pair of an ancient tarnished plate greaves and three chipped onyx sapphires for me.
I await your return. 


>What boots?

With your aid I shall make a pair of fine boots for you.
All that is needed is a pair of an ancient tarnished plate boots and a trilogy of crushed flame emeralds.
