>What is most sacred tomb?

In this block of ice lies the sole remnants of our great father, leader, and founder Colin Dain.
While leading our people to the safety of the mountains following the destruction of our beloved Froststone, Colin came to a grim realization.
He knew that there was no way our people could outrun the giants and safely get away.
And so he took thirty volunteers and led them in a suicide ambush to halt the giants' chase while the rest of our people got away.
Without his sacrifice the Coldain might very well have been wiped out that day.
Once we were safe, scouts were dispatched to the battle with the hope of finding survivors.
Amidst the field of bodies they found this crown, axe, and breastplate which belonged to our beloved leader.
Of Colins actual body, no remnant was ever found. 


氷に覆われたこの区画で我々の素晴らしき父でありリーダーであり、そして創設者であるColin Dainの遺品が横たわっています。
我々の最愛のFroststoneの街が破壊された後、人々を山の安全な所まで逃がす間にColinは厳しい事態に直面していました。
彼は人々がジャイアントより速く走り、そして安全に逃げ切る事ができる方法が無い事を知っていました。
それで彼は３０人の志願者を募り、そして自滅覚悟で追跡する巨人を待ち伏せして時間を稼ぎ、人々が逃げ切れるようにしました。
彼の犠牲なければColdainはその日滅亡していたかもしれません。
