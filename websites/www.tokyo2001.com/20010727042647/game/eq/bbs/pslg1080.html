1080SouthRo:Bounty for Cutthroat Rings (LOG)Fine@Veeshan県警 E-mail URL9/28-14:10

場所：SouthRo
対象：Ortallius (Orc)
所属：SolusekRo (unknown)

このQUESTを実行した時のFactionはindiffrentlyでした。
OrtalliusはSouthRoの西側の壁沿いのテントにいます。
Dervishを殺すことで得ることのできるリングを二つ渡すというものです。

○ You say, 'hail Ortallius'

● Ortallius says 'Greetings traveler. Please go over to the shade of
one of the tents. If you are in need of provisions [Rathamna] will
help you.

○ You say, 'who is rathmana'

● Ortallius says 'Rathmana is a great wizard. He is also by mest
friend. [Solusek] sent him to save me from the savage desert
when I was but a child in my dying mothers arms. He saved me
from being captured by the [Dervish Cutthroats].

○ You say, 'what is dervish cutthroats'

● Ortallius says 'The Dervish Cutthroats are the vile scum which
inhabit the desert. They are the rogues who assault wayward
travelers. I have put a bounty upon their heads. I offer it to any
good soul who return with three Cutthroat Rings.

☆ Dervish Cutthroat Ringを３つ渡す。

● Ortallius says 'You will make fine member to our circle. Continue
the cleaning of the desert. Let it be known that the Defenders of
Ro are here to challenge the evils of the desert.

You faction standing with SolusekRo got better

You gain experience!!

You receive 4 copper from Ortallius.
You receive 2 silver from Ortallius.

これもあんま意味ないですね。
私のようなFactionマニア用でしょうか（ｗ
