場所：Neriak Forign Quater
対象：X'Ta Tompi (Dark Elf)
所属：TheDead (SHD,NEC Guard)

Neriakの第一ゾーンのゲートの上に３人のDark Elfがいます。
この３人に指定された材料を渡すことで、
NECのペットのResarchに必要なWordsを手に入れることができます。

このQUESTを達成した時のFactionは amiably でした。

○ You say, 'hail X'Ta Tompi'

とりあえず３人のうちの一人に話し掛けてみました。

● X'Ta Tompi says 'We three are the [Sisters Dark] - [Necromancy] is
our Art - Bonded dead, they serve our will - No beat of heart, yet
faithful still.

○ You say, 'what is sisters dark?'

● X'Ta Tompi says 'We three are the Sisters Dark - Keepers of the
shadowed Dread - [Lore of Death], we resarch now - Join with us
and serve [The Dead]

○ You say, 'what is lore of death?'

● X'Ta Tompi says 'The Lore of Death is shadow bound - its [words]
are [hid], yet shall be found - Through our resarch into the dark -
Old hexs found and parchment marked.

○ You say, 'where is words?'

● X'Ta Tompi says 'The Words are hid in tomes of old - Their yielded
lore worth more than gold - This we resarch with devout minds -
But for [components] we can not find.

○ You say, 'what is components?'

● X'Ta Tompi says 'We need components for resarch - These are
tings that you could find - In return for [tasks] that you perform -
Words of [reward] we have in mind.

○ You say, 'what is tasks?'

３人のうちそれぞれ作れるWordsが違うようです。

● X'Ta Tompi says 'Bring us components for our research - We give
[Word]s by tasks performed - [Possesion],[Detachment],[Allure],
[Haunting],[Rupturing],[Dark Paths],[Suffering],[Collection],
[Obligation],[Requisition] and [Acquisition].

○ You say, 'what is word of acquisition?'

とりあえず、AcquisitionのWordsを聞いてみることにします。

● X'Ta Tompi says 'To gain the Beza Words of Acquisition - Bring me
A Fetid Skin - Scare Starw - Turmoil Warts And a Pearl.

☆ A Fetid Skin, Scare Starw, Turmoil Warts, Pearlを渡す。

運良くAcquisitionの材料が揃っていたので渡して見ました。

A Fetid Skin > Plane of Fear > Fetid Fiend (Zonbieのでかいやつ)
Scare Starw > Plane of Fear > Samhine (かかしのでかいやつ)
Turmoil Warts > Plane of Fear > Boogieman (Ghoulのでかいやつ)
Pearl > Vendor

Accept our thanks - And this reward.
