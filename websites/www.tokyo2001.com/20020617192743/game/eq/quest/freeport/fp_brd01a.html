NorthFreeportのBRDギルドから。

>Hail Sten Harnak

Greetings to you Xxxxx.
I am Sten Harnak Virtuoso of Freeport.
I have lived in Freeport and studied the art of songweaving here for all of my days.
I am always eager to see new faces here in our concert hall.
If you are here for a performance I am afraid it does not start till a bit later but if you are a young bard in training then I might have some [work] for you.

>What work?

Well I have numerous concerts here that I must prepare for so I always must be sure that I have the best possible support as far as my fellow bards go.
I am always seeking new young talent to sing in our concerts.
If you are a bard I have made tasks for you to complete to see if you are a worthy songweaver.
These tasks will test your determination and battle skills.
I would only allow one that has completed all of my [specific tests] to sing in my concert hall.

>What specific tests?

If you are ready to begin your training I will assign you a number of tests.
These tests will enable you to outfit yourself with a suit of armor only worn by the most respected musicians of Freeport.
First, I will present you with a Songweavers Assembly Kit.
In this kit you will combine certain [recipes] that will be magically formed into an armor material.
Each one of these materials will need to be combined in a forge with the appropriate mold to make your armor piece.

・Songweavers Assembly Kit

を受け取ります。作りたい部位を聞いてみます。

>What recipes?

I have the material recipes available for Songweavers Platemail [Helms], [Bracers], [Armguards], [Boots], [Greaves], [Gauntlets] and [Breastplates].
When you are ready to craft a specific piece simply tell me what armor piece you [want] to craft.
For example if you would wish to craft a helm tell me “I want to craft a helm”.
Also please keep in mind that the components necessary for crafting your breastplate material are the most difficult to collect so you might want to collect them last.

>I want to craft a helm

A very important armor piece indeed Xxxxx, one well placed attack to the head by an adversary could leave you in very poor condition.
To create your helm material you will need to combine 2 Bricks of Crude Iron Ore, 1 Ruined Wolf Pelt, 1 Deathfist Pawn Scalp and 1 Barley in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Songweavers Platemail Helm.

を受け取ります。

・Barley
