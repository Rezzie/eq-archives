1725/-1740にいるTreantから。

　
　

>hail a mosscovered treant.

I welcome thee Xxxx , is there something I can assist you with?
I am afraid my tireless searching for the incantations is going to be endless.

こんにちは 、何かあなたを手伝うことができますか？
私は呪文の探索が果てしないかもしれない事を恐れています。

>What is searching.

For quite some time now I have been seeking the teachings and scribings of Conjurers and Summoners of great power.
As I continue my personal quest towards enlightenment there is absolute knowledge that evades me to this day. 

これまでかなり長い間私は教訓とConjurersによって書かれた物と素晴らしい力を持つSummonersを探し求めていました。
私の悟りの為の個人的な探索を続けているのだが、今日に至るまで私がうまく手に入れられない絶対的な知識があります。

>What is knowledge?

There is believed to be a tome that contained only the most spectacular and powerful summoning.
Many great magicians of time passed were believe to be contributors to this magical tome.
During the meeting called by these select few something went terribly wrong. 

ただ最も壮大で強力な召喚する事が書かれた本があると信じられています。
長い年月をかけて多くの偉大なるマジシャンがこの神秘的な本に関わってきたと信じられています。
選りすぐられた者の会合の席で何かまずい事があり、破壊されました。


>What is few something went terribly wrong?

Of those in attendance, there was one servant of Innoruuk whose intentions were to steal the research and knowledge of his peers and use it to benefit himself and his Dark god.
This selfishness led to numerous Intellectual conflicts and each Arch-Mage decided to go their separate ways, only to perish so close to the answer for which they seeked.

出席している者の中に、暗黒神Innoruukに仕える者がいました。
この自分勝手な者は魔法使い達を対立へと仕向けました。
