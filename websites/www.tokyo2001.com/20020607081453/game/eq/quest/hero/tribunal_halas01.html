SHMギルドのJinkus Felliganから。

>Hail, Jinkus Felligan

Welcome! Welcome to the Church o' the Tribunal!
Do ye require healing or are ye [new to the Shamans o' Justice], then, eh?

>I am new to the Shamans of Justice

Then I welcome ye into our noble order.
Ye'll serve us well or spend an eternity in the dungeons o' the Tribunal.
Are ye ready to [contribute to yer church] or do ye [wish to meditate] within our walls?'

>I contribute to yer church

Ye'll help all o' Halas by doing the bidding o' this church.
Go to Holana. Tell her [Jinkus sent] ye to assist her.
She'll have worrk fer ye. Perhaps, if ye're planning on passing near Qeynos, ye can [make a delivery] as well?

>I can make a delivery

The Church of the Tribunal works with the Qeynos Guards to apprehend fugitives.
There's a new criminal on the loose, so we must send a wanted poster to Qeynos for them to display on the walls of their bank.
Speak with Cindl about a piece o' mammoth hide parchment and with Greta about some ink and I'll prrepare the bounty poster.

つまりCindlからMammothHideを、GretaからInkを手に入れるようです。

ではまずHalasの有名裁縫屋さん「Cindl」に会いに行きます。

>hail Cindl.

>What is Mammoth Hide Parchment?

Jinkus must've sent ye fer sum muir mammoth hyde parchment fer 'is posters.
Here, tayke it, frree o' charge, as a donation t' th' church an' t' th' will o' th' Trrybunal as well.

JinkusがMammoth Hide Parchmentの為にお前たちを遣したのかな。
タダだ。持っていきなさい。

・Mammoth Hide Parchment

が手に入ります。
次にGretaを探します。Cindlの近くに居います(屋外)。

>hail Greta Terrilon.

>What is ink?

Jinkus must 've sent ye fer some of me special ink made frrom the pigment of th' datura flower.
Ye may tayke some frree o' charge as a donation t' th' Church and t' th' will of th' Trrybunal as well. 

Jinkusが花の色素から作った特別製のインクのためにあなたたちを遣したのかな。
持っていきなさい。
