EQ2001 >> NewsPaper >> KaladimVol.2

　

　

- Qeynos not at war -
King Antonius Bayle be assurin' all the citizens of Qeynos that reported threat of war was nothin' more than rumos.
He urged everyone to remain calm and not t'be resortin' to acts that were against der ways. . .an' der laws. 		　
- The Lass Firiona -

Ag'in we bring you news from Felwithe.
The lovely Firiona Vie has managed t'git the sacred stones for summoning the avatar of Tunare.
'Tis sure t'be a sight t'see such lovely lasses together.

 　
- The Brewer's Corner -

As any good dwarf be knowin' a good drink be hard t'find.
'Tis why we added dis special section fer dis edition of the Mining Journal.
Iffin' ye want to try out a new brewin' recipe, try ya'self som' fancy Fizzlepop.

T'make the Fizzlepop, be mixin' these. . .

1 Aerated Mineral Water
1 Sylvan Berries
1 Berries
2 Bottles

Dis sure be not an Ale drink but we're sure you will find it tasty.

 　
- Lost and Found -

Baker Gretta Mottle, a lovely lass she be, has recently agreed to set up shop in Tumpy Irontoe's fine pub.

You'd be sad to hear that she lost several crates of her bakin' good durin' the move to Kaladim from Freeport.

Her assistant is sure he saw the crates come off the boat.
What happened to 'em from there, he don't be knowin'.
Grett be offerin' a handsome reward for the return of her crates.
