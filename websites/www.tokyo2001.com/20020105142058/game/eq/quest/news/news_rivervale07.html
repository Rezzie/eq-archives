>Food Shortage Problems Over? 　

The temple of Life in Qeynos has found a way to counteract the plague elements in the Karana lands.
This is glorius news indeed.
I heard that there was some farmer human there and he was growing foods just fine because he buried a wee statue in his dirt.
Seems that this no good man helped the no good men who started the trouble to begin with and those bad folks gave this bad farmer the statue as payment.
Anyway, the goodly Priests of Life in Qeynos turned that little statue over and over until they figured out what it was about it that fixed the plague.
Soon, they will try very hard to make this magic happen all over the Karanas so we can make fresh muffins and breads again.
Happy days!

 　
>Lorrk the Insane Slain! 　

In nearby East Karana, one of the worst of the bad evil eyes there, Lorrk the Insane, was recently dispatched by a band of adventurers.
I think gypsies sent them to do it, because they brought them back his parts and she gave them an old chest full of magic stuff.
Someone even told me that the chest was for Al'Kabor because he was looking to combine some things.
Now what that wizard needs with a chest from the gypsies I've nary a guess, but I am sure glad to have one less thing to be worry about when I pass through that place.
