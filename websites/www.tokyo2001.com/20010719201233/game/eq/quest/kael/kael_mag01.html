MAG専用防具

　

街の奥のKing Tormaxがいる城エリアにて。

　
　

>hail Mjeldor Felstorm.

Greetings to you.
I seek those who call themselves magicians.
Are you a magician, little one? 

>I am a magician.

I thought so. 
I have several tasks for you to accomplish. 
Once you have completed them I will have a cap, a robe, sleeves, wristbands, gloves, leggings and boots to reward you with. 

>What cap?

For you to receive my gift, I shall require an ancient silk turban and three crushed flame opals. 

>What robe?

This exquisite robe shall be yours in exchange for an ancient silk robe and three pristine emeralds. 


>What sleeves?

For these durable sleeves, you must fetch me a pair of ancient silk sleeves and three flawed topazes. 


>What wristband?

The crafting of this wristband requires that you bring me an ancient silk wristband and three crushed onyx sapphire pieces. 



>What gloves?

For this fine pair of gloves you must seek out and return to me a pair of ancient silk gloves and a crushed topaz. 



>What leggings?

This pair of leggings will yours provided you supply me with a pair of ancient silk pantaloons as well as three nephrites. 


>What boots?

These supple boots shall be yours upon receipt of a pair of ancient silk boots and three crushed jaundice gems.
