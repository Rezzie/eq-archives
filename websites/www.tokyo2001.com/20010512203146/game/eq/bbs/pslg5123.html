please ask, if not i must return to my tasks."

bless you there."

Nenette say, "I will return to your tasks"
Elia the Pure say, "It is here that the light shines brightest in
our fair city. Here I sit, transcribing scrolls of Luminescence.
Spells to light the dark, to blind the evill with purity, or to
heal those in pain.
I find it so much easier to work with such spells if I am in the
light itself."

ということでIrakさんのおっしゃるとおり光で盾を浄化して貰いましょう。

Elia the Pure squints and turns her head.
'By the gods. Such a twisted aura this has.
To see it hurts my eyes.
I will help you if for no other reason than to remove such a twisted
thing from existence.'
She unrolls a scroll from her pack and softly chants over the dark
metal shield.
She hands it back to you with an exhausted look on and a smile on her
face.
'Your heart seems pure. If I was able to help than I am honored.
May the light shine warmly upon you.'

ということで浄化した盾Gleaming Crested Shieldを返してもらえます。
