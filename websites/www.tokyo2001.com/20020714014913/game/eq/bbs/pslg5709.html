5709WAR Epic Logの一部と質問Luvfina@saryrn E-mail URL1/1-16:16

途中は省いて最後の方だけ。

You say, 'hail Kargek Redblade'

Kargek Redblade looks up from his forge and says, 'Hello there, can
I help you?' Your eyes are drawn to an extremely large red sword
scabbard strapped to his back with no sword in it. Kargek speaks
again, 'I said, can I help you or do you need help from Wenden?'

Kargek Redblade says 'What is this? A note from my brother? Thank
you for the gift as well.' Kargek reads the note and hands you the
huge sword hilt. Your mind is filled with the sound of battle and a
voice. 'Place the ancient sword blade, ancient blade, finely crafted
dragon head hilt and the jeweled dragon head hilt into the scabbard
to receive your rewards. If you think you are worthy, place your
rewards into the scabbard and see what havoc you can unleash on the
world.

You gain party experience!!


Scabbardに2つのHiltと2つのBladeを入れてCombineするとBlade of Tacticsと
Strategyが出来ます。更にその2本のBladeをScabbardでCombineすると、
Jagged Blade of Warとなります。Kargekとの会話ではJagged Blade of Warを
Blade of TacticsとStrategyに戻せるとは言っていません。
ところがRight Clickで戻せるとか(不可)Scabbardを使うとか(未確認)噂があり、
実際に戻せた人はいるのでしょうか？確かにScabbardは手元に残り何回でも
使えます。ただしゴミItem2個をCombineすると消える事から、失敗はLostとなる
ので確信が無いと実行できません。体験談、もしくは体験談の載っている海外Site
があれば教えてください。
