Obtain the Staff of Elemental Mastery:Earth, held by the minions of the Prince of Hate, The Dirt of Underfoot found on Slix something or the other, and of course, I shall need the Broom of Trilon and the Shovel of Ponz.
Bring all these things, and I shall show you what a true Master of Earth can do, and gift you with the Element of Earth.
