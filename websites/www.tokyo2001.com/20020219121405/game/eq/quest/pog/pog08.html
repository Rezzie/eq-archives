I greet thee Xxxxx you will have to pardon my demeanour I am afraid that I am not in the best of spirits.
Recently yet another brother was taken from us from an evil unknown through the dark art of mind rending.

こんにちは。
最近私の兄弟が暗黒の術を使う者によって連れ去られました。


>What is evil?

Somewhere there exists a group of outcast phantasmists that use their powers to only harvest true evil.
These masters of the mind are of origin that we are yet to uncover for they only exist in ones thoughts.
Slowly turning one insane from the horrors that pound through their mind.

どこかで、真の邪悪の力を得る事だけを求める亡霊の一団がいます。
それらは徐々に心を侵食し狂気に陥れます。

>What outcast phantasmists?

These outcasts were once believe to be part of our circle before turning to the way of evil and using the mindbending abilities that they possess to take over those who knowledge would benefit them most.

悪の道に進む前の奴らは、我らと同じ仲間でした。
そして奴らの持つ能力の中で最も役に立つものはmindbendingです。

>What mindbending abilities?

What these beguilers are capable of is unknown, but what we do know is that as each day passes we are all at risk from an attack that could very well end us.
But there is a chance that if we could find the ancient tomes of their weaknesses, this evil could be stopped.

これらの能力がどんなものかはよく判りません。しかし、我々が常に危険に晒されているという事には変わりありません。
けれどももし我々が奴らの弱点である古代の大冊を見つけることができたなら、この悪を止められることができるかも・・・と思っています。

>What ancient tomes?

Legend exists that there are two lost tomes that cover the darker sides of Mind control.
These tomes were last believed to be possessed by mortals much like yourself that stumbled upon them not knowing what they possessed because they could not understand the unknown code the books are scribed in.

最も暗黒の部分を記述してあると言われる伝説の古代の本が存在する。
これらの本は、最後には偶然あなたの様な姿の人間の手に渡ったが、その不可思議な言葉で書かれている為に中身を理解できないまま所有されていると言われています。

>What is code?

To prevent those that could not control the powerful abilities that these tomes described how to perform.
These tomes were scribed of ancient language that not many could decifer.
In search of someone that could assist them these heros fell in the city of giants.

これらの本に秘められている強力な力をコントロールできなくなる事を防ぐために。
その本の大部分は古代の言語によって手書きされています。


>What ancient language?

As I told you before these books are distinctly coded to only be understood by those that have the abilities to perform and contain these magics.
If these tomes were recovered it is very possible that I could decode them and develop an immunity to the mind altering attacks that I feel are upon us. 

私はあなたにこれらの本の内容が明らかにされる前に取り戻していただきたい。
もしこれらの本が取り戻されたなら、我らがそれを解読して、そして奴らの攻撃から身を守る事ができるだろう。
