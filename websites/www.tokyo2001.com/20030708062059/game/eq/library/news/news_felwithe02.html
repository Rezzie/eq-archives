That Which is Sacred That Which is Sacred

Several days past,our princess and divine Champion,Firiona Vie, made a plea to our High Priest of Tunare for the creation of the four sacred stones of nature.
The elves of Tunare have had little need for these stones since our migration from Tunaria,but The Priests of Tunare have kept such rituals sacred and within the archives of documented rituals.


Abiding most graciously to Firiona's request,Yeolam Bronzeleaf set immediately to work on the four-day ritual that would produce the stones requested.
The days have passed and the stones have been completed.
Now we wait patiently and with the greatest of hope that Tunare's Champion shall be successful in her need for these sacred stones.

 　
Thirst for Vengeance Quelled 　
The people of Qeynos have cried out for blood and the rumors of war have glided through the Karana air as a bitter,screeching horn played by the inept.


Antonious Bayle IV,ruler of Qeynos and the karana Plains,has called for an immediate silence to the painful rumors of blood and battle.


The young,zealous ruler demanded that all remain in peace and that he will do all within his power to see that war does not come to his kingdom so long as he remains upon the throne.
The people have since given to their sovereign's just rule and returned to their daily routines.
