Tranquility Shackled

It has come to attension that a member of the Peace Keepers has been captured by the Kobolds of the Warrens.
Though we are unaware as to the details surrounding this Erudite's capture, we do know that this young priest is a respected member of the Erudin council.
Coriante Verisue has stated that we shall not make any attempts to move this prisoner from Kobold prisons to our own as of yet.
though we are keeping a watchful eye upon the actions of the Erudin council in their motives, if any , to release this young apprentice.

 　
Cleansing of the Glade 　
Anronius Bayle IV of Qeynos has issued a search for two members that are suspected to have poisoned the waters of Surefall Glade when the curse of the Bloodsabres first touched the lands of Qeynos in the past.
These members have been charged with attempeted murder of Holly Windstalker, several sacred beasts of the Glade , and the murder of Cross Treewind who was destroyed by defiled grizzly.

Though we hold little doubt that these debased "criminals" will be apprehended , we are skeptical that the manhunt will give away to any relief of the curse that is plaguing the farmlands of Karanas.
Perhaps if the Qeynos citizens would cease their killing of the Bloodsabre members and use a bit of tact , force , and intelligence , they might discover the true composition of the curse and with it a possible cure.
But then again , they are a peaceful nation , are they not? 
Such " barbaric " ways are beneath them , after all.
