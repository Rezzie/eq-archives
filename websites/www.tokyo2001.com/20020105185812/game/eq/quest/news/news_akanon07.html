Firiona Tussles in Kunark

Now we all know why Firiona Vie made the decision to journey back to the continent of Kunark and it apparently was to find information on the current whereabouts of her staff, The Lifeguide.
We should have known it all along!

Well anyway, this gigantic dragon by the name of Lady Zavo swooped out of the sky (I'm not lying either!).
But rather than eating everyone, the gigantic lizard offered her information.
She was told that two individuals know of the location of the Lifeguide, the Iksar lich Venril Sathir and the ogre Ghargin Bublock.

Faced with the choice between confronting an eons old lich of inestimable power and a stupid ogre, Firiona chose the ogre.

Accompanying Firiona was the ranger Lyirae Oakwynd and several hundred of her other close and personal friends.
Eyewitness reports indicate that some sort of old blood feud existed between Lyirae and Ghargin.
We use the past tense because when Lyirae put an arrow smack into the middle of Ghargin's chest the feud ended in her favor.

 　
Qeynos Finds a Cure 　
Our reporters from abroad report that the humans have made some sort of breakthrough and claim that they should be able to end the plague within a matter of a week or so.
Apparently, the priests plan to use some sort of massive magical conflagration to burn away all traces of the plague.

We here at TNWR wish the humans the best in their effort.
We also hope that everyone is able to survive, as I'm sure we've all witnessed massive magical conflagrations gone horribly wrong due to tinkering miscalculations in Ak'Anon.
Humans are hardy sort though and we have no doubt that there will be at least a few survivors to carry the human race forward.
