　Sanctus SeruのZoneからすぐの所にたき火を囲んだ集団がいます
その内の１人のDwarfがくれるQuestです
このゾーン内で危険な生き物について語ってくれます

　
　

>Hail, Kanthek Ogrebane

Hello there. Welcome my friend! Please sit and rest yourself.
There are many [predators] around.
I suggest yourest a bit before continuing your journey.

いっらしゃい。ようこそ！どうかそこに座って、休んでいってください。
そこら中に［肉食動物］がいます。
あなたが旅を続ける前に、私は少し提案したいことがあります。


>What predators?

Just take a look around and see what's around you.
There's [galorians], [lightcrawlers], [zelniaks], and [rockhoppers] everywhere.
They usually let us be, but I've seen 'em kill.
It's not something you want to see.

周りを見渡して見なさい。
そこら中に［galorians ］、［lightcrawlers］、［zelniaks］、［rockhoppers ］がいる。
奴らはいつもそうしているし、襲ったりもする。
あなたがそれを見ることは望まない。


>What lightcrawlers?

Lightcrawlers are the biggest bugs I've ever seen.
I've heard of bigger, but I've never seen 'em myself.
If you're interested in getting some [armor] made out of their shells let me know and I'll see if we can work something out.'

Lightcrawlers は私が今までに見た中で最も大きな虫だ。
私はもっと大きい物がいるとも聞いたが見た事は無い。
もしあなたが［防具］を欲しいならば、その殻を持ってきなさい。

>What armor?

My cousin can make you some armor, but it'll cost ya.
What exactly are you looking for?
You looking for a [breastplate], [greaves], [helm], [bracers], [arms]?
You tell me and I'll see what I can do.
The only problem is I only have enough material to make a small set.
So you might not be able to fit in 'em.'

私の従妹はあなたの防具のセットを作れますが、それなりの危険も伴います。
正確にはあなたは何を探しているのかな？
[breastplate], [greaves], [helm], [bracers], [arms]か？
言ってくれればそれを作るのに何が必要か教えましょう。
唯一の問題は私が小さいサイズ用の材料しか持ち合わせていないことだ。
あなたに合わないかもしれないが。

>What breastplate?

'Faber Rugon can make a fine breastplate for you, but it'll require two Pristine Lightcrawler Shells.'

Faber RugonにPristine Lightcrawler Shellsを２枚持っていきなさい

>What greaves?

Give Faber Rugon back two sets of Lightcrawler Legs and two Shimmering Lightcrawler Shells.

Faber RugonにLightcrawler Legs2本とShimmering Lightcrawler Shell２つを持っていきなさい

>What helm?

Interested in a new helm, eh? Alright.
Bring Faber Rugon two Ardent Lightcrawler Shells and he'll be able to help you out.

Faber RugonにArdent Lightcrawler Shell2枚持っていきなさい

>What bracers?

Faber Rugon can only make one bracer for you, but one will be enough.
Bring him back two Stripped Lightcrawler Shells and you'll be set.

Faber RugonにStripped Lightcrawler Shells2枚持っていく

>What arms?

Alright, bring my cousin, Faber Rugon, back two Spotted Lightcrawler Shell and he'll be able fasion up a fine pair of arms for you.

Faber RugonにSpotted Lightcrawler Shell2枚持っていく

>What galorians?

It's been a while since I've seen the Tribal Council, but they have been known to show up unexpectedly.
I'm sure if you find Glenda Melonfus, she'll be able to tell you more about them'

奴らは不意に現われることで知られている。
あなたがGlenda Melonfusに会えれば彼女はさらに詳しく話を聞くことができるだろう。

>What zelniaks?

Zelniaks don't tend to mess with the likes of others, unless provoked.'

刺激さえ与えなければ奴らは襲ってはこない。

>What rockhoppers?

Rockhoppers took over the cave system near Maiden's Eye.
They are a crazy bunch of lizards.
I suggest stay out of their way.
The galorians are even scared of them and they're not scared of much.

Rockhoppersは Maiden's Eyeの近くの洞穴を引き継いだ。
奴らは頭がおかしいトカゲの集団だ。
私はあそこに近づかない事を忠告する。
galoriansでさえ奴らに怯えている。そして奴らは何者にも怯えない。
