>Hail, Queen Velazul Di`zok

Hail, minion! It is good to see a member of a lesser race striving to improve themself through service to the Dizok.
We are feeling especially generous as of late, and if you would do us a [service], we shall reward you handsomely.

ああ！ちっぽけな種が我がDizokに尽くすのを見るのは喜ばしい事だ。
もし更に[サービス]し続けるならば、我々は気前良く報酬を与えることも考えよう。

>What is service?

We are not sure you are worthy, but shall assign you this task in hopes you prove mighty enough to resolve it.
Gather your broodlings about you, and journey to Charasis.
There you shall find a Urn of spirits.
We require this, as well as [additional components].

我々はお前がよくやってくれているとは確信できない。しかしその疑念を晴らす仕事が無いとは言い切れない。
お前の仲間を引き連れ、Charasisに行きなさい。
そこでUrn of spiritsを見つけるだろう。
我々は［残りのアイテム］と共にそれを必要としている。

>What is additional components?

Very well, if you feel you are up to it, lead your broodlings to fallen Sebilis and return to us with a portion of undead dragon sinew.
Bring us the Sinew, the Urn, and your Regal Band of Bathezid as proof of service, and a worthy reward shall be yours.

もしお前がやれると言うのならSebilisに行き、そしてUndead竜のSinew（腱）の１部を持ってきなさい。
我々にSinew、Urnとお前の持っているRegal Band of Bathezidを持って来なさい。その奉仕に対する報酬を与えよう。
