Welcome to Surefall Glade friend.

If you are in need of a [sturdy outfit] suitable for work as a young ranger then I will gladly instruct you on the means to obtain one.

>What sturdy outfit?
>私は衣装が必要です

This specially prepared curing kit will allow you to craft an outfit that will protect you in the wilds of Norrath.
The materials required for the outfit vary depending on the piece you desire to craft.
Do you desire to craft Pine Scout [Gloves], Pine Scout [Boots], a Pine Scout [Bracer], a Pine Scout [Cap], Pine Scout [Leggings], Pine Scout [Sleeves], or a Pine Scout [Tunic]?
Once you are properly outfitted there is an [issue] that you may be of assistance in resolving.

ノーラスの荒野で貴方を守るであろう衣装は、特別に用意された Curing Kit で作成します。
衣装を作るために必要な材料は、貴方が収集しなければなりません。
さて、[ グローブ ]、[ ブーツ ]、[ ブレイサー ]、[ キャップ ]、[ レギング ]、[ スリーブ ]、[ チュニック ] のどれを作成したいですか？　すべてを作り終えた後、貴方の助けが必要な [ 問題 ] もあるのです。

>What is Pine Scout Gloves?
>グローブ

To craft Pine Scout Gloves you require two [silk thread], ruined gnoll chain gloves, two giant field rat whiskers, and a large whiskered bat fur.
Once you have the necessary components combine them in your Curing Kit with this Tattered Glove Pattern.

グローブを作るには、[ Silk Thread ] ２本、Ruined Gnoll Chain Gloves、Giant Field Rat Whisker ２本と Large Whiskered Bat Fur を集めてください。
それらのアイテムとこの Tattered Glove Pattern を Curing Kit に入れ、組み合わせれば Pine Scout Gloves の完成です。
