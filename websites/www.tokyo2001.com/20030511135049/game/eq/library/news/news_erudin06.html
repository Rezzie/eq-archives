*Difficult Decisions*
There have been reports that the desperate farmers in the plague-infested Karana lands have turned been acting in a manner far from normal considering the circumstances.
Antonious Bayle IV has sent his magistrate, Vegalys Keldrane, to investigate the rumors of a particular farmer who seems suspiciously free of the plague.

We can only hope that this farmer has found a cure for the plague in his crops and is unaware of his success.
It is disheartening to think that any other route could be possible -- have the Bloodsabres reached their venomous hand out to the poor citizens whom have become enslaved to their insipid blessings?

Our prayers are with Qeynos and the unfortunate innocent citizens that must suffer through these trying times and to his lordship, Magistrate Keldrane, may The Tranquil aid in the restoration of hope and peace beyond this weary curse.
