EQ2001 >> Quest >> Netherbian Lair >> Protecting Lerin Sardor

　

（情報提供：政景＠鬼兜）

　

Netherbian Lair の Dawnshroud Peaks のゾーン近くに Lerin Sardor がいます。

　
　

>Hail, Lerin Sardor

Ahh, hello there Xxxxxxxx.
I am a trader from Sanctus Seru trying to make my way to Shadowhaven for some dealings.
I have come this far with the aid of guards from Sanctus Seru, but they have turn back and left me to go on my own.
I hear there are many terrible [creatures] here lurking in the shadows, leave it to the guards of the Hand to leave me unprotected.


>What creatures?

What I was told is that this pass is filled with small horrible creatures with no eyes.
Perhaps you could [help by providing safe passage] to the other side?


>I will help by providing safe passage.

Great! If I can get safely to the other side I will give you a small trinket that I was going to sell.
Getting safe passage for the rest of my goods is worth more than the item in itself.
Surely you know a good deal when you hear one.
Take this note, if you agree to the conditions that I get across safely before you get payment return it to me and we will be on our way.

　

 

・Unsigned Agreement

を受け取ります。
つまり街まで行く間にガードして欲しいようです。
ガードする気があるなら承諾の印として彼に返しましょう。

　
　

(Lerin Sardor)
Let us go then, please be alert throughout the cavern.
Once we get to the other side safely give me the signed agreement and you will have your payment.

　
　

・Signed Agreement

を受け取ります。
Lerin Sardor が歩き出します。
何度か mob が Lerin Sardor を襲ってくるので、すべて倒します。

ゾーンの北端に差しかかると Lerin Sardor が立ち止まります。

　
　

(Lerin Sardor)
Whew, we are finally here.
If you will give me the contract I will keep up with my part of the bargain.

　
　

Signed Agreement を渡します。

　
　

(Lerin Sardor)
Thank you so much for your help!
As I promised here is a small token of my appreciation.

You gain experience!!
