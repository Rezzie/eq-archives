>What is supplies?

We have all kinds of supplies!
Normally we deliver them to the giants who live outside Kael. 

我々はあらゆる種類の補給物資を持っています！
大抵我々は Kael の外にいるジャイアントにそれらを配達します。

>What is giants?

There are several encampments in the eastern wastes that barter for supplies with us monthly.
The great divide also has a small outpost of Kromrif who strive to beat the Coldain back into their city of Thurgadin and of course the outposts in the wakening lands.
Our last batch of deliveries did not reach their intended targets.
The runners have not been heard from since they were dispatched either.

Eastern Wastesには毎月我々の補給物資を求めるいくつかの野営地があります。
The Great Divideには同じくKromrifの小さな居留地があり、Coldainを撃退しようと日々努めています。
そして勿論Wakening Landsにもあります。
最後に出た我々の補給軍はそれらの地まで到達できませんでした。
彼らからの連絡がありません。

>What is Wakening Land?

Korzak Stonehammer is the name of the man whom we usually deliver to in the Wakening Land.
His men are attempting to expand Kael further into that accursed forest.
If you buy a pack of supplies and deliver it to him I am sure he would repay you handsomely.
A single pack will only cost you a mere five hundred pieces of gold.

Korzak Stonehammerは我々がWakening Landでいつも配達する男の名前です。
彼らはその忌まわしい森林の中で更にKaelの勢力を広げようと試みています。
もしあなたが１パックの補給物資を買って、彼にそれを配達するなら私は彼が手厚くあなたに報いるであろうと思います。
ひとつのパックがほんの500gpの負担ですが。

Having those helmets back would be very useful.
If you come across any of the workers helmets return them to me and I will bestow a reward totem upon you.
