>Hail, Captain L`Rei

Captain L`Rei narrows his eyes sharply to the Ogre standing before him, his lip giving a gentle curl of his unveiled irritation.

'By your abrupt and careless approach, I see that you have yet to become acquainted with the ways of the [Primordial Malice].
Such an offense shall be disregarded by an outsider only once, and be grateful that it was I and not [his grace] to whom you presented such audacity.

Captain L`Reiは、あなたが突然目の前に立った事が不快なようである。

「あなたが突然私に話し掛けるという事は、まだ［Primordial Malice］の法を知らないようだ。
このような行為は通常許されるものではないが、まぁ私の顔に免じて今回の件は［猶予］としておこう。


>What his grace?

'The Archon, Mardeus N'Vidi, is our sovereign leader.
He is the one who gathered us when Innoruuk condemned the Dark Lady.
However, you are an outsider to us and it would not be wise to approach him.
He is our loyalty, our faith, our distrust, and the voice of Malice.'

「The Archon, Mardeus N'Vidiは我々のリーダーである。
彼は Innoruukにより迫害された暗黒の女王の残党を集めた人である。
しかしながら、あなたは我々にとって部外者である。そして彼に近づく事は賢明ではない。
彼は我々の忠誠、我々の信頼、我々のMaliceの声である。」


>What is Primordial Malice?

Captain L`Rei releases the soft hint of irritation from his features and briefly waves an armored hand toward the camp.

'We are the Primordial Malice.
A group of loyal servants dedicated to the [restoration of our Dark Lady], Lanys T'Vyl, and the preservation of her irreplaceable glory through the burning spite that binds our loyalty.
Though we are few when compared to our enemy and this small temple is all we have retained after the Dark Lady's loss, our faith and the strength of our blades are not to be trifled with.'

Captain L`Reiは苛立ちを隠すかのように手を振り上げて説明する。

「我々はPrimordial Maliceである。
［我々の暗黒の女王の復活］に尽くす忠実なグループ、Lanys T'Vylとその栄光を守るものだ。
暗黒の女王亡き後、まだまだ我々の敵と比べて小さな寺院だが、その力を侮ってはいけない」


>What restoration of our Dark Lady?

Captain L`Rei smirks darkly from the corner of his lips and shakes his head gently.

'Such information is not privileged to outsiders.
However, you seem to retain some interest in us and given our numbers and the [Call of Malice] that guides us, we are always willing to extend our sermon to those who would heed the call.
Perhaps you would be of a mind to attempt to [prove yourself to our ranks] and in turn for your success, devotion, and sacrifices. . . the call may seek you.'

Captain L`Reiが薄気味悪い笑みを浮かべる。

「このような情報は秘密ではない。
しかし、あなたは我々に対して何らかの興味を持っているように思われる。
そして我々は［Maliceのお告げ］を聞こうとする者を尊重する。
多分あなたはそうしようと思っているのだろう。そうであるなら［我々の仲間になる証明］をするよう求めるかもしれない。」


>What Call of Malice?

Captain L`Rei closes his eyes and bows his head briefly as if in recognition of something sacred before quickly returning his eye to (players name).

'The Call is not for my voice to speak, nor is it for your ears to receive.
When and if the Call beckons, you shall know it as no other.' 

Captain L`Reiは目を閉じて、そして神聖な何かの祈りをする。

「お告げは私の声ではない、同様にそれは受け取るべきあなたの耳のためでもない。
いつか知る事となろう。」
