- Al'Kabor -
That wizardy sort , Al'Kabor , has made 'imself a powerful artifact scepter from the one that he had been diggin' up from ground in several places.
His research has somethin' to do with fusiblatily .. or somthin' like that.

Al'Kabor called forward an assistant to safeguard the scepter - most likely another blaster.
Hehe .. he runs through assistants quickly these days.
Iffin' I was a wizardy sort, I wouldnae be so quick to volunteer to help him.

He dinnae rightly be trustin' many folks though.
We did hear that he does have an assistant that he trusts the most though.
Makil Rargon is his name.

Thubr Axebringer assured us that he'd let the king known of anything further. 		　
- The Plague in Qeynos -

Head up by Velarte Seline , the priests in Qeynos have been busy tryin' to rid themselves of the plague in the Karanas.

They have been testin' all sorts of anti-disease curin' things on animals.
Some of der experiments have been halted because a thief stole some of the animals they were testin' on.

King Kazon announced that we might be sendin' a few of our own priest there to assist them with it.

 　
- Dark Elf Rituals -

Apparently, the Dark Elves have been performin' all kinds of rituals lately.
They killed the old child of Innoruuk and got themselves a new one.
I guess they just cannae be makin' der minds up about it.
