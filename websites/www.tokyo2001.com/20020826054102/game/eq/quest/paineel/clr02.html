>What is summoning of Dread?

There are some components essential to the summoning of the avatar of dread.
I require the eye of a griffon, some [powder of reanimation], the brain of the Ishva Mal, and the toes of an ice giant.
Fetch me these reagents so that we may summon the avatar to forge a shield worn only by the mightiest apostles of Cazic Thule.

我々がCazic Thuleの最強の使徒にのみ装備できるシールドを鍛えて造る為に化身を召集するように、私にこれらの試薬を取って来なさい。

>Where is powder of reanimation?

There is a gnome necromancer that has been experimenting with a powder used in reanimating long dead organic tissue.
He is known to frequent places populated by undead in order to pursue his research.
Seek him out and procure a bit of his powder.

有機的な組織の粉を使って実験していたネクロマンサーのGnomeがいる。
彼は自身の研究の為にアンデッドが住んでいる場所にしばしば行くことが知られている。
彼を探し、そして彼の一つまみの粉を入手しなさい。
