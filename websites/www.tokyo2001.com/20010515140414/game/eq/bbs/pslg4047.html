4047Shestar Scaled CoifZerosignal@karana 5/6-20:38


CabilisからField of BoneへのZone際(左方向への道)にいる「Trooper
Shestar」。

You say, 'hail Trooper Shestar'
Trooper Shestar says 'My father was a great blacksmith.
He taught me how to make great items such as the [Shestar Scaled
Coif].
Alas, smithing was not my rebirth, but rather the life of a warrior.

You say, 'what is Shestar Scaled Coif'
Trooper Shestar says 'The Shestar Scaled Coif was a light coif
my father would fashion out of Loose Scales from the Scaled Wolves.
I have some scales on me and if you bring me three loose scales
then I will have enough to create the coif for you,
but I require you to also pay a fee of five gold.

「Scaled Wolf 」は「a scaled wolf」からしか出ません。
「pup」や「cub」からは出ないので注意して下さい。
Drop率は高く、殺せばガンガン出るので問題ないでしょう。
これを３枚と5gpを渡します。

Trooper Shestar quickly inserts the scales into a half finished
coif and hands you the coif.
I am afraid that is not enough and I cannot possibly complete the
task while on duty.
You must go and find an additional three Scaled Wolf Scales and take
them,
along with the Unfinished Coif, to the nearest forge and complete the
smithing yourself.
I hope you are a blacksmith.

で貰えるのが・・・

Shestaer's Unfinished Coif
LORE ITEM NO DROP
WT : 0.6
Class : WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD DRU MNK BRD ROG SHM
Race : ALL

これと「Scaled Wolf Scales」を３枚使って、鍛冶Skillで焼けと。
この「Scaled Wolf Scales」というのは、
恐らく「a scaled wolf」又は「a scaled wolf cub」から出る、
「a wolf scale」(見た目緑の苔っぽい)の事だと思うのですが、
結構RareDropです。
仕方ないので他のPCが売った奴買おうかと店を覗くと・・・約7pp。(;_;)
た・・・高い・・・。
諦めて「a scaled wolf」狩ってたんですが、
２時間程やって結局２枚しか出なかったので一時中断。
