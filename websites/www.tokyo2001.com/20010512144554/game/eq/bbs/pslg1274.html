場所：Feerrott
対象：Lynuga (Troll)
所属：BrokenSkullClan (unknown)

Feerrottを巡回するTrollのNPCのQUESTです。
Jeweled Skullに必要なLarge Ruby目当てで、
Lynugaを殺すPCが後を絶たない為、
滅多に彼女を見かけることはありません。

このQUESTを達成した時のFactionは doubiosly でした。

○ You say, 'hail Lynuga'

● Lynuga says 'Hurump?! Who you? Sheryl? What you want with
Lyunga? Me am out [collecting]. Leave me be.

○ You say, 'what is collecting?'

● Lynuga says 'I collect all sorts. Gems be my favorite. Me really like
Rubies. If you have Ruby give to me. Why you want to know?
You want to steal my collection?!?! I squish you if you do!!

☆ Ruby を１個渡す。

Rubyは市販の物を購入して渡してください。

● Lynuga says 'Mmm. Ruby!! Me thanks you! Here take this, me got
it off dead someone who try take my collection. Me thinks this
valuable thing..

Your faction standing with BrokenSkullClan got better (unknown)
Your faction standing with ShadowknightsofNightKeep got worse (unknown)
Your faction standing with DaBashers got worse (unknown)

You gain experience!!

ランダムで以下の３つのうち１つが手に入ります。

Brutechopper
Midnight Mallet
Ivandyr's Hoop

Ivandyr' HoopのInt6,Wis6は魅力的ですね。
