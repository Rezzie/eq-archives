有名すぎていまさらですが、コンプ出来てLog取れたので。(w
ちびっこの街Rivervale。
ここには６時間近いPop間隔で「Nillipuss」というBrownieが出ます。
案山子のいる畑のReebo Leafswayさん。

You say, 'hail Reebo Leafsway'

Reebo Leafsway says 'Greetings and salutations, Zerosignal ! My name
is Reebo Leafsway, loyal Druid of [Karana]. I am in charge of
helping young druids who wish to get started down the [trail to
Karana's wisdom]. I also do my share of tending the [crops].

You say, 'who is Nillipuss'
Reebo Leafsway says 'Nillipuss? Oh that dirty brownie keeps stealing
our jumjum for his own greedy uses. We certainly would not mind if
someone taught him a painful [lesson].

You say, 'what is lesson'
Reebo Leafsway says 'He needs to be taught not to steal our jumjum!
If you find some jumjum on him, I would appreciate it if you would
return it to me.

Nillipussの盗んだjumjumを返してくれと。
ちなみに「幾つ」とは言ってませんが４つ返さないと駄目そうです。
又、Nillipussは必ずjumjumを持ってるとは限りません。
取りあえず何とかして４つGet。スタックしないように返しましょう。

Reebo Leafsway says 'Excellent!! You must have taught ol' Nillipuss
a great deal! But he never seems to learn.. Oh well. The
Stormreapers and all of Rivervale owe you a debt of gratitude.
Please accept this token of our appreciation.

Your faction standing with StormReapers got better
Your faction standing with MayorGubbin got better
Your faction standing with MerchantsofRiverVale got better
Your faction standing with UnkemptDruids got worse
You gain experience!!
You receive 4 silver from Reebo Leafsway.

特にEffectとかも無いんですよねー。
一時期、Jbootsみたいな効果があるんではないか？と噂でした。
