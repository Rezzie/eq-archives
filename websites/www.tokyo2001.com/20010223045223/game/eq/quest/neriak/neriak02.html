Neriak Forign Quater
対象：X'Ta Tompi (Dark Elf)
所属：TheDead (SHD,NEC Guard)

Neriakの第一ゾーンのゲートの上に３人のDark Elf（X'ta Tempi，X'ta Timpi，X'ta Tompi）がいます。
この３人に指定された材料を渡すことで、NECのペットのResarchに必要なWordsを手に入れることができます。

このQUESTを達成した時のFactionは amiably でした。

導入部は３人共通なのでまず誰でもよいので話を聞いてみましょう。

　

>hail X'Ta Tompi.

We three are the [Sisters Dark] - [Necromancy] is our Art - Bonded dead, they serve our will - No beat of heart, yet faithful still.

我々3人は[Sisters Dark] である。ネクロマンサーは死体を操る術を持ち、従事させる。

>What is sisters dark?

We three are the Sisters Dark - Keepers of the shadowed Dread - [Lore of Death], we resarch now - Join with us and serve [The Dead].

我々３姉妹はKeepers of the shadowed Dread、つまり[死の伝承]である。我々はリサーチの研究と[The Dead]に仕える。

>What is Lore of Death?

The Lore of Death is shadow bound - its [words] are [hid], yet shall be found - Through our resarch into the dark - Old hexs found and parchment marked.



>Where is words?

The Words are hid in tomes of old - Their yielded lore worth more than gold - This we resarch with devout minds - But for [components] we can not find.

>What is components?

We need components for resarch - These are tings that you could find - In return for [tasks] that you perform - Words of [reward] we have in mind.

>What is tasks?

Bring us components for our research - We give [Word]s by tasks performed - [Possesion],[Detachment],[Allure],[Haunting],[Rupturing],[Dark Paths],[Suffering],[Collection],[Obligation],[Requisition] and [Acquisition].

　

という事で、作成してくれるリサーチ用Wordの一覧を話してくれます。

ここで各姉妹に、例えば

>What is word of possession?

と聞けば、材料を教えてくれますので指定された材料を集めて渡せばOKのようです。
