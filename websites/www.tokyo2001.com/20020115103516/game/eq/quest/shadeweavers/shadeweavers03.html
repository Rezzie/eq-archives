　Shadeweaver's Thicket のファイアーダンサーのテントに居る"Hymnist Omiyad"に話します。

　
　

>Hail, Hymnist Omiyad

Greetings young one, have a seat by the fire.
You will notice the dance being preformed.
This is much more than just a dance though.
It is a ritual that we preform to [purge the evil spirits] that are plaguing the thicket.

>What purge the evil spirits?

Lately there has been a surge in the number of Lodi Kai threatening our trade routes.
This ritual of is preformed around a fire that burns from their [remains].
Setting their remains in the fire will free their tie with this world and shall ward off their numbers.

>What remains?

Hymnist Omiyad glances toward the fire.

'I see that the fire is dying down some.
Should you come across the remains of the Lodi Kai bring them to me to keep the ritual fire burning.
Bring plenty though, four if you can.'

　

 

薪にするために

・Remains X4
（Loda Kai BrigandsやSkeletel Brigandsなど）

を渡します。

　
　

Hymnist Omiyad takes the remains and throws them into the fire, flames blaze immediately with a sickly hue.

'You have performed a great service by helping destroy some of the wretched Lodi Kai.
Take this small talisman, it is worn by the dancers here.
Take it with you and feel free to do a dance whenever you like, haha!'
