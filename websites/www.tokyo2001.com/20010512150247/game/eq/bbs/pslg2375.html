海外サイトのAllakhazamで見ましたがNeriakのWarギルドのクエストですね。
（以下抜粋）

Fire Goblin Runner: Talk to Narex T'vem in the Warrior's Guild in
Neriak. He will ask you to go to the Lavastorm Mountains and look for
a Fire Goblin Runner who is supplying an Erudite. The runner often
zips right through the area by the zone entrance, and is a fairly low
level. If you kill the Fire Goblin Runner and return his Runner
Pouch you will receive some experience, money and sometimes a
Featherweight Pouch . He doesn't give the quest to higher levels,
but still gives the reward for the pouch. (Submitted by Thrymm)
