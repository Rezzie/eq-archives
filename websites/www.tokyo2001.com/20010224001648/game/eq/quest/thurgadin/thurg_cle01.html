>What is bracer?

>What is gauntlets?

>What is Greaves?

Three chipped onyx sapphires will allow me to channel the correct energies into a pair of corroded plate greaves however those chipped onyx sapphires are somewhat rare.
Return to me if you find such things in your jouneys.
