>What serious mission?

You see, this windmill is more than it seems.
It's quite a splendid device!

What a wonderful place that is!
A virtual dream-land for any gnome!

いいか、この風車は見た目よりも凄いんだ。
なかなかの素晴らしい装置なのだよ！

一言で言えば、［Plane of Innovation］へのポータルを作るのだ。

>What Plane of Innovation?

Oh, it is such an amazing place!
I ultimately desire to travel there to learn of advanced mechanics.
Alas, this windmill requires constant upkeep due the constant surge of power that it propels.
The gears are constantly getting sandy and the axles lose their grease almost daily.
I'm often in need of [new parts] and that's where Drogguk's assistance would currently be handy, but I simply cannot sacrifice his current duties at this time.
Pity, pity.

おお、それは驚くべき場所だ！
私は革新的な装置を知りたいが為にその地へ行きたい。
ああ、この風車はそのために一定量供給する為のエネルギーを必要とする。
ギアはいつも黄土色になっている。そして車軸はほとんど毎日油切れだ。
私はいつも［新しい部品］を必要としている。そしてそれはDroggukの助けが必要だが、その任務で彼を犠牲にすることができない。
残念残念。

>What new parts?

Well, Drogguk used to travel through the portal to acquire items for me from the salvage yard.
The last time he went through in search of parts he came back [badly wounded] and that's saying quite a lot!
I was quite alarmed, but the elders nursed him to health with amazing haste.
I simply cannot imagine placing him in such jeopardy again.
He was lucky to return at all by the look of him then!'

Drogguk私の為にポータルを通じて材料を集めに行ってくれていた。

私は非常に脅えていた、しかし長老は素早く彼を看病した。
私は再び彼をあのような危険に置くことをしたくない。
彼はあの時を見るに、戻ってこられるとは運が良かったとしか言いようが無い！

>How badly wounded

Aye, you can still see some of the scars about his body.
He stumbled out of the portal that day, bloody and spouting on about shiny men trying to tear him apart.
He said they were speaking of research the whole time.
Frightful stuff, really.
Well, you look adventurous enough.
Why don't you go collect some supplies for me?
I fear that Drogguk would not be able to manage another trip to collect the [supplies] I require to maintain this place.

あぁ、あなたは彼の体にある傷跡のいくつかをまだ見ることができます。

>What supplies?

Size 12 sprockets are always greatly needed.
Any Silicorrosive Grease you find would be a huge help as well.
You can never have too much Silicorrosive grease laying around.
Also, tri-platinum foil and mini-gyro shields would be good for something or another.
Micro-ceramic sleeves are a nice find as well.
Got all that?
Now, reach around up there in the area of the transcentrifugal axle that isn't working.
You'll either be badly wounded or transported to the Plane of Innovation.
Take care!
