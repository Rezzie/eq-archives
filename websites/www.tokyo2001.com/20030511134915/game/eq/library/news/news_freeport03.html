Qeynos vs the Bloodsabers

Across Antonica, the plague in the Karanas is known to all.
Well it appears that our sister city to the west has pointed an official finger.
Qeynos has declared war on the Bloodsabers for their suspected involvement in the plague, but they need help.
Well isn't it about time?

The Bloodsabers have long been a scourge upon the land and the powers that be in Qeynos have been taken to task more that once for letting them run rampant in the west.
Finally we learn the truth behind the inaction-they simply could not handle the problem.
Now that their ineffectual policing has resulted in the start of famine-like conditions affecting all of Norrath,they seem at last spurred into action.
With the help of hired "investigators," they are finally beginning to make their way slowly up the ladder of that secret society.
It is this kind of thing that truly makes one appreciate living in Freeport under the watchful eye of the militia.
I for one sleep easier at night knowing that such corruption and apathy could never threaten my home or my way of life.

 　
Al'Kabor - What trouble is He Causing Now?

Reports are coming in from all around Norrath that famed wizard Al'Kabor has a new project up his mystical sleeve.
The question is, what is the fallout going to be this time?
As always with the surly spellcaster,the problem is his unwillingness to share information about what he is doing.
Last seen chipping away at a Wizard spire in the Dreadlands, we can only assume?
does he intend to destroy the spires and use their remains so that only he can teleport around Norrath?
Is he setting a trap for one of his many unsuspecting enemies?
Your guess is as good as mine,but rumors say that a former gnomish apprentice of his has acquired some of his work.
Keep your eyes here for more details as they surface.
