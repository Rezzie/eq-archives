>Hail, Salmekia Treherth

Welcome to Surefall Glade Xxxxx, the home of the Jaggedpine Treefolk.
I help teach young druids the ways of our people.
We have worshipers of both Karana, the Storm Lord, and Tunare, the All Mother, living here in the glade.
If you are a new druid I will help you obtain a [suit of clothing] that will offer comfort and protection while working in the wilds and help protect you from the weapons of the Gnolls that wish to take these lands.

ようこそシュアフォール・グレードへ、Ryuzi。

もし貴方が新しいドルイドであるなら、快適さと保護を得られる [ 衣服 ] を作ることを手伝いましょう。

>What suit of clothing?
>衣服

You must use this specially prepared Curing Kit to craft the clothing.
Each article of clothing requires different materials for its construction.
Do you plan on crafting Pine Druid [Gloves], Pine Druid [Boots], a Pine Druid [Bracer], a Pine Druid [Cap], Pine Druid [Leggings], Pine Druid [Sleeves], or a Pine Druid [Tunic]?
When you have been outfitted and are ready I will tell you of a [task] that you can assist with.

特別に用意された Curing Kit を使わねばなりません。
服を作るには、それぞれ異なった材料が必要になります。
貴方はどの服を作ろうと計画しますか？　計画としては、[ グローブ ]、[ ブーツ ]、[ ブレイサー ]、[ キャップ ]、[ レギング ]、[ スリーブ ]、[ チュニック ] があります。
すべての服が揃ったら、お願いしたい [ 仕事 ] もあります。

・Curing Kit
(8Slot)

を受け取ります。

>What Gloves?
>グローブ

To craft Pine Druid Gloves you require two [silk thread], ruined gnoll leather gloves, two giant field rat whiskers, and a large king snake skin.
Once you have the necessary components combine them in your Curing Kit with this Tattered Glove Pattern.

グローブを作るには、まず [ Silk Thread ] ２本、Ruined Gnoll Leather Gloves、Giant Field Rat Whisker ２本と Large King Snake Skin を集めてください。
それらのアイテムとこの Tattered Glove Pattern を Curing Kit に入れ、組み合わせれば Pine Druid Gloves の完成です。

>What Boots?
>ブーツ

To craft Pine Druid Boots you require two [silk thread], ruined gnoll leather boots, two giant field rat whiskers, and a large whiskered bat fur.
