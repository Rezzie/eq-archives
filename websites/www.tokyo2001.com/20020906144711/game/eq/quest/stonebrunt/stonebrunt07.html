>What atrocities

Long ago when our noble ancestors the Vah Shir were destroyed by an explosion that devastated the warring Heretic and Erudin armies an evil creature by the name of Tserrina Syl'Tor was attracted to the sudden abundance of new spirits in these lands.
Tserrina was capable of capturing these spirits before they were able to cross over beyond the mortal realm and through her dark magic transformed them into a [crystalline shadow] substance.

大昔、我々の貴族の先祖Vah Shirが、異端者とErudinの内戦のあおりで破壊された時、Tserrina Syl'Torという名の悪い人物が、この地で増えた魂に惹きつけられてやって来た。
Tserrinaは暗黒魔法を通して［crystalline shadow］に実体化させ、これらの魂を得ることができた。

>What Crystalline shadow?

The crystalline shadow substance was used as building blocks for Tserrina's tower on an Island near the frozen lands to the south as well as fashioned into armor and weapons for her mindless servants.
Emylie Steelclaws has developed a means to purify the armor and weapons using the special properties of our Kejek forge and a smithy hammer blessed by the Titan Spirits.
The purifying process transfers the imprisoned spirits into [soul orbs].
Ask Emylie about the purification process. 

Crystalline Shadowは氷の地の南にあるTserrina's towerの資材に使われるのと同様に彼女の非情な使用人の防具や武器として用いられた。
Emylie Steelclaws は特別な特性をもつ我々のKejek 鍛冶とTitan Spiritsの神聖なるハンマーを使ってその防具や武器を浄化する事ができた。
浄化するプロセスは［Soul Orbs］の中に閉じ込められた魂を移す。
浄化についてはEmylieに聞きなさい。

>What Soul orbs?

I have perfected a ritual that will free the imprisoned spirits within the soul orbs.
I simply require some kejek [spirit powder] to scrawl the proper sigils and a soul orb upon which to perform the ceremony.
My apprentice will perform the ceremony on fading soul orbs.
Simply bring him a fading soul orb and some kejek spirit powder. 

私はSoul Orbの中に閉じ込めた魂を自由にする儀式を完成した。
ただ若干のKejek［Spirit Powder］が必要だ。
SoulOrbの儀式については私の実習生が行うであろう。
ただ彼にSoul Orbと若干のkejek Sprit Powderを持って来なさい。

>What Spirit powder?

The spirit power of which I speak is composed of ground spiritling stones.
I will grind some powder for you if you would bring me a Valley Spiritling Stone, a Jungle Spiritling Stone, and a Mountain Spiritling Stone.
Collecting the stones does not truly harm the spiritlings for when slain in this realm their essence simply disperses until it eventually reforms into a new spiritling.

Spirit powderは私が以下に話す地上にあるSpiritling Stoneで構成されている。
もしあなたが私にValley Spiritling Stone、Jungle Spiritling StoneとMountain Spiritling stoneを持って来てくれるなら、私はあなたのために石をひいて粉にするであろう。
石を集めることは、魂を傷つけません。この地で殺された者は新しく生まれ変わるまでその本質は四散するのです。
