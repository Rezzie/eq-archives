>I am a druid of tunare

When you are [ready to begin your training] I will present you with your Leafrunners Assembly Kit.

>I am ready to begin my training

Here is your Leafrunners Assembly Kit Xxxxx.
In this kit you will place a number of [magical components] that will be infused together to create armor materials used to crafting Leafrunners Leather Armor.
Once you have created the armor materials you will then take them to a loom along with the correct pattern to fashion together your armor piece.

ここにあなたのLeafrunners Assembly Kitがある。
このキットであなたは多くの[魔法の材料]を入れてCombineすれば、Leafrunners Leather Armorを作るための元ができるだろう。
そして正しいPatternと一緒にLoom（織機）で織れば防具ができるでしょう。

・Leafrunners Assembly Kit

を受け取ります。

>What magical components

The components you will need to collect for your armor will take you to all parts of the faydark.
These items can be found on some of the deadliest creatures and also on some of our most trust worthy merchants.
Once you are ready to begin creating your armor pieces simply tell me what piece you [want] to craft.
I can provide you with the material recipes and patterns for Leafrunners [Caps], [Bracers], [Sleeves], [Boots], [Leggings], [Gloves] and [Tunics].

あなたが防具を作るために集める必要がある材料はFaydarkのあらゆる所にいく必要がある。
これらの材料は危険な生物が持っていたり、逆に商人が売っていたりする。
準備が出来たらその必要な物を言おう。
種類はLeafrunners [Caps], [Bracers], [Sleeves], [Boots], [Leggings], [Gloves], [Tunics]のレシピとPatternがあります。
作りたい部分を聞いてみます。

>I want to craft gloves

To create your gloves material you will need to combine 3 Woven Spider Silks, 1 Orc Tibia, 1 Orc Pelvis Bone, and 1 Crushbone Belt in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a loom along with this mold to fashion your very own Gloves of the Leafrunner.

あなたがGloveを作るためには、Assembly Kitで３つのWoven Spider Silks、Orc Tibia、Orc Pelvis Bone、Crushbone Beltを詰めなさい。
そしてできた物をこのMoldと一緒に織機ので織りなさい。
