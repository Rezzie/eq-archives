>What is giants?

There are several encampments in the eastern wastes that barter for supplies with us monthly.
The great divide also has a small outpost of Kromrif who strive to beat the Coldain back into their city of Thurgadin and of course the outposts in the wakening lands.
Our last batch of deliveries did not reach their intended targets.
The runners have not been heard from since they were dispatched either.

そして勿論Wakening Landsにもあります。
最後に出た我々の補給軍はそれらの地まで到達できませんでした。
彼らからの連絡がありません。


>What is eastern wastes?

The Kromrif of the Eastern wastes are more savage then those who dwell within Kael Drakkel.
Normally they can fend for themselves but recently Fjloaren of the Icebane clan has requested we help them with a shipment of supplies.
We have yet to receive payment for the supplies but you are more then welcome to purchase supplies for them and deliver them.
It has been a most unforgiving season upon the wastes I believe.

Eastern wastesのKromrifはKael Drakkelに住む人達を批判しています。
大抵彼らは自足自給でやりくりすることができます。しかし最近Icebane 一族の Fjloaren は我々が補給物資の出荷に関して彼らの手助けをすることを要請しました。
我々はまだ補給物資のための代金を受け取っていません。
しかしあなたは彼らのために補給物資を購入し、そして彼らに配達することはできます。
