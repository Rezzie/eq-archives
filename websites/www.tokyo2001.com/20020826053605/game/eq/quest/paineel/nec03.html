Greetings young one.
There are many tasks to be performed along side your studies to truly harness the powers passed down to us by our ancestor [Myragul].

>Who is Myragul?

You do not know of Myragul?!!
You have more to learn of the heritage of the Dark Truth than at first I thought.
Myragul was the first High Man to unlock the secrets of necromancy and is the founder of our city as well as the creator of the treacherous Hole.

あなたはMyragulについて知らないのか？ !!
あなたはDark Truthの遺産を知るべきだと私は考える。
MyragulはNecromancyの秘密を暴いた最初のHighManであり、そしてこの街を作り、裏切りの穴を生み出した者である。
