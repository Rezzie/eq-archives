EQ2001 >> Quest >> Qeynos >> Lightbringers Platemail Armor

　

 

NorthQeynosのTemple（通称UFO寺院）の入り口付近にNPCがいます。

>Hail, Davloran Girionlis

Good day Xxxxx, I am Davloran Girionlis Holy Knight in service to the Prime Healer Rodcet Nife.
I pride myself on being responsible for ridding my homeland of unimaginable evil during my fighting days.
Nowadays I just like to train new Temple of Life paladin [recruits].

>What recruits?

We have a number of new recruits entering our guild hall every single day.
For this reason I have developed a set of exercises that test the hunting and gathering skils of all young paladins.
If you are a [Paladin of Rodcet] I might be able to present these tests to you as well.

>I am paladin of rodcet

Fantastic Xxxxx, let me explain to you how the exercises work.
You will combine a number of magical items that you will gather from our city and in the training areas and combine them in a Lightbringers Assembly Kit.
Certain items used in certain quantities will combine into a specific armor material.
These materials can then be taken with the appropriate mold to a forge and be combined together to create a piece of Lightbringers Armor.
I can present you with the material recipes along with the correct armor molds for each armor piece once you are [ready to begin your exercises].

>I am ready to begin my exercises

Very well Xxxxx, please take this Lightbringers Assembly Kit.
When you are ready to gather the items for a specific armor piece you must only tell me what piece you [want to craft].
I can provide you with the recipes and armor molds for Lightbringers Platemail [Helms], [Bracers], [Armguards], [Boots], [Greaves], [Gauntlets] and [Breastplates].

よろしい。ではこの Lightbringers Assembly Kitを受け取りなさい。
あなたがどの部分を[作りたい]のか言ってみなさい。
そうすれば私はレシピをあなたに提供することができる。[Helms], [Bracers], [Armguards], [Boots], [Greaves], [Gauntlets] [Breastplates]の部分の。

・Lightbringers Assembly Kit

を受け取ります。

作りたい部分を聞いてみます。
>I want to craft Helms

To create your helm material you will need to combine 2 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Whiskered Bat Backbone, 1 Chunk of Meat and 1 Ale in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Helm of the Lightbringer.

作るためには、Bricks of Crude Ironを２つ、Whiskered Bat Backboneを１つ、Chunk of Meatを１つ、Aleを１つAssembly Kitに入れてCombineしなさい。
そしてできた物を、このMoldと一緒にForgeに入れて鍛冶をしなさい。

>I want to craft Bracers

To create your bracer material you will need to combine 1 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Skeleton Tibia, 1 Gnoll Pup Scalp and 1 Ale in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Bracer of the Lightbringer.

>I want to craft Armguards

To create your armguards material you will need to combine 2 Bricks of Crude Iron, 2 Fire Beetle Carapaces, 1 Gnoll Jawbone and 1 Raw Bamboo in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Armguards of the Lightbringer.

>I want to craft Boots

To create your boot material you will need to combine 3 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Wisp Essence, 2 Rat Eyes in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Boot of the Lightbringer.

>I want to craft Greaves

To create your greaves material you will need to combine 4 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Lion Tail, 1 Ruined Cat Pelt, 1 Bottle and 1 Lightstone in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Greaves of the Lightbringer.

>I want to craft Gauntlets

To create your gauntlet material you will need to combine 3 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Black Wolf Tooth, 1 Mist Wolf Pelt, and 2 Spider Legs in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Gauntlets of the Lightbringer.

>I want to craft Breastplates

To create your breastplate material you will need to combine 5 Bricks of Crude Iron, 1 Rat Eyes, 1 Fire Beetle Eye, 1 Golden Bandit Tooth, 1 Medium Quality Bear Skin and 1 Severed Gnoll Foot in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Breastplate of the Lightbringer.
Please come back to see me after you have completed your breastplate.
I have a [job] that I can only entrust to one of my finest knights in training.

>What job?

I have been working for quite some time at my blacksmithing skills in my spare time.
My initial interest was to make armor for our new recruits.
However, after I discovered these armor recipes there has been more of a need for weaponry rather then armor.
I have began to construct weapons suitable for all those that pass the training exercises but I have run short on supplies. I need someone to [gather the necessary supplies] for me.

私は空いた時間を使って鍛冶に取り組んできた。
最初は我々の新しい新兵のために、防具を作る事だった。
しかし私がこの防具のレシピを見つけた後、新兵の為の武器の必要性も痛感した。
私はそうした者の為に武器も作り始めた。しかし材料の供給がおぼつかない。
私の為に［必要な材料を集め］てください。

>Gather the necessary supplies

That's great news Xxxxx, I applaud your willingness to help me gather these items.
I would go get them myself but I must always be available here should any of the other newcomers need my help.
Please collect 2 Flawless Gnoll Hides and 2 Wisp Essences for me and I will do my best to fashion you a weapon together for your trouble.

それは素晴らしい。材料を集めようというあなたの行動に感謝する。
私が集めに行ってもいいが、他の新兵の面倒を見なくてはならないので時間が取れないのだよ。
では、Flawless Gnoll Hidesを２つ、Wisp Essencesを２つ集めてください。
そうすれば私はあなたのために武器をこしらえましょう。

を受け取ります。
