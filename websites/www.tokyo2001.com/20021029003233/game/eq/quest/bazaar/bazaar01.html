Bazaarの場合は、商人たちが集まるところに居るGnomeです。

>Hail Gliblixl Rocktok

Hail xxxx! sell the finest quality ore imported from the mines of Norrath!
It's difficult to keep enough ore available with the demands of the local smiths however and could use some [assistance] in restocking my supplies.

やぁ!Norrathの鉱山から輸入された最高級の鉱石があるよ！
でも現地の鍛冶屋の要求で十分な鉱石を入手する事が難しいので、いくらか［援助］が欲しいのだが。

>What is assistance?

Hooray! Fill this crate with either four Small Brick of Unrefined Ore or four Large Brick of Unrefined Ore from Norrath and I will pay you for your assitance in keeping me in business.

本当かい！この箱の中にSmall Brick of Unrefined Ore４つか、Large Brick of Unrefined Ore４つをNorrathから入手して持ってきてもらえるなら報酬をあげましょう。
