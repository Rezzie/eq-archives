ROG専用防具

　

　街の北側の鉱山脇の部屋にて。（ドワーフの鉱夫はROG系。Kaladimの状況より）

　
　

>hail Foreman Felspar.

Welcome to the Thurgadin Minin' Company, Erudite.
As foreman it's my job to see that me lads are well trained and well cared fer.
Unfortunately we're not in too good shape right now.
Ye see, it seems a new vein was struck recently and a bunch of unwelcome critters have come crawlin outta the works attackin' me lads.
In order ta help combat this menace I've engineered a new set of armor for the miners, but need some raw materials to get them started. 

Thurgadin 鉱業の会社にようこそ。
私は若者を訓練し指揮する職長である。
不幸にも我々はあまり良い状態でありません。
新しい鉱脈が最近掘り当てられたのだが、そして歓迎されない生物が我々の仕事の邪魔をしているのです。
この厄介な者と戦う為に私は新しい防具を設計しました。しかし作るのには幾つかの材料が必要です。

>What is armor?

Excellent, Ryuzi ! I knew ye were the right Erudite for the job.
The pieces I can make are a coif, a tunic, sleeves, bracers, gauntlets, leggings, and boots.
This stuff might not be fitting ya if ye're not stealthy like us miners though. 


よろしい！
私はあなたたちがこの仕事をやる事を知っていました。
私が作ることができるのはcoif、tunic、sleeves、bracers、gauntlets、leggingsとbootsです。
もしあなたたちが我々鉱夫のように隠密行動をしないのなら、この物はあなたにはあわないかもしれません。

>What is coif?

To create a piece of armor to protect your sensitive skull, I will require three pieces of crushed coral as well as a corroded chain coif. 

>What is tunic?

To forge a spectacular chain tunic I will require three flawless diamonds and a corroded chain tunic.
With the gems and my skills I may be able to draw out some of its magical powers. 

>What is sleeves?

Protection for your arms will come at the price of three flawed emeralds and a set of corroded chain sleeves.

>What is bracer?

For the bracers, I will require a corroded chain bracer as well as three crushed flame emeralds.
Return to me when you find such things Erudite.

>What is gauntlets?

Protecting your hands is very important. I can forge protection for your hands if you bring me a pair of corroded chain gauntlets and three crushed topaz. 

>What is leggings?

A set of corroded chain leggings might be salvageable if you were to find three flawed sea sapphires.
With the right techniques and ingredients almost anything is possible.

>What is boots?

We use our feet so often, should not they be protected as well as any other portion of our bodies? If you seek fine protection for your feet, return to me with three pieces of crushed black marble and a set of corroded chain boots.
