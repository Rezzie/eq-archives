Taintin' of Faydark
Recently, the lovely lass Firiona Vie summoned the avatar of Tunare.
As the two lovely lasses spoke, an evil being approached the Faydark Forest.
The beastly God Cazic-Thule appeared and confronted the Mother of All.

Within moments, he banished her from Norrath and corrupted the forest itself.
This dreadful evilness can still be found creepin' about. 		　
Al'Kabor's Rage

You'd be best not to disappoint or betray the wizard Al'Kabor.
he has a mean way of takin' out revenge against those that he don't rightly like.

He recently stripped one of his assistants of all magical powers.
I'd be willin' to bet that his former assistant will be needin' lots of ale to recover from that scary experience.
