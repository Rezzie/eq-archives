Qeynos Begins Investigations 　

Qeynos has officially begun formal investigations into the group that calls themselves the Bloodsabres.
These worshippers of Bertoxxulous are the group responsible for the tragic destruction and devastation perpetrated against creatures great and small that dwell on the once beautiful and bountiful Plains of Karana.

Antonius and the council have given the task of conducting these initial investigations to his advisor and right hand man, Lord Magistrate Vegalys Keldrane.
Expecting a large number of arrests in the upcoming weeks, Vegalys Keldrane has asked that any loyal to Qeynos to contact him personally to apply for the position of official investigator.

We offer our prayers to Karana that the good followers of Rodcet Nife will bring healing to the broken and savaged lands of the once pristine plains.
At the least, perhaps we may begin to feel some closure.
