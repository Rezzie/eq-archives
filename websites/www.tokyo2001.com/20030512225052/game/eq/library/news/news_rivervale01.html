Blight Upon the Plains 　

The plains of Karana still buckle under the terrible plague that is effecting the region.
A terrible green mist, swarms of insects and wide spread disease are wreaking havoc on the area.

Farmers report withered crops that are nearly unfit for consumption.
We are thankful that the terrors unleashed upon the plains of Karana have left us, for the most part, unaffected.
Druids are asked to be spread the word to travelers heading for the Plains as many of the animals have become infected with rabies, from the mild and majestic wolf to the fearsome and respectable griffon.
Priests of all kinds are asked to lend their efforts and cure those that become infected to keep the disease from spreading.

 　
Lanys T`Tvyl Searchs for Artifact 　

Lanys T`Vyl, foul Dark Elf and former chosen of Innoruuk, maintains a small but loyal following in the Teir'Dal community.
It has been reported that she has been seeking to gather the pieces of an artifact, once considered cursed by the Teir'Dal.

It is uncertain what she hopes to gain by piecing together this cursed device.
We at the Voice believe that this bodes ill not only for me citizens of Rivervale but perhaps for the Dark Elven people as well.

 　
High Elven Princess Free 　

Firiona Vie, High Elven princess and daughter of King Tearis Thex of the elves, was returned safely after an extended period of captivity at the hands of the Iksar Danak Dhorentath and his minions.
Danak is said to be the leader of a cult and is said to have ties with the powerful hell spawned creature known as Danak Dhorentath.

We join people of Faydwer in celebrating this happy occasion.
