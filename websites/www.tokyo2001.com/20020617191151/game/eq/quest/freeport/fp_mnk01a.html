I am very pleased to here about your interest in proving your worth to the Ashen Order.
First let me present you with this Fistfighters Loom.
Inside of it you will combine [various components] to form armor materials.
Once you have a material for a certain piece of armor you will combine it in a Loom along with the appropriate pattern.
When you inform me what piece of armor you [want] to craft, I will be able to present you with the component recipe and pattern needed for crafting it.

を受け取ります。作りたい部位を聞いてみます。

>What various components?

The items you will need to collect are all over the Freeport area, some in stores some off vicious monsters.
If you are up for the challenge though I will be able to provide you with the armor recipes and patterns for Stonehand [Caps], [Bracers], [Sleeves], [Sandals], [Trousers], [Gloves] and [Robes].
All you will need to do is tell me what piece of armor you [want] to craft, and I will be able to present you with the component recipe and pattern needed for crafting it.

>I want to craft a cap

A cap will almost certainly be your most important piece of armor you will craft.
To create your cap material you will need to combine 2 Matted Lion Pelts, 1 Malachite, 1 Snake Scale and a Cloth Cap in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a Loom along with this mold to fashion your very own Cap of the Stonehand.

・Cloth Cap
