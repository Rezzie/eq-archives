>Hail Tatalros the Cruel.

Tatalros the Cruel releases a low, guttural hiss, an unearthly power and fearsome voice ringing fear that echoes off of the very walls of the city.

''Greetings Erudite. It has been some time since I have had the pleasure of seeing one of living flesh not cower and bleed from their very eyes at the mere sight of my presence.
I shall not take it as an insult this time, as all things change and adapt.
I assume that merely my name, which alone once caused fear and strife within the soul of any who would lay ear to it, has fallen from the lips of history's tales.
No matter, in time that shall be changed.''


Tatalros the Cruelは低く唸る様な声を街中に響くような大声ではりあげます。

「いらっしゃい。私を見て震え上がらない生きた物が居るのは久しぶりです。
私はそれを侮辱とは受け止めないでしょう。
私はただ耳を傾けるでしょう。歴史の物語の唇から落ちた（どれ・何・誰）の精神の中ででも単独でかつて恐怖と争いを起こします。
変えられるべきである時間での、問題はなし。」
