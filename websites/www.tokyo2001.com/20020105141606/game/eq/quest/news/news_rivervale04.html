What is a halfling that only wants peace and tranquility to do in such proximity to so ugly nasty dirty a race as them?

A Cros to Bear 　

You all remember Holly Windstalker? She runs around near Qeynos getting mad at folks that fight with the bears and the wolves and things there.
Well , if I were a poacher ( not that I am) I would be extra careful for a while because she is not getting any happier soon.
Poor Holly lost a dear dear friend long ago. 
His name was Cros, Cros Treewind.

He was a very good man that helped Holly in her goals of protecting the wildlife.
Anyway , he died a while ago.
Caught some bad cold from the water or something over in those parts , but now the city thinks they know who did it.
Poisoned the water that is.

The folks there seem to be doing what they can to find the peoples responsible, but no luck yet.
With this tragedy being in the news again, residents say Holly has been in a terrible mood lately.
Anyway I think we should all pause a moment before supping tonight and remember Cros.
