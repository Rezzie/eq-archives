>Hail, Rallic Aristos

Well met Xxxxx ! I am Rallic Aristos, Governor of trade and economy here in Katta Castellum.
I know it sounds like a lot of parchment pushing and coin counting but maintaining good trade relations with the various Houses of Shadow Haven and the haggling Vah Shir of Shar Vahl is not as easy at it may sound.
Then there is the issue of the [acrylia mines] in the Tenebrous Mountains.

>What acrylia mines?

The Tenebrous Mountains are rich with veins of the valuable acrylia ore.
Unfortunately the mines were dug and controlled by a tribe of savage grimlings from the forests to the south.
Negotiations have been attempted with the grimlings but to no avail.
Several of the other governors and I have been developing a [plan] to take the mines or establish mines of our own.
In either case the mines will have to be defended from attacks by the grimlings unless when placed in that predicament they finally resort to trade negotiations.

>What plan?

The full plans are not at my disposal but one of my concerns that may be instrumental in our success is the acquisition of grimling documents carried by a grimling courier who is seen frequently running from the forest to the mines.
If one of the Validus Custodus attempts to obtain the runners pouch the grimlings will surely retaliate, if adventurers like yourselves obtained it however and quietly delivered it to me we may be able to move forward with our plans.

>What required materials?

In addition to the pattern you will need to acquire a Perfect Owlbear Pelt, Shade Silk Thread, and a Shade Silk Cord.
You may use any loom or large tailoring kit to craft the pack.

どうやら、Perfect Owlbear Pelt、Shade Silk Thread、Shade Silk Cordを集め、Patternと合わせてCombineするとバッグが作れるようです。（要Tailoring）

・Perfect Owlbear Pelt
(Hollowshade Moor等のOwlbearからのRareDrop）
・Shade Silk Thread
（Shadeling Silk２個をSewing kitでCombine / Trival 15）
・Shade Silk Cord
（Shade Silk Thread３個をSewing kitでCombine / Trival 26）

Shadeling Silkは、Shadoweaver's Thicketの Zakra Silkworm や、Lesser Shade から入手できます。

・Rallic Pack Pattern

・Shade Silk Thread
・Shade Silk Cord
