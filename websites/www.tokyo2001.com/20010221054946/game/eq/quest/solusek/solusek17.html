>What is steal your memories?

It is the nature of the Crystal of Kintaz to steal the memories of those around it ・only I am safe, and then only because of the strong wardings placed on me by Solusek Ro himself.
Are you interested in [losing] any [memories]?

ただ私だけが安全である。

>what is losing any memories?

I have recently been experimenting with the Crystal, and have found that those exposed to ruby filtered light from it tend to lose the memories of their most specialized arcane skills.
Are you [interested] in [losing] your advanced [memories] of specialization?

私は最近クリスタルを使って実験していて、ルビー色のフィルターされた光にさらされた人たちが彼らの最も不可解で専門的なSkillの記憶を失わせる傾向がある事に気づいた。
