The Lifeguide in Velious 　

Our Firiona is one step closer to retrieving among the greatest of Tunare's gifts- The Lifeguide.
The vile creatures that absconded with it oh so long ago had no idea what it was they were bringing upon themselves.
Firiona's dogged pursuit of the staff might bring her from one corner of Norrath to the other, but nothing of this plane or any other shall stand in her way.

During her last visit to Kunark, accompanied by Lyirae Oakwynd, Firiona learned that The Lifeguide had made it to Velious in the clutches of Danak and Vahlai!
Surely now it is only a matter of time before the precious artifact is retrieved to be wielded by Tunare's only chosen!

 　
Lorisyn Avenged 　
Lorisyn Oakwynd, friend and companion of Tunare's chosen Fiiiorona Vie and faithful servant of Tunare herself, was killed in the name of balance some time ago.
Lorisyn's sister, Lyirae, steeping over with righteous fury, accompanied Firiona to Kunark in pursuit of the Lifeguide recently.
There they met with resistance from Ghargin Bumblok, the vile creature that sent Lorisyn prematurely from this mortal realm.
After getting from him that which they needed, in the name of Tunare, Lyirae was given the chance to silence the screams of her heart with a single arrow... and balance was restored.
