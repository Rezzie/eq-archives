>I have come to learn of the ancient tribes of the Iksar.

There were five tribes of Iksars that rose from the ashes of the Shissar Empire and founded their kingdoms on the continent of Kunark.
Three of the tribes were extinguished many years later by the Kunzar tribe which is the tribe that the current Iksar are descendant from.
I focus my studies on learning of the extinct tribe of Kylong.

Shissar帝国の残骸から源を発し、そして王国をKunarkの大陸に築いた５つのIksarsの種族がいた。
３つの種族が、その後現在のIksar種族となるKunzar種族によって滅ぼされた。
私は絶滅したKylong種族の学問を研究している。

>What is Kylong?

The Kylong were a mountain dwelling tribe of Iksar that strove for esoteric knowledge.
They had vast libraries of ancient magical tomes that had been acquired from the Shissar sorcerers, and strove to understand and put the rituals documented in the tomes to practical use.
When the Kunzar armies marched upon the Kylong, the three greatest Kylong mystics shattered the Medallion of Kylong, a holy relic of their tribe and fled their mountain homes in order to prevent the Kunzar from reassembling the Medallion of Kylong.

Kylongは難解な知識を追求しようとしたIksarの山岳種族であった。
彼らはShissarの魔法使いから得た古代の神秘的な本がある巨大な図書館を持ち、それを理解しようと努力し、そして大冊に書かれた儀式を行おうとした。
Kunzarの軍隊がKylongに進軍した時、３人の偉大なKylong神官はKunzarがMedallion of Kylongを組み立てるのを阻止するためにMedallion of Kylongと彼らの神聖な遺品を打ち砕き、そして彼らの故郷から逃げた。

>Where is the medallion?

I have been attempting to learn of the destinations of the three Kylong leaders that held the fragments of the medallion.
I know only that they sought shelters to hide and continue the practice of their mystical arts In order to one day return their tribe to it's former glory.
Their remains may exist beneath some of the fortresses that are centers of Iksar and Sarnak necromancy.
I have seen indications that some of the tomes within those fortresses are In fact the very tomes once cherished by the Kylong.
Should you manage to recover the broken pieces of the Medallion of Kylong brIng them to me so that I may restore the ancient artifact.

私は今までメダルの破片を持った３人のKylongのリーダーの行き先を知ろうと試みていた。
私はただ彼らの隠れ家を捜し求める、
そしていつか彼らの種族の昔の栄光を復活させる為に彼らの神秘的な技を行い続けている。　
彼らの遺物はIksarとSarnakのNecromancyの魔法の中心である要塞の下に存在するかもしれない。
私はそれらの要塞の中の本の幾つかが、まさしくKylongによって大切にされているのを見た。
