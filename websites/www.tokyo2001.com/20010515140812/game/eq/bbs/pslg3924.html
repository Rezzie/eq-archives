（EQCastersより）
In a post on the Gameplay Forum, Abashi acknowledged that Verant were
aware that the Cleric shield the Aegis of Life could be also worn in
the back-slot.

He also commented that this would not be globally implemented for
other shields, as only select items are given the ability for double
slots on a case by case basis.

For Clerics the good news is that the Aegis of Life will not be
changing.
Now they just have to fix the faction problems so you can actually
get it.

つまり「double slots」の事は承知だという事。
ただし普通の盾では当然駄目だとのこと。
