>What is prayer shawl?

Gather five strands of crystalline silk thread and combine them in a sewing kit to make a crystalline silk fiber. 
Take this container and combine a glowing shardworm bile with three of the fibers to form a piece of glowing crystalline silk.
Five pieces of glowing crystalline silk sewn to your fur-lined shawl will yield a beautiful silk shawl.
Become a master in the art of tailoring before risking all you've worked for and be sure to let me inspect your work when it's complete.

Glowing Crystalline Silk５つで、あなたのFur-lined Shawlが美しいSilk Shawlになるでしょう。
