>What bunker battle blade?

The Bunker Battle Blade was crafted and enchanted for use by warriors only.
It has the power to strike down those creatures which hide behind their magic.
Once bestowed upon a warrior it must stay and cannot leave your side or it shall vanish from this realm forever. One must perform [The Green and Blue] to earn such a blade.


Bunker Battle Bladeは戦士だけの為に魔力を付加されて作られた。
それは魔法の力によって生物を打ちのめす力を持っている。
いったん戦士に与えられると、それを手放す事は出来ず、使いつづけるか、もし手放すなら永久に消失してしまう。
