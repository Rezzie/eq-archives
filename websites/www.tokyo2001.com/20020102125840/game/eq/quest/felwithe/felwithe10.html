>What Initiate of Tunare?

The Teir'Dal behind the undead plague in our forest have discovered a means of creating a terrible undead called a Ghast.
These Ghasts have been sighted in the Lesser Faydark and must be destroyed.
Bring me four of the vile creatures hearts.

これらのGhastLesser Faydarkから駆逐せねばなりません。
私に奴らの心臓４つを持って来てください。
