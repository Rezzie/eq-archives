（情報提供：Saless）

　

一部を除いて Keyword は隠し。
基本的に質問されてる事を肯定すれば Ok。
Choose〜 で Book を貰えて、それを返すとそれぞれ別の NPC が Pop します。
（変わりに Efreeti が消える。一定時間で一言残して Efreeti に戻る。）

　


>hail Thalik Silenthand.

Greetings Xxxxx. Do you believe that you are a great Rogue?



>I believe a great rogue.

I will not take your word for it!
You must prove your greatness to one of my apprentices.
Do you wish to be tested by Rayne or by Kendrick?

　
　

Kendrickを選んでみます。

　

>I wish Kendrick.

Kendrick It shall be.
Take this book and read it.
When you are finished hand it back to me and I shall summon the mighty Rogue Kendrick to test you.

　
　

Bookが貰えますので返しますと、KendrickがPopします。

Great, let us waste no more time!
Do you wish to begin your test of Thievery, Silence , or Trickery?

Travel beyond and bring forth an Ivory Tessera, a gem of invigoration, and an enlaid choker to complete the test of thievery and earn the wispy choker of vigor!
