　Neriakにて。

　
　

>hail Vorshar the Despised.

Vorshar the Despised turns toward you with an icy glare.

''Hail Xxxxx , I am Vorshar the Despised.
I trust that you have important business with me?
Why else would you disturb one such as I when it is so obvious that I am a being of great power.
I was [selected by Innoruuk] himself to reside in the Realm of Heroes.
I have sacrificed many a mortal in the eyes of hate and do not be so quick to assume that I would not be delighted to perform such a rite again.''

Vorshar the Despised curls his lip in a twisted, sinister smirk.

Vorshar the Despisedは冷たい目であなたを睨みつけます。

「やぁ 、私はVorshar the Despisedだ。
私はあなたとなにか話し合う事があったかな？
何故あなたは私のような偉大なる力をもつ者に対して煩わしい事をさせるのかな。
私は［ Innoruuk神によって選ばれ］、そして英雄の領域（Realm of Heroes）に住むことを許されているのです。
私は多くの人間を犠牲にしました、そして私が再びそのような儀式を行わないとは限らない事を覚えておきなさい。」

Vorshar the Despisedは、嫌な笑みを浮かべます。

>Why selected by Innoruuk?

Why? You dare to ask one such as I the will of the Hateful Father?!
However, you do seem to be quite lacking in the field of thought and intelligence, which would easily explain why you have not heard my name [nor of my legend]. 

なぜかって？あなたはあえて私のようなHateful Fatherに答えを望むのですか?!
しかしあなたは非常に思慮に欠けると思われる。何故なら、私の名前を聞かなかったし、[私の伝説]に付いても聞こうとしなかったからね。


>What is nor of your legend?
I, my dear witless Dark Elf, was chosen by Innoruuk's hand during the uprising and fall of the [Combine Empire.]
When the Teir`Dal had begun their decision to sign a treaty and join the Empire, I was one of the few who opposed siding with humans and the rest of the lowly creatures of this world.

私の親しい愚かなるダークエルフが[Combine帝国時代]の反乱と没落の時にInnoruuk自身の手によって選ばれた。
Teir `Dal が条約に署名しそして帝国に加入する決定をしようとした時、私はそれに反対する少数派でした。

>What is combine empire?

Vorshar the Despised narrows his eyes, blazing with hatred and a sinister amusement.

'The Combine Empire was a short-lived political structure of many of the world's races that fell shortly after my ascension.
Innoruuk had found my faith and devotion to his word and his children so appealing, that he chose me to walk within the Hall of Heroes, although now, I have [returned].
To this moment, I know not why I have been given such a punishment as to again lay sight on and give ear to the grotesque, obtuse creatures of Norrath.'

Vorshar the Despisedは憎悪と不吉な笑みを浮かべ、燃え上がる目を狭めます。

「Combine帝国は私が上がった後に世界の種族の多くが集まった短命の政治的な組織でした。
Innoruuk は私の信頼と献身に気づき、Hall of Heroesの中で歩く事を許したのです。
けれども今、私は[戻ってきました]
なぜお前のような奇怪で鈍い者の相手をする仕打ちを受けなければならないかは知っています」

>Why you returned?

It is the Dark Father's will that I have returned to this realm.
I do not question his judgement, nor shall I ever.
His will is mine, and I do not question myself.
The Dark Father wishes to expand his followers within the Realm of Heroes, and thus, I have been sent as more of a judge and jury for those who would consider themselves [worthy of Innoruuk's recognition] and divine favor.

私がこの地に戻ったことは暗黒の神父の意志です。
私は彼の判断を疑いません。同様に今もそうするべきではありません。
彼の意志は私なのです、そして私は私自身に尋ねません。
暗黒の神父はRealm of Heroesの中で彼の信者を広げることを望みます。
そして私は［ Innoruuk のお眼鏡に適う］者たちを判別する為に送られました。

>I am worthy of innoruuks recognition.

Vorshar the Despised looks at you a moment, his features emotionless beyond the searing impatience and hatred within his eyes.

'Then you have only furthered my faith in your utter lack of wit.
Those who would gain favor with The Dark Father carry a symbol, a coin, that they must give to me for proper judgement.
Without that coin, you are but another fool amid a sea of imbeciles.'

Vorshar the Despisedは無表情な中にでも目の中に痛烈な苛立ちと憎悪を一瞬浮かべます。

「お前は全く理解力が無いようだ。
暗黒の神父と共に好感を得る者たちは適切なシンボル、コインを私に持ってきます。
そのコインも無いとは、お前は山ほど居る愚か者の一人だ」
