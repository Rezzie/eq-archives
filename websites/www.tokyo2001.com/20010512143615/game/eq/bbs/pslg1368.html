>hail Kyralynn.
Hello, Ryuzi! I am the shaman Kyralynn.
If you have banded armor, my husband Vrynn and I can form it into a
new shaman armor using this totem and some reagents for the ritual.
I form armor from banded [Bracer], [Mail], [Helm], and [Cloak].

>What is bracer?
The bracer requires a Banded Bracer, 2 Dufrenite, Cauldron Shell from
an aqua goblin in Dagnors Cauldron, and a Bull Elephant Tooth from
South Karana.

>What is mail?
The mail requires Banded Mail, 2 Dufrenite, a Lancer Swordfish Fin
from Kedge Keep, and Grizzleknot Bark from South Karana.

>What is helm?
The helm requires a Banded Helm, 2 Dufrenite a Glacier Bear Pelt from
a glacier bear in Everfrost, and an Ogreskin Chamois from a lizard
page in the Temple of Cazic Thule.

>What is cloak?
The cloak requires a Banded Cloak, 2 Dufrenite, Basalt Drake Scales
from the Rathe Mountains, and a Driftwood Pipe from an isle goblin
chieftan in the Ocean of Tears.

Lesser Faydark, and Terror Spines from a Tentacle Terror in Najena.

from Everfrost, and a Griffenne Downfeather from North Karana.


>What is sleeves?

Estate of Unrest, and Charger Hoof Chips from South Karana.


>What is Leggings?
The leggings require, Banded Leggings, 2 Dufrenite, a Permafrost Snowball
from a goblin wizard, and Bone Barbs from a barbed bone skeleton in
Estate of Unrest.
