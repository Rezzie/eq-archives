BRDギルドで話を聞きます。

>Hail, Sarialiyn Tranquilsong

Pleased to meet you Xxxxx.
I am Sarialiyn Tranquilsong, Virtuoso of the Songweavers Kelethin division.
I have spent all of my years here in Kelethin training our new recruits to ensure that all are ready to defend themselves once their adventures take them away from the Faydark and beyond.
If you are a [young bard of Kelethin] then I might have
some exercises for you to complete.

>I am a young bard of Kelethin.

Well then you surely must be interested in some of the [training exercises] I have created for all young members of the Songweavers bard guild.

>What training exercises?

The training exercises that I speak of are very important in the development of a young bards core abilities.
You will have to rely on all of your skills including hunting and gathering to complete these tests but in the end you will be rewarded with a full set of your own armor that you will craft by your own hand.
Are you ready to [begin the exercises]?

>I am ready to begin the exercises.

Fantastic Xxxxx, here is your Melodic Assembly Kit.
In this kit you will collect various items from all over the Faydark, Clan Crushbone and in our hometown of Kelethin.
You will then combine these items in certain quantities inside your Assembly Kit to create armor materials, which are then placed in a forge with the applicable mold to yield your armor piece.
As I provided you with your assembly kit, I can also provide you with the armor molds for Tranquilsong [Helms], [Bracers], [Armguards], [Boots], [Greaves], [Gauntlets] and [Breastplates].
All you must do is simply tell me what armor piece you [want] to craft, and I shall give you the appropriate mold and material recipe.

よろしい。ここにあなたのMelodic Assembly Kitがある。
このキットであなたはFaydark 、Clan Crushbone、そして故郷Kelethinで様々な材料を集める事になる。
Assembly Kitに材料を詰めて防具の元を作り、それをMoldと一緒にForgeで鍛冶をしなさい。
[Helms], [Bracers], [Armguards], [Boots], [Greaves], [Gauntlets], [Breastplates]で作りたいものを私に言えば、そのレシピとMoldを渡しましょう。

・Melodic Assembly Kit

を受け取ります。

作りたい部分を聞いてみます。

>I want to craft a helm.

To create your helm material you will need to combine 2 Bricks of Crude Bronze, 1 Faerie Wing, 1 Royal Jelly and 1 Mead in your assembly kit.
Once you have created the proper material take it to a forge along with this mold to fashion your very own Helm of the Tranquilsong.

あなたのヘルメットを作るためには、Assembly KitにBricks of Crude Bronzeを２つ、Faerie Wing、Royal Jelly、Meadを詰めてCombineしなさい。
できた物をMoldと一緒にForgeで鍛冶をしなさい。
・Mold

をもらいます。
