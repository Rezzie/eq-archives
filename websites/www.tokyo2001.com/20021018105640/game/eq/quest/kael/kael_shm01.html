>I am a shaman

I sense the spirit of your totem within you and it is strong with power.
You have learned much from your travels and I shall help you, by providing you with protection from harm and the elements, in the form of armor. 

「そなたの内に強い精霊の力を感じるぞ。そなたは旅にありて多くを学び、我はそなたを護る甲冑を以ってそなたを助けよう」

>What about armor?

For you I shall make a coif, breastplate, sleeves, bracers, gauntlets, leggings, and boots.
Each piece is a quest unto itself.
To seek the components, you must search the lands for them and test the power of the spirit within. 

「そなたの為に、coif, breastplate, sleeves, bracers, gauntlets, leggings, そしてbootsを拵えよう。
それは探求により授与されるであろう。
そなたはその為にこの地を巡りそなたの精霊の力を以って試練を乗り越えねばならぬ。」


>What about sleeves?
