Crafted Quest(s)



Shakm Meadowgreen makes:

Crafted Helm -
to get it, give the horse-butt* a Fire Totem [This can be gotten off of the Fire Goblin High Cleric in Nagafen;s Lair and the Inferno Goblin Shaman in Solusek's Eye {Sol A and B respectively for you old-timers :} and a few other fire goblin casters]. Also, he wants TWO Ruby STONES. These are ONLY found in the shop next to the bank in the Erudin Palace, to my knowledge, and so evil races may want to make a mule character. Also beware they cost about 160 plat a pop.

Crafted Gauntlets -
Give Shakm an Aviak Charm [off of those crazy Avocets flying about SK] and TWO STAR Rubies. Star Rubies are a pain in the ass, because they've only been seen in (at least when my guild friend did the quest he only knew of) a female NPC in Felwithe who's standing near the fishing shop and the jewelry shop in Qeynos. They are also sold in a little hut in front of the Befallen zone in West Commonlands. They'll run you about 80 pp each.

Crafted Boots -
Now Shakm wants a Frost Totem [Ice Goblin Patriarchs and High Shamans in Permafrost] and TWO Sapphires. Sapphires can be snatched off of the same two shops as listed for the Star Rubies, only they'll run you about 120 pp. Bleh.

Crafted Vambraces -
Shakm wants an Eye of Griffon [gee, I wonder where to get one of those] and TWO Fire Emeralds. Fire Emeralds cost a nasty 110pp or so and you'll find them in the Jade Dragon, a shop in North Freeport. Also, they can be found in Qeynos in their Jeweler shop. Again, screw you, evil races! : P

Next, we have the half-man, half-dog food Ulan who makes:

Crafted Bracers -
One Evil Eye Optic Nerve [Found on evil eyes but I got them on the first kill with Borxx in Runnyeye] and ONE Sapphire. Sapphires are about 120 pp and found in Qeynos jewlery shop or a female elf in Felwithe near the fishing shop. Note that to get two bracers, you have to do the above twice.

Crafted Greaves -
A Dark Circlet [An NPC named Trazdon in Najena, he hangs out around the Bonecrusher room] and TWO STAR Rubies. Star Rubies will run you a nasty 80 pp or so each and you get them from the same shops as the Sapphires listed above.

Crafted Paldrons -
Three pieces of the Rune of Dwarves [Just smack up Evil Eyes and Borxx in Runnyeye, random drop] and ONE Fire Emerald. The Fire Emerald is found in the Jade Dragon shop in North Freeport. Good luck, inkies, greenies, and fatsos.

Crafted Breastplate -
One Talon [Supposedly off werewolves but much more easily off several named NPCs in Mistmoore, most notably the Gypsy Musician and Butler Sinclair and Maid Issis], and three good old, regular, Rubies. Yes, THREE RUBIES. Break the bank, the old fashioned way! By the way, Rubies are found at most jewelry shops - no crazy locations. They also run a sinister amount (I had to log before he got the Breastplate, let me go check some sites and send you an update if I find the price).

MULE CHARACTER TIP:
Make an Enchanter with maxxed out CHA and 5 in whatever stat you want (like Wisdom, since this IS a stupid mule character). This makes buying easier. Example: in Felwithe, create an Enchanter with 115 CHA and make your buy. Yay! =)
