Mail Quest

"I am interested"
"I will deliver mail to ..."


The numbers after the City name refer to the general level of critters in the area, so you know not to stop at all. Take care that you don't stop in difficult places like Steamfont, until you KNOW you are safe. Pay attention to what the NPC's say. Also check a few other quests. For example, there is a Qeynos/South Karana Lute quest, that goes both ways.
