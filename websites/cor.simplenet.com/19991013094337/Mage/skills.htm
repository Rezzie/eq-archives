Skills
Your skills will be the deciding factor in everything you do.  From trying to cast that gate spell, evading a nasty or making your first suit of mail.  Skill caps are set according to your level. The more you gain in levels the more you can increase your skills.  Your skill increase by either putting pactice points towards them or using them on a regular basis.

 

One handed blunt This is your skill in one handed blunt weapons such as a cracked staff.
Two handed blunt This is your skill in two handed blunt weaponssuch as a Great staff.
Defense This is your natural defense, your skill at avoiding blows this skill combines with Dodge to boost your overall defense against attacks. This skill when improved slightly increases your Armor Class.
Offense Like Defense, this skill raises your "natural" offensivness, meaning it raises your attack number and this bonus would still be there if you unequiped whatever weapon you are using.
Piercing This is your skill in piercing weaponssuch as a Dagger.
Bind Wound This skill can be used to heal yourself or another person up to 50% of their total Hit Points
Hand-to-Hand Hand-to-Hand combat is fighting with no weapon.
Throwing This is your skill in thrown weapons  such as those throwing daggers you will be able to summon later on.
Swimming This is your ability to swim and to hold your breath for longer periods of time.
Sense Heading This skill allows you to sense which direction you are heading. I'm not sure how useful this is? Watch the sky the clouds always move in the same direction.
Evocation Your skill in spells that utilize Evoking.
Alteration Your skill in spells that utilize Altering.
Abjuration Your skill in spells that utilize Abjuring.
Conjuration Your skill in spells that utilize Conjuring.
Channeling Your skill in spells that utilize Channeling.
Divination Your skill in spells that utilize Divining.
Meditate Decreases the time it takes to scribe, memorize, and regain mana.  Not Available till level 4
Research Research new spells. See the research page for more info.   Not Available till level 16.
Specialize This skill will allow you to specialize in only one type of magic like divination or evcation.  But you can only specialize in one. Not Available till level 20.  More info coming.
Dodge Allows you to dodge blows in combat. Not Available till level 22. 
Fishing Your skill in fishing determines how many failures you have before actually catching a fish, it also should help you avoid breaking your fishing pole.
Baking Your ability to bake things.
Tailoring Very useful for a beginning bard, you can take wolf pelts and make patchwork armor out of them. Patchwork is almost as good as leather, and if you get a blacksmith you can also add studs to your patchwork armor making Studded leather.
Smithing As mentioned above this is your ability to shape steel or other metals into useful objects, like lantern cases, studs for leather armor, even metal armor.
Fletching This is your ability to make arrows. There are a wide range of materials you can use to make arrows, these will be listed under a different section.
Brewing Your ability to brew alcoholic beverages, yeah!!
Alcohol Tolerance Your ability to avoid adverse affects of alcohol.
Beg This is a skill all races get, use this with extreme caution NPC's will kill you if you beg from them to much, or some if you beg from them at all!
Jewelcraft You can craft fine jewels and magic items to wear or sell.
Pottery Makes pots, jugs, vases, etc. This is your ability at making these.
Common Tongue All of the three races that a Bard can be get this skill. Common tongue is the common language of Norath.
