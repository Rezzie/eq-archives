Magicians are the summoners of the arcane arts, and wield the power to call forth everything from a loaf of bread to a huge Fire Elemental to fight for them. While not as adept at offensive spells as the Wizards, Magicians still wield considerable power in this area as well.

June 9, 1999

NEW PATCH MESSAGE

UUnet will be performing emergency maintenance on their San Diego router beginning at 4am PDT, Wednesday 9th. This will result in a brief loss in connectivity.

- Last night, we updated the servers with data that caused the Wizard's Bolt spells to become ineffective against creatures. The problem has been rectified and is currently up and running on the Test Server. We will continue testing on the new changes, and if successful, will be updating the servers on Wednesday at Midnight, PDT.

- Even though the set of items mentioned yesterday have been set Pending Lore, a bug prevents them being displayed as such. A fix will be part of tomorrow's patch.

- We have added PvP faction modifiers. If a player PK's another player, factions related to the race of the player he/she killed will be adversely modified.

- We have now enhanced and activated the Specialization Skills:

Specialization will do two things for your character.

(1) Specialization will reduce the mana cost for your spells. This will start off gradually (in some cases imperceptibly), and increase as your specialization skill increases.

(2) Specialization will reduce the fizzle percentage for your spells.

You will be able train in all 5 types of specialization. You will only be able to concentrate in one type of specialization, however. Your first specialization to reach a skill of 51 or higher is your concentration. NO OTHER SPECIALIZATION SKILL WILL BE ABLE TO PROGRESS PAST 50. Choose wisely.

Example: Mildred the Magnificent (Wizard) has a 50 skill in both her Specialize: Evocation and her Specialize: Abjuration. Mildred decides that she wants to be an Evocation specialist. She goes to her guildmaster and trains one point in Specialize: Evocation. She now was a 51 skill in Specialize: Evocation. No other specialize skill will be able to go higher than 50.

- The EverQuest Team
