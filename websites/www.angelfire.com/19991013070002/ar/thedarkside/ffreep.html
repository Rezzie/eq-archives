 Freeport Faction Standing Tips1111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111


Where: East and West Commonlands, located next to the cities of Freeport and Neriak
Who: The several Orc Camps located in these 2 zones spawn a good number of Orcs that should be killed in order to improve your faction standing.  The Orc Pawn also commonly scouts the area and is very easy to kill.


Where: East Commonlands
Who: When hunting Orcs in the The Orc camps in East Commonlands, you will pick up a few Deathfist Slashed Belts.  Do not sell them but rather, give them to the NPC name Captain Hazran, who can be found in the balcony of the Freeport Militia HQ building.  It will increase your faction with the Freeport Militia and the Coalition of the Trade Folk.  Be aware that your faction will be decreased with Knights of Truth (Paladins) and Clerics of Marr.
<Submitted by Shaithis>


Where: East Freeport
Who: Under the bar by the docks, in the thieves guild, you will find a human.  If you give him dwarvish ale, you will increase your faction with the Freeport Militia, the corrupt Guads of Qeynos, Carson Mcabe, and the Coalition of the Trade Folk 1 and 3.  You can give him 4 ales at a time to speed it up as long as they are separated to 1 in each of his 4 slots.  Also, the dwarf in the same place asks you to deliver a message to a person near the monk guild and them bring him back an item.  This will have the same faction results and will also give you a used merchant glove (+2 AGI). (Note: Make sure you can talk to these people since they might KOS)
<Submitted by Xakthan>
