Where: Observatory, past the windmills in Steamfont
Who: Female gnome named Brona Frugrin, a telescope lens crafter
What: She will ask that you bring her the retina of a beholder named Borxx, a level 30 Evil-Eye type monster found in Runnyeye so she can make you a telescope unlike any other
How: n/aYou will need a party to kill Borxx as he hit very, very hard.  A tank and a cleric will help a lot.
Reward: Once you bring Brona the lens, she will give you the awesome Observer's Staff, a weapon with attack 12, delay 35, +5 WIS, +5 INT, and +30 mana.  It also has the Sentinel effect.
