Najena's Room

 
This is the entrance to Najena's area.
This gate is guarded by two Tentacle Terrors,
two Elementals and a Magician with a pet.

 
Past that gate you will see this Elementals and
a Magician with her pet standing guard.
Go right past it and look to your right.

 
You should see these stairs and maybe an
Ogre Guard.  Head down these stairs.

 
Just down the stairs is this water pool.
This is the place you should med once you kill
her for the first time.  But for now, jump on the
water and swim to the other side.

 
Once you get out of the water you will find this door.
That is Najena's room and you will either need the
Gold Crescent Key, a Wizard's Eye of Zoom, or
your pet to open the door.

 
This last screenshot is from inside Najena's room
right after a successful battle.  Those on the picture
are Kryx, Sioned, Starr, and Xerxov.
