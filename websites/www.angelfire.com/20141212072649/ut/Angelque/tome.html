Shaman Totemic Armor Quests
The quest starts and finishes in Lake Rathetear with two Barbarains; Kyralynn,location: 258.10, 2220.98 and Vrynn,location: 196.23, 2132.95, in the barbarian fishing village. They make high quality shaman only armor. You must search the Norrath for the ingredients, Medium Banded Armor, and the dufrenite. Remember all Banded Armor must be Medium. The dufrenite can be purchased from the vendors Murtog MacYee & Holana Oleary in Halas, Barbarian vendor in West Karana, or Female Troll vendor in front of the shaman's guild in Grobb. It can also be looted from Clockworks in Solusek's Eye and from Carrion Ghouls in Unrest. The dufrenite must be processed by the one of the the three ogre shamen in the camp on the other side of the lake into Crushed, Ground, or powered dufrenitefor most items. Kyralynn makes bracers, cloak, helm, and breastplate. While Vrynn makes boots, gauntlets, leggings and sleeves.
