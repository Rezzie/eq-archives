Character Creation




Being a Shaman is a cross between a fighter and a cleric, with a little touch of wizard thrown in. We have spells that will raise every single attribute that we have; along with our heal skill makes us a pretty valuable party member. We also have the added advantage of being able to jump into the fight with a weapon and do some damage. A Shaman is always welcome in a group because of his combination of abilities. The reason we are most loved is for our spell Spirit of the Wolf.

Character Stat Table



Character Stat Table
STR: Determines how much you can carry and influences maximum and average damage, as well as influences how quickly you learn many offensive skills
STA: Affects how many hit points you have and how long you can hold your breath
AGI: Affects how quickly you can learn some defensive skills; how difficult it is to hit you and how much damage you take when you are hit
DEX: How quickly you learn weapon skills and how hard you hit with bows
WIS: Affects the amount of mana the Shaman have.
INT: Directly affects how quickly you can learn most skills; affects the amount of mana for bards and all mages, as well as shadow knights
CHA: Affects amount of cash you Buy and sell goods with NPC merchants; affects the saving throw on certain bard and enchanter spells
