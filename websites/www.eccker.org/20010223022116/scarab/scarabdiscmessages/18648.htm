An unfortunate turn of events

Posted by Senator Gyst Starblayze on February 13, 2001 at 23:34:46:

Fellow guildmates, a very unfortunate event has recently occurred. Although the situation which has transpired is less than pleasant, it is my deep hope we can learn from this occurence to better ourselves in the future.

Allow me to introduce Gertrude McSmitherson.

Gertrude McSmitherson was born back in June of Phase 2 beta, and she is now 143 years old. Throughout her long hard life as an overworked, underpaid, ignored rat catcher in Freeport, she has seen many difficult times. She's lived through corruption, betrayal, deceit, bitterness, war, hatred, famine, drought, suffering, and even the occassional nerf.

She's witnessed 18 of her great grandchildren be slaughtered by the marauding orc hordes, and one great grandchild's death caused by an unfortunate encounter with a deprived clarity addict on a boat.

When her she was only 3 years old, both her parents were killed when their country cottage caught on fire during the great fire beetle invasion of '68. She then moved to the city to be sold as a servant girl to the corrupt minions of Sir Duncan D'Lere, the father of the notorious Freeport tyrant.

By living on only 2 meals every other week, she finally saved enough money to buy her freedom and begin 76 long years as a rat catcher in the Freeport sewers.

Gertrude is allergic to barley, cotton, firiona pollen, rat hair, water, sunlight, dark holes, and even various types of cheese. She has arthritis in her jaw and what's left of her ankles. She has extreme carpul tunnel syndrome, a rare type of measels, an unusual case of vertigo, and a chronic plague of hiccups.
She is legally blind, tone deaf, and she requires the aid of three canes to walk. She has a fatal heart condition which has spread to cause 13 unique types of cancer in her bones, tongue, and uvula. She was born with two left legs, webbed toes, a hemoraging spinal cord, and incurable bad breath.

As you can imagine, her life isn't very fun, and her medical bills are staggering.

Just the other day, Gertrude spend two and a half hours hobbling from her shack in East Freeport to the Temple of Marr to pick up some medication for her grandchild who is suffering from 35 dozen bixie stings, to which he is allergic. Gertrude has made this long journey to the Temple of Marr daily for the past 6 and a half months.
However, the other day, when she exited the Temple, she tripped over a drunken dwarf who was sleeping sprawled out in the courtyard in a drunken stupor.

>

In her fall, Gertrude shattered 46 bones and punctured her one remaining good lung.

This is simply, utterly, and absolutely terrible. We cannot allow these types of atrocities to be occuring in our civilized cities. If elected to the Inner Circle, I would devote myself to the protection and assistance of these poor people in such terrible conditions. Furthermore, I would ensure that these uncaring, criminally indifferent, highly dangerous drunken dwarves would be removed from crowded areas and left to have their hangovers in nice, safe, enclosed kennels where they will not pose a threat to anyone's health.

So, when the ballots come out, don't just vote for me. Don't just cast a vote for the welfare of the guild. Don't just vote for safety and security for the children.
Vote for Gertrude McSmitherson, and the others like her. Remember, by voting for Gyst you'll be ensuring the safety and well-being of all upstanding citizens of Norrath.

Take action into your own hands, and vote Senator Starblayze for 2001 Inner Circle!

>
