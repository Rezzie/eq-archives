I'm planning on writing this once, it's our turn. If you have any suggestions to modify my list, please let me know.

I would suggest that everyone put in their suggestions, this is our chance to get some things changed. Don't pass it up.


1. PvP Problems

I'm a little biased, because I play on TZ, so this is my number one issue. I realize the PvP community is small. But it seems we have been nerfed more than any other class on any other server (aside from VZ). Even though we are small, please consider some of the changes.

a. Magic spells only. It's fine that the magic line is our only line of spells. But if that's the case, then we need to be able to stick our spells a little more often. For example our DD spells are either resisted completely or hit for full. Everyone on the server knows this, and that's why everyone above 50th has a MR above 100. Most melee's I know have a MR above 150, unbuffed, some are even above 200. Now with the addition of Blue Diamonds, MR is going to be even higher. Thus making it even harder to get a magic spell to stick.

Things that could be changed to help this:

i. Change Tashania/Tashanian to subtract more MR when casted upon other players. Tashania use to be -63, now it's -33 and Tashanian has a max of -39. I suggest a max of 45 for Tashania and 65 for Tashanian for players only.

ii. Let our damage spell line have a % resisted instead of all the damage resisted. Other line of spells get partial resists, the magic line should as well.

iii. Modify the resist tables for enchanters. We are the masters of the magic line, we should have less resists than other classes with the same line of spells. For example someone with a MR of 100 (after a Tash) should be able to be mez'd by a 50th (level * 2) at least 50% of the time. As it stands I'll get a mez around 5% of the time, to stick on a level 46 with 124MR at level 52. These tables really need to be adjusted.

b. Illusions are dropped in PvP. This was a temporary fix and a better fix should be used in order to fix the problem. To get behind enemy lines can be a very fun and tactical way to battle.

2. Spells that are useless or broken.

a. Mez and charm in Velious. There are whole zones that are unmezzable and uncharmable. On top of that most of Velious is the same way. Sure I can root, give clarity and speed, but geez, that's not what I have been doing my whole life. I want to be the crowd controller, please make us more useful in Velious. I understand I can't mez dragons or giants, but it's all the other monsters I have a problem with. I avoid Velious, because of these reasons. I would much rather go somewhere else where I am more effective. Sure, I'll go if we want to work on a quest or grab a new item, but I would much rather go somewhere where I'm a little more useful, thus so does my guild.

b. Reoccurring Amnesia. This spell is broken. It doesn't reoccur. It should be one of our most used spells. With the high casting cost, and being broken, it is useless. I use mem blur in it's place, and that can't be counted on. Thus allowing me to continue to be Super Aggro Chanter!

c. Blanket of Forgetfulness. This spell is supposed to be unresistable, but it's not. This also will allow me to continue to be Super Aggro Chanter!

d. Gift spell line. These spells are to expensive for what they give. Either lower the casting cost, longer duration, give more mana and/or give +1/2/3 for mana regen.

e. Clarity 2. This spells duration should be longer. On large raids it's just annoying to be casting this all the time.

f. Umbra. The AC addition is to low. It really needs to be raised.

g. Wind of Tishian. This is the ultimate death wish. Maybe change it so that it doesn't aggro those affected or change Blanket of Forgetfulness (see 2c).

h. Dictate. Should be raised to charm those at 60th.

i. Rune 4. The gem cost for this buff is not worth it. Once I got Rune 4, I never used it. It was more effective to use Rune 3. Maybe change the gem to something cheaper.

j. Group Clarity. This spell is not used much. The spell is useful for 2 levels and most of the time, on raids, there is another enchanter around with Clarity 2. This spell should be changed to a 49th or even a 44th level spell.

k. STR debuffs. These spells have very little to no effect on damage. They aren't worth the cost and are thus unused.

l. lower level mez spells. We can't override lower level mez spells. This just doesn't make sense to me.

m. Illusions. Illusions should hide religion. Plain and simple.

3. Equipment

a. No Tiny Daggers in Qeynos. Nuff said.

b. We need more AC armor. Aside from melee's we are hit more often (and at times even more than melee's) than any other class. Give us something to protect ourselves a little better. See 2f.

c. Rune 4 on Resplendent Robe. Most enchanters that acquire this robe would rather cast Rune 5 on themselves. The cast time is way to long and the additional effect is not worth a peridot. Maybe change the casting time, different gem (see 2i) or just change it to Rune 5.

d. Crystal Crown of Confusion. Casting time on this is almost not worth it.

e. Crystal Rode of Confusion. Same as 3d.

f. Staff of the Serpent. I feel the duration for Speed of the Shissar is a little short.

Well, I think the enchanters on this post have covered most of the major points that I have peeves about in the game. As a class I think enchanters can do a lot already and while there are a few things that can be refined. I am going to support a few points that were mentioned above and give my views on them.

-- Resplendent Robe. Please. Someone get Verant to change this. Rune IV is the worst of the Rune series and is highly overpriced. Either change the effect or reduce the casting cost of Rune IV. Also, the casting time can be reduced some. 25 seconds is far too long, save perhaps in a group raid when you are buffing for an uber boss.

-- Crystal Crown/Wand ot Confusion. They are nice pieces. But please. What are stuns for? We can't afford HUGE casting times on these things for them to be effective. Verant needs to understand that you have a limited amount of time to get a stun off. Either reduce the casting time, or give it a different effect.

-- Armor/hp gear. I get beat. I admit it. Verant needs to offer enchanters more options for a better AC. Right now our /best/ AC with utterly elite gear, is around 700-750 buffed (maybe a little over 800). Either increase our defensive caps or give us some better stuff to take damage. I have worked hard to make myself a defensive enchanter, but its /hard/ as hell. Really frustrating to see other casting classes getting higher AC gear and more options for defense.

-- Mem blur. This includes reoccuring amnesia and mem flux. Please. Make these work a bit better. Mem flux is 350 mana. That should be unresistable. 100 percent of the time mem blur on everything below god level. RA should be standard but should actually give you a good shot of bluring a mob. I believe they are really cautious to increase the effectivness of these because of power leveling and the implications of wiping the hate list like this. It still needs to be fixed.

-- Charming. I charm a lot. Dictate is a bit of a disappointment, but you know? I have seen how much damage a Jugg can do to me in two or 3 ticks. I can see why this is for balance issues. My question is, why not put an interim spell between Boltrans and Dictate. A level caped (50?) charm that would hold for a promised amount of time. Like, I wouldn't mind being able to charm a mob below level 50 or 51, for 1 min or two. Promised charm. Also, giving us someway to show other party members which mobs are charmed would be useful. Highlight their name or something. Me personally? I don't mind mobs coming after me when they break. Its a drawback to charming ..but I handle it.

-- Mezing. Yeah. Please have higher level mezes always overwrite the lower ones. I saw my self getting a "your spell did not take hold" message when grouped with a level 40 enchanter. Come on. I should be able to overwrite his spell. And, maybe have a toggle option that would somehow allow us to differentiate between mobs of the same time. Verant has to admit that pathing is bad. When you get 4 frogs all stacked up in the same area, it takes a work of ART to actually mez them all without fascination or mesmerization.

-- My wish list? Give enchanters a form of mana transfer. We can do everything else with mana but transfer it. Necros get that dubious honor and it would help ease some of their complaints that they are the only ones who ever get asked for it. Enchanters are widely regarded as the "masters of mana management and usage" -- clarity, Theft of thought, the gift series. Why not give them the ability to actually transfer the mana around? Last on my wish list? Ae, reoccuring stun! Other classes have ae's that pass in waves and do damage. Why not have a spell that you cast on a mob and it starts an ae effect that reoccurs on all mobs in that area for say, 3 ticks? This would be a large taunt, but its often no more stunning than I do in a normal situation. Its more or less a fire and forget kind of spell =)

I WILL support the enchanter thread on Friday and I encourage everyone to do the same. We have many of the same points here and if we pass them to Verant without insane suggestions, we can get at least some of our requests granted. Start posting and keep it civil!
