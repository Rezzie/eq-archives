The game got boring because Verant balances everything not on normal items/spell availability, but on what they consider the 'uberest' items for the class. Example: Verant says that the Donal's BP is unbalancing. They said it was unbalancing. How can it be unbalancing, there are maybe 6 on each server. Needless to say, nerf. Well anyways, the original Norrath (without Kunark and Velious) was excellent, it was actually balanced so that people with Bronze and some quest armor and maybe Blackened Iron Bastard Sword could actually fight things.

But after the original, came Kunark. Verant realized that people were fighting all blue cons in groups because they were alot less risk, and you could pull them faster because of lower mana usage (i.e. level faster). So Verant made the blue con of Kunark as hard as a black (even) con on Antonica/Faydwer/Odus. In addition to this, Verant realized that everyone was camping and "farming" the high quality "uber" items for each class, and that, before Kunark, some characters were becoming all too alike and powerful (i.e. ever cleric over 47 running around in full purple, people in fighting over the Rubicite camps, etc...). So, when making Kunark, Verant, in their infinite wisdom, balanced everything in Kunark according to the abilities of a fully twinked player in what Verant considered their "best" armor and weaponry/spells. Verant also 'balanced' armor drops, so that the "best" class armor could not be obtained by but a few, because Verant couldn't stand that people would camp items so that they would have an easier time getting ahead in the game. In doing this, Verant forgot the little guy, who is not a powergamer, and doesn't have the time to camp a spawn for 12 hours.

Next came Velious. Verant, after seeing the folly of Deepwater/Donals/Cobalt type armors being the only coveted things, came out with Velious: a new land to explore, but only if you are in an uber guild. They balanced Kunark according to the uberest of The Original, to what had been camped for over a year and a half in the original. Velious was balanced to the combined uberest of Kunark/The Original. The problem in that, was that people had only had about 7 months to farm Kunark, so Verant's policy of continually increasing difficulty was falling back on them because not everyone was as uber from Kunark as they were when they switched from Original to Extra Crispy, err, I mean Kunark. And yet, now Velious was released with the uberest armors/spells/weapons in mind, and since the only people with these armors/spells/weapons were the uber farming guilds, guess what Velious was REALLY designed for? Uber farming guilds.

This is why the game is getting boring, Verant tries to counteract any and all farming/easy levelling mechanism they find. Anything that allows for quick advancement or satisfaction, they despise. Your class has a certain set of armor they MUST attain in order to be powerful enough to survive in Norrath, no longer can you survive on Bronze and a Fine Steel Long sword. And yet, since you have to attain this armor/weaponry/spell to be useful, Verant goes and double penalizes you by making the spawns/drops hareder to kill, and even once you've killed them there is now less of a chance of a drop. This combined with an ever increasing population on the servers that must fulfill these armors to be useful, leads to a boring exercise in futility. Verant WANTS to quelch all creative advancement mechanisms. Any attempt at using tactics or brains to kill a mob without having a warrior get the stuffing beat outta him is considered an "exploit". Standing on the walls was, in my opinion, not an exploit, you were just outsmarting the mobs down below. If you were an intelligence caster, wouldn't YOU make sure there was a physical barrier between the melee character you want to kill and you? Or course you would. If you were a druid, and could run away really fast, turn around and nuke something, and then run away before it could beat you up, wouldn't you?

All Verant tries to do in "balancing" is attempt to stifle and overcome the creative and resourcefullness of the human mind and ingenuity. That is why the game is boring.
