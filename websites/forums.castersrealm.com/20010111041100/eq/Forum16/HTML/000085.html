I won this and he was lvl 53. Not only that but I was blind almost the whole fight.

I couldnt duel buff myself before the fight, I had exp buffs. I did get pet and give it MR and celerity though.

we duelled near the really big hole in the overthere where the Charisis/Howling Stones zone is. We were also near one of those things where little mounds stick up out of the ground that are slanted.

I started the fight with a manasieve on him and he tried for the usual root on pet which failed. I go for a strip and sieve again while he is trying to channel roots through my pet. He had run away from my pet and he casted blind on me which stuck. He then started casting his huge DD and didnt do much with it (im guessing because I was blind), He didnt like the outcome of that so my 105MR probably stopped a lot of the damage as well as my rune3. He got rid of 4 of my buffs (dont know if it was one spell or 2 I was blind for a LONG time Then I hit F1 to target myself and then hit control+#1 to rune myself again. I probably casted this after he started DD and before it finish casting because I got it off. Somewhere in here my pet got loose too and started whackin on him. I then broke blind and retargetted him and sieved. He started casting DD and this is where I found out I had low HP because he wouldnt try to channel a DD through the pet if it wouldnt win it for him. I looked and saw that I was at 20% so I ran to where the mountain thing was in between us and he couldnt see me. The dd stopped casting so either the pet interrupted or he was smart and didnt want to cast it through and get the "you cant see youre target" message wasting his mana. I casted rune on myself again as he ran arount the mountain thing to get me in sight again. He came into sight and started what would be his last DD because the pet interrupted right before my sieve finish casted taking all the rest of his mana. He then had gotten down to like 80%hp from my pet attacking him so he ran up the hill and tried uselessly to fight the pet for a while and blinded me again and I assume he tried to attack me physically while being beat on by my pet. When I was blind I remembered what I was fighting on and did my best to run around without gettin too close to the edge so I could kite him without him hitting me. I broke blind and tried an anarchy on him to end the fight sooner but he was lvl 53, 10 lvls higher, and had MR stuff on so no go on that. I chain casted my lvl 8DD every time it recycled and got him down to about 60% with that and my pet. I still had 40% mana left and could have easily chain casted it and used the pet to win with beats and he saw this so he ran down the mountain thing and then jumped into the hole and died of falling dmg. He robbed me of the win message but still acknowledged I won once he returned. He also let me know that he is an avid dueller like myself and thought it would have been no big deal to take me down Too bad i gotz da skillz to beat 10 lvls higher unless they mages, mage pet can probably solo me in 1 round at that lvl.

One of the only duels I lost lately was to a lvl 52 mage at 43 He killed me in like 10 second flat with his high powered fast casting DD's.

I dont think there was really anything I could have done here either.

He sent the pet in which got mezzed on the way then I got hit for a couple hundred points. I moved to the side a little so I could see and I chaotic feedback to interrupt his next DD with pet help. He try again and I tried to stop it with tishan and mez, his DD channeled through the pet and right into me as my mez was about to land. Mez didnt hit but it probably wouldnt have landed anyway since he was 9 lvls higher with good MR also.

I acknowledge getting megaowned in that last one. Two fire DD's and I went down hard. That was a while before the Cleric though and just brought it up because after that I felt like what must happen to all my opponents I got owned bad... I might have been better off if i talked him into using spells that were lvl 44 or below for fairness and just had the level advantage on me but I didnt. He didnt stay for a second shot either.
