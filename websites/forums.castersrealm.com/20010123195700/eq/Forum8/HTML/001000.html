This is an old bug that has been mentioned before. When you first load in npc's react to your race and religion, then read faction modifiers (quests) that you have done. So if you log in or gate in right next to a guard, he'll attack you before bothering to read your modified faction.

Best advice is to bind and camp in an area that guards don't patrol.
