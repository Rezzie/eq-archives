Gonna wait this one out:

Seeing as how I bought Kunark when it came out, and haven't even ventured down there with my main, (though my regular group is kinda getting the hankering to go. Only half of us have RoK currently, and we'll undertake it at once), I think I'm going to wait this one out for a bit.

By now, Kunark is down to the same price at retailers as it ended up being from the online store. As I understand it, there is no 'expansion only' option with Velious, so there is no real advantage to getting it right away from the online store.

Also, from reading the preview in the new "Computer Games Strategy Plus", the quote that stood out is that the whole continent can be "done" like a dungeon. Ie. Worked from point A to B. There is going to be a Storm Giant city to bind in (If you have high enough faction), and A version of the Druid circles that will allow you to gate back to the furthest point you've reached in, but it does sound that you're going to have to be serious about going in. Not quite ready for that.

And lastly: I live in Canada, and we've already had out first snowfall where I live. When it's -40 outside, the last thing I want is my virtual world to be a barren wintry wasteland as well. I think I'll stick to the warm tropics duing this winter, and consider Velious come next spring.
