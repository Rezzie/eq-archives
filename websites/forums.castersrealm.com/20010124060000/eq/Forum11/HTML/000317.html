OMG, there has been a serious assumption made here folks...

Willja asked for a list of the best stuff a druid could use. I did some online research and submitted the best I could find. I did not mean to imply or infer that I actually HAD any of these!

I am sorry for the mis understanding. These are still on my want list though...

And now for some followup:

Golden Efreeti Chestplate: Drops off of NOBLE DOJORN, a level 63 warrior in the Plane of Air.

Nature Walker's Mantle: Given for completing the Test of the Bear Quest.

Nature Walkers Scimitar: Druid Epic Quest weapon... 'nuff said!

Belt of Dwarven Slaying: Given for completing the Dwarf Slayer Quest.

Gloves of Earthcrafting: Given for completing the Gloves of Earthcrafting Quest on the Plane of Growth.

Crown of Rile and the Shroud of Veeshan: Drops off of PHARA DAR level 65-67 Enchanter in Veeshan's Peak.

Grey Suede Boots: Drops off of HOSHKAR, a level 65 bard in Veeshan's Peak.

Pulsating Gem: Drops off of NEXONA, a level 65-67 Shadow Knight in Veeshan's Peak

Rowyl's Bracer of Nature: Given for completing part of the Thurgadin Druid Armor Quest.

Supple Scale Armband: Drops off of the DROLVARG WARLORD, a level 51-54 warrior in Karnor's Castle

Runed Etched Wedding Band: Drops off of DAMAN, a level 38 Shadow Knight found in the Tower of Frozen Shadow.

Tanglewood Shield: Drops off of WUOSHI, a level 61-63 Druid (dragon) in the Wakening Land.

Maple Leaf Mask: Found on the Plane of Growth.

Ring of Empowerment: Award given at a GM event. Obtained from the Test of Steel and the Test of Mystics. This was a huge event where many different classes battled it out. The winner received this item. I believe that there is only one per server.

That's all the info I could dig up at this time. Hope it helps out some
