Howdy

quick how to on Neh ashir.

directions.
enter through locked door kill the ghiosk boss (first reaver). take either door on the back wall turn right go to end of corridir where it turns right again and goes up a long sloping corridor. go all the way up this till u reach a door. dont go through door but through the wall on the right just before door (false wall that leads out onto the sky rail thingy) its then just a case of going the only way u can.
house ontop of rail > a walkway > another house > walkway > 3rd house where it spawns.

2 keeling it.

my advise would be to pull the reaver from 3rd house to 2nd house. Neh ashir can cast grav flux so u really dont want to fight outside on the narrow walkway.

seems that usually Neh pops afters after the 3rd reaver (can be more can be less but 3 seems most common).

Neh ashir melees and casts some enc spells. she isnt too hard and has no majour abilities that need special prep for.
she is highky resistant (all CoM lords are) Lure lines are the only nukes that land.
so a few good tanks are the way to go.

one good group around the 55+ can do it without too much danger 2 groups of 50+ would also have no dificulty.

spawn time is 2 hours

drop on torch is rare. ive killed her 8 times since patch and still havent seen a torch.

On akkstaff- ive seen him spawn all over najena . in the jail in drelznas room , in the mob less torture rooms , in the room just past the 2 dark bone room. out in a corridor .. etc
he didnt despawn when i handed the quest items in and hes always been up while i was in najena.
i know the rogues on my server have been having majour probs with one of there items not spawning just on the CT server (working fine on the others) maybe same things happening with akstaff?

Walnan does despawn after you hand items in and she seems to be a 2-3 day spawn ...

Power of fire- can drop off the blazing and the lava ele (i have seen both)

just answering a few points i have solid confirmation on =)
