i am a DEF SK and i want to get a better weapon than my 2 handed sword but i cant find anywhere to get one.if u could tell me where i could buy one iwould greatly appreciate it.

Edge of Cabilis, 25 damage, 50 delay. Nasty nasty sword. At level 23 I do about 51-59 damage on a hit, average is 50. My best hit so far was for 76.

Alternatly you might get an axe of the iron back (doesn't do near the damage, but it's quicker).

As for 1h vs 2h, I use both. Of course I am a erudite, so it's easy to keep my combat skills up.

Before level 20, I'd say do 1h slash, post 20 use 2h. Of course use whichever you have a better weapon for, but before 20 you can only do 29 or so damage maximum so 1h is almost always better.
