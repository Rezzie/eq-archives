Uhm Trolls do get stunned from the front.. only ogres have the no stunning from front.
Ogres are better in all aspects. Well so do I think. Hehe I have a level 53 ogre shaman on Tunare. And she kicks butt. Well I was not very wise when I started her up, but I have never regretted anything. I only put 5 points into Wisdom (go figure). I am now up to 190 wisdom so its doable and AC is at 768 with self buffs. The troll regen is nice at the lower levels when you play warrior type and it is mildly good at higher levels for doing the shaman dance of cannibalizing, but not godly good. Starting stats are better for an ogre then for a troll. The faction aspect, hmm well don't bother too much thinking about it. You will be very strong and can carry lot more loot and coin, then any other priest caster warrior type out there.. hehe I have 238 str selfbuffed. Not many items can make me go OOOF. This makes bank runs more rare and therefor not a big nuisance.
Summary: Do not pick troll solely on the regen part, not worth it. Pick troll over ogre if you wanna rollplay trolls and n ot ogres.. Make a barb if you wan't good wisdom. Pick an ogre for its coolness factor(biased).
Do whatever you think will make you have the most fun.

Good luck

EDIT: If you are stopping your enchanter because that you are tired of buffing and stuff.. Don't. Shamans and Enchanters are in those aspects much alike. You will most of your life buff others and meditate and heal.. That is when you are not soloing. I LOVE it, but I have met shamans not willing to buff up the casters because they feel it is a waste of mana. Believe me agility and stamina makes a BIG difference when haveing mobs on you... They will much rather throw in a DOT or a DD. Well good luck again and make sure to put all the points into Wisdom, the extra 5 points.. hmm id put them into agility today. The extra AC matters for us shamans.. Other stats you will gain with buffs but are not necesary(sp?) to be high. Wisdom for mana and ac for tanking a little..

Have fun with whatever you choose..

Ok, I have begun my life as a Troll. I put all the points I could into Wisdom, and the remaining 5 points I put into Dexterity to put it on par with Agility.

I twinked him up a little with some minor items...like Blackened Iron Armor, a Combine Spear, etc...and was a Froglock slaying machine for a while there in Innothule.

I hit level 9 and am now taking on whatever single yellows/whites/blues I can find in South Ro. My spell skills aren't exactly stupendous so I get my fair share of fizzle's, but I'm a melee stud and with Regeneration and Meditation, I'm never out of the fight for too long.

The big guy seems to level a little slower than the other classes I've played, but he's so completely BIG and sinister, that I don't really care.

One thing I did notice after a couple days of playing, was the Fire Resistance of 5. I was planning to go to Lavastorm at level 12 or so, but became unsure when I saw that. Endure Fire should make it tolerable anyway, I may just have to go a level or two later than planned.

All in all, I've been enjoying myself, and I don't see many Troll Shaman on Torv. which makes it even more fun for me since it's a rare breed of character.

You guys talked me into it (though I was leaning heavily in that direction anway) and if you have any further tips or advice I'd be grateful.
