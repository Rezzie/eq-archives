And now you're getting into hypotheticals to defend your stance that you're always better off with AC gear.

Before we can make any sure judgement on whether a situation call for Cha gear or AC gear we need solid facts on the effect of AC, preferable coupled with statistics on cha, allowing for determination of what factors make Cha gear good (if any), and what factors make AC gear good (if any).

I currently don't even have a hypothesis I'm partial towards; you've given good evidence that charisma has a greater effect against mobs with low resistance, which are typically low blues. However, anecdotal evidence seems to indicate that AC has a greater effect vs low blues, so there isn't any room to make an educated guess!

As another poster mentioned, the possibility of getting hit on a mez break needs to be considered, and possibly greatly changes the optimal gear as levels progress, or even group composition; I just don't get hit much on mez breaks, so I would assign a low weight to this factor, but at other levels, of with different group compositions, or just vs different mobs the importance of this factor may vary.

And a note on HP gear, once you get a sufficient amount of HP to keep you alive, adding more HP gear has zero effect on efficiency, it just allows for a greater margin of error, so trading gear that does increase efficiency for HP gear is, in effect, trading away efficiency for a safety net. (which may or may not be a good thing, depending on the skill level of yourself, your groupmates, and the unpredictability level of the camp you're at)
