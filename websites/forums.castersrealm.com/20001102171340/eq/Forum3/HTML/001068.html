But I think they should RP the event as well. Have the Dark Elves start harrasing the bandits, then eventually take over.

Have it happen over a few days. Get some Guides to play the Dark Elves and take over the encampment. Move the bandits to a new home.

Wolf form KOS? There should have been something leading up to it. Maybe Shadow Wolves roaming cities attacking NPCs and PCs. A few weeks later wolf form is kos.

The game is fun, but changes are dangerous. Yea people die. But in D&D if you die, it's time to re-roll your character.
