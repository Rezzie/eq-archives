Kindle was an amazing pain in the ass. (Although spending 2 days in Hate and not getting the damned Vampire Essence is also torquing me off bad)

She wasnt hard to kill (and yes you have to kill her even though she is amiable) but we had 4 and a half groups there so we could have taken down Nagafen with that bunch and had no deaths.

The problem isnt fighting her its getting to her. The pathing in the Hole is laughable at best and the pathing/auto aggro situation in the Ghost Tower is even worse. We managed to get in only by camping everyone out in front of the entrance to the Ghost area and then coming back and buffing /medding to full before quick-pulling and charging up the tower. I think when we go back (shamans need that place too) we'll have an easier time of it but I'd err on the side of caution if I were you.

I know of another bunch who managed to kill the High Scale Kirn (shaman npc near Kindle) with a Rogue and a Shaman who managed to sneak upstairs non aggro after a large army died to the crappy pathing in the tower.

My advice it to get in as fast as you can and then kill Kirn and Kindle and evac out before the lower parts of the tower spawn and create an aggro train up.
