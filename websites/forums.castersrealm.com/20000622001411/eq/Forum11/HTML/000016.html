Until you get SoW, use Treeform whenever you would need to use more than 30 mana to heal yourself up.

Once you get SoW, use Treeform whenever you would need to use more than 70 mana to heal yourself up. Or whenever the time to refresh SoW is coming up anyway.

Retreating to a relatively safe place is possible in all outdoor zones. And only while meditating have I ever attracted any attention. If you must med, cast Treeform for the regen and then Camouflage on top of it. (total cost = 45 mana for safe meditation and very speedy healing). If you're meleeing at all, it's a terribly handy spell.

I don't know how many times I've been recovering in Treeform and then see an injured druid come plop down beside me, med and then stand up and heal themselves multiple times and then sit down and med again. Allow me to assure you, I'm back out hunting long before their mana reserves are restored.

It's self-only regen, people! And SoW is cheaper than multiple heals! And you can use it in Oasis as long as you are not too far away from some of the palm trees that litter the landscape. (It's castability is based on proximity to other trees, as far as I can tell.)
