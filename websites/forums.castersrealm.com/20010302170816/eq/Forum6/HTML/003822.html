Heh - Right on Peto - that one can be fun.

Some of the things I've used CoH for.

1. Replacing party members deep in a zone. (What I assume is its intended use)
2. Adding late comers to a deep raid. (another intended use I'm sure)
3. Spliting pulls
4. CRing like Peto said
5. Running from a lost battle, clearing my agro, then grabbing teammates.
6. Clearing agro on enchanter to save them
7. Clearing faction hits. (A troll and myself were ported and bound in tox by GMs for disruption during the mage tourney -- long story -- but the KoS troll was attacked by a wanderer so I hurt it enough for him to kill, then cleared myself from the faction hit)
8. Killing an agroed stray pet and clearing my agro.
9. Grabbing people who fell into an unreachable area (lower seb to upper seb usually)
10. Clearing agro for powerleveling purposes. =P
11. Clearing my own agro in a lost battle, then waiting for everyone to die, and the mobs to leave. Then CoHing the cleric and others back for CRing (if I didn't have time to CoH the cleric while people died.)
12. Showing off
13. Droping guildmates and beggars off high places. (LOL - Kelethin has lots of beggars and high places =)
14. Saving the puller from certain death (I've had CoH hit on a 4khp tank when he had 2 bubs of *purple* left. =)
15. Clearing agro left from feign death.
16. Bring my team back to zone in of a dungeon if we don't have an evacer.
17. Breaking Plane of Fear (not a trivial task for my guild =)
18. Moving large races past impossibly small spaces.

I'm sure there's even more good uses for it, so I'm saying a nerf would be simply crushing to my role in a group and a raid. =(

And if it's agro gets removed, you can bet that I will gladly cause the biggest trains zones have ever seen to relieve my stress. =P
