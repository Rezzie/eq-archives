Ok, in a nutshell, this is what we know -

Quest begins in Erudin from a guy named Stofo

Stofo needs -
Enchanted Paper
Mechanical Pen
Vial of Dark Ink.

Enchanter paper is obtained by giving the girl in WK a quill/paper which spawns the level 55 bandit who drops the paper.

Mechanical Pen is given by a gnome enchanter in Ak'anon for a SMR.

The vial for the dark ink is given by a enchanter in the Qeynos Catacombs ( there seem to be faction problems with this. ).

This seems to be the true beginning - We have not found the ink yet and this remains to be our bottleneck.

Jeb, Stofo's master, has been found in Burning Woods as a 'sarnak imitator'. He tells you of 4 other enchanter 'masters' who you must talk to in a certain order. No one has found the first guy to talk to, although supposedly he's at some 'elven outpost'.

One of them has been found in FV, an enchantress near the mages guild, who will only speak one line.

Thoughts -
We really need the dark ink. This seems to be the major problem preventing us from accelerating in the quest.

Since we can 'assume' that Stofo is going to give us the letter to deliver to Jeb, Jeb will maybe give us something to give to the first enchanter, who will give us another item to talk to the next master.. etc, etc, etc. ( This is alot like the druid quest. ).

This may also be why none of the enchanter masters will talk to us yet.

Other 'side' things that might play in -

Jade, a jewelry vendor in N FP has been replaced by a gnome who will not talk to people, only says "Hi , my name is Zligg".

There seems to be some connection w/ the plague bringers ( Brex ).

The dark ink comes from the blood of someone that we must 'tempt'. Unfortunately, we do not know who this is, but many thoughts have lead to these main areas -

Upper Guk DE Scribe.
Lower Guk Ghoul Scribe.
NPC's in Najena
NPC's in Neriak
NPC's in Mistmoore

We need to devote alot of time to finding out where this damn ink comes from. Unfortunately, I think some of us will have faction issues with doing certain parts of these quests.

We can also try and find Modani ( the first enchanter master. ).

So our two major focus items -

Find that damn ink.
Find Modani.

Hints we have for the dark ink -

Dark illusions, thievery, and the plague of the living will guide you. These are the only hints I can provide. Safe travels and may you be protected from all maladies.

While dark illusions and thievery would point towards DE's, DE's don't worship Brexx.

The other clue comes from the person you get the vial from -

"the ink is the blood of a dark scribe. Tempt him and give him this vial. He should cooperate"

Unfortunately, there are so many different 'scribes', and we don't know how to tempt them ( be it wine or money ) that this is somewhat difficult.

Hints about Modani -

We know there is a gnome master, a high elf master and a erudite master.

Modani Qu'Loni sounds like a dark elf name, unfortunately, it says that he was excited about coming to the elven outpost...so not sure here. The only other class that be enchanters are humans. Modani is not supposed to be in his 'true' form, which makes finding him even more difficult.

Elven Outposts - FV, Lesser Fay, Timorous Deep. We must scour these for NPC's that either know about the Serpent, Jeb, or answer to 'Are you Modani Qu'Loni?" ( since that's what Jeb's name is. ).

Sorry if this is all repeat information, I'm just trying to post it to clarify my own thoughts and hopefully provoke thoughts and ideas in more clever heads than mine.
