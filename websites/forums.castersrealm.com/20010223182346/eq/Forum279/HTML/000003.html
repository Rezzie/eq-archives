Foremost, i would like to make a statement : enchanters are THE most powerfull class in the game. We make the impossible situation situation become profitable, we increase the speed of the game, the thrill of the fight.

In dungeons, it s impossible to group without us, we are even more vital than a cleric.
At 35 i went inside paw, going for ishva. a "train" shout scared everyone, i just backed in the corner and started mass mezzing. 2 friends died, so did 12 gnolls, when it should have been 9 players death.
A good group in droga may result in some death. A good group with an enchanter will kill everyone, the cleric won t cry he s oom, and no one will die. not while i m here.

THIS is the greatest role in Everquest, everyone agrees.

Enchanters masters of illusions ? yeah, sure. but would you like to trade your skills at mezzing for some illusions ? i don t think so. in groups, no one even cares that the enchanter can cast illusions. the fact that we re not kos is really a wonderfull tool, it s fun and really usefull to change our appearance. But it s not the reason why we re wanted in groups.
We re wanted because we can mezz. And in situations where we can t mezz, we re wanted because we have THE mana regen tool, as much as the druid is wanted for SOW in open areas.

In the complains to the everquest team i read many things about enchanters not being masters of illusions. well, no one in the game can turn in a water elemental can they ? who cares that in EC tunnels a magician gets more attention because his pet is a griffin ?? And once again, our illusions are not what we use in battle, it s not why we re asked in a group, it doesn t make us vital for the survival of the group. Anyone disagree ?

So we made many posts regarding illusions. So they will try to make some change like "change gender", or "wear plate". I DON T CARE !!
This is one time when major changes will be made and we ask for illusions ? what is wrong with you ?!?


I m actually upset because there is a section "GLOBAL MANA REGEN IDEA", that would basically grant clarity to everyone sitting, and thus ripping enchanters of their unique power over mana. Where is everquest going ? why not grant enchanters with a sow while in wolf form ? this is as stupid.
yeah i just said that : giving enchanters a low form of sow is a major class balancing issue and it really shouldnt be considered.
"clarity for sow". this works. it s easy, fast, money free, even mana free. the druid or shaman is happy, and so are you. but if mana regeneration is globaly increased...


I quote : "Alter this so that the over all effect of meding with Clarity is the same". this would be the one most major change in everquest. this would actually would make me start a druid and stop being the enchanter. or a wizard, or a shaman. this would go so fast with a clarity at all times, a shaman would never group but always solo, being WAY faster than anyone at killing a mob.


Complaints about mezzing in kunark and velious ? yeah ok. Verant wanted these zones to be more difficult by preventing some mobs to be mezzed, we complain. We basically say : we can t be the life savers in the group anymore, this is too difficult, it s not fair. why not ? i must say i m only 40 and have met resistance to mezz only in velious. I actually have found it more difficult, making it different than the usual fight. So ok we have to park the wurms, or time the repop really carefully, or use a really good FD puller, or use the lull series, or even use our charm skills. but it can be achieve, it s just more difficult.
Any enchanter can be good at mezzing. take mezz away on some mobs, who will succeed ? the really good group, trained, efficient, where everyone know what he should do ?

I m not saying that those who complained are not good enchanters, i m just saying that this zone was intended to be more difficult, to be different. You need a really good group to succed, which is not the case if the enchanter is mezzing everything. I know that it s frustruating not being able to do crowd control, but this is intended, wanted. You can t mezz wurms ? try charm ! belieive me, it works really well.
A good group whith an enchanter in a normal situation will pull, kill, re pull, med. a great group will chain pull faster, kill faster, earn xp faster. don t you agree ? in velious hunting has been made more difficult, making it almost impossible for the not great group to go in some zones. Is that bad ?


But be releived, i m done writing. I beleive I must be the only enchanter that thinks that class are well balanced as they are, that such a change as sow in a wolf form would ruin the game, that granting clarity to everyone is going to be the doom of the enchanter... and i m pretty sure i m the only one thinking that letting a dual class advance as fast as a single one is one of the most stupid idea that verant had. yeah sure, let the tank + caster SK level as fast at the caster wizard...

I agree with your post. Personnally I find the blending of classes and the blurring of roles by this process of balancing a very worrying development. The sooner balancing is finished with (I strongly doubt that it ever will be) the better I will like it. Quite frankly the way it is headed the class distinctions are becoming so blurred that we are going to end up with a melee class a caster class and a healing class while at the moment we have some degree of distinction.

If you have to "balance" a class then stay within the established role of that class. The best example is the wizard. Wizards are having a relatively tough time at the moment getting parties. This is mainly because all the "balancing" that has occurred for melee types now mean that the average wizards is grossly under powered. Now the way that this can be address is to give wizards a new role. ie blurring the class roles or fundementally increase what they do best which is nuking. Verant do not want to increased the nuking power of wizards and so they will almost certainly blur the roles still more. imho this is a potentially disasterous. This blending of classes is a cop out, making the game too easy and will in the end destroy it.

Verant argue that no one class should be vital to play in any area. Well I disagree. If you enter an area to hunt without a well balanced party you should expect things to be VERY tough.

The issue of unmezzable mobs in Velious is another clear example of a cop out. It is far easier to make all the mobs completely mezz resistant than come up with a good game design to make an area a challenge to change the way people play. Instead of having unmezzable mobs why not have some "linked" mobs in different parts of an area. Most camps or breaks involve fighting two or three mobs at most. However if you had a system where the alarm was raised when you attacked one mob that resulted in agroing another mob half way accross a zone you would have a train of mobs for the chanter to deal with. Any enchanter will tell you 4 or 5 mobs are easy to hold, however if you had 10 now that would be a challenge. This would be a much better way of making an enchanters life hard and much more enjoyable than to just make everything impossible to mezz. All that happens with the latter is that enchanters just become buff boxes which is boring in the extreme. Now I am not saying that no mobs should be completely MR however lets have a bit of vareity please. Please Verant get creative dont just cop out and blur the classes.
