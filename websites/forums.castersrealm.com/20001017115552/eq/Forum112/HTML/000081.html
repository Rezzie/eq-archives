How come everyone is so down on Shadowknights whenever I read the forums here in castersrealm?

Me and my best friend have two SKs each. I don't see it that we are unwanted in a group. As a matter of fact, when people heard me and him were online just last night, we had like 3 groups begging us to come and join them. The funny thing was that we were already in a group and not advertising that we were Looking for a Group.

If SKs are hated as much as what your boards and Allakazam's polls say, then how come it is that both our high lvl and low lvl SKs are desired when we walk into a zone and people hear that we are online?

I don't see the SK as being useless in either their tanking ability or their casting ability. SKs have some of the very best armor suited to the SK character -- possibly better than any other class-specific armor in the game. SKs also have some of the best spells for grouping. Whenever I'm in a group with either my low lvl SK or my high lvl SK, the group prefers that I pull because of my casting ability and my ability to tank the damage while other tanks taunt off me or the enchanter mezzes the mob. By far, both my SKs do more damage than any warrior I have grouped with lately.

Please let people know that SKs are one of the BEST classes in the game with the BEST class specific armor in the game and have some of the MOST useful spells in the game. Hands down, NO-ONE pulls better than an SK once they have reached level 30. Even the level 15 and 22 spells are respectable.

As far as the SK pets go, they aren't meant to tank for you! They are simply to help you do additional damage to the already awesome damage you already do. Use the /pet notaunt command to take the taunt out of your pet and you will hang on to him a much longer time. The pet also serves in giving you a couple of valuable seconds should you find yourself in trouble and need to run for the zone. Simply turn on the taunt using /pet taunt command and then head for the zone. Your pet will surely die, but you will have a head start on mob that is attacking you and you have a chance of escaping that you wouldn't otherwise have in some situations.

In my humble opinion, Shadowknights are one of the very best and definately one of the most fun classes to play in the whole game.
But be warned, SKs are one of the highest difficulty classes and only someone who wants a TRUE challenge should play one. For those of you who want the easy road, go ahead and play a woodelf druid, but don't complain that SKs are useless just because you don't understand how valuable they are or don't want to play a real challenge in your character.
