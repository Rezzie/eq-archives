OK Folks...

Here is a complete spoiler up to the current snag in our quest and that is finding the skin a certain gnome needs out in Butcher to make his ultimate firework, so here goes!

To begin this quest, seek out Solomen in the temple of Solusek Ro. He will be a human wizard in light gray robes. Hail the wizard and ask him what history? Afterward ask him what wizards? Finally, tell him I agree. He will proceed to hand you a note that you must take to Camin. Solomen states he is at the wizards guild but like all good wizards, they tend to drink a lot and he can actually be found on the second floor of the Vastly Deep Inn in Erudin. Hand the note to Camin (and repeat this process until you have at least amiable faction). Once you have amiable faction instead of hailing him, ask him What are you searching for? He will talk about needing funds to complete his research. Give him 1000 plat, and then he will tell you about Arantir Karandor, and to not come back until you have found him. Arantir Karandor can be found in Halas near the shaman's guild. There is an NPC named Dragor (sp) who sells flames of ro. This item is lore / no drop and has 8 charges of lava shield (It's also close to 1000 plat so come prepaired). Take this item to Camin, he will talk a little more and give it back to you. Take this item and give it back to Dragor and voila, Arantir spawns in the NPC's place wearing a dark blue robe. Arantir gives you a ring to give to his wife Challice in the basement of the Paladin Guild in Felwithe. Upon doing this she gives you the ring back and you must bring it back to Arantir. Arantir then asks you if you want to hear his story. Ask about the story and he tells you about the 4 followers of Solusek Ro and how they lost their powers. The first lost his over a lake (This is the Blue Staff from Phinigel Autropuss). The second was lost in a castle to a warrior (Gnarled Staff from Venril Sathir). The third is a gnome. Ask about the gnome and Arantir gives you a note to take to him. The gnome in question here can be found in Butcher Block. The gnome's name is Kandin Firepot at loc of -1500, 2800. He takes the note from Arantir and asks you to bring him some skin and admits to having the brother. Note: the brother is the last of the wizards that solusek ro trained and Kandin has the staff if you bring him the skin he needs.

So the question is now...what's the skin?

go take a look at graffe.com to see the compliation of all those tried.
