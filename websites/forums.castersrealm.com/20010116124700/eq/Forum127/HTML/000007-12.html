That's one possible heuristic function for determining the difficulty of a quest. However, I choose to determine the difficulty based on the items one ends up having to get.

The reason I choose to do this is that in the long run, this is the "difficulty" of the quest. These quests were introduced a few weeks ago. Using my heuristic function, the necro quest and the warrior quest are indeed the two hardest so far. However, as more is revealed about the other two quests they may become harder.
