Either there is something wrong with my system, or maybe EQ. But when my toon sits down, volume is amplified greatly, making that spec pet and Cannibalize VERY annoying.

That's because Verant royally borked EAX a little over a year ago. (EAX stands for environmental audio extensions, a feature of SB Live! cards that allows 360 degree positioning of sounds, as well as ambience effects.) EAX was working beautifully until last October, when Verant introduced a patch that was supposed to "enhance" sound but actually screwed things up so bad, that it's nearly impossible to play with EAX on if you have a decent sound setup. And no, this upcoming patch (that is running on test) does NOT fix any of these problems.

Anyway, since most players are too clueless to notice or unable to articulate the problem (improper positioning), it's not likely Verant will ever fix EAX (i.e., revert the code to the original release code). The only solution to this problem is to TURN OFF the EAX feature of your sound card, by going into "multimedia properties" in control panel and setting hardware acceleration to one notch below full. Be sure to return it to full for other games, because only Verant seems to have trouble coding for the world's most popular sound card.
