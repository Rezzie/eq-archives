Currently level 44, spend my time fighting solo wherever I'm in the mood for with animation, reverse-kiting with someone, or grouped in either DL or LGuk (when I'm in the mood for a dungeon-crawl).

Entrance
Tashania
Discordant Mind/Color Skew
Anarchy/Color Shift
Color Flux
Root
Mesmerize/Tepid Deeds/Invoke Fear/Pillage Enchantment/Mesmerization/Cajoling Whispers
Swap Slot (Clarity)

Entrance is for my long term holds. Typically the pulls are single mezzing, and Entrance does the job.

Tashania goes on anything I plan to cast on. 30 mana to make sure my 100 mana slow doesn't bounce is worth it to me.

Discordant Mind is memmed when solo or partnered. Good solid nuke with horrid recast delay of 8 seconds.

Anarchy is my chaser nuke when using an animation to cover my recast delay, or a finisher when the mob is low on life.

Color Flux is 20 mana to let me get off a mez/root attempt, or the opener to a stun lock.

Root is still my baby... a 30 mana alteration spell that saves my butt and keeps the mob swinging at my pet, stops runners for efficiently than a nuke, casts faster than a mez and helps ensure that naughty tanks don't get me killed when breaking mezzes. Also use sometimes to hold AE mezzes still for a longer duration Entrance.

Mesmerise is most commonly memmed right now as it's a 4th level spell and will cast through any damage I take. I'm also really big on mez-nuking casters like specs and the great Oowomp at the moment.

The swap slot always has Clarity in it, but gets cycled with Brilliance, Radiant Visage, Arch Shielding, Rune III, Strengthen, Shade, Insight, Celerity and Feedback. (All on pages 3 & 4 of spell book).

When not mez-nuking, Mesmerize gets switched to Tepid Deeds to lengthen the pet's life, because Shiftless Deeds is a pile of crap. If a mob takes 60 swings in a minute, Tepid will reduce it to 40. Shiftless will reduce it to 36 swings, use 100 more mana, and take over 2 seconds longer to cast. Color Shift followed with Tepid Deeds is cheaper and prevents more damage.

When reverse-kiting with a partner, Mesmerize swaps out for Invoke Fear.

When in a group, I RARELY have nukes memmed. Discordant Mind and Anarchy become Color Skew and Color Shift. They are in bottom to top order to save me forgetting which one is Color Flux. While Shift/Skew can hold a complete stun-lock, the lock is much more stable with all three spells, and Flux is only 20 more mana in the cycle. This also helps the lock hold better during a bounced stun.

Also in group the 7th slot is pretty varied. In DL with spiders abundant, it usually becomes Pillage Enchantment. If I have a semi- puller getting 4 mobs frequently I'll put AE mez there... but usually it's not needed. AE mez is a pain with all the resists I see, and really if I'm going to use it, Tashania/Flux/Mez would be more effective. If they're going to rush me, might as well be one at a time. In LGuk, I usually just stun lock once and mez the ones that haven't lost life. If my group doesn't have a shaman I'll sometimes get Tepid Deeds memmed, but usually I'm either doing crowd control, or I can just stun-lock one time around for 120 mana (opposed to Tepid for 100), hold the mob still for 17 seconds, and stop a hell of a lot more than 33% of the mobs damage potential.

In groups when I can't use stuns (like various giants) I'll change my stun-line to

Cajoling Whispers
Invisibility
Memory Blur
