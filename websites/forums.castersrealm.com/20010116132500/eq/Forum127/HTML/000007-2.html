Miragul was a very powerfull magic user who went against his teachers and erudite culture and mastered all arts of magic instead of just one. He became an outcast as he quested for knowledge. He was always paranoid that his kin were always trying to track him down and kill him so he moved very frequently and tried to remain hidden from the erudite high council. He found shelter within the caves of everfrost where he quested for knowledge and artifacts from other planes. He lived for many many years until time finally overcame him and he died there in the caves of everfrost. He roams there now as a lich and is an intricate part of the original fiery avenger quest. If you kill the lich he drops Miraguls head. If you go through the steps to get the fiery avenger and return the philactrophy (sp?) to Miragul, it makes him come to life and when killed he drops not only his head, but the robe...both of which you need to turn into to inte akera along with your ghoulbane and soulfire in the plane of sky to obtain your fiery avenger.
