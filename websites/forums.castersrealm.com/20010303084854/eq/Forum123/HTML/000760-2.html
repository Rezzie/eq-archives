"Iksars will have the most AC theoretically if complete with Berserker."

Possibly--I'm not sure if the new Kael quest armor is iksar-wearable.

But EQ isn't a game about "theoretically." It's about what you're likely to see as a realistic paradigm of -- in this case -- the equipment an average warrior will have at level, say, 55. It isn't gonna be full berserker, or full cobalt. It's not gonna be that absolute best obtainable stuff.

Could an iksar have the highest potential AC in-game? Yeah, maybe. How many will? Very, very few.

Also, keep in mind the soft cap at 1100 AC. Past that, it matters almost not at all what your AC is--so your iksar having 1230 AC won't tank a damn site better than my barbarian with 1210, and I'll have a few hundred more hp than you.
