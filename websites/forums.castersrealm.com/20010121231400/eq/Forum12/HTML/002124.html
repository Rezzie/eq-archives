That group could do it easily.

Invis in or fight your way in.
The 51 druid would make it a cinch.. if dire isn't up you can harmony/single pull the blood wolves for xp. If you stay up in that little alcove the only other things you'll have to fight are 2 gargoyles and a named inkye (easy) on 15-20min spawn timers (can't remember) and the occassional wanderer will come up.
None of the wolves, including black dire, are agro so you have plenty of time to med up, harmony, and kill.

Try not to let anything run out of the little alcove or you could have a mess of inkies on you.

Also, the dire is a SK, he will open with harm touch, so you may want to have your cleric invuln the puller for the touch.
If you can land togors on black dire he's a pushover. Some of my guild came with me, couple tanks, 50+ cleric and mage, 50+ druid and some others.. Let's just say it was serious overkill. He died in less than 40 seconds and only one person ever went below half hps, and that was the mage who sent his pet in to take the HT (which doesn't work unless you are far away as he will HT the pet's owner if you're in range.)

Good luck, and congrats in advance on your pelt.

I got mine Sat, and am awaiting the return of the EJ spirit to get my boots.

i (shm 55) killed the black dire with a druid (53) and a ranger (55). a trick for pulling him:


  • bind at the entrance to mistmoore
  • memorize gate
  • invis up and go down to the spot he spawns
  • pull him, via slow, malosi, nuke, whatever - it probably won't stick :P.
  • cast gate

the black dire will still come running. he didn't bring anything with him when i pulled him - but i'm also 11 levels higher than you. he's pretty damn magic resistant (roots lasted about 3 seconds max and i couldn't land a slow). best bet is to have the rogue/bard/warrior beat him up and heal them.

good luck. this wasn't too tough .

oh, and so you're aware: the spirit sentinel isn't spawned in emerald jungle - at least he wasn't as of three days ago. they're fixing some problems with the quest where people weren't getting the boots and the spear of fate. i would expect this to go in on next weeks mega patch.
