In response to the Cha question. From all my testing (what testing?) Low CHA = unresisted fears, and very long lasting fears. On the dn side, high resists on non undead roots.

My testing included buffing my cha to 115 and feariung every green and blue I could find in skyfire. 18 out of 31 were resisted and those that landed lasted nolonger than 18 seconds. When I had 37 cha, only one out of 24 fears were resisted and a fear that lasted 37 seconds. Other fears lasted for a decent time, but only one broke under 5 seconds.

Testing my root theory on root:
Jumped into HS. Pulled Pyre gollems with 115 cha. No numbers worth posting, but a resist was rare. Dropped my cha to 37 and I had several pulls where the beast resisted 4+ times.. Loaded hungry earth, dropped dn to the Skelies and pulled with roots (not a good idead dn there. Resists were almost none. (this testing I forgot to log, but I may do it again just to see the actual numbers.)

In conclusion (not official.. only my experience)

Root + High Cha = low resists
Root + low Cha = high resists
undead Root's + low Cha = low resists and decent holds
(Haven't bothered undead roots with high cha.. I will go back some day and log a complete test)

Fear + High Cha = High resists and sort fears
Fear + low Cha = low resists and long duration

(These tests were no way official, these were for my own well being. They confirmed my suspision, but do as you like and do what makes stuff work best)
