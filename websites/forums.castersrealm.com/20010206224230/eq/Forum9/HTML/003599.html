I have a couple thoughts on this.

At 59 the druid requires more xps to level, so even if you always fight together and you are getting fewer xps, you will be leveling faster than he is.

If the classes are all balanced, then the druid should be contributing more to the group than you are. If the druid was your level, should he get less of the xps than an enchanter? If so, perhaps enchanters should be nerfed.

Another thing to think about is what happens when the shoe is on the other foot. I find that I get much better xps when I am one of the lower levels in the group. If an enchanter gets stuck in a group with lower level people, he may be getting a larger chunk of the xps, but the mobs are dying slower and the healers may not be able to heal you as well. You also run into times when a portion of what you fight is green to you.

One last thing...deal with it. The main reason for the way xps are devided is to encourage people to group with people around their own level and not be penalzied for grouping with lower level folk or get rewarded for joining a group of higher level people.
