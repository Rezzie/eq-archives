As a DE SK, I found I was able to sell to Pardor the Blessed in East Commons. There are two merchant huts just on the green side of the West Freeport Zone line. If you enter the one that FACES freeport you'll find Pardor and all will be good. Stay away from the one that faces North towards Neriak. Rinna Lightspinner (or something close to that) is a merchant that HATES Shadow Knights and will attack you on sight. She hits for about 350 points or so. (Or did last time I crossed her path)

In the Western Common Lands, there is a hut very near the Befallen entrance where you can sell as well. The Merchant is an erudite lady who sells gems.

Honestly though, I believe you do not start out KoS at all in East and West Freeport. You should be dubious there and only KOS in North (because of the Paladins). My advice to you is to kill a bunch of orcs in the commonlands to up your standing with the Paladins if you want to bank in freeport. You can also do a bunch of quests in the rogue guild to up your faction with the Freeport Militia. It takes a long time, and will require some patience and planning as most quests that up your paladin faction will give you a negative hit on Freeport Militia. However, the Rogue guild quests are easy to complete and will give you positive Militia faction without hurting your Paladin standing. For more information about specific quests, you may want to check out everquest.allakhazam.com.

Go TSK all the way!

I have a OSK lvl 18 and I regret it everyday.
Sure Regen might not be AS HELPFUL at lvl 30+ but it still rocks and man does it help at the beginning.

As for bonus points, there are many schools of thought on this one:

1. Max points into INT
This will give you 82 int and will help at lvl 10+ (ALL SKs get a Base 40 mana at lvl 9)

2. Max points into DEX
This is to help VE proc along with DR and eventually Soul Leech. Thats the only thing DEX does for us SKs adds to the nember of times a weapon will proc for you. Helps crits as well but us SKs dont get crits

3. 17 points to AGI, 3 to INT
Considerable increase in AC with AGI at 100. It is a major hump like 75 is.
Also INT will 65 and the mana calculator for hybirds is:
[(INT/5+2)* {Your Level -9}]+40
So at lvl 9 your mana is 40. At lvl 10 with 65 INT your mana would = 55.

4. Max points into STA
Will end up giving you more starting HP (alittle like 2 or something). Also as you lvl it will provide 1 or 2 HP per lvl more, not noticable difference til lvl 45+ and even then really SAD return rate on bonus points invested.

ALL in all many choices on where to place points.
Personally I would go 17 to AGI and 3 to INT.Why?
AC IS YOUR GOD! any ac you can get is GOOD!
even if only helps alittle. If you cant get to 100 AGI then just leave it be, Trolls are over the 75 hump to start with, but we CAN attain 100
Also most of our armor does not add many +AGI stat buffs, but offers lots of +STR, +STA, and +DEX.

Well hope this all helps bro
