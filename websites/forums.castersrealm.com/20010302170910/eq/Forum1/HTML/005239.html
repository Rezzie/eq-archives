You can't just go there and expect people waiting to form one. So a casual player is likely locked out of those zone to just a few ventures there, if time allowed it. And probably he/she belong to an uber guild, chances are better at venturing into those places. Now comes Scars of Velious. The game took a big turn down from playable to nearly unplayable. This new continent is even harder than the last. No newbies allowed! Everything hit like its 20 lvls higher and has hp twice the same lvl mobs in the old continent. People are few in Velious. Getting a group is almost impossible. Exploring solo is even worst. How many of us can say they have visit 50% of the zones in Velious? You can' even teleport to the zones unless you got the soul bond item to go along with it. So far only uber guild visit the zone like they visit the planes. It's very unfair to most players. We all pay the same amount of money. Yet it is almost impossible for us to play in Velious without losing death and start corpse recovery (time wasted), and without being in a group of high lvl people. Zones in Velious is nearly damn unplayable in most part except for a few zones. The risk is just too high. It is also very inconvenient, only two cities (but technically only one, since they have opposing factions). Zones are just too big. I paid for the expansion, but I can't really play there unless I set aside a huge chunk of time to play and pre-organize a group of ppl who can stay just as long. So in the end, Velious is just a new place for the Power gamers to play. As a casual player I am pretty much lucked out. I guess I will have to quit the game now, as I can't go much more than stay around the old continent, doing the same things over and over and seeing the same old places. As I am not a Power gamer nor am I part of an uber guild. I am very mismay at how the game has progress.

A toast to Verant for making a good game go bad. Eventually, all that will be left are power gamers. I can't see myself paying month after month and not be able to do half the stuff others are doing.

The reason they are concentrating more on adding higher level content is that there are now a larger number of people in the high levels. As time passes more and more people will get to those levels. The average level on many of the servers is now over 40. In order to prevent overcrowding in the near future there must be more places for these people to go. In the teens, 20s, and 30s, there are a wide range of options as to where to hunt. When the game was released however, level 40 to 50 plays had few options and soon zones oriented toward those levels became very overcrowded.

With Kunark, they added a nice broad range of zones but they greatly increased the number of zones available for the 40 to 50 crowd. The 51 and over group also was given several places to hunt. There were not that many at the time so the new zones on Kunark were plenty. As the game aged more people started getting into the 51 and up range however and the Kunark zones designed for them began to get crowded.

Velious was introduced largely to deal with that aspect. Yes, there is stuff you can hunt in iceclad in the high 20s. Yes, there are a number of zones that are oriented toward the 35 to 50 crowd (and those places can be a lot of fun btw). But, the meat of Velious is for those 50 and above . The biggest zone crowding issues in the future will be in that level range and Verant needed content to spread these people out. In addition to adding some single group content they also added more raid level areas. I am not sure if you are aware but on most servers a guild must reserve a spot in the planes or for killing a dragon sometimes more then a month or two ahead of time. By adding more raid level encounters it will help shorten the waiting time on many of these places.
