Dragons too hard??

Frankly they are all too easy. What EQ lacks is real risk. Big deal you die, you lose a little exp and time getting back corpse.

I say make it like we had in the first generation MPG games pre-internet such as Island of kesmai and Drakkar. Make em eaters. You die, your ate, back to Level 1.

Then you will really have to plan and excute the hunt or pay dearly for the lack of. Trust me, when you know that everything is at risk, then it feels like you are truly fighting for your own life. Death is far too painless in this game.

You started over. either your a player or your not. if you cannot beat the wimpy dragons in this game.....well maybe you dont really know how to fight.

The dragon in dreadlands does NOT meet any of these conditions that the giants meet, and therefore is a completely DIFFERENT situation.

2. Hunting in Oasis usually occurs from level 12-19 or so. West Commons is mostly 7-14. West Karanas is 10-15 or so. You die to a Giant, you shrug it off, and you get all your XP back in less than 15 minutes.

the dragon in dreadlands will kill level 60s the same way that giants kill newbies. Only difference is that from about 45+, it will take them over 1 1/2 hours to make their experience back from that death unless they can convince a cleric to drop by the new "zone o death" and rez them before the dragon ganks the cleric as well.

Do they honestly think that higher level characters are going to kill tougher creatures and risk more experience off their main character just to get an item that any one of their twinks can get much more easily off of Vox/Nagafen?
