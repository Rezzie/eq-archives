Rogues are one of the most group dependant classes. To solo is to commit suicide. I know you don't want to hear it, but too bad. Get in a group with a healer and a Meat Shield. Get behind the mob and poke the crap out of it. If it turns around to face you, turn off atttack and hit HIDE. Make it so the mob stays on the meat shield, they have ALOT more hp than you, why should you tank? (Oh yeah, pickpocket the mob too. There is never a time when you shouldn't pickpocket!)

After you max the skill (which will take a while!) you only have 5 new points to gain in the skill from that new level, and they will come very quickly...as long as you are in a group and keep stabbing. Save the rest of your Guild Skill Points for big things like Make Poison, Instill Doubt, and Backstab. Those 3 go up kinda slow, so when you finally reach the level to get them, you should have 20-30 skill points to dump into it.

Rogues are not monks. If we had FD, we'd be monks. Get in a group.

Even at 27, which I made just last night (WOOT!), I still miss alot with Backstab and only occasionally do max damage. I need better STR items to get Max damage. With Skill and STR, your backstab will become deadly. There is something about the feeling you get when you backstab and kill a fleeing mob for 90 some points and keep him from bringing any friends to the party.

(Side note: Fear Kiting helps raise backstab, too...so make friends with a necro)

"I'm getting my rump kicked! Wait, there is a cliff just over there! I can jump off it using my Safe Fall and try to zone. The mob probably won't follow me over the cliff, he'll take the long way to get to me." (Example: Gnolls in Highpass)

"Crap, this mob has a dagger, it's probably Fine Steel. I'll try to Disarm him so he isn't using a low delay weapon. His fists are about 30 delay and that dagger is, what...19? He won't be hitting quite as often then."

Simple logic. So few players have a grasp of simple logic.

Back to the topic of the post: Backstab. You gotta get into a situation where you can use BS alot. In other words, Group.

Skills that get used ALOT like Hide, Sneak, Double Attack, etc. will go up in time with frequent use. Backstab is the same way, it'll go up faster if you use it.
