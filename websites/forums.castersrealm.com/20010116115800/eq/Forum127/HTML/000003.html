He will give you a coin and an undead DE reaver will immediatly spawn and attack.He is blue to 53. Now, head back to BW and Give the coin to Darkforest. He will give it back and send you in search of Althelle. She is in EK along the river near the barb village. Give her the coin. You get a necklace and tell you to find Sionae. BTW at this point you need to have a cabable group of 50+ with you, all hell is about to break loose Go find Sionae and give her the neclace. She tells you to go find Nuien and begings to walk towad the gather. Go find him, he should be wandering around the nearby too. Give him the necklace. He tells you to find Teloa and begins walking toward the gathering. Find her give her the necklace. She will begin walking toword the gathering. Once she arrives after a min or too they will all begin casting. Ready your group. As soon as they start casting Innoruuk will summon undead De SK reavers with pets and a DE casting corruptor. You must stop them before they reach the gatering and loot the DE corruptor. He has a fleshbound tome. I believe that one third of the quest is now complete. There is a Withered Treant in NK that is looking for 3 corrupted items. I believe that this tome is the first.
