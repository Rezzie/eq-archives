1. "Lost in a raid on FV." Guards will tell you goblins, Drolvargs, and Pirates of Gunthak raided FV. I went with pirates.

2. Penned by elves. I figured the location has to do with elves somehow. Also, the defenders of FV (scouts, captains, etc) are all WOOD elves.

3. The scroll protector was missing along with the scroll itself. This could be a number of things: protector died, captured, or was a traitor. The traitor thing seemed plausible and a new twist if true. Raid happens, and the protector takes the opportunity to run off with a valuable item and make it look like a kidnapping or killing.

4. The only known Pirates of Gunthak (of importance) are in OOT - the 3 for the Bracer of Erolisi quest across from the Sister Isle.

5. The "boss" sister Styria is a wood elf, and has always killed players she catches fighting Pirate of Gunthak faction on her isle. No one knows why, and Verant has never "fixed" that, despite people pointing it out since beta. Some would say she's actually a pirate...

7. Did I mention her last name? Fearnon. "I fear it might be lost for good..."
