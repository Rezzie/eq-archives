Quest so far:

New info in the following..

You get the book, "Immortals" in skyfire... Give the book to the eurdite bookeeper in Qeynos, he gives you a note, give the note and a robe of the whistling fist to Leaho, he gives you the Book of Celestial Fists, give the celestial fists book to Kerian, he attacks, feign him off and his true form spawns(this info was witheld for a while, by some monks who wanted to try to further the quest then tell alot of info at once, but they finally gave in and asked for help) after his true form spawns you ask him What about Leaho, and he says he doesn't know him, but that doesn't matter, because you have the book, he tells you to kill the weakest of the 4 elements and bring him proof, do to Lavastorm and hail a new NPC, low lvl imp called a "Lava Spirit" then say "I wish to challange Eejag" wish spawns Eejag.

Eejag is a level 55 Iksar that swims in lava near the fire pit by the soleseks and under the middle smoke half way down. He WILL NOT come out of the lava, so you must fight him IN THE LAVA. When killed you bring a scale he drops the Leaho, Leaho then tells you to bring the scale to the Air master.

The Air master is 'a presence' in sky. When you give him this he tells you he is obligated to avenge his little bro's death, and tell's you to prepare, he then despawns and respawns(in the same spot) as a human monk named Gwan.

Tell Gwan you are ready, kill him, and get 'essence of air' do NOT, however, give 'essence of air' to Kerian, he eats it, you need to find(presumably) the next element, wich Gwan tells us who it is upon his death:

[Fri Oct 06 10:21:56 200 Gwan's corpse lets out one last, frustrated breath and gasps, 'Trunt! Prepare yourself!'


this is where were at, we havn't found trunt yet

but whatever you do, DO NOT HAND THE CELESTIAL FISTS BOOK INTO KERIANS TRUE FORM! IT WILL POOF!
