I'm going to post a detailed summary of the quest so far since I haven't seen one here yet.
The quest consists of five parts - you need to get the scabbard, 2 hilts, and 2 blades.

The scabbard quest starts with Kargek Redblade in EFP - he is on a ledge OVER the recessed area that leads to the SK guild. Hail and talk to him and he gives you a letter to take to his friend (Oknoggin or some such) in Feerott. Oknoggin is standing by one of the bridges, give him the letter and he gives you an item to return to Kargek. Kargek then gives you a book to take to his brother Telan in East Karana at the end of the path below the ramp. Give the book to Telan and he gives you a totem, and asks you to return the totem with a Heart of Ice from a goblin wizard in Permafrost. Return those, and he gives you a second totem and asks you to return that with red and green scales of the children of Veeshan (dragons). It's confirmed that the green scale drops off Severilous in EJ.

The first hilt is found in Lake Rathetear on the bottom of the lake directly below the aviak platform. Find that and take it to Wenden Blackhammer (who stands right next to Kargek Redblade in EFP). Ask him about the hilt, tell him you need one repaired, and give him the name of the hilt and he will tell you what he needs to repair it - in this case 3 gems. If you listened to the bard (Fellonious whatever in EK) you know the swords had diamonds, Black sapphires and jacinths on their hilts - give Wenden the hilt and one of each of these and he will return the repaired hilt to you.

The second hilt is in Timorous Deep, on the island with the chessboard, it spawns at the base of one of the chess pieces. Take this also to Wenden and ask him about it by name and he will tell you he needs 2 types of metal - golem metal and rejesiam ore. Ask him about Rejesiam ore and he tells you of a giant in FM who sells him the ore. Go to FM and talk to this giant (at the end of the mine under the giant fort) and he tells you he will give you the ore if you get him a monocle. Monocles are rumored to drop off mountain giant patriarchs in Dreadlands, I killed one last night but it didn't have one. The golem metal, Wenden says is too strong to come from this plane - and since the patch Fright and Terror in the plane of fear have been dropping what is believed to be the metal you need.

The first blade quest is in OOT - there is a dwarf on Aviak island who has it, he will sell it to you in return for 2 rebreathers, a keg of vox tail ale and a block of permafrost. Permafrost drops off ice giants IN perma (not outside in everfrost). Rumor has it the ale can be bought from a merchant for 1kpp but I don't know which or if this is true.

No one seems to know where the second blade quest is found, it's guessed to be in Kunark (because the 1st blade and 1st hilt were in the old world, and the 2nd hilt was in kunark (timorous)).
