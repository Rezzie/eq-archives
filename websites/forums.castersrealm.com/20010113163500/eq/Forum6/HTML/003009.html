I've read your type of complaints before...and frankly it always surprises me.

Mages are one of the most powerful PVE classes in the game.

Post 55...our pets are definitely more powerful than 49 pets...I'm not sure why you dont see any difference.

Summon a 49 earth pet with a fire shield and send it against a forest giant arbor.
Then use a 51 earth elemental. The 51 pet can solo an arbor without breaking a sweat...the 49 pet is hard-pressed to survive.

The 54 water pet backstabs!! How is that not noticeable? Just tell your main tank to make sure that he's always on the oppositted side of your pet...and watch your pet do dmg! I've seen water pets able to taunt a mob from a 56 warrior with backstabs.

Ok..I'll grant you that the wizard fire pets aren't as good as the 49 ones generally...but I've found them to be pretty cool in duels heh.

The air pets stun a lot more against higher level mobs than the level 49 ones.

And that's just the pets.....Call of the Hero has turned our class into a must for deep dungeon raids to cycle people in and out. Before CoH if you were late for a raid you were out of luck...now all you have to do is get the mage to summon you in.

We get an updgraded dmg shield...sow for our pets...and a haste buff for pets in general (item you hand to other people's pets).

With Velious we're getting an additional line of summonings (summon monster), a pair of DOTs, and an item summon that is currently still unknown.

I've never regretted making my mage. When I want to solo...I can solo. And when I want a group...I do not usually have to shout more than once or twice before I find one. Mages will never be what warriors, enchanters, clerics, or bards are to a group....but a mage can be added to ANY single group and always be seen as a powerful asset.

I could write more...but I'm sure your mind is made up and nothing I could say could get you back in the game.
