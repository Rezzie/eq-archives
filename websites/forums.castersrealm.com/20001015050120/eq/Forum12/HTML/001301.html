I would SO love to have a Bear Form.

I think Verant's twisted reason for not giving it to us is this:

If they give us Bear Form, class jealousy WILL result. They believe, and probably rightly so, that the biggest reason why the Druid populations are so huge is because of Wolf Form. I mean, let's face it, Druids are an amazingly attractive class to play, and then they go throw Wolf Form on top of it. You look unique, you run fast, you have ultravision, and you have increased attack rating. No other class gets that.

For awhile, a Druid could go into just about any dungeon or town unmolested. Notice how they fixed that? The reason is, DRUID-MANIA had reached an all-time high and really showed no signs of declining. Melee class after melee class, tired of running through 6 zones at a snail's pace got tired of the same old grind and started Druids for a much-welcome breath of fresh air.

Now, let's look at Shaman. For a long time, there were barely ANY SHAMAN AT ALL! Shortly after the Totemic quests were implemented, more and more Shaman start popping up everywhere. Totemic looks cool. Shortly after this, Shaman could use spears. Spears look cool on Shaman. A spear-wielding casting class is UNIQUE. All this, combined with the word getting out that Shaman "rock" at higher levels, caused an explosion in Shaman populations across most servers.

I believe Verant thinks that if they give us Bear Form, it's going to further destablize class population proportions. Of course, I say screw it, give us Bear Form! Now that I am level 45 I really can see that there's not much unique about a Shaman. We do look pretty cool with our big bad 2h blunts, spears, and plate armor, but as a nature-based priest class we ought to be able to change our form at will.

Ah well, enough ranting... it's off my chest. Time to start banging my way through 45...
