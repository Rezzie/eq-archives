That whole line of thought that Barbarians are lousy rogues is just WAY off base. They end up with STR, STA, DEX, and AGI at or over 100. Now certainly Verant doesn't want stats don't mean a whole bunch in melee, they are specifically trying to avoid having a BEST race for a class. Anyway for a rogue STR greatly increases the damage done by backstabs. Yes there are lots of easily obtainable items to up your STR, but barbarians can use these as well..read STILL higher STR. STA translates to HP. I will reiterate that my wifes Barb rogue has more HP than 'little' rogues of higher levels. DEX almost unimportant. It helps training of weapon skills(rogue specific skills as well), and improves the chance that a procing weappon will proc. AGI translates to improved AC, and speeds the training of avoidance skills like Dodge.


A rogues role is to inflict as much damage as possible. So high STR is obviously important as it increases BackStab damage. As a result of the rogues role..they also get beat on regularly(though less with the advent of evade) so AC(AGI) and yes HitPoints(STA) are important. DEX is well pretty well meaningless unless the rogue is wielding a procing weapon.

With this in mind it seems obvious to me that the best statistical races for a rogue are Barbarian and Dwarf. Sure you can have really high AGI as a halfling, but the difference in the AC bonus between 100 and 125 AGI is small, much smaller than the damage bonus to backstabs from 20 points of STR. The halfling rogue does get a BIG plus by getting the experience bonus, but they don't statistically compare to the barbarians and dwarves.

Oh and the speed points thing is utterly bunk. In Beta, and I believe the first week or so of release AGI was directly tied to running speed, but that was quickly changed as running speed was so important. This is another example of Verant minimizing stats to prevent a uber race from emerging.
