If they say a drop, you wait in line for them to get their stuff. The best solution is if you negotiate a deal, such as lower lvls can get exp if they give the upper lvl what they came for. I have done this in Crushbone before and it really works. However, nothing like this can ever happen if people don't talk it over beforehand. The main purpose of this, in my opinion, is to ovoid anyone complaining about a KS. It should really never happen. If you talk it over before the spawn then it can be resolved before people start acting like idiots. Besides that, first come first served should always rule, meaning individuals and not groups handing off to other players. Of course you should be able to call in a GM if people are not sharing after a good amount of time, but besides that FIRST COME FIRST SERVED.

Hope my opinion is shared by some of you too. And if you hate me for what I have said, remember, Im a newbie and bashing newbies is NOT cool
