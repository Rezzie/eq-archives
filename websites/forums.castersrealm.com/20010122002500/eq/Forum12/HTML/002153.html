I pleveled a druid (for ports) on a second account of mine with my 54 Shaman to 34 in 5 days played which included some time messing around.

There is no reason you cant plevel a rogue (you can buff thier dex for learning skills such as backstab faster than normal). Melee classes I can see doing much faster, maybe 30 levels in 2 days or less. With a rogues insane damage output it would take no time to demolish mobs.

The reason I can see an enchanter not being the best is because they are totally spell dependant and would have down time between battles because they would have to kill mobs with spells. I would agree that with two players pleveling one, that an enchanter would be the best to have as the second class for the mem wipe thing.

With my druid, I simply pulled with my shaman (bash), ran back to druid, root mob and get close (mobs when rooted attack closest opponent), let the druid cast a dot and beat on it till its half life, then I finish the mob off with melee. Very easy up to 34. With just chloro on and the mob beating on me the whole time my life was full after each battle, mobs couldnt outdamage the regen. The only spell I used was root, and several buffs for the druid (dex and speed) and therefore my mana was always almost max, creating very little or no down time between mobs.

So, with only one pleveler, I think the shaman would be the best, druids second and possibly chanters next.
