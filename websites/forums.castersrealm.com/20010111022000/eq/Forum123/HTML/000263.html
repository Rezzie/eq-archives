That wasn't the question. The question was, which would be a better tank?

Same skill level, same equipment, I'd place my money on the ogre based almost exclusively on the Slam and the immunity from combat stuns. Slam = less damage from the mob = better tank. Immune to stuns = more damage on the mob = better tank.

And of course we are talking only about tanking ability. Ogres aren't going to be as good of a dungeon puller as a wood elf tank. In confined spaces an ogre gets in the way of the other melee types.

But in the end, play the one that attracts you more.
