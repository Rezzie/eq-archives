Best way to deal with casters is to have a good line up of spells. Use basic Stun instead of other stuns, as it only checks for resists against the stun, not a stun and then dmg. Use root, if they cant move, you can run in and hit them, and hope to interrupt. Blind, though not preventing them from casting, will still confuse them, and you can easily step out of casting range and med and heal for a sec while the blind lasts. Fear, if they have a pet, fear it, pet gone. Resist buffs, we have them for a reason, and though you may not see many outright resists, it will lessen the dmg taken, and duration of root type spells. Cancel magic, were not the only ones with buffs, knock out theirs with this line and it will heal greatly, esp to get rid of those annying resist buffs ) And of course your heals and nukes. Throw in a nuke as often as you can, without letting your hps drop below 2 bubbles to be safe.
Unfortunatly, when we encounter a tank type, things get a bit hairy. If the tank is alone its not too hard, as long as you can get a nuke off and have some cover to loose them behind. As tanks cant heal, they will die, even if you have to med for a bit and then go back and nuke some more. This is presuming they cant find you while you med, and that they arent punding your azz while youre tring to nuke them. Oh, and it wont work against pallys, those sick bastards.
