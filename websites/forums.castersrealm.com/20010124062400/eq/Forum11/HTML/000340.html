I've not seen a definitive list of zones where wolfform drasticaly alters faction, but from memory here are some in no particular order:-

  • Everfrost. The barbarians don't like it, so don't do it
  • Dreadlands. The dragon here hates most illusions, including wolfform - definitely don't do it, she has a habit of creeping up on you and wiping out you and everybody around you (she has mostly AoE spells).
  • Firiona Vie. Avoid wolf here, virtually all the NPC's hate it and will kick your butt.
  • Overthere. Avoid putting Share Wolfform (lvl 39) on evil races, as the dragoons will become KOS to them, as a druid they'll kill *you* anyway
  • Kerra Isle. Cats'n'dogs don't mix

I've used wolf in most zones, Freeport, Commonlands (orcs con indiff), Ro, Oasis, Karanas, Rathe Mounts, Lake Rathe, Qeynos, Butcherblock, Faydark (brownies con indiff in Lower), Kelethin, Qeynos, Steamfont, Innothule, Feerott, Lake of Ill Omen, Frontier Mountains, Burning Wood, WW, Emerald Jungle, Karnors Castle - no problems encountered so far.

If your in a new zone, don't wolf unless you are a good distance away from NPC's then con from afar - most things will just be indifferent.
