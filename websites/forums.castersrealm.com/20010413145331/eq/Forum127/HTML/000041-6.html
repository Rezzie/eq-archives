Od:

This is from memory, but I have a good memory, so it's accurate. =)

Although a longer route to the Vessel, this way isn't as confusing. Start by going into the teleporter in the Shaman guildhall. Once you're up top, turn LEFT and go into the tower. (this is the RIGHT hand tower if you were to face the entrance of guildhall from ground). Drop down all the ladders, and go down the hall straight, past the iksar NPC, turn right into the "brick" hall. Follow this around to the pit. Drop into the pit, go into the adjoining cave, and into the secret wall on the left hand side. Follow this tunnel around to some stairs on your right, go up them, and look right; you'll see a pit. Go into the secret wall BEFORE the pit, left hand side, and skirt around the pit. Continue on down this hall until you come into a small room, the beginning of the 1-way-wall maze area. Prep in this room, then go out into the maze, take your first right, a left, then he's inside the room on your right.

Hope that helps and doesn't confuse you more. I had to scout it out way in advance so that when my guys were ready, I could lead them there quickly and without any incidents or guard attacks.

The way we got to Vessel -

Zone in from FoB using the right tunnel ( not the left one ).

Run straight past the 2 troopers, then go through the hole on the right ( the one w/ the FoB sign ), then turn left. ( Basically puts you into the city instead of that rocky tunnel area ).

Run straight out from there, you'll see water and a bridge that leads off to your right. The walkway makes you turn left, else you'll dump into the water. There are usually 1 or 2 Crusader's here. When we did, one crusader saw invis and we ended up having to kill both of them. They have *godly* HP. It took us 10 to 15 minutes to kill them, could not touch them with a single spell.

Anyways, there's a stairway leading down to the water right there. Jump off it, turn around and face the stair way and swim under it.

It will force you to make a right turn, then a left, another right, and another left.

You'll see an opening in the water above you, jump up ( using the method about looking straight up and pushing forward, took some of my group awhile to get up there. )

The only way to go from here is a small tunnel.

Go straight through the tunnel till it makes you go left or right. Turn right, it'll force you to turn left and put you into an octoganal room. If you look to the right, you'll see Vessel.

The first time, our cleric went afk w/out telling us, so it turned into a disaster.

Second time, with 9 people, we toasted him with only 1 death.

Now onto raiding Chardok...
