First off, I'm giving 90% of the credit for this to the original "Trakanon/transparent" UI, since that's what this UI gets most of it's ideas from in the first place.

That said, it's got a few good nice touches that improve upon the original, particularly the inventory/persona screens being merged into this style of interface. And it would be nice to see, at a glance, a SoW vs. GhoulRoot by the little red outline on negative spells.

It ALL needs to be transparent though. This version uses way too much screen real estate not to be transparent. In fact, we need to simply do away with the concept of a non-transparent screen all together. Make the persona, inventory, etc. on a transparent background (with a transparency slider to adjust transparency, as was suggested by the creator of the original UI).
