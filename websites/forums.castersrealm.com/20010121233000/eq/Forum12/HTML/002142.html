I dont think you can get a snowball realistically at your level solo. You will need a group or a raid. Three places I have gotten the snowball are Archeologist, Preacher and Alchemist.

I recommend the Preacher as I have seen the wizard mostly in this room, on the stage. You can hold the stage by just taking the three that spawn there. Make sure there are no runners by rooting and rooting and rooting, even when you dont need to root, reroot.

I have seen the snowball drop from wizards I would say 3 out of every 4, but may have just been lucky. I wouldnt recommend soloing any of these areas till 34, not that it cant be done earlier, just too risky IMO.

Perma can be very dangerous and tricky and even in my low 40's had to gate out on a couple of occasions. If you can get by without a pet (obviously 34+) I would recommend it.

If you can find out who is camping these spawns, you can also offer to help by sitting in and healing or buffing or whatever. Could be boring, but its one way to get it early. Just invis and run to the room, but dont train them, die if necessary and ALWAYS keep gate memmed here.
