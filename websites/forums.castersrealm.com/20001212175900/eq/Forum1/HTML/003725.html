After playing the wonderful game of Everquest and experiencing secondhandedly both praise and ridicule about the quality of the game and Verant's attempts to attempt any and all problems, I have thought of a possible solution that will both please the populous and decrease the amount of work Verant would have on its hands. I propose that each customer be offered one of three options whenever they start an Everquest account: Cheap, Favorable, or Snobby. The cheap option would be similar to the current Everquest system, where you start with nothing and have to earn everything you get. The favorable option would allow each player to start their characters at level 10 with about 100pp. Casters would have all spells that they have up to this point already scribed and memmed and have their casting skills maxed for the level, plus a reasonable robe. Melee classes should have fair armor and have all of their bash, kick, taunt, and weapon skills up to max for the level. The snobby option would allow customers to start characters at level 70 (Yeah, I know you can only currently achieve level 60, but I am sure that can be raised specifically for the snobby customers.) with all of the best items, and more plat pieces than they could spend in a year. Snobby characters would have every spell scribed and memmed, and every ability would be at its cap. Of course, each of these option would cost a different amount. Customers who chose the cheap option should have their monthly cost decreased to about $4.50 per month. Any customers who want the favorable option should have a monthly payment of about $20 or so. Snobby customers should be required to pay somewhere in the range of $100 per month (Hey, I don't own the rights to the program. I am sure you could make more!!!!) I am pretty sure that this will be a solution to everyone's problem with the game. The cheap option allows players who like to earn what they have and complain little about the game do so will little worry about monitary issues. The favorable option will allow customers who want everything free but just can't afford a more expensive option at least not have to worry about early leveling and sitting in the East Commons shouting that they want to buy bronze. The snobby option allows customers who already have everything also have everything in the game, and besides, what is the hurt in appeasing the rich and obsessive customers.

(If you haven't noticed that this is severely satirical, well, now you know. I am throughly tired with players complaining about what they have when the people at EQ charge us a nominal price to play a rather advanced game that is constantly being improved just to keep idiots from complaining more. Verant does everything it can to keep us happy, whether it be releasing new expansions or providing us with a wonderous goal of receiving an epic item that is so powerful that it requires many hours and days of questing to acquire. Sure, Verant makes a heck of a lot of money off of us, but don't we get a wonderful return. If you aren't happy with the game, quit playing and save your money to go to Europe or something and entertain yourself their. If you do, quit complaining unless you have a reasonable complaint. Their will be reasonable complaints, but from Abashi's responses, it is hard for him to see what needs to fixed for all the crap you all put in the way. They work hard for us. And you still complain.
