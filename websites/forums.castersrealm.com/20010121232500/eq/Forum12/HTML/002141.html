Well it's true that we exchange HP into mana, keep in mind that while plat fire opal rings gives you 55HP, it gets converted into 27 mana because of Canabalise. Only good part is the 5AC.

So I think the question is at what level are you because at my level, 6 Wis is 54 mana.

Some say that it does matter how much mana you have because they are pulling non-stop and so never full mana. This is flawed because they are never full HP either... So why not have a bigger pool of mana.

So I try to balance those 3 attributes that define us as shaman. They are AC & Wis and HP. Don't forget, we are "jack-of-all-trade-but-master-of-none" so we need to be efficient in multitudes of roles. We can fill in any roll for a short periode of time.

My experience has showed me that a well balanced shaman will have the upper hand.
