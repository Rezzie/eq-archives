the focus items you can attempt to attain are from the temple of solesek ro located in lavastorm mountains. these items are quested for and you need the following items to get them:

In dungeon of Najena drop the following items from various mobs but mostly the magicians with in this zone
A Broom
A Torch
A Stein
A Shovel

you will also need variou "giant toes" to complete the quest, the best spot i know if is gornit in ocean of tears on the seafury island, he will drop Hill giant toes, Ice giant toes, and fire giant toes, you might get lucky and be able to purchase these toes from the vendor on the sister isle if your a good race. be advised, that gornit is a Hill giant im guessing in mid to high 30s in range. You also need to get cyclops toes, you can get them in rathe mountains or from OOT off the seafuries, if you hang in OOT at Seafury island im sure some kind high level might let you loot the toes.

Then you need the following additional items:
Gargoyle Eye (mistmoore gargoyles and OOT specter island has gargoyles)

Griffon feather ( i got mine off a griffawn, you might get lucky, head to North karanas)

mermaid scale (this is tricky, get on boat in butcher block mountains, as it zones into ocean of tears you will pass some rocks that will have Sirens on them, they look like mermaids with orange tails, kill them, they drop it quite common)

Fire drake scale (in lavastorm mountains off the fire drakes, took me about 10 kills to get mine)

you will also need some high level gems
Saphire and ruby im preaty sure of, i also belive that Emrald and star ruby are the other two but not sure.

you can go to Allakazams web page via a link here in casters realm and it will have it detailed out exactly what you need and whom to turn it into.

Basically when you have them EQUIPED and summon a pet, the summoned pet is much more powerful then if you did not have the item.

How i got my focus item.

lvl 16 someone gave me a shovel.

lvl 29 camped star room in Najena and got broom, and torch.

lvl 31 camped the crocs in CT and got my stein.

This part of it is a true pain in the a$$ and takes hours literally. after this its easy.

All gems are available in jewler stores. I bought all mine in FV just cause that is where i happened to be but you can also buy them in FP. Other then that don't know but i am a good race with good charisma (HIE). Saphire was 116pp, Fire emerald was 80pp, Ruby was 140pp, Star Ruby was 90pp if i remember. all prices approximate. So get about 400pp for all the gems.

All toes can be bought skip the giant in OOT that you can't fight yet yourself anyway. Skip running around all zones asking to buy, simply buy them at merchants for about 10pp each. Ice giant toes can be bought from Merchants on Halas ramp. Hill Giant toes and Cyclops toes can be bought from merchants at the inns or gypsie camp in Rathe Mountains. Hill giant toes also available sometimes from the gnomes in EFP. Fire Giant toes can be purchased SOMETIMES from the druid ring in Lavastorm. Otherwise just stick your head in SolB (i think b if iremember correctly) and do an ooc for some, should be np. if not go to your servers homepage where raids are scheduled and watch for a Naggy raid. Toes are always dropped off the giants during those but usually decompose cause nobody on the raid will need them. Can get them then, just have someone hold them for you. ppl are cool usually when you tell them its for focus item.

Griffon feathers can be purchased in EC at the spell components lady sometimes, if not the gypsies in NK have them all the time.

Gargoyle eye bought usually in OOT sister isle merchant.

mermaid scale. Unbelieveable number of merchants sell these. again check the spell component lady in EC. I jumped off the boat at mermaids in OOT from BB killed 2 got one from second, gated to docks and took boat again to FP, but since have seen them literally everywhere for purchase, just look around as you travel.

Fire drake scale i didnt see to buy anywhere. Can either save one from when you are hunting in zone at lvl 12 or just use your pets to bash the tar outta the baddies. hehe its great. Fire drake approx lvl 12-15. your pet post 20 owns em. my 29fire pet had drake for breakfast while i sat and watched hehe was great.

Hope this helps, the focus items make a HUGE difference in your pets. I mean HUUUUGE. Must have items even if you do no other mage quests. safe travels!
