OK, here are the abc's to kill Motte:

Have gather shadows on and enter the tavern. As you look at the bar, you will see the only opening to your left. Straight through that door is an npc next to a table. That is the placeholder for Motte. Sometimes it's the tumpy tonic guy, sometimes a dark elf female and other times a barbarian.

Go through the doorway and hang a left. There is a hallway here. If you will sit or stand with gather shadows in this corner you can watch placeholder easily. Many times I was not invis and nobody aggro'd on me. From here, root the npc, envenom bolt, and keep rooting if u feel it's necessary. If npc calls for help, then use a fear spell. Npc will die fast, loot it and go back and wait. Spawn is 6 minutes or so. When Motte spawns, use root, envenom bolt and invoke fear. If he is feared he will not lay hands or heal. Keep him rooted. This is really easy people. If you can't use your skills to accomplish this, then you might as well stop here because you're not deserving enough to finish the quest. :-P LOL, safe hunting.
