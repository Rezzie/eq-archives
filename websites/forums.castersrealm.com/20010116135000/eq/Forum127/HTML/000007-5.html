1. head to Nektulos Forest and find the female NPC by the river loc -1000 -700

2. head to Lake Rathe and find Kazen Facae loc 340 -1600

3. Kill Sir Edwin Motte (level 33 paladin). He is easiest to catch in tavern by the docks in EFp where he is on a rotation with the bard and some others

4. Bring head of Motte to Kazen and recieve a symbol

5. Bring symbol to the chick in Nektulos Forest

6. Go to Najena and camp FBR and when you get it go to Nektulos Forest and give FBR to chick and get a symbol and some moss

7. Give moss and symbol to Kazens apprentice in Lake Rathe then say symbol of the serpent

8. go to Swamp of No Hope and find Ssessthrass loc 3800 1200 give him symbol (the one you get from Kazens apprentice)

9. go to Chardok and go DEEP inside, near the prince (btw, prince's head is needed for enchanter quest) and kill the royal herbalist for the herb on him. (about 3-4 groups will be needed for this, including a high level rogue to lockpick)

10. return the herb to ssessthrass and get the refined herb

11. turn in the refined herb to Kazen's apprentice and get the symbol of testing

12. hand the symbol of testing to Kazen and a huge fight will break out, first a lvl 55 golem, that is 100% magic resistant, He drops a nice earring. Immediately after, a weaker MoB will spawn that can be hit by magic, he drops some rings. Then a final MoB will spawn, also lvl 55, drops the symbol of insanity

13. take the symbol of insanity to some guy in Timorous deep

no one is really clear on what to do next, they are all very vauge about the source, but the word on the street is (like 101 posts before this) you need the slime blood of cazic (crappy item imho, could probably buy it for 15k plat) eye of innoruuk, and the cloak of spiroc feathers from the plane of sky.

I dont know either, so please post the text so we arent looting god loot cause it is a hunch from some riddle den man gives you. Happy questing!
