For me, at level 38, and understanding full well the greivances of the high level shammies, I merely want to get my level 49 buff spells and then power level my friends twinkies to high levels. I also like going with a high level wizzie friend of mine to places like Nurga, Droga, CoM, and the Ill Omen forts. It is fun to go inside and take on the bosses with just one of your exploring pals. We feel like we are 15 again and sneaking around an abandoned building or house. It has that same feel of "what if get in trouble here, who would know and who would help us?" That creates the excitement that makes you shake when casting on the Boss in his throne room with just you, rover, and your nuking buddy. Sure they are all green except the Boss, but let me assure you, 15 high greens and one blue Boss attacking a shaman and a wizard in an near empty zone is quite tense!

Find a friend that is tired of the exp camping and go into the remotest zones in the game just to see how much trouble you can get into! It is a blast.
