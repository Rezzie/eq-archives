I know exactly how you feel about having to be around twinkies all the time. I don't like them much either (but we all have to put up with them). I personally have had to scratch and scrounge up all the money I have gotten, but I have (lucky enough) gotten quite a good chunk of change from being frugal about my ways. First of all, If I were in your shoes (which I was for quite some time) I would head to Misty Thicket (provided you are a good race). While at Misty hunt the goblins at the camp, they supply with almost all of youir starting needs (weapons,cloths,backpacks,bandages) plus Mooto sometime will drop some magical thingy that gives +3 STA. The entire time also make sure to hoard Runnyeye Warbeads. After you have fillen all of your backpacks with the warbeads. Turn them into Sheriff Roglio at the bank, he will give ya a little bit of money and XP. Now after you have 8 backpacks I would probably run over to West Commons. Then you should immediatly go for the Derv camp. If the camp has been wiped clean (by twinks most likely) just make sure you are full HP and when a derv spawns pull him a ways out of camp and kill him. If the camp is full, wait for a bit and one will come walking out. Just take him out and loot him. You find that on average they drop about 5gp and they also drop alot of Bronze weapons. If these are better than the one you have, then start using it, if not hoard it and get more of the bronze weapons. Each sells to the vendors for a over a plat at least. It is a great way to make some money. I myself was averaging a total of 20-35pp per cash in. I just did that about 10-15 times and was rolling in the money (atleast I thought). After that I went out and bought a PGT for about 150 and started to kill them faster. Soon after hoarding enough I bought me some armour and some claws (now I am the halfling Wolverine). That is what I did and it worked alright enough for me. Tell me if it works for you.
