I will feel free to comment.

It is not a bug. It is not an exploit. Someone has just learned a way to use a spell - an all but useless spell - for a good purpose.

First of all, it is not Eye of Zomm. It is the 57th level wizards/mage spell Eye of Tallon. The spell makes an invisible eye for scouting. The only monster in the zone that can see invisible is Phinny. Cast the eye and run it down to Phinny. He will see it and attack it. After killing the eye (one hit point), he comes after the master of the eye. You wait at the zone line with a couple of 50+ groups and kill him.

As a matter of fact, you can do a similar thing with a brave puller. We have a 60th level druid that binds himself at the zone line (just inside KK). He casts invisibility on himself and swims down to Phinny. When Phinny comes after him, he casts gate. He pops back to the zone line and Phinny comes after him. You end up with Phinny (and maybe one or two guards) at the zone. (* If you do this, keep an eye on your puller. If they die, someone needs to be at the zone line ready to give them water breathing, or they will be in a death loop *)

The 'exploit' using Eye of Zomm to pull is done in the following manner:
Wizzy or Mage stands in corner near zone in and summons Eye Of Zomm. Eye appears inside the wall and is instantly moved right next to Phiny. Phiny sees Eye, kills it and agros on caster. Due to bad pathing Phiny can't run to Caster, so Phiny teleports up to zone in. Raid group proceeds to kill Phiny without having to deal with all of the other creatures deep in the zone.

BTW, to the person who said that only Phiny can see invis in Kedge, you aren't quite right. Estrella also sees invis, and if she has been up long enough she will cast see invis on the mobs near her.
