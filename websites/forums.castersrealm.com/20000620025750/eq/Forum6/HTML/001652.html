Well...never got a chance to solo it. Got there fine. Zoned in, got pet cleared to first fall. Kept killing all the way down there, clear through the crap to the Kly...only to find a necro there and a druid running around too. So I ask to join up and we agree on the loot order and I figured I'd be there a while. We just smashed the placeholders no problem. Would get dropped before I could get a second spell off usually. Even got xp for a couple of them =P. When the Kly finally spawned it was about the same - not very difficult - just send the pets and nuke like crazy. Has good magic resist is about you have to worry about as long as you just stay out of the way it seems. After about 5 hours the first necklace dropped, and another 2 in the next two hours (they were very kind to stick around until everyone had one). A fairly long camp but, WOW, the first time you inspect that thing is just crazy. Sooooo many stats it's hard to read =P. I highly suggest you go get one for yourselves, definitely worth it to me, though of course some people have reservations about camping for long periods of time and the Kly is a pretty rare spawn. Or you can wait a bit - despite being an incredible item, it's not a difficult camp, just a bit lengthy, so as they turn up on your world the price should drop down a bit. No clue what they're worth now though - that's always a subjective thing
