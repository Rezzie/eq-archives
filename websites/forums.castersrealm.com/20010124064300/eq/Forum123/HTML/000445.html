Haveing odd speech isnt "roleplaying". No one in EQ roleplays, because roleplaying good/evil is just to damned hard. Basicaly, in a game that is all about hack and slash you cannot roleplay because there arent any descisions to be made.

You cannot roleplay if there isnt anything to do in the game besides hunt. In a world where every area except for the cities is a hunting ground, haveing a role is pretty much impossible. Basicaly, anyone who plays EQ cant roleplay, it just isnt in the nature of the game. Im not saying that everyone in EQ is a powergamer, nor am I saying that there arent those that find something else to do besides hunt. What I am saying is that, since its not practical to be a pacifist (or just someone that doesnt enjoy hunting) and play EQ, you cannot find a role because everybody does the same thing.

Its still best to pick your race on what you prefer. You may just not want to be thought of as ugly and dumb, or you may not want to be small. Still, when you get down to it the only difference between most Elves and Trolls are stats and other features that are bult into the game, and the way some of the Trolls will talk. Thats not enough to be considered roleplaying by any means.

For those of you that might be inclined think that Im ripping on EQ, Im not. EQ may not be a roleplaying game, but it is great at what it does. Its a great hack and slasher and a really great social enviroment. I like to roleplay, but I have D&D for that. When I just want to hunt or possibly chat with some friends I get on EQ, and let me tell you its a blast.
