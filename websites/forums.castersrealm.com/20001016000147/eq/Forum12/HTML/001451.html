Well here is how I got past 35...

Solo - Cyclops in Rathe Mountainsm all blue fairly easy to find and have obvious place holders. Just dont mess with Tarskuk until your higher. He seems more resistant and can outrun SOW. Lead with Tagar's, sick pet on them Dot and nuke em down. Lake zone is nearby so ther is an easy escape route should things go bad. Group - Cazic Thule, Archon Temple (4Cs), Throne Room and Sewers all offer an almost endless stream of high blues. With a good group you'll have non-stop pulling and make an amazing amount of exp.

Ok here's the secret:

Grouping grouping and more grouping.

I've found that while you'll eventually get better exp soloing, the level you're at in relation to your spells vs. mob hps/resistances is gonna give you more downtime than it's worth. Your best bet is to get in an HG group or hit up Cazic and buckle down. Another thing to think about, one that has come back to bite many druids (read: soloers..this is not a flame druids) in the ass is that at the coming of power, 35+, you need to be known in order to pick up plane raid invites and the like. I've seen tons of higher level, non-uber guild folk who've never been to the planes much less have the friends and fire power for an FG raid, KK raid or the like.

If you're not in a power guild you're going to be doing a big big favor by getting out, grouping and getting known so that when the time come and a group needs a shaman there will be some one who goes : "/g I know so and so, hes a bada$$ shaman..let me ask him if he wants to come"
