I think they removed charm when I was level 31 or so (I am now level 40), and it didn't strike me as a particularly big loss. I have been murdering humans on vallon zek from day 1, and am still an active hunter at level 40, and will be at level 50. It upsets me to read in sarcastic posts that all we can do now is run, when nothing could be further from the truth.. I don't remember the last time I ran from a fight against an evenish con, and have taken down 2 outta 3 blues without charm (had to split from the other as he sat there mezzed).

It is my firm contention that we are clearly the best support pvp class (mezzes, clarity, debuffs, buffs, area mez, etc), and quite possibly the best solo pvp class second only to maybe wizzies or necros at high levs (like 34+)...

First off, this is what i keep up on my spells for pvp

1) Anarchy
2)Tashani
3)Root
4)Suffocate
5)Enthrall (entrance now that they changed the casting time)
6)chaos flux
7)color shift
8)color flux

The first thing you want to make sure you do is ensnare your prospective target with a mez... Now if he is melee type (which most casters cannot kill now becuause of the 60% damage of our DDs), exchange some of your spell around and debuff the bejesus out of him, med a bit, and wake him up with suffocate while getting yer enthrall back in place... Insipid Weakness + Suffocate + Listless Pwer = this tank aint goin nowhere (also great for caster, they cant move a muscle).. If you cannot kill him, since you dont have enuff mana, well you can keep him dotted and mezzed while you med.. remember, you have clarity, you gain mana back faster than they gain health back...

Casters are just stupid easy.. I took down a yellow halfling, he was just trying to gate the whole time.. I ran up on his ass, just started alternating color shift/color stun, interupted every single spell.. Don't forget that our DDs interupt too, so time them so your DD will hit that wizard right as he's trying to get that Tishan's Clash off on ya.. With Casters the important thing is to stay in point blank range. Cast color flux, when you can cast again do color shift, and then a nuke... after nuke lands, color flux is ready to cast again.. They have about 1/3 of a second to do something, otherwise they get no chances to cast or run or anything...

Another way to go is to mez and debuff a caster using yer handy dandy str debuffs so they can't run, and then a few daubs of mana sieve.. yes, it may be a battle of attrition, but you have clarity, they do not.. You med 150% normal speed, and mana sieve takes more mana than it uses... I have used this against low blues only tho, wouldn't trust it on another lev 40 person..

whirl is also nice for interupt over distance tho I prefer to stick with my point blank stuns as they cast faster and have shorter recharge times and seem to get resist less..

Oh yeah, and ppl are always slamming our DDs saying they are too slow casting and the recharge is stupid.. Well so in pvp keep up yer big and little, what's the big problem there? Our DDs are quite mana efficient, much more so than clerics or necros, and by the time one is finished casting the other is ready to go.. Also, ppl complain about them being all or none.. I personally LOVE the fact that they are all or none... If you can lower somethings magic resist to where it will more than likely not resist you, then you are basically getting a full hit 9 out of 10 times versus getting a bunch of partials and maybe a full resist every now and again. And don't forget that they interupt; only us and the clerics have that (maybe mages too, not sure, thought one interupted me the other day)...

Oh yeah and dont forget the importance of rune III.. Getting low on hps? Mez that damn rogue that's been biting on ya and piercing ya. Now he's gotta go thro another 300 more hps to get to you...

Hope this gives you some ideas about how to not only survive in pk, but how to pk back aggressively. I'm sure everythig works differently for all ppl, but these stratagies have served me well in my 40 levels on Vallon Zek. I have probably used charm all of 20 times from lev 12 to 20 just screwing around in pvp (I use it in environment combat all the time)...

Telish Khaotic, 40th level Vallon Zek
