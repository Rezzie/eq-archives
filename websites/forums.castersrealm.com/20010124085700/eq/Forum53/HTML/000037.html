The druid epic weapon is the Nature Walkers Scimitar. It's a 1HS weapon with a 20 dmg, 30 dly ratio and the follow stats: STR +15, STA +15, WIS +20, HP +10, Mana +90, SV ALL +10, Effect: Wrath of Nature (snare+1650dmg DoT).
