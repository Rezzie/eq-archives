Well if you want to put numbers on Exp I got some. Taken from :
http://www.geocities.com/orderoftheblackdagger/EQexp2/


Race:
Troll have a modifier of 120
Iksar have a modifier of 120
Ogre have a modifier of 115
Barbarian have a modifier of 105
Halfling have a modifier of 95
All other have modifier of 100

Class:
Warrior have a modifier of 09
Rogue have a modifier of 09.05
Healer have a modifier of 10
Casting have a modifier of 11
Hybrid have a modifier of 14

So do the math and you'll find that there is a big difference between a Barbarian Shaman and a Troll Shaman. But the regeneration on Trolls/Iksars can't be ignored. Now the question becomes "Is the regeneration worth 14.3% more Exp?"

(note: I still don't trust the percentages... Unless one of the programers verifies it, the tests are not conclusive.)
