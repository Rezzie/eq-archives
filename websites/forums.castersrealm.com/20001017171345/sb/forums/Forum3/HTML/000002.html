As it says in the most recent press release, there will be castles that can be at least partially controlled by players. Armies can even siege these castles (excellent!).

But how cool would it be if you could erect your OWN castles? At the very least it would be nice to build a wooden palisade/fortress for you growing army, a symbol of your power. If you get strong enough and are wealthy enough could build a castle from the ground up. It would require laborers, certain tools, certain resources and a certain amount of time to make.

How could this done? I know not. In EQ you can't add jack to the game world, save for a bulletinboard where you could leave messages. Even if you dropped something on the groud it would usually disappear within minutes. Every person that logs into the game has to get the same basic data, I think. And building a castle would be putting every changing data in so I don't know if it's possible. I don't really know the specifics of how this might work.

Maybe there can be an already established arcitectual framework for a castle you can built or on a more detailed lvl, certain pre-established parts of a castle or other structures you can add as you please to make something new.

Of course there needs to be something that prevents every joe schmo from building somethingmake . Just it really, really costly. Perhaps your armies have to be in control of certain gold wood and stone reserves to undertake such a large project.

Any other ideas?
I can't wait for this game.
