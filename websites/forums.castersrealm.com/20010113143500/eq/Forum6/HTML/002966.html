mem your level 8 shock of whatever (or whenever we get our first metal shock spell), it has the same chance of interrupting as the higher level ones but with a quicker cast time. Put your pet on his pet at the beginning of the fight, that way when he fears your pet he'll have to have the presense of mind to back his pet off if he wants it to hit you (A moment many necros won't take), also there is a chance his pet will be out of range of command before he can back it off. Use fire pet, his pet will beat itself to death on your pet's flameshield, getting it into the fight sooner. Use that staff we can summon that has 2 charges cancel magic, you may be able to knock off his shielding spell and lower his MR. Don't bother with Malise, waste of mana and time. If you see your spells stop doing damage all together then stop casting, you don't get a resist or damage message when necros have harmshield on and they are 100% protected from damage.
If you have resist gear, wear it over mana gear. I don't think necros have ignite bones at that level so just disease and poison if you can get it (and magic of course). Last of all, aside from this be lucky dueling a necro is difficult as best...

Resists are the key.

You must be able to resist the fear or you are dead period. You cant cancel or pumice while running around like a monkey.

Bad thing is he only has to deal with fire and magic and your pet will never resist anything. Your only hope is cancel magic on the pet and self and resisting things.

Overall, you need luck and he doesnt. All things being equal they are just a better PvP class.
