Okay, for those of you who do not want to waste their time on the Druid Quest site because they cannot seem to stay on track, here is what I have so far.

1) Burning Woods-Telin Darkforest-loc pos 3000, pos 3000
"Hail", "What forest?", "What answers?", "What evil?", "What do you suspect?", "What change?", "What about Innoruuk?", "What action?". He gives you a note.

2) Greater Faydark-Faelin Bloodbriar-loc pos 1500, pos 550-PH is an Orc Pawn
Give her note, she gives you Faelin's Ring. She won't stop to talk to you, just have to keep up with her and give it to her.

3) Kithicor Woods-Giz X'tin-Follow path to HH from Commons zone and you'll find him on track.
Give him ring, he gives you coin. As soon as he gives you the coin a lvl 50 Tier'Dal Reaver spawns, gate if you can, I couldn't get gate off and died there. Also, make sure you go between 7am and 7pm game time so the biggest threat will be a bixie while looking for Giz.

4) Burning Woods-Telin Darkforest
Give him the coin, he gives you a "Worn Dark Metal Coin".

5) East Karana-Athele
Give her "Worn Dark Metal Coin", she gives you an amulet. Find Nuien-Give him the amulet, he gives it back. Find Sionae-Give her the amulet, she gives it back. Find Teola at Barb Village-Give her the amulet then follow her to meet Athele, Nuien & Sionae. A DE Corruptor and 2 friends will spawn. Corruptor is lvl 50, friends are mid 40's, all 3 have pets. Kill all 3 and loot "Spellbound Tome" from the Corruptor. Give Athele the "Spellbound Tome", she gives you a scroll.

6) Misty Thicket-Elle Foodcrafter
Give her scroll, she gives you a bowl. You need to forage A) Heartfruit-Greater Faydark, B) Molded Speckled Mushroom-Innothule Swamp, C) Frozen Tundra Root-Everfrost & Sweetened Mudroots-Misty Thicket and combine in the bowl she gave you. You will get a "Hardened Mixture". She wants this bowl back along with an "Ancient Elven Bowl".

7) Timorous Deep-Alrik Farsight-loc neg 6000, pos 2500 in SW part of zone near teleport pot room.
"I seek an ancient type of bowl made by the elves of old." "I will take the artifact." He gives you a "crushed pot". *NOTE: On one web page they said he wouldn't speak to you unless he cons amiable. To do this, go to Niola Impholder in Felwithe Mage Guild and con her. Give her batwings 4 at a time until she cons amiable. (Took a full backpack of 160 wings for me to be amiable.) This may or may not be true, I did not want to go all the way to TD and find out I couldn't continue quest, so I upped my faction with her first.

8) Felwithe-Farios Elianos-In building next to casters guild building.
Give him the "crushed pot", he gives you a "Grocery List". Give "Grocery List" to Merchant Nora in the Shop of All Holds, she gives you a "Bag of Provisions". Give "Bag of Provisions" to Farios, he gives you "a receipt".

9) Timorous Deep-Alrik Farsight-Give him "a receipt", he gives you an "Ancient Pattern". This pattern reads as follows:

.........x..........w...............iea.......Pla.
.....Sp......Powder....'Encha......lay.......'This
........attern.

This is the recipe to make the Ancient Elven Bowl that Elle Foodcrafter wants, but it needs solved. One of the ingredients may be "Enchanted Clay" which is found on a bandit runner in Qeynos Hills by the guard tower near the West Karana zone. *NOTE: There are only 4 mobs on this island. Curiosity killed the dr00d. Don't go looking for "an iksar master", he hits for 350+, schmacked me down in 4 shots and I had to travel all the way back to TD to recover corpse because you can't bind there.

Next, there are three mobs found so far that request three items each which are looted off the tainted/corrupt mobs as follows:
1) North Karana-Withered Treant
A) Everfrost-Corrupt Mammoth-Chunk of Tundra
B)Rathe Mountains-Corrupt Hill Giant-Ancient Rock
C) Lake Rathe-Corrupt Aqua Goblin
Give Chunk of Tundra, Ancient Rock and loot from Aqua Goblin to the Withered Treant, he gives you "Warm Pulsing Heart". Go to South Karana, find Yeka Las-Give him the "Warm Pulsing Heart" and he self-destructs thereby cleansing Antonica.

2) Lesser Faydark-Pained Unicorn
A)Lesser Faydark-Corrupt Brownie-Green Heartwood Branch
B)OOT-Corrupt Seafury-OOT Seavine
C)Kedge-Corrupt Seahorse-Kedge Cave Crystals
Give Green Heartwood Branch, OOT Seavine & Kedge Cave Crystals to the Pained Unicorn, she gives you "Gleaming Unicorn Horn". She tells you to find on Faydwer a Gnome Priest to give the "Gleaming Unicorn Horn" to. Don't know who that is yet, but he should self-destruct and you've cleansed Faydwer.

3) Swamp of No Hope-Ulump Pujluk (Froglok)-loc neg 89, neg 1771-Use wolf form to approach him.
A)Lake of Ill Omen-Corrupt Barracuda-Pure Lake Water
B)Emerald Jungle-Corrupt Gorilla-Green Tree Bark
C) Frontier Mountains(I think)-Corrupt Forest Giant
Give Ulump the Pure Lake Water, Green Tree Bark & whatever the Forest Giant drops and he gives you something.

This is as far as anyone has gotten. I assume the part with Ulump cleanses the Kunark continent. I am led to believe that there must be the same type of cleansing required of Odus. No one has found any info on that yet. For all corrupt spawns, you must kill the tainted spawn first and the corrupt spawns directly on top of tainted corpse. I personally have gotten as far as the Ancient Pattern and am stumped by the puzzle. I will post more when I know more.

43 High Elf Enchanter (doing this quest with my boyfriends character Ladas-50 Wood Elf Druid)
