Strength has no effect on max damage. It merely increases the chances of getting higher damage on an individual swing. The relationship of your strength to 200 is of no consequence (stats can go over 200 anyway).

Below 20, no weapon will do more than 29 damage (although rare hits for 30-32 have been seen with very high STR). Past level 20 there is no damage cap on weapons. My husband's warrior was wielding a Blade of Xalgoz as well, and his last swing at 19 was for 27 points. His first swing at 20 was for 47.

Above 20, the max damage is determined by the damage of the weapon, your level, and the damage bonus of the weapon (bonus for 2h, bonus for slower weaps).
