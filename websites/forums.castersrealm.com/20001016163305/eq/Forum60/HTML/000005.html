No such thing as a Great item for a cleric. Even our armor and weapons from the planes and gods are not great.

A charred Guardian Shield is the shield for a Cleric to use (+9 wis) and they are certainly alot cheaper than 1000pp if you buy it while anonymous. The Paw of Opalla (+9 wis +9 agil, hand-held item) is alot cheaper now than it first was, no idea if you can buy one for 1kpp on your server, but I know you can on Povar.
A mithril Breastplate (+15 agil, looks like light-purple plate) from Solosek B is good. Most people will only trade those away, but they ARE very common now. You might be able to find someone who'll sell one for 1kpp.
A pair of Runed Mithril Bracers (+3 str +5 wis, each, light-purple plate) are good, too. You can definately buy two of those for under 1kpp in solb. I'd say they will run you 600-800pp.

When you buy stuff from people who are high level, do it while anonymous. When level 50s sell stuff for cash to lower levels, they raise the prices because the seller knows the lower-level buyer isn't likely to be able to obtain the item on his own. When a level 50 sells something for cash to another level 50 (which rarely happens) he wont push the price of the item as high because they both know that the buyer could just as easily go out and camp one for himself.

Zsalshun, platinum ruby veils go for a lot more than 250pp. The items alone to handcraft such jewelry cost ~235pp, and even an enchanter with 200 Jewelcraft skill will fail from time to time when making these.

You were most likely thinking of a silver ruby veil for which 250pp is too much since these trivial at 75 skill level for enchanters and cost ~130pp to craft.

A silver ruby veil is a nice wedding gift for a low level cleric and would serve them well until they can afford to get a platinum ruby veil.
