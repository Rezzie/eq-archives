Things to remember about the bubble gage and mob comparison..

One: It jumps due to res. and optics.
Two: Most mob types have a level range of 3 or more and the exp from killing a level 10 is much less than from a 13.

So to be sure using this method you need to kill even cons and kill a lot of them.

Third point on the test: In a group exp. per kill is divided based on current total exp of the group members. So a level 9 gets much less per kill with a 11 than with a 7 if the 11 and 7 have same exp. penalities.
