I play a high lvl necro in Solusek Ro, and my opinion is that necros are better soloer than druids or mages. Why? Read on:

Necros better than druid :
Druids have good dots and good DD, and they´ve got a very good solo technique (snare-SOW kiting). Besides they always have a way out of problems with SOW and Spirit of Cheetah (spelling?) which is double fast than SOW for few secs. But they lack of 2 things that put necros right on the top : pet and dark pact series. The pet is an awesome extra value that is both a lifesaver and a manasaver. Lifesaver cuz he can stick to the mob few secs while u cast Gate in case things get too tough, and a manasaver since they act as an extra powerful DOT on the mob. And the Dark Pact series allow us to med up much quicker (lich is more than double faster than clarity) and therefore less downtimes which means more exp.

Necros better than mages:
Mages got awesome DDs, and an awesome pet (better than ours at that !) but again they lack of two things : Dark pact spells (same as above) and a way out when pet dies or a second mob jumps in or whatever. They´ve got Gate, but its far to slow to cast off if a mob if hitting them hard, hence having them dead. On the other hand, necros got Shadow Step (awesome when combined with Gate), Harmshield, Fear, and of course, Feign Death (best spell in game in my opinion. Id definitely be 5 lvls lower if it wasnt for this spell). So we can always escape away without losing exp if we cant handle the situation.

Those are the reasons for what I state necros on top of the soloers list. What do u guys think?

Yaguex, lvl 50 nec, Solusek Ro.
