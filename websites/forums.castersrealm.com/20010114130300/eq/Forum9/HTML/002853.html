Curious how you guys feel:

In this last patch Bard's had the resist rates on their single target mez spells (Pixie strike - lvl 28 and Song of Twilight - lvl 53) significantly lowered.

Some background:
Bard's get 3 Mez songs - an AE song at 15 (Lullaby), A single Target Mez at 28 (Pixie Strike) and an upgraded single target mez at 53 (Song of Twilight).
All of these songs had a secondary resist which rendered them all but useless. The resist rates were in the neighborhood of 75% because of the secondary check. For a long time Bard's have hounded Verant to take a look at these songs as in their current incarnation they were junk. The AE mez was shown to be relatively useful from 15-mid twenties but then the resist check kicked in and it served no purpose other then an AE taunt. Verant re-evaluated this song and determined that it was "working as intended"(tm) but that the higher level "upgrades" (verant's description) would be looked into in the future.

Well with this last patch Verant did just that - Lullaby has not changed and is still useless over level 29 or so but the secondary resist on Pixie was lowered drastically and the secondary resist on Twilight was dropped entirely. What this has done is given bards the ability to single target mez 2 or 3 targets with Pixie (due to it's roughly 9-12 second duration) and 3 or 4 with Twilight (due to it's 18 second duration). There is some concern that Enchanters will feel that the Bard has somehow imposed upon their title of "Crowd Control Masters."

Bear in mind that the Bard can do nothing but single target mez if he intends to keep more then 1 target down during this time. They can not twist in any other songs because of the timing isues. Addtionally, Twilight is capped at mobs of lvl 55 (or is it 54?) or lower.

My personal feelings, as a Bard, are that this is a Boon to Enchanters as it eases some of their burden without encroaching upon the 100% need for an enchanter during raids and encounter mobs. We will never be efficient as Enchanters since it is single target - in the time it takes to lock down the 2-4 mobs the un mezzed will be beating the snot out of somone (usually the Bard). AE Stuns and Mez's will still be preferred in high level dungeons. With this change "pick up groups" that can't find an enchanter (more desireable for C2, slows, Haste and better CC) can grab a bard. Six man exp groups have an alternative now for low level crowd control.

I would encourage you to read this (very lengthy and passionate) thread at "The Concert Hall" for more inforation:
http://pub5.ezboard.com/ftheconcerthallbackstage.showMessage?topicID=6286.topic

How does the enchanter community feel?
