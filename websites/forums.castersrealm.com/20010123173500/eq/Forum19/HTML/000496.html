I'll have maybe 2500, although based upon what we've seen coming out of Kunark... most likely I'll have less than that, as I shift more emphasis to gear with HP's instead of int. Suffice it to say I won't be able to cast this spell 7 times, in all likelihood.

I guess the main question will be how prevelent level 55+ mobs are. But suffice it to say that should a dungeon contain a large quantity of them, that dungeon would affectively become raid only material. Mobs that high level hit too hard, too fast, too often for max damage. They've got tons of hp's. Often, I've got to stop 2 or 3 mobs in the planes, and there I've got the support of my whole guild alliance to chop the mobs down. (Being as I'm one of the highest level and one of the most active enchanters, I'm typically the head crowd control guy.) But holding 2 or 3 mobs with glamour while the remaining party of 5 takes down a level 55+ mob? I'll be OOM before they have it halfway dead. If they just come 2 at a time, then fine, I'll use this spell if they summon... if not, pull them away and root 'em down. Then I can try and cast Enthrall 7x, Entrance 8 times (85 mana, twice the duration) or Dazzle 11x (125 mana, 4x the duration). Even if these spells get resisted on all but the last one, I come out ahead in mana usage.

The second thing I worry about is this: resists. This spell from the sounds of it won't be unresistable. It will suffer the same resists we get now on sub-55 critters. Can you imagine a resist on this spell? Almost a bubble of mana gone. A highly mad mob on you... For us, with mez'ing, getting resisted is worse than for most any other class. For we are ticking off the unengaged mobs. Added to that, without another person to slow the engaged mobs attacks... well, the cleric is highly likely to be healing someone else, and I'm sure most of us know by now that healers have pretty much got to start healing us the moment we start taking damage or we die. And while we are getting pulped, we've got a few choices, try and cast a mez through the blows or do a few stuns and then mez. For casting through blows, I've found that mez is the easiest to cast successfully, then enthrall, and then up the rest of the line... Which means Glamour will be the hardest to cast through blows.

I know this: I won't go to Sky because they took away what we do up there. The same will hold for any mere dungeon. If I can not affectively crowd control, I won't be there. I am not a clarity dispenser. I am an enchanter. I have my pride.
