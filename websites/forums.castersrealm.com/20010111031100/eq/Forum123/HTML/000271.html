Actually, I think it's great that people are finding new ways to Solo as a Warrior.. Would you like to hear another?

Get a bow of decent delay. Faster is better in this case so choose a low delay Bow with lower damage over a High delay bow with high damage.

Buy a ton of arrows. They don't need to be the best type out there, but if you can afford it, go with higher damage ones.

Go to South Karana.

Get Spirit of the Wolf. Buy potions, beg from the kind Shaman/Druid nearby.

Find a bird that looks like it needs to die, then open fire. SK is wide open enough that you can run backwards easily enough and just keep firing. Then, when you get him down low enough, go in for the kill. If you aggro more birds (likely) just shout out that you will be running them past a landmark if anyone wants it. I always say, "running Avo up to East Tower if anyone wants it" there are usually shouts of "OH YEAH" and your problem is solved.

At 27 I killed an Avocet this way. Man, what a rush! I was fairly well equipped at the time, and still had a helluva fight, but I did it.

I solo'd all the time like this when my guildmates would give me Spirit of the Wolf. If you don't have friends in the area, PLEASE tip the Druid/Shaman. Or at least offer to help them when you can if you are just plain broke. This will make it easier for you to get a Re-buff.

((Beware: Egrets have Spirit of the Wolf.. don't try to outrun them.))

Oh, one more thing.. Make sure you get to know the timing for Spirit of the Wolf.. If it runs out you could have trouble. Get it renewed on the fly from that kind Druid/Shaman.

Time consuming? Yes. Good exp? Not bad at all.. Takes a while to kill a bird, but you get all the exp! Expensive? Somewhat.. I don't recommend doing this 100% of the time. This just works in a pinch if you can't find a group or just want to wow some friends.
