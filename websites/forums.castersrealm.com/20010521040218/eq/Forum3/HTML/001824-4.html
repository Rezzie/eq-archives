Haste spells only stack with haste items, not other haste spells. Just because the haste comes from your sword doesn't make it the same as a haste item. It is a non-hasted item that procs a haste spell. Once it procs, the haste you get comes from a spell, and therefore never stacked with Augmentation, either before the nerf or after. On the other hand, you could use your sword and an FBSS, and in fact you would have gained a tremendous amount of extra haste before the nerf, and will still get more haste, only not as much, after the nerf. The FBSS will count as the item, and the haste proc will count as the spell, so they will stack.
