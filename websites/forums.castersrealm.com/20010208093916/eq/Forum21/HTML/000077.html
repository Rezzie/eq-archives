More like 8p per GLS with average charisma and faction. Your profits will vary.

But yes, the research quest basically turned into a way to make wisp farming profitable again after they nerfed the money and GLS drop rates on the wisps way back when. Hand in the GLS, turn around to the other gypsy merchant, and sell the concordance. You even get some xp to go with your money.

Practice research doesn't seem to be worth the effort, so scamming the quest for money is about all it's good for.
