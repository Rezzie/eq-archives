I'm a huge fan of Iksar Necros, but I've only played to level 14. I think the AC bonus they get and the natural regen make them naturals for casting classes (especially Necros). The regen is particularly useful when coupled with Dark Pact, because you will still gain HPs like a normal person while your mana is coming back quickly.

I have no idea how they are at higher levels, but up to 14, it's been lots of fun!

Iksar is probably the best for personal stats for the reason stated by Goober. My second choice would be Erudin. Why? Because Tox is so removed from the mainstream of Norrath, Verant has given the Erudin necros an almost closed environment for leveling. Their newbie area is self contained AND very fast regenerating. There are Various quest for Erudins that take place right in the newbie area or in near by Tox.
The areas around TOX are almost NEVER crowded thus there are plent of MOBs to kill with little or no interference.
Erudins as necros are welcome in a few more places than a DE, although, I believe a necro gnome is welcome in more places than the Eruidite (Im NOT sure of this, so you might want to check it out)
