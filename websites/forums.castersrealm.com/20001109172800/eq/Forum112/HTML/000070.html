I've been a Troll Shadowknight for over a year now.

I was untwinked, bought my first weapon (a Fine Steel 2 Hander from a highbee coming out of Cazic), I trained one hand slash and piercing using rusty weapons. I wore leather armor till level 12, banded till level 24, bronze till level 40. My weapons were FS 2 hander, Grobb Cleaver (lvl 15), Bone Bladed Claymore (lvl 18), Blackened Iron Bastard Sword (lvl 22), Dark Reaver (level 35), Mithril 2 Hander (lvl 45), Beckon (lvl 50). I lived without lifetaps, without DarkForge, without a Dark Reaver for a very, very long time.

Did I suck? Hell no. Why? I knew my purpose.

Shadowknights exist to tank and control the flow of battle. We generate more taunt than any tanking class for 45 levels (until rangers get their ultra low delay weapons). We can use darkness to prevent runners, invis to scout pulls, and feign to prevent bad pulls. No other class can do everything the Shadowknight can do. Period.

You're level 23, you havn't even scratched the surface on your class. Find your role and fulfil it. Sure, our usefulness diminishes when a ranger and warrior are present in a group, but really, how often does that happen? Grouped with a ranger? You're a better tank, get in there and use your spells as a taunt. Grouped with a Warrior? How's he going to stop runners? Get your darkness in and keep it applied, but try not to draw off the mob's attacks much. Grouped with neither? Heh. That's where we shine.

For the person *****ing about the 68% penalty...Hey, I lived with it. How? Don't solo. If you group, that penalty gets spread, and suddenly, what was 68% is now closer to 11%. 11% isn't so bad, soloing necros live with 10%. I sill made 50 in 55 days played, it was my first character, and I camped every piece of equipment I use. I wasted a month in Cazic doing DarkForge when I could have been advancing faster in LGuk. I wasted weeks helping a friend do Armor of Ro quests, killing greens.

The Shadowknight class does not suck. It is not broken. The exp penalty is bull****, but it isn't a showstopper. If you're unhappy with the class now, then you never will be. Making the class into something you'll be satisfied with would change the class into something totally different. I would suggest finding a different class and seeing if it appeals to you more.

You need to remeber, our spells don't exist for quantity of damage, our spells exist to produce a desired effect.

Invis -- stealth
Feign -- reset button on pulls
Darkness -- runner prevention
Flutter -- big taunt
Vortex -- self buff

That's our bread and butter. Learn to use those spells, and you will make a significant impact on any group you're a part of. Write them off as sucky, and you'll never contribute.
