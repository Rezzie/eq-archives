Gate, in my experience is never a good evac spell, simply because it takes so long (4 seconds or so) to cast. Even if your channeling is maxed, the mob can still hit you too many times in that 4 seconds for you to recover every time.

You might try memming Flash of Light along with gate. Flash is a fairly quick cast and less likely to be interrupted. Then, when the mob is blind, you can step back a bit and have plenty of time to cast gate while the mob is running in circles.

Delirium, take these suggestions into mind because channeling is a very unreliable way to cast spells through combat or while moving. Sure it happens sometimes but not very often. The best way to avoid being interrupted is to do your best to not place yourself in such situations. Try to keep the mob at a distance at all times and fight mobs that you are faster than. Also, make the decision to run a bit earlier when you have a few bubbles of health left, or pull the mob close to guards so you have less distance to run.

When you get to 14th level and have SoW much will change though, when you can outrun about any mob you will fight.

I'm a level 39 shaman who has a lot of faith in my soloing abilities. Root kiting and pet kiting are excellent techniques but root kiting can go pear shaped quickly if you get alot of resists and pet kiting is impossible in a dungeon.
