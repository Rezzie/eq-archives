Comodities my friend...COMODITIES

It may seem like not a lot of money but i love selling things like bat wings, bone chips, malachite chips, food etc..

These items are reletively cheap from vendors
and sell for quite a markup. For instance a stack of bat wings you can buy for like 2gold or so. I sell them to people in the avaik city in SK for 5pp a stack. Good profit.

Another thing i do when I'm bored is, I get my tailoring kit from bank. Go to EK and hunt crag spiders, they drop tons of silks.
Then i combine for swatches that usually sell for 1pp each. I've made 100pp a night sometimes selling swatches.

These things add up and really don't take much time or money and you can sell while your playing.

Teleport helps cause you can go to different locations to try and sell. There's always a mage in need of malachite in NK. A necro in SK needs chips. People in the Lake of Ill Omen always are willing to buy food and water(i bind in mill, port to get supplies and gate back).

Be creative...its fun.
