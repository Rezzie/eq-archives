To those that are confused:

FBSS -- not changed
Spells -- not changed
Exe Axe -- not changed.

Bug:

Before, if you had an FBSS (15% haste) and ANY spell or bard song that added haste (say augmentation spell, 15% haste). Instead of the 30% haste you should have gotten, you got immediately sent to the "haste cap" of 70%

Now, instead of 15% + 15% = 70%, it will equal 30%.

Will this effect the SK soloing specs with his FBSS....NO! You still have the same haste effect as you did yesterday.

Does this effect the lvl 55 warrior in Karnors. Depends. If he has an enchanter casting SLTW or AQ on top of a dragon/planes haste belt/item, then no. If he's been using an FBSS + augmentation only, then yes.

The point of this change is that the high level enchanter spells will now actually serve a purpose. /gasp!

Nerf enchanters? Hell no! If anything, the way it WAS nerf'd enchanters since a lvl 16 enchanter could buff up a lvl 60 FBSS'd warriors haste the same as a lvl 60 enchanter could...now the lvl 60 enchanter is MUCH better. Same with bards (although they always play best haste song anyway).

I'll feel the change, my lvl 24 monk with FBSS, I used to grp with my lvl 12 bard who played Anthem...got super haste because of the bugged interaction, now, yes, with song will be faster than fbss alone, but not 4x faster.
