Dwarves: 20 in strength, 5 in stamina, 5 in agility.
Halflings: 25 in strength, 5 in stamina.
(Alternately put 10 in stamina for either, and 5 less in strength)

Dwarves get extra bonus points in magic resistance, the most important resist in the game. Halflings get extra points in disease and poison resistance, which is more helpful early on. And their starting location is probably the best in the game (I've heard Misty Thicket has the 4th best experience modifier of all zones--including dungeons).

Stats don't really make a difference in the game, nor does race, that is why I see some incredibly successful gnome rogues, even though they have the worst stats of them all. Stamina, matters, after level 20, when you start using heavier spear/harpoon weapons, and each stamina point provides more and more hit points.
