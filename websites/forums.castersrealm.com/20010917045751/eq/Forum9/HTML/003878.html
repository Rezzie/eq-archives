Bethene asks: So what will happen when the thing the ubers are there for are no longer on the god? Will the ubers clear the planes until they get what they want and then leave?

Well that depends on the strength of the #1 powergamer motivator in EQ: phat l3wt.

If the "new and improved" gods have phat l3wt that is superior to similarly powered bosses elsewhere (meaning for tanks, a 50 damage, 6 delay weapon with +50 to every stat, or for casters, a +3 int, +50 dex, +15 sv disease robe), they will be killed regularly by any guild who can do it. In that sense, the plane situation will be pretty close to what it is now.

If these gods do not have superior phat l3wt, they will not be killed, at least not on a regular basis. After all, who wants a challenge if there's no phat l3wt? This will have the effect of actually restoring some of the original difficulty of Fear and Hate and discouraging farming -- not necessarily a bad thing.

I do hope, for the sake of the classes who require epic items from the gods (wizard, mage, enchanter), that there will be alternative way of obtaining the item. I always felt the current epic system -- which requires high level players to farm lower level areas repeatedly, to the exclusion of the lower players -- was illogical.
