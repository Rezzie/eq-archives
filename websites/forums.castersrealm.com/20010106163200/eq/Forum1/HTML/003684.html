This is my first time posting to this forum, but I feel its time to put my feelings out there.

Verant ruined EverQuest with the introduction of Kunark. Kunark has ruined the economy causing blatant inflation and item hoarding. The new Kunark items which are better than items dropped of "Old World" mobs of the same level have caused the value of anything old world to drop. Just saying something is from Kunark adds 2x what its really worth. Some examples of Old World items that have been hurt by this..Serp Bracers- used to go for 250 on Fennin Ro, now your lucky if you get 50. Green Silken Drapes- Best Robe before Oracle Robe...once 800pp, now 300. Skull Shaped Barbute once 500pp now 100-150.
Next- items that have been made worthless because of Kunark. Ravenscale Gauntlets- replaced by Iksar Scaled Gauntlets, dropped by a fairly easy mob, drops everytime..so much that people leave them on corpses. Serrated Bone Dirks, Gloomwater Harpoons- Kunark piercers have made these worthless... now everyone can have a Trak's Tooth and Tombcarver or Spined Chokidai or Slime Coated Harpoon. Also the new Kunark plate sets...better than planes plate..and its tradeable. Anyone see a problem with this? I mean c'mon your telling me that something that I have to go to planes and pray for it to drop is so easily replaced by something I cna go to Karnor's and get by waiting a few hours..Deepwater Helm, Singing Steel Boots, Elder Spiritist Vambraces..and more are all dropped fairly commonly.
My only hope is that when SoV comes, that many of the items will be no drop. I'm sorry but it breaks my heart to see level 10s and 20s running around with a Traks Tooth and three pieces of Myrlokar's, or Tolans and a Dragon Horn Ykesha. Its ridiculous. It has ruined the game enviroment. Unbalanced gameplay. And basically ruined all the fun I could have had. Its just not rewarding when you tell your guild you just quested for your Burning Rapier and after 2 weeks you finally got it. And then have your level 12 guildmate say hes going to skip it because his Trak's Tooth and Locustlure are better.

Tadyen,

I also play on Fennin and i've yet to see the primetime population at 3000. As a matter of fact I haven't seen it reach 2300 lately or 2800 ever. It used to be the highest in population (before the split), but since then Karana, Povar, and maybe one or two others are still higher.

I agree that it is one of the more popular servers; as to why I haven't a clue. It's rarely mentioned in the news. I'll tell you one thing though...if I could move to a green server I'd do it in a heartbeat. I started a character on Morrel-Thule (sp?) for a vacation, and the lack of idiotic ooc banter was refreshing. What I don't understand is that I still see newbies starting on Fennin and wonder why, of 34 servers, they chose Fennin. These are true newbies, too, not rerolls; it's easy to tell by the questions they ask.

As for prices...

I couldn't care less that prices are dropping. Of course it may hurt me on the Paw gloves I have held onto, but it also makes things more affordable to people that can't stand camps or lists. Most items have more of a subjective value than most people think. Hell, the Paw gloves used to sell for 500-700pp and they only added a measly +2 wisdom for the most part. Now they routinely go for 20pp. Did their value really fade? No, they still give the same stats as they did before, but their rarity has decreased and therefor decreased their subjective value. Now, if someone wants them they don't have to get into a full spire group just to get a chance to roll on them. Sweet! Don't even get me started on the disparity between the batfang headpiece and the savant cap. (same exact stats yet the batfang goes for 10x the price. /sigh)

The one that does shine about Kunark, IMHO, is that they finally made random (decent) drops on wandering mobs rather than forcing a list-backed camp. Maybe the primo items are still camped to hell and back, but they aren't absolutely necessary.
