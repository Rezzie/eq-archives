Gordonfishstick
unregistered 		posted October 27, 2000 05:05 PM              
Without revealing the details of the equations the relationship is as follows.

The higher of either Int. or Wis. effects both the chances of success and the rate at which skill points are gained. The forthcoming various stat bonuses for various skills, such as the strength bonus for blacksmithing will provide a success bonus that will stack with the Int/Wis bonus.

That said, the Deep Fry skill is widely considered to be the most profitable trade-skill, well worth the effort, time, and investment if one can generate the business. The price of advancing the skill is appropriate given that profit opportunity.

- Gordon Fishstick
