Actually, it seems to me that druids are more of a group class and less of a solo class in the 50's. Starting at 39th, the group spells start coming in, making Druids more manna efficient. Velious introduced more group spells.

Mages and Necros solo better after 50 than druids do.

So - it looks like Verant was planning to make the druid a "group utility" type class - very nice little "toolbox" for a group to have, although not replaceable.

However, from "game design" and "preferences of the people" - it looks like Clerics, Enchanters, Warriors, and to a less degree, Wizards are the 4 most desired classes from high 40's up. You can do a plane run without druids, but not without the classes listed above. And to a slightly lesser degree, same for dungeons.

Velious seems to have made the druid more group desired and friendly than before - but I'm not sure to what degree.

So... yes, it looks like druids were designed (or evolved) to be group friendly, but the game design makes certain other classes more group desired by the majority of people.
