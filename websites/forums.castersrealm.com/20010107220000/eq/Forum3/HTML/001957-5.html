Why do we play such a poor game month after month, year after year? Because it's the only available game that is even close to filling the desire to play a multiuser fantasy roleplaying game.

There are definitely a lot of different styles of play. I'm another one that plays primarily the social aspect as I lead three guilds and run the desparately lacking roleplaying that the GMs fail to provide. Do I fault Verant for not providing roleplaying? No, financially it makes no sense, they can't make money giving us dynamic roleplaying.

What I do find fault with is Verant's failure to see the level of desire that players have for roleplaying and their refusal to understand or support roleplaying. There are some things Verant could do that would not take them massive manpower and would allow the players to inject the much needed roleplaying aspects to a "roleplaying" game. Unfortunately such improvements seem to be very slow to be implemented. It took them almost a year after I suggested posting the "official" history of the GM dynamic events so that players could read about the plot and story and have some idea what is going on (Harpy's Head Tavern).

Sigh... it's a great game engine with some basic game design flaws but it's really all we have. The most basic flaw is that a mere six levels separation makes one character a "god" and another impotent. Virtually everyone I've ever spoken with has said they are only playing EQ until someone produces a good roleplaying game. Unfortunately it might be a couple more years before we see one.

So in the meantime we enjoy what EQ can provide. In my opinion the fastest way to dislike the game is to TRY to level. My primary character didn't gain a level for almost six months. When the game has been good and we are doing some heavy adventuring we might gain a level once every two to three weeks. And that would be fine except that so many essential elements require higher levels.

Why is Gate a 4th level spell? Casters should *start* with this essential spell.

Why is bind a 12th level (or 14th level spell)? This should perhaps be a fourth level spell.

Why are self teleports level 19 spells? About tenth level sounds good to me.

Why are group teleports level 29 spells? Good grief I've been playing since last April (uhh... that's 18 MONTHS) and my wife and I still don't have a character that can do a group teleport!

I mention the teleports because this is a SOCIAL game. And for those of us that can only spend about three hours per session it really HURTS to spend over one hour of that session just getting our group together so that we *can* adventure.
