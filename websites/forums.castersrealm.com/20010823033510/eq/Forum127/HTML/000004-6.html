Nice work. Some random thoughts below...

I think there may be more to be had from Jeb...

"'Yes I am. Use your most enlightened magic to seek the truth of what is around you. When that is done, you will know what you seek"

Hmm... See Invis? Or maybe it's just flavoring or a general statement about using faction and illusions spells and such.

"'The first truly powerful enchanter, Krilan Pedin, crafted the Serpent years ago. He trained his students to carry on the trade in a five-step process. Each student then became a master and chose one of their own students to pass the legacy on to. Four others and I, myself, are current practitioners of the crafting. "

This is a five-step process ('what five-step process', 'what trade', etc). He indicates later that you need to seek out four others (along with the order), but is this HIS only task? I'd want to ask him about becoming HIS student, or becoming a 'practitioner of the crafting' or 'I wish to reach the plateau of understanding' somesuch.

"'Modani is a master of illusions. You should seek him out first. Keep a careful eye out for him - he is never in his true form. The last time I spoke to him, he seemed very excited about his voyage to the elven outpost. You may or may not find more clues about his location there. "

FV is an 'elven outpost', so he might be there, but that wouldn't explain the use of the term 'voyage'. The other 'elven outpost' that comes to mind is in Lessor Fay. It's a little odd that he won't likely be found in his 'true form', but we don't know what his true form is =). Then again, the clues indicate that we just need to 'ask around' to see where he resides now. I guess, like Jeb, we need to be particularly interested in NPCs with pets (and don't just hail them, hail them by name, or ask if they are Modani).

"'Mizzle is a gnome who has mastered the use of enlightened magic. Go to him once you have spoken to Modani. Mizzle is not one to travel, so you should not have to look far from his home to find him."

Ok, so Mizzle is supposedly not far from his home (Ak'Anon)... one of the zones around there, I assume. I also note that 'enlightened magic' is mentioned again. Maybe Mizzle can help clarify what this means, if anything.

"'The mistress of charm, Nadia is very beautiful. She, too, traveled with her relatives to the new land. I just recently received a letter from her. She says more and more pilgrims and explorers are arriving every day, and that the outpost is well defended now."

So far, no one has gotten Nadia to talk. Maybe if they carried the letter to Jeb with them ('what letter?'), she might open up. She might also be responsive to questions about her family (Not many folks ever tried 'who is your father', 'who is your brother', 'where is your brother', etc with Princess Linya (sp) Thex... these questions brought out some surprising background info...). I might also suggest commenting (to her) on her beauty/charm.

"'Master of the Phantasms - he is the last one you will seek out. Hmm...Polzin is native to Erudin, however, after the incident near Paineel, no one has heard from him. I hope that he is well. "

Probably too early to say much here, but it couldn't hurt to start asking folks in Erudin/Paniel some who/where is Polzin questions (he's (or some remnant of him)likely in The Hole).

Sorry for rambling, just trying to contribute to the cause (too young to do the quest just yet).
