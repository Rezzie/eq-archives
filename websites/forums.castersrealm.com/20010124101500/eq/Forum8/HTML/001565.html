Actually, necro/necro is fairly inefficient, and the reason for that also explains why necro/mage is so disgustingly powerful.

Since necro dots can't stack, one of two things happens. Each necro casts complementary halves of their set of dots to achieve maximum "dot-ization =)". Since they don't stack, though, neither player is using their offensive power to full potential. It does mean twice the fears and lots of lifetapping, but the fact is that it is inefficiant.

Mages compliment necros perfectly. 2 pets beating on a darknessed feared mob take it down FADT--especially two rogue-style pets. Plus, the magician's dd is the perfect complement to necro doT's--between the 2, plus pets, no mob lives. I have fought in a 2 person group for 6 levels, and we made 60 in the same day (he's a mage who is also a rl pal).

Necro/shaman is powerful, and loses some of the downtime, but the damage potential is a little bit less overall because one or two of the dots are common spells they share, and because shaman pets have nothing on magicians'.

For my money, a necro and a mage who know what they're doing are the next best thing to being invincible.
