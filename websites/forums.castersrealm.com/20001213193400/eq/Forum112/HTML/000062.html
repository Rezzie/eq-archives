I would not set AGI less than 75. I made my AGI and DEX each to be 75, and put the remaining points into STR for my Erudite SK.

I mostly solo, having made the mighty 11th level. Exp seems to be slow going...certainly much slower than with my halfling druid. If I were starting again, I'd go Troll SK. The regeneration really makes a huge difference when you have no way to heal yourself, other than bind wound, or Lifetap (5 hps, yipee).

I play on The Tribunal, and I mostly picked Erudite SK because it's a different, rare, combination, and they look better than the other races, including DE. Of course, that's a matter of opinion.

But for soloing from level 1, with no-twink, I'd pick Troll. The exp penalty will be even worse, though.

If your Agi is less than 75 put points into it to get it to 75, it makes quite a difference to AC. My ogre sk's agi is 70 and I have to make sure I have items that make it 75 or higher so I get a bonus to my AC. Wish I'd known about the agility when I created him.
Trolls are much better for soloing than erudites simply because of regen which can cut downtime in half or less. You may also want to consider Iksars as a solo race, their regen is just as good as a Troll's and I believe their Int is higher- although their physical stats are not as good as a Troll's. On the other hand erudites will now have much more mana than either troll's or iksar at higher levels- I still think that regen would save on the downtime more than the spare mana if you even need to burn it all.
