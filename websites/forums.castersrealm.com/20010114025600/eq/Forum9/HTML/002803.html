I spent 29-34 killing wood elves in OoT, or mountain giants in FM.

When I got sick of those places (often) I would hit up Cazic Thule (enchanters find a group pretty easy regardless of crowding after 29), and the other classes you listed all offer great abilities... from healing to snaring... save the rogue, who will drop into tanking gaps pretty easily.

I also liked the basement in Unrest, but make sure you really kick butt as a chanter... everyone gets a little erked when you HAVE to have a rogue to get your body out. I spent about one-quarter in Unrest, and one-quarter in Cazic.

The sarnak fortress is a good place too in LoIO, as well as The Overthere just starting to open up, especially for 29th to 33rd casters. I've seen a lot of the mid thirties dive into Mistmoore down by the graveyard and the pit... kinda scary but definitely a great place for xp, as long as you don't mind horrid trains. At least in Cazic once you leave the courtyard you don't get trained much.

Cazic Thule is probably my favorite zone in the game now.. I just dinged 36 and I'm almost regretting it, because I won't be able to spend much more time in CT...

That place simply rocks.. the zone has a decent exp modifier for being 'dangerous' and it can be dangerous, but if you know what you're doing, you're very safe.

I spent about a week forming parties for LMP (some people call it 4C I think?), and then moving up to UMP... got some great exp, the loot is good (at LMP and UMP, the coin loot is usually about 2-3 pp per lizard, and the lizards are definitely abundant.. as well as the fact that almost half probably drop FS weapons...)

Got some Ravenscale for my partner's lower level rogue, as well as a darkforge BP for my level 16 SK (sure beats the hell out of his old dwarven ringmail...) .. so the loot is good...

I also got a lizardskin tribal mask off of the golem in the maze... great mask, was actually using it on my mage until a guild mate gave me a shear bone mask yesterday. I'm still not sure if the shear bone is actually better than the tribal mask, but I've got other characters who could make much better use of it...

Then there are lizardscale belts... nothing too fancy, 2 ac, 10 hp, 10 mana.. but sure beat my cloth cord... got a number of those things...

So, the loot is good... the exp is good... if you know what you're doing, you won't die much, if at all... (I don't remember the last time I died in there... and I've done so many corpse retrievals for other people from the depths of the maze, that I know that place like the back of my hand)

I don't have Kunark (will get it in 2 days), but in my opinion, CT is THE place to do the upper 20's to mid 30's

I am currently at level 30 as well.
My main goal has been to go through my enchanting life in dungeons as much as possible. Here are my thoughts at 30:
* Mistmoore - graveyeard or the area in front of castle entrace. However, train central. Easy to get run over by trains from high level inside parties who just jump out.
* Unrest - as somebody mentioned, basement can be lot of fun and pretty fast exp. Helps to have another group down there.
* Sol A - has to be my favorite dungeon. Almost nobody there. Spent several mid 20's levels here. Inferno goblins are still mostly blue, and the gnome areas are a perfect level if you don't mind killing your Ak'anon faction.
* Temple of CT - haven't spent much time there except getting my silver wand and fighting some crocs one night. But from a number of accounts, a good place for an enchanter.
* Upper Guk - areas near the Lower Guk zone are blue, quite empty most of the time. You can even get the Gatorscale arms while there, as well as the mast from the monk.
* Paw - haven't been there yet since it changed to a mid-level dungeon. Definitely on my list to go re-explore.
* Permafrost - area around King can be fun, but in danger of getting slaughtered by ice giants if there is a group hunting them. (happened to me recently, but the group was able to get 56+ cleric and rez us as well Area around the scryer is blue as well, and he has nice warm wolf fur slippers
* Nurga - love it. Almost always empty despite there being 60+ people at Frontier Mountains. The first half of the dugeon is very doable at this level (spiders, goblins, slaves), we tried the burynai, but that was a stretch as they are red and top of the spell range.
* Droga - similar to Nurga at entrances. Both entrances (from Nurga and FM) are blue to yellow. Excellent fun, however, no easy way out. You either need a porter, or have a map and run out invis thourgh the goblin city (which is what we did couple of times). I like the entrance from Nurga better for variety, but the tunnel down from FM can be good as well.
* Dalnir - somebody mentioned the courtyard on the first floor. Excellent area. Do not stay by the zone, it's a long pull and not that many spawns. You can also clear one of the blind rooms and pull there. Finally, you can drop down to second level, clear that room, and set up shop. One group can hold essentially the whole floor if their puller is good. Haven't been to the third floor yet.

There probably are some dungeons I forgot, but the choice at this level is quite impressive. As several others, I do not understand why people would camp a stationary spot instead of crawling through dungeons, but to each their own. Biggest trick can be actually finding a group, as most of these dungeons tend to be populated by the 50+ players camping for loot. Since you have a group, you should be all set. Good luck, you will have LOTS of fun.
