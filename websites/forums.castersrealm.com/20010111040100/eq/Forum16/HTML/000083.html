I'm gonna have to go with shamans for high level PvP ownership.
You get all the resist buffs as well as improved group poison/disease resist buffs.
You get regen spells.
You get a wussy, and yet still horribly annoying pet.
You get SoW. And SoC.
You get a DoT at level 55 that can drop a 60 wizard in 40 seconds.
You get superior heal.
You get cannibilize (with regrowth + cann my 56 shaman friend gets mana back faster than I do on my enchanter at 55).
To top it all off, you have pretty high hp and AC (AC is between clerics and druids, HP about as good as rogues).
At low to mid levels, it's ALL about necros and enchanters. Once you hit ~40 or so, when everyone's channelling maxes out (pets are much less efficient at distracting casters since one root takes them out of the fight) and everyone realizes that all enchanter spells are magic based and boosts their MR, people who can get around resists start to rule the PvP world.
