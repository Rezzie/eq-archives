At 52 I would go to BW instead. You can solo stuff in skyfire, but in general skyfire mobs have tons of hp and hits harder compared to same lvl mob in BW. BW mobs should be mostly blue at 52 too if you look near giant fort. If you absolutely want to hunt solo in skyfire, I would recommend killing those teethy bottomless things near BW zone, but they can hit for up to 149 (devourer, they usually longer north tho). They dont summon, and got relatively low hp. Stay away from chromadracs of all sorts, they dispell all your buffs in seconds. In BW you can solo anything at 52 except Gullerback really. If you dont have mr I would recommend staying away from caster mobs, they root etc.

At 52nd as a Shaman you can Root / DOT solo the Gnawers / Devourerers, Wyverns and Wurms. Anything called Mature, Old, Elder, Ancient or Guardian is to be avoided.

Avoid the Chromodracs, they cast dispells and can wreck a buffers day if you can't Fear kite them. (which also requires a Snare)

Best to hunt with a Necromancer partner there, they have Snares and Fears.
