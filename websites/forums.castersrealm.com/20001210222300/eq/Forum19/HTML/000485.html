The value of Dazzle is in the longer duration allowing you freedom to control the situation.

Medding back 4.7 mana per second means that you make back 560 mana in the time that the mob is mesed. Noone that LIKES making xp leaves it to the last second to remes of course, so it's really something like 500 mana. 2 mobs, group on one, you dazzle the other. They chop the first down and are LOM. You cast one spell and you have the time to med up for the next fight.

AE mes is proving very useful in Karnor's Castle. There is a portion of the gate area where pulling 5+ mobs is common. They hit fairly hard (117 doubles on a low delay). getting them mesed individually and take long enough to see a melee type stripped to the bone. So, AE Mes as they approach, hopeing for all of them. Anything that pops out of all that pretty blue light will come straight at you 90% of the time. They get Dazzle and then it's time for chain AE mesing of the rest. Consumes a fair amount of mana, isn't exactly elegant, but it works.
