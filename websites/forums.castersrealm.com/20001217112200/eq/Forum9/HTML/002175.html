If you are interested in leveling quickly and still having fun i suggest you either stay in Lower Guk or head to SolB. Karnor is alright, but some of those mobs will be extremely tough to handle at level 44 and as previously mentioned, you need to mez ALOT of stuff most of the time because pulls are often 4 or 5 or even more if someone trains you. No one will group with you in sebilis until AT LEAST 47 and even then, you shouldn't go there till you are 50. Same with Chardok, many of the mobs are level 51+ and that's just too much for a level 44 to handle, too many resists. Lower Guk would be my choice unless you are horribly bored with it because it gives you a great chance to practice crowd control and gains you respect from others that will help you get into sebilis and chardok groups when you get a little bit higher.

Hope this information helps you out.
