I do not wish my soul to be torn apart piece by piece so easily. I have consulted other tomes concerning the soul and life essence. I believe I may be able to steal the life energy of another and add it to mine. I do not simply mean life, I mean their being, the thing which transcends life and death. The soul I devour so to speak must however be close enough to mine in essence to be accepted in my own soul. This is where you my apt pupil come in.
