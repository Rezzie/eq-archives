Lepton's Lesson on the Art of Raid Healing:

Rather than CH in the first 30 seconds of fighting, use divine light. When on massive encounters all the clerics should know better than to use a CH to start -- They should all be using /assist and F8 to toggle between the mob and who is getting hit. Initially, no one tank is going to be holding the mobs attention for more than a couple of seconds, so healing with /assist and divine light is more practical than using a potentially massive heal that may in fact heal nothing. Disabling yourself for 10 seconds while CH is casting is purely foolish in these situations (Yes, I know CH is only 50 more mana). In these situations you have to consider everyone on the raid as a part of your group rather than the regular 6 people you are used to paying attention to. So it boils down to using /assist healing or watching each group die one by one. We had this problem on the first few raids on my server many moons ago, but found that /assist healing caused us to go from all 36 of us dying over and over to only 5 or 6 deaths vs. Innoruuk (unavoidable death touches).

Think outside the box (your 6 person group in this case).
