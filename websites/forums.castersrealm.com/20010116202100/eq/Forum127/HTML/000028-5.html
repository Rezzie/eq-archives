All the epic quests have been designed by different people, and by now, we can tell that each designer has a different style of their own.

The Druid quest designer loves the respawning of dead mobs design (killing a tainted mob will spawn a corrupted mob which is the mob you need).

The ranger quest designer loves puzzles. They have a crossword puzzle type thingy to figure out haha.

The enchanter quest designer is obssessed with the power to Charm and enchanters have to charm a lot of mobs to advance in their quest.
