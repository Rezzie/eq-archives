I started by adding 20 to INT and 10 to CHA. If I were to do it again, I would go 25 INT and 5 CHA.

Charisma gear is a lot easier to come by and tends to have much larger CHA bonuses than INT gear has INT bonuses. Also, your high level buffs add 40 CHA but only 15 INT.

There is also some debate over whether charisma has any effect at all, while INT is known and good. None of the other stats have any significant benefit to enchanters, so I would not bother raising any of them at creation.

Arlina..

While your statements are correct, as an enchanter it is your JOB to have as much mana as possible at all times. This is my opinion anyway, and i base this on the fact that a group can handle almost anything if the enchanter (and cleric) have mana.

Having higher int (up to 200) increases your maximum mana pool. If an enchanter with 200 int at level 50, and one with 150 int at level 50 cast the same spells over the course of the battle, at the end when the puller says "mana check ?" the typical ench with 200 int will say "80m", and one with 150 int will say "50m". Groups usually will not pull if the enchanter is low mana, so you will be able to pull more, kill more, have less downtime etc.

It is true that if you have a larger mana pool and you are hovering at 70-80M, and have a smaller one and are hovering at 40-50m, effectively your doing the same job...

But when the crap hits the fan so to speak, the group expects the enchanter to save them, and that extra mana will make a difference.

My personal preference would be to go for +mana items when really low level ( lower than 20 ), then go to int items till you get to 200 and raise cha and HP/AC after that.

I currently am about 202 Int, 162 cha self buffed, and i have 1 Black saphire earring, and also wear 5 ac 55 hp rings.

When i want to charm, i switch to my Cha jewelry, which i carry with me, and can get to 200 cha.

Since quite a few enchanters are also jewelers, i am sure i am not the only enchanter who carries around a bag full of jewelry (i have a 10 slot bag with Int stuff, MR stuff, and Cha stuff in it).
