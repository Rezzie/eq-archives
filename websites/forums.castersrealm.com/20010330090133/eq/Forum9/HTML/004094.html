First, Velious can be alot of fun even for us enchanters. The magic resist has been toned down on many things since it launched.

I'm not much of an Equipment ***** , but here's the rundown if that's what you're after.

We have 4 versions of class specific armour to attain.

From easiest to hardest:

Beguilers: Thurgadmin quests.
Replendent: Plane of Growth Dropped.
Dazzling: Kael Drakkel Quests.
Illusionists: Skyshrine quests.

I believe that the beguilers is easier to attain, though some may dissagree. A 55+ group can sit in the arena in Kael and get the armour components, all the others require about 3 groups.

Replendent is certainly more common than beguilers.

Good dungeons to hunt in at level 52:

Velketors Labyrinth. Xp is bad because it takes a long time to kill things, but it's one fun place. Nice to hunt where there are no frogs.

Icewell Keep You'll need 55+ Tanks pretty much, since things hit in the 200s and again take a long time to kill. Avoid the Dwarves, they're 100% MR as a rule.

Sirens Grotto I love this zone. Fun, different, hard, challenging. It's beautiful - and wow..

Dragon Necropolis Once you're 54+ this can be a fun place. Quite a few things here require GoK so it's a wise idea ot have it before engaging the rats. Group of 6 can do very very well here. (Chetari Dustshapers make great pets)

I even enjoy Kael. All giants which means everyone is expecting you to charm

Basically - go, have fun. Don't worry about faction. As a rule if you're killing Dwarfs, the dwarfs will hate you - vice versa for the giants. I find Dragon faction the most profitable to keep. (kill Giants to get it)

--Illia

hiya Dan.

Helssen's Prismatic Trinket is definitely the item from Velious that has the best effort-to-reward ratio, especially if you know someone that already has Frozen Shadow keys so you don't have to camp them. A very close second, only requiring one group for one fight (and a bunch of porting around) is the Phenocryst's Focus quest.

Other than that...there aren't a ton of things that you can get in one group that are particularly good at your level. Certainly if you don't have Boltran's yet you shouldn't be trying anything major in Kael like trying to farm armor pieces.

You can kill sentries and guardians in Skyshrine and get lots of armor for your friends and hope to get a Guardian Robe (nodrop, very ugly, slightly better stats than Froglok Bonecaster's Robe). There is also some very very nice stuff in Skyshrine that drops off tougher things and is incredibly rare, like the Wurm Lord Shawl and Xialn Mask.

Balancing your faction is interesting because you're an enchanter, so you can get away with keeping your factions a bit lower than other people. Right now I am non-KoS with Coldain, Kromzek (storm giants), and with Collaboration I am non-KoS with Dain, Kromrif, and Tormax also. I think that overall you are best served being friends with Kromzek and Coldain but I could be wrong. They have the best and most accessible quests. And you can still hunt them to some extent with judicious use of Memory Blur...I am indifferent to storm giants (with a religion, so I started pretty bad) even though I fought in the arena area in Kael long enough to get three pieces of Beguiler's armor.

oh, yeah, something else you can do is the Shawl quest from Thurgadin -- the shawl itself is sort of a waste of time, but you get a very nice ranged slot item (Seal of Thoridain) and you can make potions of Gate to Thurgadin.

There are very few things that you can solo in Velious that will give you experience. Maybe some stuff in Skyshrine, definitely some dwarves and manticores in Eastern Wastes. Maybe wyverns in Cobalt Scar, if you're not KoS to dragons (the wyverns are on dragon faction but don't cause faction hits).

oh, yeah, if you are not a gnome you can also get Giant Scalemail Leggings either from killing in Kael or from a quest.

It depends on what kind if equipment you focus on. If you go for int/cha - you can do the different enchanter gear quests in the different cities.

However I found tons of nice things that I would wear dropping randomly in certain zones.

Kael - I'm wearing Giant Scalemail sleeves and greaves, I love the AC on them.

Velketor's Labyrinth - I can't wait to get some Silken cat fur gear. Namely the waist and feet pieces.

Dragon Necropolis - We got tons of drops here. You might get a Chetari Wardstaff, and tons of spells drop here too. This is where I got Improved Invisibility. Also many gems drop here off the rats.

Siren't Grotto - Haven't fought here yet but I hear some Netted Kelp gear is nice.

Also there are some very nice quests out there. for example, Phenocryst's Quest.

I felt the way you did at first about Velious. Kunark has all I need, Velious seems so confusing etc.. but once you actually make the trip over there it's fun. A good way to become familiar with Velious is to do Phenocryst's quest. It has you run through many of the zones. That's how I became familiar with it.

Velious is pretty fun, but you can't expect pickup groups in any of these dungeons. It's best explored with a steady group or with a guild.

Things I'm looking for, partial stats only:

Kael/giant quests:

Circlet of Tallon Zek/Vallon Zek (18 ac, 9 sv magic, 9 sv 2 of the others) also a drop.

Coldain Military Bracer. The giant version of the broodmother quest: 10 ac all/all bracer.

Hellsen's prismatic trinket (5 ac, 8 cha 55 mana, 5 sv magic)

Coldain skin gloves: 12 ac, 20 hp, 20 mana, 6 int... also a drop

High high end:

Belt of dwarf slaying
Guantlets of Dragon Slaying.

If you don't know the stats on those, well, you won't be able to get them

Kael Drop:
Orb of infinite Void: 35 ac, 75 hp, 75 mana, all/all shield.

Drops in Skyshrine:

Oiled Greaves (15 ac, 5 mana, 20 hp... better than Kromriff leggings)

Wurm Lord Shawl: shoulder piece with 12 ac, 6 int/cha, 5 sv all but magic.

Xialn Mask: 12 ac mask, 6 int/cha, 5 sv all.

Dragon Necropolis: Bat fur girdle? 12 ac all/all belt.

Velkator's Labrynth:

Lord Dijonmustard drops a 25 ac all/all hat. But that's another raid encounter.

The Quest armors:
PoG/Thur are about the same. The only pieces really worth bothering with in the long run are the robe/arms. And arms only if you want them for the 4 cha. For most every other piece, loam or insidious is better.

Skyshrine/Kael: Skyshrine has the best stats, Kael has the best AC. Both require hunting in ToV, a very hard raid only zone.

As for factions, my advice is kill in skyshrine to get what you want from there. This will raise your storm giant faction. Either do storm giant quests or kill a few dwarves to get some frost giant faction. Then do all the giant quests you want. Once you've gotten the drops from skyshrine and done the quests from Kael, then go to whatever your guild has. SoV is a raid expansion. Not much for single groups.
