The best place for levels 22-24 is the hermit in South Karana(aprox -5500, -2500). First: buff yourself and your pet. Next,look inside the crack in the front door to target the hermit. Run around the back near the chimney then tashan, enthrall, and debuff him. Med up a bit then throw your DD and DOT on him. By this time hes tried to hit you once, so you pet has engaged. Now you throw Chase the Moon on him. This will make him run away but will be stuck against the back wall so you can continue to DD/DOT and melee him. You will usually not have to recast chase the moon unless one of your DDs break it. He usually drops 1-2PP each time and sometimes a Fine Steel weapon.

Hope that helps.

I went to Kunark at 18. And rarely ever go back to the mainland. I don't know that Kunark is that better on xp but it just seems to have a lot more mobs to hunt. Go to high hold, and either kill osargen or solo the lookout room. Osargen will give you a little more than a bub per hour at 24, and slightly less each level. at 30 i'd say it's time to stop camping him. Be careful killing him though, he's a rogue and only has 550ish life, but he punches in the 40's and backstabs for around 100, and resists root on occasion, but eventually you learn how he works. Now the lookout room. It IS soloable at 24, but you have to be good. You gotta know how to use your pet, when to nuke, when to root, when to tank for your pet, what debuffs to use, and you have to know exactly how it's timed (two spawns, 12 minutes, break it so there's one every 6 minutes. osargen is 6 minutes.) However, if you could take the lookout room at 24, you would get a bub and a half or more an hour. Camping this at lvl 29, i got a bub an hour. Good luck to you.
