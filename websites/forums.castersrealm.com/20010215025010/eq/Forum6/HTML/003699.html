I spent 12 hours on the camp in S Ro and during that time I realized that there is no one single way to make him spawn ... it is totally random. I believe the SGs are his placeholders so you'll have to make sure they are killed. The AC is almost totally fire resistant so you'll have to use Magic based nukes (ie our Shock line).

Just run around the zone and kill everything you come across. I stuck to killing undead and madmen ... I believe by doing this it will cause the SG's to spawn. The game night before I got the AC to spawn I had just killed the pop inside the undead tower and when I turned around I saw the named Sand Giant Hussain pop near the DE gypsy camp. I killed him and waited until the next game night to kill the undead tower spawn again. Shortly after I killed that spot (again it was at night) the AC spawned just North of the DE camp.

There are several spots the AC can spawn as I've heard from other players. It seems that I have seen SGs spawn at all of those spots thus my theory of the SGs being the placeholders. The undead tower may or may not have anything to do with it but try my tactic of killing the spawn at night and see what happens. Maybe it's a trigger or something.

The AC was a pushover at lvl 44 when i killed him. Any lower you'll have to use the lvl 24 Shock spell which could make things more difficult (and eaisier to get KSed). I don't think you can solo the SGs below lvl 44 so you'll need a friend with you or hope that others are in the zone killing them. Guess thats about it. This quest is obviously doable ... it just takes ALOT of time more often than not.

I average just a little over 2hrs a per AC spawn. In the 12 hours I spent there, I saw 3 named SGs, 5 ACs, and 2 Terrortantulas...all "rare spawns". 3 of the ACs and one 1 of the other two were within a 3 hour period after I killed my AC for boots. The 3 hour period was to test my theory on the spawns and to get a second ring. My theory worked extremely well.

Through careful observation I figured out exactly how to dramatically increase his spawn rate. I can't force an AC spawn but I can sure speed up the process.

-- Sand Giants are place holders for the named spawns.
-- Spiders and *groups* of spiders (and *possibly* scarabs) are place holders for sand giants.
--Spiders/scarabs can spawn in groups of 1 to 5+. You must clear all the spiders that spawned as a group for that spawn point to spawn again (I think it's about 20 minute spawn time).
-- There are at least 6 spawn points that can be pinpointed exactly by watching for SG pops, or large spider pops.

So obviously, the plan is to kill all the spiders, scarabs and SGs you see. I killed some undead yes, but usually just stuff that agroed on me.

Camping it in OOT is longer process, but is a lot less work, and a lot less likely to get KSed (oh wait - you're a mage - we dont get KSed

Sure I could be wrong, but I'm pretty damn sure of myself and most of the people I've told this tactic too said they got him within 5 hours.
