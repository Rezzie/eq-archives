Verant had to make a tough choice.

On one hand, *many* lower level people were looking forward to getting some of the nice, new high level RoK items with *relative* ease. For what whatever EQ was designed to be, I'd say that for >50% of the people playing today, aquiring the best items is the main reason they play (best looking, best stats, etc.). While many of the people looking forward to those items would have never gotten them anymore, the proverbial carrot was always there. I will probably never go to the planes or VP(no guild, small circle of friends), but I could at least reasonably expect to get some (comparable?) items elsewhere. I will at least give Verant the benefit of the doubt on re-itemizing.

Even though it doesn't directly affect me (much), it is upsetting that the highest level characters can have items they got from an easier dungeon while I have to go to the (officially) hardest dungeon in the game to get them. Assuming, of course, I even make it there. And don't forget that those same people will use VP items to go VP and get more VP items, while I will have to use my, say, Sebilis items to get me to VP.

Basically, we get doubly screwed - we can't get those great items as easy as other people did, and those other people now have those great items to make it easier for them to get more great items
