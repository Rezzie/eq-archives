What you probably heard, or what the person was trying to say, was that you should go with the best 10 damage weapon you can find under level 10 because of the damage cap.

I don't know what is meant by "proper damage for your level" A tentacle whip is 4/25. A FS longsword is 6/28 and has a better damage/delay ratio, but bear in mind that the the TT whip is magic and has a proc. Generally, to determine which weapon is better, divide the damage by the delay (i.e. 4 divided 25 is 0.16, higher number is better), but keep in mind things like procs and the damage cap.


More on the damage cap
----------------------

The maximum you will hit for is calculated by the formula: (Weapon Damage * 2) + 1

Thus, if you are holding a FS longsword (6 damage/28 delay), you can hit for 13 damage maximum ((6*2)+1 =13).

However, to curb twinking, Verant has implemented a damage cap. Under level 10, you can hit for 21 max. All weapons over 10 damage ((10*2)+1=21) will behave like they were a 10 damage weapon. A 10/40 weapon will do just as much damage as a 20/40 weapon under level 10 - that extra 10 damage is "wasted."

For maximu effeciency from 1-9, use the *fastest* weapon you can get, preferably as close to 10 damage as possible. For maximum effeciency from 10-19, use the *fastest* weapon you can get, preferably as close 14 damage as possible.

Of course, if you can get an "uber" weapon like a nathsar great sword at 17/30, it's just as good as a 10/30 sword under 10 and will only get better as you level. But for example, a FS Two handed sword is better than an Exe Axe under level 10, and basically the same from 10-19.
