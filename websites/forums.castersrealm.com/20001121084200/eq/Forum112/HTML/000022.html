As an Erudite SK, I think that the Erudite race is suited for almost all classes to the same degree as humans, since Erudites are technically part of the human race, albeit having intentionally removed themselves from the rest of humanity. From a ROLEplaying perspective, the KOS/faction issues make it very challenging to be an SK, especially an Erudite. And from a ROLLplaying angle, Erudites are already gifted in the magical arts (look at base stats), use those extra points towards physical attributes and you end up with a decent warrior. Not as strong as a troll or ogre, but rather a good caster/fighter mix.

I also play a barbarian character, a shaman, and they wouldn't be suited for the necro/SK life. In addition to being created by good deities, they obviously aren't especially intelligent or inclined towards evil. Barbarians are simple people, and not suited for something requiring (theoretically) the study of necromancy, enchanting, etc. That's why we have shamans. Trolls and ogres get their necromantic skills down the road because of their deity.
