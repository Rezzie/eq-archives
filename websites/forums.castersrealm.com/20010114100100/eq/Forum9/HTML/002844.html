Well... there I am... camping the Ancient Cyclops spawn in Ocean of Tears. Suddenly the systemwide message goes up... servers coming down within the hour. Crap.

The next pop is the Ancient Cyclops and I nearly freak out. Tashania flies, take my shot, drop Tepid Deeds, chase with Discordant Mind, and Root the charging mob. Drop another Discordant and Root holds so I step in to let it work on my rune for a bit.

Step back out and drop another Discordant, this one breaks the root and I re-apply. The system message broadcasts again, but scrolls by too fast to see if it was different. Discordant again; the mob is looking pretty bad, and my pet's Rune III just wore off. The thing drops shortly after and I greedily scoop up my Ring of the Ancients! (Yay for me!)

Log out quickly and decide to browse for a bit. Soon as it comes back up I'm off to kill some shadowed men and then find Hasten.

Now here's the "cajones" part.

When you get a Honeyjum from Kizzie Minnietop, she repeatedly says "30 gold", yet 3 platinum works just fine.

During the Incandescent quests, they all want 50 gold... yet 5 platinum works just fine.

When I'm jeweling, the Fire Opal lady in Temple of Solusek Ro wants 550 gold, but guess what?

55 platinum works just fine.

Now... am I crazy enough to take 325 platinum to that speedy little gnome, after spending a week trying to get this damned Ring of the Ancients?

I mean, by all the examples, it should work, and if it does... I can hop on and say, "I had the balls to believe in my own findings!"

But if it doesn't work...

Oh man...
