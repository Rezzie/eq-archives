Here are the results of testing I did with a friend, using 2 lvl 1 necros, fighting rats:

With 1 pet:
The pet will take 50% of the xp if it outdamages the group (ie does 50% or more to the mob)

If the pet outdamages each player, but it still does less than 50% to the mob (ie the players together outdamage the pet) it takes no xp. (eg: pet does 40%, necro1 does 30%, necro2 does 30%... pet outdamages each player, but together they outdamage the pet.. result is full xp for the players)

With 2 pets:
If the pets together outdamage the players, the pets take 50% of the xp. (this one is important!! eg. pet1 does 30%, pet2 does 30%, necro1 does 35%, necro2 does 5%.. end result: players only get half the xp even though necro1 outdamages each of the pets.)


To Drakin: I don't have ShowEq, so I can't comment on it's workings, but it is very apparent that the players get only half as much xp when the pet(s) outdamage them. (at least at level 1.. for all I know this entire behaviour changes at higher levels)
