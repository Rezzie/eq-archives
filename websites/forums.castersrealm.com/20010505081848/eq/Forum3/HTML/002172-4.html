(I must preface this by saying I believe classes are fairly well balanced pre-50 but post 50 hybrids lag further and further behind.)

The first and foremost change for hybrids should be faster recycle times for HT and LH post 50. These are our native skills and should only get better with age. (Plus, tracking recently received improvements, so that further obligates Verant to an upgrade to LH and HT).

Second, the disciplines need to be revised (and/or fixed). Pals and SKs should have a defensive discipline.

Third, offensive/defensive skills still need a bit of tweaking. Paladins' damage output shouldn't be a complete joke - neither should rangers' ability to take some hits.

Fourth, hybrids should each receive a unique spell, or line of spells, that stacks with the buffs (or debuffs) of their parent class. Such spells would make hybrids more group friendly and potentially more useful in big events.

I doubt all four (if any) of these changes will be made, but even all four improvements combined would not overpower hybrids - they would simply bring hybrids up to par with the pure classes.
