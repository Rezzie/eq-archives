well well folks... I'm already prety advanced in our quest. I'm already on the 5th part, and it'll be done prety soon.

Here's some information you might want to know:

1) Our starting point is Shmenrick Lavawalker in Lake Rathetear.

2) There is only ONE path, good or evil.

Here's the steps you have to do, what I know so far:

1- You must kill Shmen Lavawalker in Lake Rathe (He's a 3 hours spawn), then, a Fire Spirit will spawn right where he died, BEWARE he is blue to a level 54. The fire spirit is KoS to anyone. When you'll kill the Fire Spirit, he will drop a damaged goblin crown, loot it and keep it with you!

2- Find Lord Berbugle in Lake Rathe mountains, he is a level 45 cleric goblin, that spawns in the middle of the roof of the tower inside the underwater tunnel in Lake Rathe. He will drop Lord Berbugle's crown, loot it and keep it with you!

3- Return to Shmen Lavawalker and give him Lord Berbugle's crown (WARNING: be prepared). As soon as you hand in the Lord Berbugle's crown, a Dark Elf NPC will spawn, she will walk to Shmen Lavawalker and kill him quickly, she will also kill the fire spirit that will pop after him. Then, hail her and she will ask you to return the damaged goblin crown (That's why you have to kill Shmen and the fire spirit BEFORE you turn in the Lord Berbugle's crown).

4- Give her the Lord Berbugle's crown, and she will tell you to seek Omat Vastsean in Timorous Deep, she will give you a shell.

5- Go to loc: -12 000 , -2000 in Timorous deep (I hope you've trained your swimming skill :P). That's where Omat Vastsea is.

6- After you give the shell to Omat Vastsea, he gives you a statue and ask you to locate one NPC who is meeting in Temple of Solusek Ro with Lord Giblox of the Fire Peak, give him the statue which is supposed to hinder his power and then eliminate him. He ask you to bring him his robe as a proof of his death.

This is exactly what you have to do... be ready for a LONG camp, cause Lord Berbugle is a very, very, VERY rare spawn. Lucky like I am, I zoned in Lake Rathetear, I was heading to Shmenrick Lavawalker to kill him, to get the spirit of flame's crown, then a druid said in /ooc "Who is Lord Berbugle, I got him on track"... hehehe

Good luck to you all
