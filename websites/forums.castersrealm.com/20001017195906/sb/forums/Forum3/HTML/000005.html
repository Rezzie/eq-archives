How will low-light and low-visibilty situations be addressed? Will we be able to creatively use the coloring of our clothing and the presence/absence of light to our advantage? Will character movement (both PC and NPC) generate sound? If so, will there be ways to alter or reduce that sound through the use of a 'tip-toe' command or something along that line? Will a character in metal armor generate more noise than a character in a cloth-type armor? Will a cavalry charge create a sound that thunders across the countryside? Will that Viking Raider who hasn't bathed in weeks be able to sneak up on someone? What about if he is upwind instead of downwind? Will the absence or presence of wind influence missile weapon combat?

In most games, lighting and sound seem to be used for only for eye/ear-candy and to create atmosphere. Will this game actually take these issues into account on the mechanics side?

From what I've read so far, this game seems like its going to be pretty detailed, but are these kinds of things going to be factors?
