AbMagFab, I'm not sure where you got your information, but i don't think it's true. I'll give a reference below to the info I've seen; if you can reference official Verant postings that say otherwise, I'd love to have the link.

The changes made back in November did *not* cause all pets to auto-dual-wield, as you seem to say.

What happens now is that lvl 39 pets and above will auto-dual-wield. Actually, the way I read Verant's phrasing it sounds like even lvl 40+ pets may actually fail the ability check and not be auto-dual-wielding.

Pets summoned with the lvl 34 spell have a chance of auto-dual-wielding, if you get that lucky RED pet summoning that would be lvl 39. How often has that happened for anyone?

Every pet below that still needs to be given weapons; that is, pets from spells of lvl 24, 29, 34, which are the ones that have dual-wielded in the past. Oh, and I guess if you were to use the lvl 20 summoning, and you got massively lucky and summoned a lvl 24 pet (which never happened for me, but then i'm not that lucky in EQ) you have a chance of getting an auto-dual-wield pet.

So, any level below the 40's (and maybe still after that) if you want to be *sure* your pet is using it's Verant-given ability, you still have to give it two weapons.

Here's a quote from the patch message made at the time:
http://eq.castersrealm.com/viewarticle.asp?Article=2964

PATCH DAY, NOVEMBER 29, 2000

- Higher level Magician, Necromancer, and Shadowknight pets now have the ability to dual-wield without being handed weapons. The calculation used to determine whether or not this ability exists takes several things into account, but for the most part 39th level and above summons (higher for the Shadowknight) will have this ability. It is possible that you could get a 'good' 34th level pet that will, or a 'bad' 39th level pet that will not have this ability.

Mage pets can dual weild at 24, however they have a very low DW skill. Thus, people who have tested these things believe that it's not worth the mana to give your pet 2 weapons with the level 24 spells as they will dual wield very infrequently. By level 29, the pets have a decent dual weild and should be equipped. At level 34, the highest level pet you can summon will dual wield WITHOUT weapons. All other pets will require two weapons.

As for summoning, waterstones disappearing on LD can be dangerous once in a while. Food bandages and water are inconvenient. And summoned bags are practically never used because of the risk of LD.

However, the biggest problem is resummoning pets until you get one of a decent level. Usually, it takes me 2-4 casts to get a decent pet. However, it can be frustrating as you burn a full bar of mana summoning green and low blue pets after you just had a monster pet before you went LD. I also hate losing pets with my favorite names. bye bye Goner.

This becomes less of a problem at higher levels when reclaim energy becomes more efficient. It is probably most noticable during levels 16-24. Especially when you want that even con pet at level 20. I have burned multiple bars of mana trying to get the perfect pet to solo only to go LD shortly after summoning the highest level one. Ack, no fun at all.
