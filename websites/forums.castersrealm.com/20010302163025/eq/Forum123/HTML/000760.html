Gnome - Shortest race, feels the fastest, look like little ninjas, COOL cultural armor, and just learn to ghetto bash. It's easy to do. 10% xp bonus (because warriors didn't lose their 0.9 xp factor, and don't share the bonus with the group anymore).

Halfling - Tied with dwarves on height, look absolutely FABULOUS with plate on, like a little armored ferret (with a pot-belly) and giant feet. Get Hide AND Sneak. (I stay bound in Cabilis--Sneak gets me past the guards--GREAT for Lake of Ill Omen.) 15% xp bonus (10 for warrior, 5 for race)

Dwarf - About the same lines as a halfling, look damned good in plate (not halfling good, but good), and they roll when the jump. I'm considering scrapping my halfling so I can roll when bringing incoming. :P 10% xp bonus

Wood Elf - Their armour looks like, but a little gaudy. They just need a few tassles on the armor and they'd look like nobles out for a day of hawking. They get Hide, and I suppose they're semi-cool. 10% xp bonus

Dark Elf - Ultravision rocks. Don't let anyone tell you otherwise. Look like demons in plate, and get Hide to boot. So they're a little fragile (no more than a halfling) and look like smurfs on some bad loco-weed. You can't have it all. 10% xp bonus

Half-Elf - Tougher than a wood elf, but you can still see... banded looks gay (sorry it does) but plate is pimp. LOTS of starting locations and access to many pimp items. 10% xp bonus

Human - Blind as a bat, great overall stats, nice homepoints, and nice looking plate as long as you don't EVER wear a helmet. If you wear a helmet you look like a robot out of Godzilla. 10% xp bonus

Barbarian - Have slam. Just means you don't needs a little dexterity to bash. Still blind, not too bright, deadliest newbie zone in the game, and I'll admit, they look purty schnazzy in just about any armor they get. NICE str and sta, good faction--a solid choice... if you can handle not looking like a rabid, pot-bellied ferret in plate mail, it's a good choice. The 5% xp penalty becomes a 5% xp bonus after factoring in that you're a warrior.

Troll - The regeneration is very nice. Not critical, but nice enough to warrant the xp penalty. Slam again, looks like crap in plate--sorry guys, you do. Especially compared to an armored ferret with a potbelly. That's memorable. I just remember yer teeth. On that note... Trolls have like 1000 teeth... which is kinda pimp, and there's a face with glowing eyes. Don't pass that up. You pay for the slam and regen with a 20% penalty, dropped to 10% for being a warrior. You can talk funny and people will laugh at you.

Ogre - They make perfect warriors. Can't be stunned in front, massive strength, talk funny as hell, and have some hilarious quests and background story. Plate look? Tired--because EVERYONE is an ogre warrior in plate. They grow on trees--that's also because they do kick ass. After warrior bonus, 5% xp penalty.

Iksar - Hope you don't like plate, and love to zone a lot and destroy loot to make room. Still, very unique choice and they hold their own just fine. About the same as a troll with a cooler city (much cooler) new speech impediment, and no plate that looks pimp.

Note on speech impediments:
Listen to how your city npc's talk--talk like them. Trolls spell quickly, leaving many letters off, and overuse z and k. (Think orcs form Warhammer 40,000.) "We needz ta killz sum frogz!" They have nasty little minds and just use a LOT of slang to talk.

Iksar are fascinatingly complex and brilliant, and other than repeating "s" a lot, don't really need much change. Just picture a raspy voice and spell appropriately.

Ogres are dumb as bricks. Picture someone talking like Barney. Now spell phonetically. Never ever use the word "I". Remember you have the pronunciation ability of a four year old, but a vocabulary of a 12 year old that repeats everything he hears. "Mebe dey wunt to fite wif us?" "Meez tinkin' dis froggy haz sum ishuuz tew deel wif, so me kill hims."

Dark elves always sound cynical and condescending, halfling use lots of weird words like, "Wooba!". You can never have to many B's and OO's in a sentence. Dwarfs are always *****ing about something, Barbarians are kilt-wearin' scots, c'mon. The wood elves usually think everything they do is because Tunare allowed it, and half-elves are actually very bitter--there a bastard race, remember that. Gnomes jabber. That's it. Just jabber and when you get to talking fast talk like Yoda. Think about Hasten--"Yes? You speak to me. I answer what is it you want--hmmmm? Answer quickly please answer you will--hmm?"

And Humans. Well... just remember that magic and dragons exist in your world, and some faction of your place of birth is corrupt and threatening to destroy your way of life. Roleplay accordingly.

Now to sum up my whole post:

It doesn't matter what race. As someone said before... you're a warrior--that's the important part.
