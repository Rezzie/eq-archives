Enchanters are awesome. So are necros. Anyone who thinks necros are useless on raids is wrong. Necros may not do as much directly, but 1) our pet 2) our quick DoTs (in fights where there is no danger of mob popping), 3) ST, 4) summon/conjure corpse, 5) mana battery ability, 6) healing ability (equal to/better than shaman/druid until they get sup heal at higher levels), and unresistable tap nukes make us very useful.

These are my two favorite classes. All I can say is that if you like soloing more, pick a necro. If you like grouping more, pick an enchanter -- enchanters are basically mandatory for most high-level raids, necros are nice to have, but not necessary.
