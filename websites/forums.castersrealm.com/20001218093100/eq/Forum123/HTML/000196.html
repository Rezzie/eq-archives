Axe of the iron back 2hs 16 37 (though you will only get the benefit of 14 of the damage rating before level 20)
Unless you have a positively ludicrous shield, you are much better off with the 2handed weapon. And you can forget about dual wield (after it becomes available) until level 30-40 at the earliest...except to practice your skills.
Depending on the age of your server, you should be able to get the axe for 75pp or so with little trouble. You will likely see people trying to sell the axe for as much as 200pp. Dont worry, other sellers will come along who arent in dreamland. If you shop around a fair bit, you will probably be able to buy one for around 50pp...again, depending on the age of your server.
Hope this helps.

oh yeah! one more thing:
you said you were a human warrior...the axe is also a lightsource!
