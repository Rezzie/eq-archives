This is a long-running debate, and unfortunately most people refuse to understand how the opposing view feels (yes, feels as it's an emotion reaction).

The people who like to inspect feel it is their "right" because Verant gave them the ability with the right click option. They argue that if it were wrong, the ability wouldn't exist.

The people who like to be inspected usually like showing off a clever biography or their equipment. They enjoy the inspection because of the work they put in to the bio or the equipment or both.

The people who do not like to be inspected seem to have different reasons.

1. They feel it goes beyond a polite glance in to more of a "pull open the collar and look at the labels". They find it intrusive.

2. Others find the inspection a prelude to begging or an undesired attempt to buy their equipment. They don't want to be bothered by that.

3. Still others don't mind being inspected, but are just annoyed at the spamming of "John_Doe inspects you". If the message went away, they would like be happy (unless they were on a PvP server).

You probably ran in to someone who found it intrusive, Orc-Man. To be courteous, I usually ask before inspecting. After all this time, I've never been refused.
