Time to clear up a few things:

The Book of Charm and Sacrifice is indeed on the left table in PoA quest room. Spawn time is roughly 12-18 hours.

Wraith of Jaxion spawns at the final building. The one that the last reaver and Lord N.A. spawns in. The wraith has a PH, and it goes around the building. Keep killing everything in that last building once you have cleared the reaver, and wraith should spawn eventually.

Ghost of Kindle spawns near the final stretch to Master Yael's lair. Somewhere inbetween city and the lair itself. There is a shaman epic quest NPC right next door from where the Ghost spawns. If you have a shaman friend that knows where that is, then you have found the ghost.

SG Cazel is most likely a random spawn, not a timed. I have been tracking his spawns, and I've seen him spawning as far as 23 hours away, and as little as 2 hours apart.
