What I've always thought would be cool is to allow players who play a warrior of the races in question to prove themselves dedicated enough to the ideals of those classes to one day EARN the right to those professions.

I don't think that just because a race can be both of the classes that comprise the so-called "hybrid" that the race should be entitled to the hybrid class too. Being a paladin is about a lot more than being a warrior who can also heal. There are ideals to uphold.

Doing such a quest would be indeed difficult and would involve ultimately taking the full exp hit at once that hybrids endure (for a halfling warrior, this would drop a level 50 one back to 40 in one swoop). If the quest were hard enough, it would be cool though. Being a gnome paladin would really mean something if there were only a couple on your server.
