Ok people lets be constructive here and keep up to date. People stop complaining that this item will suck etc etc.. If you don't care or want to do nothing but spew pessimism please do it somewhere else.

People stuck on early parts of this quest, plese READ the posts. Kazen is dubious to everyone, you can do the quest as dubious, HE DOES NOT START THE QUEST. You CANNOT skip parts of this quest, if you are gonna do it, do it, if you think you are gonna try and half ass your way through it you will not succeed.

Here is a Step by Step up to the point everyone is stuck at. Lets please only try and discuss where we are all stuck and not keep rehashing parts that have been complteted.

Step 1: Forget her name, but there is a Dark Elf Necromancer in Nektulous forest. She is along the cost to the east of the North side of the bridge. Speak to her and tells you to meet her master Kazen in Lake Rathe.

Step 2: Go to Lake Rathe, Kazen and his apprentice Emkel are near to Stone Skeleton Tower. Speak to Kazen and say "I wish to study the Dark Arts" At this point follow the prompts, and he will tell you that you need to find and kill Sir Edwin Motte and return his head.

Step 3: Find and Kill Sir Edwin Motte (lvl 33 Paladin) http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=899

Step 4: Return to Kazen in Lake Rathe with Motte's Head. Hand it over to Kazen. He gives you the Symbol of the Appretice. He then tells you that when you revice a symbol to speak its name in order to recive instructions on what to do next. Say "I have the Symbol of the Apprentice" He then tells you about the DE Necro chick in Nektulous forest that sent you to him. He wants you to take the symbol to her and bring him back the reagents she is collecting.

Step 5: Go back to where you started in Nektulous. Give the Symbol to the Necromancer. She proceeds to tell you that she does not trust you. In order to get her reagents you must kill a mage that dons the same robe as her. She is refering to Najena. She also hands you a Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice

Step 6: Obtain a Flowing Black Robe.

Step 7: Return to the DE necromancer female. Hand her the FBR and she will give you the reagents. (it is called something moss, don't quit remember atm) and tells you to take the Symbol and the reagents to Kazen's lapdog. (She is refering to Ekmel)

Step 8: Return to Lake Rathe and give the Moss AND the symbol to Ekmel. He will then thank you and give you the Symbol of the Serpant. NOTE: There has been rumor that this will not work when dubious. I did this as dubious and had no problem. If it makes you more comforatble, CoB or lich will make you indifferent to him.

Step 9: Remember what kazen has told you. Say to Emkel "I have the Symbol of the Serpant" Emkel will then instruct you to head to Kunark to find an Herbalist called Ssessthrass, and retrieve an Herb from him. He also warns you about Ssessthrass and tells you to show him the symbol to prove your loyalty.

Step 10: Head to the Swamp of No Hope. Ssesstrass is near the Field of Bones zone. Give him the Symbol of the Serpant. He will hand you the Scaled Symbol of the Serpant He will then tell you the following.
Ssessthrass says 'Sssso, Emkel ssssent you to retrieve the manisi herb? I do not have the herb. The ssssarnak guard the herb. Apparently it is ssssacred to them. Only memberssss of their royal family are allowed to partake of it. They live deep in Chardok. They are hoarding what little of the precious herb there is. If you have an army, perhapssss you can bring me the herb and I can prepare it for Emkel. Bring back this ssssymbol as well.

This is about all the progress made. Lets please try and focus on continuing from here. If you have questions please start new threads, lets keep this thread for progress, not rehashing known information.
