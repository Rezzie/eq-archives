The one good thing about the shamanic spears is that lets face it, higher level shamans arent all that common compared to other classes and the weapons dont have a high turnover. To get a good price you might have to hold onto it for many months, most high level non-shamans want the cash and dont want to hold it in their bank.

Its a lesson in futility. You can either spend hours and hours around at a bazzare talking to idiots that want to rip you off or you can set a reasonable price, get rid of it and then go do something entertaining.

If you want to melee then a weapon which would be cheap to buy is the Spear of Mortification. Dam 6 Del 23 Effect: Stun
The effect doesnt proc super fast but every little bit helps, its a pretty quick weapon, good for getting your Piercing skill up. I sold the last one i had for 250pp.

Other weapons you should be looking at:

Goblin Parrying Spear Dam6 Del19 AC4 +4 wis +5 SV Magic. WT5.0

Ebon War Spear Dam8 Del31 Range40 +4 str +3 wis +5 SV Magic. WT7.0

Harpoon of the Depths Dam8 Del25 +2 dex +20 HP WT6.5

Beetle Stinger Dam8 Del23 +4 dex +4 wis +4 agi Eff: Steal Strength Wt1.6

Tesch Val Sinisch Dam9 Del33 Range40 +4 wis +4 int Eff: Weak Poison WT7.0

Gloomwater Harpoon Dam9 Del31 +4 str +25 HP WT3.0

Barbarian Hunting Spear Dam10 Del33 Range 40 +5 wis +10 MANA WT7.0

Darksea Harpoon Dam10 Del28 Range40 +5 str +5 wis +20 MANA +5 SV Dis WT7.0

Slime Coated Harpoon Dam10 Del25 +5 wis +10 mana WT4.7 [Primary Hand Only]

Jade Chokidai Prod Dam11 Del30 +3 sta +6 wis WT4.2

Howling Harpoon Dam13 Del35 +6 wis +40 mana WT4.2 Eff: Stun

Some of these you wont be able to afford until later on, but many of them are Shaman only and that reduces the effective price of the weapon considerably.

I think the Howling Harpoon initially had the proc of Memory Blur, but the one I have has Stun. I wish it had Memory Blur, then it would rock. This proc seems to activate alot more frequently than the stun proc on the spear of mortification.
