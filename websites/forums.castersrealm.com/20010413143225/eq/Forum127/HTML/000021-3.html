How to get to Vessel:

For those who hat a useless longer smimming exercise in Cabilis, like me this morning, and now a lvl1 Iksar with Swimming skill 105. Here some Tips: ))

After entering from FoB, go left following the wall until you see water to your right and a broad stairs leading to water surface. You have to dive there, and get into a underwatertunnel with openings in the ceiling. Swim to the end of the tunnel, and from there to the 3rd last opening.

There is nothing to climb, so no need to search. just swim against the pool wall and look up. The trick is to swim exactly in right angle towards the wall, when looking up, your upper screen boarder should be parallel with the walls vertical lines, the you simply pop out of the water and stand on the pools boarder. If you dont go in right angle but a bit to either direction while swimming against the wall you jump but dont reach the boarder, and simply drop back into the water. Bad trick that, maybe its a good idea to levi up there.

Up there are rooms with lvl1 swamp spawn, moskito, leech, scorpion, and there are one way walls, semitransparent the side you cannot pass trough, invisible when you watch the other direction, and also fake walls with little holes where its transparent.

If you go wrong you end up in the last room, and have to dive down to go back to 3rd last entrance.

When going up thirdlast, take the first turn right, and i think agin right.. hm.. i have very bad orientation, so i ran around alot, just do loc, to see which turn. On the loc in Citras guide there was only a lvl1 mosquito, but if the loc is ok, i check back to night.
