At 16 your best powerleveling trick is the memblur one. Quicken them. Give them your buffs. Keep Rune I up on them.

Once you hit 29 your options for powerleveling expand since you now have a pretty nice damage shield, clarity, and a better rune. For powerleveling wizards, clerics, and druids I believe enchanters are the best. Clarity is very nice and we can do some cute tricks with memblur.

For high level melee classes I like Shamans or Druids since they have superior healing.

Make sure if you are powerleveling someone that you stop every couple levels so they can max out their melee skills. Casting skill can be maxed out while fishing/medding or working on trade skills.

a high level druid is the best powerleveler. yes an enchanter can use mem blur but how will the newbie ever get combat skills up, miss out at the low levels and you might as well not bother.

with skin like nature, cloroplast, and thorns, even an int caster can TANK well into the 20's, with pet or without. a caster doesnt even need to cast except to get the mobs attention. you can kill a steady steam of low reds AND keep the melee skills maxed. (casters can practice 1st level spells in a city to max skills later).
a druid enchanter twosome is the ultimit combo.

in the late 20's, it becomes difficult to powerlevel an wizzie, chanters can ks a mob from with a sliver or life left, and the druid can help fear kite animals for necros and mages. all the other classes can melee well into the 30's. actual melee classes can PL all the way to 50 if the druid is high enough.

tricks of the trade...
rule 1: do it in out of the ways places with plenty of mobs, forget loot.
rule 2: stick to yellows and low reds, this is the best xp/downtime ratio at lower levels and gradually go lower and look for steady blues at the upper levels.
rule 3: always let the druid pull with ensnare, then gets its attention and let the thorns do the work.

for low levels try orc camps in sro or lfey or bandits in wk, these are almost empty, get quest items for extra xp. many are close enough to take 2 or 3 camps at once.

for the teens i like ek.

for the low to mid 20's i like the centaur camp in sk.

high 20's low 30's i like shadowed men (rule 3)

beyond that i dont know, never PLed anyone past 35.
