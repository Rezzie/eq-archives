Here's what you need: a hotkey for
a. Secondary melee slot
b. An inventory slot where you keep you shield
c. A bash key.

Start fighting as normal, with your 2 weapons in your hands. Have your shield picked up in your mouse pointer and hovering over your secondary melee slot. To bash, switch the shield and weapon quickly, hit the number of your bash botkey, and quickly switch back. Pretty easy to do, once you get the hang of it.

Make sure you have a bank of hotkeys (on the bottom left side of your screen unless you've moved them) with at least one slot open. Now, open up your inventory screen and hold down the left mouse button over the secondary melee slot until a button appears in your pointer. Move that button to an empty hotkey slot and left click again.
