PARVO'S TOP TEN REASONS FOR NEW ILLUSIONS

1. Most illusions DO have at least one secondary benefit, if not more. Think of illusions as LONGER lasting buffs with a fun extra benefit of looking different.

2. Many (not all) of us started an enchanter because we saw somebody having FUN with their illusions.

3. Unless they are guaranteed to ALWAYS have a group member who can buff their INT/WIS, most people this level are not going to get rid of their INT/WIS equipment because we got a slightly better version of a spell we already have. They have maxed their INT/WIS with equipment long before they get to this level, usually.

4. I agree with others that enchanters not getting illusions after 44 is like necros not getting new undead pets after 44. It's silly.

5. I am often the person who buys and sells for the whole group in a new area BECAUSE I can change form and faction to travel where they cannot go.

6. Some of us use illusions every time we play. I am often the puller and using air elemental form allows me to run across rocky terrain more quickly without taking damage. When I solo, I use fire elemental and feedback to do 20 extra damage each time the critter hits me. Earth elemental gives me extra str that stacks with strength buffs when I need to carry a lot.

7. I hate seeing the one talent we have that isn't shared with other classes being lessened. Bards, wizards, shaman, and necros can all regen mana. A shaman can lower resistances, slow things down, and speed group members up. Tons of classes can root or stun critters out of reach of the battle. Yes, mez works better, but where is the fun in being useful for only one thing?

8. New illusion models for the illusionist class should be just as important as new pet models for the pet classes. Does anyone else feel like a second class citizen? Is it that we're only allowed to get enhancements that don't take any resources so they can focus those resources on the classes they favor more? What's with that?

9. Seeing illusionists messing with your head when you first start playing this game is a cool and fun discovery. It adds depth to this world far more than the text the NPCs spew when you type the correct key phrase. Pranks are so human, that until computers start playing their own pranks, I will never buy the label of "artificial intelligence" placed on any programmed sequence.

10. It will make the game more FUN for enchanters and the people who are pranked by them. This is a GAME after all.

Thanks for reading.
-Parvo Dragonspirit
