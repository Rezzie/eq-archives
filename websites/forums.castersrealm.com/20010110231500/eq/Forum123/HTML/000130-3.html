Weapon skill increases may indeed be affected by dex, but Verant has NOT said that it is MAINLY affected by dex. The attribute that most directly affects it IS int. I have witnessed this over the last week from a firsthand perspective. I am a 38th lev Mage, and made a warrior twink to play as an alternate character. Well, actually three twinks, not one (not hugely twinked either, just bronze and some fs weapons mostly). Made a dwarf, Troll, and Wood elf. The dwarf is now lev 10, and both the elf and the troll are lev 6. Let me tell you all something, Int DEFINITELY made a huge difference with how fast weapon skills (and surprisingly off and def skills) raised with them. The wood elf gained skill values at level 4 that took my dwarf to level 7, and the troll... well... quite frankly he will be more powerful later on in the game, but by level 5 he had not one weapon skill above 10 (though off and def were almost maxxed)even using quite fast delay weapons (such as langsax 6/23, dragoon dirk, also 6/23, and gnoll hide lariats 3/20 i think) I equipped similar weapons on all of the warriors, and the dwarf and troll will definitely be more powerful than the elf later on, but the elf blew both of them away in skill increases. Oh, by the way, all of the elves that are saying that trolls are as nimble as boulders, get a clue and look at the numbers... trolls start with agi at 88, and they can EASILY have 100 agi AND str in the 120's or maybe even up to 130 i think. Anyway, sorry for the long post, but i thought i would add my own take on this since i had so recently been experimenting with this exact stuff myself.
