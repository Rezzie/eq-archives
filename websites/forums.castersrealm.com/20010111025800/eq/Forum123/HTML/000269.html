I not one of your swamp brothers but i have a barb shaman of 42 seasons and a HE warrior of 52 seasons so i can understand why you want a warrior twink.

Ogres get massive str initially so IMHO its a waste to put bonus points into it. Besides str equipment is easy to find. Dex is only of limited use unless u have an uber weapon to proc (regretting putting 5 points into dex..). Best use of bonus points? Stamina and agility. Being a meatshield u must have the hp to get beaten down and stamina is a plus. (Compare a 52 HE warrior with 104 stamina (hp 2025) and an ogre/troll warrior 4 lvls lower u'll find the ogre/troll has more hps) Agility helps with ac and damage adsorption and in a warriors line of work, definately a plus to put points in them.

Hope this post helps =)
