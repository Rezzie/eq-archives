In Najena there are some barbed skellies that drop the focus item parts as well. They can be found in 2 locations. One is above Trazdon's room, as you go through the invisible wall... Go straight ahead and through the door, make the right, and there are 2 Barbed Skellies in teh room to the right... (Total of 3 Skellies there, though, 2 inside the room, one behind you as you face the door.)

The other location is near Rathyl's room, after you get into the Jail area. Rathyl's outer room is to the left, and the Skellies are to your front. There is a set of spikes (Like from a pit trap?) between the 2 doors.

The skellies drop all the parts and pieces... and do it faster then the Magicians in Najena do.. A plus to doing the Gators in CT though, is that you get one of the parts for the Circlet quest at teh same time... I've found the drop rate on the parts in CT to be much more rare, though.

The focusing items dropped off both the 2 Dark Elven Female Magicians (by the Najena sign and the next room in) and the Goblin Magicians in the back rooms (3 areas). The Goblin Mages have Warrior Escorts, so be sure to sick the pet on the Warrior and nuke the Mage.

The items can be obtained there, but it took awhile to get them (you may get duplicates, although each item is LORE). Received 3 items while I was there for about 8 hours.

As for the focusing items "always" working, I do believe that each pet will have the "stronger" characteristic upon summoning (for that pet level), but you will still summon low level pets...unfortunately.
