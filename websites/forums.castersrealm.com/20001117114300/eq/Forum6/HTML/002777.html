A good help, when asking PvP questions, is to ask someone who actually PvP's.

First off, don't worry about Harmshield as "me" said, since it's a level 20 necro spell.

If you want to humiliate him, pull up an earth pet, give it damage shield and start the duel. Put your pet on his (it'll happen anyways) and start UNLOADING your level 8 Shock of Blades on him. This spell has a 2 second cast time, instant recast and most importantly INTERRUPTS casting. A necro that can't cast spells is a dead necro. Be ready for him to kick off a shadowstep in desperation. If he does, and you get the "target out of range" message, fire off a bolt. If you get the "you don't see your target here" message, leave attack on and turn til you get the "out of range" message and fire bolts at him. Then try to cycle in SoB in case he tries to get back into range.

His pet will be dead pretty soon, and you'll be at an advantage.

For an insurance policy, buy some crystallized pumice from a vendor (vendors that sell potions etc sell pumice). It has charges of nullify magic which will strip off all magic based DoT's he casts on you (Heat Blood, Heart Flutter, Darkness). To start the fight, make sure your top "buff slot" is open by casting a DS on yourself, then buffing, and cancelling the DS. Pumice removes effects from the top down.

However, this isn't really necessary if you stick to SoB. Your friend will die with full mana.

Don't forget to screencap it, it's the "-" key on your keypad.

A properly played mage can gank a necro pretty easily in a duel, depending on levels.
