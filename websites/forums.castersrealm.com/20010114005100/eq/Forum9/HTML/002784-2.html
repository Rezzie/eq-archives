Who loves AE mez? AE mez is an emergency spell at best which I hardly ever load up unless I'm in an area where I know trains are common. 2 stuns, your best single mez and root will be a far more effective and flexible combination.

Two things I wanted to add to the discussion:

First, I LOVE AE mez. You just have to be careful when you are in dungeons that you are aware of your surroundings and not in the presence of the infamous Verant "leaky walls" - there's nothing worse than having a resist on a caster in the next room, who proceeds to nuke you to death when you can neither see nor control it in any way. My strategy when in a new dungeon is to scope out the immediate area and give it a whirl as needed, always letting my group know this is the first time I'm using Mesmerization in this particular room/hallway/pit/etc.

A second comment: I see that VERY few people keep charm up in a group setting. At level 41, cajoling whispers is essential to my spell lineup. Although I do not use it on every pull, it has made the difference between life and death for my party when we have an extraordinarily unfortunate pull. I'm wondering if most people who don't use charm went the INT route rather than CHA? I still have very low INT, about 155 buffed, but my CHA is 208 buffed. OK- reciting from memory an opinion I've stated on this same board at least a dozen times.

AE Mez- specifically the lvl 16 Mezmerization is arguably the most powerful spell in the game. It instantly takes up to 5 mobs out of commission. Its the most mana efficient mez you have for 3 or more mobs. Its long enough to be useful but short enough to charm a mob under its effects and use him in the battle.
