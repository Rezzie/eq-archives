Ok I finally found out to get the infamous wetnap.

It starts with Mayong Mistmoore himself. Here's how it goes. You will need a rogue for this. Go to Mistmoore, and near where the Maid and Butler spawn there is a secret wall that is only available at 7PM exactly on Saturdays.(game time) -- The wall leads to Mayong's private layer. It's got a few beds and pink couches in it plus a very big window and a mirrored ceiling. It also has a bar. Sneak in at 7PM with your rogue. You must be invis, if he or his company sees you (usually a few hottie Harbingers or a Mermaid sometimes) they will aggro instantly. So if you wait around for a while (its random, Mayong takes Viagra now) -- Mayong or his date will get up and go to the bar. After a few drinks they are pretty liquored up. Once Mayong has had enough to drink he will pass out on the floor. Make sure that his company passes out or leaves when you try this next step. When he's passed out you can go up to him and pickpocket him, you will get a "used Jimmy Hat" -- Take it and put it in a bag you will never touch again.

Ok now you need to go outside the castle, near the LFay zone line. Wonder around here a while and eventually a "Mayong Groupie" will spawn. Tell her of your recently aquired "prize" from Mayong. She will give you anything you want. Say to her "I would like a wetnap and a long kiss goodnight" -- Here's the tricky part. Give a friend all your money and clothes before you say this to her. If you don't you will be disconnected and unable to log on for a while. When you get back on you'll be stark naked, broke and only have a wetnap to show for it. So give all your stuff or bank it first.
So now you are naked and have the wetnap. Make sure you put on your clothes before going back to the Unicorn. If you don't he will run very fast away, faster than Scale of Wolf. Give him the wetnap and you get this:

Memoirs of a lonely Unicorn
LORE ITEM NO DROP
Weight: 0.1
Effect: Terrify Animal
ALL/ALL

I don't know what to do from here. I don't know who would want this thing. If you right click this thing with an animal targeted, it will break root or snare if it has it on it and run for the nearest zone line. OK for crowd control if you don't ever want to see the animal again.

I heard a rumor that there might be an animal... eh... "lover" that would want this book. Any ideas?
