I've only been playing EQ since January and will not confess to being more than a 'newbie'.....right now I've got several casters under lvl 10 because I just can't decide which one I like best.

But I've found a very common thing, at least here on the Lanys server.....

Under level 10 or so, people don't understand or pay attention to 'afk'. This doesn't annoy me if the person simply says 'hail' and upon receiving no response moves on....but people are hailing me, asking for buffs, money, items, trying to group, etc.....without paying attention to the fact that I am 'afk'.

I do NOT have this problem with higher levels, so I know it's a 'newbie' problem. I guess I just get annoyed because a lot of these people actually get snotty with me when I don't reply.

Although sometimes I am 'afk' only because I don't want to be bothered when I am medding, most of the time I am 'afk' I am truly away from the keyboard.

Just venting, I guess. I can't honestly expect every person who plays the game to know what everything means, even 'afk'....but it's happened 3 times so far this morning in less than 2 hours gameplay, so I'm just frustrated.
