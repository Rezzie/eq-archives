My mez hit the pet first but didnt stick and his Nullify hit me so my pet went for him and then his pet hit me. Both of us were solely worrying about the pets at this time. I had to do 2 more entrances to get it to stick. He tried 2 more times to root it and the second stuck but lasted around 0 seconds.

He saw me sieve him and turned to me and started casting DOT on me while i pillaged him. His first DOT got interrupted and he channeled through the second attemp through while i pillaged the rest of his buffs away. He started to ice me and i chaotic feedback'd interrupting the ice and then i tishania him.

We had a Large DD war and i got one of 2 through and he got 2 through killing my rune and a TON of my hp. My DD that got through and my chaotic feedback interrupts finished him off. I was at around 20%hp and his pet woke up and started hitting me again and my pet /taunted it long enough for me to root and sit down and start logging. The DOT wore off and I finished logging because it missed me on its initial attack.

That was an even shaman at 45 with self DOT and after tishan he said he had 66MR which means he had darn near 100MR not counting the buffs I pillaged.

Bah Shamans are the kings of PVP, someone try to tell me necros with their high MR and self dots can kick us around....
