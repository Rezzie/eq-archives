Actually it depends on what level range you're talking about. The druid/necro combo is probably best overall, but I think the druid/bard combo will actually gain xp faster at 50-60. For one simple reason: no downtime. Instead of both sitting and medding in between battles, the druid can med while the bard kills. Neither take damage. Bard provides clarity if necessary for the druid. No one ever takes damage, so healing isn't needed even though both can do so. The druid/necro probably wouldn't win in kills per minute over the long run.

This almost sounds more like an interesting contest than a poll.

1)Cleric Monk
2)Cleric Warrior
3)Cleric any tank

I've seen these three combos get to high 50s/60. (One person on two comps in all cases) Once the cleric has complete heal at 34, life is simple. Monk is the easier combo with feign pulling, though the warrior is "worth more" at 60. The group won't really shine till 34-60, but then it will be amazing in any area where you can get lots of single blues such as burning woods. Always get an experience ressurection too!

If you aren't planning to use the combo to get to 60 asap (and have some of the best classes at 60) and just want to play for fun, I suggest a druid & necro. The two best soloers in the game together will let you have a lot of fun playing and will let you level fast. Trust me: you'll get bored fast if you aren't leveling at a decent speed and getting to see and do new things every few weeks.
