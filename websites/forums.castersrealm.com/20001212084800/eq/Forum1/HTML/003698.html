There is a limit to haste, althouh I forget what it is. The cap is based on percentage of haste, not delay. The haste cap changes at certain levels, I believe 50 was one of them. 100% haste was one of the caps, sorry I can't be more specific; I seem to have misplaced that file...

Haste also works a bit differently than many people seem to think. The percantage is how much faster the weapon hits, not how much the dealy is lowered. 100% haste makes a weapon twice as fast. A 40 dealy weapon with 100% haste is hitting with a delay of 20. The same 40 delay weapon with 50% haste hits with a delay of 30.
