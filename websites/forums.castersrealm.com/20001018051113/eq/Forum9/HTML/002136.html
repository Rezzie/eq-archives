I personally would restart. (but thats just me and lots will argue on this on). I would put +25 in char and +5 int. Why? Well, with spending about 400pp (4 stein quests) I was able to solo/charm at 12th. But it doesn't really make that much difference. Charisma is so important to us, and I hardly EVER run out of mana in a fight solo or grouped and I still have base int+5 for a DE.

Anyway, as for stradigies... your biggest problem is how you look at your pet. YOUR PET CAN"T TANK... repeat it again YOUR PET CAN"T TANK. Especially at young ages. You need to look at your pet as a DoT with the ability to allow you to back up and cast ONE maybe TWO spells.

Aside from that. pulling with tashan is great until 12th (I think). Then you can start mez, tash, debuffing.

As for tweaking items. I would spend the 400 pp for all the basic charisma items. You can solo everything without the extra mana and the downtime is less if you don't cast as much. Anyway thats with the best player made rings, and wrists (elec, and gold), Oppaline earrings, +5 char necklace and a crude stein. Then I would get any extra slots with int items.

Now you will have everything set for 12th+

But until then... I don't think anything else will really help to much. basic int items might help. But I never needed any. Probably a full supply of rusty 2hs to give to your pets.. That might help more then anything else.

At level 6 I found a dragoon dirk useful. You are still meleeing quite a bit so a good weapon is essential.

Also, don't buy any rusty weapons yet. Your pet cannot duel wield for several more levels.

I always pulled with tashan at your level. Try it and see how it affects your fights.

Finally, I've done some tests with CHA and found that going from 100 to 200 does not make very much difference in resists on "mind" spells, so I think +25 into INT and +5 into CHA was the way to go. It's easier to boost CHA anyway.

P.S. I still need to do some tests to see how much CHA affects the length of time an NPC remains charmed but my initial impression at my level (46) is that it doesn't make much difference here either.

I'm gonna assume you got some cash to twink with. I don't want to hear any twink posts just stating something I think is fact. With that said the best thing to do is go all the way with cha with about 10 points into int. Why? Simply because that while cha gear is easy to obtain, most of it has low ac. There are a few items that do give good cha and ac such as the Crown of King Tranixx but they're harder to obtain. If you can afford to twink your caster with good ac/int items go for raising cha if not then maybe half and half. If you're poor, raise int all the way then go for cha.
