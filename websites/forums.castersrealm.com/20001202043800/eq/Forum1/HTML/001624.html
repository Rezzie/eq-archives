As an enchanter before the game became more of an endurance test lately forcing me to not renew my account, the ability to consistantly do things no other class could do and contribute to a partys success was awesome. While other casters simplly stood back and hurled bombs, or heals, I was up there with the tanks, freezing the mobs with stuns and mezzes so that my outnumbered party could emerge victorious or if really hard pressed, with luck i could switch a mob to fight for me for awhile. Enchanter illusions were great though not always so useful. They did allow for one to go places and see things that no other class could. It was so interesting to see sol a or unrest or mistmoore designs and scenery and what mobs look like without the blur of fleeing from a huge train... Illusions were great for pranks and general tomfoolery (second sets of ruins, cacti races, flying carpets, and running beds, the unpickupable plat piece, the 'broken' talking forge, chasing newbies as the horrible wild elemantal, skeleton,.... Heck just being able to see how clothes looked in different race forms was fun.

Another fun thing was that enchanters could make in game items that other players actually could use. At first only jade rings were decent but after the revamp, wow... Maybe someday the original promise of enchanting weapons and armour might actially come true.

What was fun was after a huge battle to hear everyone say 'Enchanters Rock!'

Tyransaer
unregistered 		posted 06-01-2000 08:45 AM              
What a unique concept for a game...have fun. I see so many people constantly unhappy over various things in this game. I always wonder how they could be "having fun". Apparently this game is more than just entertainment for them.

I play an echanter. He is level 35. His total days played displayed to me when i do the /played command shows 39 days played. Thats right. 39 days and only level 35. Why? because i mess around a lot in the game. I don't come into the game with the mindset of EXP! EXP! EXP!. i come into the game with the mindset of what can i mess around with today? I do so many little things, like wander, help people, cast clarity, kill a mob or two that may have swarmed a person and they need help, etc. For the most part, i am not concerned with gaining exp. Now don't get me wrong, i do enter hoping to get exp, but if i don't, its ok. As long as my session in the game did not result in anyone i associated with during this session did not get angry (including myself), then it means we all had fun. To me, that is the important thing. I tend to play with people that are of like mind. For example, my normal travelling companions, Shokakon and Faben, come into the world, we meet up, and then we kind of wander or explore until something strikes us as unique/odd/dangerous. At this point we either seek it out (and in Shok's case, often die) or we sit for hours and are totally amused by this.
Let me give you an example of this:
Long ago, before Tyransaer was even an idea in my mind, i played a Dwarf Cleric. Shokakon has always played the same character and Faben was at that time known as Ivaanhoeodell (later to have his name changed by a guide to Ivaan). The three of us were in Oasis, mindlessly chatting, killing the various Crocs of the desert area. We were near the Inn closest to S. ro zone and the lake shores there medding up. I finished first and got up and was just kind of wobbling around the area waiting for my companions to finish when i noticed a piece of the land jutted out over the water. The drop from the land to the water was not all that far, but enough to cause damage if i got a running start off it. Well, for stupidity's sake, i decided to see just how much damage i would take, knowing it wouldn't be a lot, plus i was a cleric, i could heal myself. So i did it. i got a running start, jumped up and into the water, and to my surprise, i took no damage! well, while i was doing this Shokakon got up, and was watching what i was doing and asked me how i did a flip in mid-air. At first i was baffled by this. On my end, all i was doing was running and hitting space bar to jump. Well, as it turned out this turned into a 3-4 hour jumping into the lake session doing cannonballs off the jutted land and laughing at each other as we fly through the air in strange positions/contortions and just dying in laughter at the occassional sound of CRUNCH as one of us misjudged the jump and didn't land in the water, but instead the land.
My point to that last paragraph is this: Even though the event was stupid, it created memories of fun and enjoyment with people that i care to game with. We didn't make any exp during that 3-4 hours, but instead, we made a collection of screenshots, which i still have to this day. I created enjoyable memories. Isn't that what this game is about? Does it matter how you obtained those memories? To some it would be getting to level 50 in 5 minutes, for others its obtaining that all elusive Nuclear device that will destroy any mob. For myself, and at least 2 other people that i know, its entering the game, and doing whatever it takes to make us laugh.


Tyransaer Delmaerth
Level 35 Enchanter
