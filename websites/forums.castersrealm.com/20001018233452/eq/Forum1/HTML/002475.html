Well Rubi dropped in Najena on RN tonight. I had just gated back from outside Karnor's and was planning to try to get the black tome for my newbie mage. I was the only one in the zone and went down to rathyl's room to start collecting keys. I had just killed the spawned and medded back up to full when Najena shouts something about the armies of King Tearis Thex and the whole zone repops with new mobs. Everything in aggs me and only being 35th lvl I gate out with a few hit points left and zone back into Najena_ALT1.

There was a 23rd lvl pally who had just zoned in and been killed during the repop who didn't catch on right away to what was going on, but besides him I was all alone so I put out the call to a few freinds and we started camping various areas.

After a few hours I had to sleep but four pieces of rubi dropped. Boots, a bracer, gloves, and a helm all dropped aparently off the frogs or the Teir'dal Guards. It seems the drops are random. Along with the rubi the enchanted weapons from the old Kith Halloween event drop randomly. Although I didn't get an rubicite I walked out with an enchanted morningstar to replace my sacrament.

It was nice for a change to actually be involved in a big event, much less be the first person on the scene.
