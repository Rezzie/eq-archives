Killed Neh'Ashiir in COM for the second time today and he dropped Torch of the Elements. It simply says NO DROP weight 0.0 Class: none Race: none

The common drop on Neh'Ashiir is the Caster shield, if the torch drops, he doesn't drop a shield. I'm assuming the torch is an Uncommon drop.

Neh'Ashiir is pretty easy to kill - need some good melee and cleric to heal them - he is very resistant to fire and magic - pretty much need a wizard to do any kind of spell damage - saying that, he can be done with a strong group of 6.

Ok another bit of information - Neh'Ashiir was changed from a KOS mob to Warmly. This means once you have killed the Reaver (50% chance another reaver will pop directly after, or neh'Ashiir) a non aggro neh'ashiir will spawn - this give you time to sit, heal and med up.

This change was added in order to let the shaman class give an item to him for part of their epic quest. you obviously don't have to give him anything - but the non aggro is very helpful - especially if you get unlucky and get lots of reavers pop one after another. BE SURE to tell the melee not to attack when Neh'Ashiir spawns - or you won't have a chance to heal up =)
www.eqmaps.com has a nice map of COM. The enterence can be found in EJ at about pos 300 neg 1900.

one more thing - once you walk through the secret wall and up the air like ramp to the first house be very careful, have a monk or experienced pller clear the entire building before entering. If you all run up the ramp, you will get about 15 adds all at once. Remember to bring a rogue to open the door to second and third house. Reaver is in 3rd house - to kill it - pull it to second house - there is a pit in the middle of the 3rd house =P

Ok - I hope this information helps - I didn't want to be an invisible lurker just getting info from other people and not helping out myself.

A couple of language comments.

1. The verant person saying it's dropping but hasn't been found. Dropping is generally used to refer to an item being on a killed mob. The phrase here can well mean, and IMO probably means, that no one has killed the right mob and had the item drop. This might mean the mob hasn't been found yet (e.g., like the mob reported to be swimming in lava hadn't been found at one point), or it might mean it hasn't been killed enough yet to see the drop on it.

Some of the quests need certain hand-ins to be done to trigger a spawn. Maybe we haven't yet found the trigger. As for confirming it's dropping, that means he went and made sure the mob is in game, and that it can drop the item, whether he checked code, or went on test and killed some himself, or how he tested we don't know.

2. The world below: the phrase refers to a sky item and two others "in the world below".
Clearly, the phrase means "below the sky plane", i.e. anywhere in non-plan Norrath, and this is further confirmed by the other item of the two being in SK, a zone that isn't "below" in any other way than below the plane of sky.
