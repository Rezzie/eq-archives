In EQ level is the most prominent feature of a class. It sets what you can hit, what can hit you (or can't since in this case it's inverse), what damage you do, how high your skills can be, what equipment you can use, etc. Sometimes you'll notice that it's other factors affecting certain things like ATK, AC or skill but first of all level directly infulences these things and secondly a fully twinked level 20 will still do less to affect a level 40 mob than a level 40 character using crappy gear.

In other words skills and sub-attributes play a minor role to the all consuming, all powerful EQ level.

Various things also start happening as you reach certain levels such as the wizards resist reduction or the ability not to have to use a spell book to med. That sort of thing, and usually at 35 so it's not continuous but still level related.

At least for wisdom casters, you gain 0.2 mana per wisdom point per level.

Mana = (Level)(Wisdom)(0.2)

Ok, what does level do...

more mana
more hp
higher skill caps
possibly higher nonvisible natural resists
for some classes visible resist increases

Hp is dependent on stamina and level. I don't know the equations for the different classes.

AC doesn't care about level. AC depends on defense skill, armor, and AGI...perhaps other defensive skills as well.

It's one big jump.

Not sure about slam, but racial abilities are usually set at a certain amount and don't increase in my experience (dwarf sense heading, wood elf hide, ect)

All skills execute a skill check...if it succeeds you have a better chance of a skill up than if it doesn't succeed. Sense heading sucks in the beginning, but with higher skill you succeed more so skill goes up faster. Eventually sense heading will get harder to train, like 150+ skill (skill maxxed here )

I am a master beggar with 101 skill. I know people with it maxxed or close. It is useless, although at maxxed skill you can occasionally get a platinum I believe. My record is a silver.

This depends on the cat...Karanas cats don't like anyone, but stuff like pumas aren't aggro to us. Oh and wolfform makes a lot more animals like you...the Karanas lions, some of the griffon family, ect.

The Tribunal worshippers don't have a single God, they have a panel of unnamed spirits that they believe will judge their actions in the afterlife.

Barbarian shamans are not evil unless they follow an evil god. Barbarians are the only "good" align shaman race.
