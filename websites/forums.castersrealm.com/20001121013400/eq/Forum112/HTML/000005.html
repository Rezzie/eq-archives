In order focus on the following:
AC, Dex,AGI Str, Int.

AC is the single most important way to avoid taking damage. The higher your AC, the lower your downtime.

Dex helps your random effect weapons and spells go off. A high dex is nice, but not a necessity.

If your agility is below 75, get some equipment to raise it to 75. Below 75 there are severe penalties to your AC, but above 75, the increases are not significant enough to warrant Agility gear over AC gear.

As an Ogre, your Strength should be great already. I wouldn't worry about this

Your Int is probably a little lacking.. you won't need all that much mana at lower levels.. and you'll have it at higher levels regardless, but the most int you can get is always preferrable.

Good luck !

IMO, the order should be this: AC, AGI, STR, DEX, INT.
AC: Makes enemies hit you less often, and if they do, it'll do less damage.
AGI: Makes your defensive skills go up faster which is good, and every 4 AGI is 1 AC. Also, have more AGI gives you an AC bonus, because if you have like 75 AGI, your armor will only get a 1-4 AC bonus, having it higher will have more.
STR: Hit harder, and you hit for max damage more often. Also learn melee skills faster. You can also hold more.
DEX: Makes weapons procs, hit for more damage with a bow.
INT: More mana, learn all the basic skills faster.
