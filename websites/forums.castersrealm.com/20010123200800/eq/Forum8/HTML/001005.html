Fear and Darkness.

Keep it away from you and your pet. You take less damage and so does your pet. Works good in a solo situation, less effective in a dungeon obviously, but if your soloing, especially outdoors make sure their slow and scared, then DOT away and med or just let the pet beat em down =)

I have used ST in a few situations, mostly in Guk, it either worked great for me, or it went really bad.

Shadow step is another spell I don't think ppl new to the necro class use to the fullest advantage. Its an excellent escape tactic, worst part about it is getting your bearings if you dont know the zone well. But it has saved my life more then once.

I never used root much, didn't see any point in it, I could kill everything I fought without it. But I could still see how some ppl think it is useful. As you play your necro more you will probably come up with your own strategies.
