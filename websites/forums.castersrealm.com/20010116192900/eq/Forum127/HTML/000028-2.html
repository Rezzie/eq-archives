Im regards to Akksstaff and the Najena portion of our quest:

-Aksstaff DOES still spawn (in Najena). He is a very rare spawn now (as of the last patch), and DOES NOT have a PH (Place Holder). Usually, the best place to wait for him is right out front of the "Najena" gate, and just wait to hear him casting (he'll make a pet when he shows up). When I saw him, he was underwater in the gobbo area leading to Najena's spawn.

-The Torn pages. These drop off of ANY female in Najena (Necros, Mages, etc). They also drop off of namaed spawns (I looted my Mastery: Air off of Rathyl Reincarnate).

Hope this helps.
