The following routine may improve your success ratio (better you get in the pull team to know what's ahead) :

- Dispell mob (recant if possible) / (Tash) / Stun / Mezz (dazzle still being the most usual form) / blur (no reo Amnesia ) later on if you err on the cautious side .

Tash is optional given the aggro, stun too if the mob doesnt aggro you or you don't use tash line.

Given above, the resist ratio is roughly the same as it was pre-patch, with maybe a tash or two now needed (not enough hard data to confirm) on some mobs.

Glamour's uses is not the same as dazzle. Its meant for some specific mobs which dazzle can't stick on.

Why would you use GoK in the downstairs of Hate?

GoK doesn't get resisted less than dazzle, lasts for a much shorter length of time, and costs one hell of a lot of mana.

GoK is NOT an upgrade to Dazzle. It's a whole separate spell line designed for level 56+ NPCs.


Rapture's a bit different in that it's got a really good resist rate (lifetap like supposedly), but it has a stupid recast time such that you can only keep 1 thing Raptured at once. Like it's an issue when Rapture costs 425 mana to cast in the first place.


But neither of these should be used on the first floor of hate. Just use dazzle.

On the AE stun question: While I've not seen it happen yet, I HAVE seen stuff aggro from the first floor to the second onto bards, so, presumably it can work in reverse in certain areas.
