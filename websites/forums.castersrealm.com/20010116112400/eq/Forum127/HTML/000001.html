Below is a description of what I have found of the Bard Epic Weapon (Fiery Avenger style) quest.

It begins in the Dreadlands near the Wizard Teleporter.

In the Beginning:
Baldric Slezaf is the start of this quest he wishes the two missing pages from his symphony and an instrument to play it on. One page is in two parts is says. From this point on I will have sections for each piece. He says that Forpar Fizfla has the instrument but will not speak to him.

Part 1: Top of Page 24

Konia Swiftfoot, a bard in West Karana that is in the first guard tower from Qeynos Hills zone will send you on a quest taking a torch to each person. From West Karana you go to Misty Thicket. In Misty Thicket the guy you are looking for is on the 'low level' side of the wall right at the main opening in it. From there you go to South Ro, the bard there is at the zone to Innothule Swamp. From there you take your torch (which you have turned in twice so far) to the next NPC who is in Lake Rathe at the Arena zone. When you turn in your torch this time you get a 'Proof of Speed' ring. Sadly you can't wear it but it looks cool. You now take this back to the first NPC in West Karana at the guard tower. Turn that in and she till give you the top of page 24. She has the bottom but will not give it to you because it is so valuable. She says it is likely as valuable as one of Mahlin's instruments.

Part 2: The Instrument of Choice
The gnome you seek out stays unwilling to talk to you and even other gnomes. To get him to talk you have to help his friend, a female bard who is at the docks in butcherblock. She lost her horn to a cyclops in the Ocean of Tears. The cyclops who has it is Queg Maelstrome... if you kill him you get the 'Alluring Horn' and once you give it to the lady bard she will give you a note which once you give to the gnome will lead you on to the next big part of the quest.

The gnome is willing to make you an instrument but he only tells of how to make the neck and head of the instrument. The items needed for this are as follows:

Amalgym Tendril (Plane of Fear)
Phingel's Spine (Kedge Keep off Phingel Autropos)
'petrified skull of a lycanthrope' I believe this is gotten in kunark somewhere off a 'lycanthrope' and not a were wolf but I could be wrong.

Part 3: Uncertain
There is a bard in South Karana (Kelkim Menkia), she is between the centaurs and treants along the zone line that is a 'drop-off'. She needs strings for her lute. I am not sure what she gives you in the end. It could be page 25 of the quest. It could be Mahlin's instrument to get bottom of 24. Or it could be strings for the lute you need. The items she needs to make the strings for her lute are as follows.

Guts of a Chromodrac (Gotten off a named Chromodrac in Skyfire Mountains. Eldric the Old or something like that) A few notes on the guy... he is like a Chromodrac but tougher, higher than level 50 AND he summons.

Guts of a Red Wurn (Gotten off Nezekezena om Burning Woods)

Guts of a 'huge' Onyx Drake (Gotten off Shardwing, a named Drake in Lake Rathe) Around level 40 but he hits pretty hard for his con. Not too much of a problem.

Miscelaneous Notes:
There is another bard in South Karana named Baenar Swiftsong. A druid, ranger, and I killed him. The rumor of him not being killable was false. However when he got to low life he did pop back to his spawn point (but at the same life we had him at). Even though he was holding a lute in his hand he did not have any item on him. He was difficult to kill because he would hit in the area of 200dmg or so (He hit the ranger for that much in fact quite often).

- Whym Dragonsworn
