Do all posts have to degenerate into flames?

Is it really that hard to post in a polite, responsible manner? Probably not.

Are druids all jerks? Of course not. Something to keep in mind is that fact that druids, probably more than any other class, are plagued by people wanting somethign for nothing. SoW's, TP's, Buffs, Powerleveling.

If I had to deal with a literally constant stream of annoying people who think that it's my DUTY to buff, sow, and TP their asses around, I might be kind of a jerk about it sometimes, too.

Get it through your heads, all of you who flame at druids, that they are in no way obligated to help you. If they do, great--they're nice. Pay them something for the trouble.

Why should a cleric heal every person who passes by?
Why should a shaman buff everyone who passes by?
Why should an enchanter clarity everyone who passes by?
Why should a druid sow, tp, or buff anyone who passes by?

Druids are also often lousy group members. Most of the time, though, this isn't because they're druids, necessarily, but because they've spent most of their 40+ levels soloing. They don't know how to group. Necromancers, frankly, have the same problem--they're often worse, actually, because they can solo better than druids in most cases.

Don't judge an entire class based on an observation of just a few jerks from the class. there are always more good people playing than stupid ones. ALWAYS.
