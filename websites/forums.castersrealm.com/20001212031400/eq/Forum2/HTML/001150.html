Gordonfishstick
unregistered 		posted October 04, 2000 06:19 PM              
We will not be implementing fish sticks to vendors in the near future, it is our feeling that such an implementation would unbalance the game and not conform to our vision tm. The idea at its very basis would ultimately make it so that anyone could acquire fish sticks whenever they would so desire, and that is not something that we would like to see happen. The risk and reward theory, that we cling to would not be satisfied if players could simply click on a vendor to acquire their fish sticks.

Currently the only way you can obtain fish sticks is by performing a quest that has been developed by our to notch quest designers. We do not wish to waste all the hard work they have already completed in creating this involved, highly time consuming quest to obtain fish sticks by simply allowing players to buy them from npc vendors.


- Gordon Fishsticks

Gordonfishstick
unregistered 		posted October 12, 2000 01:45 PM              
Thanks to numerous reports of abuse, we are once again looking into Fish Sticks at a special tuning meeting today. It was confirmed last night that they are occuring in Norrath at a rate which is potentially game imbalancing. To correct this, we have two options:

1. Remove all fish, then start respawning them slowly.

2. Slighty decrease the effect of the Fish Sticks.

We are leaning towards #1, but will be opening it up for discussion via the message boards and a log-in poll.

Also, we are going to add an additional component to making Fish Sticks: Tartar Sauce. This will be a questable item, approximately equal in difficulty to the class-specific quest armor. I will update this situation as soon as possible.

Happy Gaming,
Gordon Fishstick
