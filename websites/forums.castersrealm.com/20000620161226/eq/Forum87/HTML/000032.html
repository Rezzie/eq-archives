Dreffen,

First point I want to make is that Fear is not a place for a group of six to effectively clear the zone. I would prefer to take not less than two groups, and if you plan on killing Cazic, take at least 5 groups along for the journey. The first thing you need to do is buff everyone to the max with SOW, levitate, see invis, max hit point buffs from your clerics, druids, and shamans, and invis. Zone in two at a time, and race north towards the hourglass and the fire wall in the distance. You will run into the tenticle terrors, but do not be too concerned, they are one of the few mobs in Fear that do not see invis. Veer north along the fire wall to reach the North Camp. I do not have the coordinates on me to tell you what the are, but the first axis is around 900. Maybe someone else can supply those for you before you go. Camp out immediately after reaching the safe point and log into a private chat that you have created beforehand. When the last group has zoned into fear and camped, then log on together and begin the process of recovering corpses and clearing the zone. The best groups will include several lvl 49+ clerics, two enchanters, and a couple of monks and/or rogues. The slots can be filled with a variety of tanks and casters. You should have a couple of wizards for the amount of damage they can do as well, it really will allow you to clear the zone faster. The first mobs that you will see from this area are the spiders; begin to pull them and clear. Once the corner is cleared of all mobs, then you should move to the next corner and proceed to clear. Fear is considered an outdoor zone, so SOW and wolf form do work, and mobs can be kited by the pullers. If you follow this formula the zone can be cleared in 12 hours of all mobs.
