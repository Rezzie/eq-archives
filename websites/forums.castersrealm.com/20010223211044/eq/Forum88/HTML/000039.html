All you need to do is get invited.. I suppose it's different on different servers, but on mine guilds work together in the Planes all the time, because we don't have one or two "Uberguilds" running the show and hogging the Planes constantly.. there are a few guilds who do have the manpower to do it, but they're kind enough to let others up. How do you get up there? You could ask a group that's already there.. if you don't know anyone who's actually in the Plane at first, you probably won't get any chance at treasure, but I'd accept that and prove yourself to people for a day or three.. then when an item drops, people would agree that you've "earned" it. If the people there already know you, of course, you could probably start rolling immediately.

Another way to earn people's trust is to go to the Plane that doesn't drop your items.. there's always a shortage of enchanters and clerics in Fear, and a shortage of druids and rangers in Hate, so if you offer your services in that regard you're much more likely to be accepted.. and again, you'll prove yourself to the people there, become (hopefully) a valued member of the group, get experience at working in the Planes, and then you'll be invited the next time that group goes to the Plane your items drop in.

Now, if there's a superguild on your server who rules the Planes with an iron fist, you might be out of luck, but I can't answer that for you, I can only speak for my server.
