I don't quite qualify as high level just yet, so I can't say much about Jaundiced or anything on that level. Then again, I can. Get Jaundiced.

I do not think there's an order, at least not the way you seem to expect. After banded, Totemic is the obvious choce. The trouble is that only one piece gives WIS, so people go for other pieces of armor. A few common ones are gatorscale legs, chipped bone bracers, mammoth hide cloak. So, if you think AC is more important, wear Totemic. If you think WIS is more important, pick up something else.

Pretty much this order on armors we can use:

Patchwork
Leather
Banded (low teens)
Totemic (mid 20's to mid 30's to do quests)
Jarsath Scale (not wearable by ogres/trolls I think)
Rune Etched (planes armor, not wearable by iksar)
Jaundice (Sebilis/Karnors, not wearable by iksar)

I doubt youll find anyone giving away jaundice armor as it runs 3-4k pp per peice minimum. The bottom 3 armors you probably wont attain till your high 40's and 50's but it is possible to beg some of the Jarsath at lower levels found in Karnors, it is no drop, shaman only armor.
