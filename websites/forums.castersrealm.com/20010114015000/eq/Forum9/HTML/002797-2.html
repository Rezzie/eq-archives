Just to clear up some misconceptions and give some hope to people...

Wandering Mind.
Costs 60 mana to cast and gives 120 mana in a 2 minute time period. Logic alone would say this spell is pretty good. It is slated to be recieved at lvl 39.

The way the EQ engine is set up is we would cast this spell on a target. At that moment, the target would then get an icon of the spell on their screen (Well, not literally if they are a mob) and it would last for 2 minutes, slowly draining 120 mana. At the same time, you would recieve an icon on your screen and would gain 120 mana over 2 minutes. Even if the target dies before the spell duration is it, it will not and can not cancel the effect already on you. Doesn't matter if the creature lasts 5 seconds against your group, you cast it before it dies, you will recieve the full benefit from it.

This can easily be proven through the Necromancer spells: Leech and Shadow Compact (and their other versions).

The spell is not exactly the best, though I can assure you that if I have a spell slot open, I would use it.
