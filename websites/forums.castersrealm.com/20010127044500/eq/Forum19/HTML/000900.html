Just got some info from Skyll (fennin ro)

the dwarven mines start at loc 55 n190 in the city of Thurgadin.

The dwarf to talk to is named Normon Stonetooth.

Go in mines, take a right, go down the ladder, then take the rails to the end (crossing 3 or 4 caverns) then go left and you will find him. It's a maze in there.

Normon Stonetooth is at loc n120 n320.

After hailing and saying "what favor" he will give you an axe that you take to Hakon Brightsteel at loc n420 85.

We tried to hand in "A Small Chunk of Velium" and the axe that he gave us but it didn't work.

You need to hand in a "small piece of velium" that lookes like a small piece of ore, like what you would use in smithing. It's the stackable piece.

You can get the piece of velium from orc miners at orc camp in crystal caverns.

at orc camp loc n4250 250, in Eastern Wastes there is a steel gated hut behind the camp. Click on the crystal inside to zone into Crystal Caverns.

Kill Miners and other orcs in first part of Caverns and you will eventually loot a "small piece of velium."

Give a small piece of velium and the axe from above to Hakon and he will give you "Norman's Axe."

Give the axe back to Normon in the mines and he will give you "Velium Focus" nodrop quest item.
