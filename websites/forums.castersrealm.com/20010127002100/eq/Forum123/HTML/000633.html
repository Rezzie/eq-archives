It would seem there could be 2 ways for you to get some cash.

1> If you have a magic weapon you could hunt Willowisps. Go to the Island in Erud's Crossing and hunt. Lightstones and Greater Lightstones could easily get you the funds to purchase armor.

2> As a second option, you could hunt the yard trash in Unrest.. since small Bronze armor drops a lot from the house mobs, you could auction to buy the bronze for less money in the zone where people will sell for less to lighten their packs, and perhaps even loot a few pieces of bronze from unlooted corpses. Just be ever on watch for those nasty trains.
