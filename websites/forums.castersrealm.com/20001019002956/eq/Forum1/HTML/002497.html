Having played both Shamen and Necros extensively, I would recommend the Necro
for minimal downtime from the outset.

The Shaman has reduced downtime starting
at 24th (with the Cannibalize / Regeneration combination, augmented further if one plays a Troll or Iksar) and increasing further
with the addition of the pet and Chloroplast and Cannibalize 2.

The Necromancer starts with a pet, cheap
and mana-efficient stackable DOT lines (about six of them!) and Dark Pact at 8th
level. Combined with sitting down, Dark
Pact does not cause hit point loss, and combined with Iksar Regeneration (if one
chooses that race), even Allure of Death,
the 20th level upgrade, does not cause harm.

Don't get me wrong, my Shaman is 51st level
and I love him dearly, but if reduced downtime is what you want, the Necromancer is the ideal choice. He will get a reduced
downtime at the very beginning since he has
DOTs and a pet at level 1 and mana-recovery
boosts at level 8, while the Shaman has to wait 16 more levels to compete, by which point the Necro has already had the *upgrade* for 4 levels and has another 4 lines of DOT and an even better pet.

Happy coincidence that it also is the best class for soloing and is (arguably) the most versatile of the Int casters.
