Ok, a couple of notes for those who are doing this quest:

1. You do NOT need the Seal. I did the whole quest without it. Just make sure you use Collaboration on all the turn-ins. I made sure the Turn-in Mob actually responded to the prompts of the quest, but after that I just turn-in and went my merry way. As a note, mainly because I help on other Epics, I was 'warmly' to Jeb when I did final turn-in.

2. Modani is a pain in the ass. I heard ALL sorts of theories on how he spawns. None of them were correct. It is quite simple actually. Modani is a roamer. Wow, there are 2 roamers in that temple. If Modani is up there is only 1. Hmmmm.... Just kill the 2 roamers until Modani spawns. He is a rare spawn, so you may have to kill a while. I went into the room with the Iksar head over the alter thing. In the right corner there is a statue of an Iksar with a sword. You can sit BEHIND the statue and are out of aggro range of the roaming scorps. There is sometimes a static PH in this room. Punk him and he doesn't respawn. So, just sit there and bring up an Animation. Drop speed, MR, and Bedlam on pet. Wait for Scorp. Slow and Root Scorp and Pet will take care of the rest. Nice to put Bedlam on yourself to take the HT. Other than that it is boring as hell.
