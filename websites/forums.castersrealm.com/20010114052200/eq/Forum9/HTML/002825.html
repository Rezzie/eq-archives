Hmmm...I think you were hanging on the whims of the master jeweller--not the other players or your guild. When I saw the 30 minute mark roll by and no decent conversation, amusements or compensation to make up for my time loss--I would have told the jeweller to give me the rest of the bars and I'd enchant them at MY convenience. If they balked, well, then they'd have to get another enchanter.

Sometimes, we bring our own pain upon ourselves.

If you feel like there's a clique (and that's a seperate issue, but one feeding your frustration), you need to test the veracity of that. Sometimes a 'clique' is just the starling syndrome..the same people happen to be in the same place and do the same things over and over--more laziness than exclusiveness. Other times it is a clique. So go to where they are--ask to join. If you get any unwillingness or hesitation (if the group isn't full), just go ahead and tell them you're thinking of moving on, because there doesn't seem to be any real need for you with this group. And then go.
