It's going to take more than powergaming to run a guild.

Customer Service -- so far WP's been good about talking to the fanbase. But remember, this is a niche game. This isn't an EQ or UO where it's "play nice and don't hurt anyone," it's a competitive place to play. If you don't go against the fundamentals of the game, and only report major inbalances and/or bugs I think they'll listen.

Alright, as for the exp transferring...

Let's say two guildmates are both level 35. This means they had to attain that level through NPC killing. Member A and B decide they want member A to kill B for his exp points, and B will make a new character. So Member A kills him and gets up to say level 39 and B goes down to 26. He then erases his character -- now he's level 1. Anyone who did this would be my best friend, their own stupidity made them weaker. Remember, you always LOSE more than you gain. And if the difference is too much then you don't gain anything.

There will be a rough con system, within 15 levels I think. Remember, if you do have a close conning system you will have PK be more of a problem.
