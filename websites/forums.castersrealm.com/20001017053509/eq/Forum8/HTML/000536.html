Abashi (PLEASE READ)
below is one of your replies to a recent posters concerns.Source: Everquest Gameplay Forums.

(Poster Concern)
Add an NPC in an open area in a zone that both good and evil characters can get to. Make this NPC a robed skeleton that speaks evilly and ominously.

Pay this NPC 3000pp and ALL corpses in all zones will be teleported to your feet for looting.

(Abashi Reply)
I don't think that an NPC of this type would be effective or necessary really. You could just find a necromancer and pay him 150pp per corpse to summon them to your stronghold in the zone.

(My Reply and Concern)
Your reply raises some Eyebrows within the Necromancer Community. The poster had an innovative idea, but was answered quite poorly IMHO.

The Ishva Mal camp is probably one of the worst camps in Norrath. The % of Level 39+ Necromancers with this spell is easily eclipsed by the # of Melee Characters wielding Mithril 2 handers. My Point is this:

Please don't refer a player to another class knowing full well that the "Summon Corpse" spell is a Common drop off an ULTRA rare spawn. This spell should have been a 49th level researchable scroll requiring a high skill for success.

Instead, what we have is: Any Necro determined to possess this sacred scroll must easily block out 8-10 hours of their time to HOPEFULLY obtain this spell. I will not argue the validity of maintaining a difficulty level for Quest items, but Jesus Christ, don't make it seem that a Player can go to any 39+ Necro and Have Their Corpse summoned.

Normally when I read your replies, they are well thought out. But I must point out that this particular one, SERIOUSLY side stepped a major pit fall, since the poster evidently had no back ground to the difficulty of obtaining the spell.

Which raises another issue. Making the 51+ Necro spells RARE drops off of 45+ MOBs, You have totally discarded the Research Skill. The investment needed to successfully combine 49 words was completely in vain. The skill has no future past level 49.

Instead, The pure Casting Class (Necros/Wizards/Enchanters/Mages) have been jilted and placed in with the other hybrid casters to obtain their scrolls thru Mob slaying. Essentially, The Hybrid Casters should have been subjected to obtaining their 51+ spells thru Mob Slaying, since they have always had the convience of purchasing their spells thru a merchant.

The Pure Casting Classes should have been kept down a path to maintain a high level in the Research Skill to successfully obtain the 51+ spells. Instead, what we have in the Necromancer case. Our Pets being referred to only as spells that Flavor to a Necromancers Spell Book instead of the Core Spells which can be purchased or more importantly RESEARCHED! A response to this posting would be greatly appreciated.
