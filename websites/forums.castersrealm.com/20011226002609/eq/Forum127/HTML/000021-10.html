For anyone else going for Vessel, you can save a lot of time by not going thru the shamans guild, just go underwater, a hacked together guide is about 400 posts back I think, I'll summarize.

When you enter east cabilis from the field of bone, go straight, keep the water on your right. Past the bridge you will see some stairs going to water level, under those stairs is a underwater passage.
In the passage it goes right, left, right, left. Halfway down this part of the tunnel is the opening to the area Vessel is in, go to the right or left side and look 90 degrees up to jump out. Go in the tunnel, it branches (left and right), go right, it branches (left and right), go right, turn left and you enter a room shaped like a octagon to the right is where Vessel spawns. The door you are looking thru is actually a one way wall, if you walk thru it you cannot walk back, so it might be good to pull him from that room unless your sure you can keep him from running.
When your done, go in Vessel's room, turn right thru another one way wall, left, then right into a room with water access, jump in and turn right, after a left and right turn you'll pass the opening to Vessel and if you keep going you'll exit back into the water in cabilis.
To be sure, you might wanna make a newbie iksar to check the place out.
