Head: Runed Circlet, AC4 WIS+4 INT+4, lightweight, comes from Permafrost. 200-400 PP.

Back: Mammoth Cloak, AC7 WIS+4 Cold+5, comes from Permafrost. 2500-3500 PP.

Hands: Splitpaw Hide Gloves, AC4 DEX+3 WIS+2, lightweight, comes from Paw. 25 PP at most.

Belt: Belt of the Cenobite, AC5 WIS+7 Cold+10, comes from the Burynai Grand Cenobite in Frontier Mountains. 3000 PP.

Weapon: Sharkbone Warhammer, 1HB 8/26 WIS+5 weight 3.0. Comes from a named shark in Kedge Keep. 1500 PP.

Hope all this helps. I noticed you have a lot of items which add STR, if that is a problem for you then you might consider trading out your WIS+6 rings for the gold ruby rings that add to STR and WIS (a lot of elf clerics do this).

Congrats on level 50 Shis!

It's been a while but aren't the Quest Bracers +wisdom? You might try for those.

The Aegis is definitely an item you should go for. As a dark elf you will have to get good enough faction to enter Qeynos (kill gnolls I think?) and once inside it is a steep climb to get faction with the cleric who gives the quest.

You will read lots of ways to raise faction, including a complicated twinking exercise involving Koalindl fish, but the simplest way is to hand Lashun Novashine (North Qeynos) 2gp at a time for an hour or two (assuming he will talk to you from the start).

It will cost 100 to 200 plat, or more, depending on how he regards you at first, but it is probably quicker than collecting bandit sashes and dead fish or running errands for the NPCs.

Finally, as someone mentioned above, if at all possible, see if you can go on some trips to Hate for the purple stuff.

Good luck,
Temuli
