Hi there,

Me and my brother wanted to create 2 new characters, that we will use for role playing. We have tried other classes so far and have main characters. We want to create this new set just for fun and traveling. We also are not planning to twink them and just see what we can gather with these characters...like total newbies.

The first class we selected is a Rogue Halfling, the reason for this...we already created tanks, hybrid healers, and definitely don't want to sit and heal all the time>>>Cleric. Monks are cool and are damage dealers, but we want one to pick lock and to sneak for corpse retrieval. Role playing a Halfling Rogue sounds fun too. And Btw, they the most liked race in Everquest...of course not in Kunark or Cabilis.

Now we need help on selecting the second class. We have only 2 options for this:

1. Magician
2. Wizard

Now, with the Magician, he can summon a pet to tank for us while the Rogue practices backstabs, we can run incase of bad pulls and leave the pet, their DD spell is second compared to wizards, and can summon food, water, etc., and especially bandages to heal ourselves to 50 percent.

Now, with the Wizard, he has great DD spells, and can teleport to different places, also planes, nice for adventuring, especially to the upcoming Velious Expansion. But the drawback so far is, the rogue won't be able to practice backstab that well, and he really is not a tank. No bandages and supplies.

Can you please give me your opinions as to which class among the 2 mage types will do better and produce more fun?

BTW we do not want healers or pure tanks since we have been playing hybrids since.

Thanks

The main problem you will have with a rogue and mage/wiz or necro combo is the lack of heal which results in way too much downtime (for my liking). I tried a rogue/necro team but gave up by level 20 because of the down time we were having after tuff fights, and switched to necro/druid and shamen/rogue. Sure it can be done but one of the healing classes makes a far more viable team.

For sheer versatility a rogue/druid combination takes some beating, you have a decent heal rite from the start, snare so you can always escape, sow at 14, dots and good nuking ability. Backstab fits rite in there while the druid kites. And of course the ability to travel almost anywhere quickly eventually.
