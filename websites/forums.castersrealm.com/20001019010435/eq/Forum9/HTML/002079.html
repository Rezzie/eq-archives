I have 2 major regrets about my 51 Enchanter:

1. I did not start a female gnome so that I could use Illusion: Wood elf to help pass the time more quickly while medding.

2. I chose a religion.

The fact is, most places you go are far easier with Illusion, with Invis, and without a religion. I sincerely hope that some deity based items get added to the game to make the albatross around my neck (Brell Serillis) worth it.

Still, I haven't really been denied access any zone or quest in the game. Faction can be increased in just about any location via quests, killing specific mobs, etc. To be sure, I spent some significant white-knuckle time because of ol' Brell, but have really only died once due to faction from religion.

If you are really concerned about it, make a second enchanter. Keep your current one for trade skills. You will have to level up 20 levels, but hopefully with some help from your buds.
