Overthere is probably to high lvl for solo at lvl 16. Wait until 20, then most stuff is blue or yellow. Rhinos are good too, drop up to 1pp in cash, and rarely a rhino horn which you can sell for a few pp, depending on vendor. I saw one for sell at a Neriak vendor for 32pp!! I went from 22 to 23 in 4 hours at the statue near the warsliks zone.

Now, for where to hunt until you get to lvl 20. I did EK from 16 to 17, hunting reavers down by the river. I had a lvl 16 shaman partner for this. At 17 I went to camp Chief Shralok in Kithicor forest. I was there from 17 to 20. I know you are probably worried about the undead at night. Well, I stayed high up on the wall next to the spawn loc, which is in the SW corner of the zone, and I never even saw a high lvl undead. I could pick them up with sense undead, but never saw one, and believe me, I was looking a lot the first night I camped there.
The chief is EASY, drops from 3gp to 1pp, plus, rarely, a word or weapon. If you feel brave during the day, you can pull his cousins from the forest floor, they were blue at 18. I think the chief is on a 6 minute timer. I used this tactic. Sic pet, engulf, heat blood, then step in a whack him or lifetap. No fearing is needed or wanted. You want to keep him up on the wall at all times. He also drops orc scalps, but my faction with HH did not get high enough to turn in to the guard, BUT I am no longer KOS in HH, due to the faction from killing orcs.
