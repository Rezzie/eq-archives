Well fellow enchanters, here are some thoughts on the scroll.
When mizzle says he fears it is lost for good doesnt mean that "it is lost for good", but that he thinks it might be, but unsure. It is still out there. When you say something is lost, doesnt mean that it is lying on the ground in this case, but that it is no longer in possesion of the known group(ie. I lost my scroll to a gnoll). When somone is lost, they are dead in this case. Scenario: person protecting(doesnt mean they have it on person) scroll, gets killed, killer takes scroll. What would you say knowing this(mizzle). It was lost along with its protector. I fear it is lost for good.
So...Find who killer is, and take back scroll. This way the scroll will be lost to them.
Another thought(although a long shot) is that he says "during" one of the raids on FV. This at first glance implies it was FV where it was lost. It could mean that it was lost "during" the time, not the place.
