Aradi
unregistered 		posted October 20, 2000 05:57 PM              
The Neriak NPC is easy. Yes she spawns in the room with the gallow/noose thingie (it is directly across the hall from the coffin vendor). Leave your group (a good group of 6 can do it) at the zone line, and have someone not KOS in Neriak pull her to the zone. She is factionless, so the guards don't aggro as you pull her past them (as long as the puller isn't KOS). Be sure to pillage her because she buffs herself with DI (free CHeal when she gets low). When/if she runs she will head toward the patrolling guard, so having non-KOS people helps.

The Cabilis NPC - someone posted good directions in one of the old threads so I wont rehash. Practice it with an Iksar newbie. When I've done this, I led a 55+ mage in and had him CoH everyone. I highly recommend 2 good 55+ groups. I've killed him once with 14 and no casualties, and another time with 12ish and we barely killed him (i.e. all the tanks were dead when he died). Bringing a L60ish shm/mag who can stick a malosi on him helps TONS. Otherwise wiz lure spells are pretty much all that is going to get through. Bring beefy tanks because he hits HARD.

No idea if allure will work on Jaxion/Felia, sorry.
