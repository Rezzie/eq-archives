Hello,

There is a Bard in the Hogcaller's Inn in West Freeport. I cannot recall her name. She drops a lute that is worth a platinum, depending on your faction & charisma.

A High-Elf Lady comes into the room every 20 minutes ... she can be dangerous because she likes to ignore your pet and she can Lay-on-Hands; but, she is huntable.

Here is the scenario...

1. Enter the Hogcaller's Inn w/o your pet. The weak monk in the front room hates skeles. You can kill him but the barman and guards may take revenge.

2. Take a left and go down the hall. Avoid the stairs, to the right, because the High Elf Lady's bodyguard is at the top, can see Invisible, and will rip you to shreds if the Faydark Champions want your head.

3. You will enter the back room and the Bard will be on the right. She is the one holding a lute. The Barmaid will not attack you unless your Freeport Merchant faction is bad.

4. Summon your pet and have fun with the Bard. The Lady will walk into the room every 20 minutes but if you are in the corner, opposite of the hall she will not "see" you because she does not come too far into the room.

Even if she does see you, she will not bother you if the Faydark Champions do not hate you. I had hunted enough Sisters by then that the Champions had a reward for my death. :-)
