If you want to get GREAT EXP, especially for the last lvl before a new ring of spells you need to go to Kunark. Hopefully you have the add-on. I have gained one lvl a night since i've been in Kunark, and this includes lvl 15. I think I got all of 15 in about 3-4 hours. Just remember to keep a damage shield on your pet and to heal when necessary, hardly any downtime.

I personally went from level 12-17 soloing Misty Storyswapper in W Karana. She's found due north of the Q Hills zone, under an awning at the merchant villa. Like her counterpart in Freeport Hogcallers in, she drops a mandolin selling for 1pp plus other stuff. Unlike the Freeport bard, Misty is a perfectly safe spot to travel to, from, and camp. Use earth pet, fire shielded, and blast away. She spawns every 6 minutes.

After you make 16, there is a great spawn in Neriak few are aware of. In the 3rd Gate, in the Maidens Fancy strip bar you will find a troll guard named Ratraz. He is level 19, spawns every 11 minutes. You can kill him without any other NPC aggro'ing onto you. Again, earth pet for root, fireshielded. If you get wailed on, simply run over the merchants' bar, and Ratraz is forced to instead run through the door and around to get you. Then jump back over the bar, he's again forced to run around through the door because of a pathing error there. The entire time he's chasing you, never able to catch up, your pet is hammering him from behind. Ratraz drops up to 2pp cash, plus a warclub every time that sells to the merchant beside him for 5pp 3gold. That's incredible money for your level.

You lose faction with the merchants and inner guard each time you kill him. At the same vendor, buy "red wine" and head to library. In the only room in the library stands a scribe. hand him 4 wines, and you regain your faction.

You can work Ratraz for a ton of cash and fast exp - the only downside is his 11 minute spawn, but it gives you plenty of time to do the wine quest. You must, MUST do the wine quest if you kill Ratraz, or you will eventually get KOS. I advise you to buy enough wine and turn it in to get your faction to warmly before even starting.

When I made level 20, I had some really nice stuff, plus over 1kpp in the bank from Misty and Ratraz.
Hope some of this spew is helpful-
Anthe

since I just wizzed through 15, 16, and hit 17 last night I would love to share how I did it. This is of course under the pretense that you don't like to die (and I myself am NOT the type to take big risks).

At 15 I went into Butcherblock Mountains on Faydwer. There is an orc spawn N of "2 houses". If it hasn't immediately been camped , there will be 3 orcs (typically 2 Cents and an Oracle) waiting. At 15 it's darn near impossible to kill them all. Have someone (lvl 30 is fine) clear them at a decent interval. Ideally what you want is one spawning ever 4 minutes. That will give you time to med and your pet to heal. After the spawn has been broke, one orc will pop every 4 minutes, and after the third there will be a 7 minute interval till the next round of 3 start ( a good time to run back to two houses and sell your loot). Speaking of which, cent belts and armor and GP drop like crazy. The cent belts can be taken to kaladim for a quest. I won't elaborate.

LvL 16 I went to Lavastorm and punched my way through rock dervishes and lava crawlers with my water pet and the lvl 8 blades spell. Also, I smacked around Fire Imps and Fire elementals with ward summoned spell. I rode that to 17. Loot is good with bloodstones, brickes of ore(15lbs! heavy!) ashroot, and other various 4-9gp items. I walked out with 20pp and lvl gained.

Hope I've helped...
