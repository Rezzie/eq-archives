Here are some replies to previous posts :

* Ogres cannot be stunned from the front, it's a racial ability. I have a both ogre and troll chars, ogre has never been stunned, troll is stunned multiple times in combat

* XP distribution in a group is based on your current xp, not level, so a SK will get more xp from group total than her level will indicate. (Hybrids and monster races have xp penalties) Check out this site to see xp required for level by race and class : http://hackersquest.gomp.ch/html/xp/xpchart.html

* STR is not just the amount you can carry, it makes a difference on your ATK. It's easy to test, just look at your ATK before and after casting a syphon str spell. On me at least, it went up by 5 or more points.

* While at my current level I can solo whites and some yellows, but I am not indestructable. I also have a mage who is much more powerful at the same level (solo-wise, I mean)

* Your pet is not worthless, it is a tool for soloing. It is not designed to take damage, but if used correctly it can greatly increase YOUR damage output. Turn off it's taunt and think of it as a walking DoT. At 12th level it increases my total damage output by up to 25% if equipped with a rusted 2h (up to 20dmg a pop). The higher level pets can bash also, so that helps you when fighting casters (2 bash instead of 1).

* Each race has it's advantages and disadvantages, but the Troll SK has the biggest xp penalty for a reason. I heal twice as fast as other folks (4hp/tick rather than 2hp/tick). I slam for 8-10dmg, added to the 30 or so from my 2h slasher, and 10-20 from pet, that's up to 60dmg per round at 12th level. Not too shabby for a girl. The way I see it, I can get mana and int items, but items that duplicate these abilities will be much rarer/noexistant.

* As for soloing ability, SK is the best melee soloer because of her ability to (fear/darkness) reverse kite as well as having a pet. If you're having trouble soloing, maybe you should alter your methods =)
