I am a level 60 Necromancer. I read most of this thread and I honestly believe that alot of people are looking through blinders. I have not been much of a soloer, I always enjoyed playing in groups. There is alot a necromancer can bring to a group besides being a mana battery.

First off, necromancers can be great crowd control, I cant even count the number of times I have saved a raid when the Enchanter took a dirt nap, being able to root and mez are fantastic, but the catch here is that a necro can FD, Invuln, and heal himself, essentially making him a self sufficent enchanter.

Necros have the most mana efficent heal in the game, I know some people complain that you cant lifetap some "uber" mobs. While this is true, the pact series of heals is dispellable on your end, net result is a 500 HP heal for the cost of 40 mana or however much mana annul is.

Necromancers can do a hell of alot without pets, mages can not, for this reason necro pets should NOT be on par with mage pets.

Naturally a necro isnt the best choice for a specific role, healer, crowd control etc. But i cant think of another class that has the ability to adapt on a whim to his surroundings.

While other characters gain levels, it become more and more evident that they only serve to play "one" role in a group, while a Necromancer realizes how diverse his abilities are.

Just some food for thought.
