Folks you miss something.
You always only look at Dmage/Delay ratios.
But at lower levels I would still go with Wu Sticks.
Why? Simple:
At low Levels your combat caps are also lower resulting in much less hits. If you have a slower weapon and you miss, then the time until you get the next hit chance will higher. Resulting in overall less hits per minute.
So what I try to tell you is that if you have faster weapons like Wu Sticks, you will hit more often per minute than with slower weapons. Resulting in a higher damage output.

To Test this theory (Yes as you see in all of my postings I love testing =P) I gave a friend who has a lvl 26 Iksar Monk 2 KD's and 2 Wu Sticks. We selected to try them on blue conning Forest Giant Greenwoods in Warsliks Woods.
The result proved my theory, with the Wu Sticks the Forest Giants went down a lot faster, which means to me the damage output was higher. Unfortunately he forgot to switch logging on but I timed how fast the Forest Giants went down and I can say that with the KD's the Giants went down up to 20% faster.
Unfortunately we had no time to test Wu Stick with KD offhand, but if we have time for a test again I let you know.

So far I am convinced that Wu's are better at any level. Because when you start to hit more frequently with your well trained skills they start proccing.

As for the 50 plus Game you will be ending up 2 Hand Blunts anyways, but if no decent 2 Hand would be available to you I would stick with Wu's until you drop over a IFS or so. The most damaging weapons later in the game are 2 Hands.

Just for the record I am not a rich twinked b**** the KD's were lend from guildmates which were interested in the testing. And I personally use 2 Wu Sticks.

I disagree with Trk. Damage ratio does matter except when you factor in the damage bonus for the primary hand lvl 40+. Before level 40 or so, the damage bonus isn't enough to make a difference. Always use best damage ratio for your secondary hand regardless of level. A lot of monks feel a wu in secondary hand is a waste because you won't get many procs from secondary weapon and there is no damage bonus added to the secondary attack.

It will depend on your level as to which setup is better. Dual wielding KDs will outdamage dual Wus easily up to level 35 and will consistently outdamage them till level 40 or 45 when the damage bonus starts to kick in good. At that point, wu primary and KD secondary is probably the best setup. It isn't until level 50 that the dps of a wu (minus proc)in primary equals a KD. The KD in secondary is good until level 55 when fists become 14dmg 28 dly.
