Tanatus, why do you think a Dark Elf will have higher HP than an Iksar?

No matter what race the necromancer is, they will have 2400 mana at level 60 from 200INT. It is quite safe to say that any level 60 necromancer will easily reach the 2500 mana cap...therefor, a level 60 Dark Elf necromancer will NOT have more mana than a level 60 Iksar necromancer.

Iksars have more than a 50 - 60AC bonus above a Dark Elf. At level 51 I have 624AC self-buffed...without using Shadow Vortex, carrying a 2HB staff, and only wearing a mediocre robe. If I add on my Harvester and shield...and improve a couple items...I could easily reach 700AC self-buffed, 720 with Shadow Vortex, and over 800AC with cleric/shaman buffs.

The experience penalty of an Iksar is 20%. However, due to a smaller downtime and less of a need to "waste" mana on lifetaps every fight the Iksars will level every bit as fast as any other race.
