if it is, then start handing out those bone chips, for every set of four you get some silver and EVERY SINGLE FACTION IN CABILIS goes up! you give him enough bone chips and you'll be like a brother to all of us.

Simply target one of the Troopers and wander *far* away. Uninvis and type /con. You will find that you can con him from pretty much anywhere in zone so long as you have him targeted. There are plenty of empty rooms and hidden corners in the city where guards never go and this can be done inside Cabilis, although it is much safer to do it outside of the city.

The Troopers are on LegionsofCabilis faction, which is easiest to raise on goblins in front of the Cabilis entrance of Lake of Ill Omen (just gather up every young goblin and sarnak in the newbie area and AoE them to death, they and will drop very nicely and the faction spam will be truly impressive). Goblins raise Fironia Vie faction as well, newbie zone Sarnaks and Iksar Bandits raise BroodofKotiz faction (the Iksar Necros) also.

The bone chips to Trooper Mozo quest is also quite doable, but beware, the *Crusader* guards (the ones who often have SK pets) are on the Iksar Shadow Knight faction (GreenmistCrusaders?), make sure that none are wandering near when you are doing the bone chip quest as you will remain KOS to them for some time.

Good luck, this takes *ages.*
