The quest so far...

As learned by various sites, but primarily the Shaman's Crucible...

1) Kill one of the quest starters. There are probably 4. One is Capt. Surefoot for Barbarians in OOT. One is the named bandit in the Cyclops area (don't know his god). One is in the Field of Bone.

2) Respond to Lesser Spirit appropriately. OOT is "He threatened me". Ask "Who are you?" follow the conversation, get a gem. As many Shamans as wish to can get the gem, so no conflict of interest.

3) The Barbarian version takes you to Bondl in Freeport. Hand in gem. Follow. Say "You can buy booze." Follow. Third floor Jade Tiger, you'll find a Greater SPirit. Hail, respond "Justice". Go next door. Hail. Respond "What Heyokah?" Get gem, follow other conversation. "I wish to walk the path of the Heyokah" will despawn him. As many Shaman as wish to can get the gem, if they have the faction.

3) This stage is now supposedly fixed. Go to Erud's Crossing. Look for other Shamans in the zone and find out if the test of patience is currently running. If not, find Ooglyn. Hand in gem. Go to n1600 4200, bottom of the ocean. Ooglyn may TP there, not certain how much was fixed. Await Abe's spawn, probably not more than 2hrs (not timed yet). SPeak to each NPC, until you eventually ask Abe about the broken arrow. Get one from him. Each NPC runs off, do not follow. Greater Spirit spawns. Hail, ask "What next path?" Get gem. Do not leave without the broken arrow and the gem.

3) Find wandering spirit (wisp form) in WK. Hailing can stop it momentarily. Hand it the gem. Go to RM. Find Tabien the Goodly. Hail and converse. Kill him. Find Glaron the Wicked. Hail and converse. Then kill him. Take Marr's Promise, Woe, and Envy to the wandering spirit in WK. Hand all three over and receive "Shield of Falsehood". AC16, 5Wis/Int, 3Str, -3Dex, -5Cha, 30mana and a gem (in your inv). GEM IS NO DROP.

4) Find the Sentinel Spirit in EJ (1250, 300, inside the building). (I suggest binding here before handing in the previous 3 items so you can gate here straight from WK and cut down on zone LD's.) Hand the gem to him and converse. Your next target is the Black Dire Wolf in Mistmoore. He hits for 200, and casts spells. 3.5hr spawn, OR you can force his spawn by killing the advisor in the library (beyond the sauna where the Soulfire hilt drops). Take his pelt back to the Sentinel SPirit and receive the Black Fur Boots and a container.

5) There are 6 pages dropping off ghosts in CoM. I think they may be new spawns. Get all 6 and combine, then return to Sentinel SPirit.

6) Kill Lord Ghiosk. Hehe... spawns after you kill the first Black Reaver sometimes. He has 3 books. Loot books and read (Lizardman language). Hand to Sentinel Spirit.

7) Icon is in CoM temple. Enter CoM, pass the gate, turn right, go inside the building the waterfall runs off, take teh left door at teh bottom of the steps. It pops on a table, I believe.

Kim has not yet been found. We suspect he is in the Lost Temple of Cazic Thule, the obvious place since it's got Lizardmen and they worship CT. Kim is in a place of Brothers of the Faith, and Iksar worship CT and are a form of Lizardman.

That's the full update. If you learn something new, please pass it along to us over at the Crucible.
