OK, here goes...

First, in order to kite effectively, you need to slow the mob. For this, you use snare. Than, it may be wise to DoT the mob, to do that little extra dmg while your kiting it around.

The next thing you need (although equally important to the first) is SoW, or Wolf Form. What you do is slow the mob (by snaring) and run ahead of the mob.

When your just in range of the mob, you want to nuke it with your best, most mana efficient DD. Ignite until 19, than Careless Lightning. When you have either nuked twice, or the mob is getting too close for comfort, merely run ahead of it some more, or run in a large circle, rinse, and repeat until the mob is dead.

While killing yellows or reds by kiting, resists suck. Your nuke may only hit for 1, or resist fully, but your mana will still be taken away for casting the spell. mana is vital to kiting. Thats why there are some techniques to releave the stress of low mana, like meleeing, and more specifically, porcupining.

Porcupining is as it sounds. You toss your dmg shields on, and just tank for a bit while meleeing. the mob will have lower life, but your mana will still be plentiful (be sure to do this at the START of the fight. in case of any problems, you have full mana). It is also advisable to have a DoT on the mob while your porcupining to add to the dmg.

Well, that is all I believe (the basics anyway). Just be sure to have plenty of room to kite around, and you should do fine! *NOTE: you CAN kill yellows and reds fairly easily by these methods, and the best part is...no messy grouping! all solo!!!*
