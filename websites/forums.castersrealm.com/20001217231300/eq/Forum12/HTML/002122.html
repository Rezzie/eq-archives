There is a better way to kite with your pet, and neither you or your pet will take much damage.

This is my technique for cyclops and hill giants. First I buff me and the pet. quicken us both just beofre I go, and make sure I got regen going on myself. I park the pet out of the way (/guard here) and then go find a cyclops. I malisement it, run for a bit, turn, root the cyclops, then slow, then both dots (disease and poison) then reroot, and then I back up and sit. The slow is on the critter just so in case the root breaks I'll have enough time to re-apply it. When the poison wears off I re-apply it and then run back to where the pet it. When the root breaks the pet will go after the clops when it comes after me. BUT, because of all the damage I've already done to the clops it will usually NOT stop going after me, so I just run around in circles with the pet pounding on it from behind. If the clops does turn on the pet then I just blast it with the dd once and bingo, it's back on me again. I do this till the clops gets hurt bad enough to run, then me and the doggie pound on it till it is dead.

As long as you don't get resisted too much this works great, and even if you do you will still be the main aggro because of all the spells you are casting, so you can still kite it. Give it a shot.
