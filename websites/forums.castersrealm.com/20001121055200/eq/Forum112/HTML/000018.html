Darkrad
unregistered 		posted 07-03-2000 02:40 PM              
You definitely need a skull-shaped barbute (13 ac 35 hp 10 vs magic) for your shadowknight. And unless I had a procing weapon or vampiric embrace (lvl 22 spell), I would wear a cloak of the icebear for the extra 10 hp and the cold resistance which should help when fighting those pesky NPC shamans (it seems like I'm always getting smacked with spirits of frost wherever I fight). Other possibilities for rings include platinum fire wedding rings, at 5ac and 55hp each, they're amazing for lower levels and great at mid levels). For gloves, of course dark mail gauntlets, but if you can't find them, charred gauntlets (ac, dex, and int) make a nice substitute. If you're using 1H weapons then definitely get a cracked darkwood shield (12ac and 9 str). A silvery 1H war axe (6 dmg 22 delay) is a great starting weapon, and you can go from there with many of the new kunark weapons. If you have a lot of dex you might consider a polished granite tomahawk (6 dmg 26 delay, effect: Berserker Strength) after level 10 if you have high dex for the invincibility effect. For the neck slot I would go with a gold black pearl choker (7 agi 7 dex), especially since SK's proc a lot with vampiric embrace and the dark reaver effect. The 7 agi is good if you don't have agi above 75. The grotesque mask (3 ac 7 dex, -9 cha) is also a great face item for the dex. The more dex and str you get, the more you and your weapons will proc.

Good luck

Ok, I have to disagree with some of the previous posters...

All stats are GREATLY overrated. The only thing that matter is AC. So here is what you need:

- Dark mail gloves: excellent choice
- Lumberjack cap OR BI crown AC 10/5
- Split pawn mask: good ac and ressit.
- Straw something belt AC 10
- earings: Golden Topaz: Ac3 8 sv disease
- Azure Sleeves all the way

I cant think of the other items. But I can give you some general advices:

- get AGI over 70 ASAP, from 70 to 100 there is little difference and from 100 to more, it doesnt worth it.
- dont use massive damage 2h weapon (damage over 15) until lvl 20. Before you are better with 1h weapon because of the damage bonus. So to train your skill, use lower damage faster weapon.
- Weapon procs: dont go over the board with it... put dex at around 80 and your shaman friend will buff it at 100 easy.
- Dont use stats boosting items, instead use resistance gears, HP and Mana boost. Also, focus a lot on the AC. That is the only important stat for you at the end.
- Dont neglect the use of a shield because at the end, you will be using a shield with a 1h weapon (trust me on that). so train Bash AND slam..
- In the resistance department, the order is: Magic, Poison or Disease, Fire, Ice
You may put fire after magic. It is just that I dont like Dots, but probably you will die more from DD spells so Fire and cold are more important.
- You may also concider to use Int items to help you increase your weapon skill. But dont use them when you are fighting white and harder... Always use jewellery with AC.
- PGT is your best weapon from lvl 10 to 20, for lvl 1 to 10 use FS weapons like a long sword. Decent stats and dont cost too much.

Hope I helped!

If you are going to be an Ogre don't even bother with 1hs unless you want a shield for AC + stats. Your slam IS Bash... so you can wield a 2hs weapon and still bash using Slam skill. Keep in mind Ogres and Trolls have hellacious race exp penalties in addition to class exp penalties which make leveling a real chore. Slam/bash is an incredible advantage though in melee however... of course your mana pool will be sad.

As far as stats I would get them all to 75 first and then work on increasing INT first and dex second. You have to decide what your play-style is going to be.. if you want to fear kite then you better have the mana.. if you want to tank then you better go for as much ac as possible. JMHO.. goodluck.
