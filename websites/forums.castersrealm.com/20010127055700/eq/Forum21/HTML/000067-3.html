For those of you who are having troubles finding the words of mastery in najena, know this, :

You can get the words you want from the alligators in the underwater tunnel in Cazic Thule. I myself am not even questing for the epic item yet but have looted mastery air and mastery fire from the gators. They are VERY easy and there are lots of them. to get to the tunnel follow these directions. standing in the courtyard, with your back to the double doors leading to the zone, turn 90 degrees to your right and walk over to the ladder, go up and through the door that is right there, go down the hallway, hang a u turn, down again, and this time, when you hang a u turn, there are 2 paths, take the path in the middle, go down, there is a well there, jump in the well, dont worry about water breathing, its not a long swim, once in the well, swim to the bottom (its good to have invis as there are gators in the water) swim to the bottom of the well, and then it opens up, stick to the right wall and go up as soon as the dropped ceiling opens up into a cave, get out of the water and your in the tunnel.That pool you jus swam through is called the blue pool, if you run to the end of the tunnel, there is a green pool, abbreviations for these are bp and gp, for blue pool and green pool. the gators in the green pool drop alot more words and mage items then the blue pool ones, but be careful, there is a golem in the green pool at the end that drops ravenscale armor. hes lvl 35+ no biggy if your 40+ though, anyways, any gator down there has a chance of dropping the mastery words, it only took me like 3 hours to get 2 words, im sure you could get all of them in a day. Hope this helps! Chatty, Tunare server, level 41,

Burning embers drops off of Neze for sure. Here is a tip that I found on the web (can't rememeber the site or the poster or I would give them the credit =/ ) Go to Neg 464, Neg 2550 in Burning woods. You will want a full group to be safe, but 3 or 4 good peeps should be able to handle this easily. That is the exact spot that Neze/Phurz or the PH will spawn. It's on a 80 second timer, so it's very quick. The only problem is when you first show up, unless very lucky you will need to find the PH (wanders). What I would suggest doing is killing anything you can find wandering around the area. From what I have seen the PH can be anything that spawns in the zone (wurm, giant, hornet, skele, etc). I camped the spot, and even though it's 80 second spawn, it took me about 4 hours to get what I needed.. Neze dropped it. Good luck to you all, and I hope this helps you as much as it did me.
