I'm very surprised by haha's comments.

As far as il omen being dangerous, i agree with this only to a certain extent.

If you're going to solo, park your pet to a safe area - preferably the coast on the lake and pull your mob to your pet with darkness and apply your traditional necro tools. This way you'll avoid any trains in the zone.

Also, this is a great zone to practice your kiting skills (as an alternative to dark-fear-dot). Just get a sow and snare the mob with your pet set to no taunt, then fully apply your dots and kite. This allowed me to take down red mobs w/o too much difficulty

Lastly, in regards to fighting char and icebone skellies, these should be the easiest and most mana efficient kills for you. Don't use expulse, instead use your spook the dead spell. It only consumes 10 mn (which is virtually free). Just chain cast fear (spook) every 12 secs on the skellie till it drops (with dot's applied of course).

I'm a fan of il omen and will speak to its defense. Also, if you get bored of soloing, partner with another necro. At mid to high 20's me and my nec partner were killing mobs using only 1 bub of mana each (he snares you fear, pets do the rest). Hope this helps.
