Let me give you what I've found and then What I have seen from the new dragon in Dreadlands:

1) We had people who were level 55+ who had 200+ MR and they could not resist the AoE Fear. This meant that most of our casters were bugged by the same fear bug that has been around for ages and could not cast. This basically meant that we had to fight the dragon without any casters from that point on.

2) People who had come to watch, as well as those just travelling through the zone, when we /shouted to watch out, were feared and/or killed. This included people who were just trying to travel through.

3) Some people are KOS to the dragons from the start (not all are amiable/neutral as was told before). I've also heard that once you kill the dragon, you are then KOS to it. This makes you have a choice: kill the dragon and not be able to travel there again or not kill the dragon and the whole point of having the dragon there is moot. Oh, for all you druids using Wolf Forms of various sorts in Dreadlands, in Wolf Form, you are KOS to the dragon from the start.

4) The dragon wanders. This simply makes #3 a bigger problem.

5) People have made a sport of constantly training it to Karnor's to annihilate everyone there. I have seen people offering fair sums of platinum for this.

My opinions:

1) Putting an ubermob in a high traffic area that is likely to kill people even of faction good to it (people of all levels use Dreadlands as a highway) is one of the most bone-headed decisions that anyone could have made simply because of the impact it has on people who don't even care about the thing. I have no idea how anyone would have thought this was a good idea.

2) Putting an ubermob in a high traffic area and making it so that if you ever participate in its slaying you can no longer use the are for travel is a bone-headed decision (see #1).

3) I personally hope that the dragon in Dreadlands becomes such a large support issue (by having tons of petitions about it) that it is moved to somewhere more appropriate.
