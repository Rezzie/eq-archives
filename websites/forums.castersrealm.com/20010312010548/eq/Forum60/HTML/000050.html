Being a High Elf Cleric, you should already have started out with some pretty high Wisdom. 166 wis, in my opinion, is a little bit low for being a High Elf Cleric at your level. Here are some suggestions for equipment, and not relatively expensive. I don't know what you have already, so I may suggest some items you are already equipping. Anyways:

Ear:
Forrest Loops or regular +3wis earrings (which I'm sure you already have)

Face:
Platinum Ruby Veil

Head:
Nightshade Wreath
or Runed Circlet (cheaper)

Neck:
Goblin Soul Caller

Finger:
Platinum Jasper Rings

Hands:
Split Paw Hide Gloves

Legs:
Gatorscale Leggings

Shoulder:
Adamantite Epolets

Waist:
Crystalline Belt
or if you can afford it - Belt of the Cenobite

Wrists:
Runed Mithril Bracers


That's what I would start out with. With all those items, you should be pretty close to 200wis. I'm also not sure if some of those items can be equipped by a High Elf (I'm a Halfling Cleric). But, you should be able to. Your first goal should be to get 200 wis. Then you can start working on AC, or Resistances, or Mana items.

Good Luck!
