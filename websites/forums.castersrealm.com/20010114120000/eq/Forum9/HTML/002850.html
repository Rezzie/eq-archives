You are confusing the stuns and the mezzes.

Mez is used to park extra mobs so your party can deal with 1 at a time. Mez lasts from about 24 seconds all the way up to over a minute. Mez is broken when any type of damage is done to the mob. Any type of damage even dots (damage over time spells).

Stun will stun a creature for a few seconds and the stun is not broken when the creature is damaged. Stun is used to buy you time to mez the creature. Also used in a process called chain-stunning. You have 2 to 4 different stun spells that you cycle casting to keep mob(s) stunned the majority of the time your party/pet is fighting them.

Your Color line is your stun series. There are 4 of them.

Mesmerise is your first mez spell. Then Enthrall, Entrance, and so on.
