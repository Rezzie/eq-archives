To answer the people with religious concerns about going to the Bloodsabre's temple.. having a religion, even one like Erollsi Marr, won't prevent you from doing it in itself. However, when combined with other acts that may have lowered your faction over time, that could spell trouble. So religion shouldn't matter half as much as faction does.
