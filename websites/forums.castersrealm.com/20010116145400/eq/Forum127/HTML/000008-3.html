Well this is what I got so far:

-----
REKLON GHALEN

An NPC in Erudin named "Reklon Ghalen" speaks of a fallen paladin (Irak Altil) who abandoned his duty because of a woman (probably the Thought Corrupter in Hate) and his soul must be cleansed.

This is what "Reklon Ghalen" said:

Hail to you friend. Seek you knowledge or atonement in this temple of peace? Dedicated to the god of peace, Quellious, is this temple. It is here that her clerics and paladins pray and receive inspiration. As the clerics are the warm hand of healing, the paladins are the stalwart shield of protection, and in times of grave need the sharp sword of justice. To our post must we always remain
vigilant else dishonor might befall us.

To a paladin the upholding of his duty, or honor to his temple is foremost. To fail in his duty to stand at his assigned post in time of need is a great disgrace. Few are the paladins who have done so, and their spirits have been outcast from the god of peace. Harsh though it seems, a true paladin must always remain vigilant and dedicated to his cause. If he does not then he might find himself fallen from grace.

Many hundreds of years ago did this story begin. There was a paladin, whose name is lost to all but our oldest leaders, who was among the greatest examples of honor. It was his post to guard the chambers to our most important halls. His fall was a creature in the guise of a poor peasant woman.

Only this crest and the sacrifice of something that symbolizes your strength and purity will release his spirit into the cleansing flames of our lady Quellious. Go now.

----
IRAK ALTIL

There is a new NPC in the Plane of Fear by the name of "Irak Altil" who is the fallen paladin that Reklon speaks of. This is what "Irak Altil" said:

Irak Altil turns its head upon its dried, cracked spine to look at you with a rictus grin. After staring through you it continues to walk on. It moans and continues to shamble across the barren landscape, oblivious to the monstrosities surrounding it. It mumbles to itself softly...

Fallen light, upon this ground I fell. Left to rot, cast out. My holy arms stripped and my soul cast out of the halls. Twisted, failed, disgraced. Disgrace upon my soul for failing in my duties. Disgrace of my brethren.

Irak Altil breathes his words from his skeletal mouth as if wind through a valley. Sword, shield, breastplate. Twisted to Innoruuks foul will. Must be retrieved and cleansed else my soul is to wander for all time.

Sword! Hands of a twisted brother no longer living. Shield! Earthly children of the god of hate. Keeping stand over his dark brothers and sisters. Breast! Irak Altil lets loose an unearthly howl as it gleams with sickly light. Upon she who lured me from my post. In that place where hatred festers. Painful corruption.

The sword must be dipped in water pure. Light of clean spirit to cleanse the shield. A gem of clarity unlike any other for the breastplate. All must be cleansed and returned to the temple for my soul to be freed. Only the temple can cleanse, only they can touch the artifacts. Take the sets of two to the temple. When they are all three cleansed seperately take them to he who will grant me atonement.

-----
THE CLUE?

So "Irak Altil" needs 3 items to be cleansed so his soul can be freed:

1) Tainted Darksteel Shield: Dropped by Kirik Vel in Nektulos Forest.
The Shield must be cleansed by "the light of the pure.".
It's verified you have to turn the shield in to Eila the Pure in Felwithe.

She tells you:

Elia the Pure squints and turns her head. 'By the gods. Such a twisted aura this has. To see it hurts my eyes. I will help you if for no other reason than to remove such a twisted thing from existence.' She unrolls a scroll from her pack and softly chants over the dark metal shield. She hands it back to you with an exhausted look on and a smile on her face. 'Your heart seems pure. If I was able to help then I am honored. May the light shine warmly upon you.'

This results in:

A Gleaming Shield
Ac 10
Str: +5
Lore, No drop, Magic


2) Tainted Darksteel Breastplate: Dropped by a Thought Corrupter in the Plane of Hate. Triggered spawn by killing scorn banshees.
The breastplate must be cleansed with a gem of clarity unlike any other. Haven't got a clue what gem, maybe it hasn't been found yet.

3) Tainted Darksteel Sword: Dropped by the Keeper of Tombs in the Hole.
The sword must be cleansed by Pure Water. There is a peasant woman in Freeport that can be found near the monk's guild and will give you some water when you engage her in conversation. It's speculated this is the water that is needed.

Good Luck
