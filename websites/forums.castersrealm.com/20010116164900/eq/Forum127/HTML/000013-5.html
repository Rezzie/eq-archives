Here is a compilation of all the scabbard information for you guys.
Scabbard Quest
East Freeport, near Necro guild- Ask Kargek Redblade about scabbard, recieve note
Feerot near bridge- Give letter to Oknoggin Stonesmasher, recieve tiny lute
East Freeport- Turn lute in to Kargek and recieve book
East Karana, at base of ramp to HH- give book to Talen Redblade, recieve freezing war totem
Permafrost- Kill Goblin Wizards for Heart of Frost
East Karana- Return Heart and Totem to Talen, recieve Toem of Raging war.
Sol B, Skyfire, Emerald Jungle, Dreadlands(?)- Kill dragons and get red and green scale.

Here's the stuff for the Hilts:
1st Hilt
Lake Rathe- Find hilt in water under Aviak Stair
East Freeport- Ask Wenden Blackhammer near Kargek Redblade about the hilt, tell him that it needs to be repaired, and give him the name.
Planes- Loot diamond, black sapphire, and jacinth(what are the drop rates on these? Very Rare?)
East Freeport- Return Gems and hilt to Wenden and recieve repaired hilt.

2nd Hilt:
Timorous Deep- Go to Chessboard island, find hilt on the ground
East Freeport- Ask Wenden about the hilt. He says he needs Rejesiam ore and Golem metal
PoF- Kill named golems (Fright and Terror) for the golem metal (Do you need to get one metal from each golem?)
Frontier Mountains- Talk to named Mountain Giant (whats his name?) who says he needs a monocle
Dreadlands- Kill Giant Patriarchs for monocle
Frontier Mountains- Return Monocle to Giant
East Freeport- Return metals and hilt to Wenden and recieve repaired 2nd hilt.
