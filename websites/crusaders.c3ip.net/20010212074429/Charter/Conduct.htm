Code of Conduct



Members will treat others as they would wish others to treat them. 

Guild members will offer aid to other adventurers who may need it, and will provide aid if it is requested.  

The helping of new players will be left to the discretion of the members involved. This will be encouraged but not required. Guild members are encouraged to give aid to younger guild members, but not at the expense of learning and normal progression on the part of the younger member. 

Members will be obliged to uphold the basic goodness on this server. If a PC is picking on or disrupting a PC character, regardless of alignment, every effort should be made to protect the offended party. The reasoning is that every criminal deserves a trial before punishment. 

Obey the rules of the game - Play the game by its rules. Do not exploit bugs, do not use cheats.

Use proper etiquette - We want people to role-play, we want people to have fun. Things like kill-stealing do not increase the fun of the game. Please use good judgement and follow the etiquette of the game. 

Protect guild information - Do not distribute information gained from the guild to non-guild members unless given permission. This includes information about the game and information about other people in the guild. 

Protect your guildmates - If you see someone bearing the name of our guild and that person is in trouble, please lend a hand and save their life.

Free binding - For those guild members who have achieved the ability to bind people, please bind guild members for free. You can expect the same in return. 

Prices -  We ask that you sell your goods to other guild members at cost plus a reasonable percentage. We would also ask you try not to compete directly with other guild members when selling wares. We want the world to feel that buying from a guild member ensures quality and a fair price. Remember that you represent the guild in these matters. 

Whining - Everyone has a bad time or experience now and then.  This is to be expected due to the nature of the game.  Letting other's know about your experience is normal. However, constant abuse of /guildsay about every little thing that is bothering you, may be considered abuse of /guildsay.  Please be considerate of others and realize that constant complaining is not enjoyable to others.  If you are asked to stop complaining so much, realize that people have complained about your behavior.  We will take actions to help correct poor /guildsay behavior before punishment or banishment is discussed.

The Flaming Blade of Truth  requires that its members' reputations be beyond reproach.  We do not tolerate kill stealing, the use of profanity, or the intentional harassment of others. All members must recognize that everything they do reflects either positively or negatively on everyone else in the guild.

We want to encourage grouping with other members of the guild as much as possible. When camping for specific items, try to have a pre-planed loot order or agreement on how it will be shared and divided.  This will avoid arguments and confusion later on when that rare item finally drops.

Above all, HAVE FUN. Try to remember that it is just a game and have a good time with the friends that you make online.
