Ziffy Springer's No-Text Show
February 28, 2000 - The following appeared. serialized, within the no-text posts of Zifeer the Wanderer, on the RallosZek.com OOC Board. *applause* "Thank you, welcome to Ziffy Springer's No-Text show. Today's topic: 'People only want me for my Rare Drops' We've got a lot of special guests in tonight. I'd like to introduce my first guests, four lovely ladies all the way from Lesser Faydark, the Nybright Sisters!" *more appluase* "Hello ladies" "Hi Ziffy!" "The Nybrights are bandits by trade, nothing wrong with that mind, but they say that people keep ganging up on them and taking their necklaces. So girls, just what is it with these necklaces then? Could we get a close-up on those? Ok." *audience goes oooooh!* "Well Ziffy, you know, we're just some girls, yeah? We like to hang out and stuff, but our pop keeps getting mad at us for staying out late and dating guys and stuff, and he keeps paying people to kill us and take our necklaces." "Yeah, he sucks!" "Yeah, I mean he gave us these in the first place. He should just lighten up, you know?" "Yes girls, I can see that must be a problem. Well, we have a little surprise for you." *cut to inset with glowering High Elf* "We've invited your pop on to the show, here he is, Poppa Nybright!" *crowd goes crazy as glowering High Elf enters studio* "Hello Mr Nybright, thats it take a seat. So, you're paying people to kill your own daughters then?" *crowd boos* "Well, Ziffy, They never shown me NO respect, cos they is just filthy whores!" *Sister grabs chair, swings at Pops, stage crew wade in to break up fight, crowd loves it* "Okay, okay, let's just settle down. Ladies? What do have to say to that?" "I say he has to EARN that respect! He never did nothing for us ever and we're better off without him!" *Pops leaps up and is skilfully intercepted by stage crew* "Okay, I can see you folks have a lot to work out. Join us, after the break, when I'll be talking to two Ogres from Butcherblock who say everyone wants their heads!
