The Quest for Kunark Continued
[3 Comments] - Aurum @ 6:13 AM PDT
A commotion erupts near the warm campfires in the cave leading to Halas. A
pair of gnomes scurry out into the cold carrying odd looking equipment.
Ognit Eznertob and Dabner Drednever are hurrying to finish setting up a new
device they have created while their friend Dagda Icefury gather her
kindred to come and experience this life changing revolution.

Dagda Icefury shouts, "Come and see my brothers and sisters! My friends
have made for us a device that will keep us warm on the frigid tundra!
Follow me and see this wonderful invention! We shall never know cold again! "

Once the crowd has gathered around, Dabner turns on the Portable Heater.
The sound of a mighty explosion rolls across the tundra. Animals stop dead
in their tracks in fear as the shockwave passes by. The device is a little
broken and begins to spout out flames at those nearby. While Ognit and
Dabner argue about whether or not the blueprints were followed correctly,
Dagda jumps up on the igloo where the machine sets and begins to destroy
it. She looks toward Dabner and says "Not a word from you wizard. This
thing must be destroyed. It is a danger to us all!" Ognit and Dabner
continue to argue about who is to blame for the malfunction.

The winds howl a mournful song. Firiona Vie, the Chosen of Tunare, makes
her way slowly across the frozen ground. Flanking her are her companions,
their faces drawn and somber. Dagda, Ognit and Dabner walk over toward
Firiona Vie and her companions. As Dagda gets closer to Firiona, the fair
maiden's staff brushes the barbarian's arm. The touch of her staff,
Lifeguide, brings memories back to Dagda.

"I've come to ask the three of you for your help." Firiona graciously
pleads to them "The task set before us is difficult and the road we must
travel deadly. And I truly know not what the future has in store for all of
us." She informs them of Lorisyn Oakwynd's death and introduces them to
his twin sister, Lyirae Oakwynd. Dagda, Ognit and Dabner agree to journey
with them.

Fighting against the raging blizzard, their faces drawn and determined,
they disappear into the furious storm. Firiona Vie, Sionachie, Thubr
Axebringer, Dreezil Pocketdip, Sir Jevik Isqual, Al`Kabor, Dagda Icefury,
Ognit Eznertob, Dabner Drednever, and Lyirae Oakwynd set forth on their
journey to the new lands to confront the Ring of Scale.

More on 2Hand Damage Description
[12 Comments] - Aurum @ 6:58 PM PDT
While visiting the Gameplay Board this evening I spotted the following post that Gordon made:

    If damage is (as Abashi has said it should be) calculated by damage/dly ratio, why do 2h weapons ALWAYS have a VASTLY superior ratio to 1h weapons?

    Because you can't dual wield when wielding a 2H weapon. The damage and delays mentioned were completely arbitrary and were just listed to make the problem understandable by everyone. The problem itself is entirely mathematical.

    The problem as I explained it is that IF a person wields a 1H weapon of an equal ratio to a 2H weapon, they will do more damage with the 1H weapon than with the 2H weapon just by virtue of it being faster. The margin was fairly large, and was reduced by the changes announced the other day.

    This is still true, but not to the large margin that it was.

    -Gordon

RoK CD Patch Tip
[13 Comments] - Aurum @ 9:39 AM PDT
Emeric sent in the following tip:

If you install the ROK CD after you have patched, it overwrites the new
files you just downloaded with the outdated ones on the CD. In order to
avoid doing the patch twice, saves the files most recently edited in the
/everquest directory and then install ROK. Take the files you saved and
put them back into the /everquest directory. You won't have to do the
patch again.
Thanks!


The Quest for Kunark
[8 Comments] - Aurum @ 9:28 AM PDT
These screenshots and the text are from quests being done right now, which are leading up to the re-discovery of the continent of Kunark. Firiona Vie is gathering up her "crew" and they will be heading back to confront the Ring of Scale, or at least that's what they hope to do.

As the voices boom from the city of Kelethin in a glorious cheer of
celebration, Lorisyn Oakwynd shouts "Brothers and Sisters of the Forest!
Come, join the rest of your nation on this day. Today, the battle rests,
and the Festival of Faydark's Children shall be held!" Elves begin to
emerge from their huts atop the Tree City of Kelethin, and from the small
valley leading into the Butcherblock Mountains, a small band of dwarves
marches into the forest. A mule pulling a wagon carrying three large
barrels, each the height of an elf and the width of a dwarf, follows
closely behind the dwarves.

King Tearis Thex, the high elven king, welcomes everyone to the celebration.

"During this past year the Mother has shown much generosity and to our
people. The restoration of my daughter and your princess, Firiona Vie, has
been only the crown of the Mother's gifts to us this year. To all of you,
I give my best wishes and though I am your King, it is not necessarily I
who has brought you here today. It is your hearts, your spirits. the very
things that make you of the Fier`Dal and Koada`Dal nations, that have
brought you here. To you, I raise my glass. May your blades prove strong,
your bows aim true, your hearts remain pure and The Mother, may she bless
you with every year being twice as good as the past. To old friends."

Firiona Vie, Thubr Axebringer, and Sionachie reunite with their friend from
long ago, Lorisyn Oakwynd. They also receive the wonderful news of the
existence of Lorisyn's twin sister, Lyirae Oakwynd. They all take part in
the celebration of the Faydark.

The moment the King retired back to Felwithe, a band of villains disrupted
the celebration. Ghargin Bumblok, an ogre shaman, and a troll warrior by
the name of Rogkasth were led by Vahlai Ka`Izal from the Teir`Dal. These
villains came to kill Lorisyn Oakwynd. Once Lorisyn was dead, they warned
Firiona Vie that this was not the last they will see of them. Lyirae,
vowed herself to take her brother's place by traveling with Firiona Vie and
the others.

Firona Vie, Thubr Axebringer, and Sionachie journey by heading to Freeport to seek out the Erudite wizard and Human paladin.
