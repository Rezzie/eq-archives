EQOA: Frontiers Q&S #2
The second look inside frontiers answers a question and shows us a ratman!
October 14, 2003 - Here is the second part of this weekly question and screenshot feature. Every Tuesday we will check in with one Q&A and a screenshot from EverQuest Online Adventures: Frontiers, the expansion to EQOA for the PS2. Alex von Minden, the Community Relations Manager for EQOA, took the time to answer this first question!

We hope you enjoy it! Please, do leave your comments in this thread on the EQOA Message Board.


EQOA: Frontiers Question #2

Lepidus: The official website indicates that there will be some improvements in the graphics quality for EQOA: Frontiers. Can you talk a bit about how things have been improved, in more practical terms, and what players should expect? Also, will graphical changes carry over to the old world?

Alex von Minden: Frontiers includes some nice upgrades to the games overall visuals. First, we have all new and redesigned character models. Your own avatar and your adventuring companions are what you spend a lot of time looking at so we think you will really like the new ones. This also comes with entire new armor textures for you to gear up in. Next, we added a new environmental system to the landscape that allowed us to put in things like fog, sandstorms and lava that rains down from the sky. This, coupled with the new radial flora that fills in small plants, rocks and other details on the ground, has the game world looking better than ever. And yes, all this carries over to the old world - once you purchase Frontiers and play on the new DVD you will have all these new improvements wherever you go, old areas or new.



Exclusive Screenshot of the Week
This picture depicts a Rat Man commonly found on the barren coastlands of southern Odus. While the Rat Men of this region commonly equip themselves with crude weapons and armor, they also have an affinity for tribal magics. So, when adventure brings you into their territory, be wary of dangerous spell casters.
