Adventuring: Emote Guide

Author: Zerlargal (rtfm@rivernet.com.au)   Organization: N/A

COMMANDS

  

EMOTES - CONT

  

/adriver

Writes sound card info to file.

/blush

You blush at the targeted person or your cheeks are burning

/afk

Away From Keyboard (T)

/boggle

You boggle at the target, shaking your head in disbelief.

/anon

Anonymous

/bonk

You bonk the targeted person on the head

/auction

Auctions item for sale

/bored

You express that you are bored

/assist

Target enemy of targeted player

/bounce

You bounce around the targeted person, or just in place if no target.

/autosplit

Splits loot with your party (T)

/bow

You bow to the targeted person or just bow

/bug

Report a BUG

/brb

You say that you will be right back

/channel

No idea

/burp

You burp loudly

/consent [name]

Permit [name] to loot your corpse

/bye

You say goodbye

/consider or /con

Consider target compared to you

/cackle

You cackle gleefully

/corpse

Summons your corpse to your side

/calm

You try to calm the targeted person or you feel peaceful and calm

/duck

You duck.

/cheer

Your character cheers. An animated emote.

/duel or /d

Challenges targeted PC to a duel (R)

/chuckle

You chuckle

: (text) or /e (text)

(your-name) followed by text

/clap

You clap happily

/exit

Exits the game immediately

/comfort

You comfort the targeted person

/feedback

Feedback/comments screen.

/congratulate

You congratulate the targeted person on a job well done

/filter

Bad language filter (T)

/cough

You cough

/follow

Autofollow targeted PC

/cringe

You cringe away from the targeted person

/friend [name]

Adds or removes PC to friends list (T)

/cry

Your character cries.

/ftell (text)

Tell everyone in your friends list.

/curious

You look at the targeted person curiously, or look around curiously

/g

Talks to all people in group

/dance

You stand on your tip toes and dance, or you dance with him

/guilddelete

Delete a guild from server (G)

/disbelief

You shake your head in disbelief. [may be broken]

/guildinvite [name]

Invite player to guild (O)

/drool

You drool

/guildinvite [name] o

Make PC an officer of guild (G)

/duck

You duck

/guildwar [guild]

Declares war. With guild (RG)

/eye

You raise and eyebrow inquiringly (at the targeted person)

/guildpeace [guild]

Declare Peace with giuld. (RG)

/finger

You make a rude gesture at the targeted individual.

/guildleader [name]

Transfer leadershiip to [name] (G)

/flipoff

You make a rude gesture at the targeted individual.

/guildremove [name]

Removes person from the guild (OS)

/frown

You frown.

/guildsay (text)

Message to guild

/gasp

You gasp in astonishment

/guildstatus [name]

Guildstatus of [name]

/gesture

You make a rude gesture at the targeted individual.

/ignore [name]

Ignore ALL text from player (T)

/giggle

You giggle

/loc

Your coords X (+n/-s),Y(+e/-w)

/glare

You turn an icy glare at the targeted person, or you glare at nothing

/log [on|off]

Logging

/grin

You grin evilly

/loot

Loot targeted corpse

/groan

You groan

/msg (text)

No idea

/grovel

You grovel before the targeted person

/ooc (text)

Out of character speech to zone

/happy

You say you are very happy with the targeted person, or so happy

/pet as you were

Pet will forget all commands

/hungry

You say you need food badly

/pet attack

Pet will attack targeted creature

/hug

You hug the targeted person.

/pet back off

Tells pet to quit attacking targeted NPC

/introduce

You introduce the targeted person or introduce yourself to everyone

/pet follow me

Pet will start following you

/jk

You say you are just kidding

/pet get lost

Pet suicides

/kneel

You kneel down, you kneel before person in humility and reverence.

/pet guard here

Pet stands in one location

/laugh

You laugh (at targeted person).

/pet guard me

Pet will attack any nearby NPC

/lost

You say you are completely lost

/pet health report

Displays the health of your pet.

/massage

You massage the person's shoulders, look for someone to massage

/pet who leader

Pet will report who summoned it.

/moan

You moan

/pet report health

Displays the health of your pet.

/mourn

You lower your head and mourn the targeted person, or the dead

/pet sit down

Causes pet to sit and rest

/nod

You nod to the highlighted person

/pet stand up

If sitting, will stand up.

/panic

You scream in panic.

/pet target

Targets your pet.

/peer

You peer at the targeted person intently, or you peer around intently

/pet [no] taunt

Decide whether pet will taunt in battle (T)

/plead

You plead desperately with the targeted person

/petition (text)

Request assistance for a GM. (not much)

/point

You point at the targeted creature/person or straight ahead

/played

Birthdate & hours played with your PC.

/poke

You poke the highlighted person.

/quit

Exits game immediately

/ponder

You ponder the targeted person, what is going on with him.

/random [x] [y]

Random number between 1 and x, or x and y

/puzzled

You look puzzled

/reply

Send a /tell to the last person who /tell you

/raise

You raise your hand

/roleplay

Indicates you are roleplaying

/ready

You let everyone (or targeted person) know you are ready

/say (text)

Speak to people in local area

/roar

You emit a low rumble and roar like a lion

/sdriver

No idea

/rofl or /rof

You roll on the floor laughing (at targeted person).

/shout (text)

Shouts message to all of the zone.

/rude

You make a rude gesture at the targeted person.

/shownames

Visible names above [N]PC heads. (T)

/salute

You salute the targeted person or the gods in pure admiration

/shownpcnames

Visible names above NPC's heads. (T)

/shiver

You shiver.. brrr.

/split p g s c

Split plat, gold, silver, & copper with groupr

/shrug

You shrug

/stopsong

Stops your song (bards).

/sigh

You sigh at the targeted person, or you sigh, clearly disappointed

/surname

If lev>20, choose your surname (SP)

/smile

You beam a smile at the targeted person.

/tell [name]

Sends a message to [name].

/smirk

You smirk mischieviously

/time

Time of day in Norrath.

/snarl

You snarl at the targeted person, or you bare your teeth in a snarl

/who [all] [lev [lev]]

[class|race|guild|friends|count|name|corpse]

/snicker

You snicker softly

List details of non-anon

chars who match your filter - [all] for entire server

/stare

You stare dreamily at the targeted person, or you stare at the ground

/yell

people see "XXX yelled for help from your [direction]"

/tap

You tap your foot impatiently

   

/tease

You tease the targeted person mercilessly, or for someone to tease

EMOTES

  

/thanks

You thank the targeted person

/agree

You agree with the targeted person

/thirsty

You state that you are thirsty

/apologize

Apologise to targeted person or everyone in room

/shiver

You shiver ... brrr. or shiver at the thought of messing with them.

/amaze

Amazed, you gasp at targeted person, or you are amazed

/wave

Your character waves.

/applaud

Applaud performance of targeted person or round of applause

/welcome

You welcome the targeted person

/bird

You make a rude gesture

/whine

You whine pitifully

/bite

You bite the targeted person on the leg, or look for someone to bite

/whistles

You whistle at the targeted person appreciatively, or whistle a tune

/bleed

You bleed all over the targeted person or quietly

/yawn

You yawn rudely in targeted person's face, or you yawn

/blink

You blink in disbelief

   

SYMBOLS T - Toggle R - Reciprocal(both relevant players must do this to each other) G - Guild Leader Only O - Guild Officer Only S - Can do it to yourself P - Permanent
