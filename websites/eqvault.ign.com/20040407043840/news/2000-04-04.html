2000-04-03


Everquest Q&A With Abashi

Massive Postings from the Offical Gameplay Board

Question: Anyway, is the Bard song Kellin's Lucid Lullaby ever intended to be fixed? It currently has a 3 second duration and a roughly 60% resist rate. Even those that are in favor of it the way it is admit that it requires a dedicated healer to keep the Bard alive while using it. One resist causes the monster to go berserk on the bard and instantly interrupt the song, and then the Bard is more or less dead because all the monsters are going to be un-mesmerized in three seconds

GZ has committed to doing further tests on Kellin's after he gets the expansion spells worked out. They must take priority at present.


Question: Also, is it intended that Bards don't get any magical instruments until mid 30s or higher? If not, please examine the quests that give the unfound/missing instruments, they may be broken.

The amount that magical instruments can affect the outcome of a spell is limited, therefore the number of different levels of instruments that we can provide is likewise limited. So the answer to your question would be yes.

Question: What use does minor illusion have on a non-pvp server where most mobs see right through it? (don't get me started on how sneaky and useful it is on a pvp server)

As a level 1 spell, it doesn't have much use on the non-pvp servers. As for PvP, it is indeed quite powerful.

Question: Why doesn't the tree illusion give the same regen effect that tree form gives to druids?

Because it's not designed to. This one is an illusion of a tree, and the other actually turns the druid into a tree.

Question: Enchanters get the instill mana line fairly recently and it has proven to be useless (with the exception of Wu's gauntlets). Are there any plans to add more items for the skills (in a gradual process) or will everything be added at one time?

This is more of a tradeskill issue. It's possible that we could see more items using the crystalize mana series when the Live team takes up trade skills again post-expansion.

Question: This might be a question for GZ, but why don't enchanters get a level 49 illusion? Also, will enchanters get any illusions after level 50?

In order for an illusion to be possible, the character model to be illusioned must be in every zone that the illusion is capable in. Basically, we ran out of cool things to do with illusions. As for post-50, sorry, can't answer that yet.

Question: Is it a bug or a feature that hybrids get less mana per wis/int from wis/int items than they do from their base wis/int?

Hybrids indeed use different formulae to determine their mana. This is as designed.

Question: Also, why is it that hybrids get less mana than stated from mana items? ( i.e., 0-10 mana from a +25 mana item)

This shouldn't be the case. +mana is +mana. If anyone else can confirm this I'll be happy to chat with the programmers about it.

Question: Are the mage spells posted on castersrealm and the like really real? Or are they just some joke like the early April fools thing. (I've heard the answer about not believing
that stuff but I want to know if its truth.

Can't answer anything about post-50 spells. Sorry.

Question: Are there any plans to change the mage's phantom armor line so it's useful?

Actually yes. In order to make this series more useful, we're adding a regen component to the spells. +1 for leather, +2 for chain, and +3 for plate.

Question: A Rogue cannot evade when the only other thing attacking the creature is a pet. Is this intentional? Why isn't the creature considering the pet a valid target to attack if a Rogue evades?

**I'm not positive, but I think the skill was intended to work in a group situation, and that we assume there are other characters attacking the target.
This is indeed true. Evade works by lowering the amount that a creature hates the rogue. If it is successful, it always lowers it to the same amount. When the damage is being done by a pet, it works out a little differently, therefore we assume that there's another valid PC for a target.

Question: Any plans on adding an NPC that can reduce the weight of shadow Knight Quest Armor? It seems that this armor was designed with only Troll and Ogre Sk's in mind.

I've brought this up. I don't like it and think that we need to take into account the smaller races. I'll bring it up again.

Question: Will there EVER be a "/who guild " to list all the members of a guild currently online (and displaying tags)? I think this would be a great tool for guild leaders and officers for organizing events. Heck, you may already have something similar implemented. If this is the case, please excuse this waste of time

**You can already get the listing for some guilds by using /who all . Obviously, this method is sub-optimal and doesn't always work. Adding better who/guild functionality is on the programming list of things to do.

One additional note on this: For maximum success, enclose the guild name in quotes.

Question: Could you add a /broken command to enhance the current /bug command? This new option would allow people to "report" and EverQuest to "compile" broken quests ...(like the assassin in Qeynos not dropping [black wood chips] broken since beta)... and cosmetic stuff like typos without the information being hidden in bug reports.

**We have an email address (which escapes me at the moment - doh!) that we'd prefer players use to report problems with quests. Abashi, if you're reading this, please re-post the quest help email addy.

Actually the questhelp address has been retired. Please refer quest-bugs to eqtesting@verant.com.

Question: Planes armor is all no drop lore, however in Fear the Shaman and Cleric belt are just lore and droppable, why do you allow armor or weapons droppable at all from the planes?

Shouldn't be. I'm not that familiar with items in fear, are the two belts you are speaking of the Flayed Turmoilskin and the girdle of the diligent?

Question: In all seriousness.. I don't know how specific this question is. but are dark elf clerics ever going to get any race and class specific quests?

There are a few race/class combos that could use some quests. Druids and Gnomes come to mind as well as DE clerics. We add quests all of the time, so it's possible that a few might show up from time to time, but there's currently no driven effort to add them. Perhaps there should be.

Question: Some time ago, you mentioned that you were going to try to get a "history" put together for player consumption. It would be nice to have that for "fitting into" the world we are supposed to be role-playing in. Any status?

**Good question. I believe Abashi was supposed to be working on this - I'll ask him about it in the morning.

We're still working on this project. The guy who does our web sites is working on a layout that would fit the type of information we are planning on releasing. I hope to see the first tidbit released soon.
