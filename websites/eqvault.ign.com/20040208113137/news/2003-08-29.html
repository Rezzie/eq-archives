GameSpy's Fourth LDoN Q&A

GameSpy talks with Alan Vancouvering about Mistmoore's Catacombs

GameSpy provides some answers about one of the new dungeons in the Lost Dungeons of Norrath EQ expansion.

Mistmoore's Catacombs

GameSpy: The description makes it seem as though the Mistmoore Catacombs stretch the full length of Faydwer -- is this the case?

Alan VanCouvering: The extent of these catacombs is hard to determine and only portions of them have been unearthed. Corruption in the earth has caused sections to collapse. If they do extend the length of Faydwer, they no longer do so without interruption. It will be up to the adventurers of Norrath to discover the reach of Mayong Mistmoore.
Sounds like the place for an Addams Family raid!
