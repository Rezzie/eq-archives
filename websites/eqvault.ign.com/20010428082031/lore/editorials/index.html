EverQuest Editorials

In this area, we publish articles written about the issues that embroil Norrath. After all, EverQuest is always constantly changing. Here you will find news and views on all the latest issues, written by players just like yourself.

We strongly welcome any and all article submissions that employ appropriate use of the English language. (i.e., good grammar and spelling)
All authors will receive full credit, as well as special organization links and mentions if wanted.
If you have an issue you think needs to see the light of day, or one you think needs more attention, write and send in your article to us today!

All submissions should be sent to aurum@vaultnetwork.com. I will personally respond immediately after receiving and reviewing the piece. If the article meets all the mentioned requirements, it will be published within two days.
