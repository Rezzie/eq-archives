Beginning Skills

Alcohol Tolerance
Ability to withstand the effects of alcohol.With proficiency in this skill you will be able to drink enough to gain the benefits of drinking which are an increase in stamina and strength without suffering as much from the dissorientation and loss of vision which are the usual side effects

Baking
Allows you to make baked goods. Trade Skill.

Beg
This gives you the ability to beg for money from NPC's. Be cautious whom you choose to beg from. Some NPC's don't like beggers. =)

Bewing
Allows you to make many different alcoholic beverages with the help of a still. Trade Skill.

Bind Wound
With the help of some bandages this will let you heal the target up to half of their normal health.

Common Tongue
This is the common language which allows everyone in Norrath to communicate with each other

Fishing
Buy yourself a fighing rod, some bait and you're all set to try out this skill. Trade Skill.

Fletching
Allows you to make bows and arrows with the help of a fletching kit. Trade Skill.

Jewel Craft
With the help of a jewelry kit this allows you to design your own jewelry out of metals and gems. Trade Skill.

One Handed Blunt
This skill is most commonly used with a mace, club or warhammer

One Handed Slashing
This skill is most commonly used with a battle axe or a sword

Piercing
This skill is most commonly used with a dagger, rapier or spear

Pottery
This skill allows you to make different clay items such as bowls with the help of Clay, a Pottery Wheel and a Kiln. Trade Skill.

Sense Heading
This skill will tell you what direction you are pointing in. When it does work it only gives correct readings. Difficult to raise at low levels, but an invaluable aid for a Bard with a bit of wanderlust.

Singing
If you're not using an instrument with a song then it will default to using the singing skill

Smithing
Some of the best items in the game are not to be found in stores, but from the hands of a skilled weaponsmith.

Swimming
Increasing this skill will allow you to swim faster and hold your breath under water longer.

Tailoring
This allows you to sew your own armour. You will need a sewing kit, pattern and a ruined hide. The size of the animal that hide came from will determine the size of the piece of armour you sew.

Throwing
This skill is used for thrown weapons, commons examples are throwing axes or throwing knives

Two Hand Blunt
This skill is most commonly used with a great staff or a 2H war hammer

Two Hand Slashing
This skill is most commonly used with a 2H sword, halberd or 2H battle axe

Hand To Hand
Your proficiency in hand to hand combat. It's always good to build this skill up at least a little just incase you're left fighting without any weapons

 

Advanced Skills: Some skills you will be unable to use until you have reached the appropriate level. These are listed below.

Level 5 - Percussion
Your skill with percussion instruments such as the drum

Level 8 - String
Your skill with stringed instruments such as the lute or mandolin.

Level 10 - Dodge
Your ability to dodge blows in combat.

Level 11 - Brass
Your skill with Brass instruments such as the horn

Level 12 - Forage
This allows you to forage for food while you're in the wilderness. Wood Elves start out being able to use this skill, it will begin to raise at level 12, however you must spend at least one point in it to do so. (credit to Penaden-Veeshan)

Level 14 - Wind
Your skill with wind instruments such as the flute

Level 17 - Dual Wield
This skill allows you to equip two single handed weapons.

Level 17 - Sneak
This allows you to sneak up on npc's/monsters without them taking notice

Level 24 - Safe Fall
Allows you to fall from heights without taking damage or reducing it

Level 25 - Hide
This allows you hide from others mimicking invisibility. You will be unable to tell if you are successfully hidden when you use it.

Level 26 - Instill Doubt
This can cause your target to run away

Level 30 - Disarm Traps
Allows you to disarm a trap.

Level 35 - Track
This allows you to track others in your zone

Level 40 - Pick Lock
With the help of some lockpicks this allows you to pick locks
