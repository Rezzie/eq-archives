March 18, 2004

This week marks the 5th anniversary of Everquest. SOE is doing lots of ingame events serverwide. There's a scavenger hunt that rewards you with a indefinate firework staff. The longer you've been playing, the nicer the firework! You can find out more details on the scavenger hunt here. For those who have accounts that were no longer active, SOE has reinstated all accounts (except those banned by SOE) and you have an opportunity to come enjoy the celebrations free of charge until April 15th.

In addition to the Scavenger Hunt, for the next two weeks those old uber items from way back have been recreated and made better. There is a list at Lucy of all of these "Fabled" items. You can find the Lucy list here and there is a thread going on our boards here. These are only going to be ingame for about 2 weeks! So head over to those old world zones, course expect them to be camped just like the old days!

R.A. Salvatore will produce series of Everquest novels. While he's not commiting to writing one of the series yet, he's overseeing and editing the project. A long time EQ player himself, he's sure to bring the lore into the books, and many (including myself) are anxious to read the series.
