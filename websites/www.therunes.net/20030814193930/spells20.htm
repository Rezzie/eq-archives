 All of the mana line of spells come from the Brownie camp. Up until recently there were a few tricks for us to raise our faction though the use of elemental illusions/faction boosting spells. Recently however SOE changed all non-racial illusions so that you will now keep whatever faction you have in your natural form. Therefore making getting this spell impossible unless you have a Druid cast wolf on you. There is talk of adding this line of spells to a vendor in a city, however as of the writing of this guide, it has not been done.
