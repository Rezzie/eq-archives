How to get there:

In Gunthak off the side of the dock you will see a chain of rocks in the water. Follow the direction of the rocks and you will get to an island.

I usually invis, or be attacked. (Even if they are green cons, everything is aggresive)

Once you make it to the island stay to the right. You will come to a cave. Again, even green cons will attack, and there are alot of them in the cave.

Follow the cave keeping to the right. You will come to a townish type place. Enter the town, and stay to the right. Follow the pier around and you will see the entrance to Dulak on your right. There will be 2 guards guarding it. To my knowledge, they do not see invis.

 

Fun Spots:

I have not been through the whole zone, however there is a really neat hidden cave if you follow the left zone wall. It will be the first area that turns into a wood wall. The entrance is in there. In the far back of the cave is a quest NPC, no idea what he is for. Very creepy kind of place, very fun!
