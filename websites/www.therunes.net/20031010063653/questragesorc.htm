Rage Sorcerer Armor
NPC: Marv Orilis
-speak with Marv about the armor. He will give you a curing kit. When you ask about specific pieces he will also give you the pattern for that piece. Then he will tell you what other ingredients are required.
-Combine the pattern piece with the specific ingredients in the curing kit he gave you.
You will create the armor. There is no skill check when doing the combine.
