 Skyshrine
Ralgyn- NPC
Requires high faction

- You say, 'Hail Ralgyn'

He will tell you that he is worried and afraid for his son who has disappeared while on patrol. He will also tell you of a broken promise to take care of a talisman. His son carries half of this talisman. You will need to seek out Kardakor in the Great Divide to help find the fate of his son.
-You say, 'who is kardakor?'

Head to Great Divide and find Kardakor. He can be found in the wurm caves in a room to the right of the Guardian Wurms.
-You say, 'Hail Kardakor'
-You say, 'I am looking for Glanitar'
He tells you he has not seen him lately, and believes something may have happened to him.
-You say, 'where did they disappear?'
He will tell you he is unsure, but he last went on patrol of the Great Divide, and mentions Giants. He tells you to return to him with any sign of Glanitarm, as well as something that would symbolize who you received or took it from.

Gather 2 groups of 55+ and kill Taskmaster Aboytt . He is close to the wurm caves. Retrieve half of the talisman as well as his head.
Return to Kardakor, and give him the head and talisman. He will tell you to seek out Rolandal.
Rolandal can be found in Wakening Lands, close to the right wall from the Kael zone.

-You say, 'Hail Rolandal'
He'll tell you to bring him three rare treasures along with your talisman and he'll tell you everything you need to know.


The rare items he needs are:
the Head of Staff Sergeant Drioc, which is dropped by the Staff Sergeant Drioc= the bank within Kael Drakkal.
The Teachings of GKrean, which drops off GKrean=Arena within Kael Drakkal.
And a Chipped Fang which drops off Vilefang, a named serpent in Dragon Necropolis.


Once you have obtained all three items, hand them in with your Talisman to Rolandar and you'll receive the Imbued Talisman. Return to Ralgyn and hand him the new talisman and you'll be rewarded with the Circlet of Falinkan.

Plane of Mischief
Deck of Spontaneous Generation
This quest begins in Rivervale and takes place in the Plane of Mischief.

Go to riverdale to the female vendor who sells varied fruits and veggies in the farm area. Say to her, "I will take turn". She will give you a sack of turnups that you take to Frejorner in plane of mischeif and he will give you the Deck of Spontaneous Generation which is used to combine the cards in.

The cards can be found in the plane of mischief itself. Different combinations will create different rewards.
Cloak of Confusion - Blue Throne, Blue Crown, Blue Knight, Blue Squire.

The Throne cards are usually found in the Alice area of the zone and drop off Big and 22. Knight cards are dropped by the four halflings in the forest near the chest and on mid level npc's like Gorillas, Guards and 86. The crown cards are dropped by 86, Fenj and My Hand. The squire cards are dropped by mobs found in the forest area.

Katta

Talk to Arbogast who can be found in a building beside the bank in Katta Castellum. There is a lot of story background and many dialogues between you, Arbogast and his friend Miller. He will tell you that Phenic Dionicas is a vampire and if you wish to see the truth he will hand you Arbogast's Water Vial which he wants you to throw on Phenic's face.

Phenic can be found on the second floor of the government building. Hand him the water and he will smile at you and hand you back the Phenic's Water Vial. Now return to Arbogast and hand him the water and he will say that the dark magics are stronger than he though.

He will then hand you back a Silver Wrapping Container and will tell you to seek out Nathyn Illuminious (who he believes is actually Tsaph Katta). Retrieve Nathyn's Memoirs from the room with the locked door in his castle [Pick Lock Skill: 201]. Put it in the silver wrapping container and return it to Arbogast. He will then spout some nonsence on how Norrath doesn't exist and that the truth must be found.

Follow this conversation and he'll hand you a Gold Wrapping Container and will tell you to bring him a book on the Exodus.

Head off to the Shadowhaven Library and locate Rianna Birchard and ask her "do you know of the exodus". She will tell you that Peqi borrowed it.
Peqi can be found in one of the buildings near the casters guild in Shadowhaven, kill him to obtain it. Put the book in the gold wrapping container and hit combine, now return it to Arbogast. He will tell you to bring him two Akuel Xi Ans Vius [NO RENT] which drop in the Akheva Ruins and will then hand you a Black Wrapping Paper. Once you have obtained them, place them inside the paper and combine them, return once again to Arbogast.

He and miller will head off to the bank to get some items for you... but instead of handing them to you they will discuss it and then leave out the front door. Now kill both Miller (Lvl 47 WAR) and Arbogast (Lvl 49 CLR) and you will obtain a Heavy Wrap, 2 No Rent swords, a Clasp, a Shimmering Mace and some Baleen Plate. Place the Clasp in the Heavy Wrap and hit combine, and you'll be left with the Cloak of Truth.

Gleaming Sphere of Light (npc)

He will tell you about the outcast phantasmists which are twisting the minds of people and shaping them to evil. You must locate the two lost tomes so that we can stop these outcasts. Each tome is ripped in half, and all the pieces can be found in the Western Wastes.

The first half of Vin'Pekir's Tome drops off random dragons.
The second half of Vin'Pekir's Tome drops off the Sapara dragons.
The first half of Al'Tariquil's Tome is dropped by Harla Dar (roams near the zone to Siren's Grotto)
The second half of Al'Tariquil's Tome drops off Esorpa of the Ring (a static spawn in the scar outside of Veeshan's Temple)

Hand all four pieces of the tomes and you'll be rewarded with the Wreath of Nature.

Wakening Land
Lieutenant Krofer
 Must have amiable faction with all Kael Giant Groups

Talk with Lieutenant Krofer who can be found in the Wakening Land. Say to him "I am prepared". He will give you a Letter for War, and will tell you to give it to Staff Sergeant Drioc who can be found within Kael Drakkal. Drioc will sign it and hand it back to you, saying that the troops are ready to move. Return the note to Krofer, and he will hand you yet another note, which he will tell you to give back to him after the battle.

Four giants will spawn and will run over to the Sifaye camp [1750, -275]. Now issue the command, "troop attack" and the giants will charge into the Sifaye camp. High level Sifaye's will spawn to defend their camp, basically you are meant to lose. Now return to Krofer and hand him back the letter he gave you. He will hand you the Failure Report and tell you once again tell you to go and see Drioc. Hand Drioc the report and he will tell you to go and see his financier, and then hands you back the Failure Report.

His finacier is Noble Helssen who can be found next to Wenglawks in the Noble's building. Give Helssen the failure notice and he will tell you that it was not your fault and that Drioc was to blame. He then will divulge that he seeks to not only control the wakening land but the very force of Growth itself, and he needs your help. He will hand you a Tablet inscribed with special runes and tell you to seek out Corudoth, a named sea turtle, in the Iceclad Ocean.

Head off to Iceclad Ocean and hand Corudoth, who can be found under a chunk of ice [-6000, 6000]. Hand him the tablet, he will reward you with a Lock of Hair. He will tell you to travel to Thugadin where you will have to look for Petcas, who owns a small bar. You must kill him to obtain the Ancient Rusted Key. From here you will travel to the Tower of Frozen Shadow and locate Abrams [Incoherant Spirit on 6th Floor]. Kill the spirit to make Abrams spawn, and then hand him the Lock of Hair. You will be rewarded with the Arcanum.

Now head back to Helssen and hand him the Arcanum and the Ancient Rusted Key and he will hand you a voucher. From here, there are three different rewards that you can receive, depending on who you hand the voucher to.
Kellek =the Collar of the Storm
Wenglawks = Girdle of Reflection
Helssen = Helssen's Prismatic Trinket

Collect the following cards and combine them in the Deck of Spontaneous Generator to create the Bracelet of the Twisted Mind.


White Throne
Blue Throne
Blue Crown
Blue Knight

The Throne cards are usually found in the Alice area of the zone and drop off Big and 22. Knight cards are dropped by the four halflings in the forest near the chest and on mid level npc's like Gorillas, Guards and 86. The crown cards are dropped by 86, Fenj and My Hand.
