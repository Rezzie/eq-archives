Xi Xaui - Level 57
drops spell Glamour of Visage
Not mezzable
Map with his location

Insanity Crawler is an uncommon spawn. Reported to pop approx 3 times a day. If not killed in 30 min it will depop. It's been reported that 30 sec or so into the fight, or when a PC dies, 5 mini worms will spawn from it. Insanity Crawler cast an AOE mana drain every 15 sec or so.

Also beware of traps. Setting these off will pop several mindless borrowers. They can be mezzed, though with some difficulty.

There a two types of aliens, most are a dark color, but a few are a light greyish. These light colored ones can't be mezzed, though I'm unsure of charm. Also not rootable
