Zone Information

Arch of Chaos Map of Drunder

Glyphed Rune Words drop off the following:

Shadow Master Vinta
Tallon Zek
The Diaku Supplier
Vallon Zek

Vallon Zek
Vallon Zek once down to 30% health will spawn 5 more Vallon Zeks, though not level 70. This can happen up to 5 times. One of the tricks is to indentify the real Vallon and kill the splits first. Rumor is there is another way to kill him making him about as trivial as a tier 2 mob, however at the time of this update that has not been published. If/when I recieve that info I will update this page.

Misc info:
Slowable * AOE dot curable with Radient Cure 1. Dot does approx 2k damage in 30 sec.

Tallon Zek
Level 70- Spawn is approx 3 days- Immune to slows- Casts AoE spell Tallons Balance- unresistable/ Lowers -300 to all resist stats.

Rallos Zek
When Rallos gets to about 60% health the REAL Rallos Zek will spawn. This 2nd one is the one who drops loot.
Killing Rallos Zek gives you access to the Elemental Planes.
The fake Rallos is slowable, unsure n the real one.
