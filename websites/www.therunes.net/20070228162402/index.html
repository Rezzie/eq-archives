A new search function has been added to the site. It will search not only The Runes main site, but also the forums. This is a new type of Google search and one of the kinda cool things is the ability to have contributors from the community.

A contributor is someone who is knowledgable about Enchanters and is willing to mark enchanter related pages as they come across them. (It's easy to do, simply click a button on your browser to mark) I've elected not to have an open contribute system. Instead only registered users who have at least 10 non spam postings will be considered.

This will build our search into completely relevent, exactly what you were looking for results.

If you are interested in contributing please PM me or add your name to this thread and I'll send you the information. You'll also get a "Contributor" title on the fourms.
