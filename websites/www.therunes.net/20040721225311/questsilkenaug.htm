Plane of Tranquility Quests

Enchanter Silken Augmenters Armor- Quest POT

NPC: Bor Wharhammer in POT. Need certain plane flags in order to get him to talk to you. Believed to be BOT

Details: Go through the dialog and give him 500pp. He will give you a Class Emblem, they are specific to each class and you will need one for each piece of armor and a sewing kit to do the combine. This will allow you a no fail final combine though.

What you will need:
* The class emblem (above)
*Druzzil's Sewing Kit (above)
* Tanaan Embroidery Needle - Vendor sold by Sherin Matrick in POK, loc Pos 105, Pos 1475 approx cost 133pp (w/100+ CHA)
* Ethereal Silk Swatch Tailoring 212 trivial, tradeable
* Ornate Silk Pattern- These drop off various named in BOT, Drunder, Halls of Honor, Tower of Solusek Ro - tradable

The recipe to make the Silk Swatches can be found at EQTraders
