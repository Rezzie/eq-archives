General Zone Information

Connects at
p550, -1850 coming from The Grey
-1080, p1800 coming from Twilight Sea

EQAtlas map
Arch of Chaos Map with many mob locations marked on a map

Cheetas, lightcrawlers, rockhoppers, and sunflowers are all aggresive by nature, and there are lots of them. Fairly low MR though, so you should have little trouble with mezz and charms sticking.

Several factions otherwise through the zone. There are 3 types of aliens; Grol Baku, Tro Jen, and Vas Ren. Each on their own faction. I have never hunted these, so I'm unsure as to how bad the faction hits are on them. Generally people leave them alone, and prefer to hunt the Revenants and Reavers.

The Revenants. Several camps set up across the zone. Doesn't take much to be KOS to them. Word of warning, they DO see invis, and they have a fairly large aggro radius. They stay in their camps though, so you don't have to worry about wanderers. A great place to hunt is at the camp marked 3 on the EQAtlas map. Some of the best items in the zone drop here. On the Bristlebane server it's usually pretty camped spot.

The Bloodtribe. The Bloodtribe are social, however they have a fairly small aggro radius. I have hunted these a fair amount of time, and am not yet KOS. Very nice drops from them!
