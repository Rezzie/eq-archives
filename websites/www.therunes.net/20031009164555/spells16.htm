Mesmerization
R*
Tasarins Grimoire Left p26
Tasarins Grimoire Right p26

 Learn more about how to do research
Magic
24 sec.
Fizzle Adj: 25%
Mana: 70

Target Based AoE
Stun, Chance to Wipe Hate List.
Area Effect Range: 30 ft. around the target (I usually target the main puller)

This is the spell that makes an enchanter more than a buff whore. This is what makes us GRAND, and sets us apart.
Any damage at all will break the mezz. *sigh*

This is when you must start training people on how to group effectively with an enchanter. Learning and teaching how to properly use the assist command will save you and your group. Many an enchanter has left a group because there is a person who refuses to learn what assist means. Be patient, be crystal clear. Seriously, explain assisting Barney-style. This is a mega-taunt. When your mezz is broken count on all the mobs heading to you. Be sure to have the color line up to stun any that resist or get broken too early. If people in your group refuse to learn assist, 'tis time to move on.

Believe me, at higher levels they WILL learn. However, you do not have to be the sacrificial lamb. Charisma plays a big part of the Mezz line. Most noticable the closer to your level the mob is.

Here is a MUST read thread on the power of this spell, as well as strategies.
