To get there:
From Maidens Eye
I highly recommend your best Rune, a SOW, and invis. Head East,
and slightly south. About halfway across the zone you will start to come
across undead. If one of them breaks your invis, just run! Even if your invis is not broken the mobs guarding the entrance are undead and will see through it.
Just run for the zone.

General Mob Guide:
Dark Assasins- not mezzable, rootable, nor charmable. Are slowable. They have a fairly high aggro range.

Shak Dathar- Slowable, not stunnable

Fallen Soldiers- Slowable, mezzable, not stunnable

Fallen Sergent- Not Mezzable or stunnable, are slowable
