Art Keeper Initiate Armor

North Felwithe
NPC: Yuin Starchaser

-Speak with him about armor and he will give you a curing kit.
- Next ask him about specific types of armor and he will give you that pattern and tell you what other components are required.
-After collecting the components combine them in the curing kit with the pattern to recieve the piece.  Following the stats for each piece is the required components you must collect. You will find all of these in the near-by hunting grounds.
