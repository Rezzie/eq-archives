Omens of War - Custom Titles

With the Omens of War release players are now able to choose from a list of earned titles. They are earned based on your trade skills, amount of AA's earned and level. You are able to choose your title via a drop down list, and you are not stuck with it should your new title not suit you anymore. To acces the UI - "Shift" + "T"
