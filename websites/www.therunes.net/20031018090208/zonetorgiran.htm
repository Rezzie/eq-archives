EQMaps: Map of Torgiran Mines

Quests:

Resistance Miners Quest.
Occasionally a guard will drop a shackle key. Save these and hand these into the resistance miners who occasionally pop in the place of a regular miner. Will give exp and faction, though currently the faction message is broken. Handing in enough keys will eventually spawn Captain Nago (see below)

Mobs:

Overlord Ngrub (L57)
*You must kill one of the mobs near the end of the room to make the taskmaster targetable. Once the taskmaster is killed Overlord Ngrub becomes targetable.
*Hits for approx. 400
*Casts Feeblemind - AOE range 300ft
1: Lure(6)
2: Decrease Hitpoints by 5 per tick
3: Decrease Effective Casting Level by 200
4: Increase Curse Counter by 9
7: Decrease Mana Pool by 800
*Casts AOE Fire Damage
*summons
*Rampages


Captain Nago (52)
He will spawn with a couple of other captains after doing the resistance miner quest. Killing all 4 of the captains will allow Overseer Wrank to become targetable. Wrank is level 55 with an AOE 500dd fire proc, and an AOE snare.
