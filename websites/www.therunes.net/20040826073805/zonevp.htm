 Mob Info


Druushk
Level 70
High Cold Resistance but low Fire Resistance

Quest Reward - Amice of the Skywings
From Chardok B you will need to collect the following:

 Arm - Ferocious Chokadai (60)
Jaw - An Iksar Skeleton (55)
Skull - Shai`din Royal Guards (58)

Once you turn in these items you will recieve an item. If it's not the Amice, simply return it to get the right piece.

 

Nexona
Level 73
Class: Shadownight
Slowable
Hits for approx 650 a round
Low fire and magic resistance

Quests Reward: Shield of Dark Hues
Speaking to Nexona you will recieve, a list and a giant 6 slot container.(Vial)

To complete this quest you will need the following:

A Poison Frog Tongue - - Plane of Storms
A Hurricane Lorok - A Typhoon Lorok (60)

Fyr Jen's Blood - Umbral Plains

Double Brewed Orcish Stout- Brewing 226 Trivial
Pondweed (Dropped in LDON Deepest Guk) +
Barley + Bottle + Cork + Hops + Purified Water + Yeast in a Brew Barrel

Essence of Concealment- Alchemy 224 Trivial
Burdock Root + Empty Vial + Mystic Ash + Swamp Vine (Foraged in LDON Deepest Guk) in a Medicine Bag


Zombie Bane - Poisonmaking, 231 Trivial
Advanced Poison Vial + Amber Resin + Celestial Suspension + Undead Shissar Venom Sack (Drop from Ssra Temple) in a Mortar & Pestle

Combine these items in the container and give them to a named rat in Dragons Necropolis - Neb. He will give you Nebs Note that you return to Nexona for your shield.
