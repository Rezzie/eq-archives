By: Riachi,
(as posted on Allahkazams)
Posted @ Sun, Feb 24th 1:21 PM 2002

Ok here is how it goes, Im almost done both version of this quest, cept the major ending peices.

Scout Raid One
Turn in your gem, kill everything in camp he leads you to, loot officer when he spawns, give the body part to the Scout as long as he survived.

Scout Raid Two
Same as above, harder mobs.

Scout Raid Three
Same as above, harder mobs.

Captains Quest
(Note: Requires minimal TWO Groups)
Follow Captain out of fort after turning in the combined three medals of last three quests.
Captain meets an Officer, Send most powerful group with Captain who goes one way, and second group follows officer to an additional camp, kill everything, keep officer and captain alive, if either die, U fail and back to very beginning.

Generals Quest
After completing Captains Quest you are given Medal of the Shar Vahl Hero or something like that, you turn it into the Captain and be ready to keep his two Veterans Alive. You assault the two platforms just outside Acrylia Cavern Tunnel. Is roughly 35 NPCs as I counted the other day when there. All level 40 to 45ish. So either have a really careful AE group not to kill the required NPCs or have a loy of people to hack them down as fast as possible

Results of the Quest:
Grimling Castle Key
This key is used where the High Preist in Acrylia Caverns Stands, is a thing on the wall hes owrshipping, Use key on there, and search for your way into Grimling Castle. Is other loot items along the way, usually nothing spectacular till ya kill the Grimling High Commander on the Generals Raid.

(Note: Must be level 50+ or General wont give you the key)

Alternative Way of Quest.
Generals Challenge:

Collect the 8 items and combine them in the chest to go directly to the Last Raid on first part.

Step One to Four,
In the tunnel on way to Acrylia Caverns, Four named mobs spawn there. Place Holders are the Overseers, Sorceress and Prophet, Kill these till ya get body peice of each named.
Sorceress Gnildia's Eye
Overseer Gnerpokkel's Heart
Overseer Gizgarank's Skull
Prophet Grikplag's Tooth

Few of these guys also drop items such as
Swordsmans Armband (Haste Arms)
Grimling Warmask 7ac 7dex 7str 30hp 3%mod sneak mask all/all
just as examples

After getting those Four body Parts, next step is Acrylia Caverns

(note: grab the Forked Key from the Grimling Prisoner in the outpost in Grimling Forest)

Step Five and Six
Clear mobs until Possessor Gooplart and Corpseraiser Gilgaplank both spawn, get
Corpseraiser Gilgaplank's Toes
Possessor Gooplart's Tooth

Step Seven
Find the Summoned Burrower and kill it for the
Husk of the Summoned Burrower
(note: left of zoneline go under water, is some untargettable summoned creatures, may have to figure out how to trigger them, possibly killing the summoners above the water. Also the Wormbait Minnows spawn on 60 second timers and is roughly 15 of them, requires mass crowd control and fast killing)

Step Eight
Go to the Lava room, left side is a door, second door is locked, use the Forked Key to open the prison door. Talk to Trondhil Shir (spelling?) he is the captured Vah Shir in the prison. Somehow he is linked the the High Preist. This step is still not known how to be done. The Vah Shir Prisoner has the Shackles of the Captured Vah Shir, but we are unable to figure out how to get them, and killing him didnt work, also really bad faction.

Step Nine
Turn in the combined eight body parts, then the Generals Attack on the 2 Platforms ouside Acrylia begins etc etc. Get your Grimling Castle key and begin exploring.

And thats what I know so far on this quest.
