Minor Illusion None
Duration: 36min
25% Fizzle increase

Due to recent changes to non racial illusion factions, this spell is now a palor trick. Will turn you into the nearest object.

Occasionally, there isn't an object close enough to you to allow the illusion. When this happens, take any item (a tiny dagger, fishing grub etc)set it on the ground and then cast spell.
*Word of caution here * as well as Illusion:Tree. When you die, you're corpse will retain the illusion. Be sure that you will be able to find yourself. I heard of someone who had cast Tree illusion in a forest, died and was unable to find his body.
Strengthen None
Duration: 27min
25% Fizzle increase

Increase Strength by 5( lvl1) -10(lvl6) Gives target a Strength bonus. A must have on your pet! Our pets (if that's what you want to call them : ) are weaklings. And most casters are weak as well. This will help you carry all that loot!
Weaken Magic
Duration: 6min
25% Fizzle increase
 Decrease targets strength by 10 (lvl1)-15 (lvl10).
This will help even things a bit ,especially if you are soloing. This weakens the target so they do not hit as hard.

Items with this effect: Dagger of Dropping (When you begin hunting in South Karanas, be sure to save these from the Gnolls. Great item to give your pet!

Lull Magic
Duration: 2min
25% Fizzle increase

Makes reaction radius 15 ft. Changes frenzy radius 15 ft.
Recent changes to the entire Lull line have made this spell much more useful.

Casting this will allow you to not aggro everything around the mob you want to pull.

Charisma has a direct effect on whether or not your casting is successful
