Incandescent Quest

Incandescent Mask
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
Slot: FACE
AC: 3
CHA: +7 INT: +5
Focus: Summoning Haste II
WT: 0.4 Size: SMALL
Class: MNK ENC
Race: HUM ERU HIE DEF GNM IKS

Zone: Temple of Solusek Ro
NPC: Sultin
Talk to Sultin in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He can make various incandescent items if brought the correct components. He will ask you to bring him the following items to make the Incandescent Mask.

Glowing Mask
Rune of Fortune x3
The glowing mask can be found on the Skeletal Monk who is in the underwater cave in Guk. It is possible that the Froglok Scryre also drops this mask. For the three different runes of fortune you can purchase two of them in towns. One from Mizr N'Mar who can be found in the Neriak City Library, and the other from Madam Serena who resides in Qeynos. Each rune costs 50 gold. The last rune you can find off the Dark Elf Enynti who is in the Chasm below Castle Mistmoore.

Incandescent Gloves
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
Slot: HANDS
AC: 6
STR: +3 CHA: +3 AGI: +3 MANA: +3
WT: 1.5 Size: SMALL
Class: ENC
Race: HUM ERU HIE DEF GNM

Talk to Sultin in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He can make various incandescent items if brought the correct components. He will ask you to bring him the four following items so that he can sew them together to make the Incandescent Gloves.

Glowing Gloves
Radiant Gloves
Gleaming Gloves
Enchanted Gloves

The glowing gloves can be found from Shadowmen who reside all over Norrath. Next you must head to Cazic-Thule and kill Radiant, a named lizardman who roams the maze. Loot his body to receive the radiant gloves. To obtain the gleaming gloves, head to Highkeep and hand Tarn Vislin a bloodstone. For the last pair of gloves, head off to Lavastorm and hand Tizina, one of the gypsies, 50 Gold. She will give you the enchanted gloves in return.
