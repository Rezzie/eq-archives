This is the first part of a progressive quest that begins in Sanctus Seru.

Insignia Earring of Veracity
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
Slot: EAR
AC: 1
STR: +1 STA: +1 WIS: +1 INT: +1
WT: 0.1 Size: TINY
Class: ALL
Race: ALL

Zone: Sanctus Seru
NPC: Torsten Reidan -990,-520
Approx Level: 20

You will need the following items:
4 Bixie Crunchies - (1 Bixie part + 1 Frosting combined in oven. May also be bought from a baker)
1 Dawnshroud Cider - vendor in Sanctus Seru(+830,-600)
1 Bixie Charm - See below
1 letter - See below

What to do:
-  First give the Dawnshroud Cider to Legionnaire Dalini in the Netherbian Lair. Im pretty sure he can be found close to the entrance of Marus Seru

-That will spawn Bregun Dorey in the Trog Caves. The Trog caves are in the last cave area on the left if you are going to Dawnshroud. The Trogs will not attack you BTW. Find him and hand him four bixie crunchies. He will give you a Bixie Charm.

- Give the Bixie Charm to Torsten Reidan in Sanctus Seru(-990,-520). He will give you a letter.

- Now go back to Netherbian Lair and give the letter to Bregun Dorey. Sometimes people will kill him (though he is a tough kill) If this happens you can respawn him with another Dawnshroud Cider.

-- Return to Torsten Reidan in Sanctus Seru and give him the letter you got from Bregun for your Insignia Earring.

Faction Adjustments After Doing This Quest:
Raises Your Faction With: Magus Conlegium, Validus Custodus
Lowers Your Faction With: Coterie of the Eternal Night, Eye of Seru, Hand Legionnaries, Hand of Seru, Nathyn Illuminious, Seru
