June 15, 2004

3 spell changes are showing on Lucy. Damage shields, including Feedback, have had their duration increased to 15 min. In addition the little used spells Bounce and Reflect have had their chance to reflect spell damage uped to 100%. Personally I doubt this change will stay, but let's keep our fingers crossed!

The next patch looks to be a fairly large one. Many changes that had been slated to happen before the summit, as well as several changes as a result of the summit will be happening. I posted a list of changes on the Forums. I'll also be posting them here for those who just want a quick look at what's happening.

-The Qinimi and Barindu events now drop 3 pieces, rather than just 1. The Barindu event was lowered to a 24 hour-ish respawn.

-Sewer rares now drop their piece 100% of the time. The difficulty will be just in finding and killing the rares now.

-The cragbeast and kyv bosses in Tipt now drop their piece 100% of the time.

-Drop rates in Vxed were increased.

-The Primals and Mindrider in Yxtta will now drop their page 100% of the time. In addition, their pages are available off basepop as well, albiet at a much lower rate, to allow a single group path to this part.

-Portal from POK to Gunthak

-A raidleader will now have the ability to remove people across zones.

-Zone "hotspots" likely to be rotated with the patch as well. I will keep everyone updated on this.

June 1, 2004

A new zone snuck it's way onto live servers. The zone, called Forgotten Halls, showcases some of the new graphic capabilities. I don't think it was quite ready for production servers (and why no mention of it was made by SOE) First the bad- Lag was horrible for a couple of members of our party. I read on another forum to change resolution, then change it back. That seemed to fix it for them. Other than that, it was an interesting zone. Visually it was stunning.

Nothing being KOS, you are free to roam the zone. When you are ready to hunt, there are areas with bats and rats. An NPC will ask you to rid theses pests for them.

Pulling is interesting, and required that our group was on our toes. They are clustered together, and singles where impossible (thank goodness!) Mobs where immune to Wake of Tranquility, though they could be harmonied.

At one point I couldn't loot my corpse though it was at my feet. It took about 15 min before I was finally able to loot it. We also had about half the group get thrown to desktop. After about 30 min, things seemed to work themselves out and we had a good time in spite of the inital bugs. Pulling was interesting and required we use a bit of strategy. Overall it was fun, great exp, and visually stunning.

To get there, go to Nedaria's, Then head to the south dock. There is an NPC who will give you the expedition. The entrance is in the bear cave.
You will talk to the NPC who will tell you to click on the large rock and allow you entrance to the zone. Have fun!
