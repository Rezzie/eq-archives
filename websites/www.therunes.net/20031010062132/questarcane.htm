Arcane Scientists Gear
NPC: Shana Liskia in the casters guild
*Speak with her about the armour . She will give you an Enchanted Sewing Kit.When you ask about specfic pieces she will give you a pattern and will tell you the other items required to make that particular piece. All items can be found in the nearby hunting grounds. There is no skill check when doing the combine.
-Take the required ingredients and combine them in the Echanted Sewing Kit.
-Now take the combine results and head to the nearest loom.
-Put the combined material and the pattern into the loom and click combine.. VOILA!
