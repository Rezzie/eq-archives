July 8, 2004

Today's patch was moved to July 14th. SOE felt an additional week on test would be best considering the size and the amount of changes scheduled. In case you have no been around the boards, here's a brief summary:

Revamped Veeshans Peak - If you missed out finishing up your current VP key here's your last chance to get that Traks Tooth!
It's my understanding that the new key will involve 3 turn ins to complete if you do not have the old key. If you have the old key you can skip those turns ins and will need to kill an NPC who drops 40 books. Turn that in for your shiny new key! Several people have asked if having the old key soulbound will be a problem. SOE has assured everyone it will not make a difference.

Zoning Pets - Pets will now zone with you. For Enchanters this only applies to our summoned animation.

Safe Removal of Augments- Solvents will be available to safely remove your augments. Right clicking on it will tell you the type of solvent you need. All solvents will be sold via an LDON vendor. In addition, there will be only one solvent to remove augments you no longer wish to keep.

Selling back of LDON gear- Items that you purchased using adventure points will be re sellable to the adventure merchants at a ratio of 50% of the original cost.

Buff Countdown timer- Putting your mouse over the buff icon will show you the remaining time left on the buff.

NPC de buff slots have been increased to 50. This mainly effects raid NPC's.

Tribute Points - You will now prepay for tribute points in 10 min intervals. You will also be able to activate and deactivate them via your interface. But you can't pick and choose the benefits to turn off, they will either be on or off.  Improved descriptions will help you choose the correct benefits.

Additional Collision Fixes- Self explanatory

Players asked for mana numbers to show up just as HP's show.. we got it :) This change is already on live servers, however it takes an updated UI in order to see it. There are a couple of very nice ones out already.

June 18, 2004

Rytan answers the Enchanter Q&A. Many were not happy with the answers we received. You may read it here and feel free to add in your comments as well.

The revamped Veeshans Peak is scheduled to go live on July 6th. If you were trying to complete your key, do it now! Rumor has it VP will now be an elemental level zone.

High Elf leather skirt graphics were changed in the 16th patch. Most were not happy about this change! There is a thread here as well as an active thread on EQLive forums. Please add in your support for changing the graphic back.

The ability to remove augments should be live with the July 6th patch. No idea what route SOE decided to go with in order to remove them. Most likely it will be a trade skill solvent, hopefully similar to the dyes available now.

SOE is also taking suggestions on our Epic 2 effects. Please stop by the EQLive forum and add in your 2cps!
