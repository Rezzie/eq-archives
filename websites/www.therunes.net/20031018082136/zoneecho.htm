General Zone Guide:
There are actually 2 Echo Caverns. Shout, ooc's etc can be heard from either side, but there is no place that they physically connect. One side (side A for the sake of this guide) can be access by going through the Dwarf section of Shadowhaven. The other side (side B) is accessed through a fake wall in one of the empty rooms around the spire.This is the connection side that takes you to Fungus Grove.

Side B
There are a couple of NPC's that are well hidden in this area. When you zone into Echo, make a right, and open the hidden door. Once this area opens up (where the evil vendors are) stay to the left. You will see a small stream of water and a very small entrance. Swim through the stream a short ways. When it opens up, there will be 4-5 Arisen Skeletons. To my knowledge they are on an evil faction, so good-doers go with caution. The Arisen skeletons are level 47-49, and supposedly quite good exp. They are social so have mezz ready for the adds.

If you follow the stairs up, there is another set of Arisen Soldiers surrounding an NPC, Nightmaster Kelador. He is a 61ish Necromancer. Hails did produce a dialoge, however, I was unable to get any further with questioning. Pulling the soldiers at the water will not aggro anything upstairs as long as you don't get too far up the stairs.
Killing the bandits in Fungus Grove eventually lowered my faction with these guys to KOS
