Enchanter Spells

Obtaining spells in this expansion is different than in Gates or PoP. Muramite Runes are turned into Karsor the Mad in Draniks Scar. He is located at -1650,-40.

Muramite Runes ARE droppable, however the spell received will be No Drop and will also be in a particular order. Right now on the boards we are compiling a list of the order they are being given, this page will be updated as soon as the list is complete.

The type of Rune you turn in will determine the level of spell you get.


Minor Muramite Rune - Level 66
Lesser Muramite Rune - Level 67
Muramite Rune - Level 68
Greater Muramite Rune - Level 69
Glowing Muramite Rune - Level 70

Muramite Runes seem to be dropping most everywhere, and so far they seem to be set at a good drop rate.
