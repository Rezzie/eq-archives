Description

The Deep is an especially deep network of caverns on Luclin, which is rumored to be very deadly.

Dangers

There is rumored to be an Abyss, a pit of unfathomable depth, deep within this network of caverns.

Unknown

Travelling To and From The Deep

The entrance to the Deep is by falling into a pit in Echo Caverns.
