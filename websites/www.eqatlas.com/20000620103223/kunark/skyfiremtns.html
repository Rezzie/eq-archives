Travelling To and From Skyfire Mountains

The Skyfire Mountains can be reached by zoning at approximately 4000, 1500 from Burning Wood. Unknown what is loc of zone in the Skyfire Mountains.

The Overthere can be reached from Skyfire Mountains, although the location is unknown.

Veeshan's Peak can be reached through a large, ornate entrance that is heavily guarded by wyverns and their kin. The location is unknown.

The Wizard spell Tishan's Relocation teleports the group to location -3100, 790.

The Druid spell Wind of the North teleports the group to location -3185, 2730.
