Poll Over and Fan Faire
Saturday, June 16th

I meant to end the poll a few days ago, but didn't get the time. For those of you paying attention, there was a glitch where the poll would reset itself every now and then. I'm calling Tower of Frozen Shadow as the winner. This is partially due to the fact that, well, it won. It's also because I'm sick of people emailing me asking for it (you know who you are). Plus someone raised the point that there really aren't any maps out there for TOFS, whereas City of Mist (the runner-up) has quite a few. Point taken.

Also, I hope everyone who's headed to the Fan Faire this weekend has a good time. I couldn't make it to this one (flying to Minnesota doesn't fit into my budget of zero). Wish I could be there to meet/get trashed with you all...
