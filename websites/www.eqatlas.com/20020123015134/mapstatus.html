Map Status

The following is a chart that shows how far along I am on the maps for each of the missing zones. Hopefully this will answer the questions everyone has about "where is the map for zone x?!?" Read on...

There are a few steps that I go through with mapping out all the zones. Easier, low level zones don't require any planning on my part, but the higher level zones, especially dungeons, do. What planning consists of is me scouring the web looking for information like what the factions are in a zone, if anyone can travel safely through it (like a lvl 1 halfling in the Hole), if invisibility works, etc. That lets me figure out exactly how I'm going to go about mapping it out, or if I even can map it out. Next comes the actual mapping. Now that I have a log parser that I use, this isn't as long a process, but still can take several hours in a complicated zone. When I do the mapping, I write down all the major NPC's, all the merchants, etc. Then I ink and color it in, this usually takes at least an hour. Then I do the scanning and image prep, which takes about a half hour, and then labeling of the zone on the computer, which can take anywhere from 5 minutes to a couple of hours, depending on how much info I have to get from other places on the web, old emails, etc. Then I put the info on the web pages themselves, which again is highly variable depending on how much is there. All told, the time from scanning to posting on the web can take from 45 minutes to 3-4 hours.
