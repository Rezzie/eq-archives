Description

The Dreadlands is a vast icy crater inhabited by many dangerous creatures. It is also believed to be the site of an ancient settlement of the Combine Empire.

Dangers

Unknown.

Benefits

This is where Druids and Wizards are able to teleport into the continent of Kunark.

Travelling To and From The Dreadlands

The Druid and Wizard Teleportation spots are located at 2550, 7500 and 900, 9000 respectively.

There are many ways to get into the Dreadlands. The zone to Burning Wood is at approximately 2500, -1000. This places you at approximately -3500, -800 in the Burning Wood.

The zone to Firiona Vie is at 400, -5900. The tunnel in Firiona Vie opens up at -200, 3590 after following a long passage through the mountains.

Frontier Mountains can be entered through a tunnel located at 2170, -3390 and zones into Frontier Mountains at location -3675, -3330 (at the end of the tunnel).

Karnor's Castle is located at approximately 500, -2250.
