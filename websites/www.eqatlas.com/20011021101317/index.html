This is a Netscape Problem...
Wednesday, October 17th

There are a *lot* of people emailing me about being unable to print maps, getting the error that "there are no pages to print" or something similar. This is a Netscape problem, and has nothing to do with the site. I've confirmed this with some testing by a kind viewer (thanks Rigel!). If you have the printing problem with any browser other than Netscape, let me know. You can print by just viewing the jpg and printing that.

I've updated the Chardok information with stuff people have sent in, and I'm hard at work raising my faction so I can venture into Chardok, thanks the for info all. There are still a few items in Chardok that I haven't had confirmed yet, check them out and see if you can help.

This also raises the point that I have not had many updates to the higher level zones in a while. Danalog helped out over a month ago, but has since vanished (if you're reading this Dan, get in touch with me). I am unable to get to these zones on my own, so any help is appreciated.

Claudia Black's B-day and some Updates
Wednesday, October 10th

I forgot yesterday to mention that Claudia Black, who plays Aeryn Sun on sci-fi's Farscape, is having a birthday today (the 11th actually, but Australian time). Hope it's a good one, she seems like a classy woman.

Also, wanted to reiterate that the DA Atlas site has its own URL set up now at http://www.daatlas.com.

Also, I added in some updates to the Chardok guide, since it had a rather heavy update in the last patch. If anyone can tell me what faction I need to be non-KOS there, that would be a great help.

Also, Verant pumped in the new role-playing server on Monday as well. I haven't been on to check it out. I find it interesting that the long-talked about role-playing server just happened to go into service the week before Dark Age of Camelot was to be released (it was originally scheduled 10 days later). I find it impossible to believe that Verant took over 6 months to finalize a ruleset, and we know it doesn't take them that long to set up a new server, so if anyone thinks this is pure chance, I got a bridge here in LA I can sell you, real cheap.

Also, I found a link to some pre-release Luclin screenshots on the EQ site, that they have put out as part of the 2nd anniversary celebration. Check them out, especially the new ogre model! :)

DA Atlas
Tuesday, October 9th

I'm announcing the preliminary opening of the DA Atlas site today to coincide with the real game going live today (although I don't think anyone has the actual game in their hands yet). The address is: http://www.daatlas.com

Also, as a reminder, you can help out the site and order Dark Age of Camelot from the link at the right. The sales through here better be good, as it's already sold over 100,000 copies in pre-orders.

Right now the site has up the maps of the major cities, and should be getting more and more stuff over the days and weeks ahead. Let me know what you think of the design and layout, it's a work in progress.

Also, many people have been complaining of printing problems on this page, where they get an error stating that there is no page to print. I believe that this occurs only with the new Netscape 6, and isn't related to anything that I've done with the page, including popup ads since it was occurring before they were on the site. I won't be responding to the endless emails I get about this, but if you're getting the error and the above isn't true in your case, feel free to contact me again.
