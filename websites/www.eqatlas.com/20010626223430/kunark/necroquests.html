Necromancer Quest Guide


The Quests

First Rank Skullcap
Master Xydoz
Tower of Death
If you ask about Apprentice Skullcap, he states that he requires the brains of four Sarnak hatchlings returned to him in exchange for the First Rank Skullcap.

Second Rank Skullcap
Master Xydoz
Tower of Death
If you ask about Second Rank Skullcap, he will state that he seeks a Faded Tapestry. You need to be adept at tailoring and acquire two pieces of the now destroyed tapestry and repair them. The pieces are the Torn and Ripped Tapestry, which you find off of Sarnak Hatchlings as rare drops in the Lake of Ill Omen.

Third Rank Skullcap
Master Rixiz
Temple of Death
When asking about acquiring the Third Rank Skullcap, he states that he needs you to do something. He hands you a two-item container called a Glass Canopic and asks that you place inside the brain of a Sarnak Crypt Raider, and immediately combine it with Embalming Fluid (which you must brew). Returning it to him will get you the third and final skullcap.

New Components
Master Kyvix
Temple of Death
When asking the Master about the New Components that he needs, he states he requires an apprentice of the third circle. To this person he hands a Brood Sac, which he wants filled with Creeper Cabbage, a Heartsting Telson with venom, Brutling Choppers and a Scalebone Femur. In return for returning this combined sac, you will receive a Dark Binder Skullcap and your Fourth Rank.
