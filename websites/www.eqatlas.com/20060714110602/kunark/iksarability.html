Iksar Abilities

The Iksar possess several capabilities that make them able to survive their vast continent better than many other races who are not prepared for its dangers.

Infravision - Similar in nature to the infravision possessed by other races.

Swimming - All Iksar start out with the natural ability to swim. Their skill level starts at 100, so they are very fast swimmers, and increases rapidly with use. The city of Cabilis is lined with canals, and many of its inhabitants prefer to use them to move about the city instead of walking.

Underwater Breathing - Although not technically able to breathe underwater, Iksar are capable of swimming long distances without needing to refill their lungs, due to their efficient swimming method.

Regeneration - All Iksar have the ability to regenerate. They do so at two points per click while standing, four points while sitting down.

Foraging - All Iksar begin with the ability to forage in the wild for food. Their skill level with this starts at 50. Although they are unable to advance, they are quite successful with this skill and only rarely need to purchase more food or water.

Lizardman - They are all fluent in their native tongue.

Common - For some reason, they have learned the trade language of the mainland.

Sense Heading - They possess this skill, but with no natural inclination. Their starting level is 1.
