Description

The City of Mist is a small, haunted village in the Emerald Jungle.

Dangers

The Reavers will continually spawn new creatures many many times after you kill them. At some point, the Lord may spawn out of this.

Benefits

Melee classes are able to be bound here.

Travelling To and From City Of Mist

The City of Mist can be reached by entering it from the Emerald Jungle, with the entrance found at 310, -1960.
