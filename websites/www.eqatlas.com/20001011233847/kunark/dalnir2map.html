DALNIR - SECOND LEVEL

Location Key

2 "Shackle Room" with Kly Imprecator who drops An Ancient Coin (Common), Dagger Hilt (Common), Bamboo Bo Stick (Uncommon), and Robe of Dalnir (Ultra Rare), and Coerced Channeler who drops nothing
3 "Bedrooms"
4 "Pool Room" where pit from Area #4 on First Level drops to

6 Room with "Carpet Lumpy" (Lumpy Goo who drops Fighting Baton (Rare))
7 Secret Pit Trap leading down to Area #7 on Level Three
8 "Checkerboard Room"
Note that somewhere on this floor spawns the Coerced Crusader who drops the Greenwood Bo Stick
