Warrior Quest Guide


The Quests

Curscale Shield
Drill Master Dal
Fortress Talishan
Mention that you are a new recruit and he will hand you a Partisan Pack, a 10-item backpack! He wants you to fill it with one Giant Blood Sac of the Giant Leech, Scout Beads from Goblin Scout, one Sabertooth Kitten Canine, and three Bone Chips. Combine and return for Curscale Shield.

Marauder Armor
Drill Master Dal
Fortress Talishan
Mention that you are a legion soldier and the Drill Master will be most excited. He wants proof that Marrtail's Marauders are operating nearby. Bring him proof that you have encountered four of them, and he will reward you with an armor unavailable to most. These items are likely Marauder Snout Rings.

Militia Pike
Drill Master Vygan
Fortress Talishan
It seems that there is an Iksar traitor who keeps secreting the froglok slaves to freedom in the swamps. The demanding Drill Master does not like this, and wants you to acquire the head of the Iksar traitor and the whistle of the Froglok escort, as well as your Partisan Pike, in order to receive the Militia Pike. This quest may be broken.

Footman Pike
Drill Master Kyg
Fortress Talishan
Ask about Footman Pike and he hands you Footmans Pack and demands that you fill it with the weapons of our foes. What he requires are four javelins, two from Froglok Bounders and two from Goblin Hunters. Return this pack along with Militia Pike and you'll receive Footman Pike and a "true mission."

Geozite Tool
Drill Master Vygan
Fortress Talishan
While discussing how to upgrade pikes, the Drill Master how each upgrade requires a geozite tool. When asked about that he hands you a Pincer Satchel which he requires two Large Scorpion Pincers to return to him and prove you are a real warrior, at which point he'll send you on the real quest.
