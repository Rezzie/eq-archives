Fan Faire Update
Sunday, April 8th

Just got back from the Fan Faire in San Diego. This was a cool event that I had a lot of fun at, it's just too bad I couldn't hang around for the Saturday night after-party "festivities."

There were some announcements that came out of it. One is that the next Fan Faire is going to be on June 16-17 in Minneapolis, MN. I think summer is there by then. The other big announcement was that they are coming out with two servers next with different rules. The first is a PvP server with an unknown-as-yet ruleset. The second will be a role-playing server that Brad mentioned might have restrictions (in some way) on grouping based on race and religion, as well as a one-character-per-server rule. Should be interesting.

Brad and Jon also mentioned a few tidbits while specifically not mentioning stuff. Jon said that although they weren't mentioning supporting other platforms, they were in fact a Sony company, so make of that fact what we will. Brad expanded to say that they plan on following online gaming wherever it may lead, right now that is on PC's. They also mentioned that although they aren't announcing any expansions at the present time, they do plan on enhancing the game for as long as it remains popular. They also specifically didn't mention what race they might introduce next, although when asked if it would be pixies, Jon specifically mentioned that they weren't stupid. :)

Another Hole Map!
Friday, March 30th

I added in a larger map of the Hole that is printable, but will take up the whole page on its own, so that people can print that and see it a bit better. The one I put up yesterday, I realized, was a bit small, especially when printed.

Also, the new personal gate spells to the Dreadlands were added in with this patch for wizards and druids. They are both level 24 spells. The wizard spell can be purchased in the Temple of Solusek Ro by a new gnome vendor up on the balcony (it's not on my map yet). The druid spell can be purchased from the druid guild in Kelethin (#21 on my map). FYI, if you're looking for the Dreadlands map, realize that it's separated into two pieces, the portals are on the Lost Valley map (this question has almost reached FAQ status, sadly).

Also, shamans can now create a potion that will take you to the Field of Bone at the bindable place in Kaesora ruins. I've updated the Getting Around Guide appropriately.

New Hole Map!
Thursday, March 29th

I put up the new Guide and Map to the Hole today. Hope you enjoy it! Note that there is a paucity of information about monsters and loot drops in this zone. If you have any additional info, feel free to send it in.

As to when I'll put up a new survey to get your opinions on where to map, I'm holding off on that until I can see how deeply I can penetrate into the Tower of Frozen Shadow. If I can't, it won't be on the list, so I'm waiting to see.

Also, it appears that the official boards at the Station were closed down to be replaced by something more useful. I applaud this, since these boards have been essentially useless for anything other than complaining (hence the appropriate name "Whineplay"). They have now become a place where you submit comments or ideas that get reviewed before they are commented on by others, instead of directly posting your thoughts. I'm normally not a fan of such heavy-handedness, although that place really needed some help (and by "help" I mean swift beatings with a heavy stick).
