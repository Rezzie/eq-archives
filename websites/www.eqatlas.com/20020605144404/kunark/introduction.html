The Ruins of Kunark - Introduction

Introduction

The Ruins of Kunark is an expansion pack for the online role-playing game of Everquest, which was released on or about April 24th (the original release date). Yours truly was involved in the beta testing of this project, and so have decided to make a special section devoted to the beta test of the Ruins of Kunark as well as to Kunark in general. Some of the material in this section could be regarded as spoiler material, so if you don't want to see that, you are forewarned.

What I will go over in the next few pages is some storyline (as much as I know and have determined) for the Ruins of Kunark at the time that the expansion was released. This information may (and in fact, probably will) be out of date as time goes on, because I don't plan on updating the material in this document with new information. The information I'm going to include will be description of the history of the land of Kunark, what the areas of Kunark are like, and what you can expect in terms of benefits and disadvantages when switching from the "old" Everquest to the expansion.
