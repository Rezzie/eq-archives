KURN'S TOWER LOWER LEVELS

Location Key
1 Undead Jester who drops Iksar Berzerker Club (Rare)
2 Wandering Burynai Forager who drops Dusty Sealed Canopic Jar (Common)
3 Skeleton Cook who drops Shield of Kurn (Uncommon) and Glowing Mask (Rare), and Wandering Fingered Skeleton who drops various size Skeleton Fingers
4 Bargynn who drops Bargynn's Torch (Common) and Bargynn's Digger (Common)
Note that Thick Boned Skeleton can spawn in place of any of the above four creatures
A&B are pits that lead from Basement to Tunnels
