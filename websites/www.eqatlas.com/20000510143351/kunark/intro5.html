Tips for Kunark

Here are some tips that might help you out when dealing with the Kunark, both in terms of what to expect and to not expect, as well as how to move about.

  • Spirit of the Wolf. The zone is vast, and having a speed-enhancing spell on at all times makes life so much better.
  • Weight-reducing bags. Carry those special bags (like from the orc in Highpass) that reduce weight to zero so that you can carry more goodies.
  • Food and Water. Either bring a lot of them, have a friend who can summon them, or make an Iksar character to bring you one of the new food/water creation devices. There is no place for you to buy these items easily on the continent.
  • Basic Safe Travelling Concepts. I've used these all along to map areas I shouldn't be in. This includes not getting lost (figure out how to use /loc), /con'ing monsters as you run along (right-click on them to get a fast /con), and just general stupid avoidance (don't go running over a hilltop with SoW in an area you've never been in but you think has giants, for instance).
  • Maps. I may be biased here, but I think knowing where you are going is going to help out a lot.
  • Have fun! The expansion, for me, is a very exciting thing. I love exploring new areas and finding out things that many people don't know or haven't seen yet. Try to do this as much as possible. In the weeks ahead, Kunark will still vastly be an unexplored land for all of us. Enjoy it while it lasts, since they haven't announced another expansion yet!
