Ugg...
Wednesday, May 16th

The good news is that today was my birthday (man I feel old).

The bad news is that my email program ate the 10,000 emails I had in my EQ Atlas folder and spit out, you guessed it, nine of them. I guess I can thank the fine person who sent me a virus which, although caught, apparently messed up the program enough that my table of contents got hosed for that mailbox and all the great information you all have sent me for the past 2 years vanished without a trace. It could also be related to PacBell deciding to drop all their DSL people permanently, thereby causing me to use the really buggy dial-up networking of Windows 95 on my computer again. What a week. So, if you've emailed me in the last few weeks and didn't get a response, send it in again so I can have a crack at it. What a birthday, I hope E3 cheers me up (thanks for the tix, everyone, who's names are lost on my hard drive forever).
