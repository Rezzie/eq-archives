Description

The mines are the underground home of the Burynai, who work alongside the goblins in maintaining them. The Burynai make their homes within.

Dangers

The entrance to the Mines is of the "one way" variety, where when you reach a certain point, you fall through a false floor and will be unable to get back out again...

Benefits

Unknown.

Travelling To and From Mines Of Nurga

The Mines can be reached in the Frontier Mountains, at location -2955, -655. It is also possible to reach the Temple of Droga from deep within the mines. You enter the Temple itself, and are then confronted with a large waterfall which you must drop off of in order to reach the rest of the zone.
