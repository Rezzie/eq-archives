The Quests

Scaled Mystic Temple Tasks
Hierophant Oxyn
Temple of Terror
Hands you Component Mortar, required to fill with two Small Mosquito Wings, one Bone Chips, and foraged Mud Crabs (found in Lake of Ill Omen). States that upon your return, will give you a bigger task.

Crusaders Test of the Pawn
Lord Gikzic
Temple of Terror
Hands you Sarnak Head Pack, and requires you to fill it with four Sarnak Heads and return it with your khukri of the pawn to acquire the khukri of the knave. The sarnaks can be found in Lake of Ill Omen.

Who Sent You?
Hierophant Dexl
Temple of Terror
Asks who sent you, but I haven't figured out what to say to him.

Bone Granite Powder
Jondin
West Cabilis
He asks if you would gather bone chips, but in reality needs Bone Granite Powder, which you must create using Granite Pebbles that you find as well as three Bone Chips that you combine using your Alchemy skill and return to him.
