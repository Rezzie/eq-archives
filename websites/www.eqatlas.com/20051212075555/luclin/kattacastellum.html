Description

Katta Castellum is the city built by the Loyalists to Tsaph Katta following his assassination attempt and the relocation of his followers within the Combine Empire to the moon of Luclin. The city is built in a strong defensive position, to hold off enemies both known and unknown.

Surrounding Areas

The Katta Castellum people are surrounded by enemies. The Tenebrous Mountains holds the vampires of the Coterie, which have been infiltrating and terrorizing the city for some time. Also, the Inquisition is always hunting down the members of the city, although thankfully they are further away.

Dangers

The enemies of Katta have turned the place into something of a dangerous place. Coterie assassins roam the streets in the dark places, jumping on the unwary. The undead have also been known to rise from the ground in the graveyards, powered by the enemies of this place.

Levitate spells don't work in this area at all, so you can't float off of the ledge down to the lower level.

Travelling To and From Katta Castellum

Katta Castellum can be reached through the Twilight Sea via a large dock on the eastern shore of the sea. The other entrance is in the Tenebrous Mountains along the western edge.

Local Color

Places

Katta Castellum is a very busy city, with the descendants of Tsaph Katta and the Combine empire working daily to improve their lot and defend themselves from the moon of Luclin.

People

These members of the Loyalists have had a resurgence of energy and spirit with the reopening of the portal to Luclin. This is the first time that outsiders have moved onto the moon or that they have had the possibility of returning back to Norrath. Most of them, however, now call Luclin their home and wish to remain here, but the new energy that the travellers bring have improved conditions around the city.
