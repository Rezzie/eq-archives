Description

The Crypt of Dalnir is the hidden burial tomb of Dalnir, the ancient Haggle Baron of the City of Cabilis. It is rumored to have many riches hidden inside, if only someone could find out where it is...

Dangers

There are several levels to this dungeon, most of them filled with coerced beings or the Kly, the rulers of this small realm. There are many pit traps throughout the area, especially as you get deeper, and levitation is required to go deep into the zone, bring lots of bat wings...

Benefits

Unknown.

Travelling To and From Crypt Of Dalnir

The entrance to Dalnir is located through an old, hidden tunnel at 3490, 3965. Bear to the right down the tunnel to lead you to the water illusion that leads you underneath...

The goblins in Lake of Ill Omen have long been operating a mine in the area. Recently, they have tunneled into an ancient structure that leads to Warsliks Woods region, but buried underground. This structure is inhabited by many outcast Iksar, who seem to be guarding something. I was unable to find the entrance of the Crypt, however.
