Description

The history behind the ruins of Kaesora is lost, however most believe that the same cataclysm that struck down the wurm and permanently scoured the surface of the Field of Bone above it laid waste to Kaesora as well. Now only the dead walk these halls.

Dangers

The entrance is notable for both a spectral guardian that often hangs out there, as well as having pit traps as the only way to enter the lower parts of the dungeon. The dungeon itself is divided into essentially two areas, the front is filled with nibblers and spiders of various types, the back, near the Church of Xalgoz, is exclusively filled with undead. The spiders are mostly necromancers, and the dungeon has many small pit traps that can lead you further in as you try to find your way out...

Benefits

Pulling creatures in this dungeon seems safer than in most, since the aggro radius on many monsters is smaller than that found elsewhere. Also, due to the strange pathing that they sometimes take, the puller will rarely get hit on the way back to the group.

Travelling To and From Kaesora

Kaesora can be reached by entering the ruins in Field of Bone and travelling down through an opening into Kaesora itself. The location is approximately -1900, -275.
