Description

The City of Mist was the pinnacle of iksar construction in its time. The magics that suspended the three great halls above the grounds of the main city are lost to time, but are as impressive now as when they were first made.

Dangers

The city is divided up into several areas. The front is the yard where many random creatures appear. Further in is the city itself, with buildings that have multiple levels. The deepest part of the zone is actually the floating towers, which are connected by narrow, broken walkways. Care must be taken travelling between them because there are gaps in the stone that are easy to drop through if you aren't coordinated enough to jump and clear them.

The Reavers will continually spawn new creatures many times after you kill them. At some point, the Lord may spawn out of this.

Benefits

Melee classes are able to be bound here, which is good, because the city is a long run from the nearest bind point in Firiona Vie. It is also a good place to bind when hunting in the surrounding areas.

Much of the loot that appears here drops off of any creature within the zone. This means that you don't need to camp one specific area in order to get specific items, they will appear off of anything.

Travelling To and From City Of Mist

The City of Mist can be reached by entering it from the Emerald Jungle, with the entrance found at 310, -1960.
