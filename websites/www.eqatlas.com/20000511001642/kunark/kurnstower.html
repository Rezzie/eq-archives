Description

The history of Kurn's Tower is, likewise, lost in time. Long the fortress of the dead, recently the burynai have started to take up residence in the tower, using their necromantic magics to control the dead and guard them. The tower itself extends further below the surface of the field than it does above it.

Dangers

At the bottom of the tower lies a very deep pit, at the bottom of which is an exit zone that will take you to the outside of the tower.

Benefits

Unknown.

Travelling To and From Kurn's Tower

Kurn's Tower is the most impressive structure in the Field of Bone, jutting from the ground at the north end of the ancient reservoir.
