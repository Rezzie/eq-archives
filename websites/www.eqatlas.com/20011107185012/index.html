Netscape Printing (again)
Monday, October 22nd

I'll be the first to admit that I haven't the slightest clue why this is doing it, but I figured out how to fix it. First of all, let me reiterate that this is a Netscape problem and not a problem with the site. I imagine that anyone printing as much stuff as people do from this site anywhere else would experience the same problem. When I finally got a page to replicate the problem, no amount of reloading would fix it, regardless of what I did.

The fix: go under the Edit menu to the bottom to Preferences... Under the Advanced tab is a Cache selection. Choose to Clear the Memory Cache and Clear the Disk Cache (Memory alone doesn't work). Then, when you go to print that page, you will see it downloading it in the printing window, and it should print (or at least that fixed it on mine). If anyone finds any refinements to this method, let me know.

This is a Netscape Problem...
Wednesday, October 17th

There are a *lot* of people emailing me about being unable to print maps, getting the error that "there are no pages to print" or something similar. This is a Netscape problem, and has nothing to do with the site. I've confirmed this with some testing by a kind viewer (thanks Rigel!).
