INNOTHULE SWAMP

Location: South of the Desert of Ro

Description

Innothule Swamp is the home of the Trolls. Dank and soggy, it's the perfect place for the Trolls to call home. Located in the very southern edge of Antonica, it lies beyond the Southern Desert of Ro and adjacent to The Feerott, home of the Orges.

Dangers

For young and experienced characters alike, Innothule Swamp can be a deadly place due to it's many aggresive monsters, many of whom group. Add to this the generally low PC population here, it's remarkable easy to find oneself swarmed with little notice.

As stated above, aggresive, grouping monsters can be very hazardous. This is especially true when fighting the Frogloks, who spawn in a wide area surrounding the entance to their home, Guk.  Fighting here can be deadly since almost all Frogloks patrol and they span a large range of levels. Perhaps the best tactic to emply in this area is to grab a Froglok's attention and high-tail it to the zone edge.  Even doing this oftens results in turning around to find 3-4 Frogloks of various varieties attempting to make mince-meat out of you.

Another danger in Innothule is the swamp itself. Unless you are a very good swimmer, you never want to find yourself trapped by deep water. Most of the creatures in the swamp are VERY good swimmers, making fleeing even more hazardous if swimming is required. Generally avoid the deep water and stick to the zone edges.

There are a few human NPCs that patrol near Guk that are aggressive and aren't particularly fond of the "evil" races.  Be alert for these unless you are around 10th level.

Benefits

Innothule is a great area to hunt with its variety of monsters, high creature population, and great treasure.  A troll never has to wander far from home to find adventure. Characters can easily hunt the swamp up to 8th or 9th level, and Guk offers higher level prey and even greater treasure.

Who says Trolls have to be ugly AND poor?  Decaying Skeletons, Frogloks, and Fungus Men all occasionally spawn with rusty weapons and often have several silver to boot. And best of all is the netted and woven armor that the Frogloks carry.  Prized by monks for its magical properties and low weight, this armor can sell for tremendous profit in the human lands. Best of all, you never have to buy armor when adventuring here!

Travelling To and From Innothule Swamp

From the North, Innothule Swamp is reached through the Southern Desert of Ro which lies south of Freeport. This can be a dangerous place, and probably shouldn't be travelled by those less than about 6th or 7th level. To the south is Grobb, home of the Trolls. Be particularly wary of this area if you are of the "good" races.

From the west, Innothule Swamp is entered by coming out of The Feerott, home of the Ogres. This area lies south of Karana and Qeynos.

To the east is Guk, home of the Frogloks. A particularly stunning dungeon with many dangers and rewards...
