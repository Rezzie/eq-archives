Friday, March 9th, 2001 in the land of Norrath

Plane of Hate and The Mines of Nurga

Tonight at sundown (8EST) the senior forces of the Flames will gather at the Soutern Ro portal to enter the plane on Innoruuk. Increase activity in Kiticor forrest and lesser faydark revealed that the dark leader had spies in the taverns we feasted at and overheard our plans as one ale to many had loosened our tongues.

But even with him prepared he cannot stop us from knocking at his house, stomping it down and storming his "home".

Our Darkflame Knights in the middle of the adventuring career are following a lead found on a spy and are heading to the Mines of Nurga. Also it was heard that an abandant ruin in frontier mountains can serve as a temporary home for those that prefer the swords & axes. The Darkflame Knights should establish camp there 1 and a half hours after sundown (9:30EST) and plan the campaign to break into the kobold dominated mines.

So come prepared, it will be a tough battle tonight. We will need good support from our

cleric and enchanter

friends. Sharpen your blades and study your spellbooks, we will need it.

Please contact Matteo, Bikkin or me if you have any questions or want to invite someone.

Thursday, March 1st, 2001 in the land of Norrath

Phinigel Autropos on the Darkflame Knights black list

This Sunday, Phinigel Autropos, will take his last breather. At 2:30pm Eastern the Darkflame Knights will prepare for battle against the master of the underwater lair. Even the four swirlspine guardians that surround him, or the first wave of seahorses he will put in our way will not stop us, he will find his grave in his own rooms.

Prepare for battle, collect enough fish scales to hold the breath long enough, sharpen your weapons and practise your most powerful spells, because we will need everything we have to defeat him. He is a high wizard that brought death to many, many citizens of Norrath, but not any longer.

Salut good Flames, its is time.

Thursday, February 15th, 2001 in the land of Norrath

King Tormax welcomes the first Darkflames

After returning from a several week long compaign against the Dains and an infiltration of the fortress of Dain Frostreaver we were greeted by King Tormax himself.

Now a whole Darkflame Knights delegation is in talks with King Tormax where the next campaign is to be held. Currently the King is asking us to explore an ice cave in Greater Divine. With enough forces we could push in Velektor's lair and collect some Kobold paws for the Giants archive collection. Prior to reaching the kobolds we have to battle through a spider invested labyrinth. Be sure to walk careful on the ice.

The King is asking for an attempt on Saturday but advised us of the following:

o The spiders seems to be about as strong as the King's younger warriors who have trained for 35-45 icy years
o The kobolds match the King's senior guards that have seen anywhere from 48 to 58 years of war against the Dains.

Tormax also said that a younger delegation should be tasked with keeping the spider's at bay and the aged warriors should carefully walk the trapez walk over the ice maze to the kobold lair. Beware though, these guys can hurt.

Thursday, February 8th, 2001 in the land of Norrath

Grand Huntsman Darral and Royal Scribe Kaavin Slain!!!

Yesterday a delegation of the Darkflame Knights enter Icewell Keep on orders from King Tormax personally to hunt down to loyal followers of Dain. With skill and determination we lured first the Huntsman to the tunnels before the city and assassinated him without much challenge.

The Royal Scribe was clearly smarted or more coward and refused to leave the castle. So with magic from our shamans we sneaked into his glass floored room and engaged the Scribe. The battle lasted significantly longer than the huntsman but eventually we also put him in the grave he deserved so long.

Congratulations to Makebleed and Gobbin to the treasures we wrung out of the hand of these arch enemies of our King Tormax.

Boots of the Huntsman: AC: 15 +5 WIS, +5 INT, +5 CR Chain classes (Makebleed)
Book of the Binder:(not sure about its name) WIS+5 INT+5 mana+30 primary/secondary (Gobbin)
Royal Frosted Velium Boots: AC19 sv magic+5 (Papaa)

Sadly both friends of Dain's court lost the heads before we slayed them. But there will be a next time.

Salut good Darkflames!
