This Weekend - Jan 26-28th, 2001

We have an eventful weekend ahead of us:

Friday starts off with helping Dravin with the cleric FA. The target is the plasmatic priest in the temple of solosek ro. The raid party should consist of 2 40-50 groups and the event should not take longer than 1 hour. Gathering is at 8pm EST in lavastorm.

The Forum Thread for the Cleric FA

Saturday will be the Plane of Hate Raid for the 45+. Matteo will be leading the forces up at 6pm est. Gathering should be at lavastorm druid ring also.

Falaniel hurry to get 45 and Dravin, try to get 49! The stones for the hate raid will be around 150pp per group so everyone should contribute around 50pp for two ports up. Not that I expect a wipeout. =p

A very good Plane of Hate Guide

Finally Sunday we have a befallen raid scheduled at 2pm EST. I will bring Tymbor, the powerful lvl 16 chanter that promises not to fizzle too much. I actually have spend some time practizing spell skills. Lilita, the couragous paladin lady, will be leading the main group down. Anyone that has spare keys in the bank, please give me a tell so we don't have to camp the shadowknights for them at all cost. The raid should be mostly for the lvl 10-25 and starts at 2pm est. Mistletoe, the Magi room soloer, will be making pickups from all old world .

Salut good Flames, Senchy

Lower Guk Raid - The DFK Tradition

Last Sunday we all ventured down to lower guk with a mid 20, low 30 excursion party and made lower guk our home. The showing of the DFKs was plentiful and several groups explored the damp dungeon. We returned with two mask of the deceiver and a basalt carpase. Cheer good Knights!

In old fashion we had the traditional raid wipeout. Enjoy the picture =)

The magic of one of the Danger Rangers never fails!
