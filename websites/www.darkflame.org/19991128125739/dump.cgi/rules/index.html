Officers

All officers come up for re-election every 6 months and may be re-proposed if necessary. Dates for re-election are 1st June and 1st December each year. The officer positions are:

Guildmaster

To hold together the guild in trust for its membership.

Mage Officer

To be a focal point for all mage classes, ie Wizard, Magician and Enchanter.

Melee Officer

To be a focal point for the melee classes, ie Warrior, Monk and Rogue.

Hybrid Officer

To be a focal point for the Split classes ie Ranger, Paladin.

Healing Officer

To be a focal point for the Healing and Support Classes, ie Cleric, Shaman and Druid.

Recruitment Officers

2 of these to be a focal point to track and maintain applications to the guild, and monitor results from the recruitment quests.

Quest Officer

To be a focal point for the development of quests both internal to the guild and external.

Internal officer

To assist the GM and to help with the smooth operation of the Guild

Publicity Officer

To coordinate our presence in the outside world with information about what the Darkflame Knights are doing in the lands of Norrath.

Any officer may at any time retire from their post, at which point, the next meeting of the guild will hold an election for replacement of that officer for the remainder of that officers 6 month term.

Guild Disbanding

Just because the GM has the ability to disband the guild he/she may never do so. The Guild is held in trust by that person for the benefit of the members, not for the benefit of the GM. A 75% vote of all member is required before the guild may be disbanded.

Joining the guild

The procedures for becoming a member are as follows:
  • First, we do not accept truly evil persons. No necromancers or shadowknights. Also, we require that potential members be at least the 10th level, so a distinctive personality and playing experience has been developed.
  • The applicant hits 'Join', and fills out the name and email. This will email a password, which the applicant can use to login. Then the applicant will hit modify and fill in all the data. Once done, the applicant will be an official Applicant.
  • At this point, it is the responsibility of ALL members to try to group with the applicant, and the applicant should likewise try to group with members. If a member feels that the applicant has what it takes to become a Darkflame, he/she can 'sponsor' the applicant by selecting the applicant from the member list and hit the big 'sponsor' button. This will make the applicant an apprentice.
    It is a big help in this step if the applicant actively contacts darkflame members and talks to them.
  • Once an apprentice, the apprentice will be considered for full membership on each officer meeting, until by majority vote the apprentice is either accepted or rejected. If neither outcome has majority, the vote will be postponed to the next meeting.

Ranks

All guild members will be ranked from 1st to 5th, with 1st being the lowest, and 5th being the highest.
Advancement through the ranks will be through the completion of quests given by the officer in charge for your branch. In addition, the officer can promote people he/she feels deserve it.
Upon joining, the new member will be at a rank equal to (Level/10), rounded DOWN.

General Behaviour

We are a good guild. We are a strong guild. We are built on justice, wisdom, insight, compassion, patience, charity and close to any other good goal you know of. Act like it. It isn't enough to have the guild title, you have to BE a darkflame knight. Help those in need, and be 'nice'. And if you can't be Mother Theresa, at least make sure you aren't vicious and evil, no matter HOW provoked you are.
Breaches of this basic etiquette will mean instant dismissal from the guild.
