This is a pro-life clique. The objective of this clique is to put together all people who are, in some aspect, anti-abortion. This clique wasn't made to discuss what's right and what's wrong, it was made only to put together people who think alike.

You can join if you are totally against abortion, and you can join too if you are partly against abortion. The important thing is, you must be against abortion in some way. I am open to this because I think it's a big improvement if people think abortion in wrong, even partly. It's a beggining.

Babies do feel pain in a few time after conception, and don't matter how structured clinics can be, the way they're killed is barbaric. They aren't just killed, without pain, what I'd consider to be a better death if it had to be done. They suffer all the pain while they have parts of their little bodies taken away.

I am completely pro-life since the day i watched a video tape in school with images of aborted babies. I got shocked. It seems they have been ground and then thrown in the garbage. First, they have their heads pulled out. Then, the rest comes out looking like a mix of blood and flesh. They don't look like humans anymore. And they have been humans once.

And the fact that those babies can't speak, scream, and can't even be seen by their mothers, doesn't mean this action is not called murder. Something - someone - was alive and was killed by someone else's hands without any chance to protect himself. If this is not murder, i don't know what it is.

Look at a smiling baby sometime and consider that he/she could not have been given a chance to be there smiling. And it would not be his fault. Can't the parents raise the children? Many others can. Don't you want to lose your freedom because you are too young to have a child? Don't punish the child for the fact that you have had sex without any maturity.

Rape cases: people always ask me if I think it's right keeping a baby that will remind you of the rape for the rest of your life. I think that aborting the baby won't make you forget you were raped. Plus, don't forget that the baby is half you too. People shouldn't hate innocent babies because of something that someone else has done.
