The pro-life clique has been running since August 2000, what makes four years this month (August 2004). I am very proud of how popular this clique has become since then, because I always worked very hard to keep the clique running.

Now, I have a job and go to the university. I don't have much time to work in the clique anymore, I have some free time only on Sundays, and I don't want to let it go. That's why I'm working in a more automated page.

That's also why I have bought a domain myself. If you're an old member you must know that the clique has changed address a couple times. It's been hosted in some free webhostings, and it's been hosted by some nice people who kindly offered me some space in their private domains. Unfortunately, the free hosts went down and so did the private domains. So I had to move a lot of times and it was a very hard thing because everyone kept linking to the old places. I had a hard time getting things in track again all times.

So in July 2004, I bought darkflame.org. I plan not to let it go down, so I won't have to move anymore. I have my own database, so I can arrange things the way I need. But to have all that, I have to pay for it, of course.

That's why I'm asking for help. The domain and hosting plans are affordable, but I pay for them with my own money and I don't earn that much. Any donation would be very, very welcome. No, you don't have to pay to become a member, and will never have. That's not what this clique is about. But if you like this clique and wants to help, and if you can help by making a donation, that would really help so much, no matter how much you donate. I will be grateful forever.

I created a paypal account to make the donations easier. But if you want to donate in some other way (mail or something) let me know and we'll see what we can do.

Again, thank you so much for reading this and thank you a lot more if you can help. Let me know that you have made a donation, and I'll send you a little special jpg gift. That's not much, but that's certainly a way to show you how much grateful I am.
