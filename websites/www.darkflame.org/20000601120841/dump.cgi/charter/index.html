We are an honor based role-playing guild in the vast world of Norrath. We hold Honor, Respect and Consideration as our key values. Our goal is helping others and making Norrath more enjoyable for all. We do this through role-play, guild quests, public events and our very presence in Norrath. Honor based means actions speak louder than words or beliefs. We do not rule out evil races or classes, but everyone’s dedication to the roles and aims of the guild should be evident prior to their acceptance. Most people will have a much longer initiation or trial period with us before full membership than with other guilds. This is to ensure that all new members are dedicated to the values the guild holds dear. As a role-playing guild, we are dedicated to developing an on-going storyline to tell the tale of the great warriors and adventures of the Darkflame Knights!
