CASTERS REALM DONATIONS

The Casters Realm Network was originally founded to provide information for gamers which would help to make their gaming lives easier, so they could spend more time doing what they enjoyed - playing their games.  We then took things a step further and provided daily news, discussion forums, strategies and more.  We have even begun to take the next step in providing this information for the next generation of massively multiplayer games which will be released in the future.

Casters Realm was not originally founded to make money.  But at some point the amount of time needed to run the site, plus the amount of money it costs us to host the servers and pay for bandwidth meant that we had to approach the situation as a full time job.  The reality is it costs us a truckload to keep the site up and running and at a speed you all find acceptable.

I am sure that many people will be aware that the internet industry is currently suffering hard due to a severe drop in the number of companies who purchase banner advertising.  What this means for us is that we can no longer meet our running costs with our banner advertising revenue.  This means that we can either slowly shutdown the sites over the next few months, or we obtain some assistance from our viewers.

To immediately dispel one concern you may have, the Casters Realm Network currently have no plans to make the site subscription based.

On the other hand, we are asking for your help.  There are a few ways you can do this.

Voluntary Donations

This is probably the most effective way to help the site stay running.  It's totally up to you how much you feel you gained from our site to contribute.  We currently have two systems for this:

1.  Make a payment to donate@castersrealm.com through the PayPal system.  One advantage to this is if you sign up a new account and state us as your referral, PayPal will automatically donate $5 to us.  Be sure to list donate@castersrealm.com as your referrer.

2.  Send a cheque to the following name and address.  The only problem is that cashing these cheques in Australia costs $5 per cheque, so this isn't a valid option for people who wish to donate less than $5:

Online Evolution
PO Box 839,
Mudgeeraba, QLD
Australia.   4213

3.  Western Union or Direct Deposit information is available upon request by emailing baelish@castersrealm.com.

DVD, Video, Game Purchases

Another way you can help is to purchase your games, videos, books, etc through our online shop.  Amazon and EBWorld give us a small referral fee each time you buy something through them off our store.  This way you still get something you were going to buy, but help Casters Realm stay online at the same time.

With the Shadows of Luclin expansion coming up soon, we really hope some of you will consider buying it through us.

You can access our shop by the link underneath our banners, or at http://shop.castersrealm.com.

Other Forms of Assistance

Of course, we can still offer advertising.  If you have a game or product you wish to promote, or have any questions in general, please get in contact with Greg Short at baelish@castersrealm.com.

Thankyou all in advance for any support you can give us.
