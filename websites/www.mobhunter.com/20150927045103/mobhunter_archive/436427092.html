So why did Verant change the EQ Web site? Absor elaborates:

Those of you that post to and read the Developer's Corner are probably familiar with the old EverQuest.com. That site basically caters to the experienced player, it's where we talk about new features, gameplay and stuff like that. A lot of that information isn't really useful to a potential new customer. When I want to learn about a game, I want to see screenshots, I want to get a feel for it. That's what we're trying to achieve with EverQuest.com. I've read comments on the net suggesting that it is just a marketing site now. That's almost true. We certainly want it to be appealing to potential players without blasting them with complicated concepts like melee balance changes when they just want to see if they are interested in the game at all. We do indeed want to sell the game to potential players through EverQuest.com.

So we renamed the old EverQuest.com site, it is now EverQuest Live. For those of you that are experienced EverQuest players, EverQuest Live is probably the site you want to bookmark (www.everquestlive.com).

Truthfully, I don't know anyone who would admit to being a big fan of the old EQ site anyway. You don't look very 733t if you have Harpy's Head Tavern set as your home page. Still, it's hip to make fun of Fippy's Facts, so don't feel too guilty checking out the above link.
