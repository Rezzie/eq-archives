Accidentally hitting "a" and attacking NPCs:

It's been a while since I've mentioned this, so it seemed like it's worth repeating. If you have a tendency to accidentally attack NPCs by hitting the 'a' key, then it's probably a really good idea to remap the attack key to a less commonly used key.
