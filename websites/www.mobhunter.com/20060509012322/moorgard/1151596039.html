Absor keeps posting player responses to his questions about what inspired you to make the character choice you did. Frankly, a lot of this thread gets a bit thick for my tastes, though I know there's a lot of folks out there who enjoy this aspect of the game--and more power to them.

The questions, if you don't want to wade through the lengthy responses, are:

1) What process do you go through to come up with character backgrounds or to decide what your next character will be?

2) What inspires your character to behave the way he or she does? In dramatic terms, what is the character's motivation?

The correct answers, of course, are 1) Look for phat lewtz, and 2) Getting phat lewtz.

In more interesting news, Slow News Day (a.k.a. Once Upon a Lum) has an interview of sorts with several key figures in the MMORPG market, asking about the future of the industry. The question of the day is whether we will see "adult" online games with sexual content and graphic violence. Responses from folks like Richard Garriot, Lum, John Smedley, and Mark Jacobs--among others--seem to agree that such games are invevitable.
