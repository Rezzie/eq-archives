Well, It's up... 
Posted by Mapfiend on Dec 16, 2000, 04:13pm
The new site looks a lot different than the old one, eh? I am changing the way everything works. Instead of new files overwriting old files (Even if the old one was better), I am keeping all files that get uploaded. Maps will be sorted by zone, and when you view a zone list, all maps that pertain to that zone will be listed. That means that people can upload maps that are incomplete, but may have extra details that aren't on the "current" map, without overwriting the "current" map. This also means some changes in the gzip archiving system. Gzipping ALL the maps is no longer a possibility because many of the maps will share the same name (zone.map). Right now, I have 2 options for gzipping maps: the first grabs the largest file for each zone and gzips them. The other option grabs the most recently uploaded map for each zone and gzips them. Both options are available through the archive link on the menu. I am thinking of adding a shopping-cart type of archive system that will allow you to browse the maps and add them to your "cart" then gzip them when you are done. We will see. Still to do:
