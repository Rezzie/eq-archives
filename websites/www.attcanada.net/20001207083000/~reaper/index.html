November 13th, 2000

In light of the near release of the Scars of Velious, I have added the new songs us bards get in that expansion to the Songs page, they are in a new table that comes after the existing one, so scroll down to see them.  So what are you waiting for?  Go check em out!

October 17th, 2000

A new instrument has surfaced from the plane of air, Denon's Horn of Dissonance not only adds to stats and brass songs, but you can hold it in your offhand and weild an instrument while playing!!  I have added the stats of this bad boy to the Music Room.
I have added the Solusek Ro message board to the Links page.

The epic bard weapon quest has been completed!  The result is the Singing Short Sword:
