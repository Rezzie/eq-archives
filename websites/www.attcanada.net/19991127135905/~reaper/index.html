November 11th, 1999

First off, happy Rememberance Day to all you who know of it.
Looking at this main page lately, I noticed I haven't been giving you guys as many updates as you deserve.  Also, there has been some talk about lack of updates on the message boards.  I was recently contacted by a bard, someone who some of you may know, his name is Rtheb.  He suggested that I hand the site over to him, and was very excited about it.  Not only does he have more time to work on the site, but he actually plays a bard in EQ still, so he would probably be better suited to be your webmaster.   We are discussing transferring ownership to him, which would only involve a new web adress for you guys to bookmark, so it shouldn't be a problem.  I just want the best for you guys, and I think this is it. 

October 28th, 1999

First off, I'd like to apologize for the lack of updates these past few weeks, I know alot has happened and I haven't jumped on it right away like you guys are used to.   Between school and work lately, I've had little time for any leisure :(
Needless to say, this is going to be a HUGE update, so here goes....
I have changed a few minor things in The Armory, the Skills page, the Songs page, and added a few links.
K.C. sent me an email about his new site, K.C.'s Guide to Becoming a Master Smith, I think we should all check it out, even has some info about banded gauntlets!
I have added the stats for ALL pieces of Imbrued Platemail in The Armory.
I have also changed the Imbrued Platemail page, in the Equipment page, to be the Imbrued Platemail/Lambent Armor page, it's been updated with several new pics.  If any of you want details on how to get Lambent Armor, I won't post a spoiler on my site, but there are plenty of sites out there that have the quest, so ask around and you will find it no problem.
The Test Server has been updated with the following changes:
Charm spells now message the message when they break (Enchanter, Bard, Necromancer, Druid)
Some in-game Bardic instruments have had their stats increased to appropriate levels. Items effectedare the Minotaur horn, Small Brass Trumpet, and the Drum.
Some non-magic instruments have been made magic. These are the Mistmoore Battle Drums, the Lute of the Gypsy Princess and Kelin's Practice Lute.
Bards with magic gloves and magic instruments may now punch creatures that require magic to damage.
I was sent an email the other day from The Raging Fist Guild on the Quellious server.  They are looking for some bards, level 20 and under, if you are interested, head on over to their site and check it out.
Also, in other bard news, this Sunday, October 31st, there will be a Bardic Wedding on the Xegony server.

On Sunday, October 31st at 2 . 30 pm Central Time, Tasher Carbonara (lv 33 bard) and Tawney Treewind (lv 30 bard) will be wed in East Karana , along the path leading to High Pass , loc - 1862 , - 6030 . After the ceremony , we will proceed to lure some Cyclops on the valley down the path to have some fun. Free Food and Beer . The Oligarchia Guild cordially invites all to attend. Gifts are welcomed but not required. Hope to see you there!
