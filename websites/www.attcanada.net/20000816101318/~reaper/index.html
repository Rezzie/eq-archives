The Soerbaird Present a Tribute to Erollisi Marr

To all of those who have fallen in, been hurt by, wish for, or have any remote connection to love:

The Soerbaird will be presenting an evening of songs inspired by the antics of Erollisi Marr. Listen to ballads extolling the virtues of true love, or of those funny things we do for that special someone. Come listen to melodies inspired by moments of passion, or commiserate about the pain of love gone wrong.

This is the first in a series of concerts dedicated to a specific Norrathian diety.

What: The Soerbaird Tribute to Erollisi Marr

When: Thursday, July 13 at 7:00 PM PST

Where: Isle of Erollisi (where else?), Fennin Ro
