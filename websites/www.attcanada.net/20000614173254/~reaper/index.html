Greetings All!

On wednesday the 17th, the Miraculous Musicians of the Innoruuk
server is proud to present the 1st annual "Late show with
Ghent Swiftnote!"  This will include jokes, songs, and a
couple interviews as well!  The show starts at 8pm central (9 eastern, 6 pacific)
and will be held in Kelethin.  In order to get to the site of the show,
take the orc ramp up and make a direct left on to the first bridge. 
After crossing that, make a direct right  across the next bridge.
And on your right from that is a ramp leading upward.  The show will
be held there or in the building on that platform.  Drinks and snacks
will be provided along with free instruments to young bards in the
making.  There will also may be a party afterward with a variety of
things to do, possibly even a small raffle.  I hope that you will all come and enjoy the show and
get away from that endless spree of monster killing!

See you there!
