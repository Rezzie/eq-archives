Skills
Disciplines

Just a note about the skill caps, some of these caps are unproven, but generally the combat and singing skills are 200 and the thief skills are 40.  Also, Wood Elves start with 50 hiding and 50 foraging, so don't bother training with them.

NOTE: the offense and defense etc no longer have a cap of 200, I will post the new caps as soon as I find them out.

Bard Skills

Skill Name Level Attained Cap Description
1h Blunt 1 225 Weapon skill using one handed bashing weapons.   (i.e. Club)
1h Slashing 1 225 Weapon skill using one handed slashing weapons. (i.e. Short Sword)
2h Blunt 1 Cannot Raise Weapon skill using two handed bashing weapons.   (Bards can't raise their 2h skills)
2h Slashing 1 Cannot Raise Weapon skill using two handed bashing weapons. (Bards can't raise their 2h skills)
Alcohol Tolerance 1 200 How much alcohol you can drink without feeling the effects of it.  (blurred vision etc...)
Baking 1 200 A trade skill used to bake food.
Beg 1 200 Lets you beg for money or items from NPCs, can get you killed, also rumored at high levels to yield special items.
Bind Wound 1 200 Lets you use bandages to help quicken the healing rate of people with less than 50% hp
Brewing 1 200 A trade skill used for making alcoholic beverages, and consequently, lots of friends.  New recipes have been added to get your skill all the way to 200 now.
Defense 1 215 An overall rating of your defensive skill, adds to AC.
Fishing 1 200 How well you fish, considered to be a trade skill.
Fletching 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning bow and arrow components into bows and arrows.
Hand to Hand 1 200 Weapon skill using no weapon, also used when holding an instrument, useful for bards who use instruments in battle.
Jewel Craft 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning gems and precious metals into jewelry, sometimes even magic jewelery.
Offense 1 215 An overall rating of your offensive skill, adds to ATK.
Piercing 1 210 Weapon skill using piercing weapons.   (i.e. Dagger)
Pottery 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pots (amongst other things) out of clay.
Sense Heading 1 200 Lets you sense the direction you are facing, helps you navigate.
Singing 1 235 How well you sing, no need for an instrument, it affects songs that require singing.
Smithing 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning ore into armor and other metal items.
Swimming 1 200 How well you swim, the higher your skill, the faster you swim.
Tailoring 1 200 A trade skill used for fashioning pelts into types of leather armor.
Throwing 1 113 Weapon skill using throwing weapons. (i.e. Throwing Axe)
Percussion 5 235 How well you play percussion instruments (hand drum), it affects songs that require percussion instruments.
String 8 235 How well you play stringed instruments (mandolin, lute), it affects songs that require stringed instruments.
Dodge 10 155 This skill automatically is check and if successful, you dodge an enemies attack.
Meditate 10 1 This was put in to help bards memorize songs faster.
Brass 11 235 How well you play brass instruments (horn), it affects songs that require brass instruments.
Forage 12 55 A useful skill that lets you scrounge up food or water, lets you spend more time away from cities.
Wind 14 235 How well you play wind instruments (flute), it affects songs that require wind instruments.
Dual Wield 17 225 A great skill, lets you use two weapons at once, and get two attacks per round.
Sneak 17 75 Lets you walk slowly and quietly so that enemies don't detect you.
Sense Traps 20 75 Notifies you if any traps are in your vicinity.
Safe Fall 24 40 Lets you fall from heights that would normally hurt you without taking any damage.
Hide 25 40 Lets you hide to avoid being attacked or seen.  You cannot move while hidden, or hide while moving.
Instill Doubt 26 100 Causes your target to turn and walk away.   Can only be trained with Caskin Marsheart, a bard guildmaster on the second floor of the Freeport bard guild.  You can find it in the list of skills just above Common Tongue.
Disarm Traps 30 100 Lets you disarm traps near you, at current, there are only a couple of traps in the entire world.
Track 35 100 A GREAT skill, lets you locate creatures or people who are near you.
Pick Locks 40 100 Lets you pick locks without needing a key, apparently it is rather disappointing as some locks still need keys.
Parry 53 75 An ac buffer skill, lets you block a melee attack with your weapon.
Riposte 58 75 Another ac buffer skill, this one allows you to evade an attack and get a free attack on your opponent.

Bard Disciplines

Deftdance 55 Auto dodge and dual-wield for 15 seconds, reuse time of 72 minutes.
Puretone 60 Adds bonuses to your songs as if you were wielding the best instrument, whether or not you are actually wielding an instrument.   Lasts for 4 minutes, reuse time of 72 minutes.
Resistant 51 Adds 3 to all resistances.  Lasts for 5 minute, reuse time of 1 hour.
Fearless 54 Makes you immune to all fear spells for 11 seconds, reuse time of 1 hour.
