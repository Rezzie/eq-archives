April 9th, 2000

There have been some new songs found in recent datafiles, looks like Kunark may be bringing about the completion of the bards songs (still missing #49)!  I have added the level 39 and 45 songs to the Songs page.

April 6th, 2000

I received an email from a bard named Muffin Se'leon, asking me to post this info, it should be interesting to see how it comes out:

Hi!

My name is Muffin Se'Leon and my new all bard guild, Miraculous Musicians, has just been accepted For the Innoruuk server!  I am currently looking for members who have a desire to roleplay the bard and overall have a great time!  This includes writing funny songs and rhymes, and learning the tricks of the trade that makes the bard all worth while!  We will hold various events such as concerts and talent shows (ive done one before, it was great!  Gm was there =P) as well as fun lil dungeon raids and the like.  Overall it is a guild that promotes the true ways of a bard and has lots of fun doing it.  All those who want to join or are interested, please mail me at k_muffins@hotmail.com and I can send you a charter =).  Thanks!

Muffin Se'Leon
Guild Mommy/Composer
Miraculous Musicians 

March 31st, 2000

Just a quick update, the bards forage skill cap has been increased (unannounced of course =P) in the latest patch, its now 55, not 40.  I have changed that in the Skills page.

March 25th, 2000

Well, the info for the expansion songs for bards has been posted, and WOW!!!!!!!!!!!!   These are are the songs we have been waiting for, head on over to the Songs page and check em out!  Just note, they are taken from datafiles, and are in beta form, so they will possibly change before the release of the expansion.
