For locations, if Freeport, Kelethin or Qeynos are listed, the songs can be found in their Bardic guilds.  If Erudin is listed, there is a merchant named Altrusan Belstince on the first floor of the library in Erudin.  If Highkeep is listed, you may find the song on a merchant on the first floor of Highkeep named Justin Rhymes, right next to the merchant that sells the Enchanter pet spells.  Highkeep is the town in Highhold for anyone who doesn't know.  If Firiona Vie is listed, you can find the songs on an npc named Balik Wolftrot, he is in the High Elven Outpost.

In the "Instrument Skill" column, if the skill is in red text and italicized, then the song will only play with an instrument of that type, if you do not have one equipped, you cannot play the song.

For more in depth song information, head on over to Rokenn Swiftsong's EQ Bard Song Analysis page.
