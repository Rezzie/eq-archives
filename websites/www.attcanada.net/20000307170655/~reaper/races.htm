Bards can choose from the following three races:

Half Elf

Size:    Medium
Advantage:    Infravision
Disadvantage:    None
Starting Cities:    Kelethin, Freeport, Qeynos
Comments:    Being able to start in any city with a bard guild is nice, it makes Half Elves more versatile.  They also have the highest total primary stats for bards, but that doesn't matter too much.

Human

Size:    Medium
Advantage:    Gains levels faster
Disadvantages:   No night vision, Only knows Common Tongue
Starting Cities:    Freeport, Qeynos
Comments:    Knowing only Common is not a problem, it is easy to learn new languages.  Gaining levels faster is always nice.  I personally can't stand not having night vision but if you can handle it, Humans are a good choice.

Wood Elf

Size:    Small or Medium
Advantages:    Infravision, Hide and Forage
Disadvantage:    None
Starting City:    Kelethin
Comments:    Wood Elves are my personal choice, mainly because they can wear small and medium armor which makes finding armor easier.  Not to mention that small armor is lighter and costs less, also there is some magic armor that only fits small characters (ie Dwarven ringmail).  Hide and Forage aren't a big advantage because all bards get them eventually.

Here is the stat breakdown of the three races.   Bear in mind that minor changes in stats don't affect gameplay too much, just pick the race you like best.  Bards primary stats are STR, DEX and CHA.

Race STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA Bonus Points
Half Elf 75 70 90 95 60 75 85 +25
Human 80 75 75 85 75 75 85 +25
Wood Elf 70 65 95 90 80 75 85 +25
