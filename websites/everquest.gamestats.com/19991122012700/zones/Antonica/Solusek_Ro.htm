The Temple of Solusek Ro
Level Range:    No monsters, just NPC's
Typical Creatures:  n/a
NPC's and Quests:   Solusek quest list
Description:   This temple to the god Solusek Ro is a small zone located off of lavastorm.  It is also the home of several high level quests for various classes, many of which result in very nice items and armor.  WARNING do not hail the effrete, as it will ruin your faction with the rest of the inhabitants and could cause you to suddenly become KOS.  And he has nothing to say.
