Nagafen's Lair
Level Range:      Above 33
Typical Creatures:  Greater Kobalds
NPC's and Quests:
Description: High Level Dungeon.  Probably the hardest dungeon in the game, short of the outer planes.   Nagafen is a very high level dragon, and needs several groups working in conjunction to kill it.
