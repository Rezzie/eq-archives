The Plane of Hate
Level Range:     46 - 50  (you cannot get into the Plane unless you are at least level 46)
Typical Creatures:  The God Innoruk.  Other vile minions.
NPC's and Quests:
Description:  This is a very high level zone that is the home of the God Innoruk.  It should only be hunted with several groups of level 46 to 50 characters.   Even then, go expecting to die.  This zone is very dangerous for even the most organized parties.  It currently can only be reached through a wizard gate spell.  Rumor also has it that you may someday be able to reach the plane through the spectre tower in the Middle of the Oasis of Marr.
