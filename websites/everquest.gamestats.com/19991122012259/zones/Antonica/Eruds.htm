Erud's Crossing
Level Range:    Above level 9
Typical Creatures:  Kerrans, Zombie sailors (usually in 3's), Spiders, Wisps, Kerran Beetles, Kerran snakes, Vampire Bats , Island Madmen (some with magic spells), Occaisional fire or earth elemental
NPC's and Quests: Blurred Map; Kerran Beetle Eyes; Zombie Flesh;
Description:   Mostly an open sea travelled by the boat between Erudin and Qeynos.  There is one island with a small Kerran village, including a shop where you can get reasonable rates to save travelling (dont kill the kerran beetles if you want to use the shop and not be KOS by Kerrans).  This is sometimes referred to as wisp island due to the lage number of wisps that populate the island.  Beware of the Zombie sailors at the shipwreck, who usually come in 3's but occaisionaly a whole hord swamps the island.  These can be a good source of rawhide armour and bronze weapons on rare occaisions.  Also, know that if you hit a spider, often every spider on the Island will team you.  Very strong poison. Watch out for the Island Madmen, especially the Erudite one by the docks.
