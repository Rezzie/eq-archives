Grobb

Bone Chips:  The shadow knight guildmaster wants you to collect bone chips for him.  Get four chips,and return them for experience, faction and an item (usually a rusty 2 handed sword).  Your faction for SKN and DarkOnes goes up, factions FrogloksOfGuk, BrokenSkullClan and DaBashers(?) go down

Fire Beetle Eyes:  In Grobb, talk to Basher Nindan or something similar. His name doesn't matter, he doesn't move around. Right before the tunnel to Innothule Swamp is Basher Razbaz. Go past him, away from the tunnel and Priest of Discord, and there is a small river. Swim along it, and on the right will be a cage with a Froglok prisoner and a Basher or two. Follow this area's tunnel to the back, where the correct Basher is. If hailed, he tells you to bring him 3 Fire Beetle eyes, and he will give you "shinies".  You get a minor gem or jewel for returning the items, and occasionally a minor magic item (such as a Brass Earring).  You also get a positive faction hit with Da Bashers and a negative faction hit with the Broken Skull Clan. (Submitted by Slekk)

Froglock Feeding:  Ranjor, the head of the Bashers is hungry.  If you bring him 1 Froglock Meat & 2 Froglock Flesh to eat, he will give you a little experience & a rusty 2h battleaxe.  You also get positive faction with Da Bashers and negative faction with the Broken Skull Clan.   You can do it as much as you want. He must be really hungry.  (Submitted by KingOgg)

Froglock Meat:  The shaman guildmaster, Kalagri (not sure of name), wants you to collect 2 frogolok tad fleshes and 2 bone chips. She will then give you a lvl 1 shaman spell, Burst of Flame, some experience and faction.

Fungus Dung Pie:  Hergror is hungry, and he wants a Fungus Dung Pie (yech). He says to go to tell Carver Cagrek "Hergror wants his Fungus Dung Pie".  He is the troll on the dias in front of the Shaman's Guild near the bank.  When you talk to Cagrek, he complains that Hergror is always hungry and that he needs 4 Fungus Spores to make a pie, but not to hit them too hard or you ruin them.  The spores are dropped on a very rare occasion  by fungus spores in Innothule.  If you complete this and give Hergror the pie, he will award you with some patchwork, leather or raw-hide armor.  (Submitted by Itchyfish)

Greenblood Shadowknight Tunic:  One of Hukulks chief lieutenants in Nightkeep wants you to prove your worth by returning a Black Shadow Tunic from the Greenblood Shadowknights.   In fact, he really wants three such tunics.  He says he will open the coffers of nightkeep and pay you a reward.  This is the shadowkinght guild in Ogguk.  You can get the tunic through a quest in Ogguk.  You will get xp, some gold, and a minor item.  (Submitted by Ghorthorr and Sumdar)

HEHE meat:  This is the quest to get the Grobb Cleaver.  It starts in Grobb (of course).  Talk to Carver, by the Shaman's guild, and he will tell you he wants 3 pieces of a meat called "HEHE meat" and the recipe to make it.  You can purchase the meat in Oggok from Dooga the Ogre Chef.  (He has a seperate quest to deliver froglock legs).   The recipe is held by the Gobbler in Neriak.  If you talk to the Gobbler about the recipe, he will send you to deliver a Grub Locker of Dwarf Pickles to his Troll friend in the Ocean of Tears, Nerbilik.  You need to make the delivery.  Nerbilik is fishing on the Siren rocks.  Be careful, since I have been told the guards may kill you even though they con indifferent.  Give him the locker and you will receive approximately 1 plat, increased faction with DaBashers and decreased faction with BrokenSkull Clan, and some xp.  However, he will refuse to give you the recipe he has, so you need to kill him and loot it from his corpse.  This seriously decreases your troll faction.  Then take the recipe and 3 HEHE meats back to Carver and he will give you a Grobb Cleaver for you efforts.  (Submitted by Ashrum)

Hulkulk's Love:  Be forewarned that this quest will lower your faction with the dark bargainers.  In the Night Keep, talk to Hukulk.  He mentions [Helm of Hukulk], and upon asking him about the helm, he refers to a Lynuga who broke his heart.  Hukulk asks that you go to Neriak to see who Lynuga meets with, possibly kill them, and return the head of her lover and the Bracers of Love that he gave to her.  In return, Hukulk will give you an extra Helm of Hukulk.  I am told you need to give him both the bracers and the head (presumably of Lumpkin) to get the helm.  Lynuga can be occasionally found floating in the water near the entrance to Guk. She won't mention anything about the [Happy Love Bracers] but if you  ask her about them, she will tell you she gave the ugly things to her boyfriend, Guard Lumpkin, in Neriak and that the relationship was over.  Lumpkin will tell you that Ratraz now has the bracers.  Ratraz is a bouncer in the Maiden's Fury in Neriak.  You have to kill Ratraz. If he runs while your fighting him, and you're a troll or ogre, be aware that you may have to duck while chasing him through some of the lower arches in Neriak, so you will have to reclick your autoattack. I haven't had anyone jump in on that battle yet and I've let him run pretty far before. From him you will get the happy love bracers (AC 1 bracers, I'd use them just for the name alone if they weren't so lousy), his head, and a warclub. Watch out!  You do NOT want to give his head to Hululk! Lumpkin was the one who stole Lyguna from Hulkulk, not Ratraz! Either put his head in a seperate container or destroy it.  The warclub is a decent non-magic blunt weapon that you can sell for quite a few plat. Ratraz is about 19th level.   Now you need to get Lumpkin's head. This is more of a trick. He is found in the Foreign Quarter of Neriak. He runs around a couple of the back rooms.  Attack him when he is as deep in these rooms as he gets! He is also about 19th and tries to run when hurt enough. If he gets far enough a high level shadow knight jumps in and you're basically doomed.  From him you get his head and a rusty halberd.  Take his head and the Happy Love Bracer's back to Hululk to get your prize, the Helm of Hukulk AC5 +3 strength +3 agility, wearable by evil races only helmet and some money (even up to some plat!) , experience, and Faction.  Note about factions. If your standing is too low with shadowknights (or perhaps if you are not a troll shadowknight), you will get nothing for turning in the quest items to Hulkulk. (Submitted by Igjarjuk and ).

Oven Mittens:  Carver Cagrek by the Shaman guild (he's the guy standing by the partial corpse in the middle) will reward you with a nice pair of Grobb Oven Mittens if you bring him 3 froglock meat and 10 gold.  (Submitted by Elayna)

Skeletons in the closet: If you go into the Shaman's Guild and talk to the Shaman upstairs near the throne (I forget his name), he talks about the head Shaman, Kallie (?). He says she hates him because he let some skeletons of hers out of the closet and now they're running around Grobb causing trouble. (funny) Apparently there are several skeletons running around the city of Grobb. You are supposed to find all of them and ask them "Come Back to the Closet" and see what they say. There are four skeletons you need to get.  The three that appear in town only appear one at a time, so you have to do them in order.  (1) After talking to Urako in the Shaman's guild, the first skeleton appears by the barkeep.  Hail him then say "come back to the closet."  He will complain that it is cramped and he just got out but will go back if he had a pair of Oven Mitts.  These are acquired by giving Cleaver Cagrek 3 froglok meat and 10gp.  Give him the mitts and he then becomes a skull item (lore, no drop) in your inventory called "The Baker."    (2)  Once the gloves are given to the 1st skel, he will say something about Basher Nanrum knowing where "The Butcher" is and to ask him [wheres the skeleton].  When you ask Basher Nanrum [wheres the skeleton], he tells you that the Butcher skeleton is right by Cleaver Cagrek (which he is).  "The Butcher" will say he wants an Ogre Butcher Apron to go back to the closet.  To get the Butcher Apron, you have to speak to Chef Dooga in Oggok, and give her 3 pickled froglocks and 10 gold.  The problem for trolls is that Chef Dooga is KOS to trolls.   To get around this, create a newbie ogre to get the apron and give it to your Troll or get an Ogre friend to get it for you.  Give it to the skeleton and he will become a skull item called "the butcher".  (3) The next skeleton is The Captain.   He only appears at night, doesn't always appear, and is very hard to find (loc  - 1617 , 773).  He wants a ship in a bottle.  You can buy this either on an island in the middle of the Innothule Swamp or from the bartender who stands next to Chef Dooga.  The Ship in a Bottle is no drop, so you have to get it yourself.   If you try to get it from the bartender, you will need a guise of the deceiver mask or an illusion spell of some sort.  (4)  The final skeleton is The Minstrel.  He wants four lizardman scout fifes, which are dropped by lizardman scouts.  When you turn in all four skulls, you get a Kaglari Mana doll , which is probably not worth all of the stuff you gave up to get it.  (Submitted by Itchyfish, Grobbie and Eolith).

Spore Guardian:  This quest is also given by Hulkulk's assistant in the Shadowknight Guild.  You have to get the head of the Spore Guardian.  The Spore Guardian spawns in the Innothule swamp, and it's corpse sometimes has a "Huge Mushroom Head".  Giving the mushroom head to the the correct troll in the SK guild hall will get you a Froglok Skin Mask.  (Submitted by Sluglug)
