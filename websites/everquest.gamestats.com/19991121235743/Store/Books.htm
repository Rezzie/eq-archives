Why the book selections?  Well, I've been reading Fantasy and Science Fiction novels for 30 years, and I figure that visitors to my site, who are obviously into the fantasy world of Everquest, might be into reading as well.  Thus, in conjunction with amazon.com, I've put together a list of books which I have read over the years (quite a few) and which I would highly recommend to anyone interested in this genre.  The reviews are strictly my own opinion, and I have only included books which I personally have read and feel are top quality books.  I have also added a section of books which I haven't read, but which are recommended by various site visitors.  Those books which I believe to be particularly outstanding are marked with the cool icon.  You can get more information by following the link to amazon.com, where you can also purchase the book.  You can also use the search feature below to find and purchase anything else from amazon.com.  For comments and discussions of these selections and reviews or suggestions for new selections, go to AllaKhazam's Fantasy and Science Fiction Discussion Forum here or through the link to the left (if in the EQ frames)
