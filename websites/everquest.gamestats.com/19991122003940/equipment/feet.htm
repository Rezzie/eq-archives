If you have any additions or corrections to this list, please e-mail me at  Allakhazam@gamestats.com

All Armor items come in the following sizes:
Small - worn by Wood Elf, High Elf, Dark Elf, Dwarf, Halfling, and Gnome
Medium - worn by Human, Barbarian, Erudite, Wood Elf, High Elf, Dark Elf, and Half Elf
Large - worn by Barbarian, Ogre, and Troll
