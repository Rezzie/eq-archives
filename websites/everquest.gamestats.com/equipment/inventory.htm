Inventory Slot Items

Note that many Inventory Slot Items can be kept in the left hand.  Most will not function if kept within a container such as a backpack.

(In alphabetical Order)

  • Archeologist Pack:  6 slot item that lowers the weight of items carried within it by 25%.  Lore item.  Found on the Goblin Archeologist in Permafrost.  (Submitted by Frizzan Z`ress)

  • Bag of the Tinkerer's:  10 slot bag which carries 10 medium sized items and eliminates their weight.  Purchase from gnomes in Solusek's Eye.  (Submitted by Yeti)

  • Bottle of Drake Breath:  Heals when drank.  (Submitted by Hathor)
