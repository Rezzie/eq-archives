Toxxulia Forest

Darla Starfire:  Talk to the female guildmaster in the necro hut.  She wants you to kill Darla Starfire, a level 5ish NPC who is supplying the hated Kerrans.  You will be rewarded for turning in her head.  (Submitted by Doriath)

Heretic battle:  Markus Jaevins, the Magician Guildmaster, will ask you to get the heretic's remains from Jailmaster Lius.  Lius will tell you that the heretic escaped.  the heritics are the various Necromancers wandering around Tox Forest.  If you bring the remains of any heretic to Markus he will reward you.  Kill one of them and return his remains for experience, faction and a reward.  Your faction raises with High Counsel of Erudin and High Guard of Erudin, and lowers with Heritics.  I don't know if the reward is greater if you kill Talliger, who is the most powerful heretic.  (Submitted by Aoiryuu and Fellus)

Kobald Shaman Paws:  This quest starts in Erudin.  The Cleric Guildmaster apparantly has a feud with the Kobald Shamen.  Some sort of religious dispute I guess.  She is a bloodthirsty sort and wants you to return with the paws of her enemies.  Go kill Kobald Shamen in Tox forest and return with two paws and you will be rewarded.

Mining caps:  The NPC necro Talriger Eklorin, who is walking around in the forest on the other side of the bridge, wants to employ a young necromancer to retrieve some mining caps for his skeleton workers in the mine. Apparantly, these skeletons will not continue to  work without the caps.  The caps are sometimes dropped by kobald scouts (though not often).  Providing these caps will result in experience, faction, some nice money and an item.  He only gives the quest to those he likes (and doesn't KOS). (Submitted by Allart and Zooky)

Poacher Heads:  This quest starts in Erudin.  Those nasty poachers wandering around Toxxulia haven't just annoyed you.  They've apparantly pissed off one of the townspeople who stands just outside the Temple of Quellios.  His request is a little gruesome, but hey it's a barbaric world out there.  Bring him head of a poacher and he will reward you with a little money, experience and faction.  The faction adjustments are as follows:  KaranaBandits worse;  KaranaResidents better;  GuardsofQeynos better;  HighpassGuards better;  CircleofUnseenHands better.

Rungupp:  This annoying troll runs around the forest screaming challenges to the Erudites.  He can generally be located somewhere along the shore from the bridge to the mountainside.  He is fairly tough to kill since he casts blindness on his adversaries.  Take his head to Marzius Denison at the necromancer's hut and you will get some money and an item.

Skeleton Killing:  This quest starts in Erudin. talk to one of the clerics in the Tample of Quellios and you will be given a box to use to battle the undead.  Fill it with bone chips and combine it to get a box of bones.  When you return it to the cleric you will get a random item, some money and some faction.  You may also get an additional quest.
