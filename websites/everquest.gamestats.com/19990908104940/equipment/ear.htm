Ear Slot Items

Class Code:  B = Bard;  C = Cleric;  D = Druid;  E = Enchanter;  Mg = Magician;  Mk = Monk;  N = Necromancer;  P = Paladin;  Ra = Ranger;  Ro = Rogue;  SK = Shadowknight;  Sm = Shaman;    Wr = Warrior;  Wz = Wizard

(Arranged Alphabetically)

  • Alligator Tooth Earring:  All Classes.  +5 HP.  Found on an injured rat in the Qeynos sewers (Submitted by Terl)
  • Batskull Earring:  All Classes.  +5 to intelligence.  Found on Chief Goonda in ogre camp in West Karana
  • Brass Earring:  All Classes.  AC 2.  Found on taskmaster in Crushbone.  Also sometimes given for Grobb Quest.
  • Diamondine Earring:  AC 2, +3 str, +12 hp.  All Classes.  Found in Mistmoore.
  • Earring of Disease Reflection:  All Classes.  AC 1.  +5 disease protection.  Found on Drelzna in Najena.  Also obtained through Nillipus quest.
  • Earring of Fire Reflection:  All Classes.  AC 1.  +5 fire protection.  Sometimes found on Erudite Madmen in Erud's Crossing.  Also obtained through Nillipus quest.
  • Earring of Frost Resistance:  All Classes.  AC 1.  +5 protection against cold.  (Submitted by Frumpy)
  • Earring of Magic Reflection:  All Classes.  AC 1.  +5 magic protection.  Obtained through Nillipus quest.
  • Earring of Poison Reflection:  All Classes.  AC 1.  +5 poison protection.  Obtained through Nillipus quest.  (Submitted by Kukrapok)
  • Electrum Amber Earring:  All Classes.  +1 strength.  Can be made with Jewelry Craft.
  • Electrum Jade Earring:  +3 wisdom.  All Classes (Submitted by McNab)
  • Electrum Jasper Earring:  All Classes. +2 wisdom.  Can be made with Jewelry Craft.
  • Electrum Lapis  Earring:  All Classes.  +3 poison  protection.  Can be made with Jewelry Craft.
  • Electrum Saphire Earring:  +4 str, +2 int.  Jewelry Craft Item.  (Submitted by Morimir)
  • Electrum Topaz Earring:  All Classes.  AC 2, +5 poison prot.  Can be made with Jewelry Craft.
  • Fishbone Earring:  All Classes.  +3 dexterity. Unlimited breathing underwater.  Found on Barbarian Fisherman in Qeynos Hills (Warning - you will lose a lot of faction for killing him) (Submitted by Doriath)
  • Golden Amber Earring:  +3 Strength.  All Classes (Submitted by Grollem)
  • Golden Ear Stud:  All Classes.  +3 to intelligence.  Found in Splitpaw and on Gnoll Embalmers in Lake Rathe.  Also obtained through Qeynos quest.
  • Golden Sapphire Earring:  +2 str +4 int.  (Submitted by Morlox)
  • Ivandyr's Hoop:  AC 6.  6 charges of Spirit Tap, +6 Wis, +6 Int, +6 Hp. Races:  Human, Erud,  Ha Elf, Troll, Ogr.  Classes:  C, E, Mg, N, Ra,Ro, SK, Sm, Wr, Wz.  Sometimes obtained from Lyunga in Innothule quest.  (Submitted by Stugg)
  • Obsidian Bead Hoop:  AC 2, +3 int, +3 agi.  All Classes.  Found on a drake name Singe who lives in Solusek's Eye.
  • Onyx Earring:  All Classes.  +2 to agility.  Found in Blackburrow.
  • Opalline Earring:  All Classes.  AC 2.  +5 to charisma.  Found on undead barkeeps in Unrest.
  • Polished Bone Hoop:  All Classes.  +10 mana.  Occasionally dropped by evil eyes.  (Submitted by Doriath)
  • Silver Bloodstone Earing:  +2 stamina. Jewelry Craft item.  (Submitted by Morimir)
