Kithicor Forest

Ged's Gloves:  There is an NPC named Ged Twigborn who hangs around another NPC and they will wander the forest at times. I usually see them in one of two places: either along the same wall as the zone to the commonlands, or by turning on the path off the main road from the sign pointing towards Freeport. Ged will make you some gloves for a price. He makes ratskin, wolfskin, bearhide, or lionskin gloves. All require some money and a specific pelt.  You need to be amiable with him to buy lionskin gloves.  The Ratskin Gloves cost 6 gold and a giant rat pelt (ac 3 , negative 5 charisma),  the Bearskin Gloves cost 25 gold and a high quality bear skin (ac 5), the wolfskin gloves cost 13 gold and a high quality wolf pelt and the lionskin gloves (ac 5) cost 95 gold and a high quality lion skin. I have been told that a tiger spawns some times in the south west corner of Kith forest near the orc spawn and gives a high quality skin, and that they are also dropped by highland Lions in NK.  The gloves are good for druids who can't wear metal.

Ivy Etched Armor:  [NOTE - This quest is still only on the test server, but should go live on 10/13/99]  In the ranger camp in Kithicor, you can obtain several pieces of Ranger only armor:  (Submitted by Tsunamineko)

  • Ivy Etched Tunic:  1) Skull of meldrath from Steamfont;  2) Right side of the rune of ivy from thistle brownie in Lesser Faydark and 3) 2 rubies.
  • Ivy Etched Helm:  1) Top of the rune of Ivy from a deathly usher in Mistmoore 2. Cyclops charm from Tarskuk in Rathe Mtns and 3) 1 Sapphire
  • Ivy Etched Sleeves:  1) Heart of fire from an inferno goblin in Solusek;  2) left rune of ivy from griffon grimfether in Karanas and 3) 2 star rubies
  • Ivy Etched Bracer:  1) The bottom of the rune of ivy from redwind in Everfrost;  2) a watery ring from a tidal lord in the Cauldron of Tears (not sure if this is Dagnor's or the OoT)  and 3) 1 fire emerald
  • Ivy Etched Gauntlets:  Hunt Maldy Greenburn (a wayward  master archer) in Raithe by unkempt druids and retreive the Bow of Morin. (NPC hints at the bow being very powerful)


Morin's Sword:  Morin Shadowbane, the multisexual ranger guildmaster of the forest, sends you on a Quest for the new Ranger only weapon, the Short Sword of Morin.  If you trade in an Ebony Bladed Sword to Morin (Some people have said that Mornin must be in female form for the trade), you will get a Short Sword of Morin , a Ranger only weapon.  She will also tell you that she will give you her personal sword if you find and kill the Ghoul Messenger, who is located "between Kithicor and the Karanas" and bring back his note, or whatever he drops.  I have received conflicting reports/speculation as to whether this will result in a different sword or the same one as you get for turning in the Ebony Blade.  Since the ghoul messenger does not seem to be in the game yet, this will have to wait to be resolved.  (Submitted by Anaxagoras Ionia)
