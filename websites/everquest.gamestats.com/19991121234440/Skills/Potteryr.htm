Pottery Recipes


If you wish to contribute recipes for creating pottery items, please e-mail me at  Allakhazam@gamestats.com

(Thanks to Kozmo for submitting most of these recipes and Polynices for updating the list)

For all pottery recipes you will need to use 1 flask of water (not summoned) when combining ingredients in the pottery wheel and the listed firing sheet(s) when combining in the kiln. Do not use firing sheets in the pottery wheel or water in the kiln; you will lose all your materials.

The following are recipes for making various pottery items:

  • Cake Round = a block of clay + a cake round sketch + a glass shard + a high quality firing sheet
  • Ceramic Lining = a small block of clay + a ceramic lining sketch + a quality firing sheet
  • Clay Jar = a block of clay + small, medium, or large jar sketch + a quality firing sheet
  • Cookie Cutters = a small block of clay + a cookie cutter template (they come in different shapes) + a high quality firing sheet
  • Large Bowl = 2 large blocks of clay + a large bowl sketch + a glass shard + a high quality firing sheet
  • Lined Poison Vial = small block of clay + a lined vial sketch + an animal skin (see list below) + a high quality firing sheet [Trivial at 168]
  • Medium Bowl = a block of clay + a medium bowl sketch + 2 quality firing sheets
  • Mixing Bowl = 2 large blocks of clay + a mixing bowl sketch + a firing sheet
  • Muffin Tin = a block of clay + a muffin tin sketch + a metal bit + a high quality firing sheet
  • Pie Tin = 2 blocks of clay + a pie tin sketch + a high quality firing sheet  [Trivial at 115]
  • Poison Vial = a small block of clay + an animal skin (see list below) + a poison vial sketch + a high quality firing sheet  [Trivial at 148]
  • Pot = a large block of clay + a pot sketch + a metal bit + a high quality firing sheet
  • Sealed Poison Vial = a small block of clay + an animal skin (see list below) + a sealed vial sketch + a high quality firing sheet [this is the recipe listed in the book, but it doesn't work]
  • Skewers = a block of clay + a skewers sketch + a quality firing sheet
  • Small Bowl= a small block of clay + a small bowl sketch + a high quality firing sheet [this currently seems to be broken]
  • Smoker = a large block of clay + a smoker sketch + 2 high quality firing sheet
Poison Vials Use Animal Skins per the list below

Verified animal skins for Poison Vials:

  • low quality bear skin
  • low/med/high cat pelt
  • polar bear skin
  • puma skin
  • white wolf skin
  • grizzly bear skin
  • zombie skin (very useful - stackable)
  • blackburrow gnoll skin
  • mist wolf
  • black wolf skin
The following will not work:
  • Anything ruined
  • deathfist pawn scalp
  • giant rat pelt
  • shadow wolf pelt
  • giant snake skin
  • silk swatch
  • shark skin
  • Kerra Guard Skin
