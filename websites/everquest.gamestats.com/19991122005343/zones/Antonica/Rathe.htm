Rathe Mountains
Level Range:      Levels 6 and higher
Typical Creatures:  Brown bears, skeletons, zombies, lizard men, grizzly bears, kodiaks, orcs, bandits, griffins, skeletons, hill giants and two sphinx.
NPC's and Quests:  Gypsy camp.   Rathe quest list
Description:   Popular and large area filled with monsters of various levels.  Home to two sphynx whose purpose is still a mystery at this time.
