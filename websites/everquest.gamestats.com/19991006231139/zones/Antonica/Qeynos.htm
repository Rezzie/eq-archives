Qeynos:
Level Range:      City (1-5 newbie zone)
Typical Creatures:  Some creatures are found in the newbie zone outside of North Qeynos.  Bat, Fire Beetle, Gnoll Pup, Klicnik Drone, Prince Klicnik, Princess Klicnik,  Queen Klicnik, Klicnik Worker, Large Rat, Decaying Skeleton, Snake, Fippy Darkpaw.  There is also a set of auaducts with some much higher level creatures.
NPC's and Quests: ; The Assassin; Bard Reports; The Bayle List; Blackburrow Stout Shipment; Blank Scrolls:; Blessed Oil; Blurred Map; Breakfast for the Guards; Broken Lute; The Clothspinner Sisters; The Crate; Diseased Animals; The Donation; Drosco the Zombie; Drosco the Zombie, part 2; Fisherman Convert; Gnasher's Head; Gnoll Fangs; Holy Armor; Karana's Blessing; Kwint's Kwest; Letter to Brother Hayle; Library Book; Lucia's Dress; Milea Clothspinner; Mirkes Tabolet; Miranda's Brother; Monk Orange Headband; Monk Red Headband:; Monk Yellow Headband; Monk White Headband; Nesiff's Icon; The Painting; Paladin Hunting; Paw of Opallo; Piss off a God; Prayer Beads; Rohand's Brandy; Snead's Rat Infestation; Stumpy Iron Toe; The Tattered Pouch; Taxes; Tomer's Rescue; Trumpy Irontoe's Head; Trumpy Irontoe's Skull; Unser's Call; Vasty Deep Water;
Description:   Huge Human City with two city zones and an underground zone.  The newbie zone outside of North Qeynos contains the usual newbie monsters as well as the unique klicknicks, which can be dangerous if you mess with their queen.  There is also a gnoll named Fippy Darkpaw who can give lower levels some trouble.  There are many items for sale in the citiy's shops and a large number of quests originate from here.  For those who like intrigue, there is a secret entrance to Queynos, at the loc 332.10 -161.39, which is left of the city gate along the wall. The wall is covered with vines, and it leads to an entrance to the Queynos Aqueducts.  There is a second entrance for the aquaducts, under the dock with no boat but I dont know in which part of the sewers it ends up in.
