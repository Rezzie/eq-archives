Alchemy Recipes

If you wish to contribute recipes for creating potions and other items, please e-mail me at  Allakhazam@gamestats.com

Note:  The Alchemy Books can be found in Grobb

(Thanks to Mareth the Shaman for submitting these recipes)

  • Charming Deceit:  (Temporary Charisma Boost)  Undead Froglock Tounge + Mugwar.
  • Copal's Demise:  (Cancel Magic)  Evil Eye Eyestalk + Horehound.
  • Frosty Insurgency:  (Extra Cold Resistance)  Polar Bear Skin + Bistort.
  • Gnomish Boils:  (Infra or Ultra Vision)  Bat fur + Eyebright.
  • Gorging Toxin:  (Extra Poison resistance)  Giant Wasp Venom Sac + Boneset.
  • Gulon's Impunity:  (Levitate)  Figwort + lesser lightstone.
  • Skin of Ro:  (Extra Fire Resistance)  Vox's dust + fire Drake Scale.
  • Somber Origins:  (Hangover Cure)  Alkanet + a rat ear.
  • Soul of the Incorporeal:  (See Invisible)  Aviak feathers + Stinging nettle.
  • Troll's Essence:  (Temporary Strength and Stamina Boost)  Yebamante + a Faerie wing.
  • Undead's  Recourse:  (Temporary Strength Boost)  Warbone chips + Damiana.
  • Vox's Vitality:  (Underwater Breathing)  Froglock leg + Sea Spirit.
  • Wolf's essence:  (Extra stamina recovery)  High Quality Wolf skin + Sticklewart.
