Pottery Recipes


If you wish to contribute recipes for creating pottery items, please e-mail me at  Allakhazam@gamestats.com

(Thanks to Kozmo for submitting most of these recipes)

The following are recipes for making various pottery items:

  • Cake Round =  a block of clay + a cake round sketch + a glass shard
  • Ceramic Lining = a small block of clay + a ceramic lining sketch (Use a quality firing sheet)
  • Clay Jar = a block of clay +  any sized jar sketch
  • Cookie Cutters = a small block of clay + a cookie cutter template (they come in different shapes)
  • Large Bowl = a large block of clay + a large bowl sketch (Use a quality firing sheet)
  • Lined Poison Vial = small block of clay + poison vial sketch + flask of water + animal skin (varies)  (Use a High Quality Firing Sheet?)
  • Medium Bowl = a block of clay + a medium bowl sketch (Use a quality firing sheet)
  • Mixing Bowl = 2 large blocks of clay + a mixing bowl sketch
  • Muffin Tin = a block of clay + a muffin tin sketch + a metal bit
  • Pie Tin = 2 blocks of clay + a pie tin sketch
  • Poison Vial = Small block of clay + animal skin (varies) + Vial Sketch + Flask of Water  (Use a High Quality Firing Sheet)
  • Pot = a large block of clay + a pot sketch + a metal bit
  • Sealed poison Vial = Small block of clay + animal skin (varies) + Vial Sketch + Flask of Water  (Use a High Quality Firing Sheet?)
  • Skewers = a block of clay +  a skewers sketch
  • Small Bowl [broken?] = a small block of clay + a small bowl sketch (Use a quality firing sheet)
  • Smoker = a large block of clay + a smoker sketch
