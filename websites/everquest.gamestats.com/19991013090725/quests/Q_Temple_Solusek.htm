Temple of Solusek Ro

(Note:  The temple is only active on the test server at this time.  Since they are not yet active, these quests are likely to change.  Also this page is under heavy construction as I find out more about the items available in this zone.  And I will add the item images as soon as I start to get them).

Many people submitted information for these quests.  Thank you all.

Bard Lambent Armor:  Walthin Fireweaver and Cyrissa make Lambent armor.  This is bard only armor.

  • Lambent Breastplate:  (Cyrissa)  Basalt Carapace (from basalt gargoyles in lower guk), Gypsy Lute (from Mistmoore)Lambent Ruby;
  • Lambent Helm:  (Cyrissa)  Opaline Helm (from Deepwater Goblins), Mudwater Rune (From Lizard Fanatic), Lambent Star Ruby;
  • Lambent Vambraces:  (Cyrissa)  Fiery Vambraces (from Solusek Champion), The Top Portion of the Rune of the One eye (Spawns on Choon), Lambent Sapphire;
  • Lambent Bracers:  (Cyrissa)  Dark Boned Bracelet (From Greater Dark Boned Skeletons), Griffenne Charm (From Griffenne's), Lambent Fire Opal
  • Lambent Gauntlets:  (Walthin)  Shin Gauntlents (From Shin Knight in Guk), Black Silk Gloves (from Deathly Usher in Mistmoore), Lambent Star Ruby;
  • Lambent Greaves:  (Walthin)  Icy Greaves: (Elite Goblin Guards in Permafrost), Shin Greaves (Shin Ghoul KNights in Lower Guk), Lambent Fire Opal;
  • Lambent Boots:  (Walthin)  Firewalker Boots (From Solusek Mage), Middle Portion of the Rune of the One Eye (Froon), Lambent Sapphire.
Cleric Items:  Blaiz the Radiant and Gavel the Temperant will make special items for Clerics.
  • ???:  (Blaize) She is looking for 4 icon and ingot pairs, Reliant, Ardent, Fervent, and Penitent.  Reliant's icon and ingot are found in kithicor forest in a burned down cabin and in lake rathe.  Ardent's icon and ingot are found in befallen and everfrost.  Fervent's ingot is found on an aviak named Gull in ocean of tears, the icon is on Jovan the Ardent himself in highpass.  Penitent's ingot is hidden underwater in guk, the icon is on Maugrim, and erudite heritic, maybe on kerra island.  She doesn't say what you get for these.
  • ???:  (Gavel)  He is looking for 3 icon and ingot pairs, reverent, constant, and devout.  Reverent is found on a gypsie named lianna feresa in rathe mntns.  Constant is found on 2 aqua goblins, 1 named Sludge in runnyeye, the other named Bilge in Dagnor's.  Devout's ingot may be in mino caves of steamfront, and icon in the hidden underwater cave in everfrost.
Coin of Tash:  This is necessary to obtain a high level enchanter spell.  Romar Sunto offers to give you the tashania scroll if you can find 10 coins in different cities around the world.  He will sell you a book on where to find all 10 coins for 50gp and will give you a pouch to combine them in.  The coins are called an old silver coin, and can be found in the following places (I am missing two):  Ak'anon - Near great wheel (not in water);  Kaladim - Pool outside castle; Erudin - Pool outside guilds in palace; Qeynos - Under UFO - Fish Pool; Halas - In lake infront of city to left of docks onledge; Oggok - in lake near warrior guild north end; Grobb - in pool near bar where people always fall in; Neriak - Ouside blind fish window near ice comet rod.  Take the 10 coins and combine them in the coin pouch and give the resulting "Coin Of Tash" back to him for the tashania scroll (level 49 enchanter spell).

Darkwood Staff:  Romar Sunto will  give you a darkwook staff, also known as the Staff of Ro, which is useable by all classes, if you bring him a Lambent Stone from a Hill Giant, a Sand Giant or a Griffin.

Enchanter Incandescent Armor:  Sultin makes various incandescent items, plus the rod, which are for enchanters and Romar, in addition to giving out the coin of Tash quest, will make you a staff.

  • Incandescent Wand:   He needs a Pouch of Silver Dust, found on the dusty Werebat in the Estate of Unrest and a Silver wand, found on a Silvered Guard in the Temple of Cazic Thule.  You will need to pay Esmirella of the Northern Karana Gypsy Camp 50 gold to enchant the silver wand.
  • Incandescent Mask:  He needs a Glowing Mask, found on the skeletal monk in the caverns of Guk and all three Runes of Fortune.  One Rune can be found on the Dark Elf Enynti in the Chasm below Castle Mistmoore.  The other two can each be purchased for 50 golden coins each - one from Mizr N`Mar in the Neriak City Library and the other from Madam Serena in Qeynos.
  • Incandescent Gloves:  He needs to sew together four other sets of gloves to make these.  Glowing Gloves from Shadowed Men, Radiant Gloves from Radiant in the mazes of Cazic Thule and Enchanted and Gleaming gloves, which can  be purchased for 50 gp from Tizina of the Lavastrom Gypsies and a bloodstone from Tarn Vislin of HighKeep.
  • Rod of Insidious Glamour:  (increases charisma) He needs a golden rod (from a froglok priest in guk) and an enchanted shining stone.  The enchanted shining stone is made from a shining stone from shadow men, take that stone to Cynthia, a gypsie in the rathe mountains and give it and 50gp to get it enchanted.
The Harvester:  Vurgo will make you a harvester if you bring him a shadowed scythe from a shadow man and perhaps something else.

Lambent Gems:  In order to get lambent armor, you must first get Lambent gems.  These are made as follows:

  • Lambent Fire Opal: 2 fire opals (purchasable from Ginni) and 1 Lambent stone, Give to Orstorm;
  • Lambent Star Ruby: 2 Star Rubies and 1 Lambent Stone, Give to Ginni;
  • Lambent Sapphire:  2 Sapphires and 1 Lambent Stone,   Give to Gardern;
  • Lambent Ruby:  2 Rubies and 1 Lambent Stone, Give to Vilissia.
Mage Armor:  Joyce will make 3 articles of magician armor.
  • Clay Bracelets: (ac, + sv magic, casts eye of zomm) all 4 runes of clay, 3 of which come from gnomish necros in Rathe mntns, the other comes from goblin headmaster in ocean of tears;
  • Earthen Boots: (+ sta, animal faction?)stone marker (glyphed guard in mistmoore), heart of stone (stone skeleton), soiled boots (froglok Tonta Knight in guk), and a cats eye agate.
  • Circlet of Mist: (+ int, acts as greater light source) globe of mist (festering hag in unrest), watery ring (aqua goblin tidal lord in dagnor's),  ring of evoluoy (large alligator in cazic), and a sapphire.
Necromancer Shadowbound Armor:  Syllina will make shadowbound armor for necromancers.  She makes 3 kinds:  boots, gloves and a robe.  In addition, she will tell you how to make shadow silk for 50gp.
  • ShadowBound Boots:  shadow silk, sceptre of tears (from castle mistmoore), hand of shadow (from shadow men), and a skeletal toe (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns);
  • ShadowBound Gloves: shadow silk, sceptre of tears, hand of shadow (from shadow men), and skeletal finger (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns);
  • Robe of Enshroudment: shadow silk, werebat wing (from unrest), mask of shadow (shadowmen), and globe of shadow (Nokta Shaman in guk).
Respecialize:  Talk to Ostorm and he will offer to change your specialization. I think it requires a ruby.

Robe of the Elements: Joyce will make a Robe of the Elmements (protection from the elements, melee, and aids focus), which can be used by all pure casters, if you bring her the following:  fire goblin skin (solusek), frost goblin skin (permafrost), a "Twice-Woven Cloak" (faerie guard in faydark), and a scroll of elemental armor;

Wizard Items:  Gardern and Vilissia will make several items for wizards:

  • Staff of Temperate Flux:  (Gardern)  He needs a Heart of Fire from an Inferno Goblin Wizard in the Caverns of Solusek, a Heart of Frost from a Goblin Wizard in the Caverns of Permafrost, a Rod of Bone from a Stone Skeleton by the shores of Lake Rathe and a Staff of Ro.
  • Weeping Wand:  (Gardern)  He nees a Silver Wand from a Silvered Guard in the Temple Cazic Thule, A Bloodback Wand from the Mountains of Rathe, Twice-Woven Silk from a Faerie Guard in Faydark, and a Scepter of Tears from the Tomb in Castle Mistmoore.
  • Tishans Kilt: (Vilissia) You must take a Shadowed Ball Shadowed Men and bring it to Trankia in the Everfrost Mountains.  Not sure what else is required.
  • Acumen Mask: (Vilissia)  She needs a Glowing Mask from a Skeletal Monk in the Caverns of Guk, A Patch of Shadow from a Shadowed Man, a Darkbone Skull from a Darkbone Skeleton in the Estate of Unrest and a Bonechipped Mask from a Goblin Headmaster in the Ocean of Tears.
  • Runescale Cloak:  (Vilissia)  She needs a Lizardscale Cloak from the Temple of Cazic Thule and the three Runes of Scale.  All three can be found on Crocodiles - Saltwater Crocodiles in the Caverns of Guk, Deepwater Crocodiles in the Oasis of Marr and Firescale Crocodiles in the mountains of Lavastorm.
