Journeyman Boots:  This quest may not be active yet.  An NPC in the rathe Mountains has stolen the Journeyman boots from Najeena.  Your only recourse is to kill him and recover the boots.  He is supposed to be a very high level (at least in the 40's).

The Shining Stone:  This quest may not be active yet.  The enchanted shining stone can be made from a shining stone from shadow men.  Take that stone to Cynthia, a gypsie in the rathe mountains and give it and 50gp to get it enchanted.
