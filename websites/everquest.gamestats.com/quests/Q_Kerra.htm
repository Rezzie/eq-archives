Kerra Island

Catfish Tail: (Paladins only)Talk to one of the Paladins standing below the Temple of Quellious. He will ask if you want to "face fear"  and when you say yes, he will tell you to go to Kerra Island and kill a Cat Fisher, and bring back his tail for a little money, experience, faction and an item (usually a brass ring).  (Submitted by Izzar)
