Oasis of Marr:
Level Range:      10-40
Typical Creatures:  Red bone keletons, zombies, crypt mummies, crocodiles, caimens, deepwater crocodiles, deepwater caimens, orcs (warriors and priests), A orc warriors (higher level),  spectres, sand giants, deepwater goblins, dervish, madmen.
NPC's and Quests:  gypsy camp
Description:   A lake area between the two Ro deserts and a very popular hunting zone.  This is one of the best zones in the game for players in their low to mid teens.  Most players tend to hunt crocodiles and caimens near the shore, where there are pillars in the water that are safe for resting and meditating.  These are very good experience, but have virtually no loot.  This area also gets extremely crowded.  There is an orc camp in the SE corner and 2 derv camps in the SW corner.  These camps are good hunting, and often overlooked by players too busy hunting crocs.  Orcs wander the desert and can sometimes drop decent loot.  There is a major orc spawning point near the ruins not far from the gypsy camp where a full group or two can easily pull and kill a steady stream of orcs.  Beware of going there without a lot of players though, because the orcs tend to come in waves.  Many people also hunt orcs by the zone to North Ro, sending out a person with SOW to lure them and fight them next to the zone where you can quickly run for safety when in trouble.  There is a tower underneath the lake, which used to have relatively low level aqua goblins, but now has very high level deepwater goblins guarding it.  No word if there's anything good inside the tower.  There are a number of sand giants roaming the area that you need to keep a lookout for, and a huge crocodile named lockjaw that will kill lower level players (though he is hunted pretty quickly when he appears as his skin can be used to make several nice items).  There is also an island in the middle of the  lake with a tower guarded by several spectres.  Unfortunately, high level characters often go there to kill spectres, get killed themselves, and loose a spectre or three over the zone which wanders around indiscriminately killing everyone.
