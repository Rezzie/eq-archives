The Plane of Fear
Level Range:     Groups of 46 and above.  You cannot enter if you are below level 46.
Typical Creatures:  The god Cazic Thule.  Nightmare, Boogeyman, Glarelord, Dracholich, Amygdalan Knight,  Armegeddon Warrior, Dread, Phantasm, Fetid Fiend, Scareling, Phoboplasm, Fright Finger, Spinchiller Sppider, Worry Wraith, Tenticle Terror, Gorgon, Salhaim.
NPC's and Quests:
Description:  The dwelling place and home plane of the god Cazic Thule.  Don't even think of going here without a lot of help and a very high level character.  You have a very good chance of dying here, even at level 50 with a full group of level 50's.  Many spells do not work here or work differently, and the creatures can cast some pretty nasty spells themselves, including ones that teleport you randomly around the zone. The plane is accessed through an entrance in Feerrott.  To get to this entrance from the Innothule swamp entrance, follow the left wall and look for a hidden cave just past the second river.  It will be full of Spectres.  If you can't handle the spectres, don't bother going forward.  Following the wall to the right, you should come across another hidden cave entrance.  This cave has a level 40 necromancer in it and the teleport to the Plane of Fear.  There is an exit from the plane in the castle, which is almost impossible to get to, so the only practicle way to exit the plane is via either a personal or group gate spell.  Many of the most powerful items in the game can be found in this plane.  Just about every class has a full set of powerful armor which be looted from the monsters here.  All of these items are no drop lore items, so make sure you are looting an item you can actually use.  You can also fight Cazic Thule himself, but you had better have about 50 friends with you if you want to survive.
