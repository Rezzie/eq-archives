Black Burrow
Level Range:     5 - 15
Typical Creatures:  Gnolls of all sorts. Burly Gnoll, Gnoll, Gnoll Commander, Gnoll Elite, Gnoll Guardsman, Patrolling Gnoll, Gnoll Pup, Gnoll Scout, Gnoll Shaman, Gnoll High Shaman, Scrawny Gnoll, Giant Plague Rat, Giant Snake.
NPC's and Quests: Gnoll Fangs
Description:   Home of the Sabertooth Clan of Gnolls.  Very popular dungeon close to Qeynos, Halas and Surefall Glades.  As with every dungeon, it gets harder the deeper you go.  The top level is safe for level five or so, but lower down you need to be grouped or a much higher level, as the gnolls tend to gang up on an adventurer.  The gnolls often drop weapons and other items of decent value for this level.  Just follow the trail of corpses to get to the depths.  The dungeon is famous for the huge and nasty trains that periodically appear from the depths to slaughter all of the unwary low levels hunting up above.  The best loot comes from killing elite gnoll guards at the very bottom of the dungeon.  However, these rooms are camped pretty heavily.  This is also the pass through between Halas and the land of the Barbarians and the rest of the world.
