    Mythiran Tower--The Cackling Klaknak


    October 11, 1999

    For those of you who don't know yet, there is a big patch coming on Wednesday which will eliminate future drops of Rubicite armor (the existing armor will not be affected), and add a new zone called the Temple of Solusek Ro, which has many quests to get class-specific armor to replace rubicite.  They have been fine tuning the Solusek zone on the test server, and I believe that I now have most of the quests listed in the Solusek quest section. I would not be surprised if some of the details there change though and if some additional ones are still added prior to the patch, so use the info with caution.  I also expect that zone to be loaded with people for the first few days, so I would suggest you don't go there if you have a slow computer or are not a fairly high level capable of doing the quests.  The patch should also change the jboots so that only lower levels can get them in Najeena and higher levels have to do a quest, which I believe may go through Rathe Mountains.  I'll post more after I find out the details.

    Just a reminder that it is still free to post items in the auction section until this Friday.  After that, there will be a small fee.  Several nice updates today.  An interesting one is a quest that supposedly raises faction with the elusive beta neutral faction needed by wizards to do the ice comet quest.  Other sections with new or updated information are:   Legs Slot ;  Finger Slot ; Wrist Slot ;  Inventory ;  Rivervale Quests ;  Qeynos Quests ;  West Karanas Quests ;  North Karanas Quests ; Everfrost Quests ;  Grobb Quests ;  Faction ;  Consider Messages ;  Bug List ;  Guild links

    October 7, 1999

    For those of you that have not heard of this, there is a bug that allows a person to switch the item in the trade screen and give you a different item than you bargained for and saw in the window.  I think this is an exploit and should be a bannable offense.  I also thinkVerant should make fixing this a high priority because trading is an integral part of the game and once people start to distrust the trading process, an important part of the game will deteriorate.  Until then, I would suggest only trading for higher level items with higher level characters to avoid easily deleted mules.  Of course that is not a guarantee, but very few people would risk ruining their main character's reputation by exploiting this bug.  I bring this up because of a person named Hamek who, despite his level, exploited this bug and switched items on a person on my server (Povar).  It is his misfortune that she happened to be my sister, and thus one of the few people whose word I trust enough to post this based upon her word alone.  If you are on Povar, it might be prudent to not trade with this Hamek person.
