Inventory Slot Items

Note that many Inventory Slot Items can be kept in the left hand.  Most will not function if kept within a container such as a backpack.

  • Box of Abu-Kar:  10 slot container that eliminates weight of items placed in it. (Submitted by Bayard Brightblade)
  • Burned Out Lightstone:  Same as regular lightstone, but disappears when you log out.
  • Chipped Bone Rod:  Charged Item.  Casts Locate corpse.  Found in Befallen or purchase at various magic shops.
  • Chrysoberyl Talisman:  AC 5.  +3 wis, +20 Mana.  All Classes.  (Submitted by Kaine)
