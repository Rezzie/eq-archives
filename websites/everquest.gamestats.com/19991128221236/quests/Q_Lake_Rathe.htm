Lake Rathetear

Shaman Totemic Armor:    Kyralynn and Vrynn, in the barbarian fishing village, will make high quality shaman-only armor for you if you fetch them the right ingredients.   Dufrenite is occasionally dropped by carrion ghouls in Unrest and also can be purchased in the magic shops in Halas, W Karanas, Grobb and Ogguk for 150 to 200 pp each depending upon your faqction and charisma.  The ogre shamen in the camp on the other side of the lake will crush/grind/powder it for you.  Banded armor is made with the blacksmithing skill.

  • Totemic Bracer:  (Kyralynn) medium banded bracer, 1 dufrenite, bull elephant tooth (from bull elephant in SK) and cauldron shell (from aqua goblin tidal lords in Dagnors).
  • Totemic Vambraces:  (Vrynn) medium banded sleeve, 1 ground dufrenite, a ghoul carrion (from Unrest) and charger hoof chip (from centaur charger in SK - very rare drop)
  • Totemic Greaves:  (Vrynn)  medium banded legs, 1 powdered dufrenite, perma snowball (from goblin wizard in permafrost) and bone barbs (from barbed bone skeleton in Unrest)
  • Totemic Boots:  (Vrynn)  medium banded boots, 1 dufrenite, terror spines (from tentacle terror in Najena) and orc chief tongue (from Orc chiefton in Lesser Faydark)
  • Totemic Cloak:  [AC 10, sta +5](Kyralynn)  medium banded cloak, 1 powdered dufrenite, driftwood pipe (rare drop from isle goblin chief in the Ocean of Tears) and basalt drake scales (from a basalt drake in Rathe Mountains)
  • Totemic Breastplate: [AC 19, +5 disease, poison and magic protection] (Kyralynn)  medium banded mail, 1 crushed dufrenite, grizzlenot bark (from Grizzlenot, an extremely rare named treant in SK) and lancer fish fin (from lancer fish in Kedge Keep)
  • Totemic Helm:  (Kyralynn) medium banded helm, 1 dufrenite, glacier bear pelt (from glacier bear in Everfrost cave) and ogreskin chamois (from lizard page in Cazic)
  • Totemic Gauntlets:  (Vrynn)  medium banded gauntlets, 1 crushed dufrenite, mammoth ribs (from Wooly Mammoths in Everfrost) and griffene downfeather (from griffenes N Karanas)
