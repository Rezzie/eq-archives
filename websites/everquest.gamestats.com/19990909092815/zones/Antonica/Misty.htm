Misty Thicket:
Level Range:      1-10
Typical Creatures:  Typical newbie monsters.  Bat, Large Bat, Giant Bat, Black Bear, Young Kodiak Bear, Fire Beetle, Bixies, Goblin Warrior, Goblin Worker, Klaknak Drone, Prince Klaknak, Princess Klaknak, Queen Klaknak, Klaknak Warrior, Orc Pawn, Orc Centurion, Orc Legionnaire, Large Rat, Giant Rat, Giant Scarab, Skeleton, Decaying Skeleton, Tree Snake, Moss Snake, Giant Spider, Spiderling, Giant Wasp, Black Wolf, Shadow Wolf, bixie drones, bixie queen, mangy rat.
NPC's and Quests: Bandages for Honeybugger; Bug Collection; Honey Jum; Orc Belts;
Description: Halfling newbie area.  This is an excellent zone to hunt up to level 9 or 10, but after that your options are pretty limited.  The zone is split into two areas, with the true newbie area within the walled area, and the higher level area (above level 5) beyond the wall.  Inside the wall are the lower level critters such as giant rats, giant bats, snakes, fire beetles, klaknaks and bixies.  Outside the wall are more formidable creatures and several camps.  There are three goblin camps which are relatively close together and an orc camp which is in the far corner of the zone.  There is also a set of ruins on the hill which usually only spawn skeletons, but occasionally spawn something nastier.   Because there are several deputies at the wall entrance who will help you if you get in trouble, it is actually possible for a halfling to start hunting in this area as low as level 4.  The best hunting is the various goblins.  They give good xp, carry rusty weapons, and drop war beads which can be turned in to the Sheriff for experience, faction and some money.  The three goblin camps are almost always hunted by one or more groups, but they spawn pretty fast and generally have enough hunting to keep everyone busy.  There are also goblin warriors who just wander the zone.  The biggest danger here is a Goblin named Mooto, who is close to level 10 and able to cast magic spells.  He spawns in one camp and then wanders around from camp to camp so you cn run into him at any one of the camps.  Probably the best camp of the three is the one right next to Runnyeye because you get the added advantage of hunting the two goblin guards who spawn at the Runnyeye entrance and you are close enough to the zone border to run if you get in trouble.  The orc camp is often not camped since the orcs are more in the 8 to 14 level range (similar to those in the commonlands) and most people have moved beyond Misty Thicket by those levels.  A note for the big people.  While there are shops near the entrance to Rivervale, the doors are very samll and you can only duck through them if you maneuver yourself into the perfect position.  However, there is supposed to be one shop to the right of the entrance to Rivervale where you can shop through the window.
