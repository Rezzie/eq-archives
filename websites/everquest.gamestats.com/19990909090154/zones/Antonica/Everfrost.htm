Everfrost Peaks
Level Range:      1 - 15
Typical Creatures:  Polar Bear, Polar Bear Cub, Ice Giant, Gnoll Pup, Ice Goblin Caster,  Ice Goblin Scout, Ice Goblin Whelp, Snow Leopard, Mammoth Calf, Wooly Mammoth, Snow Orc Mountaineer, Snow Orc Shaman, Snow Orc Troopers, Skeleton, Decaying Skeleton, Ice Boned Skeleton, Giant Wooly Spider, Large Wooly Spider, Wooly Spiderling, White Wolf .
NPC's and Quests: Gnoll Teeth; Head of Granin O'Gill; Ice Goblin Necklaces; Ivan McMannus' Remains; Karg Icebear; Magic Elixer for the Warriors;
Description:   The Barbarian newbie zone.  Cold and snowy, and home to many creatures of the snow and frost.  Also the entrance to the mid to high level dungeon Permafrost. Cheflatho tells of another section that is largely unexplored:  My partner and I (Fenrick) were exploring last night and came upon an area that no one on last night had heard of.  If you go down the ice hole (same one as quest) and then swim north under the ice (use water breathing), you will come to a ice goblin diver guarding the entrance to an underground cavern.  He is green to me at lvl 22.  Anyways past that, the ground slopes up and u end up in some sort of cavern maze.  This place is really huge.  All we have found so far is the following.  A couple ice goblin divers guarding at various places, low level.  A room with 3 ice bone skels (again green to me).  After killing them (and no loot) we could not figure out the room, it has runes all over the place.  There is another room with 2 ice bone skels also and some round platform, you cannot enter the platform directly, but must go in side stepping around it.  Again, we could not figure out what to do here.  The only other thing we found was an NPC named Sulan McMoore (from memory).  He seemed to be a VERY high lvl caster.  We recruited 4 others we could trust to keep our secret and tried him out.  He killed us (6 lvl 22, varied party) in no time flat, doing about 160 damage per hit, and resisting all casting.  He was ready to attack on all our cons btw.  There are many runes on the cavern walls all over the place.  Some one thought this might be a plane portal that EQ has not implemented yet.  We did some mapping but the place is really huge with many corridors.  There was nothing else we could find and since we could not get any exp or items from what we could find, we soon left.
