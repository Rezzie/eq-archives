Qeynos Aqueducts
Level Range:      5 - 20
Typical Creatures:  Frogloks, Giant Rat, Mangy Rat, Gelatinous Cube, Cubert (named baby Cube), Large Snake, Beggar, Skeletons, NPC Necros, Drosco (named Zombie), NPC Guards, Mercenaries, Dread Corpse, Piranha, Large Piranha, Fish, Large Spiders, Thugs, Smugglers, Sharks.
NPC's and Quests:   Drosco the Zombie; Drosco the Zombie, part 2; Lucia's Dress;
Description:   The sewer system of Qeynos, used by the underbelly of the city.  This is a huge maze of tunnels and passages in two different levels.  The Bloodsabre guild is headquarted down here and can do a lot of damage if disturbed.  There are also some pretty tough undead creatures.
