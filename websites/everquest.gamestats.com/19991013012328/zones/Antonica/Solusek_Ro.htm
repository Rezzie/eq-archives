The Temple of Solusek Ro
Level Range:    City zone  [will go live on 10/13/99]
Typical Creatures:  n/a
NPC's and Quests:   Solusek quest list
Description:   This temple to the god Solusek Ro is a small zone located off of lavastorm.  It is treated as a city zone for the purposes of binding, making it a good place to stop for good classes on their way to Najeena, Solusek's eye or Nagafen's Lair.  It is also the home of several high level quests for various classes, many of which result in very nice items and armor.
