Black Burrow

Gnoll Fangs:  (All Classes)   You may have noticed that on occasion you get a gnoll fang after killing a gnoll.  You have probably been throwing them away since merchants disdain buying those dirty gnoll teeth.  But wait!  There is a guard Captain located on the top floor of the Warrior's Guild in Qeynos who seems to have a pathological hatred of gnolls (don't we all).  Take him your collection of fangs and give them to him one at a time for some experience and a moonstone.  Then take your collection of moonstones to Fish's Ale (a pub down by the docks) and go into the back room, you'll find a bartender.  Give the moonstones to the bartender, and for each stone, you'll recieve a bottle of Blackburrow Stout.  The value of a bottle of stout seems to vary, but is still pretty decent.  (Submitted by Edmond the Tall of Rivervale)

Gnoll Fangs No 2:  Qeynos isn't the only place with and NPC who hates gnolls enough to pay for their fangs.  Lysbith McNaff in the pit of Doom  will give you rations, money and experience for each gnoll tooth you give her. (you may have to give her three at a time now).  Almost makes that dental degree worthwhile.  Giving teeth to Lysbith Mcnaff raises your faction with Wolves of the North;  Shamen of Justice; Merchants of Halas; Steelwarriors and it lowers your faction with  Rogues of the White Rose. (Submitted by Kyll, Barbarian Warrior)
