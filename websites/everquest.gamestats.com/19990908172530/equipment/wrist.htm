Wrist Slot Items
If you have any additions or corrections to this list, please e-mail me at  Allakhazam@gamestats.com

All Armor items come in the following sizes:
Small - worn by Wood Elf, High Elf, Dark Elf, Dwarf, Halfling, and Gnome
Medium - worn by Human, Barbarian, Erudite, Wood Elf, High Elf, Dark Elf, and Half Elf
Large - worn by Barbarian, Ogre, and Troll

Class Code:  B = Bard;  C = Cleric;  D = Druid;  E = Enchanter;  Mg = Magician;  Mk = Monk;  N = Necromancer;  P = Paladin;  Ra = Ranger;  Ro = Rogue;  SK = Shadowknight;  Sm = Shaman;    Wr = Warrior;  Wz = Wizard

NOTE:  The items made through the jewelry skill have been changed as of 6/7/99.  The old items retain their original statistics, while the new ones have additional statistics.  This means that you can find two identically named items with different statistics.  Where I know the change, the updated statistics are in brackets.
