Najena
Level Range:      20 - 35
Typical Creatures:  Ogres, Necromancers, Skeletons, Greater Skeletons, tentacle terrors, Ogre guards, Ogre guard officers, Ogre guard captain, Moosh (Ogre NPC - unknown), darkboned skeletons, large skeletons, fire elementals, air elementals, earth elementals, magicians, Drelzna, Rathyl, Trazdon, Najena, froglok ghouls, giant black widow spiders, goblin warriors, goblin magicians, Bonecracker.
NPC's and Quests:  3 nasty magic users
Description:   Located off of lavastorm, this is home to a tribe of Ogres and other assorted nasties.  This is a pretty large dungeon with some nice fights and some good potential spoils.  Best know as the home of the flowing black robe and the journeyman boots, most of the people in the dungeon can be found at these two spawn points leaving the rest of the dungeon relatively lightly hunted.
