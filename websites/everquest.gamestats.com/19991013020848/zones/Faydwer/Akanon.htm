Ak'Anon
Level Range:    City
Typical Creatures:  Decaying Gnome Skeleton, Obsolete Clockworks, Rogue Clockwork, Rock Snakes, Rock Spiderlings
NPC's and Quests: 27 C Blackbox; Black Box Fragments; Bugglegup; Duster Models; Gearheart; Micro Servos; Red 5 Clockwork; Rusted Black Boxes; Series C Black Boxes; Telescope Lenses;
Description:   The Home city of the Gnomes.  This is the only place you are allowed to start if you start off as a Gnome.  The city contains a zoo and lots of fun clockwork creatures.  If you get lost, simply ask a clockwork guide to point you the right way.  One of the most beautiful cities in Norrath.  There is also an area of mines that can be pretty tough. A tip for those new to the Mines of Malfunction:  Don't attack the decaying skeletons.  The necromancer guild is in the mines and the guildmaster's pets will help out their decaying friends if they're close enough.  Also, the npc necromancers cast buffs on the wandering decaying skeletons.  A level 1 skeleton with level 40+ buffs on it can ruin your day.
