Crushbone

D'Vinn's Heart:  This quest is given after you complete the Orc Runner Quest.  The Paladin Lord in the basement of the Paladin's guild in Felwithe wants you to kill the Dark Elven Ambassador D'vinn and bring back his black heart.  D'vinn is in Crushbone but you had better be armed to the teeth with a solid group before trying to tackle him.   For all your work you get a few pieces of gold and some minor experience.  One of the more worthless quests in the game.  (Submitted by Tshober)

Orc Belts:  Turn in orc belts to Canloe Nusbeck in the warrior's guild for experience,  faction and minor money and items.   You can turn in as many as four at at a time.  The general consensus is that four items will just be treated like giving one item four times, so it is just a time saving device.  You can find these by killing orcs in Crushbone. Faction changes are as follows:  + Stormguard, + Merchants of Kaladim, + Miners Guild 249, +Kazon Stormhammer, - Cracknek Warriors.   (Submitted by Bigo)

Orc Legionnaire Shoulder Pads:  Turn these in to Canloe Nusbeck in Kaladim.  As with the orc belts, you can find these by killing orcs in Crushbone.  You can turn in as many as four at at a time.  I have been told that you have a chance at a better item with two at a time, but that more than two will not help.  I am not sure if that is true or not.  One will get you an item as well.  As of now, you can turn these in even if your faction is too low for him to actually give you the quest. You will get some decent weapons (sometimes even bronze ones worth several pp each) and items for these.  Faction changes are as follows:  + Stormguard, + Merchants of Kaladim, + Miners Guild 249, +Kazon Stormhammer, - Cracknek Warriors.  (Submitted by Bigo).

Screaming Mace:  You need to get a Dwarven Ringmail Tunic (found on Crush) and a Bracer of Battle (found on the Orc Warlord) to complete this quest.  You are also supposed to have a Shiny Brass Shield (found on the Orc Trainer), but you should be able to do it without one. Then, go to the Slaver hut with the Dwarven Smith and the Elven Priest.  First talk to the Dwarf smith, who talks about armor and asks for the Shiny Shield.  If you have the shield you can give it to him and he will ask for the tunic.  Give it to him, and you will receive a dwarven mace.  Alternatively, if you don't have the shield or don't want to give it up (Why would you), you need to say to the smith "the king is dead" and then ask him "what about ringmail".  He will then ask for the tunic and give you the mace.  Once you have the dwarven mace, talk to the Elven priest and say "the King is dead".  He  will then ask for the Bracer.  If you give it to him, he gives you a Prayer Cloth of Tunare.  Then give both the mace and the prayer cloth to the Priest, and he gives you back a Screaming Mace, an 8/36 magical weapon that occasionally yaulps.  (Submitted by Fflewddur)

Slave Keys:  You need to give a slave a copper and he'll tell you what key will free him, and you can use keys on slaves for faction.  When you kill an Orc Slaver, sometimes they will give keys. The keys always are the same for the particular race/sex combo of a slave, but different slaves can spawn in the same places. Each key has a number to go to a lock on the slaves.  When you match the key with the lock (slave) you will get xp and some cash.  The number/lock combinations and faction changes I have been given are as follows: (1) Gnome Mage: Key No. 15. Dark Reflection gets worse;  Eldritch Collective,  Gemchoppers, King Ak'anon get better, The Dead gets worse (2)  Dwarf:  Male Key 16.  Stormguard gets better, Miners guild 249 gets better, KazonStormhammer gets better,  ClericsofUnderfoot gets better.  - Female Key 17.  KazonStormhammer, MerchantsofKaladim, Stormguard, MinersGuild249 better and Craknek Warriors worse; (3)  Wood Elf: Male Key 18 - Female Key 19  KelethinMerchants gets better, Emerald Warriors gets better, Merchants of Felwithe gets better; Cushbone orcs gets worse (4) High Elf:  Male Key 20 - Female Key 21 KeepersoftheArt, King Tearisthex, Faydark's Champions get better, The Dead gets worse; There are also keys 22, 23 and 24, but I'm not sure who they go to.

Unfinished Molds:  Several Slaves give you unfinished molds in exchange for other items.  How they keep these items as slaves is a mystery.  The three items that get molds are a Taskmaster Earring, Brass Earring and the Shiny Brass Shield.  I am not sure what you do with the molds.    (Submitted by Caen).
