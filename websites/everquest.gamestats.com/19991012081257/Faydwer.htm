    Ak'Anon
    Level Range:    City
    Typical Creatures:  Decaying Gnome Skeleton, Obsolete Clockworks, Rogue Clockwork, Rock Snakes, Rock Spiderlings
    NPC's and Quests: 27 C Blackbox; Black Box Fragments; Bugglegup; Duster Models; Gearheart; Micro Servos; Red 5 Clockwork; Rusted Black Boxes; Series C Black Boxes; Telescope Lenses;
    Description:   The Home city of the Gnomes.  This is the only place you are allowed to start if you start off as a Gnome.  The city contains a zoo and lots of fun clockwork creatures.  If you get lost, simply ask a clockwork guide to point you the right way.  One of the most beautiful cities in Norrath.  There is also an area of mines that can be pretty tough. A tip for those new to the Mines of Malfunction:  Don't attack the decaying skeletons.  The necromancer guild is in the mines and the guildmaster's pets will help out their decaying friends if they're close enough.  Also, the npc necromancers cast buffs on the wandering decaying skeletons.  A level 1 skeleton with level 40+ buffs on it can ruin your day.

    Ocean of Tears:
    Level Range:      10-30
    Typical Creatures:  Sharks, buccaneers, bandits aviaks, cyclops, Ogres (Moog and Boog), pirates, giants, goblins.
    NPC's and Quests: The Mudtoes; Sentry Xyrinn;
    Description:   The ocean you cross when travelling between Antonica and Faydwer.  Mostly water, but there are several islands with various nasties on them. Karean of Karana, the seafearing dwarf, scouted out this zone as follows:  Monsters by Island:  Erollisi Island: You will find 5-6 high level named NPC's near the dock area. There is also a Dwarven Blacksmith on the west side of the Island with one of his friends. Scatered on the island are some Sisters of Erollisi who con blue and green at level 17. One thing worhty of a note is that the NPCs won't follow you in the water!  There is also a scripted dialog between a Pirate and one of the sisters. Basically, the pirate asks the sister to join their forces, but she refuse so he departs furiously. If you kill him (cons blue to level 17) you get a 'Pirate Earring' which is a Lore Item and is surely related to some quest.  Upon death, he tells you the pirates will avenge him.  Pirate Island: There is a single Pirate on an island right west of the the Erollisi Island.  He was red at level 17 and aggressive. There is supposed to be an ogre related to the Modtoes quest there but I wasn't able to find him. On the Island there is also a shopkeeper selling Magician spells. Cyclops Island: North of the Pirate Island is an island filled with Sea Fury Cyclops. I didn't get near one because they coned red and were aggressives. There seems to be a circle of teleportation for druids on the island as well, but if I was a druid, I wouldn't want to teleport into the middle of 4 cyclops. On the north shore of the Island is a named Giant. I didn't approach him since i'm used to Oasis, but it seems to be peaceful enough. There is supposed to be a second Mudtoe Ogre on this Island but I failed to see him too.  I was too busy dodging Cyclops (more than 8 on the island!).  Death Island: This is supposed to have a couple spectors as well as some gargoyles. I didn't set foot on this one!  Goblin Island: South of the pirate Island, this one is full of Isle Goblins. Don't let them fool you, they all coned red (save for one) at level 17. Beware. Also on the Island are 2 NPCs including an Oracle of some sort. He doesn't seem very good, but tells you that it would be great for him if he had the Tome of Age . He looks kind of evil in his dialoges. His buddy only tells you that "You'll die if you touch the Oracle". Clear enough! First Island from kaladim to Freeport: This island is devoid of everything except an Inn and a Shopkeeper and is southwest of the Aviak Island. Aviak Island: An island full of parrot-looking aviak.  Very colorful! They coned green, blue and white to me (3 different kinds).
