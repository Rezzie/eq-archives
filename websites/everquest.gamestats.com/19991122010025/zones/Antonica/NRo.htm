Northern Desert of Ro:
Level Range:      5-20
Typical Creatures:  Cats, Giant spiders, giant snakes, cutthroats, bandits, orcs, Spiderlings, Fire Beetles, Moss Snakes, Rattlesnakes, Giant Rattlesnakes, Giant Scarabs, Shriveled Mummies, Lesser Mummies, Sand Giants, Desert Tarantulas, Dune Tarantulas, Pumas, Jackals, Madmen, Dorn d'Bynn (Unique monster)
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   Huge outdoor area.   This is a very popular hunting area located just south of Freeport with lots of monsters for people just out of the newbie zones as well as some tougher ones for low to mid level characters.  Because it is relatively featureless, it is easy to get lost here, so try to always know where you are.  Also, there is very little help if you get in trouble, with the only guard post located at the very top of the zone.  There is an inn where you can buy supplies and sell your loot.  Hunting areas include three cutthroat bandit camps and an undead ruins.  The cutthroat camps are usually heavily camped.  Your best bet for killing cutthroats with minimal competition is the third camp at the very bottom of the zone.  One of the cutthroat camps (the first one) has an NPC named Dorn who is occasionally attacked by some fool and then goes on a killing spree until everyone gangs up on him and kills him.  Be alert for the Sand giants who wander the area, generally near the mountains.  Also, for lower levels, be careful of the madmen, since the guards will not kill them for you.
