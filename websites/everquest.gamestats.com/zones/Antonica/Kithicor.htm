Kithicor Forest
Level Range:      25 - 45
Typical Creatures:  [some of these are no longer there] Snakes, Bixies, Fire Beetles, Spiderlings, Skeletons, Zombies, Ghouls, Giant Pirahnas, Young Kodiaks, Giant Scarabs, Giant Spiders, Orc Pawns, Orc Scouts, Grizzly Bears, Werewolf, Giant Bats, Cliff Spiders, Giant Wolves, Shadow Wolves, Black Wolves, Darkweed Snakes, NPC wolf named "A Wolf"; and NPC bear named "Thumper".  New creatures roaming the forest include:  dread wolves, undead clerics, spectres, undead corporals, and various other nasty creatures of the dark.
NPC's and Quests:
Description:    This zone has recently been taken over by a lot of high level undead.  It is now very dangerous to pass through for low levels, especially at night when many of the undead seem to spawn.  Be very cautious going through here. There are several shops and an NPC named Ged who sells some nice gloves if your faction is high enough.  (You can find Ged through his friend Irin.  If you see her walking, she is walking away, if you see her running she is running toward Ged).  The most obvious hunting area is a family of named orcs, who all have the last name of Shralock.  The names of the orcs are as follows:  Bevlock Shralok, Unked Shralok, Kladnog Shralok, Vopuk Shralok, Barg Shralok, Curkur Shralok, Grigga Shralok, Chief Gan' Shralok, Captain Shralok, C' Habrel Shralok, Wrek Shralok.  You would think there was a quest involving these orcs, but I have not heard of any.  As far as I know, the orcs just drop normal loot, but they are generally uncamped and available even on a busy night.  They seem to spawn at different levels ranging from 10 to 20.  There is also a set of ruins with skeletons, zombies and ghouls which is also rarely camped.
