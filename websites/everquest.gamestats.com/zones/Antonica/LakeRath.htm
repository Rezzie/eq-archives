Lake Rathetear
Level Range:      10 - 25
Typical Creatures:  Bandits, gnolls, orcs, skeletons, aviaks, various deepwater goblins.  Sharks and supposedly a sea serpent in the water [Cosmosis claims to have seen the elusive Loch Rathe Monster, but of course has no pictures :)].
NPC's and Quests:
Description:   A lake containing a very dangerous sea serpent and some different camps make this a good hunting area.  There is a tunnel under water which leads to an underwater tower populated by aqua goblins.  There are several chests in the tower, but I don't know what, if anything, is in them.  It is also the home of the Arena.  Be aware that the lake is now full of deepwater goblins, who are a much higher level than the aqua goblins who used to frequent the lake.  Their home is under the island in the center of the lake, but they also roam the rest of the zone.
