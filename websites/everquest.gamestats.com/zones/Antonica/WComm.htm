West Commonlands:
Level Range:  Above level 10
Typical Creatures:   Wolves, giant snakes, pumas, orcs, spiders, cutthroats,
Young Kodiak, Kodiak, Willowisps, Plains cats, Hill Giants, Griffins, Kizdean Gix (Unique monster)
NPC's and Quests: LeatherFoot Raiders;
Description:   Mostly a zone for levels 10 to 17, this also contains a few higher level monsters.  It is also the location of the entrance to the popular Befallen Dungeon.  Hunting areas include cutthroat camps and undead ruins.  This is a good zone for people who have outgrown the Eastern Commonlands and North Ro.  Those hunting here should keep an eye out for the many wandering hill giants and griffins who will atttack and kill any player that gets in their way.
