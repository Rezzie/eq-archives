Dagnor's Cauldron
Level Range:    12 - 25
Typical Creatures:   Giant snakes, orcs, aqua goblins, undertow skeletons, mariners, bosuns, tidal lords.
NPC's and Quests:
Description:    A good (but very dangerous) area to for experience if you're in the upper teens.   Use the NPC camp as a fallback position and scout around from there for undertow skeletons, aqua golblin shamans, mariners and possibly bosuns.  Avoid the tidal lords until around 20 lvl+.   There's a lot of stuff that roams around this area randomly, so care must be taken that you don't get blindsided while fighting something else.  If you do, retreat quickly to the NPC camp and they'll hopefully finish off your problem for you.   A small group (3-4) of 15-20ish can do quite well here with much less danger than a solo, but soloing is possible.  Most everything in this area will emerge from the lake.  A viable strategy for soloing if you have the invis spell is to scout while invis'd, find something you can kill, kill it, invis again and go back to the npc camp to recover.  Goblins have pretty good loot here.  There's an aqua goblin camp further to the south.  However, this would be a tough nut to crack without help from other groups as I've seen at least 1 tidal lord in among the group.
