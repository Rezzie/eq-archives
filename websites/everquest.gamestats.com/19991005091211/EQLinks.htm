General Everquest Links

Affiliate Pages  (Become an Affiliate)

AllaKhazam's Everquest Auctions
    Forget e-bay.  This auction site is specifically designed for the purchase and sale of EQ game-related items, characters and accounts, and anything else Everquest.  The listing fees are cheaper than e-bay and you can navigate through the items by server and type to find exactly what you are looking to purchase.

The Cackling Klaknak
    A treasure trove of Everquest-related humor.  Jokes, comics, stories, idiot files and more.  If you are looking for a place to take a break and get a good laugh, this is the site for you.  Go there and read the satire or post your own on the message board.

Everevent
    [NOTE - Everevent is currently down and being revamped.  It should be back up soon]  A great place to go to to find out what's happening on the various servers.  Each server has a calendar of events so that you can see what is happening and when.  The site is updated constantly with new events and information, and contains message boards to discuss events which are planned or just hoped for.

Mythiran Tower
    The definitive resource for EQ research information.  If you are a magic user and need to know how and when to research your new spells, this is the place to go.  It has lists of information for each class and several well used message boards where you can discuss your problems and concerns.

Hall of Fame Sites

Everquest Home Page
    The Home Page for Everquest.  Now that the final release is here, they are doing a lot with this site.  Verrant keeps adding to this site, and it is starting to be a very useful resource.  There are now a whole set of message boards where you can occasionally get technical and other help from Sony employees.  They also post information about server downtime and potential changes to the game.  They promise to try to keep everyone informed through this site.

Verrant's Official EQ Development Page
    Lists skills, spells, etc that are in development for the game.
Verrant's Official Message Boards
Verrant's Official EQ News Page
Verrant's Technical Support Page
EQVault
    The mother of all EQ sites.  Huge Site with guides, maps, tips and numerous message boards which are actually read by the development team.  The first place to go to get general information about Everquest news and happenings.  The general news section will tell you what you need to know about upcoming events and updates.  The message boards here are very popular and legitimate posts on them are likely to get answered.  Be warned however, that the boards take a long time to load and are very slow to use.

EverQuest Stratics
    Just as good as EQ Vault, and better in some areas.  There is a lot of information on this site.  It is kept up to date and updated several times each day.  The general news section is very useful for keeping up on game updates and events.  This is a very good place for playing and strategy guides, maps and other information. It also has a very popular message board which is frequented by some very knowledgable players who are likely to answer legitimate questions.

EQ'Lizer
    Probably the best spoiler site out there.  Very good site with some excellent information on classes, races, areas and spells, zones, quests and equipment.  If you need information you can't find on this site, this is probably the first place you should go.

Quester of Norrath (Japanese)
    This is definitely the best map site out there.  Unfortunately, it is mostly in Japanese. There is enough English to figure it out and the work is excellent.  Also, he has done an admirable job in trying to translate some of the information into English.
