Go Berzerk
14:36:8 PST
What sounds really cool is the berzerker rage mentioned later in this post.

Hello All,

Well.. thanks to all of your help we've been able to find a significant corpse dissapearence bug. We feel that this is a significant enough problem to warrant updating the servers during normal hours today, as we don't want anyone else's corpses to dissapear. We've also extended the amount of online time characters above 5th level have to recover their bodies from 8 hours to 24 hours (online).

In nutshell, the Corpse Recovery rules are as follows:

Characters All Levels: If you have no items on your corpse, it will dissapear within 3 minutes.

Characters Level 1-5: Corpse will dissapear within 30 minutes (real time) whether you are online or offline. Those of you wondering why this rule is in place? - There would be WAY to many bodies if we didn't do it this way.

Characters Level 6 and above - Corpse will dissapear within 24 hours if the player is online (being at the character select screen STILL counts as online). Corpses will dissapear within 1 week if the player is offline.

In addition, this server patch will include the following:

1) Warrior's now get a "Bezerker Frenzy" if they are down to under 35% of their Hit Points.
2) Several server side crash bugs have been fixed.

I also wanted to mention that we have begun shipping units to Europe. Units should be in Electronics Boutique in the UK in a few days. Germany and other places in Europe will be hitting shortly...and Austrailia will be seeing units shortly as well.

John Smedley

Gnome-Elf Join In Matrimony...Ewwww
11:16:18 PST
Regardless of what the children will look like congradulations to the them and their pursuit of racial harmony (I'm taking bets on how long it will last though :)
Thanks to Raene for the news!

Annwyl Anklestabber and Kinien Northoak celebrated the joining of their lives in stunning fashion yesterday, April 17th, on the Rathe Server. The joyous event between a gnome and a wood elf took place in ButcherBlock near the Chessboard. Mills Lifestream performed the ceremony, Midoc Holywrath was best man and Taoism Wyndfist was maid of honor.

Citizens from all over Norrath traveled to the event. In attendance were dark elves, ogres, gnomes, wood elves, high elves, and humans. Only trolls seemed to be absent...one wonders if they do not celebrate marriage, or if they merely came disguised. High level Druids came in their Spirit Wolf forms, and many diverse styles of armor and apparel could be seen.

Guests disembarking at the Kaladim port from far off evil lands were given flags of truce and invised past the guards. They were also given safe travel instructions around the edge of the zone to the wedding. Unfortunately, there was one casualty when an invised dark elf ran too close to one of the port guards. Sensing something not right, Guard Clendin struck the air with his axe and felled the dark one. High Elf Cleric Ariel Moon arrived on scene for ressurection. When all guests were in attendance, the wedding began.

Attendees were asked to hold their spells until the ceremony was over. When Mills announced that the groom could bend down and kiss the bride the enthused crowd began their spell casting.

The reception followed and featured many types of brewed beverages, a drunken footrace, and the occaisional taunting and death of goblins. A ripping good time was had by all! (except maybe the goblins)

Pictures and full report can be viewed at the Rathe Rhapsody.
