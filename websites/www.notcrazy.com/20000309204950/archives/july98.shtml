    High Res = Lag ?
    Wrong. Although I knew that resoloution is an option set by the client and has no effect on the amount of data being sent to and from the server, Brad still had to clear this up for a few people.

    So, the question is: Does high res cause more lag if only using a modem (assume very fast machine and SLI voodoo2)? Does more information need to cross the phone lines if in high-res? ~~ The Professor

    The resolution you run EQ in has ZERO to do with internet lag -- the exact same amount of information is being passed between your client and the server. ~~ Brad

    Thayer Calls it Quits
    Today, Thayer, former author of EQSS, called it quits after posting an exclusive screenshot taken from Everquest Extreme that resulted in a multi-site boycott aimed at the popular Everquest site. In his parting letter to DenDragon, the lead editor and designer of the Stratics network, Thayer said:

    "They win...if you haven't seen the news, I supposedly wronged them...they started a boycott, and although I could care less about the whole thing, I am quitting EQSS."*
    * Excerpt taken from the EQSS web page.


    Some say that the boycott was a direct result of the plagaristic tendancies of Thayer. Tendancies that spanned the time frame of at least several months. Over the past 24 hours many examples of such plagarism have been flung around by sites participating in the boycott, including this one. All those that participated in the boycott did not intend for Thayer to give up his post, they simply wanted him to see the error of his ways... However, Thayer is gone, and now ColdRain has taken his place. With any luck EQSS will remain a fine site with some journalistic ethics under ColdRains suprivision...

    Trolls ala Milo
    Since the public has never really seen any up-close and detailed pictures of a troll, I asked Milo Cooper, 3D character artist for EverQuest, for a better description. This is what he replied:

    They're about nine or ten feet tall, they have classic Hallowe'en witch faces (i.e. long nose, jutting chin, mean expression, etc.), they have big, pointy ears, they're bald, they have very long, almost gorilla-like arms, they have somewhat stumpy but muscular legs, they're a little fat, they hunch when they walk, they have big feet, their bodies sport various scars and pustules, they have broad shoulders, and they're green.

    Thanks for the new info Milo...
