NoT CraZy started with playing Jedi Knight. A bunch of guys met up in Chat on Jedi Knight.Net

Most of us used to play together back in the beta days of Ultima Online, we still play Ultima Online, but the Jedi Knight faction is pretty much defunct.

Those of us in the guild that are still together have applied for the Everquest beta test.

As of right now, all the members of the guild, with two exceptions, live in Las Vegas and have been Real Life friends for some time.

We are all big fans of Monty Python, and you may notice the occasional reference to them. The most prominant reference is the Holidae Snaps... this came from their "Nudge Nudge" skit (nudge nudge, know what i mean, say no more.) (is your wife interested in... photography? Oh! holidae snaps!? Could be, could be taken on a holiday.)

This page originally started out as a guild page for my firends and I. The guild had started back in 1997 when we all started playing Jedi Knight (Lucasarts). The guild expanded to other games such as Ultima Online, for which most of us were beta testers. We quickly grew tired and disappointed with the quality of the game. The game just wasn't as fun as it was back in the beta days.. I think those of you who played in the beta and in the final version of the game can agree with me on this. Anyways, being disappointed with the game, the guild quickly fell apart. Each of us went of and started playing our own games.. for some of us it was Starcraft for others it was Total Annialation, and still others went to Forsaken. After the guild died, I changed this page from a guild-oriented page, to a diehard everquest page. Everquest not only promises to be the best game of our time, but it also promises to get the guild back together again, for all of us have signed up for the beta test. As for myself, I am still seeking to make this page a success, as many of its other Everquest news page brethren have become. Please stop by frequently, the page is updated as close to daily as possible. I hope you enjoy browsing this page as much as I did creating it.
