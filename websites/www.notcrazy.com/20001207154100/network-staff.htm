Brent Bussey: VP of GameFan Network Operations. Brent does all of the handling of the day to day operation of GameFan Network. He is also the network administrator. He makes sure all of the services we provide are working correctly, and smoothly.

Ben Hamilton: Network Site Director. Ben is the man who has his finger on the pulse of the Site Network. He also is the man who keeps tabs on the latest Site Network happenings and reports them to you on the front page, on a day-to-day basis.

Robert Howarth: News director for the GameFan Network. Apache enjoys sports, fitness, poetry and gaming. When not posting news on network sites he loves reading a good book and playing TeamFortress.

Wes Strait: Hosting Manager. Wes is the guy you have to go through first if you want to become hosted here at GameFan. He takes all the requests for hosting and filters out the ones GameFan would be interested in hosting. He also maintains such services like: E-Mail accounts, our FTP server, and our IRC server.
