Humans are the race that is considered normal... It is the race by which all other races are judged. While humans do not excel in any particular feature, and are average in every way, they are the only race that has every class avalible for them to choose from upon character creation. Humans are also the only race that has the monk class avalible to them. Trolls and Ogres do not get along with, and are the enemies of all elves and humans.
