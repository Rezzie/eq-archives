The "evil" brethren of the Woodland and High Elves. Their skin is blueish-black, their hair white, and they share the pointed ears and sharp features of their Elven brethren. Due to their life under the earth, and dedication to evil gods, they have the ability to see perfectly in the dark with an innate ability called "ultravision", which creates a sort of "black light effect". This innate ability is a must, as the underground city of Neriak is the Dark Elves' home town. Neriak is also believed to be the origin of Necromancy.
