First, Sanguine is doing really well these days and is far advanced from a mere 9th mage.

Second, I agree with everyone turning a cold shoulder to the equipment beggars until they wise up. Would they ask for your watch or car in real life, even if they were young or down on their luck? Nay. A cup of coffee maybe...

Third, it's easier to stay in character when you at least owe a debt to someone for some bandages, muffins, or a healing spell rather than owe them for giving your 3rd level barbarian a combined halberd. What's the point of playing when the challenge is taken away? Verant has done a great job keeping the challenge in every level. If you are playing along the lines the game was designed on (no deus ex machina) then coming up with a sufficient payback for a higher level character that's helped you should be a challenge in itself.

For example, I can't manage to repay Sanguine because the crazy erudite has gone and left me behind in levels, lol.

If you are interested in skipping over the parts of the game that challenge the rest of us, find another kOOl dOOd and beg him for his old rusty combined halberd he was about to throw away.
