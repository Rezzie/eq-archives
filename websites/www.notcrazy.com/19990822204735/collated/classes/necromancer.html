Race - Class Chart


These are the dark brotherhood of the arcane arts, dabbling in death. Through evil spiritually based spells, they are able to animate dead bodies and skeletons to do their bidding, as well as having spells which leach life from their victims. It is said that the greatest of necromancers might obtain a way to obtain eternal life in the for of an undead lich, a undead being with all the power of the necromancer.
