The GameFan Network provides hosting services and an advertisement program for sites that meet set guidelines and follow all rules, regulations, and requirements set out by the network and its hosting administration.

Hosting Guidelines

  • Sites must be original, both in concept and design.
  • Your site must offer a unique product or service, and may not be similar to other sites in the network. (Some circumstances may warrant these guidelines to be modified, email the hosting manager to check if you are unsure.)
  • Sites must be updated in a timely manner.
  • GameFan requires a signed contract for hosting. This contract lays out all rules and details the services we provide, and also guarantees your 50% of ad revenue generated from your site. If you are not 18, your parent’s signature on the contract will be required for you to join the affiliate program.
  • A minimum amount of 100,000 unique page views a month is required to be hosted and to participate in the ad revenue program.

Rules, Restrictions, and Requirements

Advertising must displayed either as a graphic frame or a fixed banner, as more particularly described below. The display of the advertisement must conform to the specifications set forth below. Failure of the advertisement to conform to specifications is grounds for termination.

If you choose to display the advertisement in a graphic frame, the frame must appear at the top or bottom of the display screen and must be in view at all times.

If you choose to display the advertisement without the use of a frame, then the advertisement must appear in a fixed banner within the upper third of the initial screen view of each page on which it appears, including but not limited to your home page and at least than fifty percent (50%) of all pages at the site, including each of its top ten (10) most trafficked pages.

Your site will carry the GAMEFAN or GAMEFAN ONLINE logo, as provided by GAMEFAN from time to time, and a hot link at the top of your home page. You also agree not to remove or in any way alter the content of the GAMEFAN or GAMEFAN ONLINE frame or banner.

You agree to make a good faith effort to promote GAMEFAN ONLINE and the companies that are advertised there.

You can not display pornographic or other sexually explicit material on your web site (or provide a link to any other site which does so). You also agree that you shall not display, or provide a link to any site which displays any content that is in violation of any United States federal law or regulation or violates any applicable state or local law.

You are solely responsible for the development, operation and maintenance of your web site and for all materials appearing on it and that GAMEFAN shall have the right to monitor such site to determine that this condition is being met. Any link connecting users of your web site shall in no way alter the look, feel or functionality of the GAMEFAN web site.

GameFan has the exclusive right to sell all advertising on your web site, including buttons and all fixed advertising.

During the term of your hosting, if you are presented with an offer from a third party to purchase your site, you shall immediately notify GAMEFAN and GAMEFAN shall have the right for 45 days to agree to purchase AFFILIATE on the same terms as presented in said offer.

The terms of the specific agreement with your site are to be kept confidential.
