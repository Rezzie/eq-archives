By now Im sure you have a second question.. What does all this have to do with EverQuest?
Well.. First and foremost, Thievery should be fair. If a person opts to be a theif in the game, their stealing capabilities should not be limited to wether or not the person is flagged as a pk or not. There should be one exception to this, and that is if the person is non pk. If they are non pk, they should only be allowed to steal from NPCs and other Non-pks, since a pk can not retaliate against a non-pk. But if the person is pk, they should be able to steal from anyone, since pks can retaliate, and non-pks wouldnt be able to retaliate anyways, since they cannot attack anyone, not just one specific group of people.

Rogues (thieves) should also have the ability to climb walls. Now, i dont mean they should be able to go climb the world trade center, but they should be able to climb trees and climb over walls, or climb onto the roof of a small house. What they can climb should basically depend on the surface they are trying to climb, for example, a brick wall is easier to climb than a wooden wall.

Bards should also be adept at climbing, but not as good at it s the rogue. Bards are travellers. They climb hills and mountains just to get wherever they are going. Bards should also be a useful, undespensable class. Their songs should all sound unique, not just a few chords that are only good at annoying people. The songs should also have very useful and noticable effects on party members or others in the area. For those of you who have played The Four Crystals of Trazere know how indespensable the bard was. It should be the same in EverQuest. Bards should not be like they were in UO. They should actually be useful.
