A Bards Tale


" Hail fair traveler.  Sit and be welcome. Stay near the fire this eve and enjoy some mulled wine and a tale  of a journey."  The pudgy bard leans back onto the chair, and with calm assurance says " There has been a report of a disturbance near the village of Qwilln."

My friends, let me, Taleisin, tell you the true tale...".


Chapter 1

As the wind rose from the peaks in the distance, the clouds overhead began to shift. Away went the dark clouds and they were replaced by the warming sun. Off in the distance, the forest loomed. A dark feeling passed through Montibank's soul each and every time that he glanced in that direction.. On the road ahead, a village appeared. "About time" Monti mumbled to himself. It had been a long week for Monti. He had been chasing a renegade magician for weeks now. As he rode closer to the village, he noted the outlying farms. The crops were ripening, the autumn season was upon them. "Just a little while longer old friend" he said to his mare as he rubbed her neck.

A stranger was coming. His heart quickened. "Mama mama mama" Randle cried as he sprinted towards the front door. "A man. is coming"

Shar, the townswoman, looked outside. A stranger indeed. She quickly wrote a note on some parchment and told Randle to run to the mayors house.  Watching her son run, she bolted the windows and barred the shutters. Then she went out back and rang the calling bell. Her man Rello looked up.

"What now" moaned Rello as he heard the bell. " She wants another..." He looked up and stopped in mid-sentance. She was shutting the house up.

Must be danger, he thought. Rello looked down the road. Seeing naught but a man on horse, he wondered what the woman was being foolish for.   He quickly dipped his hands in the wash-water barrel and went out to meet the man.

 


Chapter 2

The Mayor of Qwilln was drinking some apple brandy in the tavern. He heard a commotion outside and wondered what it was. The brandy looked more inviting, so he quelled his urge to look and took another swallow of the fiery liquid. "Ahhh, Marsellos makes the best".

Jav fancied himself as a guard. He could handle a sword without cutting himself, and he was strong. Currently he was guarding the tavern door,  trying to let the mayor get some peace and quiet. It wasn't his fault that some brat kid was screaming for the mayor.  So after a few minutes of looking mean and trying to quiet the kid, he stormed inside the tavern. "Mayor" he bellowed, slamming fist to chest. The mayor winced, "Mayor, this kid, Randle wants to speak with you."

The mayor motioned for the child to come closer.. "Your Rello and Shar Tralnic's child, ain't you?" he questioned.

Randle nodded, then without a word, handed the Mayor a piece of parchment.

The Mayor read the note, and called out to Jav. "Get in here you oaf.."

Jav, inside the smoky tavern, and drinking some ale, spills it all over himself in an attempt to drink it before answering the mayor. "Here
sir. "

"Gather the men and meet me in the town square" commanded the mayor. Seeing Jav salute, he winced and motioned the man to go. "Now son, why don't you stay here."

Randle, still silent, nodded again, and wandered into the kitchen. The cook offered him some warm brown bread and Randle began to eat it.

The mayor watched as Rello and the stranger, walked towards the assembled men. They seemed to be chatting amicably. The stranger slid off his horse, and stretched his back.  " unh! "  broke from his lips.

Rello said " Mayor, this is Gallin."

Monti smiled at the perpetuation of his lie.

"Gallin" asked the mayor. "What is your business here?"

"I am just passing through, looking for a friend".

"What friend might that be".

"A master magician" smiled Monti

"He be the one that the dwarves are looking for as well" called out a man. "They paid me hard gold for information of Magician Rashmar."

The mayor turned in surprise, looking for the man who had spoken. "Culli, why didn't you tell us?"

"T'was only yesterday" Culli called back, " And ye know that the Magi left late last night." the man justified.

The mayor turned white at the secret being so loosely thrown about.

"What Dwarves" spat Monti. "Did the leader have red hair and a black beard?"

Not thinking, Culli answered " Aye".

"Damn that Hulf" shouted Monti. "Which way did they go?" he yelled at Culli.

Culli, thinking to make a profit, came back with " 10 gold and I tell ya "

Monti moved faster than any of the villagers thought that a man could move. Suddenly a knife was pressed into the soft flesh of Culli's neck.

"Tell me or you die"

Jav stared for a second then pulled his sword and swung at the stranger. In mid-swing, something hot penetrated his left eye. *THUD*  he fell to the ground.

"Tell me" Monti screamed at Culli after dispatching Jav.
