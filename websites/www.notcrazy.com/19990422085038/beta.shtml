Other Beta Facts

  • Official Release Date: March. 16, 1999

  • Phase 4 is slated to begin early December and will not require an NDA, A small fee is needed.

  • Phase 3 should start in mid-November, NDA will still be in effect.

  • Phase 3 will involve approximately 5,000 testers

  • Phase 2 Beta CD's are in the process of being mailed as of September 4th, 1998.

  • Phase 2 will introduce 800 more testers into the EverQuest Beta.

  • Phase 1 consisted of 200 testers and started on July 16th, 1998.

  • Phase .05 had 40 testers and started after E3 (June, 1998).

  • There were over 40,000 beta applications.

  • Phase 3 and beyond will not require an NDA.

  • You're not allowed to state that you're in the beta, it violates the NDA.
