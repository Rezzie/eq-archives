INTERVIEW: July 19th, 2001 (2200)

Being KEG's only roving interviewer (wtf...anchorman?  reporter?  Hmm not sure.), Sanctume dispatched me to Innothule Swamp for a "surprise interview".  My instructions were to look for someone quite large, hairy and smelly (No not Snaggle).  Apparently Sanct wanted me to search out Khistrich's Mom and interview her!  Being wise to Sanc's wily ways, I headed to Innothule.  Here's what I found:

Mareth:  Excuse me Maam...err you ARE a maam right?

Vicious Brute:  RAAAAAAAA!!!

Mareth:  Feh!  Dude.  Here...have some Eclipse.

Mareth:  Nm.  I'll ask this dude...

Ogre:  Yes?

Mareth:  I'm lookin for Khistrich's mom.

Ogre:  Dats me.

Mareth:  Uhh.

Khistrich's Mom:  Whut?

Mareth:  You look like a dude.

Khistrich's Mom:  WHUT?  Me purdy!

Mareth:  Right.  Anyhow can I ask you a few questions?

Khistrich's Mom:  Ya, me likes purdy barb men!

Mareth:  I'm sure you do.

Khistrich's Mom:  Me bash them and then take dem back to Oggok fer lovins.

Mareth:  That is....frightening.

Khistrich's Mom:  At first dey don't like it but me just bash dem till dey gimmie luvs.

Mareth:  Anyhow about those questions...

Khistrich's Mom:  Ya?  Say....yer right purdy yerself!

Mareth:  No I'm not.

Khistrich's Mom:  Ya youse are!  Youse needs some gud lovins!

Mareth:  No I don't.

Khistrich's Mom:  Youse tinks dat youse enough man fer me?

Mareth:  No.

Khistrich's Mom:  Dats what me son's pa said...till me bashed him into lovin me!

Mareth:  No need for bashing.

Khistrich's Mom:  Youse follows me den?  Me gots ranger suit you can wear for me!

Mareth:  Uhh.  I had some questions....

Khistrich's Mom:  Me heard yer called "The Tool".

Mareth:  Dear god, please no...

Khistrich's Mom:  Me takes youse back to Oggok or me BASH YOU!

Mareth:  Look I just had some questions about your son, if you could just spare a moment of your time...

Khistrich's Mom:  Me son standing behind youse!

Mareth:  Khis is here?  Where?

*BASH!!!!*

Editors Note:  I got away, needless to say.  "Cuddles" and I still write to each other, we didn't part on bad terms.....unless you call me sowing and running away bad terms.
