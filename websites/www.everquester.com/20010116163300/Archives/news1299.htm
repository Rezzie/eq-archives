I have updated the Fear Break-in map to show an improved run route. The survivability of running this route is about 40% compared to the previous 20%. It should steer you towards the Tentacle Tormentors (they do not see invisible players), and away from the Scarelings, Spiders, and Shiverbacks (these will see you while invisible). This is the route I have run on the past 8 raids or so. I have always made it to the camp (sometimes I died there, sometimes I arrived unseen and camped, but I have always made it).
