April 17th & 18th, 2000

Monday, it was a rough start on the first night of the raid. But things went smooth after the divine intervention of a guide/gm help summon the derange wizard Majdar that kept going after his corpse. The troops rallied and have slain Dracoliche for the evening.

Tuesday, the second night was well underway until Cazic Thule announced that he will renew his realm within 30 minutes. The knights thought it would be great fun to charge the god of fear with along with half the population. Five minutes into the world's renewal, a call to arms ended in carnage that was a sight to behold.

The Knights of Eternal Good and friends once more rallied to execute an efficient clearing of the plane of fear within five hours--its goal slaying of Cazic Thule.

Dracoliche yielded Bone Razor and Staff of Undead Legion on his first death, and then a Staff of Witherskin on his second death.

Cazic Thule yielded the following treasures: Soul Leech and two Earthcrafter's Staves.
