April 12th, 2000

Tuesday, the heat eminating from Nagafen's lair caught the attention of the Knights of Eternal Good and friends. Lord Nagafen was slain by twenty-eight adventurers.

Lord Nagafen yielded the following treasures: three Treasure Hunter's Satchels, Book of Scale, Cloak of Flames, Goldplated Koshigatana, and a Diamond.

This news, however, marks the growing presence of a false diety, also known as Crusader's of Plilo.
