April 17th, 2000

Early Monday morning, The Knights of Eternal Good and friends have slain Lord Nagafen.

Lord Nagafen yielded the following treasures: two Gauntlet of Fiery Might, Orb of Tishan, two Prayer of Life, and Goldplated Koshigatana.

A few days prior to the assault of Lord Nagafen, there were dark-elf rangers sighted molesting imps in Sol B.
