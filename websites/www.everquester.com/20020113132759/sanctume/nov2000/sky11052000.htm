November 3rd-4th, 2000

The Knights of Eternal Good and invited friends enjoyed a weekend in the Plane of Sky.

We in KEG also we welcome our friend who joined our ranks: Kathrynn, High Elf Enchanter.

Congratulations to the many who were able to find treasures while up here (some of the list): Felwyn's Symbol of Marr, Nagatsun's Treant Fist and Monk Haste Belt, Aledark's Bard Haste Belt, Fitz' completion of the rogue Transparent Mask quest, Mill's Ranger Haste Belt, Fawnwind's Warrior haste Belt, and Esmeralda's Weight of the Gods.
