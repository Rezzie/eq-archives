March 27th, 2000

On this Monday night, The Knights of Eternal Good and friends had a successful raid in the Plane of Hate.

Many treasures have been rewarded to strengthen the armors of those who attended:

  • Indocolite Breastplates (warrior) were awarded to Sanctumeo and Vladimire.
  • Indolicite Bracer (warrior) was awarded to Deemoness.
  • Ethreal Mist Helms (cleric) were awarded to Amoura and Foodawg.
  • Ethreal Mist Greaves (cleric) was awarded to Amoura.
  • Prismatic Shields (cleric) were awarded to Ferlin and Drogo.
  • A Shadowknight Shield was awarded to Dellik.
  • Shaman Breastplate and Greaves were awarded to Rocco.
  • Elfane Spellfizzle received the Enchanter Robe.
