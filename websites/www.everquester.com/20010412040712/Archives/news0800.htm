August 25th, 2000

On Saturday September 2nd, 2000, The Knights of Eternal Good will be venturing to Old Sebilis to slay the dread wurm Trakanon. The raid will begin at 3pm EST. The raid will be very limited for attendence, we will be killing Trakanon with as few people as possible. Our strength will be in tactics, organization, and exceution of plan - in other words "KEG-style". All groups will be pre-assigned, and once everyone is signed up, all participants will be expected to have the required equipment and know "The Plan".

Members and non-members alike should post under the Trakanon forum (on the Bulletin Board) if you are interested in joining the raid. On Monday, a seperate forum (password protected) will be created to discuss the tactics involved and equipment required for the raid.
