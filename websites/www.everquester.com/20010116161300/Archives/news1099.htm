October 31, 1999

The Knights Of Eternal Good returned to Kedge Keep last Thursday to slay Phingel Atropos. This time, he dropped a Rod of Malisement. We also found a few new items off the named shark, Cauldronboil, more details are forthcoming.

KEG returned to Fear on Saturday with a much smaller group and managed to clear out Fear. There are a number of new weapons and armor that the various mobs drop (too many to list here - check EQ'Lizer). I will mail any screenshots of new equipment to EQ'Lizer if I find their listings are lacking.

October 17th, 1999

Combined guilds of the Rathe Server joined forces in the Plane of Fear to defeat Cazic Thule. More notable than the slaying of the vile god, was that it was accomplished by feuding guilds setting aside their differences and focusing on a common goal. Cazic Thule yielded a Crimson Robe of Alendine, an Amulet of Necropotence, and two Darkwood Boxes as loot. The Guilds in attendence were: Knights of Eternal Good, Brotherhood of The Spider, Clan Icewind, House Everhate, Legends, Emef Aitch, Black Talons, and The Council of Darkness.

  1. Stalking Cazic Thule
  2. Barshing Cazic Thule
  3. Death of Cazic Thule

Special thanks to the original 18 who sacrificed their lives over and over to secure a safe area around the entrance portal -- you know who you are.
