September 2nd, 2000

Excerpt from the life of Santume SpiritStone, Cleric of Brell.

The previous night's victory over Cazic Thule led to a celebration that depleted Sacntume's private reserves of Dwarven Ale. The festivity that lasted into the day break was cut short as Call to Arms was raised.

The grumpiness of the dwarven cleric did not come from that prospect of today's battle, but from the bad news that the weekly shipment of Kaladim's finest, Dwarven Ale, was not forthcoming because Timorous Deep was infested by a dragon.

Today's Call to Arms is a campaign to lay siege deep in Old Sebilis for the return of the foul poison-dragon,Trakanon. Today is also a very important moment because their friends have joined the ranks of the Knights of Eternal Good:

Aledark, Wood-Elf Bard
Autumnmoon, Wood-Elf Druid
Fazannya, Human Paladin
Jackko, Dark-Elf Rogue
Khistrich, Barbarian Warrior

Today's siege campaign was well fought given the puzzle in which Trakanon's lair provides. Today, the Knights of Eternal Good sacrificed many deaths and much time. In these sacrifices, KEG gathered and learned more knowledge, KEG tested each other's respect and patience, and KEG strengthened the forge of friendship that will carry on to more future adventures.

End of Excerpt.
