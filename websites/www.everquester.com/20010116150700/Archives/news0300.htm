    March 8th, 2000

    Guild leaders from the Knights of Eternal Good, Brotherhood of the Spider, Vanquished Souls, Emef Aitch, and Clan Icewind will be hosting a Plane of Fear Raid on March 17th, 1999. The odd part of this fear raid will be that it is designed for those level 47-50 players who have never had the opportunity to go to Fear plane. I have seen a great deal of level 47+s who just can't get in on a Fear raid because they are not associated with one of the "power" guilds on the server. We aim to give some of these gamers a chance to experience Fear.

    The lead group will consist of: Yukitaro, Mistabone, Stygar, Sargaras, Mikaela (yes, I know she isn't the MFH guildmaster but she's an officer), and Snaggle. The other groups will be composed of Fear virgins (for lack of a better term). If we don't get enough virgins to sign up, we will fill the remaining slots with guildmembers from the various guilds. This raid will also hopefully strengthen inter-guild relations between our guilds. I was extremely pleased when the leadership of these guilds agreed to come together to do something fun for the Rathe community.
