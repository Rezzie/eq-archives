August 26th, 2000

Excerpt from the life of Santume SpiritStone, Cleric of Brell.

The port of Firiona Vie was bustling with activity which is very common on for a Saturday morning. A young pilgrim from the docks carefully carried a large crate into the nearby housing. There she set her heavy burden and picked a pouch full of coins.

It was Saturday indeed because the weekly shipment of Kaladim's finest, Dwarven Ale, have arrived to his doorsteps. The unsmiling dwarf opened the crate with practiced ease. He broke the wax seal, uncorked the brown bottle, and proceeds to drink right out of the bottle. "Ahh, such sweet taste of home," says Sanctume, as he enters the house with the new supply of booze.

Just when Sanctume was about to start on his third bottle of ale, the courier arrived with several scrolls. On one particular scroll relayed the following message...

To all the Knights of Eternal Good:

The Votes have been casted, and the Results have been confirmed by Sanctume SpiritStone and Kulvarian.
Your presence is requested for today's gathering at Emerald Jungle, in the continent of Kunark.
Let us welcome our friends as they join our ranks!

signed,

Jerrica Evilbringer, Officer of KEG

The Cleric of Brell donned his armor, prayed for his blessings, and kissed his wives good bye. The day was full of hope, and will be full of celebration because the ale will flow freely. Even news of Marklin, his friend, was coming home from a far away journey. It is a good day for a celebration!

The way to Emerald Jungle was made haste from the magical winds of the Druids. Many were already there cheering and laughing. Today, they celebrate the union of their friends into the ranks of the Knights of Eternal Good:

Bradan, Dark-Elf Necromancer
Rhen, Human Monk
Squido, Wood-Elf Ranger
Vashon, Wood-Elf Bard
Winkin, Gnome Enchanter

Today's celebration was blessed with the presence and challenge of Severilous, a foul Shaman-Dragon, in the bowels of Emerald Jungle. Today, the Knights of Eternal Good sacrificed a handful deaths as they learn more and gather tactics to defeat the beast in the days to come.

Did you really think that today's celebration will only consist of friendly cheers and good booze? A KEG celebration is good barshing, spells fizzling, and corpse piling. It is one of the ingredience at work that makes us best, and bonds our friendship even more stronger.
