Outpost, Firiona Vie

Before the light of the sun breaks the darkest of dawn, there were already bustling activites from the bank to the docks. Among the busy was a dwarf hauling many precious pieces from the Plane of Sky and many parchments of magical scroll. Two local workers follow behind with crates of Kaladim's finest, dwarven ale.

The dwarven cleric had plans to journey back to Freeport in preparation for the coming tournament and had just finished donning his armor when Krieger, nephew of Marklin Bootsy came with urgent news. A sealed scroll:

Sanctume,

A conflict brews as you read this message. Our siege upon Trakanon coincided with another force wearing arachnid insignia. I request your immediate precense and speak with their leaders.

Signed,

Marklin Bootsy, Officer of KEG

Drow's Lair, Plane of Irc

Communication was polite and a resolution has been agreed upon by the leaders of the two forces involved.
