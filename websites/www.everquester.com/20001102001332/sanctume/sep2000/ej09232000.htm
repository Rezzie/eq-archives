September 23rd, 2000

Excerpt from the life of Santume SpiritStone, Cleric of Brell.

It is Saturday morning and Sanctume awoke longing for his usual shipment of dwarven ale, which was nowhere to be seen. Ofcourse, the dwarf has only been a high priest last Sunday, and he has not been back to his home in the outpost of Firiona Vie. The past week was filled with hard fought battles, and the grand day was just beginning; still, it hinted challenge.

It is great news that Bard Vashon has completed his Test of Tone from the Plane of Sky today. A scroll accompanied this describing the Mask of the Songbird he received:

Today was also a great day for rejoicing because the ranks of the Knights of Eternal Good will be filled by friends:

Cinexa, High-Elf Wizard
Esmeralda, Dark-Elf Cleric
Lewick, Wood-Elf Ranger
Sandrak, Barbarian Shaman

Alas, the evening came and a call to arms was raised for all to meet in Emerald Jungle once again. The events that followed was greatly expressed by Lady Megwedariel.

"On Saturday, September 23rd, the Knights of Eternal Good and friends gathered in the wilds of the Emerald Jungle to face a terrible foe, Severilous. The green dragon strolled over the hills of the jungle, unaware that his life was about to end. The charge was called, and the Snaggle Maneuver(tm) put into play. Only a small number of our comrades fell to his terrible breath and razor claws. It was a grueling battle, but in the end Severilous fell to our blades and magicks, his body nothing more than a rumpled heap on the mossy ground. His corpse yielded one Runed Bolster Belt (Megwedariel), one Green Dragon Scale (Snaggle), 2 Shields of the Green Dragon (Sanctume, & unkown at the time), and several spell scrolls (entrapping roots, demilich, avatar and legacy of thorns). Congratulations to the Knights of Eternal Good and friends for ridding Norrath of this beast." -- Megwedariel
