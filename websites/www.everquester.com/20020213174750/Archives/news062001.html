NEWS: JUNE 20th, 2001, 12:05pm (PST)

The Barb

So I'm sitting at home watchin the game when the phone rings. Being the good Barb I am I pick it up. "Hello?" I say. The voice on the other yet is kind, yet strangely familiar. It said "KEG needs you!". Strange I thought, so I asked what the voice meant exactly. "KEGNews needs updating." Which was odd because I was fired from KEGNews back in march. I was too rude to the guests, so was their excuse. That hasn't stopped the legions of fans from filling my email daily with love. So needless to say I am back.

So what's new with KEG? Funny you should ask since just last night I was speaking on the very subject to a group of newbs Felwethe. I would start rattling off the things that have transpired since Kathrynn's last update but they are too great and too many. I will mention that on the 17th KEG joined forced with a small group who calls themselves the "Defenders of the Gypsy Queen" to topple a long time foe named Glynus Tyndul.

I have also been informed that several KEG members have vanished and not been seen in quite awhile. The disappearance strangle seems connected to the release of the new Game Boy Advance. If you see any of them take away the gameboy and tell them we love them.

Summer is here and some of us welcome the heat...well not really the heat but atleast the season. The season full of pool parties where you always have that one kid who urinates in the pool. The season full of beach outings where you stay out in the sun to long and give yourself 3rd degree burns. The season full firefly catching, and not to mention babe watching. Start stocking up on sun tan lotion and buy that new swim suit you've been looking at cause summer only comes once a year. Of course that doesn't mean you shouldn't stay up to the wee hours of the night playing EQ and sleeping past noon each day. Hell thats what summer is all about! So relax and have some fun! This is the Barb signing off.

-- Viva La Barb
