Noel's Bachelorette Party
Sep 16, 2000

Noel had a special night attended by many bards.  The first five bards included Bazzy, Gance, Tluin, Belfalas, and Vashon.  As word spread of these bards shaking their moneymakers and several drunk KEG ladies, more bards showed up, led by Bargilo (aka Lewick).  Soon, Juwannadance, Istripfornoel, Wantneass, Fith, Idancefornoel, Dirkd, and Medancegud (a level one ogre!) all showed up.  One thing we learned is Deemoness, Annwyl, Jerrica, Winterwind, and Noel can't hold their liquor!

Special thanks to Deemoness and Megwedariel for organizing and inviting everyone.
