August 22nd, 2000

The Knights of Eternal Good and friends spent the evening in lower Sebilis poking Trakanon with a 100 foot stick.

While upper Sebilis is populated heavily by many of Norrath's denizens, KEG proceeded below past mushroom land, and scouting the lair of Trakanon. A foreshadowing perhaps?

There are Sebilite Juggernauts and Reet Frogloks dwell in these foul caves and fortress, and provide a good challege for our Knights.

One of tonight's highlights was this battle scene, a sebilite protector in camp.
