Take a deep breath, put those flamethrowers away please I don't have my fire resist gear with me =P
There were 4 different options available to all of us for the planes.

a) status quo
A lot of people aren't happy with status quo atm. For whatever reasons. Everybody pays their 10$ to Verant a month, and the ones who aren't happy are entitled to their say as well. Status quo for some, is not an option right now. I think for most of us. From my time as calendar admin, and the /tells I received in game, the majority of them weren't from uber guilds who were unhappy with what was going on in the planes. Nobody is in agreeance on anything at the moment. We can't even agree on the smallest rules in regards to the planes.

b) Absolute FFA.
There are guilds in favour of wiping the calendar, taking it down, and going FFA right now. KEG is not one of them. Anybody who knows me, and what I have tried to do with this Calendar in terms of rules and fairness knows I am totally against absolute FFA. We *all* lose. And setting up a Calendar precluding certain guilds from posting, and other guilds being in FFA mode won't work either. We all know, Verant does *not* support any Calendar system of any kind. There will be nothing stopping guilds from zoning in on days others have booked on a Calendar. Nor do they support any one guild *owning* a zone, such as "first come first serve" FFA system. FFA will mean anybody can zone in at anytime with any amount of people to set up shop. We all don't want this headache.

c) Patch day FFA.
This I am also against. The same guilds will be killing the Gods over and over, preventing some of the younger guilds from getting their feet wet learning how to kill the Gods and Dragons in the planes. Much of the God killing would be to obtain epic quest items by the guilds able to kill them.

d) Epic quest rotation in the Calendar.
This is what I proposed. Epic quest mob rotation on patch days. This consists of going in and helping a guild that *needs help* on patch days to clear the planes, kill the Gods, and let them keep all loot dropped in the plane. The rotation guild would loot only epic quest mob drops. The younger guild, gets a full clearing of a plane (which doesnt happen often), all God/Dragon loot, and out of the plane at a decent hour.

Everybody still has their posting rights on the Calendar, including KEG/CIW/BOTS. This is to help prevent Gods from sitting up for days, half cleared planes with armour going to waste, and epic quest mobs getting killed. The "Epic Quest Rotation Calendar" is open for other people to join, there needs to be *some* sort of guideline to joining a rotation such as this or anybody can join.

I am open to any suggestions. Take the intiative and make some, blindly flaming with criticism gets us nowhere fast.

How would you change this proposal? What would you add or take away from it? To me it's a win/win situation. With the bonus of helping guilds who aren't killing the Gods, kill them. -- Jerrica, Officer, KEG
