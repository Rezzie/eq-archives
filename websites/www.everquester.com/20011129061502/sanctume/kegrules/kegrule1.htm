Restructuring, Officers and the Golden Rule

Because the guild is made of dynamic people, it is impossible for KEG to be a static organization. For a long time we have had no solid rules and no solid organizational scheme, as neither were really needed. We all know how much the guild as a whole has changed, from a meager 12 people up to the 60ish active we have now. From a group of friends killing the Lord in Guk, to an association of members who go after ubers. A good many of us are all friends, which makes us different from other guilds, but as in RL ya just cant get along with everyone. Unfortunately this has become a problem, when feuding people cannot find a solution among themselves, it spills over into the larger organization. As a GM and leader of the guild it has kinda fallen on my shoulders to carry this burden and fix it the best I can, something that I don’t feel 100% comfortable with, but with some assistance there is a solution to be found.

A few things I ask of the guild before I continue is this: if you disagree with something or don’t like the way something is speak with me, I WILL listen just like I always have (my email address and icq number can be found in the Member’s message board); remember above all else I love my guild and all its members, even though I get stressed out =P I care deeply about KEG and our continued success.

Officers

To build a strong building, you need a trustworthy and sturdy foundation. For us as a guild, this means officers and GM’s. Until now for the most part officers have only had one purpose, to invite twinks so they could have guildchat; but no longer is this their main role. Officers will now be the leaders of the guild, the ones who maintain rules and order, and look after the general welfare of the guild as an entity.

As of right now (May 15th, 2001)

KEG has 3 GM’s:
Yukitaro, Kazmeir, and Cedrik (me);

and 6 officers:
Alkarn, Deemoness, Jerrica, Leahcim, Sanctume and Saxiff.

There are other names that could be added here but this is the basis we are gonna build from, if more are needed more will be added. People are made officers for a reason; they have earned the respect of the guild. In addition to respect everyone on that list is easy to talk to, if there is a problem they all have genuine concern. What officers say goes, if there is any questions concerning the actions of the officers bring them to me or Kaz or Yuki, we will listen.

Golden Rule

There are few rules that could be made, but would be better to call most of them guidelines, except when it comes to treatment of other members there is but one hard and fast rule.

Rule #1:

No personal attacks of any sort in guildgab or in guildchat. Implied or direct attacks will not be tolerated. The officers on hand will make the distinction between argument and attack. Violation of this rule will be straightforward. First a warning via tells will be sent, the only warning that is given. Then guild-removal, a sit down session will have to be had with a GM, the officer/gm who removed and the person who was removed before they are allowed to rejoin. A second violation of the Golden Rule will result in board and voting privileges removed, and a full evaluation by the GM’s with the input of the officers before reguilding takes place. A third violation will result in permanent removal from all events and activities associated with the guild. Lets hope it never comes to that, as I don’t want to make that decision… ever.

Now, that said, the officers, me and the rest of the GM’s are gonna have a mandatory meeting outa game on Thursday at 10pm est. I will relay to them what I expect from them, and how deep of a commitment I have in retuning KEG.

Keep in mind that other situations may arise, and we will deal with them as they come up. I think that the golden rule will fix up 99.99% of the problems we have had.

Me or another officer will get in touch with each and every person on our roster to make sure they have read the above post and understand the rules and what is expected of them. At this same time all officer tags will be removed from the people not on the list.

Above all we need to remember this is a game, a hobby of sorts, but when it comes down to it this is a social game and what you do to another player you do to a real life person who like you is sitting in front of their computer trying to have fun. No tolerance for personal attacks, direct or indirect, will be condoned or overlooked any longer.
