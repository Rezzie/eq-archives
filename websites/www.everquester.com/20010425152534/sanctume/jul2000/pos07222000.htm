July 22nd, 2000

Saturday, the Knights of Eternal Good (KEG) were exploring Chardok to kill Koracust and some Di'zok royal guards.

In the still of the night, a surge of challenge stirred the air as a Call to Arms was raised. KEG and some of their friends, all nineteen (19) of us, went up to the Plane of Sky to slay the Protector of Sky and his Azarack cohorts.
