February 29th, 2000

It has been a while since our last update. In that time, KEG has defeated Cazic Thule four times, Vox once, and Nagafen once (maybe twice). The Plane of Sky still daunts us. As a side note, Cazic thule now hits for around 400 points of damage per swing.

The Sky Plane Quest petition had some positive results. Our server GM on The Rathe will now witness a Sky quest being completed. I am unsure about the other servers, but hopefully their policy is now the same. NOTE: Some of the Sky quests have changed. In particular, there was a typo in the original Warrior Test of Skill. The NPC used to ask for a Tiny Ruby, when actually the "code" was looking for a Small Ruby. Other quests may have changed as well. I would not recommend using spoiler sites to determine what you need for your quest as they may contain dated information. Make sure you have the information firsthand from the NPC before trying to complete the quest.

There is a secret cabal of warriors on the Rathe that have discovered beginning of the Fiery Avenger Quest. More on this, I cannot divulge. However, paladins be forewarned that a warrior may be the first to obtain the fabled sword.

Rumor has it that the depths of Runnyeye are being upgraded to compensate for the increase in higher level players.

A small format change: I will now be posting current news in a slightly larger font size than older news. This is a bit easier on the eyes.

February 9th, 2000

Attention Keggers: we will be having a tactics session on Thursday, February 10th in Permafrost. This session will be designed to hone the skills of some of our newer members, and push our groups to the limit in terms of what we can handle. The session will be held in the pit traps beneath Lady Vox's lair. If this challenge proves too easy (depending on how many show up), we will move the session to a more dangerous environment. Please use the sign up in the members section to verify your attendance.
