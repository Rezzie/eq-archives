April 1st, 2000

Monday, 30 adventurers of the Knights of Eternal Good and friends (5 full groups) has slain Lord Nagafen with ZERO casualty.

 

With the lack of corpses however, cleric Kellor Skittles chose to overdose himself with manastone, to satisfy his need to ressurect.

 

Lord Nagafen yielded the following treasures: Diamond, Gauntlets of Fiery Might, Cloak of Flames, Crook and Prayer of Life.
