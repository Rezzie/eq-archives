August 18th, 2001

Sleeper's Tomb raid - KEG style!

Twenty-one of the Knights of Eternal Good raided the Sleeper's Tomb last night and have slain the First Warder!

 

Tonight twenty-one of the Knights of Eternal Good entered Sleeper’s Tomb. It is a KEG first to take down the Master of the Guard by KEG alone. (Previously slaying of the MOTG were with acquaintances.)  The MOTG dropped one copy of Primal Avatar (the *reagentless* version of Avatar) who went to Sardain (Shm).

A few Ancient Sentry dropped a priceless battlehammer (13/20, 5 to all stats, 25 mana/hp) for Rend (Mnk) and Antoch (Dru) for pure tanking ability. Apparently he (Antoch) is retiring his FKB title. (hah, not). Also, a priceless spear (same stats as hammer but piercing) dropped for Bintar (Rog) for butt poking action. =)

The Progenitor, second big uber mob dies to an all KEG force! Unfortunately, this bad boy only dropped three lame velious spells that will be awarded to the first vendor we meet. If they didn't end up on the floor. =(

At this point we moved toward the library where the gargoyle type mobs hang out. Kind of a neat library and home of some gargoyle mobs. Really tricky here between clean pulls (Rend did a great job at that) and gating mobs. With 20 people there, we were killing the mobs too fast. An Aged Caretaker dropped a priceless 2h axe for our trusty Caladel (Pal) for smooshing. An Ancient Sentry dropped a Priceless Warsword for Alkarn (War) to give him the mismatching swords.

From here we moved down past the library and had to find a secret wall. This lead down another ramp where we ran into more ancient sentries. The last mob on this series of ramps was a Debris Covered Guardian, first time we ran into one of those. Six sentries guard the hall leading to the Final Arbiter. The third we dropped gave up a Priceless Velium Battlehammer.  After dropping down a huge pit thing, we followed a tunnel to another pit. This pit dropped down to a chute/tunnel that leads right to the Final Arbiter, standing in front of a huge gate.

There are a few places where you find icy water. It has the same type effect as lava but freezing damage as you swim in it. Levitation is a plus here. The Final Arbiter, that bitz, went down like KEG in a Yuki train. Unfortunately, the Verant crew decided we still didn't deserver a primal. /rude them. The good part is, it only took 21 of us to do it and once we set our minds to it, we had very few deaths. The Final Arbiter fell at 630am, over 10.5 hours after we entered the zone.
Next up was the single mob before the first warder, A Tireless Servitor who died fast cause KEG could smell dragon blood. So, 700am we try our first warder. Nanzata the Warder (Phara Dar model) and SUCCESS. We started with 21, ended with 18 in zone. =)
