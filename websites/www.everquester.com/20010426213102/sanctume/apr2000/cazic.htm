April 6th, 2000

On Wednesday and Thursday night, the Knights of Eternal Good and friends have raided the Planes of Fear. Many treasures were obtained for many of the heroes and heroines.

A climactic battle versus Cazic Thule was a sight to behold.

 

Victory! The god of Fear was slain and the celebration began.

Congratulations to Mills Lifestream for obtaining the most sacred cleric weapon, Dawnfire.
