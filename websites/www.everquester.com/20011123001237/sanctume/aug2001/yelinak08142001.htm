KEG - Lord Yelinak slain!

The Knights of Eternal Good lay siege upon the Sky Shrine Castle of the dragons and slain once again the foul beast Lord Yelinak. Congratulations to Caladel (Pal) for the Gauntlets of the Dragon Slayer, Bintar (Rog) and Naadr (Shm) for the Mask of the Dragon Slayer, and to Megwedariel on the Sleeper's Tomb key.

The first ever Dorfcam(tm) was used to show a panoramic view of Lord Yelinak's dead head.
