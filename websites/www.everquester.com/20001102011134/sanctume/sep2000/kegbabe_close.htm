The KEG Babe Secret Identity Contest has ended.
An announcement to reveal the the secret identity of KEG Babe and the result of the contest will be posted soon. KEG Babe is an active member of the Knights of Eternal Good. The contest is open to all Everquest players on The Rathe Server. The person to correctly guess the identity of KEG Babe and have the most points will win a surpise. The contest ends September 12th, 2000.

This is a 100 point system contest rule.
Question 1 will give 50 point for correctly guessing the correct identity. Incorrect guess yields a final zero point total.
Question 2 may give 30, 20, 10, or -10 (negative) points. Choosing None yields zero point.
Question 3 & 4 may give 10, or -5 (negative) points. Choosing None yields zero point.

Question 1: Who is KEG Babe?

Question 2: What is KEG Babe's favorite color?

Question 3: What is KEG Babe's favorite fast food?

Question 4: What is KEG Babe's favorite ice cream flavor?
