July 22nd, 2000

Saturday, the Knights of Eternal Good (KEG) were exploring Chardok to kill Koracust and some Di'zok royal guards.

In the still of the night, a surge of challenge stirred the air as a Call to Arms was raised. KEG (dubbed as 'the night crew') and some of their friends, all nineteen (19) of us, went up to the Plane of Sky to slay the Protector of Sky and his Azarack cohorts.

The KEG vault turned up another entry for the Secret Identity Contest! Can you guess who Drummer Boy is?

Correctly guess the secret identity(EQ character's name) of Drummer Boy and you can win a fabulous prize from the KEG vault! Click here to see a picture of Drummer Boy and enter the contest.

Thursday, the Knights of Eternal Good (KEG) invaded the Plane of Hate and killed Innoruk within five hours.

July 18th, 2000

The Knights of Eternal Good (KEG) went back to Old Sebilis to kill Myconid Spores (shrooms), Sebilite Juggernauts (juggs) and Forglok Reets (reets). Our Knights also went to meet Trakanon (trak)!
