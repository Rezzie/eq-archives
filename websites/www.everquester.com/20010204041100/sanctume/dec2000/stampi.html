December Issue, vol. I

The legend of Stampi the Mascot spawns a new breed of follower... Baleen Drakkar started the day like any other normal monk would by fighting the evil lants that had infested The Overthere.

 
He also liked to get in some good cyber with wood elves...as shown here where Autumnmoon was asking to rub her chest on him.

Then as a call went out to destroy the evil Zordak Ragefire in Sol B, Baleen remembered hearing about a low level warrior in another time and place that lead his friends to victory.  Baleen decided to honor this warrior's sacrifice by doing the same. Zordak was confronted.  Baleen was shaking...outwardly he didn't show it.  He kept up his bravado and rallied his troops in shouts and deed.

But he lives!

 
The Knights of Eternal Good won the day, luckly having Baleen the Rathe's Stampi Mascot to lead them to victory.  They were drunk with thier victory and they asked him to lead them against Feydedar.  Baleen agreed, for nothing can stop the Stampi Mascot!  The guild arrived in Timourus Deep and the Dragon (tadpole?) was engaged...Baleen called the battlecry of "FOR THE GLORY OF STAMPI" and "REMEMBER THE STAMPI ELEPHANT".  

For his valor, the gods awarded Baleen with the last name of Stampi, to further honor his hero from another time and place.

THIS IS FOR YOU STAMPI ELEPHANT !!! FOR THE GLORY OF STAMPI !!!
