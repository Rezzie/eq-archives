July 15th, 2000

Saturday, the Knights of Eternal Good (KEG) continues their assault in the Plane of Sky!

The forces of KEG started with the slaying of Gorgalask and Keeper of Soul.

The Plane of Sky provided many treasures for those adventurers who participated in the raid.

The evening continued with the clearing of Spiroc Island and slaying of the Spiroc Lord.

The Bzzzt island of Plane of Sky brings new challenge.

Our Knights skillfully gained more tactical knowledge as we successfully defeated several Bzzazzts.

Alas, the night ended with a taste of what's more to come...
