The Mr. Traveller Secret Identity Contest has ended.
An announcement to reveal the the secret identity of Mr. Traveller and the winner of the contest will be posted soon. Mr. Traveller is the guy on the left side of the picture, and an active member of the Knights of Eternal Good. The contest has been open to all Everquest players on The Rathe Server (except for members of KEG). The first person to correctly guess the identity of Mr. Traveller will win a green jade halberd from Mr. Traveller himself.
