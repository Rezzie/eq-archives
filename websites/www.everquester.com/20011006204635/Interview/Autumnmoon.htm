INTERVIEW: March 22nd, 2001

Today I go undercover to the world of Druids to interview the amazing Autumnmoon! I've disguised myself as a bear to talk to her and see what she REALLY is thinking.

Mareth: Since when did you start playing EverQuest?
[Autumnmoon] October of 1999. *Yes, I'm a noob =P*

Mareth: Uhh ya noob. Yer as much a noob as Khistrich is cool. Err don't tell him I said that.

[Autumnmoon] I would put points into wisdom!

Mareth: .... Lets not go there. Its a sore subject for me. Moving on...

Mareth: Whats the most embarrassing thing thats ever happened to you in EQ?
[Autumnmoon] Uhhh...too many to count? Falling out of the treehouses rates on the top of the list. Getting lost in just about any zone imaginable is right up there too. Wondering for months what the heck an SLR was and finally getting up the nerve to ask in guildchat ;p

[Autumnmoon] FRAGILE!!!!!

[Autumnmoon] RUN AWAY!! RUN FAR FAR AWAY!!

Mareth: If you could change one thing about EQ what would it be?
[Autumnmoon] Gah...that's a tough one. Too many things..lemme think. The Verant Nerf Nazi's =P

Mareth: Any pet peeves?
[Autumnmoon] Being inspected while sitting at my bind point naked. Being called hun. Being asked for a port, zoning there to find out they made a pitstop in town to buy supplies /whistle /twirl /grumble

Mareth: You don't know what yer missing.

Mareth: If you had any question for me, what would it be?
