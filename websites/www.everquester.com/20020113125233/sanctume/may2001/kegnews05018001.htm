NEWS: May 19th, 2001

Friday May 18, 2001, Lord Nagafen was slain by the Junior Knights of Eternal good and left behind Gauntlets of Fiery Might, Cloak of Flames, Blight, Hammer of the Scourge, and Drums of Selo's March. After 27 hours of lair vigil, Zordak Ragefire appeared to lay claim of red dragon's old home. Little did Ragefire know, Senelar, a cleric of KEG has been waiting for him. With the aide of the triumbrates, Ragefire's dragon form was revealed, but he was quickly slain for his heart by KEG.

Saturday afternoon, After a trip to meet the Avatar of Water in Timorous Deep, Senelar now wields the Water Sprinkler of Nem Anhk.
Congratulations Senelar!

The evening continued in Western Waste. Klandicar was slain by KEG and left behind his Talisman for Leahcim (Key to the Sleeper's Tomb).

-- Sanctume SpiritStone, KEG Historian
