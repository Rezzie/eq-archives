INTERVIEW: June 21st, 2001

Using my l33t tracking skills (ok so I had someone track for me...bite me) I tracked the druid Raene to her lair in Surefall Glade.

Mareth:  Excuse me, could you tell me where Raene is?

NPC dr00id: I HAVE SOMETHING TO SHOW YOU!  THE EXIT!  NOW GET LOST!

Mareth:  Hrm how rude...how bout you buddy?  You know where Raene is?

NPC ranger:  ....

Mareth:  Strong silent type eh?

NPC ranger:  I DIDN'T KNOW FILTH SPOKE COMMON!

Mareth:  LOOK YOU RUDE MOTH!!$%!%!#%#$%!#@$  I'M GONNA KICK YOUR NUTS BACK UP INTO @#$@#%@!!!

Mareth:  Oh hi Raene, I was just getting to know the locals.

Raene:  Thats my landlord, don't make him mad please.

Mareth:  Ooops...uhh he came on to me, ya thats it.

Raene:  Uh huh, now what do you want?

Mareth:  A realdoll.

Raene:  Err no I mean what do you want from me?

Mareth:  A realdoll.

Raene:  WHAT ARE YOU DOING HERE???

Mareth:  Oh ya I got a couple questions for ya...

Raene:  Shoot.

Mareth:  What keeps you logging on EQ?

Raene: I've asked myself that a lot recently. The game is old, no doubt about that. Friends keep me coming back. The game itself wore off a long time ago.

Mareth:  Whats the difference between Raene IRL and Raene in game?

Raene: Raene IRL can't gate away from her class of 5th graders...the best one can hope for is Summer Vacation (soon now, soon)

Mareth:  Ever cybered anyone?  Not counting me of course...

Raene: In your dreams Mare. Though there was that Swedish brat...*sigh* he doesn't play anymore either...used to ask to see my shackles.

Mareth:  How has EQ affected your daily life?

Raene:  Talking about things with my husband...navigating while we drive being referred to as "sense heading" etc.

Mareth:  Ever fallen off the cliff going up Highpass?

Raene: Almost, I was level 14 and heading to Paw for the ultimate uber druid weapon...the Runed Totem Staff!  Broon got our party instead.

Mareth:  How do you like Zeb compared to Rathe?

Raene: I like that it's First Come First Serve rather than Calendar. I like how most seem to get along. Server name sucks though.

Mareth:  Can I date your pet?  She's pretty hot...

Raene: I just put thorns on her, you like pain?

Mareth:  Honestly?  Wait, forget I said that.  Whats the funniest thing you've seen or done while playing EQ?  And like I always say, your cat exploding doesn't count but feel free to mention it...

Raene: Most fun was the "Nekkid Halfling Mud-Wrestling Newbie Night". Everyone in Muppets From Hell made Newbie halflings and turned in PVP books to the Priest of Discord. Hilarious sight to see 20 odd nekkid halflings with newbie weapons, red names, bashing the daylights out of each other. We had prizes for the FFA survivor, for the winner of the "Run-all-the-way-around-Kith-at-night-and-live" Race, and for the Scavenger Hunt.  Other than that, actually meeting people in RL that I've talked to and played EQ would rank up at the top.

Mareth:  If you had any question for me, what would it be?

Raene: Ever hear of personal hygiene?

Mareth:  Isn't she one of them porn stars?  I think you spelled her name wrong tho.  Anyway why do you ask?

Raene: /holds her nose  Oh, no reason.

Mareth:  If you could do your character over, what would you change?

Raene: I'd have gone back and given her more wisdom. Back when I started there were no high level druids to ask what to put into stats. The "book" said Str was important, since if you couldn't carry items back to sell, you wouldn't ever make money. So I got my base wisdom to 100 and put the rest into strength. It wasn't till just recently I was able to get 200 wis. I never kited much since I was always running out of mana, but definitely learned how to group well.

Mareth:  Speaking of change, if you could make one change to EQ, what would it be?

Raene: Better graphics than what I've seen so far for Luclin. Scrawny figures to compensate for puffy armor just doesn't do it for me. I'd rather have characters that look like Glamour Heroes & Heroines than half-starved marionettes.

Mareth:  Any pet peeves?

Raene: Yeah, several: "p-l-z"...it's not a word, and it should be spelled "please"; level 2 characters twinked to high heaven that ask you for super buffs, and being asked for ports when I'm dead.

Mareth:  Ever been trained by Yukitaro?

Raene: Who hasn't? Sol B and Sol A, Yuki went for the Norrath Book of Records by simultaneously training each dungeon to the same zone line.

Mareth:  Who's the most dashing Shaman you've ever met?

Raene: Dashing??  "Dashing" would apply to a Rogue more than a Shaman.... a skillful "highwayman" rather than a tribal witchdoctor that plays with his bag.

Mareth:  "Tribal Witchdoctor".  That sounds so distinguished!

Raene:  Well actually I heard you are more like a "Tool" than "Witchdoctor".

Mareth:  WTF???  DUDE WHO'S SPREADING THESE RUMORS???  I'M KICKIN SOMEONE"S ASS!  THATS IT, THIS INTERVIEW IS OVER!  GOOD DAY MAAM!
