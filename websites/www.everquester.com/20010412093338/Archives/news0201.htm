February 22nd, 2001 15:12

The barb has been deep in thought reading over boards today. I will try not to make this too long. With all the real life events that have transpired lately to EQ'rs, both KEG members and others, I got to thinking how much time I have invested into this game. Yes, remember when EQ was a game? Somewhere along the way it went from a game we played for fun to rushing home to login and say hi to your friends. Somewhere it turned from characters too real people.

The barb can still remember the first day he logged into EQ. It was an April afternoon just after release. Silentone, now of Deus Factum, talked me into buying EQ. I was skeptical at first. We started out on Innoruuk with Sylvaen joining us. An old mud friend told us to go to The Rathe and join him. So we did. Things have not been the same since. One day Silentone was ogling a Flowing Black Robe, remember when those were uber? So I went up to a stranger by the name of Selayai. We hit it off and he became our first EQ friend. He no longer plays but I still think about him, wonder how he's doing. I later met Snaggle and Mareth who are 2 of my best EQ friends, and from there everyone else I know.

I do not login to play anymore, I login to have fun with people I have never met in real-life . ... people I call "friends". When they leave EQ its the closest thing to real world death in a way. Sappy? Yes. Weird? Yes, very much so. I'm prolly the only one who thinks this way, but its my news section (editors note: so long as he learns to use paragraph breaks) . . . so suffer. In approximately 20 days I will be heading to Las Vegas to meet some of these people. It feels weird, honestly, but I think it will be fun.

To wrap this nonsense up I guess what I'm trying to say is when EQ has long since passed and the new fad of the day comes, or we're all off having lives and families, I will still be thinking about the "good ole days". I hope to keep in touch with everyone past, present, and future. And as bad as EQ is or has become to some people, just sit back and think to yourselves how lucky you are to have real life imaginary friends to slay with.

Point is: I love you guys!$!@
