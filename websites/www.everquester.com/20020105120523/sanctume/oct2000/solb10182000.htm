October 18th, 2000

The Knights of Eternal Good visited Nagafen's Lair and killed Zordak Ragefire and his dragon form Zordakalicus. Congratulations to Esmeralda of KEG on completing her Cleric Epic Quest. So far, the wielders of the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh are: Sanctume, Midoc and Esmeralda.
