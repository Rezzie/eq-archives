April 1st, 2000

A Formal Announcement

The Knights of Eternal Good and Brotherhood of the Spider hereby announce a full fledge merging of their respective guilds in The Rathe server, EverQuest.

The discussion of the merge has transpired throughout the month of March 2000. Several meetings were held in various "hidden" locations in Norrath such as Miragul's caverns in Everfrost, Kedge Keep, and Watergoblin stronghold under Lake Rathe.

A formal ceremony will be held in the Arena on April 15, 2000. (R.S.V.P.) Invitations will be sent via e-mail to all friends of the guilds. Not all details of the merge will be made public until after the formalities have been presided by The Rathe's own GM Daruka.

This will be a glorious day for The Rathe! Everyone will know that this is the MEGA UBER GUILD that will be in the position to OWN the upcoming continent of Kunark. Congratulations to everyone!
