Testing results:


*bard tunes-

VoV: fists stack with Verses of Victory (bard, lvl 50 song). They overwrite it at first then VoV pulses again and stacks. The haste on VoV is much lower than the fists. Thus you gain no haste but gain ac/attack/str/agi, the stat portion.

Cel: fists dont take hold on Villas Celerity (bard, level 54). Its a pure haste of 40-45% attack rate increase

CoB: fists stack with Chant of Battle (bard, level 1). Its a pure stat buff with no haste component so was expected I guess.

DoTB: fists dont take hold and robe overwrites Dance of the Blade (bard epic) DoTB is very similar in effect, has a comparable haste, bit higher str and attack buffs but no ac so you most likey wont want to overwrite it.

*enchanter spells-

Augment: fists dont take hold on Augment (enchanter)

Augment: overwrites Augmentation (ench)

SoS & VoG: dont stack with Speed of the Shissar or Visions of Grandeur(ench epic/lvl 60) didnt note overwrite or no hold on these, hopefully didnt take hold.

summary: Fists do not stack with any enchanter spells with a haste component in one way or another either by overwriting or not taking hold.

*other notes-

robe effect stacks with visions of grandeur. so you get the stat buff from Jonathan' s Whistling Warsong and the haste from the ench buff.

robe and fists do not stack, fists overwrite RotWF effect


my opinion-

i would assume these stacking behaviors are by design. it goes against alot of the "norms" of haste stacking though from a bard standpoint.

when you group with bards they will likely be playing Celerity (54) so monks will be pissed that their effect cant take hold (fists are same or a tad less haste but have stat component). many bards will be singing that as its their best haste tune.

with enchanters the much faster haste may be preferable. fists overwrites the slower enchanter haste buffs. if it doesnt take hold on VoG or Shissar there shouldnt be any prob. if it overwrites them monks will hit the effect out of habit and overwrite the superior haste. i would assume, like augment, that it wont take hold. augment is a 50% haste i believe

*general summary-

Celestial Fists overwrites slower hastes and does not take hold on faster hastes. they are done so the stats/haste do not stack with any spell/song with a haste component. the only exception is Verses of Victory which stacks.

sorry this isnt more detailed, we were focusing more on finding something or some way to stack stuff than getting overwrite/nohold info for each buff.
