First - throw away your POS winmodem garbage or your intagrated POS modem and get a real *hardware* modem (Don't get software modems) a good modem will cost at least 90 bucks. If it says rockwell throw it away. This will fix alot of problems.

NEXT - Download a ping program or continuously ping something while your playing, something local would be best. Open a DOS prompt and type -->> ping 206.112.6.10 -t This will help you to stay connected to EQ.

THIRD - Get rid of your POS ISP. If you have AOL as an ISP and you are complaining, then please shut the *^(*) up. Find a list of places around the nation and a few in Canda and ping them. ALL of your pings should be under 300, if they are not then you can find better a better ISP. Yours SUCKS.

FOURTH - If you are getting disconnected from your phone line it is not EQ related. If you are getting disco'd from EQ and don't have an active connection (can't pull and push data) and have to redial up, then it is NOT EQ related. If you are getting disco'd from EQ with a live connection (Still can pull and push data) then there are *rare chances* that it is EQ related.

Most likely your problems are with some other backbone tho, like UU, or Gridnet, or Cerf.

Hope dees help.
