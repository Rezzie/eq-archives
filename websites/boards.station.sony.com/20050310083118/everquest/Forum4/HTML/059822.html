There have been a 'few' changes.
I have been playing 24/7 since early '99.
I'm not sure what I would do if I walked into EQ at this point after 3 year absence.

Some good things happend and some not good.

EQ has gone high end. The newbie levels no longer exist and it shows to a fair exent in the mid levels. Then the newbies either smarten up and get serious and actually learn their class, or they quit.

There are tons of new zones to explore. Too many items to list in ways of weapons, and armor. New races added..the Cats and the Frogs and God introduced the Berserker.

The Trolls now live in Neriak (sob) because the dang Frogs (rofl) drove them out.

The Planes of Knowledge opened up the entire game from level one up to allow instant transportation to anywhere (big mistake-they should limit to level 25 and up-you have newbies in game now that cant literaly find their home guild.) When you see someone in FP crying for a TP to the Commons, chances are they aren't joking-sad isn't it.

The Bazaar allows us to be own traders, unfortunately it killed the markets in town.

There is wonderfull strides in tradeskills,I love the new UI for it. But it will tip the economy upside down Hehe.

We have double bank slots and 2 shared slots now. No more 'dropping a bag' and racing to log off and re-log an alt hoping the bag was still there.

We have mounts, both horse and the lizards- starting at about 9k

Newbies start out with better armor than we had at 25.

We have SoulBinders in every city, more or less.

Twinking is out of control. Way out.

The boats still dont work.

I love the game, not sure why.
