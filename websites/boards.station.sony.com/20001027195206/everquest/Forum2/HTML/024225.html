Station Member 		posted 10-16-2000 07:32 AM           
The argument here is kinda simply and is why verant make mobs heal quickly when not engaged

player A engaged mob M
player A flees from mob M and zones or dies.
mob M is at 10% health.
Player B sees mob M on low health and kills it quickly.
Player B put in little risk (only has to do 10% of Mob M's health and still gains the full reward of XP. (Player A is no longer in zone so doesn't get xp)
Thats not risk Vs reward, if mobs healed slowly out of combat it would escalate this alot more.
Verant want you to earn your xp where possible and so make mobs heal quickly so its hard to catch them low on health, ensuring you put alot more risk and effort into killing them.

Hope that makes sense.
