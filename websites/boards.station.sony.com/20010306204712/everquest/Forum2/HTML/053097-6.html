Orodruin writes: "If a 35th level character can solo a blue mob for exp on his way to 36th level, then an efficient group of 35th level characters can blow away 15 or 20 of these and level 3 times as fast. To prevent that, the mob power compared to it's /con increases to the point that soloing becomes less and less viable.

20 levels later, you don't find very many 55th level soloers any more. Most everyone at that level groups because it is more efficient. But the same logic applies.

If a group of 55th level characters can kill a blue mob for exp on their way to 56th level, then an efficient multi-group raid of 55th level characters can blow away 15 or 20 of these and level 3 times as fast. To prevent that, the mob power compared to it's /con increases to the point that single grouping becomes less and less viable."

The multi-group raid bunch had better be EXCELLENT at dividing damge properly, or one of those groups is going to be getting a lion's share of the xp...

And guess what? Let's look at the Giant fort in Frontier Mountains.
A solo person {Monk usually, Paladin or Ranger sometimes} has to be very careful pulling one giant at a time. Assuming NO OTHER people are there, then the first time he pulls and the door spawn pops on him... he's doomed.
A single group {Have to have a Druid or Shaman for SoW for the puller} can generally pull fairly steadily. Assuming NO OTHER peopler are there, over-pulls are pretty likely so you either end up under-pulling until re-pop or running MAJOR risks.
A number of groups {the most common situation at the fort} means everyone can pull as steadily as their group can handle. Over-pulls are much more rare, and you can always yell for assistance from that group/soloer just off to the side of you. Plus, if your group doesn't have a Shaman/Druid... your puller can go bum a SoW off one of the many who spend time there. If your group doesn't have a Cleric, you can probably get a Revive for your dead buddy from one of the few who spend time there.

If all the groups outside the Fort were organized raid-style? Forget it... the giants would have about as much survivability as the Giants did against the Ravens.
