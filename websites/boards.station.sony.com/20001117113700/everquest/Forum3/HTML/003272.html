Hello, I've worked my way through quite a bit of the druid FA quest, as of this time I would like to report the areas where I, and several others, have reported getting stuck. First off, Ella Foodcrafter is now broken on the Bristlebane server. She now spawns as !Ella Foodcrafter and is a human male (obvious broken spawn by the ! tag on her name). The second place where the quest is broken is at the end of the Feydwer part of the quest. Upon handing the unicorn the three items from the Fey mobs, you recieve a horn and are instructed to give it to a gnome priest who is "nearby" unfortunately, this priest has yet to be found by anyone as far as I know. The next part of the quest which is broken is in Kunark. Currently, the mobs involved in the quest that have been found thus-far are the Tainted/Corrupt Barracuda, Tainted/Corrupt Gorilla, and Ullump, the mean and nasty froglok. In any event, there is supposed to be a third Tainted/Corrupt mob, but he is not spawning anywhere in kunark on any server as far as players posting from both test server and regular servers have found. Finally, and perhaps I should have mentioned this first, there is no NPC who initiates the quest. Those are the "broken" parts of the quest for the extent that druids are able to accomplish. I would also like to make a suggestion to Verant that all major npcs (i.e. dragons, Venril, Gods) be moved to a 24 hour timer to handle the number of people currently trying to accomplish the quests. This is particularly needed in cases where two or more classes must kill the same high level monster. For example, druids and rangers share Venril in the early stages of their respective quests. Venril being on a 7 day spawn whereas many other classes "main" monsters are on a 3 day spawn is currently causing a lot of unneeded frustration amoung the player base. I have no problem with the fact that these monsters must be killed, but having them have such a long spawn time is unneccesary and I would suggest a shorter spawn time. These are the current issues I have found that are causing the quests to be incompletable at this time. Thank you for your help.


Xean Dreadwolf
