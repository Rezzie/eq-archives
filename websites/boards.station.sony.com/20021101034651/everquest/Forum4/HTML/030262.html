Go to EQ Diva and download Reed's Bardsong Analyzer. Runs through Excel and is an excellent tool to see exactly what your songs are doing per level and per instrument.

At your level, you're doing 41/64/64 points per tick for Fulfil's, Chant of Flame and Chant of Frost, respectively. Which works out to 28.16 DPS if a tick = 6 seconds (correct me if I'm wrong )

This intrigued me so I had a play with these figures, I'm not sure I fully understand the calculations involved, just used a coulple of tools I've found over time. First off I used Copelands weapon analyzer (http://www.geocities.com/copelandeq/meleestats.html) to look at Scenario 1, It comes up with 10.6 DPS

I used a Bard damage spreadsheet I came across that works out that the DPS singing would be, and it was 25.2 DPS. As Raheli points out Reeds comes up with 28.2 DPS. (I'm more inclined to believe Reeds).

So it looks like songs are twice as efective at causing damage for this.
