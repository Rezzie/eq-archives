Azul_Mininec
Station Member 		posted 01-16-2001 11:10 AM           
it works like this.

Diamond made the card.
3DFX made the chip on the card.

You can either use 1) Diamonds manufacturer drivers since they made the card, or 2) 3dfx's drivers since they made the chip.

The card manufacturers (Diamond) drivers are usually a little more stable and provide more support, while the chip drivers (3dfx) are usually newer, contain more features and are made by the creator of the chip.

3dfx gives the chip drivers to Diamond so they can make drivers for their specific card. Normally either one will work but the choice is yours on which one to use.
