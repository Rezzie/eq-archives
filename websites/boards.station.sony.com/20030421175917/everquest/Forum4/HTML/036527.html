quote:
I know you cannot imagine there really are people playing who have no character over level 40 or so but I assure you there are.

You don't have to believe this, but until recently, after two years of playing I didn't have a character over 40!

The anecdote I used was to highlight how someone would rather play the low/high game to temporarily "control" the market at the detriment of other players. I suppose it's entirely possible that he was a rank newb who never played the game before but had some epiphany about the "worth" value of a SoW potion, but I personally doubt it. Rather, I think they probably have the ability to make 25pp easy, they just choose an even easier route.

And I shouldn't have even said quad kiting (which can be done in the early 30s btw). You could group in your 20s in High Keep and sell off the FS drops. You could solo in your teens in East Karana, collecting Spider Silks to sell in the bazaar. Heck, you could get lucky below 10 hunting stacks of bone chips and Spiderling Silks to sell.

There's a veritable cornucopea of ways to make money quickly that don't involve trying to gouge players (which a forced 25% upcharge on SoW potions is essentially doing). Yes, they were "playing the market" and yes are allowed to. I just prefer to not nickle-and-dime my way through the ranks of riches when I can easily do so other ways.
