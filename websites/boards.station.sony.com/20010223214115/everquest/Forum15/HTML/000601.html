As a 50th level paladin, I would first like to say that I love the class. It is the first character that I started in EQ 2 years ago. I have never been a power-leveler (proven by a 93 days played mark, and still ONLY 50th) and have enjoyed myself immensly. I have started my share of twinks and only one has gotten past 20th level. I always come back to my Paladin, and I am very glad to see many of the considerations made for Pallies lately. But...

It has become painfully obvious how unbalanced the drop rates are for paladins and shadowknights, for thier Plane of Fear armor drops. Our raid on Saturday was a prime example. We killed everything on the plane. Included in that were several Fetid Fiends, the mob that drops Valorium. Only the male Fetid Fiends drop Valorium, however, and of the several ff's we killed, only 8 were male. None of these dropped Valorium. The only other thing that has a possibility of dropping Valorium, the Phoboplasms, didn't drop any either. (the Phobo's can drop any class armor from either Hate or Fear.) So there was no Valorium to be had on the entire trip for the 5 Paladins that went.

The SK's, on the other hand, were in heaven. The mob that drops their armor is the Amygdalan Knights, and are quite abundant. We must have killed 14 or so in the temple pulls alone. In one fight, 3 Amys dropped SK helm, arms, and a bracer. Three planes drops for SK's in one fight. And yet no Valorium at all in all the plane. The main reason for this is the vastly larger number of Amys than FF's.

Would it be inappropriate to consider making the Amy's and FF's as equaly likely to drop Valorium as Umbral? Especially considering that there are generally fewer SK's in Fear than Paladins anyway?There was a level 48 SK up there Saturday getting his 4th and 5th pieces on his 3rd raid. There was a Paladin there who, after 23 trips, is still missing 3 pieces. This most recent trip gave no Valorium, the trip before gave only 2 pieces (both boots, I was the only Paly so now I have 2 pair <shrug> ), and in the same 2 trips I have seen everything but the SK breastplate drop. In those 2 trips there were a total of 8 Paladins (a couple backed out early on the first trip) and 4 SK's. ??? Something isn't adding up. These 2 classes would appear to be meant as inversely proportional to one another. The drops in Fear are not even close.

Thanks for considering. Please, comments are welcome.
