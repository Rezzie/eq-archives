You can forage anywhere, anytime in my experience. In buildings, in barren Dagnor's Cauldron, wherever. Just keep that forage button always pressed and be patient. It's slow going in the beginning and water is really "scarce" at first, but eventually you'll have food/water coming out your ears (I give away roots, rabbit and water to friends and still am able to keep fruit, berries and veggies for brewing).

Everything but roots and water is also used in baking, but I don't find a high enough demand to bother auctioning for that reason. Veggies is the big one...master brewers need those to max their skill. Also, once you hit forage skill of 100 you will occasionally begin to forage the special items that you can actually sell to vendors. Good luck.
