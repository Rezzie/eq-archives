A very simple one that will get you tattered leather armor is to turn in the white polar bear pelts (save the ones with a "quality - low, med, high) and turn them in to Cindl in Mac's Kilts in Halas.

GIVE them to her, don't sell them to her.

Also, check with the Female Trainer for warriors in the pit...She has some other ones for money.

When you can take them, collect the Orc wristbands from the orcs in Permafrost - The "tundra" outside of the Nebie area. Cindl will also take these TWO at a time and give you Leather armor in return.

ALlakazam's actually does list these quests...Try looking for quests by zone - Under Halas, Everfrost Peaks, and Permafrost.

Let us know how it goes
