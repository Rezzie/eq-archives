Levels 1-40 LH = ((32 x Level)+1)

Impudent: "actually... Lay of hands heals your target fro the EXACT number of hp's YOU have."

Not true, and it never was.

Shakana: "Levels 1-40 LH = ((32 x Level)+1), Levels 41-60 LH = (1281 + (76 x (level -40)))"

This is exactly right. Once long ago, only the first part of the formula existed. Verant added the second part later when it became obvious it wasn't healing enough for post 50 players. At level 60 I LH for 2801 points everytime, even though I can self buff to 3978 hps.

Aszrayel: "Check some of the previous patch messages also, the max LOH has been slighlty nerf'd for higher level players."

LH wasn't nerfed. After one patch it was bugged for a while. The second part of the LH formula above got dropped somehow, thus maiing it appear to affect higher level paladins- which it did. This was fixed with the next major patch about 2 weeks later. LH works as it is suppose to now.
