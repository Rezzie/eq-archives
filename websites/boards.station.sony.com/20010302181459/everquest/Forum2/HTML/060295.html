Nam does have a point, in that the quests and their rewards are not balanced. I am not taking sides but some facts are:

1. Some quests require multiple god/plane drops others none.

2. Some quests have triggered spawns others have rare drops off rare seven day spawns.

3. Some quests components are purchaseable while other quests use many no-drop items.

4. Some quests can be done largely solo with just a few spots needing guild help while others its an almost raid mentality for many steps.

5. Some quests require items from that classes
specific quests, while some require no rewards from that classes specific quests.

6. Many quests require nice items that our useable by other classes and increases the loot pool wanting the items.

7. Some quests require the questor to ruin certain positive factions while others have no faction reprucussions.

To the author of this comment:
"The harder epics that fewer will be able to get will be the ones that people will gawk at for a long time to come. " I have a one word comment - QUACK!

The quests rewards are not balanced still. Many epic quests rewards granted a new ability to the class (Shaman's got a Splurt type DOT) or granted them one of thier best spells with no mana (clerics lvl 56 rezz). Other epic quests grant worse versions of lower level spells (necros get a darkness spell worse than their lvl 49 version).

I know Verant said we should not compare the quests to each other but how can you not? This is a grouping game, if it takes a guild to do the quests then the guild is probably gonna want a grp benefit by obtaining the epic not something "situational". In addition, the effect on EVERY epic should be unique - the druid and necro items share the same effect.

I think Verant needs to make the quests more equal in some regards. If god/plane drops are gonna be required, then make them required for all quests. If a reward from a class specific quests is required, then make it required for all classes not just some. Spawns should either be all triggered or all rare multiple day spawns.

Current epic drops should be looked at and potentially moved to other mobs for consideration. Vox and Naggy now have level caps - bad choices for epic quests. Drops of a 1/30 (black silk cape from PoA) chance verses an always drop should be balanced as well.
