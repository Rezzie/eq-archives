This is a server-age issue, Hrafn.

I began playing on Luclin the week it came up, which was almost exactly a year ago. It's been a long, slow burn but the server is now just beginning to display the signs of the mature economy I recognise from Prexus.

I predict that bone chips will steady at around 4pp-6pp per stack for a few months now, which I think is very good news for lower level characters. I don't follow your logic about overinflation hurting the low-mid level players at all - both the higher buying cost of standard items like bones, HQ pelts and Greater Lightstones and the big drops in prices on many of the unfashionable and superceded old-world magic items (Yaks for 500pp, Staff of the Observers 150pp, Flowing Black Robe 100pp - seen them all recently on Luclin) - are directly favorable to low and mid level characters as far as I can see, with no downside that I can think of except to the high-level farmers (and I'm not wasting much sympathy on them).

However, if you want to collect your own bone chips without annoying anyone, I suggest you go to Paineel newbie area. Anyone can get into it and so long as you don't approach the gate guards they won't attack you even if they con "scowls". There are dozens of skeletons there, the respawn time is standard newbie-zone "a few seconds" and if my experience both as an Erudite necro levelling up there and a gnome necro collecting bones is anything to go by, most of the time you'll have the entire zone to yourself.

I collected 280 bones in about an hour and a half there, all for my own use. Lasted me from about level 26 to 31.
