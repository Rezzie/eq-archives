Well met, travellers.

I will not bore the readers of this thread with comparisons between Epic rewards. It is clear to anyone who has played EverQuest more than a few days that the Paladin epic reward pales in comparison to all the others (the Shadowknight reward being second to last -- however the Will of Innoruuk & Head of Valiant as sub-rewards widens the gap!)

The problem with the Fiery Defender can be broken down to this:

It is not a HOLY SWORD.

To date, Paladins have had very few Holy Swords to wield in EverQuest. Ghoulbane is true to tradition. Soulfire is as well (although it is only 5 charges, the Complete Healing is a divine effect.)

Fiery Avenger strays from the Holy Sword definition by making it's attack Fire based. The fact that Ghoulbane and Soulfire combined to create Fiery Avenger led us to believe we would receive a more divine effect. But that is neither here nor there.

The Fiery Defender's effect, while called "Holy Shock", simply isn't sacred at all. It is Magic based this time around, but barely does more damage than the Fiery Avenger and adds a 1.5 second stun. This is hardly "fires of the heavens".

One with a background in fantasy roleplaying would assume that "fires of the heavens" would pack a little more punch. Or perhaps this Holy force could not be resisted by SO MANY creatures in Norrath, esp. the denizens of Kunark.

I tested my Fiery Defender last night, after the nerf, and I was resisted 14/26 times. I was playing in Old Sebilis. What's that you say, Verant? Frogloks have a higher magic resistance than other creatures? Okay...

I took my Fiery Defender to Karnor's Castle. Kobolds and undead (beings that Paladins should vanquish with no problem at this level) and the blade was resisted 6/10.) At that point I logged off, disgusted by what my class has become.

Either Verant did not test the saves on the Effect, or this is their plan. To make the Paladin class even more pissy.

I have banked my Fiery Defender. I will be using Zephrwynd and a Sarnak Battle Shield (which gives me more AC than our E P I C W E A P O N deemed the "Fiery DEFENDER"!)

Be careful, Verant. You are very close to losing a good percentage of the high level Paladin base with these constant blunders. The informed Paladin class is fed-up. We are letting you know here and now. Please hire someone that understands this class and the lore of fantasy.

There have been dozens of great suggestions in this thread concerning what type of Effect should be on the Fiery Defender. Yet, the only thing Abashi can say is "Is it not the best Paladin weapon?"

The answer Abashi is, no. It is not the best Paladin weapon out there. The latest nerf to the blade makes it only a slight improvement to the Avenger. While the other classes truly get EPIC rewards, we are given a slight upgrade. I prefer speed of a one-hander, with AC of my shield. With a Shaman in group the stat bonuses of the FA and FD are irrelevant. Therefore it is not the best weapon out there for us.

Risk versus reward should tell you that the Paladin class has to do as much if not more than any other class to obtain the Fiery Defender, yet we have the weakest recompense.

Two dragons, three altered Planes, over 200 mobs slain, and countless friends aide -- all for 155pt direct damage and 1.5 second stun that is resisted more than 50% of the time. That is Verant's ignorant definition of Epic.

Better plan something big for us in Velious, because some of us are ready to walk away.
