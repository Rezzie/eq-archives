The problem regarding being unable to shoot an arrow after casting a spell is unintentional. The dev team is working on the issue and it should be fixed soon. The problem is occurring because we just moved some components of archery from the client to the server. This will let us do some neat things that we weren't able to do before. For instance, we can now place randomly triggered effects upon bows and have them work.

-Gordon
