My characters name on this server is Xanik. He is a 30th level Shaman. I have worked very hard and at great expense to elevate my character to a skill level of 132 in blacksmithing. Today I have spent over 250PP trying to make the new Scale armor without success, while my colleagues were successful one out of two times on many items. Finally, I was notified that my skill level increased to 118 on the forge. 118? I keep pretty meticulous records, and my level is 132 on the forge, not 118. What the heck is going on? How come there is never a GM on our server to answer questions unless you stay on for hours waiting for a response? I fully expect recompense to be made for the lost plat, and a restoration of my skill level in blacksmithing.
Thank you.

Ok, just to keep everyone up to date on the latest in trying to deal with people who refuse to listen. It took me 20 hours to get a GM to respond to my petition. Actually 4 petitions, because Guides would answer and gave me such a wide array of possible reasons that I quickly came to the conclusion that the right hand doesn't really know what the left hand is doing at Verant. Guide Lothir (sic) said there is NO glitch that causes a characters trade level skills to drop. Therefore he cannot restore my level. Well, I said, if there is no glitch, then my level must still be at 132, but it is not, therefore I cannot accept the fact there is no glitch.
And that's how we left it.
In short, it appears that VERANT has taken the CUSTOMER out of CUSTOMER SERVICE.
This is the second unpleasant incident I've had with unresponsive GM's and unknowledgeable Guides. The first time, I died while zoning, even though my health was very close to maximum.
Sorry, Verant said, there is no known glitch that caused that, so you are left to your own devices.
Of course Verant also tells us that the boats to Kunark work too! And rather than fix them, they'll just put their efforts in making new expansions.
WAKE UP! We don't want new expansions with NEW GLITCHES. We want a game that runs smoothly, honestly and fairly. And we want the people we pay the bucks to, to be responsive and tolerant and kind. Don't tell me there isn't a glitch when my character dies in while zoning of no apparant causes. And don't tell me there isn't a glitch when I waste 300PP on a new forge trying to get some cool armor, only to find my efforts are futile because I have just increased my skill to 118!
If Verant cannot be held to a miminum of service, then we have a tyrant that cares for nothing but our hard earned money. And that means it is time to begin a cross server boycott of their products.
You want service? Perhaps that's the only way we'll get it. Otherwise, we will continue to suffer ever GM and Guide imposed indignity offered, while treating us like we are all liars trying to take advantage of the system.
