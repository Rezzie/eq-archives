Toklin:

Since You brought up the cookie analogy, let me develop it to its fullest extent so not even YOU can miss the point of the ress stick (or, if You wish, the whole cleric class and community):

Imagine this really nice guy or lady who knows how much others like cookies. So he/she decides to become *really* good at making cookies. He/she gets so good that she can make different kinds to suit different purposes. There's this cookie that makes You feel better when You are ill (heal). Then there's the kind of cookies that are really nourishing and that can make You stay without cookies for an extended time (buffs). And finally it's this wonderous cookie... The one that is the hardest to make... It can actually raise the dead (most get what analogy this is but since this post is meant for Toklin I'll clarify: ress). The trouble is that many times the process of making this kind of cookie is so fatiguing that the nice guy/lady has to rest for extended periods.

After a while it becomes quite clear that making cookies gets kinda tedious. It's actually all the nice guy/lady gets to do - the demand for cookies seems endless but since our cookie maker knows how much people need the cookies he/she trudges on. But the demand for that wonderous cookie that can rasie the dead seems especially high. People call in from all over the land to get this one. But the nice guy/lady can only make so many cookies and sometimes he/she has to turn people down. Some people just can't handle that - they try to convince him/her that they must have their cookie. Sometimes they even curse or threat the nice guy/lady.

Then our cookie maker gets to hear of this wonderous device. It can make the ressurecting cookies automatically - without the strain and the effort. So he/she sets out to find this wonderful cookie machine. The journey is long and full of peril but at the end he/she has it and the nice guy/lady rejoices - Now he can give cookies to so many more.

But one day this little boy speaks up. He demands that the cookie makers machine should be destroyed. Bewildered the cookie maker asks the boy "don't You like cookies"? "Of course I do", the boy answers. "But then why? I don't understand. Please explain why You want to destroy this device that can help so many and that I have worked so hard and long to attain."

"Because it is TOO powerful", the boy answers. "You are able to make too many cookies with it."

The cookie maker smiles and says "I don't think that I or any of the people that I've been able to help want this machine destroyed but I will meet You half way, if it makes You happier". The nic guy/lady pulls out a little black book from his backpack where in he scribbles down the little boys name. He flashes another smile at the boy and says "there You go. No more cookies for You."
