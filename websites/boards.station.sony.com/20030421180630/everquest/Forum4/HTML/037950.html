Finished the first step tonight...East of the docks to Gunthak is a camp with a dark elf, 3 erudite shiphands and Cedric Trallus (Spawns inside the tent : loc -4436, -3415)

Cedric tells you of how they ventured to the new lands and after initiating a battle with the Deepwater Knights, a third force (luggald's) joined the fray. They managed to survive and brought back a luggald corpse for research. When they returned to port, the corpse mysteriously came back to life and escaped back to sea. If you bring Cedric 2 organ samples from these creatures he will share the secrets of their magics with you.

Killing these luggald's located in the secret caves in Dulak Harbor will net you :
Luggald Organs
MAGIC ITEM NO DROP
WT:0.1 SIZE:TINY
CLASS:SHD

Turn 2 of these into Cedric and he gives you Spell: Blood of Pain.

Now for the next quest. After examining the organs, he requests further samples for research. He asks for purified sampled of Luggald Bile, Blood and Saliva. Id assume this will net Comatose.

All times are PT (US)
