Something which hasn't really been discussed in this thread is whether class specific stuff is a good thing. My limited experience with Kunark is that there is some very specific items (Hammer? of Flattening SK/WAR BAR/OGR/TRL 2HB) comes to mind. I don't imagine that a lot of these items are being traded because at least in this item's case it's extremely specific and not likely to be very useful. This is admittedly not a typical example, but I think that it illustrates a point: class/race restrictions on items lower an item's value.

To my mind class/race specific items compare to more general items as follows:
A. Specific items are worth less than more general use items because,
B. Trading in these items is to a smaller market, (i.e. only 1 class/race combo rather than all int casters of all races). Microeconomics - less demand due to smaller market = lower price.

So to my mind maybe as an enchanter I would like more ENC specific items becuase they'd likely be cheaper than the equiv. ENC/WIZ/MAG/NEC item (I'd only be bidding against other ENC rather than all casters). Drawback being that resale would be a bigger pain after the fact and a fracturing of items into class/race specific types complicates trading significantly. I'm not into the whole trading thing personally. I'd be curious to hear from those who are what there experiences are with class specific items like the Robe of Foci versus general INT caster items.

I don't really mind items being shared by ENC/WIZ/MAG. As an enchanter I'd biased towards an INT/CHA item which would likely be a much lower priority of other casters. I don't see the need to have them restricted though.

Just wanted to raise the issue of the desirability of this sort of change to class-specific items. BTW, Robe of the Foci gets a thumbs down from me as an Enchanter item: where's the CHA bonus? I'm trying to increase that stat right now, so I'm biased.

Another observation is that I think we'd all like greater variability in appearance of our characters. Wearing robes given the way EQ renders characters pretty much leaves most of our appearance up to a single item. So, yes I'd like to wear something other than an FBR that a ton of other people have. However it seems from previous discussions, Abashi has indicated that there is a pretty limited capacity for different styles of robes given colors and tints, etc.

So, in conclusion (ramble, ramble) I think that it's unclear if class specific stuff is a great idea. I'd guess part of the reason why necros have so much stuff is that the whole evil, raise the dead thing lends itself to a whole bunch of themed items, and frankly the other Int casters tend to be lumped together (share guild space in cities, etc. etc.) and aren't as distinguishable in temperment. (I'm sure that comment's going to annoy a bunch of people.)

Just my 2cp.
