Polarbear,
I say go for it! It's a blast to play in a single race guild. There are lot's of naysayers, but I guarantee you won't regret it. As a member of a barbarian only guild, ( which means we have only warriors, rogues and shamans ) I have had the time of my life.

You will meet a LOT of resistance, and have to endure name calling and such. Don't worry about it. Most of the light races accept cross-teaming normal, and want everyone to play good vs evil. While this is certainly an option for most players, there is a lot to be said for playing team vs. team.

What it all comes down to is how do YOU want to play? If you can ignore the insults and threats, you'll do fine, and it WILL add a lot to the game for you.

Start your guild! You won't regret it!
