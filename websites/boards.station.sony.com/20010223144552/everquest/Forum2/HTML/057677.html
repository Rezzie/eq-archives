The above popst was very true btw, but if these are your first characters, I would suggest this..

Take you highest level char to Sro, pray that someone is clearing the desert to spawn the AC, loot all the corpses there (ask them first so you don't seem like a ninja looter). If you can sell to the merchants there all the better, but collect everything. Rogues need the zombie skins for making poison vials so even they are worth plat.

After a night or two of this head to EC tunnel with your cash. Watch for a while, get used to the prices, make some good deals on weapons and armour, many a great deal is to be had there. Many people trash stuff that would be a great upgrade for you and are willing to sell it very cheap. As a side not: Never beg, people will hate you for it.

Now you should have a magic weapon and some decent armour. Go kill some orcs, I personally always preferred Orc 3 in WC because it is less camped. Collect as many belts as you can (do not be greedy, share with the group and be fair), you can use these belts for one of two things.

You can turn the belts in for exp and faction at one of two places in Freeport, or you can sell them for 3-4pp each to some rich high level dude who is using them to PL a twink or working on faction. This will earn you some good plat if you hunt orc camps a few levels and sell the belts.

I would also suggest working only one char at a time, once one gets up a few levels (low 20's) then you can use him to subsidize the other char a little bit. If you keep trying to outfit 2 chars at low levels, you will generally always be a bit behind in equipment and such.

Hope that helped to get you started, the next question you need to ask is "Where do I get the best experience at XX level?", some places give way better exp than others
