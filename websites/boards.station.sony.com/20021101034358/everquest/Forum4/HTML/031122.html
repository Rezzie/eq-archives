Generally speaking the only chance you have to out-run a mob unless it's snared or you have SoW or a similar speed-enhancing spell is to strafe-run; that is, turn sideways and hit the forward and left or right keys together. I've survived a few potential deaths that way, but a fair number of mobs can run fast enough to keep up with you.

Some people have suggested jumping too, but I haven't tried that.
