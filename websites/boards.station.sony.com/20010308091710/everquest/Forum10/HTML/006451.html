There are newbies out there who truly require assistance. Most of those, however, never think to beg.

The next time I saw her she was level 4. I gave her some quick tips on pet control, and now she's a higher level than me. I ran into her in WC last week - she gave me 100pp and a Bracer of Erollisi for my previous efforts.

Some newbies deserve your help. Beggars typically don't.
