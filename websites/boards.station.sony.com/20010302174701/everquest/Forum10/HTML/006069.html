Well, the first thing I would do is to run the "Thorough File Check". That will check all your files and compare them with the currently active ones. That helps in most cases. If you still crash, delete the following files:

freporte*.* (East Freeport Files)
nikkon.opt (options for your character)

The "nikkon.opt" also holds your hotkeys, so you will lose them and have to recreate them. Oh and DON'T delete the character, just the file on your harddisk....
