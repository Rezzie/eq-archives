Abashi
Station Member 		posted 08-11-2000 03:02 PM           
>>>Will anything ever be done to make the Disarm/Sense Traps skills even the least bit useful?

We have no plans to increase the usefulness of these skills either by increasing the number of traps in the game nor through revamping the skills. The lack of a use for these skills was one of the driving forces behind redefining the rogue as more of an assassin rather than a theif, and as such, rogues have been thoroughly compensated (as much as I hate using that word). We would remove the skills from the list if it were possible, unfortunately some technical issues prevent that.

-Gordon

Station Member 		posted 08-11-2000 03:56 PM           
A valid and doable way to implement traps in EQ with a minimum of fuss:

TRAPS:
Create a bunch of static mobs that are immune to magic and weapon attacks.
Make the images of these mobs into things like spikes, pendulums, moving dart-throwers,
etc.

The mobs would be unable to move, and you can pass through them,
but at a very slow speed (That is, you can walk through them, but your speed
slows to a walk while you are doing so.)

The "Trap Mobs" could attack anyone who passes through them, doing damage, or
perhaps casting spells that poison, slow, etc.

---
Rogue lockpicks then become weapons that are ONLY good for "attacking"
traps. Detect Traps becomes the "defensive" skill to avoid getting
hit/resist the trap mob's spells. Disarm Traps becomes like a weapon
skill, and lockpicks become a new category of weapon. Note that ALL
non-trap mobs are automatically immune to the "lockpick weapons".

ADVANTAGES -
-Non-Rogue groups are not destroyed by this, since they can opt to run
through and take the effect. (Some traps could be made deadly, but that's an option)
-Disarm and Detect traps are both already available as skills for a rogue.
-Rogues become useful for a group.
-Traps can have MANY different effects, from straight damage, to blindness, to poison.
-Different strengths of lockpicks allow for new quest items for Rogues!

DISADVANTAGES -
-This would involve a fair bit of coding, and some downloads.
-Zones would need to be re-populated with traps for this to be useful.

===========
