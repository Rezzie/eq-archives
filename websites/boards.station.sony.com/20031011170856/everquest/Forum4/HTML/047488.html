Dex affects following:
Chance to skill up a combat skill.
Chance to crit.
Chance to proc.
Chance for a 'special' to work (i.e. Head Shot)

Pretty much every ranger I know always wants either NDT from me (120dex), or Ferine Avatar(140dex) if we have a 65 shaman present. They all say their crits and procs go through the roof with these buffs.
A guildmate of mine, lvl 63 ranger, currently has EQ/AM2, 6AA away from AM3. She uses recently introduced Karana cultural: Blessed Guardian arrow: 11dmg + 4cold, 170 range. Does some impressive damage with those.

I suggest you do some experimenting - hunt vas ren tegi in Twilight Sea for condensed ice, and grimlings in TM/GF for acrylia. You'll need real high fletching skill, ice tipped acrylia arrows triv at 250+, but they are 10+4cold damage. Get a couple stacks, and see what damage you do with these.

Str does have an impact on ATK rating, though not a very large one. And of course, ATK affects your ability to hit and hit harder. From RP standpoint, higher Str allows you to draw your bow harder, giving more velocity to the arrow.

See http://www.steelwarrior.org for more in-depth discussion of how Str factors into ATK, and what exactly ATK does.

But while more Str gives quantitative improvement (slight damage improvement on every shot), Dex is qualitative (higher chance to do outstanding damage)
