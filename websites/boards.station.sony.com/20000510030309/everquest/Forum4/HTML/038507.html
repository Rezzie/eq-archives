Does anyone else think the spawn rate in karnor's castle is a bit insane? Im sure the spawn was set fast so that everyone has enough to kill but it is defeating the purpose. We just can't get very far into the castle with a group (and im talking 1 group, hunting for xp, not a raid type deal) without getting slaughtered by the respawn. The other night we tried to go take a room not very far into the castle that we'd managed to pull clear. Once there, we had to fight TEN straight mobs due to the insane respawn, eventually having to zone because I was oom (Im the enchanter.) Now keep in mind this was with a full group.. lowest level was a 48 druid, had a 50 ranger with swarmcaller, 2 51+ warriors in full indo w/ god caliber weapons, 51 cleric and 51 enchanter. During prime time karnors is almost a waste of time due to the constant trains. Not even going to get into the awful pathing, and mobs aggroing through walls, ceiling, etc..

Personally I think the spawn time should be increased 5-10 minutes... which would at least give groups a chance to move in and hold rooms without having to train the rest of the zone constantly.
