They have mentioned that a 'generic' targetable Illusion spell would be unworkable due to the faction issues, it might be interesting to consider a higher level variable illusion that varies by zone, just as the Egress spell is coded to have a different effect for each zone.

In this case, a spell cast in South Karana would turn you into an Aviak while the same spell cast in Runnyeye would turn you into a Goblin and if cast in Crushbone it would turn you into an Orc, in Toxxulia / Kerra Ridge into a Kerran, in the Overthere into a Sarnak, etc. No new global file additions, and the race selected for each zone would have to be present in the zone (creating some fine choices in some areas, is Lake of Ill Omen more of an Iksar, Goblin or Sarnak zone?) and also judged by the factions that would be adjusted.

As with Egress, the spell might not be particularly effective in ALL zones (Egress in Dreadlands takes you to the Wizard / Druid portals, which anyone who can cast Egress can obviously already teleport to...), but would be in enough of them that it would have some use.
