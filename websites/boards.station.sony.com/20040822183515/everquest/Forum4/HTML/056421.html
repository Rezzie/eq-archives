Before this digresses even further, I thought I would try to answer the question originally asked by inoffensive_name.

quote:
Give me their impression? Level 52 fire pet, is what I'm talking about, or about the "Wizard" pets in general.

The level 52 fire pet is useful in a couple of situations.

The biggest use I had for it, was to have a chance of stunning creatures before I got the level 53 air pet. Unfortunately, if you actually parse the fights, you'll notice for most creatures the level 49 air pet is actually better at stunning AND puts out more damage. Once you get the 53 air pet there's absolutely no question as to which is better. The only reason to use the 52 fire over 49 air (for stuns) is on mobs where the level difference between the pets makes a difference.

Another use I've had for the 52 fire pet is that it occasionally casts the wizard snare spell. I've gone and done a bit of kiting using this fact by sending in the fire pet and waiting until it snares the mob, then reclaiming it and summoning the 54 water pet to backstab while I kite. Unfortunately, this isn't a very reliable method of killing something since the fire pet does not reliably snare anything and on any mob you would want to use this tactic (i.e. a very hard hitting critter that you can't just chain cast earth pets on) you are better off using 'yo-yo rooting'. In other words, send in the earth pet to root, back it off, nuke, repeat.

In most solo situations you'll find the 51 earth or 53 air pet a much better choice. In most groups the 53 air or 54 water are the best choice. At level 52, you haven't got the option of the 53 air pet so you are limited to 51 earth, or 52 fire. As others have mentioned, the 52 fire pet casts root (aaarrrggghh) so really there's no reason to chose the fire over the earth pet. Both have the downside of rooting, but at least the earth pet can put out decent damage, and act as an off-tank if needed.

I don't have my numbers handy, but just FYI when I parsed the damage output of the 51 earth versus the 52 fire pet, the fire pet did less than half the damage of the 51 earth pet in a 2 minute fight. Longer fights look even worse since the pet goes oom in about 2 minute of combat.
