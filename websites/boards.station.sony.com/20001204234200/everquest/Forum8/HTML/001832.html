Work a bit on smithing. Skinning knives are player-made...small brick of ore,hilt mold,dagger blade mold,flask of water. Get up to the trivial for toolboxes(51) and then work on making studs,then use those MQ pelts for studded masks.

Skill ups are cheaper that way. Once you hit trivial on studded,make every non-HQ into padding and sell them by the stack. You'll be working on cured silk from then on in to get up to 82,then HQ pelts become needed to go any higher.

Have fun-tailoring's the Tough Trade.
