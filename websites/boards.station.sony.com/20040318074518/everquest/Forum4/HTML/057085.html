Hold the item in question on your cursor.

Have someone with "Identify" (or a Bard with the equiv Song.. low level) cast/sing on you.

Hardest part is remembering to organise the items as they are Indentified, after the fact. Arrange some kind of order in your bank/backpack... and write it down.

An Enchanter, a Wizard, a Magician, and a Necromancer also uses the spell for identify, lvl 16+.

For items with identify, look in the bazaar for Rod of Identification, Silvery Mask, Star of Eyes, and Holgash Grand Vizier of Beads. Before buy check the amount of charges it has left or unlimited. Target yourself, and click the item in one of your inventory/armor slots.
