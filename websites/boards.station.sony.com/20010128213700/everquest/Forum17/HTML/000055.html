Give a tell to Lynet or Willaena - both are the same character and Lynet has been selling a lot of fine steel lately. Snacko and some other folks also sell it - in fact if you go to East Commons and auction that you are buying fine steel you will get some answers.

As for the pricing, expect to pay 75 plat per sheet of metal, with a discount for some of the easier items and surcharge for the things that folks fail a lot on. So face, bracer and neck will be 50 to 75 plat, chest legs and back should be 225 to 350 plats, and the other pieces should be 150 to 200 plat.

Many smiths will offer you a meaningful discount if you include a stack of leather paddings in your sales offer.

Good luck with it
