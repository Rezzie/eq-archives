Colors of names are as follows:

Blue: Normal player name
Red: PvP (player vs. player) enabled player (not on a zek server)
Purple: Anonymous or Roleplaying player (class, race, level are hidden, guild is also hidden for anonymous, but showing for roleplaying)
Green: Guides/GM's

There is a screenshot floating around showing a gold name for a player with 500 days played. Do a search and decide what you think. I personally think it's just the lighting.

The bazaar has nothing to do with the planes, fortunately. No part of Luclin is off limits to you (except for when you need keys, of course) so you may happily use the bazaar to sell and buy whatever is available to you.

Luclin and the Planes of Power are completely seperate things.
