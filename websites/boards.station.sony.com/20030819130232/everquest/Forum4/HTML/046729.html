WHen you /ooc a price check, you are looking for a consensus. What do most of the people say it should go for? If someone quotes an absurdly high price, usually the bazaar hawks will immediately tear him a new one. If he quotes too low, someone will immediately correct them. You have to boil down what you hear, and use a little commom sense, but if you get 5 answers, and 4 of them are close together, then that is usually the "going rate". You can then price a bit higher and hope for someone willing to pay, price in that neighborhood and probably sell fast, or undercut and sell almost immediately.
