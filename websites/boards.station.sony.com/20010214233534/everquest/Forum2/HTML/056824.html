The next big MMORPG is going to knock EQ off as “King of the Hill” because:

I’m not going to go into a whole lot of detail here because much of what I am going to say has been discussed in detail in other threads. What I want to do is get the major issues with the game all in one post so VI can see them “in a nutshell”. I would also hope that all you creative people out there will point out anything I miss, and I hope you can make great suggestions for improvement! Let’s get everything in one post, and let’s hope they address the major issues soon:

Those who reply please try to stick to broad major issues; let’s not get into nit-picky details. Here’s why EQ will go the way of the dinosaur as soon as another great MMORPG comes along:

1) Lacking content, lacking of fun:

a) The game is supposed to be Ever“Quest”, not Ever“Hack-n-slash”. Quest rewards need to be better at all levels of play. Too much of the really awesome equipment in the game is just dropped.

b) There aren’t enough fun things to do at the end-game for small groups. Not everything fun should be a major production requiring you to get your whole darn guild together. It’s hard to get 2 or more groups of high level people together at the same time, so it happens infrequently.

c) Trade skills are lacking. Some of the best items in the game should be made by master craftsmen. All items should be producable at a reasonable price and sellable at a reasonable profit based on the level of the craftsman and the buyer.

d) 100 real days played to reach level 60 is too long. 50 Days played seems more reasonable. How much hack and slash can you stand? Wouldn’t it be nice to have a couple of uber-toons in your stable if you are a hardcore gamer, instead of just one? If you have gotten to be high level, but you decide you don’t like your toon, don’t you continue to play it anyway because the time investment to start over is too great?

2) Class Balance (the current hot topic):

The problem here is that the classes most in need of help don’t seem to be getting the necessary attention, and I anticipate little help from VI based on what things have begun on the test server. These are the classes I think are most in need of help with balance, particularly at higher levels.

a) Rangers (offense only marginally better than warriors, yet pay heavy defensive penalties)
b) Monks (same as rangers but pay even more defensive penalties, plus too much taunt built into their attacks)
c) Bards (they die to much – agro needs to be lowered for songs)
d) Shaman (are supposed to be the best at buffs, they aren’t)
e) Clerics (are technically balanced, but boring in battle – give these guys something to do besides heal)

Ok everyone, please post any major issues I missed, and let’s have some awesome ideas on how to make the game more fun!

Thanks for reading.
