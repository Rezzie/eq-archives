A few things to consider:

1. If you twink too high, your next post will be something like "I'm level 30 but my 1HS is stuck at 13! Help!"

2. Many of the things you take for granted now (faction, neat quests, ...) will be blown way out of proportion unless you take the time to level appropriately.

3. You are bored of the high-end. Find a new angle or objective:

a. make a point to walk around Norrath
b. visit some new places
c. Orcs are my friend, kill the halflings instead
d. Try a PvP server
e. develop an eccentric personality

Twinking some helps avoid the basic need to plat farm but killing an even or harder con in just a few whacks gets dull. You want to explore the depth of Norrath so do it.

Do your newbie armor quest. Look on alakazam and find some neat low level quests - e.g. screaming mace, jaggedpine cloak, wayward paladin daughters (felwithe), ...

Give yourself a graduated twink budget - say 250pp for the 1st 10 levels. Note that 250pp allows you a tremendous equipment advantage over your targets at those levels.

If you think twink and level, you have defeated yourself before you have begun.
