Author Topic:   E'ci Server's inability to hold players for extended periods of time.
Mauna-Eci
Station Member 		posted 01-19-2001 03:01 PM           
I write this message not to degrade any one person nor to poke fun at any company or persons but to express a growing concern among the players of the E'ci server. It is widely known that E'ci handles a large player population at a given time. This enormous amount, while great for the game, causes some problems in real life. I thoroughly enjoy, like most people I know that play on E'ci, the amount of players and the diversity they give the game. To be in a game where at level 60 you can find a group just as easy as level 10 is remarkable but this also poses the enormous strain on the server.
One can not go into East Commonlands and expect to make it to the other side without fear of a drop. Raids are expected to be "boggy" because of the nature of them but more raids are unsuccessful more often than not because of link-deads and the time it takes to recover the corpses without the help of GM's. Most people, if not all, do not expect help from LD's unless it's a global situation. Which is fine and understandable considering the situation of the server. It is necessary that some action take place on E'ci soon.
So to make this not a complain only thread I wish to give some options that I come up with and also have heard from many other players, be it Out of Character or in guild.
A server split would be great but that takes away the uniqueness of E'ci. I am sure something can come out of it that not much is taken in that area.
A cap on the amount of players that can login to E'ci. That would be cruel to do but would help one situation while creating more conflicts and eventually more lost accounts from people canceling EQ. But perhaps it would also force more people to leave E'ci and go to another server.
Not much one can do or come up with ideas to do since I'm not behind the scenes of the hardware and my opinion is valued only so far. I will say that all who are behind EQ have done a tremendous job and have made a great impact on the future of online games. I just imagine the technology 5 years from now on online games will make this look like a 16-bit DOS game. So in conclusion I ask you to ask more players what they think they should do, especially on E'ci. Let them know what you CAN do and keep them up to date. Remember, they have invested a lot of personal time as well as those behind the game and the current runners. If we work together, we will have a happier game.
Thank you for reading.
Mauna of E'ci

All times are PT (US)
