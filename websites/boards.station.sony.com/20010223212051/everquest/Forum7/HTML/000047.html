Dear fellow EverQuest players-
I have a dilemma in which i need your help. I have created a character named Caution. This name was allowed by the filter over a year ago. Six months ago he reached the 2oth level and I choose a last name of Explosive. In order for me to get my last i had to petition. Therefore the name Caution Explosive was allowed by GMs. On 11/19/00 I reported a bug to the Gms. During this time they Now considered my last name inappropriate and proceed to delete it. When I repeatedly ask for a reason they refered to rule number 6. Rule number 6 states "Common words and phrases that would not be found in the place and time setting of the game (e.g. Switchblade, Phaser, Toaster, Cannabis, Sloegin)." Since the words caution and explosive ARE relative to the game setting why was my named change? IF you think that that is all listen to this. When asking for a explanation of why it was inappropriate I was ignored. Since I repeatedly ask a gm for a explanation I was PROMTLY BANNED from EQ for 7 days. I am asking for the players of EQ to give me some feedback on this issue if they think it was inappropriate or not. Please all comments are welcome.
IN addition this happened to 5 other friends of mine in the party that i was in.

Caution

I personally see nothing wrong with the name.

Perhaps the GM’s consider that since it is something found in the real world they will not allow it here. Or maybe they felt as if it lacked the appropriate imaginary spirit that they wish to instill in the game. Or maybe even they are just jealous that they did not think of it first.

What ever their reasons are I feel the very LEAST they can do is explain their decision to you in detail. I personally find a generic response such as refer to rule 6, impersonal and inappropriate for a gaming environment that promotes a since of community.

As far your being penalized goes, I do know that the GM’s here are very busy people. I fully understand why they do not wish to deal with repetitive petitions from players. I am also aware that a lot of players seem to have little or no understanding of they work load that is placed on GMs and quite often are rude and discourteous when they do not receive the answers they want.

I am not saying that you are one of these players, but your post does say that you repeatedly petitioned therefor your penalty is just. Perhaps in the future when dissatisfied with the results of your petition, you may wish to try alternative methods of communication in effort to resolve the problems.

Lastly, the thing I have learned from your post is if a player has a boarder line name, they should think twice about reporting bugs, or petitioning at all. Seems the reward for concern over the gaming environment as a whole, just may be a penalty.
