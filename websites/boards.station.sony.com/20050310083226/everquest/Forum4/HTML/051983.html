There are kind of 2 answers to this. The first is, to read the thread from oldest to newest post, usually makes more sense.
The second is, if your following up reading a post that is longer than 1 page. The bottom of the first page, on the left hand side, will have a series of numbers. 1 2 3 etc. Click the page you want to jump to. You'll still be reading oldest to newest, you can bypass stuff you've read though.
Something to remember, if you've recently read a thread, and there have been new posts on it? You often times have to hit Refresh on your broswer to see the new ones.

Hope this helps!
