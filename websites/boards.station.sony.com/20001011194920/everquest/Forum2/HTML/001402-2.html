I'll thank VI for looking at the issue =)


BUT! ( you knew it was coming =p )

Please, also look at either a meditation cap increase at the same time, or instead of a mana pool increase removing the 'hybrid penalty' from our spells, which would have the same effect as a mana pool increase AND meditation increase.

Increasing the mana pool really only helps when you are sitting at FM all the time or going from FM to OOM. Since that is not the case all the time, your mana regen rate is what limits your casting ability, along with spell cost.

IE: It takes me 5 minutes to go from OOM to FM now. I can cast 6 GHeals.

mana pool is doubled (!!!), but my mana regen rate (meditation skill) stays the same.

It takes me 10 minutes to go from OOM to FM now. I can cast 12 GHeals.

Well, when I'm at FM, yeah its great! However, rarely am I at FM if I'm casting in any contributing role to a group, and same if the group is killing in an efficient fashion.


Now, lets remove the hybrid spell penalty (lets say its 50% for arguments sake).

IE: From OOM to FM takes me 5 mins. I can cast 6 GHeals.

remove hybrid spell penalty. mana pool and meditation rate stay the same.

I can go from OOM to FM in 5 mins. BUT, I can cast 9 GHeals now instead of 6.


-or- increase mana pool and meditation skill cap.

IE: I can go from OOM to FM in 5 mins. I can cast 6 GHeals.

mana pool doubled (!!!), meditation skill cap increased.

I can go from OOM to FM in 7.5 mins. I can cast 12 GHeals.


What needs to be changed is the amount of med time per 'x' *visible* mana of spell casting cost, not how much I can cast off of a full bar neccesarily. THAT is what will make medding in combat a viable option, medding faster, not more mana.

*when I say 'visible' mana, I use that term because as a Hybrid I have no real way to figure out how much *real* mana I have because I don't know what percentage of spell cost the 'hybrid penalty' is. Ergo, I just have to assume that I have less mana, and that the casting cost on the spell is correct. So, visible mana would be what looks like the casting cost of a spell, but I know really isnt. If you give us a higher meditation skill cap, my amount of med time for 150 'visible' mana is reduced. If you remove the hybrid spell penalty, the amount of med time for 150 'visible' mana is reduced, because visible mana becomes actual mana.


I hope that makes at leat partial sense =).

The main reason hybrids with low wis/int have problems with achieving close to 200 wis/int is because we *are* hybrids. As hybrids we are expected to melee most of the time, using our spells only occasionally in combat.

As melee is our bread and butter, we tend to look for gear with higher AC or melee stats, as that helps us to survive a fight better. Wis and Int bonuses are great, but hybrids, being primarily meleers, prefer AC, Str, Agi, or Dex. As a Ranger, I know I'd take a 10AC helm over a 5AC +5wis cap anyday.

Sadly, very few decent melee items have good int/wis bonuses, so it's hard to get close to the 200 softcap and still have armor tough enough to keep us alive while toe to toe with a mob.

So, Verant decided to increase our base mana pool to compensate for the lack of melee items with good wis/int bonuses. Aside from putting new hybrid wis/int armor in the game (and making all the hybrids have to go out and camp it) this was the only possible solution.

Thanks Verant =)
