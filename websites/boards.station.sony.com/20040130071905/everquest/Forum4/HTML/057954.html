Well. I tellya. It depends. On a lot.

You will skill up faster in DW if you have the faster weapon in the offhand. If your skill is going up fine, you don't care.

GENERALLY, once your DW is high (it usually fires the offhand weapon), you want the faster weapon in main (for main-hand-damage-bonus). In the offhand, skill being up, you want the best ration available to you.

BUT, if you are not a main tank, you may want to give up a bit of damage and have slower weapons with excellent ratios in both hands.

Once you get up above 56, I have no experience-based advice on melee weaponry choices, sorry.

I don't follow any fast and hard rules. I put the weapons with the highest DPS that I can afford in BOTH hands. With dual wield you want to be ripping stuff up with bost hands, and high DPS ratio is the best way to do it. If you can find high DPS weps with crazy proc damage, all the better.
