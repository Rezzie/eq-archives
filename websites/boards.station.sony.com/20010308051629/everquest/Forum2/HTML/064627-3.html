While I agree that multi-questing does lead to a few problems, especially with Ancient Clops farming and a few epic work arounds that I'm sure Verant didn't intend, making it impossible would lead to far more problems.

Sky quests are nearly impossible to complete at the current time, due to the absolutely insane amount of time needed to farm the quest components. If you nerf multi-questing, I guarantee you that next to no sky quests will be completed. No one has the time to commit to getting all those drops for items as lame as the ones in sky.

Some of the epics, notably the warrior, mage and wizard ones, require no drop components off rare spawns, many of which are rare drops. Maybe it's part of the vision than an SK spend a week's worth of nights in hate looking for an ashenbone hide, but it's not realistic to expect the SK to devote that kind of time for a small part of a long epic quest.

It's equally unreasonable to expect a mage to spend literally months in south karana, hoping for a shot at a pegasus hide cloak. The Earth Staff off Innoruuk, the Cazic skin off Cazic, the hand off of Maestro, the trak guts off of Trakanon and the water staff off Phin also fall in the 'unreasonable to farm' category.

Allowing multi-questing of these items not only makes it possible for people to complete these quests in some sort of sane amount of time, it also reduces farming. Reducing farming is one of Verant's goals, isn't it?

Multi-questing doesn't increase the number of items that enter the game, they just increase the rate slightly. It also allows people who can't spend hundreds of hours collecting rare, no drop quest components an opportunity to complete these quests.

If multi-questing is nerfed, the least you can do is remove the no drop tag from almost all quest components, especially the ones required for the epic quests. The only epic components that shouldn't have the no drop tag removed are those that are either 100% drops on timed/triggered spawns (like the note on overking bathezid and the various drops off of triggered mobs in the planes), those that are no drop for reasons that aren't related to the epic quests (soul leech, fiery avenger, robe of whistling fists) and those that are uncommon drops off of common mobs.

Anything that's so rare a drop it takes more than 8 hours for someone to aquire should most definitely be made droppable. I bet there's next to no rogue, mage, warrior or sk epics on the live servers that weren't multi-quested.

That's not because we're a bunch of exploiting jerks, it's because the epic quests, particularly those four and the wizard quest, take incredibly unreasonable time expenditures to complete. A few non-epic quests, like the robe of whistling fists and robe of the lost circle quests also suffer from this.

I know a positive change is intended here, but we all know that any change of this type will be seen as a nerf. If nerfing multi-questing is the objective, you're going to do it anyways, but I implore you to please either redesign quests that are multi-quested currently, or make everything that's multi-quested droppable.

Holden.
