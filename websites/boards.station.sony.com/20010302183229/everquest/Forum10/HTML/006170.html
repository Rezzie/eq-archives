Just a note for any real newbies who may be reading this thread: giving someone a mod rod for the express purpose of getting them killed technically falls under the category of PvP-flag avoidance, and can get your account permanently banned.

Just so you know. If you want to be able to deal properly with beggars and jerks, you have to play on a PvP server -- where they just might be higher-level, or happen to have more friends nearby, and kill you. At least on a non-PvP server they're merely annoying, and if they ever become actually dangerous (intentionally training or whatnot) you can petition.
