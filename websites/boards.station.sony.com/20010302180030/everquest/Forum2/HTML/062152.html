Many items have some 'control' on the number that come into game. JBoots are one of them. The control is the rare-ness of the two MoBs that drop the needed ring.
Spreading out what drops it will also increase it's availability. Won't happen.

I agree single spawns that have a needed quest item suck. It only leads to camping and all the rudeness that goes with it.

I also agree that JBoots need to be controled. The only ways I can think to control an items "spawn" rate is:
- Quest/awarding/droppng NPC/MoB is a rare spawn.
- Needed item self spawns on a timer.
- Needed drop is on random MoBs, but item still on an "available as loot" timer. (No idea if EQ can do this with loot table items.)

1st option is current, and best choice.
2nd option is way too easy, still promotes camping.
3rd option (if possible) is worse than other two. If someone else gets lucky and loots the item you need, timer re-sets unknown to you. It does eliminate camping specific MoBs. The result would be the uber-guilds "raiding" the zone. Instead of just one spawn camped, the whole zone would be camped (ala the planes). Make it multi-zoned and the camping is reduced as much as possible. But, now it's pure luck if you get it, not a "quest" you can work on per say. Might as well make the item itself drop.

Suggest a way to control the quantity entering the game that doesn't promote camping and I'll bet the live team will listen. Given the needed restrictions, I think they have done the best they can.
