I answer Yes, with certain modifications.
First, server should be tagged 'Roleplay preffered' or 'Roleplay encouraged'. While EQ is considered 'as a whole' a role-playing game, a lot of people do not roleplay. I am talking not about people who don't fit under MY or someone else's definition of roleplaying - I am talking about people who do not fit under their OWN definition of roleplaying.

I agree that tag alone is not enough. There must be some difference justifying the tag. How to justify 'roleplay encouraged'? Now, many people consider that PvP is essential for good RP. However, many people choose not to engage in it because of fear of loosing posessions. Therefore, I propose to make the server 'blue', but remove ability to loot from PvP encounters, be it a duel or between players who decided to go PvP+. Disabling of loot would encourage people to go +PvP and have a fuller RP potential. However, since everyone starts out as -PvP, they would be safe from newbie slaughter that is the norm on PvP servers, and would be able to switch to +PvP when they feel more comfortable about it.
