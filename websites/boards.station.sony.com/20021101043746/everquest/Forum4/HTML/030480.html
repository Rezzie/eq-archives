You do indeed need to be using the New UI (standard UI now since it is the default now).

Simply left click on the item in your inventory and hold it down so it keeps the Item Box up. Drag the cursor over the item box so that the box stays up when you stop holding the button down then click on the little picture of the item. This will throw the link into your chat window at the current point.

So if you wanted send it to a friend you might do:

/tell Jackson Check out my new toy!!

And then do it and it would send the message and put it at the end of the line. Works well for raids especially for No Drop items.

As for the /pause function, it puts in a pause equal to the number divided by 10 in seconds. For instance, /pause 15 will put in a 1.5 second pause.

What is the purpose of this? Well, most everything you do has a time set to it. For instance I timed how long it took for me to do one cycle of Bind Wounds so I could set up a button to do it 3 times at once rather than hitting the Bind Wounds button 3 times. Seems to me it was like 10 seconds. My Bind Wounds is the first button in my Ability's Boxes. So then my button looks like this:

Line1= /doability 1
Line2= /pause 100
Line3= /doability 1
Line4= /pause 100
Line5= /doability 1

What this does is have me bind my wounds and wait 10 seconds (the amount of time it takes to Bind Wounds 1 time) then Bind wounds again and wait 10 seconds while I bind wounds, then Bind again.

The only downside to this is that when you are doing an ability setup like this you cant use another Hotbutton until this one is completed. Like my Hotbutton for /who all friends. Although I can type it out and it will show me, I cant use the hotbutton version until I finish doing the Bind Wounds button.

Another example is with spells. Many people set up buttons to do things with spells. Now if were an enchanter and wanting to cast the debuff spells Enfeeblement (1st spell slot and 1.75 sec cast time) and Tashan (2nd Spell slot and 1 sec cast time) you might do it like this:

/cast 1 (casts the spell that is in the 1st Slot)
/pause 20 (Waits 2 secs for Enfeeb to be cast)
/cast 2 (casts Tashan)
/pause 15 (waits 1.5 seconds for Tashan to cast)
/sit (makes you sit down)

Hope this helped

All times are PT (US)
