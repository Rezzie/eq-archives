Skills are not that expensive. Raising Research in 150 ranges is about 18pp per point.

Stat raising 'gyms' are really a no-go. Everyone would just max stats to cap just like they do their main skills. Might as well create characters with maxed stats right away. Besides, there are tons of stat raising gear that will take your stats to max eventually.

What I would like to see are training halls where a melee could train a weapon skill to say... 50. I always found this unfair: if a caster neglects a skill like Conjuration, and decides that he needs it when he is already pushing 50, he han just load a low level Conjuration spell, and cast it over and over till skill maxes.

For a melee to gain weapon skills, he has to fight blue con mobs. If a melee levelled to 50+, and never used a 2HB, and suddenly got a real nice 2HB weapon, raising a weapon skill from scratch at 50 is a royal pain. If you somehow could get those first 50 points under your belt in a training hall, it would make things easier.
