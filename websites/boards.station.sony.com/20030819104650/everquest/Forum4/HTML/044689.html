There shouldn't be any problem sharing the collapsable kits; it's the same as creating your no drop container/kit, then passing the collapsable one to someone else to create the same no drop container/kit. The bank only makes it easier to do it.

The reason they created the collapsable kits was so people didn't have to carry around a bunch of kits for doing tradeskills. The no drop kits you create are how many slots(4-8?)? Most people would rather have 10 slot containers in their inventory for carrying stuff.

As for sharing your opal stein, you still have only one stein, but all your characters share it. If you forget to put it in the shared slot in your bank, none of your other characters will be able to get it.
