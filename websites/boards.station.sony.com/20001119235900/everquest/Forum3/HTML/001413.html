-------------------------------- Fact List Post ----------------------------
-------------------------------- Spoiler Alert---------------------------------------------
(Source Caskorrn on EQForge) me :O)

* Hero Goxnok says
"Very well. I shall follow shortly. You must depart on your own. Go to the PLAINS of the OLD SKINLESS OUTPOST. Beware, they have RETURNED and PATROL the HILLSIDE. Keep an eye on the GATES of the outpost. I will be disquised within their ranks, I shall KNEEL as a sign. Give me this coin as your sign."

*The Overthere outpost is in a huge plain, Firiona is not. No other outposts mentioned host "skinless" beings that have "returned".

*Dark Elves likely count as skinless to Goxnok, because he refers to "spider riders" as "skinless stubs", and the upper halves of drachnids look identical to dark elves. Presumably, scaleless means skinless to an Iksar.

*The hillside of the Overthere is patrolled by dragoons starting at 11pm. They go to the end of the path, then respawn back in the outpost in the morning.

*Charasis is a dungeon in the canyon in the Overthere, The Howling Stones.

*The Overthere does not have abundent drachnids, Firiona does.

*According to Goxnok, the ONLY chance of meeting the traitor is during the meeting, which will be at night outside the outpost.

*The deliverer of the tome (the traitor) is "rumoured to be from Cabilis" but "may be a disguised sarnak"

*The Overthere is teeming with sarnaks

*The Iksar prisoner in the Overthere is level 15, scowls at Iksars, and pleads(the /plead emote) when hailed.

*Dom K'Perl, the book seller in The Overthere outpost, is interested in rare Iksar books.

*Dom K'Perl kneels (the /kneel emote) when asked about [rare finds].

*No other NPCs regularly present in the Overthere outpost kneel when hailed by a level 9 Ogre shaman (aprrehensive con), or kneel when their bracketed text is mentioned.

*Dom K'Perl cons dubious to Iksar SKs, while all dragoons, gargoyles, golems, sentinels, admirals, generals, undead heralds, and at least some undead marines, undead workers and merchants scowl.

*No common NPCs in Firiona Vie kneel to a level 8 indifferent high elf enchanter.

*One undead worker cons indifferent and is part of an Iksar necro quest. The two skeletel pets con indifferent.

* Hero Goxnok is exactly level 30 (source: a level 30 SK posting on Everlore)

* Dom K'Perl is exactly level 30

* No other common NPCs in the Overthere outpost are below 40, except for the skel pets of the general and admiral.
(source for the above two facts: allakhazam beastiary)

* There is a place you can stand uninvis next to Dom without aggroing the sentinels or guardian.

* Handing Dom the coin yields the standard "trying to buy friends?" message that he gives when you hand him any random item.

*Doing the same thing but getting invised before hitting the Give button in order to get an indifferent con yields the same results.

* All of the level 30 guards in Firiona Vie, ie Private Blackbark, Private Goldtouch, and Dune the Wolf scowl at Iksars. So does Sergeant Greenblade, Scout Treekill, and all the other guards anyone has conned.

*The Forbidden scribe in the hidden West Cabilis tunnel sells the exact same set of 3 books that Dom does.

*One of these books is lore no drop, written in Lizardman, and tells of Dag, the Blasphemer of Kotiz (Kotiz is the founder of the Necro guild, thus "The Brood of Kotiz"). This seems to be tied to a Dalnir quest.

*You can hand Dom an emerald to recieve "Before Green", a tome of ancient Iksar history that can be purchased for 3 copper in Cabilis. Giving the emerald yields no noticable change other than the book appearing in your inventory.

*While sitting in the dragoon barracks at 5am norrath time, I once heard a smack followed by the death groan of some unseen mob. The sound seemed to be coming from right next to me.

*I have never seen confirmatin of ANY inter-zone trigger in EQ. (ie, doing something in one zone that causes something in another zone to happen). Nonetheless, I often tried giving the "ready to depart" phrase to Goxnok with a lvl 1 mule, and this had no immediate or delayed effect in the Overthere or Firiona Vie that I could detect.

*Dom K'Perl wants his spare reading spectacle, and blood ink for some unknown reason. His spare reading spectacle comes from froglok raiders in the swamp and is called "a lens of sorts". Ticket stubs from Oasis to Kunark, and ticket stubs bearing Dom's slogan "strain the brain not the eyes" can also be found on raiders.
(I got the lens and ticket stubs off raiders, others have said other frogs can drop them)
Source: SSlith on this board, see "Dom K'Perl - matter of betrayal".

*Blood ink can be obtained from the War Historian in the Warrior Guild in ECab. He needs blood drops from leeches, carnivorous plants, and mosquitos, and one from the blood pit in the Tower of Death.

* The Tower of Death is the Necro Guild. There is a prominant pit of blood at the entrance to the Necro Guild.
(Source: the intro description you get when creating a newbie Iksar Necro, and the necro newbie note. Both reference the Tower of Death as your guild hall).

*Nobody has successfully obtained blood ink and posted their solution.

*Lord Qyzar wants "The Charasis Tome, it's copy, and your zealots khukri"
Source: Xander on this board

*Sometimes, when Dorn B'Dynn is killed, "a dark elf spy" pops up. When the spy dies, a "widow mite" spider spawns and drops "The Tome of Charasis", which ID's with "Item Lore: Charasis Tome Copy".

*All 3 mobs yield "agents of mistmoore" faction hits, while the normal Dorn does not. Note that sometimes Dorn appears as Dorn B'Dynn, and sometimes as Dorn BDynn (no apostrophe). I had heard that only Dorn BDynn can spawn the spy, but this is NOT true as I saw B'Dynn spawn the spy with my own eyes.

* Dorn can technically be level 15 or level 16 but if you are not level 30 or twinked to the gills, good luck soloing him, the spy, and the spider in succession. Dorn double attacks repeatedly for max damage, has a lot of hitpoints, and extremely high magic resistance.

*The spy is a level 22 necro (red to a 19, xp giving green to a 30) and the widow mite is (I believe) a level 10 sk.
Source: Everlore boards.
