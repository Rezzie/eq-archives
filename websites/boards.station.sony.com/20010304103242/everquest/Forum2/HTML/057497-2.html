The 'Major' zoning bug that Prince_Thra?? (something) mentioned is known and has been around for a long time. It apparently comes from the logistics of a 3d world with gravity. Occasionally when zoning you drop thru the world and end up with z (and perhaps x and y) coordinates which don't make any sense. So there is software which places you in a 'safe' spot within the zone. It can't do much else cause it by that point has lost track of where you should be. At one point the 'safe' spot in Kithicore was at a crossroads in the middle of the zone. Very unsafe at night. They have since moved it to close to the entrance to Rivervale. Nevertheless everytime its happened to me since that change, I've died anyway.

This particular bug bothers me less since it happens much less often, and its more understandable to me given the difficulties of modeling a 3d world.

It is not the case that I had actually died because of hit points. Calculations of zoning time and hit points happen on the server, and so are not affected by time delay. Also it is possible to end up dead on the other side of the zone. This is unrelated to ending up at the 'safe' spot for the zone.
