I'd try a different hunting area, a Cleric is only a "nicety" in Lake of Ill Omen or FM. If you make it to level 33 or so I'd try Overthere, its at least easier to get groups there. Unfortunately for you though groups can easily get by with a shaman or druid, in fact they are seen as better in an outdoor zone with their sow and outdoor type DOT's. Go somewhere that you are needed, aka dungeon. Unrest is awesome for a Cleric all the way thru 39th, I say that because I leveled exclusively there from 33-39th in about 2 weeks doing the ghost room. Cazic Thule is also great experience up until 36th or so, you could maybe go higher if you just lived in the Avatar of Fear room..not really sure. Anyways, damage in dungeons is usually far beyond the capacity of backup healers===> you will be greatly needed there. Also, there is a bonus to exp in dungeons so that should be better for you as well. I wish I had some advice for you about Kunark dungeons, I THINK Crypt of Dalnir is good for low to mid 30's but never played Kunark when i was that low...maxed at 50 when it was released Maybe someone else can post advice for there, at least Unrest and Cazic Thule I know are good places for you.
