DaNailz, this may sound a tad cynical, but....

The history of EQ, with which I have been associated since early beta, is that the game runs smoothly for a while. Of course there will always be a handful of players with problems, usually of their own making in the sense that they have 1998 drivers, 28 different applications running in the background, over-clocked processors or whatever, but for most it runs fine.

Then a patch comes out with a major code change. Many more players have problems. Loads post here, more pack out the Chat Server, and we go through the same ritual each time. First, Verant deny there's a problem. Then they say its our systems at fault and we should update our drivers, remove our soundcard, reinstall DirectX, whatever. Finally they admit - some weeks after the patch that caused the problem - that they are working on a fix and eventually another patch comes out that sorts the problem.

At the moment we are approaching the final stage in this all-too familiar ritual. Players have undoubtedly had a major problem with crashing to desktop since the major November update and the subsequent Velious installation. Informally, a GM has now casually indicated on the Chat Server that a fix is being looked into, but can I get an official confirmation of that on this board? Can I heck!

I have no doubt that in a few weeks time a patch will come out that will address the issue for most people....until the next time!

In the meantime, its "check your drivers" as if we hadn't already done that a million times over!
