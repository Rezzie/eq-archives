Unless you are in a heavy duty raiding guild, Takish is a requirement for mages. Level 49 is the last pet your ToSR foci will work on; the replacement will be the Diamond Prizm of Companionship for 760 Takish points. Also usable by necro's. Maybe BL's too. Granted, it doesn't start working till lvl 59 but you really are going to take a while to get those Tak points, so head that way every chance you get. Plat and xp can be farmed anywhere.

What about lvls 50-58? Staff of Elemental Mastery is your only option and they are damm hard to get. Earth and Fire are from Plane of Hate, rare drop from forsaken revenants. Water is from Phinigel Autropos, uncommon drop. Air is from a Plane of Sky quest, and if you get the Crown of Elemental Mastery you will want to turn it in for Epic instead of an Air staff.

So save those Tak points and shoot for lvl 59. I believe all other high end foci are from upper tier planes or Vex Thal.
