Lol, comical.

Perhaps you should find out how the game works before asking for changes.

Str is meaningless in EQ. Although it changes the ATK value this doesn't effect your accuracy and due to the way the combat engine works, the part it does effect (maximum damage value) has virtually no effect on Average Damage per hit.

It doesn't really matter that your Warrior is an Ogre. His higher Strength is meaningless - there is no effective difference in damage output with 100 Str or 300 Str (assuming no cap - which is actually soft capped at 200).
