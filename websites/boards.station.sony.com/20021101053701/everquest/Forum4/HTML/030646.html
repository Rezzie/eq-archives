The Giant Field rat whiskers are one of the insane drops for newbie quests. They are out there, they are no drop. Periodically (without being annoying) let people know that you want them if they don't.

I have found that killing the Giant Rats seems to make the Giant Field Rats spawn. Others say kill all the rats. I mostly stay between the path, the undead ruins, and the Sayer's store for the Giant/Giant Field Rats. Not too many near the Qeynos border area.

Just to let you know, I (a 51 shaman) and a 54 druid friend spent several hours killing every rat that moved and we got no Field Rat Whiskers. I have gotten them 3 times now in my various bodies doing/helping with the quests.

It can be done, but you will need luck and patience.
