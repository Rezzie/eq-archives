I view balance as a compromise between the ability to solo and ones desirability to a group. Pets are still somewhat undesireable to a group. Post 50 they are better but not to the point of them being a desired item. Fear is seldom used while grouping. The lifetaps are useful only when there are MOBs to tap that are not capable of doing more damage to you than you are able to tap from them (need more snow bunnies and fire beetles). I think our power is comparable to other hybrids and melee when viewed from the ablility to solo a MOB. Perhaps we are even stronger than some in PVP.

Were we lack is desirablity to a group. Most of our benifical spells are self only. At the level we get them, our offensive spells are mostly to taunt. Groups react very poorly when the Shadow Knight fears something. Not to say that fear is not a powerfull spell, just that is applications in a group are limited.

Harmtouch (pause for dramatic effect). When I was level 1, harmtouch rocked. It left that moss snake with a sliver of health. At higher levels, it takes off only a sliver of health. If harmtouch had the dramatic effect on a MOB's health that it does at lower levels, a recycle time of 72 minutes is approprate. Every 72 minutes a lvl 50 SK adds 601 points of damage to the group total. Were we to reduce that cycle time and scale the damage back so that it would cycle with kick/backstab/etc., damage an SK would add would be less than 3 points of damage per harmtouch. I understand that a group is in constant combat so many of the potential kicks etc. go unused, and many miss. Assuming the group is in combat 50% of the time and the kicks/etc. hit for an average of 50%, a kick that could hit for 12 is more benificial to a group than harmtouch. As harmtouch will no longer quickly finish a MOB, nore even make sure I am on top of the hate list, the effect of saving up 72 minutes of 12 damage kicks into one single smack is not much more usefull than the 12 damage kick. Would I trade harm touch for a normal damage kick? Without hesitation! I would lose the ability to destroy a single MOB of lawn trash caliber for the benifit of providing more output to my group.

I am not saying that I want to outdamage a rouge. I want to have more apeal in a group setting.
(1) Raise the cap for piercing to that of 1HS and allow a weak backstab whose cycle prevents harm touch.
(2) Trade kick for harm touch.
(3) Allow me to give my mana to casters.
(4) Provide a group benifical spell that I can use up my mana with. Resist cold is usefull, but certianly not something you would seek out a SK for.

None of the above suggestions would make us any better at soloing than we are now. Soloing is what a Shadow Knight does only when they cannot find a group. The SK is not an efficent solo class, they were meant to be in a group. The changes to the experiance table was probably the best change yet to allow a Shadow Knight back into a group. If the changes on test go live, it will be another step toward getting the SK back into a group. Have we reached balance? We are getting there but still have a bit to go.

When I want to solo, I play my Necromancer. When I want to group, I play my Enchanter. When I want to fish, I play my Shadow Knight.
