Jumpin in (before this thread locks down) with a little story:

A while back, I was playing my wiz (then 29th) with my wife (then 26th shaman) and a friend (then 29th druid) in North Karana. We where happily hunting Griffenes. While wandering around, a Griffon [note the spelling] aggro'd on us and we ran toward the spires and hopefully nice and friendly guard who would kill the big mean bird.

The Druid cast a snare because he had a bit of a lead and didn't want to just run away. The snare stuck!. He then cast a DoT (crud?) and it stuck! He told us to try to run the griffon in circles and keep it off him -- he wanted to try an experiment. My wife and I did so, and he proceeded to DoT the Griffon contually. We were able to keep it off him (being hurt in the process) and he could med up enough for another DoT every few minutes. I emptied my entire bar of mana into the Griffon only to see a constant stream of resist messages. My wife didn't even try, she just healed (and renewed SoW when it chose a really bad time to begin blinking) us.

Point of the story? Us at less than 20% life each standing over a dead Griffon. Wizard contribution? Druid pet -- and I never let him forget it!
