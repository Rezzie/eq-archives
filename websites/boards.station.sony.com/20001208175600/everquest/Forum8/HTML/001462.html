Several things are wrong with the recipe you posted. The actual recipe is this:

Enchanted folded sheet of metal
Royal Temper
Long Blade mold
Hilt mold
Pommel mold
An emerald or a ruby (Not imbued)

You can confirm this recipe in-game by asking Dren Ironforge "What about Antonian Longswords?" I am confident that, although he does not say one way or the other whether the gem must be imbued, it does not have to be, since that would make it a deity-specific weapon.
