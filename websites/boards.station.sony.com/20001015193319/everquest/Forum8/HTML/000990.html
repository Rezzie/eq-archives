I dropped my new fine plate bracer into the forge along with dark blue dye, acid, and lacquer (I think that's the combination), clicked the combine button, and got the "You have created a new item", and the "Your skill is too high to advance" or whatever it is that the message says now. Then, I put my bracer on, switched to an alternate view to examine the coloring, and found myself looking at...

The normal black forearm sleeve that is found on standard male Erudite plate armor skins.

I took my other bracer off to confirm, swapped them around to compare possible differences, and otherwise examined my character's forearms.
No difference at all.
A couple of possibilities come to mind with this.
1.) Somehow, even though I got the success message, the piece didn't dye. This seems highly unlikely, however.
2.) For whatever reason, dye won't show up on some or all of the Erudite armor pieces. Unfortunately, my bracer is my only current piece of Erudite armor, so I was unable to test the dye on another piece to see if another area of armor would work or not. But the possibility that the male Erudite plate armor is bugged with dyes is not particularly surprising, considering the well-known bugs for the chain and plate gauntlets, and the plate helm.
Anyway, that's what happened, and those are my ideas as to why it might have happened. I hope that whatever the problem is, its correctable. Erudites got less than almost every other race from this patch, and I'd hate to see yet another thing screwed up.
