Well, I logged in last night with my Ogre Warrior, intending to get him that last couple blue bubbles to 11th level.

What's the first thing I see? That neato yellow messgae telling me that the Paladin Best of the Best tourney started in the arena an hour ago!

Guess where I am? Right outside the arena hunting Aviaks! SWEET! So I zone in immediately. There's 2 GM's patroling the entrance.

I ask "Kin me go's ta see da fightin?"

GM "Sure, sit down"

me "Sit? *looks confused* otay, if'n you's sez so.

GM "/grin"

YOU HAVE BEEN SUMMONED!

Yeah, they had another GM go to the edge of the platform there and summon me. It was cool. I just switched my view and could see both fights going on. I got there just in time for the final 8, final 4, and Championship. They had 2 fights going at once until the Championship round. The last 2 could use the whole platform.

Can I just say here, that PALADINS TAKE A DAMN LONG TIME TO KILL! ROFL Between their buffs, healing spells, and LOH, these guys fought forever! OMG!!!

It was exciting though. It came down to Berglad, 60th Dwarf Paladin, and Kilt 60th Human Paladin. Best 2 of three for the Championship. They split the first two fights, with Berglad winning the first, and Kilt the second.

Aand the winner is? Berglad, now called "The Champion"!!!

I had to laugh though. After a fight, the GM would Death Touch the winner, so he'd get his LOH back and come back with full mana. He'd also dispell both of them about a dozen times prior to the start of the fight so neither started with an advantage.

All in all, it was fun to watch, though I tell you, them're some LONG fights!

Congrats to "The Champion!

Bashim 'Ard <Winterborne>
10th Ogre Warrior
