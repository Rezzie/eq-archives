In Highpass Hold, if you camp the gnoll side level 17 is good with smaller groups, maybe 4 people at level 17. A shaman would contribute well in a group here.

On the orc side...lets look at 2 cases

Case #1 - FFA (free for all) you can't do enough damage to get the kills alone, and a shaman will lose out to a caster in a group due to the FFA atmosphere.

Case #2 - one or two groups camping the orcs, trading the spawns. A shaman will do well here.

Runnyeye is a little used zoned for a couple of reason. The main one is that some high level mobs wander a bit. So something very pwoerful (Borxx??) can wander in on your little campsite. The other reason is that since it is empty of adventurers, a runner can pull some powerful friends creating bad trains.

I would recommend SK since you can bind in the zone as a caster. You will need to group but at the aviak village there tends to be a FFA pulling atmoshpere. Everyone will setup their own camp outside the village, then go grab a bird and pull it to the campsite. Spawn rate is good enough to keep everyone happy.
