Windows ME came out with a new PPTP (point to point tunneling protocol). this caused many internet specific programs to stop working. I suspect that it may be a problem related to that and your ISP. I would try different ISPs to see if that is the case (Juno, Altavista, Excite, and about 20 other servers all have free ISPs that run with banner ads. I use these for troubleshooting). If you can prove it to be an ISP problem then call tech support for your isp and see if they can fix it.
