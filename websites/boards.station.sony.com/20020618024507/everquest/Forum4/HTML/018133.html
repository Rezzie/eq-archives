No, go to the Warrens. At your level you will be after several of the named mobs: The Trainer, Smithy, Foodmaster, Packmaster and perhaps the Prince. Most of these drop Soft Wicker armor as well as bronze weapons, coin and other loot. At Level 11, my Barb Shammy staggers out of there completely encumbered. Collect the kobold molars and turn them into Tiam in the Pally guild for, XP, faction, coin and occasionally a piece of ringmail. If druids can't wear it, then sell it for plat.

All the green mobs have a chance of dropping bronze weapons, molars or dusty durlap armor. It's a fun place, relatively safe (I play on Rallos, which has other issues) and extremely profitable.

You are looking for something that is easy to kill and will give you money, if I understand you correctly. Unfortunatley, you will have to violate your druidic ethos for what is my recommended solution.

You didn't mention your race, but I will assume that medium size armor will fit you (all races but half-elf, basically). Kill cats and bears in West Commons and stock up on the pelts. You can sell medium and high quality pelts to tailors for a few plat each, or maybe trade for some armor. Studded Leather is decent and Reinforced Leather has an almost identical AC to chain mail.
Kunark is the best place you can go for exp, but it is not that great for loot until 25+. Kill a few cats and bears to enhance your bankroll and then give it a shot. Giants in FM drop great oot around 25, and at 29 you get ports....

Cats will be a lot easier to kill than stuff in the warrens and you don't need to enter a fairly confusing dungeon to hunt them.
