It's a level 55 spell. So first of all using 2 peridot (of which all 55 enchanters probably have at least one stack of 20 if not two at all times), at a cost of 21pp, is essentially insignificant.

1000 HP isn't bad; it will keep you alive, and, more importantly, uninterrupted, long enough to cast an emergency spell. Not as good as Divine Aura or Harmshield, but OK. Casting time of 1 sec is nice.

The killer is the spell slot. There just aren't any free spell slots for this type of spell. Saving your life is fairly irrelevent. It's a rare 55 enc that doesn't know a 56-60 cleric; death is just an inconvenience - it's not worth a slot that could be used for a spell that helps the group. Most high enchanters don't even mem gate; they certainly won't mem this.

So the cases where this spell could be used:

1) Situations where if the enchanter dies, the whole group will die. Probably, an attempt to break a difficult area. But it means everyone sits there after the enc mems it, until his spell slot lights up 10 minutes later, you break, then unmem it and replace it with something useful. Kind of useful, but a bit tedious.

2) Quests (esp epic quest) where you have to get in, do something fast, and get out, hopefully alive. Or soloing.

On the other hand, if this were a clickable item effect, it would be quite cool - say the item that currently casts rune IV - this would be a whole lot more useful. You'd need to keep the 1 sec casting time, though, and the 10 minute delay.
