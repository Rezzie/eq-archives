Increase DEX, dex effects your chance to hit even if it is a week blow.

Your race usually governs how fast you learn, dark elf warriors learning fast with 99INT and a good DEX and Trolls and Ogres and halfings falling in last. (halfings are really dumb for some reason)

Fast wepon over damage, the more you swing the more chance of improvement, dual wield will also help i find 2 hand blunt a nightmare to raise.

This is the list of easyest to harders to raise:
Percing (V fast delay)
Slashing (fast delay)
Blunt (average delay)
2 hand slash (slow delay)
2 hand blunt (slow delay)

One hand slash and piercing are easy to raise at low lvs, with superior weopns available, blunt wepons just dont cut it here, For blunt you might want a CIMMS first to start rasising blunt (fine steel warhammer is delay 30, fastest blunt) or a Dwarfen mace for magic wepons.
