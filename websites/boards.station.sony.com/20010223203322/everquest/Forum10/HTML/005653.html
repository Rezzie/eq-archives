Here's what you can do though to keep from being mind numbingly bored.

1) Guild chat! Always a plus.
2) Read a good book! Read a book you've already read, so you can keep half an eye on the screen and still not miss anything.
3) Watch a movie! You DO have the computer in the same room as the rest of the electronics right??? I can see the TV screen with just the turn of a head!
4) Listen to some music. Tastes vary here...
5) Do quest research, or print out zone maps, reorganize all your EQ notes so you're more efficient. You DO have a second computer right next to the one you play on right?
6) Do laundry. Not to bad if the laundry room is close. I even fold clothes when medding or when regen'ing HP!

On long, boring camps, I like to re-arrange my spell book. In case you don't know, you can move around spell icons in your book by Right-clicking on the Icon you want to move, then right-clicking on the slot you want to place it. If you place it in a slot where an Icon already exists, it will swap out those two and place the old one in the new spot and the new one in the old spot. Get it? Also, you can create fun emote hotkeys like:

"%T, have you ever seen a Halfling with moves like this?"
/dance

You can get your spell skills up. Divination is usually pretty low.
