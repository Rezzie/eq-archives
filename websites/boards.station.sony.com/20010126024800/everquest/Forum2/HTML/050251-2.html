and now:

9) Powerlevelling is killing the game

It is funny, for all the things killing the game, it sure is doing well subscriber-wise.

With regards to powerlevelling, I have done it a lot and I can assure you that I do most of my power levelling away from other people. I do this so no one else is affected and so there are few distractions for me.

Most people that powerlevel are PLing their twinks, not their main character. After you have played the first 20-40 levels with two more characters, the desire to play low levels is long gone.

The only way I could see someone having a problem with this would be if they are jealous. A good powerleveller will go through multiple levels a day so you won't see the same person more than a day or two so it is not like it is an ongoing distraction for anyone.
