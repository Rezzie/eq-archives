Up to level 40-45ish I think Paladins are balanced wonderfully. It's 45 - 60 ish range when we start becoming underpowered. I don't expect to be "as good as" anyone at anything, there'd be no point in having different classes, but, I would like to maintain the same equivalent effectiveness we had back at 45.

Couple ideas that would help this;

1) increase offense skill max. Warriors, Monks, Thieves etc. hit both more often, and faster. It would be nice if the 2 swings we DID get, connected at least close to as frequently as those classes. You're still only getting 2swings to their 3-4swings, but atleast you're not missing 50% of them.

2) maintain stun & bash effectiveness. A major benefit paladins have is the ability to disrupt spells with this, and to prevent damage for like 2 seconds (enough to switch monster's target) when it b-lines for the wizard. Possibly change the 49 or 53 stun to make it go through magic defense better - like how high level wizard spells ignore resistances better.

3) maintain healing effectiveness. At 30th the healing spell heals about a bubble of a casters health, at 39th greater healing heals about a bubble of casters health, at 52 or so superior healing would heal a bubble of casters health but.. oh wait we don't get it til 57. Maybe drop superior healing down some levels and put in celestial healing at 57 or something like that? Again, just maintaining base effectiveness throughout 60.

4) defensive capabilities should be higher. I like the 252 defense cap proposed, I think the other defensive skills should be equally upgraded, we'd still have less ac & less hit points, but we wouldn't get slaughtered to the same extent.

On a side note to defense (pipe dream), It would be great if armour class was different from defense & dodge & riposte. Armour class would be solely concerned with the AMOUNT of damage you get hit, and the rest would be solely concerned with the NUMBER of times you get hit. The way it would work -
Monks might get hit 2-3x out of 10, but for 200ish damage, I mean come on, they're wearing robes!
Paladins might get hit 6-7x out of 10, it's hard to move fast in all that armour, but for maybe like 40-50ish damage.

Cap'n Touk
