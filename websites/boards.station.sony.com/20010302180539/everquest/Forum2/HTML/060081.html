I noticed in another thread somebody wishing for caster haste. A Druid friend the other day that the he would probably keep the FBSS he was buying for a warrior twink if it sped casting up by that amount as well.

What it comes down to is melees have a whole range of items to drool over (haste) that dramatically affect their usefulness, but casters have zero equivalents. Then you have bards, where they can improve the effects of their songs with enhancing instruments. Caster's only have items which affect the size of their mana pool - it is the equivalent of only letting tanks have HP increasing items.

I understand that there can't be an instant mana potion or spell. Limiting the amount of mana coming into the game is key to how EQ is tuned for providing a challenge right now. And reducing cast times would have some situational advantages, but not do much to differentiate a caster in the long haul as their mana is still the limiting factor.


So finally I get to my idea... A line of equipment that influences the EFFICIENCY of casting.


Instead of a 21% haste belt for a warrior, it's a 5% efficiency belt for a caster. This means that a 300 mana spell now takes 285 mana. Same cast time, same effects. Differentiate the items by class or type of spell. Now you have a LOT of casters fired up about equipment, i.e. something to look forward to besides a higher AC/resist version of reaching max (casting stat).

If done carefully I can't imagine this would upset the balance of encounters in the game, as casters are already not the main damage dealers on uber MOBs. Also, introducing items like these would allow you to address the complaints of many nuker classes on large raids. The wizards feel their damage is a drop in the bucket against a 200Khp MOB? This is a way to adjust their contribution by the amount of the item. You could add buff spells and songs in much the same way as haste works. Add the items as you rework the old world zones, I guarantee you will get folks back to the mainland for this stuff.

Probably too late a stage in the game for this sort of addition, but I thought I'd throw the idea out.

I had suggested something similar in the wizard class discussion, and Absor blew me off. There are five different buffs that are needed for casters:

1) lower mana cost on spells

2) higher damage on spells

3) faster casting

4) Lower resists

5) Improved mana recovery(clarity is in game, but the effect isn't on items).

Each of these has a similar effect for melees, stamina, speed, dex, agi buffs for their melee abilities, yet nothing for casters. Casters also see zero increase in ability based on stats, while melees see a dramatic improvement based on stats in the form of increased damage output and defense ability.

Again, Absor seems to feel that any request for this sort of change is a waste of time based on his response to my clearly worded suggestions. You decide if I am justified in losing all respect for him for his dismissal of a good idea.
