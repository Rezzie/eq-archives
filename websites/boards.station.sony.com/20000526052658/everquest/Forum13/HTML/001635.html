Crippling blow is a warrior only ability that was recently added. For those of you who don't know what it is, crippling blow is the ability to land quad damage hits ONLY when a warrior is berserk.

There is a serious problem here on how "rare" this ability happens, and I think it should be changed to happen at any given time.

Warriors DO NOT solo past 12 or so. I could see crippling blow when berserk be useful when a warrior solos and fights past the 2 bubble, fight or flee decision time. But that fact is, they do not solo because downtimes are way too great and without twinked equip, it's just too dangerous.

This leads us to Situation #2, the grouped warrior. Warriors almost always are grouped with healers. Now, in order for a warrior to crippling blow, he has to DANGEROUSLY go under 2 bubbles of health. But with a healer in the group, THIS NEVER HAPPENS unless the fight is WAY TOO CLOSE, or they are going to die anyway. Healers keep warriors and other tanks ABOVE 2 bubbles because it's not worth the risk, and if they do not, they are FAILING as a group member. Therefore, warriors NEVER crippling blow, their ability is a complete, and TOTAL waste.

Now, if you teach a cleric to "let you go berserk," you are really playing dangerous. When your health dives under 2 bubbles, your stats start taking a hit. Yes, you may be berserk and may fight better, but your agility goes WAY DOWN, and likewise your AC does too. When your AC is low, you have a much better chance for taking max damage, and it is so much easier for you to lose those last 2 bubbles of health than it was the first 2 bubbles. At one moment, you may be berserk and doing fine, then WHAM, double attacked max damage... cleric starts to heal ya, DOUBLE MAX DAMAGE, dead.

Whoever designed Crippling blow, IMHO, did not think it through. It should NOT be tied to berserk. It should be an ability that occurs ANYTIME, slightly rarer than critical hit. From my warriors career from 7-20, I have seen 3 crippling blows. One when I pulled 2 aqua goblins by accident and had to fight while berserk. The other time was when I was jumped by a white gargoyle and died, but I scored two crippling blows. Way to go! I'm still dead. Does it really make sense that a warrior will score a more powerful shot when he's almost dead? No.

I cannot agree with you, Gordon, when you said "warriors will be quite happy with Crippling blow" for such a rarely seen and anti-group mentality ability. Please look into this.
