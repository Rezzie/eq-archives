You didn't mention which server you were on... Most Tailors actually understand your growing pains and may try to help you through them. I would.

I recently picked up 2 backpacks full of Medium Cat pelts. I hate making studs, but I do enjoy making studded armor more than trying to work through having my cured become trivial for the same reason.

Power sewing can be very frustrating, but don't give up on Silk Armor yet. There is a demand for it. I usually carry a variety of patterns and offer to make pieces for as little as 1p if the casters/monks supply their own silks. This works very well out in West Commons and even NRo Platform.
I tailor as a hobby and know I will be a master one day, until then I just fill my vault with HQ Pelts.

Again, if you are determined to sink money into failure, that is your choice, but at least wait until raw silk armor goes trival around the high 30's.

My tailoring is in it's mid 50's and every once and a while, I will shred a HQ cat & Bonings just to see if I can even make a simple reInforced mask. I haven't been successful yet.

My Smithing skill is above trivial for making studs, but I continue to fail attempts despite the 40 bits I had before starting. I try to make useful pieces to give away to the young'ns and they look so sharp in them too.

Good Luck
