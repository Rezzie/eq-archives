I would spend 400pp or so and buy a treeweave. I'm currently level 51 and still using mine. So, suffice to say that it would be a good investment to pick one up now. Not to mention the 25AC in your main armor slot will be nice.

After that I would make sure you have decent weapons. I would look for any of the following weapons (prices based on Vazaelle so may be different on your server)...

Blackjack - 50ish PP 1hb
Needlite Stinger - 100ish PP Piercing
Master's Etchikata - 100ish PP Secondary H2H
Jagged Chokidai Spine - 100ish PP Piercer
Centi Warhammer - 200ish PP 1hb

and possibly a 2hb if you have some extra cash...

The point being to have one good weapon of each 1hb, piercing, and Hand 2 hand. 2 hand blunt becomes good much later in life. Once you get dual wield I would mix and match weapons trying to keep all your weapon skills up.

As for the rest of you armor look for Ulthork hide armor. It has great AC but awful stats so is usually sold for less than 10pp per slot. That should fill in nicely and other spots you have.

With any left over money look into the 4AC/45HP, 5AC/55HP, 6AC/65HP rings. Buy the best you can afford.

With all of that (which should be in your 800pp range) you will be well equipped into your 30's. After that you should have enough to start swapping things out piece by piece as you get enough money.
