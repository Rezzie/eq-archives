Sorry to have to say this, but there won't be a level 49 song for us.

We are Bards, the "red-headed step-children" of Verant.

Ours is a class that was rather poorly thought out in the first place. The songs we have are nerfed versions of the enchanter class spells with a few of our own thrown in (Mana song is our claim to fame). But we are far from warrior/enchanter hybrids, not even close to how a ranger, paladin, or shadowknight are based.

We get plate armor and the same hp as a shaman/druid/cleric...so we get no hit points for being a "hybrid". We get dual wield, which is much weaker than double attack, which all the other hybrids get. We don't get any kind of secondary attack form either, like kick or bash.
