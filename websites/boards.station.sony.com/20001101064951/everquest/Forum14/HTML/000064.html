Mwa-ha-ha-ha! Having been a 30 Cleric for a solid week now, I feel your pain.
However, dream on if you think you're going to break the hell level solo. I'm sure it may be possible, but by Brell it's gonna take you FOREVER! You'll spend so much time sitting on your arse meditating; time that could be spent getting experience with a group.
Your best bet is to find a good dungeon, get in a good group and stay there. When you have to camp, camp, but when you come back, stay there! Find another group. Don't go flitting around all over Norrath. That's what I did for the first few days of this level and my xp bar barely budged. Now I've been in Unrest for two days with groups camping the tower or pulling from the basement and I'm halfway to 31. My husband broke his hell level 30 by spending the entire time in Splitpaw with groups. Clerics soloing can be done...but why?

We are designed to be a group player. Trust me, those you see at a lower level and group with, you will see again. Learn who to trust, and who not to trust at 10, 20, 30 even 40, rather than groups at 40+ when the items you really want get ninja'd because you did not know who to trust (the rest of the zone says "we know" when you complain in /ooc).

Also, grouping will become needed the higher you go for survival (thinks of Karnors and shudders). Would it be nice to know which players will pull everything onto your group just to kill the "rare"? Or which will bail on you in a bad situation? Learn it young!

Who can you trust to drag your corpse (since it can't be looted anymore) to a safe spot, not dropping it in the Lava in SolB or under the King in Lower Guk.....See above.

The reverse is also true. Can people trust you to heal them (and not blast yourself to OOM early in the battle). Can groups trust you to share the loot fairly (or are you a ninja). Many of my favorite groupmates I met very young and met several times on my way up to higher levels.

I often get tell's now... please come group with us we are going to (fill in the blank). You better believe I consider all of the above before I say yes. Just as others think of my past behaviour before they choose to ask me along.

In my opinion and observations, soloing is the resort of the cleric who has no skills, or a bad reputation in grouping.
