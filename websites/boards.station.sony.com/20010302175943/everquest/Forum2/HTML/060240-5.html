A post supporting REZ affects instead of item loot.

The problems with item loot have already been clearly detailed. When you think about it, item loot is really a penalty of time, the time that a person spent in order to acquire that item. Why have item loot? Why not just penalize the time *directly*? Rez affects do just this. I believe Rez affects are a viable alternative for the following reasons:

1. All players share the same risk. (no bagging, no wearing all no drop, no differences based on item necessity for melees)

2. There will be retribution for cheap kills. With item loot, if you are killed in a less than honorable fashion there is no retribution because the killer wears nothing and risks nothing. With Rez affects, the penalty for death is unescapeable.

3. Reward. Items are vital, at least in the perception of the players, to the game. This is why item loot causes players to go out of their way to exploit the rules in order to receive the reward. I firmly believe that rez affects (ie. having the offending player be put out of commission for ~30 minutes) is a much more appropriate reward for the killer, and introduces the dynamic of zone control.

Concerns:

How would the rez affects impact corpse retrieval and therefore corpse decay? I would not advocate a rez penalty that was so harsh that it would not allow you to retrieve your corpse. A player should be able to retrieve their corpse (but not have a chance in hell in pvp) while still under the affects.

Lastly, a question for Absor.

You said this was not a topic where we were likely to see a list. Does this give an indication as to the amount (or lack of) resources that will be committed to these issues?

What of things like Deleveling, where not a single poster is in defense of such a tactic?

There are many differences of opinion in this thread, but there are also many areas of consensus. I hope the topics of agreement will be heavily considered for implementation.
