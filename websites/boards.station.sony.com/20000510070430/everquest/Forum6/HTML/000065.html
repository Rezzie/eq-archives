Hello Fellow Guild Leaders of Lanys T'Vyl,

I am Angralad, proud leader of The Light's Tribunal. You may or may not have heard of us, for we are still in the growing stages and fairly new to Norrath. To summarize what we are about (although you can check our WebPages at http://www.simming.org/eq for further details about us): role-playing. While we play the roles of Light’s Soldiers in search of ridding Norrath of all evil and its friends, family and pets (and anything even remotely resembling evil) we do so with a twist. Our members (while in-character) are extremely fanatical and while the end result is for the good (as is their ultimate intentions), the methods used to achieve this are... questionable. Take a look at the website for further details.

Like I said, we are about Role-playing and as such we are looking to have engaging role-playing experiences in the game. While we can do some of this by ourselves using internal plot lines and guild events (like our recent raids on Dervish camps and Crushbone where there was some sort of Role-playing reason behind it) we wish to REALLY get into it and have lots of interaction with other people. Obviously we need help from willing partners. It is so that I am looking for other guilds with similar Role-playing interests
(and similar age, for our members are still between levels 10 and 20) to role-play with.

Basically we need one (or all) of these:

A--. An evil guild. I don’t mean a group of people being a** holes under the cover of evil characters, but a group of Role-players who wish to give Good, something to fight. With them we can have lots of role-playing fun in the traditional Good Vs Evil way. (Not to mention guild wars)

B--. A good guild who, like us, claim to champion the light but whom we consider to be blatant impostors (and they think the same of us). Who's right? The Nameless knows but in the meantime role-playing scenarios of intrigue and espionage (no to mention conflicts here and there) abound.

C--. Another good guild that, in our ultimate knowledge of what is good and is not, my guild
considers legitimate defenders of The Light and so allies itself with it. Not as terribly
interesting as the first two, but its a good start. Lots of stories and joint ventures can
be woven out of an alliance with another guild.

Role-players are rare in Norrath and frankly, we want to share our sandbox with someone who wants to role-play too. If you are out there, please send me an email at isfmata@simming.org and we can discuss the details either in game or through e-mail.

Thanks

Angralad
Paladin of Mithaniel Marr
Lord Captain of The Light's Tribunal
http://www.simming.org/eq

Ahhh... greetings friend! Allow me to introduce myself! I am Mukhtar Ibn Asim, gatekeeper, wanderer of Norrath, and an officer of the Guardians of Dawn... at your service!

ooc: The Guardians of Dawn are another RP / storytelling guild that was formed a couple of weeks ago on Lanys. While our primary interraction thus far has been offline in the form of fiction writing with our even more recently formed nemesis guild Shadow's Chosen, I think it would be interesting to add a group of firebrand waving light worshippers like the Light's Tribunal into the mix. Right now I think all of the roleplaying guilds on Lanys are struggling to achieve that critical mass that will enable them to create a true RP environment... perhaps by banding together for a few joint events now and then we can spark even more interest.

Overall I think we would be most likely to fall into category B. Though we are good, we have many characters who approach the light / darkness issue much more carefully and thoughtfully than a group of "fanatics" (as I perceive the word) would... My own character bios in essence forces me to, and I think would raise more than a few eyebrows in your ranks. If you're interested in finding out more, please send me (Mukhtar) a tell in game. Also, you can visit our website (nothing fancy and no flash or shockwave, but lots of writing and character bios) at http://www.members.tripod.com/silverdragen/index.htm
