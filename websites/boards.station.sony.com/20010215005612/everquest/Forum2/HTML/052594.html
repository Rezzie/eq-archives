I just wanted to toss out another suggestion.
At present lambent is 90% a twink item. When you see a lvl 20 or 18 in full lambent, you can be pretty sure they didnt kill the Basalt Gargoyles or Goblin Elite guards themselves. I'd like to propose two modifications to the lambent quest which would of necessity go hand in hand.
First (wait for it) make all lambent armor NO DROP.
Heh Heh. Good to hear the gnashing of teeth. This would not totally end guild twinking, or even make it that much more difficult to get the armor. You sit and wait til the appropriate creature is killed and ask to loot the NO DROP component. Guilds could still twink their guildmates, it would just be harder. It would end the twinking of newer characters by higher level characters, or at least make it a LOT harder. (Park bard, bring in the high level, camp until the component is dropped, bring in the bard, loot, run).
The second part of this (a necessity to ameliorate the effects of the first). Make the components drop off of more than one spawn. Reduce the drop rate perhaps, but have more than one spawn drop them. When the quest was originally put in place I believe there was just the original world. Now there are two new continents. Spread the drops over all of the continents. Have there be two or three spawns that drop each of the components.
As it stands now, many of the components require a group of 30+ hunting zones/spawns that arent necessarily that popular (oasis gobs and the mistmoore spawns particularly). I realize that verant isnt known for sympathy but what is happening (as I'm sure you know) is that non bards are picking up these items as they adventure and then completing the quest and selling the lambent. As I said at the beginning, most of the lambent now in existence is a result of either twinking or buying the armor in EC.
It would be nice if Lambent was a symbol of a difficult quest completed, rather than simply and expensive purchase.
