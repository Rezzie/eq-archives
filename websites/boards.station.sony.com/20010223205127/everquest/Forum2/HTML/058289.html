The thing is, the way EQ works, there's a certain distribution of character classes across the levels. Some classes are more represented than others.

So, you get a lot of high-level bards who copy their characters over, because every spell change seems to screw up one song or another. Now you have about 7% of the population are bards on Test, which is double a prod server.

So they make some change to a dragon mob, for example. And on Test, there's a bard in every group, buffing resists, and all goes well.

Then on prod servers, the dragon is unkillable, because there just aren't that many bards.

That's a rough example of why the Test population has to level up normally. It's also why they don't generally buff up levels there either.
