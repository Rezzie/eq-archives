Hope you fellow monks out there can answer a question for me? I learned Feign Death a level ago and am curious: When I successfully Feign, and the mob that was attacking me leaves, does the Feign still affect any other mob that happens to wander by or is trained by, if I am still lying there? I thought of this while hunting in Unrest. We got a bad pull near the fountains and got overwhelmed. Everybody else ran, I Feigned. After a little while, I got up and moved to the wall. Just then, another train came roaring by, which led me to the question. Had I continued to lay there, would the Feign have fooled the next mob, or is a check made every time a new mob gets within a certain range of me? Would be nice to know for future reference. Thanks for reading and safe travels to all of you!

If you feign successfully, the net effect is that you now con "indifferent" to all creatures. You could have napped in the way of 50 trains and never noticed... once the feign works, until you get up, nothing will attack you. Note that you can still get caught in area effect spells... so don't feign in front of Naggy
