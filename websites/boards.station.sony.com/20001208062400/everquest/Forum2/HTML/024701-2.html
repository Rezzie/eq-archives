Hi Absor. In case you didn’t see the original post, I’ve updated it and placed it below. A quick summary of what you’ll see:
1. Easy fixes – graphics & item issues.
2. Biggest bard issues: lack of any real mez abilities (not functional now) and haste stacking.
3. Class neglect issues: stuff that hasn’t worked in long time or ignored for long time. Broken quests, useless songs, lock picking, disarm traps, instrument pieces, and bugged clients.
4. FA weapon. Issue for high-level bards.

If you need any clarification on any issue, please let us know or post on Concert Hall site and we’ll get you any empirical data you need (test data/logs).

Bard Bugs & Issues/Not a Whine (v2000.10.24)

BROKEN GRAPHIC ISSUES
* Drums look like flutes when equipped. Strumming a flute looks quite silly.
* Brahm’s Horn has no graphics when equipped.
* Raw bamboo (wind instrument) has no wind animation – should be same as a flute.
* Female bards stab themselves in neck when playing horns (try a mino horn)

BROKEN ITEM ISSUES
* Mask of Songbird – Plane of Sky item weighs 5.0. Obvious typo. ALL other POS masks for all other classes weigh 0.5 or less. Not sure if this has been fixed yet.
* Belt of Concordance (POS item) Has AC6. Bards are plate class not chain class (War AC10, Pal AC8, Rng AC6)
* Songweaver’s Mantle (POS item) Has AC9. Bards are plate class not chain class (War AC12, Shd AC10, Rng AC9)
* Imbrued Bracer (Hate item) – has ac9 (chain class), the same as lambent. AC should be plate equivalent.
* Imbrued Gauntlet (Hate item) – has ac12 (chain class), the same as lambent. Also has 5wis. Wis is the one stat not useful to a bard. Change to str/dex/cha/int/sta.
* Imbrued Helm (Hate item) – has 9wis. Wis is the one stat not useful to a bard. Change to str/dex/cha/int/sta.
* Both Lizardman Scout Fife and Froglok Escort Fife are unusable as instruments. Gordon – “not intended to be used as instruments”. Rebuttal – any unusable swords in the game? So few instruments as is (see suggestions below).
* Singing Steel Effects. Single largest problem is that not made with bards in mind. The stamina regen (arms) and health regen (bp) are inferior to existing bard songs that would provide 2x the effect and would be to entire group (versus 1 target). Eye of Xoom (helm) useless to a class with bind sight, invis, and sneak/hide. Summon food (pants) and water (boots) redundant with foraging, bards can travel to towns quickly to get food, and besides a stack of 20 food and water costs a couple gold at most. UW breathing (even though redundant with a song) and fire damage shield are nice effects – no complaints there.

BROKEN SONG ISSUES
* Crission’s Pixie Strike (28) – Mez song that just doesn’t work. Unless a highly resistable, 0.01 nanosecond mez is what was intended. Tons of posts at Concert Hall site with statistics on how poor this song works. VI promised to look into song prior to and then after Kunark, but no answers yet.
*Syvelian’s Anti-Magic Aura (40) – Song appears to dispel magic, but the bard receives no feedback message when used on mobs (e.g. “MobX looks dispelled”). Not sure if this has been fixed yet.

BAD TUNES (MOST OVER LEVEL 50)
* Kellin's Lucid Lullaby (15) – Song is essentially unusable due to 80% resist rates. Song also overrides higher-level enchanter mez spells. Bards have done extensive testing of this song at all levels, charisma, and mob levels and have found that: higher charisma increases resists and higher level increases resists. GZ has stated song should have 35% resist rate, but actual numbers are twice this (from what bards believe, bad coding in secondary resist). VI has stated that song was tested and is working as intended, but offers no proof of testing or what was actually tested. Without this song, the only form of bard “crowd control” is charming or single target mezzing (not that it works either). The amount of evidence of this song not working is enormous and can all be found at Concert Hall site.
* Selo’s Constant Chain (23) One of two songs unintentionally nerfed when 54 song was changed. Song used to stop green mobs in their tracks at 50% health, since patch does not stop mob until down to 20%. Song also overrides higher level snares of other classes. Song needs to be rebalanced in light of unintended nerf. Prior balance was based on greater power, at the expense of short duration 0-9 seconds and higher resists. Now, song has short duration, 3 second casting time, and higher resists than snare/root. If left with same strength, please consider increasing duration, resists, and make lower than higher level snare spells.
* Solon’s Song of the Sirens (27) – Charm song that cannot be refreshed before breaking on non-PvP servers, but is refreshable on PvP servers. Should be refreshable on both. 10/5/00 Hobart “Tested on PvP and non-PVP, there is no difference. You cannot refresh charm.” 10/6/00 Bards on Rallos insist that it is refreshable. Currently an issue because it is only real form of bard crowd control.
* Denon’s Dissension (35) – Intended to be a mana draining song. Drains mana at an incredibly slow pace. In PvP, a player with breeze or clarity can easily outpace mana drain done by this song. Use against mobs of same level an exercise in futility unless bard has about an hour to drain the mob’s mana.
* Solon’s Bewitching Bravura (39) – Charm song that cannot be refreshed before breaking on non-PvP servers, but is refreshable on PvP servers. Song also lowers magic resistance, which seems to be intented to ease recharming the mob, but on non-PvP servers – a useless debuf. 10/5/00 Hobart “Tested on PvP and non-PVP, there is no difference. You cannot refresh charm.” 10/6/00 Bards on Rallos insist that it is refreshable and that is what MR portion of song is intended for.
* Jonthan’s Provocation (45) – Song is a mix of Str+AC+ damage absorption shield. If shield is used up, all other effects fail. Bards don’t want shield removed (it has uses), but song would be much more useful if other effects could stay up after shield fails.
* Nillipus’ March of the Wee (52) – Song gives ac+agi+ damage absorption shield. All effects fail when shield is used up. The problem is that the shield cancels out from one quarter of one mob hit. Can other effects remain up even if shield fails?
* Song of Twilight (53) –Upgrade to mez song (28), that mezs and reduces magic resistance. If target is struck, the stun is eliminated as well as the magic resistance. Can magic resistance debuf stay in effect when mez is broken?
* Selo’s Assonaint Strane (54) – An AE slow song intended to be upgrade to level 20 AE slow song. The problem: Instrument has no effect on 54 song, resulting in a 20% slow versus 35% from level 20 song.
* Song of Highsun (56) – Supposed to stun, cancel magic, then self shadow step. Problem is, if target resists the song you don’t get to shadow step. This should happen regardless of target resisting the song. If cannot be separated, perhaps increase the range and change the order to: self shadow step, stun, cancel magic. Low priority, as there are no bards out there using this song.
* McVaxius Rousing Rondo (57) – Haste song intended to be an upgrade to level 50 and level 54 songs. The problem: Instrument has no effect on 57 song resulting in a 22% haste versus level 50 (30% haste) and level 54 (45% haste). Using an instrument should double the effectiveness of this song.
* Jonathan’s Inspiration (58) – Song gives str+ attack speed+ absorb damage shield. Yet another song
where when damage shield is used up (1/4 of 1 mob hit), all other effects fail.

BAD QUEST ISSUES
*Fabian quest. Currently costs more to do quest than reward. You must kill a rare 32 mob in north karana, (~8 hour spawn), take guts to qeynos, pay 50gp, and bring strings to Fabian in FP. He will give: a few sp, increase to faction and an etched silver coin (1/3 of the time). No clues anywhere as to what coin is for.
* Winds of Karana. Kill hermit in SK (level 20+ mob) to get a level 1 song.
* Bard epic quest weapon: many bard threads on this. Biggest issues: (1) use of red AND white dragon scales conflict with warriors, paladins, and casters. (2) affect is a non-stackable proc that is marginally better than songs and worse than enchanter/shaman hastes. (3) includes 10 mana - should go to different stat (4) bards would have preferred a universal instrument that is better than all other instruments – now that would be an item worth getting.
* Temple of Solusek Ro. NPC has been saying “come back and ask me about a special bardic weapon” for over a year. Not in game. Take out the text if it isn’t going to be added anytime soon.

OTHER PROBLEMS
* Stuck songs – bards occasionally get stuck on a song and are unable to stop it. Difficult to replicate, but appears to be associated with lag. Once stuck, the /stopsong command has no effect. The only know cure is to spam the hotkey/gem repeatedly (a few dozen times) or log.
* Start/stop songs – client issue where bards twisting songs have the song stop immediately after it was just started. Looks like: “Your feet begin to slow down” (bard starts selos) “Your feet move faster” then immediately see “Your feet slow down” (and left without any speed effect).
* Songs breaking rogue sneaking. Two issues: bards able to break rogues sneaking even if rogue not in same group and bards singing mana song break rouges sneaking even if rogue a moderate distance away.
* Chords of Dissonance (2) – no animation when playing song and no mobs in range. Low priority, but logically, the bard is playing a song so should be able to see something is going on.
* Disruptive Discord (18) – no animation when playing song and no mobs in range. Low priority, but logically, the bard is playing a song so should be able to see something is going on.

ODD CLASS BALANCING
* Lock picking. Bards get skill at level 40. Skill is capped so low that bards can only open doors in Befallen (level 20 dungeon) and gate to the Hole (50+). Raising skill would be nice to open Kunark doors – why is a level 40 bard opening doors in newbie dungeon? (check bard count at primetime hours, and you’ll be lucky to see more than 12 45+ bards on any server).
* Disarm Traps. Ok, no traps to disarm. Rogues got new skills (backstab & evade) to make up for this, bards were promised it would be looked into as well, but never got anything.
* Skill caps on singing, percussion, string, brass, and wind still at 200 for bards over level 50. Other casting classes have casting skills up to 250. Gordon said he would look into increasing these.
* Dodge/Parry. Bards get skills at 53 and 58 respectively. Far too high for most players to ever see them and excessively high compared to other classes that get these skills in 20s and 30s. Both parry & riposte capped at 75. Gordon said this is a reward for getting to high levels. Below is summary of all melee/hybrid combat skills to put this into perspective:
Bard: O1/D3: Dual Wield(17)// Dodge (10), Parry(53), Riposte(58)
Monk: O6/D3 Round Kick(5), Tiger Claw (10), Dbl Attack(15), Eagle Strike(20), Dragon Punch(25); Flying Kick(30)// Block(12), Disarm(27), Riposte(35)
Paladin: O2/D4: Bash(6), Double Attack(20)// Dodge(10), Parry(17), Riposte (30), Disarm(40)
Ranger:O3/D4: Kick(5), Dual Wield(17), Double Attack(20)// Dodge(8), Parry(18), Disarm(35), Riposte(35)
Rouge:O3/D3: Backstab(10), Dual Wield(13), Double Attack(16)// Dodge(4), Disarm(27), Riposte(30)
Shadow Knight: O2/D4: Bash(6), Double Attack(20)// Dodge(10), Parry(17), Riposte(30), Disarm(40)
Warrior: O3/D4: Bash(6), Dual Wield(13), Double Attack(15)// Dodge(6), Parry(10), Riposte(25), Disarm(35)

OTHER SUGGESTIONS/OBSERVATIONS
* Denon’s Desperate Dirge (43) – 800 mana for AE 275 damage (including bard if in range). Breeze/Clarity/Mana song have no effect on bards mana regen (takes 45+ minutes to recharge when OOM), making this song the absolute worst in terms of damage/downtime for any class. With 39 charm song now using mana, bards never use this song. Other classes could easily do 275 damage and recover the mana in a couple minutes at most. Suggestion: compare against other caster’s AE damage spells relative to downtime, remove mana component, and put song on timer (say 3 minutes)?
* Bewitching Bravura (39). Upgraded charm song that requires mana. The biggest problem with mana requirement is that: (1) bards have 0 mana when rezed (2) bards are not affected by clarity/mana song/ or mana stones (3) it can take bards over 45 minutes to get back to full mana. High level bards are extremely constrained for significant amounts of time after a rez.
* More instruments! Huge gap between newbie instruments (~5gp at local bard hall) and stuff found on dragons/gods, and virtually nothing in-between. If planes get upgraded, bards will really be hurting for instruments.
-Brass – brass horn at local guild OR McVaxius Horn of War (+15% to brass) off Lady Vox. Nothing in between.
-String – lute/mandolin at guild OR gypsy lute from mistmoore (+5%) OR Kelin’s 7 String Lute off Maestro of Rancor (+22%). Enormous gaps.
-Wind – flute OR unicorn horn (0%) OR mino horn (+15%) OR Piccolo off Cazic-Thule (35% to song).
-Drums – decent mix from newbie to high end. Hand drum OR Mistmoore Battle Drums (+15%) OR Sharkskin Drum (+22%) OR Nostrolo Tamborine (+25%) OR Selo’s Drums of the March (+35%)
* Would also be nice for better instruments to look different in some fashion from newbie stuff. A tuba, bongo, or 3-drum percussion set would be really cool. The tamborine was a nice touch.
* Sing emote. Nice for roleplaying. Low priority though.
* Still missing 49 song- thanks for asking for our input Abashi. Lots of good suggestions out there.
* Could musical instruments (when viewed) contain information on which skill they are for? Percussion/String/Wind/Brass. Not always obvious on wind/brass instruments. Also, would be fantastic to see something like “+10% to some brass” or “+5% to combat wind”. Often, only very high level bards can baseline instrument improvements today (hard to see 5% improvement if song only adds 5 strength).
* Instrument pieces. Lots of bardic instrument pieces at vendors (including one in Kunark), but no way to put them together. Perhaps add to tinkering or put in bard only instrument skill (like research). Low priority, but pieces have been around since beta and still nothing to do with them.

Enchanters will always be the masters of CC, bards will never be their equals -- but we _could_ (&, again, it seems we were meant to) fill in as the cheap seconds. That's all we're asking for on the CC issue.
