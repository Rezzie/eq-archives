(34-15) * 5 = 19 * 5 = 95

To research you must first buy a tome of the appropriate type for your class. This tome is actually a container. You then combine the proper ingredients for the desired spell in the tome, and if you are lucky and/or sufficiently skilled in research, they transmute into a spell scroll which you can then scribe into your spell book.

The recipes for the various spells are available in books sold in-game, or you can save yourself the coin and find them on various web sites like Caster's Realm (eq.crgaming.com), The Mage Compendium (www.magecompendium.com) etc.

There's also a thing called "practice research", which is a way to raise your research skill without wasting research components, but I don't recommend it. The items you need to make the practice runes are often hard to come by. Better to just dump your training points into the research skill, in my opinion.

If your character is wealthy you can skip research altogether and just buy the spells in the Bazaar.

All times are PT (US)
