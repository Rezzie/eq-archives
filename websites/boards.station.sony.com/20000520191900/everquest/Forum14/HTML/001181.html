I am now a druid of the 22nd season. I too really liked Oasis until lvl 19. It was then I went with my pally buddy over to North K and East K.
In North K we hunted Borer and Systhe Beetles. Good exp poor loot. In East K we hunted Crag Spiders and Gorge Hounds and at night we hunted the Undead Gnoll Reavers.. Better loot...
However at lvl 20 we took off for Unrest. Good zone. Many trains though.. so be careful, on the upside very good loot. Many things drop Fine Steel and Bronze, plus you can sell in Dag.

Well that is my thoughts I what I did.. Happy hunting..

East Karana is about the best place I found for leveling. Just sit at the Beholder Maze zone and kite hounds around. If you get into trouble there is a couple NPC's near by to help you out. NPC's will also buy stuff from you there and makes in very convenient. As an alternative you can try South Karana as it has a lot of things to hunt at and around 20+ level. I have soloed almost completely up to 24th level before deciding to move on, as the overcrowding was making pickings slim.

Hope this helps.
