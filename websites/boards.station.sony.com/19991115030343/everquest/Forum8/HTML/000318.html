Originally, the trade skills were intended to produce a "small amount of side cash" (look in your everquestmanual.txt file.).

IMHO, they are currently broken due to an over reaction and should be titled "Hobbies" instead.

If you have 4000 plat, you can make player desirable items with jewelry.

If you have 300 plat, you can make player desirable items with smithing. However- because it only costs 300 plat to take up, over competition periodically makes it unprofitable.
