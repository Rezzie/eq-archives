Not sure how it goes on other servers but on Mith Marr most high lvl guilds are going through major major changes.

Guilds that have been around for almost 2 years are falling apart. The reason? Many guilds are forming up that are following the Afterlife "model"

Afterlife was founded a while ago by players from many guilds that were raiding together a lot (before kunark was released) they left their guilds and formed a new raid guild.

Afterwards they recruited players to help them from other guilds and still recruit players they need (mostly clerics)

When the drinal came along Cats in Hats and Clan Skallagrim and some other guilds left for that server.

Afterlife continued on Mith Marr, after a while, many lower lvl guilds began losing players , and a couple new raiding style guilds sprung up. Complete with level limit requirements, gear reqs etc...

These newer raid guilds were built on the premise similar to afterlife, forming a guild for the purpose of raiding high lvl areas.

Now what has happend is they too have to recruit needed players for raiding.

So what is happening. These raid guilds are recruiting all the high lvl druids, paladins, rangers and necros for their raid guilds.

Err.. I mean CLERICS WARRIORS ENCHANTERS hehe. Of course they are NOT recruiting high lvl rangers and necros.

yes they are taking clerics from other guilds, most noticably high lvl clerics.

This leaves the other guilds full of the "useless" raid classes.

Lets face it, even with the cleric nerf, they are still the core batteries to any raid.

So basically slowly but surely older guilds fall apart and die, because as more and more of their clerics and warriors leave little by little, the effect on the guild is exponential. 1 lvl 55 cleric leaving a guild is like 10 of any other player.

As the new raid guilds recruit the "needed" classes the other players get left behind wondering what went wrong.

The recruiters say "we cant help it if they want to join our guild because your guild is boring to them"

Those that lose the clerics say " we are growing and were getting more ready to do the high lvl content but with a cleric lost there and a warrior over there, we get less and less able to do so"

I have witnessed no less than 10 lvl 50-60 clerics leave guilds that they were in for a year or more, even officers and leaders of, watched them just pack up and say "later"

Why are they doing this? because Verant felt it necesarry to design Velious to be raid only high lvl uber zones.

Basically if you want to do anything on Velious you need a raid guild behind you.

Now months later Verant decides they are going to "fix" this problem by nerfing clerics. Well nerfing clerics just means instead of 10 clerics on a raid now we need 15 clerics, which means more recruiting which means other guilds dieing off again which means the rangers/druids/paladins/necros are left sitting wondering what happend to their medium powered guild..

You know the guild that raids hate/fear a couple times a month, raids Kael/Thurga a couple times a month, does Sebilis / Karnors most of the time, has 1-5 epic weapons and maybe 20 lvl 50+ players.

A guild with 20 lvl 50+ players is fine, but take 1-2 clerics and 1-2 warriors out of that make up and suddenly you have 16 lvl 50+ players and not enough clerics to do anything other than farm Hill giants.

that guild is essentially dead.

thanks to $0V uber guild only apply, guilds that have flourished for 22 months are dead in their tracks, devistated by the raid guilds NEEDING to recruit clerics.

The ONLY solution on my server is to join them, yes and this is what I see, friends giving up on their main characters and twinking up clerics so they can join the raid guilds too.

Its very sad, and its the nail in the coffin for many respected old guilds. They are dieing, guilds that used to have 100 members and 30 lvl 50+ players have been completely picked apart, 3 lvl 50+ clerics leave and boom the guild has been reduced to raiding Naggy and Vox again.
