Looks like a can of worms got openned here actually. In thinking about it, I probably would not buy Velious again knowing now what I know. The Graphics are good, the dungeon and outdoor zone designs are generally good and the quest system is great, but the mobs are awful.

Risk via Reward:

For me, the reward of playing a game is simply having fun, hopefully with both new and old friends. Things that I have fun with involve exploring new areas, seeing new things (and hopefully killing some of them), playing with new toys ... while I am certainly not Farmer Fred, I do love having a new and neat piece of equipment to exclaim over, and advancing my character to become more capable of doing all of this.

Velious does not really fit the bill here for me. My cleric at 44 can barely make the trip to Thurgadin alive. Usually I spend about 30-45 PP on Invis potions and SoW potions. And even then invis seems to drop a lot - usually right in front of an elder snow griffin. Risk vs Reward. I don't make this trip anymore unless someone brings me along. What that means is that I am not usually anywhere near Velious for rezzes or grouping. Someone else's risk vs reward.

My druid does hunt a bit in EW and GD when bored. Typically I group with Necros or Mages since their pet/spell combinations work well. But I frankly prefer DL or Burning Woods where I can be more than a snare machine with SoW. Oh, I forgot, I can also save the mages some mana by damage shielding their pets.

I don't have any of my 39 or 44 Velious spell drops. Nor am I in a hurry to make a long run through a city filled with scowling giants who automatically see invisable on people doing Corpse Recovery. So I don't have any of the ports. Risk vs Reward - a major corpse recovery filled with multiple deaths vs getting a TP spell to a Dragon circle where the dragon is ALIVE! Or one where roaming wurms might kill me before I even know whats going on if my ISP gets overwhelmed as I port.

I will probably not see Wakening lands or anything past the front door of Kael Drakkel. Experience is slow in Velious and risks are high. I have had people rave about the experience hunting bears in Great Divide. I get more experience than they do in Dreadlands, Kaesora and Overthere (I've actually been hunting in these zones and compared exp). Again the exception is a party of pet users. Oh, and I actually stand a chance of soloing a mob that jumps me on my way to those locations.

I guess what I am trying to say is that I just don't have a good feeling out of Velious. At level 44 as a cleric or as a druid, I have died so often that I have lost any desire to explore it (6 deaths in Kael Drakkel trying to get to Wakening lands for example).

Kunark on the other hand I am still enjoying after a year.

I have a suggestion for any future expansions. Make the maximum size of a guild 35 members. Then scale encounters and mobs to groups appropriate to what a 35 member guild might field. The vast majority of EQ players do not belong to Uber guilds. Stop trying to force people into supersizing guilds (and would you like biggie fries with that) in order to explore and "do" the new content.
