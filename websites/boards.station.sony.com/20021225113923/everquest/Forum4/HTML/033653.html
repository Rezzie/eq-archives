The only time you have to pay more than your basic subscription price is if you want to go with the "Legends" service and play on the Stormhammer server. For the $13 a month you can play on as many of the other servers as you'd like, with as many characters as that server's rule set allows (usually 8).

You do have to put out some money on a one-time basis for any new expansions that come along (ie. Planes of Power).
