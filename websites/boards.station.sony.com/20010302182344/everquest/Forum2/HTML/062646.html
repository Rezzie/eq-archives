Feburary 28th, 2010

In this patch we did some major zone updates.

After completing our one year server test surveys, we've concluded that the average player populous level base was, 60th level. This patch address some of your concerns and helps to alieviate the boredom factor from "our world" of EQ.

- Retooled Blackburrow. Please be aware that the Blackburrow now has creatures of porportionate level equivilant to the EQ player base. We have also renamed the zone, Fippy's Revenge.

- Revamped Crushbone. As with Black burrow we ahve increased the creatures levels in porpotion to the server base. All of the crushbone legions are now 58th level and higher. Emperor crush himself sports 512,000,000 hit points. You've been warned.

- All outdoor zones are now considered unstable rifts. Thus any player attempting to hunt in these will have to make a successful save verse death in 15 second intervals.

- On the 4 planes. We once again retooled the planes. You will be automatically be killed upon entering the plane. Cazic, Innourukk, Bristlebane, and Tuanre all have 5 billion hps and are 99% resistant to all spells.

- In order to make the game difficult, we feel that healing classes are no longer needed. We have removed Clerics, Shaman, and Druids from the game. The only healing class that we kept in is Paladins and Rangers. As an added bonus however, we have placed light healing potions on various vendors around Norrath. Since there is an abundance of platinum in the game, we have made the cost 100,000 per does.

- Be aware... If you are found with more than 1 million platinum on your account, you WILL be banned without review. We will not tolerate any player hoarding platinum under their matresses.

- We have underestimated the ability of the most powerful guilds on the servers once again to get around changes that we've made to the game. Thus we have placed additional code to the server.

* Battle lag. We have increased the amount of lag that untra high powerful creatures output, in order to force more than 50% of players in a major combat to go linkdead. We find this is absolutely nessisary in order to ensure that game difficulty is retained.

* Reduced item placement. With the amount of items already proliferating the servers, we feel it makes no sense to even create any additional items possible from creatures. We have placed Fine Steel weapons back on the gods.

* Random item deletion. Additionally, in order to reduce the amount of items on the servers, we have instituted code to randomly destroy one of your items everytime to log out or go linkdead. We figure since alot of people will be going LD due to the new battle lag code, we should have enough nekade charaters in the game to make it difficult again. Please note, GMs will not do any item replacement due to this new code.

In other news! Rallos Zek and Fennin Ro, being our last two server sets are going to merge next week. This should bring the totally combined server populous to 600 at peak times. We realize that this may cause some issues between the PVP players and NoN PVP players, but we are working on some code that will allow the NoN PVP players to be immune from attack. More on that during the next patch.

Once again we have done it! The Forgotten Lands exspansion will be released this summer. This expansion brings you 6 completely new zones with 60th level creatures and beyond for your pleasure. Remember all those free zone updates we promised 8 years ago, well here they are, all wrapped up in a nice little package.

This weeks bonus bargain!

Buy another 6 months subscription to Everquest and get a one month demo of subscriptions to Star Wars Online! free! This is an exclusive offer only for those dedicated Everquest lifers. May the Frogluk be with you.
