Hello,

I recently acquired the Sorcerer/Sage Robe and am very proud of it (3rd on the server I believe) The only problem is that the effect has been rendered useless in the patch that increased the cast time of Donal's to 30 seconds because it increased the cast time of the Sorcerer/Sage Robe to 30 seconds as well.

The effect is the 44 spell, Enticement of Flame (6 second cast, 240 mana), a 360 point lure dd. The normal cast time of this spell is 6 seconds (1 and a half seconds slower than all the rest of our lures). The cast time of the robe is 30 seconds. That is 5X the normal casting time of enticement and 6.6X the cast time of our other lures.

Here is the problem, at the level you get this robe, you already have normal lures (I did not even bother getting enticement) that do twice the damage of enticement (640 dmg for 320 mana and a 4.5 sec cast time). In 30 seconds with just clarity, I can med enough to cast a regular lure doing twice the damage. I also can not afford to basically be stunned for 30 seconds (can't cast, can't med, can't move) for 320 worth of damage. That makes this totally ineffective for a combat situations.

One of the main reasons given for the increased cast time of Donal's was that it was not meant to be used in combat. The Sorcerer/Sage robe is obviously a combat piece since it is a direct damage spell. Also, a warrior on one full tank of HP can do substantially more damage than 360 points but yet both the Donal's and The Sorcerer/Sage have the same cast time.

There are other DD items that have MUCH lower cast times that have not unbalanced the game, especially considering the fact that there are currently 3 on my server. I understand that you can not count rarity as a real factor but this si not exactly bought at the local market.

I was looking forward to using this robe for high HP Velious mobs. Unfortunately, since I can med faster (I almost always have C2 on at the level you need to be to acquire this) and nuke for more damage than casting the robe and being stunned, there is NO advantage to using this Sorcerer/Sage robe effect. If I have overlooked a situation that this robe can be used in over med and nuke myself, please let me know.

Please change this to a realistic cast time of 9 seconds (2X the cast time of our normal lures)so that is has some use in specific situations. At 9 seconds, I would still rather use my regular nukes (draughts 740 for 215 mana 3 second cast)the majority of the time but the Sorcerer/Sage Robe would come in handy for Velious HP mobs and those rare times I am OOM and need an extra bit to finish off a mob, which is what I believed the intention was for the effect.

Hopefully this is a well thought out post and Absor can see the issue clearly. Thank you for your time and I look forward to a fix of the Sorcerer/ Sage Robe to make it the really nice piece it was intended to be.
