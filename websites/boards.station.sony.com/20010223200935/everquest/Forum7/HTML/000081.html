Aowyn

My guild found itself in a similar situation recently. Our leader simply went AWOL. We never saw him for months. Eventually, we decided to try and get a GM to appoint a new leader. We were told that the only way was for us to disband and choose a new name. We could however disband, wait a month, then keep the same name. Apparently the Gm cannot take leadership away from a guild and give it to others. Even tho the leader has been banned/died/stopped playing, whatever. We were saved by the fact that the leader showed up and turned the reins over to our new Leader. I hope thatv he see's your case differently. Good luck.
