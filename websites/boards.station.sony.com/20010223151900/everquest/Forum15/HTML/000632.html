Pause command is measured in 10ths of a second, so pause 30 is a 3 sec pause. Twisting scripts are good for downtime/non-battle times when you're singing clarity/hymn, or during travelling singing levitate/selos. They are not good for battle, if your song fizzles that leaves you with 3 secs of doing nothing.

/pause 27,/cast 1
/pause 3,/stop
/pause 27,/cast 2
/pause 3,/stop
