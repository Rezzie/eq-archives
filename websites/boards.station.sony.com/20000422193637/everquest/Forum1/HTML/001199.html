As a former GM for several games (though not EverQuest), I am well aware of the standards for behavior and, in turn, standards for discipline. This situation was handled VERY poorly. A GM is very much like a doctor. Their first rule is DO NO HARM.

That said, these people were exploiting a bug, and should have been given a warning. In the event of further exploitation, the account should be suspended - any further use of bugs after the player returned would be grounds for banishment.

cdjc: I agree 100% with your view on cheaters/hackers/people who exploit bugs, but that just isn't the way to handle a situation. I haven't seen the code of conduct for EverQuest GMs, but I have no doubt that what this GM did was against it.

Unfortunately, the GM positions are much like many of today's police officers - those that want the power are the ones most likely to abuse it.
