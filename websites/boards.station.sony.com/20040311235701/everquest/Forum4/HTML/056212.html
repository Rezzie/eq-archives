quote:
I think a necro or chanter could pull it off solo.

That would have to be practically a miracle. I've seen 3 person groups fail (SK, chanter, cleric). There isn't a class in the game with enough sustained DPS to solo a 3-man ldon. Any caster is gonna have massive downtime and/or miserable DPS (those mobs are gonna go down extremely slow with just a pet and dots). Any melee wouldn't have the enough healing power. A chanter MIGHT have a chance IF a charmable mob happened to be the first thing in the dungeon, but that hardly ever happens (and would be practically suicidal anyway - I've gotten splatted by charmed ldon mobs even with a cleric around on occasion).
