It's kind of hard to say what the exact stats would be, but having a single flag will have all resists at +1 or +2, I forget which. You can be sure that none of the stat boosts will be any higher than +2.

Getting Magelo Charm stats to appear

I would've thought for sure that entering all my flags would make the charm stats appear, but it didn't. Took me a couple weeks to find the spot to make the charm show stats because I never thought to look in the one spot I had been avoiding.

This is how you do it:
-Go to the "Update this character" section. Yeah, this is the spot where you enter your character's level and base (no gear) statistics.
-Scroll down until you get to the "Charm bonus" area.
-In the box "Bonus applied to your charm item" enter a number from 0 to 200. I kept using increments of 10% until I got the charm to show the statistics I wanted.
-In the box "Bonus applied to your augment charm item" enter a number from 0 to 200. Yes, putting an LDoN augment on your charm is possible, and this is the spot where you show that.
-Don't forget to scroll down the page and hit "Update".
