Page 1: Velishouls Tome Page 8
Page 2: Velishouls Tome Page 9


And yes, page 8 is faded. If you have access to the bazaar, you can usually find someone selling faded pages. Just put a single faded page in your tome and combine. On sucessess, you will create a page. Yes it's tedious, but, if you're like me, you like having high skills and you can increase your research by "restoring" faded pages. Also, people like me will usually have extra copies for sale in the Bazaar for around 10pp, for those of you that don't want to waste resources learning to research.
Not sure about being sold in the Great Library. Prolly is, as most of the old world research spells are (at least for chanters).
