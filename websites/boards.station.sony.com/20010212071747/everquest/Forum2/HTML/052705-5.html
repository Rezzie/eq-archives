Finally, asking for spirit of wolf with illusion spirit wolf seems grossly unbalancing to me. I would rather see the illusion removed than have spirit of wolf added.

Low level training spells:
I don't feel that enchanters need a low level evocation spell. Evocation is a skill that should be difficult for enchanters to learn. Enchanters aren't generally thought of as great nukers. There are 50+ enchanters who have almost no evocation skill. However, if an enchanter chooses to work on evoke, I feel it should be a challenge to them to add, what I feel, is a very useful tool to the enchanter toolbox. This is one of the things that balance enchanters. Slow direct damage with a good chance of fizzle due to a low skill. Asking for a low level training spell for evoke is like asking for 0 dam 15 delay weapons so melee classes can easily work up all their melee skills. You have to choose what kind of enchanter you want to be. If you want to be able to use direct damage, you have to work at it.

I feel the same way for conjuration. Conjure is used primarily for the mesmerize series of spells. This skill is very important for enchanters and some what difficult to raise. Mesmerise costs 20 mana can can be cast every 4 seconds or so. Another way to train conjure is to cast pendril's animation and then reclaim energy. Enchanters are supposed to be a difficult class to play and I think this is one of the aspects to that. Fizzling on a mez at a critical moment can spell doom for an entire party. For my enchanter, that means a few hours spent at the guild working on skills for each level. That is a small price to pay for a reduced chance of a fizzle at a critical moment.

Finally, there is one thing the enchanter class should have. A group invis and see invis seems like a useful addition in tune with the theme of the enchanter. It seems like that enchanters are one of the best classes at invisibility, however, druids get a group invis and enchanters do not. No one, that I know of, gets a group see invisible. I think that would be quite useful for moving a party through a dangerous area. Perhaps a group invis and later a group invis? This is mostly a convenience issue as invis is very very cheap to cast.
