Hello,

I read the message boards very infrequently, usually just getting my news snippets from Everlore. I know that melee binding has been covered, and I agree totally with Verant on not allowing them to be bound anywhere but cities. My question, however, is this:

Is there a mechanism that is in the game, or could be inserted into the game (via spell or potion) that would allow a melee to be gated or teleported back to their bind point?

I don't see how this would upset balance of the game. It would just allow melee classes to be bound at a certain point (i.e. a bank or such in the city), and at the end of the night of adventuring, 'gate' to the bank to deposit their loot and such. The melee would also be more flexible to go on a guild raid with friends on Antonica, then at the end of the night 'gate' back to their bind point in Kunark (FV or OT) where they may be soloing. It would not change the fact that melees can only be bound in cities, but would be very helpful to the flow of gameplay.

Thank you for your consideration,
