say I am a beastlord (I am a cleric to be honest).

I have the Group Paragon (1200hp HoT +400ish mana regen).
I have Mass Group buff.
Paragon is on a 15 minute timer
Mass Group buff is on a 72 minute timer.
I can hit Mass group buff, and 1/10th a second later hit Paragon, for the effect of Mass Group Buff Paragon. There is no limit for your AA use times except their own timers
