Hollowshade moor is constantly fought over between the Grimlings, Owlbears and Sonic Wolves.

If there are no Owlbears at all there, you have to wait until either the Grimlings control the whole zone, or the wolves do, then kill the Prince that spawns in the Shar Vahl Outpost (I think), or wait for the zone to be re-set, since the war is often bugged and gets stuck. I have better luck with perfect pelts in Paludal Caverns. I think I have got 4 from there, while only 1 form Hollowshade, while I have killed many, many more owlbears in Hollowshade.

Actually, you may have just PO'ed quite a few people who are looking for smithing supplies. Hollowshade has a continual, scripted war. There are three opposing factions (actually 4 if you count the vah shir). They are Owl Bears, Sonic Wolves, and Grimlings.

The owl bears reside in the caves in the north west region.

The Sonic wolves reside in a cave on the east wall, close to the zone to Grimling Forest.

The Grimling camps are in the South East corner of the zone in and around the moores (on the islands).

Every now and again, agressors from one camp attack the other camp. Under normal circumstances, they will loose. However, if a player intervenes and helps the invaders, they can win. When that happens the camp respawns as the aggressors (that is, if grimlings attack owl bears and win, all the owl bears poof in the zone and are replaced by .... more grimlings).

When one side dominates, the shar vahl outpost respawns with a boss mob. Killing the boss mob resets the war.

Now, the reason this may have screwed smiths is because smiths usually manipulate the war so that they can get owl bears and/or sonic wolves to take over the grimling camps. When that happens, they can farm mass amounts of Wailing and Shrieking substances (which are used to smith shadowscream steel armour). If you manipulate the war properly, you can get tons and tons of drops in but a couple hours (I have collected 2 whole stacks of shrieking substance in 2 hours before by farming wolves).

The interesting part is that the wolves/owl bears will not drop the substances unless they have the grimling camp. Even then, only the replaces ones in the grimling camp will actually drop what is needed.

The other interesting part is that in most instances, the war "breaks" right about when one faction gets the entire zone. They are suppose to populate the vah shir outpost where you can go in and kill the head guy, thus resetting the war.

I have yet to see this happen. What is the usual case is that the outpost never gets taken, thus the MoB (or trigger) that you need to kill doesn't exist. In short, you cannot reset the war. This is more prevelant when the grimlings take control. The only way to reset the zone is to get a GM to do it. Which after many a petition, would still not get done.

When you went into the owl bear caves and started to farm them for pelts, you pretty much ensured that the next invading force would win, thereby wiping owl bears from the zone.

There are other places to get Perfect Owl Bear Pelts. They drop off of all OwlBears. You could take the wanderers in Hollowshade (which does not effect the war) or take the owlbear cub pack in Paladul (4 static spawns on a fast timer) or the owl bears in grimling forest (which is a scarey place to hunt solo).

Anyway, just thought I would let you know

>> When you went into the owl bear caves and started to farm them for pelts, you pretty much ensured that the next invading force would win, thereby wiping owl bears from the zone.

Actually, not true in my experience, only if he was killing the 5 defenders that spawn at the entrance.

When one force attacks another side, 5(?) attackers are spawned and start marching towards the opposing side's cave/hut. At the same time, 5 defenders are spawned in front of the cave/hut (these are the ones that will summon you if you start to fight them). This is typically where players need to intervene (killing off the owlbear defenders) as the attackers will only interact with defenders (and will usually lose).

The problem is though, owlbears are the weakest of the 3 sides and will easily lose to the grimlings/wolves - even if they're left alone. (I'd give a rough guess that owlbears have a 33% chance of losing the encounter.) While grimlings and wolves will completely stalemate, unless one side gets extremely lucky.

Drop rate of perfect owlbear pelts is darn close to 1:100 (I forget the details of what I posted a month or two ago when I was slaughtering them for hours on end) - off of ANY owlbear in the zone.

Aside from the spawns in Hollowshade - there are only a handful of other owlbear spawns:

Yelloweyes in Paludal (folks who are considerate leave this spawn to the newbies hunting the bandit camps)

(4-6?) spawns in a cave in the south section of Paludal

(2) spawns in Grimling Forest (shared spawn with a special type of sonic wolf) on the north side of the outpost.

All times are PT (US)
