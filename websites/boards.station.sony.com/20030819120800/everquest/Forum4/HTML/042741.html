Stuff in Marus Seru should still be DB to you at 32. You can stay there until 35. Since there's amost no features in this zone, there is no lag.

Best place to hunt is at the Sanctus Seru tunnel. There's a "bowl" in front where Stonegrabbers, Lightcrawlers and Greyhoppers all path to or spawn. The occassional Zelniak will aggro from a long way off too.

If you get too many Zeniaks or Lightcrawlers, you can run to the guards, they will help. The guards will not attack the Greyhoppers or Stonegrabbers, so be warned! And each creature type is social with others of its kind, but not with each other. When you need to med just run to the back of the Sanctus tunnel.

You won't get any loot to speak of, but the Greyhoppers drop LQ hides that can be tailored into leather padding and sold in the Bazaar for 10pp each, or you can sell the raw hides for about half that.

If you want a break from those critters, the Recuso (bandits) give faction with Sanctus Seru and have decent coin. The ones around the camp at the NE corner of the zone (north of the Netherbian zone entrance) are easier to solo.

One never-camped place is the Golra's in TD, the first island over from the FV zone. If you are not KOS you can gnome transport to the Ranger Outpost and sell there, plus it is an easy hop to the island.

At 32 all the basic Golra's are dark blue. The next step up is yellow. Run for cover if the named one should spawn.

Also around this age you can do the tunnel from the porting area of Dreadlands.

If you don't mind the faction hits to the corrupt guards of qeynos a small group can do the two corrupt guards in the tower near the barb camp in WK. Then you run the bracers up to captain at the stone bridge (zone to SK) and get even more exp and better loot for the turn in.
