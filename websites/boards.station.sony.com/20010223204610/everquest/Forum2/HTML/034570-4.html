Absor,

IMHO, having the staff of elemental mastery: earth as a rare drop off a god in a plane is criminal, and not just because it drops at the rate of 2 a year for mages vs 200 epics a year for rogues.

It is criminal because the guilds that can clear island 7 in Sky and thus complete magician epics probably left Hate and Fear behind them long ago. Sending them back in there many times over just to get an epic piece simply crowds out younger guilds who want their planar armor, and causes unnecessary crowding, waiting times that stretch into the months (remember rubicite?) and endless friction and angst.

The solution is easy, and the idea came from a previous poster. Move the Staff of Elemental Mastery: Earth to the UNDERUTILIZED zone full of earth elementals that is called The Hole, and put it on Master Yael with a 20%-50% chance of a drop. Do the same with a number of other epic items for other classes that were wrongly placed on Cazic and Inny (move them to boss mobs in underutilized zones).

This proposal will do wonders for the whole game in terms of drawing guilds to raid empty zones, reduce crowding in the planes, and reduce the luck aspect while maintaining the difficulty aspect of completing the magician epic.
