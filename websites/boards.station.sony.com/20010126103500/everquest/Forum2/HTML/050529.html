I agree with rogue4hire - trying to find a group at low levels is very difficult, unless you're on new servers, and even then it is hard. There's a lot less "mystery" to the game now, so encounters are naturally easier, and then the twinking makes it even easier.

But Jyn has a point, too - I'm not quite as cynical but it would be a major task to level cap all of Everquest's items.

The easiest way would be to add a field to the item database which would contain the level of the person who looted/quested that particular item. That level limit wouldn't stop lower levels from using it, because that would be kinda hokey (you're in a group of mid 30s, highest level loots, nobody else can use it, etc), but maybe it could lower the effectiveness of the item based on that level. Example, if an item is looted by a level 40 character, it'd be 25% as useful on a level 1 as it would on a level 40, and then maybe 100% as useful for a level 30? I dunno, they'd have to work out the math to make it balanced.
