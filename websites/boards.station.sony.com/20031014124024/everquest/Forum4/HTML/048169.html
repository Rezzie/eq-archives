If you check up on Iksar lore...

Towards the end of his reign/lifespan, Venril Sathir abandoned Cazic-Thule and made a pact with Innoruuk that would grant VS undead immortality in exchange for his allegiance. Venril's son Rile found out, thwarted the ritual, and took over the crown.

That's why the dark elf outpost is on "Venril Sathir" faction -- VS is an Inny stooge now. As all modern-day Iksar remain loyal to Cazic, there's some friction there.
