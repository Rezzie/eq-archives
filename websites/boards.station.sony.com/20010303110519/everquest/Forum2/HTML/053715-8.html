One more post of ideas, basically a summary:

1. Reduce the mana cost of wizard damage spells by 20% or so. Reducing the cost would be equivalent to improving mana regen and increasing the mana pool. This would improve the wizard's ability to "keep up" while grouped in early and mid levels.

2. Make it so grouping with a wizard lowered the resists and fizzles for all spell casters in the group. This could be an innate ability (always on), or a spell. This would help wizards find groups, and would help balance all casters a bit more with the melee types.

3. Add high level creatures which can not be hit by weapons, normal or magic (possibly allow weapon procs though). This would help satisfy the high level characters and would provide variety in the game. The only other reasonable solution would be to add a percentage based damage spell. 4. Make harvest scale through the 50s better.

5. Another idea, which may be interesting: Increase the chance of specialization going off based on how many people are in the group. Apply this to all spell casters.

6. Scale the 6 level resist limit with # of chars in a party. When soloing, it'd still be capped at 6. With a full party, cap it at 11.
