This may have been a one time glitch or perhaps it is a bug, let me know if any of you have experienced this.

I was fighting in karana with my buffs up (SOW, Strength of Earth and Skin Like Rock) at the end of one fight I tried to heal myself to full.
I cast healing on myself and it didn't seem to do anything, so I checked my hit points they were at 372 of 431, I tried casting healing again, and again there seemed to be no improvement, again 372 of 431.

I did a report with /bug and gave a tell to a guide that was on-line, he asked me several questions like "do you have food and drink?" and "how is your stamina?" he then asked if I had tried camping and then logging back in. this is something that I had not tried so I let him know that I would give it a try. When I came back into the game my Skin Like Rock was gone and I had 372 hit points, I recast the buff and all was well.

Apparently the skin buff had faded but all of my indicators showed that it had not with the exception of the max hit points not being attainable.

Just wondering if this is a known bug or if I just experienced some kind of computer glitch?
