I have spent a great deal of time hanging out with my pal Bristlebane in the Plane of Mischief, and we got to talking.

I came up to him and said, "Hey BB, you are a pretty cool guy, but man, that Quellious, I've worshipped her ever since I was a wee lad playing with my very first Forehead Polishing Kit. It would be darn cool if I could head on over to her plane and just drool in reverent awe."

The big B grinned and laughed and wiggled his halfling toes at me, "But everyone knows the Plane of Tranquility is a noob zone! Why would she want to show her face in a zone that only serves to get you from PoK to the reall cool planes."

Well, that sorta ticked me off that my bud BB would be so blunt, so I had to take my dire charmed rat and kill about a dozen of his halflings (faction free mind you).

So where has my Quellious gone? As a devout follower of her for so very long (I'm an old Erudite who goes around beating on young wippersnappers with my cane) I feel a little let down that I can't even meet my Goddess face to face like they can in so many other planes.

And if anyone HAS seen her, please give her a hug for me.

Actually, EverQuest is rated "T" for Teens. I've not really seen character models or NPCs in the game that resemble human children; the cases cited use adult models and are ambiguous about the ages being specified.

In either case, I agree that the right way to have introduced Quellious would be to have her banish all who would engage in combat with her. That being said, I think that the in character reason for her not manifesting ("the time is not right in this chapter of history") is compelling, given the Celestial War saga backstory.
