1. In a group camping a rare spawn, do that '/em has looted a <insert uber-rare no drop lore piece here>' trick. If they are half asleep and don't notice the text color is different, you will see just how loud someone can get .. hehe

Make sure it is an amount the person will probably have on them. I did this to a level 5 guy once and he FREAKED... started calling me every name in the book, spamming the zone that I was a thief and trying to get a GM.. meanwhile a dozen other people are telling him it's a joke and too relax.. lol

3. In a dangerous zone where there are a bunch of others in long boring but dangerous camps. You know the ones... where an hour can go by without even one /ooc or /shout.
