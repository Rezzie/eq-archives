Thogg, what were yours and the monks positions during the battles you parsed?

I am guessing that you were tanking toe-to-toe with the critters you fought while the monk, if sufficiently sneaky, was mimicking a rogue and circling behind the critters where they could not parry, dodge or riposte.

Monk disciplines you mentioned in your previous post were directly from Verant presss releases for Kunark, I believe. They read well, but the actual game impact they have is less than noticeable. Quadrupling the base damage of a weapon for a brief time is quite handy. The self-only speed buff is something I must wonder about. Certainly the damage dealt while under 100 fists is not as great as under inner flame.

Warrior damage dealing seems quite nice, and tanking is the best in EQ by class mandate. Rogues were a broken class for a long time, but now have been buffed to the point where they are clear favorites in damage dealing by a huge margin.

Monks have Mend going for us, effectively placing us halfway between warriors and rogues, at least in the game designers' minds. Our Block skill supposedly does work more often than Parry, but I have no idea whether or not this is true. Mend additionally scales with HP buffing so that we can in effect have nearly the HP of warriors if this skill counts as additional HP.

FD is unreliable enough that it seems to be useful only as a butt-saving measure during the early phases of being attacked. Once a monk has low HP and has several critters aggroed, Feign Death simply stops working.

Warrior triple attack and rogue double backstab seem to set a precedent for a similar upgrade for monks. I would suggest allowing Double Attack to apply to our extra attacks the same way it does to rogue backstab.

Whirlwind is very nice, as is inner flame. Thunder kick is merely for show. Void dance is not entirely useless, actually. When running from a horde of angry things it can give you precious seconds to jump ahead of the crowd without getting stunned.

All the weapons listed by Thogg are impressive, however I have yet to see any. Only the top few monks and a few twinks on my server have Tranquil Staves or the like. Shuriken of the Tranquil look impressive but are entirely for show due to their rarity.
