The best caster class is the one needed for that specific situation. You need something burned down fast? Wizard. Crowd control in a live environment? Chanter. Crowd control in an undead environment? Necro. Sustained DPS? Mage and necro. DPS and heals? Druid with shaman close second. Buffs/debuffs and heals? Shaman with druid close second. A little bit of everything but nothing the absolute best? Bard.

Don't forget that at higher levels, necros can assist healing with group life tap, and mana sieve against casters will allow added mana regen. Also, lots of classes can fill in here and there on specific tasks. If you play a caster, get creative with your skills and spells, you might surprise yourself on how flexible the class can be.
