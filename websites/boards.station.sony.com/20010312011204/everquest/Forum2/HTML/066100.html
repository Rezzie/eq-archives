Actually, Surefall is no more "prime" than western WK. One zone to get into into QH from either, run to Blackburrow for Halas/Permafrost. WK will be a bit closer to Qeynos, if that's where you're going. It's about equal distance in QH if you're headed for the northern route.

GFay is a better port location than BB. The BB ring is close to the dock -- but you're probably not porting so you can get on the boat. The first thing people do porting into BB is get lost by missing that little turnoff by the skeleton tower to get to GFay, which is where they were going anyway. The GFay portal takes you right next to Kelethin and a bank. GFay is better than the Misty portal by a whole zone for banking and trading.

NK is about a wash, but the wizard port is a little better. They have equal access to NK, but the wizzies get marginally better access to SK. Does GrimFeather path over the wizard spires? He does into the druid ring. The wizard portal is right by landmarks if you're travelling elsewhere.

South Ro versus North Ro are a wash. Depends on where you're going there. NRo has better access to Freeport than WC, which is the main reason for using WC. About equal to EC for merchanting.

Wizard portals are usuable by evils and those that hunted treants; half the druid portals are not.

Druids rule on getting to Lavastorm and the dungeons there.

Feerrott is probably marginally better than Cazic. If you're a group of 34s, you can handle Cazic, and that's about the only reason to port to Feerrott anyway. Might as well go straight there. But Feerrott gives you more time to organize and set up, even if you do have to dodge Bouncer Hurd. And maybe there's the odd lone ogre that wants a ride home. Druids win by a tail-length here, IMO.

Dreadlands and Tox are effectively the same. Velious portals are literally the same.

Only wizards can port to the planes.

I don't see that wizards have a lot to complain about. Their ports are arguably better, and even if you prefer the druid ports, there's certainly there's not such a huge discrepancy as to warrant whining. It would be nice to lose all the anti-druid rhetoric. It just obscures the valid parts of any post that contains such.
