Hi all...

Does anyone else think it's really hard to be a Guild Leader on EQ because the game doesn't give us a lot of tools? Example - there's no way to see a list of all your guildmates, including anonymous and offline. It becomes rather challenging if your guild gets big, and you will never know if those members who stopped showing up actually left the guild or the game. I spend countless hours offline working on spreadsheets to keep track of everybody's guilded characters. Given that we all have 8 characters, (which change constantly)... it's a nightmare.

I also would love to be able to promote a member to officer even he is on the other side of the world - because that's usually the situation. On top of it all, a suggestion- could we have more than 3 ranks? (leader, officer, member) )


One tired Leader,
