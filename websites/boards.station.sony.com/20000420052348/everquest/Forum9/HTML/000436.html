How about this guys and gals.

Level 20 spell (since that levels spells suck)
Orb of Storing- conjuration skill.
This spell would summon an orb (graphic of a lightstone) that is Lore/no drop so that you could only have one at a time. The orb once summoned would be useless until you picked it up and with it on your cursor cast a spell you want to store in the orb for future use. The orb could light up to let you know it has been charged. You can store any spell into the orb that you can cast normally.(even the summon orb spell, since the orb disappears when used, although that would be pointless) Then, when you needed to use that spell, like when OOM, you could simply right click the orb with the proper targeting and viola... one last blast when it counts.
Of course the spell would have a casting time and be interruptable as usual. The orb would simply be a mana storage unit so to speak.

Personally I think I would use mine for an evac, since I tend to overburn a bit in tense situations and not have that last bit of mana needed for an evac. But you could store any spell you wanted in it, even Ice comet
Then you could med up to full!!!

At higher levels, say 29, 39, 49, the upgrades could hold several charges of the spell at a time. The orb would still be lore/no drop to prevent exploiting, and the spell stored would have to be the same exact same spell for each charge.
So at level 29 you could store 2 charges of Inferno shock if you wanted.

Here's the kicker though. The summoning of the orb would be VERY mana costly, since conjuration isn't the wizards specialty. Say around 150-200 mana at level 20 and then up from there for the upgrades. That is JUST for summoning the orb, the charges are on top of that. So what this in effect does is make you med the mana back in a fair amount of time for the large benefit this gives, therefore balancing its power with downtime.

This would give the wizard a MUCH needed way of increasing his/her conjuration abilities well into the upper levels. As well as giving the wizard a balanced way to be useful to a group when OOM. As well as just being really COOL!!!! As seekers of knowledge shouldn't we have a way to STORE that knowledge for later access?!

Anyway, I think this would solve a LOT of the wizards main problems. Namely being totally useless when OOM and not being very group friendly. It would solve the first problem totally, and help somewhat with the second.

What are your thoughts????
