I would like to see new Alchemy Potions.

POTION OF THE BONES Gates you to Field of Bones right outside of Cabilis, Cost 120p to make and is trivial at 170. We have Potion of the frost and Potion of the Swamp we need this one.

HEAL POTION 80 point heal. Cost 20p to make and is trivial at 130.

BIND change the point your bound to, works in City zones only. Cost 20p to make and is trivial at 130.

GATE take you back to your bind point. Cost 50p to make and is trivial at 120.

GROW increases size by 33% works in outside zones only. Cost 20p to make and is trivial at 120.

ULTRAVISION upgrade to the infravision potion. Cost 20p to make and is trivial at 110.

BREEZE Mana regen, must stack with all clarity spells. Cost 20p to make and is trivial at 110.

These new potions would greatly help Shaman Skill up in Alchemy. There are not that many potions that go trivial between 110 and 140. These would also be useful Potions in the game and at the cost/level should not unbalance any class.
