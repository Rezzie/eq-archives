Howdy,
I'm having a problem for about a month now. The casting bar disappears often when I play a song. No, it's *NOT* a missed note...I get no message saying so, and the icon pops up in the song box when it's supposed to, indicating the song *is* indeed being played correctly. It's just the casting bar is AWOL for some reason.
This doesn't happen when I solo, just when grouping and the swords and spells are a-flying. I'm assuming this is a graphics glitch then, but it's only been since a few patches ago, maybe a month ago.

All my hardware is supported (according to this sites listed supported hardware), using dual-booted 98/2k pro (happens in both OSes), DX8.1 and latest Nvidia graphics drivers. Dxdiag shows no problems.
This is only happening with bard, none of my other casting characters.

Any other bards out there with like problem?
