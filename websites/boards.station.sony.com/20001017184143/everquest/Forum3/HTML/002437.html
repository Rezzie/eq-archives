Here's my analysis of why this occurs:

A mob's hate list is fairly small (12 players?). If the mob dies (due to a dot) to someone not on it's hate list, the corpse poofs. Since the only place you typically have more than 12 people attacking something is in the planes, this is where you see this issue.

I suspect the small hate list is difficult or impossible to fix, so I wouldn't expect any resolution on this.

I think you are pretty right on about the Dot bug Xaax. It does have to do with the hate list. You can actually reproduce it with an enchanter. If you cast a dot on something that is nearly dead and memblur it the mob will die and go poof if 'tick' on the dot has not rolled over to get the caster back on the hate list.

That sounds kind of convoluted but here is exactly how to reproduce it.

1) Wound something to near death.
2) Have druid/shaman/necro/whoever cast dot while enchanter is membluring or start memblur soon enough to hit right when dot hits. (some enchanter mezzes will also work as they have a memblur component, entrance is one)
3)Mob dies before dot repropagates hate list, corpse poofs.

I was helping a younger druid friend a bit when I had this happen a couple of times.
