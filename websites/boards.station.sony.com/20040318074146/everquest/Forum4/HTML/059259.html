Uhm...
My first twink-xperience was when i was level 7, with my first char. I was trying to do my newbie armor quests (i was told to, since i rejected some "gifts" from some friends) and after getting all the bricks of iron from the gnolls i started to check the merchants for the other pieces. That was also the first time i abandoned the security of the Qeynos zone... With only a bag with some bread, water and my cloth armor and rusty longsword i headed alone trough the (unknown to me) dangers of west and north karanas, and was in the last one where i bought all the parts that my few gold pieces were able to buy. Was in that when...
LOADING... PLEASE WAIT... (grizzly bear)

At least i was able to finish all but two parts of my armor...

----------------
My second experience was around level 15.
After a hard day of work on the Commonlands, was time for put the money on the bank and go to sleep. First i checked the merchant on the bank on north freeport. "Uhm, acrylia reinforced armor, and looks beter than mine... 3gp? buy it..."
One week later thanks to that armor i earned my first 500pp on the bazaar, and thanks to that money my equip has received upgrades each time i have the chance...

This post is to give thanks to the person who sold the armor to the merchant, and gave to a newbie the chance of becoming a twink on his own :P
