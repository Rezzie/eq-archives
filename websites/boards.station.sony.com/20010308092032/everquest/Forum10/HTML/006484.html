What the others said, plus:

SoW stands for Spirit of the Wolf (so-and-so is surrounded by a breif lupine aura) and makes you move faster.

Movement rate is increased by about 50% (you run faster).

SoW can be cast by three classes:

Shamans get SoW at lvl 9
Druids at 14
Rangers at 30 (used to be 39)

Bards also get a movement song called Selo's Accelerando, which is group only and short term (they must keep singing it for it to stay in effect), but faster than SoW in some circumstances.

Also: a note: please never demand SoW or just shout over and over "SoW plz" - because this will get you ignored.

Last night outside of West Freeport, I /auctioned "Free SoWs and Binds- ask nicely please!"
One jerk came up and said, "SoW."
I said, "Don't be rude."
He said, "Come on- I haven't got all day."
I repeated the auction line, thinking, he might take the hint.
He said, "Come on, time is plat."
I said, "Then give me some."
I then turned away and helped other folks who were asking nicely- for free.

Meanwhile, a true paladin kneeled before me and said, "Fair maiden, may you be so kind as to bestow upon me the Spirit of the Wolves?" I smiled at this gentleman, and immediately did so. He then gave me every buff he had- so I gave him every buff I had. This was very nice roleplaying, and fun for me.

So remember that when asking for SoW- be nice and polite.
