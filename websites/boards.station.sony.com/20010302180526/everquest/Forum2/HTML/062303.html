Xelene
Station Member 		posted 02-26-2001 04:18 PM           
The UPSes that most of us are familiar with are little shoebox-sized things that give your PC enough power to shut down.

Some who work with larger servers have seen big rack-mounted ones that do the same thing for large server systems with RAID arrays and the like. Those are still single machines.

Each EQ server is a cluster of about 25 machines (almost 1,000 machines total). The amount of power needed to keep that running requires diesel, ventilation and a huge footprint.

Just something to keep in mind.

BTW, "emergency maintenance" could be emergency maintenance on the powerlines to the servers, or could still affect them without being actual server maintenance.
