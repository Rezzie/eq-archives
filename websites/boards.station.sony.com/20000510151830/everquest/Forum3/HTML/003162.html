By register, he means, when you purchase the game, and install it on your PC, you are required to register your copy with Sony/Verant. Each game comes witha unique "Key" or string of numbers that validate your "legal" copy of the game. As this is an internet based game, the registration is done via the internet, not sending in a card. Once the game is registered, your "account" is sealed, no one else can use the CD to regeister. It can be loaded on multiple PC's, but only one Person can be logged into the game at one time. Hope that does it for you!
