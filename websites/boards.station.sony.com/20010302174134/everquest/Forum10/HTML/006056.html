EC is probably a better place to sell things, but you have to be careful. If you want to do any hunting in that area, make sure to get a bind in Freeport first, because if you die, it's gona be one long boat ride to get your corpse. Getting 10 plat for a stack is probably too much to expect, it happens only rarely. On the other hand, it just may be bad luck. Greater Faydark is usually a crowded zone, with many people passing by as it has a wizard teleport located there, so you might want to sell those again later. I hope this does not seem patronizing, you did say it was a "newbie question"

You were very lucky to sell batwings for such a price! They really only sell to people who do not plan ahead, since there is a vendor in West Freeport who carries them. You might not be able to sell any more for a long time, even just at 1pp a stack.

For the bone chips, those are best sold where there are high-level necromancers hunting, and not a lot of skeletons to kill. You might have better luck in Butcherblock, if one of those evil practicioners of the dark arts should happen to be killing dwarves.
