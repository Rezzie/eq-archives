If you have damage shield messages turned off in the chat filters, you won't see another player/pet/mob getting hit by the damage shields. There's a bug in that feature though, if you're meleeing against a mob with a damage shield on, you won't be able to see that YOU'RE getting hit by theirs. This can be very dangerous, and at the least very annoying, in that you're taking damage but aren't being told that you are, or why.

OK, did the "greenie" test and here's what I learned:

1. If you cast the spell on yourself, you do indeed get told (in blue text) that the mob took non-melee damage and how much. But the mob is only damaged when it actually hits you.

2. If you cast the spell on someone else, you only see "a mob has been burned" (in white text) when the mob scores a hit on your spell target.

So I was missing the "mob has been burned" messages in all the other white "battle spam" text, and didn't realize the damage was only done when I was hit. Since I try to let my pet do most of the fighting, I missed most of the messages.
