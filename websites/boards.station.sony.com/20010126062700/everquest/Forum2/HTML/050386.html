Thanks (Aradune wrote that )

Kunark and Velious were both planned out a bit quite a while ago, back when we'd planned to release all five continents at launch.

Sometime into development, however, we decided that it just wouldn't be possible to include the density of zones desired across five continents. So we decided to put more zones in less continents, and hoped we could work on Kunark and Velious later.

Thankfully, EQ turned out to be even more popular than we ever expected, and we've been able to release two expansions including the two continents.

I can't wait to see what the future holds

-Brad
