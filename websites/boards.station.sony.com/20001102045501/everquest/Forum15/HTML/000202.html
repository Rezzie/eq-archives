Got to get from point A to point B in a hurry and no ports availible? No one can get ther faster than a bard. No one. No spell, no item, nothing can get there faster.

Who else can cast spells ON THE RUN? No one. Play a caster and you get an idea of how annoying that can get. Not for us though.

So if you HAVE to be the blood covered hero standing on a pile of your enemies, then yes, change classes immediately. But if you want the ABSOLUTE knowledge that that warrior standing on a pile of enemies wouldn't be up there without YOU then the bard is for you.

And we just flat out look better than anyone else too.

Mmm, Bards. Certainly one of the most difficult classes to play and everybody seems confused at what we are or what we can do for a group. Not even Verant seems sure. Case in point: we are not hybrids apparently (we are a 'special' case) but which forum are we in? Thats right, hybrids P

To the original poster, who was reaching level 26, I would say crack on...things are about to get more interesting. In the next few levels you will get Fear, Charm, Mez and of course, Mana song. For me these completely transformed my playing experience, especially Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity. Your melee will really suck by now, making that nice armour and weapons look like all show and no go. But you now have a different dimension to your game. You can still melee but can buff/debuff at the same time. Or you can do some mob control with Charm and Mez.

Bard buffing can be amazing. If you want to see jaws drop at how a Bard can buff, try out any of the MR songs with a drum; then see who in your group wants to be without it when tackling mobs that can charm THEM.

Charm to me was tremendous fun and still is; turn an angry mob into your plaything and watch it dish out more damage than your tanks. Sure it doesn't last long but you can chain cast it without mana cost.

The acid test on whether I had picked the right class was the ability of getting into groups in busy zones. This seems to have got easier as I levelled, partly because of the mana song but more generally because Bards are so versatile. As group members change, the Bard can adjust their role accordingly. In one group you can be enchanter, buffer/debuffer or tank. Which other class could do that?

In case anyone thinks I am trying to paint too rosy a picture here, I am not. Did I ever regret picking the class? Lots of times and each time we get stiffed again with a nerf I vow to start another class. What keeps me going? A sense of achievement. I look at (say) a level 50 <insert own class here!> and i think bah, easy. I look at a level 50 Bard and I think yeah, nice going!

I'm not a bard, but I'd like to add one thing that really hasn't be stress too much.

A Bard doesn't do many things well, but a bard will make several people in a group excel at what they do.

Your songs can add AC to your entire group
Your songs can haste your entire group
Your songs can help regnerate mana and hp of your entire group
Your songs can raise your groups resistances to absurd levels

Here is the greatest part. All your spells stack with other classes spells. That's very important.

Remember, a bard helps each member of the group with his songs, not one person at a time and can do everything there is to do as well.
