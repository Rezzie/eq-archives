A few points for all to remember when asking for hybrid class changes:

Verant has set as immutable design law that, no hybrid shall possess skill abilities in any area owned by either of it's parent classes, BETTER than either of those parent classes.

So, those of you who are doing so, stop asking to be better than a warrior in archery. Stop asking to do more melee damage than a warrior. You NEVER WILL.

Rangers do the most damage in melee of any hybrid class and do ALMOST the same damage as a warrior of equal level using the same weapons. In that instance the ranger has only a 5 point offense skill difference than the warrior does form level 20 to level 40, which is when both classes have dounble attack, duel wield and kick. Warriors do get criticals but those only occur in 3-4% of attacks so....no amazing biggie.

Rangers are the ONLY hybrid to get both duel wield AND double attack.But they generally have lower AC than the rest. Balance. You pay for your increased attack ability by putting your ass out in front. I find if more Hybrids of all sorts concentrated on getting the best AC items they could rather than on stat buff items they would do far better in overall combat. I knwo it worked literally WONDERS for me.

That having been said:

A) Rangers, as they currently are, have one major problem: High level tracking needs to be fixed so it is usable and sortable to the rangers need.

B) Rangers have NO NEED for a SINGLE SPECIAL 'CLASS DEFINING' SKILL. The clas as it stands doesn't need one. The request is strictly a 'grass is greener' one. Ask any Paladin and SK how much they RELY on their 'Class Defining' ability and they will tell you they do not. They are good to have of course but they are far from all powerful abilities. Especially at one use per hour and a half. A well played group will almost NEVER require a Paladins ability.

So appreciate what you have. You really have no NEED for more. Your class weakness' are there to balance your class Strength's.

That having been said if you STILL NEED a once per hour and a half, class ranger only skill I say do the same thing SK's and Paladins did: Lose one of your main combat skills.

Keep duel weild and lose double attack to get this Ranger only class skill usable once per hour and a half. That would be balanced with the rest of the hybrids.

I haveno idea what skill it would be mind you but if you really want it I think that losing double attack would be a good trade. It is at least in keeping with Hybrid design philosophy if you look at the rest.
