Obsidian, I honestly do not see why any warrior would focus on dex/agi. To begin, as a warrior, you should never have your primary focus as any stats. Your primary equipment focus should be AC, followed by HP. Next thing you should worry about is getting your AGI up to 100, and then your focus on your STA and/or STR. Dex should just be a skill that comes naturally with your gear (granted, you may want to choose 3str and 2dex over 4str, depending on your equipment)

Verant has already announced the worth of each stat...for a warrior, I will paraphrase them for you:

STR: Weight Allowance, Damage Bonus per hit
STA: Time able to hold breath, HP (at 53 I get roughly 5hp per point of STA)
AGI: Chance to dodge/parry, AC. 3agi = 1ac for each point of agi under 100. Past 100, you get 1ac for 9agi.
DEX: Chances to raise melee skills, chances to proc. Unless you are wielding proccing weapons, this stat could be at 2 and you wouldnt notice. I notice zero difference between my skill at 98 and my skill at 198 (with boon of the gareau).

My stats, which seem to suit me quite well, are 1004ac 2400hp 233str 163sta 100agi 98dex. If you put me toe-to-toe with a dark elf who focused on agi and dex, their stats may be something like this: 970ac 2200hp 120str 100sta 170agi 150dex...same level, same weapons...I would rip them apart.
