Abashi,
First off I think i can speak for most shamans when i say thanks for finally revamping the alchemy skill.

Just a few comments on the recently implemented Alchemy Skill.

1) There seems to be 2 seperate potions that raise Disease resistance and none that raise magic resistance. Is this an error in the data or is this intentional?

2) the Upgrades on the Scorching skin series do not change. the low level version: Scorching skin (Cost: 11plat) works exactly the same as the mid level version Kilva's skin of flame (Cost: 42400).

3) Resale value of potions: Unlike Jewelry craft, where you can sell a crafted peice of jewelry for about 90% of the cost of the components, Alchemy potions sell for a fraction of the cost of the components. Given that shaman intelligence is low to begin with, how are we supposed to increase our skill level without spending an obscene amount of plat? Yes, on the older server players have accumilated huge amounts of plat. However, now that Dwarfs can no longer be harvested the newer servers are seeing platless shaman. In essence, i don't mind the high cost of potions, what i mind is that there is no way to raise the skill to expert level without spending close to 6000 plat. What i suggest is either make it like Jewelry craft or make jewelry craft like alchemy.

4) Lack of Potion books....Don't really mind this.. its kinda fun experimenting.

5) Are the rumors that Necros are going to get alchemy true? As a shaman i find that totally unacceptable. Necros already are one of the most played classes, giving them alchemy completely takes away the shamans defining skill.

Any input would be appreciated,

Duhara
