Author Topic:   Healer/Enc hate-list propagation bug in Kunark outdoor zones
Kadiya_luclin
Station Member 		posted 09-28-2000 08:54 AM           
The bug: some spells cast during battle with a social mob cause the caster's identity to be placed on the hate lists of EVERY member of that social mob's "family," across the zone. This hate list presence is even inherited by new spawns of the same mob type.

Example:
Step 1: A group of 4 (enchanter, druid, warrior, necro) is pulling and killing wandering mobs in Burning woods. The warrior brings in a Sarnak Knight. During the fight, all casters cast offensive spells of some sort, and the druid casts snare and also heals the warrior, before the mob dies.
Step 2: If the bug "goes off," the next time the warrior pulls in a Sarnak of ANY type, as the mob approaches the group the mob will stop chasing the warrior who has damaged said mob, and instead rush to attack the Druid or Enc, whoever was affected by the bug.
Variation: A sarnak wanders somewhat near the group, far out of aggressiveness range but within "sight range" (the range at which Kunark mobs stop chasing someone and warp back out of sight). If the druid or enc is bugged, the sarnak will come at a dead run and attack the unoffending druid or enchanter.
Step 3: The druid or enchanter, whoever was being attacked from far distance, camps out to "clear" the list. This solves the problem, for now.

Location of Bug: This has occurred multiple times in the Kunark outdoor zones: Overthere, Dreadlands, Burning Woods, Warsliks Woods, Emerald Jungle, Trakanon's Teeth, Frontier Mountains, Lake of Ill Omen.

Classes: The bug does not happen EVERY time a healer casts during a fight, or an enchanter casts, but it seems to ONLY happen to Enchanters and the healing classes: Druid, Shaman, Cleric.

Verifiability (is that a word?): I recently called in a Guide to watch this bug in action on the Luclin server. She was an apprentice guide (Amataz or similar) and was being watched over by a full GM (Belos). I demonstrated the problem with my 21 druid who had recently caused the bug among Goblins in Lake of Ill Omen. I demonstrated by approaching any goblin, and the guide saw the goblins rush to attack me while I was far, far out of aggressive range.

Narrowing the issue:
1. This bug ONLY causes monsters of the same "social group" to come rushing to attack the healer or enchanter. For example, if the bug is affecting Sarnaks, Wurms and Giants will not be affected, unless the bug is triggered by them as well.
2. The hate list is "passed down" to mobs who haven't even spawned yet! If sarnaks are affected by the bug, I have personally watched as one spawned, and then a moment later took off on a dead run, passing myself and heading straight for the healer/enchanter.
3. This only seems to happen in outdoor zones.
4. Since the mobs will actually bypass the PULLER to run straight to the healer/enchanter, the bug causes the healer/enchanter to not just be ON the hate list, but to TOP the hate list. Successful taunting WILL change that list, but it's still interesting.

Thanks, and lets get this long-standing healer-killing bug fixed already, eh?
