As mentioned in the previous post, you can go into the options menu and select "same screen resolution" off. This will change your resolution back down to 640 x 480 for the inventory/merchant screen due to the fact that the images used for those specific screens are made for that resolution and are only made up of a certain amount of pixels. This is the same reason that windows and icons will appear to be smaller on your desktop when you increase resolutions. This feature of EQ drove me so crazy that I now just play in 640 x 480 full time. A somewhat easy way that verant could fix this is to make separate inv screen image files for each resolution setting. I'm sure this would make a bunch of us happier.
