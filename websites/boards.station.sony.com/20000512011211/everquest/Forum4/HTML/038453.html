Its really simple. The RMB shows up as plate, and no plate wearing Iksar model was ever made, so they prevent any Iksar from using an item that would be visible as plate. However, non visible plate pieces still can be worn by Iksars. It wasn't due to gameplay that it was changed, it was to save them the work of making a new model.
