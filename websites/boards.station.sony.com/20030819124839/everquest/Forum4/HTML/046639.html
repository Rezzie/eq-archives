I'll be posting this on the Tech board later tonight when I'm home but I thought I'd share this with you folks.

About 2 weeks ago I broke down and purchased Star Wars Galaxies for my son. I discovered as I loaded it that DirectX 9.0 was required, and even though EQ is only officially cleared for 8.1 I decided to risk it rather than listen to the complaints of my sons.

I have the latest NVIDIA drivers and DirectX 9.0a installed, and had not experienced any trouble until last night.

I entered The Grey from Mons Letalus, climbed the zone wall and turned to survey the area. When my point-of-view hit the temple in the distance the game crashed with an EQGAME.EXE error. I rebooted, deleted EQGAME.EXE and all the files for the zone then repatched and logged back in. Everything went fine, got back in the game and noticed my view was towards the wall so I turned and *bang* another error, this time EQMAIN.DLL.

Rebooted. Deleted the file and repatched. Got back in. Looking at the wall again.

Moved down the hill to just in front of the zone entrance. Turned slowly -- no problems. Thought the error was fixed and moved up the wall again. Looked at the temple and *bang*. Unfortunately I am now stuck looking at the temple and anytime I try to log in to that character I get the error again.

Tried virus scan (none found). Scandisk (no error).

Things left to try:

o Download DirectX 9.0b.
o Post to Tech Board.
o Email to SOE to request the character be moved out of the zone.

Hopefully the new error reporting function in the current patch will be of some use.

Scarn
