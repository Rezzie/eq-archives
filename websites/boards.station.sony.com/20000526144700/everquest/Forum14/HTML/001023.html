Without insane good luck this quest could take hours. Fortunately with a good group you could gain some xp. while doing this quest. Overall though the mace just isn't that good.

Definitly get help if you are going to do it, but you would be better server getting a nice 2HB weapon. In the long run the only really nice shaman weapons are 2HB. I would go try to get a Glowing wooden crook. Lockjaw drops them. You may have trouble getting lockjaw himself, but while you are in Oasis you can gain 3 more levels easily. You also wont need a magic weapon in Oasis so you could get an orcish mace (part of the SM quest and use that until you get the GWC).

Basically you should keep 1HB up just in case you get a nice 1HB weapon, but I would concentrate on 2HB most of the time. Forget about spears as at higher levels you will be hampered by the inability to raise your piercing skill.

If you do go for the SM be prepared to spend as much as a full week trying to get it because sometimes it does take that long.

Yes, the screaming mace is worth it, and very doable at level 16. However, Crushbone is just about the worst place in the game for overcrowding. It's a very small zone, and routinely has between 40 and 50 people in there. The tower where DVinn and Crush spawn is a nasty place, and very dangerous if you are under level 18 or so. If you want to camp the tower, you'll have to get a group. Otherwise, you're toast. If you are serious about the doing the quest, simply go during a time when the tower is camped by higher level folks, and offer to pay them for a tunic. Most of them are there for DVinn's dirk, and will gladly toss a tunic your way for a very cheap price. I personally give them away. It's a reasonably common drop (about 1 in every 4 or 5 spawns) so there shouldn't be too much problem scoring one of these.

The bracers of battle are another story. It will take you a little while to get one of these for the simple reason that the warlord is an absolute pain in the butt to find, let alone kill without getting KSed. About the only advice on this is bring a wizard along. The warlord rarely lasts more than 10 seconds with all kinds of people slinging mana at him at a frightening rate. It isn't impossible to kill him at 16, but you'll have to be absolutely ready to blast the snot out of him when he appears. Otherwise, the druids and rangers camping DVinn will pick him up on tracking, descend upon him and wipe him out before you knew what the hell happened.

If you're serious about doing the quest, it will take some time, but it isn't impossible. But if you just want to acquire a better 1hb, save your money to either buy one of them, or possibly a CIMS from Permafrost. It will save you a lot of time and grief in the long run.

A third option is Kithicor at night. No, I'm not nuts. Simply find where the groups are camping, and ask them if you might loot a battleworn morning star, should they come across one. They are no drop (hence can't be sold), so are essentially useless to the groups, unless there is a high level there that wants one for the wis bonus on it. It's a very nice weapon to have, but can not be transferred or sold when you get something better.

Well, hope this helps...
