"There is an argument that if you are sitting at the moment of the "tick", you gain mana as if you sat for the entire 6 second "tick"."


There is no such argument. This is how the game works. Every 6 seconds the game updates health and mana based on your position at that moment.

Druids have made use of this for years by sitting down periodically while Kiting.

Well, as a druid, I don't know anything about "canni" but I can confirm, for a fact, that as long as you are sitting at the exact instant that the 6-second "tick" hits, you will get the increased mana regeneration of sitting regardless of what you were doing the other 5 seconds.
