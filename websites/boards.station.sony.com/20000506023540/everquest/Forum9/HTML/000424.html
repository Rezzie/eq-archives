Many wizards have complained about needing some spell that used in combination with other spells would be more powerful ie
Druids: Sow, Snare, DOt
Mage: Damage Shields, Pets
Enchanter: Pet, Debuffs

Circle of Sorcery
Level 34 Conjuration (wizards have no real conjuration and should start learning some)

Creates a mystic circle about the caster (duration 1 or two combats .. like a haste spell) which fixes the caster in place (rooted) and helps to focus their magical energies (reduced all resists or icrease damage). This also affords the caster a good deal of mystical protection ... (AC boost and much higher resists)

Since this is a short duration and it roots the user (dangerous to cast) this would give Wizards the boost they want without affecting play balance very greatly. In good groups this will be a great boon but in bad pulls it will be dangerous.
