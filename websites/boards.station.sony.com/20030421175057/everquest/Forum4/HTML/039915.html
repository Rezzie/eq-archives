The charred guardian is the shield with the wisdom, and is one the "standard" shaman shields. I carried one myself for about 40 levels.

For piercing weapons I used first the Jade Chikodai Prod (until level 22 or so) and then the Spine Chill Spear, which proc'd around 34 and kept that until I got my epic at 52.

You can continue meleeing at least until your mid 30s or so... after that root and rot or other techniques starts to work a lot better. I still get up and poke things a few times every chance I get but teamed with my warrior hubby, I am much more often sitting or casting than meleeing.

BTW, my wis was about the same as yours at your age. Now at 53 is about 240 un-KEI'd. Keep in mind that Misty Thicket Picnics and Qeynos Tea are a cheap way to raise Wis and other stats, 10 points between the two of them without losing an armor slot.
