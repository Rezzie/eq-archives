Some other spell issues were left out.


ITEM: Succor

DESCRIPTION: Currently Succor drains around 700 mana from every party member when cast yet it does not clear aggro. Either have succor clear aggro, or remove the 700 mana drain from each player.

ITEM: Wake of Karana (anti-rain spell)

DESCRIPTION: Currently, the spell has too many limitations to be useful in a group/raid situation outdoors, and is rather steep in requirements for an effect that is in no way over-powering. The spell costs 400 mana, requires an expensive opal (17-20 pp), and recast time is like 6 minutes. In addition, the spell only removes rain, not snow which is frozen water. This spell could be desirable in outdoor raids/groups to allow pullers to pull in clear weather for outdoor dragons, etc.. However, the spell has too steep of cost. It needs to be fixed by: 1. have it remove snow as well as rain, 2. reduce the mana cost 3. make spell component slightly cheaper like peridot (and a component most people have around) 4. change recast time to something reasonable like 30 seconds so the spell can actually be cast when needed.

ITEM: Spirit of the Oak

DESCRIPTION: This spell gives slight mana regen at 59, but the spell is not stackable with Protection of the Glades (60 spell). Thus, the spell has use for only a single level and the spell already has many limitations - self only, outdoors only, and rooted. Having it stack would not be imbalancing and actually give the spell more use. Also, the spell ought to be moved to a slightly lower level - say 57 or 58. Druids start getting some mana regen at 54 from Form of the Howler (outdoors only too), but have to wait for 59/60 for any more mana regen. The effect of Spirit of the Oak is minor so why not allow its use at a slightly lower level to spread out the gain in regen.

ITEM: The 55th level pet.

DESCRIPTION: The pet is too weak for a 55th level spell. For balance reasons, the pet is not meant to be able to tank, but the equivalent of a magician/necro 29th level pet spell is too low. Other class novelty spells from velious (form of the bear, boon of the garou) look cool and give some "real" added bonus that benefits the player. To fix the problem, raise the pet to the equivalent of a magician/necro 39th level pet spell. This pet would still never be able to remotely tank anything that one fights at 55+, but the pet would have a better chance of dealing out some damage (not much, but just enough to have a little bit of use).
