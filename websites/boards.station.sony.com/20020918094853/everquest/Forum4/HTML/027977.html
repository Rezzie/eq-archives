I have been playing on the Karana server on and off for well over a year now. But recently I decided to go the Firiona Vie server to experience the newness of the game again and without twinking and being near hundreds of other twinks.

I have to say, I really do like the server. No common tongue, meaning you need to learn the languages of the other races to socialize. (however, I have found that /emote works in a pinch)This brings out some realism to the world.

Very few twinks that I have seen. Although there still are a few.

As for roleplaying, there is not as much roleplaying as I had hoped for, but still a bit different than the other servers.

I greatly recommend this server to those who need a refreshing taste of EQ. It is kind of fun (and frustrating at times) to start with nothing and have no means of acquiring things other than your own effort all over again.

I am a memeber of the bestest guild on Firiona Vie. I can't even imagine going back to any "blue" server except as a momentary diversion.
