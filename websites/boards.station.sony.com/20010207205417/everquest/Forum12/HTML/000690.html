First of all I lets take a look at wizards as defined by Everquest. I will post it here verbatim as a easy reference point and it is as follows:

Wizards are the master seekers of knowledge, and their talents and spells are focused on this goal. They use powerful spells to not only see things from afar, but to transport them there as well. This thirst for arcane knowledge has also made them the true masters of the strongest form of defense: offensive spells of pure destruction. In their ability to do magical damage, they have no rivals.

Balance Issue # 1
Magicians can come pretty close to dealing the same level of DD a Wizard can at nearly any level. Also a level 44 Cleric can outdamage a 43 Wizard.

Solution(s)
I propose one or a combination of the following solutions:

1)Increase DD of spells.
2)Increase mana pool of wizards.
3)Increase mana regen rate of wizards (naturally).

It makes the most sense to me that wizard dd spells should by far exceed that of any other class in terms of actually damage per cast as well as speed of casting.


Balance Issue #2
Wizards have no pet, no heal, no damage shields worthy of note, and harvest is only good up to a certain level.

Arguments to support wizard is out of balance:

1)Magicians get a spell granting HP Regen and powerful DD line.
2)Clerics get Direct Healing and Good DD line.
3)Druids get Direct Healing, HP Regen Line, and Good DD line.
4)Enchanters get Mana Regeneration and Good DD line.

Mage and Chanters get a pet in one form or another potentially not only saving ones life but also allowing them to use far less mana than a Wizard. (Supporting the suggesting of increasing the wizard mana pool).

Clerics and Druids get the ability to heal and Do strong DD casting too. (Supporting wizard DD should be far greater).

Wizards suffer on both HP and Mana Downtime.

One of the following paths should be taken.

1) Increase wizard DD spells. I think they should do 50% - 200% over any other class of equal level.

2) Increase mana pool if increasing wizard DD is unacceptable.

3) Increase wizard natural mana regen rate since we rely more on our mana than any other class. (ie. no pet, no heal, no significant mana regen).

4) Decrease wizard spell cost if increasing mana pool is unacceptable.

5) Provide wizards with some sort of heal and mana regen ablity. Granted this should be to a lesser degree of a druid/shaman regen and to chanter clarity lines and like many wizard class spells they would be self cast only to not unbalance other classes.

I realize high level wizard get manaskin which is a step in the right direction but it comes pretty late in life and is not as valuable as it should be. At the level attained it should be around the power level of clarity. I would venture to say for this line of spell it should be (wizard level / 2) giving the same level of power as a chanter of that level as a guideline.

Also Wizards are very weak in the early levels and rightfully so but in the latter levels we should have no equals. I would say as an example a level 60 wizard should throw a DD spell around the 3000 level and the next strongest class a mage should hit for about half that for about the sama mana cost vs any target. Their pet should make up the other half in some way. For specialty DD like Clerics/Necros to Undead and Druids/Mages to Summoned they can about for about half as much as well but for a lesser mana cost.

To me DD is what a Wizard is mostly about. I think the balance should be focused here since according to wizard definition our DD is our Defense. These are just a few of the ways from my personal perspective as how the wizard class needs to be balance as in my personaly opinion wizards are the class that most needs to be balanced.

I've played a wizard to 34th, a mage to 35th, and an enchanter to 40th (so far). The wizard was my first character, and after playing these other classes, I am left wondering exactly what "balanced" means?

It seems to me that Wizards are not enough different in terms of a clear practical benefit they deliver to a group as compared to other classes. Wizards have potential to deliver a lot of fast damage. This would be a clear practical bennefit if it were true for MOST of a wizards life. This does not seem to be the case as in order to survive an encounter in a group, a wizard is forced to space out the damage done. The primary goal of a group is to kill things, and currently a wizard does this about as well as other classes with DD spells but brings no other benefits to table (assuming the wizard has an interest in self preservation).

Perhaps wizards should be to allowed to cast at full throttle with impunity. Where another casting class would get the mob frenzied upon itself, the wizard would be relatively safe.

One thought as to how to accomplish this might be to allow a taunt to push the wizard below the taunting attacker on the mob's hate list (or something equivalent based upon taunt). While this would not help a wizard in a solo situation (which could use some help), it would help a wizard in a group, and let the group benefit from a wizard chain casting DD's or AOE's where other classes would risk death.
