The bad news is that, somehow, I took a faction hit when we killed Skargus. I think someone in my party aggroed the Traitor and my pet took a swipe at him. The net result is that the faction hit is relatively large - a bit more than an entire level (I was max ally when we started).

The good news is that Jabober and I only needed to slaughter in those caves for about 90 minutes before I was back to Ally (/con the Traitor to check). The goblins in these caves seem to be of the same levels as those previously found in Nurga.

The better news is that a Circlet of Shadows dropped, which I gave to my baby SK.

The best news is that the salts/skins that dropped took me back to Max Ally, I'm best friends again with Herald Telcha, and I have the Regal Band to show for it.
