The general consensus is that the hardcap is 252. Since 5% of 240 is 12, this is the "magic" number that you need to effectively have max GM status in a skill (with geerlok equipped). The number gets lower (220ish, 230ish) for smithing with the 10 & 15% mod items out there. Skills that cap at 200 (alchemy, forage, swimming, etc) are still hardcapped at 252 and so are worth maxing out even if you have a +% skill mod item for them.
