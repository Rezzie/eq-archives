Rangers? LOL Number 1... A ranger tanking post 50 scares the bejesus outta me. It can be done but not very well with a tank with the AC around that of a shaman... IF THAT! Number 2? Although they can taunt quite well (assuming they don't use their dots which probably won't stack with whatever the group evac'r is casting unless it's a wizard) they don't really outdamage warriors. And eventually (although extremely late) the warrior gets triple attack which although rare still boosts the warriors output. And lastly? I've played both a ranger and a warrior to post 50. Anyone who says a ranger would make a better tank than a warrior (OR EVEN COMES CLOSE!!!) needs serious psychiatric evaluation.

Shadowknight? Some cool abilities and can be a tank in a pinch but doesn't really seem to have the taunting ability of a warrior even if he nears him AC wise. This translates into basically the problem of keeping the mobs off the rogue or the casters and if the mob is aggroing the cleric and can't be taunted off him? You're looking at a CR.

Paladins? Basically a shadowknight with less battle friendly (or battle taunting spells) spells who carries some buffs that are ok if there is no cleric and some ok heals later in life. One get out of jail free card LoH but even that doesn't bring all the hp back of a fully buffed tank from what I've seen.

Bards? LOL Well their songs are pretty taunting but they don't have any real taunt ability past that and the dual wielding skill. I've often wondered how bad a singer the bards I travel with frequently are to be able to irritate the heck out of the mobs the way they do as it is.

Warriors? Best AC. Most HP. Best Weapon skills. Most consistent tanks in the game (aside from the recent quasi nerfs that aside from the UBER encounters I don't see causing an enormous amount of trouble)

Simply put I find it more disappointing that the game is designed on so much of a bring 30 people and hack away to beat this mob or that rather than (and I'm not saying it doesn't take skilled tanks/clerics to do it) a group of 6 overcoming adversity. The *Vision* doesn't really allow for that though. Oh well. I can still hope for better games though.
