***WARNING: THIS POST CONTAINS SPOILERS***

The following quest has been unsolved since about the middle of May, and it is causing some distress for those of us who play Iksar Shamans and actually enjoy the quest aspect of the game.

At the conclusion of the fourth skull quest (fifth overall) the Iksar Shaman is told to turn in his items to Mystic Dovan in the Swamp of No Hope for the next quest. Mystic Dovan gives the Iron Cudgel of the Prophet and sends the shaman out to collect the "Skulls of DiNozok," which must then be returned to Hierophant Zand in the Temple of Terror.

After much searching and identifying, it was finally discovered that the "Iksar Skull Helm" dropped by a Sarnak Adherant in the Sarnak Fortress area of the Lake of Ill Omen zone identifies with the magic spell as a "Skull of Die Nozok." When this is returned to Hierophant Zand, he says, "We are Dai and Die and we await our skulls and your Iron Cudgel of the Prophet. Become a channeler." The problem is that every level 20-30 MOB the length and breadth of Kunark has been slain and nothing drops a skull that identifies as the "Skull of Dai Nozok." My gut hunch, based on the previous quests in the series, is that the Dai skull is supposed to be a rare drop item in the loot table for the Sarnak Adherant, but that it was inadvertantly missed when the loot tables were populated, or if the claim of one player can be believed, that it was inadvertantly scrubbed from the loot table in an update to the game.

A thread detailing the search has reached 90 posts over at the EQ Forge/Forums/Shaman/Quest and Special Items board, and it is rather distressful for those of us trying to complete the quest because we have faith that the ultimate Iron Cudgel upgrade will be a truly special weapon instead of the ho-hum 5/23 magic-hitting toy that we have in the Iron Cudgel of the Prophet.

If Hobart or Esther could please look into this quest and give us some acknowledgement that they have determined that (1) the quest is indeed bugged, or (2) that they have found the Dai skull in the place it is supposed to be and we all need to keep searching, it would make a lot of players very happy. Thank you.
