Even with autosplit on you won't know how much he was getting and how much should be split.

Reason: Autosplit is gimp.

Works like this... A group of 6 people kill a mob. Mob drops 5pp, 5gp, 5sp and 6cp. Looter has autosplit on. Guess how much everyone gets?

The looter gets 5pp, 5gp, 5sp, 1cp.
Everyone else gets 1cp.

Reason: Autosplit only splits if there is enouch coinage to split evenly between all group members, the undividable rest goes to the looter. So you _are_ better off just letting the ML loot everything without split and then divide afterwards.

Most LDoN groups I've been in established a few rules beforehand and they were all very similar:

- One Master Looter who loots ALL droppable items.
- Dropppable items are rolled on AFTER we leave the dungeon.
- NO DROP stuff (augments) are rolled on the spot (1 100) among those who want it, once you won one you cant roll again until everyone has one or noone else wants it.
- Leave the effing chests and boxes and whatnot alone until we accomplished the mission objective.

Just four short rules, but they cover about everything that can happen and really ease the whole experience.

Oh, it would have worked in Paludal. You just were not patient enogch

If the spell puts 3 disease counters on you (as Spreading Crud, the proc from the Fiends in PC does), you will have to cast a low level spell (Cure Disease removes ONE counter) THREE times to get rid of the disease.

Now for Plague Mites:
Plague Mites puts a yet unknown number of disease counters on you, presumably based on proximity to the chest. It has an AE range of 400 and it's effect (at level 65) is a 131 DoT which sticks for 60 minutes (100 ticks). Just cast Cure Disease (or better a higher level equivalent) a LOT, and you'd get rid of it.
