From GFay, Tox, NK or Dreadlands you are taken into a zone on Luclin called "The Nexus". Everyone who arrives on Luclin lands in the same spot no matter what the starting point was on Norrath.

There are four tunnels leading from the center room of the Nexus. Each tunnel contains one Scion that will port you down to either GFay, Tox, NK or the Dreadlands. So yes, you get to choose where you land as you leave Luclin.


Nica
Terris~Thule

PS ~ You can be ported down from the moon by any regular <wizard or druid> portal spell.

PSS ~ There are wizard portal spells that are only good on Luclin; they are used to move between spots on Luclin. They cannot be cast from the mainland to get to Luclin.
