It can very well be a scam. About the only way to swap characters would be to swap passwords and allow each other to play on their accounts. You would probably just get dummy account information passed on, while he will have free reign over all your characters and in no time flat strips them off everything that can be traded by passing it on to a character on one of his other accounts.

If he is feeling malicious (some do for some unknown reason) he then proceeds to destroy all your NODROP gear and then strands you in a bind death loop to make you loose some levels.

In other words, you have little to gain and everything you worked for to loose. Just say no.

You might want to /report them to the GM's so they can keep an eye on him though.
