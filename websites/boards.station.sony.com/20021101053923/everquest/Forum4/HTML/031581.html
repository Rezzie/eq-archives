Short answer is you always become visible upon dropping any item upon the NPC to turn in. Even if your icon is up, as they say, you are not invis. I've always had the icon instantly disappear.

In high hold and some other old areas, wolf form does NOT disguise your diety (nothing does) but rather hides your race and gives you kind of an indifferent faction race base. It is actually an old bug which verant did not intend, making you more like an Npc. The weirdest area is Unrest, where it simply makes you totally indifferent. In any newer areas they've compensated by making wolf form kill on sight for the most part. They even changed Halas/halas factions so you are kos in wolf form (big surprise for us first druids finding that out .. ahaha)

Doing some testing with my Dark elf cleric, innoruuk diety of course, I've seen that for the most part wolf form just masks her dark elf race. She is still kos or very low faction to npcs who started as scowl. Some factions get raised to dubious though. Wolf form is usually one half to a full faction step higher than when she uses the one use halfling disguise mask from the regent of innoruuk quest line (DEF TRL HUM, SHM CLR only)

In one particular case, with the mask she was still threaten to the freeport militia in halfling form while wolf form made her dubious (she was low threaten in dark elf form) After turning in some belts in wolf, she was still threaten in dark elf form but upon getting the halfling mask again found she was now dubious in halfling. So she did not need a druid's help to turn in belts to get non kos in dark elf.

Judging by the reaction of innoruuk hating npcs, wolf/halfling or whatever form does not disguise your diety. This is why halfling cleric is an unraiseable faction for a dark elf cleric. Their only faction raiser is a quest with them and the dark elf cleric is still threaten in either form.
