You may need to expand your window if you don't see a couple more buttons that say "Track" and "Cancel." (I think - I use a modified version, but I think that's correct.) There is also a place for Track Type or Sort - you can order them by distance, by /con or reverse them, I think. I just use distance all the time.

When you see the name of something you want to track in the window, click on to highlight it, and click on Track. You'll start getting messages that "<MOb name> is to ahead and to the left," or right, or behind you, etc. Then when you turn, "<MOb name> is straight ahead." Keep going that direction you'll eventually find what you're tracking.

Handy for avoiding things, too.

Since you are a Druid you will not be able to sort the track window as Kharlisht suggests. Rangers can do that but Druids do get get that type of sort function.
