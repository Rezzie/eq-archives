If you have about 20 to 30 platinum and a few hours you can master pottery real quick. Just get a whole lot of medium bowl sketches, medium blocls of clay, and water and pot away. Not many use the wheel so it is not in high demand, meaning you rarely gotta wait for or share the wheel.

As a rogue it was important for me to become a master potter for the use of poison. I picked up my medium bowl sketches in Highpass Hold at the Top of the World, after getting faction there of course. Bought a backpack full (160) of medium bowl sketches, went back to Neriak and made bowl after bowl for 2 hours. No need to fire them. You could make money but will double the time you will be here. I destroyed the unfired products and lost about 25pp but for a 122 skill it was worth it.

I just recently made to Master Potter.
I took my time and enjoy making other than bowls. [Baker too]. Vials got me beyond Above average.
