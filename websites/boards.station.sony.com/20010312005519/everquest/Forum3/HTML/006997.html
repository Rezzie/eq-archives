This might have already been posted (didn't see it on the first few pages), but as of last night, it was still in the game.

The way EXP is given out is broken. Last night, in a 6 person group, our puller would go pull something, bring it back to us, then leave about halfway through the fight to go get something else. If he was outside of the 'exp giving' radius around the mob when it died (if you're too far away, you don't get xp), NOBODY in the group would get xp.

Another, more broken case, last Sunday in Fear, we're pulling mobs back to our raid group to kill. First off, the pulling team was getting nearly all the experience, even through NOBODY was fighting in the group. Second, if everyone from the pulling team was outside of the XP radius, the corpse of the mob would disappear.

These bugs are trivial to reproduce. Get a group of 6 people, have one person do most of the damage in the group (make sure everyone in the group is on the hate list, tho; everyone run up and whack the mob a couple of times), then have the person who did the most damage run way out of range, then kill the mob. Nobody in the group will receive experience.
