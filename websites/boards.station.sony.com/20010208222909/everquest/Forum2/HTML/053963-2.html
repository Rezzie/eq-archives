Paladins and Shadowknights should be able to replace a warrior if they use their spells to full capacity. The warrior should still be able to tank better, but pal/sk have others skills.

Up to 50, this is pretty much accomplished.. all are desirable tanks.

Post 50, it's like pal/sk are wet rags compared to warriors, and forget it, rangers are like casters.

Why? Because defense skills lack about 30 pts behind, and warrior armor always has more AC (compare a 50 or more AC breastplate vs a 30 for pal/sk). Combine that with higher hitpoints from a warrior, and you get a much better tank (able to suck up hitpoints).

Also, pal/sk lag about 50 pts behind on offense. Their weapons are usually pretty bad, with some exceptions. But they can't dual wield. So the offense points reduce pal/sk output to about 40% of a warrior (when both are average equipped).

Pal/sk used to be able to use their stuns/dots to improve defense/offense respectively, but post 50 those are pretty much a joke. Stuns get entirely useless on all 55+ mobs (they just don't work), and shadowknights lack a simple DD for their dmg output to be improved.

So at this point, ALL groups would rather take a warrior over a hybrids. Is that balanced? Nope...
