The problem with the Mage epic quest is not so much the difficulty as it is the randomness of it.

For example the Power of Wind, Earth, Fire, and Water. These are drops off various mobs in the game. I spent 15 hours just getting the power of Fire. 15 hours killing green mobs over and over. There was nothing challenging about that in the slightest. For that matter what the heck do gypsies or faeries have to do with the elements?

Instead of blazing elementals in Solusek what about a triggered fire elemental(buffed up) that spawns in Lavastorm. Power of Earth...why is that on Faeries? Why not put it in Cazic Thule on the Avatar of Fear? The point is these items could have been put on mobs that would at least be challenging. A time sink is not challenging or fun.

Torn pages of Mastery..another time sink. Pieces for the mage focus items drop in Najena so I guess that was the reasoning behind putting these here. Again, not challenging or fun. It's just a matter of going around killing greens until all the items drop. Same deal here as with the various power drops. To complicate matters though these items have to be turned in to a mob that gets killed constantly and has a very long respawn time. Nothing challenging about standing around waiting for it to pop...just tedious.

Let's look at the sub-quest for the Element of Fire. One of the items needed is the Torch of Elements from a tough mob deep within the City of Mist. It's not enough to kill him once...you have to kill him repeatedly because as difficult as this is the item does not drop every time. Another item needed...Burning Embers off a named wurm in Burning Woods. Same deal. A very difficult mob that does not drop the item every time...and has an even longer respawn time.

The Element of Water...I have no complaints about this one. Each mob is challenging amd with the exception of Phingel they drop their items each time. The water mastery staff needed off Phingel is a rare drop but this is offset by the fact that he spawns every 9 hours or so.

The Element of Wind...ouch! First off you need the crown of mastery from the 7th island in Sky. Now that's challenging. Perhaps a little *too* challenging. I don't think many guilds are capable of getting that far even if they want to dedicate a whole day to it. So be it though. The Pegasus Cloak though...again there is nothing challenging about sitting around killing green mobs for hours on end to get the rare drop off a rare mob.

Now we get to the real kicker..the one piece that has every mage in the game ready to lynch the whole Verant staff out of sheer frustration. The Element of Earth. The problem...one of the required items is the earth mastery staff. This is the rare drop off the boss mob in the zone but unlike Phingel he has a very long respawn time.

On both the cloak and the staff you have to be in the right place at the right time or you aren't getting it. At least with the pegasus cloak you don't have to drag your guildmates along and subject them to sheer boredom and frustration as well. It's frustrating in the extreme to have to sit around and wait a week for Innojerk to spawn knowing full well you have a slim to none chance of the staff dropping....if you can get to it before another guild drops him first since everyone is after this guy.

If you're going to keep the Staff on Inny then increase the drop rate significantly. He should drop it every 1 in 3 or 4 times. Or you could move it to another mob that is just as hard as Innoruuk but spawns more often. Even then I'd say increase the drop rate a bit. It keeps the players happy, it lets the uberguilds get thier staff and get out so other guilds can hunt there, and it at least gives us the hope that we actually have a chance to complete this quest.

Thanks for your time in reading this
