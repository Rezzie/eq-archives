Abashi - I know you're busy, but please keep one eye on this one...

1) Shestar's Scaled Coif - Approx. 5-7th, Trooper Shestar in East Cabilis. Requires 3 wolf scales and the unfinished coif. Both Loose scales and Wolf scales have been tried to no avail. If there is another component, please change what the NPC says.

2) Granite Pebbles - Approx 3rd - 5th, Toilmaster in East Cabilis. Requires 2 busted froglok shackles. This quest is FAR too time-consuming for the rewards. The rarity of the spawn, combined with that fact that it is a component in no fewer than 3 quests (all low-level) makes this an absurdity.

3) Monk Sparring Armour - Approx 3rd - 5th, Master Bain in East Cabilis. Requires 3 piles of granite pebbles (see above) and a Training Bag Husk. This first half of this quest is fine - leech husks are dropping on average about 1 in four, which is good. The second half, however, is the real problem. You need three pebbles - which requires the above quest to be done three times. THEN you have to combine them using tailoring skill. And since there is no mention of how much tailoring skill you need, you can (and will) flub this. When you do, you lose the three piles of pebbles - the hardest part of this quest! Please explain how pebbles can be destroyed by sewing? If anything, the bag should be the consumed component. Please change that. Or make it an automatic success. The reward for this is simply not worth the aggravation - the armour is exactly the same as the much easier to attain Curscale armour, except for looks. Big deal - cured silk isn't that far off. This quest needs to be tweaked to be of any value.

Thank you - and everyone else, please JUST use this to report further errors or correct errors in the reports.
