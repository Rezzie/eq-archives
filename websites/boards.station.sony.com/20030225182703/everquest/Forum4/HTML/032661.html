A few of places you can go/try: I am currently a 25 Necro:

1) Needites in Echo Caverns. (Haven't tried this)
2) Sister Island in OOT.
3) Ak'Anon. There's a Gnome inside the city towards the back. His/her name is A Mechanic. He/she is next to a named NPC. They are right next to some water. You will see a broken wheelbarrel. Just pull Mechanic. The NPC will not attack (unless you AoE). Once the Mechanic is low on life, he will run into the water. You will need to jump in the water and blast it before it gets to a lvl50 spider.
4) Lower Faydark. Near the Steamfront zone, there is a Gnome inside a hut. Forget her name. Blast her. None of the others will help her.

Don't know about any other places right off the top of my head.

Millers in QHills outside SFG.
Guards in EC.
Rangers in WC.
Named Sisters in OOT.
Dwarves in OOT.
Barbarians in WK.
The Hermit in SK.
Single spectre in Feerott.

These are all single or 2 mob spawns that are on a 6 min timer and give 1 blue bub per kill.

At 25 Millers, the Dwarves in OOT., Named Sisters in OOT. And I think the Hermit in SK are all good exp.

To hunt Undead at 25 you looking at going to a dungeon like Unrest which would require a group.

However at 29 you can solo the lesser Ice Shades on the first floor of TOFS in Iceclad Ocean. Best way is to split the spawn by charming one. Kill the other one. Zone out. Wait for the one you charmed to return to its spawn point. Then go back in, summon a pet. and pull with your Undead slow. then let your pet tank. Just keep Renew Bones mem'd to heal your pet.

Yeah, the needlites are NOT on a six-minute spawn; twenty is more like it.

There are undead of around level 24 or so in Shadeweaver's Thicket, between Shar Vahl and Paludal Caverns, if you're really intent on undead.

But if you can get to the Needlites without finding them camped by others, just keep Banshee Aura up and melee them with your pet (even the level 20 pet will work fine) acting as a DoT.

The Millers are a bit tougher now that they have a friend nearby - a Druid that's much tougher and assists them.
