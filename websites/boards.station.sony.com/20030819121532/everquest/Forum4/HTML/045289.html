quote:
just had a thought..if you are in elemental form, would Kindle work on YOU? heh

Nope, the spell is flagged pet-only.

Spells always have one of the following flags:


Self: Self only buffs, pet summons, enchantments, gate and self-portals (the yellow gem).

Group Beneficial (blue gem) : Group buffs, group ports, songs

Target Beneficial: translocates, single target buffs (can only be cast on players)

Taret Detrimental: can be cast on NPCs only, nukes/debuffs/dots etc.

Pet: auto targets and casts on pet, only works if a pet is up.
