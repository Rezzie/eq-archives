quote:
We can add the Earthshaker to this list now that it no longer drops. Much fun can be had with an Earthshaker and rampage AA. Great for faction farming.

Yes I am bringing this thread back up, as I did a search on the Earthshaker to find little information about it here on TNZ. I only found out about it's unique ability with 'Rampage' the other night. I bought it cheap in the bazaar back in Oct/Nov of last year before I cancelled out (but I'm back in now). I keep searching for it as my Heirophant's Crook on my druid had the same proc. I did a lot of reading on Steelwarrior in the few posts that included info about how it is best used and wanted to share that info here. Apparantly a few warriors can take out TONS (80+) of critters (where the more critters the better) with one 'swing' of the sword.

1) Earthshaker no longer drops (for obvious reasons).
2) Take off all hastes & get someone to slow you (to increase proc rate for the first swing).
3) Round up tons of critters (the more the better).
4) Have Divine Aura or Fortitude handy so you can survive the onslaught, or have someone else bring all the critters to you.
5) Hit Rampage & with one swing of the sword (the slow increases the chance it will proc) 'each' critter in your Rampage proximity gives the sword additional chances to proc the PBAoE.
6) Thousands upon thousands of points of damage will be caused by the massive number of PBAoE procs.
7) With the right AA's, each mob undergoes the skill checks for double hits (double procs attempts) and triple hits (a third proc attempt).

Concluding remarks: Earthshaker has limited use, but when it can be used, it is AWESOME! Reportedly you can gain between 1 to 1.5AA's per roundup (for each groupie) if three Rampage warriors and support classes are available.

Current Value: Approx 500,000pp
Bought it for less than 6k, maybe even less than 2k.
