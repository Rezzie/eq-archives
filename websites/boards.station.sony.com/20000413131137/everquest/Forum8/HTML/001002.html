racial meat can be found on most NPCs. here's a list of what I've found so far and where.

Dark elf parts: on dark elves in Najena and Befallen
Woodelf parts: woodelves on Erollisi island in OOT
gnome meat: on gnome necros in Befallen
dwarf meat: dwarves on Erollisi island

I have yet to find more. I suppose you can get them off the guards of each city. I know of recipes with halfling meat and high elf meat. I haven't seen any recipe with:

human meat
troll meat
barbarian meat
ogre meat
erudite meat
human meat
half elf meat

of course I haven't seen any of those meats either. good luck
