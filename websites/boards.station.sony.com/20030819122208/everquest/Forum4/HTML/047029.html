Necros are probably the overall best soloers, they're tactics work well from 1-65. Wizards have it hard in the beginning because they don't have a lot of soloing spells until they can snare and quad-kite. Mages have a consistent tactic all the way, but it decreases in effectiveness the higher you get, they also can handle adds nearly as well.
