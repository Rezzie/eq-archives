If your intent is only to play on one machine at a time, then you do not need to purchase a second copy or account. If you want to be able to play 2 characters at once, then you do need to buy a second copy and second account.

All your character information is stored on the server, but your eqclient.ini file, friends lists, bazaar logs, logfiles, hot key setups, and such files are local to your machine.

you just copy the appropriate files over...

Do a search of that directory with *yourname*.*

The copy those files to the directory...

Charname_01.ini = Friends list
Charname_01.opt = Options for that character
UI_Charname_01.ini = UI set up
BZR_Charname_01.ini = Bazaar prices

Charname - Your character name....
01 = Server number

For instance: Tyrellan_26.ini is my character's friends file on Cazic Thule
