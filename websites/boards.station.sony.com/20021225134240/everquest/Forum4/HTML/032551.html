Manually opening & closing ports on a router pretty much defeats the purpose of having one...

If you are having those pop-up ad problems you've either a virus of some type on your machine or you've inadvertantly gotten some ad-ware installed on there.

Preventing those kinds of things from happening is usually easier the cleaning up after the fact. First thing to try is running the latest version of your anti-virus software. If it finds a well-known virus (or trojan or whatever) you can find cures for them on-line.

If that doesn't find anything, use something like IEGuard, which looks at your cookies and scopes out any suspicious executables on your machine.

And if neither of those fix your problem, sad to say, but sometimes wiping your HD and starting fresh is the best thing to do.
