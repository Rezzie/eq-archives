here is what Major Shielding does: This information taken from HTTP://LUCY.FNORD.NET

1: Increase Max Hitpoints by 69 (L24) to 75 (L30)
2: Increase AC by 16 (L24) to 18 (L28)
6: Increase Magic Resist by 14

notice it only increases the MAX hp..

now lets look at another HP buff item Donals Arms of Mourning.. Cleric Kunark Arms.. it has a right click of VALOR.

1: Increase AC by 12 (L34) to 13 (L40)
2: Increase Max Hitpoints by 168 (L34) to 200 (L50)
3: Increase HP when cast by 168 (L34) to 200 (L50)

Notice on this it increases BOTH your MAX and your CURRENT hp when cast so this will in fact HEAL your target the hp it adds to their max..

even IF Major Shielding was a 75HP heal with a 10 second cast time a complete heal from a cleric would be much more efficient then standing there for 10 seconds doing absolutely no damage to the mob, plus having the likely chance that you will not channel the spell through the beating you are taking thereby requiring you to recast wasting yet another 10 seconds.. and lets face it at level 48 75 hp heals mean absolutely SQUAT in the first place.

If your fighing a dragon that dispells, a 10 second cast right click item will do NOTHING for you.. you will spend the entire fight clicking your BP to save the buffs underneath instead of doing damage.
