Okay, I've read a lot on this faction issue in Kurnark and thought hard about it. I read Verant's reasoning behind their decision, and weighed it versus the impact this situation has on the game.

After deliberation, I think the situation is not acceptable. Here's what made up my mind:

I play a High Elf magician. I'm currently working on my focus items quests. These quests involve getting some items from Najena (among other places) and bringing them to the Temple of Solusek Ro.

Now, what I asked myself was: What if killing the magicians in Najena to get these items made me KOS in the Temple of Solusek? How would I feel?

I'd be really, really ticked off, that's how I'd feel. I'd feel that Verant was being deliberately cruel in a situation such as this. From what I understand, this situation is pretty much the way it is for the Evil races in Kurnark.

This just really isn't fair. I don't care if there are compelling roleplay issues for the decision (and I roleplay heavily, I might add). If there are roleplay reasons to not allow you in the city if you hunt in these dungeons, then DON'T PUT THE SPELLS THEY HAVE TO QUEST FOR IN THE CITY.

NPC: "Go and kill the might Jim Bob, and bring me his head, and I will give you a Good Thing(TM)"

Player: "Okay!" (runs off, kills Jim Bob, brings back his head) "Here is the head you asked for!"

NPC: "Oh my god! You killed Jim Bob! Now I will kill you!"

I mean come on, this is garbage. Something should be done about this.
