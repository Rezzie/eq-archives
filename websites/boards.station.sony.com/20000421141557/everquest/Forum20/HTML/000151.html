I thought I'd go ahead and answer this question since most people would look right over it.

There is a way but it is -extremely- difficult. Once in a blue moon (I've heard of him on our server only once in the last whole year) the Priest of Order will appear (he was in Qeynos that time) and will make you go through some excruciating quests to remove that tag.

I think that it is wrong of Verant to make it so easy to go +PvP. Understand that I mean that I -agree- that it should be extremely hard to go -back- to blue... But-- Everyone starts with this clever little book and information saying to give it to the Priest. How many people think Hey, this is a quest, this is Everquest, I need to do this! And then they regret it forever..

Seriously, if you've given the book to that damned priest, and you're still under level 10, recreate your character. You're seriously better off, and the first 10 levels fly by. As a matter of fact, I don't think I've ever seen a red player on our server over level 10.

I hope this information helps ya, good luck in rejoining the blue population.
