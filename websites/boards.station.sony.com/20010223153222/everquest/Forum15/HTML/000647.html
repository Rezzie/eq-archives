My suggestion for a new weapon would be a Crystal Fiber whip (8 dmg 26 delay). These can be bought for less than 75pp (as low as 50pp) and is one of the best deals available to a lower ranger. The whip can be equipped in either hand.

Once you get higher in levels, Icetooth's Claws are a good choice (11/29, main hand only, look like claws when equipped). You will really see the damage start with this weapon.

For a 2HS, the Axe of the Iron Back (16/37) is also the best buy for the money (50pp-75pp).

At higher levels, you can forget about casting spells during combat, since you will be able to do far more damage with your weapons than any spell. The one exception is Stinging Swarm (level 30, 100ish DoT), which can be used to pull or at the start of combat.

I would stress that you keep your 2HS skill maxed at every level. At very high levels, dual wield with 2 good weapons is such a taunt that it will get you killed or make you a mana sink for your cleric. If the warrior tank in your group can't taunt the mobs off you, switch to 2HS. You still get lots of damage, but th emob won't stay glued to you.

Hope this helps. Despite the problems with the Ranger class, I still think that this is the most fun I have had with any character.
