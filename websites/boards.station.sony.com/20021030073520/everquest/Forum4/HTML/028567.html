Its all the way at the bottom. The best solution would be to make a lvl 1 halfling rogue worshipping bristlebane. Use sneak/hide and walk down all the way to the bottom. You can't miss the graveyward - it kinda sticks out.

Drag the corpse (if you can target it in the mess) up to the 'ledge' area near the zone-in. Your friend can drag it himself if he consents then logs out again I think.

Some critters in there will con as scowling, but won't attack if you sneak/hide. Ratmen and the undead will kill you if you aren't hidden - or so I have been told. I think there is a guy down there that may see through invis and be KOS to all - Yalnik (sp?) um - begins with Y anyways.

Your other option - and far easier - is to grab a necro and a coffin and summon the corpse to the zoneline. The corpse pile location will take about 10 minutes to reach even with SoW on before hand and knowing exactly where you are going... so the coffin is a nice quick solution.

Good luck.
