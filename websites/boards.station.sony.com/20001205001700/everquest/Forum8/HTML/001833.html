Well, the big problem with the current instruments we have (basic storebought, MMDrums, LuteOGP, and THEN Selo's DOTM, Lyssa's DWP, McV HoW, Sharkskin Drums, etc.) is that there's not much to choose from, especially at mid-level, like 30-45. Sure MM Drum is nice. And eventually you can quest for the Tamb, but until you can slay dragons and go Plane-Hunting, what about other instruments? I mean, LuteOGP is only 5%, MinoHorn is nothing, and there *is* no brass between StoreBought and HoW (as far as I know?).

They could let us get the Instrument Crafting skill at 25 (when Shamans get Alchemy; I'm not sure about Rogues?). Until about 100 skill we'd be crafting storebought instruments. Say "Brass Trumpet" would be trivial at 30. "Wooden Flute" at 50. "Hand Drum" at 75. And finally "Lute" at 100. These would all be made out of storebought items too.

THEN we get to fun stuff. Until 110 we could be crafting "Silver-Plated Cornet" which could be maybe 10% to brass. Or "Enchanted Gold-Plated Saqueboute" that's cha3 int3 and 12% to brass and trivial at 130. Maybe at 250 an uber instrument of each class, though absurdly expensive (Jewelry-at-250 expensive), would finally become trivial.

There are a HUGE NUMBER of things they could do with this skill with just a little thought, intuition, and cleverness, without ever trivializing the usefulness of Uber-Dropped isntruments.
