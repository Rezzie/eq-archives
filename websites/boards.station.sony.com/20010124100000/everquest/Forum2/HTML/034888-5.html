Some points here.

This spell doesn't add 30 AC, it adds 18 - the rest comes from the +50 Agility buff.

It doesn't stack with Augment, which also adds 18 AC and around +33 Agility. So we get some extra Strength and Dex instead of the Haste that comes from Aug.

But perhaps most importantly, this is a change to the whole nature of the spell.

Sure, it's a decent self buff. The Dex buff doesn't stack with other Dex buffs, it doesn't stack with Augment, but it's a spell we didn't have before. The people who didn't use the Rage line are happy because they have a new buff they can use. But the people who did use it are left high and dry, because this in no way replaces what they lost.

There's no Regen in this new Rage, and that was the most important part of the Rage line.
