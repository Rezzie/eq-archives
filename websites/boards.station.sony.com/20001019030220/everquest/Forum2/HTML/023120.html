Trackers, esp Rangers, have been asking for tracking to be fixed/enhanced for some time. Several VI replies have implied something would be done, but I've seen nada. This fix seems to have been put on the back burner for months and months.

For non-tracking folks here's the problem. The MOBs that show up on your track are not based on proximity (what's closest), it's based on what most recently spawned. It's a serious flaw.

For example, a rat 100 yards that just spawned a second ago will show up on the top or your track list, but a named MOB which is 10 feet away and been around all day will typically not show up.

This can be a real problem when it's a KOS MOB that will kick your butt and rarely shows up on track b/c it never repops unless someone kills it - which might be rare if say it's a high level DE Dragoon (50th) in the Overthere. This very thing nearly got me killed last night.

It's plain silly since something that's been around for a while would actually leave more tracks, while something that just showed up would hardly leave any tracks.

This glitch is really hazardous to a puller's health, I keep hearing you're going to fix it - but WHEN, as in WHEN, like a date or something. Please don't say 'we are working on it', you've told us that for so long. It's really serious issue to people who are higher up in their tracking abilities.

Thanks
