Level4 is probably too low but these are my favourite zones in the game -
Greater Faydark; I just really like the whole atmosphere of the plave (in offpeak hours - onpeak its greater fey-mart), and the kelethin music is the best. My favourite zone to "chill out" in, and where I always go to clear out the unwanted stuff from my bank for newbies.
Lake of Ill Omen; Its just so quiet - not always in the sense of zone chat, but its quite calming after the HELLISH run from firiona vie past the drachnids (DE/spider crossbreeds). Hearing the sound of the rushing river or the lapping water of the lake on the shore is really...nice. Plus its good exp from 15-30
Innothule swamp/feerot; Although these are troll/ogre newbie zones, I really like the whole swamp thang. Feerot is green and lush with that fantastic lizard temple on one side, Innothule is deep and dank and brown - perfect for trolls
The whole across the Karanas; I guess because I am a bard so I can outrun most stuff, the trip across the karanas is kind of lonely in a nice way. If I ever do bump into anyone on the path, I usually stop and /wave at them I especially like the ramp up to Highpass hold. It is SO damn high! And there are giant faces carved into the sides along the way, it just makes you stop and go "wow".
Well Im sorry I cant reccomend any places for a lower traveller, but when you get higher you will be able to explore and drift around with the best of 'em

Aegyn Eldarian 32 bard of bristlebane

First where are you right now? Traveling at level 4 can be fairly dangerous, escpecially when going to unknown areas...unless you're a rogue of course! You mentioned being human, so I'll take a stab at it and assume you're from Qeynos/Surefall area or Freeport (oo, great deductive mind there huh?).

Okay, if near Qeynos, and if you haven't already, GO SEE EVERFROST. It's beautiful. Just beware of vengeful skellies, and watch yourself when running past the gnolls. In fact, on second thought, wait until you're about 6th level or higher, so you can take on the gnolls if need be. But trust me on the everfrost thing, it's just an awesome (if under utilized) zone.

If you're from Freeport, I'd say try jumping the boat to Faydwer, much nicer scenery than found on Antonica. Just be sure to get a bind in Kaladim or (even better) Greater Faydark.


Other places of interest that I personally found spectacular were (in no particular order):

Neriak
Lavastorm
Dagnor's Cauldron
Warsilks Wood

Keep in mind, you'll want to be at least around level 15 to go to any of the above, and, to be frank, you're just not going to see Neriak. Period. Wait, I take that back, you can prob'ly camo and see it, and lavastorm too. But I digress.
