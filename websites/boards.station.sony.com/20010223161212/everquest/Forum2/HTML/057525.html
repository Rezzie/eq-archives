I love hell levels personally.

Knowing how absolutely horrid it is to try to grind out mob after mob in the same place hour after unendless hour, I make it a point to do exciting and dangerous things while in hell. I did more dungeon crawls in hells then any other levels. Yes, it took me longer, but i had much more fun. Its is hard to leave a good (albeit boring) exp camp when the bar moving, but in hell with very little movement, its much easier to be encouraged to find other fun stuff to do. At least thats how it works for me.

Of course, I also dont mind dying much, so I may be kinda wierd.
