Those two factions appear to be unrelated. My main is deep, deep KoS to all Frogoks, live and undead, everywhere in the game......except Gukta. In fact, he started off Amiable to them (possibly due to his religion - he's a Mithaniel Marr worshipper, like they are).

However, if you're a race/class/religion combination that starts off KoS to Frogoks of Guk, my guess is that you will probably start off KoS to Frogloks of Gukta as well.
