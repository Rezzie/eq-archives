sean852,

Our www.station.sony.com website has a forgot station name and forgot password function. If you attempt to login on the website you will see a SIGN IN icon on the upper left hand side of the screen. You would click on the SIGN IN icon and a Pop up login window will appear. At the base of the Login Window there will be a forgot station name function and a forgot password function. If you are unable to use these because you did not put in proper information, or your email address has changed from the one you were using before, you may call us at 1-888-STATION. Please be prepared with your account key. We can link your account key with your station name and update your information so you can use the password recovery system.

I would prefer you to call us up and ask us to ban the account. You violated the rules.
