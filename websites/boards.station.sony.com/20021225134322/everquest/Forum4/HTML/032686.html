I'm a little suprised at the above posts, they missed what many consider is the default exp grinds, so here they are:

20's in LOIO. (Lake of Ill Omen)

30's in Overthere.

40's in Dreadlands

Low 50's in Karnor's Castle

Hi 50's in Old Seb and Velks.

60's in Planes, especially the first tier zones. Those areas will give u max. grouping chance. Not to take away from the other suggestions, but the population density in the above is highest.

Regarding 'ninja grouping' for an hour, I would definately try to adjust your schedule to get at least two hours out, but one hour stints are really not that uncommon.

As you get higher though, such as in key camps in Seb, it takes 30 minutes just to fight your way to the camps. Upper 50's and beyond you may need to stick to soloing. You will also miss the fun of Raiding.
