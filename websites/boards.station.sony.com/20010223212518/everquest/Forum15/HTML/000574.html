Alright... I play a Paladin on the Xegony server, and I want to make a few comments on the posts I've just read.

1) Guilds and "not inviting hybrids." This is just plain stupid... not to mention rude. Any guild who's primary thought on who to invite is "how will this guy help us kill Trak/Inny/Whatever" is NOT a guild I want anything to do with. The guild I am in (with all of my characters) does not have this attitude. We help each other and help others. We have fun, socialize and enjoy the game. That's what I believe a guild should be about... not about who can kill Trak the fastest.

2) Soloing. Paladins (and SKs), I have found the ultimate weapon for soloing in the 25-35 range... the Two Handed Practice Sword. Yeah, a 6pt damage shield may not seem like much... but when you've got it, it helps like crazy. From level 26 to 30 I was soloing Goblin Warlords and Sarnak Recruits in LoIO... and when the skellys showed up I could often chain-solo for a bit. Yeah, I had some down-time... yeah, I had to do the Root/Back-off/Heal routine sometimes... yeah, I was in serious trouble if two or three mobs started hitting me at once... yeah, it was tough at times finding things to fight... (the competition is fierce; and without SoW to move around quick, finding a mob that isn't taken can be hard.) But I did it. Whip out the 2h-practice, beat on the mob until it procs, change up to sword & shield or DW Harpoon & spear (the best weapon for working on peircing for a Pally, btw) and go to work. You get the practice on all your melee skills, plus you're doing what you've always heard about... Soloing.
Oh, and at level 30 I can... and have... soloed hillocks & tumps at the giant fort in FM. Only just hit level 32, so I may go after a Brae soon.

3) Balance. If Verant wants to call Hybrids like SKs and Paladins the "defensive tanks", then our ability to take hits should be higher than a Warriors. Our taunting ability should be the same (or just slightly less), while our resistances and AC should be dramatically higher. Personally, I think Paladins (and yeah, SKs) should be nearly immune to spells cast by mobs that are more than a couple levels lower than they are... and very resistant to spells from the rest. I think that Dragon-Fear should be substantially less effective against "the defensive tanks" than against other classes. Let the Rogues, Warriors and Monks do all the damage. I want to be the one who everyone says "Did you see how that Paladin just kept pounding away on that dragon? It seemed like the dragonbreath didn't hardly slow him down!"

Obviously, this is a pipe-dream... But, it wouldn't be hard to put in. Simply increase the defensive abilities of Paladins and SKs to the 252 mark... raise the AC of mid/high level hybrid armour and double the "taunt factor" of any taunts done by a hybrid. Heck... triple the taunt factor against Undead. They should hate us even more!

Who cares if others do more damage... I want to be the one who is ALMOST (Not totally, mind you) required to let them do all that damage.
