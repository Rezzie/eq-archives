Just proceed through the quest as your suppose to. go back to the investigator and say "Summon a Guard"

A guard will spawn outside the building where the first suspect is, and take him away.

Say "Where is the 2nd suspect"

Lather, Rinse, Repeat.

"Where is the Final Suspect"

he *SHOULD* spawn down by Raz the Rat Misk by the docks. Once, I had to agro the investigator, pull him to the dock, and feign death when he was close enough to spawn the guy.

The final suspect, just hang on to the confession document for the next part of the quest, and kill the guy. you fight him anyway so you'll still get his head regardless.
