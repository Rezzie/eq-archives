Oh, boy.

Well, go to the appropriate city for your good/evil faction on Kunark. The quest givers are there.

Basically, you take three ornate bottles you get from them to Trakanon's Teeth, Karnor's Castle and the City of Mist.

There, find A Human Skeleton in each, and give them the bottle. They give you back a bottle of swirling smoke. Return these three to your quest giver and get Concussion.

There are complications. First, only the TT skeleton is easy to get to. The Karnor's Castle one is in fairly deep in the citadel (near the hand room), and depending on how camped KC is on your server, it might be hard to get to him. The City of Mist skeleton is upstairs and to your right when you zone in, a place that requires some work to get to.

Second, the real problem is that the CoM skeleton is KOS to you for some odd reason. You need to have a 51+ enchanter along, or a high level necro, to charm the skellie, once you've either pulled a half dozen angry monsters down to the entrance, or fought your way through far more to get to the skeleton.

Yes, that's right, a level 39 spell literally requires you go with someone 12 levels higher to complete the quest.

It's a booger of a quest. Having said that, it's one heck of a spell. Everyone I know swears by it. I'm THIS close to getting it (I got the last bottle, then went LD, and the bottle poofed, although the GM's logs said I did do the turn in) and I hope I can give reports soon on how well it works.
