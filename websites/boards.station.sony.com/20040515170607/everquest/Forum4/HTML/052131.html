Truthfully, most of the teen players are probably in a place called paludal Caverns, on Luclin (the moon). Experience comes fast there, but there are a lot of rude people there and your skills can lag if you level too fast.

You might also find groups in Oasis of Marr.

Truthfully, grouping is not especially common until 20ish except for certain camps in certain zones. After 20, you will see more consistent grouping, although it will tend to be most common in certain zones (the Overthere, Dreadlands, Dawnshroud Peaks, etc.).

Typing "/who all 12 20" will tell you where the people from level 12-20 are gathering (your level range). Generally, those are the places to go to get groups. More people in a zone usually means more grouping......
