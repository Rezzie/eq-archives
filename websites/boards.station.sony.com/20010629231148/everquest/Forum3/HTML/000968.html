I had the same problem in Windows 2000 that was causing me grief. I don't know if this will help with ME, but here is what I did:

1. Turn OFF Plug and Play support in your BIOS. This will force the BIOS to assign IRQ's. Apparently the BIOS does a better job than Windows ESPECIALLY if you have APM enabled.
2. If this doesn't work, try turning off ACPM support off in your BIOS and disabling it in Windows.

With Windows 2k, I actually had to re-install fresh, while forcing 2k to use a standard PC configuration WITHOUT APCM.

Most of my problems have been solved, although I still get a lot of screen flicker.

My video card is also the Radeon 64 DDR.

Hope this helps.
