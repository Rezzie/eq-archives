> 2. tier`dal chain boots are broken

Although I saw no mention of it in the patch, I do hope this is now fixed (how hard can it be?).

I was a bit put off by the coif.. it looks like an ill-fitting purple wig. I was expecting the chain/banded texture and model, not a colourised leather one for this. Intentional perhaps? I'm hoping it's another bug.

The rest of it though is cool, the AC is low (lower than banded) for the skill and cost involved, but only when compared to those who can wear banded or better anyway, but high for the casters that can wear it, so I guess it's just one of those 'pros and cons' things.

It does look incredibly dark, some people assumed it was black until I told them otherwise (and used different illusions to illustrate the point) -- not everyone has ultravision to see things the way we dark elves do =) I'd like to see that pic of the femmie dark elf in a normal light.. she looks positively unwell or ghostly and doesn't represent how the armour is seen in-game too well.

One thing I love the armour for is its weight. 0.5 for a bracer, 1.5 for the helm, and 3 or 3.5 or so for the chest piece.. compared to banded (or heaven forbid, the anvil-esque blackened iron) that was very welcome.

Given the effort collecting the stuff to make this (eg: elf blood.. ugh..) and the subsequent costs to make it, I get more than a few raised eyebrows when I tell prospective buyers how much it would cost for me to make it for them after they've seen the stats.

Just my experience so far with the armour =)
