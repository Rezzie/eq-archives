Armour and weapons really depend on what you are after, and what you can afford.

The +wis items are nice, but a bit expensive for our level (I'm a level 20 halfling druid).

The split paw gloves mentioned by WulfgarStormrider are +wis, so well worth getting a pair.

The Foremans tunic is good, and +3 Wis - but at 200pp is a trifle pricy unless you spent two or three seasons bashing wisps (gotta love those GLS).

Neckpiece - at this level a black Iron medallion for 15pp (+2 or +3 wis?) is a good bet.

Shoulders - a prayer cloth of tunare (3AC +3WIS) should set you back around 60pp, or get one yourself if you can find the uncommon spawn 'Warlord' in Crushbone. The Warlord drops Bracers of Battle (AC3 +1STR +3DEX) - hand those in to the elven priest for the prayer cloth (or wear them, they are DRU/ALL). It can be a bit difficult to get hold of these though. An alternate is the lizardscale mantle (AC5 +3WIS, +3INT), but those go for 70 to 80pp I believe.

Headgear - the leatherfoot raiders cap. A 7AC +10 Charisma +MR +AGI +STR halfling only piece. Its a Rivervale quest thats easy to do, except you need to find an alligator skin (amongst other things) and these are a rare drop (I've camped bull and fat alligators for around 8 hours with no joy). When you reach level 20 and get track, head down to the swamps of innothule and start bashing fat and bull alligators. You'll also need a Dragoon dirk, but they are fairly easy to come by (50 - 60pp).

You might want to check out the snakeskin armour (jerkin, gloves). Its a decent AC (no +wis I'm afraid), and you can get it yourself from an 11th season gnoll-noble in rathetear. I guess the jerkin (+9AC) would go for around 15pp?

Your FS Morning star is an OK weapon, the main drawback being its non-magical nature (i.e. you can't hit magic creatures (Yah I know you know this by your level, I just mention it for newer readers)). The FS Morning Star stats are 8/38 I believe. For a weapon that is a little better you could try a screaming mace (8/35, magic, procs the 'Screaming Mace' effect - a brief boost to strength) - but you pay 90pp for one of these (you have to ask yourself is it worth that sort of money).

A better bet might be to get yourself a Kerran fishing pole. The stats are slightly worse (3/17 +2 STR, magic, lore, nodrop) but its a magic item, its free (a level 15 quest on the kerran island) and because of its low delay it is excellent for raising your 1HB.

Hopefuly some of this info is of use to you. I've tried to verify the stats where possible.

I'll keep an eye on this thread as I'm keen to learn what else is worthwile for our level.
