I love it! This spell has been given new life since it was made a non-aggro spell, and it no longer kills your pets (unlike invisibility).
This spell just makes mages and wizzies incredibly useful to dungeon crawl groups (though they were before anyway).
The summoned eye can be sent ahead of the group to check corridors or rooms, then the mage or wizzy can target mobs one at a time, so that everyone has their appropriate mobs targetted BEFORE they are pulled.
The puller/tanks and DD can all assist the wizzy/mage to target the first mob to die, and mezzers can then assist after the other appropriate targets have been targetted.
Sometimes in a room with many mobs, it can be useful for every one to target a different mob then charge the room, everyone then hits their target once to aggro it on themselves before switching to assist the main tank if he doesn't have the hitpoints to last the time it will take to heal him with all mobs beating on him/her, then the mezzer steps in and can mez at will.
The ability to allow everyone to target seperate mobs before entering a room (if there is no rogue in group)is just incredibly useful.

The eye can also be used to target people out of group who are otherwise unable to get in line of site of anyone for an invite, once targetted, they can then be invited into the group, and cothed to a location.
Eye of Tallon has a much greater range, though this spell kills the casters pet, shame pets can't see invisible, even if they have see invis cast on them At level 57, they should be able to.....

In addition to Eye of Zomm being able to come into being behind closed and locked doors, it can also appear beyond zone lines! Are you near the invisible barrier at the edge of a zone, or a real zone line where one more step does "loading please wait"? Either way, get right up against the invisible line, cast the eye, and you can explore areas no player can reach. Easy way to see the cat room in Befallen (follow the zone out passage with the eye to the secret room). Normally the eye disappears when you run it into a zone line, or it stops like a player when running into the zone boundary, however it can be made to appear beyond the line to begin with, and hence avoids the zones!

Note that Pacify is _not_ Line of Sight, though it does have a max range. Wake of Tranquility is supposed to be LOS, but I've seen instances where it isn't. Sooooooo... is there a mob you can't see but want single pulled, and no monk is available?

Cast Bind Sight on a mob you can see. Hit F9 to change your viewpoint around the mob, pacify everyone around him that you couldn't see from where you stand. Then single pull. I use this in POI Factory, whenever we don't have a puller who can survive splitting the golems. Since there's four in the first room (two facing you, and two facing away but around a corner) , I bind sight on one I can see, then use Wake (Pacify does't work) on the two I couldn't see from the hall. Then I can approach close enough to Wake the two I could see on the far side. Send the puller in, and get only one of them!

However, in Chardok, I sometimes can't get Wake to work like that, and have to use a character on
Sarnak faction, and Pacify--which makes pulling the Prince or King solo a snap.
