Something you should think about too is what if the class that INTERESTS her isnt necessarily a good combo with a Wizard? What if she decides that a Warrior or a Monk sounds like something she would like? As a pair, those wouldnt necessarily make the best combo but she may love her character just as you love your wizard.

Keeping that in mind, it will be a bit before her level would match yours so what would you do in the mean time? A good idea would be to try another character and rise up with her. As you do so you will get to know her strengths and weaknesses in her class so that as she gets closer in age to your wizard, if the combo would still work, you would be able to know better how she works and what she can and cant do.

You must remember that while you are seeking for her to play a class that compliments YOUR wizard, it is more important that she plays a class that she enjoys. If she doesn't enjoy it, it wont be as much fun for her and you will be a lonely wizzie again:-)

I would think a better idea is to roll up *2* new charactors .. 1 for you and 1 for her.

Give her first choice of char type (ie melee/caster/pet/priest class) and then choose something that goes with it. I think her experience will be enhanced actually playing a charactor she calls her on *WITH YOU* rather than inheriting a class and waiting around for another one to be levelled up to meet it.

Example, She chooses Paladin, so you roll up a Druid/Shaman/Cleric to go with it.
