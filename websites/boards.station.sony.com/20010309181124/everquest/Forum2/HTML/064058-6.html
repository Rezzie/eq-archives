Ok, please excuse me, but I skipped a few pages of this thread as it seems to have denigrated into a name calling contest...and who has time for that nonsense.

So, to address Drakezius and his original post:

Dude, I think many of us, especially after spending a year or two in-game start wondering if the time has been well spent. EQ is brilliant about providing a doorway to escapism.

Escapism is a double-edged sword. Either you can handle real life as it happens, or you choose to avoid it and wait until you feel better equipped...as it were. If you select EQ as your method of avoidance, and you never find time to deal with RL, well, then EQ will seem addictive. (And quitting anything addictive is always difficult.)

I have seen friends become "addicted" to EQ to the point that they have very little contact with the real world anymore. I believe these people would have become "addicted" to anything that allowed them to escape the absolute horror that was their real life.

By the same token, I have plenty of friends who play EQ on the weekends. It doesn't interfere with their jobs, their dogs get walked, their house is not crawling with unidentified bacteria and the fridge doesn't attack.

Ergo, EQ, like anything else, is what you make of it. If you find yourself avoiding your real life in favor of your virtual life, then perhaps it's time to evaluate what in real life makes you unhappy or uncomfortable.

To address point 3, where you say that you are getting nowhere in game...at the risk of being too Jungian for the board...perhaps what you are really unhappy about is that you see no progress in any other aspect of your reality and you are transferring those feelings onto an easy target.... sprites on a screen.

I say none of this as a flame or as an attack. But, I believe that if you feel thusly, perhaps it’s time to explore those parts of your reality which you feel to be neglected…i.e., friends and family. In other words, you may have had an epiphany which truly speaks to your situation. If so, you owe it to yourself to follow that path.

EQ, or it’s equivalent will be here. You can always go back, you don’t always see a clear path ahead.
