Some velious mobs are undercons, but then the ulthorks and walruses at the north of EW are not, and the Wyverns in CS certainly are not. It's all a matter of finding the right mobs Wait until you get to 44 (Conflag) before trying the shardwurms again, when you can do 600+ damage per nuke they will die a lot more quickly . Also instead of root nuking try using bonds of force (like you do with sarnaks) and kiting. Don't use Cold based spells on most Velious mobs, you will get a ton of resists unless you are quite a bit higher level than the mob you are fighting.
