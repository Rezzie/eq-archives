I didn't read this whole thread to see if this was mentioned as it was too long and I'm just skimming, but..

The situation you described isn't really new, in a way. Tanks have pretty much always done more damage than casters in Plane groups(aside from the bosses where they can go full blast, heh).

What has changed is Verant recently expanded the hate list. See.. before a caster would -always- get the experience not because they did the most damage.. but nine times out of ten it is the caster to get the last hit in who got the experience.

When there's only room for eight people on the hate list and thirty people beating on it, you getting that last big nuke bumps the tanks doing the damage off the list just in time for you to get the experience. Now that there's room for around twenty people on the hate list this no longer happens, so the casters getting that last big nuke no longer get the experience.

I hope that made -some- kind of sense. ;P
