For the last few days I have been dutifully searching the world of Norrath for the components for the spell research practice - my frustration in doing this is perhaps the subject of another rant... for now I have a question regarding the nature of this "skill".

For those that dont know, this activity or "practice" allows pure caster classes the ability to increase their research skill without spending practice points (or wasting runes). I only have 20 points saved at lvl 33 and would rather max out my dodge skill than spend points on research.

I have practiced my research up to a skill of 61. According to the widely accepted formula (max skill level = (level - 15)*5)research skill is supposed to increase a maximum of 5 points each level. Can anyone tell me definitively <b>if the skill is capped for each level?</b>(like combat skills), <u>or</u> <b>are they not capped at all - but capped for training at the guild? (like trade skills)</b>.

Reading through the Mythrian tower, I became confused, they seem to say that research skill increases by 5 each level, but that there is no limit to how high one can practice.

According to the formula ((level-15)*5), the skill can only ever acheive a <b>MAXIMUM</b> of 175 @ lvl 50, <u>HOWEVER</u> it has also been stated that the maximum level of the skill is 200 - AND that this should be practiced to maximum at your guild... confused yet?

I really think <b>many MANY</b> pure casters are confused about spell research practice (and spell research in general). For those of us who have spent their practice points in other things than spell research, HOW to increase the skill should be made less cryptic.

Any thoughts guys?
