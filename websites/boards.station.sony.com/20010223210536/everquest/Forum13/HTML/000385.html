Not sure if this is the board they check for asking on balancing but if not Ill just have to write it again.
The main things monks need to be rebalanced to where they once were... DAMAGE OUTPUT... yes we still do the most dmg head to head in a fight over warriors and rogues... but in a group the rogue will be behind mob backstabbing and not only outdamages us but severely outdamages us. I would also like to point out that the dmg difference in monks to warriors/rangers is not overtly large either... yet monks have drawbacks to being able to do so much dmg where as warriors/rangers/rogues do not. We have to worry about weight ALL the time. We dont get higher ac items like they do. We dont get anywhere near the pick of wpns/armours they do. (at least not seriously for someone who says most blunt wpns are monk usuable..if its weight is over 3.0 its more weight than any monk needs.)
Sometimes the wpn and armour lists look like they are specifically targetting monks. Look at orc fang necklace and earring .. both good melee class items.. but I dont think Ive seen any other earring or necklace that weighs 1.0 besides those.
But if you are satisfied with monk damage then let us have some other abilities to make up for the weight/ac thing. Ideas I have heard include stuns (which would be very neat) Could either be made to stun with special or as a special itself or my favorite would be to have it randomly work in background like dodge and block...randomly emote you strike target in a pressure point temporarily stunning them! After all monks being master of hand2hand should know the pressure points and use them in combat (maybe actually expand that for aimed pressure points for various abilities ex. pressure point leg--snare type effect by weakening leg--pressure point temple--loses casting concentration)(might have an eye rake for blindness)
Another ability monks might have that would not be really extreme is a meditate skill similar to casters...call it trance... monk sits and goes into trance to both purify body of poisons/diseases and to heal itself. Monks are masters of their bodies so not far out of line.. can even balance it a bit with having us see a blank screen in place of casters book. so we cant see things approaching til maybe like lvl 45 or so. or maybe make mend go down in repop time with lvls.
monk only quests on small 100% wt reduction bags to carry food only in. Because food is usually one of the big things that monks carry. or some type of quest for a never ending water/food item.
something neat but I doubt able to do this late in the game would be to have various fighting styles for monks. one for damage at cost of defense. one for defense at cost of damage and speed. one for speed at small cost of dmg and defense.
another thing that would be neat but I doubt we will see is another random attack. after we hit lvl say 20 we could have a small random chance to throw in an elbow or knee.. heh
one thing that has always been seen with monks in any other game I play (and yes I know this is not any other game Im just making suggestions here) would be quivering palm type DOT. (not that i think it will make it but...)
Something I think all monks want to see is flying kick being put on par with rogue backstab. We are best toe to toe and we use flying kick ..they go behind and use backstab... but the difference is that their backstab is minimized for their lvl while our flying kick can do a minimum of 1 dmg. Put flying kick at a minimum of lvl to. This is our main damage special. I believe it warrants the same consideration as the rogue main dmg special.
I would say something about monks irl being masters of all forms of combat not just blunt wpns but that would be to overpowering heh.
Another suggestion Ive heard is that monks get built in speed bonus while walking/running... not up to sow (well maybe not til like 60) Would help with pulling.
I would also like to point out that along with a lower ac/weight than warriors (not sure where we stand ac wise in comparison to rangers/rogues) we also have much lower hp and because we dont have the same str we have lower ATK rating. Maybe think of revamping our hp/ac/atk ratings in comparison with warriors/ranger/rogues. Not sure if this is where you planned it to be but thought Id mention it.
I know this is a long note but I hope youve read the whole thing. These are just some ideas I believe would help monks out a bit. We are very nice as is, but I would like to see monks being the dmg doers we once were compared to everyone else. Or at least allow us some small side things to help stay up with the other melee so that new players will still pick monk because its a viable class.
Thanks for your time
Drakol Dreadfist of The Tribunal Server
