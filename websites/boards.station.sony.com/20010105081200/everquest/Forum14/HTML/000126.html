Check http://www.eqprices.com/ - they seem to have the most realistic prices. Other sites seem to have highly inflated prices, and it's usually very difficult to get those prices, unless you find someone who is desperate. =)

Anyhow, in case you're too lazy to check on that page, it's 8.5k - they usually go for about 6k on Solusek Ro.

If money is important to you, then you are far better off playing a druid and leveling him. Granted, your druid will need to be at least 30-40 to start camping rares, but at least he can. Your paly will never make much of a money-maker.

Personally, my encouragement is to play for fun, and to not worry about getting money. (well, unless money == fun for you) It's been my experience that if I'm drooling over an item, all I have to do is wait a few levels, and I can get it myself, vs. having to spend thousands.

Good luck,
