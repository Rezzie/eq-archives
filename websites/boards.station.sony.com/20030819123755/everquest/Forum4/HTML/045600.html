First and foremost, get a mount. Without a mount, you'll get jumped every time you sit.

Yes, PoP groups need chanters to slow stuff. Problem is, at lvl 46 you'll have trouble landing your shiftless on mobs. You always will have to Tash first. If your tank is a good aggro holder, especially pally, you can tash right off the bat. But the combo of low level and high resist rate will often make you a liability. As a slower, you are useless to a group, if your first slow gets resisted, and next thing you see i 'loading, please wait'.

Get at least to 49. Berserker gives you a barrier that can help you survive a wee bit longer if you draw aggro. Dazzle is an awesome 96 second mez, which you'll come to worship.

Learn to use and love your Pacify line. Next time you in KC, work with the puller. Cast Bind Sight on your puller, and Pacify a mob to break aggro chain - so you ger at most 2-3 mobs, instead of 4-7.

Also, buffs. Yeah, there's KEI, but most folks will want a C2-capable chanter. That means lvl 52 (was 54). Also, haste: Celerity just don't cut it in PoP. And lvl 49 SLtW takes forever to cast, eats tons of mana, and lasts not very long. Once you get AQ at 53, you are gonna love it - your group gonna love it too.

That said, I personally was able to function quite well in PoJ Death Row at 51. Lots of deaths, but exp was insane.
