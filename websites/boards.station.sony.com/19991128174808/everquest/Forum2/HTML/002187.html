Hello,

You can contact your ISP and have them run a trace route to chat.everquest.com and see if they find any problems with any routers. Make sure that they run both a TCP/IP and UDP trace. Also you might want to get into Device Manager and get into 'Port(COM & LPT)'-->Communication Port(COM1)-->Port Settings-->Bits per second...it should be at 9600, try putting it to a higher setting. Also for Flow control Xon/Xoff change that to Hardware and click ok. See if that might be able to help you out any as well. Thank you...
