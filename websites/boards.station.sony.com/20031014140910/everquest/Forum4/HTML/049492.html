I've been trying to level my Paladin in Butcherblock over the past few days, and I've run into quite a problem from the LDoN adventurers. They seem to get bored quite easily while the are ooc'ing and shouting for groups, and take off and kill whatever they want. I don't see anything wrong with killing the passing by Scarab or Greenblood, but let's not forget that this IS a newbie zone and people do still level their young characters there.

With the experiences I had this week, I can only consider one to be a truly rude one. I was camping the Towering Brutes, and while I was desposing of one another popped and some lvl 55 comes running over and kills it. I had a few choice words for him, to which he actually seemed somewhat sorry for his actions. However, most of the time it is just upper lvls wandering around and killing at random.

Personally, I think that the location of the Wayfarer camps are poorly placed in this matter. It would have been better in many ways to just have one centralized locations for ALL the starting points. There could have just been portals similiar to PoK stones to take the group to the proper zone for the adventure. I believe this also might have made finding groups easier.

Anyway, I'm sorry this post is so long, but I would just like to ask that next time you are looking for and adventure, try to stay your sword and leave the mobs for the younger levels.
