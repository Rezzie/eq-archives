Let me point out what many of you seem to have missed.

Pezz is saying that at level 30 a monk will not outdamage a warrior with good equipment. He is not saying that monks are worse damage dealers than warriors at level 40 or 50. Just that prior to getting speed increases and big damage increases they are not as good at dealing damage (though close) and are definitly not as good tanks.

As for the vaunted 14/22 weapons.... that is effectively 30 damage per second total with your fists.

Warriors with SSoY's are doing 21/second plus procs at that level. You have them beat. But if they have a Spined Dragon Claw and an SSoY they pop up to 25 damage and a proc. If they also have bloodfire they are up to 27 and a proc. Yes you have them beat, but not by enough to make up for the extra HP they have.

I agree that monks do more damage at high levels, but upto level 30 warriors still have the advantage with good equipment. They do fall off rapidly from there. However as a level 47 cleric I have a hard time using complete heals on monks effectively. Basically I would rather CH a warrior with 3000 HP than a monk with 1800. Overall as a tank goes the warrior wins by a long shot. Prior to level 40 of course the monk is better because he gets hit less and has less HP so I can use superior, or even greater heal on him and restore a significant number of HP.

At low levels it doesn't matter which you have, you can make it work. At high levels every job needs a specific class. Monks and warriors are equally valuable, so why worry who does more damage?
