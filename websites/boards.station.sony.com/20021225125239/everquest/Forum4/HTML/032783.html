OK, repeat after me . . .

"I'm a cleric ... no one cares about how good my equipment is for a long time yet. I'll be in demand everywhere I go. EQ life is good. I'll save my money right now and roll a twink with it later."

Seriously, you are way too early in the equipment purchasing department. Plus mana items are nice, but you'd be better off giving 5 or 10pp to someone casting KEI until you are in your mid-20s at least. You will have equipment thrust upon you from time to time as well. Even if you refuse it, your abilities won't be too greatly affected. The big mana pools become more important when you hit 39 and have that huge heal. Then you'll want the +WIS and +Mana items.

Now at 52, I am just beginning to acquire some noteworthy items for a cleric. Prior to this, you'd be amazed at how bad my stuff was.

/sigh. OK, you want some gear.

Adamantite Epaulets (9 AC)are about 300pp and WIS +7.

Chitinous Belt is about 700pp (nice 13AC with WIS +5 and stats.

Acrylia Studded Cloak (WIS +10) now runs about 650pp - 750pp.

So, for about 1k, you can up Wisdom by 17 pts and gain two nice items that will last a very long time. (epaulets and cloak)

If you want to splurge, a pair of Shissar Scaled Gauntlets at 1k will probably last you into your 50s.

Buying wisdom items is a waste at lvl 15, focus more on mana / hp items.
(these are prices from the Nameless, but would assume with bazaar, most servers are similar)

Jaded velium rings in bazaar 5ac 30 hp 30 mana usually 300pp ish.

Black sapphire electrum earrings 2ac 35hp 25 mana for around 300pp as well. (will spend the same on forest loops and get less from them in mana til low 50).

The Rod of Oblations is 6/27 1HB, usually 500pp and consists of resists and 60 raw mana.

Cone of the mystics is about 250pp and is 5 wis 5 int +50 raw mana.

Enshrouded veil 7ac 2 wis 2 int 30 hp 30 mana +10 svf for your face, around 500-600pp.

Quest the Testament of Vaneer, 10 wis 10 mana secondary slot, easy quest here... http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=144 skip all in Erudin and begin it in qeynos.

Various sebilite or ry`gorr chain items can be obtained for under 300pp and can fill many slots.

I think your best bet is to go to the bazaar and search. As a cleric stats are AC/HP/Mana/wis then all others. Lower levels focus on mana, mid levels on hps and high levels on AC, wis is always useful, but more noticable to your mana pool post lvl 40.

Oh, last side note, get in and take your beatings now, get those defense and dodge skills maxxed early on, it better then letting level 60 mobs beat on you for skill ups.
