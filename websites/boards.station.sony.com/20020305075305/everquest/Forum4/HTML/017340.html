Your mileage may vary, but I usually find there are enough people in Nagafen's lair (Sol B) for a pickup group. It's a blast at level 40 if you can get a full group together for the King/Priest/Champion rooms. It gets dull very fast once you get too many people around though.

Most 'fun' places though, are the BYOG type. As a wizard you at least have the option of porting people, but most spots are pretty far from teleport sites. One place that is reasonably close to a wiz port, and _can_ be quite fun is Mistmoore. Again it depends on how many (and what level) of people are around. If you are the only group of level 35+ there, Mistmoore is great.
