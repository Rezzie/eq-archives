I 2-box with one PC - 512MB RAM, geforce3 64 Meg video, and 1.4Ghz processor. I have little to no trouble most of the time.

You don't have to use EQW any more; you can put EQ into Windowed mode. However, I found that I got considerably less lag with EQW. Try it both ways and see what works for you.

Lag can be reduced by turning off the Luclin models and adjusting your clipping plane. I have most Luclin models disabled, and clipping set to 35%.

The biggest problem is remembering which character you're on when you're screen switching. I switch a lot, and make sure they're both doing what they should be doing.

Have fun,
