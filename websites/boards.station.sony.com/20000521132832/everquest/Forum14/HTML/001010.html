My order of training:

1HS: FS scimitar until I save enough plat for a combine scimitar. Plain and simple, the scimitar is fast. My 1HS is maxed at level 15.

1HB: Dwarven Mace from the first part of the Screaming Mace quest. After 1HS maxes out, I switch to this. My 1HB is about 10 points from being maxed.

2HB: Just got the Wee Harvester. I ran way too much for this, but nice weapon. Between this and the Bloodforge Hammer, my 2HB skill is lagging behind (low 40's) but it will eventually catch up.

Train them all, but choose one as your primary weapon. It always helps to have a weapon you can count on for doing some damage.
