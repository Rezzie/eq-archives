a_Strakar_00

I don't know about you guys, but I wouldn't lose any sleep over these items. With the possible exception of the mantle, they are either comparable or weaker to several items we can get outside of the plane.

They've been changed once, and they still suck. Maybe in the wake of the spear controversy, we can get the boys at verant to do some more work on these. Most of the other classes have respectable quest items after the last fix, but the shamans are still lacking in good PoA rewards.

Consider:

Ring-

- 2 charges of feign death may save your life twice, which means little because there is rarely a time I can't get a rez
- +3 dex does nothing
- +20 sv disease has no value, even in pk because no one uses disease spells

Let's look at comparable rings:

- plat jasper +6 wis that I can buy for 120 plat from my guild jeweler--the sky ring is basically the same thing with a couple junk stats, very weak
- golem tear 2ac 2str 6wis 10mana--I think this is the standard, and the crimson ring is inferior while being tougher to get

What the stats should be: 7wis 7sta or agi 15sv fire, magic, or cold, 2 charges feign death--this seems fair, swapping out worthless stats for decent ones and giving more wis than a damn jasper ring.

Mantle (probably the best item, still could be better)-

- +9 AC is decent for the slot
- +5 STR is pointless but a nice touch, more useful than dex or int
- +6 WIS is good, hard to find decent WIS in the shoulder slot
- I like the resists being on a pk server, otherwise not as valuable to others

Other items:

- Prayer shawl 5ac 8wis--only hard to get because of the rarity. Even on TZ we could kill Estrella as fast as she can spawn, so difficulty is no comparison. Unparalleled wisdom with less AC and no resists.

- Imbued granite spaulders 20AC 10 to all saves--harder to get than the prayer shawl, pretty rare as well. No wisdom, but incredible AC and more resists.

- Obulus death shroud 10AC 3str 5sta 3wis 25mana +1 to all saves--pretty much the same as the sphinx hide mantle, although the potential is there to get this quicker with a lot less manpower

What the stats should be: drop the charges of guardian spirit, which is absolutely moronic. Replace it with some AC and maybe some HP or mana for good measure.
11ac 5str 6wis 10mana 10sv fire cold magic

Amulet (I would use this with more AC)-

- 2 AC is pitiful
- 2 CHA, well urban legend has it that charisma has some value besides lowering prices
- 6 wis 10 mana, top of the line mana stats for the neck slot
- 10 sv fire 5sv magic, probably the two most useful resists

Other items:

- Chrysoberyl talisman 5ac 3wis 20mana--very easy to get, a slap in the face that a tough plane of sky quest is comparable to this.

- Ivory imbued collar 8ac 5wis 10mana 2sv cold fire magic 10sv poison disease--better AC, comparable saves and mana stats, and not as rare as you think. Easily obtainable with any length of time in chardok

- Bone amulet of blade turning 2ac 6wis 10mana 5 charges of guard-- basically the same thing, rare drop in the packed confines of karnor castle.

- Necklace of superiority 5ac 10hp/mana 5 to all stats/saves--Better than everything. I don't know how rare it is, but considering it's from a mid level dungeon, probably very rare.

What stats should be: The item is fair right now, and the components are from the first three islands (although I haven't even seen the first two yet). The only thing I see is an AC boost. 6-8 AC, other stats remaining the same.

Bracelet (just horrible)-

Let's just skip the stats, they suck. I can hear the laughing at verant HQ right now because of the two charges of alacrity.

Other items:

- JB bracer 14 AC and winters roar--the ultimate item

- RE bracer 9ac 5wis/int/cha--another plane item, much better

- RM bracer 4ac 3str 5wis--the fact that we're even comparing this speaks volumes of the crappy ceremonial bracelet.

- Chipped bone bracer 4ac 5wis--see above.

- Supple scale armband 3ac 5wis/int 25mana 10sv fire--common drop off the puppy warlord, pretty easy to get.

- Jarsath wristband 8ac 4wis/cha--I can't tell you how often these rot. The PoA bracer makes this look good.

What the stats should be: It really isn't as bad as I made it sound, but the first impression has got to be, "What's with all these lame add-in stats and 2 charges of alacrity?" Drop the alacrity since that's just a slap in the face, and raise the AC to around 10. That would be good enough.

I'd like to know if everyone agrees with me or if I'm just embarking on a one-man crusade here. PoA is tough, and I'd just like the rewards to justify the effort for shamans on these raids. The way it stands now, I would never go back to that place.

Strak
