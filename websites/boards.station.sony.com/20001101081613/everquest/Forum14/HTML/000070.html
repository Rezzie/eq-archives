As a 33 druid, my melee skill in 1hb, the only skill I've ever used, is only 135, far short of the 170 cap for my level. You will find once you get to 14 that you melee much less. Grouped you will snare, dot, heal, nuke some, and med a lot. Soloing kiting ends up being the main strategy, so melee skills lag far behind. It doesn't hurt to try to keep the melee skills up as long as you can. Nothing like being oom at the end of a long kite and not being able to finish off a mob that is snared and not moving--whiff, whiff, whiff. Ack! Here comes a roaming Hill Giant!

To answer your question more directly, druids get weapons at high levels that add wisdom bonuses, so there is no need to train hand to hand, leave that for monks. Personally I like 1h weapons so that you can have a stat booster in that second hand.
