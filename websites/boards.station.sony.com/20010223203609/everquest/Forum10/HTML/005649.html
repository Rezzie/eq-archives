I think anyone from Feydwer (and maybe even Freeport) is getting ripped off when they sell their bone chips to a necro. Look, you can turn them into the Dwarf Paladin guild for exp, faction, and a piece of small patchwork leather. What are you going to spend that 3pp on? Probably armor and weapons!

5th - 7th level is a pain, IMHO. Food is amazingly expensive at that time, and all the places to hunt are overcamped. It really bugs me to see Dwarves and Elves run around without armor. They sell their bone chips to a necro for a couple of lousy plat and gain what? A leather cap?

Here's my advice:

1) Don't sell the chips unless you are offered some outrageous ammout of money, like 5pp

2) Spend enough time at your level to get your skills up.

3) Turn in bone chips to the Dwarves until you level up.

4) Repeat steps 1-3 until you reach 8th. You will have a set of crummy armor that you can now think about replacing with leather and you will have good faction with the Dwarves.

And as far as necros and shadowknights go ... Kurn's Tower baby. If you're 20th+ level you can go to the upper levels and just rack up the bone chips. Spend two hours there and get a backpack full of them.

Just my two cents...
