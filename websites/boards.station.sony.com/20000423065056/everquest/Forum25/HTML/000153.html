Make a true exp. bonus for grouping, it's tougher to get 6 people to co-operate on things a lot of time than to go solo something else plus you don't have to split anything solo.

Make the trade skills compliment each other, right now there are two skills that are worthwhile to trade with other people for. I only fletch for my own supply since there isn't a demand for arrows, there is a demand for armor(at low/mid levels) and enchanted jewelry though across the range.
