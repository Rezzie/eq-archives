Ill Omen is a great place to go. I went from 20 - not quite 29 in just over a week there, grouped with a warrior (I play a druid). There are always groups looking for joiners. You can make a lot of friends there too!

Loot-wise, I would say it's ok. Goblin warlords sometimes drop 3pp and change, which is not bad money as far as I'm concerned.

Pulling from the hills to the lakeshore is a pretty safe bet, or by the wall near the sunken pillars if you're younger (levels 17 - 22 or so). Oh, and there is a little dwarven merchant in the windmill to whom you can probably sell your loot if you get loaded down and don't want to run back to Firiona. I'm not sure what faction he's on though.

In any case there are almost always plenty of monsters for everyone even when the zone is quite crowded.

Station Member 		posted 02-26-2001 05:54 PM           
The problem with the loot in LOIO is that most the mobs drop coin which can vary a bit. Its not bad loot but its nothing to write home about. It sure feels bad to someone who's as underequiped as I am

However that changes in the Sarnak Fortress, there you get the cash loot and occasional items as well. Frontier Mountains kicks in right as LOIO tapers off. The XPs not so hot but the loot is generally good, 50pp in one sessions my best EQ haul to date.

Dave, you'd get a lot more soloing than I would grouping. Most of my times spent FFA, split on and roll for specials. I loot rarely (by choice I admit) and 2pp won't get split, it'll just go straight to the looter so I get zippo. I prefer mobs with saleable loot to get the cash balance up.

There's no doubt about the xp though. Its taken me two months to get to 30th with Kaeper. Considering I can't play often thats lightning quick. The only bad part about LOIO is getting through Firiona Vie & the drachnids. Try to get a sow or invis for that & be sure to get the maps so you don't waste anytime getting there.

I carry a full sack at least of food & drink (three stacks of each) so I have little need to return to FV as possible. Copper gets universaly destroyed

For hunting spots; the Windmill is a great place to hunt from to begin with. The lake shores are good also pulling fom the hills. Its best not to be in the area of the shore by the ruins unless you have a good group as the adds can get crazy. The next stop on from the windmills is often the undead house pulling from the house & from the wandering mobs. I found it could get a little hairy there at times. There's a nice spot midway between the windmill & undead house where you can pull from the house & surrounding wandering mobs but get less adds.

Another place is the Goblin mines, best for mid twenties IMO but can go up to 30. We usually pulled to the outside rather than entering. Mobs are pretty constant & one of my two best XP gain rate spots.

Then there's the Sarnak fortress, in the tunnels & under the sky. There are a number of good pull spots at the fortress. I'd say the safest to busiest would be; outside the gates on the hill to the right or outside the backdoor, just inside the gates to the left or right and on the left or right platforms from the tunnels.

When fighting inside the tunnels in the sarnak fort there's a good spot for ease of pulling and relative lack of adds. As you go in the backdoor you go up a ramp that leads to a long corridor. There's a room to the left and more tunnels & a room to the right. The room to the right is add crazy but can be good xp if you can handle it, healers dies first here. Directly behind that room is a small area, not a room just a widened corridor really. Thats my favourite spot for inside the fort. I'd say inside the fort is best for levels 25-30.

Both the mines & the fort have a high level mob squirrelled away in them. Its a good idea to get the maps of these tunnels just so you can avoid them. The Chancellor in the fort sees through invisability & hide/sneak so corpse recovery is near impossible if someones stumbled into his room by mistake.

Thats just from my own experiences & not definitive, but an idea at least of what the commons spots are.


Kaeper Shadowborn
