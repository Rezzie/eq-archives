I noticed I needed to adjust my healing tactics slightly, and I was more on my toes, but no major change really.
Next, we went to Kedge to kill Phinigel Atropos for an epic item. Got hit one or two times by a guardian seahorse and by Phinny himself. Again, they were able to be pulled off me. I haven't even used DA or DB as of this point.
Then we went to the disco room in Sebilis. A monk, ranger, druid, enchanter, me and a magician. More summoning mobs. Also noticed the increased aggro, but only had to use DA one time.
I only died once the entire night because our monk (and he admitted this) screwed up the pathing in the pull room and brought 12 frogs to us.
That was it. Three dungeons, numerous post-51 mobs, a ton of complete heals, one death.

But you didn't kill Trak! Or VS! Or Gorenaire!
I can hear the whiners now. It's always something.
But seriously, with a good group (and these were all guildmates - people I've grouped with for 20 levels or more) the aggro wasn't that bad for me.

On the occasions when I was aggroed, it was nice to have that druid with his new, improved healing backing me up. We made a good healing team.
