If Absor is still reading this far down...

My point of view is as a melee (56 warrior) on Rallos.

From the melee point of view the problems in EQ PvP are LOS and lag exploits. With those fixed I wouldn't have a problem removing the DD damage nerf (especially if coupled to a 51% to loot rule). Unresistable DoTs are a major problem with egg shaped pumice gone from the game. The cast time is too long for classes with 0 channeling.

LOS exploits- you don't need LOS to cast on players and players can't home in on you like mobs can. Nuff said.

Lag exploits- others have mentioned this, it's especially bad in regards to the z-axis, but basically it is nigh impossible to hit someone running directly away from you and very easy to lose someone by exploiting lag. As far as melee combat is concerned a few potential ways to address the problem would be to increase the range you can melee from and/or loosen the LOS restrictions for when you can't see your target. Alternately (or in tandem) you could make melee attaacks work similarly to the way archery does now, in that if you attack a target that you can't hit you don't loose the attack for the cycle. So on the server side you do all the checking for range and LOS, then you check if the delay requirement of the weapon has passed, and if that all checks out then the attack occurs. So basically you don't have to time your auto-attack and you wouldn't waste sequences trying to guess when you are or aren't in range. That's do a lot for melee vs caster fights.

My issues are related strictly to Team PvP servers (My main is on Vallon-Zek):

1. Cross-teaming, faction and healing problems. Faction on the team PvP server starts on the Good vs. Evil standpoint, and immediately throws off the idea of pure team play. Team faction should be set up from the start just as it is for the dark team, *between* the light teams, i.e. as an elf I should not be able to walk into Kaladim; a dwarf should not be able to walk freely into Felwithe; a human Ak'Anon etc. This may be a problem from the questing standpoint - but how does the dark team get around the problem now, and would that be any different for the light teams? People would be less likely to cuss you out because you killed thier dwarf friend (as both of you are elfs) and the conflicts would be resovable via PvP instead of /petition in most cases.

Related to this is the current ability to cross-team heal. This has been used by players who come to the server and want to play Good vs. Evil, against the basic premise of the 4-team server. Some have taken it to grief levels - I have seen a level 8 gnome being healed by a level 55 wood elf druid cutting down young elfs in the newbie areas at Kelethin and Felwithe. All of the people(elfs) I could gather that were in the 8 level range of the gnome were not able to kill him - and we were not able to attack the wood-elf traitor because of the team structure. The gnome was essentially immortal, and frustrated many low level players who experienced this for the first time. Elimenate this loop-hole to make the team servers function as intended.

2. The GMs need to have a more consistent view of the rules of the Team PvP server. Issues that are currently handled on an informal basis such as 'loot and scoot' (LnS), 'bind rushing', corpse run etiquette, proper uses of /ooc and /shout etc. need to be solidified and published for the team server populations, and *firmly* acted upon when violated. This will elimenate much of the argumentation and /petition'ing that goes on now. This will make the already swamped GMs job managable, and the level of customer service better from the customer's standpoint.

3. There are many PvPers who would prefer to play a Good vs. Evil game. For these players I would recommend another server (or conversion of one of the team servers) to a 2 team server. This server would be coin loot. So that we end up with 1 pure PvP server with 1 item loot(Rallos-Zek), a 4 team PvP server, and a 2 team PvP server. I would allow /move between the 4 team and the 2 team servers if one of our current 4 team servers is converted. No /move allowed if the new 2 team server is not a conversion from a 4 team server.

I feel that if these items were addressed, PvP on team servers would be a much more enjoyable experience.
