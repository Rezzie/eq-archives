I am a level 30 Enchanter and have been adventuring with a Paladin since we first started. He is also level 30. Recently we discussed the fact that we will need to join a guild eventually to go on the big raids for the sole purpose of taking out mobs that no two characters could ever handle. My question is how do you find or pick a decent guild?

I have been searching the net a bit, but there isn't much info out there about active guilds. Does anyone have a suggestion about where to do some research on finding a decent guild? I would think these message boards would be the place, but there isn't much here either.

Lhoramel
Station Member 		posted 01-19-2001 07:20 AM           
My suggestion: Look at the player you are grouped with. For about a week, loot at the players that are around you and that you like. How do they seem to you? Would you like to participate in raids with them? Write down the names of the Guilds they are members of. Then ask to speak to an officer of those Guilds and ask if they have a website. If they do, visit it and read what people have to say about their Guilds. Otherwise just talk to the officers and determine what they like and what they do together. Then you can join one. Be ready to be disapointed. I remember joining a Guild once because the people I was grouped with were very nice. I spent a few evenings listening to the Guild channel and I was appalled by the language used on there and the insults that were flying (stuff that could get someone banned). Some people like that stuff, I don't and I left. Since then, my friends from that Guild have moved into mine. I hope this is useful.

Fanelaine Celebehl
