>>>So, I have to *SIT* and wait 7 hours for the spawn

You aren't required to "sit and wait" for the NPC. You are only required to be there when the NPC spawns. That can happen at any time, and you could choose to leave and come back later to try and complete the quest. Now, if the item is such that you *choose* to sit and wait, you've made the decision that the item is "worth it", which is your decision to make.

>>>From what I understand, most quests include a *long* camp to get an item. If you want to control how often the quest is done, just give it more requirements (20+ no drop item list).

More requirements do not limit the amount of items that come into the world. The proliferation of items in the world is tied only to the amount of time it takes to get the rarest item. Even if the quest had 20 one-hour steps, taking you at least 20 hours to complete it, the rate at which items from the quest can come into game is one/hour. The more items that come into the game, the less valuable the items, and the less prestige garnered from acquiring it.

-Gordon
