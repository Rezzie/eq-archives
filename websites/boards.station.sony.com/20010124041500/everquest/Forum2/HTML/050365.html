Author Topic:   Rare items lost unfairly to bug, Absor please read and take pity
Neaon
Station Member 		posted 01-20-2001 06:19 PM           
Hail This Saturday evening on the Ayonae Ro server, my guild, and two of our allies were raiding the Plane of Fear when we encountered a rare and unexcpected bug that resulted in the loss of two very rare items that the GM that i spoke with indicated he would not be able to return. Allow me to elaborate.
Early Saturday morning we awoke and begain our raid. We fought for hours, 10 hours to be exact, hoping for some good equipment to reward our guildmembers. There was another guild in the Plane however, long after we broke in, and they selfishly stole most of the good monsters. Regardless, after they had left we continued to fight on in the hopes of gaining one or two more good items to reward those that fought alongside us with their only reward loss of experience.
What happened next proved to be very unusual and frustraded every member of my guild that atended the raid. We had a normal pull, which consisted of two Amygdalian Knights. Our enchanter pulled one of the amys to the side, and proceded to mezmerize him. The rest of us ganged up on the first kinght and tore him a new one. After we finished with him we turned our attention to the second knight, and prepared to attack. What happened next was very unusual. After we broke his mez, he made one quick attack and then dissapeared into thin air. We all knew he was not dead, because we still had the beast selected. However he did not appear anywhere nearby. We didnt know what to do so we continued to fight and after awhile forgot about the incident.
Later, we decided to move up to the castle to continue to pull the mobs conatined therin, but when everyone sat down we were reminded of the knight. Someone said out of nowhere " That amy that dissapeared earlier just dropped Snare! " Then as if on cue, he came running out from behind the wall of fire, with two of his friends in tow. This was not a terrible blow for us, but we were low on mana and we went to work, mezzing two and fighting the Tentaceled Terror. The TT didnt prove to be too difficult, but to our amazement he dropped two rare items - A ranger Helm, and a Dreamworvine Garland! We were estatic, but we had to turn our attention back to the mobs currently in tow before we worried about looting. To our horror, more mobs began pouring out of the castle, quickly overruning our dwindling forces, forcing us to die or camp.
The items were lost, and all because of a silly bug that allowed an apparently subdued monster to escape. I am officially requesting that these items be returned to their rightful owners, those that fought long and hard for them only to see the slim fruits of their labors snatched from them by a cold and buggy pathing system. Please dont allow my love of Everquest to be destroyed by this silly bug, it seems so terribly unfair to work for so many weeks to get to the point where you can earn these kinds of items just to have them taken from you, and then to top it off have an unsympathetic and uncaring GM shoot down your last hope of having the items returned.
Anyways thank you for reading, ive also sent a copy of this message to eqtesting@station.sony.com in the hopes of having someone at VI do the right thing and return these unfairly lost items.

All times are PT (US)
