I think the enchanter stacking issue is even more complex.

One of the largest problems is not only that a higher level spell cannot overwrite a lower level one, but a lower level caster cannot cast the same or higher level spell to overwrite as well.

When enchanters work in a team, inevitably they are of varying levels. Most of us would love to be able to back up the other enchanters, especially if they are dead, by "refreshing" an existing mez that they cast. But, if that enchanter was higher level, even if the mez is the same spell or lower, it cannot be overwritten.

It would make sense to me to simply allow any spell of the same casting level or greater to overwrite and existing one, when it comes to the mesmerize line, and ignore the level of the character that cast it.
