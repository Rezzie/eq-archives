I hope you have played EQ enough to realize that no one chooses a race and then chooses a class after that. Class is THE defining characteristic of an EQ character. No one with any degree of experience with the game decides, "Hey, I want to play an Elf! Gosh I feel bad that they can't be a shaman though and I really wanted to play a shaman!".
