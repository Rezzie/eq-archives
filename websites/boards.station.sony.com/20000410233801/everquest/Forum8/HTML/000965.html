I checked out the web site and it is helpful, but the information is patchy at the least (what msg board isn't?).
>;-)

Here's what I was able to distill:
Cakes seem to be the only thing after pies that gives skill experience.

There is only one cake recipe that works and that is birthday cake. Any recipe for wedding cake is a fake.

I was not able to learn when cakes become trival.

Winter chocolate (which is required for cake) is trivial by the time you're trivial with pies (somewhere between 89 and 142 - no exact number).

Attacking brownies at any level is asking for a serious monkey-stomping without a good high-lvl party (lvl 30's find it hard to solo them, so I plan on hanging out in LFay and begging for these brownie body parts).

There are less-dangerous brownies in GFay, but you'll need a tracker to find them as they wander and don't spawn in one spot (don't know how MUCH easier they are).

If anyone has additional info they can provide, please post it here. I will add stuff every week or so, so this thread doesn't become lost like all the previous threads, related to this topic, have.

Something else of note:

Having a hard time getting hold of pie tins and cake rounds? Want a smoker?

Rather than find a potter or blacksmith to make them for you, just become a potter yourself. It took me very little (under 10 pp ) to become a master potter.

Couple months ago, I had spent two days trying to acquire my baker wares, and only recently picked up pottery. I spent less time (and probably the same amount of PP as buying pie tins, etc.) making master potter than I did trying to find a potter to make me my wares, AND I made extra baker wares along the way.
