Anyway, as far as the healing hate stuff on Test goes, here's what's going in:

1. The healing-hate cap is being reduced. (less hate max per spell)
2. The calculation of hate is moving a ratio of less than 1:1. In other words, currently if you heal 1000 (assuming the target is over 51), you gain 1000 hate. After the change you'll gain less than 1000 hate.
3. We're fixing a problem with the temporary hate from sitting. Sitting was causing an extraordinary amount of hate making it nigh on impossible to med when in combat.
4. Heal spells assign hate on what we call an "assisted" basis. In other words, any NPC that sees you cast a beneficial spell on their enemy (someone on their hate list) will add hate since they saw you doing that. We're fixing it so that critters that are mezed or stunned only add enough hate to prevent exploits of mez and stun, rather than the full load they do now when they see you cast a beneficial spell on their enemy.
5. Along the same lines, we're fixing that problem whereby hate lists would propagate across zones. Everyone has been sitting in the dread lands between combat when something ran from half way across the zone to slap your cleric, right? Basically the same thing.
