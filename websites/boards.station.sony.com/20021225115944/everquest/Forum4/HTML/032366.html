Just to clear up some stuff here.

When your sta gets low (the yellow bar):

< 100 sta feel the effects. ie slower hits, reduce agi (or dex cant remember).

> 100 sta once your bar is completely gone you slow down a bit. thats all.

Invigor restores the Sta bar. Useful for both < and >.

All times are PT (US)
