Good LORD the ignorance is thick in this thread. Let me set you vicious, revenge crazed clerics straight.

First, the Jaundiced Bone Bracer casts a 263 point COLD resist based nuke every 8 seconds.

During this 8 seconds a Shaman:
* Can not meditate.
* Can not cast heal, refresh buffs, or slow mobs.
* Can not move.
* Can not be attacked or they will be interrupted.

You want "free" damage? You can do NOTHING else during this time. The damage may be manaless but it is not "free", you have the above disadvantages. Your enchanter gets aggroed on while you are happily blasting away? He dies, you were locked into a cast.

A lot of you are falling into the trap thinking a Shaman will outdo a Wizard in maximum damage over time against 1 million HP mobs. You DO realize you are talking about Velious uber mobs? Mobs that if not highly resistant to cold are immune to it? These are not LURE nukes. They are HEAVILY resisted in Velious, especially against the uber encounters. The Wizard will still land more nukes then the Shaman. Besides any shaman bracer nuking during this fight is an idiot, they need to be assisting the cleric and keeping party members alive.

Let me also point out the many other items which cast nukes and do damage for free. It makes me wonder why you whiners keep FIXATING on Shaman. Necro bracer that nukes for ~500, Wiz robe that nukes for ~700s, Mage staff that nukes for ~300s.

As for the actual performance of the bracer VS. mana based Shaman DOTs, I can truthfully say by practicing efficient shaman meditation and cannibilization techniques, I can do JUST as much damage with this bracer for almost no mana using my spells. What this means is because Shaman ALREADY have the ability to do this, this bracer is not overpowered because it does not allow them to do anything they can't already do. All it does is allow a Shaman to eliminate tedious, repetitive, and unnecessary clicking. Allow me to illustrate:

A tick is 6 seconds. There are 10 ticks per minute. A tick is one animation of your class symbol on the left while you are in Inventory mode.

Using this bracer you can cast up to 7x (60 sec divided by 8 sec casting time=7.5 casts) per minute for 1841 dmg MAX (7*263). Keep in mind EVERY time this spell is cast there is a resist check. It can resist full OR partial. Just because under the BEST circumstances (No resists, no interruptions) you "might" get 1841 dmg out of the bracer does not mean you WILL. More then likely, and your mileage may vary, you will get 1200-1600 damage per minute out of this due to resists and interrupts.

In LESS than that same minute, I can drop Bane of Nife on a mob and do 1648 dmg (150 DD+214 dmg/tick for 7 ticks) for the cost of 425 mana.

Mana returned per meditation tick=(Meditation skill/25). Thus a player with maxxed meditation could expect to med back approx 25 mana per 6 seconds. A Shaman can simultaneously sit then stand and cannibilize then sit again, adding another 38 mana per tick at a cost of 75 health per cannibilization.

So during the 42 seconds BON is doing its work a Shaman will recover ((25+38) * 7) or 441 mana at a cost of 525 hitponts. I have Regrowth and a Fungal tunic on at all times so during this time I also regenerate ((15+15) * 7) or 210 health back.

To recap, for a cost of 210 health a Shaman can do near the full damage a JBB does in only 42 seconds.
