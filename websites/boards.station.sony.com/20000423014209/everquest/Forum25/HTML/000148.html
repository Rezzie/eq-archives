Hi Zapo - you may remember me. Give my regards to Blew.

A gathering of guilds is a nice idea, but if you want to affect Norrath in a big way then you will need the support of the big guilds (Vikings, Legion of Avatar, Clan Stormhammer etc). That said, I see a lot of people leaving big guilds lately cos things can get too big, too impersonal and too FORMAL. This is a game so I would advise you not too organise things too much - keep it fun and you will hold people's interest.

I'd suggest a few small events between the guilds like bazaars, duelling competitions and joint dungeon raids. After a while you could move on to guildwars and dragon raids I guess .

My guild is a band of brothers, not a mighty federation, but I know I can trust them all with my life and my possessions, which matters more to me than fame or might. We may be too small to take a major part in your grand schemes but you have my support nonetheless, and my help should you need it.
