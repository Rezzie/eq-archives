Cleric are the masters of HP buffs, and this symbol line is why. It does cost a gem to cast, but I think the benefit outweighs the cost.

Soloing in unrest, I would cast Symbol of ryltan which requires a bloodstone(5gp 2 sp). This is easily compensated for with 1 loot. If I happen to loot a FS weapon then I'm in great shape. 1 FS weapon pays for about 6-11 bloodstones, depending on what type of weapon it is.

I have been grouping with guildmembers lately, so I don;t mind using my "best" symbol buff if we are going to a dungeon(Paw, Unrest, MM).

I use the lesser symbol for areas that are not that dangerous. I usually also trade a Bravery and lesser Symbol buff for a SOW, or Bravery and "best" symbol buff for Clarity.(lesser being my worst, and best being my current one)

I always carry at least 20 Cats Eye agate and 20 Bloodstones with me.

You don't have to use this lne, but I know that it has saved many people. It give you more time to get off that Critical gheal.
