quote:
can a PKr loot my newbie armor? I am on Rallos Zek with my first charactor. Started there by mistake.

Depends..

Is your newbie tunic 'No Drop'? If not, then anyone who pleases will swipe it.

quote:
A guy "buffed" me into a wolfe. It was great fun until I zoned. Why did he want to do this? Can I fight while buffed?

'Share Wolf Form'... it has a SoW-grade speed buff, and makes you look like a wolf. It also raises your ATK rating, which means you'll do better in combat.

quote:
As soon as I hit lvl 10, I haave been attacked by nearly everyone, very annoying. Is there a way they can tell I am lootable? I did go anonymous.

Players can /con other players, just as they can con mobs (you know how to con mobs, right?). On PvP servers, player /cons only yield three responses.. Green (too low to kill), Red (too high to kill), and White (in range to kill).

quote:
Can't seem to use or find anything but a rusty scimitar. Is the scimitar the best or only slash weapon for me at my level?

Grab your quest scimitar..it's best to get it now while you still don't get exp loss on death. If you don't like Scimitars, then work on Blunt instead of Slash.. there are very few Druid usuable Slash weapons that aren't Scimitars or Scimitar-like.
