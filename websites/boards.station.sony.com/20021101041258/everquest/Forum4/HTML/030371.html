You do understand the reason behind the MB nerf/MR2 nerf/Spell effects changes?

MB nerf: So they can have low-hp, extremely difficult, mobs in PoP, without tossing around "MB immune" flags all over the place. Also the Kunark problem (Kunark mobs are tougher than their 32k hp implies -- they are harder than Vox/Nag, other 32 k hp mobs, because of regen, effects, and the like).

MR2 nerf: So mana means something on a raid. MR2s give a raid nearly infinite mana -- 1 mage can keep 10+ players from running oom, no matter what they do. It makes mana as free as cheal made hps.

If they want fights where mana is a limitation, they can't have MR2.

Spell Effects: Have you seen ice comet? The old engine couldn't do that. With some effort, they can make all spells look that good, and cause less lag than it did before.

Why not a per-zone nerf? Because mobs/zones immune to powerful abilities like MB/MR2 break classes. Do you balance the mage with mod rod 2, or do you balance the mage without mod rod 2? If you balance with, where it is disabled they suck. If you balance without, where it is enabled they are overpowered.

Ask a L 60 enchanter what they think of having mez/charm disabled where the developers think it is too powerful. Go ahead.

Second, any enchanter knows that our spells are HUGE aggro. If we get overpulls on unmezzable mobs I have to wait until SOME aggro is established on the adds so I dont get summoned. True I have mem blur spells, but if I get summoned, I'm dead before they go off. They are more for prevention. Cast a high aggro spell, then blur so you dont get aggro on your next spell, if you've already got aggro....

Used to be if we get a few adds I mez/root park them. Now I watch my group die.

So yes, I'm all for restructuring, not crippling abilities in certain areas. I MUCH prefer reducing options but still making them viable rather then making modrods just not work in certain zones, or making some mobs non manaburnable.

Redefining abilities is ALWAYS better then simply taking an entire ability away.

Manaburn will still be there, just more limited.

Modrods will still exist, just in a more limited fashion.
