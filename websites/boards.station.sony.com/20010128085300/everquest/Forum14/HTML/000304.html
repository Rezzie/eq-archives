I'm a level 30 druid, so I'm not that far past you. Since nobody has replied to your post, I'll give it a shot.

1) I almost exclusively group (unlike most druids), so I know nothing about quad kiting. However, let me take this time to mention that you're only hurting yourself if you only solo. Druids are important parts of groups and, especially since the exp patch, grouping gets you as good or better experience as soloing.

2) I don't get any higher level characters to help me in quests (I do most quests solo), so I don't know about any quests that you can't complete before level 30. The quest item I have that I appreciate most is the Testament of Vanaer. +10 WISDOM, +20 mana. Can't go wrong with that. It is held in your off-hand. You can find a pretty good description of the quest at AllaKhazam's Magical Realm.

Hope this helps a little.

Regarding quad kiting it can't be done for xp until 49. You get a very upgraded version of that level 5 lighting spell that can hit four targets at once. Snare 4 mobs, run around em in circles til they are a tight group, and blast away.
