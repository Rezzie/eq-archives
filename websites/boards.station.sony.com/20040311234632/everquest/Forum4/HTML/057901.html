Well.

Were you able to finish your citizenship quest? That is, have you been sent to see the king?

Do you intend to do any quests or spend any time around Shar Vahl or were you planing on leaving to level in the old world?

Did you start the quests at birth or do you have some levels on you?

If you think you ever may want to do Vah Shir Quests you should finish the citizen/guild quests. The slate you have now is not enough to do them.

If you are under level 10, I'd probably just start over. If not, well there is a way.

You need to have a friend make a vah shir of the SAME CLASS as you they don't intend to play. They run the quest to the point you were at, that is coming back from the Tax Collector. They turn in the item from the Tax Colector, you turn in your replacement slate. And then you go on your merry way, while they delete the character.

A character of any race of a Vah Shir class (shaman, beastlord, warrior, rogue, bard) can do the sonic wolf bane quests in this way. No, they can't use the weapon or cloak, but they can help make it. Puma3,

I know you got the new slate but I am not convinced it is fully atuned to you as a vah shir. You can test it by going to the two NPC Vah Shir just outside the entrance to the palace, there is one merchant under a little tent on one side and two merchants each under their own little tent on the other side. Talk to either of them (they both give quests) and when they ask to see your slate, give it to them and see what they say. If they accept it and give you the quest you will be fine. But otherwise, you will need to complete your citizenship quest.

So yes, when you need to turn in two things you need to do them at the same time. You can turn them in one at a time, but apparently someone else got there first and threw off your timing.

I like PlanesDrifter's suggestion of simply making another vah shir. I am sure that would work if you have an available character slot. I would ask for a new slate and then camp the vah shir you've been working on by the registrar.

Make the new one (and I would use the same class), do the running to the Registrar and then to the banker and back again. Turn in JUST the tax collector part and camp. Then, wake up your vah shir and turn in your new slate.

Then you should be good to go.

After your basic citizenship quest you are then told to go back to your guild. You then get your guild quests. As you complete various stages of them, you will get a zone-wide shout saying that you are now an Apprentice of your class. Personally, I think those are really cool

Here' a tip for all the Vah Shir Shamans out there who need to work on brewing skills...

Make Shar Vahl Essence. The recipe is: a payala fruit and a flask of water. That's it.

The payala fruit are a ground spawn in Shadeweaver's Thicket, found near the trees with the blue leaves (most, but not all, of them). You can gather around 26 of them on one circuit of the Thicket. Their respawn time is such that the first ones you harvested will have respawned by the time you finish the loop.

The trivial is quite high, somewhere in the 160's. My kitty shammy made 150 in brewing tonight doing this combine.

(Oh and my Human Ranger has his Vah Shir citizenship slate, so I can personally attest that the multiquest method described above does indeed work. I know Vah Shir can't be Rangers, but I thought I might want to do the progressive fletching quest.)
