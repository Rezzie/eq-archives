-Cobalt Scar: Nice zone with little use. A few sirens, wyverns and cobalt drakes. Nothing really exciting here - mainly a pass-through zone to WWastes. The Othmir give nice quests though.
-Siren's Grotto: Total waste - enough said.
-Plane of Growth: I like the idea of the Protectors of the Growth regularly spawning, but the drop rate is hilarious.
I haven't been to the other zones but one thing they all seem to have in common:
The mobs are WAY too hard. It should not be the case that a blue mob needs 2 groups to kill. Resistances are WAY too high. Did you never think of the poor bards who get every song resisted (well, all classes do)?
Another VERY sad point is: A PC never resists NPC spells (lvl 54 bard on 2nd level of Tower of Frozen Shadows with both poison and magic resist over 150 did not resist a SINGLE Shock of Poison). Basically this counts for some Kunark zones as well (lvl 54 bard with close to 200MR gets nuked by a burynai cenobite).
To do something in Velious, you need raid-sized groups in order to fight 2 or 3 blue mobs. I can't say anything good about exp because you lose more exp dying (even with res) than you make.
