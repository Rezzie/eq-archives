You CAN climb out of the crater -- stand under one of the two bridges and looking AWAY from the city go to the left side of the bridge. You can work your way up the side there, although on the Shadeweaver side you'll have to jump to clear the last bit before the top.

Yeah stand under the bridge that leads to the Shadweavers zone..(no idea what side, get a map for the north or south of it)

The face away from the city towards the outer crater wall...

Walk up the left side of the bridge and you will see a slight ledge for lack of better word leading diagonally up and to your left...it will reach an end and then switch up and to your right...

This will take right up to the bridge and the guard there. Yo may need to jump a bit at the very end, but not too hard once you figure out the trick to it.
