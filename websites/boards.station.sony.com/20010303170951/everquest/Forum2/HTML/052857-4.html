Lets take two situations, first is a cleric standing still, chain clicking the BP every 30 seconds as it cycles.

Mana cost per hour zero, mana recovery per hour, negligible.

Next lets take the cleric who is medding then CHing every 30 seconds to match the first one.

First a few base assumptions:
- spends 3 ticks medding and two ticks standing/sitting and casting the spell.
- recovers 50 mana/tick normally when medding

That gives us 11 ( Boon of Clear Mind ) + 2 ( Gift of Brilliance ) + 6 ( Protection of Glades ) + 13 ( Cantana ) = 32 mana per tick.

This gives 160 mana regained from buffs per casting of CH, with the 150 mana from medding for 3 ticks, it leaves us with 310 mana regained every cast of CH.

Given it costs the cleric 450 mana to cast CH, then it will only be 18 casts, or 9 minutes before the cleric is fully OOM.

Of course, in this situation you are actually sucking a substantial amount of mana out of other people to sustain this. With gift of thought at 300 mana/33 minutes, gift of brilliance at 450mana/100 minutes
and protection of the glades at 1200 mana/ 100 minutes, over the course of two hours you are consuming
3000 mana from other classes, as compared to zero from the first situation. Not to mention the fact that
