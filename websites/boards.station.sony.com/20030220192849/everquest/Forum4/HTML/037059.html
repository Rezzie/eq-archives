The reason your hide is sometimes taking more than one try to break the charm is because your hide isn't working. Depending on your skill (probably 50, I'm guessing), you have a chance of not actually hiding. Thing is, you're not getting a message saying you failed or not (only rogues get that), so you have no way of telling other than the pet doesn't break charm. A good idea might be to find a clickable invis item you can use so you don't have to waste a spell slot on invis.

I wasn't able to find the parts for that spell either, but I ended up buying a copy in the Bazaar for a couple hundred plat (was getting desperate by then, figured it was worth it).

Your hide doesn't work all of time because as a DE it is only going to be 50 no matter what.

As for finding the pages - just put in a full day slaughtering every gator you see and /auctioning that you're paying 5-10plat per page for the specific pages - my chanter did it in about three hours at 16 and had enough for three attempts.

Highpass is also a good place for those pages if you lose confidence in the oasis.

Also, don't forget to check all of the merchants in the areas that the pages will drop near - plenty of folks who have no clue or interest sell them for cash at that level.
