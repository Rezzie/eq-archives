/pause 35,/cast 2
/stopsong
/pause 35,/cast 3

is not correct, because you need more pauses:

/pause 35,/cast 1
/pause 3,/stopsong
/pause 35,/cast 2
/pause 3,/stopsong
/pause 35,/cast 3

Like that. Abashi said they might fix this in the future so that you don't have to have the additional pauses, but don't count on it.

Also, 35 is quite a long pause. You could probably shorten that to even 28.

Traditional Bards would probably snicker at Bards that use macros in battle, but oh well. To be honest, they don't work and probably never work as well as manual twisting, but they make life easier and the game funner. "Don't like twisting? Why'd you become a bard then!" I do like twisting, but it can get tiresome. Ok now for THE only reliable battle twisting.

Go to you custom socials and make 8 macros. For example the first is:

/cast 1
/pause 25
/stopsong

and the second:

/cast 2
/pause 25
/stopsong

Simple eh? I have Heal on song bubble 1 so I call the first one '1Heal' and color it blue like the song. Then I add it to my 6 (currently) slot macro menu. Ill do that for say CoD and Bellows and Anthem, for example.

This works just like a warriors Kick and Bash - You push the button once and itll work! (heh usually) It waits 2.5 secs and kills the song a perfect time to wait I believe. When you see your song end due to the macro, push a another button!! Wow, sooo hard huh?? You can do two songs to a macro the same way, but I wouldnt reccomend for battle like someone said above, only for downtime.

Twisting song...

First things first... If you want to be a bard... a decent one you will not ever use macros in battle. Let me explain why in a quick example:

Monk pulls a MOB: you hit your macro key.
Before you finish your first song 2 more MOBs join in the fight. They both come to visit you because you are cheering on your group, MOBs hate that. Now you get pummled for about 16 seconds. Most likely you healer will have to waist at least one heal on you. Bards can't take hits very well. Now being a good bard you charm MOB 1 and have it attack MOB 2... so they kill each other instead of you or your group. You instead use your 3 song marco you use your 2 song macro knowing that most of the time charm will last about 15 sec. But guess what it fails right after you hit that macro button again... get the idea.

If you think you can play a bard and not twist in battle forget it... and all you will do is leave a bad name for the other bards.

However, twisting after battle sounds awsome! I am going to create a nice clarity, clerity, heal macro tonight. The selos heal and selos lev sound like good ideas too. Just don't fizzle lev when running though warswoods, outch!

By the way for those that do not know you can actually twist 4 songs... you will loose the effect of each song briefly but the extra effect is usually well worth the effort. Please not that this will only work with songs with a 3 tic duration. i.e. DOT, heal, DOT, selos (good one for kiting since you only have to swap 2 instruments)

my 2 cents
