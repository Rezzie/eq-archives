Hey sweaty! Welcome to the game!

Your best bet for equipment and armor is to do the newbie quests for armor. Go talk to Arinna Trueblade in the West Freeport wariors guild to get you started.

Note: you can get pp pieces if you hunt the rats. Every so often a rat will drop a plague rat tail that sells for pp pieces to merchants

To be able to tell if you chracter can equip an item, right click on the item and look at the stats that list the race/class for that piece of equipment. You should look for HUM to be listed as a race and WAR to be listed as a class.

Warriors are a class that does not get any magical ability to replenish your health. Buy som bandages and start practicing your bind wound skill. Bind wound can replenish your helath up to 50% of your health. Other then that you have to sit down and wait or group with another player that has healing spells like a cleric, druid, or shaman, or bard.

1) To see if you can wear armor, right-click on it. This will bring up a window which tells you the races and classes that can wear it.

2) Bandages. You are a warrior, so you need to learn how to use Bind Wounds.

3) Kill everything you can, loot everything you kill. Sometimes you'll see people killing without looting their kills. If they run off and leave the loot behind, pick it up. Sell everything, and put the money in the bank. Those coppers add up.

Do not ask other people for items or money. That will annoy them greatly.

Hail the NPCs in the Warriors Guild. They might talk to you about armor.

Best of luck, and welcome to Everquest

quote:
To see if you can equip an item right click it,

Don't forget to right-click and HOLD. You can also left-click on an item and press "alt" at the same time.

If it is a piece of armor, it will tell you:

-its name
-might be flagged as "No Drop" (cannot be dropped and/or sold), "No Rent" (will disappear when you exit the game) and/or "Lore" (can only have one of this item)
-its weight
-its AC value (the higher the better)
-might be other bonuses (example: STR +2, AGI +1, etc.), but not always though
-Races that can equip it
-Classes than can equip it
-might be restricted by deity, but it is quite rare in the low levels

ok, I'll pass on a little knowledge that I've picked up here, and that hasn't been mentioned. Although you won't be able to do this immediately, when you are high enough level (and if you have the luclin expansion), go to East commons and kill every spiderling and cat that you can . Keep every spiderling silk and cat pelt (with the exception of ruined cat pelts) so you can sell them later in the bazaar(also, you can kill wolves and bears to get some pelts). The ruined pelts can be used to make patchwork armor if you want to start the tailoring tradeskill. To know how much to sell the silks and pelts for in the bazaar, search for the same items and price your items around the same price as other vendors.

If you do this, baking is a good tradeskill to take up, because you will get meats from the animals and it will save you a little bit of money later.

Another good thing to farm for the bazaar are snake eggs. They are used for the baking tradeskill and can usually sell for around 1pp each.
