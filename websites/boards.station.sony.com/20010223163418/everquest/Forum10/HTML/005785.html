IMO, one of the best quests for your level is the greater lightstone quest in NK (or SRO for evil players).

You get a buttload of exp and faction, plus about 9pp (depending on cha) per GLS you turn in. Just be sure to have a general inventory slot open when you turn the GLS in, because you will get a book that is a container.

Most of the quests in Qeynos are pretty fun to do but have really crappy rewards (e.g., tax collection).

Above all, just play and have fun. Go to a new zone if you're bored. Ever been to crushbone, blackburrow, the mino caves in satemfont (don't go too deep in), everfrost, rathe mts (this is doable, just stay away from hill giants), or nektulous forest killing DE guards by the bridge?

Two proposals, both involving lots of travels and getting a magic weapon for it:

1) Bloodforge Hammer: This involves traveling to Qeynos, kill a renegade dwarf there and bring his head back to Kaladim. You get a nice 2HB weapon.

2) Shining Star of Light: The most difficult part of this quest is to get the amiable faction with the temple of light in Qeynos. But at level 9 to 15 you could go hunting bandits in the Karanas and turn in Bandit Sashes. They bring good xp and lots of money as well as good faction. Once you have the faction, you need to travel to Rivervale, back to Queynos, to West Karana and back to Qeynos again. But you get a very nice 1HB weapon, the Shining Star of Light.

All times are PT (US)
