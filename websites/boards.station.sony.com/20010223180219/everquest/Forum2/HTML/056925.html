Lake of Ill Omen

I love the "wide open" feel that most of this zone has. You can run around without bumping into stuff if you have to. Bolt spells can be used to good effect in this area, and there is a wide level range of critters, and several zone areas (to cut down on train bottlenecks).

The art for the ruins and such gives a VERY good impression to the area, almost Greek Myth-ish. Sarnaks are fairly cool looking critters, and the goblins are interestingly strange.

Already a beautifly put together zone, it also has great EXP, stuff to kill for faction, and some good loot to be had. It's a small wonder that so many people are going there now.

We need more zones like this one.
