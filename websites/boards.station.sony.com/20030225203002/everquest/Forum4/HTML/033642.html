Depending upon your level, there is a quest in Mistmoor that will raise faction with temple of Sol Ro. In tyhe graveyard there is an invisible man (A reformed Shadowed Man now a worshiper of Solusk Ro) If you go into the crypt that he stands behind there is a ground spawn on the coffin (A flower). Give the guy the flower for faction.

The ground spawn respawns in about 90 seconds to 120 seconds later and can be repeated. Of course you have to be able to keep the graveyard clear of monsters and that means being mid 40's and at that point you should be able to kill shadowed men and they give faction for Sol Ro also.

There is also a group in Lavastorm mountains, quite handy to the Temple of Sol Ro.

Killing Shadowmen definitely raises faction with Temple of Sol Ro. Camps of Shadowmen are all over the place. West Commons, Innothule Swamp, Lesser Faydark, South Karana, Nektulos Forest, and Lavastorm Mountains, to name a few. Personally, I found the Lesser Faydark camp the best because it was very close to the Steamfont zone line.

Ortallius is the orc in South Ro that others have mentioned. He takes 3 Cutthroat Dervish rings and gives Temple of Sol Ro faction raises plus some garbage items.

I haven't done the Mistmore quest, but I would definitely look into that.

Lastly, you are an enchanter. Do the spells Alliance or Benevolence or Collaboration ring a bell?
