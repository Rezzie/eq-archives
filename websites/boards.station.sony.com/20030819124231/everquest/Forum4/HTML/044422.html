Well, wisdom is the stat associated with casting for a druid - it determines how big a pool of mana you have. So you can't go wrong with that. After wisdom, there is strength (so you can carry more) or Charisma (which helps a tiny, tiny bit with the animal charming spells you will get eventually), or Stamina (which adds a tiny, tiny amount to your hitpoints).

www.eqdruids.com is a site for druids, I believe.
