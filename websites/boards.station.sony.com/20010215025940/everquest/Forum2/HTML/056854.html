Small races will level slightly faster than large race warriors. (Barbarians pay a 10% race penalty, ogres 15% and trolls 25% and nowadays the player pays for it, not his group). Not needing to be shrunk to fit in dungeons is a huge advantage in midlife. Your disadvantages are lower STR and STAMINA and inability to "slam". High level warriors are wanted more for their ability to absorb damage ("tank") than for their ability to dish it out but your total hp's have more to do with your class and equipment than your stamina. I doubt you'll ever be turned down for a raid or group because you're small but you might be at a disadvantage in the Best of the Best competitions or in duels with large race warriors. (Btw if your race is dwarf, they're quite a popular choice since they have good warrior stats.)

I play a 51 Gnome Warrior.

The novelty of playing a small race warrior started taking a nose dive once I hit my 30's. This was when I spent a long time questing for the Hero Bracer only to finally win it and find out it didnt fit, ouch. There goes 10str I needed real bad.

This experience was repeated many times over (treeweave, sleeves of the ancients, and alot of stuff here and there) as I realized that as a small race I had no options other than crafted until Velious finally came out and I got a full set of Guardian armour.. at 47.

Gnomes have it pretty bad since the strong just get stronger (with thier gear) and we are just neglected until we hit the planes. I am not completely sure there isnt something just as good out now that Velious is being farmed.. But the life of a small race warrior really sucked when Kunark came out and had nothing for us, unless you had 20k to spend per peice of cobalt.

When I fight side by side with an ogre or troll the differences are very apparent in our abilities. I do ok though, they just happen to do much, much better. Did I mention I had 110str until level 47? You can thank my guardian armour and cougar claw earrings for the boost, they gave ac too.

All in all though, I still would not play a large race warrior. I live in dungeons, and the chore of buying shrink potions would grate on my nerves. But if I had it to do over again I would have been a dark elf. They aren't as girly looking as wood elves and they can wear all the medium sized armour not availible to the small races or large races.
