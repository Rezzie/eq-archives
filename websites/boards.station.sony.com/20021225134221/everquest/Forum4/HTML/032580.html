Two suggestions:

Blackened Crystalline Robe - Beautiful black set of robes that would look awesome on a dark-elf. They have nice +Int, +Cha and some other stats. They have been selling in the Bazaar for 300pp or so lately, prices may vary on your server. But they are a fairly common drop in Crystal Caverns on Velious off the Toilmaster I think. Our group down had one drop after 2 hours the one day and only the second time killing that guy. My Magician ended up winning that roll, woohoo!

For a weapon, get him a Shaded Staff. It's a 1-Handed Blunt Staff useable by All/All. It gives +5 resists to all resists except for fire which is -5 resist. It has a translucent/clear greyish look too it. My Beastlord uses one as it yields a very *GOOD* ratio at 10/22. Again I can't say for Xev but expect the Staff to be around 300-800pp, normally more like 400pp from what I've been seeing as lately, that's what I picked mine up for.

With these two items together and a Stein of Moggok in the off-hand you'll have a well built, great looking Dark-Elf Necromancer beside you. Hope it doesn't go to his head! LOL!
