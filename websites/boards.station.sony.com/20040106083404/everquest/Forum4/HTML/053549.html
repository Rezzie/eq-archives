Zone into SG from CS. There is usually a walrus-type mob that is directly in front of you. Depending on how brave you're feeling, feel free to drink an EB and invis potion.

[Note: "Running SG" is a misnomer, the most difficult part is the swimming. Please take the 1-2 hours required somenight during off-time and swim around to max out your swimming skill.]

I run it regularly now without invis, EB or sow and it's not a problem. But if you're unfamiliar with it, having EB and invis on may ameliorate some angst.

Ok, go out to the ledge where the walrus is sitting and look out. Off to your left and down in the pool you will see a tunnel underneath the water. That is where you are heading. The tunnel entrance is usually guarded by 2 seahorses. These 2 usually do not see invis. /con them to confirm. Swim into the tunnel. There is a 3d seahorse at the midpoint of the tunnel. This one almost always sees invis. He will likely attack you. I have managed to get by him before by hugging the left wall, as he is on the right. But count on him aggroing.

Don't fight him. Just keep swimming, you'll very soon come to the end of the tunnel. Look up and swim up. As you swim up, turn about 90 degrees to the right. You'll see a white-ish line that is the lip of the waterfall. Swim over this line and over the waterfall into the pool below. After you drop, do not continue to swim to the 2d waterfall and drop a 2d time. Drop ONLY once.

Once you have dropped over the water fall, turn left and swim up to the shore. You will see a large golden fish statue. Run by the statute. Hug the left wall and take the first left hall. Continue to hug the left wall past another walrus mob, and zone out into WW.

Once you do it a few times, you'll never need to ask for a succor again.

Now you know, and knowings half the battle.
Don't die in WW. Consume a sow, lev and invis potion and run.

Actually if you don't have a great ammount of hp and no porter, the best way is to camp across. This takes advantage of game mechanics (not sure if its a cheat or not, but its been widely used for years now).

When you zone in, back into the corner and sit down. Camp out. At the character selection screen log bag in. Don't move an inch when you get back in game. You should be partially in the wall (if you can see other rooms, etc you have done it). Sit down and camp out again (don't move). Log back in and you will have been moved to the zone safe point (succor point).

This method makes the game think you are stuck in the wall and moves you.

This also works if you fall down the well in thurg. anyzone that has a proper corner will work.

Here's an old school trick. Don't get caught as it used to be (and probably still is) considered an exploit.

As you zone in there is a wall right in front of you. Get as far close as you can to it and sit down. Camp out. If you get close enough you fall thru the wall. The game automatically moves you to the succor point which is across the zone at the WW end.

Sometimes it works, sometimes it doesn't. In the past I have tried 3-4 times to get it to work.

I don't really know if anyone bothers to do this now. Most folks just run like hell. I hit selo's accelerating chorus before I zone in, fire up invis after I get in, and run (swim). You will agro the seahorses but if you can get out of the water up at the top they won't pursue. Nothing at top of the water tunnel sees invis.

I can normally run much faster than it takes to get everyone together and succor accross.
