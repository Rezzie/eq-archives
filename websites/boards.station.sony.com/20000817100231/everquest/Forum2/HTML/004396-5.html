After looking at all the suggestions, I think the best compromise would be the following:
1) Have 3-teams: good, evil and neutral.
Good = current light races
Evil = current dark races
Neutral = any race at all.
2) Membership in neutral is voluntary. I don't know whether it would be easier to make this a one-time choice, or whether switching back and forth within some limits would be practical.
3) No cross-team healing or buffing. Since belonging to the neutral team would be voluntary, the loss of the ability for, say, a light race character to be healed or buffed by the "good" team would be a free choice made in return for the right to PK members of the good team.
4) No out-of-range healing or buffing in PvP situations, as per Abashi's suggestion. To prevent exploits though, have this be triggered by attacking another PC, not being attacked by one.

This above solution would remove the Invulnerable healer exploit, allow lights the freedom they have to group together, allow those who want to PK all races, even their own race to have a team in which they could do so.

3-teams would allow a more complex, ****ing set of alliances and enmity, and also avoid perhaps shifting the balance too far against the dark team.

A little something for everyone ...
