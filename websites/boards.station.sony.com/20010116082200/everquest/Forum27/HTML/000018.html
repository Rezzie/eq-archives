I am intrigued by the guild system on this very, very complex game. Wanting to add diversity, which Norrath is unparalleled in, and wanting to further blur the line between RL and VR, as there are many of us who enjoy doing that, I am thinking about creating a new guild. The name I have not decided on, but a first attempt at a name is the "Q Militia".

There are many time that people in guilds find common ground with other guildmembers. I choose to take the latter. Find people that have things in common with you, take that kinship and go monster mashing.

Queers have long been shunned in the Real World. I choose to be an openly gay person in the real world, but for some odd reason, find myself hesitant at best to identify as such online. Interestingly enough, as I first thought that VR would be a great place to exist as who we want to be, not who we have to be.

I don't know that there are a lot of Queer Guilds out there... perhaps there are. The Q Militia is a little different. We have ideals. Among those ideals is the desire to help newbies advance, the desire to pool resources to complete quests, and the desire to defend those who are defenseless. As an oppressed group of people, we have honor, and we have swords in the event that honor is disrespected. It's nice to know that you can always whip out a sword and settle differences the Norrath way if you feel that someone is treating you unfairly because of who you are.

Let me make one thing clear, however. The Militia, despite its name is NOT a military or mercenary organization. We are a group that seeks to help others, and we are skilled craftsman. Primarily, we are merchants. The name "Militia" only refers to the fact that we can't and won't abide a lack of respect. We can cross swords and cast spells as well as any other member of EQ, yet we need a place where we can act as a family. Often times, people who you group with are not very forthcoming about their personal lives for whatever reason. I am intending on this guild being the opposite. A place where a sense of community is harvested, and where members know that they can have a friend, and an ally, both in Norrath, and in the real world. We are about family, and we are about mutual respect and understanding of ourselves and those around us. In a perfect world, there wouldn't be war. In Norrath, things are far from perfect. It's important that people who have a common thread band together and harbor a true, accepting family.

Please note, I am *not* insinuating that other guilds have discriminated or even cared about my preference. This has not happened to me, and is not the motivation behind this post. I am just looking for something a little more high-profile, and a guild seems like the right way to go.

I know there are probably a ton of things that I forgot to say in this post, and like I said I am just starting out with this guild thing. I myself am primarily a Level 15 Mage named Nahualli. I can often be seen trying to eek out a living sewing clothes and sneaking around in the desert of Ro. My personal ambitions in Norrath include one day challenging the PoD outside Kelethin, as well as Kizdean Gix and Cazel, who both have claimed my life many times because of random hapless encounters. I hold a grudge, I won't lie.

I would like to get this started as soon as possible, please email me at nahualli@psyborg.com or send a /tell Nahualli if you see me online. I am online a lot. I am also on the lookout for forging alliances with other guilds. Fledglings and their guilds such as my self will do well to seek friends in power.

By the way, I am a roleplayer, and I do have to point out that this guild is mainly a roleplaying guild. It is also not only open to queers. As long as you don't mind, we don't either.

A million thanks.

Nahualli
