Adjust the ZEM (Zone Experience Multiplier) by the number of people in a zone. As the number of players in a zone increases, lower the ZEM (and announce when it changes). When the number of players in a zone drops, increase the ZEM.
Create a way for players to find out which zones are not crowded, and currently have the best ZEM? Just making something up here, a player looks at the current zone populations and sees that Unrest is empty. She can then go to unrest and capitalize on the great ZEM in that zone.
