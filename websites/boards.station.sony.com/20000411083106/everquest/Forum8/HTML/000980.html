It's been known for a very long time that there are certain Tailored items that can NOT be made in the game following the published formulas. Specifically, a number of Studded Leather and Reinforced Leather pieces, as well as the Quiver and Fleeting Quiver. In addition, despite a large effort spaning multiple servers, no formula for either quiver can be found.

These issues have been known for at least 6 months by players in the game, and have been reported to Verant numerous times in all imaginable ways, including GM petitions, e-mail to Verant employees, and an incredible number of Message Board, mailing list, and newsgroup posts.

Many of us understand that Trade Skills are not the highest priority right now; there are very few players who would pursue Tailoring of Studded or Reinforced armor or Quivers regardless of their working status, for reasons that are valid but will not be gone into here. However, many of us wish to play EQ as its makers intend, as a Role-Playing Game, or are interested for matters of personal pride. We feel as though we are playing within the guidelines of the game, and are being slighted because of it.

Please. These issues have gone on for far too long. I don't know whether these fixes will be easy or difficult; right now, though, we can't see that they have EVER been looked into, and we feel it's high time it was done. Allow the Tailors of Norrath full access to their Trade.

I will bump again.

I got a reasoned response from Abashi on the Gameplay forum (an unrelated issue). Maybe we can attract his attention here.

Abashi, let me reemphasize a few points:

A)We are NOT looking for 'cheats' or hints.

B)We ARE looking for confirmation as regards to the working/non-working status of certain Tailored items.

C)We ARE asking that those items which are not working be corrected in a reasonably timely fashion (NOT 'now', but as soon as can be reasonably done, taking priorities into account), and that we be let know that these fixes are 'in the works'.

For example, certain studded and reinforced items can not be made according to the in-game published formulas. These are obviously not working, and we would appreciate confirmation of this and the assurance that they will be. Quivers, on the other hand, do not have a published in-game formula (which is understandable and can add to the enjoyment of the game); for these, a "Yes, they work, you just haven't figured them out yet" or a "No, we found out they don't work, we're fixing them, but we're still not telling you the recipie" would be fine.

I think our main concern is not necessarily the lack of action on these items, but current lack of acknowledgement of the problem. We know that if YOU know there's a problem, it'll get addressed eventually. Just reassure us you know there's a problem.
