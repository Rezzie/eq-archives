Hi,

I play a 40 Wizard on E'ci. I agree that Wizards could use a bit of tweaking in terms of reducing down time. (I also play a Necro and a Mage - neither of which has nearly the same down time as my Wizard.) I also agree that Wizards have more difficulty getting groups than, say, Enchanters or Clerics. However, for the most part, I am satisfied with my Wizard character. If you know what you're doing, soloing (especially at higher levels) is easy. (It amazes me how few Wizards use their Yonder line of spells. A crucial escape spell for soloing.) In groups, the primary job of the Wizard is *not* to deal out massive amounts of damage quickly. If you play your Wizard that way, it's no wonder you can't get a group. The Cleric is always healing you, instead of keeping the tanks healthy!
The job of the Wizard is to (a) slow the mob down with Root/Snare spells and STUNS, (b) help drop the mob quickly from Berzerk health (about 50%) to fleeing health (about 20%), and (c) in DIRE emergency, chain cast to taunt a mob off a nearly dead party member (like the Cleric <G> ) and use Root/Stun or Yonder (if outdoors) to escape the thing after pulling it off someone. Keep a damage absorbing shield (Shieldskin, Leatherskin, etc...) on to help you survive if you have to do (c), or if you just get jumpy with the nukes.

However, I do have suggestion for improving the desireability of Wizards in group situations. One thing would be better Evac-type skills. For example:

(1) Have more Evac or port locations, closer to dungeons. Evac is pretty much useless in Velious - it takes forever to get back to where you were before.

(2) Have a group type Translocate spell, so you can port the entire group to your bind point (say, right outside the dungeon that you're crawling through).

(3) Have a form of mob teleportation. Ie have a way of porting a monster AWAY from the group; a kind of reverse Yonder. Would be VERY useful for over-pulls, over aggroed mobs about to kill someone, etc... Basically useful in situations where a Mez would be used, but not *quite* as useful as a Mez. Wizards are masters of Teleportation, so this kind of mob control for Wizards would make sense. It also would be quite a bit less useful for soloing than a true Mez; would only help with over-pulls.

As for down-time, solo down-time is expected. Verant has said that you should suffer long down times if you choose to solo. Down time in groups isn't nearly as bad, *IF* you know how to play the class. Harvest was a boon to the class, in spite of everyone's complaints about it. However, having some way of increasing mana regen, or storing mana for later use, would be a good idea to explore. Just not sure how to do it in a way that isn't over the top...

<Relinquishes the soap box>
