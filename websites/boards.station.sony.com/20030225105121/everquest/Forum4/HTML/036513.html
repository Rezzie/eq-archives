Stamina is one of the more difficult stats to raise as well as being important for any melee class character. That would be the stat I gave first priority to. Stamina = hit points and that is of high priority for an SK for two reasons. Not only are you being in melee contact with the mobs, but many of your spells trade hit points for other benefits like mana.
