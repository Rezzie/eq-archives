I'm afraid that only one kerra beetle spawns in Erud's Crossing at any given time, and that when you kill it, the death of said beetle causes a high level creature to spawn that must be killed for another beetle to show up.

Generally, you'll see one beetle, one madman, one beetle, one vampire bat, one beetle, one fire/air elemental, etc. Just keep killing the beetle, then the toughy, and you'll do fine.

Do remember though, that beetles are FRIENDS of the Kerrans (cat-people) and thus, you risk their ire!
