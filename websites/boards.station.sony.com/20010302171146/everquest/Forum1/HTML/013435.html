DirectX 8.0 is backward compatable with all previous versions of DirectX.

If you could post the dxdiag.txt file from your computer, it would provide more information that may help to fix your problem.

To generate the dxdiag.txt file, you'll need to open up the DirectX Diagnostics Tool.

DirectX Diagnostics Tool: Click on Start, go to run and type: DXDIAG [enter]

Once in the DirectX Diagnostics Tool, locate the button along the bottom labeled "Save all information." Click on it and it will generate the dxdiag.txt file into your C:\My Documents folder. Copy the text and paste it onto the message board.
