Shadowstump

Posts: 1247 		posted 03-01-2004 08:16 AM           user search     report post
For example:

Total AP: 3000

Tak: 800
Mir: 1000
Ruj: 300
Guk: 600
MMC: 4000

With the 3k you can:
Buy 3k worth of items in Tak worth no more than 800AP each.
Buy an item worth upto 4k in MMC.
Buy any item worth less than 1k in Mir etc.

All times are PT (US)
