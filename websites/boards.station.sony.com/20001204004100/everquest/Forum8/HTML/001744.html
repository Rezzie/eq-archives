Bugs in Dwarven smithing:

1. All dwarven weapons and shields require earthen temper to make. Every other cultural recipes only require a temper if the item is imbued.

2. Making dwarven chain jointing consumes the dwarven smithing hammer.

3. There is no recipe known for making brellium chain mail. This means no cultural armor for small race rogues, druids, casters, etc.

4. Imbued dwarven plate recipes do not work- even though they are described in game by an NPC (Tortuk Everhot in North Kaladim).


Dwarves cannot (at this point) make any imbued armor without the assistance of a non-dwarf. They are the only race that faces this problem- ogres, barbarians and iksar can all make imbued chain class items. Humans can even make imbued non-enchanted field plate.

Suggestions:

1. Minor point, but worth looking into.

2. Should be easy fix

3. Either add the recipe, or if the recipe exists, have the NPC’s instruct or sell a book.

4. Either add the recipe, or make the dwarven chain class armor and also make it imbuable. If imbued dwarven plate recipes are not added, fix Turtok’s dialog so that he stops wasting peoples time and money.
