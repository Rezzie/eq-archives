The ability to remove coin from the trade window has always been intentional. It allows for the negotiation of prices without having to completely agree in advance.

Being able to remove items was easily exploitable due to UI limitations, but the coin "exploit" is just playing on the gullibility of the players. Trades are supposed to go as follows:

Player 1 puts items into trade window.
Player 2 puts coins into trade window.
Player 2 clicks "Trade".
Player 1 clicks "Trade".

If you follow these steps, you'll never get scammed. This is not a bug and does not need to be fixed.
