No names being mentioned but yes this thread is getting a little gen-dis like..

The price on an item is set by an average price for a particular server and to a certain extent the zone you are in..

There is NOTHING wrong with selling for less or for more than the average price, the price being offerd out is up to the seller as is the actual accepted price at the end of the transaction, its that simple.

If you are unhappy with the offer, politly decline and advise of the area you was looking for, (also be prepared to haggle a little or selling might become more difficult the higher level you get).

That goes for the other direction, there is nothing wrong with advising the seller that the price is a little too steep for you, would you possibly accept this offer instead?

Not everyone in the game has the vast resources that some players seem to have and they will try to buy what they can for a price they can afford.

Not everyone in the game is trying to rip you off when they offer less money than may or may not be the standard price on a particular server.
