I have NO problem with being required to find a 49th level (or higher) enchanter to cast the spell. I have no problem with Royal Temper being required (despite the fact that it seems that no one has yet actually MADE Royal Temper on my server, the ingredients are insanely hard to find) and I have no quarrel with the Emerald/Ruby being required (which adds 14pp/140pp to the cost).

What I do take issue with, however, is the Jacinth that is needed for the Enchanter to cast Enchant Ore for me. Although I personally do not KNOW this, I understand that it is a planar-drop (drop ONLY) gem, and sells for approximately 1000pp. I have asked 49+ enchanters in-game several times, and have yet to find one who 1) knows how much I should expect to pay for an enchanted block of ore, or 2) Tells me I should expect to pay ANYTHING under 1000platinum.

I'm not saying this is WRONG. I don't know yet, obviously, since I haven't made the Longsword I want to make. It may be that the stats and bonuses on the Antonian Longsword make it worth having, despite the high cost. I also want to point out that I have my entire guild looking for Royal Temper components, and for Jacinths, but we're only about 30 strong, and have only 1 member 50+.

I would very much like to hear your position on the reagent for the Enchant <metal> line of spells, Yakatizma. If you do not feel that the currently required reagents are out of line for the benefit the spell provides, could you please tell me why? I appreciate any response you can make. The smithing upgrade overall is awesome, even though I've only made 2 pieces of Fine Steel, and a couple of the new cultural weapons (but I did buy a Northman Claymore. Durge_Chanter,

The reason that Black Pearl prices dropped through the floor is because Abashi finally came out and said what the stats actually do.

When people finally found out that DEX and AGI were almost worthless above the 75 point, they started to divest themselves of gear that had those stats for gear with higher AC values and STR. This is also the reason you see Serpentine Bracers sold for 20pp or just sold to merchants.

The reason that Black Sapphire, Diamond and Jacinth have kept their prices is because of their rarity (Any mob level 50+ drops them), the difficulty in making pieces with them, and the fact that they make good jewelery pieces. (Note, they dont make superb pieces, but the pieces are good).
