I really hate seeing people use Rogues as an example.

Rogues are extremely handicapped until they reach level 51. That's when they become gods. Monks are godly up until around 53. Monks can easily replace wars in most mid level dungeons. Lguk,SolB for example. Rogues can never do this. Although at 60 Rogues can push replacing wizards.

The few things I think Monks should get...

1) Triple attack at 55 capping at 5-10 more skill points above wars at 60. It never made sense to me why the one's taht are at one with their bodies couldn't do this when Wars could with brute force.
2) Lowered time on mend at 51+ levels. Be a HUGE benefit as the gap between War and Monk AC begins to increase.
3) Flying Kick- This one is segmented into either, ors. I;d like to see one of the following.
a) Min Flying Kick per Level. Should probably be a min at 3/4 level. Seeing a monk flying kick for 3 (for some reason the low is always 3) is just plain sad.
b) Leave Flying Kick as is but introduce a method of letting other skills be used in a combination of moves. I think this would actually be neat.
c) A Completely new move. (Although the jump kick is one of the most fun things about playing a monk).
4) Do something about FD. There needs to be another tier or something. The tier at 35 is reasonable but when a monk reaches 55-60 it is silly that lev 40-45 Mobs arn't forgetting/believing a Monk is truly FD.

Monks with adequate gear can solo up to 50 fairly easily. With an Epic and a Fungi Hide Tunic they can solo into even higher levels and are not bound by having to med. And being ale to bandaid 70% life at 51+ witha 200 skill is sick as well.

Yes they may lose what they had prior to the expansions release. But they are still very wanted in NUMEROUS situations. Rogues are only very useful at the high level end of the game. I agree it is a weird way to have balance but I guess it is balance none the less.
