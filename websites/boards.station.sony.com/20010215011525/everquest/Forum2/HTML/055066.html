All right, due to the impression that you all have given me, it seems that I need to cover wizards next…
I’ve already made several notes to myself about wizards, and I may as well start out this discussion with those notes. Keep in mind that these ‘talks’ are geared toward making sure that the classes are ‘balanced’. Please work toward that goal when posting (yes, even if that means you suggest a ‘nerf’). But I’m also interested in hearing about bugs and such too, I’m just not going to focus on them.

Here are the notes that I already have. Keep in mind that these are only my notes, and just because I’ve listed something does NOT mean that I like the idea or that the designers are working on making changes based on these notes. This is just what it is, a list of ideas. I’ve also added my notes to you all under the header of Note:

AoE spells: Mentioned elsewhere… (earlier on my list, probably under mages: basically the idea here is that maybe pets should be removed from the equation).

Gaze/Glimpse: Due to the clipping plane, these spells don’t really allow further seeing, just zooming in (which makes your resolution worse anyhow).

Note: this may not be ‘fixable’ at all due to the way the game works. It’s not like we can force your PC into a higher resolution when you use this spell…

Fade/Shadowstep/Yonder: There seems to be a problem with these spells. Since they will not teleport you up, they don’t work too well in hilly terrain; because the spell only sends you a few feet (stopping when it reaches a hill or building).

Note: Again, this may also not be something we can change, but it’s on the list.

Bind Sight: Seems to cause viewer to see the target as running into walls or spinning in circles if target goes up an incline.

Enstill: It seems that this spell is not a more useful or more powerful root, it’s just more expensive.

Cazic Gate: At level spell is attained, using it will most often cause death because the caster appears amidst many aggressive monsters that /con white to them.

Note: We’ve actually discussed this before. If I remember correctly, the general feeling was that having a dangerous gate like this wasn’t necessarily a bad thing. But it is also still on my list.

Tishan’s Relocation: This spell evacuates you to a very dangerous location, a monster crossroads in the Skyfire mountains. Could the location be moved, or was it intended that this be a very dangerous evacuation spell?

Note: See note about Cazic Gate.

Concussion: A level 39 spell dropped by a creature in a location that requires a party of level 50s to handle. Should it be in such a location?

Icestrike effects: Graphical effect looks like fire.

Lightning Bolt effects: looks exactly like Fire Bolt.

Other lightning and cold effects: It seems that there might be other spells that have the look of fire when they should not.

Having just re-read this list, I see nothing at all related to actual balancing stuff. This is all nit-picky stuff about minor effects and bugs and such (with the possible exception of Area of Effect spells, changing those would certainly have an effect on class balances). So, please, try to focus on balance issues…

Alan

I’ll be as brief as I can so that Alan doesn’t have to strain his eyes any more than needed.

BALANCE ISSUES
These are things that relate directly to the balance of the Wizard class in the game.

-Too weak and easily killed at lower levels
-Unable to contribute in highest level Velious fights (due to hit point totals)
-Damage Inequities (levels 1-15 and 34-38 outgunned by other classes)
-Resistance cap at 6+ or 1.2x level reduced Wizard to meditating when party needs him most
-Harvest post 50 appears to not work properly
-Needing to dip as many as 15 levels for a given damage type of Fire, Cold, Magic
-Damage poorly scaled compared to monster’s hit points (damage output and value to group by level lowers each level)
-Mana efficiency and damage too poor for group contribution when compared to melee and pet classes at lower levels
-Fizzles at highest levels too common
-Tishan’s Relocation is a deathtrap, that is unacceptable for an evac spell.

REPAIR ISSUES
These are things that are only slightly related to balance but contribute to imbalance because they are not working properly.

-Al’Kabor line costs too much mana and does too little damage to be of any use
-Concussion Spell quest requires you to be significantly higher level than the spell
-Imbue Fire Opal is in Nagafen’s Lair, nothing you can achieve at the level of the spell
-Area Effect Rain spells STILL have problems (not enough waves of effect, highly resistable)
-Bind Sight spell sticks and stops moving
-Fade, Shadowstep, Yonder will not teleport you UP and will not port beyond objects
-Bonds of Force has Z-axis problems
-Enstill costs more mana and casts slower but almost never has any more effect than normal root.
-Staff of Temperate Flux worthless in its intended use (needs more than 6 points of resistance reduction on cold/fire)
-Post 35 innate resistance reduction does not appear to be in the game, if so it is very insignificant
-Area Effect spells are sporatically efficient, often are not, and in no pattern (making them consistently useful would encourage their use and learning)
-Cazic Thule gate in a poor place, move outside
-Color/Graphic issues (red ice, etc)
-Level 44 interrupt blast is too slow to hope to interrupt spells unless you really get lucky
-O’Kiels Flickering Flame costs too much mana.

ENHANCEMENT ISSUES
These are areas that, like giving Resist Cold to Rangers or better heals to Druids would make Wizards a class that people want to group with more and more useful.

-Push Ports (teleports with y/n dialog box that send the target to the location but not the Wizard)
-Bigger blasts past level 50, so people won’t have to use AE Rains on single targets to be able to do damage
-Lower level Draught-type spells (without the mana efficiency) for that quick blast in an emergency
-Lower level Lure-type spells (Verant keeps adding EXTREMELY resistant creatures to the game even at levels lower than 44).

CONCEPTUAL DIFFICULTIES
These are issues with the concept and design of the Wizard class, where the idea behind the class or areas in it are troubled.

-UV spell is at highest level of all classes and self only (sight spells SPECIFICALLY mentioned in description of class and concept, why is this so?)
-Sight line (glimpse etc) have no actual effect, would be nice if these could be fixed
-Quick damage is supposed to compensate for downtime and no contribution without mana, does it? Is Wizard damage really faster and better in the short term, and is this a valid design concept, given the nature of groups now and aggro?
-Start out weak and grow to be the same power level as everyone else, valid concept?
-Planar Gates, we get 2 of the 5, one requires an expensive gem and one we don’t have much reason to go to. Why keep adding more that Wizards don’t port to?
-Why was the description of Wizards written the way it was, and what changed between its writing and now that required it to be altered and for it to not be true?
-Wizards have notable weaknesses
--no contribution without mana
--unable to assist group if over resist cap
--any other class can clear a dungeon of low greens without stopping
--related to above, helpless without mana (low greens easily kill)
Is the rest of the class design adequate compensation for these weaknesses?
-Gravity Flux at higher level than Enchanters, who get NO other AE spells.

WISH LIST
Just goodies we can dream about, nothing critical but hey, why not bring it up while you are paying attention?

-Sense monster line (gives basic information about monsters like if they have high resistances, their social tendencies (groups up, runs, etc), what kind they are (undead, summoned etc).
-Make Staff of the Wheel look distinct (Wizards have nothing like a pet etc to look unique)
-Eye of Zomm that can swim (present one has swimming of 1)
-Spell that lets Wizards see through any atmospheric effect as if it wasn’t there
-Spell that lets Wizards see underwater clearly (crocodile eye effect always).
-Pointy hat! (with a brim or the top bent over)
-Familiar (non combatant pet mostly for show)
-WK portal moved
-Ocean of Tears portal
-Low level skin spells (using low level reagent)
-spell that only interrupts casting, quick cast time
-spell that lets Wizards read any language
-spell that lets Wizards comprehend and speak any language
-tracking spell (have to recast each target, limited time period)
-bigger, powerful blasts with poor mana efficiency, low casting time and long recast time
-Guild Gate (Clerics got the idea from this-makes more sense for Wizards) ports you to your racial home guild
-Shadowstep type spell that ports you in random distance within 90 degrees of facing
-Magic Resist self buff
-Poison/Disease self buff upgrade.
-More Wizard quests (class with the fewest class-specific quests in the game, seekers of what?)
-More Wizard only items (see above).

Thank you for your time.
