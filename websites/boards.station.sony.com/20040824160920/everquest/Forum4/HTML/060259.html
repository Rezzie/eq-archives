Highest tier planar zone

i'll explain abit. I can't go into too much strategy detail or i'd be murdered.
WHen you zone into Time - you'll be in TimeA. From here you can zone into 5 different portals - each leading to different trials of Phase 1 in TimeB.
PoTimeB consists of 6 'phases'.Each phase consists of doing something within a certain time (essentially a trial) Phase 1 includes 5 trials, you can split your raid up into a few grps of 18 and do it fairly quick. Phase 2 is boring - just more trial type deals. Phase 3 consists of jumping down into a huge pit - pulling trash / and named mobs for a while. When they are all dead in the time allotted, 2 bigger nameds will spawn. Kill them, and click a portal to a big building (phase 4)which holds 4 gods. Tallon Zek, Vallon Zek, Terris Thule and Saryrn. Kill these four within the time allotted and then proceed to click into Phase 5. P5 is 4 more Gods. Rallos Zek, Bertox, Innoruuk and Cazic Thule. Kill these fellas then port into Quarms lair (p6)
Kill Quarm - then kill a targetable egg thing and Zeb wil spawn - do a dialogue, Druzzil will spawn, etc, and port you back to PoK.

PoTime -was- the 'endgame' until GoD was released recently, it requires a great deal of time and effort to gain access, though it is becoming easier as time goes by. LDoN augs and Gates AA's are further trivialising the PoP progression allowing 'lesser' guilds to gain access much faster than the first guilds who entered PoTime.

You can check Alla's Beastery here: http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?zone=178 and read the stuff people say about the zone and the gods. Usually easy to find spoilers and such, as well as having all of the gods' loot tables there for you to peruse.
