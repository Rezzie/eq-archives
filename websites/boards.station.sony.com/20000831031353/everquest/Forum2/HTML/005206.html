Abashi, a bit of info on "train size limits":

Yes, there's code in there that limits the number of mobs that will actively attack any one player at once. This is set to three mobs.

In the event that more than three mobs are agro'd on the player, the fourth and up mobs will either hang out at the edge of casting range, or simply run away.

The caveats are thus:

As soon as you start running, these three mobs are no longer "engaged" with you, and all creatures agro'd on the player will resume the chase, until the time where the player stops and again three mobs are beating on him.
