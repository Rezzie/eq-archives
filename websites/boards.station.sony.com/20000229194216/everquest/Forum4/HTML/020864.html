Meet Kushinata, a Wood Elf warrior of the 30th season. She stands holding the fruit of her labors, a fire totem, obtained after five full days of questing through the warrens of Solusek's Eye. Her heart leaps with joy, yet it is also saddened, for before her is the most common sight of all those she has beheld since she first took up a sword: The butt-crack of a goblin.

This particular butt-crack belonged, in life, to an Inferno goblin shaman, therefore it shares the properties unique to those of Inferno goblins generally--it is pink-ochre in color, glistens with a sheen of sweat, and pokes insolently above a tight yellow breachclout. On the other hand, it clearly shares the distinctive features of goblin butt-cracks throughout Norrath--It is tall and narrow; protruding yet deep and dangerous as the crevasses of Dagnor's Cauldron (a region known for its abundance of aqua goblin Mariner butt-cracks, Bosun butt-cracks, Shaman butt-cracks, and Tidal Lord butt-cracks)

/em Kushinata points at a Inferno goblin sh's corpse's butt-crack.

Do I hear you say "Enough with this butt-crack stuff already?" That's quite a coincidence, BECAUSE KUSHINATA IS SAYING IT TOO! And so am I. For crying out loud...

It wasn't like this when she was younger. Kushi was a happy young elf who loved the Crushbone orcs, and everything about them. She loved popping over a ridge and spotting them loafing by their squat little huts, loved the suspicious way they glanced about, loved the indignant "HURG! HURG!" sound they made as she taunted them, loved the perky "ur-WUR!" sound they made as she smacked 'em, and most of all loved the "HURR! ohhh..." sound they made when they finally went down, stubby legs a-flailing. Life was good, but all good things must come to an end. One day, even the Legionaires and Emissaries ran away after only a couple sword-strokes, and she knew she had to bid Crushbone farewell.

Kushi's face no longer smiles. It is care-worn and weary, for since that sad day her life has been an endless procession of goblin butt-cracks of every hue and texture: angry butt-cracks on the attack, and terrified butt-cracks fleeing down winding tunnels to fetch more, butt-cracks from above, and butt-cracks slipping beneath the waves, butt-cracks in the distance,and butt-cracks at a proximity and angle normally reseved to proctologists and parents of newborn infants. Every adventure is now for her a tale of woe whether it end in victory, with her foe doubled up on the ground, mooning her even in death; or if it end in defeat, as mighty Kushi founders beneath a furiously churning sea of butt-cracks.

Now, as she nears the peak of her career, the door of opportinity should be flung wide for her, yet consider how limited and distasteful her options: THREE full dungeons--Sol A, Perma, and Runnyeye--occupied almost exclusively by hot, cold and lukewarm gobs, respectively; Unrest, heavily populated with ghouls which are, on close inspection, little more than black gobs with asthma; Paw, filled with hairy psychotics who drop heavy loot she has to lug halfway across South Karana to sell; Cazic, which is similar to Paw except that its denizens are bald and religious, and she can forget about selling altogether and worry about running the nude biathelon from Arena when she dies; and Mistmoore, where she can do battle with... beautiful little dark elf girls who punch harder than monks and wear bikinis and tight leather chaps with, you guessed it, butt-panels in them.

There is a problem here. A subtle theme underlies what should be a varied and diverse menu of adventuresome challenge. Something alien, unnatural, and just plain wrong taints the field of battle before Kushi, like pineapple chunks on a pizza. Can't quite put your finger on it?

/em Kushinata points at a Inferno goblin sh's corpse's butt-crack.

Let's put subtlety aside. Verant, you need to cut back on all these damn gobs! How did these critters erect a civilization ranging from Dagnor's Cauldron to the Everfrost Peaks when they can't even pull their britches up? This is completely implausible, and it is high time that balance be imposed on the dungeon population of Norrath!

I demand the utter liquidation an entire tribe of goblins, and their replacement with wholesome, family-oriented orcs. Your best opportunity is Permafrost. Bald, skinny creatures don't last in the arctic anyhow. Have an "event" and tell everyone they all died of hypothermia. Replace 'em all with ice orcs. Have you guys seen the ice orcs? Know they exist? They're all wandering up and down a frozen river bank homeless and unemployed--it's a cryin' shame, I tell ya! They're big, they're buff, they're purple, they're hella cool, and they're decently clad in furs, as they should be! I want every last squealin' gob evicted from the lands north of Blackburrow and a healthy purple orc in his place by Saturday noon.

You have 90 hours, by my watch. Now, get cracking!
