NetProphet,

The other side of that coin is that many people would consider the item dropping to the ground a bug.

There are many possible solutions. (1) trade is cancelled and item returns to its original spot (btw, this is another exploit... as mentioned in passing by another poster, people will ask to see a Wurmslayer in a trade window, then hit "cancel" really fast hoping you don't have a main inventory slot free for it and it will fall). (2) item lands on pointer. (3) Trade window stays up, with a warning showing up in chat box saying "You must have a space available before you complete the trade."

One more thing, though, for the original poster... the "no warning" issue. It does, in fact, tell you "there is no place to put that! The item has fallen to the ground!" when you complete the trade. *You* would not have seen the message, but the customer would. granted, it's not much... but if the buyer claimed there was no message at all, they were undoubtedly part of the scam.

And as a general warning, arrange trades in /tells, not in /say. If people hanging around can know you're going to trade a Wurmslayer, you're more susceptible to this kind of scam.
