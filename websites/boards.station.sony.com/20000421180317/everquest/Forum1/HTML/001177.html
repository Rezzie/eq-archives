Easy peasy. =)

Just email (or see if you can catch them on the phones. Their hours are 11:30 am to 8:30 pm EST) them at accounts@station.sony.com and ask them for a password reset. Be sure to include your Station name, birthdate, zip code, and the email address you signed up with (just so they can verify). They'll probably also need the last eight digits of the card number you were subscribed with, since they're cracking down on security so not just anyone can get your password changed.
