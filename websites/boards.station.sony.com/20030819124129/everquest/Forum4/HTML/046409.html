Best bet: load your lowest DoT and hit the non-Charmed mob with it. That will make sure you get most of the XP.

Root the non-charmed mob. Make sure your Charmed pet barely wins. Then break charm and nuke him for 100% of the xp for that kill.

You get most of the xp from the first mob and ALL from your pet in the time it takes to get one kill, at the risk of having both come after you if Charm breaks.

Risky, but exciting.
