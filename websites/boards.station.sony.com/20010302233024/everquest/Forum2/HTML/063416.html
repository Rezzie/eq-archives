By quoting Alan out of context, the Ranger post represented Alan as saying that Druids had tracking raised in this most recent patch because they asked to have tracking raised.

What Alan meant (and what should be clear from context) was that Druid tracking skill was previously kept lower than Bard skill (e.g., 50 cap vs. 100 cap) at the request of the Druid community. If you follow the history, Druids had a cap of 20, which is barely enough to see the MOB hitting you in the face. Verant wanted to raise the cap to make Druids better; given the way that tracking worked, Druids asked for it to be kept low.

In other words, Verant wanted it to be good before, so they kept it low. Now that they've added the ability to scroll, they wanted it to be as relatively good as it was before, so they're raising the cap. Raising the CAP was NOT done at the request of the Druid community at this time.

It's an out-of-context quote.
