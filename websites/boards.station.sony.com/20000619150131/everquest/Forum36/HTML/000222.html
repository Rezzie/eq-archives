First off:

There are level limits for pk'ing. You can only fight those up to 8 levels above or below yours.

Second off:

They cannot institute a respectable looting system, because their character and item system is too weak to support it. Were they to improve that system, then perhaps intelligent looting systems can be considered.

Third off: Because the item system is weak, people try to make arguments about how "no loot" equals "more twinks" to justify their desire for a looting system. This is misplaced blame for sure, because if the item system were better designed, low level twinks could be "coded against" resolving that issue and leaving looting to be "fixed" based on it's own merits.

Finally:

If you want to come to Tallon or go to Vallon, just start a new character over there. Not only will you be able to keep your current character where he/she is at(if you so desire), but you will get to meet friends as you level up and experience the challenges that each individual server presents to varying levels. You will also avoid any harassment by people who think that although you earned X equipement on one server, you have not done the same on the other and therefore don't deserve it.

My take and advice...
