Howdy folks. I've been toying with the UI changes to what we can do via socials. The following are some that I've found helpful; I'm posting them for others to review, and to troll for other ideas.

----General Pointers----
-I like to begin them with /sit off because I spend a prodigious amount of time meditating.
-Similarly, /book 4 is what I use to go back to meditating. Page 4 is where I keep "useful forever but not always" spells like Invisiblity & Gate.
-I prefer using the F2-F6 keys to target groupmembers, most of these assume I've done that before I hit the social.

#1 -- MezIt
/sit off
/g Attempting to mez %t
/cast 4
--------Slot 4 is where I always keep my single-target mesmerize spell. Having /sit off at the beginning allows me to keep a mob targetted, sit down to meditate, and then hit this hotkey if I need to re-mez the same mob.

#2 -- NukMez
/sit off
/g Casting AE Mez, be prepared for the worst
/cast 5
------AE Mez has saved me more times than it has killed me, but I've found the party is quicker to react if they know there's a risk I'm about to mez myself.

#3 -- War Cry
/sit off
/assist [tankname]
/pet attack
/say It's %t like you that have spoiled your own lands!
/cast 1
------Actually, I'm stealing this from a necromancer friend of mine. He changes the [tankname] part regularly.

#4 -- PillOn
/assist [tankname]
/g Hey! %t is attacking [tankname]!
------Surprisingly useful hotkey for secondary tanks. By announcing to the group who the proper victim is, it helps *me* figure out who to mez.

#5 -- ToArms
/sit off
/say Justice demands your death, %t !
/cast 2
/pause 40
/attack
--------- Been using this one with my Shaman when he solos or pulls; slot 2 is a DoT. The pause (which I'm still experimenting with the #) is for the casting time.

#6 -- Buff
/g Recharging Buff for %t
/pause 40,/cast 2
--------- Slot 2 is Clarity for my enchanter; I also use a social /cast 3 for Augmentation. I can see where the /pause 40,/cast # could be used repeatedly for several bufss for the proper classes.

Most needed UI improvement -- a way to target group members (ala f2-f6) in an emote.

I'd be very interested in hearing what works for others.

Station Member 		posted 11-09-2000 10:40 PM           
JoeFark,
Northerner almost had it right. a /pause must be the first thing on the line, and if used with something else, separated by a comma:
/pause 40, /cast 5

But it will execute AFTER the main command.

So you need
/pause 40, /cast 5
/cast 6

And you're good to go.
