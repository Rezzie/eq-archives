About getting off of Odus. Forget the Odus to Faydwer boat. It's not needed.

Instead have a tunnel system under Erudin like that under Freeport and Qeynos. The Kerrans could get out and the evil races could get in. Several races benefit. It should be fairly tricky to use but should be doable if you know your stuff.

If you suggest base Kerran stats over 100 I'd say you are going too far unless you want to also suggest about a 30% experience penalty or something.

If Kerrans are KoS everywhere then give them good stats. If they get along with both good AND evils (except Erudites and maybe Iksar) then their stats can't be very good at all. In short, the better their stats, the worse their faction.

I'd give them high agility (cats being agile), not particularly high wisdom and perhaps relatively average for other stats.

It might be best to not let them be monks. The old time human monks are still trying to cope with Iksar monks. Adding Kerran monks might send them into convulsions.

Making a playable race is a LOT of work. Textures for everything they can possibly wear; a city with all the necessary guilds, merchants, and quests; models (they can't use a 3 year old low poly model anymore then the Iksar could use the old lizardman model, they'll need a new one); additional racial detail (male and female Kerrans should look different); LOTS of testing for balance and bugs; and probably additional recial details such as special weapons, histories, whatever.

I personally would be a little surprised if they introduced another playable race this late but you never know.
