Absor
Station Member 		posted 10-25-2000 01:16 PM           
Let me try to clarify (or understand) the reason for this no-exp switch idea. As a member of a small and infrequently gathering guild, we had a problem. Some of our members played a lot more than others. So when we did meet our levels were so disparate that we had trouble finding combat things to do, and mostly spent our time playing hide and seek (try it, it's fun).

Those that couldn't play enough to keep up with the rest, didn't play enough do to real-world things that were not malleable. So the only recourse for this problem was to ask the frequent players to stop playing their guild characters as often. But they loved playing their guild characters, and not playing them meant having less fun with the game.

This toggle would help those people. They could play like mad. They could help strangers and not worry about accidentally gaining experience. They could hunt up items for the group without gaining experience. They could play the game without losing the ability to play with their guild (replace 'guild' with 'spouse', 'relatives' or 'friends' as needed).

Having a character commit suicide to lose levels wasn't something that would ever occur to us. It's not a particularly in-character thing to do.

This toggle would probably only help a very small portion of the EverQuest population. So it may not be something that we do. I haven't brought this idea to anyone, and I may not be able to any time soon. But it's on my list.

Alan

Absor
