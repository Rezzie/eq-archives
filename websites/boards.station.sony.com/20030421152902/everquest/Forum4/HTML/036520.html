You have to change it in the "chat" room that you'll find on the server selection screen after logging onto EQ.

Here's the syntax for changing your chat handle:

#rename gatorstang23 <newname>

#rename gatorstang23 gatorstang24

It takes a couple days to take effect on these boards but the it changes immediately online.
