So if you don't want free phat lewt, the best thing you can do is back away when I try to inspect you. I'll go give the uber weapon to the newbie next to you who DOESN'T think he's too good for me to inspect.

6) I repeat, on a blue server, the only clue you are being inspected is the message. You are overreacting to a silly message. It does not mean he's got his hand down your pants. It does not mean he's checking all of your clothing labels. It means he is trying to determine what equipment you might have equipped, which he would have been able to do just from visual identification if EQ supported the graphics necessary in the first place.
