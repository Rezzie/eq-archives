The Rathe is one of the less-explained demi-gods of Norrath. The Official EQ sites have less than no information about it, but from what I recall and have read, The Rathe are a group of spirits responsible for all things relating to earth. They apparently are fairly powerful, as they cursed the Fire and Ice giants, causing them to become made of their elements, and losing all intelligence. The Giants in Velious were unaffected by The Rathe's (Probably incorrect pluralization, but YOU try it when you don't know WHAT you're pluralizing) curses, and therefore have a culture, intelligence, and their native forms.
