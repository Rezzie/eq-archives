The closest that you come to mixing trade skills is that some trade skills require items from other trade skills. An example is baking a pie. A baker does bake the pie, but it requires a pie tin which must be made by a blacksmith. And in order for the blacksmith to make the pie tin, they need a ceramic pie liner from a potter. As you see, it involves 3 different crafts to make one item. I don't know if this is the same as what you are saying, but I hope this helps.

Lady Loliana is correct; however, not to nitpick, potters can make pie tins without smithing; however, muffin tins will require metal bits (which are easy to make).

Potters also require named Skins (considered a unuseable raw material for tailors) to make vials.

Tailors require metal studs and steel boning from smithing. Heady Kiola made from Brewing to make cured silk armor.

So for those that are closer to mastering their tradeskill er hobbies, many of the easier recipes are non-dependent however, as you become challenged you will find need to dabble in other skills too.
