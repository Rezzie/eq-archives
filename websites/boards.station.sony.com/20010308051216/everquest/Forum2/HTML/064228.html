It has been said that many of the greatest guilds to enter EQ are fed up with the many recent changes, from Agro to the multiple nerfs that have hit the cleric class (mok, Bp, Bp, Bp, Hate, and now new PoG Bp).

What we have are the Guilds like FoH, LoS, AL and other Uber guilds all about to have the bulk of there members walk away from this game. Am I being extream, not at all, many have made posts that there members are dropping like flys. FoH has reported that they are on the outs with Verant.

This is not a flame, this is just a heads up. Yes EQ will survive, the Uber Guilds are only 5-10% of the game population but the thing is.
The bulk of the game changes and balancing over the past 2 years has come on the backs of Guilds like FoH that have basicly tested the game and had dev teams follow them on raids and new zones (see VP or even first Vox Raids etc).

So lets say the bulk of the "Uber Guilds leave" now we have a game that will take longer for problems and mechanics to be solved. The people us newer players look up to will be few and far between, yes even though you might not like a guild the fact they can kill a rare dragon etc is something to be in awe of.

The game economy will suffer as medium quallity gear now becomes more uber because the guilds that get the best will no longer be feeding the world items they have no use for.

People will assume that since the best are leaving the game might just not be that good.
If you dont think this is the case see past PC titles.

Do I think Verant should cave in and do everything it can to keep uber guilds happy not at all. However I do think that by nerfing or making things to easy or hard from them will effect every one of us down the road.

Personally despite what many feel about Uber guilds and people like Furor in demi god status as a player and character. We need to understand that without them we are far worse then it may seem.

Just some thoughts.
Good luck to all the big guilds that are awaiting the next couple of patches I certainly hope there is open comunication with Dev to find some balance in the changes that will be acceptable for both sides.

If not its been a blast hearing about your triumps and failures.

Not a Uber character or a power game
