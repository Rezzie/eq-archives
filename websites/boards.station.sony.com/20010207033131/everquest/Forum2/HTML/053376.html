Take a given warrior. Call him wurl.

Wurl hits a mob for 2 damage per second with weapon A. Wurl hits a mob for 3 damage per second with weapon B.

Now, by this simple comparison, you would think that weapon A must be a whole lot better. Unfortunately, thats not the whole picture.

Suppose weapon A hits 10 times a minute for the damage it does (yea, i know, by these numbers the weapons suck, but im tryin to keep it simple). Suppose weapon B hits 50 times a minute to do its damage...

If your goal is to taunt, weapon B is FAR BETTER than weapon A.

If your goal is outright damage, by all means, weapon A all the way.
