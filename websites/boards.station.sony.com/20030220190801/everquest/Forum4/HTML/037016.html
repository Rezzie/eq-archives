OK, high quality fish bones are a player made item

Using that page from EQTraders, we see it requires a tackle box, a filleting knife, an appropriate fish, and that the skill trivials somewhere between 181 and 200 fishing skill.

Checking out some of the fish listed on the page, by using the fish location link at the top of the page, we see that Wreary Wrass can be gotten in Sleepers Tomb, Red Roughy from Western Wastes, and Wulf Fish from Karnors.

So, you need to be of a fairly advanced age and fishing skill both to get the bones yourself.

Not sure on the smithing skill to make the darts or the knife. Most knives are fairly low skill around 35 or so.
