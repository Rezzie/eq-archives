Ok, here's the problem.

I did some more testing.

If it begins to snow in Dreadlands, and you cast Wake of Karana, it *appears* to have no effect.

I have two different machines with two different kinds of graphics cards, one has a Voodoo2, and one has an ATI RAGE Pro. The fog/snow does not go away on *either* machine when I cast this spell.

BUT!

If I zone or camp on one machine, then come right back, it is a bright sunny day on *that* machine. The other machine still sees snow/fog.

So, the bug is that it does not update the client properly.

The server thinks it is no longer snowing, and any people who zone in after you cast the spell see only sunshine. But people who were in the zone when you cast, including yourself, get no benefit from this, unless you all camp out and come back.

Definitely a bug, since the server recognizes the change, but the client does not.
