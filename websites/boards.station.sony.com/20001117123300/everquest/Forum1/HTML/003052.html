Assuming you're running under Windows 9x or ME, 96 megabytes of RAM is fine. Go for a video card upgrade. Get a card using the Geforce 2 MX if you want to go the budget route, or a full Geforce 2 GTS based board if you've got cash to burn on higher end performance.
