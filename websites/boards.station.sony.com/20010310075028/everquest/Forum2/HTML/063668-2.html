Cabriolet --

I do the same, give them the old /d trick. Here's my favorite:

My rogue is up in Kelethin on the flot between Newbie Lift and POD Lift (near Bard guild). She's sporting a dragoon dirk and a scout dagger. Some 2nd level ranger comes up to me and says "Can you give me some money?"

I looked around, and said "Excuse me?"

"I need some money, do you have extra PP"

I looked again (for guards, hehe) and said "You're joking, right?"

Long story short, he was told to target me and hit /d and when he did I started stabbing him with both hands... of course when he turned to run I Backstabbed him with great satisfaction.

The beauty? In his panic he RAN off the flot, ending the duel with his unfortunate demise, and I didn't even have to suffer a faction hit.

Afterward, he /t 'ed me, complaining that I ruined the game for him. I told him I hated beggars, and if he wanted a rogue's money, he better be prepared to take it by force.

He said "How was I supposed to know you were a rogue, this isn't my regular character!?"

I let him know that I was not anon, and he could have /who'ed me. I also pointed out that the duel-wielded piercers were a really good sign. And with that, I went on my merry way!

I have to say, its not so much that people beg off me (although I wish people would figure out that, while we are the fastest class on Norrath, BARDS CANNOT SOW), its the air entitlement that these geeks effect, like "Hey you should give it to me!" rather than "Would you give it to me?"
