Alright now... Rallos Zek is a PvP server. We're clear on that, right? Yes, the option to duke it out with another player adds to the roleplay experience. Someone jacks you in a trade? You can retaliate with physical violence. Some one follows you around all night kill stealing? Roast 'em. Someone starts talking crap to you and offers a challenge? Good, hack the piss out of them. But I see none of this on the so called PvP server. I see people on this board writing about duelling and fair fights, standing your ground instead of running from PKs or giving up... BAH! I have NEVER been challenged to anything LIKE a fair fight by a PK. I have lost faction and been killed by guards for defending myself. People, GROW UP. If I was to be attacked and had anything like a chance to fight back, even if I DID die, you wouldn't hear a word out of me. BUT... You know when PKs go after people? When they're down to one bubble of life and they're trying to bandage themselves and can barely move. They come looking for that LD guy, or the AFKs. THAT is not PKing, that is just plain cowardly stupidity. If you're so big and bad and tough, then why should you have to attack someone that a YK has already just about killed. Why should you have to go searching for that LD guy who can't even begin to defend himself? There has been one... ONE guy who attacked my friend the other night and won, but my friend faught back and actually did a good bit of damage, but he still lost. The PK looted him, of course, but then you know what he did? He sat down beside me and asked me to thank my friend for being a good sport, and for his time. He said he'd like to have a rematch when my friend got stronger, to give him a chance to reclaim his property. I was flabbergasted, but impressed. This guy had style, and more balls than all the other PKs I've ever encountered. He gave warning before he attacked, he didn't come slithering up behind us while we were half dead or medding, attacking from behind. He gave a chance to actually prepare to defend ourselves...
There is a difference, a DISTINCT difference, betweet PKing for the Role Play value, and PKing to be a general nuisance and all around pain in the ass S.O.B. Most people I believe come to this server for the former, but saddly end up with the latter.
So don't sit there telling me "Well it's a PvP server blah blah blah you're going to get attacked..." I EXPECTED Player vs. Player. Not Jerknuts Moron Naked Caster vs. Low HP Low Mana Defenseless Individual.
That's not roleplay, of fun, or in the spirit of the adventure of anything LIKE it. It's rude, inconsiderate STUPIDITY. Get a life, get some balls, and play the game like it was meant to be played.
