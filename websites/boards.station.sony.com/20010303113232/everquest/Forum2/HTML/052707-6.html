-Velious zones feedback-
.Overall feedback.
1. Low xp ratio on mobs for mobs over 50 plus, possibly untended as the raid concept is expanded.
2. Varying Risk vs Reward on majority of zone from Great like Iceclad,EW,Thurgadin,WW,PoG to Complete Crap like Sirens Grotto,Kael Drakken , Velketor.
3. Excellent Icecandy in textures,new mobs, landscape and some wonderful quests / scripted events.
4. Alot of Broken quests , Unfinished quests, Unfinished zones all add to the idea that SoV was rushed to beat the upcoming releases of other MMorpgs
5. Increased mob HPs on a general scale , Increased MR , Increased damage in line with the "raid" concept. Underconned mobs aplenty.
6. Increased Mudflation mob stats lead to marginalisation of majority of classes due to lack of effectiveness in mob encounters. ( what happen to the dependency? Encouraged class variation? ).
7. Increased Faction situation. Leading to unbalanced risk/reward situations. IE Skyshrine faction seems to be the least useful as equivalent loot from SS mobs and SS based faction mobs does not exist as questable or equivalently gathered . As the result many guilds choose against SS and there is no balance or fairness or reward in not choosing against.
8. Wildly varying Loot rewards vs risk, IE Difficulty of Dain vs Tormax or Yelinek killing, Equivalent Loot from Kunark seems superior overall in many cases for less risk. Many items seem to be too heavy or less useful than expected. IE Black Blade of Torment, 1 handed sword with average stats and a good proc but weights 10.0, etc etc. OTOH alot of loot is balanced is some cases given risks vs reward IE necropolis items etc. Majority of Loot is however poor including many Boss drops like From Velketor, Yelinek etc.


-Zone Feedback-
1. Iceclad
Zone in to Velious, made to seem vast and inhabited. But the Majority of the zone is a great big waste of server space and resources. Casual Mobs ( not humanoid or tied to the backdrop story ) do not drop cash . In comparison the majority of equivalent kunark mobs drop cash and are generally weaker to the velious. Nice quests,Great scripting, Lodizal and rare spawns add a spice to the zone. Dervishes designed to kill newbies present and often serve as a deterrent to exploring.

2. Tower of Frozen Shadow.
As described earlier all the mobs are generally underconned in terms of difficulty and level. The majority of items dropped partly to encourage appropriate lvl PCs to come, does not . Higher lvl chharacters come hereonce and seldom return. The top lvls challenge PCs greater than the appropriate lvls while resulting in poor loot reward. The necessary collection of 8 keys to advance which are dropppd by random rare spawns also prevent casual exploring. The 8 keys are also soulbound which will explain why many PCs do not bother. All in all, its a waste of a zone.Cant we cobine the keys eventually and when u receive the top key be allowed access to all the tower rather than fumble with 8 soul bound keys??

3. Eastern wastes.
Generlly considered one of the more sucessful zones in velious. General mobs do not drop coin, only the orcs/giants do. Orc camp and various giant, dorf and ulthork areas as well as appropriate lvl drops make this a popular hunting area. Excellent quests/ scripted events exist here. This zon would eb more popular if the animals in this zone ( like many in velious ) dropped items which were more useful generally to the players of eq. Having Low Quality,Medium Quality and high quality skins/pelts which for msot part cannot be used by tailors, or having no coin or low value pieces do not encourage widespread use. Instead much of the mob turnover is directed at the ORcs in the zone. Excessive mobflation of hps and resistances in comaprison to Kunark lvl mobs mean underutilisation.

5. Great Divide
Nice zone, with problems in animal creatures as in EW. Tizmaks, Shardwurms also drop individual type of armor which franky is seldom useful at the lvl gathered. Even twinks turn their noses at them. MObflation evident here.

6. Thurgadin/Icewell Keep.
Problems with doorway size, increased mobflation, varying reward vs risk all is balanced against great scripted encounters and generally a complete city for PCs. Unfortunately alot of quests remain broken, risk vs reward for oppsing the dorfs is generally good but increased MR, HPs and damage by NPCs means many classes are even more dependant on the core classes to survive. IE it would be fun if if all the various classes was able to contribute to the sucess of a raid in Icewell keep.

7. velketor
Another zone which mob are underconned and lack of risk vs reward management. A mob who quads for 850, has tons and tons of HPs, estimated at 200k plus, should not drop items which cannot be pawned off to the twinks. Kobolds are incredibly underconned, being extremely MR, 5 times the Hps of a same lvl mob in kunark and fights like a lvl 55 mob, yet is considered green to a 60.

8. Crystal caverns
Beautiful zone, bad pathing issues, nice risk vs reward ratio. One way zone makes life unnecessary difficult.

9. Kael Drakken.
Easy access, great quests, some good risk vs reward drops. But generally this zone is a bad one. Named mobs which are even tougher than expected drop very little reward for the risk. Some of the toughest mobs in the game are here , yet their drop is pathetic. Faction problems occur as well since The high lvl Tormax faction can be garned by killing mobs in Icewell but Dain faction nor Yelinek faction is not returned Vice Versa. Many top guild have sided with the KD gaints since the reward is substantially greater / easier then with the dwarves or dragons.

10 Wakening
One of the ugliest zones around, terrible Frame Rate from even the BEST Hardware computers resulting from the abundance of trees, lvl 60 mobs wander around, low reward vs risk in most cases.

11. PoG
Considered by many great zone others call it too easy. Often this cry is reflected by the unbalanced drops and who has gotten them. Personally i think this is a great zone, individual faction gripes aside. However Drop loot vs quested loot is unbalaned as the drop loot seems easier and equally rewarding, yet quested is harder to obtain /shrug

12. Skyshrine
Again risk vs rewrd is out of whack as well as difficulty of faction gathering. ( compared to giant faction ). Many unfinished and broken quests. Underconned mobs. No equivalent quest vs loot drops available.

13. Cobalt scar
Great zone, but mobflation is evident here. Genrally not a much used zone due to the lack of named drops with the one or 2 exceptions.

14. Sirens grotto
100% voted by all as the worst zone in the game hands down. Insane mob power coupled with design and pathing issues as well as possibly the worst risk vs reward ratio in the game makes this easily the crap of velious. Worst, the designer who made this obviously was a sadist in making this zone a thruway to enter the deeper zone in velious. Mst high lvl parties just pass thru here as quickly as possible. Uber guild seeking a challenge avoid this death trap.

15. Western waste
like Eastern waste is one of the top zones in the game.

16. Necropolis
Another top zone with great risk vs reward. However genral zone bugs like pathng, traps not being disarmed and general niggles like mobflation make this still a great zone tho.

17. Temple of Veeshan
Top zone with pathing and reward issues like many zones in Velious.

18. PoM
Zone is unfinished, enuf said..

19. Sleepers Tomb
No access yet...
