Welcome Golden Wolf to the walk in the shadows!
I'm pretty new myself and my Necro is a human who's never been anywhere near Neriak. But I can give you some very basic fundamental advice to get you to level 8 or so solo.
Levels 1-4 are basically hack and slash with a good amount of your lifetap spell thrown in. Con your intended target, blacks and blues are it even with your pet, yellows are pretty much impossible. Your level 1 pet is very weak and is useful mainly for practicing pet control and commands. A good spell for finding bonechips is "Sense the Dead". It will guide you to decaying skeletons (well anything undead).
Level 4 get "Leering Corpse" asap. The level 4 pet is alot tougher than the level 1 pet.
Level 4-8 basically hack and slash with a good amount of your lifespike spell thrown in
You will die much less often tho and your pet is strong enough with a rusty battle axe or so to allow you to kill yellows. The "Fear" spell is very useful at this level to help you escape a losing battle. If your losing, fear the mob then zone or run to the guards.
