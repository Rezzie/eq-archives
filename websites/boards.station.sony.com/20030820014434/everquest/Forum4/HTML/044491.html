A few nice features in DAoC housing are:

* Sharred vault with all your alts
* Your own merchant that you can stock up with things to sell to other players at any time (even while offline)
* You can bind there
* You can have "portal merchants" that allow you to go to many convenient places around the world from your home
* You can have your own tradeskill tools in your house (like a forge, alchemy table, etc.)
* And all the fluff stuff like decorations, etc.
