The idea is there, but not completely cooked ...

Yes, it's hard to test everything when you have to take a character and beef it up and beef it up and beef it up, suffering the item wipes, etc.

The problem is that every change made affects every player, possibly every level, and hopefully doesn't break something in the process.

Everyone wants a powerful character, at least one equal to the work they've done elsewhere (if they plan to test). What if everyone did that, there would just be a bunch of people playing level 60's checking out areas they hadn't been to before (just one extreme example).

The rules have to be the same for the players on the Test Server as a regular server. Also note there used to be a problem with playing the same character (namewise) on 2 different servers.

Currently, I do not know why they will not allow people to duplicate a character they have on another server for purposes of testing (especially if the testing is about to change the way they play their main characters).

Anyway, just my commentary. Feel free to ignore it.
