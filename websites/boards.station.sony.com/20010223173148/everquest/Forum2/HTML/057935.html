...as related to animal faction since someone brought this subject up on another thread:

Definition of druid according to Official strategy guide:

"The druid is the original earth-child. You just haven't seen a tree hugged until you discuss deforestation with a druid. They get their power from directly tapping into nature, so they have views on its sanctity. They are so in tune with nature that most animals won't attack a druid unless the druid attacks first."

Ok...now force a druid to play like a druid as per definition.

1. Most animals will not attack unless provoked, therefore an attack on ANY non-hostile animal
leads to bad faction with ALL other animals.

2. For the druids who ignore their call to nature and continue to destroy their faction, link animal faction directly to all the NPC druids who are placed at the stone ring portal sites.

3. Druid guilds will not sell nor train druids that have left the way of nature.

How to restore faction? Go hunt monsters that defile nature. When you kill an orc, it should reflect that:

Your Faction with CrushboneOrcs went down.
Your Faction with AnimalKind went up.

This will inject some more difficulty and roleplaying into the druid class which will be felt among those starting out. Perhaps then we won't have so many druids out there when it's not such an easy and carefree road to travel. There will be trade-offs to playing the class.

Acid
