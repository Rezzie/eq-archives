Well, hello all, once again! As promised, I am starting yet another thread. I challenge all of you to join with me in my Quest (heehee) to make this board active and fun!

What I wanted to discuss today was some of the best, funniest, most interesting names you've seen on the Innoruuk server. Granted, I would be the first to discourage non-rp names, and if I saw a newbie with Luke or Leia as their name I would certainly bring it to their attention, but there are times when you see a name that's so funny or classic, you just can't help finding it amusing!

I dont want to give all my examples right off the bat, as I wnat other people to contribute as well, but, here's a couple that I have seen that cought my attention.

Firstly, and I mention this one because he reads this board, I would submit Leadar, because his last name is Whichwaytheygo. The first time I saw that name in-game, a long time ago, I cracked up. It was a classic.

Then there was the gnome I saw a few times in Butcherblock, or perhaps it was a halfling, that would make more sense, but anyway his first name was Squirt. Not too funny by itself, until you find that his last name was Gunn. Squirt Gunn. I laughed for a good solid 10 minutes on that one!

I have others but I'll save them. Now, my dears, don't let me down, post here! Tell us what you've seen that struck you as interesting.
