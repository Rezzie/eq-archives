Iksar/Troll regen is no big deal at all for shaman. Lets work it out at level 50 say...

Barb mana regen = 25 per tick (meditate skill) + 3 per tick (just for sitting) + 1.5 per tick (cannibalizing normal HP regen) + 5 per tick (cannibalizing off regen from chloroplast spell) = 34.5 mana per tick

Troll mana regen = 25 per tick (meditate skill) + 3 per tick (just for sitting) + 3 per tick (cannibalizing troll HP regen) + 5 per tick (cannibalizing off regen from chloroplast spell) = 36 mana per tick

Hmm 36 per tick vs. 34.5 per tick = 4.3% bonus, assuming they are able to sit the whole time to get the full benefit (standing regen is only different by 1HP per tick). This justifies a 15% higher penalty how?

With all the disadvantages Ogres and Trolls have (see above), they should get a bonus if anything, not a penalty.
