At the bottom line.. higher level battles are won by mana. Clerics must heal the tanks who are keeping the clerics and other casters from taking damage. The economy of it is.... Have mana group survives... run out of mana group flee's or dies. I suspect this is to keep players on par with the content that is suitable for someone of their level.... IMHO it would cheapen the game if a cleric could just load up on mana potions and never run dry regardless of how heavily he / she was casting. WOuld make the group much more powerful than it actually is. Although KEI, flowing thought, clarity, bard mana songs, etc greatly enhances a casters ability it is still possible to run a caster dry of mana if the battle is big enough. If the caster had a huge supply of mana potions in addition to those.. well things would just become too easy and unbalanced. 0.02

Ways of getting mana back:

Sit down and wait - basic meditating

Get an enchanter to cast clarity (or better) on you.

Have a bard sing one of the songs that replenishes mana.

Necromancers can cast a spell that transfers some of their mana to you.

Use a modulating rod (1 charge) or rod of mystical transvergence (3 charges) summoned by magicians - exchanges health for mana.

If you are rich enough and can find one, buy a manastone (similar to mod rod, but has unlimited charges). No longer drops in the game.

I heard there were mana potions that shaman can make, but never seen one.

Thought there was also some item that dropped of mobs that could be used to replenish mana (some sort of spore, 1 charge), but would need to check on this.

Obtain equipment that has Flowing Thought focus on it - these increase your meditation rate.

Spent points in the AA skill Mental Clarity to increase mana regain rate.


There's probably others, but those are the ones I can think of off-hand!
