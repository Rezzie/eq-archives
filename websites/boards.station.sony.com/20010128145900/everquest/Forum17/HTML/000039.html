I'm starting this thread on all the server message boards for people to describe their server to the outside world. If you don't see this thread in the front page of your server board, chances are, not enough people from YOUR server are visiting this site, and your server issues are probably not getting dealt with as a result. Please, no bumps, only descriptions of the server by it's inhabitents.

If you are looking for ideas of what the rest of the world might be interested in about your server here are a few starter topics:

1) Birthday of your server (when did it go live)
2) The fan site you go to most often to get current info and gossip about your server
3) Your guild, some of it's history
4) Who you think the "power" guilds of your server are.
5) What is the most valuable item you've bought or sold for platinum, and what was the price? (actual plat changing hands, not "equivalent value")
6) what zone is the bazaar zone of your server? what zone do people go to to advertise long lists of items and actually expect a result? What zone is the one that if you hunt in it, you need to turn the auc channel off?
7) What character has any particular gm event artifact on your server, if you know (butcherblock hammer, shield of qeynos, etc...)
8) the class/race/level/guild of the characters you play on the server.

Please write your reply's to people not of your server thinking about which server to choose. Make them advertisements or warnings, as you see fit.
