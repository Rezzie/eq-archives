There IS an answer to your problem.

If you look in the display options, there is a setting named "Screens Same Resolution". Set his to off. It will cause your Full-Screen mode to be whatever resolution you're currently set at, while your Frames (for lack of a better word) mode will be set at 640x480. You can then click through all of the resolution settings until you get the one you want, then use F10 to switch over to the Full Screen view.

There is a drawback to this: If you open a trade window it'll flip you back to the framed screen, with a noticeable delay of 2-3 seconds due to the video card having to change modes and your monitor compensating for the new synch rate.
If you can live with that, then you're set.
