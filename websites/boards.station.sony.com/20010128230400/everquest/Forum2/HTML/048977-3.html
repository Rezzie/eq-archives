I started within the first week EQ was retail. My first char was an attempt to recreate my D&D fighter. So I created a dwarven warrior, dumped all my str points into him, the rest to stamina, and went out into the world.

Butcherblock had hideously low rates of anything that dropped cloth armor, or even cash, and by the time I was level 7, I was hanging out at the crossroads without even a half suit of cloth. Needless to say, I was pretty much a chewtoy for stuff at the crossroads, so I said, sc**w this, I'm starting an ogre warrior!

So I start a ogre warrior, maxxed strength, and I remember some fun, if stupid, stuff we did. This was still within the first month of release, mind you.

Around level 10 or so, we all decided (my regular evening group of ogres) we'd go kill this spectre thing that was sitting on a hill. It was tough, but so were we, and we outnumbered it. WHAM WHAM WHAM, we were in serious trouble, and we knew it quickly. One guy died right there on the hill underneath the spectre's spot. He had to run in, lead it away, /corpsing, die, do that several times until it was far enough the spec wouldn't reagro.

Shortly thereafter, that same guy (a SK) found the hole in the wall that leads to the big portal to the plane of fear. At that time, fear hadn't opened, yet, but we didn't know that.

He and I stripped at the bank and ran there, and he cast invis vs. undead on us and we went in. We made it all the way to the big portal room, but there was this Cindi woman there, and she'd kill us because we were ogres. Clearly invis vs. undead wouldn't work on a living woman. What to do?

Well, we hatched a plan. We'd both run at the portal from different directions, and one of us would make it through! We started running, she went after him, and I leaped -- boing! The portal didn't let me through, but bounced me back out. Then I died to one of Cindi's pets.


So from that we learned spectres guarded portals.


Four levels later, at level 14, we were in Oasis at the beach fighting alligators. There was this island with a tower and spectres all over it. OBVIOUSLY there must be another portal to another plane in there. No one wanted to try, though.

One day this high level group (they had last names, for god's sake) came juggernauting down the beach. One was an ogre who stopped for about 10 seconds and looked at our ogre party, said nothing, and ran on.

What a God he was! He was head to toe in golden bronze plate armor! OMG, I'll bet that group just came thundering out of that plane portal! What adventures they must be having! Some day I will go in there, and back in Feerott's, too! My god, the excitement they must experience!
