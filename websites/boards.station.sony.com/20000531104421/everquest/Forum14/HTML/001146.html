Hm, well 2h blunt is the easiest weapons to get, but as you get higher its 1h slash. I used a 2h blunt first, but swapped later on to 1h slash. So put skill points in these.

Druids can use other items, but its mainly misc. stuff such as: froglok crown, azure sleeves, splitpaw hide gloves, chrysoberyl talisman, bcp, dwarven workboots, golden efreeti boots etc.

But as a newbie i guess leather is mainly the stuff youll use.

Stats wise I would suggest this:
Put as about 20 points into Wisdom
The rest Agility and a bit of str.
Strength is not that big an issue but one or two points won't hurt you. Since druids can a get a str buff this will come in handy if you get encumbered.
Most important thing is wisdom and then agility to help you avoid being hit.

Stats:
All you can do into wisdom. The rest is free cause the effect you will not notice.
Skills:
Use points for the caster skills, as soon as you get a useful spell. (example Harmony)
Focus on 2h blunt and 1h slash. You will use 1h slash around the 30s or maybee from level 1 when twinking. But the best weapons (from Dragons and Gods) are 2h blunt.
Armor/Equipment:
Leather is best until you get special items.
Keep your eye on wisdom, try to max it to 200.
