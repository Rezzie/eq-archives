I play on Stromm but I am to high to group with you. Some tips though.

o Go to Butcher Block and you'll find a group.

o Until about level 28 you can do normal with no real difference in adv pts so I would only do normal to begin with. When you feel comfortable switch to hard to get the better xp and loot.

o The only difference over static dungeons or pulls is that you will move after so many pulls to get more.

o Kill bitten victims (creaking crates/hollow trees) before a round of pulls. Some yield bonus xp, improved mana/HP regen but some will pop nasty mobs. Doing this will allow the chanter to use stuns and AoE mez to control adds without setting off a trap.

o Beware of boxes. Curiosity killed the cat (it was a rogue that decided he could disarm the trap but instead the trap went off slaughtering the group - damn Val Shir).

o AoE spells, including mez and stun will set boxes/chests. Pull to a location where there are none.

o Get an Adventure stone.

o Several items only drop or purchasable from certain LDoN themes. Tak tends to be plate armor, MMC cloth armor and some one else will fill in the rest. Find out what items you want from LDoN and adventure at the theme(s) that have em.

All times are PT (US)
