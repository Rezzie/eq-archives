Greetings Absor, and thank you for taking the time to examine PvP, as alot of PvPers feel like the 3 PvP servers are under-rated and overlooked by you folks.

I guess I'll just list a few issues that I'd like to see fixed/modified, and comment on the other popular ones. I play on Tallon Zek, and have since it came online. Unless I say otherwise, I am referring to Tallon Zek, when referencing gameply, rules, etc..

1. Deleveling- Hopefully, after seeing the amount of times it's been mentioned so far, you've now marked it as a REAL problem. No grey area here.

2. Team PvP 8-lvl range- Saw that some folks do not like the 8 level spread for Team PvP. Dropping it to 4 is too drastic. Personally, I have no real problem with 8, but if you decided that enough people are complaining, than might I suggest only dropping to 6. On Tallon Zek, we thrive on fighting, and pride ourselves on our victories. Dropping the range to 4 would slow things down for us quite a bit.

3. OOR anything- Some people complain about same team OOR buffing/healing. If the OOR person is not x-teamed, I simply see this as a legit act. The person is only OOR, and not Invul. When I come across it, I call on guildmates to bring up heavy hitters to even the fights. I do understand that sometimes it gets taken to the extreme, i.e, Lvl 50 keeping lvl 8 uber-buffed and healthy while they terrorize an entire zone of newbies. Duuno how you can really fix it though, without messing alot of other things up.

4. Invul/ X-teaming- I'll bet you guys are hating this issue :-) This is the hairy one for Team PvP. You gave us 4 teams, but did not restrict interaction between teams. Now, (at least on Tallon), we've adopted to this ruleset, and the community is mainly playing with a Good vs Evil/ Dark vs Light mindset, with a few guilds sworn to single team play, and one guild sworn to mixed team play. While we have almost always had at least one single team guild on Tallon, it never became overtly popular, until recently. Now, we have at least 2 major one-team guilds, and a few upstarts. They are suffering through Invul healers while they try to stay dedicated to a true Teams environment. On one hand, Invul acts are seen as an exploit, (Dwarf fights Human and another Dwarf steps in to heal the human, with no risk to self, because attacker cannot stop a same team player), but on another, it is seem as roleplaying because the 'good' races have united to fight the evil races, (which are fortunate in that they are all one team anyway and do not have to suffer through this particular issue), so the Dwarf attacking the Human is seen as an outcast, because 'were all supposed to be one happy, Light/Good family'.
Then you have all-out cross-teaming, where players and/or groups of players decide to play together, and in PvP, this makes for some seious exploitation. I have not had to deal with this personally, but the population of Tallon Zek seems to mostly dislike having a guild made of all high levels from all teams. Yes, they are doing PvE things that before their formation, had not been accomplished, but in PvP, they are apparently becoming notorious for exploitation. Again, I have no direct contact, as I am not high enough to be afftected by them, but our community messageboard is filled with complaints. Hopefully they, (both the xteamers and the complainers), will voice their concerns here now...

5. Exp. issues- Please do NOT mess with experience in PvP. The PvP servers are made FOR PvP, so penalizing PvPers is just WRONG.

6. Faction Issues- I do not believe that taking away all faction hits is good. It is the one true penalty for PvP that makes sense. If I become an Elf murderer, (heh, "Daisyman want Kung Fu?"), than I deserve to be hated by Elven NPC's. However, I agree that killing in self-defense should not cause faction penalty. That would be a nice fix.

Thats all I can think of now, and it's late...

I thuroughly enjoy the Team PvP play on Tallon Zek, and I hope that you folks have taken notice that our population level is nearly mainstream. Here is my one drastic change request: Create a 4th server, making it either STRICTLY teams or STRICTLY Good vs Evil. Large scale changes on the current servers, (at least on Tallon), would destroy the community we have created.

Again, thanks and I hope you are really listening to our pleas. Anytime you want to get an idea of Tallon Zek's current disposition on ANYTHING, just take a peek at our community messageboard. It is extremely popular, (and like any typical board, has it's share of flames, though the moderators keep things in line for the most part), and most of TZ reads and/or posts there about everything TZ.
