Station Member 		posted 11-18-2000 05:54 PM           
Two major changes occured to the Magician class in the last patch. The first is obvious and clear: the Sword of Runes' proc has been nerfed to the point where its gone from 4-8 procs for a decent length fight (2-4 times per sword) to if you're very lucky once per fight, mor elikely much less than that.

This has reduced the damage output of the average 29+ Magician by something in the order or 160-320 dmg per fight, as well as hurting our group prospects (necros in particular will be less likely to push for the Mage).

The second is a semi-confirmed series of changes to the Call of the Hero spell. Casting it on yourself (to clear agro) no longer kills your pet, and there is a lot of discussion about whether or not it still clears agro - at the very least it seems that these changes have meant that it has a chance fo failing to clear agro, or it simply doesn't at all.

Both of these changes are huge for Magicians. Our Swords and the CoH spell are two of the best aspects of our class, yet they've been changed without so much as a hint of a message in the patch.

This means that Magi all over Norrath have no idea if the changes are intentional, and if they are then permanent.

All we want is to -know- about the nerf, if its a genuine nerf and not a code slip-up. Absor, Abashie, someone, just let us know?
