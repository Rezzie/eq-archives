Let me introduce meself to ye, I am Aengar Wrathand;Slayer Of The Netherborn and Warpriest Of Brell Serillis. Im sending this summons accrossed the lands of Norrath for I have been witness for many days now to a travesty that should not be allowed to exist in our land! Ye may be thinking:"He`s been drinking those Tumpy Tonics too much" but I assure ye that not one sip of that foul poison has past these lips!
But I stray from my topic,let me tell you about The Estate Of Unrest , this place is seeping with foul Undead , pouring out of that haunted estate like a wave of black corruption , tainting all that it touchs! In my weeks spent fighting these beasts,and rotting corpses I have often wondered who rules these hordes?What hand controls them? What wicked creature,be he Man or Animal,could be sick and twisted enough to raise these poor souls from the grave? Many of time I will admit I have seen the Necromancers raise them,but these are loyal dead who serve they`re DarkMasters for whatever reason they have chosen...I may not not like it,and believe me it erks me Dwarven soul tro see it happen,but I understand it. These creatures in Unrest are murderous,wanton and down right demonic!

I say it is time to cleanse The State Of Unrest , time to put an end to the tormented souls that inhabit the House! So I am sending out a Summons , a call to arms for the citizens of Norrath. On Tuesday the 29th of February I will be forming a concerted effort to rid the world of the Undead Blight infecting Unrest! Concerted meaning simply this. We will form groups based on the following table.

Yard Patrol; Levels 14-20

First Floor; Levels 20-25

Second Floor; Levels 25-30

Third Floor and Basement; Levels 30-40


Rare/Special Loot will be randomed between groups of the appropriate level to hunt the Mob that drops it.(ie:Levels 25-30 will Random for the Bloodstained Tunic,Gloves,Savant Cap. Levels 30-40 will random between themselves for loot such as: Jagged Band,Dwarven Work Boots.) This my raise a lot of concerns as I have noticed lately ppl using "Lists" in Unrest. /e spits on the floor in disgust .But hopefully we can get a lot of ppl there to help.

I am asking that if you wish to take part in the Purging of Unrest please e-mail me with the following.

Name

I will contact you in game via / tell starting about 2 hours before the hunt,that is plenty of time for most ppl to make it from Everfrost to Butcherblock running with SOW. If youd rather not recieve a / tell in game then simply show up in Dagnors Cauldron at least 20 mins prior to the hunt,I will still need your Level and Class as I and a few others will be forming groups based on that.

I cannot yet guarantee that there will be Ressurection and Binds available as I am just starting to form the hunt.If it appears that the 29th is not a good time I will then reschedule the hunt and post the new date.
