Vamphyri you are currently in your 30 free days. At the end of the 30 free days your credit card they have on file will be billed for the next 30 days. Unless you cancel the account which can be done via the My Account link, calling customer service(weekdays from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM PT (11:30 AM to 8:30 PM ET), except holidays. From anywhere call: 1-858-537-0898) or clicking the account button (third button below the connect button as you log in).

Don't be afraid to ask for help here...Yes there are a few grumpy board trolls, but most people will try to help.
