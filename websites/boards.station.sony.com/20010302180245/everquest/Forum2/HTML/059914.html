The reason I do not play on PvP servers is because EQ was not designed with PvP in mind. If EQ was DESIGNED for
PvP, then the PvP aspect of it would be more appealing to me.

Here are the reasons I don't play PvP EQ:

1) I play a wizard.
2) Wizard AE spells will hurt my own group.
3) Equipment in EQ is SO time consuming to get that losing a pair of GEBs would just suck too much to have to deal with.
4) Grief players.
5) Grief players.
6) Grief players.
7) Grief players who run around killing people for no reason.
8) Grief players who want to KS you, so they kill YOU first, then take your rare spawn.
9) Grief players who can kill you all they want, but never have any consequences of faction.

Here is a list of items that would influence me to play PvP EQ:

1) No level loss on death. You can lose down to and empty bar on current level, but NEVER below.
2) No EXP loss EVER from a PvP death.
3) Wizard roots with about 25% or less resist rate, but break on one DD cast if rooting another player.
4) Spells hit for FULL damage.
5) You have a "PvP" bind as well as your normal bind. When you die to PvP, you go to your "PvP" bind spot which is ONLY in towns regardless of class, and your corpse is right beside you with ALL your money, items, and equipment on it. Only thing is you respawn there at 50% health, 50% mana, and 50% stamina.
6) To qualify as a "PvP" death, at least 50% of your health must have been taken due to aggregate player damage. (Meaning if a NPC takes 90% of your health, your buddy can't kill you as an exploit)
7) Players should face some kind of consequences from the NPCs in the world due to PKing. My idea is that if a person attacks you first, then you can kill them with no penalty. If you kill someone unprovoked, then you take a faction hit if they are on the same alignment as you. If you kill the same person 3 times in a certain time limit UNPROVOKED, then you are flagged as a murderer for a certain time limit, and are KOS to NPCs aligned with the victim.
8) AE spells cast on NPCs would NEVER hit other players, but AE spells cast on a player would hit that player and any other player who has attacked you, and is in range.
9) If you are killed by a PKer, you could hire a NPC "assassin" to get revenge for you. The "assassin" would be of equal level to the person who killed you for fairness.

These are just some of my ideas. Feel free to revise them, and add ones that I missed
