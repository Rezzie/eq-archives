OK, ever have one of those days when things just work PERFECTLY? mine was yesterday.

My guild was doing xanamech, the mechanical dragon in POI, we kill him and are doing the loot and the adds in the room start repopping, no biggy, we kill one, kill the other...and the junk beast spawns...VERY rare spawn, very, very rare.

We kill it, I bid and win on the power source, yippee, I'm a bit step closer to concluding a quest I need...

Now I need a planar blood brew, never seen one for sale in the bazaar after a month of looking, but what ever, I check...and there one is, bought, done.

Next stop is rattican's head...I figure I'd wait a few days on this...when lo and behold, someone is selling the MQ, I talk him down to a low price of 7500pp, and bingo, now I have my intermediate stealth, intermediate combat, and advanced magic (finished some time ago) books.

I happen to have the stuff to finish the advanced combat, so then I go finish that...

Now I'm needing a symbol of the diaku and a blizzard gem from bastion of thunder...well I figure I'll wait a few till I can get a group together in tactics, but I'll pop into BoT to try and get a blizzard gem..

Zone in, there's a dead giant and no one around...I figure..what are the odds? but there it is, a blizzard gem. I ooc that I'm looting if no objections, none, so I loot.

So now I need a symbol of the diaku, and a blue diamond, stop off at a merchant in PoT to search for blue diamonds and WHAT do I find for sale that someone had dropped off earlier? a symbol of the diaku, bam, done, all 3 advanced manuals done.

Now the tricky part, I need a nodrop item from elemental planes and I'm not flagged, but it CAN be MQed, so I figure, I'm gonna beg borrow and steal from all my uber friends, MAYBE one of them has a guildmate who will sell me an MQ for a ludcrusly high price.

Well I ask a Township Rebellion enchanter friend...and lo and behold, she has one, and she's MQing it...for FREE.

Now I'm the proud holder of a http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=18123

Life's good sometimes.
