For those who haven't seen PoWater, it's a nice zone. Mostly filled with water, it's more interesting than Kedge because it's not as open and roomy, rather it reminds me something of a giant coral reef zone.

The entry is a cave/grotto with sand and a few fires (and graves) at the back, and the waterway looks like a pond at the edge of the grotto shore. To get to the 'action', one has to hop in the water (2 mobs right in the water), and swim through various tunnels.

Really it's kinda fun.

Prior to the patch that removed some safespot (that I'm not aware of), one could sit on the beach and fish. Typically, that's how fishing's done, as I've never gone fishing in the real world, and dove in.

Now, half of the sand area (the zone in) is the aggro radius of the two mobs in the water. The respawn is just over 20 minutes on these two critters, so it's fairly pointless to recruit people to off them because of an err in planning.

Why fish? Well there's no quests involved, however the baking tradeskill can use 2 types of fish that are found there. The PoP baking recipes can be found over at eqtraders. Great way to spend downtime Although attaining the pieces seems to be more... deadly than necessary.
