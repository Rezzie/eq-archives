I know this topic has been brought up before, but I thought I'd bring it up again and perhaps nag Verant enough to actually do something about it. It is pretty much common knowledge that the casters have gotten the "shaft" in terms of loot in Kunark. While tanks have a never ending list of OMG items (when they are first found and stats read, the group all goes "OMG") there are absolutely none for the casters (only one comes to mind that casters can wear, the Heriphant Cloak which, of course this is not a caster only item). In fact there are semi-common weapons that put the weapons that drop off the gods to shame. Yet there is nothing for the caster. It's pretty sad when one of the better items for casters in Kunark is a +2int +30mana item that goes in the ranged slot.

This lack of caster loot was again brought to my attention with the Mana Robe which drops off Trakanon. First off, why are we revisiting Cryosilk again? Can't casters get their own class specific equipment instead of being forced to share it with every other caster class? Secondly, THAT'S IT? That's our OMG item? 14ac 8int and a really watered down version of the manastone? This is like taking an int, mana, and stats hit to do something most of us were doing pre-kunark (without the casting time). Can someone please explain to me why can't casters at least get an OMG item off of Trakanon? The CRoA is still the best caster robe outside Veeshan's Peak, yet all of the tanks plane's gear has been upgraded significantly.

Perhaps Verant thinks that casters "don't need loot as much as tanks" however I think you need to realize that we'd like to be rewarded for our efforts as well, so please get with the program and ballance this thing out a little bit.

- Have items that boost the strength of spells in a certain school. Said items could be limited by class and possibly religion.
