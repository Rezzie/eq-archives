BALANCING THE MONK CLASS
I have been reading though a lot of posts and decided to list here probably the most important points and some of my own suggestions for monks. This would make it easier for you and I also feel(as well as many other monks, you can see from reading replys to your monk post) that you are missing some of our points.

POINT I-damage output
I agree with chickenlegs on his damage table idea, but I do have a suggestion of my own.
-perhaps a very easy way to balance would be to give monks a stackable natural haste on weapons as well as fists. If we have a 50 percent stackable natural haste, we will always be doing at least 50 percent more dmg then the warrior as long as the weapons are similar. I also agree that the monk should do at least 50 percent more dmg then the warrior. At this point, we are at least 50 percent weaker then warriors in defense..maybe even weaker...this includes hit points. A 50 percent defense deficiency should warrant a 50 percent offence superiority. (Not to mention that the speed boosts would make us look damn cool! hehe. Off course, short dl/dmg attacks should be reevaluated for their taunts

POINT II-fd rediscovered
Now with the higher dmg, we will be getting hit a lot more, to lower ourselves in the hate list, why not just return fd to its original glory? When every monk was proud to have it as their greatest skill. Let FD completely erase the hate list. I believe the reason why this was even changed was because Verant did not want us feign pulling (I may be wrong but I am pretty sure), but didnt Verant recently change the MOBs so that they will walk back in groups? If they didnt they should. Wouldnt this by itself rid us of feign pulling? Returning FD to its original state would satisfy half the monks who post on these boards complaining how there is no way for them to lower aggro. It would also make the game more fun for us monks...who enjoys fding 20 times just to lose aggro on a mob? Or /q and reloging? Or even taking a fd nap while waiting for another group member to rise above us in the hate list? This just plain takes away from the fun of the game, not to mention the fact that without something similar to this, our dmg changes will make no sense,we will be forced to keep our dmg output low anyway just to reduce aggro.
-Also FD is currently horribly bugged, beneath 2 bubs of health, and while party members are attacking a mob, there seems to be almost no capability for a succesful fd. I have also read of other instances of it failing...the code needs to be looked at.
-Please give us a message to let us know that fd has failed. I know it may not seem realistic for us to automatically know if the mob is fooled. But because of us remaining on standing view we can not see the mobs attacking us...so this does lead to a need for a message such as this. Sure we can switch camera views...but it takes too long and it would be easier to just wait until we take that first hit. The XXXX has fallen to the ground message is just not cutting it anymore.


POINT III-armor
Please look at the monk only armor pieces and make them worth getting over all/all armor. Or at least make them equal to them. Allow us a larger selection of weaps/armor, especially in the mid-level range. Also please give us some new looks on our armors, perhaps even something like a ninja suit...just a suggestion.

POINT IV-Resistances
Every monk that has gone toe to toe with a caster mob would agree that we are just insects to them, pests that they can easily dispose of. Even low green casters can do extreme amounts of dmg to us. Blue casters can just decimate us. Please look at either adding more resistances to monk armors, or even better, providing the monk with natural resistances.

Well its all i can do for now, please take a look at it.
