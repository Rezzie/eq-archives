I completely agree with Shadowstump. The single largest increase to a rogue's standard damage is double backstab at 55 ...

After that ... big weapon upgrades to your main stabber are pretty good (going from 6 to 12 damage on your main hand weapon will help those backstabs a lot, just don't sacrifice too much speed - note, I don't know what your weapons stats are, I don't have access to an item site from work). Making sure your backstab skill is maxed is a good way to keep up (as if you hadn't figured this out to begin with ... ).

Statwise ... str will increase your maximum possible damage on both swings and backstabs, but it won't affect your average a whole lot. at 180 str, you're pretty much within buff range of 255 (presuming you get the really good str buffs). Raising the ATK stat directly helps some, but there isn't much that's easy to get that'll give any +atk straight, and not much that's tough to get that'll give you any appreciable amount of atk. The goblin earring will only give you 10 to 15 (I dont' remember exactly), which isn't much at that level.
