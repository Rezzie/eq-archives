I only have experience with this bug as a Mage, but I have no reason to believe that it does not apply equally to the other pet classes.

The bug is very simple: when 4+ creatures are meleeing my pet, creatures beyond the 3rd hit me INSTEAD of my pet. This is regardless of distance between me and the mobs. I can be half a zone away.

This problem most frequently occurs when as the result of a quad-kiting gone awry, but I have also experienced it in several dungeons that are prone to trains (noteably Unrest). My solution at the moment is eboots for fast pet disbursement, but, seriously, this bug is ridiculous. I have died to it three times since lvl 25, which accounts for more than half my deaths in a 26 lvl span.

Yesterday, a wizard ran 6 raptors into me. I ran over the water as a knee-jerk reaction. They didn't follow but instead turned on my pet. I hit 'i' and reached for my eboots.

A raptor hits you for 110

A raptor hits you for 110
You are stunned.
A raptor hits you for 110
LOADING ... PLEASE WAIT.

I petitioned ... not for a rez but simply to make the GM aware of the bug. I know that it is not verifiable, and that the GM thus can not help me. I didn't expect him to.

Sigh. Please look into this. It disturbs me that most of the negative aspects of playing a mage (the 'challenge') are technical rather than gameplay oriented: pathing, this bug, LD. I understand and accept the pathing problems and the issues surrounding LD, but this really ought to be examined.

Also, please note that in my experience, mobs hitting me through my pet always hit the maximum number of times for maximum damage. This includes green creatures. Bashes through my pet also always stun me.
