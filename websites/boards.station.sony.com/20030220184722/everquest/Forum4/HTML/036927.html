Beta Neutral is kind of a catch all faction for many NPC that don't "fit" into one of the common factions. Many Quest related NPC are on Beta Neutral as well as some merchants etc.

Beta Neutral is usually really hard to lower to a point where you are KOS. But, their is a rare bug were 1 kill will lower it to "could not possible get any worse". You can petiton and a GM will reset it.

Their is more information on this faction and the bug at:
