That's pretty easy. There is a quest to get a stone that allows you to port from wayfarer camp to wayfarer camp. That is what the 'whispering voice' is leading you to.

You don't have to get the adventurer stone to hunt dungeons. Just put a group together and the group leader gets the adventure from the adventure recruiter by right clicking like a merchant. Everyone in the group will get a adventure window detailing the adventure and where to go to enter the dungeon.
