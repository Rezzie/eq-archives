What this warrior thinks?

I think that the Shadowknights and Paladins did get a bum rap...namely, who wanted one in their group if a warrior was around?

Why is this? Paladins and Shadowknights didn't have abilities or skills that set them apart from warriors or let them expand into different areas...rather, they were watered down warriors with a few spells.

So rather than do something to make each of these classes truly unique, they raise the defensive caps to my level.

Warriors are totally and completely dependent on equipment.

I can't even run through Kael without someone assisting me. I am babied along by casters with buffs and heals...for quick transport, to a corpse recovery. Why have I accepted this for 56 levels now? Because once my casters have fawned over me...dressed me in nice buffs and got me to where I needed to go. I was an incredibly formidable force. I could take the beatings that NO one else could survive, and for that one reason was very desirable to a group.

And while yes, a warrior will still be needed for some of our disciplines (namely defensive and furious)...we're not as necessary as we once were...and even now, with the defensive discipline now working as intended, with the subsequent increase in hate, we're going to be wanted less.

Heal my ass and my 4k hps and see how long you live.

And please, don't tell me that I was keeping the mobs attention with ease. If you root things for me, I have enough time to build up enough hate to keep the enchanter from being raped...but again, I'm not doing that alone, some wizard or Pally has to do that for me.

I'm a bit saddened that my one role, the one defining characteristic of my class is now not so unique. For all the other things that the warrior class couldn't do, we did make a damn good shield for you casters. We're not alone in that anymore...it saddens me that rather than find a unique and original way to enhance the Paladins and Shadowknights to make them more appealing in groups...they take from the one thing that made warriors stand out.
