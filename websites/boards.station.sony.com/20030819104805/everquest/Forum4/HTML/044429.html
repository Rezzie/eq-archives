It is absolutely essential to make friends and join a guild.

The major skill of this game is not "Knowing your Class" or "Knowing How to Raid" or "Knowing all the Zones."

It helps of course, but the most helpful skill in EQ is social engineering, the ability to manage friendships and influence people.

That's the only way you will ever get help getting your epic, or help with quests, or help with getting keys and flags, or decent "Need Before Greed" groups at high levels. Otherwise you're stuck in pickup groups rolling on any piece of garbage that drops.

At low levels, just join any ACTIVE (lots of people) guild that will take you. Go to all their low level raids, make friends, establish connections. When opportunities to move to better guilds present themselves, take it and swing to a higher rung until you find a guild you love or one you REALLY want to join.
