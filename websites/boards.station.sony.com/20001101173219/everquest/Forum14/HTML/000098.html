What Am I doing Wrong?
Everyone I talk to says that Druids Can't Tank. I have been very successful as a tank for a group when no Warior or Hybrids were available, Just meant a little longer downtime, and closer cooperation with the casters.

Everyone I talk to says Druids Can't be effective Healers. I've never had a party member die because I couldn't heal him as Group healer in the last 9 months I've played a Druid. Just takes me a little more mana than a Cleric, and Knowledge of the Strengths and Weaknesses of my Party members.

Everyone I talk to says Druids Can't burn like Casters. I guess I can stop blasting with my Burst of Flame series, and Whirling wind series spells. I guess that timing my casts, as well as giving the tanks a slight head start on damage was all a waste of time. I guess I was wrong on my assumption that Druids were just slightly less effective Burn Mages, but still useful.

Here I was all this time, Controling Pulls with Harmony and Rooting, Buffing Party Members with Damage Shields, Stacking Dots with Necros, and All this other stuff that I thought was helpful, to be told that I was wasting my time.

Tracking, SOW, Group Teleports, I always thought that these made helping groups find and finish quests so much easier. I guess I was wrong.

Gee I guess I've been playing my Druid Wrong all this time. I never realized how much of a weak pathetic class I play. All this time I thought I was the king of versatility, and instead I find out that my class is useless, and none of the friends I've met online wanted to group with me.

Wow, Thanks for the Info. I think I'm going to Delete my Character and try something else.

A lot of the perspective you mention comes from higher level play. At low levels anyone can take some hits from mobs and be ok to some extent, but at high levels you better have full plate and a ton of hitpoints if you are going to try to stand toe to toe with mob's and absorb damage.

Druids aren't good as *primary* healers in most situations. At lvl 40 as a warrior I have around 1800 hp's buffed. A cleric of equal level can complete heal me for 400 mana. Or...superior heal me which will give close to 600hps for 250 mana. A druid in the group will only be able to heal 300hps at a time and will go out of mana in a hurry if they are trying to heal multiple members in the group. This difference just gets even more pronounced the higher you go...

But being a tank or the primary healer is not what most people want in a druid. Druids are GREAT in a group. Regen, damage shields, evac, sow, teleports, backup healing, some buffs, decent nuking ability, etc, etc. Not to mention one of the most important abilities in a dungean: Snare.

Anyone who tells you druids can't contribute to a group probably hasn't grouped with a good one. :-)
