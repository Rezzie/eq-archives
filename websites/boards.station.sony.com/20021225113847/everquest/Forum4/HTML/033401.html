Scenario 1:
Just got done kiting, things didn't go to well but I won anyways. I'm low on health, out of bandages, and not enough mana to heal myself. Treeform can save a lot of mana in just it's regenerative abilities.

Level 39. I'm in a group and I have Spirit of Ash which has a mana regen too. I have no clarity type buff nor is there an enchanter available. So having a self mana regen would be beneficial in minimizing downtime.
