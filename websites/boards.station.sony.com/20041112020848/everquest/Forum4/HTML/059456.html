Things that I like...

People - People can make or break the game and luckily for me, I managed to avoid real idiots most of the time. The help and laughter that I shared over the weekend with friends and guildies contributed so much.

Exploring - I always try and avoid the usual grinds - I have 2 50 characters that have taken 2 years to nurture and several alts. I have died more times than I care to mention throughout my journey, but that was because I was learning new zones or trying new things, not being reckless or foolhardy. I had a fantastic time at the weekend visiting 'The Hole' and 'Dragon Necropolis' for the first time. My Ranger Epic is within touching distance now.

Helping New People - I love helping and giving advice to new players. Several have sent me tells out of the blue saying that they were a low level something or other and now they are high level thanks to me helping them to learn. (This is not a 'look at me' statement - I just get a glow from it).

Acts of kindness - someone helping you out of a problem who you have never met before.

Banter - Guild chat that can make you bend double with laughter.

EQ has so many positives. It really is what you make of it. It is massively complex and I for one, just put up with the anomalies and glitches and just get on and enjoy what it is for me - a nice break from the old routine, but something that I am always learning to do and to do better.
