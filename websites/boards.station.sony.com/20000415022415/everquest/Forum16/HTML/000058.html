Dynasty of the 14 Destined is just as it’s name states. Dynasty because we treat
anyone who joins like family (no prejudice, racism ect.) except we try not to have giant
fights on holidays. And 14 Destined for the 14 Destined Divisions that are to represent
all of the classes (warrior, mage, ect.). There is no absolute ruler, just many equal rulers
that vote on all major issues. The reason that we wish to be a exceptionally large guild is
so that each division leader can chose a certain spot(s) that his Division will usually be
around, and be able to call near bye divisions if in need of assistance. This guild is based
on honesty, valor, and loyalty (well, more so trust). We are simply a band of people that
are tired of all of the unfair evilness and killings going on in Norrath and wish to provide
some security for people to hold on to, even those that are not members. Rank in the
guild does not matter on level status, just whom ever comes first (well for Division
leaders) and if you are a good person. Here is the list of current open divisions.
