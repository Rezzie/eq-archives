Pickup raiders usually don't have good raiding gear. You usually end up with Troll warriors attacking Nagafen with 50 FR (completely buffed)... If you know the implications of that you know what I mean. The real killers of EVERY Vox and Nagefen raids are their area effect fears and Hot/cold AE DDs. Most people just come plain unprepared.

Back when Vox and Naggy were big-hitters of the old world, most high end raiding guilds required a minimum 100FR (Nagafen), 100CR (Vox) and 100MR (both) BEFORE BUFFS. Nowadays Vox/Naggy raiders just show up with what they usually wear and hope for the best... Since most people dont wear diamond and blue diamond jewelry everyday, the raid gets wiped when the key MA, healers, mana sievers, raiders get AE feared and fried/frostbitten.

The last successful Vox and Nagafen raid I attended we used the "buffing commitee/camp out" system. A buffing commitee of level 52+ players buffed the raiders en-masse. The raiders then camped out into the EQ chat rooms to preserve buffs. Once the whole raid was buffed, we rushed Naggy and Vox. No better feeling than to go into battle with level 60 buffs on (RuneV, SoS, COS, Brells, FOS, GMR, BOA etc.!) Nowadays since the major guilds have usually given up on Vox and Naggy, most pickup raiders dont have the expertise or the patience to set up a successful Dragon raid.
