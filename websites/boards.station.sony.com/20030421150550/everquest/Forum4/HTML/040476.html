*rolls her eyes*

This is getting silly. First we have a post from a European's view point saying how it's unfair that the game gets patched during their play hours, then from an American viewpoint saying that the Europeans are monopolizing everything because the game comes up during European play time?

Which is it? Is the game being patched during their play time or is it coming up in time for them to monopolize all the spawns?

I'm not disagreeing with you about some guilds monopolizing spawns, because I've seen it with my own eyes. I don't think it's just "european" guilds. The ones monopolizing content are simply the ones that have the most time. If your guild is made up of people who play 6+ hours a day, can log on from work, raid daily, and can be on by mid afternoon, then your guild is going to see a lot more content than the guild where most members work full time jobs and can't log on until 5 eastern.

It sucks. I hate the way this game is designed. I have no clue who thought it would be a grand idea to make spawns that take 2 days to respawn or even longer. I mean, when I play a game I'd like to think I have a reasonable chance at accessing the content of the game. As it stands now, even if my character and guild are CAPABLE of doing some targets, I will never see them because another guild has more time. So it is not skill or effort that determines what conent I'll see, it's how much time everyone is able to dedicate to claiming certain spawns.

My guild can muster a raid force of just over 40, with the average level being 61. Yet we have many nights when there is literally nothing up for us to take. Our skill, our gear, our past efforts, none of that means anything because the bulk of our raid force can't log on until 6-7 eastern time. The fact is, the higher in content a guild gets, the more of a timesink it becomes to try to lay claim to mobs.

Mobs go from being a 4 hour respawn, to 8 hour, to 12 hours, then finally to days. My biggest complaint with this game has always been the fact that Sony equates time with difficulty. No game should require you to quit your job, or take time off, to accomplish things in the game. Games are supposed to be things you do in your off-time, they are not supposed to be full time jobs in and of themselves.

Raids should not have to take 6+ hours, and guilds should not have to log on with nothing to do because other guilds with more time logged on 3 hours ago.

And I've heard so many times people say things like "If you want to do the high end content you just have to focus and be ready to run on a moment's notice" My answer to that is Why? Why should I have to stop giving my kids a bath, neglect reading them a bedtime story, just to rush to catch Zlandicar before GuildX beats us to him? What would be so game imbalancing if the warders in Sleeper's Tomb respawned every hour? It's not a question of an overabundance of items ruining the economy because let's be honest, the mobs I'm talking about have very little in the way of droppable gear anyway.

The reasoning that only a select few should ever be able to be at the top of the game is seriously flawed. We all pay the same amount of money every month (on regular servers) we all should have a chance, given the same amount of effort (Not time! See my above remarks about time != effort) to see the same content. My guild is full of very skilled players who just happen to have real lives, so we miss out on a lot of content that we are perfectly capable of doing simply because we can't log on at 12 eastern, that is wrong in my opinion.
