If you don't have one already I'd highly suggest picking up a Shaded Staff. On older servers these run from 150-350pp (Sometimes more but they shouldn't be as they are fairly common!). They are a 10/22 1HB weapon and have fairly nice damage output. It'll last you easily until your 50's as I'm still using mine until next level when I can finally equip my Club of the Ice Ocean stashed away in the bank. For a Hand to Hand weapon I'd suggest a Bear Fanged Glove unless you can actually make the 1000pp or so for a Sheetmetal Ulak. Just a couple of suggestions but hopefully they'll help.

For Sleeves I'd just save up for some Acrylia Studded / Reinforced Sleeves for the time being until you can afford gear that is 1kpp+ more easily. Unfortunately, there isn't a lot of selection in Beastlord armor that is *good* until you get into your 50's and that gear costs 1k+ a piece most of the time.
