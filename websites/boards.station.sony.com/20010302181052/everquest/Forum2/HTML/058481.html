Currently in Vellious there are ONE to TWO ways to get class specific armors, depending on what class you play (Rangers, Druids, and Paladins only have ONCE choice).

(1) You can go to the Plane of Growth and farm armor all day long (unless you are a Paladin Druid or Ranger) and get your armor.

(2) You can quest in one of the three cities for your armor.

I'm SURE when Verant put this on paper, it looked WONDERFUL. I'm sure high-fives were exchanged over the masterful work done by the designers to give players such VAST choices, then, just like almost everything Verant puts on paper and tries to execute in game, it failed TOTALLY AND COMPLETELY.

But Furor, WHY doesn't this work? It sounds great...

Reader, yes, it sounds great, until you realize what each QUEST requires.

Thurgadin armor quests are the easiest of the quests to complete yet easily 100x more difficult than simply enterring the Plane of Growth and farming class armors. In order to complete a piece of armor in Thurgadin, one must kill Kromzek - these are level 52 and above giants that spawn either in the arena or in King Tormax's chamber. Just to get a PIECE of armor is rare enough, unfortunately there is FAR more to the questing process. I will go into this more when I describe the other two cities and their respective quests.

If you are allied with Giants and have high enough faction with King Tormax (a chore that takes at least a weeks time to raise high enough) you can do Kael armor quests. The Ancient Armors that are required for Kael quest armros drop in the Temple of Veeshan. This is a zone that words simply cannot do justice to in describing. It is TOTALLY bugged in regards to pathing, mob AI (mobs are untauntable completely 100%), and worst of all almost ANY mob in there would kill anything in the Plane of Growth (where class armor actually DROPS, no quest required) within 20 seconds. Add to this the rarity of actually aquiring Ancient Armor and you have a problem.

Lastly we have Skyshrine Armor quests. If you are allied with Lord Yelinak and have proper faction (again, something incredibly mundane and difficult to raise) you can participate in these armor quests. You are asked to bring back Unadorned armor pieces to the quest givers in Skyshrine. Unadorned drops in ONE place and one place only, the Temple of Veeshan. Not ONLY that, it drops in the Testing Halls of the Temple of Veeshan. ANY mob in the Testing Halls would wipe the floors with ANY mob in the Plane of Growth (some would wipe the floors of Tunare herself) within seconds.

SO, to summarize, the three cities have the FIRST part of their armor quests drop in three very difficult areas. As IF this wasn't bad enough, the quest armors ALL require gems. The gems for certain parts of armor drop with some frequency, enough so that one would be able to reasonably complete their piece of armor within a few days once the primary armor piece was obtained - HOWEVER, the gems for the Tunics are SO rare that not ONE breastplate/tunic for any class has yet to be completed on my server (Veeshan). My guild, arguably the most powerful and resource heavy guild in all of Everquest has a total of THREE Flawless Diamonds, TWO Pristine Emeralds, and THREE Black Marbels. These are the gems that are required to make tunics. Each TUNIC requires THREE of said gems. Since Velious open, the most resource heavy guild in EQ has obtained enough gems to make TWO breastplates/tunics. TWO! Meanwhile, we could spend ONE day in the Plane of Growth and farm five tunics - ONE DAY. Add to this that Paladins, Rangers, and Druids CANNOT even go to the Plane of Growth for their armors and you can see that this is a major issue.

Was it the intention of this company to make the entry of armor from quests SO rare that the ratio of PoG class armors to quested armor was literally 500:1? Is there a concrete reason for this? We have seen the armor pieces, they range from identical to Plane of Growth pieces to MARGINALLY better.

My request: Increase the armor drops for ALL quested armor significantly and Increase the gem drops for ALL quested armor significantly. ESPECIALLY taking care to increase the drop rate of tunic/breastplate gems 10 fold.

Oh, and if you were wondering where tunic gems come from, they come from, again, mobs that would decimate mobs in the Plane of Growth in under a minute.
