This is the first of 3 corpse summonning spells you will get.

This one requires a, Black Ceremonial Coffin to cast it. These coffins can be bought on alot of Container merchants. I want to say there is a vendor in the Bazaar that is across from Merchant Tekrama that sells them. Should cost less then 3 plat a coffin but they weight 10 stone so watch the weight.

You must be in the same zone ase the player and the corpse and yes you can summon your own by targeting yourself. Simply target, cast and look at your feet and viola!!!!

Lesser summon corpse only works on corpses up to level 35. Summon corpse works on bodies of any level. Conjure corpse works on corpses of all levels and the component is cheaper and smaller than summon corpse.
