God, you newbies.

Humans, Erudites, and Barbarians have no vision enhancements.

Dark elves have Ultravision.

ALL other races have Infravision, including ogres and trolls.

Here's an easy test. Go make a newbie, and look at a decaying skeleton (without a lightsource equipped), which can be found in any nearby newbie ground:

If it's pink colored at night, you have Infravision.

If it's bone colored at night, you have ultravision.

If you're saying "What Skeleton?" You have no vision enhancements.

When people refer to balance, they are referring to it as an issue of equality. So a troll has regen, then an ogre must have another ability, so a elf has another so an halfling has another..etc etc..which all must be judged as in equal value, meaning balance.

My reference was to the notion (stated by a few) that everything has to be balanced "equal" to their satisfaction. I much prefer the concept that each class has relative strength and weakness. Balance isn't an issue for me. Class balance is even less of an issue.

Especially when you start talking about the synergetic effects of combing the various classes/races. Sometimes what appears to be not equal or unbalanced turns out to be the opposite, depending upon the combinations of classes/races.

I could easily take every single class and race and perfectly legitimize why it's not balanced compared to another. But that's silly. You pick a class, a race, and utilize it's various strengths a weaknesses. Simple as that.
