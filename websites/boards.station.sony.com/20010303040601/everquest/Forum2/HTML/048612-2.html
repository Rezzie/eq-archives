And when a mob has an AE spell it causes a huge burst of even more spam because it hits everyone near you."

Chromite is exactly correct. You are filtering the message spam on your CLIENT, but the SERVER is still flooding your connection just as badly as if you turned off all the filters.

There are a few things you can do to cut down the spam, but they're VERY crude and you will lose a lot of combat efficiency in the process.

Dispell any damage shield the monster(s) may have right away.
Do everything possible to limit the number of people being hit by the AE at once (ie no pets, healers stay outside of the AE range, casters go in range only to nuke at the end, etc etc)
Have all the tanks use slow two-handed weapons
No pets, and go with the *absolute minimum* number of people you can get away with. If you have extra visitors or what have you, have them stay far away.
Nobody uses AE's of any kind whatsoever. No AE stun or mem blur or any of that.

Combine all this together and you just might get through a fight without any linkdeaths.

The problem will still be there, and Verant will still need to fix it, but this might do the trick in the meantime.
