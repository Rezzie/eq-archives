Abashi
Station Member 		posted 08-13-2000 01:47 PM           
No, we wouldn't consider use of EQCLIENT.INI editors as cheating. They are just fancy text editors I should probably mention that none of them are, however, officially licensed or endorced by Verant/SOE, and that any use of them is at your own risk. Though I'm sure that the vast majority of people would never do this, with the ease of implementing password-stealing code into a 3rd party program, I'd recommend that you be certain of your source.

-Gordon
