My brother was attempting to roll a new char tonight, but had to clear a couple of slots first to make room.

He decided to delete his Froggy toon, so took the delete option; a message box popped up asking if he was sure he wanted to delete Froggy? He answered that he did indeed wish to consign Froggy to the great lily-pad in the sky and the message box disappeared.

He then noticed that Froggy was still alive and croaking in his character list, but that his newly-levelled 51 Cleric had bitten the bullet!!

D'oh!

He logged in on his main and petitioned a GM, but the last I spoke to him, he had not yet been contacted.

Understandably he was fuming at having 'lost' his Cleric and told those members of the guild who were online about what had happened. One of the other guild officers, who was online at that time, said that the exact same thing happened to her main 2 weeks ago!

Blimey, that's two users in one guild who have been affected by this - I wonder if it is a widespread issue?

So, as the title says, "Has this happened to anyone else?"

Our server, by the way, is Karana, but I don't think that is likely to make any difference.
