After taking two enchanters to 50 on two servers I thought I would dispels some of the myths stated here.

1) People believe Enchanters are overpowered. There are surprisingly few situations where the enchanter truly shines, these situations just happen to be fairly common. They are :
a. When the enchanter is at a even or greater level than the mobs being pulled
b. The fighting takes place in a area where multiple pulls are common
c. The mobs aren’t Kunark or Velious
any other situation the enchanter my contribute, but only at a equal to lesser degree than the others in the party (haste, clarity, debuffs). Some examples would be:
a. Pulling single mobs
b. Fighting any giant outside of Warslick Woods (did ya know you can mes and stun those =) )
c. Fighting in a over-crowded dungeon
d. Fighting in most outdoor zones
e. Epic battles
f. Raids
g. Dragons

In almost every situation, more than one enchanter in a group is a bad thing.

2) Enchanters are essential. No, crowd control is essential and Enchanters happen to be the masters of crowd control. The disparity between a enchanters crowd control and his closest rival is the same as a cleric’s healing compared to a druid or shaman. We have other key abilities but none of them make us as valuable as crowd control. If we need to share a greater portion of that power with other classes, that is fine (as we need to do with Clerics and healing). A nerf in this area would not only cripple the enchanter (as the growing number of mes-proof and resistant mobs do) but greatly alter the game dynamics for the existing dungeons in Antonica and Faydwer

Just my 2cp
