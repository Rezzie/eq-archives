In DL, I both grouped and soloed near karnors north wall. I found the best group on northwall consisted of a healer, bard, and me... Quick xp from 42-46. Another good techinque for 47-49 is camping the static spawns in the front of karnors. You'll need a cleric to kill the ragers effectively. From 47-49 I soloed anything I could find that was not a ravaging Drolvarg... At 49 and 50 I solo those too.

Fear kiting is relatively safe in DL. Just make sure to not let the mobs get too far out.

BW is an odd zone. For 37-44 you need a full group to take most of the critters there... 45 and up you can have much smaller groups. Fear kiting works well if you can find a good path line. A group of 37-42 can kill a wurm.
