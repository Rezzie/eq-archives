The Syndicate is a more than three year old Online Gaming guild located
at http://www.erols.com/seanst/synd/iiiguild.html (click on the logo to
load the pages after the movie plays). We are more than 400 members
strong and very organized, unified and professional. Having been an
integral part in the beta testing of most of the online games currently
released or in development, we are now shifting a branch of our guild to
Everquest. With several hundred members planning on playing, we aim to
be the largest and most successful guild in EQ as we have in all other
games we have been involved in.

Syndicate members have been preparing for EQ since the very beginning of
the beta. Some of our members were among the very first beta testers
into the world. Since that time we have built momentum in our
membership and are EXPLODING into the game as roleplayers and team
oriented friends.

We exist, together as one team, on Tarew Marr server. We have
squads dedicated to each of the races offering opportunities for all
members. And we are currently open for recruiting of new members and
are receiving many Everquest applications a day. We respond to all
applications received within 24 hours so if you dont get a reply it
means your email address was incorrect or there was a communications
problem. We are looking for professional, mature players to join our
ranks.

We are one of the largest, oldest and most successful online gaming
guilds. We pride ourselves on being mature, professional, dedicated and
motivated. Despite being a large guild we are a tight knit TEAM of
friends. We have a well organized heirarchy and every member is
important to the guild from the oldest veteran to the newest member.

Our guild is run by Guildmaster Dragons who has run the guild since the
beginning. We are anti-cheat, anti-bug and what is termed "anti-pk".
In each of the major games we participate in we muster more than 100
players online at peaks times and 50+ at non-peak hours giving us a
large 24 hour online presence. Its nice to have dozens of members able
to help you out at any given time.

Besides playing in online games, we also operate related sites to
support them. We have a "pagan magic" team well known in the UO world
dedicated to promoting role playing, locating and getting fixed game
bugs but most importantly to researching the secrets of the game making
it more fun for all. We also maintain a RPG gaming news site known as
The DEN (Dragon's Eye News) currently being revamped to include an
specific Everquest section. In addition our members include game
designers from many of the leading RPG companies as well as staff of
several print and online gaming magazines.

The Syndicate is already a major power in the EQ world and plans only to
grow larger, stronger and more unified. We are dedicated, professional
gamers who are looking to have FUN! We have a STRONG PvP section, a
large merchant section and a fun loving monster hunting section. We
watch each others backs and hold a great many in game events, parties,
games and quests.

Long Live The Syndicate!
