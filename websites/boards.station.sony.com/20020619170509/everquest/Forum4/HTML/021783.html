Hello,

I was playing in a small group (4) last night, two of us were Lvl 14-15 and the other two were about Lvl 5-7.

We were in Crushbone on Faydwer.

After a while it became obvious that the two lower level players were not getting any experience (exp) points when we slew Orc legionairs etc.

The manual,(Page 66, Party exp), suggests that in groups exp is shared based on lvl. Now it would seem to me that the lower lvl players should get more exp than the higher. (In real life a novice learns more from the expert than vice versa). But here they got nothing!

Comments please.

The rule of thumb is a 5 level limit under 15 (if I recall right), and after that, the level range is determined by (lowest level)*1.5

i.e. a level 20 can group with people up to level 30 and be fine. At level 40, you can group with anybody up to level 60 and get exp.
