This is just a tip for all you Newbies out there who are looking for $$ to buy new spells, weapons, armor etc. This is VERY easy and gives you decent xp for lvls 1-5.

KILL BATS AND COLLECT THEIR WINGS!!!!

As you may or may not know, Bat wings are used for the Levitation spell used by several classes. You need a Bat Wing every time you cast it. So, higher lvl characters who have this spell will gladly pay 1pp or more sometimes to someone selling a stack (20) of Bat Wings.

I just recently started a Cleric after a bad night with my 21st Ranger (played 4 hrs, died 3 times and lost 2 bubs of xp!). I started killing Bats right off the bat (pardon the pun) and sold one stack for 3pp and another for 1 pp and some nice buffs. Now to a lvl 3 character 4 pp is a LOT. So, instead of begging or whatever, kill bats, sell the wings. You get xp and $$. Bone chips can also be sold in stacks to Necro's and SK's. just fyi...

a few additions to the wings: be patient! Sometimes nobody will want to buy the wings. Or they offer less (like 5 gp for 20), so just wait and try to sell them later. Just continue to collect wings and in the end, you are maybe able to sell 2 or 3 stacks.

And an addition to the Spider (not Spiderling) Silks. Buy a small sewing kit and put one training point into the Sewing Skill. Then you can put 2 Spider Silk in the kit and combine them. You will get a Silk Swatch and these are stackable. So in theory, instead of holing 8 silks in a backback, you can hold up to 160 Swatches in a backback. A swatch can go for 1 plat, so if you have 20 to sell, you can make up to 20 plat. But beware, don't try that too early. Most spiders will poison you and especially the crab spiders have a nasty poison. It helps to have a healer around :-)) Oh and spiders help each other. If you fight with one and anoter one shows up, it will enter the fight and attack you too. I remember one time in EK, where I had to run from spiders around level 22 or so, because I had 5 on my butt. And with the constant attacks I wasn't able to cast my spells... So fleeing and shouting "Huge spider train to bridge" wasn't my most heroic day in Norrath :-)))

Every time anyone replies to any particular message thread here on the boards, that particular thread is moved to the top of the list.

With the above explanation, you could just type the word "bump", or any word, and the thread you typed it in will go to the top.

Why would someone do this? The usual reason is to show support of that particular thread. Message threads that receive no replies after a while tend to get old and rapidly go down the list. Most people only review threads on the first page. Once it drops to the second page or older, the chance of it being read decreases, so someone may "bump" it back up.
