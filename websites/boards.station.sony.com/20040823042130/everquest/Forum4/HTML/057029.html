All characters used to spawn with a "Tome of Order and Discord" or something like that - along with their food and drink (and no backpack).

If you wanted to be a Player vs Player character on a non PvP server (ie, if you wanted to be able to attack other PvP players and be attacked by them) then you would hand in your book to the Priest of Discord and he would announce that you were joining the forces of discord, or something like that.

Then your name over your character's head would turn red and you could no longer get buffs or heals by people who hadn't turned in their books. BUT you could kill and be killed by others with red names.

The ability still exists - you can ask the Priest of Discord for a Tome and then hand it right back in.

Generally not enough people on a server get 'red names' to make it worth it though - you end up with very few people to group with - and quite a few people who can't help you at all.
