Zeik,

Didn't find the original post from Absor, but found a clarification he posted today. Here's the deal:

If a rogue successfully evades, their hate will be reduced to place them between the second and third person on the hate list (if I recall correctly, the number they get is the average of the second and third person) UNLESS that does not bring them down at least 500 points, in which case they go down by 500.

So say the rogue backstabs and is at the top of the list:

Rogue 10,000
Ranger 7,000
Warrior 6,000

Now he drops to second:

Ranger 7,000
Rogue 6,500
Warrior 6,000

Right? But, say when the rogue evades, the hate list is like so:

Rogue 10,000
Warrior 9,900
Ranger 9,700

Now, the Rogue doesn't go down to 9,800, because that would only be a 200-point reduction. He goes down 500 points, and the new hate list looks like this:

Warrior 9,000
Ranger 9,700
Rogue 9,500

Is that any clearer?
