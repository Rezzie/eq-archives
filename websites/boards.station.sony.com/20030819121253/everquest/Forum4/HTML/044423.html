When you reach the server selection screen, the default view has the special ruleset servers at the top (include the Player vs Player servers), and the rest are ordered by current online population (least at the top, to most at the bottom). If you want a crowded server, choose one that is consistently at the bottom of the default view list during the hours that you play.

As for players not being mean, I cannot help you. There are some on every server, and it all depends on who you meet.
