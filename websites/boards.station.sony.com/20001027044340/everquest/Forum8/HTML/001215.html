This may be out of date and all that whatever but I just want to put up a clarification. Usually (always has been with me) when failing on a skill check any smithing recipe where you get part back (eg. smithy hammer in failed folded sheets, fine plate & smithy item in dying) the line is not:

"You have lacked the skill to fashion these items together.
You have fashioned the items together to make something new!"

it's:

"You have lacked the skill to fashion these items together.
You have fashioned the items into an alternate product."

Or something very close to that effect. This may have changed, but this has *always* been true for me on failing on skill checks.
