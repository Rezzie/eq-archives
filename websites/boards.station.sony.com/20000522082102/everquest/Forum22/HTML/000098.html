About T'or Drakkon...

We are here, on Povar, to have fun and be a family. We do not KS, train, farm, etc. We are OPPOSED to all those things, and fire people for violating the rules. The rules are posted on our website, and this is the first 3:

#1 No Kill stealing
#2 No training anyone EVER
#3 No Vulgar language in OOC, shout or guildsay, NONE

It is VERY hard, in a guild our size (easily 80+) to police everyone all the time. I hope our memebers are doing their part to keep T'or Drakkon in good standing with all, but occasionally a bad apple joins our ranks and causes our name to be tarnished.


If you see this happening, please take a deep breath and relax for a sec, then think about the situation. Is it possible that the T'or member didn't KS but that the mob aggroed onto them when your puller ran by? Is it possible there were multiple targets in a FFA and someone mistakenly targeted a mob you were on? Has this happened to YOU personally more than once?
If you think it was intentional or happens more than once, I would hope you would seek out a T'or officer and let them know of the incident. Please use good judgement, don't get caught up in what other people say, go with your personal experience. If person X killsteals from you several times, even after you talk to the person, let us know. However, if person X has been rumored to be a KSer and you see person X get a kill from your group, don't jump to the conclusion that the rumors are true, use your own judgement.
Again, we are a "proud" and "honest" guild. We don't want to be the biggest, or the most powerful, we just want to be known as a great guild with great people in it.
Also keep in mind that when we receive these reports, we will go to the person in question and get their side of the story, and we like to give people a second chance to redeem themselves if they deserve it.
It's pretty easy to have a really bad day, log onto EQ, have a small run-in with someone that is in ANY guild, and get mad at that person's guild. We stress to ALL our members one simple thought that we value:

If you do something nice, people will remember you - If you do something wrong, everyone will remember your guild.

Unfortunately, due to human nature, anytime someone has a bad experience with one or two people in any guild, the image of the entire guild is tarnished. Don't let one bad T'or, or one bad GC, Harm, NA, AF, Hawk, or another other guild, big or small, determine your view of that guild.


an Officer of T'or Drakkon
