Well, what is good for one person might not be good for another. If all I am doing is hunting for experience, I want a rather safe camp with reasonable downtimes, few adds, and hopefully some loot.

I think when I was 37 is when I spent my now regretted decision to kill the corrupt qeynos guards in West Karana for a couple levels. They can be difficult to break, but there are only two of them. At 37 am not sure if one will be lt blue and the other dark blue or if both will be dark blue.

Anyway, they are in the tower near the barb camp. Once you break the camp you have a minute or two between each spawn. Save the bracers and take them to the Captain at the stone bridge in North Karana. You will get about as much exp from turning in the bracers as you do from the kills, plus better loot than the kills themselves.

This will completely trash your Corrupt Qeynos Faction. But the good guards will love you.

Oowomp in OOT is another camp starting at about this age. You might want to wait another level or you might want to try him about now. Greens out around 45. Fairly slow respawn time (about 5-7 minutes) but pretty good loot.

Splitpaw
Invis and jump off the bridge.
Open door to peak if One eye is up.
If so find a safe spot to gate out.
If not and Nische is there, back out of the door and head to your left near the stairs (don't go up) and peak around the corner to see if the Scribe is there.
If so find a safe spot to gate out.
If not leave those two Gnolls alone.
Either corner by the stairs is a safe spot to med up.
Take out the one gnoll directly in front of the stairs; however, you are used to fighting. Just make sure he's rooted and does not run.
Bring each of the gnolls back to this safe spot.
If you get a runner just go ahead and gate out.
Bring a friend here for the first time to help out till you can get the gist of it.
