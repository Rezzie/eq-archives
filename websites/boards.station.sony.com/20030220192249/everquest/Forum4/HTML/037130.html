Adjust clipping in Shar Vahl.

Its sort of a double edged sword, Shadow haven and shar vahl are two of the worst zones if your clipping is way high yet two of the BEST if its very low. Umbra seems to work particularly well in those zones (while it doesn't help at all in outdoor zones like POK)

If you adjust clipping to 5 or even 10 in shar vahl, you'll still see plenty far since buildings will be in the way anyway. The clip plane works in conjunction with Umbra (which is on by default) to automatically only render what you see in those and other indoor zones. This means that you'll be seeing like four buildings around you and if you have clip plane set at 5 or 10 you'll only be rendering those four buildings around you. If you have clipplane set at like 50 percent or more you'll be rendering buildings and everything else out that far which would still be in your field of view.

You can change this on the fly while in shar vahl, the clip plane, and see this in action. It has little to do with models being on or not, its rendering all the buildings and junk.

All times are PT (US)
