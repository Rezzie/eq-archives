With thanks to JM for the inspiration. I have one more song written on a similar theme, but could not decide on a presentation. This one was more alive to me, since the theme is so much like the Bard and its songs, when listened to that song. We do not get the real meaning of that song, until it hits us hard. And since I am a Bard and do not role play at all ... here we go!
