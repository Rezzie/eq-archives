el_mejor
Station Member 		posted 02-14-2000 06:35 AM           
As a wizard of the 44th level I feel that my character is under powered. I'm not going to sit here and whine about being resisted all the time, because, quite frankly, I am rarely resisted.
The problem with wizards is not an inability to inflict massive amounts of damage. I can drop almost any creature in the game. Hill Giants only take 60% of my mana. Soloing is no problem as long as I am not confined to a dungeon. All I have to do is click my J-Boots and attack the nearest enemy. I can root almost anything except for guards since their resistance was increased. But who needs root when I have J-Boots that allow me time to cast my spells?
No, the wizard is not overpowered as long as there is only 1 enemy. With two enemies on me, I can root one and pull the other far enough away that I can take them one at a time.
Well, now you must be thinking WOW! A level 44 wizard can do all that?!?!?! I'm surprised Verant hasn't nerfed them already. Well, there is another side of the coin. Us wizards depend solely on our mana to inflict damage. A high level wizard without mana is like a large rat, weak and defenseless. This is the origin of the problem.
Verant needs to figure out some way to make wizards useful when they are out of mana. Here are my suggestions:
1 - We are the masters of all that is magic; therefor, I see no reason why our mana should not regenerate twice as fast as other casters, just as trolls regenerate HP twice as fast as other races. This is not clarity. It should be an innate quality built in to all wizards level 30 and higher, as that is the point where meditation times begin to become ridiculous. This would allow wizards to have less downtime from meditating, thus increasing group efficiency.

2 - Give us a pet. I'm not talking about a pet anywhere near as powerful as a magician or necro's pet, but a pet that would be much like a wizard. It should be able to hit relatively hard, yet not as hard as magician and necro pets, but it should have very little HP. This would allow wizards to benefit their teams when out of mana, yet not give wizards the benefit of a tank to assist them while soloing.

3 - Increase the wizards damage shield line of spells. Wizards only have one damage shield, the pathetic level 4 spell, O'Kiels Radiation which is a 2-damage shield. If spells were given to wizards so that they could buff people via damage shields, it would increase the wizards performance while out of mana.

I feel that any of these changes would greatly enhance the group effectiveness of a Wizard. I would like to see either 1 or 2 coupled with three. Giving wizards all three would definitely throw off class balance; however, granting two would put wizards at a respectable level of group effectiveness.
In conclusion I would like to say that wizards are not overpowered as long as we have mana. When we are out of mana or facing an enemy that is too high a level for us to hit we are currently useless. Verant must do something to correct this; we will not be ignored. Either give us pets and increase our damage shield line of spells, or give us the innate ability to regenerate mana twice as fast as other casters and an increased damage shield line of spells. This will fix the problem that is a wizards grouping burden.

Thank you for reading my post I hope you all will help aide an under powered class by coming out in support of wizards,

Fazula Magikhand
Level 44 Wizard of Brell Serelis

My current character is a level 31 enchanter. Every group that I have been in with wizards has proven them to be very effective in combat. Even without clarity. If you use your mana wisely, you will not be spending all your time meditating and you will help your group in the process. Don't try to blast every mob into oblivion in the first 10 seconds of a fight. Let your tanks, clerics and enchanters do their job. Hang back and do your damage at opportune times. It has been my personal experience that wizards are very powerful group members because they can finish off a mob before it gets to run and draw a crowd. They can also inflict massive damage if someone is in danger of dying and that alone can save someone's life. The game is designed for grouping as you get higher in levels. Change your tactics slightly and you will find yourself to be a very productive group member.

Just my 2 cents worth.
