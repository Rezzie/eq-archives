Here are the advantages:

Best damage in the game: The combo of Mage pet and DD's means no one (not even a wiz) can bring more single target damage to a fight. Especially when you start getting damage shields. Wizards have better area of effect spells, so can bring more damage to multiple creatures. This is a moot point in all groups with an Enchanter.

Best utility spells: Waterstones (Enduring Breath), Heatstones (Infravision), Coldstone (Ultravision), and Ring of Flight (Levitate) all come in the form of items. This means you can pre-cast these spells, mem something else, and hand them out when needed. If I give a warrior six waterstones he can now cast Enduring Breath on himself six times. Give a Barbarian a Coldstone and he/she has it until log out.

The bad news:

If you want to be on every groups must have list be a Shamman, Cleric, or Enchanter. These three classes are on every groups must have list.

My understanding is they have even ruled out extending the time it takes for items to disappear after LD.
