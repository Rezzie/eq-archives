You may not like the zone, but honestly UNREST is the best place for a Cleric of your level.

Why?

- You will level faster than most other zones (because finding a group in Unrest is terribly easy for a Cleric) In addition, you can make great exp if you simply team up with one tank and then pull mobs from the house.

- Loot is actually pretty good. At level 20 you and a group can take the Magi and Hand rooms allowing you to make a good amount of money.

- At 24 you'll be able to join the tower groups and try to get a Bloodstained Tunic which you can turn around and sell for roughly 400pp (depending on your server)

I admit, trains are a mess for this zone, but once you hit 24 dying becomes a great deal more difficult. You'll be smarter in the zone, know when to run and most mobs won't be able to take you down quickly.

I am now level 25, and exp is starting to flow faster than it ever has before. Unrest is my zone of choice. :-)
