If I may offer my opinions on this, these are a few of the things I think casters need to be worthwhile in the newer areas of the game.

Items effects:

Items that increase your mana pool by a percentage of your current max. Maybe 5 percent for a lower level item, and as much as 40-50% as a rare drop off of a super-uber-boss-mob_01.

Items that increase your maximum damage on DoTs and DD by a certain percentage.

Items that increase the percentage of hitpoints restored by a curing spell.

Items that decrease mana cost for ALL spells by a certain percentage.

Items that decrease the aggro effect of spells and magic damage by a certain percentage.


Spell stuff:

Spells that are inherently resisted less.

Spells that do more damage. ESPECIALLY for wizards. "Masters of magical damage" should not out-damage other nukers by a mere 20 points.

Spells that do a percentage of an NPC's total hp. Note, not a percentage of their CURRENT hp, but their total. To be balanced, these types of spells should have long recast times and an additional effect on the mob so they cannot be used by several casters at once.


Some more mana efficient spells and some more items that grant STACKABLE mana regen effects would be good, too.

I doubt all (or any, for that matter) of these things will every happen, but they would really help out the poor casters who can't do squat to these uber mobs. I am sick and tired of seeing the monopoly Warriors, Rogues and Monks have on dealing damage to mobs in Velius. Casters need to feel worthwhile too.
