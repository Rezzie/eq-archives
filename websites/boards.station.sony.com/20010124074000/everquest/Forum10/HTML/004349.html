Well the servers came back up and I had a half an hour at lunch so I got on.

I had been practicing trade skills in freeport before the patch, but there's only like 200 people on the server when I log on and I wanted to do some fighting, so I headed for WC. Got a SOW from a kind druid before I left. Hooked up with another pally along the way, but she didn't have SOW, so I ended up switching to Walk so she could keep up. Got to WC. Headed for the Dervish camps - saw an ogre standing there looking at the camp so I thought Great - we can form a big group and get all those exp bonuses. So I walked up to him and hailed him. Of course, he was a Dervish thug - nobody else was around the camp so it was just chock full of Dervishes. Even with walk on I had managed to lose my partner - so I've got like four assorted Dervishes hammering on me. I'm thinking - well she'll get here in a minute - I'm level 15 - I've got my lay hands - I should be ok.
Before I finish that thought I notice that my hit point bar is dropping like a rock.

It's at this point that I remember that when I do trade skills I always drop some of my armor at the bank so I can carry more. I do lay hands once - then stand there for another minute until I notice the 'try fighting someone other than yourself' messages because I forgot to target back on the Dervs. My partner has shown back up and she's still full bar - off fighting someone solo. My hit point bar keeps dropping and I figure I'm toast but somebody else shows up and starts hitting my crowd and says 'run' and I figure - oh yeah - I've got SOW - I can make it. So I take off and they keep hitting me and while I'm laying on the ground bleeding to death it finally dawns on me that I'm still walking.

Well, at least I got to experience one of the experience related changes.
