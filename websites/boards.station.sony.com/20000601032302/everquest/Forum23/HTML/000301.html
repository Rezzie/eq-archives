The First Order and Equinox Council have decided to merge guilds. The First Order will keep it's name and accept all members of the Equinox Council. In addition, the leaders of the Equinox Council will become officers in The First Order.

This merger is taking place becuase both guilds are relatively small and having trouble finding good members and becuase the play style of the two guilds is very similar.

Even though The First Order has become much larger, we are still recruiting new members. If you are intrested in becoming a member, please contact me directly (LegenThorn@Hotmail.com) or in game play type /who all "The First Order" to find an officer.

Guild Mission: To establish a set of mature players and form a comunity of trust amoungst ourselves.

Membership Requirements: You must be sponsered by a guild member. This means that you must group with that guild member for at least 3 hours over 2 different gaming sessions.
