I can't say I've done this quest - yet.

If you are given a container, then yes, you probably will need to combine them. Make sure you have a open base slot or you may not be given the needed quest bag. Sometimes they will be placed on your mouse cursor and other times not.
Usually the Questor will tell you to combine and bring back the container.
The quote you gave does not mention such.

HOWEVER...

What is your /con faction with Mizzle Geeple?

Many questors will tell you: "I'm.. erm, not quite sure what to do with this, but.. thanks, I guess." when you have indifferent or below faction. You should try to raise your faction to aimable or even higher. Try collecting more black boxes and plague rat glands.

I will let you know about a similar experience I had up in Halas with Lysbith Mcnaff. It seems that gathering dozens of blackburrow gnoll teeth is her first proof - to be trusted by the Wolves of the North. She does offer a couple of more quests, which I knew about, but was a tad frustrated that I couldn't do them. So.. off I go to do more Shaman guild errands and then returned later, but this time she was aimably and happy to tell me more about the pesky ice goblins. Now over at Mac's Kilts, I'd been giving Cindl Polar bear pelts and she also asked me to bring her pairs of Wrath Orc bracers. Well, one of the locals told me that Lysbith would also give a reward, but for two pair. I inquired further, but again, she was silent about them. I gave them to her anyways and "..erm, not quite sure".

Well, the next day, I returned with the bag of goblin beads, which I had combined as she requested and THEN she gave me the next challenge to help the Wolves of the North which was return four Wrath Orc bracers.

So there may be other dependencies than just the items themselves given to the correct Questor.

If I recall, the Bard Sylia in Kelethin asks for spiderling silks, but her FACTION is Songweavers which is different than the LegueOfAntocnican Bards. I've already done several Mail runs across Norrath and I know I'm Kindly amoungst them. I don't know of any Faydark quests offered yet that will raise Songweavers, so that does sound like a /buggable quest issue. Now if you had started out as a Wood Elf Bard, I'm sure you can bring here dozens of silkings, so technically the quest is not broken.
