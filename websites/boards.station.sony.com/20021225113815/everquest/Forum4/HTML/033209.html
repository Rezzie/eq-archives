Good answers by the others.

The bandages are indeed expensive but consider that the spiderling threads can sell for as much as 10pp+ each in the bazaar (typical closer to 4-7). You should be able to go to the bazaar and do an auction of silk threads or even just the spiderling silks for 1 or 2pp each and have them gone in a heartbeat if you need immediate money. If nothing else, those who are selling them will buy them to sell at a markup.

As for whether this is something most healers use, unfortunately the answer is No. Most people who have healing spells seem to be of the opinion that "I have heal spells, why do I need my bind wounds skill?". Well, the answer is when you are OOM and low on health, bind wounds will let you get to a safer level to med at not to mention that if you can bind wounds and get your tank to half, you use half as much mana getting him or her healed. Many argue "I can med the mana back faster". In 30 seconds I can bind wounds and get back 150+ hit points. There are also times when sitting down isnt an option. In many areas, if you sit, you get the aggro seemingly no matter how far away from the mob you are at the time. Binding your partner's wounds might be the only way to keep him/her alive when you are OOM.

The tradeskill recipe books are where the original recipes came from way back when. Other times they come from talking with NPC's but most of them came out in the books you see. These days with EQtraders.com most recipes which are listed there have come from those books you see. There are some who believe that having the book open while doing the combines will help your success rate. This is false or at least has never been proven. Its like faith, you cant argue with people on it. If they believe it works, to them it matters:-)

The newsletters were coming out pretty much every patch for a while but have seemed to stop being updated for many months now. There is no practicle use for them. They are simply the newspapers of the day.

Regarding the trainers, I know people in RL who speak different languages. That doesnt mean I could go to those who speak Spanish as well as English and get them to teach me French since they dont know French. Same applies. Not all trainers are trained in all arts. Most of them are but some have skills they dont know. The Enchanter Trainer in Shadowhaven apparently wasnt taught research so for my enchanter to train that one time to learn the basics I need to go elsewhere.

Wish you luck!

Safe fall is a racial skill for some and a class skill for others. All Vah Shir start with a safe fall skill of 50. Not sure if any other races start off with it.

But unless your class eventual gets the skill as well, then your skill will stay at 50 and it won't got up. Players with safe fall as a class skill will start with thier score at whatever level they first train it. Since it is a class skill, it will gradualy go up until it caps. Different classes have different caps for this skill if they have it at all.

Your best source of cash is right in your hands... Spiderling Silks.

Those simple little things are worth 5pp EACH. That's 100pp for each stack of 20. I can routinely sell as many stacks as I have for that price without any problems by leaving my character in the bazaar for an overnight stint.

Do NOT combine them into threads. Other players tend to do bazaar searches for the silks more than the threads, and as such, you will sell the silks faster.

Above all, do NOT combine them in into bandages since players can buy those from NPCs or summon their own.

I would point out that what you get for spiderling silk, and if you are able to get it sold over night at all, is dependent on what server you play on as well as the current amount of farmers flooding the bazaar.

On FV, it's not uncommon not to get that silk sold, even if you set a price as low as 1pp a piece. Low population, lots of newbies who are hoping to make a fortune. I don't really bother with spiderling silks at all anymore, unless I have some other reason to be in the bazaar and have found some silks on vendors. I definetely don't have the patience to spend hours farming it, only to have to wait 3 days to get my 50 (or whatever) pp.
