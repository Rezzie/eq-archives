You might prefer the Polished Bone Bracers from evil eyes in the mino maze to the grass bracers from the scryer. +3 wis instead of +12 hp. You don't need the wisdom, sure, but they're more tradable and easier to get.

Faction on the scryer is just like any other froglok. They'll hate you eventually. I wouldn't worry about being KOS in Guk. Some people like to preserve their live frog faction to make it easier to get to LGuk and camp loot.

No faction on the AC that I recall.

You'll have a tough time by yourself. The scryer can charm your pet. (Or you, which is sort of depressing if you're alone.) You'll want snare to stop the runners; frogs run early, and fast. Take a ranger with good resist gear with you, or a bunch of nukers.
