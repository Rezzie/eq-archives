- Verant tries DESPERATLY to make encounters next to impossible for guilds like FOH, Afterlife, LOS and the like. They try to challange them, and nerf their tactics and buff the monsters to make encounters harder for them.

- This has a negative trickle-down effect. In the past, this trickle-down effect usualy only effected other large guilds who would be doing the same thing in a few months time.

ie: FOH kills Lady Vox, they kill her again and again and again. Verant buffs lady vox, she gets harder. FOH has previous expierence at killing vox and adapt, and ahve better items from vox .. and continue to kill. This cycle repeats 2 or 3 more times. Then "another group" comes along. - Hate
- Fear
- Sky
- Kunark
- Vox
- Nag
- Inno
- Cazic
