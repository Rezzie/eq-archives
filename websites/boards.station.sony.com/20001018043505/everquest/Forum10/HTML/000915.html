Ok, before I tell you guys this please keep the flames in good humor. This happened with my 1st charachter the first time I played the game. I took a gnome necro and learned from the people around me that I was supposed to kill snakes and stuff to get xp. So I did. Figured out how to sell to merchants and all that stuff. (also learned very quickly what kos is beong a necro). Anyway a "high" lvl 16 necro started telling me the basics, and that I can get more spells now that I was lvl four other than the 2 I started with. He showed the necro place in akanon and I got my new spells. He was telling me all the cool stuff in the game, but the one thing that he said that I thought was really awesome is that necros can turn themselves into skeletons when they get to a high lvl. So anyway I get all my spells scribe/mem them and go back to killing small wild life. I was trying out knew spells when I came to one that said that I needed bone chips for it. I had killed quite a few skeletons and I knew what they were so I got some and recast the spell. Nothing happened. So I backed up and started to get back mana a nd health and a skeleton jumped in front of me. Startled I got up and attacked it. Didn't do any damage and it wasn't attacking me back, than I noticed the name. AHHH, a pc - and he looks like a skeleton, he must be high lvl!!! So I start talking to him and asking him if he knows what my spells do and that jerk wouldn't answer me. After a while I gave up and said something to him to the effect that he was rude and stuck up because he wouldn't talk to a newbee. I went back to kill rats and things and do you know what that S.O.B. did? He tried to KS me!! (I found out at that time what it was because I was accused of it not knowing any better). I start really laying into this guy calling him every name you can think of. Asking what kind of fun was it for someone as powerful as him to come to a newbee zone and ks rats from a lvl 4. After a while I just started making fun of him because I kept getting all the xp. At any rate this went on for about 2 hours, when some elf cam up to me and said "dude, who are you talking to?" I explained the whole story to him and he told me what a pet was. Go ahead - flame away
