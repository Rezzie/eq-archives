Based on the patch message, I am guessing you made the decision not to make any changes to the existing twigs and branches in the game. Can you confirm this? (I say this because I would not expect you to fix an item, by adding classes to it, if you intended to nerf it).

If this is the case, I wish to commend you on this decision. I think it was the right one. The reason I think so, is these items have an inherant anti-twink factor. I would go so far as to say these items are the sorts of things that should be found on Trakanon and Veshans Peak. Without needed a NO DROP flag, they are only good for people over level 50 or so, AND they get better relative to other items the higher level the user.

Yes, they dropped off level 20-30 mobs, and that is bad. However, it is level 55s and 60s who are using these items. So some people got lucky. This is no different than those who got some great items in the first weeks of kunark. Because it is very high level players using these items, it does not throw the game out of wack, like it would if a bunch of level 30 players were using items better than what level 60s had.

I would like to see Verrant item designers take a lesson from this, and make some moss covered like weapons found in very high level zones. It's probably too late for FA (which if upgraded, will still undoubtably remain 2HS), but some of the other class uber weapons could be made with very low delays. I believe this should be done (although it is best for classes wanting to tank, ie paladins, warriors, shadowknights, because of the great taunt). So, make the SK ubersword 3/10 or the like, with FA like stats. Or the warrior uber-sword 4/11 with stats. These low delay weapons are perfect as high level items, _because_ they are no good for low level players. I think this is a very desirable factor for uber items.

-Iustus
