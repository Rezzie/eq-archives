Click on the book in your starting area to take you to PoK. In PoK, use your map (backspace key) to locate the "Nexus" stone. Click on Nexus stone to be transported there. You will pop into the Nexus faceing the book back to PoK. Turn 180 degrees and head into the grand hall. Take a right out of the hallway into the grand hall. Take the next hall on your right from there. Go down the hall and take the left. That will take you to the Bazaar zoneline.

( Your maps should work in all zones you will pass through. )

Outside the city of Halas, in Everfrost, is a pedastal with an open book on it. This is a portal to take you to the Plane of Knowledge (PoK). This is what they are refering to as a "book."

Once you have gotten to the Nexus from PoK, run the opposite direction from the book that you'll be facing. You'll be in a circular room, the main room of the nexus. Face South and run to the hall, then follow it into the Bazaar.

An easy way to remember which hallway in the nexus to take, think of this...$ is $outh, so when you want to make or spend your $, go $outh.

The closest Spires to Neriak would be in North Karanas. They are lovingly patrolled by some guards that would love to run a sharp steel blade through a wicked Dark Elf such as yourself.

The Nexus Scion is tolerant of all, though, and will grant you the travel Shard... best remain invisible afterwards 'til the Spires shoot off... roughly once every 15 minutes.

There is a functional Wizard Platform in Nektulous Forest, btw. You could always arrange to meet a Wizard there, instead. Getting a wizzie to touch down in foul Tier'Dal country may require a bribe of some kind, to ease their guilty conscience.

Rather than taint (or possibly corrupt) the upstanding Wizards of Norrath, if you end up dashing to the North Karana Spires...

Neriak >>
Nektulous Forest >>
East Commonlands >>
West Commonlands >>
Kithicor Forest >>
Highpass >>
East Karanas >>
North Karanas >>
Spires.

You can safely bind in Shadowhaven, once arrived in The Nexus. From there, back in the Nexus you will have access to the "return" Spires to 5 different points of the globe.
