People are getting way off topic here with lots of wish lists and class envy things. If you are not a necro that has played at least a year or you are not 50 or above, please dont post here. You have no clue of the class.

1. On uber mob fights. Uber mobs (dragons, etc) in general are almost totally immune to magic. Most of our spells are magic based, therefore we can do little direct dmg. Pets can take the place of our missing magic dmg by supplementing melee dmg, but as everyone knows, pets are feared 90% of time in these fights. I do not see why Verant has a huge problem with our pets being immune to fear , disease or poison. Arent mage pets immune to fire, etc? So does that mean they will give fire immunity to all pet users? No. I think perhaps since there is little dmg or hp difference in our hard earned post 50 pets, perhaps one could be immune to fear, one to disease, one to poison. Or perhaps give necros a general pet buff that can raise resistances significantly (at least enough to matter).

2. They point out we may have little to do during the fight but can help before or after. I dont know if you guys have noticed, but epic mobs take a certain number of people. Any more, and you risk crashing the zone or causing massive lds which will destroy the fight, any less and you are also doomed to failure. Usually 35-50 is acceptable. With so few limited spaces, they are A LOT less likely to invite necromancers along. Sure we can help with mana regen (slightly), but if that means there will be room for one less wizard that will be able to do 8k of dmg during the fight, guess who will be left at home? Sure we can summon bodies if the raid fails, but I have news for you. Necros will not be on the raid, but will be called in from wherever they are to assist afterwards. Thats really fun. Let me drop all that i am doing to assist this raid i will get jack from.

Necromancers are damage dealers pure and simple, epic mobs are one of the few beasts that last long enough for our dots to effectively work on, however what is so frustrating is they are immune. Can I give healing and mana during the fight to help? Sure, but where am i getting the replacement hp? I sure cant tap anything, and the cleric is busy healing the tanks. This leads to the necro usually falling prey to the mob's aoe as he cannot heal himself. "dont worry we will rez ya after the fight".

Is it not possible to perhaps make the deflux line less resistable if not unresistable? All i know is the class quickly deteoriates after 50. It is a solo class... at 56 most mobs in skyfire become green. So, move over to the skyfire drakes and elder wyverns...um they summon; impossible to solo. at the 56+ range, the class become near impossible to do anything with! If i want to sit around and summon corpses at cost, hell im in business. Otherwise, im left to trying to pick through mobs on the far side of skyfire praying i dont run into a summoner. (on a side not, why do such mundane monsters have the ability to summon? i believe the summoning lvl should be raised to 53 at least).

Pet pathing: A really really good nec that has nothing at all to do can probably keep a pet in perfect control in a dungeon. Dont cast spells, no giving mana, no heals, no dots, nothing. Walk backwards watching your pet. Why is it SO hard to have a pet follow DIRECTLY in its master's footsteps? Why does it arbitrarily turn around and run the opposite way and around the corner? is it on a different tracking system? If a krup was on my tail and i am running, what are the chances it will suddenly turn around and run away?? 0! If pets pathing was made 100% more reliable, necros would at least have an opportunity to group in dungeons.

Also, a command that tells the pet to not attack even if the master is attacked would be GREAT.

the group feign death: an excellent idea. this would raise necro grouping tremendously. if you are going to change how the class works post 50, at least give us a chance (from solo to group). other ideas: group levant, or aoe fear spell. this would give group time to evac or something, at least buy some time.

the mana giving and hp giving spells are nice bells and whistles. but groups have very very limited space:
1. Warrior
2. Cleric
3. Enchanter
4. Evac (druid wiz)
5. Rogue/Monk (pulling or locks)
6. Wiz/Druid/Monk/Rogue/shammy (whatever spot wasnt taken above.

What can a nec bring to a group looking to fill that last spot that would be better? Not much really. we have a pet we cant use, dots that dont work in 20 sec(fight time) and ruin mez if tank changes targets.

There need to be some changes made. Sure, we can still solo stuff, but nothing like in the past. The mob lvl (summoning, 15k hp mobs , etc) has determined we have to move away from soloing to grouping, yet we arent given any viable group weapons. Druids and wizards and mages can solo just as well as a necro, but also can easily get into a group at any time. Necromancers are the only class that apparently do a complete role reversal post 50, yet are given nothing to do that with.
