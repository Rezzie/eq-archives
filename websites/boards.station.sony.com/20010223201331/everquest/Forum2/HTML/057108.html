I started opening weekend and have become an EverLowbie, but I really don't mind. My stable includes a dozen over several servers.

I tend to play on different servers which fit a variety of moods. Bristlebane hosts my Troll & Iksar while Morrell-Thule the enjoyment of /Roleplaying. My first is Veeshan.

I have a trio on Test, but ever since the item-wipe, they haven't been much fun and more work to get back to bare essentials like patchwork and other than a rusty weapon.

If you are getting squoze for characters, so probably are your friends on the same server. Pick another that each agrees to. Or drive the choice yourself.

One of my often recommendations is using disposable newbies to learn zones and faction quests. It's a lot easier to travel and see sites that your first wouldn't until later in level or opportunity to relocate bind points.
