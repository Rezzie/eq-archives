Heya monks and those of you not monks,

Patches are patches ... lived through lots of them in 3+ years as an EQ player. I dont much mind this patch really.

What I mind is the constant bashing of monks on these pages and the over-the-top complaining by some monks on this patch. Just play the game or dont play it if it upsets you.

That doesnt mean I understand the patch though. The decrease in mitigation was fine really. Plate should mitigate more damage than leather. Although if you add the HP factor which is actually part of mitigation ... then it makes less sense. There is no reason a warrior should have more HPs than a monk. All melee classes should be on the same HP scale, then shamans and priests, and finally another scale for pure casters. Then they should have a range for race...small, medium, and large...but enough babbling...Verant will do what they do...

The other thing I dont like about this patch much is the increase in dodge for all other classes. It makes no sense to me at all... They argue for realism in and then they make heavy plate wearers and chain wearers dodge better. Speaking of which...how do chain wearing rogues dodge as well as us leather wearing monks...and how do they sneak in chain...or sneak-run in it at high levels. And on top of it they even gave an INCREASE in mitigation for some classes. Why decrease monks and then give all other melee classes two boosts?

This is just a few of the reasons there is so much arguing on message boards lately by monks and at monks. But Verant says they are changing MOBs also...maybe this will all even out. Just need to sit back and see I guess. Maybe Verant knows what they are doing...and maybe not...only time will tell.
