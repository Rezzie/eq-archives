The very best two handed weapon is... Trantor's Tusk. 40/60, 2h-piercing. At least, it should be.

Why?

Because there is a "damage bonus" for weapons with a delay of 40 or better.

This is why the Tempered Velium Claidhmore (32/35) is nice, but a Wurmslayer (25/40) will outdamage it on the long haul.

Now, if there were a minimum damage level for weapons... two handed weapons would be better.

As it stands, dual wielding is ALWAYS better... unless it's against a mob with a damage shield.
