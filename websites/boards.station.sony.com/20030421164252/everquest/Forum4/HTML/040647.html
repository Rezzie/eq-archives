Actually, old world zones like Unrest have become great havens for soloers or good training grounds for Raiding practice, but rarely both

Sounds like a great night! It's just getting harder and harder for younger levels to find pickup groups outside of whatever is considered the safest/best XP at those levels. Generally, this means the "powerlevel" zones of places like Crushbone, Oasis, Lake, Paludal, etc. The world is getting far larger while the amount of players per server isn't going up.
