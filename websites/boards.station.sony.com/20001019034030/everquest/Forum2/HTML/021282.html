I have been playing this game on and off for the last year and a half. I have seen a great many things. I have been a guide for a few months on two different servers. I have played my play character all the way up to the mid 50's and have alts ranging from level 14 up to level 34. I'd say I've seen my fair share of issues come up in game as well as out of game. But never before have I been so frustrated and disturbed as I was last night.

It seems a player from my play server has been banned. Now, thats not a big deal, right? Well she was banned for posting a story on an indipendant messageboard. Now, I'll be the first to admit that the story was a bit on the graphic side. But how far can Verant and the customer service staff go to banning someone? This was not posted on a Sony-owned board nor a Verant owned board. This was posted on eqvault's boards. If EQVault found it necessary to ban her from their board, then fine. But I don't see how Verant could come onto that board, read what is posted there and determine that it's okay to apply what's posted there to someone's IN GAME account.

Gordon's post on the Gameplay forum was "We make determinations based on information at hand regarding who is or is not having a positive affect on EverQuest's community. If we determine that one person's actions make EverQuest a game that other people do not want to play based upon those actions, we will excercise our right to refuse service to the extent necessary to provide a reasonable and enjoyable gaming environment."

Now, does that mean that if I type up a review on Everquest as a game and decide that its utter crap for my own reasons that I will be banned as well? I mean, it wouldnt have a positive effect on the community because it would keep people from buying and wanting to play the game if my opinion was valued enough, no?

I see this banning as stretching the limits of Verant's authority. I'm ashamed to say that I have stuck up for the actions that Verant has made up until now. No longer will I continue to do so if actions like this are made.

A game like this is supposed to inspire creativity. It's supposed to stretch the imagination. The story this person wrote was VERY WELL WRITTEN. If it was on any other topic, it would be praised. But it was honest. It was about dark elves. It was about necromancers. It was about hatred and torture. Now, its okay for the programmers to put torture racks in Neriak, but its not okay for us to write about them on indipendant messageboards?

I've probably said too much, and I'll probably get my guide account taken away for stating my opinion, but that's okay. I'm frustrated and I am not sure that I could go back to my guide account and remain a supporter of Verant Interactive in that way anyway. I thank you all for taking the time to read what I have to say. I would really appreciate hearing what you all have to say in reply.

Jessie Lawrence
AKA Sithic the Creative
