First, the epic -- it aint.

The 2H one has a nice ratio, but I've heard rumors of better.

Both of the 1H'ers can be beat by drops off blues and such in dragon necropolis (info from a 57+ warrior).

Most of the other epics are good enough that one would not be using another weapon... period. The relative difficulty of the quests is completely laughable as well, ours requires drops from Kunark dragons, which can be somewhat rare, and a rare drop from the Maestro. Add on top of that having to slaughter the queen, and you have a damned hard quest.

They need to be fixed to be somewhat worth the risk involved. 2H could use getting beefed to one of the monk staves, 36/38 or so would be nice.

The 1H'ers need their delay reduced so they can outdamage other more easily attainable combos, and taunt better than a lamentation.

The Tempered Velium Claymore has a better ratio than the epic, 32/35, about 1.09. Monks get items with the sick ratio of 0.875. Give us a 2H with a straight 1.0 ratio. 40/40 would be nice.

There are no less than 11 1H weapons with a better ratio than ours. About 3 of them are warrior usable, and two more are epics for the other two dual wielding slashers. The Wurmslayer beats our epic quite well on straight up melee damage. Putting them at about 18/22 would be about right I think.

2H vs 1H -- you need to look into this. Right now 1H so overpowers 2H it's sickening. 1H both does more damage AND taunts more effectively due to the greater number of attacks. This should be fixed so that they are about equal once again.

The wurmslayer was the last straw on this. You get the damage output of a quite decent 2H (Razing Sword of Skarlon) PLUS the damage of the offhand. That was an insult to all 2H using warriors in the game. Your "fix" to help 2H vs obscenely low delay 1H'ers helped the wurmslayer as well.

Critical hits (and crippling blows) are so rare using 2H as to be ignored, and most of the time hit for less than a maximum hit normal slash. 3/4 of my crippling blows with a Soul Leech are for less than 150. Most of them hit for about 120, after the 18 point level/str bonus. That's the Cripplers. Most of my criticals (ignoring bashes, which make up about 30% of both) hit at least as hard as my cripplers, if not harder. They also happen about once every 6 fights.

Let's compare someone dual wielding two lamentations for instance, vs a Jagged Blade of War.

Unhasted, the JBoW is going to swing once every 3.6 seconds. The Lamentations are going to swing every 1.9. Throwing double attack into the equation, that's 2 possible hits for the JBoW, 8 for the lamentations. On average the lamentations are going to have 4X as many crits. Usual crit with a lammy ends up at about 45-60, usual with the JBoW is probably about 120, going by an avg crit of 100 at damage 30. Triple attack at 60 doesn't help this any at all.

In terms of raw damage, the problem is due to the "sweet spot" damage range of a lamentation being between about 20 and 35, where a Soul Leech is about 60-120. The total ranges for them are about 9-50 and 21-135 respectively. They miss about the same regardless. Misses with a lammy do not hurt total output NEAR as much as misses with a Soul Leech. Minimum hits with a lamentation do not hurt total output NEAR as much as minimum hits with a Soul Leech.

2H damage output flys all over the board at the moment, whereas 1H is typically fairly constant. At the very least, make the minimum hit with a weapon equal to the weapon's rated damage, my minimum has been around 20 no matter WHAT 2H I use. The range of values needs to be tightened.

The wurmslayer gets you the damage output of a good 2H plus the advantage of an offhand weapon, for 5k or a few hours questing if you're lucky on drops.

The sheer number of attacks the 1H'ers generate also allow them to taunt better.

2H should be better at one of these, either damage or taunting. Please get a real warrior to play and test these, because the current quest rewards and such haven't indicated that you are.

If rumors are true, and the cast time on a cobalt breastplate is 34 seconds, then it needs to be lowered. At 34 seconds, a fungi tunic heals you faster, doesn't require you to stand completely still, and works mid-fight in a number of cases. Remember that we cannot move at all during the cast, and hotkeys are disabled. Also consider that we have NO channeling skill, so there is no chance of us recovering if we get hit.

The various warrior related quests and such are laughable, and some downright insulting in terms of risk/reward. The epic and wurmslayer come to mind, especially when compared with items you can acquire by camping various static spawns in sebilis and velious dungeons.

Overall, I'm quite happy with the class as is. My main wish is that 2H be just as viable as 1H, so I dont' end up looking like every other warrior on the server, and dualwielding a lammy/wurmslayer and a CSS or some other decent offhand.
