To climb down a ladder, do what you would do in real life-- face in the direction you'd be facing if you'd just finished climbing the ladder. Back up until you start to fall, then quickly move forward. Your character will contact the ladder and cease falling. Back up again to descend further, move forward to stop. Repeat as necessary.

To be a little more specific on ladder climbing.

Approach the ladder normally, preferably straight on rather than at an angle. Use the Page-Up button to look up to the ladder's top. Press your Up arrow to move forward and thus climb the ladder. Use the left/right arrows to make sure you stay on the ladder.

Once at the top, just keep climbing upwards and you will eventually leap off the ladder onto your destination platform.

To climb down, do as KatLover suggests. It's not really possible to climb down when starting at the top. It's more like you jump at the ladder and kinda stick to it. Then you press your Down arrow button to unstick, and then the Up arrow again real quick so that you re-stick and don't drop all the way to the bottom.

Climbing down is often so tricky that most players just don't bother. In most cases, it's easier to just jump and take any falling damage, then heal up. Of course, this does not apply to extreme heights.
