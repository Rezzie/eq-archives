The 1500 number was pre-Kunark.

You don't seem to be aware of how EQ is built.
Each "server" (i e "instance of the world"), pre-
Kunark, was run on 13 NT servers. Some of those
servers only served a few zones with high loads;
others serve many more zones which are typically
not so populated.

When Kunark came out, they added more machines
for all the new Kunark zones. Thus, people playing
in Kunark do not impact your machine's load at
all. Now, suppose Kithicor forest and Qeynos were
sharing a physical machine. If a lot of people
suddenly crowded to Kithicor, Qeynos would also
get more laggy. However, as long as they spread
the zones in a reasonable way across the
machines, they can easily run with 2000-2500
people per "server" because the now have many
more machines per "server".

Incidentally, I believe this is why you sometimes
see messages like "These 5 zones will go down in
10 minutes, and then be right up again: ....".
Those zones share a machine, and that machine
needs a kick in the behind for some reason.

Anyway, given the attitude in the original post,
I wouldn't want to be grouped with that player,
so no doubt he's having a hard time finding
enjoyment in the game if others feel the same.
