While you're fixing archery bugs please fix the way that critical hits (don't) stack with double damage hits.

For example, a 'normal' hit is 1 - 23 damage with dam 2 arrows and Rain Caller, and the usual critical hit is 45... note this is an *odd* number. When shooting against a stationary, non rooted target (which isn't blocked by tanks in melee against it) double damage hits are possible.

Quite often (before the bug was introduced) the double damage hit was doing more damage than a 'critical' hit.

What happens then on the 'once in a blue moon' occurence of a double damage critical then? You might expect that the critical hit was determined and then the damage doubled... unfortunately it doesn't work like that. The double damage is applied, and then the critical hit is applied, overwriting the damage calculated for the double damage hit... you can see this easily because although a message that "Your shot did double damage" is displayed, the damage scored *is still an odd number*... even my 5 year old nephew knows that if you double any number you always end up with an even number

This should be a relatively straight forward fix... just make the critical hit check before the double damage check, and then double the critical hit damage instead of overwriting the double damage hit with a 'single damage' critical hit. Of course it's never quite that easy but I hope you can see what I'm getting at
