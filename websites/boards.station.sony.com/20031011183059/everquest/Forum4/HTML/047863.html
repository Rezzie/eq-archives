Im not sure why I haven't thought of that. I run a barbarian two box with a barb warrior and barb shammie. I think a troll (or even ogre) combo would probably do much much better. Mainly because the SK has awesome taunt capabilites, can fear, and snare, and has a pet (even though its weak).

1. Snare/Fear Kiting with pets. Have your shammie and SK bring up their pets. Have the SK pull, fear, and snare the MoB. The three of you (2 pets and the SK) beat it down while its on the run.
2. Snare/Fear Kiting with Pets and DoTs. For those high HP MoBs (like in burning wood, skyshrine, etc), do the same. Have the SK pull and tank the MoB to gain enough aggro. Send in the pets. Start stacking shaman poison dots until the SK looses aggro. This will give the SK ample time to fear it once it breaks for the shaman. You then have a badly poisoned MoB that has allready been beaten half to death, running afraid snared, with two pets on its tail.

FD Chain Pulling combined with Root/Rot. Have the SK pull MoB's to the camp. You DoT while the SK maintains taunt. Once the MoB is to the point where poison alone will kill it, root it, sit down, send the SK out for another MoB. Effective chain pulling.
