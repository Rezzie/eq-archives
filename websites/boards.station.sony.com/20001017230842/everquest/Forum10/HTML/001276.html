Station Member 		posted 09-26-2000 11:31 PM           
As many know i finally, FINALLY am going to the planes. And today was the first day.

Essentially it started out with me logging on in Field of Bone. Across the guild chat i see people shouting "Run for it now and camp" or "Am i gettin a res?" even a few, "Where is the spot?" scrolling up.

I said to myself "Oh crap i'm missing it" and quickly announce my intention to go, and see if there is anyone around that could teleport me. There wasn't so i ended up running to Overthere and auctioning for one (100p for a tellie ) It took me less than a minute to get one, for some odd reason

As some of you may/may not know, the gate to Plane of Fear is located in Feerot, and guarded by a number of spectres and a necromancer that is about L40 odd (blue to me at 47). I knew it was in Feerot, but i did NOT know the actual location of the gate. Thankfully another guildmate of mine had to gate out of fear, and i was able to follow him (along with 2 other guildmates) to the portal. (DAMN i forgot to get a shot)

We stood outside the gate, not getting anything over guild chat for a bit, until the raid coordinator (guild leader too) told us to zone in and run for it. My job was to keep the creatures off the cleric i was accompanyin (more on that later). The second we zone in we take off running straight at a huge wall of flame. By this time we had attracted the attention of several 'friends' (2 scarelings and 2 shiverbacks and 3 spinechiller spiders) While i didnt get to do my job (there is no way i can taunt 7 things off a cleric that all hit for over 140 each fast enough), we managed to get to the "safe spot" where i quickly feigned death, and the cleric just.. well, died. Now this may seem like a disaster but it isnt unheard of -- we already had a ressurrection capable cleric camped there. During my feign i talked to the guild leader who was also feigned (a monk) and then i did /q. (Doing /q while feigned eliminates your name from the hate list of things without posing any danger to yourself)

I logged onto an alternate character i have (L28 iksar necro) and proceeded to the Commons to meet with another guildmate's alt. (My alt isnt in the guild) Where we waited for the log on signal.

As soon as it was given i was back in Fear. Along with me were 13 other people (kinda small, but the job we had was important). After some minor confusion we were able to successfully recover the bodies of a few people that died on the way to the 'safe spot' Within 30 minutes everyone was res'd and ready to go. Then we started to hunt.

We took down several shiverback (BIG monkeys), a couple spine chiller spiders (look like spiderlings but are pure white_, a few scarelings (think imps), and two glare lords (Miniature-sized evil eyes) No one died during these until one bad pull. Which had 2 scarelings and 2 fright fingers (teeny tiny reanimated hands). These were to much and pretty much everyone died (but one cleric, who managed to camp) and i died last, amusingly 1 of the scarelings and 1 of the frightfingers kept summoning me out from each other

It took us about an hour to recover from that one When we got back together (res'd and all) we actually gained 2 more people. (an enchanter and another warrior) At this point we had a good chunk of the area secured and moved to a different spot which was flatter and more open. Then more trouble. After 2 good, well done pulls of a single shiverback each, someone (who wasn't in guild, not saying they are bad but they did not listen to directions) grabbed 2 of the very things i *want* to kill very badly; Amygdalan Knights. They're massive octopus headed shadowknights, both were L50 or 51. Originally we weren't doing that bad against them, but then one went haywire on the enchanters we had, killing them and proceeding to kill all the clerics (this time no clerics lived ) and once again i died last.

After an hour we got ourselves set up once again, and this time it proceeded very smoothly. We pulled several scarelings in succession and then a pair of shiverback (2 shiverback at once, IMO one of the easier to kill things there).

After this, we were able to call in the cavalry and an additional 12 people showed up. We didnt get a chance to 'use' them but our goal today was to merely break our group into the plane. out of all the things we killed we got 1 cryosilk swatch, shiverback tunic, shiverback gloves, some odd-named Rogue weapon and a shoddy wand with charges of lava shield on it.

We weren't able to go for the amygdalans that day i wish we had killed those two -- i've been looking forward to umbral for a long while.

Even through the deaths and the downtime, i had fun. It was dangerous, new, exciting, and i am VERY much looking forward to tomorrow, where we may actually bring down Cazic Thule

- Dran
