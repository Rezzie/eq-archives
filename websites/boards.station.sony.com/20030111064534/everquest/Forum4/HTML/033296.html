I think the Warrens has an XP bonus, but it's not a large once; certainly much less than Paludal. I'm not sure which bugs danewhitel was fighting in Paludal, but at 13 my Mage was fighting white and yellow cons and getting a large fraction of a yellow of XP per kill... as a Necro you can probably do the same.

My favorite low-level hunting spot in Paludal is a tunnel where there are about six static bug spawns and one wanderer. You enter from Shadow Haven, go invis, through the first bandit camp, turn right, then follow the ledge full of red-con fiends to the third tunnel leading up to your right. Then take the first left and you're at the bug tunnel; start at the top killing the wanderer, then you can work your way down until the wanderer respawns and then head back to the top.

AFAIR at 13 my Mage could kill the wanderer and three of the static mobs in one spawn cycle, by 17 I could kill all of them and the shrooms at the bottom... I think I got to 18 from 13 in about 4 hours (but had to take a break in the middle to get the new pet spell). Just watch out for the occasional fiend train if people are fighting them in the cave at the end of the tunnel.

At 18 you should be able to move on to the Fiends, though my Necro didn't start until 20, having spent time in the Warrens and then killing Paineel guards for cash.

As for Shadow Haven merchants, you shouldn't have a problem with most of them, but some won't trade. You can do the Trader quest I mentioned yesterday to get a faction increase and an amulet that should raise faction further with those who won't otherwise trade with you; takes about five minutes if you bind in front of the guy who gives you the quest (then you only have to run one way and can Gate back).
