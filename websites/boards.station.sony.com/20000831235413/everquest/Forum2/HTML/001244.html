I have been requesting "fun" spells for magicians in game feedback for some time, but no one seems to pay any mind to this. By name, a magician should be something of a trickster. Surely there are many possibilities for spells which enhance the magician experience and the experience of those around them without affecting battle. A spell which turns water to wine would be quite a hit. What about one that allows elementals to appear as something less generic. For instance: a fire elemental might be a phoenix, an air elemental might be a dust devil. Should a magician not be able to produce a rabbit from a hat? A dove from a sleeve? Such things would be harmless and yet compelling. And certainly, one should not have to wait til levl 50+ to perform parlor tricks. These should be introduced as low-mid level spells. I have to say that, in general, I love being a magician. But EQ on the whole needs more substance. More story, more politics, more dynamism, more fun. In Meridian 59, you could have presents gift-wrapped to give to others. You could go to an inn and buy a chessboard and actually play chess with someone. It's these nuances that make a great game. And it's nuances like this that would round out the magician.
