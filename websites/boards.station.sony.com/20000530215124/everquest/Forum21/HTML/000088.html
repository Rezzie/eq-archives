Don't be discouraged!

I for one have a history and background for both of my main characters. I seldom use thr /roleplay flag because it sets my class and level anonymous, thereby dpriving people of my services.

I have a lvl 26 Ranger named Ernathe Darkelfbane and a 31 Necromancer named Venadin D'Myz. Look for me, I am always happy to roleplay. Not all characters can be measured by power as in what level and lott they carry... my ranger is a typical RP character: Low level for the time played, equipment less than optimal. But every piece has a story, and certain items, although less then optimal, will never be chantged for the story of its acqusition that follows it. His level does not discourage him from attempting any task set before him... and what amazes many other "powergamers" is that he often succeds.

Venadin is evil without a doubt and is strongly motivated by personal gain. He is a Teir D'al of his word in all cases, for why lie when the truth is a much better vehicle for hate? He is rather well equipped for his level, and has an in game justification for his greed. Yes, I consider him a powergamer for certain, but he is an evil elf looking for the shortest path to power, and well, he is succeeding. Kinda like life that way.

The factor that tempers both of my characters is that as a player I accept that there are many people out there who pay ten bucks a month just to play the game engine, and if I can help their fun, fantastic. Ernathe is a hero who helps anyone except Dark elves, for it is in his nature. Venadin helps himself, but in all cases I will help another player in trouble and edit my Roleplay accordingly so they can play the game. and if the RP inspires them, then I would like to think I left them a better gamer for it.
