You don't have to use the entire name in your /who, even if the guild has a multiple word name.

try /who all "dragons "
try /who all "norrath "

(And yes, I use double quotes. And yes, I leave a blank space after the search-word and before the closing quote.)

If there is a guild named "Dragon Keepers" and another called "Dragon Revolt", the /who all "dragon " will list the Keepers (since K comes before R).

You'd have to use /who all "dragon r" to find the Revolt folks.
