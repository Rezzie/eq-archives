You want to go to Morrel-thule the unofficial RP server and get into a RP guild. We have several guilds working together, including leaders meeting in a RP council.

To give you an idea of the type of events, right now my guild, The Order of the White Hand, dedicated to Innoruuk, has used the occasion of a combined raid on Mistmoore to, with help of a few other guilds, kidnap the leader of the Mark of the Draconians, and we're marrying her off to one of ours this Sunday in the Arena. Security to be provided by a mercenary RP guild and a neutral guild.

All together, probably 100-200 people (gods know how many alts) involved in that particular event/storyline.

And it's not just organized events, a number of us were on a journey out of Nektulos and just met some Draconians and duels and debates on good and evil followed. Entirely unplanned.

I don't see anything like this happening on other servers.

http://www.morellthule.com/
http://www.geocities.com/otwh
http://www.draconians.com/

If you don't want to go to MT, there are great roleplaying guilds on other servers, but honestly, if you add them all up, I'm not sure you'd have as many people as you have on MT. I was looking to start an evil RP guild on Vaz and when I saw what was going on in MT, I just restarted my char from scratch.
