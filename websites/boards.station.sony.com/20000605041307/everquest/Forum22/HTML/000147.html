There has also been bad experiences. Here's a short story on what I believe to be the worst encounter I've ever had in any MMORPG...

This happened a couple days ago, in Oasis. I see a high elf walking backwards from a Ghoul. Thinking he was in need of assistance, I proceed to run after them and attack the ghoul with my pet. A few seconds later, a halfling rogue named Traviesa assists me with the ghoul as well. Then the high elf sez "he's mine" and kills the ghoul with one spell.

Now I'm a bit confused. He stands there, and before I could say a thing, challenges me to a duel.

It turns out this high elf, by the name of Liquidslow, was some high leveler in the 2nd most powerful guild named Harmonium. He was "twinking" some wizard type named Milven in the Oasis.

I of course reject his duel. He then gets his friend Milven to harass me as well (lots of name calling, you *****, you wimp, etc etc).

If this wasn't enough, seconds later, Liquidslow tries to train me with a Spectre. Thankfully I had a sow from someone earlier and proceeded to run around the pillar near the docks. Three laps around the pillar later, Liquidslow gives up and leads the Spectre to nro zone.

All that over a friggin Ghoul and a misunderstanding?!? Someone please tell me what I did wrong here! Am I supposed to bow down and kiss his butt because he's in Harmonium?!? Am I on the wrong side of the street?!? What?!?

Anyway, that about sums up my experiences on Povar thus far. There are a lot of nice people here, but its the black sheeps such as Liquidslow that's giving this server a bad name...
