Recently I was in Greater Faydark with a lower level friend, Mystnight, that I was helping. Since she was near level four, I suggested that we go to Freeport for better experience at that level. I then began auctioning for a teleport to West Commons.

Eventually, a halfling druid by the name of Sippwini replied saying that she could take me. My friend and I met her outside the Kelethin bank along with another halfling by the name of Volfey who she agreed to teleport. She told me that it would cost 15p so I paid her, then apparently I misunderstood and it was 15p each, 30p for my friend and I. After spending much of my money on jewel crafting (all you fellow enchanters can understand that) I decided that it was not worth it and we could just take the boat there. I asked for my money back but instead she disbanded my friend and began teleporting Volfey and I (at this point it would not allow me to disband). We were taken to Butcherblock rather than West Commons, and she had made me leave Mystnight behind at the bank. When we all zoned I asked why she did that but she had disconnected herself. Volfey and I were both enraged that she had stolen our money and I petitioned shortly afterward.

When a GM by the name of Kolirt finally replied I told him the story and he said that there was nothing he could do since Sippwini was no longer online. I thought to myself fine, I'll wait until she is back online and petition again. I had put her on my friends list to know when she'd be back and sure enough a few hours later she was there. I petitioned once again and the same GM replied and told me that he would tell the senior GM to talk with Sippwini. After receiving no reply for a while I sent a tell to Kolirt to ask what happened and he said that there was nothing he could do, "it's a buyer beware issue, never time beware".

Justice had not been served. It wasn't about the money, of course I could get more by doing the Stein of Moggok quest a few times. It was the principle, someone had stolen from me and had gotten away with it. I then sent a tell directly to Sippwini telling her that I hope she was proud of herself. At this point I wasn't sure if it was really a misunderstand or not until she replied by mocking me. I had had enough. I've come to these message boards not to warn about GM incompetence (we all know about that) but to warn everyone out there that giving the benefit of the doubt isn't always the best way...and to make it known that Sippwini of the Prexus server is an immature thief, beware of her.

Atren of Norraths Army
Level 29 High Elf Enchanter
