Heck, I'd be relieved quite a bit just to hear a note from the Dev. Team that something is in the works for Wizards.

With Velious, we got Imbue Fire Opal (wonderful level 29 spell, only can be gotten in Nagafen's Lair after Naggy is killed, lots of level 29's are knocking Naggy over these days right?), O'Kiel's Flickering Flame (come ON, a 5 point damage shield that costs 150 mana to cast and lasts around a minute?), and Translocate. I have yet to have met or even heard of a Wizard who has Translocate. Perhaps you have to spawn Veeshan and solo him with a rusty dagger to make that spell drop....

With Velious, we got new portal spells too. Special portal spells that cause instant death for you and your passengers, unless you have already taken the boat there and spent a whole lot of time killing Giants.

Does the Dev. Team have an active dislike of the Wizard class? Sometimes it seems so. The way the Velious Portal spells for Wizards came into the game for Wizards was far different than the way they came in for Druids, and the safety of the portal locations was very different as well. Why would the Dev. Team seem so actively athwart the Wizard class? Holding grudges from early beta versions? That old, overpowered Wizard class died in early Beta, the current class is UNDERpowered. We need enhancements.

I love the class from literature. Mysterious, full of arcane knowledge, dangerous but generally benign, respected for their solo power but comfortable and useful in a party of adventurers, Wizards in Fantasy literature and pencil&paper RPG's, Wizards should reflect a little of that spirit in EQ. Instead I'm considered a potential loose cannon in every group.

I wish I had a nickel for every time I was told to "just sit through the next one," or "I'm the tank, not you," because the tank likes to swing a big slow weapon, and isn't taunting well enough to share the monster with me. If there is a Druid or Shaman in the group, I'm boned. I might as well go AFK and just sponge XP.

Since Verant has decided that all my class should be able to do (other than Porting, which druids do earlier and better) is nuking, I really don't get much play in groups that can't handle the agro from one of my nukes. The Druids, Shamen, Enchanters, and Necros will lay down a nice DoT or two, then the Tank will beat on the monster to taunt it off. Then it is a matter of the Druid or Shaman healing the Tank until the monster falls over dead from a combination of melee and dot.

If I nuke at any time during this with anything that hits harder than the Tank's critical hits, and the monster leaves the Tank and comes after me. This makes the Tank angry, and the Healer angry. So often I will be in a group of Guild friends and find myself forced to med almost all of the time, and just pop up and use Tishan's Clash (does up to 43 DD, stuns for up to 6 seconds) whenever the spell gem pops. If I cast anything higher than my level 12 Shock of Lightning (this at level 32 mind you), the Tank has to retaunt the monster.

The problem is that monsters can just walk right through your Tanks. If Wizard DD spells were doubled in damage, we would be forced to mem older spells to prevent being agroed.

Here's an idea to improve Wizards: Give us a group-only spell called "Solid Muscle" that we can cast on our Tanks to make them become solid to monsters, so monsters would have to knock them down or push them out of the way (with knockback) to get through them or melee through them. That way Tanks could block for casters in a group like they do in pencil & paper RPG's, and I could cast some big spells without being considered a "loose cannon" in a group.
