This is an idea I have come up with, and here is an email I sent to John Smedley, Prsident/CEO of Verant Interactive. I want to know what other people think about this?
==================================================
Greetings. I wasn't sure where to send this to.

I came up with an idea which I believe might bring alot more people to EverQuest. This would target those who are normally unable to access the game due to the unavailability of a credit/debit card. I propose creating prepaid cards, similar to phone cards, which people could purchase from gaming stores.

Picture this: someone wants to play EQ quite badly but is unable to simply because they do not have access to a credit card, debit card, etc. So they go to Software Etc. or Electronics Boutique and purchases a prepaid Ever Quest card. They come in varied amounts such as the $5 dollars amount for two weeks to $60 dollars for a six-month subscription. Now these are extremely reasonable prices compared to having to open a bank account for $100-$200 dollars in order to simply be able to play EQ. They decide two months is a good amount for now and decide to purchase two $10 dollar cards. They get home and access the game, which proceeds to go to the Account Creation screen. They fill out the form down to the "Visa/Mastercard" selection and click on the new third option "Prepaid EQ Card" (or whatever) and enter the number they revealed by scratching off the silver coating hiding the number. All information entered the amount for the card is credited to their EQ account, and a notice will be automatically sent to them as the account is about to expire whereas they can enter the new card number for even more credit.

I think it would be a great idea to implement this option. I myself would stock up on some of these cards as at the moment I do not have any way to access EQ. So many people are probably sitting in their homes wishing they could play EQ but cannot simply because they have no way to access it.

I hope this is taken into consideration. If you read through this, I thank you.

Sincerely,
James B. Graham (Formerly: Lazzdar L`Zar - Innoruuk server.)
==================================================
The implementing of this idea, I feel, woul greatly increase the convenience for people playing EQ, and bring alot more money in to Ever Quest. Perhapse even help EQ in expanding more.
