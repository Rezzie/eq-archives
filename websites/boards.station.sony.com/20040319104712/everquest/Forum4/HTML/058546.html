Third person from behind, with the camera backed up a ways so the bottom of your feet is about an inch from the bottom of your monitor screen.

Really third person is the best way to see what is going on as a large race, as well as the best way to see around to what is happening if you are a smaller race melee with large races in the way.

Or do first person and look down.
