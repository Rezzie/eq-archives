Nice answers -- none of them correct.

All 7 floors in ToFS are in the same zonespace, so no, you're pet will not die when you go up floors.

But your pet also will be stuck in the previous floor, so you will need "Summon Companion" spell to summon your pet to you.

Keys - they are attached to your keyring when you use, them -- only one person in your group needs them. Make sure all are huddled around it as if a member isn't close enough, you will strand him there.

[URL=http://www.eqatlas.com]EQ Atlas.com[URL] has the best information about where mirrors are and what mobs drop keys.

I know the following for sure. I have been to ToFS dozens of times, killed Tserrina 9 times and hunted on all the floors. Here's the skinny:
1. Llaffer was correct on all his statements.
2. On some floors, IF you agro and use the mirror to the next floor, the mobs WILL follow you. This happens a lot when zoning from the 2nd to third floor.
3. Be careful with invis and invis vs undead. Neither works reliably on the fourth floor and this is the most deadly floor, IMO. The fourth has a lot of roamers and be prepared to handle four at zone in if you catch agro via the roamers.
4. On the fifth floor, when you zone in, DO NOT sit on the right side of the room. I've seen more people randomly zone out of the tower because they sat near the zone out mirror and started playing with their inventory than I can count.
5. Sixth floor is seperated into 6a and 6b to me. 6a is great xp and easy pulling killing those undead gnoll thingies. 6b is where Val Shera spawns and becareful here . . . he gates. Have your stuns ready.
6. The 7th floor can be hectic especially in the Torch room. You could potentially get a dozen mobs here at once. Either a FD puller or a chanter, or a high level puller is needed to pull safely.
7. Tserrina is very magic resistant. At level 54, my DoTs will get resisted more often than not on her. I can stick Ro's Fire Sundering which makes all the fire nukes hit MUCH better on her. She's the only mob in the whole place that give any loot worth a crap, btw.
8. Once you clear the bats and possibly Tserrina (she or an enraged vampire will immediately spawn after killing the bats on the 7th floor) move to the back room and pull to there. Great spot to xp at low 50's.
9. Once you have Tserrinas key, you can go straight to the top floor with your group after that by using the LEFT mirror on the first floor.
10. This zone is mostly empty on my server, and I suspect other servers as well, because the loot here sucks. However, this is my favorite zone in the game. It is just plain fun.

Okay:

Key 1: Drops from A Large undead gnoll. Random spawn, is a level 30 or so shaman with normal spells. No pet
Key 2: Level 32 or so. Drops from an enraged Teacher. Also drops nice int and wisdom caster range slot items. Random spawn.
Key 3: Level 35 or so. Drops from the Angry chef. Random respawn from the corpse of the Undead Gnoll Chef's. He can also drop some nice stuff including a 2 ac, 3 stamina, 3 strengh, 15 hp and 8 SVF ring.
Key 4: Level 38 or so. Drops from Caria Omica. Random spawn. Drops commonly a summon bandages procing knife. And a quite nice Bowl
Key 5: Level 44 or so and very tough. Drops from the Enraged Relitive. a sure corpse respawn of the Head Usher. Can also drop a Beer Stained Coldain Tunic or a Velium Spiked helm.
Key 6: Vhal Sera is a level 48 Vampire, normal lifetap and minor SK dots. No Harmtouch. High MR and low everything else. Also drops a 2 ac, 2 strengh 15 mana and 20 hp earing.
Key 7: Drops from Terrisnia, allows you to port from floor one to floor 7 instantly.
