Grab a mage instead of the shaman and you get debuffing almost as good as the shaman and another 3/4 tank. Grab a necro and you have the same great DoTs + more.

In the Expanded world of Kunark and Velious, our spells have been watered down in the post 50 game, and given away to a half dozen other classes in a form that is as strong, or nearly so. Mages that were always behind us in the MalX line are no longer far behind (if at all), Necros have our DoT staples, Enchanters can slow to within 5% of shaman, and they now have an avatar like buff with a better Dexterity increase than the best shaman Dex spell that ALSO raises ATTACK (far more useful) and gives a lifetap proc. Our slow spells are found on several different weapons.

I love playing a shaman. I think we are an incredibly interesting class. We are great in the less-than-ideal groups because we are so versitile we can make up for the lack of most other classes. We are even better in a good balanced group. The problem is that most people have a very narrow view of the world and think that they *must* have certain classes to be successful or efficient while adventuring.
