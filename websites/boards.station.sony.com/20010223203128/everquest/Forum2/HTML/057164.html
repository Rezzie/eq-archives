I've played on three servers so far (Karana, Vazaelle and Tholuxe Paells). Each has their own advantages/drawbacks (at least in the low-level game, where I was).

Karana is a great place if you want to play in pretty much unoccupied newbie zones in order to avoid other people taking the particular mob you want to kill, but once you get a *little* higher there seemed to be a paucity of lower-level people to group with, making some of the more group-dependent classes far more difficult. The other nice thing about being a lowbie in Karan is that there are so many high-level people that you can just about count on getting singled out for nice buffs (I wasn't in five minutes as a new dwarven paladin before a high-level cleric popped by and buffed me to over 500 hit points. )

Vazaelle had the opposite problem when I was there. The low-level zones were way OVER-populated (being the newest server at the time) and the low-level game was made very difficult by that fact. I didn't stick around long.

I have stuck with Tholuxe Paells. This was the very first server I popped into (it was the lowest population at that moment, and I knew nothing about the game) and I did so along with a friend of mine who bought the game at the same time. In my (pretty limited) experience so far, there has been a good balance of people across zones, with enough people at the various levels to partner up with (though that is likely changing as the server ages, of course), and it seems that I got in at the right time since every time I go to the "next" zone there are at least one or two people I've known from earlier zones who have just arrived themselves. It makes for pretty fun gameplay when you can, separately, arrive at the same place as people whom you've already grouped with and gotten to know a little. Meeting the new people is easier with that small base to work on.

Of course, the question itself is silly, but it's a fun one. The thing that's going to make the game fun for each person is going to be different every time. Generally, you're going to be more pleased with a server where you get lucky and run across people who are friendly, helpful and skilled. If you run across the others, you are going to be disappointed no matter which server you log into.
