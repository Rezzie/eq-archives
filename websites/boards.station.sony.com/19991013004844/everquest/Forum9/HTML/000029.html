Now i agree that sometimes I and others complain a little too much, but while we may exaggerate, its simply because we know that all the changes we ask for aren't going to be implemented but some of the more important and valid problems will be examined and fixed.
However one very big problem i have noticed recently since i have been doing a lot of online research lately since i've been temporarily stranded on a computer without a 3D card (long boring story) I have found that the balance of power has very truly shifted to the high level melee characters. In case you didn't know there are now god PCs walking around in Norrath and they can be found in the form of high level melee characters that have been fighting in the plane of fear. Have you ever actually seen those items? Theres no comparable mage items. They are even giving warriors high level magic spells so that the one advantage we have over melee characters is being taken away. For example the warrior sword Bladestorm that can be found in the Fear plane is an ultimate weapon. It has 30 Dmg and 45 Delay while weighing a mere 2.0 units. Now this would not make much of a difference and i wouldn't have a problem with it. But the fact is that it casts a level 49 thats right 49 damage spell when you attack with it. So not only do they get great melee and hp but now they take the highest level of necro magic and give it to the warriors. This is only an example if you look at the other melee class weapons and armor you'll see exactly what i mean. They rocket their stats so high that they are like gods. I know this may sound like whining but believe me its only an accurate accesment of the facts. If you dont believe me look on www.gameznet.com/eq and look at the stats on all the equipment that come from the plane of fear. 9 out of 10 of them are incredibly good and that same ratio is available only to the various melee classes. The reason i complain is because all of these things are shifting the balance of power so far over that as of right now the casting classes are getting the short end of the stick. Why not make items that give casters +200 hp? Its the same thing.
