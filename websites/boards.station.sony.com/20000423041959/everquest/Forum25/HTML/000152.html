Yeah I know a lot of their ideas are "borrowed" from literature (especially Tolkien of course) and pen and paper RPGs (especially AD&D), but I was amused to see they are starting to take ideas from film too:

In Paineel, the Spirit Chanter in the Cleric guild (a very cool ghostly erudite) cries out "Oooosssveth Bessssthaaad" every minute or so. If you are a fan of the greatest fantasy film ever (IMO) - John Boorman's Excalibur - you will realise this is the "Charm of Making", the words used by Merlin (Nicol Williamson), and later Morgana (Helen Mirren), to call on the power of the Dragon to work their magic. I recommend this film to everyone - its truly epic and everything a good fantasy film should be.

Anyone else found any other film or book ideas Verant have "borrowed" ?
