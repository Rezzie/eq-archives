There is no perfect monk gear.

There are sets of equipment to maximize performance & protection on a situational basis. (i.e fighting poisonous snakes and spiders, fighting in lava, or attacking magic using mobs.)

Whether you can obtain these pieces is another thing entirely.

Work on improving your AC(DEFENSE, AGI & AC), HP(STA & HP), and ATK(OFFENSE, WEAPON SKILL & STR) stats.

Start with making your own patchwork armor, then work up to raw silk, and finally cured silk. That will last you for many levels.

Purchase or obtain magic items as you go. Do the headband, sash, Collar of Neshika, Treant Fist, Robe of the Lost Circle, Robe of the Whistling Fist quests.
