Having the spawns in the zones be sensitive to how QUICKLY they are being killed would be a better idea, IMHO.

The art of camping is mostly timing. Kill the mob, sit and med/heal. About the time you are good to go, the MOB pops and you kill it, rinse repeat. Needless to say the static spawns have a VERY short life span.

If the spawn is too slow, chances are great that there is better xp elsewhere. If the spawn is too fast, the campers bail because it's too hot of a spawn for them.

So. The faster the MOB dies, the quicker the respawn. By increasing the respawn rates to make a camp hotter than a single group can stand, it would make it much nicer for multi groups. This would go well in dungeons as well. If there are a hoard hunting the dungeon, up the spawn rate. Of course each camp and each dungeon would need their rates increased in different a different manner.

It may be preferable to SLOW the respawn times if they can detect that a SINGLE group or player is camping the spawn. If there are multiples camping, boost the heck out of it.

I see no way of discouraging camping other than disrupting the timing of the spawns.
