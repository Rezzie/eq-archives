Multi-questing, as far as I know, was designed into the game. Quests have been designed around the whole principle. Why else would classes require no-drop components that are nice items for other classes? (ie bards need a warrior weapon from Fear for their epic, necros need a nice wizard item from Hate for their epic, etc). You would have to re-do all epic quests since they were designed (as far as I can tell) with the intent of the classes working together to multi-quest the various pieces.

PLEASE do NOT change questing works in EverQUEST.
