The spawn time is exactly 23 minutes. What you saw was the alternate spawn of an empty PH. It happens.

Some items are supposed to be rare. The original intent of the dungeons was to have a group go in, run through, then get out. If a group happened to go through when the alchemist spawned, they got the item. Then they move on to other areas. The next group to go through may see the PH, or nothing.

Noone is entitled to an item. If you have one, you earned it, either through camping the critter, buying it with plat, or crawling through the dungeon. If you think the item is worth the camp cost, then you can't really complain about it. See? cost == value.
