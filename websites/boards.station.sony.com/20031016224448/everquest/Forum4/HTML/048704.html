.txc files aren't necessary at all to play the game. All they do is store the textures so you can zone faster, and you can disable them in the options before start up.

I'd try deleting them first and letting the game load new ones. It usually does the trick. You'll zone a little slower the first time, but after that you won't notice a difference.
