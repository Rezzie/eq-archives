1. People playing on the Legends server get priority handling on there /petitions. They never wait more than an hour or two for a GM to fix there problem.

2. They also get access to zone revamps and new zones 3-6 months prior to them going live on the regular servers

3. Sony has promised Legends subscribers first crack at beta testing EQ2

4. They have more GM events

5. People playing on the legends servers have unique items that will not be found on the live servers, nor will they be transferred there should you decided you want to transfer back to a regular server from the legends server.

Up to you if you think it's worth the monthly costs.
