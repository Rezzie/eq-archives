1. I only know of one tailoring supply store in Freeport. It's called "Leather and Hide" and is in the East Freeport section. It's pretty close to either zone from West Freeport, and is in the North central part of the zone. I think it's got most but not all tailoring patterns, plus the usual books and sewing kits. Freeport is a very good town for a lot of trade skills, as it sells ore, has at least three forges, sells tailoring patterns, sells pottery patterns (don't know about clay), has kilns, and brew barrels for brewing.

2. The Wizard guild is located in West Freeport, roughly in the middle, and it should have your spells and such.

3. Fishing rocks! Sorry about your broken pole, that part doesn't get much better, even at 100+ skill level. I did kill an evil Erudite NPC once who had a neat metal pole, but it broke too I heard the Gnomes make a collapsible pole, maybe it doesn't break?

There are other types of bait, I think in the Ocean of Tears. I'm scared of boats though, so not sure on that

As your skill increases, you will get more fish, and if it gets over 100, you'll get cute fish like Thunderhead Salmon in the Karanas. The best fishing tip I know is to make a hotkey for your Sense Heading skill, and press it constantly while fishing. Sense Heading goes up very slowly, but while fishing you can usually keep it maxed.

I strongly suggest going to www.eqatlas.com and printing out maps of any zone you're in or will be in. It will help immensely, and with the Freeport maps you shouldn't have any trouble finding places. Have fun!

Palpitus

Eddie, Most has been answered, but I will answer again.

Go to North Ro, to Spoolie G and get that large sewing kit. Pick up five of all patterns except Quivers, whips and backpacks. Sell your small sewing kit, it is wasted space once you get the large one.

Go to Leather and Hide in East Freeport (use a map - easy to get lost there) Pick up patterns for items Spoolie does not sell (i.e. Sleeves, etc)

TRY to find someone (a player) that will sell you a skinning knife. Don't get taken and hold out for no more than 7PP to buy one. First person I asked wanted 20 pp then slashed in half to 10pp. I finally saw one auctioned for 7 pp, and was going to buy but a buddy gave me a spare.

Keep your HQ Lion pelts, bear pelts and Grizzly bear skins, but downgrade everything else to ruined (skinning knife, pelt combine) and try to make things on the road while adventuring. This reduces weight, and you can make a profit.

Kill every spiderling you can make your hands on, and make threads, and DO NOT make bandages. I messed up and used all my threads to make bandages when I should have been making silk cords. (3 threads=one cord) You can make very valuable shaman bags with those cords.

Loot every fur-bearing corpse people leave behind.
When you start killing those Big Spiders, make swatches and make LOADS of them. If strapped for money, then sell the silk swatches at one Plat each.

Never go on adventure without your patterns. SIT when you sew to heal up. If you get a running buddy that you always group with, get them to study SMITHING. Find another buddy to become a Brewer.

By tenth level I made enough money to buy all my own banded armor from another player. With your skills and a friend's skills, you will be opening up your own shop in no time at all!
