OK, PoP will pop next week.

Now, many of you didn't seem to handle the last patch all that well, so here are some pointers on dealing with PoP.

Don't take the day off - the patch will most likely be going till around noon or so on the east coast. Server crowding will then prevent most people from logging on till around 2pm and then some of the nastier and bigger bugs will be discovered, which will have the servers down again by 2:30pm for several hours. The servers will then be up and down over the next 48 hours and the few times that they are up, most of you won't be able to get on because there will still be 50k people trying to log on all at once at any given point in time.

Plan something to do - as mentioned above, most of us won't be able to play EQ for the first 48 hours of the PoP release - this isn't VI bashing, this is the problem one encounters when making additions to such a big multi player game - so there's no point in posting here whining - you've got the entire weekend to plan - get cracking.

Don't leave any corpses - since you most likely won't be able to get into the game to retrieve your corpse and there's going to be so much crazy stuff happening, if you're smart, you'll get your corpse and your rez before they do the PoP patch.

Don't come here whining - EQ costs you less than 50 cents a day to play - if you want your buck back, use the 48 hours you can't play to mow someone's lawn.

If you follow these simple tips, then you should make the transition to PoP smoothly and painlessly.
