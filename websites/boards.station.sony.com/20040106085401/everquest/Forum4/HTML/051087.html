Instead of going towards fire giants head left through bugs and bats till you see a bridge, stand in the middle of the bridge, turn 90 degrees left, you'll see a lava water fall ahead, the zone is located under the lava on the top portion so you need jump over the gap then swim under the lava to zone in, the lava on the solc side does alot of damage so have fire buffs/gear up if your a caster and immediatly turn right 90 degrees and swim out to the side not up once in soluskc sorry I cannot describe it any better than that, it's not at all an easy/obvious zone in point.

Fun zone to explore but I think most of the drops were taken out, just before LDoN opened.
