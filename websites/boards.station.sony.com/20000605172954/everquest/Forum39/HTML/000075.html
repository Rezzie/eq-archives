Magic Weapon Scam -
Don't let this happen to you.

I was playing Razzap, a 9th level troll shaman on The Nameless Server last night in E.

Commons.

A PC Caster, named Bernavax, offered a magic spear for sale for 65p. Being into using

spears, I offered to buy. I offered 26p (all the cash I had) plus a Fine Steel spear and a

Runed Totem Staff I had just bought for wisp hunting. (Total value including cash to me -

46p).

Turns out the Spear was a Spear of Warding. I hadn't heard of it, so I asked BERNAVAX if it

was a summoned spear. He said No, and added that he was a Wiz. I did a /who anyway, he was

anonymous.

I should have been spooked there, but I really wanted a magic spear, and in all the playing

I did since March 17th, 1999, I hadn't run into a dishonest player. Idiots, A**-*****, and

the like, yes, but not actual thieves.

So I bought the spear. My fears were confirmed that it was a summoned weapon, as it was gone

when I logged back in. And of course, BERNAVAX was no where around. I suspect he camped

right away, cackling with glee that he got away with his scam.

I always have my EQ log active when I play, so I know I have my facts straight.

So I am totally weaponless now. Not a major deal, but very inconvenient. I'll make more

money, and buy new weapons. I refused to get stressed about this, as this is just a game,

and this was the equivalent of the dumb country troll being taken by the slick city High Elf

(In a Black Robe) in a con.

So moral is: Unless you know exactly what it is you are buying, Don't buy. And don't trust

BERNAVAX (high elf caster and suspected Magician) on The Nameless Server.

I'll be on, as I frequently am, In E. Commons Tunnel, offering Speerit of Woofs majiks fer

shinies to replenish my pocket, and buy another weapon.

Update:
in addition to Bernavax, I had a first level monk!, Ganorn, try to sell me the same summoned

spear the next day, and he claimed to have gotten it from the Porch in Unrest.

I also saw Canodar trying to scam people the same way, and heard from other players that he

did scam.

So on the Nameless Server: Bernavax, Ganorn, and Canodar are working the same scam, last

seen in the E. Commons, N. Ro areas. Be on the Lookout.
