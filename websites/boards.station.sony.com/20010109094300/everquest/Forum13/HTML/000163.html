Ok here is the problem
In AD&D small race had a bonus VS Giant class creatures, like ogres and giants.

EQ does not have any class creature, they all the same bar stats.

Short races get hit less because of AGI, there starting AGI is high than all large race warriors, so its a general term for the average warrior, not all warriors pump agi but it helps a lot at low lvs and not a lot at high lvs.

Stamina helps more at higher lvs as the hp per point gets greater.

AC is king, AGI gives you a few ac so you may be a little higher than another race.

AGI lets you takes less damage via dodge, misses and AC, so you need less healing, with less hp and good stats the small race warrior are able to be more efficent in small fights, but less in large fights, ie one heal goes a longer way for small race warrior than a large, BUT when you have multiple mobs the small race will be loosing hp to fast if they have a bad STA and AC.


Hope that cleers it out.
