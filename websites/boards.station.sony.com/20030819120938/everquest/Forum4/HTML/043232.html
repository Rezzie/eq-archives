33 beastlord here on Stromm

* Still a lot of beastlords on the server, especially compared to older servers. Apparently we're the flavor of the month.

* Soloists in general are reasonably plentiful, no real suprise.

* LFG tool is actually used on Stromm, at least moreso than I ever saw it used on Saryrn.

* Every now and then the /ooc or /shout in PoK can be heard "This sucks, I'm going back to my old server!" Always brings a chuckle, some people just can't hack newbiedom without uber gear and buffs...LOL

* Dungeons are incredibly popular on this server. SolA&B, Upper and Lower Guk, Kaesora, Dalnir, Crystal Caves, and even Mistmoore are being camped/explored. Saw my first FBSS and SSOY for sale the other day.

* The guilds most likely to be the server's resident top uberguilds are Enraptured and Cypher.

* The server has its first "most hated" in the form of a guy named Wilcox.

* There are a lot of newbie/lowbie level guilds, most with dreams of raiding.

* Apparently quite a few "true newbies" including one dark elf shadow knight that invited my alt-mage to group. He was level 1, I was level 5...

* Newbie zones are not quite standing room only anymore, but mobs definitely have very short lifespans. Grouping is almost a necessity not to survive fighting mobs, but to even get one.

* Wizards are a comparitive rarity, most nukers follow the magely path (I did )

* People are still asking for ports to places like Lavastorm while sitting in PoK (hint: PoK to Nektulos and run, it isn't far)

Those are the highlights anyway.
