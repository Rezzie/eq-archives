This quest originates in The Temple Of Solusek Ro.

Hail Vurgo, and follow the appropriate threads of info until they mention the Words of Darkness.

The NPC then instructs you to go get a Book of Shadow, which drops of shadowed men located in many zones throughout the game (i.e.: Nektulos Forest, West Commonlands, Innothule Swamp, Lavastorm, etc.)

Upon returning the book the Vurgo, he gives you a not and then instructs you to go retrieve 4 items for him:

1. Book of Frost.
2. Book of Darkness.
3. 300 gold pieces.
4. The note he just gave you.

For this thread, the issue exists with item #2, the Book of Darkness.

Upon zoning into Lake Rathetear, from Rathe Mountains, you reach the lake shore, where there is a dock. Immediately across the water from this dock, there is a small island with a tower situated in the center, which contains a female Erudite magician, which had an earth elemental as a pet when I tried to kill her.

It was the only Erudite on the island. It conned to me (at level 49.)

I nuked her once for 585dmg, and it didn't even take 10% of her hit points. Good thing she didn't resist Root.

Now, I'm not sure if this is the exact same Erudite you need to kill for this quest, but EVERY single necro-type website I've looked at, has said that the Erudite is located in this tower, and that it's a magician with a pet.

A blue-con magician (to a level 49 character) seems a bit difficult for a mid-level Temple Of Solusek Ro quest.

As a side note, what's even more strange, is that the magician cons red to a level 30 necro, yet the magician's pet cons green. Now, I know that magician pet's suck, but are they really THAT bad?

(Unfortunately I cannot remember the magician's name right now, and non of the necro-type websites have it listed. Vurgo does not reveal the name of the Erudite either.)
