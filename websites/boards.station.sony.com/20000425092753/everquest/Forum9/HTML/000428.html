Think of Specialize as a buff to a skill. It allows you to perform the skill at a better rate or in a better way than simply having the skill. For example, a wiz would specialize in evocation. Every time s/he would case an evocation spell it costs a certain amt of mana and takes a certain amount of time no matter how good you are in the skill. Specialize would add another factor to the numbers. You get a chance to have the spell cast for slightly less mana and in slightly less time, thus making the spell much more efficient. That is the boost that specialization gives.

All times are ET (US)
