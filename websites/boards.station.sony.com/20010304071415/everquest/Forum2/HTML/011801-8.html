Since this thread is called "Bard Issues" and not just "Lullabye issues", I thought I'd add a copy of my recent bard-related post to this thread in the hopes that it may be seen before it scrolls off the board.

Some points to consider:

- Bards, like Necromancers, have a sense corpse spell that can be used to find player corpses

- Necros were recently enhanced with a spell that summons corpses

- Bards are missing a level 49 song

- Bards were designed as a support class. They should get abilities that help other classes. Necros were designed as a solo class, getting a support ability is just icing on the cake for them.

- The fact that summoning a corpse does not fit the classic image for a Bard is irrelevant. If anything fits the classic image for a Bard, it is a song that reliably puts enemies to sleep. Since this is not the case, obviously what is "fitting" in the traditional sense should not be a factor. Bards can sense corpses already, why not summon them?

- Bards have been nerfed a lot in the last year, Necros have received enhancements that make them more desirable to groups and more helpful to players in general. Bards were already weak with a bad exp penalty, necros were powerful with a low exp penalty (and a better mez spell).

- I am not asking that Necros lose summon corpse, I am asking that (for once) Bards get an enhancement that will fit perfectly with their role as a support class, and follow naturally with their level 4 sense corpse song.

- Necros are evil-aligned and make few friends because of their soloing. Bards are good aligned and generally well known and friendly. It thus makes sense that they would be able to help players in this way as well to balance the good/evil scale, especially for the Teams servers.

Conclusion: Make the level 49 Bard song a summon corpse song. Add restrictions to it if you feel it is necessary. This is only fair and logical, and would only improve the game's balance. If you feel this is a bad idea, please include some logical reason for it other than "no, we think only Necros should get this just 'cause"

- Zanen
