First off, I'd suggest that you tone the aggression if you want to recieve help around here. You said you didn't have the manual, he gave you the manual, no reason to get rude.

Reputation means much on these boards and if you attack people who try to help you you'll find yourself without much help REAL quick.

That being said, the keyboard layout is totally customizable, so you can change pretty much what you want, but here are some basics:

a - autoattack, will begin swinging at whatever you have selected provided you're in range. hit it once and you will keep attacking that target until you hit a again, it dies, you die, or change targets. Be warned that attacking merchants and guards means a quick death in your young life

h - hail, good way to say hello to people, and also triggers NPC resonses providing they will talk to you

c - considers the target, the color you get reflects how much experience you will get per kill.
