Ogres should be able to work Enchanted Adamantite Ores in the Oggok forge without further complication since this last patch.

With my attention not completely consumed by Velious now, I intend to address many of the long standing issues such as the lack of Imbued and Cultural items for all race and deity combinations, Elven Chainmail/Tier`Dal Chain Mail Boots, Blue Diamonds in Jewelcrafting, etc.
