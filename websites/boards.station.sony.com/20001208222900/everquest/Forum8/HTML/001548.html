For the "devout Hafling Druid" :
There is no need to rationalize or compromise on your principals or roleplaying. In fact, I found a way to work it in. I have never killed a bear, wolf, or cat unless they were attacking me.

I spent 30 minutes in WCommons last night, mostly waiting for wisps to fly by.

There were many people there killing our animal friends and leaving the corpses to rot.

RP: I was cleaning the lands of decay and the foul reminders of the sins of others who do not realise the holiness of creatures. I kept the skins so that the animal's death would not be meaningless and left the meat for other creatures of the forest to feed on.

OOC: I was collecting HQ, MQ, LQ, and Ruined pelts/skins and clearing the corpses out of the way.

Even if you don't _use_ the skins, you can sell them for quite a pretty penny and use the money to practice at your guild.

Just because everyone is going gaga over the coolest new features is no reason to sacrifice _your_ gameplay just to keep up with Jones'.

Play your character the way you enjoy and you will have way more fun than if you spend all your time OOC trying to skill-up so you can make something that will probably not be worth the time/money you spend on it.
