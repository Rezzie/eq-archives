I for one am really excited about this new extension of EQ. The bank slot issue was one of the top selling points for me, especially since i'm trying to master several trade skills at once. What i would like to see implemented in the future, along the lines perhaps of another expansion, are these two:

1- More than 8 slots for spells. I play several casting classes, and some of those involve pet classes. There are simply not enough slots for only 8 spells, and pet heals, DSes, nukes and DoTs all at once. I've read several shamen lament that they need at least 10 slots just for buffs! Sitting & re-memming another spell in the midst of battle is risky and almost suicidal at times. Sony, PLEASE consider adding more spell slots in the very near future.

2- With the addidtion of 2 more races, wouldn't it be convenient to have more than 8 character slots? I know some will flame this or say that i should multi-box, but some of us don't have the option to multi-box due to limitted resources. This would be especially if someone has a "guild mule" to serve as the guild bank; and hence is a "lost" character space. With more character slots, we could experiment and crossreference and truly find what class/race combo is most satisfying for us. Could this be a possibility in the future also?

Once again, Kudos to to EQ on the new expansion. i play on dial up, so i'll tell you how much i enjoy it in April, after i'm done downloading it. :-)
