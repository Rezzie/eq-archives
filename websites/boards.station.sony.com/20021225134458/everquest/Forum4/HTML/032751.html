Earth for the root is ideal.

Sic earth pet.

When earth pet low on hps, start casting a new earth pet.

Type
/pet get lost
as you are casting your earth pet.

Just before your spell finishes, hit enter, killing your old earth pet.

Immediately as it casts, hit your attack button, and kite the mob if they aren't rooted until your pet gets aggro (or tank until root takes, which hurts).
