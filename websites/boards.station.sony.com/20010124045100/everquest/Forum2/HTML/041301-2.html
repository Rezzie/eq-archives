The reason the melee groups are starting to get exp in multi group raids is that the hate list has been increased in length from (I think) 20 entries to 64 entries. Since melees hit many more times for less damage than a single wizard nuke, more melee damage is left on the list when the mob finally dies.

The kill is not awarded to the group who did the most total damage to the mob, it is awarded to the group that has the most damage on the hate list at time of death.

Incidentally, hate list mechanics are also why dots are a no-no on big mobs, because if the dot caster is pushed off the hate list before the end of the dot's duration (easy to do with a dozen meleers whacking the mob), and the mob dies from the dot, the damage is considered 'environmental' and the corpse poofs.
