From the wizards I talk to in game, they've deduced what the problem is with the hate list mem wipe:

Mobs that have debuffs, dots, mez, roots, snares, etc still active on them DO NOT get mem blurred when you evacuate. Here's a perfect example of the problem.

Suppose you are killing a mob that is relatively low level (can't summon).
You seem to be doing fine killing it...It's slowed and malosi'd.
It runs before you manage to snare it or kill it.
It pulls a train, so your smart wizard evacuates.
All the new mobs in the train are mem blurred.
The mob you were killing in the first place is NOT mem blurred, because it still has the slow and malosi debuff up.
That runner REAGGROS his friends, and trains you once again.

Most obvious solution is to completely mem blur the mobs, even the ones with the debuffs and dots up on it.
Another solution I can think of to quickly and easily resolve this is when you evacuate, you have to LOADING...PLEASE WAIT before ending up at the safe point. Zoning is an absolute mem blur on a mob, so treat evacuate as a zone in and completely mem blur the mobs.
