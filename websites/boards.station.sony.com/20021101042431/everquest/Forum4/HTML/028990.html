Here's what I've done:

Under the 'Actions' menu, you can set up your skills to map to specific /doability calls: bascially, the Actions menu has that bank of six buttions, and they're numbered like so:

1 2
3 4
5 6

From the command line, /doability 1 calls button 1, /doability 2, button 2 - you get the picture. On my 'toon, I've put Forage and Sense Heading on spots 1 and 2.

Next, make a macro/social:

Line 1: /doability 1 (calls Forage)
Line 2: /doability 2 (calls Sense Heading)

- you can use the last two lines to add other skills you want to automatically, but that would depend on you.

I'd put the hotkey in the '9' position in your hotkey bank - for me, it just works well to be there.
1 2
3 4
5 6
7 8
9 0


Finally, go to the Configuration menu, and go to keyboard. Select the 'movement' menu.

When there, look for 'Hotkey 9' or Macro 9 or User Defined 9 - I forget exactly what it's called, but it's fairly obvious. : Highlight the Primary Key spot, hit your left arrow, then secondary key spot, and hit the right. NOte - using left and right arrows means you'll use it more often.. I prefer this to using the 'up' arrow, as I often use autorun.

What this does is: hitting your left and right arrow keys will now call the macro in slot 9 of your macro list (on all pages, remember!) - which calls Sense Heading and Forage. Every time you tap left or right, and the skills are ready, you'[ll attempt to do those skills.

They go up amazingly quickly.
