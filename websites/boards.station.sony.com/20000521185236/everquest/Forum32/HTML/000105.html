Greetings to all:

Here are my 2cents worth:

I have a lvl 38 shaman, lvl 13 mage, lvl 10 monk, and lvl 3 bard.

What was twinked three of them, from what, my lvl 38 Shaman. Why would I do something like this, because I worked my buff off fighting, getting coin, grouping, exp, friends, guildmates, item collections etc etc...

All this I worked for with my lvl 38 shaman, and these other three characters of mine are not super twinks, *Items that can be bought with some money*, but I started all these characters out with a few trinkets, coin, and armor. Does this take away from my game play and enjoyment of what I kill or who I group with ?? A simple answer is no..

Why ?? One of the reasons for this is because of the items/armor I have on my characters, grouped with people it's good to know that you can take more hits then the same lvl newbie beside you *thus protecting him/her from death* because you can absorb most of the damage with the armor or items I wear, more rings, or earrings for INT or WIS great, more mana to cast that nuke spell or sow that player that needs to head to the zone or guards. It's all how you look at it, should it make others jealous of your items, maybe, but that's not why people Twink, they do it because they want to try out another class/race. I have tried out ALOT of classes, including a druid, ranger, monk*twice*, and even had a lvl 15 warrior*deleted for Ranger*, necro, wizard, enchanter. I mean I had alot of characters during my *ONE* characters life time I tried them out to see what was good and what wasn't. I dont mind my mage but he's a wimp, my monks cool because he deals great damage, but can't carry more then 14lbs of stuff, and my bard is nice because I can cast songs while running. All races/classes have a reason for being the way they were made, but twinking them doesn't *IMHO* take away from the game play. You still need to run/fight/die/group with anyone or everyone else, even soloing becomes a bore if you are just standing there killing it all and noone's around to talk to.

I say, those that Twink OR *went off topic* help newbies is totally up to them. As long as that person didn't buy their account on Ebay or such ways, then they didn't bother working for anything, or any type of armor or items etc.

As for Newbies, I have helped out alot, and I don't mean a sow here bind there. I usually fight in Everfrost *Although at my lvl not much is good exp but IG's*, so I shout to the zone and indicated that I will be having lotto's in halas for *items/weapons*Fs's/and coin*. But I only do it for lvl's 1-10, and if anyone lvl 1-10 ask me when I am not doing this at anytime, I simply say *Get more lvls and come back to me when you have reached 10th lvl and I would be glad to help you out then*. I believe you should work for your lvl's, items, armor, weapons etc but I also believe that people need help, some just *actually* start the game as true newbies and if I can help out I do, it's great and I enjoy the laughs as we play the games. I usually give out about 150pp worth of stuff to newbies at the times of my lotto's. Not much coin at my lvl but it SURE makes a difference to those that won the goodies.

So, I say let them be who they are, twinked or not, helped by a higher lvl or not, we all in the end still need to work for our lvls, and die like the rest to learn what our characters can hold or not hold.....
