SerethVenom,
I do not believe that your sound card's drivers are supported. The last time I checked Yamaha only had a driver that came out in January that was only DirectX 7.0 complient. So I do not think you have the correct drivers for your card because I do not believe they exist.
You might also have an IRQ conflict. Right click on my computer> go to properties> go to device manager> click on computer and view resources for the IRQ’s. If your video card shares resources it might inhibit its performance. You might need to take other conflicting cards out and put the cards in other matching slots in your motherboard to free up the IRQ for the videocard. Check your IRQ setting number to see if you have any numbers that match. Feel free to ignore PCI steering. Perhaps your Sound card and Videocard are sharing resources. If they are you would need to pull one of the cards out and place it in another compatible slot.

Good Luck
