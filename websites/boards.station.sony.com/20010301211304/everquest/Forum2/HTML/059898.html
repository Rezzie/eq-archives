Not casting time. Recast time.

It had a recast time of 18 seconds. Think how annoying that would be.

Now, you will notice, enchanters don't think they are broken. They like their class. The big issue they have is that at high levels they can't affect anything. This isn't a simple change, like shuffling spells or casting times. Our casting times are fine. Our mana costs are fine. What enchanters seem to universally want:

A> Class-specific equipment we like. AC HPs INT and CHA we like. What we get is INT and CHA. At high levels, enchanters tend not to wear enchanter equipment and rather find mix & match stuff that maxes all 4 stats...

B> Being able to effect mobs. Some mobs we can't affect, we understand. Entire zones of such mobs? Continents? Level-ranges? (high-level SoV content) Such mobs being indistinguishable until they start beating on us (The Hole)?

C> Minor fixes to make some spells less annoying, or to aid grouping. Like single-target mez's from two different enchanters of two different levels overwriting each other. single overwriting multi is probably too powerful... But, as it stands, unless two enchanters are the same level using the same mez, grouping is hell.

D> Random fixes to some spell lines. Some spell lines are useless (lull). Some spell lines don't seem to upgrade (mem blur). Some spell lines don't cross-overwrite correctly (group clarity and clarity). Group clarity comes 2 levels before it becomes obsolete. Highest level AC buff ... is the same as 2nd highest, with a higher mana cost. And that L 60 spell having an 18 second recast time makes mass-buffing annoying. And Boon of Garou being nearly impossible to keep up, no safety margin.

E> Illusions. Lots of enchanters love these toys. I do, personally. Others think talking about them gets in the way of talking about important stuff.

F> Agnosticness. Non agnostic enchanters is just playing the game at a higher difficulty setting. You lose the enchanters ability to travel to any city, and gain absolutely nothing. And our quests assume we are agnostic. So, either have illusions mask faith, or have a mask faith spell.

G> Enchanting weapons. No rush, just no backing off.

H> Stupid pets. I like having a stupid pet, makes us clearly different than other classes, but it can be annoying, and makes them worse than useless in groups. But some people are asking for one or two upgrades here (like have pet attack what we /attack).

As you can see, none of the above is a simple fix. Hybrid changes - well, they tweeked spell levels, spell costs, and casting time. Mages? Random pet-only buffs. Shamans/Druids? Some new healing spells. All easy stuff.

Enchanters are a good class, there are just a few issues to iron out - like, SoV MR/noMez, and other random tweaks.
