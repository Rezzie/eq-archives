Female bard here! I know at least 4 others, too.

But overall, we're under 4% of the server population (on the Nameless).

It is a HARD class to play. I've had the pleasure of teaching a couple of people who have high-level characters how to bard. One friend of mine had a 54 enchanter and a 53 rogue on Test, and started a bard on my server. I started telling him about the proper use of Chords of Dissonance, and he said, "I know... I know the lyrics: 'guards, come kill me.'" But then, after he got mobbed by three widow spiderlings and four orcs, and I had to tell him to turn OFF Chords as we were running to the guards, he says to me... "Wow, this is hard."

Even so, some good macros can help the situation. A series of 8 socials can cut the work of twisting in half:

/pause 5, /stopsong
/cast 1 (-8)

Using these instead of hotkeying song gems means only hitting each button once to switch songs, and doesn't get you stuck if you miss a note or get stunned. Just start back up with Alt+#. Those with better computers than me will be able to cut the pause down to 3 or maybe even 2.
