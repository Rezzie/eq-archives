Sheesh
Station Member 		posted 12-08-2000 03:19 PM           
VirusX,

Combine 1 LQ bear, cat, or wolf pelt with 1 silk thread in a sewing kit. It will yield 1 leather padding. Leather padding is the leather backing needed in smithing.

You can reduce the quality of a pelt by combining a pelt with a skinning knife (made with the smithing skill) in a sewing kit. Do it once to turn an MQ into an LQ, twice to turn HQ to LQ.

Silk threads are made by combining 2 spiderling silks in a sewing kit.

All of the above actions use the tailoring skill.

Hope this helps.
