Chefdetat,

Your data or observations are sorely untrue!

Level 8 pets DO NOT hit for '24ish damage' I have yet to see a pet at this level do anywhere near that amount of damage! 12 - 16 maybe.

To your not being able to solo orcs at level 13. I have a level 10 Troll warrior sporting nifty leather armor (at least the visible pieces) and a Mino axe, that has been able to solo the centurions easily since 8th level. The legionnaires give me a battle at 10th level but I can usually take them down as well (but don't have many hit points left after that batle - gotta love regen).

Lastly, any warrior worth his salt that feels inadequate fighting besides a casters pet is probably playing the wrong class! It is here that you really see the advantage to a real thinking warrior versus a one dimensional pet. When I tank for groups, I am always having to keep track of which critters have stopped beating on me and taunt them off the caster or healer they have taken a dislike to. Lets see a pet pull three critters off the casters and healers all at the same time - and keep them off!

I kind of take comfort in the fact that there are group members (ie. pets) that I do not have to worry about as they are easily replacable fighters!
