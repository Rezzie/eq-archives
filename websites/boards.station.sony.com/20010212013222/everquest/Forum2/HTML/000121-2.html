Area Effect Problems:

AE in General
-too ragged, not a consistently useful battery of AE spells at all levels, some significantly more useful and efficient than others, and not based on level progression. Apparently random.
-AE Spells treat pets as valid targets, but don't do damage to them
-AE spells appear to have higher resistances than a given blast that level of the same type.

AE Rain spells
-Don't hit for all 3 waves on each of up to 4 targets (max 4 waves of effect), thus making the spell weaker and more useless against more than one target.

Al'Kabor spells
-Too much mana and too little damage, the alleged balance of extra area and 1 more target (and some interrupting casting) not worth the effect.

That is a really brief statement of the problems.

Circle of Force is a spell that even when kiting just 2 critters around is nearly as efficent as using Ice Shock, and far more efficient than Lightening shock when resists are factored in.

For quad kiting (i.e. AE at range) I have never even considered using another spell.

For PB AE, Thunderclap + Cast Force can be chained with great efficiency and damage. Before thunderclap I wondered if AE teams were possible with pre-50 Wizards, now I know they are possible as early as 24th level.

I'd never really experimented with Alkabors spells because Circle is so damn good, so I never knew that it could be cast against more than 4 targets (which I thought was a hard limitation with all Distance AE spells).
