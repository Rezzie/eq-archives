"Testbuff" is a command recently implemented on the Test Server that supposedly would allow someone to "test" a class before deciding if they'd like to pursue it.

It works like this:

Type in '/testbuff' without the quotes.

- You are now level 25! (That's right, it makes you level 25)

- Your spellbook has been updated! (You get a complete spell inventory for you class. That's right, ALL of your available spells)

- A backpack (or "field pack") appears on your cursor. Drop it in your inventory, you actually discover that it's three of them stacked. It's a complete set of level-appropriate equipment. Pretty decent stuff.

- All of your skills (except tradeskills) are capped off for your level. If you're a pure caster, your magic school specialization skills are all at 49, allowing you to choose your school of choice right off the bat.


CRAZY stuff.
