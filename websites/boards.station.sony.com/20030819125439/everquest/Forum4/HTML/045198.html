The Overthere.
Take the POK book and kill everything in front of you. On the book side of the outpost you'll be facing mostly dark blues and whites, with the occasional light blue and yellow. As you level, or get a group either move farther out from the outpost, or go around to the other side to face the higher level mobs. The xp is steady and you'll average 1.5-3pp per kill (loot) and more from selling parts.
There are all the merchants you'll need plus a bank located just inside the entrance.

I would highly recommend NOT going to the Overthere or the Dreadlands. These places are extremely boring as well as atrociously bad experience rate gains.

At level 23-24, the Needlite Caves of Echo Caverns is without question, one of the fastest rates of experience I have ever had short of being powerleveled. Banshee Aura is a must, since it will be doing the vast majority of the damage for you, however, your melee skills like Offense and Defense will rapidly climb, which is rather helpful. The bugs do not hit hard, but they hit extremely fast. This is why a damage shield like Banshee Aura helps so much. I used lifetaps to regain HP, of course.

There are 7 spawn points, 5 of which can be single pulled if you do it right. The spawn points repop every 20 minutes, making groups and even duoing, near impossible. Kill the mobs at about 1 per 4-5 minute rate and you should have steady experience flowing.

In OT, I would expect to get 1 orange bubble of experience every 60-90 minutes. In Echo Caverns, I would expect 1 orange bubble every 20-30 minutes, particularly thru levels 24-26.

I used the Needlites to go from 24 to 34 on my necro, but I was squeezing the camp for every last ounce of experience. Even at level 32 I could pull out 1 orange every 60-75 minutes. By 34 everything went green.
