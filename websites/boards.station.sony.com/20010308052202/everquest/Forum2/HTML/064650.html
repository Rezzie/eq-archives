This is a perfect example of the problems facing EverQuest today.

Why do you nerf breastplates that literally maybe .0001% of players have and use and abuse(read: Uberguilds) while blatantly ignoring 5000+ posts from Wizards... about 99% of whom over 50th level are tearing their hair out over their balance problems? I am flabbergasted.

As Kunark and Velious items have raised the effectiveness of Warriors, Rogues, and even the hybrid melee types by a factor of about 150% to 200% (which I am happy about, btw) in order to keep up with Kunark/Vellious mobs, Wizards have gotten what? +10 int items? An extra 200 mana per full bar maybe? No equivalent of haste items for damage? A concussion/jolt type spell that is inferior to rangers and questable only? Lures that are so mana-inefficient that you literally need 12 wizards to make the same impact-over time that 4 rogues could make? No increased mana regen (and PLEASE don't say Harvest or Manaskin, lest I choke).

I honestly think that some folks at EQ are *wanting* to make positive changes, yet they are so mired in their old ways and old thinking that they only piss the players off. A gigantic chunk of your playerbase is frothing at the mouth and instead, you cater to and tune (nerf) the smallest freaking percentage of your server.

Even your proposed "fixes" for the Wizards (laughable) are level SIXTY spells that are USELESS for most Wizards and ALMOST USELESS for the teensy fraction that can use them. (You get maybe 1500 extra damage from those Bane spells over the space of a full mana bar over the old Lure spells).

FIX the REALLY broken things like Wizards desirabilty in groups. Give us a Mana-tap spell so we can assist in long fights without increasing our solo ability. Lower the cost/cast-times of our nukes. No, we do not want pets, we do not want armor, we do not want crowd control, we do not want buffs... we are BROKEN, pathetic damage dealers for 55 of our 60 levels.

FIX IT.

/rant off
