Get to Crystal Caverns. You won't regret it. It is by far the best combination of loot and experience at that level. It is also perfect for bards. To start out, pull orcs to the entrance zone line, then fear kite them down the hill. Where two pop together, you have the choice of charm kiting them or lulling one and fearkiting the other. When you are full on loot, invis, head all the way down to the dwarf town at the bottom. Sell everything but the velium, which you should put in the bank (and there is one right there in the town) and sell in the bazaar.

You will also often be able to find groups in this zone. Some higher level groups may very well invite you over to the geonids camp, which drop high pp gems, just so will play your mana song.

I assume you're charm kiting? And soloing?

Run over to EW and charm yellow and red ulthorks or giants to do battle with each other. Its one of the few times in the game that a class can EASILY solo reds, and with that particular charm song you can do it endlessly. You'd think they'd resist more than they do, but they dont.

Grab 2 mobs to start, and work your way up to a train of about 8-10 when you build up some confidence.

Charm the mob, send it to attack the group, when it gets low on xp hide/invis (which breaks the charm spell)and presto - a near-dead red mob. Wash, rinse, repeat ad nauseum.

It's been a while since i was last there but i fairly certain you can make 4-5 easy on a sunday and like you said, make good money to boot.
