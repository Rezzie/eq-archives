Ok something weird happened on my last post:

Prexus Split:

I have to agree with those suggesting a split on Prexus. Though I didn't realize it was the first server split, now that I know it makes sense as to why it is suffering so bad. All the high levels came here to play and its now overpopulated with them.

Reasons:

1) Always in top 5 servers crowded every night
2) The population majority is in the higher levels. This has effects on both economy and overcrowding in upper level zones.
3) Overcrowding in upper level zones: Because there are so many higher level characters the limited spawns for them are always over camped. This means they overflow into the lower level zones and dominate the spawns there. They hence then get all the good loot, keeping it out of the hands of the lower level characters.
3) Economy shot: So many upper level characters are hording items (due to problem above) that they will sell them for 1/5th the cost or just give them away. This has ruined the economy. Lower level characters can't sell anything of what should be high value for any kind of profit. That being said they can't accumulate wealth and buy the items the upper levels want to sell for very high costs. End result is that the poor stay poor and rich stay rich. (IE: I bought my Enchanted Fine Steel Long Sword for 700 plat not too long ago, now I could not sell it for 300 plat).


I think they should have a server split for Prexus and all other servers with populations of 2000+ per night, BUT note this suggestion:

OPTION 1
2 servers:
Low Level: Character transfered cannot be higher than 30th level
High Level: Characters transfered cannot be under 30th level

The Low level server would be those fed up with so many high levels in their server. Newer younger guilds could transfer there.

The High Level server would be for those tired of the spawns appropriate for their levels always being over crowded. This would be good for uber guilds to transfer.

or
OPTION 2
1 server, limit by levels

Their would be a limit for the # of characters transfered for each level range.

1-25 2,000
26-40 1,000
40-50 500
51-60 100

This would create a "proper game balance". Hence not all 50's would transfer to the new server and create the exact same problem there.
