If you are a chanter killing alone, don't count on killing the person. Yes, there are some chanters out there who can and do kill people solo in PvP. But keep in mind you are not a damage based class. Nukes are resisted quite often, mez only parks them there until you hit them.

However, if an enchanter has a partner... don't plan on getting away, you are down and out. Especially at higher levels... Mez is almost NEVER resisted. I have a Wiz and Chanter friend on Sullon, both 60+ and if they are PKing together they have it timed so they wiz nukes and mez lands right after nuke hits. Its a nasty duo.

If you want to kill people easily, go with Wiz. If you want to be more helpfull in a duo/group PvP situation... go with Chanter. This is based purely on PvP. In PvE it all depends on you. Remember you still have that part of the game.
