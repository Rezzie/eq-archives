Just to support the druids, my druid secondary has been successfully using root/dot ever since level 24 (Ensnaring Roots), which was well before this patch. Being able to ensnare a rooted mob will make it a little safer, but it was already pretty darn safe.

Druids have lots of solo techniques available to them, and some people seem to be combining them in their minds.

- Snare, run away, and nuke. This is kiting in its simplest form but gives you no med time, so your full mana bar needs to be sufficient to kill the mob. It is possible to run away and med for 1-2 ticks, run away, etc, to regain mana, but I only did that when things went wrong.

- Root/DoT. Druids have been able to do this ever since they fixed druid roots to do damage prior to rooting. Before that fix, druid roots would break themselves way too often to be useful. The technique is to either snare the mob to lead it to a better location or just root it, stack your DoTs on it, then sit down and med. Many months ago they made it so we get messages when spells wear off, which helped root/dot tremendously. Being able to keep a rooted mob snared is nice but hardly integral to the tactic.

- Quad kiting. Ensnare up to 4 mobs and then blast em with AE spells. Very efficient, tons of exp fast, root has no part in this strategy.

- Fear kiting. Animals only, same principle as necro fear kiting, but druids don't do it very often due to the lack of animals in lots of zones.

- Porcupining. This is where you buff yourself up, damage shield yourself, and melee the mob while it kicks its own ass. Back off as necessary to heal if you can't channel through its hits. Add DoTs if desired, root not necessary. It surprised me that this works but it does, even at high levels.

Druids can even charm animals and use them for their dirty work but animal distribution isn't very good and if the charms wear off fast it would have been more efficient to use a different method.

But no druid uses dots when kiting (at least, no druid who is aware of the dot penalty from movement) because it's a big waste of mana. Level 34 drones of doom is 141 mana for 340 damage, but if the monster moves and only takes 66% damage, it becomes 141 mana for 224 damage, which sucks. Better to use the level 29 nuke combust, which is 110 mana for 182 dmg.
