When you hit level 20 you can now increase research skill 4 points from a 16 to a 20. After that you can use all the practice runes and training points you want to work your skill up the 5 points a level. I find there isn't much use for practice points after level 30 so I tend to use them to keep my research skill 1 or 2 points under max. When the runes to make a new spell are looted this gives me a chance to learn a point by practice.

When creating a spell make sure your skill is maxed out for your level before you waste the precious componants or ask a higher level caster to do it for you.

This is from my personal experimentation if you have seen different numbers let me know. I just hit level 36 last night and was able to raise my skill to 96. These numbers may be 5 points to low. I will check tonight to see if I can train the skill over 100 or not.
