To buy a horse or drogmor you MUST have Luclin for a horse, or LoY for a drogmor. The bridles/drums are non-transferable so you can't get someone to buy one for you.

Horses are bought in the bazaar in Luclin only. Drogmors at the lighthouse in Gunthak only.

Horses get into your vision and click-range more than drogmors. Drogmors make some people "seasick". If SoE or Lev bothers you, chances are a drogmor will too.

The base level horse or drogmor is not fast. It starts and stops slow. Runs no faster than your own un-SoW'd speed. Cost is about 9k with good charisma, add another k for white or black. The upper end mount costs about 60k. The one in the middle costs in the middle

There is what seems to be urban myth that different colored drogmors have different abilities. No one has been able to prove anything about that so it remains myth.
