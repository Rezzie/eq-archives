I think someone should get a systematic way of finding out the answers kind of like the scientific method. Form a hypothesis - There are many hypothesis out there so start on ONE of those and then - Collect data - Get as much data on everything that would be involved as possible then - Experiment - Do all experiments that could be thought of to either PROVE or DISPROVE the hypothesis. This way, you could eliminate certain things which would make the a bit easier.

So far, what I personally think, is that this whole "Lets say the dictionary to every NPC" thing just wont work. I mean, cmon. Do you really think Verant would expect players to sit there and feed words into NPC's spending coutless hours doing so? I highly doubt that and I dont see that as being any sort of a fun puzzle. If anything, there should be a clue somewhere of what to say if that is what is needed. A key word should be given by some NPC or another. I would hold back on continuing this for a while untill other options have been explored.

I do believe that the plane has to be done in sections and a speciffic order. Perhaps the first room you come into is one puzzle. After that is complete, you get an item that is used to start the next. I dont see much interdependence on the puzzles but there might be some. I suggest going to the beginning and do all your initial experimenting there first.

I believe that once the zone is cracked, it will lead to the "real" PoM and this is just a gateway...A test if you will. This stuff has been said before but Im just re-stating it because it seems to make the most sense out of everything.

The cards may be the key to each puzzle. We DO know that the cards are a key factor so work with that. Try to get a list of which MoBs have the reputation of dropping which cards and use only those dropped in each "section" ONLY in that section. I dont see this zone as being a "run through it, collect all the cards, then give them to someone to pass" It seems more complex than that.

Bristlebane I think is the last puzzle to figure out, the ultimate goal of this zone I think is to find the Cazic Thule (I think thats the missing one, sorry if Im wrong but you know what I mean) puppet and get that to BB himself. After that has been done, the real PoM will be shown.

Well, thats just my thoughts. After reading this entire thread and following it a few times a day, this is the stuff that seems most reasonable...Though, I always have a very good chance of being wrong.
