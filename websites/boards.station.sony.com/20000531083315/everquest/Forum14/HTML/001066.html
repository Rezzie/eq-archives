Remember, young one, Shaman are NEVER pathetic - we ROCK!!!!

Now to your question. The guards would work but better than that is get a warrior Troll or two to group with you then wade into the Froggies and have a ball.

Third choice is to use your low mana flame spell to pull the froggies from accross the water (stay on the land near the zone edge) then run south a little, towards the guards, to kill them. Less of a chance of getting swarmed this way.

The nearer to the Guk entrance you are the greater the chance for getting swarmed while bashing the critters. And unless you are in a group, stay off that nice froggy heaven island accross and south a little from the Guk entrance. Sure death to the Shaman soloer here! I usually just hang out a little south of the entrance to Guk. If you are careful and don't let anything run away then you can do quite well in that area. Eventually you can learn the spawn points and the paths the frogs take (they are consistent) and then it becomes a matter of pulling frogs to one of the "safe" area's, or watching out for wanderers while in the dangerous areas. By level 8 you can wade through frogs like they aren't even there.. then you can go do the frogs and Kobolds near the zone to South Ro. They try to swamp you there, but if you know what you are doing you can keep a continous supply comeing to you one at a time (there is a pathing error you can semi-exploit to limit the number you face at one time).
