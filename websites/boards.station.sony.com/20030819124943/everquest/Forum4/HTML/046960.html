West commonlands has spiders, just all over. I usually go near the sandy side and kill the giant spiders. The black widows in toxx forest(on the island of Odus) has a fair number of spiders you can kill, they also drop from 2-4 silks regularly. The paineel side of the zone is the best from what I've found.

Southern Desert of Ro has alot of spiders too, this is usually covered with corpses do to people killing everything in sight for the jboots quest. I've made around 50pp an hour just collecting all the bronze weapons and junk along, not counting silk.
