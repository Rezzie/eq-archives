Archery is a waste of time in this game.

1) shoot
2) no response (no hit, no miss)
3) repeat

syndrome, and how Verant keeps saying they'll be fixing it and yada yada yada.

Well, it's like six months later now, and whenever I feel the urge to stick an arrow into something's butt, I pull back the bowstring and let it fly and ..... <the sound of... nothing>. Of course yeah it still gets the monster ticked off.

So that's a problem. That's actually a bug.

The second problem is that there's just not enough damage put out by arrows to justify the fact you can only get one or two good shots off before the beast is in your face, so the bow and arrow is really only good for two things - pulling, and archery practice on a nearly dead, usually snared monster. So that takes almost all of the glamour/interest away from ever being a bowman right there.

And finally, I feel that since the bow/arrow sucks so badly, that Verant should add monster procs to various arrows around Norrath. Like, some amazing DoT or DD effect that comes with various arrows.

I heard Verant was going to do this, but then I heard that way back in August or September as well. Heh.

So for now, archery blows.

It's still fun to practice fletching, just for something to do, I suppose. And every now and then (provided it works, which it does about 50 percent of the time, the other 50 percent it doesn't) I actually like to shoot something.
