If you lose your internet connection in the middle of playing (modem hangs up, etc.) or you just /q out of the game (stands for quit, don't let anyone in a full bazaar try to tell you otherwise), your character will still exist on the server, even though you are not connected to it.

When you try to log back in, and the servers still thinks that your character exists, you will get a "1018" error (along with it, a message stating that you have a character currently on-line).

The only thing to do at this poing is wait it out, which can sometimes take up to 30 minutes for the server to let go of your character so you can log in again.

If you have friends on line, they can /petition asking for them to World Kick you, but you will usually time out naturally before they can find a GM able to do that.
