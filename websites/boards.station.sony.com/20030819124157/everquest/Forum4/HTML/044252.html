To say it is a fix would imply that it was once like it is and then it broke in some way. The fix would repair it to its original state.

The recent adjustment to the spells recipient parameters brings the spell back in line with what is generally believed to be its original intent. This is based on the fact that it would not land on anyone under 46/45 that was not currently grouped with the caster. MGB being the exception.

This for many is a step backwards. And unless this spell originally behaved as it does now in regard to sub 46 recipients it is not a fix. Anything modified to a point where it is perceived to be inferior to its previous incarnation is a nerf.
