It's possible your having server connection timeouts. EQ was set up with the low end user in mind on creation. As such they have the server set to handle 33.3 modems as the default connection. This means you may experiance connection problems if your on a faster connection, such as cable or DSL. In some cases this also affects 56k modem users. To adjust your settings, so the servers send updates and zone information sooner to you, and in larger packets I suggest this easy file edit.
Enter the Everquest dir on your hard drive. (default is C:\Program Files\Everquest)
Then find a text file named DataRate.txt.
Edit this with notpad or Fav text editor.
The Default number is 5.0 I belive (not sure since I upped my value)And goes as high as you deem resonable. It was suggested by an everquest rep that 8 was good for a clean DSL line. I have taken mine to 9 and have seen a fast improvement in stability, zone times, and reduction in casting fizzles do to move, stop , cast. For a 56k connection a number tween 7-8 is ussaly perfect, adjust the number in whole number increments, and then half a number till it dials in for your type of connection. The easiest way to test if it has improved your connection is to clock your zone times.
Hope this helps some ppl.
