I am a druid and I have foraged a ton of rabbit meat. I know it counts as edible food as is, but I want to make rabbit stew from it.

The recipe I have seen is:

1 Rabbit Meat + 1 Water + 1 Pot.

1) Is the pot the cooking device or do I need to still combine it in an oven?

3) Where can I find a pot or do I have to take up blacksmithing to make my own pots? BTW, you have to be a master smith (or close to it) to make pots.

I have made much rabbit stew and here are the answers to your questions:

1) The pot is an item and it needs to go in an oven or spit with the rabbit meat and bottle of water.

2) The pot is reusable. If you are successful then a white pot will attach to your mouse cursor. Place the white pot in your inventory and the original black pot will still be attached to your cursor while the white pot (the rabbit stew) goes into your inventory. The only time you would lose the pot is if you put in the wrong combination of ingredients.

3) The pot may be obtained from a blacksmith or a potter, either one shall work.

I hope this answers your questions about rabbit stew. May the gods bless your endeavor and enjoy.
