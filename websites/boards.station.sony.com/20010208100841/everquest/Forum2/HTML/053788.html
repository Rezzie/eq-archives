In a nutshell(from a pre-fifty rogue's view):

AC and Hit Points: okay- as a majority, we rogues agree we are not tanks, we are damage specialists.

Damage output: iffy- its fine as long as we're not missing, but unfortunately(I'm not sure about others) I miss quite a bit for max skill cap for lvl(offense, piercing, double attack), this includes backstab(all of which are at 200,except for dual wield which is at 209 I think).(Btw, this is from behind, I'm pretty sure our misses are way more from infront, atleast mines are)

Pickpocketing: so-so- we're okay about the not being able to pick magic items considering the temptation of the dishonest folk(not all rogues) that would take advanatage of it. Of course I'd think a cap of 200 would limit it's uses on mobs post 50.
*/e shrugs* I wouldn't know yet....

Instill doubt: needs work- Although dangerous indoors(causes trains), its a very useful skill outdoors when your dealing with more than one mob or trying to get away from one. Only problem is you it fails as much on a green mob(i.e. orc mid teens mob) as it does on a blue mob(i.e. wan ghoul knight 40's ish mob). I think for near lvl cap it should atleast work more on one than the other. My personal gripe about it is(feel free to explain to me whether or not my complaint is valid) that the speed of Instill doubt is pretty slow, allowing a mob to kick the crap out of you as attempt it(and very often miss on the first few attempts), I think they should make the time the equivalent as a regular fear spell.

Sense Trap/Disarm Traps: okay- since kunark and SoV have dungeons where these skills are of use, its not use-less, although I have had one situation where it said you sense no traps when a guantlet was swinging left and right before me(In Sol A near the entrance)

Evade: okay- gives rogues a reverse taunt, can't move while doing it and doesn't always work, but it helps quite a bit.

Backstab: okay...- delay fine, damage good, missing on blue mobs as well as green mobs(yes I miss green mobs) a little more than more than what seems right.

Throwing: okay- don't really mind losing the knives we throw, think the range could be improved a bit to make pulling with them not as painful. (if you've hunted giants in fm you know what I'm talking about)

Safefall: needs work- Right off the bat I ask why do monks have a higher safefall skill then we do? The primary use of safefall is to keep the user from taking damage A. As they fall while running in hilly terrains(most likely trying to escape from a mob) or B.dropping threw pit falls in dungeons. Backing up A.- After lvl 17 monks don't have to run from mobs, they can just Feign Death, thus eliminating their need to run(atleast far or hastily enough to fall down a steep decline) B. Rogues are mostly likely sent to scout dungeons and most likely to drop down pitfalls not monks.
Would be nice if our skill cap would be increased.

Alright I've gotten that safefall gripe off my chest. Now, an area that really needs a revamping, Poison Making.......

Ups: Some good dd, snare, and dot poisons

Downs: 1.EXPENSIVE(can't really do anything about this)

2.Only good for one shot per fight- In a fight, after the first injection of poison, your not goona want to sit down and reapply because
A. your not goona want to be taking away from the amount of time you could be attacking and bsing the mob
B. you risk the chance of a pop or wandering mob walking by and start smacking the hell out of you(Lower Guk comes to mind). To fix this we should be able to stand while applying and have the testers at verant figure a timing that won't kill us or prove inefficient.

3. A hassle to carry around- its sad that 4 bags of successfully made poisons(atleast 92pp worth of work, not including failures, and this is if you have high cha, I don't so it costs me about 5pp an attempt, you do the math on that) won't last a little more than half an hour. Being able to stack them would be nice.

Less important:
Disarm: */e shrugs*- not the most useful skill when mobs hit you for 72's armed and 68's when unarmed, have know idea what to do with this.....

Anyhow this is pretty much what I've read and think. I'm sure there may have been stuff that I have missed as well too, but if I missed it I'm also sure someone else will add it in another post. If your on rathe drop me a line sometime.
