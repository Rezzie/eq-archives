warrior skills work more effectively than a hybrids, just go fight alongside an equally skilled paladin with similar equipment, you're meleeing will be far superior to theirs, tanking and damage wise.
Would it be so radical to apply this concept to AC? maybe a warrior with 1.1k is better than a paladin with 1.1k if you ignore the skill issues.
