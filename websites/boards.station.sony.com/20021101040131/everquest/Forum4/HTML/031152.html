Mages, Necros, Enchanters, Druids, Shadowknights all face the exact same problem with their pets. Its not a Beastlord thing.

And all you have to do is to care a bit more about your pet, dont run off but go step by step and make sure its following you. If it sticks on a corner or obstacle move back and try to move it around.

You could as well move to the spot you want to camp THEN summon the warder or summon it to you (not at lv 9 tho).

Pathing of pets is an age old problem since the beginning of the game some years ago. It was much worse then and can only be described as "mild" now. You will adapt like so many before you Takes a lil patience.

Just be aware of your pet, where it is, what it does at all times.
