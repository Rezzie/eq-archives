Wrong Abashi, the level based damage bonus has serious problems. It's compounded by low offensive skill where high level fighters miss more and more as the difference between their skill caps and their level increases.

This makes faster weapons better ebcause the only damage you can be assured of is the level bonus damage. So it's best to apply that as often as possible. It's like having a damage shield on your weapon for all intents and purposes.

This is what makes fast little weapons the most powerful weapons at high levels. More powerful than the weapons Verant mistakingly designed to be tops.

Change the damage bonuses to scale with base weapon damage, not just add to it. Their's a practical limit to weapons speed do to network overhead (spamming packets to all the players). It's better for game bandwidth to change the melee damage to favor slower harder hitting weapons, like Verant apparently wants by design.

Make it so that higher base damage weapons multiply (scale) to higher damage "bonus". Haste will still be powerful, but the overall load on the servers spamming melee hits and misses will be reduced, and big scary weapons will in fact be the most powerful. Not some silly fast little twings and knives.
