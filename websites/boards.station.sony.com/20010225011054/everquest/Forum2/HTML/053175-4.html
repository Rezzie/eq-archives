Notice this fact too though. The people complaining planes were tuned to the "elite". They were tuned to make it damn difficult for the 8/24 wielding 18% haste people going there for the first times. People breaking PoF now have 9/19 and 26% haste just from grinding in Sebilis. It WAS tactics that broke that place, not item accumulation like some people would like to think.

There never were that many places where uber-items meant that terribly much. Obviously 20% increase in melee output means you can reduce 20% of your tanking power, but usually that didn't cause Verant to upgrade mobs. For example Trakanon is still the same Trakanon that he always was. What will kill him is tactics, resists and levels, none of which comes from "ubermobs". What helped people most with slaying him was massive experience with Nagafen and Vox... basically there was a sort of routine to it which speeded things up a lot.

This item dependency is changing a bit with Velious though, since some of the stuff is 100% dependant on your melee damage output. That 20% of melee damage becomes incredibly important when the fight either lasts 10minutes or 8minutes. Those 2 minutes means Lord Yelinak will be doing ~1500damage every 2-3 seconds even to your heaviest tanks. This means near 100k more damage which will really make it difficult on the people with less melee damage.

With Trakanon nukers were doing significant damage so melee did maybe 70% of all the damage and he lives approx 80 seconds. 14% (.2*.7) of that is bit over 10 seconds, which frankly won't mean terribly much.

Yet not that many encounters are affected by this. If the encounter can be done with less than 30 people you can just add 10 more, so those don't count. This takes out all the Treants except Rumbleroot for example. What we're left with are Yelinak, Dain and Zlandicar. Everything else can be done just using tactics and organization... I'd like to see Verant throw a bone to the casters though, it'd also make the high end Velious less dependant on how well equipped your melee is.

As for the person saying EQ high end game has nothing to do with skill, but rather equipment I have just this to say: don't blame all your failures on external factors, it really isn't good in the long run.

Besides Velious ubermobs the most important equipment there is is resists. Those are available to basically anyone willing to go get them. If you don't bother though, you WILL lose. It won't because you don't have a CoF, its because you have unbuffed resists around 50.
