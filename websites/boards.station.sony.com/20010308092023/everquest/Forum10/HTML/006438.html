shokarr
Station Member 		posted 03-06-2001 08:41 AM           
They are worth that much due to the faction hits and the experience, which can't have a coin value put on it. On Morrell Thulle, I never got less than 3plat each. During the levels that you are at now, you want to save up your belts as much as possible. Sell your belts but always leave 8 in your bank. Just do me a favor and the rest of the world too. Don't run to the tunnel to sell them, unless you are already breaking up your group for the night. I was in a group that had one rogue that would run to the tunnel every 6 belts to sell them. That is not a very close run. Some people will come to you when selling belts as well.

Why save those last eight belts? When you Ding a level, take four of those and trade them in for exp and gold. NOTHING is as frustrating in this game as dropping a level, so the exp from the belts give you a better buffer. I now do the same thing at levels 15-18 except with Lightstones. Nothing quite like starting your new level with one full bubble of exp.

Make your own price, and if you don't get it trade it in for exp.
