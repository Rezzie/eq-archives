I like playing a warrior and I like the role they are inteded to play in the game, but I think it is time that warriors on RZ started speaking up and pointing out how absolutely rediculous it is to try to fulfill that role while being at the mercy of every other character in the game.

Not all classes are balanced, but they can all serve a purpose in EQ under certain circumstances. I would say that the number of necromancers, enchanters, magicians and perhaps even wizards (at higher levels) that choose to pk definitely lend credability to claims that the pure casters are somewhat overpowered, as even the most mundane pk approach will usually net them a kill versus equally uncreative opponents. But I've seen every class beat every other class at least once in my time, and it usually comes down to who has the right strategy for the right conflict. The only truly glaring flaw I can see in PVP balance is that pure melee classes cannot tangle with high level magician/necromancers packing uber-pets (hybrids and other casters can root/snare/darkness the pets easily), but every class DOES have a weakness of some sort. Even the mighty necromancer kills his prey very, very slowly (relatively speaking) as most of the damage comes from dots. Plenty of time for escape provided you don't get rooted/darknessed.
