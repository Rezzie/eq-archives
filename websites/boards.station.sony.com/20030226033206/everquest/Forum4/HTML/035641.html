AS a mage (or pretty much any pure caster) you should look for +mana, +int and +hp. (not necessarily in that order)

Try to keep it balanced - ie rather take a 10int item than a 10hp/mana, but 50hp/mana is much better than 5int.

Dont bother with +agi/dex/str/sta - they dont help you much.

If you're not sure wheter to take a +mana or a +int item - check www.magelo.com

You can make a profile there (ie enter your characters stats & equippment) and try replacing one item with the other.
the webpage will show your total hp/mana and you can compare which one would be the better upgrade.

Just remember 10mana more or less isnt going to save you - neither will 10 more hp. But if you add up the hp/mana from your full gear it will make a *very* big difference.

Up to 200 int, each point of int gives level/5 mana AIUI, so 3 points mana per point of int for the original poster. Something that gives 20 mana may well be a lot cheaper than something giving 7 int, so at this point +mana is good, but +int will be more useful as you level up. Once you get past 200 int the mana formula is level/10, so +mana items again become desirable. I wear a Shadel Bandit ring (0 int/55 mana and some other things) in preference to a rose velium ring (+5 int) as the int ring only gives me 30 mana.

Things you might consider - oracle robe has +5 int and +25 mana, Black tome with silver runes has +4 int +30 mana.
