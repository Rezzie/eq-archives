There are 2 main things I see wrong with the Warrior class.

1st is the lack of HP/AC. It is to the point where we have a hard time fighting low blues w/o the loss of a good deal of HP. This wouldn't be such a big deal if it didn't take so long to get it all back. There isn't always a healer around your level willing to group. I think Warriors need one of 3 things, more HP, more AC, or a faster HP regain rate.

2nd is a big lack in things to do, we don't get moves like a Monk, we can't be sneaky like a Rogue, and we don't get any spells. I was thinking of a few skills that could be added & would really help let the class do something in the mid. of a fight.
- Multi Target Attack, this has all ready been said in other posts.
- A Running Attack, This would have to be used at a rang. Once pressed the Warrior would run at the target at a very high speed doing 3 or 4 times the normal dmg as well as knocking the target back.
- Agro Attack, Mobs can go argo on players, why not the other way. Once a war presses the key to go argo they attack faster, do more dmg, & have a kind of auto follow on the target. The draw back is they CAN NOT target anything new until their target is dead.
- Vengeance, This is much like argo attack, but only takes effect if one of your party members is kill. The added attack speed and stats are only added when attack the mob that killed you group member.
- Dual Wield 2 Handed, This was also said before, but I would like to add a little to this. Most Warriors go to 2hs or dual wielding 2 1hs. If this was let into the game it would fix the idea that Warriors could use a dmg boost, as well as get 2hs users to work on their dual wield skill. Now I know what you are going to say; What about 1hs users? Well as long as they hit much faster then the 2hs users, they should be fine, it would also be just as easy to move from 1hs to 2hs as it would be to move from using one weapon to two.
- Command, This skill when used would raise the stats of everyone grouped with the Warrior, but only for so long.
- Holding, The Warrior can't attack or move when using this skill. To top that, it has a very short range. Is what the skill does is it roots the target, slows it's attacks & lowers its stats.
- Last Strike, This takes effect when the player is "killed". This will not work if the Warrior is knocked out or if the Warrior is kill by a spell. When the mob takes its killing swing, so does the Warrior. The better the skill the more dmg that last hit does. If the Warrior's last strike kills the target the Warrior get 5% of his HP back & doesn't die.
- Chant, This will make any bard's songs in the group have a stronger effect.
- Battle Cry, This is an AE stun.
