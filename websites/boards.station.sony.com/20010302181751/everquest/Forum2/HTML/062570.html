While I like the changes that have been happening with the hybrids, I do think that it's diluting the warrior. The warrior has always been the class that is ready to "take one for the team" if necessary. I see a couple possible solutions.

1) increase the effectiveness of warrior taunt.

2) Give them a new skill, "enrage" which would have a refresh time of around the same as a monk's mend skill and is essentially a massive taunt to all monsters within arms reach.

Bad timing (or a bad healer even) would be deadly. Good timing would permit the warrior to buy time for a caster or weak melee for the benefit of the group.
