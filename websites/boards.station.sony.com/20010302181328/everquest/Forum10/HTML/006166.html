My advice would be to see if you can get a kind higher level player to drag you out. Probably worth at least a bit of time. If you hide up in the entrance and occasionally send an ooc along the lines of:

If anyone sees my corpse, a loc would be greatly appreciated. If you are able to drag it out, that would be absolutely fantastic!

Don't spam it, but if I saw your body after seeing such a message, I'd certainly tell you where it was, and I'd probably drag it out for you...

Normally there would be other good options, but Blackburrow is a tough place to have that happen. For instance, while a Chipped Bone Rod is very useful in some circumstances for finding your body, I doubt it would work very well in the twisting tunnels of Blackburrow...

You might also get someone to invis/camo you so you can look around on your own. Just make sure you know your way out when you see it starting to wear off...

Good luck!
Dave

You don't want SoW, you want invis or camo.

SoW'ed you'll be running around in the BB maze, creating more trains and getting lost/falling.

Invisible, you can wander around and the gnolls should leave you alone. Con them, especially the high ones, and you should be indifferent, which indicates that they cannot see through the invis/camo and you are safe to walk right by them. When you get to your corpse, use /corpse command to pull it to a safe place before you loot it, as that will break the invis/camo effect.

You should at least get a location (as others have pointed out) before you head down, as invis/camo don't last a super long time.

Is there any safe spot for level 7s in BB? LOL. I didn't go into BB until the typical gnoll was green to Nikkon. Man that's a crowded place.

FWIW, I would try pretty hard to do the corpse recovery yourself (with the help of invis and a loc from someone). If nothing else, it will teach you how to do CR for later when it is important, and take it as a quest/punishment so you will remember to always do a /loc when you are lost and dieing.

Nikkon
