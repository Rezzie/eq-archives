Station Member 		posted 01-22-2001 11:01 AM           
Clerics DO need changes.

Two aspects:

1. Making the class more "fun" to play -- as clerics have very few "fun" spells compared to other classes and are in general boring to play.

2. Putting clerics on a more equal footing spell-wise with other pure casters, ie giving clerics abilities that ALL other pure casters have.

To fix this--

to fix "1"-

Make Mark of Karn somewhat useful. Noone uses this spell anymore. It's level 56. Undo part of your UBER-NERF from a few months ago. Also do something -- anything -- to make the hammer pet somewhat useful. Noone casts that spell as anything but a joke.

Also, please. Give clerics a group AC buff. It will remove part of the tediousness from buffing making the game more fun for clerics. There are two AC buff spells at level 57 for clerics. Make one of them a group spell perhaps?

to fix "2"-

Give clerics invis and see invis like every other caster class, even if self-only.

Also give clerics a form of self-mana regen like every other caster class. Perhaps incorporate the mana regen as a part of armor of protection (level 34 sov spell) or as part of aegolism (level 60 sov spell).

In regards to general cleric issues:

Divine light fizzle rate is far too high. Comparable to druids/shamans and superior heal.

Aegolism is not worth the mana for the buff it gives, due to the availability of heroic bond and naltrons mark. Clerics have done much analysis (and ranting) of this on EQ Cleric boards. It should be a group buff costing more mana, or have mana cost reduced, or have an additional (clarity, like the level 60 druid buff) component.

Heroic Bond should overwrite heroism, and vice versa, based on level of caster. Same with naltrons mark and other symbols.

Death pact / divine intervention line is currently basically useless due to the such low % of the time it actually works when the target dies.

Those are the most basic and needy issues for clerics. The reason not many clerics post is because after so many nerfs in a row the class has become apathetic and expecting doom. Please seriously consider these issues. I've been living with it on my cleric(s) for a combined 83 levels now.

-Spec
