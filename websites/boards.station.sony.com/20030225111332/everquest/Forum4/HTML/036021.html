A non-tinker can make fireworks with about a 40% success rate (that's what my chanter gets atleast) using the tinker recipes and a toolbox.

To get the best effect from the new fireworks graffics, you've got to aim the fireworks at yourself or someone else before you fire them and have your partical setting jacked up - it's REALLY worth it though.

Fireworks used to cause aggro though - I think that this was removed, not so much because of all of the poor newbs being killed when given fireworks (that part was a feature) but because a firework has an unlimited range and folks were using them to pull (that part is an exploit).

All said though, it's really worth your time to score a few to set off just for fun - the effects are really neat and you can mess around with folks who aren't familiar with them while grouping - my enchanter showed a group that had never seen fireworks before her "MEGA NUKE" spell not to long ago.

One firework is one shot, but 4 are made per successful combine. They are trivial to gnomes as soon as they get the Tinkering skill, and do not stack. Each of the 4 made/combine is a different effect, as can be seen when you right-click-and-hold on the item. They cost under 1p to make 4 of them, so maybe people would be selling them for from 1-5p each...I sometimes give them away, just because they're funny :P
