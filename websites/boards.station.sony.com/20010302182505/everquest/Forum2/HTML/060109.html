Yes this is in response to all the complaining warriors.

Everyone complains about druids. How druids are too powerful and how they infringe on every other caster classes abilities. They are rarely the strongest in any given ability but always near the top. Because of this there are more Druids than any other class. People hate them for this versatility

Well, lets look at warriors in the melee world post hybrid changes.

- They tank as well as any class in the game.

- Their offense is near the top of the melee scale. Monks and rogues do better in some situations.

- Their AC is the best

- Their HP are the best

- They can dual wield

- They can double attack

- They can kick

- They can use 1 handed weapons and a shield or they can use 2 handers as well as any class. shoot they even can use blunt weapons and bows.

- Some of their armor has spells.

- They are the most played melee class on the face of Norrath.

So what if a Shadow Knight has the same defense or a ranger has the same offense or that a bard can dual wield. You have all these things tied up in a nice package you can do it all melee wise.

How does that make you any different than Druids in the magic world?

You are and will remain to be the UBER of the melee classes.

And in case you were going to ask "Why play a warrior now?" It's because in a game that only has 6 people per group it would take 2 hybrids to match the abilities offensively and defensivly of 1 warrior.
