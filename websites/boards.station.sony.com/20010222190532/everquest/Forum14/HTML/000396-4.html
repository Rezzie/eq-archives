Here goes another summary of issues that should be important to ALL shaman:

* Healing

I don't think shaman want to even be close to cleric power when it comes to healing. They do want to have a level of healing that is sufficient so that the group doesn't have to disband when the cleric calls it a night. With the current level of healing there are SoV mobs (not dragons or bosses) that I can barely keep the tank alive against chain casting superior healing. I don't want CHeal or anything close, I just want something viable so that I can assist the cleric or as least be functional in a healing role. My proposal is the following:

- Move superior healing to level 49.
- Move the proposed Chloroblast (on test) to level 53 to replace superior healing in the line up.
- Add a celestial healing type spell at level 56. I would suggest a spell that does 4 ticks of 180 hp for 200 mana.
- Keep Torpor as is at level 60.

The last two spells have a heal over time characteristic are more fitting with the shaman background (imo).

* Stat buffs

I am sure we can all agree that we would love stat buffs to be more effective; that their effects would be greatly noticeable. Unfortunately, I think that such changes would be have such a wide impact on the game that implementing them in a balanced fashion would be a monumental, if not impossible, undertaking. I think the following considerations would help though:

- Mana costs on the level 51+ single target stat buffs should be lowered to 120 (manical stregth should stay at 150 due to the huge duration upgrade).
- The group buffs should have their effects increased to those of the best single target buffs. (Preferred solution) Another option would be to keep the lower level effects but lower the mana cost to 250.

As I commented in an earlier post, both of these changes are consistent with the mana cost progression of the spell line and with the mana ratio other classes have for their group buffs.

* Value on high end raids/SoV encounters

The above improvements to healing will offer some help for shaman in the high end raid environment. A couple other small solutions that might help (in order of priority).

- A lure type attack speed slow down spell. I would suggest at 25% slow down, level 58, 225 mana, 5 mins duration.
- A small improvement in the resists (chance that mob resists debuff) against the maloxxx line of spells. I would suggest a 10% improvement for starters.
- SoV added 2 poison DD spells for shaman, one at level 34 and one at level 44. I would add another at level 54.

* Stacking issues

Without listing all the stacking issues previously raised, it is clear that their are lots of problems with the stacking of high end shaman spells. I would be helpful if at least the issues with Avatar-Focus of the Spirit-Rage line could be addressed.

* The pet

The biggest concern here is the enormous mana cost increase. Lowering the mana cost by 200 would be a good start. Some have suggested adding a second pet at level 59. I don't know if this is truly needed. How many shaman at level 59 use a pet in a group environment?

I think these are the big five issues that could really impact ALL shaman. If there are any issues that could use attention, these are them. Now there are alot of other issues, but they are either not universal (affect only soling efficiency, affect only one race, are trivial after a certain level) or not particularly impactful (alchemy, shrink outdoors). I think it would be for all shaman if we stay focus on these core items and present suggestions that have reasoned support.
