Station Member 		posted 01-22-2001 12:21 PM           
There are definite problems with the monk class as it exists today in Velious.

Due to the hard-cap of coding, our skills have been virtually static since the level 50 days pre-Kunark. We were the kings of melee at that time. With Kunark, however, all of the other melee classes came VERY close to our skill levels. They also gained very powerful new weapons and armor, allowing them to EASILY outdamage us when we used our fists. This was a problem that Verant apparently tried to fix with ungodly rare weapons. I'm not even going to go into the problems with our armor, pre- or post-expansion.

Fixes to the monk class, in my opinion:

1) Up fist damage post-50 for those without epics. While the delay continues to drop, the damage never increases once a monk hits level 50. Very few monks use fists anymore, but this would make the RP'ers and casual players a tad more viable near the end-game, if they ever get there. This is the most trivial of my proposed changes.

2) Minimum damage on Flying Kick. It won't make a huge difference in our damage output, but it will at least make us FEEL better. There is nothing worse than doing a flying kick for 3 damage as a post-50 monk. Even making this minimum damage cap post-50 only would be an improvement, and would not unbalance the pre-50 game where monks did have an advantage.

3) Alter FD for aggro removal. Leave it as it currently functions in all but one area -- If the monk is not removed from the mob's haste list, give it an automatic "Evade"-type effect. In other words, allow a monk to sacrifice a couple of combat rounds to lower themselves on the hate list. This would DRAMATICALLY increase monk survival rates on uber-mobs, when we're super-spamming them with epic fists. It also provides a check to ensure that monk damage is not overly high, if Verant is still worried about that. Finally, since this is linked to a SKILL, it still has a chance to fail, just like a rogue's evade skill.

4) Fix the Celestial Fist effect. Celestial Tranquility is a great effect on our epic, but it stacks with almost NOTHING. Most monks rarely solo post-50, and most common group buffs will keep the effect from working. (Call of Earth, All enchanter haste spells, most bard songs, Speed of the Shissar, etc.) At least alter it so that even if the haste is not usable then the additional effects -- the extra +AC and +ATK -- will stack anyway, similar to the way the Robe of the Whistling Fists worked. It's pretty bad when a well-meaning ranger tosses me Call of Earth and costs me 40ATK and 12AC.

5) Some type of minor upgrade to damage output. Rogues are incredibly powerful when they are behind a mob. Warriors are incredible tanks. Monk damage -- either with an "uber" staff or with epic fists -- should be halfway between a rogue's potential when the rogue is behind a mob using a Ragebringer and a warrior who is attacking with his/her best weapons.

6) Increase Warrior, Paladin, and ShadowKnight taunting skills. Yes, I know this isn't an enhancement to MONKS, but it would DEFINITELY help us out. WE ARE NOT TANKS. Unfortunately, it's very hard to taunt off of us due to our extreme aggro, ESPECIALLY with our low-damage, low-delay epic fists. PLEASE, PLEASE, PLEASE implement this one change if you don't make any other!!
