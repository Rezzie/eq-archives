Temple Cazic-Thule got a nifty workover quite a while ago!

There were several quested armour bits that involved CT and drops inside. When it was revamped, so were the drops. (The quests would have become impossible, otherwise)

The lizards you seek were thrown out of their Temple and now lurk suspiciously outside the building in various spots. Start checking the lizard-infested areas in the Feerott... outside the Temple and quite a bit safer

Yep, no more lizard page inside the temple. I did both the totemic and the bard helm at the same time. (at least, I think it is the helm).

There is one temple that has the Page, and about 4-6 others. Although there are other temples nearby, those lizards are not in agro range, but you will want to root any lizards that run off. There are also no wanderers in that area.

Now, if it is the helm and my memory is working correctly, you will also need the skin of the great bear from Everfrost. A ranger can track this from above the river, sort of between starn and the river. But that was scarier getting the bear than it was getting the lizard. The Lich who also lives in the caves is one tough NPC.
