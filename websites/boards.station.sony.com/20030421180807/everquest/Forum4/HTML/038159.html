quote:
I'd also add skill points to Sense heading the first few levels. Sense heading seems to be a "seed" skill, you have to kind of add 10 or so points for it for it to go up easily while hitting a button.

Save your points and put sense heading on a couple of macro buttons. Put sense heading in both your #9 and #10 hotkey slots, then go to your keyboard options. From the drop down menu select the 'macro' option. Click the line for Hotkey 9, and set it's alternate to your left arrow button. Then click the line for 10 and set it to your right arrow button. Now everytime you turn, you'll practice your sense heading.

It does make for a bit of extra spam, but it's pretty easy to ignore, and a very easy way to raise the skill without much effort
