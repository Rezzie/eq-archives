Way back in December 2000/January 2001 the /camp command got new parameters. If I recall correctly, /camp desktop has been broken since the release of Luclin.

quote:
Added parameters to the /camp command (the command that will cause you to camp out if you are already sitting).
"/camp server" will take you back to the server selection screen.
"/camp desktop" will take you out to the desktop following a successful camp.

Despite popular request, "/camp spawn" will not be implemented.

There has been a work around for casters where if they did the following in order:

-- /sit
-- /camp desktop
-- Open spellbook
-- Wait 30 seconds

they would be brought back to their desktop instead of having to click the "Exit" buttons.

Before Luclin, just sitting and doing /camp desktop would bring everyone to their desktop, but post Luclin this only worked for casters if you used the above work around. To my great surprise though after downloading the patches from the past couple of days, this now appears to be working for melee classes as well.

Huzzah!!
