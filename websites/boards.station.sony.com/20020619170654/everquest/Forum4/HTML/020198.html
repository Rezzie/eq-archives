Unfortunately you can kill all the Kobolds you want and not get any better with the merchants. Killing the kobolds will get you in good with the guards and the Heretics.

Depending upon haw far you want your faction to go there is a rough guide you can use to follow.
To go from REady to attack to Glares at you threateningly (3-400 faction hits).

Glares at you Threateningly to Dubious (3-400 faction hits)

Dubious to Apprehensive (6-800 faction hits)

Apprehensive to Indifferent (1-200 faction hits)

Indifferent to Amiable (1-200 faction hits)

Amiable to Consider Kindly (6-800 faction hits)

Consider Kindly to Warmly (3-400 faction hits)

Warmly to Ally (3-400 faction hits)

You're average Kobold equals one faction hit. The named guys may equal more.

I killed the Heretic Elial Brooks in Toxx forest 75 times and went from Ready to Attack to Amiable to the magic wielding classes in the Erud Palace.

He equals about 20-25 faction hits per kill, but then you would have to go back into the Warrens to repair your Heretic faction.
