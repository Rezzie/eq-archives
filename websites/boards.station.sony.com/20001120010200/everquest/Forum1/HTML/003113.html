Taliseian,
These are strict hardware issues, I believe Drakkus is on to something with the power features idea. If you do the control alt delete idea of cantplaybug, you should keep systray open as well as explorer. You might also have an IRQ conflict. Right click on my computer> go to properties> go to device manager> click on computer and view resources for the IRQ’s. If your video card shares resources it might inhibit its performance. You might need to take other conflicting cards out and put the cards in other matching slots in your motherboard to free up the IRQ for the videocard. Check your IRQ setting number to see if you have any numbers that match. Feel free to ignore PCI steering. Perhaps your Sound card and Videocard are sharing resources. If they are you would need to pull one of the cards out and place it in another compatible slot.
You might want to go to the www.3dfxgamers.com website and try either the new drivers or the beta drivers they have.
Another idea is to actually set up a dual boot operation system, where you can boot up in Windows 98 and play using windows 98 drivers. Windows 98 is a little more stable and was designed more as a gaming platform. Windows 2000 was designed for business applications
