I have read many well written posts, all seeming to ask for the same thing. The reason being Rangers REALLY need these changes to be viable addition to a group.

1)Defense as all have stated, 230
2)increase dodge, possible parry as well.
3)I think a bow damage bonus is in order, or possible a replacement for disarm, a double shot skill(double attack with bow), currently disearm is a skill not used, at 55 max I have never seen it succeed. Our min damage with bows needs to be looked at, hitting for 1 dmg with a dmg 6 arrow is silly.

4)I am still a few levels from trueshot so cannot comment, I do however almost never use my bow at 51, it was my most important tool at lv 30-40.

5)On the panic animal I understand the mixed reactions, however look at the Knight classes. They both have spook undead at lv 22, the fear animal spell is common line of spells for druid, makes sense for ranger to get first one. I would use the fear spell, as I cannot get into groups I could at least solo the higher animals in velious. Could even argue for charm, but I won't, not necessary for balance.
6)HP increase is needed, for all the good reasons others posted.

7)I do not agree Bind,gate, or self only ports will actually balance the class, they might be nice for self use. I don't need them.

8)I would not mind seeing summon arrow spell at 50+, it becomes very hard to make enough arrows to carry around, or someother viable methods to carry arrows, I run out of room fast. Also cannot seem to find anyone selling Tolan bracers, so having a spell I can cast to create them at this higher level would be nice. Remember lv 53 mage gets a summon quiver spell, so should not step on their toes.

9)I would agree with others who have stated we need greater heal at lower level, I think lv 54 would fit, even then with our mana pool, and the damage MOBs do at that levelm, would still be after fight heal.

10)I saw an idea from a cleric, in one page or other I really liked, a type of regen when we get down to last 2 bubs hp, I cannot mention the number of times I have been at 2 bubs health with full lv 60 cleric buffs, then dead seconds later before Complete heal could land. I don't really have any great suggestions on how to impliment this, but we could use semething to by us that extra time. How about this, our parry, and riposte skills get a huge bonus while our hp's are in the danger area.(look at it as we shift our focuse from trying to kill the monster too trying to stay alive, go all defensive)

Again I apologize for having some repeat suggestions, but they are THAT important too this class. I look forward to the ranger track changes in the near future. Until rangers get some changes you can find me on as my lv 50 enchanter.
