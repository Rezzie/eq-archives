First off, great job on the smithing update. This is a lot more widespread than I ever imagined. However, as with every new product, there are going to be areas that need a bit of fine tuning. Here's my take on the Barbarian smithing stuff.

Weapons - Northman weapons are made with Medium Quality sheets or folded sheets of metal, appropriate molds, and water. The gear turned out from these are pretty decent, for what you put into making them.

Imbued Weapons - Magical versions, usable by Tribunal followers, are made by adding Imbued Ivory to the mix, and replacing water with Frost Temper. Only made the mace so far, but again, pretty decent for what it takes.

Northman Ring Mail - This one puzzled me, but I finally got it figured out, and made some. (Thread up on the Shamans Crucible at http://eqforge.com/forums/Forum16/HTML/008975.html about it.)

Imbued Northman Ringmail can be made by the addition of Imbued Ivory to the mix, no other changes, and adds (to tunic) +1 Str, +2 Wis, +2 vs Cold. Nice stuff, but only for Tribunal followers.

This is where I see a problem - Northman Ringmail takes MQ Rings, Smithy Hammer, Large Leather armor, Large Chainmail pattern, and Frost Temper. All for armor that is a little better than bronze (Banded tunic - AC 15, Wght 6.5; Bronze BP - AC 17, Wght 12.5; Northman Ring Tunic - AC 17, Wght 8.5). Frost Tempers are brewed using one Brandy (bought at bar), one Ice Goblin Blood (from Ice Goblins), and two Essence of Winter (from Ice Giants). Essence of Winter seems to be a somewhat rare drop off the ice giants... was grouped there for about 3 hours the other night, and we only got 3. With the seeming rarity, unless there are a lot more sources for it out there, I doubt if there will be a full suit of Northman Ringmail on any server - I know I won't make any; I'll be saving my Frost Tempers for making Imbued stuff. If the regular Northman Ringmail took water or something more common than Frost Temper, it would be a good upgrade to banded, and even bronze. Needing Frost Temper is just going to make it unused.

Couple of suggestions on this.
1) Change the recipe for the Northman Ringmail to use something other than Frost Temper, water or even some other higher end brew, like Ol' Tujims.
2) Change Frost Temper to require only one Essence of Winter, not two.
3) Increase the drops of Essence of Winter - perhaps add to drop tables for other winter creatures, be they in Everfrost, Permafrost, that iceberg in the Timorous Deep, and the snow region of the Dreadlands.

Hope you read this and look into the situation.
