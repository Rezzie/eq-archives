As for terminology, make sure you use the correct one when creating arguments.

DD = Direct Damage. Any form of spell that is hostile in nature and inflicts harm instantly after the spell is cast.

Nuke = Any form spell that is hostile in nature that does full damage to its target in one hit. All Damage spells that do all their damage in one shot are under this category.

DoT = Damage over Time. Any form of spell the does it's full Damage over a period Time.
