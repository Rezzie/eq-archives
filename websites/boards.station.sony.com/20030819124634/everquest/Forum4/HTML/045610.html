The Evil Eyes are casters as you know so I think it's important to have your pet beating on them to try to interrupt their casting as much as possible.

I'm pretty sure the Eyes can have their own pets up and charm you or your pet as well (unlike us PCs who get only one or the other). Best bet here is to have good Magic Resistence (an MR buff from a friendly Enchanter will help here). The EEs like to cast Malaise early, which will make you even more succeptible to the charm spell.

Yep, I did evil eyes at 25 with a shaman. These are great for a level 25 caster. All you need to do is nuke it to death. If you are a pet class, the pet may help by adding interrupts.

Evil Eyes very frequently have pets themselves, and very frequently charm. Thus, expect to be charmed at least once. Usually, its fairly harmless unless there are other PC's in the area. Only once I can remember where an evil eye charmed me, and then sent me up the cliff to drop me on my head. It took a necro to find my corpse even. Expect to die a couple of times at this level. But overall, its a very good camp solo for that age. If you are a melee, you may want to skip it. The trick to killing these is to kill them very fast! The longer it takes, the more often they will cast on you, inflicting more and more damage.
