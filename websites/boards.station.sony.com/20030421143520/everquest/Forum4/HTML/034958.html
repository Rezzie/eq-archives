This isn't so much a question but more just my truly enjoyable grouping experience. Last night I got into a full group in DSP of 2 monks, 2 rangers, a cleric, and me, a paladin. We were together for about 2 hours. It was just nonstop fighting with zero downtime..The two monks were just chain pulling anything that was near us.. Our damage output was so intense that the cleric was often standing with us meleeing..the mobs never had a chance to do any real damage. the cleric was actually complaining because no one needed his services other that replacing buffs...there were so many corpses around, no one ever was left out on loot...we all walked away with more than we could carry, including several bricks of acrylia each.. I was having so much fun that I quickly lost track of time...I wish all my groups were like this one. Everyone seemed to be having a great time! It was one of those nights that really makes you look forward to the next time you play.

See, that's how people are different.

I was in a group in Marus Seru with my Enchanter that was rolling like the one you describe, StBern. Killing everything in sight, everyone full mana and full health, xp pouring in etc. etc.

After about half an hour I found a good excuse to leave without offending anyone and went to Dawnshroud. There I got into another full group. We found a spot that was *just* within our abilities and commenced to hunt.

First pull we got two wolves and barely survived. We got ourselves together and things steadied. I had to do a fair bit of mezzing as adds were walking through or joining in the pull. The necro had to manablast me a few times and the cleric was medding pretty much constantly. We needed co-operation, forethought, and a bit of luck.

Our luck finally ran out when we got both a "named" wolf and a "named" Zelniak as agros while we were resting up from the last fight. I died fast, someone else died running and everyone else managed to zone.

The cleric rezzed the dead and we set up again and carried on for a fair while longer with a few close scrapes but no more deaths until finally the group broke up around midnight rl time.

I didn't even think about whether I was making good xp. I was just having fun, using tactics and riding my luck with a bunch of amusing, friendly people. When I did check my xp before I logged out, I'd done rather nicely, though not as well as I would gave done solo.

That's what I group for, not for xp or loot. I know easier ways to get both of those solo with nearly all my characters, but I don't know a better way to have fun in EQ than in a group operating just at the edge of its combined abilities.

All times are PT (US)
