This is an observation, and even though this board is barren, it seems to be the appropriate place.

I just want to say that I have unfortunately had to have several instances with our guides and GM Therdir (sp?) lately, and it appears to me that E'ci has really improved its CS.

Although I don't like the slew of bannings for having different people log onto different accounts, and I hope they rethink that policy quickly and apologize to all who have been suspened/banned unwarranted recently.

The reimbursement I needed was verified quickly and the item returned I have seen several others receive reimbursements for items lost to bugs, and the staff has been courteous and empathetic weeven on things they could not help with.

I think Therder cares about keeping the players happy as much as he can, and the guide quality seems to be much higher than it was. For a volunteer staff that is overworked and not paid they are really trying and dedicated. Its too easy to forget that.

I think E'ci is moving in the right direction in support, except for that one above horrible mistake. I say this because I myself am a customer service rep and I know we rarely get enough people thanking us for the hard work we do, and the way some customers treat us. Its not always fun to be a target with limited powers standing right in front of the abuse.

I know how the boards work and if this gets read some will think I am sucking up. I'm not and I don't care what you think so waste your time responding if you care. I have merely seen a positive trend I like and want to observe on that.

Thanks again and Good Day!!!

and bring back Bolenie, I dont want to have to map SoV myself for goodness sakes. Seriously, I don't understand how a player of Bolenie's character can get banned, a policy like that is just plain wrong!!!!!!!
