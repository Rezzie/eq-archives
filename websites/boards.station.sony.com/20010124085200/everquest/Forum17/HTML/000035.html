I mean no offense, but I do want to be taken seriously so I'll word it like this: I don't know whether or not the banning was appropriate, but I definetly agree with the decision to take away your last name, and would be upset with the GM that originally approved it. I'm tired of seeing names like "Elemental Summoner" or "Hard Hitter" or "Mean Person" etc. They're names, not descriptions of your abilities or characteristics. I honestly wouldn't want to take the fun from someone else playing the game, but when I see a name so obviously OOC it detracts from the appearance of a Fantasy world, and you might as well be playing a superhero game with names like "Spider Man" or "Green Goblin".
