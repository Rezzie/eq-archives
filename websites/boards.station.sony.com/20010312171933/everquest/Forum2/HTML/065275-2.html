Here's an interesting, unique and even balanced idea for a new proc.

Name: Mark of DeNerf
Target: Self only
Cast time: Instant
Effect: For each of the cleric's successful melee attacks the cleric performs a 12(?) point group heal.

Discussion: basically its a proc with 100% success rate. The proc is a group heal of some balanced ammount. Balancedmeaning, it should heal the group for ~33% more than the ammount that could be group healed with the mana that would be regenned with meditation, C2 and mana song2 combined in the same time frame. This would be useful when the cleric is fm, when a cleric would normally just be standing around. As it is a group heal, it is not nearly as good as medding and single target healing but it would at least allow the cleric to do something in those situations. Useful and not overpowering.

Formula:
mana regenned per tick * (spellheal / spellcost) = heal per tick with group heal
heal proc * proc % * attacks per tick = heal per tick with proc
bonus = heal per tick with proc / heal per tick with group heal
heal proc = ((1 + bonus) * mana regenned per tick * spellheal) / ( proc% * attacks per tick * spellcost)

Example:
(Guess)Cleric regens 30 mana a tick with meditation a, c2 and mana song2
(Guess)Cleric melees 4 times a tick with haste
Word of Restoration spellheal = 750
Word of Restoration spellcost = 900
proc% = 100%
bonus = 33%
heal proc = (1.33 * 30 * 900) / (1 * 4 * 750) = 12

thats a 48 pt group heal a tick, approximately the same as a bard using 2 heal songs. Not unbalancing, not overpowering, but at least useful.
