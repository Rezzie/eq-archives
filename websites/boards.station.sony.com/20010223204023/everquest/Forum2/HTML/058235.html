No rent stuff disappears when you log out, not when you zone. Pets do disappear when you zone, but that's for a different reason. Pets kill themselves if they lose their master, so if the master hits the zone, the pet suicides before it could get the zone. (Not that NPCs will zone anyway.) I'm sure someone somewhere is pleased that pets can't zone; it's bound to be considered a feature by now. But the regular norent items do stick around through zoning.

There were relatively recent changes (not "since day one") to the linkdeath code that deletes all norent items if you go LD. Apparently it's part of some anti-duping fix. On day one, you stood some chance of keeping your norent stuff if you could get right back on. Now, it just gets deleted.

So, if the AE dragon roar really tags you as LD through some bizarre kludge, then it also has the effect of deleting your items. I seriously doubt this is an intended effect of dragon roars. Mass LD is pretty common in raids anyway, so it may not even be that the AE fear is meant to tag you as LD; perhaps it just usually happens that way.

It's a bug, or interaction of several bugs. Ought to be fixed. When? Who knows.
