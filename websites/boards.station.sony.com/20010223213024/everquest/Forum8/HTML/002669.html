Yakatizma
Station Member 		posted 01-30-2001 01:45 PM           
-Fixed the Gator Roll baking recipe.
-Repaired the text instructions for creating a Mixing Bowl with pottery.
-Repaired Holgresh Fur Cap and Holgresh Fur Moccasins tailoring recipes.
-Made it so that the file is returned in addition to the hammer when a character fails to forge any of the various chain jointings.
-Repaired Recipe for Enchanted Tier`Dal Adamantite Bracelet
-Mithril and Velium Bonings will now be stackable.
-Mithril Studs will be stackable.
-Made it so that the Dwarven Smithy Hammer is returned on a failure to forge Brellium Chain Jointing.
-Repaired Field Plate Boots smithing recipe
-Changed the name of the smithed Enchanted Cabilis Scale armor to Imbued Cabilis Scale armor for naming convention reasons.
-Repaired the recipe for converting 3 Enchanted Large Adamantite Bricks into 1 Enchanted Block of Adamantite in the Tier`Dal Forge, it was previously only possible in the Oggok Forge.
-Made it so that the Bread Tin is returned upon a failure to bake bread.

Thank you for taking the time to report trade bugs and suggestions for improvements.

Great! Stackable Bonings in particular will make my tradesguy happy!

Sounds like some other items remain unimplemented as of yet including;

1) Ogre splint armor only fitting on Size Medium people and not size Large Ogres and Trolls.

2) Poisons not yet stackable / charged.

3) Imbued / Enchanted armor types are not available for all race / class combos yet, not even the expected ones. Any race with Clerics / Druids / Shamen should have an Imbued armor for each priesthood represented, at the minimum. Any race with Enchanters should have a form of Enchanted armor. These armors should be seperable, as mentioned before, allowing a Cleric to choose to have his Plate Imbued, but not Enchanted, and an Agnostic Magician to have a suit of Enchanted chainmail that is not Imbued to any diety. Imbues will be primarily used by Wisdom casters and hybrids, thus Wisdom bonuses will be more common here. Enchantment is going to have nothing to do with a god, but make the materials lighter and stronger and perhaps include a plus to Intelligence or some +Magic Resistance... (Thus a Tier'Dal Shadow Knight will be more likely to choose Enchanted Tier'Dal Plate over Innoruuk-Imbued Tier'Dal Plate, but might also find BOTH options desireable, just as a Wood Elven Druid would find that his Tunare-Imbued Chainmail would be superior if also crafted as lighter and stronger Enchanted mail during the creation...)

4) Blue Diamonds are now EXTREMELY unlikely to be used in Smithing, all are hoarded for (frankly out of whack) Jewelcrafting uses. Jacinths were always rare as well. *ALL* other gems can be made into at most three or four items of simultaneously wearable jewelry. Blue Diamond jewelry is vastly superior to the rarer Diamond jewelry and can be worn on EIGHT different body slot locations. If this completely unbalanced introduction is to be retained, please consider changing the Enchantment process require other high end purchased gems like Rubies (Ogres), Sapphires (Dark Elves) or Fire Emeralds (High Elves) so that smiths do not have to compete with jewelers for rare drop stones.

Given that an item of Blue Diamond jewelry is statistically many times superior to a finished item of Enchanted Smithcraft, which might require a half-dozen Blue Diamonds to enchant, a person would have to be a complete moron to actually waste these valuable gems (selling for 2k in East Commons now in my server, TWICE the going rate for normal Diamonds) in Cultural Smithing.
