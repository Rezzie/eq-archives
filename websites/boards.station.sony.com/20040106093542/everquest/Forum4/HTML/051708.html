For some reason not all trainers can train "sense traps" -- I ran into this with my rogue. I think you have to talk to the Dark Elf trainer in East Freeport, or maybe the Rogue trainer in PoK in the building near the Great Divide stone (he's upstairs inside that building). You can train it all the way up if you have enough points and enough cash... after I think level 10 or 20, points start costing you coins as well as training points.

Remember, too, if you blow all your training points on this one skill, then in a pinch you won't have points to use for other skills -- like pickpocket, make poison, etc..

Best thing to do is hotkey it and set it to trigger every time you turn or something.. that's what I did, and casually playing I was able to max it in under a week.
