I have no problem with druids and shamans getting better heals to help balance them against clerics. I do have a problem with clerics and shamans getting nothing at all to help balance them against druids.

I have never had a problem with shamans getting better heals. Since shamans can not port, and are limited by their running speed, be it SoW or whatever. They are in the same boat is clerics when looking for groups.

Druids, on the other hand, with new improved healing spells, should have no problem EVER finding a group. Even if the group is 20 zones away. This is where I have a problem. Clerics AND shamans are going to be in direct competition with druids for pick up groups. ONLY druids have the ability to port 20 zones to join a qroup.

In my opinion, shamans and clerics need to be balanced vs the improved druid heals and their ability to port around to find or join groups.

I believe clerics need the following to help them compete with druids.
1) SELF invisibility.
2) SELF port to HOME diety specific temple. Make it 1k mana with res effects.
3) Incresed cap on CURRENT melee skills.

I believe shamans need the following to help them compete with druids.
1) SELF port to HOME diety specific temple. Make it 1k mana with res effects.
2) Add a complete heal over time for shamans at level 60. The CH should heal a players hit points over a 10 second period.

How do I feel this balances the three "priest" classes?

Clerics retain the best possible healing and resing ability, but lack the ability to cover great distances to join or find groups. Clerics would still be cursed with very difficult solo ability and still require groups. Invisibility would help in travel as would the self port. Clerics would still be required on raids for their instant complete heal and resing ability.

Shamans retain their buffing and DoT abilities and gain the ability to CH at the highest level. The CH helps to add to their usefullness in raid encounters with highly magic resistant mobs. The self port would help in travel.

Druids retain their unmatched ability to travel and with improved healing spells, become a quick and easy addition to any pick up group in need of a healer. The improved healing spells make them a good addition to raids but does not overshadow either a cleric or a shamans CH ability.

So many players are fixed on this "uber" encounter mentality. Uber encounters for the normal player are a tiny percentage of actual game play. No one can deny a clerics importance in uber encounters but no one can truthfully deny that clerics will be delt a painful blow in every day pick up groups.

Many seem to find this amusing that "clerics will have to look for groups now". When have clerics not? Clerics have to wait for groups just like any other class, and unlike the other two "priest" classes, clerics have very little solo abiliy when not grouped and travel painfully slow from zone to zone when in search of a group.

In my opinion I dont think anything, anyone has said has mattered in the class threads. I appared to me that VI already had an agenda when the threads were started and already knew what they were going to add with each class. I believe thats why Absor comes off so hostile in many of the class threads opening posts.
