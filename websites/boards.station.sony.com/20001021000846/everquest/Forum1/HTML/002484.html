I have been banned from EverQuest for excessive swearing. I am Pencroft, from Tallon Zek. A GM told me that I was going to be suspended for ten to fourteen days. This saddened me at first. I always thought that being suspended from EverQuest was a way to teach a player that such misbehavior is not acceptable. It worked for me. During the ten days, I had come to a conclusion to put my swearing to a halt. I was going to change.

I felt like I was a Jean Valjean, right when the bishop changed his life. The first thing I was going to do was go to chat, say, HELLO HELLO! And say, THANK YOU SO MUCH VERANT FOR TEACHING ME A LESSON, EVERQUEST IS THE BEST GAME EVER! I was also going to run around every zone with all of the people who hated me in it (Which is quite alot of people, I must admit) and say, I'VE CHANGED! I SHALL NO LONGER SWEAR! THANKS TO VERANT, WHO SUSPENDED ME, AND WHO TAUGHT ME A LESSON, THAT PROFANITY IS SIMPLY NOT ACCEPTABLE! OH THANK YOU SO MUCH VERANT! Seriously. I think during those ten days my mind must've snapped or something. Anyway, what's important is, that I was going to change my ways.

Yet when I entered my password, I got this message: Error 0026: You have been BANNED from EverQuest due to inappropiate behavior. To contest this decision, please contact tech support. This was devastating. I had typed up four pages of things to do when I got back to EverQuest, all for nothing. All of my resolutions were for nothing. I couldn't believe it. I had seriously learned my lesson, and I was determined to stop my swearing, but I was banned.

So here's a message to everybody: If you like EverQuest, then don't swear. Because being banned just isn't a most pleasurable thing. It wasn't very enjoyable for me.

Being banned really sux. So don't swear.

With this I conclude this topic.
