Station Member 		posted 02-21-2001 01:50 PM           
Basiclly every time you have a successful PP you either get half of the coins that are left on a mob in a certain set (ie Plat, Gold, Silver, Copper). But Rogues can not take the last coin in of each type that a monster had.

Ex.

Mob Coinage
5pp 0gp 10sp 9cp
2pp 0gp 10sp 9cp Rogue got 3pp
1pp 0gp 10sp 9cp Rogue got 1pp
1pp 0gp 10sp 4cp Rogue got 5cp

Final loot
1pp 0gp 10sp 4cp


Almost all items can not be PPed at the high end of the game. No magic, 'no drop', worn or wielded items can be PPed. Or course, I have had success at PP mobs body parts sometimes (orc fist pawn scalps, undead frogloc tongues, etc) At the lower lvls you have a larger selection of things you can PP, but by the time you get up to fighting the big stuff about the only thing left is gems and the rare spells. PPing items I would have to say happens very rarely in my exp. And mobs dropping good gems or spells happens even more rarely.
