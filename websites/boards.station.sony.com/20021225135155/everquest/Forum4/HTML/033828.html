The higher your level, the smaller your agro range. My first visit to hate I was dubbed main puller. I was me and an sk pulling. I would get the agro (usually just by standing) get close to where my FD spot was. FD there. Then when it was safe I told the sk to tag off me. Then the MA tagged off him. Doing this we had no problems at all. How did your brother cause the wipe out? If you or he feign to close to the raid and its an overpull or even just a blown FD (fallen to the ground) that can cause lots of problems. Feign spot needs to be in sight of the group but not agro range. If you have too much agro or get grav-fluxed (read draco lichs) FD and /q out.

And the biggest responsibility you have is to tell the healer when it is safe to heal you. You dont want the cleric to get your agro and train the group. And mob you really have to watch out for is the Deathrot knight. BAsically a giant DE SK. He can cause wierd agro.
