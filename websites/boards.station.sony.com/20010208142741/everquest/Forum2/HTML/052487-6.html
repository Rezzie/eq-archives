ABASHI, please take the time to read this... (yes, that was a shameless attention getting ploy)

Level 60 enchanter here from one of the 'uber guilds' on my server.

I love Velious, or at least the idea of it. Many people have covered the same ground so I won't go over it again. I do however want to make certain points.

Velious has many wonderful things, however the faction idea is a great idea gone wrong. Currently, there is a circular problem. Most of the stuff for higher levels requires guild raids to achieve. Guild raids happen on the mobs that drop the loot people want, either for quests or as drops. Currently in Velious on the upper scale there is no good reason to choose any faction to ally with other than Giant.

Giants currently give the best (read that as nice reward plus actually doable) quests. It's obvious that the quests for Dragon faction are not finished. The higher end quests for dwarf faction are not comprable either.

If you are in good with the giants you can kill dwarves AND dragons. You can even kill the Dain and not be KoS to thurgadin dwarves if you desire (what's up with that? Kill their king but that's okay?).

As you know if you are a high level player it's generally about the end game. End game in Velious is Sleepers and you have to ruin your dragon faction to hunt there!

Temple of Veeshan has a good idea - hunt dragons in the test area. Problem with that is the test area is a nightmare. Far, far too difficult to fight in (pathing and aggro is just awful) for the rewards.

No dropped armor for Rangers/Druids/Paladins and those of the other classes that don't want to kill in Growth.

Some really nice quests. Kudos to the team on some of them. The Eastern Wastes quests and the Iceclad Ocean quests are really neat and doable. Armor quests are just out of whack. The Kael quests are really neat as well.

Put in more quests with dragons (in both skyshrine and Western Wastes) and dwarves (especially high end ones with the Dain and his court) that are doable and have good rewards ala the Kael ones.

Velketor's in the Kobold area is a great zone. Tough but doable and the exp is wonderful. I wouldn't suggest changing that part. The spiders up front do need a bit more loot for that level crowd.

Siren's is just bad. I WANT to like this zone. Please, please fix it.

Especially givent the difficulty of the SOV mobs.

I play a warrior, but this indirectly affects me, as well as my guild performance in these zones. This needs to be addressed as well.

The "pick your poison" faction system really sucks. You design zones for raiding partys and make it so guilds will be divided on what they want to kill. Druids/rangers wont kill in POG for fear they will be KOS in Kelethin. Druids/Wizards want dragon faction so they can port to zones where a dragon is present at the ring. Can't raid Dain as some want to keep their coldain faction up. I could go on, but I think you get the point.

Greens attacking you. This is just plain annoying and another one of your time sinks. If it has a chance of killing me then it had better give me exp. The only zone I have seen so far that has issues that should be addressed is Velk's Lab. This on top of the high resist making most mobs unmezzable, really takes the fun factor out of this area. The entrance to the kobold area is assinine "SP?".
