Greetings

Instead of wasting my time wading through these message boards, which are built up to nearly 100 pages, I just read the posts listed on everlore.com. At certain times in the day, somebody from Everlore uses the search engine here to find posts from Abashi, Arudane, or Rowyl. It saves a lot of time, and lets me keep on top of what's going to change. Anyway... to the topic of this post.

Rowyl rules! Let me count the ways.

1. Buying a stacks with a single click is wonderful.

2. Larger hotkey pages, even if the total number doesn't change.

3. Adding more and more customized colors.

4. Having a snazzy name. The sort that personal trainers have.

5. Making an effort to respond to posts, even though it's not part of his job.

6. Rowyl knows his Cartoon Network! When posting about spell gems, he gave a quote similar to one spoken by Mojo Jojo in an episode of the Power Puff Girls. This was the episode where Bubbles (the blue one) gets hit by an I-Beam, and is convinced she is the evil monkey, Mojo Jojo. Mojo Bubbles wreaks havoc on Townsville, and the other girls don't feel right beating her up. So, they get creamed. But Mojo Jojo (the real one) doesn't like being impersonated, so he hits her again. When Bubbles goes back to her old self, Mojo Jojo say something like this: (not exact quote)

"The number of Mojo Jojos in the world shall be one, and one is the number of Mojo Jojos. Two is too many. Three or more is right out!"

It doesn't take an erudite caster to realise that this is similar to Rowyl's quote:

"The number of memmed spells shall be 8, and 8 is the number of memmed spells. 9 or more is right out!"

7. Also, if I know my Johnny Bravo, Rowyl is the name of the fellow who tried to seduce Johnny's mamma and get to her savings. I don't know if this is a connection or not.

Far too much time on my hands,
Mecherath
