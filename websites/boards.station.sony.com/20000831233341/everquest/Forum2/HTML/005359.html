For those of you that wonder how to raise faction with the spouse guild after becoming KOS after that last plane raid here are some of the mobs that raise faction:

1. Weeds (kill 'em and earn faction, majic users with a good AoE poison rain type spell will find this an easy quest)

2. Paint flake (This mob had extremely high magic resist unlike weeds which can be handle with AoE rain spells, definately a tough melee mob)

3. The Frosty Ice Box (this mob often takes time to defeat, be careful using melee, many a rogue has pierced the Frosty Ice Box and gotten sprayed by the frustrating "Freon Mist" which instantly kills all faction you are trying to raise.)

4. The Greater Laundry Goo (takes special equipment to handle this mob but unlike other mobs it is possible to defeat this mob and gain faction while playing EQ at the same time, provided you have the proper equipment on hand.)

There are also a few quests to raise faction:

1. The Flowers Quest, very effective, just run down and purchase a bunch of flowers and return them to the guild master. Your reward is faction and sometimes a nice prize (if you're lucky). Often the quest is combined the Candy Quest making extra trips unneeded.

2. The Dinner Quest. There are two versions. Take the guild master to a nice inn located in a non KoS zone and treat them. Although effective riasing your baking skills and preparing a meal to bring to the guild master gives more faction in most cases. After doing this quest however you must defeat the Dish Mob.
