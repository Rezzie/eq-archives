There is no 'etiquette' that applies to this, the person left for whatever reason, and thats all there is to it.

Its completely up to YOU, the person at the camp how to deal with the situation. You can share, you can leave or you can tell the person trying to come back to the camp to take a hike.

If it was me, and I showed up at a camp, that was empty or players, all the monsters were spawned, and a half hour later (according to your post) someone shows up and tries to claim the camp, I'd tell him/her to take a long walk off of a short pier.
