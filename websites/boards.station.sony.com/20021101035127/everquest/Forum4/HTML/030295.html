Velks is entirely doable at 54. You two may even be able to duo it if you don't get adds. As Aveen said, the ramp area tends to be a bit of a FFA, especially if there are mor than 3 groups in it. The best spider camp is Upper Spiders, but there are other area,s you can safely camp. You should be able to pick up folks to ad to your group.
The Upper and Lower Dogs camps usually require a full group with enchanter and cleric though, so stick with spiders for your first time.

I came to velks at L 51, before AAXP existed, and before people knew about it. =)

We crawled the place without anybody over 55 in the group.

So, I'd say Velks at 54 is doable, the question is, will it be overcroweded?

If it is, the two of you can probably duo in here. If not, you can get a group. =)

Trains when the place is full aren't bad. There aren't 3 mobs up in the area where you could train. =) And if 3 mobs do run, odds are there are people at the zoneline waiting to kill them.

The only bad trains are castle trains. Do NOT pull from the castle, you +will+ kill random people, there is a glass wall in the way, and the mobs take the long way around.
