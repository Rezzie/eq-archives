Hey Abashi

I really like all the Velious zones I have visited, and have been to all except Temple of Veeshan, PoG, PoM, and Sleeper's Tomb.

>Siren's Grotto -- Well like everyone else says, it's *ahem* quite a challenge. If hunting Othmirs makes this zone passable, those otters are going down! The funniest thing about Grotto: takes a few dragon raiding parties or high level wiz to get from CS to WW, but a lowly 15 druid with camo can get from WW to CS without problems, except for taking about 150 in fall damage.

>Dragon Necropolis -- Why are all the ratmen singly throwing themselves into the small pool of fire on the upper e side? Me no understand. There must be a roleplaying reason behind it, which I aim to find out. It would be kool if the traps were not set off when you had lev or dmf on, but usually when you are in this zone your party is big enough to handle the traps. Usually...

>Western Wastes -- One rule. When fighting Dragons, pull to northeastern corner, because they gate to that corner when low. Otherwise it may be time to camp and relog. Pirate ship run aground was a neat idea, and a nice safe spot in a zone of wanderers.

>Cobalt Scars -- I'm fascinated by this zone. When I first ran through it, I said to myself, "That's it??" But there are many quests given here and involving this zone, which makes it a very interesting place. Othmir are very likeable, perhaps even mascot material. The whole zone just grows on you. If it was melee bindable +bank npc I would practically live in Cobalt Scars.

>Wakening Lands -- Makes my comp lag a little, like Warsliks Woods did. I would like to see some Lumberjacks sent in, but the overall ambiance of the zone probably wouldn't be as rich if it were changed. Pretty zone. Wuoshi? As the name suggests, a Wario/Luigi/Yoshi hybrid, but hits as hard as Bowser.

>Crystal Caverns -- Ornate, intricate, Large. Intriguing place for a city. Just plain amazing zone.

>Velketor's Labyrinth -- Deathtrap lol. And I love it.

>Kael Drakken -- A plentiful supply of giants to kael - er, I mean keel.

Those are the ones that come to mind for now, Velious really turned out to be an excellent expansion. An explorer's paradise.
