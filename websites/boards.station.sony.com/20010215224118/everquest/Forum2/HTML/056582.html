Hello again.
This will be the thread for discussion about the rogue class. The idea is to look at the things that need to be changed to make the class 'balanced'. With that as the primary goal, I am still interested in hearing about bugs and other such things. But, primarily, I want to talk about balance.

My take on the rogue is pretty similar to that of the warrior. They are pretty well balanced. Obviously with EverQuest as a primarily player vs. environment game, some of the things that thieves traditionally do are not really part of the package. That's why, in EverQuest, they are titled rogues.

Again, I have had little time (actually, no time at all) to do research on rogue concerns before starting this thread. So please be as detailed as you need to be to get your point across. But, at the same time, please remember that this thread will get long. So being concise and readable is important too.

I do want to report one thing that we have already discovered internally about rogues that is broken and will be fixed when we make these changes. Yes, it is a nerf, but not in the true sense of the word.

In the old days, NPCs had a limited size to their hate-list (basically the way that they track who they are angry at). In those days, evade would just drop you to the bottom of that list. Recently we increased the size of that list dramatically. This allows us to do a lot more with NPCs. But evade was never changed to reflect this, so it still drops you to the bottom of the hate-list. Essentially, this allows a rogue to do significant damage to an opponent, and then with the use of evade, become less 'hated' than someone that has done no damage to the NPC at all, but just has a somewhat negative faction with that NPC and is standing someplace on the other side of the zone. The rogue can then beat the NPC up with impunity. Basically, evade is far too useful for avoiding aggro from an NPC, and we're going to change it to be more in line with its original function.

I wanted to let you know that at the outset. Please don't make it the focus of this thread. It’s certainly something we should talk about. But there are certainly other things that are worth discussion as well.

So let's hear what you think.

Alan

Greetings Absor

First off, i would like to thank you for the Improvments to the Bind Wound, Pickpocket and Apply Poison Skills.

As for Evade, I do not like the way it works Currently, although i think the changes you have talked about are a bit too extreme. Evading to the very bottom of the Hate list in a 6 man exp group can spell trouble for the Cleric/Chanter/Casters that are sitting medding while the melees are fighting. I try to keep my aggro somewhere between the Main tanks and casters in case the tank dies or goes LD.

The proposed change works pretty well to achieve this in a 6 person exp group. by lowereing us to number 3 on the Hate list, it puts us between Tanks and Casters rather nicely. Staying in that spot requires a bit of effort and well timed evades, but it's the Ideal place for a Rogue to be in order to deal dmg through Backstabs, yet still be able to pick up and take some hits in case the main tanks (number 1 and 2) die or go LD.

On a Large Scale raid though, Only dropping us to Number 3, isn't very effective. With full buffs/haste, it is VERY Easy to get to the top of the hate list. If you only drop to number 3 after a successful evade, then you'll be right back at the top in no time as soon as you turn attack back on. Doesnt' take too long before you end with a dead Rogue. It only gets worse with multiple Rogues.

In my Opinion, the best way to fix this problem, would be to implement the 33 percent idea. It would put the Rogue in the top third of the Hate list easily, Still with the Chance of aggroing back to the top, but not as fast.

Major change to the Evade skill could mean Death for Rogues on high lvl Raids. Is Evade really that Overpowering? I don't think so. Sure, you can change it to make it a bit harder for Rogues to live through big raids, but is REALLY necessary? As it is, through all of my numerous High lvl fights, (Dragons, Gods, Etc...) I have only been alive to see the mob fall to the ground ONCE. Rogues do Lots of dmg, but we already pay for it. Evade does not totally Eliminate a Rogue taking dmg, and eventually, we'll die anyway. The Change will just cause us to die faster.

As for my Wishlist:

Just a few minor things that I'd like to see implemented.
