Actually, the Stromm economy is significantly different from the other servers I play on. There is no overstock of high end items bringing the prices of middle and high end items down. So even acrylia reinforced masks are priced over 100 plat each, for example.

On the other hand, there are few (no?) folks with way too much money to burn who want to do tradeskills. So stuff like pelts go for 1-2 plat each. HQ cat pelts are routinely 5-6 plat. Spiderling silk only sells at less than a plat each. Et cetera.

So unlike the other servers, the tradeskill items don't rake in the plat, and middle- to high-end items ain't cheap.

The other servers have folks who have been "saving" up money for several years and have an abundance of money and middle- to high-end items. Not so on Stromm. Cool!
