quote:
My ranger is using a Wurmslayer at 49th. I have tried using a much faster weapon in the primary, but the damage from the extra hits/duals/procs all added together doesn't approach the damage from the Wurmy

You're very wrong here... Bigger damage does not equal better damage... Lamentation for instance, especially as you near the 50's does far better damage than wurmy - especially if you're STA is under 100...

You have to remember that the primary weapon damage gets a damage bonus every time you hit...

At level 50, with wurmslayer - the ranger is going to do around 550 damage per minute...

At level 50, that same ranger with a lamentation will hit for around 600 damage per minute...

quote:
I do pretty much nothing with my ranger but fear-kite because the xp is so ferociously fast

Even more of a reason to get rid of it... Remember, if your fatigue falls below 20% (something wurmy does VERY fast) and your STA is below 100 - your attack damage drops in speed...

All to many people see a couple hits of 100 as far superior to a hit of 40 - but they don't realize you swing 3 times faster with a lammy than you do with wurmy...

Lamentation is a far superior weapon at level 45+ ... No matter how you look at it.

I should add - for a long time, I used a Rev whip (5/14) with a Centi Longsword... Now you'd think that 9/19 is far better than 5/14 damage output - but you'd be incredibly wrong... People just plain ignore that weapon bonus without realizing how much it truly increases your damage output..

Level 50 ranger - 150 str - no haste:

Wurmy (25/40) + Centi LS (14/27) = 779 dpm (approx)
Lammy (9/19) + Centi LS (14/27) = 789 dpm (approx)
Rev. Whip (5/14) + Centi LS (14/27) = 793 dpm (approx)

Those don't include the damage bonus (as the program was written before those were officially released...)
