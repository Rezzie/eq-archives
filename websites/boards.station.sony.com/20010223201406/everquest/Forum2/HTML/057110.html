I was considering the problems that face Everquest, why so many people find that the sheen *quickly* vanishes once they get into the High Level game post-50.

In terms of balance, there are massive problems, only one class can heal, another single class has an absolute monopoly on all dungeon groups and at the very high end, only one class is really effective at tanking.

But those aren't game killers.

The game killer is travel - or the lack therefore. IT IS BY FAR the number one problem in EQ.

I've played deep in Sebilis without Enchanter, Cleric or Warriors, the same for Chardok and most of Velious. If you are smart and with it, its *playable*. It's not easy but it can be done.

But the actually killer is time.

I don't *HAVE* two hours to wait for everyone to get in position for a Raid. I don't *WANT* to waste over an hour of my time simply travelling.

Hours are lost for thousands upon thousands of players. Most classes have no viability to travel and those that can travel are burdened by constant demands and expectations from others to be ported around.

The world seems no larger when I have to run through Burning Wood, Skyfire, Overthere to get to the empty (due to its remote location and poor loot) dungeon of Charasis.

IT JUST IRRITATES.

The world seems no larger when I need to wait in Wakening Lands for the best part of an hour while my friends who kill Dragons have to port 4 zones away then run to join up with us.

IT JUST IRRITATES.

MORONS!
