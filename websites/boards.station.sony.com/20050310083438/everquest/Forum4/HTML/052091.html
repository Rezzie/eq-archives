Ways to increase mana regen:

1) Enchanter Clarity line
2) Druid Skin Buff Line (Protection of the Nine)
3) Flowing Thought items
4) Bard songs
5) Beastlord "spiritual" line (Spiritual Dominion, etc.)
6) Using mod rods summoned by mages
7) Buy a horse or drogmor (outdoors only)
8) Have a necro feed you mana
9) Beastlord Paragon of Spirit AA

There may be others, but that's the best I can do off the top of my head.
