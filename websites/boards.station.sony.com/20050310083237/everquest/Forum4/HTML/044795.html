General answer - Not all help is wanted. Ever hear of staggering spawn times? Let's say you have 8 spawns on a 16 minute respawn. Each kill is 1 minute, leaving you 1 minute of med time before the next. If somebody comes up and just starts 'helping you out' for a few kills, than your entire system is thrown off. Suddenly you have a series of 4 or 5 mobs spawning very quickly, leaving you little med time. In fact, some might start spawning while you're in the middle of a fight, adding on and generally causing havoc. Again, not all help is wanted, because you might be doing something the other person hasn't even considered.

For the specific example - This other guy just moved in to your camp area, sat in the path of your pulls, and than sent in his pet without a word? Trust me, if that happened to me, the other guy would be getting a lot more than a polite word asking him to back off.
