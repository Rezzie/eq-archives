I love EQ. I also like what I hear about UO2 and Shadowbane. I've played other games such as Baulders Gate II, Diablo 1 and 2, and I also have friends who spent a long time playing UO. So...here are 5 ideas that perhaps Verant will consider in future patches:

1. Housing - UO1 had housing and their are rumors that UO2 will eventually have it. At a minimum, a housing zone or expansion of the space in existing zones for housing would be cool and add variety.

2. PvP zones - UO2 after many years of experience from UO1 is providing PvP zones. This provides players who want the PvP experience to coexist with players that don't or aren't ready for it. A great idea that solves the chaotic random PvP killing problem in PvP games. These zones should be easy to add to EQ and you don't have to use them if you don't want to PvP. Sorry Verant, but this idea is cooler than the "PvP Switch". Origin looked at the PvP switch idea and hated it.

3. A "classless character": One of the big differences between some other games and EQ is the development of a character using skill points to determine what capabilites they will have. Verant could add this character type to EQ without violating the existing classes and it would provide even more variety to an already great game. What's more, it is this type of character that is best for PvP. Again, if you don't want to PvP, you don't have to. (As a side note, I play a 22 level cleric so you know I'm not a PvP maniac!).

4. Character details: Verant should update the character details to stay current with the industry. Facial hair etc. should be added. This capability could be added with a patch or as a new CD.

5. Update graphics: Update the graphics in the existing game with an add-on CD or a download. Easy way to stay modern.

All five of these things could be done with the existing game. What is great about them is that they don't have to violate the happiness of current players but will add variety and novelty for those people who desire it. Go EQ, I love this game.
