After being confonted with a ridiculous amount of misinformation, disagreement, and general lack of knowledge from people who should know better, I wanted to rehash the current group xp bonuses for anyone who cares or doesn't know.

2-person group 20% bonus
3-person group 40% bonus
4-person group 60% bonus
5-person group 80% bonus

Adding a 6th person still nets an 80% bonus, but each person gets a full share as though there were only 5 in the group. The sixth share is free.

Please, spread this around. People are clueless about this at all levels of the game, as today reminded me repeatedly.
