"First, is there a limit to the amount of exp you can get from a kill."

Yes, there is.


"Also, if it is, could I have a buddy group with me and share the exp so we could both get the cap?"

Yes, you can group with someone and split exp. Will you both reach the cap? Well, that depends what and where you are fighting.


"Are there still such things as 'hell' levels."

The exp curve has been smoothed out considerably, so hell levels do not exist as they once did. However, the curve is not perfect - there will still be levels where the exp requirements are different from your expectations.
