If he's brand new to the game, and doesn't know about it being night and day in the game, my guess is that he has no way to get a lightstone until he's around level 9 with a magic weapon.

What is your starting city? Try killing fire beetles in the meantime, they are about level 3, and you can put the fire beetle eyes in your secondary slot or in an inventory slot and that will help brighten things up a bit until you are high enough to kill a willowisp and get a lightstone. If there are no fire beetles where you are hunting, keep checking merchants for the eyes in case someone passing through has sold one recently. Not sure what else at the really low levels is a light source.
Oh, you can also do the command /time to see what time it is in game and if it will be getting dark soon.
