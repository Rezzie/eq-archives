An essential spell for any pet user that makes controling it a whole lot easier. Tell your pet to guard here at the zoneline, go to your camp spot and summon it in. This spell also makes it possible for pet users to roam a dungeon without headaches. Just leave the pet behind and summon it at the next rally point.

And if you don't have a fast casting invis ready (shame on you!), it can even be used to recall a pet that is chasing a runner.
