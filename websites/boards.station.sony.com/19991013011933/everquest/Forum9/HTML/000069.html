I don't mean to sound cruel, but this isn't real life. We're talking about magic here (well, I mean, if you have magic, then I guess this would be real life - but I don't).

In a RP fashion I don't think of Words of Transcendence and a water flask as physically combining to form a scroll with the writing of a new spell. I think of a magical transformation and the knowledge is passed on and represented by the spell we then inscribe in our books.
