As long as I'm hopping around these boards, let me take a stab at this one too.

When a client goes LD, the character become an npc exhibiting non-aggro AI (ie. it doesn't attack unless attacked). The game dictates that if an AI lands the killing blow on a monster, the monsters corpse will poof. It seems pretty conceivable that the PC is just being flagged as an NPC while linkdead which would explain why corpses poof when a linkdead player gets the killing blow.

You will see this behavior most often on boss level mobs around 50th to 55th level because the fights against those creatures usually have a lot of people and the fights don't last very long. So, when the fight starts, the servers overflow the clients involved causing several to drop, but the fights don't last long enough for the linkdead players to actually drop from the game completely, so they are still around when the fight ends. Also, most boss level mobs have an AE which will aggro the now-npc linkdead players. You won't see this happen very much on fights against creatures like Trakanon because the fights typically last long enough for the linkdead players from the beginning of the fight to drop completely before the creature dies.

Of course, this is just speculation based on watching what happens when a player goes linkdead and knowing what causes corpses to poof during normal gameply.
