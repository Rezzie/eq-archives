Go to Rathe mtns when your around level 49+..maybe 44+ if your a necro. Start killing HGs alone. You will need to visit bank often and it will help to have Jboots or SOW potions. Do this for a few weeks to a month until you have close to 20k saved up in pp. Then start shopping.

Now start looking for int items. You should definitly have by now (if your level 49):

Batskull Ear Rings (+5 int x 2) - 400pp
Saph Plat Necklace (+7 int) - 800pp
Ruined Cowel (+9 int) - 4k pp
Polished Bone Bracers (+3 int x 2) - 400pp
SMR (+9 int) - 4k pp
Sheer Bone Mask (+3 int) - 250pp
Ring of Frost (+3 int x 2) - 300pp
GEBs (+9 int) - 8k pp
Stien of Moggok (+10 int) - 250pp
Black Glowing Stone (+9 int) - 11k pp
Gaterscale arms (+4 int) - 600pp
Frozen orb (+3 int) - 3k pp

Total int gained = 84 Cost in pp (est..) = 33 - 35k pp.


These are non-"uber" items that can take a lowly 100int caster like myself up to 184 int.

These prices are for my server obviously and the price may be higher or lower on your server.

Hill Giants in rathe were the mainstay of my experience and pp up to level 49 (and pp to this day). Also getting in on a Gebs group in sol b a few times helpled..well more then a few times and I was lucky enough to win the roll once.
Unfortoantly I used it to pay for a spell I need (level 54 water pet) and did not feel like going to find a group again (very rare if your not uber guilded) so I killed a few hundreth or more hill giants and bought myself another pair later.

Point is you need a bit of plat (thus a bit of boring camping is required) if your not in an uber type guild. It can be done though. Good Luck.

Anyhow, I have posted this specific issue under a "race balancing" thread to point out how disadvantaged human casters are compared to other races. We have NO advantages whatsoever.

Facts:

1) We have NO EXP bonuses at ALL compared to other caster races.
2) We have POOR base INT.
3) Stats other than INT are useless for casters, except for CHA, which humans don't have either.
4) We have NO night vision (yeah, neither do erudites, but...)
5) We have NO racial language (wouldn't make a difference anyway as far as gameplay goes)
6) Human casters cannot smith ANYTHING they can wear for themselves, where high elves and dark elves CAN.
7) Faction bonuses??? What "faction bonuses"??? I haven't seen any places where any of the other races start out KoS when humans don't. Even IF we had some "faction bonus", factions can change, but base stats can't.

What we need to "fix" this problem is more human-caster-specific items that have increased INT to make up for our lack of base INT. I understand that giving "high INT" items to ALL races could lead to overpowering of the other race casters, so that's why humans need some specific stuff. After all, the lore says that humans are resourceful, and good at overcoming obstacles. I would think that we should have made some specific items to give us a leg up on the "smart" races.

This isn't about race-envy or nerfing anyone. This is about making some new items to help put human casters on equal footing with the others. The way I see it now, starting with 140+ INT allows you to use 40 INT worth of armor/equipment slots to add to your OTHER stats like resists when WE have to sacrifice those for "high-INT" items.

Look at my item listing, and ignore all the +INT components. Now, it's pretty CRAPPY equipment, isn't it? Let's see what we can do with only TWENTY additional INT points.

Replace GCB with a Velium Blue Diamond Bracer for +8 across resists.
Replace shawl with the one from kedge or the other nice stat items.
Replace one ring with Djarn's HP ring
Replace other one with something else.

Anyway, I hope you see my point. You can add +MANA items and such, where we cannot. The human caster is severly disadvantaged when compared to other race casters, and NEEDS to be tuned.
