DL might be good, for groupiong, but soloing there'd be tough. For solo work, go to LGuk, hunt the undead frogloks.

To raise plat, start farming. Typically tradeskill supplies (Silk Swatches, Leather Padding, as two examples) are the best source of income for lower level characters, because they can farm a ton of them and charge a nice amount to those higher levels who don't want to take the time farming. You could also try making things such as Heady Kiolas, that are used en masse but add a ton of clicks to the tradeskiller.

I can tell you where I soloed with my paladin around your level - the mines in Thurgadin. Scuttle bats and rats, tar goos, and spiders.

Within the mines there is a series of rooms where the train tracks end, and from there you can more or less constantly pull from the mobs that spawn in the vicinity. You will get adds occasionally, but root works well there.

It's handy for banks and shops and guards to run to (though I never needed to) and if you want to rejoin society the Great Divide PoK book is just a zone away.

I don't think your ability to solo there depends on your equipment, but as a measure, I was using an Arbitor Combine Greatsword and a 15% haste sash. Such things are relatively cheap in the bazaar these days.
