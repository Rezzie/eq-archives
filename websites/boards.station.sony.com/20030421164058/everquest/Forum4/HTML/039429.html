It is easy, but it takes time. More like 20 minutes solo without an epic. At 54, it took me about 9 minutes to drop them with epic. Just because it takes awhile doesn't mean it is difficult. And Breath of Ro is not your second best dot, it gets resisted too often. A level 60 druid would have a hard time kiting these guys, it is possible but why wait until level 60? I see level 51-53's solo them successfully, without epics, but it takes someone without winged death way too long to kill them, imo. I have seen a 53 and a 51 druid duo them with little downtime. Finding a partner there isn't hard. Breath of Ro is simply too flaky to ever rely on, it doesn't matter what mob you are talking about. Even mobs with low FR, like velium hounds resist it often. For the record my necros fire based dots never get resisted by the elysians, so they don't have very high FR. They do have high DR and PR, but you don't have to worry about that.

Root is iffy with them, sometimes it lasts 5 seconds, sometimes the full duration. And the skellies level seems to have little to do with how long snare lasts. It rarely is resisted though, which is good and snare is also very rarely resisted. Even when the elysians are not behaving, you have to not be paying attention to get hit. They don't move very fast when snared. Frequent root breaks are way more annoying then dangerous.

20k HP may seem like alot to you now, but it really isnt. It is more then the average mob of that level, but not terribly so. Once you hit level 55, the soloable mobs that give decent XP have 10-20K+ HP. 12-14k is probably the average. So we are not talking about a large increase.

Also Elysians do not cast, nor do they summon. They will, however enrage. But that is of little concern to a druid soloing them. There is nothing at all remarkable about them.

Think of it this way. The elysian armor is better then nearly everything in SoL except for what the boss mobs drop. It is way better then old world planes gear, better then most of the cultural armor and better then most of the Velious quest gear. I think Grey Flesh gloves, Tunurean Soldier Wristbands, and Velious quest BP are all better, but the other elysian is better. Grey Flesh are easy to get solo or cheap to buy. The wristbands can be pricey, 4k on my server, and the quest BP drops are insanely rare and you have to compete for the leather drops with other classes. and it takes a raid force. Meanwhile you can get outstanding gear for free, it just requires a little bravery, and get it solo. No other class gets such nice drops, and relatively common, off of soloable mobs.

The other class drops in UP are hard to get and very rare. The last few dark masters that my group of 5 people, level 48-60 fought, took nearly as long as soloing an elysian. They drop nice chain armor, but usually just drop essence of darkness. They have 50K HP, HT's, casts, summons and hits hard. Elysians are a cakewalk.

Sure, sitting at level 43, it is intimidating. But don't worry about it, you have a ways to go anyways, and it wont look so bad when you are level 53.

Elysians are fairly easy in the low to mid fifties, very easy 55 plus, considering what the loot is worth

Get KEI, pull to safe corner (no wanderers), use snare to pull, add Ro's smoldering disjunction (best debuff in game, iirc), root, add epic dot, winged death, breath of ro (BoR is not resisted more oftem , rather it just fizzles more often then most spells, but that's always true). They don't cast, they're snared so they shouldn't catch you.

If you have a horse and have the nerve to use it (can make it a bit awkward when root breaks), you can more or less chain root. I've often managed to kill Elysians without them moving AT ALL after the initial root.

XP on them sucks, tbh, but you're there for the armor, which is top banana. I have all except the tunic atm. I left the gloves and wristband, as I had better, but boots, legs, sleeves, crown and tunic are well worth having.

In fact, rather than being a dangerous camp, the main reason I have to talk myself into going there is it's far to easy and rather boring.
