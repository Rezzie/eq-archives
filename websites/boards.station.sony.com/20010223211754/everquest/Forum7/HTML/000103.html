Let the change begin here on our server.
First - I enjoy EQ a lot (so much so that I have started 8 char just to find out who I like the most.)
Second - do not name your character something you cannot picture your game parents naming you. I have seen 400+ posts on a name that was taken away from Managirl Beachbum. I think it is safe to say that this char's game parents did not have the last name Beachbum or that they named their child Managirl.
Third - I dislike the people who talk like a d00d. I dislike the people who abuse the shout/ooc/auction chat. Let me state that the rules governing this system need to be widely understood. First - if you are buying/selling/trading you need to use auction. Second - if you have a question/statement in character you use shout, if you are giving something away you should also use shout because likely auction is turned off for a lot of us with no money. Third - if you need to say something to the whole zone which cannot be said in character then you use ooc (don't ooc your RL problems/questions or you will be considered a really big PIA.) Fourth - keep it in tells (do not shout to the whole zone your conversation.) Fifth - realize that even when you are using the chat system in a proper way you are most likely not endearing yourself to your fellow players. The exception to this is the auction channel which is widely spammed and is widely turned off by those who think that battle spam is quite enough for them (or those who are actually playing in EC and not shopping.) The only reason my ignore list is not huge is that I think some people do not realize that there are rules to the chat system and that following them benefits everyone trying to play. When you get ignored your name stays there even after the player has likely forgotten what a tool you are and is very unlikely to group with you after the incident that put you on ignore.
Fourth - if you are the type of person that likes to group please log in and type /lfg on. If everyone understands they can look in /who and see who is available to group then there will be a lot less traffic on shout. Like I said, keep it in tells. In case you are reading this and did not know that the star next to someone's name meant they are looking for a group, well, now you know. Also, if you target them and type /whotarget you will save yourself and him some time by asking what class/level they are.
Why do I feel that chat is such a problem? It is called suspension of disbelief. If you are watching a play about the middle ages and there are 2 knights about to fight it out over the princess and suddenly someone off stage yells, "That girl is a hottie! She needs to come over to my crib!", this blows your sense of disbelief in a big way. If you are playing a paladin and concentrating on serving your god and some fool starts shouting about his new motorcycle this pretty much does the same thing.
In any case I hope that this helps the few people who come to this board. /smile

Good luck
Cyrcyl - Magician of T.T.
