No, there isn't any duping. This item is just incredibly easy and fast to get for a mid to high 40's group.

That night I traded it away (didn't get a whole lot of value for it, I had to trade too many items to quantify tho) so the very next day, I got another group together and we got 7 more in a period of 9 hours, 3 of which were spent looking for help in getting the tektite scale.

Now, realize something. In both days, we actually got the dragon scales off the wurms that needed killing in about 3 or 4 hours, for 7 people each time. The getting of the third scale (which 10,000 people could do at the same time if they wanted) and waiting for the Squire to spawn (which another 10,000 people could find at the same time too, if they wanted) was by far the most time consuming part of this quest.

So, it is only the spawn rates of the two named wurms determining how often these come into the game. And as far as I can tell, the rate at which these can and do come into the game is about one every half hour, maybe even more. That's about 50 a day. In 40 days, you'll have 2000 on each server at that rate.

For a 25/40 one hand slashing weapon with an AC of 5 no less, I agree that this has the serious potential of replacing damn near every other weapon in the game. If it weren't primary hand only, I don't think anyone else would use anything else right now.

Damn near every paladin and shadow knight, and quite a few rangers and warriors I've hunted with in Dreadlands in the last week has been using one (I'm really not just saying that) on Tunare Server. Even as low as 35's - probably lower elsewhere, but I haven't left DL and BW much lately. And it didn't seem that many people even knew how to get this weapon around 4 weeks ago.

The only hard part of the quest (in terms of group power) is to kill Gullerback, a level 50ish gorilla that is the PH for Azaziel (sp?), one of the two necessary named wurms. A friend of mine went for hers yesterday. Her low to mid 40's group killed Gullerback once. Then Azaziel spawned 4 times in a row over the next 40 minutes. Yup, 4 people got the more difficult of the dragon scales, one every 10 minutes.

When I quested that, I saw the scale-dropping wurms spawn several times in a row as well, with a respawn of around 4 to 8 minutes usually.

I don't think I've ever asked for a nerf before (tho I've recognized some of them as reasonable and necessary after the fact) - but in this case, I'm seeing the inevitable coming - where every single melee will be using this weapon, cause the risk/reward for getting it is just totally out of whack compared to anything of equal power. It may be a pretty cool looking weapon, but seeing EVERYONE with one is making for a pretty boring world.

By the way - don't nerf the weapon itself, just the spawn rates - they're unbelievably fast. They may be the fastest respawn of any mob I know of in game (I really did see a repop within 4 minutes several times - I can't think of any other 4 minute spawn - everywhere else is at least 6 I think)
