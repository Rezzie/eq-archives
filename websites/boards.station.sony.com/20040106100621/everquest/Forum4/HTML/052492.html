F9 switches camera modes. I believe that hitting F9 once (assuming you are starting in first person mode) puts you in 3rd person mode with the camera locked behind your character (just like using the wheel from 1st person mode).

I just discovered this a couple of days ago - didn't realize that you can actually assign the camera angles to a keystroke. Use the Options/Keyboard/Camera menu. I have first-person view on Page Down, and a third-person view on Page Up. I use the keys between the number pad and the keyboard, since I move with the number pad and mouse with my left. Makes it pretty convenient.

All times are PT (US)
