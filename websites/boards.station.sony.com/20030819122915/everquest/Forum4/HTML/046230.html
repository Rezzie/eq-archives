Don't bother investing in the research yourself until you are older. Just gather up the words you need and ask another necro with 200 research to give you a hand. I have never refused to do combines for a fellow bonecaster.

Once you hit the 50s gather up around 3500pp and run your skill up to 200 with a trainer. From there you can start making your own high level spells and helping out the little ones who lack the skill.

The reality is there are very few essential 50+ spells that you will need to make yourself. The words are pretty rare until you can farm them yourself. You will find that buying scrolls in the bazaar is a little more time efficient, just keep looking for good prices. Once you get 60+ you will start getting Words in abundance and you can then start making 50-60 scrolls to sell.

The skill level cap for research is (lvl-15)*5, so at level 30 you should be able to train points above your current level of 54. What type of message do you get from the trainer when you try to train? Are you sure you have enough money on you at the time you try to spend practice points? At higher levels it requires money along with a practice point, and if you aren't carrying enough money it won't let you train.

Also, as someone above pointed out, research skill actually raises quite quickly for a tradeskill, and you could begin collecting words and/or practice rune components and doing combines. Here is a good site to go to, which lists necromancer combines along with trivials (which are mostly accurate; I found the lvl 34 spells to trivial at 123 though).

For my necromancer, I worked up my skill to 182 using only word combines. I started this at lvl 20 and worked at it as he was leveling. Good luck to you if you decide to follow a similar path.
