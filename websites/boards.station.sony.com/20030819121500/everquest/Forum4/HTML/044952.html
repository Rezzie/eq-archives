Buy.

At lower levels (like, pre 40), you can get much better equipment by saving up the money and looking for a good deal at the Bazaar (the only way to shop) than you can by killing or questing.

Some quests are kind of fun, some are kind of meaningful, some are reasonably convenient, but in general, they're not worth it at low levels. (Note that this is due largely to the wacked EQ economy, but that's another story.)

For saving getting money at low levels, I highly recommend stocking up on trade skill supplies (like silks and pelts), and selling them at the Bazaar.

Best of luck,
-Mori

If you buy gear, you can almost always get a better deal in the bazaar.

The quests, at least for lower level stuff, are good if you are new to the game, they teach a lot of the basics of the game, or if you are broke. Once you learn a tradeskill or have enough loot to sell in the bazaar to make plat, you can generally buy better gear than a lot of the quests offer
