Here's what I did on the fairy and bone quests.

In lfay, do a /shout and offer 30pp for each fairy dust. Having 3 or 4 lower tanks from Sisters come over and help you drastically shortens the wait for the dust, and you don't lose so much fairie faction. 30 minutes of this and you will have enough dust for one soil.

For the bones, I was paying 10pp per stack. I went to gfay, BB, ans Steam, did a shout, and let the bones flow in. I think I ended up having to give on the order of 200 bone chips to get faction high enough.

I'm assuming you are a decent level paladin or ranger doing the ronium quest. By decent I mean 30+. At your level, you ought to have some money in the bank, money enought to pay a fair price for these items.

It never ceases to amaze me how people will give out large sums of money to some schmuck who begs for it, but then suddenly turns ultra stingey when it comes to paying a decent wage. You want these crap quest items fast, put a crowbar in the wallot and pay some newbies. It's a win/win.

Having dome most of my Ro quests now, I have the following advice...

1) All Fairies drop the dust, but not every time. Note -- Pixies are not fairies. I went to the Fairy town in Lesser Fay and killed lots and lots of fairy maidens who drop the dust and are boringly easy. It gets interesting when a fairy guard or Gearheart tries to defend the maidens, but its a pretty boring camp. If you are cash rich, by all means, spend the big bucks to get fairy dust, but if you are like me, the fairy town is the place to get the dust.

2) I found that I needed Kindly faction to get the Soil of Underfoot. I must have turned in a good 300 bonechips trying to get there. I also did the quest where you go to South Karana for the dwarven bone chip, but since its lore, it makes for a rather long and boring quest to get some faction. I'd stick to buying bonechips at 3pp per stack.

3) See #2 above

And don't forget that Temple of Sol Ro faction! Silly me first tried killing shadowmen, but found I had to have a group to do so, and shadowmen groups can sometimes be hard to find. But there is an easier way. Look for a quest in South Ro... hint, take about 50 plat worth in gold pieces to turn in a few at a time. Got my faction there up really fast.

Ok, this is from my personal experience forging the Breastplate, Greaves, and Boots of Ro.

Fairy Dust - the quickest way to get it is kill the maidens. They are the easiest to kill and drop the dust about 1 out of 4 kills. You won't find many people selling the dust because most players don't want to hunt them. Their village is in Lesser Fay in the western part of the zone. You will need a small group if you are in the lower 30's; however, at level 39, you can easily solo the village since they don't aggro once they turn green.

Cleric Faction - Medium Amiable should do it. I am still amiable and can get the Soil every time I turn in the dust. Just keep turning in bone chips until the she will give you the fairy dust quest (hail her to see if she gives you the quest). Don't worry about killing skeletons yourself. Just buy chips off of other players.

Good luck.
