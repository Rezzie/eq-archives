Q:Whats the blue line in my exp bar do?

A: Exp in Everquest is measured in bubbles and bars. You have a Yellow Bar, segmented into 5 bubbles. Within the Yellow Bar, you have a blue line.

1 bar of yellow = one level.
1 bar of blue line = one bubble of yellow.
1 bubble of blue line = one fifth of a bubble of yellow.

Simply speaking, the blue line tells you how you are progressing through a single bubble of yellow exp.

I think I have explained it to people in-game a million times already. (some still dont get it)

Maybe I just explain it badly.. hehe as you can see above

-Henora
