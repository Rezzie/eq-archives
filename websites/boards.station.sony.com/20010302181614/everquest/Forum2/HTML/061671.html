First of all the problem I see is that Verant doesn't want their uber mobs killed. Whenever they are killed they watch the players do it (which of course are the pioneers in the game and also the best equipped and skilled) and think of ways to KEEP them from doing it! Why is it that player ingenuity and innovation is rewarded by counters to their strategy? Have you guys ever played a AD&D game where the GM would make up new rules or do something to make it harder for the players just because the players were doing too well? That is exactly what is going on here.

With some of the changes you have made to the game it seems apparent that you DON'T WANT YOUR UBER MOBS KILLED. How in the hell are we supposed to kill a Dragon that quads for 650, has 500k+ hps, breathes an AE manaseive without donals BPs?? You guys said yourselves that some mobs were TUNED FOR DONALS BP yet when you nerf them because they were too powerful for EXP groups (no argument there) you do not retune the monsters that were designed around the donals BP? WHY?

The three staple classes for Velious Uber mobs are Warriors, Rogues, and Clerics. ALL THREE were hit hard by this patch. Warriors, the class that MUST absorb all the hits when your fighting mobs that can do over 2000 per round, have had their taunting ability impaired because of the change in the defensive discipline. Rogues evade isn't as effective as it used to be and their damage output must be cutback or they will suck up too much mana for heals. Clerics casting CH on Warriors whom can't taunt now is like asking for a Death Touch. So here you have the Warrior that can't do his job tanking effectively, a Rogue that can't do his job doing damage effectively, and the Cleric that can't do his job healing without getting killed.

Quit designing this game for the Elite players! When you design the game around the absolute best equipped and skilled players in the game and even THEY can't kill your mobs where does that leave the rest of us gamers?

What about Sleeper's Tomb? I heard you upgraded the Warders so that they were as Sturdy as Velious mobs but with the AEs of VP dragons. WOW none of them has been killed since your upgrade to them, don't you think you went overboard? Will you only be satisfied when no one can kill your mobs?

Next the Dain. It is aparrent that you think the idea of killing the Dain in the well is an exploit. You WANT people to fight him in his room where all his royal guardsman pop every 6-8 mins. You WANT people to either bring 80-100 people or have a Mage in EACH group to summon people back up. Players improvised by fighting the Dain where he ports people and your answer to that is to make the Dain poof if he's pulled down there? Thanks for rewarding our innovation.
