Couple o' things:

1) Evade does NOT currently make you "virtually" immune to high level mobs. Hardly. I have been killed by critters that I have successfully evaded. They chased me, as they were not rooted I was not at low health when they went nuts on me. It just happens sometimes. And in a multi-mob situation, when crowd control just isn't up to snuff... rogues EASILY get slaughtered. Evade was the skill that saved the rogue class, but it's not the magical uber-skill some people think it to be.

2) Rogues aren't tanks, IMHO. As much as I'd love to see warrior-quality armor, resists, and hitpoints, it's just not appropriate. Rogues are, however, tank-dependent. Make sure we can continue to work WELL with other tanks, and our skills complement theirs.

3) I think, post-50, rogues are quite well balanced. Sure, when you look at a fully-hasted rogue with their epic at 60th level during their 10-seconds-of-fame that their discipline gives them, they could look pretty awesome. But rogues have a LOT of weaknesses to balance that out, which people also tend to ignore when they see a couple of really big backstabs in a row. All the usual melee difficulties, plus the rogue-specific ones. Like having pretty much NADA for escape skills. And having such low AC and Hitpoints that we can often go from full health to zero before a cleric can get a CH off. And there's the HUGE fact that rogues are the only melee class (well, maybe bards go here too) that NEED another melee class in order to function normally. Dependency on OTHER melees when you are melee is intriguing, but often frustrating when forming groups.

4) Pre-50 is another story. From around level 15 to about level 35 or 40 (I *think* - it's been a long time since my rogue was in that range), untwinked rogues are still a little under-balanced. Not nearly as bad as they used to be, but they still find themselves outdamaged, outclassed, and sometimes out-rogued by other classes. Face it, in areas with very little mix of living vs. undead, there are MANY other classes with better stealth ability than the pokey little rogue. And the fact that people are still afraid of rogues stealing all the loot with pickpocket still hurts the class in a pickup situation.
