Ok I am here to clear up a few things for the Voodoo5 5000/5500.

There are some known Issues...the Computer reboots, It looks worse that before...etc. I have some answers for you.

If the computer Reboots on you while or before game play that means you need a bigger power supply.

If the Game play is Choppy and looks worse than before, then the problem is your Computer not the card, you will need to turn off one of the 2 CPU's on the video card in order to run properly (systems under 500mhz). It can be adjusted in the 3Dfx Tools. Below also is the FAQ Page for the Voodoo 5 as well...

http://www.3dfx.com/prod/sup/faq-v45.html
