Replace both rings with Velim Firew Wediing Rings. Replace both Earrings with Something with HP on it (Orc Fang for 5pp if you don't find better). Get Crystal Spider Eyes as face piece.

At your level, you will mostly be tank for a group. There are only few stats that will make you a better tank, HP, AC, mana regen, HP regen, in that order. Maybe dex, if you have a procing weapon.

Int will increase your mana pool, but a large mana pool does not really help you. It gets you longer periods between med breaks, if you need any, but the med breaks will be longer, so the real benefit is slim.
