XraX:

The Lost Order of Akalabeth has a code of conduct that prohibits guard killing in newbie areas. The idea is that killing guards in newbie zones (such as the gnomes in Steamfont or the Ogre guards) negatively impacts the weakest players, and if consistenty done, is an offense that can get a player removed from the guild.

We recognize that there are certain times killing guards cannot be avoided, either to complete a quest or because you are KOS in a zone and are attacked. These instances are understandable and don't affect your standing with the guild. However, killing guards in low level zones simply for experience is considered bad form and hurts others, so is not tolerated. We know others do it, and that's up to them, but it's not something we practice or allow of our members.

For more information on the Lost Order of Akalabeth (LOA), visit our website at www.lostorder.com
