Paly's and SK have legitimant beafs but at least they don't have to say that their defining abilities are shared with other classes.

Rangers have nothing to make them unique or desirable in a group. anything we can do someone else can do better. Including tracking. Any group would prefer to have a pure class over us. We can't heal enough to make even a decent 3rd string healer. We can't nuke enough to justify stopping swinging our weapons.
