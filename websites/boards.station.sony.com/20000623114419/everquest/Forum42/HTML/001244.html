After alot of debateing from both sides on coin only loot, i found myself leaning towards the coin only situation. Why you might ask? Well its not whining and its not because i really give a damn about looseing and item. Its because after coming from Ultima online to EQ, and then joining the pvp server in beta and since, i just wanted a place to where i could test my skills against other players. I was just thinking that maybe coin loot only might actually increase pvp in EQ.
But then i started thinking on that subject. And i have came to a more sad no conclusion. I just feel pvp combat is doomed in EQ, because of the levels. Let me explain. When i played in the beta as a dark elf, there used to be huge fights against humans and darkelfs. And up to about level 20 after the beta you could still see the same occurances. Huge wars would take place that would get the adrenaline pumping. But something happened shortly after that, and its called leveling. IF anyone came from uo like me, then you realize what im saying. You got your character up to Master to gm status, and then well you engaged in pvp combat. I bet my whole last year that i played Ultima online i can count on 1 hand how many monsters i killed. And those where just to get out of the way before a duel or something. But most of the diehard true pvpers wanted nothing more than to fight one another.
Now in EQ on the other hand you are forcefed haveing to slay 4 zillion monsters in order to get to that prize level 50 in EQ. And then when you think, hey this is it 50 finally i can chill out and engage in pvp combat. no one has a level advantage over me and i have all the nice spells. But what happens now,"Expansion Pack". Now we have level 60, Weeeee!!!! more monster bashing goodness... I myself welcome the newlands, but not the 10(Hell levels), they call it in other to keep up with the yellow guy who can mop the floor with me.. True pvpers dont want this. Now dont get me wrong i like smacking and A.I. monsters brain in as good as the next occasionally. But i just feel the pvp lands would have benifited more with the level limit at 50, for that we can do the one thing we want to do. PVP!!!!
Btw, on my earlier scenerio about all the pvp at the beginning. It all stopped once people had to level. The reason alot of people in pvp level is because we dont want to be nuked by reds. We want more of and even fight. Anyway i just think that there would be alot more pvp, regardless of the coin loot situation if it wasnt for the leveling,. argghh
