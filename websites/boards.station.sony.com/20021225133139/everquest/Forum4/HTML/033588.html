"Only a Bow...poo...Unfair"

Rangers are not Warriors - they are Rangers. Everquest is built around the concept of classes being different (but balanced), so Rangers do not have the same abilities as Warriors.

Warriors get Critical Hits with all weapons.
Rangers get Critical Hits with Bows.
Rogues get Critical Hits with Thrown weapons.
Paladins have a high level Discipline that gives them the ability to score Critical Hits against Undead.
No other classes get Critical Hits as class abilities.

However, all melee and hybrid classes can get the Alternate Advancement ability Combat Fury, which gives them the ability to get Critical Hits with all weapons. Note that Warriors can also get this ability, and it increases their probability of scoring a Critical Hit.

See http://eq.crgaming.com/archetype/Details.asp?Id=28 for information on Combat Fury.
