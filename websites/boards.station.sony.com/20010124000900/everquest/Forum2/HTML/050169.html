If players had more abilities to escape a bad situation the game would not lose any challenge yet taking risks would be more encourageable.

If you give groups without clerics more abilities to escape and recover from a risky encounter without dieing it can help make up for those two cleric strangleholds without giving any clerical abilities away.

Compare to druids: Their HP/AC combination buffs are inferior or comparable to cleric buffs but cost significantly more mana (= downtime). Nukes and DoTs are resistable and highly inefficient in a group that is built around melee damage, as most high level groups are.
