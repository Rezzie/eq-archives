Chainmail and AD&D are separate as far as rules and everything else but inspiration. Chainmail inspired Gygax to write AD&D.

An old DM of mine was one of the original players (I believe he said playtester of the commercial version) and according to him, Gygax gave out everything under the sun...including the sword Bane (4000 automatic damage) and the like.
