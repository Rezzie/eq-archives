Tiny Daggers should be sold in Felwinthe as already stated. Look for an icon that is a *VERY* small dagger. It's just white at the one end (the blade) and then a little brownish on the very small handle. They are tiny and easily missed.

A great trick is when you are looking at a vendor hold down the ALT key so it pops up text beside what he is selling. Then you can see if he has the right thing or not that you are looking for.
