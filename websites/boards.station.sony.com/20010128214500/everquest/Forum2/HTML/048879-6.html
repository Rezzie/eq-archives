This server that we all want should be good versus evil.. if you have dark Vs, the dark team has four classes closed, including the vital bards, and the good team has all classes... and as to the levelling areas thing, Misty thicket is the best one, followed by innthoule, so its a fair split =)

Anyway... the teams would be... Evil Gods are bertoxx, cazic, innoruuk, rallos zek, solusek ro and veeshan.
Rallos and solosek, while being nuetralish in outlook, are bad enough to be evil, and are needed for balance...
Agnostic characters are put into their most suitable category. Agnostic ogres, trolls and dark elves are on the evil team, as are agnostic necros and shadowknights if they exist, cant remember =) Agnostic human rogues can choose which team, and so forth...

This works out so that the dark team has all classes but paladin, ranger and druid, and the light team is without only necroes and shodowknights...

the programming work is a little *although not too much so* heavier, but it would give a truer, balanced experience =)

Evil gnomes can start in their home town *suicide probably* =) or Neriak. Rallos zek following dwarves may start in neriak also, as neriak is the lynchpin of the evil team. Evil humans get freeport, neriak or paineel. Evil Erudites obviously get paineel =)

Faction: All evil racdes have neurtal faction with neriak, paineel, overthere, cabilis, oggok and grobb, unless they have higher to begin with and get that.. good teams have likewise with the good outposts.

(optional extra- I would suggest, to be implemented later, another two factions, The Dark Alliance, and the Bright Hope, or something like that, an evil and good faction, which encompasses the cities... would make things easier and has a good use.)
Killing a member of the other team increases your faction with the Dark Alliance *see why it would be waaay better to have one facion....* and decreases it with Bright hope, if you are evil, and visa versa for good teams. The higher the opposite faction is, the more you gain and lose, to encourage killing powerful opponents, and prevent farming of vulnerable newbies or made accounts.
Killing NPCs of magnitude *such as Sir Lucan, Growth mobs, guards* also increases and decreases the factions appropriately...

on NPCS... many will be on relevant factions and killing them is good for the team, but there are needed changes.. newbie guards are unbuffed.. that includes ones in West commons etc. Newbie Guards when killed stay dead for three times as long as normal, to make raids easier.. internal non newbie guards five times, mercahnts and quest mobs 10 times, and guild staff 20 times =) If you whack a city, it should stay whacked a while.

This should give a true PvP team combat situation, with balance between the teams, and hopefully goals.

What is the point of this faction, you may ask? seems like a waste of some poor souls programming time? well no =) First it handles cross teaming. Now, no one can buff or heal anyone on the other team, the mechanism is in place, red people on a blue server =) but anyone who gets both factiuons below KoS becomes red flagged and can be killed by either team... and can alos aid either team.. risky but may be fun for those with guts. Once you get a faction back to at least decent with one side, you return to that sides faction, but you will most likely be marked as a person to be wary of trusting. This also allows team changing, with some diffiulty obviously.
The second part would be that there would be a running total of faction kept for each player, as a stat, like XP, for the shifting change, and a summation to see the overall war status. So if the dark team has lots more faction with dark alliance that light does with bright hope, then the darks are winning, and the GMs can dispense 'minor' occasional gifts to the better factioned souls *and some random to members of the winning team, to encourage the newbies and stuff' such as a combine sword maybe, or a title. This requires no extra work, just pick one or two people from the winning team each day and it takes 5 minutes =)
So this would stimulate teamplay, as completed quests, city slayings and PKings would earn points and possible rewards for you and the whole team. Likewise dont help and the other team will get buffed and you may be Pked by someone with a shiny new toy =)

This would definitely promote proper PvP team action =)

now some minor details.. attack level would be 6 levels up to 20, then 8 levels to 40, 10 levels to 60. with a skew at 20, i.e 20 is 14 to 28, 40 is 34 to 50, so that there is no downward bias =)
There would also be white conning for all levels in the range to prevent weaker hunting =)
Loot would be one item, all coin... ANY item slot, including bag contents and even bags themselves =) if possible looting a spell as a scroll would be good, but about half of researchable spells and all questable ones would be NODROP spells.
Even if this is not possible, bag loot should help.
Fear and charm would be institted back to enchanters etc, why hamstring them? if the other team dies, they die. There is no PnP in place for the other team regarding killstealing etc, just for bad language and crudity. This should make it bloody and grudgeful...
Epic quest NPCS should also be on a neutral facrtion, so that all players can get their epic. The conflicts between teams for those would be fine to see...
oh yes, and movelog no.. keep it pure =)
i think thats it =) read and assess =)
