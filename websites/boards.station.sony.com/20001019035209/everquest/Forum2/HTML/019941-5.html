It's been a few days now since the tuning meeting. Still no word from Abashi that I've seen. For anyone who hasn't heard my opinion yet, I agree with the majority that this weapon is far from "uber" in its current state.

As far as changes go, I can't see it being equipped along with an instrument since that would probably require a code change to all instruments and would be even more difficult to prevent other weapons from being equipped also. I also can't see it remaining solely as a weapon, as most high level bards only equip weapons maybe 20-30% of the time at most. There are only 2 viable solutions I can see for it: 1) To produce a bonus to vocal songs, or 2) To count as an all-in-one instrument.

My logic is that every other class will keep their quest weapon equipped at all times, as it will always provide a better bonus than anything else they might wield. A bard on the other hand will only wield a weapon a small portion of the time, favoring instruments to magnify his song effects. We all know we're not wanted for our "superior" melee skills. The song maketh the bard, and the weaker the song the weaker the bard. Even as a clickable effect, it would still only be used for that effect before being sheathed again for instrument use. Might as well call it the "J-boots for bards" in this case.

Life at the orphanage must surely be better than being Verant's red-headed stepchild. If neither of these solutions becomes a reality, the Singing Short Sword will never be more than a novelty for show.
