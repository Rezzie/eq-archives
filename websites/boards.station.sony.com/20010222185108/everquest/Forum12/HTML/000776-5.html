Alan,
This is the post you want to read

I'm sorry if I tend to disagree with you on this. The magician class needs as much if not more than any of the hybrid calsses that are getting a lot of attention. You think the majority of the issues are trivial. Well let me list the issues that are not trivial, and are indeed broken

I will write this keeping in mind the issues you are and are not aware of, and have prior knowledge of. I will also try not repeat the issues already addresses (lev 60 aegis of ro, lev 54 pet spell drops, etc)

I will refrain from naming Mage Epic a “class balance” issue, consistent with your other posts

EXTREMELY IMPORTANT ISSUES

1. Lev 52 and 58 Fire pets

Problem :
I want you to understand the issue. These pets are supposed to be wiz casters. Currently they use wiz lev 16/20 spells. Which are very mediocre nukes, which get resistsed (since the pet levels are much lower than PC or mob levels during fights) and they dont even chain cast these mediocre nukes, they move in to melee. And they melee for only 28 dmg when comparable pets (earth, air, water) hit for 56-60. All in all it's damage output is pitiful, PLUS it has the least hitpoint.

Solution:
Now that you understand the problem, I propose that you either raise the lev of spells cast by the pet to the wiz 24/29 spells and stop making it melee but just chain cast from a distance (it's a wiz after all), or remove the whole wiz concept, and give us the old fire pet back which casts a huge DS on itself and melees for as much damage as others despite it's low hp.

2. lev 44 and 55 DoTs

Problem :
We dont hate the fact we got a DoT like you said. It's fine. It adds a little diversity. But it's useless right now. e.g. the 55 DoT compared to 57 DD. the DoT takes longer to cast, takes 30 sec to complete it's effect, has stacking issues, has a worse ratio that the instant hit DD, gets resisted more, has an all-or-nothing type effect, etc etc.

Solution:
I think all classes with DoTs have to deal with stacking issues, the fact that the monster may move and reduce ratio and all-or-nothing type effect. That's the nature of DoTs. But other classes get excellent damage ratios. e.g. even the Enchanter Torment of Argil lev 56 DoT does 560 dmg for 150 mana with 2.5 sec cast time and debuffs int and wis and drains mana!!!! The damage ratio alone is superior to our lev 55 DoT does 800 dmg for 265 man for 7 sec cast time!! how pitiful. Why would I use this over my lev 57 DD, which does 825 dmg for 275 mana with 6 sec cast time and hits instantly!! Lets not even compare to the far superior dru/nec/shaman dots. We're not even better than Enc DoTs !! Please make this DoT do nearer to 1100 dmg or move mana cost down to 200 ish AND make cast time 3 sec or something to make it useful. It's worthless right now.

3. Mage DD mana ratios and resists

Problem:
The mage DD and Bolts in the post 50 period received extremely pitiful upgrades. This was done to remove the magician from being second behind the Wizard in nuking power to third behind wizard and druids. Druids received far superior upgrades to their DDs. Did you ever wonder why no druid ever complains about their DD ratios. Plus Necro (lifetap) and Wiz ( Lures ) get far better opportunity to inflict damage in Epic encounter while all mage nukes are resisted. Don’t tell me that the mages do damage via their pets, so do the necros.

Solution:
Please review the mage dmg/mana ratios for 50+ spells. Esp our bolts, which have an inherent flaw which makes them difficult to use (line of sight, z-axis variation) on top of which they don’t even have better ratio that the Druid DDs. I think the ideal solution would be to give the mages a crappy version of a Lure type spell at lev 59/60. Something with a poor mana ratio like 600 dmg for 300 mana or something, compare that with the Wizards Lure of Ice which is 825 dmg for 320 mana. There must be SOME appropriately nerfed version of a wizard lure spell that would be balanced in not pissing of the wizards, giving mage Epic encounter options, and basically adding to our arsenal in a balanced manner. Hence we might have poorer ratios than Druid DDs (and they get better heals now too) but we have added variety in our nukes. Believe me, some crappy version of a lure would make all parties happy. Just have to find the right ratio.

4. Anti – Summoned DD

Problem:
Magicians receive a line of DD spells which we can use against summoned creatures only. Currently there are extremely few summoned-only mobs out there, as opposed to the hordes and hordes of undead creatures out there. It’s hard enough to find a mob to use it on, but then it has a dmg/mana ratio which is WORSE than our regular DDs which we can use on all mobs. Compare lev 56 Exile Summoned DD ( summoned creatures only ) 725 dmg 250 mana to lev 57 Shock of Swords (can use on all mobs) 825 dmg, 275 mana. Why would I ever use the summoned-only DD when my regular DD is better !!

Soultion:
Please raise the dmg/mana ratios of the summoned-only DDs. Raise the lev 56 Exile Summoned’s dmg to like 900 for 250 mana to make it more balanced. Also add the “summoned” tag to more creatures if possible.

5. Rain Spells

Problem:
You know all about it. The resist rates are ridiculous (try casting it) and it hits our pet.

Solution:
Remove pet as being counted. This does not make them unbalanced. Keep in mind that we cant use these spells 90% of the time since it’s an AoE and will break mezzes. It’s very situational. Plus resist rates are incredibly high, please look into them, they need to be somewhat lowered.

IMPORTANT ISSUES

6. Lev 56 Dyzil’s Deafening Decoy

Problem:
You know it. The pet is useless because the taunt is non-existant. There is no reason to use it.

Solution:
I like your suggestions. Make the AoE Taunt REALLY grab everyone’s attention. Reduce casting time.

7. Lev 55 Rage of Zomm

Problem:
I couldn’t care less what the pet looks like. I wouldn’t waste your time with such trivial issues. Right now the pet has 100 HP. Yes only 100 HP. You need to select a mob to cast the pet, since it will only attack that mob, and suicide after the mob is dead. Obviously the pet was never supposed to tank, but right now it will sometimes kill itself on the mob’s Damage Shield or will die on 1 riposte. Plus this pet has a 8 sec cast time, which you cast *during* battle, not before like the other pets. This cast time is a major hindrance. The pet is going to suicide after the mob dies anyway, why not give it a 2 sec cast time. Plus why does it have a 2 min re-cast. Is a 100 HP pet really that over-powering ??

Solution:
Give the pet atleast 300 HP so it wont die on a single riposte. It still cant tank, 300 HP will get wiped out in one round of a mob’s damage. Plus give it a 2 sec cast time, and rather remove or significantly lower the unnecessary re-cast time. The pet has extremely limited uses as of now.

8. Pet mana costs

Problem:
Mages used to be the masters of summoning cheap pets. All our pets were 200 mana from lev 20-50. This was out trademark as the other pet casting classes had increased costs. Post 50 our pets costs were increased to 400 mana while our pets were given minimal improvements. e.g. Take the max dmg by the best possible Air pet summoned at various levels. Lev 29 Air pet (28 max dmg), 34 air pet (34 dmg), 39 air pet (40 dmg), 44 air pet (48 dmg), 49 air pet (56 dmg), 53 air pet (56 dmg – Yes , same !!), 59 air pet (58 dmg). The pre-50 pets were 200 mana and gained new abilities every few rounds (dodge, kick, bash, parry, dual weild, regen etc) , post 50 went to 300 and 400 mana and gained no new abilities.

Solution:
Either reduce costs on post 50 pets to 200 mana again., or give them better max dmg (I doubt this is acceptable) or , more appropriately, give them more skills. Give the Voceration pets lev 51-54 the riposte skill as well, and increase bash/kick skill caps. And give the Greater Voceration pets 57-60 either triple attack, or critical hits, or even higher skill caps on dodge, parry, kick, bash etc. Hence you would only minimally increase their power, but would justify the increased mana costs and the negligible increase in damage output

9. Pet Utility spells

Problem:
Brainstorming better ways to make pets useful, and to really hone the idea that mages derive their power through their pets.

Solution:
The valiant companion spell is an excellent addition. Making pets immune to fear is a wonderful idea and will increase our usefulness on Epic encounters. Would a pet-immune-charm spell also be considered. On the same lines. Also I like the idea of summoning my own pet back if not agroed, thanks. The new Expedience spell introduced on Test at lev 29 makes our lev 58 Velocity obsolete. How about a new spell at lev 58 which essentially acts like a war/rog/mon discipline. Giving us the ability to increase our pets damage potential (most of the damage done is melee dmg except for the broken fire pets) with some spell which last e.g. 3 min depending on the strength of the spell and has a 15 min re-cast time. This obviously has a downside since it will grey-out one of our slots for a long period to just use the spell, and then pets will after all be pets. They cant be used in all situations. Possible spells can be doubling all dmg, giving crit/crips, riposte attacks (useful for decoy pet when fixed) etc etc. Something useful at lev 58+ mages for special situations

10. Pet Heals

Problem:
We get pet heal spells at lev 8 and 20 and then the line disappears. We can make a new pet for 200 mana. Why not give us better heals for our pets ? Why was this line abandoned ??

Solution:
Let us CH our pets for the same cost of a new pet, you can even take the component. This has the upside that if we spent hours and hours summoning and re-summoning till we get our best pet out we don’t lose it. Also we don’t need to buff it all over again.

RELATIVELY MINOR ISSUES

11. Robe of Conjuration

Problem, Solution:
As you know the robe has our lev 49 DS. Plus it has a huge casting time. The “mana-free” spell costs only 120 mana. Even the 56 DS costs only 155 mana. The necro robe has a 700 mana costing spell and the casting time doesn’t matter since the spell is cast during CR and hence downtime is irrelevant. It speeds CR since the necro doesn’t need to med. I think the best equivalent would be the lev 55 Call of the Hero. It will save us from the 500 mana cost and the casting time wouldn’t matter when the spell is cast outside of fights. But as of now the lev 49 DS effect is messed up since I can med up the 120 mana in the 30 sec cast time. So the whole point of mana free is irrelevant.

You know what to do =) Fix it

We get summoned throwing shuriken that only Monks and Rogues can use, not even us. Ok. !! So monks and rogues will come to mages and ask for it right ? no !! But only 2 classes can use this, and monks get a armor to make it now !!

Make our version of shurikens better, so monks still come to us, or give monks our lev 20 throwing daggers spell, or less reluctantly, our lev 53 Bandoleer of Luclin, a lower version of our lev 55 Pouch of Quellious.

14. Improved Invisibity

Mages earned the right for this as much as any other caster.

That is all I can think about for now. There are probably other valid issues but I cant remember. I am not going to waste your time by asking for defensive spells (root, snare, fear), mana regen spells or other such outlandish suggestions. Most of these issues listed above are suggestions on corrections on abilities we already have. The only new spell ideas I think would be balancing are some pet buff at lev 58 + (suggested above) improved invis, maybe an upgrade to our lev 52 fire based DD (not mentioned above) , and maybe letting us manipulate (charm, fear, root, mez) summoned only creatures. There are few of them out there to make this work if balanced.
