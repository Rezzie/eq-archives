No, this problem is related to getting constrained bandwidth (slow packets). Its very similer to packet loss, except since you never lose any, you never see breaks in the bar. You can dimonstrate this problem on a network, by having someone use up your bandwidth while playing.

do a tracert and see if you have any hops that are constantly over 300MS on average, this is a sign of a congested hop, and is the most common source of these problems.

If you have a large number of hops (over 16), lookup the server IP addresses from one of the fan sites, and try one out of your "set" of servers. This will cause you to take a diffrent route to eq servers, and if it works, points to the other route as having a problem.

If you are on a modem, make sure you have a good connection. Try redialing your isp and check the connect rate. If the connect rate is exactly 56000, 33600, or 28800, or some number above 56000, then its lying to you and is not a accurate connection speed (its cosmetic, not an actual report)

If on DSL or cable, try late at night or early morning PST time. If the problem is not there, it points to lag server side, or closer to verant, than you.

hope this gives some clues. Also, you can always petition and ask if its a server wide problem. Guides would know by the numbers of petitions if it is or not.

All times are PT (US)
