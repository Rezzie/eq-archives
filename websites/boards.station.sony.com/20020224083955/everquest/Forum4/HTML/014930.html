My level 30 enchantress has a research skill of 67.

I am going to raise my skill to 95 with the practice runes.. the components for the Journeyman Runes aren't that hard to get

Jasper
Pixie Wing
Bat Fur
Star Rose Quartz
Burned out lightstone
Raw-Hide Armor
(any size, belt
arms, wrist, hands
legs, boots)

I plan on getting revenge on the drowned citizens for more raw hide armour, looted up more somewher else... Mistmoore I think.. or you can buy in oasis.
Bat fur and pixie wings were easy.. I rode the luclin bus trip down to Gfay and bought a stack of each of a merchant.. they are always in abundance there, don't even need to farm them.. although farming bats is easy as a chanter.. stand and tash pull everything you can see and let pet kill for you while you loot.
(Obviously don't kill steal or do where there are newbies around, since I play on Sydney time the busiest zones are usually quiet when I am on)

Jaspers and Star Roses pick up in Felwithe while you are getting the pixie wings. Also get the quill and parchment.

Then I gated back to the nexus.. love the fact you can bind there now.. and down to NK for burnt out lightstones, and to get the concordance from Myrsila.

Hope this helps!
