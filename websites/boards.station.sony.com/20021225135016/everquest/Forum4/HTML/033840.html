Even using the free "basic" EQ download, once you have an account you have an account and don't lose your characters.

As the others have said, the game card is only a payment option, for those who do not want to use a credit card.

As far as I know, there is no easy way to find out how much time you have left on a card, best solution is if he likes it, to have another one ready for the day he can't log in.

Worst thing that happens if your card passes its date is you can't log into the game. You can still log into the billing information and either give it a credit card option or enter in a new card code. When you get back in the game, all your characters and gear are still there, and it hardly takes any time at all to do the payment.

All times are PT (US)
