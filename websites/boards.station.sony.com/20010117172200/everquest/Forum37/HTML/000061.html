Hail and well met all..

I am Norlalos Iebaine, Leader of Global Menace on Vallon Zek.

I have a sad tale to tell about a member of our server. I've seen some pathetic tactics, and some sad individuals, but this has got to be the worst case.

Scene : Upper Guk, I'm camping the AC.. I am sitting there, just finished off a peck, as us Global Menace do. The shortie respawns, and we commence talking. ALong comes this inkie.

Rahvinn was his name. I immediatly jump up and attack. Bah, he's too high for me to hit. O'well. We exchange some bantar.. The usual, "You would not stand a chance." blah blah blah.. The last thing he said, is this, "You need some excitement, I'll make it more exciting." About that time, the shortie left. Which was the right thing to do, Loot and Scoot and all. But the inkie stayed.

Now, what would an inkie that gets no EXP, for the crocs, too high to hit me, be doing there waiting at that area? Resting..? maybe... Attempting to KillSteal? Probable.. But neither turned out to be the case..

After I had killed the 2 spawned crocs, he proceeds to pull a couple of froggies on me. He does this by way of pulling them to that area, then fiegns death.. Thus the froggies no longer aggro on him, but instead turn to me. End result, I died.

Now, I ask, what was the point of that? Just to cause Exp loss? Just to harrass someone? But why? because he did not like my guild? He did not like me? (OOC, He don't even know me..) Either way, it made no sence, other than his being an ass.

I petitioned, and made a report. But unfortunatly, he had logged off before the report went through. 8(

I made a tell to our new head GM on Vallon, and after about 30 mins of explaining to Kriggen (sp?) and another Apprentice GM what happened, Kriggen offered me an exp res. (Little off the subject, but I think it worth mentioning.) This is not the level of service I was use to, and was almost in shock! I mean, a GM actually taking time to look into something, and making an effort to fix a problem? If this is the way the Head GM acts, I hope he instills it into all the GMs, and maybe this server will become the type it needs to be. Thanks Kriggen, and you have your job cut out for you. Good luck.

Back to my tale. Rahvinn had logged off immediatly after he trained the things on me. How sad is this? Very. I've NEVER seen anything as blatent, and as intentional as that before. Hope I never do again.

Just be warned of this individual, and watch if he's about. He has no concern for the rules, or the problems he causes.

To everyone else, take care, and safe travels.
Norlalos Iebaine,
