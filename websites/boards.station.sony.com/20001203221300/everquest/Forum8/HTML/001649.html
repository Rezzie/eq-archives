The reason the elves have a caster-wearable chainmail (and humans don't) is the Tolkien Legacy. He set the precedent of magical elven chain that is light and flexible as cloth, made of the magical metal Mithril that doesn't interfere with the wearer's channeling of magical energies. Humans, on the other hand, don't have any such legacy in fantasy - to the contrary, iron (and therefore, steel) are supposed to be anathema to the use of magic (or at least make the use of magic extremely difficult). I believe there was also a stated desire by Verant (way back when, at EQ's toddler days) to not emulate the Ultima series, where casters in full platemail are all too commmon.
