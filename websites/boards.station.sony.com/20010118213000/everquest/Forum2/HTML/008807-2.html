>>>Is there a way to get back the items you accidentally combine? I lost my squad ring due to a lag-induced wrong button press. The squad ring is worth several hundred pp to me, and is a no-drop item. I tried to force LD but that didn't work, anyone have any solution?

No, there is no way to get items back after an accidental combine. As for "lag-induced wrong button press", sluggish mouse movement, which I imagine is what you are describing, is caused by framerate, not internet or server-side latency. People frequently call sluggish framerate "lag", but lag has always been applied to internet or server-side latency..

>>>What you were attempting to say (I think) is that the team doesn't want to make normal game mechanics that are inconsequential unneccesarily difficult for people.
