Last night was my first night selling in the Bazaar on the Tholuxe Paells server. I priced my pelts and skins as follows using other vendors Bazaar prices as a guide:

Ruined - 2p
Low Qual - 5p
Med Qual - 7p
High Qual - 10p

All of my High and Low Qual sold but none of my Ruined or Med Qual. I will lower my Ruined to 1 tonight.

I also sold Spider Silk for 5p and that sold out.

I made about 170p which is great since I only had about 55p to start with.

... and a note for others entering the Bazaar for the first time. The lag can be horrible. I had entered a few days ago and could not move without staring at the ground. I upgraded my video to an ATI All In Wonder 9600 yesterday and it made a world of difference. I also upped my memory to 768MB. Still had a little lag, but it wasn't too bad.
