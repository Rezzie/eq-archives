Greetings all,

I am only 44th at the moment, but I feel it necessary to post to support our class. I hope Absor realizes that although we may be few, we have legitimate concerns.

I'm not going to repeat the list of great ideas that have already been presented, but I do want to mention a few that I think need reaffirming.

Pet Control - Under most circumstances, I can keep my pet under control. But it would be a nice addition if there were commands to "force" the pet to do what we say. If I give my pet a target to attack, I want it to stay on that target until it's dead. No matter if another monster is pounding his backside. It will be my job to focus his attacks. This would help immensely when working in tight spaces and with enchanters. Along the same lines, it would be nice to have a "park it" command. The pet will not move even if I get hit. I appreciate him wanting to rescue me, but there are times he would better serve his master by staying put. If I need his assistance, I will command him to attack.

Mana Recovery - I'm still ok at 44, but with meditation capping out soon and our mana pool still increasing post 50, we need a way at higher levels to offset the additional down time. I think the mana bandages idea have merit. Balance would have to be worked out for amount recoverable and spell cast cost etc.

Research - My biggest complaint here is chain researching. I've spent a lot of money and time maxing my skill and gathering components and I don't have my 44th Earth pet due to failures. I'm 3 of 9 for 39th pet and 0 and 3 for 44th. Now I'm broke and frustrated. All I can say is I don't find this very "fun" (it is a game right?) and I'm already dreading the chain research for 49th pets.

Pet Health - The blue bar in our hp for our pets sounds great as long as group members can see it. I have no problems using report health, but it gets tiring having to constantly update my shaman partner as to the status of my pet. I can't see this having any effect on game balance, it is just a nice user interface enhancement.

Summoning - I agree that nearly all of our summoned weapons are useless. The only weapon I had ever been asked for was the SoR and that doesn't happen now since they fixed it. Considering how few spells we get, it's depressing that nearly 1/3 of our spells are useless. Proccing weapons seem to be the easiest fix... I would be happy even if they were pet only

Link Death - Please, please implement your idea. Having to spend 10 to 15 minutes resummoning and medding back up after every link death is horrible. All this link death recovery playtime adds up to a considerable amount. Plus my group usually isn't too happy. If another class disappears, they can join back up and start fighting again ASAP. We have to summon a new pet etc to be effective.

And here is my idea contribution. I haven't seen it suggested yet. I have no idea how difficult it would be to code but here it is. Give us a spell post 50 that would allow us to assume control of our pets directly. Even if only for a short time (18 secs). Something like the Glimpse spell, but allow us to move the elemental about. Not just see through its eyes. This could be VERY useful in pulling a pet out of a fight or making a pet that is chasing a runner return when he is out of voice range, while avoiding nasty pathing problem areas. I think it would be balanced in that the Mage would still have to be protected while he is "in" his pet, but would make us less of a risk to groups.

Just to end on a positive note, I would like to say I am VERY happy with my magician. I have played every class type and a few into their 30's, but the magician has by far been my favorite. I hope Verant will help us keep our role and usefulness post 50 as we are pre 50.

Thanks for reading!
