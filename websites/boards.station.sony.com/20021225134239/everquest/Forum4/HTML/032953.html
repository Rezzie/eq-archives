"How do you run out of stamina as a paladin? I never do as a shadowknight when I'm the puller, the only time I run out of stamina is if we take down several dozen mobs with little to no time in between."


"Running out of STA for a SK or Pal is quite easy if you are using a heavy high delay 2hander especially if you are hasted out your butt."


Note that what Kyel1 is implying here is correct: the amount of fatigue (stamina drain) from each swing is dependent on the weight of the weapon(s). Many two-handed weapons (and a few 1-handed weapons, such as the Wurmslayer) are quite heavy, and will cause you to become fatigued after a handful of swings. You can help this if you pay attention to weight when selecting which weapons to use/buy. This is one of the reasons I strongly recommend the Blackened Alloy Bastard Sword for mid level characters for a 2-hander - it not only has an excellent Damage Ratio (24 Damage/39 Delay), it is also lighter than many 1-handed weapons (2.5 wt).
