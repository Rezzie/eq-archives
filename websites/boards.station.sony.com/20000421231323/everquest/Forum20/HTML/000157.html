Here's a new thread I'm kind of curious about, what is your best battle story. I'm start, might not be my best, but I do at least remember it.

It was a night in Nagafen's Lair, the normal number of 60 people had dwindled down to a meer 3 groups. My group had set up shop in the pool room and expected to continue pulling like had been going earlier in the night. I went off to pull and brought back a greater kobold with his shaman friend. The two looked as if they would be slain easily, however then the kobolds friends heard their cries and started to come from the caves in the surrounding area and more kobolds began to come and team up on us. We continued fighting as the clerics healed and the wizards damaged, kobolds fell one after another until a total of 12 corpses lay on the ground, all without an enchanter =)
