Hi all,

My wife, a couple of my friends and myself, want to create a role-playing guild loosely based around Robert Jordan’s ‘Wheel of Time’ book’s on Xev.

I’m quite aware of the current controversy over the guild ‘Tolkiens Revenge’ (on Morrel-Thule) being disbanded because their name was in breach of naming conventions, so I have already taken the step of posting a name approval message on the General Discussion Board, so we’ll just have to wait and see what Verant come back with.

The original idea was that only European players could join the guild, due solely to time zones, but in retrospect we would really like to open it up for worldwide membership too.

The concept for the guild is this:-

The guild would initially be made up of the following;

‘Aes Sedai’ - Female only Spell casters; Wizards, Enchanters, Magicians, Clerics, Druids and possibly Bards. (No Male casters, as they are 99.9% evil in the books)

‘Warders’ - Male (and possibly Female); Warriors, Rangers, Rogues, and possibly Monks. Bards are under consideration… are they ‘really’ classed as spell casters, I thought maybe as 'Gleemen'?

Allowable races would be Half-Elf, Halfling, High Elf, Human, Wood Elf, Dwarf, Erudite & Gnome.

No Paladins, male casters, or evil race/classes, save these for the Whitecloaks and The Dark One himself!

The intention is for the guild to be active all over Norrath, but we think it would be best to create it in Greater Faydark.

People who are familiar with the books will know that most Aes Sedai bond with a Warder, who is sworn to protect her. Therefore this guild would be ideal for couples that want to play together.

Of course the guild would be open to single players too, as there is scope to 'bond' with someone else and in fact you don't have to be bonded at all. For instance the Aes Sedai of the Red Ajah hate men so never bond, and those of the Green Ajah sometimes bond with more than one!

There will be no restrictions on Males playing Females and vice versa. Playing a member of the opposite sex is ultimate role-play in my mind *grin*.

The guild will have a full website with its own domain name, and will offer members e-mail address’s from the domain. It already has a temporary website at http://www.lyndon.org/guild/

The names I have suggested to Verant for pre-approval are:-

The White Tower
Aes Sedai
The Amyrlin Seat
Dragonmount

If you can think of a better one then please drop me a line.

If you are interested in joining us then please send an email at guild@lyndon.org. It would be useful if you could mention in the mail the race/class of the char you would like to join with, your time zone, your age, and if you already have a partner to bond with. I promise that I will on no account use, or pass on, your e-mail address to any other party for any reason whatsoever, and will only use it to contact you about the guild.

Looking forward to hearing from you.


Lyndon

Dellenn,

In Jordan's books there is a organisation of religous soldiers called 'The Whitecloaks', and they are in esscence what you would call Paladins.

They hate and distrust the Aes Sedai and consider them Witches. The Warders that bond with the Aes Sedai are fighting men (and in one of the books, a woman), who come from all backgrounds, to side on the Aes Sedai cause. Some of these men are real rogues in character, but use their skills to keep their Mistresses alive. Therefore I do not view either Rogues or Paladins as evil, just different from the EQ norm.

In reality the Aes Sedai and the Whitecloaks are both 'good guys' fighting against evil, but they dislike and distrust one another. So much so that the evil forces have spies within them that try to bend them to their ways.

I have left Paladins free so that maybe someone will form a Whitecloak guild... and then we could have some REAL roleplay!
