Hello Tyis, and welcome back to EQ!

Do you have a preferred race/class combo, or are you also wondering what they should be?

If you don't really know, there are a few Websites out there dedicated to particular classes and/or races. But one good starting point would be Caster's Realm. Check the "Class Areas" section on the left and click on one of them to get more details on that class. Most, if not all, have a character "Creation Guide" section.

But most of all, I encourage you to try things for yourself and see what you like and what you don't.

Good luck!
