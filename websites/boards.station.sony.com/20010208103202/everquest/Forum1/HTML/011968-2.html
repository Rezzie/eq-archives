I've discovered hardware issues with over a dozen of the people i've talked to still having trouble. The usual issues are:

1) Improper IRQ Settings or Conflicts.
2) Wrong drivers or driver conflicts.
3) Broken CPU cooler. (incredible but true)
4) USB conflicts via shared IRQ/DMA controls.
5) AGP/Video Card conflicts via overclocking or AGP slot. (big no-no guys)
6) Redundant and outdated DLL's.
7) Excessive loading of unregistered DLL's and outdated TSR's.

Etc. So please, correct the basic problems related to the maintence of your PC before emailing me directly. I've analyzed over 120 dxdiag.txt files and i'm turning blue. Hehe. Chances are, if things other than EQ run poorly, or crash, you have many other issues that need to be solved before we can get down to the core problems with Everquest.
