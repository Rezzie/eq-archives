Well, first did you know you can turn a lot of battle spam off?

Is under Filters, you can take out your misses, the mobs misses, other peoples hits and misses, all sorts of things.

Second, to create a new box is very easy. Maybe too easy for me to describe well. I right click on on the right bar of main chat window. Then I click on the new window to tell what filters to send there.

Since I have most of my battle spam off anyway (sorry those who like to brag about your critical hits and blasts but I don't care so I don't see them) I don't use it in battle very often. But when I am in trader mode I have any direct /tells sent to me in there own chat box.

Hope this helps and if not, I am sure someone will give you more lucid directions.
