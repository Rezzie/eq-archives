Actually...planes + pets do NOT equal a disaster.

You can use pets in both airplane and fearplane just fine (as long as you have good pet owners...my guild hasnt had a problem in the planes yet caused by pets).

However...EVERYONE knows ( ) that pet pathing in fear is completely screwed up. In fact, a lot of the Hate mobs in fear used to have pets....and they pathing for them was soooo bad that

1) they would get stuck in all kinds of weird places and cause HUGE lag because mobs would summon multiple pets (heh...used to happen with aqua gobs too sometimes).

2) They would cause huge trains which would then require CRs often involving GMs etc.

To fix this...mobs in hate no longer have pets. The pathing has NOT been fixed. As such you almost never should use pets in Hate. You might from time to time have to summon a pet for an emergency...but this would be very rare...and generally only for advanced players/tactics when something goes really wrong.

So...

Fear: Pet's ok
Air: Pet's ok
Hate: NO PETS!!

*shrugs*
