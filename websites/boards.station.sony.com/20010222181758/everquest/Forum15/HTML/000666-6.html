The recast time on this spell does appear to be rather ridiculous for only a 200hp mass heal. I believe this should be reduced. I have yet to see anyone say anything good about this discipline and I tend to follow the general consensus on this one. It is pretty weak.

This is not a wish list, but simply the problem areas the Paladin faces along with my proposed fix for each problem area. Along with a fix on the 2-Handed weapon problem. I would also like to take this opportunity to say Thanks to Verrant for listening to our opinion on these matters.

Problems with the Paladin class:
1) Paladin buffs are seldom used or needed due to the other classes we group with. We have nothing that others want to be cast on them.
2) At lower levels, Paladins are good back up healers, but at higher levels this is not the case.
3) We are drastically inferior to all melee classes in dealing damage, Warrior AC and Warrior Hit points, this margin gets huge at high level.
4) Our spell abilities do not make up for our difference in damage dealing or lack of AC and lack of hit points.
5) Paladins/SKs are the only class who uses shields, warriors never do, yet are much better at using shields.
6) Other classes have many weapon options and many of those weapon options are not hard to get. This is not true for the paladin.
7) Paladins miss their target in combat an excessive amount.
8) Our resurrect series of spells are too low for our level when we get them.
9) Two handed weapons do too little minimum damage.

Proposed fixes for the previously listed problems:
1) Paladins need some buffs that are useful and wanted by other classes that no other class can cast and this should start at level 9, not 50.
2) Paladins need a "Lesser Superior Heal" around Level 52-53, and Superior Heal around 55-56.
3) AC, hit points, dodge, parry, and Riposte need to match the warriors abilities.
4) Adding fix #1 and #2 with some other buffing/healing improvement would be a fair compensation for our lack of offensive skills.
5) Paladins/SKs should have the highest skill cap in all shield related areas and the skill "Block" should work when a shield is equipped.
6) Paladins should be able to get good (not epic) weapons just as easy as Rangers and Warriors do. We also need more of a weapon selection.
7) Paladin hit/miss ratio needs to be looked at. When you only have one weapon and you miss non stop, it hurts a lot more then if you had two.
8) The paladin should cast resurrect exactly as the Cleric but with a 10 level delay.
9) Minimum damage ratio fix figured by the weapons damage stat (used Mithril Two-Hander for example):

Level 40: (damage stat x0.5) Mithril two-hander @ level 40: Minimum damage of 10 points damage.
Level 50: (damage stat x1.0) Mithril two-hander @ level 50: Minimum damage of 21 points damage.
Level 51: (damage stat x1.1) Mithril two-hander @ level 51: Minimum damage of 23 points damage.
Level 52: (damage stat x1.2) Mithril two-hander @ level 52: Minimum damage of 25 points damage.
so forth...
Level 55: (damage stat x1.5) Mithril two-hander @ level 55: Minimum damage of 31 points damage.
