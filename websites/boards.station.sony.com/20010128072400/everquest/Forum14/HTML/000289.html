I agree that the manual's focus on shaman as healer is off target. However the manual is off on a LOT of things. If you want to be prime healer, and are not happy being anything else, then shaman is not the class for you. But I love being a shaman. At lvl 44, within the past three weeks I have been focused exclusively on each of the following roles in different groups: healer, buffer, dotter, blaster, puller, tank (yes tank) and debuffer. I did them all very well, thank you, and against mobs that were blue or harder, wasn't helping out youngins on green stuff. (In fact when I was tanking it was myself and a lvl 41 enchanter and 45 druid, on kobolds in Solusek B.) I revel in having that kind of versatility. Clerics can heal, but they cannot heal and tank at the same time, not in their 40s.

If anyone has a link to where Verant is quoted as saying buffs are useless I would really like to read it. I know that they have stated that they try not to let twinking have too much of an effect, which implies that the benefits gained by stats on armor (or spells) will not make the difference between life and death. However, there is quite a difference between 3 agi and 7 dex on a bracer, and an extra 30 or 40 of each from higher end shaman buffs. Having tested myself by trying tanking and pulling, I know that one of the main reasons I can do it is not that I can take a tremendous beating, because I can't, but because between stamina and agility (and Togor's helps too), I can reduce the number of hits I take and how badly they hurt.

I would really say that the defining characteristic of a shaman is that we can fill any roll in a pinch. We don't have to worry about being refused a group because they already have a shaman. Yea, our pets are pretty weak, but we ourselves are stronger. No, we don't heal as well as a cleric once we hit our 40s, but our ability to reduce the need to heal in the first place can take up much of the slack. We at least have cannibalize and regen to boost our mana when we're without an enchanter...clerics have no such luck.

I would love to see us get better heals, because I do see it as a weakness later on. But I also see a lot of strengths that help make up for that weakness, and can't imagine abandoning the shaman class just because of that one drawback. I don't see myself as a second rate cleric, I see myself as a Shaman.

-Heyokah Verdandi Snowblood of Karana
