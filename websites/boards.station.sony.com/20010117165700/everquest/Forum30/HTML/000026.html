Rusty weapons are dropped by numerous creatures in the game...However, if you are very new...then I would suggest the easiest source for rusties...decaying skeletons. They are very numerous, and you can visibly see if they are carrying a weapon... They are not difficult to kill, and when armed, they usually have one of the following...
1. rusty dagger
2. rusty short sword
3. rusty broad sword
4. rusty long sword
5. worn great staff
6. cracked staff
7. rusty axe
8. rusty battle axe
9. rusty mace
10. rusty war hammer
11. rusty spear
12. rusty shortened spear

Decaying skeletons can be found in any newbie area. Just make sure they are decaying, for regular skeletons are tougher and would make short work of a newbie character. I hope this was of help to you.

good luck

The best way to do this is a quick out of character message asking any other newbies in the zone if they have any and if so you'll swap another newbie item for them. Rogues get a similar newbie quest and that worked for me.

Skellies will drop them.

Have a look in the shops if you have a little bit of cash. Vendors often sell rusty weapons that players have given them.

I know the quest you're speaking of--it's in Greater Faydark. Since you're on Tunare, attacking other players is out of the question anyway, so that solves that.

In that area, rusties will drop off of Orc Pawns and decaying skeletons. (They'll drop off of other Orcs, too, but they're a bit high for you as of yet.) The only sword I ever had trouble finding was the rusty broadsword, but I think that was just my bad luck. (I eventually got one.)

The tip about checking merchants is good, too.
