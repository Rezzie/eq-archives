Look, in the past clerics didn't have to worry about aggro when they used CH because it didn't taunt. Now, its a big taunt, so you'll have to change your tactics. For example, cast Celestial Healing (Or whatever that heal over time spell is) the second the tank is engaged with the mob. Its a slow heal, so that it won't build much aggro. It will, however, prevent the tank's health from plummeting so quickly. If his health is dropping too fast, use the smaller heals to keep it up until the mob's health has dropped significantly. Then, when the mob is at an acceptably low health, you can let the tank drop to 20 or 30% before you use a CH.

Is this method as mana efficient as only using CH? No, and I suspect that was the point of this nerf. I suspect that Verant thought that CH as used was unbalancing. However, does this mean that clerics will be replaced in groups by Druids and Shammy's? No, of course not. CH is still the most efficient method of healing, and having it available in between fights and at the end of fights is still a big advantage. Also, don't forget that clerics can also resurrect, and so will always be wanted in groups.
