The practical upshots are two-fold. Second, if that doesn't occur, and someone else kills the bugged sarnak, the guy who popped up on the hate list takes the faction hit, even if he did nothing to the MOB whatsoever. As far as I know, however, the bug doesn't exist anywhere else.

The Sarnak hate list issue (which I have experienced, great xp until you die. Note that the group had hunted over 10 hours before the problem started, and hunted 3 more hours after, until finally wiped out), is, I think, part of the Shared Hate List feature common in Kunark. Basically, because of the large zone size, it is possible to get onto a mob's hate list without actually pulling the mob (A mob with your name on it's list goes past and shares it's list, but the mob receiving the list is out of attack range). This new mob moves around and gives it's list to others. Soon you are hated throughout the zone, and spawning mobs get the new hate list almost at once. Soon any mob that enters attack range charges. Then you sit down to med. They charge from even farther off. I think it was originally referred to as the 'aggroing green froggies' issue.

This was debated a great deal after Kunark came out, and the decision was to leave the hate list unchanged. If this starts to happen I suggest the group zone and return, which should reset the hate list.

The healer seeking sarnak that people have described isn't a bug. It's a natural result of game mechanics and has been in the game from the beginning.

What happens is simple.

Puller runs out and pulls a couple mobs. Brings them back and you kill them rinse and repear right? What happens is that pull aggro sarnak X and doesn't realize it so out runs it. Once the puller is far enough away the sarnak stops chasing but doesn't forget that he hates the puller. He passes that around to everyone he gets near too. As soon as the healer starts healing the puller he gets added to all those aggro lists too. As he heals he moves higher and higher up that list till sarnak really hate him even before they've gotten anywhere near him.

Simplely logging out the cleric will greatly reduce the amount this happens. The best way to prevent it is to make sure you pull and kill everything you aggro.

The sarnak fort seems to be particularly bad for this because of it's layout it's easy to aggro something and not realize it and outrun it. With all the wandering sarnak this quickly spreads the aggro to all sarnak and everything basically spawns already aggro on the healer.

Bards seem to get hit by this pretty bad too since thier songs seem to act like constantly refreshing buffs so they build up the aggro fast.

Another place where you can really see this at work is in the Plane of fear. When Cazic is up the pullers need to be very careful not to loose anything they aggro or the aggro works back to Cazic and he will eventually build up enough aggro to HT a cleric and send his minions charging at you.
