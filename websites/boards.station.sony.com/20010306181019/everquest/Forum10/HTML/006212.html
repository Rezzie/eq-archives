I had an idea that I thought might be kind of fun for a player, and maybe bring them a little new outlook on the game, and a new avenue for roleplaying.

Upon reaching a preset level, of say 40 with your character, you get a BONUS character, a player MOB, which you can play whenever you want.

TRAITS and stats of this MOB: You are confined to one zone, but all of the mobs of your race can communicate with each other. (Think a smart orc camp) Any players going into the SMOB zone (smart MOB) knows that they are facing players controlling mobs, and not a computer program. These MOBS will not be UBER powerered, as it IS a players game after all, and the zone will have safe zones with guards and the like, places to shop, heal and join a group.

Thus players going into SMOB zones will know to look out for:
Organzied attacks
Ambushes
TRAPS (woo hooo...look at me...solo lonely mob wandering about aimlessly...attack me and watch me run....RIGHT INTO MY FOUR other buddies waiting in the bushes!)
AREA WIDE SMOB Communication. Your Smob can talk to all other SMOBS.
The only PLAYER chat a SMOB would hear is SHOUTS, and being a SMOB, not know what it says (not speaking the language), but hearing an area-wide shout would lead SMOB to shouter's location: Player_01 is shouting to the right and behind you, about xxx parsecs away. (that should cure useless shouts, eh?)

These SMOBS would only be able to group in groups of say FOUR, and be unable to engage others already in battle. (Explantion) Not be able to jump in on an already in progress battle by another mob of four.

Spells such as LULL would still work. SMOB_01 wants to help buddies, but feels too lazy to join in. When the SMOB dies, it spawns down a long corridor, that it needs to go back down to get back into play.

AUTO ATTACK is ALWAYS on, and anything in your Agrro zone will be attacked by SMOB, and SMOB does attack while running. This prevents SMOB players from just taking a dive to help friend players.

You would get 4 SMOB choices, depending on your current class. A fighter, A Caster, A quickling, or an UBERSMOB.

The zone would be NORTH KARANA-sized, thus keeping higher and lower smobs in their areas.

SMOBS can zone, but at a magical Routing penalty, shall we say. This means a MOB can venture out of its range zone, but takes a penalty hit, due to being away from home earth..the further it goes, the more damage it takes, and a zoned mob cannot be looted. (again that player friend thing).

SMOBS feed on grubs, and drink stream water (or player blood), can use ANY abandoned weapons they find dropped, and are given a random item when reborn.

Oh yea, all players look roughly the same to you (like all orcs look alike), and no names are revealed, just a HUMAN Paladin tag. Thus preventing unkind SMOBS from targeting players they dislike.

So? Do I just think too much here? If you were given this chance, would you play the zone, would you be a SMOB?

Why would a player rather play this MOB than their own character?
