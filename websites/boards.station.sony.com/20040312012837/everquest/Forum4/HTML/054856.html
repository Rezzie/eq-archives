I hear lots of complaints from people like druids or monks, not being able to find a group. What I have found is that, for example in PoV (a lev 59-62 playground) you can usually recruit successfully a lev 65 druid to join your group. They also stay for hours unlike the lev 65 cleric that joins you for 30 minutes and then leaves for something else.

Maybe you don't advance as quickly if you were one of the preferred classes, but there are groups available if you are willing to be in a dungeon slightly below the optimum level.
