1. Stay away from the Sullon Zek boards unless you enjoy stuff like nails driven into your head. There is no 'official' one anyway. But you have been warned should you decide to search for one anyway...

2. I don't know about class ratios per team, but I do know there's an awful lot of necros, druids, wizards and sk's. If you want a slightly rare class, play a warrior, or be a cleric there's never too many clerics.

3. Spells only do 66% damage to other players, and now with the patch so do bows. You will lose xp if a player within 5 levels of you kills you and vice versa. They will con even to you if they're within this range, if they're below it they'll be green, and if above they'll be red.

There's no 'play nice' rules here. Corpse camping is legal, kill stealing is legal, training enemies is legal etc. Only thing you can't do is harass, and of course training and corpse camping isn't considered harassment.

Oh, and be prepared to be killed by lvl50+ players on an almost regular basis. That goes for any team you choose.
