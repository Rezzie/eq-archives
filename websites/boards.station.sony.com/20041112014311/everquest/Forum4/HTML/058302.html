I have to agree with neurro. If you look for items just below the bright shiny one on top you can usually find some good deals.

Recently my ranger was sadly in need of WIS gear for Trade skills. I decided I could spend about 3000 plat on this gear that would do nothing but be used for skilling up. Puting in a 500 plat cap and going slot bt slot I noticed that some of the gear was far better than what I had on, even though what I had on had cost me more plat months ago.

I decided to do the same thing with my enchanter and easily got her INT to 240 unbuffed. If you set a price limit and look for very specific places to fill in armor or weapons then you can find items with a little dust on them, maybe a nick in the finish and get some great deals.
