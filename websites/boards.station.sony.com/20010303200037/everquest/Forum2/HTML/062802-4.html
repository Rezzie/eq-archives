My comments are in terms of a group that is trying to pull mobs as fast as possible in a sustainable fashion - ie the experience group.

If I have clarity and I get no casting resists, I can sustain my share of the damage (1/6th of the total mob health) over time. However the damage made possible by clarity represents the contribution of the enchanter and my spells are regularly resisted. Consequently I am never able to contribute my fair share to a group over time. This is the argument for increasing the wizard's ability to deliver damage - particularly over time.

The desire by VI to make classes interdependent to encourage grouping can easily be accomodated by making any wizard enhancements buff dependent - like clarity. However, if you make our abilities buff dependent, we must be able to deal significantly more damage so that groups are anxious to have us.

The idea of caster buffs analgous to tank buffs gives some ideas of potential improvements. Buffing increased casting speed, complete mana heals, faster mana regens (read better clarity) are all candidates. These threads have also mentioned improved mob debuffs needed to reduce resists and otherwise enhance spell impact. Root and snare are already debuffs at least as important to casters as slows are to tanks

VI has done a good job of creating a grouping need for tanks and healers and has found a good group niche for enchanters. However, it has been less successful in finding a niche for necs, mages, and wizards. It is not surprizing that much of our threads focus on soloing.
