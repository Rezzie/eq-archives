A possible rare equation multiplied onto the current success equation would be
([Current.Skill minus Trivial.Skill]/Current.Skill)
You will not be able to make the special items till your current skill passes the trivial level, equating into a positive percentage.

Ex: Wolf sandwitch is trivial at 26. If your skill is at 52 the following should happen.

Skill check to see if successful sandwitch.
If successful, do a random chance where chance modifier is below.
(52-26)/52 = 0.5
So if you are going to make a successful sandwitch, you have a 50% chance to get a Wolf Sandwitch with an effect of SoW. With a skill of 200 your chances would improve to 87%.

Because of the one skill beyond 200 deal, modify this past a skill of 200 so that the bottom will not go beyond 200. Above example again with a baking skill of 225.
(225-26)/200 = 99.5%
This is an excellent percentage for Grandmaster Bakers.

I'm not sure what the skill cap is on Grandmaster, but if it is 250 then you have a 112% chance to make a SoW Burger as I'm sure they would be called.
