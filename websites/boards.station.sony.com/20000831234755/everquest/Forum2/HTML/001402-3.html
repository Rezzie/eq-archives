>>>Wisdom and Intelligence have no effect on the mana pool of a shadow knight.

Indeed it does (Intelligence anyway), and now it will have the same effect as it does for a pure caster of the same level.

>>>Does this also mean that stats will have a greater effect on hybrid mana, just as it does on the parent class?

Buffs that increase the stat used to determine mana (int or wis depending on class) will indeed affect the mana pool.

>>>I'm a Bard. I'm a Hybrid. I have an experience penalty. This change doesn't do doodly for me.

Only two bard songs use mana currently, and one of them may be moved to a "resing timer" in the future. The change really doesn't affect bards, as they aren't dependant on mana for most of what they do.

>>>This thread is what I hope to be a large "Thank You" card for Abashi. Yesterday he announced that the hybrid mana pool has been increased on the test server, which will possibly lead to the same on the live servers. I consider that a great leap towards our salvation.

Actually the thanks technically belongs to Roger Uzun, who is the lead programmer for the server-side code. He reads the boards here quite regularly, saw the post, gave it some thought, did the math, and decided that the point had merit. Since the spells already cost more mana than those cast by the parent caster, and since the hybrid doesn't have the +int/wis and +mana items available to the caster, the naturally smaller mana-pool was unnecessary.
