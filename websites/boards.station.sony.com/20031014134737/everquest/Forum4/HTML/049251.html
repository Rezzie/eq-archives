The first 20 points of sense heading are usually pure agony. Transfer your sense heading key over to your hotkeys and click every time you notice you can.

After 20 the points start coming a lot faster.

It will help your compass stabilize in addition to telling you the direction. And if it says the "you think" message, that is correct. I was worried when I was a newbie that I only THOUGHT I was heading the right direction. No matter how high your skill is, that message is always the same.

You'll want to press and press and press and press the button. You may only go up 5 points per level, so it will take much time before your compass becomes accurate.

In the days of old, people used to use LOC to determine their direction. North/South would change one number, East/West would change the next. (The last set on numbers are altitude and generally not that useful.)

Since LOY came out with online mapping, LOC to me has become a lost art.

And to provide a more direct answer to your question, yes, the very low levels of skill will not succeed in providing an answer to which direction you are heading.

When you get to a skill of about 50, you will notice your Sense Heading attempts finally producing a success with only a few button presses. Good news actually, since skill 50 can be achieved at level 9.
