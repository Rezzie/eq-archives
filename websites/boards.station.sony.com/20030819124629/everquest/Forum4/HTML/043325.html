As a human paladin your easiest way to raise Freep Militia faction would be to turn in deathfist belts to the bad miltia guy on the balcony, at dubious faction or above. You'll
keep your good paladin guild faction fine by killing the orcs for the belts.

Another good way to raise militia is to destroy Crushbone, bordering Greater Faydark, several times. The Orc Scalp from orcs there can be used in the high pass guard captain "orc scalp" quest, near the orc area in highpass hold. These stack and you get a whopping ton more in Crushbone than you would in the pass itself. Besides killing them in CB, virtually no one loots the scalps from corpses so you can just check passing corpses. You could even offer a couple gold per scalp there, since the captain in high hold pays 4 gold per scalp.

That quest raises Militia as well as corrupt qeynos guards, I think. A goblin ear quest in high hold keep itself also raises the factions but, as with the orcs, its a volume problem. CB ends up being the best bet.

As Skrax mentions, it is possible to raise Freeport Militia faction quite easily. However, most of the methods involve destroying your Knights of Truth faction at the same time. This is bad.

The best method of raising FP Militia without lowering KoT, is to go into Highpass and kill goblins down in the mines. Gather as many "Left Goblin Ears" as you can and turn them in to the Captain that usually stands next to the merchants on the stairs that lead into the mines. The thing is... if you kill Sir Lucan you will need about 1000+ ears just to get out of KOS. It's a ruthless price for an extremely long quest that yields a not-so-great weapon.

As a human paladin you don't have to worry about paladin faction when turning in deathfist belts to the militia, as I said the pluses for killing the orcs balance out the minuses from turning in the belts. Your knights faction will ALWAYS be higher from killing orcs than it will be lowered from turning in belts, even if you turn in 7 billion belts. Why? Because in order to turn in 7 billion belts, which are minus one knights of truth, you had to kill around 28 billion orcs which are all plus one knights of truth.
If you've ever tried to get belts you can see that even if the belt quest was minus 2 or 3, you'd still be ahead from the pluses in killing the orcs.
He is KOS anyway though, to militia, so this is moot.

The only concern is really that the militia belt one also lowers the north freeport clerics. Again, if you are a goody goody this isn't a very big deal since it would take whopping tons of faction minuses to make a difference. If you are a bad race/diety combo, this will put you in the hole more with the clerics.

Militia is milita, they are the guard looking guys in east and west freeport. They all are on the same faction and will have the same consider.
If you are KOS to lil bobby the militia guard, you are just as KOS to Sir Lucan the head of the guard.
