HHK till 29 or 30 (a bit high for the zone, but you can duo or trio in the lower rooms for good experience).

DSP for another few levels. Zelniaks to begin with then get a ME group.

Ideally hang off till 34 and get decent nukes.

The onward to Rathe!

Ideally, you need to snare the big buggers (Bonds of Force - lvl 29 or 34? Cant remember) cos they run pretty fast and hit hard.

If you are under 34 you will be using your force strike spell (thunder strike / force shock - sorry i dont know spell names!) - it does 250 damage.

But be warned it knocks the HG's out of range of your tanks, but not far enough to be out of range of the giants. So, tanks get hit, giants dont!

Make sure you have all your buffs for your level - lesser familiar at 29 - stops you getting hit a lot of the time - skins, etc.

Also O'keil's Levity is great for Rathe - in hilly areas you can use it to get far away from a mob without needing sow - just float off the top of a hill!

By 34 you should be able to duo in rathe with another caster.

I was duoing at 32 with a 36 wiz. But we died some.....

Your ideal duo partner should be a rooter! Keeps the HG's still while you nuke!

Shammy's and druid's are great duo partners for wizzy's in rathe - sow and dots too!

Clerics are good to - minor nukes, buffs and heals.

My wizzy is now 37 and im duoing in rathe with a 31 cleric - his buffs add 30pct to my HP!

Technically, with KEI i should be soloing in Rathe at 37, and ive had a couple of successful attempts, but my soloing sucks and i prefer groups anyway!

When you get to rathe you'll stop caring about exp! I bought 11 of my level 39 spells last night with some of the loot from HG's!

Your only problem will be that your weak wizzy body wont be able to carry all the loot! Good porting business in Rathe too - make sure you have Nexus port.
