Easy. For some strange reason, they really, really don't want to see rogues partnering with mages or beastlords (necros have fear, so no biggy there).

I would imagine, though, it's just a simple matter of game balance. Yes, it sucks, but it has to be done. Otherwise, you could have groups comprised of ---


1 mage
5 rogues.

1) Sic pet on mob.
2) Pet tanks mob for a while, gets good taunt.
3) Mage sends in 1 or 2 nukes.
4) 5 rogues come up, hit backstab once. Yay, exp.

5) Mage pet heals itself 10x faster than a PC. More if water pet.

6) Group repeats process.

Where's the danger? Oh dear lord, you might *gasp* lose a malachite and some mana if the pet dies!!


Really, I don't like how it works out now (mostly because I really dig the mage and BL classes), but I can see why Verant has it like this.
