Seafuries in OOT.
If you have the Jaundiced Bone Bracer, you can kill these guys with no downtime at all. They start to green out around 53 I think - about half are blue to me at 54.

Ice giants in Everfrost. Boring, but decent xp and loot (6-minute spawns).

Your soloing choices will diminish greatly as you hit 54 - you will likely have to find groups to get any higher.
