In favor of buffing Innoruuk/Cazic:

- From a roleplaying sense gods should be nigh-unkillable, con red to 60th level players, and so on.

- Gods should have well, "godlike" loot no? A buffed god would have buffed loot. This is good

- Epic quest items would have to be moved to a more accessible mob, or else the quests would become impossible for many more guilds than they are now or at the very least impact the risk/reward for these epics severely.
