This was a response to a thread complaing about high lvl players wanting "favoritism"

If you dont have a lvl 51+ player, actually it should be 55+ you have no idea. Everquest at lvl 55 is a FAR FAR different world than it was at 35.

At 35 I could pretty much walk anywhere and get XP in a zone. Getting a rez was a non issue. heck you can get by without a cleric. 6 person groups are viable. Cash was always an issue. Never had enough cash, spent time at auctions, selling fine steel, etc...

Buffs didnt cost 11pp a shot, there was no such thng as a "raid" you didnt need 30 people to do anything. In fact most times you could log on and actually start killing within minutes.

lvl 55+ you need a key to go anywhere, you cant get any loot without a cleric, you pretty much need a cleric for everything unless you want to risk losing 20+ hours of XP from a single mistake.

You cant walk anywhere to get XP, almost everything in the old world is green, anything above green in kunark requires a key or a raid.

In SOV you are high lvl yet greens can kill you easy. at least in the old world, greens were no threat, go to SOV i dont care if you are lvl 60, get 1-3 greens on you unprepared, and you are toast.

Its 2 completely differnt games. 1-50 is fun its challenging etc.. but its completely differnt.

Until you have planned a raid for 1 week only to spend 8 hours getting everyones corpse and rezzing because of a single mistake or because the cleric went linkdead, you have no idea.

The 55+ game is extremely stressful, it can be exciting, rewarding, but usually its lots and lots of effort and honestly not really worth it.

XPing takes too long, raiding for loot takes 8 hours at a clip, getting groups together takes too long etc..

at lvl 35 you can run down to lower guk or droga or FM or dreadlands or anywhere in SOV and start hunting.

at lvl 55 you need a gate everywhere, or a key or a rogue, there is no running over to a zone and getting a group. YOu have to bring your group everywhere you go.

Excellent question. At lower lvls, you can go to almost any zone and get a pick up group. You can get a druid or shaman to heal, if you have a cleric and chanter thats just a bonus, a paladin can be your main tank etc..

At higher lvls there are less zones to hunt in, however those zones require perfectly balanced groups to hunt in, and in the case of plans and SOV you often need 2-5 groups to hunt effectively in those zones.

So say you gather up 3 friends, but you dont have a cleric... you arent doing any dngs, maybe just do some outdoor hunting.

Lets say you get a cleric but no enchanter, this rules out about 3/4 of all high lvl dngs. Sebilis without a chanter? waste of time too much downtime for the cleric.

Now you assume that when you log on the high lvl players will be consolodated in a few zone. Uber guilds raiding the dain. or sleepers, another guild raiding Kael, another in necropolis.

Look over to sebilis? pretty much every player in sebilis is already in a full group, its a keyed zone in diff zone out zone, which means you dont just walk in and hang around looking for a pick up group. Its VERY out of the way from any city, you pretty much need a gate there and if you are a melee player you are crazy to go there alone and hope for a pick up group cause if you zone in , you have no way out without begging for a gate.

ALso all the groups in sebilis are usually full and if they are looking for a replacement usually its done through tells or guildchat.

Basically sebilis is bring your own group. Chardok is similar (only no key) this zone is bring your own raid 2+ groups. Howling stones?> need a key and no safe zone in, basically zone in and get ready to fight.

Kael? you need at least 2 groups and have to go very deep in the fight. necropolis is a 3 group min zone etc...

There are basically no dngs that you can hang out in like lower guk or karnors and look for a group or just start a pick up group.

The other problem is shortage of high lvl chanters/clerics.

Uberguilds constantly recruit high lvl clerics, 99% of high lvl clerics are grouped when I log on and have 3-5 offers on the table for raids that night. Enchanters? ususally getting XP in sebilis, or farming XP in karnors, the rest are on raids or playing their alts (usually rogues or warriors)

then you have the burned out clerics. They perma camp in the plane of hate, and only log on when there is a hate raid so they can get their armour.

I have never been on a hate raid with less than 5 clerics.

Most of my time online is spent planning stuff, convincing clerics/enchanters to join my raids, trying to form XP groups, gating people around to the spot where we are going to farm XP etc..

Dont get me started on the Epic quest time sinks, almost forgot about these. You have no idea what a time sink in EQ is til you try the warrior or wizzard or SK epic. Raiding the plane of fear/hate for the 44th time, it gets really old watching Cazic Thule drop lame bard weapons that no one wants.
