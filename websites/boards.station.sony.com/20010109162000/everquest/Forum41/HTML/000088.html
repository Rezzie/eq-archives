I had 3 guild offers before i chose the one i'm in now and 4 since then(one was a little later the same day i joined KISS)(Knights In Shining Armor).. I did not get an invite until after lv 16, Basically your early levels are when you show what kind of person you are and begin making the connections and friends you will share your adventures with throughout your characters career. Sometimes people will not fully realise just how much you will bring to their guild until later, depending on how outgoing you are or if you happen to be in the wrong places at the wrong time to be noticed. I do know that begging or /shout or /ooc is NOT a good way to get into a guild. If you see a guild you would like to be in, get to know its members and see if they are really the type of people you want to be with. If you find a guild that you feel very strongly about joining, then let them know you are interested and continue on your adventures as usual. Chances are they will keep an eye out to see how you act( if you're an anoying loudmouth, a calm team oriented player, the shy type that will fight to save a friend to the bitter end, etc...) It may take several levels, or it may take 3 seconds, but if it is where you want to be, it will be worth it in the end. There are many good guilds out there with a couple of bad members and many bad guilds with a couple of good members...Enjoy the game and don't worry about guild membership, worry about finding others that share your play style and learn from those who dont(you might find a better way)..keep a list of those you find most enjoyable to group with, and more importantly, keep one for those you never want to group with again.
