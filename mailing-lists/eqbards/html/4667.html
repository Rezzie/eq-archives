We (well, most of us) are trying to kill that term because we
think it's a terrible, non-descriptive term. We try to use
weaving/juggling/trans-chord-demodulation instead, though
weaving seems to be the frontrunner. JM (developer from
Verant) called it cycling, which I dislike because it can get
confusing when you refer to the previous "cycle" (is that the
previous song, or the previous set of songs?).

We won't hate you for using "twisting." :-) Just be aware
that there are other (IMHO better) terms for it.
