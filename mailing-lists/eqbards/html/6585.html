Like someone else previously posted, the Lesser Blade Fiend comes out of the
basement. I had the fortune to kill one while camping in the courtyard.
Someone pulled a train from the basement that included a bunch of nasty
stuff. My group watched it run by while sitting along the wall. The train
headed to the zone chasing the party that started it. The Lesser Blade
Fiend was at the end of the train. Our party attacked it and killed it with
relative ease. It is one of the coolest looking monsters I have seen in the
game. It looks like an air elemental with several swords that swirl around
and around. Imagine an invisble cuisinart and you get the picture.
