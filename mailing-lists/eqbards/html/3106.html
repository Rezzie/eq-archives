Subject: Getting out of a group


I know that I like some of you are struck with an overriding amount of
guilt when you are stuck with a very bad group. It is both difficult for
you escape without looking like the villian. Whenever I feel the need to
leave a group that I dislike after they have ignored my comments for
improving theoverall nature of the group, I usually tell them that a guild
member or friend is in need of some help. To leave on a good note I only
leave when they are in a good condition, fully healed are ready to fight.
And to leave them with a good impression of me I will usually wish them
luck. A good number of these bad party members will improve and
eventually learn to play in a group, so it is best not to provoke them who
knows when they might save you in the future. =)
