WHY DO I NEED CHARISMA
Spoke with GZ this morning regarding Charisma and its effect in relation to
resists.

In summary Charisma effects the resists of Charms, Mesmerises and Blur-Type
Spells across all classes.

Charisma will not lower resist rates of debuff spells, slow spells, direct
damage spells, or DoT's.
