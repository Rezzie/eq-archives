Well, after asking my friends who all were hardware guys before becoming
software programmers, I decided against Compaq for their use of cheap mother
boards, Gateway for mixing and matching motherboards (you might get a good
one, you might not) and settled on a Dell p3 700 with 256 meg and a geForce
64 meg video card. I now run with clipping on full in all Kunark zones
except Warslik's Woods. I also got a cable modem which helps.
I have to say that this is the first computer I ever bought which I hooked
up and it *just* worked..what a freakin relief. I had always built them from
parts before.
One note, pay the little bit extra for a MS mouse with IntelliEye, it uses a
light sensor instead of a trackball and is a HUGE improvement for very
little money.
