The aviaks are probably the ones in Lake Rathe, though I could be wrong. I
don't think there are aviaks in Rathe Mountains.

At level 24, I was killing gargoyles in the ocean of tears, but depending on
your server that area could be pretty crowded right now. You can definately
solo them at 24 though.

You're starting to get into the dark days of soloing though, as monsters really
tend to hit hard at this level, you don't do much damage, and you don't have the
two big mid-range bard power/solo songs, Angstich's Appalling Screech and
Solon's Song of the Sirens.

If you want to solo, look to the aviaks and gargoyles. If it were me though,
I'd head to Cazic Thule and try to join a group. Great exp, really fantastic
loot and lots of action. Groups there like having bards too, as lullaby and
hymn are very potent there.
