Subject: Sol A and preventing lava deaths


The best method that I have learned for lava corpse recoveries is Don't Fall
In!

Of course it's not always as easy as all that, so there's a little trick you
can do when you fall into lava (at least by the Lavastorm entrance of Sol A,
haven't tried it in any of the other lava, but may work).

/quit

Yes, that's right, /quit, or /q for short.

When you log back in, you should be back on land and alive.

Now, I'm not saying that this will always work in every lava pool you come
across, but it has saved my blue butt more times than one, and I teach it to
every person I group with that doesn't have much Sol A experience.

Hope this helps...
