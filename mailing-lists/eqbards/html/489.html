I have one of these. The Berserker Strength raises your strength, lowers
your agility, and gives you temporary magical skin that absorbs a certain
amount of damage (or maybe a certain number of hits, not sure). It also

to use. I've heard reports that the Tentacle Whip and Screaming Mace also
have this bug. We suspect that all magic weapons that cast spells in
combat probably do the same thing. Please, everyone, file /bug reports so
this goes higher on their priority list to fix! Currently my spiffy
Tomahawk is gathering dust in the bank, because I can't use it without
totally hosing myself. :(
