Step number one, if you get down there and a group is camping the mob that drops the guise
(Assassin? I don't remember) is to just ask if they would mind if you looted it. Be creative,
offer to sing them a song or something in exchange for it.

Chances are, they're nice people who all have masks anyway and just want to pull Serrated Bone
Dirks and they'll happily let you loot the mask if you entertain them.

If they're jerks about it, then offer to pay. If it was my group though, we'd happily let you
loot it for free.
