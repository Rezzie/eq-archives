Subject: New Macros


Has anyone found any more interesting uses of the new macro commands?

I have experimented with a few for weaving, basically turning a 4 song weave
into a 2 macro weave. The most powerful combination I have found thus far
is using Chants with Charm. The macro lays the Chant, Charms the mob, and
sits the pet. When the pet gets back up I know to reapply the hotkey.

/pause 30, Cast 1
/pause 2, /stopsong
/pause 30, Cast 2
/pause 2, /stopsong
/pet sit
