Subject: Mask fix


Don't know if anyone else notice but the vision problem with the masks was
fixed in the last patch.

The bug was when you had the masks effects up and played a song that also
included a vision enhancement you would return to human level vision when
the mask effect wore off.

Example being.
1) start up mask of deception. get ultra-vision
2) start playing psalm of warmth... get infra-vision but ultra is better so
no change in sight
3) stop playing the song and wait for the mask ultra-vision to wear off

And pouf! You had human vision by default till you zoned. I am so glad
they fixed this.
