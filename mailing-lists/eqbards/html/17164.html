Subject: Avoiding 50s doldrum


My current pasttime is pulling in Fear. Find something to pull, bring it
back near the group, if there's more than one then run in circles
alternating selo's and chains till one (chain) sticks. Then call out to
the puller that one will be lagging behind the rest, let them pick it off,
and keep running in circles till they're ready for more.

Heck of a lot more fun than the alternative.
