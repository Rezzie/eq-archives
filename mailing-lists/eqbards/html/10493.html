Sol A is really really bad near the entrance. Those steps,
that lava, that hole in the wall you have to crawl through
make it probably the most dangerous entrance of any "dungeon"
aside from the planes. It is much better inside. Don't let
the entrance discourage you.

Wizards are always a problem. I didn't think they were any
different than casting madmen, ice goblin evokers/diviners, or
even the healers in Cazic. Just keep your resist songs ready,
have a good healer in the party, and you should be fine.
