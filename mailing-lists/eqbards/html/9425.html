Subject: The benefits of helping others


I bask in the glow of attaining my first Lambent item, the Lambent Greaves!

have TWICE now had 50th level casters that I didn't know be very friendly
and follow me around for an hour or so helping me get pieces. Why? Because
for no reason and without payment I had stopped and healed their low level
secondary characters up, or had bailed them out of sticky situations with
orcs or something.
