Ok, in Oasis? Head south through the pass, perhaps stop off at a few
dervish camps for derverision. Head to SW corner, enter Innothule, hand
left, and visit lovely Guk, so beautiful this time of year. Course, I had
fun for a while at the bottom of Oasis lake killing aqua goblins, but you're
probably too high level now, as am I.

If you take all the advice and head west to the Karanas, well, you'll soon
enough get bored more than likely. If you have a few magic weapons, I'd
recommend jaunting through Blackburrow to Everfrost Peaks. There's
something there, something very interesting. When you find the frozen
river, look for a break in the ice, and get aquatic ayre ready. Bring a few
people with you, and swim north, up the river (Here's where you see how
brave those barbarians really are!). Eventually you'll come up to a dry
cavern, a maze, and you should find a few Ice Boned Skeletons to slaughter.
Stack guardian and elemental when you tangle with them, and carefully
explore. What an infernal place hidden under those peaks! Be warned, I
only explored alone and don't know much of it, but I do know I saw a one
Sulon McMoor, who looked quite deadly.
