Subject: Running Through Beholder


Is it possible to run through Beholder with Accelerando? I made the trip
through last weekend crawling along high on the cliff and it was nerve
wracking. I would much rather make a fast dash through and get it over
with! But I saw at least a dozen evil eyes from my vantage point on the
cliff wall...can I run past fast enough to avoid trouble?
