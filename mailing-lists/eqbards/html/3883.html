Subject: Lullaby tip


You'll recall I did some song timings and came up with
6 seconds between song pulses, and 3.5 seconds to start up a
song.

If you leave Lullaby running, heads will nod once every
6 seconds. If you immediately stop it and restart it when you
see the "nods" message, you can shorten this to 3.5-4 seconds.

I tried doing it the latter way last night, and the rate that
it interrupted spellcasters went from about 50% to about 80%.
We even had several fights where the spellcasters didn't get a
single spell off because they nodded asleep before they could
finish casting.
