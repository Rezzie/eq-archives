Author: Jennifer McComb
Subject: Level 12 needs a new goal


Over the last few weeks, Adagia's goal was to acquire a Dragoon Dirk
and to reach level 12 so she could finally sing Brusco's Boastful
Bellow. Last weekend her call to purchase a Dirk in Crushbone was
finally answered, and last night she earned the right to scribe the
12th song. She's currently calling E. Freeport home, and she's
finding the adventures there entertaining, but she has suddenly found
herself without any burning immediate goals. What excitement waits
for a Bard of 12 songs?

Adagia,
Bard of 12 songs,
Luclin.
