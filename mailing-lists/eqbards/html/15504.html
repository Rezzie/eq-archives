This is really exciting for me..

Fennin Ro is splitting today/tomorrow. The new server that many of us will
be migrating to should be up on Tuesday. My guild is moving in its
entirity, although the migration list is strictly first-come-first-served.
There is no special treatment given to guilds, and in fact our guildtags
will not transfer. Many players and guilds are staying on the old server
because of longstanding ties, and that's certainly understandable.

This means that the server population will be made up largely of people that
are high level and complete newbies. The middle level bracket is likely to
have some awesome grazing at places like Cazic-Thule, South Karana, Rathe
Mts, Lake Rathe, High Hold, etc.

I'm a bit leery of how all of this is going to work out, but I'll keep you
guys posted of any interesting developments.
