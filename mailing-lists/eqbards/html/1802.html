>Is there any clean way to tell if you have a skill maxed?

What I do is not start on my secondary weapon skills till my primary (for me
piercing) skill is topped. Then I will switch a secondary weapon (1h blunt
or 1h slash) to my primary hand. Occasionally I will check the attack value
and swap weapons in the primary hand. When the numbers are equal the
secondary weapon is topped.
