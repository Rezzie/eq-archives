Subject: My first Hate raid


I just wanted to drop everyone a first message about my first Hate raid. I
experienced no deaths, and in fact there were only about 8 deaths the whole
evening (5 of which were on the break-in). I was the only bard up there and
had the honor of looting an imbrued bracer:)

Some of you guys warned about singing while the revenants were being pulled.
I didnt seem to have any problems at all with creatures agrooing me at all
during the entire time in Hate. I went with an instrument combo all
evening, and some of the group commented that their resist rates against NPC
spells were higher than normal for a Hate raid.

I also took care of all curing for my party, which allowed our cleric to
keep more useful spells memmed.

This was just a "fun" raid, and we cleared the entire zone except for those
areas around Maestro and Inny (who were both up, but our pull team had 3
members who had to work today and wanted to get to bed).

Last but not least is the fact that my party only got exp on 10 or so kills,
but I made almost 3\4 of a bubble of exp!

Lago Bardoon of Bertox, 48th composition, Defenders of Aleria
