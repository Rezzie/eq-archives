An interesting update to the patch server, for those who don't frequent.
Remember this doesn't mean all these changes will filter down to the
standard worlds.

September 24th, 5:30pm
----------------------

The following enhancements have been made to the Test Server:

- Experience and looting rights is now awarded based on group aggregate
damage (i.e. If a group's combined damage if greater than an individual
player, the group members will be given the exp. and looting rights).
- NPC corpses will only remain for 8 minutes.
- If a party member goes link-dead, the remaining members will be
notified. When the link-dead player returns, the group members will be
notified as well.
- Pets with greater than 20 hit points cannot receive hit point buffs.

- The EverQuest Team
