Ok, after a bit of testing, here's how it seems to work:

Root overrides SoW
Chain overrides Root
Accelerando overrides Chain
SoW overrides Accelerando
Root overrides Accelerado
Chain overrides Accelerando

Accelerando does not override SoW
Accelerando does not override Root

And someone else reported here that Chain overrides SoW, which
I didn't get to test.
