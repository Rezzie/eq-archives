Regarding whether Bards or Rangers (or some other character type) pushes
more buttons/is more active: I'm not convinced that Bards have to do
more stuff than other character types, but what exhausts me about playing
my Bard vs playing any of my other characters (warrior, enchanter,
shaman) is the unceasing regularity of it all, and the fact that there
is no time when you can afford to take a break. It's even worse when
you have mediocre healing, and you have to twist during downtime. Add to
this constant rhythmic undertone all of the other stuff we have to do,
it's pretty darned tiring.
