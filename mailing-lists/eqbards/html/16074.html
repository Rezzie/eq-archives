Subject: Twisting Anthem and Warsong.


On Rokenn's Site it states that the speed component of Anthem and Warsong
will not stack. I ran a little experiment last night and here are the
results. I was naked at the time CT will do that to you.

No Songs: Attack - 275

Anthem: Attack - 290

Warsong: Attack - 290

Both Woven: Attack - 303

So it looks like even the speed components do stack, but only partially.
