> Can someone please explain how to play two songs at once? Whenever I switch
> songs the other one ends.

When you stop playing a song, at least one of the songs that is not pulse based
like Hymn of Restoration or Chords of Dissonance, the effect does not wear off
immediately. To verify this for yourself, start running with Selo's
Accelerando, then stop singing. Note the delay before you slow down.

To play two songs at once, or even three (which I call juggling), start a song,
wait for the effect to kick in (look for the words describing the song or for
the effect icon to appear), then stop the song and start another one. By
skillfully starting and stopping your songs, you can have more than one "active"
at a time.
