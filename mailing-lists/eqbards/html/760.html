I've done some experimenting in this regard, as there is a druid in my
party and I played with druids a great deal in beta. When both of you
are around level 14, Spirit of the Wolf is faster than Selo's without a
drum. With a drum, Selo's is faster.

Now, I've never tested it, but we seem to get faster as we go up in
levels, while Sprit of the Wolf seems to stay constant despite the level
of the druid or shaman. It could be that at a high level, we're faster
than SoW even without a drum.

I doubt we're ever faster than Spirit of Cheetah though.

Some experiments in this regard would be interesting, if anyone wants to
take them on.
