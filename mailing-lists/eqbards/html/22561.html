I am frequently in a group with a cleric and a druid. The cleric
roots and heals, druid DOTS, sows and roots if cleric is agroed, and I pull
and chant.

Great thing about this is that no one is getting continually
pounded, so there is no down-time.

Great tactic in the late 40's -early 50's in the Dreadlands and
Burning Woods.
