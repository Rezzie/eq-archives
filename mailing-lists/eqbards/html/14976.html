Pathing in MM is insane, and usually what causes the most problems. Also -
if you see a pet at the zone - DON'T attack it - it is misspawned from the
other side of the castle wall - and if you do - not only will it's master
come - but a goodly percentage of the castle with it.

Grouping with someone that knows MM well - and all the buggy path problems
is by far your best bet - and have them point out all the problems along
the way.
