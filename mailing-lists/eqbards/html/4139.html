The 3 different types of dmg shields are the Coat Series (Druid/Ranger
Self Only), Shield Series (Druid/Ranger Targetable), and Heat/Frost Series
(O'keil's Radiation, etc)

Psalm of Warmth & Cooling fall into the 3rd set and wont stack with others
of that type. The three types are meant to stack though, and if it's not
working currently it is a bug. I do know Warmth stacks fine with SoT
though, as last night I was getting 13's of the hits (9 from SoT, 4 from
PoW).
