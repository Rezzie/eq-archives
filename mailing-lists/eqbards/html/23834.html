Hymm (heal song), Chant of Battle and chains would have been my mix. I play
a cleric as my secondary and I still notice an appreciable ease on my mana
use at lvl 36 when a bard is mixing in some Hymm. Also allows necros and
shamans to chow their health a lot more.

Another spell to think about is Binding. It has a nice AC Debuff and is
great for keeping mezzed mobs mad at you rather than the shaman/enchanter.
