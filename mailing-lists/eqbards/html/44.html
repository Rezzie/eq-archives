In my opinion, strength is the only statistic that bards should put
their points into. Yes, there's no question, the other stats are
important, but if you want to wear plate or banded mail, and you WILL
want to wear plate or banded mail, you NEED the strength to carry the
armour. Items to increase dexterity and agility are much easier to find
than strength enhancers.
