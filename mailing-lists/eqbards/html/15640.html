Subject: Impaling yourself


I'm sure someone's done this, but the other day I was recovering my corpse
in Mistmoore, looted my Minotaur Horn, sang Shauri's, and finished looting
everything else, including putting my Runed Bone Fork into my ranged weapon
slot.

The Runed Bone Fork, replaced the graphic of my horn, and left me looking
like I was standing around writhing, holding a spear which was sticking out
the back of my head!

Not so much of an Easter Egg as a bug, but it was amusing.
