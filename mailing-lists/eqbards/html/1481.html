If you're not trying to be too adventurous, I've found the orcs north of
Kelethin and not too deep into Crushbone well supplied with weapons and
armor that will turn a bit of cash. You won't gain much experience but you
can make steady money.

Going into Crushbone is not entirely safe. Trains abound, and there's this
dark elf called Ambassador D'Vinn who will clean your clock in short order.
Keep your Accelerando handy, and know the way out.
