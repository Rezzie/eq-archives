Subject: Alenia's Disenchanting Melody


If anyone has fought Lord Grimrot when he is carrying the Opal
Scythe, you know how devasting his debuffs and DoTs can be.

Steal Strength
Heat Blood
Disease Cloud

Enter Alenia's Disenchanting Melody... Not only did this song
cancel the debuffs, but it eliminated the DoTs as well. This
song does indeed work as intended as a Party: Cancel Magic
effect. It left my Valor, Guard, and Endure Magic untouched.

Very good song to weave when fighting SKs or necros that use
their dark magic.

Harmonic Distortion, 30th drummer.
League of Antonican Bards, The Wind Spirit's Song
