> Where do you find pumice stones?

Any of the alchemist merchants carry it. In Freeport, it is
near the monk's guild. Head North and hang a right as if you
were going to East Freeport. Straight ahead is a sign saying
"East Freeport." On the left is a opening in the wall with a
building beyond it. The alchemists are in there, selling
other useful (or useless) items such as gate and invisibility
potions, harvest and spring crystals, etc.
