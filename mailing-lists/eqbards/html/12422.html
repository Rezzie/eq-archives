Subject: News on stats from Abashi via EQCaster's


According to Abashi and reported by EQCasters, stats affect your chances of
raising your skill level in skills. Agility increases the chance of getting
a skill raise in dodge is a listed example. This would seem to greatly
downplay the value of stats once you hit your skill cap in many areas sadly.
