analysis and it indicated that lullaby didn't effect more then 4 targets.

stalkers while my party was engaging undead reavers (3 of them). I struck
up lullaby with my lute and sure enough, I got 10 head nods messages (7
stalkers and all 3 undead reavers). There was so much chaos so I'm not sure
if it took or not, but it at least allowed us enough freedom to dispatch of
the gnolls and then eventually the stalkers. I definitely felt like more
then 4 were really sleeping...
