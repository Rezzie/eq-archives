At this level i've spent a lot of time soloing at the goblin warrior camp
in Butcherblock. It usually isn't as crowded as the chessboard and the loot
is much better there.
Another good choice would be the orc camps in Lesser Faydark. They are
always completly empty and the level range there is 7-10 at most of the camps.
With the lull song you can even go solo there.
If you just want good loot and don't care about the EXP go to the bandit
camps in Lesser Faydark. They are always very crowded but you can earn about
10pp per hour.
