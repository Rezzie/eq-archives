>HOW do you get an inventory slot into a Hot Key Slot? I've tried doing the
>"drag" Thing and it doesn't work. Could I be doing something wrong?

It's not so much "drag" as it is clicking and holding the left mouse button
over the inventory slot - your mouse pointer should then visually turn into
the inventory slot - you can release the button - then move the inventory
slot mouse over the hotkey slot you want to put it in and click it in place.
Make sure the upper left hand corner of the inventory slot picture is in the
middle of the hotkey slot in which you are placing else it won't work.
Again, hope this helped.
